## Left Page

### July, Monday 7, 1879

Overcast mtf., Rain began to fall about 2 o'clk. great change in temperature — charmingly cool. — After bkfs. laid rebiting ground on Jacque plate & Jefferson St. — "stopped there out" and at 10 took a walk up town then back & to work rebiting. Dinner at 12.30 — Rebiting until after tea time — In clearing up in the evening lost my walk. Cut paper & sent it down for printing. To bed at 10 — Heavy rain during the night.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 8, 1879

Overcast mtf., but cleared during the afternoon very warm then thunderstorms north of us. After bkfs. laid rebiting ground on Up the hill — varnished it & went out for short walk — back & spent the mtf. finishing rebiting Jacque & the foreground of "Up the Hill" — Dinner at 12.30 — all the P.m. printing. found that I had done too much rebiting — worked hard until after 7 — near 8 when I had my tea — Evef., a short walk with Mother — very fine cloud effects. wrote to Reinhart about etchings for Houghton, Osgood & Co. To bed soon after 10.
