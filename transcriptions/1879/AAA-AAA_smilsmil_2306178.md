## Left Page

### April, Thursday 24, 1879

Clear & very warm — Got to studio at 9.30 — then to N.A. & made a slight sketch from George's painting "A Goat Pasture" — back to studio & commenced a drawing of it upon wood — Miss Halloivell of Chicago &c. called — To lunch at 1.30 worked on drawing until 5 o'clk then finished it. Chas. Miller called late in the afternoon (that is "little Miller.") Chas. M. the big, called in the mf. At 6.30 went to Anna C's to tea — at 8 with Geo. C. Anna & her mother, went to Chickering Hall — W. C. Baird's concert — parts of it exceedingly enjoyable — Aftwd walked home with A.C. — then to 37th St. & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 25, 1879

Still very nied — Got to studio soon after 9 & commenced a little sketch 4×5 in. to fit a little frame made for a Boughton that did not prove satisfactory got it finished soon after 1. then to lunch & after that went to Aldpeeton's with "Goat Pasture" block for which I got $25.00 — then to B.N. Co & arranged to make a drawing of New Mex. Ter. scene. Got blankets for Gifford & Tiffany — then at 5, up Town — went to Gifford's, left blankets at Tiffany's — then to studio & at 6.30 to Kimball's where I had tea — at 8 — a light shower of Rain — called on Miss Kate Hillard at 33 W. 37th St — to my lodgings at 10.15 & to bed about 11.
