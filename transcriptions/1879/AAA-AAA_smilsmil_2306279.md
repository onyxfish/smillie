## Left Page

### November, Sunday 16, 1879

Charming day — clean and crisp — Had [illegible] at 10 — to Hastings at 11 + heard him preach from Col. III — 16" — after services walked down to studio + at 1.30 with Geo. went to Ashland Ho. + had dinner — after that for a while at studio — then walked to Dr. Yale's — There until 4.30 then walked down to studio — went in to Colmari's for a few minutes — then to the Abbatts — 349 5th Ave. — to tea at 7.30 — at studio, matte until 10 then to 39th St. + to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 17, 1879

Very pleasant day — at 9.30 downtown to Photo Eng. Co. — to 74 Beckman St — To see facsimiles started on the press — To studio at 10.30 aftergoing to the [illegible] + getting pictures by Boughton, Bryant + J.G. Brown. Worked until 1.30 + had lunch. Bruce called — + so did Wunderlich who bought three of my Jacques for $5.00 each — at 2.45 out to Mad. Sq. for a breath of fresh air + saw [illegible] Regt, Pres. Hayes, Warts, etc — back to shop at 3.10 + worked until 4 but got only Evans and J.G. Brown done — at 5.30 went to Century + had dinner — after that looked over L'Art until 8 — then to Hoen's + at 9 back to shop, at 10.30 to 37th St. + right to bed.
