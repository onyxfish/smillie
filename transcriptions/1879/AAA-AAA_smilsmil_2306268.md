## Left Page

### October, Thursday 23, 1879

Very nied again — beautiful mef. cloudy day — I got up very much out of sorts —
after bkfo. I went to Mary's & finally got to work about
10.30 — worked on end piece & over, Stanza IV that I had
moss Return to me. Dinner at 12.30 — then finished end piece & to work on intro-
ductory page. Could not see to work after 4.45.
Had a walk before tea at 6. Mef. Read awhile "The un-
seen Universe." Stewart & Jait-
then, mother dictating, wrote
a letter to aunt H. concerning
mother's trip to Westhill — at
9.30 went to P.O. Back &
to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 24, 1879

A very cold, windy day with snow-flurries from the west. Bkf-
at 8 — Kindled fire in father's room
after 10, got to work on introductory
page for Bruce. at 12.15 went up
town for a walk — Dinner at 1 —
again at Bruce's work & finally
finished it about 4 — packed
it up, made a letter to J.S. Moss
of the Photo. Eng. Co. & took pkg. to
sup. Co. & letter to P.O. Then
walked around by Parker. Will
& home to tea at 6.15. Mef
at 8 went with father & mother
to the Recpt. given at Baptist Ch.
to Dr. Kendick who has just
ret. from a four months trip abroad.
There until after 10. Then home
with Kate & to bed at 11.
