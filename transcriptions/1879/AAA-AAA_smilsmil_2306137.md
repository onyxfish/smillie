## Left Page

### January, Thursday 30, 1879

Beautiful day — got to N. A.
about 9.30 & soon had 3 sketch.
finished Hinton, Brennan &
Chase — At noon went to [illegible]
Eng Co — thens to Sackett's & at
1.30 up town to lunch. Miss
Armstrong & Mr. Lord waiting for
me — they left after 4 o'clk —
at 5.30 Sackett, came with [illegible]
sheets of Ill's — with him until
after 6 — got to Anna C's at
6.45 — Mrs. Arnold being ill,
no music there — so I spent
the evng with A.C — until
9.30 — thens to studio — there
until 10.30 — thens to 37th St.
& to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 31, 1879

Still fine weather — morning
after bkf't — went to Sackett's and
finished up last of Ill — cart —
then to studio at 10.15 — but so
tired as to be unfit for anything
fussed about getting things
into some order — To lunch
at 1.45 — After that worked
etching of Bellaire Willows &
at 4 went to N.A. with it — then
to Scribner's & saw Drake — he
gave me to drawings to do —
Back to studio at 6 — tea at
7 — Soon after 8 went to N.A.
Stag Party — opening of Water Color
[illegible] — Lots of fellows of course,
there until 10.30 — very tired
thens to 37th St. after 11 when
I got to bed.
