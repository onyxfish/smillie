## Left Page

### June, Monday 9, 1879

Very pleasant day — After blcfs — went downtown to A.B.N. Co — from there, up to Earl & Wilson's — then to Dewey & Keating & ordered suit of clothes — Got to studio between 11 & 12 — Had lunch at 2 — the afternoon got away from me with very little done on my Jacque etching — Out at 6 & met Fanny K. went scurrying around with her to get up a Piano Quartette for this evng. After going down 4th ave — then to Addams [illegible] & sent pen sketch to Koehler then to 37'S — dressed, then to Anna C's — out — then to studio — then to Fanny K's at 7.15 — tea — at 8.15 went to Missis Clynch — 10 W. 39 St. for Anna C. — Rid with her to K's & then — Rienzi overture — Cherubini Water Carrier Beethoven Septet etc — by Mr. Coleman — Mumma — Fanny & Anna — nearly 11 when I took Anna home & 12 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 10, 1879

Very hot day — To studio at 9.45 — there to Dewey K's — back at 11 — then to work on etching — very much fagged by the heart — had a lunch in studio of strawberries & claret — worked on Jacque during the afternoon but did not make much headway. Wallace Bruce came in bet. 6 & 7 — Recited his poem on the Yosemite & wants me to illustrate it for him — Lest in studio & at 8 to Anna Conk's — with her, her br. Geo. Miss Williams [illegible] Adams, went to Gilmore's Garden — very hot & nothing very interesting, met Julie Spring Emma J — M. Mrs. — — Fanny & Will K. & Geo. Took Emma home at 10.30 — To 37'S — & to bed soon after 11.
