## Left Page

### April, Thursday 10. 1879.

Rain commenced about 10 A.M. & fell nearly all day. Late in the afternoon it came down in torrents — feeling very much out of sorts, little was done beside lounging & trying to tuck things away. The studio is now in a terrible state of dirt & disorder — Lunch about 2 — The P.M. went as did the mf. Binstob & daughter called — At 6.30 went to Anna C's — caught in tremendous shower & thoroughly wet below the knees. After tea Ge° & J took fanny to Anna to Arnold's — then I went to Mrs. Longstreet's in 42d. St — there until 10 — then to 37' St — & to bed.

Maud S'esba & Tut Reade called —

## Right Page

### April, Friday 11. 1879.

Overcast with now & then a sprinkle & then a ray of sunlight — About 10 when I got to studio. Spent the mf. in closet turning out & burning up trash & paper — Lunch at 2 — All the afternoon putting sketches & things for which I have no places in drawers into bundles. Tea at [illegible] — About 8.30 Ge° & I went to 187. E. 21st St & called on Maud S. — but she was not in — then I went to U.A. — there until 10 — then to studio — a light flurry of snow — to 37' St — at 11 & right to bed.
