## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 12, 1879

Very mild — pleasant mng. up late & did not get to
studio until 10. Men putting
in Steam Radiator — Avery
sent up Lien, Dupré, Corot
& Detaille — I set right to
work on the Detaille — at
12.30 went to Deweys & paid
my bills — back at 1.15 & had
a light lunch in studio —
finished Detaille & Lien
at 3.30 went to Green's — 74
Beekman St. to see Bruces
work — did not like the type
setting & gave orders to have
the poem reset in smaller type.
Then to studio at 5.30 — Paine —
dinner at 6 — then to studio
& worked over pen sketches —
at 9 went to Wyant's Room
& at 10.30 to 374 St. & right to
bed.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 13, 1879

Uncomfortably warm — at 9 at
Avery's — at 10 at Green's Printing
Establishment. There until 11 —
when the pages were made to
suit me — then to Manhattan
Photo. Eng. Co. in Peare St. — no
one in. Then to Franklin St.
Met. Elev. R.R. Station & to 14 St.
Got to Avery's at 12 & right to
work — at 1 had lunch — at
1.30 again at work — made
pen sketches of Claeys, Dupré
& Corot — gave up work at 4.15
as I could see no longer —
then to studio — to Ashland
Ho. to dinner soon after 5 —
at 8, after writing & resting in
studio, went to Nicoll's & with
him went to Reception at the
Union League Club — there
until about 10.30. Then to 374
St. & to bed at 11.
