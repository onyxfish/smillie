## Left Page

### September, Monday 1. Room 5. 1879.

*Summit House. Tops of Mt. Washington*

oppressively warm day — George
went to Jackson by 9.12 train
Kitty, Imma & I spent the m'g
among the rocks of Idlewild
Reading & writing — back to hotel
abt — dinner at 2.15 — all ready
after that to leave but 3.15 train
did not come along until 3.50
then on to Fabyan's — detained
there so that we did not
start up the mt. until 5.05 —
Ev'g. closed down col[d] very
thick so that we had no
sunset. Reached Summit at
7.10 & got two good rooms — Tea
at 7.30 — after that paced the
Promenade outside watching
the moonlight & flying scud —
no view — a pleasure to visit
to Gov't Signal Station — & to
the Printing office of "Among
the Clouds" — to bed a little
before 11.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 2. 1879.

*Jackson Falls House. Jackson, N.H. Room 29.*

Up soon after 5 — dismal morning, high wind
with driving, wet mist. Bkfs. about 6 — no
signs of lifting — Stage for Glen House to start at
7.30 — but all the passengers agreed to wait
until 10 — Som after opening in the quick
flying clouds gave us momentary views
to the S. + S.E. A few minutes after 10 we left
the Summit House in Glen Stage — Kitty & Imma
in front seat with driver, Kitty having found a
number of interesting botanical specimens on
the Summit — wind still very high — the views
down into the Gulf of Mexico & Tuckerman's
Ravine very grand. No horizon visible owing
to haze. Got to Glen House at 11.30. fixed up
a little, then sauntered to a little [illegible] a
short distance from the house & patiently awaited
dinner — very hot & very hungry — At last 2
o'clk, came & we had dinner — at 3. off on
the Stage — good top seats & had a delicious
15 miles ride to Glen Station on P.&O. R.R. Got
in at 5.45 — train due at 6 — behind time, of
course — waited until 6.50 when it came —
Kitty & Imma off on it for tea at Fabyan's and
at 10.30 on to Burlington & thence to Mr. Harris's
on Lake [illegible]. after they left I hired a
horse & returned to Jackson — 3 m. — Found
all at the "Thorn Mt. House" — No Room for
me — got a very nice Room at Tricky's Jackson
Falls House — wrote a letter to Mill and
to bed at 10.
