## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 16, 1879

Clear & cold — got to studio at 9.45 — fussed about over my pfs until after 11 — then proved da Vinci plates — Colman came in & offered me Tiffany's horse for a ride this p.m. but I felt so under the weather from a cold — with headache & backache that I begged off — Dinner at 2 — after that Mr. Koehler came in, then Falconer — Sackett — Nicoll & others — I got time to mount a few etchings — Tea at 7 — Evef. went to Will's — there with Mary until 10 — took her to G.N.V. & & then to 37 S. — to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 17, 1879

The mf (charming weather) was spent in preparing pf of pf of da Vinci to send to Roberts Bros. & letter to accompany them, also in preparing some pfs for other etchings — Dinner at 2 — after that went down from 5 P.o. — then to W. 9o? Zabriskie's, 68 & 70 Wm St — to C.B. note Co. to whom I delivered a little sketch of Sidney Yts, Bldg. which I made this mf & for which I got $10 —, back to studio at 5 — Evef. in studio mounting sketches until after 10 — then to 37 S. — to bed at 11.
