## Left Page

### February, Friday 21, 1879

High wind, clear & very cold.
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on 33 × 44 — Painted very
slowly & in much trouble
over the Devil's Pulpit Rock.
After 2 when I went out
to lunch — Painted until 5.
Baldwin called to consult with
me concerning N.C. nominations
for next year — At 5.30, at Wed
Coaks Receptions — 40th St. & Park
Ave — there until 6.30 — then
escorted Emma J. & Julie B. home —
then to studio — had tea at 8 —
lounged — wrote a letter & at 10
went to H.Q. to 39th St — at 10.30
& right to bed —

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 22, 1879

Dubious mth, later sleety snow.
Got to studio about 9.30 & to work
on 33 × 44 — Painting over foreground,
miserable cold, mainly catarrhal
in character, bothering me — Father
came in about noon. Out to lunch
at 2 — Did not do much in the
afternoon & finally made up my
mind not to go to Brooklyn to see
Kate Hillard in Private Theatricals.
At 6.30 took dinner at the Springs
[illegible] went to Anna C's — a little
company given to the Lewis sisters
of Phila — there until 11.30 — then
to 39th St — & to bed at midnight.
