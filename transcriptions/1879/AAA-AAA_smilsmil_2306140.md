## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 5, 1879

Dull mtf — snow before noon — got to studio's about 10, having called for a few minutes at the St. Cloud. To work for Harpers on "The Narrows & Blk. Mt. Lake George" — at 1.30 out to lunch — worked until 4 & got drawing finished — think it pretty good for me — Fired — Rested awhile on lounge & at 4.40 went to Grace Ch. to a Warren Organ Recital — didn't warm up much — beastly weather — back to studio at 6 — Tea at 7 — & at 8 at Emma D's — went with her to Hoyt's in 5[6th] St. a Reading by Mrs. Vandenhoff's pupils — back to studio a little before 11 — To 37. S. at 11.30.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 6, 1879

Pleasant Day — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on sketch "Harbor Islands, from under the Deer's Leap — Lake Geo." — worked until 1 o'clk — then Miss A — came & I started her at Water color work & worked for her on the back ground of a large figure piece that she has commenced — near 3 before I got my lunch — worked until after 5 & all tho' I gave some attention to Miss A — I got sketch finished very nearly — At 6.30 at Anna Cook's — after tea went to Miss Arnold's with her — leaving her I went to the Payne's — found them all going to Riding school — went with them & stayed until after 9 — then to St. Cloud — Wells Joe & Minnie — with Father & Mother — there until 10.20 — then to 37. S. & to bed about 11. [illegible — ~2 lines]
