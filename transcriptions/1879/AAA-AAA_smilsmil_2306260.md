## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 7, 1879

Misty, overcast m'g., clearing
away before noon — charming
weather for the rest of the day.
After bkfst — went to my lot for
awhile then back & about &
got to work on Bruce's Ill. n's —
Stanza VIII. Worked until
1 — dinner time — After that
worked until near 4, finishing
sketch, then out, walked
to the lake & sauntered about
R.R. depot until near 6, then
to W.J.S.'s — at near 7 went to
P.O. then to tea — most
of eveg. spent at H.F. Tyle's.
To bed at 10.30.

Rec'd a card from Geo. telling
me that the house (home) is
full & will be all the week
— so I am not wanted there
yet —

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 8, 1879

Overcast m'g., breaking away with
very hot sun about noon — overcast
later & short, heavy shower in the
night. Bkfst. at 8 — after that
wrote awhile, went to Post Office
& to my work between 10 & 11 — worked
until dinner at about 1 — After
that a rest for awhile then
worked until 4 on Stanza X —
then went out riding with
W.J.T. Went to lake where we
found men at work gathering
stumps on his lot & firing them.
So much interested that we stayed
until near 6 — slight, spattering rain
then to P.O. & home — tea near 7 —
eveg. Read newspaper until
after 8, then on sofa until 10 —
then to bed.
