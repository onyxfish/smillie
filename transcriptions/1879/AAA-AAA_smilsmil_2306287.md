## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 2, 1879

Mild day — P.M. overcast. Got to studio at 9.30 — most of the mng spent making paste, mounting etchings & remounting sketch of Pali's Chasm. At 2 went to dinner at the "Westmoreland" — After that the afternoon got away with but little accomplished. Rec'd a note from Bunce stating that the cost of printing my etching would oblige them to give up the idea of using it. Also went to 37 St made a change in dress then back to studio & had tea at 7. At 8 at Mrs. Wyntcoop's took her & her mother to the Recep'n at the Students' League — there for half an hour or so — then took them home — then to studio — there until after 10; then to 37 S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 3, 1879

Very warm day — After bkfst went to D. Appleton's & saw Bunce found that he really wanted my etching for the Art Journal — the great trouble being the expense of the printing — then went to Kimmel & Voigt's & had a talk with them about printing — then back to Bunce & he said he w'd take the plate. Then to studio — wrote letter to Koehler and made out my bill. Abt. nearly 3 when we went to dinner — little done during the rest of p.m. — tea at 6.30 — At 1.30 to 37 S. — dressed and then to Mrs. Wynkoop's where Miss Slaughter was spending the even'g — there until after 10 o'clk — then to 37 S. — and to bed.
