## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 21. 1879

Very pleasant w'f. In the afternoon it clouded over.
Bkfs. at 8 — wrote notes to Mr. Emerson & Nicole & at noon
11 went up town with Kate to Baptist Ch. & for au & own
worked at putting up letters Welcome Home — then to my
home & worked awhile on Po-no-no. Dinner at 12.30.
Afternoon at work over the several Bruce ill [illegible]. Uncle
W, aunt Agnes & her sister Kate called. After 5 father & I
went out for a walk. Tea at 6 — at 7.15 went with Kate
to Matt's & then to old Baptist Ch. to meeting of Literary Soc'ty — and
heard discussion "Power of Oratory vs the Power of the Press." At. J. Platt
of the Eagle spoke for the Press. Home at 10.30 in slight rain. Heavy
rain during the night.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 22. 1879

Rain during the m'f — the rest of the day cloudy. After bkfs.
went to Matt's, then to Mary's & after 10 got to my own work,
for a time fussing over dedicatory page, then commenced on Fall
piece — a view of Yosemite Fall by moonlight. Dinner at 12.30.
After that had a paper, had a short nap & again at work at
3 — at 4.30 the City Gate Guard of Atlanta, Ga. came to Eastman's
grounds & had a parade — I went out to see it — in at about 5.30
found Jno. Jessup at home — he took tea with us & went north
on the 6.35 train. After tea I wrote a letter to Charl. [illegible] —
& went to P.O. then to Matt's & at 8.30 to Heltull's, Market St —
where Mr. & Mrs. C H S Williams were. 17th anniversary of their
wedding — Home soon after 10 & right to bed.
