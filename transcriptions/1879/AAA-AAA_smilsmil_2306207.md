## Left Page

### June, Saturday 21, 1879

Charming day — very mild but not uncomfortably warm —
After [illegible] went up town and called on many — then home
& spent the m[illegible] in getting my things put away and
ready to work — About noon started on Harper's Weekly illerd.
"Quebec" Dinner before 1 —
after it, a nap & then again at work, finishing the St. Johns
gate section & part of "Breakneck
Stairs". At 5.30 with [illegible] & [illegible]
went out & walked until 7 —
then in to tea — At 8.30 went
up town — back in an hour
& to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 22, 1879

Mild, overcast day, occasional light rain falling. Breakfasted
late — went to Christ's Ch. ([illegible])
& heard Rev. Mr. Geissenfuss —
After service, with [illegible] — went
to [illegible] — there for a few minutes
then a short walk — & home to
dinner at 1 — After that, read
until after 4 — then miting —
to Falconer, Dr. Yale — Dr. Kelsey & Dr.
Guernsey — tea at 6.30 — About
8 o'clk. went to Baptist Ch. and
heard tail end of Dr. Kendrick's
discourse — then home & to bed
before 10 —
Weather cleared charmingly
in the afternoon.
