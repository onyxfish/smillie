## Left Page

### October, Monday 13. 1879

Overcast again but cleared as usual. Bkfst at 8 — then to Rennie's
& to studio — Got to work at once on pen sketch, Stranza IX. Bruce —
Had some callers — abt. Colman came in & invited me to lunch.
I accepted — after that, went to his studio & saw some of his
studies & etchings — then again to my work & at 4 finished
IX. Then to Deevay the Tailor & to Knoedler's — at 6 to Kimball's
& had tea — there until after 8 —
Mr. Haines (a Cargill) there — then to Dunlap's & bought a hat, then
to 331 E. 17th St. & called on the Trelawns —
a regular family party. Oscar & wife — Fred & fiancée. Emma
& Grace — there until 10.30 — then to 37th St. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 14. 1879

*at home. Poughkeepsie.*

Clear milf. & much cooler. Bkfst at
8.30 — then to Rennie's — then to studio
& prepared to go down town and
at 12 o'clk — at 16 Beekman St. to
meet Bruce. waited 20 m. for him
then to Photo. Eng. Co. — saw most of
what has been already done of
the Bruce work & left 4 designs.
Then again to Beekman St. & saw
Bruce — then to Ned Cooke's, cor.
Maiden Lane & Bdway — then to
Wills — A.B.N. Co. then had lunch
then to Chare's — waited for him
30 m. did not see him; then to
studio, said some hurried good-
byes & then to Grand Central
Depot & off at 4 o'clk. Train; got
to Po. at 6.30 & home at 6.45 — had
tea at once — Eveng in chatting
& to bed at 10.30.
