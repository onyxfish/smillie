## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 11. 1879.

Very nice, pleasant day. got to 44 St at 9.30 & had a man from Renner's help me down with the big Schroeger & up with it again at 1 — worked after Schroeger, Compte-Calix & Broughton —
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2 1 15 & worked on Passau & Jean Dubert — finishing at 5.30 — there to 37 St & dressed & at 9 to studios — had tea there & until 9.30 covering sketches — then took them down to Avery but did not see him — got to 37 St soon after 11 & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 12. 1879.

Charming day — got to studio at 9.30 & commenced finishing on 33×44 — worked until 1.30 glazing into foreground rocks & painting solidly on middle distant cliff. Then to lunch & at 2.30 again at work — Myant & Coleman came in & gave me the benefit of advice — Mr. C. came also & Mr. Camp. At 6.30 I hobbled out for a short walk, my feet hurting me very much — then in to tea — Everf, loafed in studio most of the time with Calverley. Got to 37 St soon after 10. gave my feet a rub down with salt & alcohol & to bed about 11.
