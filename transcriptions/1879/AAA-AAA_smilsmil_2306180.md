## Left Page

### April, Monday 28, 1879

Overcast day with seasons of sickly sunshine — Bkfs. at 8 —
Did not go out for a walk until about noon but fussed about indoors — After a short walk had dinner at 1. then tried to get to work on drawing of Arms of New Mexico for B.N.Co. but feel so stupid & bilious that I could do nothing. At 4 o'clk. it commenced to rain heavily breaking up my plan of taking Father out to ride.
It continued to rain very gently all the rest of p.m. &
evef. Tea at 6.30 — Evef.
Reading & chatting until near 10, then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 29, 1879

Cool, breezy mf. After bkfs. walked to [illegible] — In delicious balmy atmosphere — Uncle W. came & took Mother out to Hillside. I tried to work on New Mexico Arms but my head troubled me so that I was quite unfit for work — Dinner at 12.30 — Again tried to work & at 4 went to Livery Stable got a pair of horses & two seat wagon & took Father, Kate & [illegible] out to Hillside by East Road — Charming & muddy — horses very spunky — back at 6.30 — then tea. Mother arrd. later — Evef. lolling lazily, with some family chat — . To bed at 10.
