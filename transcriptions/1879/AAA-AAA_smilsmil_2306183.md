## Right Page

### May, Monday 5, 1879

Pleasant day — got to studio soon
after 9 & worked until near 12 on
water color drawings — then down
town — first to Harper's — got pf. of
most of my "Lake Geo." illustrations
— a good lot except Fuengeling's —
then to A.B.N. Co. after getting
my lunch — there until after
2 — then to studio after stopping
at Swain Gifford's with some strong
oil — worked on Water Colors
until after 6 — Mrs. Colman called
& invited us in to her house in
the evg. Tea about 7 & at 8.30
I went in — Emma, Fanny and
Anna there — quartettes — at
9.45 I went to opening of R.E. Moore's
Am. Art Gallery — Kurtz Bldg. — back
to Colman's at 10.15 — & at near
11 went home with Fanny K.
& Anna C. — 10 37° S° — & to bed
at midnight. Since a heavy
thunderstorm passed north
of us.
