## Left Page

### February, Saturday 1. 1879.

Got to studio at 9.30 — then to N.A. & made careful revision of Catalogue printing & made instructions to Sackett — Then to studio — Cleared the deck for action — Stretched two pieces of paper for Sanburn sketches & Miss Armstrong's drawing — To lunch at 1.30 — P.M. I worked up on Sanburn Ill. Natural meadows with surrounding pines — among sierras. At 6 went in to Minna's & had dinner & at 8 — with Minna & Anna C. at Damrosch's Symphony concert at Steinway Hall. Very wild audience over Wilhelmij, got to 37° 8° — about 1.30 — Bitter cold night — & windy —

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 2. 1879.

Very cold & windy — most of the day overcast — Bt. bp° — at 9.30 — then to studio — then to Dr Hastings's & heard him preach from John 3° - 13° — Afterwards to St. Cloud Hotel & at 2 had dinner with father, mother & Kate. At 4.30 to studio — a nap on lounge — then made to G. W. Adams flutist & to the Zalkins — Tea in studio at 8 — Before 9 went down to Colman's — there until near 10 — then again to studio — there until 11 — Then to 37° 8° — & to bed at midnight —
