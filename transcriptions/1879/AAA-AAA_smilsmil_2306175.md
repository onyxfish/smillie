## Left Page

### April, Friday 18. 1879.

Horrible day — driving wind & rain — Did not get up until 2 o'clk — At 3 o'clk. had my [illegible] at the Hotel Royal — then to Studio — fussed over a lot of blankets & printing material sent from A.B. N. Co. for Etching Club — Tea at 4.30 & at 8 — feeling the need of entertainment — I went to the Bdway Theatre & heard "Cavalare" by the Rica Church Choir Co — was very much pleased — to 37° S. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 19. 1879.

Still very disagreeable weather. Got to Studio at 9.30 & to work at N.C. sketches — did not make much progress — Lunch at 2 — made a pen sketch for Miss Henrietta Beebe — Then I tried a little printing — tea after 7 — & at 8.30 at the Seelting Heard Mr. Wm H[illegible] Goodegear on "The Illusive Principle in Architecture demonstrated by original masonry measurements". A very confused & confusing speaker on an interesting subject — About 1.30 started for 37° S. & to bed at 12.15.
