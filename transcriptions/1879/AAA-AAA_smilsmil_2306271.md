## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 29, 1879

Charming day — got up late,
& & with a determination
to go right down to N.Y. City
& have some of my teeth out,
& so late to catch the 8.30 train
so, as my face was almost
without pain my ardor cooled —
Spent the m'f packing things —
at 11.30 went out for a walk.
went to Many's — then to Barlow's
a dentist. He administers nitrous
Oxide gas — but had not courage
to ask him to operate — To dinner
at 12.45. During the afternoon
I got a number of my things
together — & at 5 went out to walk
with Father — Dr. Kendrick at the
house to tea — Glorious moonlight
night, at intervals I suffered
much from neuralgia — To bed
at 10.30 & passed a restless
night.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 30, 1879

Still charming weather. After
b'f't, Father's fire being out I
fixed it, then, at about 9.30 I
went straight to Barlow's and
had my lower, back wisdom teeth
& bicuspith out under influence
of nitrous oxide — very near an-
aesthetic as I was not entirely
unconscious, but suffered no pain.
Then home & during rest of m'f wrote
letters to Miss Hallowell at Chicago
(who has sold $84.00 worth of my art truck)
to Gilson, to Charl & to Geo & at 12 went
to P.O. with them — Dinner at 12.45 —
afternoon fussing around — did
not go out for an eve'g walk —
Tea soon after 6 — Eve'g spent
reading & loafing & to bed
shortly after 10.
