## Left Page

### February, Sunday 9, 1879

Bright day, but windy. Did not get up until noon owing to headache — then to St. Clend & Frost. dinner with Father, Mother, Kate & Geo. At 3.30 went with Kate to Chase's — they went on to St. Thomas's & I to studio — found a card from Cyrus Butler asking me to be at V. A. of D. between 3.30 & 4 — went over there & found quite a party — some French Ship Canal officials — back to Studio at 4.30 & at 6 to Emma J's — tea & there until 8 — took E. to Mrs. Doorman's & then I went to Studio. Wrote there until about 10, then to Bgs. S. & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 10, 1879

Pleasant day. Got to Studio at 9.30 — worked awhile on sketch for A.B.M. Co. — Hill's Seculper I dem label — then down to Harper Bros. — there for some time with drawings & criticising engraver's pfs — then to A.B.M. Co — there until aftnl — then to Harley's about blocks that he has engd — then uptown, getting to Studio a little before 2 — Miss A. & W. L. waiting — I went out to my lunch — then back & spent the rest of the p.m. with them at Water Color work — tea at 6.30 — At 8 o'clk — Etchers began to arrive — Etching Club meeting until 10 — then T. m. wood came in & we talked until after 11 — then to 37th St. & to bed at 11.40.
