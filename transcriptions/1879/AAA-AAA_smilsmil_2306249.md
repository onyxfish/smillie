## Left Page

### September, Monday 15, 1879

Brilliant day. Bk fo- a mile
walk. Then wandered about
for some time taking a look
at my orchard study. Finally
got to work at Yosemite and
finished the firstiller. Then
a walk before dinner and
after that, planning for some
illustrations until 4 o'clk. Then
out for a walk & before I had
quite made up my mind
where to go, found myself well
up on Thorn Mt., pushed on
at 5.40 got to summit. The
color of surrounding mts. superb.
Sunset about 6 — then down the
mt. to House in 40 m. Part of the
way across lots — rubbed myself
down & changed clothes — tea at 8 —
went in parlor until near 9 —
then to Thorn Mt. Ho. there until
10 — back & chatted with D. Smillie
until 11, then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 16, 1879

Clouded heavy day — after bkft.
walked around the mile square
then for awhile studied at my
orchard sketch — made a call
on Mrs. Hardenburgh then young
charges at her cottage & at 11
got to work on Yosemite (Bruce)
mrk planning the illustrations
a bit until dinner — 1 o'clk —
after that, another walk and
then worked until about 4.30
then Geo. came for me & we went
to the Bowling alley & I bowled
with Mr. H., Miss Vault & Miss Van C.
Then walked around the mile sq.
with the Misses Van. Tea at 6, went
in Parlor until after 8 — then to Thorn
Mt. Ho. Rains — there until 10, then
back & to bed at 11.

Rec'd check from A.B.N. Co. for
$132.70s.
