## Left Page

### January, Sunday 26, 1879

Windy & cold — up late & had bkfst. at 10 — then to studio &
got fire started up — feeling out of sorts I stretched out on the
lounge — Between 12 & 1 Nyant came in, then Smith of Springfield — then Gifford — It was after 2 when I went with Geo.
to Smith & Green's Res't. & got some-thing to eat — then for a few minutes to see Miss Dunhee — then up to
Sagamu's Room — & at 4.30 to studio. At nearly 6.30 go to A.N.N's where I took tea with everybody Mr. J. S. &
his wife — At 9.30 walked down to 37. S. with Geo. & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 27, 1879

Overcast & rather mild — got to studio about 10 — very soon went to N.A. & to work on Cat. sketches —
At 1.30 again to studio for a little while & at 2 to dinner — then again to N.A. & worked there until 5 —
getting sketches & then T.H. Smith, Chas. Parsons & W. S. Coleman finished. At 6 o'clk — at St. Cloud Hotel where
at 6.30 Father, Mother, Kate, Geo. & I started "the old folks in their new quarters" by taking dinner. Charl
came in later — Father & Mother seem to be very much pleased — With
Charl, at 8.45 I walked to Cor. of 4th Ave & 37 S. — then to studio — there,
fussing over Cat. — until 10. Then to 37. S. — Raw & wet — to bed ab 11.30
