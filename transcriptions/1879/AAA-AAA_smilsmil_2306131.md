## Left Page

### January, Saturday 18, 1879

Dull m'f. with light fall of snow — then a scatter of rain — then a clear up — must to Anna C's at 8.30 to arrange to go out with her next Tues. en then to blfs — To studio at 10 — finished pen sketch for Ill. cat — then to work on 7×11 — "Study at Shrub Oak" — worked also on other small sketches — several callers — among them Josie Jessup, M.P., Charlie — at 5 dressed & went to Miss Waller's Recpt'n. 35 W. 15th St. Back to studio at 6.45 — Tea at 7.15 — Spent the rest in studio until shortly after 10 — then to 37' S — & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 19, 1879

Very cold clear day — went to Studio before going to church & invited his — then to Dr. Hastings — heard him preach upon Prayer & praying — then walked up to A.V.V's — Father, Mother & all there — am? from to K. yesterday p.m. dinner about 2 — got up at 4 — nearly sober — Regular quitter dinner — about 5 with Father, went to Will's — back to A.V.V's to tea at 6 — At 8.30 walked down the Ave. to 47th S. with Charl — then to studio — then about 10 to 37th S. & to bed.
