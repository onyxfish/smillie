## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 11, 1879

Another very hot day — After going to Millers to have my umbrella recovered — got to studio at 10 oclk. To work on Jacque etching, had it in the acid for 15 m — To lunch at 1.30. In the afternoon worked at press. Taking 1 p/p of Sam. Coleman's plate. Market House of Bruges & my Up the Hill & Highlands on the Hudson. I [illegible] might came in & offered me $240 — for my Rough [illegible] and Raquette lake — I accepted the first, away the pictures — At 6.15 I went into Coleman's & had dinner — there until near 9 — then to studios & fussed there until after 10. Then to 37 S. & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 12, 1879

Very warm day — muggy and cloudy about noon with heavy thunderstorm later — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on etching — had it in the acid — To lunch at 2 — Again etching & biting on my Jacque — Rec. note from Anthony asking me to take a share in an enterprise started in the early winter — that of getting up an ill. gift or Christmas book — etchings — Altho' I feel anything but complimented by so late an invitation, I accepted — Geo. & I Froate dinner with the Colemans then to studios & fussed & wrote — At 10.30 called at the Wellington 42 St. & Mead. Ave. to find Wight — I hear such a bad reports that I am afraid I have lost my picture — no clue to his (Wight's) whereabouts — Then to 37 S. & to bed som after 11.
