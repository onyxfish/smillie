## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 3, 1879

Overcast, warm & threatening rain.
B'kf's before 8 — after that, got my trunk
up to my room & about 9.30 to
work Tracing S.A. drawing for A.B.N.
Co. my head bothered me, I felt
very tired & sleepy; so I spent
nearly two hours of the rest in
my bed, a little [illegible] — then
dinner at 1 — the afternoon
passed much as the morning — so
I made little progress with my
Tracing — Tea at 6.30 — Every
went over to Thorne Mr. House when
I heard a shocking bad Reader —
Back about 10 — until 11.15 sat
& chatted with Mr. Roberts [illegible]
of Boston — then to bed —
Rain falling.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 4, 1879

Heavy rain nearly all day —
After 8 when I had b'kf's — at 9 went
over to see Geo. At 10 back & tried
to work. my head still far from
right — but I did something on the
Tracing — at 12.15 went on piazza
to walk — dinner at 1 — more chat
with Messrs Roberts & Reinhart —
at 2.30 again to my work & at
last finished Tracing; then out
in the mizzle & wet with my
rubber suit on & walked
away up the hill behind
the house — found exercise
if nothing else. Tea at 6.30
after that Miss Greene sent me
her card from the parlor & I
went in & was introduced to Mr.
Dyer & others. Spent the eveng
pleasantly until 10 o'clk — then
Geo came over — Storm cleared
away — To bed at 10.30
