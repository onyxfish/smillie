## Left Page

### March, Thursday 13, 1879

Very pleasant day — got to studio about 10 o'clk — and spent mtf. Repainting a little 6×10 sketch that I am going to give to the Grannies fund — at 1 o'clk my class came. Misses Armstrong, Morrison, Nittenbe + Mother + Mr. Lord — I worked with them until near 3 — then out to lunch — back with them until 5 — at 6.30 at Anna C's to tea — Fanny K. unwell — After escorting Ann to Arnold's, went to 37 S. dressed + at q went to Gordon's Reception No. 1 & 38 S. — a very pleasant entertainment — & a good supper — got to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 14, 1879

Overcast, with now & then slight spatters of rain — got to studio at 10 o'clk. + to work on X "the Tops of the hills" — worked in a very desultory way, doing as little as possible, carefully leaving the first painting wherever it told my story. Lunch at 2.30 + went to Bank + drew bal. Resulting from Sale of Water color pictures in last Exhib. Then to Knoedler's + spent half an hour among pictures. Then to Studio & put last touches on Hill tops & fastened it into its frame — Tea at 4.30. Eveng. in Studio writing letters until after 10 — then to 37th S. + to bed at 11.30.
