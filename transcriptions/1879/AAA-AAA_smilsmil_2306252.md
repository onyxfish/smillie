## Left Page

### September, Sunday 21, 1879

Delightful day — m[illegible] walked
twice around the mile square
with Ge° & Mr. R. — then to the little
Church where there was service
but must confess that I slept
through the sermon — Chatting
on piazza until dinner at 1.
Soon after 2 I started for a
walk with miss Woodbury — we
went around the five mile walk
& besides started to climb to the
top of a hill for a view of Mt. Washin[gton]
but found that we had undertaken
too much — back to tea at 6 — Eve[ning]
at 7.30 service in parlor — a very
excellent & interesting discourse from
the[?] Wynkoop followed by general
conversation in which he answered
a great variety of questions in a
very satisfactory manner — Later
Messrs Roberts, Mr. Miles & Rogers of Boston
& self had a more private confab.
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 22, 1879

A very pleasant but cool day.
After bfst. walked around the mile
sq. & then to work with the pen on
Bruce's work. At 12.30 again went
around the mile sq. & dinner about
1.30 — Afternoon again at work &
finished the illustration commenced
in the m[orning]. At 4.30 or 5 went to
the bowling alley alone & bowled
three "strings" then walked the
miles walk & had tea. After
that engaged in a whist bout —
Messrs Brown & Wingate ag[ain]st Roberts
& self — we have played 23 games,
13 won by R. & self, 10 by B. & W. — me
had some close exciting work
between 8 & 10.30 — we played
5 games B. & W. winning the "Rubber"
— then to bed.
