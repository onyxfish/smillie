## Left Page

### July, Friday 11, 1879

Warm day — afternoon over-
cast — Rain began falling very
gently at 7 — Later in the ev'g
it poured in Torrents — In
the m'f I felt so "played out"
that I did not get to work
but wrote letters until noon — then to Post Office.
Dinner at 12.30 — After that
to work on Jacque plate &
scraped down & then stoned
much of the foreground about
the sheep — it being very much
overbitten — then wet down
paper for proofs — Tea at 6.30.
Prof. Ritter called with John
Burroughs's "Locusts & Wild Honey"
& a proposition that we drive
over to B's place & pic-nic next
Thurs — At 8 I went to Baptist
Ch. & met Matt & Kate at concert
of Po't. English Glee Club — At 10
walked home in a heavy, drenching
Rain — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 12, 1879

Charming day after the soaking
Rain — fresh cool breezes from the
N.W. — After b'kf's — a short walk
— then a little dry pointing on
Jacque — then pulled a proof
& decided the plate finished —
As I still felt very tired I
worked slowly & got three pfs
before dinner at 12.30. The
same in the afternoon, getting
altogether 8 pfs. Uncle W.
called with During. Emma
B. came & stayed over night,
Tea at 6.30 — Father arr'd from
N.Y. in very good spirits after
his four days of vacation.
After tea, with Geo. called
on the Loomis's — pleasantly
there on piazza until 9 —
then after a short walk, home —
Read Burroughs until 11, then
to bed.
