## Left Page

### July, Thursday 3, 1879

Very hot. Did not get to my work until after 10
— then to work on Osgood block. "Black shaders fall
from Leudens Fall" — Dinner at 12.30 — worried all the
afternoon over block, but could not get what I
wanted — it seemed either too black or not dark enough
— gave it up at 6 o'clk. —
Tea soon after — after that, while writing to R.V.S.
Anthony, Tom & Jim Shearman came — on their way to
the Overlook House — They stayed until near 11 —
Tom's conversation was mainly speculations concerning hereafter. To bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 4, 1879

Horribly hot day — After bkfst.
wrote & fussed until 10 o'clk —
then up town — got a mench of Tom Smith, back, & spent
the rest of the mrng. working
over father's press — Re-set the
journals, cleaned & oiled it
up — Dinner about 1. Mr. &
Mrs. Vivians, Mary & children,
Matt & El at dinner with us.
After dinner finished press
& then to work on plate
"Up the Hill" — laid Rebiting
ground — Tea at 6.30 — same
party as at dinner — they left
before 8 — I wrote awhile.
Charming shower with fresh
breeze about 5 P.M. Went
to P.O. with my letters & to bed
soon after 10.
