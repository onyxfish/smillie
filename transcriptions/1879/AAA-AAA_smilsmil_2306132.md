## Left Page

### January, Monday 20, 1879

Very cold m'f. Nearly 10 when I got to studio — John sick — fire not attended to — Ther. 42° — I was mad — Started up fire & kindled one in grate — finally got to work — made slight outline 10×14 on a piece [illegible] rough Whatman paper — but before I got fairly started on my washes Miss Armstrong came — I had many interruptions so that I did not get to lunch until 3 — then back & had an hour I comparative quiet on my sketch which I got finished — making one of the strongest things that I have done — then a rest — tea at 6.30 then by fire until 9 — then to 37 S². Got my feet into hot water & tumbled into bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 21, 1879

Very pleasant m'f — still cold but moderating — overcast p.m. — [illegible]. Got to studio at 9.30 — worked awhile on storm sketch then at my large drawing & spent most of the day redrawing parts from which the Sap-green had faded — to lunch at 1.45 — mother & Kate came about 3 — got to Mrs C's — 23° St — but let me off — I worked awhile on 10×20 got that in better shape — much of the afternoon lost with callers of whom we had quite a number — Kate Hilland — [illegible] Paynes — Sacketts — etc — at 6 went to Anna C's to tea & at 8 to Standard Theatre where a party of 11 of us had seats together & saw the pretty little opera burlesque of Gilbert & Sullivan's — to bed at 11.30.
