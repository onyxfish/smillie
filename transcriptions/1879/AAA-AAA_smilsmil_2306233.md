## Left Page

### August, Thursday 14, 1879.

Fine day — I worked on A.B.N. Co. drawing but was
much interrupted by visitors as Gel was holding a Re-
ception & showing his studio.
Dinner at 1 & more callers in the afternoon but I got
my drawing finished, paid & in 24p. man's hands & wrote
letters to Macdonough & father.
About 4 a beautiful thunder storm came up & lasted an
hour. Tea at 6.30 — Evening on piazza & in parlor until
10 o'clk. Then to my Room & fussed with packing
until 12 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 15, 1879.

*Ocean Bluff House. Kennebunkport, Me. Room 55.*

Up soon after 6 — dull & very cool
mf. Finished packing my trunks & at 8 had bkfst. After that, until
9.45 making our adieux — friends
plenty & very pleasant. Rode
to depot & took 10.15 train, got
to Salem at 10.55 — wandered
about the streets there & went
into Peabody Institute — Fallen
short & had to give up walking
next to Holley. Free run at 12 &
had lunch — then to station &
waited until 1.10 for train, then
to Kennebunk at 4.02. Had to
drive 6 m. to K. port. in buggy —
trunks in wagon. Got to hotel
at 5.30. Tea at 6.30 — very cold —
about 8.30 a fancy dress party in the
dancing room — Gypsies, Babies, Indian
Princesses etc, etc — about 25 girls & 2
men in costume — watched the goings
on until near ten o'clk — then to bed.
