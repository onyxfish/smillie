## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 22, 1879

Raw, overcast & disagreeable. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on large drawing 13×28 — finished it — lunch at 1.30 — Spent the afternoon on 10×20 & got that finished — tea at 6.45 & about 8 as I felt too tired to do any more studio work, I walked up to A.V.V. 5th Ave. & 64th S. & visited with father & mother until a little after 10 — then walked down with Charl — got to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 23, 1879

Mixed overcast mff. clearing toward pleasant p.m. & the mff. Fought all day against a headache — got to studio at 10 & spent the mff over catalogue work. At 1 o'clk Mr. Ford & Miss Armstrong came & until 4 o'clk I gave my time to them except when the R. Dains came & jungling, the evg. came — Spent an hour with them over W.C. sketches for Harper. At 4.30 I gave up & was on sofa until near 6 — at 6.30 to Anna Cook's to tea — took her to Arnold's — then to N.A. of D. there until 9.15 looking over the pictures with an eye to selecting for Cat — then to studio there until 10.20, then to 37 S. sat & chatted with Mary Titjens until 11.30 — then to bed. Anna Cook's coffee kept me awake until 1 o'clk.
