## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 9, 1879

Charming day — Father got away for N.Y. immediately
after bkfst. After my walk
I got to work on my etchings.
As I had over-bitten
much of my work was
with stone & charcoal.
a short walk before dinner
at 12.30 — Most of the
afternoon spent printing
got bkfst. of all three of my
plates & am somewhat en-
couraged. Tea soon after 6.
After that took another proof
then washed up. Rev. Mr.
Wheeler, wife & daughter
called. a short walk in
the evening & to bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 10, 1879

A very warm day. Part of the
time a dry, strength-sapping
wind blowing — After bkfst. a
short walk — then to work
on my etchings until 11 o'clk
then commenced proving
Jeffersons. Dinner 12.30
— then again at proving &
worked bkfst. off "Up the Hill" —
at it until tea at 4 o'clk
— then very much worn out.
Everjim rocking chair until
near 10 o'clk — then to my
own room & sit at one of the
windows enjoying the night
breeze from the west until
11 o'clk — then to bed.
