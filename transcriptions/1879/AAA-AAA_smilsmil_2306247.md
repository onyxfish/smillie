## Left Page

### September, Thursday 11, 1879

overcast wtf., After bkfo: walked
around the mile square with Mr.
Roberts - then met Mr. Hardenburg
Mr. Moses & Miss Van Horn - went to
their rooms for a few minutes and
saw their sketches, then to my
sun room at 10.30 - Quite used up
with a headache - upon my
bed until dinner time, then
feeling better, got up & had
dinner - afternoon got to work
upon Bruce's Poem & half finished
first illo. Spent part of the
evefg in parlor, at 8.30 from
Mt. Ho. & played whist with
the ladies there - back soon,
then played Poker with Mr.
Roberts & others until after 9 -
head hurting me very much,
then again in parlor - chatting
with Dr. Daville - To bed at 11
& suffered all night with a
headache.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 12, 1879

Charming day with a light shower
or two about noon - I did not get
up until near 9 - ate a very light
breakfast, ate the wtf on my bed
got up between 2 & 3 & had some
crackers - out at 4.30 & climbed
the hill back of the house in a
leisurely manner & got away
up from Mr. to within a mile
of the top - then across lots to the
house, just in time for tea -
feeling better - After tea in parlor
for a time & at 8.30 went over to
from Mr. Ho. back at 9, chatting
with different one until 11 -
then to bed.
