## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 19, 1879

Pleasant day, with quite a snow flurry about 2 o'cl.
Got to studio about 9.30 +
spent most of the m[orning] in
going over George's Water Col[or]
accts. To lunch at 1.30 +
in the afternoon wrote out
Catalogue Com. Report —
Tea in studio as usual
at 7 — + at 8.15 went to
N.A. — annual meeting
of the Water Color Society.
To 37 S[t.] before 11 + to bed
som. after.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 20, 1879

Pleasant day, altho' overcast part
of the time. + B[illegible] late — After that
went to Jungeling's, the wood engraver
222 E. 49 S[t.] with a little blk + white
sketch that I have made for him.
Did not get to studio until 11 —
Rest of a.m. getting things in order
\+ reading Daisy Miller — Wrote a
note to Avery in answer to his
request that I would "shade" my
bill. Miss Morrison, Miss + Mrs.
Motherbee + Mr. Lord came som.
after 1 — at 3 I got out to my
lunch — at 5 they left, I
went to Fanny K.'s to tea —
Annie C. is in Boston and,
Fanny did not go to Arnold's
So, I spent the eve[ning] there
until 10.30, then to 37 S[t.] +
to bed — Raining.
