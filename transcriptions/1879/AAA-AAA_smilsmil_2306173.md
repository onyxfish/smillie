## Left Page

### April, Monday 14, 1879

Miserable muggy. Rainy m[orning]. After b[reakfast] went
down Town & spent the m[ornin]g
getting things ready to print
on my new Press — went to A.
B. N. Co. & got blankets — ink
oil — etc — then to R. Hoe & Co.
& paid bill for moving presses.
Got to studio at 2.30 & had
lunch — P.M. cleared off
pleasantly — I fussed over
my press but was not able to
get to work. Tea at 6.30. At
8. members of Etching club began
to arrive — Had a very lively
meeting — great interest in
the experimental printing.
Beer at 10 — good night at
11 — to 37 St. & to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 15, 1879

Overcast m[ornin]g — again went down
Town to get printing supplies for
the club — at A. B. N. Co — Got to Studio
about 12 & bothered for some time
trying to get ink to my liking.
Rain fell heavily most of
the afternoon — lunch at 2.30 —
Still fussing with printing all
the rest of the day — Should
have gone to Minnie Tiffany Mitchell's
[illegible] in the late P.M — but did not —
Tea at [illegible] Snell, wrote letters
— to 37 St. at 10.45 & right
to bed. [illegible — entire passage]
