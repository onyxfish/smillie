## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 9. 1879.

Overcast with pleasant and threatening streaks of weather.
After my mg walk I got to work on C.B.N.Co. drawing and
finished it soon afunds - then out & had a walk - dinner
soon aftr 1 - the afternoon was
spent in making paste, getting
my drawing packed to send by
Exps. to N.Y. & writing letters to
Will. & to Wallace Bruce.

A walk before tea at 6.30.
Evtg. a little whist with [illegible]
in the office, then in parlor with
the ladies until 8.45 - then
Mr. Hardenburgh & Misses Van Horn
Van Cleef, Jacobs & Geo. came
in & made me a [illegible] - a little
before 10 I ret'd to their hotel with
them - back soon aftr 10 - a chat
with W. Roberts & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 10. 1879.

Delightful wf. after klfo - as I
had a sort of headache I walked
down to Good rich falls with Misses
Lightner & Woodbury - there we were
overtaken by Geo. Trickey & caragon, the
girls went on with him & I returned
to the house. Got out my oil traps
& went into the orchard back of the
house & charcoaled sketch - then
in & had a short rest before dinner
at 1 - After that got out my Bruce
material & planned the first [illegible];
wrote a letter to Anna C. and at
5.45 walked around the nice square
tea soon after 6 - Overcast with a
light rain. Played Poker for an hour
- at 8.30 went to the Thom W. House
& played Whist with Mr. H. & Misses Van
Cleef & Vanthorn until 10.15 - then again
to Trickey's & chatted until 11 with
W. Roberts & to bed about 11.30.
