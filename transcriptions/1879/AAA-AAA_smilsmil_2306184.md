## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 6. 1879.

Very warm — got to studio at 9.20 + to work on small W.C.
Sketches — worked over the different ones until about
1 o'clk + then to lunch — At
2 Mr. Koehler called + we
went to Auguste Belmont's to
see his Diaz that he wishes me
to etch — a very fine picture
but as I cannot work directly
from it, I'm afraid to undertake
it — At 3 went to 109 Jane S-
to see J. A. Ward + his Heroic
Equestrian Statue of Genl. Thomas
that is now ready for the founder.
Grand thing — back to studio at
5 — Tea at 7 — Every mounting
small drawings until after
10 — to 37° S- at 11 + to bed

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 7. 1879.

Very pleasant day — got to studio
at 9.30 + to work finishing the
preparation of my small water
color sketches for the Union League —
Mrs. Wm. Dart + her niece called —
+ I made a small W.C. Sketch
to show them the process, which
resulted in one of the best things
I have done. Gifford came in —
At 2 I went out to lunch + back
+ spent the p.m. finishing the
7×11 Sketch "Long Lake". Tea
at 4 — at 8 called at Colman's
— out — then went to Moore's the
Art Gallery — Back to studio at
9 + mounted Sketches — wrote
a note to Sellstedt of Buffalo.
There to 34° S. + to bed about 11.
