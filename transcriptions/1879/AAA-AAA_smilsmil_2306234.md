## Left Page

### August, Saturday 16. 1879.

Up late — dull, heavy morning &
cold. Later in the day a steady
rain from N.E. Ther. at 3 p.m.
58°. Bkfs. at 8.30 — out with Geo.
wandering until driven in
by the rain, then got to work
on A.B.N. Co. drawing. S² Am
Bridge — until 12 — then a rest
with a little more work before
dinner at 2 o'clk. — after that
on piazza for awhile with Mr.
& Mrs. Wyman — then found a Trib.
got by a stove & read awhile —
after 4 again to work on drawing
until 5.30. Then out for some
exercise — Bowled with Wyman
— tea about 7 — walked on piazza
then to dancing room where
the band was playing & watched
the dancing & playing of games —
blind man's buff — fox & goose
etc until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 17. 1879.

Another cold, cheerless day.
Bkfs. about 9 — at 10 dressed
myself in big boots & Rubber
suit & started out for a prowl —
followed the shore & found half
a dozen old junipers & cedars
that interested me — good sea
& breakers & the "Blowing cave"
was in fine working order —
back to hotel at 12.45 — then
lay down & had a nap. Dinner
at 2 — after that on piazza —
reading news paper & writing
until 5.30 — then went over to
Rev. W. May's cottage — George
there & Miss Jacobs — very pleasantly
received by the Mays — stayed
to tea & rel² to hotel at 8.15.
Service in parlor Rev. W. McIlvaine
of Newark, N. J. — after that went
to my room & tried to write a
letter to Emma J. — then to Reading Room
& parlor, finally giving up & going
to bed about 11 o'clk.
