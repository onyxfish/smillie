## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 30. 1879.

Still charming weather. Very sore all over from pulling horses yesterday & having Falcon cold — also bothered with a headache. After bkfst walked for awhile in Eastman Park. Then to Harding's & ordered a change of gas to the old Co. for burning at home — then back & worked on Arms of New Mexico — much bothered by the palper. Dinner at 12.45, worked until 3.30. At 4 went to stable & got horse & buggy & took father out riding — Hooker Ave — & around Driving Park & on to Smith Road — then took Mother out for 20 m — & to stables at 6 — back to tea — Evng spent reading newspapers & chatting — to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 1. 1879.

Partly cloudy — fresh & cool. Bkfst at 7.30. After that walked to Judge Smott's house — being Rebushed through it — then by the "old farm" road home — Still very stiff from my exercise on Tues. & taking cold — got to work with watercos on little sketch, commenced in NY, a free rendering of a bit on Colgate's place at Riverdale. Finished it — also another — hard to describe — Sky — distance — trees & water — Dinner at 12.45 — Worked until after 4, then out & walked about until 6 — part of the time with Geo met father & went to Mary's with him — home to tea at 6.30. Evnt at 8, Geo & I called on Mr. Hull & Mr. Williams, there until 9.30, then home & to bed at 10.30.
