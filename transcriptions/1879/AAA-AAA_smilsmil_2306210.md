## Left Page

### June, Friday 27, 1879

A very stifler of heat —
Did not go out for walk
after bkfst — Sowork on
Saguenay block & after
a couple of hours, finished
it. Rec'd letters from J.W.
Wood, Will Kimball. &
Charl. enclosing samples
of Jap. paper — wrote to
Charl. Wm K. & Flora d. —
Dinner at 12.30 Mattie
spent the day with Mary.
About 4 o'clt. Uncle W. came
& took Father Geo & me out to
Hillside — Splendid thunder
storm — Mrs. Embury there —
After tea, Pumpelly & Ferguson
called — Ret'd to town about 8 —
spent the evef with Mary
— home a little before 10; sat
up until after 11, so that I
might get some sleep during
the night — then to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 28, 1879

Another very hot day — After
bkfst; wrote to Chas. Parsons.
Packed Saguenay block & went
to Exp. Co's office with it. Met
Prof. Ritter in town — then home
& commenced work on Osgood
block illustrating lines in Miles
Standish. Dinner between
12 & 1 — Storms all about us,
a dash of one early in the after
noon — About 5, Father, Mother,
Matt & Kate went down to call
on the Forbes. Atb, Jas. Winslows
train ran away in front of our house
with coachman, Miss Margaret W. &
[illegible] Ferguson. Miss W. jumped out
& was badly cut about the chin.
I brought her in — Dr Carter came &
dressed her wounds & at 8 she
Ret'd home. Then I took tea —
Heavy Rain soon after — another
thunderstorm north of us. Emma
B — came about 9 o'clt to stay
over night — About 10 I went
to bed with a bad headache.
