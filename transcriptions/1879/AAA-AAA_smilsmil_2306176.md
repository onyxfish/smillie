## Left Page

### April, Sunday 20, 1879

Glorious clear day — but rather thick inside of my head —
Bkfst. at 10 — at 11 to D. Hastings'
at 11.45 at St. Mulford's + with
him went to So. Brooklyn,
136 Pacific St. + took dinner
with the Jaquebins + Miss Mary
Morrison — left there at 4 —
got to studio's at 5 — Kindled
fire + wrote a letter to father
at 7 went in to Spring's +
at 8.30 to Sandford Gifford's —
spent the eve[ning] there until
10 — then to studio — wrote
awhile + at 11 to 37. St.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 21, 1879

Very warm + cloudless — After Bkfst.
at 9 o'clk. went to Anna C.'s
— from there to Rosalie Butler's
who with her sister Helen + Emma [illegible]
went to ferry foot of E. 34. St. —
There met Lib Howe, Geo. Cook,
Mr. Valentinue + Chas. Collins —
crossed to Hunter's Point + took
10 o'clk train to Woodbury, getting
there about 11.20 — At once went
into the woods + found quite
a lot of Arbutus — lunched at
[illegible] under some evergreens on
a pleasant hill-side — at 3.20
took return train, getting to
N.Y. about 5 — went to studio —
Tea after 7 — At 8.30, with Geo.
went to 4 E. 38. St. (Slade's) + bid
Ogilvie + wife good bye — They
go to Europee on Mrs. [illegible]. Then
to 116 E. 39. St. (Hunt's) — out — then
to 597 5th Ave. (Charle's) — out — out got
my ½ doz. of port — then to 37. St.
+ to bed soon after 10.
