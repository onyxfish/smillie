## Left Page

### May, Monday 12. 1879.

Overcast & threatening in the
mrf. but cleared to be a
very warm day — Got to studio
about 9.30, & to work on Hay Rick,
Stuck to it closely & got it
so far finished that I took
it off the stretcher — Had tea
about 7 & made ready for
the Etching Club. Meeting
not called to order until 9 o'clk
— beggarly show of fellows — I
was elected Pres'. Soon after
10 me adjourned to Pusch's for
beer — & at 11 o'clk. in 37.
S. & in bed. [illegible — ~8 words]

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 13. 1879.

Pleasant day & very warm —
9.45 when I got to studio — made
a note to Dr. Yale & did some
other little things but did not
get to work owing to callers
etc — After 2 when I went
to lunch — & from my return
until I left the studio, we
had a continuous run of callers
— so I did no work. At
6.30 went to the Kimbal's. Kate
there — had tea & later, taking
Kate Gde & I, went to Standard
Theatre & saw Burnand's "Cox
& Box" & the "Pinafore" — altho' very
warm it was enjoyable — after
taking Fanny & Kate home — then
to 37. S: & to bed shortly after
midnight.
