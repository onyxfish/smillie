## Left Page

### July, Sunday 13. 1879

Very warm day — mistemby
went to Baptist ch. Heard
Alonzo Parker preach about
putting away childish things
— then home & dinner at
1 — then Read Burroughs
& a little Embed. napping
in Rocking chair — then
writing — Tea after 6 —
Every. loafing — Read
a little & to bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 14. 1879

Very hot day — feeling much
under the weather — After a
walk to P.O. had to lie down
until pain in stomach subsided.
Laid a ground on Copper
plate to begin an Osgood
etching upon. Uncle W. and
During spent a good part of
the mff. experimenting in
printing with a water color ink.
Dinner at 12.30 — Afternoon
I made pencil sketches, ill.
Whittier's "Summer by the Lake Side"
& the "Old Burying Ground" — Traced
the first & transferred it to the
Copper. Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g
Prof. & Mrs. Ritter called and
spent the eve'g. I got to
bed after 11 o'clk.
