## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 25. 1879.

Another hot day after bkf: —
wrote notes to S. W. Wood & D.
Yale — then to P. O. then home
& commenced Saguenay block
for Harper's — worked until
dinner time — 12.30 — & from
2.30 until 5 getting a full
third of the block finished.
Then out & wandered
about until 6.30 — then in
to tea — At 8.30 went
up town & met Kate, 2d —
& Matt, then back home.
Earlier in the eveg I
had been to Mary's with
father & Geo— A. P. J. —
started for Lake George &
Palmyra this mng —
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 26. 1879.

Still hot — After bkf: a
walk — then to work on
Harperblock — Got central
drawing blocked in — a
short walk before dinner
— Dinner at 1 o clk — then
worked until 6 & got all
my block "laid in", needing
only some pencil finishing.
Then Pumpelly & Ferguson
called & I went Riding
with them south of town.
A heavy thunderstorm had
passed about 2 miles below
town & in our ride we got
into lots of mud & wet from
dripping trees — Returning
home at 8, found it dry &
hot as tinder — Took tea
& at 10 went to bed.
