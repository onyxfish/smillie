## Left Page

### December, Monday 8, 1879

Very pleasant day — Up late & did not get to studio until 10 o'clk. Went to work proving "Cedars by moonlight" — worked moderately until near 2 o'clk. Then Louis Tiffany came in & offered me his horse for the P.M. — at once made arrangements with Coleman to go with him — went to dinner — then to 37' 8? and changed clothes then to 62.
S'. at 3.40 met C — me rode in the Park until 4.30 — at 5 I was at 37' S? & took a Rub down — at studio's at 6.30 & had tea — Etching Club at 8 — & until near 11 — then to 37' S'. & to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 9, 1879

Charming miff — overcast afternoon — got to studio at 9.45 after having gone to [illegible] on an errand. — Again at work on my proving, & seeing the effects of my ride yesterday I worked slowly. — Dinner at 2 — then for awhile at the [illegible] P.D. Proved until 4.30 — then Rested — Tea at 6.30 — [illegible] studio writing & fixing up 9.40 37' S'. about 11 & right to bed.
