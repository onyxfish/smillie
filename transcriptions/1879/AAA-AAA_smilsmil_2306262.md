## Left Page

### October, Saturday 11, 1879

Heavily overcast with fog, everything
dripping wet. B&b: at 8 — then to
studio & gave it a final looking up
then down town & about, bidding
goodbye. Dinner at 12. & at
12.45 I got away on Montrose
Depot Stage — weather a little
lighter — Train at 2.45 — Ride
without incident except that
we passed through some spattering
rain about night fall. Just
as I put my key into the door
of No. 21 the clock struck 10 —
I got to bed very soon after.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 12, 1879

Still overcast, but yet no rain.
B&b: at Hotel Royal at 8.30 — after
that went to studio — from there
to Dr. Bellows' ch. & heard an
interesting discourse on conscience
with practical application to
our Indian Troubles & to coming
elections. He declares that the Indians
are victims of bad faith & I judged
that he is opposed to Senator Conkling's
machine power. Then to "Century"
& at 12.50 to 1 am. Colmais where
I had dinner. At 3 to my studio and
at 5 to R.V.'s — tea there & at 8 to Anna
Cook's — She is out of town but I spent
the eveff with her mother, bro. Geo. &
Ed. & Mrs. Ed. & Miss Nelch or Walsh —
until 10.15 — then to 37 St. & to bed
