## Left Page

### August, Monday 4, 1879

much cooler & overcast, grew thicker later in the day. Bkf.
at 8 — During the m'f. I wrote a letter to Anna C., listened to piano music by Mr. McKengie,
Miss Blake & Miss Lewist. Got my water colorbox ready for work & had a walk along a part of the beach. Dinner at 1 — much of the p.m. loafing until 4 when I ret'd. to hotel & again had playing on piano until near 6. Tea at 6.30
Charming sunset, followed by a few drops of rain — much of the ev'g. with Miss Blake on piazza & lobed som. after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 5, 1879

Very pleasant day & cooler — In the m'f. after bkf. had some more piano music — then got my W.C. traps ready went out & found a sketch spot under the willows, soon determined to make an oil study of it. Returned to my room & got out my oil traps. then back under the willows & spent the rest of the m'f. charcoaling and receiving callers. Dinner som. after 1 — After that loafed about until 4 when a party walked over to Rafe's Chasm — Glorious breezy headland. Worked closely for an hour & a half & got a W.C. sketch — back to tea about 7 — ev'g. G'p° & I went to Mr. Bray's & called on W.C. P. Crauch and family. The ev'g. passed very pleasantly until after 9 — I then walked down to the rocks & to bed soon after 10.
