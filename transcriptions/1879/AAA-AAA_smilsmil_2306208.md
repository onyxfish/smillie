## Left Page

### June, Monday 23, 1879

Clear, warm day — after bkfst
a walk. — Settled to work on
"Quebec" block after 10 — at
12.30 had dinner — after that
went to P.O. with letter written
to Nicoll. — back & to work at
2.30 — stuck closely to it until
6\. then tea — at 8.30 went
to Matt's for Kate & brought
her home som after 9 — To
bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 24, 1879

Very hot day. After bkfst at 8
wrote a note or two, then up
town — to work on Quebec
block soon after 10. Uncle
Willie came in about noon —
Dinner at 12.30 — At 1.30 went
to P.O. then to Manjos — met
there Gilbert P., his wife & boy.
at 2.30, Uncle W. came along
with the Canadian Paper he
had promised me, but it gave
me no material for introducing
Dufferin Terrace into my Harper
drawing. Home & to work at 3.
at 6 finished the block. — Tea
at 6.30. After that father & mother
went to St. Atwater's — I packed
my block & took it to Express
office — then home & lounged
about until 10 — then to bed.
