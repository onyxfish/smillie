## Left Page

### November, Friday 14, 1879

Very dark, overcast day with rain in the afternoon. Up early & had bkfo. before 8 —
then up to St. Jale's — there until after 10, then walked down to Aveny's & to work on pen sketch after Jan. [illegible] —
Lunched with the family at 1, then made sketches after Daubigne & Pallon [illegible] —
4.45 got to studio — went to dinner at 5.30 — after that, at lounge until 7.
30, went over to Cor. 36 S. to call on [illegible] & Mitchells —
all out, then back & spent an hour with Bellows. At
10 went to 34th St. feeling very much fagged & distressed by my dinner — to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 15, 1879

Sultry but otherwise pleasant. On getting up found that the old gastralgic pain had possession of my stomach — got about with troubles — eat a very light bkfo. then to studio — in big chair until 11 when I went to the N.A., got a painting by Boughton then to studio but had not strength to work — had a light lunch between 1 & 2 —
& did nothing during the P.M.
After 5 went to "Century" & had a light meal & at 8 went to Steinway Hall & heard Wallace Bruce lecture on Wm Cullen Bryant —
then back to Century — there until 11 — then to 34th St. & to bed at 12 feeling much better.
