## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 26, 1879

Pleasant day — got to studio before 10 feeling somewhat
shabby but grew better as the day advanced. Tried
to get started on A.B.N. Co. drawing but had callers &
was interested in little matters so that the day
passed without my having accomplished anything of
real importance. Dinner at 2 — at 5 went to 34th St.
& dressed — then to Studio & had tea & at 8 Geo. & I
went to Mrs. Woodbury's — No. 52, 13th St. Saw Mrs. W.,
her daughter Katie & Mrs. Wynk[oop].
A very pleasant eveg. until after 10 — Dr. Wynkoop came
in just as we were leaving —
Glorious moonlight night —
walked to 34th St. & to bed
about 11, [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 27, 1879

Thanksgiving Day — got to studio before 10 & tried
to get to work. Fussed around doing one little thing
& another until 1.30, then to the Abbatts — 5th Ave. near
34th St. & had dinner. After that sat up an on with
misses A. — Canning & Folger's
until near 4, then to studio —
then out for a walk and
back at 6.30 & had tea in
studio — after 8 Geo. & I
went in to the Colmans &
spent the eveg. there. — to
37th St. before 11 & right to bed.
