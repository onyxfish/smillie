## Left Page

### November, Thursday 6, 1879

Dined a fall of snow during the night — overcast day until the afternoons when it commenced raining quite heavily & continued during the evening. Got to studio soon after 10 — unpacked various files with etching materials — found all right except a glass acid tray, broken. At 12 went to Avery's by appointment and arranged with him to get right to work upon a series of about 40 pen sketches to'll [illegible] of sale of Sherwood & Hart pictures — then to Dewey's — then to studio & at 2 to lunch — after that to Knoedles — got to studio about 4 — Saw Miss [illegible] Horn, at 5 went to Dr. Yale's — He says I am completely run down — at [illegible] to Hotel Royal & had tea. Then to Renne's — at 8.15 to Kimball's. Matt Kate here — to 37 St. Soon after 10 to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 7, 1879

Beautiful mor. Blcks soon after 8 — got to studio at 9.30 & for some time engaged in getting ready to start my Avery job — wrote a short note to father. Then to work on Avery's catalogue — [illegible] heavily sent up a Stevens [illegible] & Madvazo. Interrupted by callers & other things so that got only the Madvazo partly finished — Soon after 2 went to [illegible] to with Joe & had my dinner — back to studio & worked until 4.30, near to went to Avery's with the paintings — as they are insured in his house they have to be set every evening. After [illegible] took tea in a Creamery under Daly's theatre — and at 8 got [illegible] went to Cunnys Cooks — to the Kimball's — [illegible] until 10 — thence to 37 St. & to bed.
