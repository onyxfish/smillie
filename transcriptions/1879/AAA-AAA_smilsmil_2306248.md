## Left Page

### September, Saturday 13, 1879

Pleasant mng. gradually thickening weather until
hf p.m. was quite dark and overcast with showers on the
mts N. & E. fus. After a short walk I went out to orchard
& commenced to paint upon the sketch — which I made
charcoal sketch the other day — wind & cold too much for me.
aft. walked around the mile square with Miss Lightener. Then
dinner — after that, painted in my room serving my pvp
work "together". Then walked twice around the "mile square"
(about 2½ ms) then tea at 5.30 —
Eveŋ. until nearŋ divided bet.
the gentlemen in the office and the ladies in the parlor — then to
Thorn Mt. Ho. & played whist until almost 11 — to bed soon after —
Showers in the afternoon — heavy rain at night.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 14, 1879

Very heavy rains until about 10
o'clk. A.M. Then clouds lifted until 4 P.M. then more rain. Bkft.
about 8.30 — about 10 I went to see
the falls, which were much swollen
& quite furious. From there I walked
on with Rad. Wyukoafs; a boy of 12,
around the five miles square — I
sauntered & enjoyed the changing
color of the foliage and some very
fine cloud effects — the gloom of the
clouds & the heavy turnpee over the
mts. In just at dinner time. 1 ocl.
after that in parlor for some time
reading newspaper — then washed
up brushes & had a nap — at 4.30
more rain. Wrote letters to father
& Howard Welmunt — S. Sons fair.
Tea at 6 — Eveŋ. divided bet.
Thorn Mt. & Jackson Falls Ho. — to bed
at 11.
