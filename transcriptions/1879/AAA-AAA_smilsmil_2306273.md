## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 4, 1879

Overcast mf, clearing about 10.
Aftr blcfo. I picked up & packed.
Got every thing finished by
noon, then went to Syfo. and
had dinner at 12.30. About
1 Father arrived from N.Y.
After dinner I went up town
to Matt's with Kate — then to
Mary's — back home about 4.
Had a dish of wheaten grits —
& left home at 4.20 & took 4.50
train to N.Y. getting in at 7 —
Had tea at Hotel Royal. then
found that Syfts. man had
got more No. of house 91 instead
of 21 & had to go back to [illegible] depot
then went to Kimball's at 9
& soon after to 37th St., to bed
at 11 & at 11.20 my trunks
came.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 5, 1879

Very pleasant mf but
later in the day it was overcast
& by 11 at night snow fell.
Bkfst at 8.30 — after that, for some
time unpacking trunk, then
to studio & at 2 went to lunch
with Geo. then downtown to
Photo. Eng. Co., to Green's printing
office, then to Chase's where I
waited 35 m. without seeing
him, then to Willes & at 5.30
back to studio. To tea with
Geo. at 4. & about 8 me went
to Moore's opening of his fall
exhibits — saw a number of "the
fellows" there & spent a pleas-
ant evening until 10.30, then to
37th St. & to bed soon after 11.
