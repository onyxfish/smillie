## Left Page

### March, Friday 21, 1879

Overcast wtf, but pleasant later in the day — Got to
studio at 9.30 & to work on
14×18, The Top of the Hill — I
got pretty well over it when
Swain Gifford came at 12.30
& with him I went to Coalsen
Union to see Jat. McDonough —
not there — me went down town
getting lunch in Fulton St.
then to A.B.N. Co — Saw Mr. &
with him went to 53 B°way
& saw a lot of plate presses.
Made arrangements to buy
14 for the Etching club at $25.
then to R. Hoe & Co — to see what
it would cost to set them up
in the different studios and
at 4.30 got to studio — Finished
my days work — tea at 4.30 — at
8.30 went to Sturvevant Ho — to
see Faw. Connell — not in — then
to San Francisco Minstrels & saw
H.M.S. Mud Scow — funny — to
37' 82 & to bed at 0. 45.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 22, 1879

Lovely wtf, but before noon
it began to rain & at night
it was a deluge. Got to Studio
between 9.30 & 10 & spent much
of the wtf clearing about &
getting ready to start on the
last of the Lake Gel sketches
for Harper — but don't feel
the least bit in the world
like work — Nearly 3 when I
went out to lunch — Spent
some of P.M. charcoaling in
a subject "A Lake Gel Landing."
At 6.15 went to Emma J's &
took her to Jannif K.'s where
we had tea about 7 — at 8 me
went to Miss Ireland's — 30 W
37's — left the Girls there & for
a little while I was at 21 —
then to Sturvevant Ho — & with
Faw until 10 — then to 30 W 37 &
there for an hour listening to the
rehearsal of some piano music
when as it poured in torrents
Mrs. D. ordered a carriage — The
Girls & Will K. were sent home
in it & I went to 21 & to bed
ab 11.30
