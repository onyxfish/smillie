## Left Page

### July, Thursday 31. 1879.

*Hesperus House.*
*Magnolia, Mass.*

Roused by the landing of the boat at Kate River shortly after 5 — Had slept but little & could not get to sleep again — then up & had blkfst — at 6 — & at 6.50 took train to Boston, getting there at 9 — Took hack, went to Am. Xp. Co, then to Eastern R.R. depot, then to Estes & Lauriat — Mr. Koehler not in — left name & went to Houghton & Osgood's — Saw O — Told me that Arthur was expected at 1.45 — again to Estes & d. not K — but saw E — then out went to off of Equitable Bldg & in to Old South — at 11.45 to N.D. & Co. Saw A.N.S.A. & O. & had a long talk over etching matters — Shortly before 1 — again to E. & S — K. there — about 2 we went to Parker's & had lunch — after that we walked over the Common to Trinity Ch — went in — finally left K. at 4.20 & took car to Eastern R.R. & 5 train to Magnolia — 2 miles ride to Hesperus Ho. which arrived after 6 — near 7 — Found Geo. Joskin for me — also Joslyn man — had tea — then sauntered out to a point of Rocks to see the moon rise & watch the tide — C.P. Cranch with us — we wandered about, going to Hunt's cottage, until 10, then to our Room & to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 1. 1879.

Got up with a headache — Blkfst at 7.30 — after that, spent a little time getting some things out of my trunk — then to some rocks at the waterside, there for about an hour, then gave up & went to my room & to bed — did not get up until 6 — Rec'd letters forwarded from home, from E.N.S., Anna C. & Koehler. Tea about 7 — then to the Rocks & watched the tide & moonlight until 10.30 — then to bed about 11.
