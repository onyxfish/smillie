## Left Page

### March, Friday 7, 1879

Charming day — At 9.30 at Mr.
Spencer's & spent the morf on
pen sketches after Cabanel &
Meree. To studio at 1.30 — lunch
at 2 — back to B's at 2.45 &
worked until 5.45 on sketches
after Lefebvre & Gerome — then
walked down to studio — very
tired — head & eyes very much
fried — Rested in big chair
until 4 — Tea at 4.30 — mate
until 9 — then Press View of
All Artists exhbn at Hentz Galery
— there until 10.30 — then to
37 S. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 8, 1879

Beautiful day — Got to Spencer's
at 9.20 & spent most of the mfe
on Pasini's picture — troubled
with a boring headache so that
at 12.30 I gave up & went to
studio — lunch at 1.30 — & soon
after 2 went to Avery's with the
sketches I had already done.
He presses me to work more & I ves
but I am anxious to get at my
Academy work. From Avery's
went to Photo enf Co. 61 Park
Place — left sketches with Moss —
Back to studio at 4.30, found
Mr. Forbes there — my headache
increasing — got a little nap on the
Lounge — at 6.30 went to Kimball's to
tea — Matt & Kate there — all went to
Phil. concert — when we came out,
sleety rain — I Ran to Century, borrowed
two umbrellas & got girls into [illegible] car
with Will K. — I left them at Cor. 4 avct 23.
St & Ran to studio, got another umbrella &
overshoes & then to [illegible] — Paid to Century
with borrowed umbrella's then walked
to 37 S. where I am? about mid nighty
wet & tired — Rain freezing as I fell —
to bed at once.
