## Left Page

### September, Sunday 7, 1879

Pleasant mng., overcast afternoon.
Spent a good part of the early
mng. in piazza with Messrs
Roberts, Sawlee, Harvey & Burham
sunning myself & chatting. Then
to my room with a rheumatic
sort of headache & wrote to Emma
& sister Kate. Before dinner
at 1, walked around the "mile
square" with Mr Roberts, then
again at letter writing until
3.30 when Geo. came for me &
I went with him, Mrs Hardenbrough,
Moses, & Miss Van Cleef (Millie Lent's
step daughter) walking through the
flume. Back at 6 & had tea.
Singing in the parlor until 8.30
then I went to Thom Mt. House.
There until 10.30 — back & had a
short session on the stairs with
the young people, then to bed
at a little after 11.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 8, 1879

Thunderstorm during the night.
Heavy rain in the mng. until after
9 — then cloudy & clearing. Worked
during the mng. on A.B.N. Co's drawing —
a short walk before dinner at 1.30
Then a little recess in the parlor
& again at work before 3 & until
5. Then out for a walk with
George, chased in by a gusty
shower that gave us some very
fine effects — tea near 7. —
Eveng. spent in parlor with
Misses Lightener & Wood burying —
Geo. came over for a little
while. To bed at 11.
