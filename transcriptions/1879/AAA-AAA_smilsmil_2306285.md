## Left Page

### November, Friday 28, 1879

Dark day with rain much of the time — got to studio at 9.30 + to work on A.B.M. Co. drawing "View of Deadwood". At 2 went out to dinner + had a short walk — very dark afternoon so that I could not do much work. Tea at 6.30 + at 9.30 went to Booth's theatre + heard "La Belle Helean" — it is 11 years since I last heard it + I was disappointed — "Pinafore" seems now to me preferable. To bed after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 29, 1879

Overcast day, clearing [illegible] charmingly at night — got to studio at 10 + to work on drawing of Deadwood which I got finished about 2 o'clk then out to dinner — the rest of the p.m. I was mounting etchings + finishing up little chores. At 5 went to Avery's + saw pfo of sketches for the Sherwood Catalogue — Back to studio at 6.30 + had tea — cleaning up until 8.40 then to Mr. Wyckoff's — 5 E. 13t — to arrange for Art Student's League on Tues. eve'g. There until 10 o'clk then to Century — there until 11 — walked up to studio with Nicoll + to 34th St. at midnight — to bed.
