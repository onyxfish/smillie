## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 8, 1879

overcast day — not so cold as yes-
terday — Got to studio about 9.40
had a number of calls so that
I really did not get to work
until 12 o'clk — Mr G.W. Adams &
Utica called — I finally got at
little upright twilight sketch &
worked for an hour — then out to
dinner — back & worked until
about 4.30 — Miss James called —
At 5.30 went to Gauthier's — then
to Saintes — Got to studio near
7 — found Chase there — had
tea & [illegible] spent mounting
large Japan paper etchings and
water color sketches — to 39°
S- & to bed at 11.45 — miserable
slushy rain — half snow —

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 9, 1879

Horrible day of slush & rain — Got
to studio a little before 10 — worked
on small water color sketches
until 1 o'clk — then Mr Jas.
Nord came — & a van after Miss
Armstrong — I went to lunch
at 1.30 — P.M. — Spent entirely
with Pupils — At 6 went
to Anna C's to tea & at 8
escorted her to her singing
club & Miss Arnold's — then
to Remeis & ordered some
frames — then to Dickee's
riding school & saw Chase
& his class — at 9.30 to 37°
S- bright moonlight — cold
wind — to bed at 10 —
