## Left Page

### April, Sunday 6, 1879

Very pleasant day — Bkf[a]st at
9.30 — at 11 to Dr Hastings &
heard him preach from Prov. 25:
2° v — after service, to studio
then, at 1.30 — to Miss Abbattis —
Had dinner there — at 3 Re[?]
to studio — mat[te] to father —
then walked up to M[r]s Pantoni
135 W 56' S. — & paid her $16 [illegible]
for W.C. paper — at near 6 started
to walk across to Will's but felt
so sick that I got into a car
& rode down to studio — on
lounge until 8.30 — then had
a call — about studio until
10.30 — then to 37' S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 7, 1879

Miserable, raining morning —
got to studio about 9.30 &
finished block for Harper's
drawing of "Adirondacks" now
in N.A. exbn. — Lunch at
2.30. After that made a
slight pencil sketch; "Camps
Manhattan" for Harpers —
tea at 6.30 — at 7.30 Calverley
came & at 8 I went with him
to Renner & Co — & ordered a
frame for a little Boughton
picture he has. Then called
on Kate Hillard — not in, then
to Coleman's — there until
10.30 — then a few minutes
in studio & to 37' S. & to
bed at about 11.30. [illegible — ~4 lines]
