## Left Page

### March, Saturday 1. 1879.

Tolerably pleasant, altho' over-
cast most of the day — Got to Studio
soon after feeling very unlike
work but drove myself to
attacking a little sketch in
blk. & white for jungling the sugram
— did not make much out of it —
altho' I tried hard enough.
Lunch at 2 — Tea in Studio
at 7 — wrote out a little statement
concerning W.C. affairs for Van Cleaf
of the Herald. — at 9 o'clk. — to W.C. —
[illegible] — before 10 to Cantuny, there
until 11.20 — walked up with
T.W. Wood & Nicoll. — To 37' St. —
& to bed just at midnight.

### March, Sunday 2. 1879.

In bed all day with a
painful nervous headache —
got up about 9 o'clk. — p.m. —
& before 10 went to St. Cloud
Hotel — there with father,
Mother & Kate & Matt — until
near 11 — then to 37' St. — & to
bed a little before 12 —
