## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 6, 1879

Very hot & very breezy —
About 9.30 got to my work
under the willows — found
a pic-nic party there that
disturbed me very much.
The wind also was very troublesome. Miss Mary McLane
came to me while at my work.
Dinner at 1. In the afternoon
I fussed awhile over morning
study — feeling very unfit to
work. wandered over rocks
on shore & had tea at [illegible] — again
out on the rocks with the Savins
until 9 o'clk — then to hotel & until
after 10 with Mr. McKenzie & Miss
Blake — playing on piano — Rec'd
from Estes & Lauriat check for $210.
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 7, 1879

Very pleasant day — About 10 o'clk
got to work under the willows
on oil study & stuck to it until
5 o'clk — getting in nicely a part
of foreground willows — then to
dinner — After that worked for
a couple of hours in A.B.N. Co.
drawing of Fulton Ferry suspension
Bridge & elevated R.R. depot.
At 4 o'clk, went to cottage
where Mrs. Town is — with
her, her daughter Helen & Miss
Shaw we went up on a spur
of rocks & read Signor Nom-
aldini's niece until & then
to tea, after that on piazza
of the Shaw's house & lots of
[illegible] — until & — then to Parlor of Hotel
where Mr. Knapp gave a lecture
on Medieval art with stereos.
[illegible] — more piazza and
moonside — again into parlors
Mrs. McKenzie & Miss Blake playing —
to bed about 11.
