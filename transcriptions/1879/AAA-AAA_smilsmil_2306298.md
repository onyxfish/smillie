## Left Page

### December, Friday 26, 1879

Beautiful day, but very cold. Charl went to N.Y. in the mt.
After my mt. walk I got to work with the pen on sketch
after Corot belonging to Gibson
of Phila — one of a series of
six that I am to do for
Koshler — after photos from the
originals. A short walk, then dinner at 1, finished
sketch — at 4.30 built a
fire in back parlor gratitude.
At 5.30 went to Matts for
Kate — Tea at 7 — Every
at home enjoying big fire.

Uncle M. & Aunt A. called in
the afternoon.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 27, 1879

Another beautiful, cold day —
Ofc. at 8.30 — after that, a walk
& shortly before 11 got to work
with pen on Baldini sketch.
At 12.45 went out for a short
walk — dinner at 1 — worked
until 3 — finished Baedini
& commenced outlining tracing
of Kaulbach. At 4.15 went out
with father — had a walk to
top of Mutt's hill — then to
many's & home at 6 — Tea
at 6.30 — Every in parlor
enjoying big fire — To bed
at 11.

News rec'd by telegraph
of death by drowning at Concord
Mass — of C. H. S. Williams.
