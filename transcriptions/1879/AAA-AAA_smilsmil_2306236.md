## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 20, 1879

Cloudless & very mild. After bkfs. bowled two strings alone then got to my work on A.B. N.Co drawing & worked until near 6 clk. Then out & took a walk around by Blowing cave — there a short rest & dinner soon after 2 — again at drawing & finished it about 5 — then out to Carpenteis & got boards then back & packed. The two drawings to go by Exps. Wrote letters for Macdonough & Will — Finished ups by 9 clk. Then had tea — letters from Nicoll, Colman & Miss Abbott. Well wandering up & down Piazza — lovely effects over beach & low tide — wet sand reflecting sun-set — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 21, 1879

A very mild milky day. After bkfs. went to Mr. May's with geo — understood that he was to go with us on a trip up the Kennebunk River — mistaken — so I left geo to go alone with Miss J. — while I went off to the junipers and worked until 12.30 where I finished the sketch. Then wandered until after 1. Then to hotel — dinner at 2.15 & at 3.30 off again, altho' Rain was promised — started another sketch of junipers but in less than an hour the Rain caught me — worked as long as I could but before I had to give up — then more wandering & got to hotel about 6 — Rain stopped. Tea at 7 — a little gleam of light in the west. Well pacing Piazza until 10 — then to bed.
