## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 23, 1879

Overcast & muggy. After bkfst. wrote to Juengling,
George & Bishop Putnam —
then to P.O. with letters —
& at about 11 to work on my
printing. Finished proving
Osgood plate & commenced
on "Up the Hill" — Dinner
as usual at 12.30 — worked
at intervals all the afternoon
until tea time — then washed
up & had tea — Every very
tired & sat still until 10.30
when I went to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 24, 1879

Delicious day — but still feel
very shaky. After bkfst. wrote
letters to Juengling, Bishop
Putnam — then up to P.O. &
back to my printing — finished
the paper I had ever for "Up
the Hill" — then commenced
proving the Jacque etching —
Dinner at 12.30 — I worked
very cautiously, lying down
now & then — Gave up at 4 —
& at 4.30 went out riding
with Father — Had a delightful
drive around by Manchester.
Back at 6.30 very much in-
vigorated — Read about ½ —
Every in Rocking chair —
Matt & R[illegible]. at the house until
after 9 — to bed at 11.
