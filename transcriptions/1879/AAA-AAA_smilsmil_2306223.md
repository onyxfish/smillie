## Left Page

### July, Friday 25, 1879

Rather warm & close. After [illegible] —
made letters to Anthony & Will —
made up [illegible] of plates & [illegible] for
Osgood Etching — & pen sketch
of drawing for Will — then to P.O. —
& P.O. Office — Back & proving my
Jacque plates before 11 — Dinner
at 12.30 — again proving until
4 — found that I had to stick
closely to my work to do three
[illegible] in an hour — Overcast
& threatening Rain but at
4.30 I drove with Father down
to Barnegat — caught in the
Rain but as we were prepared
me did not mind it — Tea at
a little before 7 — Kate at Hill
Side. Evg. made a note to
Juengeling — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 26, 1879

Cool, overcast [illegible]. Rain
commenced falling before noon &
continued, like a steady [illegible].
Rain well into the night. After
[illegible] went out for a walk, then
in & commenced my printing —
finished Jacque & took up Jeff.
5. Took a rest about 11 & dinner
at 12.30 — At noon Rec'd. Paper
from Koehler & at 4 took an imp
each plate — worked until 6.
then cleaned up for the season,
very tired — After tea spent
the eveg. Resting until about
10.30 — then to bed.
