## Left Page

### August, Saturday 30, 1879

Crawford Notch Ho, White Mts
Room

Charming mng. Up before 6. Bkfst
at 6.45 & at 7.30, Emma, Kitty,
Messrs Hudson & Green, Geo & I got
away on the "Steambr." "Mt. Desert"!
Enjoyed the sail to Rockland
me, very much, getting there about
12. Dinner on the boat at 11.30,
cars on the wharf — off for Portland
a long, uninteresting ride. Stopped
at 5.40 just outside of P., at Boston
& Maine transfer depot — half an
hour for tea — & at 6.12 away on
Portland & Ogdenburgh R.R. for
Crawford Notch — glorious moon-
light night. Messrs Hudson & Green
went on to Boston — at Bartlett
"Observation car" added, & we had
a grand moonlight view of the
notch — got to Crawford's at 10.15
& after a little delay got to
bed about 11. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 31, 1879

Delicious day. After a good
nights rest had bkfst — at 9 o'clk
About 10.30 walked down through
the gate of the Notch & sat under
the trees — wandered about until
2 — then back & had dinner —
It was after 4 when we all
got off for a walk up Mt. Willard
it was after 7 when we got
back, having had an exceedingly
pleasant walk. Hitchcock's
Flume pleased us very much.
It was quite 8 when we had
our tea. Andrew H. Green saw
our names on the Record & sent
his card. We had a pleasant
chat with him — about 9 me
walked down the R.R. & enjoyed
some fine views — back at 10 —
wrote awhile & to bed at 11.
