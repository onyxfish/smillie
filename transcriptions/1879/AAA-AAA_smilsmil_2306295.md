## Left Page

### December, Thursday 18. 1879

Charming day. Got to studio at 9.30 — Spent the m'g making
a sketch in lith. crayon on egg
shell paper of a bit a little
Sister Island, Niagara — for
Reuce's series of Artists sketches.
To dinner at 2 — a short walk
then to studio & began planning
my large drawing — Kennebunk-
port cedars — Wrote to Judge
Hale of E. Forum & sent him
check for $113.87 — Payment
for my lot in Teenie Valley —
at 6 went to 37' St. changed
dress & then to Anna C's —
After tea took her & Fanny K. to
the Arnold's — then to C.J. Smith
25 W. 4t' St. — not in — then to Char[les]
where I spent the rest of the
eve'g with him. Many tree
tgro. To 37' St. & to bed at
10.45.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 19. 1879

Heavy wet snow. Got to studio
a little before 10 — Stretched brown
paper for large sketch for W.C.
Kennebunkport cedars — &
pieced stretcher for the drawing
30×28½. Lunch at 1 and
at 2 went to Phil. Rehearsal
at Academy of music — Back
to studio about 4.30 — Tea
at 6.30 — At 7.30 went into
Colman's & walked up to
Anna Cook's with him and
Mrs. C. — Playing on two
pianos by Mr. C — Anna C —
Emma J. & Fanny K. —
To 34' St. & to bed. Soon after
11. Miserable sleepy night
