## Left Page

### July, Thursday 17, 1879

A charming day after the
storm — cool & fresh — at
9.20, Prof. & Mrs. Retter came
for me & we drove to the ferry
just missed 9.35 boat — took
10 o'clk. one. crossed the river
& rode north 6 or 7 miles to
house of Jno. Bemmighs. Were
pleasantly greeted by him
& his wife — gotten about
noon — it was near 2 when
again we started on & went
about 2 miles, part of the way
over a wood road to a water
fall where we all lunched —
J.B. & wife & baby — Prof R. & wife
& self the party — near 5 when
we ret. to the house — at 6.15
we started homewards — I had
fought all day agst. a headache
it now closed down upon me
& my ride home was wretched
beyond description — got in a
little before 8 & went right to
bed — After midnight got up and
undressed myself & went regularly
to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 18, 1879

Another charming day. After
b'kfst., a short walk — then
at my etching — finished the
biting — Uncle W. & During
again printing — Dinner
near 1 — after that cleaned
out Water Tank & at 3 o'clk
at work on my plate — took
a pf. — not satisfactory —
then laid rebiting ground
& until after 6 o'clk. varnishing
& stopping out — then tea.
Every. Mr. Loomis called &
we walked out together —
I ret. at 8.30 — Matt & Ed.
at the house. I got to bed
at about 10. [illegible — entire passage]
