## Left Page

### April, Friday 4, 1879

Still windy weather + cold. Got to studio at 9.30 + to work on India ink sketch "Dome Island, Lake George" — worked until 1 — had a light lunch in studio + at 2 at Phil. Rd. an exceedingly enjoyable one. After it, walked up to studio with Anna C. — + worked while day light lasted on drawings + figure in "The top of the Hill". Tea at 6.30 + at 7.30 at Herby's took Mary to Park Theatre + had a good laugh over "Engaged" Got to 37th St at 11 + right to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 5, 1879

Clean but still windy + cold. Got to studio about 9.30 — commenced sketch on block for Harper's weekly my 33 × 44 "Adirondacks" — At 12 go next to Harpers with two Lake Geo. drawings — back at 12.45 — went to Kurtz Gallery to see the Matthews collection — then to N.A. lunch at 1.45 + then again to work on block — Kept at it until near 6 — At 6.30 went to Kimball's to tea — then to Philharmonic — last concert — enjoyed it very much — walked to Maillard's with Will K. Fanny + Lib Howe, — then to Century — Monthly meeting — there until near 12 — then to 37th St + to bed at 12.30
