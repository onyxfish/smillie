## Left Page

### October, Monday 27, 1879

Pleasant mf., overcast P.m. After hlp. fussed about for awhile getting table ready to work at my etching & at 10 went up town to Bank & drew money on Bruce's check then back & got to work finishing up some of the [illegible] ph. that I took last July & on plate "the Farm Lane" taking out all the distance & sky on R.H. end (ph.) Dinner at 12.45 — Again at work on ph. & plate. Took ph. of "Farm Lane" & Jefferson S. No. 8 then up town & tea at home at 6. Wef., suffering so much from neuralgia in face that I went up town & bought some chloroform — got temporary relief — to bed at 10.30 — again disturbed at night by pain in face.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 28, 1879

Overcast day with some good honest rain between 12 & 2 — then it tried hard to clear. Hlp. at 8.30 — after that worked for awhile with my ph. then up town to P.O. back & worked at laying grounds & finishing ph. — until near 1, then dinner. Rebit little plate, No. 8 & between 5 & 6 walked for exercise about the streets — Tea some after 6 — Wef. Father & [illegible] went to Literary Club meeting to hear read an essay on Tasso. I stayed at home suffering considerably from my neuralgia — to bed at 10.30 & passed a purgatorial night.
