## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 10, 1879

Dark, threatening day, but no rain — Got to studio at 9.30 & spent the mng proving "moonlight" — finished before I went out to dinner at 2 — the afternoon was spent mounting etchings pfs. for framing — Tea at 6.45 — & went. Go went to Wash. Wilson's but I strayed at studio until 10.30 mounting etchings & fixing them in their frames then to 37th St. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 11, 1879

Dark, dismal and raining — Got to studio some after 9 — laid a ground, traced "da Vinci" — transferred it & commenced etching — at it until after 1 o'clk — Robbins came in & invited me to dinner at his house this eveg. Go went away. Go & I went out to lunch a little before — I could not see to etch after 4 o'clk — had had my plate in the acid twice — at 4.30 went to 37th St., dressed, & at 6 at Horace Robbins' — 137 E. 60th St. — Dinner at 7 — Miss Phelps, Mr. and Mrs. Sanford Gifford — the [illegible] hostess — very charming — Spent the evng until 10.45 — then walked to 37th St. & to bed at 11.30.
