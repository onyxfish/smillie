## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 29, 1879

Very warm day — to office by 8.25 train — Rode down
with Pumpelly — went to
21 W. 37th St & arranged to stay
there overnights — then to the
barbers — then to Putnam's
book store & had a long chat
with Bishop B— then to
Ashland He. had my dinner
then to studio. At 2 went
down town to A.B.N. Co. & got
a lot of work — then up town
again, stopping at Brooks Bro.
& buying a duster — Teleg'd
to Father to come on & go to Boston
with me. Sat in Mad. Sq. from
6 to 9 — then to Ashland He. &
had tea. Then called at Winston
Springs — Saw Julie. P. is sick —
then to Gilmore's Garden.
There until 10.30 — then to
37th S. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 30, 1879

Muggy & overcast — at 8.30 had
bkft. at Hotel Royal — then to
Rennie's — wrote a note home,
got to studio at 10.45 — found
telegrams from Father & Geo —
finished up my business in the
studio as soon as possible & then
went to A.B.N. Co. made arrange-
ments to do another drawing — went
to Chase's & there for some time —
about 2, had lunch — again at
A.B.N. Co. until after 4, then to
foot of Murray St. & took possession
of my stateroom — a blistering sort
of dog-day — got away at 5.30 —
thick & hazy up the East River &
into the sound — but about 9 o'clk
it cleared into a charming moon-
light night — enjoyed the trip
exceedingly — had a light supper
about 8.30 & after lounging about
until 10.30 or 11, went to bed.
