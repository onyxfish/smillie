## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 22, 1879

Still clear & warm — Got to studio soon after 9 — feeling very "springy" & unlike work —
Set palette — Smeared over 11×16 "Yosemite Fall & Merced River" & Repainted it out to lunch at 2 — Painted until after 6 when I finished it — Tea at 7 — After 8. Geo. & I went to the "Bella" 48 E. 26", & called on Mrs. Mitchell — [illegible] her husband's bro. being very sick — There in to Louis Tiffany's —
Pleasant everywhere until near 10.30 — then went to studio — fixed things for the night — then had a glass of beer & went to 37. S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 23, 1879

Overcast, cooler & breezy — Got to studio about 9.30 & to work on 12×18 " moonlight & camels fine " — Painted persistently altho' I felt so unlike work that I could not accomplish anything. After 2 when I went out to lunch. At 5 went to 53rd S. & Mad. Ave. Ch. & saw the double wedding of J.S. Hartley & Rosa Innes & Geo. Innes Junr. & Miss Smith — After that walked home with the Hertzels. Then to studio & had tea after 7 — At 8.30, with Geo., called on Mrs. Desk — H. J. 2, 34 S. — out — then at Abner Colgate — 50 E. 34. — W. & Mrs. C. in — had a very pleasant call — did not get away until 10.45 — Then to 37. S. & right to bed.
