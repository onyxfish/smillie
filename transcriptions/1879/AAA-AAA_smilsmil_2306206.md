## Left Page

### June, Thursday 19. 1879.

Still beautiful weather. Spent the m'g in Studio getting things into order & packing such things as I am to take away with me — J. W. Wood called & so did J. M. Falconer — I laid a ground for him — Had lunch about 2 — worked until 4 — then as things were about finished I went out to do some shopping. Bought a Rubber coat, under clothes etc etc — Paid Studio Rent up to Sept 1st — then to Kimball's — Fanny & Willi out — Took tea there — after't went to Studio, started out with a lot of pkgs — & at 8.30 called on the Darts at 144 Park Ave — there until 10 o'clk — then to 37 5th & to bed before 11.
Called on Dr. Marcy abt. 30 and got Mr. Cassard's check for $60.00. Pay't for "Raquette Lake from Owl's Head"

## Right Page

### June, Friday 20. 1879.

Glorious day, cool and clear. After 6[illegible] went to Rennie's, then to Dr. Daroch's — 54th S — He examined my teeth — all right — then to Lifts Co — & to 37 S. To work loading trunk & valise — finished at 1.30 — then rode to 70th S — & left foot of printing ink at 51 for Wm M. Chase. Then to 337 Fourth Ave — had lunch & then bid my good byes and at 3.30 started for Grand Central Depot — then to Po'k — by 4 P.m. train — Home at about 7 — tea at once — Evelf Matt & Ed. came — At work unpacking what I had been most of the morning packing — to bed soon after 10.
