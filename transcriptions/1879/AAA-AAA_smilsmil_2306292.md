## Left Page

### December, Friday 12, 1879

Beautiful day — clear and crisp. Got to studio at 9.30
& at once to work on etching of da Vinci's head — finished
the etching & biting — took a proof. & laid a rebiting ground
& talked it out before 1.30 —
Mrs. Hy Bouton called — also Miss Jessie Bigelow — Dinner
at 2 — After that — to Y.M.C.A. & called on Ferguson &
Robbins — then again to work & at 4.30 had finished rebiting — Walked to Renner's
& back & had tea at 9. At work with my etchings until
near 9 — then to Kimball's & there until after 10, then
to 37th S. & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 13, 1879

Fine day. Up late & got to studio at 9.45 — to work on "da
Vinci" — a call from Mr. The[illegible]
[illegible] & then one from S. R.
Koehler — then at 2.15 — to
dinner — Afternoon, worked on etching as long as I could
see but did not finish it as I had hoped to. at 5.30
went to Renner's with etching.
Tea at studio at 9.30 — at 8
went to Colman's — there
until after 10 — then again
to studio — there for some
time — then to 37th S. & to bed
at midnight.
