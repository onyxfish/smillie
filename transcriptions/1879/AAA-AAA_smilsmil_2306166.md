## Left Page

### March, Monday 31, 1879

Overcast, blustering & cold —
Got to studio at 9 & tried to work
on pen sketch "Fort George, Lake
George" — not in any humor for
work but getting along slowly
when a telegram came from
Macdonough saying that presses
must be taken from 53 B'dway to-day
then Gifford came in & I prepared to
go down town — Young Palmer (son of
E.D.) came in & Van Cleef — G. & I
went to A.B.N. Co — then to 53 — then
I got lunch, went to R. Hoe & Co, then
to Harper's — then at near 4 got to
studio feeling used up — could
do nothing — Tea at 7 — at 8.30
went to Anna C's & wish her to
Private View of M.A., great crowd
with hot & cold draughts —
Stayed until near 11 — then home
with A. C — then to 37th St & to
bed at 12 —

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 1, 1879

Bright windy day, growing
milder — Got to studio at 9.30 —
Still very unlike work — Spent
the mng putting crows & figure
in "The top of the Hill" — & again
splashing over 6×10 "Spasm in
dark blue" — Lunch at 1.30 —
P.m. spent with little advance
trying to do some of the three
remaining Lake Geo. subjects —
Tea at 7 — then some cleaning
up — near 9 where I got to
Mr. Hertzgel's — 47th St — Saw
sister Mary who came to
town last night — there
until 10 — then to 37th St —
& to bed —
