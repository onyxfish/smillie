## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 26. 1879

*Room 4, Newport House, Bar Harbor Mt. Desert*

Cloudy & windy — awake early —
got up & dressed at 5.30 then
again went to bed & fell asleep —
up before 7 & had help — m had
then left Rockland — altho the
wind was very strong & cold
we enjoyed much the sail
among the islands & got to
Bar Harbor about 11.30 — After
a little searching, located
as above, then went to Prof.
Thayer's cottage but found
that Emma & Kitty J. were off
on an excursion — Dinner at 1 —
After that took a row for an
hour & a half — then wandered
about until 5.30 — then rested
until tea time at 6.30, wrote
a card home & soon after 8
went to Prof. Thayer's & spent
the eve'g there until 9.30 — then
to our room & to bed at 10 —
At 7 I went to wharf & met Emma
& Kitty on their return

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 27. 1879

mild overcast — wet. [illegible] before
8 at 8.30 went to Thayer Cottage
& after a time Geo. & I went out
with Kitty & Emma in a boat with
a man to row — went over to Round
Ing & Bald Porcupine islands &
made some sketches from the boat
of clefts in the rock, clefts of Seng. —
Back at 11.30 & until 12.30 on the
shore rocks — Dinner at 1 — at 2.30
again to Thayer's & party up Green
Mt. Mr. & Mrs. Howland, Geo. & I rode
up in Buckboard — the others walked
through a gorge — the weather cleared
so that the day was almost cloudless —
the ascent very interesting — the view
from the top very grand — Mr. & Mrs. Mc.
Taylor Johnston up there — Geo. & [illegible] by
buckboard & the Rest by — as remained &
had tea in the house on our return — saw
sunset & about 8 started on return —
walked down the road & got to the
Thayer's cottage about 9.15 — glorious
moonlight night — to Newport House
& to bed at 10.
