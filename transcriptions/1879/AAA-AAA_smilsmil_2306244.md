## Left Page

### September, Friday 5, 1879

Clearing day. bright & warm.
a large party left at 11 A.M.
the confusion broke up my
work so that I did not get
to work until 11.15 — at it
for an hour, then out & had
a walk — Dinner at 1.30 —
then Read Couture's Con-
versations on Art for a while
— then a nap — then to work
until 5 — then out & up the
hill again — met Mr. Roberts
sketching by the Road side.
Back soon after 6 & had tea
— then chatting with Mr. R.
until near 8.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 6, 1879

Charming day. After breakfast
a short walk & at about 10 o'clk
got at my A.B.N. Co's drawing
& worked until after 12 — then
another walk before dinner at
1 — then Reading & a little
walk until 3, then work
until 5; then another walk
& tea at 6. In parlor with
the ladies until after 8 — then
to Thom Mr. House — there
until 10, then back — two or
three ladies got into parlor, so
I had a little chat with them
until 11, then to bed.
