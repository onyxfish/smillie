## Left Page

### May, Sunday 18, 1879

muggy, sticky & overcast — woke up very early — got up &
had a curryjing scrub & then went back to bed & had a
little more sleep. Blkf.
between 9 & 10 — to Dr. Hastings
in the [illegible]. After service &
went with Charl. to Payne's —
Cor. 40th S. & Park Ave. & had
dinner — at 3.30 Samuel and
up 5th Ave. calling at Sherwood
& [illegible] Yale — neither of them in —
then to Maud Silsbee's. 205
W. 52d S. & visited there until
near 6 — then across the Park —
glorious walk — to A.N.N.'s — Wm
J.J. & Aunt H. there — had tea
at 8.30 walked down the Ave.
called upon Mr. Sherwood — found
him in & with his son-in-law —
looked over & discussed the
plans for his new Studio Bldg.
to 37th S. at 10 — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 19, 1879

Heavy, muggy most after pouring
rain during the night — Blkf.
at 8.30 — then to Reimer's & ordered
frame for "Hay Ricks" — then to Reimer's
shop — then to 37th S. as it had stopped
raining & changed clothes — 10.30
when I got to studio — Spent
the [illegible], making paste, mounting
etchings, etc — to lunch at 2.30
then up to 37th S. for my pocket-
book & back at 3.30. More
mounting of drawings & etchings —
tea at 7.30 — then washing and
cleaning up & at near 9 went
to "Century". Pasted my photo.
into Club Album. Chatted with
Collins & read Linton's article
in Atlantic — left club after 11
— to 37th S. & to bed a little
before 12.
