## Left Page

### May, Friday 2, 1879.

Cold, bleak & overcast day.
After bkfst, kindled a fire
in Father's Room — then a walk
& at 10.30 got started on a
7×11 water color — "On the Canal
at Elmira" — Before dinner,
took a short walk — worked
until 5 & then, with Father,
went to Mary's — Tea at 6.30
Evef. until 8, chatting
with Mother — then a little
writing — & at 8.30 went to
Matt's — there until 10 —
then home & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 3. 1879.

*Rec'd to New York —*

Delicious day — up early —
9.° Rec'd to W.Y. by 8.35 A.M. Train
After bkfst — I walked with Father
by the So. Road, by the "old farm"
& up the "big hill"; then to work
on Water Colors — finished canal
& commenced one, View on Long
Lake; worked until 12.30, then
dinner — After that went uptown
& bid Mary & Matt good bye —
back at 2.30 — packed valise.
Mrs. Susk (Thorne) & Miss Jewett
called — At 4.15 I left the house
& took 4.50 Train to N.Y., getting
into G.C. Depot at 7 — To studio
& had Tea — Evef. at N.A.D.
for a little while — then to
Century — there until after
11 — then walked to 37th St —
& to bed at 12.15.
