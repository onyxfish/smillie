## Left Page

### May, Thursday 22, 1879

At last, a welcome change —
bright, clear m[illegible]. with wind from
the west. After bkfs — went to
Belmont's & arranged to begin
my copy of Jacque this p.m. —
then to studio — finished pen
sketches for Treadway & [illegible]
Rock Ho. Changed design for
Miles Standish — wrote several
notes, laid a ground for Colman
& at 2 had lunch — After 3
when I got to Belmont's — worked
until near 5.30 — finishing out-
line — then to studio. Tea at 7
— at 8 went to Parlors of Dr. Crosby's
Ch. & heard Miss Kate Sanborn read
a paper on "Vanity & Insanity,
the Shadows of Genius" — very interesting.
At 9.15 went to Emma J's — nohin — then
to Fanny K's where I found Emma —
Anna C. & Miss Brush with Fanny playing
8 hand music — no company — enjoyed
it — after 11 when I left — walked
up to 38th S[treet] — with Anna & [illegible] C — to
37th S[treet] — & to bed at 12 —

## Right Page

### May, Friday 23, 1879

Delightfully fresh & cool — After
bkfs — went to studio & at 10 went
to Belmont's & worked with water-
colors copying painting by Jacque
until 1 — then to lunch. Back
at 2 & at 3.30 had finished my
copy which I think very good
for me. To studio at 4. Had
tea between 6 & 7 — wrote a
letter to mother congratulating
her upon her 70th Birthday which
comes to-morrow — At 8.30 at Grand
Central Depot to study time table
— at 8.40 at Anna C's — out —
At 9.15 at Shurtleff's — Mr. McKnight,
[illegible] & Mr. Maynard there — at
10 went to studio — wrote awhile
& then to 37th S[treet].
