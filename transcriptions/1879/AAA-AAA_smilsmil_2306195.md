## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 28. 1879.

Very pleasant day — after getting to studio worked until I finished sketch of Quebec — Took me most of the mng. Went out to lunch abt 1.30 then went to Harper's — saw Parsons & left sketches for page blocks in the Weekly of Sanguine & Quebec. Then to Shark's & got copper plates — then to A.B.N. Co & at 4.30 to studio — laid grounds on my plates & took some etching [illegible] — as I had some baker wet — left after 7 — Near 9 when I got to Will's where Father & Mother are, having arrived from Po'k this mng. Visited with them until after 10 — Then to 37'S. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 29. 1879.

Very hot day — Got to studio at 9.30 & after writing a couple of letters, Traced Jacque drawing for etching. Mother came in about 11 o'clk. & stayed for an hour. At 1.30 I went out & got strawberries & claret, brought them back & had lunch in the studio. Made pencil sketch 5¼ × 9¼ — Churchyard at Highlands — upon Hudson for an etching for Koehler. Father came in between 4 & 5 o'clk. Men came & put up my skylight curtain. Had tea about 7. Then wrote a note or two & got to Will's about 9. Gel. & Chanel there — Stayed until about 10.30 — Then to 37'S. & to bed — Father & Mother return home to morrow p.m.
