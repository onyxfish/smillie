## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 17, 1879

Charmingly cool day —
Get got off to N.Y. by early
train. I spent the day
clearing up in studio &
packing things for &
to getting off for the summer
about 1 went to Dr. Maney's
there for near an hour he
Talking about buying my
Rough Sport for $150 — then
to 6th Nat. Bank — there to
Cheeny's & brought Travelling
bag & at 2.30 to lunch —
To studio about 3. E. N. S.
called & I packed until
near 8, then out to tea &
at 9 went to Mr. Ireland's
— Soon after 10 took Emma
home — then to studio —
there learned from Janitor
that E. D. E. Greene had just
died — To 37th St — & to bed
shortly after 11.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 18, 1879

Still very cool & pleasant,
got to studio at 10. All
day at work getting things
in order — Falconer called
& I laid a ground for him
At 1 o'clk. I went to the
Kimball's — Matt & Kate
in town for to-day — met
them & E. N. S. there and
had dinner — then to
Knoedler's & made purchases
At 3 again at studio — there
until 4.30 — then to tea
fussed in studio until
about 10.30 — then to 37th St —
with both arms full and
to bed soon after 11.
