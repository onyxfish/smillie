## Left Page

### July, Saturday 5, 1879

Charmingly cool day —
Afterblép. made awhile,
packed blocks for Anthony
& took them to E.U.p. office
then to work at my etchings
Rebiting on "Up the Hill" &
"Jeffersonb". Dinner at 12.30
See the afternoons at my
Rebiting. Mumab — to
Tea at 6.30, + stayed all night —
my plates until after 7 —
about 8 Took a walk
out & went home with
Mary & Mrs Vivians. A.P. 1—
arr'd this mg. Home
& to bed before 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 6, 1879

Beautiful day — cooler
than yesterday — After
blép. commenced reading
Howell's "The Lady of the
Aroostook" — went to Baptist
Ch. & heard Dr. K preach —
"Cast thy burden on the Lord".
He sails for Europe on W'd. next
— 6 mos. leave — Mumab — to
dinner at 1 — after that
Read until about 4, then
out & walked out by Hodges
Ave, back of the Winslow
place & out up on the South
Road about 2½ m. S. of P.k. —
a walk altogether of 5 or 6
miles — in at 6 — then tea.
Read until after 8, finishing
my book — Enjoy Howell's
quiet, dainty manner exceed-
ingly — To bed at 10.
