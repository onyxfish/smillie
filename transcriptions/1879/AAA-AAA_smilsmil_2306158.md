## Left Page

### March, Saturday 15, 1879

Charming day — got to studio about 9.30 & to work on 16×25 "Up the Hill" — Smeared it over with Blk. Bitumen & Madder Brown & then to work repainting — used my palette knife freely — got along swimmingly — to lunch at 2 — a short walk & again at my work & by 5 had practically finished it — the best days work I think, I have yet done — Tea between 7 & 8 — at 9 — went with Geo. to the Century — there until 11 — then to 37th St. & to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 16, 1879

Beautiful day — Bkfst at 10 — my head ached — so I went to studio & stretched myself on the lounge & slept until 1.30 — then to Preston Springs and had lunch — about 3 rec'd to studio with Emma J. & true Cas well — I painted three crows in the foreground from picture painted yesterday; then again gave up to my headache — at 6. took a little to eat & at 8.30 went to the Kimballs for an hour, then again to studio — had a visit from Bellows (A.F.) — Soon after 11 went to 37th St — To bed a little before midnight.
