## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 8, 1879

Charming day — got to studio
some afternoon & at about 10 went
down town — to Harker's first, then
to Hoe & Co, [illegible] & to Jacquebin's — got
$15.00, lessons to Miss Morrison — then
to A.B.N. Co — At 1.30 had my
lunch — then again to Hoe & Co —
there to D. Appleton & Co — Saw
Bruce — there to studio shortly
before 8 — lived out & for a
time on lounge — then up &
spent an hour working over
my printing press — Tea at
6.30. Shurtleff came in &
gave us tickets to a Burbank
Reading at Ass'n Hall — went
there & after it to Matthews
Sale at Kurtz Gallery where I
have a picture — there until
10.30 — then to studio, fixed
fire — then to 37 E. & to bed
at 12 —

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 9, 1879

Clear, very mild & spring-like.
Got up with a bunged up eye,
& feeling bunged up generally.
After bkfs — went up to Dr. Yale's
& saw him — Did not get to studio
until 10.30 — the rest of the mng
spent laying grounds, most of
them for 4 — Lunch at 2 —
the p.m. spent fussing about
Miss Armstrong called — at
4 out for a short walk — near
8 when I had tea. After that
went to Balcuff's & had Yale's
prescription made up — At
about 10 went in to Kurtz Hall.
2d night of Matthews Sale, on
slaughter — My Lake Placid
failed to find a purchaser —
Got to bed about 11.30.
