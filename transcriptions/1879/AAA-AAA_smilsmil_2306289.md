## Left Page

### December, Saturday 6, 1879

Horrible dark rainy day — with thunder & lightning in the early evening — Got to studio about 9.30 — Got boxes ready and to work proving "Moonlight & Cedars" — Slow work — To dinner at 2 — Printed until about 4, then could see no longer. Felt sick after my days work — Mr. Coleman came in and made a sociable call, just in the dusk of the evening — Tea at 7 — The rain falling furiously — I waited until near 10 before I went down to "The Century" — there until after 11 — then walked up to 37.
