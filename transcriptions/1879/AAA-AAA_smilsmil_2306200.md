## Left Page

### June, Saturday 7. 1879.

Delightful day — to studio soon after 9 — Had two or three letters to write — Got $100 from Boston — To work he Belmonts Gallery on some sketch for Koehler after Hebert. Finished it about o'clk — & Ret'd to studio — Then to lunch — After that worked on my etching "Highlands on the Hudson." About 6 went to Earl Shinnns & we chinned it until 8 o'clk, then to studi. had tea & between 9 × 10 went to Century. June meeting. Left there abt 11.30 — To 37 1 S. & to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 8. 1879.

In bed all day with a head ache — got up soon after 4 P.m. The girls made a bowl of gruel for me & at 8 I went to Anna Coaks there until near 11 — then walked down to 29 S. and back — to bed at 12.15.
