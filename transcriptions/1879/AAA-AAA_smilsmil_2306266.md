## Left Page

### October, Sunday 19, 1879

Very pleasant day in spite of some mud & the wind — in the mng. went to Baptist Ch. to hear Rev. Mr. Burlingham. He preached a missionary begging sermon. Dinner at 1.15 after having had a short walk. At 3 o'clk. Mr. Bruce came & wrote out his dedicatory lines & stayed until between 4.30 & 5. Then I went out & had a good walk. Tea about 6. Mr. Burlingham, Ed & Matt to tea. Itself again to Baptist Ch. B— preached on double text — Christ weeping over Lazarus & Jerusalem. Home soon after 9 & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 20, 1879

Delightful day — cool & clear — after bkft. to work on stove in father's room, took it apart, cleaned it & then kindled fire — then started out to hunt up Mary Diamond, a servant; went to a number of places, left a message with her cousin & at 11.30 got home. Read until dinner at 12.40. After that to work on Bruce ilt., Stanza XII — Po-ho-no. Worked until near 5 — then out for a short walk with father; met Prof. & Mr. Ritter in their garden on So. Montgomery St. Got to Baptist Ch. at 5.45 for Kate. Escorted Miss Sheppie Hull to her home & stopped for a moment at the Hulls' in Market St. & saw Mr. Williams & rest of family. Tea at 6.30. Itself wrote a note or two — to P.O. after 9 — then home & to bed at 10.
