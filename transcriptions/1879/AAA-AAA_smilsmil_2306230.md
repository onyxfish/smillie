## Left Page

### August, Friday 8, 1879

East wind & stormy day. Heavy rain about 9 a.m. — then at intervals during the day. Spitting rain or mizzle. Got to work between 10 & 11 on A.B.N.Co. drawing but my head was very troublesome — worked until past 12, then out again for more walking. Dinner at 1.30 — after that read papers for awhile. Tried to work but could not — again out walking & at 4 went to Mr. Jones — on the piazza until 6 reading — then to tea. After that in parlor for awhile, then to the cottage across the street where Mr. Shaw gave us some black art exhibits etc — then out on piazza to see the moon rise — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 9, 1879

Dull grey day, but very pleasant after help; a walk & then to my work on A.B.N.Co. drawing until near 12. Then out. Took a boat & rowed near the surf for an hour. Dinner soon after — again at my work and at 4.30 at Mr. Jones — we went to the rocks & there I read aloud "Zig Monaldini's Niece" until 6 — then to tea; another short walk, then to parlor where an entertainment was given — "Shadow pictures." Scenes "in our village" — over at 9, then a little dancing — then some classical music by Mr. McKim & singing by Mr. Scaif — after 11 when I got to bed.
