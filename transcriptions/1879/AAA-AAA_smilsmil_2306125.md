## Left Page

### January, Saturday 4. 1879.

Beautiful mrf — after blpo — went up town — back to my work at 10 — worked awhile with the graver & at 11 commenced proving — Could not get things to go right for some time & so spoiled much of my work — Dinner at 12.45 — After a short rest, again at my printing & got 5 very good Jap and paper pls — but made a number of failures on other papers — worked until 5 — then one of pa for half an hour — Tea at 6 — at 8, as I had some more wet paper, again at my printing — paper & ink used up at 10 — then half an hour clearing up — Exceedingly tired — Rested until 11 — then to bed — my frost bitten toes feeling very like burns — bathed them with Pond's ext.
Took 17 pls — only 7 of which give me any satisfaction while 4 are total failures.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 5. 1879.

Beautiful mrf, but later overcast & winds, same as preceding two or three days — did not get up until 9 — feeling quite tired & sore — Spent much of the morning in father's room getting things to rights — Dinner at 1.15 — After that a nap in front of parlor fire — At 4 to work packing up things to take to the city with me. Tea at 6 — Everg spent in parlor father reading one of Hamilton's Sermons — After 10 when I got to bed.
