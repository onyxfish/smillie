## Left Page

### December, Sunday 28, 1879

Mild, pleasant day, slight overcast. Up late, feeling out of sorts — stomach pains.
After bkfs., what was left of the mfg. was spent lying about on sofas & in rocking
chairs until 12, when I went to Mamy's, met Father & back home before 1. Then dinner — afternoon. The weather grew thicker —
I poked around — went into Christ's Ch. & heard again a part of the Xmas Carols — Tea at 6 — the rest went as the day had done — to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 29, 1879

Dull day — altho' at times it struggled to be pleasant.
It had rained during the night — a film of ice over everything. Later in the day slush was triumphant —
Geo went to N.Y. in early train — after bkfs., a short walk — then to work on pen sketch after Kaulbach — Dinner at 1 —
finished sketch at 3 — then wrote a letter to Mrs. C. H. S. Williams — at 5 walked uptown with Mary & Watts.
Tea at 6. Left home at 7 — by 7.45 train got to N.Y. at 10.10 — to 37 St & to bed at 11.
