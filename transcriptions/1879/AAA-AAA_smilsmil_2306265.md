## Left Page

### October, Friday 17. 1879

Not so warm, overcast & breeze from S.E. Bldp-
at 8 — a walk up town
then to work on Stanza
XIV. Dinner at 12.30
then mounted three of
my summer W.C. sketches
& worked on pen sketches
until near 5 — then out
& walked down lower road
up the "big hill", to Mary's
to shoemakers in Washington
St. & then home to tea at
6.15 — Sueff, some reading
& a little meting — to bed
about 10.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 18. 1879

Dull, overcast day — after
my mf walk, got to work
on Bruce, Stanza XIII — Cabs of
silvery — at 11 went out to see
the Tally-ho coach come up
from New Hamburgh, did not
get along until 11.40 — effect
much marred by dust —
Dinner at 12.30. Afternoon
worked until 4.30 & finished
ill. Tea at 6 — after that went
up town with Matt & Kate —
back with Kate & soon after 8
went to Bruce's — there until
9.30 — then home. Slight Rain —
To bed at 10.30 — heavy Rain
about midnight.
