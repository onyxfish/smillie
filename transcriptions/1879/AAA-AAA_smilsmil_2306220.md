## Left Page

### July, Saturday 19, 1879

Another charming day — so cool, as to be almost an uncomfortable change. After a short walk got to work on my Osgood etching and spent the m'f. Retinting it. Dinner at 12.30 — Afternoon, Took p't. of my etching, working upon it with dry point & graver between times, until near 6 o'clk. Charl arr'd from N.Y. by Saratoga E 4/5 — Emma B. stayed with us over night — Card from G'o. who is with Nicole at the Hesperus Ho. Magnolia, Mass. Tea about 6.30 — Sent a walk to the River & about, with Charl. — In after 8 — Evey in chat — To bed soon after 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 20, 1879

Delightfully cool & pleasant but I suffered more or less all day from a nervous headache. In the m'f. went to Baptist Ch. & heard Alonzo Parker preach a very good sermon — Then home & wrote a letter to George — Dinner at 1 — The afternoon was, most of it, passed in a Rocking chair — At 5.45 went to P.O. with George's letter — Then tea — again in Rocking chair until 8 o'clk — Then went to Baptist Ch., heard the end of the sermon & walked home with Father, Mother & Kate. To bed soon after 10 — had a grand time during the night murdering off most of the family — a tired time — the result of my overwork.
