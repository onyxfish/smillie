## Left Page

### November, Thursday 20, 1879

Snowflurring at 9 am. Got to studio
at 9.30 after going to N.A. & getting
Tirmin Girard & Barnard. Worked
upon them until near 1 o'clk —
then W. Geo. Whitney came
& we went to the Albemarle
& lunched with him. Back
at 2 o'clk. Stopping at N.A.
& getting Beaufain Irving
& working on that as long as
I could see — then down to
Green's — found the printing
of the Yosemite exceedingly blk
& smudgy — a few unpleasant
words passed between Green &
myself & I left — To tea at Anna
Cook's at 6 — left there soon
after 8 & went to studio —
made notes to Bruce & to
Roberts Bros & fussed about
until 10.30 — then to 371 S. —
bitter cold — & to bed at
11.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 21, 1879

Clear windy & very cold —
Look Japanese paper (480 sheets)
to Rip's office for Koehler — then
to N.A. Had a W. S. Richards
& Steele sent to my studio
& got to work upon them
soon after 10 — Wallace Bruce
came in but I would not
go to the printers with him —
Finished Steele at 6 o'clk —
then to lunch & at 2 went
to first Phil. Rehearsal —
Enjoyed it very much —
met many friends — back
to studio at 4.30 — with
[illegible] to Century at 5.30 & had
dinner — there until after 9 —
then to studio — then to 34th S. —
to bed at 10.30. [illegible — ~3 words]
