## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 16, 1879

Clear, mild & enervating Spring day — Got to studio soon after 9 — went to N.C. & got slight sketch from "Up the Hill" — went to Supt. office with Polly for W. Hart & there met Elisha Sylvans & Ben Mulford — Then to work on block for Appletons after "Up the Hill". To lunch at 2 — a short stroll in Mad. Sq. Then again to work & finished block about 4 o'clk — After that the Payne girls called. Tea at 7 — Felt so much used up in the evg that I begged off from going to Ogilvie's as at first intended — Before 9 at Anna Cook's — there until 10 — then to 37. S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 17, 1879

Miserable Raw, mizzling wet day — A little party were to go to Huntington, L. I., may-flowering to-day — did not do it — Got to studio soon after 9 & to work on Water Color sketches — finished a little fellow begun sometime ago & commenced another — Lunch at 2.30 — Tea, as usual at Anna C's — Mark family & H. to Miss Arnold's — then Geo. & I went to Steinway Hall (Bdway bet. 42-3d. St.) & heard a Quartette concert — H. Schubert & Beethoven — to 37. S. at 10.30 & to bed with a villainous headache.
