## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 1, 1879

At Home. Po'keepsie.

Mild, overcast day — did not get up until 10 o'clk — owing to headache — then had blkf't — fixed fire in parlor — then to work on my etching "Cedars by Moonlight" & took a proof — Dinnerat 1 — again at work, burnishing & at 4.15 again took p — am both encouraged and discouraged. — At 5 went out & walked until 6 — then tea. Then dressed & went to Wallace Bruce's & saw Mr. B. for a few minutes — then to Sormiss & Wicks — then left cards at Hulm's Hull's & Honda's — left letter for Anna Cook at P.O. — then to Dr. Hasbrouck's & before 10 saw most of the family there — at 10, home & to bed at 11.
