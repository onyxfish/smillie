## Left Page

### February, Saturday 15, 1879

Fine day — about 10 when I got to studio — To work on 16×25 "Up the hill". Ames Van Want called — I stuck to my work until 1.30 — then to lunch — worked as long as I could see & very nearly got first painting finished, but not the color I wished — Rested on lounge until I had tea at about 4 — after that, fussing until 9 — then to N.A. for a few minutes — got to St. Cloud at 9.40 — visited with father & mother for an hour, then to 37. St. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 16, 1879

Very charming day — 34°F°. between 8 & 9 — then to studio — got to St. Hastings's son after 11 & heard him preach from Matt 9th ch. 23 v. After service, to St. Cloud Hotel father under the weather with a cold — Chare & Geo. came & we had dinner about 2 o'clk. — At 4 went to Mr. Panton's — met there. that Hicks & we looked over a lot of drawings, papers, colors, etc., etc. — until 6 o'clk — then I went to St. Cloud. — had tea about 7 — there until after 9, then to Studio — found my fire out, then to 37. St. — got to bed about 10.30.
