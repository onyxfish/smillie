## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 23, 1879

Mild & pleasant day — after blef.
my walk as usual — then to
work on "Jacob's Dream" for Bruce's
Yosemite — worked until dinner
at 1 o'clk. After that sat about
& read for awhile — then again
to work, finished Jacob & planned
mirror lake, then, after 4, out
& played five games of quoits
with Mr. Clark, he winning 3 games
to my 2. Then up to Bowling
alley with Geo. & Middleton —
there until tea time. Evef.
Mr. Wingate & I played whist against
Messrs. Roberts & Mr. Niles. We beat
4 straight games — then to parlor
& until 10.30 making pen sketches
for Miss Woodbury — then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 24, 1879

Overcast mrg. drawing to rain at
about 10 — did not get to work until
11 as Mrs. Woodbury & daughter
then got away for Boston. Then
at pen sketches of mirror lake —
Dinner at 1 — afternoon, finished
sketch — read awhile Mcmaldm's
niece — then out & had a sloppy
walk around the mile square with
Mr. Roberts, then out to Eagle Mt. He
meeting Geo., Chakeley & the two
Niles's, went in to Thorne Mt. He
& saw Geo.'s studies with Mr. Roberts.
Tea at 6.30 — storm cleared
away — moonlight & clouds &
cold north wind. Evef. played
whist with Mr. Wingate ago. Roberts
& Mr. Niles — quite exciting. About
9 o'clk played a rubber on our 41st
game — Roberts won making him
21 to our 20. To bed at 11.
