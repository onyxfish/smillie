## Left Page

### February, Sunday 23, 1879

Fine day, but my cold in head
prevented me from enjoying it.
B&ft between 9 & 10, then to studio
then to D. Hastings & heard him
preach from 41 Psalm 1 v. on the
Tenement Houses of N.Y. After that
to St. Cloud — Father, Mother, Kate
Charl. Geo & I dined together
about 2 — after that I gave my-
self up to the lounge & did not
get up until 6.30, when I took
tea at the hotel, altho' I had
an engagement at the Rodgers
in 48' St — then again on lounge
until near 10, when I got up
& went to 37' St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 24, 1879

Clear, cold & breezy — very stupid
all day with my head — in the
mng. could not work — at 1 o'clk
Miss Armstrong came in & I
painted a little 6×10 sketch in
oil while she watched — then
I went out to lunch — later
in the afternoon. Mr. Gesner and
Miss Satthofer called for Miss A — at
about near 5 — Anna C — Mrs. Oakey
& the Misses Lewis called —
I did not go out for any walk,
but in big chair until 7 — then
had tea — [illegible] wrote notes
to Mr. Paulton — White, Griffits
Dusk — Kate Hillard — Nicoll
& Price Hasbrouck — to 37' St
ab [illegible]
