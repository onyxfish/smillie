## Left Page

### February, Friday 7, 1879

Very pleasant weather — after-
noon to Rennie's — met Miss Abbott
+ worried about frames — Got to
studio at 10. Then to work on the
outlet of Lake George from top of Roger's
Rock. — Stuck to it — to lunch
about 2 — a short walk + again
at work + a little after 5 got
sketch done. Mr. Coleman came
in, said a few friends were to be
at Eastman Johnson's informally +
suggested that Gee + I go up in the
evefg. — Tea in the studio about
4 — At 8 made up my mind to
go to J's — got there before 9 +
found Mr. & Mrs. Coleman — Mr. & Mrs. Jones —
Tiffany — Mr. & Mrs. Sanford Gifford +
Mr. & Mrs. Edwin Booth. — Passed a
very pleasant evefg. — quite a
chat with Booth, who seems to be
very ready to talk about himself
& his affairs — near midnight when
we left — I walked down from 55 [St.]
to 37 [St.] + to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 8, 1879

Pleasant day but somewhat
under the weather from headache —
A little after 10 when I got to
studio — Commenced Lake Geo —
Sabbath day Point — At 12 Miss
only came + I drew from her until
near 2 — then out to lunch — back
+ finished sketch — Miss Lizzie
Bullard & her father called —
About 6.30 I went to the Nicholls —
had tea + then to Philharmonic
Concert — a very tame affair to
a poorly filled house — home
with the K's — then walked up
6th Ave. + met Kate + Charl coming
out of Standard Theatre — been to
see "H.M.S. Pinafore" — walked with
them to St. Cloud — saw father +
mother for a moment — then to
5th Ave. + 45th St. with Charl — then
to 37th St. + to bed a little before
midnight.
