## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 30, 1879

Quite a showery morning but before noon it cleared
off charmingly — Got to studio
about 10 — worked a little
on Kaselback sketch — then
to work Tracing & outlining
big W.C. drawing — Dinner
at 2 — after that, went to
the barber's — back at 3:30
worked on drawing until
after 4 — out & had a walk
near [illegible] — went in
studio writing letters —
to 34th St; before 11 — Beautiful
moonlight night — to bed
at once.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 31, 1879

Dull, cheerless m'f. A.M.
went to Clarence Cook's — 191
W. 104. & with him went to
the Historical Library Bl't — 2d
ave. & 11th S; & spent a couple
of hours looking over the Bryan
gallery to find something for
me to etch — did not come
to any conclusions. When
we left the Bld't. it was snowing
heavily — I went to studio —
Heavy, sleety rain in the afternoon.
To dinner at 2. Afternoon
made pen sketch of my proposed
W.C. picture "Old Cedars — Coast of
Maine" — for W.C. Catalogue —
Tea about 4 — then to 37th St. & at
7:30 to Charl's — 532 5th ave — Willy
[illegible], Minn, many Shaffer, Walter
& Miles Palmer were there — had
a supper about 11. Before 12
I was in my room — 37th St — and
in bed shortly after midnight.
Beautiful moonlight night
