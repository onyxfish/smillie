## Left Page

### February, Thursday 13, 1879

Pleasant day — got to studio about 10 o'clk & to work on Stamp
Idem. design for B. B. N. Co —
worked until 1 o'clk. when Miss A. & then M. J. came for their lessons — before I got away to my lunch Mrs. Morrison came for her first lesson. She left before 3 & then I went out to my lunch. The rest of P. M. with Miss A. & W. J. — Nearly 1 when I got to Anna C's to tea. At C left her at Arnold's & went Reuvein's — then to Mr. Paulton's, 35 W. 56 St. & looked over some of the papers & other materials left by H. & Paulton — Abg. 15 got to Payne's & there played "[illegible] ante" with the girls until 11 o'clk. then to 39 E. S— & to bed shortly before midnight.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 14, 1879

Very cold, clear m'f. Got to studio shortly after 9 — found a very comical burlesque of a Valentine from Emma — J. spent an hour in making answer — then put up on stretches 14×18 & 25×16 — & sketched latter in charcoal.
Lunch at 1.30 — afternoon worked on 16×25 until about 4, then feeling tired I spread myself for awhile on the lounge.
Tea at 6.30 — at 7.30 went to Emma J's & with the family went to concert at Chickering Hall given by Vocal Union — very enjoyable — after that, with Emma to Metropolitan Museum of Art — last Recip'n in the 14th St. Bldg — there until 11, then took E. home — then to 39 E. S— & to bed at 12.
Heavy snow squall for a few minutes about 9 p.m.
