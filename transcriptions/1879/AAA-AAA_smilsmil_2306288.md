## Left Page

### December, Thursday 4, 1879

Very mixed, pleasant day — did not get to studio's until 10 o'clk there until after 1 very busy in giving a general cleanup to the studio's, especially to my stchaing corner — Soon after Mr. Wynkoop & Miss Leightin came in & made a visit until 2.10 — then Ge. & I went to dinner — a short walk then back to studio's and filed around until nearl when Ge. & I went to Anna C.'s to tea — Fanny K. there — at 8 took them to Miss Arnold's, then Ge. & I went to the "Irvington" cor. B'way & 53d St. & called on Miss Kate Hillard & Mr. Coal there until near 11 — then to 37' St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 5, 1879

Much of the day overcast but mild — got to studio's at 9.20 & as soon as I could got to work on plate "Cedars by moonlight" & worked over husky about the moon with light dry point trying to make it less harsh and ragged. Moraur (the scribe) came in & spent an hour or so — other callers kept me until dinner time at 2 — then out & after dinner went down town & got printing paper & glazed boards & back soon after 4 — miles mounting some etchings Mr. (Becher) Hooker came in with Mr. Stanton & a young lady whose name I did not hear. — "Sea about" [illegible] — at 9 went to N.A. — Private view of Sherwood & Hart pictures to be sold next week — there until after 11 — then to 37' St. & to bed.
