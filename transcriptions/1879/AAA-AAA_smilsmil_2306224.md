## Left Page

### July, Sunday 27. 1879

Partially cloudy — warm and
muggy — Wrote a letter to Go-
then to Baptist Ch. & heard
Bruce preach a sermon —
Luke 17th ch. 14th v. Saw him
a few minutes after service.
Dinner at 1 — most of the
P.m. Spent writing a long
letter to Koehler in answer
to a longer one rec'd from him
yesterday m'f. Matt, El-
& Mary to tea at 6.30 — Well
I spent cleaning up my muss
in Father's room until 8.20
then to Ch. for Mother. Left
her at Miss Booth's while I went
home with Mary — Returning,
I had a little chat with Miss
B. & Matt. Vassar. Near 10 when
I got home & 11 when I got
to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 28. 1879

Very warm day — most of the m'f
putting away my things & getting
[illegible] of [illegible] up to Koehler — I
took things easy. Dinner as
usual at 12.30 & the afternoon
was nearly all spent packing
my trunk — Tea at 6 — Sat
chatting with Father & Mother
until 8 — then to Stan's where
Matt, Ed., Kate & Aunt Jessie
were — Soon after going back home
with Kate — To bed about
