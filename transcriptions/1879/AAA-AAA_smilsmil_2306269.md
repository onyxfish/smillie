## Left Page

### October, Saturday 25, 1879

Brilliant day. Clear & cold.
As my nitric acid bitten toe
hurt me I thought best not
to go out to walk. Got to
work for A.B.N. Co. washing
effect on today, outline of
ab.a. subject — a statue
in a public square.
Dinner at 12.30. Worked
until near 4 when I got
drawing done. Put things
up &c. Wrote card to Will
& then took them to P.O.
Went to Matt's, there for ¾
of an hour, then home to
tea at 6. At 8 went to W.
Bruce's — got check from
him for $230.00. Home &
to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 26, 1879

Another delightful day —
but I got up with a wretched
head ache — tooth ache & toe-
ache, so that I managed
to be fairly miserable —
ate some blfs — but at noon
went to my bed where I
spent the afternoon — up
for tea at 6 — Rested & sat
in big Rocking chair & to bed
again at 10.30 where I
would have passed a fair
night but for toothache
which torments me altho'
the teeth are soundly filled
& appear to be in good
order.
