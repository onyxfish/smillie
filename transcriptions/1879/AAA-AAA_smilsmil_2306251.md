## Left Page

### September, Friday 19. 1879.

Charming day, but unfitted for work by a headache. Until after 10 o'clk writing to C.H.S. Millie of Boston & waiting about for Mr. Hardenburgh then party to get away. Rode down to depot with them; off on noon train for Boston & N.Y. Then rode back in light wagon with Gee over Thorne Hill — delightful ride. Dinner at 1, after that sat about keeping out of bed only by an effort of will. About 4 went sauntering up on Thorne Mt., feeling much better, back to tea at 6 — At 8 went to Thorne Mt. Ho. with Miss Kate Woodbury to hear a country boy play on the piano — rather stupid as he had no real talent & played only his own messes. Back before 10 & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 20. 1879.

Overcast & disagreeable day — Before daylight Mr. Woodbury and daughter started for a trip to the top of Mt. Washington — got into a snow storm. I walked a little bit with Geo. & Mrs. Roberts & at about 11 started with the orchard to repaint my meadow study. Fine rain. Gee soon afternoon but I persisted in painting until 1. Then dinner — Afternoon very cold & cheerless — worked in my room for a little while, but gave it up & had Dicky put into a stone for me — again walked with Geo. & Mr. Roberts. Twelfth played 3 games of whist with Mr. R. Then Miles & Chalmers came in with Gee & until after 10 we chatted by the parlor fire — To bed before 11.
