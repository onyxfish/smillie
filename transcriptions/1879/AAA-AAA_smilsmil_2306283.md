## Left Page

### November, Monday 24, 1879

Still delightful weather. got to studio at 9.30. Avery had sent up a Rousseau, a Jacque & a Trayon — I pitched in &
managed to get the three done by 4.30 — went out for my dinner at 2 & then took a walk around Mad. Square. Eve'g — Ge. & I took tea at the studio — at 8.30 I went to the Ireland's. 331 E. 17 St. & saw Emma & Julie Spring — there until 10.20 — then to Studio & to 34 St. & to bed at 11.30. Charming moon-light night.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 25, 1879

Overcast & threatening m'g. Bkfs. at 8.45 — after that went to Currie's — then to 8th & 9th [illegible] Ave's & 50 [illegible] S. — great fire — 8th Ave R.R. stables — then to Hayek's. 441 S. & 10th Ave — finally got to studio about 11 — wrote several notes & at 2 went out to dinner — during the rest of p.m. entertained callers ( Mr. & Mrs. Wm Carpenter & Mrs Hooker, née Beecher called & finished up correspondence to my sorrow. I took a glass of lager beer at dinner and was in much pain all the evening. At 5.30 I went to Munderloh's — he paid me for my etchings — tea at studio at 6.30 — then sat/lounge until after 8 — then amused myself looking over etching [illegible] until altering. then to 34 St. & went to bed with my hot water bag.
