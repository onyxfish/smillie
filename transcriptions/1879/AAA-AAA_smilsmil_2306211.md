## Left Page

### June, Sunday 29, 1879

Owing to headache did not get up until noon. Very
cool & overcast — heavy Rain storms from the N.E. — Dinner
at 1 — After that I did a little writing & soon after
4, as it had cleared pleasantly I walked down to Winslow-
Pumpelly's — was very pleasantly Rec'd by Madame
P— P. & Ferguson were out wandering over the grounds but
came in in about half an hour. It was nearly 6 before I
got away — Then walked home — Tea at 7. Every at
home — To bed at 11 after sitting an hour or more
waiting to feel sleepy.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 30, 1879

Charming day; fresh & delightfully
cool — very slight spattering of Rain while I was taking my walk after
[illegible]. Among my letters, Rec'd one from Will ordering a drawing of some
Smelting works for the Q.B.N. Co — and sending me photos. I set to work
upon it at once. Dinner at 12.30.
Quite a heavy shower at that time.
Worked at drawing until 5, then out with father & walked below
Bisbee's & along the River. Tea at
6.30 — At 8 went with Geo— to Prof Ritter's & spent the even'g
there until 10.30 — singing by Mad.
P — then a walk — & to bed at
11.30.
