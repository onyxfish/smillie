## Left Page

### November, Saturday 8, 1879

Pleasant day — Got to Studio at 9.20 — some time before
Avery's pictures came, then to work & finished Madago.
Had a light lunch at 2 intending to dine at 5 —
then made pen illo. after Stevens — Colman invited
us to dine with him — At 5.30 went to Avery's with
paintings, then to Colman's. Had dinner shortly after
6 — Eveg. spent delightfully looking over his etchings
published in L'Art and listening to duetts played
by Mrs. C. and Carrell Dunham and her nephew. To 37½ S. —
soon after 10 — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 9, 1879

Dull mf. with a scatter of Rain, clearing very warm about
11 o'clk. Had bkft. at 9.30 and then to studio — then to 37½ S. &
made a change in clothing — to Dr. Hastings's church. After
service met Charl & Geo. — walked a short way with them, then
to studio & rested for awhile. At 2 went to Ashland Ho. for
my dinner. Then to studio & wrote until 4.30 — then to
Dufais's — missed him — then walked to the Kimball's and
had tea there. Charl came in later in the eveg. At
9.30 I went to Studio to see to the fire — A scatter of Rain —
Got to Reading & forgot
myself — After 11 when I got
to 37½ S. & to bed.
