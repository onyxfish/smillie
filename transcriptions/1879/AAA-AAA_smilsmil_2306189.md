## Left Page

### May, Friday 16, 1879

Exceedingly warm day opening muggy & overcast —
a little party of Colemans, Tiffanys, Miss Wiles & self
were to go to Brighton Landing Coney Island — but owing to
the weather & general sickness did not make it out — I
was on the lounge most of the day with pain and
nausea — At 4 P.M. I got up & printed until 8ten
of, saving my met paper that otherwise would have
moulded. Then had some tea & at 8.45 went in to
Coleman's & visited until 10, then to 34th St — & to bed
at 11 — soon after, heavy shower.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 17, 1879

An alpine-like day with sunshine & shower — very warm —
I did not get out of bed until 4 P.m — at 5 had a little
wine & cracker — then walked to Studio feeling very shaky.
Had tea after 7 — At 8.30 went
to Emma's — no one in —
Sat a while & looked over the
June Harper — then back to
Studio — wrote a letter or two
& at about 11 went to 34th St —
