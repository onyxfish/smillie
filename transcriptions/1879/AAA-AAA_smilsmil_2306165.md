## Left Page

### March, Saturday 29, 1879

Dark day — A short call on Mrs. Coleman at 9.30 + at 10.30
went to N.A. of D — Varnishing
Day — a large turn out of press-
men + Painters — Spent the
m'f. looking about — could
not work on either of my Paintings
in so great a crowd. — Lunch
at 2 — about 4 again went to
N.A. — at 5 went with Swain
Gifford to Kurtz Gallery — after
a look about there, then to Avery's
+ to 10th St. + to Kimball's + to Ireland's
+ near 7 to studio — had tea +
at 9 went to Wallacks + bought
tickets (8) for next Wed. evg.
Heavy Rain since 5.30 — then
to Loto's — there until 11, then
to 37th St + to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 30, 1879

Charming, spring-like day —
Bkfst at 10 — at 11 went to Dr. H's
+ heard him preach — at 12.30
went to studio — at 2.30 went
to a B'dway Restaurant + got
some dinner — again to studio
+ at 4 went to N.A. there
until 6 — then to studio —
had a call at 8.30 — then
in to Emma J's — there
until 10.30 — once more to
studio + to 37th St. at 11 + to
bed at once.
