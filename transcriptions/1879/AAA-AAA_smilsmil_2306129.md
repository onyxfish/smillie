## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 14. 1879.

Pleasant day — got to studio at 9.30 & to work on 10×20 — got along rapidly — after having finished up a background for Miss A. — at near 2 o'clk — Miss Jacobs brought in a lunch of oysters for me, so I did not go out — & worked steadily as long as I could see — Will K. with his friend Murray called — at 6 went to the K's to tea — Eve.f Will, fanny & Mrs. K — Rodney & his wife, Geo & I went to Grand Opera [illegible] — $1 ave & 23d S. & saw Bourcicanet in The Shaughraun — a very ordinary cast — at at 10.45 got to 37 S. about 11.15 — to bed in half an hour after.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 15. 1879.

Pleasant day — I attended part of it being overcast. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on 10×20 — m.c. — my head rather shaky — at 2 o'clk — went out with Geo & got a light lunch — back, & worked as long as I could see — got into a mess in my foreground — at 5.30 went to [illegible]'s — back at 6.30, then tea — made notes to P.S. Church from M. Chase & then to W.C. Meeting with A.f.D. Adjourned at 9.15 — Back to studio — fussed there over Ill. for Cat. until 10.30 — ther. to 37° S. & to bed — Snowing like fun —
