## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 24. 1879

*Po'keepsie —*

Still dismal weather —
after &lf for went to 37'S., packed
bag — then to Dr. Noyes — 37' & Mad.
Ave. with W.C. Sketch to pay my
bill — he seemed to be very much
pleased & spent more than an
hour examining my eyes — got
to studio after 11 — then to work
finishing the mounting for some
of the Lake Geo. drawings — To
dinner at 2 — Left studio at
3.30 — Met Charl at 9. central
depot & by 4 o'clk. train, with [illegible]
we went to Po'lk. — Got home
about 7 — had tea — after that
the opening of [illegible] & presentation
& presents. About [illegible] we went
to Mary's, where Geo. & I stayed
all night as the Dr. is away —
To bed about 11.
In the m'ft Rec'd — notes from Mi[illegible]
Dunning (with $30. for [illegible] ch[illegible])
& from Clarence Cook. — answered
them.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 25. 1879

Overcast & cold. Up about 7 &
got home soon after 8 & had bkf't.
Between 10 & 11, Geo. Charl. and
I went out & walked for an
hour or more. — Matt, Ed. Mary
& the children to dinner with
us at about 2 — I had another
walk with Charl towards eve'g —
went out with "Bob sled" on the
"Franklin St. hill & had a little
"coasting" — quite a snow flurry —
Tea at 8 — Eve'g. passed in
general hanging about until
11 o'clk. when I went to bed.
Beautiful moonlight —
At 9.30 am. went to Christ's
Ch. & heard the children sing
their Christmas Carols.
