## Left Page

### October, Thursday 9, 1879

Heavy rain just before daylight, overcast mrg. Bkfst at 8. heu mote to Saml. Coleman & Wallace Bruce. Took letters to P.O. and got back to my work before 11 — finished Stanza X. Dinner at 1 — after that, a rest. Sun out again very hot. Ther. on Piazza 82. Worked on Stanza XI until 4, then over to the lake to watch the log burning there until near 6 — then to W. J. J's — Tea near 7 — Eveg after Reading news paper, at 8.20 went to Mr. Cooper's & spent the eveg there until 10 — then to W. J. J's & to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 10, 1879

Another very warm and partially cloudy day. Bkfst before 8 — then I mote cards home & to the Munshewoods & a note to Nicole, then to P.O. & to work before 11 on [illegible] for Stanza XI. Out for a walk at 12.30 & at 1 oclk took dinner alone as W. J. & [illegible] were off somewhere. Finished XI & got started on XII when W. J. J. came along at 3 oclk & asked me to ride over to the lake with him, which I did & for a time superintended the burning of stumps there — Tea near 7 — Some packing after that — at 8.15 went to Mr. H. F. Drinker's and called on the Misses Biddle — to bed at 10.30.
