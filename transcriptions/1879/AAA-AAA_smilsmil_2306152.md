## Left Page

### March, Monday 3, 1879

Delightful, spring like day — after bkfst — went to
St. Cloud — Father & Mother pre-
paring to go home this p.m.
got to studio at 10 — went
to N.A. — then to Century & to
Avery's at 1 o'clk & lunched
with the family — then visit
him to 150 E. 44' S: W. Spencer
& to work with pen & ink upon
sketches from his pictures for
an ill. Cat. there until 5 —
then walked to 26' S. & called
upon Louis Tiffany. At 6 o'clk
to studio — tea at 7 — at 8.15
called on Mrs. Albert Mitchell (Annie
Tiffany) there until 10 — again
to studio — there until near
11 — & again midnight when I
got to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 4, 1879

overcast & raw — Soon after[noon] at
Mr. Spencer's & spent the a[fternoon] making
pen sketches from Bouguereau &
Boldini — at 1 went to studio then
to lunch & back to 44' S. at 2.
Could never see to work until 4 —
got sketches from Clayp & Corot —
then to Rennie's & ordered plate
glass to take the place for one on a
Boldini that I was unfortunate enough
to break — then to Avery's & at 6.30
to studio — tea at 7.30 — at 8.30 went
to Saml. Coleman's where a few
friends met to condole with him
on his birthday. At 11, heavy
rain — to 37' S. at midnight
& to bed at 12.30.
