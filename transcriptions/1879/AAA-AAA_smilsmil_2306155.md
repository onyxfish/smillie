## Right Page

### March, Monday 10, 1879

Milde, muggy, muddy, with rain in the mfg, but clear by 10 o'clk. At 9.30 at Mr. Spencer's & spent the mfg working on sketches from Madrazo & Jacque — to lunch at 1.30 — near 3 when I again got to work — Spent the p.m. on Meissonier & Kraus & got to studio about 6 very much used up — tea at 7 or 8. Etching club — a poor turn out of members & nothing done. Adjourned before 10 o'clk — then went with Nicole & had some beer — to 37th S. & to bed about 11 ½.
