## Left Page

### April, Saturday 12, 1879

Bright & breezy — Got to studio at 9.30 & such time as I did not have callers I gave up to putting my house in order. Mrs. Mr. Dark, Miss Rodgers & others called — went out to lunch at 1.30 — at 2.30 at Harlem station met there Geo. & Anna C. — Emma D., Leb Howe, Miss Woods — Mr. K. & Mr. Valentine — went to Scarsdale — walked a short distance to a wooded hill & found lots of Hepatica — walked to Tuckahoe & missed the train — then on to Bronxville & took 6.45 train getting to G.C. depot at 7.40 — Started to go home with Emma but changed our minds & went to Anna C's where we had dinner — at 9.30 went home with Emma — there until 10.30 — for a few m[inutes] at studio — then to 37. S— & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page
