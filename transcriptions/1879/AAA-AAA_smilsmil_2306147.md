## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 19, 1879

Overcast m'f. but later it cleared pleasantly — Got to
studio at 9.30 & spent most of the m'f making alterations
in "Semper Idem" label drawing. Suffering from my cold and
headache — Got a light lunch soon after 1 but all the p.m.
unfit for work, so that I did nothing. — At 4 Smurtheff
came in with two tickets to Oratorio of St. Paul. at Steinway.
Geo & I went. I must confess that I don't enjoy that form
of music — on my way back stopped at studio & fixed for
— 11.30 when I got to 37th St. & after 12 when I got to bed.
Snowing again quite hard. [illegible — ~3 lines]

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 20, 1879

Overcast m'f. with flying snow, but later it cleared pleasantly.
Got to studio at 9.45. Suffering from miserable headache so
that I could do no work — I tried, but was obliged to give
up — did a little planning for Keane Cottage — At 1 Mr. Lord
came — & worked at water color painting until 4 — at 5 I went
with Belows to W.H. Vanderbilt's Reception — left at 6 & went to
Anna C's — tea at 6.45 — & at 8
went with her to Arnold's — then
to St. Cloud — 15 min. with father
& mother — then to Mr. Perkin's
in 44th St — Misses Ripley and
Rogers there — Spent the eveg
until 11 o'clk — Then to 37th St.
& to bed at 11.45
