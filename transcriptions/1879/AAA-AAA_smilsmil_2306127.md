## Left Page

### January, Friday 10. 1879.

Bright pleasant day — got to studio at 9.30 & to work on the outlining of 10×20 W.C. "Out-skirts of [illegible] Oct. [illegible]" worked until a little after 12 — then out & ordered coal — had lunch & at 2 o'clk. at Phil. Rehearsal — heard the Schumann Symphony — then back to studio & continued work of the w.f. Mrs. Forbes — Miss Kimball & others called. Tea about 6.30 — then fussing around — about 9 o'clk at Mr. Rich. Butler's — 59 Park Ave — got there — passed a pleasant hour & a quarter — then to 37 W S — & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 11. 1879.

Overcast day — milder & some-what like rain — got to studio at 9.45 — finished outline & got to washes on 10×20 — this is the first W.C. that I have made sky washes upon while it stood up upon easel. Lunch at 1.30 — Continued work until after 5 — then to Jourkil's & ordered colors etc. for Kate Risley — then to Kimball's to tea. After that to Phil. Concert — Miss Helen Nelson of the party — After the Schumann Symphony I went to Century Club — Annual meeting — Considerable excitement over the election of Huntington Pres't — walked up to 37 St — getting there at 12 — to bed at 12.30.
