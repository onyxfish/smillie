## Left Page

### August, Friday 22, 1879

Overcast mg. — went out with my W.C. fraks to finish the group of twisted cedars that I began yesterday p.m. A few drops of rain fell — Miss — came & began a study from the same group — I finished it by 12.30 & then went to another study nearby, another twisted fellow growing out of the rocks over the water — the weather had cleared into a wonderful clear, breezy day — went to my dinner at 2 o'clk — about 4 went back to my study & working until 6.30; finished it — tea at — then up to cups of & tried to catch something in color of sunset; the beach & piers got little — tea at near 8. The servants of the hotel had a "hop" from 8.30 to 11 in dancing hall — much amused — to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 23, 1879
