## Left Page

### March, Monday 17. 1879.

Miserable day — stormy + wet —
Rains, snow + drizzle — Got to
studio at 10 — worked on several
little odd jobs — Re Painted
Spasm in Blue for Emma —
To lunch at 1,30 — back at 2.
Spent p.m. — Painting 6×10
& frame — another Spasm
in blue — Tea at ½ at 9 —
went in to Emma's for half
an hour — then with the
Shurtleffs for another half
hour, Then, at 11 to 37th St.
& to bed. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 18. 1879.

Pleasant day — got to studio
at 9.30 + to work on 6×10
sketch that I propose to
give to R.H. Stoddard — then
finished another 6×10 that
I have given to Granniss fund
Lunch at 2 o'clc. In the
afternoon went to Avery's —
did not see him but left
my bill, then to Jungling's
in Bond St. not in — then
to Brook's Bros & bought a
suit of clothes — then to
Vauthier's — then to studio
& had tea at 4,30 — at 8, at
Miss Abbott's & went with
her to Miss Bristed's. 330 E.
14th St. Miss B. had gone out —
but we passed a pleasant
eveg with Miss Canning —
at 10 took Miss A. home, then
to 37th St. & to bed at 11,30
