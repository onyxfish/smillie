## Left Page

### September, Thursday 25. 1879.

Very clear & cold. Bkfst at 8 after that out on [illegible] Mt. to see
Mt. Washington covered with snow. an intoxicating atmosphere
then in & made notes to Anna C.
& Belle I. walked around the
miles sq. & had dinner at 1.
About 2 Mr. Wingate & family
left for their home in Portsmouth.
After that the afternoon was
spent in chatting with one &
another, some getting together
& things & twice around the
miles square before tea.
Went in parlor & about
10 o'clk got to packing and
to bed about midnight —

## Right Page

### September, Friday 26. 1879.

*Steamer "Providence" Stateroom 47 —*

Bkfst before 8 — clear, cold mfg.
finished up all packing & little
chores before 10. At 11.15 started
for depot — Glorious air & ride — Mts
never looked lovelier — Train at 11.54
Mr. Gel, Niles & family & Mr. Howard
went with us. At Portsmouth, at
3.17, met Mr. Wingate & got walking
stick from him. The R.R. ride through
the Mts wonderfully fine — Got to Boston
at 5.30 & made a rush for Old Colony
depot, no trouble in getting there our-
selves & baggage behind time — be-
lated in getting away from Fall
River — altho' I had telegraphed
for stateroom could not get even
a tolerable one until after we
left Newport. There to bed
as soon as possible — ab 10.30
— glorious night but too cold
to be enjoyable out of doors.
