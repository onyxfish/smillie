## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 12. 1879.

Still clear — but my head more cloudy than ever — worked all
the m'g in real distress on
A.B.N. Co. drawing — my trip
yesterday, instead of bettering my
trouble seems to have aggrava-
ted it — but I stuck to work
until 1 o'clk — then dinner —
after that, again at work until
3.15 — my head like to split — a
visit from Dodge & [illegible] — at 4
I took shower & [illegible] — &
joined Mr. Smit & Miss Shaw, went
to Rocks & Mrs. J. Read Sig. Monaldi-
ni's niece — I rolled up & Resting.
At 5 I felt much relieved — then
tea — at 8.30 a little informal mu-
sical entertainments in the parlor.
Mrs. McKenzie — Miss Blake, W. Blake
& Mr. Schäff the performers — a very
pleasant affair — over at 10 — but I
did not get to bed until 11.30

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 13. 1879.

Clear day. After bkfs. went under
the mellows & finished painting in
study of Crescent Beach — worked
hard until 1 o'clk. but my study
was not a success — it could
hardly be with my head in
such a confused condition —
After dinner I worked on A.B.
N.Co's drawing "Brooklyn Elevated"
until 4.30 — then to Mrs. Smit's &
listened to Reading until 6 —
then tea — after that, Seyman
[illegible] & I went to Miss Knowlton's
cottage — there for an hour & a half
in her studio looking over his char-
coal sketches, then back to
hotel — on piazza until 10 — then
washed up brushes & got to bed
before 11.
