## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 3, 1879

Quite a cool day with considerable rain. Got to studio before 10 & right to work in etching — biting & etching all the mff. At 2 went out to lunch — & all the P.m. Continued my biting & etching on Up the Hill — Dr. Yale called. I worked with the acid until 6 o'clk, then Geo. & I went in to Colemans to dinner. About 9 rec'd to studio — lounged there until 10, then to 37 S. & to bed at 10.30. Heavy Rain during the night.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 4, 1879

Overcast mff, cleared about noon — delightful breeze and comparatively cool. Got to studio at 10 after going to 3d Ave & 18¢ S. for acid — before & fairly got to work Mr. Kochlen & Daniels his printer came in & stayed until noon. Then I went to work again on Up the Hill — gave it one more bite, cleaned plate & at 2 went out to lunch — P.m. Commenced etching on "Church Yard at Highland at Hudson" — took pfs of Up the Hill — & at 6 walked down to E. 12 S. & left pkt for Earl Shinn — No. 126 — to Dunlap's & got a hat — then to Smith & Green's & had something to eat & at 8 to studio. At about 8.45, at Anna C's — not it — then to the K's — just in from a S.S. Pic-Nic — there until 10.20 — then to 37 S. & to bed at 11.
