## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 25, 1879

Pleasant day — altho' much of the time overcast. After [illegible] went to Renner's — met Lawrence of [illegible], about framing a large photo that he has — Settled my bill with Renner — to Studio at 10.15 — to work on "Recluse Island" Lake Geo Harpers — At 2.30 went to lunch on way back met Maud Silsbee she Ret'd to Studio with us — Mr. Koehler, of Boston, called to advise about starting a New Art Journal to be illustrated with etchings. Tea at 7. About 9 went to [illegible] gallery, 817 Bowery — Private View [illegible] call — Delmonico Supper — to 37'S. to bed at 11.30 —

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 26, 1879

Very pleasant wf., but feeling headachy — Got to Studio at 9.30 & after finishing "Recluse Island." I made pen sketch of "Lake Geo Island." At 12.15 went to Avery's & from there to Photo Eng. Co. & gave orders for two sets [illegible] from Spencer Cat; blocks for large paper copies — then to Harpers — then to A.B.N. Co — then with McDonough to 5th R[ailwa]y & saw presses — then had lunch — then to Harper Bros — & at 4.30 to Studio. Soon after 6 went in to Colman's & at 6.30 to Kimball's — Tea at 6.45, at 8. Meek, his Mother, family. To House, Gee & J went to Park [illegible] & there met W. & Mrs. C — & saw Gilberto piece of nonsense "Engaged" It was very jolly but I was at the end quite used up with my headache & was barely able to get to 37'S. Rain began to fall — to bed at 11.30.
