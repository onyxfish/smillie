## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 14, 1879

Very warm — to studio at near
10 — Spent the mf writing
& printing — Took pk. for and
Coleman & done for myself.
Knudsen called. & fat —
About 1 went to N.A.D —
Annual Meeting — the afternoon
spent there — a very stupid
entertainment ruined by an
outrage reason & good manners
by Geo. Innis, who was madly
mad at the Hanging Com. — sat
down to dinner about 6 — very
so, so — no speeches after
that were worthy the name
— left at 10 o'clk — to 37 S.
& to bed at 11. [illegible — ~2 lines]

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 15, 1879

Another very hot day — got
to studio at 10 — more callers —
lots of them — Mrs. Wm. Dart & niece
called — I got pen sketch friece.
Maud S. & Ella Diven called — I
went to lunch at 2.30 — in the
afternoon I tried to [illegible]
but got very little done to
my satisfaction owing to my
paper being too wet. At 6.30
I went to Anna C's to tea —
no Annual club meeting, so
I spent the evef there with
A.C. & Fanny K — [illegible] went
to Maud's — I went to 37,
S.E. after 11 in a heavy [illegible],
to bed before midnight.
[illegible — ~3 lines]
