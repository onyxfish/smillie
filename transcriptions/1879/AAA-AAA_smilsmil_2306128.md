## Left Page

### January, Sunday 12. 1879.

Raw — overcast & rather disagreeable sort of day — R[ickf?] —
at 9.30 — went to Dr Hastings'
& heard him preach — After
that, to Colman's & at 1.30
sat down to a very pleasant
dinner — A. F. Bellows, Geo &
I the guests — At 3.30 I left.
walked up to 128 E. 71 S. & saw
A. Fredericks about Catalogue
cover — then to Will's & from there
to Uncle A's where I had tea —
at 8.15 walked down to studio.
wrote to Kate — to Kate Riley
to W. Dodd & to Jimmie & at
10.40 walked to 37 S. & got
to bed shortly after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 13. 1879.

Still overcast, but late in the
afternoon it cleared pleasantly.
Nearly 10 when I got to studio.
to work on 10×20 & stuck to it
until 1 o'clk. When my pupils
came — near 2 I went out &
got my dinner & divided my
p.m. between my work
& my pupils — at near 6 I
went to Vienna Bakery — tea
in studio at 9 — got things
somewhat cleared up for
meeting of Etching Club in
the eveng. Tea at 7. Ready
at 8 but meeting not called to
order until after 9 — owing to absence
of members — by that time a quorum
adjourned after 10 — 11 when I
started for 37 S. & 11.45 when I
got to bed.
