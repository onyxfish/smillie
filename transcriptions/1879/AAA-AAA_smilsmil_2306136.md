## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 28. 1879

Up early — Dark & foggy — got to
S. Cloud at 7.45 — Some time before us
had bkfs. & after & before I started
for studio — Made Reduction of fortung
then to N.A. worked there until
after 1 — then to lunch & after
2 again at N.A. & worked
until dark — Weather cleared
& very mild — feel tired out
Rested awhile & had tea at
7 — went at studio's making
final arrangement of pages
of illustrations in catalogue.
To 37 St at 10.30, to 37 S. at 11
& Right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 29. 1879

Splendid day — Got to studio
at 9.30 — then to N.A. made two
sketches — at 1.30 had lunch &
at 2 went down town — to
Photo-eng. Co. first — then over to
Harper Bros. from there down to
Sackett's & from there again to
Photo-eng. Co. Thought that I had
finally got my illustrations arr —
but one by Walter Brown turned
up when not expected & threw out
all of my plans, so again I went
to Sackett's — much perplexed —
To studio's at 6 — Tea about 7 —
then determined to make two
more sketches & arranged a
new page. — At 10, at S. Cloud —
Mr. & Mrs. Varians visiting father &
mother — there until near 11, then
to 37 S. & to bed —
