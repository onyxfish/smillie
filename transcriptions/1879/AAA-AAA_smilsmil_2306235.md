## Left Page

### August, Monday 18, 1879

Another day, divided between fog & rain, but milder than yesterday. Finished my letter to [illegible] before b'kf'st. After b'kf'st on the piazza until after, trying to get my exercise — then to work on S.A. Bridge for A.B.N. Co. — Rev. Jos. May called about noon & we had a pleasant hour. Dinner at 2. After that more piazza walking & then to work. Finished drawing about 5 o'clk — very heavy rain, so I rigged myself in rubber suit & went out along shore for about an hour & enjoyed it immensely. Had a rest on my return & tea soon after 9. Eveg. most of it on piazza — a short time in the parlor where charades & games were being played. — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 19, 1879

Day opened like yesterday with wind & rain, but before noon there was quite clear — wind backed around & blew [illegible] from the west. I commenced on A.B.N. Co. drawing Santiago de Chile — about noon I went along shore & for a couple of hours enjoyed the fine surf & blowing rock. Then & after a shore rest had dinner at about 2.20 — after that, some piazza walking then again to work until 5 o'clk. outlining drawing — then called 9 [illegible] with [illegible] — then walked until after sundown & had tea at 9.15 — at 8 a ventriloquist entertainment in dancing room — very fair ventriloquist — at 9 dancing — at 10 to bed.
