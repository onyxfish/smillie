## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 15, 1879

Charming day — Bkfs. about
8 — then up town with Ge. to
Mam's — back home with Matt
& Kate. Some excitement in
the street over annual parade
of Fire Deb. Unpacked much
& did little else during the
rest of mtf. Dinner at 12.30.
Ge. went to NY. for the season
on 1.20 train. After dinner
I took a walk & then the rest
of the afternoon wrote letters,
then walked up town later &
went with Father to Mam's.
Tea at 6 — Aunt Jessie with us.
Everf. Reading until 10
o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 16, 1879

Clear, hot day — Bkfs. at 8. After
that, a walk, then some
loafing & at last to work,
mounted W.C. drawing of
Rafe's Chasm — then planning
remaining designs for Bruce.
Dinner at 12.30, P.M.
about in jussing & planning.
About 5 went to walk with
Father — went to Mam's —
back to tea at 6.30. Everf.
wrote a letter to Charl & at
last went to P.O. with it —
To bed about 10.30.
