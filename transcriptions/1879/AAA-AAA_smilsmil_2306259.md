## Left Page

### October, Sunday 5, 1879

Charming hazy, dreamy Oct.
day. After a thorough grooming
had bkfst. at 9 o'clk. Then to
my hill & dreamed as I
watched the golden shower
of leaves float from the tree
tops across the violet blue of
the sky. At 10.45 went to the
Baptist Ch. & heard Mr. Sherwood
preach from 2° v. of 3° Epistle of
John. After service went with
Uncle Wm. to his bro. Hy's — no one
at home. Dinner at 1.30 then
to my lot to meet Wheatley —
back before 4 & went to bed,
very wretched — got up at 6.30
had tea & lay on sofa until 9.30
then to bed, fevered & sore all
over with a splitting head-
ache.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 6, 1879

warm, smoky day. Up at 7.30
after a tolerable nights rest and
feeling much better. After bkfst.
went to my lot & found Wheatley's
mr. at work clearing up about
my trees — after giving him di-
rections &c. to house, harnessed
horse. Having written to father &
Gibson, went down town, met M.J.S.
went off & rode until about
12.30 — Part of the time the
heat was oppressive. Dinner
at 1.30 — all the P.m. on my
place superintending distributing
manure. Mrs. Agur & [illegible]
joined me with a call while on
the piazza of W.J.S. Tea at 6.30
after that went down town and
called on [illegible] Crandall —
& [illegible] Stroud, & on Mr. & Mrs. Wm.
Mulford. To bed soon after
10.
