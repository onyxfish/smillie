## Left Page

### March, Sunday 23, 1879

fresh, cool & overcast — after
blkp., to Studio & at 11 to Dr.
Hastings & heard him preach
from text "Labor to enter into
that Rest" — After service walked
walked home with Miss Rodge's
— went in for a few minutes & saw
some of her work in Water Colors.
Then up to B.N.V's & had dinner
at 2 o'clk — After 5 when I
walked down to studio — at 6
went in to Colneaus & through
the form of taking tea — at 8
to Emma J's — She was out —
then to studio & wrote a short
note to father — At 9 called
at Sanford Gifford's & found
them in bed — then to 37th
St. & got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 24, 1879

Overcast most of the day, with
wind from the east. Up early
& at 9. 45 at Sturtevant Ho. & at
8 had blkf. with fam. Ben &
Dr. M. — left there at 9.30 and
went to Park Theatre & bought
6 tickets for Wed. [illegible] — Then
to studio — very stupid about
the head — Tried to get on —
started on Jake Geo. work for
Harper's — but could do nothing
Miss Amie Putnam called in
the wtf; no lunch at 2.30 — Nash
Wilson called in the afternoon.
Avery sent me his check for $117 —
Tea at 1 — at 8.30 called on Mary
Whitney at Mrs. Sturgis — Cor. 36 S. & Park
Ave — Abq. 15 left — to 37th St. — dressed
& went to Musical Party at Mrs.
Ireland's — 30 W. 37th St; to bed
at 11, 30.
