## Left Page

### August, Thursday 28. 1879.

Delightful day - after bkfo-
went to Thayer's Cottage and
with "all hands" went to the wharf
to see Mr. & Mrs. Howland off on the
Lewiston — Tremendous bustle —
many people leaving — At 10.30
Emma & Kitty, Mr. Green, Geo. & I
went sailing, to go to "the Ovens"
almost as soon as we got away
a calm fell & for an hour we
drifted in sight of town — then a
little breeze sprung up — at 12
we determined that we could not
reach the ovens & be back in time
so we put about & got to the dock
abt. 30 — then dinner & before 3
Kitty, Emma & Geo. & I got away
on a buckboard for Schooner Head
and Great Head — me spent [illegible] m
at the Spouting Horn & someone came
at Schooner Head & at the cliffs on
Great Head — noble red birches near
Great Head — met Miss Woodward
there — Rode back to Bar Harbor
in the twilight — Sea about 7
lovely, very tired — made a short
call on Miss Woodward at the
Rockaway & then to bed before
10 oclk.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 29. 1879.

Delicious day — but my head
bothered me very much. After bkfo
went to Thayer Cottage & arranged
with the girls for a trip to Otter
Cliff this P.M. Then to Photograph
to & spent some time there
selecting photos — to [illegible] my
sketches — then to hotel & for
two hours was on my bed. Then
dinner at 1, & at 2.30 went
to the girls & at 3 started on
buckboard for Otter Cliffs — the
ride there very charming and
the cliffs the finest I have
seen — overcast sky & as trucks
had to be packed we started
on our return soon after 5 — Sea
soon after 6. At 8 went to Photo
[illegible] & met Emma & sister there
looked over photos — then to Prof.
Thayer's — some Rain falling —
back to "Newport" shortly after 9 &
to bed soon after 10.
