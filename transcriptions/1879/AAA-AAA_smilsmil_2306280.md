## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 18, 1879

Miserable dark day with at times, heavy rains — Got to
studio at 9.30, then to N.A., & got
& brought over Boughton, Myatt
& Homer — was much bothered
to see — had lunch soon after
1 & at 4 o'clk. had finished
over the Boughton & Bryant.
Then went to Grecia's — 74
Beck man St, & saw 2d. from
of Yosemite in press — then to
67 Park Place. Photo. ing. & saw
moss — then to studio & with
Ge?, at 6, went to Ashland Ho.
& had dinner — back to studio,
stretched 3 oil studies — at 8.30
went to the Shurtleff's room —
there for an hour, then to 37[illegible]
& to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 19, 1879

Pleasant, crisp mff. clouding
over later in the day, closing with
a fine sunset. Got to studio about
10 & to work with pen — stuck to it
until 2 when I went out & had
lunch — back at 3 & worked until
4.30 when I had finished
Winslow Homer, James Hart &
[illegible] of Barnard — then down
to Avery's with what I had fin-
ished — Mr. Ge?. Whitney came
in while there — then to Century
with Mr. Case of Baltimore there
& took our eve'g meal together —
had a pleasant time until near
8 then to studio & at 8.30 to
Water Color meeting at N.A., there
until 10.15 — Ge? dined with Bishop
Putnam — to 37[illegible] S. & to bed at
11[illegible].
