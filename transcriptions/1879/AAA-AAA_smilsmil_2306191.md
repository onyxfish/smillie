## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 20, 1879

Still heavy & overcast — after
bleft, went to August Belmont's
& arranged to copy his Jacques.
Then to studio — wrote several
letters — then prepared to
make design for Anthony (A.N.S.)
for Miles Standish — Before I
got out to lunch, W. Hus. Harvey
of Buffalo came in — bought a
little etching — Lunch at 2.30
P.M. Made sketch for Miles
Standish ill. Tea about
7 — About 8, went with Geo.
& called on Dr. H.W. Bellows — found
him & Mrs. B. at home & disengaged.
— a very pleasant even'g until
near 10 — stopped in at Century
for a few minutes, then to 37 S.
& to bid at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 21, 1879

Another heavy, threatening mf.
After blcfo — called on the Colemans
to see if Manhattan Beach plans
for the day were to be carried out.
— no — then to studio — wrote some
notes — worked a little on Miles
Standish design, then commenced
a pen sketch of Roger Rock house
for Treadway — At 1 went down to
Harper Bros. & saw Mrs. [illegible] make
Geo. article — then to Sharpe's &
to A.B.N. Co — back to studio at
2 — had lunch & then to work
on Treadway sketch — Tea at
7 — Even'g with Geo. at Coleman's
to 37 S. — to bid at 11 — A
pleasant change in atmosphere
— Thunderstorms to the north.
