## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 15, 1879

An exceedingly hot & uncomfortable day — After bkfst I worked over some pfps. that I had taken from the Koehler plates and at 10.15 went up to town, back at 11 & tried to get at my etching but had not enough energy. Uncle W. & During again for printing — Dinner at 12.30 — after dinner got started on my etching, but very uncomfortable as I could not get a light to suit me — Had it in the acid once — Tea at 6 — Mr. Nivans to tea with us. After tea get started for N.Y. by the Propeller — there to go on "Down East". I went with Mr. W. & Kate to Mary's — May & Mrs. W. to go to N.Y. to-morrow. Home at 9.15 — Read Mr. Burroughs awhile & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 16, 1879

Infernally hot — in a languid way spent most of the mng. fixing my pfps. to send to Koehler — did a very little at my etching — dinner at 12.30, on my back until near 3 — then again at my etching, but work without spirit — my etching will scarcely show it — after tea I packed pfps. & wrote letter to Koehler, then took one to Sup. Office & the other to P.O., after that went to Prof. Ritter's & arranged to go on a trip with him to-morrow. About 4 o'clk — a tremendous storm of thunder, rain & hail came — others followed — the air was made very much cooler. Home at 9.30 — to bed at 10.30.
