## Left Page

### June, Sunday 15, 1879

Very close hot day — Up late & headachy — bkfst. at 9.30 —
At 10.30 to Studios & spent the most there, in big chair and
trying to straighten things to
leave for the summer. After
service Geo. came & at 2 we
went to Smith & Green's Rest.
& had dinner — then I ret. to
Studio & during the p.m. did
some more picking up — Soon
after 4 a thunderstorm with
very violent Rain passed over —
About 8 I went to the Ashland
Hts & had some Strawberries &
Rice — again to Studio, sat
for an hour with Shurtleff &
his wife & then some visiting —
Another thunderstorm threatening
soon after 10 I started for 37t St —
got there only a few minutes
before it came rushing down.
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 16, 1879

Beautiful breezy day — After bkfst.
to Rainier's, then to drug shop, then
to 37t St. then to Dr. Marcy's & saw
the gentleman who bought Owl's Head —
then at near 11 to studio — most
of the remainder of the mtt. spent
in arranging for the sending away
to Chicago, Cincinnati & St. Louis of
my pictures — Lunch at 2 — Most
of the afternoon passing in news-
papers, Rugs & things that moths
might get at. At 6.45 went to
Kimball's to tea — back to Studio
at 8.45 — fixed up some etchings
for Chicago. To 37t St. & to bed
about 11. [illegible — ~4 lines]
