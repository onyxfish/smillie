## Left Page

### December, Monday 22. 1879

Dismal day, divided between snow, sleet &
rain — After 10 when I got to studio, having
[illegible] Renners, ordered frame for
large W.C. drawing — at studio
charcoaled sketch for it.
Dinner at 2. Finished
sketch. Tea at 4 — Ever
mounting some of the
original Jake [illegible] Illus.
used by Harpers — getting
them ready for [illegible]
possible sale. To 37. [illegible]
at 10.30 — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 23. 1879

Overcast day — miserable overhead, horrible under
foot. Got to studio at
9.45. Set charcoal sketch
& Bartley made tracing
— Some time spent over the
mounting of drawings for
room of the Salmagundi
Club. Dinner at 2 — after
that to Renners & Voigt's —
242 Canal St. had [illegible]
taken of my moonlight etching
then took my [illegible] & we [illegible]
Appleton's — left plate &
[illegible] there — then to
the shoemaker & was measured
for pair of boots — abs to studio
— Tea at 4 — Ever[?]. Hard at
work [illegible] away on blk.
& white drawings — Jake [illegible]
Illus. getting them ready for
[illegible] — to 37. S. — & to bed
about 11.
