## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 17. 1879.

Heavy showers with wind in the m'g. - after b'k'f. - started out for a walk but driven back by the rains. After 11 when I got to work on Yosemite, having written to Kochler & Will. Worked on Yosemite falls until after 12.30 - then to P.O. & got a letter from Anna Cook. — Then walked around the miles square & had dinner at 1.15. After that until 3 awaiting departure of Dr. Saville & family — then again to my work, finished Yo's falls & almost finished Cathedral Rock. About 5 o'clk. went out horseback riding (on Mollie) with Miss Sightner & Woodbury. Rode to Glen Station & back at 6.15. Rubbed down & tea about 7. Evening in parlor & playing whist with Mr. Roberts until near 9.30 then to Thom Mr. H's — later, again to Thom Mr. H's & to P.O. with Miss Woodbury — back soon after 10 & chatted in parlor with Miss Sightner until 11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 18. 1879.

Charming m'g. feeling a little sore from yesterday's horseback ride. I did not take my walk after b'k'f. but rested on my bed. About 11. Mrs. Sightner & daughter & Mr. Mjukoolp & family left for their homes. Then I went to work on pen sketch of El Capitaine. Dinner at 1 — after that more resting & quite a nap — then again to work, finished El Capitain, planned other illt's & at 5 went out & walked around the miles square with Geo. & Mr. Roberts — the weather overcast & threatening rain which came in slight showers later in the ev'g. Tea at 6.30 — Ev'g two games with Mr. Roberts — then to Thom Mr. House & with Mr. Hardenburgh party until 10 — to bed at 10.30.
