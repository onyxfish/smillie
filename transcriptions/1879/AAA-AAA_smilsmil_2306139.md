## Left Page

### February, Monday 3, 1879

Very pleasant day — 10 o'clk when I got to studio — then to work on Scribner drawing "Forests of Silver fir on Moraines of Hoffman & Tueuaya" — worked until shortly after 1 o'clk, Miss Armstrong came but Mr. Lord did not. At 1.30 I went to 37 E. 21st St, in answer to a note from Maud Diven Silsbee then I met Mr. Granbeny thad lunch. Back to studio at 2.30 + divided what remained of the p.m. bet my drawing & Miss A — Tea in studio at 6.30 — latter to part of eve't. at N.A. to studio at 10.15 + to 37 E. 8th St at 11, then right to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 4, 1879

Still pleasant, though at times overcast — again late at studio. made a short call at 8; Claud. + saw Mother + Kate — worked on Scribner drawing — to lunch at 1.30 — got my drawing finished soon after. Kate N. + her sister Mr. White called — At 4.30 I went to Scribner's + for my two drawings got $42.50, then to Gauthier's + to Leavitt's + saw some cups that pleased me — Back to studio at 6.30 Tea at 7 — was going to Mad. Roch's to hear Wallace Bruce on Bryant, but felt too tired — wrote a note to Belle Tweedy + at 9 went to N.A. to 37 E. 8th St. + to bed at 11.
