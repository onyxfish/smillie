## Left Page

### November, Monday 10. 1879

Mild & pleasant. Got to studio soon after 9 & to work with the pen. Stuck to it so that it made my head spin. At 2 went out & had lunch then a short walk & at it again until I could see no longer — then I had finished the Pottas, Decamps and Schreyer. At 5 went to Dr. Yale's — there until about 6.30 — then to studio & had something to eat, then lugged paintings to Avery's then to "Century" — met Geo. & we stayed until after 10. Then to 37 & 38 & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 11. 1879

Mild, dismal day — heavy rain until late in the afternoon — got to studio at 9.30 & to work on pen sketches for Avery's Cat. Found things easier than I did yesterday & so finished over the Picco, Fortuny & Meissonier. Had lunch in studio between 1 & 2, worked until 4.30 — at 5 went with Geo. to Coleman's & had dinner — then to studio & rested & at 7 went to Avery's & at 7.45 to Haverly's Theatre — 14th St. near 6th Ave. Met Fanny K., Matt Kate, Charl. & Geo. & saw "the Pullman Car" — a lot of exceedingly laughable & absurd nonsense. Out at 11, stopped for a short time at the K's, then walked up to 37 & 38 with Charl. & to bed very soon after midnight.
