## Left Page

### December, Sunday 14. 1879

Horrible storm of driving wind & rain — 10 o'clk when
we had bkft. To Dr Hastings at 11 — he announced that in consequence of the storm there would be no afternoon service — To studio at 12.30 — To dinner about 2 — then again to studio & spent much of the remaining P.M. & early in clearing out closets. Had a Roll & cups of Beef Tea at 9 — To 37th St — at 11. & Right to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 15. 1879

Overcast with some rain — Got to studio about 9.30 & tried da Vinci, alternately taking off & then scratching with dry point — many interruptions — dinner at 2.20, then worked until 4.30 but could not quite finish — the weather cleared charmingly fresh & crisp. At 5.30 went to 37th St & changed my dress — then to studio & had tea at 9 — Intended to go to Chas. S. Smillie's, but could not lay my hand upon his address, so went to Mrs. [illegible] at 2.17th St — Emma not in — at 9.45 to h.a. met there the Payne's. They went to my studio with me & there until after 11 — then walked up town with them to 37th St. & to bed at 11.45. A.N.V. & [illegible] Gunther called at the studio this mtg.
