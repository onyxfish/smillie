## Left Page

### May, Thursday 8. 1879.

Delightful weather — Got to studio at 9. 30 — stretched paper on an open backed stretcher & commenced a W. C. drawing 9½ × 17½ — Hay Ricks near [illegible] working on the method of keeping my paper constantly wet at the back. — In many respects I like the plan very much. Lunch after 2 — worked until 6 & about ¼ got to Anna Cook's to tea — Geo & I played escort to the Arnold's house as usual then to Ned Coats, cor. Park. ave & 40th St. — Soon after 9 — to Union League Club — monthly meeting & water color show — a little supper. Sat next to Stephen Tyng Jnr. and had a very good time until after 11 — to 37th St. & to bed at 12.30

## Right Page

### May, Friday 9. 1879.

Still charming weather. — At 9.30 went to August Belmont's — just in time to see him drive away, [illegible] in hand. Then to studio & worked on Hay Ricks — & at 12 again to B's — out — then to Knoedler's — to lunch & back to studio before 2 — Mr. Dart & Hiece there — after they left I worked on Hay Ricks until 5, then again to B's — out — then to Avery's saw his pictures & ret'd to studio. made a note to Knoedler — had tea & at 8 went to Anna C's — walked with her to her bro. Walter's — cor. 5th ave. & 14th St. — there until 9.30 — then walked back to 36th St. — went in & had a discussion. [illegible] being the text, until 10.30 — then to 37th St. & to bed at 11.
