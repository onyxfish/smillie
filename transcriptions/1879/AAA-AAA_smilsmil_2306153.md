## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 5, 1879

Charming spring-like day —
To Mr. Spencer's at 9.30 & worked
all the mf. on sketches of Millet
& Diaz — at 1 went to studio —
then to lunch & back to 44 "s
at 2.15 — worked until 5.15
& finished van Mardke &
Dupré. At 6 to Renner's
then to studio — nearly 8
when I had tea. Spent
the evng until 10.30
in writing & fixing covers
on pen sketches — at 11.15
to 37. St. & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 6, 1879

Mean day — with drizzle
part of time & at 9.30 got to
Mr. Spencer's & spent mf.
until 11.30 on Diaz landscape
then to studio & prepared
for my class. Mrs. Lord —
Mrs. & Miss Witherbee & Miss
Morrison came & I spent
the p.m. until 5 with them.
At 6.30 went to Arnold's to
tea — at 8 left her & Fanny K
at Arnold's — then to studio —
there until 10.30 writing & pasting
covers on pen sketches, then
to 37. St. & to bed, after a short
visit with uncle Willie at
11.30 — Clear moonlight.
