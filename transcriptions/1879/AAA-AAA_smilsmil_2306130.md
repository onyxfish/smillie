## Left Page

### January, Thursday 16, 1879

Old fashioned snowstorm —
heavy fall of snow — Got to
studio at about 10 o'clk — having
made a short call on Miss Wood
ward — Before I could go on with
my work had to finish a back
ground to Nellie G's w.c. — then
at my own work until 1.30
when I went to lunch — much
trouble on my foreground
on 10×20 — have washed it out
three times — Louis Tiffany
called — also Champney and
Pres. Seeley of [illegible] University —
they are buying pictures — I
went to Anna Coale's to tea at 6
She is suffering from cold — so took
Fanny K. to Miss Arnold's — then to
studio — there until after 10. Trying
to arrange all Cat. Then to 34th
St — very tired & nervous & to bed
after 11 — but not to sleep
until midnight

## Right Page

### January, Friday 17, 1879

Bright m'g, but overcast later
in the day — Got to studio at 9.30
& spent the m'g on catalogue
matters — writing notes to "The
Graphic" — to Photo. engravers &
others — making lists & determining
[illegible] — Sent first batch of Sketches
to Photo. Eng. Co. & at noon got at
my own works — finished up
on 10×20 — & started pen sketch
large drawing — Mrs. Forbes &
others called — did not go out
for my every walk but rested
on lounge — Tea at 6.30 — Eveg
at studio until 10.30 — mounting
& putting into frames small w.
drawings — then to 34th St. &
to bed at 11.45
