## Left Page

### September, Sunday 25, 1887

Still raw, windy & smoky — a little more sunny than yesterday. Up late. Before dinner wrote letters then went out & had a walk around the Warner lot & top of the hill. Dinner before 2 so that the "domestics" could go out "chestnutting". Again wrote before tea — Annie quite used up by severe headache so that she had to go to bed before tea — Jim was just as cross & unreasonable as he could be, so that at a time things were very uncomfortable. Eve'g. wrote a long letter to Ehninger. After 11 o'clk. when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 26, 1887

Cold, but in all unpleasantness, a modification of the preceeding days. I got a fire started in the furnace of studio & worked there upon the carving for mantle piece. Payne & team — Judson & Will. Cuny worked clearing up the last of the stone heaps in pasture — went down Town about noon. In the afternoon again worked on the cherry carving and at to again went down Town. Eve'g. Read newspaper & wrote a letter to Uncle Willie in answer to letter from him asking me to make a drawing for his Canadian Co. Beautiful, luminous moonlight night. To bed about 11.
