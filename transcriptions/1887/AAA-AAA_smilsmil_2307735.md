## Left Page

### August, Thursday 18, 1887

Quite a heavy wind & rain storm about 2 A.M. The day opened showery & windy but gradually cleared very pleasant. Hotchkiss & Judson got to work in the mt. O'Brien &
team & Battles in the afternoon —
I worked all the mt making the little changes suggested by Mr.
Huntington on the Durand plate & took some final proofs before dinner. Afternoon I took Remarque off plate, packed it & expressed it to Putnams. Looked after the men until 4.30 — then down town with Jim — Had my hair cut at the barbers — Tea before 7 —
Eveg. wrote a letter to Kimmel & Voigt — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 19, 1887

Very pleasant day — after seeing the men at their work — Thomas & team Battles, Blair, Judson & Hotchkiss —
I went down town after 10 o'clk.
got horses & phaeton at Parego's
stable — It was 11.30 when we left
the house — Annie, Kate, Jim & I —
we went by Hart Lake — through the
woods to the Summerville road and
home over Mulford's hill, getting to the
house at 1 o'clk. Dinner as soon as
I could go to stable with team & back
Smith came before I finished and
I spent the afternoon with him in
preparing the colors & experimenting
upon the gable of pantry & wash Room.
Eveg. wrote two business notes for
Annie to 2d. Nat. Bnk. & Wilson & Toms
[illegible] Co — Rec'd Winter & Smillie
telegram saying that Charl goes to
Amsterdame on Monday.
