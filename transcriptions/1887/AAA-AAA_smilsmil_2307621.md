## Right Page

### January, Saturday 1, 1887

Rain in the m'g. Thawing —
freezing before night — a dismal
sort of day — To studio about 10 &
spent the m'g on the bat drawing
making some changes in the figure
& fussing generally — near 2
when I got home to lunch — &
after 3 when I got back to studio.
Chase came in soon after &
sat with me until after 5 — then
we walked up town, meeting Geo.
who turned & went with us up
to Chase's rooms — Home to dinner
soon after 6. Even'g. reading
& to bed before 11 — Jim's
cold does not get better — he
coughs a good deal and is
restless at night.
