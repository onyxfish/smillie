## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 9, 1887

Bright & pleasant — 10.30 when I got to studio — worked awhile on Statue of Liberty & N.Y. Bay for A.B.N. Co. A little after 2 went to N.A.D. To see Mr. Greer about sale of etchings — then with camera to cor of 23° S. & B'way — where I got half a dozen snaps at street cars & horses — Home to lunch about 1.30 — back to studio & worked on Liberty Statue sketch — About 3.30 Geo. came in with W. T. Richards & we three sat & chatted until dusk — Home to dinner at a little after 6 — Evef. Maney commenced to read aloud to Mamie but she feel so desperately sleepy that she had to go to bed & at 9 I went to N.A.of D. For a little while in the Social Meeting, in the Library — Too much tobacco smoke for me, so I beat a retreat to the galleries. Home soon after 10 — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 10, 1887

Pleasant day — at times overcast. To studio soon after 10 — For a time dampening etching plts. &ounting them in press — Then commenced Tracing plate S.A. view for A.B.N. Co. Home to lunch at 1.45 — at 3 went over to look at studios in new bldg. cor. of 45° S. & B'dway — one pleased me — Rent $800 — Then to studios at [illegible] of big bldg cor. 33° S. & B'dway — studios too small — and too high — To studio at 4.15 — worked on A.B.N. Co. Tracing — Home before 6 — Jim quite out of sorts again — at 6.30 — at Mr. Gibbons — 55 W. 47° S. — To dinner to meet Mr. & Mrs. W. T. Richards & Miss Fidelia Bridges — After dinner looking at studies by W.T.R. & F.B. — At 9.30 we left & went to Duffale's to see him about Jim — he was not in — Then home & to bed soon after 10.
