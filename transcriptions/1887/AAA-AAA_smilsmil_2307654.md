## Left Page

### March, Monday 7, 1887

Sttle dismal, dark & foggy —
It was not until 10.30 when I
got to studio's — Some time
clearing up & getting ready for
a start again upon Academy
picture "A meadow Pond" — I
repainted middle distant —
hill side — Home to lunch at
1.45 — back at 3 & painted
until 5 getting the whole work
into more harmonious relations —
Dinner at 6.30 — Evely wrote
several notes & took them
out to mail — About 9 Charle
came in. Stayed until after
10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 8, 1887

A bright, pleasant day at last —
To studio's soon after 10 & to work
on "A meadow Pond" — painting
over the sheep. Went with
Waller into his studio & looked
about with a view to taking it
as he has determined to give
it up. At 12.30 went to Bank &
made a deposit — then to Richard's
— then to Newman's — then to Moore's
gallery — then to Heaton's & ordered
a pair of black trousers — then home
to lunch at 1.40 — After that wrote
a note to Berrians, proposing to
take Waller's studio & sending check
for rent to No. 1 — also sent check to
Payne — Essex Co. I was paying taxes
at Keene Valley Plat — To studio's at
3 — painted & worked on other
things until after 6 — then home
to dinner — Wely Annie read
aloud from "Tom Brown at Rugby"
until 10 — then to bed.
