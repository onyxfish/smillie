## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 30, 1887

Cold, windy day — Spent the morning at Studio — color man cleaning up the woodwork & coloring walls in closets. The carpenter came at 10.30 & got to work putting up door casings — I worked at clearing things out of one place into another to give the men a chance to work. Home — lunch at 1 — Afternoon worked on A.B.N. Co. drawing S.A. Street Scene with Water Cart. at 4.30 went to Studio to see how things were progressing — then walked down to 34th S — around Union Sq., up 5th Ave. & home before 6. Dinner at 6.30 Evening looking over back Nos. of Ill. London News — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 1, 1887

Clear & cold. To Studio in the m'f & found carpenter & two color men at work — watched them for a time & saw that their progress was provokingly slow — then to bank & made deposit for Annie — then to A.A.A. galleries & spent an hour looking over the new exh'n. Did not feel much stimulated. Home at about noon — lunch at 1 — worked on A.B.N. Co's drawing. S.A. Street Scene with Water Cart. until 4.30 — then could see no longer — To 37th S — for Jim — met Annie there — then to Studio — then to O'Neil's for photographs of Mr. J.S. Rives — then to W.S. & went into furniture stores looking for a child's high chair — Home at 6 — dinner at 6.30 — Well Chit. Collins came in & stayed until a little after 10 — To bed about 11.
