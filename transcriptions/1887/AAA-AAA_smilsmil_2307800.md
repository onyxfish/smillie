## Left Page

### December, Monday 26, 1887

Overcast day with snow at night. Up with a headache. Bp. rather late — It was near 11 o'clk. before I got my marketing & shopping done & to the studio to work. — I made little progress owing to the condition of my head. Home to lunch at 1 — After that gave up & went to bed — there until 5 — then up & took charge of the baby, giving Annie a chance to get her rest. — Eveth. about 7, we had dinner. Geo. Cook & wife & Walter Cook & his wife being our guests. Later we read from "The Pinky" & looked over Emma's Pictorial Record of Adirondack camp life — To bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 27, 1887

Bright & beautiful day — got to studio a little before 10 & to work on Water Color drawing that I shall christen "Feeding by the Wayside" — I introduced three cows feeding along the road side — Home at 1.30 — the decorators have made a start on dining room ceiling — back to studio at 2.45 — & again on drawing but was interrupted by the carpenters who came to fix my closet & by a call from Cab. & Mr. Meuse (?) — (Miss Scott) made paste & mounted piece of drawing paper — then to Lord & Saycors & home at 6 — dinner — Eveth. most of it, helping Annie over the balancing of her Diet Kitchen accts. Worked awhile on drawings for Herrick — for my studio's closet — To bed about 11.
