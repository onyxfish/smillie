## Left Page

### June, Friday 3, 1887

A pleasant day, with clouds & sunshine. Although there was much work to be done I felt tired & rested & even napped — Trunks were unpacked & things put in order. I wandered over the place & renewed my acquaintance with out of the way corners of the place — Eve'g, cool enough to enjoy a pleasant wood fire — It was about 11 when we got to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 4, 1887

Glorious day — a marvel of brilliancy & warmth — all the day Annie was out on the piazza & at night, the moonlight was so fascinating that we sat out until after 9 o'clk — Then indoors and read awhile before going to bed.
