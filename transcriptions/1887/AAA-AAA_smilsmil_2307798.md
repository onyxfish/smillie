## Left Page

### December, Thursday 22, 1887

Bright, cold day — Douglass came to the house at 9 o'clts. with a decorator to look at dining room ceiling & after consultation it was agreed to begin work upon it next Tues. To studio after 10 & got to work upon Water Color study from which I painted last Spring Academy picture: "The Meadow Pond" — I will make material changes in composition — worked on it as I have not done on any W.C. before — scribbling it nearly out & then working upon it while the surface is wet. Home to lunch abt — detained X was bundles — near 3 when I got back to studio — worked on drawing until 4:15 — then fussing with curtains — home at 6. — Eveth. Edtd. Cook, Walter & Maine came in — Annie worked on my studio curtains — so did I — and I packed a small [illegible] for [illegible] Keepin & also took [illegible] to the Edtp. Office — To bed about 11.

At 3.30 Am. Jim gets us up with his "croupy cough".

## Right Page

### December, Friday 23, 1887

Bright, ins[?] day — To studio about 10 to work on Water Color — Put in some trees on extreme right and after working all day over them came to the conclusion that the composition was not well printed there by — Home abt & back before 3 — worked on drawing until 4 — then put up some of the new curtains — At 6 went to Adams' [illegible] Office with China copy of Porto [illegible] a late wedding present to Mrs. D. Wm. Murray (May Tiffany) — then bought a small Jap. leather card case X mas present for Annie — Home at 6.30 — Eveth. Charles came in & spent an hour with us — I sat & read until 11 — then to bed.

Jim seems to be only fairly well to-day.
