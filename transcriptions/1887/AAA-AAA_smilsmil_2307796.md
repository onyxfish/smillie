## Left Page

### December, Sunday 18, 1887

Several inches of snow fell during the night — day, dull & raw — Bkf't late — Read most of the mff and spent some time looking over and burning up old letters — Sly & Howe came to dinner at about 2 — Part of the afternoon I drew some plans for a small closet that I wish Herrick to make for me. At 5 Annise & I went to 2. 17th St. & called upon Aunt Anna Irelands — Home soon after 6 — Tea — Westf, Geo., Nellie and Chase spent the evg with us — Chase stayed until near 11 — Before I got to bed No. 2 got into a tantrum & I took him until mid night — then to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 19, 1887

Brilliant day — To studio at 9.30 & to work on India ink drawing of portrait of Mr. Rives — Home to lunch & back as usual — worked on drawing until 4.30 — then could see no longer — then to Stokes & Bo.? as requested in a note from the firm — wish we have an etching in their next year's book — came to no conclusion as they are not willing to give more than $100 — then walked down B'way, went into Vantine's & Jap. trading stores — bought small laquer tray. Home about 6.30 — dinner. Westf. at work on closet plans commenced yesterday. To bed about 10.30.
