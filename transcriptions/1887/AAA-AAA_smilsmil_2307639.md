## Left Page

### February, Saturday 5, 1887

Cold, Raw & overcast — after 10 when I got to studio — For some time worked on the Maunnforde Rose brought up 11¼×24½ frame for Marblehead Neck sketches & as the painting looked very well I sent it down to Century with my "Quiet day on the French Coast." Home to lunch & back as usual found a line from Greer — has sold 4 Ps. of Voice from Cliff — went to N.A. & saw him — then back. Will came in — begs me to do work for A.B.N. Co. Then Ed. came in with a fair report from home. I mounted some etching pf. Home at 6 — At 7. with A. to Ed.w? Cook's to dinner — Mr. & Mrs. Sprague & Mr. & Mrs. Delano there — At 9 took Mr. Sprague to Century with me — good time. At 11.30 put him into 3d. Ave. car — then walked up with Yale — Hills & Gifford — Home at 12 — To bed, but not to sleep until after 1.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 6, 1887

milder but Raw, overcast and nasty. Bkft. after 9 — Read for awhile — then wrote letter to mother — at 12 walked down to Century where Annie joined me — there for some time — Home at 2 — dinner — between 4 & 5 had a nap — at 6. we left Jim with Kate, the cook — & went to Mary Willard's to tea where we met Prof Compton & wife — Miss Wilson did not put in an appearance. Pleasant evening — until 9 — then home — wrote notes to Geo. & Charl. — & to bed before 11.
