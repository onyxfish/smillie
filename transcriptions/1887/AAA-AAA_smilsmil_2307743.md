## Left Page

### September, Saturday 3, 1887

Cloudy mtf., clearing pleasantly.
O'Brien & team at work — also Payne
& Freane — Ambrose Payne — Battles. Willie
Wood & Willie Curry — Later I had Coyle
come down & help them rake up stones.
The smiths painted all day. Herrick
put down weather mouldings on the
piazza — I went down town about
11 — at 12 called at G. Fordhams &
saw him — went into his gardens &
saw his fruit trees & berry bushes —
Dinner at 1 — afternoon with the
men — Down town before 8. Eve'g
at home — reading — to bed
at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 4, 1887

Superb day — Ther. fell to 43° last night.
Bkfs. after 9 — soon after 10 I went to
Small's livery stables & got horses & wagon
& Kate. Annie, Jim & I had one of the
most delightful of drives down this south
road, turning at D. Norris's, across to Elk
Lake & thence home over Butterfield's
hill, getting in at 2 °c. Dinner at
2.30 — Kate was unable to take her dinner
— a glass of cream at the creamery being too
much for her — Afternoon, I wrote a
letter to Charl. — Taking dinner so
late I did not take any tea —
Eve'g, a little writing — Took
letters to P.O. & to bed at 10.
