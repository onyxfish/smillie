## Left Page

### February, Sunday 27, 1887

A bright, blustering march day, falling colder at ever[y] and freezing solidly at night. Had help between 9 & 10. Then Reading for some time — about noon started out very cautiously & by taking it very slowly I managed to get down to studio — very groggy on my pins — about 1.30 where I got home & found Will there. He stayed only for a few minutes. Dinner soon after 2 — then wrote my letter to Mother — Just as I finished it Charl came in — at 5.30 he walked up to G.C.D. with me. Tea at 6.30 — Eve[ning] some reading — more writing until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 28, 1887

Very cold, clear, windy day — about 10 went to G.C.D. — & saw Webb. V.P. Sleeping Car Co. about A.B.N. Co. V.P. Then struggled against the wind over to 8th Ave. & 42d St. to look at the bldg. that Mr. Ernest Flagg proposes to transform into "the Clinton" with studios — don't like it. Then home & took half an hours rest — then down Lex. Ave. & looked at bldg. cor. of 27th St. Then to N.A. of D. & left some etching [plates]. Then to studio's & saw Geo. Home about 1.30 — To studio at 3 — Sam. Coleman made quite a call — Finished the mounting of some etching [plates]. Then had to wait until the man finished the bringing up of ½ a ton of coal — Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eve[ning] Charl came in — after he left I worked on some plans for a proposed bldg. cor. 27th St. & Lex. Ave. — To bed after 11.
