## Left Page

### August, Saturday 6, 1887

Overcast m'f, growing cooler
& clearer — by ev'f. It was
charming. All day I stuck
to my Durand etching closely
— taking a proof — then working.
I took four proofs during the
day — most of my troubles being
on the face, in getting a likeness
— Ev'f. went down town about
8. Later, we sat in Kate's bed
room & read until almost
10 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 7, 1887

A glorious clear, cool day. Ther
Ther. last night registered 52° &
at 8 o'clk. when I got up, it stood
at 58°. Jim & I spent the m'f
in the new pasture lot, weeding.
It was a pleasure to be out of
doors. Dinner soon after 2 —
The afternoon, much of the time
I was fixing the vines on the South
front of the house — Mr. & Mrs. [illegible]
Courtright called — Ev'f. Annie
came down stairs for the first
time since her confinement.
Took tea & spent the ev'f
with us until 9.30. I wrote
a letter to Gel. About 10 o'clk
went down to P. O. Then to bed.
