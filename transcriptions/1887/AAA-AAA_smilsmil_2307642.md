## Left Page

### February, Friday 11, 1887

Jim down with measles —

Mild. southerly winds — showers in the
afternoon — In the evg. after a restless
night, Jim gave unmistakable signs
of the measles & by night they were
fully out. Poor little fellow bears his
discomfort with wonderful patience.
To studio soon after 10 & worked on
Potosí drawing for A.B.N. Co. Home
abt 1 — Jim's face is a picture —
back at 3 & worked on Potosí —
Home at 6 — dinner — The eveg
spent in transferring our quarters
to upstairs, so that Jim can be
in hospital quarters in the nursery
— Annie sleeps beside him & I now
my bed in the next room — To bed
soon after 10 —
Clear night & high wind.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 12, 1887

Cold & clear — Jim's measles
progressing regularly & favorably.
To studio about 10.30 & to work on
A.B.N. Co's drawing "Potosí" — Home
at 1.30 — back at 3 & worked until
5 but did not succeed in finishing
drawing — Then to U.S. f.S. & saw
Greer who begs me to let him
have more pt. 1" "rice from Cliff"
He has already sold six at $12.00
each, unframed — I will not sell
any more — Home soon after 6 —
Eveg. until 9.30 reading — Then
a game of cards & to bed at 10.30.
