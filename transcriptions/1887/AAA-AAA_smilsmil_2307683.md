## Left Page

### May, Friday 6, 1887

Cooler — overcast during most of the
day — got up with a headache, made
uncomfortable by it all day — To studio
at 10 — worked on Po'k. Bridge — At
11 Mr. Cone came & I telegraphed to
Will for blue print of bridge — wrote to
Herrick about Parke's offer to hire him.
About noon went to exhibit of Old London
Street to see 3 paintings by Mignot —
by invitation — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
After that had a nap & feeling better
at 3.30 I went by appointment to
Dr. Allen, 155 W 47th St — & he took an
imp. flower [illegible] — At 5 walked
down to studio & home at 6 —
dinner about 7 — Reading until
9 when Charl came in — Stayed
until 10.30 — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 7, 1887

Dull, Raw day — Rain for a short time
about 2.30 — again after 10 p.m. — After
[illegible] — to butchers — then to Dr. Allen's —
155 W. 47th St — & he took my "bite" —
then to studio before 11 — To work on
Po'k Bridge drawing. Cone came in
before noon — & gave me the benefit
of his suggestions — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — At 2.30 went to 42nd St & 6th —
and thence by Elevated R.R. to Am.
[illegible] Note Co. with drawings of Po'k.
Bridge & large locomotive — There
for some time — then to Walter Cook's
got a roll of [illegible] wall paper,
then to studio & then home at 6 —
dinner — [illegible] Charl came
in for a little while — The news from
Mother is depressing & I propose
to go to Po'k. to-morrow — To bed
at 11.
