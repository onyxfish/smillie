## Left Page

### November, Friday 18, 1887

Overcast w[eather], but again the weather cleared charmingly — I went to studio at 10 & met coming Mr. Cornick by appointment and arranged to have a paperhanger commence work there next Monday. Then to Bank — It was noon when I got home — Lunch at 1 — Then I set to work on Ruppert's Beer Trade Mark drawing for B.M.Co. & worked on it until 4.30, when I could see no longer. Thence to Studio — home at 6 — dinner before 7 — Eve'g at 8 went to the Calvary Bible lecture alone, Annie being too tired & sleepy — This eve'g it was in the Church building — after the lesson — went to the Germania Lager Bier Saloon — 17th S. & 3rd Ave. with Mr. Sprague, Ed. Murray Cook, & had some beer — there walked up home with them — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 19, 1887

A wet day — sometimes very wet. It was after 10 when I went out, attended to marketing etc — Then to 2nd Nat. Bank, & deposited $80.00 to Annie's c[redit], to cover a shortage made by a mistake & overdraft. It was near noon when I got home. Lunch at 1 — After that worked for about an hour but then it became so dark that I could not draw, but instead gave up to house work — Between 5 & 6 went out for a walk & was caught in a heavy shower. Dinner at 6.30 — At 8 went to Phil. Concert at Met. Opera House — Annie gave her ticket away — I enjoyed the concert very much — There met Geo. & Charl & after the concert went to C's room with J — Talked over Lance Creek Cattle Trust matters — As a consequence did not get to bed until midnight.
