## Left Page

### June, Thursday 23, 1887.

A very pleasant cool day —
with showers about. Eve'g
about 8 Rain came & fell quite
for some time — I was miserable
with a headache — Tried to work
on A.B.N. Co's drawing but was
able to do but little. Spent
some time looking after human
Eve'g, chatting & reading
until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

*[Written at top of page: mother dead at 3.30 A.M.]*

### June, Friday 24. 1887.

In bed all day with a very
severe headache — Before 11
Annie brought some a letter &
a telegram from Charl. The
telegram was ambiguous but
no doubt meant that Mother
was dead — It was from N.Y.
About the middle of the afternoon
another came from Po'k: "Mother
passed away peacefully unconscious at 3.30 this m'g. — Supposed you
had been notified when telegraphing
this m'g. Don't attempt to come
to-morrow — Funeral 11. Monday m'g."

I was not able to get up
until near 7 — then had tea —
walked about the place — &
then sat by fire while Annie
read aloud. To bed at 10.30

Payne & Coyle finished and
seeded the new bit of ground
out of Tyler & Turrell [illegible].
