## Left Page

### April, Sunday 24, 1887

A lovely day — B&p: late & the m.f. Spent reading newspapers &
marking linen. It was about
12.30 when Jim & I walked down
to the N.A. of D. Annie following in a
car. we spent the time until 2
in looking at the pictures. Then
home to dinner. After that I
had a short nap and at 5 went
with Annie & Jim to Mother Cook's,
where I left them & walked up
to Central Park feeling very stiff
in the legs & uncomfortable generally
left the Park at 82° S. & home by
car getting in after 7 — Took a light
tea — wrote along letter to Herrick
— posted it. Then wrote letter to
mother — went up to G.C.D. with
it. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 25, 1887

Bright, mild m.f., overcast & cool
before mid day — Rain at 6 o'clk —
To studio at 10 — Then downtown — first
to Charles' office — then to Howard White's
then to Babb, Cook & Willard's — then to Boyli's
& got a lot of canvas for ceilings & Matrose
Home at 1.15 — Emma J. took lunch
with us. I took Jim to Kindergarten
& then studio at 2.30 — The afternoon spent
in minting instructions to Herrick & receiving
calls — Miss Abbatt — Mr. Eaton — Dr. Yale
&c. & writing notes — Yale walked
up home with me about 6. Dinner —
m.f. reading & writing until 10
— then to bed.
