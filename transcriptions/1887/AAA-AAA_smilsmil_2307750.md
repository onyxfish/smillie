## Left Page

### September, Saturday 17. 1887.

A heavenly day — The ther. went down to 31° last night & stood at 39° when I got up at 7.30 — the middle of the day was delightfully mild — brisk fires in the m'l & open doors & windows at noon. I got up with a headache which passed off — I spent most of the day out of doors feeling much better than for some days past. Payne & teams at work — Battles with Nom's trave — Ambrose Payne — Battles' boy Willie Curry & Willie Wood — Coupe [podged?] about. Heinck commenced work on cherry room mantle piece — Hotchkiss worked on road gutter & the Smiths painted. I went down town about 5 o'clk — posted a note to Will & one to Kate enclosing a short note [rec.?] from Charl. from the Grande Hotel, Paris. Ev'g reading & chatting. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 18. 1887.

Another delicious day. In the m'l, went with Elez. Howe to the Episcopal Ch. & heard the Rev. Saml. Sires — Annie came in later with Jim & after service we all walked home together. Before 1 o'clk I took some photos on upper piazza of Annie, Jim & the baby — They were excellent photos but the sitters behaved badly — After dinner I tried again on the piazza on the north side of the house but there was not light enough. Eve'g I wrote a letter to Charl. Took it down to the Post office before 10 — To bed at 10.30.
