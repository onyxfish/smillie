## Left Page

### January, Monday 24, 1887

A nasty, rainy day. To studio
after 10. Spent the mtf. painting over foreground of "The Edge
of the Wood" giving it much
more freshness of color & snap.
A note from Bierstadt asking
me to act on a committee of 9
to select pictures for London Exbt.
Home to lunch at 1.40 — back
before 3 — Got out "Evening" 20×30
single painting & sketched in
a group of foreground trees — very
dark so that it was hard work
to see — Ge. came in & gave acct.
of visit to Po'k. Mother is in a very
low, depressed state & I am much
concerned about her. Ev'g.
Home to dinner at 6 — Chark
came in about 9 o'clk — with
mt. policies — To bed about
10.30 c

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 25, 1887

Overcast after a bright mtf.
To studio at usual time & spent
the mtf. painting sky & working
over "Rocks on Marblehead Neck"
11½ × 24½ — Home to lunch &
back at usual time. Afternoon
worked again on foreground forms
on "Evening" — At 5 went down to
Curree & Co's & got a copy of Am.
Art; then home to dinner at
6.30 — Ev'g. mounting french
photos — Chark also mounted
some. It was 10 o'clk. when we
went to his dark room tober-
nish them & 11.30 when I got
home — To bed at midnight.
