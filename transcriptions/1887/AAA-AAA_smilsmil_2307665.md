## Left Page

### March, Thursday 31, 1887

*Cambridge, at Mrs. Wm. Cook's, 25 Appleton St.*

Beautiful day — up at 6.30 — finished
packing — help a little before 8 — left the
house at 8.45 & took 9 o'clk train for Boston
via Springfield — an uninteresting ride
getting to Boston at 3.45 — 15 m. late owing
to hot boxes. By street cars we go out
without difficulty to Sue's at 5 o'clk —
I went out & had a short walk before
tea time at 6.30 — Eve'g. chatting
until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 1, 1887

Overcast, cold & raw east wind — some
slight snow flurries. Up at 7 o'clk
& had a walk before breakfast at 8.
Read most of the m'g, & before 12
went down to the Square & did some
shopping for Annie — back before 2
when we had dinner — again more
reading — then a nap & at 5 o'clk out for
another walk — Tea at 6.30. Eve'g
Mr. & Mrs. Chas. Talpan called — also Mr.
John Reid, a neighbor — Mr. T. invited
us to take tea with them to-morrow
eve'g. To bed soon after 10.
