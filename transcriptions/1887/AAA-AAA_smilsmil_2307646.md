## Left Page

### February, Saturday 19, 1887

Disturbed during the night —
two or three times by sudden
dismals and violent pains —
In the m[?]. was so anxious to
go on with A.B.N. Co's drawing —
that after taking [illegible] indeed —
got up & went to studio between
10 to 11 — but the exertion proved
unwise — I was unable to do
anything & returning home at
1.30 I went to bed determined
to keep quiet until my trouble
passed. Ev'g, Annie read
aloud to me — again during
the night I suffered disturbances
as the night before —
