## Left Page

### April, Friday 22. 1887.

Mild & very pleasant day. To Studio
at 10. To work on a large locomotive
drawing started long ago for the
A.B.N. Co. Home to lunch at 1.30 —
After that, took Jim to Kindergarten
then back & with Annie went to
Chickering Hall to Mad. Schiller's
Piano Recital — a small audience —
after the concert went in to see Mad S —
& found her really ill with a cold &
excitement. Put A. into a car, then to
Am. Nt Ass'n. & saw Sutton about etching
after Johnson's Stage Coach — then to N.A.
f.D. until 6. Home to dinner —
evg. Annie quite used up by
influenza — Read awhile and
to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 23. 1887.

Dark — Rain all the m'g. Fog in the
afternoon — Heavy rain with some thunder &
lightening at night. Up late. Emma J —
came in after bkf't. It was afternoon when
I got to studio — To work on big locomotive
drawing for A.B.N. Co. until after 1 —
Then in to Frank Walker's studio where he
is having a final exhibition before leaving
the business — there until near 2. Then
home to lunch. After that, rested awhile,
soon after 4 went up to Marquand's new
house, cor. 68 St. & Madison Ave & there
met a number of pleasant people —
Mr. M. & his son, very pleasant. The house
& its decorations, very rich & in good taste.
Left at 6 & walked down with Geo —
Dinner at 7 — Charl came in for a
few minutes with new Dr. Dolacey
for Montrose — Ev'g. Some reading
& some planning for a kitchen garden
at Montrose. To bed about
10.30.
