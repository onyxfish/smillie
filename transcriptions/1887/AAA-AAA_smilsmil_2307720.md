## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 19. 1887.

Much of the time cloudy and muggy. Wind from the east. Up late — [illegible] at 9 — Herrick not at work — doing some jobbing for himself — Went down town in the m't. after 11. Dinner at 1.30 — The afternoon was spent studying over the earth closet problem — & fixing up things in my etching & dark rooms — pasting paper on blinds, etc — Went to P.O. just before 8 — then sat with Annie & read to her from newspapers. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 20. 1887.

A half pleasant m't — overcast afternoon — wind east & threatening rain — a heavy thunder storm at night, before daylight. I spent much of the day in etching room & studio trying to clear up some of the confusion & get things ready to begin work — Herrick not at work. In the afternoon I felt so much out of sorts that I took a nap — felt worse after it & had quite an attack of pain before 7 — so that I took no supper — by keeping very quiet it abated so that I was comparatively comfortable in going to bed at 10.30.
