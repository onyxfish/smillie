## Left Page

### December, Saturday 24, 1887

A bright, pleasant day, but overcast at night with a little flurry of snow. To studio at 10.30 — Worked on W. C. drawing fussing & mussing with the trees introduced at right hand end. Home at 1 back at 2.30 & again worked on drawing as long as I could see — then wrote a note to Mr. G. L. Rives to leave with photos & drawing at his house in 29 S—. Then to Lord & Taylor's & got some curtain rings — then to W. Rives & found that he was not in town — then home. Evef spent in getting things ready for the youngsters Christmas and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 25, 1887

A bright day — up late — After afternoon — Sat about until 11 & then went out for a walk, taking Jim — It was about 12.30 when I got back — read until 2 — then dinner — After that had a nap of an hour or more — then played nurse while Annie got her nap. Tea about 7 — Evef I read a little while — then wrote notes to Mr. Wyatt Murray (May Tiffany) Bierstadt, G. L. Rives Charl — & Mary — All went out & mailed them. Then to bed.
