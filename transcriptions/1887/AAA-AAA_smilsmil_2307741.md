## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 30. 1887.

Delicious m't. with quite a heavy shower soon after 1 o'clk — another passed to the west of us a little before 6. Thomas & team + Payne & team worked all day — also Ambrose Payne — Battles — young Curry & two other boys. Coyle pottered about cutting grass & picking up stones — Herrick finished shingling his studio — Hotchkiss worked on garden wall & the Smiths painted. I spent all of the day out of doors with the men. Went down town at 6 o'clk. — Then tea — Read newspaper with g. Then made a nice terra tops for one of the lamp chimnies & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 31. 1887.

Another delightful day. Up early & worked with the painters, getting tints for portions of second story — Thomas & Payne with teams at work — also Battles — young Curry — Ambrose Payne & his small boy — O'Brien came on with his team in the afternoon & Thomas, feeling used up left his team with Battles — Hotchkiss worked at the wall — Herrick laid floor in his shop & Coyle weeded in the pasture — Soon after 10 A.m. we went out riding — Annie, Kate, Jim & I — drove down to Fairdale & back over Fairview hill — getting home at 1 — Dinner at 1.30 — I spent the afternoon out of doors — Kate went to tea with the Streeteds — I went after her at 8 & had a pleasant call until 9.30 — then home — glorious moonlight night — To bed about 10.

Got photo prints from Doolittle.
