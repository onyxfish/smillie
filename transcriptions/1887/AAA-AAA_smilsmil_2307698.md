## Left Page

### June, Sunday 5. 1887.

overcast with easterly wind.
Got up late — Spent part
of the m'f. out of doors working
about the vines etc. — Dinner
about 2 — Afternoon wrote
some letters — & in the eve'g
a number more. Took
them down to the P.O. at
10 o'clk — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 6. 1887.

Raw & wet. Got up late — Jim's
fourth birth day — Nelson Warner
came at 9 o'clk. & through the day
I directed him about painting
the ceilings of the Cherry Room and
the studio. I was about out
of doors a good deal — Got a
letter from Kate (Sat.) very hopeless
as to Mother — Dr. Case saying that
she can be with us but a short
time longer — Eve'g. wrote a
short letter to Mother — also a
note to Charl. Annie read
aloud for a short time. To bed
at 10.30.
