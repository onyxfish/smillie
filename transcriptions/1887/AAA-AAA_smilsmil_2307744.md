## Left Page

### September, Monday 5, 1887

Lovely day. O'Brien & Payne &
teams at work — also, Battles. Am:
Payne, Coyle Curry & Wood — Raking
off the little surface stones. Hotchkiss
& J.D. Payne on the walls. The Smiths
painting — Herrick at home on work
of his own. I spent most of the day
with the man — went down town a
little before noon — Dinner — In
the afternoon a call from the Parkes
with Mr. & Mrs. Jno. Scranton of Scranton
— Mr. Wm. Lusk also called. — Evef
studying problems of drainage
& pasture making — To bed
at usual time.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 6, 1887

Cloudy & threatening rains — at night
thunderstorms near us — O'Brien & team
Payne & team — Ambrose Payne — Coyle —
Willie Curry & Willie Wood at work. For
a short time in the m'f. Battles worked
then his son took his place until noon.
Payne got the Timothy seed over the
ground when it was then raked in
with garden rakes. Afternoon. O'Brien
ploughed again over the garden. Payne
worked with Hotchkiss on the wall. The
Smiths painted — Herrick worked for
himself at home. I went down town
before 8. Evef. Rec'd. letters from Hunger.
Bro. George & Annie rec'd. letters from Sit-
mons & others —
