## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 26. 1887.

A nasty day, beginning with rain
& ending with snow — clear, cold
night. Got up with a head-
ache but was able to go to
studio & spent the with paint-
ing over parts of little picture
"A bit on Marblehead Neck" —
Home at 1.40 & gave up to
my headache at once — On
the lounge all the afternoon
until 6.30 — then got up and
ate a little — At 9 went to
studio & cleaned up things —
Home before 10 — Read a couple
of chapters in Koehler's Am. Art.
To bed about midnight.

The news from Mother to-day
more favorable.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 27. 1887.

Bright pleasant day — To studio
at 10 & to work on "Meadow Pond"
21×31 — painting over sky & thence
generally over the picture — Home
at 1.30 & back before 3 & worked as
long as I could see — Just as I
was leaving Benian came in
& I had a long talk about leaving
the studio next May — of course he
urges me to stay. — Home at 6.30
Telegram from Koehler saying that
he cant keep his engagement to
dine with us so we sent out
& brought in Geo. Cook & wife &
many Willard — they spent the
evening with us until 10 — after
that I wrote some notes and
to bed about 11.30

News from Mother continues
favorable.
