## Left Page

### January, Sunday 16, 1887

Pleasant winter day. R.t.f. about 9 — then the baker — at 10.30 went up to Charl's with him for some times — then walked down 5th ave to 17th S. & across to Aunt Anna's where I met Annie, Jim & the girls — we visited until 1.30 there home to dinner — about 4 I wrote my letter home, before 6 Charl came in with his M.S. for newspaper articles written in the Cattle men's interest — went with him to his dark room & got some mounting boards etc — Tea about 7 — then for some time looking over Charl's paper & writing out suggestions for changes. Then chatting & reading until after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 17, 1887

A nasty, dark, rainy day — at 9.30 went up to Charl's (47 S.) with his Cattle M.S. then to studio, getting there at 10.30 — I spent the day working over old Water-Color drawing "A glimpse of Lake George from Black Mt. Mael". I have made it much stronger in color & light & shade but it is rather heavy & tends to blackness — Considering the under tones that I had to work upon it is perhaps as good as I could hope to make it — Home to lunch as usual & back — Home to dinner at 6 — Rvetf I was to mount some photos & later, with Charl, was to burnish them but I was so used up with pain from flatulency that I could do nothing — C. came in before 9 & stayed until after 10 — To bed before 11 feeling easier.
