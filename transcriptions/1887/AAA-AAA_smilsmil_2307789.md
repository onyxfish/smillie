## Left Page

### December, Sunday 4, 1887

Dull, damp & overcast — a late blcf — about 11.30 feeling
that I wanted more rest I lay down upon my bed & did
not get up until 1.30 — Dinner at 2.15 Ed [illegible] Cook & Carleton
dined with us, as this morning Mary & other children went to
Buffalo. Later in the afternoon I wrote a couple of notes — Ge-
& wife, Walter & daughter I just
Valentine came in — About
7. tea — Annie read over some
of her old letters to her Mother
from abroad in '84. Charl came
in later — just from Po'ke — left
at 10 o'clk. Then I finished a
letter already commenced to
Kate & took it to G.C. Depot,
To bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 5, 1887

Rain in the m'g, clear before noon,
mild & pleasant. To studio at 9.30.
finished cleaning & assorting books
& getting them upon the shelves of the
book case. Jaurie called — first
time that I have seen him for a
number of years. To lunch about —
back at 2.30 — worked the rest of
day light making but little visible
progress. Home at 6 — dinner —
quietly read until 10 — To bed
before 11.
