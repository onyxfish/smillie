## Left Page

### May, Thursday 26. 1887.

Still hot weather with a heavy shower in the afternoon — Spent
the rest getting things into shape
to move studio — Fixed a couple
of pfts. between blatters for Mr. Hoss
— Worner & Rev. Famer of Pond.
Lake. Summer School called for née
came in & wanted pictures —
Home to lunch — back at 3
when an old man came to
help carry things down to new
studio — worked until after 6
— then home, very tired — Dinner
— even after a little reading &c
Chark came in — stayed until
after 10. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 27. 1887.

Charming day — breezy & cool — Got
to studio about 9.30 — also the old
man came & until 1 o'clk he worked
camping things from my old rooms
down to the new one. The engineer
also worked a part of the time, getting
my printing press down & several of
the heavier pieces of furniture, etc.
Home to lunch about 1.30 — very tired —
took a nap — & at 3.30 went down to
Sargent's & bought Quaker City commode
— then to Pomroy's — then to Reebher Co.
at 4th St — to Macy's — to Flinn's — then
to other furniture stores & at last found
a small oak table for Montrose Bay-
window — then to studio & home before
7 — dinner — before eve got through
Dib Howe & her friend Pomroy came in.
Later in the even Chark came in.
