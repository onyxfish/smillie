## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 1. 1887.

Cold, windy march day — snow in
the mt f., but little fell. After 10
when I got to studio's having spent
some time running about to learn
something about the house for sale
cor. Lex. Ave. & 27° S. Finally got
to work on vigt. for A.B.N. Co. — a
Train of sleeping & drawing room cars.
Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — back
at 2.30 & worked until near 5 —
Then rode up to Eastman Johnson's.
65 W. 55th S. & he signed half a day in
"Glase with the Seizure" pfe. for me — Then
home to dinner at 6.30 — met Walter C. —
& spoke to him of my project of studio's
cor. 27° S. & Lex. Ave. He did not take
to it very savagely — About 8 took A. to
mother Cook's — got V. & the Adams girls
with A. went to Thomas Concert — I Ret'd.
home — Read & looked over some of
Father's pfos. A. came in at 10.45
— To bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 2. 1887.

Milder — sunny — humid & raw. To studio at
about 10 & to work on A.B.N. Co. drawing of
sleeping car Train. Mr. Ernest Flagg called
about his building project in 42°. St. — About
noon I went to N.A. with some more etching
pfos. for Jongreer — Then to 2° Nat. Bk. & deposit
ed some money — then to Newman's & bot. some
nails for Montrose — then to Morris Gallery
& saw Wm. M. Chase's pictures — Sale to-night
& To-morrow night — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
the Adams girls there — back to studio at
2.30 & worked on drawing until near 5 —
Then Ge°. & I went to Grady & McKeave's — &
thence to 42°. S. & 8° Ave. to see the old bldg.
in which Flagg is interested — Then to 44° S. &
Barny — looked a new studio's bldg. Then
home to dinner at 6.30 — Welf. wrote
some notes — amused myself for some
time looking over photos — & to bed
soon after 10.
