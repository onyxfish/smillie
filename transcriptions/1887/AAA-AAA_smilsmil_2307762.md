## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 11, 1887

more bleak, overcast weather —
the wind getting around to N.W.
& blowing quite cold — in the
afternoon it cleared with promise
of a crisp, cold night. Zerfurs
came & fixed gal. iron tops on the
chimnies — I painted away all
day on my Oct. sketch 15 × 21½ &
enjoyed my studio exceedingly.
The Smiths painted away on the
window blinds. I went down
town about 6 — then tea —
Evef. wrote to Will about some
work he wishes me to do for the
A.B.N.Co. — Read news paper
& got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 12, 1887

The Ther. went down to 29° last night.
High wind from N.W. very cold and
bleak — overcast most of the day growing
quieter & pleasanter towards evef.
Fires going all over the house &
in the studio making things very
comfortable in-doors. Made fire for the
first time in new stove in S.W. bed-room
& at night Transferred Annie, Jim &
the baby to that room. I painted
steadily on 15 × 21½ & got the "first
painting" finished. Went down town
before 6. Every store closed — all gone
to great Band to celebrate the centennial
of the settlement of Susq Co. Evef.
Read news paper & drew some plans
for Herrick's work. To bed at the
usual time.
