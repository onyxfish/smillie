## Left Page

### May, Monday 30, 1887

*Decoration Day —*

overcast m't., clearing very pleasantly.
Got to studio at about 10. ab. work
all the m'f getting the many little
articles together into pkgs. to pack
into big Montrose box. Home to
lunch at 1.45 — worked there for
some time getting trunks down
from store room, etc — after 3
when I got back to studio —
Packed big box — Charl. came
in after 4 — It was nearly 6
when I went over to Hoboken to
get Drawing room seats for Thurs.
It was 7.° when I got home —
Dinner — At 8.30 went to Walter's
for a few minutes — then until
after 10 writing out detailed
programme of action for the next
two days. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 31, 1887

Pleasant cool day — ab.o went to Gus-
nij's Exps., but as his men were still deco-
rating graves, as he expressed it, he could
not move stove & book case from studio.
I spent the whole day in running
about doing up little matters — went
to 2° Nat. Bk. & Safe Deposit & many
other places — Near 2 when I got
home to lunch — out at 3 several
to Fulton St., Park Pl. — to Anthony's, 591
[illegible] — then to Jeane & Taylor, 10 & 8th Fifth
ave. about some turning — to Bingman's
Brook's Bros. etc. & got home about
6 o'clk — Dinner — Evef. got
a number of things together for
packing trunk & case — Finding
myself very tired & not making
much progress I went to bed
at 9.30 — Just then Mr. Chas. Sprague
& Ed. Cook called — Annie being
in bed, we excused ourselves.
