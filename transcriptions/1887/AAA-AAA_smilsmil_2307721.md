## Left Page

### July, Thursday 21, 1887

Over cast — east wind — heavy showers in the afternoon — with muzzling rain between — I was out of sorts with remains of last night stomach trouble & headache. I wrote a long letter to Charl — giving him advice concerning his trip abroad. I wrote upon it in the m'f & again in the afternoon. Warner came in the afternoon & painted the last coat on studio walls. I went down-town in the m'f & again before 8 o'clk. Rain at night — I sat with Annie until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 22, 1887

A miserable, sticky, muggy day with east wind — most of the time overcast — but when the sunshine it was burning hot. A heavy shower passed over us early in the afternoon & a flood, with thunder & lightning just before 8 p.m. I spent a part of the m'f. writing to Mother, Cook, Nelie & Kate — At 11.30 went to P.O. then down to Herrick's to see what had become of him — found him at work on the foundation of his house. Home at 12.30 — dinner after t — then a nap — then for some time working with a weeding spade taking out a lot of stout dock roots that have formed a colony on a part of the Coolper-lot lawn. Went down town at 6 — then tea — Rain fell heavily all the ev'f. I sat with Annie. To bed at 10.30 Rain through most of the night
