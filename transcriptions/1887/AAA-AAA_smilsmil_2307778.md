## Left Page

### November, Saturday 12, 1887

A pleasant day overhead — windy,
dirty & cold — I worked on drawing
for A.B.N. Co. The Casa Grande — Arizona
and finished it about noon — Then
I went out with Jim for a walk —
in to lunch at 1 — Sister Mary took
lunch with us + went to Po[illegible] on the
3.30 train — I worked on a drawing
by Monebarger as long as I could see —
a car co. vignette — Trying to put some
life into it — Then went to Grand
Central Depot & bought car seat for
Sue. for Monday — Then to Studio and
opened big freight box from Montrose
— then went to Mr. Dav. Huntington's
but he was not in — Then home —
Dinner at 6.30 — Edith Wright with
us — Even[ing], chatting + reading.
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 13, 1887

A charming day, altho' at times
windy. Before 11 went out with
Jim — first to 39th St — then we walked
down to Studios — Home before 2.
A series of calls that continued
through most of the day — First,
Elig. Stowe — Geo. Smillie — Edith Wright
Emma J — Nelie Adams. Mary
Willard — Mr. Sprague — Ed[w]. Cook
wife & children, etc — Dinner
at 2.30 — I had little chance
to do anything until even[ing]. I
wrote to Dr. R. Lathrop and to
Frank Herrick.
