## Left Page

### November, Monday 28. 1887.

A warm, showery day — clearing late in the afternoon — a cold, bright night — To studio soon after 9 — The wall-colorers there sizing the walls said they would not get at the color until after dinner so I returned to the house & worked upon the outline of A.B.N. Co's S.A. drawing — Street scene & Water Cart; Lunche & at studio at 1.30 — The men had color ready as J. had directed — it was satisfactory & they made progress, working until 4 o'clk. I then made a call on Miss Abbatt — home at 5 — dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g. Charle came before 9 & wanted us to see his new rooms Cor. Mad. Ave. & 40th St so we went around with him & looked at his quarters & some of his possessions — home before 10 — To bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 29. 1887.

A brilliant, cold day — Got to studio soon after 9 & spent the m'f. there with the wall colorers — Home at 12.30 — Mr. Brown (Eleanor) took lunch with us — Mr. Jenny Whiting was also at the house but with so bad a headache that she took a rest instead of lunch — I worked on S.A. Street scene & Water Cart until 3.30, then to studio to see how the work had progressed — no carpenter there as should have been — about 5 went to A.A.A. galleries & saw Sutton for a few min. Then up 5th Ave — Stopping at art galleries on my way home — Dinner — Ev'g W. Parks came in for a few minutes — I spent the ev'g until 10.30 looking over the summer Nos. of Ill. London News — Then to bed.
