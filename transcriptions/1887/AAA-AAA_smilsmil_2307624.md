## Left Page

### January, Thursday 6, 1887

Snow + freezing last night after the
rain — Clear day — beautiful over
head — very nasty under foot. It
was after 10 when I got to studio
after putting Oct. Study in frame
spent rest of morn. putting figures
into Cowboy Country sketch — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 —
Stretched drawing paper to
make a new drawing — then
started a sketch — bit of cliff +
sea near [illegible] — + worked
awhile on an old Water-Color
Sketch that I found — E². Parker
came in at 5 — with him + Geo.
for awhile — then walked with
[illegible] up to depot — home to dinner
at 6 — Eve'g Reading awhile —
then Annie read aloud +
after that a game of cards +
to bed about 11 —
Jim seems very much
better to-day.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 7, 1887

Half overcast — very bad walking — To
studio after 10 — Nailed big [illegible]
into frame — then to work on a
little sketch commenced years
ago — washed out as well as in +
Radically changed everything —
color + composition — + I think
got a very fresh + creditable bit
of work — To bank — then to Hunter's
\+ home at 1.45 — back before 3 and
spent what remained of daylight
in finishing up little sketch — Made
paste + mounted it — at 5 went to
Bedell's — Bdmy near [illegible] — about a
bill suit, after being paid — Home at
6 — dinner — After 8 when Jim got
to bed — Alt his better his evident
that he is not yet well — Eve even'g
being short; after reading, Annie
\+ I had a game of cards + to bed
soon after 10.
