## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 12. 1887.

Pleasant day — ten o'clk when I got to studio — Spent the m'f. fussing about, at odd jobs — Did some work on old Water-color drawing "Tongue Mt., Lake Ge? from Harbor Islands" — Took Walter's plans for interior finish, Montrose Studio, to Adams Eif. to sent them to Herrick — then to bank then home to lunch — to studio at 2.30, after having taken Jim to Kindergarten School. — Mr. S. R. Koehler came in until I went to N.A. at 3.45 judging pictures until near 6 — then home to dinner — Ev'f. feeling very much used up I sat by the fire until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 13. 1887.

Mild, pleasant day — got up feeling very much out of sorts & at last gave up to it entirely & stayed in-doors all day, most of the time on the lounge.
About 4 o'clk. Hale came in to make a social visit —
Ev'f. Chas Collins came in — called before 11.
