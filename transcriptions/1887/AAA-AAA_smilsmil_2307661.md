## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 23, 1887

Clear, cold & windy — Feeling better
I ventured to go to the Studio — got
there at 10 o'clk. All the mf was
spent in writing letters in answer
to notes etc. that I found awaiting
me — At noon went to Bus Effe, with
Arms drawing for Uncle Willie — then
to Knoedler's — to Bank — to Schans's &
at 1.30 home to lunch — To Studio
at 3 — commenced work on a drawing
for H.B.N.Co, view of Brooklyn Bridge
worked until 5 — home at 6 — dinner
Evef, read awhile — then wrote
to Coleman & H.P. Bellows in "Poets
& Etchers" business. To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 24, 1887

An April-like day — sunshine & shadow —
About 10.30 when I got to Studio — To
work on Brooklyn Bridge drawing —
much trouble with the washes as the
drawing is on card board — not Bristol
board — At 1. went to Bank on my
way up Fifth Ave. Stopped into Moore's
Gallery & saw the 2d Haseltine Collection
offered this winter. Nearly 2 when
I got home to lunch — 3 when I got
back to Studio — worked on bridge
until 6 — then home — dinner —
Evef, read Howell's Poems until
after 9 — then a game with Annie
& to bed before 11.
