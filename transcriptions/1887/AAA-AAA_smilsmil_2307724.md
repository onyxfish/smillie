## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 27, 1887

A beautiful day, altho' very hot. After bkfs. to etching
room & put my press in order & then got a beautiful transfer
of my Durand tracing, commenced the etching before dinner
Continued it in the afternoon until 5 o'clk. Then out to the
pasture & pulled weeds until 7 — then in & had tea — after
that — down town to P.O —
Rec'd from Charl the Waiver
document to get County Clerk's
certificate that Amos Nichols is
a regular Notary. Swelf
wrote a note to Charl & one to
E & H. T. Anthony begging them to
hurry up my order for Sulph. Soda
gram. Glorious moonlight.
At 10. went to P.O. & posted letters
— then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 28, 1887

A very hot clear day. Up late —
bkfs. after 9. Rec'd by Exp. a box
from Anthony with Sulph. Soda & a doz.
3×5 Cramer plates (too large) I made
boy & kit? Cramer plates — completed
my solutions — went down town and
got the Prothonotary's certificate that
Amos Nichols was a regular Notary
attached it to waiver & Rec'd it to
Charl by noon mail — Then home
& worked in dark room until
dinner, getting nothing but failures
after dinner I made further experiments with no better success —
probably the plates are too old.
It was after 4 o'clk. before I got
to work on the Durand etching —
Before 6. W & HW. Parkes & Bessie came
in — tea at 7. Swelf wrote a letter
to Charl on Lance Creek business.
