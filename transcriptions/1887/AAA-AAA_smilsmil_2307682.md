## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 4, 1887.

Very warm day — went to Union league
to see how Charl was. walked up then
with St. f Sprague who came to the house
to deliver to Annie some Western Mort-
gages — To studio at 10.30 — disturbed by
progress of work in Waller's old room.
Had to send for gas fitter & get him
started — I worked for awhile on
big locomotive drawing — at 2.5 went
down town with Jeo. to Charl's to make
legal transfer of the Estate of Jas. Smillie
from Charl & self as Executors to Charl &
self as Trustees — went to Tom Shearman's
& to Safe Deposit. Then to Boyle's & got sam-
more ducks — 2.30 when I got home to
lunch — 3.30 when I got to studio. Then
to N.A. Saw Richards — am on auditing
com — then to Adams & up — then to Daut.
Huntington's to get his permission to etch
his gold & mith's daughter — then to studio
wrote to Mr. Young of Po'k — Home to
dinner at 6.45 — every went
to Edwin Cook's to enquire about
terra cotta. — home before 9 —
wrote to Little the nursery man
Rochester — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 5, 1887.

Pleasant day altho' changeable — To
studio at 10 — some time with gas
picture man in Waller's old studio — then
to work on large locomotive drawing.
Dining came in for a short visit —
Then Mr. Cone with drawing of Po'k.
bridge that I sent to him last
Monday — desiring several changes.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45
for the rest of the P.m. — worked on
Cone's drawing making changes.
Home at 6 — dinner at 7.15. Wt. &
Mrs. Philip Lockwood (connections
of Annie's) & Geo. & Emily dined
with us & spent the evef. until
10 o'clk.
