## Left Page

### July, Monday 11, 1887

A very breezy, cool day — the thermometer marking as low as 56°. In the m'f
we had fire in the bed room & the
large room. As the day advanced
the temperature was delightful.
I spent most of the m'f. with
Jim in the pasture, weeding.
After 11 I came to the house and
worked awhile on the
finished the Tuito Theatre drawing.
Dinner at 1 — after that wrote
letters to Will & to M. Korhler, then
packed drawing & at 4 o'clk
worked until near 4 when my
head felt so uncomfortable that
I was obliged to give it up &
spent the rest of the afternoon
with Derrick & out of doors — in
the eve'g my discomfort increased.
Annie & the boy get along very
nicely — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 12, 1887

Delightful m'f., the day proving
to be very hot — tempered by a
pleasant breeze — all the m'f
out fixing the vines about the house.
About noon when I got to my
work & almost finished the Tuito
Theatre drawing — Dinner at 1 —
after that finished drawing &
wrote to Will & to M. Korhler —
at 4 went to E&P. & P.O. with
drawing & letters. Waited until
the mail came in — after 5 o'clk
— then home — after tea, working
about the vines until after 8 —
a very close, warm eve'g.
Sat with Annie until 9.30. No
musquitos making us quite miserable — Before 10 A— killed it and
had a peaceful night — To bed
at 10.
