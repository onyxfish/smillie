## Left Page

### June, Sunday 19, 1887

Charming day with two showers, the first at about
one & the second after 6.
much of the time the sky
was overcast — a late bkfp—
after 9 — then wandering
about the place — Dinner
at 2 — a sort of nap in big
chair — then wrote to
Mother — then to Sluinger
Miss Abbott, Ge Kelly &
others — after 8 when we
had tea — more writing —
went to P.O. about 10.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 20, 1887

Very warm day with indications
in the early m'g of coming rain —
a very few drops fell — then cleared
with beautiful clouds — a storm
came up between 7 & 8 P.m. but
very little rain fell — another
threatened between 10 & 11 o'clk
p.m. but did not reach us.
Pkg of bristol boards from Knodler
came in the m'g & I got to work
on one of the A.B.N. Co's drawings
but made very little progress
but so much about with the men
— Down town twice — Rest
on piazza until 9 — then reading
papers until 10 — To bed, but
the threatening storm kept me
awake for some time.
