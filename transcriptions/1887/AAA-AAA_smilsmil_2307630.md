## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 18. 1887.

A very cold day — after 2 when
I got to studio — got out a study
roughly laid in at Marblehead [illegible]
( x ) — got out box of colors sent
to me by Reynolds for Trial, &
there I set to work, Repainting the
study & testing the pigments — I
made a something of the sky —
merely a preparation for another
painting — with the foreground I
did better. Home to lunch & back
as usual — worked until 4·15. I was
to meet Annie at 14th St. shopping, but
Rec. a message that she had gone
with Fanny K. to rehearsal of Choral Club.
I went to Supper of Salmagundi Club.
Home to dinner at 6 — Eve'g writing
letters until after 9 — Annie & Adams
girls at Mother Cook's.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 19. 1887.

Milder, but still very raw and
overcast. Nearly 10.30 when I got
to studio. Set palette & to work
on my Montrose picture. x
Hillside in Oct — with sheep —
I glazed over it very generally
forcing the color & then painted
over the glaze with a grayer color
not solidly, but showing the richer
color through. Home to lunch and
back — worked until after 4 —
then went to 14th St. & at DeGraaf &
Taylor's furniture store I met Annie.
Bought parlor table & chair — then to
[illegible] & but 3 [illegible] 2 chairs for bed room.
Home at 6. Dinner — Eve'g. Water Color
Socy meeting — did not go but stayed at
home & mounted several photos. At
9·30 Charl & I went to his "dark
room" where we burnished the
newly mounted prints. Just
11 when I Ret'd home. To
bed at once.

Wells called at studio in the
morning.
