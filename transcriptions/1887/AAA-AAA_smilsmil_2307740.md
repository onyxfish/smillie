## Left Page

### August, Sunday 28, 1887

Clear, cloudless mtf, clouds coming later, making the day quite cool — cloudless, moonlight night. Got up with a headache & spent the morning out of doors — at 10.30 went with Kate down to the Baptist church door, trees home. After dinner read aerlies, after which I spent more time in trying to get a nap on the chinny-room lounge but the flies would not permit it. Eve'g I wrote a letter to Charl and a letter before to went to the P.O. — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 29, 1887

A charming day — Spent it all out of doors, most of the time with the men. Thomas & team at work — Battles & Payne — Hatchkis at the wall. Coye cutting grass — Herrick shingling. The Smith's painting — got them to work upon the large South gable — Trying in vain to get the color effect that is in my mind — went down town about 11 — got a long, enthusiastic and interesting letter from Charl — written in London 19th ins. Went to Doolittle's wk. photo. negatives to finish — Took four negatives of a grey stallion colt — home & developed them before dinner — Took two photos of the infant taking his bath but got very little — afternoon with the men — went down town at 6 — Eve'g O'Briere called & wished me to set him to work again — I wrote a short note to Geo & with it sent Charl's letter — I took it to P.O. after 9 o'clk. Then to [illegible] — quite a shower about 11 o'clk
