## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 20, 1887

mild & overcast threatening bad weather, which came at night.
To studio at 9.30 & spent the m[orning]
trying to get a portrait of the late
Mr. G.S. Rives, but with all my care
the likeness evaded me. The
photo. material from which I am
working is very imperfect — got
Chaumkurey to come in & talk to
me about it — Home at 1 — in the
afternoon I went with Annie to
Lord & Taylor's to do some shopping
— then alone I went to Arnold & Con-
stable's — & to Johnston & Faulkner's.
It was after 4 when I got to studio
& found that McCormick had been
there after 3 o'clk — Home before 6.
Evelf. wrote a brief sketch of Father's
life & work for Miss Janes.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 21, 1887

Bright & pleasant — to Douglass' in
41 S. — got painted signs & put up on
Studio directory — then to studios & got
30 pf. copy of P.T.E. — & took it to Mrs.
Sarah Leggett Nuery — then to Bank and
deposited A.B.N. Co. check — then to
Johnson & Faulkners & selected certain
stuffs for studio & at last got to studio
at 11.30 — Fussed a little more on
India ink portrait of Mr. Rives and
home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30
McCormick came in to see me about
putting in a grate, etc. — I got out draw-
ing made last Spring "Conbeteture Flowers"
& did some work upon it — more work
in putting up rods for curtains — Home
at 5 — met & walked up with Geo. Cook
& Mrs. Valentine — after dinner went
with Annie to Walter's — Home at 9.30
A— sewed for awhile on one of studio
curtains & I read aloud — To bed
before 11 o'clk.
