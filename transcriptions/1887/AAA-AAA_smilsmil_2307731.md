## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 10, 1887

Overcast & warmer — in the afternoon an attempt at a shower. White, one of the men who came to work for me yesterday turned up missing — had gone to work for somebody else. The other men at work. I spent the mtf at work on my Durand plate finishing the lower sky & distance & by charcoal & burnisher getting more light & vivacity. Dinner at 1 — Men about the place looking after things until near 5 — then down town with Jim & about — back before 6 — tea — evtf reading newspaper & lounging. Sat for awhile on Piazza before I went to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 11, 1887

Overcast & muggy, thunderous weather — atmosphere saturated — neither O'Brien & team or Blair at work — a shower before noon — & a very heavy one in the afternoon with some thunder — I worked at proving Durand plate, taking in the course of the day four thin Jap. paper — four India, three Jap. Mikenas paper & two plain inks. — none of them very good & some of them decidedly bad. I was very much tired by my day's work which was not finished until after 6 — Evtf I read newspaper until about 9 — then for an hour sat on piazza wrapped in big blanket enjoying the cool air — after the storm in the afternoon the weather cleared charmingly — To bed before 10. 30.
