## Left Page

### January, Monday 10, 1887

A fine winters day — To studio at 10 & to work on Cliff sketch. & by 1 oclk. got it as far as I think it worth while to carry it. I cannot understand how any one can find it possible to make a successful water color painting on such board. A man carrying ½ ton of coal kept me until 1.30 — then home to lunch — After that, Mary (red) with her Tom came & with Annie & Jim went to the first session of a Kinder garten at Mr. Gilbert Jones' W. 37th S. only the three youngsters. As I did not feel well I lay down & took a nap until 3.30 then walked down to 51 W. 105th S. Nicoll's studio. Special meeting of Etching Club on subject of hanging etchings in N.A. Corridor — It was hardly debated — all opposed it. To studio at 5.0 Uncle Willie had been there — Home at 6.15 Walter there with drawings — plans for interior of Montrose studio — dinner — Evelf. Chard came in about Carpenter's sick at Montrose behalf — Stayed until after 9 — To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 11, 1887

A fine cold day — got up with a headache which made me uncomfortable all day — To studio at 10 — until 1 at work finishing the mounting of two W.C. sketches "Cliffs near Etretat" & "October Morning" & getting them into frames, also getting three Etching [illegible] into alpha for W. Schoff — art went to Adams' W.p. with Schoff's pkg & then to 2. Nat. Bk. then home to lunch — At 3 went to U.C. of D. met Water Color Board of Control & we passed upon a lot of pictures — back to studio at about 4.30 — home before 6 — dinner — Evelf. wrote to Schoff & uncle Willie — read until 10 — then to bed
