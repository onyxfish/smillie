## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 2, 1887

Very hot wtf. — overcast most of the afternoon — heavy thunder storm north of us — Two short showers passed over us, late in the afternoon. Got up at 4.30 A.m. & took a dose of Bromo-Caffin then to bed until near 8 — Soon after 10 went down town with Jim — on my return, with Herrick putting up vines for vines to run upon — After dinner got to work upon Durand etching & did not stop until 6 — with dry point and graver. After tea went down town — then sat with Annie until 9.30, then on piazza until after 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 3, 1887

A very warm day growing cooler at night. I worked all the morning on Durand plate, on the face & head, with dry point & graver — in the afternoon laid a rebiting ground & stopped out pre-paratory to biting — Soon after 9 o'clk Mother Cook & Kate arrived — Kate having left Po'k this morning. It was a glorious moonlight night & they found the ride from the depot refreshingly cool — It was about 11 o'clk. when I got to bed.
