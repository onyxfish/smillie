## Left Page

### December, Friday 16, 1887

Beautiful, clear, crisp day — To
studio at 9.30 & to work on Plaza
Victoria — Rio. Will came in & spent
part of the with with me — Annie
& Jim came in at two walked
home to lunch — back at 2.45
after going to Sarah Leggett Swenip's with
a 2°. proof copy of P. & E. — & to bank
where I deposited $95. I worked
on drawing as long as I could see &
got it nearly finished — Before 6
went to Grady & McEleavers with a B-
copy of P. & E. — ordered all. C. frame.
Home to dinner at 6.30 — Before 8 went
to 112 Mad. Ave. got Eliz. Home and
brought her home with me — spent
the evening chatting — at 10.30 took
her back — home & toasted my toes
before the fire & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 17, 1887

Dull, raw day threatening a storm
which came on at night — Snow and
sleet. Got to studio at 10 & to work
on drawing — Custom House Burrus Hyres.
Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30
& before dark got drawing done —
D. W. Male came in for awhile.
After losing light I spent some
time planning & measuring for
Photo — & Etching closets that I am
going to have Heinrich make for
me — Home at 5.30 — Dinner
at 6.30 — Eve'g spent looking
over some X mas books bought by
Annie & back Nos. of Ill. Lond.
News — To bed about 11.
