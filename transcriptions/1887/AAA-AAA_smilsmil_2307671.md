## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 12, 1887.

Bright wtf., much cooler - here,
clouded, raw & chilly - clearing again
in the afternoon. Took 9 [illegible] car into
town - went first to Vickur & Co's &
there met for the first time S.A. Schoff.
Then went to Elwell's Studio. 175 [illegible]
57 - then to Art museum, getting
there about 11.40 - After being with
K- for a little time Schoff came in &
we had a pleasant time until
1 when I hurried off to catch a
Mt. Auburn car. Got home to dinner
at 2.5 - 15 m. late. After dinner
a disturbed nap in the library.
Miss Clarkson Ret'd from her visit.
Everf. Reading & chatting
until about 10 - To bed about
10.30

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 13, 1887.

*At home, New York.*

Delightful day - Up at 6.30. Packed
my valise & had everything ready for
our start before bkfst - at 8 o'clk - just
before 9.30 we bid our Adieux & walked
down to meet the car - as it came in sight
utter pain came upon Annie & she very
nearly fainted. She was just able to get
upon the car. Reviving as we went along.
We got to Boston & Albany depot at 10.30
which gave her a needed half hour for
rest - at 11 the train started & we had
a tedious & rather dirty ride. The mon-
otony somewhat broken by exchange
of calls with Mr. Woodin, who, with his
wife & son were in the next car. We got
to G.C.D. at 6 - went right home
& soon had a good dinner. Geo.
Cook & Valentines came in after
dinner for a few minutes. Annie
went early to bed having got thro'
the day better than I had any
reason to hope in the wtf. I
went to bed at 10 o'clk. with
a bad headache which troubled
me through the night.
