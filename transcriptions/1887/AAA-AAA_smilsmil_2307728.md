## Left Page

### August, Thursday 4, 1887

A very cool, pleasant day —
our visitors seem none the worse
for their journey of yesterday —
I worked all the mng on plate
rebiting it — afternoon I cleaned
it & burnished. I worked with
graver. Rec'd a note from the
Putnams enquiring about it.
Evelf wrote notes on this sub-
ject to Putnams, Kimmel, Voigt
& Paul, Huntington. To bed
before 10.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 5, 1887

A change in the weather —
cooler but still very damp. over
cast most of the day — shower
about 10 — I spent part of the
mng. taking working pt. of Durand
etching. At noon, went down
town — Dinner soon after — all
the afternoon I worked with graver
& burnisher on my etching. The
timbers etc for Herrick's shop
were hauled up to-day — Evelf
went down town before 8 — then
read newspaper — and to bed
at 10.
