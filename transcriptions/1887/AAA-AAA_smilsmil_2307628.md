## Left Page

### January, Friday 14, 1887

A dismal day with heavy rain in the m'f. Still feeling out of sorts I determined not to go to studio — so, after b'kf. & reading newspaper I wrote a letter of instruction to Herrick about the studio plans I sent him the other day — Lunch promptly at 1 so that Annie & the gives could go to Phil. Rehearsal — I giving up my ticket. At 2.30 I got Jim off to his Kindergartin with Tom & his nurse. — I got out my last summers photos & prepared a number of them for mounting — Dinner at 6 — Eve'f. I mounted photos —     To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 15, 1887

Pleasant day. Got to studio about 10 & spent most of the m'f putting things in order — much needed — also did some mounting — After going to B'k home at 1.15 — after lunch, A — having gone with the gives to some German Theatre, I took Jim around to Mother Cook's then to studio where I did a very little water-color work & some pressing of etching proofs — It was 6 o'clk when I got home —    Spent most of the eve'g reading —
