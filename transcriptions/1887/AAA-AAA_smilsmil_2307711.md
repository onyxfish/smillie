## Left Page

### July, Friday 1. 1887.

The hottest day yet. Ther.
got up to 80° in the house. I
did no work — only directing —
Herrick finished the ceiling
of Cherry Room & the scaffolding
was moved out. Spent the
day mainly in keeping cool
— all out on the piazza again
met after 10 o'clk. — Beautiful
brilliant moonlight & delicious
breeze.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 2. 1887.

Hotter than ever. The Ther. got
up to 86°. A pleasant breeze
from the S.E. made the heat
bearable. The mf was spent,
a part of it, in getting Cherry Room
fire place ready for the laying
of the tile hearth — It was about
3 o'clk. when with Herrick helping
I started that work. Altho' the
tiles were simple square ones
I found unexpected difficulties
the very simplicity making the
defects more apparent. I got
through by 6 o'clk. tired & very
much disappointed at the result.
Every, again on piazza met
after 10 — Beautiful moonlight &
delightful breeze. Thunder
storms passed north of us.
