## Left Page

### March, Monday 21, 1887

A pleasant day, but as I awoke at 4t o'clk. this m'g, with pain
& heat in throat & bronchia I thought it wise to stay at home & nurse
myself, which I did. Aunt Agnes came in for a little while in the
m'g. Then Mother Cook who had been to an occulist's — Later, Emily
came in & told us that the occulist said that cataract was forming on
mother C's eyes — he left one being more than half covered — She is
not informed of her condition. In the afternoon Dr. Yale came in
— an accidental visit — Every Charl came in & stayed with
us until 9.30 — I wrote to Hemck
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 22, 1887

Beastly day — sleet & ice last night
— flying snow & rain during the day —
of course I did not go out but stayed in doors all day reading & amusing
myself as best I could. George came in about the middle of the
afternoon having been with the comm. of artists to pass upon pictures
offered for the Am. Exhib'n in London —
a miserable showing, he says — about 50 pictures offered — only 27 passed.
Geo., with Sheldon, went to Po'k. yesterday & ret'd this m'g. — he brings
encouraging acc'ts from mother.
Every — Reading until 9 — then a
game of cards & to bed before 11.
