## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 1, 1887

Bright for awhile in the mng. then overcast & raw — To studio at 10 — then to Union Sq. Hotel, found Homer & got him to sign 8 or 10 Kt. for me — he did not do it in the most obliging way — then again to studio — Bierstadt has got these babies mixed. Consequently, the honor of position on jury for London exhibn is contramun & Ge. is put in my place. I worked on "Study — Marblehead Neck" 11½ x 24½ during what remained of the mng. repainting part of sky & the sea. Home at 1.45 & back at 3 — more work on Study — Soon after 5 went to N.A.J.D. Greer. in the lecture room has sold two Voice from Cell' for $12. each. then to 5th Ave. Hotel & bought two tickets for Fidelio for Thurs. Weff. Home at 6.30 — dinner — Weff. Reading — a game of cards — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 2, 1887

Snow & cold. Raw wind — It was 10.30 before I got to studio — there to work on "Breezy day on the coast of France" — Repainting the sea so solidly & so changing the mood by making the water so smooth as if uneasures, to reflect the sky, that I will have to change the title to "A quiet day" — Home to lunch at 1½ — back at 2 15 & worked as long as I could see. then walked down to 14th St. & home at 6 — dinner — Evef reading — To bed before 11. Heavy rain, freezing as it fell.
