## Left Page

### May, Saturday 28. 1887.

Caught in a shower before I got to studio at 10 A.M. other considerable showers during the day. left order at Gurney's [illegible] to call at studio Tues. mtf. & get book case & store. The old man & hall boy worked with me until 1 o'clk carrying things from old to new studio. Gas man came & transferred fixtures. Home abt 1.45 after lunch a short nap, as I had suffered all the mtf. from headache. — To studio soon after 3 — nearly when Charl came — he took another photo. from Huntington's "Goldsmith's daughter" El Parker came in. Home at 6.30. Dinner Evelf. suffering from headache Annie read aloud a short story & we got to bed at 9.30.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 29. 1887.

*went to Po'keepsie*

a cool, cloudy mtf. took 9 o'clk train for Po'k — Charl going up with me — gentle Rain from about 10.30 until near 1 — Train late — got to The Terrace about 12.30 — found Mother quiet but suffering discomfort when swallowing — she is much weaker than when I saw her last. This now a very considerable effort for her to speak. We had dinner about 1 — sat with her until 3 — then Charl & I went out & walked — went to Uncle Willie's & saw him. Aunt Jessie & Emma. Back to Terrace at 4 — again with Mother — a cup of tea at 5.30 — then rode to station — No. 6 on time & got down to C.D. at 8.15. went for a moment to dark room with Charl. — then home. Found Mother's cook there — stayed with us over night — Geo came in for a few minutes while I was taking a cup of tea. wrote a note to Will — Tobed about 10.30.
