## Left Page

### July, Sunday 3, 1887

Very hot with strong breeze —
the mf spent wandering about
with Jim — about noon took
a little nap on the lounge in
our new Cherry Room — at 4
went down town, saw Raynsford
about a letter of Annie's posted
last night that she wishes
returned to her — Dinner
at 2. Then for some time
under the trees with Jim —
Later, wrote letters to Mary
& to Koehler. Another pleasant
evef on piazza & to bed after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 4, 1887

Very warm, with strong south east
breeze & some clouds. Herrick
came up & worked as usual — I
found a great deal to fuss about
out of doors altho' I tried to do
some writing & commenced work
on drawing of Duis's Theatre for A. B.
N. Co. Afternoon I put up tiles on
face of our bed Room fire place &
got my fingers on left hand well
burned by the cement — and the
tiles were badly put up. Evef
being breezy & cooler we sat indoors
— some reading — I read aloud a
short story from July "Century" —
To bed about 11.
