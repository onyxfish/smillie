## Left Page

### June, Monday 27, 1887

*mother's Funeral.*

Got up at 5.30. made my toilette with
the aid of two tumblers full of ice water
crossed 23d S. Ferry at 6 — To Ashland
Ho. at 6.45 + had bkfs. Then to 104 E. 39th S.
left my bag etc. there — then to G.C. Dep.
— met Will + went to Po'k. with him on
the 8 o'clk. Train, getting there at 10.30 —
Drove to Terrace, getting to house at 10.45
— It was very painful to look upon the
poor, wasted form of my dear old mother —
some details I will write out + put in
one of the pockets of this Diary. I found
Geo. + Charl at the house, with Mary, Kate,
Aunt H. + Miss Jordan the nurse. The
simple funeral service was at 1 — Dr.
Kendrick officiating — + over at 1.30 when
the body was removed — Will, Geo. + I waited
for 15 m. longer — I read mother's Will —
Then W, G, + I drove to Station + took the
12.10 to N.Y. getting to G.C. Depot at 2 — we
had a very good dinner in a Dining Room
car attached to train. Dining meeting at
the depot + was with us while the body was
being transferred — we crossed E. 23d S. Ferry
+ got to Greenwood Entrance at 3.40 — there
we met Dr. Elder who went to the grave
with us — nearly 4 when we got there — a
short invocation — then the burial — we
left the grave at about 4.30 — Dr. E. again left
us at the Entrance — we drove to Hamilton
ave. Ferry — Reached Battery at 5.15. Geo. went
by West Shore R.R. to Kingston — Will to his home —
I went to mother Cook's by East Side Elevation —
mother C. at Ridgefield — I was put in her
Room — Dinner with Edw. Geo. + Walter at 7 —
Every went to Dr. Yale's — not in — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 28, 1887

*Back to Montrose.*

After a restful night up at 7.30. Bkfs.
at a little before 8 with Edw. Geo., Walter
+ Valentine — left the house at 8.10 and
stopped at O'Neill's for a pair of Rubbers
got away comfortably on No. 3 — 9 A.M.
Train — and dined at Scranton — the
latter part of the ride rather hot +
dirty + my head threatening to ache.
Got to M. depot on time — Livery Stable
buggy waiting for me — Had a quick
+ delightfully refreshing ride home
where I am. at 4.15 finding Annie
+ Jim all right. I spent what
remained of the afternoon loafing
about — delightful moonlight evg
was spent upon piazza until 10 o'clk
— then to bed.
