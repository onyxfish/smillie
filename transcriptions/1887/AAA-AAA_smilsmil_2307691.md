## Left Page

### May, Sunday 22. 1887.

*went to Po'keepsie.*

Cool m'f. + overcast. Took
9 o'clk Train to Po'k — getting to
the Terrace about noon. Found
Mother somewhat brighter but
suffering much discomfort from
her raw mouth & throat. I
spent as much time with her as
she had strength to bear. April
cleared very hot. I did not go
out of the house during the day.
About 5.40 I went to depot to learn
that the 6 o/s Train was 1 3/4 hours
late. I tramped up to the Po'k bridge
& about there until after 7 — then again
to depot — got a light supper — Train
started for N.Y. at 7. 48. Got in to
G. C. D. at 9. 45 — at once home —
found Geo. & Chas. there. — To bed
at about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 23. 1887.

again hot weather — To studio's
after 10 — a woman trying to clean
Waller's studio on Sat. made little
impression — wrote a note to Beman
that he must send a man & hurry up
matters. I had a dumb dutchman,
Wolfe, by name, trying to put up the
shades. I worked on Liberty —
Home to lunch at 1. 45 after going
to Bank & depositing the proceeds
of sale of two paintings at N.A. of D.
— back at 3 — worried with Wolf —
at 4 — to 755 W 47' S. Dr. W. D. Allee —
Paid him $50 — on my way back to
studio stopped at Newmans & ordered
a spark catcher for Montrose. To studio
at 5. Made paste & mounted Liberty
— home about 7 — dinner. Sweet
Chas. came in — after he left I
wrote a letter to Mother on her birth-
day to-morrow — at 10.30 to G. C. D.
mailed it — To bed at 11.
