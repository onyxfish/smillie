## Left Page

### January, Sunday 30, 1887

Raw, overcast & foggy with some
rain. Bkft between 9 & 10 —
sat with paper until near
noon — then started out with
Jim but was driven back by
rain. Then went to studio
& dawdled & tracked down 2 [illegible]
of Neighbors Howland — Home to
dinner at 2 — At 4.30 out
with Jim for an hour — Very
light meal at 6.30 — Wrote a
letter of condolence to Mr. Shuninger
on death of Mrs. Wayland, her
mother, a note to Schoff & my
letter home — At 9.30 went to G.
C. D. with them. On return
home found Charl there. He
left at 10.30 — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 31, 1887

Bright pleasant day — To
studio about 10 — At work over
many little things until about
12 when I went to Union Sq.
Hotel with [illegible] to get Winslow
Homer's signature — not in — then
to N.A. & learned with pleasure
that Mr. W. D. Sloane has bought my
"Old Mill — Crecy." Home to lunch.
Sent checks to Herrick & Morris —
To studio at 3 — Went to N.A. to
see Greer about Vitchnil [illegible] that
are enquired for. Then to Am.
Art Ass'n. Private View of the Graves
Collection — Home at 6 — dinner.
Evng. fixed a number of im-
perfectly mounted photos for
Charl — about 9 took them up
to him — home before 10 and
to bed soon after.
