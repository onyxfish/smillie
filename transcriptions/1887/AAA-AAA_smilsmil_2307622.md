## Left Page

### January, Sunday 2, 1887

Clear & very cold — all hands got up very late, so that it
was nearly when we had our bkf. Work [illegible] with after
11 — then to studio & fussed
awhile with camera trying
to take reductions of some of
my water-color drawings. Home
to dinner at 2.30 — Chave came
in after 4 — I wrote letters home
& about 5 we went up to D. Yale's
— not in — so we walked up to
59th S. & back — Y. being in he
ret'd to the house with me to
see Jim — who had a fever and
enlarged tonsils — Y. prescribed
— Swett. wrote some letters &
got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 3, 1887

Another very cold day. Studio
at usual time now — that is,
10 o'clk — worked on October
Study; introducing cows — It
took me all the aft. At 1.15
went to Bank — then to Hunters
& get my spectacles — near 2
when I got home to lunch —
About 2.30 Yale came in — Jim
seems to have improved, altho'
his fever has not all gone — To
studio about 3 — about 3.30 Yale
came in — At 4 I started for
51 W. 10th St — Y. walking part way
with me — Called on Nicoll to
talk with him about putting the
Etching Club Exhibit in Corridor
instead of West Gallery as before —
Home at 6, after stopping at Studios.
Dinner — At 8.30 went to Chave's
"dark room" by appointment — C. not
there — very cold — back home — At q. 15
C. came but being too late, instead
of going to dark room I went to bed
