## Left Page

### July, Saturday 23. 1887.

Steady + heavy rain during part of the m't. Then continued overcast — very heavy rain for sometime in the afternoon — after which it cleared with a sunset that gave signs of a change for the better. Hemdc came + we worked together to get the studio's finished and cleared out — and finally we succeeded in doing it — I spent some time in washing my big window. Between 8 + 9 o'clk. I went to P.O. + other stores — then home — sat + read awhile to Annie + to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 24. 1887.

Muggy, warm + overcast all day with some light showers. I did not go away from the house — did a little in the studio + then to work writing letters — I got one to S. C. F. Hammond — Belle Tweedy — Mary Sprague Coat. Chat Collins + Mrs. Luck written + took them to P.O. after 9 P.M. Part of the afternoon I read aloud to Annie. Boy No. 2. very fretful during the day. Eve'g read aloud from Aug. Harper to A — To bed soon after 10. Rain during the night.
