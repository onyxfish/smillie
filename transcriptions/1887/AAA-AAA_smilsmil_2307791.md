## Left Page

### December, Thursday 8, 1887

Light rain in the m'g, clearing about noon — delightful afternoon.
To studio after 9 — To work on Water.
Cart. S. A. street scene — drawing for
B. V. Co. Geo. (Grady's man) came
after 11 & began putting up picture mouldings —
Home to lunch at 1 — after that, went with Annie to Sloan's
& to Johnson's & Faulkner's looking
for window stuffs, portieres, etc. —
I got to studio soon after 4 & there
found S. R. Koehler, to see me about
contributions for Nat. Museum at
Washington — Home soon after 5 —
dinner — Eve'g spent reading
portions of my old journals kept while
I was abroad with Father in 1862 —
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 9, 1887

Pleasant day — cloudy. To studio
between 9 & 10 — did little work in
the m'g on A. B. V. Co. drawing —
most of the time with Mr Koehler
selecting material (sketches, studies
etc.) that I have given him for the
National museum at Washington.
He has been appointed curator &
is trying gather a collection of
works to represent the graphic
arts of the country — Home to
lunch — after that went to
2° Phil. Rehearsal — after
that, at 4 o'clk, to studios where
I spent an hour in the interests
of order & cleanliness — Home
at 5:15. dinner — at 8 went with
A. to 42nd St. There for half an hour,
then left her & went to Etching Club
meeting at Shelton's room in "The Alpine" —
Busy & 33°. S. — Home at 10. Found Charl
waiting for me — we talked general
business until 11 — about probable dissolution
of the firm of Winter & Smillie — To bed at
11.30.
