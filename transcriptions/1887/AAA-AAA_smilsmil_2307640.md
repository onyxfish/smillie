## Left Page

### February, Monday 7, 1887

*Went to Po'keepsie.*

Another nasty day. Went to P'k on 10.30 train getting to the Terrace
a little before 1 — Found Mother very feeble but suffering no pain —
Her appearance was not reassuring — she looks very old & thin
& scarcely eats anything — We had dinner soon after — I sat with
mother all the afternoon and looked over many of Father's
working pfs — making some selections for uncle Willie — at
5.30 — ate a little — Bid goodbyes to Mother & others & at 10m before 6
started for depot — snowing hard —
6.20 train, got to 42d St. at 8.45
then home. Jim has not been
well today — again heavy and
inclined to sleep — To bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 8, 1887

Another beastly day — Raw, foggy
& wet. Very late when I got to
studio — The remainder of [illegible]
spent in putting the place in
some order & preparing to begin
some blk. & white drawings for
A.B.N. Co. Home at 1.30 — back
at 3 & see the rest of afternoon
until 6. mounting etching pfs —
then home to dinner. Eve'g —
writing letters — 9°. Cook came
in before 9 — on Jackson Iron Co.
business — Charl came in a
little before 10 & stayed nearly
an hour — To bed soon after
11.
