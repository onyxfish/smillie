## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 18. 1887.

Overcast in the m'g with a few spatters of rain but it proved to be a dry shower — clearing not again. To studio's soon after 10 — time divided between carpentin & Liberty drawing — Home soon after 1 — back at 2.30 — worked until 4.30 — then to Arnold's & Constable's & bought curtains for Montrose studio — then down to Sargent's in Bdway below 12th S. & looked at some commodes — Home at 6.30 — dinner — at 8.30 att. A. of D. Special meeting to act upon proposed amendments to the Constitution — no quorum — an informal discussion that dragged wearily to me so that I left at 10.30 — home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 19. 1887.

Hot & dry — To studio's at 10 — made better progress on Liberty drawing — Home to lunch as usual & back before 3 — worked until 4.40 — then to Dr. Allen's in 47th S. — by appointment that he might take a look at my teeth — all right — out at 5.30 — walked over to 45th S. bridge over G.C. Depot tracks & for nearly half an hour watched the ceaseless coming & going of trains & the inexplicable movements of the yard engines. At 6 — went to Dr. Yale's — nothing — Home to dinner at 6.30 — Ev. f. Geo. & Nellie came in for an hour — I wrote a note to Henick & sent A's check for $100. to Dud. — Saturals — then out, posted letters — went to 6th Ave. & got a pint of Ice cream — Home at 10 & ate it with A — To bed at 10.30 —
