## Left Page

### December, Monday 12, 1887

Overcast but not unpleasant — Got up feeling very seedy —
To studio about 10.30 — most of my time spent putting away
small things — Will sent up for drawings & I sent to him
the Water Cart. Home at 1 —
back at 2.30 — Gas men put
in some small fixtures — I
made paste & mounted two
A.B.N.Co. Tracings preparatory
to making drawings — left
plain copy of Ports & Etchers with
Turner to sell & a proof copy
with Sarah Leggett (Mrs. Many).
Home at 6 — Swelf writing up
old accts of back years —
after 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 13, 1887

Charming day — To studio at 10 —
At 11 Mr. J. D. Lange & his friend
came — Mr. L. decided to take "the Edge
of the Wood" & he paid me $75 for it.
Then Dr. Yale came in — Then Conkling,
machinist from A.B.N.Co. came in — To
him I gave a note to Will telling him
to send me certain Rio photos. Then
Annie & Jim came in & together we
walked up home to lunch — back
before 2.30 — Messenger came from
Will with 1 photos — Then 2 machinists
who took away my printing press — I
did a little work tracing portrait
photo of Mr. Ge'd. Rives — Mr. Frank
Waller called — Home soon after 6 —
Dinner. Swelf some writing
also reading old California
this diary — To bed at 11.30
