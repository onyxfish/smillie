## Left Page

### May, Sunday 5, 1872

Up at 8.30 very headachy washed, dressed \+ brakfasted then gave up \- on sofa all day in a perfect purgatory \- Up at 8.30 \- about 10 p.m. took tea and to bed at 3 o'clk. a. m.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 6, 1872

Splendid day \- got to studio at 9.10 \- Spent part of A. M. getting in Colman's big easel \+ some other things at 11 commenced working over a drawing by Geo. \+ abt. until 1.30 \- Jno. Rogers, his Sister \+ another yng lady called \- Home to lunch at 1.45 \- back at 3, Morse waiting \- commenced again on "Snowy Ylivanis" drawing \- Elisha Mulford called, then Geo. P. Putnam \- Then Meta Adams, then Colpcutt \- but I got my drawing finished at about 6.30 \- Uncles A. \+ Mr. J. were at Studio in the mtf, Uncle a. offers to buy my "Moat Mt. \+ Saco River" Home to tea at 7 \- Eve'g Reading Colpcutt's M. S. \-
To bed at 11 \-
