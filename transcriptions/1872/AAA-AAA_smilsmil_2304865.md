## Left Page

### May, Sunday 19, 1872

Delightful Rain at last. Brkfst about 9 o'clk — Soon after 11 the Rain so abated that I started for home, which I reached about 11.45 — got the things I was after, went to studio, got more things then walked over to 8th ave — at 12 45 again started for M.ville — got to Uncle a's, a few minutes before 2, just in time for dinner. The P.M. spent Reading \+ chatting. Young Burrell came up — Tea at 6. Beautiful Sunset — walked for some time — the moonlight delightful — To bed at about 10.30

## Right Page

### May, Monday 20, 1872

Bright \+ beautiful m'f. Got up very stiff having caught cold on Saturday. Rode mth Uncle a in the m'f so that it was after 11 when I got to work. About noon a thunder squall came up and soon after 1, another quite heavy, so that I got no work done. After lunch got at it again \+ worked quite steadely until near 5. Mr. Mc[illegible] \+ Son called in a grand turn out. Dinner about 5.30 — Ev'f. went to Harlem with Uncle a — back at 8 — more showers. Read \+ wrote a note to Mr. Wales \+ to bed at 10 —
