## Left Page

### February Friday, 9 1872

Overcast, blustering and cold. Had a "head on me", as might have been expected after last night — got to studio's at 10 — Mr. Hewitt waiting for me — fire out — Kindled it — stretched some canvases and otherwise busied myself until home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 at 3 — went to Astor Library — Saw Mr. Saunders — hunted up illustrations of the Coffee Plant — made me tracings \+ back to studio's at 4.20 — Then Miss Woodward \+ Mr. Hawley came in — stayed until dusk. Home to tea at 6.30 — evening mounted three photos — \+ got to bed at 10

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 10 1872

Overcast most of the day; clear p.m. \+ wet. Mr. went to cor. 22 Ave. \+ 42d St. \+ found a coffee plant in possession of a Mr. Baker — got sketch of it \+ then to studio's at 11.30 Sat for Angero from 12.15 until 1 o'clk. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.20 \+ worked until 5 o'clk. on drawing of Coffee — Home to tea at 6.20 — Surprised to find there Uncle Wm Turrell \+ Aunt H — they went to the Astor House at 10 p.m. — evening spent chatting with them — To bed at 10.45
