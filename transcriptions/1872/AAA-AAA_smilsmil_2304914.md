## Left Page

### August, Thursday 29, 1872

Up a little before 8. Overcast mrg. Bkfst at 9.30 — About 10.30 it began to rain \+ we gave up all ideas of going to Watkins Glen. Got to work on "Meck" or Rock Slide \+ worked closely until 2.45 then a cup of tea. — Played billiards with Jack until supper at 6. Bray Hall, his mother \+ Miss [illegible] at supper. Walking on piazza and on carriage road for nearly an hour. — The eveg. Spent in billiards room until near 10. To bed soon after 11. — Wrote letter home for noon mail.

Walking about the house with Allie \+ Josie when I was proposed to take a long tramp. — Horses ordered, when we rode to water cure. — Left carriage \+ walked up the hill — crossed lots \+ came down a very rough, steep place to the meadows back of the house. Very fresh, invigorating air — sky heavily clouded with now \+ then a spatting shower — rubrab enough to wet. Home abt. 30 — dinner soon after. — Then to work commenced "views from foot of Sentinel Fall" — Overcome with sleep. About 3.30 — down on the floor in my big blanket and slept for an hour. — Then worked until 6. — Then played billiards with Jack for an hour. — Tea at 7.30 — eveg. Bray Hall \+ Miss Van Duzer called.
