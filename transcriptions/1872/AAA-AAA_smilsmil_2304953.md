## Left Page

### November Sunday, 17, 1872.

Clear wintry day. In
bed all day with a
headache. Got up
about 6 o'clk. P. M.
Sat in my big chair
until 3. a. m. — then to
bed and to sleep shortly
after 5 a.m.

## Right Page

### November Monday, 18, 1872.

A pleasant day. Shaky,
as usual, after yesterdays
spree. Did not get to the
studio until after 10. Kindled
fire and about 11 o'clk. started
painting on 11×14. Yosemite
worked until 12.20 then
to barber's & had hair cut &
head shampooed. Home to
lunch at 1.40 — back to
studio at 2.30 & painted
until 4.30. Home to tea
at 6. Evng. writing
addresses on envelopes for
Matt and to bed at 11.
