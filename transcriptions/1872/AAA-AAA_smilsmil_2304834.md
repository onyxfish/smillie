## Left Page

### March, Thursday, 14, 1872

Springlike day — overcast mrg. \+ evg. — clear in the middle of the day — Rain at night — got to studio about 9 o'clk — found some W.C. letters that required attent. — wrote answers — went to N.A. of D — then back to work — could not work — pain in my head. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 o'clk. a number of calls during the afternoon — Head getting worse. At 5 o'clk went over to N.A. of D — then to Wm. Danl. Huntington's — then to Mr. Murray's 2 E. 15th S. — Then to A.P. Ryder's. 3 4 8 W. 35th S. — After 7 when I got home — Evg. so used up with my headache as to be incapable of doing any thing.

## Right Page

### March, Friday, 15, 1872

Cold \+ very windy — made two or three notes \+ got to studio soon after 9 — found more notes \+ answered — went to N.A. of D — then to Louis Tiffany's \+ Tileck's \+ back at 11 o'clk — worked on "Little Joker" drawing for A.B.N.C. Home to lunch at 1.30 — then to Phil. Rehearsal. After, with Belle \+ Mary J. — went to Snedecor's — Then to Studio — worked half an hour — then with the girls to St. Denis Hotel. Home to tea at 7 — Evg. writing letters \+ to bed at 11 — Uncle Willie arr'd — from Poughkeepsie —
