## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 26 1872.

Uncle a. surprised us by making us this m't, having just arr'd from the Catskill Mts. weather still heavy. — Drove in town with Uncle a. Stopped at home — back at 1.25 — lunch soon after — then to work on small drawing for a,B. N. Co — Atlanta Horse Cr — at it until dinner time 5.30 and after dinner as long as I could. Eve — Every sauntering about until 9 o'clk. — Read Mrs. Yelverton's absurd "Yo Semite Story" Zanita until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### June Thursday, 27 1872.

Exceedingly close, muggy and overcast, at times the sun coming out scorchingly. Got to work soon after bkfst — on block "Sentinel Rock — from the north side" — worked until lunch at 1 — and then again until 5 o'clk. when I got done with it. In a cheery nee until about 6. Matt came up with Charl to go to New Haven with Uncle a \+ [illegible] Orville Balcer, Mr. B. P. Balcer daughter \+ child \+ Mrs. Magom called while we were at dinn. Every spent walking and sitting about until after 10. To bed at 10.30.
