## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 12 1872.

Up before 7 & had an early
Help. Day overcast & windy
but not really cold. Did
not dare to work out of doors
altho' my cold is much better.
Commenced drawing "Sentinel
Rock" on the wood. Made
but little progress. Dinner
abt 1.30. Jack has gone to
Binghamton to bring home
his bro. Geo's wife & family.
Worked on drawing until 6.
Then practiced billiards, Bray
Hall came in at 7.30. Tea
— Poker — feeling very much
under the weather. No Jack
but Lucy & family passed him.
Heavy Rain commenced abt.
9 o'clk. Bray had to stay
all night. Sent to depot for
Jack, at midnight — no Jack —
got to bed at abt 12.30

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 13 1872.

Up at 7.30 — Headache — a
charming mrg. Took a cup
of tea & a cracker. Rolled
myself in an afghan and
sat on front piazza for a
while — then in lounge in
Maud's Room — there until
6 o'clk — then up, washed &
had tea — no word of Jack
until near 8 o'clk, when he
presented himself in person.
Even some poker, as usual.
Allie & I on the rug in front
of the fire until 11 o'clk.
Then to my Room — glorious
cloudless moonlight.
