## Left Page

### October Friday, 4 1872.

A most glorious day — After bkfst drove Miss Morris over
to the fair ground — the fog
had yet hardly cleared away.
When within fair enclosure
the nigh horse behaved badly —
jumping & pulling — Miss M.
frightened — Ret'd to house with
Mr & Miss M. about 11.30 — had
a lunch & at 12.15 W. & Miss M.
Allie & May started for N.Y. —
Cap't & I harnessed the grey —
drove into town — Stopped at
Mary Reed's — Josie Reutty has
gone home — back at 2 — At
3 I started on the grey — again
into town & along the Chemung
for about five miles — perfectly
enchanting — turned & came over
the hills & out by Aleck's Reservoir —
Home at 5.45 — having ridden about
12 miles. Tea before 7 — We &
Aleck & his wife at the house —
Jack & I hanging pictures —
got to bed soon after 11 —

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 5 1872.

Another very charming day.
It was about 10 o'clk. when
I got to work in the Carriage
house making crayon sketches
from the grey's legs — At it
until nearly — then in to
lunch — At 2.30 again
at work & kept at it until
about 5 — but did not get
a very good day's work
as after fair dissipation
& my ride of yesterday,
I feel tired — Tea soon
after 6 — Eugene wife &
child to tea — Surely W. &
Mr. D. Cap't & I at whist
until almost 10.30 — To bed
at 11 —
