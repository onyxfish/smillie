## Left Page

### May, Thursday 23, 1872

Considerable Rain during
the night. Muggy morning —
warm day with heavy showers.
Spent all the m'f re-miting
some Mail letters — lunch
at 1 and all the afternoon
until 5 o'clk — outrunning on
A.B.N. Co. drawing of their
building — Dinner at 6.
Every cleared gloriously
walked until near 8 o'clk.
enjoying the fresh breeze,
Until 10 o'clk, looking
over Chicago Committee
letters & papers.
Then to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 24, 1872

Woke at 5 o'clk. and came near
getting up, but thinking better of it,
went to sleep again until after
7 — then up & b[illegible] at 8 — morning
rather milky, got to work on the
drawing of house but the weather
thickened up so that I gave up
& started for home in 10.30 stage.
Stopped at house, then to Studio
& partly packed Mulford's book.
Home to lunch at 2 o'clk and
during the rest of P.M. worked
at home on A.B.N. Co's drawing of
their building — Tea at 6.30
at 8, again went to Studio &
finished packing book, then
went to office & arranged for
expressing it. Home at 10.
Took a sort of bath and
to bed at 11.
