## Left Page

### May, Friday 17, 1872

Dry, cloudless weather still continues. Got to studio at 9.5
\+ to work on small sketches for J o Smillie Article, worked
all the m'f, with great interest.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 — Mary \+ Belle Truedy
called between 3 \+ 4 o'clk \+
at 4.30 I went to Appleton's
\+ showed Bunce my sketches
with which he seemed very
much pleased. Home at
7.10 — Eve'g at 8, called on
the Tiffanys — then on the
Colvans', who said to morrow
\+ bid them good bye — Home
at 10 o'clk. Mounted paper
wrote two notes \+ to bed at
11.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 18, 1872

Dry \+ windy — Rode to Uncle.
a's in 9 o'clk stage, prepared
to commence drawing views
of his house. Spent m'f-
ing Table out of doors and
outlining subject, us'ng camera
lucida. Lunched ab't, all
alone, the others at Ravenswood.
Worked until 4 o'clk, got
am'd about 5. Took tea together
at 6. Uncle a. \+ the rest am'd
about 8. I read \+ chatted
until near 10, then to bed.
