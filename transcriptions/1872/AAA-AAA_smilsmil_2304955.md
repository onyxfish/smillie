## Left Page

### November Thursday, 21 1872

Fine day — got to studio at 9.30.
Spent the mf., making sketch
of Big Trees (Jallery) — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.20. Worked
on View of Golden Gate until
4.15 — drawing to be engraved
on steel for Appletons — went
to A's at 4.30 — Saw Bunce —
arranged to eng. steel plate
4 mts short Yo-Semite article
for the "Journal" — on my way
up, stopped at Lauthiers. Then
at Studio — Clapp called & I
bought 3 etchings — Home to
tea at 6 — Wert spent over
Matts cards until 10.15 —
To bed about 10.45

## Right Page

### November Friday, 22 1872

Heavy rain & very dark. Got
to studio at 9.30 and spent
the mf., continuing Golden Gate
drawing. George came down
to studio. Home to lunch
at 1.15 — back at 2.30 & spent
the afternoon charcoaling
new subject of "Descent into
the Yo-Semite Valley" for
steel plate engraving. Home
to tea at 6.45. Evening
spent looking over English
Illustrated papers. To bed
at 10.45.
