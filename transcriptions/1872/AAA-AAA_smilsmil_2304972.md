## Left Page

### December Friday, 27 1872.

Still snowing during the mrg, but in the afternoon it cleared charmingly. Got to Studio at 9.30 & worked all the morning on Yo-Semite fall, finishing abt 1 o'clk, when I prepared lunch. Afternoon, worked on Cathedral Spires (1 page) tracing, transferring & outlining it on the block — could not see to work after 4.45 —
Home at 6. Evening, until near 11 o'clk mounting photos. Then to bed.

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 28 1872.

Splendid winter day — 10 o'clk. when I got to the Studio, to work on Cathedral Spires (block page) & at it until 1. Home to lunch abt 1.15, back at 2.15. McMaurand called with his wife — has just engraved my block "Good News to Ship" — can't speak a word of english — Mr. M. who speaks a very little acts as interpreter — worked as long as I could see. At 5, went to Snedcor's — missed him — Called in at Snedecor's — back to Studio at 6 and home at 6.30. Eve'g, mounting more photos. and to bed at 11.
