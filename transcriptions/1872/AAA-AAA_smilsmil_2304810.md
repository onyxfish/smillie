## Left Page

### January, Monday 22, 1872

Fine day — at home working on drawings — part of my worked on Glacier Rock Sketch the South Dome however engaged my attention until after 4 o'clk P.M. — Then went to Studio with sketches. While there went in to see Misses Blauvelt \+ Woodward, who were having a Reception — Then to N.A. of D. with drawings then to Y.M.C.A. Library and Read up on Mannerism \+ home to tea at 7.20 — Evef Read and wrote and got to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 23, 1872

a sort of a pleasant day. At work on "Half Dome from Cloud's Rest" until 4½ o'clk, when I gave 'twp \+ carried it to the N.A. of D — There saw the half-hung exhibitn of Water Colors and saw that mine were miserable in color \+ execution — At 5.30 went to Turkish Bath \+ had a thorough pummeling and scrubbing — Home to tea at 7. Swift, Read some \+ wrote two or three notes — got to bed at 10.30
