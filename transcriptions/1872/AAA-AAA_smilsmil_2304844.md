## Left Page

### April, Friday, 5, 1872

Delightful day. Got to studio at 8.45 \+ to work on that bête nair, the A.B.N. Co. architectural drawing — did very little, feeling horribly tired. Soon after 11 went to J.M. C. a Beds, stopping at Dr. Adam's where the Morse funeral was in progress. Called on Wood and Gifford \+ Ge— the others were out — Then went to Tailors in 22 St \+ ordered pantaloons \+ vest — Home to lunch at 1 — At Phil. Rehearsal at 2.15 — After Rehearsal, went with Matt to 17 University Pl. \+ called on Miss Anna Dawns of Mass., then to Sommerville Gallery — called on Hofse. Walked up to studio with Sam. Coleman. Home to tea at 7 — Back to studio at 8.30, wrote until 10 — Home at 10.15 and to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 6, 1872

Delightful day. Got to studio a little before 9 o'clk. Mr. Dr. Bartlett called — then W. Ge— Whitney. At 10.30 left with him \+ walked to 24 St. Then I called on Carpenter — nob'in — then to Sommerville Gallery. Met Coleman \+ others — At 11.15 went with C. to 22d Regt. Armory \+ gave directions for picture gallery — at 12 went to 10th St. Studios — saw several fellows and home to lunch at 1.30 — At A.B.N. Co. at 3 — left at 3.45 — called at Hatch \+ Co.s — then to 10th Beds again — then on Hofse at 82 Fifth Ave. \+ home to tea at 6.15 — May \+ Belle J— there — at 8 at Phil. Concert — took girls home, then met the Kimball's — went in there — stayed until 11.45 then home \+ to bed at 12.15.
