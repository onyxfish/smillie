## Left Page

### December Monday, 9 1872

Overcast muf, snow during
the P.M. Swerf, brilliant
moonlight + very cold and
windy. Got to Studio at 9.30
Kindled fire — freshened up
+ set a charcoal drawing by
Saml. Colman — Finished block
"Fallen Sequoia" + traced and
Transferred "View from Cloud's
Rest" — Home to lunch at 1.15 —
back at 2.15 — worked until
4.30 on Cloud's Rest block — Then
wrote a note to A.T. Bellows — then
Charcoaled 12×20." View in the
Yosemite" — Matts wedding present —
Home to tea at 6.15 — Swerf
at 8 o'clk — called on Mr. & Mrs.
Tiffany — not in — Then to Mr. & Mrs.
Charley Smith's (Corinne Coleman
18[?]) found them in + visited
until 10.30 — Home abt 11 — To bed
at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 10 1872

Savagely cold, windy day — Got to
studio at 9.30 — worked on Cloud's
Rest block until about 11 when Mr.
Walter Brown came in. He had
some important news about Water Color
affairs + invited me to lunch at his
house with Clarence Cook — I went
at once to N.A. of D — Saw S.A. Richards
then to Mr. Harts — Myants Gifford's and
Tiffany's — then home at 12.15 — dressed
at W. Brown's at 12.45 — Lunch & am
aftn 1 — fried oysters with new cider
topped off with some fine old Marsala
(Roman) Wine — Mr. E.C. very agreeable.
3 o'clk before I got to studio, worked
until 4 — then to 10[?]S; — Saw Beard,
Perry, Bradford + J.G. Brown — back
to studio + home at 6.15 — Swerf
wrote a letter to Saml. Colman
+ got to bed at 10.30
