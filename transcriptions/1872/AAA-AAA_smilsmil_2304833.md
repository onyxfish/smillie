## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 12 1872

mf, snow falling heavily and nearly 4 in. deep — about 8.30 started for down-town — Met Will \+ went through Hatch's Lithographic establishment — at a B.N.Co. at 10.30 saw Van — went to Union Trust Co — then to Comm. of Taxes — then to Appleton's — saw Bunce \+ was told to go ahead with Yo Semite article — then to Sauthier's — then N.A.of D — then Silleck's — then Studio — then Fifth Nat. Bank — Re-opened acct — Home to lunch at 1.40 — By this time all was bright overhead \+ snow running away fast — Back to Studio at 3 o'clk — worked a little on W.C. study made in North Conway — M. G. D. Morgan called about W.C. drawings he has bought — then Misses Bruce \+ Bright — Then a young man from M[illegible]John on Society work until 6.30 — Home to tea at 7 — Every some more writing \+ to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 13 1872

Fine day. Got to Studio at 8.45 — at 9.30 — went to N.A.of D. there an hour — then back to Studio — worked on Study from Nature, North Conway meadows — Water Color — much interrupted by W.C. business — Home to lunch at 1.45 — wrote notes to J. Rondel \+ Misses Bridges \+ Bigelow — Back to Studio at 3.15 — Miss Bascomb came \+ drew for an hour — at 5.30 — went to Sauthier — Back to Studio at 6.30 — Home to tea at 7 — Every wrote to W. S. Richards, Ed. Moran \+ W. Morgan — To bed at 10.15
