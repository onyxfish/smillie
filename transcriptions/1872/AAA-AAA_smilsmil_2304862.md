## Left Page

### May, Monday, 13, 1872

Delightful day. Up early & about
the place — Bkfst before 8 & after,
J wine. Uncle Wm, Gel & I Rode
into the city — After stopping at
the house, got to studio at 9.45
Set palette & to work on 10×18
Mr. Diablo & San Joaquim River
wedding present for Louis Tiffany
worked until 1.15 then home
to lunch — back at 2.45 and
worked until 6 — getting it
done after a fashion — Home to
tea at 6.30 — Father & Mother at
Uncle A's — Young Hewitt called
at the house & I delivered to him
Coffee No. 3 — Spent most of the
evening fixing up Report as Treas. of
Art Com. of Hon. Fair — Got to bed
at about 11.30 after washing brushes,
& drinking ale with Charl
& the girls.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday, 14, 1872

Still delightful weather — Got to
studio at 9.15 — A son of Borse,
a court clerk waiting for me, to let
me know that Gel is on the jury.
Set palette & commenced painting
"A bit on the Mariposa Trail"
11×14 in. Painted until 1.15
then home to lunch, back at 2.30
painted until 6.15 when I
got it all in & cleaned up —
Home to tea at 7 — Went
at 8 o'clc at Clinton Hall, at
a Leavitt Book Sale — Bought
a superb copy of Turner's
Gallery for Elisha Mulford,
for $19.5 [illegible] — Home at 10.15 —
washed brushes & got to bed
at 11.
