## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 24, 1872

Up about 5. bkfst. at 6 \+ nearly 6.30 before we got away. Glorious mfg — Cool, cloudy and at times with some threatening drops of Rain 2 or 3 miles beyond Champney's the road became horrible \+ so continued for about 8 miles. 18 miles beyond Minerva, (where we stayed last night) came to the "old State Road", a very good one — Got to Davis's, 22 miles, at 12.20 — 14 miles yet to go to Long Lake — Had a first Rate dinner \+ at 2.20 off again. Some very good \+ some very bad road, a large part of the way through very close woods. at 5 o'clk a very heavy Rain Storm but we were all so well prepared that it was only fun — Got to Long Lake village at 6 — went to Mitchel Sebbatis' — then to Kellogg's — public house — Had a Trout supper at 8 o'clk and then a right jolly time after — So bed at 9.45

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 25, 1872

Up at about 5.30 after a very sound sleep. Fog that soon lifted leaving a cloudless morning. Bkfst. about 6.30 — At 8.30 the party got off in 2 boats with 2 guides, every thing perfectly charming — Beautiful cloud effects — After a 50 minutes row, got to the mouth of the Racquette River — a short carry and again the boats 15 or 20 minutes row and another carry of less than ½ a mile then quite as long spell on the River then the long carry of 1½ miles. We reached Forked Lake at 11.40, waited ¾ an hour for Miss Mary Cook to come up — then off — perfectly delightful still. Got to the Carry at about .30 \+ walked the mile to Carry's at once — a miserably, uninviting place, dinner ordered — while waiting, went down to Racquette Lake — Dinner at 2.45 — all horribly hungry — pork, pie and crullers — as the bread was pretty good I got through. About 4 o'clk got started for Camp 2, a mile \+ a half down the lake — ½ an hour getting there — then at work clearing up, fixing beds etc. etc — everybody jolly — coffee \+ crackers at 6.30 — general Jollification until 10.30 when all turned in — much fun caused by Mary Cook's energetic calls for Mitchel to build a fire at about 2 o'clk. a.m.
