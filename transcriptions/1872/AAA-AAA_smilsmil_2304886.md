## Left Page

### June, Sunday, 30, 1872.

Another intensely hot day. Up at 5,30 — morning delicious — out on piazza \+ Cater's point until 9 o'clk — then bkfst — all the rest of the mng, loafing about under the walnuts. Dre —
Dinner about two — then more loafing — anything to escape the extreme heat.
Took a troublous nap in the Billiard Room floor — Joining Turrell \+ Gel. McCabe to tea. At 8 Uncle A. drew down with me to 594. 8th — home about 9 — Changed my plans so as to start for Po'Keepsie to-morrow eve —
To bed soon after 11 —

## Right Page

### July, Monday, 1, 1872.

Another scorcher — up at 6. After bkfst, went to Kehm's, had hair cut \+ had shampoo'd — then to studio \+ then home \+ to work at about 10 o'clk. Started drawing of New Brunswick arms for A.B.N. Co. Bet. 11 \+ 12 o'clk Father came in, just Ret'd from Hill Side. Lunch at 1 — Worked until 3. then packed \+ at 4 started with the girls for the "Miller" foot of Franklin St in Cars, met Charlie Tweedy — did not get away until shortly after 6 — The sail up the River was very delightful. Parker took us all up to the Pilot house, where we enjoyed unobstructed view \+ breeze. Turned in at 9,30 and got through the night tolerably well —
