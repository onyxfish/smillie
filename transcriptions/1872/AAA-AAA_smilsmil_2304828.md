## Left Page

### February Thursday, 29, 1872

Overcast and threatening — got to studio at 9 o'clt — Mr. Hewitt called soon after. Tried to get at my work but suffered severely with pain in the stomach, so that I did nothing. Ab't 2 I went to Astor Library — met H — then to Buchanans, the florist. Got Coffee leavers, etc. Home to lunch at 2 — back to studio at 3 — Used up with pain — Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g, Reading, — Parkin went home at 3 P.M — got to bed, taking my big pain with me, at 10.30

## Right Page

### March Friday, 1, 1872

Beautiful day. Called on young Hewitt at 49 W 42° St. Then to studio at 9.20, feeling very slim. Found my coffee leaves very much withered arranged them as well as I could \+ painted a study. Home to lunch ab't 1.30 \+ at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal walked with Gel \+ then I medys to Ma, \+ D. — Looked about there for a time, then with Florence T. to 71° \+ St. — left here \+ called on the Divens in 72° St. Then back to studio at 7 o'clk — Fired fire \+ cleared up — as I was going out the bldg. met a young woman who begged my escort as a man was annoying her. Took her to her home in 25th St. bet. 7th \+ 8th aves. Home to tea at 8 o'clk — Eve'g doing nothing — To bed ab't 9.45
