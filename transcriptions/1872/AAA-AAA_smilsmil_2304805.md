## Left Page

### January, Friday, 12, 1872.

Weathr still charming — got to studio at 9, 45 and to work on drapery — at it until 1.30 — then home to lunch — wrote little letter to Mrs. McHarg, accompanying photo. It was 3.15 before I got again to studio — Mary Willard \+ a friend called — then Miss Covell \+ 10 or 15 yng. ladies — then Mr. Hart, of Glasgow (we met in the [illegible] Semit) then Ryder, who with Colman walked home with us at 6 — Eveg wrote an Mail letter until 10 — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### January, Saturday, 13, 1872.

A delicious day — got to studio at 9 o'clk — To work on Crayon drawing. Still feeling very much out of sorts and keeping at my work with difficulty. Just as I was going to lunch, Delnoce came in — got to lunch at 1.50 — back to studio at 2.45 — Miss Torney called with a friend — then Corinne Colman Smith \+ husband — then Charley Baker — Newland \+ three young ladies — They stayed until 5 o'clk — then I went to Turkish Bath — J. J. Pease called immediately after I left. Had a severe scrubbing in bath — home to tea at 6.45 — Eveg spent Reading and to bed about 10.20
