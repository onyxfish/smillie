## Right Page

### January, Monday 1, 1872

Very disagreeable day, overcast overhead and muddy underfoot. Up late, feeling little improvement in condition. After lunch, about 2.30 o'clk I started out \+ called on Misses Percy, Treedy, Schuyler, Ripley, Heaton, Fowlers, Martin and Kimball \+ leaving cards at Munroe's, Smith's, Tiffany's and Ireland's. About 8.30 stopped in at Mr. Hyde's where the Treedy's were; had tea there, \+ at 9.30 Rode to St. Cloud Hotel with them; there until 10.30 — then home \+ to-bed about 11.15.
