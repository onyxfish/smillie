## Left Page

### March, Saturday, 2, 1872.

Very cold, bleak and threatening. Got to studio at 9 o'clk. feeling very much used up. Spent the mng. painting a 2º sketch of Coffee leaves from a cutting. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Snow commenced — Afternoon, painted on the 2º "Coffee, flower \+ fruit" for Mr. Hewitt — Home at 6.20 — Med sort of night — Snow \+ wind — Just before 8 o'clk got to Academy of Music — Enjoyed H. Phil. concert very much. Mrs. G. escorted the Smeedys to Mr. Condert's in 43º. S. Home soon after 11 and to bed at 12 o'clk —

## Right Page

### March, Sunday, 3, 1872.

Very miserable with headache and cold in the head — Did not go out of the house all day. and grew worse towards night. got to bed between 10 \+ 11 o'clk.
