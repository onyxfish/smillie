## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 5 1872

Delightful morning — very mild —
later in the day, overcast. After
5lfo. went with Chare & cast my
vote for Grant. Diy & Havemeyer —
every thing very quiet and orderly.
Inice got to the house before I
left. Got to studio about 10.30 —
Ned Dunning & a friend called.
I kindled fire & set to work
trying to get some approach
to order & cleanliness out of the
dirty chaos. Prepared lunch
at 1.30 — Introduced to Mr. [illegible]
an enthusiastic stock-breeding
friend of Angers. Worked until
6.15 clearing out drawers & port-
folios — Home to tea at 7 —
Dr. Returned from a shooting
expedition with his pockets full
of soft shell clams — nothing else.
Every — Resting — [illegible]
with galvanic foot bath and
to bed soon after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 6 1872

Overcast mf. Rain beginning
about 6 clk. & falling heavily
during the evening. Got to
the studio at 9.20 — Spent
all of the mf. arranging
sketches and material, of
which I find that I have a
great load — Dolph called
about noon — Prepared my
lunch at 1.30 — Mr 2 commenced
a little twilight sketch — a
very long way off from a re-
collection of Racquette Lake —
finished it at 4 — Washing
up until near 6 clk.
Went over to the Hoffman Ho.
to Divens there — Home to tea
at 6.30 — Well trying to put
my closets at home to rights
to bed at 10.45
