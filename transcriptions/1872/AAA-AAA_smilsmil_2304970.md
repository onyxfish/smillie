## Left Page

### December Monday, 23, 1872

Not so cold as yesterday —
10 oclk. when I got to the
studio — kindled fire — Revised
M.S. for Bruce + at 11.40 started
for Appleton's — Saw Bruce —
left M.S. with him — got two page
blocks + back to studio at 1 —
Prepared lunch + then to work
making sketch of Cathedral Spire
worked until 4.30 — Cleared
up + soon after 5 went over to
Y.M.C.A. — found that I had
been dispossessed of gymnasium
Closet + my "traps" not to be
found — Home at 6 — tea at
6.30 — Even'g spent Reading
about the Yellowstone and
to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 24, 1872

Another very cold day. Got
to studio at 9.30 + until 2
oclk. drawing pencil sketch
of Senaya Cañon from Glacie
Rock. then Prepared lunch
at 2.30 went to Knoedler's
got brushes etc — etc. back
about 3. Traced 4 0-8-
[illegible] — wrote to Mr Hirch Lewis
\+ note to Jas Sutton — at
5.30 went over to Y.M.C.A.
home at 6. Matt, Mary,
Willie and from Po'keepsie
ca at 6.30 — Even'g about
— Even'g spent buzzing,
went to Burgraf's — + to
Jenning's — To bed at 10.30
