## Left Page

### March Monday, 18 1872

Splendid day — got to studio at 9.30 — after kindling fire, spent the rest of the m'f making alterations on a drawing of the Great Eastern for Uncle Willie — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.15 — worked until 4 o'clk trying to correct my drawing of the little Jolcer — Then to Ruch anans, the florist, to have him correcting painting of Coffee plant. Stopped at Knoedlers \+ at studio at 4.30 worked on charcoal sketches until 6 o'clk — Then went to see Mrs. Dy Bartlette — from there to 35th St. bet. 5th \+ 9th Ave \+ saw A.P. Ryden. Home to tea at 7.45 — wrote one or two notes during the eveg and got to bed about 10.45

## Right Page

### March Tuesday, 19 1872

A mixed March \+ April day — cold, high wind with alternate sunshine \+ shower. Got to studio at 9.30 — set palette \+ Repainted "Coffee" making it look better, I think. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Painted until about 4 on "coffee" — then Repainted water on study of Yosemite Fall — wrote a note to Gifford \+ home to tea at 6.30 — At 8.30 — went to Learitt art Rooms — Snedecors Private view — met there lots of people — home at 10 and to nd at 10.45 Uncle Willie started for home this P.m.
