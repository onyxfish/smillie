## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 16 1872.

A perfectly glorious day \- Bef. soon after 8 \- walked \+ at 9.30 commenced work on "Sentinel block\- ab't it until near noon, when I gave it up, very much dissatisfied \- went out to Stable \+ painted in first study of Daudy. Dinner about 2 \- after dinner went again to Stable \+ worked on a study of the grey (drawing for oil) until near 5 \- then dressed, saddled Daudy and went out to Reformatory School hill \+ to the new Reservoir \- Back at 6.30 \- having had a splendid Ride \- evtf. cloudless \- Tea soon after 7 \- evtf. another glorious moonlight \- Reading \+ playing "Seven up" with Mr. D \- s, to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 17 1872.

Early mtf. overcast, but the weather cleared into a mild \+ charming autumn day \- before 9 o'clk \- It was after 10 before I got to my work in the Stable yard. I Drew in a pair of fore legs (Daudy) \+ painted them in \- before dinner at 1.30 \- Overcast again \+ sight sprinkle of Rain \- After dinner, practi. at billiards. At 3 \- made a crayon study from hind leg of one of the blacks. At 5 o'cl. saddled Daudy and Rode in town \+ did some shopping for Allie \- caught in a sharp shower \- on way back, Splendid storm cloud effect. Enjoyed bath \+ toweling \- Tea soon after 7 \- Allie went to Fireman's Hall \- ours, four. \- Evtf. Old Sledge with Mr. D \- until 10.30 then to bed.
