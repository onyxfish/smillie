## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 20 1872.

Very cold and almost a gale of wind. Got to studio
at 9 o'clk. Headache again so that I could not work with
comfort or steadiness. Worked on second attempt at "Little
Joker" — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Miss Bascomb
at the studio, drawing — I worked on Joker but did not
get it finished. Home to tea at 6.15 — at 7 with Matt & Kate
and M.C.a. to see Mrs. Jarley's
wax works as presented by
a Mr. Countrell for the benefit
of the Art Com. of the Homoeopathic
Fair — Charls — followed — met
then Mrs. Kimball — waited
upser bus home — there until 11 —
then home — To bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 21 1872.

Clear very cold — got to studio
at 9.5 and until 11 o'clk worked
on "Little Joker" — Then charcoaled
on 40-Secunde from Cloud's Rest, 12×20.
Home to lunch at 2 o'clk — back
at 3.15 — Outlining subject of the
mts, Misses Maury, Jones and
Bayard called — at 5 o'clk I
went off after a lot of odds & ends
of business, calling at Knoedler's,
Wilmarth's, Schill's & Lauthier's —
Back to studio at 6.20 — Home
to tea at 7.30 — Every morning
canvas, drawings etc. until
near 11 o'clk — To bed at 11.
