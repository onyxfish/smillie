## Left Page

### April, Monday 15, 1872

Dull day \- at 8.45 called on Mr. Chas. Frost at No. 3 W. 38 S. He was very jovial \+ kept me for an hour looking at his pictures \- Then to Avery's \- then to Page and Penny \- Then to Printer in H. \+ S. \- Catalogue for Fair \+ c. He could'nt do it, so went to Fair \+ saw Mr. Bartlett \- got to studio at 11.30 \- built fire \+ commenced on drawing of head for A.B.N. Co. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- but could'nt work on acct of pain in stomach \- on my lounge most of the P.M. Miserable snow \+ sleet. Home to tea at 7 o'clk. but did not eat until after 8 \- then only a little gruel \+ beef tea \- at 9 o'clk. Rode down to the Fair \- there until near 11 \- home at 11.30 \- to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 16, 1872

Cold, windy \+ March-like \- got to Studio at 9.15 \+ to work on Gaucho Head for A.B.N. Co. \- worked until 12.15 \- then went down to Fair \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back to studio at 2.30 worked until 6.15 when I finished drawing. Stretched out on lounge for few minutes \- Home to tea at 7.30 \- mended drawing and at 9 o'clk went to Fair \- Home at 11.15 and to bed at 11.45.
