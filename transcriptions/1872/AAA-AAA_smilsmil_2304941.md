## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 22 1872

Pleasant day — About 10 o'clk. got to A.B.N. Co — left there at 11 & Rode up to Appleton's. Saw Bunce submitted three blocks & was paid $75.00 — got [illegible] for some of the engraved blocks — Bunce was anxious that I should present a plan for a work to be illustrated with etchings — Home to lunch about 1 o'clk — Touched up [illegible] blocks. At 3.30 Mrs. McStaara called & took Matt out riding — I saw her — then I went again to Appleton's — Men over to Schaad's to see if I could possibly get a Phil. ticket — no use — He offers me a seat in a Proscenium box — Home at 6 — Sea about 7 — twelve spent doing nothing — To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October Wednesday, 23 1872

Dull day — Spitting rain in the afternoon — more in the even. Spent the morning writing letters — Lunch at 1 — in laziness took a nap after dinner then finished letters wrote to Schaad, Josie Benuty — Jack Diven & a letter of condolence to Gen. Mr. Diven on the loss of his daughter — About 4.30 walked up to Will's — Stayed there to dinner and until 10 o'clk — walked home and to bed soon after 11.
