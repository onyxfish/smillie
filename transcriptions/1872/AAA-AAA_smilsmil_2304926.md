## Left Page

### Sept. Sunday, 22 1872

Aml. D. am² home this mng.
very warm & hazy — went to
church with Mrs. D— Heard
a sermon by the Rev.
from 1st Cor. 13 ch. 13th V. Chaining
on our way back, stopped at
Aleck's — then to P.O — then
home — wrote letters to Mary &
to Father & Mother at Hillside.
Dinner at 3.30 — Eugene & wife
to dinner — About 5, Jack &
I harnessed the blacks and
I took out May, Allie & Mrs. Euge.
besides two children to ride.
The evg was delightful and
we rode until many —
Eveg wrote to Maud & Ella
& got to bed about 10 —
A thunder storm came near
us with slight shower about
3 o'clc P.M — again, during the
night, between 3 & 4 A.M — There
was quite a heavy one.

## Right Page

### Sept. Monday, 23 1872

A very warm & luminous fall day.
Spent the mng in Carriage
house with the grey. Worked
with crayons, getting studies
of his eyes & drawing in a
sketch of his hind legs for
an oil study — Dinner som
after 1 — after dinner, went
with Jack & [illegible] & gathered
hickory nuts — very warm work
until 2.30 — Showas after 3
when I again went to work
on grey — Started large
crayon study of face of
grey — worked until 5 — gave
up feeling fevered & very sore
in the region of stomach thru —
Aml. D. went to N.Y. in the evg.
Bray Hall & Jenny M.D. came
in & we spent the eveg with
cards — To bed at 11 feeling
but little better.
