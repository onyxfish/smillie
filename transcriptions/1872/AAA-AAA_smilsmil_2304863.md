## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 15, 1872

Charming day, got to studio at 9.20. Spent the mfg making sketches for Jo-Senite article — Home at 1.30 — at 3, down-town, at W.B.N. Co's \+ other messages Hunting for mounting board etc. — At Lauthier's at 6 — met Smyley — walked to 22d S\. with him. — Stopped at Studio, Home to tea at 7. Fred Smillie at the house overnight — Father, down from M Villes for this night — wrote a little \+ to bed at 9.45

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 16, 1872

Yet another clear day. The drougt is beginning to be felt severely — Rode down to Freucht German exhbn of pictures contributed to Chicago — Then to Parooks Pons — \+ ordered a suit of clothes — got to studio at 10.30 — Worked awhile on "Little Joker" die, but most of the day was spent on drawings for Jo-Senite article. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — at 6 went to S'illecka's then to Boese, 342 3d ave. Home to tea at 7. Evening writing \+ to bed at 10.30
