## Left Page

### December Saturday, 7 1872.

Overcast mrg, but charming
later in the day — Got to studio
at 9.15 & spent all the mrg
until 1.30 working over
Chromolithograph of De
Soto, by Seibler, sent up
to me by Will, who wishes
me to make some improvements in color and effect.
Home to lunch at 1.45, back
at 2.30 — worked until after
4 on "block" "Fallen Sequoias"
Home to tea at 5.30. Eveg
went around to Mr Rogers's —
10b W. 42d St. Had a chatty,
pleasant time until 10.30 —
then home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 8 1872.

Dark day with Rains during
the P.M. — clearing at night —
Mrg went to Hastings' &
heard him preach from Matt
22d c. 45d v. "What think ye
of Christ"? Much of the service
taken up with Sprinkling tables
& installation of governing elders.
Home to dinner — Afternoon
spent lazily doing nothing —
Tea some after 6 — Eveg
alone with Mother & Willie.
Wrote notes to Wattkins, Reilly,
& Sackett — To bed at 10.45
