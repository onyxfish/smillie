## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 4 1872.

another crisp, breezy day —
feeling very headachy — got to
work painting sketch on Ma[illegible]
Liscomb's platter about 10 oclk
in Cupola — best light — worked
until 2 o'clk, when I got it
done after a fashion — then
took a cup of tea \+ went to
bed. There until after 9 olk.
Then got up — washed \+ dres[sed]
\+ at 10 made my appearance.
Quite a little wood [illegible]
party — Genl. Diwen had [am?]
home — guests did not get
away until nearly 1 oclk \+
it was just about 2 when
I got to bed. Rain falling [illegible]

## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 5 1872.

Overcast my until noon.
Bkfst about 9 — gut. off again.
made at letter to father and
soon after 11 much to defoot
with Allie \+ Josie, both going
to N.Y. Train late — Rode aboard
— Train came about 1.30 — there
home at 2 — Rec'd letter from Kate —
waited for dinner until 4 —
too hungry to do anything.
At 5.30 — drove into town with
Jack — back about 7 — Genl.
D. Rec'd with W. Brown abt
7.30 — tea soon after 8 —
Even playing Pin Poole
until 10.30 — then to bed.
