## Left Page

### June, Friday 28, 1872

Up early — fog — Uncle a, Junie,
\+ Matt got away to take the 8
o'clk Train for New Haven at
Harlem. Day cleared very
hot \+ close — Thunderstorms
near at 2 o'clk — \+ a few drops
of Rain. — Every Thunderstorm
ale about. Got to work early
on Pencil drawing of Mariposa
Big Trees — About 2 very sleepy,
napped for ½ an hour — Lunch
abt — at 3 another nap for ½
an hour — at 5.30, Uncle a.
\+ Junie get? Matt went in home.
Dinner at 6 — at 6.40 saw the
Miller go up — Father on board,
\+ changed salutations, worked
as long as I could see — then
packed up \+ took 8.30 Stage
down home. There at 9.30
Bathed etc \+ to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 29, 1872

an extremely hot day. About 9
ment to Appleton's — Saw Bunce
\+ accepts "Big Trees" — got order for
[illegible] — went to "Mail" with Satterlee
letters, left them — Townley nothing —
then to Dutch \+ Fulton \+ got wood —
then to A.B.N. Co — then to Cochin's \+
got enamelled cards — then to
Studio \+ at 1.30 home. — P.m.
Some sketching, writing, getting
ready to start for Hill Side Monday
myf, and considerable loafing —
Tea at 6.30 \+ then took 7 o'clk
Stage to M'ville Delicious evening
on front piazza until 10.30, then
to bed.
