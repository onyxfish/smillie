## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 30, 1872

A splendid winter day — got to studio at about 10 o'clk — Tried things to go to work \+ commenced in color on my drawing — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30 and worked until 4 when I went with Coleman to Dr. Bartlett's to attend a meeting of the Art. Com. of a Hancock attic fair to come off — at 5 went to Scauthier's and got mounting board \+ home to tea at 6.30 — Worf until after 10 o'clk, mounting photos. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 31, 1872

Another fine day — got to studio at 9.30 — very cold — could'nt work. Re-arranged some pictures, etc. etc. then to work on drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — most of the p.m. a son of J. J. Pease visited with us. After I lost light I wrote a note to Nicoll on Water-Color exh[ibition] business. Home to tea at 7 o'clk — Worf until 11 o'clk, mounting photos. To bed at 11.30
