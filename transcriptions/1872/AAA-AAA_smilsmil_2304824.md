## Left Page

### February Monday, 19 1872.

Milder and almost spring like.
Got to studio at 8.50 — Kindled
fires, set palette & painted another
figure on 8×13 & at 10.30 went into
Colman's Room to see his process
first painting — there for an hour
then back & started painting a
view from foot of Yo-Semite fall, —
tried to apply Colman's method —
failed — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.30. Mr. Thayer waiting
for me to ask about B.V.V. Painted
until about 4 — Townley came in.
Rest of P.M. with him — Home to
tea at 6.30 — Self, washed brushes
finished up a little Water-Color
business and loafed — To bed
about 10.30

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 20 1872.

mild and delightful day. Got
to studio at 9 o'clk. and at once
at work on painting commenced
yesterday (2¼ days). About 1 o'clk
C.P. Cranch came in — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
and worked until 5 when I
got the canvas covered. Then
cleaned up, stretched canvases
&c & home to tea at 6.30 —
W. Bobbett sent up a youngman
this m't for drawing of "Coffee"
with $3.50. Evett Mill &
Joe came up at 8.30 & stayed
for an hour or so — I did
little & got to bed at 11.
