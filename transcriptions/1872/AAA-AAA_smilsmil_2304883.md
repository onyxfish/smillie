## Left Page

### June Monday, 24 1872.

Day overcast — air moist, hot
\+ chilly at same time — Picked
a big basket of cherries before
bkfst \+ sent them down home —
Spent the mng finishing up
Y. Semite drawings. Lunch
at 1 — afternoon at the same
thing until near 5 o'clk —
Out for a walk — dinner
at 6 — Every — walking —
about \+ writing — wrote
a note to Verplanck and
got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### June Tuesday, 25 1872.

Steady, chilly Rain, went
to the city in 8.30 stage. Stopped
at home \+ changed suits. At
10.30, went to Appleton's. My
sketches all approved. Then
went to A/B, N. Co — Drew $164 [illegible]
for 10¼ days work — got material
for two drawings. At 1 went to
lunch with Father — Called
on Yorston — then on Suttons of
"The Aldine" — then again to
Appletons — then Southiers — then
Brooks Bros — then Studio and
at 5.30 — home — Had a bath
\+ tea at 7 — Took 8 o'clk
stage to A.N.'s — There at 9 —
Read until 10.30 and
to bed at 11.
