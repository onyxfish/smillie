## Left Page

### February Sunday, 11 1872.

A mild day, neither good or bad, not or dry, hot or cold. Up late and late to church. Dr. Hastings had passed his tift. Home to dinner. Uncle mr \+ aunt H. with us \- After dinner, lounging \+ napping until near 5 \- then out and walked up to the park and about for more than an hour. In to tea at 6.20 \- Uncle W. \+ aunt H. left at 9 \- after that I mote to Mr. Meddell \+ to Chas. Knickerbacker. To bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### February Monday, 12 1872.

Mild, enervating day. Got to studio at 9 \- lighted fire and to work on drawing of Coffee. About 11 went to Knoedler's and brought a new brush. Then back \+ worked until 1.15 \- Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30 and worked until after 5 o'clk. Old Mr. A.B. Durand called \- went Home to tea at 6.30 \- geo. down at Plainfield. I minutes drawing, mote two or three notes and to bed at 10.15
