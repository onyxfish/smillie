## Left Page

### May, Friday 31, 1872

Up at 6. Very fresh, cool mrg. At 8.30 with Gel and Junie Rode down to the city in omnibus. got home at 9.30. Made paste \+ stretched paper for a drawing. got to Studio at 11.15. Letters from Ferguson \+ Stafford — went to Lauthiers, my water colors sold on Wed — went for $135.00. Then to Sutphens — then home to lunch at 1.15 — Made a start on the drawing of Arms of City of Valparaiso — I with Gel went to Plainfield on 5.30 P.M. train. Very cordially Rec'd by the Treedy family — After an evng of very general chatting, got to bed soon after 11 —

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 1, 1872

Delightful mrg. Up at 6. Bkpt. at 7.30. After bkpt. worked awhile on drawing for U.B.N.Co — Arms of the City of Valparaiso — about 10.30 went out to walk with Bille \+ Mary. Strolled about until after 12. Back \+ lunched at 1 — again worked on drawing until 4 o'clk. When carriage came \+ Bille, Mary, Florence, Gel \+ I went out to ride — Sky overcast but air very pleasant. Called on Mrs. Aug. Shepherds on my way home. Back about 6.45 — tea. Rain soon after 7 — continued until near 10 — Evng chatting — So bed at 11.30.
