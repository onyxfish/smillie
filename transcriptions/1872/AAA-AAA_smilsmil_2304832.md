## Left Page

### March Sunday, 10 1872.

Steady, down pouring rain
all day — Did not go out of the
house — During the morning
wrote a note to Miss Maury
and a letter to Meta Adams
Dinner about 1 occk —
P.M. wrote to Fau Mulford
and J. J. Reeley — Tea at
6.30 — Went wrote to
Mr. Geo. Whitney and got
to bed about 10 30.

## Right Page

### March Monday, 11 1872.

Very pleasant day, altho' over-
cast. Got to studio at 9.30 —
Kindled fire, — then swept &
garnished — at 11 went over to
N.A. of D. & was kept there until
12.30 winding up the affairs —
called for a moment on L. Tiffany.
Home to lunch at 1.30. Carried
Hertzel their Mother at the house.
Back to studio at 3. Maud &
Ella D'wen called — Painted
on Coffee Flowers, from orange &c
that I got from Buchanan. J.A.
Richards called on business — at 6.
went to Bayard Taylor's & got from
him his autograph in my "Faust"
Then, at 7 — called on J.P. Morgan on
W.C. Business — Home to tea at 7.30
Dr Stearns at house overnight — Busy
until 11 writing W.C. letters
and to bed at 11 30
