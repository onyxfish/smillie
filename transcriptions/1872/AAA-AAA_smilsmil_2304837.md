## Left Page

### March Friday, 22 1872.

Milder, clear \+ pleasant — got to studio ab 9.45 — over slept myself — Spent the m[orn] on tuning 12×20 general view of Yosemite valley from Cloud's Rest. Ab 11 went to Barbers — back at 12 — Gramins called — Then Ryga's mother — Home to lunch at 1.45 — At Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30. After, Belle \+ Mary S. walked up to studio with me — There until 6 — Then walked with them to 14th St. \+ 2nd ave. — Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g, writing \+ mounting canvas. Parker at house over night — To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 23 1872.

a miserable, dark, cold \+ raw snowy drizzle — got to studio a few minutes before 9 o'clk — made a note to Townley and sent it off with an article I have written on the subject of water-colors. Tried to sketch out subjects for Yo. Semite article — but my fancy \+ faculties seem to be perfectly torpid or dead. This whole winter I have done nothing and am more discouraged than I can express. Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 3.15 — Kurtz, the photo[grapher] made me a long call — I worked better during the P.M. — Home to tea at 6.45 — Eve'g pasting \+ mounting, etc., the. To bed ab 11.30
