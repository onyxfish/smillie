## Left Page

### July Sunday, 14 1872.

Up late — dreamy day — half
sun light, half mist. — Morning
sat at my window overlooking the
hazy river & its farther bank —
listening to the slow tolling bells
intoxicated with their music —
the twittering birds and the mild
sweet breezes. Wrote notes to [illegible]
Cook, my 2, Brown, Greene etc.
Geo. and. during the mrng, and
stayed overnight. Dinner ab.
2 — most of p.m. ab cut under
the apple trees — Tea ab 6.
Lovely moonlight night — but
very warm — to bed shortly
after 11 —

## Right Page

### July Monday, 15 1872.

Very muggy & close. Got to
work about [illegible] on page [illegible] ch.
Descent into the Yo. Semite. At
it closely all the a.m. — Lunch
abt — At 3 a very heavy Rain
storm came up — During a lull
went down to meet 3.30 Stage, but
it rained heavier than ever and
no stage came — I took refuge in
an Irishman's shanty — Back
to the house at 4 and to my
work — Dinner about 6 — An
almost uninterrupted succession
of thunder storms until I went
to bed at 10.30 after spending
the everg on west piazza —
