## Left Page

### October, Sunday, 20, 1872

Glorious day. Up late, head-
achy & did not get breakfast
until 10 o'clk — went to Dr.
Jhos. Hastings — too late to hear
his text — The inside of the bldg.
has been decorated, or frescoed
& it is simply hideous — After
service walked down to studio —
got letters & saw Auger & Saun[?] —
Then to the Kimball's — saw all
of them — Home at 1.45 — had
dinner & before 4 o'clk went
to bed & stayed there until
tea time — Every at home
and to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### October, Monday, 21, 1872

Another glorious day. At 9 o'clk
went to Dr. Wm. Thompson's, to
decline after made to Gee — of a
position as Artist to an Expedition
to the Holy Land — Then to Jordeans
& Martins — trying to get Philharmonic
ticket — Home soon after 10 o'clk
— to work drawing on block
"Bridal Veil" — Lunch at 1 o'clk.
Uncle W. & Aunt a. arr'd from
Po'keepsie — Finished block at
4.30 — then out, walked down
to studio — called on Jas. Hart —
Back at 6 o'clk. At 7.30 went
to meeting of "Palette Club" —
The dutchmen had a good
time smoking, squabbling &
drinking lager — Home at
10.30 — To bed at 11.
