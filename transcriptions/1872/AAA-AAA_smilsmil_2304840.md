## Left Page

### March Thursday, 28 1872.

Mild, spring like day, got to
Studio at 9.35, after calling at
Ryder's house in 35th St. — Spent
the m'f. drawing in Charcoal,
S.A. Walter Carrier. Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — traced
charcoal sketch + commenced
drawing in India ink — Mr.
Wilder + Perry made a long
call — at 4.30 went with Sam.
Colman to Armory in 14th St.
to make arrangements about picture
gallery for fair — There until 6 —
Home to tea at 6.45 — at 9
at Y.M.C.A. — Reception — There
until near 11 — Home + to bed
at 11.45

## Right Page

### March Friday, 29 1872.

Partially overcast, but mild
\+ pleasant, got to studio at
9.15 + to work on drawing of
Walter Carrier. At 11.30 Mr. De
Bartlett + Dunham called on
fair matters — at 12 went to Avery
on same business, then to D's
B's + then home to lunch at 1.30.
Back to studio at 2.35 + worked
until 5.30 on drawing — then
home. Mr + Mrs Elder — Jost Mee
to dinner — Uncle A. + Junie
later in the eveg. Got to
bed at 11.15
