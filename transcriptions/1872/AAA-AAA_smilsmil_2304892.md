## Left Page

### July, Friday 12, 1872.

Muggy \+ overcast \- flies still like glue. Did not get to work until 9.30 \- After that, at it very closely on Yo.S. Block, tracing, etc., lunch abt \- then worked until 5.30 when I had dinner. Besides tracing two large drawings about half completed one of Half Dome. A brisk shower came up a little before 6. Evert cleared charmingly \+ by 10 ock it was cloudless moonlight. To bed about 10.45

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 13, 1872.

Up at 7 \- delicious morning \- after bkfst, about 9. got to my work and a little before 12 finished Block of Half Dome from Point Aeolus. Then worked mite page block "Descent into the Valley." Lunch abt 1 \- worked until 4. Took 4.30 Stage into town \- Home at 5.30 \- Saw George. Tea at 6.30 \- at 7 went to Miller's about camp shirts \- then to Studio \- then home \- then a bath \+ change \+ 9 o'clk. Stage. Got to Uncle a's at 10 \- Evert quite cool \- To bed soon after 11.
