## Left Page

### January Monday, 8 1872

Pleasant m'f, overcast later in the day — It was quite 10 o'clk. before I got started on a water-color study from Kati's head — worked diligently until lunch at 1.30 — at 2.30 went out and to the Union Depot to see departure of Train with Fisk's body — great crowd. In at 3.30 \+ worked as long as I could see — Tea at 6 — Ev'g at 7.45 went with Geo to St. Cloud Hotel and took Belle \+ Mary J — down to Louis Tiffany's Studio where we met Mr \+ Mrs Coleman, Corinne and her Charlies \+ Clara Dunham. a very pleasant ev'g. Left at 10.30 — home at 11.15 to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### January Tuesday, 9 1872

Delightful day — after making a water-color sketch from Mary's hand, went to studio at 10.15 — worked the rest of the m'f on charcoal sketch of "Shadowed Path" \+ home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45 \+ commenced a new charcoal sketch of same subject, altering the proportions of figure \+ landscape — worked until 5 o'clk. Then went to 308 W. 20th St. to see Jessie Hayland, a model. Home to tea at 6 — Ev'g at 8, Belle \+ Mary J ready at the house until 10.15. I got to bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
