## Left Page

### August, Sunday 25, 1872

Delightfully cool day by comparison — Up at 8 — Helpst at
9.30 — not feeling very bright —
took a nap on sofa — at 11
went to Uncle A's at M.ville —
Charl there + Will joined us
soon after noon — Dinner
at 1.30 — all sat under the
shade trees until about 4
then Will left — Sunie, Charl
\+ I sauntered out for an hour
or so — young Baker joined us
and stayed to tea — 6 o'clk —
sat on piazza until 9 o'clk
then Reading until 10 — to bed
soon after.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 26, 1872

Up at 6.45 — Charming mng. about
until 6kfst at 8.15 — caught 8.30 stage
home at 9.30 — worked awhile on
wood blocks + at 11 went to Appleton —
Bunce out of town — Delivered two
blocks + Recd payment — $50.00 —
Then to Geo. Cook's — p.d. him balance
on Camping Trip — Then to Sutphen's
then to barber's — Shampooed — then
bought ticket + bed for tomorrow
night. Then home to lunch at 2.30 —
Worked until 5, on block of "Mede
in Yosemite" — Then to studio —
Home to tea at 7 — Recd letters
from Geo. & Allie Divens. Sueff
went into Blaney's inst't after —
Mary + made a call on Sam —
Jissup — just arr'd from Syria —
Packing until 10 o'clk, to bed
about 11 — Matt + Charl, with
Uncle A. + Sunie went to Metuchen
to call on Kate Poole.
