## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 27 1872

Superb day but still very muddy and dirty — got to studio's at 9 o'clk — and soon to work on 20×24 (10th day) painting over foreground rocks. Discouragement still continues — no color — no handling. Just at 1 o'clk Colman's pupil came in. Home to lunch at 2 o'clk — back at 2.40 — Painted until 4 — then went to N.A. f.D. Selectd Swain Gifford's — 9 M Gilbert in 16" S\. Snedecor's \+ to Bayard Taylor's — then back to studio's at 6.30 — Home to tea at 7 — Wile, Munn. \+ Parker there — Eveg. Spent in general chatter and to bed 10.30

## Right Page

### February Wednesday, 28 1872

Still clear \+ very muddy — Got to studio at 9.15 and to work on Tracing of A.B.N. Co's building — it is atrocious work and tries my patience exceedingly. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.40. Miss Bascomb called soon after — the names of Maud \+ Ella Divan were on the door. Snurtiffes called this mng. Worked until 6.30 — Suffering from dyspepsia — Walked up to 53d S\. — then home to tea at 6.30 — at 8. walked down to D. Bartlette's — 11th S\. Meeting of Art Comm. Homeopathic fair. Walked up with Colman — home at 10.15 — to bed at 11.15
