## Left Page

### April, Friday, 19, 1872.

Pleasant day — got to Studio at
9.20 — Commenced etching
on "Indian Head" die. Got
in background and home
to lunch at 1.30 — At 2.30
at Phil Rehearsal — After
Rehearsal, went to Bonneville
Gallery, Avery Collection — with
Jet, Mary & Belle Smeady —
walked up the avenue with
Fanny Kimball & her mother
— at studio at 6 — Home to tea
at 7. At 9, at Homeopathic
Fair. Met there, Mary & Belle
Smeady — took them to their
aunts in 44th St. — then home
at 10.30 & to bed abo 10.45.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 20, 1872.

Delicious Spring morning — got to
studio at 9 o'clk. and to work etching
on "Head" (2 days) — Abt until 12 — left
then off — Rode to the Battery — took
Steamboat for Staten Island at
1 o'clk. met Kimball's, Cook's and
Ireland's — by appointment — went
to Huguenot Station, then walked
about a mile to a piece of woods
gathered Arbutus flowers — The
sky hazed over, so that it was
rather a sad-colored afternoon
but everything went off very pleasantly
— About 2, had lunch in the woods.
At 6.15 took train back, getting to
the city at about 7.40 — Got to Fair
at 8.45 — met Mr. & Mrs. Coleman —
Home at 10 o'clk. and to bed at
11 —
