## Left Page

### November Saturday, 23 1872

Up at 7 — Pleasant morning —
At Studio at 9.15 & worked on
Composition of Yosemite Valley
to mg — for the Appletons. until
12.45 — then home — dressed and
caught a lunch — Just in time
to take Kate & follow Matt & Parla-
in to the slaughter — Rw. [illegible] Elder
did the marrying — getting through
at exactly 2.15 — about 30 members
of the family were present — several
of them being Parke's family —
About 3.30 went down to the table
& spent there an hour or more —
At 5.20 Matt had changed her
dress & Mr & Mrs. E. P. Parker started
on their wedding trip — to stop over
Sunday at Phila. The last. Joe, Will
& Annie Bradley left at 6.10 — Waited
until 7.45 — then went to the Kimball's
— there until almost 9 describing the
work of the P.M. — then to Emma J's.
She was out, visited with her mother
until after 10, When Emma came
in — Stayed until almost 11 — then
for home, stopping at Apollo Rooms
for Charl. — Home at 11.30 and to
bed at 12 —

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 24 1872

Charming Indian Summer
day — Myself, went to Dr. Hastings
& heard him discourse upon
the subject of prayer — Text,
Psalms 65th. V. 2 — After service
went up to Uncle A's — Wandered
over the place with him until
dinner at 2.30 — Soon after 3,
with Susie & him walked down
the R.R. track to 96th — then across
to the Boulevard & back to his
house — Tea at 6 — at 7 walked
down to Will's — there until
9.30 — then walked home —
having walked about 10 miles.
To bed soon after 11 —
