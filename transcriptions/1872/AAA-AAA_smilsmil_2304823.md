## Left Page

### February Saturday, 17 1872

Mild-like and skimmed over sort of day — Got to Studio at 9.10. — feeling very unlike work, Stretched canvases etc. \+ planned some work. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Charcoaled on canvas a view from the foot of the Yo Semite fall, looking over the valley — Home to tea at 6.40 — S.C.F. at the house between 3 \+ 4 o'clk — Telegram from A.V.V. on his way home at Albany. Went at 8.30 with Ges went up to Private View of Metropolitan Museum of Art, 681 Fifth ave. Interesting lot of old pictures — Home abt 11 — To bed Som after 12 —

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 18 1872

Brilliant winter day — went to Dr. Hastings' \+ heard him preach a sermon, pleading for the education of young men for the ministry. Text, Hosea 14th ch. a' v. "And there shall be, like people, like priest" — Home at 12 \+ at 12.30, Started with Ges for Manhattanville, Charle met us on the way — Dined with Uncle A.V.V. at 4 o'clk, home in omnibus via Boulevard. Tea at 6 — Self wrote a note to Mr. A. Adams. Then, picking up Wilkie Collins' "Poor Miss Finch" I read until I finished it at 12.30 — Very angry with myself and much dissatisfied in the book.
