## Left Page

### June, Thursday 6, 1872

Had a splitting head ache with some fever. did not get up. Improved slowly during the day \+ at 3, ate some strawberries \- got up at 8, took tea \- rolled in my big chair and again to bed at 12 o'clk \- soon asleep and enjoyed Refreshment until the next morning

## Right Page

### June, Friday 7, 1872

about 8.30 o'clk, I then got up, feeling pretty well hammered and sore about the stomach I did not attempt to work. Went to studio, thence to Brooks Bros, then to Putnam's \- then to Y.M.C.A. \- called on Mrs. Culver and go. There again to studio after stopping at Selleck's \- on my way up, stopped at Dr. Mulford's \- Raining briskly. Home to lunch at 2 \- arranging things \+ loafing, until gel. \+ I took 4.30 P.M. Stage for Uncle A's \- there at 5.30 \- heavy Rain \- dinner \- eveg. Reading "Arabian Nights" until 10.30 \- then to bed \- Heavy Rain during the night \- Slept very little.
