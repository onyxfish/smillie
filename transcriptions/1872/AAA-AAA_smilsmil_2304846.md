## Left Page

### April, Tuesday, 9, 1872.

Dark, Rainy day — At 8.30 went to 157 W. 45th St. to see a big painting by Wm. Thurston — then to Studio at 9.20 \+ to work on "Dollar" drawing for A.B.M. Co. Considerable interruption from fair business — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 \+ worked until 6, but did not get drawing finished. Belle \+ Mary S. came in at 5 o'clk. followed soon after by Geo. At 6 I went with Belle to 16 n8. Geo. with Mary to St. Cloud Hotel. Home to tea at 7 — Swell passed Resting myself \+ got to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday, 10, 1872.

Spring like with one little shower. Then cleared charmingly. got to studio at 8.40 \+ worked on "Dollar" drawing until noon when I finished it. F.B. Carpenter and daughter called. At noon went to Art, D. Varnishing Day. Think the collection very poor. Home to lunch at 1.30 — at A.B.M. Co at 3.15 — there until 4.15 — On my way up town called on Bunce at Appleton's, on Lauthier. at Scaett's to see the Lockwood Paintings — then to Studio \+ home to tea at 7 o'clk — went, lying off \+ chatting with father. To bed at 10.45
