## Left Page

### December Thursday, 5 1872.

Overcast day with an attempt at snow. Got to studio at near 10, very shaky & feverish —
Commenced drawing n work of "Fallen Sequoia". Had a very pleasant call from a gentleman & two ladies unknown — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 & worked as long as I could see — about 4.15 — Father came in at 5.10 & me talked family matters until 6.45 — then walked home together.
Tea soon after 7 — quiet lying about — to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### December Friday, 6 1872.

Charming day — went up to Scurgraff's — then to studio at at 9.30. Set to work on block "fallen big tree" and at it steadily until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch. At 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal — after, walked up to studio with Belle Smeedy — At Studio until nearly 5.30 — then home to tea — Evelf, Charle & I went to Wallack's & saw Sothern in his play of "Our American Cousin" — was disappointed altho' I laughed heartily at his nonsense. — Here at 11 o'clk & to bed at 11.35
