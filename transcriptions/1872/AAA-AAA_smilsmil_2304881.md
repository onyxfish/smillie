## Left Page

### June Thursday, 20 1872.

A very close + hot day. Got to work in Yo.S. sketches soon after bfst. about 9.15. Uncle a. left for the city at 10.30. He is to go with Mother to the Catskill Mts this P.M. Junie Bell at with Minn. Then lunch. Worked until 3.45. Then with Minn \+ Junie down to the River where we saw Mother + Uncle A. in the "S ho. Cornell" as she passed at 4.25. Up to the house at 5. dinner at 5.30. After dinner, worked as long as I could see. The walked about \+ larked with the girls until near 10. To bed soon after.

## Right Page

### June Friday, 21 1872.

Intensely hot day. Awake between 3 \+ 4 A.M. \+ heard the birds. Up for all day at 7. bfst. near 9. Worked in Yo.S. sketches. Lunch at 1. Picked cherries for an hour. more work \+ at 4 o'clk. Rode down home in stage. Dressed \+ went to Hoffman He \+ saw all the Divens. Met Sister Mary there. bid Maud good bye. Went to studio got some things \+ home at 7.30 Tea, short chat with Father then Charl \+ I went up to Uncle A's in 8 o'clk. stage. There at 9. glorious moonlight. About until 11, then to bed.
