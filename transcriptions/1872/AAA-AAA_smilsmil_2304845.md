## Left Page

### April, Sunday 7, 1872

Steady pouring rain all day. Did not go out — wrote during the mf — Dinner at 1 — Slept most of the afternoon, tea at 6.30 — Evely did a little more writing and to bed at 10 45.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 8, 1872

Pleasant day. At 8.30 called at Mr. DeForest's No. 15 W. 30 St \+ bugged a picture for Fair. Got to Studio at 8.50. Kindled fire. Spent some time in the Coleman then to Y.M.C.A. Bldg. Called on Kensett \+ other Studios and back at 11 o'clk \+ commenced work on drawing of Dollar medallion for A.B.N. Co. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 — worked until 6 — Then to various places, calling on Sauis Tiffany \+ home at 8 — Evely wrote notices for a number of newspapers \+ to bed abt 11.20
