## Left Page

### December Sunday, 29 1872

Overcast day — must ment to
Dr Hastings — late — Heard
him preach a sermon from
from the opening verses of
St. John — Portions very
interesting, but the most
of it way above my head.
Home to dinner — P.M.
spent doing little & nothing.
Tea about 6 — Well went
to Tabernacle (Dr. Taylor's).
Left at 8 o'clk. + went to
Emma Ireland's — Kitty
on from Boston — a very pleasant
eve — did not get home
until almost 1 — To bed
very soon after.

## Right Page

### December Monday, 30 1872

Bright pleasant autumn day — Got
to Studio at 9.25 — Kindled
fire — cleared up a little
then to work on Cathedral
spires — worked until 1.15
then home to lunch — back
at 2.15 + worked until 4.30
but could not get block
finished — at 5 got to
Appletons — saw Bunce and
delivered block of Yosemite
face, with which he seemed
very much pleased — Back
to Studio at 6 — after stopping
at Sautherin's & Snedecorn's —
Home to tea at 6.45 — Well —
Reading Whymper's scrambles
amongst the Alps until 11 —
To bed at 11.30
