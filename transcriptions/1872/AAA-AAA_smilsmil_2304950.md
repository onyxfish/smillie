## Left Page

### November Monday, 11 1872.

Wild mist, becoming gradually overcast with a sprinkle of rain
at night. Got to studio at 9.30.
Kindled fire — 10.30 before I got
started on "Chenwick" block —
Miss Bascomb called — also Woo[d]
Kimball to bid me good bye — he
starts for Cal. to-morrow. Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Mrs.
Mulford + a lady friend waiting —
After 4 o'ck — Meta Adams came
in — left at 5 — Blackman also
called — Home to tea at 6 — Then
went in to Harrower's Church &
saw some Stereoptican pictures
of Cal & Yo Semite — he at 9 o'clk
to bed at 10.20

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 12 1872.

Dismal Rainy day — got
to studio at 8.45 — At 10.30 had
finished "Chenwick" drawing
then went to the Kimball's
to bid Woods good-bye — Then
walked down to Appleton's
with my two blocks — got $50 —
for them — got proofs of 3 blocks
that Quaritey has engraved —
they please me very much —
Back to studio at 1 — after a
long visit with Jas. Hart. Mr.
Geo. Whitney came in — went to lunch
with him then to Knoedlers + back to
studio at 3 — Charcoaled "Horse
Racing in the Yo-Semite" — Home
to tea at 6 — At 7.30 — went to
Mr. Hart's Studio — Water Color
meeting — walked home with
Louis Tiffany — Cloudless and
windy — to bed at 11 —
