## Left Page

### May Friday, 3 1872.

Charming day, but rather windy — Got to Studio at 9.10
\+ to work on" Snowy Mountains" drawing — Fau M. \+ Mrs. Jessup called — at 12.30 D. C. H. came in \+ stayed for an hour, then I went to Horse Market to get some information for Uncle Willie — Home at 1.40 — Got to Phil. Rehearsal 2 or 3 min late. After Reh. called on Sillick, then at Truedler's then to Studio \+ home to tea at 6.30 — Svef wrote some \+ to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 4 1872.

Fine day, but blowy — Got to Studio a few minutes before 9. Started 3d painting of "Coffee" for Robt. Hewitt — worked sluggishly and home to lunch at 1.30, — back at 2.45 \+ painted until nearly when I got it all in, washed up \+ home to tea at 6.15 — At last Phil. Concert at 7.50 — enjoyed the concert exceedingly — walked home to Mrs. Hyde, 195 Madison Ave. with Belle \+ Mary S — Home to my own home at 11 o'clk \+ to bed just before 12 —
