## Left Page

### July, Wednesday, 10, 1872

Up a little before 7 — R[illegible] about
8. Then, with Miss Rogers, spent
some time in V's studio — At
10, in a phaeton, Miss R. took me
out to ride — the morning over-
cast, very muggy \+ hot, but breezy.
Drove down to the Landing, or rather
to "the corners" — did some shopping
then to old Dr. Rumsey's — then
to Howland's place "Beacon Side" —
then through Mattewan — on our
way home, stopped at M. Mrs.
Verplanck's \+ had a very pleasant
visit — Home at 2 — dinner at
3. P.M. put out nickets out
with Mr. Sam. V. \+ Miss R. played
ed Croquet until tea time —
Tea, \+ at 7.30 started for the
station after being very strongly
urged to extend my visit —
Took 8.10 train for N.Y. Got
in to Grand Central Depot
at 10.20 — Home at 6.30 \+
to bed at 11 —
