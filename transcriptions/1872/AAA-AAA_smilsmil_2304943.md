## Left Page

### October Saturday, 26 1872.

Another miserable day —
up about 8 o'clk — a m —
feeling very slim — Worked
along very slowly, Reducing
by squares, drawing of "Good
News to Ghent" for "Aldine"
block — Went & Registered
for voting — Dinner about 1 —
Did little during afternoon
Finished Reduction — At 4.30
went to studio — from there
to Knoedler's — much interested
in fine new paintings & water
color drawings. Home to tea
at 6 — Even'g doing nothing
and to bed about 11.30 —

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 27 1872.

Pleasant m'g, promising better
things. Went to Hastings' and
heard him preach from Jer.
10th ch. 23d v. After service walked
to 5th Ave. with the Jessups. Then
home — Ed. Parker av'd — Then
by 8th Ave. cars to Manhattanville.
Dinner at Uncle A's at 1.30 —
Wandered over the place with
Susie until after 4 o'clk — A sad-
colored, overcast, but very charming
Oct. day — Edgar Turrell called —
Tea about 6. and soon after 7 —
E.S. & I walked down the Boulevard
Passed but three vehicles. The horse
distemper seems to have affected
almost every horse in the city —
very few are to be seen in the
streets — Stopped at Will's and
there until 10 o'clk — then walked
down home and to bed soon
after 11 o'clk —
