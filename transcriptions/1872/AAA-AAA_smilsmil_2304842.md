## Left Page

### April Monday, 1 1872.

High winds \+ snow squalls \- After going up to P.O. Station "Q" got to studio at 9.25 \- Kindled fire \- directed \+ posted a lot of fair circulars \+ then to work painting in on over, a canvas 8x13 a twilight that I \+ Farted several years ago \- Think I will give it to Ogilvie for wedding present instead of the 10"x20. painted the other day \- At 12 had a very pleasant call from Fanny \+ Woods Kimball, Emma J, Annie Cook \+ Miss Howes. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- Finished painting at 5 \- washed up. Home to tea at 6.50 \- Evely, tried to write \- could not \- Read a little and to bed about 10\-

## Right Page

### April Tuesday, 2 1872.

Charming day. Got to studio a few minutes before 9 o'clk \- Suffering from severe cold \+ headache \- Started the painting in of Upper Ausable Lake \- x 22 \- Barker Sew\- called then Mr. Ensign, her sister and miss Brookes. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.15 \- Painted until 5 \+ finished the laying in, but not very successfully. washed up \+ Corrged \- then home to tea at 6.50 \- Evely, doing nothing and to bed at 10. 45
