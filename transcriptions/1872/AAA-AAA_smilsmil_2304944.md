## Left Page

### October, Monday, 28, 1872.

Pleasant day — Did not go out in the mf, but traced, retraced & continued "Aldine" drawing on the block. Rec'd telegram from Belle & reedy — wants Phil. seat that Matt had promised to Mrs Phelps yesterday — wrote a note to her at once — Dinner at 1 — Worked on drawing until 4.30 — Meu to Y.M.C.A. — & to Georges Studio — Met Nicol — Then to my own studio — Home at 6.30 Sea — At 8, called on Julia Phelps & at g on the Kimballs — Home at 11 & to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday, 29, 1872.

Superb day — worked all the mf on "Aldine" block. S.C.F. came in about noon — just on her way home from Malone — has been visiting Kate for five weeks past. Lunch abt 1.30 — At 2 started for Uncle A's — got there about 3 — Sketching about the house until 4.30 — Dinner about 5.30 and at — Started out & walked down to Wills — Matt & Charl. (C. came home this mf. early) met me there — left for home at 10.30 — in at 11 — Uncle W. & aunt A. at the house over night — To bed at 11.30
