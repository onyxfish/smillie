## Left Page

### November Monday, 25 1872

Pleasant day — got to studio at 9.30 — Kindled fire & worked on Charcoal "Descent into the Yosemite Valley" — At 12 ment over to Y.M.C.A. Bldg & called on Tiffany, Gifford & Wm Hart on Water Color Matters — Back to studio at 1.30 with Sonis & made up with him — home to lunch at 1.50 — back to studio at 2.30 worked on Charcoal drawing until 4 — then wrote paper W.C. Society to N.A. & D. — At 4.40 went to Wm Hart's studio W.C. Board of Control — [illegible] went to Gifford's Room & colored paper that had been approved — left it at N.A. & D. — Back to studio at 6.30 — Stopped at Coleman He & saw T.A. Richards — Home to tea at 7 — Eve'g, tired & lying about — To bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 26 1872

Dark & Rainy day — got to studio at 9.5 & spent the m'g painting on 11×14 "Descent into the Yosemite" for Lawtner — Home to lunch at 1.40 — back at 2.45 — Spent the m. until 6. writing short Yosemite articles for Appleton's Journal — then washed brushes & fixed up — Home to tea at 8 — Eve'g Read paper — wrote to Mr. Main etc etc — To bed at 10.30
