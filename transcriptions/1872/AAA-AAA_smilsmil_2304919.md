## Left Page

### Sept. Sunday, 8 1872.

a very warm day. Between 9 \+ 10 when we had bkfst \- all Ready to go to church but Allie (who am\- from My. this mts.) came down so late that the Genl. ordered the carriage put up. Sat \+ chatted until near noon. Wrote a letter home and nearly finished one to Belle Sweedy \- Rec'd a letter from Geo. Dinner at 3.30 \- Heck \+ wife came up \- in grapse house \+ under the trees until Sunset \- Ats 7.30 Genl. \+ Mrs. D \- meutaur the Rest of this even'g on piazza made a cup of tea about 9 o'clk \- on piazza until 10.30 \- then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sept. Monday, 9 1872.

Another very warm day. Quite useless with a cold in the head, having slept but little for two nights. After bkfs: drove into town with Jack \+ Calp: Bought a lot of peaches \- back about noon mote a letter to Geo. \+ finished one to Belle Sweedy \- Dinner at 2 \- P.M. until 5.30 at work in Stable yard, preparing a drawing to paint from "Dandy" about 6. went out on Dandy \+ Rode for half an hour. \- he was hid, changed \+ had tea soon after 7 \- Bray Hall, Heck \+ his wife \- Jack \+ Heck at Billiards until 10.15 \- Then we played Poker until near 11 \- to bed at 11.45
