## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 26 1872.

A horrible day of driving wind and rain — Up with a headache, got to studio at 9.30 — Unable to do anything — most of the day on the lounge — Just able to get home at 8.30 — Did'nt go to bed, but in my big chair all night. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 27 1872.

A very pleasant day — got to studio at 9.35 — At 11 Started for down-town — Saw Govt. Goodall etc \+ Mr. Chas. Tappan \+ Son. — Got subject \+ photos for Water Carrier. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back to Studio at 3. Started work on a B. N. Ca's drawing. W. Jol. Carey \+ wife came in — Then Dr. Dunning, then A.P. Ryder's sister — then Miles Palisher — then Dr. Milford at 6.30 — went to Mrs. P. Bartlett's Home to tea at 7.15 —
Suel[?] hard at work settling up sales acct. of Water Color subjects to and abt 11.45.
