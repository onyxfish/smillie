## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 19 1872

Charming day and cold — got to Studio at 9.20 — Spent the
mf. making small pencil
sketch of general view over
the Yosemite Valley from Clouds
Rest. Home to lunch at 1.30 —
Mr. McHtaarg at the house —
back at 2.20 — Afternoon
set palette & tried to work
on 11×14." Descent into the
Yosemite" — Dr Percy spent
most of the p.m. with me —
at 4.30 — went to Wm Hart's Studio
Water Color B.d of Control. Back
to Studio with Louis Tiffany at
5.40 — Home to tea at 6.40 —
Eve'g spent writing addresses
for Matt & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 20 1872

Dubious m'f — but cleared beautiful —
got to Studio at 9.45 — Finished
pencil sketch of General View
from Cloud's Rest — also drawing
on wood — "Horse Racing in the
Yosemite Valley". Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45 —
started sketch of Big Trees —
No 2 — Miss Bascom came
and I showed her how to
lay preparation on the wood
& made a little sketch on a
block for her — at 5 o'clk —
went down to Sommerville's —
Saw him about hiring his gallery
for Water Color Exbt'n — then to
[illegible] — Met J.G. Brown — Page & Perry —
walked to 3d Ave, with them — then
to Currie's in 6th Ave — then to
D.O.C. Tourneau's — 22d St — near
[illegible] Ave — then home to tea at
7 — Eve'g, until 10.30, at work
on Matt's wedding Circulars.
So bed at 11.
