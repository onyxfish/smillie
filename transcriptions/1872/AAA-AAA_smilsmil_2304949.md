## Left Page

### November, Saturday 9, 1872

Delightful day — got to Studio a few minutes before 9 — then over to Hoffman Ho. There half an hour — bid Allie & Jack goodbye (they sail for Europe this noon) then back to Studio. Worked all the mng on Bathers Block — Prepared lunch at 1.30 — after that took a short walk. Then commenced on new block — "Indians making Chemmick" — Worked until almost 4.30. Then went to Mr. Hart's Studio to meet W.C.S. Board of Control. Full meeting — Subject [illegible] discussed — after in [illegible] Room back to Studio at 6 — Home to tea at 6.30 — at 8.30, called in Mr. [illegible] Mr. McHoarg — 133 W. 36 St — There until 10 o'clk — Home and to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 10, 1872

Charming day — mng, went to B. Hastings and heard him preach from Text (Jer. 11¼, 3d.) "As the tree falleth, so must it lie" — Home to dinner — Parker down from P. — After dinner Read — fell asleep in my chair — At 4.30, walked up to Central Park — glorious sunset and a moonlight that was perfectly delicious — so soft and dreamy — Enjoyed my walk exceedingly — home to tea at 6.30 — Eve. doing nothing in particular and a little step Mod Reading — to bed at 11.15
