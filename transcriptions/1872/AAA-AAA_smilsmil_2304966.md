## Left Page

### December Sunday, 15 1872.

In bed all day with a
splitting, tearing headache.
About 4 o'clk, P.M., I began
to feel relief & soon after
got up. Had more beef tea
at 5 o'clk — again at 10. Sat
in my big chair until 11,
having been much refreshed
by a wash & dress up. Lay
awake until near 2 a.m.
Then slept fairly until
8 a.m.

## Right Page

### December Monday, 16 1872.

Overcast day with sun about mid-
day & rain at night. Up about
8.30 a.m. Head very light, legs
very weak, but generally better.
More beef tea — Rolling in my
big chair unable to do anything
else. Tried to read — could not.
Chewed up a chop at 1.30 but
was not allowed to swallow it.
Belle & Mary Tweedy called
about 4 P.M. Had quite a visit
with them. Rec'd a pleasant
little note from Emma Ireland.
Eveg lying about until 10.30
then to bed.
