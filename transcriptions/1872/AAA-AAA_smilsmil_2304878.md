## Left Page

### June, Friday, 14, 1872

another very hot day. Spent the morning working over an Italian drawing for a B. N. C. improving effect, etc. — Mr. Main & Nephew came at about 12.30 — Stayed to lunch — Heavy thunder shower between 2 & 3 that went into a steady Rain that lasted until after 6. Worked until 7.30 on a drawing by [illegible] for Uncle Willie. Some visiting after — Mr. Main came to tea & stayed all night — Tea at 7. Evening spent Chatting — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday, 15, 1872

Milder, but still close & hot. got to Studio with Mr. Main at 8.40 until 10.30 looking over Studies, sketches & photos. Then to Knoedlers — Left Mr. M. at 11.15 — went to Dunlap's \+ bought a hat, then again to Studio — Home to lunch at 1 — went down town after, saw Vaux, andt, Goodall and Menger — settled some business at Sackett's — then up town — stopped at Brooks Bros then to Studio — then home at 6.10 — Tea — at 8 — with Father, went up to Will's in 61st St. Home at 10 & to bed at 10.30
