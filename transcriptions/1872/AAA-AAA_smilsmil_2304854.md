## Left Page

### April, Thursday 25, 1872

mild \+ milder, until eveff, when it was almost oppressive \- Got to studio at 9.10 \+ worked with graver on "Head" until 1.30 \- then home to lunch \- back at 2.45 and worked until 6. Laid a ground to etch cross-line in background \- Home to tea at 7 \- Eveff doing nothing in particular \- To bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 26, 1872

Tremendously hot day. Ther. 80° in studio \- Got to Studio at 9.10 \- Etched in cross line on die \- then bit it in. Home to lunch at 1.15 \- at Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30 \- Enjoyed it exceedingly. After Rehearsal with Matt, Kate, Mary \+ Belle J\- went to Geo's Studio, then to N.A. of D. There until 6. Then to studio \- then home to tea at 6.30 \-, Eveff, Read papers until 8.30 \- then to Fair \- Home at 10.30 and to bed at 11.20
