## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 7 1872.

Very fine winter day \- got to Studio at 9.30 \- Mr. Hewitt called about drawing & coffee \- Did not get to work until near 11 \- at W.C. drawing until 1.15 \- then home to lunch \- back at 2.30 and worked until 4 o'clk \- Then went to Buchanans, florist in 17th S \- back at 4.30 \- Uncle Willi came in \- Home to tea at 6.30 \- at 8.30 at N.A. of D. met then Messrs Whitney, Lewis \+ Richards also Misses Josie Jessup, Annie Hardu \+ Allie Diven with young Jessup \+ Webster \- at 9.30 went to Studio with them \- there until 11 \- Home at 11.30 \- To bed at 12.45.
Mary \+ the baby \+ Matt went to Po'keepsie this mf.

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 8 1872.

Fine day, but the weather evidently fixing for a change \- Mess. Gel \- Whitney, Snoch Lewis \+ W. J. Richards came in som after I got to Studio \+ we spent a pleasant mf until 11.30 looking over Studies, photos. etc and Talking of our last summer's Trip. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- a little work on my Water Color some fixing up for to nights spree. Calcutt came in at nt dusk \- got home to tea at 6.45 \- back to Studio at 7.45. Soon after Will \+ Fanny Kimball, Emma Ireland, Annie Cooke \+ her br. Geo. and 2 with my br. Geo. we made a seven \- Had a little supper \+ a very pleasant mf. Broke up at 12 \- Home and to bed at 1.
