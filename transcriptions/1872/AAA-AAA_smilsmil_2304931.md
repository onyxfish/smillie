## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 2 1872

Cold, cloudy & breezy — wind from N.W. Bluffs. Som after 8.
Before 10 drove over to the fair ground, the blacks, with a quit.
D. Mr. & Miss Morris & Allie. W.
M & the quit then left us & we
spent the morning driving
over the grounds & among the
horses until 12.30 — Subject
for a fine picture — judges &
stallions — Drove home before
1 & lunched then back & saw
the prize horses in the ring —
Then drove to the Park & about
home at 4 o'clk — Men took
horse car into town to meet
Stuart & Josie Beatty at depot,
could not find them — back
at 6.30 — Dinner about 7 —
Miss Minnie Morgan a guest
over night — a very strange &
interesting woman — an evng
of general chatting & to bed
about 11.15

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 3 1872

A very fine day, altho' cloudy.
The morning I spent indoors
cleaning up the new pictures
rec'd by Genl. D. Lunch at
1 o'clks, then over to fairground
with Miss Morris, Capt. & May
& Allie in carriage. There for about
an hour, then drove down to
the Races south of town. There
through 3 heats — nothing of
particular interest — slight
shower — on way home, stopped
to see if Stuart B & wife had yet
arr'd. Home about 6. Supper
about 7 — Until after 8, chatting
— billiards with Capt. until
near 10 — To bed soon after 11.
