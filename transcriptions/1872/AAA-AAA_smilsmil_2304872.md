## Left Page

### June Sunday, 2 1872

a most glorious day. Up at 7. Bkfst at 8. went to the Episcopal Ch. \+ heard a sermon from St. Luke. 16th Ch 25th v. The old story of the horrid Rich man. back at 12.30. Dinner about 1. It was near 4 when B. M. \+ I started out for a walk, went to the boundary line of Plainfield \+ enjoyed birds, breezes and color hugely. Back at 4.45 tea soon after. Evening spent in general Chat and to bed at 11.20

## Right Page

### June Monday, 3 1872

Beautiful day. Up at 7 o'clk. Bkfst at 7.30. After bkfst, walked to Uncle Geo's. \+ saw Aunt Ollie. Then to his office \+ saw Uncle G. Back \+ to work on Arms drawing about 10 o'clk. Worked until after 12. Then with Geo \+ the girls walked for half an hour. Dinner soon after 1. worked on drawing until 4 o'clk, when I got it finished. Geo. \+ I started for home on the 4.45 Train. got to 44 45; at 6.45. aunt Jessie there. tea at once. went to Baurgraff's \+ mounted drawing. To bed at 10.45
