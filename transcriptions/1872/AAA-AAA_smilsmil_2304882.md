## Left Page

### June Saturday, 22 1872.

another sweltering hot day —
got at my Y o S — sketches about
10 o'clk. \+ worked until lunc
abt — Then again until near
4 when we went out for a
drive — Out only 3/4 of an
hour. The horses seemed
to suffer \+ it was so hot as
to be no pleasure for us —
At my work again — Dinn
at 6 — Glorious evng, Will
\+ Joe came up at 8.30 \+
left at 9 — I paced about
the house \+ grounds until
11 o'clk — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### June Sunday, 23 1872.

Still very hot — Up late and a
late bkpt of course — Most of
the mng on West Piazza —
everything exceedingly lovely —
made a note to Belle Tweedy —
Swing for an hour in Charls
hammock under the trees —
Dinner at 1.30 — a lot of
young fellows came during the
P.m — Young Turrell — Miles Palmer
the — sauntered about until
tea time — at 6 o'clk — They
stayed until 9.30 — It was
after 11 again when I got
to bed.
