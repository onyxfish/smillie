## Left Page

### August, Saturday, 31, 1872

After 8 when I came down to bkfs — late. \- Walked on Piazza \+ then to work on drawing on wood. The day very breezy \+ quite cool. Dinner about 1.30 \- Nearly 3 when I got at my drawing again \+ continued until after 5, nearly 6. \- I worked in the library \- several callers \- Tea at 6.30 \- after tea a short walk with Josie \+ Allie. Eve'g at 8.30, went with the girls \+ Cap't to Aleck D'wens. There met his two sisters in law \- Mrs. Falke \+ Miss McJua \- Mr. Reed \- Miss \+ Mr. Jack Reyn[olds]. Had lots to eat, but did not eat it. \- Home at 12 and to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### Sept., Sunday, 1, 1872

Up about 7.30 \- Beautiful m'g. Gen'l D. \- Prof. Lane \+ Haas arr'd by night train. Bkfs \- soon after 8 \- Sauntering about until near 11 when I went out to walk with Allie \+ Josie. Back at 12 \+ with them rode into town to the P.O. Back, \+ dinner about 2.30 \- After dinner rode over to "the Lake" a Park \- no service, J.K. Beecher out of town \- back about 5.30 at 6.30 with Jack \+ the Cap't drove into town on a message Back \+ tea about 8 \- Eve'g chatting with the girls \+ the Prof. until 11 \- To bed about 11.30 \- Rec'd letter from father, enclosing one from Josie Beatty.
