## Left Page

### November Friday, 29 1872

Quite a snow storm — Got to
studio about 9.30 + most of the
mng was spent on Goldung [illegible]
then on some general sketching
\+ fussing until 1 o'clk. Prep and
lunch + at 2 went to Phil's
Rehearsal — Walked up with
Fanny — back to studio at about
5 — worked on crayon sketches
for Meta Adams until 5.30 +
then hurried home, but too late
to see Matt who went off in
the 6 o'clk. Train. Went
to Mary Butter's + had a real
old fashioned talk with her
until 10.15 — Then to Grand Opera,
42, saw Bromley + last act of
"Round the Clock" — Home at 11.15
To bed at 12

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 30 1872

Clean, bitter cold and very windy —
9.40 when I got to studio — most
of the morning spent scattering
over drawing for Meta Adams,
then at Charcoal sketch of the
descent into the Yosemite —
Just as I had started for lunch
met Miss Sterling — Home at
1.50 — back at 2.45 — Afternoon
again at charcoal, but made
little progress as I felt under
the weather — At 5 took an uneasy
nap until 6 — Home to tea at 6.30
Went at 9 o'clk. went down to
Lotos Club, hoping to meet a Mr.
Blackburn, a sort of agent for a
proposed foreign water color
[illegible] — didn't meet him —
Left soon after 10 — Home at 10.40
To bed at 11.15
