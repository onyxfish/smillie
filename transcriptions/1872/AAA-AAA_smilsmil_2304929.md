## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 28 1872.

Superb day — Late in getting started on my morning work —
got out Dandy and in trying to get the effect of sunlight reflected from his coat I quite spoiled the study that was already passable. Worked until 1.30 — Dinner at 2
from 3 until 5 worked on study of grey. Believed that M. & Mrs. Knight came down to the yard to see me at work. — At 5.30 — away on "Dandy" — a lively time at the back of the house — calling in "Democrats" — Many [illegible] run among them — Rode until almost 7 — Bathed — dressed & tea near 8 —
Evety, billiards with Bray Hall, Cap't & Jack — until near 11 — To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 29 1872.

Up at 8 — b'kf'st, after 9 — mng squarely looking with high wind from South — Gen. D. arr'd early from NY, went to church in the mng with May. Heard Rev. Fowler 1st Thess — II Ch. 13th v — a very good discourse — on way home went to P.O. & stopped at Aleck's — Rec'd a short note from Kate —
About 1, walked up on the hill with Cap't. At 3.30 had dinner — Heavy shower — cleared with superb rainbow — just at sunset 5.30 saddled the grey & rode for 3/4 of an hour — enjoyed it ever so much — just the right thing. Tea at 8 o'clk — Evety — Reading and to bed at 10.30
