## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 18 1872

Delightful day again — at 9.30 in Stable yard and painted in Study of black horse — much encouraged — got it done just in time for dinner at 1.30 — After dinner, a game of billiards with Jack + the Capt. who Rd from NY. This mvy., Before 3. again in the yard — finished drawing in of the gray and painted it in — Near 5.30 when I got off on Dandy. Rode for an hour — back — washed + dressed — into a tea fight — Knights — Rices — Hamblin + Hall + Mrs. Gro D. Later Alec + his wife — Rain commenced before 9 — all away + got to bed soon after 11. ## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 19 1872

A changeful day — Sun, Rain \+ wind. Nearly before we got Help. Everything late, wrote a letter to Sister Mary and fussed about doing a number of little things — Then painted background to white horse that I painted yesterday — Finished just in time for a light lunch at 1.30 — Soon after 2 dressed — with Cap. walked to G. M. Diver's. Mr. D, May + Jack joining after. Dinner at 4.45. Everything very pleasant — About 6, Jack + I walked home. Allie quite unwell. Suey with Bray Hall at Pin Poole — a very heavy shower — Got to bed soon after 10 — A letter from Father enclosing one from Bunce
