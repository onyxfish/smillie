## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 10 1872

Another great change in the weather. Thunder storm near us in the mt. Blowing \+ growing colder. Still very much under the weather. Mt, painted background on study of "Dandy" that I began yesterday. At noon Jack, Cap. \+ May went to Painted Post. P.M. I painted until 5.30 on Wooden wedding platter for Cap. Miss Dickinson to tea, about 7 o'clk. Poker with Miss D. Allie \+ Bray Hall until near 9.30. Jack and from Painted Post about 10. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 11 1872

Still overcast, but mieder. Up at about 8. Perfectly used up by cold in head. Did not attempt to do anything until towards noon, (except write a letter home.) Then I went out to the stable yard and Started study of black horse worked until 1.30. Billiards \+ dinner som after 2. P.M. painted in background to study commenced in the morning. More billiards \+ tea at 8. Jack went off to Horseheads without telling us of it. Miss Jennie McSum \+ Mr. Mercur called. Left at 9 o'clk. Allie \+ I played poker until 11. To bed about 11.30
