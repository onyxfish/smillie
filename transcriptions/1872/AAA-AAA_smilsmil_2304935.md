## Left Page

### October Thursday, 10 1872.

Superb mrg. Jack arr. from NY. with his tre. Get at 7.30 we walked down to G.M.D's to bkfst. — Mr. A.S.D. had Riddell on before — G.M. looks well — Bkfst at 9 — Sudden change in the weather — back home at 10.30 — Rain soon after 11 — at times very heavy — Spent the day very busily at work mounting large proof "Rocky Mts" hanging paints, washing brushes, cleaning cloths etc etc — Dinner at 2 and tea soon after 7 — Eveng spent in library in front of cosy fire playing cards with Mr. D. — To bed at 11.45 — beautiful moonlight.

## Right Page

### October Friday, 11 1872.

Cloudy & quite cold — Allie, May & Capt. arr. from NY. early this a.m. Bkfst. about 8 — Spent all the mrg. with Jack & finished hanging the paintings — Dinner at 2 — Between 3 & 4 saddled "Dandy", rode down to Mr. Hall's & borrowed Sam Hall's horse and brought it back — saddled it and shortly after 4 Allie & I started out — went north west over the hills out by Car's Corners, rode up to Heck's Reservoir, then home at 6.20 after a charming ride, enjoyable for its rather sombre fall effects. Tea about 7.30 — G.M.D. well called — then Bray Hall — Whist with Mr. D — Allie & May until near 11 — To bed soon after.
