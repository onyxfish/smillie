## Left Page

### May, Thursday 9, 1872

Very hot. Ther. about 90°. Got
to studio at 9.15. wrote a note
to Elisha Mulford. Mr. Gee.
Whitney came in — then Mr.
Culpecutt — Worked on A.B.N. &
drawing "Valparaiso" — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — after, Rode
down to 10th St. Studios. Saw
Page, Perry & Beard — then to
Greene's in 30th St. Then to my
studio at 3.15. Brewer there,
worked on drawing, but it was
so hot that I did not do much.
Home at 6.10 — tea — at 7...
[illegible] & I Rode up to uncle A's
— every chatting and to bed
at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 10, 1872

Another very hot day. Up early
& wandered about, enjoying the
birds & blossoming trees. Rode
down to the city with Junie &
uncles A. & W. got to studio at
9.40 & to work on Valparaiso
drawing. Home to lunch at
1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked
until after 5, then I gave up
with pain in head & tired out —
a heavy Rain storm came up
but I got to 5.30 Stage and
rode up to uncle W's. got
there at 6.30 & had tea — a
very charming evening —
all the folks went down to the
city to Booth's theatre — got &
I had it all our own way. I
played billiards & read &
to bed at 10.30.
