## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 16, 1872

Trying to snow all day — about as much sunshine as snow. Got to Studio at 9.10 — Suffering from distress in the head — after working for a time, determined to pack my traps, sketches, drawing board and all \+ go home to work, which I did forthwith — Finished my W.C.S not F notices — lunch at 30 afternoon on my back, unable to do anything — Tea at 6 — Suef, felt easier — Traced a little on drawing — some Reading and got to bed at 11 —
Miss Ripley \+ sister called at the Studio this afternoon,

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 17, 1872

Delightful day — at home at work on water color sketch "Glacier Rock from Yosemite Cañon." This in opaque color and one that I commenced in the valley — worked until 4.30 — then out — went to Silleck's to see about my frame — then in to Tiffany's \+ Gifford's Studio met Misses Torrey \+ Corlie's — then down to Gauthier's — then to Studio. gathered a lot of things and home to tea at 6.45 — Suef arranged some W.C. accts \+ made paste \+ mounted paper for some new work to-morrow — Got to bed about 10.45
