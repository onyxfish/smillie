## Left Page

### February Thursday, 1 1872.

Delightful winter day — got to studio soon after 11 having stopped first at Greene's — did not see him — then at Elleck's — then Wm Hart's then Brewort. Worked on my drawing + home to lunch at 1,45 — back at 2,45 \+ worked until 4,30. Colman came in \+ me had a Talk about water color matters — wrote a note to Bellows. Home to tea at 6,30 — [illegible] finished mounting small poles and got to bed shortly after 11 —

## Right Page

### February Friday, 2 1872.

Glorious day, milder than preceding ones \+ cloudless. After fussing about home got to studio a little before 10 \+ spent the m[illegible] until about 1 sweeping and arranging studio. Belle and Mary S. called for a few moments. Home at 1,15 — dressed, lunched \+ at Phil. Rehearsal at 2,30. After rehearsal, walked with Misses Stout \+ Arnold \+ back to studio — washing up until 7. Then home to tea — back to studio at 8 — to entertain Mr \+ Mrs Saml Colman — Conner \+ her husband. Clara Dunham, Louis Tiffany, Belle, Mary \+ Florence \+ [illegible] Matt, Kate \+ George — Chatted — looked over photos etc. until 10 then all were cooks — made chocolate etc, spread the table \+ had a little supper — then washed the dishes — Home at 12,30 — to bed at 1.
