## Left Page

### July, Thursday 18, 1872

Muggy, moist and oppressive atmosphere. Did not recover from my discomfort all day — worked on Merced Gorge block. Lunch at 1 \+ worked until 3.30 — took 4 o'clk stage into town — slight rain falling all the way in. The air getting rapidly cooler. Found Uncle Willie at home. went to tailor's with pantaloons — arranged with Kate to fix sack for drawing board — Returned to N.Y.'s in 7 o'clk stage — A shower at 9.30. To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 19, 1872

Glorious day, but suffering and fighting with a headache. The morning was just delicious and I did not begin work until 10 o'clk — got my Merced Gorge block finished by 1 o'clk lunch time — after lunch quite used up with headache — slung Chas'. Hammock and swung in it until between 3 \+ 4 o'clk. Then up and at work generally over the blocks already done. A telegram came announcing the death of Uncle Jacob V.V. — Dinner at 6. Mr. Dowd \+ son called — Glorious moonlight — wandered about until near 10. Then packed up my traps and to bed at 10.45.
Charl came up sick.
