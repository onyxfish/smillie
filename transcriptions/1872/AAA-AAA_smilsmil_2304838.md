## Left Page

### March Sunday, 24 1872.

Charming day, fresh and clear. Wt went to Dr Hastings and heard him preach from Edcle. 9th Ch. 10th V. — an energetic serm. for action — Dinner at home —
P.M. went to St Thomas's \+ heard Dr Morgan — after service walked in the Park — Home at 6.45 — Wife did not go out but did some letter writing \+ to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### March Monday, 25 1872.

Pleasantly, but overcast later in the day — Got to studio at 8.50 — Kindled fire and started painting 10×18 — "Mt Diabalo \+ the San Joachim." Worked with much enthusiasm, getting some qualities that interested me — Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.35 — and painted until 5, when I got it all in. Brewer called — also a lady, writer from "The Standard", also a gentleman \+ two ladies whom I don't know — Cleaned up \+ home to tea at 6.45 — Worked awhile on Water Color accts \+ at 9. at Sommerville Art Gallery to see the Colman \+ Hall collection. — Walked home nit J.G. Brown. Home at 10.45 to bed at 11.15
