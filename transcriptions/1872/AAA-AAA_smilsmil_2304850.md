## Left Page

### April, Wednesday, 17, 1872.

A fine day \- at 10 o'clk, at the Court of Common Pleas. Part 2. New City Hall \- Summoned as a Juror, Saw the clerk and managed to get off \- then to A.B.M. Co \- back to studio at 11.45 \- Home to lunch at 1.30 about 3 when I Returned \- Miss Bascomb came \- drew for her awhile \- then to work on Ogilvie's wedding present picture \- finished it \- also Moore's \- Home at 7 o'clk. At 845, Matt Kate, Junie Chase \+ I went to the Fair \- there until 11 \- Home \+ to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday, 18, 1872.

Pleasant m'g, cloudy p.m. Rain beginning about 4 o'clk. At 10 o'clk, at City Hall, completing jury business. Back to studio at 10.35 \- fixed desk for work and continued dag. of Head. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- at A.B.M. Co at 4 o'clk \- got transfer \+ back to studio at 5 \- wrote something for Mr. B. Dunbar \+ home to tea at 7 \- heavy Rain. At 845 went down to Fair \- Mary \+ Belle J\- there, Took them home \- Home at 11.30 and got to bed at 12.15
