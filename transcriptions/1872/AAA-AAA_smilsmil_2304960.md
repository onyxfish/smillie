## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 3 1872.

Very pleasant day — went up to Burggraf's & ordered a pair of boots — got to studio at 9.30 & actually spent the whole day over charcoal drawing "Down into Yosemite" — Mr. Knudsen called between 11 & 12 — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30 — Set drawing with fixative & almost ruined it — paper changed color so. At 5 went to Lauthens with little Yosemite painting 11×14 — He expressed himself pleased — Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g spent mounting last summer's studies. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 4 1872.

Pleasant day — almost 10 o'clk when I got to studio, nearly used up with a headache — Rev. Mr. Lundy of Phila called. The Greeley funeral passed studio Bldg about 1.30 — an imposing display of carriages — that was about all — Prest. Grant, V.P. Colfax & Mrs. Wilson in an open carriage. Spent a good part of the mf. sorting & hanging upon the wall my oil studies — Home to lunch at 2.15 — back soon after 3 — all the P.M. fighting with my headache & hanging up studies — Home at 5.30 — gave up entirely — took no tea — upon sofa until 8.30 — then to bed.
