## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 12 1872

Hot as fun. Ther. at 80° in the shade — again worked on drawing — but did not get started until 10 o'clk — abit until 1 — lunch — \+ from 2.30 until 5.30 — Strong breeze all this afternoon — about 7.30 grand effects of coming thunder storms — watched them from the Roof — Smart shower. Walked piazza until near 9 o'clk. To bed at 10 — more showers.

## Right Page

### June Thursday, 13 1872

Up at 6 o'clk — glorious mg. Early bkfp. Uncle a. \+ Junie got off for West Point. I remained \+ worked on drawing at 1 o'clk — lunched. [illegible] worked on until 5.30 getting the old thing into a sort of shape, but not by any means gratifying to me. Tea at 5.30 — alone — After, packed traps \+ picked \+ cleaned up \+ at 7.30 took omnibus \+ got home at 8.20 — Buzzed about — Sponge bath and to bed at 10.45
