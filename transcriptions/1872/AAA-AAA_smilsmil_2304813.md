## Left Page

### January, Sunday 28, 1872

Snowing until about 10 o'clk — amounted to nothing. With Uncle Willie, went to Dr. Hastings's and heard him preach a sermon on giving — text in Proverbs, concerning "Honoring the Lord in giving." — Dinner at 1 — afternoon spent in lounging and napping. Every went down to Madison Ave. Ralp. Ch. \+ heard Rev. Oscar Hugo, a Hungarian Exile, warning us against Papists. Home soon after 9 — General talking until after 11 — then at 11.20 — to bed —

## Right Page

### January, Monday 29, 1872

Very cold clear day — almost a hurricane during the mrg. Finished outlining "Sunlight on a Shadowed Path" and at 3.30 went down to Anthony's to learn something about mounting my Stereoscopic photos. from Cal. — Stopped at Dechaux's — then at Studio \+ home to tea at 6.20 — Evening mounting photos, until after 10, then went to Studio, taking my drawing in progress, on the board. Home at 11 and after writing a note to the Tweedys, to bed at 11.45.
