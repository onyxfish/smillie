## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 18, 1872

A very hot day — went to [illegible] thru to Snedecor's theu to Brooks fork — at 10.15 got to studio. At work on sketches for Yo.S. Miss Alice Buckingham called in the m'f + we had a pleasant chat over Yo.S. Recollections — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 — worked until 6 — at 6.30 went to Kimball's to tea, after meeting Allie D. + her sister May in the Street — at K.'s until 8.15 — theu to Cook's, 192 W. 10 [illegible] — there, talking Adirondacks until 10.10 — Home at 10.40 — To bed at 11.35

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 19, 1872

Another very hot day — 10 o'clk when I got to studio after calling on the Divens at the Hoffman House. Spent the m'f, working on Yo.S. sketches. Suffered so from heat that at 1.30 I went home carrying my material with me — Geo. in from Plainfield. I went to M'ville in 4.30 stage — gloriously cool + breezy at uncle A's — He came up late, so that it was 7.30 before we had din[ner]. Evn'g on Piazza enjoying the air + moonlight — To bed at 10.30.
