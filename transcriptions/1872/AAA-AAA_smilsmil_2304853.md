## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 23, 1872.

Froze last night — Splendid day; clear, fresh & cool — Got to studio at 9.10 o'clk and commenced biting in etching of "Head" — Home to lunch at 1.30 — wrote a note to W— Geo. Whitney of Phila — Back to studio at 2.50 — Engaged on biting \+ on charcoal sketch of general view of [illegible] until 7 o'clk — then home to tea — There found Uncle Warren Petit, Mary Norton \+ her 3 children — well spent looking over English papers — Got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 24, 1872.

Splendid day — Got to studio at 8.45 and to work into graver on die of "Head" (4½). [illegible] 1.5, then home to lunch — back at 2.30 — Miss Bascomb came to draw — then an art critic — do not know his name — then Addie Dunning [illegible] — First time since she was married — a very pleasant visit — then W— Geo. Whitney \+ Enoch Lewis — They stayed until 5.30 — Brevoordt came in \+ went away with miss B — Went to Albemarle \+ dined with Messrs. W. L. and [illegible]. Home at 8 o'clk — at 9 went to fair — Home ab[ou]t and to bed at 11.15
