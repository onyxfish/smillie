## Left Page

### March, Monday, 4, 1872.

Did not get out of bed until 5 o'clk P.M. — Tremendous headache with extremely sore mucus membranes of head and throat. —
When I got up, very weak — in my big chair and on the sofa all night. Unusually cold weather.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday, 5, 1872.

Still very much under this weather. — Pain in head — so cold \+ windy that I dare not go out, so the day spent in doors — much depressed at my condition — Every spend in big chair with a little Reading — [illegible] bed at 11 o'clk.
