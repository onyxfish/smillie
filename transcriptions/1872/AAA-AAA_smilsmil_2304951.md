## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 13 1872.

Fine day — got to studio ab't —
Mr. Geo. Whittiey made a short
call. Got started on charcoal
sketch "Horse Racing in Yo. Semiti
and at it until 1 o'clk. Then
home to lunch — back at 2.30
and afternoon spent over the
same work, trying to arrange
composition & drawing — At
4.30 went down to Southern's —
Home to tea at 7 — At 7.30 went
to Dr Percy's — back at 8.30 —
At 9.15 Kate, Charl and I
went up to Central Park —
Glorious moonlight — Trouble
with the gate keeper, but we
got in & walked up to Belvidere —
on our way out met Jno. Englaud
No 18 + had a passage with him
out at 72d St + walked down
5th Ave to 59th St — Home at 10.45.
To bed at 11.00

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 14 1872.

Miserable, dark, Rainy day —
Got to studio about 9.30. Commenced
by Reducing, by camera-lucida
my charcoal sketch of "Horse
Racing in the Yo. Semite Valley"
to the size of block for Appleton's
article — Traced it + transferred
it to block — Home to lunch at —
back at 2.15 — Worked on
block until 4.15 — could see
no longer — Then worked trying
up screen until 6 — Walked
up to Station "G" 7th Ave 49th St & got
P.O. Stamps — Home to tea at 6.30
Evef. felt "played out" so wrote
a note of Regrets to Son's Tiffany
his Wedding Party or Reception this
evef.) & took it 'round there —
To bed at 10.15 —

Beautiful moonlight.
