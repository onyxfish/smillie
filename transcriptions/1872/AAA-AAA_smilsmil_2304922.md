## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 14 1872.

au overcast m'f, but gradually cleared off to be a most charming day. Ricp't. about 8.30. It was nearly 10 before I got to work upon block". Sentinel Rock. Worked very steadily until 1 o'clk. Then practiced on billiards for half an hour or more. Lunch about 2. After \+ 5 alone in library. After dinner again at my work until 5 when I had nearly completed my block. At 5 went out to stable and saddled "Dandy" \- Rode until 6 \- Jack had Rit'. with Cap't. \+ May. Before dinner Gent. Mr. D. Came home. Lively all together. Got to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 15 1872.

Glorious day. Up soon after 7, but nearly 9 before I had old p't, walking until after 10. Then wrote a letter to Father. Then a letter to Maud, with a good deal of Running about. Dinner at 3. Mrs. Geo. Divien with her children, at the house. After dinner, with Jack \+ the Cap't. drove into town with Aleck's team, message, then back by the longest way. Jack \+ the Cap't. determined to go to N.f, so I writ notes to Father \+ Bunce \+ packed two blocks \+ sent them by the Cap't. The Gent. also went down this even't. Spent the even't chatting \+ Reading and got to bed about 10.30
