## Left Page

### May, Saturday 11, 1872

Quite a cool day. Breakfasted alone \+ took 8.30 stage in to town. Got to studio at 9.40 \+ worked faithfully until 1.15 then home to lunch, back at 2.30. Will Kimball called with invitation to Geo \+ me to join a party to go camping among the Adirondacks — Neta Adams also called with her boy. I worked hard until almost 9 then home to tea. Mounted drawings for A.B.N.Co. \+ in 9.de. Stage Rode out to Uncle A's getting there after they were all in bed, at 10.30 — Geo waiting for me. Got to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 12, 1872

A very pleasant happy day. Bkfst. about 9 \+ with Uncles A. \+ W. and Geo — Sauntered about the place all the morning. Dinner about 2 o'clc — on the piazza after, A. Mc. Cabe came — also young Turrell, then two of Julie's friends — more sauntering with Uncle W. Tea about 6.30 — Every wasted and to bed soon after 10 o'clk.
