## Left Page

### November Friday, 1 1872

Beautiful day — About 10 o'clk started for down town — Called first on Bunce at Appleton's the to Dawry — tailor — ordered dress suit — then to "Mail" office to see Bundy — then to Niepsons — cutter — then to Sutton's with my Aldine block — he gave me $50 — for it — then to A.B.N. Co's — had lunch with them — then to Equitable Ins. Co. Then to 4th Ave. cars — Met Will Kimball & together we went to 1st Phil. Reh. Met the Tuesdays & lots of others — sat part of the time with Kimball & part with J's — After Rehearsal went to Snedecor's — then to studio — paid Rent — Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g Making up accts — and to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 2 1872

Very pleasant day — got to the studio about 10 o'clk — Set to work clearing up the horrible disorder — Got up big ladder & puttered about skylight — made application to Dodworth for certain repairs — Made a cup of tea and lunched at studio — Spent the P.M. sweeping — Had a call from a Mr Dickenson — to get a promise to make a drawing for the "Aldine" — About 5.30 — went down to the Century Club with a note from George — then to Dutton's to see a Reed organ that Charl. fancies — Home to tea very tired at 6.30 — Tea at 7 and eve'g Spent doing nothing — To bed about midnight after a scare caused by finding 3d story water closet locked on the inside — Charl went out & got two policemen who valiantly climbed up and looked through the small window into the closet — nothing in it but a broom which they kindly offered to arrest.
