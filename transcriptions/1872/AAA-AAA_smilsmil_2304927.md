## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 24 1872.

Very warm, clear day — cloudless rather than clear — Ther. at 10 a.m. 78° on north wall, outside of greenhouse. Very much under the weather. Pain in Epigastric Region — worked all the mf. in stable finishing crayon sketch of grey's head. Dinner at 2. Miss Dickenson with us. P.M., unfit for work, pain so great that I couldn't sit at my work. Between 4 & 5 o'clk. took grey & buggy & drove Jack to town — messages — then with him went down the River towards Baden Baden. The P.M. perfectly delicious — enjoyed the ride extremely — late afternoon effect over the valley & River, very lovely. Back at 6.30 — Evey, feeling better, played billiards with Bray Hall & Cap — Just about 11 when I got to bed after taking a glass of Boneset tea —

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 25 1872.

Pleasant mf., but signs of a change. Spent this mf. in carriage house, working from old black — Prepared outline of his head to paint. About 12.30, played billiards — expecting dinner until 3 o'clk — then had it — Rain commenced falling shortly after noon, very heavy most of P.m. Suffered very much from my dyspepsia. Tried billiards — gave it up — tried to read — could do nothing. Bray Hall about — some tea about 8 o'clk — Spent the every Reading some Irish stories aloud — It was after 11 when I got to bed.
