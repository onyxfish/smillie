## Left Page

### December, Thursday 19, 1872

Late when I got up. Bkfst
& other morning duties used up
this time so that it was nearly
11 before I got to studio. Slightly
fine — looked over letters and
Rec'd a call from Miss Caud[?]
Parker. Called on Jas. M. Hart
& then it was after 1 — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — Worked over
blocks & Retouching pfs. from
blocks until nearly 4 — Then
Rode down to Appletons — My
Golden Gate (for eng. on steel) &
other drawings gave satisfaction,
got my money ($125.00). Walked up,
stopped at Brooks Bros & paid bill —
then to Hoffman Ho. Mrs. Diven
gone home — then to studio — Home
at 6 — tea near 7 — Eve'g. Reading
newspapers & lying about — To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 20, 1872

Miserable, dark, sleety and sloppy day.
Got to studio at 10 o'clk. and at once
set to work composing full page
view of great Yosemite Fall. At it
all the m'g. until about 1.30 when
I prepared my lunch. Finished
sketch & planned in charcoal
three other full pages. Mr. Meign
& her sister Miss Rose Ballon called
about 4 o'clk. After I lighted the
gas made a note to Filmer, the
wood engraver & a letter to Mrs.
Genl. Diven — Home at 7 o'clk —
tea about 7.30 — Eve'g. quiet
in my big chair — Read a little
and to bed soon after 10 o'clk.
