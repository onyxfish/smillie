## Left Page

### November Sunday, 3 1872

Miserable rainy day &
in bed until after dark
with a beastly head-
ache — Took quite a
hearty meal about 10 o'clk
P.M. and enjoyed it —
Spent the rest of the
night on the sofa —

## Right Page

### November Monday, 4 1872

Charming day — Somewhat
under the weather still from
headache of yesterday — Got
to studio at 10 o'clk — a man
until 12 o'clk — Painting inside
of skylight — Miss Bascomb
called — also Mr. & Mrs. James
A. Hind — Home at 12.30, worked
a while on George's drawing of
Montreal — Lunch at 1 — Back
to studio at 2.30 — Fussed
about, cleaning up, all the
afternoon — Miss B. again
called about drawing on wood.
Home to tea at 6 — Spent
doing nothing in particular
until 11 o'clk — then to bed —
