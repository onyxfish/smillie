## Left Page

### June, Sunday, 16, 1872

A very pleasant day — Myf went to Dr. Hastings + heard him preach a very spirited + excellent sermon from the 11 V. of the 29th Psalm. He does not want a smooth path, but more strength + courage. Home to dinner — then some writing + a nap — at 3.45 — out to walk — up through the Park to Mr. Jessup — 5th Ave. bet. 8 gnt 86th St — Stayed to tea + until 9 o'clk — the call made very pleasant for me — walked home through the Park — delightful moonlight — a little writing and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Monday, 17, 1872

Clear, hot day — Myf, went to Brook's Bros, on way back, stopped at Hoffman He + saw Maud Drew — did not get to studio until 10.45 — set palette + worked over sketches for Borse + the Watkins painting — Home to lunch abt — back at 2.30 — until 6.30 working on pencil sketches for illustration Yosemite article — then home to tea — Matt, Kate + Charle home from West Point — also Parker + Uncle Ch. — Aunt J. + Emma left for Brooklyn — At 8.30 went to Kimball's — out — at 9 went to Hoffman — there until 10.10 — Home at 10.30 + to bed abt 11.20
