## Left Page

### February Monday, 5 1872

Wild & enervating sort of day —
got to studio at 9.15 — Kindled fire
passed about and got to make
on Water Color drawing — George
takes possession of his Room
in Y.M.C.A. Bldg. to-day —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 — Jonnie Jordon called
to say that Allie Diven is so unwell
that we will have to give up going
to Josie Jessup's to-night — Comme
Smith & a young lady called —
Also C.C. Ward — At 4.30 I
went down to see Perry — Saw him
& lots of other fellows — then went
to Dechaux's — There met Paulton —
went to his Room & 7 o'clk. home
to tea. Went, & spent an hour at the
Rogers's in 42d St — Made up W.C.
Report — Repaired damaged study
& to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 6 1872

Very dense fog — Up early and at
9 o'clk, was at the West mail office
with three letters — Saw Bundy — Got
to Studio just about 10 o'clk. Worked
on W.C. drawing — Home to lunch at
1.30 — found new policy on Life
from Continental Co — Didn't
understand it — went down-
town again to see about it — back
to studio about 4 o'clk — slight
drizzling Rain — Home to tea
at 6.15 — Rain quite heavy —
At 7.30 — at N.A. of D., Annual
Water Color Meeting — Wm. Hart
consented to act again as Pres.
and I went in as Treas. —
Home soon after 11 & to bed at
11.31¼ —
