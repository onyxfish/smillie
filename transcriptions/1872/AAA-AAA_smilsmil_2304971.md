## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 25 1872

Very beautiful, cold day,
thickening up late in the after-
noon. Got to Studio at 9.15 &
pitched into drawing on block
Yo Semite fall — Worked closely
& got a good days work
done by 2 o'clk — Home to
dinner at 2.30 — Spent what
Remained of the afternoon
lounging — Christmas tree
lighted up soon after 6 o'clk
Millie very much delighted
and an hour spent enjoying
the wonders — Tea about 7.30
to bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 26 1872

Heavy driving snow storm
all day long — Got to Studio
at 9.15 and to work on block
Yo Semite fall — Worked at
great disadvantage owing
to cold and want of light,
but pegged away at it until
2 o'clk. when I was obliged
to give it up as the snow had
banked entirely over my
sky light & the Room was as
dark as a prison — Prepared
lunch & spent Remainder
of P.M. fixing up closet and
getting things somewhat to
rights — Home at 6.30 after
a long walk in the storm.
Rested lying about as is now
my habit, until 10 o'clk. Then
to bed.
