## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 21, 1872

A very pleasant day — mng — went to Dr. Hastings's. He preached concerning "Spiritual Mindedness" from Romans 8th ch. 6th v. Sat awhile in the little park, enjoying sun \+ air — then home to dinner — W. Parker there — After dinner again in the park until 4.30 — then home \+ slept until 6.30 — Tea at 7 — went to Broadway Tabernacle \+ heard Prof Seely of Andover; an excellent sermon but must confess that at this moment I cannot recall the text. Home at 9 — Read, and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 22, 1872

Rain until after 10 o'clk — then cleared charmingly — Got to studio at 9 o'clk. Kindled fire and went on mth etching "Read" — finished that noon — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked until 6.30 on charcoal drawing, 12×20 in. "general view of Yosemite Valley from Cloud's Rest." Home to tea at 7 — Aunt Jessie at the house all night — Evtg. Reading and chatting — To bed at 10.45
