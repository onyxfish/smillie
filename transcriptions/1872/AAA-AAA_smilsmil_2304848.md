## Left Page

### April, Saturday, 13, 1872

Quite a heavy rain during the m'f. Got to studio at 8.30 Mr. Robt. Hewitt \+ then Goodall called, both on business — Settled that; wrote a letter to W. Andrews \+ at 9.30 started for 22d Regt. Armory — weather clearing up. Worked until noon — home to lunch — back at 1.10 — Finished hanging and at work cataloguing until 6.30 other things kept me until 7.15 then home to tea. After tea went to W. Miller's, 578 5t Ave. then to Jemy Milbank's 6 & 38th St; about catalogue. Got to Fair again at 9.30 — left for home in the $115.50 got to 11.15, got to bed at 12.15

## Right Page

### April, Sunday, 14, 1872

Glorious day, but in bed mth a savage headache until near 5 o'clk P.m. Then up — bathed, dressed, had something to eat \+ felt Refreshed. At 9 o'clk, called at Avery's — not in — then at W. Jas. Fraser's, 59 W. 19th St; — information for Catalogue. Stopped at Studio, got drawing board \+ home at 10.15 — got to bed about midnight.
