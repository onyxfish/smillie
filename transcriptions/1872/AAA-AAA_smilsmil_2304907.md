## Left Page

### August Tuesday, 13 1872.

Still muggy \+ miserable weather with thunder showers all about us. Went out with Will K. at 6 o'clk \+ had a good swim. Owing to showers, spent most of the mrng in doors — but about 11 went out with Will in bout \+ around the island. — back abt 6 clk. \+ dinner soon after. The afternoon about as profitably spent as the mrng. Mr. Griggs of Boston displayed his art treasures in the parlor. Then a fine exhibition of passing storm from cupola — Could not Resist, so took at nap at 4.30. Tea at 7 — Showers — Evng. a "Hop" — on piazza with Kimball — some fine effects of moonlight. Rec'd a letter from Matt, mailed from N.Y. Aug. 9 [illegible] — To bed abt 11.45

## Right Page

### August Wednesday, 14 1872.

Up at 6 o'clk. and out with Will for a swim — Heavy fog \+ wetting mizzle. — back to bed \+ slept until 7.45 — then up \+ b'kfst. Weather improved during the mrg. so that all the K's went over to the island. — There met Amelia Rice \+ Mr. Owen — all together till near 1 — then back to dinner. Heavy showers — In the midst of one Annie C's mother arr'd. I worked over my sketches until after 5 — then dressed \+ dinner at 6.30 — Evng. still very showery — on piazza \+ in parlor — Introduced to one or two — chatting — games — took no part \+ to bed about midnight.
