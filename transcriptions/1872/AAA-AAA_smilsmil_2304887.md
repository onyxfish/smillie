## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 2, 1872

Up about 5.30 — Took carriage at abt 6 — went to Mr. Parkers' — got some bed clothes — then abt to Uncle W's — there a few minutes after 7 — They at bkfst — som had ours — a little chatting \+ looking about \+ then to work at abt 9.30 — Dinner at 12.30 — afternoon suffered very much from heaviness and the heat — did not work well — Tea at 7 — at 8. Kate \+ I went into town in the Phaeton \+ did some shopping — Excessively hot in town — delightful on the way back — Home at 9.30 — Got to bed on my sofa about 11 — Passed a tolerable night.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 3, 1872

Bkfst. at 7 — Got to work on Arms drawing just before 9 o'clk. and soon after dinner, abt 6 clk. I finished them. During the remainder of the afternoon I traced sketch of Big Trees for [illegible] article — After tea at 7 o'clk I went in town with Emma — Horse behaved very badly at some lumber by the roadside — Returned a little before 9 — worse actions at the same lumber pile — but got in all Right. — Got to my sofa bed at 10.30 and to sleep about 1 o'clk. a.m.
