## Left Page

### December Friday, 13 1872

In bed all day — High fever,
splitting headache — Sore throat
& very weak pulse. Running a
trifle over 100 p. minute.
Under Dr. Throop's care.
Had some sleep during the
night.
Missed Philharmonic Rehearsal
to 2d. concert.

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 14 1872

Got up out of bed about 11 am —
Very groggy on my legs — my
head so giddy that I could
hardly stand — got into my
big chair, rolled in my
blanket & fed up on beef tea
& Elms wine — a good deal
of pains in my head — but
I say that altho' my turn
this time has been very sharp
as well as sudden, I am
progressing quite as well as
could be expected — fever
less & throat looking better.
To bed at 11, suffering more
than ever in my head — but
I got some good sleep never-
theless.
Missed 2d. Philharmonic
Concert.
Kensett died in his studio
to-day, very suddenly.
