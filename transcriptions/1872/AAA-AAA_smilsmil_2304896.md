## Left Page

### July Saturday, 20 1872.

Superb morning — fresh + breezy.
I out 8 o'clk. Stage — home at 9 —
went to Miller's — then down town
\- called on Harley, showed him
my drawings on wood and asked
his technical suggestions — then to
A.B.N. Co — got $60.00 then to
Cont. Valentine + Co — then to D.
Appleton + Co — Bunce mt — went
\+ lunched — on return saw B. and
[illegible] — my drawings freely criti-
cised — got check for $140.00
then to Knoedler's — then Studio
then to harness makers for straps
and home soon after 4. — Jobs
from to get everything ready —
Eve'g spent between Burgraff
and the Tailors — Will + Jos
at the house — To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### July Sunday, 21 1872.

Very delightful day, so fresh
and cool — Mrs. [illegible] went to
Young Tyne's + heard him
preach a thoroughly mag-
netic + earnest sermon from
Romans 13th Ch. 11th V. "now it's
high time to awake out of sleep" &c.
I was much moved + enjoyed
it. Home to dinner at 1 —
Parker with us — P.m. worked
getting things put away and
in order — a nap at 5 — tea
between 6 + 7 — Eve'g, went
with "the folks" to Gilette's
Ch. in 25th St — + heard their
pastor "the Something" —
To bed soon after 11.30
