## Left Page

### May Tuesday, 21 1872

A most delightful day. Got
to my work on drawing at about
9.30 & spent the m'f, completing
a very careful outline — Lunch
at 1. Afternoon commenced
washing color on the sky —
Had my troubles, of course.
Worked until 5.30 — then
dinner — Ev'f Rambling
about, glorious night —
To bed about 9.30

## Right Page

### May Wednesday, 22 1872

A milky m'f, getting more &
more overcast until ev'f when
a good rain set in, with
some thunder. Up at 6.30
made some notes & walked
before b'f't. Got to work on
drawing soon after b'f'ella.
& spent the whole day mussing
over the sky — a Regular mess
Dinner at 6 — Ev'f Read
a week in a French Country
House". & got to bed at
9.45
