## Left Page

### January, Friday, 26, 1872

fine, clear, cold day. Up with threats of a headach — went down to the Barbers at 10 o'clk — had hair cut — then to studio's — then to Bogardus \+ home soon after 12 — lay down on bed until 2 o'clk — then to Phil. Rehearsal — Suffered greatly — on my way home, stopped with the Tweedy's at Knœdler's — then home and from 5 until 8 o'clk in great [illegible] misery — then Sam. Coleman \+ Bellows called \+ me spent the eveg until 10 o'clk. looking over my Cal. photos — after they left, I continued to look over the same until 1.30 A.M. then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday, 27, 1872

Milder and overcast — During the m't, went up to see J. D. A. Ward — did not see him. Outlined on drawing — but did'nt succeed in doing much — Lunch at 1 — then went to Greene's Studio — then to Sam. Coleman's to see about the matter of having our Water Color Exh'n open at night. C — urges me to "Run" for Prest. of W. C's — then to 10th St — saw Perry, Beard, Guy \+ Cas'lear. Then to Sautrier's \+ got mounting board. Home to tea at 6.30 — Uncle Willie's — at the house — Eveg, mounting photos — until after 1 o'clk a.m. — then to bed —
