## Left Page

### January, Thursday 18, 1872

Another glorious day — all day closely at work on sketch in distemper of "Wall of Half Dome, from Tenaya Canyon" — Lunch at 1.30 + then worked until 3 — gave it up — not quite finished nor satisfactory; still, I think a step forward. — At 3.20 went in to Dr Throop's to the Homoepathic Hospital in 55th St — Took notes of two specimens of diseased uterus to make drawings — then walked in the Park until tea time — Home to tea at 6 — Ewry blocked out subject for another distemper sketch — "The Half Dome from Cloud's Rest." To bed at 10.30.
Joppan + his son at the studio (I worked at home) Letter from Mita adcams.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 19, 1872

Overcast all day — at 8.40 went to Lauthier's about frames — then to Snedecor's + home at 10 o'clk — at work on "Wall of Half Dome" and at it until lunch abt 6 clk. — At 2, went to Phil. Rehearsal — Miss Katie Poole there with Matt — at 4 o'clk, Rode down town with Mary + Florence Tready + Geo — went to A.B.N. Co. + saw Goodall, Walked to St Luke's Pl. in the Will — took dinner there — It rained — abt 9.15 walked up home in a heavy driving Rain got in at 10.15 — abt. Took a sponge off and then to bed — [illegible — ~15 words]
