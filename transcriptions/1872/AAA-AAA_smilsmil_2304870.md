## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 29, 1872.

Splendid day. Up at 6 o'clk. Got started on W.C. drawing at 8.30. but wasted nearly an hour. worked until 1 o'clk — then lunch — After that, worked on A.B. M Co's drawing until nearly 6 o'clk — getting it almost finished. After dinner walked from 7 until 8.30. Superb sunset. Read one of Louisa Alcott's Sketche and to bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 30, 1872.

Up at 6.30 — overcast mrlf. Rain began about 10 \+ fell heavily until 2. Got to work on water color drawing about 10.30 and kept at it until 12.30 — a walk on piazza and lunch att — again at work until 5 o'clk. Then took a turn at Rolling the walk — Dinner at 5.30 after dinner walked for nearly an hour when a damp, mizzle set in — Read until 9.30 — Then to-bed.
