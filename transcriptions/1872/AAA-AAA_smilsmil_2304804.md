## Left Page

### January, Wednesday, 10, 1872.

Glorious day but I felt again so much under the weather that I did not go to the Studio. Spent the m'f at home writing on a "Mail" letter \- lunch at 1.30 \- at 3 met with Chas to 26 [illegible] \+ took a Turkish Bath \- my first experience \- I rather enjoyed it \- Eve'f at 7.30 went to St. James's Theatre (tickets given by Carpenter) to see Mackaye play in Monaldi \- Rather enjoyed that besides mildly falling in love with Miss Griswold. Walked up home with Louis Tiffany \- m at 10.45 \+ to bed about 11 \-

## Right Page

### January, Thursday, 11, 1872.

Fine day \- got to studio at 9.20 worked on charcoal drawing \+ at 10.30, Jessie Hayland, my model came along, worked from her until 1.30 \- Home to lunch at 1.45 \- Nette Sutphen at lunch \- back to studio at 2.45 \- arranged lay-figure \+ worked until 5 \- Mr. Beach daughter \+ Miss Wheeler called. Home to tea at 6 \- Eve'f wrote on letter to "Mail" \+ got to bed at a little after 10 \-
