## Left Page

### December Saturday, 21 1872

Beautiful day, but it was
9.30 before I got to Studio —
Spent this day writing
& copying an article on
the Yosemite for Appleton's
Journal — Soon after noon
went over to Y.M.C.A. Bl&
& saw Gel in his new Room —
then to N.A. of P. & saw Richards —
Back to studio & prepared
lunch — Worked at my writing
as long as I could see. Home
at 6 o'clk — Tea at 6.30. Eve'g
mounted drawing for Gel —
& oil study for self — Some
Reading and to bed at 10.30

All very much shocked
at the sudden death of
G. P. Putnam yesterday P.m.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 22 1872

Brilliantly clear & very cold
day — Ther. 10° in the m'g & 7° at
night — Went to Hastings and
heard him — Rev. 3° ch. 2° v. —
At 12.20 started with Charl
for A.V.V.'s — Walked there in
¾ hours — Enjoyed it very
much as I was well protected
against the cold — Dinner at
2 — Lying about in-doors until
4.30 when I started for home
alone — walked down — got
in at 5.45 — Tea at 6.15 — Eve'g
Read a sermon aloud — then
finished some writing and to
bed at 11.
