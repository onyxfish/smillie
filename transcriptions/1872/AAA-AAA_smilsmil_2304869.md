## Left Page

### May Monday, 27 1872.

Beautiful mf. Soon after b'clp'. got to work out of doors on water color drawing — wind got very high by noon \+ a skim over the sky. At 12 went off on a walk — back at 12.45 \+ lunch at 1 — Over cast and threatening. heavy Rain at 3 o'clk — \+ on until 6 — then it cleared. Worked steadily until 5.30 on drawing for a/B.N.Co. Evelf until 10.30 — writing a composition for Junie —. To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### May Tuesday, 28 1872.

Charming day. Clear \+ crisp with superb cloud effects. Up soon after 6 \+ had a good walk before b'clp'. After b'clfor. to work on drawing of house \+ stuck closely to it until 12 o'clk. Then again walked until near 1 — then lunch — at 2, Uncle Mr \+ aunt H. left for Montrose. Immediately after, to work on a B.N.Co. drawing — Did not get up from my seat \+ hardly raised my head until almost 6 — then dinner —. After dinner walked down into the village with Uncle a — back \+ walked alone until 8.30 — then in \+ read for an hour. To bed shortly before 10.
