## Left Page

### February Thursday, 15 1872.

Very cold, clear mrg. afternoon overcast. Got to studio at 9.15. Painted in a figure in "Gothic mountains" — then set to work on an old subject, laid in in Alp? 1864, 8 × 13 in. very bad in composition, but giving me a chance to take a lesson in handling while producing something that may bring me a few dollars at some auction sale. Home to lunch at 1.15 — walked up with Louis Tiffany. Matt, Mary \+ the baby arrived from Pok'eepsie — Back to studio at 2.30 — Painted until 5 o'clk — washed up \+ home at 6.20 — eveg loafing — to ked at 10.45.

## Right Page

### February Friday, 16 1872.

Charming day. got to Studio a.m. but unable to do any thing owing to headache. About noon Belle, Mary, Florence \+ Jennie Tweedy came in — Left at 1 o'clk — walked up home, unable to eat any lunch — at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal — barely able to get through it — home at 4.30 wild with my head.
