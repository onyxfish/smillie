## Left Page

### March Saturday, 30 1872

Pleasant day, somewhat overcast. Got to studio at 8.45 — to work on drawing of Water Carrier. Mr. Chas. Toppan \+ Son came in about 1 o'clk and stayed until near 2 — Then home to lunch — back at 3 — finished drawing about 5 o'clk — Ab b. It began to Rain — Home to tea at 6.45 — Swell Mailing newspapers to Water-Color fellows \+ mounting drawing —
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 31 1872

Dark, windy and rainy — Sick and sore with a heavy cold — So I spent the day in doors very diligently doing nothing. Charle also under the weather — Parties with Matt during p. m. \+ went — Heavy Rain at night — Got to bed at 10.15
