## Left Page

### August, Saturday, 17, 1872.

At Thos. Vail's — Peach Lake.

Hot, muggy and thunderous — Bkfst. at 8 — then went to Central depot \+ got Harlem time-table. Spent the mrf. in clearing up \+ putting away camp traps etc. — Took a bathe \+ dressed — Lunch at 1 — then Rose down to Hoffman House to see Mr. D'veau — not in — then to Studio — letters from Meta, G.A. and Ferguson. Home at 2.30 — clear but terribly hot. Took 3.55 Train for Brewster's — There at 6.10 — delicious evng. met Preston, Spring \+ bro. \+ Mr. Hoyt. Emma at Station. Rode up in stage-house about 7 — pleasantly greeted by the Ireland family — Faces \+ hands washed — tea — munal. Miss Kitty \+ Mr. Hoyt \+ S. — Then, with many J. all five in boat rowing. Cloudless full moon — water most of the time without a ripple — Everything \+ everybody delightful — Out until after 10 — walked piazza with Emma until 11.15 — To bed at 11.20 — Sharing Mr. Hoyt's Room — an extra bed having been put up.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday, 18, 1872.

Bkfst. at 8 o'clk. Very warm, cloudless mtt. About 10 o'clk. wandered off with Emma, Miss Kitty S. \+ Mr. Hoyt. At a little distance from the house, on the top of a hill, under a large chestnut found a flat Rock and there sat, enjoying a delicious breeze until I got to be somewhat confidential and sentimental — a weakness that is growing upon me as I get older. Mens, must put a stop to it. Back to dinner at 2 o'clks. Sat on piazza all the P.M. Fred, J am? \+ Oscar expected — a short walk before tea with Emma. — Tea at 7 — Superb moonrise. unexpected arrival of Geo. Cook \+ Miss Hoyt — out on the lake until after 9 — then Miss Hoyt \+ C. left to North Salem. out again in boat until after 10 — night without a cloud \+ the Harvest Moon doing its very best. The night a perfect success — To bed at 11 —
