## Left Page

### January, Thursday 4, 1872

Bkfst. late and feeling a little better. Most of the m'f walking about the streets and arranging back numbers of the "Mail" with my letters. Lunch at 1 o'clk. more headache — Belle and Many Tweedy came about 3 o'clk, and shortly before 4 I went out with them and walked for about an hour — then home — Tea at 6.15 — Eve'g. spent doing nothing in particular and to bed at 10 30

## Right Page

### January, Friday 5, 1872

Clear \+ beautiful winter day. All the m'g at home working on water color Study of dress on my Rag-baby, to be used in my drawing of "Sunlight on a Shadowed Path" — Got something that encouraged me. at 2 o'clk. went to the St. Cloud Hotel \+ went with B. \+ M. T — to Phil. Rehearsal. Enjoyed it exceedingly — Home about 5 o'clk — Eve'g did a little Reading \+ got to bed at 11 —
