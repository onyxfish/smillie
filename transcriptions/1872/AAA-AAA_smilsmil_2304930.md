## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 30 1872.

Beautiful day altho' with signs of change. Spent the morning in carriage house making study from head of grey — all things together I think it the best one yet. Worked until 1.30. Then lunch — After, worked for about an hour — cleaned up & at 4 off on the grey — Cloudy sky that way about by Satta Brook & among the hills, through Jerusalem & out by the Water Cure — a ride of about 10 miles & exceedingly enjoyable — In about 6 or soon after. About 7, Mr. Morris & niece arrived. Dinner near 8. Spent between chatting in library & billiards in the billiard room. To bed with Jack about 10.30

## Right Page

### October Tuesday, 1 1872.

Cloudy & overcast. Spent the whole morning on the Fair Ground, chiefly among the cattle & horses. Slight showers & threatening weather — Back at 1.30 — Lunch at 2 — Heavy rain all the p.m. Rec'd letters from Ged & Josie Beutly. Wrote to Kate, Jonney & part of a letter to Emma J. Dinner about 6.30 — Mr. Morris's son with us — Evening again chatting cards & billiards — To bed at 11.30
