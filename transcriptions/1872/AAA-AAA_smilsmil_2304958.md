## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 27 1872

Delightful day — Got to Studio
at 9 & worked all day (2d day)
on drawing of the Golden Gate.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & worked until after 4
when I could see no longer.
Home at 5.40 — Matt & Parker
awd from Washington about
6 P.M. — Tea at 7 — Eve'g
lying about & doing a little
Reading — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 28 1872

*Thanksgiving*

Pleasant day — Got to Studio
at 9 o'clk. & worked on drawing
of Golden Gate until 2 o'clk. then
home to dinner — took things
easy until about 4, then
walked up to Central Park
to 85th St. & Fifth ave, & called
on the Jessup's for an hour, from
6 to 7 — then Crossed the Park
to 5qd St & 8th ave — & to Will's. There
met Uncle A, Janie & Uncle Mr. &
Montrose. Charl, Matt, Kate, Mary
& Parker came later — Stayed
until 11 — then home & to bed
just at midnight.
