## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 7, 1872

A very warm day — Got to studio at 9.15 — Swept up a little &
set palette. A.F. Bellows called. Tried an experiment in color,
9×15, Werf, in eastern sky —
Painted it in in two hours —
Home to lunch at 1.30 back
at 2.40 — Worked until 6.30
on "Coffee" for Hewitt (1 day)
Home to tea at 7 o'clk —
At 8, called on Mr. S.H. Wales
520 Fifth ave. — Then, at 8.30,
at N.A. of D. Quarterly Water-
color Meeting — There until
10 o'clk — Home at 10.30 and
to bed at 11.15. Almost a
thunderstorm about 7.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 8, 1872

Tremendously hot day — Ther.
86° in my studio. Got to Studio
at 9.15 — Set to work on "Coffee" —
Worked until near noon — then
went to barbers — then home
to lunch, then back to studio
at 1.30 — Worked on Coffee until
4 o'clk — then for an hour on
10½×18 — Mr. Salayette for Watkin
the photo. of San F. At 5 went
to Sauthier's. Then to Seavitt's
Home to tea at 7 — Evening
meeting & arranging accts —
Got to bed at 10.30
