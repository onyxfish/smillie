## Left Page

### May, Saturday 25, 1872

Muggy, spitting day — my after 8 bef\. mate letter to Elisha Mulford. Then to work on drawing for A.B.N. Co. At it until 12.30 — Then went to Greenie's — my studio — Borraki's Bros\. \+ the Searitt Gallery — back at 2.15 — lunch — then again on drawing until 6 o'clk when I finished outline. Tea about 7 — until after 8. putting some Cal, seeds into pots — After that, writing \+ Reading until 10 o'clk. When I went to bed —
Father \+ Mother went to Plainfield this P.M. —

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 26, 1872

Threatening weather, but otherwise pleasant. went to Dr. Hasting's and heard him preach from 2°. Cor. 5th ch. 4th v. After service went home \+ with Charl. at 1 o'clk started for Uncle A's — got there in time for dinner — Afternoon spent wandering about. Evening cleared charmingly but my headache increasing, about 9 o'clk. I went to bed.
