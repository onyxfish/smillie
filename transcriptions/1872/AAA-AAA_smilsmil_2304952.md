## Left Page

### November Friday, 15 1872

glorious day — got to Studio at 9.20 & to work on Horse Racing block. Miss Bascomb called with wood blocks. Worked until after 1 — then prepared lunch & at 2.30 at Philharmonic Rehearsal. Enjoyed it very much, Rubenstein and all. Walked up with Fanny K. & Annie Cook. At Studio's until 6.30. fixing up top screen over engraving desk — Home to tea at 7 — Evening fussing about until 10.30, then to bed.

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 16 1872

Pleasant mf. — gradually thickening up until every, when a miserable sleety snowstorm set in. Got to studio soon after 9. Painted on 11 × 14" "Descent into Yo.Seimit" for Gauthier — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Doris Deluroe at the house — back at 2.30 & painted as long as I could see — Home to tea at 5.30. At 7.30 — went with Geo. Charl. — Matt & Kate to the first Phil. concert, enjoyed it very much. Rubenstein Rec'd quite an ovation — Home about 11 and midnight when I got to bed.
