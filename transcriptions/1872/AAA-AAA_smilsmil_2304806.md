## Left Page

### January, Sunday, 14, 1872

Brilliant but very cold day —
Mng, went to Hastings'. I heard
him preach from Matt. 6 "10".
"Thy kingdom come" — walked
for half an hour before dinner.
Dinner about 1 o'clk. —
About 2.30, on my bed and
slept until 4.30 — then out
\+ took a short walk —
Tea at 6 — Even'g wrote
a letter to Mr. Main \+ notes
to Reilly of Stockton and
Jannlay — To bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### January, Monday, 15, 1872

Beautiful day — got to Studio
at 9.10 — got ahead some — he
kindled fire — at work on
Charcoal drawing — finished
it — set it — went to Silleck's.
then to Knoedler's — back to
studio \+ home to lunch at
1.40 — back at 2.45 — Mr.
Hines, an amateur in Water
colors, called — Traced on
Charcoal drawing — at
5 stretched on lounge, uneasy
nap — Home to tea at 6.20 —
out of sorts — at 8 — called
on Hannah (Barnaby) Appleton
at 246 W. 15th St. \+ Renewed old
times — Home at 10 — wrote
Water Color notices until
11.30 — To bed shortly before
12 —
