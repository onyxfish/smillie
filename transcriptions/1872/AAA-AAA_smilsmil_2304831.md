## Left Page

### March Friday, 8 1872.

Very pleasant wth, got up feeling quite like a white man — got to studio about 10 o'clk. Kindled fire about 11.30 — Uncle A. called — He wished me to ride up in carriage to his house + spend the rest of the week — could n't — we went over to N.A. of D. together — then to Geo's studio — there met M. + E. Dixon — I went up to Wyant's + saw Shurtliffe — then saw Louis Tiffany. Again went to Geo's + with the D's to N.A. of D — soon excused myself + home to lunch at 1.45 — back to studio about 3 — Did but little during the P.M. — Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g mussing with photos (large Watkins'.) trying to mount some that that fool Bly, at Bogardus's got on the wrong mounts. got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 9 1872.

Miserable, sleety day — got to studio at 9.30 — Set palette, and to work trying to "paint" "Coffee" No. 2 for Hewitt — Gradually got into this way of working. Miss Sterling called about noon — Weather so bad that I thought it best not to try to go home to lunch. made a cup of tea and eat crackers. Worked as long as I could see. Home to tea at 6.15. Eve'g, again fussing with photos. \+ Reading Barny-Gould's Legends of Old Testament characters. To bed shortly before 11. —
