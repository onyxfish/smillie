## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 13 1872.

Mild, broken day, cloudy and clear. Got to studio at 9.10 \+ spent an hour or two working on Coffee drawing — then to work on painting for Mr. Jno. Whitney. Drew in and painted old Hemlock where I had painted out some other trees — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 \+ painted until 4.45, when feeling very tired I stretched on lounge \+ slept for half an hour. Waked by Rain on Sky-light. Home to Tea at 6.40 — Eveg very heavy Rain, hail \+ thunder \+ lightening. Wrote Water Color notes, etc until 10.30 — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February Wednesday, 14 1872.

Snow squalls and sunshine with lots of cold wind — Got to studio at 8.45 — Charcoaled \+ outlined on "Glimpse into Yo-Semite Valley" 11×14. until near 11 — then went down-town — Called at Sutton's "Aldine" — not in — at Continental Life As. \+ at B.N.Co— Home to lunch at 1.20 — back to studio at 2.30 \+ worked on 11×14 until near 5 — (Brewer called) then to Miller's \+ had a Turkish bath — Home to Tea at 6.40 — Eveg Reading newspapers — Tried to write, but called in — got to bed at 10.30
