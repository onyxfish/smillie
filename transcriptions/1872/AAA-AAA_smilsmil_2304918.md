## Left Page

### Sept. Friday, 6 1872.

Miser weather again and looking like Rain — Spent all the mfg until dinner time, 2 o'clk — finishing drawing view from foot of Senticiel fall — Then dinner + about 3 o'clk. went to stables + tried to get crayon sketches from Dandy — Worked until 5 o'clk. without any success — Then went out with Jack, he on Dandy — I on the Grey — Rode over to Aleck's Reservoir then home — Grey fretted, chafed, jigged, Ran, did every thing but walk — Got in at 6.45 wet with perspiration \+ tired — Tea after 7 — two ladies from Ralston — Went, Pin Poole, Bray, Capt. \+ Jack — then Poker \+ to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Saturday, 7 1872.

Warm weather again — Very warm hot — All the mfg feeling very lazy — effects of horseback ride — Read — Prepared sketching materials \+ finished letter to Will K. \+ wrote to Stuart Burt — Dinner at 2.30 From 3.30 until 6.30 trying to make a study (oil) from one of the Grey's legs — not a success — Tea at 7.30 — Later in the eveng. with Capt \+ Jack at Pin Poole — from 9.30 until 11.15 at Poker — then to bed —
