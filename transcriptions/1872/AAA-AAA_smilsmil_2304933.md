## Left Page

### October Sunday, 6 1872

Still a beautiful day, altho'
evidently preparing for a change.
Did not go to church, but spent
the mf. writing a letter to Mrs.
Ireland — An hour picking up
apples before dinner which was
served about 2.30 — Wrote a
letter to Geo. — Aleck, Mrs.
Children & Jennie Mc D. came
about 4 o'clk & stayed to tea.
About until tea time — 6.30 —
Genl. D., Jack & the Capt. went
to NY by night riff. — Mrs.
D. & I left all alone — We
sat in the library & wrote
to Maud & Ella until after
11 — To bed at 12 —

## Right Page

### October Monday, 7 1872

Heavy, downpouring Rain —
Telegram rec'd early that Geo.
D'rew and Last night in "Oceanic"
(so reported — but not so.) Spent
part of the mf. tracing sketch
of "Cathedral Spires — Yo. Semite."
Lunch at 1 — then continued
drawing on block — About 4.30
or 5 o'clk — Put on his boots and
rubber coat & went out and
walked in the mud and Rain
for an hour — Enjoyed it im-
mensely — Mrs. D. rode down
town, hoping to see her son Geo.
I took tea alone — Mrs. D.
Ret'd between 8 & 9 o'clk —
We played cards until 11 —
Then to bed —
