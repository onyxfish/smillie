## Left Page

### May Wednesday, 1 1872

Cold, blustering, overcast and cheerless day — After writing more for Mr Lewis — got to Studio at 9.45 — A Rebit on father's die of the Water Carrier — Rossell \+ Mrs Bartlett at Studio. Home to lunch at 1.30 — Back at 2.45. Miss Bascomb there — worked with graver \+ burnisher on die until 6 o'clk. Then went to Snedecor's — paid his bill — then to Mrs. Bartlett's — then to Studio \+ home at 8 o'clk. — Tea at 8.45 — Tired and did nothing — to bed at 10.20

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 2 1872

Got to studio at 9.15 o'clk — Rain. Felt perfectly played out and unable to work — Forced myself to begin S.A. drawing of "The Snowy Illimani's" for A B; M Co. Home to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.45 and worked better during the P.M. getting drawing well advanced. At 4.30 Belle \+ Mary J. called. At 6.30 went to Y.M.C.A. and Renewed subscription. Home to tea at 7. Wrote a couple of notes \+ at 8.30. went to Leavitt's where Moran's" Yellowstone Gorge" picture (7×12 ft) was on private view. Prof. Hayden gave a general description — Home at 10.45 \+ to bed at 11. The day has been very sunny \+ Sultry — !
