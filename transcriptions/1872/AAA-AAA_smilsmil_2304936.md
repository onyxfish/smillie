## Left Page

### October Saturday, 12 1872

Cold, cloudy & windy — Up
at 7 o'clk — Help'd at a Geo.
Alick & the gent at table —
Eugene & wife coming in while
we were at bklg— After, a sort
of business & family council
between father & sons — At 10
I went out to canal bridge
on Gypsey Lane & made pencil
sketch — Back home chilled
through — Gent & Mr. D gone to
Watkins — Out again & sketched
on Willows Brook — In at 1 —
Dinner at 2 — Dressed, but was
delayed until after 4 when
May, Allie, Cap't & I got away
in carriage making calls —
on Mrs Reed — Mr. Brooks — Alick
& Eugene's — Home at 6.30 — Tea
about 7 — Chatting by a big
fire then billiards until 10.30
to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 13 1872

Dull & overcast — Bkf't after
9 o'clk — Wrote a letter to
father & at about noon Allie,
May, Cap't, Jack & I climbed
away up in the hill — The
day a fine specimen of
the Adber[?] all day. Ret'd
to the house about 3 — Wrote
a letter to Meta Adams.
Dinner at 4.30 — About
5.30 Jack, Cap't & I walked
down to Geo. Diven's — Bid
them good bye — Got a
letter from father — Back
about 7 — The gent & Mrs. D.
Ret'd from Watkins. Even'g
chatting, reading letters
from Maud & Ella and tea
drinking until near 10 —
Then to my room & packed
until 11.45
Steady rain before 10 o'clk.
