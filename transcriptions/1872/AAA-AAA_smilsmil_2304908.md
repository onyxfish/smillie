## Left Page

### August, Thursday, 15, 1872.

Up at 6 o'clk - out with Will and had a swim - wind blowing, water Rough \+ weather still unsettled - Back at 7 \+ packed \+ bilped at 9 - then Telegraphed to the St John for Stateroom - then went rowing with Will in one lake boat loaned by Mr. Van. Something - Rowed over to the ceremony of placing Buoy on "Blood's Reef" - back at 11 - lunch a little before 12 - at 12.25 off on Steamer "Effingham" - the K's all on the dock - Pleasant sail down the lake reach-ing lower end at 1.40 - Stage late - got away soon after 2 - Reached Riverside at 4 - then R.R. - Saratoga at 6.10 - went to Springs \+ through the new Hotel - left card for C.V.V. at Grand Union - back and off at 6.45 - Albany at 8 o'clk - Drove at once to St. John, got State-room. Met Capt. [illegible] \+ wife (May Diveu) an hour or more with them - then went to my Room - outside \+ aft - ventilated it \+ eat peaches - to bed at 11.30 -

## Right Page

### August, Friday, 16, 1872.

Did not wake until 7.45 - at the dock - got up home a little before 9 o'clk. general surprise - Breakfast - then rain - muggy and close. \- headache - abt. gave up \+on sofa until 4 o'clk - "Then up - tea and more peaches. Unpacked traps. Every[illegible] in the bosom of my family - telling my experiences \- got to bed at 11.45
