## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 31, 1872

An atrocious day, dark and stormy, snow, rain and ice.
Got to studio at 9.20 — at work on Cathedral Spire blocks —
About noon Bellee & Mary Trudy called in the midst of a heavy
Rain storm — Prepared lunch at 2 o'clk. & worked until 4
when I walked down to Appleton's, stopping at Arnold's & Harris's & buying
gloves for Josie Beatty —
Found Bunce — Pleased with my drawings but gave
me a regular setting down about my article on Yosemite
written for the Journal — Home at 6.15. Mr & Mrs. McStaary to
tea — Ed and from Po'keep ste.
Junie & Uncle A. with us during
the eveg — To bed at 11 —
