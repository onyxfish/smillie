## Left Page

### February Sunday, 25 1872.

gentle rain, savey m'f, but before noon cleared to a mild Spring day \+ by night was bleak cold \+ mindy. M'f went to Dr Hastings' \+ heard him prea from Matt. 16 ch. 24 v. — must confess that I slept sweetly through the most fit. Home to Dinner. Park in the [illegible] Afternoon, Read until about 4 o'clk, then walked in the Park until 6 — Home to tea eveng. Reading \+ a little writing \+ to bed at a few minutes after 11.

## Right Page

### February Monday, 26 1872.

[illegible] overhead, but a regular march day below. Got to Studio at 9.45 — Kindled fire, set palette and to work on 20×24 (9th day) Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.35 — painted as long as I could see — J. C. Nicoll came in after 5 — Washed up, and home to tea at 6.45 — Willie has been unwell — glad to find that he seems much better. M'f, went to Rungraff's \+ got boots — home \+ Read, Clarence King's" Mountaineering among the High Sierras" until 11 o'clk — To bed abt 11.20
