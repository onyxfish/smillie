## Left Page

### January, Saturday 20, 1872

Mild day, with sunshine and shadow. Got to my work at once after Help, and stuck to be like work until 5 o'clk, hardly stopping to take my lunch. My first day on the "Half Dome from Cloud's Rest". I am sure the subject is good, with elements of grandeur or bigness it will be altogether my fault if something good doesn't come out of it. At 5.30 went to Studio \- brought home frames. Home to tea at 6.30 \- Kate \+ Parker just in from Poughkeepsie. Charl gone to spend Sunday with the Tweedys. Every spent morning Georges drawing \+figuring up my own \- got to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 21, 1872

Overcast mrf, but fine afternoon. In the mrf went to Dr. Hastings \+ heard him preach from text 71st Psalm 3d v, "Be thou my strong habitation, whereunto I may continually resort." \- Dinner at 1 \- napped on the sofa until 4 \- at 4.30 went out and walked until nearly 6 \- then home to tea, just in time to bid Parker goodbye. Every at home \- did some writing and got to bed at 11.
