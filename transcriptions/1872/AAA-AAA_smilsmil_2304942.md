## Left Page

### October, Thursday 24, 1872

Dull Rainy day — went to
Devry's — Tailor — B'dway below
Broome to meet Will — didn't
come — I ordered a black
Coat — went in to Snedecor's
— saw his new Water Colors —
Home at 11 o'ck — worked
over large Tracing of "How me
carried the good news from Ghent
to Aix" — Dinner at 1, Made a
Reduction by Camera-lucida on
the block — so distorted that
I washed it off — Rec'd a note
from Will Kimball offering me
a new seat in Philharmonics —
Wrote a note to Belle Tweedy —
Emma Bourne and tea at 6.30 —
At 8 called on the Kimballs —
all in — Emma + Fred Ireland
called — I stayed until near
10.30 — then home — To bed at
11.30 —

## Right Page

### October, Friday 25, 1872

Miserable stormy day — wind
from the N.E. In bed all day
with a sick headache — Up
at 7.30 P.M. — had some
gruel — Spent this night
in my big chair and on
the sofa — At 5.45 a.m.
went up to my own bed —
