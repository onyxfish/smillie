## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 26 1872

A delightful day — feel somewhat improved — Worked until almost 2 o'clk. on a sketch study in oils of the cream colored horse — Jack & Alec went to Ithaca at noon — Dinner at 2 — after, worked in-doors painting background to morning's study — At 5 o'clk — Saddled "Dandy" and Rode until 6 — Washed dressed — a cup of tea and crackers & at 7 — with Mr. D. Capt. & may Rode to [illegible] D's — Cards until 9 — then coffee, oysters, crabs, fruit & cream — Did it do it — Mr. & Mrs. Covell, Jack Reynold & a Miss Partridge there — Home before 11 & to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 27 1872

Up at 7, feeling very much better — Glorious mth Had to wait until 9 o'clk for my bldp. Then waited for the big black to be groomed, so that it was 10.30 before I got started painting in his head. Worked faithfully until after 1. Then had a lunch at 2. After that worked in-doors on background of head, finishing at 3.30 — Before 4 o'clk — was off on the Grey — Rode nearly to the Reservoir — Met Aleck & turned back — Rode about until 6 o'clk — Washed and dressed — Supper at 7 — Evg, mood fine in Library & whist until 10 o'clk — To bed at 10.45 [illegible]
