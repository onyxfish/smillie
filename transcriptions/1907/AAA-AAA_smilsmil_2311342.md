## Left Page

### Wed. April 10, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another nasty day — overcast with showers or flurries of light snow. Bkfs with Jim before 8 — At 10 went to studio some work there making list of catalogues \+ pamphlets that I have given to N.A.D. Home to lunch at 2 — a nap from 3 to 4 — then wrote on letter to Bessie till near 6 then out \+ walked for 3/4 of an hour — then in to dinner — Evening loafed away with Jim \+ to-bed early —

## Right Page

### Thur. April 11, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day — clear sky \+ dry, invigorating atmos-phere — little trace left of snow. Bkfs before 8 — to studio at 11 — continued, with interruptions, my work of classifying catalogues, reports \+ pamphlets. Home to lunch at 2 — Afternoon printed n.dolio some more of my S.A. photos to send to Bessie. also wrote for some time on a letter for her — Jim did not get home to dinner till 7 o'clk — He is improving in condition — Eve'f. I wrote more on Bessie's letter \+ got to bed early —
