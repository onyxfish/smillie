## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 12, 1907

**Weather:** Nasty day \- Rain or fog all day  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- I stayed in doors all the m't. writing notes & working on accts. At 2 o'clk. this m't. I was waked by ringing of doorbell \- out of bed in answer to it \- night clerk with Telegram \- I was scared \- it was notification from Ham\-Am. line that S.S. Kronprinzessen Cecilia would sail Feb'y 19 \- not on 5th as adv't? \- Then I was mad \- Lunch \- after that I went to Fine Arts Bld'g. 59th St. expecting to see C.Y. Turner's large mural painting for Court House \- Newark \- Saw Turner, but Painting had been taken down unexpectedly \- Then went into N.A.of D. upon & spent some time there \- not greatly edified \- Then home \- did some work hanging pictures that were brought from studio the other day \- Dined alone \- Jim went to Fred Dreland's to go with the D's & Katie Elkins to the Opera \- Eve'g I worked till after 10 hanging pictures.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 13, 1907

**Weather:** Charming m't. \- bright sunshine & pleasant air \- overcast afternoon & sleety rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- had my scrub at 8 \- Bkfst. \- before 10 & out for exercise on Riverside till 12.30 \- Dinner at 2 o'clk \- Then Reading for a time \- then to work hanging pictures \- at 4.30 went out with umbrella & had a short walk. After my return Jessie Smillie came in to borrow an umbrella having been caught in the rain \- A singular accident happened while I was at work with my pictures \- I had set a frame, with copper hanging wire attached, on the floor beside my charging box in which was a 50 candle electric lamp \- I was going to show Jessie how it worked \- as I turned on the current the copper wire slipped down across the two poles of the switch & instantly it made a short circuit \+ with a flash flew out a fuse, the boy bring in the next apartment \- I had to send for the engineer as it put five of my lights out of commission \- Supper at 6.30 \- Every\- fill helped me with picture hanging till 10.30 \- Then to bed.
