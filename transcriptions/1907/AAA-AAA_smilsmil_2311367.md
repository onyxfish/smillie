## Left Page

### Sat. June 1, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A little sunshine — overcast most of the time — raw wind from east. Bkfst. in bed at 8. Up after 9 — at 10 went to 2°. Nat'l Bk. \+ consulted Case, Cash'r about investments — then walked up to Schuffs in 42° S. \+ bought a second St. Frank \+ arranged with him to take back valise bot. of him in '04 \+ never used. Then to Meyrowitz' \+ left new spectacles to have frame of different pattern then home before 1 o'clk. — Lunch at 1.30 — a long nap after that — then a short walk. Wrote to W.G. Parke. — Dinner at 6.30 \+ a dismal evef. loafing about. Jim went to New-Haven this mf. to see the Yale-Princeton ball game — to be back sometime to-morrow. This is the game for which I had made arrangements to attend.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 2, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dismal day — cold \+ wet all day long. I got up before 9 \+ had my bkfst. in the dining room — I did not go out of the house but paced my cage like a captive bear. I wrote a letter to Prof. Marquand. — Jim came in from New-Haven just before 11 P.m.
