## Left Page

### Mon. May 6, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dark, rainy day - early bkfs' - to studio soon after 10 - too dark to see to work - I spent some time in fixing a mount on a print for Ralph \+ finishing the photo-mounts of Sat - then I worked for an hour or two on Father's etched portrait - home \+ lunch soon after 2. Heavy showers prevented me from going to Wanamaker's in the afternoon to make some purchases for the Ark. Jil. Kinny, Jim's friends, came \+ took a pot-luck dinner with us \+ spent the evef.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 7, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

26th Anniversary of my Wedding -

Dismal day - overcast - raw east wind - Jim up at 6 \+ off to Creedmoor - to shoot with his [illegible] Co. I had bkfs' - before 8 - to studio before 10 - finished mounting the Dresden print that I shall give to Ralph - then worked on portrait of Father till 1.30 - then lunch in studio - before 3 o'clk. I went to Wanamaker's \+ was there till after 5 shopping for things to be sent to the Ark at St. Hubert's to make up deficiencies in various departments. I was after 6 before I got home - waited dinner for Jim till 7 - as he had not returned. at that time I had my dinner alone. - at 8 o'clk. he called me on' phone to say that he was dining at a Restaurant down town - Charles came in at 8 \+ spent the evef with me till 10 -
