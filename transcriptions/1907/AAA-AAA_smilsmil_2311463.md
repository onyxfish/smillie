## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 12, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another day of hope deferred — It was practically absent in bed, altho' for a time I was wrapped in blankets \+ in my big chair while bed \+ bed room were changed \+ aired. Kate \+ Katie are both very assiduous in their attentions \+ care of me,

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 13, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull \+ threatening some of the time — we are having very singular \+ much bad weather — I got up \+ dressed but the dragging pain in stomach \+ the discomfort in region of gall bladder \+ kidney was continuous — It was a dismal day for me I could walk about my room for short spells only \+ at intervals of about four hours fed lightly on broths or gruels — It is very depressing —
