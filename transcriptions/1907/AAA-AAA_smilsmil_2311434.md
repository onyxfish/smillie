## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 15, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day - up at 7 - bkfs. at 8 - worked over prints from Ralph's negatives sent to me from Cullen's - mailed them to R. - wrote him a letter - got to studio at 10.30. Jenning's there + he worked till after 12 getting my cor. in cellar put to rights + getting large trunk from studio down into it - It was 1.30 when I got home to lunch - while at lunch an employe of the Mc. German Lloyd Steamship Co. called saying that a written complaint had been sent to the Co. concerning the food served on the Kronigprinsse on her last trip + that had been sent out to get testimony from the passengers - I told him that I had not complained + did not wish to - I worked aethe p.m. over negatives of my last trip photos. sent me by Cullen trying to arrange them - The man who developed them pretended to keep the spoils separate - he sent them by dozens in envelopes, but he got them badly mixed + I find it difficult always to recognise, in the negative, the place represented - At 5 o'clk - I went out + walked on Riverside for an hour - lovely twilight - Got a letter from Scott Brown telling me that I had better not take the trip to St. Huberts as the weather is very unsettled - says Mr. Brown + Emma O'is will put the [illegible] in order. Every Jim went to theatre with Gil. Kinney - I wrote + read -

Jim went to Western Electric Co. + arranged to return to work there to-morrow.

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 16, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day - up before 7 + bkfs. before 8 - Jim went to his work this m'g with the Western Electric Co. I went to studio + podged about there till 1 o'clk. + accomplished a start if no more - the confusion + dirt are discouraging - lunch at 2 o'clk - worked till near 5 arranging photo. negatives of last summers trips - then out + walked up Riverside Drive to 110th St. then back thro' the Park - lovely hazy sunset + twilight - in at 6 - dinner at 6.30 - well loafed - Charl called me up in 'phone + arranged that we shall go to Mauch Chunk to-morrow to see the working of the electric mule that is working there on a two mile section of the Lehigh Canal at that place - Rec'd a pleasant note from Prof. Marquand, dated Sep. 13 - sent to Europe + from there chased me home
