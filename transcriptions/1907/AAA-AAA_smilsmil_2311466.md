## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 18, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unpleasant weather I judge from what I am able to see + hear. I kept my bed for sometime in the m'g altho' the fever had passed — but my head was very uncomfortable — Ralph visited by my bed side in the m't + we had confabs in the afternoon. I sat at table with Jim + him at 6.30 + tantalized myself with a little light food. Eve'g Jim went with Edna + Janice Cook to a Hockey Team Match — St. Paul's + Pomfret — Ralph stayed with me.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 19, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cold, raw day, a few gleams of sunshine. My principal business during the day was nursing half an ounce of Castor oil that I took at about 7 a.m. I had my bldp. in bed — got up at about 10 + loafed uneasily about during the rest of the day regretting the Castor oil incident. Some visiting with Ralph during the afternoon — a light meal at 6.30 with the boys — Eve'g Jim went to dine + at 8 Ralph followed after him — Mary Mooney came in + waited at table at dinner time. At 8.30 She + Kate went down to the Bradley home — I have not seen Katie since Tues. Old Kate was out all last night — Evening, alone.
