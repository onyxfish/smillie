## Left Page

### Sun. May 12, 1907

went to Bronxville

**Weather:** Bright cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright cold day — Bkfo. about 9 o'clk. went to Grand Central \+ took 11.36 train. much interested in the new electric cars \+ the work going on in the excavations for new yard \+ station — Got to Bronxville soon after 12 — Geo. waiting for me at station — Marie Jacobs Swift also on train — we walked up to G's — house \+ soon after had luncheon — After a time Geo, Val, Gordon \+ I started out upon a tramp which proved to be a long one. We went over a large part of a property adjoining Lawrence Park (where Geo. lives) \+ the boys explained in part a scheme that they are working upon to acquire the property, about 60 acres, lay it out, after the manner of Lawrence \+ many other parks, thereby securing for themselves choice lots \+ great gain Lawrence bought his park property years ago for $3000 or $4000 per acre — Now the remaining lots are selling at the rate of $17,000 — The boys are figuring upon such profits immediately — we had a long tramp going quite around the settlement of Bronxville — Dinner at 7. after that Geo. \+ the boys went with me to station where I took 8.18 train back to the City — I got home before 10 — Jim did not return from Yonkers.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 13, 1907

**Weather:** Bright \+ warmer  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ warmer — Bkfs. before 8 \+ to studio about 10.30 after stopping at Weyrowtz's and ordering spectacles that are to cost $12.00. Then to 3d. Ave. \+ bought screws to mend my camera — then to studio \+ spent some time getting my printing corner in order for printing — Chas. Huntington came, bringing a much damaged ph. of Goldsmith's Daughter for treatment — this took much time — at 2 I had lunch in studio — after \+ I went to Alexander's — 6th Ave. 49th St. \+ ordered shoes — then to Brooks Bro's \+ ordered suit of clothes — then to studio — then, having developed a headache, instead of going to Ireland Real-Estate meeting to perfect sale of Cleveland dock I went home \+ lay on my lounge till dinner — My presence at the meeting was not necessary as my proxy was held by Carleton Cook. Jim came in to dinner. My headache hung on making me useless during the evelf.
