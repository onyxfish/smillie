## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 26, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain \+ snow in the m't. very dark \+ disagreeable \- To studio soon after 10 \- to dark to see to paint so I sketched in charcoal plan for Vesuvius. Sep't. 1904. Herbert M. Smitt called \+ we talked of the Ms. problem that we are now up against because of the auto-garage that has been established next to the studio building \- my rate has been raised from $26 to $68 \- after 12 I went to the office of Com't. of Combustibles etc. \+ made complaint of the garage \+ asking that an examination of the premises be made as I did not think that all requirements of the law were being observed. I was treated civilly \+ the chief gave directions to a subordinate to make the examination. Then I walked across the park \+ up home \+ had lunch about 2 o'clk. \- Busied my self in various ways till 6.30 \- then to "Charles". Dinner at. [illegible] then we went to Mendelsohn Hall. Meeting of the Geographical Soc'y. address by.

Jim dined with the Dielman's then went to Club dance with Miss D.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 27, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph came from N.H.

Bright m't. weather growing dubious later. To studio soon after 10 \- Painted until after 1 o'clk on a sketch. "Down into the Valley", painted Oct. 1895. It was crude \+ had some promise or suggestion in it but after three hours work I managed to eliminate pretty nearly all of that the result being commonplace. \- Home to lunch at 2 o'clk \- then lunch \- much of what remained of the afternoon was spent in clearing up preparatory to a visit from Charles in the eve't. to see my photo. work of last summer \- Ralph came in soon after 4 o'clk. from New-Haven to spend Thanksgiving Day. Jim did not come home to dinner, overwork at office \- Ralph \+ I had our dinner at 6.30 Jim came in after 8. \+ Chat. came. soon after that \- The eve't was spent till almost 11 o'clk. looking over photos. \+ only about 2/3's of them had been seen.
