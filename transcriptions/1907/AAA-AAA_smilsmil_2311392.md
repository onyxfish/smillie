## Left Page

### Sun. July 21, 1907

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast until noon - after which the sun shone out. Ther. indoors did not get above 62°. Pleasant out of doors in the sunshine. - Bkfs- after 9. after 10 when we went out without specific purpose - we went into a number of churches besides the Cathedrale + heard more or less service. we went to S. Ursula, but of course could not go about, we went to the Rathaus + were more successful - had a guide thro' the bldg. Then to S. Martins where we were in time to hear some congregational singing that was impressive to me. I did not dispose of my bkfs. satisfactorily, so I told the boys to get their dinner while I shopped for photos. Afternoon I went alone with camera + took a number of street snap shots. At noon there was a military band concert in the open air - space in front of hotel - I was so much disturbed last night by noises under my window that I had my room changed to 4th floor. The boys ret? at 5. + we all had a rest before our "Supper" at 7 - At 6 I slipped out + into the Cathedrale. The tones of the big 27 ton bell that rang for about 15 mins. in the Cathedrale were exceedingly fine - after it ceased tolling there was organ + singing by a small congregation that was gathered up near the choir - from my position, near the entrance, the effect was impressive - We'd out again with the boys but I soon ret'd + went to bed early.

## Right Page

### Mon. July 22, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Misty - not quite clear - Had our bkfs. at about 9 + after a short time went over to Cathedrale + climbed to the gallery outside of the choir + spent some time there then to the top of the S. W. tower under the spire. It was less of an exertion, altho' a hard pull, than I expected. The distant view was cut off by the mist but the city at our feet was very interesting - we were not at all hurried + took plenty of time - saw the big 27 ton bell + learned that it is rung only seven times in the year - The bell that I have been listening to supposing it to be the big one is one of the five smaller ones that hang in the same tower. When we went down we revisited the cathedral + went the rounds of choir, chapels + treasury - It was after 12 when we ret'd to hotel - Lunch at 1 - after that trunks were packed + we loafed till 3 o'clk. when we got away in the bus. on the whole, the hotel is satisfactory - not in the same class with a first class London hotel - we had no trouble in getting a carriage to ourselves and keeping it - the ride to Coblenz was not interesting as seen from the train - a point that did interest me was the Drachenfels - a point that father + I climbed 44 yrs. ago - The hotel we are now in is next to the one we then stopped at. - We got to Coblenz at 5.45 - at hotel there good rooms had been assigned us - we had dinner at 7. Then out + walked to the point at junction of Rhine + Moselle where the great Wilhelm Denkmal is erected - an immense structure, clumsy + grotesque to my eye. \+ then left the boys + returned to hotel + to bed early.
