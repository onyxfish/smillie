## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 6, 1907

**Weather:** Rain all day — dark + disagreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain all day — dark + disagreeable — I did not go out of the house but fussed all day with my photos. I am disgusted with myself — I am making no progress with serious that I should be doing — a spell seems to be on me \+ I can dawdle over unimportant things.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 7, 1907

**Weather:** Clouded, blustering day  **Temp:** Bkfst. before 8

After that attended to several small matters up town — took lace curtains to Rees\+Rees to be cleaned — etc — then to second Nat'l bank — then to Brooks Bros \+ tried on suit of clothes being made to order — then to Tiffany's \+ got watch — then to studio — Home to lunch at 1.30 — After that went down town to W. C. Cullen's with trimmed prints of last summer's work to be mounted — then to Budler \+ Warbels — then home at 4.45 — a rest — At 6.30 at Charl's — dined with him \+ then to Carnegie Hall to the concert of the Boston Symphony Orchestra — There was an hour long Symphony by Bruckner which was entirely beyond my comprehension — then a Bach suite with flute obligato given by Maquarre — then overture to Leonore, No 1. Altogether, I must confess that I got but little pleasure from the performance — Walked from 57th St. to home.
