## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 11, 1907

**Weather:** Bright m't + cold. 28°  **Temp:** [illegible]

after 3 o'clk. a number of snow flurries with blizzardy wind - very disagreeable - After 10 went to studio + till after 1 o'clk. at work cleaning up printing corner which was in desperate need of it as no attempt has been made to clean it since last I used it nearly a year ago. After 1 o'clk I prepared lunch + at 2.30 went down town - Took my camera to Cullen's + ordered 25 Rolls of films - then to Colombian Consul 78 Broad St. to have my Passport viséd - was told that it w'd cost $7.- - then I went to Costa Rican Consul 66 Beaver St. and was told that no visé was necessary - then to Ham. Am. line - then home by subway. got in at 4.30 - had a rest - Dinner alone at 7 - Jim came in before 8 - Ev'g. I wrote to Miss Anna Nicofely sending check for $36.46 + to F't. Tuttle + to Herbert Smith + to Ralph whose new overcoat has been stolen from his bed room while he slept. by a sneak thief.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 12, 1907

**Weather:** A clear, better cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ther. got to within 1° of 0 in the early m't. High N.W. wind all day. I got to studio after 11 + worked till 4 over printing table + press - an astonishing amount of dirt has sifted in + settled over everything + the press has rusted surpris-ingly since I last printed, almost a year ago. I prepared my lunch at 2 o'clk + got home at about 5 + had a rest before dinner at 6.30 - Jim went to Yonkers this m't + spent the day there - as it is Lincoln's birth-day he has a holiday - he got home before 7 - dressed hurriedly + went to Dielman's to dinner - I spent the ev'g loafing, as I feel tired, + went to bed early.
