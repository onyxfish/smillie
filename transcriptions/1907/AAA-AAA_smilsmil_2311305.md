## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 26, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another fall of snow — a dull day \+ clearing colder in the afternoon. I got up late after an unsuccessful night from trouble in head \+ throat — an influenza like condition. I thought it wiser to remain indoors \+ did not go out at all during the day. — had bkfs. after 9.30 \+ did little else than loaf about, read \+ try to get back some of the sleep that I lost last night. Miss Murphy came just before 3 o'clks. \+ we had our last session in making notes from father's autobiography — Dinner at 6.30. Well, I regretted much that I could not go to the University Glee Club Concert to which Chas. had given Jim \+ me tickets. Jim would not go without me — so both tickets were not used.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 27, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The 12th anniversary of Duine's death.

overcast — some struggling sunshine about the middle of the day — more snow at night — very light fall. The experience of yesterday was repeated — very troubled sleep after 3 a.m. much discomfort in the head \+ extending down into the throat — up late \+ bkfs. at about 10. When the sun shone came at about 12 I went out \+ walked up Broadway to 101st St \+ back — 2 miles. Pleasant overhead but sloppy \+ disagreeable underfoot — Dinner at 2. Finished letter to Ernest Roth that has been lying on my table since New Year. Charl came in at about 4 \+ stayed till 6 — I felt so uncomfortable that I ate no supper — wrote a considerable part of a letter to Katharine Brooks in the evef.
