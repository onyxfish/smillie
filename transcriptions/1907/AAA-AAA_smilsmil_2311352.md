## Left Page

### Tues. April 30, 1907

**Weather:** overcast - wind easterly - air raw with m[illegible]  **Temp:**

gradually clearing to very hazy \+ mild \-
Bkfs\. before 8 \- To studio at 10 \- worked on
Etching \- Father's portrait till 1 oclk \- then
home \- lunch at 2 \- a rest \- then got out
Roth's etchings \+ wrote letter to him till 5
but did not finished \- then walked in
Riverside Park till after 6 \- then in \+
had dinner \- Evef attending to bills
etc \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 1, 1907

Annie's Birthday.

**Weather:** Overcast all day \- a little sunshine at sunset \-  **Temp:**

Bkfs\. before 8 \- at Charl's before
10 \- then to studio \+ under the inspiration
of budding spring as seen in Riverside Park
I set my palette \+ brought out my old 12×16
"Hazlebrook" \+ repainted a considerable
portion of it \- of course, I think it is much
better now than it was \- At 1.30 I went
home tired \+ had lunch at 2 \- then a
rest \+ after 3 I went to studio \- cleaned
palette \- then to Bayard orfer's \+ got my
watch \+ home at 5.30 \- Dinner at 6.30
Eve\. with news paper \- wrote to Dr.
Gerster in reply to a note rec'd. from him
