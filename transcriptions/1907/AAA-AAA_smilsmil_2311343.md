## Left Page

### Fri. April 12, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast day — Bkfp. at 8 o'clk, or before it. Rain came in the afternoon + continued steadily far into the night — After bkfp. I finished my letter to Bessie + posted it with alot of photo prints to her — Then I went to studio for the rest of the m.f. accomplishing nothing of any importance — home + lunch at 1.30 — Afternoon wrote letter to Ralph. Dinner with Jim before 7 — Ev'f. I rec'd. an interesting letter from Katharine T. at Palermo — At 8 o'clk. went to Charles + together we roughly laid out a plan for my Trip next summer — Home + to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### Sat. April 13, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

weather still disagreeable — light rain \+ wet — now + then a smart shower — Bkfp. at 8 — To studio soon after 10 + spent rest of m.f. marking a great lot of N.A. of D. catalogues & pamphlets with Rubber stamps — J.D. Smillie collection — preparing to give them to N.A. of D. library — Home to lunch — at 2 — Part of afternoon given to a study of inventory of ark. + making a list of things to be sent up there — Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'f. Jim went to Regimental dress parade + I got tar paper + tied up some pkgs. of woolen blankets + heavy coats.
