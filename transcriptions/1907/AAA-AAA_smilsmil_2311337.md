## Left Page

### Sun. March 31, 1907

**Weather:** Fairly pleasant m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fairly pleasant m'f. — some warm sunlight — but that changed to rawness, dampness \+ finally rain in the eve'g. Up at about 8 \+ had a scrub — b'f'st — after 9 — newspaper — Ralph \+ I went out for a walk at noon — Riverside Park \+ Drive \+ back to dinner at 2. Jim got up in time for dinner — After that read for an hour or so \+ then rested — Charl came in at about 5 \+ we talked about plans for European Travel till 6 when he left — Tea at 6.30 — The boys were in during the eve'g — we loafed \+ read.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 1, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An inch or more of snow fell last night and early this m'g., Raw \+ disagreeable all day — B'f'st — before 8 — to Studio soon after 10 — spent the m't trimming Costa Rican photos. for mounting \+ arranging N a p'd. Catalogues for the binder — At 12.30 I went to 2°. Nat'l. Bank \+ home to lunch at 1.30 — an attack of indigestion came on in the afternoon \+ lasted thro' the afternoon making me very uncomfortable — Dinner with the boys at 6.30 — (Even) — R. went to theatre with some of his friends — I rec'd. from Geo. Cook — check stubs, journal \+ ledger — he has written up my accts that I have allowed to run behind — I spent some time over his work.
