## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 23, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day — at times it clouded over + looked like rain but later all was clear again — I got up after a fairly comfortable night + had my b'f't before 9 o'clk — Geo. came in + spent some time with me — I prepared a number of photos. bot. abroad in 1904, for mounting — had a short walk before a very light lunch — After that I went down town + at Wall St. found great excitement — the street filled with crowds + policemen afoot + mounted — a financial panic is on — I went up to Chas'd office + saw him — then to Cullens + left my 1904 photos. to be mounted + some more negatives to be printed from. Then to Findler + Webel's — [illegible] Nassau St. — for 1908 Diary — did not have the No. I wished — Then home soon after 4. Had a rest — dinner at 6.30 — Relf just loafing

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 24, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day — B'kf't. at 8.30 — after a fairly restful night — I wrote a note to Mrs. Alena Marquand thanking her for her note of a couple of days ago. I feel so much improved in condition that I took quite a long walk up West-End + then over to Riverside + home thro' the Park — I am now encouraged to believe that the doctor was right when he said that my trouble might pass away in time — Lunch at 1 — Then I went to Schuffs in 42d. St. + arranged with him to send up the two light-weight steamer Trunk. protectors — There to Tiffany's in answer to notification that repairs on my watch w. cost $15 — Was told that many things inside of it needed repairs so I said, go ahead — Then to Studio — Chas. Collins overtook me, went in with me + we had a pleasant chat — I saw Geo — home before 5 — had a rest before dinner — Evel\[illegible\]. Jim went to dine — I read news paper + magazine.
