## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 13, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very bright, pleasant day — got up early — bkfst at 8 — worried for awhile trying to get a balance of my bank acct. in harmony with the bank's figures — I cannot find just where the discrepancy lies. After 10 I went out \+ took a long stroll up Riverside, then over to Columbia University taking notes of improvements — then home to dinner at 2 — then a nap — later Ernest Rottis' sister called — I had written her asking for E's address, as I could not find him when I was in Florence — from what she told me I fear that the young man is starving himself — she promised to come again bringing some of his home letters that I may judge from them \+ so be in better position to advise him — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g I read some ancient history.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 14, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day — Bkfst at 8 — I had a letter from Carr Moody with bill — he writes that he has been seriously sick or unable to work — I wrote \+ sent him a check. Again I worried over bank acct — I cannot locate a difference of $2.40. I had an exercise walk after 12 on Riverside — then lunch at 1 — After that I went to Brookes Bros. \+ tried on light overcoat that is being made for me — then to studio — trouble there caused by tenant adjoining in Sniffen Court — it is now occupied as a garage for automobiles — one of them, some time last night was run into my cellar door smashing the glass \+ bending iron work — I got name \+ address of Party leasing the place — Tillinghast, B'dway near 39th St. — I went there — saw him \+ made my complaint — he was regretful \+ apologetic and promised all sorts of good behavior. Then home. Dinner at 6.30 — Cullan sent home all my films developed — also Ralph's films — prints came with R's — I spent the eve'g until 11 o'clk looking them over — among my 35 dog are many failures \+ some very fine ones — I shall now select the best \+ have prints from them —
