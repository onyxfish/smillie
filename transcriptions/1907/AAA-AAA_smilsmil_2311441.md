## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 29, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull m't with some rain — This m't at about 1 o'clk — there was a severe thunder storm — the weather cleared at about noon After a m't spent working over negatives of our 1904 European Trips I selected 100 of the best of them from which I shall have Cullen take prints \+ then I shall complete the Albums commenced for the boys in the fall of that year \+ get up others of this years trips. At noon I went out — walked up Riverside Drive To 108th S't then back thro' the park — a good 2½ miles, without fatigue — After lunch I went down town with negatives to Cullen \+ got there a great lot of prints from this years work — then to Findler \+ Weibels about a Diary — then to studio — made a list of various things I wanted at home — then home at 5 — lost no time in getting at the new prints — the general effect being gratifying — Dinner at 6.30 itself — I trimmed a lot of the prints — about 10 o'clk when preparing for bed Jim had a 'phone call from Harry Gorell asking if he might share his bed to-night — Jim could not well refuse \+ G. came along at about 11 —

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 30, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day, bright \+ pleasantly cool — Up at 7 \+ had bkft before 8 — got to studio at 9.30 — the woman Jennings engaged was there \+ had made a good start at house cleaning — I set to work with her \+ had a busy dirty time — At 12 o'clk. I went out for air \+ a change — went to Looke's — neither S't or Jnr knew anything of the old lace curtains I am looking for — then I went to Macy's about mending a piece of cut glass — was surprised to find an expensive restaurant there — I had a satisfactory lunch — then back to studio — Jennings came \+ worked for some time cleaning windows — I left at about 3.45 — home at 5 — until dinner time working over prints from last summer's negatives \+ at it again after dinner till 10 o'clk — Rec'd a letter from Bessie T. \+ one from Ernest Ratt. —
