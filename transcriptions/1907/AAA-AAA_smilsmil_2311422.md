## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 19, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day — but not a good one for the ascent of Vesuvius — there was a veil of mist over sea + distance + a stiff breeze — so, we congratulated ourselves upon our good luck in having gone up on the day before. The boys prowled about but were greatly restricted in their sight-seeing because to-day is a great fest in honor of St. Gerolimo, whose blood liquifies, as I was informed, to-day in a church here. I did not leave the hotel being content on the Promenade deck.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 20, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

S.S. Königin Luise — Stateroom 303.

Fine day, rather warm + rather misty but a pleasant air was constantly stirring + moderating the heat. Up early — Ship. after 8 — It was near 10 when we took a cab + rode down to Cooks where I drew $20. Part in Am. money. Then for some time hunting, unsuccessfully, for good photos of Vesuvius in eruption — then to the N. German Lloyd's office + from there back to hotel — lunch at 1, settled bill, paid fees + left at about 2.15 for the steamer — got to dock before 3 + waited on the uncomfortable little tender which served as bridge for another boat over which a crowd of ill savored emigrant passed for an hour or more — at last we were taken aboard + our luggage was got into our room which is very much smaller + less convenient than our room on the Pres. Lincoln — it gives me a feeling of homesickness for that noble boat. A fleet of lemon laden [illegible] + freight barges lay about the ship delivering their loads which was done smoothly + silently so far as the machinery was concerned — but the human contingent was loudly in evidence — as they struggled + crowded for place to deliver their loads their demonstrations were comically violent — they danced up + down, gesticulating wildly, grimacing \+ pouring out torrents of [illegible] musical invective — it was constant amusement to watch + listen to them — no blood being shed — boys were in the water diving for money thrown to them + musicians in boats, played, sang + danced — Funiculare, Santa Lucia, + Adio bella Napoli doing duty constantly — Two women in one boat sang a spirited duet with a kick-dance Refrain — Sunset, twilight, moonlight, very fine — dinner at 7. The work of loading went on from daft + on back + the wait on without returning from this near 10 o'clk — the hydraulic cranes working assiduously + noiselessly — 3 000 tons were taken aboard we were told — about 10.30 we left the harbor + then I went to bed.
