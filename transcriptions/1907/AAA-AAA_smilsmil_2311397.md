## Left Page

### Wed. July 31, 1907

**Weather:** almost cold + not yet quite agreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather almost cold + not yet quite agreeable two or three very light sprinklings - I was up early + finished my letter to Geo. - had bkfo. alone - after that, an hour with a late London paper - after 10 we all went down town on a shopping tour - before 12. Chas. + I separated from the rest + walked to the R.R. station, he hoping to meet Miss Howell of Charleston + her friend - but she came not - we got back to hotel a little after 1 + had dinner - Charl rec'd a note from Miss H. - they had slipped us by going out a back way - After dinner he went down for them - Afternoon the boys + I went to the Castle + prowled all about it - Supper after 7 - Charles entertained Miss H \+ friend - we sat 9 at table - by ourselves - + had a merry time - later in the eve + Katie Elkin \+ William arr'd from Kissungen - altogether we had a busy time -

Miss Howell + Miss Tessier -

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 1, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A rather disagreeable day - Opened bright but cold in the early m'g. - then suddenly clouded over - air quite raw - Ther. about 50° - The day gradually grew warmer + at times there was considerable sunshine - Ther. 66 - Strong wind, but we got about comfortably + I did considerable photographing - Up before 7 - bkfo. at 8 - It was after 10 before Kathanie \+ I got away with our cameras to the Castle where we had sufficient sunlight to get a number of snaps. met there Miss Mc. Murty + Miss Beard, Boston ladies at our hotel, introduced by Miss Howell with whom they crossed the ocean - Later, Kathanie ret'd to the hotel + Charl + I went into the town - Back to dinner at 1 - we met the boys in town - Chas. went for Miss H. + friend - the boys + I ret'd by funicular - brought miss H. + friend to dinner - About 3 - Ch. -- his friends + I went to Castle + loitered about there till nearly 5 - then they went to Concert on terrace \+ J's ret'd to hotel + had a rest before supper at 7.30 - our family + the Elkins had possession of the large smoking room till 10.30 - a pleasant chatty eve't - Jim + I amused ourselves for a time at the billiard table - I found that the little I ever knew of the game had left me.
