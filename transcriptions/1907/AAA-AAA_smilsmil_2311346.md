## Left Page

### Thur. April 18, 1907

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- bkfs'- early \- after that walked across the Park to Lenox Library \- 5th Ave. \+ 70th S'. \+ spent some time in Print Room comparing list of fathers pfs. with my Haushelwood list to check off duplicates. It was after 11 when I got to studio \+ then finished the cat. of the pfs. I have selected for the N.Y. Public Library Print Room. Home to lunch at 2 — At 4 went down town to Cullen's taking the unused Rolls of films brought back from my Southern trip. Then to Charl's \- I waited for him \+ we came up town together. I got home at 6.30 — dinner \- But if I was engaged for some time making selections from my last trip photos. for lantern slides.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 19, 1907

**Weather:** Very disagreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very disagreeable \- Snow flying till late in the afternoon, melting as it fell \- clear \+ cold at night. Bkfs. before 8 \- To studio soon after 10 \- Spent my time completing arrangement \+ Cataloguing of Haushelwood prints, duplicates, for the Print Dept. of the Boston Museum of Art \- Weitenkampf of Pub. Library Print Room came \+ we had considerable talk about how I shall have my collection of pfs. cared for in the Library \- I am promised that they shall be mounted \+ arranged according to my ideas \- Home to lunch at 2 \- Afternoon wrote letter to Ralph \- At 6.30 went to Charl's to dinner \- Found him suffering from an indigestion \- My itinerary for next Summer was considered at some points \- left at 9.30 \- Home \+ to bed.
