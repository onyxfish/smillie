## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 7, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A charming day — sunny + milds in spite of snappish breezes that sometimes blew chilly thro' shady streets — I spent a good part of the m'f. in writing + getting my accts. in order — then to the bank + from there to Rogers, Peet + Co — bought a hat — then home + had lunch with Jim at 1 o'clk — Today the sunshine seems to have thawed out in great measure the discomfort I have suffered since landing. It has relieved the depression I have felt. A nap in the afternoon again. Dinner at 6.30 — an attempt at a "course dinner", as I have told Kate I w'd. like her to try it — Eve'g. devoted to arranging my 36 doz. film exposures preparatory to taking them to Cullen for development.
By the way — there was a smart show'r at noon which I was caught out in.

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 8, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up at 5 o'clk. mopping up water driven in at open windows — floods of rain at 9 \+ high wind that did much damage. In the afternoon it cleared splendidly + cold, wind still high. I spent the m'f. completing arrangement of exposed films — also preparing list of photos. brought home for mounting. After 12 Jim + I went out + had a breezy walk before lunch — Afternoon, more time wasted in sleeping — another breezy walk in Riverside before dinner — Eve'g. Jim went to his Club + I busied myself with photos.
