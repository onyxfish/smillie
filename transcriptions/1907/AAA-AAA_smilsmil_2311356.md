## Left Page

### Fri. May 10, 1907

**Weather:** dull m.f. but soon cleared bright & warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

Soon after 2 o'clk. there was a thunderstorm followed by stray showers & colder weather. I got to studio at 9.30 - got out a lot of pls. to show to Mr. Kost who has awakened to an interest in Etching - He was to call at 10 but it was nearly 11.30 before he put in an appearance - he had been waiting for more than an hour in Ryan's, at intervals ringing George's bell - I did not hear it Tf - not in. The man had got us mixed & thought it was Q - Mr. whom he had made engagement - He stayed till about 12.30 - I showed him lots of things - I put in about a couple of hours on Father's portrait - Home to lunch at 2 - Afternoon so gloomy on acct. of storms that I did little but read & sleep. Dinner at 6.30 - Jim went to see Floyd Holsapple \+ I went to Charli's - Found him at his orchestrelle - He was playing Beethoven's 9th Symphony - parts of it brought to mind so vividly days forever past that I could not restrain my tears - I had a vision of a group of merry young people, Annie, Emma TJ - Fanny & Will Kimball & others, in their Phil. seats, Thomas leading - It was overwhelming the way that familiar music brought the whole picture before me - We talked over some points of my itinerary for next summer \+ I left it with him for advision - I got home about 11 \& to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. May 11, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim went to Yonkers.

Rain during the night & early m.f. - clearing cold - Bkfs. before 8 - To studio before 10 - worked on Father's portrait all the m.f. Home to lunch at 2 - Afternoon wrote to Mr. Scott Brown instructions concerning putting the Atk in order for Prof. Marquand. To Scott Brown about work on Water Pipe that I want laid to connect Reserve Water directly with my pipe - Dinner at 6.30 alone - Jim has gone to Yonkers to spend the Sunday with Gould - About 8 o'clk. Charl came in bringing with him an amended itinerary for my summer trip to Europe with notes as to hotels etc - To bed at 10.30
