## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 6, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull mf, some sunshine in the afternoon. Mr. Jas. E. Tolfree, 26 E, 49 St. called me up on phone at 10 o'clk — he is owner of stable next to my studio that has been rented for garage. He is evidently in a state of mind" over the row that has been raised — says he has consulted lawyer — that lease runs till next May + that he doubts Telling hast — I gave him address of C. B. Smith, advising his lawyer to confer with him. I went to bank — telephoned to Clarence of my talk with Tolfree — then to studio + worked for awhile on "Down into the Valley". Scare in garage — an auto caught fire — put out promptly — but I went home and at once wrote to Bureau of Combustibles telling what had occurred + protesting against giving a license to the concern — lunch at 2 — Then I wrote to Mr. Tolfree + phoned the story to Clarence — Then I had a nap + made up. Some of the sleep lost last night. Dinner with Jim at 6.30 — He went to Yale Club — I worked on one of the albums that I am preparing for the boys, arranging the photos made last summer so that Callie may know how + where to mount them.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 7, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim went to Yonkers.

Pleasant overhead — Bkfs — early — The peculiar pain in my hand + right arm so troublesome that after Bkfs. I went to Dr. Tuttle's + saw him — I have some fear of being disabled by neuritis or something of that sort — The D. could not be very comforting + advised Iodine + Salol — Then to studio — Got "Passing Showers" out of its frame + cleaned preparatory to work on it — Then to bank — + then home to lunch at 2 — Got a note from Bureau of Combustibles saying that "Spam" of garage in Sniffen Court was progressing. Afternoon I dressed + went down to Reception at Walter Coals — old Walter + young Walter + their wives — a crowd + jam — saw a lot of people that I knew — a few words with each — then escaped — Jim brought young [illegible] home with him — they went off together in the even — Jim went to Yonkers with him to spend Sunday — Eveg — I went to Century — monthly meeting — Mr. Bigelow — 90 years old presided — no business — the Sec'y — Prof Sloane of Columbia was called upon to tell what he had learned during his summer of study + investigation in the Balkan Provinces — his address was interesting but was not conclusive — he said he went, an interrogation mark — he came back with the mark greatly increased in magnitude but yet unanswered — Home at 11.
