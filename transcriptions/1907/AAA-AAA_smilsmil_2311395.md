## Left Page

### Sat. July 27, 1907

*Charles arrived -*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant mild day - after 9 o'clk the family got together + had bhfp: on the terrace - it was chilly for me + smutty as well - we are much bothered by smut that falls about as freely as in London - a surprise to me - After some time we all wandered down to the Castle + prowled about the ruins but did not enter Museum or reserved part - back to hotel + had a short rest before dinner at 1 o'clk. After dinner I did a good deal of loafing on my lounge as I had a troublesome headache - I wrote to Kate Leonard. As 6 o'clk. approached we made preparations to meet Charl. There was some misunderstanding - M. + the girls started down on foot to get to the town + then by train reach the R.R. station but they sel'd. for some reason - The boys had been gone all the afternoon - After 6 o'clk. the Porter told us that he had a telephone message from the station saying that a gentleman had arrived + was on his way up in the bus accompanied by two young gentlemen who had met him there - Then M. + the girls + I walked down the road + met the bus with C. + the boys - they got out + we all walked back to hotel - After [illegible] we had our supper together + later all retired to C's room + there sat upon an open balcony till 10 o'clk - The twilight, falling night, + then the electric lights in the town + beyond were exceedingly beautiful - I hope that the memory of them will never fade from my mind - To bed soon after 10 -

## Right Page

### Sun. July 28, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A rainy day - showers at intervals - one thunderclap in the afternoon - I got up at 7 - wrote part of a letter to Dr. Tuttle - then out + had a short walk - then bkfst. alone - The rest of the family appeared at will, Chas. being the last one - he came to life at about noon - we had dinner at 1 - we loafed about - Chas. + the boys spent most of the afternoon in town - I did some writing + some sleeping - finished my letter to Dr. T. + mailed it - Family gathering + chat in the boys room before supper at 7:5 — Well more loafing about till 10 - Tobed soon after.
