## Left Page

### Fri. March 29, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm day — at noon Ther. got up to 72°. Bkfs. before 8 — but it was nearll before I got to studio — There I found the Ther. at 82° could int stand that — opened windows \+ then went out \+ spent rest of morning visiting picture galleries — Home to lunch at 1 o clk — then down town — To 17 Bdury to see W. Lawson Sandford — at Charls suggestion he was not in — then to French Line office — then to German Lloyd office \+ told Clerk that J. w. Fake Chief Steward's cabin on Königsdrisse from Naples, Sep. 19th — Then to Ham-Am. \+ after waiting a long time saw W. Meyer, the agent \+ had a pleasant interview with him — he assured me that he had every reason to believe that altho' every good room on the new S.S. Pres. Lincoln is engaged, he will be able to secure one for me for June 22 — \+ he gave instructions to one of the clerks to telegraph certain parties \+ to keep him posted as to all changes — It was after 4 when I got to studio, shut the windows \+ then, home. I had a nap before dinner with R. — Jim dined with a friend — After R. \+ I went to Charls we discussed the proposed trip without coming to any conclusion — I want to do too much — I must think the matter over carefully \+ be content to undertake little.

## Right Page

### Sat. March 30, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — not so warm as yesterday — About 10 o clk. I went to the Wernicke Co — Bdury below Canal \+ p². a bill of $2° — that was sent to me for locking device on catalogue cabinet — then to Irving place \+ p². studio gas bill \+ arranged to have such bills left with Ryan hereafter — then to studio \+ there the afts. \+ trimming photos bought on my last trip — Home to lunch at 2 \+ at 3 o clk Ralph went out with me to Riverside Park where I took snap shots at Soldiers \+ Sailors mon\- using up the last of the roll that commenced using on my way home from the south — Dinner with boys — they went out later — Jim to the Armory to shoot \+ Ralph to theatre with young Kendrick Banks — I podged about till bed time.
