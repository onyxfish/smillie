## Left Page

### Tues. May 14, 1907

**Weather:** Clear  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear bright day, growing very warm about mid day - nearly 80°. I think - then much cooler in the evef. - Bkfs. before 8 - got to studio about 10 \+ as promptly as possible got to work taking a print from portrait of Father - then I worked on plate - then another print \+ soon through the day. AT 2 o'clk. I prepared my lunch \+ then at my work again till after 4. Then washed up \+ got home at about 5 - had a rest - dinner at 6.30 - Evef. met Charl at 8 o'clk at Mus. of Nat. Hist. \+ together we listened to Peary's repetition of the address that we heard at the Annual Meeting of the Am. Geo. Soct. describing his last attempt to reach the North Pole - It was interesting even when heard for a second time.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 15, 1907

**Weather:** Bright  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright mf. overcast later \+ at 4 o'clk a mizzle commenced that wet the walks \+ made conditions very disagreeable. Bkfs. at usual time - Got to studio at 10.30 - Spent the day working on Father's portrait. Had my lunch in studio at 2 - worked till near 4 - then home - Got a letter from Mary, written at Ragusa - She is a remarkable woman - She is as en-thusiastic a traveler \+ sight see'r as her daughters \+ quite as able - She fills me with envy \+ admiration. Dinner at 6.30 - Evef. Reading.
