## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 1, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dismal day, \+ in. of snow fell last night \- early this m'f. There was sleet \+ that thro' the day was followed by muzling rain \+ gloom \- I had a fairly comfortable night \+ little pain or fever in the m'f. I spent the day indoors \+ varied the passing hours by tramping back \+ forth, by reading, by writing to R.P.P. \- \+ making out checks in paymt. of monthly bills \- Time hung heavy but I kept my philosophy generator running \+ altho' the article produced was rather frazzled it was far better than nothing of the kind \- I had my lunch \- a nap \- wrote to Ralph \+ dinner with Jim at 6.30 Ev't Charlie came in after 8 o'clk \- had left his home to get away from himself \- business cares \+ such weather as we're having don't agree with him \- he left at 10 \- Then Jim \+ I hung a heavy picture \+ after 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 2, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull m'f., sunshine after 1 o'clk \- overcast again before sunset \- Then fog \+ rain before 10 P.M. My improvement continues. I had help \- before 9 \+ the day was spent indoors. Time divided between reading \+ writing of one kind \+ another \+ pacing the floor \- Dinner with Jim at 6.30 \- Then he went out to do some shooting \+ I spent the eve't trying to get some good photo. prints from "Arki" negatives to send to Mr. \+ Mrs. Allan Marquand \.\. The bad weather seems to have got into the sealed pkgs. of paper \- altho' date limits have not yet been reached \- All of the prints were more or less queered, greatly to my vexation.
