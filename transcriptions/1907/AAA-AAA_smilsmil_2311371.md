## Left Page

### Sun. June 9, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

went to Bronxville -

A charming day. Bkf's after 8 - Ralph got up later - about noon we went out \+ strolled about - I had an old film roll in my camera \+ I took a number of experimental shots - we wandered in Riverside Park \+ back at 1.30 \+ soon after had dinner - Before 3 o'clk. we went to Grand Central Station \+ took 3.36 trains for Bronxville - Chave met us on the train - we got to B- at about 4.15 \+ went up to the house now occupied by Geo.- a much pleasanter one than the one in which he has lived during the past winter - we found Geo. looking better than I had expected after his illness during the past fortnight - He wandered out with us \+ over the Hill - etc - to the house 20 m. before 6 - Nellie had tea for us on the piazza \+ at 6 we left for Station - 6.17 train - got in to G.C. at 7.45 - home at 8.40 - tea - a quiet ev'g. \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Mon. June 10, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph left for New-Haven.

Pleasant, about equally divided between cloud \+ sunshine. R. \+ I had bkf's. at 8 o'clk - He left at about 10 to take a m'f train to NewHaven - I wrote a letter to Mr. Percy Ballantine (Bessie Parks) congratulating her upon the birth of her third boy \+ telling her that I had sent a Water Color painting "Vineyards near Ravello, Italy" as a Wedding present. It was almost noon when I went to D.B. Butler's to see about the picture for Bessie Park \+ then to Bayerdorfer's about Ralph's watch - then to Rogers, Peet \+ Co. 32d St. \+ Bdway. \+ bought a hat \+ gloves - then to subway \+ home before 2 o'clk. lunch at 2 - At 3.30 to studio \+ worked there putting things away till near 6 o'clk. then home - dinner alone - Ev'g. writing in mem. book, itinerary \+ notes for our summer trips - Tabed early.
