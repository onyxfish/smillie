## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 18, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very disagreeable weather — milder a raw nizzle filling the air — snow melting freely — In spite of darkness I went to studio getting there soon after 10 \+ worked till 4 o'clk. on portrait of Father — then home — had a rest dinner at 6.30 I wrote to Ralph \+ after that mounted a number of the photo prints that I made last night.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 19, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Despicable weather — dark, foggy, wet \+ Raw — the streets aslush with snow \+ water. It was nearly 11 when I got to studio — worked till 2 o'clk. on portrait of Father — then home \+ had lunch at 3 — loafed till dinner at 6.30 — Eve\. \+ Jim at home \+ I did more loafing over newspapers — Sent check for $30 to J.W. Otis. for filling icehouse at St. Huberts —
