## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 9, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day \- Bkfst at about 8\- After it called Dr Tuttle on phone. Finding that he wd. be in I went to his office taking specimens of my urine \- considerable pus in it \- then to Brooks Bros. Taking fall overcoat delivered to me yesterday \- I do not like it \+ decided to have one made to order \- went to Customs Dept \- selected goods \+ was measured for fall overct \+ for a full suit \- then to 2d. Natl. to got bank book \- then walked up to Tiffany's \+ left my watch there to be repaired \- then to subway \+ home to lunch at 1 \-     I have felt uncomfortably out of sorts all the m'f \+ increased after lunch. George came \+ lunched with me, immediately after he left I lay down \+ slept \-   on my way up 5 Ave. to Tiffany's I stopped at Walter Cook's office \+ had a pleasant half hour call with him \- After my nap I felt but little improved. Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g occupied with Bank acct \- puzzled over what seems to be a mistake of £5. chg'd in letter of Co.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 10, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- overslept \- near 9 when I had bkfst. after working for sometime over my summer accts \- having got from 2d. Natl. the citrous from letter of Co \- amt. drawn \- I found that I was chd. Aug. 9th by the Scheuber bureau. munich, £75 \- I have a mem. statement given me by Albert Sidlit, manager, showing that I got but £70 \- I went to 2d Natl. Mr. Case said that he had sent the letter to the City Bank that issued it \+ that there could be no doubt that the charge of £75 was clear enough up on the letter \- so it looks as though I had signed a draft for 75 \+ taken 70 \- Mr. C. took the mem. \- that I got from Sidlit \+ said he would stir the matter up \- then I walked up to studio \- saw Gus \- \+ arranged to have fire started in furnace \+ then home to lunch, after meeting Geo. \+ Maynard on the way \- after lunch I got my exposé films \+ unmounted photos. in shape to take to Cullen \+ at 4 o'clk. went down town with them \+ left them all with him \- then to Chan's office but he had gone \- then home \- Dinner at 6 as Jim wished to get to the Armory early to do some shooting before drill.   I spent the eve'g worrying over my bank book \-
