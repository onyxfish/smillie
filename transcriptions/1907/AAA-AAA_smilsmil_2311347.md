## Left Page

### Sat. April 20, 1907

**Weather:** Fine day, but cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up early - bkfs. at 8.30 - to studio before 10 - spent a good part of the m't cutting mounts as standards to guide Weintenkampf who insists that he can have his woman deputy at the library do them in a way that will be satisfactory to me - I wish to direct the mounting of father's fels. \+ some of Hinshelwoods - I agree to supply the board \+ pay a competent man for cutting them. Home to lunch at 2. Availed my self of the bright sunshine to print some Solo prints from negatives made while on my last trip - I wish to send them to W. Wykoff \+ Holyoke - At \+ had a walk on Riverside before dinner - I dined alone as Jim dined at some club affair - I loafed away the eve't altho' there are many things that are crying for attention.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 21, 1907

**Weather:** Clear, cold, windy \+ dirty  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up before 7 - had a bath \+ then went to bed again - had a restful nap - up at 8 \+ had bkfs. before 9 - After 10 I went out with my camera \+ took some photos. on the river about 74° \+ 75° Also some from window of my apartment. Then I went out for a walk but I did not enjoy it \+ did not go far. Dinner at 2. Afternoon, as the sunshine was bright, I made a number of Solio prints - Charl came in at about 5. - Tea at 6.30 - Eve't Reading \+ doing some writing.
