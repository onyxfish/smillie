## Left Page

### Sun. June 23, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day with some slight drawbacks, after the fog last night there was a luminous fog during much of the m'f. luminous but nevertheless so dense that the whistle was kept going for a good part of the m'f. The afternoon was bright \+ the moonlight at night cloudless \+ serene. I was discomforted somewhat by a dull headache \+ loafed on my bed for some time. Afternoon I spent half an hour in the Gym. Riding horseback, on the camel \+ doing various other stunts. I loafed about fore \+ aft on the lower deck where I could have sunshine \+ air, as there are few 3\. \+ 4\. class pass. on board I had also plenty of room. Dinner at 7 \- a good one as steamer dinners go \- but the Room is very full \+ not well ventilated. Ev'f got into my ulster \+, away aft, on a deck reserved for 3? class I found a sheltered seat where I could enjoy the moonlight over the sea \+ I sat there till the man came to scrub the deck \- to bed at 11. ## Right Page

### Mon. June 24, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** 66°  Stateroom

A fine day \- no fog but some hazy clouds that deepened forward ev'f. Light Rain when I went to bed at 10.30 \- heavy Rain later, with wind, some considerable leaks thro' roof on upper deck. The day passed without incident. I read, chatted with my chair neighbors, W. \+ Mrs. Wilson of Phil. loafed \+ slept. Bath at 7 \- bkf. at 8. exercise in Gym. at 9.30 \- lunch at 1. dinner at 7 \+ to bed at 10.30.
