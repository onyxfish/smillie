## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 27, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dismal day - dull m'f. then rain for the rest of the day - I had b'kf'. at about 9 - I spent a large part of the m'f. carrying on the work of last night - Jim helped to get some lace curtains hung in the parlor - I have been bothered all day by a dull, rheumatic-like pain in my right eye where one of the small veins has broken, suffusing a portion of the cornea with blood. We had dinner at 2 - the afternoon was passed very lazily - part of the time I slept - A light supper at 6.30 - Eve'f. reading \+ loafing about.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 28, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, wet day - some heavy rain in the m'f. - late afternoon it held up for a time - wet again in the eve'f. Up at usual time \+ had b'kf'. before 8 - Between wet weather \+ my right eye which is much blood shot \+ painful I did not feel much like work or going out - Practically I spent the whole day arranging negatives \+ prints of our 1904 trip to Europe - I plan to have Cullen make points from the best of the negatives \+ then I shall complete the Albums started for Jim \+ Ralph. - also, make Albums of our 1907 trips - Lunch at 1 — Between 3 \+ 4 o'clk. I went out \+ walked about for the better part of an hour - the air was damp \+ heavy - Dinner as usual - Eve'f. I continued work with photos - Ralph has written that he expects to come to N.Y. on Friday, to stay over Sunday - so Jim went down to Mad. Sq. Theatre \+ got three seats for "Madam Butterfly" for Friday eve'f.
