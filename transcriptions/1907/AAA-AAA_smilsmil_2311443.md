## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 2, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dismal day - light rain till evef when there was heavy rain + strong wind - I did not go out of the house but kept busy getting my photos ready for mounting - Cullen sent up 100 prints from negatives of our 1904 trip - I had thought that his man could do so much better than I could but I was greatly disappointed - some are better than I have been able to get from certain negatives, others were very much worse - I had lunch at 1 - Raelph went to the Yale Club, met Jim + lunched with him there - They were both home to dinner - a rousing big turkey + fixins - after which the evef was a period of rest.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 3, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright mif. but soon clouded over, with strong wind - I had help at 8 - The boys did not get up till about 11 - then I went with them for a walk - up Riverside to the Sigel Statue - there to Columbia University then to the Cathedral of St. John - just as mif service was over - went down into the Crypt Chapel - then home - Dinner at 2. The boys went off together + I pottered about Dinner at 6.30 - very soon after dinner Jim, Raelph \+ I went to Gr. Central Station to escort R. to the 8 o'clk. train - time changed to 8.30 - so we all went to Charls in 38th St. R. had but a few minutes there + then he ret. to the station - Jim + I remained + spent the evef in looking over his photos taken last summer - Home before 11.
