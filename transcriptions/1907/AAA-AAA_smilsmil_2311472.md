## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 30, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable wet day - I got up at 8.30 - 4/5° below normal \+ think I so continued all day. Tax bill that I wrote to Clarence Smith, asking him to send it to me, did not arrive till near 3 o'clk. p.m. I then at once wrote check \+ sent in to Receiver of Taxes. I sent check for Keese Tax to W. Scott Brown - also a check to Wm. C. Queen for $100 on acct. Ralph's class mate, Graham, who seems to be an exceptionally fine fellow dined with us \+ in the evef but Ralph went to the Theatre - They spent the p.m. together at the automobile show. Jim \+ I spent the evef together.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 31, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold \+ windy - Up at about 8 after a fairly quiet night - Temp. 1½° below normal \+ so it continued throughout the day - I wrote several notes in the m.f. to Clarence Smith. Herbert Smith \+ others - About noon I went out for about 20 m. on the sunny side of Broadway - Got 50¢ money order for Jno. Wanamaker. Ralph took my bank-book down to 2° Natl. to be balanced. R. \+ I had lunch at 1. Before 3 o'clk. I again went out for a walk on B'way I went up to 86 S. \+ back. After 4 I had a nap. Then Bro. George came \+ made a visit. Dinner at 6.30. Evef Jim \+ Ralph went out to help the old year out \+ the New Year in - I loafed about. And so endeth the Year of our Lord, 1907.
