## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 12, 1907

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- to studio at 10 \- most of the m'f spent in putting new tissues on blinds \- getting them in place \- putting up curtains \+ spreading rugs. At last I am ready to go to work. Home to lunch at 2\- Got a pleasant note from Natze Elkin asking me to spend Sunday with them after the Yale-Princeton foot-ball game on Sat. I wrote to her in reply. At 4.30 I went to Walter Cook's office 3 W. 29 st \+ attended meeting of Trustees. Ireland Real Estate Co. \- to discuss the matter of franchise Tax \- Home to dinner at 6.45 \- Eve'g Chael came up expecting to see prints from my photos \- He discussed Electric Mule with enthusiasm till
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 13, 1907

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day \- got to studio at 10 \+ prepared to go to work on the Ciceri landscape painting that I have, repairing the cracks that deface it very much. Then W. S. Miller came \+ we went to Maynard's studio \+ planned to change opening to roof so that ventilation may be had \- Miller ret'd to my room \+ talked till Hugh Douglas came \- again we went to Maynard \+ planned to stain \+ varnish his floor \- It was 12 o'clk. before they got away \- Then I went to Pomroy's \+ had new pads on my Truss \- then to Second Nat'l Bank \- then to Civic Forum \- 44 St \- about seats for lectures \- then to P. C. Station \+ then home to lunch at 2.30 \- Then again I succumbed to a nap \- \+ had a long one \- newspaper \- dinner \- a lot, not all, of prints from my photo. negatives from Wm. Cullen \- I spent the eve'g looking over them
