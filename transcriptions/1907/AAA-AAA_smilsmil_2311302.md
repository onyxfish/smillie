## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 20, 1907

**Weather:** Rain fog + darkness till the after noon, then a change — wind from west  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- clouds lifted + the sidewalks dried off growing much colder at night — Bkfst at 9.30 — called up Dr. Tuttle + made appt. to call at his office at noon — did so + we talked over our possible Jamaica Trip. I proposed to go down to Ham. Am. office to-morrow mf. to see what can be done to save our trip as first planned — walked up home from 48th & 6th Ave. in spite of mugginess + slush — Dinner at 2 — Some writing + some loafing during the afternoon — Tea at 6.30 + after that Jim + I went to Charl's + had an eve'g with his slides — Home before 11 — high wind + very cold —

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 21, 1907

**Weather:** Bright, cold windy day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkfst before 9 — after that went down to Ham. Am. office + saw W. Schmolek + arranged with him for stateroom 17 on [illegible]Itel Friedrich to sail Feby 19th for Port Antonio — then to Charl's office — he had not yet come down — then to Cullen's + got some photo. supplies. Then to studio getting there before 12 — worked on father's portrait till 1.30 — then lunch — after that mounting enlargements of my Portrait — + changing some from black to sepia. That was the most of my afternoon — left the studio at about 4.30 + then home — Dinner alone — Jim working overtime — Eve'g I did some photo. printing.
