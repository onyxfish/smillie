## Left Page

### Wed. May 22, 1907

**Weather:** Pleasant but unusually cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant but unusually cool - To studio after making along call at Dr. Tuttle's - I have been much troubled for some days past with distension + unusual pains in lower abdomen - Dr. says it is a form of indigestion + gas - I worked till noon on Father's portrait - then having paper prepared I took a careful india pf. + was much pleased to find it come out looking better than I had hoped. - Fixed my lunch at 2 - Then continued proofing till 4. Then I had a good deal of cleaning up to do + it was after 5 before I got away from studio - home + had a rest - Dined alone at 7 - Jim dined out. So tired that I did nothing but loaf in the eve + got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 23, 1907

**Weather:** Overcast - mild  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast - mild - To studio before 10. and worked for awhile on Father's portrait. Then took some pfs. on large paper - now I shall lay it away + forget it for some months. I had my lunch in studio before 2 o'clk - Then got out plate + pf. of the etching I made a year ago based up on photo. of myself "Study of an old man's head." - I studied over it for awhile then took two prints but ink was not right for it - gave up + left studio after 4 o'clk - went home + rested till dinner time - Rec'd. a note from Charlotte Kent of Buffalo saying that her Mother is so seriously ill that they can not occupy their house at Montrose + wish to rent it - + ask if I know of any one who w'd. take it - I wrote in reply.
