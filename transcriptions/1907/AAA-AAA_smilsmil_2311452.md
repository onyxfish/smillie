## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 20, 1907

**Weather:** Bright m[illegible] for a while — then overcast \& threatening rain which did not come  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at about 10 — then to Brooks Bros. with trousers — back to studio \+ to work on painting "Evening in the Woods" the "Giant" lighted by setting sun. I think I improved it. Home to lunch at 2. After that I worked for a time on negatives \+ at 3.30 went down town to Cullen's taking the negatives with me — got from him the last batch of mounted prints — got Excelsior Diary for next year from Finders Wisel — Home at 5 then till dinner time looking over the prints I brought up with me — Dinner — then Jim \+ I went to Carnegie Hall. 8.30 — Opening meeting Civic Forum, to hear Gov. Hughes \+ Chief Justice Brewer — Gov. H. spoke of "Public Office \+ Justice B." his text being "Public office in relation to Public Questions". Hughes spoke for about 3/4 of an hour, very forcibly \+ I had quite difficulty in hearing him but it was a constant strain to catch what the Justice said \+ at the end of his remarks I had no single impression of his argument or purpose.
