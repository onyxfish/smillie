## Left Page

### Thur. April 4, 1907

Pleasant day — after an unsuccessful night I got up feeling very wretched Headache, sore throat, some fever \+ general discomfort — I did not go out during the m't at all, but spent the m't trimming the photo prints that I got from Cullen yesterday lunch with Ralph at 1 — afternoon I walked with R — up Riverside Drive to 114 St — \+ back thro' Riverside Park — more than three miles, in spite of feeling very unfit. At 6.30 we had dinner — I was so much out of sorts that I was unable to go to R.R. station with Ralph for the 8 o'clk. train to New-Haven. Jim went with him \+ then Rel?. dressed \+ went to his neighborhood dance — I got rid of the eve't somehow \+ then to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 5, 1907

After a dismal night of headache \+ discomfort I did not get up for my bkfs't but took it in bed — I got up at 9 o'clk. \+ called Dr. Tuttle on 'phone \+ told him of my miseries — He advised me to keep quiet \+ this afternoon take a course of calomel \+ bi-carb. \+ so to-morrow m't citrate of Mag. — I Rec'd. a note from Mc.German deoy'd Co. saying that at the request of Ham-Am. line they had Reserved Room 303 on Königin Luise to Sail from Naples Sep't 20 I wrote to German-lloyd accepting the Room \+ to Mr. Meyer of Ham-Am — thanking him for his influential assistance — a light lunch at 1 o'clk — I spent part of afternoon on my lounge — wrote to Geo. Cook \+ fussed away the time.
