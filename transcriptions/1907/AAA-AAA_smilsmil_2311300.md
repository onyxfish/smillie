## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 16, 1907

my birthday —

**Weather:** Overcast \- cold \+ raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast \- cold \+ raw \- Bkft. before 9 o'clk \- To studio soon after 10 \- worked till 3.30 on father's portrait \- had lunch in studio \- At 3.30 walked down 3d. ave to P.O. Station. 28th St. Then to D.B. Butler 4th ave \- Then to Bayerdorfers \- Then to Walter's office at 4 o'clk. (3W. 29th) Special meeting Inland Real Estate Co. \- to elect successor to Edw. M. Cook \- Walter, Geo. Carleton \+ self, present \- The business under direction of Walter amounted to a Reorganization \- Geo. is treas. on salary of $1000 \+ is to have charge \- Carleton is to be Sec'y, Young Walter is made a Director \+ he \+ Carleton are to be associated with Geo. in the transaction of business. The business was done promptly \+ harmoniously \+ over at 5 \- I then walked to 4th ave. \+ 28th St. \+ by Subway to 72d St. \+ from there walked home \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Ev'g. Putting away things \-

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 17, 1907

**Weather:** Winter at last \- snow, in small dry flakes  **Temp:** [illegible]

Winter at last \- snow, in small dry flakes, fell until late in the afternoon \- air very cold \- Bkft. before 9 \+ before 10 started for studio getting there at 10.15 \- worked all day on portrait plate of father \- had my lunch in studio \- Fearing a sub-way crush I started for home soon after 4, getting there without meeting any crush \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Ev'g. Jim went to drill \+ a dance \- I worked at printing some of last summer's negatives.
