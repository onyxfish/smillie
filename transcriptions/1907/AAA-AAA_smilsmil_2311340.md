## Left Page

### Sat. April 6, 1907

**Weather:** Sunshine & shadow — quite a cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkfst. before 8 — I commenced the day at 6 o'clk. with a dose of Citrate of Magnesia following the Calomel of yesterday — It gave me some relief I think — I spent the m't. Reading & doing some writing. I went out & had a short walk — Geo. Green came at 1 o'clk, begged to have $4.— added to last months wages because of unusual amt. of snow last winter — Lunch — I went out & got some solo paper & developer & then took some solo prints from negatives made south. That took most of the afternoon time — Dinner with Jim at 6.30. He is complaining of sore throat & influenza symptoms — The rest was loafed away by both of us — I did not feel well enough to go to Century meeting.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 7, 1907

**Weather:** A disagreeable day — overcast, raw, windy & cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up at 7 & had my bkfst. alone — Jim did not get up till after 5 p.m. being used up by a severe cold — I got to work taking solo prints from negatives made on my last trip — About 10.30 I went to Century to see George's large painting, evening effect — I think it looked very well. Then to studio & got several 4×5 printing frames — Then home at about 1 o'clk — At 2 had my dinner alone — More printing in the afternoon — progress slow for want of sunlight, & sunshine direct — Charl came in about 5 & left at 6 — Jim & I had a light meal at 6.30 — Eveng. busy with photos & to bed early.
