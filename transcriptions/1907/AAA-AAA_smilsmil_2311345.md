## Left Page

### Tues. April 16, 1907

**Weather:** Overcast m'g — clearing afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

before 8 with Jim — To studio at 10 — got out old Water-color drawing "on the Susquehanna River near Binghampton" \+ worked over a portion of it to its betterment I think — I am going to give it to young Walter Cook's bride they are to be married on the 23d. of this month — Then I spent some time putting my stamps on the Hinshelwood p\[illegible\] \+ home to lunch at 2 — At 3.30 went to N.A. of D. Exbin \+ was there till near 6 trying to understand some of the pictures on Exbn. walked up home \+ got in rather late for dinner — Eveg wrote to Ripley Hitchcock answering some enquiries about Camp Choconict.

## Right Page

### Wed. April 17, 1907

**Weather:** Overcast m'g — clearing later to bright and windy — streets dreadfully dirty  **Temp:** [illegible]

before 8 — Arote note to Miss Ropes to go with Water Color "On the Susquehanna, near Binghampton". Went to 23d. S — got Ruby electric bulb — then to Reuter's \+ left orders \+ later he sent for Ropes drawing — then to studio and worked there over Hinshelwood p\[illegible\] — Home to lunch at 2 — back at 3.30 \+ worked till 5.30 dividing the p\[illegible\] between the N.Y. Pub. Library Print Room \+ the Boston Mus. of Art. \+ writing a list of them — then home — dinner at about 7 — Evening loafing about — Jim was out.
