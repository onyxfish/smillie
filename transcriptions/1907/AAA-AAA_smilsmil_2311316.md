## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 17, 1907

Ralph returned to New-Haven.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful clear day with strong, cool n.w. wind — I got up after 7 feeling refreshed by a comfortable night's rest. At 8 o'clk I had my bath \+ after 9 I had bkfs. with Ralph — Jim had gone to meet a fellow to inspect a downtown Telephone station that is being refitted by the Western Electric Co. About noon R. \+ I went out \+ had a walk up Cent. Park W. to 1 0 1 0 5'. St. We stopped in at the First Ch. of Christian Scientists \+ had a good look at the remarkably handsome interior — Then home — dinner at 2. After that Jim \+ Ralph went out — at 4 o'clk. I went to Mary Cook's but she was out. Then I went to Walter's \+ had a friendly chat with him till after 5 — then to Charl's \+ there found J. \+ R. — we were there till after 6. Then home — had supper \+ at 7. 30 J. \+ R. left — R. for Grand Central to return to N.H. \+ Jim to call on Alice Irelands. I read \+ wrote \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 18, 1907

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful clear day — up soon after 7 \+ had bkfs. before 8 o'clk — Did not go to studio but was engaged attending to many matters preparatory to getting away on Thurs. Before noon I went to P. O. station W. cor. Columbus Ave. \+ 84 "S. \+ got a money order for $10. \+ sent it to Ernest Roth at Florence, Italy — Home at 1. lunch at 1.30 — then out again — got lease forms \+ filled in a lease for Jos. P. Ryan for five years from July 1 [illegible]. — Dinner with Jim at 6.30 — later he went to a political caucus — Charl came up \+ after consultation I acceded a form he drew for codicil to my will giving to Katharine \+ Bessie Throop all my share of the Estates of father \+ mother — I wrote to Director Maguire of Corcoran Gallery in answer to a letter Rec'd from him \+ told him that I would let the trustees have Cliffs of Normandy for the price of the frame, $100 — The trustees will not meet again till the third Monday in April —
