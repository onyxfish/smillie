## Left Page

### Fri. Aug. 30, 1907

*[Header note: To Fluelen & Altdorf.]*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain falling in the mts when I got up. I had bkfst. before 8 — The boys came down later. After bkfst. I wrote, finishing letter to E.M.J — Sunshine at about 11 — but it was steaming hot — went out with the boys & loitered thro' the woods & over the park areas till lunch time at 12.30 — then we arranged for a trip down the lake to Fluelen — Altdorf — back, stopping at Rüttli — the Mt. Vernon, in a way, as I understand, of the Swiss — took car down at about 1.30 — then boat — very pleasant sail — At Fluelen an electric tram car was just starting for Altdorf — with a lot of others we rushed for it — got on — in less than 15 min. we had reached — in the car are met Miss Robertson \+ her party, fellow passengers on the Pres. Lincoln — at Altdorf the end of the route was in front of the fountain by the Tell monument — we jumped off the car, took two snap shots at fountain \+ monument, then on to the car \+ back to Fluelen just in time to catch the 4.10 boat. By that we went to Rüttli — went ashore — up to the historic meadow where the thirty three Swiss in 1307 took the oath to liberate the fatherland from the oppressors \+ into the quaint little house, now a restaurant \+ to the denkmal erected to the memory of Kraner, the author, \+ to greet the Comps over of the Rüthlied — In less than an hour the next boat came along \+ we ret. to Brunnen — Wm \+ Katie were on the boat. On getting to B — we took a little turn about the town with them — bought some photos. I were amused by an Italian showman who had a Camel, a performing donkey — a bear \+ several baboons — The crowd had great fun over the donkey who threw boys over his head — We got back to hotel at 7 — dinner at 7.30 — a dance in the eveg. — I did not attend.

## Right Page

### Sat. Aug. 31, 1907

*[Header note: The Rigi-Kulm. 5905.]*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

About 6 o'clk. when I looked out of my window not a fleck of cloud be seen in the sky — at 7 I called the boys — we had bkfst. before 8 \+ at 8.37 we went down to Brunnen \+ took the 9.10 boat — Katie \+ Wm came along \+ joined us — the weather had changed rapidly — a veil of mist gathered over the water \+ by the time we got to Vitznau the mist had taken the form of thin, low flying clouds \+ when at 10.30 we started up it was only at times that we could see thro' it to the lake below — but it did not very materially interfere with the effects — before we got to the Rigi Kast had rise rose entirely above it \+ a beautiful effect of a sea of clouds was below us — this extended over the low country in the direction of Lucerne \+ beyond — over the high Alps beautiful clouds of higher level were forming \+ entangling themselves with the snow covered tops — I made a number of snap shots — I can hardly hope to have got any thing of the effects that were so charming to look upon — We got to the top in an hour \+ a quarter of slow climbing — from the station to the crest was a climb of 2 or 300 feet — at 12.30 we went back to the hotel \+ had a good table d'hote lunch — After that out again, hung fascinated over the lake of Zug at our feet \+ waited impatiently for half an hour to get a clear chance at the mts. then \+ lake towers below them — I did not feel equal to a walk to one of the peaks, proposed, so that Katie went on \+ the boys \+ I descended (2.20) to Arth-Goldau getting there in less than two hours — The descent is very interesting \+ wholly unlike the Vitznau side — It is down an open valley that becomes a gorge near the foot — At A-G — we made a close connection \+ got to Brunnen at 4.10 — by a smart walk we caught the 4.25 Axenstrien car \+ got to hotel at 4.45 — I was tired \+ went at once to my room \+ had a nap before dinner at 7.30 — a peripatetic band of Italians played \+ sang on the terrace before the hotel after dinner.
