## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 11. 1880.

Pleasant day — to studio at 9.30 but did not feel like work — made some notes &
let the whole outfit get away from me — dinner soon after 2 — Back at 3 —
mr. Moses of Baltimore & some friends called — at 4 I went up to the Sherwood
Studio Bldg. 57th St. & 6th Ave. — climbed the ladders to the upper story & found but two
studios that I would care to occupy — then to Renner's & spent some time worrying
over frame patterns — to studio at 7 — had tea —
went to N.A. "Stated meeting" no quorum — then to W.C. —
Sydbu & to 37th St. at 10.30 &
to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 12. 1880.

Dull, threatening day and ried — got to studio a little before 10 — to work painting
over parts of "October Shadows" — Dinner after 2 — then to R.
S. Moore's — he paid me $17.50 for my drawing (Harker)
of Paradise Bay — back to shop & painted until about 4.30
when W.C. Board of Control met & I cleared out until
about 5.30 — at 6.30 at Anna C's — at 8 left her at Arnold's — then got & called upon
mrs. Chas. Watrons — 40th St. & day. Ave. — her niece mrs. Larabee there — at 10.30 to 37th St. &
to bed before 11.
