## Left Page

### July, Sunday 4, 1880

Charming day — after
bkfst. walked out to foy's
& under the trees enjoyed
the air until after 11 —
then home & wrote letter
to Addie Haurs & Dinner
abt — Afternoon, after
some lounging, wrote a
couple of letters — Matt
& Ed. came to tea — Sumy
went with father & Matt
to church — weather
quite warm again —
met down Jap. papers for
[printing?] to-morrow & got to
bed about 11.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 5, 1880

Very pleasant [illegible] — gradually
growing overcast with soft,
steadily falling rain after
4 o'clk. A short walk after
bkfst — then back & to work on
"The way to the River". After
some [illegible] got to work at
my printing — Dinner at
1 after it, feeling tired
I took a nap, then again
at my proving, which used
up the rest of the p.m. —
Tea at 6.30 — about 7.30
father & I went to Mary's
— back in an hour — Evng.
I tried to write an article
on Etching for the Ill. Chris.
weekly, but not in the
humor — to bed soon
after 10 — [illegible — ~4 words]
