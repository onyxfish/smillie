## Left Page

### June, Sunday 6, 1880

Pleasant breezy day, but warm —
Up late — bkfst. at 10.30 — then to
Dr. Hastings's — after that, to
studio — wrote two or three letters
& had dinner at 2.30 — Then
to Anna C's & with her went
to "Century" — then by elevated
R.R. at 14th St. & 6th Ave. — to 93d
St. Walked to Riverside Park
& around again to 82d S. Station
& back to 38th S. at 6.30 — then
I went to Studio — had a
bite to eat & at 8 went to
Windsor Hotel & passed the
evening with Miss Hallowell &
Mr. Scott — At 10 took Mr. S.
to her home — then to 37th S.
— to bed at 11. Every
quite cool & overcast —

## Right Page

### June, Monday 7, 1880

Blazing hot again — got to studio
about 9 — and tried to get to
work in an orderly way upon
the thousand and one things that
must be done before I came
away for the season — I
"wet-down" some of my etchings
& made paste — & fussed about,
& dinner before 2 o'clk — in the
afternoon I mounted half a
dozen etchings & at 6.30 was
at Anna C's where I dined —
Later Fanny K. & Emma J. came
& with Geo. & Anna C. we went
to the new Concert Hall. Bdway &
41st S; there until after 10.30 —
then home with Fanny & Emma —
[illegible] got to 37th S. — & to
bed at about 12.30.
