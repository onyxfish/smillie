## Left Page

### July, Friday 16. 1880.

Very hot day — after bkft —
wrote a note to Thuinger.
Then up town, did shopping
& back at 10.30 — Some packing
done — dinner about 1 —
gave myself up to the
heat some time — Shower
about & some air stirring.
finished packing at 5 o'clk
Then had a bath & change.
Tea soon after 6 — Later
Geo. & I called on the Ritters
then on the Seemiss and
returned home about 9 —
Father just in from NY, in
very good spirits over the
finishing of his West Point
plate — Glorious, cool
moonlight night — To bed
about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 17. 1880.

*Saratoga — at J. W. Thuinger's*

Beautiful m'g. After bkfs.
went up town & bid Matt & many
good bye — there. Returned home
& bid good bye there & at 10.40
went to depot — took 11.15 train
getting to Saratoga at 2.25 — had
a very pleasant ride — no dust
or heat. Mr. E. met us at the
depot & we rode at once to his
house — a cute time in our rooms,
then a little chatting & at 4 we had
dinner — at 5 we went out to ride
to the lake & away beyond. Returning
to Meyers, on the lake, where we had
tea at 7.30 — at about 8.30 in drove
home by a glorious moonlight &
then for an hour on the square;
Chinese-lantern-lighted porch or
loggia & about 10.30 to bed.
