## Left Page

### February, Monday 9, 1880

Crisp, cold day. Got to
studio about 9.30 & to work
on one sketch based on
study made in Ravine back
of Smith Reede's — To dinner
at 2 — short walk — back
to studio & after 5, finished
sketch — Had tea at 6.30.
Evey. Etching club — barely
a quorum present and
nothing done — except
drinking beer — To 37. S.
at 11.15 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 10, 1880

Pleasant day, altho' at times
overcast — Got to studio at 9.30
& to work on a sketch in one
based upon a Pike. study —
Wash Wilson came in during
the mtf. — To dinner at 2 —
after that, a short walk. To work
some after 3 & finished sketch.
Abb went to Anna C's and
had dinner — at 8, with C.
herbs, Joe & Miss Touché went
to hear the Pirates — The Kimballs
joined our party — a very pleasant
time — went home with C.C, then
walked down to studio & back
to 37. S. & to bed at 11.40 — Had
an invitation for Sorosis Reception —
Jno. C. Townsend's daughter
married to-night.
