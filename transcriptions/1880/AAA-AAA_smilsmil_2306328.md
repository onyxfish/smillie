## Left Page

### January, Monday 26, 1880

Still delightful weather —
got to studio at 9.30 & spent
most of the mf. making
pen sketch for the Graphic
— Dinner at 2 — after
that, receiving callers
until 4.30 when I went
to Scribner's & saw M. Burlin-
ame, then to Laughter's —
then to Wunderlich's & at
6.30 to Studio & had tea.
At 8.30 went to Louis Tiffany's
out — Albert Mitchell's — out —
then to the Kimballs — there
until 10.15 — then to 37 St.
& to bed before 11 —
Glorious moonlight night —
[illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 27, 1880

Dark, damp day. Rain beginning
to fall about noon. Got to
studio a little before 10 and
spent the mf making pen
sketch for Koehler from a photo
after Dupré — Dinner at 2 &
at 3 went down town — first
to see Parsons — then to Shark's
— to Juineau's & to A.B.N. Co —
got an order there for a large
drawing — at 5 went to Graphic
then to Studio — tea at 7.30
— rest of evening spent making Irish
moss tea — at 10.30 went
to 37 St. in drenching rain
& right to bed.
