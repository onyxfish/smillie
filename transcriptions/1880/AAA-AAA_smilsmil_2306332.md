## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 3, 1880

Horrible wind & snow. Heavy
drifts filling the streets — got
to Colman House at 8.40 —
Father already there — then had
bkfs. got to studio at 4 30 &
to work on Columbia drawing
for A.B.N. Co. worked until
noon when I lay down inst
stomach pains — Dinner at 2 —
then to Water Color. [illegible] met
Miss Canning & spent an hour
looking at pictures — then to
studio & made washes on
drawing — Father came
in about 6. we had tea
& spent the eveg. together
in the studio — to 37 St — & to
bed at 10.
Clear, starlight night.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 4, 1880

Beautiful m[illegible]. At 8 made
a call on Anna C — then to
Colman Ho. met Father & had
bkfs. He goes home this p.m.
got to studio soon after 9 &
to work on "Columbia" — to
dinner at 2 — after that a
short walk — worked as long
as I could see — Mr. Isabella
Hooker. Mr. & Miss [illegible] came
about 8 & made quite a long
call — then we went down
to Chamboreig's room where he
& wife were holding a levee
— there until 10.30 altho' we
had intended going to the
Private view of Artists fund.
to 37 St. at 10.45 — & right
to bed.
