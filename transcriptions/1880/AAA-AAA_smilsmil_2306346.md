## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 2. 1880.

Charming day — got to studio about 9.30  got a sketch from miss Jacobs for my milkmaid there to work painting it in  Spent all the m'f at it —  to dinner at 2 — a walk in Mad. Sq. after & then again to work on 16×21. althi much interrupted by the water-color business going on about me. Tea at 6.30  at 8 went to Mr Rolt. Gordon's f 2. 38' S. Committee Meeting f Met. Museum Joan call. there until nearly — then to 37' S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 3. 1880.

Pleasant day until the afternoon. To studio's soon after 9. Smeared my 14×30 twilight with a glaze & attempted to repaint but was a grand failure — to dinner at 2 — after that went to R.S. Gifford's met Robbins & Waller & then we started out for a visit to the studios in behalf of the Opening system of the Met. Museum of Fine Arts — went to 10! S. Be[illegible]. there until nearly — Raining when I came out — Kate Foak tea with us at Studio — at 8 me went to see "Pirates of Penzance" — had a front seat in the orchestra, very bad — after the play took Kate up to A.V. P.'s — there, as it had stopped raining, walked down to 37' S. & got to bed just about midnight.
