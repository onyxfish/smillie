## Left Page

### December, Monday 27, 1880

Still dismal — Up before 7. Hefin at 7.30 — hack at 8 — good bye there to depot — 8.25 Train 20m late — Mr. Nevins joined us — got to N.Y. a little before 11 — went right to studio — To work putting in big washes on "Blk. Mt. from Harbor Island" — out to dinner soon after 1 — After that continued my work & shortly before 4 washed out all that I had done during the day — then with Colman to Riding school & had a good ride — Soon after 6 to 37. S. & had bath — 7.30 at studio & had tea — 8.30 at Annie's until 10.15, then to 37. S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 28, 1880

Bitter cold mng. Got to studio at 9.30 — Mr. Daly, of Providence R.I. called — I again washed in sky on Blk. Mt. picture — worked until 1.30 — there to lunch & after that went to 106 Madison an to see about rooms for Father, wrote that — Mr. Parker not in — then to studio & worked until after 4 then a short cast; then some writing & at 5.30 again went to Mad. Ave. & found Mr. P. in & saw the rooms — Ret. to studio & wrote letter home & at 8 o'ck at Annie's — Will, Joe, Minnie & Walter Pierce called & Annie Minnie announced her engagement to Walter. They left soon after 9 & I had a pleasant visit until about 10.30 — then to 37. S. & to bed.
