## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 7, 1880.

Fine day — Still recovering from my debauch — Got to studio about 9.30 as usual. most of the m'f spent in loafing — lunch at 2 — then called on Miss Durfee. the rest of p.m. writing letters — out for a walk — called on Mrs. Hy Panton — tea in studio at 7, at 8 went to the Woodbury's — 5 E. 13th St — all out — then to Leavitt's Gallery & about an hour spent looking at Avery's pictures to be sold to-morrow night — then to Century then to h.a., then to 37e S. & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 8, 1880.

Fine day — to studio soon after 9 & commenced painting "A Kennebunk Port Veteran" — 15×20 — wrestled with the sky all the m'f. lunch at 2. a short walk — again at work — very tired, but scrabbled in foreground — at 6.45 at Anna Cooke's — Mrs. ned — Mrs. Warren Cook & Miss Williams also to dinner — at 8 took a [illegible] f. k. to Arnold's — there got & called on Miss Mellick — E. 33d. S. — not in — then on Isham — had company — we would not go in — then to Sanford Gifford's — W. Whittridge & Mr. Holt there — at 9.30 went to Union league — there until near 11 — then to 37e S. & to bed at 11.30.
