## Left Page

### September, Thursday 30. 1880.

Up at 7. Bkfs. at 8. Beautiful day — wrote a short letter to
A.C.C., condensed from the one written last night, and posted it. Soon after 9 went to Mr. Davis's & with Mr. D—
gee & Hughes, went to boat which did not start until 10.30 — then went to Big Hog Island & soon after 11 got started on a W.C. sketch. Stopped to eat my lunch at 1.30 — finished my sketch before 4 — then we all walked a mile or two, finding some very fine material — took the boat about 5 o'clk. & got to the city about 5.45 — cold & windy. Tea at 6.30 — then rested for half an hour, then wrote a letter to Mr. Ehninger, then went to Davis's room for [illegible] & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 1. 1880.

Superb day — after bkfs. spent an hour & a half walking about the city — went out to the western promenade & had a splendid view of the White Mts. the central group being capped with snow. Then to my work. the back of No. 1 Portland Pier — Shea's boat building shop — met Mr. Hammond in the street — he introduced himself — Got outline finished & went to dinner at 1 o'clk. — after that, feeling headache, lay down on my bed & fell asleep, losing 2 o'clk boat to the cape — I wandered along the wharves & took a later boat, went to the Mariner place & made a pencil sketch — back to hotel at 6.15 — tea — Welf, Davis and Hammond came in — we all went to D's room — there until 10 — then to hotel — to bed at 10.30
