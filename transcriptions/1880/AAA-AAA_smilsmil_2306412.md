## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 14, 1880

Another very pleasant day & much cooler. I
spent the mf. in clearing
up & putting away my
etching & printing material
in father's room. Dinner
at 12.30 — Afternoon I was
arranging pfs. for Koehler
& Thunigen also writing
a long letter for t. con-
cerning printing & papers
also material from which
he will write notice of me
to appear in Oct. No. of Am.
Art Review — Matt & E?.
to tea — delicious eve. f.
which was loafed away
— about 9 I walked up
town — Returned & to bed
at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 15, 1880

Rain & heavy thunder about
6 a.m. Rain continued until
after 9 — then it cleared hot
& muggy, altho' clouded.
All the mf. finishing
my letter to Koehler and
packing pkg. & proofs for
him — at noon went to Eyp
& t. to P.O. then back to
dinner — After that wrote
an answer to Kate Riley's
note. The rest of afternoon
spent getting ready to leave
town — father went to N.Y.
by evng. boat — Mary,
Matt & E?. were with us
part of evng. I went to
P.O. at 9 — then home & to bed
before 10. [illegible — ~2 words]
