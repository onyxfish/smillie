## Left Page

### April, Thursday 29. 1880.

overcast m'f — changing to rain which came down furiously at night. Got to studio about 9.30 & spent the day finishing over etching "Old Cedars" — working and proving — Dinner at 2 — worked until 6.20 then to Anna C's — after taking A. & F. to Arnold's, 9oo + I went to Ned Cook's and made our dinner café — a pleasant ev'g until 10. I was invited to a private meeting of the "Young Scratchers" at Mr. Morgan's house, 178 Madison Ave, but did not feel that I was enough of a politician to accept. To 37t St + to bed before 11.

Matt & Kate fell to Po'k by night boat.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 30. 1880.

Cold breezy day. Nicola's wife died this morning at 6 o'clk — I got to studio at about 9.30 & spent the m'f trying to get satisfactory pts. from old cedars but for some reason could not — Dinner at 2 — the rest of the p.m. spent in general preparations for other work & going down to Scribner's & Dewey's — the tailor — Tea in the studio & at a little after 8 I went into Coleman's where we had piano music by the quartette. At 11 went home with Anna C thro to 37t St + got to bed about 11.30.
