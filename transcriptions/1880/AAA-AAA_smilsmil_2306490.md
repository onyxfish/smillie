## Left Page

### December, Friday 17, 1880

Very pleasant day — 9.30 when
I got to studio — again at
work upon Mariner's mell
— to me it is a failure as
it is very commonplace in
color — out to lunch at 1.30
on my Return found Koehler
waiting — he wishes me to etch
ahead after Shirlaw — I promised
to do it — worked on W. C. as
long as I could see — Bridgeman
& others called at dusk — at 4.45
I went to 37 S. dressed & then
to Annie's — dinner at 7 — at 9
we went to 242 Mad. ave — Mr.
Kerouwen's Rec'n to W. & Mrs. Jul.
S. White — there until 10 — home in
A — then at 10.30 went to Salmagundi
Club entertainment at Sarony's —
there for half an hour — lots of
fun, but too late & too much
tobacco smoke — then to 37 S.
& to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 18, 1880

Fine day — to studio between
9 & 10 — worked on "Twilight —
Dutchess Co." a drawing made
last May — By Yale came in
about lunch time & we went
off & lunched together — after
that I passed over some
W. C. sketches & soon after 4
went down to Wanderchis,
met Annie there & we shopped
together — went home with her
& had dinner — at 8 left,
went to 37 S., dressed and
then to Mr. Mynkoolp's Rec'n
5 E. 13 S. there for half an hour
then to Salmagundi Rec'n —
at N. A. & D — there until near
11 — then to 37 S. & to bed.
