## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 28, 1880

Mild, clear may like day —
Got to Studio's at 9.30 & to
work Tracing & Re-Tracing
a photo after Villegas for
Koehler — at 2 went to
dinner & for some time
after, loitered in Mad. Sq.
Back to Studio's & tried to
work but my head troubled
me so that part of p.m.
was passed on the lounge.
Tea at 6.30 & about 7.30 Miss
Jacobs, Geo. & I went over to Jersey
City & called on Mr. Hardenberg
in Barrow St; Miss Vantaone & Misses
Van Cleef there. — Glorious moon-
light night — To 37. S. & to bed
shortly before 12.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 29, 1880

Beautiful, winter-like day —
Got to Studio's at 9.30 & to work
on pen sketch after Villegas, but
much interrupted by callers.
To dinner at 2 — after that
called on Robbins & Wadsworth
Thompson — loitered awhile
in Mad. Sq. then again to my
work & at it as long as I could
see. At 6.30 at Anna C's to
tea — Took her to Miss Arnoles
then called on Mrs. Hunt S.
39 St — dinner party — then left
cards at Miss Henrietta Beebe's
the 128 E. 39) then walked up to
Will's — Anna Bradley there; Clan
Brainerd came in with his
fiancée — Glorious moonlight
night — walked down to 37.
S. & to bed at 11.
