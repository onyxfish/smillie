## Left Page

### January, Sunday 18, 1880

Still another day of raw fog, but in the afternoon the weather cleared. After bkfs. went to studio and spent the mff on the sofa about Mrs. Coleman coming in tasted [illegible] + true to come down to dinner which I gladly did — my throat very sore. back at 2.30 + spent the rest of p. m. + every lying around — had tea at 6.30 — wrote to father + Koehler, to 37 S. + to bed before 11,

## Right Page

### January, Monday 19, 1880

Still overcast + raw. Got to studio before 10 sto work on big drawing — worked over the sky — improving the tone + bringing the whole thing together — dinner at 2. and in the afternoon put my final touches on the picture — tea in studio as usual, about 7 — at 9 went to Kurtz Gallery. Private view of Salmagundi Club Blk + White Show — there until about 10.30 — then walked up to 37 S. + right to bed.
