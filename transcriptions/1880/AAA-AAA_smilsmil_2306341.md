## Left Page

### February, Saturday 21, 1880

Charming day — Up with
a mean headache. Got
to Studio before 10 but was
unable to do anything —
Miss Hallowell & others called.
Dinner at 2 — at 4 gave
up & went to 37th St. & to bed.
Did not get up until
Sunday m'g. George
went to Philharmonic Concert
in my place.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 22, 1880

Fine day — Bkf't about 10 o'clk,
feeling rather seedy after
yesterdays experience — did
not go to church but to the
studio — about 11.30 began to
write an article on Etching
for Harper's — & kept at it for
an hour or more — Went
with Geo. to dinner at 2.
— walked about Madison
Sq. for an hour or more —
then some more writing
& at 6.30 went to Emma J's
& at [illegible] with her to Mrs Caswell's
in 13th St. Dinner there at
7.30 — left there about 11 —
glorious moonlight — To
37th St. & to bed at 12.30.
