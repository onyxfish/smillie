## Left Page

### October, Saturday 30. 1880.

Heavily overcast — B&p. at 8 —
after that out for a walk —
Rain began falling soon after
10 & for the rest of the day &
night it fell heavily — —
wrote back minutes of Sketching
club most of the day — also,
answers to some delayed cor-
respondence. Dinner at 1 —
Again, writing during the after-
noon & had a walk before
dark — Matt came to tea —
about 8 I went home with
her — got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 31. 1880.

Rain until about 7 A.m. then
the wind began to blow & during
the m'f., at times it was almost
a gale — but the air was bland
& delightful — B&p. late — m[illegible]
went to Baptist Ch. Kendrick
preached about making "calling &
election sure" — we must do that
on Tuesday — After service saw uncle
Willie who has just returned from
Manitoba — To Mary's with Father
then home — Matt & Ed to dinner.
After that I read & at about
4 went out & had a walk —
at 5.30 stopped at Mary's —
home at 6 — then tea — Matt
& Ed. left before 8 — then Kate
read an excellent sermon by
Deacon Channing on Christ —
after that I wrote awhile —
then out & had a walk in the
beautiful starlight night —
To bed at about 11.
