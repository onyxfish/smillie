## Right Page

### January, Thursday 1, 1880

Charming overhead, atrocious
under foot. Breakfast as usual
then to studio — made out a list
of calls to make — At 12.30 made
calls in the Bldg — Mr. Chambney
Shurtleff, Telles, McKnight & Miss Abbott
then on Mr. Colman & with Geo. to
Emma J's — also Dr. Bellows, where at
1.45 I took lunch, Gel going on —
then to Wyukoof's, Avery's, Waller's
Martin's, Kimball's & Morris's, getting
to Mr. Colman's again at about
6 — tea there until after 7 — then
to Isham's, Melleck's, Burston's,
Black's & at 9.45, at Anna Cook's
there until 10.30, then to
37.5. & to bed.

Left cards at Greene's —
Walter Cook's — Mr. Paulson &
Mrs. Dart's — [illegible — ~2 lines]
