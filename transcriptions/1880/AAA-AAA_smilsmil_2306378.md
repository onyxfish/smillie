## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 5, 1880

Slightly overcast & still warm — to studio at 9
& to work on drawing of
West Point for Scribner's —
To dinner at 2 — & after
a short walk, again at
work — did not get along
satisfactorily — Tea at
4. Out of sorts in the even
but went to A.P.N's & made
a short call on mother —
at 9 & a little after, walked
down to 34th St & got to bed
about 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 6, 1880

Warm — breezy & very dirty.
Got to studio som after 9
& to work on Scribner's West Point
drawing, fussing with it — trying
to get something better — Spent
all the m[or]n on it — about
1.30 went out for lunch and
after that went to funeral
of Mrs. Jacobs — then to Fanny K.
— back to studio at near 3.30
Several callers, but I worked
some upon plate of "Old Cedar"
at 6.30 at Anna Cook's — The
last Thur. even[in]g duty of the
season — Fanny & Willet there —
at 8 with Fanny, Anna, & Geo
went to Standard Hall & heard
Prof. Ritter's illustrated lecture
on the growths of Chamber music.
To 34th St. & to bed at 11.30.
