## Left Page

### July, Saturday 24, 1880

Delicious day — went to [illegible]
Stirring as usual before [illegible]
about 10.30, with Mr. E. —
drove down to Grand Union —
Took in Mr. Bergh & after
some driving about we went
out to the Race Track — got
good seats on the grand stand
— never saw more charming
effects of sky & distance — Had
a lunch at 1 — back before
3 — a number of callers on Mr.
Bergh — a wee dinner at 5.
B — with us until 7 —
family chatting until 9.
then I walked down to
Congress Hall & listened awhile
to the music — then back &
to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 25, 1880

warm but beautiful day —
Up late — a very short walk &
no spring water before breakfast
After that a short walk
with Mr. E — then I walked
alone until after 11 — then
in studio Reading aloud to
Mr. & Mrs. E. & Mrs. Wayland.
& Miss Shepherd — lunch
at 12.30 — Afternoon wrote
letters to Emma J. — Watt
& Geo. & went to P.O. with them.
on my way back to dinner at [illegible]
o'lc. was caught in a very heavy
shower — Eve'g. at about 7.30 went
with Mr. & Mrs. E. to the U.S. — then to
the Grand Central Union & spent
a good part of the eve'g with
Bergh — Home about 10 & soon
to bed.
