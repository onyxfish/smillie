## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 6, 1880

Heavy rain most of the day. To studio at 9.30 — Spent the mf finishing up a number of Lake George blk & white Sketches (originals ill & Lake Geo. article in Harper's for August last) for Salmagundi Club exhibit. To dinner at 2 — then to Knoedle's and fixed up my Water Color box for action — Avery called in the afternoon to see if I could undertake to ill. a cat. of J. Abner Harper's call, to be sold in Feby. I could not. Tea at 4 — even in studio — fixing up Salmagundi drawings to 37 St at 11.30 & to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 7, 1880

Very pleasant day. Got to studio at 9.30 & soon to work with water colors. Will came in & spent an hour or more — he is under the weather — stomach trouble. Mr. Forbes also called — I got to work upon a sketch started last spring, a view upon the tops of Palisades. To dinner at 2 — worked until 4 when Water Color Socty Bd of Control met in studio — there I went to Dargin's studio & after that walked up to 37 S. & back to tea at 7. At studio until 9 o'clk — then to Miss Abbatt's — there until after 10 — then to 37 S. & to bed at 11. [illegible]
