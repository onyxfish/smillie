## Left Page

### January, Monday 12, 1880

Overcast, disagreeable day with rain at intervals
especially at night — Got to
studio about 10 & to work
on big drawing — Put in
all the color on groups of
trees & foreground. Dinner
at 2 — worked until I could
see no longer, then put my
room in order — Tea at 7
& at 8 Etching club began
to arrive — the matter of
an exhibition was discussed
— everyone was sure that an
exhibition was just the thing but no
one was willing to do the work —
so, on my motion, it was in-
definitely postponed — J.W.
Ehninger was our guest — at
11 walked to 34th St. in a
sleety rain.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 13, 1880

The sleety rain changed to
a furious snow storm and in
the mtf. it seemed as if
everything must be blown
away — Was with sore throat
& pains in my head — Got
to studio at 10 & managed
to work about a couple of
hours — was then obliged to
give up & grew worse until
at night I was almost
alarmed at my symptoms —
I lay upon the lounge until
10.30 when Jel got a hack for
me & we rode up to 34th St —
Tobed with hot water, heavy
blankets, aconite etc. etc. and
