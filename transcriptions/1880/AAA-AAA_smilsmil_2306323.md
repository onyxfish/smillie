## Left Page

### January, Friday 16, 1880

A mean sort of day — and I feel like the day — Got to studio a little before 10 & to work on big drawing but did not have all the head that was required — weather cleared before noon — to dinner at 2 — a walk around the park then again at my work. Tea at 7. Everyone to studio. mtng. to 37 S. at 10.30 — to bed before 1 A. [illegible — ~4 words] [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 17, 1880

Another hateful, raw, foggy, dismal day — with my heavy head & my hot sore throat — I feel that I never can get well until there is a change of weather. Got to studio at about 9.45 — to work on big drawing but without spirit & almost hopeless of being able to finish it — Dinner at 2 — dark very early — after a rest, at 5.30 went up to Dr. Yale's — Mad. ave, bet 49 & 50 Sts. to hear what he has to say of my condition — there until 7 — then again to studio — had tea late & soon after to went to 37 S. & to bed.
