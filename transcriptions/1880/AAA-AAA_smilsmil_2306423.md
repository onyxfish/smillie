## Left Page

### August, Thursday 5. Lake 9, 1880.

*Hawe's Cottage — Hulett's*

Charming day — Pt. off. at 8 — then settled with Brown and at 10 o'clk left Bolton on the Horicon — Reached Hulett's before 11 — Found Addie + Mr. Dix waiting for me — Rowed right down to the Cottage + with Charley (the boy of all work). Returned for my trunk — after 12 when I got it to my Room Dinner soon after 1 — Later I Rowed over to the Harbor Islands + amused myself by going around them all — Addie + Mr. Dix went out fishing + capsized their boat — no lives lost. Tea after 6 — In the early every two of the misses Terry + a lady friend came in for a few minutes — Mr. D. + I took them home — on piazza until 10 — then to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 6. 1880.

Delicious day. After bkf. wrote letters to Father + Anna C. — About 10 started out in my boat with water color traps. Rowed along at the foot of high rocks until I caught sight of Butler's hulk "Minnehaha" at Black Mt. Point. It was 5 miles away but did not seem half so far — I pulled for it + got there about 11.30. Mrs. Butler was there + I visited with her for half an hour then pulled back getting home at 1.15 — then dinner — feeling tired after my long pull the afternoon was spent in a hammock swung at the point on the lake Reading Arnold's *Light of Asia* — Tea at 6.30 — Evening on piazza — To bed before 10.
