## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 30. 1880.

Still cooler — a very charming day — Wrote letters until near 10, then to Post Office — back & made tracing of "pen sketch," "the way to the River" — Dinner before — After that, transferred tracing & to work up on etching Kept at it until 5 — at 5.30 went out driving with Father, Mother & Kate. Around by way of Manchester — Several showers scared us but did not reach us until just as we got home soon after 7 — but we did not get wet — near 8 when we had tea — [illegible] Rec'd from NY. I wrote a letter & at 9 went to P.O. & to Ed's with check for $80 that I Rec'd from Cyrus Davis this mfg. To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 1. 1880.

Pleasant day, partially overcast — light rain during the night. Wrote letters after Bkfst & took them to P.O. then returned & before dinner (which was late — 2 o'clk) had finished etching "The way to the River" & had made up some new mordant — Spent the afternoon biting — altho I was also fighting as much a headache — Father went down to NY. this eveg by boat. About 9 I went to P.O. then back & to bed before 10.
