## Left Page

### June, Thursday 10, 1880

Pleasant day, partially overcast. Up late, breakfasted alone & got to studio at 10.30 — some picking up, but most of the m[orn]ing was about sketching from Miss Van Horne. Dinner at 2.30 — then to am S[up]. Sent Salise to Po'k. All the afternoon I worked diligently & got things quite finished. Up by 6.30 then took a rest on lounge. To tea after 7 & after 8 went to Anna Cook's — she was out & I went at 9 o'clk to the Kimball's — there until after 10 — then to 37th S[t]. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 11, 1880

Overcast m[orn]ing, but cleared warm — Spent the morning finishing up at the studio — at 2 Miss Van Horne came to Miss Jacob's studio & while she [illegible] painted from her head. I made two sketches — one in pen and ink, the other, pencil. Had tea at about [illegible] — after that again went to studio & got some things to take to 37th S[t]. At about 8.30 went to Anna Cook's — there until 10 — then to 37th S[t]. & to bed before 11.
