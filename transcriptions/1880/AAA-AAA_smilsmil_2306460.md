## Left Page

### October, Monday 18, 1880.

Cold, clear day — much of the m'f spent measuring
my lane + driving stakes to guide the fence builders —
Dinner at 1 — the afternoon was actively spent running
around ordering lumber + finishing ups odds + ends of
business — Tea soon after 6. Eve'f. called on Bessie
Riley — the Biddles + Mr.
Wm Coolper. At 10, J's soon
after 9 — packed my trunk
+ got to bed at about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 19, 1880.

*Plainfield — N. J. — Uncle Geo's —*

Delicious day — Bkf. at 9 — Stage
did not come until 10.30 — train
started from M. late — got to Tunk-
hannock late so that we had no
time for dinner — fortunately had
some lunch with us which we ate
in the cars — no parlor car on the
train — at Whitehaven was able to get
a cup of tea for Mother — had to
change cars at Penn Haven Junction
— much astonished to meet Chape and
the three Burnham sisters of Phila. —
came in at Mauch Chunk after a
days spree — left us at Bethlen going
back to Phila. — Got to Phillipsburg
at 6.30 — no tea — another change of
cars — at last got to Plainfield at
8.30 all very much used up by the
hammering — changing — foul air — draughts
etc — drove to Uncle Geo's — had tea
about 9 + got to bed soon after 10.
Eve'f. overcast + threatening a
change.
