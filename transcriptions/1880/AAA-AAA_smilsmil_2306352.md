## Left Page

### March, Sunday 14, 1880

In bed until 5 P.m. with hiadache — then up & went to Studio & fixed something to eat — later in the evey went to Mill's & stayed with Father & Mother until about 10.45 — then to 37
St. & to bed. Air still very raw.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 15, 1880

Overcast day — feel very shaky after my experience of yesterday — Got retracings on my places of Sunnyside & big cedar — went out to dinner at 2 — at 3.30 started out with Gifford, Colman & Waller but was soon obliged to give up — distress below, stairs & returned to Studio where for the rest of the afternoon & early evey. I lay upon the lounge — at 8.30 went to Mr. Robt. Gordons — Met museum. Com, there until near 11 — then to 37
St. in light Rain.
