## Left Page

### January, Friday 2, 1880

Very pleasant day — still shocking bad in [illegible] —
got to studio's before 10 — letters from Roberts Bros. & Koehler —
& set to work on pen sketch after Photo from Troyon — Dines at 2 — afternoon, finished sketch, also outline of my big drawing — at 6.30 to [illegible] C's — took her to the Arnold's, then called on Mrs. Morrison — 47th St — not in —
then in the Rodgers 48th St —
pleasant call until nearly —
then to Mr. G. P. Putnam's — 144
E. 60th St — met Geo. there, and had another pleasant call
until 10 o'clk — walked to
37th St — & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 3, 1880

Beautiful day — went to "St. Cloud" for blép — got to studio about 10 — many callers all day so that I got little work done, did not even get tracing & transfer finished of the Coture photo. As we were invited to Louis Tiffany's to dinner we ate a light lunch in studio — Misses Parker, Culbertson & others called — at 3, went to N.A. with Bellows to look at the galleries there & judge of the lights for an exhn. of etchings. At 5.30, went to Bishee's (shoemaker) & tried on boots — then to studio & at 6.30 to Louis's, and had dinner — very charming —
at 10, again to studio — to
37th St — about 11 & to bed.
