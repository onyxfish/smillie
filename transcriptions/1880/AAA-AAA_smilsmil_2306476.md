## Left Page

### November, Friday 19, 1880

Pleasant day — more painting stretched. For charcoal sketch.
Painted & commenced a sketch of Mariner's Barnyard at Lake Elizabeth — Dinner at 2 — out of sorts with sore throat & cold so that I did little during the afternoon — Sea in studio at 6.30 — after that to 37 S: and at 8.30 at 128 E. 39 S: miss Henrietta Beebe's musical party — a rich & enjoyable evening. Met Miss Spence the Reader & was much pleased — Midnight when I got to 37 S: — To bed at once —

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 20, 1880

Horrible rainy day — was not able to get out of my bed with wretched headache & cold — did not get up until 8 — then had some gruel — lay upon sofa & sat in big chair until about 4 o'clk A.M. — then to bed. Brilliant, cold night.
