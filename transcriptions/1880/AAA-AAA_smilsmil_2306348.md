## Left Page

### March, Saturday 6. 1880.

Pleasant day — got to studio at 9.30 — fussed with etching pfte then put "effect" into A.B.N. Co. eng't "Nice good + [illegible] nine". Dinner at 2. No particular work accomplished in the afternoon — went to 37' St at 5 & dressed — then to Chas. S. Smith's — 47' St — at 6 & dined — at 9, his coupé came & we rode down to the Century — then until midnight — then to 37' St & to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 7. 1880.

Overcast w/f. up late + had b[reakfast] at 10 — then went to Anna Cook's & took her to Century to see the pictures — out at 12.30 caught in a mizzle — went to studio — got umbrella — A.S. went home with it & I went to wash. Wilson's — Dinner at 1.30 — left at 3.30 — went to 37' St — on my bed & slept until 6 — then to A.S.N's — raining hard — took tea there — Kate & Charl. there — also with Charl. went to Will's there until 10.30. Then walked to 37' St & to bed at 11.30.
