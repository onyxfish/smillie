## Left Page

### February, Sunday 15, 1880

Beastley day — soft, slushy snow — B[illegible] at 10 — abt 11
at Dr. Hastings — Jab. 35¢, 10v.
after service went to Mr.
Hertzel's to dinner — Mr.
Carmen of Manhattan there.
at 4 — to studio — about
6 Mr. Colman, then Florence
Grey came in — Geo + I
took tea at the Colmans.
I stayed until 9 — then
to studio, made a letter
home — then to 37! S. +
to bed abt 11.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 16, 1880

Charming spring-like day — got
to studio about 9.45 and to work
on Twilight — 17×30 — Daubed
away, getting nothing but mud
until about 2 when I went
to dinner — after that went
to W.C. [illegible] for a short time
then to Moore's — then walked
about the Park — don't feel
well — got to studio about
3.30 — the Hertzel's called —
out for a short walk after 6.
Tea at 7 — About 9 went to
W.C. Salon + met several friends
walked [illegible] fifth ave. with Mr.
+ Mrs [illegible] (Delia Black)
to 37! S. + to bed before 11.
