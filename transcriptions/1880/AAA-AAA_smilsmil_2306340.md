## Left Page

### February, Thursday 19. 1880.

Windy & cold — got to studio at 9.30 & to work in a very leisurely way on 16×21 — until 2 when I went to dinner — a short walk — then back & soon after 4 completed the laying in — washed up & at 5 went to 37 S. dressed & at 6.30 went to Cook's & excused myself — then to the Putnam's — 144 E. 60th S — to dinner — pleasant evening until 9.15 — then I walked down to Chas. Isham's. 5th Ave & 33rd S — Reception to the Tile Club & friends — a jolly affair until midnight — then to 37 S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 20. 1880.

Fine winter day — up as usual — got to studio at 9.30 but felt unpleasantly the effect of last night's dissipation — Spent the morning very quietly & had lunch in the studio at 1 o'clk — then to Phil. Rehearsal — not in any mood to enjoy it — walked up town with Charl — to W.C. [illegible] then to studio — on lounge until 4 — tea at 4.30 — Perf in studio at odd jobs — To bed about 11.
