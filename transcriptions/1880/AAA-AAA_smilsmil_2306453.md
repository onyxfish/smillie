## Left Page

### October, Monday 4, 1880.

Uncertain weather — mild but hazy + in the afternoon strong wind — At 10 went to Long wharf + worked away at "Rear of No. 1 Portland wharf — Wm Shea's boathouse + little John + Tom's Junk shop — under difficulties, boats coming in + discharging cargoes between me + my object — worked until near 1, then to dinner, + after a rest again at it, altho' there was no direct sunlight — The tide was running out + by 4 o'clk. the sewer stench was unbearable, then I went out riding with Malcolm Hammond to Cape Cottage — Tea at 6.30 — At 8 I went to see the Stewart family in their Rainbow Revels — met Hammond there and sat beside him — Geo. joined us later + we walked up to H's house, then back to hotel + to bed soon after 11 — a slight shower then.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 5, 1880.

Portsmouth, N.H. Rockingham St°  Room 26.

Overcast + raw — Bkfst at 8.30 — after that a short walk, then to work in my Room on my W.C. sketches, finishing them up a little — at noon took my "little Diamond Island" sketch and left + with Davis — Dinner at 1 — most of the P.M. in my Room — Resting + packing trunk. About 5 walked to Observatory at the Eastern end of Town + had a splendid view of White Mts. + out to sea — back to hotel + at 5.45 left for Train — at 6 off for Portsmouth where we arr'd at 7.50 — went at once to the Rockingham, a remarkably nice house + had given us a charming parlor + two bed rooms off it — had tea at 8.30 — walked awhile + to bed soon after 10.
