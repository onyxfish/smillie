## Left Page

### August, Saturday 7. 1880.

Another delicious day — Very tired after yesterday m[illegible] pull — not muscularly but in a nervous way. After b[illegible]; made notes to Ehninger + Gel. Then went to the Kimball cottage + called on Mr. K — then climbed to top of Rock south of the house — + about 12, pulled over to the Harbor Islands — back to dinner at 1.15 — About 3.30 again pulled to the islands and prospected for studies until near 6 — then home + had tea at 6.45 — About 8, Hddie + I pulled in canoe to Hulett's. Mr & Mrs. Dey + Miss Burtons went in another boat — me were very much pleased with the reading given in dining room of house by Miss Bant — Some good bass singing by Mr. Hicocks. Lovely pull home after 10. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 8. 1880.

Very warm but with a strong, tempering breeze. About 9.30, after b[illegible] + singing, started for Bl[illegible] Mt. P. Wind dead ahead — hard pulling. Stopped an hour among the floating Battery Islands — + pulled an hour + a half before I got to Black Mt. Point — Then sat for some time under the trees with Marion Butler — then on board the Minnehaha — Hulk Cyrus B's sister + two other ladies there (one Miss [illegible] of Ithica) + Mr. Sears the architect — Dinner at 3 — as the P.M. was very warm we did not leave the boat until after sun-down — then we went up to the house that is being built — at 7.15 I left + pulled back in 50 minutes — lovely twilight — getting home I heard singing + pulled after the Terry girls — then to the house + at near 9 had something to eat then sat chatting until 10.30 — then to bed.
