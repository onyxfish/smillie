## Left Page

### October, Sunday 24. 1880

Very breezy, cool, day — B[illegible]
between 9 & 10 — then found
that I had "a head on me"
— went back to my room &
altho' invited to A.V.S.'s to dinn
I lay rolled up in my blank
until after 4 — then for a
few minutes I was at Anna
C's — & then on up to A.V.S.'s
where I took tea — Kate is
there — at 8 walked down to
the Buckingham & spent
the rest of the eveg. with
Will in his new rooms —
Chare came in — we left
at 10.30 — I walked to 39th
S[t]. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 25. 1880

Very cold, windy m[or]n. B[illegible]
at 8.30 — then to studio — found
it as cold as a barn — most
of the m[or]n. spent trying to
get it warm — commenced
a small charcoal sketch
for an ill. for A.V.S. Anthony.
Dinner at near 2 — after that
went to Knoedlers to see pictures
— then again to studio & part
of p.m. was spent visiting.
At 6 went to Anna C.'s to
dinner — at 8 we walked up
town & on our way back stopped
into Thomas's Garden Concert —
out at about 10 — another short
walk & I left her at her home —
met a political Torch Light Pro-
cession — Tramped some time
with that — near midnight when
I got to bed & about 5 a.m.
when I got to sleep.
