## Left Page

### May, Monday 31, 1880

Someday after yesterday's rain — Geo. started for the Delaware Water Gap with Mr. Dod at 8.30 this m'f. I got to studio before 9 and during the day packed and to get away for the summer, — painted some sheep into the picture ("October Shadows") Had lunch about 2 — among other things, during the afternoon, made a charcoal sketch for page of illustrations of Mr. Desert. Tea at 7 — then rode up to St. Gale's — then to H.W. Robbins — not in — then to Mrs. G.P. Putnam's 144 E. 60 St. where I spent the rest of the eveg. then walked down to 37 S. & to bed soon after 11 —

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 1, 1880

Very pleasant m'f. After Mp: sat in Mad Sq. & read newspaper for half an hour — at 9.30 got to studio — to work at my printing — Farrer came between 10 & 11 — worked with me until near 1 — altho' he did not do what he wished I got some valuable hints — to dinner at 2 — back at 3 & until near 7 at work at my proving, getting the best pp. that I have yet taken from "Old Cedars — Coast of Maine" — tea at 7.45 — after that went to the Kimball's — there until 10.15 — then to 37 S. & to bed about 11 —

Heavy shower with thunder about 4 P.m. the air then very much cooler.
