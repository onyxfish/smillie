## Left Page

### April, Monday 19. 1880.

Disagreeable day — cold easterly winds with some drizzling rain — Got to studio at 10 after leaving some arbutus at Fanny's — Soon after arrival — Edith's night [illegible] her friend came & I went to N.A. with them & through the schools — Back to studio & made reduction tracing 1" "Old Cedars on the Coast of Maine" preparatory to etching — Lunch at 2.30, after that a short walk. — Suite work during the rest of afternoon — bits up on zinc plate & transferred "Old Cedars", Tea at 7 — & at 8.30 went to "The Berkley" 20 Fifth Ave — & saw Ehninger who insists that I shall go to Saratoga with him to-morrow — To N.A. at 9.45 — thien to 37 [illegible] & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 20. 1880.

Raw driving wind in the early mrf — changing to Rain after 4 P.m. — cleared off charmingly — Got to studio about 9.30 & commenced etching & biting "Old Cedars." To dinner at 2.45. & then again at my etching — about 4 found that my ground was not biting to such an extent that I cleaned my plate & took 3 proofs — Rather surprised us about 6 p.m. — He took tea with us & in the evef I went to N.A. with him — theres until 10 o'clk — then he went to Well's and I went to 37 [illegible] & to bed about 11.
