## Left Page

### February, Saturday 7, 1880

Pleasant day with a heavy snow flurry before 12 m —
Got to Studio at 9.30. Set palette for the first time
this winter & painted in a sketch of what I hope
to make my Academy picture. To dinner at 2 — after that
to W.C. [illegible] & met May Whitney —
then again to my work and painted as long as I could
see — Tea at 7 — [illegible]
went to Canteen — there
until 11 — then to 37. St.
& to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 8, 1880

Delightful crisp, winter-like day. Up late with a
mean headache — [illegible] at
10 — then I went to Studio
& spent the m'f on [illegible].
At 3 went to Anna Cook's
& took her to Canteen — there
for a few minutes in to
W.C. [illegible]. Home with
her at 5 — then to W. Thos.
Hunt's — 116 E. 39. S. — where
at 6 we had dinner with
Misses Whitney & Redfield —
Mr. Livingston — Mr. & Mrs. Triss
4 —. A very pleasant
evening until 10 — then to
Studio. Then to 37. St. and
to bed at 11.45.
