## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 25. 1880.

Slightly overcast, pleasant day — got to studio about 10
& spent the m'f attending to callers & finishing up my etching article — Coleman,
Gifford & Waller called, and I was asked to join a committee (sub) to arrange the opening exh'm of Met. Museum of Fine Arts.
To dinner at 1.30 & at 2.30 went down to Harpers — Saw Conant who expressed himself very much pleased with my effort — then to A.B.N. Co. There with Will until after 5 — Rode up to 23rd St. with him — then to studio — tea at 7. Went in studio until 9 — then to N.A. — there until 10 — then to 37th St. & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 26. 1880.

Very mild, overcast & rainy — got to studio before 10 & to work on Hail Columbia — making changes —
To dinner at 2. & at 3 drew from a model for the figure until 4 — Several callers —
At 6 went to Kimball's — was caught in a tremendous shower & got quite a ducking — after tea went with Fanny to Arnold's — left her there &
ret'd to studio where I worked until near 10 on Water Color [illegible] figures —
Then to 37th St. by tenderest moonlight & to bed at 11 —
Kate called about noon.

Geo. Hawes died last night after a short illness — Typhoid Pneumonia —
