## Left Page

### September, Sunday 26, 1880

Windy day with threats of
change — Late breakfast —
After 10, Anna, Emma, Helen
& I started out for a walk.
— much loitering — went to
the Cascade & on our way back
to the woods near Foster's brook
back. at 1.30 — dinner about
2 — after that Read for an
hour or so — then Mr. Jeliffe
came & spent an hour or
two with us — After 6 Anna
Emma & I went out for our
last Row together this season —
breezy with rough water
but it was charming — Tea
about 7 — Busy much cut
up with preparations to
leave to-morrow m'g.
near midnight when I got
to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 27, 1880

*American House, Burlington — Room 40*

Up at about 6 — Showers — finished
packing my trunk before breakfast.
After that, with the girls, Ran to the
Jemps & Kimballs & bid hurried good
byes — Soon after we got off in the
"Kitcup" (Rowboat) Strong wind, heavy
sea & showers — quite tried and
charming. Waited for some time
for the Garouskie & at Baldwin
waited some time for the locomotive
& at Tés, some time for train and
Steamboat — The girls & Mr. White
got away at 4 & immediately after
I got away on the "Vermont" — had
dinner — Showers & charming skies —
enjoyed the sail — Burlington
at 5 — found that I had to stay
there all night — went to hotel —
then out walking & made sketch
from the hill — Tea at 6.30 — at
7.30 had a boy take me to the
Residence of Hon. E. J. Rikes where I
spent a pleasant eve'g. back to
hotel at 9.30 & to bed soon after 10.
