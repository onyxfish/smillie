## Left Page

### October, Thursday 28, 1880

Overcast but mild. Bkfst
at near 9 — 10 when I got
to studio & to work upon
drawing for Anthony — all
the mrg at it & then thought
that I had it about finished.
Near 2 when I went to dinner.
Painted another sketch for
Anthony & at 3.30 went to Grand
Central depot & took 4 P.m.
train for P'k. getting there
at 6.30 — Right home and
found Father & Mother alone —
had tea — spent the evening
chatting & got to bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 29, 1880

Mild, nearly overcast & perfectly
quiet day — the promise seeming
to be a snow rather than rain.
Bkfst. after 8 — after that I
spent most of the mrg writing
Constitution & minutes of Etching
club — a note to A.C.S. etc, etc.
At 12 went to P.O. & to Matt's
back to dinner at 1 — after
that made a couple of notes,
took them to P.O — then to the
Opera Ho. & heard Col. Ingersoll
make a speech — tremendous
crowd. Home about 4.30 and
did more Etching club writing.
Mary came bet. 5 & 6 & I went
home with her — Back to tea
at 6.30 — Most of the eveng
spent writing up Etching club
Constitution. To bed at about
10.30.
