## Left Page

### April, Friday 23, 1880

Pleasant day altho' a little dubious in the afternoon.
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on painting "Calpe Porpoise
Coast of Maine" — painting over the foreground principally.
Mr. Forbes called. — At 1 o'clk. I had lunch & at 2
at Phil. Rehearsal — the last of the season — Matt & Kate
there, just down from Po' R.
After Rehearsal, again to studio & worked until 6.30 — then to the
K's where the girls are visiting & had tea — Soon after 8 — all went
to Mrs. Saml. Colman's where the [illegible] were to play — Anna C.
not coming, Mrs. H[illegible] took her place. Near 11 when we left
— then it was raining —
To bed about midnight.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 24, 1880

Disagreeable day with some rain — To studio between 9 &
10 & to work painting on "A Kennebunkport Veterans" — I
managed to improve it, I think. — Dinner as usual
at 2 — Painted also on Calpe Porpoise in the afternoon.
At 6.30 to the K's & had tea
in the evg. went to Phil.
concert. Nasty rain — Grand
concert — Tremendous house —
after concert pleasant evening — Took Anna C. home
to bed before 12.
