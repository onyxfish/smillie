## Left Page

### July, Friday 30, 1880

Charming day — up late — went to Empire Spring for
my [illegible]. B[illegible] at 9 — all day fighting a g.s. head ache
working on W. C. Sketch all the m[illegible] + coaching E. with
his etching. Lunch at 1.
Afternoon, gave up to my headache + lay up on my
lounge until near 5 — then up to dinner — at 6 drove
out to the lake to Mr. Durke's log cabin — met [illegible] there —
B. Rowed us in his Adirondack boat on the Kind of Rhinoceros
River — very lovely bits —
back to town at 8.30. I was dropped at the Park. Fire
works, illumination + music — quite a spectacle — home
soon after 10 + to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 31, 1880

Hot day — at Congress Park by 8 —
B[illegible] at 9 — all the m[illegible] in studio
E. biting 2nd plate of "Gathering
Water Lilies" + I finishing the
W. C. sketch for Mr. E. Lunch
at 1 — Caly. Mr. Hair called — went
to R.R. Station + met Char[illegible] just
from P[illegible]. on the 2.25 train — to
U.S. with him — met Mollie
Shaffer — back to E's before
4 — E. had finished biting and
was preparing to take a proof.
Dinner at 6 — Char[illegible] came —
after dinner on the [illegible]. Mr.
\+ Mrs. Hall called + Col. Balch
\+ wife — at 9 W. + Mrs. E. + I
went to U.S. There until 11.
I went in to the Hotel — met
lots of people that I knew
\+ had quite a gay time.
Home soon after 11 + right
to bed.
