## Left Page

### September, Friday 10, 1880

Considerable rain during the night. + more rain during the morning, all of which is precious. After a late bkfs. I got to work doing some re-painting on my ups right study of pines — About 12, in a gentle rain, Addie, Anna, Emma, Helen + I went out rowing for an hour. Dinner at 1.30 — After that, finished work on my study — weather clearing — we all went out again about 4.30 and rowed about among the Harbor Islands + up to Deer's leap — the water like oil + a tender atmosphere over everything — In at 6.15 — Tea at near 7 — Evening in chatting, whist + tricks by Arthur until 11, then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 11, 1880

A charming day, cloudless + fresh. The smoke has almost disappeared after bkfs. Anna + I rowed over to Harbor Islands where I spent the mf. painting on study of Blt. Mt. from the channel. — Back to dinner at 1.15. Between 2 & 3 I started out again and met Cyrus Butler's steamboat, the "Rambler." Miss Stebbins, Searce + others were on board — I was picked up and taken to the Floating Battery Islands where the rest of the party were pic-nicking — Mr. Chase was one of the number — good time until near 6 when I was taken home. met the girls out in the "snark." I was unhappy again because of a return of my dreaded pain — Tea about 7 — Evng. divided between whist + reading — To bed at about 11.
