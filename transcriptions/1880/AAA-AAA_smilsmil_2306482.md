## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 1, 1880

Metched day — about 2 in of snow here last night, the rain & the rain continued to fall most of the m[or]f, making an awful slush. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work painting on 33×44 — Piled on the paint until near 2 — then to dinner & after that a walk — then again at work as long as I could see — After 5 went to Y.M.C.A. Gymnasium for a few minutes, then down to 10th St. & stopped at Cutting — to studio & had tea at 7 — after that wrote to father & to Mary. Then went up to the Buckingham & saw Will & his family — there until 10.30 — thru to 37th St. & to bed — am after 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 2, 1880

Pleasant day — to studio at 9.30 & to work painting cow & figure — I mick maid in "Weing" 33×44. — Marie Butler called — out to dinner at 2 — after that a short walk — back & finished up painting — to Y.M.C.A. Gymnasium for a few minutes — then to 37th St. & at 6.30 to A.C.C.'s — Tirnra N.S. joined our Thurs. evef. dinner parties — at 8 went with the girls to the Arnold's — left them there — called at the Payne's — not in — at Thos. Hunt's — not in — Henrietta Beebe's — not in — then to Flora Grey's — she was in bed, so chatted with her mother until 9.30 — then to studio & thought about things until 10.30 — then to 37th St. & to bed after 11.
