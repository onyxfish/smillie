## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 11. 1880.

Shower in the mf — then cleared, very warm — Got to studio som after 9 and to work on Water Color sketch. Worked on sketch commenced a year ago on top of Palisades. To dinner at 2 — worked on sketch made on Saturday — went out for a walk through in Mad. Square. — then back to Studio & had tea — busy until 10.30 mounting W.C. sketches — then to 37 S. & to bed.

At 5 P.m. called on Miss Rose Durfee at Grand Hotel on Water Color business — She sails for Syracuse to-morrow mf.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 12. 1880.

Cooler & pleasant day — got to studio som after 9 & to work on W.C. sketches cleaning up frames & getting them ready to send to Water League. Rossell came about 1 o'clk & I gave him five — these to N.A. where I had lunch — then annual meeting which dragged along until after 6 — then we sat down to dinner — a pleasant affair to me, as I had some good company. [illegible] missed being made an N.A. by just 2 votes — to 37 S. at 11 & then to bed.
