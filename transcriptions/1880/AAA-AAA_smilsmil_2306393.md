## Left Page

### June, Friday 4, 1880

In bed all day with a headache — Got up at about
6 p.m. — & got something to eat from the girls — At a
little after 8 went to the Windsor Hotel & called on
Miss Sara T. Hallowee — at 10.30 went to Charl's —
his Birth night — found there Will, Joe, Neum, Mary
Schaeffer — Walter Pierce —
Miles Palmer &c. There
until about 11.30 then to
37 St. & read newspaper
until 1 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 5, 1880

Pleasant day — overcast during
latter part of afternoon with
some rain at night. Got to
studio about 9 but could do
nothing — tried to arrange
things for getting away for the
summer — most of the m'f
was spent writing letters — got
Chiungsi's pkg. of p[illegible] off to him.
Dinner at 2 — During p.m.
tried to make a little figure
sketch for etching — failed — then
'sorted' things — tea in studio
at 7.30 — at 8.15 went in to S.
Colman's — there until near
10 — then to Century — there
until 11.30 — walked up town
in light shower with J. G.
Brown & got to bed about
midnight.
