## Left Page

### July, Thursday 8. 1880.

Lovely day, altho' very warm. The mttf. was spent etching
& biting some extra bites on "The way to the River". Dinner
before 1 & at about 2. I got to work proving and
testing some of the samples of Jap. paper recently sent
to me. Worked hard until near 6 & got just
half a dozen pfs. Tea at 6.30 — then a rest — then
wet down paper for to-morrow — at 8.15 went up
town — to P.O. & to hardware store where I got zinc for
printing press — then to Mary's — there until
nearly 9.30 — then home & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 9. 1880.

Warm day — I took things easy — ground wht & got
to work proving "The way to the River". Faking about
half a dozen before dinner at 2.30 — In the afternoon
altho' out of sorts, I kept on printing until 5 when I
washed up — enjoyed a regular grooming & change
of clothes & had tea shortly after 6 — then wet down
paper for to-morrow — began to feel decidedly ill & after
an hour or more of misery my stomach relieved itself
— I finally got to bed at about 10.30 & passed a
very restless night.
