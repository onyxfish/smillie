## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 22, 1880

Cool, overcast, charming — up at 7 — [illegible] about 8 to soon after out to ride with misses Emma B — Bullard and Barnum — Mr. B + Gel out + about all the m[orning]. Dinner at 1 — after that until near 5 on piazza or trying my hand at archery — Mr. Col Beecher made a call — all out for a short walk — tea at 6.30 — then all hands went to the high ground to see the sunset + the moon rise — back about 8 — beautiful moonlight. The bal[ance] of the even[ing] on the piazza + to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 23, 1880

Another clear warm day — B[reakfast] about 9 — after that, horses and wagon at the door + much of the m[orning] was spent riding about over the grounds + through the woods — for an hour or more in Ella's little house reading aloud + chatting — Dinner between 1 + 2 — the afternoon was spent on the piazza + near the house riding. Mr. Beach came up from N.Y. Tea about 6 — did not feel well — after tea, with Mr. B — + two wagons we went across Mr. Carhart's + to "Cedar Lane" — delicious eve[ning]. Back at about 9 — the rest of even[ing] on Piazza — To bed after 11. Later in the afternoon I drove by the Dog Cart over the [illegible] + around to Mr. Beecher's.
