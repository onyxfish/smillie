## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 20. 1880.

A very delightful day. Bkfp. before 8 o'clk. Father went in to the city at about 10 — I spent most of the m'f in Uncle G's chair having impressions of my mouth in wax & plaster — at 12 went to the Sweedy's — stayed to lunch & had a very good time until I went to the depot — met Mother & Kate — all took 3.51 train into N.Y. & got up to A.V.V's at about 6. found father there — had dinner at about 7 — at 8.30 I walked down to 37th S. found my trunk there — unpacked it — George came in at a little after 10 — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 21. 1880.

Overcast, threatening but not unpleasant — got up with a mean headache & fought against it all day — Bkfp. at 8.30 — then took a lot of my things from the house to the studio — the morning spent making some calls and arranging things — a light lunch near 2 o'clk. much of the rest of the afternoon I was on the lounge — could not take any supper — at 7 went in to Colman's — there for an hour — then to studio — waited about there until 10 — then to 37th S. & to bed before 11.
