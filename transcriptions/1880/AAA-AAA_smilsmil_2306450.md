## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 28, 1880

*Preble Ho. Portland. Me. Room 49*

Up at 4.30 after a good night's rest —
no breakfast — got to depot at 5.10
then rain began — dismal & dark —
Train started at 5.15 — Three passengers
besides myself — I rolled myself in
my shawl & slept — At 7.45 got to
Cambridge Junction & made a
close connection on P.&O. R.R. Rain
fell heavily until about 10 — from
St. Johnsbury to Faybians, pleasant —
got to F— at 1.15 & waited 40 m. for
dinner — Mt. Washington heavily cov-
ered & dense mists & Rain in the
notch where there was wonderfully
bright autumn color — dreary ride
the rest of the way — Portland at 6 —
to Preble Ho. & found Gel — Tea at 7 —
at 8 went to Mr. Cyrus Davis's —
there until 9.30 — then to hotel
& to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 29, 1880

Charming day. Bkfst. about 8
& the rest of morn was spent
running around, most of the
time about the docks with
Mr. Hughes. I found some very
picturesque material. Back
to Hotel at 12 — had hair cut
& sat down to dinner a little
before 1 — at 2, Hughes, Gel &
I went over to Cape Elizabeth
where I made a W.C. sketch
of two old boats up on a mud
bank — Splendid sun set —
back to hotel at 6 & at 6.30
had tea — in my room until
about 9, then to Cyrus Davis's
room — Gel & Hughes there —
back to hotel at 10. Then
wrote letters — among others,
one to A.C.C. — to bed shortly
after midnight.
