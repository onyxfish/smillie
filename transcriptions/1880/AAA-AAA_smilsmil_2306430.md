## Left Page

### August, Thursday 19, 1880

Rain at last, but it continued only an hour or two, from 6 to 8
A.M. During the rest of the mtt it gradually cleared
away — After blls, I wrote
to Saml. Coleman + part of a
letter to Harry Fenn — Dinner
abt 1.30 — Read awhile + at
about 3 climbed the mtt.
back of the house — had a
hard time, loose rocks,
fallen trees + steep incline.
Got a very good view over
the Floating Battery Islands
to Tongue Mt. Back at 6 —
Tea soon after — at 8 Rowed
up to Herlitt's with Arthur Harns
Miss Del Jenny + Miss Weiner —
Addie, Miss B + the boy in the
other boat. Much wind + some
Rain — Heard Miss Spence +
another lady read — Returned
\+ to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### August, Friday 20, 1880

No Rain altho' it promised
well during the early mtt.
About 9.30 I started out with
water colors to make a sketch
at a slide about ½ a mile south
— bothered for nearly an hour
trying to find a place where
my boat would lie quiet, at
last gave it up + pulled to the
Floating Battery Islands where I
made some pencil sketches,
then across to Shadow Bay +
then along pull home — with
many wonderfully beautiful
effects of sunlight + cloud shadow.
After dinner went to Harbor Island
\+ completed painting in upright
pine trees — Spattering Rain — Tea
abt near 4 — Mrs. Weiner + Jewel
am— Evett spent on Piazza —
beautiful moonlight, later
clouded over — To bed at
about 10.30.
