## Left Page

### May, Monday 17. 1880.

Tremendously hot day —
got to studio at 9 + to
work trying to fake some
pts from "Red Cedars" but
I cannot get what I
want — out to dinner at
2.30 — did not wash up
until after 6 — then very
tired — tried to get to
Robt. Gordon's to make my
dinner call — but could
not get ready — went in
to N.A. for a few minutes —
To 37° S— + to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 18. 1880.

Pleasant day — not so hot as
yesterday — at [illegible] — went
down town — for some time
a C.B.N. Co. On my way up
stopped at Dewey + Pomroy's
got to studio after 1 — at 2
went out to lunch — then over
to Goupil's — then back + spent
the p.m. working over drawing
of Hail Columbia — Wm. Titus,
Dare + daughter of Chicago +
Miss Hallowell called — I was
so much engaged that I did
not get tea until 8 — at
about 9 went in to Coleman's
where Emma, Anna, Fanny +
Mr. Coleman played quartetts
until after 10. Night — chilly
+ threatening rain which
is much needed.
