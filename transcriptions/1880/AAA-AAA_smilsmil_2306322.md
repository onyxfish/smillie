## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 14, 1880

Mild pleasant day — did not get up until after 11. Then to studio, stopping to call on Miss Durfee. I went to lunch at 1 — finished pen sketch for Salmagundi club efftu — wrote letters & rested — Chas. Parsons made a long call & Nicoll came in. Tea after 4 — Evelif in studio writing letters — to 37 S— between 10 & 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 15, 1880

Mild, but raw. Weather overcast in the afternoon & wet at night. My cold still needs possession. Fine — had a late [illegible]. Got to studio about 10. Spent nearly a couple of hours over a pen sketch after Nicoll's picture, for the N.S. Catalogue — then to work on my big drawing — out to dinner at 2 — back & worked until near 5 — then a rest & at 6.15 went to 37 S—. To Anna C's at 6.45 — Geo. went with A & F. to Miss Arnold's. I stayed & spent the evef in a game of letters with Jas. C— his brother — to bed at 10.45.
