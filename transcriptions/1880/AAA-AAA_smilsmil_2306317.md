## Left Page

### January, Sunday 4, 1880

Part of the day overcast &
disagreeably raw — went
to St. Cloud for bkfst. bet. 9 &
10 — then to Dr Hastings
& heard a sermon on "forgetting
the things of the past & reaching
forward to the things that
are before" — After service to
studio — to dinner at Ashland
at 2 — then to studio —
wrote letters to Koehler &
Roberts Bros. at 5 went to
Emma D's to ask her to go
with me to see the "Pirates
of Penzance" on Friday even —
then to Colman's & at 6 ¼
to the Jacobs' where I took
tea — there until after 9 —
then again to studio —
to 34° S. & to bed at 11 but
not to sleep until after
1 o'clk.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 5, 1880

Very pleasant day. After a
late & poor bkfst. at Parker's
Rest. to studio about 10 — Had
a number of callers during
the day so that I was barely
able to finish pen sketch
after Couture — Dinner at 2
worked all long as I could
see & finished up by gas
light. At 6 went to [illegible]
Co. with pkg. for Koehler &
Roberts Bros. then a walk.
Tea at 7 — part of even. in
Colman's house — then in
studio again & at 10.30 to
37° S. & to bed.
