## Left Page

### July, Thursday 22, 1880

Mild, pleasant with rain
beginning to fall before noon
& continuing quite steadily
during afternoon & evening. Went
to Sunrise Spring before half past —
After that spent the m[orning] in
studio, E. sticking closely to
his work, etching girls gathering
lilies — Mr. Thos. Buckley (Eastman
Johnston's bro. in-law) took lunch
& dinner with us — during the
afternoon I bit in ahead that
E. had etched — out for a short
walk in the rain, then had
dinner at 6 — after that, whist
until 9 o'clk — then chatting
with E. until near 10.30 —
then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 23, 1880

Pleasant day, partially overcast —
out for a walk at 8. Bkft. at 9
after that in studio biting in the
etching of "Gathering Water Lilies"
— closely at work until lunch
at [illegible] then again at work,
finishing the biting & taking
off before 5 o'clk — then out
for a few minutes walk and
dinner at 6 — after that Mr.
& Mrs. E. & I walked to the Grand
Union Hotel & with H.H. Bern[?]
sat on the piazza listening
to the music & watching the
effects of light upon fountains
until 9 — then to H. S. Hotel &
called on Mr. Beach & Miss
Morgan also on piazza &
with music — near 11
when we got home, then
to bed.
