## Left Page

### September, Saturday 4, 1880

Charming day with beautiful
cloud effects — but not feeling
like work — it was after 10 o'clk.
before I got started on a little
sketch from the summer home
near the pier — I tried to
work without a fritte — Dums
after 1 — & after that I started
another sketch from the
rocks near the pier, looking
South, much trouble and
finally gave it up to go with
Arthur Hawes in his sail boat
across the lake to bathe — went
to a fine place & had a glorious
view of east side mts. in evening
light — reflected in smooth water —
Tea at about 6.30 — Evening
chatting, reading & card playing
until after 10 — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 5, 1880

Extremely hot day — about 10 o'clk
took my boat over to little channel
Harbor Islands, moored it in a
shaded spot & made a letter to
Koehler — about 12, rowed to the
place where we bathed yesterday
& had another little swim, then
back to dinner at about 2 —
During the afternoon suffered
very much from the heat, but
wrote a letter to Father — The
forest fires are coming near to
us — this m'f, the fire that has
been burning for some time upon
the western side of the mt. opposite
us came over the summit —
Tea as usual — Evelf on the
water with Addie — a marvelous
night — without cloud or ripple
— in before 10 — To bed at 10.30.
