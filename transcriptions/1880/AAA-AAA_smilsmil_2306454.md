## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 6. 1880.

Splendid day — Bkfs — soon after 8 — about 9, called on St. Wingate — He walked about town with us & then over to the Navy Yard — back at 1 o'clk. & had dinner — after that a rest and at 3 St. W. came with horses & carriage & his wife and we had a delightful afternoon riding about town, out to the Wentworth Ht. at Little Harbor, where we spent nearly an hour & then to the beach where, just as the sun was going down, we drove along the sand & saw some wonderful effects of color — Back to hotel at 6 & had tea at near 7 — W. made us a short call — then read & wrote until 11 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### Parker House, Boston —

### October, Thursday 7. Room 1880. 219.

Brilliant day, but windy — Bkfs — at 8 — about 9, called on Wingate & then went out by the South Pond sketching — battered by the wind. Back to Hotel at 10.30 — then to R.R. Station — train left at 11 — we got to Boston without incident at 1.30 — then to Parker House & had dinner soon after 2 — after that went to Estes & Lauriat — Koehler not in — then to Osgood's saw him for a few minutes, then again to K's & found him with Simton — there until after 3 — then to Parkerh.? & rested until 7 — then tea — after 8 went to 92 Mt. Vernon St. to call on Kitty'd — not in — wandered about the streets until after 9.30 — then back & to bed at 10.30.
