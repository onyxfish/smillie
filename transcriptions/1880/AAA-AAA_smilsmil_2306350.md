## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 10, 1880

Pleasant day — to studio at 9.30 & to work on 16×21. The
Patient milkmaid", Repainting
part of middle distance &
much of the foreground —
think it improved. dinner
at 2 — worked until after
5 — out for a walk — cold
raw & threatening a storm —
went in to Colman's for
awhile — tea after 7 —
at 8.30 went to Will's where
father & mother now are &
stayed there until after 10
then to 37 S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 11, 1880

Heavy snow storm all day
just like mid winter — got to
studio at 9.30 — Spent the
morning laying etching grounds
to dinner at 2 — then to Pike's
to select glass & stand for my
etching work — back to studio
& varnished plates etc etc
— at 6.30 went to Anna C's —
at 8 — to Arnold's with A.C.
& T.K. then to 37 S., dressed
& soon after went to Mr. Pott,
Gordon's — of 2,38 S. Annual
Reception to artists. very pleasant
evening — there until 11.30 — then
to 37 S. & to bed soon after
midnight.
