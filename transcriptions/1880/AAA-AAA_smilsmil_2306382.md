## Left Page

### May, Thursday 13, 1880

Crisp, cool day — on getting to studio, kindled fire —
didn't feel much like work — Miss Hallomel
called & I spent most of the mf with her — out to
dinner at 2 — back after a short walk & then to
work on W.C. 10×14 — an every effect — worked
until after 6 — at [illegible] out for a walk — Tea in studio
— Evef, wrote letters etc
& at 10 o'clk, went to Union League Club — water color
246[illegible] — very poor — to 37°
S. about 11 & to bed ab—
11.35.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 14, 1880

Cool, cloudy day — Up with a headache — got to studio
at 9.30 & to work on 10×14
Evening — W.C. Stuck to it
until near 2 — then out for lunch — then a short walk
& back to my work & again was diligent until after 6 —
feeling out of sorts I did not go out for a walk but
took a nap in my big chair
Had tea about 8 — Rec'd a
telegram from Ehisweger at
Saratoga letting me off from
the visit I promised him
next week — wrote letters
until 10 o'clk, then to 37°S.
& to bed soon after 11.
