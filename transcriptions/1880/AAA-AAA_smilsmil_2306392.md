## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 2. 1880.

Cool, overcast day — Spent the m'f. Faking 6 large paper [illegible] from "Old Cedars" — then out to dinner — then to R.E. Moore's — spent some time looking over Christmas Card designs & making pen sketches from my painting "Cape Porpoise" for Chicago Cat. Tea about 7 — after 8 went in to Sam. Colman's — Ned, & Anna Cook came in. I stayed until after 10 — there to 37 S. & to bed abt 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 3. 1880.

Pleasant, April-like day — Got to studio at 9 o'clk. & spent the m'f. packing things preparatory to getting away for the summer. Sent "Old Cedars" to Boston & packed up Ihringer's Etchings — out to dinner at 2 — then to Fanny K's & with Anna C. went to 3.25 Coney Island Boat — there met Emma J. Geo. C. & Will K. Reached Coney Island in about ¾ of an hour & until near 7 wandered about seeing all the side shows — then took dinner at the Hotel
as it was a good dinner & we were hungry & tired we enjoyed it very much — left to N.Y. by last train at 8.20 — I went home with Emma — to 37 S. about 10 & then to bed.
