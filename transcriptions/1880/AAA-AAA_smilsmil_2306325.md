## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 20, 1880

After passing more than three hours of the night trying to get the breath of life through my nostrils & with a throat that felt like live coals, when it came m'f. I could not get up I had a headache & was out of sorts in the stomach so could eat nothing that was prepared — slept a good deal during the day did not get up at night but passed it quietly in bed, awake. Took some cold beef tea about 5 a.m.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 21, 1880

got up feeling tolerably well after my siege of yesterday — Pleasant day & hoped for the best — got to studio about 10 & I did not feel like work I deferred the mounting of my big drawing until afternoon & called in my big chair — immediately after dinner at 2.30, pains commenced in my stomach — with much trouble & George's help I got my drawing mounted — the pains gives called too much pain for me. able to eat a little at 7 — could not go to water Color Society meeting — to my great disappointment — as F. H. Smith had organized a movement to run in Edw. Brown as salesman — I was anxious to oppose him — I got to bed soon after 9 with hot water bag at my stomach but it was inefficacious the pain was unceasing & horrible until 5.30 in the m'f. when being utterly worn out I fell asleep for a couple of hours.
