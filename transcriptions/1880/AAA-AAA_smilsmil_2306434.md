## Left Page

### August, Friday 27, 1880

overcast & high wind — After breakfast
fixed myself at my window
& commenced a sketch of the
view looking to the S.W. At
12 went out & rowed for 3/4 of an
hour — Dinner a little
before 1 as Mr. Meinen & Mrs.
Jewell left in the 2 o'clk boat.
After dinner, bothered by the
weather which began to clear —
had to give up my mf. study
& as the sun shone went to the
Kimball piazza & finished the
painting in a sketch there
very uncomfortably — all put with
get for a short row — Went to a
celebration of Mr. K's birthday
at his cottage. A pleasant
little time — To bed very
soon after 11.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 28, 1880

Bright, breezy mf. Soon after
9, got away to the Harbor Islands
& started a sketch in the narrow
channel looking out to Black
Mt. Painted away until 12.30
but only got in distance & water
& when I got in-doors with it
found it to be very bad. Dinner
at 1.30 — Weather changed in the
afternoon — overcast, windy and
threatening with thunder storms
all about. Again went to Harbor
Islands but could not work on
any study — prowled about & found
subjects for new studies there back
before 5 followed by a slight shower.
The drought is now something terrible.
Tea at about 4 — Went about
walking piazza & playing whist.
Arthur, Barnes & I ago, Addie
& Miss B — To bed at 11 —
very high wind.
