## Left Page

### March, Friday 26, 1880

Delicious day — Got to studio about 9.30 + spent the mf
until about 1 working on etching of Sunnyside and taking pho. of it. Then took to Putnam's + at 2 had dinner — then went to Met. Museum of art + assisted in hanging loan collection of pictures with Coleman, R. Swain Gifford, Waller + J. G. Brown until 6 — then, with Coleman, rode to his house + had dinner, then ret. to Museum + worked until 10 — then to 37t St. + to bed about 11.30, very tired.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 27, 1880

Horrible day — heavy rain +
wind — After bkfs. went to
N.A. of D., Varnishing Day — and
took a hasty look around, then
to Met. Museum — got there
at about 11 — + worked at the
hanging of pictures until 6,
having a lunch served at
12.30 — The collection of picture
is a very fine one and the
American ones hang with the
foreigners much more creditably
than I thought they could.
At 6.30 again rode with Coleman
to his house + had dinner —
after that, so tired that I
did not return to the Museum
but at 9.30 went to 37t St. +
to bed.
