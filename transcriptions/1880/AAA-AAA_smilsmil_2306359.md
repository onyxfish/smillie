## Left Page

### March, Sunday 28, 1880

Heavy, overcast day, with some rain & some snow —
After a good grooming &
bath in the mf., had bkfs.
about 10, then, feeling very
tired I went to studio &
spent the mf. on lounge.
At 12.15 went to Hastings'
where I met Geo. & then we
went to the Hertzels' & had
dinner. At 3.30 returned
to the studio. Rested awhile
wrote a note to Mad. Roch.
& at 6.30 went to the Kimballs'
to tea. There until 8.30
— then to Anna Cooke's —
there until 10.30 & thence
to 37 St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 29, 1880

Still overcast — at times try-
ing to clear up. After bkfs. went
to studio & from there to Met.
Museum, getting there about
10.30 — Helped hang the Hunt
pictures & at 1 returned to
studio — dinner at 2. & at
3.30 went to Grand Central
depot & took 4 o'clk. train
to Po'keepsie — home at 6.45
Tea was served to us in Father's
room — Quite a company
came to celebrate the 48th
anniversary of Father's & Mother's
wedding. Both Father & Mother
were very well & enjoyed the
affair exceedingly. I, being
tired, got to bed early — that is,
before 11 o'clk.
