## Left Page

### June, Friday 18, 1880

Charming day — up rather late & after b'kf't wandered out about the outskirts of town until 10.30 then in to work on block for Harper's Weekly "Mr. Dessert". Dinner at 1 & worked until 4 — then all hands went out for a drive — father, mother, Matt, Kate, Geo., & I — men down the South Road & then back by and over Vassar College — back, a little after 6 & had tea — I was out of sorts with stomach & head and went to bed soon after 9. ## Right Page

### June, Saturday 19, 1880

Very warm day — part of the mg. pleasantly overcast — I was out of doors until after 10, then to work on Harper's block — Dinner at 1 and loafed until near 4 — then to work diligently upon my block until 6.30 — then had tea — At 8.30 Charl., Minn & Mary Schalker & Mary Powell from N.Y., & Aunt Jessie, Emma, Mr. & Mrs. Brewster came from Hillside & made a call until near 9 — then the girls, Geo., Charl. & I went up town & returned at about 10 — to bed about 11 — glorious moonlight —
