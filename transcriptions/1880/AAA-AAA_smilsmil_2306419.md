## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 28, 1880

Beautiful, fresh day — went to
Congress Park at 8 — B'kf't at 9.
after that went down town
with Mr. & Mrs. E. — then took a
carriage & drove for an hour.
Then to U.S. Hotel & left card for
Mollie Shaffer — home soon after
12 — lunched and again went
down town for mounting board
— back & mounted etching &
bossed E. while he "bit" —
Dinner at 5 & at 6 out for a
ride — stopped at Morrisʼ
until a slight shower passed
over — air very cool — Returning,
while opposite the Clarendon
off-horse fell down — excitement
but nobody hurt — mended
harness & got home a little
soon after 8 — the rest of the
even't played whist with
Mr. & Mrs. E. & Mrs. Wayland and
to bed ab't 10.30. Tantrum.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 29, 1880

Cool & pleasant — up early &
got to the Park at 8 o'clk & Frok
my tippee & back to b'kf't at 9.
Morning in studio, divided bet.
Mr. E. & a w.c. sketch that I
have commenced for Mrs. E. —
Mr. Durkee took lunch with us.
Cap't. McNair called — I worked
awhile after lunch & at 3 went
down town — for awhile in the
park listening to music — Shower
threatened — went to U.S. until
it was over — left card for Mollie
Shaffer. back to E's before 6 —
Mrs. Hunt & Mrs. Hall to tea at
6.45 — Even't so cool that fire
was lighted on the hearth —
charming effect. Mr. Sheppard
called — to bed before 11.
