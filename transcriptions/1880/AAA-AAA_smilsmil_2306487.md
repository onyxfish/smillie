## Left Page

### December, Saturday 11, 1880

Much milder & pleasant, but still a cold day — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on "Manners mell." — finished outline & got in first washes on the sky — Out to dinner at 2 — back at 3 & worked for an hour — then I could see no longer. Look at Rest. & at 5.30 [illegible] 37° S. — dressed & then to Annie's at 8 o'clk. at Phil. concert — aside from the concert, had a jolly time with our friends to 38° S. about 11 — then to 37° S. & to bed about 1.30

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 12, 1880

Yet milder — Part of the time overcast, then bright & pleasant — Did not get up until 10.30 — at 11.15 went to Annie's to spend the mng chatting — and took a walk up 5th Ave to the Park & back — dinner at 2.15 — at 3.30 we went to Walter C's in 31st S. & with him. Returned to 38° S. Then I left & went to studio — wrote a letter home — at 6.30 back to Annie's — there until 10.15 — then to 37° S. & to bed —
