## Left Page

### May, Thursday 27. 1880.

Infernally hot day — Ther.
reached 99° in my studio —
Got there about 9 a.m. — in
the m'f. finished Seal [illegible]
the city of New Orleans —
out to dinner at 2 — P.M.
worked on Arms of Montana
Will called & I worked a
little on "Hail Columbia"
out in Mad. Sq. & sat for
some time under the trees —
then, at near 8, went to the
Ashland Ho. & had tea —
after that, sauntered down
to Mrs. Woodbury's, 52 E. 18.
& spent the rest of the ev'g
there — walked to 37 St.
& to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 28. 1880.

Got to studio about 9.30, after
sitting for some time in Mad. Sq.
Day rather cooler than yesterday.
About 4 P.m. a little dash
of a shower — much cooler after
that. M'f. worked on arms
of Montana — finished it about
1 o'clk — To lunch at 2 — then
back — & made pen sketch
for an etching from a sketch
that I got at Irvington "the
way to the River". Out to tea
at 4.
