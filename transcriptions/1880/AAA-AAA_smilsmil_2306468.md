## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 3, 1880

Mild, pleasant day — Bkf. at 8.30 — then to studio — the whole day spent unpacking things. Receiving callers and writing letters — out to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Farrer Bellows & Colman called — much interested in etching & proving baker — at 6 o'clk walked out for half an hour. At 7 had tea in studio — to 37' S. at about 9. Then took possession of my own room on the 4th Story & moved my things up there & got to bed at 10.45.

Jno. Durand called & I promise to go out to Orange next Friday P.M.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 4, 1880

Overcast & mild — some spattering rain came at night. Bkf. at 9 — to studio about 10 & after a while got to work upon Anthony sketch "moms of India" — Had to read us a part of "Lucile." To dinner at 2 — worked on drawing until 4.30 — then I could see no longer — to 37' S. at 5.30 — to Anna C's at 6.30 — first night of Miss Arnold's singing club — Fanny K. & Geo. S. there — after dinner Geo. & I took the girls to Miss A.'s, then we went to the Paynes'. Chase also came in — then I left, Ret. to 37' S. got a big pkg. & carried it to studio — caught in a shower — at 10 Ret. to 37' S. to bed at 11.
