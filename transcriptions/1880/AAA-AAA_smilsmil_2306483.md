## Left Page

### December, Friday 3, 1880

Very pleasant day — 9.30 when I got to studio — no work on charcoal sketch
"A lake George landing Place" Miss Van Horn was in — she was posing for Miss Jacobs —
to dinner at 2 — after a short walk back to studio
& fussed over my sketch as long as I could see, then a walk — Tea in studio at 6.30 — at 7.30 went to 9 E. 22° S. & with Miss Abbott
went to 60 S. & called upon the Putnams — saw Mr. G. P. & Miss Annie — at 9.30 Rel° to 22°. S. with Miss A — then to 37 E. & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 4, 1880

Charming day — to studio a little after 9 — three to Ward's, the tailor — then to Wunderlich's & spent some time there, coming away with several etchings — then to Throedlen's & after 12 to studio again — near 2 when I went out to dinner. Called on Benner & Frost — Johnston — not much more of the day left — that was given up to callers, among them N. T. Oakey — Tea in studio at 7. At 8 went to [illegible] J's with [illegible] my etching "The Way to the River" — there until 9.30 — then to Century — there until near 11 — then to 37 S. Found note from Annie C. C. asking me to call to-morrow at 11 o'clk — To bed after 12.
