## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 8. 1880

Dry, depressing day the smoke from fires all about us & in sight, that we could not see across the lake. I did not feel quite so well as yesterday — tried to paint a little but could not — much of the time on my bed — early dinner — Addie & Arthur H. went to the landing & returned with Anna C. & Walter night — a good part of the afternoon I spent with them — also washed up a lot of brushes — Tea between 6 & 7. the even spent playing whist, Arthur H. & Walter W. ag[ainst] Anna C. & me. To bed some after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 9. 1880

Another matched day of dryness & thick smoke — while suffering less discomfort & pain than yesterday, still am unable to eat or exercise. Nearly all of the morning was spent with Addie & Anna in the light boat Adirondack — the girls pulling — me next to Vowers's — then to Hotel at the landing, then up to Meadow Pt. & through Butler's cottages, then up past Knowlton's, around Agnes Island & then home at noon. Dinner at 1.30 — P.M. spent reading Rienzi & knitting. Tea as usual at 7. Anna, Walter & I walked out beyond Vowers' & met the stage with Emma & Helen Butler. [illegible] in general chatteration & all to bed early — that is, some after ten — Rain —
