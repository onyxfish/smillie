## Left Page

### September, Monday 6, 1880

Still very warm clearing and clouding in a threatening manner but the drought remains unbroken — I was used up with Stomach Trouble so that I did not leave the house, but sat doubled up on the Piazza watching the progress of the fire over the mountains. The day passed wearily but in the evening I felt better & played whist until near 10 o'clk — then to bed — at which time a few drops of Rain fell.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 7, 1880

Much cooler — almost uncomfortably so — feeling much better than yesterday but weak and still in considerable pain —
Got my oil traps ready in my room & tried to paint a memory sketch of evef. effect that I saw on Saturday — at 12 went out sailing with Arthur Hawes — Dinner at 1.30 — in the afternoon, painted again but did not finish my sketch. Took my evely exercise walking on the Pier — Tea at 6.30 —
Addie has been in bed all day, used up with a cold. Evef. alone much of the time & managed to do a little writing. To bed before 11.
