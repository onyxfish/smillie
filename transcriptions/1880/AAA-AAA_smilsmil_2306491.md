## Left Page

### December, Sunday 19, 1880

Charming day — up at 9, feel very well — bkfst — at 10 + att ment to Dr. Hastings — he did not preach — don't know who did. Began to feel very uncomfortabl about 2 — after service walked to studio, growing more uncomfortable every moment — lay upon lounge for a few moments then started for 37 S — while in 4th car I suffered such agony that for a few minutes I was unconscious — came to myself at 40 S. & got out — the pain in my stomach gone — went to 37 S — in bed until 3 — then to Dr. Male's — there until afternoon he could give me no satisfaction — but gave me a note to Dr. Janeeway — Then to Anna's and spent a delightful evening with her, she was so tender and sympathetic.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 20, 1880

Overcast day — feel tolerably well after yesterday's experience. Got to studio at 9.30 and spent the day fussing, mounting drawings of "Mariner's Mill" "Portland Pier" etc. — Had a light lunch in studio at 2 — continued my misc. work until after 4 — at 5 went to Dr. Janeeway, 51 E 25 S — by advice of Male. Had him make a careful examination of me. He told me to return with myself. went to 37 S — dressed, & at 6.30 took Anna to her bro. Ned's where we dined — at 9.30 took them home & I went to Reception Studio given by Mr. & Mrs. E.L. Henry to Mr. & Mrs. Ernest Parton — a very enjoyable affair to me — at 11 walked to 37 S. with Brown & to bed at 11.30.
