## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 6, 1880.

Overcast with showers in the afternoon clearing away beautifully in the evening. Spent the mfg. stoning down parts of etching "the way to the River" which is unfortunately, generally overbitten — Dinner at 1 — Afternoon until 5, proving them lay down in my bed for a rest as I felt decidedly used up — at 6 dressed & at 7, at Prof. Ritting, with Geo — there met Miss Clones & Miss Hart & had tea at 7.30 — after that improvisation on piano by the Prof.; singing by Mad R — & conversation until 10 — then Misses C. & H. were called for — Geo. & I left at 10.30 — home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 7, 1880.

Charming day, but I so much out of sorts "a general debilitation" that I did very little work. Father went to N.Y. by early train & ret'd at 8.30 — I fussed for a time over my etching — Mary came with Carrie Hertzel — I walked up town with them — dinner at 12.30 — at 1.15 Mother & Geo got away for the Catskill mts. — I went to depot with them & then to River+ crossed — Spent the p.m. wandering about — trying to tire myself physically as an antidote to nervousness — Returned at 7.30 & had tea — Matt & El at the house — As I was well tired I got to bed by 10 o'clk.
