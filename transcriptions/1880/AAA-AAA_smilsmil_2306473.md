## Left Page

### November, Saturday 13. 1880.

Cold, overcast with, threatening snow.
B'kf't at 8.30. Then to studio and
finished charcoal sketch — to
dinner at 2 — In the afternoon
made a slight charcoal sketch
for W.C. of a bit near arts mouth —
At 4 o'clk went to Emma J's —
met her in 17th S. & gave her ticket
for Sarah Bernhardt's Reception at
the Union League Theatre to-night —
then to studio & at 5 to 37th S. to dress —
A little before 6 to Anna C's & had an
early dinner — At 7.15 at Union League
Club Theatre & saw Sarah's paintings &
sculpture — Slight & passably clever
things — we left at 7.45 — Sarah did not
arrive until later — then to Phil. concert —
a very fine but long one — near 11 when
it was over — to 38 St. with A — then
to 37th St. & at midnight to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 14. 1880.

Still slightly overcast with the nip
of snow in the air — B'kf't at 10 — at
11 to Dr. Hastings' & heard a very good
sermon upon this life & that which is
to come — After service, to studio and
read papers until after 2 — then to
dinner — back to studio & wrote
until near 5 — then walked up
to Buckingham & saw Will for a
few minutes, then on to A.N. V's —
there until 7.15 — then walked
down to Robt. Collyer's Ch. — Park Ave
& 34th S. & heard Moncure D. Conway —
His text was the last incarnation of
Krishna from one of the Hindoo Vedas —
altogether a good pagan sermon —
After service again to studio &
wrote four more notes & letters —
to 37th S. soon after 11 & then to bed.
