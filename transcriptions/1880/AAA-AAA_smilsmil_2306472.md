## Left Page

### November, Thursday 11, 1880

Heavy rain with driving wind until after mid morning, then
it cleared to be like a Spring day — I charcoaled for a while
on 33 × 44 making little head
way as I had something of
ahead on me. Out to lunch
at 2. Afternoon stretched
brown paper to make some
charcoal sketches for Water
Color drawings — also fussed
with some Jap. paper &c &
wrote some letters — At 6.30
at Anna C. — dinner — Mary Vellam
there but no Fanny K; after leaving
Anna at the [illegible]'s I went down to
Emma's in 17th S. — her 36th birthday —
found Mr. & Mrs. Caswell there to dinner —
there until 11.15 — thens to 37th St.
& got to bed just at midnight.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 12, 1880

Pleasant day — Belt. at 9 — then
to studio & spent the morning
charcoaling sketch for water color
of Blk. Mt. from Letts. Channel, Harbor
islands. Didn't make much progress
— Farmer brought his four etchings
for the Osgood book. I wrote to
Osgood & Hosheer — out to lunch
at 1 & at 2 went to first Phil.
Rehearsal of the season — it was
an interesting but long one — after
it returned to studio — had
tea at 7 — & soon after 8 went
to 128 E 39th S; to call on Miss
Henrietta Beebe — not in — then
to 116 & called on the
[illegible] — there until 10.15 —
then to 37th S. & to bed.
