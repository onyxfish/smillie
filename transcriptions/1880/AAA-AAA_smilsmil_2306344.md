## Left Page

### February, Friday 27. 1880.

Very mixed, beautiful day. To studio at 9.45 & to work finishing Hail Columbia — then started drawing of Paraguay arms — Dinner at 2 — at 3 went with Waller, met Gifford & Wm. [illegible] Johnston & all went to Met. Museum of fine arts — Central Park — committee on Joan [illegible] for grand opening — There until 5 — walked down to 59th St. 55th Ave with Jno. S. Johnston & Wm. Loring Andrews — then to A.V.N. where Kate is & had dinner — at 8 a short call at Will's, then to studio where I helped gel to 37th St. Soon after 10 & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 28. 1880.

Over cast m'f. Trying to clear later, with steady rain at night. Got to studio at 9.45 & spent the m'f working on arms of Paraguay — Dinner at 2 — little done during the rest of afternoon owing to the confusion incident to the winding up of water color exhib. Seat 4 — after that helped gel until far 9, then to N.A. for a little while, then to "Century" to meet Gifford, Robbins & Waller. g — did not arrive until 11, then we worked on Met. Museum committee work until 1 o'clc — then I walked up to 37th St. & to bed at 1.30 a.m.
