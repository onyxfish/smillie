## Left Page

### February, Sunday 1. 1880.

Clear, windy day — growing to be exceedingly cold at night. Had [illegible] about 10
thens to Dr. Hastings & heard him give out his text — was sure he would preach a good sermon — then went peacefully to sleep — After service, 9.00 I went to Wash. Wilson's — met there Dr. Wm R. Williams' two sons. Had a very pleasant time & at 4 went to Studio's — at 5.30 went to Emma D.'s and had tea at 6 — at 7 took her to the Caswell's & then went to the Century — wrote notes to Mein & Belles. I weedy & at 8 went to Coleman's — there until near 10 — then to 37'S. & to bed at 10.30 — bitter cold.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 2. 1880.

Very cold m'f. with wind. Got to Studio's about 9.30 & began charcoaling a sketch — "group of horses" — "Hail Columbia" for C.B.N. Co. To dinner at 2 — Afternoon, washed in an effect on architectural subject also for C.B.N. Co. At 5 went to Scribner's — then to Bisbee's (shoemaker) — back to Studio's at 6. Father came & took tea with us — At 8, he & I went to Water Color exhibition — there until near 10 — then I walked up to Chase's with him, then to 37'S. & to bed at 11. My big drawing "Old Cedars on the coast of Maine" was sold to-day for $450 00.
