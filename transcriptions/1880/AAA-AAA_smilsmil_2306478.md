## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 23, 1880

*Father's Birthday — Po'keepsie*

Still clear, cold & windy — Got to studio at 9.30 feeling unlike work — my throat troubling me — Fussed a little over charcoal sketch of Manning well at Cape Elizabeth, Portland — A.B.N. Co. sent mining drawing for me to make — learned that Will has been sick for a week — bilious trouble — Dinner at 1.30 — at 2.30 went to Buckinghams & saw Will — he is very wretched — threatened with Jaundice — then to depot, met Charle & Geo. & took 4 o'clk. Train to Po'k — got home at 7 — found the Hill Siders there — a family gathering to celebrate the day — after 8 before we had our supper — an evening of chatting & singing — all left at about 10.30 & I got to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 24, 1880

Much milder — at times overcast & threatening snow, which came at night — BKlp. at 8.30 — then busied myself indoors — Loomis called about 10 & stayed until near 11 — then I got to work upon a mining drawing for the A.B.N. Co. Uncle Willie called & soon after 12 I went out for a walk — Back at 1 — Dinner at 1.30 — after that with Charl, went to Telegraph office & telegraphed to A.V.S. Hutton — then to Gronk's — then to 2d. to will — then back to Mr. Weeks house to see Gronk's carpenter work there — & home bet. 4 & 5 o'clk. — Tea after 6 — fixed back parlor to be very comfortable & home like, then, at 7.30, with Geo., called on the Loomis's — there until 9 — then home — snow — to bed at near 11.
