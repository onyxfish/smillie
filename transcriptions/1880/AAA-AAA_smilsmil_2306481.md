## Left Page

### November, Monday 29, 1880

Rather cold & raw — with sunshine & heavy clouds, not unlike a cold Alpine day — got to studio soon after 9 — finished A.B.M. Co. drawing — then smeared 33 × 44 canvas intending to block in painting of Cedars & River. Many callers prevented it — Young Ritchie called & asked me to prepare & read a paper on Etching before the Rembrandt club of Brooklyn in Feb. — I said I would. A.T. Baker called & Yale & Coleman & Ferguson — out to dinner at 2 — more callers — after 5, went to Renner's & ordered frames — to studio & had tea at 7.30. Evening writing to Bernard of Yokohama detailed order for Jap. paper. Near 11 when I went to 37. S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 30, 1880

Very pleasant day — got to studio about 9.30 & soon got to work painting in 33 × 44 Cedars Evening — Miss Van Horn called — then W. M. Hammond of Portland, Me. It was 3 o'clk. before I got out to my lunch — Back and painted as long as I could see — near 6 when I got to Renner's & completed my order for W.C. frames — then to 37. S. — 7 o'clk — no time for supper — had a few crackers & at 7.30 went to A.C.C.'s — there until 10 — then down to studio & had a roll & a cup of warm drink — then to 37. S. & to bed at 11.30.
