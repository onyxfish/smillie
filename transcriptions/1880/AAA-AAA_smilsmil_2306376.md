## Left Page

### May, Saturday 1. 1880.

Charming day — still fresh & breezy — After calling at Colman's house it was 10 before I got to the studio — All the m'g. spent in the morning at some etching proofs & in writing letters — Just as I was going out to dinner, A.V.S. Anthony came in on business concerning etchings & drawings more — got to dinner about 2.45 — the rest of P.M. spent clearing up — about 7. Anna C., Fanny K., Emma J., Elizabeth Hone & Geo. Cook, Chas. Collins & Wm K. and, by invitation to celebrate Anna C's birthday — we had supper & a jolly time until 10 — then the Co left & Geo. & I went to the Century — there until near 12 — then to 37 S. & to bed at 12.40.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 2. 1880.

Charming day — still milder. Did not wake until near 9 — Had b'kf'st at the Hotel Royale after 10 — then to St. Hastings' & heard him preach — then to studio — Read the Tribune & fell asleep in my chair — awoke at 3 & went to dinner — at 4, at Anna C's & took her to see the pictures at the Century — then to studio — made letters — had something to eat about 8 o'clk — then more writing — to 37 S. at 11 & to bed before 12.
