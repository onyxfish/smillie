## Left Page

### May, Monday 3, 1880

Very warm & uncomfortable after bkfs — went to funeral of Mrs. J.C. Nicolle — Got to studio before 11 & commenced work on Anthony's block — The grave of Washington Irving — out to dinner at 2 — then to Moore's Gallery to inspect some additions & alterations — then to studio & worked on block without much pluck. Had tea about 5. Later in the evening went to A.V.N. where Mother was — came down from Po. this mft. with Chase — left there som. aft. 9 — on my way down stopped at Chas's — there until near 11 — then to 37th St. and to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 4, 1880

Very hot — To studio soon after 9 & to work on work for Anthony's. To dinner as usual at 2 — a staunter about Mad. Square — then again to work — finished drawing & got it off by 5½p. to Boston — also wrote to [illegible] — Tea before 7 — then to 37th St. — dressed — & at 8.30 at Standard Hall — Broadway near 43rd St. where Miss Arnold's Club gave a swell concert. After it, walked home with Misses Cook & Hove — went in for a few minutes & at 11 went to 37th St. & to bed at once.
