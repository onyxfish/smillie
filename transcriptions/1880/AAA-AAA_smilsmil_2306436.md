## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 31. 1880.

Fine day — After bkfst went to Harbor Island channel hoping to get a study of still water but the wind came up so that I had to find a new study — worked until 1 o'clk. on a study of rock & could not get it all in — then to dinner — again to the islands — beautiful day but very breezy. Worked on large up right study of Pine Trees & finished all that I will do out of doors — then after sun down, rowed to the other side of the lake & then home. Tea at 6:30 — Rvert. cards with Arthur H — . To bed abt 10:30.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 1. 1880.

Mild, overcast m'f. Geo got away for Burlington & Keene Valley at about 10 & I got to work upon study from my window which I finished shockingly before dinner abt — Clouds breaking & threatening to clear — Afternoon I painted sketch looking up the hill from door of shop — as usual, not a success. A row on the lake before tea — Rvert. I read aloud awhile to Haddie & then played cards with Arthur H. To bed soon after 11.
