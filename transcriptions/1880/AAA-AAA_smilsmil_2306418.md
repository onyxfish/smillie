## Left Page

### July, Monday 26, 1880

Another fine day - up late &
barely had time before Hlfs:
to go to the Sulpine Spring for
my drink. After Hlfs - went
with Mr. & Mrs. E. down town &
after doing several messages
went to the U.S. & listened for
awhile to the music - back
at about 11. very warm - t
Took out some work on Etching
"Gathering Pond Lilies" - Lunch
at 1 - After that a short nap
& then commenced a sketch
in Water colors to give to Mr. E.
for the Episcopal Ch. here - at
5 went out for a walk - Tea
at about 7 - Mr. Hunt, Mr. Noin &
her daughter Mrs. Penniman wer
invited to spend the evng until
10 o'clk - to bed before 11.
