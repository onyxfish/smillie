## Left Page

### October, Saturday 2, 1880

Another beautiful day — Before b'kf't at 8.15. Took a half hour walk about town — about 9.30 went to Long wharf & began study of the rear of No. 1 Portland wharf. Stuck closely to it until almost 1 o'clk. Then to dinner — After that took a short rest & then hurried & caught 2 o'clk boat to Cape Elizabeth. Went then to the Mariner house & made sketch of his well & barn by late p.m. light — Stopped at 5.30 quite chilled — went to hotel & rested & warmed up. Tea near 7 — At 8 went with Mr. Hammond to "Theatre & saw" My Partner". very hot place — the play, both — out at 10.45 & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 3, 1880

Still beautiful weather — up soon after 8 — late breakfast — Mr. H. came for us at about 10 & we went with him to church & heard the pastor, Mr. Heuck, preach on the unchangeableness of Christ. After service we walked and sat on a bench on the Western Promenade. Back to H's house soon after 1 & had dinner — Hewes & Davis came at 3 o'clk. & soon after we all went out by horse car to Deering & called upon Kimball the Carpenter artist — there for an hour, then ret'd to H's & had tea — At 8 we walked down to Telegraph Office & telegraphed to Father & to miss Lightner — then to hotel wrote awhile & got to bed about 10 o'clk.
