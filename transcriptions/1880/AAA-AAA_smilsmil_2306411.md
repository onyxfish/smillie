## Left Page

### July, Monday 12. 1880.

Very pleasant day — I still under the weather
& did little else during the m'f. but lie around.
I did some etching on Farm Lane — No. 1 — making
changes. Kate went out to Hillside very early —
Dinner — father & I alone,
abt — In the afternoon I tried some experiments
improving, taking half a dozen p.p. on different
kinds of paper — A lovely rain set in before 6 o'clk.
Kate returned at about 8 — cool, delightful eve.
Rain during most of the night — to bed at 10.
