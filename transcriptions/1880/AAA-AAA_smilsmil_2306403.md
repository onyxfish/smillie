## Left Page

### June, Thursday 24, 1880

Tremendously hot day —
Bkfst at 8.30 — for some time
after bkfst, very quiet —
then Emma ordered horse
& buggy & go. Emma, Miss
Barnum & I went up to
the woods & there I tried
to make a sketch in our
ink — back to dinner at
1 — after that, on shady
side of house with Scribner
until near 6 — then Will
B. with 3 piced team & 3
seat wagon took Misses
Bullard, Barnum, Violet
gee & I, around by Locust
Ave. into Peekskill & then
home — Tea under the big
birch on top of the hill — a
very delightful affair —
back to the house at 8.30
& on piazza until 10.30 —
To bed at 11.30.
