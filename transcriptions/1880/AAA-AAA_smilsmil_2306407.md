## Left Page

### July, Friday 2, 1880

Close, cloudy day — Rain at 6 charming shower & fine t-
night. B'kfst. at 7.30. After
that made a letter to [illegible]
& took it to P.O. Back soon
after 10 & to work biting on
"The way to the River." Dinner
before 1 & again at biting
until near 7 when I had
tea. Father ret'd from
N.Y. about to —. I cleaned
my plates & found that I
had a very successful
biting. About 8 went to
Matt's — there until 9 —
then home. Raining sweetly.
To bed about 10.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 3, 1880

Delightfully cool, breezy day
after a rainy night with clouds.
Showers about late in the day —
made notes after b'kfst. Took them
to P.O. then to work at my
etching, laid ground over all
& etched sky. Dinner at 1 —
all the afternoon spent
over my acid & reading
St. James Junt's "Confidence."
[illegible] came up from N.Y.
at 6 o'clk. Tea at 6. After
that, dressed & with Geo.
called on Mrs. Ch. S. Williams.
There until about 9 — then
returned home & to bed
soon after 10.
