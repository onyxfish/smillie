## Left Page

### September, Sunday 12, 1880

Another charming day — B&B:
late — about 10 Emma, Anna,
Helen B — & I, went to the top
of the high Rock & spent the
m'g there reading & writing.
Cyrus B — called for me in the
"Rambler". We exchanged signals —
Dinner about 2 — at that time
the Rambler again passed & I
went to the dock & saw B — for a
few minutes — much of the p.m.
was passed in a hammock in the
summerhouse by the water — about
5 went out rowing with Anna & Emma
until 6 — then tea & at near [?] Rambler
came for us. Addie, Anna, Emma, Helen
& I went in her to the Minnehaha where
B — entertained us for awhile & at
9 all went aboard the Meteor & thru
to Paradise Bay & the islands — glorious
moonlight — men landed at my
dock at 10.45 — & to bed soon after
11.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 13, 1880

Overcast m'g, with promise of rain
After B&B: Anna, Emma, Helen & I
went to P.O. then on to landing &
thus beyond that when it began
raining so heavily that we ret'd —
Dinner as usual — Rain continued
all the afternoon & we had reading.
At sunset it began to clear &
we went out for a little rowing.
Tea between 6 & 7 — Eve'g, had
a mixture of reading & cards —
got to bed a little before 11.
While out in the m'g, got
a telegram from Geo. asking me
to meet him at West Park on Wed —
to go over to Portland, Me. I
answered that I could not
as I had so much unfinished
work on hand.
