## Left Page

### June, Saturday 26. 1880.

Po'keepsie.

Still terribly hot + dry — Pt.Ps.
at 8 to most of the morning
was spent on the piazza
making a sketch of it —
Miss Sallie Kittridge came
to dinner on invitation,
but left soon after 2 —
I read most of the
afternoon + about 5 went
to Ella's little house when
Emma was taking a lesson
in oil painting from Geo.
Tea at 6 — Immediately
after it we were driven to
the station + took 7.10 train
to Po'k — getting there about
8.20 — then to the house —
then uptown on a message —
back + for some time reading
letters + sorting samples of
Japan paper just Rec'd from
Japan — To bed about
11.30.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 27. 1880.

Horribly hot — had a
good scrubbing + then
my breakfast — late
to church — Dinner
at 1 — after that had
a nap + was tormented
with flies — then wrote
a note to Miss Jacobs —
one to Emma Beach +
another to Kummel + Voigt.
Tea after 6 — Ed. + Matte
here — later, Many + the
Dr. at 9 went to the P.O.
Returned + sat awhile
