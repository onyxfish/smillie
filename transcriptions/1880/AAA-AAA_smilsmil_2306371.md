## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 21. 1880.

Pleasant day — so far as the
weather was concerned, but
unpleasant enough for me
as I was in bed until 5
o'clk. P.M. with a miserable
headache — then up, had
some gruel & after 6 got
to studio — wandered for
awhile about Madison Sq.
& about 9 went in to Sam.
Colman's — there until
10.30 — then to studio &
wrote to Anthony & Parrish
& at 11.40 went to 37 S. &
to bed shortly after mid-
night.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 22. 1880.

Very mild, clear day — got to
studio before 10, after going to
the Madison Sq. Garden to see
the walls & roof that fell last
night, killing several people at
the Haineman fair. Said a re-
sting ground on "Old Cedars" —
in the afternoon reset it.
Every had tea as usual
at Anna C's — after leaving
her at Arnold's, ret. to 37 S.
& dressed then went to the
Ostman-Aitken wedding
reception — 31 W. 52d. St —
tremendous crush — got
away as soon as possible
& went to the Hertzel's where
met Geo. — to 37 S. & to
bed before 11.
