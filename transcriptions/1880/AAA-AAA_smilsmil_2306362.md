## Left Page

### April, Saturday 3, 1880

Dark day with some heavy rain. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work. Painting trees in "Cape Porpoise." Mr. Dornet called with tickets for Josephy matinee — I preferred to stick to my work — Dinner at 2.30 — about half used up with a headache but I got my trees done — Tea at 7.30 about the studio until near 10 — then to N.A. then to Century & at 11 left there for 37th St. got to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 4, 1880

In bed until after 2 o'clk with headache — then up & at 3 went to Hotel Royal & had [illegible]. A heavy rain storm came up — after my meal I took elevated R.R. at 42d. St & rode to the upper end on Harlem River — then ret'd to 23d. St. & walked to studio — called on Mrs. Coleman for a few minutes & at 6 went to Emma J's — had tea & spent the eveg. Julie Spring there. Dr. Bellows came in — had a very pleasant time until 10 — another heavy shower — went to Century — there until 11 — then to studio — got to 37th St. & to bed at 12 or so.
