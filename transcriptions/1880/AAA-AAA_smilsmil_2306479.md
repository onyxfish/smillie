## Left Page

### November, Thursday 25, 1880

Comparatively mild, overcast day. Bkfst at 8.30. The mng was spent about the house until near noon. I wrote a letter to [illegible] Then I went out & walked for more than an hour. Came in & had dinner at 2 — After 3, a nap in a chair — then a little more writing — Tea at 6.45 — Hack came for us at 7.15 — Train 20 m. late — so that we started from Po'k at 8.05 — got to N.Y., 9 o'clock. Chas & J. at 10.20 — To 37 St. and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 26, 1880

Raw & overcast most of the day. Bkfst at 9 — then to studio — after some delay, got to work on mining design for H.B.N. Co — but still feeling the effects of influenza I can not work with satisfaction. Out to lunch at 1.30 — then down town & made slight sketch in Wall St. for mining design & back to studio at 3.30 — worked as long as I could see — Then to Besbee's with my new boots — [illegible] — back to studio — call from Frost Johnston — Tea at 6.30. Nasty night, sleety snow — went up to Buckingham to see how Will was getting along, but stopped at Dt. Yale's — there until 9 — then to Will's — gone to bed — then to Chas. — met Joe there — there until near 11 — then to 37 St. & to bed.
