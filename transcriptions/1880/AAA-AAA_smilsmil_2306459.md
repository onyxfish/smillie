## Left Page

### October, Saturday 16. 1880.

Dule + overcast, some light rains during the m'f, much of the time wandering about. Between 10 + 11 went out with father to walk — caught in a shower — got an umbrella at Mr. Jessup's — Returned but in so doing made a call of half an hour — Dinner abt — after some running about at about 4.15, Father, W.J.J. + I went out for a ride — very mild — stopped at Trunk's and Blackmore's — back at near 6 — then tea — Flora Lathrop called — I went with her to her sister Ella's — there until 9.30 — heavy showers — To bed som after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 17. 1880.

Heavy rain until about 3 o'clk. Bkfs. at 8.30 at 10.30 went to Baptist Ch, + heard Rev. Sherwood preach a very good sermon — Dinner at 1.30 — After 3 o'clk. Running about — beautiful clearing weather — About 5, lay down + had a nap — tea at 6.30 — Evg. — called on Fred Crandall + wife — then to Episcopal Ch, + tried to find Bessie Riley or the Biddles — then to W.J.J's before 9 — Reads until 10 — To bed at 10.30 — glorious moonlight.
