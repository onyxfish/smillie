## Left Page

### February, Thursday 5, 1880

Fine day — got to studio
soon after 9 — & fussed over
Columbia drawing for [illegible]
N. Co. Mounted it — Wash
Wilson came in with W.
Clark — a picture buyer —
interested in George. To
dinner at 2 — then to W.C.
[illegible] for a short time —
Afternoon, washed the face
of Adirondacks & rubbed
it "out" with Siccatif — mtu
mtf. I had varnished
"up the hill" — getting them
ready to send to Springfield.
At 6.30 to Numa's to dinner —
at 7.30, with [illegible] J. & Grace, went
to Hilasko Hall to hear Mrs. Ar-
nold's club sing — met all the
Cooks there & had a very nice
time — went home with Numa.
Then to the Century for awhile
then to 37'S. & to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 6, 1880

Beautiful day — got to studio
at 9.30 & for some time engaged
in writing notes & arranging
things — then commenced to
plan out (17×30) my idea for
Academy picture. At noon
went to Mad. Sq. & made
some sketches of tree forms — to
studio again & at 2, to dinner.
After that, a walk, & again
at my work with charcoal.
Out for a walk at 6 — tea at
7 — near 9 o'clk when I
went to W.C. [illegible] — there
until close, at 10, then to 37.
S. & to bed before 11.
