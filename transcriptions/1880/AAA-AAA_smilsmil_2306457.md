## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 12. 1880.

*Providence St. Rhode Island — State Room 74*

Still pleasant weather. After
B'kf't at 8, finished packing my
trunk — Then called on H't Shaw
— not in — left a note for him — then
rode out to Harvard College &
spent some time rambling
among the buildings — at 1
rode back to town — met Geo.
& at 2 had lunch at Tremont
Ho. Restaurant — from 3 to near
4.30 it rained heavily with
one or two peals of thunder. I
waited until near 5 — then,
the weather being beautifully
clear, I walked to Kitty Ireland's
— not in — Then to Providence dep't —
met Koehler & Geo. there — at 6, train
started — Providence, soon after 7 —
boat got away very soon — Sea
on board at 8 — glorious moonlight
night — but quite cool — wandered
about until 10.30 — then turned
in & had a fair night's rest.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 13. 1880.

*Montrose. Pa.*

Up at 6 o'clk. Glorious mrg.
B'kf't — on boat at 7 — foot of
Barclay St. at 7.40 & crossed to
Hoboken & at 8.35 (8.20 train)
started for Montrose — no incident —
Took things easily — Had a good
lunch at Scranton — & got to
Montrose depot at 3.30 — found
Aunt Jessup's there & rode to
town with him, his daughter
Nina & Mr. Mr. Mulford — Got
in before 5.30 — took a quick
run over my place — tea at
6.30 — after that, went with
W. J. T. down to his bro. Henry's
— back at 9 — chatting with Father
& mother until after ten — to
bed at 10.30.
