## Left Page

### January, Saturday 24, 1880

charming day — being mild & clear. Got to studio at 9.30 much of the w[?] spent in getting an etching & drawing in Blk & white in frames, then taking them over to N.A. Dinner at 2 — did some shopping & back to studio soon after 3 — Anna C — & miss Williams there — I did no work. At 6 went to Kimball's — at 8, with them to Phil. Concert; left them before 9 & went to Tile Club Reception at Sarony's — a very enjoyable affair — left there before 11 — walked to 37th St. & to bed before 11.30

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 25, 1880

Beautiful day — up at 8 — groomed myself, then went to bed & waited until 9[?] was ready. [Bfft?] at 10, then to Dr. Hastings' & heard him preach about Reaping what we have sowed — after service a short walk — then to the Hertzel's & had dinner — the 21st anniversary of their wedding — at 3 went to Dr. H's again & heard the children of Faith Chapel sing their Christmas carols — at 5, to studio; at 6, to Miss Abbott's & had tea — at 8 to Glenham Hotel to see J.W. Wood — not there — then to Emma D's — not in, but sat until 9.45 with Grace & her mother — then to the Century — there until 10.45 — then to 37th St. & to bed at 11.40.
