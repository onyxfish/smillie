## Left Page

### August, Sunday 1, 1880.

Another hot day. Up early but did not drink any spring
water. All went to Episcopal
Ch. & at 12.20 walked home
with Mr. & Mrs. E. Lunch at
12.45 — after that went to
watchmakers to have my
watch fixed — it has stopped —
he was not in — then to U.S.
— by the way — after lunch, E. &
I, called on M. Shepherd — back
to E's at 4 — Charl called —
dinner at 5 — after 6 called
on the Darts — then a short
walk with Mr. & Mrs. E. — Then
I wrote a letter home & at
9.30 went to U.S. met Charl
& Mollie S — back at 10.30
& to bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### August, Monday 2, 1880.

*Mohican House*
*Lake George.*

Up early, packed my trunk
& at 8 went to Empire Spring.
Bkfst at 8.30 — said good byes
& then to depot. Met Charl. &
we took 9.10 train went to Glen
Falls & thence by coach to Lake
Geo. getting to Fort Wm. Henry Hotel
at 12.15 after a red hot ride.
Dinner at 1 — at 2.15 off on the
Ganouski — at 3.30 a tremendous
squall of wind with rain came
upon us & for a few minutes it
was exciting & blew more furiously
than I have ever known except
at sea — landed safely at Bolton.
Gel met us. General introductions
— tea at 6 — after it Charl & I
went off rowing — stopped on
Green Island to put out a fire
& caught in light rain before
our return to hotel — for awhile
chatting on piazza. Wrote a
letter to Ehninger & got to bed
at about 10.30
