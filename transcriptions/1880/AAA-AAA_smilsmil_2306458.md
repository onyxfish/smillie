## Left Page

### October, Thursday 14. 1880.

Wonderfully brilliant day —
BStfs at 8 — the mrg v.
Spent running at out over
m. place — Father being with
me — About noon, had a
rest + then dinner at 1 —
Afternoon went as the mrg
did — another ramble
with Father — Tea at 6.30
Reading until near 9 —
Then, the young Republicans
turned out with fife +
drums + torches + had a
procession, speeches and
a bonfire in front of the
courthouse — There until
near 10, then in + to bed.
Heavy rain during the
night.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 15. 1880.

Fog during the early mrg,
then it burned away + the
day became clear + warm —
I ran about doing up little
chores + at about 11.30 I met
Father + we had a walk for
an hour — then a rest and
dinner soon after 1 — after
that I read for some time
+ at about 4 Father + I went
to call upon Mr. Sherwood, the
Baptist minister — not in —
then we took another walk
in at 5.30 — Tea at 6.30 —
Ratf. Read until near 9 —
then Dan. Sayre + wife came
in making a call of an hour
— to bed soon after 10 —
again more rain during the
night.
