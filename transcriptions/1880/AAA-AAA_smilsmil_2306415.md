## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 20, 1880

Gentle rain early in the mrg.
continuing at intervals during
the day & evening — At 8 o'clk
went to Sulphire Spring, then to P.O.
& back to Help. at 9 — After that
got to work in the Studio with
Mr. E. Helped about until lunch
at 1 — then again at etchings
— Retouching & pronings until
near 5 — Miss Shepherds called
& was interested in the process.
At 5 went with Geo. & called
on Mr. Jas. & Mrs. Dart at 595
Broadway — very near us — at
6 returned to dinner — after
that a game of whist in the loggia.
& at about 8 Mr. & Mrs. E. Geo. & I
went to Mr. Drexel's & spent
nearly two hours looking over
etchings — then back & to bed
shortly before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 21, 1880

Beautiful day. After a short
walk & a glass of High Rock, had
breakfast at 8.30 & Geo. went to
Lake George by the 9.10 train —
I spent an hour after Help.
in studio laying grounds, etc.
then went out riding until after
11 with Mr. E. & her mother —
then back & again assisting E.
with his etching — Lunch at
1 — after that transferred a
tracing — wrote a letter to Miss
Abbott & at 3.30 wandered down
to the hotels & to the Park where
I listened to part of a concert —
back to dinner at 5 — Out to
ride at 6 — delightful ride
— back at 9 — in loggia with
E. until 10, then to bed.
