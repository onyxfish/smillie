## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 17, 1880

Mild. Parts of the day exceedingly pleasant — Bkf. at 8.30. thu to studio soon to studio & stretched paper for a charcoal study — All day planning W.C. painting & getting ready for W.C. meeting to-night — Dinner at 2 — at 6 went to Avery's for a moment — back to studio & had tea at 7 — at 8 went to N.A. — water color meeting there until near 11 — glad to get out of the cloud & tobacco smoke laden atmosphere — [illegible] my bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 18, 1880

Overcast with heavy showers of rain — got to studio soon after & to work on charcoal study of "Sea Trees Repeating the Tale of the North Seas" etc. Van Cleef [illegible] came in — also Davenport who was wild & crazy with a bleeding cut in his throat — a very unpleasant visitor — out for lunch at 2.30 — expressed Hanniger's printing ink to him. Afternoon still working on charcoal sketches — soon after 5 went to 37 St., dressed & at 6.30 was at Mr. Thos. G. Hunt's. 116 E. 39th St. where Ge & D were invited to dine with Mr. or Prof. & Mrs. Van Hanninge and Mrs. Jeffers — a good dinner & very pleasant evef until 11 — thur to 37 St. & to bed at midnight.
