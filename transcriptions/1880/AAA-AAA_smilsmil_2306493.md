## Left Page

### December, Thursday 23, 1880

Charming day — To studio at 9.30 & to work on old trees. At 11.45 went to White's cor. Bdway & 9th S., met Uncle Geo. picked out teeth for a new set — then went to J. J. Brown's and borrowed three studies of fishermen & at 1.30 again at studio — went to lunch — worked on drawing until a little after 4 — then rested until 5 — 5.30 to 37th S. & dressed — at 6.30 to Anna's — After dinner took A. & Fanny K. to Jno. Johnston's — leaving them there I went to 33 1/2 17th S. & spent the eve'g with Kitty, Ireland & the rest of the J— family. Mr. & Mrs. Hoyt & Avery Brown — at 10.30 again at Johnston's & took Annie home — to 37th S. soon after 11 — & right to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 24, 1880

Dull day — snow beginning to fall about noon. Got to studio soon after 9 & to work on "old trees" altho' I had intended to put it aside for the present. Charl came in bet. 1 & 2 o'clk. Then out to lunch, back before 3 — Pa went to Polk at 4 o'clk — worked as long as I could see — then nl orange for half an hour — then to 37th S., dressed & soon after went to Anna's — had tea at 7 & at 7.30, carriage came — then to Academy of music & heard the opera of Lucia (Don Giovanni was promised) — home at 11 — then to 37th S. & to bed.
