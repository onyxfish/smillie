## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 15, 1880

Clearing — got to Studio's as usual & worked as usual on W.C. — dinner as usual — walk as usual — work as long as light lasted, then went down town & bought a diary, back to Studio's at 6 had tea there & spent the evening until 11 writing letters — then to 37th St. & to bed shortly before midnight.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 16, 1880

Cold & windy — To Studio's soon after 9 — & at work upon Mariners' Mere — Dinner & walk as usual — and work until after 4 when I could see no longer. Mr. Janicvian (Kate Drinker) of Phila. called — At 6 I went to 37th S. dressed — & then to [illegible]'s — neither fanny or muma there — after dinner I took A. to Mr. Taylor Johnston's, where the Armed club will meet for the present — then to Mr. Morse's 112 W. 39th St. — there until 10 — then returned for A. — walked up & I got to bed about 11.30.
