## Left Page

### May, Saturday 15. 1880.

Very pleasant day — Got to studio about 9 + to work on my "Old Cedars" etching. Etching over + biting the parts that I had stoned out — as I worked in a very deliberate way I was all day doing what might have been done in two hours — Lunch as usual at about 2 — + tea in the studio after 7 — Evertf. went to Emma Sh's — there until after 10 — then to 37' S. + to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 16. 1880.

Charming day — but very warm again — [illegible] at the Hotel Royal after 10 — then to Dr. Hastings' + after service, [illegible] + I went to 37' S. for a few minutes — then I walked up to A.P. N.'s — M.T. + aunt H. are to go home on Tues. so I dined there at 1.30 — at 3.30 went to the park for a little while — then to Emma's where I saw Julia Storing for a few minutes. She is to start for her new Maryland home on Thurs. — At 6.30 went to Mr. H. P. Gray's + took tea there — Mrs. + Mr. S. Colman being also there — at 9 went to studio + wet down [illegible] paper for printing + at 10.30 went to 37' S. + to bed.
