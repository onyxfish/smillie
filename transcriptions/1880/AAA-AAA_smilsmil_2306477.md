## Left Page

### November, Sunday 21, 1880

Cold, windy day — up at 8 +
went to Colman's & had
bkfst, then to studio — then
keeping quiet until near
2 — then out with Geo. and
had dinner. Some after 3
went to Anna C's — I had
planned a walk, but it was
too cold & I was not in trim.
Stayed to tea & until after
9 — then walked down to
studio & back to 37th St. To
bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 22, 1880

Very clear, cold & windy — got
to bkfst — about 9 — still feel very
much out of sorts — hot head,
sore throat, cold feet — don't
feel like work — did a little
musing over charcoal sketch
of Manner's Mill & had a very
light lunch — about 2.30 went to
37th St. got shawl & scarf, then to
foot of Liberty St. & took 4 o'clk Train
to Plainfield — went at once to
Uncle Geo's & gave him my bite
for a new set of teeth — Shortly
after 6 I went to Tweedie's and
there had dinner & spent a
very pleasant evening until
9.10 when I left to take the 9.32
to NY. 8 m. late — got to 37th St. at
just 11 & right to bed.
