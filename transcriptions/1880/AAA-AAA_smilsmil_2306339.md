## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 17, 1880

Charming day — got to studio at 9.30 — stretched canvases & charcoaled 16×21.
Didn't work very hard & the self soon got away —
at 12.30 went to barber's &
had my hear cut — at 2 had
dinner — after that a walk.
P.M. charcoaled on 20×30
did it work after 4 —
went to Scribner's & saw Mr.
Burlingame about West Point
drawing. To studio at 6 — Tea
soon after 7, wrote awhile
& also went to W.C. at 6 [illegible]
there until 10 — here to
37th St. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 18, 1880

Very mild & overcast — Rain
at 6 & in the evn'g. Got
to studio at 9.30 & began to
paint on 16×21 — Continued
until 2, then to dinner —
then a saunter in Madison
Sq. — back at 3 & painted
until near 5 — then washed
up brushes etc — Tea at
7 — for awhile read "The
Fool's Errand", about 9
went to W.C. [illegible], to 37th
St. at 10.30 to bed.
