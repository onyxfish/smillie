## Left Page

### February, Sunday 29, 1880

Beautiful, clear day but very warm — Bkfst — at 10 o'clk, & then feeling very heavy — headed I went to studio &
lay on the lounge until 12, then walked to Hastings' in 42d St., met Geo. Kate &
Mary Schaffer — walked with her to 51st St — then on to 9, V.V. S — Dinner at 1.30 — at 4, walked in the Park with Charl. &
got to studio soon after 5. On lounge until 6, then in to Colman's & had tea — at 8.30 back to studio — at 9.30 Messrs Turnure & Whitlock of the Art Interchange called at 10.30 to 37th St — & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 1, 1880

Clear, cool, windy — March-like — got to studio at 9.30 but owing to the rabble and racket from the Treas'rs Office of W. C. Soc'ty could not get started at my work until 11 o'clk — then tried to work on 16×21 "Near Po'keepsie" — Dinner at 2 — a walk in Mad. Sq — then again at my work. — Constant interruptions — am afraid that I have spoiled a good start, after a walk — had tea at 7 — well in & about studio meting etc, etc. To 37th St soon after 10, then to bed.
