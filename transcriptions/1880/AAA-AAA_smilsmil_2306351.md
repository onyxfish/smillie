## Left Page

### March, Friday 12, 1880

Pleasant day — but overcast —
After 5 &c. went to Leavitt gallery
& saw the J. Abner Harperpictures.
Men. to studio a little before 11.
To work, getting my etching
material ready — making pencil
sketches, etc. — To dinner
at 1.30 — then to Pike's about
a magnifying glass — back
to studio — new frame for my
"Patient milk maid" — Tamer
came in & is positive that
the figure is obtrusive — the
picture would be better with-
out it — so I worked as long
as I could see, trying to subdue
it. Tea at 7. Evy went
to Wills where father and
mother now are — there
until after 10 — then to
37 S. & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 13, 1880

Cold, raw, windy & overcast.
Got to studio at 9.30, had
med. up with a head ache —
made a little progress, finishing
some sketches & outlines for
two etchings — Dinner at
2 — did nothing in the
afternoon but go to Pike's to
see about my glass & then
on sofa — Took some tea at
7 & again on sofa until 10
then to 37 S. & to bed with
a regular headache.
Snow again at night.
