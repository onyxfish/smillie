## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 27. 1880.

Beautiful day — Up at 6.30 went to Hotel Royale — bkfst & at 8 o'clk. off in train for Jarrisons where I am — about 9.30 — then climbed to the top of a hill & had a view overlooking West Point & the Hudson — Sketched until after 11 — then to Highlands House & at had dinner — at 2.06 train to Tarrytown — back, & drove to Sleepy Hollow Cemetery & sketched Irving's grave then to train & got to N.Y. at 5 — To 39th St & had a good bath and rubbing down — dressed & at 6 lay down on bed for 40 m — then up & went to Anna Cook's — & at 7 to Robt Gordon's — 7 & 38th St where I dined with Mr. & Mrs. G — son & daughter — Cornelius Vanderbilt, Howard Patten — Marquand — Mr. Taylor Johnston — Genl. di Cesnola — Saml. Colman & J.G. Brown — Had a charming dinner — sat between C. Vanderbilt & young Gordon — left at a few minutes after 11 & got to bed before midnight.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 28. 1880.

Delicious Spring day — Got to studio at 9.30 & after some delay got to work on Cape Porpoise — putting on what I hope to be the finishing touches — feeling remarkably well after the experiences of yesterday — Dinner at 2 — a lounge in the Square — much of my afternoons spent with callers — at 6.30 went to the Kimball's to tea & at 8 — with Fanny, Matt, Kate & Geo went to Daly's theatre & saw & heard "The Royal Middy" — a comical, spectacular, semi-operatic affair — the performance was funny — Catherine Lewis being roguish & very wicked. — out at 11.15 to 39th St. at 11.45 & to bed at 12.
