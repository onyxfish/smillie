## Left Page

### December, Saturday 25, 1880

Still dismal weather. Got to studio at 9.30 & spent the mtg finishing up study "Farm of W. Portland Pier" feeling much out of sorts, abd o'clk went to 37½ & from there at 1.30 to Annie's — by that time very uncomfortable — had lunch before 2 — then a cosy, fireside time until 3.30 then to Grand Central Depot & took 4 o'clk train to Po'k. getting home about 7.30 — tea soon after — my evelf. Rather unhappy on acct. of pain below the belt. The house looked charming, decorated with holly & mistletoe sent up by Charl — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 26, 1880

Stormy day, but more wind & bluster than anything else. I did not go out during the mtg but nursed myself and chatted with mother. Dinner soon after 1 — Mary, Matt & Ed. came in the afternoon. Som after 4 Geo. Charl, Ed & I went to Christ's Ch. & heard the children sing their Xmas carols — then to Ed's for a few minutes, then to Mamy's — there until after 6, then home to tea — Evelf all together in the parlor until after 10, then to bed.
