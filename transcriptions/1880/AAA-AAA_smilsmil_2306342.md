## Left Page

### February, Monday 23, 1880

Blustering day with snow flurries, rain & bright sun-
light. Got to studio before
10 & tried to finish my
etching article. — Several
callers, among them Chas.
Adams (Meta Faint's husband)
To dinner at 2 — then to
W. C. Sybtm, to Moore's and
about — back to studio and
finished up the first rough
draft of etching article — at
5.30 to 37 S— dressed — back
to studio & at 8 went with
Mr. Colman & Mima I— to
private theatricals (Ruth's Romance)
given before the "Idlers" (a
little club) in a house in E.
21st S— very entertaining —
glorious moonlight — to 37 S—
after 11 & to bed before midnight.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 24, 1880

Beautiful, clear day — got to
studio before 10 — several callers —
Dr. L. M. Yale among others —
it was after 11 when I went
to Astor Library — had a letter
from Swain Gifford to Mr. Sette —
the extended every facility in
the way of studying up etching.
There until 1.45 — then to
dinner — at 3 went to see
Bishop Putnam about an etching
— Mrs. Wm Dart came & I went
with her to a studio on B'dway to
see an old frame. Back to
studio about 6 — tea at 7 &
at 8 went to Mad. Adele Roch's
to see some pictures she has —
to 37 S— at 10.45 — to bed
about 11.
