## Left Page

### March, Thursday 4, 1880

Overcast mrg. - clearing to a mixed, april-like day - Up with a heavy head - got to studio about 10 - Spent most of the mrg. in preparing duplicate pfs. of etchings for delivery - To dinner at 2 + at 3 went to Sacketts R.S. Gifford's studio - met Waller there & we went to 10th St. Studio Beal - then to Walter Shreeves & Alden Meirs - on Met. museum business left there at 5.40 - then to studio & at 6.30 to Emma Cook's - after taking her & Fanny F. to Arnold's called on the Blacks 9 W. 37 S. There until 10.30 - then to 37th St. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 5, 1880

Tremendously heavy rain before 8 o'clt. a.m. Later in the day it cleared charmingly. Got to studio at 9.30. Then to work on my "Patient Nick-maid". 16×21 - it went very generally & improved it I think. To dinner at 2 - worked until after 5 - then to "Cantring" - got card & mailed it to W. Wilson. To tea at studio at 7. At 8.45 went to 203 W. 52nd St. & called on Maud Diven - at 10 went to Bishop's - E. 69th St. - with Ella - on way down, stopped in at Riding School - 59th St. & to 37th St. & to bed at 11.30
