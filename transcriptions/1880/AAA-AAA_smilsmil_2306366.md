## Left Page

### April, Sunday 11. 1880.

Unpleasantly windy day —
Had bkf. about 10 o'clk —
then to studio & at about
11.15 went to Dr Hastings &
heard him preach from Luke
14th ch. 21° v. "The kingdom of
God is within you". After
service went up to Dr Yates
& waited for him until 1.20
then gave it up & returned
to the studio — at 2.30 went
out with Geo. & had dinner
then again to studio & had
a nap. Then wrote to father
after 8 had a little something
to eat — wrote a letter of
condolence to S.C.T. Hammond
& at 10.30 went to 37th S. &
to bed. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### April, Monday 12. 1880.

Pleasant — but still windy &
dirty — near 10 when I got
to studio about half used up
with a headache. Consequently
spent the m'f in a sort of
housecleaning work — trying
to bring some sort of order out
of the dreadful confusion in
the studio — to dinner at 2.
& after that a loiter — then
again at housecleaning —
at 6 to 37th S. & dressed, then
back to studio — had tea &
at 8 was ready for the Etching
Club — only Bros. Baldwin,
Colman & Nicole came — at
10 Colman & I went to Louis
Tiffany's housewarming &
entertainment of "The Idlers".
There until after 11, then
to 37th S. & to bed at midnight.
