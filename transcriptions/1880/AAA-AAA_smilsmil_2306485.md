## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 7, 1880

A flurrying sort of morning — snow flying, but not enough to make a mark — Towards eve'g. It blew off clear + cold. Got to studio some after 9 + to work with water colors on a sketch 7×11 — Did it get along very well — Dinner about 2 — then a short walk — after that, a little more work — feeling a reaction + played it — Father came in at 5.15 + I surprised him by telling him the news — then I went to 37 S., dressed + at 6 went to Annie's — Ge being away I took my seat at the head of the Table — at 8 — at Henschel's Recital — To me, with Miss Bailing's singing, an exquisite treat — after concert Annie + I walked up with Mr. + Mrs. Colman —

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 8, 1880

Dark, snowy morning — flurries during the day until towards 4 P.m. — B[illegible]. late + got to studio a little before 10 — all the m[illegible] giving up to minting, callers and sketching drawing paper. Father came in at 2.15 + we went to the Ashland Ho. + had dinner — after that he went to Po'k. At 4 o'clk I went up to 57 S. to Riding School with Colman + had a good ride — then to 37 S. + had a [illegible] — then to studio at 6.45 + had grub — At 8.15 to Payne's 98 4th Ave. + at 9 to Annie C—s — there until 10.15 — then to 37 S. + to bed.
