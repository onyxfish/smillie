## Left Page

### November, Sunday 7, 1880

Breezy, cool day. B[illegible] at 10 o'clk — then to Dr. Hastings —
he preached from Deut. 32 ch
11+12 v. After service, with Geo,
walked to the Buckingham +
called on Will — saw Joe + Minn —
then to A.N.Y.'s — about 2 had
dinner — at near 4 went out
to the Park — walked up to Art
museum, then out to Boulevard
+ 93rd St + saw the obelisks on its
journey to the Park — then to Studio
at 6 went in to Coleman's — had
tea + there until 8. Then to
studio — there until 10,
to 37th St + to bed shortly
after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 8, 1880

Charming day. B[illegible] at 9 —
then to studio + got to work
upon a canvas x —
roughly charcoaled a sketch
from the Rainy day study
that I made at Lake George
last summer + then, upon
the bare canvas I scrubbed
in, in the crudest way, an
under painting — when this
I will then glaze + paint upon
that when this is dry — This is
my first experiment in this way.
Kinneel came to see me about
printing — to dinner at 2 —
Painted as long as I could
see — Bellea + Mary Tweedy called.
At 5:30 went to Bisbee, shoemaker.
Tea at studio's at 7 — Rest in
studio's writing notes until
10 — then to 37th St + to bed.
