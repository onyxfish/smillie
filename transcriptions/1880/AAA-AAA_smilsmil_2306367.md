## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 13. 1880.

Still breezy but mild and pleasant. Got to studio about
9.30 + at tin some fussing around
got started etching "Cedar
Meadows" on Zinc. Out to
dinner at 2. After that went
to barbers + when I Ret. to
studio at 3.45 found Mrs. R.
Korhler waiting for me. He
made quite a long call, then
to my etching until 6 — then
to Emma Ireland's + had
dinner — Nelly, went with
her to Call on Wm. Cassmell — Mrs
C. ill + Mr. C. engaged — made a
short call + Returned — Geo. at
Mrs. J's — we remained until
10 — then to 37th St. + to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 14. 1880.

Very mild + spring-like — got to
studio a little below 10 + found
a note from C. D. Morse enclosing
check for $300 — for sale of "La Cascade
Pacid + the Adirondack Mts, from
White Face" — 23×40. To work
on etching + biting of "Cedar
Meadow" — Tried mordant on
the Zinc + ruined the line —
to dinner at 2 — after that, to
the Bank, then to Morse's — not
in — then to Schinitt + Seeger —
then to Gifford's to find why
the etching club meetings for
two months past had been such
failures + learned that F. Schuch
had taken great offense because at
the W.C. meeting I had dared criticise
the ill. cab. To studio at 4 — Mr.
Koehler called, again. Out for a
walk at 6 — then in to tea —
very warm — even. in Shnudffs
Room + doing chores in my own
abode. To 37th S. + to bed at
11.30.
