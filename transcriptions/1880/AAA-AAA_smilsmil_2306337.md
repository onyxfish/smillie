## Left Page

### February, Friday 13, 1880

Rain in the m'f — tried hard to clear in the afternoon & more Rain at night — got to studio about 10 & spent some time painting on "Oct Shadows" — then got out "Lake Placid" — Rubbed it up & did some painting & glazing on it. Reimer has remodeled the frame & I will send it to Moore's opening next week. — Dinner at 2 — Tea at 4 — Every I wrote for a while, then went in to Coleman's — there until near 10, then again to studio & at 10.45 to 37: 8t & to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 14, 1880

Mild pleasant weather — to studio at 9.30 & finally got started up on the laying in of my "Twilight" — 17×30. Many callers — Dinner after 2 — a short walk — then again to my work — at 5.30 went to Reimer & Co's with my painting "Oct Shadows" — to be sent to Utica. Back to studio — tea after 7 — there seated myself to answer Emma J's Valentine & as it was finished & I went to her house with it — She had gone to Fort Washington — there for half an hour — then to 37: St. & to bed before 11.
