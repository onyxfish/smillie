## Left Page

### March, Monday 8, 1880

Charming day — after b[reakfas]t went to Renner's to order a new frame — met E.M. Murd who insisted upon my going with him to his studio, cor. H[igh] S[t]. & B[roa]dway — 10.30 when I got to studio — then fetched in to 14×30 — Welf. Dinner at 2. Mium came after & spent part of p.m — I worked as long as I could see & covered my canvas — second painting. Tea at 7 — Etching club night — but only Nicoll & Tiffany came — I went to Met. Museum arr. com. meeting at Mr Gordon's. To 37t S[t]. abt 11 & to bed —

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 9, 1880

Beastly weather, — slush & snow. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on 14×30 "Evening" & fussed over it all day — Dinner at 2 — then to Tweedee's — back at 3.45 — Tea at 7 — Welf in studio writing notes until after 10. Then to 37th S[t]. and to bed about 11.
