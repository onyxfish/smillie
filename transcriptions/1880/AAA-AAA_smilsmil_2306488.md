## Left Page

### December, Monday 13, 1880

Miserable dirty mtf. Snow & sleet having fallen during the night — Clearing during the day & at night cloudy moonlight — Got to studio before 10 & to work a "Manner Well" washing in the sky etc — out to lunch at noon 2 — a short walk & back to work until 4 — Mr. Morse & two ladies called — At 4 I went to Riding Academy with Coleman but did not enjoy my ride — Then to 37 S: at 6 & had a scrub — at 6.45 to Coleman's — he, Ge 2 & I had dinner — Mrs C. not there — to Studio for 3/4 of an hour — then at 9.15 C. & I went to Annie's where a doll dressing was in progress — There until shortly after 11 then to 37 S: & to bed —

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 14, 1880

Pleasant mtf. The reverse of yesterday, for it continued to grow worse until eve'g when rain came — Up late — went to Annie's for stitching — then to [illegible] — & at 10 to Studio — to work on Manner's Well going into it with more force than yesterday — to dinner at 2 — worked until 4 — then the Payne girls came — At near 5 went to Steinway's & after play — caught in rain & back to Studio had tea there — made several notes & at 9 went to Anna's there until about 10.30, then to 37 S. & to bed —
