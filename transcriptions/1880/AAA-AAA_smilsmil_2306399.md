## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 16, 1880

Another cool, overcast m[orning]
but clearing to be a charming
day. After b[reakfast] I wandered
off & as I was returning home
at 10.30, met Uncle Willie &
with him went looking for Geo.
found him at Parker's Mill
& then, with Mrs. Brewster
(Lucretia Cullen) we drove out
to Hill Side — we wandered
about until dinner & after
that I went out to the house
on top of the great Rock —
there I spent most of the
afternoon — later the family
went over the Sturges ant house
& back to tea. — About 8 we
were driven into town. —
found Matt & Ed. & Mary at
the house — To bed at 1.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 17, 1880

Delightful day, but rather warmer
than yesterday — after spending
a good part of the m[ornin]g wander-
ing about I got out my etching
matters & pencilled changes
& on "Farm Lane" (No. 1.) Dinner
at 1 o'clk. Made tracing &
retracing on block of the
"Rocks at Mt. Desert" for Harper's
weekly" & soon after it went
to foot of Main St. & crossed
the river in little Propellor
"Gypsey" — found friends on
board — we loafed in the
grove & sauntered along the
river front until near 8 — then
returned — At the house —
found Prof. Ritter — I had a
late tea & the evening was
spent, most of it, in a
rocking chair — To bed
about 10.
