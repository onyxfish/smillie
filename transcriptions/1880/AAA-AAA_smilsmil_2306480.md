## Left Page

### November, Saturday 27, 1880

Unpleasant m'f. but clear later in the day — to studio
soon after 9 & to work on A.B,
N. Co. currency design — Out to
lunch shortly before 2 — back
& worked as long as I could
see, nearly finishing drawing.
Several in studio — among others
Mr. Slade & two sisters from Brooklyn.
At 6 o'clk. I went to Mr. Waller's
33 W. 15th. Reception of Dr. Duryn
Waller & his wife — married last
summer — met friends, among
others the Kimballs, the Drelauds
& Anna Cook, went home with
A.C.S., dined there & then
to Steinway Hall — oratorio of
Elijah — after that, walked
home with her — then again
to studio at near midnight —
thru to 37 St. & to bed at
12.30.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 28, 1880

Overcast & mild — at union
came — afternoon, heavy rain
w'f. clearing — B'k'fs at 11 —
then to studio — then to Hastings
— Hepworth preached — after
that, up to see Mill — found
him better & sitting in his chair
— has had a severe bilious de-
rangement with jaundice —
after that, rode down to Ashland
— had dinner — then to studio
& spent the rest of the day
reading & writing — At 6 went
in to Coleman's — had tea and
there until 8 — then to studio
& at 8.40 to Anna C's — there
until a little after 10, then
to 37 St. & to bed.
