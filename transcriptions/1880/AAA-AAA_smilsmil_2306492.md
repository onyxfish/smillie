## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 21, 1880

Dismal day with winds and flying snow — Got to studio before 10 & to work on small sketch of Blast furnace for A.B. N. Co. — Finished it & then commenced sky washes on "Old Trees" — "to lunch at 2 — & again at work until 4.15 — could see no longer — then to Riding school & rode Coleman's horse & altho' rather tired, had a good time — then to Buckingham & saw Mill, Jo. & Minn — then to 37' S. — Bathed & dressed — then to studio — 7.30 — had tea & at 8.30 went to Anna's — there until 10.30 — then to 37' S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 22, 1880

Brilliant surf. after 9 fp. went to Dr. Janeway's & he completed his examination & pronounced me sound but that does not alleviate pain that I suffer — then to studio (after paying him $10) & to work on "old Trees." Stuck closely to it until dinner at 2 — then after a short walk, again at my work until 4.15 — then to Riding school with Coleman & had a good ride on "Rosalie" on my way down. stopped at Fale's there for half an hour, then to 37' S. & had a scrub — to studio at 7.30 — had tea & at 8.30 to Anna's — there until 10.30 — then to 37' S. & to bed.
