## Left Page

### August, Sunday 15, 1880

Superb day — clear & cool —
after bkfst, Rowed down to
the Floating Battery Islands
& around one or two of them,
then returned & went to the
Kimball cottage where Mr.
Fisher preached a sermon
on "Grapes from Thistles" — after
service wrote a letter home.
Dinner after 1 — after that
wrote to Geo. & to Emma J —
& Anna Cook — Tea between
6 & 7 — then Mr. Jeliffe came
in & the eve'g was passed
in general conversation.
At 9.45 I went out with
Addie & Rowed across the
lake — heavenly night —
In at 10.50 — To bed soon
after 11.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 16, 1880

Another superb day — After
breakfast went over to Harbor
Island & worked on foreground
rock study — not very happy
over it — back to dinner at
1.15 — after that again to the
island & worked on upright
pine study — Rec'd a call
from Mr & Mrs. Dix & Addie.
Worked until sundown —
Tea at 6.30 — Before 8 o'clk.
we went to the Kitchell's
where Mr. Jeliffe was to try
a mock case between Deluoe
& Mr. Dunshea. It was adjourned
on act of the illness of Deluoe's
wife. About 9 went Rowing
with Addie & Miss Boughton.
— a delicious night — water
without a ripple & sky without
a cloud. In soon after 10
& to bed at 10.30.
