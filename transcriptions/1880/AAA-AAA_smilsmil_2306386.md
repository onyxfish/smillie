## Left Page

### May, Friday 21. 1880.

Horribly hot — I got up nearly used up with a headache & hard by hoped to get through the day, but I did — most of the rest on the lounge. After a visit to Renner's in the mt before I had had [illegible], where there boy with Sewell pictures and — Renner [illegible] chgp. amt & [illegible] $9.60. At 1 went to Tweedle's & to Adam's [illegible], with [illegible] [illegible] etching of "Old Cedars" for Koehler — then to Kimball's where I ate dessert with them & begged an invitation to tea — Back to Studio but did little — At 6.45 went to [illegible]'s to tea & spent the eve[ning] there until near 10 — then to 37 [illegible] St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 22. 1880.

Breezy but hot — went to Anna C's before [illegible] — to [illegible] & trip [illegible] for this P.M. — got to Studio about 9 — Spent the [illegible] matting Montana Arms & a New Orleans Seal — Soon after 12 had chops cooked & put up my lunch & then to Grand Cen[tral] depot — met there Anna & Geo. C — Helen Butler & Mary Millard & Chas. Collins — we took 1.05 train to Dobb's Ferry — signalled ferry-man & had a delightfully rough & breezy sail across the River — we then went into the woods and spent the rest of P.M. there — At 6 we re-crossed the river — took 6.44 train & reached N.Y. at 7.30. I dined at the Cook's — there until about 9.30 — then walked down to Mad. Sq., met Geo. & ride to 37 S. & to bed at 10.30. Rain threatened late in the afternoon & some free at night.
