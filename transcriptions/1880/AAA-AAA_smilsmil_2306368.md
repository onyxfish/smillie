## Left Page

### April, Thursday 15. 1880.

Exceedingly warm day — got to studio at 9.30 + to work on "Cedar Meadows" finding that my ground was giving way + that the line was much injured — I finished the drawing in a bit + stopped out as usual. To dinner at 2. In the afternoon completed the biting + think I may have saved the plate — Near 4 when I got to Anna C's to dinner — after 8 when me left A.C. + F.K. at the Armeds. Then to Mr. Thos. G. Hunt's + played Écarté + supped Roman Punches until 11.45 — then to 37½ S. + to bed about 12.30.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 16. 1880.

Muggy, overcast + threatening — got to studio shortly after 9 + to work printing from "Cedar Meadows" — took three proofs + just as I was washing up to go down town Mr. Thuinger called — stayed with him for an hour, then Koehler called — stayed until 2 when I went to my dinner — then went down town to Graphic — A.B.N. Co., Charl's Shoemaker + Sharpe for copper. To studio at 5.30 — mounted etching "Up the Hill." + at 6.45 to Kimball's — Anna C. + Emma J. there to dinner. At 8 all went to Colman's and had music until 11 — after taking A. C. home — to 37⅞ S. + to bed shortly after 12.
