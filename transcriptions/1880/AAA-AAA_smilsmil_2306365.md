## Left Page

### April, Friday 9, 1880

Charming day — to studio
soon after 9 & to work on
t. 6. port Veterans — Painted
in the veterans — dinner at
2 — then a short walk &
again at my work — again
over waters & foreground —
at a little after 5 Mr. Geo.
Whitney came in & at 6.30
Geo. & I went to the Albemarle
& dined with Geo. W. Miss
Ely & her sister — about 8
we went to N.A. — at 9.30
back to hotel & left the party
there — I went to studio —
wrote awhile & at 10.30
went to 37th St. and thence to
bed.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 10, 1880

Pleasant day, but quite windy.
Got to studio about 9.30 and
to work — Painted in new
foreground on "October Shadows"
10×14 & then went over other
parts of the picture — about
1 had a light lunch in the
studio — went to Schinett & Siegle's
bought a Secretary with drawers
then to Elevated R.R. at 23rd St. &
went to 42nd St. station — there met
Anna C. — Geo. C., Nell K., Valentine
& at the next station, Mary Willard
— went to High Bridge & spent rest
of p.m. wandering — back to 37th St.
at 5.50 — dressed & got to Ned
Cook's — 101 Park Ave. — at 6.50 — Had
dinner about 7.30 — Mr. & Mrs. Oakey,
Mr. Breed, Miss Williams, Anna C. —
Fanny K., Geo. C. — & Geo. & I — Had
a very pleasant time until
11 — thence to 37th St. & to bed.
