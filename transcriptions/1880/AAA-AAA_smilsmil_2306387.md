## Left Page

### May, Sunday 23, 1880

A very hot day, altho' not without a pleasant breeze.
Up late, spent some time grooming myself & had
bkfst at the Hotel Royal at 10.30 — At 11 went to Dr.
Hastings & heard him preach from 1 Cor. 15th ch. 42 v. After
service went to Chare's — then to McYale's — neither of them in —
met Charle in the street & ret'd to his quarters — met Mr. Browning
& looked at his pictures & before 2 ret'd to studio — Dinner at the
Ashland. som after 2 — a short time in Madison Sq — then to studio.
Read & wrote until near 6, then in to Saml. Coleman's to tea.
At 8.30 went to Emma J's &
at 10 went to Century and wrote a note to Jno. Durand.
To 37 S. about 11 & to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 24, 1880

Still very hot & horribly dry —
Got to studio soon after 9 and spent most of the mng. writing
to Miss Z. O. Robbins & to Mr. F. H.
Chase of the Sewell Art Ass'n.
& entertaining Mr. Farrer. I also did some work again
making changes on drawing "Hail Columbia" — To dinner
at 1.30 & during P.M. finished changes on "H.C." At 5.30 went
to Harlem River by Met. Elevated R.R. & for an hour or so rambled
in the woods — back to studio about 8 & after some delay
had my tea in studio — then fussed & wrote & rested
until after 11 — To 37 S. and to bed before 12.
