## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 16, 1880

Heavy, driving rain in the
m'g. Feel as little like
work as I did yesterday.
Did a little for Geo. in
helping him along with
his Water Color account —
& something in the way of
changes on A.B.N. Co. drawing
of "Hail Columbia". Dinner
at 2, figured a little with
Geo. in the afternoon, had
tea as usual about 7 and
later went to Will's & spent
the rest of the ev'g with
father & mother. To 37.5.
& to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 17, 1880

Pleasant day but I was used up
with a cold that gave me a hot
head, cold feet, fever & shiverings.
I tried to work but it was of no
use. much of the day was spent
on the lounge. Out to dinner at
2 — the afternoon passed as the
morning had — tea at 7 — at
8 went to N.A. annual meeting
of the W.C. Soc'y. Quite an in-
teresting meeting, discussing the
new catalogue. & a proposition
I made to pay Miss Durfee the
difference between the sum she
rec'd last year & full 5 p'. cent on all
sales, amounting to about $140 —
session continued until 10.30 — it
was after 11 when I got to 37.8;
& to bed.
