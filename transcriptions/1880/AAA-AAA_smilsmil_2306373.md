## Left Page

### April, Sunday 25, 1880

Pleasant m'f. up early — Bkfs. at 8 — was going to Plainfield to spend the day, but, Met, Elevated R.R. not running trains threw me out of my calculations — went over to 9' ave. + 42°. S. but too late — then to studio + until near 1 o'clk on lounge feeling out of sorts then to the K.'s to dinner. There until 3 o'clk — then with Matt & Kate to stu[dio] — they went up to Will's + I spent most of P.M. on lounge — wrote to Mary Ivory had some gruel bet. 8 + 9 — then to the Kimball's — there until after 10, then to 37' S. + to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 26, 1880

Still dubious weather — Got to studio shortly after 9 and to work on etching of "old Cedar on coast of Maine". Said ground etched in sky — Dinner at 2 — afternoon spent over the etching, working with burnisher & graver — hope that it is now about finished — Tea in studio — then some writing & after 9 went to Kimball's — ate out — gone with Matt & Kate to Wallack's — To 37' S. about 10 o'clk — very soon after, quite a heavy but short thunder shower — To bed at a little after 10.
