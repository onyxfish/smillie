## Left Page

### May, Saturday 29. 1880.

Delightful change for the better — altho' very warm it is cooler & a pleasant breeze stirs. Got to studio about 9 & to work on drawing of Quebec for A.B.N. Co. — To lunch between 1 & 2 & again at work, finishing the drawing — To tea about 7 — then for a few minutes went into N.A. exhib. last night — & at 8.30 went to Anna Cook's & spent the rest of the evening there — to 37 S. at 10.30 & for an hour read poems by Hedrich & Howells — then to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 30. 1880.

The blessed rain has come at last — falling freely last night & at intervals during the day. — After bkfst. went to Dr. Hastings' & heard him preach a good sermon. Then [illegible] & I went to Wash. Wilson's & had dinner — I was very stupid with a headache — at 3.45 went to studio — wrote a short note to Father — on the lounge until 6 — then to Emma J's — took tea there & at 8.30 went to Anna C's there until 10.30 — then to 37 S. & to bed.
