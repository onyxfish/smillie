## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 30, 1880

Glorious day — up early — had Hefs. at 7.30 & Will, Joe, Minn
Charl, Geo. & I read to N.Y. on the 8.25 Train, getting in to
P.C. Depot at 10.30. Then to 37! S. & rested for half an hour
Then dressed & at 12 went to Hotel Royal & had lunch — at
12.45 went to Anna Cook's & with her went to Mr. Taylor Johnston's
where a lunch & reception were given to Pres. Hayes & wife — at
2.30 drove to Fanny K's — gather and Kitty Ireland, then to Anna C's &
from there to the Met. Museum, when the formal ceremonies of opening took
place at 3.40 o'clk — very imposing and successful. Wandered about
through the galleries until after 5 then, by Mad. Ave. R.R. Returned down
town — went home with A.C. & stayed to dinner — at 8.30 went to N. R. [illegible]
there until 10 — then to Studio for a few minutes & to 37! S. & to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 31, 1880

Another superb day — up savey but did not get to Studio before
9.30 — Spent the m'f lazily making pen sketches & my
painting in N.A. for the "Graphic" To dinner at 2 — then to [illegible]
for awhile — then to Studio &
charcoaled in "Cape Porpoise," 16×29. Feeling very mislike — not
at 5.30 went to Dewey's — then to Scribner's — then to [illegible]
& at 7.15 to Studio where I had tea — Spent the evn'f fussing,
writing & reading & lying in the lounge until after
10, then to 37! S. & to bed.
