## Left Page

### January, Saturday 10, 1880

Beautiful day, but was not able to get out of my bed, owing to an unbeautiful headache until after noon — then I got up, went to studio, prepared some granum gruel, ate it & my head growing worse, for the rest of the afternoon, back of screen in my big chair. Have not had so fierce a nervous headache for a long time. Between 8 & 9 got up and had something to eat. At 10 went to Century & at 11.30 walked to 37 S. & to bed soon after midnight.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 11, 1880

Very pleasant wf. — Late up — at 8. Cloud — then to Dr. Hastings & heard him preach a very good sermon from John 17. 43 — on the divisive power of Christ — After, to studio — dinner at 2 — At 3 went to Emma J's to take her to Century but she had other engagements — then to Century alone — At 5 went to Kimbal's — tea there and until 8.30 — then to studio & until 10 fixing the mounts of some drawings — at 10.30 walked up to 37 S. in a mizzling rain & to bed.
