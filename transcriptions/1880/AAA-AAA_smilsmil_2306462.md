## Left Page

### October, Friday 22, 1880

Dismal, rainy day — was not able to get out of my bed, owing to a wretched headache until after 3 o'clk — then up & had a good general scrub down — after it a rest & at 5 o'clk. The girls gave me a light tea — After 6 I rode down to studio with some of my traps — driving wind & rain — found Geo. there — at 8 I walked up to Anna C's — there until 10 o'clk — then to 37th St. & to bed some time after 11.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 23, 1880

Breezy & clouded day — clearing up of yesterdays storm — Blckp. at 8.30 — then to studio — much of the mtf. spent in "tinkering" — at 12 went to Schaad's — bl third S. to find out something about Philharmonic seats — but did it do it — then to Kelley's — not in — then to Steinways — my man not in — then to studio — father there — near 2 o'clk. we went to dinner — at 2.45 he left to go to depot to meet mother & go to Po'k. at 3.30. I read and wrote until 4 — then down to Steinways to see about Phil. seats. Sea in studio at 7 — at 8, to 37th St., dressed & at 9.45 got to Union League club — Genl. Grant's Reception — a very fine affair — there until a little after 11 — then to 37th St. & to bed about midnight.
