## Left Page

### April, Thursday 7, 1892

A pleasant day — growing cooler. At 7.30 went to the druggists & took a dose of Castor oil, as directed by Dr. Gevster — I took no [illegible] — At g. 30 took a cup of Tea & in a few minutes there was a circus — growing faint & sick — I tried to get up stairs but fell to the floor in a faint in the presence of a strange lady who was waiting to see Annie — Walter came in & I was helped to a sofa where I commenced operations as a geyser — as promptly as possible I started for up stairs but before I could get properly seated the bottom fell out — I was literally streaming with cold sweat — it was a horrible experience — In about half an hour I got bundled into bed & felt somewhat better after an hour or more of disturbed sleep. Took some food about 1 o'clk — Then got up & at 3 o'clk went to studio, got Jade plate & went to K & V's — they had forgotten their engagement & had no paper ready — left the plate with them — then to the [Goruell] Co — got certain rods etc for library — then rode up B[?] to Hunters & got glassed — then to Amende's. At way & 34th St. got antiseptic dressings — then walked home — 5.30. Found a note from Dr. G — saying I must have a trained male nurse — giving names & addresses of four. Annie went right out — visited two without being able to make an engagement. Had our dinner before 7 — After 8 I rode up to 73rd St. — went to 202 E — Wichmann — unpacked — his wife went for another one — Knew he was not engaged — he is to call to-morrow mng. I got home about g. 30 — Read until 10.30 to bed before 11 —

## Right Page

### April, Friday 8, 1892

*Surgical operation performed by Dr. Ampad G. Gevster.*

A very lovely day, mild & spring-like — Up in usual time & began preparations for the event of the day — was allowed to take a soft boiled egg & some arrowroot and for [illegible] — After 10 I sauntered down to the studio with Ralph & there made my arrangements to leave my matters in the hands of George — then sauntered back, getting home before 12 — Junna I [illegible] — also [illegible] I [illegible] — I was so hungry that I had to take some food about 12.30. At 2, nurse [Kochanak?] came (trained nurse) & she shaved me preparatory to the operation of removing a growth from the left groin — At 3 o'clk Dr. Gevster, [illegible] assistant came & Dr. G's black man "Roy". They were soon stripped for action — every one with his coat off, his shirt sleeves rolled up. The operating table — the basins — the big bottles of solutions — the instruments, dressings & bandages made a formidable display but my nerve was admirable — I did not have the slightest sense of tremor or flinching. Dr. J. administered the ether at 3.30. I was about 10 m. in going under its influence — very quiet — like an infant sleeping. The operation took 35 m. After consultation, the left testicle was found together with a cystic tumor. It was about 4.30 when I became conscious & found Annie sitting by my side, all the doctors & assistants gone together with every trace of the operation. At first I could only say, "why, this is a pic-nic" — but as consciousness became clearer, pain and a general feeling took possession of me. No morphine or pain alleviator of any kind had been given to me — my back & every bone in my body began to pain & ache — I could not move and to lie still was misery — It was a sore night — but like all nights before, it had an end in the morning.
