## Left Page

### September Monday 26, 1892

Another murky mf & afternoon with some pleasant weather between the two. I had a short walk in the Park before bkfs — & got to work at 10 o'clk — at 12.30 I stopped & went to the Pond where A — & the boys were sailing boats. Home to dinner at 1.30 — worked until 4.30 then took Jim to the Paddington Baths for his swimming lesson. He tries hard to understand his English swimming master who shouts at him in a bewildering way as though he were a dog. I helped give him a rubbing down after his lesson & then he had a run in the Park & home at 6.15 — Tea —

Evg. I rec'd letters from Geo. Kate and Charl. C's letter quite upset me as in it he told of an interview with Heber Bishop who would not pay for the Jade plate & said that my bill was exorbitant. He said that he was to sail for London in a few days & he would see me there.

morgan — 4, 30

## Right Page

### September Tuesday 27, 1892

Gloomy morning & evening with about two hours of sickly sunlight during the middle of the day — I had a short walk before bkfs — two hours on my plate — another Park walk — dinner — two more hours on my plate & then another walk before tea —

Evening I read awhile & then finished my letter to Wm. G. Parke and to bed at 10.30.

morgan — 4 h — [illegible]
