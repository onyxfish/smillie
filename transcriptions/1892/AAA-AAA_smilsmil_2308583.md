## Left Page

### March, Thursday 24, 1892

Bright & beautiful day — to studio soon after 10 — then in to Tiffany's for a while about change to be made in glass for light over front door — then back & to work on Jade plate — only a little over an hour — then I went to Dr. Allen's — 155 W. 47° S — to make an appointment as my teeth need his care. Home to lunch abt. 15 — After lunch, taking Ralph with me, I rode down to K. & V's in Canal S — got a couple of Rey p/p. & then to studio where I tacked them up on boards to stretch. It was nearly 5 where I got home — had a nap before dinner — soon after 8 A. & I went to Walter's — a little musical entertainment — & had a delightful evef. Trios. Piano, Violin & 'Cello & Song. Miss Bower. Piano & 'Cello — Duo Piano & Violin — Solo — Violin — etc music ver ab all — then a supper After 12 when we left — to bed after 1.

Jade 1 h

## Right Page

### March, Friday 25, 1892

Beautiful day — bright, breezy & tried after 9½. Rode down to K. & V's in Canal S — getting there before 10. Young Bright took two p/p. from Jade plate — very satisfactory condition — to studio at 11 & tacked p/p. down — At 12 went to Barber's — haircut & head shave good — to lunch at 1½ — Annie not there — after lunch wrote to Herrick — then lay down & had a nap — then again to K. & V's & got two more Rey plate p/p., then to studio & had them tacked down — Home soon after 6 — dinner — Evef. Geo. Nellie and Chase at the house — At 8 I went to meeting of the Etching Club at Nicolay's Room. 135 E. 15 S — only eight members present — to discuss the matter of Exhi[b]n of Etchings at the Chicago Ex[h]n. next year — A little headway was made. Home before 10.30 — To bed at 11.

Jade 2 h.
