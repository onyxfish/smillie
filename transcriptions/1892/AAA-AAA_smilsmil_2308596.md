## Left Page

### April, Thursday 21, 1892

Dull & chilly — Rain falling all day —
sometimes heavily — Had my bkfst. in
bed — up at about 10 — & before 11, Mr.
Halsey C. Ives, Chief of the Art. Dept —
Columbian S[o]c[iet]y, called with C. F. Mielatz.
He stayed until near 12 talking over
the composition of committees for the ex[h].
He came to offer me the com. on Etching —
of course I could not accept.

Dinner at 1.30 — a long rest after
it — then wrote a letter to Mr. Ives.
Dinner soon after 6 — Evely Annie
Kate & the girls went to Choral
Club concert at Mad. Sq. Garden
concert hall — Mary stayed at
home, having a headache. Geo
came in & stayed until 10.
I got to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 22, 1892

A dull day with a good deal of
rain — Aroused early by bowel trouble
— then to bed & slept until 9 o'clk — then
up & had a light bkfst. Wrote a note
to K. & V. saying that I could not keep
my engagement with them. Then
wrote to Heinele. Lunch abt.
Immediately after, Annie, Mary,
Kate & the girls went to Daly's to
see "the Foresters" by Tennyson —
About 5 o'clk — Dr. Yale came and
gave me some advice concerning
my bowel trouble — In the afternoon
A — M — K — & the girls went to Daly's
& saw Tennyson's "Foresters" — Evely
chase came — Annie & I were very
tired & went to bed leaving him
talking to Mary & Kate.
