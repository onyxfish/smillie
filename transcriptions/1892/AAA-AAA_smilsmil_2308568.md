## Left Page

### February Tuesday 23, 1892

Cloudy day — to studio at 10 — Very
soon went in to Tiffany's with one
of the drawings for front door glass —
Saw Soris & was with him selecting glass
until 12 o'clk — Then to studio and
finished the remaining drawing —
Took it to him — Spent a few min.
at N.A. looking at Etchings etc —
Home to lunch at — back at 2.30
wrote a course of notes — Then went
to Wunderlich's & spent some time
there looking at his mezzotint coll.
trying to learn something — Then to
Kaspeel's & spent some time there —
Then to Klackner's with pfp. of my
[illegible] aqua-tint plate — He says he
thinks he can sell 50 pfp. for me — So
I will have them printed — back
to studio & got two pfp. into frames
to take to K. & N. as samples to print
from — Home at 6.15 — dinner —
Evg spent making a list of veg.
& flower seeds for Montrose — until
10.

## Right Page

### February Wednesday 24, 1892

Cloudy & very Raw — Got to studio
at 10 — Then in to Tiffany's & spent some
time arranging glass for front door — Then
to studio & soon Dr. Gerster came in —
He spent most of the mtg. with me
& bought $39 worth of my pfp. I went
to bank with his check & then home to
lunch — To studio at 3 — again in
at Tiffany's for a little while — Then
to studio & worked on Jade plate
for awhile — cut down to paper to
take some pfp. to-morrow — Home
at 6.15 — dinner — Evg. worried
over my lists of vegetable seeds —
wrote a short note to Koehler
& took it to G.C. Station before 10.
Then to Chase's Room taking with me
box of plate-holders & plates exposed to
Mad. Sq. Garden tower some time ago.
Home at 10.30 — then to bed.

Jade 2 h.
