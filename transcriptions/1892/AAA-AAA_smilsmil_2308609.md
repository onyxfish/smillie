## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 17, 1892

Beautiful day. Got to studio at 10 after having done my marketing
for the dinner to-night. Then went in to Dr. Gerster's & Dr. Lilienthal
looked at my wound — & commenced a treatment of my soft corn — then
I took camera & spent some time about Madison Sq. getting photos.
of the Mad. Sq. garden tower etc —
Then to studio & cut boards to send
pfs. to Boston — Home to lunch at 1.30
to studio at 3 — made a pkg. of pfs. of
Vickery & Jade plates for Boston museum.
& took it to Adams Exps. before 5 — Then
home & rested awhile before I dressed
Dinner at 7 — Edw. & Mary — Geo. &
Emily. Walter & Louise — Jno. Valentine
& Miss Williams — The dinner was
a very good one & the guests I think
enjoyed themselves. They did not
get up from the table until after 9
& did not leave the house until 11 —
We got to bed at 11.30 & the coffee
kept us awake for an hour longer.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 18, 1892

Clear & warm — Got to studio at 9.30
& 10 Anderson's two men & truck came
& took all my books, portfolios etc. etc
up to the house — They got away from
the house before 11. I went ahead of
them & "bossed the job." Then set off to the
studio stopping on my way at Dunstie's
& arranging a frame full of my etchings
for Dr. Gerster. Home to lunch at 1 —
Annie cut off Ralph's curls — A darkie
carried books & things up to the attic —
then he went to studio with me and
did some heavy work, until 4 o'clk.
I worked until to getting things into
greater confusion than ever — Then
home to dinner — Eve'g I wrote
notes etc — Geo. Cook auditing Annie's
& ch Kitchen Accts.
