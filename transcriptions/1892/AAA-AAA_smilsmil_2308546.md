## Left Page

### January, Friday 8, 1892

A beautiful winter day. I got up with a headache & fought through it all day. Got to studio about 10.30 & painted on Charl's picture until 1 — then home to lunch — no one there but Kate who had arr. from Po'k. Back to studio at 2.40 & painted until 4.30 — then to Saml. Colman's to see him — very sick with [illegible] & other things — saw Mrs. C. only. Home soon after 5 pretty well used up myself — on the bed until dinner time. Evelf. I begged off from going to the Morse Reception to meet Stockton. Charl came in & took Annie, Mattie Adams & Katchen Cook to the cars — Katie Adams was in Harlem for the evelf. I took a dose of Castor oil for my entertainment & got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 9, 1892

A raw & disagreeable day. For reasons entirely personal I got up at 5 a.m. but soon got back to bed. I got up to help: & after that went to the butcher's but feeling very much under the weather I returned to the house & spent the rest of the m'f on the bed. After lunch I went to the studio & painted awhile on Chas' picture. Later in the afternoon Charl & his friend from Japan, Mr. Bernard came in. Before 5 I went to the [illegible] of the Architectural league at Ortgies' [illegible] — there until 6 — then home to dinner. Katchen Cook & the Adams girls dined & Walter's. Annie, Kate & I went to Phil. concert. After our return waited up until a little after 11 when Walter brought the girls home.
