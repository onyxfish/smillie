## Left Page

### May, Friday 13, 1892

*Annie & the boys went to Newhaven*

overcast, cool day — after 10 I
went to Dr. Gerster's & Dr. Lilienthal
cauterised me — Then to studio &
packed portfolios — got paper
web & the thin jalp. Jade [illegible] bet-
ween sheets to dampen. Home
to lunch ab't 1.30 after going to the
2° Nat. Bk. for Annie — At
2.30 I went to Grand Central Station
with Annie, Ralph & Jim & got them
off on the 3 o'clk train — Then to studio
Spent a couple of hours mounting
jade [illegible]. D. Yale came in for
awhile. At 6 Geo. & I walked
up home together — Dinner — Eve'g
I went to Charl's Room & got his
camera etc — Then home — about
9 he came & stayed until 10.30
Then I got to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 14, 1892

*To Newhaven*

wet m'f. Then a little dryer — then
again rain enough to wet the pave-
ments — I got to studio at about 10 —
soon went over to N.A. of D. to take
a last look at the pictures — spent
some time in making a transfer
to the Academy of my painting, "A
[illegible] brook with some sheep". By this I
fully complete my qualification as
Academician and withdraw my
Siena sketch that served temporarily
to gratify me. Then Shurtleff [illegible]
[illegible] me with a petition for specific
duty on foreign pictures — and I wrote
out one that I liked better than the one
he had — At last I got a short look
at the pictures & home to lunch at 1 —
after that I had a nap & came near
losing my train at 3 o'clk — I got
to New Haven at 5 & walked up to
the Adams house — found all well —
Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g Annie went with
the young folks to a Comic operetta
performance by the Columbia College
boys — I chatted with the Doctor
until 9.30 — To bed at 10.
