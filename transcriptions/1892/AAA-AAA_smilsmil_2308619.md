## Left Page

### June Monday 6 1892

*Home again.*

Bright & again very warm — up before
7 — Nellie breakfasted before us & went
in to N.Y. by an early train — we had
H.kp. at 7.30 — then a pleasant little
ride & to R. R. Station in time for the
9 1st train — changed car at Branchville
& again at So. Norwalk & got to N.Y. at
11.25 — home — changed clothes &
got to Dr. Gerste's at 12 — He went
with me to D. B. Butler's & selected
my little oil painting — "Sketch from
nature — a bit on the Pattens Brook".
Then to Bank — then home with
some ice-cream & had lunch —
After that A. & I went to Murray Hill
Hotel to see the Colemans — not in —
a little thunder shower — then I went
to Douglass' — to grocery — to market
& home — at 4.15 went to Niclatz's in
2.15" $— Sub com. Chicago Exp. [Etching]
no one there but us & myself — Then
went shopping for shoes & bought a
rubber suit — Home at 6.30 — dinner
Eve. W. & Mrs. Saml. Coleman
came in & we had a pleasant
visit — They left before 10 & we
got to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### June Tuesday 7 1892

Overcast w.f. & cool — cleared by noontime
— delightful cool afternoon & eve. It was
about 10 when Annie — Ralph & I went to
Deming's on a shopping trip — then to
Kulp's where I got an outfit of linen &
underclothes — then, leaving A. I went to
Wall St. & saw W. J. Pierpont Morgan — he said
I might take the plate, portrait of his father,
with me & finish it abroad — He introduced
me to his credit clerk — then I went to Charli's
& with him returned & arranged for a Co.
of $1000 — then to Curran's — to see negatives of
Madison Sq. garden photos — then home at
1.30 & had lunch — Spent the afternoon
cleaning & packing away things — Dinner
at a little before 7 — Eve. Charlie came in
& stayed until after 10 — to bed at 11.
