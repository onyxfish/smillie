## Left Page

### February Tuesday 9, 1892

A dull m'f. but sunshine came out before noon — I got up with a headache but having an engagement at K. & V.'s to have finished p.p. taken from my Rey plates I had to go whether able or no. Got there at 10 & young V. took four very satisfactory India p.p. for me — now I consider the plate finished and it is a great relief. To studio & left the p.p. in a rubber sheet — then home at a light lunch & then gave up to my headache & slept until after 4 o'clk. Then walked down to the studio — Dinner at 6 — Evening went to George's sale at Ortgies' Gallery — First night — a large number of people there but not many buyers — Charl sat with me — He bought one painting — I bought two for Annie — The sum of G.'s sales was a trifle over $3000. He had hoped for $5000. After 10 Charl & I walked to the cor. of 5th Ave. & 45th St. for Annie, but the Choral Club had gone — then home — 11.30 before I got to bed.

## Right Page

### February Wednesday 10, 1892

A bright day — was not able to get out of my bed owing to a horrible headache until 3 o'clk. Then I got up & dressed & at about 4, with Rackh, went to studio & tracked down the four India p.p. of the Rey plates. Home before 6 — dinner — Eve'g A. & I went to the 2d. night's sale of George's paintings. A very good number of people present but very slow bidding & rather poorer prices than last night — I bought a 16" x 24". Franconia subject by Walter for $95.00 It was 11 o'clk when we got home.

The amt. of G.'s sale Tonight a little short of $3000 — Total, for the Two nights — 68 paintings — about $6000.
