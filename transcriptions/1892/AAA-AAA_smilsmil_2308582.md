## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 22, 1892

A wtf. A pale, milky sunlight gradually growing duller as the day advanced - and getting damper & more raw until evening when some rain fell - Ralph & I had a little walk before bkfs. After attending to marketing etc I went over to Fifth Ave Art galleries & took a look at the "Cutting" pictures - to studio about 11 - worked on Jade plate - Annie & Ralph came & soon after I we walked home together - lunch - back to studio at 2.30 - Dr. W.B. Wood called & spent some time with me - Paid for & took away his dry-point pfs. I worked until near 5 - then to K.&K. & got two damps pf. of Jawbins Rey plates - back to studio & tacked them to stretch - Home before 6.30 - dinner - Evening Annie went to her Choral Club & I wrote to W. Clinton Folger, 312 California St. San F— in answer to his note asking for information concerning aqua-tint - He owns a pf. of my "old House near Boulogne." Annie came in at 10.30 - then to bed -

Jade - 4 h.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 23, 1892

Dark, wet day - some very heavy rain in the wtf - After bkfs. stopped at Walter's with his sketches for frame for hall mirror. The [illegible] at bkfs. met there Miss Bower of Phila. To studio at 10.30 & worked until 1 on Jade plate - then home to lunch & back at 2.30 - on Jade until near 5 - Miss Abbott came in for some of her plaster casts - I waxed 5"×7" aqua-tint plate & carefully wrapped it & took it to the long Island Exps. Co. - addressed to Pratt his. Ryerson S. - Then home before 6 - Dinner - Evtf at 8.30 Annie & I went to Walter's & heard Miss Bowen sing & Louise play. Miss B. being very tired excused herself after one song & we [illegible] home at 9.30 — Chat with Kate - [illegible] left soon after 10 - to bed before 11.

Jade - 3 h.
