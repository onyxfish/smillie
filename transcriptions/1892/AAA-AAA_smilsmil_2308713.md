## Left Page

### December Tuesday 13, 1892

A really pleasant day, frosty, cloudy,
less & almost clear. I got to Goreding
at 10:15 & was there until nearly 1.
while he took five [illegible] of the Morgan
portrait. I tried to get him to take
them a trifle cleaner but he did not
seem to be open to suggestions — I wish
I had a press & could try my hand at
a few p[illegible]. He took one Vellum [illegible] — that
seemed to be a fine one if not too dark.
I had to take a cab to get home I had
such awkward bundles to carry. Dined
near 2 o'clk. before I got my dinner.
After it, A— went to Hampstead & I
walked to Mr. Frank Short's studio where
I had a very interesting talk with him
about megatint grounds & rockers. I had
mine with me. It was after 5 when
I got home. A— has a sewing woman
at work for her to-day. Evey I wrote
a Xmas letter to Kate.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday 14, 1892

A nasty, wet day — owing to some house-
cleaning operations things were delayed
after [illegible]; but as soon as I could I got
away for the British Museum where I
studied Goltzius until near 2 o'clk.
Then went to the Restaurant Frascati, but
one trial do not think that his quite
up to "The Holborn" — At Print Room until
there, as there was but little rain I
walked to the Arts Club & wrote a note
to Wm. Chase concerning Am. Etchings for
the B.M. Print Room. Then on to Tracy Corbyn
& Co., Druggists & got a lot of medicines to take
with us when we leave London. I got
home at 6. Annie got in but a little
before, having been to a Ballad Concert.
Evey I fussed with Morgan [illegible] getting
me ready to send to the Morgan office &
another to give to the Museum.
