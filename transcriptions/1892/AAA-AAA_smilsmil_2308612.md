## Left Page

### May, Monday 23, 1892

Pleasant cool day. Bkfs. at 7.30
got to studio at 9.30 — Gasman
took down all gas fixtures & I
worked at my packing until noon.
Then to Na[illegible]. & to 2. Nat. Bk.
back to studio — Geo. came in —
just down from Ridgefield. Home
to lunch at 1 — after lunch went to
Manhattan Storage warehouse — then
to studio & worked until near 6.
An [illegible] from Storage house came
& thought that I had 1½ van load.
I gave orders to have a van &
men on hand for removal on
Wed. at 9.30 a.m. Home soon
after 6 — very tired — dinner —
shelf, looking over newspapers
until after 9 — then attending
to my foot.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 24, 1892

Beautiful day. Bkfs. at 7.30. When
I got to studio at 9 Jerry was there
with his wagon & two men — he quickly
got out my paintings & took them
to D.B. Butler's — Then I went to Dr.
Gerstein's & had a very pleasant visit.
He refuses to present a bill — Then
again to studio & worked until
after 1 — then home to lunch — back
at 2.30. It was after 5 when the
engineer came up & it took him
more than an hour to take down
the press — It was 6.40 when I
got home — dinner — About 8
I went down to studio — brought
back some [illegible] — Mr. Valentine
had come to make a short call.
I attended to my foot & got to
bed soon after 10.
