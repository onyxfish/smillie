## Left Page

### January, Monday 4, 1892

A very cold day — clear until overcast afternoon — Got to Kimmel & Voigt's at 10 o'clk. with Rey plate Young V — Took three india [illegible] very unsatisfactory — very unlike what he took last being harsher & blacker. I was very much discouraged. Got to studio before 12 — Fussed about until 1 o'clk — worked a little on Gifford's etching — Home to lunch. Galbraith's put up picture moulding in library in the afternoon — I spent the rest of the day painting a sketch for Century 12th night. Taking 7. Hopkins on brietts for my text. Home at 6 — dinner — Evening spent hanging etchings in the library & arranging things to some extent — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 5, 1892

Brilliant until, overcast afternoon. Got to studio about 10, Fussed awhile over 12th night sketch for Century club — Damps brought Char's painting done & I washed that & got out a sketch made last summer that I am going to work upon. Home about 1. Katie & Mollie Adams arr'd during the mft. To studio at 2·5  Gifford came in just as I was leaving at 5 o'clk — stayed for half an hour — Home at 6 — Katcher & the Adams girls dined at Ed's Cook's,  After dinner Took Annie to Mr. Greene's 39 S. & Lex. Ave, to go to their Choral Club Rehearsal — I ret'd home & finished up the Etching Club paper to get it into the printers hands to-morrow. At 10 went for Annie — To bed about 11.
