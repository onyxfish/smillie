## Left Page

### February Sunday 21, 1892

D is agreeable day — Rain until
the afternoon. continuing cloudy &
Raw. — Blfso. at 9.30 — nitter coun-
f the mf. Jas. Wright was brought
to the house with his bag by his
son James. I wrote some notes
& a letter of instruction to Herrick
Dinner at 2 — Jas. Wright & Marion
Geo. & Smisely & Frank Adams
with us & at dessert Walter &
Louise & Edw? & Mary came in —
later came Jno. Valentine. At
4 o'clk I walked down to studio
with Jim & Ralph & home again
soon after 5 — Tea after 6 —
Evelf, Jas. W. Marion, Annie &
Frank went to a Damrosch concert
in 57. 8. I stayed at home &
had a quiet time — Meg ret. —
at 10.30 — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February Monday 22, 1892

Brilliant day — Jas. Wright & Marion
off for Boston on 9 A.m. train. Frank
Adams ret? to New Haven at 3 P.m.
I got to studio at 10 & had several calls
during the mf., St. Yester among them.
I spent the mf. in Taking aqua-tint
[illegible] from off the board — trimming them,
selecting etc. Home at — back at
2.30 Taking Jim & Ralph with me.
Jim tired & left at about 4 — Ralph
remained until I ret? I worked
at a number of odds & ends — while
in 20" × 24" noming among Adirondack
mis" — sand [illegible] sky etc —
Home before 6. Dinner. Evelf
going over sale catalogues trying
to make up the usual spring
lists — To bed at about 11.
