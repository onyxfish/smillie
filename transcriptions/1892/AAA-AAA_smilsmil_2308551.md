## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 20, 1892

Clear & very cold — This is a very
great comfort once more to see the
sunshine & I feel as though now
I might get well. F. Yale came
in during the evef. to see how I
was getting along. I felt much
better & spent some time writing
up my Montrose summer garden
instructions for Apollos Stone —
Evef. Chare came in again
& spent the evef. with Annie
& me.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 21, 1892

Still cold & clear — Got up to my
off p. & at about 11.30 Ralph & I went
to the studio where I dampened some
[illegible] Taken from my dry-point plates
last week & put them in press. It
was nearly 1.30 when we got home
to lunch — Afternoon, again I
wrote on garden instructions —
Evef. with Annie, quietly in
front of a fine fire, [illegible — ~3 words]
