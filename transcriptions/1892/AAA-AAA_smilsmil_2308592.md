## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 13, 1892

Was helped out of bed & into
a suit of clothes & then by Frank's
aid I got into my chair & was
moved into the parlor where for
a time I sat & enjoyed au
beau fine.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 14, 1892

A dull day - with light rain & snow
in the afternoon - rain at night - It
was near 8 o'clk. before I started Frank
from his bed - I then had my massage
& after that my help - then a rest & then
Frank had other duties - after them I
rested & had a short nap - Then Dr. Yale
came in & learning that Dr. Forster was
expected about noon he went away to come
back while Dr. F. was here - Then I was
got up & dressed - Dr. F. came - & so
did El - my progress was pronounced
to be entirely satisfactory - After they
left I had a beefsteak dinner - I
sat up until 4 - then another nap -
Then up - Walter called to see me.
- then Mrs. Geo. Tea before 7. - Eve'g
Annie read aloud more of "Cranford".
About 9.30 I submitted myself to Frank
when I was again put through a course
of massage & then to bed for the night.

Mr. Vaile, of K. & V. came to the house bringing
with him 82 K&P. of San Luis Rey mission plates
for me to sign - He took one signed one
away to be delivered as directed by Mr. Vickery.
