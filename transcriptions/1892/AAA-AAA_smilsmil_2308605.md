## Left Page

### May, Monday 9, 1892

*Returned to N.Y.*

Delightful day - Up at 7 - Bkf. at 7.30
After it we wandered about the place
(not much area for wandering) until 9 o'clk
then a hack came & we rode to the
station & took 9.25 train to N.Y. After
a comfortable ride got to Grand Central
Station at 11.25. Annie ret. to Yonkers
by Northern R.R. & lunched with Mr. Weller
& other friends - I went to the house &
did marketing etc. etc before lunch
at 1 o'clk - At 2.30 Ralph & I went to the
studio - I packed large case with rugs
& curtains for Montrose. At 4.30 we went
to Union Storage Co. in 22d S. - then away over
to the East River - 20th & then 19th St. - to find
packing cases - without success - Returned
went to Stern Bro's in 23d St. - no cases - Then
to Arnold & Constable's - wholesale dept. - &
at 5.30 found them closed - Men back to
studio - got our coats & home at 6.30 -
Annie got in before 7 & we had dinner
Evg. Jack Downs called. Then Charl
came in - To bed at 10.30 -

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 10, 1892

Pleasant altho' overcast most of the day.
Geo. came to the house before 10 - just
arr. from Ridgefield - we walked to
the studio - I screwed the top on to
Montrose box & at 1 o'clk, Siffp-
man came for it - I worked at the
packing generally - Annie went to
Flushing to spend the day - Geo.
went home with me to lunch - I
went to studio before 3 - after a
while went to Arnold & Constable's
& got three packing cases - again
to studio - cases delivered - Home
at 6 - Evg. at 6.40 Annie & I
went to Edw. Cook's to dinner -
Emma & Grace J. - there - Later,
Mr. Valentines came in - We
left before 10.30 - Geo. spent the
Evg. with Charl & the night
with us - To bed at 11.
