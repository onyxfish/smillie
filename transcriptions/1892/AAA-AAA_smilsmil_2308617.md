## Left Page

### June, Thursday 2, 1892

Ther. got up to 91°

An exceedingly hot day - got away soon after b'kfst. I had a busy m'g —
went to market — then to Morais's - to
Higgins' - to Manhattan Storage - then
to O'Niels - Clark's, Bayerdorfer's - to
Tiffany Co. - to Dr. Gerster's where Dr.
Iklinthal attended to my corn - Then
with sketching umbrella to Hemming's
- to Denning's - to Kimmel & Voigt's &
got Onion skin paper (Jap.) p'd f the
Kidney plate - Then to 2d. Nat. Bank &
attended to Annie's June business
Then to Horton's - got ice cream & home
to lunch at 1.15. After lunch young
Moran came & attended to some
small matters - I did some work
about the house & wrote a number
of notes - Dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g
took camera & plate box to Charl's room
Then read M.S. of Koehler's on Jengling
& the new school of wood engravers -
Charl came in at 9 & stayed
until after 10 - To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 3, 1892

Much cooler — overcast — with some
sunshine at times - attended to market
up before b'kfst, work about the house
until 10 - then to Dr. Gerster's to have
him go with me to D.B. Butler's & make
a selection from among my paintings
there - he had not the time - arranged
to call at 5 p.m. on my way home
tried to buy shoes at O'Neil's but
could not find what I wanted -
at 12 rode up to 70th St. & left at Mr.
Helen Bishop's house, 881 Fifth Ave.,
jade plate & p'f. Then home at 1 -
lunch - work about the house until
near 5 - again to Gerster's - he had
not yet come in - Dinner at 6.30 -
Eve'g. Chas. Collins came in -
To bed soon after 10.
