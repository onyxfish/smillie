## Left Page

### February, Monday 1, 1892

A disagreeable day — overcast, rain
but not cold — quite a shower bet.
12 + 1 o'clk. Got to studio about 10.
lighted gas under printing table, then
went to 2° Nat. Bnk + attended to
some business for A —. Among other
things effected a loan for her of $1500 —
to pay Wickham for Carpenter work in
the house. To studio at 11 — but Nielaty
Colman, Yale + others came + at 1
o'clk. I had not taken a proof —
Home to lunch — back at 2.30 + worked
hard as long as I could see — among
other [illegible] Took four fine India pk of
5×7 aqua-tint, "See old dawn".
Home at 6 — Margery Spring there —
She dined with us. Self, She + A —
went to rehearsal of Mendelssohn
Glee Club — I stayed at home +
wrote a couple of notes + did some
reading — They came in at 10.20 —
Margery's maid was waiting for
her + took her to the Irelands' in
2.17ᵃ S.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 2, 1892

A disagreeable day. Rain. To studio
am about 10 to 4 — To studio som [illegible]
to [illegible] the mf. burnishing on
the key plate — Home to lunch + back
as usual — A little more work with
the burnisher — then I met [illegible] for
printing to-morrow + home before 6 —
Dinner — eve'g. Annie + I went to
Chickering Hall — 2°. Mendelssohn
Glee Club concert — a very charming
entertainment — a full house + a hot
one — Emily sat with us + G[illegible] not far
off — Dr Willard Parker in front of us.
Home before 11 + to bed.
