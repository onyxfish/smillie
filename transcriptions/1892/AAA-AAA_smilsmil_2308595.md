## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 19, 1892

A delightful day — It was after 10 when I got started for the studio.
I got one pl. of etching after Homer's
"Voice from the Cliff" — one of Johnson's
"A glass with the Squire" & after 11 I
went to Dr. Gerster's with them — I
was taken up to his attic where I
found him busily engaged taking
some prints from one of his etchings.
I ret? to studio after 12 and got
home before 1 — lunch — wrote a
note of thanks to Mr. Hydes for his
present of a box at one of the Mem-
orial Phil. Concerts — This week — At
about 4 Annie, Ralph & I went to Denning's
about curtains for library. Then I went
to the Studio — found there letter, with check,
from W. K. Vickery — Home at 6 — Dinner
at 6.30 — Ev'g. At 7.30 Annie, Mary and
Kate went to the third (last, this season) Con-
cert of the Mendels[s]ohn Glee Club — At
9 o'clk. Charl came in for a few minutes
on his way to his Rooms from his train —
Just in from Asheville — 6 hours behind
time — very mad & very hungry —

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 20, 1892

Brilliant & beautiful day — At home
writing notes until 10.30 — Then to studio
& from there to 2° Nat. Bk. where I
made a deposit to my cr. Got home
with some difficulty & had trouble
with bowels — I kept quiet during
the afternoon — but assorted the 80 pls.
of Sandelin's Rey mission & numbered
& signed 40 of them. Dinner at
6.30 Ev'g. Mary, Kate & the girls
went to Charl's & spent the ev'g
there. Annie & I had a quiet
ev'g at home — To bed at 10 —
Temperature normal — Pulse 84.
