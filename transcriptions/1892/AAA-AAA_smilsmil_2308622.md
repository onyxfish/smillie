## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 14, 1892

Another tremendously hot day with a grand thunderstorm bet. 8 & 9 o'clk
P.M. Annie & I met Geo. Cooke at the
2nd Nat. Bk. at 10 A.m. where A. gave
him power of attorney to act for her
during our absence — also to have
access to the safe deposit box. We
made a careful list of the contents
of the box — finishing at 11.30. Then
Annie, Ralph & I went off & did some
shopping — we parted in 4th ave
at 12.30 — I then went to the tailor,
bother places getting home at
lunch. Then rested awhile before
getting to work at packing up the
remainder of the loose back numbers
of the Ill. Lond. News. Finished that
& worked at other things until after
6 — Dinner before 7 — Eve'g arranging
my etching pf's — some of them I will
take with me.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 15, 1892

Pleasant day, growing steadily cooler.
Another busy day — of work in doors
and out. Kate came down from Po'k
getting to the house before noon. She
has just come down to take a look
at our steamer & to say "good-bye".
In the afternoon, about 4 o'clk, I
went to Sarony's to see why no pf's
of photos. had been sent — no excuse.
Then walked up town stopping at
various places "shopping".

Even'g, Charl came in — just arr'd
from Chicago. Dr. Yale also came
in & spent the eve'g with us —
To bed at about 11.
