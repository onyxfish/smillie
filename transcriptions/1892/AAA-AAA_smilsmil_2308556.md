## Left Page

### January, Saturday 30, 1892

As breezy as March & as fickle
as April — some sunshine in the
m't — overcast afternoon. To studio
soon after 10 & spent the morning
burnishing on aqua-tint. "An old
dam" — Home at 1.30 — back at
2.45 — a little more burnishing
then cut paper & prepared to
print on Monday — Began to
feel uncomfortable about 4.30
— could scarcely drag myself
home at 5 o'clk — had trouble
with bowels & so much discomfort
that I went to bed instead of going
to Louise's reception where A.
was "assisting" & expecting me —
After 7 was able to eat a little —
after 8 Annine came in & after
9 Charl came — he stayed until
after 10 & then I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 31, 1892

A very mild, pleasant day — I
felt the remains of yesterday's un-
pleasantness — mean head ache
& general dissoluteness of feeling.
After bkfs — spread myself on my
back & read until 12.30 — Then
Emma D. came in for a few
moments while A. was out.
[illegible] went to studio, taking Jim
& Ralph & was down to paper for
tomorrow's printing — Home to
dinner at 2. At 4 I walked
up to 72°. S. & 5th Ave. & back.
Katie & Mollie Adams took tea with
us — they left early to go to a concert
with Ed & Mary. Eve'g I wrote a
note to Kate & an answer to Geo. Toms,
Pres. of Central Trust Co. concerning
their second default in payment
of matured loans made by Annine.
[illegible] at 11.
