## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 3, 1892

Cooler & overcast. A short walk before bkfo. after my marketing & other duties I went to Mitchell & Vance's — Bdway & 13th S. — about the hall light ordered more than a month ago — then to Kelpel's & to Wunderlich's — Bought veg. seeds at Bridgeman's & shopped for curtain cord at Lord & Taylor's & at Constable's. To studio at 12. Home at 1.30 — To studio at 3, Taking Ralph with me — I spent the p.m. cleaning out of my writing desk the accumulation of 10 years past — Home at 6.30. Dinner. Eve'g. Annie went to the Annual Business meeting of The Choral Club — I spent a part of the eve'g reading old letters & looking over old accts. Near 11 o'clk. I went to Fifth Ave 045th S. to bring Annie home, but she had left with Mrs. Greene before I got there. To bed at 11.30 —
A thunderstorm during the night

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 4, 1892

Cool & overcast w'f — gradually clearing until the afternoon when it gets to be oppressively warm — Got to studio after 10 — then to 2d Nat. Bk. & got Milwaukee 4½% bonds — then down town — got interest on them at Bk. of Commerce. went to Mills Bldg & saw Mr. Paterson — Representing Mr. Bishop — very civil & offered to pay for the jade plate. Went to Seamen's Saving Bank — had a pleasant time with the officials & closed my acct. drawing out $105.42 — accumulation of about 20 years — the principal being only about $24 — if I remember right. Then to 2d Nat. Bk and deposited $150 for Annie & $100 for myself — then home to lunch. — Before & Ralph & I went to studio where I spent a couple of hours sorting papers & old letters — Home at 6.30 — . Dinner. — Eve'g Charlie came in to sit with us until near 10 — to bed soon after —
