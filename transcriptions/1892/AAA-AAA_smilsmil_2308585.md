## Left Page

### March, Monday 28, 1892

Bright & breezy. Got to studio at 9.30
& stopped out & varnished aqua-tint
background on jade plate & a little
before 11 went up to Dr. Allen's in 47
S. was in his chair until 1 o'clk. him
a gold cap fitted over one of my teeth —
then home to lunch — Telegram from Charl
saying train from South delayed, so we
need not go to Greenwood this afternoon
To studio at 2.30 — got acid on jade
plate background — Annie left Ralph
at studio. at 4 o'clk. I took him & went
to Lyceum theatre where I met H. &
we then went to Hoboken & over the
Steamer "Werra" to look at staterooms.
Home at 6.20 — dinner — Charl came
in to say that arrangements had been
made to go to Greenwood to-morrow
mnt. Evef. at 9 o'clk. A. & I went
to Walter's to talk over some small
plans that I had drawn — back at
10.

*Jade 3 h.*

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 29, 1892

*Aunt Huldah buried in Greenwood*

Glorious day — Bkft: before 8 — abq. 15
Charl & Mr. Rylander (Son-in-law of Mrs.
Benson — i.e. Martha, daughter of Jas. V.N.)
came to the house — Mr. R. arr. early
this mnt. from Americus with the body
of Aunt H—. we went to elevated R.R.
at 34th St— Geo. joined us at 28th St— we
crossed the bridge & on the Brooklyn
side took elevated R.R. for Greenwood
getting there in one hour from 38th St— we
found hearse & body waiting for us at the
gate — after a little delay, Mr. Force, pastor
of the 5th Ave. Baptist Ch. joined us & we
went out to the lot on Ocean Hill — an opening
had been made beside mother, so close that
the box enclosing her casket & was uncovered
in part— Mr. Force made a prayer at the grave
then we ret'd— Charl & I left the rest of the
party & crossed at Wall St— At his office for
a few minutes — then to Bowling Green — Office
of North German Lloyds & secured two State
rooms on "Werra" to sail — June 1 82. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — after. Ralph at 2.30 — in his
chair until 3.15 & he did not finish — To studio for
a few minutes & at 4 went to Hoboken & took a
good look at the rooms engaged this mnt —
home soon after 6 — dinner — Evef. Annie
went to her Choral Club Rehearsal — I wrote
a short note to S.C.T. Hammonds — Charl
came in for a little while — at 10 went
for Annie & thus Greene — near 11 when I
got home & after 11 when I got to bed.
