## Left Page

### June, Monday 20, 1892

miles made - 339.

Another very warm day - part of the time sunny & bright - wind directly aft - Some heavy rolling last night & again this evening. About 8 P.M. a heavy sea was shipped forward drenching the Italian contingent & washing overboard a lot of shoes & wearing apparel. The day was absolutely uneventful - Between the warmth of the weather & the rolling of the vessel it was hard work to walk - I loafed about and tried to read or write but accomplished nothing - It was intolerably hot below where the portholes had to be kept closed - In the evening I slept awhile in my chair on deck - About 10.30 we turned in & perspired through the rest of the night.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 21, 1892

miles run 366.

Another gray day but the wind changing more to the north it was delightfully cool later in the day - Up at 6 & had my bath at 7 - a short time on deck and then below - soon after 8 - nothing of interest - had a pleasant chat with Mrs. Mitchell concerning education of children - Tried to read but felt too heavy - could only loaf - after lunch had a long nap. Soon after which it was time to go with the boys to their dinner — then a short walk on deck & it was time for our dinner. The usual two hours was spent at that — what remained of the evening was got rid of somehow & to bed after 10 — a cool & comfortable night.
