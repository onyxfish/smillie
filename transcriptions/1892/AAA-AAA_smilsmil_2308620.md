## Left Page

### June Friday 10 1892

overcast, cool & pleasant — clear late in
the afternoon — Got up with a severe
headache which passed away during
the day. I busied myself about the
house most of the m'g. After 11 went
to Kelpple & Co — 16" S. with a lot of etching
p'f— Jordan not in — then walked
on up Town attending to several little
matters with small success & home
at 1.30 — lunch — after 2 I went to
Brokaw's to get a suit of clothes — none
to suit me — then to Tomaro's & got a
new trues — then to Kelpple's — Jordan
in — he bought my etchings — among them
a lot of my "Old Dam" aqua-tints — for $79 —
much to my gratification. Then to
Smith's Sons with sketching umbrella —
to Brooks Bros. & got a suit of clothes —
to Kneedler's & to O'Neil's & got a pair
of shoes — nearly 7 when I got home
to dinner — Evett! Chase came in —
he starts for Chicago to-morrow m'g — I
marked all my new purchases of linen
& underclothes & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June Saturday 11 1892

Delightful day — after my usual domestic
duties I went down to Savony's, getting there
at about 10.30 — I posed for 4 photos & was
there about an hour — home a little after
12 — lunch at 1 — after which I went to
Yale's — found him in & gave him a little
water-color that I called "October" — in
recognition of his professional kindness
to me — Y. was in a very agreeable humor
& it was more than an hour before I left
the house — then home & worked until
6 o'clk — pasting wrappers about unbound
volumes of "The Century" magazine —
Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g, scanning
some new books until 9 o'clk.
