## Left Page

### January, Saturday 2, 1892

A dismal day — dark, with very heavy rain. Got to studio soon after 10 and bit the fine aqua-tint ground over part of Rey sky. I also did some work on small dry-point plates. Home to lunch soon after 1 o'clk — back before 3 & burnished on Rey plates. Mrs. Blackburn came with a couple of plates that she has ready to bite and I gave a little time to her. Home at 6 — Natchen Cook had arr- to make a visit — I spent most of the eve'g skimming through a book, an acct. of the mutiny on the "Bounty" & the little colony on Pitcairn's Island — It was after 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 3, 1892

A pleasant day but growing colder — some slight snow flurries in the m'fg. B'kf's about 9 — after that got to work upon Etching paper & finished before 1 o'clk, then walked up to Dr. Hales — arranged with him to call in the eve'g & have him look critically over my W.S. Home to dinner at 2. Afternoon, I made up my "dummy" for Etching Club Publication — At 5 went to Charl's & arranged with him to take Annie & Natchen to the Damrosch concert this eve'g. Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g at 8 I went to Hale's & was with him until near 10 — then home. Sat up until near 11 for Charl & A. & K. — near 12 when I got to bed.
