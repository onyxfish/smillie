## Left Page

### April, Sunday 17, 1892

Bright m'f. Rain afternoon with a
slight spotting of rain in the evening.
Bkfs. late — about noon I went
out & had a short walk — after
Dr. Gerster had paid me his last
professional visit, so he said, for this
operation. Dinner at 2 — after
that Annie went with the boys to
Church for an Easter Celebration —
Ge° came in to see the girls — I got
into an altercation with my bowels
& started my wound bleeding slightly
& got a small scare. Before 6 [illegible]
went to D'. G's & saw him & was re-
assured. Tea about 7 — many.
Kate & the girls went to Church —
Annie read aloud "Crawford" for
awhile — To bed at 10 —
Ther. 99°. Pulse 90.          — O'

## Right Page

### April, Monday 18, 1892

Bright & pleasant m'f. P.m. chilly &
overcast. Up at 7 o'clk. after a tolerable night
Up — newspaper — & at 10.30 drove down
to Dr. Gerster's office in 25th S. Found him
& had a long visit with him — then
to studio — saw Ge° & Nellie — then by
easy sauntering I walked all the way
home — getting in at about 12.30 — then
a rest — took lunch at about 1.30. Annie
lunching with a Buster lunch party
to celebrate the 8 birthday of Miss Anna
Clinch. During the afternoon I went
up & walked about the block two or three
times — & then again a rest. Dinner
at 6.30 — Evg. Annie went to Chorale
Club rehearsal at 59th S. & 7th Ave. The
rest of the family at home. After a
long chat with Mary I got to bed
after 10 —
Temp. 98⁸⁄₁₀° — Pulse 90.
