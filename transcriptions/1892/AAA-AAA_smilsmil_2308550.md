## Left Page

### January, Monday 18, 1892

A dark, wet day. I did not go
out of course — just hung about
feeling a little too uncomfortable
to do anything — tried a little
reading & did a little writing
— Katey & Mollie Adams got
to the house just at dinner
time on their way home from
Germantown — they stayed
with us overnights. Eve'g
I had pain in the head —
violent sneezing & many tears
— to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 19, 1892

A worse day than yesterday —
sleet & snow all day — I was
about as cheerful as the weather.
Did not get out of bed until noon.
Rec'd. Revised pf. & dummy for Etch-
ing Club Cat. with request to make
some slight changes in the text
to improve appearance of the pages.
I worked through it & returned it
by messenger. Katie & Mollie
went to New Haven at 3 o'clk. P.m.
Eve'g. Annie went to her Choral
Club rehearsal. Charl came in
& spent most of the eve't with me.
