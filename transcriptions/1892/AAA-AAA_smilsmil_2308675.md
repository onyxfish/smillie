## Left Page

### September Wednesday 28 1892

A really bright, sunny day after heavy rain last night — Got up with a head-ache that quite used me up during part of the afternoon — Had a good brisk walk in the park before bkfst. after that at 9.45 I went to swimming bath with Jim (& Ralph) Why the swimming master having promised to be there at 10 — waited until 10.30 & as he had not there come we returned home — I worked from 11 to 12.15 — then went out and walked until 1 — then back to dinner — In the afternoon I worked for another hour & then gave up & lay up on the bed until 5.30 — then out & walked in the park until 6.15 enjoying a lovely twilight & a young moon.

Morgan plate 2 h.

## Right Page

### September Thursday 29 1892

A bright & beautiful m'g. Had a good run in the park before bkfst. with the boys — After, as Jim complained of rheumatic pains I thought it best for him not to take a swimming lesson, so I went to the bath to see his teacher — I got to my work at 10.30 & worked until 12.30 — Being michaelmas our landlady gave us a goose for dinner — At 3, being then cold, cloudy & windy — with now & then light showers, I went to Goredings with my plate & got two p'fs. Showing good progress but with a bewildering lot of work to do yet. I am much disappointed to find that all of the drapery is considerably over-bitten — I got home at 5 & tacked my two p'fs to a board to dry — Found a letter from Heber Bishop asking me to reconsider the price I ask for my Jade plate — very civil — says he thinks the plate very artistic, etc — Tea — every I tried to write an answer to B —

Steady rain from early eve'g. until after we went to bed.

Morgan — 4 h.
