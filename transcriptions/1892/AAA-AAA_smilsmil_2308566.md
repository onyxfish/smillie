## Left Page

### February Friday 19 1892

*[Note in pink ink above printed header: "This should be Sat. 20"]*

A dismal, dark day with rain. It was late (10.30) before I got to studio — Had a little work cleaning up the mess left from last night, then to work taking p[hoto]s from aquatint. 5"×7" "An old dam" — back for lunch at 1.45 — then feeling pretty well used up I took a nap & did not wake until 4.30 — then, the rain having ceased for a time, I went to studio taking Ralph with me — & there tacked down upon boards the p[hoto]s that I took this m[ornin]g. Home at 6 — rain again — Frank Adams arr[ive]d from New Haven to spend Sunday with us — Evelf. he went out to a concert. Annie & I read —

Marion Wright arr[ive]d at the house at about 11 P.M. — to stay over Sunday.

## Right Page

### February Saturday 20 1892

*[Note in pink ink above printed header: "This should be Friday, 19"]*

A hazy, overcast day with sleety rain at night — I got to studio after 10 & began at once to prepare for the final p[hoto]s of Pratt Mrs.' place, 5"×7" aquatint, "An old dam". There were a number of interruptions so that it was after 2 before I pulled the first proof. Then to bank for Annie — home soon after 1 — To studio at 2.30 — took a few p[hoto]s — stopping that work at 4 — cleaned up & spent the time until 6 o'clk. in getting the studio into condition for Etching Club meeting to-night. Home at 6.30 — dinner — At 7.30 again to studio. A very full and interesting meeting, much to my surprise — It was 11.30 before I got home & 12 when I got to bed.

Afternoon, left 12[th] night century sketch — [illegible] Smith sketching in [illegible] at his house.

Edith
