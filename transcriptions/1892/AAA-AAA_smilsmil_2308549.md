## Left Page

### January, Saturday 16, 1892

Got to studio at about 10 — lighted gas under printing stone & then went in to Tiffany's to see my glass window in such condition that I could not see it — then back to my printing took some p[roofs] from the 3 small Yale plates — then from my 6×12 dry-point — got some very rich p[roofs] — then home at 1.30 to lunch — back at 2.30 & prooved from Hollyhock & Sweet Pea plates until my paper was used up — D[r]. Gerster came in just at dusk & we sat chatting until near 6 when we were interrupted by a young beggar professing to be an artist — I walked home. Dinner — Eve[ning] I felt very much used up & sat dozing in my big chair in front of the fire — Read a magazine story & got to bed after 11.

Bright cold & beautiful winter day.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 17, 1892

Fine day but was not able to enjoy it. Did not get out of my bed until near 2 o'clk. Used up generally — Headache, sore throat & other miseries — was able to go down to dinner — on sofa in dining room all the afternoon — At 6 o'clk. made up my mind to send for D[r]. Yale — He came at 8 & stayed with me for about an hour — says I am tired out — Geo. came in & sat with us until 10 — To bed about 11.
