## Left Page

### May Sunday 15 1892

Foggy m'g. with a raw (but not
cold) east wind — weather improving
untit about 5 o'clk. when the sun-
shine struggled out. I got up at
about 7 & had a good walk up &
down the asphalt pavement of
Prospect St. before bkfp. at 8.30 —
It was after 11 when Annie, the
boys & I went down to the Downs'
— back to dinner at 1. About
4 I had a long nap. Tea
at 6 — Ev'g, chatting in front
of a small fire untit 10 — then
to bed.

## Right Page

### May Monday 16 1892

Bright, breezy & warm — Up at 7 — a short
walk before bkfp. — at 7.40 — At 9 we
left the house & took 9.35 trains
getting into N.Y. at 11.33 — I went at
once to the barber's & had my hair
cut, then home & had a general
clean up before lunch at 1. To
market — then to studio at 2.30 — met
Dr. Leo & had a talk with him
about etching some family portraits —
Worked at studio packing untit
near 4 when Mr. S. R. Koehler came
in & we talked about my projected
European trip & my work untit
after 5 — then a little more work
& Mrs. Champney came in — It
was 6.30 when I got home to dinner.
Ev'g, wrote to Herrick.
