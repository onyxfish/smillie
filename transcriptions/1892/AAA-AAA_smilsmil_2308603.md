## Left Page

### May, Thursday 5, 1892

A delightful day - a sprinkle of a
shower between 6 & 7 o'clk. p. m. -
A walk before bkfst - to studio a little
after 10 - Delved in dish - until 1 o'clk
packing things into drawers to store
Home to lunch - after that went
to Mr Wm Macbeth's new gallery in
Fifth Ave. just above 27th St - & spent
a half hour with him - then to
studio & continued the work of
the m[illegible] until 6 - then home.
Dinner at 6.45.    [illegible] Chas
came bringing proofs from photo.
that I made last fall of the
Madison Square Garden tower.
To bed about 10.30.
Quite a heavy shower after going
to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 6, 1892

Varying weather from sunshine to rain.
Dull m[illegible]. sunny midday - a very little
rain about 5 p.m. - Annie spent the
day at Yonkers - the last day of the Unitarian
league. Before going to studio I went to
see Dr. Gerster who made an application
of caustic - 11 when I got to studio -
Until 1 worked in the dish - then home
to lunch - after that to Dr. Westervelt's
the chiropodist - Bdway near 28th S[t] - to
see if he could benefit soft corn on
little toe of right foot - smeared it with
Iodine, charged 50 cts. but would promise
nothing - 4 o'clk. when I got to studio.
Continued my work until 6 - then
home - dinner - wrote to Blaney.
[illegible] in Boston - To Herrick & to
Halsey C. Ives - ab[ou]t 9 went out and
posted letters - ab[ou]t 10, to bed.
