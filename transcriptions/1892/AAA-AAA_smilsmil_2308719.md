## Left Page

### December, Sunday 25, 1892

Really a sunny day — but below freezing
all day — with a dry, east wind. — we had
our bfst. at 9 — & then, after that, our Xmas
presents. It was after 11 when we all
started out — walked to Marble Arch — then
was to Victoria Station — then walked to
Westminster Abbey — of course, could not
get in — then across Westminster Bridge —
along the Albert Emblt. to Lambeth Palace.
— about that & then back — taking R.R. to
Praed St. & getting home before 2. Soon
after our dinner was served — grand style
— a Turkey & fixings — a plumb pudding — cakes
& fruit — a very nice dinner — what little
was left of the afternoon I spent reading
& sleeping. — To — soon after 6 — Every.
wrote a letter to Saml. Colman.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 26, 1892

Another cloudless day but with much
smoke that made the sun look like a
wafer — very cold. Ther. did not get above
freezing. I was in-doors all day at
work packing up my things — except for
a short walk before dinner —
Eveg. I wrote a letter to S.R.
Koehler.
