## Left Page

### February Monday 29 1892

A dull day — very cold but not so
cold as yesterday — Before 10 o'clk I
started in town + got to the Museum
at about 11.30 — entrance by back
door — found S. R. K. in his dep't —
+ until after 1 o'clk. we looked
over old prints — especially aqua-
tints by dePrince & others — Then
we went out to a restaurant +
had a good luncheon + did not
get back to the studio until about
3 — From that time until 5 we
were in the galleries of the mu-
seum. Then I set out to Cambridge
walking, as usual, from Harvard
Sq. to Sue's house. Getting in after
6, cold + pretty tired — Supper —
we all had a good toasting in front
of a big fire — Mr. & Mrs. Rob. Topping
spent part of the eve'g with us —
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March Tuesday 1 1892

*Returned to New York.*

Another dull mt. — B'kfst: at 7.30 — At
8.30 Annie + I started for the city and
reached the Providence Station at 9.30
+ had to wait until 10 for our train — Before
noon snow + sleet began to fall — high
wind — the parlor car draughty and
uncomfortable — about 1 had a light
lunch — got in to N.Y. on time at
4.30 — walked home through a nasty
slush. Found all well — had
dinner after 6 — Eve'g Annie went
to her Choral Club Rehearsal — I
had a quiet time at home & wrote
a note to Kate — on my way for
it at 10 o'clk — mailed it at G. C. S. —
Home at 10.30 + to bed at 11.
