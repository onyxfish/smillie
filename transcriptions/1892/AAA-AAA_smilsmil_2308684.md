## Left Page

### October, Sunday 16, 1892

No gleam of sunshine to-day — consider-
able rain in the am — cold, raw and
windy. Bkfst. late — Annie went to ch.
to hear Moncured. Conway on Tennyson.
I took a short walk in the Park with
the boys but was driven in by the
rain. Then I wrote for some time
a letter to Kate — a few min. walk
before dinner at 2 — After that a
series of naps — then a long walk along
Hyde Park to Whitehall — some exploring
there — bus from Trafalgar Sq. to Marble
Arch — walked the rest of the way home.
Tea at 7 — Evng wrote reply to Mr.
Theo. Satchell's note putting a number
of questions concerning etching, prints
and study — posted it & took a short
walk at 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 17, 1892

A bright, breezy, cold m'g — overcast at noon.
Heavy showers after 4 — all clear again at 5.
After bkfst. I went to Swimming school to tell
Whyte that Jim was out of sorts to-day & would
not take his lesson — then to Whiteley's & bought
two sets of heavy wool underclothing — also
some asbestos dumplings for our gas grate fires.
To work on my plate from 11.30 to 1.30 — then
dinner — To work again at 2.45 & quit
at 4.45. Then Jim & I went out and
had a walk to the Marble Arch and back.
Tea — Evng I finished a long letter to
Kate.

Morgan — 4 h.
