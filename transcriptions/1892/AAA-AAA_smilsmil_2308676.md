## Left Page

### September Friday 30 1892

Showers & sunshine all day - more shadow than sunshine - caught in
my walk before bkfst. After that
I wrote reply to Mr. Bishop's note.
Then, at 11 got to work on Morgan
plate - stoned down the over bitten
work on coat & gave the background
a hard papering - worked until
near dinner time - at 3 - at it again
& worked until 5 - with a graver &
dry point on face. Annie went to
call on Mrs. Mercure D. Conway in the
afternoon - Between 5 & 6 I walked
to the Marble Arch & back - then
tea - Eve'g, reading old Nf[?] papers
- wrote note to Charl & part of
a letter to George.

morgan - 4 h.

## Right Page

### October Saturday 1 1892

Steady rain until after 12 - then suddenly
it cleared sunny & very pleasant - moon-
light at night. I worked on my plate
until a little after 12 - then cleaned up
& at 1 o'clk. with A. & boys went to Praed St.
Station & thince to Swiss Cottage Station
then to Dr. Walter Satchellers. H Roseleyn
Gardens, Belsize Lane, Hampstead, N.W.
His br. Theodore & his wife to meet us. Dinner
at 2. After dinner, walked on the Heath
with the brothers - back at 5 - a cup of
tea & some thin bread after a little music
by the family band - violin 'cello - piano
& piano organ. We left soon after 6
& got home soon after 7 - some of the
dwellings about Hampstead, detached,
with large plots of ground & vegetation
very pleasant - also some of the Crescents
on getting home we had a little
more bread & butter & weak tea -
Eve'g I read a little & felt very stupid

morgan - 2 h.
