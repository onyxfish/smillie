## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 12, 1892

A pleasant day - less sunshine than yesterday - A short walk in the Park before bkft. Got to work at 9.30 - did until 12.30 - then walked until 1.15 - then dinner - Got to work again from 2.30 to 4.30 - It was 5 when I went to Praed St. Station & by underground R.R. went to Westminster Bridge. I was too late in starting - took me 29 m. to get there I had but 15 m. to cross the bridge & walk a little ways on the Albert embankment. Home at 6.30 - Tea. Eve'g. very much used up by acidity & flatulence.

morgan. 5 h.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 13, 1892

Fairey pleasant day - much cloudiness some sunshine & one or two smart showers I was very much used up by my last night's experience - I then dosed myself with magnesia which had the usual effect in the n't. I was on the lounge much of the time until 11.30 - then I got up & went with Jim to swimming school. On my way home met Annie & walked in the Park. After dinner I kept on my back until 4 - then dressed & at 5 A. & I started for W. Tho. Satchell's - Putney - to dine. We left the boys in charge of Clara Jones. We got to "The Orchard" without trouble soon after 6 - a very pleasant house with cheerful fires - a very enjoyable dinner, nicely served - after that went in to Mr. S's studio - surprised to find that his hobby is art & that he does much dabbling with himself - we were obliged to leave soon after 9 - young Mr. S. walked with us - we went ahead of the bus & walked all the way to the station across Putney bridge - train at 9.48 - got home ab't 10.20 - found Clara in watch & everything all right.
