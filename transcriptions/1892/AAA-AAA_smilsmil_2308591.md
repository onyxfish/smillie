## Left Page

### April, Monday 11, 1892

Still improving — temp. down
to 99° — little pain — Bowels
very effectually cleared by enemn.
— first movement since last Thur.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 12, 1892

Propped up in bed & feeling
much better. Temp. fell below 100°
being a trifle higher than the lowest
of yesterday.
