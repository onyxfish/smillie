## Left Page

### June, Sunday 12, 1892

A very hot day — Pkp. about 9 —
The whole of the day was spent
indoors airing & packing away
things. Dinner at 2 — a rest
& work again until near 7 —
Evelyn, Geo. & Emily & Jno. Valen-
tine came in & stayed until
near 10 — we got to bed soon
after — but it was a trying night.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 13, 1892

**Ther. got to 98°.**

A cloudless & terribly hot day — wind
from the south — Ther. got up to 98°. After
Pkp. I went down town — first went
to Brown, Bros. to enquire about a cable
message — addressed to Smillie — inform-
ing him that he could apply to B. Bro.
for fifty — intended for some other S —
Then to Drexel Morgan & Co — met him
Emma S — & Margery Spring — introduced
them & they got letters of credit — then
we went to the office of Oelrichs & Co &
made various arrangements for comfort
on the steamer — then to Jno. While's
& got some foreign money — then back
to Drexel's & got letters of credit — E. & M.
Lippme & I went to Mr. Heber Bishop's
office — saw Mr. Patterson & left with
him a letter & bill concerning a jade
plate for Mr. Bishop — then uptown to
Y.M.C.A & left with them the cable
message to Smillie — as it was addressed
to them — then home — lunch — Frank
Adams with us — immediately after I
went to 2nd Nat. Bk. with chlo. for deposit
— rode up to Allen's — the dentist — & saw
him — there to barbers in 42nd S — & had
hair cut — home at 4.30 & made back
nos of Sec. Lend. News into Vol. Pkp. until
after 6 — Dinner before 7 — Evelyn wrote
letter to Hronck & sent him checks
for a lot of bills — mailed it — to bed
before 10 & passed a very uncomfortable
night.
