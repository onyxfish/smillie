## Left Page

### February, Thursday 11, 1892

A nasty day with light rain & snow
until about 5 o'clk. when the clouds
broke away. Got to studio about 10.30
& spent the mf. in getting my last
Ray plts. from off the boards, cutting
edges & fussing over them — Home to
lunch — Geo. pictures, bought at his
sale, arr'd. before 2 o'clk — To studio
at 2.45 — Made plt. of plt. & original
oil sketch for W.K. Vickery & took
it to Adams Exps. Home at 6 —
Evelf. Alice Ireland came in for
awhile — also Chat. Collins who
came at Annie's request to tell
her about his West Indian trip, a
year or more ago. A. is anxious
to get me away somewhere for the
benefit of my health. I wrote a
letter to Mr. Vickery & got to bed
after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 12, 1892

A bright, clear day growing to be very
cold & windy at night. Got to studio
about 10 & spent the mf. clearing up
getting all my Ray working plts. together
& stripping the India from the backers
& otherwise getting all remainders of
the plate out of sight. Home at 1 —
after lunch wrote my regular letter
to Herrick — to studio at 2.45 & all
the rest of the afternoon I was at work
arranging plts. for Dr. W. B. Wood. Miss
Mary Willard & Dr. Gersten — & getting
other plts. into press. Home at 6 —
Annie at Miss Drucell's committee
of Choral Club — did not get home
until 7 o'clk. So our dinner was late
Evelf. Charl came in at 9 & stayed
until 10.30 — To bed soon after 11.
