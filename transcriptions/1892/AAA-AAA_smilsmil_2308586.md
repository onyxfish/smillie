## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 30, 1892

Charming day — B[k]f[s]t. early, but the
many things to be done about the house
kept me until 9.30 — went to Dr. Gaster[s].
He examined me & thinks that I
had better submit to an operation
— a growth in the scrotum involving the
left testicle. To studio at 10.30 — worked
at biting aqua-tint background until
12 — then went to Dr. Allen's & he finished
capping my tooth — home before 1.30 —
To studio at 2.30 — finished biting —
Took plate to K & V. after 5 o'clk. to
get p[f]p in the [wf] — then rode to
2.79 [illegible] St. & left proof of an aqua-tint
at N[o] 82 for Mrs. Dr. Hume — Home at
6.20 — dinner — Sw[e]f[t] wrote to Vicker[s]
Treas[urer] of Essex Co — & to Dud Hastings.
After 9 went with A. to Edw[ar]d[s] & 
took an old gun & some fishing rods.
Home at 10 & to bed at 10.30.

Jade 4 h.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 31, 1892

Pleasant day — After bkf[s]t — at 8.30 went
to Yall[e]s to consult with him about the
Gaster operation — from there rode down
to K. & V's — Young V. had taken me proof
from the Jade plate when I got there —
He took me more — then to studio and
backed them down — Being Varnishing
Day at the N[A]. I went over there. Found
my two paintings in very good places
on the line in [large] Gallery — Home to
lunch at 1.15 — To studio at 2.30 and
worked on Jade plate until near 6.
After having tried to find Arthur Turnure
at 267 Fifth Ave. — Dinner — Sw[e]f[t].
wrote to Herrick & Turnure — mailed
notes before 9 — Later George & Emily
came in for a few minutes —
To bed before 11

Jade — 4 ½ h.
