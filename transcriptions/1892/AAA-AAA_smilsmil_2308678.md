## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 4, 1892

*Buffalo Bill.*

Pleasant m'f. Light rain about 4 +
heavy rain soon after, continuing thro'
the eve'g. Had a run in the Park with
the boys before bk'f't. Worked on my plate
from 9.45 to 12.5. Then out for a few min.
Dinner ab't. About 2 all started for
Earle's Court to see Buffalo Bill's Wild West
Show — got 3/ seats — a good view of the open
space — larger than Reservoir Sq. N.Y.
Some very wild riding + some astonishing
shooting. The arena was a mud puddle
but in spite of that I never saw horses run
as they did there — they seemed to enter
into the fun with great zest — especially
the bucking parts. The Cossacks did some
remarkable riding — their chief was very
curious + their dance amusing. A herd
of 15 or 20 Buffaloes were self-possessed
under a tremendous running fire + after
a rapid run around the enclosure
were dismissed in peace. Performance
from 3 to 4.30. Then we went out to see the other
part of the show — numerous buildings and a
bewildering lot of all sorts of stuff. All this is
called a Horticultural Exhibit — many pictures
+ pieces of statuary, among the other things —
are soon tired of it + returned as we came
by underground R.R. getting home at about
6 o'elk. Kindled a fine — had tea and
passed a quiet, comfortable even'g.

Morgan — 2 h.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 5, 1892

*condpers afternoon*

Dismal m'f. — Damp + foggy — my
work windows, owing to the breaking of a
weight cord, could not be closed; so,
while it was being repaired I walked
down to the British museum (3/4 of an
hour) Mr. Colvin, head of Print Dep't. is
still absent in Italy — I presented my
letter of introduction from W. J. Linton
to the deputy in charge, Mr. Fabian, +
was very well rec'd by him — He was
exceedingly kind + made everything
very easy + pleasant for me — I spent
some time looking over impressions
on paper of Italian Nielli — Home
by bus in 25 m. Dinner — worked on
plate from 2 to 4.30 — then went with
[illegible] to get his swimming lesson but
his teacher had gone home. Then I
went out + got three twilight sketches
in at 6. Tea — Every spent some
time looking over a book by [illegible] Herschell
on Indigestion.

Morgan — 2½ h.
