## Left Page

### January, Friday 22, 1892

Pleasant day + milder — went to studio at 10.30 taking Ralph with me — Annie walked a part of the way down with us — Mielatz came in + some time was spent discussing Etching Club exhibit — then Yale came in — I spent the m'f cleaning up — fixing p'ps. + etchings etc. — Ralph amused by Water-Color work — Home to lunch at 1.15 — Annie + Emma Ireland's to studio at 2.30 — went in to Tiffany's to see window — not ready yet. — fussing over various things until near 5 — then to J. Harrison Mills' hunting for stray pictures — found them all in Rose & Co's. — Home at 5.45 — dinner — Eve'g W. A. Menryx Satchell called + amused us until near 10 o'clk — he is so English, you know!

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 23, 1892

Mild, bright w'f — afternoon overcast + raw. Got to studio a few minutes after 10 — to work a cleaning up + fixing, pressing, etc. Etching p'ps. Damped + stretched one of the p'ps — paper p'p. that I took a few days ago from the Saunders Ray plate — W. S. R. Koehler came in + made a hurried visit on his way to R.R. station — going back to Boston. Mielatz brought M.S. + Etching club cat. then Wm. Pate came in + it was 1.45 before I got home to lunch — after lunch went to DeVinne's with Cat. M.S. to studio before 4 o'clk. + fussed during what remained of day light — studying my Saunders Ray p'ps. with a view to going on with that work + getting my drying p'ps. into new boards. Home before 6. Annie was out + we did not get our dinner until she came in at 6.30 — A quiet eve'g writing some notes + reading.
