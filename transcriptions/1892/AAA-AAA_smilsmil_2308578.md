## Left Page

### March, Monday 14, 1892

Brilliant day. but cold & windy.
I got up with a splitting headache
& after bkfst. went back to bed & then
spent the rest of the mng. Got up
to lunch, feeling somewhat better
& improved gradually during the
afternoon. wrote some notes — one
to S. C. F. Hammond informing her of
Aunt Huldah's illness. Dinner
after 6. Eveg. Charle came in &
spent a part of the eveg working
us. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 15, 1892

Bright & cloudless. but cold and
windy — the air full of dirt — Got
to studio at about 10.30 & soon to work
on jade plate. It is so long since I
worked upon it that I have lost
my clue — to get hold of it again I
set to work cleaning the margin — Soon
after 12 went to 2d Nat. Bk. & dep.
Pratt Inst. check & S. R. Koshler's P. O.
money order for $5.00. Then to Lincoln
Safe Deposit Co. in 42d St. to see W. Van
Warner concerning storage of studio
contents after June — not in — home
to lunch — at 2.30 again to 42d St.
Mr. VanW. not in — Then home & took Ralph
with me to studio — burnished on jade
margin until 4.30 Mielatz came in —
It was near 6 before I left studio — to
B. B. Butler's & ordered two frames.
Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eveg.
Annie went to her Choral Club — I
stayed at home — wrote to Koshler &
[illegible] — Kate to bed early — Annie
came in at 10.30 —

Jade — 3 h.
