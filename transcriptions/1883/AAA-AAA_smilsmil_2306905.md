## Left Page

### February, Monday 5, 1883

Wretched day — Snow in the m[orning]
but clear at night — with less of a mess
underfoot than I expected — Got up
with a headache — after bkfp. went
to St. John Harper's in 14th S. & left a lot of
Haden tickets for Art Students League —
then to Keppel's, Wunderlich's & to Roswell
Smith (Century Magazine) — then to K[eppel's] &
left tickets there for School. Met Hammond
of Boston, just back from the Bermudas —
then to Studio — Keppel came in, from W.
Haden — I went right over to him & we
went to Chickering Hall together — then
I went to Gilden's to arrange for the
meeting between Dr. Potter & W. Haden.
Then home at 1 — had a light dinner
& to bed, where I stayed all the p.m —
Up at 6.30 — a little tea before 7 — & at
7.30 — Annie & I at Chickering Hall — Haden
soon there — everything working smoothly —
Lecture began at 8.25 — over before 10 —
W.H., out of sorts — headache — & much
dissatisfied with his effort — nearly
800 present — Yale walked home with
us — to bed about 1 & to sleep at
2.30.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 6, 1883

Raw & overcast day — after bkfp.
went down to Chickering Hall &
counted tickets 75¢ — 11 o'clk when I
got to studio — Put some pictures
in frames & pottered about. Home
at 1 — Lurie Gunther, Florence
Sattersby & E.N.J. there to lunch —
we had quite a pleasant little time.
Back to studio about 3 — mounted
my W.C. drawing of "Old Ice Houses
near Saratoga" — solidly on trunk
board — A.F.J. made quite a long
call — so did Dr. Yale. Home at
6 — Rested a few minutes — Then
dressed & at 7 to F.H. Smith's, 150
E. 34th S. to dine with F. Seymour Haden.
Gifford, Quartley, Eastman Johnson, Isham
& Dr. Peters were the guests — sat down at
7.30 — a very pleasant chatty time, Mr.
Haden making himself very agreeable &
a mild, good natured controversy on
the subject of Etching v.s. Engraving took
up a little of the time — H. left at 10 to go to
Union League Club 20th Annual Recptn. — Gifford,
Quartley & I stayed until after 11, then
home in a horrible snow & sleet storm.
