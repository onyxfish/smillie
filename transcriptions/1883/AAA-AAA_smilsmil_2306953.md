## Left Page

### May, Monday 14, 1883

Cold, east wind with rain after 5 P.M. Edith left us for home in the mtf, got to studio at 9.30 — Wrote a note to Frank Dent & Reply to Mr. Watts on "Tests of Water Colors" for publication in "The Studio" then worked for a little while coaching Geo. on his Koehler etching. Then commenced Reduction & Tracing of sketches for Lamb etching "Books & Reading." Home to dinner at 1 — at 2.30 to Kummel & Voigts — Paid bill — then to Whiteley's — Franklin & Centre St. & ordered copper plates — then to Hudson b. 73 Am. Tiling Co. — moved to 23° S. — then to A.B.N. Co. Saw Will & made out bill — then to Walter Cook's — at 6, to studio — at 7, home & had tea — Eveg, wrote to Cottrell telling him that I had determined not to go to Montrose until after Annie's confinement — Read & wrote until near 11. Thunder storm — then to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 15, 1883

Chilly & raw, with rain. After bkfst. made a second study sketch from A. as she sat in the parlor with book — for etching to ill. Lamb — got to studio about 11 — Chase came, used up with a cold. I finished Reduction & Tracing of Lamb subject & home to dinner at 1 — rack room after 3 — then Chase came in, & not in the mtf, Annie came in at about 4.30 & we went to Johnston & Faulkner's & bought portières for M— house — then to Vantine's — so near 6 that we accomplished but little. Then to Bridgeman's where I bought 2 bushels of lawn grass seed for M— A. rode home & I walked — tea at 7. The middle of the day was warm & sunny — the eveg, wet again. After tea, wrote a few words to Father and to Cottrell. Took the notes out and mailed them. Read some & soon after 10, to bed.
