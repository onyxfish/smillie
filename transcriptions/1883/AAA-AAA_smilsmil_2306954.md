## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 16. 1883.

Beautiful day — After bkfs. worked
until after 10 on Montrose plans for
Main Hall — then to Studio & com-
menced 2d painting of Black Mt.
from Harbor Islands for Charl. Calvin
brought in his bas-relief of my head
& modelled on it. Home to dinner
at 1 — a little more work on plans.
Then to Tiffannys & spent an hour
selecting & cutting glass — then to
Studio — worked a little on paint-
ing — much out of sorts from headache
of a neuralgic variety — Home at 6
to get Annie to go to the Park with
me — she was out so I went alone —
I rode up to 86th St. & entered Park
at the gate beyond that & walked
down & out at 72d St. gate — then home
at 7.15 — having had a refreshing
hour — Tea — Evefg. Annie
read aloud part of a sketch by
H.F. James jr. — To bed very
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 17. 1883.

Another delightful day — worked on
Montrose plans until near 10 — then
walked to 712 Mad. Ave. with Annie.
She stopped to call on Mrs. Howard White
& I went on to studio — laid grounds on
two plates that I am to etch for Putnam —
varnished the backs — Painted on Charl's
Lake George — Home to dinner abt — after
dinner worked on plans until 3. Then
to studio — Painted until 5. when
it seemed to me that I had got
the Lake Ge°. painting about done —
then to Dickel's & rode up to Central
Park on Charl's horse — we both (horse
& I) enjoyed it exceedingly — The
horse is a good one — the afternoon
delicious — I made the circuit of the
Park & back to N. Fabee at 6.30 — then
home — bath & scrub down — Tea at 7.30
Evefg. Annie completed the reading
aloud of one of H.F. James'. sketches
I marked some underclothes &
got to bed about 11.
