## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 18. 1883.

Brilliant, mild day — To studio at 9.45 — M. Ticknor called. Some time was spent over the business of having remaining 25 sets of auto-facs. pfp. of Porto & Etchers mounted. Then I finished the mounting of Minnis drawing. Willie came in for a moment — then home to dinner. After dinner, Rode down to Kimmel & Voigt's & ordered pfp. of Yosemite & Clintein plates — back to studio at 4 — W. Frederic Condit there. He stayed until Annie came for me. I don't know whether he liked his Nake &c. Picture or no. Then A. & I went to Exhib. of Society of Am. Artists — there until 6 — then home at 6.30 — tea at 7 — Every went to Walter's to discuss plans that he has just finished for Main Room. Home before 9 — found Chas. Bostwick waiting for me — he had come to borrow a dollar which I gave him, hoping never to see him again — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 19. 1883.

Mild. Wind from the east. Portents of change — Got to studio at 9.30 — Had several matters to attend to in getting Condit's picture away — in relation to Porto & Etcher pfp. That Grady is mounting & plans & letter for Catnell — Near 11 when I got to work on W.C. Rough Sport in Yosemite — at 12.30 went to H.P. with plans of Main Room for Catnell — then to Station (P.O.) 3d Ave. & 28th S. Then home at 1 — Nelie, Katie & Mallie have come to stay until the end of the week — At 2.30 back to studio & to work on Rough Sport. Calverley modeled on my bas-relief — about 5 Annie came & I went to Wunderlich's with her & we looked at etchings until about 6.30 — then home. Tea at 7 — Evg. I worked on plans for main hall — To bed about 11.
