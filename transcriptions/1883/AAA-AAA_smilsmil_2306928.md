## Left Page

### March, Friday 23, 1883

Overcast, blustering & rain, with occasional flakes of snow. To
studio at 9.30 — made a few sketches
after T. A. Richards for Kurtz — then
to my ox cart etching — put second
etching over sky & some other parts.
also, commenced on Street in font-
arabia. Mr. Isaac Riley called.
Home at 1 — back at 2.45 — bit on
plate — laid another ground &
etched second line over cattle
but did not bite it — At 5 went
to Dickle's & had a horse back
ride — Charl came in & rode
a while with me. Home at 7 —
tea — Eve'g — a little reading
& writing, some worrying over the
problem of Montrose studio-cellar
stairs & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 24, 1883

Bright & breezy, & consequently, dusty.
To studio at 9.30 & to work on ox
cart & Grande Rue etchings — etching
on one while the other was in the acid
& had varnish drawing — Scribners sent
up for them. Mr. Isaac Riley called
Home at 1 — back before 3 — Dr. Hall
called — Gave Calverley first sitting
on my bas-relief Portrait — Took home
plaster cast of Auinin's, which pleases
me very much — worked on ox cart
as long as I could see & home very
tired at 7.15 — tea — eve'g, loafed
awhile — & worried awhile over
some Montrose plans — to bed
about 11.
Cornelia went home this morning
before we were up.
