## Left Page

### July, Monday 23, 1883

A muggy, showery day with spasms of breeze & suffocation altho' the ther. on piazza did not get above 74°. Up soon after 7 & spent an hour in the kitchen making lamb & beef croquettes to show Anna how — in consequence, had a late bkft. but the croquettes were good. After I went down town & did a variety of shopping. Home at & had dinner about 1.30 — for some time engaged putting up bamboo rolling curtains on piazza — then to studio & to work on etching No. 25. Worked on a pf. & laid ground on the plate. With Austin, mixing colors — tea soon after 6. Before 9 I began to feel nausea & had to lie down — to bed for the night at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 24, 1883

Very cool — overcast with a few light showers — Bkft. at 8.30. Nearly all the mfg engaged upon the many little things calling for immediate attention — overflows to cisterns & tank — color for sashes, etc. — at last got a little work done on plate 25 — etching on 2nd ground — near 2 before we had dinner. After that — a little time at my plate — more, to the drawing of plans for a printing table — It was after 7 before I went down town for mail etc. — back & had tea about 8. On piazza with a — amusing late sunset — a look over the Tribune & to bed at 10.
