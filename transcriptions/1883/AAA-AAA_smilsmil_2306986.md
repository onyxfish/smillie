## Left Page

### July, Thursday 19, 1883

Nice a [illegible] day — so cool, clear &
breezy — the mtf spent in
getting things into order, altho'
I felt as though I had been
on a spree all night — went
down town in the mtf & did
my marketing — Dinner som
after 1 — the afternoon went
like the mtf. Mr. detchrients
& her mother-in-law called &
saw A. & the boy — At 5 I walked
down town with them — more
marketing & shopping — Home
& tea about 7 — By a new
arrangement, Bella, the nurse
sleeps in the room with Annie
& I have my quarters in the
N.E. Room — To bed before
10 & did not turn over until
about 6.30 when I was waked
by the girls.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 20, 1883

Another very cool, brilliant day —
many pkgs. came by freight &
[illegible] — & the mtf was spent in
unpacking — Dinner soon after
1 — more unpacking during
the afternoon — very tired when
I had tea at near 7 — after
8 went down town & did my
shopping — Home soon after 9.
A little writing & to bed soon
after 10.
