## Left Page

### September, Sunday 23, 1883

Another very pleasant day, but with more marked promise of bad weather to come —
Belp. about 9 — the day spent out of doors, very lazily, with the
trees — dinner at 2 — again loafing about all the afternoon.
Tea about 7 — after 8 Mr. & Mrs. Park came in, making a pleasant
call until 10 o'clk. — Then I wrote a letter to father and another one to Charles — at 11
went to P.O. — Home to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 24, 1883

Heavy wet day — Home chores kept me going until after 10 —
then down town — home before 12 — To work in studio all. C.
study of hill side, going over all of it, strengthening it in color
according to observations that I had taken while at work Sat.
mf. Dinner at 1 — Tremendous heavy rain, worked all. C.
until 4 o'clk. — at about 5.20 Julie Spring, Margery & Edw., Emma
& Mary Ireland arr. Tea at 7 — soon after 8, almost a hurricane
of wind & rain — wind veering to South & West — To bed soon after
10.
