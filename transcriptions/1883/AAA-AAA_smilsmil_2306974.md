## Left Page

### June, Monday 25, 1883

Pleasant day — up at 6.30
Bkf. at 7 — Packed & got
ready to leave — gave final
directions to Cottrell & superintend-
ed the mixing of a new color for
wall of main floor 3rd Room —
Soon after 10 went down-town
& did up chores there — got
train at Mitchell's & up to house
at 10.45 — Mr. Park Mr. Keeler
there with Charl. We hurried
things & got away at 10.50 &
drove over to Montrose depot in
50 m. without the slightest
trouble — in ample time for
the new Express at 12.02. Got
to Scranton at 2.30 & had a very
good dinner — To foot of Chris-
t. at 6.40 & home at 7.10 — Found
Annie & boy all right — Had
tea — welfq., chatting — To
bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 26, 1883

Rain during the night. Cloudy,
muggy day with chilling east
wind — got to studio at 10 &
spent the mfg. stretching Jap.
paper &c. & the two Putnam
(Jamb) etchings — Home to dinner
at 1 — back at 3.15 — Found Miss
Abbott in her room — after
she left I spent the rest
of the P. m. until 7.15 mounting
[illegible] & wetting paper to begin
proving to-morrow — Home
at 7.30 — tea — Every with
Annie — wrote a noted to
Catharine Drinker Janvier on
the subject of quarters here
for this summer for herself &
husband — To bed soon after
10.
