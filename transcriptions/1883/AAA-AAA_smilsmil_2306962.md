## Left Page

### June, Friday 1. 1883.

Got up to breakfast. but the day am
spent lying about on lounge or bed.
Yale was sent for & came at about
11 o'clk & prescribed for me — my
trouble with the bowels continuing
obstinate. Tried to do some reading
but did not have head enough —
Wrote a letter to Catnille & as Annie
went to studio & got check-book, I
sent him his check before 2 o'clk.
I tried to work on Montrose plains
but was not up to that — so, I
did much of nothing with con-
siderable silent swearing.
Evng. Chase came in for a
short time — To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 2. 1883.

Delightful day, altho' the
wind blew steadily from the east.
I did some little work on Montrose
plains — Dr. Yale called again at
about 11 — Soon after 12 Annie & I
rode up Mad. ave. in a car to 75th
St. & back — for an airing — Dinner
soon after 1 — again worked on
Montrose plains & at about 5
Annie & I rode up to the Park
& at 59th St. climbed up to the front
seat with the driver, of a public
park wagon — and made the circuit
of the Park — music on the mall —
ball players on their greens —
pleasure seekers every where — a
charming scene — Back to 59th St. at
6.10 — then home & tea at 7 —
Evng. Annie read aloud, some
of Rosetti's poems. To bed soon
after 10. Had something
of a scare during the night —
thinking that Annie's time had
come.
