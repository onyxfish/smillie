## Left Page

### November, Sunday 4, 1883

Charming day — quite mild — B[illegible] after 9 — Sat
some time & read news-
paper — After 11 went to
studio — there until near
2 — then home & had dinner
— Between 3 & 4, with Annis
boy & nurse, went to Morton
Cook's new home, 104 E. 39[illegible]
There for a short time —
then home — Geo. there —
Tea at 7 — Well, Chas.
Collins called — To bed
soon after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 5, 1883

A very mild & pleasant day — near
10 when I got to Studio — Scrubbing
woman came & I spent the mfg
with her — she scrubbed the floor.
About 1, Koehler called with Mr.
Griffin, head of the canvassing
department, of Cassell & Co. They
stayed until 1.40 — then I
rushed home to my dinner —
Mamma there. — Back to studio
at 3.15 — Koehler came with
[illegible] of the 20 plates for the new
work. & spent the rest of the
p.m. with me — walked about
Madison Square in the dusk —
near 7 when I got home to tea.
Geo. Cook came in for a little
while — I wrote notes to Stroud &
Smiets & to Jack Starne — To bed
at 10.30
