## Left Page

### November, Monday 12, 1883

Very cold, clear day, with blustering wind, almost a gale.
Did not get to Studio until after 10 o'clk — commenced work
on drawing of 4th Ave. horse car for Q.B.N. Co. Hateful work
& made but slow progress.
Home to dinner at usual time — back at 3 — worked
until near 5 — home at 6.
Tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 Willie
Jos, Minn & Walter called to
see Matt — Annie & I were
on our way out to see Irving
& Miss Terry in "The Lyons Mail".
Met at the Theatre — Emma and
Collins. Willet — Fanny — Lib. Home
& Mary J — we celebrated Emma's
birthday. Irving & Miss Terry
pleased me but I was not
wonderfully moved — Home
at 11 — To bed

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 13, 1883

Still cold & blustering & overcast — again late — worked on
horse car — At noon Mr. Marvin
called about the Mitchell
etching & left material for it.
Home to dinner at 1 — back
at 3 — Fanny & Lib H. called.
I worked on Horse car as long
as I could see — then rested
in big chair until 6 — then
home — Tea at 6.30 —
Eve'g — looking over Howard
Pyle's Robbin Hood, sent
me by Scribners & writing
notes until 10 — To bed
before 11.
