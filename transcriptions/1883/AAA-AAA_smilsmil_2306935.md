## Left Page

### April, Sunday 8, 1883

Charming day — Brfe soon after 9. Annie went to All Soul's church & I followed, but did not get a seat with her. Heard Ed W. Everett Hale dis course upon the difference between Religion & Religiosity — After that, went with Annie & Geo. Smillie to Casteny. Then a. went home & I to studio — Home just before 2 — Mr Koehler there by my invitation — We took dinner soon after 2 — Mr & Mrs. Shruinger came just as we finished — then Emma — At 5 I walked to 27th S. with E — then to 28th S. & called on Geo. & at 6.20 to 34th S. & called on Wille — then home & had tea about 7 — Evef. Reading and writing. At 10.30 to G.C. depot with letters — To bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 9, 1883

Pleasant day — Hard work to get up, owing to headache — Went to Gradeis & ordered a framing frame for H.f. Sprague's wedding present — then to studio at about 10. F.H. Smith came to talk about Philpot's Jordon Water Color 5×4[illegible] — does not like the notion of Silva's pictures being accepted by Philpot — I worked on Old Ice House near Saratoga, finishing up generally, while Calverley sat by me & modeled on bas-relief portrait — Home to dinner at 1 — after that — went to bed where I stayed until eveth. Took a light tea — then on bed again until after 9 when I transferred myself to lounge where I stayed until 5.30 a.m.
