## Left Page

### February, Saturday 17, 1883

at Mr. Wm. Justice's Germantown. Pa.

More nasty, foggy wet weather.
After bkfst. went to Walter's and
spent some time explaining to him
measurements so that he can make
drawings for large room at [illegible]
to studio before 11 — Spent the time
before 1 in writing to Cotrell, washing
brushes etc, etc. then home to
dinner — after that, dressed +
packed valise + at 3 Annie + I
took Coupe to foot of Liberty St. +
then 4 o'clk. Train to Wayne Junction
— met at station by coachman — got
to house soon after 6. Jas. Wright
\+ Edith came — dinner about 7 — E.
left early to go to the Hobby Club —
Jas. W. stayed until near 10 and
we got to bed about 10.30. Heavy
showers about 12 — Passed a
restless night.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 18, 1883

Up soon after 8 — Snow storm —
two or 3 in. of snow — My head very
light, + generally uncomfortable.
Bkfst. at 9 — I nursed a fire in
the library while all the others
went to church — The Justice, his
wife + two children dined with
us at 2 o'clk — I feeling much better.
The weather cleared in the after-
noon + there were a number of
callers — among others, Fanny Wright
Davis, her husband + baby — also Edith
\+ Marion W. I took an airing on
piazza — Tea at 7 — Evening,
chatting + looking over photos
\+ reading — To bed at 10.30.
