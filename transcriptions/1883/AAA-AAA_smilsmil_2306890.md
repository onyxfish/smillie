## Left Page

### January, Saturday 6, 1883

Overcast + disagreeable, with now + then a little snow or sleet.
Got to studio at 10.15. after bidding Mr. + Mrs. Justice good bye — they return
to Phil. this P.m. Worked all the m'g on "Causeway", holding consultation
with Geo. Champney + Gifford —
Gifford came at 2.30 + found the dis-
cussion f[or] N.C. Soct'y matters so inter-
esting that he stayed until near 2 —
then I went home to dinner + got
back to studio at 3.30 — P.m. so dark
that I did not accomplish much.
Frames came from Brady's + I put
drawings into them — The Bo'c. W.C's
were rei?. Home at 6 — tea — + at 8
to Phil. Concert — I was not very
enthusiastic — enjoyed the Dvorak +
the Beethoven of course — Home + to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 7, 1883

Dismal day — left at 9.30 — then
read paper + after 11 got to writing
some letters. About 1 went out
+ walked up to 5th Ave. + 57th St —
then back home to dinner at 2.
After that, did some more writing
+ about 5 went down to studio —
there wrote a note — then walked
up to Dr. Yale's — saw him + Mrs.
Y — Home about 7 — feeling much
out of sorts — the middle part of
this day having cleared bright +
mild I felt like a run-down
fiddle — After tea, Annie read
aloud to me for awhile — then
I wrote my letter to father + at
10 took it to G.C. Delpost — then
back + to bed.
