## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 2. 1883.

Pleasant day in spite of some foremonitions of a storm somewhere in the vicinity — got to studio a little before 10 & to work on causeway drawing. Home as usual at 1 — back at 2.45 — worked until 4.30 then home — Mr. & Mrs. Justice arr'd from Phila at 5.30. Annie & I started for Mr. Beach's 96 Columbia Heights — got there in an hour — Besides the family, Misses Barnum & Bertha Lewis were there & also [illegible] Mr. Cha. Beach. Tea at 7 — we left soon after 9 & got home at 10.20 — Mr. & Mrs. J— & Mother Cook out to a theatre so Annie & I went to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 3. 1883.

Raw, windy & partially overcast after 8:15 — chatting with Mr. Justice so that I did not get to studio until 10.15 — there to work, & all the [illegible], fussed over the surf on the left middle distance. Home to dinner at 1.30. Emma J— there. — After dinner took a rest — got to studio at 3.15 & worked until 4.30 — then to Angell's, the printer & to Putnam's about printing cards & circulars for Haden lectures — Home at 6 — another rest — tea at 6.30 — Evening at home with Mr. & Mrs. Justice — Mr. & Mrs. Vaux — Mrs. Dillon & Mr. Jervis McEntee called — Later I wrote notes home & to Estes & Lauriat — took them to G.C. Depot — to bed before 11.
