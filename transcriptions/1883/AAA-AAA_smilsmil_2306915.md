## Left Page

### February, Sunday 25, 1883

very heavy fog in the m'f. with
occasional showers. B'kf'. at
9 — after that Read & wrote
until near 12 — then I walked
up to Gifford's - 152 W. 57' — then
met Dr. Yale — arranged list
for W.C. Soct'y nominations —
Home at 2 — dinner soon after.
At 3.30 Annie & I went to N.A.
& took a last look at the W.
C.'s — then to studio for a few
minutes — then A. & I. went to
E. 17' S' & saw Aunt Anna and
Emma — brilliantly clear sun-
set — home at 7 — tea at 7.30 —
after that A. sat at piano for some
time, I on sofa enjoying her play-
ing — at 9.30 wrote letter home
to father. Took [illegible] to station
then home & to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 26, 1883

A windy, march-like day — after
b'kf'. went to studio & from there
to N.A. — arranged to have pictures
sent to Brooklyn — etc — then
to J.S. Church's — 58 E. 18 S' — not in —
then to 10' S' — Studio Bldg. Janner
not in — saw Chase for a few
minutes — then to 57' S' & saw Deluce
— urged him to run for Sec'ty of
W.C. Soct'y — then to J.D.A. Ward's —
119 W. 52' S' new studio — found
him at his lunch — he showed
me over the building — he has
a grand studio — went up to
E.M. Ward's Room — not in — then
home to dinner at 1. To studio
at 3 — too tired to work — Ogilvie
& Bellows made calls on me —
went to Hubert, Piersson & Co. & saw
plans for large bl'g in 25' S'. Studios
on upper floor — Saw Shurtliff there —
I called on his wife in the m'f. Home
to tea before 7 — Ev'g, hanging
new water colors in Reception Room
— to bed at 11.
