## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 18. 1883.

Routed out before 7 — again
at 9 — feel shaky — Dark day.
fine snow — After M.b". — went 5
Dulfale's — not in — then to studio
at 10 — worked awhile over plt.
bit in yesterday's etching — No.
33 — Ferguson called — Home at
1.15 — Dr. Case came at 2.15
& prescribed for me & the boy
as well, who has been disordered
slightly. Did not get to studio
until 3.15 — finished biting
No. 33 — also did a little work
on plt. Home soon after 5.
A little doll dressing party —
All out & to bed by 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 19. 1883.

Quite an old fashioned snow
storm that continued nearly all
day — got to studio before 10 —
spent the m'f facing plt. from
small plate for etching club
Cat. — Home at 1.15 — walked
to Park Ave Hotel with W. Moscheles
— dinner — back before 3 and
as it was too dark for other work
I spent some time mounting
etching plt. — Home at 6 — Tea
— eve'g with Linnie went to
concert of N.Y. Chorus Socy. at
Steinway Hall. Swell affair.
Enjoyed parts of programme
very much — Mrs. R. W. Guilden
sat next to me — pleasant —
we left before the concert
was over — home at 10 — to bed
before 11.
