## Left Page

### October, Monday 1, 1883

Brilliant day — Bkfst. at 8.30 — went to foundry after 10 — home & to work all — finished tracing of No. 30, by introducing figures of Dorsey & the young. Dinner at 1.30 — after that Transferred No 30 to the ground. & at 3.30 went riding with Annie, Julia & many — to Fairdale & up the hills on this side of it from which we had a charming view. Home at 6 — tea about 7 — Eveth. we all felt the effects of our ride in the cool air & were quiet & sleepy. To bed at 10.30.
Ther. Last night went down to 34°.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 2, 1883

Heavy storm all day — much rain — up at 7.30 — Bkfst. at 8.30 — I spent the day drawing plans — most of them for transom, etc. to opening to bay window on main stair — dinner at 1.30 — for a time some fine cloud effects, then again, rain — had another encouraging letter from home — Matt seems able to continue to improve. about 5. went to foundry — then to P.O. — then home & on piazza watching bleak eveth. effects until dark — tea at about 7 — Eveth. reading paper and chatting until 10. To bed soon after.
