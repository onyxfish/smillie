## Left Page

### September, Saturday 1. 1883.

A warm but delightful day — B&lp. at the usual time — the usual chores took up the time until after 10 o'clk. when I went to foundry, then to Latrak's mill about moldings then to Warner's & home shortly before noon — Laid grounds on plates 27 & 28 — Dinner at 1.30 — after that, feeling very sleepy I took a short nap — Piano Tuner made the house musical from 2.30 to 4.30. — At 4.30 team came — out with brother, Mother & Will K. — back at 5.30 — left mother & took Annie — back at 6.40 — Tea after 7 — Ev'g. with papers — wrote notes to Kummel & to A.T.S. Hubbard — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 2. 1883.

Dull day with light rain in afternoon — B&lp. about 9 — failed to get to church — wrote a letter to George — and at 1, walked over to the lake & prospected for a study subject — Back to dinner at 2 — had a good nap between 4 & 6 o'clk. — Tea after 7 — Ev'g. writing letters — after 10 o'clk. went with Will K. to Post Office. Press home & to bed about 11.
