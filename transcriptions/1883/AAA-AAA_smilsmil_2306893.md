## Left Page

### January, Friday 12. 1883.

In bed all day with a wretched headache — the
first really severe one since
I was married — more than
21 months ago — Matt &
Kate down from Po'k for the
day — I did not see them
I got up at 9 P.M. & had
some gruel about 10 — from
12 to 3 I sat in big blanket
in front of parlor fire —
three to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 13. 1883.

Feeling pretty shaky after my
experience of yesterday — the day
very raw & partly overcast. P.M.
commenced snow & wound up
with a cold, persistent rain —
Got to studio about 10 — Put
drawings into frames & otherwise
got things ready to send over
to W. C. [illegible] — Home to dinner
at 1 — back about 3.30 having
taken a rest — Miss deJolier
called but I got things finished
Home in the rain at 6 — At 8
went to F. H. Smith's private view
of W. C. Sketches at More & Curtis's 290
Fifth Ave. F.H.S. buried the hatchet
again — Met Champney there & walked
to Century — Annual Meeting — much
interest in proposed movement of
club to (?) 38th St — lost by 3 or 4 votes —
I voted aye — a pleasant eve[ning] —
left at 10.40 — walked home
alone — To bed soon after 11 —
but not to sleep until after 12
