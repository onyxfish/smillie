## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 11, 1883

Very pleasant day, but warmer. Got up with remnants of headache which gradually wore off as the day wore on — To studio soon after 10 & put in a good mtf. of packing work — Shaw came in with more photo prints. Home at 1 — found there Mary & aunt H — come to see "the boy" — After dinner rode down to Vautrin's & got curtains & screen — then to Arnold & Constable's & got curtain rods & rings — then to Flint's & got [illegible] — then to Tibbals & got Baby Carriage — then to Donnell's & looked at lamps & at near 6 to studio — wrote a letter to Gifford — home to tea at 7 — A. had had a good ride in Park — Eve. very quiet — to bed at 11, but not to sleep until after midnight.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 12, 1883

Much warmer — Up rather late — Got to studio about 10 & spent the mtf. in general packing — Home at 1 — dinner — To Donnell's & ordered lamps for Montrose — then to Vautrin's & ordered screen & [illegible] bamboo curtain — then to Neerwain's & ordered some hardware — then home soon after 6 — Shower at 3 o'clk — overcast most of the afternoon. Found Father & Mother at home — they have come down to see "the boy" — Tea before 7 — Eve. divided between parents & family — Father & Mother slept in our bed — I slept in the nurse's bed alongside of Annie & had several disturbances — a heavy shower after 2 H. M.
