## Left Page

### July, Friday 27, 1883

Very pleasant day, after some threats in the [illegible] & one little
sprinkle — After b[rea]kf[as]t. had Austin
mix under my direction color
for wall of main Room — Then I
painted a section as example
for him — Also worked on bamboo
curtains for piazza — Dinner
as usual. & most of afternoon
on piazza on bamboo screens.
Down town — worrying about
printing Tables — Ed. Smitts, who
was to do it is off on a drunk.
Tea at 7 — Evening in the
season of my family until
10 o'clk — then to bed, after
a promenade with Annie &
Mother C. on upper deck — The
air deliciously soft, promising
rain.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 28, 1883

Heavy showers during last night.
Rain until about the middle
of the n[igh]t, then clearing. About
3.30 Thunder showers came up
& we had rain until after 7 —
Spent the n[igh]t with the men
painting the walls & getting up the
finishing mouldings in main room.
Down town at noon — Dinner soon
after 1 — Afternoon got up East
windows in main Room — They look
gorgeous — Downtown at 6 — Tea
at 9 — Even[ing]. Reading — to bed
punctually at 10.
