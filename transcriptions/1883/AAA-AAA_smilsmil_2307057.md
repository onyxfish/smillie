## Left Page

### December, Thursday 6, 1883

Beautiful day — To studio at 9.30 — Said Rebiting again on plate No. 30 & I spent the m'f. stopping it out — Home at 1 — back at 2.30 — I stopped in at Mrs. Jno. Pope's Room at 12.45 & for a few minutes with about 20 others watched Mr. Moschelles paint in a head from a model — The afternoon I spent Rebiting on No. 30 — Wet down paper for printing to-morrow — at near 6 — went to Knoedler's then to Newman's — then home — tea at 6.30 — Every Walter came in & for an hour or more we talked over some plans he has been drawing for Montrose — wrote a note to father — at 10 went to G.c. Depot with it — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 7, 1883

Very mild & partially overcast. To studio at 9.30 — To work at once grinding ink for printing — Took some p'f from No. 29 — much improved by the softened ink — Tried one of No. 30 — but strong ink & weak paper caused a failure — Mrs. Pope brought in Mr. Moschelles who was smeared with ink — Home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 — & succeeded in getting two proofs of 30 — Chas. Kurtz came in to ask me to make some illustrations for the Art Union cat — wet down paper for more printing to-morrow — nearly 6 before I got away from the studio — wandered about Mad. Square for exercise — home at 6.30 — Every. — wrote notes —
