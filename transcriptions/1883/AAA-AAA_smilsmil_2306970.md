## Left Page

### June, Sunday 17, 1883

Overcast m'g. with indications of
rain but it cleared late in the
afternoon — Had a poor H'p't at 9.
then out & met Cottrill & got home
key from him & then Charl & I
went up there & spent the rest
of the m'g prowling about — at
2 o'clk. went to Wm. Park's and
had a very enjoyable dinner.
about 4 went to see the widow
Powley who is to keep house for
us this week — then again to
the house — about 6 Charl & I
wandered about, back of fair
ground & after 7 down to hotel
where we got something to eat —
from 8 until near 10 writing.
To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 18, 1883

Tremendously heavy rain in the m'g
— lighter rains during the day — very
heavy thunder-storms from 9 until near
11 p.m. Bk'f't soon after 8 — Then out
& bought many things to eat — went to
Payne's & arranged with him to take
our trunks from hotel to house — Got
to house at about 11 — Mr. Powley there
— plenty of work until dinner abt o'clk.
Mrs. P. gave us a very good dinner —
more work, getting bed-clothes, fussing
with glass, etc — fixed pumps, started
water into tank & found that the pipe
was burst & water running all over.
a nice mess. Tea at 7 — about 8.
tiles, cement & dumb-waiter arr'd
I wrote a letter to Annie & at 9,
in a heavy rain storm, Charl. & I
went to P.O. Found that the tele-
man had come & was at Tarbell's
hotel — back to the house at 9.30.
Kindled a fire on hearths in bed-
room & sat there enjoying it, while
the storm raged outside, until 11 o'clk
To bed at 11.30.
