## Left Page

### February, Monday 19, 1883

Overcast mtf., clearing very bright later in the day — Bkfs. about 8 —
We sat in library, chatting and reading until 10 — Then W. J. & I walked to station & went in to Phila by 10.20 train — walked up to 1334 Chestnut St. & made a long call on Stephen Parrish — then away down town to J's office —
then at 1.30 to a Merchants Club Restaurant — where we had our lunch & met Jas. Wright — Moch Lewis, his son Theodore & others. then went to Penn. A. of Fine Arts & saw some of Claghorns Etchings. then to station & took 4.15 train to Wayne Junc. — then stretched myself on the bed &
had a nap. at 6 — up & with A. & Mr. J. (Mrs. J. not feeling well enough) we went to Jas. Wright's & had tea — a family party — back at 10.30 & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 20, 1883

*at home again.*

Another overcast mtf., clearing in the middle of the day. again overcast in the afternoon — air, chill & raw. Bkfs. soon after 8 — Read from 9 to 10 — By 10.20 train went in to town with Mr. Justice — I went at once to Geo. Whitney's works. at Callowhill, 16th & 17th Sts — & there saw him & Mr. Outerbridge. — had a very pleasant chat with Geo. for about 3/4 of an hour — Then to 9th & Green St. & took 12.5 train back to Wayne Junction — Sauntered up to house — lunch at 1 — more reading.
About 3. went to Jas. Wright's with Annie & made a short call. — then back by a round about way — went with Mr. J. to his stable — dinner before 6 — at 6.40 rode to Depot with Mr. J. & the I Red. to N.Y. by 6.56 train, getting to Jersey City at 9.17 & home a few minutes after 10 — Our neighbor, Mr. Towle died suddenly this eve'g.        To bed soon after 11.
