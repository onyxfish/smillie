## Left Page

### June, Monday 11. 1883.

Charmingly breezy altho' still very warm — Got to studio about 10 — laid relisting ground on "corner at home" — did some topping out, then got to work on Sun dial with dry point — Found it very comfortable in Miss Abbatt's room. Home to dinner abt — back before 3 — Mr. S. R. Rochler called — also Charl — I worked until near 6 then to Dickel's & from 6.30 to 7.30 Rode "Queen" in the park — found it very breezy & delightful — Home & had tea at 8 — Part of eveg. with Geo. Cook & Valentine, part with Annie & J. [illegible] & part alone, reading — to bed at 11, & had a very Restless night.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 12. 1883.

Heat modified, but still very warm — pleasant breeze — Got up feeling very shaky — Got to studio before 10 — Got 9 — had been at the house after 9. a.m. & saw Annie & the boy — When I got to studio, laid ground on his plate, then worked on my own plates, varnishing on "A corner at home" & dry pointing on "Sun dial" — Charl came in — Home to dinner as usual — D.Y. came to house at 2.30 — to studio after 3 — Rebit & dry pointed until 6 — then to Central Park & Rode in Park wagon from 6.30 to 7.30 — then home — tea at 8 — Eveg Will & Joe called — saw the boy — I wrote to Cottrell & got to bed about 11.
