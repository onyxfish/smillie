## Left Page

### September, Saturday 15, 1883

Cloudy mtg., clearing & growing milder — brilliant moonlight night.
Soon after hlp. went to piiny with my plate & worked until 12 o'clk
when I got my etching nearly all drawn in — About 12.30
went down-town & walked over the old Post-hill — delicious air —
Home to dinner abt 1.30 — men at work putting drain at entrance to
lane connecting Tyler's with Justin.
Hamick not quite done with putting seats in bay window & Cottrell hanging
big double Kitchen doors — I worked awhile on etching No. 29, pulling it
together — At 4.30, as Annie was not ready, I had to go out riding alone —
back at 5 — the boy still nursing —
again out alone — back in 20 m. —
then Annie & I rode until 6.30
tea after 7 — mntf. as usual
with news & paper & walking
on piazza. To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 16, 1883

Delightfully warm day — Ther.
on north Piazza got up to 77°. Brlp.
at 8.30 — Soon after 10 wandered
off to the N.W. side of hill back of the
house & spent the mtf. until near
1.30 under the Trees — then home
& had dinner at 2 — don't
know where the afternoon went
to. After 4 went to find Mrs.
Naylor, a colored woman — to come
to work to-morrow — Wandered
about until near 6 — then home
& wrote a letter to Ehninger —
tea after 7 — Wife wrote to
Gastier & Mrs Roch. — went to
P.O. at about 10.30.
