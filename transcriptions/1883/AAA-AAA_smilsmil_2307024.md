## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 3, 1883

Very pleasant day — Bkft: after 8 — busily engaged until 10.30 when Julie, the children & Mary got away for N.Y. City — Then to work again on plans — The weather too cool for work out of doors. my head troubled me through the day & night. Dinner about 1.30 — for a time tried to work on my plans but could not; so I went off to the woods and meadows & found relief studying effects of sunlight & color, of which I found several charming bits. Tea at about 6.30 — Even[ing], I lounged in big chair until after 9 — To bed before 10 and had a disturbed night.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 4, 1883

Very cold, windy, & part of the time, cloudy — Last night, Ther. went down to 32°. & the highest that it got to-day (on north side of house) was 42°. Up with a bad headache & did not make much headway with my plans for seats for the bedroom windows — Pettus began ploughing — Dinner abt. 30 — Mr. del Schreisinity called in the mtf & Mr. del — in the afternoon — I finished plan after dinner & rode "Nellie" from 5 to 6 & felt much better for it — Tea before 7 — even[ing]. Kindled fire in coal stove that was put up to-day in S.W. bedroom. To bed soon after 10.
