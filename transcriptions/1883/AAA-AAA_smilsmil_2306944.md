## Left Page

### April, Thursday 26, 1883

Pleasant day — after bkfst. walked over to Grady & McKeaver's shop — from there to studio & after some delay got started on repainting Yosemite, fall from Merced River — 11×16 — Chase came in — went home to dinner with me — back to studio before 3 — and painted again under difficulties from interruptions. Calverley tried some modeling — Mr. Neilson of Phila called — so did Annie. Home at 6.30. Geo. & Nellie came to tea at 7 — they spent the evng. with father & mother while we went to Chickering Hall to the 2d Private concert of the "Choral Club" — Mother, Cook & Geo. C. with us — Chase, Miss Prevost sat back of us — The concert was very creditable & enjoyable. Of course, Annie did not sing with the club but sat with us — Home a little before 11 — to bed right away.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 27, 1883

Pleasant day — growing warmer — after bkfst. went with father to J. S. W. Drexel's & saw 2d Moran's picture of the Bartholdi Statue — then with him to Geo's in 28th St., where Annie & Mother had gone, left him at the door — then to studio & worked on Yosemite fall — at 12 went to Dr. Stephens Smith's in 45th St. & had a talk with him about Matt — He does not give much encouragement — says that such cancerous tumors are rarely eradicated from the system & sooner or later prove fatal — Home at 1. Emma to dinner — Mother at A.V.V's — Annie went to Damrosch Symphony — to studio at 3 — painted on Yosemite — Calverley modeled — Father came in about 5 — after 6, we walked up home in a shower — some heavy thunder with lightning afterwards — Tea about 7. Mr. & Mrs. Dewing called in the evening. I wrote a letter to Kate & sent it to Dr. Otis of P'k. to deliver to her — after 10 went to G.C. depot & mailed it — then home & to bed.
