## Left Page

### November, Friday 16. 1883.

Cold — windy — dirty — again 10
o'clk. when I got to studio — To
work etching red Farm house
(No. 32) for Scribner's — & struck it
& very steadily — Home at one —
new coat — late dinner — Emma
dined with us — back at 3 —
on etching as long as I could
see — near 5 — Then a nap —
then a walk — home at 6.30.
Evg. wrote a note to Laff am
in reply to his suggestion that
I etch a Van der Velde in the
Historical Socty rooms — To
bed at 10.30 — a circus by
Jas. Junt. which made
things lively 11.30 — 12 —
3 & 5 a.m.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 17. 1883.

Pleasant day — as usual, after 10 when
I got to studio — then fixed mordant
& to work biting Scribner etching —
bit all the mtg — home to dinner
at 1 — back at 3.30 — [illegible] Smith
made a long call on Matt — I waited
for half an hour to get a word
with him privately, but had to
go away without it — Worked on
my biting until after 4 — cleaned
plate — Sea as usual — Ed
came down from Po'k. late — and
spent a little time with Matt
before she went to bed for the
night — To bed before 11.
