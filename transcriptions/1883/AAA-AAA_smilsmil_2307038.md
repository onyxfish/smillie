## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 31, 1883

*Returned to the city*

not an unpleasant day — succession
of cloudiness & clearness — B[illegible] at 8 —
after that saw H & Turnell for a few min-
utes — all hands hard at work preparing
for our start — Ready at 10 — two trains
— Anna & Rosina with 6 minutes away at
10.15 — then we drove down town
stopping at the Bank — P.O. & Lathrop's —
then to depot — Train at 12 oz — no
incident — car uncomfortably hot
— the boy behaved very well —
Hoboken at 6.15 — carriage waiting
on W[est] side — Home at 7 — Tea
soon after — Edw'd C. Geo. C. & J[illegible]
Valentine came in later — They
occupy their new quarters at 109
E. 39th S[t] for the first time to-night —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 1, 1883

Colder & cloudy at times — B[illegible]
at 8.30 — Ed.[?] Geo. & Valentine came
in to see the boy — I went to Fred
& McKeever's — then down town — to
Home Mut., Equitable Life — A.B.N. Co. —
Western Union — To Dewey & Keating
& ordered an overcoat — Home to
dinner at 2 — About 3 Mr. & Mrs.
Ehninger called — At 4 I went
to Studio's — Met Mother, Mary &
Aunt H[?] — in the hall — Visited
Geo. then to my own Room for
awhile — at 5.30 went to 28th
St. & called on Nellie & saw Sheldon
— Home to tea soon after 6 —
Mother Cook gone to her 39th S[t]
house — Tea at 6.30 — quiet
evening — very tired — looking over
Punch — & circulars — To bed
before 11.
