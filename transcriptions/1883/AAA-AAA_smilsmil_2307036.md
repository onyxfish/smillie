## Left Page

### October, Saturday 27, 1883

Still another dull day — up at 7.30 feeling pretty well after yesterdays experience. Set the men to work digging holes for trees near the house. About 1.30 down town & transacted considerable business — went to H.f. Turrell's about extending lane — then saw Tyler — also went to the quarry with men & selected 3 ash & 2 maples they took there up — I went to dinner at 1.30 — the rest of afternoon until 5.30 with the men superintending the transplanting — Tea at 7 — Evening, wrestling with [illegible] accts until 10.30 — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 28, 1883

A dull & leaden day, but mild — Bkfst after q — Spent the m'f in getting things together preparatory to a return to Town on Wed — Dinner about 2 — wandering over the place & picking up generally — wrote home & to Ger- & to Koehler — Tea at 7 — Took letters to P.O. Then, packing things until 10 — Then to bed.
