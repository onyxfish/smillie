## Left Page

### July, Saturday 21. 1883.

Weather getting quite warm again. B[illegible]. as usual — after that, at my general work of unpacking little things, especially getting my etching traps ready to begin work — Dinner at — the afternoon spent like the m[illegible] — with a little more progr[ess]. About 5 went down town with Mother Cook & did marketing & shopping — Home to tea before 7 — Eveg. Mr & Mrs. Park, two ladies & a gentleman called — we eno[yed] them in the studio. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 22. 1883.

Rain during the night — overcast m[orn]g. threatening rain — clearing — muggy & warm — Thunderstorms in vicinity — breezy afternoon and pleasantly cool — B[illegible]. before 9 — spent the w[eek] with Annie doing nothing in particular. Dinner before 2 — wrote a number of letters — to Father, Charl, Dr. Yale — Donnell — Neuman. Sir [illegible]rake. Kathleen — walked on piazza with Annie — tea at 6.30 — between 8 & 9 — wrote again. About 9.30 went to the Post Office with my letters, then home & to bed soon after 10.
