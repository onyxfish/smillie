## Left Page

### September, Thursday 27, 1883

Charming mtg., afternoon, overcast & raw. As soon after 7, made fires & went to foundry to see how pattern of fire place casting got along — Home to blkp. at 8.30 — all the mtg. with Hamlin, laying the foundations for bed room hearth — also with Austin, painting — also with Payne, hauling leaf mould & earth from Wm Posts — also with Hef Tyler, getting carpenter's risk added to Policy — then down town & got much for dinner, which we had abt. 30 — As we had been very much disturbed by the boy last night, he having taken cold, I took a nap in big chair — At 3.40 out with 3 seat wagon — all in but Annie — went around the lakes — back at 4.5 & got h. & drove until 6 — bleak ride — tea at 7 — Evef., genl. confab — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 28, 1883

Windy & half overcast day — Blkfp. as usual — got to my work on vdrnies at about 10 & stuck to it until after 12 — laying grounds & doing some relating on No. 29. Then with carpenters until dinner, soon after 1 — After dinner wrote a note to Seut of Studio asking him to cut out parts of notice that he proposes to publish of me. At 3.40 out riding with Annie, Julie & Emma — not a very inspiriting afternoon for riding — Home at 5.40 — then down to P.O. Met my lessee & tried to arrange for the extending of my lease, but made no progress — Tea about 7 — Evef. much chatting, but I managed to write some notes — To P.O. at 10.30. — about 11.30 when I got to bed.
