## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 6, 1883.

mild, overcast day with light
rain at night — all day at
studio — still putting things to
rights & spending much time
in rummaging among sketches
& through portfolios. Dinner
as usual — about 6 walked
down to "Cantring" with Geo. Tea
a few minutes, there back with
Ferguson & Geo. Home to tea
at 6.30 — Eveng. wrote note
to Cottrels — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 7, 1883.

Brilliant breezy mng. Afternoon
slept, to studio — then to Hunter's
& left my spectacles there — Then
to Newman's & bought carving knife
etc. etc. — then to Dr. C. Delphi
with Coulsé — Matt, Ed, Kate and
Miss Brennan arrived. at 11.30 —
Matt got down very well, all things
considered, but she is very feeble.
I did not go again to studio —
dinner at 1.30 — to studio at
about 3 — Tried to make a char-
coal sketch — did not do much.
at 5.30 went up to Dirkle's &
had my riding tickets, bought
last Feby., extended in time.
Home to tea at 6.30 — The new
nurse, Miss Wilcox, & Ed, there.
Geo & Nellie called in the eveng.
Mr. Hoadley called to see me
about Phil. Tickets —
To bed soon after 10.
