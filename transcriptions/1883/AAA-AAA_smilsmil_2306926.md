## Left Page

### March, Monday 19, 1883

Mild, pleasant + stormlike overcast at night + Rains about
11 P.m. — To studio at 9.30 — then to N.a. + made sketch after
Gilbert Gaul's "Silenced" — Home to dinner at 2, which had been
delayed on acct of funeral of Mrs. Chas. Butters — Mother + [illegible]
came home in the m[illegible]. After dinner, took a rest — at 4.
to Tennis — not in — then to N.a. for a few minutes, then
to studio + prepared for the water color meeting that I had
called — about a dozen came — no satisfactory results — no can-
didates agreed upon — and we parted as we came — Home
at 7 — tea later — Evelf. Chas.
Collins called — I tried to work on Montrose plans — To bed
after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 20, 1883

Bleak, cold, overcast m[illegible]. Bright wintry afternoon — Spent near an
hour with Fenn — then to N.a., a little before 11 — made drawings
after May + Bridgman — then home at 1.15 to dinner. Nellie down from
Ridgefield — Back to N.a. at 3. +
made drawing after E. M. Ward —
out at 6 — went to studio — then
walked about Mad. Square and
home at 7 — Tea soon after —
Evelf. rested — then wrote a
letter to Cotrell + to bed about
11.
