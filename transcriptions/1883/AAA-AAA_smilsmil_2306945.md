## Left Page

### April, Saturday 28. 1883.

Very nice spring like m[illegible]. To studio & worked on tracing & out-
line of E°. Morais painting of Bar-
tholdi's Statue until near 11 —
then to Drexel's where I worked
from the original painting mak-
ing a small india ink drawing
from which father is to engrave —
Home at 1 o'clk., Mr. Sprague
made us a short call. Father &
mother both in to dinner — I was
about 3.30 when I got back to D's —
Weather overcast — so dark that
I had much trouble to see, but
I stuck to it until 6 — then to
studio — Home in a smart
shower before 7 — then tea —
Ev'g. Mr. & Mrs. Dielman
called — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 29. 1883.

Dismal snow-storm during most
of the morning, melting as it fell —
The afternoon delightfully clear, cool
but spring-like — M[illegible]. W[illegible]. about 9 —
Father went to church. cor. 39th St. &
Park ave. I followed soon after and
heard a Tennessee man deliver a
commonplace address to a very small
audience — Home with father — Then to
studio — Mounted drawing made yes-
terday — Home to dinner at 2 — At
5 walked up Madison ave. to 85th St.
then to 5th ave. & down to H.V.V.'s — where
I saw him — Charlie & Junie & aunt
H.I. — then home at 7.15 & had tea.
Ev'g. wrote a letter to Kate and
after 10 took it to G.C. Depot — then
home & to bed.
