## Left Page

### October, Friday 5, 1883

Up late — help. at about 9. Superb day — but cold — Ther. 32°. again last night & did not get above 50° during the day. Down town bt. 10 &11. Annie Rec'd letter from Mrs. Ehninger announcing their intention of visiting us next week — worked on plan for bay window for studio & gave some attention to Pettus, who was ploughing, & Payne. Dinner abt. 30 — about 2.30 took plate and Fable & went to Darkie's place back of Agar Lathrop's & commenced 9×15 etching — No. Bundled myself up, but my fingers got quite numb with cold — worked until near 5. Then down town — met Annie — back with her — again down town — tea at about 6.30 — Evetg. more work on studio's bay window — To bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 6, 1883

Sunshine for a brief space in the mg — then overcast & at about noon, rain — wetly, heavy steady rain — Pettus ploughed for half a day — Bt. help. late — thru mg mg. chores — first in studio and got to work on large etching, No. Went down town about 11 — back at noon & worked until dinner soon after 1 — worked at etching until near 5 — then to Courtright's, Parke's & de Schweinitz's with note from Annie stating that on acct of the weather she w'd postpone her little tea party — Home soon after 6 — Tea about 7 — Evtg. worked on Fransom plans for main hall — To bed at 11.
