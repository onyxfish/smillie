## Left Page

### December, Saturday 22, 1883

Bright cold day — To studio at 9 30 — Sent more pk. to Grady & M's — wrote something for Perry concerning the plate by Shirlaw after 2 Johnsons "painting?" The Repremand" worked awhile on outline of "Stray Lambs" — Home to dinner at — After [illegible] Dr. [illegible] came in to see Annie & the boy — detained so that [illegible] after 3 before I got back to studio — wrote a note to Mr. Weekes & at 5 took it to his house in 20" St. Home before 6 — did not go to Mr. [illegible] Drake's reception but wrote a note to her — Tea — At 8 o'clk — at Century — Nom. com — anxious work, broken in [illegible] by a special meeting of the club — Com. Resumed its meeting after 10 — completed its work at 11 — Then went home — to bed shortly after midnight.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 23, 1883

Very cold — bright m'f. overcast afternoon — Snow at night — Up late — [illegible] after 9 — Read until after 11 — then walked down to Century for my spectacles — then back — Geo. & Nellie came shortly before 2 — dinner soon after — Geo. stayed until near 4 — Coleman came in between 5 & 6 — saw the boy & wondered — Tea at [illegible] 45 — wrote letters until 9 — then out & mailed them — in at 9.30 — read until 10.30 — then to bed before 11.
