## Left Page

### April, Monday 16. 1883.

Miserable, wet, east wind day
not rain but a very wet mizzle.
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on Coal mine Skafe for A.B. N-
Co. Stuck to it until 1 — then to
dinner — after laying an etching
ground for Waller who works at
my desk. — Back at 3. Commenced
7×9 water color sketch "Narrows
Lake Ge- from Blt. Mt. trail" — for Minn's
wedding present. Worked until
near 6. Took my W.C. "Digging
Potatoes" over to N.G. & left it for
Alfred Jones, as exchange for his
drawing "Feeding Dolly" that I got
from him in 1876. Home to tea at 7.
Quiet evg. did a little work
on Montrose plans — Main hall —
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 17. 1883.

Dark, wet morning — clearing to a
pleasant day. To studio at 9.30 &
to work on 7×9 W.C. sketch for
Minn. Home to dinner at 1 —
to bank before 3 — then in to see
Bishop Putnam about etchings &
demonstrate Lamb's Essays — then
to studio — made [illegible] & mounted
Minn's sketch. — Left at about
5 o'clk & went up to Dickle's and
had a half hour's ride on a very
poor horse — no enjoyment — home
at 6.45. — Found D. Adams, Nelie,
& four children at tea — they are in
town on a spree — stopping at the
Delano House — They left before 8.
I wrote a letter to Father.
