## Left Page

### October, Friday 19, 1883

Dull, grey day — milder weather. Bhlp: at 8.30 — After that, for some time with graders who got p'nvy into to new place at about 10 a.m. — then with carpenters at bay window. then I spent an hour signing 56 fab. + 14 parchment p.p. of my Bit on Marblehead Neck. Technic — Did them up in p.p. + before 1 o'clk. Took them to E's. Office — Dinner at 1.30 After that again with the men — then to work on Bay Window drawings until near 5 — again with men — then a half hours rest — tea at 6.30. Eveg. general chat — Mrs E. + Annie with their work — E. making sketches while I wrote a couple of letters. E. + I went to P.O. at 9 — slight shower — To bed at 10.30.
Heavy rain during the night.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 20, 1883

Heavy fog + quite mild in the mtg. wind changing to north with some driving drizzles — all the men at work through the day — I worked on Bay window plan during the mtg. Ehninger painted sketch from East window of Main Room — near 1 o'clk I went down Town — dinner at 1.30 out with the men — carpenters fixed their workshop in studio — again I worked some on Bay window. E. + I went down town at 5.30 — cold + windy — tea at 6.30 — Eveg. after reading papers, which until 10 — then clear moonlight and Ther. down to 34°. To bed at about 11 — a hard night of it.
