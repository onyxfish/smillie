## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 19, 1883

Pleasant day — got up with "a
head" after a disturbed night.
Austin came & painted part of
the canvas in main hall while we
were at help. After help. I worked
on hall plans until nearly —
three to my W.C. study ground.
The light, being pale & milky, was
bad, so was my head, so I sat
there under the trees until 12 —
then home & to bed until dinner
at 1 — After that, went down town
back & worked on etchings,
stopping out part of privy plate &
putting lake ge. in the acid to
bite part of 2° etching — afternoon
overcast — at 5 went to stable —
on a pony — back very soon —
then on "Nellie" — a good mare, rode
round the lake on her — in at 6.
Tea at 7 — everg. felt tired —
read for awhile, to bed [illegible]

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 20, 1883

mild & generally overcast — fair
day — but I did not go on the ground
until 12 o'clk. worked on plans for
m. wall of main hall — then, as my
head was very uncomfortable, I lay
on the bed until dinner at 1 o'clk.
Before we had finished, a basket
of peaches came, so Kitze & bruised
that & I sat down & prepared the
most of them for canning — at 3.30 I
went to rear of Hgur Nathul's chicken
where there is a picturesque combi-
nation of apple trees & outhouses & made
an outline to transfer to copper for an
etching. At 4.45 A. & I went out riding
home at 6.30 — Mr. Wm Mulford, Elisha
& the Dr. called, just as we came home.
Tea at 7.15 — after that, with news-
paper — then on piazza & at last
at 10, to bed.
