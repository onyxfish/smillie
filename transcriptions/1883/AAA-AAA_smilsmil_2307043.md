## Left Page

### November, Saturday 10, 1883.

Dark, rainy day — 9 o'c came in for a short time after bkfst — Rec. a letter from S. C. F. congratulating us upon the birth of our boy — To studio about 10 — little work done — looking over sketches & planning — Home to dinner at 1 — very heavy rain & very dark — back to studio at 2.¼. Worked until dark on charcoal sketch 20 × 30 in. based on large W.C. sketch made at Montrose last Sep; made a call on the Chambneys — Home at 6. Tea before 7. at 8, Annie & I went to first Phil. [illegible] season. Home before 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 11, 1883.

Gloomy with more rain at night. BKP a little later than usual — spent much of the morning writing — between 1 & 2, went to studio — Home to dinner at 2. Ed Parker there — The afternoon soon got away without anything being accomplished — Tea at 6.30 — wrote letter to father which Ed mailed for me — Between 8 & 9 went to mother Cook's for a little while — Home before 9 — to bed soon after 10.
