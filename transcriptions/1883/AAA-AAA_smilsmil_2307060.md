## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 12, 1883

*uncle A.S.S. died this m'g.*

Delightful day — but I felt Rattrin came in — got to studio before 10 + spent the morning stretching + mounting etchings — also, did some stopping out on N°. 30 — Home at — dinner at 1.30 — back about 3 and more mounting + wetting of paper for printing to-morrow. At 5.15 went to Putnam's store + met Annie — should have gone there at 4.45 — we went to Sterne's where A. got for me a dressing gown — then to Lord & Taylor's — put A. in stage, then I went to Denlake's + got a hat. Home — tea — at 8 I went again to studio + worked over etchings &c. until near 10 — then home — Mended the Cook's bedstead + got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 13, 1883

Pleasant day — got to studio at 9.45 — ground ill + to work at printing from some of my plates. I took it easy during the m'g., not feeling well — Home to dinner at 1.15 — Back about 2.30 + worked as long as I could see. — Then washed up and home to tea at 7 — Annie was at her mother's — doll dressing. I went there at 9 o'clk — Home about 10 — To bed before 11.

Learned to-day that uncle A. died yesterday m'g. between 5 + 6 o'clk.
