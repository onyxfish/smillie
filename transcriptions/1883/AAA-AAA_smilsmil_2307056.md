## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 4, 1883

Another charming day — to studio before 10 & spent the rest. working over etching plates — laying grounds — rebiting & etching — etc — Home at 1 — After dinner Annie went to Recital at Chickering Hall. by Mr & Mrs. Geo. Henschel — I went to studio shortly after 2.30 — Mr. Koshber came in with a parchment p.p. of my Mt. Head neck etching which I signed for him — considerable time was taken in discussing etchings — I worked some on my plates, altho' much discouraged by some of K's criticisms. At 5 went to Archer & Pan Mitchell & Vance. B'way & 19th S — about some gas fixtures — Then, by green car, rode to 10th Ave. & 42nd — then to Hayek's Furniture Rooms — Home at 6.30 — Tea — evening Reading news paper — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 5, 1883

Rain during the night — Pleasant, warm day — To studio soon after 9.30 — Spent the day over 3 etching plates — 29 — 30 & 31 — Rebiting — inter-etching, etc — Home at 1 — back before 3 — worked as long as I could see — got my three plates cleaned off — Then to Dickle's soon after 5 & rode for about an hour — Then home — About 7 had tea — Sister Mary with us — At 8 I went with her to Charles' — Spent the evef. there until near 10 — Then home & to bed at 11.
