## Left Page

### August, Thursday 16, 1883

Mf. Threats of rain which did not amount to anything. Got to my printing as soon as I could after bkfs. which was after 10 — Experimented with a kerosene stove for heat — smells — could not get what I wanted in the way of [illegible]. Don't know whether the trouble is in the flat (No. 26) or the printing — Dinner soon after 1 — then more printing from 25 & 26 until 5 o'clk. Then washed up & went down-town, got the horse & buggy & took [illegible] out — Rather a cold ride — also much discomforted by headache. Back a few minutes after 7 — tea at 8 — Pleasant bright fire in main room — looked over newspaper & got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 17, 1883

Got up with a dismal headache — Heavy fog that lifted about 10 o'clk — a hot, dry day. During the mf. little more than able to get about — went down town at eleven o'clk & did a number of "chores" — back after 12 — Rested awhile — dinner at 1 — At 2 started, driving family to depot — near the depot she became very restive, frightened by trains — Elig. Stowe came on 3.27 train — much trouble with the horse that for a time threatened to be serious — At last we got beyond hearing R.R. trains & all went well again — Home at 5 — tea at 7 — Spent on piazza — fine doors — brilliant moonlight — to bed about 11.
