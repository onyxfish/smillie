## Left Page

### August, Saturday 4, 1883

Oct. weather again — Ther. fell to 49°
last night — 60° at 9 a.m. — All day
very cool & breezy — most of the time
spent directing the laying of floor
in large Room — Dinner at 1.30
Payne & son got to work in the
afternoon with new horse — Backus
at work fixing canvas in main hall
& doing wood work — I mixed a
lot of Kalsomine & went over main
hall canvas — Carpenter's bench
moved out of main Room — west
windows put in — Rough printing
table put up in Etching Room — all
together the day has shown progress.
At 6 went down Town — home at
6.45 & had tea at 7.45 — then, until
after 9 with Annie — wrote awhile —
To bed at the usual hour, soon after
10.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 5, 1883

A turbulent, blustering day with
a severely quiet death — Ther. last
night down to 52° — did not get up to
70° on the shady side of the house —
Beautiful clouds & much wind —
even serene & cloudless — P.M. before
9 — I spent much of the morning
writing — also, walking on Piazza
with Annie — Dinner at 1.30 —
after that, more walking & more
writing — at 6.30 went to Wm Parks
& talked about horses for 5 min.
Home at 7 — having had too much
dinner I took no tea — Ever
lounging about until 10 o'clk —
then to bed.
