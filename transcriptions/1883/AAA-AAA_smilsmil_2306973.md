## Left Page

### June, Saturday 23. 1883.

Another glorious day. Part of mtf.
given up to Austin — another part
was spent in an Elm Tree gathering
worms to the extent of about half a pail
full — went down Town at 11 — saw Hy.
Turrell & spoke to him about buying
his lot near me. Home at noon —
dinner and — Calls in mtf. from
Mr. Westcott & friend — in afternoon
from de Schweinitz & Creamer — more
time with Austin & more time up
the Elm Trees gathering worms —
Went down Town at 6 — back at 7
& had tea — About 8.45 went over
to Courtright's. Flora & there — back
at 9.45 — for some time looking
over Prints from Chas. Photographs
— to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 24. 1883.

Very warm day — B.K.p. — soon after 8 —
Myself spent podging about, with
now & then a rest — took measurements & looked after odd ends —
gathered a final crop of worms from
the elms — At 2 went to Wm. Parks
to dinner & had a very pleasant
time until 4.30 — then started
up the hill — stopped at Wy. H.
Jessup's — Kate & Hetty Searle there.
Home at about 6 — tea after 7 —
wrote a letter to Annie on my
way to P.O. stopped at Flora
Dabrook's where Charle was —
after mailing letter. Home at
10.30 & to bed.
