## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 21. 1883.

Bright, cold day — Got to studio about 9.30 + to work Etching on Basque ox cart — Had it in the acid before I went home to dinner at 1 — Matt + Kate there — Ret. to P.h at 4. Back to studio before 3 + finished biting + took pf — looks rather promising. The Socy of Am Artists sent back my little Marble head neck sketch — not good enough for them — Home to tea at 6.30. At 8 — to N.A. Annual meeting of Water Color Society. 45 present — much curiosity to know who was running for Pres. Wood's vanity + weakness was too much for him + he allowed his friends to put him in — F. H. Smith was perhaps a little more outrageous than ever when he found that he could not carry his constitutional amendment over all Rule & precedent — Chase + Beckwith much incensed because Lundgren + Alden weir were not elected members — meeting adjourned about 11.15 — Home at 11.40 + to bed about midnight.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 22. 1883.

Brilliant day — to studio at 9.30 — owing to its being so cold I was delayed some time before getting started at my work. Re-biting on Basque Ox Cart. Some time also lost in talking over the incidents of last nights' W.C. meeting — Home to dinner at 1. Mary Sprague Cook + her daughter Catherine there to dinner — Back to studio at 2.30. Finished Re-biting + took pfs. At 5 went to N.A. + made hasty sketches of Dolph's picture for Hurty — Home to tea at 7 — Nettl. Read — dozed in front of fire — wrote notes to Pinchat + Nakkes and got to bed before 11.
