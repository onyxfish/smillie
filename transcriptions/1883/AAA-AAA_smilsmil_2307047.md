## Left Page

### November, Sunday 18, 1883

Deliciously mild, soft day — Up with a headache — after a late bfpi. I read paper & then out & slowly sauntered up Mad. ave. Park Aves. to 72°. St. then down ave. Home to dinner at 2 — Matt looking extremely ill & having much trouble with her breathing — Will & Joe called after 6. Annie & I dressed & at 7.15 we went to Walter's house where we sat at table until near 11 eating his 7th wedding anniversary dinner — Mother Cook, Aunt Anna, Mr. Willard & son. Ed M. Cook & wife — Geo. D., Jno. Valentine, Walter & wife, Annie & I. — After getting home read until 12 o'cl[ock] then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 19, 1883

State mild, delightful weather, to studio before 10 — wet paper & got to work proving Scribner etching over which I spent all the m[ornin]g. Home at 1 — after dinner spent some time trying to arrange Matt in a chair so that she could breathe more easily — her appearance very alarming — could not get her into a comfortable position — to studio about 3 — worked on Scribner etching as long as I could see — then home Tea as usual — every part of time in parlor with Matt & Kate then up stairs, reading — To bed before 11.
