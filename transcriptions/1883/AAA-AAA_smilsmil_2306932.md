## Left Page

### March, Saturday 31. 1883.

Bright mng., but soon overcast —
Got to studio at 9.45 — Laid ground
& sketched sky on Fontarabia plate
About 11 got to printing — Had
several calls — Home abt. 1.5 —
back at 2.45 & printed until
6.30 — then washed & cleaned
up — Home at 7.15, very tired.
Annie had been to "Private
View" at N.A., & reports that my
little Yosemite picture is sold.
Apo has had two offers of $800 —
& $825, from two men, for his large
Marblehead neck picture, besides
selling his small one. After
tea, Read news paper & got
to bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 1. 1883.

Beautiful day — B'kf'st — after 9 —
Spent part of mng. writing, then
walked up to Central Park and
over to 8th Ave. To 72d St — then home
to dinner at 2 — About 4 o'clk
Annie & I went to studio — there
until 6 — then home — tea
after 7 — Evg., Reading and
writing — at 10 o'clk. took my
Sunday home letter to despatch
box — To bed at 11.
