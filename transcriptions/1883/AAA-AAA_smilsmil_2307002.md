## Left Page

### August, Monday 20. 1883.

A hot, windy day with some clouds & slight showers. Spent the m'f. podging about, getting p/p ready to send to Koehler — f plates 25 & 26 — also getting things in order to begin drawing for B. B. N. Co. To-morrow. At noon went down-town with Annie & Elizabeth. Sent my p/p if by 4/p. Dinner soon after dinner — the p.m. went much as the m'f did until 5 when, with Annie & E., I went out riding — in at 7.15 then tea — Ev'g Reading paper & then aloud "Bourbon Lilies" until 10 — To bed befor 11.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 21. 1883.

Delightful day, breezy & clear. B'f's soon after 8 — Spent the m'f. in attending to many little things & shortly before noon went down town & attended to several other little things. Dinner at 1 — After that, a swing in the hammock — then to work on a B. N. Co's drawing — Yellowstone Falls, Geysers etc. At 5, out with A. & E. — had a very pleasant drive — home at 7.15 — found Emma Richards & sister — soon after, Mr. Comstock, Mr. Shaffer — Mr. Cookeen & Dan Sayre came & made a short call — We had tea at 8. Ev'g on piazza & loafing in big Room — Returned to my own bed by way of experiment. To bed about 10.30.
