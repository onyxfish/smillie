## Left Page

### December, Thursday 20. 1883.

Dark day — up with a headache
but got to studio at 9.30 — Did
a little work on Plate 33 but
in no condition to do anything —
thing — but did fuss some
with the mounting of etchings —
Home at 1 — dinner — then
gave up & went to bed where
I stayed until 5 — then up &
to Wm. Vanderbilt's Reception —
Saw more people than I did
pictures — Home at 7.30 — tea —
wrote notes to Century Nominating
Com. — Chas. Collins called —
after 9 I went to Mary Sprague
Cooke's about some etching [illegible]
her mother — home before 10 — and
finished visit with Collins —
to bed about 12 —

## Right Page

### December, Friday 21. 1883.

More snow — clearing in the
afternoon — another remarkable
red sunset — got to studio at
9.45 — Traced & outlined the
the drawing [illegible] "Stray Lambs"
for Water Color. Home at 1 —
dinner — Back to studio at 3 —
Kurtz left Shindaur's [illegible] etching
after E. Johnson's painting [illegible] and
wants me to write critical opinion.
Saml. Colman called — also
Muybridge the photographer.
Got two [illegible] packed to send to
Mr. Sprague — Took them to Exp.
office — To Century at 5.40 to see
[illegible] who came in after 6 —
Home at 7 — tea — went to
Grady & McKewan's with some etching
[illegible] — then to studio & worked on the
mounting of [illegible] — until after 10 — then
home & to bed soon after 11.
