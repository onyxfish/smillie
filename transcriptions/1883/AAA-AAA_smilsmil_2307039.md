## Left Page

### November, Friday 2, 1883.

Peasant day - B'kfo: at 8 - 9.30 when I got to studio - Father
came in before 11 & we sat &
chatted, Geo. with us, for some
time — I unpacked box of
Jap. Graving paper. Home
at — Mother & Mary took
dinner with us. Back to
studio at 2.45 — Scrubbing
woman with me all the
p.m. Scrubbing out the dirty
closets. Father came in
bet. 4 & 5 o'clk. Went to 28
S. to see Geo's family — then
to 38' S. where he took tea
with me. After he left, Charl
came in — he left before 9 —
then I wrote notes to de Schmit
& to Mr. Hoadley. To bed at 0.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 3. 1883.

Peasant day — after 10 when I
got to studio — Father came in
for a few minutes — He, Mother
& Mary are to return to Po'k by
3.30 train — I spent the m'f
sorting out things, throwing away
lots of Rubbish — Home to dinner
at — delayed by butcher until
near 2 — did not get to studio
until after 3 — then for two hours
again at the dirty work of
cleaning out closets — Home at
6 — after tea, a rest — before
9 to Century — Henry Irving
there — he brought George's
picture, a Nble Head Nick Sketch —
walked home with Geo — then
to 38' S. & to bed at midnight.
