## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 25, 1883

Brilliant cool day — like Sept —
most of the mfg about the house
with carpenters & plumbers. also
fixing bamboo screens for piazza.
Shortly before noon went down
town with drawings for printing
Fable & arranged with Alec Smith
to make it — Dinner at 1.30.
The p.m. went as the A. m.
did. About 6 again went
down Town — Tea after 7 —
another long sunset — some
reading of paper — then wrote
to Geo. & to Louis Tiffany & Co
about glass. To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 26, 1883

Warm, beautiful day. Up
at 7 — Started Backus Tacking
up canvas in main Hall &
after Bkfs: at 8.30 with Austin
getting glass into Bay Window
Sash. After 11 went down town
to see Alec Smith about my
printing Fable — exasperatingly
indifferent & slow. Box with
andirons came but no soak-
stone. Dinner at 1.30 — again
with the men, in spite of my-
self as I wanted to stick — at
4 o'clk. Goodwin came with
Barouche & from that time to
6.30 Annie, Mother, Oak & I
Rode — South Road, Butterfield
Hill, Wyalusing Creek Road, etc.
Tea at 7. The usual quiet
evening. & to bed at 10.
