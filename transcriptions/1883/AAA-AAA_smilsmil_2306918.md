## Left Page

### March, Saturday 3. 1883.

overcast wf. - clearing again befor 11 — To studio about 9.30 & spent som Fine fixing an inch strip on the bottom of 13×19 - Cathedral Rocks - making it 13×20 to fit frame. I mixed a sort of cement of Plaster of Paris & thick Shellac varnish & made a smooth surface with the — then worked on Telephone transmitter or Switchboard for B.N.Co. & finished it at 1 o'clk — then home to dinner — back before 3 & painted figure of old man in orchard show — also, inch strips on 40-saints subject. Then to 23 S. station & to Sherwood Bldg at about 5 - met Annie there — went through most of studios — saw Beckwith about Am W.C. Socy Treas. ship — He declines — Home at 6.30 — tea — to Century before 9 — Extraordinarily full & interesting meeting — interest, the project to move club to 15&17 W. 38 St. Discussion until near 11 — then a vote — 97 to 189 — lost by 3 votes — walked home in a snow squall — to bed soon after midnight.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 4. 1883.

Overcast day with quite a snow storm between 12 & 3 o'clk. Wf. went with Annie to Colyer's church & heard him — after service, rode down to Century to see the pictures — met Geo. Paston & wife & Fred. Ireland & wife. Rode home before 2 o'clk. during the heaviest of the snowing — Dinner soon after 2 — then read awhile. Then writing — Weather cleared cold & windy — about to went over to call on Will & found that he & Joe are in Washington. Then to studio then home & tea about 7. Busy lounging & reading — wrote letter home & took it to G.C. depot — then home & sat before fire and had a little nap — to bed at 11.30.
