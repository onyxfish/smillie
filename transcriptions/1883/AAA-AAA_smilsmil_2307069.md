## Left Page

### December, Sunday 30, 1883

mild, bright mtf. overcast afternoon — Bkfp. about 9 —
Loafed all the mtf on the [illegible] at 12. walked up to Junie's —
found Charley quarantined with the mumps — then to Will's in S.
S. to get views of the new arrival there Friday night — all well —
Home to dinner at 2. Will Cook
& his daughter Matthchew dined with us — after 5 I walked down
to Cautery to see some foreign pictures there — met F. Dielman &
his wife there — home at 7 — in
— every wrote letter home & at
10 took it to G.C. Depot —
to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 31, 1883

Dull day — got to studio about
9.30 & to work on Lake Geo. drawing
commenced by washing out
a part of it — After 11 Annie
came with 6 others, Matthchew,
Mollie & Katie Adams, Miss Will-
iams & her two nieces — we went
into Geo's studio — Miss Abbatt's &
Champney's — Home at 1.30 —
back at 3 & worked as long as
I could see — then took a nap
in my big chair & home at
6.30 — tea — at 8 Annie & I
went to Hunt Anna's & Kathrinie
then — just arr'd from Harkey —
left at 10.30 — home & to bed
at 11.30.
