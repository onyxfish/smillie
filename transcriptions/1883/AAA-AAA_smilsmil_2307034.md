## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 23, 1883

A dismal day — winding up with
a light but steady rain at night.
Up late. Help. at 9 — Annie suffering
from a headache — the E's amused
themselves while I looked after
the men + worked on bay window
drawings — Dinner at 1.30 — again
worked until 5 — then E. + I
went down town — Rain beginning
to fall — Annie in bed — Tea at
7 — Eveth. E's played cards
by themselves — I with Annie
\+ news paper — at 10. A. took
something to eat. By 11 we
got to bed —

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 24, 1883

Brilliant cloudless day — Help.
about 9 — the m[e]n spent with
the men + making final drawings
for Bay window — Mr. [Thos.] Park
called — Annie + [Ehrninger] went
to Warner's lot just before 1 —
Dinner at 1.30 — Soon after 3 we
went out for a ride south[?] [from]
Back at 5 — Tea at 6.30 —
Eveth. from 8 to 10 — whist —
To bed before 11.
