## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 30. 1883.

*Decoration Day —*

Brilliant m'g — then overcast — Rain
about 2.30 — clearing later with rain
& high wind again at night — To studio
at 9.30 — to work etching & lamb. plate
"Reading", being the corner of Parker at
home with Annie seated with a book —
I drew in the figure & bit it — then
home to dinner abt — back, through
quite a heavy shower, at 2.30 and
worked until 4 when I felt so much
out of sorts that I was obliged to give
up for awhile — Sam. Coleman came
in — he & Mrs. C. are visiting for 2 or 3 days
at Louis Tiffany's — I made transfer of
Reduction of Causeway. Mble.Pt. Neck. &
varnished plate — At 7 rode to 42d. St.
& called on Dr. Stephen Smittr — not in town —
then home to tea at 7.30 — again so
used up by condition of stomach
& bowels that I spent the remainder
of eveg. on the lounge & got to bed
soon after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 31. 1883.

Spent the day in bed, having
come to the conclusion that I
had better give up and see if
perfect rest will bring restoration —
With a headache added to my
other ills I spent most of the day
sleeping — Mr. Coleman called
to see Annie during the day —
Eveg. Geo. & Nellie called — I
got up about 9 o'clk — Transferred
myself to the lounge & spent the
rest of the night there, Returning to
bed at about 4 a.m.
