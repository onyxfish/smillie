## Left Page

### April, Monday 30. 1883.

Beautiful day — to studio at 9.30
& spent some time finishing on
drawing of Statue of "Liberty" — then
to work painting on "Yosemite Fall".
Home to dinner at 1 — got to [illegible]
at 3 & made a deposit — then
to Studio & painted until 5 —
Madam Roch called & asked
me to read my paper on Etching
before her school, but I had to
decline for want of time. At
5.30 got to Dickle's & rode for
about an hour without much enjoy-
ment — Home soon after 7 — had a
wash — Tea at 7.30 — eveg. doing
nothing — A.P. [illegible] called &
saw Mother this afternoon — Mother
& Annie called on Mr. & Mrs. J. Norton.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 1. 1883.

Bright & beautiful m'f., late afternoon
overcast with raw, east wind. To studio at
9.30 & to work on Yosemite Fall. Father
came in about noon having been to
la Roche's & bull. a. with Geo most of the
m'f. Calverey modeled — Home
to dinner at 1 — Mother at A.N.Y.'s —
About 3.30 I went with Father to
the Spring opening of the Met. Museum
& went through all the Halls & galleries.
Then out to the obelisk — then to
Mad. ave. cars — Father left me at
64th St. at 6 to go to A.N.Y. where he
met Mother & dined. I went
home & had Tea about 7 — at 8 went
to N.A. — then to Studio & home at 9 —
Father & Mother got in before I left —
on my return found Geo. & Nellie —
A great display of flowers, presented
to Annie in recognition of her birth-
day — To bed after 10.30.
