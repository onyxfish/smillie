## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 25, 1883

Heavy clouds & strong westernly
wind — Bkfs. between 8 & 9 —
I put in a fair morning's work
in studio with my etchings —
About 12.30 — with Anna & Emma
Took a walk about town, getting
home to dinner at 1.30 — About
3 o'clk. discovered that fire
had worked under hearth in our
bed room, a place that I have
been suspicious of for some time
past — Had Cottrell tear up the
tiles & cement & found a beam
charred nearly half through —
drenched it with water — the fire
did not break out into a blaze —
but was sufficiently exciting —
Wrote a letter to father & took it
to P.O. at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Evng
in general chatting — To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 26, 1883

Day of cool wind & beautiful clouds —
Bkfs. at 8.30 — After that, directing
work on new hearth in our bed room.
Went down town at 11 — saw Boyd &
Hamlin, the brick-layer — went to
foundry & gave orders for a casting
for hearth in main-room — Home
at 12 — Dinner soon after 1 —
At 3.30 out-siding with Julia,
Emma & Mary — To S2 Montrose,
over the hills towards Elk. Lake —
back over Butterfield & Searle's
hills — in at 6 — after a charming
ride — Tea before 7 — Evng
Annie & Emma played duetts
for awhile — then E. at piano
until 10 — then to bed —
Got ins. on house this mf. of
Tyler & Birchard in Continental of
N.Y., for $5000 — Rate 2¼ — for
five years — double rate on act.
of the house being unoccupied for
half the time.
