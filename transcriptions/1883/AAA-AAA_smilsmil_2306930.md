## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 27, 1883

Rain in the morning — light
& spatters during the day until
afternoon when it cleared very
pleasantly. Got to studio at
9.45 — Nicoll called & we spent
a little time discussing the
W.C. Socy situation — then to
work on Fontarabia etching — Home
to dinner — after dinner to Bank, then
to studio at 3 & stuck to my etching
until 6.20 — walked about Mad.
Sq. & home at 7.15 — after tea —
Annie read aloud more of
Democracy & played awhile
upon the piano — To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 28, 1883

*Poughkeepsie —*
*51st wedding anniversary —*

Charming day — Annie & I went
to Po'k. by 10.30 train — Met Geo.
at depot. Charl. failed to come
to time — got to P. at 2.35 & rode
right to Terrace — Dinner soon
after 1 — Father & Mother well, but
both suffering for want of out of door
air & a little healthy excitement.
The wedding anniversary is celebrated
one day ahead this year, because of
an important church meeting that
Father is very anxious to attend to-
morrow night. After dinner, a nap.
after 4.30 — Father, Geo. Annie & I
went to Mary's — there until after
6 — then home & had tea — Evely.
Unckett — Aunt A. — Aunt Jessie &
Emma, Mary & her children came
& we had a quiet evening —
Rev. W. Lawrence coming in later —
To bed about 11.30.
