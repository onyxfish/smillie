## Left Page

### November, Friday 30, 1883

Most of the day overcast — at times a few drops of rain — Annie, not sorts, so that she did not get up until noon — To studio at 10 — Spent some time putting things away & getting ready to begin Water Color work — Stretched large sheet & cobbled a frame for a smaller one — Home at 1 — back at 3 — Stretched some of the lots that I took yesterday — After 5 went to Park Ave Hotel & had hair cut. Home at 6.30 — Tea — Evening worked in basement & cell [illegible] for awhile, with butter crocks etc. & breaking up kindling stuff — then for an hour Annie read to me from Helen Newton "The Right & wrong uses of the Bible" — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 1, 1883

Delightful day — to studio a little before 10 — Shellaced drawing boards then set palette & painted in oil a little sketch to get a scheme of color for a larger Water Color, a view near Montrose to be titled "Stray Lambs" — Home at 1 — back before 3 & worked as long as I could see on sketch — After 5 went to Newman's & got a base warming arrangement, looking to the gradual weaning of James from — then to Grady that evening & had my account with them straightened out — discovered a mis take of about $100 on their books against me. Home & tea at 6.30 — After 8 went to Salmagundi Reception — So many people that I saw no pictures — after 9 walked down to Century with Nicole. Belmer & his friend of Boston — left Century for home at 11 — found that there had been a sleet storm — home & to bed shortly before midnight.
