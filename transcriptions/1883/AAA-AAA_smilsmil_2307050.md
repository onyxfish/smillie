## Left Page

### November, Thursday 22, 1883

Still strangely mild with light showers & sunshines — depressing.
B[illegible]. at 8.30 — Ed. with us — After
that, to butchers — then home — Will,
Joe & Charl on hand attending to
details for funeral — also Jimmie.
I went to depot & met Father &
Uncle Willie. Trains half an hour
late — got to house soon after 11.
Dr. Elder conducted a very simple
service — only members of family
present except Mr. Virreans & Fannie
Kimball. About 12, Will, Charl,
Uncle Willie & I started with the
body for Greenwood — got to the lot
in 1¾ hours. Saw the body placed
in its last Resting Place — then
home at 3.30 — Father, Kate & Ed.
had Ret. to P'k in 3.39 Train —
Miss Wilcox took her departure —
I Rested — Fra at 6.30 — Annie
& I alone at Table — Suey.
writing notes & Reading.
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 23, 1883

Mild, overcast & dark — Goes
to studio soon after 10 & to work
with Water Colors on "Crisp October"
— worked over foreground especially
— At noon, man came & took it
& "Old Rice House" for Providence
Art Club's [illegible]. At near 1 o'clk
went to 28th S. & saw Geo. who is in
bed to-day — bowel Trouble. Home
to dinner at 1.15 — wrote notes —
back to studio at 3 — fussed
over Etchnings & [illegible] — laid
ground on from my "Study Door"
for additional work. — at 5.30
went up to Yale's, visited with
Mr. Y — awhile before he came
in — wanted him to see Annie.
Home before 7 — tea — Suey.
trying to read — heavy and
stupid — Annie given over to
sleep — To bed at 10.30.
