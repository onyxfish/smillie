## Left Page

### October, Sunday 21, 1883

Cloudy & milder with fine sun-
set. Up late — Bkfs. at 9 — the
E's went to Elpis's [illegible] ch — I
stayed at home & wrote letters.
Dinner after 2. I tried to get
a little nap — about 4 went to
Payne's — with him crossed the
Post hill — went to top of Tarbell's
hill & crossed over to other side
& back through the woods out at
the old Searle farm house, looking
for trees to transplant, but could
not find any — Home at 6.30 —
Tea after 7 — Self — chatting
& writing more letters — went to
P.O. with them after 10 — home
& to bed at 11 — another bad night.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 22, 1883

Raw, nasty day — some cheery
rain in the m'fg. but the graders
put in a full day — I got up late
feeling very seedy — Bkfs. about 9.
In some time with the men, then
down town — had my hair cut —
Home before 12 & worked a little
on Bay window plans — Dinner
at 1.30 — after that, a short nap.
Then out with the men — After
4 came in — by 5, lighted up
& tried to do a little more on
plan drawings — Tea before 7 —
Self & whist until 10 — To bed
before 11.
