## Left Page

### February, Friday 9, 1883

Charming m'g. growing to be
airdy, raw & very dirty later in
the day. After b'kfst - went to Knoedler's
then to Chickering Hall & counted
the tickets - 717. Then to Keppler's &
to Wunderlich's. Got checks from
them - to studio after 11. Home
to dinner at 8 - Then to Philharmonic
Rehearsal - after that, went to
Mitchell, Vance & Co. with Annie,
to look for a hall lantern but did
not find anything that pleased us -
Then I went to studio - forgot to say
that at 12 I went to Hotel Brunswick
to see W. Haden about some bills, not
in - left a note for him. I eat at
about 6.30 - near 9 went to Ames Van
Wart's Redg'm, 20 E. 48 St — his Studio
on the upper floor. He showed a clay
bas relief "Battle of Centaurs" - very poor -
but the supper made amends —
gentlemen only invited but the ladies
of the family Rec'd. there. — Home & to bed
at 12 —     Rob't Hoe just gave a
dinner to Haden this ev'g.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 10, 1883

Very raw, uncomfortable sort of day.
Clear in the m'g - overcast in the after-
noon & snow at 10 p.m. After b'kfst -
went to Knoedler's & got his Haden check
— then to Bank & made my deposits.
Then to studio & worked until near
1 on details of ceiling for Main
Hall at Montrose - mailed them -
dinner at 1.30 - back to studio at
3 until near 5 finishing accounts
& report of Haden lecture business.
- net balance $103.00 My big frame
came from Met. Museum - Geo. tried
his picture in it & was pleased. - at 5
to Century - Yale - Gilder & Hoe there.
My Haden Report accepted - $1000 to be
given to it -, $31.00 to Annie to buy an
etching. Home at 6.30 - at 8 a. to
to Phil. Concert - Academy of music -
enjoyed it very much - walked
home in a snow storm & to bed at
11.
