## Left Page

### May, Sunday 6, 1883

Delicious day. Fresh & bright. Up
at 8 feeling tolerably well but
rather light about the head. Had
Help. at 9 — then spent most of what
remained of the mfg. on a lounge.
At 12, went to studio, made a short
call on Bellows who is in wretched
plight — wrote some short notes & sent
Dr. Hamilton's receipts for dues to
Church — Gifford, Farrer, Falconer & Van-
Elten — Home to dinner at 2 — At 4
walked up to 55th St. & 7th Ave. & called on
Thos. Moran — gone to Easthampton. Then
walked in the Park until after 6 — then
to A.V.V's — saw him, on his bed — Aunt H.
Charlie, Turie & the children — Home at
7.20 — tea — self. Reading — then
wrote to Parrish & to Father — Took
letters out & mailed them — then
in & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 7, 1883

overcast, with promise of rain — To
studio about 9.30. Kindled fire, in
the grate & got to work Painting
on Yosemite fall from Merced River.
Worked until 1 o'clk. Charle
called — just down from Po'k —
he tells of the deplorable state of
things there — Matt suffering severe-
ly — & no one realizing the true
state of the case — Home to dinner
as usual — Emma J. there — After
dinner went to Bierstadt's & got [illegible]
of Rocky Mts with his signature.
Then to 23d St. & 6th Ave. & saw Thos.
Moran, just moving — gave him
receipts from P.E. Soc'y & got $10.40.
Then to studio at 3.30 & worked until
near 5. Acting on a suggestion from
Chambney, improved my painting
greatly. Then to Archer, Pancoast & Co.
in Wooster St. & met A. & looked at gas
fixtures — Home — tea at 7 — Charl
came at 8 & we went to see Dr. Comenins
about Matt's case — he gave us no
hopes — Home about 9 — To bed
at 10.45.
