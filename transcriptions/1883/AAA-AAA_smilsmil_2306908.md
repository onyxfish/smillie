## Left Page

### February, Sunday 11, 1883

Eight or ten inches of snow fell last night — heavy rain this mng. which continued all day — Up late blkb — about 9.30 — At 10.30 went to Hotel Brunswick — Wt. Haden at blkb — Keppel with him — Sat circle them chatting for nearly an hour — H. + K. very much pleased with pecuniary success of lecture com. Then to studio there until near 1 — then home — dinner at about 2.30 — after that wrote a short note to Wt. Haden, to accompany check — then a nap — At 6, rode down to Giede's + got his signature — Then to Betts' + got his — in both cases had pleasant visits — Home to tea at 8 — wrote short letter home — at 8.45 went to Wt. Hor's — found him in + had a pleasant visit — got his signature — at 9.30 to Yale's — found him in — got his signature + home at 10.45 — wrote for an hour + to bed about midnight.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 12, 1883

Bright, clear day. Blkb. as usual — man came + commenced papering wall in dining room — putting a band of lighter paper about. To Hotel Brunswick at 10. Saw Haden + gave him check for one thousand dollars. He was much pleased. Then to studio — disgusted to find that I was in no humor for work, but tried to charcoal a large sketch from studies on Marble Head neck. Miss James came + talked of a project for a group of Am. Etchers to be published by Dodd. Mead & Co. Koehler sent me Cassell, Peter + Galpin contracts to sign — went to Bank then home — dinner at 1.30 — did not go back to studio until 3.30 — Father came in about 4.30 — after 5 I went to N.A. with him, the two alone, down to cor. Mercer + West — wash Rt. + bought folding screen for P's K. — then to Castuny — then home at 7 — Father there — tea — evng. wrote a letter to Koehler — to bed after 11.
