## Left Page

### August, Sunday 12. 1883.

a hot day with very slight but pleasant movement of
air. Bkfst at 9 — much of the m'g. writing to Colman.
Dinner at 2 — Lounging on Piazza — + more writing.
Before 6 Annie + I went to call on the Courtrights —
not in — then home — tea before 7 — Annie + I on the
Piazza enjoying the moonlight until 8.30 — then the
boy awake — a. attended to him + I wrote to Philf &
Kate Janvier + S. R. Koehler.
at 10 went to P. O. with
letters then home + to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 13. 1883.

Slight shower about 11 a.m.
cooler + overcast with a
shower about noon — a little
later, other showers passed
near, then it cleared charmingly.
Spent all day in putting the
glass into windows of main Room
transposing + arranging so as
to get best effects of color. Some
of the glass was so large that I
had to cut the Rabbits of the
sash. The floor of main hall
was finished to-day — Dinner
at 1 — At 5.15 went to Wm.
Cosper's where Annie was
making a call, got her and
took her out for a ride until
6.30 — Tea about 7.30 — Even'g
Reading newspapers in large
room which seemed rather
bare + chilly — To bed soon
after 10.
