## Left Page

### October, Sunday 7, 1883

Dripping wet day — heavy fog &
drizzle most of the mtf. [illegible]
before 9 — much of the time
taken up with my domestic
duties — then walked [illegible]
for some time before dinner
enjoying the dinks & the faded
colors. Dinner at 1.45 — The
afternoon was squandered —
altho' it takes nearly all [one's]
time to attend to the wood fire.
At 5 wrote letter home — At 6
went to P.O. then walked
about town until 7 — then
home and had tea — Evening
in big chair — to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 8, 1883

Heavy fog that melted before noon
then a mild, hazy day — Pettis &
two men at work on the grading —
I had many chores of all sorts to do.
Stove put up in charm room — went
down town after 11 — back at noon —
made up a lot of new mordant &
varnished 9 x 15 plate — Dinner,
soon after 1 — Then out on the hill
with paper & pencil & made a
nasty sketch — back at 4.30 — and
found Mr. Agur & [illegible] making a
call on Annie — Phaeton at the
door — we got off to ride at about
5 — back at 6 — I went down town —
met Billings Strond & learned that
I had paid Tyler double what I
need to for insurance — Home to
tea at 7 — Evef., worked for a
while on plan for Transom — to
bed before 10.
