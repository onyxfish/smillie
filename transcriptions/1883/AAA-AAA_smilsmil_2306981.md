## Left Page

### July, Monday 9, 1883

Overcast & very cool — Got to
studio at 10 & spent the m'g in
picking up things & getting in
order the material to be left &
gathering together that which
is to be taken to Montrose. At
12 went to Aspinwall's File place
for bill — then to Putnam's & got
$125. for my two etchings — then
to 2d. Nat. Bank (met Dr. & Mr. Ken-
drick) then home to dinner at 1 —
Back to Bank at 2.30 to get a book
that I had left there carelessly —
got it — then to studio & worked
as in the m'g until a little after 4
then home & with Annie, Mother &
Mrs. Hervé & the boy, went out for a
ride in Central Park — Cool & very
pleasant — back at 6.15 — Annie but
little tired — she took tea with us
down stairs — Walter C — came in —
I wrote a note to Koehler — at
10 took it to J. C. D. Then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 10, 1883

Very pleasant day but I got
up with a stupid headache
that I fought all day — Got to
studio about 10 & spent the m'g
in my room, putting things
away for the season etc — Home
at 1 — After dinner (miss Juliette
& miss Sophie J. dined with us) I
went to King's & ordered a Landaulette
for it at 5 — then at about 4 went to
studio but I could do nothing on
act of headache — hung about
until after 6 — then home — took
a light tea & to bed about 9.
