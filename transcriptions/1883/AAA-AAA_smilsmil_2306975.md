## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 27, 1883

Heavy Rain in the night with continuations during day &
eveg. Got to studio before 10 & put in a big days work
proving etching of a Corner at Home — To dinner at 1.30 — back
at 3 & worked until 7.30 —
Took 20 [illegible] [illegible] — When I
got home was surprised to
find Father — Had tea about
8 — Eveg soon passed — To
bed at 10.30.

At 2 o'clk. Walter's. Edward
cut his right arm severely
thrusting it through a window.
Emile came with him and
Marie & Mother Cook went
off with them to find a
Doctor to dress it.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 28, 1883

Very bright, warm day — after bkfs.
went with Father to Studio where
he saw my etchings — I spent the
rest of the day trying to get [illegible]
of Sun Dial Etching — but did not
work right — Home to dinner soon
after 1 — they came in at 2. Mr.
Hervé quite ill with a chill —
got to studio at near 4 — after
having delivered [illegible] & one plate
to J. Bishop Putnam. Feeling out
of sorts I did little else than
write some notes — Had a call
from Mr. Frank Waller — Home
to tea at 7 — Eveg wrote a
number of notes to Painter Staten
Parrish, Farrer, Bellows, Van Elten
& Falconer — To bed about 11.
