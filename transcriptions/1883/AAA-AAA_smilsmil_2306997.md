## Left Page

### August, Friday 10. 1883.

Hot in the sunshine but a cool
air stirring — After [illegible] dampened
& stretched p.p. Taken yesterday
& spent some time studying
them — more time spent with
the carpenters & old Dorsey —
Payne & team not at work to-day.
Dinner soon after — Tried to
get to work on my etchings but
was too much interrupted —
Feeling tired & sleepy, Took
a nap at about 4 o'clk — At
5.40 went out with Annie
& had a pleasant ride, getting
in at 7.30 — After 8 when we
had tea. Much amused by
Lyon's article in "The Democrat"
pub. to-day, descriptive of our
house — Read newspapers &
got to bed at the usual time.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 11. 1883.

Still warmer, dry & hazy — [illegible]
after 8 — Got my etching p.p. in
good shape & studied up on
them awhile — with carpenters
laying floor on main hall — then
to work on No. 26 with drypoint
graver, burnisher, charcoal
& water of ayr stone. Dinner
at 1.30 — For some time with
Herrick laying out part of floor —
for awhile again at work on
26 — then putting main room
in order, to take possession of
it to-night — At near 5.30
out with Annie for a ride —
in spite of heat & dust, had a
pleasant time — back at 7.15 —
Took tea in the main room —
Lighted all the lights — Took
a frugal meal in stately
manner — Walked on upper
piazza until near 11 — Then
to bed.
