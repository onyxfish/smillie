## Left Page

### October, Saturday 13. 1883.

Up late + feeling very seedy —
Heavy fog again — after bkfst got
things ready for printing — then
out + spent the mf. with the
graders — all nine men + six
horses at work until 3 o'clk, when
a very heavy rain that continued
into the night, drove them from their
work, so that they made only 3/4 of a
day. Dinner soon after — all
the afternoon at work printing —
got two prints each from four
plates. After 5 when I washed
up — very tired — on bed for a
rest when Mr. + Mrs. Park came
to tea which was served near
7 — at 8.15 went to P.O. — very
heavy rain — the P's left before
10 — To bed about 10.30 — when
owing to last night's experience
I basely abandoned my own
bed + slept in the N. E. Room,
leaving Annie to fight it out
with the boy alone — but he
behaved very well —

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 14. 1883.

overcast day — very mild in
the mfg., with wind shifting
to the N.W. when it became quite
cold — Up late — bkfst at 10 —
loafed during the mfg — dinner
at 2.15 — more loafing until
after 4 when I went out on the
Warner hill + over to the Sayre
hill + down to the outlet of the
lake — enjoyed the drear Oct.
effects in the woods — Home
at about 6 — tea at 6.30 — Evefg
writing until after 9.
