## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 13, 1883.

overcast & rather raw - After bkfs.-
went with Annie to Warren & Co's &
looked at paper for frieze to dining
room. At 10.30 to studio & spent
the rest of the m'g worrying over
charcoal sketch on canvas x
marblehead neck. Home to dinner
at 1 — back at 3.15 — & worked over
same sketch until after 5 — then
to W.C. [illegible] there met F.H.
Smith & he appointed himself &
me a committee of two to select W.C.'s for a London [illegible]. As we talked
the subject over he got quite enthus-
iastic about it & says he will put
it right through. Home to tea at
6.30 — In the eveg. Geo & Nillie
came up to see Father — I spent part
of eveg. patching a Chinese screen.
To bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 14, 1883.

Nasty day — snow in the morning,
soon turning to rain — Bkfs. as
usual. Father went down town — to go
home by 4 P.M. train — Spent some
time with Paperhanger who is
at work in dining room — then to
studio about 10.30 — set palette & to
work on oil study of old orchard,
painted in Jacksonville. Home to
dinner at 1 — Matt & Kate there —
have come down to stay a day or two
— more time with Paperhanger —
to studio about 3 — Carpenter shops
back of N.A. on fire — so dark that
I could not see to work much of the
time — but smeared away at study.
Home at 6 — tea at 7 — Charl.
came in to see Matt & Kate for a
few minutes — I read a little for
awhile, worked over Montrose
plans. To bed soon after 11.
