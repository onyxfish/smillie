## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 31. 1883.

Cool, breezy day with clouds & some short showers. Got to work again at my etching on 25. Finished ready for the acid & found I had no bellows. Payne & Dorsey came with team & worked on carriage road. R's boy at work in the m[orning]. Went down town at noon. Dinner at 1. After that went to Smitie's & then to Warner's about printing Fable — then to foundry & got pair of moulder's bellows. Home before 4. Smart short shower at 5. Then Dan Sayre came with his new carriage & team. A & I went with him. Mr. Dan. S. joined our party & we rode until 7. D. offers the whole turn out for $750 =. Tea at 7.30. A quiet ev[ening] with wife, baby & newspaper. To bed about 10.30.
The oak for front door arr[ived] from Binghamton to-day.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 1. 1883.

Weather, seasonable, — misty, sunny & warm. B[rea]kf[ast] after 8 — then I had to give herself to making a drawing for floor of main room. After 12 when I went down town. Dinner at 1.20 — after that got at my biting but as my acid was too cold it did not work well — decay in warming it — finally got it to go, but was not able to finish. Down town soon after 6. Home & had tea at 7.30. Rested after 9 in the bosom of my family — then to studio & did some writing. Started a letter for [Thuniger] but did not finish it. To bed soon after 10.
