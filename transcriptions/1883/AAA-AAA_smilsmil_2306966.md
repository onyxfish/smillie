## Left Page

### June, Saturday 9, 1883.

A very warm day, but with a pleasant breeze. Got through with my domestic duties & to studio at 9.30. Gave Ge° a little more help in biting his plate — made a 2d pen drawing "View from Governor's Island" for Parsons as I was not pleased with the first — at noon Fisk Condit's pf. to W.p. Office then home to dinner at 1 — back before 3 — stopping to see Ge° who went to Po'k on 3 P.M. Train. Finished Parsons sketch. D[illegible] called at 5 o'clk. Then I went to 124, 5th Ave. Woman's School of Technical Design (should have gone at 4) to act as a judge — other judges gone but I looked over competing designs — Home at 6.30. Sea fog coming in made it pleasantly cool — W[illegible] Mrs. Julia Dillon sent to Annie a present of Study of morning glories — clever —
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 10, 1883.

Tremendously hot day — the worst one yet. D. Lusk came about 9 — help a little before 10 — nearly used up with a beastly headache — did little else than lie around. Chap. Collins came for Ge°. They went to Coney Island for the day. Dinner at 2. — again lying around loose until 6.30 — then Tea. Sat with Annie until 9 — then wrote to Father — Charl — Chap. [illegible] & Chap. Parsons. Out at 10 to mail letters (also one to Mrs. Julia Dillon) — at 10.30 went to Walter's for a few minutes to see him about some Montrose work. To bed about 11.30.

About 5 P.M. Nellie came to the house & brought Sheldon. He & little Jim had an interview —
Thunderstorm about midnight.
