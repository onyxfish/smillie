## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 14. 1883.

Cold day — partially overcast with slight snow flurries —
Blcp — later than usual —
after 10 when I got to studio
To work on Horse Car — Home
to dinner at 1 — back before
3 — awhile on horse car —
then to work on sketch for
Scribner etching for Donald
G. Nirtchell — Home soon
after 6 — tea at 7 — every
coming letters to Hoadley,
Collins — I. D. Paupn — str —
G.e.? called for a short
time on Matt — To bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 15. 1883.

Clear, windy & cold — 10 o'clk after, when I got to studio — and
got to work at once on sketch,
with pen, of new england farm
house for Scribners & abt 2 o'clk
went here with it — Alp'd roved.
Home at 1 — dinner — near 3
when I got to studio — laid
around — made reduction —
got transfer & varnished plate
— call from Mr. Galt. Home
soon after 6 — Tea — at 8 — with
Annie & Kate, at Union Square
Theatre & saw Jefferson in "The
Cricket on the Hearth." (Caleb
Plummer) & "Send me five
Shillings." (Mr. Golightly) — very
much amused — out at 11 — home
at 11.30 — To bed at 12.
