## Left Page

### June, Friday 15, 1883.

A pleasant day, but warmer than yesterday — Got to studio by 10 — wrote letter to Catnell — then to work proving my Putnam plate — also adding a little work from time to time — With my usual experience, began to get du hang of things forward the end of the day — home to dinner abt — back at 3.30 — having gone to Douglas' & got some blue paint for m— then to work again proving & stuck to it until 6.30 having taken 16 pp. in all on different kinds of paper. Bryase called. — Home at 7.30. Charl called in the evg. to say that he would go to M. to-morrow. Tea at 8 — Eveg. so tired out that I lay upon lounge until 10 — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 16. 1883.

*Montrose Hotel — Room 37.*

Very pleasant day — Bkfst as usual. Then commenced packing trunk with curtains, blankets etc for Montrose as well as some minor articles for myself. Soon after 11, out with Hale & did some messages. Put up my lunch at 2. Coufé came at 12.15 — said my goodbyes to Annie & the boy & then to foot of Christopher St. Met Charl there & we took 10 ck. train & got to New Milford at 7.40 — found a wagon waiting for us and had a refreshing drive to Montrose. Eveg. cloudy with some threats of rain — got to "Montrose House" before 9.30 — the old Wm Post Mansion — was put into Room 37 a part of the old back parlor where I have had many a boyish frolic years ago — To bed at 10.30
