## Left Page

### May, Saturday 26. 1883.

Hot day — overcast in the afternoon,
with rain at night. Got to studio
about 10 & ate the wife worked on
tracings to transfer to etching gr[oun]ds
in the afternoon. Home at 1 — back
at 3. feeling out of sorts very much
got read. Reductions & tracings to
transfer through S. Pot. Causeway,
Nüsle[?] Neck — Lincoln Ave. Po'k.
the Sun Dial & sketch of corner at home.
Wet paper, dampest tracings, set
press & got excellent transfers of Rough
S. Pot & the two small Putnam pl[ates]
but the Causeway & Lincoln Ave. were
not successful much to my chagrin.
Home at 7 very tired — tea — the
eve[ning] spent mostly on my back &
to bed at 10 o'clk. but passed a
very restless night.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 27. 1883.

Very warm but delicious day —
Bkf. at 9.30 — the wife on lounge —
dinner at 2. — after that more
general lounging. — the family
returning to usual appearance.
About 6 Annie & I sauntered
to Reservoir Square — sat there
a few minutes — then home to tea
at 7. Eve[ning]. I wrote letters to
Father & to S. R. Koehler. Also
took them to G. C. Depot — then
home & to bed before 11.
