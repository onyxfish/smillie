### November, Wednesday 21, 1883

Depressing sort of day — very mild — at times heavily overcast with rain. Got to studio after 10 o'clk. and got to work. Faking impressions from Scribner etching — Home down after 1 — dinner — constant interruptions — Mrs. Lord called — Dr. Throop came to took dinner — Dr. Smith completed his post Mortem examination before 2 — found the left lung entirely destroyed by cancerous infiltration — Back to studio at about 3.30 — Took H. Jake [illegible] Home before 6 — tea at 7 — Welly, Geo. & Nellie and Chare came — Annie used up with a wretched sick headache — Ed. went home with Chare & spent the night with him — To bed at about 11.
