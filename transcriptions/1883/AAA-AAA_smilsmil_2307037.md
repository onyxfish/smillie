## Left Page

### October, Monday 29, 1883

A hurricane of wind &
Rain nearly all the m'g.
Rain ceased about noon —
Remarkable sunset & more
wind at night — All the
m'g hard at work preparing
to flit — Soon after dinner
about 2 o'clk — Saw Hy. Tunnell
measuring the ground around
lane that I want to buy and
to him — Long talk — no issue —
Then down Town — lots of work —
among others saw Mr. Tyler
about small plot of ground I
want to buy of him — much
Talk — no result — Tea at 7.
Every — packing up things
until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 30, 1883

Almost a hurricane blew all
last night & a fierce wind blew
all day — some heavy rain in
the m'g, then clearing — Beautiful
sky — Eve'g clear & quiet — All
day on a drive — men at work
setting out Pine Trees — went down
Town about 10 — settled lots of bills
Got a note from Mr. Tyler telling
me that he will sell me 1/10 of
an acre to widen my road for
$100 — I saw him & told him that
I would not give more than $50 —
Dinner soon after 1 — again
at work packing until 5 — then
down Town — back at 6.30 —
Tea at 7 — at work for an hour
or so on accts — then packed
trunk & to bed at 10.
