## Left Page

### August, Thursday 30, 1883

mild pleasant day — Up late &
Hfs. at 8.30 — after attending to my
"small chores", got to work on
etching No. 27 — & finished putting
it in — Dinner at 1.30 — then
bit in etching — carriage came
at 4.30 — so did Mr. de Schweinig
& her friend Miss Bradshaw —
After awhile, Rose out with father
& mother & Willet — back in about
half an hour. Mr. Dan Sayre and
Mr. Comstock & friend there — made
a change & again out riding with
Annie, Elizabeth & Will — back
at 6.45 — tea about 7.30 — Will
lolling about — Annie read aloud
a short story — To bed about 11.
Simon Casey's house was burned
down last night — or about 2 o'clk this
mng.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 31, 1883

Charming day — Brf. Soon after 8 —
as soon as possible got at my etching
No. 28 "Neighbor Eldred's Barn" and
worked at it out of doors, direct
from Nature. Enjoyed it very much.
worked until dinner time — at 2 o'clk
At 2 Elizabeth Howe left us, —
Willet. went to depot with her —
she goes right on to Chicago. I
finished 28 — bit in it & took
imp. of it — also of 27 & at 5 o'clk
washed up — very tired — tea
after 7 — after 8 Will & I walked
down to P.O. — the rest of evng. read
newspaper & looked at my pfs —
To bed about 11.
