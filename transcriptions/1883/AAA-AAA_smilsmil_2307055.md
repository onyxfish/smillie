## Left Page

### December, Sunday 2, 1883

Dismal morning — up with a headache, against which I fought
all day — Weather cleared at about 11 o'clk. gusty & cold — wrote letter
home — about noon, walked to the studio — home to dinner at 2.
After that Geo. Cook & Valentine called — then Walter & his family.
Soon after 5 — went with Annie to Ed. W. Cook's — about 6 — went to
G.C. depot with Father's letter — then walked to 5th ave. & 50th S. —
then down to 34th S. — & thence home.
Tea at about 7 — Spent some time looking over a portfolio of photos
— then some reading — To bed at about 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 3, 1883

Splendid day — Bkf. at 8 — Annie walked down to studio with me at 9.30.
I painted on "View of Assinins Falls" that had just been returned from Atlanta —
Miss Hearn called soon after 12 — at 12.30
I went to 2nd Nat. Bank. Home to dinner at 1. Studio at 2.45 — Painted on "Old
Orchard" — Mary (sister) surprised me by coming in about 4 o'clk — just down
from P'k. At 5 I went to Dickle's — rode — home at 6.30 — tea — eve'g
Annie read aloud "Much Ado about
Nothing" — To bed about 10.30.
