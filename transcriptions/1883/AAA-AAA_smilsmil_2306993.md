## Left Page

### August, Thursday 2, 1883

Overcast m[illegible]. with a very little
rain — clearing & warm intu aftu
noon — Afterbkfo — some time with
carpenters who have commenced
laying floor in large Room, also
with Payne, Jon & Dorsey, Road-
making — also did some biting on
my plate 25. Went down Town —
had a man come from [illegible] to
fix up my press — Dinner soon
aftu 1 — more general overseeing
work — finished biting on my plate
— cleaned off ground & did some
dry pointing — Mrs. H. F. Tyler & her
mother called — down-Town at 6 —
tea aftu 7 — until 9 with Annie.
Then wrote a letter to [illegible] —
to bed soon aftu 10.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 3, 1883

Superb breezy cool day — also
went down Town with a long list
of messages to attend to — got back
at 11.30 & then to work on No. 25
with graver, cutting in a general
way over the dark parts of foliage —
Dinner soon aftu — some time
spent over floor in Main Room —
Backus fussing with vines & vines
at Studio piazza — again put in
some time on 25, with dry point —
Mr. Read & Miss Hyle called — After
6 Annie & I went to Florence [illegible]
I left her there & went down Town —
called for her about 9, then home
& had tea — suef with the
family & to bed very soon after
10.
