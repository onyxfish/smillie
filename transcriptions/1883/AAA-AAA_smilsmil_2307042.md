## Left Page

### November, Thursday 8, 1883.

Charming day. Quite late before I got away after bkfs —
then to market, then to studio,
then to Jno. Starns's — Pier 19 — foot
of Courtlandt street to hurry up by
& crate sent from Montrose (Oct 3).
was promised that they should be
sent home this P.M. Then to
Uriiderlich's & saw Whistler's
etchings. Impudent trash — then
home — dinner at 1.20 — Studio
at 2.30 — made charcoal sketch
11½×15½." The Narrows, Lake
Geo. from Blk Mt. Trail." Griffin
Cassell's head canvasser came
in & I promised to write a note
for him to Drexel. Home to tea
at 6.30 — Welf, Geo came in
for awhile — I wrote note
to Drexel — To bed before
11, Will & Jos came in between
9 & 10 o'ck.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 9, 1883.

Dull rainy day, heavy rain
in the afternoon — After bkfs, to
market & grocers, then to Star Theatre
& bought 8 tickets for next Monday
night — Irving & Miss Terry "The Lyons
Mail". Then to studio before 11 —
spent remainder of mfly in unpacking
box &c. from Montrose — Mr. Marris
of Scribner's called to get me to etch
a plate for them — new edition of "Dream
Life" — Home at 1 — dinner soon after —
at 2, Kate & I at Phil. Rehearsal —
walked up town with Wm. Martin —
went to Hunter's & got eye glasses —
then to studio — Home & tea at 6.30
then went to Mother Cook's & got
some of Geo's tools to open crate &
set up Baby wagon — Walter Wright
came back with me — most of evelf
tinkering over the wagon — To bed
soon after 10.
