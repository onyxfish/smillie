## Right Page

### January, Monday 1, 1883

Charming day — Got to studio
a little before 10 & to work on
14×26 "Causeway connecting
Marblehead neck to Mainland"
washing in sky — Put in a good
m'f work & felt quite gratified.
Home to dinner at 1 — Very
little seemed to be going on in
the way of calling — Matt had
been to Dr. Smith's & had a second
application of caustic from which
she suffered exceedingly — so much
as to distress me very much, but
she, with Ed & Kate started for
home on the 4 o'clk train — I went
to studio & worked from 3 to 4.30 —
then went to Kimball's — Martin's
in 21 S. & Gray's in 28 S. Home
at 7.30 & had tea — too tired
to go out — To bed at 10 — but
did not secure a good nights
rest.
A slight fall of snow at night.
Called also on Miss Abbatt, Mrs.
Chauntpney & Mrs. McKnight, in our
building.
