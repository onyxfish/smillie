## Left Page

### October, Thursday 25, 1883

Delicious day until after 3 p.m. then overcast & at night, light
rain — B[illegible] after 8 — the Chimneys
left us soon after 10 — going to N.Y.
city — I spent the day with gardeners
& carpenters — clearing out chimny
room on Main floor — getting things
ready for packing & hunting for
trees to transplant — Dinner at
1.30 — went down town at 6 —
tea at 6.45 — Even[ing] with Annie
going over Montrose Bank accts
in which I found three mortifying
errors — about 11 o'clk. when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 26, 1883

Dismal day — up with a
wretched headache — Kept
about until 11 o'clk — then
to bed, where I stayed until 10 o'clk at night — then
up & attended to my chores —
Annie had the chimny room
fixed for me & to that I re-
tired after 11 o'clk. making
for myself a cup of tea and
eating some bread & butter —
Sat in easy chair until 1 —
then in with Jas. [illegible] for a
time until he got quiet[ed] —
Back to chimny room & to bed
at 2 a.m.
