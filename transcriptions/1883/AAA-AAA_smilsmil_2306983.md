## Left Page

### July, Friday 13, 1883

Up soon after 7 after a somewhat restless night. Bkf. soon after 8 — much time with Father &
Mother — also with Annie, as there is more trouble with the cook — Abt 1 I went away over to 632 E. 17th. to see Rosena Q. — our to-be-chambermaid, to learn something about a friend of hers willing to go with us to Montrose as cook — found her at 36 E 7th S. — then to Donnell's & bought night lamp — then home. Dinner at 3.30 — Grace D. with us — also Father & Mother. They Ret. to Po[keepsie] on 3.30 train. I went to studio & completed packing of large box — very heavy thunderstorm for a few mins. just before 6. Annie out riding & just caught in the edge of it — Home to tea at 7 — Eve'g. quiet — To bed abt 11.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 14, 1883

Hot weather again — After bkf. went to Newman's — then to Knödler's — got a carman to take big boy from studio to freight depot — then to Tiffany's where I spent the rest of the mf. selecting jars & having [them] cut — Home to dinner at 1.30 — after that took a nap — got to studio at about 4 — re-made a box in which to pack glass for Montrose. did other packing. Home to tea at 7 — Eve'g. took a little walk & a ride in the Mad. ave. cars with Annie — home soon after 8 — To bed abt 11.
