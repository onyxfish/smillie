## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 27, 1883

Very cold, windy day — after [illegible]
did some writing — & g at to studio
at 10.30 — cleared up my room
& got ready to paint on "Cathedral
Rocks, Yosemite" about 14 × 19 —
Home to dinner at 1 — Matt &
Kate there — back to studio at
3 & painted until 5 — then to
Dickel's Riding Academy in 56th
St. arranged to take exercise
rides — after that walked up
to 57th St. to see new buildings
Home at 6.30 — Tea at 7. At 8.30
went to F. H. Smith's — 150 E. 34th St.
& met a number of water-colorists & Mr. Hamler Stanley Philpot
an English art dealer, who desires
to take a number of Am. W.C.s to
London for exhibit & sale. — there
until after 10 — left as the company were sitting down to supper —
F.H.S. C.Y. Turner & self on a
committee to "assist" Mr. Philpot.
Home & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 28, 1883

Breezy, dirty day — go to studio
before 10 — & painted on Cathedral
Rocks — home to dinner at 1
Matt & Kate much distressed
because Dr. Smith is out of town
& they cannot learn where he is
likely to be home. go to studio
at 3 — painted until 5 — then
went up to Dickel's with Chuck
in payment for riding tickets
— part of riding visit — got my
closet, then walked home — tea
before 7 — drew some cash [plans]
for Catnell & at 8.45 went to
[illegible] gallery — B'way & 12th St.
to see the Runkle pictures —
the fellows & the supper — home
about 45 & right to bed.
