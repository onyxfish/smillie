## Left Page

### January, Saturday 20. 1883.

a horrible day of slush & rain.
To studio first & then, about 10.45, to N.A.
Chase came later — & we got along
pretty well until noon — at 2.30 I
went to Bank & to Knordler's with Haden
placards. Home to dinner at 1.15. after
that I went to Keppel's & Wunderlich's
with placards & to N.A. at 3.30. worked
until near 6. Then home & had a
short rest — back to N.A. pouring Rain
at 8.30 & worked until 10.30. piling
things up on the wall & corridor, finishing the
north & East Rooms — Home at 11
& right to bed —

Saw Uncle Willie for a few min-
utes at the studio in the m'f.

Geo. & family went to Po'k.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 21. 1883.

clearing day, most of the time
more or less overcast. Slept. at
about 10 o'clk — then read news-
papers for awhile & after 11 lay
down on bed & had quite a nap —
did not get up until after 1 —
Had dinner soon after 2 — after
that did some writing — about
5 o'clk. Annie & I went around
to Mr. Peters' in 37 th St. To see Mr. &
Mrs. Saml. Colman — C. was in and
while we sat chatting with him,
M. & Mrs. P. came in & then Mrs. C —
we had a pleasant visit — left
soon after 6 — Took a little walk
then home to tea at about 7 — after
that, wrote my letter home — then
Annie read aloud Howell's new
story in the Century "A woman's Reason"
— at 10.30 Took letters to G.C.
Depot — back & to bed about
11.
