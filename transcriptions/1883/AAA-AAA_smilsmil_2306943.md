## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 24, 1883.

overcast, with showers — After bkfs. went with father to la Roche's, in 54th S. to have his teeth fixed — left him there & walked down to studio, getting there at 10.15 — Worked all the m'f on W.C., Rough Sport. Calverley modeled a head — Home at 1 — back at 3 — again C. modeled & I worked on W.C. until 5 — then to Dickle's and rode horseback — home before 7 — Tea soon after — Evert. Gel called — I worked on Montrose plans until after 10 — to bed before 11 — clear night.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 25, 1883.

Bright, breezy and cold. After bkfs. went over to Wee's in West 34th S. with a water color drawing that I have made as a wedding present for Minn — Fake it to her to-day because it is her 22d birthday. To studio soon after 10 — worked on Rough Sport — Charl came in about noon with some photos that he has taken from a Moran painting owned by J.W. Drexel. He went home with me to dinner — Back at 3 — Calverley modeled again — I finished Rough Sport at last. Home to tea about 6, walked up with Annie. Wee & Joe and Minn & Walter came to tea. Spent the evening at home and to bed soon after 11.
