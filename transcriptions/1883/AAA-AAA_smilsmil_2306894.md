## Left Page

### January, Sunday 14. 1883.

Overcast [illegible]. clearing to be very cold & bright — Help — at
9.30 — after that read & after then sat by dining room fire
& wrote until after 12 — then down to studio, from there to
Century & from there to 5 Ave. Hotel
& telegraphed to Cotrell to come to
N.Y. to have his eyes doctored — then
home at 2.15 & had dinner. After
that rested awhile, then wrote
to Cotrell & father — about 6 went
up to Central Park & back — Tea at
7 — Trouble with the gas — would
n't burn — [illegible]. Reading &
writing until after 10 — to bed
before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 15. 1883.

Cold & rather dirty day — got
to N.A., after first going to studio,
at about 10, to begin hanging the
W.C. Exbn — a bewildering lot of
stuff — H.P. Smith on hand, but no
Chase — little was done besides look
ing over pictures — at 12.30 went to
Putnam's — then home & after dinner
back to N.A. Chase put in an
appearance & we got started —
worked until 5.30 — then to studio
— home soon after 6, tea — eve. [illegible].
Annie & I worked until 11 o'clk,
getting cards & notices ready
for subscribers to Haden lectures
to bed at 11.30
