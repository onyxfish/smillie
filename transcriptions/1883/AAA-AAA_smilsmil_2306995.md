## Left Page

### August, Monday 6, 1883

Delightful day — very cool — I had intended to work on my etching No. 26, but had all I could do to oversee the laying of floor in main room — painting of walls of S.W. Room & filling in on carriage road — I went down town about 11 & soon back. Mr. Reed came & took Annie out for a ride — Dinner about 1.30 — Again superintending things until 5 when I went out riding with Annie until 7.30 — found it almost too cool. On our return found Mrs. Cooper & Fanny Payson — Tea at 8 — Evening, podging about & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 7, 1883

Beautiful day — Had to go down to the butcher's for my [illegible] — Had intended to "put in" a long day on my etching No. 26 — but the need of constant watch over the laying of the floor in the main Room & the painting of the walls in the bedrooms did not permit me to do more than a couple of hours of dry pointing — Dinner about 1.30 — Then W.A. Lyon called & spent some time going over the house with me — He is to write a little description of it for the Montrose Democrat. At 5 Annie & I went out & rode until 7 — Tea at 7.30 — Swell, as ever, podging about, Owing to painting wall in N.E. Room I slept in Cherry Room — To bed about 10.30.
