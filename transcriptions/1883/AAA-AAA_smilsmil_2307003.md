## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 22, 1883

Hot day — Ther. got up to 86° — Up
at 7.30 feeling as though I had
been on a spree. Part of morning
spent in carving for Trees, chaining
off dead bark — dead wood, dirt
etc, preparatory to an application
of coal tar — Did a little work on
A.B.N. Co's drawing, geysers, etc.
Dinner soon after 1 — Tried to
work but made very little progress.
At 4.30 team came &unites & H.E.
rode out on Forest lake road and
through a long piece of wood. Home
at 6.45. Rec'd a letter from father
telling us to expect them Friday
p.m. — Tea a little before 8. Evng
on piazza. Just about 11
when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 23, 1883

A hot day until soon after 3
when a violent storm of wind
& rain came up — Ther. fell from
85° to 75°. More light rain
about 9 p.m. Blks: as usual,
part of mtf. with Trees & with
carpenters — then to work on A.
B.N. Co's drawing — little progress.
Dinner at 1.30 — some more
work on drawing — At 5, the
weather had cleared so that
we went out riding until 7
o'clk — The air deliciously fra-
grant after the rain — Tea
at 7.30 — Evng reading paper
& writing to Bellows & Garner.
11 when I got to bed.
