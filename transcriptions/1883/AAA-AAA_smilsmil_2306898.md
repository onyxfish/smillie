## Left Page

### January, Monday 22, 1883

the predicted cold wave has
arrived — after writing some notes
got to N.A. soon after 10 — Chase &
Smith there — worked, mostly in
So. Room, until near 1 — then home
to dinner — after that, to Dist. Tel.
book
& then to N.A. before 3 — worked
until 5.30 — then home & had a
rest before tea at 7 — after 8
where again I got to N.A. &
worked until 10.30 — home at
11 — bitter cold — a little while
in front of a pleasant fire, then
to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 23, 1883

Exceedingly cold — after 10 where
I got to N.A. Chase not there —
Smith & I worked — at 2.20 I went
with Beckwith down to Vantine's to
look at some Jap. stuffs for covering
walls — on my way back, stopped at
studio — saw Geo for a few moments
& then home to dinner at 4.15, walking
up to 37 St. with Coleman — back to
N.A. at 2.45 & worked until 5.30 in
So. Room — Chase came in at 4 —
Home at 6 — tea about 7 — Again
to N.A. soon after 8 — all three
of the Committee on hand —
made some progress in hanging
So. Room — worked until 10.30 —
Home at 11 — & to bed soon after.
