## Left Page

### September, Monday 17. 1883.

Rain during the night — muggy & close & heavily clouded in the mntls. Bkfs. soon after 8 — made a hot fire in etching Room so as to heat up printing Tables to get drawers open that have swollen tight shut. Ther. over 100° — got them open in the afternoon — Spent the mtlf. biting 8×10 etching from Privy door. Cleared off ground about 3 o'clk. Sudden fall of Ther. from 70° to 60° between 10 & 12 a.m. Dinner soon after 1 — about 4 o'clk — felt very unwell — went to bed & slept for near an hour — got up feeling much better. Sized canvases in main hall (a part of it). Herrick finished seating bay windo & Cottrell finished hanging big kitchen dors. Glorious sunset — Tea at 6.30 — my wrote notes to Koehler, Newman & Grady & McKeever, at 9 o'clk. went to P.O. wrote letters. Then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 18. 1883.

Brilliant day — as soon as I could get ready after bkfs., which was not until after 10 o'clk. I took water color trucks & went out to a hillside continue s'ight of the house, & commenced a study — worked until near 4 o'clk. when Annie came for me — then home to dinner. After dinner, worked awhile on the study of the mntlf. — then laid a rebiting ground on a portion of the "privy" plates — at 4.45 a. & I went out riding — back at 6.35 — tea after 7 — Evmtlf. for a time on piazza, then to work on plans for north side of main hall — worked until near 11 — then to bed.
