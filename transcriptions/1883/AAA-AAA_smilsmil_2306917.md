## Left Page

### March, Thursday 1. 1883.

Delightful day, altho' at times a little raw & windy. Got to studio about 10 o'clk. having been delayed at home by some little matters. Painted on foreground of Cathedral Rocks. Will came with a subject for a drawing that he wants right away. Home to dinner soon after 1. Matt & Kate ret. to Po'k. by afternoon train. To studio at 3. Made Tracing of Telephone subject for A.B.N. Co. Annie came at 4.30 & we went to A.B.C.A. Reception, Studios there until near 6. Then to Aunt Anna's in 17th S. There to tea — Will & Fanny K. there. To Singing club, then to Canteing — there until 10.15 — then for a. — then home & to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 2. 1883.

mied & overcast with light rain — clearing, bright & warm before 1. Got to studio at 9.45 & to work on "Telephone Switch Board" for A.B.N. Co. frames for biel paintings came. The one for Cathedral Rocks, too small — worked until near 1 — then home to dinner. Back soon after 3. Found Dennis O'Donnel waiting. Painted a rough sketch from him in spite of callers. Koehler came in — also Ferru. to talk of the nomination for the Presidency of the W.C.Socy. Home soon after 6. Anthropological Tea party — Emma — Grace — Mary Willard. Helen Butler. Fanny [illegible]. M. Chas. Collins, Geo. J. & Jut. Valentina. Later in the evng., Woeter & wife and Edw'd C. came in. Every [illegible] passed pleasantly — company left before 11 o'clk. To bed before 12.

Gifford's little drawing was sent to the house in the afternoon.
