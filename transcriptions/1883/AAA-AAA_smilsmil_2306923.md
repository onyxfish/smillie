## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 13, 1883

Overcast with, but clearing to be a charming day — Afterblp — wrote notes & did not get to studio until 10 — Before I got to work T.H.S with came in & discussed Water-Color Soc't matters with Gel. & self for most of the wf. Then Mr. Osgood came with a proposition concerning the selling out of proof sets of Poets & Etchers — Sam Coleman called while I was in Calverley's Room with Annie — on our way home abt — met Mrs. Helena Brown & took her to dinner with us — Back at 3 and did a little work on tracing for etching of Street in Fontarabia — wrote a note to Fenn — Abt 5 — to Dickel's & had a horse back ride — home & tea soon after 7 — eveg quietly reading & writing. To bed abt 11.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 14, 1883

Charming, mild, Spring-like day — To studio before 10 — worked during the wf on 6¼×15 Sketch "on Marblehead Neck" — finishing it — Home to dinner abt — back before 3 — finished sketch and did some work on Colpkin plates to get them ready for Scribner's etchings when Fenn & then Ehninger came in, taking the remainder of p.m. — Trouble is brewing for the W.E. Election nyt Wr? — night. At 6 I went up to 57'8'. to see Gifford about it — not at home — wrote him a note — Home to tea at 7.30 — Eveg. writing until near 11 — then to bed.
