## Left Page

### March, Saturday 17. 1883.

Bright day. Got to studio at 10 after having called on Feun at his studio — wrote to Farner & Stuart & will & 16 or 18 Postal card invitations to Water Color men to meet in caucus at my studio Monday at 5 p.m. Then made reduction & tracing of Basque ox cart with team & driver — successfully transformed that & Grand Rue — Port. arabia to copper — Home to dinner at 1 — To N. A. at 3 & worked as long as I could see on sketch after Donoho for [illegible]. Home at 6.30 — much used up by headache — lounged until 11 — there to bed —

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 18. 1883.

Bright, mild, spring-like day. In bed with head ache until 2 o'clk — then up & had dinner — Emma J— with us — Spent much of afternoon on lounge — at 5.30 — out & walked for an hour — Tea at 7 — Evening wrote notes to Munroe & Meinden to Osgood & Co — Condit & Cottrell & father — after 11 when I got to bed.
