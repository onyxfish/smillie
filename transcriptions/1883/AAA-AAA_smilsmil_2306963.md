## Left Page

### June, Sunday 3, 1883

overcast, muggy & raw — B[illegible] —
before 9 — felt like the day —
Rather overcast — Lounged about
until near 1 o'clk — then to
studio & back to dinner at 2 —
After that a long nap until
after 5 — Tea at 7 — then
out & sauntered about — went
to Dr. Nesk's, to make sure that
he would be at home — not
in — Home soon after 8 —
Evef. wrote letter home — at
10 Took [illegible] to G. C. defeat.
— back & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 4, 1883

muggy, overcast mtl. clearing to
a very hot afternoon & eve'g. Got to
studio about 10 — wrote letter to Cotrill.
Then etched another stage on Home corner
& bit true — then to D.A. & W. E[illegible]. with
set of plans for Cotrill — then to Bank &
deposited Annie's check — Home to dinner
at 1.15 — Annie had been out all the
mtl., made selection of wedding present
for Miss Canning — At 3 I went to South
ferry & got a boatman to take me over
to Governor's Island where I made a
sketch of N.Y. & bridge for Harkers — Back
before 5 & went to Walter's office — not in
— got some figures that I was after, then
to Boyles, in Fulton St — & bought 2¾ yds
of duck for Montrose — then home by
Mad. ave. stage at 6.30 — Tea at 7 —
Evef. wrote another letter to Cotrill
& one to Mr. Condit — To bed soon
after 10.
