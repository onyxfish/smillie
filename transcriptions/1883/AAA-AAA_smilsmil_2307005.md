## Left Page

### August, Sunday 26, 1883

Dubiously overcast in the m'g.
but before 3 p.m. cleared charmingly.
went to Presb'n Ch. with father
& heard Mr. Jessups — a strictly
dogmatic sermon on the love of
Jesus Christ for sinners — a nap
in hammock before dinner at 2
— the afternoon dawdled away
until about 5, when with Annie,
Elig, & father we walked out by
the quarry, around the hill, fold
& home. Tea after 7 — Were
bright fire & play with the baby
— some writing — after 9, went
with father to the P.O. — wind
blowing from N.W. very cold.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 27, 1883

Brilliant day — Ther. down last night
to 48°. & did not get higher than
69° on the north side of the house.
most of the m'g scurrying about
attending to many things and did
a little work on A.B, N. Co. drawing.
Dinner soon after 1. Worked on
drawing until it was done but
could not pack it for ship. to N.Y.
before 4.30 when I went out with
father, mother & Elig'a for a short
ride around the lake and about
town — came home with mother,
to take out Annie, when Mr. Sellitts
& Mrs. Gould called — after a little
delay, Annie joined us & we rode
until near 7. Tea at 7.30 —
were in general, family chat —
some piazza promenading and
to bed before 11.
