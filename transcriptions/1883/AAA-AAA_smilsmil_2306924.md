## Left Page

### March, Thursday 15. 1883.

Pleasant but cold & windy — To studio at 9.30 & spent the m'f. pursuing over sky & sea on 12×16 — "Near Crowninshield's Pt." Home to dinner soon after 1 having first gone to Fenris. 16 W. 23°. S° & talked for the W. C. matters — Back to studio at 3 & worked on sky awhile — then took hold of some copper plates & papered them & laid grounds upon them — after 5 Van Cleef came in & at 5.30 I walked up town with him. Home at 6.20. Fanny K. came & we had tea — at 8 — to singing club in 14th S° — then to Century & met W. H. Hart, Bristol, Perry, & J. W. Wood in consultation about a proposed Botany Exp'tn Co. at 10, went for Annie — home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 16. 1883.

Pleasant day — After'n'k'p° went to Warren, Fuller & Co. paper hangers in 45°. S° — to have their bill explained — then to studio — varnished plates — made pen sketch after Marblehead neck 6½×15½ — to aid me in etching it. Home at 1 — Rosalie Butler there to dinner — back to studio before 3 — a little painting, then to N.A. & spent the rest of the P.m. making a sketch after Innis for Kurtz's Academy notes. Then a walk & home at 6.40 — tea at 7 — Even'g. divided between reading paper, writing and listening to piano — to bed about 11.
