## Left Page

### December, Saturday 8, 1883

Miserable wet day — To studio at about 9.30 — mounted some b[illegible]
there to work Proving No. 30 —
Home at 1 — back at 2.30 — very
dark — as I worked deli[illegible] with
& with half a head ache I suc-
ceeded in Faling but 4 [illegible]
from No. 30 — three of them on full
sheets of Imperial Jap. paper
making very impressive looking
works. Home soon after 6 — Tea
before 7 — although suffering from
headache I scanned Moscheles
Biography by his wife — a com[illegible]
preparation to calling on Mr. Moschel[illegible]
who is, I understand, the son of
his father — To bed at 10 & passed
a restless night.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 9, 1883

Raw & blustering m[illegible]. clearing to be
a fine day. In bed with headache
until noon — then up & had a cup
of tea — Charl came to tell me that
father would come to town next
tues. to consult Dr. Knapp about his
ear Trouble. — Dinner at two. Mother
cook — Nelie — Qe & Valentine Took
dinner with us — I spent the
afternoon quietly as I did not
feel well — Walter Cook & Walter
Wright came at about 5.30 — for a
short visit. Tea at about 7 — Swif[t]
wrote letter to father & after 9 went
to J.C. Delpat[illegible] with it & after that
took a short walk — Lay on lounge
until 12 o'clk. Then to bed.
