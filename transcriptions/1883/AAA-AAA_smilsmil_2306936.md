## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 10. 1883.

Mild, pleasant day — grey in the
mf., clear in the afternoon.
Got into very groggy — Got to
studio about 9.30 but did not
feel equal to my work — Spent
most of the mf. stretching &
paper etching &c. Wm. Wilcox
& Rice called. — Home to dinner
at 1.30 — Matt & Kate there. After
dinner, on lounge until after 3 —
then to studio & worked on W.C.
Marblehead Neck Causeway —
— general finishing — Home at
6.30. Even'g spent quietly.
Resting. Matt & Kate Returned
home at 4.30.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 11. 1883.

Raw, wet day without rain —
After 9 p. went to Grady, T.M. & ever's
& arranged to have remaining auto.
p. sets of Posts & Etches mounted —
To studio before 10 — Worked awhile
on Causeway & Marblehead Neck — then
on Crisp October — At 2.30, Wm. Philbat
made me a visit — Home to dinner
at 1.30 — back at 3.15 & worked till
near 6 on Crisp October — bettering
it very much in sky & dis tance
but losing freshness in foreground.
Wrote a note to F.H. Smith on Philbat
business & left it at his house
on my way home — Stopped at
Walters. Home before 7 — tea — then
wrote to Osgoods & to Anthony — Took
letters to G.C. Depot before 10 —
Annie read aloud awhile some
of Browning's Poems — To bed
at 11.
