## Left Page

### September, Friday 7, 1883

Mild & hazy Sept. day — Spent
most of the mf. fixing canvas
on the walls of main hall &
stairway — Down-Town between
10.30 & 12 attending to various
matters — Dinner about 1 —
again at work in main hall
until 4.30 — Then went out
with Annie. (Fanny & Phaeton)
& rode until 6.45 — a few
drops of rain fell — Tea
at 7.30 — Evef. read Baker
& wrote to Belle [Tuesday] —
Kimmel & Voigt & Newman.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 8, 1883

Overcast mf. Rain began to
fall about 3 — heavy rain & wind
at 6 — clear, cold night — Ther. down
to 39°. As soon as I could after
[ble?] I went to Privy & made, upon
tracing paper, a sketch of the view
from the door, looking under the
Beeches to the Havelocks beyond —
then to studio & transferred it to
a copper plate. Dinner soon after
1 & after that took my plate down
to Privy & etched on it until 3 oc
when it rained & got so dark
that I gave it up — About 4.30
went down Town — back at 6 —
Tea before 7 — Evef. a little
reading aloud & to bed at 10.30
