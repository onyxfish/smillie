## Left Page

### December, Sunday 16, 1883

Routed out before 7 in the m[orning].
Had b[reakfast] in bed & did not
get out until about 10.30 —
Day, cold & overcast — did
not go out but lay on lounge
most of the time until dinner
at 2. — After that again on
lounge. — Tea before 7 —
wrote letter to Farren &
took it to depot before 10.
also notes to Cottrell, Paym[?]
Giles Watrous & Clinton
Ogilvrie — To bed soon after
11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 17, 1883

Pleasant day — got to studio at
9.45 — laid ground on plate 3¼
×5¼ — made reduction of etching
No 30 & transformed it & commenced
etching for Etching Club Cat. Home
at 1.15 — dinner — back at 2.30
finished etching — Before 6
went to Huntington's & saw him
about Century Nominating business
— home & had tea before 7 —
at Century at 8 — Nominating
Committee consisting of Messrs
Weeks — Otis — Van Antmelinge —
Pinchot & myself organized
at 8.30 — I was made Secy.
General deliberation until
10.30 when we adjourned
until next Sat. evng. Trouble
already arising from conflict
between Board of management
& House Com.     walked home
& to bed at 11.
