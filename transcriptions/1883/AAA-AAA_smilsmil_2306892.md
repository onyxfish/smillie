## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 10, 1883.

Snow storm all day — cold. with
wind — Got to studio about 10. Rec'd
a letter from Mr. Fred. H. Betts & wrote
an answer — Pasted on mounting
board for "Causeway" — Waller came
up with a plate that aroused it-
bite — I started him at my desk
but the plate bit only in parts —
Home to dinner at 1.20 — back at 3.15
Made pen & ink sketch of Causeway
for W.C. Cat. Charl came in at about
5 o'clk. Home at 6.15 — Tea — Rueff
wrote notes to Nicole Jeffford
Drexel & Cottrell — To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 11, 1883.

Disagreeable day — very
raw & cold & I feeling out
of sorts — Nearly 10 when I got
to studio — Mr Warren came
in to see about getting his wife's
work into W.C. exhbn. I mounted
Causeway drawing — went
over to Putnam's about Haden
printing & home to dinner at
1 — back to studio at 3 — found
enough to do in mounting drawings
& transferring to a copper plate
a Tracing of Causeway that I
have hoped to etch — Went
to Kappel's before 6 — Then to
Fanny K's — Annie, Summa & Grace
there to tea — Then to singing club
— Then I went to Century — There
until 10.20 — most of time hunting
names & addresses in Directory
— Then again to club for Annie
— after 11 when we got home
& to bed very cold.
