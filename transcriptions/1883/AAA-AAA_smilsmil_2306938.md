## Left Page

### April, Saturday 14, 1883.

Brilliant + charming spring day —
got to studio at 9.45 + had lots
of little things to do — also mounted
3 ph. of Cedars by Moonlight, old cedars
\+ Lady of Cairo, visiting — about 1.40
Rose down to Scribner's — saw Mr.
Marvin + gave him ph. ph. of Basque
Ox cart + Grand Rue, Fontarabie — The
Scribner's had sent me a copy of D.
Marvin Vincent's book "from Basque
land to Carcassonne". Home at 2.30.
Took my 3 ph. around + showed them to
Mary Sprague Cook who selected "Cedars
by Moonlight" for a wedding present to her
Dr. Carl— from Mother Cook. Dinner at 1 —
Miss Voorhes there — Then A. + I went
to Academy of Music + heard Gounod's
Faust very well given — Albani as
Marguerite Galassi as Valentine. Dum[b]
as Melposta[phales], Scalchi as Sieble. [illegible]
At 5.30 to see Mr. Philpot — notion, then
to Avery's — saw his pictures — A. home, + I
to Studio — Home at 7 — tea — Jas. Wright
there. Wm Justice + wife sailed for Spanish
Trip this m[orning]. Eve[nin]g doing nothing —

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 15, 1883.

Wind from the east, overcast m[ornin]g,
\+ eve[nin]g — clear during the middle
of the day. Bkf[as]t after 9 — I stayed
in until about 12.45 — Annie went
to hear Mr. Calthorpe at All Souls church.
When I went out I met her + Emma —
Took Emma to a 3[rd] ave. car — + then
wandered about for an hour, then
home to dinner — Jas. Wright — Mr.
Bache + Edwd's Catharine with us —
afternoon had an extended confab
with Walter about Montrose plans
out + wandered again from 6 to 7 —
Then in + had Tea — Eve[nin]g, wrote
letter to Father + a note to Mr. Philpot
— mailed them after 10 — To bed
about 11.30.
