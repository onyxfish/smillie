## Left Page

### December, Friday 14, 1883.

Mild & wet — got to Studio about 9.30 & to work printing over which I am wasting much time — Printed a few proofs of No 29 — without getting just what I wanted — Home at dinner — & at 2 with Annie to Phil. Rehearsal — After that we went to Johnston & Faulkner & spent an hour looking at curtain stuffs for Reception room — Home soon after 6 — Tea before 7 — About 8.30 I got to Nicole's Studio where the Etching club had a regular meeting — Home about 11 and right to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 15. 1883.

Bright, windy & very cold day — After help rode down to Johnston & Faulkner's in 17th St. — & spent some time looking at the curtain goods that A. & I looked at yesterday p.m. & made final selections. Then to Duncalps — Putnam's and Sterne's — then to Studio soon after 11 — a little work with some etching p.f. at 12.30 — went to Butchie's & then to funeral of Uncle A. V. V. at the house in 5th Ave. Hebaroth made the remarks — met Annie then — home to dinner at 2 — To Studio at 3.30 — & worked mounting pt. until after 5 — Home to tea at 6.30 Evell. went to 2d Phil. concert — Home at 10.30 & to bed at 11.
