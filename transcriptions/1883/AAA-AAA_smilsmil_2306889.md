## Left Page

### January, Thursday 4, 1883.

Cold, raw & overcast — got to
studio at 10 & to work on Causeway — working over water and
foreground until 12 — then to
Putnam's & made selections
of cards & note papers for the
Hadeu printing. Home to dinner
at 1 — Kate & Matt down from
Po'p. — Dr. Smitts not able to keep
his appointments — they dined with
us & went home at 4. at 3 —
back to studio with Mr. Justice —
& talked for awhile about Etching
— Miss Durfee & Mr. Dr. Bond called
& a Newspaper interviewer on the
subject of Etching — also Dr. Yale.
I had just ½ an hour to myself —
At 5 went to 6th Ave. & 23rd St. to see some
Files — then home — tea at 7 — at
8, W. & Mrs. Justice went to theatre &
Annie & I to singing club — then I went
to Century — wrote — had a chat with
Swain & Gifford & Perry — at 10.15 went for
Annie, Will K. sick — took Fanny home
to 22nd St. — then to 38th St. — chatted by the
fire & to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 5, 1883.

Snow all day — very light & fine
with cold wind — to studio at 10
& spent the m'g. working over
Causeway foreground. — at 12.30
went to Bank & made deposit
for Annie — Home to dinner at 1.
at 2 — to Phil. Rehearsal — very
cold & uncomfortable & not much
interested in the music — out at
4.15 — a chat with Drexel. then
to studio — there until near 6 —
then home — Tea before 7 — after
that, chatting with Mr. J. — until
8.15 — then to work writing. to
bed at 10.30.

Wrote to Peter Codper & J. W. Drexel
on the subject of putting up a
new studio bldg.
