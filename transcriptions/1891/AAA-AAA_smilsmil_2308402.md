## Left Page

### April, Thursday 2. 1891.

Dull day — Rain commenced about 4
p.m. & fell steadily & heavily during the
eve'g. Got to George's about 9.30. He
was much disturbed last night by a
cough — this m'g he was very weak and
sore — Got to studio before 10 & after a
little went to N.A. f.D. Varnishing Day.
my 3 small pictures are all hung in
good places — their appearance does
not make me happy. At 11.30 went to
2°. Nat. Bk & attended to some of Annie's
business — Men again to Geo's — & home
to lunch at 1 — Annie & Kate lunched
at Mary's — I got to studio before 3 but
my headache prevented my working.
Miss [illegible] Oakford called — & so did
Saml. Colman, bringing me one of his
studies from nature painted about 25
years ago — Men Charl came in and he
liked his picture. At 5.30 I went again
to George's — It was nearly 6.30 when I
got home — dinner — went — Annie
went to her Choral Club Rehearsal —
I lounged in front of the fire — then
arranging orders for garden seeds
until 11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 3. 1891.

Some rain before 9 o'clk — cloudy day after
that. I got to George's about 9.30 & found him
somewhat better. To studio before 10 & then
to work on x a study that I painted
out of doors, on a little brook on Josh. Petties's
farm, Montrose — Oct. '89. After an hours
work I finished it, making something
so direct & so complete that I was quite
pleased & determined to send it to the
exhibn of Socy. of Am. Artists. Home to lunch
abt — Annie & Kate at woman's Unitarian League
— Brooklyn — then to Fanny K's. To studio at
2.30 — Colman waiting for me — Painted
on small pictures "A back lot", correcting
crude color — then worked on "near Crown-
inshield's Point" — cleaning up in color.
Washed brushes before 5 — went over to
see Ge° — & found him much brighter —
Home at 6. Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g I
wrote to Herrick & a note to Galt. I
read a little & to bed at 10.30.
