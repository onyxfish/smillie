## Left Page

### July, Monday 13, 1891

A cloudless, hot day with strong S. easterly breeze blowing. As usual
it was 11 before I got to work upon
jade plate — worked until dinner
at 1 & again in the afternoon
from 3 to 5. The men cut the grass
for hay, of which we will have a
good crop. Tea before 6.30 and
at 7 Mrs. Satchell, her daughter,
Miss 7., Ralph & I went out for a
ride — out for little more than
an hour — very pleasant. While
we were out Miss Foshay came
to the house with Mr. & Mrs. Wright
of Cin, now boarding at the Sayre house.
After watering my plants I sat
with the others upon piazza until
after 10.

3½ h. on jade plate.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 14, 1891

Another hot day with some clouds that
gave promise of thunder showers that did
not come. Pleasant breeze all day & even.
After b'kf't. in garden — big flat stones moved
up to barn — At 10 went down town with
some lettuce for A. B. Burns but he was out
of town so I took it to Florence Lathrop,
& gave her a lesson in tying Cos to blanch
it. At Wm. Jessup's stopped & had a
half hours chat with the girls on the
piazza. It was 12 before I got to
work. Dinner — loafed & worked
through the afternoon — got jade
plate so far advanced that I hope
to take an imp. to-morrow. At
7 we went out riding & had a very
pleasant time getting back at 8.45
then, meet 10 on piazza —

3½ h. on jade plate.
