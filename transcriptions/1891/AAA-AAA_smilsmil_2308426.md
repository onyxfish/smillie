## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 20, 1891.

Pleasant day - with a gentle &
acceptable shower in the after-
noon between 4 & 5 o'clk. Got
up & went down to breakfast -
Spent the rest of the day in the
dining room. Dr. Yale was with
me a good part of the m'f. - He
evidently does not regard my
case as serious as I get nothing
professional out of him. I wrote
to Herrick sending him checks.
Eve'g. Geo. & Charl came - and
they, with Kate, went to Charl's
room - Geo. left our care this
m'f to stay at Dr. Swift's where
Sheldon is. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 21, 1891.

Rather close & muggy & at times clouded.
Got to studio after 10 & spent the m'f
putting things away in summer order.
At 11.30 went to J.H. Mills' place in E.
23d. St. & got check for $112.50 on Indian-
apolis Bank for sale of painting "A back
lot." Then to studio, packed etching
p.p. for Miss Morley & took them to Adams
Exp. & to 2d. Nat. Bk. & deposited check.
Home to lunch - After that to Exh'n
of Socy of Am. Artists & spent an hour or two
carefully looking over the pictures -
Then to studio & continued my work —
getting things into order for leaving -
for some time experimenting with
mezzotint - rockers - grinding, etc. -
Home at 6.30 - Eve'g. Mr. Cleur. Wood in
called for half an hour - then Charl
came in & took Kate to his room -
After g Summa & Mary Ireband came
in & stayed until 10. Then to bed.
