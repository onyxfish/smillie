## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 7, 1891.

*Mr. Satchell & daughter arr.*

Rain began early in the m'f & very gradually increased until during the latter part of the afternoon it was a down pour. I was out transplanting lettuce & such like work until after 11 — then to stocking room & worked on jade plate until 1 — Ralph has been quite out of sorts — apparently some digestive disturbance & slept a good part of the m'f. — Later, in the afternoon, he revived & seemed quite himself. Miss Joshay left us in a heavy shower to go to Chas. Sayre's where she will board for awhile — I worked on my plate until after 4 — about 5 Herrick got away for Montrose Station — and I went down town for the mail — Annie & Eliz. went to the Kaffe Klatsch at Chs. Sayre's & I presided at home — It was not long after 8.30 when H. returned with Mrs. Satchell & her daughter. Annie came in a few minutes later. After the new comers had taken a light meal they went to bed — To bed soon after 10.

3 h. on jade plates.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 8. 1891.

*Miss Frueauff arrived.*

A cold, cloudy day with strong wind from N.W. dates breakfast — after that sat with Ralph for a time — In the m'f. he did not feel much like celebrating his birthday, but before night he seemed again quite himself. I was out of doors doing garden work from 10 to 12 — then worked for an hour on jade plate. Dinner — worked another hour on jade plate — About 5 we went out with the carriage & drove about until after 5.30 before the trains on the little R.R. came in — Miss F. on the train — we drove through town to P.O. & then home — Eve'f — nothing of interest.

2 h. on jade plates.
