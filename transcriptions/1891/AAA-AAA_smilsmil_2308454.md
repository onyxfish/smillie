## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 15. 1891.

After a windy night a clouded morning
and a few drops of rain — gradually
clearing until the afternoon when the
last vestige of a storm disappeared —
After 9 P.M. clouded again with a few
drops of rain. After my out of door
work I set to work preparing to take
prints from jade plate. It was 1
o'clk. before I got press prepared —
oil stone cleaned & trimmed. Ink
ground & paper wet — Dinner —
After that I took two prints — in
some respects they were not so bad
as I had feared, & altogether I
was much encouraged. Tea
at 6.30 — & soon after Annie,
Edith S—, Ralph & I went out for
a ride — in at 8.15. Then, watered
I watered planes — then sat upon
piazza & rested until 10 — then
to bed.

3 h. on jade plate.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 16. 1891.

Very cool, clouded m'f — delightful aft-
noon & cloudless eve'f & night — Annie
got up a little excursion & at 10 o'clk.
Derrick drove down to a small waterfall
on Millydenburg — about 1½ miles from the
house, taking all hands & Cynthia Richards
& Steve Garst. H— Rel? before noon — Mrs.
Christman, with her bro-in-law & his wife —
called & I showed them about. I was
able to do about an hours work on jade
plate before 12.45 Then I drove down
for the party & brought them home. It was
nearly 2 when we had dinner — I
worked on jade plate another hour &
spent some time directing the laying of
stone floor in cow stable. After
watering plants in the eve'f I read
news paper for awhile & got to bed
at 10 o'clk.

2 h. on jade plate.
