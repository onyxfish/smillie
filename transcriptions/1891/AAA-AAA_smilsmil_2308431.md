## Left Page

### May, Saturday 30. 1891.

*Decoration Day.*

muggy, overcast & threatening morning
clearing very warm by noon. I got to
studio at 9.30 & to work packing big box.
Home to lunch soon after 1 — after that
went to Shirlaw's, only to find that
he had done nothing in the way of
making an outline, carefully stud-
ied, of figure "Reflection". I was much
disappointed & took large photo. to studio
he promising soon to make the study & send
it to me at Montrose with his painting.
Back to Studio before 4 & continued
work on big box. Home before 6.30
dinner — W. & f. Annie & I went
to Walter's to question him about pro-
posed improvements in Reception
room — Home about 9.30 — Read awhile
& to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 31. 1891.

Very warm & pleasant — B[illegible] — about
9 o'clk. The m[illegible] spent in doing
many little things in the way of pre-
paration for our Montrose flitting.
Dinner at 2 — Walter came in
& Annie & I talked with him about
desirable changes to be made in
the Reception Room — changes that
Annie thinks it better should be
made at the same time that the work
is done in the hall — It was after 5
when Annie, Ralph & I went down to
Aunt Anna's to say our "good bye" —
saw also Emma, Mary & Grace — we
left there at about 6.30 — Annie and
Ralph rode home, I walked. Found
Charl awaiting us on our arrival —
He left as we sat down to tea at
about 7 o'clk.
