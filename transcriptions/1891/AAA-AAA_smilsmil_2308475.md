## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 26. 1891.

*Kate, Katie & Bessie arr.*

A pleasant day' tho much of the
time overcast. I got to work in
studio on jade plate & finished
rocking second megatint ground
& commenced scraping margin —
worked for some time putting tints
from some wild white strawberries.
After dinner I again worked on
jade plate — tea at 6 o'clk & at
6.20 Jim & I started on the new
R.R. to go down to D.L.&W. junction
to meet Kate & the girls. I was
much pleased with the ride —
it is really interesting — down in 40
ms. waited 10 — train on time — girls
in it. Home at 7.55 — no trunks —
wrote a note to Jackson's Exp. N.Y.
& took it to P.O. To bed at 10.30.

3 h on Jade Plate

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 27. 1891.

Tremendous rainfall during most
of the night and all the m'g. Delightful
afternoon altho' very warm & humid. I
got through with my out of door work as
soon as possible — then to work on jade
plate, scraping away megatint ground
outside of limits of vase. Dinner abt
Before 3 o'clk went down town — met
W.D. Luck & signed a note (4 mos. from
Sep. 1st) for $500 for 10 shares of the Monroe
& Lackawanna R.R. Spent more than
an hour running about on Village
Improvement business — home at 4.30
& then went out riding, taking Annie,
Kate, Bessie & stopsking & Park's for Mattie.
Home at 6.30 — tea — Eve'g, Mr. & Mrs.
Edgar called — after they left I wrote
some N.J. matter for the "Republican" —
To bed soon after 10.
Kate's trunk arr.

3 h on Jade Plate.
