## Left Page

### March, Friday 27, 1891

Bleak, raw - windy & overcast - got
to studio about 10 & soon to work upon
Charl's painting - At first it was a
good deal of a struggle - I did not
feel equal to the work - nothing went
right & I was much discouraged
but before I went home to lunch I
got into a better frame - To lunch at 1.30
Rested after lunch & did not get to studio
until 3.15 - then painted about an hour
& a half when I got to a good stopping
place - went to Knoedler's & got some
brushes - Home at 6 - dinner at 6.30
Evelf Annie & Kate went to a neighboring church to hear some Easter Music
I remained at home & studied
over seedsmen's catalogues until
A. & K. came in with Charl - It
was after 10.30 - when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 28, 1891

Dull, unpleasant day - clearing before
evelf. Got to studio at 9.30 - Jim went
down with me - Annie & Ralph followed soon
after & Champney got another sitting from R. -
Uncle Willie & Aunt Agnes came in for awhile -
In spite of interruptions I made good progress with Charl's painting - Home to lunch
at 1.30 - at 2 went to 192 Broadway & saw
Mr. Drummond & W. Peake - they tell me that
they are ready to begin upon the Montrose
Water Works at once. To studio before 4 and
painted until after 5 - washed brushes -
Then Mlles Diana & Heva Coomans called
- At 6 I went to Geo's lodgings to see
him - he has been in bed all day with
some stomach trouble - severe pain - Home
at 6.30 - dinner - Evelf, some reading,
then another pull at my seed catalogues
- To bed at 10.30.
