## Left Page

### July, Thursday 9, 1891

*Walter's gives rel? to N.Y.*

Beautiful day with charming cloud effects. Annie got up before 6 o'clk & took charge of the gives & went with them to Montrose Depot & put them on the 8.22 Train. I did not get up until 7.30. Bkfst with other members of the family — Annie ret'd at about 10, having had a very pleasant ride — I went down to A.B. Burns & gave him to Annie heads & lettuce. Got to my work on Jade plate at 11 & worked for an hour and a half. Trimmed vines until dinner at 1 — Afternoon, not feeling comfortable about the head, I did not work. At 4 o'clk. an alarm of fire from the Harrington's Mills — the Roof took fire from sparks — much excitement — after a good fight the fire was put out without extending beyond the Roof. I ret'd home soon after 5 — after tea I went down town for mail — Eve'g. Annie read aloud to us for awhile — The Choral darkies came up & gave us a serenade.

1½ h. on Jade plate.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 10, 1891

Very pleasant day — out in garden & directing men until after 11 — then to my work & got in about 1½ hours of work — dinner — after that a little more work and at 4.30 Miss J—, Mr. S & daughter Edith, Ralph & I Rode — home a few m. after 6 — Early eve'g I watered many plants, working until nearly 9 o'clk — then on piazza, rolled in a blanket & enjoying moonlight until 10 — then to bed.

2 h. on Jade plate.
