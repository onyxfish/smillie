## Left Page

### December, Saturday 12. 1891

Still beautiful weather — I feel rather seedy, naturally,
after yesterday's experience. Went to Warren, Fuller & Lange's & gave
order for Wall paper — then to [illegible] & to studio — did some etching —
Rey plate & got it ready for the acid —
about 12 went to Thos. Moran's studio in 22? S; interviewed him concerning his resignation from Etching
Club. Then to Vautrine's & got some silk for front door — Home to lunch
at 2 — At 3 Annie walked part way
to studio with me — I got Rey plate
into acid once — after 4 walked to
Washington Sq. & left Shurtlew's painting
"Reflection" at his studio — then home —
Dinner at 6. Eve'g Annie, Kate
& I went to Phil. concert, a Mozart
memorial — we thought it rather
dull.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 13. 1891

Delightful day — the parlor having
a carpet down I kindled fire in the
parlor & enjoyed myself after breakfast — Dr. Yace came in about 11 —
at 12.30 I went to Union League by
appointment & met Charl. & George,
we spent some time with the pictures —
then to C's Room — & after that I
went with him to his "dark room"
& got his small camera & plate holders.
I wish to take a number of views of
the Mad. Sq. garden winter — Home to
dinner at 2 — with the children
until near 4 — then out for a walk
with Jim & Raelon — back in an
hour — Tea — Eve'g Annie went
with Kate to Church. I stayed
at home — Read some — wrote a
note or two.
