## Left Page

### February, Thursday 5, 1891

A brilliant winter day, cloudless & cold —
Got to studio soon after 9 & to work
upon tracing in gelatine of the Jade
vase. Düring came in & made a call.
At 12 I went to Safe Deposit — 2° Nat.
Bnk. then walked up Bdway to 47
S: getting to D. H.D. Acren's No. 155 W.
at 1 o'clk — he got an impression of my
"bite" & I got home to lunch at 1.30 —
back to studio at 3 — scraped my
gelatine tracings & worked until
near 5 — then went to Klackner's
with photo. of Lathrop. — then to Keppel's —
then to N.A. where I saw C. T. Reinhart
for a few minutes — home at 6 — Annie's
2° Reception afternoon — She & Kate had
had a very pleasant time, friends calling
in numbers sufficient to keep them
constantly busy — Dinner at near 7 —
Eve'g A. went to her Choral Club &
I sat & read until about 10 — then
to bed —

## Right Page

### February, Friday 6, 1891

Rain in the m'g, clearing & warm
in the afternoon. Got to studio at 10.15
& spent the rest of the m'g finishing
gelatine tracings, scraping etc. — and
laying grounds upon the Bishops &
Morgan plates — about 1 went to 2°
Nat. Bnk. then home to lunch.
Annie at Unitarian League, Brooklyn.
Back to studio at 2.30 & after much
careful preparation I got a success-
ful transfer of the Jade Tracing onto
the Bishops plate. Then wrote some
notes — went to Geo's room for awhile
& at 5 o'clk to N.A. of D — met Platt there
then to Butter's & from there home.
Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g. Annie
spent most of it at the piano for
Kate's amusement. I wrote letter
to Herrick & mailed it at 9.30
