## Left Page

### February, Sunday 1. 1891.

In bed all day with a severe headache. Got up & dressed at 6 P.m. —
Tea at 6.30 — At 7, Annie not having
come in I went to Ed's to enquire
of Mary from whom I learned that
she had gone to Mrs. Runkle's. While
there Annie came after me & we
then returned home. Every
[illegible] came in for a while — I
read newspaper until 11.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 2. 1891.

Brilliant & beautiful day — Got up
feeling rather flimsy after yesterday's
experience — After the marketing I went
to Century Club to take a look at last
Sat's mf papers — then to Dr. Allen's,
155 W. 47th St. — made an appointment for
next Wed. Then to studio where I
spent the rest of the day clearing up
the disorder of the past few weeks. Home
at 1 o'clk. Met T.C. Farrer in the street
& he took me into the vestibule of the
Park Ave. Hotel to show to me a mezzotint
plate of his doing that he had with him.
After lunch wrote some notes so that it
was 3.45 when I got back to studio.
After 5 went to barber's & had my hair
cut, then home. Miss Williams dined
with us — At 9 o'clk. I went with
her to Walter's where, besides W. & Louie
I found Ed., Mary & Mrs. Sprague. There
for a few minutes then home — To bed
about 10.30.
