## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 25, 1891

A very pleasant day — About 10 o'clk. went
down town — first to F.W. Devoe & Co. & got
a number of Etching Tools for the Pratt
Inst. — Then to H.B.N. Co. Saw Mac-
donough — he passed me up to see
Mr. Doe from whom I bought a no. of
old meggotink rockers — Then a long
Talk with Alfred Jones. Then to Sellers
17 Dey S. & bought more Tools — Then
home at 1.30 — lunch — Before 3 to
Studio — visited awhile with Geo —
then experimented with new tools
— got 8 p[illegible] of "Sleeps" dampened & into
press. Home at about 6 — dinner —
Evef. reading until Charl came
in — He left soon after 10 & then
we got to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 26, 1891

Brilliantly beautiful day — got to studio
soon after 10 — wrote notes to Paré & Co. & to
Mr. Doe enclosing checks to each of
them — Then fussed awhile getting tools
in shape for the Pratt Ins. Then went
down town to Fulton S. to F.W. Devoe &
Co. & made some exchanges of things
left there yesterday — then to 192 Broadway.
Cor. John S. & called on Mr. Drummond
The head man in the Montrose Water works
Had a talk with him over the situation
there — nothing very encouraging. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — to studio at 3 — Packed
painting & photos. of Shirlaw's "Reflections"
& then to his studio — He agrees to let
me have the painting next summer
so that I may work from the original —
then to Bridgeman's — the florist — in 14 S.
Then to studio — on my way home left 3
copies Etching Club Cat. at Chas. A. Platt's.
Dinner at 6.30 — Evef Annie went
to her Choral Club Rehearsal — To day
she lunched at Mrs. Wm. Choate's.
Spent the evef. studying out my
flower & vegetable seed list — To bed
at 10.30
