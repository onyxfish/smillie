## Left Page

### July, Saturday 11. 1891.

A brilliant, beautiful day, both warm & cool. As usual, it was
11 o'clk. before I could get to my
work on Jade plate — this excessively
tiresome with nothing to commend
it beyond the fact that I hope to
get some money for it — worked till
dinner at 1 — After dinner, for
some time directing work in the
cow stable — then to work on plate
for about an hour — then riding —
Miss J — Annie, Edith S. Ralph
& I. Home at 6.30 — Tea at 7 —
after that, watering plants till
about 8.30 — then on piazza
enjoying the moonlight till 10 —
then to bed.

3 h. on Jade plate.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 12. 1891.

A very warm day — cloudless & quite
still. After bkfst. I spent some time
in the garden. It was almost noon
before I settled myself in studio to write
a letter to Walter Cook concerning the
drawing rec'd yesterday of plans for
alterations in Reception Room, 38th St.
Dinner at about 2 — After that, lounged
& finished letter to Walter — also,
wrote a note to S. R. Koehler. Tea
soon after 6 — before 7 Annie, misses
J. & S. Jim & I went out riding — the
sunset — the twilight merging into
moonlight & the mild air were very
delicious — we did not get home
until almost 9 — then sat on the
piazza until 10 — To bed at 10.30
