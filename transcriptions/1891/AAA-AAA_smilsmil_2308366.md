## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 20, 1891

Overcast w'f. with a feeling as if snow
in the air, but it cleared pleasantly
in the afternoon with a beautiful moon-
light night following. Got to studio at
10.30 – the m'f. was spent writing letters
to printers + other work connected with
Etching club Ex'n – also in putting
some small etching pfs. in frames for
W. C. Woodin. Home at 1 – a back.
Then made prf of pfs. Miriam's magazine,
etc. for R. Koehler + Boston Museum +
at 4.30 took it to Adams' [illegible]. Then to
Kurtz's – disgusted to find my work
there very much behind – then to h.q. +
there met Nicoll + Frank Jones – also Bacher
whom I saw when I was over for a few mo-
ments in the m'f. The hanging of etchings
is more than half done – Home before 6 –
dressed – dinner – Elig. Howe dined
with us – Ev'g Elig. Annie, Kate + I
went to Chickering's – 1st concert of
the season of the Choral Club – a very
pleasant affair. Charl joined us after
the concert – Took A. & K. home while I
escorted Elig. To bed at 1.30

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 21, 1891

Bright, clear w'f. but overcast before noon –
mild, moist + raw. Got up with a headache –
to studio at 10.30 – met Baker – ground ink
\+ took proofs from dry-point portrait sketch
of [illegible], working on the plate a little between
proofs. My headache increased so
that at last I gave up – went home
a little before 2 + went to bed where
I had some sound sleep – Up about
5.30 – went to studio, cleaned up +
cut down Baker for to-morrow. Home
at 6.30. Dinner – Ev'g, Annie
went with Sam + Mary to the first of
Mrs. Jas. Morse's receptions.
