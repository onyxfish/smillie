## Left Page

### January, Sunday 4, 1891

Overcast with light falling snow.
Did not go out of the house all day.
R&R — after 9 — Spent some time
with Annie before dinner in
going over accts of last year, es-
pecially those of Montrose — Mr.
Valentine came in just before 2 —
Then we had dinner. V. leaving
soon after — Then Charl came in
& then Geo. Cook & Thiely — so the
short afternoon soon passed. After
5 I lay down & took a short nap.
A light tea at 6.30 — Evel.
I finished reading Janvier's
Aztee Measure House & got to bed
soon after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 5, 1891

Light snow that did not amount to
much falling all day — got to studio
soon after 10 & to work on Water color
& by noon got it into better shape,
but still nothing to brag of. Soon
after 12, after a visit from two young
Japanese students, I went to the
Am. Art Ass'n. & took a last look at "The
Angelus" — also the collection of paintings
by Alex. Harrison — Dodge & Stetson —
then to Bank — then home — Annie
had gone to Brooklyn to the funeral of
Mrs. Kimball — & ret'd to lunch. Back
to studio at 2.30. D. Gerster came in
& spent the rest of the afternoon with
me. Ordered a frame for my W.C. at Butler's
on my way home — Dinner at 6.30 Evel.
Roger & his sisters went to the Opera. Charl.
came in & stayed until after 9 — I
was busy until near 11 with Etching
club publication matters.
