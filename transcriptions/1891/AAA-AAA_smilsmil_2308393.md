## Left Page

### March, Sunday 15, 1891

Pleasant day - milder than yesterday but still cold. [illegible] at 9.30 - then newspaper - Before 11 Ralph & I walked with Annie to Collyer's Church & then we walked on to 5th Ave. and up to 44th S., then to Murray Hill Hotel to see Mr. C.R. Woodin - not in - then home after 12. Dinner at 2 - At 4.30 Annie, Ralph & I went to E. 17th S. to Aunt Anna's & visited there until near 6 - then home. Turns near 7 o'clk. when Mary, Nate & Charl came in, having been to Trinity, the Battery, the Bridge & other places sight seeing. Tea. Eve'g. Reading until Charl came in - To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 16, 1891

Something of March & April combined. To studio at 10.15 & to work subpainting Chis picture 30×20 "Up a Hill, with calf & ducks". Before I got fairly to work Miss Tossing came with two plates, one bitten - a failure the other unbitten. I told her to varnish the unbitten one & we would bite it - then Dr. Gerster came to say good bye - he sails on Sat. - other calls very much interfered with the painting in of my sky - Home at 1 - back at 2.15. Miss Tossing came soon after & the plate was bitten in, very successfully - then I gave her a note to H & V. & she went to them for a proof. I worked at my painting until 4.30 then cleaned up & went to Vantine's about my leaky teakettle - then to Winder lichs' - & home soon after 6 - Dinner. A quiet eve'g in front of a pleasant fire & to bed early.

Mary went back to Po'k. this m'g at 10.30.
