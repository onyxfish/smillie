## Left Page

### September, Friday 25. 1891.

Hot with every promise of its continuing — We sat about on the piazzas & on the shady side of things as though it were midsummer. I worked for awhile on Jade plate, commencing at the top to reduce the color. Then spent the best part of an hour before dinner on the piazza with Amine & Collins — After dinner another hour was spent in like manner — Then we went out for a drive & got back at 6.5 Tea — Ev'g on Piazza, where I had hung Chinese Lanterns — this is like midsummer weather. To bed before 9 o'clk quite used up by my headache where I passed a very uncomfortable night.

Jade — 2 h.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 26. 1891.

Still very hot & dry — feeling very shaky generally & having a severe headache. Did not get up to breakfast but after 9 I got up & dressed. I wandered about until near noon — then I went down town & attended to a number of small matters getting home at a little after 1 — and once we had dinner — After that more wandering about, gathering seeds etc — It was about 3 o'clk when we started out for a ride — went to a farmers, Lillie by name, to look at some oats that he has for sale — then completed our ride, getting home a few m. after 6. Tea before 7 — Ev'g, general chatting — Later Collins & Amine played Cribbage.
