## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 30. 1891.

Cloudy & breezy — To studio at 10 +
spent the m'f. burnishing on Rey
sky — then laid another etching
ground over most of the plate.
Home to lunch & back at 2.30.
Etched in a lot more of work.
At 5 o'clk. Miss Abbatt called.
Home to dinner at 6 — Ev'g
Chas. Collins called — then
Chas. came in — To bed
at about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 31. 1891.

Pleasant day — errands of one kind
& another prevented me from getting
to the studio before 10.45 — Spent what
remained of the m'f. biting the new
work on Rey plate — cleaned it before
I went home to lunch — Mr Galbraith
came & took measurements to [illegible]
picture mouldings in library. To
studio at 2.40 — made several efforts
unsuccessfully to lay an aqua-tint
ground over portion of Rey sky — Anthony
came in with photo-gravure print of
Yale — other interruptions so that
I accomplished nothing — Home
at 6 — dinner. Ev'g — wrote for a
little while on Etching Club paper.
A'g went to Century Club to consult
some works in the library there. Home
about 11 & to bed.
