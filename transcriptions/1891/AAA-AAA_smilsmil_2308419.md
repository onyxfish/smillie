## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 6, 1891

Very cold day, bleak, cloudy & windy.
Did not get to studio until 10.30 —
Ab 11 went down to N. Y. with
my jade & Jifobpincatt plates & got
two prints of each. The Jifobpincatt
plate is a mixture of encouragement
& disappointment. To studio ab 1 &
then home to lunch. Geo. came from
Ridgefield this mf. & has taken quarters
with us — for the present. He lunched
with us. After lunch, feeling very
heavy & stupid, I took a nap. Then to
studio — from there to Bridgeman's & to send
some grass seed — Then to Denning's and
there met Annie — a little shopping
& then we went to J. D. Millett's Reception
92 Clinton Place — a crowded house. A.
& I could stay but a few minutes. I
had a talk with Lockwood De Forrest ab.
the Wadleigh memorial Tablet in Woman's
College. Then I went to Siner & Armand's
& got a large Acid bottle. Then to studio
& home at 6.30 — Dinner at 7 — Katie A —
Kate S — & Geo. S — with us — Evsf. Charl.
came in —

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 7, 1891

*Annie & the boys went to Hazelbrook*

Cool but very pleasant day — to studio
before 10 — no water running & waste pipe
stopped — my plan to begin biting the Morgan
portrait upset. A very disagreeable
pain completed the upset. It was a stom-
ach pain but not quite like the one that
attacked me so severely some weeks ago.
After a time, as it seemed to be increasing
I took a ¼ gr. Tablet of Codeine — given me by
Yale. Relief came very promptly and I
had a nap in big chair in front of fire.
Home to lunch ab 1 — Geo. with me —
At 2.30 I went with Annie & the boys to
Desbrosses St. Ferry & crossed to take 3.30
train — found that the 4 o'clk train stopped
at Queen Lane, so, we waited for that. Then
I ret. to city — to Siner & Armand's — got Muriatic
Acid, then to studio & completed a 90 x 6d.
Dutch mordant. Found the engineer & Jab.
in my room, having climbed in over transom —
water backing up through waste pipe & running
over the floor. Home, with Geo. at 6.30 —
Katie A — dined at Walter's — Dinner —
Evng., loafing — feeling generally
unhappy — To bed at 10.
