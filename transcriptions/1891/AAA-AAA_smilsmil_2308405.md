## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 8, 1891

Delightful day. Got to studio before 10
I had a number of notes to write & some
other small matters to attend to that
took some time — Geo. came in, having
had strength enough to go to the N.A.
D. [illegible] for a short time — I painted
for awhile on 20×24 "A bit of brook with some
Sheep". Before I went to 2°. Nat. Bk. and
deposited Chas's check — Home at 1.20
— lunch — to Ad. Gimed in 23° S. & saw Mr.
Mills about sending another painting to the
Indianapolis [illegible]. Then to studio before
3 — Mr. Chas. Parsons came in — & then Geo.
& they made a long visit — At last I got
to work & painted on the study of Harbor
Islands — Lake Geo." — clearing part of sky &
water & enlivening the foreground — After
5 I went to Brooks Bros & bought a suit of
clothes — back to studio about 6 — Cleaned
up & home at 6.30 — dinner. At 8.30 Annie
Kate & I went to Chas's room to see his
paintings — we spent some time looking
over his foreign photographs — Started for
home before 10.30 — then to bed —

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 9, 1891

Charming day. To studio at about 9.30 —
Painted for awhile upon Lake Geo. study —
"one of the Harbor Islands". Mr. Koehler
came in — very much under the weather.
Then Dr. Yale came in — I washed the
painting "A Pool — October" & commenced
the repainting of that — Trying to get the
red-hotness out of it — Home to lunch
at about 1.30 — back to studio at 3 and
painted until near 5 — Geo. came in
having been up to Central Park, feeling
better — I went to Eimer & Amend's and
got a bottle of Sesqui-chlorate of Iron, to ex-
periment with as a mordant — Then to
Wunderlich's & looked at some etchings —
then to studio — home at 6.15 — dinner —
Annie went to her choral club re-
hearsal & I spent the evening
writing to Herrick a long letter of
instructions concerning the garden —
To bed at 10.30.
