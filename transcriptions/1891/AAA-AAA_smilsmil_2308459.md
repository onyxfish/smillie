## Left Page

### July, Saturday 25. 1891.

A cloudy day with some sunshine & two or three showers — growing much cooler towards night — Jim was quite out of sorts — headache — coughing etc — so that at noon Annie took him to Dr. Richardson who advised Rhubarb & soda — He spent a good part of the afternoon sleeping. I was out of doors until 11 — then to studio & commenced painting "A home on a hill" — laid in sky before dinner — Painted a portion of the landscape in the afternoon — Did not ride owing to showers — After tea went down town — back at 8 — Even'g with newspaper & "Harper's" for Aug.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 26. 1891.

A delicious m'f., clear & crisp — the afternoon marred by two or three rather cold showers. Bkft. about 9 — after that, Annie, the boys & I spent most of the m'f. in the garden — It was almost dinner time before we came in — doors near 2 o'clk. when we had dinner — after that I lounged, the showers preventing a drive — I wrote a letter to Mary that I took to the P.O. about 8 o'clk. after our tea. Even'g while Annie read aloud I busied myself packing string beans in salt. To bed about 10.
