## Left Page

### May, Saturday 16, 1891

Clouded with light sprinkles of rain clearing at night. In bed all day —
headache & pains in back — effects of a general cold, affecting also nose
& throat. Got up before 6 p.m.
& went down to dinner. Evening
reading — & to bed again at 10.30

The engagement of Edith Wright to
Mr. McCarty, a lawyer of this city, is
the surprise of to-day — leaking through
two channels, one reaching Annie in
the [illegible], the other in the eve[ning], both
appearing to be very direct and
reliable.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 17, 1891

A glorious, brilliant day — very breezy
& quite cool. Up at 9 o'clk. — out very
stiff in the back & generally sore & flimsy
& spent the rest in dining room & had
some naps — then went out in the back
yard with the children & sunned
myself for some time — Dinner at 2.
After 3. Charl came in & took Annie
& Kate to the upper part of Central Park
Walter & Louise came in for a few
minutes. then I took Ralph out for
half an hours saunter in the sunny side
of Park Ave. Tea before 7 — Kate
went to church where Charl met
her & brought her home after service.
