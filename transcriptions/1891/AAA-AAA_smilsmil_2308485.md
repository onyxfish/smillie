## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 15, 1891

overcast & raw — with a little rain from S.W., then a clearing before noon.
Early in the afternoon a thunderstorm west of us passed over in a light shower.
Some more pleasant weather before sunset in the eveg. At 9 A.m.
went downtown to see what the men were doing who were hauling dirt to the front of the Court Ho. for the V.I. Soc.
To studio at 10 & to work on jade plate.
After 3 oclt. Parke came bringing with him Mrs. Sayre of So. Bethlehem
They stayed for more than an hour.
I worked until 5.30 — then went downtown. Tea at 6.30 — Eveg. at 8 went to Davd. Sayre's — meeting the Executive Com. of Village Imp.
Socy — 8 members out of 11 present —
adjourned about 9.30 — then home.
To bed soon after 10. Annie sat up until after 11 for Katie & Besse J.
who were out with Bessie Parke.

3h. on jade plate.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 16, 1891

Overcast and raw in the mg., cloudy most of the day — after 4p it cleared and the day was followed by a lovely moon-light night. — Got to work on jade plate at 10 & kept at it until 12.30.
Dinner — again in studio until 4.
Then out for a ride — left K. & B. at the J.'s to take tea there. Eveg. after my newspaper & studying some fall catalogues just Rec'd from seeds —
then I worked for awhile on plans for wood boxes. To bed soon after 10 after a walk on piazza.

4h. on jade plate.
