## Left Page

### September, Thursday 17, 1891

A beautiful day, cloudless, hazy
& very warm. I got to work on
jade plates a few minutes after 10
& stuck to it until 12.15. Then
I got things ready to go out &
paint a study this afternoon —
Dinner a little after 1 — & after
that I had Rutau put the horses
before the wagon & take me out to
a place about 2 miles from home
on the Binghamton Road, to a old
dam where some days ago I got
a pencil sketch. The material
was very fine, light, atmosphere and
color were delightful & I worked
like a house afire — By 5.30 I got
my canvas covered & my light all
gone. Then Henrick came with the carriage
& Annie & Bessie ?. I put my traps aboard,
they went on & I walked. Took a sponge
bath when I got home — tea at 7 — Sua[?]
washed my brushes & read news[papers]

2 " — on jade plates.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 18, 1891

An uncomfortably hot day — ther. got
above 80° in the shade. I got to work in
studio at 10 o'clk. Painting upon the
study that I painted out of doors yes-
terday — I worked hard all the morning
but think I only tightened it up. At
10 Annie went riding with W. & Mrs.
Parke & Katie went off on horseback
with Bessie P. & Charles Post — It was
near 2 before they got back. I took dinner
abt. 30 — Painted awhile — then
Bessie P. came & I made a char-
coal sketch of her head — I worked
until 6 o'clk — then took a little air-
ing before tea — Before 8 o'clk, went
down town — on my return cleaned
my palette — Read my newspapers &
got to bed at about 10.
