## Left Page

### May, Sunday 10, 1891

Another hot, copper-colored day. Up at 6.30 & had a scrub & a bath
then back to bed & dozed until 8.30 — then up for all day & had a
walk in Bryant Park before bkf. After bkf. Read papers & wrote to
Capt. Beardsley — Mrs. M.S. Beach &
to Saml. Coleman — At 2 o'clk. Kate
& I went to Ed W. Cook's & dined.
Mary is in almost momentary expectation of confinement but she
insisted upon our dining with her.
She appeared to be in the best of
health & spirits — Ret'd home at
3.30 — Charl waiting for us. About
4\. we went to Central Park — the
display of flowers is very beautiful
— the Wistaria is simply wonderful.
We wandered about from 5th to 8th
Ave. — from 72d to 86th St. — & got home
soon after 7. — tea — Evng. Charl
again came in for a little while.
I wrote a letter to Annie — To bed
at about 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 11, 1891

Another warm day — cooler in the evng.
The drouth is now getting to be serious —
Rec'd a long letter from Annie, written Sat.
mf. Got to studio about 10 — Spent most
of the mfg. drawing over the entire background of Morgan portrait with multiple
needle a close mesh of lines then gave it
a half hour acid bath & stopped it all out.
Before noon went to Adams & Co. with
pkg & pk. for Saml. Coleman. Home to lunch
at 1.30 — Geo. came from Ridgefield — Back
to studio before 3. At my biting until 5.
Sauntered in Madison Sq. — Home —
Dinner at 7. — After 8 went to Walters
for a few minutes about proposed improvements in hall way. Home again.
Charl came in. It was nearly
11 when I got to bed.
