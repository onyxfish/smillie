## Left Page

### October, Saturday 3, 1891

Home again —

Foggy until after 10 when it cleared
to be a regular scorcher — After an
8 o'clk [illegible] I went over to Mr. Tho. Jones'
house, "Fieldstone" & spent some time
with him there looking over it, then to
P's & with A. ours. P. rode over to Mr.
Townsend [illegible]'s (Priscilla Bently) & had
her in, had a pleasant call — then
home & had lunch before 12 — After it
P. took A. & me by R.R. to Mayfield where
we went down the [illegible] mine — over the
Breaker — A's first descent into the bowels
of the earth — took a return train & got
back soon after 3 — then lay down for a
little rest — Dinner at 4.30 — P's brother
Dr. Chas. & his wife there. — At 5.45 were
driven in to R.R. Station & started on
our homeward journey at 6.15 getting
through very pleasantly, in time, at 7. [illegible]
walked up home & found everything
as we left it — looked over some of
our letters & papers & got to bed before
10.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 4, 1891

An almost cloudless, hazy & very
warm day — [illegible] at 9 — then I went
to garden & spent the rest of the
mng there, getting to the house at
noon — Then I lay down and had
quite a rest — dinner soon after 2 —
dawdled about from 3 to 4, gathering
seeds & pruning — At 4.30 went
out for a short ride — saw very
lame. back before 6 — Tea before
7 — Self wrote a note to [illegible]
in which I made a sketch of a
rider on horseback on his way
up Bala Mt.        At 9 took letters
to P. O. & then to bed.
