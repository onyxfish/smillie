## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 1. 1891.

A dull day - wind east, with a light fall of rain about 4 P. M. Another day spent out of doors, finding something to do in the garden among vegetables or flower beds. In the afternoon I put together small frames & set them to protect the young strawberry vines in east beds. The rain prevented us from going out riding — Every Reading news paper & writing up garden mem. book — nearly 11 where we got to bed. A good fall of rain during the night.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 2. 1891.

A dull day, but warm — Rain fell until about 8 — clouds lowering until near sunset when they broke & we had a fine color show. I worked in the garden & at the Poppy bed until noon then in etching room preparing for work at last. After dinner I pasted tissue paper upon my blind frames. At about 5 I went down town with Eliz'd., Sally, Mary, Jim, Ralph & Stephie [illegible] to buy fireworks for the 4th of July — got what we wanted at Sparks — I attended to some small matters and home after 6. After reading news papers I wrote a short letter to [illegible] & took it to P. O. at 10 o'clk. To bed at 11.
