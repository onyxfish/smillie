## Left Page

### December, Saturday 26, 1891

Dark & wet — at times rain falling
very heavily — Got to studio after
10 & to work on Rey plate with
etching point — my head
ached so that when I went
home to lunch I gave up &
went to bed & stayed there
until dinner time when I
got up & took a little to eat.
A quiet evef. Mr. Hyde, the
Pres. of Philharmonic Soc. called
for a few minutes — very pleasant.
I gave to him my old file of
Phil. programmes & reports.
After looking over back nos
of London Punch I went to
bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 27, 1891

A breezy, cool day. Bkfst. bet. 9
& 10 — then sat some time with
news paper — then a little writing
on Etching Club paper — abt 1 o'clk.
I walked up to 5th Ave. & 57th S. with
the boys & back in time for dinner
at 2 — some loafing — about 4 went
to studio for a few minutes. Tea
a little before 7 — Evef. I wrote
awhile on Etching Club paper
& got to bed after 11.
