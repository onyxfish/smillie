## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 14, 1891.

Very pleasant day. To studio soon after 10 - worked for awhile pasting about my Water-colors to get them into frames, then to N.A. of D. Taking one of my drawings with me - Saw the Hanging Com. Then to DeNuime's & saw Mr. Hofskins & left final orders for the printing, the last arrangement of type setting being satisfactory. Then went to Shirlaw's. Saw him & his wife - He went with me to Of's where I got a tracing from his painting "Reflection" - 12×20 that I wish to mezzotint - then home, riding in car with Mr (Cornell) Smith - In at 1.30, Elie Howe & Miss Lillie Hess lunching with us. Back to studio at 3. Framed another water-color. Took it to N.A. Saw Farrer & others - back to studio at 4.5 & found W. Heber Bishop's card & wrote a note to him - then to Kurtz & saw p.f. of Coleman Photo - then home at about 5.30 - Dinner at 6.30 - Every Annie & Kate went to the opera - I loafed about & being tired went to bed early! I did not get to bed early! No, Charlie came in at about 9 & in one way & another I was kept up until 11. Then did not get to sleep until A came in at 12.45 - after hearing the clock strike 1.30 I fell asleep.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 15, 1891.

A very pleasant day. To studio at 10.30 feeling very seedy after last night's suffering. I finished framing etchings and before noon went to N.A. of D. & saw Schilling & Bloodgood - Soon after 12 went to Kurtz's about Bicher's portraits - then to bank & home soon after 1 - lunch & after that had an hours nap & got to studio soon after 3 - Mr. Heber Bishop came in & spent some time with me consulting as to best method for engraving a copy of a piece of jade - agreeing upon mezzotint. Soon after J.C. Nicoll came in & we went to N.A. where we met Frank Jones, we three being the Decorating Committee - we talked over plans in a general way until near 5 o'clk. Then to studio & home at 6. Dinner. Every Annie went to her Choral Club rehearsal & I loafed at home. Edw. & Mary came in for a few minutes. A. & k. went to Brooklyn this p.m. & saw Carrie & Fanny Kimball. To bed at 10.
