## Left Page

### January, Friday 30, 1891

Pleasant day with a milky film overhead much of the time — got to DeVinne's at 9.20 to see Cat. in its complete form — found that they were binding it with 3 of the 5 etchings misplaced — got that corrected then to bank + to Kuertz's + to N.A. 4 D. From there back to Am. A.A. Ass'n — to see Thos. B. Clark about his lot of blue ware loaned to us. Saw Kirby — to return at 2.30 — home to lunch — back to A.A.A — at 2.30 — Clark not yet returned, borrowed of Kirby 5 light colored pieces to take the place of some of Clark's too blue pieces — there till after 5 helping to finish the decoration — then home, very tired + lay upon the bed until dinner. Eve'g at 8.30 went to N.A. Private View. Press + Profession — a great foul air, heat + tobacco smoke — the very young men had a great time — met a number of pleasant people — met Fischel + Adler + made an engagement to be at their place tomorrow afternoon — It was about 11.30 when I got home + midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 31, 1891

Dull + f. — rain commenced freezing about 3 P.M. — heavy rain at night. I got up feeling very headachy but got to studio about 10.30 — put a couple etchings pf. into frames then took our pfs from soft-ground etching plate. Home to lunch + after that went to Fischel, Adler + Schwartz's, 94 Fulton — taking with me a few pfs. from my etchings + a tracing of Shirlaw's painting "Meditation" or some title of that sort, that I wish very much to do in mezzotint. The impression made upon me was far from favorable — they want me to do a large plate for them of some common place picture — they named two that I declined. The Shirlaw outline produced little effect. I stopped at Patt's for a moment — then rode up town, stopping for a little while at the studio — when I got to N.A. there was a great crowd there — I did not attempt to go through the galleries — chatted for a while with the Farris, H.f. + T.C. Home + B — dinner — Eve'g very much used up by a headache against which I had been fighting all day — to bed at 10.30
