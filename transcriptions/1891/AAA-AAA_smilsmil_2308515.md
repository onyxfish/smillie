## Left Page

### November, Saturday 14. 1891.

A chilly day with a feeling of snow
in the air — a streaked day of sun-
shine & cloud shadow — got to work
a little earlier than usual —
about 10 went downtown & did
a great deal of running about
until 11.30, settling up. Found
much to do until dinner at 1.30
All sorts of work in the after-
noon, but little done towards
the packing of the two main
cases, for house & studio. About
5.30 Annie & I went to Mrs.
Hy C. Tyler's to call — not at
home — A. Kath. & I went on,
finishing up what was left un-
done in the mtf. Ewett got
seriously to work upon the two
cases & made good progress
getting the house one nearly
finished — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 15. 1891.

Dull, raw & disagreeable day with
a sleety rain beginning at 8 p.m.
Was Skip. earlier than usual for
Sunday m'f & right to work at
the many things everywhere. Wrote
a note to Northrop of the Sentinel con-
cerning Village Sanitation — Got big
house box screwed up by noon, then
at Studio box. Dinner at 2. after
[illegible] tried to get a little rest but
Dan. Sayre & wife called — after that
Annie helped me get curtains into
box. At 5 I commenced packing
trunk — Tea at 6.30 got through
with packing trunk before 9 o'clk.
To bed at 10, all ready for a
start in the mtf.
