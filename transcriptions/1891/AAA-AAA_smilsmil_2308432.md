## Left Page

### June, Monday 1. 1891.

Pleasant day — hat + coat, bath —
To studio after marketing etc + having
gone to Hawkinson's to have him shake
my studio rugs — Found letters from
Gifford + Burt — the latter, a picture
dealer, wants to know what I will sell
him my study [illegible]? in Socy of Am. Artists
show for — answered both letters — then
down Town to A, B, N. Co. Saw Maedonough
+ gave him India paper + dies from
father's effects. Saw Doe about mezzotint
rockers — then to M. Heber Bishop's — not
in town — then to Siment Armend's + bot
large glass battle + acid for Montrose. Then
to Moran's — in 41st St + gave order for
packing boxes — then home to lunch
ab't 1.20 — To studio at 2.30. Hawkinson
came for Rugs at 3 — then to K + V's and
got printing ink — then to Rogers + Peet
+ bought Soft hats — then to barbers + had hair
cut + home at 6 — dinner at 7. Every
wrote a note of instructions to Herrick.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 2. 1891.

**Ther. 84°.**

A very hot day — in the mff packed
a box containing a heterogeneous lot
of things — bamboo tables, screens, trash
baskets, etc — To studio soon after 11 —
then to Bank + arranged Annie's
matters until Nov. 1st — Home at 1 '5
Fanny K. [illegible] with us,
lunch — then to Moran's (carpenter). Then
to D, L + W. R.R. offices in Hoboken — saw
Sub't Reasoner + he telegraphed to Genl.
Sub't Halstead + from him got permission to stop No. 3 at New Milford on Friday
my neyt — very much to my gratification.
Then back + to Lord + Taylors where I
did some shopping — then to Knoedler's
+ then to studio + home before 6. A
gathering of cousins to celebrate in
advance Jim's birthday. Ten came
counting Merrill Bishop's + they had
a royal time until 9 o'clk — They
had a big supper + then a riotous
[illegible]. We got to bed soon
after 10. A sharp thunder-
storm soon after midnight.
