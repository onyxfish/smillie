## Left Page

### July, Sunday 19, 1891

A cloudy mf. - the weather clearing gradually to a lovely afternoon &
evef. After bkfst. I spent some
time in the garden & among the
flower beds — Then I wrote to
Macdonough - to J. Harrison Miles +
to Mr. Glover. Art Sub. of a Tacoma
Syp. Dinner at about 2 - m.
the afternoon I wrote to Walter
concerning alterations in 38 St.
house. Tea soon after. — At
7 we all went off for a ride
& took a quick drive around the
4 mile square. Back at 8 - Miss
Toshay had come up to the house
- She sat on the piazza with us
enjoying the moonlight until
after 9 — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 20, 1891

A fine day, but very hot. A
thunder storm with heavy rain from
7 to 8 o'clk. P.M. After 10 o'clk. I
took sketching umbrella, pencils
& paper & went out a short distance
north of the house & sat there until
after 12 making a study of a group
of maples — Then to studio & worked
on it there until dinner at 1 o'clk.
Some superintending work out of
doors — Then I prepared jade plate
to send by Exp. to A.B.N. Co. for Joe
to clean the margin — also the papier
maché specimen bit of ceiling to
return it to Walter. Tea at 6.30 —
At 7 - the horses being harnessed, we
drove down to Exp. Office in the face of
the approaching storm — & expressed the
two packages — Got back to the house
just before the shower came.
Evef reading newspapers.
