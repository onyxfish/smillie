## Left Page

### January, Friday 16, 1891

A very pleasant day — my birth day was remembered for when I got to the breakfast table I found a number of packages ranged about my plate & on examination they were found to contain a variety of small things, chiefly useful except a handsome dark blue neck handkerchief. Got to studio at 10, got busied myself for an hour there to Broad St. to office of Mr. Bishops. Spent an hour with him looking over his wood cut & etching illustrations of Jade. Home at 1.30 — lunch — dressed & to studio at 3.5. Busy with odds and ends — Gifford came in — before 5 A. & M. came — with A. I walked to [illegible] St. to Lockwood DeForests — Reception — half an hour there, pleasantly. A. rode home — I walked — Dinner — Every Geo. Cook came & audited Annie's Diet Kitchen Accts. I read until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 17, 1891

Disagreeable wt[illegible], changing later into a horrible storm of sleety rain with high wind — the streets covered with two inches of icy slush. Got to studio at 10.30. The m[illegible], divided between pursing with pf[illegible] & etchings, getting them into frames, and working upon dry-point portrait of George who gave me another sitting — Home at 1 — disappointed to find that Yale had not come, as promised, to remove the stitches from Ralph. Then back to studio & again George gave me a sitting — About 4 Frank Jones came for me & we went to N.A., met J.C. Nicoll & decided to cover corridor wall with matting upon which to hang etchings. Back to studio at 5 — home at 6 — Yale had come to the house about 3 & having no chloroform had had another resultless picket with Ralph. It was about 7 when I went to Edw. Cook's where a "Reception" has been going on for some hours — found the remnants — that is, the receivers, discussing the events of the afternoon — Walker & wife — Gri & wife — Mrs. Mrs. Brownell, Miss Cumming, Annie & Kate — of course, Mr. & Mrs. Edw & Miss Williams — we had a very enjoyable "High Tea" and got home before 10 & then soon to bed —

about 5 oclk I went in to Champney's tea for a few minutes.
