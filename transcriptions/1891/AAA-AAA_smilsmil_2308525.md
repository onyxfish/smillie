## Left Page

### December, Sunday 6, 1891

Charming m't. very bright and mild — overcast afternoon. Bible:
bet. 9 & 10 o'clk. after that read newspaper until near noon — then
A — the boys & I went to Century & looked at the pictures — then
walked up to 57° St. & home —
Dinner at 2 — at 5 I went to Dr. Yale's & he dressed my troublesome soft corn — Tea at 6.30.
Ev'g. Read awhile, then wrote instructions to Herrick
until after 10 — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 7, 1891

A very dark day — rain all the m't., slowly clearing in the afternoon — I got to studio at 10 o'clk &
got Santini's Rey. plate & then rode
down to R. & V.'s in Canal St. where
I had three india pf°. Faku. I
am pleased with the result on
the whole, altho' at present it looks
mealy — Home to lunch at 1 and
back at 2.30 finding Mr. Blackburn
waiting for me — She had her
plate ready for biting — I worked
upon it for awhile with the point
& then got it into the acid — I
was able to do very little for myself besides tacking down my pf°.
The etching was not bitten in and
at 4. 45 she left to call again to-morrow.
Home to dinner at 6 — Ev'g. I
wrote to Koehler. Charl came
in, having spent Sunday at Po'k.

mr. B, J°.
