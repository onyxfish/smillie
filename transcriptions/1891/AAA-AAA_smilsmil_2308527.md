## Left Page

### December, Thursday 10, 1891

Charming day — After bkfst. Saw Colman came & after looking at some Wall paper & specimens at the house we went to Warren, Fuller & Lange's & saw more, making a new selection — saw both W. & J. Then to house again — & then to studio, getting there about 10.30 — To work etching on Ray plate — Home after 1. Janu Kimball lunched in the laundry with us. To studio at 2.40 & worked until 4. Then to 137 W. 23d. St. with negatives of my photo. & saw Anthony & W. Edwards then down to Canal St. Kimmel & Voigts & left with them mezzotint ground to have a p[roof] for S. R. Koehler. Home at 6 — at 6.30 As. & I went to Walter's & dined — Mr. Babb there. After dinner Louise played on the piano. Home at 10 & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 11, 1891

In bed all day with a very severe headache — It began to abate after 3 o'clk and I was able to get up at 6 & take a little food and at 7.30 I walked down to studio. Alex. Schilling soon came & we got from Geo's studio the Tilford supplies. In the course of the evng. six others struggled in making but eight, nine being a quorum. We got through with some routine business inspite [of] lack of quorum. Three had ginger ale, whisky crackers & cheese & all appeared to be much interested in my soft ground & aqua tint work. Home before 11 — got to bed before Annie & Kate came in from the Lyceum where they had been with Charl.

Today the carpenters got out of the house.
