## Left Page

### February, Saturday 21. 1891.

Very dark - mizzling rain with frequent
showers. Uncle Willie came in after
bkfs. & sat with us until after 10.
Before going to studio I went to B'nk
& transacted some business for Annie
It was 11 when I got to studio, but
too dark for work — I varnished
the inside of large and tray, etc. &
went into Champney's studio & saw a
clever Pastel head of a child & made
engagement with him to make a like
sketch from Ralph next Sat. m'g. Home
at 1.30 — Mollie Adams lunched with
us & went with Annie to the Opera in the
afternoon — I Ret'd. to studio at 3 & spent
the rest of the afternoon mounting p'ps. of
etchings. At 6. went to Ortgies gallery
& got two working p'ps. of Heaths plate
after Reynolds portrait of West.
Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g. Charl came
in at 8.30 — we looked over photo-
graphs until 10.30 — To bed at 11.

Barns, Real estate agent, came in to
the studio & announced that Tiffany &
Co. had taken the entire building — that
he was to be their agent & asking me
if I wished to retain my studio.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 22. 1891.

Brilliant day — up late — bkfs. after
9 — after that read newspaper until
about 11 — then with Jim & Ralph went
to studio where I changed some proofs
that were in press. Home soon after
12\. Geo. & Smiley came in at 1.30
& stayed to dinner (2 o'clk) with us.
After 4 o'clk. Jim & I walked up
to Central Park & back at 5.30.
Tea soon after 6. Ev'g. Charl
came in — I spent a little time
in laying out a drawing for a seat
for piazza at Montrose. To bed
before 11 —

wrote & mailed a letter to Hoddes Drive
engaging him as gardener at Montrose.
