## Left Page

### June, Sunday 7, 1891

A pleasant, gentle rain falling till
10 or 11 o'clk, after that a gradual clear-
ing until after 5 when the sky was al-
most cloudless. Bkfs. after 8. —
Spent the mf. going about, most
of the time engaged transplanting
lettuce. Dinner at 2 — after that
a rest & a short nap. Bet. 3 & 4
o'clk, Daut. Sayre & wife & Fanny
Connell called. Fanny is making
a flying visit & leaves tomorrow mf.
After 4. Annie, the children &
I went out for a ride — over to W.
Allen's & interviewed him about
fire wood — Home at 6 — Tea at
7 — Early evef. Reading newspapers
— then Annie read aloud to me
& I was inconsiderate enough
to go to sleep. To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 8, 1891

A beautiful day, altho' hazy — cool
in the mf., growing warmer. Bkfs.
about 8 & after that out of doors working
among bushes & vines until dinner; a
short time after 1. Reading for awhile
after that, then again out in garden
with Stone until 4 when Annie, R-
& I went out to ride — had a pleasant
time — back at 6 20  Tea — Evef.
wasted over newspapers. To bed
soon after 10.
