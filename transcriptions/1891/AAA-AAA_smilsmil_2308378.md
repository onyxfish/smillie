## Left Page

### February, Friday 13, 1891

Overcast m[u]f clearing very pleasantly before night. Got to studio at about 10. & to work up on the Morgan portrait — completed the laying in of the drapery so that only the face remains without work upon it. Home to lunch — on my return laid a ground on 9"×12" plate — then dampd gelatine tracing & fo[r]epared it and got a very good press transfer —
Before 5 I went to Mr. Dav[i]d. Huntington's with 3 G.D. [illegible] for his signature. He was not in — left them for him — then to N.A. — there for awhile — then home —
Dinner at 6.30 — wrote a letter to Herrick — At 8 went to Murray Hill Hotel & for a few moments saw Mr. Woodin — then to 6° Ave. & 57° S[t]. Chapman's Studio meeting of the Etching Club — only Farrer, Shilling, Nicoll, Wood, Bacher, Chapman & myself there — but we got through with a lot of business — Passing bills etc. It was 11 o'clk. before I started for home. Walked down — To bed before 12.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 14, 1891

Pleasant day, but windy & cold. Got to studio late & was annoyed, just as I was about starting in my work, by plumbers who said that they must take up a portion of the floor in my closet. Then [illegible] came in — finally did get to work Etching 9×12 —
[and] after luncheon call — got the main lines all drawn in except the face & soon after I went home leaving the carpenter & plumber in my room —
After lunch I went over to [illegible]'s in W. 47th S[t]. & he did a little work upon my teeth — then I walked down to studio getting there at 3[:]5. Laid a couple of small grounds — Charle came in — then I went to Dav[i]d. Huntington's & got 3 [illegible] (G.D.) with autographs. Sat and chatted with him until 6 o'clk — then home. Dinner — At 8.30 went to Walter's & waited for him to dress — It was after 9 when we got to the Century — W's first visit since his election — met [those] who took charge of W. — I spent the evening with different cronies — After 11 Walter, [illegible] & I left the Club. To bed before 12.
