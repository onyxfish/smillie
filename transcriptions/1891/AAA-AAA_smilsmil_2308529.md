## Left Page

### December, Monday 14, 1891

Cloudy day with now & then a drop of
rain. Douglass came with a painter
in the [illegible] & made a start upon the
ceiling of library. Then I went to Ann
& failed to see him — then to May's — then
to studio — stopped out on Rey plate —
galving acid in hot water — then went
to 13) W. 23. S. & saw A.V.S. Anthony & Mr.
Edwards about Photo-gravure printing
for Etching Club. Then to Tho. Moran &
induced him to promise plate from him-
self & wife for Etching Club Cat. Then to
Studio & put plates in acid — all went
to Curran's old shop & saw his successor.
He came to the house at 2 & is to make
certain broken plaster mouldings etc.
After lunch back to studio — stopped out
Rey plate — Mr. Blackburn came —
Then Tho. Moran & later, Sam. Colman — &
gave plate one more bite & cleaned
it off — wrote notes to Schilling & [illegible].
Home at 6 — Kate had Rel. to Police
dinner. Every. some reading —
wrote a note to Gifford —

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 15, 1891

Overcast & dark — Rain began to fall
at about 5 P.m. Painter at work on
ceiling. Mason came in the afternoon
& fixed library cornice. Front stoop was
stoned. I got to studio about 10 o'clk.
& work on Rey plate — after rubbing
it with rough emery paper I filled
the work with etching ground, carefully
cleaned the surface & then floated
an aqua-tint ground over the entire
plate — To my great delight it appear-
ed to granulate successfully — Then
I went home to lunch. The after-
noon, until 4 o'clk., was spent
varnishing the plate back & front — Then
to Rose & Co. & selected pattern & ordered
11×22 passe for Rey painting — then to
Baudry — 5. Ave. near 20. S. & ordered a
panel back stretcher — then to 23. S. &
got Reframed Ther. Home to dinner
at 6. Annie went to Choral Club
rehearsal & I kept house.
