## Left Page

### October, Thursday 15. 1891.

Very pleasant day — worked in studio forwarding Pratt Mrs. aqua-tint plate
"the old mill dam" until about 11.30
then, as Rutan had got the bed ready
I went out & set out the 36 wild white
strawberry plants that I got potted
finished in in time for dinner.
Immediately after that Aniie, the
boys & I went down town to see the
parade of the Fire Dep't. & visiting
co's & bands from Binghampton —
after the parade, about 4 o'clk —
a co. equipped with Chemical app[aratus]
atrus, from Binghampton, gave quite
an exciting exbn. A small house
was constructed in front of the Court Ho.
— filled with combustibles & set on fire
a spirited trace with the apparatus
came dashing up & within a minute
or two had quenched the roaring blaze.
Then to P.O. & Market & home before 5 —
with Rutan completed my strawber[ry]
bed work, but not until after sundown.
Evef. — wrote to Walter Koehler
& D.B. Butler — took notes to P.O.
at 10 o'clk. Beautiful moonlight
night.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 16. 1891.

A disagreeable day — overcast — strong
wind from N.W. with a number of spitting
scurrying showers. Got Rutan to work
on the rearrangement of the Peony
bed before the front door — then I got
to work in studio, dividing my time
between the Pratt Mrs. aqua-tint
5" × 7" & the San Luis Rey Reduction.
After dinner, again with Rutan
resetting Peonias — It was 3.30 before
I got back to my studio work &
could not see after 4.30 — Then
took nippers & went out & trimmed
shrubs. Evef. I wrote a letter
to Aunt Huldah. Aniie wrote to
Chase & to Kate & before 10 I went
to P.O.
