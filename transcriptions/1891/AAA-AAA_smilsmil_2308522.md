## Left Page

### November, Monday 30, 1891

Brilliantly clear - Ther. at 16° at 10
A.M. A number of things at home
kept me from the studio until about
10.30 - As steam had not been turned
on I found the studio Ther. at 42°. So
that, owing to the cold I was still
further delayed from my work. At
last I got at San Luis Rey with my
pencil on the soft ground. Mrs.
Schoff made a short call. Home to
lunch at 1.30 - A. + boys not at home.
I lunched alone - some time spent
with the carpenters - To studio at
2.45 - + by 4.30 had finished drawing.
Eve'g. A. + D. went to 55th St.
just W. of 6th Ave. To the Mendels-
ssohn Glee Club Rehearsal and
enjoyed it very much. Home
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 1, 1891

Bright, beautiful day - not so
cold as yesterday - It was ten
o'clk. before I got to studio & then
to work varnishing San Luis Rey
plate + getting ready for biting -
From 10.30 to 11.30 at 2nd Nat. Bk.
+ safe deposit attending to Annie's
business - Home to lunch at - had
alone - A. + boys at Mary's - abt. 45
went to Mary's to get a check from A. -
then again to bank + paid off the
loan made to purchase the Dozier
place in Montrose. To studio at
2.30 + found my Etching pupil, Mrs.
Blackburn, waiting for me. She
spent the rest of the p.m. in studio
I laid a ground for her + gave her much
advice - I was able to do a little
work for myself - D.H. ale came in -
Home soon after 5 - dressed - dinner
at 6 - Eve'g. Annie + I went to
Chickering Hall - first concert of the
Mendelssohn Glee Club - we have
Emma Ireland's tickets this season -
— a very enjoyable concert — of course —
Home about 11.
