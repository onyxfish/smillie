## Left Page

### March, Saturday 21, 1891

A very disagreeable day — again I took my breakfast in bed and then got up. I did not feel as well as I did the day before — depressed etc — In the m'g, for a time, Kate read aloud from Howell's "A Hazard of New Fortunes" — In the afternoon she & Annie went to the Met. Opera Ho — The close of the Opera season — I became very much worried before they got home at 6.50 p.m. fearing that some accident had befallen them — The wild enthusiasm of the audience who would not go home was the explanation — Dinner — Ev'g, Charl came in — then Mr. Champoney made to pleasant calls to enquire after my health. It was after 10.30 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 22, 1891

Another unpleasant day — dry until about noon when some heavy showers fell — later the rain was continuous — Slept late — bkfst. in bed — after that, lay there & fooled away the rest of the m'g over a newspaper. Up about noon — took along walk in the parlor. Dinner at 2 — Late in the afternoon I subjected myself to an enema with the usual disagreeable results — then a nap — Tea at 7. Ev'g — a little reading until W. Chas. Collins came in — Chatting until 10.
