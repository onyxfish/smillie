## Left Page

### November, Sunday 22, 1891

Overcast & mild. It was near 9.30 when we had H[illegible]. After that
some time was spent over Sunday
paper. Geo. & Emily came in for
awhile. Ralph & I went out for
a walk a little before 2, then
to Ed W. Cook's where Annie, Kate
& Jim joined us & we had dinner.
About 3.30 I ret'd. to the house
to look over some samples of
paper for Reception room wall,
got from Tiffany's — wrote part
of a letter to S.R. Koehler. Soon
after 5 went down to studio but
only to find that the lock to the front
door had been changed & I could
not get in. Home again —
Tea. Eve'g, wrote to S.R. Koehler,
to Vickery & to Amos Nichols.
Kate went out with Charl. &
did not get in until after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 23, 1891

*Kate ret'd. to Po'keepsie.*

A disagreeable day with high wind
& heavy rain. Waited until near
10 o'ck. for Louis Tiffany — he did not come.
Then to studio — hanging curtains most
of the m'g. Two ladies called — one
desiring lessons in etching. Home to
lunch soon after 1 — served in great
confusion in the nursery. Edith Wright
was at the house when I arrived. Alice
[illegible] came before I left. Owing to
heavy rain I did not return to studio
until after 3 — then got my engraving
tools ready to work upon. It was
6 o'ck. when I got home. Dinner
at 6.30. Eve'g, I read until 9 o'clk
in Dec! Harker — & to bed before 10.
