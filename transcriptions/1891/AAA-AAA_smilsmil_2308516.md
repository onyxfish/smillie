## Left Page

### November, Monday 16, 1891

*Ret? to NY. for the winter.*

A very disagreeable day. Cold &
windy & threatening all sorts of
weather nastiness — light rains in
the afternoon & eve'g. Up at 5.30 —
Blkp. a little before 7 — our trunks all
sent over to station at 7 & we followed
at 7.30 — The two girls were left to
clean up things & follow on the 12.30
train. On train left at 7.45 — made close
connection on D. L & W. At Scranton we
saw Mrs. Parke for a moment — left miles
book for the girls at Station Restaurant —
our R.R. ride would have been pleasant
enough but for the overheating of our car.
Had a buffet lunch in car, eating out the
lunch we carried with us — got to [illegible]
at 3. on time — then right to the house
in possession of the carpenters — a terrible
[illegible] of unfinish & dirt — Man &
money & mrs. Bowder had got our beds
ready for us. At 6. Annie & the boys went
to Edward's for dinner — I had to sit alone
in the empty house & wait for my time —
Annie at? to put Ralph to bed — Jim
stayed all night with Tom — & then I went
to Edwd. & got a cup of tea — before I returned
the Exp. man came — before 9 — Nettie &
Gretta came — all right — In the afternoon
May Cok. Alice & Emma Ireland & Walt
Cook called — Eve'g. unpacked trunks
& to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 17, 1891

An unpleasant out day — light rains
most of the day — It was nearly 8.30
when Annie, Ralph & I got to Edw.'s
to bkfst. After that I ret'd. to the house
to spend the m'g fussing about and
"bossing" the mechanics in the house —
A noon I lay down for a rest. A —
H— went to Edw.'s to lunch — About
2 o'clk. I had a very light lunch at
home. In the afternoon I went to
studio — a most discouraging state of
dirt — called on my neighbor
de Haven — & up on Geo. On my
way home went into the Ortgies
Gallery, 5th Ave. & saw a collection
of paintings comprising about fifty
by de Haven — left note at Union
League for Charl. — At 6.30 A. & I
went to Edw.'s to dinner — After'd. I
took Ralph back to the house — then
Charl came & I spent some time
with him inspecting the new work
until 10 o'clk. when I went up to
Hall in 45th St. for A. — Choral Club
rehearsal, C— went up with me &
we walked home together.
