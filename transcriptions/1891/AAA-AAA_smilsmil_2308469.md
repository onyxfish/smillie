## Left Page

### August, Friday 14, 1891

*Miss Foshay left us.*

Very pleasant day — got up with a headache, the price of last night's jolly —
Miss Foshay left us before 10 o'clk. to go
to Auburn to visit friends — There
was another rehearsal of "The Vaucend
Savage" at which I was present
& was pleased to find so much spirit
in the acting of some of the participants —
James Mulford & his sister came up — also
Mr. Jones & Parke — my mtf was a busy
one. After dinner I gave up for a
time to my headache & had a poor
nap. Before 5 o'clk I went down
town & spent some time attending
to many messages. Tea at 6.30
Evg. Mr. & Mrs. A. Smith called.
Rain after 9 P. m.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 15, 1891

Overcast mtf — gradually clearing till
the afternoon which was beautifully bright
& cool — cloudless moonlight night.
Wenick away all day attending a picnic
& Bessie off attending a pic nic. I spent
much of the mtf. as usual, in the garden.
Went to work taking pf. of "Sweet Pea"
Dry-paint & making changes in to effect
of light & shade. Dinner at about 1.30
Again at proving & dry-painting
until 4.30 — then out for a ride — had
a very pleasant one & did not get back
until 7 o'clk. Tea — Evening
spent with newspaper & planning
over pf. of dry paint.
