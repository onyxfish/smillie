## Left Page

### May, Friday 22, 1891

*Annie, the boys & I went to Ridgefield.*

A very muggy, hot day — Got to
studio at 10.15 — Rec'd check from N.A.
of D. for small painting "A bit in River-
side Park". After making a tracing of
figures in Sherlaws' "Reflections" and
preparing it for S's emendations I
went to Bk. with check — then to
Salmagundi club with Etching Club
acct. book for Schilling — then to 47
W.22d with etching pl. for Shurtleff —
Had a short visit with him & his
wife — again to studio & home before
1 — after lunch — dressed & prepared
for our trip to Ridgefield — At 3 I went
to Barber's & had my hair cut and at
3.35 went to N.H. R.R. office — bot. tickets &
checked valise — at 3.45 Annies and the
boys arr'd & Geo. We started in 4.02
train & got to Ridgefield about 6 —
Dinner soon after — Some wandering
about before we settled for this eve.
chatting until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 23, 1891

*Kate went to Po'keepsie.*

Bright, cool day. Help. before 8 & then
all hands wandering over the place
until noon — seeing all that was to be
seen, in doors & out of doors. As I
lifted Ralph I felt a twinge in my
back — the beginning of a very stiff
back that discomforted me much
during the rest of the day. Lay down
for awhile before dinner at 1.30 — After
that wandered about uneasily and
after 3 Annies, Ralph & I walked to the
P.O. & Drug Store where we got some camphor-
ated Vaseline for my old back — back &
had a fine lighted in parlor fire place —
Tea at 6.30.
