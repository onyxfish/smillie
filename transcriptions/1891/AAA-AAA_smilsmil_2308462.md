## Left Page

### July, Friday 31, 1891

Glorious day — brilliant, crisp & cool.
my Jade plate reached me this
m'nt & after my out of door work
I took two prints of it — then dinner.
After dinner I painted for awhile
on 21"×31" but gave it up owing
to discomfort in my head — cleaned
up palette & washed brushes &
worked among Blk. Raspberry
vines until 4 o'clk. where we
went out riding — taking Miss
Foshay with us — we got upon a
new road & went much out of
our way & did not get home
until 7.30 — tea — after that
Annie & Miss F. went to a concert
at Presbt'n Ch. I read newspaper
& before 10 went to the church &
brought them home & then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 1, 1891

Pleasant m'fg — later, overcast, with
appearance of rain. My head bothered
me again so much that I did not try
to do any studio work but kept out of
doors all day working mostly among
berry vines & fruit trees. After getting
through with my garden work in the
m'f, I started out with a basket of lettuce
leaving some at Mr. Garst's — then to Florence
Lathrob's — had a chat with W. & Mrs. Edgar
& Mrs. Eno — then to Ben Mulford's and left
more lettuce — then to Parke's — met there W.
& Mrs. Hosey & spent some time — then to
several stores attending to small matters —
then to Barker's & had my hair cut — Home
to dinner at 1 — Afternoon pretty nearly
used up by my headache, but I was
able to keep up & about — after 8 o'clk. I
went down town, attending to marketing
etc — after getting home read
newspaper & Century for August —
until 10.30 — then to bed.
