## Left Page

### September, Sunday 13, 1891

Pleasant w't. but with threats of a weather
change. Thunder rumbled after 12 &
about 2.30 the first of a series of heavy
showers fell — the last being about 6 p.m.
I was in the garden until 11.30 —
Men Annie & I wandered about
the place. Dinner at 2. Wrote
notes to Gifford, Turner & Schilling
— then had a nap — a walk on the
piazza, enjoying the rainfall —
Tea — Evd'f, some reading and
a little writing — Nearly 11 when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 14, 1891

Cool, breezy day with many clouds —
Some wonderful effects of color & atmosphere
on the distant hills — All the morning
out of doors — just before 12 went to New
School building to see brick layer
about relaying the tops of our chimnies —
Annie & Ralph went down with
me. It was 12.30 when I got back.
Did a little work on jade plate
before dinner — & after it, worked
until 4 o'clk. Then out riding
spending much time hunting
for the boys, donkey & cart — found them
at the Station of little R.R. — then had
a short ride, getting home at 6 —
Tea at 6.30 — Evd'f — Bessie Parke
took tea with us — about 9. Mr. & Mrs.
Parke, Mrs. Sayre, Mrs. Atherton & Max. P.
called for half an hour. To bed at 10.30
