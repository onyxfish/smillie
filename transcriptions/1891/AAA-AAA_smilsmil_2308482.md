## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 9. 1891.

**Ther. fell to 39°. last night.**

Brilliant, beautiful day. After bkfst —
Ralph & I went to new R.R. & saw Page —
him down town & transacted general
business. Home at about 11 & then went
to garden. It was noon before I got
to work on jade plate — dinner about
1 — after dinner I took Ralph up to the
fair ground, Veterans' encampment,
where we saw the ox that had been
roasted whole — or what remained of
it — for it had been carved down to the
bones all over. Back to studio & worked
for an hour on plate — then walked out
on Binghampton Road about a mile and
made a slight pencil sketch — home
at 6 — tea — Evey. after 8, Annie &
I went to Mr. Schaeffer's, where Kate had
been to tea, Joe Williams, Frank Beuty,
Priscilla Pom & a group up or there — about
10 we went to Parke's, where Charl had
been to dinner — & at 10.30 we all walked
up the hill.     To bed before 11.

on jade plate 2 h.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 10. 1891.

**Charl & Kate left us.**

Glorious day — cool in the m'g — warm
at noon & afternoon — Up at 6.30 — Charl
got an early bkfst & at 7.15 I drove with
him to the station where Mr. Parke met him
& together they went to Scranton where he
will take a later train to N.Y. Home at
7.45 & we had bkfst. I got to work early
in studio sketching & laying in, in a palette
of transparent color, a composition from
my sketch of yesterday. The team at work
all day hauling hard stone from R.R. cutting,
stone spreading it before the road to the
house. Kate had an early dinner and
we all went with her to the station at 12. [illegible]
when she went on to Buffalo to visit Miss
Brock. Dinner soon after 1. I tried to
get a sketch of Jim & his donkey but could
not do what I wanted to — then I went
to work trimming into shape a maple that
grows at the S.W. cor. of the house — tea at 6.30.
Katie has come up from the Parke's to stay
a few days with us — Priscilla Beuty, [torn]
Mrs Comstock & Frank Beuty called, between
4 & 5 o'clk. p.m.   A very quiet evening &
to bed at —
