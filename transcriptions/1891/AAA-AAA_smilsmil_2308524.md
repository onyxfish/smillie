## Left Page

### December, Friday 4, 1891

A mild a.f. overcast before noon and a driving rain storm before dark, raining at it until about 8 — after that, bright starlight — Got to studio about 10 & spent the m'f cleaning up the Saudin's Key plate — a portion of the sea was so frostbitten (very lightly) that I had to clean it — Home to lunch at 1.15 — Amine attending her [illegible] League in Brooklyn. At 2.30 I rode down to Kimmel & Voigt's to attend to printing of Etching club plates & to arrange for working p.p. of Saudin's Key. Back to studio before dark & did a little work — a drenching rain & driving wind when I went home at 5.30 — Eve'g. Read awhile, then wrote my regular letter to Herrick. Posted it & got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 5, 1891

Brilliant beautiful day — after my m'g duties I got to studio at 10 — then had to go to Pach Bros. to meet Amine with the boys — they posed for photos. Then to 2°. Nat. Bk. — then to a Paperhangers in 23°. St. to look at papers but saw nothing that interested me. Then to studio — after 11 when I got there — very soon St. Agoster came in with sketch of a head that he is going to etch — It was after 12 when he went — I worked on Key plate until about 1, cleaning it — then home — lunch. Back at 2.40 & finished cleaning plate, made aqua-tint solution — went to Simonnd's & got a glass arrangement for dropping solutions on plate — Home before 6 — dinner — Eve'g at about 9 o'clk went to Century and had a pleasant eve'g there until near 12 o'clk — then home, walking over with Chas. Collins.
