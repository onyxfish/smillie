## Left Page

### August, Sunday 30. 1891.

Overcast until near noon — then a mizzling rain that continued at intervals through afternoon & eve.
After bkfp. I wrote "notices" of the V. J. meeting to take place next Tues. eve &
sent one to Res. Mr. Wanner — took one to Rev. Anines Hughes (Baptist) & then to
Parke's where I gave one to his father who is to preach in the Presb'n church —
Then home & to the garden — I had a nap before a late dinner at 2.30.
After dinner, between showers, I transplanted a couple of dozen of Henderson lettuce plants — I wrote to
Mr. Vickery — also to Shark's Sons for a plate for Mr. V — Eve'g, I walked upon piazza & read until near 10 —
then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 31. 1891.

Cool & cloudy — after bkfp. I had a number of things out of doors to look after, so that it was nearly 11
before I went down town — there I "buzzed" for V. J." for awhile — Home at noon — got a telegram from Charl at Lake Geo. saying that he would come here on Thurs. or Friday — After dinner wrote to him & sent Jim on his donkey to the P. O., but the donkey tumbled him off & I had to go with him to the P. O. to see that the donkey behaved herself.
At 4.30 Annie, Kate & I went out for a ride — Home at 6.30 — While at tea Mr. Chas. Read called —
Read news papers for awhile &
got to bed before 10.
