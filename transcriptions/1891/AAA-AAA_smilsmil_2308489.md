## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 23, 1891

Heavy fog in the a.m. which lifted
before 11, the day clearing to be very hot
& almost cloudless. I spent some time
looking after the men who had commenced
laying the board walk in front of Herrick's
house. I spent some time on the
Jade plate, working on the teak wood
base & cleaning margin. At 5 p.m.
I lay down for a rest — at 6, dressed
— at 7, Annie & I went to Mr. Christ-
man's by invitation to tea. & there
met mrs [illegible] Mrs. Garst & Mr. &
Mrs. Ben Mulford. It was 10.30
when we left & 11.30 when I got to
bed.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 24, 1891

*Charles Collins arrived*

Very warm — cloudless sky. Bkfst
after 8 o'clk. Got to work on Jade plate —
finished cleaning margin & before
dinner time I had taken a
couple of proofs. I was prepared
for a disappointment — the whole
thing is very much darker than it
appears upon the plate, but all
things considered it is in very
good condition. Dinner. After
dinner A.R. Smith called about
the plot of ground and later in
the afternoon I went down to see
about it. Found Coyle finishing the
sodding. Eve'g, after an early tea
I went to R.R. station & met Chas.
Collins as he came in on the 7.55
train — we walked to the house
& the eve'g was passed in chatting
until 10 o'clk. Then to bed.

*Jade, 3 h*
