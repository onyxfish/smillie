## Right Page

### January, Thursday 1, 1891

An out & out nasty day — Arthur coat
of snow fell last night — then a mizzle
of rain that froze as it fell — more or
less mizzle all day — growing warmer —
at night the streets filled with pools
of slush & snow water. It was
10.30 when I got to studio — Being
too dark for painting I made up
my mind to begin a water color.
Got a piece of mounted paper from
Geo. & planned a subject from a
sketch on So. road from Montrose.
Home at 1.30 Katie, Mollie & Roger
Adams had am°. from New Haven.
Back to studio at 2.45 until near 6.
was engaged cutting mats & mounting
etchings &c. Charle came in about
5 — we walked up to 38th S. together.
Dinner soon after 6. Evening
reading Janvier's Aztec Treasure House
& [illegible] Century until near 10 — then
to bed.
