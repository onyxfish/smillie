## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 3, 1891

Very disagreeable day — Snow all day, with Raw, cutting wind — Got to studio at 10.30 & after a time got started re-painting a canvas × 30 that I began last Spring — an attempt at something sunny. Before I went to bank then home to lunch — back before 3 & painted for awhile — result unsatisfactory — could not get sky painted in as I had hoped. Got proof of Shirlaw mezzotint ready to send to Dr. Cha. E. Clark of Boston & at 6 o'clk. went to 42ᵈ St. & expressed it — then home — dinner — Self. wrote a note or two — then read awhile. Just before 10 Charl came in bringing profile photo. fine. He left at 10 & I went to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 4, 1891

Another beastly day — overcast until after 4 o'clk. It was 10.30 when I got to Studio — To work painting on 20 × 30 that I have christened "A Meadow Creek". It was 1.30 when I got home to lunch & 2.45 when I got back to Studio & again I painted until 4.30, then, being very tired I cleaned up and got home before 6 o'clk. Dinner — Self. wrote a note to Mrs. Alsop (miss Mary Spring) — Charl came in after 9 o'clk. & left soon after 10. To bed at 11.
