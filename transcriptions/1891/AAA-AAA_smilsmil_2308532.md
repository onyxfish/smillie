## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 22, 1891

Foggy, mild & disagreeable — up early.
Before I went to studio I went to
Steme Bros. & bought a hair brush &
for A's Xmas. Then to studio & to work
upon Rey sky — burnishing & going
over a part of it with Roulette — about
noon A.N.S. Anthony came in with
photo. negatives — Rec'd by mail Gifford
plates — Home before 1 — [illegible] hanger
at work in library — Painter finishing
in hall — May putting up shades, rods
& curtains — To studio before 2.30 and
worked as long as I could see — Then
to Baudry's & got panel stretcher — Then
home — dinner — Jim feeling but little
better — Hale came to see him this [illegible]
Tonsilitis — After dinner worked awhile
at taking down curtain rods & putting
them up again properly — Then took
to Mrs. Greene's — Choral club rehearsal
I worked on Etching paper writing
until Charl came in to upbraid me
for not being present at meeting of [illegible]
Lance Creek Cattle Co. — this P.m. —
forgotit — at 10 went for Annie

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 23, 1891

Heavy rain last night — a warm, muggy, sunny day — very enervating — Had
all my Xmas errands to do besides some
work at the house — it was 11.15 before
I got to studio — No work on Rey plate
passing over the sky — Home after 1 o'clk.
After lunch I rode down to K. & V's in
canal st. & got 2nd working p[roof] from Rey
plate — also p[roof] from the Gifford plate
for Etching club — It was nearly 5
before I got to studio — & 6 when I
got home to dinner — Eve'f. Annie
went with Rosalie Butler to the
Opera — I sent a $5.00 check to Herrick
for his Xmas — Geo. Cook came in
after I had written awhile on my
Etching paper & he helped me until
10 o'clk. hanging paintings in the
parlor — I got to bed about 11.
