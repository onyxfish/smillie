## Left Page

### May, Monday 4, 1891

Very pleasant day — To studio at about 10 — Everything about the bldg in a terrible condition, an army of men tumbling down the east end of it as rapidly as possible — dismayed to find my water supply cut off. Went in to see Mr. Nietchell & had two pails sent in & instructions given to keep me supplied — Then to work biting & etching on the Lippincott plate. Home to lunch soon after 1 — Annie & Kate had ret'd. from New Haven. To studio before 3 — biting & doing odd jobs about — After 5 went to Bridgeman's & acting for Charl, got a lot of flower seeds & mailed them to Mary — Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eve'g feeling very stupid I lay down up on the lounge & had a nap — At 9.30 Charl came in but I left him with Kate & went to bed before 10.

Nellie went to Ridgefield to-day with her cook — Geo. & the children to follow to-morrow.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 5, 1891

Cold, windy day — Got to studio at 10.5 & at once to work biting the Lippincott Etching — Spending the time while waiting for varnish to dry, or while the plate was in the acid, in fixing etching proofs. At 12 went to Safe Deposit — got Wil. Lake Shore & Western Income bonds & went to Bk. of Commerce away down in Nassau St — got interest — had the bonds registered — then again to 2d Nat. Bk & deposited interest — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Katie Adams arr'd. this m'g. Back to studio at 3 — Carlton Chapman called — finished biting & cleaned plate after 5 o'clk. & after all, found that I had not bitten nearly as strong as I wished — Home at 6.30 — Kate S— late to dinner — she had been to Greenwood with Charl. Eve'g. wrote a note to Herrick — also to Amos Nicholes, Cashr. of Montrose Bank. To bed before 10.
