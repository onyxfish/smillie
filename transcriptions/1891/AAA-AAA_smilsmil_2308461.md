## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 29, 1891

A brilliant, beautiful day — All of
the mtfs. as usual, spent out of doors.
Mr. Hosey came up with a number of
friends & wandered across the place.
At noon I went into the studio
& did a very little before dinner.
In the afternoon I painted for a
little while on 2½ × 3½" & at 4.30
we went out riding — called for &
got Miss Foshay. We rode out to
Hart lake & saw an engine & train
on the road about half a mile this
side of the lake. Back at 6.30 —
Miss F. took tea with us and
left soon after — Evelf with
the inevitable newspaper until
after 9 — then a walk on piazza
& to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 30, 1891

Overcast with light sprinkles of
rain from time to time — It was
nearly 2 o'clk. before the rain came
heavily & about 3 o'clk. a thunder-
storm from the west drove back the
easterly rain & there was a sultry
clearing with a sun shining shower or
two afterwards. As I had passed
a very uncomfortable night and felt
uneasy about the head I kept out of
doors all day except when it rained
working with plants — During the
rain I took a nap & worked for
some time at laying a meggotint
ground upon a 7" × 10" plate. Evelf
I read a little while — then, as my
head bothered me I walked on the
piazza & to bed soon after 10.
