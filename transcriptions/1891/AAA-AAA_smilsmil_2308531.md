## Left Page

### December, Friday 18, 1891

A fine winter day — 10.30 when
I got to studio. I worked biting
on Rey plate — At 12 I went to
Photogravure Co's & saw Mr. Edwards
took the Gifford negative Re° — this
my f. — then to 2° Nat. Bnk. — then to
Kurtz's & paid $20 for the Shirlaw photo.
Then in to Am. Art Ass°. Galleries and
took a friend took thro. the collection
of works by W.T. Smedley — W.H. Gibson
Mr. & Mrs. Rob. Sewell & Stokes — Home
at 1.30 — near 3 when I ret° to studio.
Mielatz & Schelling used up part
of my short afternoon — Bit as long
as I could see — then out & tried to
do some Xmas shopping — Too
many women on the same errand.
Then went to Ortgies gallery — last
day of 2° Annual Exhib. of Water Color
Club — met Hassam, Miss McChesney
& others there — was not favorably
impressed by the exhib. Home at
6 — dinner — Eve'g, wrote letter
to Heimcke — Charle came in &
stayed until after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 19, 1891

A very pleasant day — sunny & mild.
My many home duties kept me
so that it was 10.30 when I got to the
studio — I got to work at once up on
the biting of the sky of Rey plate.
By noon it was finished and plate
cleaned — At 1 took camera to 26° St.
& got a couple of shots at Tower of
Madison Sq. Garden. Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & spent the rest
of daylight burnishing & working
with dry-point on Rey. At 4.30
to Geo's studio — Charle there — we
walked up to C's room & saw his
new arrangement of electric light.
Then a short walk with Geo. & home
at 6 — dinner — Eve'g, was
heavy, sleepy & uncomfortable &
got to bed early.
