## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 1. 1891.

Village. Improvement Soct
and Mrs. Gill. Parki's Masquerade.

Overcast wt. with some slight attempts at rain - from midday until about 3 o'clk. Thunder - after which time it cleared charmingly without rain. I got to work in studio on jade plate but about 11 went down town to Rep. office with [illegible] of V. I. Soct. Constitution - more work on jade plate before dinner. Some time with carpenter on new barn after dinner — a little more work on my plate — a short nap — then I went down town, taking Jim & Ralph with me. Went to Judge Searle & McColleum. Home at 6 - tea - then dressed - Took Bessie Throop down to Parki's then to Universalist Ch. bldg. - First Public meeting of the V. I. Socts — hoped for a crowd but it was a sorry, slim affair, much to my mortification. Did not begin business until near 8.30 & got through by 9.15 then many of us adjourned to Mrs. Parki's masquerade party — The young people had lots of fun but I did not — I was too much out of humor at losing to the V. I. fizzle — Annie got herself into a costume without any of us knowing it but her fun was spoiled by the too early removal of masks — Katie and Bessie were in costume — It was about 10 o'clk when at last we got home and 1.30 when we got to bed.

2 hs on jade plate.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 2. 1891.

Lovely day. Near 9 o'clk, when we had breakfast. After seeing to things out of doors I got to work on jade plate still cleaning — just before noon walked down to new R.R. to see the grading work now being done on the site for the new station. — Back & worked until dinner. After that, out at new barn directing work there — then downtown & met Judge Searle & A.B. Smith by appointment and talked on the proposed improvements on Public Ave. Got back to the house at 4.30. Annie, Kate & Katie (with me) went out riding. Home at 6. Tea at 6.30 — At 8 I went down town met again Searle & Smith & a little later Smith & I went into the Town Council meeting where action was to be taken upon the Pub. Ave. help. A com. was appointed & then I went home. To bed before 10.
