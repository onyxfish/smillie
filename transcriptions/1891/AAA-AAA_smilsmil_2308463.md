## Left Page

### August, Sunday 2, 1891

Overcast wtf., clearing delightfully in
the afternoon — R'cls. at 9 — after that I
cut some fine heads of Henderson lettuce
& at 10.30 went down town with Jim &
Ralph & left specimens with A.B. Bam[s]
& W.F. Parke — Then home, after walking
along High St. to see where the grading
for the new R.R. is going on. Then Annie
& I went to the garden & gathered the
vegetables for our dinner — Before that
time, wrote a couple of notes — Dinner
at about 2 — after I had a short time
to read & at 4 o'clk. out riding with
Annie & the boys — had a very pleasant
breezy time & got back at 7.15 lea.
Evef., a little Reading — then wrote
a letter to George & at 10 o'clk. went
to P.O. with my mail.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 3, 1891

*miss Joshay came*

Pleasant wtf., overcast afternoon —
I had intended to make an early
start on my jade plate, but just as
I got seated to it Ben Mulford came
to see my garden — so I went over the
place with him — Before he left, Mrs.
Garst & Mrs. Quackenbush came & I
took them into the studio — & then Mrs.
Schafer came — & then dinner time
came & all the wtf. had gone —
In the afternoon — a carpenter came
to work on new stable under Herrick's
direction — I worked on jade plate
& commenced to lay over it a light, full
ground with very fine tool. About 4.30
we went out riding — calling for Miss
Joshay, who, after the ride, returned
home with us, the Sargnes [illegible] having
come for her & she not being yet ready to
leave Montrose — Evef. some chatting
reading & newspapers and to bed
about 10.30

*worked on jade plate 2 h.*
