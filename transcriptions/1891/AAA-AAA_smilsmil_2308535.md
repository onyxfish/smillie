## Left Page

### December, Monday 28, 1891

Brilliant, beautiful day — got to studio soon after 10, completed the etching on Rey plate & about had it ready for the acid — wrote some letters & home at 4 o'clk — after a short call from Thos. Moran — Found Ralph quite sick — asleep & feverish. Back to studio at 2 [illegible]. Took camera & went to Mad. Sq. & got four shots at Stanford White's Tower. Then to studio & continued biting as long as I could see without finishing. Alex Schilling came in & left his Etching club accts to be audited. Before 5 went to Lord & Taylor's & tried on some wrappers or gowns. Home before 6 — Ralph seems [illegible] brighter altho' Ther. shows 102°. Eve'g I wrote articles on Etching Club paper — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 29, 1891

A cloudy, windy m'g — dashes of rain commenced at about 2 o'clk — a gale of wind & rain at night. Got to studio about 10 and finished biting on Rey plate, using some nitric & water & getting some by-lines — went to Bank & attended to some of Annie's business — home at 1 — back at 2.30 — cleaned plate & burnished as long as I could see. Ordered frames at D. B. Butler's for Rey Proof & for 6" x 12" dry-point "Hollyhocks" — dinner. Evening, at 8 took A. to her Choral Club. Drove home & wrote Etching paper until 10 — then went for A. — on getting back found that my keys were in another coat — the girls had gone to bed & it was impossible to arouse them — at last I had to break a glass in the front room & let myself in.
