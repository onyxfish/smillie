## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 31. 1891.

Dull day — some snow fell about 4 o'clk
& later rain set in, falling freely in
the evef. Up early & with Jno. & Ralph
went to the grocer's & butcher's before hbf.
Called on Geo. & found him very miserable
— bunged up with a cold — To studio at 9.45
& to work painting on Charl's picture. I
went up to Geo.'s studio at 11 & gave his
class a little time. Home at 1.30 back
at 3 — went in to Tiffany's & with Mr. Jones
looked over a lot of glass — I want some for
Montrose. — Then to studio — painted awhile
in 2.30 — Afternoon. Pmd's outlet. Montrose —
When I left at about 6 o'clk, found S.R.
Koehler's card in my sign at front door —
where some imp had turned my tablet
"out". Went over to see George — I am
concerned about him — He does not
seem to be as well as he was yesterday &
his cold appears to be increasing. Home
at 6.30 — dinner — Evef wrote a note
to S.R.K. & posted it. Then lounged about
& to bed before 10.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 1. 1891.

Clouded all day — with raw east wind.
To grocer's & butcher's, with Ralph, before hbf.
To George's at 9.30 — found him somewhat
better in some respects than yesterday, but
still very weak. Then to studio — Before I
got to work, S.R. Koehler came in and
we spent the m'f together discussing
everything. Home to lunch at 1 — back
at 2.30 — then to work upon Charl's picture
& repainted all the foreground, throwing
a shadow across it. Cleaned up at 4.30
& then to George's — then walked up
Mad. Ave. to 57th St — back by 5th Ave. home.
Dinner at 6.30 — Evef, read awhile
in Century for April — Charl came in
— left about 10.15 — I got to bed soon
after.
