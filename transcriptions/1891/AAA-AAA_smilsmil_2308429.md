## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 26, 1891

Muggy mt., threatening showers — one
came bet. 3 & 4 P.m - after which it was
cloudy, raw & breezy. To studio about 10
& got to work taking down curtains,
dusting & folding them & putting things
in shape for departure. — wrote letter
to Heinck & mailed it on my way
home to lunch at 1. Back at 2.30
& worked until after 4. Then to Kurtz
about Etching Club bill. Then to Shirlain's
— saw him — Then to Col. Turner's & saw him
for a moment — & then home about 6 &
rested until dinner at 6.30 — Euef
Geo. came in for a few minutes — I read
awhile — then took down paintings in
the parlor & packed them away. To
bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 27, 1891

Pleasant day — at times overcast and quite
cool. After bkfst. I wrote notes to Kate —
to Schilling & to Kurtz. It was 10.30 when I
got to the studio — continued taking down
& cleaning curtains & after 12 went to Bnt
& there arranged Annie's income for June.
Home at 1.30 — After lunch went to DeNumin's
in Lafayette Pl. & saw Mr. T. S. Del — concerning
Etching Club Publication for next year — a
very pleasant interview. On my way up
town stopped in at Dunning's & looked
at some curtain stuffs for Montrose Studio
— then to Johnston's & Faulkner's & bought
some remnants. To studio at 5.
wrote note to Miss Dalzell & to Seward
Gifford — Home at 6.30 — dinner —
Euef. Chase came in & stayed until
after 10. To bed before 11.
