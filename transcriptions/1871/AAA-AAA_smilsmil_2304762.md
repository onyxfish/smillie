## Left Page

### Sept. Sunday, 17 1871.

Weather still unsettled and threatening — went to Presb'n Ch with Matt — Met Fan Mulford at the door — Sat with her — W. Miller gave us a sermon from Proverbs — Dinner at Uncle Wm's at 12 o'clk — at 2 o'clk, Rolled into my bed and slept until 4 — About 4,30 went up to the Fair Ground with Matt & Geo. On way back called on Mr. Bard — Tea at 6 — At 8 went to Jarbell's \+ made arrangements with him to escort Matt & Chick \+ trunks over the Erie — Then went to Fan's — There until 9,30 — got from her Copies of the "Mail" with three more of my letters — Home at 10 \+ Read until 11. To bed at 12 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Monday, 18 1871.

Cool and almost cloudless — a sort of weather breeder. Up early. Bfst. at 8 clk. and Matt got off in 8 o'clk stage to go over to the Erie R.R. and so home. I spent the whole m'g trying to get a telegraphic message home, that Charl might meet Matt on the train — At last I wrote a note and sent it down to Case, at depot, by stage \+ had my message sent on from there — Dinner at 1 — Spent some time arranging traps, when Fan M. came with horse \+ buggy \+ me went off riding for the rest of the afternoon — Back to tea at 6. Went tried to do a little reading but so sleepy that I went to bed at 9 o'clk.
