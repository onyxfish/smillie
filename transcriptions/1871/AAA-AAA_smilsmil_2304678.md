## Left Page

### May, Thursday 18, 1871

Glorious day \- got to studio at 9 o'clk. Rebiting on engine die (6 days) Fan Mulford came in about 11.30 \- Stayed until 12.45 \- walked to Park ave \+ 3d\. with her \- then home to lunch \- went to Schmitt's to see about sketching bag. Back to Studio at 2.30 \- Etched crossing, bit line \+ worked with graver on die until 6.30 \- Had a call from a Singular personage J. W. See. \- Maury Tweedy came in about 4.30 \- At 6.30 \- again went to Schmitts \- Back to Studio \- Home at 7.45 \- After tea \- until 10 o'clk \- mounting drawings \- got to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### May, Friday 19, 1871

Another Glorious day \- Got down-town at som after 9 o'clk \+ at work on engine die (7 days) finishing and getting pfs. Spent an hour with Goodale Talking new drawings \- at 1 o'clk went with Father to lunch, After went to Congdon \+ Crouch's \- Frank was \+ bought bag \- to Flung Smith \+ McNaulton \+ back to work at 3 \- Home with Father at 5.30 \- Stopped at Studio \- there until 7.30, washing \+ varnishing mo\- at Mr \- "Lifting of the Clouds" \- for which Mr. Wilson offered me $4.00 \- yesterday \- Tea after 8 o'clk \- went to Burgraff's got boots \- Home at 9 \- Read english papers and to bed at 10.45
