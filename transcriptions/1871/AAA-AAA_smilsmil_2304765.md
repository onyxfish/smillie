## Left Page

### November Friday, 10 1871.

Raining and dark — used up with a headache and did not get out of my bed until about 7 o'clk P.M. — then loafed in my big chair until 3 a.m. when I went to bed —

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 11 1871.

Very windy \+ quite cold — nearly 10 o'clk when I got to the Studio, feeling very un-like work. Fussed about generally \+ worked some on water-color sketch for Miss Jones — nearly 2 when I got home to lunch \+ almost 3 when I got back to Studio. Worked as long as I could see on Body-color sketch "Tenaya Canon \+ Glacier Rock. Home to tea at 6 o'clk — Eveg. at work mounting Summer oil studies — got to bed at 12.30.
