## Left Page

### June, Thursday 1, 1871

a delightful cool day — got up rather headachy, but weathered it through. Up t'als Early mf, after bkfst, getting accts \+ money matters straight. At 10, went to barbers, Studio — 3d ave, Savings Bnk, Carpenters etc, \+ home to lunch abt 1,30 — At 2,30 went down town, got Ret tickets, went to Equitable, Continental — \+ Travelers Ins. Co's to Ring Smith, Seitz \+ [illegible] B.N. Co — Home at 5,30 — Hard at work until 11,15 when I finished packing \+ got to bed about 11 \+0

## Right Page

### June, Friday 2, 1871

Logan House — Room 36 — Altoona —

Up at 6 — bkfst — Goodbyes \+ at foot of Desbrosses St at 8,10 — baggage checked \+ over to Jrsy City — Secured first rate seats in Pullman Parlor Car \+ went through finely — The day charming the at times close, clouded \+ thundering — Lunch at Parksburgh about 2 \+ dinner at Harrisburg at 5 — Met Mr. Whittredge on the train on his way to Denver — me stopped here — he went on — Night glorious — got here at 9 — a cup of tea — note home — to Mr. Coleman \+ to Matto — got to bed about 10 45 pretty well tired out.
