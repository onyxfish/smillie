## Left Page

### July, Thursday 13, 1871

Very warm, clear a.m. but afternoon rather cloudy. Got to my work on Yo-Semite fall soon after 8 o'clk \+ got it all painted in by 11 o'clk. Then back to hotel \+ wrote until dinner time at 1.15 — out again at 2.15 for afternoon study Sentinal Rock \+ by working pretty steadily but feeling unwilling \+ unlike it, by 5 o'clk, I had it too covered but very badly. About 5.30 took out Ridgway's little mare \+ was very much pleased with her. Rode for an hour \+ a half or more, down to Cathedral Rocks, forded the River, Rode under El Capitan \+ back by Coulterville trail. After tea, loafing until 9 o'clk. Then washed brushes \+ got to bed at 9.45

## Right Page

### July, Friday 14, 1871

Overcast \+ muggy, feeling very unlike exertion of any kind — Ab't with papers \+ crayons went to meadow between Black's and Hutchings's \+ commenced sketch of Sentinal Rock — Rain sprinkled then sun came out, no umbrella \+ at 10 o'clk back to the hotel, disgusted. Wrote until 1.30 — then dinner — At 3 started for the foot of Small fall South of Sentinal fall — from there climbed along the foot of the wall to the Sentinal fall, tremendous chasm from the water wash — never was in such a mild hole before — an hour coming down, the hardest sort of climbing — got in at 7, dripping wet with perspiration, got two or three sketches — Bathed and toweled thoroughly — tea at 8 o'clk — went on piazza, delightfully comfortable and to bed at 9.30
