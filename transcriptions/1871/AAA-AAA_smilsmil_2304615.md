## Left Page

### January Friday, 6 1871.

Very mild \- Rain commenced about 11 \+ continued until 2 \- Got to Studio about 9.30 \- continued tracing on drawing (7 days) finished it and commenced the Regular outlining with pencil upon the paper \- Home at 1.15 \- Lunch \+ then walked down to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30. Enjoyed the Rehearsal very much \- Walked home with the Tweedys \- went in for a little while \- at 5 went to Snedecors, then to studio \- home to tea at 6.30 \- Evg. very tired \- looked over English papers and to bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### January Saturday, 7 1871.

Splendid clear, cold day \- Got to Studio at 9.15 and got to work on outline of drawing (7th day) getting it finished by 1 o'clk when I went home to lunch. Back at 2.30 \- At 3 went in to Brevoort's \- Perspective class \- Mr. Saml. Coleman came, Saml. being sick with rheumatic headache, \+ made arrangements with me to take her to Phil. concert this evg. Home at 5 \- Read "Tempest" until 7.15 \- walked down to Studio Building \+ took Mr. C. to concert \- Enjoyed "Ocean" (Rubenstein) \+ Ruy Blas, also Barcarole (W. S. Bennett) Rich. Hofman \+ orchestra, exceedingly. After concert \- back with Mr. C. \- went into Ogilvie's Room where he \+ Tom Smith were smoking \- there until 11 \- home at 11.15 \- Read until 12 \- Then to bed \-
