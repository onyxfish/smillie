## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 25, 1871

Miserable, dark \+ Rainy day —
got to studio at 9 o'clk, \+ worked
fairly during the m'g on etching
(7 3/4 days) Home to lunch at
1.30 — back at 2.15 \+ worked
until about 4 o'clk when I
could not see any longer —
Took a nap in the lounge —
Home to tea about 6 o'clk —
everg rewriting minutes
of Chicago Relief com. meeting
Read from 10 until 11.30
then to bed —

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 26, 1871

Still disagreeable, mizzling and
muggy weather. My stomach
very uncomfortable — got to the
studio about 9.30 \+ worked on
etching (8 1/4 days) until after 1
o'clk — Started home for lunch,
met Alfred Jones in the street — a
long chat with him — lunch about
2 o'clk — back at 2.45 — and at 4
had finished etching \+ had it
ready for biting. Worked awhile
charcoaling on sketch that I
expect to paint for Chicago fund.
Home at 5.75 — tea at 6 — at 7,
went to N.A. of D. Committee meeting
Chicago Fund — left at 9 o'clk —
Home \+ Read until 10.30 — then
to bed —
