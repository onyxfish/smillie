## Left Page

### December Thursday, 14 1871.

Fine wintry day — some very slight snow flurries — Got to studio at 9.45 \+ right to work on a sketch for Miel Saitz 10×14 in. — Belle \+ Mary Tweedy called at about noon — abt. 30 home to lunch, back at 2.15 \+ worked as long as I could see — at 5 went to Sauthiers — There until 6 — on my way back stopped at Studio \+ home to tea at 7. Evelf, Revising letter for "The Mail" To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### December 01 Friday, 15 1871.

Overcast \+ threatening snow — Got to studio at 9 o'clk. \+ to work on one fmy Yo-Semite Studies — at it until 1.30 — then prepared lunch — ate it \+ at 2 started for Phil. Rehearsal which I enjoyed very much — after, went to Shark's, in 6th Ave — and paid bill, then to Studio at 5 o'clk — Home to tea at 6.15 — Evelf, tried to write an "Mail" letter but could not. Read, went up to Burgraff's \+ "fussed" until [illegible — ~3 words]
