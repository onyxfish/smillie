## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 7 1871.

Up at 6. after a poor night. Bkfst at
and at 8.35 got off in stage for Lake
Tahoe. Stopped at Fish Ranche. 4½ mile
from Truckee. 800,000 Mt. Lake Trout — after
an uninteresting ride got to the lake co
after 11 and at 11.45. I unice Matt got &
I started in little steamboat Emerald for
Glenbrook — 13 miles, on the other side of
Lake — The water a beautiful blue. the
atmosphere so much the same color that
we could not see the most distant mts.
and hardly the nearer ones — Got to Glen
brook at 1.40 — went up to hotel — Saw
Hank Monk, the stage driver and had
lunch. Back to boat off at 2.40 and
after a really delightful sail got back
to Tahoe City at 4.40 — atmospheric
effects — Ripples & Reflections, Charming.
Got to Truckee at 7.45. Cold, dirty
and tired — had tea — then [illegible]
settlements — last Sacramento and
adicus — At 11 o'clk from towns
along, me soon got into quarters
all right. Away at 11.25 and
to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 8 1871.

*C.P.R.R. Sleeping Car*

After a tolerable nights Rest. up
at 6 o'clk — just in time for
bkfst — at Humboldt at 6.45 — at
bkfst table, to our great surprise
met the Dawes party — they
expressed equal surprise and
pleasure at meeting us. They are
in next car. The mill [illegible]
visiting and snoozing — Did not
take dinner at the dinner place
Sage brush wilderness and fine
mts. beyond until about 2 o'clk
then Canyons, Palisades etc.
until near 6 when we came
out to a great valley and the
Humboldt Range — Dinner at
Elko at 4.30 — Hannibal Hamlin
tribe on my left; very agreeable.
Misses Smith & Dawes called on
us in early evening. I got
to bed before 9 o'clk.
