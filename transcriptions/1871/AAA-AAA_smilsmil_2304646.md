## Left Page

### March Saturday, 11 1871.

Wr pleasant, mizzle during P.M.
\+ Rain at night — Got to studio at
9 o'clk, \+ to work fitting "Colorado
arms" (3½ days). Between times
while waiting for my varnish to
dry, Rebit on "Niagara" — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — Aunt Jessie & Emma
there — back at 2.10 — Hard at it
all P.M. — going in to Perspective
lesson for about 20 m — only — Got
by fitting done \+ die cleaned off
at 8.10 — Did not get home to tea —
until 7.45 — Ed. Parker there —
Welf spent until 11 o'clk —
Studying \+ drawing anatomy —
To bed about 11.20

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 12 1871.

Heavy Rains at Church time — got
to Hastings' a little late \+ heard him
preach from Numbers 21st Ch, H. v. —
last clause — vigorous \+ good, as usual.
at noon, high wind, ruled \+ no Rain,
With Charl, went up to A.V.V.'s to
dinner. Miss Young \+ Mr Whiting (young
folks) Romaine \+ Wm Vault, \+ Gil there
to dinner. Very heavy Rain \+ wind
clearing over at [illegible] — giving a bright
sunset. Walked about some, out of
doors. Got home about 6.30 — Charl came
down in carriage with Miss Y. \+ Whiting
at 9 o'clk — Welf I did not go out
but amused myself with Layard's
Nineveh — To bed a little
before 11.
