## Left Page

### March Friday, 17 1871.

more horrible weather \- Up with a headache \- got to Studio at 9 \- at 9.30 my lambs began to come along Miss Ripley (15) Tiffany (14) Maury (9) Jones (9) Whiting (8) a young girl came about 10 o'clk \+ Misses Tiffany, Ripley \+ Whiting drew, in alt. from her head \- I Rec'd \- a pleasant letter from Dr Lewis \- at 12.15 I gave up \- \+ begging permission, beat a Retreat \- leaving the ladies to lock up while I went home and to bed \- Intense pain \- Up about 9 o'clk \- heavy Rain \- To bed about 4 a.m. Emma Bourne at the house over night.

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 18 1871.

Heavy Rain during the m'g, clearing up about noon \- got to Studio just before 9 o'clk \- found my fire out, kindled it, then to work on drawing (3½ days) getting along with it very slowly as my head is very uneasy \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back a little after 2 \- Stole a little time for die j"Niagara" \- Perspectives from 3 to 4 o'clk \- Mr. White (son of Norman White) \+ two boys came in a little before 6 \- pleasant call \- Home to tea at 7 \- at 8.30 got to the Tweedie's \+ visited there until 10.30 \- Home at 11 \- Reading anatomy until near midnight \- then to bed \-
