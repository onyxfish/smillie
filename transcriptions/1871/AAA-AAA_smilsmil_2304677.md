## Left Page

### May Tuesday, 16 1871.

fine day. got to studio at 9.15
\+ to work on Engine die (4th day)
with graver. Worked until near
1.30 then home to lunch. Rode
down to Fred Durand's. He looked
at my mouth — nothing needed, then
to Sauthier's. then to Schaus's \+
Snedecor's where I selected brushes
\+ paper for Ferguson. then back to
studio at 3.30 \+ worked hard
until 6.30. Mf Fan Mulford
called. P.M. Mrs Forbes — At
7 o'clk went to 21, 2, 3qd \+ saw
Fan M. for a few minutes. home
to Tea at 7.45 — soon after, Belle
Mary \+ Florence I came around
\+ spent the evef. Heavy Thunder
Storm. took them home through the
Rain at 11 o'clk. to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### May Wednesday, 17 1871.

overcast mf with slight Rain
\+ promise of more, but cleared before
noon with cloudless \+ very warm P.M.
got to A.B.N.Co. about 9.30 — got [illegible]
of Engine die (5 days) \+ had Rebitring
ground laid. called on Corgdon \+ cousin
about R.R. ticket. Back to studio at
12 o'clk. Home to lunch at 1½ o'clk.
back at 2.30. Misses J (24) M (19) \+
M (18) there; last Lesson. Misses M. \+
J. paid me $150.00 \+ expressed perfect
satisfaction. Miss Jones wishes to buy a
little drawing. At 7.15 at Mr. Garrjutaus
took Tea — visited with Fan M.
until 9.30 — then home — to bed
at 11.
