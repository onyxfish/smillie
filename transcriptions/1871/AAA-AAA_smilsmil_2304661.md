## Left Page

### April, Monday 10, 1871.

Tremendeously hot day \- Ther. $82°. in Studio \- Overslept myself \+ did not get to studio until 9.45 worked all day transferring \+ outlining "Elaina" drawing \- (1/2 day) \- Home to lunch at 1.40 \- back at 2.20 \- a Mr. Fred. Spooner with intro n from Robt. Dodge called. Then Wenzler took me in to see his great picture, just finished worked on my drawing until 6.30 \- Home to tea at 7 \- went to Monthly Recpt n Y.M.C.a. at 8.30 \- Home at 10.15 \- Read newspaper awhile \+ to bed at 11 \-

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 11, 1871.

overcast day \- wind N.E. \+ Rapidly growing cooler \- got to Studio at 8.40 \+ to work "Elaina" drawing (1/2 days) Dechamp brought up clay figure horse \- I am to give him $75.00 for it \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.20 \+ worked until 6.15 \- Then brushed up about the Room \+ home to tea at 7 o'clk \- Found there Uncle Wm \+ aunt H \- just from Florida. Rec'd. long letter from Fan Mulford quite a heavy thunderstorm about 9 o'clk \- Spent trying to get accts straight and to bed about 11 \-
