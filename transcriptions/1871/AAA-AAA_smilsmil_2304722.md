## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 8, 1871.

Up Early, Gil \+ I had bkfst alone \- get off to work before 8 o'clk \- but with something of a headache, went to my study of Sentinal Rock as seen from down the valley (2d sittg) worked until 1.30 when I got it finished. Then took my lunch \+ came up to a point previously selected \+ commenced study of Red Wood Trees, my head growing worse, but I stuck to it until the sun light left, about 5 o'clk \- then dragged back to the hotel, and right to bed \- got up about 1.30 A.M., washed out a brush or two, then undressed \+ went regularly to bed where I lay awake until about going dawn.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 9, 1871.

Up \+ bkfst about 7.30 \- The Ashburner, Dawes, Brown party left for [illegible] this A.M. \- Bid them good bye and soon after 8 o'clk went to the River where I Rubbed in a study of water; Reflections, color, etc \- back to hotel at 1.30 \- dinner \+ then to another place on the River where I Rubbed in another study of water \- worked until near 5 \- Eve'g with Gil walked up to Hutchings's for mail \- got a lot of papers \+ a short letter from Matt enclosing one from Fanny K \- dated Scroom Lake, July 26th, long, the painter walked part way home with us \- got to bed about 10.45
