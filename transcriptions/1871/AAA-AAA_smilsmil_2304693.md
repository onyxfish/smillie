## Left Page

### June Saturday, 17 1871.

Up soon after sunrise after a tolerably good nights rest, out on rear platform as we passed Green River City. got two or three sketches of the peculiar bluffs, so beautiful in color. Amused by hand car load of [illegible] men hitched on to us. Not a cloud — got to Bryan + breakfast at 7.50. Spent the A.M. working in Water Color and writing a letter home. Amused and interested by many Indians that we saw by the way — got to Wahsatch, to dinner at 1.30 — at 2 started with "observation Car" — Rode on't through Echo + Weber Canyons. The "Weber" I enjoyed. Got to Ogden about 4.45 — Changed Cars for Salt Lake + off at 5.30 — Salt Lake City at 7.30 + at once to Townsend House — Tea — + at 9 o'clk. to Theatre — very funny — everything so poor + common — Pors. Young was there — we left before things were over, at 10.40 — To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### June Sunday, 18 1871.

*Townsend House. Salt Lake City
Room 62 —*

Up about 7.30, [illegible] + lounged about until near 10 o'clk — then went to the Tabernacle + heard Brs. Bywater harangue — an ignorant, wordy and self opinionated man — then Elder Wood Ruff, a much stronger man, but a bad speaker. Back to hotel — Immensely hot. Ther. 90°. Young Chadwick met us at 2 o'clk went to Tabernacle with us. Sacrament — Orson Pratt preached — evidently a genuine and conscientious enthusiast — after service, sauntered out beyond Young's Residence + up on to the "Bench" — a height, a spur of the mts. where we had a charming view over the city + out to the lake — Tea about 7 + as the evg. was very warm, much of it was spent under the trees in front of the Hotel —
