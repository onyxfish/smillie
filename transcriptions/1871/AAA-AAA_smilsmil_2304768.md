## Left Page

### November Thursday, 16 1871.

Snowy, blowy \+ miserable generally. Got to studio at 9.30 and the whole mng was spent clearing up the disorder \+ dirt left by Curry. \- discussing with Derby his offer to the N.Y. artists Chicago Relief fund, $5000 for their pictures \+ writing to Leavitt, Shattuck \+ Lang. Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.15 \+ worked as long as I could see on Japanese drawing for C.B.N.Co. (1 day) Wm Craig called. Home to tea at 6 \- Werf, finished mounting my studies \- stretched drawing paper \+ mounted the Japanese drawing.
To bed at 10. 45

## Right Page

### November Friday, 17 1871.

Glorious day \- Got to studio at 9.30 and worked on Japanese drawing until 1.15 \- Miss Jones, a sister \+ Miss Maury called. Miss J. took her Water Color Sketch \+ paid me for it very handsomely. \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- walked down to Phil. Rehearsal \- met Fanny \+ Woods Kimball at the Rehearsal. The Tweedys \- After Rehearsal \- stopped in at Snedder's \- then to studio at 3 o'clk \- In at Bogardus's to show him my High Sierra Photos. Home to tea at 6 \- Werf, divided \- Writing, Will Black \+ his wife \+ writing to J.J. Reddy. \- [illegible] To bed at 11 \- [illegible — ~4 words]
