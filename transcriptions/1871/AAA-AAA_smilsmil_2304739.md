## Left Page

### October Friday, 13 1871

Charming day — got down town at 9.30 — Ruled close, up right line on die — dragged ruled line on face of Shields and bit in — Did not get out to lunch until 3 o'clk — Then with Chare went to Produce Exchange to see the building — on my way back stopped at Kurtz's \+ lunched — Back to office at 3.45 \+ worked with graver \+ dry point until 5 — then walked up to Brooks Bros — then Rode home — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g wrote to Mama D'iven — at 8.30 Tired \+ spread out on Sofa — Up at 11 — \+ wrote until near 1 — Then to bed —

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 14 1871

Another charming day — got down town at 9.30 — worked a while on die — had it proved \+ passed. Then fussing with Gavit about work — at last decided that I commence "etching" Chasing Ostriches" Set to work outlining dag. — at 1.30 went to lunch, after calling at the Con. Bonk N. Co. to tell them that some time, in a month or two, I will do the piece of engraving that so long I have promised them — Rode up to Sauthier's — then to Snedecor's, who showed me his foreign pictures, then Brooks Bros, then Studio worked at taking up cartoon — Home to Tea at 6.30 — Letter from Father — all well in Montreal — Eve'g, studying over photo's arranging oil studies to mount and writing a letter to Hiram Ferguson of Albany — To bed soon after 11 —
