## Left Page

### April, Thursday 20, 1871.

Mild, showery day \- Got to studio at 9 o'clk \- \+ to work on "mule, S.a," drawing, (2½ days). Dr. F.W. Lewis called bet. 11 \+ 12 \+ stayed until 1 o'clk \- Home to lunch at 1,40 \- worked on George's drawing (Japan) for the Continental Bank Note Co. Got to studio at 3,15 \- Robt. Dodge called, worked steadily until after 6 \- Louis Tiffany called \+ I walked upo with him \- Home to tea at 7 o'clk \- Swe'f went to St. Cloud Hotel \+ visited with the Tiffanys who are there for a short time \- Home at 10,30 \+ to bed at 11 \-

## Right Page

### April, Friday 21, 1871.

Very mild, beautiful m'f \- went with father to Dr. Noryes' \+ had a second examination of his ear \- Got to studio at 9,30 \- Soon after Misses P (25) J (18) M (17) \+ no (2) came \- worked with them until [illegible] o'clk. Miss Ripley paid me $79.50 \+ bid us good bye \- Home to lunch at 1,30 \- Phil, Rehearsal at 2,30 after \- with Belle \+ Mary Tweedy \+ all went to Hy. J.D. There met Schuyler \- left at 6 o'clk \- walked to St. Cloud Hotel with the [illegible] \- Home to tea at 6,30 \- at 8 went to St. Cloud Hotel and called on Mrs. Porter \+ Miss Annie Lowden. Home at 9,40 \- Read paper until 10,30 to bed at 10,45 \- Heavy thunder shower while at Philharmonic \- Shower again in the ev'f.
