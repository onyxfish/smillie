## Left Page

### October Sunday, 1 1871.

Up at 6.30 - suffering from what seemed to me very like neuralgia of the stomach. Got in to Jersey City at 8 o'clk - and am home at 9.15 - walked into the house, surprising the folks - added to my pains, a headache commenced - so I rolled myself in a blanket + slept all the m'f - after dinner showed my photographs to father - then gave way to my headache again + did not get up from the sofa until about 2 o'clk a.m. when I went to bed -

## Right Page

### October Monday, 2 1871.

Beautiful day - Up at 7 - feeling very slimpsy - Spent the m'f in getting traps + things out of my trunk + arranging my Room in grand disorder - Lunch at 1.15 - Started for down-town at 2.30 - went at once to the A.B.N. Co - very pleasantly Rec'd - arranged to go to work for them at once - I must have money — went over the new part of the building - Home with father at 5 — Eve'g shewing sketches etc, to family \+ trying to arrange accts, until midnight - got to bed about 12 o'clk.
