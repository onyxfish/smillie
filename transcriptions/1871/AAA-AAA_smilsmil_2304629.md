## Left Page

### February Friday, 3 1871.

Glorious day \- got to Studio at 9.10 \- cleared up \+ got ready for my "lambs". They came about 9.45 or nearly 10 \- late \- excuse that Louis Tiffany was in his way up the Bay. Gave most of my time to them \- working a little with water color on some of my autumical drawings \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30 \- After Rehearsal walked with Mary \+ Jenny Tweedy to Mrs. Sam Tweedy, then went looking for the home of a began in East 14th St. He lied to me \- At 5 o'clk went to N.A.A.D \- there met Miss Julia Black \+ had pleasant chat \- then to Studio \- Home to tea at 6.45 and learned to my amazement that Uncle Frank Bourne died this A.M. about 10.15 \- Hemorrhagica Variolis \- or Bloody Small Pox \- a dreadful thing \- The authorities will not allow a funeral \- Aunt Margaret came over during the evening \+ we had a scene concerning yesterdays performance. Got Uncle A. makes me a proposition to go to California \- got to bed about 11.15

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 4 1871.

Clear, cold day \- got to Studio soon after 9 o'clk \- the whole m.f. taken up with callers \- long discussions with Burling who is anxious to have a tilt with \+ at art critics \+ so has written a letter to Tournly "the mail" to publish. Then a call from Uncle A.V.V. who detailed his California proposition to me \- simply, to pay passage there \+ back \+ my board for one month in San Francisco \- Then Corinne Olena Smith called \- worked a little on drawing of locomotive then home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.45 \- Perspective from 3 to 4 \- After that calls from J. F. Cropsey \+ C.C. Ward \- Alex Dunn called A.M. \- At 6 o'clk. walked down to Tweedys to say to Belle that I could not go with her to the Union League Theatre. Home at 7 \- Tea at 8 at Academy of Music Phil. concert, enjoyed the Mozart \+ "Sakuntala" \- Very cold night \+ glorious moonlight \- walked home with Emma Ireland \+ Fanny Kimball \- Went in to the K's \- Home soon after 11 \- looking over English papers and to bed about 12.15 \-
