## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 27 1871.

overcast on getting up in the mf, but clouds broke away, giving a cloudy, breezy day. Bkft. at 8 then arranged odds & ends, so as to get away in the 1 oclk stage. at 10 oclk went "down-town" — bid some good-byes, little "chores" &c — back at 11.30 — wrote a note for au Mulford took it down to P.O. at 12 — back to dinner at 12.15 & off in stage at 1 — At Jot. Carmalt's, Miss Churchill & Mrs Schantz got onto the stage — m escorted them to Utica & Binghamton, & started them for Utica — our Erie train late — got away at 4.40 — got to Elmira at 6.30 — Maud Diven waiting for us with Carriage — Drove to Willow Brook — Dinner about 7.15 —
Spent the every with the family very pleasantly until 11 oclk —
Then to bed —

## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 28 1871.

Bkft. at 8 — Heavily overcast \+ quite cold — Spent the morning riding about Elmira with Maud, Ella & Ger., I driving —. Back soon after 12 — Lunch abt — about 2 off again, Maud, Ger. & I in close Carriage — Called on George Diven — Saw his wife, children & house — Then on Eugene D—, ditto — ditto — Home at 6.15 — Dinner soon after, most of this evening spent in the billiard Room "playing" pool with the girls — To bed at 11 — with the understanding that if it is fair to-morrow, we are all to go to Watkins' Glen —
