## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 22 1871.

Fine day — Got up in the mf with a headache but managed to get to Studio at 9 o'clk. Kindl fine \+ got to work on arms of West Va. \- Mr. Brewer of Rocky Mt. memory came in \+ made us a very pleasant call. \- I was obliged to go home \+ to bed about 1 o'clk. where I stayed until 6 when I got up \+ took tea. Evel. went to The Tailors \+ fussed about at home until shortly after midnight when I went to bed at 2 o'clk. but not to sleep until after 3 —

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 23 1871.

Fine day. Before I went to the studio wrote a letter to A.F. Bellows — Got to studio at 10 o'clk. \+ worked closely all day on Arms of West Va. — Home to lunch at 1.40 — back at 2.30 \+ worked as long as I could see. Louis Tiffany came in just at dusk. Home to tea at 6 — at 7.30 at N.A.D. Chicago Relief Fund Com. Full \+ interesting meeting — How are we to dispose of the money? — At 10 went with Page, Ward, Perry, Beard, Jay \+ Shattuck to the Cor. of 4th Ave. \+ 24th St — where I had a chance to see how those folks enjoyed themselves. Home at 11 — Joe, Will \+ Mrs. Bradley there — got to bed at midnight.
