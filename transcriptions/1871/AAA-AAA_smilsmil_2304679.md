## Left Page

### May, Saturday, 20, 1871

And yet another glorious day — got to Studio at 9 o'clk. Stretched paper \+ worked on pf. of cardie, penciling in a Tunnel at one end. Worked a little on some Water Color sketches \+ commenced blocking in drawing of Seal of State Dp't. of Insurance — Home to lunch at 1.45 — after a visit from Townley who expresses great pleasure at the idea of my writing letter for the Mail". Back at 2.30 and worked until 6.45 when I finished drawing of Seal, Circle, about 10 in. Sq. I did not begin washing on it until 3.45. The quickest work I have done, went to Miller's about umbrella — Home to tea at 7.30 — at 8.30 went to St. Cloud. Home at 10 — at work mounting drawings until near midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday, 21, 1871

Still cloudless \+ beautiful — got up with a mean headache that bothme in all the m'f. Edw. Parker spent the day \+ night with us — At 3.30 P.m. went to St. Cloud with Geo. \+ then with Belle \+ Mary T. went up to St. Thomas's Ch. The music, as usual, charming — After services walked with Belle, Mary not feeling well, Geo. went home with her — I got home at 7 — tea — then wrote notes to Miss Whiting \+ Townley — called T's \+ took Miss W's to her house 253 fifth Ave. — Went to Studio \+ got a number of things that I wanted at home — back at 9 o'clk.
