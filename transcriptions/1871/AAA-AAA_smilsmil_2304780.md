## Left Page

### December Sunday, 10 1871.

Peasant \[illegible\] went to Dr Hastings's \+ heard him preach very earnestly from 11 cor, 6th ch, 2d v, "Now is the accepted time" &tc — but suffered very much with my head — Immediately after service, went to bed where I stayed until near 7 — every lying about — in the lounge until after 5 o'clk A. M. — thus to bed \+up

## Right Page

### December Monday, 11 1871.

at about 8.30 — Did not get to studio until 10 o'clk — Everything in such confusion that it took me all of the mst to arrange things. Tom Fessenden called — So did Schenck — Home to lunch at 2.10 — back at 3 o'clk, Spent what Remained of the afternoon blockding into shape my subject for a water color drawing — "Sunlight in a Shadowed path" Home to tea at 6 — \[illegible\] mounting maps — Joe \+ Will called — got to bed at 11.15
