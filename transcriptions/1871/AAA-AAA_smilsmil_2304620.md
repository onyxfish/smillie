## Left Page

### January, Monday 16, 1871

Mf. gloomy \- noon promising \- P.M. Cool, breezy \+ fair \- My birthday \- don't know how old I am \- Got to studio at 9 o'clk. after stopping at Mr. Tiffany's with a note \- Kindled fire \+ then to work on Water Color study from Nature \- washed out a piece of foreground water \+ put it in again more to my liking \- Home to lunch at 1,10 \- back at 1,40 \- washed an ochre tint on the grey margin surrounding Study then to work in large drawing (14 days) on Rump of horse. etc, Calls from Magrath, Townley \+ Bellows I had not seen B. since his Return from England \- his visit very interesting \& stopped work at 4,45 \- at 5,15 \- on lounge until 6 \- no nap \- Home to tea at 6,20 \- At 7,45, called at Tiffany's \- agreed to take Miss Annie as a pupil \- at 8,15 at Y.M.C.A. \- bought gymnasium boy \- at 8,50 \- at Mary Butler's where I spent a very pleasant eve\[illegible\] until 10,30 \- walked home and to bed at 11 50

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 17, 1871

Glorious clear \+ crisp day \- got to Studio just about 9 \- and at once to work on drawing (15 days) finishing over the horse and part of the rider \- Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2, after going to Knoedler's and getting some paper \- Worked all the P.M. \- and Rec'd Calls from Mrs Feldman \+ baby \- Belle and Mary Treadey. Misses Stewart \+ Mild \+ master Kelley Prentices \- Rec'd letters from Geo. Whitney \+ W.T. Richards \- Walked home with the G's \- then to Sauthier's \- back to Studio \+ worked until past 7 getting glass in frame. Home at 7,30 \- Dr's Sister \- Mr \+ Rieue arr'd \- Well a surprise party to me) to celebrate my birthday (yesterday) \- the Treadeys \- Will, Joe \+ old B. \- Uncle T. \+ Aunt J. \+ Minnie \- Big cake \+ 40 candles \- Some fun \- all over before 11 \- To bed at 11 20
