## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 10, 1871

Brilliant mf, but overcast before noon — Got to Studio's at 9.30 \+ to work on drawing (9½ days) — On the sky — Had it in good shape, but not content to let well enough alone, I spoiled it \+ at night left it in very unsatisfactory condition. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — worked until 4.30 — At 5 went to Avery's to get A. F. Bellows address — then to Christern's then home at 6.15 — Evef Read news papers \+ note to Bellows — W. T. Blodgett — Jos. Jefferson \+ S. C. 7 — got to bed at midnight

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 11, 1871

Overcast, threatening snow and much milder — Got to Studio at 9.30 and to work on drawing (10½ days) All day washing in the general masses of form and color getting it Roughly together. Rec'd a long letter from Ferguson in the morning — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 — Worked as long as I could see — 4.15 — Grannist Fulton called — At 5 o'clk went to Knoedler's and bought a Sable brush — Then to Mrs. Forbes' — 22° 8: 44" ave — and borrowed leather gauntlets — Then home at 6.25 — tea — at 7.35 at N. A. of D. Rimmer's 4th lecture — out at 9.15 — went up to galleries until 9.45 — then to Y. M. C. A. to see about Gymnasium boy — Home at 10.25 — to bed at 11
