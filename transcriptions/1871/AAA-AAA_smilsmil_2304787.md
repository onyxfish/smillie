## Left Page

### December Sunday, 24 1871

a very mild and pleasant day. Went with Kate to D. Hastings to morning service. D.H. preached from Luke II° ch. 40th V. Home to dinner. — a very short nap after + then at 3.30 at St. Thomas where I heard the first part of the service, then walked up to Central Park + about until 6 o'clk. Home to tea — Evng. spent lolling on sofa \+ Reading some newspaper articles. — To bed about 10.45

## Right Page

### December Monday, 25 1871

a very mild, pleasant day. — I stayed at home and all the morning was drawing from Kate or trying to get something from the baby in the way of study for a water-color drawing that I wish to make — Dinner about 1 o'clk — and then again trying to get something from the child but I am very much disappointed, as I find it almost impossible to do anything. Went out and walked between 5 + 6 o'clk — damp + foggy — When I got in, Joe Will + Mimi at the house + a Christmas tree under way for the boy" — fun — got to writing about 10.30 and to bed soon after 11.
