## Left Page

### January, Saturday, 14, 1871

Foggy, muggy and mild — Got to Studio at 9.15 and all day working on drawing (13½ days) going over the ground under + back of horse. Home to lunch abt. 30 after Taking a Run over to George's Room to see the little drawing that he is engaged upon — Back to Studio at 2.15 + worked until 3.45 — Mr. Ch. Knickerbocker of Chicago called — gave commission to make drawing for Western Bl. Note Co. at 3.45, with Sam. Coleman went to Nicoll's Studio — N.C. Board of Control meeting — to fill vacancies of Hanging Com, then to Nehlig's — not in — then to Lauthier's — then to Kelly's — then to Studio + home at 6.30. Eveg Reading Lemkee on Early Christian architecture — I wish to paint "The death of Hypatia" — To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday, 15, 1871

Mean, Raw, foggy, north easterly day — Wlf went to Dr. Hastings — heard a very energetic discourse upon the text 1st Timothy 6th Ch. 12 v. fight the good fight of faith, lay hold on Eternal life — The service + discourse was to me very impressive — went to A.N.U's to dinner — Aunt N. there — Charle came late — Home at about 5 o'clk — Eveg at home Reading \+ made notes to Miss Tiffany \+ to Mr. Johns — of Pitts burg. Art assn. Rain began about 10 — got to bed soon after 11 —
