## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 2, 1871.

Clouded mtg., but cleared off charmingly — Got up with a headache
\+ spent the day on the Sofa or
in my big chair — Soon after
7 o'clk. P.M. — walked out for
an airing — Stopped into
Chaplins Church \+ heard
most of the discourse of the Rev.
\- Bowles of Brooklyn. Text
Matt. 21st Ch. 22d v. "Render unto
Caesar" etc. The sermon seemed to
me too much of an oratorical effort.
Home at 9 o'clk — wrote a letter
to Matt \+ a note to Bellows
\+ got to bed at 12.15

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 3, 1871.

weather, mixed — overcast \+ clear —
Got to studio at 9 o'clk — Kindled
fire \+ after some delay started
on a sketch for "Hunting ostriches
in South America" — got my painting
\+ Georges. [illegible] to N.A. [illegible] D — early —
Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at
2.30 \+ worked miserably, feeling
very heavy, until 4 o'clk — Then
rode to Central Park to see and
sketch Rheas (S.A. ostriches) \+ Llamas.
The Rheas are dead — got studies of Llamas
met Clara Dunham there — Walked about
until near 7 — then home to tea —
Spent the eve'g Reading and
got to bed som after 10
