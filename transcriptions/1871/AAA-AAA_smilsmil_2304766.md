## Left Page

### November, Sunday 12, 1871

Delightful day. Mus[?] ment to Dr. Hastings's \+ heard an excellent discourse concerning sins of mission — Afternoon went to St. Thomas's \+ heard H. Morgan in justifying one's self — After service walked to and in the Park — Home to tea at 6 — Eve. ment to Bdway Tabernacle \+ heard Dr. J.P. Thompson's farewell discourse a very interesting history of his connection with the Society for the past 27 years, great crowd. Home ab. 10 — Some Reading \+ Talking \+ to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 13, 1871

Charming day — got to studio's at 8.45 — kindled fire \+ then to work on foreground of "Sifting" [the Claud?]. Worked until 1.15 — then home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 — met Dr. Percy in the streets — He went up to the studio's with me — there found Mr. Wayland \+ [Hoyt?] — entertained the two for nearly an hour — then to work \+ at it until dusk when I got it done — then Father came in — just been seeing Mother off \+ [hastening?] to the Catskill Mts. — Home to tea at 6 — Dr. Stearns there all night — worked at mounting my studies until 10.30 — To bed at 12.15
