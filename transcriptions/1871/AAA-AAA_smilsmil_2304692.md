## Left Page

### June, Thursday 15, 1871.

Up at 5 o'clk — a hurried bkfst. and at 6 Started on stage for Denver. Sun not yet in the valley — Cool + fresh. The Ride altogether very pleasant the dust as usual something tremendous. Dined at 11.30 at Wheelers. Reached Apex, at the foot of the Mts abt 1.30, changed coach, Rose over to Golden City, saw some superb effects on the way, just in time for Col. Central R.R. Denver at 3 o'clk. Went at once to American H[otel] Washed + dressed + then to P.O. + Photo. on way back Met Calman, just in from the "Garden of the Gods". He has been telegraphed to return home. Mr. Dunham very ill. Mr. + Mrs. C. at Am Ho. over night. Ben Martin surprised us while at tea — we't mote home, to Matt + to Jenny R. Chatted with the C's until after 9. then to my room, Repacked trunk + to bed soon after 10. Inferonally hot night.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 16, 1871.

On Pullman Car "Savannie"
Sect. 59 7

Up at 5.30. bkfst soon after 6. Walked to Station, attended to baggage + off for Salt Lake City at 7.30. The Colemans with us. Baking hot, but after we started, a breeze that soon blew half a gale + whistled through our car as though absan. Some splendid effects of mirage. Got to Cheyenne abt 1. Dinner. Re-checked baggage — train met came up. Secured sleeping compt. + at 1.30 moved off. The Colemans going east. Enjoyed the day immensely. Effects of Wtr + Plains superior to any thing we have yet seen. The plains looking E. from Shermans, wonderful — also Laramie plains in tea at 5 o'clk at Laramie City. Was very perfectly delicious. Could see until we passed Lake Como. Had a chat with Senator Corbett. Ore agone + got to bed about 10.
