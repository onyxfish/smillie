## Left Page

### May, Monday 22, 1871

A very warm day — Rather muggy — got down-town to A.B.N. Co. — at 10 o'clk with Drawing of Fw. Delp. Seal, and made on model, drawing of [illegible] — arranged with Vantgandt to work for A.B.N. Co. hereafter. Ab 11 o'clk. left for up town — stopped at Westermann's \+ got Yosemite guide Book — got to studio at 12 o'clk — Commenced to sketch "Havana" subject — Home to lunch at 1.45 — after, went to [illegible] then to Wm. Hart's \+ got to studio at 3 worked on sketch until 5 — then Jessie Hayland came in, \+ she posed for me until 7 o'clk — I making a study of figure for my "Havana" drawing — went to Miller's \+ home to tea at near 8 o'clk — Well tired \+ did nothing but a little reading \+ to bed about 10 o'clk —

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 23, 1871

Very warm but breezy day — got to studio at 9.15 and worked on sketch for "Havana" drawing until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — Back at 2 \+ soon after miss Whiting came (19th lesson) \+ sat with me until 3.40 watching me draw — She then left \+ again I went to my work — stretching paper \+ commencing sketch for another subject "Mechanics" ab 5.30 Fan M. came in — ab to I walked to 48 St. with he — Home at 6.30 — Well \+ wrote a note to Mr. Jas. Wilson \+ getting accounts with father arranged — worked until 11 o'clk — to bed at 11.30
