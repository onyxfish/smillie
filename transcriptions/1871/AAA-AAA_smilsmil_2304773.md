## Left Page

### November Sunday, 26 1871

Overcast with drizzle during the m'f. Cleared late in the afternoon. Got up late, feeling out of sorts — did not go to church in the m'f. At 3 o'clk, went up to St. Thomas's + heard a very good sermon from D. M. the music of course was good — after service, walked up to the Park — charmed with the twilight — on one hand sunset, on the other, moonlight — have rarely seen a more luminous or lovely moonlight — Home to tea at 6 — very. did little or nothing but lounge in my big chair + to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### November Monday, 27 1871

Got to studio at 9.20 — weather rather mixed — overcast, then clear with cold, breezy + brilliant moonlight night. Painted all day as hard as I was able on 16×26 "The Saco + Mts at North Conway" 2d day) worked on the mts, middle distance and water — Home to lunch at 1.40 — back at 2.15 worked until 4.30 — then went to Pamp's Studio with a lot of bills — then to Leavitt's where I got them cashed — then at 5.40, back to studio — cleaned up + home to tea at 6.30 Evg. about fooling over a photo — Matt + with Burrograff and the tailor — to bed soon after 10 —
