## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 5, 1871.

Rec'd letters from home that were Read in the little house of "Snow" between the Nevada and Vernal Falls —

A brilliant + delightful mf., all Ready to start for the Nevada Fall soon after 7. But one of the horses coming up missing we did not get off until near 9. The ride was perfectly delicious, the change beyond Hutchings from meadow + River to oak Specimens then to pine + wilder woods, then Rugged + more wild until at last the Trail culminated in the most exciting climb that I have yet Taken on horseback. About 11 Reached the Summit 25 or 2700 ft. above the valley, tied my horse, climbed a little higher + took a sketch then down to "Snow's" where we took dinner. After went to foot of Nevada Fall + about 2.30 started back. Thunder storm threatening — Sent horses down to "Register Rock" while we went down the ladders, caught in a heavy Rain with very large hail stones, got under Register Rock + found a party already there. When our horses came up there were 15 horses \+ 15 people (4 ladies) a fire was built + we waited for an hour nearly, when the storm cleared off + and we were quite dry. The scene was very picturesque — Then we went on, enjoying exceedingly the Ride to the Hotel, where we are between 5 + 6. Tea about [illegible] feeling unlike anything else I spent most of it on my bed + slowed myself for the night soon after 9 —

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 6, 1871.

Overcast with considerable quite rain — Spent the mf. Reading + working on crayon sketch that I made yesterday — Dinner about 2 o'clt — P.M. Trying to write those confounded letters to the "Mail". I think I never was such a fool before. I am absolutely without fancy + words seem like paving stones, at last, tired + disgusted wandered out, across the Merced River, back of the Three Brothers, climbed a slide, picked out a place for a study of Cathedral Rocks, then down to the foot of El Capitan — Back about 7 — then tea + from near [illegible] until 11 I wrote — then to bed —
