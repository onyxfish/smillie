## Left Page

### July, Friday, 21, 1871.

Overcast again, but cleared charmingly before night. Myf. Started a Sketch from Right at back down, looking up to Sentinal Rock. At it until dinner time, after 1 o'clk afternoon, painted sketch of Illilouette fall from little pencil note that I took yesterday — worked until tea time, about 6.30 when a shirt blocked in. Uncle G. arr'd about 4 o'clk. Evelf. washing brushes and arranging to climb Yo Semite fall to-morrow. Got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Saturday, 22, 1871.

Superb day. Shortly before 8, got off with Ridgway for the foot of the upper Yo S-ll — got there at 10 \+ a little more, after stopping several times to take notes. got sketch in oil of the foot of the fall from under the Rock — Lunch at 1. \+ got sketch finished soon after 2. — About 3 started to return, taking the South side where there is no trail — We had very hard work \+ at times thought we would have to give it up, but by dint of nerve \+ muscle we accomplished it. I had one very narrow escape \+ for a moment a nightmare horror seemed ready to siege me, but I did not lose my [illegible] \+ got safely through. — Down at 5. took pencil sketch from the Meadow \+ home at 6. — a bath \+ a good Rubbing tea. Evelf. wrote a letter to Kate. To bed about 10.30
