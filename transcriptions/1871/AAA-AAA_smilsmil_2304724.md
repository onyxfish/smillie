## Left Page

### August, Thursday 10, 1871

Clear, working weather, as usual. Bkfst about 9 — then to work on study from the back door, a por of Sentinal Rock — got it finished by 12 o'clk. Dinner abt — at 2 went across the River to my study of old White Cedar Tree, worked without stopping until 6.30 when I got that finished — Tea about 7 — got letters via Clark's, mail sent by Matt before her letter Rec'd yesterday. Rec'd a letter from Minn. "Gel" Rec'd one from Mary Tweedy — Self walked up to Hutchings' bridge for exercise. Washed brushes and got to bed som after 11.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 11, 1871

Very warm, but a glorious, stiff breeze blowing all day — Bkfst. Rather late, so that I did not get off to my work until near 9 o'clk. Went to my study of Cathedral Rocks and the Valley (4th sitting) \+ commenced the repainting, got over the sky \+ distant rocks — then back to lunch abt 30 — At 2.30 went over to my study of the Domes \+ commenced repainting that (5th sitting) — worked as long as I could see — nearly 7 — then to the hotel, very tired — tea \+ lounging on the piazza until after 9 — washed some brushes \+ got to bed about 10.30
