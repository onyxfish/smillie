## Left Page

### December Friday, 22 1871.

Got up in the mng and tried to eat a little bkfst. but my headache was too much for me and I went to bed again where I stayed until after 7 o'clk — then up until 4 o'clk. a.m. when I went to bed —

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 23 1871.

Miserable day — Up late \+ got to Studio abt 10 o'clk. Sleety, slushy \+ horrible overhead \+ underfoot. felt the influence of yesterday's headache to such an extent that I could do nothing. Home to lunch abt 1.30 — did not go back to Studio but wrote or made letter — about 5 o'clk. Rigged myself with rubber coat \+ big boots \+ went down to S.S. with things for the girls. Home to tea som after 6. [illegible] some more writing — Parker [illegible] abt 8 o'clk — got to bed som after 11 —
