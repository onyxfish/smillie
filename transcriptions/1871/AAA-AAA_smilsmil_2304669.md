## Left Page

### April Wednesday, 26 1871.

Glorious day \- got to Studio ab't 5
\+ to work on drawing of a M. Goodw[in]
Arms \- ab't until 1 o'clk (½) then
a little clearing up \- Home to
lunch at 1.45 \- back at 2.30
Misses M. (18) J. (19) \+ W. (13)
at the Studio. Labored with
them until 6 o'clk \- Miss Tiffany
sent down a check to me \-
Home to tea at 7 o'clk \- at
7.30 went to St. Cloud \- Saw the
Smeedings \- at 8.30 went to Burggraf's \- then to Will's in St. Luke's
Place \- getting there at 9.10 \- Found
then Mr. Roe \+ others \- music \-
borrowed Will's dress coat, there
until 10.45 \- home at 11.15 \-
to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### April Thursday, 27 1871.

Horrible day \- got up with a headache. Down town to A.B.N.Co. at 9.35
\+ directions for some drawing and
engraving \+ back to Studio at 11.15 \- worked
on Small locomotive drawing for Coupon
back (½ day) until a little after 1 o'clk
\+ Farrer came in \+ p'd. $20.² dues for
W. back. Water Color S.F. Home to lunch
at 2 o'clk. Dressed, full, \+ at 3.15 carriage
with Belle \+ Mary Smeedy came \+ we went to
the Tiffany wedding. A magnificent display.
Ceremony at 3.40 \- Reception from 4 to 6 \-
Saw lots of people that I knew \+ enjoyed
myself \- Walked home through a horrible
mud \+ Rain at 6 o'clk \- Tea at 7 \-
at 8 called on Mr. Clarke, No. 8 W. 24th
St. \- not in \- then went over to Gramercy Park Hotel \+ made wedding call on
Mr. \+ Mrs. Will. Black \- Enjoyed that
also \- there until 9.40 \- then home
\- wind \+ Rain still heavy \- got
to bed at 10.45
