## Left Page

### July, Friday 7, 1871.

Killed a Rattlesnake with 4 Rattles beyond Mirror Lake.

Glorious, cloudless day — Got off about 7.35 for Mirror Lake, the whole party of us. Got to the lake about 8.15 — The sun had risen upon it, but we were very much disappointed — The lake was muddy \+ nothing looked quite as fine as we had expected — In addition I found that there was no trail (for horses) to Tenaya falls, about 3 miles farther on but Gil \+ I determined to try it — We got our horses on for perhaps 1½ miles then gave it up. Worked on a sketch until 12.30 — Made a fine, cooked some beef tea \+ lunched at foot of Lincoln falls. Started back about 2 — sketching by the way until near 5 — delightful ride back to Seidee's about 6 — Found the rest of the party were at the Bridal Veil fall — Rode down to meet them \+ had a good run back with them. I eat at 7.30 — Well on the did \+ to bed for the night about 9.30

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 8, 1871.

Another day like yesterday — Went out about 8.30 \+ started my first oil color study for the season — a view of the Yo Semite falls from back of Black's — worked until noon, then the sun got to where I could not see to work — Back to hotel at 1, but did not get dinner until 1.30 — then went to my 2° study, a view of Sentinel Rock — Both studies are very badly commenced — We \+ took a walk, crossed the River \+ wandered the meadows on the opposite side seeking for studies. Back to tea soon after 7 — Spent chatting with Mr. Allen until after 9 — to bed about 9.45
