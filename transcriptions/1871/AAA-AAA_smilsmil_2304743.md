## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 17 1871.

Beautiful day - Up at 6 - Bkfst - \+ off for Pond's Music Store where Geo. Charl \+ I got at 7.50, rushing to buy Phil. tickets - two ahead of us - the store not open - At 9 the sale commenced - me. managed to get the seats we wanted and placed nearly 40 for Paid - went to Brooks Bros. Am to have a new coat - got to A.B.M.Co at 10.45 \+ spent the rest of the mf finishing dag. outline and making two etching points from Rat-tail files - out to lunch at 1 - Called at S.B.M.Co for Gel- \+ at Ratellier's \+ Co. to see Seitz - After lunch, got transfer \+ commenced the etching of Hunting Ostriches (2d day) At 5.15 walked up home - Tea at 6.30 - Eve'g Read aloud awhile - then working at cutting studies until 10 o'clk \+ to bed soon after -

## Right Page

### October Wednesday, 18 1871.

Pleasant, breezy day - got to A.B.M.Co. at 9.5 \+ went on with my etching (3d day) until 12.30, then went to Cotton, Zahn \+ Roberts - then to Eve'g Mail - saw Townley - then after a map of the Yo. Semite - then to lunch \+ again called at Con. B.M.Co. Back at 2 o'clk - A young man called from Hiram Ferguson about H\+ H's things - worked until 5 o'clk. when I could see no longer - Rode up to studio my new carpost down - Home to Tea at 6.30 - Eve'g Read aloud some of Clarence King's High Sierra article in "Atlantic" - Feeling very tired - got to bed before 10 o'clk.
