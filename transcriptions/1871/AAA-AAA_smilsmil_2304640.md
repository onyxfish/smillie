## Left Page

### February Monday, 27 1871.

Overcast w\+ clearing into a breezy cold night. Feel very Rheumaticy and sore all over. Got to studio at 9.30. Worked part of a.m. on "Colorado Arms" A.B.N.Co. and part of the time, drawing from nude model, an immense Minnesota girl. Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30 — P.M. spent between working on figure drawings and Arms (Friday) At 5.45 went to Macy's 6th ave and 14th St and bought 3 camp chairs. Back to studio thence at 6.40 every wrote letter to Mr. Smock Lewis — To bed at 10.30. Miss Woodworth at studio. P.M.

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 28 1871.

Splendid day — clear, breezy \+ cold — at 9.30 at A.B.N.Co. Had a talk with Danzandt about selling myself to the Co. for 3 months — agreed to at the Rate of $5000. pr. annum — to begin at once — Called at Schenck's \+ got "Among the Hills" — circle not sold — the circle "In the Wilderness" sold for $22. without frame. Back to studio at 12. went to Kerhin's \+ had hair cut. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked on Colorado Arms (1 day) until 6 o'clc. Home to tea at 7 — Water Color business until 8.30 then dressed \+ with 9. at 9.20, got to Wm. J. Blodgett's — Reception. great crowd — fine affair, supper etc — enjoyed meeting notables — left at 11 o'clc — home at 11.20 to bed at 11.40
