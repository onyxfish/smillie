## Left Page

### March Monday, 13 1871

Beautiful day \- got to studio at 8.45\. Kindled fire \+ worked on Colorado arms (4 1/2 days) with graver until 10 o'clk. Then went to Y.M.C.A. Bldg to see about sending pictures to their Reception to-night. Back with an hour and again at work \- Sent "Lifting of the clouds" \+ "Good news to aix" to Y.M.C.A\. Home to lunch at 1.45. back at 2.45 \+ worked until 5.30. Then worked on "Niagara" die mth burnisher until 6.30. Louis Tiffany came in \- went to Y.M.C.A. mth him \- then walked home \- Tea at 7.05 \- well, tired \- did not go out and did little by staying in \- to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### March Tuesday, 14 1871

Pleasant day. Started for studio at 8.15\. went to Knoedler's for paper \- got to studio at 9\. \+ commenced on drawing for Western Nat. Co. (1st day) Rec'd note from Dr. F.W. Lewis about sending drawings in to Phila. At 12.30. went to see Mr. Hart about it \- Home to lunch at 1.45 \- back at 2.30 after meeting Belle \+ Mary Smedley \- Very much used up with distress in the head \+ dyspepsia\. At 5 went again to Mr. Hart's to show him how I blow my Shellac Solution over Water Colors \- back in 3/4 of an hour. Home to tea at 6.15 \- then went to H.R.R.R. depot \+ saw Matt \- O.V.V. \+ Juney off for San Francisco\. Home at 8.15\. Read newspaper \+ wrote part of a letter to Dr. Lewis but went to bed \&c. at 10 o'clk
