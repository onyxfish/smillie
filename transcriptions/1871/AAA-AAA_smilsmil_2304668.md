## Left Page

### April, Monday, 24, 1871.

Glorious day but did not feel in the humor of it. \- bad night \- got to studio abt. 30 and to work finishing on "Mules", making my 4th day on that drawing. Annie Tiffany called for a few minutes on business. \- Home to lunch at 1,15, back at 2.30 \- worked on drawing "Hunting Ostriches S a". (1st day) Tracing \+ Retracing. Bob Ives called between 2 \+ 3 o'clk. \- a flying visit \- at 5 went down to Sauthier's \+ got a lot of mounting board. Met there Dr. Bartlett \- Home at 7. 20 after stopping at Studio. Self, hard at work until 11 o'clk mounting drawings for Misses Tiffany, Ripley \+ self \- to bed abt 1.45 —

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday, 25, 1871.

mild \+ overcast, got to Studio at 9 10 \+ to work on "Ostriches" (2d day) worked until 1.15 then home to lunch \- back at 2.45 \+ worked until 5.75 \- Jno. Snedecor called during the mt. At 5.30 went to J.M.C.a. \- called on Mr. Hart, on Mc Burney \- then to Sneddie's at 5.45 \+ got a front of water colors for my small boy \- Back to studio at 6.15 \- Slight sprinkle of Rain \- Home to tea at 7 \- self, some Reading \- wrote a letter to J.W. Pattison at St. Louis \+ to bed at a little after 10.30
