## Left Page

### August, Sunday, 6, 1871.

Very hot. Up soon after 6 — but did not get bkfst. until after 9. made acquaintance of all the party (Ashburner) and spent the mng with the Misses Dawes, Brown, etc — Took blanket down to the River, under the trees, then crossed in the boat \+ wandered on the other side, back about 3 — lunch — washed out some brushes \+ about 4 went out with pen \+ paper \+ made for two hours or more. Tea at 7 — Eveg general chatting until near 10 —

## Right Page

### August, Monday, 7, 1871.

Still very hot. Bkfst. before y — of to my work at 7.45. finished study of Yo Semite Fall \+ made a pencil sketch besides, getting back to dinner soon after 1 o'clk. Mr. Gammel, Burchard \+ Mike the Georgian left for Snows after dinner. At 2.30 I went to my study of Sentinal Rock and worked until 6 o'clk when I got it finished — Tea about 7 and Eveg. loafing and chatting with Messrs Dawes, Ashburner \+ Brown until near 10 o'clk — got to bed soon after —
