## Left Page

### August, Thursday 24, 1871

at Grand Hotel, Room 135

glorious m'g, as usual, Motif — 7 — bkfst — at 8, \+ at 9 left for San [illegible] — Enjoyed every part of the way, the plains, the Livermore pass, then the Mt. Diabalo Range, then the Livermore valley, wonderfully beautiful with its broad fields, oak trees \+ surrounding mountains — then through Vallejo Canyon and out to Oakland — getting into San F. at 2.30 — got to Grand hotel at about 1 oclk \+ surprised Matt — lunched then had hair cut \+ head shampooed, feeling much refreshed by the [illegible] washed, dressed \+ re-arranged baggage — Dinner at 7 — wrote note to Father and got to bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### August, Friday 25, 1871

Up about 6.30, after a good night's rest — Bkfst at 7.15 \+ at 7.40 Rushed off with Uncle A. to Oakland Ferry to start for San Jose. Found that we were an hour ahead of time. Spent it going about Oakland, then on, getting to San Jose at 12.30. Climbed to the top of Court House Dome, then to Auzerais House \+ had a lunch of such peaches as I haven't had for years. At 2.30, Rode over to New Hall's orchard, never saw such fruit, then on to Mr. Mc Laughlins, then through the Alameda drive to Santa Clara — went into the old Mission Ch. \+ was delighted — Took train there at 3.30 getting in San F. som after 6 — very cold \+ foggy. Tea at 6.30 \+ about 8 with Mr. Theo. Hoomer Miss Satrals \+ others went to Fair at Pavilion — [illegible] Mc [illegible] [illegible] Home at 10.20 and to bed at 11.30
