## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 20 1871

Bright, breezy and very cold — got to studio a few minutes after 9 o'clk \+ commenced work on study of "The Merced Groups" painted last summer, but the foreground was not finished — so I spent my morning over it — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back \+ 2.30 and som after Mrs Adams came in and stayed until 4 o'clk —
Home to tea at 6 — Swelf, made "a Mail" letter until after 11, and midnight when I got to bed —

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 21 1871

Another very cold day. Got to studio at 9.15 — worked for about an hour on sketch for Sauthier. Then on painting for Emil Seitz. at 12.30 went to 3d Ave. Savings Bank as I doubted its safety, and drew $700 \+ of Water Color Society money — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.15 — about 3 fan Mulford came in and visited for half an hour — Evef, home to tea at 6 — the time until 10 o'clk spent doing nothing much —
got to bed at about 10.30
