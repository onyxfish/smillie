## Left Page

### November Monday, 20, 1871

Miserable stormy day \- Down to blk. before 7 \- did not get it until 7.30 \- cut a brig. \+ took our departure in the 5.30 train. behind time \- 9.45 when we got to N.Y. Rode up to N.A. of D. saw T.A. Richards \+ made arrangements for committee meeting next Thur. every \- Thur. to studio at a little before 11 \- kindled fire \+ drew from model for West Virginia arms until near 1 o'clk \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.45 \- At 5 stretched on the lounge there until 6.30 \- then home to tea \- At 8 o'clk went to Chicago Fund Sale at Leavitt's \- Very agreeably surprised \- in spite of untoward circumstances the average of the Sale was all that could have been expected \- My picture went for $75.00 nett \- Home at 11 \+ to bed at 12.00

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 21, 1871

Very fine day \- got to Studio at 9.00 and to work on A.B.N. Co's drawing "arms of West Virginia" \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \+ back at 2.15 \- worked as long as I could see \+ home to tea at 6 o'clk. Every at 7.30 went to Leavitt's Rooms \- 2° every of Sale for Chicago Relief Fund. There until the Sale was over at 10.15 \- walked up home \- in at 11 \+ to bed at 11.45
