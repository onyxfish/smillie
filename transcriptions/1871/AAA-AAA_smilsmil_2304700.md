## Left Page

### July, Saturday 1, 1871

*Leidig's — Room 6   Yo Semite Valley*

Up soon after 5 — bt[?] at about 6&30 +
At 7.15 on saddle Train got started for the
Yo Semite. 7 of us, Jat Ridgeway, Guide +
two pack-mules. We rode until noon
constantly climbing, the timber sometimes
very fine, but generally scattered. The Ride
was less picturesque than I had anticipated.
At 12 o'clk. Reached Mt. View House (Peryzon's)
a nicely built new log house. Got a very good
dinner & about 2 o'clk. Started on again with
a thunderstorm threatening us. Crossed several
lovely natural meadows with beautiful flowers
& streams & a little before 4 o'clk came out upon
"Inspiration Point" — Disappointed — the color too
cold a gray & the view too topographical —
many views half way down the mt very much
finer. Our party more tired going down the mt.
than all the rest of the ride — 2 hours from Mt.
Point to the bottom of the valley — then about
3 miles to Leidig's. I led into the valley, but
in the valley, Geo. & I loitered enjoying
immensely the evening effects. The tree
groups were found very fine — at last at
about 7.30, we cantered up to Leidig's, after
10½ hours in the saddle — took a cold sponge
bath — then tea & sat watching the moon rise
& the light as it crept down the opposite
cliff — and the Yo Semite fall — a writhing
spectre in the uncertain light — went to bed
very unwillingly at 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 2, 1871

An immensely hot m'f. Did not get
up until near 10 o'clk — all of
us very tired & lame. I spent
part of the m'g lying in the
shade of a tree on the river bank.
About noon a thunderstorm threat-
ening — hoping to get it — but did not —
however the atmosphere was
somewhat cooled — spent a
part of the afternoon snoozing,
then took a walk out beyond
Hutching's — very charming. Back
to bed before 10 o'clk.

I had hoped that here, at last
I had got away from modern "im-
provements", Rail Roads & Telegraphs —
but last night, the very eve, I am—
a telegraph wire was brought into the
valley & communication completed.
I find also, a Saloon, with velvet
carpets, two billiard tables, &c. &c.
