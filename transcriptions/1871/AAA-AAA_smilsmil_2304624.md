## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 24, 1871

Every thing looking \+ feeling wintry under the snow of yesterday. Snow falling slightly during the mng., clearing up in the P.M. — got to S. at 9.15 and at once to work touching into shape msp. on Bristal board of medalion Ruling \+ Gold Coins for A.B.N.Co — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.15. \+ worked very closely until after 3 — Then Miss Annie Tiffcany came in to arrange about Taking lssns — Home to tea soon after 3 — About 8.15 got S.N.A. of D. Private \+ Press view of Water Color Society — my drawing takes a great deal of attention \+ I have been so patted \+ stroked that I have come home purring all over like a cat — got in about 11 — to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 25, 1871

Superb morning — clear \+ cold — P.M. — Somewhat cloudy — Got to Studio about 9.30 — Rather headachy — Did not get to work until about 11 when I got to Repainting 9 x 12 "Chappeal Pond" — went over the old key from the top, down — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 1.50 \+ painted as long as I could see — Then washed \+ cleaned up — at 5.30, stretched on lounge for half an hour — Home to tea at 6.30 — at 7.30 at N.A. of D. 5th Pimmer lecture — after that up in Gallery, there met all the Immedips — Misses Stewart, Wild \+ Vandevcuter — about walked down with the S's — Home at 11.30 — to bed at 12
