## Left Page

### November Thursday, 30 1871.

Clear, but exceedingly cold \+ blustering day. Got to studio at 9.20 and to work on die (12¼ days) with graver. Home to dinner at 1.15 — almost a Reguler Thanksgiving affair — back to studio at 2.45 \+ worked on drawing of West Virginia arms that I mounted last night. Home to tea at 6 — every, wrote out minutes of last N.Y.A.C.R. Fund meeting \+ then wrote a letter to Maud D [illegible]. To bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December Friday, 1 1871.

not quite so cold as yesterday, but clear \+ windy. Got to studio soon after 9 and right to work on Yo-Semite studies, varnishing and retouching over them. Mrs. Mary Rogers Griffiths called. Home to lunch at 1.15. At 2.30 — at Phil. Rehearsal after that, at 5 o'clk — at studio — cleared up \+ home to tea at 6.30 — Every. Dodging about trying to get some of my Yo-Semite stuff into presentable order. To bed at 10.30.
