## Left Page

### February Saturday, 25 1871.

Over cast most of the day — got to studio at 9.15 — Wile told Praiverd came in soon after — Bank Note business. got to work at about 10 on Niagara die (3 days) graver and dry point — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — At 3. Porevoorti's perspective — at 4.30 — back to work \+ kept at it until 6 o'clk — Home to tea at 6.30 — Edw'd Parker abr'd from Po'keepsie about 7 o'clk. I worked with nearly upon a problem in perspective and got to bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 26 1871.

Miserable Rainy day — Mrs went to D. Hastings \+ heard him preach from the text "am I my brother's keeper?" — of course me all know that me are — and the D. was very good — Dinner at home — P.m. in my chair, Reading \+ napping about 5 o'clk. Prof. Rood called \+ asked me to his house to Tea — Tea at home at 6.30 — got to Prof R's at 8 o'clk — Salad, Sandwiches and Tea — cake \+ mine — professors literary men, artists and things, not to mention several varieties farmers — Spent a very pleasant evng, making a dead set at R. H. Stoddard \+ wife — Home a little before 11 o'clk — through several heavy showers and to bed at 11 ½
