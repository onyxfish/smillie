## Left Page

### March, Sunday, 5, 1871.

Glorious day \- wf went to Dr. H's as usual, \+ heard him preach an energetic digging sermon from Acts 10 ch. 4 v. as a memorial of the union fold \+ new school Presbyterians, he wishes his church to finish their mission chapel. With Chark went to a.V.V's to dinner, Mr. \+Mrs. Thayer \+ daughter, Mr \+Mrs. Fisher \+ daughter there \- a regular dinner \- Soup, fish, quail, Turkey, vegetables \+ sauces \- pastries, pudding, fruit, champagne \+ coffee. \- through at 4.30 \- Rode down in Thayer's carriage \- Eveft went to J.P. Thompson's. "The mosaic code a proof of Divine inspiration" was his subject. Home at 9.15 \- Read Bowles' "Colorado \- Utes Parks \+ Mountains" and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Monday, 6, 1871.

Horrible day, driving rain \- got to studio at 9.10 \- Kindled fire \+ then commenced etching on "Colorado" (1st day) at 12.30 \- Russell brought up drawings from Water Color exhibition, at 12.40 went to [M.C.A. Bldg] and saw Mr. Hart about sending drawings to Boston \- Called on Miss Walters \+ Mr. Culver \- Home to lunch at 2 o'clk \- back to studio at 2.50 \- worked very badly until 4.30 suffering very much from indigestion \- stretched myself on lounge until 6 o'clk \- then home to tea \- Weather cleared up \- strong west wind \- Eveft, some excitement \- fire in frame buildings cor. of 41 st \+ Broadway. Mr. \+Mrs. Townsend, Mr. Newland \+ Dr. Hawks spent Eveft \- got to bed about 10.45
