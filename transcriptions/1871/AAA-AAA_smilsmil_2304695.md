## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 21, 1871.

at Grand Hotel, Room 111 —
San Francisco.

Up at 5 o'clk — Misty \+ blustered
with any number of snow sheds
but before we reached Alta 't
cleared charmingly, the Road
to Colfax immensely interesting
"Cape Horn" the gold diggings —
Canyons, etc. at Colfax, Hkfs —
after that plain down hill work
until we reached Sacramento
about noon — intensely hot, dusty
and baked — a hurried dinner
at 1 — an interesting Canyon on
passing out of the Sacramento Valley
— great change in the atmosphere
at Oakland at 5.30 — Real Cold.
On the ferry boat, met Uncle a.
\+ Junice waiting for us, Matt did his
dare trust himself — Reached Grand
Hotel about 6.30 — Had Shampoo'd
washed \+ dressed — then dined
at 7.15 — chatting together until
near 11 — then to bed —

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 22, 1871.

Cold Raw m'f. — the day no better
altho' at times it seemed to me
to improve. Up at 7.30 — Hkfs — at
8.30 — about 9.30 Uncle a, Matt,
Geo \+ I rode over to the Cliff House
then along the beach about 3 miles
I walked most of the way, enjoying
the strong air \+ surf extremely.
Back to Hotel about 2 — lunch \+
after, went out with Uncle a. \+ about
to a number of places, business —
back shortly after 5 o'clk — worked
at packing for a start to-morrow.
Tea at 7 o'clk. Every chatting
\+ packing in a general way.
Young men in the hotel, Henry
Reddington \+ others with Matt
\+ Junice — got to bed about 11.
