## Left Page

### August, Sunday 20, 1871.

Our last day in the Yosemite —
Very hot, with clouds — atmosphere
getting to be very hazy — Rkfst
after 8 o'clk — Spent the mug.
until near noon, gathering flowers,
seeds — Then back to hotel and
fixing up things for leaving —
Dinner at 2.30 — Packing sketches
etc. until 5 o'clk. Then, with [illegible]
walked beyond Hutchings' and
bid the valley good bye — The
tall pines, the twilight and the young
moon, exquisite. Called on Hutchings,
chatted awhile \+ bid him good bye.
Back to tea at 7.30 — From 8.30
until near 10, at work when at
last I got my packing completed.
To bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### August, Monday 21, 1871.

at Paregoris —

Up about 5.30. Bkfst soon after 7 —
and at last, at 8.15 we got started
on our way out, I ahead, tugging
on our pack mule, Geo. following
\+ wholloping him — It was glorious
though, the valley never looked to me
grander \+ I felt deepest shame and
humiliation when I thought of the
work in my pack. Bid each object
good bye, as we passed it — got to
Illinois Spring at 9.30 — at Inspiration
Pt. at 11 \+ at Paregoris a little before
1 o'clk — Dinner soon after — Went
up on the hill \+ at 3 o'clk, started my
study of the mts. ahead of my light —
heavy cumulist hanging over them —
but by 5.30, clouds all gone \+ light
superb — worked until 7 — getting the
mts \+ middle distance done — Tea
at 7.30 — Even chatting with
Mr. Sowrey, a Brooklyn lawyer.
To bed before 10 o'clk.
