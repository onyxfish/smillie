## Left Page

### May Sunday, 28 1871.

Charming day \- morning went to Dr Hastings \+ heard him preach from the text that the children of this world are more wise than the children of light \- a begging sermon \- After service walked up 5th Av. with Prof. \+ Miss Carrie Torrey \- Home to dinner \- P.M. went to St Thomas's \+ heard Dr Morgan preach upon the necessity of a new birth \- Met there Mr. and Belle Tweedy \- walked with B. \+ took her home at 7.45 \- I got home at 8.30 \- took tea \+ wrote to Matt \+ note to Cornish \+ Congdon \- To bed at 10.40.

## Right Page

### May Monday, 29 1871.

Another hot cloudless day \- at 8.45 went to 210 W. 59th \- Saw Mr. Jas. Wilson \- got to Studio at 9.45 \+ all the rest of A.M. at work cutting figures in drawings bought by Colton, Zahm \+ Co \- Home to lunch abt 1.30 \- back at 2.20 \- \+ worked on "Havana" until 5.30 (3 days) Miss Whiting came \+ brought me a check for $65.83. at 5.30 \- went to Laurthier's, then to Snedecor's \- then to Schmitt's \+ at 7 o'clk \- back to Studio \- Home to tea at 7.45 \- Busy mounting drawings for Misses Ripley \+ Whiting \+ A.B.N. Co \- until after 11 \- To bed abt 11.30
