## Left Page

### July, Monday, 31, 1871.

Glorious day, cool and clear — Up soon after 6 — after bkfst, wrote a letter to Matt and sent in by Leidig, who goes to San F. this A.M. — At 9 o'clk, got off to study of Cathedral Rocks from the Rocks foot of Sentinal Fall First sitting — worked until 10'clk then to hotel to dinner. At 2 15 Crossed the River \+ went to study of "The Domes" from Slide back of Leidigs Winter House — first sitting worked until 6 — then back, washed out brushes — tea — wandering about for an hour, then wrote a few words to the Tweedys \+ about 10 got to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday, 1, 1871.

Still glorious, cool \& clear. Up at 6 o'clk — got off to my work at 8 o'clk \+ worked until 12 15 on study of Cathedral Rocks (2d. sitting) Back to hotel at 12 45 — Nearly 1 30 before we had dinner — The Kitteredge party arr'd at noon. At 2 30 went over to my study of the Domes \+ worked on that until 6 o'clk — Had a little wash in the River \+ tea at 7 — Sweet chatting until after 8 — then finishing up 3d letter to the Mail until 10 o'clk. Rec'd letters from home through Matt, now in S.F. To bed at 10 30
