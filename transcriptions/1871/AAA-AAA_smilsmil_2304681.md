## Left Page

### May Wednesday, 24 1871.

Cloudless, breezy \+ brassy — Got down Town at 9.15 \+ submitted sketch to Goodall — Changes had to be made — then went to Riiny & Miths — Seitz's — Conway's \+ Norton's — back to Studio at 11.30 — Home to lunch at 1.15 — Mother \+ Mary spending the day at Newark — went to Schmitt's \+ N.A. of D— back to Studio at 3 o'clk worked until near 7 on new design for "Havana" drawing drawing Hands etc. from Jessie H. Went to Miller's — got [illegible] Home to tea at 8 — Tired — Spent the evev't Reading Whitney's Yosemite Guide and \+ to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 25 1871.

Another cloudless, breezy day. Got to Studio a little before 9 \+ at once to work transferring \+ outlining 2° Sketch "Havana" drawing (1 day) fighting with a headache — Home to lunch at 1.15, back at 1.50 — Most of the P.M. — quite used up with headache — Col F— \+ Zahm called — took away my Ausable Lake drawing, Falking \+ Chromoing it — Rec'd a note from Frazier Kreps in which he acknowledges that he has cheated me out of $130.00 \+ has gone west, seeking employment he says. Home to tea at 8 o'clte — Evev'g marking clothes — to bed at 11 —
