## Left Page

### July Saturday, 15 1871.

A beautiful day — all up pretty early — Uncle A. got off with Ridgway for a day in the little Yosemite — Abg. I got to work outlining a study looking out of the valley S.W. from the wash at foot of Sentinal fall — Back to Hotel at 11 o'clk to take a nap, as I had hardly any sleep last night. Great alarm over sick child brought in by a German \+ his wife last night — Dinner at 1.30 — At 2.30, crossed the River \+ climbed wash back of Leidig's winter house \+ outlined study of The Domes — back at 6.30. Uncle A. came in about 7 — also a party of 6 men — News of Riots in N.Y. between Orangemen \+ Papists on 12" ins — Got to bed about 10 o'clk —

## Right Page

### July Sunday, 16 1871.

Charming day, but hot. After [illegible] about 9 o'clk, all walked up to Blacks and spent most of the m'f chatting with the party there — don't know all of their names, Messrs Coburn, McKee — Beecher, [illegible] — etc — quite a lot of gay young girls Back to Hotel — great excitement with two drunken Indians — they got pistoles \+ threatened to shoot Barkeeper leveled a Riflesion them \+ at one time I thought would surely shoot; a horse Race — then dinner — Afternoon wrote home. Diamoved from Sonora for sick child — Scarletina — Every the Black party called on us — left about 9 — I wrote or [illegible] letter until midnight, got to bed about 12.15
