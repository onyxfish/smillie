## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 28 1871.

Very clear + very cold. Stopped at Louis Lane's + then to studio at 9.15. Painted on 16×26 — (24th days) getting over the fore ground + so finishing this painting. — Now it needs harmonizing. Home to lunch at 1.40 — at 3 went to Reception at Metropolitan Museum of art in Central Park — Home again at a little after 5 — Evening with Geo. went to call on J. D. A. Ward — could not get in — then to Emma Ireland's — Had a very pleasant call with her until 10.20 — Home at 10.40 + to bed som after 11.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 29 1871.

Another very cold day. Did not get to the studio until 9.45 — Cleared up my desk + got to work with graver on die for "A. B. N. Co." Ostrich Hunting — S. C. V. (1st? 14 days) Home to lunch at 1.40 — back at 2.30 + worked as long as I could see, until 4.30 At 5, went to Currie's — paid my bill, then to Sharp's — tiv man — back to studio + home to Tea at 7 o'clk. Evening — mounted drawings + fussed around until ten o'clk. — To bed about 11.
