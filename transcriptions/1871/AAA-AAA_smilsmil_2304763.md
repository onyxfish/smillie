## Left Page

### November, Monday, 6, 1871.

Splendid clear, cold day. Gab to Studio at 9 o'clk. Kindled fire \+ made several notes to newspaper people \+ worked on Water-Color sketch of Vernal Fall for Miss Jones. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked all the p.m. on the same drawing, using body color \+ white after the manner of distemper, to me, a fascinating experiment. Well at 6 went over to Y.M.C.A. \+ left a note for W. Thompson — then home to tea — well, cutting \+ trimming my Yo. Smite oil studies — to bed from after 10 —

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday, 7, 1871.

Clear \+ cold — Up at 6.30 — After bkfst. went with father \+ voted — then to Carpenter's in 31st. \+ got to Studio at 9.5 — worked on water color sketch for Miss Jones until 2.30 — Then home to lunch — After that, rode down to Sauthier's — not in — Then to Leavitt's — not in — Then to Gallery with T.B. Way \+ saw Dubufe's painting of the "Prodigal Son". — back to Studio at 3.20 \+ spent what remained of daylight finishing mount for Miss J's drawing — Home to tea at 6.30 — At 7.45 at N.A. of D. — Quarterly Water Color meeting — no quorum — sat about until near 10 o'clk. then home — to bed at 11.20
