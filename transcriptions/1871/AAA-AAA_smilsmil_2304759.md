## Left Page

### November Thursday, 2 1871.

Beautiful day — Got to the studio soon after 9 \+ spent an hour or two making anew the little drawing that I tried to do last night — then got notes together preparatory to writing Salt Lake City letter for the "Mail" — About noon Belle Tweedy \+ Miss Julia Stewart came in \+ stayed for an hour. Home to lunch at 1.30 — after that went to Geo. T. Kall's — then to Wm. Hart's ab't Y.M.C.A. Building — Back to studio at 3 — made an Mail letter until near 5. Mrs. C. H. Davis called in the mid. Home to tea at 6 — At 7.30 at U. of D. — Chicago Fund Com. — There until 9.30 — walked up to 38th St with J. Z. Avrard, after stopping at the Union League Club — To bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### November Friday, 3 1871.

Glorious day — At 8.30 — at Johnston \+ Fon Tassel's — Johnston gone down town — followed him — did not catch him — went to Continental Bank Note Co — then to Ring Smith's — informed by him that he had "busted" \+ I must take up my note of $250.00 before 17th inst or it would be protested — then to A.B.N. Co — Nearly before I started up town — Stopped at Map Co. in Park Place — then to Westervain then to Sloane's — then again to Johnston — Engaged — selling — could not see him — then to Brook's Bros — then rode home to lunch at 1 o'clk — At studio at 2 — Young Leavitt called — About 3 Belle Tweedy came in — Walked down to Bdway \+ 13th St. with her — then to Johnston's — Saw him \+ soon arranged matters — then to Leavitt's \+ arranged matters there — then to Page's studio — \+ Beard's — Camp's — then to my own — then to the Coleman House to see Richards — then home to tea at 6.15 — every tired, but wrote notes, to Townley \+ Miss Jones and got to bed at 10.45
