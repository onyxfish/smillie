## Left Page

### April Sunday, 16 1871.

Sulperb morning — After a Horse-hair Rubbing — sponge bath —
Toweling and breakfast I went up to
Central Park & loitered about
there until Church time, then
sauntered down 5 Ave & stopped
in at S. Thomas's Ch. and heard Dr.
Osgood. I don't like him any
better as an "Epis." than I did as
a Unit[arian]. He preached from text
"I am the vine — Ye are the branches"
Stuffed & titled — a lot of "Epis."
generalities — Home to dinner at 1.
napped from 2 till 4 — then again
up to Park until 7 — Evening
note to Matt — Fan M — & a note
to H. J. Brown — To bed about
midnight.

## Right Page

### April Monday, 17 1871.

Another glorious day — quite cool.
Walked down to Dr. Noyes with Father
— not in. Got to studio at 8.30 &
to work on Llama drawing (4 days)
worked until 12 o'clk — then went
had hair cut & head shampooed
Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at
2.15 & worked until 6 o'clk —
getting drawing about finished.
At 5.30, Belle & Mary & Medy
called — at 6 walked home
with them — Home to tea at
7.10 — Every mote part of
a letter to Fergus on and
got to bed at 9.45
