## Left Page

### March Friday, 3 1871

Disagreeable, muggy, rainy day — got to studio at 8.45 & at once set to work outlining on dag. "Colorado arms" — about 10 o'clk Miss Tiffany came, then Miss Ripley (11th lesson) then Miss Maun, then Miss Jones (5th lesson). They left before 1 o'clk — about 1 — Wm Start called — Home to lunch at 1.30 — down to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30 — Miss Kellogg sang — great crowd — walked to 26th St. — with Belle & Meedy — back to Studio at 4.30 and worked on outline with near 6 — Gel Harvey came — paid him $47.00 water color drawing — Home to tea at 7 o'clk — at 8 went to Mr. Lily Gillman's 116 Lexington Ave — saw her, Missis Fanny Parson and Molly Biddle — Wm Payson & Gillman — Home at 10.30 and to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 4 1871

Miserable, bleak day, rain coming about 2 o'clk — got to studio at 9 — and to work on 2d sketch of North Western Mutual — Home to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.20 & worked until about 5 o'clk, excusing myself from Perspective — took a nap on lounge & home to tea, through a sleety snow storm at 6.30 — at 7.30 went with Matt & Kate to Philharmonic concert — George not feeling like venturing out in such weather — enjoyed the Schumann Symph. & "Aladdin" — Miss Kellogg created quite a furore — Matt & Kate rode home with Nettie Jessup & Mr. Moulton — I walked home alone — storm over — To bed at 11 v. 5
