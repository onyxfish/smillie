## Left Page

### Sept. Sunday, 3 1871.

after 5 o'clk before I got up and nearly 10 before I finished bkfst. went to Church with Matt \+ George — through service at Trinity then to Scott's and .
Read newspapers until about 2 then wrote a long letter to father \+ finished a letter to Ferguson Had no lunch and took tea at 6.30 — at 7.15 with Mr \+ Mrs Mc Han Junie, George \+ Matt went to the Dolemission — Vespers — got there at 7.45 — over at 8.15 — Enjoyed the quaint old building immensely — after Service took Matt \+ Junie to the Chinese Theatre — Stayed there for an hour from 9 until 10 — The hut but fiercer than last night — the gives thought they were in — — walked home and to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### Sept. Monday, 4 1871.

Up at 7 — Bkfst about 8 — out soon after and bought tickets home — went to Genl. Hardenburgh's office \+ spent an hour or more among old Spanish M.S.S — containing much of the early history of this coast — in to Watkins and there until noon selecting photographs — then to China town, asking questions — back to hotel at 1.30 — lunch at 2. Between 3 \+ 4 commenced to pack — at it until 6 — Dinner at 6.30 — Grand Republican Torch light Procession — great display in front of the hotel — introduced to Miss King (daughter of Starr King) \+ her mother, Mrs Morris — went to their room — very cordially rec'd — met Miss Stone \+ a number of others — Mr \+ Mrs. Newhall at Uncle A's — then Mr \+ Mrs. Mc Hang — finished packing and got to bed soon after 12 —
