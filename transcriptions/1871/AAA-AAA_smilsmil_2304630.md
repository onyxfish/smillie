## Left Page

### February Sunday, 5 1871.

Very cold clear day \- but much under the weather with headache
\+ dyspepsia \- went to Dr. Hastings late \+ altho' in time, can't claim
to have heard the sermon \- Home to dinner. After dinner, slept
and so most of the P.M. \- met Every. Read some of Taylor's
Translation of Faust and got to bed at abt 11,30

## Right Page

### February Monday, 6 1871.

Clear and bitter cold \- got to studio at 9.15 and kindled fire \- Then to work on the Repainting of 12×22 in "Indian Pass" \- worked until 10clk \- home to lunch \- back at 2.30 and worked until after 4 o'clk \- getting it about finished and very much better than it was before, I am sure. Feeling out of sorts \- headachy \+ bilious \- or nervous or generally sore with a cold, I don't exactly know what. I stretched myself on the lounge until 6.30 \- then home to tea \- D[illegible] sister Mrs. Pierce, went home at 4 o'clk P.M. \- Every so much used up that I spread myself on the sofa until near 11, then to bed \-
