## Left Page

### May, Thursday 4, 1871

Miserable mud + driving Rain — Got to studio at 9.15 — Worked on drawings of "Mules" + "Jamaica" + at 10 o'clk started for A.B.N. Co — Submitted drawings + Worked on them as required — Went to lunch at 1 with Father — Back at 1.45 — Got p.p. of car dies — left at 3.30 after getting paid $256.00 — Stopped at Snedecor's — paid him $100.00 then to Brooks Bros. Bought suit of clothes — then to studio — Home at 7.10 — very wet — All day fighting with a headache — Well, Read papers + got to bed about 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 5, 1871

Down pouring Rain + wind most of the day — Got to studio at 9.15 — Kindled fire, waited awhile for my pupils + then Rebit on car die (4½ days) Miss Maury came about 10.30 + spent the mf at work but I gave very little time to her — Lunched at studio + at 2.30, at Phil. Rehearsal — Heard Miss Kreb sing — first appearance — butter stick to her piano — Back to studio at 5 o'clk — a little more dry pointing on car die + home to tea at 7 — wrote to Uncle Aaron + at 9 o'clk went to St. Cloud — Belle + Mready alone — there until 11.20 — then home + to bed —
