## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 11 1871.

Muggy day with heavy rain — After 9 o'clk when I got to the studio. Gathered tools \+ went down town to A.B.N. Co. got there at 10.30 \+ worked until core o'clk on a piece of Ribbon work for Will. To lunch at 1.30 and spent the afternoon etching ornaments within large oval on die. At 5 started for home walked to 6th Ave. \+ 12th St. Car home. Tea at 6.30 — Reading until about 9 when W. [illegible] called — He left som after [illegible] and I got to bed about 10. [illegible — ~2 words]

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 12 1871.

Cool, breezy day, but not exactly clear — at A.B.N. Co. at 9.30 — Ruled \+ set background in oval on die — Lunch at 1.30 — Graver \+ burnisher during afternoon, but the general effect weak, So I concluded to put another line over it — Laid a ground \+ started for home at 5 o'clk. Stopped at Brooks Bros. \+ home to tea, walking all the way — at 7 o'clk — Swelf getting files \+ English papers" to right 70 bed at 11 —
