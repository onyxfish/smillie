## Left Page

### February Monday, 13 1871.

Pleasant day — Snow Running away — Got to studio at 9.30 — kindled fire — then went to Cahill's message for George — back at about 10.30 got started on Circular (8 in) Sketch "among the hills" — Am going to try to get something that will sell at Schuedis auction next week — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.15 Painted until about 5 o'clk getting it finished. Mr. Durand called — then Mrs. Hunt. Home to Tea at 7 — Evef spent Resting and Reading — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 14 1871.

Very heavy Snow Storm all day — Got to studio at 9.30 — To work on circle 8. in" "In the Wilderness" (1st day) Miss Mauny a friend called, anxious to take lessons in Water Colors — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 \+ worked as long as I could see, getting my Sketch finished — Met Belle \+ Mary Treedy Cor. of 26th \& 5th ave, as I Ret'd from lunch — They went up stairs with me \+ made a call — J.W. Bell came in at 3\+4 o'clk — Home to Tea at 7 o'clk — Evef wrote letters to Dr F.W. Lewis, Mr. G.O. Whitney \+ notes to Mr. J.H. Wood \+ E. Schenck — got to bed about 11.30.
