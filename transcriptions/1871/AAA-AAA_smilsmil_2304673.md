## Left Page

### May, Monday, 8, 1871.

Up at 6 o'clk, + wrote letter to Matt — got to studio at 9.25
\+ to work on drawing "Hunting
Ostriches" (2½ days). Found notice
to serve as Juror in Putnam-Foster
murder case, also notes from Dr
Lewis, Seitz + Rob. Dodge. — Mr
Suoch Lewis came in about
1 o'clk — I got home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — worked
until 6 when I got drawing
done. Home to tea at 7 — Aunt
Jessie + Emma got off for Montreal
at 4 P.M. — At 7.45 I went to
Mr. Jas. Ingersoll's + left my Jury
notice with him — at 8 called
on the Treacys — at 9.45 — home —
wrote to Dr. Lewis + to bed
just about 11 —

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday, 9, 1871.

Overcast day — Got to the studio
at 9 o'clk — Lighted a fire and
got to work working on drawing
"Loading Llamas" by Hitchcock.
Miss Bascombe came + drew
during the M'g. At 12.30 went
with her to N.A.D — Saw drawing
in Library, by scholars of N.A. Then went
to Y.M.C.A. — Called in Mr. Culver
Wm Hart + R.S. Gifford — Home to lunch
at 2 — back at 2.45 — worked on
"Llamas" until 5 o'clk — then took
up Miss Ripley's head + worked
on that until near 7 o'clk. Home
to tea at 7.15 — Evening
writing + fussing about until
10.30 To bed a little before 11 —
