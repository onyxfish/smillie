## Left Page

### July, Thursday 27, 1871

Up about 5.30, after a very cold night \-
Blfst. bet. 6 & 7 \- At 7.30 Uncle A. J. met Matt
got away for Clark's \- at 8 we were off with
the Kittredge party of Hartford, for the Valley \-
About 10 we Reached Inspiration Point
\- there for half an hour, then slowly down
the Trail, gathering Sugar from the Sugar
Pine \+ enjoying myself generally \- Geo.
\+ I stopped below the Hermitage and
got general views of the valley. I also
paid forfeit to Sugar Pine \- then on and
at 2.30 lunched at the Illinois Spring
\- on at 3.15, stopping to get a Sketch
of some oaks \+ the Bridal Veil \- The
afternoon effects over the valley, exquisite.
got to Leidig's about 6 o'clk, found the
Kittredge party there \- a bath \- then tea \-
Eve'g with Mr. K. and to bed also [illegible]

## Right Page

### July, Friday 28, 1871

Up early \- after b'cfst. got to work on
my sketches that I have brought back
with me, aided by many photographs
that I have found here. Worked
during the m'g with pencil \+
afternoon with water colors \-
Eve'g, magnificent moonlight;
walked beyond Hutchings's, \+ over
his bridge \- Never saw anything
finer \+ was much impressed by
the grandeur of the Rock forms
in the still, mysterious light of the
moon \- Did not get in until near
[illegible] \- then to bed \-
