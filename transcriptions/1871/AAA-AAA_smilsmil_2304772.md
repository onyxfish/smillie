## Left Page

### November Friday, 24 1871.

Miserable Rainy day — Up late feeling headachy — got to Studio at 10 o'clk. — Spent the m[orning] my Petronchini's [illegible] Studios Sawrie came in \+ sold me the little lay-figure that belongs to my house — $16.— — Home to lunch at 1.30 — at 2.30 — at Phil. Rehearsal — after that, went to Seavitts thru to Pennys thru to Studio \+ home to tea at 6.30 — feeling unfit for anything else, went over to Durin[g's] — there until 11 — home \+ to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 25 1871.

Charming day — got to Studio at 9.20 and set to work painting on an old canvas 16×26 — "On the Saco at North Conway" — a subject that I commenced two or three years ago and for which I have a frame — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 \+ worked until near 4 when I got over Wiley \+ mountains, Wordsworth — Thompson came in — Home to tea at 6.30 — myself "bumming" around — went up to Burgegraff's \+ washed brushes. To bed at 10.30
