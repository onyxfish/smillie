## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 25 1871.

Charming day \- Bkfst at 8.30 \- a few little messages attended to \+ soon after 10 o'clk \- Misses Miller, Drinker \+ Churchill \- Mr. Schentz \+ Jas. Carmalt came \+ spent the mng until 12.30, looking over our photos \+ talking California \- Rec'd a letter from Maud D'Iveu, saying Come on Wednesday \- Telegraphed to her "all right" \- Dinner at 1.30. At 3.15 Mr. Carmalt called \+ took gre \+ me down to Miss Sarah Walker's with Mr. Schentz \+ Misses Miller, Drinker \+ Churchill \- a very pleasant drive \+ visit \- Back at 6.30 \- tea at 7 \- at 8.30 went to Mr. Dud. Lathrop's by invitation \+ there met Miss Jane Post \- Sue Holt, Kate Searle, Mollie Sayre \+ Emma Richards \- At 10 \- went home with Miss J. P. Sue \+ Kate \- went in \+ talked with Kate until 11 \- Home \+ to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 26 1871.

Raining heavily \- Bkfst at 8 o'clk \- Very much disappointed that we could not go to "Lake-Side" to dine with Elisha Mulford. Mr. Jas. Carmalt called about 9.30 to say that they thought it best not to go, as we had arranged to \- So, spent the mng in packing trunk \- Dinner about one o'clk \- Some reading, after \- then some napping \- then some packing \- then note at note to Mr. Elisha Mulford \- then tea at 7 \- at 8, with Gre called on Mr. Wm Coolper \- at 8.45 on Dr. and Sayre and his household \- Home at 10.20 \+ to bed at 10.45.
