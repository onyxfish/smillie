## Left Page

### July, Saturday, 29, 1871

Still cloudless weather and decidedly cooler. Up at 6 — The Kittredge party got off about 8 — I got to work with water colors on sketches in my small book that had been taken while off on my little Yo-Semite trip, trying while the impression is strong to get something intelligible. Worked very steadily all day \+ at night did'nt feel at all well — stomach out of order — got to bed shortly after 9.
At 3 P.M. had a call from Mr. King, "an artist" boarding at Hutchings's.
