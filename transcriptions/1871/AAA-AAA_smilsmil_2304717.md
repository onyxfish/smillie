## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 23 1871.

Milder with some indications of a change — Bkfst at 8 — Read papers + about, waiting for some of the town's people, to whom I had promised to show my photos. Mr. Azur Lathrop came a little before 11 — with her until 11.30 then I went down town — about until 12.30 — Then went with Joe Williams — Joe \+ I dined with him in his Rooms — a very pleasant little old batchelor dinner — around town again until after 3, calling on Albert Post \+ Fannie Holet \+ Sue — Went to bed at 8.30 \+ slept until near 6 — tea at 7 \+ at 8.30 went with Joe \+ Ge. to Fanny Drinker's — a few friends there — home at 11.30 — Read until after 12 to bed at 12.45 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 24 1871.

Quite a warm, pleasant day — Bkfst at 9 o'clk — Went to the Baptist Ch — in the mf and heard the Regular pastor — then, with Uncle Wm \+ George, went to the "old mother Tumell," home at 1 o'clk — dinner about 2 — chatting on piazza after — some reading — then a nap — Up at 4 — wandering over the garden — then with Uncle Wm, Joe W. \+ Ge. \+ Carrie went up to Fair Ground — Tea at 7 — about 7.45 went to Episco. Ch — heard Mr. Warriner — then to Fan M's — walked with her \+ Mr. Childs for awhile — then with him alone until 10 o'clk — then home — Read until after 11 — To bed at 11.45 —
