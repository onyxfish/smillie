## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 6, 1871

another very blustering, cold day \-
Up late + did not get to Studio
until 9.45 \- then to work on Summer
studies \- worked until 1.15 or m.
then home to lunch \- back at
2.30 + worked as long as I could
see on another. Home to tea
at 5.45 \-. at 7.30 at the last
meeting of the Chicago Relief
Fund committee at the N.A. of D \-
from there home at 11 \- a
few brushes to wash, then
to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 7, 1871

Up late, as usual now. Dull m\-
a fall of snow during the night \-
got to the Studio at 9.45 and
set to work on summer studies
again, at it until nearly
2 o'clk. then home to lunch.
Spent the afternoon writing
a "Mail" letter \- at 5.45. walked
down to 6th Ave + 13th St, a message
then back to tea \- After tea
tried to write, felt stupid \-
went to bed until 10 o'clk \-
then got up + wrote until
1.30 a.m \- then to bed \-
