## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 18, 1871

overcast day — got to studio at 8.25 — fixed study in frame and thus on big drawing (16 days) working over the figure \+ horse — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Rode down to Nehlig's to borrow a book of him — not in — back to studio at 2.30 \+ worked until near 5 o'clk — Kate came in to sit with gauntlets for me — she sick with headache, so that she went home — At 5.10 I lay down on lounge there until 6 — Home to tea at 6.30 — Mr. Brush daughter of Duncan Dunbar — to tea — At 7.30 at N.A. of D. Dr. Rimmer's 5th Lecture — Home at 9.30 and to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 19, 1871

overcast day \+ rather cold. Rode down to Nehlig's studio, borrowed a book (costume) \+ got to my studio at 9.5 — so work at once on large dr. (17 days) all day working over the figure \+ horse which are now in a condition to leave until I finish up the other parts of the drawing — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.5 — Rec'd notes from W. T. Blodgett \+ Culver of Chicago — about 4 o'clk. Dr. Schuyler came in \+ soon after Annie \+ Mary came in with her younger sister and Allie Peck — They stayed but a few minutes — Dr. left at 4.45 — Neusfer came in \+ stayed until 6.15 — Home to tea at 6.30 — Ev'g Reading newspapers touching up Rimmer notes and writing to B. \+ Culver — Chicago \+ T.H. Wood. Utica — To bed at 10.30
