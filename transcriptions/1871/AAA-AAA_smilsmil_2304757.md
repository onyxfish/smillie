## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 31 1871.

Fine day \- got to studio at 9.10 and to work biting in die \- stuck to it pretty closely until 1.30 then home to lunch \- back at 2.30 \- Mr. Davis came \- I introduced her to Blackman \- she is to give her lessons \- I worked until about 5 o'clk \- I could see no longer \- So gave two [illegible], cleansing [illegible] in die thoroughly + leaving it to go on with biting to-morrow. Home to tea about 6 o'clk \- Evep spent writing notes + being entertained by Will \+ Jos \- got to bed shortly before midnight.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 1 1871.

Pouring down Rain \- Up late, did not get to studio until near 10 \- went on with biting of my etching + was pleased to find that it went off very nicely \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- Cleaned off die + found the line all that I had expected \- weather cleared during the afternoon \- worked until near 5 \- D. Yale came in \- I walked down to 10th St. Studios with notes \- then to Currie's about curtains \- then Rode to Hq'rs \+ left note for Ward \- Home to tea at 6.45 \- Aunt Agnes + Belle there, Evep making pencil drawing for Northern Pacific R.R. pass \- got to bed about 10 o'clk \-
