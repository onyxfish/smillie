## Left Page

### October, Sunday 15, 1871

Very mild, over cast and windu — El. Parker, of Po'keepsie — came in just as I was going to ch. — So I get to Hastings' rather late — Sept

On the Chicago fire of course — very stirring, eloquent \+ excellent — After service, walked down to studio — back to dinner at 1 — a short nap \+ at 3.30 went to St. Thomas's — fine music as usual \+ a passable sermon from a stranger — Chicago, mildly. Tea at 6 — We'd did not go out — writing — a short walk at 10 — Then in and wrote a letter to father — To bed about 11.20 —

## Right Page

### October, Monday 16, 1871

Threatening with Rain between \+12 o'clk. Rode down to Brook's [illegible] then to 33 Delancey St, at 9.30 to see Schaad about Phil. tickets — Back to studio about 10 — Woman there to scrub — worked with her moving \+ dusting until 11.30 — Then to 5th Ave Savings Bank, Saw the Pres't \+ Cash, — Reassured me. Then at 12.30 home to lunch — To A.B. M. Co. at 1.20 — went to Delisser's — back at 1.45 — Then to work laying a ground \+ finishing dap. outline of "Hunting Ostriches" — At 5 o'clk went again to Schaad's — Rode up town — Called at Ireland's — Saw no one, then to the Kimball's \+ saw all of them — Home to tea at 7 — After g Will \+ Joe came in — they left at 1.20 \+ I got to bed about 11.30
