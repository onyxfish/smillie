## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 18, 1871.

Superb day \- At 8.30 went with Father to Dr Noyes, who examined very carefully his ear, + declared the drum of the left ear destroyed got to Studio at 9.30 \- Transferred + continued drawing of "Mules S.A." (1st day on drawing.) Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- Suffering with a headache \- I only called. worked as long as I could \- See then home to tea about 7 o'clk. Even, some Reading, a little writing on Fergus's letter and to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 19, 1871.

North-Easterly wind \- Raw \- overcast + Rain about 5. P.M. \- Got to Studio at 9.10 + to work on Mules (1st day) working steadily until 12.30 \- Then rushed up the room + home to lunch a few minutes before 2 o'clk. \- back at 2.45 \- Miss Tiffany had come, but left when she found that I had finished her drawing. Misses R. (24) J. (17) \+ M (11) were there. \- labored with them until near 6 \- Miss Robbins [illegible] made a very pleasant call. Home to tea at 7 o'clk. Showery and cold \- Even, Reading Atlantic until 10 o'clk \- then finished letter to Fergus on + got to bed just before 12 \-
