## Left Page

### April, Thursday 6, 1871.

Glorious day, but a sharp headachy. got to studio at 9.20 \+ spent the mft. finishing crayon sketch of "Llamas". Home to lunch at 1, back at 2.15 \+ worked all the afternoon on sketch of "Mules, mts. of S.a." At 4.30. ment to Schmidts Sileck \+ meeting of Art commi. of Y.M.C.a \- Back at 5.30 and worked until 6.30 \- Then ment to 57 W. 19th S\- to see Jessie Hayland model, to have her come to studio to-morrow \- Home to tea at 7.10 with drawing anatomical studies \+ Reading newspaper. to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Friday 7, 1871.

Very warm Spring day, at times sunny, then almost threatening Rain. got to studio at 9 o'clk \- worked on sketch "mules S.a." until about 10 \- misses R. (21) J. (20) \+ W. (9) came \+ drew until 1 o'clk \- Then Fanny Kimball called to invite me to go to Staten Island with them to-morrow P.M. Home to lunch at 1.45 down to A.B.N. Co. with sketches at 3 \- at 4 started up town, stopping at Seitz, Sarony, Snedecor's Brooks \+ Dechaume \- Stopped at Siffany's with Miss Annie's purse, on my way home at 7 o'clk \- Quite very tired \- Read newspapers and to bed before 10 \-
