## Left Page

### December Monday, 18 1871.

Miserable day with some sleet & Rain — Got to studio a little before 9 — Kindled fire & then to work on Cathedral Rocks study — Hard at it until 2 o'clk when I went home to dinner — back at 3.15 after stopping a few minutes at E. D. E. Greene's Studio — The P.M. — short & dark — very little accomplished. Home to tea at 6 — Svening writing Mail letters until near 11.30 — To bed a little before midnight.

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 19 1871.

Dismal snowy day — Got to studio ab't 9 o'clk — and to work on study of "Cathedral Rock from foot of El Capitán". Painted over it quite solidly & more than I have done on any other of my studies — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 & worked over other studies until after 4 — Then, at 5, being very tired, stretched myself on the lounge & napped — Home to tea at 6.10 — Evef[n]g, mounted sub. for Jel— & wrote until 10.30 — to bed soon after 11 —
