## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 29, 1871

Superb day \- Got to studio at 8.30 and to work on Colorado Arms (8 days) Laid solid ground \+ dragged main line of clouding back \+ shield \+ bit Jr. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- S.C.F, called while home \- back to studio at 2.30 \- Met Wm Hinshelwood at thedon \- wonderously civil \- All "my lambs" in \- Miss R. (18.) J. (17.) M. (12) J. (12) \+ M. (6) Worked hard with them until 5.30 but whether they learned any thing I cannot say \- Home at 6.40 \- At Rimmer's last lecture at 7.30 a poor affair, professing to be upon the face, expression, etc. \- out at 8.40 \- went over to gymnasium with Brevoort, there until 9.30 \- then had a glass of lager \+ home at 10 o'clk \- Aunt Jissie \+ Myma with us all night \- Gel arr'd from Boston at about 8 o'clk this A.M. \- to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 30, 1871

Miserable, muzzling day \- Got to studio at 9.10 \- there until 10 o'clk \- then went down town to A.B.N.Co. \- all the mth at work on a drawing for Mars: Soldiers Certificate (½ day) \- went to lunch at 1 o'clk with Father \- then to Continental Bk Note Co. by Request \- they wish to decum me \- but told them I was already mortgaged. Back at 2 o'clk \+ worked until 6.12 on "Colorado Arms" (8¼ days) Walked home stopping at studio \- Home at 7.15 \- tea \- vaccinated \- the rest of eveg loafing in my big chair \- to bed at 10 \-
