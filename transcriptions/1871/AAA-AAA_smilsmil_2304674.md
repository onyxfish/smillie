## Left Page

### May Wednesday, 10 1871.

Fine day. got to studio at 9 o'clk
Cleared up a little \+ spent the
Rest of the a.m. outleuing m
dag. — negine for Coulson back.
Mr Merriam \+ Senator somebody
called. also Miss Bascomb. Home
to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30.
Misses Maury (21) \+ Jones (22)
worked from 2.30 until 5.30 —
Miss Whiting came but would not
stay (16) went to Miller's about
sketching umbrella — then after
boots — nearly 8 when I got home
to tea — Welf went to Burggraf's
then to Mr Morse (Lucy Gibson)
there until 10.15 — Home \+ to
bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 11 1871.

Charming day. got to A.B.N. Co's at
9.30. got transfer of Coulson back die
(1½ days) at 10.30. went to see Charles
bootmaker — at 11 started for up town.
Stopped at Uttsheis \+ bought shirts \+
underclothing. Home to lunch at 1 o'clk
\- at studio at 2.30 — Etched on die
until after 6 o'clk — Home to tea
at 7 — Welf went to Prof Tomayes.
got there at 9 o'clk — stayed
there until 10.30 — Home at 10.45
and to bed at 11.
