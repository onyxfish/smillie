## Left Page

### November Saturday, 18 1871.

Got to the studio about 9 15 and soon at work putting effect into a day, outline, by Deluroc of the new fountain in Cum. Worked until 1 30 then home to lunch. Changed + re-dressed near 3 o'clk. When I got back to studio. Delurocis son came for drawing which I gave to him about it 6 o'clk. At 4 15 with Geo. started for down-town took 5 o'clk train for Plainfield glorious evening. Got there about + walked with Ollie, Joe + his friu Drizzler to the house. Dinner about 7. Went, initiated into the mysteries of Poker. Had a high time betting paste board money. Cheating + being cheat. To bed about 11 45

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 19 1871.

Thick, overcast morning. Up some after 7. Bkfst about 8 30. Morning. went to Ch. (Episcopal) with Mary. After service, all walked for about an hour. Dinner after 2. and at 4 Belle, Mary, Florence, Drizzler, Geo. walked over past Uncle Geo's the others went on, we went in + after visiting half an hour. Re-joined the Treedays getting back about 5 45. Tea about 7 30. very informal. Spent [illegible] in general chatting until 11 o'clk. To bed about 11 30.
