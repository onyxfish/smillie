## Left Page

### July Monday, 17 1871.

Up at 7 o'clk, worn out by another sleepless night. Did not get spunk enough to start out for work until 9.30. Then went to back of Black's \+ by painting mishaps stopped until 12.30 I got a sketch of Sentinel Rock blocked in. Then back to hotel \+ dinner at 1. About 2.30 crossed the River \+ painted until 6 o'clk on sketch of Redwood Tree, getting that blocked in \+ so completing a pretty good days work, altho' thunder storms threatened in the afternoon. Tea about 7 \+ at 8 went with Matt to Black's to call on the party of new acquaintances who are to leave the valley to-morrow. Left at 10 o'clk. To bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### July Tuesday, 18 1871.

Up about 6. Found a stray mule with side saddle. Rode up to Black's \- Miss Alice Buckingham's \- the party just starting. Returning to Seidigs, they passed \+ gave us a salute. B[illegible] at 7.30. Much annoyed about my work owing to the uncertainty of uncle A's movements. At 9.30, got off with my pack \+ wandered down the River a mile or more and painted in a view of Sentinel Rock (No 3). Threatening thunder storm interfered with my light. Lunched at 2 o'clks. Then on to Cathedral Rocks. Picked out a study \+ returned \+ more thunder storms \+ clouds, so as I was tired at 4.30 I started back getting in at 5.30. A bath, reading the paper \+ tea at 6.30. Every mate until 10. To bed soon after.
