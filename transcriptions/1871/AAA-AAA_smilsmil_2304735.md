## Left Page

### October, Monday 9, 1871.

Charming Indian Summer day — got to Studio at 9.30 — worked until after 11 on die mth grave — then down to Brooks Bros. then to Sloans's where I bought a carpet for Studio, then to A.P. Co — went to dinner with Father at 1 o'clk — great excitement over the burning of Chicago — Ruled faces \& ovals on die and at 3.30 started for Studio — another excitement — a Run on the 3d Ave. Savings Bank, where I have deposited Water Color funds. Stopped out \& bit in die — I could hardly see, so late — at 6 went by 3d ave. Bank — could only see the crowd — then home to Tea — Wrote mote letters to Uncle A.V.V — Rulley and Hofkins — To bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 10, 1871.

muggy, overcast m\[illegible\] — got to Studio at 9.30 and worked until about 11 o'clk — then walked over to the 3d ave. Savings Bank — "the Run" still going on — then down-town in a 3d ave. car — Stopped at office of Werf Mail — Townley not in — then to Colton, Zahn \+ Roberts to see Chromo from my Airable drawing. Saw it \+ gave them a "bit of advice". Then to A.P.N. Co — To dinner at 1 with Father. Spent the afternoon making drawings \& ornaments to work into the oval on die — at 5 o'clk went to Mc'Drew" foot of Canal St, \+ saw Father \+ Mother off on their Trip to Canada — went to Wills — 16 St Lukes Pl — \+ had dinner — Light fall of Rain — Home at 10 o'clk — Read newspapers until after 11 — To bed at 11.30
