## Left Page

### August Saturday, 26 1871.

a beautiful m'f, for San Francisco —
Up a little before 7 — bkf't about 8 —
waiting Uncle a's movements until
near 11 — then went to Watkins' the
gallery, saw him + made arrangements
to exchange paintings for his photos —
there until 2 o'clk — then to the
hotel to lunch — at 3, out with Uncle
a, to get tickets to the Geysers —
then to Newhall's + got p't pantaloons —
then again to Newhall Watkins —
+ there until near 6 — Dinner —
7 + at 8 with Uncle a + a party
of 8 went to the Minstrels — had a
hearty laugh — after supper, then
some dancing, etc, etc in Uncle
a's room — and to bed near
midnight

## Right Page

### August Sunday, 27 1871.

Did not get up until near 8
o'clk — Bkfst soon after 9 —
at 11 Rode out with Uncle a
to the "Presideo" and Fort. The
wind very high + fog so thick
that I could not see across the
golden gate. Rode over the hills
to the Cemetery, saw China women
performing Rites. Then to Dolores
mission — went into the old church
+ through the burying ground —
then to the hotel after 3 o'clk,
too late to get lunch. Afternoon
motts to Townley + Ferguson and
took a nap — Dinner at 7 o'clk
— Ev'f a call from W. Mrs. Brown
who would see some of our sketches —
Read from 11 till 12 — then to
bed
