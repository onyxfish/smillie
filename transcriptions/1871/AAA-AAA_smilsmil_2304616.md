## Left Page

### January Sunday, 8 1871

Overcast day — Snow fell briskly from noon until about 5 P.M. — very dry — Wt went to Dr Hastings — heard him preach a most excellent discourse from Acts IV Ch. 32? v — "and the multitude of them that believed were of one heart and of one Soul" on Christian unity — Home to dinner — went to bed at 3 \+ slept until 4.30 — went out to walk soon after 5 — Went up to Central Park — back soon after 6 — Tea — Welf wrote to W. Gu[illegible] Whitney — By W. Lewis \+ Mr J. Richards in behalf of Water Color Sn[illegible] — To bed soon after 11 — to sleep at 12 —

## Right Page

### January Monday, 9 1871

Splendid day — clear \+ cold — got to studio at 9.30. Kindled fire. J.C. Nicoll called on W.C. business. I commenced washing color on my drawing (8½ days) — Spent the day working over \+ over the sky, getting in crude \+ over charged with color — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — The Smeedy girls at the house to see Mary's baby. Worked until about 4 o'clk — Home at 5.30 — tea 6.30 — at 7.30 went to Mr Chas. Tiffany's and spent the eve'g until 10.30 Reading "The Tempest" to Miss Annie T. \+ Allie Peck. Home at 10.40 — and to bed at 11.15.

Aaron McCabe took lunch at our house —
