## Left Page

### April, Friday, 14, 1871.

Pleasant day — feel groggy after yesterday's experience. at 9 went to 19 8= to get model to sit for my class — Got to Studio after meeting Dan Sayre \+ wife, at 9.40 Misses R.(23) S.(22), M.(16) J.(16) \+ M.(10) there. worked with them until 2.30. Home to lunch at 2.45 — back at 3.20 \+ worked on tracing \+ correcting drawing "Ostrich Hunting. S.A." Also worked late on Miss Tiffany's head — Home to tea at 7.20 after stopping at Grand Hotel to Du Sayre \+ wife — notin. Evely went to John Rogers' — 43°, 87 — party call. met there Mr \+ Mrs. Moore (Ningittons) — pleasant even — left at 10 o'clk. Read newspaper until 11 — to bed at 11.30 Uncle Mr. \+ aunt H. went home this p.m.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 15, 1871.

April like day — clouds \+ sunshine — very slight show of shower — Got to Studio at 9.10. Kindled a fire for George's benefit as he paints in my Studio to-day. worked on outline "Ostrich Hunting" correcting drawing of horse, etc. — Lost time by getting interested \+ working some on Misses Tiffany's \+ Whiting's drawings. Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45. Last Perspective lesson from 3 to 4 — worked on Llama drawing until [illegible] o'clk — Home to tea at 6.20 — at 9.15 went with Kate to Rivarde concert at Apollo Hall — met there lots of pleasant folks that I know — so, passed an agreeable evening. Home about 11 \+ to bed at 11.45
