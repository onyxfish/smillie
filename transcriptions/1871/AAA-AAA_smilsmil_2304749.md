## Left Page

### October, Monday 23, 1871

Very mild delightful day \- got to studio som after 9 and at work etching horse on ostrich die (5 3/4 days) Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30. Afternoon considerably broken in upon \- Allie Diven came to ask us to call to-morrow evef. Misses Maung \+ Jones called; then Mary I needy \- to them me showed Sketches, photographs etc, etc \- woman came \+ blacked stove. Home to tea at 6.30 \- evef, wrote notes until after 8 \- about 9 got to Palette club \- at 10 \- home, Read awhile and to-bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 24, 1871

Rain when I got up \- Raw but hardly a cold day \- Got to studio at 9.20 \- Etching in die (6 3/4 days) Mr. Ford, the Baptist minister of Montrose came in about 1 o'clk \- I got home to lunch about 1.45 \+ back at 2.30. So dark that I could not see after 4 o'clk \- went over to Y. M. C. A. Bldg to see Wm Hark \- Saw Brevoort instead \- then to Knoedler's \+ bought mill board. Back to Studio \- Magrath's there. Sketched out idea for Chicago Relief Picture. Home to tea at 6 o'clk \- At 8, Geo. \+ I went to No. 19 2. 31st \+ had a pleasant evef with Misses Loudon and Diven \+ young Jessup \- Home at 10 \- to bed at 10.30.
