## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 14 1871.

Overcast — muf., about noon it began to Rain + blow and by night it was a Regular driver. Got to Studio at 8.45 + worked on Japanese drawing for Continental Bk. Note Co. until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — back at 2.15 — worked for about an hour when Gen. Hearn came in, then his wife — He decided to take my "Lifting of the clouds" + give me $250 for over two months hard work. Reinhart came about Chicago Sale — then into Colman's Room — Board of Control of Water Color S. Home to tea at 7 — lighted fire in the furnace. Wrote minutes of last Chicago fund meeting + a note to Elder Post + to bid at 10.30

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 15 1871.

Cloudy + overcast — feel very tired. Got to Studio at 9.30 + to work on Small drawings for Pass over Northern Pacific R.R. Clurny came + spent most of muf. putting up new shades on Skylights. Fred Smillie's came up about o'clk + got drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — then went to Leavitt's on Chicago fund business — then to 10½ Studios on the same errand. Saw Page, Perry, Beard + Townley — Back to Studio at ½ p. 15. Rec'd a set of 47 photos from Reilly of Stockton, Cal. Very much pleased. Home to tea at 6.30 — At 8 o'clk. called on Mr + Mrs. Sant. Colman. 106 Madison Ave. — There until 10 — home at 10.30 — to bed at to bed at 11.45
