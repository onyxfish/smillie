## Right Page

### January, Sunday 1, 1871

commenced the year badly — was routed out of bed before daylight with colic or something else — near 9 o'clk, before I was up for all day — Got to Dr. Hastings late, but heard his text for the year" Rejoicing in hope" — Rom. XII ch. 12" v. — Dinner at home with the usual noise & confusion. P.M. in my big chair, as I did not feel able to go out altho' it was a fine day, mild with cloud & sunshine — Read most of the time — Eveft went with father to Madison ave & heard Mr. Elder "For now is the appointed time" — He was very good & earnest — Home — beautiful moonlight night — until near 11 o'clk writing out New Years list — just 50 calls — To bed about 11.15
