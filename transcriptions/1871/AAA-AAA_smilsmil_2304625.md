## Left Page

### January Thursday, 26 1871.

Heavy snow storm all day. Got to studio at 9.30 — Worked all the mf on Chapel Pond 9×12 — getting it retouched but not much to my satisfaction. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 — P.M. working around on Mr. Whitney's 20×24 painting out parts — Sketching Niblie's big tolo trots for Marylands arms, etc — at 4.30 went over to N.A. of D. through big storm much vexed to find the title of my drawing wrong in catalogue. Back in half an hour — washed up — Home at 6.50 — Evef wrote notes to Misses Blodgett, T. Saunders, Sackett \+ Dickel — To bed at 12 o'clk —

## Right Page

### January Friday, 27 1871.

Glorious day — up late — got to studio at 9.30 — Soon after 10 Misses Tiffany \+ Ripley came to take their first lesson of me. Gave a little talk on perspective then a drawing in blk — Spent most of the A.M. with them. Home to lunch at 1.30 — Rode down to Philharmonic Rehearsal at 2.30 Enjoyed "Sacuntala" immensely Jenny Tweedy there — Back to studio at 4.30 — Belle \+ Mary Tweedy with me — Home to tea at 6.15. Evef writing dunning notices to Water-Color members — To bed about 11 —
