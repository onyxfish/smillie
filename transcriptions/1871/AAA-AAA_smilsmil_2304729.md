## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 3 1871.

Muf overcast — Enjoyed a regular Scrub — Bkfo. \+ got to Studio at 10 o'clk. Most of muf going about my Room, dozed like — Saw Jas. Hart — Foreworth, Blackman, Colman \+ Dodworth — Rain about noon — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30, but did n't manage to accomplish anything on Bank note work — Burling \+ Colman called — \+ later, Angers — Home to tea at 6.30 — Mr. Hy Thompson called to urge Geo \+ me to join a little literary Social Society the Portfolio — Lots to do, but feeling unequal to it, loafed the evening away and to bed at 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### October Wednesday, 4 1871.

A very pleasant day — altho' part of the time overcast. Got to Studio about 9.30 — worked awhile on A.B. N. Co.'s drawing, Some S. a. arms — then went down town — completed the drawing \+ spent the rest of the day with Dickinson, getting ovals etc, drawn up on the die for the engraving of S. a. arms. At 5.30 walked up home, stopping at the Kimball's in 25°. 32. Home to tea at 7.15 — Eveg, arranging money accts with George \+ writing to Knickerbocker \+ Townley — To bed at 11 — Belle \+ Mary Tweedy at the Studio during the P.M.
