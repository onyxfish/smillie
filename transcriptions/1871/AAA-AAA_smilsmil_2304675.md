## Left Page

### May, Friday 12, 1871

Pleasant day — got to studio's at 9 o'clk — worked on water. color sketchu for pupils — they came about 10 o'clk — Misses M. (22) J. (23) \+ V. (17) and worked until 1.30 — I got home to lunch at 2 — back at 3 and etched on Engine, coupon back (2 days) until 6.30. Interrupted by calls from Mrs. Wilder \+ friend Wm Hart \+ Jno. A. Fraser. Home to tea at 7.15 — very tired and useless — Ab\+ went to St. Cloud back at 10 o'clk — To bed at 10. 15

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 13, 1871

Charming day — Up at 6 o'clk and made a letter to Dr F. W. Lewis — Got to studio's at 9 o'clk \+ etched all the mft on Engine die (2½ days) Will Kimball called \+ at his invitation went home at 2 — to Kimball's at 2. 15. Had a lunch — then with Fanny \+ Minnie I went to foot of Chambers St. there met Will K — \+ Ned \+ Annie Cook \+ Mr. Valentine — Took 3.30 Train on N. H. J. R.R. to Tenafly — there at 4. 40 — walked to the tofs of the Palisades, Hudson River — a little below Yonkers — The woods lovely — plenty of wild flowers which were largely gathered — at 6. 45 started back — Sunset \+ Hirlight walk — got to station at 7. 30 — tired \+ jolly — all sorts of detentions so that we did not get into N.Y. until 10 — Took Fanny home \+ I got home after 11 \+ to bed about midnight — Rec'd note from Mr. Enoch Lewis enclosing R.R. passes for J. \+ I. from Phila to Cinn. \+ from Chicago back to Phila.
