## Left Page

### October Friday, 27 1871.

Still horrible weather — quite used up with a headache but a little after 9 got to the studio determined to fight through it if possible — Worked all the mng. on an old drawing by Deluoce + some S.A. arms. Home to lunch at 1 o'clk \+ then to bed where I stayed until 7 o'clk — then up, some tea — Read awhile \+ lounged on the sofa until 2 o'clk. A.M. when I went to bed. Glorious moonlight.

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 28 1871.

Crisp \+ charming — got to studio at 9.30 \+ worked on another S.A. drawing by Deluoce all day. At 2 went to the barber's \+ had my hair cut. Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — on my way back stopped at Geo. Hall's studio on Chicago business — Worked until neardusk when I got drawing finished. Home to tea a little before 6 — After tea Read awhile — At 7.30 went to Am. Mr. fair, 3d ave, 63d St. — particularly to see a dove-tailing machine for Mr. Brown of Sacramento. Saw it. At 10, walked over to the Central Park — through to 6th ave, \+ down home — 10.45 — Read again \+ to bed at 12.
