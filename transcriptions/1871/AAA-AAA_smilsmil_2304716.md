## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 2 1871.

A very hot day — Up at 5. a.m. \+ gathered Mistletoe for Mr. Kittende. Bkfs. at 6 30 — at 7.30, off to my work; took pencil sketch of the Domes from Black's Point, then to my study of Cathedral Rocks \+ the Valley from back of the house finished the painting in at 12 — Then to the hotel very tired and took a nap — At 2, went over the River \+ worked until 6 on study of the Domes, 3d sitting. Tea at 7 — Wef wrote a scolding letter home, then to bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### August Thursday, 3 1871.

A hotter day. Bkfst at 7, and about got to work on study at the back room of the house. Infernally hot \+ all sorts of trouble, so that I worked in misery until 12 o'clk, my eyes so troublesome that I stretched myself on the Verandah an my blanket at 3/4 of an hour. Dinner at 1 \+ at 2 went over to my Dome study, 4th sitting and worked until 5.30 Did not get as much done as I had hoped to — Wef, no boarders so we had everything our own way, chatted with DeNaney \+ Stegman until 9 o'clk — To bed at 18.20
