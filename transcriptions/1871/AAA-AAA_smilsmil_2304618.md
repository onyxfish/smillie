## Left Page

### January, Thursday 12, 1871.

Cloudy m'f, but cleared to a bright mild and Spring-like day. Got to studio at 9 o'clk — again on drawing (11½ days) finishing up the distance and then again going over foreground with crude color — About 1 o'clk went to Walen \+ changed the sable brush I bought there last night — then home to lunch at 1.15 — back to studio at 2.15 \+ worked until 4.50 — then as my head felt very tired I stretched myself on the lounge until 6 — Home to tea at 6.30. Evef, Reading White's "Words \+ their uses" and talking domestic matters with Mary. To bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 13, 1871.

Another mild \+ charming day. got to studio at 9 o'clk. On drawing (12½ days) working all day on the horse — getting very much interested in the head — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 — and worked until near 5 — Mr. Prof. Barnard called and invited me to call on any Monday evef. — Some friends of Emma Beach called later — At 5 went to Sawthere's then to Snedecors — then to Wilworth's — then to Studio — fixed — fine home to tea at 7.15 — Evef tired — Read a little — wrote notes to Mr. Culver — Treat. of Chicago Academy \+ Mr. Wm Mulford — To bed at 10.30
