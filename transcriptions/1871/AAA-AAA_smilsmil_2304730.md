## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 16 1871.

a very hot day. Rlkft about 7
Ab 8, went to mtf, River study
(2d sitting) and worked on
until after 12, when I got it
done — back to lunch and
at 2, went to afternoon River
study (2d sitting) \+ worked on
that until 6 when I got it
done. Then began to feel
unwell \+ was so uncomfortable
that I could not eat anything
at tea time. Surf [illegible]
around — washed brushes and
got to bed soon after 10 oclk.
