## Left Page

### February Sunday, 19 1871.

Trying hard to be pleasant but mainly overcast \- Just at Church time walked down to Dr Shrodes Pharmacy \+ got some Podelphillin for Father \- got to Dr. Hastings late but in time to hear a first rate Sermon from John 8th Ch. 31-2nd V. \- Home after service \- Father having a hard time \- At 12.30 started for A.V.V.'s \- got there at 1.40 through mud and snow \+ water. Uncles A.V.V. \+ Wm Surrell \- Aunt H. Junie \+ a young lady from Montrose to dinner \- Everything lovely \- Home to tea about 6.30 \- At 8.30 went to Studio carrying a p.k. \- then to Madison Ave. Baptist Church, met the Whites (Hemands.) and walked home with Matt and Kate \- To bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### February Monday, 20 1871.

Very pleasant day \- got to Studio about 9 \- Kindled fire and until after 11, sweeping dusting and clearing up my dirty nurse \- Then got to work with graver on die of Niagara (2 days) Mrs Forbes called \+ gave me a ticket to the Dramatic entertainment and Ball of the "Palette" club to-night \- Home to lunch at near 2 o'clk \- back at 3 o'clk \+ worked until 5.45 then home \- tea \- dressed \- \+ at 8 o'clk. was at "Allemania Hall" 16th St \- the performance commenced about 9 o'clk Supper at midnight \- then dancing \- During the Eve'g with W. \+ Mrs. Forbes and a lady friend \- Enjoyed things generally but not enthusiastically \- left at 12.45 \- quite Rain \- Home at 1 o'clk \+ to bed instanter
