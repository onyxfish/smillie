## Left Page

### July Sunday, 9 1871.

Superb day \- Late bkfst \+ waited after it for letters \- none from home \- Matt Rec'd 2 from Po'keepsie \+ Geo. Rec'd one from Mary Tweedy \- I went down to the Riverside \+ spent the m'f on my blanket, writing. Wrote a note to Fanny Kimball \- Dinn. at near 3 \- Afternoon finished my first letter for the "Mail" \- At sunset, went across the River with Geo. to see the sun set on the Domes \- I climbed to the top of the talus under the Three Bro. back at 7.30 \- Tea \- With Rec'd a letter from Father with the news of Aunt Margaret's sudden death on Wed. June 28 \- After writing got to bed about 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July Monday, 10 1871.

Still splendid weather. Up early \+ had bkfst about 7 as M. Webb \+ daughter start for home this m'f. Bet. $19 got to my work \- Yosemite Fall, Repainting all that did on Sat. back to Hotel at 12.30 \+ waited until 1.30 for my dinner \- at 2 went to study Sentinal Rock \+ Repainted all that. Gave up at 5 o'clk. Crossed the River \+ climbed the talus back of Leidig's winter house \- got a light sketch \- Back to Hotel at 8 o'clk Tea \- loafing \- \+ to bed at about 10 o'clk \-
