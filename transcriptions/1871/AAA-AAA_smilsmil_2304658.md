## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 4, 1871

Cloudy m'g. Changing into a magnificent day — Went to Book Bros. paid bill \+ got to studio at 9.30 — Worked on sketch for drawing "Hunting S.a. oo Frriches until 12 — Then to Knoedler's, \+ to Wm Hart's \+ B rain Gifford's — Home to lunch at 1.45 — back to studio's at 2.30 \+ worked on ostrich \+ Llama sketches until 6.15 — Wenzler came in \+ talked until 7.30 — Home to tea at 7.45 — Eve'g wrote a letter to Dr Lewis, \+ notices to Art Conn. Y.M.C.A — Got to bed about 11

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 5, 1871

Superb day, but windy — Uncle Willie am. from Ottawa about 7. a.m — I got to studio at 8.45 — Swelp up then to work on sketch for Llama drawing — Feeling very heavy \+ dyspeptic — worked as well as I was able (would count the ½ day against a B.N.Co) until 1 oclk — then home to lunch — found there Jerusha Gordler \+ daughters \+ Nette Sutphen — Back to studio at 2.30 — all of my lambs there, Misses R.(20) J.(19) M.(14), D.(14) \+ W.(8) — Miss Perkins called — All left at 5.30 — Then [illegible] Wood came in — Then Wenzler — Home to tea at 7.10 — \+ We'g went down to the Tweedy's to call with them on W.P. Tom P.meedy but just as we were about to start Mr \& Mrs. Tom. J. came in — Home at 11.10 \+ to bed. at 11.30
