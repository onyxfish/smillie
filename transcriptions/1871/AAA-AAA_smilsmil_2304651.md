## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 21 1871.

Heavy Rain during the morning got to studio at 8.30 and to work on drawing (5 days) \+ stuck to it until 1.15 — then home to dinner — Rode down to Emil Seitz's to get mounting board — not in — back to studio at 3.5 worked on drawing until 4.30 — then on Uncle Willie's die "Niagara" as long as I could see — Colp Cutt called at near 6 — went over to the "Albemarle." to see Messrs Whitney, Lewis \+ Richards — not in — home to tea at 7 — A long, chatty, clever \+ characteristic letter from Meta gave it Adams — Swept \+ Read "Atlantic" until near 11 — clear, cold night — To bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 22 1871.

Superb day — Got to studio soon after 8 o'clk — Swept \+ picked up — then to work on drawing again — At it for two hours, until 11 o'clk, when I went down town — In the mean time Messrs Whitney \+ Lewis came in \+ bade me good bye — Went to Fate's at 1.30 to get "Niagara" proved, but could not, then to A.B.\&C. — All pleased with drawing — Lunched at 1 with Father — Got [illegible] from Graley \+ back to studio at 2.15 — found Misses Ripley (16) Tiffany (15) Manny (10) Jones (10) and Whiting (4) also model — Hard at work with them until 6 o'clk — Home to tea at 6.45 — At Rimmer's lecture at 7.30 — at 9 went to studio — home at 10 \+ to bed at 10.45
