## Left Page

### November Saturday, 4 1871.

Overcast \+ mf and quite wintry. Got to studio at 9 o'clk. Wrote a note to J. Q. A. Ward — then commenced painting (2d. day) on Chicago [illegible] picture 12½×16 — worked until [illegible] then home to lunch, — back at [illegible] \+ worked until 4.30 when I could see no longer — am half pleased — the composition is very [illegible] but some parts of the color — the sky etc. seem to me promising — Took it home with me — Tea at 6.30 Eve'g assorting seeds that I brought from Cal. \+ washing brushes — got to bed about 11.45

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 5 1871.

Charming day — Up late \+ did not get to Dr. Hastings' until near 11 — Heard him preach from the 114th V. 90th Psalm — After service home — Aunt Agnes \+ Belle there, dinner at 1.30 — after dinner, fixing Cal. flower seeds in pots until near 11 o'clk — then walked up to St. Thomas's — [illegible] service — out at 4.35 — With [illegible] walked up to Central Park — home to tea at 6 — Eve'g didn't go out — tried to write but did n't make it out — got to bed about 11.
