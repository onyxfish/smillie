## Left Page

### February Thursday, 23, 1871.

Overcast day — got to studio at 9.30 \+ spent the A.M. finishing small drawing of Union Dime Savings Bank (3|4 of a day) — Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.30 — coud n't get to work as I was anxious \+ waiting to see Dodworth — Saw him at 3.30 — The interview severely polite — My Rent is Raised $104.56. making it $450 00 p. annum. — I keep on for another year — Mr. Hunt and another lady called — my P.M. wasted. Experimented some with fir fumes by vaponsing etc — Shellac \+ gutta percha — at 5.45 walked to Schans's mth Jas. Hart — then to Snedecors — then to Macy's — then to Schmitts — then to Studio \+ home at 7.40 — tea at 8. Read newspaper — put pins in drawing board \+ to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### February Friday, 24, 1871.

ned hard to be an exceedingly disagreeable day and for the most part succeeded. — Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. — About 9.30 Miss Siffany, then Miss Ripley came (2d lesson.) Soon after Misses Jones \+ Maury (3d lesson) worked all the mf with them until 12.30 — Home to lunch at 1.20 — at 2.30 went to Phil. Rehearsal — Enjoying again exceedingly the Schumann No. 3 — Symphony. Met Misses Fanny Parsons \+ Many Biddle — Back to Studio at 4.30. Young Verplanck came in — then \+ Johnston — Home to tea at 6.30 — Uncle A.V.V. in for a time — California Falls — wrote notes to Gaylord, Grannins \+ Nicoll — got to bed at 10.45
