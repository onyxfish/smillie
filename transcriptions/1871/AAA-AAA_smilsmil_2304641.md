## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 1 1871.

Delightful spring day — got to Studio at 9.20 — worked until 12 o'clk on Colorado Arms (1½ day) — got it finished — at noon went to 3d Ave. Savings Bk. \+ deposited money for Water Color Society — then to Knoedler's — met there Mr. Wm Dart — then to Studio at 12.50 — Russell with pictures from Sommerville — Didn't get home to lunch until 1.45 — back at 2.20 — all my haunts there ahead of me — 10th lesson for Misses R. \+ J. 4th lesson for Misses M. \+ J — a Miss Whiting called to see if I would give her lessons — couldn't. Home to tea at 6.30 — Rimmer lectures at 7.30 — Home at 10 — mounted Colorado Arms and to bed at 11.20

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 2 1871.

Another charming Spring day — feel it in my head — got to Studio at 9 o'clk — To work on Sketch for drawing for North Western Mutual Life Ins. Co. — feeling very stupid — Mr. Burney called — wants me to serve on Art Com. Y.M.C.A. Home to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.25 and worked as long as I could see, finishing the Sketch — Miss E. G. Field — Miss E. M. Ward \+ ladies \+ others called — Home to tea at 7 — Spent matte note to Bellows — arranged Water Color accts and got to bed at 10.30
