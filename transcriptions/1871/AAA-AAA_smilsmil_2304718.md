## Left Page

### August Friday, 4 1871.

Very hot. Up late, so that I did not get off to my work until 9 o'clk — worked on 3 central Rock Study, back of Black's until 1 o'clk, when I got it finished. Back to hotel at 1.30, at 2.30 crossed the River to my study of the Domes and worked on that until 6.15 when I got it all laid in (5th sitting). Back to hotel at 7 — After tea washing brushes & trying to get oil out of a crayon sketch, spattered over it by gel — got to bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### August Saturday, 5 1871.

A very hot day. At 7.45 started up the valley to make a new study of the South Dome. Hutchings called me in to give me a p[ac]k of letters from Father, Mary & Matt. I read them as I walked. Got to my study & at work soon after 9 — worked very closely until 12.30 when I got my study finished after a fashion — left, chased by a threatening thunderstorm that amounted to nothing after all. Got to the Hotel at 1.30 — lunch — felt very tired, eyes hurt me, no sunlight, so I went to Pilley's photo tent; saw him & negatives that he has just brought back from Mono. Back to hotel soon after 6. The Ashburner party with Rip, Dawes, wife, two daughters & others arr— Chatting with Mr. Gammel until after 10 — to bed about 11 —
