## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 11, 1871.

Still splendid weather — Bkfst at 7.30, So I got off to my work tolerably early, about 8.15 — Worked on Yo. Semite fall until noon, getting along very slowly — Dinner, soon after 1 and at 2 went to my study of Sentinal Rock. Found a lot of Squaws, near my place, at work making acorn bread. Interfered with my work materially as I was so much interested in the process that I had to watch it from beginning to end — They were very friendly \+ entertained me more hospitably than my stomach cared for. They also cooked some kind of greens — I did not like either the bread or the greens — Back about 6. Wrote awhile — Tea after 7 — Read newspapers \+ to bed at 9.45.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 12, 1871.

Cloudless day. Got to my work on Yo Semite fall a little before 9 — worked steadily until noon — at the Hotel at 12.30 — Dinner at 1.15 \+ at 2 was at my study of Sentinal Rock. — Worked at it tolerably steady until 5 o'clk — Run back to Hotel. Uncle \+ Junie came in soon after from a ride — I took Uncle a's horse \+ rode down to Cathedral Rocks. Met George, who had been down to the Bridal Veil — then back. Everg out of sorts — too much lime juice in my lemonade I guess — To bed at 9.45.
