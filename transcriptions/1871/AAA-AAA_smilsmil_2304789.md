## Left Page

### December, Thursday, 28, 1871.

Splendid day — Immediately after bkfst, went down town, called at Eve't Mail Office, then to A.P.N. Ce got fo fo. of my die "Ostrich Hunting" — after burnishing and working over it. Then went to Seaman's Savings Bank, to open account for Water Color Society - then for a moment back to "Mail" office at 2 o'clk, home to lunch — at Studio at 2.45. Dory Lewis, Dec Tiffany \+ Frank Tweedy there. Varnished up "Twilight" \+ commenced drawing in blk "Sunshine on a Shaded Path" — Miss Sterling called. Home to tea at 6.30. Eve'g resting, went up to Burrgraff's and to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### December, Friday, 29, 1871.

Horrible day — Snow, Rain \+ Slush, got to studio at 9.15 \+ to work on charcoal drawing for Water Color "Shady Path" — at it until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — back at 2.30 feeling very much out of sorts — but worked through — Home to tea at 6.30 — well lounging about doing little else than write a note to Townley resigning my position on the "Palette" Life School committee — To bed shortly before 11 —
