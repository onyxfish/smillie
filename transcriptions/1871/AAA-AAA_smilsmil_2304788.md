## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 26 1871

Miserable muggy day with Rain. Feeling out of sorts — tried to draw from the baby but gave it up — got to Studio at 10.30 — Tried to charcoal for Water-Color drawing — gave it up \+ went to engraving — Mr. Geo. Yewell \+ friend called. Mr. Y. to say "good bye" as they start soon for Rome — Then N. Robbins. Home to lunch at 2.30 — back at 3.15 \+ worked as long as I could see. Then over to Silleck's and Y.M.C.A. — worked awhile in gymnasium — Home to tea at 7.30 — wrote during evef and got to bed at 11.15 —
Gilbert Pettit with us over night.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 27 1871

Splendid day — but with fitful, April like changes. Got to studio at 9.20 and at work on die with graver. (133/4 days.) Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked as long as I could see. Snedecors at Studio — mounted paper \+ ordered frame for Water-Color drawing — at 5 o'clk mkt to 38 ave Savings Bank and drew tab. of Water Color money Home to tea at 7 o'clk — Evef did a little writing \+ got to bed at 10 45
