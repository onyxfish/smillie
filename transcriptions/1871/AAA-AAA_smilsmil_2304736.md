## Left Page

### August Tuesday, 22 1871.

at Mariposa House — Schlageter's

Up soon after 5 o'clk. Brkfst at 7. and soon after 8 we were on the Trail for Clark's — The mt. glowing with a thin film of cloud — Enjoyed our drive, or Ride, through the woods very much — Got to Clark's about 12.30 — Dinner soon after 1 o'clk — Determined to give up my idea of getting a Sketch of Grizzly Giant \+ go on to Mariposa — So, Started in Stage at 2 o'clk. The woods very fine until within 3 or 4 miles of White Hatch's — after passing W. H.'s the Road grows worse until it is very bad — Soon after 7 got into Mariposa very tired — Tea at once — Sat about and then stretched myself out on my Rd a little before 9 o'clk.

## Right Page

### August Wednesday, 23 1871.

at Lathrop's Station — Room 12.

Up at 2.30. A. M., Refreshed ourselves by bathing our faces in tepid water; had a cup of lukewarm Tea and at 3 o'clk. Started in the Stage — could see the time by watch at 4.30 and soon after 5 the Sun rose — Execrable Road, very hard wagon and dust supreme — Got to Hornitas at 7 — Bkfst. and off in new Coach at 7.40 — Got to Millmp about 10, the Ride over the plains the nettlest thing I ever experienced a strong wind blowing, hot as the breath of a Sirocco, but I did not suffer — Mr \+ Mrs. Merritt of San F. pleasant fellow Travelers — Got to Modesto at 4 — Welcomed by Mr. Jas. Cole of the Ross House — Took dinner there — at 5 — Cars to Lathrop's. Enjoyed a complete wash \+ change — Glorious evening and night — went to bed about 9 o'clk. feeling jolly \+ very proud of myself for being able to do such a big day's work —
