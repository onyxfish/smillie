## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 30 1871.

Up before 6 o'clk. \+ under the lead of a guide \+ with several others again went the round of the Geysers \- Bkfst. soon after \+ at 8 started in Stage for Calistoga. The most delicious \+ the scenery the most beautifully wild that I have yet found among the virts of Cal. About 10 o'clk me Reached the highest elevation, 3700 ft — magnificent view. Then commenced descent \- "Wash" drove us splendidly amusing me exceedingly by his talks to his horses \- At Pine Flat, 8 miles, we changed wagon \+ horses, the great [illegible] with bills in hand driving us the Ride of the way to Calistoga \- in one place he made four miles in a little less than 20 min. After getting into the valley, very dusty \- got to Calistoga at 12.45 \- this crab Lunch at "The Springs Hotel" \- R.R. to Vallejo at — down the Napa Valley \- to Vallejo at 6 o'clk \- boat to San Fr \- where after a bleat rough sail me arrived at 8 o'clk. Mc \+ Mrs Porter, of California Theatre \- our companions \- got to Grand Hotel at 8.45 \- supper and and got to bed about 11 \- tired, but delighted with our trips to the Geysers.

## Right Page

### August Thursday, 31 1871.

San Jose. Auzerais House
Room no. 5.

Bkfst. at 8.30. Spent the morning going about, at Watkins' \+ Muybridge's \- photographers \- then to art gallery, Snow's, on Kearny St. \- there met Bierstadt \- made a "composition" for Tunie after lunch at 1 o'clk \- at 3.20 started by R.R. for San Jose, with Matt \+ Gel. Got to Santa Clara at 5.40 \- got off train \+ walked over to old mission church \- went into it \+ through part of College \- then back to train at 6.40 \- got near to San Jose \- stopped by train off the track \- Carriages came up things with fields \+ me got to Auzerais House at 7.15 \- Grand hubub \- Fair \+ great political meeting \- not a bed to be had \- consternation \- luck \- hackman put out of his room \+ gel. Matt \+ I put into it \- Small the ventilation \- went out meeting in front of hotel \- heard Booth Rep. candidate for Gov. speak \- got to bed about 11.30
