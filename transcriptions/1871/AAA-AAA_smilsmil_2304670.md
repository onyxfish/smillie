## Left Page

### April Friday, 28 1871.

Disagreeable day. Got to Studio's at 9 o'clk. worked on Samuel Bond Coupon Back 'u green until 10 o'clk. Then Misses J. (20) M. (19) \+ W (14) came. Heavy showers — worked with them until 1 o'clk — Home to lunch at 1.40 Down to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30 — Back to Studio's at 4.30 — worked on drawings — Coupon \+ Goddard arms until 6.30 — Home to tea at 7.15 — at 8.30 went to St. Cloud Hotel — Tweedys — there until 10 o'clk Home — made note to Pinny Smith \+ more work on Goddard arms — Will \+ Joe at the house — got to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### April Saturday, 29 1871.

An april day — Beautiful m'g, very heavy shower bet. 2 \+ 3 and clear evr'f. Got to Studio's at 9 laid ground on Cardie \+ spent the day etching out ends and crossing n 'it — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — worked on etching (1st day) until 6.30 — left two of Miss Ripley's drawings at her house — Home to tea at 7.15 Swr'f went to Beurgraf's about books — at near 9 called at St. Cloud — Belle Tweedy very much used up mth a severe attack of Lumbago — got there Home at 11 \+ to bed at 11.30
