## Left Page

### April Wednesday, 12 1871.

April-like day — splendid m'f
\+ sweet, cloudy mid day. Got to
studio at about 9.30. At 10.30 went
to N.A. of D. Varnishing Day — Spent
the m'f looking at the Exhibition
— fair to middling — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back to studio at 2.45 —
Misses R.(22) S.(21) M (15) \+ J(15)
already there. Labored with them
until near 6 — Home to tea at
about 8 went with Kate \+ saw
8th Co. 7th Reg't drill at their
Armory Tompkins Market —
Home at 10.30 \+ to bed soon
after 11 —

## Right Page

### April Thursday, 13 1871.

Fine m'f. Showers during the
afternoon — Got to studio t'em
after 9 a good deal under the
weather with headache. Worked
on tracing f "Mules — S.A." cor-
recting drawing, using lay figure
\+ horse, etc until near 6 o'clk
when I was so used up that I
went home \+ to bed where I
stayed until about 9 o'clk —
then up \+ in my chair
until 3.30 a.m. when I
went to bed —

Much disappointed that
I could not go to Private
View, N.A. of D.
