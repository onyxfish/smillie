## Left Page

### January, Friday, 20, 1871.

Another rather thick day —
but not unpleasant — ab. 9
o'clk. called at W. Wm. J. Blodgetts
182 Fifth Ave. Saw him in his Room
(agency) about water colors (foreign)
for our sub. — Then to studio at
9.30 — So work on drawing (18 days)
general finishing & bringing
together — all over sky, particulars —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2 — & worked until near 5 —
Mr.) at. M. Hart & Miss Bogga
called — Also Schenk, auctioneer —
Home to tea at 6.45 — Evef.
Reading & writing to Ferguson.
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday, 21, 1871.

About half an inch of snow fell
during the night — overslept (the
first night of sleep for more than a
week) — did not get to studio till
9.30. Spent an hour mounting
paper over stretcher for my drawing
— done splendidly — then to work
on drawing (19 days) finishing
generally, color and effect —
Home to lunch at 1.45 — back
at 2.20 & worked as long as I
could see — 4.50 — Mr. Wm. Dart
& niece called — Day cloudy —
Home to tea at 6 — Rain —
Evef. Reading "Atlantic" —
To bed a little after 11 —
