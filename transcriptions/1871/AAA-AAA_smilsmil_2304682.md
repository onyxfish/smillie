## Left Page

### May Friday, 26 1871.

Tremendously hot day \- Cloudy p.m. with promise of showers, but nothing more \- Started for Studio soon after 8 o'clk \- but stopped at the new Steven Bedf, Bdway \+ 27th S\. \+ climbed all over it \- Got to studio at 9 o'clk \- soon after, Misses Bascom came in \- Then Misses Maury \+ Jones \- then Miss Whiting who stayed until 12 o'clk \- I giving her her 20th lesson. Then Calton \+ Zahm came in \- Took my drawing at $150.00 to Chromo \- Home to lunch at 1.15 \- after, went to Knoedlers \+ made purchases \- back to studio at near 3 \+ worked until 7 o'clk on "Havanna" drawing (1½ day) \- Self very much used up until after 9 o'clk \- Then began to work, cutting up sketching paper \- at it until after 11 \- Got to bed at 11 H5

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 27 1871.

Another hot day \- Got to Studio at 9.15 \+ worked all day on "Havanna" drawing (2½ days) \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \+ worked until 6 o'clk \- when Belle Smeedy with Edith \+ May came in \- Walked home with her, went in \- had a cup of tea \+ got home a little before 9 o'clk \- To bed at 11 \-
