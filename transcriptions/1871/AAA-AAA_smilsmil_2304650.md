## Left Page

### March, Sunday, 19, 1871

Deliciously mild, clear and enervating day — Went to Dr.
Hastings' & heard him preach
from Matt. 12th Ch. 30th v. & Luke 9th Ch.
& 50th v. to Reconcile the apparent
contradiction — Dined at home
at 1. o'clk — napped in my
chair after — At 4 o'clk went
to Studio & fixed five for
to-morrow — Sauntered about
until 6 o'clk — Then in to
tea — Welf wrote a letter
to Matt and to bed about
10.45

## Right Page

### March, Monday, 20, 1871

Delightful mf, mild & clear with
wind from the East — Got to Studio at 8.35
and to work on drawing (4½ days)
M. Randel called before 9 — about 10 —
J.C. Nicoll — much exercised over a letter
from Mrs. Murray published in Sat's "Post" —
we cooked up an answer for her — George
came about noon — at 1 I went to ½, M.C.A.
hofome to find a set of Harper's Mag. in library.
Called on Mrs. Culver — message from George —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — Carrie Hertzel and
mother called — Rode down to Lotus club
& hunted through Harper's Mag. for [illegible] article
on gold mining, did not find it & back to Studio
at 3.45 — worked as long as I could see — At
6 o'clk, Messrs. Geo. Whitney, Meck Lewis
& W.T. Richards came in & had a jolly time
for 3/4 of an hour — Home to tea at 7 —
Welf a little Reading & some anatomy
— Rain — To bed at 10.30
