## Left Page

### December Friday, 8 1871.

Mild + pleasant morning — got to Studio's at 9,10 — and to work on 10×18 " Mt. Safayette" that I am finishing up for Watkins — for photographs — much interrupted by callers Mr & Mrs. Dr. Dodge + Elkins of Chicago + others — worked until 1,30 — prepared lunch and at 2,15 started for Phil. Rehearsal — Then to Dechamp's — back to Studio's at 5 — Home to tea at 6,30 — Wetf, went to the Rogers's in 42° — for a few minutes — back — some writing + Read Tyndal's "Hours of exercise among the Alps" — To bed at 11, 45

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 9 1871.

Beautiful day, got to the Studio's at 9 o'clk — just ready to start on one of my unfinished studies when Elkins came in — then the man with gutters + pipes to arrange inside of skylight against leakage — This threw me entirely off my work — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Mow then until dark — Miss Newton & Mrs. Beers called to see Studios — Home to tea at 6,30 — evening spent Reading Tyndal + doing some writing — just on midnight when I got to bed.
