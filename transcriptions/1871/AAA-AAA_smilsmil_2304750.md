## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 5 1871.

Up at 6 — [illegible] just before 7 — then to Oakland wharf where, after a sharp freight I got trunks check'd. \+ paid $13. extra freight then to Vallejo's boat, where we got just in time 8 o'clk — a most delightful sail, smooth water and rich, hazy atmosphere — got to Vallejo's at 10 — then R.R. met Mr. \+ Mrs. Allen. — Sacramento at 12.15, Mr. \+ Mrs. Brown at the depot. Rode once to their house — washed then lunched — magnificent fruit. Then to depot with Mr. B— and by his kind influence was able to get one of my trunks off the overland Train, at 2 o'clk — altho' 'twas checked through to Ma[illegible]. Back to the house \+ soon after 3 — all went out in two carriages — drove first to the Capitol in course of erection — went over it and into the vaults of the State Treasurer where we handled some bags of gold — then to the shops of the C.R.R. where we spent an hour — Reld? to the house to get Mrs. Brown — then to the Race track and around that — then about town and in at 6 — slight spatter of rain — soon after sat down to a very fine dinner — Besides Mr. \+ Mrs. Brown \+ our party, old Rev. Redington, this two sons Chas. \+ Henry — and Mr. \+ Mrs. Chas. Talbot — sat at the table until 9.30 — about 10.30 — the Redingtons \+ Talbots left and abt. we went to bed — Quite a shower in the evening —

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 6 1871.

Up at 7 — glorious morning — \+ Hobson after 8. My Redington \+ adopted sister called while from 9½. at 11 — carriages \+ all drove to Mr. Chas. Crocker's house — went through it — his family now on their way to Europe — Splendid house \+ furniture \+ some good pictures — back to lunch abt 1 o'clk — at 2 drove to depot, large basket of fruit — Chas. \+ Hy. Redington met us at depot with more fruit — off at 2.30 delighted with our visit to Sacramento — R.R. ride without any incident except that we ran off and left our conductor just before we got to Cape Horn — backed up a mile or two \+ got him. Arrived at Truckee at 11 o'clk — miserable dirty hole — night of election day — boisterous, drinking crowd — Trouble to find out anything about stages to Lake Tahoe — got to bed at 12.15 in a good deal of a muddle —
