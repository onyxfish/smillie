## Left Page

### May Tuesday, 30 1871

Fearfully hot day — Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. — Worked on "Havana" drawing until 1 o'clk — then a little more touching on "Upper Ausable" drawing. Home to lunch at 2 o'clk — about 3 started for down-town, went first to 172 Mrs St — Colton Zahm \+ Roberts mit my Water-Color. Got $150 — then to Conroys — then to A.B.N. Co — \+ delivered "Havana" — then to Congdon's about R.R. ticket — then to Norton's \+ at 6 o'clk home. Went arranging English papers etc — until near 11 — then to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### May Wednesday, 31 1871

Very hot muggy day with heavy thunderstorms — at 9 o'clk. went to Jas. Wilson's — helped he wrned buy "Lifting of the clouds" — then went to Carpenters in 31st S: very heavy Rain then to Studio \+ there fixing up things until 1 o'clk — then home — caught at Peter's Cor. in heavy Rain \+ had to wait ½ an hour — Home all the P.M. arranging things — a little Rain — but very pleasant but hot \+ close — Went to Miller's then to Kimball's — a very pleasant visit. Imina Ireland \+ Oscar then then to Studio — then to Miss Whitney's then to Miss Ripley's — another pleasant call \+ at 9.30 at the Sweedy's where I met Gel. Home at 10.30. To bed som after 11 —
