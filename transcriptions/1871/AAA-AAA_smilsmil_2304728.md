## Left Page

### August Monday, 14 1871.

The weather still inexorably fair \- no rest from work because of bad weather. Bkfst about 6,30 \- at 8 got off to my work \- a study of El Capitan from a Rock side back of Leidy's \- worked very hard until 2 o'clk, when I got it finished after a fashion \- one sitting \- back to hotel about 2,15 \- sour milk \+ blackberries and at 2,45 tramped down the valley to study of white cedars (2d sitting) \+ by working very closely, got them done by 6,15 \- at hotel a little before 7 \- tea soon after \- Evening washing brushes \+ preparing for the morrows work \- To bed about 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### August Tuesday, 15 1871.

Beautiful day \- Bkfst soon after \- about 8,30 got off with lunch to the other side of the River to make a study of Manzanita \- worked until about 12, when I found my study done \+ my eyes hurting me so that I could not do the pencil sketch that I had intended \- so I went back to the hotel \+ ate my lunch there \- at 1,30, went back and climbed a slide \+ by painting as hard as I could until 6,30 I painted in a sketch of Union Rocks. Back to tea soon after 7 \- Mr. Hatch, Mr. White \+ Miss W. \- White \+ Hatch's, at the hotel \- Everg loafing, Reading news papers, washing brushes etc \- until nearly 11 \- then to bed.
