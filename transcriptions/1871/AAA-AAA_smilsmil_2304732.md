## Left Page

### August Friday, 18 1871.

Very hot day. Blfst. soon after 7 — overcast for a little while with a few drops of rain — then cleared. Threats of a thunder storm about noon. Got off with pencils \+ paper about 9 o'clk. and wandered down the valley having a royal day — gathering flower seeds, lying under the pine trees listening to their song and clambering about the foot of the Cathedrals. Took my lunch on the bank of the River about 1 o'clk. back to hotel about 7 o'clk. with several pencil sketches — a party of 10 arr. at hotel —. Lounging on piazza until 10 o'clk — to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### August Saturday, 19 1871.

Very hot and I feeling pretty well — stayed out. Got away from the house about 9 o'clk. with paper \+ pencils — sketched the sentinel barn beyond Black's — then climbed a cliff back of Hutchings — about the roughest climb yet — went up through a slit in the rock and got a great slit in my breeches — wondered how I was ever going to get down — by the aid of my long strap, let my sketching traps down ahead of me \+ by bringing to use every muscle in my body, I got down safely — thank all got a sketch — back to hotel to lunch at about 2.30 — after that, could not bring myself to my work — loitered about — cut two or three manzanita sticks — tea about 7 — [illegible] doing nothing and to bed about 11 —
