## Left Page

### December Saturday, 2 1871

Splendid day \+ milder \- got to Studio at 9 o'clk, and right to work on Summer Studies at it until 1.30 \- home to lunch back at 2.25 \+ worked as long as I could see, getting seven of them licked into presentable shape. About 4.30 \- a call from Mr. Sam. Coleman \- Home to tea at 5.30 \- Belle, Mary \+ Florence Treedy there to tea then all together went to Phil. Concert \- walked home with Belle to Mr. Condant's in 43d st. Home soon after 11 \+ to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 3 1871

miserable sort of a day and I had a head to match \- myself went to Hastings \- He preached from [illegible] \+ 2d cho. [illegible] part of [illegible] V. Must confess that I did not hear much of it \- Home to dinner \+ spent the P.M. until 6 o'clk in bed. Then up, not feeling much better \- head cleared during the eveng. Got to work on Moonon \+ Salt Lake letter for the Mail \+ kept at it until 10 o'clk. A.M. then to bed but did not get to sleep until after 2 o'clk \-
