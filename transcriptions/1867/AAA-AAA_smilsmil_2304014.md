## Left Page

### Friday, October 25, 1867

Milder + still clear. Spent the m'g until 11 o'clk. clearing up at home — then went to Studio Bldg, called on Jas. Fairman + J. R. Brevoort, then home to lunch — At 2.30 went to 1st Phil. Rehearsal at Academy of Music with Mary — Heard Beethoven's Pastoral Symphony — Schumann's "Manfred" + Liszt's "Mazeppa". Rehearsal over at 4.20 — went to Studio Bldg + called on J. Q. A. Ward. I then home to tea — Eve'g writing + bathing my "poor feet". Got to bed about 10 o'clk — Uncle Willie arr'd from Poughkeepsie after I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Saturday, October 26, 1867

Up a little after 6 o'clk. weather still fine — Got to Studio at 9.30 and spent the m'g tacking down the carpet, kindling fire + getting things to rights generally. Then set to work fixing up my studies. Home to lunch at 1.30 — Before I got off again to the Studio for the p.m. Father came home from down town used up generally with a cold. Spent the p.m. fussing about the Studio — still stretching + fixing studies. About dark Jas. Fairman came in and [illegible] until 6.30 detailing to us his quarrel + Rupture with Wm Hart — Eve'g spent at home — to bed about 10 o'clk.
