## Left Page

### Monday, October 21, 1867.

*Parker House, Boston.*

Up at 6.30 — got valise packed and started for Boston at 8.40 by Eastern R.R. passing through Portsmouth, Newburyport, Salem, Lynn etc. etc. The day was overcast, almost threatening rain and as a general thing the views were dismal — a few times me caught glimpses away over the ocean & some of the salt meadow views near Newburyport dotted over with small hay stacks, up on spiles 2 or 3 feet high were very singular — Reached Boston at 1.45 & drove right to the Parker Ho. Room 87. Took dinner & then out among the Express Co's to find my missing money pkg. Traced it to Gibbs & Co. who had given it to Bagley & him I am to see to-morrow — Then walked over the Common, there meeting Misses Grace & Kate Peirce — then to Commonwealth ave. & home — very tired — Every for an hour called on Misses Gransoner & Curtis at 18 Pinckney St. then home & at 9.30 to bed —

## Right Page

### Tuesday, October 22, 1867.

Up a little after 7 & admonished to look out for a headache — On the bed & about my Room very carefully all the m[orn]g. About 1 o'clk. went & saw Exp. man. My pkg. has gone to Great Falls. Ordered it back. After dinner, with Jel. Rode over to the Navy Yard & looked about there for an hour or two & about 3 I reached Miss Gransoner's where until near 5. I was drawing upon blackboards — 2 Cartoons for the [illegible] Entertainment — The Ascent & descent of Chocorua — Returning to Hotel, dressed — took tea & stretched myself on the bed until 8 o'clk. when we went to our Chocorua party at Miss Gransoner's — We met there Misses A., G. & K. Peirce — Misses Hill — (2 sisters) Green & Littlehale — Mr & Mrs. French & Messrs Crocker, Binney, Reed, Curtis, Weld & Hill, not to forget altogether, Mr. Hall — The Company was very jolly and Conway times were fully & generally discussed — "going to Jerusalem" — "Swedish dance" & a movement of Beethoven's 2d Symp.h were gone through with & given for the Recollection of the Three Siens — At 12 we broke up amid demonstrations of kindness — Regards and Rain — walked with Binney & Weld — to bed at 1 o'clk
