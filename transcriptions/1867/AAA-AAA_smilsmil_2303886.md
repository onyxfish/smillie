## Left Page

### Thursday, February 7, 1867

Fine day — went to at 3 N. C.
in the mt. — Saw Goodall &
from him got material for two
drawings — a camp scene on the
Pampas & a Lassoing subject —
Got mounting board from Cohen's
did not reach studio until
after 2 o'clk. Rather demoralized
so far as work was concerned, so
didn't accomplish much before
4 o'clk when Geo. & I started for
the 4.30 stage to Manhattanville —
— pleasant ride — Reached Uncle
A's at 5.20 — dinner a little
before 6 — Billiards after —
Fan in for a Romp — She
and the Quails at dinner, too
much for me — glad to
get to bed at 10 o'clk — after
getting to bed, slept well.

## Right Page

### Friday, February 8, 1867

Up at about 8 o'clk — Heavy fog —
Breakfast at 8.30 — Took the
9 o'clk stage in to the city. Stopped
at home to see how Charl was —
he appears to be "in" for Scarlet
fever. Got to Studio at 10.20
worked upon Sketch of Camping
on the Pampas — did not work
as I should, so did not finish
it by night — Tremendous Rain
