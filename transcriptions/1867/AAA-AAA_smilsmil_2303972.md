## Left Page

### Friday, August 2, 1867.

Up at 6.30 after a delightful nights rest —
Rain pouring steadily & softly down —
Breakfast at 8 o'clk — good — a little walking
on the piazza. Then to our Room, Sketching
Canvases & writing letters. Before dinner
walked in the Rain, blow town a little my
-warmed up for dinner at 2 o'clk — After-
noon tried to Sketch Note Mt. upon a box of
canvas — from the piazza, but clouds and
Rain interfered — At 4.30 Geo and I
started off up the Road & walked
about 1½ miles, as far as Mudgetts
boarding house — back in time for tea.
After tea conversation turned upon
Spiritualists & Spirit-Rappings, after
the evening trying to get tables to
tip and telling ghost stories, much
to the disgust of one of the Misses H.
who occupies a haunted Room.
bed at 10.30. Our boarders are Mr. Son,
Miss Graubner & footage Miss Green. 2 Miss
Hill. 3 misses Pierce — Miss Curtis & the
br. a young fellow & another youth — a
cousin of the Curtis's —

## Right Page

### Saturday, August 3, 1867.

Still steady, persistent Rain, about the
house until I could stand it no longer,
About 10.30 started out over the meadows
to the River & along River bank and brook
until near 1 o'clk — got thoroughly wet
but enjoyed much the many meadows
& groups of trees — The mountains
of course were entirely shut out by the
heavy hanging clouds. Dinner at 1
after dinner a number of us must
through the Rain, nearly a mile to Eastmans
where there is a Bowling Alley — played
there until 5.30 — then returned just
in time for tea — After before dinner
our new boarders came. An elderly lady,
two younger ones & a child — Trunks marked
Haven. After tea, all in parlor trying to find
how many words could be spelled with the letters
T.r.i.b.u.n.e, 70 words were spelled — Near 9 o'clk
young Martin called — his Aunt & Leila at Cliff
House, waiting for Mr. M. by Stage — Geo & I
went over — had a jolly time — introduced
to the Misses Blackwell & brother — 3 or 4 Stages —
the last arr. about 10 — Mr. M. on it — Excitement
soon over — Clouds broken away — Starlight — home
and to bed at 11.15
