## Left Page

### Monday, May 20, 1867

Miserable cold Rainy day —
At Studio kindled a fire — Went
to Danl. Huntington's about 9 A.M.
to see about Water Color Socy. Exh.
next fall. He took me up to his
Studio — pleasant call — worked
on "Storm Coming" 10½×18 for Auger.
(3d day) and call it done. I
give it to him for a portrait
that he has painted of me
& a little sketch of "day Apple
girl." Home to lunch —
Rec'd a letter from Haseltine
acknowledging Receipt of
my sketches & enclosing
Check for $105.00. Evng.
at home — tried to write Report
of Committee on Exhibitions,
but feeling very stupid was
obliged to give it up. To bed
before 10 o'clk

## Right Page

### Tuesday, May 21, 1867

Another disagreeable day — Rain
from about 10 until 3 o'clk. Had a
strange dream this mng. — Saw a noble
pile of architecture — a church — Rich
with Carving — and as I watched, a
gigantic, shadowy hand, beautiful in
form, passed the forefinger down the
spire & as the finger passed, it glistened
and blossomed — the finger passed over
all the rich Tracery & white lilies bud-
ded out & filled the air with their
fragrance. I was much moved & make
not frightened, but awed — I have no
interpretation, but the dream has im-
pressed me very much — All the mng.
worked on "Passing Shower" 14×18. (4th day)
P.M. for an hour or two worked on "Mrs."
7×9. — Evng. Adjourned meeting of
Water Color Society at Studio. Submitted
my Report & it was decided to have
first Annual Exh. in connection with
1st Fall Exh. of N.A. of D. So I con-
sider myself successful — got home
about 10.30 P.M. but was not able to
get to bed much before midnight.
