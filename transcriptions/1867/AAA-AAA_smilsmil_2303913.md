## Left Page

### Tuesday, April 2, 1867

Up at 7.30 — glorious morning
walked half an hour before
bfcfast on Eastern piazza —
Bfcfst at 8.15 — off in stage at 9
o'clk — Glorious sky effects
that charmed me as I rode
into the city. At Studio by 10 o'clk
— lazy — Home to lunch at 1.
P.M. commenced 2° painting
on "Seeking the Shade" 14×18, for
Mr. Tracy Harris. (5th day). I
painted the sky, trying to get
some of the qualities that had
so delighted me in the morning.
Am pretty well pleased, but of
course, not satisfied. Even'g
5th Regular Monthly meeting of
Water Color Society at Colitian's
Studio — But few present +
little business to be done. Had
however a pleasant time. Home
at 10. to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### Wednesday, April 3, 1867

*Fred. Durand. at 9 o'clk*

Another magnificent Spring day.
at 9 o'clk, at Dunning's where I
spent an hour + 10 m. in Fred. D's
chair having a final filling put
in one of my back teeth — Thank-
ful that it is over — Bill $53.00.
Stopped in at Schaus's + Snedecor's
on my way up. At Studio at 11.30
Set palette + glazed foreground
on "Seeking the Shade" (16th day). Home
to lunch at 1. P.M. — painted
over foreground where I had gla-
zed. Done at 4.30. Af. went
down to Fred Durand's + gave him
$10.00 to send to Jno. D. in Paris for
Frolich's "Cupid + Psyche". Even'g
with Gel. + Mary went to Munroe
in 38th — Pleasant visit until
9.30. On my way back I stopped
in at Willis's for a few minutes —
fled the house to avoid $0 cts.
thrust at me by Meta Gavit —
To bed at 10.15
