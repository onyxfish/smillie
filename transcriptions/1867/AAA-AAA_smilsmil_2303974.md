## Left Page

### Tuesday, August 6, 1867

Up at 6.30 - charming mrg, with promise of a hot day, which was amply fulfilled - as usual, bkft late - did not get to my sketching ground until 9.15 - Artists brook + Saco - finished outlining + painted in sky - all sorts of trouble, of course - cut in mountains + meadows - home to dinner at 1.30 - dinner at 2 - after dinner shower threatening - sufficient excuse to remain about the house - Repainted sky. Indeed shower did not arrive until nearly 5 o'clk - glorious cloud effects over mote Mt. + meadows of intervale closing with fine sunset - Every night young moon full of charming suggestions - clouds hanging over Mt. Mt. + ledges - all out on piazza until after 10 - I find that I have been poisoned by Ivy, probably - which grows in quantities where I sketch - It has broken out upon my Right wrist + causes me much irritation.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, August 7, 1867

Very close, muggy mrg. up at 6.30. Bkft at 7.40. Refused invitation to go to Fryburg - got to sketching ground at 9 o'clk - Got in, in a rough way all of my trees - Before 12 o'clk, thunderstorms got to playing around - home at 1 o'clk I started for the house - terribly hot - dinner at 1.45 but no appetite - after dinner, another storm threatening - a doctor came in, took a look at my wrist + rubbed it with dry Calomel - advises a Sedelitz powder tomorrow mrg. Took 15×26 in canvas + stationed myself on troll back of Tiansarge House, overlooking the meadows + to the Ledges + mote Mt. By fixing my Camera lucida on the end of my mahlstick - sticking that in the ground + then laying my canvas on the ground I was able to get a reduction by some sketching + the use of a very long pencil - Got it very nearly outlined with Spts. Turp. + Brown by 6 o'clk - back to house much fagged + sick - very little tea - At 8+9 walked to doctor's for my powder. I took one that he prepared with ginger and aromatic Spts ammonia - then he charged me a dollar - Returning, wrote a letter to Throop asking for an antidote for Iaus. tox. Doctor has given me sugar of lead to make a wash, but I wont use it - To bed after 11 - very warm
