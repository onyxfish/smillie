## Left Page

### Wednesday, February 13, 1867.

Muggy, foggy, overcast day. Got to the Studio late in the mf — near 11 o'clk. Went to work "washing in" on "Camping on the Pampas" — and by evef had it pretty near done — I rather like it. About one o'clk. Fanny Mulford called — stayed 3/4 of an hour — About 4 o'clk P.M. Mr. Rutherford & Miss — called — very anxious about my Sketches for an Album — Evef had a days work mounting drawings, sketches and stretching paper.

## Right Page

### Thursday, February 14, 1867.

Miserable sort of a day — Spent the mf finishing up "Camping" and think it now one of my most successful drawings — Afternoon reduced "Lasooing cattle" and got it partly penciled in preparatory to using India Ink. Evef by appointment Geo & I went to Dr. Noyes 65 Madison Ave. to see some wonders of the eye & to give him some instruction as to how to put some of the wonders on paper. He had a patient from the Infirmary to show us — A pleasant & satisfactory Evef — Saw Mr. N. & her father — left at 10.30. Stopped in at Will's at 10.45 & there met Meta [illegible]. She was in jolly humor and had a pleasant visit with her until 11.20 — Then home, wet and cold — Raining hard.
