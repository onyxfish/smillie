## Left Page

### Monday, April 8, 1867

Clear, warm day. At the Studio early — Painted all day on "Seeking Shade" 14×18in (8 day) painting over large tree trunk &foliage above — besides improving tones generally — I am getting more in conceit of the picture — Home to lunch at 1. So warm that I went out without any overcoat. Eveng. Jos. Mill. Meta Gavit & her Charley Adams — old Gavit — old Brainerd & D'Throop at the house — Amusement of a very general character — Singing and running around. Meta & Adams for a time in my room — also Gavit & Brainerd — Refreshments at 10.30 — and all out at 11.30 — got to bed myself just before midnight — [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~4 words] [illegible — ~4 words].

## Right Page

### Tuesday, April 9, 1867

Remarkable weather. Still cloudless & warm — Feel the effects of dissipation somewhat to-day — At the Studio early, but worked languidly over the foliage on "Seeking Shade" (8½ day) Home at 12.30 — went to bed and napped for an hour — then lunch & back to Studio at 2.15 — Set to work charcoaling & outlining some little 6×10's for Hazeltine — at 5 o'clk commenced painting at Storm Sketch, 7×9 — & at 6.5h had it done — It will do for one of the Hazeltine Sketches. Eveng. lazily doing nothing.
