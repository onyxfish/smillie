## Left Page

### Monday, March 11, 1867

Dubious mrg., but cleared
off fine. By appointment,
spent from 9 to 11 2o o'clk a.m.
in Fed. Durand's dental chair.
Had 2 fillings in lower left back-
most tooth. Then went right down
town with "Gauchos" drawing —
Found Jno E— in one of his
hoggish humors — Got $50.00
for my drawing. Rode home
in car with Mr. Edw. Anthony.
Lunch at 2 o'clk — Returned
to Studio. Set palette & worked
over distance of "October" 10½×18
for M. Winans — I now call it
finished — Went down to White & Fleming's
at 4.40 to meet Father — he didn't come.
Pete, Aunt & Uncle a at Studio.
Home at 6 — All the evening
waiting for Jno. E. Gaits — he he
didn't come — altho' he promised.
Young Bell came this mrg. while
I was at Durand's — He went away
to come to-morrow.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, March 12, 1867

The worst of bad days, Pouring down
Rain and wind. Commenced
repainting little Sunset 6×10 in
that I painted for Mrs. Greatrex
nearly a year ago — Got well
started, when, at about 11 o'clk.
Fan Mulford & Miss Ella Diven came
to studio for me to go with them
to select some pictures & statuary —
After more than 2 hours of wind
Rain & waiting, we selected some
chromo-lithographs of Prang's, and
"Taking the Oath," plaster by Rogers —
left the girls at Tiffany's at 1.30
Rode home to lunch — back to Studio
by working as long as I could see
managed to finish "Sunset" —
Eveng. Geo. & I went up to Diven's.
There met Sylvester Mulford and
Rich. Dorman — Fan starts for
home at 9 o'clk to-morrow mrg.
We had a very affectionate
season — left for home at
10.30. To bed at 11.30. Young Bell
came this mrg. & drew. but I went off with Fan.
