## Left Page

### Wednesday, January 30, 1867

Splendid day — went to
Studio by noon got my palette
set. After lunch got to work
painting upon a little 6×10 in
view of Lake Mahkeenac for
Uncle Willie — Got only the
sky & distance in — of course
cold — not little a no color.
[illegible] Minans called and
approved of the picture Jo
has painted for her.
Every evening at Wills.
M. Jno. E. Jant there.

## Right Page

### Thursday, January 31, 1867

Snowing during the morning.
Just enough to make things
very disagreeable. Went to the
Studio and painted again
upon little 6×10 for Uncle Willie
Got it painted in after a fashion — it is but a poor thing.
Worked upon it as late as
I could see. Every did
some heavy lying around
being too tired or lazy to do
anything else.
