## Left Page

### SATURDAY, JUNE 1, 1867.

Up at 7.15 — Breakfast at 8 — About
9 went to Church spire to take note
of general view of Hartford & compare
with photo. Glorious day & delightful
view — Finished a little after 10. Then
Geo. & I went to Mrs Colt's place &
looked about beyond — Ret'd to Hotel
& got dinner at 12 — Paid bill ($4 p'dg.)
& at 12.50 took R.R. train for New Haven —
Reached there at 2.05 — Passing through
uninteresting & poor country, very dusty,
met Louise Qua at N.H. Station going to
N.Y. — went at once to New Haven House —
took a Room & washed up & dusted off — at
3.30 went over to Yale College buildings & into
Library & Art Gallery — miserable pictures but
glorious surroundings — the Elms — went to
Bob Ives — not in — went to stable — got a
horse & for 2 hours drove all about —
Charmed & delighted — especially with
Elms in Temple Street & Hill house
Avenue — Took tea at Hotel at 7 —
Soon after Ives came to Hotel — we
Returned home with him & spent
evening until 9.30 — Saw his mother-
wife — two cousins, Misses Sharps and
another cousin — no name — To bed soon
after 10 —

## Right Page

### SUNDAY, JUNE 2, 1867.

Up at 7.30 & found it a fine m'f. Did
not get b'kf't. until near 9 o'clk — Sat awhile
then sauntered out & before service went
into a number of the most prominent
churches — Heard a dry sermon on
faith in College Chapel — After service
wandered around again — up Hillhouse
Ave & back to Dinner at Hotel at 1 o'clk.
After dinner, a Rest & at 3.20 went to Ives'.
Bob met us & we walked out to the
top of East Rock & had a splendid view
in all directions. Especially Quinnipiac
Valley — Sleeping Giant & Meridian Hills —
Ret'd by another Road — Whitney Ave and
Temple St. Tea at Dr Ives' bet. 6 & 7 — good —
Immediately after tea — the old gentleman
had to drive out to see a patient & offered
me a Ride — went out to West field — and
to the foot of West Rock — & returning drove
through many new & fine Streets, giving
me a higher idea than ever of New Haven.
Ret'd a little after 8 — At 9 we bid them
goodbye — went over to Hotel — pd. $8.00 &
took a long dreary dark Rainy walk to
the propeller at end of Long Wharf —
got there at 9.50 — a gloomy end to a
charming visit — Start for N.Y. at 12.30
