## Left Page

### Thursday, June 27, 1867

Up at 6.30 — Misty & drizzly, threatening Rain which came about 9 o'clk a.m. from letter Rec'd. from Mary yesterday Matt had determined to go N.Y. City to-day — Rain prevented — Got to work before 9 o'clk — Repainting 6×10. "Catskill Mts. from Pell's Hill" — went over it solidly, & while I think it much improved it is still very bad — heavy in color — two long days work on it for $15.00 — Went in the Rain for Calser — found Jno. R- is his boat house, tinkering — Stayed with him for half an hour & then to work. At 3 Rec'd. letters from Father — Geo. & Sam'l. Coleman — S.C's note was of 21st — inviting Geo. & me to spend the 25th with him. Geo. is very anxious that I go to N.Y. & with him spend a day or two at Irvington — visiting Coleman & Tiffany — Can't do it — At 5.30 cleared up for the day — about 7 went down to River — about there for an hour — Ret'd. to house — Matt & Kate on piazza, singing until after 9 o'clk — Got to bed about 10 —

## Right Page

### Friday, June 28, 1867

Very warm, muggy day — Up before 6 — wrote letters to Sam'l. Coleman and George — After bkpt. Started on sketch 6×10. Up Right, after a study in the woods about 10 o'clk the stage came along & Matt started for N.Y. City — I then went to train & got N.Y. paper — about 12 o'clk commenced work again & at 5.30 had my sketch finished, after a fashion. It is painted over a beginning of the same subject made by Mr. Choate some time ago — So I have made use of the canvas & subject. Tea at 6 — then went to River, 6.30, & found that J.R. with Stevens & Crosby had gone off in the 3 single shells, leaving a cranky little tub of a dinky [illegible] 10 in. long for me to follow in if I chose. I pulled Rt. across the river into the shade of the west bank & then down to New Paltz — about 7.45 the affair of the ev'g. came off — a Race between editors & employees in the Po. "Press" paper — Davis came in ahead — a few minutes after 8 I started for home pulling up on the east side — Sky cloudless — air cool and bracing & water rough enough to make my little boat dance — enjoyed it hugely, also the glorious light & the Rock shores — Got in before 9 & found the 3 shell men in just ahead me, having come up on the other side —
