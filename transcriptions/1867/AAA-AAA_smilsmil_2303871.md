## Left Page

### Tuesday, January 8, 1867

Fine bracing cold clear day.
Up at about 6.30 feeling very
well & enjoyed my breakfast.
The whole morning employed
clearing up. Some corners in
my room at home. Looking
over old pkgs. and rubbish
that has collected in closets
and drawers. At 1.30 had
my dinner — at 2.30 walked
down to Studio — called upon
Sam. Coleman & had a very
pleasant visit — his wife coming
in while I was there — at a
little before 4 o'clk P.M. went to
Uncle Geo's to get my temporary
set of teeth — trouble — wont fit —
have to call again to-morrow
at 2.30. Walked home. stopping
at 10th St. Studio Bldg. to see the
Gambart pictures. Wish only one,
by Alma-Tadema. "A dance in Rome —
1st Century" — Even at home
very quietly.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, January 9, 1867

Sharp, muddy day — Up
rather late in the morning.
Went to Studio at about 11
a.m. Picked up a lot of my
traps; drawing board, water color
brushes etc. & made a p[ac]kge.
to carry home to work. Did
not get home until 1.30.
Delayed in my dinner, it
was 2.30 before I had finished
it, at which time I had an
engagement with Uncle George
but feeling tired out & sleepy
I just went to bed & slept
until nearly 5 o'clk.
Even quiet — to bed at 12 —
