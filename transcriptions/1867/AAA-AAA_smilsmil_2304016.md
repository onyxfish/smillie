## Left Page

### Tuesday, October 29, 1867

Up at 7 — Rain pouring down &
wind driving, got quite wet
on my way to the studio. Found
things there almost afloat. The
day very dark, but I worked
all day on water color study 12×18
finishing up foreground and
working a little on the sky.
It is so poor that I cannot
make up my mind to exhibit
it as I had intended to do.
Every intending to go in to
see Mill, but Rain prevented
it, so got rid of the evening
at home & went to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, October 30, 1867

Weather threatening & disagreeable.
Spent the day working with
pencil on sketch of Carter Notch.
Making something tolerably
effective — but after all, very
unsatisfactory to me. I cannot
work with a view to breadth of
effect alone — the detail constantly distracts my attention, to the
detriment of the whole. An
evil contracted while engraving.
Had an offer to take a niece of
Wm Cullen Bryant's as pupil —
Have no Room for her — Robt. Peele
called. — Every Dr. Smith &
Throop called — also Joe & Will.
Mr. Geo. Williams joined the
company in my Room — Quite
jolly all — did not get to
bed until after 11 —
Rec'd my money pkg. at last
from North Conway — This is the money
that Snedecor sent to me on the
11 inst.
