## Left Page

### Friday, July 5, 1867

A very wet day, without rain — close muggy and moist — a day when the slightest exertion overheats — and to sit still, is to be chilled — worked on Hartford drawing until 11 o'clk then took camera 7×9 & 6×10 went to Roosevelt's Point and got a couple of subjects for sketches — old Hemlocks overhanging the water — Ret'd at 12 — Charcoaled another 6×[illegible] Composition — after dinner, spent the p.m. until 4 o'clk outlining mts &pts. Turp. the three subjects — J.P. sent me a note inviting me to play Croquet, take tea & Row with him this eve'g — Kate came in 3 o'clk stage — at 5 went to P[o?] 2 games — I was badly beaten in both — introduced to Mr Woolsey Rew — very pleasant enthusiast in subject of geometrical [illegible] — at tea table, everybody lovely — after tea hurried home, changed clothes & down to boat house, met J.R. & at 7 started for Po. met Rev. Corning in his shiff — about Kaal Rock & dock until near 9 — many Powell came up, after 8 — the pulled for home, in just before 9 — very dark — for[illegible] lost in the woods — home at last & to bed 10.2[illegible]

## Right Page

### Saturday, July 6, 1867

Muggier & warmer than yesterday — up late with headache — Mr. Woolsey called on father & me at 9.30 AM, an hour or more — apparently very much interested in all he saw — Mother got off for N.Y. at 10 AM. I went to R.R. & got paper — spent an hour on Roosevelt's Pier — then Ret'd home & gave up to my headache — on sofa until near 2 o'clk — George arr'd in stage at 2.45 — Had met Mother on the dock in Po — all well — Sat talking until 4 — then went to River — there until after 5 — Ret'd & tea at 6 — After tea Geo and I went up to Mrs Beaches — She not yet come — Saw Sullivan — man in charge — he promised to have boat ready for one Monday M'g, walked home by Rail — Saw J.R. — gave me permission to keep the boat in his Cove — all serene — driven home by slight shower — on piazza &a until after 9 — To bed at 10.45 — the shiff in which I rowed last night, was a new one & me 32 ft 9 in long — one foot wide & weighs 42 lbs —
