## Left Page

### Friday, March 1, 1867

Pain again, & much of it during the morning. Worked lazily upon 6×10 Harvesting, doing little before I went home to lunch at 1 o'clk. Ret'd at 2.30 after having eaten too much. P.M. spent as lazily or more so, than the morning. Think I must be sick. Pain stopped by 4. P.M. Eveng at home gluing down & mounting sketch in oil for Addie Dunning of Monument Mt. Spent an hour in at Will's. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Saturday, March 2, 1867

True to the Sat. weather of some weeks past, to day is a bad day — disagreeable & threatening. Tony Bell took his 6" [illegible], and left at about 12.30. Geo & I then set to work & gave the studio a good sweeping. During m'f., I had but a few finishing touches upon little pictures for Uncle Willie. Home at 1.10 to lunch. Re-dressed & Ret'd to Studio at 2.20. Charl. arr'd at 3.10. Geo & I then left him in charge of Studio while we went to Phil. Rehearsal. Enjoyed again Beethoven's 2d Symphony. Liszt's "Tasso" & Magic Flute — walked up town with Fanny Kimball & Emma Orland. I Ret'd to Studio about 5. Home to dinner at 6.30. Spent eveng at Diven's with Fau & Maud. Home & to bed at 11.30. Night quite cold & blowing up fresh.
