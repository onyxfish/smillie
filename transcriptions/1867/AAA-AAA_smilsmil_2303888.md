## Left Page

### Monday, February 11, 1867.

Windy day and cold.
Morning at Studio made
sketch of "Pasoing Cattle."
At 1 o'clk, went down town
submitted my sketches &
with slight modifications
they were approved. Walked
all the way up-town. Eve'g
I went away over to Mrs
Rutherford's — 11 th St & 2d ave —
to find her "out." Walked
to 6 th Ave & took car to 52d
St when, at Divens I met
George — Had a jolly
visit with the girls and
at 10 30 started for home
and bed.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, February 12, 1867.

Very mild sort of a day
looking at times almost
like Rain. Commenced
on drawing "Camping on the
Pampas" & got it all drawn
in ready for the India ink —
Uncle Mr Turrell & Uncle A.
called a little before noon —
Dr Noyes called about 5 o'clk
P.M. & we made an appoint-
ment with him for next Thurs.
eve'g. Eve'g Gel & I took Jane
M. & Maud D. to see Hartz,
the Illusionist — Some of his
tricks were very clever — We
had first rate seats — At 10,
adjourned to Delmonico's &
had Ices, Cream & Eclairs, got
the girls home by 11 o'clk — On our
getting home we found Dr
Throop — Just midnight
when we got to bed.
