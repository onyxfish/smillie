## Left Page

### Thursday, January 24, 1867.

A bright day and Regular January thaw. Charcoaled during the mng., trying to get a design for end-piece vigt for A.B.N. Co. of a Gauchos lassoing a Bull. Ab't 1 o'clk went down-town with one — Saw Mr. Goodall — he knocked all my plan of composition. Horses do not Rear_ etc, etc, I have too much action — So at 2 o'clk Pet. home — Stopped at Will's (Will + Joe, Pet. last night) + got a letter there for me from Meta Gavit. After dinner tried again to get a design. — Evng., a Mr. Williams came + took Mary + Matt up to call on Miss Pardue in 47 Yt. Joe went to call on Louise Qua. Joe + Will went to a party given by the Misses Hinshelwood. Matt, Kate + I were in at Will's bet. 10 + 11 P.M. Mother Ret. from Uncle A's during the afternoon.

## Right Page

### Friday, January 25, 1867.

Pleasant mng., but thick P.M. with a sprinkle of Rain in the evng. Worked all day on my Gauchos drawing — I have made him upon a horse under full headway + swinging a lasos. About 12 o'clk, S.B.F. Called — chatty + stayed nearly an hour — just bef. he she left Words + Will Kimball called, just fresh from California, the Sandwich Islands, etc, etc. Worked on my drawing until I could see no longer, then went to Studio + walked home with Joe. Evng. spent in a great mess, Contents of occupied closets, spread over the floor waiting to be replaced. Mounted my Gauchos drawing — packed files of Punch and did various other things very diligently until I got to bed.
