## Left Page

### Sunday, February 17, 1867.

Clear + beautiful day, but having my usual Sunday indisposition, did not get up until between 9 + 10 o'clk. At 11.30 started for Uncle A's by the 8th Ave P.P. Enjoyed the walk up to the house found Aunt Huldah, + Toony out getting the fresh air — wandered about with them until after 1 o'clk. Ges joined us, then Uncles A. + Wm. who had been out riding. Dinner at 2 — oysters, Turkey, etc, etc. Afternoon wandering again. At 5 o'clk walked over to the cars — home a little after 6 — found folks at tea — Mr. Edw. Carter visiting Miss Martha. Went to Tabernacle + heard Prof. Shedd. "Self knowledge Concerning our Sinfulness" — did not listen as I should — After service walked home with Mr. Jas Brevoort. Glorious moonlight night.

## Right Page

### Monday, February 18, 1867.

Yet another fine day. Worked all the m'f on "Gasoine" dw'g about noon set palettes and during p.m. painted over Uncle Willie's little canvas 6×9 in. "Lake Mahkeenac" — it is bright, but as at present very much "cut up" — Addie Dunning called to say that she could not accept my invitation to spend Tuesday ev'g with the Irelands + Kimballs at No. 20 W. 24th St. — All right — Every night to Irelands, getting there about 9 o'clk — took with me the "Koran" for Emma — Growled a little to Emma concerning Addie — Very foolish — home at 10.30 — but late to bed of course.
