## Left Page

### Tuesday May 14, 1867

Up at 5 o'clk A.M. after a miserable
night — a Regulation bilious upset
— a thorough derangement of
stomach & bowels. Unable
to go to the Studio — so spent
the day lying about the house.
Afternoon a very heavy thunder
storm. Evg. Uncle Willie
back from Phila. I Rode to
Burlings Studio Bdway near
32d St where I had engaged
to meet him & Frederick's as
Committee — Fredericks late.
I found that I was the only
one that had obtained any
information — Read my notes
& agreed to draw up Report
for next Tuesday Evg. —
Home a few minutes after
10 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, May 15, 1867

More of April than May —
Talked with Uncle Willie to
Studio — Still feel very much
under the weather & unlike
work — Spent the mf packing
sketches for Haseltine (7) &
getting them off — with letter —
Home to lunch — Nette Sulfores
there — P.M. from 2 until 4.30
outlining 3 or 4 subjects on mill
boards for Haseltines sketches
at 4.45 took Bdway Car to
Central Park & wandered about
there enjoying it intensely, until
near 7 o'clk — A heavy shower
passed over about 6 o'clk — the
air, perfume & songs & birds
were almost enchanting —
Late tea & spent Evg. Reading
Newspaper & Du Chaillu's "Journey
to Ashango Land" — This Evening
Mrs Knudsen invited us to spend
with her & friends at No. 1 Fifth Ave.
To bed at 10 —
