## Left Page

### Saturday, February 9, 1867

Threatening, muggy sort of a day — dark & lowering with occasional showers. Yonng Bell took his 3d lesson — He brought a pencil study from a deer's head that he has at home that showed decided progress. I finished camp ing sketch. Afternoon given up to Reception — quite a number called. Mr Jas Fraser gave me a commission for a picture worth $150 or $200 or — About 6 o'clk heavy Rain — one very vivid flash of lightning & a clap of thunder to match. Read a letter from Uncle Willie — went in to Willis for a few minutes & to Flemings — To bed at 11 o'clk.

Addie Dunning & Geo. Hawes at the studio this afternoon.

## Right Page

### Sunday, February 10, 1867

Clear cold cloudless day with strong west wind. Almost a gale. Mrs went to Hastings & heard him preach defining true Christian Self-denial — which is to lose one's self in Christ — He was very energetic — Afternoon spent in a manner quite unusual for me — Got comfortably fixed after dinner & slept until after 4 o'clk. — Eveng spent enjoying my fire — chatting & writing [illegible — entire passage]
