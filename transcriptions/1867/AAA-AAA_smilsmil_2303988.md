## Left Page

### Tuesday, September 3, 1867

Up at 5 o'clk — Saw Mr. Loring + Miss
Greene off in the Centre Harbor Stage
Then with Jes took a walk u/o the
Artist's Brook Road. Before 8 o'clk
Rain set in and continued at
intervals all day. During the mny
finished pen sketch of Moat Mt. from
above Intervale House — with two
or three girls went up to North Conway
House to Bowl — Alley not in Rep ain —
home again. Afternoon, ferroted
on Kiarsarge from White Horse Ledge
with pen until 3.30 — then 8 of us in wagon
went to Eastman's + bowled until near
6 — Rcts home — were told of a bear just
caught at McMillan's — all went to
McM's — horses Ran away with Mrs
Hall — she stopped them — good girl —
but she could nt keep down hysterics
— Excitement — home again — our strange
Mr + Mrs French — Messrs Crocker, Reed
\+ Binney — Every Miss Granps ner
Played up on the piano for us
Beethoven — Chopin + Bach —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, September 4, 1867

Up at 6 — very unpromising mng.
walked a couple of miles before bkfst —
spatter of Rain — after bkfst Mr + 
Mrs King drove away — got to work with
can + finished Kiarsarge from Ledge —
at 11 a.m. it had so cleared up that
went back of Kiarsarge Hts + worked
on Moat Mt study until 2.30 — then
home to dinner — at 3.30 went to the
River + commenced 12×18 Study
that I outlined monday p.m — got
the sky + part of distance Rubbed
in — worked until after sunset —
It has been very windy all day, so
as to interfere very much with my
work — a couple of young fellows
visited me while at work this p.m —
say that they are from the West —
no names — unpromising looking birds —
Spent the evenig doing some work
on sketches + washing up brushes —
den etc — some walking for
exercise — then to bed at 10.30
