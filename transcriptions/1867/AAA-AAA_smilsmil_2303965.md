## Left Page

### Friday, July 19, 1867

Up late again — started at 8 o'clk.
Mrng cool & breezy — great white clouds
and beautiful pure blue. Went down to
River & about there with J. A[illegible]. until nearly
11 o'clk. Then home & to work — fixed up
library light & worked all the rest of the
day on 6×10. "Landscape" for Uncle Wille.
I find it very difficult to make any
thing out of it. To-day is the 4th day
that I have spent upon it. Weather
quite showery — afternoon, almost Rainy.
At 7 went to boathouse — Mrs. D[illegible]. [illegible] —
there & two other ladies. About 7¼ Joe & I
got off — water quite rough — pulled down
below Kaal Rock — all sorts of swells from steam
boats at [illegible]. — at one time almost swamped,
at another time almost over — very pleasant
excitement. On Return, pulled 1¼ miles
agst. strong tide & wind in 14 mins. Had 30 or
40 lbs of water in my boat, but thought that
I could have done better. In at 9.5 — Cloudy
& quite dark — Moon up just as I got home.
Found myself the worse for over exertion —
got to bed at 10.20 — passed restless night
with headache.

## Right Page

### Saturday, July 20, 1867

Rather a wet & cool mrng, having rained
considerably during the night. Up very
late. 8.30 — Threatened with headache — after
[illegible] got my feet into hot water and felt
better for it. Got to work on 6×10." over
"the Hills of Northern Penn." for Uncle
Willie & by dinner time got it finished.
Principally adding greys & solidity to the
thin painting & reducing some of the crude
color. It is rather showy & I hope will please.
Afternoon spent carrying a headache & doing
some little work on sketches — until 3 o'clk when
mother arrived by stage from Po[illegible] & N.Y. at 5 o'clk.
took out Beattie's boat — wind high N.W., water
rough & showery. Had a good pull & dance
for an hour. In at 6, pretty wet. at 6½ J. A. [illegible].
called, looked over my sketches & took away the
Buttermilk Ravine sketch that I painted for him.
At 7.30 went to boathouse & met him — too squally
to go out. With him & Jel. Sat there until near 9 o'clk.
Then home — heavy Rain soon after getting
in more Rain during the night — got to
bed about 10.30
