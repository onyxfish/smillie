## Left Page

### Saturday, September 21, 1867

Up at 5.30 to see Mr. Hall & Miss Williams off on Centre Harbor Stage — Sunrise on Moat Mt., and on Washington and Adams, very beautiful — The day very mild & pleasant — Mrs before I went out to my work, wrote letters to Ogilvie & Sam. Colman — A little after 9. went to my day bid study & worked until 1 o'clk. our foliage & foreground — think that I can finish it now in one more sitting — After dinner went to work on large Moat Mt. Study, painted in the ledge getting it very strong & rather heavy — After working until the sun would come under my umbrella, went in doors & continued painting as long as I could see — finishing what I had already laid in — Beautiful sunset finished a tip-top working day — Ev'g went to Kiarsarge House Hall & heard "The World Renowned Barker family" sing in a most execrable manner a number of Common songs — Rec'd letters from Father & Frank Martin. Brilliant aurora at 10 — to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### Sunday, September 22, 1867

Up late — after 7 — Bkfst. late — after bkfst. went to Kiarsarge House with Sel — no church service at Cong't Ch. — left and sauntered over meadows to the River — then along shore, to Artists Book from there, home, consuming all of the Mf. until 1 o'clk — Wrote a letter to Will — then dinner. Have had a dull headache all day. The weather has been cloudy, warm, threatening rain — giving us a little dash between 1 & 2 o'clk — After dinner wrote a letter to Fan Mulford and a note to Alfred Jones — Afternoon, — cleared up delightfully — Ev'g fine sunset — heavy clouds backed away to North & N.W — looking very threatening — Ev'g, Miss Graupener entertained us with some music — To bed about 11.
