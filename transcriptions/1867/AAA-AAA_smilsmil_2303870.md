## Left Page

### Sunday, January 6, 1867.

Miserable blustering sort of
a day — Father went out in
the morning, & of course,
caught more cold, as
I felt rather used up,
from yesterdays exercise
I kept very quiet and
did not go out of the house.
Afternoon, took my walk
in the parlors — Twilight
had a pleasant family
sing — Every most of the
family at church (D. Weston's)
— baptizing.

## Right Page

### Monday, January 7, 1867.

Blustering cold raw day —
Between 10 & 11 o'clk A.M. went to
Uncle Geo's by appointment to
get the "set" of my teeth — through
with him, or he with me, before 12 —
went to Dunning's & saw Fred D —
& made engagement with him
for Thursday Mf next at 11 o'clk.
Then went to Miner & Sommerville's
gallery to see the Must pictures —
Did not expect much & was not
disappointed — some of the smaller
pictures, very bad. Must has a studio
in the bldg. called upon him —
very pleasant, — Interesting sketches
of old church in Norway. Home at
1.30 — went out & bought oysters —
big fellows. At home all the P.M.
Every went in to Bro. Wills, to see
about some drawings for A.B.N. Co
— All very lovely until Mr.
Robt. Hinshelwood came in, then
I left — Slept again, after
several weeks change — in my
usual bed with Geo.
