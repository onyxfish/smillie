## Left Page

### Thursday, July 11, 1867

Cloudless, breezy day — at 8.30 went to [illegible]
house to meet J. R— not here — but he [illegible]
with horse & wagon for me to ride into town [illegible]
him as it was too rough to pull shells — I
declined & amused myself in Beache's [illegible]
for an hour or more — wind strong from [illegible]
& water quite rough — waited for newspaper
train — then up home & to work again (3rd day)
on 6×10 — managed to get it all in after
a fashion — but not as I had wished —
at 5.30 stopped work & went to J. R's where
as he had promised, he had left the key [illegible]
his tool chest & instructions that I should
work at his bench — By the voluntary aid
of the butter & one or two others I was able to
make what I wanted — a [illegible] — at
7 Ret'd home & found all leaving for the boat —
went to River — got out the boat & Matt,
Kate, Geo & I started out — Father refused
to join — boat small & water still rough — out
from 7.30 until after 9 — air glorious —
water glorious — moon glorious — pulled
across the River & close along shore —
great — Father worrying when we got home —
to bed after 10 —

## Right Page

### Friday, July 12, 1867

Overcast, very warm & muggy day — Up
late but got to work a little after 9 on
a small panel "The Cliff — Monument — Net" —
Painted faithfully about five hours upon it
before 5 o'clk P.M. — had succeeded in
finishing, a somewhat crisp, cool and
greenish gray sketch for Haseltine —
had hoped to paint something better —
am glad his is no worse. This good
enough for the $15.00 — (a very bad way to
[illegible]) — and yet, not too good —
During the day J. R. sent a note inviting
me to Row with him at 7 o'clk P.M. —
went punctually — It had rained a
sharp shower from 4.30 until 6 — So at 7
altho' all overcast, the air was delightful —
glorious sunset effect over Catskill Mts —
reflections on water — flood tide — swells
from "Jas Baldwin" caught us at Ferry —
[illegible] things — but did not upset us — bully
[illegible] — pulled back — tide & wind with
us — everything very lovely — in, about
[illegible].40 — after a delightful pull for me.
home at 9.15 & to bed about 10.
