## Left Page

### Sunday, May 26, 1867

Raining as usual — stopped raining by about church time but showered during the p.m. & rained like split at night. Mf went to Hastings. 42d St. & heard, as usual, an excellent discourse. Text, 2d Cor 2 &16. Afternoon, spent a good part of it religiously asleep — and part of it finishing a letter to Addie Dunning — mailed it on my way to church. Evef went to Bd way Tab[ernacle] & heard Missionary Services — begging aid for persecuted Christians in Spain — very interesting. The speaker from Spain was quite fluent in English — the one from Mexico I could not understand — a third, pastor of a Spanish congregation in this city was "very fervid" — Services over at 9.30.

## Right Page

### Monday, May 27, 1867

Lovely Spring day. Cloudless from morning until night. But very springy. On way to Studio met Emma Ireland & walked with her to 36th St. near 5 Ave. Back again to studio & worked nearly all day on "Circle" "Noon" or "Day" on Hudson River above Po'keepsie". (2d day) It is better, altho' metallic & hard in its greens, in the foreground. Late in the afternoon worked awhile on "Morning" — 7kg m. Maud Diven called at Studio a little after 5 o'clk at 6.20 Gil & I walked up home with her. Evef at Fleming's — at Burgraf's and at home — Will & Joe in for a while on a visit.
