## Left Page

### Monday, January 14, 1867.

Clear cold day. All
day trying to get some-
thing picturesque out-
of the photo's of Buenos
Ayres transportation wagon
for A. B. N. Co. Ab-
about 4 o'clk p.m. went
down to Studio — brought
home my portrait by
Augers in its showing
frame. Evef, nothing
in particular — to bed
before 11.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, January 15, 1867.

Very cold day. Morning
dull and unpromising,
looking like Snow. Went
down town to A.B.N. Co. with my
sketch of B.A. Transportation
train — long talk with Goodall
— vast number of suggestions
and an addition to my stock
of Photo's & Litho's. Then more
talk with Gavit — home at
1.30 — dinner about 2. At
3.30 commenced an illustra-
ted note to Meta Gavit. 7 pages
4 Illustrations — finished by 6 o'clk
Evef. Jel. went to Fanny Kimball's
to meet Rosalie Goodman, the Irelands
& the Haws — I could not go.
So sent "Regrets" with one "Ill."
No party — but just a few
that met at Lenox last
Summer — To bed before
11.
