## Left Page

### Friday, December 20, 1867

My excitement + overwork have
culminated today in a furious
nervous headache — It is a long
time since I have passed such
a day, suffering so much pain &
sleeping so little — Did not get
up until after 10 o'clk P.M. & then
sat in my chair until 2.30 A.M.
when I went to bed again.

## Right Page

### Saturday, December 21, 1867

Mild, thawing & raw sort of a day.
Did not get up until near 8 o'clk
feeling very slim after yesterday.
About 11 o'clk. Rode down town &
called first upon A.H. Ritchie — he
wishes me to help him upon the sky
of a large plate — I tried to beg off —
but he persists & offers me $20. per
day for my services — I promised to help
him. Then called upon Leeds to see
him about selling my 4 ft. picture to-night.
— Then to Father's — went to lunch with
him — Then to A.B.N. Co. & passed some time
with Jno. E. Gavit. Selecting more material
for drawings, then to Studio which I reached
at 3 o'clk P.M. Young Bell after waiting
for me some time had gone home — Went over
to N.A.of D. for a few minutes. Then to Burling's
then home — At 7 started for Philharmonic —
stopping at N.A.of D. for 15 m. Got to Phil. just
as the Concert commenced — Enjoyed the
Schumann Symphony in C. exceedingly —
After the concert Gol. & I went to Leeds Gallery
& found that our 4 ft. paintings had been sold
for $225 & $200. Home & to bed at 12 o'clk.
