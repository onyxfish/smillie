## Left Page

### Thursday, October 31, 1867

Up at 7.30 with a half headache. Day overcast & disagreeable — at Studio shortly after 9 and worked all day under the disadvantage of a headache on pencil sketch of "Detached Rock near the Summit of Chocorua." Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — At 4.30 P.m. Many called at Studio & with Geo & me went over to Uncle Frank Bournes in Jersey City, to take dinner. We had dinner at 6.30 and from 7.50 until 9. I enjoyed Purgatory, as with my lame head I Sat & listened to Uncle Frank discourse upon Canadian Politics. Left there a little after 9 — got home at 10.30 & went right to bed.

## Right Page

### Friday, November 1, 1867

Up at 7 — clear cool morning — got to Studio a little before 9 and set to work with pencil on "Crest of Chocorua" finishing it up. — much interrupted during the day. Deluice called about 12 o'clk & stayed until half past one. At 2.30, Geo and I went to 2d Phil. Rehearsal. After Rehearsal walked partway home with Nettie Jessup — then went to Mr. Hart's Studio — Saw him & there met A. H. Ritchie — Looked over his (Hart's) Summer Studies in Water Color and was very much surprised at the strength & facility exhibited — went with him in to see some of Page's alterations. Returned to Studio before 6. Paid Mrs. Dodworth part of back Rent. Evening at home, interrupted and doing but little. To bed early.
