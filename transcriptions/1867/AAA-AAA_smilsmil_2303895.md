## Left Page

### Monday February 25, 1867.

Mean day over head —
meaner under foot.
Commenced repainting, 3d
time, on Winan's 10½×18in.
Worked over sky, distance
and middle distance.
Improving it very much
I think — Home to lunch
at 1 o'clk & found Uncle Wm
Turrell — Dan Sayre at
Studio — agreed to meet him
at Diven's in the evening.
Ge. and I went up there —
Dan there & girls amiable
— Stayed until 10.30 Dan
walked down home with us
& on to the Hoffman House
where he is staying.

## Right Page

### Tuesday February 26, 1867.

Morning dubious — but p.m.
charming — Worked on 10½×
18in (for Winans) through the
middle distance until near
one o'clk — Cleaned up & went
home to lunch, dressed &
started for Uncle A's by 8.
Ave R.R. Reaching there about
3.15 P.m. Went into stable &
tried sketching from horse
"Gipsy". but with little success.
About 4.30 went to the house
played billiards with Uncle
m until dinner time, 6 o'clk.
Geo. Fan & Maud came by 4.30
stage. Dinner of Oysters, etc.
etc. Turkey etc. etc. — & things.
After dinner, more billiards
girls taking part. Drove over to
8 Ave R.R. at 9.30 — girls home about
11 o'clk — home ourselves at 11.30
to bed at midnight, tired.
