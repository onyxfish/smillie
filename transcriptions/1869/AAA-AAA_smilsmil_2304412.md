## Left Page

### December Friday, 10 1869.

The glorious weather still holds \- Enjoyed my walk to depot \- 8.39 train \- Studio at 9.10 \- To work on "Boquet River" (2° day, Readjusting) \- meutrm big trees \+ foliage of upper r. n. Corner. C. P. Cranch \- E. J. Gray, old Mr. Dunham \+ old Mr. Wood of Utica, called. \- I worked until 12.30, washed up. \+ at 1 went to Kehm's \+ had my hair cut \- called on Mrs. Brevort \+ offered her [illegible]'s Phil. R.R. tickets. Home at 1.45 \- Lunch \+ Rode to Phil. Rehearsals. \- Met Bell, Mary Tweeedy \+ Clara Dunham. They had a seat \+ I sat with th after \- me walked to Studio together \- fixed fire \+ rushed for 5.10 train \- Spent the eveng all alone, studying French \- got in bed \- Uncle A. \+ Junie at Dodworth's \- Miss Portu[illegible] somewhere in Conn \-
got to bed about 10. 45

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 11 1869.

Delightful day. \- By 8.33 train got to studio at 9.5 \- at work on "Boquet River" (3° day) painted in a very general blockesque way over L.h. trees \- water and foreground. \- This now Reddish instead of greenish, as before, \+ in a condition for me to finish up. Mr. Walter Brown called about 2.30 \- Home to lunch at 1.15 \- back at 2.40 During p.m. \- Mrs. Mulford \+ 2 yng lady friends called, also 2 other ladies that I did not know. Took 5.10 train for M. ville \- Eveng alone again, Reading \+ a little French \- got to bed at 8 fell \+ Uncle A \+ Junie at a German at Dodworth's.
got to bed about 10.45
