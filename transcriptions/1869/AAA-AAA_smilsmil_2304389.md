## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 19 1869.

Overcast, blustering & dis[agreeable] —
agreeable — got to studio at
9.15 — Soon went down town mki
sketch to show to Mr. Tahnstock —
got there at 10.30 — Saw him — he m[ade]
pleased with my sketch & ideas —
went to Halpin's & got Jones's draw[ing]
"Feeding Dolly" & took it with me —
went to C.B.N. Co— was told to go
on with the Jay Cooke-Tahnstock
drawing — back to studio at 12 —
Home to lunch at 1 — back at
2.30 & worked outlining drawing
on paper that I sketched last
night (2° day) — Ned Dunning
called — Home to tea at 6 —
Evelf Will & Fanny Kimball
called — I mounted two large
water-color studies — George
getting along very slowly —
I got to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### October Wednesday, 20 1869.

Clear & cold — [illegible] sorts with
threatening of a headache —
got to studio about 9.15 & to work
on Pacific R.R. drawing — About
noon Felly sent home my
stretchers for studies and
until after 1 I was engaged
stretching studies — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — Met Mr.
Isaac Riley & Mr. Hunt, Jessup —
back at 2.45 — at 3. Frank Savigla[y]
came along & bored me for more
than an hour so that my P.M.
was quite spoiled — Varnished
& otherwise fixed up studies
doing but little to the drawing.
Home to tea at 6 — Glorious
moonlight night — Spent the
evelf Reading Derby's
trans. of the Iliad of Homer —
got to bed at 10.45 —
