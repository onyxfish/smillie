## Left Page

### March Friday, 19 1869.

Overcast, about 7 o'clk P.M. getting into a snowy drizzle.
Got to the Studio at 8.20. Set to work on 24×40 (17th day) Repainting
bushes + banks on L.H. Side. — Am afraid that I am getting it too
green, but am certain that it's better than it was before —
took lunch at the studio + at 2.15 went to Phil. Rehearsal.
Did not enjoy it very much.
Back to Studio at 5 o'clk —
washed up some dishes — put
things to rights + got home to
tea at 6.45 — After tea went up
to Burgorat's for my boots. Read
Dana's Geology until after
9 o'clk. — Then got my feet
into hot water + alumn and
sat another half hour — Read-
ing Tom Hood — Got to bed
about 10.30 —

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 20 1869.

Dark + not N.F., lifting before noon + clearing finely at night.
Got to Studio at 8.35 — set to work on 24×40 (18th day) painting
over foliage on R.H. upper side making it all grayer + lighter.
Home to lunch abt. — Back
at 2 — afternoon, finished up foliage + worked on trunk
+ stems of big Tree — Home
to Tea at 5 — Read until
[illegible] interested in new books +
newspapers — gave my [torn]
feet another hot water + alum
bathing + got to bed about
10.30.     Father still under
the weather + at home.
