## Left Page

### February Friday, 19 1869.

Snow again in the m'f — clear at
9.30 clk — with squalls during
the day — at times all clear — at
others all dark — At Studio at 9.10
Spent the m'f getting lower Ausable
Lake, charcoaled & continued on Canvas
12×22 for Dr. Kingsley — Rec'd a
letter from Dr. Lewis & Circular
for his Water Color Exh'n in the
next A/c — Lunch at the Studio
at 2.15 went to Phil. Rehearsal —
Enjoyed it & immediately after
went to Gauthier's — Snedecor's
& then to Studio — Finished out-
lining 12×22 — Home to tea
at 6.30 — Emma Ireland at
Studio about 12 — Uncle George
took tea with us — Ollie Treedy
spent the even'g with the girls —
my even'g Ran away from me —
got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 20 1869.

Another disagreeably day, with
more of threatening than Result.
got to Studio at 9.20 & spent the
m'f on a water color sketch about
10×17 — view on the Saco at N. Conway —
home to lunch at 1.15 — at 2 started
for down-town, stopping at Sarony's
then to J. P. Ridner's — he was out —
then to Will's & with him to a
store in Maiden Lane & bought a
fine shade for my Table light —
then to Meucci's in Dey S't to see
about his gathering & forwarding
the drawings for the Proposed
Philadelphia — then Rode up to Kelly
from there walked to the Studio &
then home at 6.15. Hegeman to-
day made to give me a present of two
large bottles of Cologne — Even'g
did little — tried to write one
of the several letters I owe —
but failed — got to bed
about 11 — To-day Mary
& the D't Ret'd to their boarding
house in 34th S't.
