## Left Page

### October, Monday, 11, 1869

Very pleasant day — Feeling slim after yesterdays per-
formance. I got up late &
spent the mng until 1 o'clk
in Central Park — Then
home to lunch — lying
about until 4 o'clk when
I went to studio — Saw
Mr. & Mrs. Brevoort — just in
from Conn — then made to
Y.M.C.A. building & looked
through the studios — then to
Kelly's — then to Snedecor's &
7 o'clk. home. Have made
up my mind that owing to
bad weather — cold in the head &
impecuniosity I wonts to Mt.
but will stay at home — pitch
into my work & see if I can
get some money — Eve up
used up with my Cold —
pain about nose & eyes very gr[eat]
got to bed about 9.30

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday, 12, 1869

In bed again all day
with a beastly combination
of cold in the head & sick
headache — Got a little
rice at 5 — at 6 got up &
at 7.30 took Tea, spending
the rest of the night until
11 o'clk. in my big Chair —
Emma Bourne & Walter
here to Tea & until 10 o'clk.
The day has been a very
windy overcast one — So
that I feel no disappoint-
ment in not getting off to
Montrose to study out
of doors — Heavy thunderstorm
a few miles south of us, between
1 & 2 o'clk. a.m. —
