## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 22 1869

Shocking day, did not get up
until 10 oclk — got to studio
at 11 — Spent the day getting
studio fixed up — getting
engraving on, Ready for work
also the tint plate + sketching
paper for my water color
drawing — Did not go home
to lunch — The topic of the
day, the Ogilvie's wedding —
The Sun this mng. publishes
an acct. that cannot be called
flattering — Went home to
tea at 6.30 — Went wrote
to Mr. Mulford — To Dibble
at Keene Flats + acceptance
of Mr. Spring's invitation to
Private Theatricals next
Tues. Went, got to bed
about 10.45

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 23 1869

Fine day — got to studio at
9.20 — Delayed at home getting
acid, bellows + things. Put wall
on tint plate — Toning Skinner
called to explain anything
that I did not understand —
got acid on about noon — did
not act right — evidently biting
unevenly — Fought with it for a
while + I gave it up + mrs. Beatie
drawing [illegible] — went down town, stopped
at Parker's + getting a lunch at
2.30 — then to Continental Bank
Note Co. Robertson took the bad
news very good naturedly and
at once put things under way
for a new plate + Ruling — on my
way up, stopped at Brooks Bros.
+ bought a suit of clothes — then at
studio + home at 6.30 — Evening
feeling out of sorts went to Bryant's
Home at 10 + to bed at 11.15
Father Mother, Kate + Matt went
to Mrosby to Aunt Polley's funeral at
2 o'clk —
