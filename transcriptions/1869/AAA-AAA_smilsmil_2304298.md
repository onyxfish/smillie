## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 18, 1869

Beautiful day — Up early and at 10. a.m. went up to Uncle a's via 8th ave. R.R. Spent the mf until dinner time sitting in the sun \+ enjoying the fresh air. Ate my dinner with discretion at 2 o'clk. George joined with us — at 3.30 Uncle a. \+ I went riding in his new buggy after Blk Hawk — we went across McComb's Dam Bridge — up Central Ave. to Jerome Race course across to King's Bridge \+ then down home — getting in at 6 — took tea Uncle a's \+ at 7.15 went over to the 8th Ave Cars — getting to 41st about 8.30 — got to bed shortly after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 19, 1869

Very warm \+ sultry — part of the time overcast. Got to studio at 9.10, set palette and commenced painting on Pate's picture 9 × 10 in. "Upper ausable Lake" — Felt very much melted \+ spring-like. Home at 12.30 — Took lunch and at 1 o'clk went over to Düring's to attend the funerals of his little girl — Back to studio at 2 o'clk — Painted pretty steadily until 4 o'clk — getting sky, mts. lake \+ part of middle distance done — Mrs Tiffany \+ Belle Breedy called — Fanny Kimball called in the mf with some trailing arbutus. Home to tea at 7 o'clk — at 8.30 went to Sommerville Gallery — Avery's Private View of his pictures — music and a Supper — a very pleasant affair — Home at 10 o'clk and to bed about 10.45.
