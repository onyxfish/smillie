## Left Page

### June, Saturday, 19, 1869

A glorious day, but very warm — Still feel a good deal out of sorts and added to dissatisfaction with my work I have made but a poor day of it on my etching (3rd day) worked until about 6 — Then Tea — after that a saunter to the River and at 8.30 Father Kate & Matt drove into town & brought out Charl. who came up on the Mary Powell — The night was heavenly and I paced the Roadway to the gate until 10 o'clk when Father got back — Then went about the place with Charl. & at 11 again went to the River & saw the Albany Night boats go up — Got to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday, 20, 1869

Very Close, muggy, showery day. Sprinkling at odd times & giving two good showers — Still feel an oppression about the head & generally dyspeptic — Strawberries are no doubt very good but not as an exclusive diet — Spent the day lying about lazily — Afternoon, between 3 & 4, Parker came out, walked — Everybody after 8 o'clk. Matt, Kate, Parker & Charl. drove into town — Matt not to come back — She will take the noon boat down to-morrow. Kate & Charl. Returned at 9.20 just in time to avoid a heavy shower — Put the horse up and got to bed about 10 o'clk.
