## Left Page

### March, Monday 15, 1869

Shocking day \- Sleet, hail and Rain freezing as it fell \- Go to Studio at 9 o'clk \- Kindled fire, swept up \+ by 10 o'clk, started on 24×40 (13th day) Repainted the water \+ banks \- I think that it is progressing in the Right direction and I only had more time, would feel very sanguine of making a creditable work of it \- I see distinctly all that I wish to do, but can only approach it by Repeated paintings \- Home to lunch at 1 o'clk \- met Eaton \+ stopped at his studio for a few minutes \- Back at 2.15 \+ worked until after 5 \- Weather cleared during the P.M \- Clear \+ Crisp \+ [illegible] \- Home to Tea at 6.30 \- Even Reading Siiskce's" History of Art" \- got to bed shortly after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 16, 1869

Clear, misty end of day \- got to studio at 8.30 \- \+set to work at once on 24×40 (14th day) painting over the R.h. lower corner \- at 4 o'clk, home to lunch \- back at 2 \- worked steadily until about 5 o'clk, when I felt pretty well played out \- I am full of discouragement and yet desperately hopeful over this work \- Mc. Birney \- Y.M.C.A. called \- also Miss Kate Drinker \+ miss Hetty Biddle \- Home to Tea at 6.30 \- spent [illegible] of the even looking men \- Gil Blas and got to bed about 10.15
