## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 14, 1869

Felt much better to-day —
got up about 10 o'clk and
loafed about the house, now
in me easy chair, now in
another. Charl still at
home \+ Katy at her work.
Geo. dined with Mr. Geo.
Whitney, Mr. W. T. Richards \+ Mr.
Nilcoy, all of Phila. at the
Albemarle.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 15, 1869

A lovely cloudless day — got
up about 10 a.m. — not feeling
as well as I had hoped.
Heavy \+ giddy in the head,
weak in the knees \+ infirm
of purpose. Sat about the
house until 3 o'clk P.m. —
then walked down to Studio,
left there at 5 — Stopped \+
had a pleasant visit with
R. S. Gifford \+ Kuntze — Home
at 6 — Spent the even'g
until 9.30 writing a sketch
of my life for Redpath \+
about 10 got to bed —
