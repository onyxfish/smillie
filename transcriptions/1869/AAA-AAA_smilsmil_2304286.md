## Left Page

### March, Saturday, 27, 1869.

a genuine spring day, mild
& enervating — got to studio
at 8.10 & at once at work on
24×40 (23d day) working over
the foliage in upper R.h.
Cor. and on the big tree —
am not at all satisfied with
it — "it is" chewed up" and
monotonous in color — a
criticism that applies to
the whole picture. at 12.15
rode down town — paid prem.
on life ass. — lunched & back
to studio at 2 — worked until
5.30 — Young Jodhunter called —
also Dr. Kingsley — Home to
tea at 7 — evef spent doing
nothing — gave my poor feet
a dose of alum & hot water
& at 10.45 got to bed —

## Right Page

### March, Sunday, 28, 1869.

Beautiful mf, thick & overcast
during p.m. & evef. went to hear
Dr. Hastings in the mf. His text
was Col. 3d 1st v. I must confess
that I heard very little of the
sermon as I was suffering with
a stupid headache — after
service, went with Charl up
to Uncle Al's — met Watson V.V.
in the car. Spent the p.m.
rambling around & at 6.30
home to tea — Evef. went to
hear J. P. Thompson on the autho-
rity of man — He explained
to us that he did not know any
thing about it nor did any one
else — but still he made a very
interesting discourse — after
service went to studio & fixed
the fire & took a look at my
picture by gas light — home
at 10.15 & to bed in about half
an hour —
