## Left Page

### June Saturday, 5 1869.

Very warm clear day \- Mr[illegible] rode down to Sauthier's \- then to Meug[illegible] \+ left with him his drawing of West Point. to at B. N. Co \- got Acid. went to Sellers \- did not see him \+ to Kissam's \+ ordered tin box for water-colors \- home to lunch at 1.45 \- back to Studio at 2.45 \+ etched on Patis' plate all the P.M. (5½ days) \- Evening home to tea at 7 o'clk \- at 8.30 Geo. \+ I went to the Freeds \+ had a very pleasant chat \+ even[ing] until 11.20 \- walked up home \+ got to bed about 12.30

## Right Page

### June Sunday, 6 1869.

Delightful day \- Head achy in the m[orning] \+ did not get up until after 10 \- did not go to church in the morning \- Dinner at home at 1 \- Mr. Bigelow, my old Teacher called about 1 clk P.M. \+ took tea \- at about 5 I went to the Irelands \- there until 6.15 \- then to the Kimball's \+ took tea there \- stayed until 8 \- then went to Mary Butler's \+ stayed there until 9 \- then to Miss Dewry's with books that she had lent me \- not in \- then to Brevoort's at 9.20 \- Found there Mr. \+ Mrs Beans \- there until 10.15 \- Home \+ to bed at 11.15
