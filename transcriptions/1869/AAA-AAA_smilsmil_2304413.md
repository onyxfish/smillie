## Left Page

### December Sunday, 12 1869

Did not get up until 8 o' clo —
M[illegible] overcast — quite a shower
about 10 after which it broke
away, at noon being warm &
sunny — again clouds — but
later clear with glorious sun-
set & superb moonlight —
greased up my big books &
spent the [illegible] from 10.30 to
12.30 wandering over the place
& along the River — Dinner
at 2 — P.M. again out until
after sunset, which I enjoyed
amazingly — The snow &
the glowing colors of sunset
was something exceedingly [illegible]
Tea at 5.30 — Eve[ning] Riddle[?]
& to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### December Monday, 13 1869

Superb day again — Rushing with
half my bkfst in my mouth caught
8.33 train — Studio at 9.5. After
kindling fires commenced the
painting in "Dark against day's
golden death" 33×44 (4th day.)
Home to lunch at 1 — back at
2 — & worked until near 4.30
getting sky & distance in —
am rather disappointed at
the result — too generally
red — neither so strong or
so brilliant as I had hoped
to get in that the first painting.
Went down to Snedicors and
home to Tea at 6 — 9 o' Rell
home about noon — all are
very much pleased at his
improved appearance —
Eve[ning] at Burggrafs — washing
brushes and chat — to
bed at 10.45
