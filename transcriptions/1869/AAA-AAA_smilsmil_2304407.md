## Left Page

### November Sunday, 28 1869.

In bed all day with a wild, nervous headache —
Got up at 6 — took tea &
Spent the rest of the night
until about 1 30 a.m. in
my big chair doing a little
Reading, but more "laying
off."

## Right Page

### November Monday, 29 1869.

overcast day with a weak
attempt at Rain at night —
Got to Studio at 9.20, Kindled
fire & got to work on 8½×14½ a
"Lake Placid & Whiteface" finishing
over it generally, scumbling
sky, mt. & water — This cold
perhaps, in tone, but it is a
movement in a new direction
for me, that is, in atmospheric
gradations & color. I have
worked on it 4 days. Home to
lunch at 1. Just missed Belle &
Mary Tweedy — 9p. Mother went
up to Uncle Aaron's at 11 o'clk. —
Back to Studio at 2.15. Worked
on little twilight "Alone" & then
again on Placid — at 5 went to
Brooks Bros. P. bill & ordered vest
& p.k. then to Snedecor, home at 7.
Menger p° to Will to-day $100. bal.
for my West Point drawing — Eve.
Enjoying something of a headache
did nothing & to bed about 10.30
