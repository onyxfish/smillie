## Left Page

### November Saturday, 6 1869.

mild, clear mrg clouding
over before noon — got to
Studio at 9.15 and finished
the 8 × 14 that I commenced
painting in yesterday —
About noon Nellie's Josie
brought in a Mr. Harker — an
interesting young widow and
a young lady friend — Had a
short pleasant visit — Home
to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15
P.M. painted in small sketch
"Grain field — Stockbridge" Worked
until 4.30 — Home at 6.30 —
Eveg until 10 o'lk mounting
drawings — 3 of Georgia's studies
& my "Bay Mills" and an oil
sketch — got to bed some
after 11 —

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 7 1869.

Cold, blustering day with
some attempts at snow
flurries — Mrg went to
Dr. Hastings' & heard him
preach concerning the straight
way & the narrow path — He
was evidently very earnest
but I must confess that a
large apple that I ate just
after 11 o'cl't was too much for
me & I slept during part of
the sermon — Home to dinner
walking with Düring who
was at the church — Afternoon
walked in Central Park —
very cold & windy — Back
at 5.30 — Eveg at home
reading & writing a
letter to Han Mulford —
got to bed about 11 —
