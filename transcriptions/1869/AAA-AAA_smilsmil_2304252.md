## Left Page

### January, Monday 18, 1869

Snowing briskly all day. Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. Worked like a good fellow until 4.30. Got my drawing all in & now am Ready to go over it finishing & giving unity — "Getting" my foreground pool of water the last thing. Took lunch at the Studio & at 1.30 went over to the N.A. of D. to see if there was any thing that needed my attention. Colman is very kindly doing my work. Back to Studio in half an hour went to Academy again at 5. Home at 5.45 — wrote some dunning letters to Water Color members & tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 at N.A. of D. There "hanging" with Nood & Wyant until 10 — then to Studio — Home at 11 and Right to bed —

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 19, 1869

Brilliant mild and sloppy. Got to studio before 9 o'clk. at once, set to work on sky of my drawing — washing more color & general effect into it — waiting for the washes to dry — worked over the rest of the subject, getting it more into harmony. Home to lunch back at 2 — Until 4 worked over the sky, stippling with pumice rubber or ink eraser — getting it generally soft, but unable to correct the mealy & dirty effect — much to my disappointment. Home at 5 — got paste & my tea & Rete to Studio with Geo & mounted my drawing all Replet — Went to N.A. of D. — there at work until 10.30. Rete to Studio with Colman — there for half an hour — home at 11+ immediately to bed —
