## Left Page

### October Friday, 15 1869.

A very beautiful mrg — overcast about 2 o'clk — and Rain by 3 which kept up steadily all the rest of the day & evng. Got to Studio at 9.15 & spent the mrg arranging & cleaning up. Geo B. Wood called — just in from E. town — Then Kate Drinker, just in from Phila — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 spent the rest of the day trying to make or find places for things. Home to tea at 5.30. Geo still disheartened but in less pain than yesterday. Spent evng with Father & working a little on some summer sketches — got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 16 1869.

Fine day but windy & cool. Went to studio with some bundles & from there down town — went to the Continental Bank note Co — Saw Robertson & agreed to bite some ruling on a large tint plate for him — then to A.B.N. Co & after waiting until near noon went with Stiner to Jay Cooke's office and saw Mr Fahnestock concerning design for vigt. on Pacific Railway Bond — took dinner with Father — Rode up to Lauthier's — then to Snedecor's and Kellip's — having forgotten a note to Will, Rode down town again & gave it to him — got back to studio about 3.45 P.M. — Set to work scrubbing up at Studio & worked until 6 o'clk — then went to 25 St. to saw Oscar Ireland for a few minutes — Misses Bascomb & Wilder at Studio about 5 o'clk — Home to tea at 6.30 — Evng reading Hamerton's "Painters Camps" — got to bed about 10.30.
