## Left Page

### March Saturday, 13 1869.

again overcast, but cleared off 9 & 10 a.m. — got to Studio at 8:45. Until 11, putting strips around 24×40 (12th day) & painting over them. — By a mistake Smedicino made my frame is just a trifle too large. — Painted on trees in upper R.h. Cor — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 — worked as long as I could see — until 5.30 painting tree trunks etc —
Home about 6.30 — Evening about 9 o'clk — went with Gil — to Knoedler's private opening of his new place Cor. of 5th Ave & 22nd — Met many interesting people & had a very fine Supper — Introduced to W. T. Richards & had a pleasant chat with Prof. S. F. B. Morse —
Left at nearly 1 — home and right to bed —

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 14 1869.

Charming day — got up about 9 o'clk. a little headachy from the Punch & Champagne of last night, no doubt. Mrs — went to Hastings — He was urgent in behalf of a Mission School building, to be put up by his people — His text was Matt. 25: 40 — me to dinner — bet — 3 & 4 o'clk. P.M. — went up to Central Park — sauntered about there — home to tea at 6 o'clk — went with Gil — went to D. P. Thompson's — heard him preach a very interesting sermon on the Creation of man, combatting the development theories of Darwin & Huxley — Emma Bourne spent the Sunday with us — got to bed about 11 —
