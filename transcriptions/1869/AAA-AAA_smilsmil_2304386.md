## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 13 1869

Storm & Rain in the mrng — going off into a blustering cold day & closing with a clear, chilly moonlight night. Did not get up until near 9 o clk. a.m. — still feeling the effects of my cold — worked on the water-color studies that were damaged with water on my way home, Repairing them to look about as good as new — Did not go out of doors at all — Eve'g worked with chalk & pencil on some of my slighter sketches — all out to a 22. [illegible] Reception except Kate — Joe Treadway called & she entertained him. I got to bed about 10.45.

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 14 1869

Charming mrng, but cool, by noon however, overcast and rest of day cold & cheerless — got to studio shortly after & at once set to work clearing out things. Spent the mrng on the closets & home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 — on way met R.S. Gifford, just Retd. from Cal. — Oregon — called on Jas. M. Hart & Ferguson — at 3. got to work again at Studio & dug into the sweeping & at it until dusk — Salvo came & took away the furniture that he left with us while he was in Europe — glad of it — home to tea at 6 o clk — note from Oscar Ireland — [illegible] not so well this eveng. — wrote to Uncle Wm at Montrose, to J.V. Noel — [illegible], Redea & to Sid. Hay — getting to bed about 11.
