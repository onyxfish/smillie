## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 8 1869

another very pleasant day. Took 8.33 Train to the city, got to studio about 9.15. worked all the mrf on Jay Cook's draw, taking it home with me at 1. lunch \+ wrote to C.P. Cranch \+ Walter Brown. Back at 2.30. About 3. Belle \+ Mary Treedy called. leaving after a pleasant call of about an hour. after them Ned Dunning. At 4.5 left stu \+ took 4.25 Train. Uncle a., Junie \+ Miss Porter, with James, horses \+ sleigh met me at depot about 4.50. Evening spent in general chat, George appearing very lively \+ sitting up until near 10 o'clk. I got to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 9 1869

Superb, clear day. enjoyed a delightful walk on east Piazza before breakfast. walked to depot \+ took 8.33 Train. got to studio at 9.10. Set to work at Reident-ing" on the Boquet [illegible] my Academy picture of last Spring. Home to lunch at 1. back at 2.15 worked until after 4, getting over sky, distance \+ part of middle distance. I am now sanguine of making something that will be more creditable to me. walked to depot \+ took 5.10 Train to M. ville. on walking to house, met Uncle a., Junie \+ James with sleigh, Rode with them. Got not so well to-day. constipation troubles mine. Evef chatting, Trying to learn French verbs \+ listening to Junie play on the piano. To bed soon after 10.
