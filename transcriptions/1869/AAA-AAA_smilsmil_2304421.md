## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 28 1869.

Dark, miserable and Rainy —
got to Studio at 9.10 and at
once set to work biting the
little temple on Continental
tint plate — finished it be
about 10.30 & then at it with
the graver — about 12.30, bo[y]
came for it by 1 o'clk. I
packed him off — Haven &
Portsmouth N.H. called — Home
to lunch at 1.20 back at 2.5
the Rest of P.M. outlining my
charcoal on 20×24. for M[r]
Gs. Whitney — Home at 5.30
went at 8 went to the [Melands?]
in 25[th?] — Private Theatricals
"A Session in Love" — very cleverly
acted — Miss Rosalie Goodsma[n]
there with many others — I
spent a very pleasant eve[ning]
& did not get to bed until
near 2 o'clk A.M. —

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 29 1869.

As usual — Miserable day —
colder & not wet — Up as
usual with much wonder
that I have no headache —
got to Studio soon after 9
and after some cleaning
to commenced on my
Water Color drawing — W[ork]
unable sake. about 18×20 —
Repeated washes in sky
until 1 o'clk when I went
home to lunch — back at
2.10 & all the Rest of P.M.
on sky — at 5.30 went to
Snedicors — at 6.30 home —
at 8 went to Burroughs
& spent rest of evening
writing out acct. cash
between Will & self —
got to bed at near 11
