## Left Page

### April Monday, 12 1869.

Charming mrg. got to studio at 8 oclk. Meuger's carman there just before me, got off drawings for Phila. \+ about 9 oclk. Rec'd letter from Dr. Lewis anxious to have him, meuger hurried up \- Rode down town to M's \+ found that the delay was not his fault \- Shipped 20 drawings on Sat. \+ still had 25 \- went to Sudsi[illegible] bought a gold pen \- back to studio 12 oclk \- went to N.A./D \- my most fears concerning my picture Riddi[illegible] \- it looks horrible \- went home to lun[illegible] sick \+ disheartened \- Back to studio 3 oclk \- Louis Tiffany asked me to ride horseback with him \- Did so. In no mood to enjoy it \- By the sudden shy[illegible] of my horse lost my seat \+ had to jump to save myself, which I did all right, but have been less mortified if I had stayed on the horse. Home at 5.30 \- Took a warm bath \- In a little fever \- Rolled up in blankets in my chair taking aconite \+ bryonia. Fever broke during the night, but had no Rest \-

## Right Page

### April Tuesday, 13 1869.

Not able to get up \- Still have fever \+ unmistakable evidences of biliary derangement with sore throat \- got up about again rolled in blankets in my big chair. got to bed about 11 oclk \+ had another night of broken Rest \-

N.A. of D. Private views in the evg. One went \- Mr. Whitney, Phila. among other friends there \-

Charl \+ Katy, the waitress, both in bed with derangements like mine, the Result of taking cold while watching a drunken woman that was raising a great row at Dr. Manning, a neighbor opposite, last Sat. night about midnight \-
