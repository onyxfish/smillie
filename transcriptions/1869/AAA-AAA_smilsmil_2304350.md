## Left Page

### August, Monday, 2, 1869

After 6 o'clk this m'f my trouble seems again to be checked — all day pretty quietly on my bed. George packing \+ getting ready for his start for the Adirondacs this P.M. — Charl saw him off at the boat at 6.20. Everg pleasantly cool — got to bed about 11 — Up once during the night at 3 o'clk A.M.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday, 3, 1869

Very warm day, but I kept so quiet that I did not suffer from it — Feel very weak but have no Recurring of my trouble — Spent the day between lying on my back \+ grinding up some colors for my water-color box and eating a new sort of wheat gruel — Imperial Granum — Irish moss and Beef Tea — I hope that at last I am getting along — Everg lying about feeling quite tired — Got to bed about 10.30
