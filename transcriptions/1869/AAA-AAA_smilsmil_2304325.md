## Left Page

### June Sunday, 13 1869

Fighting all day with a headache — Hazy, filmy day, warm with a strong South wind blowing — Spent most of the mf on the big Rock, lying under the shade of Cedar enjoying the wind as it blew over me — Afternoon on Mt. Airy until after 3 when I gave up \+ went to bed \+ slept until near 6 — Up \+ took Tea — Every walked about until 9.30 To bed at 10 — Rain during the night —

## Right Page

### June Monday, 14 1869

Delicious mf — warm, but breezy \+ very fragrant — After 8lps — a boy from Jno. Roosevelt brought me a letter from Jno — Spent the mf working with graver (9½ days) on Jno's plate Am very much interested in it — at noon wrote to Jno — Afternoon about 4.30. a smart shower. worked until 6 then tea \+ shortly after Mr. Ed. Parker \+ his sister called — bringing a letter from Charl. About 8 they left \+ I went to Jno. Roosevelt's — all out — They walked up to Mr. Beach's — all in — a pleasant call until 9.45 — then walked home \+ to bed at 10.30
