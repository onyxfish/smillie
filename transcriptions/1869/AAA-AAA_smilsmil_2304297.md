## Left Page

### April Friday, 16 1869.

In bed all day with an
atrocious headache —
Got up about 6.30 P.M.
Rolled myself into my
big Chair & lay there until
about 1 o'clk. a.m. — Then
went to bed & got to sleep
about 3 o'clk. a.m. —

## Right Page

### April Saturday, 17 1869.

Wet morning but delightful
afternoon. Spent the day
about the house, at times
napping, for I am very weak
tired & at times writing out my
autobiography for Redpath —
at 4 o'clk P.M. went to the
N.A. of D. — met Will Kimball —
there until 6 o'clk — Home
at 6.30 — Evg. about 8.30
went to St. Cloud Hotel & called
upon Mr & Mrs. T.A. Richards — Home
at 9.15 & to bed about 10

C.A.A. Diiring's little girl
died this mg about 9 o'clk.
by exhaustion after a very
severe attack of Scarlet fever.
It is a very, very sore trial to
them & they take it exceedingly
hard —

Rec'd a letter from Mr. Lewis in
which he tells one that he will take
my large Adirondack drawing at $300.
