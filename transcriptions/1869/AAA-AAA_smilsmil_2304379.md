## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 29 1869.

My looking so like Rain that I waited until near 10 o'clk before I started out — then went to Ausable gorge near Phin. B's \+ worked until 11.30 on a slight crayon drawing, looking up the gorge. Then went at my W.C. study \+ worked until near 3 o'clk when I cooked my lunch. The day tolerably clear, quite mild — made another crayon sketch \+ at 5.30 started for home — Find that Gel has made up his mind to go home with the Colmans \+ he is to meet them on the boat at West Port as they go down the lake — I Read my newspapers until near 10 o'clk — when I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 30 1869.

Charming day, clear \+ very warm — The Colmans \+ Miss Elliott get off for Ausable Forks \+ Plattsburgh about 7.30 — I went up to the Phin Bedee Ravine, getting to work about 9 o'clk on an oil sketch — found it damp \+ chilly in the shadows, but pitched into my work until about 1.30 or 2 o'clk, when the light gave me a little rest \+ I had my lunch and a bit again until 4.30 when the sun having set, I gave up, having finished my best sketch for the season, I think — home a little before 6 — Gel still complaining — Suppose he packed his trunk \+ got to bed about 10 o'clk.
