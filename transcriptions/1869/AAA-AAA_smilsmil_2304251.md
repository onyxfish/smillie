## Left Page

### January Saturday, 16 1869.

only tolerably clear — got to
studio at 9 — set to work on
the Rocks of foreground and
think that I improved them
very much — much hindered
with many callers — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
Mr. Beach — daughter & Miss
Wheeler & her bro called while
I was away — worked closely
all the P.M. — but could not get
my foreground finished — The
Misses Slade sat by & saw me
work for an hour or two. Mr. A. B.
Marks & Miss Fuller — also Hegeman
called — at 5. stretched muslin
& backed over panel stretcher for
my drawing & used passe partout
& Ausable Lake study for ex. for
Home to tea at 7.30 — at 8 — at
N.A. of D. — to do anything necessary
but Burling had arranged
things — Home & to bed at
10.30 —

## Right Page

### January Sunday, 17 1869.

Dull day — with wet snow
afternoon & every — got
up late, feeling rested
after a good sleep — went
to hear Dr. Hastings — He
gave us an excellent
Sermon from the double
text Isaiah 2d Ch. 5 v. and
John. 8th Ch. 12 v. — my big
drawing however, destroyed
its unity very much — at
2.45 went down to studio —
saw to fire — picked up
things a little — made a couple
of notes & walked home through
the snow at 5 o'clk — Evng —
did not go out — but wrote
replies to invitations from
Jimmie Tweedy & Mr. Morse
to visit Boston — got
to bed about 10 —
