## Left Page

### June Friday, 11 1869.

Charming mg. Uncle Willie and before bkfst. with him \+ Father wandered about until 11 o'clk. Then set to work on my etching (7½ days) About noon a smart shower. Dinner about 4 — got my etching finished about 5 o'clk and plate varnished for biting by 6 o'clk when I went out and walked on the Road until near 7 when Uncle Willie came in from town. Another heavy shower with hail \+ wind passed over about 5 o'clk P.M. After tea with Uncle W. Father \+ Kate walked about until 8 o'clk finished my walk with Uncle W. at 9 — coming in \+ getting to bed at 10.15

## Right Page

### June Saturday, 12 1869.

Glorious day — after bkfst. for some time with Father \+ Uncle W. enjoyed cutting down some trees near the house and clearing out some places where the growth was too heavy. Sosby arr'd from town with info. from Mr. Walker's photos. views of the house. Satisfactory. About 9 got to work biting in my etching (8½ days) and steadily at it all day — everything going beautifully — about 5 wandered off over the grounds for half an hour or more \+ filled up with strawberries. About 6, acid in for the last time — tea at 6.15 — at 7 cleaned off my plate — all seems to be lovely — Father much pleased. Down to the River — sky serene \+ rich in color — moon young and water like glass — back to the house about 9 and to bed ab[illegible]
