## Left Page

### October, Monday 25, 1869

Up at 7 — delightful mild out enjoying the air until bkfst. at 8 o'clk. with Chas. Uncle A \+ Miss Porter I took the 8.45 train for N.Y. got to Studio at 9.20 — worked an hour or two in drawing (Pacific R.R. Bond) \+ then at Small Canvases, charcoaling subjects — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 P.M. outlining with Spts. turpentine brown — worked as late as I could see — near 6 — Burke called \+ Abner Colgate — Home to Tea at 6 \+ well mounting drawings and writing — George out to-day \+ much better — got to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 26, 1869

Another beautiful day — got to Studio with Father at 8.15 — went to see my Studios — James Hart \+ Louis Tiffany also called — Louis insists upon my going up to Irvington with him this afternoon — Spent the mfg charcoaling \+ outlining "Lake Placid" on a canvas 12×22 in — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.45 — outlining in brown a number small Canvases — Louis came for me at 3.30 — agreed to defer my visit until to-morrow, hoping that Charl will be able to join me — Home to tea at 6 — Evg went up to Divons — Maud \+ Elmira — Helen \+ Allie at home — Home at 10.45 and to bed at once —
