## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 13, 1869

Not well — up last night — overcast day, but hot —
again at work on aus able (16th day) until afternoon trying
to make use of my filling in and ground — then gave it up —
bit a part of what I had done + cleared out the plate — then
set to work on foreground with graver — when father
came home he brought up Geo's plate all Ruled —
I varnished it — worked until after 7 — Geo. went up to
Colgates — Riverdale — to stay over night — some rain
about 6 — Every tired and did but little — figuring columns
+ accts — got to bed at 10 [illegible — ~3 words] and had some [illegible] new [illegible] and [illegible] and [illegible — ~3 words] and [illegible — ~3 words] — [illegible — ~2 words] and [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 14, 1869

Overcast day with occasional spatters of rain enough to wet the sidewalks — at work shortly after 8 biting in Ruling that goes over the entire surface of George's plate (15th day)
much troubled by evidences of a broad shallow line — at 2 o'clk, cleared off
the ground and found shall that I had raved. I am anxious to know what it will print like — all the p.m.
until after 7 o'clk, burnishing over the entire surface — Very tired — after tea walked up to
Central Park + then wandered into Thomas's Central Park Garden
to listen to the music, hoping that it might rest me + give my
mind a change — At 11 o'clk walked home through a slight drizzle and right to bed —
