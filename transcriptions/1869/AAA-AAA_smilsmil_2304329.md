## Left Page

### June, Monday, 21, 1869

Cloudy with showers \+ very warm. Mr\. after 6tfs \- wrote a letter to Fan M. \+ got to my etching about 10 o'clk (4½ day) worked without spirit \+ badly \- Charl went in town, got horse \+ ray came back \+ took Kate Riding then in to town \+ Returned by Hyde Park stage at 3 o'clk P.M. At 4.45 I gave up work and until 6 o'clk Charl King down some Evergreens west of the house \- Rain commencing bet 5 \+ 6 \- Everg pacing the piazza \+ enjoying the Rain \- to bed ab 10 \-

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday, 22, 1869

Rainy disagreeable day. had hoped to go to Vassar College \- Class Day but gave it up and tried to work on my etching (½ day) but succeeded no better than yesterday \- Now I wish to get home to see if I can find inspiration at my own desk \- Charl left us in the 10 o'clk stage for Po'keepsie to go down in the noon boat \- I gave up attempting to work at 4.45 \+ set to work chopping trees, small things that are evidently out of place \- after tea a bit again \- wrote a note to Kate Fessenden and got to bed shortly after 10 \-
