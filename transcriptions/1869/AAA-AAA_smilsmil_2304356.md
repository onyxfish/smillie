## Left Page

### August Saturday, 14 1869

5 o'clk a.m. found myself rush
\+ ride awake — we were still
some miles below Albany \+ moving
very slowly — at 6.35 me Canada
\+ Rushed for the Delevan House
there at 6.45 — bothered with a slow
darkie \+ with half my O&Pt. in my
mouth Rushed for the train at 7 —
at 7.20 we got off \+ after a comfortable
ride, having half a dozen changes, got
to Whitehall at 11.5 \+ off on Str Boat
"Canada" at 11.30 — My unpregonable
trunk has had its lock hasp busted —
I saw it have a tumble of about
five feet — weather on the lake
delightful — got to Westport at
3.40 \+ by stage, after an extremely
nasty dusty ride, to E. Town at 6 —
after considerable trouble, got a Room
in the Valley House — Found D. Seuris,
or west wood. Hart, Clark \+ Neilson
all like old times — We't went to
the Mansion House where I found
lots of girls \+ two or three fellows —
They were having a romping time —
Charades, etc — I was very tired
\+ with Clark, beat a Retreat at 10.15 —
Got to bed before 11 —

## Right Page

### August Sunday, 15 1869

Valley House — Elizabethtown

at 7 a.m. pouring Rain — Before
9 o'clk it lifted — at 10.30 went to
church and heard a plain \+
simple sermon from the Regular
Peaston — after service, with Mrs.
Hy A. Marks \+ Miss Corlies —
lunch at 1 — about 3 o'clk — as it
seemed to have cleared for the
day — Clark \+ I went to Partridges, about a mile down the
valley, to hire a team to take
us to Bedees' to-morrow mng. Made
agreement with a man there, name
unknown — as P. could not go with
us — Had to hurry back to avoid
a shower — Dinner at 5 o'clk —
after dinner went up to Mansion
House \+ saw Jat. Hart — and studios
then to see Dr. Seuris \+ others — another
shower \+ then at 7.30 back to
Valley House — Every went to church
\+ heard a Mr. Haskall — who came
up in the stage with us — got
to bed about 10.30 after more of
the company of the ladies
aforementioned —
