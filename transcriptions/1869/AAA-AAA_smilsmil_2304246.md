## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 6, 1869

A bright clear day at last — did not get to studio until 9.30 — at work at once on drawing — Soon in a peck of troubles — could not get a single wash to go right. my whole sky is beautifully granulated with a fine Rock texture. Miss Decry spent an hour at Studio — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 washing out \+ washing in all the afternoon — very much discouraged. Home to tea at 6 — Spent the eve folding, fixing \+ getting addresses for Reception Circulars — Nette Sutshen \+ her husband paid Many quite a long visit — Did not get to bed until 11.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 7, 1869

Charming \+ very mild day — Up at 7 Before bkft. wrote a letter to Mr. Do. Clay of Phila asking the loan of one or more of his drawings for our Water Color Exh.tn. Got to Studio at 9 — Yesterday nt when I got to Studio I washed out what I had done the day before — today I did the same and in addition sand papered the sky all smooth — then in desperation worked very crude \+ broadly over all the landscape down to the foreground Rocks — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 and worked closely until 4.30 — Home to tea at 6 — Spent the eve until 10 o'clt fixing \+ folding Reception Circulars — Got to bed about 11.
