## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 11 1869

a fine day at last. all stirring pretty early & at 9.15 the whole party started for the "giant" — we got to our sketching grounds at 10.45. I set to work upon my W.C. study at the head of the falls — & worked until 12.30 when I joined the party above, I indeed fine & got up lunch. About 2 o'clk. started for my study away up the brook looking over to "gothic" — as it was very warm, I went slowly & did not get started at my work until about 3 — then I worked as tight as I could drive until 5.15, accomplishing very little in a very unsatisfactory manner. The color & light over the mts was wonderful — joined the party below at 5.45 & all started down. getting home about 7.15 — when I found that I had lost my camp stool on the way — Evening, Reading. Rec'd letters from Father & Katie. got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 12 1869

another glorious day — up very late — "Mcfo" — at 9 o'clk — About 10.30 — Coleman & I started off — to find my camp stool — he, for a tramp. I found my stool near Smith Bedee's & then went with him away along the old Schroon road to near Chapel Pond — then we crossed the brook & climbed to the tops of a cliff at the foot of Giant — the outlook was superb — we then went through the woods crossing Par[rott's?] Brook & coming out on the stream back of Smith Bedee's getting home to dinner at 5 — Well writing to Father & getting ready for Lake Placid trip to-morrow.
