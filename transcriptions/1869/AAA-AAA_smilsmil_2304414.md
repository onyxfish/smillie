## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 14 1869.

Pleasant, but with a raw threatening feeling in the air. Got to studio at 9.30 & worked on Twilight. 33×44m (5th day). Painting in distant rocks and tops of trees coming up against the sky. At 12 went downtown to Cont. Bk. note Co. to see about a ruling they wish me to bite in — then to A.B.N. Co. & got the $150 they owe me. Then to Sackett the printer about Water Color forms. Then lunched & back to studio at 2.40. George there "visiting" — could not see to paint after 4.15. Home at 5.30. Eve. making sketch to send to Mr. Frank Scott. — To bed about 10.45

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 15 1869.

Overcast, dark and raw. Got to studio at 9 o'clk & worked on Twilight (6th day); painted in about half of the trees on in the l.h. The want of light prevented me covering as much space as I intended. George came to studio about 12 o'clk and stayed for an hour & a half, not feeling strong enough to take hold of any work. Robbins called at home. Home to lunch at 1 o'clk, stopping to see Reurling for a few minutes. Back at 2.20 — worked until 3.45 could see no longer. Home at 6 o'clk. Eve. made another sketch for Frank Scott, wrote him a letter & one to Fan Mulford — To bed about 10.30
