## Left Page

### June, Friday, 25, 1869

Very warm day with little inclination to work — got to studio with my traps about 10 o'clk — Ther. 86°. On my way or another fooled away the morning — left at 12 — home at 12.40 — lunched + read + at 3.15 back to studio. Did a little on George's plate in the way of cleaning out with scratch brush and papering. Wm Rayner called to see if me had any pictures that he would fancy (Real estate man) at 5.30 went to Rehm's + had my head shampooed — Home to tea at 47 — after tea finished reading "the Amber Witch" and at 8.45 went up to the Diven's, bill in and well — Mrs May — home on a visit from Georgia. Home at 10.30 and to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### June, Saturday, 26, 1869

Murky + muggy — mind S.E. — weather more sticky + dis-agreeable than yesterday, altho' 5° cooler in the studio. Got to work on my etching about 10 o'clk (6th day) but did little during the m[orning]. The new frame for my Water Color White mts. came up from Snedicor's this m[orning] — I think it improves the drawing very much. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15. Worked better during the p.m. Hope that once more I am on the right track — about 5 o'clk. Charley Adams came in + soon after Meta, his wife — they visited us pleasantly until 9.30 — I did not get home to tea until after 7 — amused myself in thy the microscopes until 10 — then allowed myself to sleep in my chair until near midnight, when I went to bed. It rained during the early part of the evening.
