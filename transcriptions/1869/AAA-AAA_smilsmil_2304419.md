## Left Page

### December Friday, 24 1869.

Bright mild day \- Before going code down to Brooks Bros \+ changed clothes that I got last night \- the color by daylight \- dissatisfied me. Got to studio at 10.15 \- Then set to work blocking out subject for Water Color drawing. \- I have selected as a subject "Upper Ausable Lake, from the Slide" very nearly what I painted \+ sold for Pate, but treated very differently with the aid of studies made last summer. Got at studio, drawing with Charcoal, until near 1 o'clk. \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.40. By dusk got outline of my drawing finished \- Home to tea at 6 o'clk. Smyf., after going to Beurgraff went to the Treadys \- Charln. me there \- we left at 10.45 and walked up home \- to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 25 1869.

Mild overcast day with some Rain in the evenf. Got to Studio at 9. 5. \- Prepared wall \+ otherwise got ready for plate from Continental \- Skinner brought it about 10.30 \- at once finished stopping out \+ put mall on it. Did not get the acid on until after 12 o'clk \- J. Jay Griffin called going to sell some pictures for me. \- Skinner \+ I lunched in studio at 1 \- Shortly after, by spilling some water on the stove, Scalded my left hand so that it was extremely painful during the Rest of the day \- Got picture finished \+ plate Cleared off by 1.30 \- Biting seems to be all right Skinner's assistance was invaluable.
