## Left Page

### April, Friday, 2, 1869

overcast + threatening, but did not Rain until 5 oclk
P.M. Wet. Rained heavily —
got to studio at 9.30 a.m.
feeling very unlike work —
Debating whether or no I shd.
take a holiday — Miss Deury
came + drew for an hour
and I commenced charcoal
drawing of subject that I am
to eng. for Mr. Pate — The [illegible]
Ausable lake from a slide [illegible]
the mt. At 1.30 home to lunch
\+ at 2.15 Rode down to [illegible]
Rehearsal — back to stud[io]
at 5 + worked on charcoal
drawing until 6.15. Home
to tea at 6.45 — [illegible] dawdl[ed]
about getting to bed at 10 o[clk]

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 3, 1869

Pleas[ant] day — got to studio at
[9].45 prepared for dirty work and
had plenty of it — cleared out
swept out studio — hard at [it]
until 11 oclk — then went home,
bathed, dressed + felt like
new — lunched + at 2 oclk
got back to studio — until
after 5 oclk had a constant
stream of friends + visitors,
making it a very pleasant
afternoon for me — Miss Belle
Treedy brought her friend, Mrs.
[K]ellogg, who bought a little
water-color sketch from her
$20.00 — Home to tea at 6.30
— [illegible] at 8 went down + called
on the Tweetys's — Stayed until
[10].45 enjoying a chatty every
[n]ight — Home + to bed at 11.45 —
