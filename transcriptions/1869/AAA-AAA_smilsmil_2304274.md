## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 3, 1869

A pleasant day again — got to Studio at 8.30 — at work (6° day) on 24×40 — painting in foliage in middle distance \+ on L.h. side — worked under much discouragement — I do not see how I am to get this painting finished with any degree of care or completeness by the end of March — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30 — worked until 4 — then went over to the N.A. \+ Dr. Munns Emma Ireland — a pleasant chat with her until 6 — then home with her — back to Studio — Home at 6.45 — Eve'g with Charl. went to the Academy of Music and heard Kellogg \+ Marquirille in Faust — Her singing pleased me, as did some of her acting — but as usual, the whole was to me, a dismal farce — That is my idea of Italian opera — Home \+ to bed at 7/[illegible]

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 4, 1869

Miserable sloppy Rain \+ snow until every when it changed into a wicked gale from the west — got to the Studio at 9, brushed up a little \+ to work on 24×40, glazing with Mars Yellow the foliage on R. h. side \+ painting it over — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30 very dark p.m. — unable to do much painting — Cleaned up at about 5.30 — then took a nap on the counge, getting home to a little before 7 — Shortly after 8 — Dr. Covell \+ his daughter came as per agreement and invitation \+ spent the evening until after 11 o'clk — I got to bed about midnight —
