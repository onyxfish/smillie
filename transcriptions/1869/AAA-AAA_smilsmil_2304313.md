## Left Page

### May Tuesday, 18 1869

Fresh pleasant day \- but fighting with a headache. After 6 Klash worked over A.B.N. Co's drawing of Sheep under Trees \+ touched up several die proofs for Father. Got to Studio about 9.45 but was unable to do anything. Home to lunch ab't \- Slept in my chair until 3 \- at 4 Rode down to Fred. Durand's made appointment with him then on to A.B.N.Co\- Saw Gavit \- went to Prentis' and bought a pair of eye glasses. Called on Meuger to tell him that his drawing was done. Rode home with Father. Evt writing to Kate Drinker and to delinquent members of the Water Color Society \- Got to bed about 10

## Right Page

### May Wednesday, 19 1869

Dull mt. Got to Studio at 9 \- before 10 it commenced Raining \+ kept it up steadily all day. Felt headachy \+ indisposed \- work but forced myself to take hold of Mr. Pati's "Upper usable Lake" 7×9 in (3d day) Home to lunch ab't \- back at 2.5 \+ worked until 6, getting over all but the foreground. Got \- Spent the mt down town attending to a Jury Summons. Dr. Kingsley gave us a call in the p.m. \- Home to Tea at 6.30. Tuney V.V. Meather. mid at our house all night.
