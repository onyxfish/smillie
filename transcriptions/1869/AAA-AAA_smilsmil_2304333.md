## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 29, 1869

My cool, but the day at last, quite as hot as yesterday. Got to Studio at 9.30 \+ continued etching foreground trees (8th day) Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30, and worked until 7. Then home to tea — Evening Mr \+ Mrs. Jessups called. I excused myself at 8.30 \+ went down to the Treedays. Many, most of the eveg engaged with an Uncle \+ Cousin — so visited with Belle — did not get away until 11 oc. Home at 11.30 \+ to bed about 12.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 30, 1869

Quite heavy Rain in the mg leaving very hot \+ muggy about day — Relieved by considerable stirring from S. \+ W. Got to studio at 9.20 \+ at work on foreground of etching (9th day) Home to lunch at 1 — Everything upside down with Matts arrangements to start on her overland trip to California with Uncle a. \+ Tuney. Back at 2.45 \+ etched until 5. Then as it sprinkled, went home \+ got umbrella \+ down to the Drews at 5.40. Father, Mary, Clark \+ I at the boat to see them off, which was done very nicely at 6.10 — Sky pleasant — air cool \+ signs propitious — a safe \+ pleasant journey to them — Home to tea at 7 — Until 8.30 a family talk touching plans for the future (times) — from 9 to 10, tinkering on my new trunk — at 10.30 — to bed —
