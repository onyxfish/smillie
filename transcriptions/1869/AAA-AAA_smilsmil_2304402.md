## Left Page

### November Thursday, 18, 1869

Did not wake until near 9 o'clk — then up, bkfst \+ to studio at 10 — Portght. Clear day, after a little fuffing got at Whiteface from Placid 8×14 (3d day) Second painting. Ned Dunning called with a friend — I went home to dine at 1 — had my Thanksgiving dinner \+ back at 2.30. I got in my stay \+ mountainvy unsatisfactorily, as [illegible] I am now seriously questng whether I can ever be even a 2d. or 3d. Rate painter — Mr. Hegeman called \+ spent about an hour with me — Home to Tea at 6 — Dt \+ Mary with us — Mr. Benj Jessup called I washed up brushes — took a little look at French verb \+ got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### November Friday, 19, 1869

Bright mng but overcast day. Got to studio at 9 o'clk \+ set to work again on Whiteface \+ Placid, painting in water \+ trees. Discouraged beyond everything — I do not believe that I can ever be worth anything; It seems as though I knew so well what is wanted \+ yet am so powerless to do anything like it — Home to lunch at 1 — at 2 went to Phil. Rehearsal — Academy crowded — Belle Tweedy gave me a seat by her — after Rehearsal went home with them, thro to Snedecor's — then to studio, then home — about 7 — every lying about \+ to bed at 10.
