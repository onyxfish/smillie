## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 10, 1869

Charming day, but very warm. Spent the m't biting [illegible] line on sky — Ausable (25 day) after lunch went down to A.B.N. Co's at about 4 o'clk had ground laid and at 4 45 commenced Ruling a perpendicular line over mountains \+ water — got through with it at 6 50 — very much tired when I got home at 8 o'clk — well grounded some colors \+ finished the arrangement of my W.C. on [illegible] of Po'keepsie Spent the ev'g with Kate.
Walter Dr. J. Smillie arr'd from Ottawa this morning in time for [illegible].
got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 11, 1869

Clear \+ very warm — until noon, biting Ruling on Ausable plate (26 day) then, after lunch started for A.B.N. Co's got there at 2 — got line filled with wax ground laid \+ commenced Ruling at 3 — finished at 6 — Ruled diagonal line through all of the middle distance — Well touched die [illegible] for farther — varnished plate \+ partially lined trunk — got to bed about 10.30
