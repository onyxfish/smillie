## Left Page

### March Monday, 29 1869

Very heavy easterly storm of wind & rain — got to studio at
8.45. Set to work on 24×40.
(24th day) & painted during the
m'f. over the water — getting
it much nearer Right, but not
at all as I want it — Miss Dewy
came & drew for an hour —
Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.15, worked until
after 5 on foliage of big tree.
Conant of the Times called.
Louis Jeffamy came in too —
He has just returned from
a two months trip — Cuba, N.O.
Memphis, Chicago etc — Home
to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g spent
trying to work up sketches
for Bank Note drawings —
Got to bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### March Tuesday, 30 1869

Rain nearly all day — got
to studio at 8.15 and worked
my last & 25th day on 24×40.
Spent the whole day painting
over the foliage against the
sky & some little bits here
& there of finishing. The whole
thing is now harmonious, after
a fashion — perhaps monotonous
— would be freer — I hardly know
what to think of it — at times it
appears suffused with Zinc
yellow — then again Mars Y —
and then Burnt Sienna tinges
all — at other times, Cobalt blue
seems to carry the day — Home
to lunch at 1.15 back at 2.15 —
Home to tea at 6.45. Eve'g
washing brushes & cogitating
over my disappointment at
the way I have finished my
picture — took a general wash
about 9 o'clk & to bed about
9.30
