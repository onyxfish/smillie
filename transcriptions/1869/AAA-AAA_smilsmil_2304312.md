## Left Page

### May, Sunday, 16, 1869.

Bright m'g, but indications of a change when I went to church. D. Tho. Hastings preached from a compound text. Rev. 13 ch. 9 \+ John 6 ch. 6§ v. dwelling upon the vitality of Christ's teaching \- On coming out of Church, found raining, as though in for a day of it \- storm from the East \- but cleared up between 2 \+ 3 o'clk \- about 5 a fine thunderstorm came up from the west, giving us about an hour of Rain with now and then a little hail \+ clearing off to a glorious clear \+ cool even'g \- Went to J.P. Thompson \+ heard him preach from 1st Timothy. 6th ch. 6th v. "But godliness with Contentment is great gain" \- After Service went to see [illegible] many had gone to bed \- home before 10 \+ to bed by 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Monday, 17, 1869.

Cool \+ fresh, delightful day. Got to studio at 8 o'clk \- Took my water-color sketch of Apple blossoms and giving it a coating of Siccatifde Har\- lem set to work to finish it in oil. Worked steadily all day, going home to lunch ab't \- back at 2.30 \- sticking to it until 7 o'clk P.M. \- finishing it after a fashion and having an agreeable question in my mind as to whether what I have accomplished is worth all the trouble \+ time I have taken \- Home to tea at 7.30 \- even'g at home; washing brushes, making over die [illegible] etc \- Got to bed about 10.
