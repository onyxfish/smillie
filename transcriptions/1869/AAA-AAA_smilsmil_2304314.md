## Left Page

### May Thursday, 20 1869

Glorious day \- got to studio at 8.30 but feeling out of sorts, not able to get fairly at my work until near 10 \- upon "Upper Ausable Lake (4th day) painted over the foreground \- Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2.15 Mr. Ge. Whitney had been at at the studio while I was away \- By 5 o'clk got over the second painting \- In some Respects, I do not like it as well as my Charcoal drawing. At 5.45 went to the N.A. f D \- there until 6.30 then went over to the Mts Mens Ch'y als'r Rl'y \+ climbed all over it \+ home to tea at 7.30 Eve'g Reading some papers from "English Essayists" \- Ollb. Sreedy spent the eve'g with us \- Got to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### May Friday, 21 1869

Bright m'f \- got to studio at 8.35 \+ after working over study of Apple Blossoms, set to work on Kingsley's "Lower Ausable Lake" (Fridays.) I worked over it all glazing \+ scumbling \- touching sharper touches \+ making tones purer \+ more delicate \- Miss Deur came \+ drew in the m'f, Leuch the Studio at 1 went to Fred Durand's by appointment \+ had something done to me of my back teeth \- back to studio at 2.30 \- a quite Rain just commencing \- kept up the Rest of the p.m. briskly. worked until 8 o'clk \- home at 9 \- Eve'g Reading \+ to bed at 10.30
