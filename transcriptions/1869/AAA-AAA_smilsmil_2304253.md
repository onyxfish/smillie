## Left Page

### January Wednesday, 20 1869.

Patchy day — Sunshine &snow squalls — Father walked with me
to the Studio, got there at 9.15 —
got drawing into frame. Mulvany
Carried over to the Academy for
me shortly after 10 o'clk — All
the m[illegible] there at work — Home
to lunch at 12.30. Stopping to
see Farrer on my way up — Saw
Gifford — back at 1.30 — meeting
Farrer in the street — Worked
at the Academy as long as I
could see, getting things at last
all up — Home to tea at 6 —
wrote letters to Darley & [illegible]
— back to N.A. & D. at 8 to give
a final look at things & see
to the Cataloguing — home
at 10.45 — did a little writing
& got to bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### January Thursday, 21 1869.

Clear, mild & sloppy — Got to
the Studio about 9 — about 9.30
Jel. B. Wood Junr — Mr. Noch Davis
& Mr. Justice of Phila — Came in —
left them & went over to the N.A.
& D. with Colman — There for
about an hour — Back to Studio
Blocked out charcoal design
for Dabt. Gairt, for a medal
for Some Poultry Assn — Home to
lunch at 1 — back at 2 and
as long as I could see, at work,
making a more careful sketch
of my work of the mill. During
the P.m. a Mr. Ingersoll & Woodruff
came in — Ingersoll buying for
Supervisor Tweed. He bought
my 3 foot picture "On the Saco — at
Mt Conway" unfinished, for $300 so
a piece of real good fortune for
me — Evng went to private
viewing of Water Color & [illegible] then
until near 11 — home & to bed
about midnight —
