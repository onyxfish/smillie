## Left Page

### October Friday, 29 1869.

another overcast mt\. Rain beginning about 2 o'clk and continuing until about 5 \- got to Studio at 9.30 after calling at Bogardus's Art Gallery \- Painted in\. p\] ×12\. "Afternoon \- Bennetts Pond \- Adirondacks" \- Home to lunch at 1.15 \- back at 3 \- after calling on Kuntze \& C. P. Cranch \- got my Sketch very unsatisfactory "in" owing to short hours \+ want of light \- at 5.15 went up to New Synagogue of E. Manuel \+ heard [illegible] Service \- Home at 6.15 \- Eve\[ning\] spent Reading Darby's Homer's Iliad until 9.30 \- got to bed at 10.15

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 30 1869.

Bright mt\. but soon overcast \- dirty, cold \+ blustering day \- At Studio at 9 o'clk \- at 9.30 went down town with drawing to Jay Cooke's \- Saw him \+ Mr. Fahnestock \- they expressed themselves perfectly satisfied with my drawing \- then to [illegible] N Co \- Saw Mr. Hill \& Bay Mills \- agreed to make a drawing for him \- then Rode up to Sauthier's to Schaus's \- Snedecor \+ to see Doré's lectures at Somerville\. Then to Studio \+ home to lunch at 1.45 \- back at 2.45 \+ spent the p.m. finishing large pencil drawing "Gothic Mts. Adirondacks" \+ charcoaling \- Mr. \+ Mrs. Brown \+ miss Brown made me a short call \- home to tea at 6 \- at 7.30 went \+ Registered \+ then to Brevoort's where I spent the evng until 9.50 then home to bed at 11.15
