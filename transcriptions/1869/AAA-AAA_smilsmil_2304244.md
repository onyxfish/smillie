## Left Page

### January, Saturday 2, 1869

The disagreeable weather still continues — Feel much better this onlf after my good nights Rest. In the morning Father went to the Studio for me \+ got my drawing materials for me \- I spent the day at home, making alterations on Brs. note drawing "Gaucho \+ guitar" \- To-day was the 2d. Reception day of the season at our Building. In spite of the weather quite a number were out \- Of course I regretted that I could not be on hand \- Evef sitting around until 10 o clc then to-bed \-

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 3, 1869

Still a very disagreeable sort of weather \- feel better than I did yesterday \- but did not go out of doors \- Spent most of the time pacing up \+ down the parlors \- wrote a letter to Fan Mulford \- now in Boston and one to Hy Coops, now in Paris \- Got to bed about 11 \-

Dr Thos Hastings, gave to-day as his motto for the year \- Be patient in well doing.
