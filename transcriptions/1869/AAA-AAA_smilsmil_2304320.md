## Left Page

### June, Thursday 3, 1869

Cloudless & very warm — got to the studio, at mting & set to work on my etching (4½ days) & kept at it until 1.30 — then home to lunch — at 2 started for down town at 3 took train for Orange — at 4 — got there & was met by Eden Sewellym Haskell — He drove with me over the Park — to Eagle Rock & back of the Orange Mts — at 6.30 was left at Mr. Chas. Martin in the park — pressed to stay to dinner & accepted — passed a very pleasant evelf & missed the 8.50 train — had to wait — was taken to the 10.45 train — got into NY. at 11.45 & up home at 12.20 & to bed right away.
Chas. Burt's Silver Wedding this evelf — father there —

## Right Page

### June, Friday 4, 1869

Close, chilly, hot, cloudy very disagreeable day — got to studio at 9 — fighting with a headache. Set to work on etching (5th day) T.A. Richards brought to us our N.A. of D. Diplomas as Associates — worked until 1.15 then home to lunch — note to Fraser, Toronto, acknowledging Receipt of Water Color dues — at 3 back to studio — very much under the weather — at 4 started out for a number of messages — went to Anthony's — Smithier's — Heim, a tin man to make a water color box, but he would n't — Kelly & then back to studio — worked there fixing up things to leave until 7 o'clk. — then home to tea — worked all the evelf hard, mounting Mengius drawing of West Point & Applt Blossom study — got to bed about 10.30
