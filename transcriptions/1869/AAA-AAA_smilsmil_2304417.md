## Left Page

### December Monday, 20 1869

Overcast + dark — got to studio at 9.15 — Kindled fire + got to work upon Twilight 8" Day, + painted in foreground rocks — I had hoped to finish the first painting in, but could not do it. Considerable foliage + finishing about it vnd me — I am yet sanguine that it will turn out my best work — about 12. Mr. Sterling + daughter called — then young Nicoll — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.15 + worked until near 4.30. Home to tea at 6 — Evening went up to Divans — French night — Miss Josie [illegible] there — did not leave until 10.45 — Home — Read + to bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 21 1869

Pleasant day — got to studio at 9 o'clk + to work finishing up some bits of foliage etc in Twilight (8½ days) which I got done with by 1 o'clk — Mr. Colman called — home to lunch at 1.25 back at 2.20 — first plate from Continental came up. Spent time getting things ready to go up — at Studio Robertson called to see about it — Mr. + Mrs. Robt Dodge + a young lady called — Home at 5.30 — Heard of Aunt Polly V. V.'s death. With Matt, Kate + Charle, went to Church cor 4th Ave + 21st St. Saw Ida Ogilvie + Jot [illegible] Ingersoll married — great challenge — Home at 8.15 — after much tribulation dressed in full eve'g dress, feeling very like a monkey — went to studio snowing — then to 273 Fifth Ave. to to Ogilvie Reception — a wonderful display of flowers + a very vulgar display of Bridal presents, silver ware + jewelry, valued at over $100,000. Left at 11 o'clk — Home + to bed at 12.
