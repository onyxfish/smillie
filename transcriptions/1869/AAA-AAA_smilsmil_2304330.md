## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 23 1869.

Disagreeable, cloudy, muggy mt\- Ab\+o took pencils \+ paper and started up the Hyde Park Road to get some sketches of elms \- Stopped at EmmaReach\- with a "Regal walnut" moth that I captured yesterday \- She not at home \- In the old Clew place got a couple of outline sketches \- Home to dinner at 1.30 \- P.M. spent getting things together, packing \+ chopping more trees \- after Tea, with father \+ Kate went down to the River, \[illegible\] cleared to be a beautiful moonlight \- back to house at 8.15 \- Emma Reach stopped in her carriage and I Rode to her house \- Spent the eve\+ there until 10.30 Then walked home and to bed at 11.10

## Right Page

### June Thursday, 24 1869.

Glorious morning \- after bkfs\+ got things ready to start and for an hour \+ aunted about taking pleasant partings \- at 10 o'clk \- by Hyde Park Stage, Rode into town. Saw baggage safely placed then with Father, went to Wilson's book store \+ saw A.W. then to Jimmy Cummin's blacksmith shop \+ saw him \- Ab\+2 we dined at Smith's Restaurant. Kate dined at the Parkers' \- At 4 me were at the dock waiting ab\+40 the Dawn, Drew came along \+ we had a charming sail to the City, getting in at 6 o'clk at 6.30 home to Tea \- all well. after unpacking things, Spent the Rest of the eve\+ amusing myself with Willis' Microscope. To bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
