## Left Page

### July, Saturday, 3, 1869.

Very warm day with considerable air stirring — Did not get to Studio until 9.30, worked on etching until 10 45 — then with Augers went to [illegible] + drew my $110 — then to [Proots?] [Par?] + bought a linen coat then to the Smeedys in Waverly R. + met [Geo?] with them went to [R?] [my?] — saw them into a 4th Ave [bus?] + [Ret?] to Studio got bags + umbrellas + at 11.45 again met them at N.Y. R.R. Station. Train did not leave until 12.15 + we had some very uncomfortable waiting — The Ride to Norwalk, Pleasant — Darley, Head I.A. Richards + Mr. Childs on the train — Changed Cars from N. to Danbury where we arr. about 3 P.M. — Met Mr. Nicolls on the train — a friend of the Smeedys and on his way to Danbury — walked to Mr. [Edg?] Smeedy's girl Belle, [Mamy?] — Met at Mr. [?] by Miss Sminie J — Mr. Chas. Benydick Miss Kellogg + Mr + Mrs J. — Dinner at — Everybody jolly + all things pleasant — The house is delightfully situated, and at once I feel at home — After dinner at drive to the hills about town the air deliciously cooled by a thunder shower we had about 5 o clk — Ret from Ride about 8 o clk — Spent chatting on [piazza?] surprised to meet Lottie Crooker now Mrs. [Burnght?] + visiting next door to us — To bed at 11.30 o clk —

## Right Page

### July, Sunday, 4. 1869.

Delicious day, but rather smoky — [illegible], with Misses Kellogg + Jennie J — [illegible] Mr. Chas. Benedick + Jacob Nichols went to the Sandaminian Ch — very few, perhaps 20, there. Reading scripture + singing — Back at 12 + until 3, out under the trees, Reading + chatting — then dinner — more lounging + chatting + at about 6 me went to the cemetery + walked about until after 8 — Returning, stopped at "Aunt Ann's", a brisk old lady of [illegible] yrs. Ret'd home at about 9 o clk and found the house full of visitors, [illegible] members of the family — Singing + gossiping until after 10. When the company left — then a lesser circle until 11 when we went to bed — [illegible — entire passage]
