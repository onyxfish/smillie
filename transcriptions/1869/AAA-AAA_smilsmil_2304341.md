## Left Page

### July, Thursday 15, 1869

Up at 4 o'clk A.M. on a regular jolly, bilious bust — Cascaded until more than tired, then to bed again — not able to get up again from weakness \+ intense pain in my head — So much for getting into a fret about work and trying to do more than I am able — About 8 — got up to have bed changed — aired — Sat up in Chair until after midnight, then again to bed —

## Right Page

### July, Friday 16, 1869

Intensely hot day — Still on my back — Have promised to go with Geo. up to Saml. Colman's at Irvington, this P.M. — D. Throrop \+ all at home advise me to do so if I am able — So at 3.30 feeling very slim I Rode to the H.R. R.R. Station — 30.S. \+ took 4.15 train — met Iul. Rodgers \+ Rode up with him — his family are at Irvington this summer — Got to I — at 5 just after a very heavy thunder storm — Everything quite Cool \+ fresh — Colman at the Station waiting for us — we walked up to his house — greeted by Mr. C — old Mr. Duswing \+ the Rev. D. Canfield. Dinner at 6 — after that — the Rest of P.M. \+ Eve'g on west Piazza, and a thin storm to the south of us — then a glorious clear up \+ delightfully cool with. To bed about 10.30 after general Chatting \+ story-telling by the party —
