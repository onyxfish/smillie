## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 26, 1869

Splendid winter day — got to studio at 9 o'clk — got to work at once on "Saco" — painting over stretch of middle distance \+ River until 10'clk when I went home to lunch — Jonny Verplanck made us another call — back to Studio at 2 — Jonny Bell all the p.m. at Studio drawing — Mr Craig called to pay W.C. dues — worked as long as I could see \+ got R.h. bank \+ Reflections done — At 6 went down to Snedecors at 7.30 home to tea, tired \+ cross — Eve'g spent writing &c — got to bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 27, 1869

Another fine winter day, not so cold or clear as yesterday — got to Studio at 9 \+ set to work on the middle distant trees, C.h. of "Saco" 20×36 — Miss Devry spent part of the m't drawing — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — worked faithfully until 4 when I got my "Petit" done. Young Mr. Bruce called — I went into Vedder's Room (Walter Brown's) was much interested \+ made very opinons of him \+ his talents — He is very strong in some peculiar vein of fancy \+ exceedingly artistic in expression — At 5 down at Snedecors about Academy frame — Got his list of addresses for Studio Recpt. Circular — at 6.30 at Brainerd's in St Luke's R. — took dinner \+ spent the eve'g copying Snedicor's list into my book — at 11 walked home — got to bed about 12 —
