## Left Page

### February Sunday, 7 1869.

Headachy + did not get up until 11 o'clk — Joe is better to day — Mary Resumed her work at Sunday School —
Dinner at 1 — Spent the p.m. in my big chair — at 5 o'clk walked over to Prof Roods 15th. near 1st Ave. to explain matters concerning his check given in payment of Water dues. Saw Mrs R — walked home — glorious evey — Tea at 6 —
Evey went to Dr Thompson —
He preached from Galatians
Hc Ch. 18 v — It was a funeral sermon — the subject — Mr.
Pardee — a great S.S. man —
Home at 9.30 — Made a note to S.C. 7. + got to bed shortly after 11 —

## Right Page

### February Monday, 8 1869.

Charming day, but changeable. At Studio at 9.15 — Kindled fire when Miss Dewy came. Spent nearly an hour with her, over some of her drawings — the Rest of the m.y. Reading + thinking up illustrations for Bryants "Among the Trees" — G.P. Putnam wishes me to get up something for the Holidays — Mr. Pate called — Home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2.30 having called on J. D. a Ward at his Studio —
Spent the p.m. getting my accts straight with the Studios on Rect. business — Home at 6 — Evey writing to Alfred Jones + Dnt a. Fraser — ht — got to bed about 11 —
