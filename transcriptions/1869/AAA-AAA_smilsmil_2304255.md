## Left Page

### January, Sunday, 24, 1869.

a mild pleasant day. — up late mys went to D. Hastings + heard a Rev. Martien — home missionary — had a big map of the U.S. and a long fish pole — was very eloquent + earnest — and I think got a good collection — Home to dinner — afternoon in doors — Resting — every went to Mad. Av. Baptist ch + heard

## Right Page

### January, Monday, 25, 1869.

a clear cold day and windy — going to Schmitt's to pay him $100 on acct — I did not get to the Studio until 9.45 — Miss Dewy then came in + I spent some time with her — also Dr. Atkinson — then going Gavit + I worked some time making changes on Poultry seal So I did not get at my sky on Saco. 20×36 — until near noon. Mr. Verplanck called, with a painting that he has just finished Home to lunch at 1.15 back at 2.15 + by 4.30 had Repainted sky + mts. at 5. with Jo. walked down to Snedecor's — Home to tea at 6.30 — At 8. left a cepo tance of invitation to dinner at Mr. Thed Roosevelt's. 28 & 20 St. Then went to N.A. of D. — until near 10 o'clk copying names + addresses of Fellows N.A. of D. — for our Studio Reception list — Glorious moonlight night — Home + to Bed abt 11
