## Left Page

### October, Thursday, 7, 1869

glorious day — all the morning
at home unpacking trunk &
somewhat arranging things —
found that my two large W.C.
studies had been considerably
damaged by water — After lunch
went down to A.B.N. Co. - to see
if I could get any drawing to
do — none wanted — on way
up stopped at Schaus's,
& Nedicors — Knoedlers and the[ir]
Studio — found there a summons
for me to act as Juror. Sept. 21st. Home
to dinner at 6 — Evening went to
call on the Tweedys at No. 20
Waverly R. — Found them at home
& had a "full meeting" until
11.20 — Charl came in before
10 o'clk, from drill — & we left
for home together — got to bed
just about midnight —

## Right Page

### October, Friday, 8, 1869

Another splendid Oct. day —
I am like a caged bear in
this city — after 9 o'clk walked
up 5th ave inspecting the new
churches that are going up, back
at work about 10.30 Painted
over oil study of Gothic Mt. from
the Giant. Scumbling, glazing,
touching according to notes
that I had made when last
up there — getting it to look much
better, I think — worked as long as
I could see — At 6 went to the new
Synagogue, Emanuel El (Liberal Jew)
& heard the Vesper Service — music
all very fine — back at 6.45 — found
that Kate & miss Allie Parken had just
arrived from Pokepsie — Dinner —
[illegible], Belle, Mary, Ollie & Joe Tweedy
spent the evng with us, ostensibly
to drink lemonade made from
the lemons that have been to Keene.
They stayed until near 11.30 — Mary
& the Dr. were also here — got to
bed at 12 —
