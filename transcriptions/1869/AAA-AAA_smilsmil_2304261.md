### February Saturday, 6 1869

Got to the Studio at 8.30 — Found that
Mulvaney had got the thick of the
dirt out, so I fell to \+ gave it another
sweeping — dusting \+ clearing up — at
10.15 Geo Came along — at 11 I went
home — took a bath \+ dressed myself.
Jr is quite sick, threatened with pleum-
or pneumonia — Took lunch \+ got
to studio again at 1.15 — The day
being fine, we had an overflowing
Reception — much of the time the
rooms so crowded that it was im-
possible to see the pictures — at
5 o'clk closed our door \+ at 5.30 went
home — tea — \+ at 6.30 off for Phil
concert — The maddest struggle yet
at the doors, \+ but just managed
to get a box — much dissatisfaction
pressed all around. The feature
of the even'g, Ole Bull — he is wonderful
to me, but that is all — after the Concert
a presentation of Silver a Ring was
made to him — but few of the audience
saw it — Walked home \+ got to
