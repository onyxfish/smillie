## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 7 1869.

Pain — and I'm rather glad
it. I am out of sorts — tired
out and feeling very much
work — So during the m[orning]
I worked over the pencil
study that I laid in on
the slides at the Upper
[illegible], looking over to
the Gothics. Lunch at —
after that it cleared [up]
beautifully — giving fine
cloud studies all the
P.M.. Strong wind from
S.W. + now & then showers
of Rain — Everg. spending
general chatter — a Mr.
Smith, a friend of Corinne
Colman's — arr'd about
8 o'clk P.M. — I got to bed
about 10.45

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 8 1869.

More wind & Rain with
close, muggy & enervating
atmosphere — All are complaining [illegible] effects — I
feel very much out of sorts —
Worked on pencil study
of "Gothics" — as my traps
are all up on the giant
my scope of work is limited
— The day passed without
incident, as did the
evening — Got to bed
about 11 — Rain then
falling quietly —
