## Left Page

### June Tuesday, 1 1869

Got to studio shortly before 9. very hot day + feel the effects of my yesterdays headache. At work however, steadily on my etching (3d day) finding much comfort, as I sat at my window at work, in the cheery note of a little Pee-wee and the chatter of the sparrows — towards "The Mail" called — Home to lunch at 1 — Mary Norton (Petit) + & children just from Gorgey, arrv. this mng while we were at 9 o'clk — Did not get back to studio until near 5 — worked until 6.30 — then Rode up to Central Park — listened to the birds sing their Well — Hymn + tried to sing mine — Home at 8.15 + took tea — Mr. & Mrs. Parker at the house — Read until 10.30 — then to bed — Miss Devry at the studio this P.M. + brought to me 3 vols. handsomely bound, full of Poet Browning — a present —

## Right Page

### June Wednesday, 2 1869

Close + overcast in the, beginning to rain about 10.30 + keeping it up quite steadily until about 6. Got to studio at 9 + at work on etching (4th day) Considerably interrupted by Callers — Miss Woodward called — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — S. Colman came in — worked until about 5.45 — then gave up for the day — Went to Knoedler's then called on Dr Kingsley — got home to tea about 6.30 — Mary Norton + her children got off for Lexington, Ny, this P.M. at 5 o'clk — Evng spent Reading Gray's descriptive anatomy — Got to bed about 10.30.
