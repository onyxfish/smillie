## Left Page

### February Monday, 1 1869.

Clear cold day — got to Studio
shortly after 9 o'clk — Miss Dew-
son came in & spent an hour
drawing — Mr. Clark of Phila came
in at about 10.30 & with him went
to the N.A. of D — there met Dr.
Lewis — Mr. & Mrs. Jas. Roosevelt & Miss
R— with the Dr. & Mr. C talking Water
Color, until 1 o'clk — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.45 — Spent
the afternoon finishing addressing
invitations for our Recep'tn & sticking
on the P.O. Stamps — About 3.30
Fan. M. & Miss Marion Rossell called —
Home at 6.30 — Posting in my way
100 invitations — Welf went to
41 W 48th & called on Fan M—
Home at 11 & Right to bed —

## Right Page

### February 8 Tuesday, 2 1869.

Overcast, cold & raw — Got
to studio about 9 — all the mf
distributing, Recep'tn invitations &
sticking up 375 stamps & envelopes,
finishing at last such a job as
hope never to be caught in again —
Home at 1 — Posting the invitation
at Station G — after lunch went
to Sixth National Bank & deposited
my Water Color Society surplus —
Then rode down to Brooks Bros. with
a coat to be altered — walked up —
stopping at Sarony's, Mr. Murray's
& Snedicors — At Studio at 5 — Home
a little before 6 — Took tea & made
out my annual Report as Treas-
of the Water Color Society — went to the
meeting at Wood's Room, 82 Fifth Av —
was Re-elected for the coming year —
Through with business at 10 —
Walked up home — got my books
(accts. of Society) all straight and
at near 12 got to bed —
