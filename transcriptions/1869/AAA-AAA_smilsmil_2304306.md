### May, Tuesday 4, 1869

Room 178. Continental Hotel — Philadelphia

Pleasant spring like day. breezy \+ Rather Cool. \- breakfasted about 9 o'clk and immediately after met Sam. Colman in the Hall \- Saw too D. Lewis \+ arranged plans for the day \- Lewis had business. Ps C. Gel and I went to the Penn. ## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 5, 1869

Am thankful to find myself much better than I had hoped, but for a very sore throat feel tolerably well \- took a light bkfst at 9 o'clk \+ settled \- $8.00 \- never go to Continental \- with Gel went to Penn. Acf at 10 \- met Mr. Whitney, D. Lewis, Clark, Colman, \+ Williams a Phil. painter about 10.30 Mr. Whitney Gel. \+ I. Started off in Mr. W.'s carriage \- stopping at his house \- looking at his pictures \+ getting a heavy coat \+ scarf for me \- He then added Mr. Enoch Lewis to our company went to Mr. Whitney's Car Wheel factory \- can Colonwhile 17 \+ 13 \[illegible\] from there drove out to Broad S\+ and to Germantown \- there visited Mr. W.T. Richards \- at near 2 o'clk started for home again, Returning by the mission \+ getting to Mr. E. Lewis's about 3 o'clk \- warmly greeted by Mrs. L. and children and invited to dinner \- none of us and accepted it \- Mr. W. driving off alone \- after dinner \- with Mr. L. we walked down Green S\+ to the Fairmount Park \- enjoyed the same about until near 5 \- then took cars to near the Continental \- there left Mr. \+ \- Got our bags \+ umbrellas and to Mr. his just before 6 \- all waiting for Mr. \- and Mr. W. \- Their son Willie \+ daughter Annie \- Took tea \+ \[illegible\] in the eve\[ning\] at 9.45 I went to Artist's Rooms with Mr. W. \- there met Messrs Lewis \+ Clark \- Excused myself from going to the Club with them and at Returned with Mr. W. at m\[illegible\] 10.30 to bed with an excessively sore throat which disturbed me a good deal during the night \-
