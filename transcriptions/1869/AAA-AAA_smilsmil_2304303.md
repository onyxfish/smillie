## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 28, 1869

Warm pleasant day — got to Studio at 8.45 and after setting "my Charcoal drawing (Pattis plate) I commenced work on Meuger's drawing of West Point after much trouble. Got the subject crowded into the limits of the drawing, but it Requires a good deal of study as the actual view took at least 80° of the horizon. I question whether it is a legitimate art undertaking —
Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 worked until near 7 P.M. when I finished outlining the subject with pen \+ ink — Home to tea at 7.15 — Eve'g Reading Vathek by Beckford — My eyes have troubled me for some time past this eve'g I tried make spectacles and the difficulty is overcome — I shall get glasses — I am growing old — Got to bed shortly after 10 — Mother \+ Charl. up at Uncle Aaron's —

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 29, 1869

Dully with driving Rain storm from the East — wrote a note to W. Main and got to Studio at 9 o'clk — About 10.30 tried to kindle a fire \+ was nearly smoked out. Set to work on Water-color "West Point" (2d. day) and got the sky and distance washed in crudely — the paper is bad and gives me much trouble —
Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 Rain stopped — Miss Deiry called \+ invited me to dine with herself \+ sister Mr. Cobb this p.m. Dr. \+ Mrs. Kingsley called — Young Bell spent part of the P.M. at Studio — Home to tea at 7 — Eve'g finished Reading "Vathek" \+ wrote a note to Watson's Art Journal concerning a stupid statement that my pencil study at the N.A. of D. was made on a Claude mirror by tracing.
Mother \+ Charl still at Uncle [illegible] — Got to bed about 10.30
