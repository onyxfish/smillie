## Left Page

### December Monday, 6 1869

Up at 7.15 after a tolerable night. Wild day of snow and wind, got off my 8.33 train — stopped at [illegible] in 34th St. went with him to Pharmacy. Got medicine for my giddy head & at studio at 9.30 clk — All sorts of trouble getting stove lighted — gas & smoke & very cold room — Unable to work during the mng. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30. Mr. Frank Scott called during the P.M. — but came to no definite agreement with him as to drawing for him — at 4 started for Hudson R.R. depot — there at 4.15 — took 4.25 train — James met me at depot & got in nicely. notwithstanding the driving snow — dinner at 5. Evening feeling better — spent in general chat — got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 7 1869

Superb clear mng with about 5 inches of snow — Unable to get to the depot until 9.15. James took me — train behind — got to studio about 10.15 — Set to work on little drawing for A.B.N. Co — Seligman Bond. Finished it — home to lunch at 2 — on way back, called in Mr. C. P. Crouch, then to N.A. of D — Saw Mr. Richards & agreed upon date for our 5th annual Water Color Exbn to be held at Academy Rooms. Back to studio at 3.45. Worked on Jay Cooke's R.R. Bond drawing until 4.45 when I went to the R.R. depot to take 5.10. Train — on account of storm & derangements no 5.10 Train — Had to wait until 6 o'clk — James waiting at M-ville dinner waiting also. Every[illegible] wrote a note to Burling & got to bed about 11 o'clk —
