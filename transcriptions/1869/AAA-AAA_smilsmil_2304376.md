## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 23 1869.

Beautiful day \- clear + warm. At 8.30 started up the "Giant" alone \- got to 2° fall at 9.4 + at once set to work on the oil sketch that I Rubbed in some time ago \- worked until 1 when I stopped long enough to make a fire \- cook some beef tea + eat my lunch \- then a bit again + worked until about 4 o'clk \- when all light for my study being gone, I gave it up \- I feel encouraged + think that I have learned something on it \- Home short before 6 \- C. spent the day away up Ausable gorge \- got near the house \- with Mrs. C. Ready along to us Mr. Siri[illegible] Lodgings" \- got to bed some after 9 \-

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 24 1869.

another fine day \- Som after 8 started for the Ausable gorge near Rin Redea's, in going through the bushes got very wet \- nearly to my waist, on getting to my study place built a big fire of drift wood + in a short time was as dry + warm as toast + all ready for my work which is a water color study of a fall \- worked until 1.30, then fixed and eat my lunch, + worked on until about 5 o'clk \- as my subject is towards the south + the sun is in the gorge + on it only from about 2 until near 3, I can, under difficulties, work on it all day \- Coming out by Rin B's, met Miss Elliott \- Mr. + Mrs. Jal. Hart have been in the valley to-day \- then met + walked home with the Colmans \- Wolf wrote a letter to Mary and got to bed about 10.30. mind strong from South + threatening a storm \-
