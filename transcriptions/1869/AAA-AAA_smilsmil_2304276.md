## Left Page

### March, Sunday 7, 1869

Tired \+ did not get up until about 10 o'clk \- Mf went to Dr Hastings. He discoursed upon the wonderful qualities \+ powers of our memory \+ associated it with conscience \+ penisskmeu as long as memory lasted here after Cold, cutting mind \+ clear day \- Home to dinner \- Afternoon in my big chair \- Every went to J.P. Thompson's \+ heard from him the first of a series on "Creation \- Moses, Genesis and Geology" \- Interesting altho I heard nothing new from him \- Met there Jos. Williams, Dan Sayre \+ Ben Mulford \- Jos \+ Dan, just in from Washington \- got to bed about 11 \-

## Right Page

### March, Monday 8, 1869

Charming day \- mild \+ pleasant \- got to studio at 8.45 \- Soon had fire under way \+ set to work on 24×40 \- (8½ days) painting over the r.h. Corner \- Miss Devry came \+ spent her usual hour \- our drawings from the Water\-Color came during the mf \- Samuel Coleman was on hand \+ relieved me of the necessity of going over to the N.A. of D \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- Worked until 5.30 \- I am hopeful, but fear that it will be impossible for me to finish the 24×40 in time for the Spring 46th \- Home to Tea at 6.45 \- Welf went to Burggrafs \+ ordered a pair of boots \- then to Meta Adams \- Saw her, her Charley \- her father mother \+ her br. Will \- Home at 10.20 \- To bed at 11
