## Left Page

### July, Monday 19, 1869

Cool day and overcast — Myf spent burnishing on George's plate (16th day) and after lunch, laid a ground over corner for water and at 3 o'clk went down town to A.P.N.Co's and Ruled in the water. Returning mitt father at 6.15 — Rec'd a letter to-day from Matt, from Salt Lake City — She has had an interview with Brigham Young, got his photo with autograph. Pvett. went to Burgeral's to have moccasins fitted — at 5.30 Mr Pliny Smith of Continental Mr Co called and did not leave until I agreed to take out an endowment policy for $10000, to be p'd in 10 years — got to bed shortly after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 20, 1869

Very cool day, overcast with a mizzle of rain, just enough to keep things wet. Spent the day (17th) on George's plate, biting the ruling for the water and then burnishing over it, worked until 7 o'clk — after tea, at 8.20 went down to see the Meedys.
