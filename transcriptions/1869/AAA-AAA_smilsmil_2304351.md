## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 4 1869

Got up feeling only tolerable well \+ powerfully weak yet. At 9.10 started for down town getting to the A.B.N. Co's about 12. Started at once to Rule the close line that goes over the entire surface of my aus ab[illegible] plate (20° day). In the midst of much tribulation \+ anxiety. At last got over it nearly — The machine would not take in the whole plate by 1/10 inch. Finished up to that at a few minutes after 6 P.M. — To me a very long, tedious \+ harassing day. — Rode home with Fath[er]. Got my feast gruel \+ at once stretched myself out on my bed lying there until 10 — when I undressed \+ ment[ioned] decently to bed —

## Right Page

### August Thursday, 5 1869

Disagreeable chilly morning with Rain — Clearing before noon, but not bright until [illegible]. Got to A.B.N. Co's at about 10 \+ set to work on another machine. Matched my Ruling perfectly, finished it (21st day) — At noon Rode up to Sauthier's \+ Snedicor's — Got colors etc. B. S Kaast offers $150 for my drawing of the White Mts from Randolph Hill — Home at 2 — P.M. — Stopping out on my Ruling \+ preparing Water colors in box — Ridner sent some Blue \+ Zinc White [illegible]. Herself lying on bed and saving myself generally. Got to bed about 10 —
