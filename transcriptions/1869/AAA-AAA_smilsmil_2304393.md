## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 27, 1869

at Mr. Tiffany's —
Irvington on Hudson —

Clear, cold & blustering
day — got to studio at 9
A.M. — outlined 10½×18 — view
of Mt. Lafayette — & drew
Cu, in charcoal 12×32 —
Indian Pass — Home to
lunch at 1 — Back at 2 —
Mr. Breevort & Miss Brown
of Farmington called —
I outlined in brown the
Indian Pass & at 3.45 started
for Hudson R. R. depot &
took 4.15 train for Irvington
getting here about 5 o'clk —
Louis & carriage waiting —
Mr. Colman & Miss Tiffany
came up on the train with me
but I did not see them — Mr. &
Mr. A[illegible] at the Tiffany's — I
was cordially rec'd — & passed
a pleasant eve'g getting to
bed at 11 o'clk —

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 28, 1869

Overcast m'f, like clearing
at noon, but later, rains — Up
at 6.45 — nobody else up — took a
run out & met old Mr. Dunham —
then called on Mr. & Mrs. S. Colman —
back at 7.45 for bkfs — which was
Rushed that we might take the
9 train. I forgot my cattle
traveling bag — down to city
with Mr. S. & Louis — got to
studio at 9.30 — Painted in
7½×12" "afternoon — Adirondacks" —
Home to lunch at 1 — back at
2.30 — could not see after 4
o'clk — Home to tea before 6 —
with Matt & Kate much down
to see the Tweedys — getting
there at 8.30 — great chatting
& noise until near 11.30, Char[les]
came in about 10 o'clk — Home
at 11.45 and to bed about
midnight —
