## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 6, 1869

a genuine april day, bright sunshine \+ sudden showers \-
got to the Studio's at 8.40 and worked until near noon
finishing" On the Saco "making 3½ days on it \- Then went
to Sauthier's \+ ordered the pass-
partouts. Home to lunch at
1 \- fixing up clothes for Riding
I did not get to the Studio
until near 3 \- at 3.30 went
with Louis Tiffany to his
stable \+ at 4 went out on horse-
back with him \- Enjoyed
my Ride in the Park exceed-
ingly \- Back at 6 o'clk, went
down to Studio, home at 7 \-
at 8.30 went to Colman's
Studio \- Water Color Society
meeting \- Home shortly after
10 \+ right to bed \-

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 7, 1869

April day Rather more so
than yesterday \- a Succession
of showers after 12 o'clk. Then
we had a brisk Snow Storm
with thunder \+ lightning \-
got to Studio's at 8.45 and
Commenced the day by
stretching a piece of drawing
paper \- Miss Dewy came
\+ drew for an hour \- I
worked over the Sky \+ distance
of Large drawing", The Track of the
Torrent" with opaque color \+
I think have much improved
it \- atnoon went to Sauthier's
\+ Sarony's \- Caught in the
Thunder \- Snow Storm \- Home
to lunch at 1 \- back at 2.10 \-
P.m \- Started a little sketch
in Water Color \- Home to tea
at 7.15 \- Every felt heavy,
did nothing \+ got to bed
about 9.30
