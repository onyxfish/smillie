## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 24 1869.

Raining hard all the morning.
Got to Studio at 9 o'clk and
continued my drawing on
33×44 — Don't get much encour-
agement to go in with it. [illegible]
wants to know what I want to
paint that for. Home to lunch
at 1, back at 2.30 after getting
some paper at Knoedler's + then
meeting Whitney + Richards. [illegible]
soon after came to my studio
+ I had a very pleasant visit
with them until near 4 o'clk.
Got started Charcoalure on
Subject for large M.C. draw[ing].
Home to tea at 6.15. afternoon
clear + cold — Eve'd. Read[ing]
+ got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 25 1869.

Delightful day — got to Studio
at 9.5 and commenced painting
on 12×22 "Lake Placid from
Whiteface" (1st day) Got the
sky painted in by 1 when I
went home to lunch — back
at 2 — I worked at the mts
getting them all in when at
2.30 Mr. Cha[pma]n a Fisk came
to see if he could hire one of
George's Rooms at the Y.M.C.A
a Bath. I went over with him
saw La Farge + the room — got
names + reference — then went
to see Mr. Rumney — then to see
[illegible] in 2d ave. near 12th St —
Chapman, near there in [illegible]
stone Place — Both spoke highly
of the young man — Home a
little after 7 — got tea, feel
tired, sat about + got to bed
soon after 10 —
