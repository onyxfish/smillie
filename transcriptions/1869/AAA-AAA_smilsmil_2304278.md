## Left Page

### March, Thursday 11, 1869

A glorious day — got to Studio at 8.35 \+ Repainted sky, distance
\+ part of middle distance on 24×40. (10° day) — It is manifestly
much improved, but not so much as to make me & engine to believe
that I can get it done in time for the 46th, a very busy day
will decide me, as to whether I shall go on or give it up for
the present. My frame for it came from Snedicor to-day —
Sent my big drawing over to the Brooklyn 46th — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 —
at E. went with Gle. to Y.M.C.A. \+ he took two Rooms (studios) in
their new building. Went to look over it with Mc.Kinney. Eaton, [illegible]
Robbins \+ Gle. — Back to Studio at 4.0 —
worked as long as I could see —
Home at 7 — Mother R'd. from Newark —
Eveg. wrote to Newman \+ D.
Lewis — got to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### March, Friday 12, 1869

Overcast Wf \+ trying to snow, but before 10 oclk it cleared off — Got to studio at 8.35. Painted all day (4° day) on 24×40. — going over the middle distance trees on both sides of the painting. Miss Derry came in the mf \+ spent an hour drawing. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — Painted until near 5.30 — then went down to Schau's \+ Snedicor's.
Home to tea at 7.30 — Spent most of the eveg. washing up my brushes — got to bed at about 10 o'clk.
