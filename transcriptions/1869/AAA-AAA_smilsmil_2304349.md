## Left Page

### July, Saturday 31, 1869

Feeling better in the mng. my troubles seems to have abated — Kept quiet on my back — about 10 o'clk a headache took hold of me where under the sun it came from I cannot imagine — all the p.m. and until about 10 o'clk. suffered severely, then the pains began to subside — Soon after midnight made for myself a cup of tea \+ took a little beef broth — Sat in his chair until after 4 a.m. then went to bed and slept until after 6 —

Evng. George went to call on the Smeedy gives who have just Ret'd from Newport Kate Ric'd — a letter from Matt at San Francisco —

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 1, 1869

woke feeling very much better and hopeful that my troubles are over — Kept lying abed — about noon pains in my bowels returned and by 6 P.M. I was worse than I had been before — spent a miserable night.
