## Left Page

### March, Monday, 1, 1869.

Another very fine day — got to studio at 9.10 — Built a fire and got things somewhat to rights. Miss Deury came for an hour. I spent the day on the Harris picture, 14×18" (2d day Repainting) I am trying to make a pleasant green picture of it & call it "Summer Time" — It is monotonous in color, but I think I can to a great extent Remedy it & make it more delicate — At 1 o'clk went to Kahn's & had my hair cut — Home to lunch at 1.50 — back to studio at 2.35 — worked until 5 — then went to Southiers, Snedicor & Knoedler's — Home to tea at 6.45. At 8.30 went to Mary's in §45 St to meet a Miss Corson that I first met some ten years ago — Passed a very pleasant eve'g until 11 o'clk — then home & right to bed —

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday, 2, 1869.

Miserable day sleet & snow. Got to studio at 8.45 — & swept up the room & after getting things in order got to work again on 24×40, in". On the "Boquet" (5th day) Painted over the sky & mountains getting it, I am afraid, again too strong in color — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 worked until 4 o'clk — when Mr. Forbes & Miss Sterling came in — I mounted a drawing of Mrs. F's for her & until near 6 o'clk was giving her hints concerning her work, etc — Home to tea at 6.40 — Spent the eve'g writing to Dr. Lewis of Phila. Got to bed about 11.30
