## Left Page

### July Wednesday, 21 1869

overcast with clearing off into a clear + very pleasant day. Worked on Ausable plate (17th day) during the morning with graver + drypoint. At 1 laid a rebiting ground + spent the P.M. until after rebiting — got what seems to be a splendid line — About 4 o'clk. Mr. Ring Smith called with my $1000 Endowment Policy + I made my first payment, $404.00 — my next payment is to be a picture, due Jan'y next. Self father brought up imps. of George's plate. It looks bad enough, but not as bad as I had feared —

At 9 o'clk. went to studio, picked up a lot of things and brought them home — Glorious night — cloudless and moon[lit.]
To bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### July Thursday, 22 1869

Delightful day — almost cool. After bkfst, enjoyed a walk then set to work on Ausable plate (18th day) with graver and burnisher. Worked until near 7 when I laid a ground and arranged to have bit of blue used in to-morrow —

Evening spent preparing water colors in my new box — got to bed about 11 —
