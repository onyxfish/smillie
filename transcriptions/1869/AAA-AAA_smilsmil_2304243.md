## Right Page

### January Friday, 1 1869.

a wild beginning for the new year — horrible snow, sleet
\+ driving storm — In-doors
all day very much used
up with a mild headache
but stayed about the parlors.
In spite of the storm the
girls had 22 callers, which
altogether was more than
they expected. Dan Sayre
called, fresh from Montrose.
As I passed such a miserable
night lastnight, with stuffed
up head \+ sore throat — I spent
this night in my big chair
in the back parlor — Slept
well until 4 A.M. then
went up to my bed \+ slept
the rest of the night.
