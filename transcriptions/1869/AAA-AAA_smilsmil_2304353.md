## Left Page

### August, Sunday 8, 1869

glorious day — Up at 7 — spent the mng arranging various little things to facilitate my getting out of town as soon as possible. Afternoon went out and took a little walk — after dinner wrote a letter to George and between 4 \+ 5 o'clk. Rode up to the Park \+ walked over to 7th St \+ 5th ave — then to 3d ave \+ took car up to 84th St. Called on Meta Adams — took dinner there — at 7 we all walked down to the garden at the foot of 84th St \+ E. River — found it really chilly about 8 Rtn. Took 3d ave car to 42d St. \+ got home just at 9 o'clk —

## Right Page

### August, Monday 9, 1869

Beautiful day — got down to Bates with my plates at about 9.45 and at once had them proved. Stopped at ten man's \+ at Ridner's got to A.B.N.Co about 11 o'clk — Rebibing ground over water due on available plate (24th day) \+ just colored it, then Wm laid a ground over the sky for me \+ I went to dinner with father — then to Menger's \+ managed to get $30 \+ out of him on acct of Welsh Point drawing — Back to A.B.N.Co. at 2 \+ until 6.30 Ruling cross line on sky. Then rode home with father every Rec'd a letter from George Alfred Jones took tea with us \+ stayed until 10 o'clk — I got to bed about 10.30
