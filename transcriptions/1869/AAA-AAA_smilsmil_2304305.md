## Left Page

### May Sunday, 2 1869

In weather, a continuation of yesterday — My went to Dr Thos Hastings + heard him preach from 51st Psalm. 3° "my sin is ever before me" He dwelt upon man's several property, of or Right of Possession, in sin "my sins" — a property that might be augmented but could never be reduced — and from that he enlarged upon man's property in Christ, my Savior. About 4, 15 started on before the Rain for a walk — got into to Central Park + was Charmed with the budding trees + green grass — walked on — getting my rich until I came to 79 ot and 5th Ave — walked over to 241 E. 81st St + called upon Meta Adams — Charley Adams walked nearly home with me — getting at 7 — took tea — went to Ino. P. Thompsons and heard him on the Deluges — I think Hugh Miller more Interesting and fuller on the subject — mother trying to Clear — Home + to bed about 10 o'clk —

## Right Page

### May Monday, 3 1869

overcast, windy + forboding. got to Studio before 9 o'clk + built fire. Miss Decoy came + drew for an hour — I worked on my drawing of West Point — W. Q. P. Putnam spent part of the mng with us trying to arrange for some little holiday work to be illustrated — but without coming to any conclusion. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 worked until 4 — Horace Robbins gave us a call — at 4. I went home — packed bag — got tea and with Geo. at 5.25 left the house to take the 6.30 train to Phila. crossed from Cortlandt St. in the 6.15 boat — had a Comfortable Ride to Phila — got in to Kensington at 10.10 + to the Continental Hotel at 10.45 — were give Room No. 175 — went at once to bed —
