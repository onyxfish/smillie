## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 4, 1869.

a true March day with two
brisk snow squales — morning
went to Madison Ave. Bap. Ch.
expecting to hear young Hanna
of Wms burg — a mistake. a
Mr. Hammond of Porratclyn
preached — I did not think
much of him, Rating him as
compared with Hastings — as
J. S. Arthur compares with
Hawthorne in story telling &
literary merit — Suffered with
a headache & when I got home
went to bed & stayed there
until 6 o'clk — Spent the evg
hanging about until 10 o'clk when
I wrote a letter to Tau Mulford —
got to bed at 12 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 5, 1869.

Blustering March-like day
got to the Studio at 8.10 —
Kindled the fire — washed &
cleaned up water color Frames
& set to work (3 days) on
water color sketch" On the Saco"
Miss Devry came & drew for
an hour, then young Bell
came & worked away at
water colors until 2 o'clk.
went home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2.15. Shortly after
Nettie Jessups, an aunt
cousin came in — pleasant
call — I managed to get my
sketch into general shape
after 5 Miss Sterling & her bro
did "came in & stayed until
6.15 — I got home to tea at 7 —
Spent the eveg mounting
my drawing, etc, etc — got to
bed about 11
