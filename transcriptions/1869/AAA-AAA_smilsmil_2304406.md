## Left Page

### November Friday, 26 1869

Another fine day — got to studio at 9 — Fisk there ahead of me, agreed together the is to take the room from the 1st Dec, then to work on 12×22 Placid (2d dy) Charley Adams called \+ Dr. Sylvanus Mulford — got middle distance painted in \+ at 1,30 home to lunch — at 2 Rose to Phil. Rehearsal — Jammed home Mr. Scott Siddons Read Tept of Mid-Summers nights dream — to Mendelssohns exquisite music — a great mistake, his excellent things, the text \+ the music being spoiled by the combination. Did not get out until 5,45 — walked home with Belle \+ Florence \+ Freddy — back to studio \+ home at 7,15 took tea \+ spent the eve with french — got to bed about 11 — Uncle W. \+ aunt Agnes got started for home this mng.

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 27 1869

About as miserable a day as need be — wind \+ Rain — got to studio at 9, 10 \+ to work on 12×22 "Lake Placid" (3d day) \+ had difficulty to see to paint it was so dark — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 \+ by 3,30 had my "carrying in" finished. Maldelmay came to give me my 2d French lesson — Mr. Saml. Colman called also Wr. Rob. Dodge — he borrowed a pencil sketch — had my lesson \+ home at 5,15 — Tea at 6 \+ at 6,40 with Matt Hgalt to Phil. Concert — met Belle \+ Joe Freddy they sat with us. Had no difficulty in getting good seats \+ the house just exactly full — no Rain when we went in — Mr. Siddons' Reading of Mid-Summer Nights Dream spirited \+ interesting but to me, it ruins the music — By considerable cutting of Reading we got out at 10,50 — Home at once \+ to bed at 11,40
