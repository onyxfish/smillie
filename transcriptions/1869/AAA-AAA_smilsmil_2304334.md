## Left Page

### July, Thursday 1, 1869

Overcast, but comparatively cool \+ pleasant \- got to his studio at 9.30 \+ to work on foreground of etching (10th day) Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2.30. Mr. Phelps had been waiting to see George, but left before he came. \- Mrs. Forbes paid us a visit \- I finished the foreground of my etching \- This is not what I had hoped to make it, being too close \+ formal \- About home D. Kingsley's painting "Ausable Lake". Home to tea at 7 o'clk at 8 went to Mr. B. T. Jessup's \+ spent a pleasant eveg until 10 o'clk. Nettie is growing to be a very lovely girl \- Home \+ to bed before 11. [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~2 words] [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### July, Friday 2, 1869

Another day of comparative comfort \- got to studio shorter \+ until 10.30 fixing drawing of White Mts \- into its new frame \- then to work at etching (1st day) Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2.30 \- felt heavy \+ very unlike work, so that I napped at my desk \- got at it at last \+ worked steadily until near almost finishing the etching Home to tea at 7.15 \- Eveg wrote a letter to Matt and attended to various little things getting to bed about 10.30. \- At 2 o'clk, with [illegible] \- stopped at Dr. Norman M. Kingsley's to arrange the hanging of our pictures \- He gave me a check for $110 \+ [illegible — ~3 words]
