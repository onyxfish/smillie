## Left Page

### January, Sunday, 10, 1869.

A very agreeable change in the weather — Cool \+ brisk \+ clear — mf went \+ heard Rev. Tho. Hastings. He seemed in excellent spirits and preachied a most admirable serm. from Galatians — 4 Ch. 18 v. "But it is good to be zealously affected always in a good thing." —
Home to dinner at 1 — Spent the afternoon in-doors doing nothing with considerable energy — Every went to J. T. Thompsons \+ heard him preach from Isiah 16 ch 25 v — His purpose was to set the belief in Christ upon such high intellectual ground that none could possibly be ashamed of it as childish, weak or suited to times past. Home at 9 o'clk — Got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### January, Monday, 11, 1869.

Commenced Raining about 10.30 \+ finished up with a horrible night of pelting Rain \+ wind. — Got to Studio at 9 — got fire started \+ then at my drawing — Miss Dewy came in \+ drew — Burling called \+ Avery sent up for my "White Mt." drawing — I saw Colman's drawing of the "Giralda Tower, Seville" and its new frame — This I think, the finest work in Water Color that has yet been produced here — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 light failing som, with interruptions ill, had a short day of it Worked over the trees on the R.h. of drawing feel now so much encouraged that I entertain hopes of being able to finish it in time for the Exhibition opening — Every at home writing notices to members of the Water Color Society to pay up their dues — Got to bed at 11 o'clk —
