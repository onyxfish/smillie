## Left Page

### June, Monday, 7, 1869.

Charming day + quite cool, about 9 went to the studio + gathered things that I wish to take to Bo— keeps die with me — all of my [illegible] takes, etc — Had to make two trips shortly after 11 got to work at packing — Hard work — for Father — Kate + [illegible] — food, bedding — clothes + cup, + [illegible] materials — one trunk + a valise — Done before 1 — Suuch — + at [illegible] appressmaie came for baggage — Kate met us in the Many Powell to Po'keepsie at 3.30. P.M. — I spent the P. M. Resting until about 4. when I went to the "Miller", foot of Franklin St. There at 4.35 — Father waiting — Barge in tow + did not get away from the City until near 6 o'clck. Very cool night on the River — at 10 o'clk, as we were passing West Point — "turned in" —

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday, 8, 1869.

Father roused me about 5.30 a.m. — after a night of indifferent rest — Mts very still, rather chilly + looking like Rain. walked about on the dock. waiting until 6.30 when we got our bt/pt on the — Rain came down hard — at 7.15. walked from lower landing c/o to Mr. Parker's — there met Kate — I made a few purchases + arranged with Mr. Walker, a photo. to take views of Hill-side — At 8.10 with Kate + Father started to walk out to Hill-Side — sun shining beautifully broken up with clouds. out in 1¼ hour — Looby drove in on our baggage — Heavy hail + Rain storm about noon — a funny dinner of baker's bread + 2 boiled eggs for the us — Spent the P.M. unpacking + wandering over the place — Showers heavy thunder all about us — but very cold — another storm about 5 o'clk — cold, clear quietly + to bed, very tired about 9.30.
