## Left Page

### January, Saturday 30, 1869

a lovely day, clear + very mild —
To my astonishment got up all
Right in the W[illegible]. at 9.15 called
at the "Albemarle" — Saw Fau M. +
Mr + Mrs Childs, of Boston. Got to the
Studio about 10 — about 11 — Dr Lewis
Mr Clark + Mr Clay came in + spent
about an hour. at 12.15 I went to J.S.
Ward's Studio — There until 1.30 home
to lunch — back at 2.30. Young Bell
at the Studio — about 3. Fau + Mr +
Mrs Chilas came in — went to the
N.A. of D. with them — There until 4.30
took Fau over to the 6th Ave cars, then
back to Studio. Home a little after
6. Every wasted over Chocolate
Caramels — + general rompng —
got to bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 31, 1869

Cold, blustering, windy day — Went
to Dr Hastings — Heard him
preach from Col. 2d chap. 6 + 7 —
after service — got ready + went
up to Uncle Aaron's to dinner —
got up there about 2.15 — Mother +
many have been there since Friday.
Dr Came up about 3. + Ret'd early —
walked down about 5 o'clk —
finding it very disagreeable —
Went in Will's Room — Gil +
Charl. over to hear Beecher, with
the Treedie's — Father pretty
well used up with a headache —
got to bed about 10.30
