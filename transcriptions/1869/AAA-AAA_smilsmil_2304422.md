## Left Page

### December Thursday, 30 1869.

Glorious day, clear and mild. Got to Studio at 9 o'clk \+ at W. C. drawing (3° day) still working over the sky and distant mts. Home to lunch at 1.30, back at 2.20 and again all the p.m. Repeatedly washing over sky \+ distance \- Home to tea at 6 \- Suep at 8 o'clk called on Kate Tessenden at Mr. [illegible] No 8, W. 29 [illegible]. She is spending the Holiday week at home in North [illegible]. Then walked up to Tiffany's Madison ave, near 34 [illegible]. Miss Annie at home \- She Rec'd me very cordially \+ I passed a pleasant eve until 9.30 when I left \- Home \+ to bed at 11 \-

## Right Page

### December Friday, 31 1869.

Another Superb day \- Got to Studio at 9.10 \+ at drawing (4th day). My sky too light \+ high in key, so all the mfg working over it \+ lowering it. [illegible] Haven called. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 15 \+ worked as long as I could see, getting nearly all of my drawing covered. Home to tea at 6 \- at 8.30 went to Mr. Rutherford's to see her autographic album \- Quite a display Church \- W. C. Bryant \+ a number of lesser lights there \- Had a pleasant chat with Bryant about my Illustrations of "The Woods" \- Left at 9.40 \- Home at 10.20 To bed at [illegible]
