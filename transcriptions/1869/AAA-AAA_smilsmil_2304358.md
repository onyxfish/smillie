## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 18 1869

Warm \+ Smoky, got off with Colman, got \+ Tiffany soon after 8cls. \+ went to the [illegible] near Thin Redee's in the gorge where me set to work. Until 1 oclk. - when we cooked our lunch. Beef tea etc. and then at it again - I worked in water colors on my block making a slight study of Running water \+ Rock - very unsatisfactory to me - the paper working badly \+ the color being brownish \+ untrue. Home to dinner at 6.30 -
Evelf, more Reading \+ the excitement of an arrival of an upbress [illegible] for Sones Tiffany — got to bed about 10.45

## Right Page

### August Thursday, 19 1869

Very warm smoky day, and until we'f quite headery \+ over after 8cls. most of the party got off with Colman to the top of the Giant's Leap - I stayed to make a rough sketch of the A.B.N. Co. \+ Pacific R.R. [illegible], at which I worked till oclk. then lunch \+ at 2 I started for the top of Giant's Leap. Near the top I found a most trunk shivered \+ splintered in a most satisfactory manner. Made a rough sketch of it — got out to the party about 4 o'clk. soon after we all came down. Returning to the house about 6 ¾ the first fine evlf since I set? [illegible] fine moonlight - Evelf all work with pencil while Mrs. Colman Read "old town folks" — got to bed about 10.30 —
