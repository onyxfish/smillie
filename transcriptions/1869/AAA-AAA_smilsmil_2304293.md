## Left Page

### April, Thursday, 8, 1869

More sunshine & showers, but not to the same extent as yesterday. Got to the Studio at 8.20. Worked all day over my large drawing — the track of the Torrent — most of the time being spent on the foreground Rocks, over which I have worked with opaque color — On the whole, I think the drawing is much improved. At noon went down to [illegible], to get him to withdraw his Resignation to the Water Color Society — He did so. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.10 — Saw [illegible] Bell at Studio, most of the p.m. Every at home writing sketches of my life for Redpath — got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Friday, 9, 1869

Charming day — Got to Studio at 8.15 — Commenced again (2nd day) on Charcoal Study for Mr. Pat's subject — "Upper Ausable Lake". Miss Dewy came & drew for an hour — Worked steadily until 1.30 — home to lunch — and at 2.10 Rode down to Phil Rehearsal, after Rehearsal at 4.45 walked up to Studio with Mary Tweedy — Sister Mary met me at the Studio. I worked on my drawing until 6.40 — Home to tea at 7.15 — Every mounted drawing for [illegible] & wrote some Water Color Society dunning letters — Got to bed at about 10 o'clk.
