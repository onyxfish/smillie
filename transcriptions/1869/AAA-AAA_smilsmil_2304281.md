## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 17 1869

another fine day — got to the studio at 8.15 \+ set to work on 24×40 (15th day) painting over the foreground stumps. trunks. etc. I have now completed the painting over it \+ it's harmonious by horrible — Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — back at 2.15. after noon pothered away until near 6 o'clk — home at 6.30 — Everg. with Geo. went to the N.A. \& D. — invited to a special Social Reunion of N.A's and A's — Comparatively few \& the invited out. a very good lunch with plenty to drink — amused myself with Vedder, looking over Illustrated works on the Alhambra — left at 10.15 — Home \+ to bed before 11 —

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 18 1869

Still another fine day — at studio at 8.45 — Commenced 3? Repainting of 24×40 (16th day) Scumbled the sky and distance with white \+ Madder lake then Repainted thinly but solid improving the tone very much getting every thing lighter \& sweeter — home to lunch at 1 — back at 2. Afternoon painting over middle distance trees — at 5 o'clk went to Knoedler's new gallery to see his paintings by daylight — Back to studio at 5.50 — Washed up some brushes — cleaned up — home at 6.45 to tea — Father home all day, used up with a cold — Eveg at 8 o'clk. met \+ called on Mr. \& Mrs. Marks at 603 Fifth ave — near 49 St. There until 9 o'clk — then went to Diven's — there until 10.15 — then home \+ to bed at 10.45
