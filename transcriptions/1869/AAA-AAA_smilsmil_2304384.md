## Left Page

### October Saturday, 9 1869.

Charming day. Troubled with
my throat + evidences of a
general cold — worked all
day on my studies, touching,
varnishing + retouching generally
— not a safe practice for
studies, but my summers
work has been such a Rush
that almost always on leaving
a place that I have painted
I would note on my study
certain things to be done —
to glaze down a shadow — cool
or heighten a light or something
of that kind — Father + Mother
got home from Montrose about
5.30 or 6 P.M. — well + glad to
get here. At night, I gave
up — bathed my feet in hot
water + got to bed about
10.45 — very Restless night
— Rain during the night.

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 10 1869.

Rainy Sunday, as has now
become usual — In bed all
day — Slept all the m[orning],
when I had a light [illegible]
Slept again until about
2 o'clk — about 5 o'clk
I got up, dressed + went
down to the parlor. George
is very much better to day.
I kept my big chair until
about 11.30 — when I went
to bed.
