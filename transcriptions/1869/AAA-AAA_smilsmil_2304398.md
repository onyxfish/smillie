## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 10 1869

Bright mng but soon clouded over — got to studies at 9.10 Set to work on 16½ × 26¾ —
"Moat Mt & Saco River" 2d painting of the sky — Much troubled with visitors —
Teny called to Falk academy matters & Verplanck came near noon — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Bothered with my sky — original painting too general in its ideas. I cannot reduce it to form — so my 2d part aiming at Realization is not so good as my first general paint. Mr. Josephine called — also Emma Ireland & Miss How — also Mr. Forbes — Home to tea at 6. Well tired & did nothing to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 11 1869

Pleasant day — got to Studio at 9.5 & to work upon "Moat mt" By noon, had painted over the mountains when Jame from Bogardus came along & at once I set to work to get the little 8×14 finished for it — Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — back at 2 — worked until 3.30 on 8×14. then went to Tnoedlen's, took a look at his pictures & bought a piece of German Twilled canvas for my Academy picture 33×44 — Reth to Studio & stretched canvas — Bought 3 sets of Philharmonic tickets. Home to tea at 6.15 — Well not feeling well — did nothing — got to bed at 10.15
