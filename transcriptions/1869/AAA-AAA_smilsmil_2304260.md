## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 3 1869.

a most horrible day, commencing in the m'f with Snow + sleet and ending at night about 9 o'clk with thunder + lightening + torrents of Rain. Got to studio about 9.45 after calling on Wyant. Set to work blocking out my academy pic. first on a canvas 24×40 — then on a large piece of crayon paper. Almost driven from work by the cold + by the coal gas driven out of the stove. Walked home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — worked until 4.30 — then gave up — Home to tea about 6 — Evel W. M. E. Gar came + with him I went to Steinway Hall to hear Prof Cook of Harvard lecture on the Spectroscope — The lecture was very interesting, but his experiments were unfortunate — Got home shortly after 10 + got to bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 4 1869.

A very cold blustering day — got to studio at 9.30 + set to work on charcoal drawing 24×40 — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Drew on cartoon, but listlessly, feeling tired — about 4.30 stretched myself on the lounge + slept for an hour — Home to tea at 6.30 — Mother home from uncle Aaron's. Fan M. at the studio about 10 o'clk a.m. — at 8.30 P.M. Matt, Geo. Kate. Tom. Hooker + I went to the N.A. of D. to the Reception. Had a very pleasant time, seeing many friends — Got home at 11.15 and Right to bed
