## Left Page

### April, Saturday, 10, 1869

Beautiful day — got to studio at 8.20 — worked all the mg on Pat's subject — Charcoal drawing, am disappointed in not being able to get what I had hoped in the way of general effect — Home to lunch ab't — back at 2 — Afternoon pasted up & prepared drawing to go to Phila — worked again on Charcoal drawing and outlined a Reduction of it on 9×12 in. Canvas — Home at 5 o'clk. P.M — Tea at 6 & at 6.45 at Academy of Music. Had a good to[ne] & enjoyed the Concert — Home at 10.30 & got to bed about 11.15

## Right Page
