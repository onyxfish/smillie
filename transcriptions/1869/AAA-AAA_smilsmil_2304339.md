## Left Page

### July, Sunday 11, 1869

Insufferably hot day —
I surrendered unconditionally — Did not get up
until 10.30 — Heavy headache — Lay about the house
all day in full undress uniform — About 9.30
P.M. got cooled off — The wrote to Emma [illegible]
and a short note to
Jenny Tweedy — Got to
bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### July, Monday 12, 1869

A delightful day as compared
with yesterday — Burnished an
ausable plate (15 day) until
near 10 o'clk — then went to Pate's &
[illegible] — then to A.B.N. Co. — Saw
about the Ruling in Geo's plate &
filled in my plate with sealing wax
laid a ground all over it — at 2 o'clk
went out to lunch — then to Continental Jw. Co. & saw Mr. [illegible]
Smith who insists upon my
taking out a $10 wo endowment
policy — Ret'd & Ruled in blue
on my plate — Got 4 & 5 Ret'd
home — Tried to etch over the
lines filled in with wax — was
disappointed — Wax chipped
out & I could not use my point
freely — My Ruling proved to
have a bad line in it so that I
could not use it — Evg until
12 o'clk, grinding away at
some Raw Sienna — some in
oil — some in water —
