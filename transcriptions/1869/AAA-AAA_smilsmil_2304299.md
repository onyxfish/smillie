## Left Page

### April Tuesday, 20 1869.

Very close warm day with strong wind blowing from S.E. with a chill in it — a Regular Rheumatic sort of weather — Got to Studio at 8.45 and soon to work on 9×12 for Pate (2d. day) worked until 1 oclt, then home to lunch — at 2 down to Uncle Geo's & at 3 back to Studio — Painted away, but much bothered with foreground which at last, I changed Radically from my Charcoal drawing — Home to tea at 6.30 — Evef. wrote to B. F.W. Lewis — Rain commenced shortly after 9 & I got to bed about 10 —

We heard to-day that Helen, one of the girls that Uncle Wille adopted, died of Congestion of the brain —

## Right Page

### April Wednesday, 21 1869.

Cleared between 8 & 9 — a m — to a beautiful day — Got to Studio at 9.10 & commenced painting on 12×12½ — the River Ausable Scene (4th day) Repainted the sky and middle passage of mts — Miss Dewy came in the [illegible] & young Pell in the afternoon — so that I had a pretty busy day — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 every — home to tea at 6.30 — Spent the evef. writing to Jos[illegible] and to Mrs. Mr. Mann — Got to bed about 10.30 —
