## Left Page

### February Thursday, 25, 1869

Cool, clondless day — Got to Studio at 9.15 \+ worked all day repainting an old picture, that I made 2 yrs ago for Tracy Harri 14×18 in "a glimpse of Lake Mahkeenac." I have changed entirely the composition \+ have painted solidly over the whole thing, I hope now that I can work something creditable out of it. Home to lunch at 1. back at 2 \+ worked as long as I coned see — Home to tea at 6.30. \+ the evng until near 11 diligently engaged preparing Circulars for Dr Lewig's Phila Water Color Exbn Thoroughly tired out I got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February Friday, 26, 1869

The same old sort again — Snow \+ sleet \+ sleety — Got to Studio at 9.40, after stopping at [illegible]'s \+ other studios with Phil. Water-color Circulars — Miss Dewy spent an hour at the Studio — I spent an hour of a picture of "Storm on the Mts" 14×20 that I painted nearly two yrs ago, I gave it some glazing of blue \+ black, taking the brown off of it \+ bettering the whole thing I think — then gave an hour to Miss Sterling \+ Mrs Tobes — there worked on a water color sketch \+ at 2 o'clk home to lunch — at 2.15. Rode to Phil. Rehearsal with Mr. Rogers Griffiths — enjoyed Rehearsal — after that went to a lot of the Studios. with Phila circulars — home at 6.30 evng until 11 working on a drawing of Arkansas Arms for A.B.N.Co — then to bed —
