## Left Page

### November, Thursday, 4, 1869

another charming day — up late & got to studio at 9.45.
M'F spent morning with chalk on large pencil stud
— stretching & fixing all stud of M'rs McIntyre & Colden
& working on drawing of [illegible]
Mills — Meta Adams & a [illegible]
Morse called shortly after noon
Home to lunch at 1.45 back
at 2.45 — On the way met [illegible]
Fessenden & walked with her
to — Sane's — No. 8 W. 29 8th — at [illegible]
Studio worked again on
drawing (3½ days) Home
to tea before 6 — At 7.30
went with Matt to Syrie
Hall to see Some Tableaux
Vivants — Some good things
much patient waiting and
a little vivant — Home at
10.30 and right to bed
