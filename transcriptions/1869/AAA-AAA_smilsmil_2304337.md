## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 7, 1869

Charming day, but got up with a miserable headache, so that I could not bite in my etching as I had intended. At 10 I got to studio — Took Geo's plate \+ went down to Pate's \+ got the first proof. (13th day) — Stopped at tin man's \+ got new water color \+ then to A. B. N. Co. — had a ground laid to Rule sky — lunch — then Ruled in successfully the blue — Detained by W. Javieb \+ Van [illegible] who are determined that I shall make them a drawing, altho' I have no time — Ret'd. to studio a little before 5 — Varnished plate \+ home at 5.30 \+ to bed, used up at last — up at 8 \+ took some Tea — Spent the Rest of eve'g until 10 looking over [illegible] Ill. papers, then to bed — Willie Cumming [illegible] arr'd this p.m. on a visit and stayed over night —

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 8, 1869

Pleasant day, almost cool, with a strong breeze from S.E. — got to studio at 8.50 \+ set to work biting etching of Ausable Lake (13th day) Every thing went nicely — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45 — father spent the aft. in Central Park with Willie Cumming \+ the p.m. sitting for portrait to Augero — W. W. Hegeman the druggist sent us an invitation to go \+ me to spend next Sunday with him at Long Branch — could not do it — Worked at my biting until 7.30 — Result very much to my satisfaction — Home to tea at 8 o'clk — Until 9 Resting — letter from Matt — wrote awhile \+ to bed at 11
