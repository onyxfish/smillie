## Left Page

### December Saturday, 4 1869.

In bed & in misery until 2 o'clk. P.M. —
when at last I got up as weak & feeling as
broken down as though I had been in bed for
a month — only able to sit up in a Rocking
chair, giddy & light of head — This is a fit
culmination of my month vacation & woriment —

Mother Rec'd this P.M. from Manhattanville.
She Reports [illegible] improving

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 5 1869.

miserable, sleety, dismal day — Up about 9, not feeling
weak in the knees & light in the head. Took a nitric acid
bath & felt much better (by the way, a large quantity of water
with the nitric acid). About 12 o'clk, with Charl, started
for Uncle Aaron's via 3rd Ave R.R.
by hack to his house, then about 1 clk — Dinner, George taking
Turkey (sparingly) with us &
altogether appearing much better — at 10 o'clk, Charl
& I got off to bed.
