## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 12, 1869

Cloudless & very warm — Ther.
in Studio 82° — Got to studio
at 9 o'clk and at work on W[illegible]
Point drawing — (8th day) — went
over it generally, trying to
add color & give it breadth —
both of which it lacks — It seems
to me heavy — but at present
I cannot see how to remedy it.
Home to lunch at 1 — back it —
worked on a water color sketch
that I am making for [Sauthier?] —
About 4 o'clk Maud & Ella D —
came & took us Riding in the
park until 6 o'clk — Enjoyed
it exceedingly — At 8.30 with
G[illegible] called on the Tweedy's —
Jenny J — there — also two old
batchelors, older, that is, than I
am — There until 10.45 — walked
home & got to bed about 11.45.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 13, 1869

Tonight mf[illegible] but overcast by 10 o'clk
— brisk Rain between 2 & 4 o'clk —
Got to studio at 8.30 — Soon after
getting there Mr. Geo. Whitney
called — Set to work on 6×10 in
oil sketch " White Mts. from White
Horse Ledge — N. Conway" — for [illegible]
[illegible] — Home to lunch at 1.30
back at 2.30 & worked until
after 6 when I finished the sketch.
Mr. Whitney called again at 4 o'clk
& Kingsley, about 6 — All day
the N.A. of D — Academicians and
[illegible]s have been buzzing about
over what was done yesterday.
Unfortunately, as it seems to me
most of it is petty personality
& bad blood — Home to tea
at 7 o'clk — Spent the evening
lazily with the girls & got
to bed about 10 o'clk.
