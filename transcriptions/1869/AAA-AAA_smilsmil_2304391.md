## Left Page

### October Saturday, 23 1869.

Dark & threatening, Rain
beginning to fall about 10 AM
& continuing heavily all day.
Spent the am't at Studio
stretching Canvas & planning
subjects until 1 o'clk. when
I went home to lunch, Ret-
urning about 2.30 - Afternoon
working with Charcoal - I
have determined to paint
a number of small pictures
varying in size from 7×12
to 16×26, in. based upon
my Summer sketches, hoping
to find a sale for them —
Even't at home Reading as
much of the Iliad as I could
with Ollie. I'm ready, Tolt &
Matt at the table with me.
Got to bed shortly after 10.

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 24 1869.

Sunday, a superb day —
M'f went to Dr. Hastings,
heard him preach a very
earnest, energetic Sermon
from Rev. 22°. 17th V. — After
service, with Charl, went
up to Uncle Caron's, dined
at 2 o'clk — Spent the P.M.
wandering over the place
until 4 o'clk went I went
along the R.R. track to the
Iron Smelting furnace —
by having an Irishman I had
a guide over the entire estab-
lishment was much interested
until 6 o'clk — when I Returned
to Uncle C's — late for tea —
Even't. chatting with Miss
Porter until 10 o'clk — when
Charl & I went to bed —
