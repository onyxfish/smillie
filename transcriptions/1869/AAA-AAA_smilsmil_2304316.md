## Left Page

### May Wednesday, 26 1869.

Clear day + very hot. Ther. 88° in the
studio. Got there at 9 o'clk and
set to work on Patis 9×12 — Ausable
Lake (5th day) Scumbling + thin-
painting over the sky + distances
+ repainting down over the fore-
ground — It is now more as I want
it in light + shade but I cannot
do what I wish in color. — At
12 o'clk went to Brooks Bros. Paid
bill $7 44 + ordered a summer
suit $50.00. Home to lunch at 1.
back at 2.15. worked on 9×12
until 4 — Then painted two little
sketches on the Camp Tweedie big
wooden spoon — at 5 heavy thunder
storm came up. Miss Nellie [illegible]
sat at my window until 6. Then put
her in an omnibus — Home at 7.15
at 8, with Charl + Miles Palmer
went up to Central Park [illegible]
+ heard Thomas? Orchestra —
Home at 10.30 + to bed at
11.45

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 27 1869.

Overcast day, chilly + threatening
rain in the mfg. All day fight-ing
a headache — got to studio at
9.20 — getting things ready to begin
the etching of Patis plates — went
down-town to buy hand-vice, and
did several messages, getting back
to studios at 12.20. Fixed up my
corner by the window, swept
up + home to lunch at 1.15. After
lunch, slept until 3 — then went
to studio + spent rest of p.m.
fixing table, blind + laying
grounds with which I had con-
siderable trouble. Did not get
home until 7.10 — Sort settled
myself for reading, but the
girls had callers, so I had
to beat a retreat — Got to
bed about 10.30
