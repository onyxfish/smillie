## Left Page

### February Saturday, 13 1869.

Delicious, mild \+ spring-like — got to studio at 9 o'clk. and in a short time finished outlining on canvas "On the Boquet." then set to work on the charcoal cartoon — just before 1 o'clk Williams, the fossil, of Eliz-town mum came in, as black \+ polished as ever — Home to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.15 Found Mr. Griffits \+ sister Miss Rogers at studio — had a very chatty, pleasant call — Then Mr. Forbes came in and asked me to Ride in the Park with her — Just as the horses were at 24.St. one kicked up, fell \+ broke the pole — so our Ride was given up — Fau. M. came about 4 — Then Mr. Hegeman — at 4.30 with Fau. Rode down to Putnam's. Saw him — then to Snedecor's — then Rode home with her. Home to tea at 6 — evening writing to S. C. F. who has sent me as present of 3 handsome hokfs. \+ doing other little things — got to bed after 11 o'clk —

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 14 1869.

A change from yesterday, cloudy \+ strong wind from the N.W. After a Royal, home-made Turkish bath went to Dr. Thes. Hastings' \+ heard him preach one of his characteristic sermons from II Peter, 1st ch. 10 \+ 11 v. After service, Rode up to Uncle A's. got to his house about 1.15, had a quiet dinner at 2 — Feeling sleepy about 3.30 I stretched myself on the lounge in the library \+ enjoyed a good nap until 5.30 Tea at about 6 — Miss Porter very agreeable — Shortly after 7 started on 8th Ave. Cars — very dark \+ beginning to Rain — got over all Right and down to 41 W. 48th St. at 8.10 — Spent the Rest of the evng. with Fau. — Home at 10.30 — then Raining heavily — got to bed about 11.30 Tremendous Storm — wind \+ Rain all night.
