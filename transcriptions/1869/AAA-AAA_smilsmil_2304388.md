## Left Page

### October, Sunday, 17, 1869

Beautiful day, but still
breezy & cool — Myself went to
Dr. Hastings's & heard him preach
an excellent sermon from
1st Peter — V Chap 7th v. "Casting
all your Care upon him, for
he Careth for you" — After
service walked for half an hour
until dinner time — Dinner at
home — about 2.30 P.M. walked
up to Central Park & there
until 5.30 — Beautiful sunset
& charming moonlight night —
Home to tea about 6 — feeling
under the weather — did not
go out but until 9.30 with
Grd. & chatting with Mary
and the Dr. — I got to bed
about 10.20.

## Right Page

### October, Monday, 18, 1869

Clear cool mfg., clouding
over about noon — cold, bluster-
ing & cheerless — Evely brilliant
moonlight — got to Studio at
9 oclt & spent the morning
washing things up, so that now,
at last, I have things nearly
arranged — Home to lunch
abt oclt — Back at 2.30 and
spent p.m. arranging new
design for the Jay Cooke & Co.
Pacific Railway Bonds —
Home to tea at 6 — Evely
Charl went with Matt, Kate
Miss Parker down to Dan Bryant's —
Mr. & Mrs. Mulford called from
9.5 to 9.45 — I stretched paper
for drawing, & fussed with
some of my studies — got
to bed abt 10.15
