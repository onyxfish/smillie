## Left Page

### January, Thursday, 14, 1869

Pleasant day \- got to studio at 8.45. Found a spicy letter from Mr. Wm. H. Willis on Water Color business \- answered it \- Mr. Jno. E. Gavit came in \+ spent an hour or more getting a slight sketch from me of a Poetry subject. Worked on my drawing on the dark foliage in the foreground \- Home to lunch at 1.30, back at 2.30 \+ worked steadily until 4.45 \- Then home to tea at 6 \- wrote a short letter to Jos A. Clay. Phil\. Water Color bis having been to the N.A. of D. and finding that his drawings had arr\. Safe\. About 8.30 the Treedy's came \- the father, 3 daughters, one son \+ a nephew \- Every thing seemed to go very pleasantly \- got to bed a few minutes after 12 \-

## Right Page

### January, Friday, 15, 1869

miserable weather \- a good part of the day it has been Raining dismally \- Up with threatening of a headache, but by judicious management, got a days work done \- altho' I'm afraid my work suffered from my feeling out of sorts \- got to studio at 8.30 \+ set to work on foreground Rocks. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- worked until 4.30 \- when I could see no longer \- My work for the day is very dead \+ colorless, I regret it, as I know just what it ought to have been \- I hope to Remedy it to some extent, to-morrow \- Home at 6.30 \- Well \- pretty well used up \- so did nothing \- but mounted Georgi's W.C. drawing for him \- Jno. E. Gavit came in to see me about drawing a design for a Seal for Poetry ass\. \[illegible\] \- went away at 10 \+ I went to bed \-
