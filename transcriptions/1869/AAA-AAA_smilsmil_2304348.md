## Left Page

### July, Thursday, 29, 1869

Mrng — opened with a fine thunder storm — I was just
able to sit up long enough to complete burnishing —
then George took the plate down to Fate + had it proved
from there he took it to the A.B.N. Co. — where William laid
a surprising Rebiting ground over all of it — I was unfit
for anything + was obliged to keep on my back — very
quiet — The night passed with but two or three dis-
turbances —

## Right Page

Feel somewhat better to-day.
Father insists upon staying home + working for me on
