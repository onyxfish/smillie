## Left Page

### November Saturday, 20 1869.

Quite a hurricane during the night. Mf cleared off into a charming day — got to studio at 9.30 & started painting a 12×22" view of Indian Pass from Scott's — [illegible] & part mts. in when at 1 o'clk I went home to lunch — met Kingsley on the way — back at 2.20 and worked until near 4 when Madame Feldmar came & gave me a lesson in French. Home to tea about nt 5.30. Deacon Phelps called in the evef & gave some encouragement to hope that he will purchase my "Moat Mt." Got to bed about 10 o'clk —
Uncle W. & [illegible] are visiting with us —

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 21 1869.

Up late with a miserable headache — Beautiful, bright Mf but as is usual now, became clouded over & became raw & blustering. I went to the 23d Baptist Ch. expecting to hear Pentacost of Covington Ky — heard instead Mr. [illegible] of Dr. — walked home all the P.M. in bed with my headache — got up at nt 7.30 — took some [illegible] & spent the rest of the evef in my big chair — to bed again at about 11 o'clk —
