## Left Page

### January, Friday 22, 1869

Cold \+ part of the day overcast. went up to Gavit's with drawing of medals. Got to Studio at 9.40 mess'r Gil Whitney, Enoch Lewis \- Gil Wood \+ Justice called about 10 or 10.30 \+ made a very pleasant visit until near 12 \- Then S.G? came in, after sitting awhile \- she went to see her \+ Tom's wife, promising to be back at 1 to go to the N.A. of D. with one \+ then to Phil. Rehearsal. I saw nothing more of her after that. Took lunch at the Studio \- worked until 2.25 \- then went to Rehearsal \- after that \- called at N.A. of D. then to Studio's \- then to Emma \+ Clauds with an Academy ticket for her. Home to tea at 6.30 \- Evell spent writing to Van, Ingram and Alfred Jones \- Rec'd to-day from Ingersoll $3.00 \- Got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 23, 1869

Cold clear day \- Got to Studio at 9.15. Got out my painting of the Saco. 20×36 in \- Scraped the sky \+ pumice-stoned it, preparatory to working over it \- Spent about an hour touching on a die proof for father \- Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2 \- Miss Conmne Coles called \- after her Mr. Mr. Raynor \+ Miss Raynor \- my afternoon pretty well lost by visitors \- Dau. Gavit called about Chick in drawing \- made drawings for Monogram for Miss Wilder \- at 6. went to Fanny Kimball's to give her ticket for Water Color exhibit. Home to tea at 6.30 \- All went out to the Rivard Musical Soiree \- but feeling tired, enjoyed my warm fire at home \- wrote a letter to Joseph Gavit \- Got to bed about Midnight.
