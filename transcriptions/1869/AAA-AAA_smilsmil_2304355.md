## Left Page

### August Thursday, 12 1869.

Very warm day — up at 6 [illegible] and at work soon after biting in cross line over the foliage on Ausable [plate?] at 1015 went down to Pats \+ from him got [illegible] that gave me much encouragement also $1504 on acct. which was pleasant — went to a[t] N. C. 130 \+ left plate with Father — lunched \+ until 4.300 doing up messages, buying Knapsack, Rubber Blanket, Cardigan — Cuffs — Colors, groceries etc. etc. — at Home set to work lining my trunk, which I did not finish until midnight when I got to bed thoroughly tired out —

## Right Page

### August Friday, 13 1869.

Overcast m[uch] with threats. Rain \+ not clear \+ hot the after noon — Will \+ Joe got off [to?] Manchester at 6.30 a.m. — I worked as tight as I could drive getting things together \+ packing my trunk until 130 — lunched \+ at 2 lay down for an hour to rush — Shortly after 4 a heavy storm came up that lasted until after 5 — At 5 I left the house and reached Str[ea]m[er] "Drew" at 540.
