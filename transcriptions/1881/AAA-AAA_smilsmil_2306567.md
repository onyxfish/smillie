## Left Page

### April, Thursday 28. 1881.

Overcast with, but before noon
it cleared to be cloudless and
hot. To work in Osgood etching
— after some dry; painting, Took
[illegible]. then determined to rebite.
Laid ground — stopped out &
went to lunch — Annie called
for a moment just as I was
leaving — after lunch sauntered
in Mad. Sq. for a time, then
to studios & rebite. At 5.30
went to 37th S. dressed — & at 6.30
at Annie's — dinner — then
to Old Union League Theatre, where
the Choral Club gave its last
concert for this season — home
with Annie & her mother at
10.30 — then to 37th S. & to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 29. 1881.

Overcast with some rain until
about 11 when it cleared charmingly.
At 9.30 met Annie — Minnie, Miss
Williams & Haut & W. & Mrs. Bowman
& went with them in to Frank
Millett's to see the costumes that
he's getting up for the Greek play
that is to be acted at Harvard
next month — then the ladies came
up to my room — when they left
I took impressions from the Osgood
plate — Dinner at 2 — Walker
called — P.m. — I worked on S.A.
architectural drawing for A.B.N.
& a saunter in Mad. Sq. then
at 7 tea in studio. Near 9 when
I got to Annie's — there until
after 10 — then to 37th S. & to bed.
