## Left Page

### February, Monday 7, 1881.

Mild & delightful day — Got to Studio about 10 o'clk — to work & finished painting on Cathedral Rocks — Had my lunch in Studio at 1.30 then out for a walk — Back & worked until after 5 — then to 37' S. dressed & to 38' S. to dinner — after that Geo. C. Emma J. Annie & went to Booth's Theatre to see Salvini in Othello — It was a savage sight — I was on the whole, very much displeased & having seen such a display of morbid emotion — diseased mind. I felt very much as I have after visiting an Anatomical Museum with its horribly real looking representations of hideous sores & such horrors. He must have studied his death throes in an Abattoir — Out about 11.50 — 12.45 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 8, 1881.

Still mild and overcast — Got to Studio about 9.30 and until 12 was writing letters. Then to work on Twilight effect x that I painted last Spring — I managed to get the sky painted but was not in humor to do much — At 4.45 went to 37' S. at 6, to Annie's & with her & Emma went to Fannie's — Was not invited but went — Many Millard, Geo. Cook & his mother were also there — Had a game of letters — 11 when we got to 38' S. 11.30 when I got to bed.
