## Left Page

### December, Thursday 8, 1881

Charming clear crisp day. Got to studio at 9.45 & to work painting on "Adirondacks" — made a great improvement by painting out much black in the cliff in L.h. middle ground. Rec'd a letter from editor of "The Etcher" — London asking me to contribute an etching. Home to dinner at 1.30 — Matt & Kate gone to Fanning K's — Emma J. to dinner with us — Back to studio at 3.15 — more painting. Skinner, of Brooklyn Baker, gave me two seats to Opera tomorrow night — Thel Wilson called — At 5.30 went to Rennie's — Home at 6.30 — Tea — made a note to Dr. Adams of Ridgefield. Took Annie to her club — then to Century — then to studio — & at 9.30 went for Annie — Home & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 9, 1881

Overcast at times but not unpleasant — Got to studio soon after 10 — having waited to write a note to Dr. Scheveder about Phil. Seats — Spent the mf over Montrose house matters — writing to Cottrell etc. — Home to dinner at 1. To Phil. Rehearsal at 2 — Annie did not go — gave her ticket to Matt — Kate sat in my seat. At 4 — (after Rehear) went to J.W. Bouton's — then to Dewey's — then to Wunderlich's then home — Tea at 6.30 — Evg. — Annie & I went to Opera (tickets given by Skinner of Brooklyn) & heard Mephistofell — can't say that we enjoyed it — both of us had headaches & we left at 10.30, at the end of the 3d act — Right home & to bed.
