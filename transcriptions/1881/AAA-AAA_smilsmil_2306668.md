## Left Page

### November, Friday 18, 1881

Overcast & tried with some rain about 1 o'clk — After [illegible] went to Pereira's shop — then to studio — There during the rest of the [illegible]., most of the time writing letters concerning Montrose [illegible]. Sent checks to Cottrell & Cooper & Co — To dinner at 1 — Headache P.m. — went with Cuisier to Rug sale at Deavites & bought seven Rugs for $70 — Then to studio. S.R. Koehler there. Home at 5.30 — About 7 Annie, her mother, Geo. Valentine & I went to Walter's to dinner to celebrate his 5th wedding anniversary — Will & Fanny [illegible], Mary Millard, Aunt Anna and Ned C — the other guests — a very pleasant affair but my headache got the better of me & soon after 10 I excused myself & went home alone to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 19, 1881

[illegible] threatening
Pleasant day. Got up in the [illegible], after a good night's rest feeling quite relieved — got to studio about 11, after going to Deavite's & settling for Rugs. Met S.W. Perry there — Rugs came to studio before 1 o'clk — Those who have seen them admire them & think them cheap — To dinner about 1.30 — threats of Rain — a shower — Mother Cook went to Ridgefield at 3 o'clk — I went to depot with her — then to studio — Rain set in before 6 — Home to tea at 6.30 — Suey Annie read some aloud & I worked awhile on my wiles — To bed before 11.
