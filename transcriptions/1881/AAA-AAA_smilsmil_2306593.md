## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 21. 1881.

Overcast & pleasantely cool. To studio's before 10 & to work on my etching — scratching & biting alternately — Dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon cleaned my plate & got a couple of impressions. Disappoints me by looking too much all of one color — Tea at 7 o'clk & evening spent listening to H. Read & play on the piano. To bed before 11.

At 5 o'clk. went to Leavitt's & met Annie — Auction of Rugs. She bought one handsome one — ordered it sent to studio.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 22. 1881.

Charming, cool day — Got to studio's before 10 & to work on etching — cooling down the sky, dry pointing & burnishing about in places and taking off between times. Home to dinner at 1.30. Emma Ireland Margaret Parker White there to dinner — After 3 when I got to studio — More impressions — find the difference between sky & foreground lines greater than at first appeared — a little rubbing with charcoal reduces the sky line without appearing to affect the larger lines running across it. Home to tea at 7 — Evening spent loitering about. To bed before 11.
