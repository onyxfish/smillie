## Left Page

### August, Sunday 14, 1881

Delightfully breezy cool day —
after bkfs. at 8, went to studio
& wrote a letter — then went to
to Presb'n Ch. & heard a very good
discourse from Rev. W. Munger —
again to studios & wrote a letter
to R.S. Gifford — then to dinner
at 3 — after that a short nap &
at 3 to studios — wrote again.
Then out for a walk — tea at 6 —
Eve'g wandering about & reading
until about 10. Home to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 15, 1881

Superb day — after bkfs. went
to Mill & saw cornices for house etc
then to stables & got buggy & horses
& started out with Annie for a
days lark. We drove to J.C. Morris
beyond Friendsville, getting there
at 1.20 — too late for their dinner, but
Mrs. M. got up a little dinner for us.
Young Woolsey Carmalt & sister there.
After dinner we went out to see a
herd of 15 Shetland Ponies — about
4 we drove to Jas. Carmalt's at Lake
Side — Woolsey riding horseback as
a pilot — Very charming at Lake Side
& cordially rec'd. by Jas. C. his wife &
sister — there until about 5.40 then
left to M. by way of Silver Lake
& had a delightful ride getting
in at 8.30 — we ate a lunch by the
way & Mrs. Courtright gave us a
cup of tea at 9 o'clk. — To bed
abt 10.
