## Left Page

### December, Saturday 24. 1881.

Clear, crisp minring day —
got to studio at 9.30 — finished
"Hundred Islands. Lake Georgi
& mounted it — also finished
small sketch for Kate. Home
to dinner at 1 — back at 2.30
& finished "Moonlight & Snow"
Then prepared a Xmas present
consisting of old postage stamps
for Fanny N—. Too late to go
to Ned Costa's Christmas Tree.
Home at 5.30 — then out again
& walked up 5th ave. to 50th S.
then down & over to Rennie's
& home at 6.40. Tea. Then
on a sudden thought A. &
I went to Booth's Theatre —
got first rate seats & saw
the Vokes family in "Cousin
Joe" (very neat) & "Belles of the
Kitchen." — Found Victoria V.
amusing in her exuberance of
animal life — an excellent
dancer — as is Fred. V— Home
at 11 — to bed before 12. —
locked out but got in without trou[ble]

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 25. 1881.

Another charming, cloudless
day. B[illegible] at 9 — at 10.30 went
to the Anthon Memorial Ch. in
48th St — an extensive programme
of Xmas music, an orchestra of 13
instruments added to organ and
choir — my enjoyment was not
very lively — nor was I very much
impressed by the sermon of Rev.
Heber Newton — After service I
walked up to the Park, through
it & out at 72nd St. & 8th ave. — took
elevated R.R. to 42nd St. & 6th ave. &
home to dinner at 2 — After that
a nap & at 4.45 to studio where
I wrote until near 6 — then up
to J. C. Detolf & mailed a letter
that Annie has written to Mother.
Then home — tea at 7 — Evening,
some writing & reading. To
bed at 10.30.
