## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 23, 1881

Blustering, March-like day — after bkfst went to Anthony's for Acid trays — none — then to Rubber Co. 11 Mercer S. — They sent me to Schoville Man'l Co. in Broome S. where I got what I wanted & reached studio about 11 o'clk. — Lost no time in getting my plate in the acid — it went like a house afire — lunch about 2 — Cleaned my plate at about 4 — & at 4.30 went to Stewarts & met Annie — then to Nicol, & Co's & looked at curtainings — & chair coverings — then to Syphers — then to 38 S. — After dinner Annie & I went to Walter's with our plans for Montrose house & consulted with him until 10.30 — then home — I got to bed about 11.50.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 24, 1881

Bkfst at Annie's. Coleman met us at new house in 38 S. & talked of curtains. March like day of breezy sunlight & shadows & drifting plumes of almost showers. Got to studio about 9.30 & spent the day working over etching after Bridgeman, preparing to bake first pls. To lunch at 1.30 — after that to Wunderlich's with some etching pls — then again to studio & worked until 4 — at 4.30 to Beck's, Cor. 7th Ave & 29th S. & met Annie — spent some time looking over wall papers — then to Warren's in 42d S. & selected papers there. At 6.20 to 38th S. — Geo. Fanny, Emma & Grace J. there to dinner — then to Singing Club — then to Bridgeman Redpts. — at Sarony's — there until near 10, then to Century — there until 10.20 — then to Singing Club & home with Annie — got to bed with a mean headache at about 11.30
