## Left Page

### March, Friday 25, 1881

Charming day out of doors — not
so to me, who was in bed all
day with an atrocious headache.
At 11 this mntf. Annie & her
mother went to Cambridge.
mass, to visit for a few days at
Will Cook's — I got up at 6
P.M. The girls gave me a cup
of tea & at 7 I went to studio —
Coleman, Bellows & [illegible]
there, to talk over details of
Osgood's Itchuing book — all
away before 10, but I lay upon
lounge until 11, then to
37 S. & to bed at 12 — to
sleep between 1 & 2, a.m.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 26, 1881

Overcast and threatening —
a ~~charming day overhead~~, but
cold & blustering — Got to studio
about 9.30 & to work preparing
for Faking first pfo. from my
Bridgman Etching — At last
got at the printing — the line
does not need as much
color as I thought — To lunch
at 1.30 — Printed until after
5 — Tired out — but had taken
only 9 pfs — all told — then
washed up & at 6 went to
[illegible]'s & had dinner — at
8 we went to Henrietta Beebe's
Farewell Concert at Chickering
Hall — very enjoyable — home
with S — then to studio — then
to 37 S. & to bed at midnight —

Gte. went to Po't at 4
o'clk this P.M.
