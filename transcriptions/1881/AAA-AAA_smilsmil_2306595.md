## Left Page

### June, Saturday 25, 1881

Ridgefield - conn.
Mr. D. Adams'

Very pleasant day — went with Annie to look at other tea sets & things for presents for Geo. & got to studio after 11 — worked over my pls. — prepared some to send to Koehler — also wrote him part of a letter — at t went to Nellie Jacobs & saw her for a few minutes on the subject of presents. Then home to dinner at 1.30 — At 3 took Train for Ridgefield. Conn. Reached there at about 5:15. had a short ride before tea, altho' it was overcast & somewhat threatening — tea at 6.30 — Pretty chatting until 10.30 — then to bed —
After bkft. called on Dr. [illegible] but he was out.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 26, 1881

Overcast mtf., Bkft. at 8.30 about 10.30 went out to ride — Annie, her Sister (Mrs. A.) & the two boys. Drove out to Peach Lake & back through North Salem a thunder storm threatened it for some time we had a race with it. It caught us about 3 m. before we got in, while we were within sight of the house. It poured tremendously but we did not get wet. Dinner after 2 — storm cleared away — had a nap — tea at 6.30 — after that had a walk with A. on the Ridge — when we ret. had a call from Mr. & Mrs. Whiting — then out to see the comet. To bed soon after 10.
