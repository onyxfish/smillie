## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 24. 1881.

Charming day, but very smoky.
Annie went off on a morning
spree with a party, inspecting
the Crausdale factory. I spent
the mrf. inspecting drains —
making a corrected plan
of the basement floor and
writing to Kimmel & Naigh & &
father. Dinner at 1 — after
that worked upon basement
plan & bossed matters out-
side — tea at 6 — After that
dressed, then called upon
Alice Strond — found that she
& Miss Sherroe had gone to [illegible]
shore this mrf. — then to Presb.
Church & saw Mr. Van Wormen &
Miss Maria Lyons married —
then to Wm. Park's & played a
couple of games of whist — to
our quarters & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 25. 1881.

Hot, dry day — still very smoky.
most of the mrf in Studio at
work on Revised plan for base-
ment — To P.O. at noon — dinner
soon after — again a work upon
plans until about 5 — then to
mills & saw Cotrees — then to
P.O. & to tea at 6.30 — Rueff
& a & I went to W. J. S's — he was
out of sorts — we sat upon piazza
until near 8.30 — then back to
Courtright's & a. & C. ago — Flora &
& I played a game of whist —
then a. & I took Miss Alice Foster
home — back & to bed at 10 —
