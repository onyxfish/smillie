## Left Page

### March, Sunday 13, 1881

Dark dismal day — Bkf. —
at 9.30 — then to studio and
wrote letters until near noon.
Then to Annie's — Dinner
at 2.30 — About 4 we went
to Central Park & through to
66th St. & 2d Ave. & called upon
Fred Dreland & wife — then
back through the Park & home —
Tea before 7 — Heavy Rain —
Eve'g passed in discussing
plans — At 10.30 to 37 St. &
to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 14, 1881

Dark mtf. — but about noon
the weather cleared charmingly.
After bkfs. I went to Wunderlich's
& spent the mtf. there looking over
etchings — Selected a number
for my use — Then to Studio at
noon & prepared to transfer
Bridgeman tracing. Dinner
at 1.30 — Then made a very
successful transfer — got
all ready to start etching
& at 4.30 went up to Dicken's
& out on Charle's horse — Had
a delightful ride — met Chal.
Holt & Saml. Colman in the
Park — To 37 St. soon after &
had my scrub — To Studio
at 9.15 & had tea — wrote
several notes & at 9.15 got
to Annie's — there until
10.30 — then to 37 St. & to
bed.
