## Left Page

### January, Thursday 6, 1881

Nasty, dirty day — sleet storm all night, slush all day — Got to Studio about 9.30 + after several interruptions + delays got to work trying to wash mat of frame for "Old Trees" into harmony — also, finished pen sketch for W.C. Catalogue for Miss Jacobs — To lunch at 2 — The afternoon spent working on "View near Portsmouth". At 5.30 went to 37th St + dressed + at 6 went to Annie's + took her to the K's in 22nd S. Lovely evening. — had tea — + then to Jno. T. Johnston's — there to Berkeley Cor. 9th S. + 5th Ave. + called on the Ehningers — About 10 2. + I went to Twelfth night celebration at Cantering — Pandemonium — as my head hurt me I left before 11 — went to 37th S. + to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 7, 1881

Charming day but I was in bed all day with a tantalizing headache. — I was most anxious to be at Studio to get watercolors ready for cantering but had just head ache enough to keep me in bed. — Got up at noon but had to give up + got up again about 4 + went to Studio — fixed something for myself to eat soon after 5 — was the only one working over my drawings — got three of them ready after a fashion — to 37th S. about 11 + to bed.
