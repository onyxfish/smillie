## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 9, 1881

Dismal Rain — got to Studio at 9.30 — Still house cleaning at Studio — preparing to begin my Bridgeman Etching — went to see Kursty about Kate's pictures — lunch about 2 — & at about 3 in a pouring rain went to 106 Mad. Ave — got Father & Mother into a coupé — then to R. R. depot & got them off for Po'keepsie in the 4 train — then to Studio — at 6.30 at Annie's — Kate & Matt making a little visit there — Spent the evng drawing plans for Montrose house — To 37th St at 10.30 & to bed before 11 —

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 10, 1881

Still heavily overcast — got to Studio before 10 & managed to get a camera-lucida reduction of Bridgeman's Painting — find such work very trying to my eyes now — lunch at 1.30 — In the afternoon got my desk ready for work — at 6 — to 37th St and dressed — then to Annie's and had dinner — Matt & Kate still there — then to Singing class with Annie — then to Studio & opened box of Jap. Papers, just came & find less than a 10% of what I ordered — then to Mr. Rob. Gordon's Artists' Reception & had a very pleasant evng there met [illegible] home after 11 — then to 37th St — in slightly sleety rain, & to bed before midnight —
