## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 4, 1881.

Snow began falling during the day — Got to studio about 9.45 & spent the most of the time writing letters — 12 o'clk before I was ready to get at my work. Then commenced a sketch of Mariners well for W.C. Catalogue — To dinner about 2 o'clk — Little done during the remaining p.m. At 5 went to 37' S. & gave my self an exercising, currying & grooming — scrubbing & grooming generally. Then, at 6.30, to Annie's — dinner & at 8 were at Steinway Hall, Henschel's Second Recital — a charming every entertainment but missed Miss Bailey very much. Walked home in a heavy snow storm — went in for a few minutes, then to 37' S. and near midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 5, 1881.

Dull day & very mixed — Got to studio before 10 — worked awhile on Miss Abbott's picture — then at sketch d"Mariners well" for W.C. Catalogue. To dinner at 2 — after a walk again to studios & worked on "View near Ports mouth" until about 4 o'clk — then to Riding school with Coleman — Rode until near 6 — then to 37' S. & had a couple of minutes ahead of me — At 10.30 to 37' St. & to bed at 11. Sleet & Rain then commenced falling.
