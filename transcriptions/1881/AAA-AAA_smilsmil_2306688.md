## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 28, 1881

Still nasty, foggy, muggy and wet without rain — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on "Oct. near Saratoga" — worked until 1 o'clk — also made a new sketch for W. C. Cat — Home to dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.30 & worked until after 4 making progress that are, at any rate, interesting to me. About 5 started for 10th St. Studio bldg, stopping at Wunderlich's in 14th St — then on to 10th St. & left cat, sketch for Farren — Home at 6.20 — Tea near 7 — Annie & I alone — Spent part of eveg. looking over the portfolios & selections from Ill. Longfellow that Houghton, Mifflin & Co. presented to me — at 9.30 went around to Ned's for Mortu Cook — back & to bed about 10.45.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 29, 1881

Very dark day with heavy rain. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on "Oct" — but owing to the weather, did not make much headway — Home to dinner at 1.20 — back at 2.30 — Pouring torrents — could not do much — mounted little sketch for Kate — Home to tea at 6.30. Evening reading & writing to Cornele. To bed at 10.30
