## Left Page

### November, Monday 14, 1881

Very pleasant day — [illegible]
at 8.30 — near 10 when I went
to Reumer & Co's — here met
Ehninger & with him walked
to the studios — the mf spent
with callers & odds & ends —
To dinner at 1 o'clk — Half
past 2 before I started
to return to studio. There
all the afternoon — tea at
6.30 — Evelf, with Annie
went to Mr. Colmans when
we enjoyed Quartette playing until near 11 — Carroll
Dunham, Mr. C — Emma
J & Fanny K. playing.
Then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 15, 1881

Windy & dirty — most of the
mf at studio — about 1
went to Kimmel & [illegible] to
see how the printing of [illegible] from
the [illegible] plates was progressing —
from there to Dewey & Heatings
& from there home to dinner
Back to studio after 3 o'clk.
After 5 went to 47. 3? S. to
see Adolph Schst — not in —
Then up home to tea. Evelf
very quiet — dyspeptic fit —
Annie played on the piano
& read aloud — To bed at
11 o'clk.
