## Left Page

### November, Saturday 26, 1881

Very pleasant day — After
Bkfst, went to market, then
to Standard Theatre & bought
six tickets for next Friday
to see "Patience" — Then to
Jackson's & ordered flowers for
gratis at home — then, ordered
a load of oak wood & at 10.30
got to studio — worked a little
in water color — Miss Stowe
& some friends called — Home
to dinner at 1.30 — to studio
at 3 — made up pkg of W.C.
sketches & expressed them
to Cyrus F. Davis at Portland —
Home to tea at 6.30 — Self &
Annie reading aloud —
near 11 when we got to
bed.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 27, 1881

Charming day, altho' rather windy
& dirty — Bkfst before 9 — went
to West Presbyn Ch. (Dr. Hastings's)
& heard a stranger — no pastor
has yet been settled — Mr. Hastings
sat in the seat with me. After
service, walked up to Central
Park — Home at 1.30 — several
callers — Dinner at 2 — Ducks
shot by Walter a few days ago —
Valentine & Emma to dinner —
more callers until near 5 —
then I had a nap until 6 —
Did not feel like tea until
after 8 — when I took a light
meal — Annie read aloud
until near 10 — then I wrote
a letter to father. Took it
to G.C. Depot letter box — back
to bed after 11.
