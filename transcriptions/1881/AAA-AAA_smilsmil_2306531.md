## Left Page

### February, Sunday 13. 1881.

Cold, windy, march-like day — Bkf. at 9 — then to
Reina's & ordered a frame
— then to Dr Hastings's & heard
him preach — After service
walked down to 106 Mad. av
& saw Mother for a few minutes
then to studio & wrote a couple
of notes — then, at 2 — to Annie's
& had dinner — After that
Ned & Walter with their families
came in & Mr. Valentine —
the matter of moving & of
Building an apartment bed-
was discussed — After 4
Annie & I went out for a
walk — went to Mad. Ave.
& called on Father & Mother
again. To 38 S. at 6 — Tea
before 7 — Evening passed
in chatting — at 10.30 to 37
S. & at 11 to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 14. 1881.

Charming day — Got to
studio's between 9 & 10 & after
some delay got started upon
my 33 × 44 [illegible] — Painted
in the sky — out to lunch
at 1.30 — a little walk —
back & painted until after
4. At about 6 to 37 S. &
at 6.30 to 38th S. After dinner,
Annie, Minnie, Edith & I
went to Municipal Hall stopping
on our way for Kate, and heard
the final Rehearsal of Mendelssohn
Glee Club before their 3d concert
of the season — crowded room &
stifling — out at 10 — took Kate
home, then on to 38 S. — To
37 S. & to bed before 11.
