## Left Page

### December, Monday 12, 1881

Overcast day — Rain began to fall about 5 P.m. — Got to
Studio's about 9.30 & spent the m'f. drawing windows ashes
for Montrose House. — At 12 went to Bank — then to Knoedler's
& to Putnam's — Home to dinner at 1.15 — Back to studio's at
2.45 & again at work upon sashes — made a note of in-
structions to Cottrell — Went in to Coleman's & saw Mrs. C — Told
her that my head ached so that we would not be down
to her Piano Party as usual.
Then home — Tea near 7 —
feeling used up I was on lounge until 9 o'clk. Then
feeling better, did some writing & got to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 13, 1881

Overcast and mixed — Dark,
foggy m'f. After h'fp. went to Dr.
Mr. Thompson's — he advised me
to call again — and I'm afraid
that is all it will amount to. I
seem to be going down, physically.
Look to the future with much
anxiety. Stopped at Ferrie's shop
to see my frame — & at Newman's to
find locks suitable for Montrose Ho.
After 10 when I got to studio's — spent
rest of m'f. writing notes & drawing
our plans for house — Home to dinner
at 1 — back at 2.30 — & all the after-
noon on plans — Took them home
with me — A little before 6 went
to Will's — Got W. Dounsbury's address
20 W. 58th St. & then went there and
made arrangements to send picture
home to-morrow afternoon — then
home to tea at 6.45 — Evening
until near 11, working on plans
to send to Cottrell — then to
bed.
