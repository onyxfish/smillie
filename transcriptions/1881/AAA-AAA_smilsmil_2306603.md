## Left Page

### July, Monday 11, 1881.

Heavy Rain in the early m't making the air much cooler. Thunder north of us. A. + J waded over to studio + J worked at my drawing until noon - then to P.O. + to Cottrell's Dinner about 1.30 - + about 3 when I went to studio — worked until after 5 and lounged until 6 - then to tea - At 6.30 again to studio where I met Cottrell + Clifford — me staked out the corners of proposed house + talked till I was tired trying to get an estimate from Clifford for digging + walling cellar — then to house + at 8.30 a + J walked down to P.O. — back + read paper until 10 — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 12, 1881.

muggy + close — clouds moving slow-ly — Possible to keep comfortably col- if perfectly quiet. B left. at 8 - at 9 went with A. to studio + spent the m't there drawing plans. Clifford came with his estimate for cellar digging, etc. — At 12, A + J went to P.O., then to dinner abt. + at 2.15 again to studio. worked on plans until 5 — then down town — Tea at 6 - after it, Ben, Mee + Minnie Mulford, Alice Foster + Fritz called — then after walking the piazza to bed about 10.30.
