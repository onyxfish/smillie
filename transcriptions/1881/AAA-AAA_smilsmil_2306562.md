## Left Page

### April, Monday 18, 1881

Still pleasant, altho' at times overcast — All day printing —
got some better pkp. from
the Bridgman plates — Mr.
Mr. Cheney, Miss Cheney & Chl.
Pratt called in the [illegible].
To lunch at 2 — worked
until near 6 — then washed
up — Rested — Took a short
airing in Madison Square
& had tea in studio after 7 —
nearing when I went to
Annie's — there until 10.30
then to 37th St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 19, 1881

Very mild & spring-like
in the mtf. went to Kimmel's
[illegible] — then to Creamer the
French paper agent — back
to studio & spent most of the
rest of the day mounting pkp.
[illegible] went to Mr. Dod's, Hoboken
to lunch — I was invited
but at the last moment was
obliged to back out as I had
to get my pkp. for Mr. Koehler
for Boston — To lunch at
2 — at 4.30 took my pkp. to [illegible].
& then to Archer Pancoast & Co
where I met Annie — walked
uptown — stopping at studio —
after 7 when I got to 110 E. 38 —
then dinner — [illegible] spent
with Rennie's man hanging pictures
about the house — To 37th St. & to
bed at 11.
