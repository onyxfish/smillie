## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 18. 1881.

Pleasant day — wet, overcast — got to studio about 9.30 and to work. Trying to make a water color sketch from Miss Septon's head — made dismal work of it. At 1.45 out to lunch — back + worked until 4 — then went to dentist E. M. Flagg. 48 W. 10th S. To try the teeth that Uncle Geo. made for me — they were a failure — at 6 to 37 S. at 6.20 to Annie's. Took her mother (+ A.) to Ned Cook's in Park Ave. where we met Mr. Spague + her son Henry and dined at a little before 7 — soon after 8. I was excused + went to Mr. Moss's (Emma Peele's) 238 W. 55 S. to see some paintings just sent out by her bro. Mr. F. Peele — met there her husband, her stepson + his wife — got back to Cook's at 9.30 + at 10 took my ladies home + at 10.20 went to 37 S. + then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 19. 1881.

Very fine day — got to studio late + spent the mf. cleaning up the accumulated stuff + rubbish that has got into the corners + out of the way places. To lunch at 2 + at 3.30 went to Depot + met Father, Mother + Kate, escorted them to their quarters at 106 Madison Ave. with which they seemed much pleased. Then to studio — tea there at about 7 — soon after 8 went to N.A. to Water Color Socy meeting. There until after 10 — then to 37 S. + to bed about 11.
