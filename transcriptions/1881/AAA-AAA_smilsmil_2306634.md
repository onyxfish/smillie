## Left Page

### September, Sunday 11, 1881

A little rain at 7 a.m. — the weather lowering & rather muggy in spite of a breeze — after bkfst — up to studio — went down the well — To Pres. Ch. with A — after service, again to studio & wrote to Toehler in answer to his letter to me announcing to death of the Am. Art Review — also to father who is now at Shandaken with mother & Kate. Dinner at 2 — again to studio — had a nap — more writing — Tea at 6 — after that to P.O. with letters. A & D evenings to Ep's. Ch. — heard Rev. Mr. Waminer — Then to Sayno's — chatting until 10 — then to bed —

## Right Page

### September, Monday 12, 1881

Overcast much of the day — During the day promises of rain passed away — much interest in getting down the sills & floor beams of the main floor of our house — Dinner at 1 — all the afternoon drawing & out of doors — Tea at 6 — then to P.O. — Mr. & Mrs. de Scheinwitz called — me got to bed about 10.30.
