## Left Page

### April, Friday 22, 1881

Overcast m[illegible], coming off bright & pleasant at night
in spite of an easterly wind. find myself today extremely
sore all over, the cold having settled in my muscles from
head to foot — I mounted some etchings but otherwise was
unable to do any thing out to lunch about 2 — Late
in the afternoon I took a short walk — then tea in the
studio — It was near 9 o'clk when I got to De[illegible] Hall — 5th
ave. bet. 41st & 42d. St where was a festival in aid of the Diet Kitchen.
Met Annie & others there — altho feeling very matchead I enjoyed
some of Kate Hillard's recitations very much — Home with Annie
also — about 11 got to 37th St. &
then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 23, 1881

Very pleasant & very mild — but in bed all day with a stupid
head ache — About 7 P.M. the gives made a bowl of gruel for
me & at 8 I got up & dressed —
at about 9 I went to Annie's
there until 10.45 — then
again to 37th St — To bed about
midnight & got a little rest
during the night.
