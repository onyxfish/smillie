## Left Page

### March, Saturday 5, 1881

Beautiful m'g. hlp. at Annie's
& after it walked down to 2nd.
St. with her, then to Studio —
The m'g got away from me
without my having accomplished
anything — at 2.15 went to N.A.
to see Rosselle — then to Barber's
& had hair cut — back to studio
— Minnie & Joe there — T.S. Church
called — to lunch at 2 — The
afternoon was about as successful
as the m'g in getting away without
leaving a mark. — My large picture
was taken to the Century, from thence
to be taken to the N.A. early Monday
m'g. — Smaller one to N.A. direct —
Tea in Studio — wrote awhile then
to Century — Fitch has hung my picture
so badly that it made me sick — I
left at 10.30 — got to 37th St. & to
bed at 11.30 —

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 6, 1881

Lovey day — up at 7.30 — had
hlp. at Coleman Ho. & then to
studio where I wrote diligently
upon my Etching paper until
after 1 o'clk — Then to Annie's
to dinner — there until near
4.30 — then to Century with her
& from there to 106 Mad. Ave. —
& saw father & mother — After to
when we got home — then had
tea — sat awhile by fire light —
Annie much disturbed because
the house is let & another one will
have to be found. About 8 I started
for studio — there, writing until
10.30 — then to 37th St. & to bed
soon after 11.
