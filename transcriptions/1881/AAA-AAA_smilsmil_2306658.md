## Left Page

### October, Saturday 29. 1881.

Heavy Easterly storm of wind
& rain — all day & much of
the night. Mrs. after bkfs:
went over to the house & found
bricklayer & carpenters working
under great disadvantage and
discomfort owing to the storm —
then back & worked on my drawings
until noon, then again to the
house & after that down town to
P.O. Dinner soon after 1 —
Afternoon, more drawing and
about 4, again to the house — then
until about 5, then, being pre-
pared I took a short walk
in the dismal wet woods enjoy-
ing it very much — Met Florence
d. — we ret. to C.'s — she took tea
with us — Ev'ef. whist until
near 10 — 10.30 when I got
to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 30. 1881.

Very mild day — Sun out in the
m't, as though it would clear —
luminous fog — Bkfs — at 9. at 10.30
went over to the house — there
for an hour or more — then back
wrote some letters — Dinner at
2 — after that, again over to
house — many strolling callers
from town — back about 4 and
wrote as long as I could see —
then to P.O. with letters — Tea
at 6 — Ev'ef. wrote to Father,
& took it to P.O. before 9 o'clk
Heavy rain then began falling
& continued through most of
the night — To bed just before
10.
