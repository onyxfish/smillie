## Left Page

### July, Thursday 21, 1881

Charming day — the afternoon particularly lovely — B[illegible]. at 8.30 —
after that, to studios — much out-
doors with the diggers — when
in doors, working on front ele-
vation — Dinner at 1.30 — again
on the hill & some drawing
done — the trouble about grades
still persistent — Did some
work on front elevation & at
5 went down town with a.
Tea after 6 — Rustf at 8 went
to call on Mr. & Miss Jessup (Mr. M.)
Miss Seeley at home alone — Later
Mr. J. came in — We left at
about 9.30 — To our room &
to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 22, 1881

Still pleasant weather altho
overcast & strong southerly breeze
blowing — Have a heavy feeling
about the head, only relieved
by being out of doors — Tried to
do some work for the A.B.N. Co.
Got out material for drawing of
Mt. Hood, but made no progress.
At noon went down town and
ordered horse & buggy — back to
dinner at 1 — At 3.30, after
being at studios, horse came & a.
& I went out to ride — went over
old forest lake road for about 4 m.
then north & back over new forest lake
road — Charming — in at 6 — at 7
Misses Paine, Hollenback — a. & I
went to Mr. Parks's to tea — met there
Mr. & Mrs. de Schwinezt — & had a pleas-
ant time until after 10 — then
to our quarters & to bed about 11.
On our way to Mr. Parks, we
called upon Mrs. H.F. Tyler.
