## Left Page

### December, Saturday 10, 1881

Pleasant day, but cold.
got to studio at 9. 45 — Spent
the m'f working over scale
drawings of windows sashes
for Montrose house — Home
to dinner at 1.30, after a visit
from Miss Maria Messenger —
Back before 3 & again worked
over drawings — Home before
6 — Edith & Earnest Night
to tea — Every, all went
to Philharmonic Concert.
Introduced myself to Dr.
Schnedler, to whom I have
written a note & who holds
the seats next to mine and
which I so much covet.
Home at 10.30 — to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 11, 1881

Raw & chilly — at times, overcast.
Blép. at 9 — headachy — Soon
after 10 went to studio & spent
the m'f there. Home at 1.30
Dinner at 2 — after that a
short rest — Then Walter & family
came — Edith W. went down
town to return home but missed
her train — at 5 I went to studio
& note to father & to young
Ritchie regretting that I can
not accept invitation to the
Rembrandt Club annual dinner
next Tues. Home to tea at 7
Every. Annie read aloud
for some time. To bed about
10.30.
