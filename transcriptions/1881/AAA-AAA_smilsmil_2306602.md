## Left Page

### July, Saturday 9, 1881

Deliciously cool mrng. —
growing warmer during
the day — After bkfst., N.J.I.
came & took Annie out
riding for an hour. I went
to studio & to work on plans
until after 12. Then A. & I
went down town — on our
way back stopped awhile
on Mrs Mulford's piazza —
near 2 before we had our
dinner — Then a little
nap & at 3.30 out riding
with a — South of town, thro'
Brewster's woods & over
Butterfield's & Searle's Hills.
Tea at about 7 — then to
studio for a short time.
About, among my trees, then
to house — Mrs. Mr. Jessup & daughter
called — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 10, 1881

Very hot day — Bkfst. soon after 9 —
mrng. Annie & I went to Presbn
Ch. & heard Mr. Miller. Walked
part way home with Kate P. Riley
then with Mr. & Mrs. Mr. Mulford
& stopped awhile on their piazza.
Then on with Miss Alice Foster —
Dinner about 1.30 — About 3 I
went to studio & spent most of
the afternoon there — wrote a letter
to father. Tea at 6 — Suef. a.
I went to N.J.I.'s — Aunt H.
at home alone — we sat on
piazza with her until after
9 — Thunderstorms N.W. of
us — we got a cooling breath
from them — To our bedroom
& to bed about 11 — the heat
so great as to make sleep
almost impossible.
