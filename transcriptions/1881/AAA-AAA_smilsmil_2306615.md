## Left Page

### August, Thursday 4, 1881

Very hot & smoky — to studio as usual, after bkfo. & to work on pen
sketches for Osgood book. A. posed for me for one sketch but found it
unexpectedly hard work, becoming faint. Down to P.O. before dinner.
After that, again to studio & worked until near 6 — then dressed &
had tea — after that, took a. to W.J.T.'s where I went on to
the Searle's to make a call —
all out — went to P.O. then to W.J.T.'s & sat on piazza
until near 10 — then to our own quarters & to bed about
10.30 —

## Right Page

### August, Friday 5, 1881

Hotter than ever. Ther. getting to 84° in studio — Again at work
upon pen sketches. Re drawing a sketch made yesterday — the
work is very unsatisfactory to me done as it is under such very unfavorable circumstances — Dinner
at 1.15. I went to P.O. alone. After dinner to studio where I continued my wrestling with
the pen sketches — about 6 went to tea — After tea, at 7, went to livery stable, got horse & buggy
& took a. out but had only about 3/4 of an hour as a threatening
thunderstorm drove us in —
Thunder & lightning very strong but the rain light — on piazza
until near 10 — to bed at 10.30
