## Left Page

### September, Friday 23, 1881

Clear, hot & sticky — much of the mng. spent at home in domestic cares, then to Cor. Astor Place & Lafayette R. & selected goods to cover a lounge for Annie — then to Schmidt & Siegke's — then to studio & studied up subjects for an Etching — Van Cleef & the Herald came in — Home to dinner about 2 — After that, feeling very heavy, I lay upon the bed & slept for about an hour — then to Schmidts & gave order for lounge — then to studio — there until after 6 — then home — tea at 7 — wrote a note & sent check to Cotrell — a very little shower — then out — met the Paynes on their doorstep — went in for awhile — back before 9. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 24, 1881

Still very hot & clear — put on my lightest Summer clothes — got to studio about 10.30 & to work with charcoal upon a sketch for etching — Cedars & snow — Home to dinner at 1.30 — about 3.30 when again I got to studio — did not like my sketch — discouraged, but after a time commenced another — Pollard Willows — Mso. ave. Polk. Home to tea at 6.30 — Went with Annie who is getting along nicely until after 8 — then went to Dr. Gale's — not in — back at 9 — Read until after 10 — then to bed.
