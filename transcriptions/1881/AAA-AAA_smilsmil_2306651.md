## Left Page

### October, Saturday 15. 1881.

Rain during the night and nearly all day, at times quite heavy. Bkfs. at 9 — about 10 Shrimger & I took a short walk in the rain, then back & to work upon W.C. of old mill — lunch at 1 — worked on drawing until after 4 — then a nap & at 5 went down town with 2. — back at 6 & had tea. Evef. in studio — Reading news paper & later, playing whist — the old ladies Shrimger & Wayland apl. Annie & J. — To bed about 11.

Rain again nearly all night.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 16. 1881.

Delicious mf. after the rain — a short walk before bkfs. at 9 — during the mf. took a short walk with A. Lunch at 12.30 — In the afternoon had quite a long ramble with Saffy & Mr. Mr. Polpe's dog, Toots, the weather was really very warm — wrote a letter to father — Dinner at 5 — Evef. M. trnr. Thompson called — we all sat in the parlor. At about 10 we went to our room & did also our packing preparatory to a start to-morrow mf. To bed before 11.
