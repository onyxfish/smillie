## Left Page

### June, Sunday 19, 1881

Very hot day — blp — late —
A. went with me to hear
Dr. Hastings, but he did not
preach — we heard a stranger
& he took us in. After service
I went to studio — home to
dinner at 2 — After that a
nap — Then the family came —
Walter, wife & child — Margaret
Parker White & child — Emma
& Grace J — & miss Williams
& Jno. Valentine — Emma J —
& miss W. stayed to tea — I
afterwards acted as escort.
Quiet every. & to bed before
11.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 20, 1881

Very warm but overcast & at
times, pleasantly breezy — After
blp — went with A. to Leaute's
to look at some rugs — Got to
studio at 10 & to work on etching
"ll. Snow" — Voigt came, telling
me that Ticknor said that no
more than 1000 "Porto & Etchers"
would be printed — Note to J —
Dr. Gale called — Then home to
dinner at 1.45 — back to studio
before 3 & got my etching into
the acid.
