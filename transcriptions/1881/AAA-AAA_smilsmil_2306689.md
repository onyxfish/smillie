## Left Page

### December, Friday 30, 1881

fine winter's day — Got to studio at 9.30 — Had several letters to write — sent checks to Catnell, Harper, Rayns[on] & Wunderlich — Then to work on "Crisp Oct." & in a little time came to the conclusion that I could not profitably do much more upon it, so set it face to the wall. Home to dinner soon after 1, back before 3 — fussing about during the rest of afternoon. Between 5 & 6 went to Rennie's — met Miss Abbott here — walked home with her to 25th St. & Mad. Ave. — Then home at 6.30 & soon after had tea — a lazy evening — a little reading & more lying upon the sofa while A. played the piano — to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 31, 1881

Clear & cold. Got to studio at 9.45 — a number of small chores to attend to, then to work Tracing & outlining "Old Icehouses near Saratoga" — 10½ × 22 — Home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.30, finished Tracing & outlining & then attended to the mounting etc. of some other W.C. sketches. At 5.30 went to Rennie's & after that walked up to 48th St. & 6th Ave. — then home & had tea about 7 — Sueff. soon after 8 A. & I went to Geo's & spent the evening there. Home at 9.45 — to bed at 11.30 — to sleep before the New Year came in.
