## Left Page

### September, Saturday 17, 1881

Early mng, cloudy, but by 10 o'clk the sky was all clear.
I went to studio before 9 —
went down the well & gave
last & general orders. Left
at 10.30 — Called on Aunt H.
— then down town & did up the
last of my little messages
there — then to Sayre's and
completed packing — Dinner
at 12 — Alarm of fire — woods
near little depot burning — at
12.40, A. & J. & Dr. Mulford started
for the depot — Pleasant ride —
Train 23 m late — off at 3 o'clk. Wm
deSchwinetz going part of the way
with us — horribly hot, gasy & dusty
in cars — cooler later — evening
quite cool — got to Hoboken at
9.10 — home before 10 — Trunks
did not arrive until after
11 — to bed soon after 12 —

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 18, 1881

Delightful day — Up late and
had bkfst — at about 10 o'clk,
after 11 went to Grand Central
depot — then to Hoffman Hse
& had my hair cut — then to
studio & at 1.20 started for
home — Dinner about 2 —
in the afternoon the usual
family gathering — Ned & wife
& [Mrs.] Valentine to dinner —
was told of Dr. [Gali's] engagement
& went up to congratulate
him — just missed him —
went to R. C. Cathedral & back
home after 5 — Mrs. Jacoby
called — tea at 7 — Evening
wrote to father. To bed
between 10 & 11 —
