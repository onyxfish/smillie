## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 19, 1881

Lovely breezy m'g. B'kf't. at 8.30 then out to studio for a little while — then down town & got two horses with saddle & side saddle, then up to the house & took Annie out — we rode an hour & a half — until 12 o'clk — horses very good — the air delicious — Dinner at 1.30 — didn't feel good for much in the afternoon — heavily overcast strong wind & heavy shower at 4. Down-Town as usual at 5.30 — tea at 6.30 — Bret & A & J called on Mr. Kate Riley — not in — then to Mr. Webb's, where we saw Mr. Douglass & then is ter Bessie Riley — there until near 10, then to our quarters & to bed —

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 20, 1881

Middey overcast day — after b'kf't went to studio for awhile — Payne finishing the mowing of my lot — then back to the house & accepted Miss Hollenback's invitation & A & J went out with her to ride. J drove — Miss Payne & Jn°. Courtright also went along — on our way back stopped at a creamerie & saw the process of making skim-milk cheese. Back at 12 — nearly 2.30 before we had our dinner — after that out to studio & staked out the line of carriage road up to the house, much troubled over the grades of the hill that will not conform to my plans & preconceived ideas of them — Annie used up with a sick-headache — Tea at 6.30 — Bret & J went down-town — called at Mr. [illegible]'s & saw Josie & Hetty & Kate — Mr. S. is very low — went to Cottrell's but did not see him — then back to A — she did not get up — to bed at 10.30.
