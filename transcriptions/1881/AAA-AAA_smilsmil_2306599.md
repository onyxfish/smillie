## Left Page

### July, Sunday 3, 1881

Delightful, breezy day. After a good nights rest, up & had B'kf't at 8.30 — the m'g was spent with a on the fair ground, she reading to me. It was so breezy that we had to seek a more sheltered place — Dinner between 1.30 & 2. Afternoon about studio & on piazza — wandered over Warner's lot — through the woods around the fair ground & then down. Foun, getting back between 8 & 9 quite tired. To bed before 10.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 4, 1881

Charming day, in spite of a bit of a shower about noon. Wakened at sunrise by the usual rumpus of Bell Ringing & cannon firing — up at 7.30 — B'kf't at 8 — M'g spent on piazza & wandering about among the crowds of country people in town. Procession about 11 — Shower at 12 — Dinner at 1 — To fair ground at 1.30 Sack & wheelbarrow Races — very funny. Then what was called a Tournament of Knights — fairly dressed fellows on horseback racing with spears at little suspended rings. After them a grotesque parody — a darkey band in a charcoal wagon — a hook & ladder co. & knights. The fun was broad, the crowd great & the weather delightful — left the ground about 6 — then tea — Early eve'g Mr. & Mrs. Wm Mulford called — then we went to the green to see the fireworks — again amused by the crowds — To bed at 10.30 — Sky overcast — slight rain sometime during the night.
