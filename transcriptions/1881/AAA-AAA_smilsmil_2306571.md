## Left Page

### May, Sunday 8, 1881

*The Aldine — Rooms No. 115 + 116,*

Delicious day — up + had our bkfs
about 9 o'clk. The house is a fine
one, the management accommodating
but at table the delays are tempter-
trying, the food tolerable + always
cold — After bkfs. we went to our
Rooms for a while + at near noon
went out for an airing — Went up
to Rittenhouse Sq — then rode over
to Green St + walked to Fairmount
Park — After loitering about there
for some time, Ret. to Hotel — had
dinner between 3 + 4 — again
to our Rooms — had them changed
on acc't of noise from the street, +
115 + 116. Much pleasanter Rooms
looking over the garden — At 6
out for a walk — in at 7 — at 8
had a cup of tea — After that went
out + had a moonlight walk and
about 10.30 went to bed + enjoyed
a night of unbroken sleep.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 9, 1881

Very warm cloudless day — Bkfs
at 9 — about 10 went out for a
walk — up Chestnut St + through
Wanamaker's — Back to hotel
after 11 + to work on a S.A. drawing
for A.B.N. Co. Finished it + took
it to Haseltine's where I had it
properly wrapped up — then to
Exp. Office + sent it to N.Y. — then
to hotel + at 3 o'clk. we had a very
good dinner, thanks to having
bought one of the waiters — a rest
after dinner + at 4.30 started out
with a very fine team of horses
+ a top buggy — went to Fairmount
Park + drove along the River side
to the Nissahicon + along it, beyond
the old Red Bridge — to 7 miles beyond
Fairmount — then back, getting to
hotel at 7 after a delicious drive —
tea at 8 — then a little writing.
