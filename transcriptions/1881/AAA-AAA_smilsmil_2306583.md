## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 1. 1881.

Rain in the mtg. then clearing not. Got to studio's about 9.30
tools, pen, pencils, etc., etc. + set? to Huntington's house where
I worked. Faithfully until after I making a pen sketch from
a painting by Casanova. A young man sat by me + watched
me like a detective all the mtg. — Wont go back to do the Mere.
At 1.30 home. Matt + Kate here to lunch. Joe also. The girls came
from P'k this mtg + return this P.M. To studio's at 3.15 + made
sketch for 3° Osgood. Sketching ill. Emerson's Apology. — To
tea at 7 — Rest a quiet, steady rain. Will + Joe called
+ spent the evng. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 2. 1881.

Hot + overcast with sunshine + sprinkling rains — Got to studio
at 10 — + spent the mtg writing letters — talking to Sontag about
water colors + trying to improve my impresson sketches. To dinner
at 1.30 + back by 3 — Traced sketch + worked at a dis-
advantage because I wanted to get who was off with Nellie
to pose for me — They have determined to be married
on the 28th of this month. —
Skinna made a long call —
Late afternoon overcast with cold east wind — To tea at
7 — at 8.30 went with Annie to the Gibbons' in 44th St. — a
little company to meet Mrs Rusch — Home at 11 + soon
to bed.
