## Left Page

### September, Saturday 3, 1881

Cooler in the m'f. & for a part of the time overcast. Hot's ing for Rain — Spent the m'f at studio — divided between plans & Reading Hammertons Hand Book on Etching for Review — Our last dinner at Courtright's — after that packed our trunks and went down to Sayre's — then to studio until 6 — then to P.O. with pkg of Revised pfs for Osgood & Co — To tea at Sayre's at 6.30 — Later in the eveg A. & I went over to Aunt H's — Uncle A rode to depot at 9 o'clk to take night train for N.Y. — about 10 we took our departure & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 4, 1881

Cool m'f (67°) & overcast, threatening rain — Bible at 8 — after that went to studio & made awhile — Agur Nathans came — stayed too late to go to church — so went to Sayre's for Annie & we took a long walk around by Sayre's Farm & over Brewster's hill — Sun came out hot — Road very dusty — walk hardly pleasant — got in at 1 — dinner at 1.30 — after that a nap — then to studio & wrote several letters — To tea at 6 — then quite a walk — at 8.30 went to call on aunt — house closed — then a short walk back — some singing of sacred songs — To bed at about 11.
