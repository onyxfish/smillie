## Left Page

### January, Sunday 2, 1881

Overcast m'g. & much milder. Had help alone about 9 o'clk & then to studio where I spent the m'g writing letters. At 2, at Annie's where I had dinner — at 3.30 we went out walking — up to the Park — tout at 72° 3° — down 5th Ave to 38th S°— in at 5.15 — lovely, cloudless afternoon — At 6 we went to Coleman's & before 7 had tea — I spent the ev'g before a pleasant fire, chatting. — There to 38th S° about 10 and to 37 S° & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 3, 1881

Delightful day — got to studio about 9.30 & to work on View near Portsmouth until after 12 — Laura had lunch in studio — left at 12.45 and on 1.30 train met Will Chase & Geo. went to Plainfield & direct to house of Uncle Geo.: funeral service promptly performed — then to burying ground — services there very brief & then to R.R. Station. — Charl. & Will Re[turned] to the city by 4.02 train — Geo. & I, by invitation went to the Tuesday — Mrs. I. & daughter of Danbury, also the Misses Reynolds there — we stayed to dinner & Ret[urned] to N.Y. by 8.20 train, 20 m. late. At 10 I went to studio and fixed fire, then to 37th S° & to bed about 11.
