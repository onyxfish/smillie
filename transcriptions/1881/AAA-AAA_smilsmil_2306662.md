## Left Page

### November, Sunday 6, 1881

Delicious day. bkfs. at 9 —
after that went to the house
where Annie joined me
then we strolled off to
Warner's lot where we
sunned ourselves under the
shelter of a great rock —
Back to Courtright's — wrote
a letter to Father — Dinner
at 2 — After that, with Annie
— W. & Mrs. C. & the baby, went
over to the house & for a time
enjoyed the view from the
Piazza — then, with A. to
Aunt t's — later I went
for a walk & on my return
stopped for a — Tea at 6.
Everg wrote to Bourdet,
I took the letter to the P.O.
about 9 o'clk. — To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 7, 1881

Overcast mrf. — Heavy rain
began to fall about 10 o'clk.
& continued throughout the
day, proving a regular soaker
& very much retarding work
at the building where I spent
most of the mfg. Worked on
drawings of chimneys — Had
dinner soon after 1 — Again
worked awhile on drawings.
About 4 went out & had a
tramp in the pouring, driving
rain, enjoying very much some
of the effects. Tea at 6 —
Everg — some more drawing
— some reading of Spencer
& a game or two of cards.
To bed before 11.
