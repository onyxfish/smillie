## Left Page

### August, Saturday 20. 1881.

Delicious m'f. after the nights rain — After b'kfs: a. went riding
with Miss H — & Mrs. Coles — out all the m'f — to Hart Lake — I
went to studio & made another sketch of lounger in the ilex shadows — To P.O. at 12.30.
Dinner after 1 — again to studio — trimming trees etc
until 3.30 — Then out riding with Annie — to Heart Lake.
— back at 6 & had tea — again to studio puzzling over west elevation. — back to the house
with a new plan, couldn't make it work & got sick fretting over it — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 21. 1881.

Pleasant m'f. After b'kfs. went to studio & wrote one or two short letters — then, not feeling well enough to go to church I spent the rest of the m'f in hammock under big maples — Down to P.O.
at 12.30 — back to dinner at 1.5
— after that again to studio &
wrote to father & to Lawrie —
Light shower bet. 3 & 4 o'clk —
Annie read awhile to me.
Tea at 6 — after that went to
studio — charming sunset and twilight — wandered about until dark — then to the house
— To bed at 10 —

Before tea walked down to
Cottrell's — then to Clifford's and ordered work on west wall to be stopped.
