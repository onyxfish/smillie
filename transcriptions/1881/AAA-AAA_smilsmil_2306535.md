## Left Page

### February, Monday 21, 1881

Dismal mist, rain + then snow. I felt very like the weather —
Got to studio's about 10 + wrote a note to Charle concerning
payment of freight on etchings out to England. Then to work on 33
×44, painting the cows — lunch at 2 + after that took Valentine
to Horny [illegible] — Back to studio + worked with difficulty on
painting, putting in a part of milk maid — Then on lounge
until about 7 when I had tea. After that went to
see father + mother — on the lounge there until after
10 o'clk — then to [illegible] 4 St and to bed.

weather cleared before noon delightfully.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 22, 1881

Charming day — Got to studio's before 10 + to work on Cow and
milk maid (33 × 44) until 12.30 — then had a lunch in
studio + at 1 went for Kate.
at 1.30 we met Annie at the
42d, 2d + 3d Ave. R.R. Station + all
went to 84th St + thence to Met.
Museum where Warts delivered
an address on the presentation
of the Obelisk to the City — Horrible
crush owing to bad management
in getting into Museum — also
no end of mud — could get no
seats — could hear nothing, and
but for the fun of seeing crowds
of people floundering through
depths of mud the trip would
have been a disappointment.
At 4.30 went home with Annie —
then to studio — had tea there
alone + at 8 went to 106 Mad. ave.
[illegible] got to Annie's — at 10.30
went to 37th St. + to bed.
