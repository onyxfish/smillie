## Left Page

### March, Monday 21, 1881

Bright breezy day — a delightful change — Got to studio about
9.30 & to work on Bridgeman etching — Being somewhat at sorts I did not progress rapidly — To lunch at 1.30 —
After that went to Beck's cor. 29th St. & 7th ave. — to correct a blunder made there in sending wall paper to Po'k. — then to studio & worked until after 4 — then again to Beck's —
then to 37th St. — dressed — & at 6.30 to Annie's to dinner —
At 8 we went to "Private View" — N.A. of D. — Outrageous jam. — So, soon after, we left —
At 10.30 I went to 37th St. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 22, 1881

Breezy, sunshine & shadow sort of day — Got to studio about 10
& to work on my etching —
completed it before I went to lunch at 1.30 — After that varnished up, making ready for the acid — About 4.30 I went to Annie's & with her went to Warren's in 45th St. —
then to Hayet's — cor 44th St. and 10th ave. — then to Camille Belle's in 41st St. & selected paper for 3° stone front room. — then to 38th St. & had dinner — At 7.30 I left to attend a committee meeting on Bridgeman Reception at Townsend Evans' in 23rd St. —
There until after 9 — then to N.A. — there until 10 — then to studio & to 37th St. — at about 11 & to bed.
