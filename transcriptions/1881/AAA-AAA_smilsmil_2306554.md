## Left Page

### April, Saturday 2, 1881

Bright, windy & quite cold — got to studio at 9.30 & to work on Bindeman etching — J.W. Ehninger called — then Ab. S. Anthony & some time was spent discussing details & publication of Book of etchings — Lunch at 2 — about 4 Fanny K. came in — I went to Douglas, the Painter, 3d. ave. & 34th St — then met Fanny & Annie at 110 E. 38th St — then with Annie to Warren's & to Pattier & Stymnus — then home with her to dinner. At 8.30 went to Century — enjoyed myself there until midnight — then to 37th St — & to bed at.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 3, 1881

Clear, cold & windy — up late — had bkfs. at 10 o'clk — then to Dr. Hastings's & heard him preach — after service went to studio — there until 2 o'clk then to Annie's to dinner — at 4 went with her to Century & to N.A. of D — then to studio & back to 38th St. at near 7 o'clk — then tea — we all spent reading & chatting until a little after 10 — then to 37th St — to bed —
