## Left Page

### June, Monday 27, 1881

Heavily overcast, with, at times, mizzle enough to wet the side walks. Bilp. at 7.30 took 8.12 Train & got to N.Y. at 10.30 — a few min. after getting to the house, Matt & Kate arr. Annie & I went ff looking for something for Geo & at last found a handsome mahogany folding Table at Sipples which we bought for him — then home but did not have dinner until after 2 — To studio at 3.30. Geo there — callers & visiting occupied the p.m. Tea at 7 — Evening with girls in parlor & writing letter to Ehninger, Will & Fanny K. — called at 9 — Quite a heavy Rain — K's left at 10.30 — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 28, 1881

Very warm day — made notes & got to studio soon after 10 — weather clearing hot & thunderous. Geo at studio all the m'g. I do work getting things in shape to leave — To dinner at 1.30 — after that dressing etc — until about 3.30 — then with sister Mary to Dr. Bellows Ch., Annie, her mother, Kate & Matt having gone in a carriage — Church very full of friends — the wedding went off very smoothly — after it, much talking among the old friends — then to the Bride's home where the family called with congratulations — there until 5.30 then to studio — then home & at 7 had tea — We lf. went to the Thrushelewoods & got my blankets — back at 9 — very close — then a thunder shower but not very heavy — To bed at 10.30 — Geo & Nellie went to West Point on 6 o'clk Train — Father went up to P'ok on same train — Mother did not come down.
