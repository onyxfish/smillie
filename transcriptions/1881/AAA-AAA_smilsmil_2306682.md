## Left Page

### December, Friday 16, 1881

Bright, crisp cold day — got to
studio at 9.45 & after writing
my weekly letter with check
to Cattrel, I started a 10×14
water color sketch "Moonlight
& Snow". Chas. A. Pratt called
abt 1 o'clk — I got home to dinner
abt 1.25 — back at 2.45 and
worked as long as I could
see — Then to Knoedler's &
there met Diffale — Walked
up to 42d St with him — then
to Renner's & at 6, home —
Dale dressing — found half
a dozen friends at work in
the Parlor. Tea at about 7,
then more work as dale dressing — Bro. Geo. & two or three others
came later — all went away
before 10.30 — To bed by 11.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 17, 1881

Very pleasant day — After bkfst. went
to Dr. Mr. Thompson's & was detained
there for some time — got to studio
at 10.30 & to work on W.C. sketch
10×14 — Moonlight & Snow — Before
long Lounsbury came in & I went
to Miss Abbatt's room with him &
then to Tho. Staris to see some
paintings etc. that I— wishes to sell
him — Home to dinner at 1.20 —
Back at 2.40 & worked as long
as I could see on W.C., callers
coming in. I did not get away
until near 6 — After a short
walk, home to tea — Every. at
8 o'clk, Annie & I went to the
Salmagundi Sttm at N.A.d.
There until 9.30 — Then over
to studio — went in to see
Bellows who is sick — Home
at 10.30 & to bed after 11.
