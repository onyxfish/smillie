## Left Page

### September, Thursday 29, 1881

Very pleasant day & much cooler — went out for a walk at about 10.30 — To G.C. Depot again to see Telegraph operator — then a walk up 5th ave. & back about 11.30 — then to work with water colors on 7×11 sketch of Lake Geo., looking So. from So[uth] and [of] Harbor Islands — Dinner at 1.30 — after that, again to work & at it as long as I could see — then to Studio & found a box of Jap. paper, not at all what I have been waiting for, being but a small [pattern] & what I ordered — another stupid blunder somewhere. Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g writing & lounging until near 11 o'clc — then to bed. Slight rain.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 30, 1881

Overcast & muggy — things damp from a slight fall of rain — but the weather cleared almost as hot as before — After [illegible] went to Studio to see my Jap. paper, am much disgusted — back home about 11 & to work on W.C. Lake Geo., from Harbor Islands. Dinner a little before 2 — at my work again, finishing the sketch soon after 5 — then to Hunter's with my glasses — to Knordler's — to Siegel's and to Studio & home at 6.30 — Tea soon after — Eve'g notes to [illegible] — & to Dr Hamilton — that Painter Etcher Socy London concerning Boneset. To bed at 10.30
