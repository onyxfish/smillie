## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 21, 1881

Mildly overcast — wind still east — after bkfst, Rode down Bdway in bus to see the drawings which are wonderfully general — to Art Interchange & saw Turners — then to Chas & saw him — then home to dinner — After that by 3rd Ave. Elevated to Harlem, then by Str boat to High Bridge, then a Row on the River looking for sketching material — found nothing — then by R.R. to 155th St. & back by 6th Ave. R.R. Tea about 7 — Ever feeling very stupid, tired, Rheumatics, bilious, nervous. Sat about and lounged about until after 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 22, 1881

Overcast, with strong, Raw East wind, but no rain — After bkfst — went to Dr. Yale's. There with him until 11.30 — He is very happy over his engagement to marry — I took away with me a plate of his — Walked through 42d St. to the East River looking for Etching material — To dinner at 1, but near 2 before I got it — Marion Wright to dinner — on her way home from Ridgefield — Mary Willard & Fanny K — called on A — who is getting along nicely — I went to studio & laid Rebiting ground for Yale — then to several furniture stores looking for a lounge for Annie — Home to tea at 6.30 — part of evening again at Yale's — Home at 10.30 & to bed at 11.
