## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 1. 1881.

Snowing nearly all day, growing much colder. At night a heavy blow & very cold. Got to studio at 9.30 — all day spent in writing letters & yet have not caught up. Out to lunch at 2. — then to Moore's Gallery. Back at 3 & wrote with many interruptions until near 6 — then to 37 S. — dressed & at 6.30 to Anna's to dinner. Emma still visiting there. Spent the evening until 10.30 then to 37 S. & to bed at 11 — Very cold night.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 2. 1881.

Brilliant cold day — Got to studio at 9.30 & again at my writing, making little progress owing to callers — Rec'd a congratulatory letter from S. C. if, wrote to her at once. — To lunch a little before 2. — again writing — Baby went to Riding class with Saml. Coleman — at 6.15 to 37 S. — Rub down — at 7.30 to studio — Tea — at 8.45 to 38 S. & passed the rest of the evening there until 10.30 — then to 37 S. & to bed.
