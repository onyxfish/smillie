## Left Page

### March, Thursday 3, 1881.

for a short time the weather was delightful in the mf. but before noon the promise of better things was gone &
at nights again we had rain. Got to studio at 9.30 — my head bothering me — made a drawing after my "Evening Shadows" for Academ notes — Lunch at 2 & after that about a little time going about the Asst. Bunedin Studios — Reception — then to studio & did a little mixture. At 6.30 went to Annie's to tea & at 8 went with him & Minna to Singing Club — 4th ave. & 20th S. — then to 106 Mad Ave. & until 10.15, with father looking over many of Mr. Grigg's collection of old Skets — at 10.30 went for the girls — took them to 38th S. — then at 11.15 to 37th S. — Rain — to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 4, 1881.

Horrible storm of sleety rain with wind in the mf. — no rain in the afternoon but dismally dark & blustering — almost used up with a headache all day — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work and before lunch had finally finished (for this exhibit only) my 33 × 44 "Evening Shadows." Out to lunch at 2 & after that went to Knoedler's, back to studio and finished up on 17 × 32 "Twilight in the Woods" — then had a cut of orange — head feeling better — at near 7, tea in studio — then finished letter to Secty of Society of Painter-Etchers in London & at 9.15 went to 3rd ave. & 28th S. P.O. Station — then at 9.45 got to Annie's — there for 15 or 20 m — then to 37th S. & to bed.
