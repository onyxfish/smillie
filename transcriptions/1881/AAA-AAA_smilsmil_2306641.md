## Left Page

### September, Sunday 25. 1881.

Hot & clear — Up late. Bkf.
at 9.30 — mtf spent with
Annie, Reading washing,
writing etc etc — Dinner
at 2 o'clk. — In doors until
after 4. when I went up
to Gale's — there until
after 6 — then home to
tea — Eveg with Annie
— very hot & close — a
little before 9 went out
& posted a letter — then
went in to Young Tyng's ch.
& heard the last hymn —
met Calverley there & walked
to 34th St. with him — then
home & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 26. 1881.

Clearer & hotter than ever.
This like mid-dog-days —
Garfield's funeral at Cleveland
— a national fast day — went
to studio in the mtf & worked
on Charcoal sketch "Wayfarers
& Shavers" — Pollard. Willows
on So. ave., Polk. Home to dinner
at 1 — Did not go back to
studio until after 5 — Wrote
a letter to Bourlet, the agt.
in London to whom I sent
the etchings last Feby. —
Tea at 6.30 — Eveg with
Annie & writing to Bellows
& Farrar. To bed at 10.30.
