## Left Page

### December, Thursday 22. 1881.

Muggy, wet day — Got to studio
before 9.30 & to work on two
sketches, upright, one, 7×11 in.
the other, smaller. Both being
the same subject nearly —
"a hundred thousand Islands — Take geo
the smaller one I intend to
give to Kate — Home to dinner
at 1.15, back at 2.30 & worked
as long as I could see. Then
went to Knoedler's with Formun
R. That Ehninger sent me —
Showed it to Roos — he offered
me $10 — for it — E. wants $50 —
Took it back to studio & home
at 6. Emma & Grace J. to
tea — took them & a — to club
singing — then to studio & mat
to Hammerton — Cundall &
Ehninger — at 10.15 went for
a — very heavy Rain — home
before 11 & to bed about
11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 23. 1881.

Overcast mf. Got to studio
soon after 9.30 & to work on
7×11 upright — also some work
on smaller sketch for Kate.
About 12 went down to Dilling-
ham's book store with order
from Ticknor to get a copy of
Poets & Etchers to send to Father —
looked at a number — very badly
printed — Took one copy but after
all, decided not to send it. Home
to dinner at 1.30 — to studio at
2.45 — Did up a bundle of little
things for Po'keepsie presents
& Geo. Took them to Sufs. Office,
Auditing Com., Water Color Socy —
Baldwin, Bunner & Champney
came at 4 to pass on Geo's accts —
Home to tea at 6 — at 7.15 in
a cold, driving storm of snow, went
to Dr. Wm Thomson's by appointment
to find him out — waited until 8 —
then to Renner's & ordered 3 frames
for W. Cs — Home at 9.30 — Read
& to bed at 10.30, after writing
a letter to Father & taking it to depot
to post — clear, starlight night.
