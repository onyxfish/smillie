## Left Page

### May, Sunday 22, 1881

Still Rather dismal weather —
Bkfst. at 8.30 — after I had enjoyed
a half hour walk — the air very
fragrant & the birds full of song.
About 10 the C's, Annie & I
started out for a sauntering
walk & went down the road
to Held's place — back at 12.30
& had a little nap before dinner
soon after 1. Then in the C's
room — Mrs. C. Reading aloud
until about 3 when Mr. & Mrs.
Higgins came with their horses
& took us out for a ride — went
down to their daughter's — Mrs.
Barrett — while there, many thunder
showers passed over — we waited
until after 5 — then back to Peck's —
Tea soon after 6 — a wet evening —
in C's room — more Reading
aloud — To bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 23, 1881

*Home again.*

more overcast weather — more
rain & more sunshine — Up
before 7 — Bkfst. at 7.30 — Mr. H.
called for us & took us to 8.18
train — got in to Grand Central Depot
at 9.20 — thence to house — then to
studio & to work biting etching —
Mr. Bellows called — just in from
Boston. Home to dinner at 1.30
after that directed a number
of supplemental cards & got
to studio's after 3 — went on with
biting until 6. Home at 7 —
Every wrote a letter to Mother —
to-morrow will be her birth-day.
again some piano playing —
To bed at 11.
