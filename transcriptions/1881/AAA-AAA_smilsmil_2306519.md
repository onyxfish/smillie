## Left Page

### January, Thursday 20, 1881

Charming day — After bkfs. went to see Mother + Katie.
Katie complaining of a head ache. Then to studio at about 11 went
to Reuier + Co (stationery) for a moment at Annie's) + ordered
two frames for his pictures.
Back to studio at about 1, +
to lunch at 2. After that,
still clearing house — at
5 left studio — at 5.30 at Annie's
\+ at near 6, A. + J called on
Father + Mother at 106 Mad. Ave.
At 6.30 at Fannie's + had tea +
at 8 at Mr. T. Johnston's — then
to Averip's — there for a few minutes, then to "Victoria" (5 Ave. + 27's)
\+ called upon Gen. Frank Jessend's
wife — At 10.30 again at Mr.
J. J's — walked home with a —
\+ to 37' S. + to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 21, 1881

Dreadful storm of sleet + rain driven by strong wind and
freezing as it falls — Got to studio
about 10 — found congratulatory
letter from Chas. Collins — Spent the
mst writing. To lunch at about
2 — after that did little else —
than some clearing up as I
felt much out of tune —
Had tea in studio at about
[illegible] after a short call on Father +
Mother — At 8 in 37' S. at 8.30
went to Annie's — we were to
have gone to Mr. Vielland's
reception, but on acct. of storm
gave it up. At 9 J went to
Press View of Water Color Socy
\+ had a very pleasant time
until after 11 — then to 37' S.
\+ to bed at 12.15.
Snowing freely then.
