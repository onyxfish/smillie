## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 9, 1881

Overcast — got up with a mean headache & spent most of the day on lounge with head ache & stomach ache — Mr. T. Richards made a long call in the m'fg. I had a light lunch at 2.30 — Mr. Gray came in the afternoon. Had tea at studio — made a letter to Fau. Mueford & at 9 o'clk went to see father & mother — Willie, Joe, Charl there. — left at 10.30 — to 37 S. & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 10, 1881

Heavy Rain in the morning. Too during the rest of the day with no end of mud & slush. Got to studio before 10 & spent the rest of m'fg with visitors & etchings. Koehler came & promised that I would etch Bridgeman's "Lady of Cairo visiting" out to lunch at 1.30 — at 2.30 at Steinway Hall. Herschel's Recital — on Annie's invitation — met many friends & aside from the delightful concert had a good time — then, with Annie, went to Averys — then to studio — then to 38 S. — then to Renner's about London etching ex'bn, then at 6.30 again to 38 S. — tea — & with girls to 40 W. 32? S. — singing club — then to studio & wrote a number of notes & a letter to Margaret Parker White. At 10.30 went for Annie & took her & Emma home at 11 o'clk. Then to 37 S. & to bed at 11.45
