## Left Page

### October, Monday 31. 1881.

Rain fell until about 10
a.m. After h'kp. fixed my-
self to meet the weather & then
went over to the house. Dismal
work but the men managed to
get along. I devoted myself
to the chimney most of the
m'f. Went down town at
11.45 — Dinner at 1 — again
most of the afternoon watching
progress of chimney — had a
short walk & to P.O. before 6 —
then tea. Most of the eve'g
trying to draw a plan for
the chimney tops — near 11
when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 1. 1881.

overcast m'f. Before h'kp.
wrote a letter to B.H. Ticknor —
about 10 went to house — then
back & drew up on bay window
plans — About 12.30, again to
house & then to P.O. Dinner
soon after 1 — worked on plans
until after 4 — then to house —
beautiful sunset — there
until after 5.30 arranging
for well stone & wall — then
to P.O. — Tea after 6 — Most of
drawing on bay window again.
Florence came in & we had
a game of whist — about 10
took her home — cloudless
moonlight — To bed a little
before 11.
