## Left Page

### June, Sunday 5, 1881

a very pleasant day, altho we had several slight spatterings of rain — BKp before 9 — In the mtg. Annie & I went to Baptist Ch. & heard Hendrick — after that a short walk, cut shorter by a light rain. Dinner at 1.30. After that much napping & a late walk with Annie — Tea soon after 6 — Eveg very chilly with a little rain — we sat about until about 9 when Matt & Ed. went home — we got to bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### June, Monday 6, 1881

Wonderfully bright & beautiful day — After BKp — Annie & I went up town — then back & I got to work upon my 3d. Osgood Etching illustrating Emerson's "Apology" — I laid a new ground — made a camera-lucid reduction of my subject & then got a poor transfer — owing to the stretching of wet tracing paper my other transfer was too large for the plate. Dinner at 1 — Worked on my etching until a little after 4 — then wagon & horses came & I took out Annie, Father & Matt. — We went to the top of College Hill — then by East Road & by Denman's Hill down upon South Road & Uncle Millie's & out towards Hyde Park for a mile or so farther — On our way back stopped at Uncle W's — Saw them all (also Rev. Mr. Lawrence) drove over the place & then home at 7 — had tea — Eveg. yawning until 9.30 — To bed at 10.
