## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 25, 1881

Heavy rain during the n'tl. Got up, still feeling wretched about the head. B'kf't. soon after 8. Vail called — went over to building in pelting rain to see how the brick laying was going on, then downtown, got back quite exhausted & lay down on the bed. Annie read Romola aloud. Dinner soon after 1 — Storm cleared away — bright sunshine — strong wind but not cold. about 3.30 went out with A. & had a ride of 3/4 of an hour. She bought some towels. I left her at about H's. very tired, when I got back but began to feel better — Tea at 6.30 — Florence & — there — Still feeling better — a couple of games of cards — To bed at 10.30. Some heavy rain & wind — Enjoyed a good night's rest, the first in four nights.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 26, 1881

Brilliant m'f. with strong N.W. wind. Got up feeling much better. After b'kf't., wrote a letter to Alonzo Roberson of Binghamton Sash Manuf'r. Between 10 & 11 went over to the house with A. & spent some time directing matters, then back. — again at 2, went over & then down to P.O. Dinner about 1.15 — After that, A. Read aloud to me until near 3. then I went over to building — there until 4.30 — then in & Rested until tea time at 6.30. Eve'g. A. Read aloud — me had a couple of games of cards & I worked upon my plans or house drawings. To bed at 11.
