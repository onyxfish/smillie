## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 29, 1881

forty ninth anniversary of
fathers & mothers wedding —
Cold, wintry day — very quiet
& overcast — much like a
late Nov. day — Bkfst about
8 — after that went up town
ordered a doz. shirts at Eighmi[illegible]
& did other messages — called
on Mary — After 11 when I got
home — matie until 1? — then
up-town to P.O. — Dinner at
1.30 — Claire came at 1 o'clk[illegible]
About 3.30 we had a saunt[er]
together over Springside &c —
Tea at 6.30 — We [illegible] celebrated her —
in a very quiet way the anni-
versary (49?) of Father's & Mother's
wedding — The Hillsiders all
came in & seemed to have
a good time — To bed
at about 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 30, 1881

New York

Dismal day — Bkfst at 7.30
left home in a drizzling, sleety
storm — got to N.Y. at 11 & then
to studio — I continued the
exhibiting of Bridgeman plate —
Had lunch soon after 1 —
worked until after 5 —
then Mr. Koehler came in for
few minutes — but I had to
go to Grand Central Depot when
met Annie at 5.45 just in
from Boston — went home with
her — To 37th St. for a half hour —
then again to 38th St. and had
dinner — Spent the evening
there — Annie & I alone — I
made a general sketch of
Montrose grounds for Walter
as requested — To 37th St. at
10.45 & to bed.
