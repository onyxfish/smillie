## Left Page

### October, Friday 21, 1881

Charming day — out to bldg
before bkfst — Cottrell started
this mtf. for Binghamton
to get a bricklayer to put
up the chimneys — Bkfst. at
8.30 — All the mtf. out of doors
with the men — Eugene Sartinbs
did not work at all in the mtf.
& the team but 3 hours — I lent
them to a neighbor in trouble.
Dinner soon after 1 — Then
a nap — about 3 again went
to building — A. sunned her-
self on her piazza — At 5.30
went down town to P.O. met
Casey & got his bill — Tea at
6.15 — Evelf. very tired —
Read the papers — fussed
awhile over my drawings,
played a game of High Low
Jack with A. & to bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 22, 1881

Lovely day. But an unpleas-
ant feeling of languor amounting
late in the day almost to in-
ability to move made it hard
for me to enjoy it — I kept
about with Sartinbs & the team
directing grading of road —
Dinner between 1 & 2 —
Early part of afternoon
again at the house — Then
made out checks for the week
— once more to the house &
& then down town — Had
hard work to sit up to
take my tea & spent the
evef. on my bed, altho'
Florence Sartinbs had come
up to play cards with us
got to bed for the night
soon after 10.

In the mtf. A. & I rode over
to Jno. Frink's & he said he note
he held of H.C. Tyler's ago. me
dated Sep. 29, 1876 — for $250.00
