## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 15, 1881

Another very fine day, altho overcast late in the afternoon.
Got to studio at 9.30 + to work on 33 × 44. Painting over the
foreground, — dug away at it until 1 o'clk — out to lunch
at 2 — back at 3 — painted until 4.30 — then a rest, as
I feel very tired — t at 6 to 37° 8°, dressed + at 6.46 to
38° 8° — Ned. + Mary Cook, to dinner, also Emma + Edith.
At 7.20 Ned, Mary, Annie + I went to Chickering Hall and
heard Mendelssohn Glee club concert — an extremely enjoyable
affair — sat with Fanny + Will K— To 38° 5° at 11 — t to 37° 5°.
+ to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 16, 1881

During last night, a fall of an inch or more of snow,
which made bad walking until the afternoon. Got to studio
at 9.30 + spent the mtg. in getting etchings into frames
+ filling up lists for the London [illegible]. Out to lunch at 1.45
— then to Moore's Gallery — back at 3.30 — at 4 went to ride —
Coleman not going I rode his horse. — Scribbled as usual
+ got to studio at 7.30 + had tea. — wrote awhile + at 9
went to see Mother — found her in bed, unwell: — + Will, Chare. Geo.
Nellie J. here visiting — To 37° 5°.
at 10.30 — + to bed before 11,
among the callers to day —
Miss Rich of Boston + Stephen
Mellstood, + Wm Pate.
