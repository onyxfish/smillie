## Left Page

### August, Monday 22, 1881

Delicious mng. Went to studio right after bkfst — could not resist Miss Hollenback's invitation to go out riding — 3 seats — all full — I drove & we had a delightful time getting back at about noon. Dinner aftn & then to studio — worked on West elevation drawing — went to P.O. & to see Cottrell at 5. Tea soon after 6 — Evening promenading on piazza & later along the road — the heavens a glorious sight — To bed about 10.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 23, 1881

Clear, breezy day — Three men commenced digging the drain from foundation of house to cesspool at foot of hill — Cottrell straightened out the little gone on Mr. Lusk's fence — money lane, by agreement with Mr. L. The rest of mg spent writing letters — to P.O. at 2.30 Dinner after 1 — again to studio — wrote letter to A.F. Oakey — & superintended work generally — to P.O. just before 6 — Tea soon after — at 8 A & I Fosk Jr, C. & wife to Court Ho to see the Cora Van Tassell Troupe play Casita — Can't say much for the playing but we were amused until 10 — To bed at 11 —
