## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 20, 1881

Overcast & threatening in the
mfg, but in the afternoon
it cleared charmingly — got
to studio at 9.30 — Soon after,
Mr. Jat. R. Osgood called & some
time was spent discussing
details of "Poets & Etchers" — to
be published next fall.
I mounted some of my etchings
— got out a set to send to
Cyrus Davis & cut paper, etc
— Lunch about 2 — worked
over my pfs & wrote a letter
to Koehler — at 4 went up to
Dickli's & got Cha's horse "Nell"
went to Central Park & met
Sam. Coleman & we rode together until 6 — then to 37½ S[t.]
— to studio & had tea at 7.30
— Atrg went to Annie's —
at 10.15 to 37¾ S[t.] & to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 21, 1881

Cool mfg, but warm later in
the day — Nearly 10 when I got to
studio — Spent the mfg pressing over
etching pfs. Mr. Frederic Condit
came in about noon — Took us
to Delmonico's to lunch — back to
studio at 3 — then Mr. C's sister
came & went off with Mr. C — at
4.30 I went to see W.S. Macy &
then to subrm of Am. Socy. where
I met Annie. to 38½ S[t.] at 6.30 —
Fanny, Emma, Grace & [illegible] there —
to tea — at 8, to singing club —
9, to studio. Mounted some
etching pfs — wrote some notes
— at 10.30 went again to club
— Took a. home & got to 37½
S[t.] & to bed soon after 11.
