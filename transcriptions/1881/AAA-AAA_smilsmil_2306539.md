## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 1. 1881.

a lion-like entrance. Rain in the m'f., Snow ale day + a cold, tearing wind at night. Got to studio at 9.30 + spent the m'f in doing a variety of odd jobs. All went to Hamlet Black's wedding at the house. No. 9 M. 38' S— Got through my congratulations + at 2 at Ashland, sto to lunch — then to Stony's studio, to Kurtz's to Dewey + Featurg's + to Brook's Bros. Back at 5 — Tea in studio at 7.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 2. 1881.

Cold raw day — Spent the m'f with Father, Mother and Kate at Kurtz's having photos taken — After 12 when I got to studio — then to work on a new sketch after Geo. H. Story's painting, for Academy notes. Lunch at 2 — finished pen sketch + at 4 went with Salman to ride horseback — after that, my usual send to studio at 7.15 where I had tea — then made a short call on Father + Mother — Matt. and — from Po'k this afternoon — after 9 when I got to Annie's, feeling very subdued + tired — to 37' St + to bed about 11.
