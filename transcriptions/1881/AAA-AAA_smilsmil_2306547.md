## Left Page

### March, Saturday 19, 1881

Dismal day — Rain commenced before 11 & continued nearly all day — BkFs. with Annie — then to studio — at 10.30 to N.A. of D — Varnishing Day — Scrubpalette and worked on "Evening Shadows" until after 1 o'clk — intensifying both darks & lights. This well hung in the large gallery — After 2 when I got to Ashland H°. & had lunch there to Studio, wrote letters to Father & Kate & spent the rest of P.M. with Kate Pilsey & friend & Misses Abbate & Canning — Tea at 7 — At 8.30 went to Annie's — there until 10 — then to 37th S. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 20, 1881

As dismal as yesterday, but not quite so wet — most of the day mizzling rain & fog — heavier rain at night — BkFs. at 9 o'clk — then to studio and wrote one or two letters — then to D. Hastings' to church — met there Geo. & Charl — after service went with them over the new Union League Club H°. At 2 went to Annie's to dinner — about 5 we went to Ned's & made a short call on Bertha Lewis. At 6.30 I went to studio & fixed fire — back to 38th S. at 7.30 & had tea — Everg. Annie read aloud — I left at 10.30 To bed at 11.
