## Left Page

### April, Monday 4, 1881

Somewhat overcast — blustering
& cold — All day fighting
against a miserable head
ache — Made believe work
but really did nothing —
Mr. Chadwick (the Rev.) came
at noon & I went with him
to see the Am. Socy's pictures
at Morris's gallery — Lunch at
1.30 — Niel called — at about
3 I went up to 110 E. 38th St. to
see how the painting was
progressing — then to Warren's
in 42d St. — then to Hyock's & to
Campbell's — got to Annie's at
6.15 — then to the K's — Emma
& [illegible] there — tea — then all to
Standard Theatre & saw Billie
Taylor — In spite of my headache
it was very funny — Home
with Annie at 11 — then to
37th St. & to bed before 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 5, 1881

Dismal, blustering cold day.
Got to studio before 10 — Nearly
all of the morning was spent
fussing over etching &p. getting
them ready to send to the exhib.
of am. etchings to open at the
Boston Museum of Fine Arts — Young
Oakey called at 1 & we went to
the Ashland to lunch — All
the p.m. I worked on A.B.N.
Co's "Night, Portland, O. & Mt. Hood".
At 6 went to Bisbee's & ordered
a pair of shoes — Tea in studio
at 7 — After that, wrote a note
to Mrs. Shunger. After 9 when
I got to Annie's — there until
10.30 — then to 37th St. & to bed.
