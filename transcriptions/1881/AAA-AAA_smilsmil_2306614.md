## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 2. 1881.

Charming day. Worked all the
m'g on pen sketches, ill. "The
Lakeside" by Whittier, "Walled
Round by sombering Pines" — To
dinner at 1, but did not get it
for 1½ or m. Then to Studio and
worked until after 3 — At 3.30
went out riding — lost our way
over towards Brooklyn on the
way to Dimock got astray on
some cross roads — very pleasant
notwithstanding they were very bad.
got to Town at 7 & then had tea.
Evg. a. Read aloud & after
we took a short moonlight
walk — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 3. 1881.

A hot day with milky atmosphere
veiling the distance — after 1½ p., to
studio & worked on pen sketch
illustrating Emerson's "Snow Storm".
At noon went down Town. Dinner
soon after 1 — Courtright. Ret. home.
After dinner Read aloud awhile
then to Studio, finished sketch
of the m'g, also tail piece for
same poem. At 5.45 went to
P.O. Tea soon after 6 — Evg. very
warm & perfectly still — Sat upon
upper piazza until 10.30 — then
to bed.
