## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 16, 1881

Another very cool, charming day — much of the evg. spent at the mills attending to the details of building — also looking for quarters for father & mother as well as ourselves as Mr. C. has notified us that he cannot take care of us after Sept. dinner at 1 — afternoon spent in doing some drawing & a good deal of bossing building for some walls. Rev. Mr. Sherwood called after 5 P.m. Tea at 6 — Mrs. Coles (nee Woodward) Miss Hollenback's aunt, arrived — after tea A & J went to P.O. Met Priscilla Pome coming to call on us — we walked to P.O. then to Priscilla's & called upon her — then to our quarters where the evg. passed in playing cards & reading. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 17, 1881

Another beautiful cool day — after blkfst — called on Saml. Sayne — then to fair ground & finally to studio — most of the evg. writing home & in consultation with Cottrell. Dinner at 1.30 — again to studio & laid out sketch of "Evening Shadows" on Chicago catalogue but managed to do very little work — Tea after 6 — on our way to P.O. stopped at W.J. T's — & found on Diagya. Mr. Thayer & Mrs. Barlow & Americus Ga. — Mr. & Mrs. Cornelius Forde & Mrs. H.T. Turnill — then for half an hour — then on down town — back to Country Hts after a game or two at cards to bed soon after 10.

On going to P.O. was amused by the entertainment of a nomadic vendor of a vermifuge — dressed in Border costume — long hair — two assistants — negro minstrels — two children — singing — jokes — lecture on scalping, rattlesnakes, & false worms — bitters served free — pills 25¢ a box — Place, music stand in Public street
