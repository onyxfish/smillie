## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 29. 1881.

Very hot day in spite of a breeze — Went up to Yale — missed him as usual — then to Rennie's — then to Sloan's — then to studio at near 11 — Mng spent in arranging pictures for the summer — To dinner at 1.30 — Matt & Kate went to Po'k. on Mary Powell — I. to studio at 3 — all the afternoon spent in clearing up. Dr. Yale called; Home to tea at 7 — Evening, Chas. Collins called — To bed before 11 —

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 30. 1881.

Warm, but with a very pleasant breeze blowing — Wrote notes to Will & Farner & got to studio about 10 — then to Bank & to Knordlar's — again to studio packing all the mng. To dinner at 2 — After that went to Rennie's — then again to Knordler's & at 4.15 to studio & packed boy for Montrose — very tired. Home to tea at 7 — Evening spent quietly resting. To bed soon after 10.
