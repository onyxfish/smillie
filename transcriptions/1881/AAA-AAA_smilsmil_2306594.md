## Left Page

### June, Thursday 23, 1881.

Delightful day, altho' at times cloudy & threatening showers. Got to Studio about 10 & to work printing "Delayed Travelers" — Soon got it finished & well on Jap. paper for pp. To dinner at 1.30 — back at 3 & printed until near 6 — getting 7 pp. to my liking. Then had a short rest & at 6.30 went to Nellie J's with a pkg left at the Studio. There for some time looking over her wedding presents — then home to tea late & very tired. Partly spent Reading newspapers & Resting — To bed abt 0.30.

In the mng. went with Annie to Black's & to Starr's to look at some Silver Tea Sets for wedding present for J.J.?

## Right Page

### June, Friday 24, 1881.

Another charming day — After bkfst. went to Dr. Case's but he was out — then to Studio — then to Krimmel & Voigt's at about 11 — Started the printing of Delayed Travelers — then had pp. taken from Bridgeman plate kept printing until about 1.30. Then home to dinner at 2 — Miss Juliette Ireland & Mr. Satchell? to Bridgeport this P.M. — to Studio at 3 — Printing with Jo during the Rest of P.M. Home to Tea at 7 — Partly more reading aloud & to bed abt 0.30.
