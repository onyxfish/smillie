## Left Page

### July, Saturday 23. 1881.

Pleasant, breezy day — Part of the mtg. overcast in a middly threatening way. Until noon in & about studios — watching the digging of the cellar and trying to work upon a drawing for A.B.N. Co —, a view of Mt. Hood & city of Portland. Oregon then down Town — dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon, more watching & work on drawing — at 6 went down Town — Tea at 6.30 — feeling unwell. I was not able to go out with Annie making calls, but spent most of the evng on my bed. To bed at 11 o'clk. feeling better.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 24. 1881.

Beautiful day, but warm — Bkfs. a little before 9 — went to Presbych & heard a Mr. Conduit — after service I went to studios and wrote until dinner time. Dinner at 2 — then again to studios & wrote for about an hour, then out in hammock under maples, a Reading aloud to me. Som aft 6 went to house — after 7 I went down Town, then back and at 8 a. & I had our tea — Evng on piazza until after 10, then to bed.

Mr. Leonard Searse died this mtg.
