## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 15, 1881

Charming day — after bkfst went to Dewey & Keating's & tried on some new clothes. Did not get to studio until near 11 — then found Kate Parker & her friend there. Did not get to my etching until near noon — had lunch in studio at 2 + at 3 went to Kimball's for Matt & Kate & out with them & selected wall paper for Po'k house — back to studio at near 5, worked for a while etching then to 37 S., dressed & all go took Annie to Ned Cook's where we dined with Bishop & Annie Putnam & Miss Bertha Lewis & [illegible]. To 38 S. at 11 — then to 37 & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 16, 1881

Very mild & somewhat overcast — got to Studio about 9.30 & to work at on Etching — stuck closey to it all day. Out to dinner at 1.30 — then to Hunt & to the Kimball's — bid Watt & Kate good bye, as they return to Po'k — his a.m. — then back to studio & etched until 4 — at 4.30 went to Dickel's & out in the Park on "Sargent" for an hour — very delightful — To 37 S. and came d — to studio at 7.30 & had tea — to N.A. of D. at 8.30. Annual meeting of W. L. Socy. To 37 S. about 1 — & to bed.

Today Annie bought house No. 110 E. 38 m S.
