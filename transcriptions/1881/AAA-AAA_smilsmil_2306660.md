## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 2. 1881.

Heavy mizzling fog, with rain at times, making a dismal, wet day. After bkft. went to house & for a time superintended matters there — then back & drew on plans. Again to house at 12.30 — then to P.O. Dinner at 1 — After that wrote a letter to Geo. & went to P.O. on my way back I stopped for Annie at Auntt's — then to house — Mr. Colpe called — kept by different things until after dark — then to P.O. & to see Henry Turrell — Tea soon after 6. Evtf. worked awhile on drawings & had a game of cards with Annie — To bed about 10.30.

To-day Recd. letter & statement of acct. from Jas. Bourlet of London.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 3. 1881.

Overcast mrf — still mired — Bkfp. at 8.30 — After that went to bkf., Studio let down upon its new foundation partially — Heavy showers came up from the S.W. — very much interfering with work — back & to work on full size drawing of details under broad sills — Soon after 12 again went to house — then to P.O. & to dinner at 1 — After noon, drawing on bay window plans — very heavy rain — at 4.15 — again to bldf — Later, went down town & paddled around in the rain until near 6 — then to P.O. & tea. Florence there — Evtf. a rubber of whist, then I wrote until 10 — a long letter to Charl answering him concerning the Bourlet business which seems to be a grand muddle — Heavy rain all the evtf. To bed a little before 11.
