## Left Page

### February, Friday 11, 1881

Delightful spring-like day. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on hirlight painting. Out to lunch at 1.30 & had a short walk. — Painted until near 4 — then to Riding school & rode for an hour, then to 37th St. & had my scrub. Got to 38th St. at 7.15 — Father, Mother & Kate there to dinner — we had a pleasant even. Father much interested to [illegible]. I went to 37th St. at 10.30 & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 12, 1881

Horrible day of storm — went to Annie's at 8.15 & had [illegible] — then rode down with her to 25th St. Got to studio before 10 o'clk. & in spite of the darkness worked on my hirlight painting. Out to lunch at 1.30 — Painted until after 4 — then had an unrestful nap in my big chair. At 5.40 walked up to 37th St. in a dense fog — feeling quite strong — to 38th St. at 6.30. Edith Wright came on from Phila. & went with us to Phil. Concert. I suffered from headache — Kate in my seat on George's ticket — I took her in charge leaving her at 106 Mad. ave. then home with Annie & Edith to 37th St. & to bed at 11. Beautiful moonlight.
