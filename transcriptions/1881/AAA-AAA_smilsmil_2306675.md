## Left Page

### December, Friday 2, 1881

Charming day — after bkfs: went to Standard Theatre & changed seats (Patience) for Fo- night for We? night — next, then to Newman's — then to Studio spent the rest of my? writing to Cottrell — then to Bank & to Post Office Station & home to dinner at 1 — Back at 3 — Did a little on Gold Hill drawing — Miss Lilian Taylor called & I showed her how to sharpen etching points — Home at 6 — Tea about 7 — Matt & Kate went to Opera with Charlie & Jennie — I read a couple of chapters of Romola aloud to Annie then to work on N. Elevation of M. house until 11 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 3, 1881

Dull & threatening — got to studio at 9.30 & spent the m'f languidly drawing on Gold Hill for A.B.N. Co. a headache growing upon me — to dinner at 1 — after that, gave up and went to bed — up again at 6 — Annie & I had tea together in 3d story front — Susy, lying around until about 11 when I went to bed — Rain came in the afternoon lightly but at night it settled down to it.
