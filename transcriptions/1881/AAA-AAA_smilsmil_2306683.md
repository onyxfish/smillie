## Left Page

### December, Sunday 18, 1881

Charming day — Wlp — after 9.
Annie & I went to the church
of the Divine Paternity, mainly,
I fear, to see Tiffany's decorations.
Don't altogether like it — nor was
I spiritualized by the sermon.
After service — a. walked up to
50th St. then got into Mad. ave car
for home while I went on up
to Central Park & Lounsbury's.
Home to dinner at 2 — After
that, headache & a nap. Up
at 5 — wrote letters to Father
& Ehninger — Tea at 6.30 —
Eveg. Mr. & Mrs. Saml. Coleman
called — at 9.30 went out &
had another good walk —
up to Central Park & back.
Then read awhile & to bed
at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 19, 1881

Very mild & charming — got to
studio at 9.30 — at work on W.C.
mounting "Moonlight" & stretch-
ing paper for other drawings —
at 12 went to Bank — G.W.H.
Ritchie called on my return.
Home to dinner at 1.20 — back
at 2.30 & for an hour or so
was engaged with paper
stretching — then went down-
town — to Sackett's — got an acct.
book for Annie — to A.B.N. Co. —
to see Will about some work —
to P.O. to see Dup. of R.R. service
to Patterson Hardware — then
home at 6.15 — Near 7 when
we had tea — at 8.15 A. & I
went to Coleman's — Piano
quartettes — I went up to
studio for a while — wrote a
letter to Ticknor — then again
to C's — there until 10.30 —
then home & to bed soon
after 11.
