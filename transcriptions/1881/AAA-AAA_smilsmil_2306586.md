## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 7, 1881

Overcast & very cool — Bkf-
at 7.30 — then had a fire made
in furnace & stove in Father's
room — Then to work upon my
etching — At 10 Took a walk
up town with Annie — then
again at my etching. Dinn-
at 1 — before which time it
commenced to Rain. Uncle
W. came in — we were all to
have gone out to Hill Side, but
for the Rain, which later in
the afternoon fell steadily —
I continued the work on my
etching, getting it ready for
the acid — Tea between 6 & 7 —
Part of evef. Annie read
aloud "A Fair Barbarian" by
Mrs. Burnett — To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 8, 1881

Overcast & milder with more
or less of mizzling Rain during
the day — To work at my biting
after bkf. About 10 took a walk
up town with Annie, Matt &
Kate — then again at my work
until dinner time at 1 —
continued biting & stopping out
until 6.30 — then tea — After
that, as it ceased Raining
for a time Annie & I went
out for a walk — When we
Ret'd we found Mr. & Mrs. Ham
Booth & Rev. E. A. Lawrence —
Soon after Mr. & Mrs. & Miss Loomis
were added to the callers — a
pleasant evef. Near 11 when
we got to bed.
