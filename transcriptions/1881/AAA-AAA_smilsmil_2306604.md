## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 13. 1881.

Another hot day — worked all the mrg on plan of second story & about 12 went down Town to P.O. & saw Cottrelle — To dinner about 1.30 — Afternoon very breezy — Sky overcast, mitigating the heat. Casey, the well digger called — so did Cottrelle & much of my afternoon was spent with them — Mr. Isaa Riley also called. At 5.30 I walked down Town with her — Tea soon after 6 — Fanny Payson & Bessie Cooper called — Between 8 & 9 A. & I walked out to the lake — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 14. 1881.

Cool, brilliant mrg. After 9 when I got to studio. To work upon front elevation of he. Soon got into trouble with the roof. About noon went to P.O. & other places down-town with A. & back to dinner soon after 1 — At 2.30 went down Town and opened Bank acct with Mr. H. Coulper & Co. Then to Mr. Boyd's & borrowed one of his architectural books — then had my hair cut & about 5 Rei? to studio — fussed awhile over the drawing of roof then to tea at 6 — After that A. & I walked down Town to P.O. then to Payne's & Clifford's — back at 8.30 & found that Mr. Douglas, Bessie Riley & Mr. Janvier had called — At 9 we went to "Lawn Party" at Fair Ground — too early — back to house & again to the ground at 10 — very cool every. Picturesque effect of dancing floor under the trees lighted by Chinese lanterns — Back & to bed soon after 11.
