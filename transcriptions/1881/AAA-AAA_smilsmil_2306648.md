## Left Page

### October, Sunday 9, 1881

Charming day. Bkf. at 9 - after that, out with E - went to Mr.
Shepherd, then to Dreye's, then I left him & went alone to the
Park. I walked until 12, enjoying the air & views of distance immensely
— then in — lunch at 12.30. After that, went down town & got camp
& returning, soon after 2 My & me
E. & I started for Ballston Spa.
Annie was not well enough to
go — the drive was charming —
we had a very pleasant time
at Col. Baker's — delicious light
as we drove home to dinner at
5 — (while at Col. B's — looked
over some clever studies in oil
from nature by his son Willie) —
Evef. — Evef. in studio —
to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 10, 1881

Clear, crisp mtf., had a short
walk before bkf. — Bkf. at 9 —
about 10. E. & I started in a
buggy for the old mill — When
we got there, sky was overcast
& slight showers fell — cold wind
from the N. W. — we struggled at
our work until near 1 o'clk.
I got a pencil sketch of the
mill — then back to the E's &
had lunch — after that, to
work in studio upon a water-
color sketch of mtf. subject
until 5 o'clk. Dr. Pearsall
came to see Annie who was
not so well to day & so did
not get out of bed. About 5
I went out for a walk — m[illegible]
at 6 to dinner — Evef. in
studio taking [illegible] from a late
Etching by E — in chatting
& in writing. To bed soon
after 10.
