## Right Page

### January, Saturday 1, 1881

Brilliant, cold day — B[illegible] at
8.30 - got to studio at 9.30, at
10.30 went to 37. S. for something
I had forgotten. Returned at 11
& then to work on "views near
Portsmouth" — J. W. Ehninger
came in about noon, and in
the city with Mr. E. last
night. Out to lunch at 2,
back to work at 3 & stuck
to it as long as I could see
getting in a good days work.
Rec. telegram saying that all
was well at home. Rested for
awhile — then a little writing
& at 6 went to the Dreland's —
Kitty & Minna snow bound at
Julie's in Maryland — At 7.15
Coleman's — Mr. Gray & Florence
there — had a pleasant tea time
& at 8 went to Annie's — there
until 10.30 — then to 37. S.
& to bed.
