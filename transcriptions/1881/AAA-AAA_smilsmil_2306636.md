## Left Page

### September, Thursday 15, 1881

Still bright & clean, but blowing
hard from the east — hard work
to put up timbers against
such a gale — Spent most of
the mf up on the timbers
setting up 2d story partitions —
Early dinner on acct of the
fair — Again went to studio
& for a time A & I went to the
fair, a very poor show —
back to studio at 3 [illegible]
until after 5 — then to P.O.
Tea at 6 — Evg. reading
newspaper & pacing piazza
— to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 16, 1881

Still blowing very hard & cold.
After blkft went to foundry & gave
orders for some castings to go under
piazza posts — Mr & Mrs. Read off for
Saratoga. Soon after A & I went to
studio — little progress in building
owing to the wind — I spent much
of the mf arranging money
matters & making out checks —
Dinner at 1 — the afternoon
I spent in planning and
directing work to be done
during my absence — before
6 went down town — to
tea at 6.15 — Evng part of
eveg, scanned Verne's "voyage
to the Centre of the Earth" — then
to work packing trunk — to
bed soon after 11.
