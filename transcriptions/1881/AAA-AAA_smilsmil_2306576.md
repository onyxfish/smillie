## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 18. 1881.

Raw & cold with considerable rain. Bt fo'. at 8.30 — did not get to studio until near 10 —
feeling out of sorts did not do much work — Made charcoal for drawing for A.B.N. Co that was called for at 1 — at 1.30 walked up home to dinner in quite a shower of rain. Back about 3. Worked on charcoal sketch for an'll. to atch. for the B& good book Aldrich's "Bellagio" — D om at [illegible] 6 up home in a wetting mist. — Ned & Mary to tea — Eve'g spent in Revising list and listening to Annie's reading from "The Mill on the Floss" — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 19. 1881.

Heavily overcast & much milder clearing late in the afternoon —
got to studio before 10. Spent the morning battling with charcoal trying to work out a design illustrating Aldrich's little poem "Nocturne. Bellagio." Home to lunch at 1.30 — [illegible] K. there — back at 3 — worked on design & spent part of P.m. writing friends for addresses to complete my list. — Home at 6.30. Emma Mary J. & Julie Spring there to tea. They left at 9 o'ck. — then Ned & wife & Miss Williams came & stayed until 10.30 — About 11 when I got to bed. Rain again during the night.
