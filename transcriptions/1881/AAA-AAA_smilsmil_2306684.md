## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 20. 1881.

Mild & slightly overcast most of the day — Got to Studio at
9.30 & charcoaled sketch 10×20 "Crisp October — near
Saratoga" — Fixed up pk
of ink & jap. paper for Ehninger
& pk + copy of Poets & Etchers for
Gifford & took the two [illegible]
to Efts. office at 12.30 — then
home to dinner — back at 3
a little work on charcoal
sketch — made a letter to
Boulet & sent him order on
Hamilton for his bill — Home
at 5.10 — dressed & at 6. A.
& J went up to Surie's to
dine — to "meet aunt Huldah
who is going South on Thurs."
Geo. & Nellie also there —
Sat down at 6.30 — pd until
8.30 — a very good dinner &
course & every thing seemed
to go smoothly — adieux at
10.30 — home at 11 — To bed
at 12 —

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 21. 1881.

Rather raw — later part of day
overcast & at 6. Rain — Got up
with a heavy head — hardly
hoping to keep up through
the day — mistaken, happily.
After Bkft. went to Bates drug
store, then to Renners' — then
to Putnams' — then to Knoedler's
& about 10.45 got to studio —
Pk. am from Ehninger arth
fortung [illegible] — & a letter from Mr. E. —
made a reply & traced part of
"Crisp Oct." Home to dinner at
1 — back at 3 — a little work —
Mr. Morse called. Soon after
4 went to Arnold & Constables'
& met Annie — we looked at
Rugs & curtains — Home to tea
at 6 — Dr. Adams arr'd from
Ridgefield — Evy drawing
sketches for curtains, & to bed
at 10.30
