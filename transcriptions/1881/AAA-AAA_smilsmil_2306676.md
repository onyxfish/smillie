## Left Page

### December, Sunday 4, 1881

Cold rain — Blkp. about
8.30 — after that walked up
to Dnyale's — but he had started
to get married (next Tuesday
last Friday P.M. Then home
& handled wood five in part
Annie dressed & came down
the m'f spent in reading
& writing — Dinner soon after
2. a nap in front of the
fire — a little more reading
— tea at near 7 — Eve'g
writing a letter to Father
& Mary — Young Baker of
Ballston, called — after
10 went to G.C. Depot with
letters — to bed at about
11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 5, 1881

Brilliant day — got to studio
at 9.30 & to work on Gold Hill
finished it before 1 — Miss
Liggett called — Home to dinner
afternoon, went down town,
first to A.B.N. Co. with drawing
then about, looking for heavy
slate taken for mounts for Jap.
b/s "Poets & Etchers" — could
not find what I wanted.
Got some light mounting b°
from Pate — Home to tea at
6.30 — Char. there — he took
Matt & Kate to Mad. Sq. Theatr.
Annie read aloud to me
while I signed Jap. pks —
P. & E. To bed soon after 11.
