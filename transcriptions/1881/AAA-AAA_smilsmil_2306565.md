## Left Page

### April, Sunday 24, 1881

Very hot & dusty — up late &
had help: after 10 — at 11 I
went to A's & found that she
was in bed, used up with a
headache, after having started
with Geo. C. Valentine. Chas. Collins
Emma S. & Helen Butler for
Woodbury, L.I. — I went to studio
& lay up on lounge there until 1 —
then rode up to Charley Quintu's
& had dinner — at 4.30 called on
Wile at the Buckingham & got
to Annie's at about 6 — She
was up & feeling better — Tea
before 7 — Evening spent quietly
neither of us feeling very playful.
Left soon after 10 — To 37° S. &
to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 25, 1881

Very mild day — To Studio
as usual — did not do much
but recieve callers & write
notes — Got a letter from S.C. F.
now in Georgia — answered it,
Farmer called — To lunch at
2. & then a stroll in the [illegible]
late in the afternoon went
to Tailors there to see P. Johnston &
saw him about the Bridgman
picture — he agrees to buy it —
then to Studio & had tea at
4.30 — then to Annie's —
Rennie's man to hang pictures
but he did not put in an
appearance — worked awhile
on list — at 10.45 to 37°
S. & to bed — Heavy shower
during the night.
