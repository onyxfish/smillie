## Left Page

### February, Saturday 5, 1881.

Still lousey weather and much milder — got to studio at 9.30 + to work painting over an old canvas, a view of the Cathedral Rocks in the Yosemites — Mr. Nesbrit called + Father took her to lunch with him — M. Morse also called with a lady friend — I went to lunch at 2 — back + painted until 5 — at 6 to 38 S. dressed + at 6.45 to 38 + S. Annie used up with a headache — at 8 went with Emma to Coleman's music — Trios by Mr. C., Mosenthal + Berguer. About 20 friends there — near midnight where Mosenthal, Emma + I walked up to 38 + S. M. entertaining us with his + Tones of Tho. Thomas + others — To bed soon after 12.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 6, 1881.

Charming day — after bkfs. I went to Mad. ave. — found Father + Kate preparing to go to church. I sat for a time with Mother then to studios, wrote a note + at 12 went to Annie's — there until 1 — then to A.V.V.'s + had dinner with him, aunt N. + Mr. J. T. — Tunice + Charlie being at Yonkers — left at 3 and then to Annie's — with her went to Centeeny + saw pictures — after that, stopped in + saw Father + Mother — To 38 S. soon after — tea — the rest spent in reading — and to bed about 10.30.
