## Left Page

### January, Saturday 22. 1881.

A mizzling rain in the m'f. dull — disagreeable day. Trying to clear, with a rift of sunshine about 4 o'clk. Got to studio about 10 but was so useless that I realley did nothing all day — Father came & got started at work in my etching corner — with him I went to 106 Mad. ave. at 5.30 — & at 6.30 I was at Amin. had dinner & soon after 8 went to W.C. [illegible] at h.a. there until 10 — then home & a little after 11 to 37. 8. & to bed —

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 23. 1881.

Very pleasant m'f. Up at 8 feeling much better than yesterday — Shlp. at 9 — then to Studio where I spent the m'f on lounge & writing a note to Chas. Collins.
