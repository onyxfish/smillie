## Left Page

### July, Thursday 7, 1881

muggy, overcast and threatening
wet — but before noon it cleared
charmingly clear morning
but in the afternoon the
weather thickened & light
showers fell. Much of the
mff. was spent running
around down town — Was
at the studio only for a short
time. Dinner abt. After
that, feeling stupid I took
a nap. Before 4 horse & buggy
came & A. & I went out & drove
around until 6, several light
showers falling without harm
to us. Tea at 6 — then a walk
on piazza — & Reading until
after 10. Then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 8, 1881

Cool, wet day — after bkfst. paced
the piazza for awhile — then to
Studio with A., lighted a fine
in furnace just to dry the atmos-
phere. To work on ground plan
of house & about steadily until
after 12 — then we walked
down town & back to dinner
at 1 — again to Studio and
worked until after 5 — then
again a walk & to tea at 6 —
In the afternoon it was much
warmer — rather muggy — after
tea walked the piazza until
Dan Sayre & wife called — about
8.30 Miss Hallenback, Courtright,
A. & I commenced a game of whist.
Mr. & Mrs. Park & Joe Richards called
they left about 10 when we
finished our game & to bed
at 10.45.
