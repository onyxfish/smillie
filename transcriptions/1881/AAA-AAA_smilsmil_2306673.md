## Left Page

### November, Monday 28. 1881.

Charming, clear winter day —
To studio about 9.30 — the
m'f was all given to work
& worry over an elevation
of north side of Montrose
house & writing a number
of letters. Home to dinner
at 1 — back at 2.30 — little
accomplished — but began
a mining mill drawing
for A.B.N. Co. Tea at near
4 — Eve'g. Reading until
9 — then to work upon north
elevation & stuck to it until
11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 29. 1881.

Very mild, pleasant day — to
studio at 9.30. Did some
work on A.B.N. Co. drawing,
"Gore Hille", but not much.
Home to dinner at 1 — and
back to studio about 3 —
Miss Lilian Taylor (daughter
of Bayard Taylor) called and
I gave her some instruction
in laying etching grounds.
About 4.30 — went to Century
& paid dues — then to 51 W.
10th St. with Farrer's "Poets &
Etchers" [illegible], P.k. for his
autograph — walked up
to 23d with Nicoll, then to
studio & home soon after 6.
Matt & Kate arr. from Po'k.
Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g in
general confab — Geo. and
Nellie called — I made
my autograph upon a
number of Poet & Etcher
[illegible] — to bed at 10.30.
