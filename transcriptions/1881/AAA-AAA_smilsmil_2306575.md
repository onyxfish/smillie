## Left Page

### May, Monday 16. 1881.

Overcast & growing cooler during the day with light rains in the afternoon & evening — Bkfs. at 8.30
Got to studio, at 9.30 & spent the mng. with friends, writing and getting ready for work — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30.
Worked until 5 on A.B.N. Co. 3.a. drawing, a cathedral front, with spires — Home at 6.30 — tea about 7 — [illegible] Mrs. Parker & her daughter Margaret called — also bro. George — and we worked awhile over wedding cards not yet sent out owing to want of proper direction etc — etc — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 17. 1881.

Quite a chilly, raw mng. Bkfs. at 8.30 — 10 o'clk when I got to studio & built a little fire — Finished A.B.N. Co's drawing and at 1 o'clk Mills sent up another one for me to do — also, a sketch for a finished drawing was requested. I finished second S.A. drawing before 6 o'clk. — Home to tea at 6.30 in a heavy misère of rain. After tea, awhile with Geo. C. going over lists of those to whom wedding cards have been sent — there for some time worked upon corrections — then for a time Annie read to me and at 10.30 we went to bed.
