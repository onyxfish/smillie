## Left Page

### July, Monday 25, 1881

warm but breezy — feeling badly about the head — B[illegible]. at 8.30 — at 9.30 to studio and for awhile in easy chair — about 11 went down town & excused myself from going this afternoon on a long ride with the Parks. To house at 12 — lunch & at 12.45 Annie, & Misses Hallenback & Paine went off with the Parks to go to Montrose depot & then to Brooklyn — I went to studio & ate lunch there — worked on a B.M. Co's drawing "Mt Hood" — a heavy but short Thundershower passed over us just before 5. Tea after 6 — & another storm with very heavy thunder & sharp lightning about 8 — on piazza & in-doors until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 26, 1881

Charming day with light showers about — Still out of sorts but managed to finish drawing of Mt. Hood — Dinner at 1 — after that, wrote a letter to Wills, packed drawing and book called "The Workshop" & at 4 o'clk went to W.P. office, with it — at near 5 o'clk went riding with a, & Misses Hallenback & Paine — back at 6. Slight shower — Tea at 6.30 — Rueff at 8 — a. & I went to Court House & saw Theatrical representation of "Neighbor Jackwood" by an Amateur company from New Milford — a very bad play badly given — we were able to stand it until 9.30 — Then we gave out and went home & to bed at 10 — The play continued until 11.30.
