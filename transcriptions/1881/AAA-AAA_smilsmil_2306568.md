## Left Page

### April, Saturday 30. 1881.

*Trial Trip —*
*at James Wright's — Germantown —*

Very warm — Spent the morning finishing A.B.N. Co's drawing of a church in Ecuador — Ferguson called — in wretched health — much emaciated & coughing — I think he is a doomed man — Out to lunch at 2 — then to Bank at 3, Annie came to studio & we started off soon after. Got the 4 o'clk Bound Brook train — to Wayne Junction a little before 6 — walked to Jas. Wright's & had tea a little after 7 — J. W. not at home — Ernest sick with mumps. Evelf by a light wood fire until 10 o'clk — Rev. Mr. Saul, Long fellow — Took tea & spent part of the evelf with us — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 1. 1881.

nearly 8 when I got up — had up — about 8.45 — Delicious day — clear, breezy & warm — The morning was spent in the woods with Annie — Marion Wright and Spot, the dog — Back at about 1 with a headache — had a little up before dinner at 2 — Jas. W. his son Theodore & wife there — After dinner again on lounge as my headache was very severe — About 4, J. W. Marion, Annie & I went out to ride — Edeth traveled with a sore throat — Roads very dusty — breeze strong & quite cool — back about 6.30 — Headache still active. Took 1 gr. of Caffine & had relief at once. Tea between 7 + 8 — Evelf in general chatting — To bed at 10.45 —
