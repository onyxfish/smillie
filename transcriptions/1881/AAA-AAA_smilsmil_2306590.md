## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 15, 1881

Delicious m[illegible]. but cloudy
& showery — After b[illegible]. A. & J.
were out walking until after
10\. Slight shower soon after
11\. Spent rest of m[illegible] in
packing. Dinner about 1.
at 3 went out riding — for
3/4 of an hour had mother.
father & a — then returned
& took Kate in place of mother
Had a delightful ride over
the hill beyond the Trotting
track & to the Boardman place
Back before 6. Tea at 6.15.
A little before 7 Geo. arr.
from N.Y. city. A hurried
chat, & we (A. & J.) left at
7.15 — Took 7.45 Train and
got to N.Y. at 10 — Waited
until near 11 for baggage
& after unpacking, got
to bed about midnight.
Mrs Satchell & child & Miss
Juliette Ireland. visiting
at the house.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 16, 1881

Beautiful day — Got to studio
about 10 — near 11 Mr. Ticknor
of Osgood & Co. came — we
talked "Poets & Etchers" for
awhile — then went to Kimmel
& Voigt's — V not in — so J. ret.
to studio — home to dinner
at 1.30. At 3, again at K. & V.'s
& started the printing of my
3° Osgood plate, also the Job.
pfs. of my Bridgman Etching
— after 5 when I left — then
to studio — there until after
6 — then home — tea after 7 —
Evef. Avery Brown came in
for awhile — To bed at
10.30.
