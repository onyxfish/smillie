## Left Page

### April, Friday 8, 1881.

Very pleasant morning, but later the sky became overcast, the wind east, and promise of more bad weather
— Got to studio's before 10 — feeling collapsed in stomach region — made some notes & finished the A.B.N. Co. drawing of Portland & Mt. Hood. At 1.30 took a very light lunch in studio & at 2 went to Phil. Rehearsal. After that went with Annie to see some wood carpeting & then to Solomon's to see curtains — then to 110 E. 38' S'. & at 6 I got to 37' S'. lay upon my bed for half an hour — then to Annie's & had tea after 8 — dressed & with George went to Jno. Taylor Johnson's entertainment — to artists & friends. There until 10.45 — then to 37' S'. & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 9, 1881.

Very windy, spring like morning. Bkft. as usual at Annie's — then together, walked down to 25th S'. got to studio at 9.30 — to work on Bridgman Etching — Mr. Frederic Vinton of Boston called — Out to lunch at 2 — Oscar Viel came to my table & lunched with me. In the afternoon pulled 4 pfs of 2°. condition of my plate. Got to Annie's at 6.15; thoroughly tired — then to Fanny K's — Tea — then to last Philharmonic Concert of the Season — Enjoyed it — Rode up with Mary Cost & her friend to 40th S'. & Park ave — then home with Annie — then to 37' S'. & to bed at 11.30.
