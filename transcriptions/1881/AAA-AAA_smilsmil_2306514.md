## Left Page

### January, Monday 10, 1881

The nastiest possible mess —
Rain most of the mtf. Clearing
in the afternoon — Got to studio
soon after 9 + worked all day
on Mariner's Well — making it
look better but it will never
be any thing but an over worked
dull looking thing — Dinner
at 2 — at 4 went into Coleman
to Riding School + had a
ride — Then to 37t S. + had a
scmb + at 7.30 to studio +
had tea + at 8.30 to Annie's
— there until a little after
10 — the Reading "h. Mill on
the Floss" — then to 37t S. and
to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 11, 1881

Dull day — to studio at 9.30
much of the mtf spent in
cutting mat on Mariner's Well
frame — also in working on
the picture itself — Dinner
at 2 — after that worked on
drawings rec'd from Century —
at 5 out for a walk — called
on Mr. Parker, 106 Madison Ave,
to studio — Tea at about 7 —
at work mounting sketch
+ fixing frames until 9
then to Annie's — there
until 10.30 — then to
37t S. + to bed.
Many came to town
this P.m. to visit for
2 or 3 days.
