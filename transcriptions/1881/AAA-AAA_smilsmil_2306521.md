## Left Page

### January, Monday 24, 1881

Pleasant day — got to studio late, little done during the day as I was out of sorts with dyspepsia. Lunch at about 2.30 & the rest of the afternoon I was wandering about. About 6, at 37 S. & at 6.35 at Annie's who gave a very pleasant dinner party to Mr. & Mrs. Thuringer. Sumy & I — Geo. S. & C. — & Ned. C. & wife. Everything seemed to go off very nicely — left at 10.30 & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 25, 1881

Charming day, but cold. Got to studio before 10 — did very little but write a note or two. Still out of tune. Mr. Koehler came in about noon. So did young Oakey. At 1.30 — H. Geo. & I went to lunch. After that I went to Wallack's & got some tickets for Coleman — then to Sarony's & to Knoedler's & [illegible] Co. with etching [illegible] for J.C. Bates of Providence, R.I. Back to studio at 4 o'clk. then to Riding school with Coleman — After that, scrubbed, dressed & got to studio at 7.15 — had tea there, then to Annie's at 8.20 — then we went to Will's — there until 10 and back to 38 S. at 10.45 to 3 [illegible] & to bed.
