## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 23, 1881.

Another dismal m[or]n[in]g with rain & snow, clearing before noon to a clear, cold day with March like winds —
All day fighting with a headache — Painted on milkmaid & milk pail — didn't do much. Out to lunch at 2 — walked about & back at about 3.30 —
Dr Throals & Mrs. Hees called —
I did not go out for afternoon exercise & had tea alone in studio about 7.30 — At 8 went to see Father & mother — father suffering from sore throat & cold generally — at 9 got to Annie's — at 10.30 to 37 St. & at 11 to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 24, 1881.

Very cold, clear & windy. After bkfs. I went to Moore's and there met Mr Geo. Whitney — Annie & Emma came in and we looked at the Christmas competition card [illegible] just opened — then to studio and to work on 33 & 44 — working on sky & distant trees — Mrs. M.S. Beach, Mr. Chas. Beach & Emma B. called. To lunch at 2. After that, a walk — again at work until 4, then met Annie at 33d St. Station & went to Sherwood Studio Bl[oc]k Reception. There until 6 — then home & had tea at 6.45 — at 8 to Glee Club with Annie & Emma — then to studio, tried to write — head aches — at 9 went to see father & mother. There until 10. Then to 4th Ave. (Bellows' School Room) for Annie & Emma. There until near 11 — then to 38th St. to 37th St. & to bed after 11.30.
