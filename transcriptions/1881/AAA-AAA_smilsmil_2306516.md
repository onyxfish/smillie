## Left Page

### January, Friday 14, 1881

Beastly day — Up late. Blg after 9 — Rain — t very dark. Mary + Carrie Hertzel were at the studio when we got there — I got to work, after writing a letter or two, upon pen sketch of View near Portsmouth for "The Graphic" — To lunch at 1.15 — then it was snowing + making desperate walking — to Phil. Rehearsal — after that it was clearing + freezing. Walked up with Annie — Bel ee. Tweedy + Mrs. Colman — Tea in studio at 6.30 — then to 37 E S., dressed + took Annie to Colman's — then called on Miss Stebbins at Mr. A. R. Stone's 13 E. 36th S. Grand Party — then back to Colman's where the Tweedys, Florence + Harry Gray + the Kimballs were having a good time — left about 11 + got to bed at midnight. Clear, very cold + brilliant moon light.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 15, 1881

Fine day — Got to studio a little before 10 — worked some on sketch for Graphic f "View near Portsmouth." After lunch made a sketch in Miss Hunt's Album — Soon after 5.30 went to 37 E S. dressed, then took Annie to the Kimball's where we had tea (after stopping at Walter C's + giving him two Phil. Tickets that Chase had given us) + at 8 at Phil. Concert. After that walked up to 37 E S. left A. soon after 11 + got to bed before 12.
