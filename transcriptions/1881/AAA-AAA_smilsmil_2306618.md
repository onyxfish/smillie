## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 10, 1881

Pleasant day — after bkfst — a.
& I went to mill where we
spent an hour with Cotnée.
Then I went to Will Parky &
with him to his house where
I made a slight pencil sketch
from his figure, then to studio
& commenced another pen
sketch of "I lounge in the ley shade"
based upon my Park sketch.
To dinner at 1 — then again
to studio & finished pen sketch
to P. O. at 5.30 — tea at 6 —
Eve. we went out calling.
first to Mr. Richards', where
we saw Mr. R. — Lilly & Thurma.
After 9 when we left so me
ret'd to Courtrights, had one
game of cards, then to bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 11, 1881

Charming day — awoke after bkfst —
to quarry then down town, then to
studio — wrote letter to Mr. Tichnor
finished & packed pen sketches
& at noon took them to P. O. To
dinner at 1 after that again
to studio where I swung in
hammock while a. read
aloud until 3.30 when we
went out riding — back at 6
& had tea — then on piazza
for awhile — then a couple
of games of cards, then we went
up to the 3rd blast of the series
of Lawn Parties upon the fair
ground — quite a picturesque
party — Mr. Morris & her family
& in from Friendsville we visited
with them until nearly 11 — then
to studio to see how things looked
by moonlight — then to our
quarters & to bed at 11.30.
