## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 12. 1881.

Very pleasant day. Got to studio soon after 9 —
Mr. Water Colors were all taken away in the mtf.
Mr. + Mrs. Ehninger called + while they were in the studio
Annie came in — I lettered old tree- frame with a couple of lines from [illegible]
Dinner at 2 — In the afternoon did little but loaf —
At 6 event to 37! S; + dressed at 6.30 to Annie's to dinner
At 8 we went to Daly's + saw Needles + Pins, a very
absurd piece that seemed to caricature much of my
recent experience — Gel + Mary sat back of us —
Brilliant moonlight night,
got to bed a little before midnight.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 13. 1881.

Mild + overcast — Got to studio late + spent the
entire morning writing letters.
Out to dinner at 2 — then a walk in the square — and
at 3 to studio — what little there was of daylight left
was spent with callers and in "picking up" — At 5.30 went
to 37! S; dressed + at 6.30 to Annie's — dinner + then Gel
+ Fanny — Annie + I went to
Mr. T. Johnstons — leaving the girls
we went to The Berkeley — met
Charl. + left cards for the Ehningers
who were out — then to Union
League Club — there until a
little after 10 — at 10.30 again
at Jne. T. J's — did not leave
until near 11 — walked up
— to 37! S; + to bed at 12.
