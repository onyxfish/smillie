## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 30. 1881.

Still milder weather — very beautiful but muggy & enervating — got to studio about 10 — much of the mg spent in writing instructions to Cotrell. A little work on Gold Hill — Mrs. J. P. Thompson called — she is to join George's class — Copies of Poets & Dickens arrived from Boston — Home to dinner at 1.30. Mother Cook Mrs. Adams & Katie a.m.? Back to studio at 3 — The p.m. spent over etchings & with callers — At 5.30 went to see Dr. Mary Blackwell. not in — Home to tea at 6.30 Eve'g spent in bed room. — Annie lying down — Kate & Matt there — I Reading aloud from Romola until after 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 1. 1881.

Heavy fog — then heavy rain all the m[?] — Showery afternoon & beautifully clear moonlight night — Went to market after [illegible] — then to the Plumbers and got to studio about 10 — very dark — did little work — home to dinner at 1 — Afternoon so dark that I did not return to studio but stayed at home & drew upon N. Elevation of M. house — Dr. Emilv Blackwell, upon whom I called at noon today & with whom I had a free conversation concerning Annie's case — called about 4. Soon after I went to studio & back at 6 — Emma, Grace & Fanny to tea — then they went to their singing club — Annie on her back — and I went to Salmagundi Private View. Home at 10 & to bed before 11.
