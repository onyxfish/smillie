## Left Page

### April, Thursday 14. 1881.

Overcast & windy but no rain until night. Studio at 9.30 &
worked until 12 on etching. Then commenced proving &
working between p[late]s. Took 3 during the afternoon. Out
to lunch at 2 — Suffering very much from stomach
pain & discomfort — At 6 went for Annie — To-day
they have commenced moving into 110 E. 38' S. Me went to
Fanny's to tea — then to Singing Club — Rain — then I went to
Studio & wet down Baker & wrote until 10.20. Then to
Singing Club — got Annie & to 22 W. 38' S. in a nasty
rain storm — their last night there — To 37' S. &
to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 15. 1881.

Lovely day — quite April-like sun & shade no effects — Got
to studio at 9.30 & after a little work upon the Bridgeman plate
got at the proving with the usual result of no end of trouble
with ink — Too black or too brown or too thick or too thin — anything, so
that I do not get a brilliant [illegible].
To lunch at 2 — again at proving until after 5 — Tea in studio.
Between 8 & 9 went to 110 E. 38.
Everything upside down and everybody tired out — there
until about 10 — then a short walk up the Avenue & to bed
before 11.
