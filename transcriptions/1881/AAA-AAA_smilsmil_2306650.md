## Left Page

### October, Thursday 13, 1881

Considerable rain last night. overcast m'f., had a short walk before bkfo. then some writing. wrote to Aunt to Courtright. Bella Tuesday — Cottrelle & Geo. took these to P.O. in time for noon Train — Lunch at 1 — all the afternoon working with S. over his etching — Rain falling at intervals — close & muggy — Mr. Baker & her son Geo. & a friend called soon after 5 called on the Shepherd & then had a walk — Before 7 Mr. Ehuinger & her daughters arrived from No. Conway — "thick tea", as Mrs. E. calls it, soon after 7 — Evelf. old Mr. E. & Annie played Whist ag st. Mr. [illegible] W. E. & me — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 14, 1881

Crisp, brilliant day — after bkfo. at about 10 started out with W. C. traps & walked out on Greenfield road to Adirondack R.R. then along track to a trestle work where I made a rapid sketch in one hour & a quarter. Then hurried back getting in quite tired but in time for lunch — after that, a little more work. At 3, still feeling tired, took a nap. Soon after 5 Annie & I called on the Shepherd's — then to Mr. Thompson's — She was not in — back before 6 — Tea soon after 6 — Evelf. Reading news paper & playing whist with Annie ag st. Mrs. Wayland & old Mrs. Ehuinger — to bed before 11.
