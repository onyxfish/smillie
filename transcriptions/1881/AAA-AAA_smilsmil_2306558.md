## Left Page

### April, Sunday 10. 1881.

mied, lovely mef. Bkfo-
at 8.30 — then to Studio and
mote — at 1 I went to the
Buckingham to see Nee —
at 2, to Annie's — Dinner —
Eliz, Home there — after dinn
E — a + I went to 110 E. 58 —
then back — at near 5, as
I felt out of sorts I went to
my room & lay down until
6.30 — then to a's to tea —
Spent the everg on lounge
in dining Room — to 37[illegible]
S— + to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 11. 1881.

Quite a mied, spring-like
day — got to studio at 9.30 &
to work on Bridgman — most of
themef with graver about
the figures — Lunch at 2 —
again in place until after
4, then went to Archer, [illegible]
coast & Co., Greene St near Spring
— met Annie & Walter, selecting
gas fixtures for the house. I
was very much under the
weather with a sore stomach.
Home with a — at 6 — then to
studio where I had tea — mote
four notes, then, at 8.30 again
at a's — with her to Charl's
— geo + Nellie there — C. is
preparing to break up his
quarters — at 10.30 we left.
I got to bed at 11.
