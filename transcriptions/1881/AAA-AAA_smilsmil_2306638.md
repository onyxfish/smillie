## Left Page

### September, Monday 19, 1881

Another charming day, but warm for exercise — Bkfst —
at 8 — after 9 went to G.C. depot to find that the 3.30 train
does not now go through to Saratoga — had to change plans
thru to Kimmel & Voigt's — from there to foot of Canal St. and
bought tickets on "Drew" for to-night thru to 87 Beaver St; Saw Charlie
then home to dinner at 1 —
after that went to Oakeys &
to Knordles & got home at
4.30 to find Annie in bed
Dr. Smiley Blackwell says she
is threatened with a miscarriage —
of course there was running
about & countermanding to do —
I telegraphed & then wrote
a letter to Ehninger — Tea
after 6 — Evng. wrote a note
or two — did not get to bed
until after 11.
President Garfield died
at 10.35 P. M.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 20, 1881

Still charming altho' warm
Bkfst — soon after 8 — after 10
I went to Miss Blackwell's
to ask her to call on Annie
then to studio & got some
things & about 11, home again —
The rest of the day spent
in looking over English papers
& in unpacking trunk to get
my working materials —
Dinner near 2 o'clk —
Then again got out my
water colors & other sketching
materials ready for a start —
but my leg hurt me so that
I thought it safer to stay
at home — Reading & writing —
Tea near 7 — Evng. very
quietly with Annie and
to bed soon after 10.
