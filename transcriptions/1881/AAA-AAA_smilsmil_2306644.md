## Left Page

### October, Saturday 1, 1881

Another bright, dry day but breezy & comparatively cool. Had quite a walk after blfs — then home and to work on drawing for C.B. N. Co — miners making a pat steam drill — Fanny K. called — at 12 went to Studio for some sketches — back to dinner at 1.30 — Emma J. & her mother to dinner with us — worked on drawing — Mrs. Muers on called — about 5 I was quite used up with a sick spell & for an hour & a half was on my back on the bed — there feeling better I ate a little — Sue M. wrote to Koehler & Farrer — To bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 2, 1881

Dull & overcast with cool breeze from the East. Blfs after g — head dull & over-cast like the weather — on the lounge part of mrng — at 12 walked up to Central Park getting back at 2 — Dinner, then visiting the family at home until 5 — then wrote a letter to father & at 5.45 went to G. C. Depot with it — then walked up to Central Park again, meeting Mr. Roos & Truesdells — went to his house & saw some etchings & then he walked to my door with me. Tea at 7 — there wrote a note to Dr. Yale about [illegible] paper. Annie read aloud for a while & at 10.30 we got to bed.
