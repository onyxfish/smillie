## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 19, 1881

Bright, breezy mrng. after
the storm but later in the
day it became still and
lowering but the sunset
was bright. Bkfst. at 8.30.
After 9 I went down town
& bought a small second
hand wood stove — then
up to house & after a time
to Courtright's where I set
to work upon drawings for
window sashes. Dinner
at 1. After that, a nap —
then more drawing & after
5 Annie went with me to
the new house — at 5.45 I
went to the P.O. — Tea at
6 — Revf. Read papers
& did some letter writing.
To bed between 10 & 11.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 20, 1881

Charming day — Before bkfst.
went out to see that the ten
men doing masonry, carpen-
try, hauling & grading were
properly at work. Eugene
Lathrop with another man &
a team commenced grading
about the house. — Bkfst. soon
after 8 — then down town to
P.O. after finishing a letter to
Ehninger — then to building
— near noon when I got back
to my work drawing plans for
window sashes. Dinner at 1 —
after that A. & I went over to
the house. A. taking a sunning
& airing on the piazza — after
an hour or so, back to house
& more drawing until 5.30. Then
to building — then to P.O. — Tea
at 6 — Revd. — more drawing
& writing. To bed at 10.30
