## Left Page

### May, Monday 30. 1881.

*Decoration Day —*

Beautiful day, but very hot — Got to studio a little after 10 & spent the morning writing letters. To dinner at 1.30 — back at 3 — a good part of afternoon spent in preparing plate for 2°. O. etching — went home early & wrote notes to Sarah Hammond — Kate Gilbert & Addie Haws. Tea at 7 — Evert spent quietly in dark parlor by open bay windows & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 31. 1881.

*2°. Reception Day —*

Very hot day, altho' at times somewhat overcast. At 10 o'clk. went to Kimmels & Vogt's & spent most of the m'g there getting proofs started on my 2°. As good plate, ill, T.B.Aldrich. — Then to Dempsey & Carole's & paid bill — then to studio — Earl Shinn called & was very anxious to have me do a couple of pen & ink sketches for him, from pictures owned by C.P. Huntington so, at 1 o'clk. he & I went there, S.E. cor. Park Ave & 38 S. To lunch at 1.30. After Mr. Woodman there — Before I could dress several called — a heavy thunder storm came up & caught some friends at the house & kept them away — Tea at 7 — Evert the parlor was crowded much of the time with perspiring friends who drank iced tea & ate ices. Near midnight when I got to bed & after 1 before I got to sleep.
