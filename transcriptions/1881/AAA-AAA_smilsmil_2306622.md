## Left Page

### August, Thursday 18, 1881

Cool, overcast & threatening — me hopes for rain — After bkfst. on Fairground looking at dancing floor — bought it for $20 — Then to studio — so cool that I kindled a fire — then to mill with Armine — after 11 when I got back to studio — Elesha Mulford called — he left after 12 — I did a little sketching preparatory to again doing the lounger in the 'lex shadows — then to dinner — got it at 1.30 — nearly the whole of the afternoon was spent in worrying over grades & [illegible] steps — couldn't work by problems nb. Mr. Taylor & Mr. Barlow called — to tea at 6 — after tea to P.O — then back & Miss H, E, Mr. C. played Whist ago. A. & I until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 19, 1881

Pleasant wife, but I am somewhat stupid with a headache — After bkfst. with Mr. Coles, A. & the 4 children we went to quarry & then over Warner's lot & back through Fairground — got to studio before 11 — Contrelle there — Still worrying over West elevation — Dinner after 6 oclk — Afternoon, near used up with a headache — after 4 went to bed & had a nap — in about an hour up & dressed & at about 6.45 — A & I went to Mr. Mulford's to tea — Mr. Melles (Sarah Dutton Cook) there but we saw little of her — Ben M. also there — Pleasant quiet evening. — To our quarters at 10 — gentle steady rain continuing all night.
