## Left Page

### January, Sunday 16, 1881

Partially overcast, but not un-
pleasant. B[illegible] at 9.30 then
to studio — note to father, then
to Dr. Hastings' + heard him
preach — after service, walked
up to Buckingham + called on
Will — then to H.N.V.'s + had
dinner — at 3.30 to Annie's
— there until after tea — then
at 8.30, we went to the Berkley
+ called upon Mr. + Mrs. Shuminger
+ Mrs. Wayland — after a call
of an hour or more returned
to 37th S. — left there at 10.30
+ to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 17, 1881

Very pleasant day — got to studio
late — found Miss Sevin (model)
waiting for me — after some delay
got started on sketches from her
for my picture of a Lake George
dancing place — out to lunch
at near 2 — he back + worked
on her until 4 when I went
to Riding school with Coleman
at 5, in 37th S., bathed + dressed
at 4.30 at studio where I
had tea — at 8.30 went to
Mr. Dusk's, 47 E. 34th S., met
there H.H. Holt, H.H. Ferguson +
two ladies, names unheard.
After a half hour call went to
Annie's + she read aloud
fr'm "the Mill on the Floss" — at
10.30 went to 37th S. + to bed —

Gent. Frank Fessenden
called at studio in the m[orning]

This m[orning] Geo. announced his
engagement to Miss Nellie Jacobs.
