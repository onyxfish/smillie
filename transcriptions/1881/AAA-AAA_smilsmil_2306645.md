## Left Page

### October, Monday 3, 1881

Pleasant, quiet rain during most of the night — ceased before 8 a.m. — cleared by 11 — muggy, hot day — very light shower about 4.30 p.m. and another about 6 — clear, warm evef. After bkfst — a short walk — then to work on drawing for A.B.N. Co. — about 1 went out with Annie for a few minutes walk — her first since her illness — near 2 when we had dinner — Mary Ireland with us — p.m. again on drawing until 5 — then to Hunter's & Knoedle's & Mrs. Blackwells & to Studios & to Mr. Jacobs' — Home to tea near 7 — Evef Annie read aloud some very interesting letters from Rosalie Butten, from Switzerland — Then I wrote to Ticknor and Oakey. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 4, 1881

A breezy, cloudy day with threats of rain — at night quite cold — After bkfst — finished mining drawing & went down town — called upon Charl — then on Will — then to Western Union Tel. Office — then to Kimmel & Voigts & at 1 reached Studio & met Geo. W: Henry Néé of New Orleans there — walked with Geo. to 28th St. then home to dinner at 1.45 — After that, wrote some letters — then to Steamboat Office foot of Desbrosses St. & got Stateroom for to-morrow night — then to Dr. Allen's — 18 W. 11 — for advice about teeth — soon after 6 — to Studio — then home to tea — Evef. Geo. & Nellie called — I wrote letters to Montrose, etc etc — & got to bed about 11.
