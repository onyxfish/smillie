## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 11, 1881

wonderfully bright, crisp day.
Had a walk before bkfst. The
green mts looked heavenly.
After bkfst a number of duties
to attend to so that I did not
get started upon my water
color work until near 11 —
lunch at 1, & immediately
after, Mr. & Mrs. S. & a. & I went
out riding. Very brilliant
views of green mts. & over Sandy
lake — back at about 5 — Dinner
at 6 — Early in the eve Mrs.
& Mr. Shepherd & daughters
called — after 9 o'clk. chatted
read & music until about 10.50
— then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 12, 1881

Milder & overcast — up at 8 o'clk
— a short walk before bkfp — at 9.
much of the mng spent working
some water color drawing of aed
mile from Deucie sketch — lunch
at 1 — after that again at W.C.
sketch until near 5 — then out
with Mr. & Mrs. S. for a walk —
met Mr. Marvin & his daughter & son-
in-law Mr. & Mr. Gage — slight shower
— dinner at 6 — after that made
notes to Kinnee — Yale & farmer —
took them to P.O. at 8 — then
Mr. S. & I played a rubber of
whist agtst Mr. S & a — to bed
a little before 11.
