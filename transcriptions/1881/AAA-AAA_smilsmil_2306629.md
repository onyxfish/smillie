## Left Page

### September, Thursday 1, 1881

a ghastly day — smoke filling the air so as almost to obscure the sun, making the eyes smart & shutting from sight even the near hills — though not so hot as yesterday. still very oppressive. Did some work on plans and wrote a couple of letters — I called on Aunt H. on my way to P.O. at noon — dinner at — Rested for awhile — then most of afternoon at studio working on South elevation — Tea at 6 after a very pleasant call at studio by Priscilla Poore & Emma Smith of Binghampton — Thunder — went to P.O. at 8 — Rain. Heavy rain came in scant quantity about 9 o'clk. — To bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Friday 2, 1881

Pleasantly cool & overcast with now & then a few drops of rain — After helps went to studio — Cotwell started work on the framing of our house — then again to Cartwright's & waited until Miss Hollenback & sisters left, to go to their home in Wilkes some — Ab[illegible], with a went to Aunt H's — at about noon we went to studio. Dinner at 1 — again to studio & at work — about 4.30 went for Annie & together we went to funeral of Uncle My after services at the house went to cemetery — a song at the grave given with fine effect — no rain. After that, again to Aunt H's for a little while, then to tea — Annie & I went out & met Emily Smith, Priscilla Poore & Mrs. Dan Sayre — first went in to D. S's — then on to Mr. Jessup's, with mamas & called on her — then to Geo. Beatty's & called on Priscilla — To bed about 10.30.
