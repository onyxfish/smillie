## Left Page

### November, Sunday 20, 1881

Charming day — [illegible] —
about 9 — Annie & I went
to Dr. Colley's ch. & heard
Jas. Freeman Clarke — I
was very much pleased
by his sweet, spiritual tho[ughts].
After service I walked up
to the Buckinghams & saw
Will & family — Home to dinn[er]
at 2 — after that, made a
letter to Father — after 4
walked up to Park and
on my way back stopped
at C.P.V.'s — then home to
tea at 6.30. Quiet eve[ning] —
Annie reading aloud —
to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 21, 1881

Overcast day — Rain coming
after 9 P.M. — Got to studio
soon after 9 — Bought stone of
Coleman — mounted some
W.C. sketches — To dinner
at 1 — Back at 3 — did
little & home to tea at 6.30
— Evef. Annie & I went
to Mr. Coleman's & enjoyed
an evef. of music until
after 10. Mr. C., Carroll Dunham,
Minnie & Fanny playing —
Home about 11 — Rain —
right to bed.
