## Left Page

### November, Saturday 12, 1881

left montrose at 1.

Early m'g. very unforums id. Strong wind, very cold & dark. Rain commenced about 9. I completed packing before bkfp. & after that went over to blds & gave final directions & wound up accts with Clifford & Dillon. Then hurried down town & finished up there. Then to Aunt H's & begged her close carriage to go to depot, furnishing horses & driver. Then to C's & had dinner soon after 12. & at about 1 started for Montrose depot in a wild driving storm of wind & rain. Enjoyed some studies along the road exceedingly. Train came along on time — 2.40 — lounged in Parlor car all the way down — Hoboken at 9.12 — warm, pelting rain — Home at 10. Annie, Edith Marion Wright got in from Phie. concert a few minutes later. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 13, 1881

at home.

Bright, charming day. Up late & had bkfp. after 9. It was near 12 when I started out for a walk — went to studio & at about 12.45 Geo. came in. After awhile I went with him to his room in 28th S. & had a short visit with Nellie — then home to dinner at 2 — About 3 settled myself to do some writing when the Cook families began to arrive. Edw. & two children & Walter, wife, 3 children & nurse & pie. Valentine with Edith, Marion & Ninulton might — quite a confusion — a little before 5 I started out for a walk & went up to Central Park, out at 72d S. gate on 5th ave. & home at 6.15 — tea at 6.45. Edith & Marion rec'd home at 5 o'clk. Eve'g. very quiet, did a little writing. To bed about 11.
