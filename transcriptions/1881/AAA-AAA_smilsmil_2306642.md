## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 27, 1881

another extremely hot day — this weather is simply terrible — after 6½ — went to Dr. Yale's — but — my ears stuffed up — then rode down to Hummel & Voigt's — then to Schmidt & Siegle's — then to Studio & at 1 home, had dinner at 1.30 — afternoon made a water color sketch experimenting with tube colors — then out for a short walk — a very light shower passed over at about 5 — Tea at 6.30 — New lounge for Annie came — Evely wrote to Koehler. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 28, 1881

another piping hot day — after 6½ — got to work upon a 7×11 W.C. sketch — view on Lake George — from Vicar Island look ing north — worked until dinner at 1.30 — after that I had a rest, then finished sketch & at 4 walked to Sloan's 35° S. & Bdway & bought paper — then to A.F. Oakley's in 14th St. not — then to Knordler's — Gaupil's Siegle's & to Studio & at 6 home — a dry storm of wind & dirt — Tea at 6.30 — Evely. made a letter to Ehninger who has again invited us to visit them at Saratoga. About 9.30 went out & mailed it — then to G.C. Depot to make enquiries of Telegraph operator as desired by Ehninger — then took a walk up 5th Ave to 48th St — back & to bed at 10.30.
