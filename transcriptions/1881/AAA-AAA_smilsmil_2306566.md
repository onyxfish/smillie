## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 26. 1881.

Overcast, with a slight drizzle in the mtf. After bkfst went to Timmels & Voigt to get proof of one of my Osgood etchings there some time — then to Earl & Iredson's — then to Dewey & Keatings — then to studio at 12.15 — made notes — fussed with etchings &c &c — to lunch at 2 — then to Bank — back at 2.40 — Sent Bridgeman's pictures down to Jno. Taylor. John Stons — at 4 went in to saw Frank Millett. Worked on Osgood Etching — Tea alone in studio at 7.30. Weather cleared to be very warm — Soon after 8 got to A's when Renner's man was hanging some pictures on the 3d floor wall — After that a read some of Rosalie Butler's Italian letters which are charming — near 11 before I left & almost 11.30 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 27. 1881.

Overcast but very mild — still spring-feverish & suffering from my cold — when I got to studio laid a ground on Osgood plate & spent much of the mtf etching all over it. To lunch at 2 — Spent the afternoon biting my etching of the mtf. A short walk in Mad. Square between 6 & 7 — then tea alone in studio & about 8.30 went to Annie's. There until after 10. Chas. Collins called — At 10.30 walked up & down Fifth Ave. until after 11 — then to bed.
