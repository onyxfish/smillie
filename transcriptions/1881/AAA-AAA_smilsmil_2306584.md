## Left Page

### June, Friday 3, 1881.

Disagreeable cold raw day with a niggling misty rain in the m'f &
heavy pouring rain most of the afternoon — got to
Studio at 10 — Transferred tracing after making a study
sketch from geo. Home to dinner at 1.30 — a call from
Mr. Shurtleff. To studios at 3 — most of help. m. packing
to get away to Po'k. Oscar were spent most of P.m
with us — Home to tea at 7 — Geo. Coale had Rel? from
his fishing trip — at 8.30 out to 21 W. 37 & got some things
for mine there — back at 9 —
Then addressed some wedding cards. To bed
at 11.5

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 4, 1881.

*Poughkeepsie*

Very pleasant day — after b'kf-
made a note or two at home —
then to studio — Did little
else than pack up a few
additional things & then home
to dinner at 1.30 — Packed
my valise & wrote awhile
& at 3.45 hurried around to
Grand Central depot. Having
bought my tickets in the m'f
me had time enough to get
the 4 P.m. train & without
incident got to Po'k. at about
6.30 — Rode right home — found
Father, Mother, Matt & Kate waiting
for us — Sam had tea — about
9, walked part way home with
Matt & El, then Annie & I did
a little strolling by moonlight,
then home — nearly where
we got to bed.
