## Left Page

### January, Saturday 8. 1881.

Overcast much of the day —
Beautiful. mtf, at 6.25 went to
Annie's & had [illegible]. after that,
walked down 5th Ave. with her to
25th St — then to studio — for some
time, worked for Miss Abbott, then
Resumed work on "Mr. Mariner's
Well" examining in more effect
& color — out to lunch at 2 —
a short walk, — then back
& worked until after 4 —
Then walked to 10th St. track-
feeling much out of sorts with
that indescribable discomfort
in the Region of the stomach — on
lounge until near 7 — then tea,
again on lounge until after 9 —
Then to Century — did not
feel altogether healthy, so I
went to 34th St. & got to bed
about 11.30. Brilliant
moonlight night.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 9. 1881.

Overcast day with snow in
the afternoon & heavy storm at
night of sleet & rain. B[illegible]-
alone at 9.30 — there to studio &
from there to church of Messiah
& heard part of Robt. Collyer's ser-
mon on the doctrines of Election,
Predestination, etc. After service
walked down 4th Ave. met Marie
Jacobs & went home with her — con-
gratulated her upon her engage-
ment to St. Dunfer — then to studio
& at 2, to Annie's — Dinner —
there all the afternoon & even-
to 37th St. at 10.30 & to bed.
