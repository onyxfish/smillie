## Left Page

### October, Monday 17, 1881.

*Binghamton —*
*Dewis Ho. Room 52*

Up at 6.30, overcast day but not cold. Bkfst. at 7.30 — at 8 — Mr. + Mrs. 2., a + I rose to depot where a + I bid our friends good bye + took the 8.25 train to Albany getting there about 10.15 — a + I took horse cars to Capitol bldg — where we spent some time in looking through the corridors + halls open to the public — much disappointed. Then to the Delevan Ho., left a in parlor + again out + sauntered about until after 12. Then back + with a. went to the Windsor Restaurant in Maiden lane — again to Delevan + waited until we went to 3.30 train, Albany + Susy. R.R. — uneventful ride — few passengers, no dust + a cool car — got to B. about 9.50 went at once to Dewis Ho., then back to Station + had my trunks transferred to D.&.M. R.R. Then to hotel + to bed about 10.30 — Heavy Rain during night and not much sleep owing to noise —

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 18, 1881.

*Montrose —*

Up at 6 — bkfst. at 6.30 — off on D.L.&M. Train at 7 — steady rain fall — Met Mr. delSchweinity on Train — got to M. depot at 8 — Rob. with aunt H's carriage with pillows + wraps took us to Montrose — Reached Courtright's house at 10.30 — after getting a. settled I went to aunt H's for a few minutes, then to our house + saw Cottrell + Clifford — climbed all over the house + found fewer blunders to be corrected than I had feared — Dinner at 1 — a good part of afternoon given to nap-pining — then again to the house — there until after 5 — then to P.O. Tea soon after 6 — Quietly in-doors writing and reading news paper — Some very heavy Rains during the day + later, strong + cold M. + N.W. winds. To bed soon after 10.
