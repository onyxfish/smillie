## Left Page

### August, Monday 8, 1881

Delicious day — cool & brilliant
after bkfs — A. & I went to the
mill & saw Cottrell & the window
frames — Then I took my sketch
book & went out to the Road
where Sherman was working
with a yoke of oxen & I made
a rough sketch of them — The
rest of the mf. was spent
wandering over the fields — To
dinner at 1 — after that went
to studio & did a little work.
To house at 3.30 & out riding
with A. — around by Elk Lake —
Superb afternoon — back to tea
at 7 — Swelf on piazza until
10 — glorious moonlight — to
bid at 10 —

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 9, 1881

Cool mf., warmer at noon —
afternoon overcast with a light
spatter of rain — Much of the mf.
spent out of doors with Cottrell
figuring up cellar excavation.
Built a light fire in furnace.
To dinner at 1 — 1/2 an hour too
early — After dinner to studio
& worked on pen sketch of oxen
for Osgood book. ill. Mers his
apology. Tea at 6 — Evef.,
A. & I called on Flora Lathrop,
walked with her down town &
called upon Mr. Hf. Turrell,
after leaving Flora at her father's
office, & at near 9 called upon
Mrs. de Schweinitz — there until
about 9.45 — then to our room
& to bed where we talked
over matters especially interesting
to ourselves.
