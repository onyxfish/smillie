## Left Page

### May, Monday 2, 1881.

*Ret? to New York.*

Up about 7 — B[illegible] before 8 —
Day, overcast & muggy with light
falling Rain — During the m[illegible]f
Annie & I sauntered out for
about a couple of miles, with
an umbrella up nearly all of
the time. — Dinner som after
1 — At 3.56, after riding to the
station with "Edith" we took
Train at Wayne Junction and
reached N[illegible]. & home & about
7 — then, dinner — Eve[illegible]f
spent unpacking & setting
up a large, old fashioned clock
a present from James Wright —
To 37°S. & to bed before 11.30.
