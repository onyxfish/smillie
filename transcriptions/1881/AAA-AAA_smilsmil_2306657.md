## Left Page

### October, Thursday 27. 1881.

Brilliant, cool day — Bkfst.
about 8.30 — After that, drew
on plans for awhile — Mrs.
Schafer (Kitty Beatty) called.
about 11 I went to the house
to see how things were pro-
gressing — at noon, with A.,
went to Aunt H's — & then down
town — back to dinner at
1.30 — After that, again to
building — there until after
4 — then in & rested until
tea time — Evening, drawing
on plans, A. reading awhile
& some writing — To bed near
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 28. 1881.

Very mild, overcast day, went
to building, about 10 & started
Dillon on brick wall under studio
— & looked after the other men —
near noon went down town & on
my way back, stopped at Aunt
H's, where Annie was, & all went
up to our house, Aunt H's first
visit — To dinner at 1.15 — After
that A. read aloud a chaps. from
Romola — then I went to quarry
& again to the house — back after
4 & drew as long as I could see.
Tea soon after 6 — We read another
chaps. or two of Romola, then I
drew until after 10. To bed
before 11.
