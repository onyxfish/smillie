## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 5, 1881.

Very hot day — B[illegible]. Soon after
8 — near 10 when I went down
town & for near two hours
was engaged running about —
Saw Cottrel the builder and
others — for an hour, sat on piazza
reading newspapers — dinner
soon after 1 — After some time
went with Annie to Studio &
spent the afternoon there —
at 5.30 went down town — tea
about 6.30 — Miss Hollenbach &
3 children arrived from
Wilkes barre — at 7.15 went
again to Studio — met there
Cottrel & Payne. About 8 a. &I
took a short walk, more
until after 9 — To bed about
10

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 6, 1881.

Muggy m[illegible]f., threatening rain.
Before 11 it cleared & the evef
was clear & positively cool.
After[illegible]. went down town
— back about 10 — then A. & I
went to studio, where we passed
the rest of the m[illegible]f. Part of the
time one of us reading aloud.
Dinner soon after 1 — Again
at studio where for a time I
worked over building plans.
About 4 went down town,
hoping to get a horse to go
out riding — not one to be
had. Big box from studio up
by [illegible]. I unpacked it. To
tea at 6 — at 7 with Cottrell
& Payne on the grounds until
near 8 — then off with A. & had
a brisk walk. — beautiful
moonlight — in at[illegible] — in
parlor until after 10, chatting
with Miss Hollenbach of Wilkes-
barre & Mr. Courtright — then
to bed.
