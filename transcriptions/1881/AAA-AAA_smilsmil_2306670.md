## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 22, 1881

Pleasant day — cold — after
bkfst went to depot & bought
tickets & seats to Po'k — then
ride? to house & with Annie
went to 6th Ave. & 33d. St to a
Notary where As. & swore to her
Diettitchen Report to State
Financial Dept — Then to
studio — attending to store
man & mounting W.C. Sketches
Home to dinner at 1.30 —
Back at 3 — much of p.m.
lost in getting up Columbus
Stone & a fine Starlid — Part
of time occupied in correspon-
dence concerning Painter-Etching
matters — Home to tea at
6.30 — Annie & I alone —
Every. chatting — a game
of cards & &c. Reading
Romola aloud — To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 23, 1881

*At home. Po'keepsie —
Father's Birthday.*

Dull, raw mtf., sleety rain
before noon — nasty storm before
night — Got to studio about
9.30 & spent the mtf. in
mounting & finishing moments
for 3 or 4 small water color
sketches — To dinner at 1.30 —
dinner about 2 — Annie & I
went to Po'keepsie on 4 o'clk
train — Mr. Nevins sitting opposite
us. Geo & Nellie, in one of the
rear cars of the train which was
very heavy — Trouble all the way
up — an hour late at Po' — Will
Jos & Minn went up in the mtf.
Charl went up yesterday — Got
to the house about 7.45 — found,
beside Father, Mother, Kate, Mary
& the Dr., Matt & Ed. & Uncle Willie
Aunt Jessie — Emma & Bella —
About 8 we had supper —
about 10, Uncle W. left — later,
Matt & Ed. later, our large family
was distributed through the house.
Annie & I slept in Kate's room
To bed at 12 — Snow about 4 in.
