## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 26, 1881

Milder + overcast — got to
studio about 9.30 + spent the m'f. fussing + writing. Mr.
Bennett of Phila. called.
Out to lunch at 2 o'clk. —
went to N.A. after that +
spent some time looking
at the Water Colors. Met
Annie + red's wife there —
Back to studio after 3 and
succeeded in doing nothing
until after 5 where I went
with father to his Rooms +
called on Mother + Kate. At
6.30 at Q's to dinner — at 8 we
made a short call upon
her cousins in 23°. St. Back
to 38' S'. soon after 9 + until
after 10 I read aloud from
the Nile on the Poros —
to 37' S'. + to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 27, 1881

Very cold day — father had
trouble to keep warm in his
corner of the studio — Another
m'f passed without really
accomplishing anything. Out
to walk at 1.30 — + back to
studio where I had lunch.
Spent the afternoon working
in Water Colors. Finished
a sketch for Miss Place.
At 4 went to Riding School
+ rode Charlie's new horse
— Servant — at to. to 37' S'. + at
6.45 to 38' S'. Annie having
a very bad cold did not go
to singing club — At 10 I went down
to visit Fanny + Emma — took [illegible] home
to 38' S'. with E. where she
is now staying — Had a quiet
tête à tête before a pleasant
fire for half an hour — as the
clock struck 12 I cleared out.
To 37' S'. + right to bed.
