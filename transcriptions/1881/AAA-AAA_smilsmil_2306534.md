## Left Page

### February, Saturday 19, 1881

Rain in the mtg. which soon ceased — Up early to go to Chain at 7.45 to see but were too late to forward any freight on box 1 etchings to London alas! his, at 8.15 went to Annies to help — + after that walked down to 25th S. with her — then to studio — wrote a letter to Boneset and took it to Station 7. — too late to catch the Nectar this P.M. then to studio + to work upon my 33×44 — lunch at 2 + a walk + again at work until near 5 when I felt completely used up — to 37th S. + on my bed there until near 7 — then to tea in studio — after that to see Father + Mother for a few minutes + also to Annies — there until 10.30 — then to 37th S. + to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 20, 1881

Pleasant mtg. but with a raw feeling — afternoon overcast. Up late + had help — about 10, then to studio, wrote a note to Mother, then to Mad. ave. + cab + chatted with father until near 1.30 — then Mother + Kate came — at 2, at Annie's I had dinner — After 4 we went out for a walk — She stopped at 106 Mad. ave. with Father and Mother while I went down to the studio — after that a walk and home at 6 — Tea at 6.30 — Self illustrated some Rhyming lines by Annie written in answer to lines sent me by Fanny Gray. To 37th S. + to bed ab't 11.
