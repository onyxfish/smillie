## Left Page

### December, Monday 26, 1881

Mild, overcast day — after
[illegible] — went over to Dr. Wm. Thomson
& had a long talk with him
then back home at 10.30 — all
the Cook family there, big and
little, receiving their X mas
presents — ceremony over
by noon, when I went to studio
& made pen sketch for W.C.
cat — Home to dinner soon
after 2 — back to studio at
3.30 — some general work &
wrote a letter to Ticknor about
the Oakey designs for cover to
"Poets & Etchers" — Home to tea
at 7 — light rain & fog —
Read awhile — then, at 9
out for a short walk — then
more reading & to bed before
11.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 27, 1881

A warm day with heavy showers
of rain — got to studio at 9.45
to work on "Crisp Oct." 12×19 —
washed in the sky before I
went home to dinner at 1 —
back at 2.45 & washed in the
rest of the picture — whether I
will be able to make a crisp
Oct. if it remains to be seen —
either the tube colors, which
I am using for the first time
on a large drawing, or the hard,
old piece of paper makes the
color to curdle as I wash it
on, consequently I cannot get
the crispness I had hoped for.
Home to tea at 6 — tea at 7 —
Evng. Wash. Wilson & wife
called on us — To bed before
11.
