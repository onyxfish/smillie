## Left Page

### March, Sunday 27, 1881

Charming day overhead, but still raw & blustering —
Bkft before 9 — then to studio where I spent the morning
picking up things & printing.
At 2 went out to dinner —
at 4 went to Emma's &
with her to new 38th St. house
went over it — left her at
5.30 — thinking it was 6.30 and
went to Coleman's to tea —
& then came in to laugh
at me for my blundering
loss of an hour — After tea
sat with the C's until about
8.30 — then to my room &
completed a letter to
Annie — at 10.30 went
to 37th St. & got to bed some
after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 28, 1881

Delicious day, altho' very dirty
in the streets — got to studio
at 9.30 & laid a re-biting gr-
over most of Bridgeman etching
— thus out & got acid to make
up a new lot of Dutch Mordant.
Then "stopping out" until 1.45
when I went out to lunch —
after that, went to Bank &
then to Kurtz's & got photos of
Father, Mother & Kate — then to
Coleman's studio & looked at
some of his curtaining stuffs —
At 4 o'clk. took train for
P'ok — got home at about 7 o'clk —
tea — every spent in family
chatter & to bed at 10.30
