## Left Page

### May, Saturday 14. 1881.

*On board "Old Dominion" Steamer "Wyandoke" - Stateroom No. 9 -*

Bright breezy mng. Bkfst. at
9 — nearly 11 before we started
out upon a walk up the beach
towards the woods north of the
fortress. The tide being out we had
fine hard sand to walk upon & the
fresh breeze from off the sea the made
the excursion delightful — we visited
the Fargets & model casements ruined
by experimental gun firing — then to
the woods of Pine, Live oak & Holly
back of the sand dunes where we
found a picturesque but very shabby
cemetary — Back to hotel & had dinner
at 2.30 — Settled up, packed & off on
Northampton for Norfolk at 3.30 — just
as we were starting Frank Walker appeared
with his horse & requested me to take
charge of him to NY. which my pleasant
task. I had no chance to decline — an
uninteresting sail of about two hours brought
us to Norfolk — Thunderstorms threatening —
Delayed at it until 7 when at last we
got under way — Tremendous effects of lightning
with some heavy thunder that very gentle
Rain — Had tea on the just after starting —
sat about rather uncomfortably until
after 10 — then to bed — I cruse stateroom
a very comfortable one — not a very
free list of passengers.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 15. 1881.

*at home — 110 E. 38th St. NY*

Charming day — but the East wind
had made a swell that was quite
the reverse of pleasant to us after
a good nights rest — up about 7 —
Bkfst. at 8 — too much for Annie
— then I was somewhat demoralized
but in an hour recovered & finished
my bkfst. I had lost none of what I
had eaten — Annie spent most of the
mng on her back — I kept about and
tried to think I was enjoying it — At
2 o'clk. had a fair dinner — put it
away carefully — then gave Annie her
dinner in the stateroom — nearly 4 when
we went up on deck again — my head
ached miserably & as Annie grew better
I grew worse. At Sunset we passed the
Highlands & Sandy Hook — Stopped at
Quarantine just as the Church bells were
ringing — Got up to the city & landed
at 8.30 — Rode up home & got to the
house before 9.30 — later Geo. C. & Valentine
came in — V. staying all night — It
was after 11 when we got to bed.
Rain during the night.
