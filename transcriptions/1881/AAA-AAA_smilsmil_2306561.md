## Left Page

### April, Saturday 16. 1881.

another very pleasant day — again all day at my proving
with little better success than yesterday — I am satisfied
that there are qualities in the plate that I cannot
get in the [illegible]. Many calls — among others, Prof. & Mrs. Ritter
— Mrs. Chas. & Josie Jessup — etc — lunch at 2 — Proving until
about 5.30 — then washed up — Rested awhile — had a short
walk in Madison Sq. — tea in Studio & at [illegible] to 116 E. 38th St —
Gee came along later — me left [illegible] after 10 — then to
37th St. & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 17. 1881.

Lovely day — [illegible] alone — then to studio where I spent the n[oon]
trying to arrange things, so many little odds & ends have
got ahead of me that it seems as though I could not find time
to catch up — At 2 o'clk. went to Annie's & had dinner —
At 4.30 walked to Central Park
about there until after 6 —
then to Buckingham & saw Will
& Charl for a few minutes — to
A's again at 7.15 & had tea —
there until about 9 — then
again to Studio — wrote a letter
to father & wet down paper to
do more printing to-morrow.
to 37th St. & to bed at about
11.30.
