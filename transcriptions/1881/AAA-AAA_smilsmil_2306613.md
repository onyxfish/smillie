## Left Page

### July, Sunday 31. 1881.

Rain all night — up late in the mng. bkfs - aftrng — went to studio about 11 & wrote until about 1 — then Mr. Paine & Wilkes Barnes came & stayed with us until 2 when we went to dinner — In the afternoon again went to studio — wrote, three in hammock while A. read aloud — about 7 we went to P.O. with our letters — then to tea — Eve'g on piazza — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 1. 1881.

More rain during the night — fog in the early mng., then clearing. To studio & to work on pencil sketches for [illegible] book, making a second study from wild flowers to illustrate "Emerson's Apology" — Heavy shower & rain came up just before noon. — Courtright went off for a day or two of fishing. Dinner after 1 — more rain in the afternoon, so that the men did not work at the cellar digging. Casey, who commenced digging the well on Sat. came upon hard rock & had to go to work blasting — Tea at 6. After tea, A & I walked out towards the lake & around by the little depot into town, went to P.O. — then to our quarters & spent most of the eveng. playing California Jack — To bed soon after 10.
