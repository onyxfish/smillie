## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 10, 1889

A wretched night & a wretched day — about midnight the fever returned, also the soreness in my chest. In the n'tf. I was not able to get out of bed — in addition to all my other ills a violent headache came on — The day was passed in a sort of fever stupor. Yale came in the n'tf & again in the afternoon when he gave me some Antifebin which seemed to break the fever promptly — After that I took a large dose of Bromo-Caffiene which relieved the headache — Then beginning to feel hunger, as I had eaten nothing all day I had a bowl of clam broth that was most grateful to me — Annie wrote to Ma & in the ev'g went to Choral Club with the Dosé sisters. Charlie came in & sat with me untit near 10 — I was not out of bed all day.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 11, 1889

Some wet & some sunshine — I did not go out of the house, feeling very shabby. Will Parke & his brother called in the n'tf & I saw them in the dining room — before they left Mr. Male came in & made quite a long call. I was much troubled all day with pain in stomach — Took a nap in the afternoon & woke at 4 o'clk. with a slight chill — Sat in front of a blazing fire until a half an hour & it passed away — then I had a little fever — Sent a note to Y— asking advice & he said take Quinine. Ev'g Mr. Parke came in, followed later by W.G.P. & his br. Maxwell — Geo. Smillie also came & sat until near 10 o'clk.
