## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 6, 1889

A delightful day — m'f I was very busy completing the mounting of [illegible] — one for Pete — one for Boston museum & one for Farrer — I also took two [illegible], but they were not satisfactory. After dinner I completed the packing of the [illegible] & when we went out to ride at 3.30 I took them to [illegible] Office. Got a buggy horse from [illegible] & in it Katie & Mollie Adams followed us — Annie, Alice & Fred (& Ralph) out to Dr. Norris's & around by Elt Lake — Got home at 7. Eve'g Mr. Butler — two of the Jessups girls & one Miss Straalsburg called — After that we had a general time of chat — card playing — newspaper reading &
what not.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 7, 1889

A glorious day — after some work out of doors I spent the m'f in the studio writing letters to Koehler, Farrer, Pete & others — Dinner at 1 — Soon after that Wm. Parke's party came up, he on horse back Mr. P. & Mr. Sturgis of Scranton in one carriage — Norman driving a second with Miss Dimock in it; Mollie & Katie went with Norman & in our carriage Annie, Fred, Alice, Jim & I went — we drove to Salt Spring & spent the rest of the afternoon there — I was very much surprised at the wild beauty of the place — Of course there was an unusual amt. of water there but it was entirely beyond my recollection — we had a delightful time — I felt that I had been in a bit of the Adirondacks — We started for home at 5 40 — Selim dead lame — could not account for it — Got home at 7 — Tea — newspaper & general chatters not until after 10 — then to bid —
