## Left Page

### August, Sunday 18, 1889

A pleasant day, but dubious
in some respects — Began it late
& accomplished very little. Went
to the garden & spent an hour
trying to get enough for dinner.
The wet & cold has wrought great
damage there — Then spent
the rest of the m'f in studio.
Dinner at 2 — About 3 started
out with Annie, Alice, Fred, Jim
& Ralph & drove to Pine Hill — The
light was not all that could be
desired but it was clear — Home
at 6.30 — tea — Ev'f wrote a
note or two — Took them to P.O.
at about 9 — then sat for awhile
chatting — To bed soon after 10

## Right Page

### August, Monday 19, 1889

Overcast m'f — struggling sunshine
through the day — Showers in the ev'f
Up before 6 — P'kf't at 6.30 — Fred &
Alice were taken over to Montrose
depot by Albert, starting promptly
at 6.45. I worked at pruning
Apple trees & berry bushes until 10 —
then into studio & spent some
time, as I so frequently have to do,
at clearing up. Dinner at 1 — then
I felt so heavy & sleepy that I lay
down for awhile — Wrote a letter
to Charl. — Tried a game of Croquet
with Annie but was driven in
by a shower — then had tea —
after that went down town &
came home at 8 in another shower.
Sat & read news paper & got to
bed before 10.
