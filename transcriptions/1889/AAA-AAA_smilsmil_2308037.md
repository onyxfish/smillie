## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 2. 1889.

A gloomy day — I was able to get up before noon but had
only strength enough to lounge about — tried to do a little
in the way of keeping Ralph.
Evelf. Charl came in again —
got my letter off to Mary.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 3. 1889.

A little sunshine — a very little
rain & a great deal of shadow.
was able to be up all day but
did not dare to go out until
about 4 in the afternoon when
Jim & I went down to the studio
& then home again — Dinner
at usual time — Evelf. Gel
& Emiley spent part of it with
us — To bed between 10 & 11.
