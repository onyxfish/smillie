## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 1, 1889

A brilliant, beautiful day —
Got to studio about 10 & to work
on small water color — 4×5½. at the
last W.C. 4×5½ "Attending to Business"
Washed more color into the whole
thing & made the principal cow
a white one — dark. — Home to lunch
after 1 — Found that Annie had
gone to Orange with Jim — I
got to studio at 3 & got large
drawing "Fronts of Cows" — into it
frame, after cleaning the glass and
frame — At 4, I commenced a letter
to Mary but owing to interruptions
I retreated to the Century & wrote it
there, posting it on my way up
town — Dinner at 6.30. Edeth
did not come in until after 8 —
after 9 Chare came in — It
was after 11 when we got to bed
