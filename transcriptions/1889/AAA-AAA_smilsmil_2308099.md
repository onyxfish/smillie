## Left Page

### August, Sunday 4, 1889

Weather still unsettled — overcast in the
mtf. then clearing with promise of a
beautiful day & at 1 o'clk. all overcast
with a few drops of rain — beautiful
afternoon — evef. leaden with So. wind.
Bkfst. late — then repairing some of
the damage done to flower beds — thru
to garden, where yesterday's storm
worked great damage — At 1 o'clk.
went down to Herrick's to look at the
2 loads of hay put in by Tarbell a week
ago — not at all satisfactory — clover and
weeds — put in before it was cured — musty.
Dinner at 2 — at 3 went to Tarbell & made
my complaints to him — At 3.30 the Sawings
came for me there & we went Riding
— had a delightful ride — home at 6.15
Tea at 6.30. wrote some letters and
took them to P. O. at 9 o'clk —

## Right Page

### August, Monday 5, 1889

Heavy rain in the mtf. falling in
showers — cleared after noon, the
latter part of the day & evef. being
delicious & quite cool. I worked
in the mtf. at my printing &
again in the afternoon & also
prepared some of the plts. by
stretching, preparatory to mounting
them. Albert started with team
at 1 o'clk. for New-Milford & Rel-
at 5 with Ned & Alice Ireland.
Evef. I wrote to Mary & sent
letters that I had rec'd. — in the
mtf. from Mary (through Charl.)
& from Katie T. — to 9 o'c — near 11
I took them to P. O.
