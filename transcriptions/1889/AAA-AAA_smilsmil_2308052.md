## Left Page

### May, Thursday 2. 1889.

Pleasant wtf. much of the day was cloudy & quite cool. To studio before 10 & to work painting on 12×16 sketch. "Haglebrook — April" — worked very closely. Home to lunch at 1 + back at 2.30 & then painted all the afternoon & home soon after 6 — dinner at 6.30 — at 7.45 A— & I went to Miss Van Deuning's & took her with us to Chickering Hall to concert of Musingia club — male voices, who gave us a pleasant amateur concert — It was near 11 before it was over — took Miss Van H— home — then to our beds at about 11.30.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 3. 1889.

Quite cool & cloudy — Having some shopping to do for the dinner to-night it was about 10.30 before I got to the studio. Painted on Haglebrook until 12 — then went down town to Bul. of Commerce. To get dividends on some Milwaukee R.R. Bonds. Saw Charles — then to 2°. Nat. Buk. & made it was 2 o'clk before I got home to lunch. Annie had gone to a meeting of the Woman's Unitarian League at Yonkers — S.C.F. Hammond called in the wtf & saw Annie & children. It was after 3 when I got to studio — again worked on painting — wrote a letter to Herrick & sent checks — It was 6 when I got home — dressed — dinner soon after 7 — Walter & Edith Cook — Mary (ned) & Louise, her sister dined with us. Even. Louise played admirable pieces on the piano — They left at 10.30 —
