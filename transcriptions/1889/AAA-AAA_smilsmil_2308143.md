## Left Page

### October, Thursday 31. 1889.

**Ther.** 36° last night

A heavy fog most of the day with some heavy rain after 2 o'clk.
I spent the m'f packing &c. to E&p. & writing notes — got a note
& proof off to Mr. Woodin. In the afternoon I got down to the more
serious work of getting together the things to go into my big studio case.
At 5.30 I lay down & had half an hours rest — then tea — after
that I went down town — read newspapers until near 9 o'clk —
then commenced packing big home case — very nearly finished the work
at 10.30 — but it was midnight before I got to bed —

## Right Page

### November, Friday 1. 1889.

**Ther.** last night 89°

Still another foggy, damp day, but quiet & mild. — The fog in
the m'f was at times very thick.
Some of the m'f I was about the place giving final directions —
at 11 I went down town with Durie & was engaged, paying
bills etc — until after 12 — home —
dinner at 1 — then packed studio box & finished house box by 5 o'clk
— then a rest — tea soon after 6 —
Owing to Jim's behavior I was not able to get at the packing of my trunk
until well on for 9 o'clk — It was
11 before I was able to get to bed
pretty well tired out.
