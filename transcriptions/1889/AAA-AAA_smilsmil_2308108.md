## Left Page

### August, Thursday 22, 1889.

A warm hazy day, cooler in
the eveng. Up late, & another
day of loafing about & working
in a desultory way — as I do not
yet feel quite enough rested to
pitch again into work — At 11
went down town & attended to
several little matters — doing some
photo. printing at Doolittle's —
After dinner Parke & his Scranton
pastor (Stoll) came in — At 3.15 Annie &
I drove over to Dr. Norris's. Taking
Gretta & the children. We were
very cordially Rec'd by the Dr. & his
wife & taken up to the new house now
building where we stayed until
near 5 o'clk. We then drove home
getting there at 6.30. Rec'd pkg. of
6 K.&V. pfp. from Pate & Co — they are not
satisfied with the printing — & ask my
advice — I wrote them in reply
& took my letter to the P.O. before 10.
then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 23. 1889.

A cool day with struggling sunlight —
Quite heavily overcast in the afternoon
with appearance of Rain — but none
came. Spent the mfg. studying
over the 6 K. & V. pfp. Rec'd from Pate
yesterday & him getting ties that I
had taken ready to send to P.&Co.
Dinner at 1 — After that I wrote a
letter to P.&Co. to accompany my pfp.
explaining at some length my method
in taking a proof — At 4 o'clk. Annie,
Ralph, Gretta & I went out riding
going first to P.O. & lyp. office — back
at 6 — Tea at 6.30 — Eveg. Read
newspapers & some other little things.
