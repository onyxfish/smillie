## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 31. 1889.

Still muggy & rainy — Jack Downs
could wait no longer for good weather
so he went home this m'f by the
"Little Road" & Lehigh Valley — Rain
recommenced between 9 & 10 o'clk
& kept it up vigorously at intervals
during the day — I worked at my
printing altho' feeling very seedy — I
took two p[ills] in the m'f — Two more
in the afternoon — 3 good — one too dark.
Eve'f — a little reading — then a
little card playing & to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 1. 1889.

Still it rains — last night it came
down in torrents — with no wind & a
little thunder — Some rain during
the day but there seemed to be efforts
on the part of the weather to clear up.
I did not print — Spent part of
the m'f in our inundated garden.
Before noon got interested in some
Hollyhocks — Started an experiment
on a small copper × upon which
was an incomplete mezza tint ground.
Drawing my subject with dry point
& worked over the scraped ground
with dry point lines more or less scraped.
After dinner worked until 4 — then
out riding until 6 — Eve'f
some reading & some cards.
