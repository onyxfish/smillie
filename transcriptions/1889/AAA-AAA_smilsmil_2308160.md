## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 4, 1889

Bright & very cold. Got to studio before 10. Some time in getting fire made in the grate & the room warmed. — Three wrote notes to Berrian & Charl. — Then worked a little on mezzo-tint "Hollyhocks" Home at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & from a young woman in waiting for me upon mezzo-tint, who had a place & proof upon which she wished advice. Got to work at last on my printing & took too [illegible] (3°, working [illegible]) That seem to me to show satisfactory progress. The day was so short that I was not able to do more. Home at 6 — dinner — Eveth. Reading and visiting (Mr. Woodieu & Walter Pierce) — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 5, 1889

A disagreeable day, dark & overcast. Rain at night. To studio before 10. at work on "Hollyhocks" — mezzo-tint. worked over the lighter flowers taking out much the drawing & detail. Went to bank before 10 clk — then home to lunch. & back to studio at 2.30 — Disappointed reinforcing the darkest parts — then took a couple of [illegible] showing satisfactory progress. After walked down to Union Sq. & up 5th. Ave. home. Dinner — Evelf., went with Annie to Walter's — then took her home at [illegible] to Ed[?]'s — then, a message to Geo — Cook's — home at 9.20 — back for Annie — dinner — Eveth. back for Annie at 10.
