## Left Page

### September, Friday 13, 1889

A mild, dull day with showers, though not enough to
put a stop to the brick laying in the new milk room — very heavy rain
at times during afternoon & Evef.
I spent the aft. about among the men directing things. So far finished fixing up the Strawberry
bed — Dinner at 1 — In the afternoon, went to foundery & gave orders
for overhead beams for milk room &
then to saw mill & ordered other
stuff required for changes in Piazza
Evef., reading & promenading
piazza until 10, then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 14, 1889

A dull day with a number of showers, but not enough to drive the
men from the work laying brick.
I spent the aft. with them and
about the place but was much bothered with a headache that
grew worse until I went to bed.
Just before noon went down town
to mill & other places — caught in
a shower — most of the p.m. was
spent dodging showers while [I]
set out a lot of hollyhock plants —
Evef., newspaper until 9 — then
"stop" until 10 — to bed at 10.30.
