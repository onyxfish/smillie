## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 8, 1889

mild & pleasant day but I was
unable to get out of bed — I had
passed an exceedingly uncomfortable
night, stuffed up in head & sore
of throat — in the m'f I had a
violent headache added to my
other miseries — Dr. Yale was sent
for & came before noon — I got
up for a little while about 9
o'clk in the eve'g, to give the
bed & room a chance to air —
To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 9, 1889

A great storm of wind & rain —
after another wretched night I was
unable to get up in the m'f — T
Yale did not come to see me. I
had a very severe headache still
that so far passed away that I was
able to get up about noon, when I
took my bkfs. The p.m. I vibrated
between the nursery & the parlor —
Geo. came in just before dinner
& Charle. came in about 9 p.m.
with another of his diary letters
He left soon after 10 — Soon after got
to bed to pass one of the most horrible
nights that I ever experienced. The Catarrh in
my head & throat was so severe that I could
not breath without greatest discomfort — I slept
for perhaps an hour between 1 & 2 o'clk —
after that I sat up in bed & resorted to
all sorts of expedients to help my breathing —
I got to be quite frantic about it — Up at
3 & bathed my head — finally got up at 5.30
washed & dressed & went down to parlor
where in big chair, I got another hour
of sleep.
