## Left Page

### March, Monday 11. 1889.

Bright day — but I was not in tune
with the weather — Got to studio a
little after 10 feeling out of sorts gen-
erally. — At 11:15 a telegram came
from G. W. Sheldon directing me to
have Kurtz photograph my painting
so, in absence of further details I went
to K's — Saw Mr. Getz & made arrangements
to send "Spring is Coming" at once — then
to Bank — it was before 1 when I got
home. Annie had invited Sam Coleman
& his wife to lunch with us & everything
was provided — but the guests did not
come — nor word of explanation — or
apology. We had our lunch before 2.
It was after 3 when I got to studio but
I could not work — home before 6 —
could take no dinner — went to bed
at once & spent a wretched, fevered
night.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 12. 1889.

Was not able to get out of bed in
the m'fg. Annie sent for Dr. Falls.
He came before noon — my fever had
abated but I still suffered much
discomfort — I was able to get up in
the afternoon & lay upon the lounge
in the nursery — Eve'g. Annie
went off alone to an Extra Rehearsal
of her Choral Club — Mr Williams brought
her home. I was able to finish my
regular weekly letter to the [illegible] —
got to bed soon after 9. Chas.
Collins came in & chatted with
me for half an hour between 8
& 9 o'clk.
