## Left Page

### November, Thursday 28, 1889

*Thanksgiving Day.*

A cloudy, windy, cold day. I was
up before daylight — bowels — trouble
through the day. I spent most of
the mtt on my bed. Dinner at 2
& the afternoon as quiet as the mtt
— Reading most of the time — just
about sunset I walked a short
time in front of the house. But
chatting until about 9 o'clk. Mr
had an hour of 6 handed euchre
nearly 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 29, 1889

A very bright, pleasant day. My
trouble not all gone yet — About 10
o'clk. I went out & joined Annie
& Jim & we walked over to the Reservoir — then to the Pumping Station
& saw the big steam pumps at work —
then to Lake Whitney & saw the big over-
shot wheels at work — then home by
Whitney Ave. getting in at 12. I lay
down & had a nap — At 1 o'clk. with
Annie & Jim I went to the Davids'
& had a second Thanksgiving dinner.
After dinner I Ret. to the house & met
Nelie, Gretea & Ralph. Then back
to the D's — Annie & Nelie went to
call on the Brushes while I stayed with
the children — all back to Dr. Adams'
at 5 — most of the evtg. spent playing
euchre — to bed at 10.30.
