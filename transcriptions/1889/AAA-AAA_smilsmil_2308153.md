## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 20, 1889

Pleasant sunny m'f. — Then overcast & during the afternoon it was very dark & threatening — a light spitting rain at times fell. Uncle Willie came to the house while we were at b't f'. I was late in getting to studio. got my printing del'. m's order & took two or three p't. of dry point Poppies — am satisfied that I have worn the plate out — have taken less than 25 p't. D'. Yale & a niece came in at about noon. I went home to lunch at 1.30 — back about 3 — Yale came with a small plate of his & I took 3 or 4 imp. for him. Uncle W. came in bet. 4 & 5 — on his way home. I went home at 6. Eve'g went to Water Color Soc'y meeting at N. A. D. Found myself on Cat. Com. with Chas. Platt. Before 10 o'clk. went up to N. A. galleries for a few minutes — Home at 10:30 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 21, 1889

Bright for a little while in the m'f. — then overcast & between 3 & 4 p.m. it commenced to rain & continued steadily all the eve'f. After going to Hunter's for my glasses I got to the studio at about 10:15 & to work on 6×9" mezzatint "Holly Hocks", upon which I worked steadily until 1 — then home to lunch. Back at 2.30 & worked as long as I could see — not much after 4. Calverly came in — then Ree & we chatted until after 5 — walked with G. to his door — then home. Eve'f., after reading for awhile I spent some time unpacking baby carriage that came from Montrose.
