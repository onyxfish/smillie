## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 27. 1889.

A nasty, snowy, slushy day — got to studio at 10. feeling very unlike starting painting as I had planned. I pottered about the studio all day doing lots of little things of little or no importance, with a respite when I went home to lunch. I went home in the evef very much hoping that A. would not care to go to Mrs. Mary Mapes Dodge's to-night — but she ordered a coupé & we went, for which, afterwards I was very glad — we went to the De Cordova in 59th S. & had a very pleasant evef, meeting many agreeable people we left at 11.15 & got home & to bed before 12.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 28. 1889.

Considerable sunlight about the middle of the day but the air was raw & disagreeable from much slush & melting snow. Soon after 10 when I got to studio — a note or two to write — then I went to Kimmel & Voigt's with my Dry Point "Poppies" & had them take me some pfs. I do not think they took as good, certainly no better pfs. than I did — to studio at 12.30 — then to 2d. Nat. Bk. Safe Deposit & got Lehigh Coal & Gas Bonds — home to lunch at 1.30 — to studio about 2.45 & worked upon drawing of closet for Montrose Etching Room. Evef. Charl. came to dinner & to talk about some investments — McDosé girls came for Annie & Charl put them all in at 'bus & Ret?. to me — Saml Colman came & sat with us until 10 — then I went to 14th S. & brought home Annie & the B's — To bed about 11.
