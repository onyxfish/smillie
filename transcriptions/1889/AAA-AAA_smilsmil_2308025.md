## Left Page

### March, Saturday 9, 1889

Blistering, dirty, raw & cloudy day —
Ralph continues to improve but Jim
seems out of order — complaining of his
head — etc. — Got to studio about 10 —
Set palette & painted on the 12½
× 17 "Back lot" study that I painted
ever yesterday — Home at 1 & back
at 2.30 & painted as long as I could
see — the result is discouraging —
it is without directness — vitality —
snap. Home soon after 6 — dinner —
Evelf. lounging in front of fire
with newspapers. Annie read
aloud for awhile — one of Cable's
articles in a No. of The Century —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 10, 1889

Some bright sunshine — some bleak
clouds — much wind & lots of dirt.
[illegible] at 9 — with children and
visiting until near noon — Then
went out with Jim & walked until
1.30 — then home — dinner at 2 —
afternoon — again with children a
part of the time & writing more
letters — Annie took Jim near
5 o'clk. & went down to 7th St. &
called on Aunt Anna — Back
after 6. — a cup of tea at 7 — Evelf.
Annie read aloud for a little
while — Charl. came in & stayed
until near 9 — Then I wrote to
Kate until 10.30 — Then to bed.
