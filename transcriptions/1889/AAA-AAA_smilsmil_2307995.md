## Left Page

### January, Sunday 6, 1889

Still disagreeable & wet — up
all day, but did not go out of the house
spent most of the time with the
children — Charle came in for a
time after dinner & Geo. came in
a little before supper. I wrote
my Sunday letter to Kate early
in the evef. Annie read aloud
from Rob Elsmere — & we got to bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 7, 1889

Disagreeable day — Got up feeling
somewhat better in the mfg. but as
the day wore on evidences of cold
disturbances all through me increas-
ed — especially in head & throat — In
the afternoon I determined not to
run the risk of going out in the evt
I had tickets to Daly's Theatre — so
Annie sent to Geo. & Minnie Cook to go
with her — I played nurse all day.
Evef Geo., Emmy & Annie went to
Daly's & I took Gretta's place in
the nursery until 10 o'clk — then to
bed. Annie came in before
midnight.
