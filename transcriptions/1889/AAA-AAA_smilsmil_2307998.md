## Left Page

### January, Monday 14, 1889

A bright, pleasant day — Got up feeling much out of sorts — About 9 o'clk. Wm. Parke came in & sat for more than an hour. I then attended to my household duties & got to studio at about 11.30 — Geo came in with a letter from the girls — Dec. 31st — We soon fell into conversation concerning the Century election where he took offence at my remarks & declined to be lectured on honesty — as he phrased it — on which we parted — & so the work goes on — Home som after — back — went to Pike's with old Magic Lantern slides — then to Kurtz & got my "Passing Herd" — then to studio — did a little work on White Cow — home at 5 — Dinner — Eve'g rec'd. from Charl. letters from Dresden of Dec 24, 25, 26, & 28th — Read news paper & wrote part of a letter to Mary. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 15, 1889

Another charming day — To studio at about 9.30 — went over to Kurtz's with my Passing Herd drawing & ordered one of his Atgeline (?) photo's from it (16"× 20" — Then again to studio & worked until 11.30 on "where's my ma?" Then went down town & called upon Mr. Heber Newton, by appointment, to talk about an etching after some piece of his jade. We did not get far — what he showed me did not interest me. He sails for Europe to morrow & promises to see me on his return in May — Then to Charl's for a few minutes — Home to lunch at 1.30 — After 3 when I got to studio — worked awhile on "Landscape sketch "W.C. Home at 5.30 — at 6.30, dinner — Eve'g. finished letter to Mary & wrote one to Herrick — out at q. 15 & posted them — then to druggists & got ingredients for bitters — To bed soon after 10.
