## Left Page

### June, Thursday 27, 1889

Overcast, muggy day, clearing late
in the afternoon. Mrs. went down
town & picked strawberries, so &
got but little done on my plate.
Dinner at 1 — afternoon, a little
more work — Coyle dug out the
tree on the jail walk that has
been banked up with earth &
ashes in the grade of the walk 2
or more feet. I have this done to
save it. Out riding from 4 to 6
— Tea — evef. Annie read aloud
from Bryce's American Commonwealth
I struggled with sleepiness and
heard but little of it — In bed
at 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Friday 28, 1889

A very hot day with thunderous clouds
& showers about us in the afternoon.
Got to my work on plate at about 10.
Then went with Hotchkiss to tree by the
jail walk & instructed him about
putting a wall about three sides of it.
Then back to my work again — Con-
stantine worked at cutting grass &
Coyle also — Dinner at usual time
& again at work on my plate until
near 5 when, having got my plate
ready to prove, we went out riding.
In at 6.30 — Tea —
