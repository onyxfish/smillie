## Left Page

### July, Saturday 27. 1889.

Heavy rain during the night & through much of the morning. After bkfs. I got to work at my proving & was able to take two kfs in the mlf & two in the afternoon — the best that I have yet taken — my printing rags are now rich & fat & I have got a trick or two that helps me wonderfully. Parke came up in the mlf & insisted that all hands should dine at his house at 5 o'clk. The weather improved somewhat at that time & all went down but me — I kept at my work & Jim & I took tea alone. At 7.30 I went to P's & brought all home at 8.30 — Geo. Cook arr. at the house at 9 o'clk — All hands to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 28. 1889.

Heavy fog in the mlf. Bkfs. about 9 o'clk. [illegible] until after 11. in the garden — then wrote until dinner abt 1 o'clk — Soon after 3. Geo. Smiley, Annie, Jim, Ralph & I drove over to South Pond & back at 6. Strong S.E. wind blowing & sky overcast but the air was soft & mild. Tea before 7 o'clk. Eve lf. wrote in the studio where I found upon the floor a beautiful dead bird of a kind unknown to me.
