## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 14, 1889

Heavy rain in the early m'f. For awhile in the forenoon the weather was pleasant but before 1 the clouds banked up & by 2. it rained briskly — a succession of showers followed with thunder all about us. I was bothered by a headache but put in a days work writing letters. I had a little croquet with Alice & Annie before dinner — After dinner I took a nap & then resumed my writing. After tea I went down town & on my return spent the ev'f in my dark room. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 15, 1889

Heavily overcast m'f. with some rain — Pleasant, cool afternoon — commenced work immediately after bkf. — Finding that I could yet make a good combination out of the old Hollyhocks in my studio I commenced a study in oil of them & worked very diligently until 1 o'clk — then dinner. At 2.5 again at work & finished sketch at 4 o'clk — At 4.5 went out driving, taking Alice & [illegible] to Mr. Dud. Lathrop's where Annie was making a call. — I met there among others Vic Dimock — Jas. Carwalt & wife — w'tml. Hose — we rode until 6.30 when I left Annie at Flora Lathrop's 4 to 7 tea — the rest of us went home to our tea — Ev'f. Read news paper for awhile — then cleaned palette etc
