## Left Page

### February, Friday 1, 1889

Bright, cold & windy — got up feeling
out of sorts — my heart Rheumatism
or whatever it is, depresses me very
much — I got to studio about 10 but
did not feel like resuming work
on "Cliffs" — got out a study that I
painted at Étretat — looking over a
grain field — over the cliffs — away to the
ocean — & I worked all day on that
repainting the foreground. Home to
lunch as usual & back to studio —
Evg. I went to Press View of the
Water Color Society & had a very good
time — I left tickets at home & later
Chas. Collins, Geo. Cook & Jno. Valentine
came in in them — the Exhbn has
some very interesting works to me —
my White Cow ("Passing Herd") does not
look as well as I had hoped — I
got warm commendation for my
little dry-point "Poppies" — Home
& to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 2, 1889

Bright day, but windy, very dusty
& raw — To studio at 10.15 & made a
short days work on "Cliffs" — getting
the painting of left hand corner
finished — At 12.30 went to Water
Color Exhbn & spent an hour there —
then to Mad. Sq. Bk. with Jacob Seggett's
check — too late to cash it — Home at
1.40 lunch — to studio before 3 — and
did a little work — the discomfort
or pain that I think Rheumatic
about the heart bothered me very
much — Chas. Collins came in for a
few minutes about 5 o'clk. Home
before 6. Miss Williams dined with us & at
8 o'clk. I took her & Annie to Steinway Hall
"Anton Seidel's concert" — leaving them went
to the Century — met a number of friends
& had a pleasant time before the meeting
which was very full — the important
business being a Resolution going to the
Board of management & Com. on Sites —
con- ferring power to select & buy a site
for new Club house — not to exceed $200000.
There was no wrangling — only two addresses
one by Mr. Berean & one by Mr. Nash — after
which, following a little skirmishing and
confusion — a vote was taken on calling the
call — Resulting at 10.30 in 202 votes for
the Resolution to 74 against, — About 11
Geo. Chas. & I walked up town — I got
to bed at about 11.30.
