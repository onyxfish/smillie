## Left Page

### July, Monday 29, 1889

A bad day — strong S. E. wind in the
mng with some early showers with
thunder — then the sky brightened
with prospects of a hot day — but the
wind changed to the West & at about
12.30 brought up a heavy shower with
more thunder — The rain continued
most of the afternoon — I got started
on my proving at about 10 but spoiled
all my mng work because I used
the ink that I mixed up on Friday.
It was very depressing to me — Geo &
Emily left us at 45 Albert drove them
over to New Milford — when he ret'd at 5.30
he brot a note from Geo saying that having
made a mistake in time they did not
get the train — after a very muddy ride —
they had to wait until after 7 P.M — take
train to Binghampton — arrive until after
1 A.M. — then train to Buffalo — getting there
next mng. a chaplet of mistakes &
accidents to begin their journey with.
Eve'g I wrote a letter to Kate & took
it to P.O. at 9.30 — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 30, 1889

Heavy rain in the early mng — some
muttering thunder — heavily overcast
until after 4 o'clk when some faint sun-
beams struggled through — everything
damp, sticky & warm. I got to my
printing & by sticking to it closely, by
5 o'clk had taken seven pfs — one
of them, a fine one, was ruined by
a fold in the paper — It was after
6 when I got washed up — & I was
very tired — Tea at 6.30 — In the eve'g
Mr. & Mrs. deSchwinitz called — At 9 o'clk
I wet down paper for to-morrow's work
Then a game of cards & to bed at
a little before 11.
