## Left Page

### April, Monday 8, 1889

A layer of snow over everything
when we got up this m't and
rain still flying but before noon
the weather improved & continued
to do so until just before sunset
the sun shone out clear for a
few minutes. I got up feeling
somewhat better — spent most
of the m't with Ralph — Finished
my letter to Kate — about 4 o'clk
we all went out — got a baby carriage for Ralph, we were out for
an hour & a half. met Mr. Theo.
Justice & she sat to Hotel with us.
Ev'g we sat in our little room
I wrote to Mr. Thayer, of Montrose
Mr. C.H. Greer & Herrick. To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 9, 1889

At last, a delicious day — cloudless
sky, pleasant breeze & warm sunshine.
A short walk before bkfs — Soon after
10, all things being ready, the family
took horse car & rode up to the mills,
about 2 miles — there some time was
spent by the two boys digging in the
sand — before 12 Gretta took Ralph
back to the hotel for his nap. Annie
Jim & I sauntered along down the
beach — I got two slight pencil
sketches that interested me very
much — one suggests an oil sketch &
an etching — the other, a water-color.
Got in at about 1 — dinner before 2 —
then read newspaper & had a nap.
It was near 5 when again I went
out & joined Annie & the children.
In before 6 — Tea at 6.30 — Ev'g
finished a long letter of in-
structions to Herrick — then
for a time sat down stairs
while our bed room was aired.
To bed at 10.
