## Left Page

### December, Monday 2, 1889

Beautiful day — near 10 when I got to studio — feeling pretty well —
felt better when I Read Pittsburg letter enclosing check for $33 50
for "H.C." "Group of Cows" — wrote to Pittsburgh — also to R. W. Hitchcock —
Saml. Coleman came in — I am to let him paint in my studio for three
or four weeks — It was after 1 when I went to 2° Nat Bk's — it was near
2 when I got home to lunch —
& almost 3 when I got back to studio — J. B. Carpenter & his daughter
Mrs. Ives spent some time with me
Butter sent frame & I made paste and pasted in my pet proof of Goldsmith's Daughter —
Coleman came in again — Home before 6 — dinner at 6.30 — Evelf I Rec'd a letter
from Charl. announcing that the Lance Creek Cattle Co. would default on its
faut interest — I have felt uneasy for a long time about this investment, but
this news was a great shock to me.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 3, 1889

A nasty day, opening with rain &
closing with snow & sleet. I was
could not about daylight — bowels —
& had an unhappy day — Got to
studio at 9.30 & spent some time
getting things in order for Coleman's
advent, putting all of my things,
as far as possible out of sight —
Coleman did not come along until
12 o'clk — when he brought two canvases
with him — It was 1.30 when I got
home to lunch — so much used
up in the afternoon that I spent
the most of it on my bed. Took
a very little dinner — Evelf Annie
went with Miss Bosé to her Choral
Club Rehearsal & they Ret'd together
at 10.35. I finished my letter
to Kate — It was 11.30 when I
got to bed.
