## Left Page

### September, Saturday 7, 1889

Rain pretty much all day —
quite, steady & soaking. Routed
out by my trouble at 6 A.M. and
feeling much out of sorts all day —
I sent Albert down to Dr. Richardson's
for some medicine. I did little
else than hang about — In the
afternoon I wrote a couple of
short letters — The evef was
passed in reading until town
7 o'clk. Then we all took a hand
at cards & played "Stop" until
10.30 — then to bed — Lovely,
silvery moonlight over a land
scafe wreathed in mists

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 8, 1889

Overcast & muggy much of the
day with some streaming sun-
shine about the middle of the day.
Late [illegible] — about with Ralph
until 11 — then had a long nap
before dinner at 1.30 — After
dinner some reading then wrote
a letter to Kate — Tea at 6.30 —
Evef wrote a letter to Koehler.
Then Annie read aloud from the
Letters of Motley — To bed at 10.30.
