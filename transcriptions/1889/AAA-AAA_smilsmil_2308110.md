## Left Page

### August, Monday 26, 1889

Another very pleasant day — Hot in the sunshine — cool in the shade. I spent some time about the place overseeing Herrick at work upon the "cold frame" for Strawberries & Coyle, making a bed for Hollyhocks. Went down town before 11 & home before 12. Worked awhile in studio on Study in crayon of Poppies. Dinner at 1 — more Poppies — At 4 Annie, Ralph & I took Mr. & Mrs. Christman out for a ride — Home at 6.30 — Tea — Eve'g I wrote to Mary & took the letter to the P.O. at 9, then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 27, 1889

Another perfect day — brilliant & cool. Did some work in the studio on crayon Study of Poppies — At 2.30 went to Nelson Warner's but he was not at home. Dinner at 1 — again worked on Poppies — At 3.30 Annie, the children, Gretta & I went out for a ride — in before 6.30 — Got a note from Charle enclosing a letter from Kate — Munich — Aug 11 — Wrote a note to Geo. enclosing Kate's letter — At 8 o'clk took it to P.O. Then went to Warner's house & arranged with him to commence papering the kitchen to-morrow. Then home. Read newspaper until near 10.
