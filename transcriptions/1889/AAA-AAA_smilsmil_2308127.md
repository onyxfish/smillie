## Left Page

### September, Sunday 29, 1889

A brilliant day — a perfect model for light, color & temperature — It was very late when we got up — near 10 when we finished bkpt. I spent the remainder of the mtf. wandering about & cutting out the tops of some more trees. Dinner at 2 — At 3. with Annie & the boys I went out riding — went first over the hill, to the west, down to the brook where we spent some time — I had my camera, took some shots & looked at a bit I want to study — then suddenly the sky clouded over — we went on & drove over Butterfield hill & home by E. Montrose — getting in at 5¹⁵ then turned some wounded trees — Tea — then an hour in my dark room. Then my bowel trouble came up on me with great force & fairly prostrated me — I spent the rest of the eve'g on my bed until 10 o'clk — then turned in for the night.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 30, 1889

After a quiet night I awoke feeling so uncomfortable still that I took my bkfs. in bed & did not get up until noon. — I then loafed about in easy chairs & on the sofa — After dinner I wrote my letter to Mary & a note to Dr. Yale asking for his professional advice — The mtf. was mainly overcast with now & there a little rain — In the afternoon at times it rained freely — Well read my paper & loafed — To bed soon after 10 — High wind at night
