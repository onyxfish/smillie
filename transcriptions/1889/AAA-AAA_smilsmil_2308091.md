## Left Page

### July, Friday 19, 1889

Rain commenced about 10 A.m.
& some heavy showers fell during the
day. I was among the pea vines
until nearly 11 — then to work and
bit in remarque after which I
worked with dry-point & graver. I
wonder if a time could come when
I would find nothing to do on this
plate. I worked until after 5.
After 6 I went down town and at
about 7 had tea. Every. I
walked the piazza for some time.
Then Annie read aloud to us
some of Motley's letters until near
10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 20, 1889

Rather bleak & cloudy with some
sunshine. Spent an hour in the
garden before bkfs. After bkfs.
out of doors until 11 — then down town.
Got a very satisfactory letter from
young Late writing in high praise
of my plate. I wrote an answer
at once. After dinner we started
on our ride rather early — about
3.30 & drove to Fairdale & back
over the hills, getting in at 6.30.
Every with newspapers & to bed
early.
