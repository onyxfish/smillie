## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 12, 1889

A pleasant mild & hazy day. Got up with the makings of a headache but managed to stave it off. It was about 10 o'clk when I got to the studio. Mr. Duff came soon after & until after 12 o'clk. was cleaning the big window. I ground a new scraper to send to Koehler & otherwise busied myself to little purpose. Wrote a letter to K. to go with the pkg of [illegible] & the dry-point plate I will send him this P. M. Home to lunch at [illegible]. After 2 went to Sellers. 17 Day S. & exchanged imperfect scraper. Back to studio soon after 3. Got K's pkg ready & took it to Exp. office. Home before 6 — dinner — At 8 took A. to [illegible] Club Rehearsal — then to Century — wrote letter to Mary — at 10.15 waited for Annie — home & to bed —

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 13, 1889

A nasty day — heavy rain — So dark in the studio that I could hardly see to do anything. I hung curtains & otherwise tried to get things ready to begin work. Home to lunch & back but did little in the afternoon. — Evef some writing — Chas. Collins came in & stayed until 10 — then I wrote & posted a letter to Herrick & got to bed at 11.30
