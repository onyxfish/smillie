## Left Page

### July, Saturday 13, 1889

A very pleasant day with heavy shower before 6 p.m. Miss 7—
spent the day with her cousins the de Scheveinintzs — my head was
very heavy & the day passed with little to be set down to my credit —
most of the time out of doors —
accomplished some letter writing —
After tea, or at 7¹⁵, we went out
riding, dodging showers & en-
joying the fresh coolness following
the earlier showers — went to del.
& got Miss 7. who rode with us. In
at 8¹⁵ Got letters from Pate & Co. B& Ge-
[illegible] D. Jill, enclosing draft for $38.⁷⁵
payment for Water Color "Paying attention
to Business" — Pate & Co. accept my Hunting
for plate, but the letter is not inspiriting.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 14, 1889

A charming day, breezy with many
clouds which banked up in the west
at night — some light rain — Up
late — after help — with children and
in the garden — Annie went with Miss
7— to the Episcopal Ch. taking Jim —
as Jetty too had gone to Church. I put
Ralph to sleep & then settled myself
to writing a number of notes —
Dinner at 1.30 — after that I tried
to get some photos of Jim & Ralph —
more writing — then out for a walk
about the place — Tea at 6.30 — then
to P.O. with letters — Lively chatting
with Miss 7. about Indians & western
experiences.
