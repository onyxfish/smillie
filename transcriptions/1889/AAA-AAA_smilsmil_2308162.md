## Left Page

### December, Sunday 8, 1889

Got up late in the m'g. Thinking that considering my dissipation
of the night before I was doing pretty well — but by 11 o'clk
I had to go back to bed feeling as though the bottom had fallen
out — very weak with sore throat etc. — Go? & Emily took dinner
with Annie. I was not able to take anything — during the night
I was desperately sick without reaching a climax.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 9, 1889

In bed until 4 o'clk in the afternoon — Dr. Yale came in the
m't & seemed to think that I was doing well enough on the medicines that we had in the house.
When I got up my head pained & my eyes burned so that I could
not read — nor was I able to eat any thing having suffered much
from nausea through the night before.
Ev'g. W'm G. Parke came in for a
better time, having just reached the city, with Mr. P—, from Scranton.
Later, G.S. came in — to bed again
soon after 10.
