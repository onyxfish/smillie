## Left Page

### February, Sunday 3, 1889

A pleasant day — Bkfst. late, as usual — then the mng. spent with youngsters & newspaper — Before
12 walked with Annie down to the Century & looked at the pictures —
then back, stopping at George's for a
few minutes — Dinner at 2 — Geo.
& Emiley with us. They stayed
until near 4 — againg with the
children, with Annie (Gretta's
p.m. out) until after 5 — when as
Jim's throat was evidently out of
fault, I went up to Yale's — not in —
went on up to Park. On my way
back stopped & Yale came to the
house with me — Tonsillitis — says
keep the children apart —
tea between 7 & 8 — Every
Charlie came in & stayed until
10.30 — I managed to get a
little writing done — then to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 4, 1889

Bright m'g. but overcast before noon
— saw indications of snow. Got to
studio about 10 & got to work upon
my painting of a Road bit near
New Haven. Sand papered it, then
smeared a glaze all over it & at last,
at about 12 started repainting of
the sky. About 12.45 went to Bank
for Annie — then to Mad. Sq. Bk. with
Sarah Leggett Simong's check — met Harry
Fenn there — Home at about 1.30 — again
to Bank — then to Brook's Bro's. & paid
bill — then to studio at 3 & painted until
near 5 — Charley Pratt came in
& made a short call. At 5.30 went to
Century Club — Home at 6 — Pain in
my chest, severe & very depressing —
continuing all the evng. Dinner at
6.30 — Evng. Annie read aloud
for a time from Robt. Elsmere.
Jim seems to get along nicely
so that we did not again send for
the Doctor — To bed at about 10.
