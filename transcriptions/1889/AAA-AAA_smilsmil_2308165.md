## Left Page

### December, Saturday 14, 1889

Snow began falling in a very business-like manner about 11 A.M. &
kept it up all the rest of the day, even. I felt better when I got
up in the w[?] after a good night's rest. Yale came in at about
noon & after test pronounced my temperature normal — but I don't
feel normal by any manner of means. I gradually gained in
strength during the day, walking the floor as much as I was able.
Evef. Annie went to Symphony Concert with Geo. Cook. Gretta and
Mary went out — Ralph walked & I was in charge. I worked with
him until 9.30 before he went to sleep. Annie came in before
11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 15, 1889

Pleasant day overhead, but of course shocking under foot. I spent the
day in-doors beginning to feel my imprisonment in several discomforting
ways. Played with Ralph & read my newspaper during the w[?]. George
came in between 2 & 3 o'clk. Dinner at about 2. In the
afternoon Chare came in — then Walter & Jno. Valentines & Geo. Cook.
Tea at 6.30 — Evef. more reading & wrote notes to Pate & Co. & Herrick.
