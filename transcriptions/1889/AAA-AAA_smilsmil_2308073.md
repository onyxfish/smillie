## Left Page

### June, Thursday 13, 1889

And again — not exactly rain —
but the appearance of it — Sprinkling
showers fell before 8 A.m. Later it
tried to clear, but it was not a brill-
iant success — I worked on straw-
berry bed before & after blef., & at 10
o'clk. went to work on my plate —
worked until 1 — then dinner — &
again at 2 until 4 when we went
out to Ride — Annie, Ralph, Jim & a
young acquaintance of his, Arthur —
we got in before 6. Tea at 6.30 —
Eve'g., after some writing Annie
read for a little while aloud.
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 14, 1889

Another threatening m't. with a
very few drops of rain & altho' the
weather cleared there were still
many storm indications — I got
to my work soon after 10 and
continued at it closely until
dinner at 1 — & from 2.30 until
4 when we went out Riding —
In a little before 6. Tea soon
after — Eve'g very still & mild —
Annie & I sat on the Piazza until
nearly 10 — a little rain fell —
To bed at about 11.
