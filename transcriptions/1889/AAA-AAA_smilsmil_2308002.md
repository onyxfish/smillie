## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 22. 1889.

A fine winter day — got to studio a little before 10 — finished charcoal sketch "a Promise of Spring" — & commenced painting on it — scratched off one sky & before one o'clk got in another — which, altho' unsatisfactory, I allowed to remain. Went to Bank & drew money for Annie. Home at 1 30 lunch — back at 2, 30 & continued painting until near 5 — much disappointed that I could not paint nearer to what I had hoped to do. Home at 6 — dinner. Eveg. wrote to Kate — then made out a number of checks & to bed about 10, 30.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 23. 1889.

A bright & beautiful day — At b&p. we rec'd a telegram telling us of the death, by apoplexy, of Jno. W. Ehninger at Saratoga, yesterday — Annie & I immediately wrote notes to Mr. E. — It was 10, 30 when I got to the studio — to work a once on painting "Promise of Spring" & worked faithfully until 1 — then home to lunch — back at 2, 30 & painted until 5 — home at 6. Annie thinks that Jim has Chicken Pox — he seems perfectly well but there are some eruptive pimples on his body. Eveg. Reading until Emily & Geo. came in — then Charl came in & stayed until 9, 30 — read awhile & to bed a little before 11.
