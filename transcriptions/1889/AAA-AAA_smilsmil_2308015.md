## Left Page

### February, Sunday 17, 1889

overcast & mild — now & then
a gleam of almost sunlight —
Spent the m'f with the chil-
dren & wrote the greater part
of my letter to Mary — Then
went to Geo's & left Mary's letter
to Charl — Then home to dinner
at 2 o'clk. — Afternoon again
with children — Mr. Valentine
came to tea & left at 8 o'clk
to bed early.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 18, 1889

A nasty, wet day — very heavy
rain in the early m'f — Passing
into a drizzle later — Ev'f, cloud-
less moonlight. To studio about
10 — the day being so dark I
could not see to paint — so I
jussed about & spent some time
among other things, in touching the
Dry Point Plt. with India ink. —
A large trunk & a roll arr'd by
Exp. from Cap't. McNair, of Saratoga,
containing a great lot of Ehninger's
studio truck — old canvasses, studies
etc. etc — I spent some time in
getting them out & assorting them.
Home before 6. Dinner. Ev'f
Katie Adams went to Rehearsal
of Mendelssohn Glee Club with Fred
Ireland, while Annie, Mollie
& I went to Charl's & spent the
ev'f there until about 10.30 —
Then home & to bed at 11.
