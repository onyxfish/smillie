## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 17, 1889

A heavy fog in the mt with
light showers — At 10.30 the rain
fell so continuously that the brick-
layer & his helper gave up work —
The rain fell all day & well in-
to the night, at times very heavily.
I wrote some letters — To Geo. To
Miss Hallowell & to Miss Lambert
to the Indianapolis Art Club about
some missing paintings — I went
down town in the mt & again
at 5.30 o'clk. — Eve'g reading
news papers until 9 — then [illegible]
read aloud from Motley's letters.
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 18, 1889

overcast mt — clearing before noon —
breezy, brilliant & quite cool. Spent
some time directing Burgers, the car-
penter who did some necessary job-
bing to further the bricklayer's work
about the milk room — At 11.30 to Jonuday
to look after progress of Standing press —
then down town — After dinner Carey
came with the 3 stones for ceiling of
milk room — scant in measure. Then
man came with lime — 4 bush. in-
stead of 6 — Albert came with new sills
for Piazza — wrong measure — none such
as I wanted at the mill — & so it went —
The life of a "boss" is not a happy one —
Mrs. Chandler & her son Will's wife called.
At about 4 we all got off for a ride
— back before 6 — Went down town at
7.30 — back soon after 8 — Read news-
papers for awhile — then cards
until 10.30 — To bed.
