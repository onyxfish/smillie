## Left Page

### January, Friday 4, 1889

Clear & singularly mild. Got to studio before 10 o'clk, & to work upon water color sketch "Where's my ma?" worked closely until after 4 o'clk. — Then to 2°. Nat. Bul. for Annie. — Home to lunch at near 2 o'clk — Annie at some Unitarians convention — I got back to studio at 3 & worked as long as I could see & got my sketch finished. — Home soon after 5. Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g Annie read aloud from Rob'. Elsmere until after 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 5, 1889

A wretched day with wind & rain — I was in bed all day very much used up with a variety of discomforts — chief among them an unusual sort of headache. Late in the afternoon a note came from D'. Otis asking me to attend a meeting of the Nom. Com. at the Century — Annie wrote a short note for me & sent it by messenger. I got up in time to go down to dinner & in the eve'g sat in the parlor where Annie read aloud from Rob'. Elsmere until near 10 o'clk. To bed at 11.
