## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 10, 1889.

Another glorious day — out before
blfp' — & added to my pencil notes
for one of my subjects — before 10
we all got away for our outing —
The children dug in the sand —
I made further pencil notes and
mew. About 11 I put Jim on a
pony & gave him a short ride —
Then Annie, Jim & I drove in a
buggy along the beach up to the
inlet & back — Got to hotel at
about 1 — dinner before 2 — After
noon I went to R.R. office to
make some enquiries about our
tickets — Then joined the family
on the beach — in at 6 — Eveg
wrote short notes & then Annie
read aloud to me until 10.30 from
Mrs. Humphrey Ward's "Miss Bretherton"

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 11, 1889.

*Germantown —
At Jas. A. Wright's.*

Delightful mrg. but gradually
the weather thickened until eveg.
Had a brisk walk before bkfs — after
that took charge of Ralph for awhile —
then Annie & I went down to the
dunes, west of the town — Got one
pencil sketch — Back to hotel at
about 12 — settled bills — finished
packing — dinner at 1.30 sharp &
all got away at 2.30 — Took 3
o'clk train & got to foot of Market
St. at 4.40 then Street cars to Broad
St. Station & just caught the 5.13
train & reached Queen Lane
Station at 5.30 — Jas. Wright had gone
out with us, on same train —
Tea at a little after 6 — Eveg
chatting until 10 — then to bed.
