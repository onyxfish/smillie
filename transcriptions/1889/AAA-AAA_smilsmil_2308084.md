## Left Page

### July, Friday 5, 1889

Clear, cool day with pleasant, brisk
wind from N.W. Got up with a
headache — About out of doors until
near 11 o'clk — Then got at my plate
& worked until 7 o'clk. Dinner —
Worked again until after 3.30 —
Then out riding — Annie, Mary, Ed[wd]
& the two babies — Rode until a
little after 6 — Then tea — We'[re]
so cool that I kindled a small
fire in Main room hearth — We
chatted & read newspapers.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 6, 1889

A very warm day — I worked about
out of doors — in garden & over flower
beds until after 11 — Then to my
work on plate — Polishing the flesh
work & softening edges of shadows
until dinner at 1 o'clk — At
a little after 3 Annie, Mary, Ed[w]
& I started for New-Milford &
got there about 5 after a hot &
dusty drive. Ed[w] went on to Bing-
hamton & by Erie R.R. to N.Y. city —
We drove slowly back & got home
a little after 7 — Tea — Eve'g
a glorious moonlight — was spent
on Piazza — To bed before 11.
