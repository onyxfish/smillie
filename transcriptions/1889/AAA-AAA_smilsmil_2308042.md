## Left Page

### April, Friday 12, 1889

Overcast & very mild — some light showers in the mtf. I spent the time rambling about the place & with Ralph. Lunch about 1.30 — About 3 Jas. Wright ret? from town & we had a very charming ride along the Wissihickon — Jas. W. Fanny & me & her boys — Annie, Jim & J. After going about 4 miles a little shower caused us to turn about. Annie made a short call at Ed W. Wright's — In about 5 — a rest & then dressed & Annie, Edith, Jas. W. & I went to the 2 Justice's & had tea — left then before 10 — heavy showers during the evef with heavy thunder & lightening.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 13, 1889

Rains in the early mtf with promise of a dismal day, but it began to clear before 10 — After noon [illegible] very cool & clear — I went into town by 10.20 train & spent some time going over the "Public Bldg" — on the Roof & up to the top of the tower — then to Enoch Lewis at 233 So 4th St — he asked me to dine with him — then to 1334 Chestnut St — & had a pleasant call upon Stephen Parish. Then to Penn A. of Fine Arts & spent an hour looking over Exhibn of Amateur Photography — then to Broad St. Station & met Enoch Lewis & went to his house & soon after 2 had a very pleasant dinner — Anna Burnham Lewis & her two children & Miss Justice, Mr. & Mrs. Lewis & I — It was 4.20 when I took train back to Jureen dame — It was after 7 when we had dinner — Marion came home to spend Sunday — Evef general chat & card playing. To bed at about 11.
