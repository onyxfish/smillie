## Left Page

### July, Sunday 21, 1889

Delightful day — Bright, breezy
& fresh — I spent much of the
mf in the garden. — Then,
about the house until dinner
at about 1.30 — Albert being
away I harnessed the horses
& at 3.30 we started out, going
around by Dr. Norris's & Elk-
lakes — Home at 6.30 — tea —
Had a few strawberries — our
last — Evef. watering plants
& attending to horses — etc — etc
until about 8.30. — wrote a letter
to Mary & got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Monday 22, 1889

A very pleasant day — [illegible] — a little
earlier than usual — At 8.30 Annie
& Miss [Frueaeff?] drove over to the
station of the "little Road" — Miss [illegible]
going home. I got to work on the
printing of plates — Some experimenting
— no success — Took two prints
for copy righting purposes — At
3 o'clk Albert drove down to the
D.L.&W. Montrose depot for the
Adams girls — Emily Cook & Jack
Downs — He did not return
until after 9 — Train late — Emily
did not come with them — as
all were very tired, got to bed
soon after 10.
