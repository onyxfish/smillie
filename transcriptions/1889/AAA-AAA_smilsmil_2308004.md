## Left Page

### January, Saturday 26. 1889.

Overcast with a drop or two of rain late in the afternoon — Got to studio's before 10 & at once to work putting away things — Mr. Duff soon came & she worked until after 12 washing the floor & doing other cleaning — I was until after putting things in shape for company — Home to lunch at 1. 30 — back about 3 — Geo. Insley gave a great Studio's Reception — so did Champney & I took the overflow. Annie came before 4 o'clk & we had a very pleasant afternoon — more friends coming in at times, than we could find room for. Had a pleasant chat with W.D. Howells & E.C. Stedman — also with M. Coomans and daughters — Annie acting as interpreter — It 6 45 before we got home — Charl walked up & took dinner with us, going soon after — Eveg Annie read aloud Robt. Elsmere until after 10 —

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 27. 1889.

A nasty, steady rain day — Did not go out of doors — Spent the day with the children — Reading news paper & magazines & doing a little writing — Eveg. Annie again read aloud from Robt. Elsmere.
