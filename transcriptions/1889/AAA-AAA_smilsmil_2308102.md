## Left Page

### August, Saturday 10. 1889.

A very fine day — as soon as I could after bkfp. I started to make a color sketch (oil) from the Holley rocks that I got last night — I also took some orthochromatic photos of the flowers — I worked furiously & in spite of many interruptions I got it about finished by dinner time — After dinner I made a camera-lucida reduction & pencil sketch for detail. It is my purpose to make a copy of the sketch in negative & dry-point upon a 6×9 plate — At about 3:30 — Nattie, Mollie, Alice Ralph & I went out and had a very pleasant drive, getting back a little after 6. — I rec'd. from Putn & Co. a check for $500 — they had not credited for my bill — Eveth, after some reading all hands joined, joined in a game of "Stop" & then "Dominos whist" — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 11. 1889.

A glorious day, very cool & clear, like a Septr. day — Bkfp. late — after 9 o'clk — the m't spent out of doors — after my duties in the garden I went with the others out to the hill side north of us & over the top of the hill to the east side & home thro' the fair ground — Dinner at about 2 — Soon after 3 Annie, Mollie — Fred — Jim, Ralph & I went out & had a fine ride — home at 5.30 — Eveth each one read for himself — I wrote a couple of notes & took them to P.O. at 9 o'clk.
