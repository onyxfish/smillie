## Left Page

### June, Friday 7, 1889

A brilliantly clear mf. Afternoon
overcast with some indications of rain.
All of our freight cases from Mf. were
delivered before bkfs. & all of the mf
until 12.30 I was hard at work un-
packing — Then I took a short rest
before dinner at 1 — A little before
3 Annie, Jim, Raelph & I rode over
to Mr. Joseph Oakley's & I bought
from him a pair of heavy, second-hand
trucks for use about the place.
Then home at 6 — Tea at 6.30 —
Evef. feeling used up I lay
upon lounge while Annie read
aloud until I fell asleep.
To bed at 10. Heavy wind &
rain at night.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 8, 1889

Heavy rain most of the morning —
close & cloudy afternoon — W. Oakley
came in the mf. with trucks. I
spent some time out of doors plant-
ing seeds of different kinds — Then
I went to work at putting the
Studio & etching room in order —
hanging up curtains & putting all
the small things into their places.
Dinner at 1 — worked until 3
then feeling tired I took a short
nap after which I went down
town — Home before 7 — Then Tea —
Evef. reading magazine and
newspapers until 10 o'clk.
