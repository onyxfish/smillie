## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 19, 1889

Bright, cold & windy — a combination of mid-winter & March. After 10 when
I got to studio — Set palette & got out
my view near New Haven "Spring is
Coming" — worked over it generally.
Home to lunch after 1 — after lunch
Annie, Katie & Mollie Adams went
to Avery's Gallery in Fifth Ave — then
to my studio — after they left I
sketched in & painted a calf on
the road for "Spring is Coming" —
It was about 5 o'clk. when I gave up
work & rested until 6 — then home —
dinner — after that, full dress for Mr. Villard
Grand Soirée Musicale — carriage came
at 8.15 — we got to the V's at about 8.45
a great crush of all sorts — Politicians
— literary celebrities — a very few poor artists
— millionaires — musicians — Princesses and
Kindergarten teachers — Ma. Illi Lehmann
Kalisch sang for us — Bergner played on
the violoncello & Bendix on the violin —
Then we were amused by a phonograph with all the latest improvements
Frau Kalisch sang to it & it repeated her
song — not perfectly as to tone & volume
but very wonderfully — a very fine supper
was served soon after 11 — we got away
at about 11.45 & home & to bed at 12.30

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 20, 1889

Fine day — It was about 10.30 when
I got to studio — Gifford came in
soon after, bringing me a Pf. of one of
his etchings & making quite a long
call — other callers came in & I
did little else than sort over, cut
up & burn old studies & canvases of
Ehninger's — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
The Adams girls rec'd. to New Haven
by the 3 o'clk train — It was near
3 when I got to studio — I daubed
some of my Dry Point proofs & put
them in press — again gave some
time to the Ehninger studies — got
a letter from Mr. E — concerning them
— At 5.30 went to N.A.D. to see Freer
about Dry Point &c — met W. H. Gibson
there — Home soon after 6 — dinner.
Chard came in at 9 — stayed until
10 — Then we went to bed.
