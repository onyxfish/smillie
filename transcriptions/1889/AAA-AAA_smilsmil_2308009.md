## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 5, 1889

A mild, overcast day, with light rain in the afternoon — Got to
studio at about 10.30 & to work
on upright — near New Haven —
Painting over the foliage & middle
distance. Home to lunch & back
as usual — & Painted until 4
when feeling tired & used up I
rested in big chair for an hour.
Then to Grady & McKeever's and
ordered changes on an old frame.
Home soon after 6 — dinner —
Evef wrote to Kate & mailed it
before 9. — Then until 10 Annie
read aloud from Rob't. Elsmere. —
To bed soon after —

Jim seems pretty well
during the day — but his right
tonsil is still much enlarged.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 6, 1889

A light fall of snow last night —
Cold, windy day — Got to studio at
10 S, much bothered through the day
by pain in my chest that depresses
me & makes me unfit for work — but
I painted away on my upright view
near New Haven — Home to lunch &
back — a little later than usual — I
got to work at my painting — about
4 young Harry Watson's came in — I
painted as long as I could see and
just in the dusk S. P. Avery came in
to see my "Cliffs" & to talk business
— but we made little headway altho'
he stayed until after 6 — I suppose
that I don't know how to talk bus-
iness — then home to dinner —
Evef Annie read Rob't Elsmere
aloud from 8 to 9.30. To bed about
10.
