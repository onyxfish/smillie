## Left Page

### September, Saturday 21, 1889

A horrible day — a succession of driving & cold north westerly
showers. Crowley & Halpin worked
at the brick wall until 11 o'clk. — then
gave up for the day — I got to work
soon after [illegible] & kindled fires in
studio & etching room — Min carefully
looked over & cleaned some china
[illegible] & washed down — then ground
some ink & experimented in printing
some [illegible] from 6×6 dry-points ([illegible])
for [illegible]. Before dinner got one
satisfactory [illegible]. After dinner took 8
more — did not get cleaned up until
after 6. Bet. 7 & 8 o'clks. went down
town & attended to some messages —
Evg. Reading.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 22, 1889

Very cloudy & windy & cold — the clouds
blowing away in the afternoon & the wind
dying down at night. Up late in the
m'g. About noon Jim & I went down
town — hunted for [illegible], the stage man,
then walked out to the Saupe farm
& through the woods, having an eye out
for leaf [illegible]. Dinner abt. 30 — at 3
we all went off for a ride — down to
Fairdale & back by the hill road — at
5 o'clk. — Being well wrapped up we
found it very delightful. Before
tea again went down town — saw
[illegible]. Evg. Jimmie read aloud
some of Motley's letters until near
10 — then to bed. Brilliant, quiet
night.
