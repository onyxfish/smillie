## Left Page

### June, Monday 3, 1889

A light showery day with some
good weather in small bits — I
spent the day in working about
the house & Studio — Had stones
set up in nursery — hall & [illegible]
room. Went downtown in the
m'f & spent some time shopping.
Dinner at 1 — the afternoon went
as the m'f did & at 6 P.M. again
I went downtown & did not get
back to tea until 7 — after tea
I wrote a letter to Kate & at a
little before 10 went to P.O. with
it — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 4, 1889

Wet early m'f — clearing later to be
sticky & hot, tempered by a fresh breeze
— cloudy after 5 P.M. — with rain at 7.
The m'f was spent at work with
Herrick — most of the time at the
vines on south side of Studio — Dinner
at 1 — after that, felt so sleepy that
I indulged in a nap & then again
worked at putting up wire for vines
until 4 when, the team being ready,
we drove over to Joseph Oakley, near the
D.L.&W. depot to see a pair of heavy
trucks that he offers for sale. We were
much belated & did not get home
until 7 — just before the rain — then
tea — eve'g reading newspapers.
