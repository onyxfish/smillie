## Left Page

### November, Friday 22, 1889

Bright m'f, but heavily overcast in
the afternoon threatening showers that
did not come — got to studio about 10
& to work on mezzotint" Hollyhocks"
Before I went home to lunch I
prepared paper etc, & on my return
I took two india pfs of the plate
& found that I had progressed satis-
factorily. After cleaning up it was
so dark that I could not see to do
any more work. — Then I went to
Sloane's — Bdway & 19th S't. Then to the
in 23d. S't & got a coat of juice — then
to Hunter's — about my glasses — & home
at 6 — dinner. Ev'g. after reading
newspaper I wrote a long letter to
Herrick & sent a note with check
to Amos Nichols, Cashier of Montrose Bank
took letters out & posted them before
11 o'clk. then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 23, 1889

Another bright m'f & another cloudy
afternoon. To studio soon after 10 &
to work on mezzotint in which I
am very much interested — home
to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — late & so.
sent up a roll of 22 pfs for signature
while I was out. I worked on
Hollyhocks as long as I could see —
about 4.45 — then a rest — & home
at 6.15. After dinner Charl came
in & after 8 o'clk. Annie & I went to
his room with him to see some very
fine Chrysanthemums — left there at
9 o'clk. & went to Walter's where we
stayed until 10 — then home and
to bed.

Him seems to be rather better
to-day & has been allowed by the
D't. to eat something.
