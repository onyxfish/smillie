## Left Page

### August, Monday 12, 1889

Pleasant wtf — clouding up in the afternoon with a smart shower between 5 & 6. Up at 6 o'clk — bkfst — at 6.30 and before 7 Parke came with his team and took Katie & Mollie over to M. depot where they took the early train to NY. I put in a good wtf work on Hollyhocks — getting a reduction & tracing of my color sketch — I then made a charcoal retracing upon the plate, a matter about which I am much puzzled, not having experience or teaching to guide me. Dinner at the usual time & then worked awhile before going out to drive. — I kept watch of showers but managed to get caught after all — Fred & I got wet but not seriously — Evtf I wrote to Mary & one or two other notes — took them to P.O. after 10 & to bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 13, 1889

Weather rather dubious but it managed to keep dry & at times was very pleasant. I worked upon my plate in the wtf (Hollyhocks) making a dry-point outline of my tracing (charcoal) — then with scraper blocking the whole thing out in a very general way — then rubbed it over with oil & black — a process, or series of them, that seems to work well — It was 4 P.m. when we went out for our ride & we did not get in until 7 — after a late tea I had to go down to deScheveuitz's about butter. I felt used up but had a pleasant little call — home before 9 o'clk — to bed at 10.30.
