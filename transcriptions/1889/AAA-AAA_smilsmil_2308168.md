## Left Page

### December, Sunday 22. 1889.

A nasty, dull, damp day — Up twice
during the night — trouble below the
belt. B.h.p. before 9 — All day
coninged about the house — Dinner
before 2 — In the afternoon Charl
& then Geo. came in — About
4.30 I walked with Charl up to
the new Century Bldg. Home
in about half an hour — Tea
after 6. Evely wrote notes to
Washington Wilson — W.G. Parker &
Smith of Smith & McCollum.
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 23. 1889.

Charming day — About 9.30 I started
& after attending to house purchases
went to Sub. P.O. 3d Ave. & 28th S. — then by
Elevated R.R. to Canal S. — to Kimmel &
Voigts — there for some time — then
to Findler & Wikel's in Nassau S. — then to
Pate & Co. in William S. — then to Charl's
in Wall S. — then to A.B.N. Co's — there
for some time — getting printing cloths
&c. — left at 1.30 — Rode up town — went
to studio & left [illegible] — then home at 2.20
& had lunch. To studio at 3.30
met W.M. Laffan — Spent what
remained of daylight wetting down
paper to take [illegible] to-morrow and
in progressing the pressing of some dry-
point [illegible] for Keppel & Co. At 5.30
went to 5th Ave. Hotel to buy Opera tickets
for the Adams boys & girls — none to be had —
thru home — dinner at 6.30 — Evelf
wrote a note to W. Laffan & took it to his
house 39th S. & Lex. Ave. — wrote other
notes & read awhile —
