## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 25, 1889

A mild wth with some faint sunshine — overcast before noon with a
very wet drizzle before 3 o'clk. which
continued into the night. Up late,
as my bowel troubles came upon me
again in the early wth. I wrote
a letter or two in the wth & after
11 went to foundry — then to Dr. R—
& had a professional talk with him.
Thro to P.O. & other places & home
at 1. Dinner at 1.30 — Crowley
& Halfpin gave up work before 4 o'clk.
I wrote another note or two & then
to etching room trying to fix up
some of my Poppy dry-point pls —
Eveth. some Reading.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 26, 1889

A miserable day of fog & heavy rain
until soon after 5 p.m. when the clouds
rolled away presenting a brilliant
sunset. Altho' feeling much out of
sorts I worked very steadily in the
etching room all day. In the evth.
I cleaned & wet a number of sheets
of Chinese india — ground up new
ink & took four pls. of dry-point
"Poppies" 6×6 — In the afternoon I
took six more pls. After 5 Annie
& I walked down town — to de Schwer-
nitz for brethen — P.O. & other places. I
got a letter from Charl telling of an
offer of $7000 for the Terrace House —
Evth. wrote to him on the subject
& at 9.30 went to P.O. with letter —
feeling very uncomfortable from a re-
currence of my bowel trouble — To
bed at 10.30.
