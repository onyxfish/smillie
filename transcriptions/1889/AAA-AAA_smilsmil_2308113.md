## Left Page

### September, Sunday 1. 1889.

Cooler — thick atmosphere with east-
erly wind. B&fp about ½ — the rest
& the wtf. was spent in the garden
& about the place — wrote part of a
letter to Charl before dinner at 1.30.
After dinner, loafing & napping until
near 4 — finished my letter to Charl
wrote a note to Pate & one to Sharp
ordering a copper plate 11½×17½ for
my big dry-point. At 5 oclk. we went
out riding — back at 6.30 tea before
7 — Evelf, a little writing.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 2. 1889.

Still cool, very dry with hazy at-
mosphere. Up last night twice with
trouble in the bowels, consequently
felt very weak & unfit for work —
G.o. Halpin & two men started the
work for the new milk room this
m.f. Taking down all of the stone
wall about the well — this a very
dirty job & everything is horribly
upset by it. I was unable to
complete a letter that I commenced
to Kate — I got to bed early —
