## Left Page

### May, Monday 20, 1889

A dull day with some heavy rain.
Got to studio before 10 & to work on
big plate — pitched in with the
burnisher — I hope that now I have
at last made some impression —
And went to Bank for Annie —
Home at 1.30 — lunch — wrote a
note to Dr. D. M. Stinson — Then again
to studio & worked until 5. Then
having got my plate ready for a
proof I went down Baker for tomorro.
— Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eve'g
wrote part of my letter to Kate.
Had a headache & went to bed
early —

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 21, 1889

Exceedingly dark mt. — a strange
yellow light, passing away about 10.
Some heavy rain — close, muggy
sunshine in the afternoon — more
rain at night. Got to studio
about 9 o'clk — & to work at my
printing — Took two pfs — a very
full one & a close wiped one — It
does not prove all that I had
hoped but is in such shape that
now I am ready to show it to Mr.
Huntington & Lauckner. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — to Studio at 2.45.
Busied myself about a number
of things until five o'clk — Then to
Kelpke's with another ½ doz f dry
point pfs. Then to Lauckner — who
promised to call at studio's t-morrow
mt. to see my plate — & to Mr.
Huntington's, where I left a note
asking him to call at the studio.
Home before 6.30 — Then dinner —
very much used up by headache
but was able to finish letter to Kate
to bed before ten —
