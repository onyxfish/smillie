## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 11, 1889

And again — more rain — gentle
rain about 8 Am. continuing most
of the mf. at intervals — in the after
noon some heavy showers — temper-
ature cooler than yesterday. I
worked about until after 10 — then
to work in studio on my plate until
a little before 1 — then dinner —
with the children until after 2.
then to work until 5 — It was
after 6 before the rain held up
so that I could go down town.
Back at 6.30 — tea — walked
on piazza until the lamps were
lighted after 8 — then wrote notes
to Gel. to Douglass — to Jul. Doe
& to A. H. Ritchie. — to bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 12, 1889

And again — more rain — gentle and
at intervals during the mf. — At 5
p.m. the weather cleared brightly from
the W. W. The horse lawn mower was
got out in the mf. & Selim was put
in harness & with Helbert & Constantive
the lawn was mowed — I got to work
on my plate at 10 o'clk & for the rest
of the mf. worked with graver adding
finish to the golden flagon & some
portions of the drapery — Dinner at 1 —
As I did not feel very well I thought
it best to give myself some out of
door work, so I spent the afternoon
working over the vines that climb the
piazza — A little before 6 went down
town — to de Schemeintri's for butter &
to other places — tea before 7 — Eve'g
Annie read aloud for awhile,
nearly 11 when we got to bed.
