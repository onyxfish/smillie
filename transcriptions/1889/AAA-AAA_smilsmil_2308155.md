## Left Page

### November, Sunday 24, 1889

Weather still mild — delightful
m'f overcast afternoon & at night —
trouble with my bowels that kept me
quiet all day — B&b: aftr 9. I
some notic' in the m'f & at 1.30
went to G. C. Depat & corrected
watch by R.R. time — Dinner at 2.
After that D. Yale came in with
his boy Leroy — spent half an hour
or more — thinks June is getting along.
Then I wrote a little more — Sometia at 6.30 — Ev'f. I completed
notes to Mr. Woodin — Herrick — Pet[illegible]
de Schevescintz & International new[illegible]
Co. & posted them before 10 — to bed
soon after.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 25, 1889

A dismal wet day — and I was
dis-maller — between a headache,
a dose of Rhubarb & soda and two
doses of Citrate of Magnesia I
had enough to attend to at home
without going to the studio. Most
of the time was spent upon my
bed. Sleep relieving me of much
of my woe. Ev'f. feeling
better I wrote part of a letter to
Mary & got to bed at 10.30.
