## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 25, 1889.

Still overcast, for a time, about mid-day, the sun shone out, but in
the afternoon & evening light sprinklings fell. With Henrick & among
the straw berries until near 11 — then
got to work at my printing & before
dinner time, at 1 o'clk., had taken
a proof which was very gratifying
to me in appearance — then dinner
after that again at printing — made
a stupid blunder that spoiled what
promised to be an excellent [illegible]. I
put the sheet of india ink stanched
side upon the plate — rushed through
a third [illegible] & then off for a short
ride with Annie, Mary & three of the
children — back at 5.30 — washed
up things in etching room — Tea —
Ev'g. wrote to Ritchie, Campbell
& Jn. Doe & atg to P.O. Tobed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 26, 1889.

Much heavy rain during the day &
at night — For a short time in the after
noon there was some very hot sunshine
I got to work in the m'f on a small
india ink sketch for Charl — a coat of
arms & Crests — About noon went out to
the strawberry bed — there until dinner
time picking berries — after dinner
finished sketch for Charl & again
went to strawberry patch & completed
the picking — I think about six quarts
— many of them immense — then Tea
— Early ev'g. Mr. & Mrs. Husted called
after they left I wrote to Charl &
to Yale — a heavy shower before 10.
