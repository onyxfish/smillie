## Left Page

### Fri. April 17, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A bright, cloudless day with sharp wind shifting to the east. To studio by 9.45 worked for a time on Frauen Kirche dome made preparations for printing next Monday. Jane \+ Mary Hinchelwood came at about 11 \+ spent some time. Home to lunch at 1.30 - lunched with Ralph. At 3.30 we went up to the College of the City of N.Y. \+ wandered about the bldgs \+ watched a lacrosse team at practice play for some time, then to Riverside Drive at 138 St. — walked down the Drive to 116th St., then home by subway. We walked not less than a couple of miles - it seemed more. Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g Jim \+ Ralph went off together. I read \+ wrote.

## Right Page

### Sat. April 18, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A little sunshine in the m'g — rain after 1:00. I got to studio before 10 \+ put in a morning's work in preparation for more printing — washing old print cloths — writing down paper \+ such like work — also taking a number of photo prints from album mounts. Home \+ had lunch with R. at 2. Later, the weather turning to bad, we had conferences on various subjects. Then he went out \+ met Jim \+ they came in late to dinner. Eve'g. we went to Chares' \+ spent the eve'g there planning details of our projected trip to Europe next summer. It was after 11 when we got home.
