## Left Page

### Thur. Nov. 19, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day + rather mild \- Bkfst. at 8 to Grand Central Station after 10 \- bought tickets to New-Haven \- then to barber's \- hair cut \- then to studio \- loafed awhile with Geo. who is not well \- home again at 1 \- lunch \- After 2 I went down town to Ringle's + got plates left there a week ago \- then up-town to Bouche + Sohm Opticians, Brunswick Bell + left my engraving glasses (Father's old ones) to be polished + put in new forms \- then to top floor + saw Mr. Jno. Carrere + had an interesting talk with him about the furnishings for the Print Room of the Public Library now being planned \- much pleased to find him so Responsive \- gave me card to Mr. Humphreys (Champney's son in law) who is in charge of that dept \- I made an appointment by 'phone to meet him at the library on Monday next at 10. So far as I know there is no expert directing that work \- Neither D. Billings or Weitenkampf are competent \- I hope to be able to carry some light to them \- Home at 5 \- dinner \- Eveg. writing.

## Right Page

### Fri. Nov. 20, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*went to New-Haven.*

Very fine day \- cloudless, beautifully clear + bracing air \- not too cold. I spent the m'fg at home, sorting, arranging + trimming the hundreds of photo prints taken from last summer's negatives for Albums for boys + self \- It is a difficult work because so much of the printing by Cullen is unsatisfactory \- I had a brisk walk on Riverside before lunch \- packed my kit + took the 3 o'clk train to New-Haven, getting there on time a little before 5 \- Ralph met me at the station + together we went to the Elkins on Prospect St. where we were cordially welcomed \- Ralph stayed with us over the evening meal, leaving in time to meet Jim who came up on the 8 o'clk train getting to New-Haven just before 10 \- I took a number of last summer photos. with me + some time was spent looking at them \- General chat till 10 \- then to bed.
