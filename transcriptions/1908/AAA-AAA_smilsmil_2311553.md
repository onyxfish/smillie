## Left Page

### Thur. June 4, 1908

**Weather:** Overcast m'f. but cleaned to be pleasant a some day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Stopped at Tiffany's with Jim's watch on my way to studio — I got there before 10 o for some time worked on plates bitten yesterday cleaning them up preparatory to taking prints — I wet paper & made other preparations before going home to lunch —
Ret. at 3.30 & took two prints from Frauen Kirche.
It was 6.15 when I got home — dinner at 6.30
Jim went for a sail in the ev'f & I fooled away the ev'f alone.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 5, 1908

**Weather:** Another fine day —  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got to studio at 9.30 into work printing from Frauen Kirche — I printed some [illegible] on old crayon paper that has fine color & texture — The result of the aqua-tint process in backing a line etching is disappointing so far — It mars the vivacity of the pure line — but I do not give up hoping that I may yet be able to use it satisfactorily — I expect better results when I print on india as I plan to do to-morrow —
I worked till near 1 — then home — lunch —
back at 3.15 & worked till near 6 — I took some [illegible] from Pont Marie. I got thro' an unusual amt. of work with little sense of fatigue or discomfort — Dinner with Jim at 6.30 — much disappointed because Ralph did not come as expected — do not know what prevented him — While at dinner Josephine (Mrs. Wills' widow) called — she reports that Minnie is in a very low condition — Ev'g I loafed — one of Jim's acquaintances came — they went away together.
