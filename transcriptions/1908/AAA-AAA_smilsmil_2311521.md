## Left Page

### Wed. April 1, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - out of sorts all day owing to severe headache. - from the house went direct to the bank \+ attended to business there. - then walked up 5th Ave. \+ over to studio, getting there before 11. - Hammered up hollow made on Port Marie plate by scraping out portion of original work - laid ground to put in new work \+ made sketch of the work that I propose to do - left studio about 12.30 - walked to 42d St. - subway to 72d - walked home. - lunch before 2 - was not able to accomplish much during afternoon - walked for awhile on Riverside \+ in the Park - no enjoyment in it - Dined alone - Jim dined out - Stupid evef.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 2, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant day - getting very breezy before evening - Ther. above 50°. To studio at 9.45 \+ to work at once on Port Marie - drew in on plate new tabs to groups of trees - varnished plate \+ bit in new work. - Weitenkampf came in just as I was putting in acid for last time with the result that it was over bitten - I left studio at 12.45 - went to Pt. Tuttle's - got there at 1 - man in office with Dt. \+ another in waiting room ahead of me - I waited till 1.30 - man still in office - I gave it up, went home \+ had lunch - rested awhile, then went to N.A. of D. exbn There till near 6 - I had been told that it was a "freak" exbn It is not so bad as that - there is much brutal technique \+ much devotion to dexterous brush work but there is an almost entire absence of the old fashioned namby-pamby niggling work. There is more force, virility \+ carrying power. - Dinner with Jim - Evef he went to his fraternity meeting \+ I got rid of the evef some way
