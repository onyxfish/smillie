## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 27, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A little sunshine in the m'f. then a dree day \+ clear at night — I had bk's at about 10. \+ after that loafing with news papers — feeling very seedy — Dinner with Jim \+ Ralph at 2 — Afternoon Charles came, reporting that Mary \+ her girls hardly felt well enough to come with him — he stayed till 6. Evef with the boys.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 28, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful winter day — clear, crisp, quiet. Rlph's at 9 — as the bug expert promised to be here at 10.30 \+ I wished to be present during his exam. I did not go to studio but waited for him — he came \+ my bed room had a thorough upsetting — Very little game was found except in elementary form — Two or three adults only — After the Buggist left I devoted myself for a time to the everlasting negatives — Then Ralph \+ I went out for a walk \+ back to lunch soon after 1. To barber's \+ had hair cut in afternoon — Then a walk — Dinner — two of Ralph's classmates dined with us — John Baugs \+ Keithpfall. After dinner they went with R. to Theatre — I wrote to Scott Brown to \+ Mr. Brown.
