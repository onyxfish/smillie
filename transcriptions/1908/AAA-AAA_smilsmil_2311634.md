## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 17, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day as viewed from my win-
dows — I did not go out of the house. The
Calomel, advised by D. T. did its appointed
work + I spent the day loafing about, reading,
writing + working over the lot of prints sent
by Cullen from last summer's negatives to be
put in Albums for the boys — Evelf. I wrote
to Katie Elkin + read the last chapters of
the Pennell's Life of Whistler — To me the whole
showing, whether of the man at the height of
his career, a poseur, a dandy, a brilliant egotist
with sychophantic followers, or in his days of
physical failing + at the end — it is all a
pathetic story to me — A man of charm + ability
— a genius, immensely over rated by himself +
his measure of himself accepted by blind adorers,
he is beyond my comprehension in his higher
+ later flights. I suppose the absence of genius
in my composition explains it.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 18, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Wet mf. — clearing in the afternoon. I
was up + had bkfs. soon after 8 — feeling much
better — think if I could get some dry sunshine
out of doors I wd. then be all right — I divided
my time between reading, writing, trimming
photo prints + pacing the floor like a caged
bear — In the afternoon, at 3 o'clk. when the
sun shone thro' a hazy mist I went out for awhile
but the air was damp + raw + I soon had enough
of it — Dinner with Jim at 6.30 — Evelf he
went out + I continued my vigils alone —
Charl. called me up on phone to tell me
that Ozone Reorganization was now accomplished
that $50000 was paid yesterday to the opposing
holders for their stock at $150 per $100 share.
$25000 more to be paid in a fortnight + a bal. of
$100000 in 18 months from now — This puts Charl.
Thompson + Girard in control — C. is optimistic —
I wish I could share it — Wallace is Pres. —
Thompson is V.P. — both men have proved their
entire untrustworthiness + will not scruple to
do anything that may promise to advance
their selfish interests.
