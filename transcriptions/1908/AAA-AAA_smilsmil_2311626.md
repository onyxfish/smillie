## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 30, 1908

**Weather:** Clearing cold with a very high wind  **Temp:** [illegible]

Stlfs. at 8 - at Charl's at 10 - he showed me the rooms on third floor that he has fixed for Mary \+ her girls - They are to have the whole story, en suite, beautifully furnished \+ very complete. Then I went to studio \+ worked there over books to be bound till 1 o'clk. I got chloride of lime \+ washing soda \+ made up a lot of Javelle water that I am going to use to bleach prints. At 1 went to Stikeman's \+ took Roberts's Holyplaned to be bound in six volumes - It was after 2 o'clk when I had lunch - Then finished letter to Mary \+ Katharine \+ mailed it - Then a nap - Dinner - Evening newspaper, writing etc.

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 31, 1908

**Weather:** Cloudy \+ windy  **Temp:** 39° to 46°

To studio at 10 - Jennings left water turned on heater all night - cellar flooded - asbestos jacket soaked \+ Radiators throughout the bldg. filled with water that had to be drawn off - his second offense - I worked till after 12 arranging the letter press \+ plates of an old work for the binder - Before 1 I went to Stikeman's - as I crossed Fifth Ave. \+ 36th St. The Great Republican parade was on its way up town - I dodged across it followed by an automobile \+ narrowly escaped being knocked over - Stikeman was out \+ I left China Ill? - Then to 2d Nat'l Bank - it was closed but I got in \+ the Paying Teller kindly cashed my check - Then home to lunch at 2 - Afternoon with my negatives - Dinner - Jim brought David Harris with him Later they went together to a theatre - I fussed about until bed time.
