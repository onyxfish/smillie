## Left Page

### Thur. May 7, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A steady down pour of rain all day \- I did not go out of the house \- but fooled away the day, much of the time being spent arranging my photo-films taken since 1904, especially with a view to completing my own collection of prints \+ the 1904 Albums for the boys \-

I was to meet Dr. Tuttle at the Presby\[terian\] Hospital at 2 o'clk \- to have some X Ray pictures taken of my insides \- At 1 o'clk. when the rain was coming down hardest Dr. T\- called me up on phone to tell me that it was no day for me to be out \- So, I did not go \- Jim had dinner with me \-

## Right Page

### Fri. May 8, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

X Ray pictures \-

A somewhat sullen attempt at clearing \- light rain about 5 p.m \- Some heavy rain in the eve\[ning\]. After bfst. I did some writing \+ did not get to studio till about 11 \- Vondrous came \+ took considerable of my scant time \- I wet down paper for printing to-morrow \- At 1 o'clk. I prepared lunch \+ then went to Presb\[yterian\] Hospital by appointment \+ met Dr. Tuttle in the X Ray Room \- I had 3 pictures taken of my innards \- not developed before I left \- so, dont know the result \- Then I went up stairs \+ called on Jessie Smillie \+ was fortunate in finding her sister Lizzie there \- I had a pleasant visit with them for about an hour \- then home \- dinner at usual time \- Eve\[ning\]. wrote to Ralph.
