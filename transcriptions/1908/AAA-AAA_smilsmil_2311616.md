## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 10, 1908

**Weather:** Overcast but not unpleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast but not unpleasant — I was aroused at 2 a.m. discomfort in stomach \+ cold — could not get warm, no chattering, by 5 o'clk I had a considerable fever — then I got up \+ took a small dose of castor oil — pulse 110 — I had snatches of unrestful sleep till after 8 — then I took more oil \+ got busy at about 11 — I kept my bed till 1 o'clk then dressed \+ had bkfs'. at 2 — At 4 I went with Ralph to Registration place \+ we Registered. Then back home \+ loafed till dinner at 6.30 Evg. while Ralph worked over his cat'gn. \+ arranging of our books I read \+ moused about.

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 11, 1908

**Weather:** Very pleasant day — comfortably warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day — comfortably warm — I had bkfs'. at 9 o'clk — The Sunday newspapers took up much of my m'g. — The remainder was given to my negatives — About noon Ralph \+ I went out for a walk — we went up Riverside \+ 110th St. Went into the large, new apartment house facing the Drive — 110–11th St — saw the plans \+ enquired concerning rents, but could not get the information wanted because the office was closed on Sunday — then home to dinner at 2 — Afternoon more work on negatives — Tea at 6.30 — At 8 went to Station with R. \+ saw him started for New-Haven — then to Charle's \+ there till after 10 — then home \+ to bed —
