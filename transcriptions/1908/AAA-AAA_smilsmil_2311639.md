## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 27, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful, bright day - breezy but mild - After bkfst. at 8, I went to Charl's - found him up \+ preparing to go down Town tho' still suffering from a cold - then to studio \+ painted all the m'f'g on "Evel' in the woods - the Giant" \+ think that I bettered it - It was after 2 when I got home to lunch - Afternoon I went to Bausch \+ Lomb, about magnifying glasses he I left there some days ago to be mounted - then to 23? P. O. \+ got P. O. Money order to send to Nat'l Geographic Soc'y Washington D.C. for some back No's of '05 - then to Palette \+ bought some colors - near 6 o'clk. when I got home - Dinner at usual time - Evening most of the time trimming photos for album

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 28, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A bright, pleasant day - air refreshing - not cold - Got to studio at 10 o'clk. \+ painted till after 1 on a slight sketch that I painted out of doors, one autumn afternoon at Montrose on Warner's lot back of the fair ground - I did not better it in my attempt to heighten the color effect where sunlight fell upon some distant trees - I shall have to try again failures of this kind tire me very much - Home to lunch \+ lunch at 2 o'clk - After that I took up interminable work of trimming photo prints - So much time has been given to these photos that I now regard it as a waste Dinner - Evel' Jim went out \+ I continued my work with the photos.
