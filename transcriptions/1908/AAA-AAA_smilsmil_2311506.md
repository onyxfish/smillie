## Left Page

### Mon. March 2, 1908

**Weather:** wretched day — foggy + dark — wet + raw  **Temp:** Ther. 36°

To studio at 10 — worked on "Port Marie" plate + got new work drawn on it — varnished + ready for the acids — Home at 2 — lunch — loafing + reading Dinner with Jim at 7 — Eve'g, more reading + a little writing.

## Right Page

### Tues. March 3, 1908

**Weather:** Very pleasant day — bright + crisp  **Temp:** Ther. 36°

To studio before 10 + spent the m'g biting the work put on the two plates, Marblehead neck + Port Marie, within a few days — It seems like old times; it is so long since I have had my acid room cleared up + in action. I finished the biting on Marble head neck + cleaned the plate — a little more acid work is needed on Port Marie. I cleaned up at 1.30 — then walked up to Lincoln Bank in 42d St. + made a deposit for Jim — then home + had lunch at 2.30 — after that wrote some notes, checks etc — + a little reading — about 5 went out to Riverside + had a pleasant 2 mile, twilight walk. Dinner with Jim at 6.30 — Eve'g he went out to a theatre party + I stayed at home + got [illegible] through with a serious job of mending.
