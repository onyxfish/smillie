## Left Page

### Sat. May 23, 1908

**Weather:** Dull  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull morning but much warmer + nicer — I feel much better than for some days past but did not yet dare trust myself to work in the atmosphere of the unwarmed studio I went down town to Cullens taking a lot of trimmed photos, to be mounted — 30 or more for the 1904 Albums for the boys — I got home before 1 o'ck — then lunch — Afternoon, as it cleared bright, I went to Riverside Park + loafed there for some time — I read a good part of Stevensons Dynamiter. It interests me as an example of Style (I'm not quite sure that I know exactly what style is) His bits of epigramatic wit, his fancies + choices of language are charming but, withal, his New Arabian Nights don't satisfy me — It is not the kind of reading that leaves me craving more — Dinner with Jim — Evef. reading

## Right Page

### Sun. May 24, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably warm day — Bkfst. at 9 — sat with papers for some time — then put a Roll of films into my camera + went out \+ made several street snap shots in the neighborhood — then in — out again before 1 o'ck + wandered in Riverside Park — Dinner alone at 2 — Jim has gone with a friend to VanCortandt Park to play golf — a rest of half an hour — Chase called me up + we arranged to go out together — He came before 4 + we went up to N.Y. City College — we heard the end of an organ recital in the great Hall — then out \+ saw part of a game of base ball on the athletic field — I had my camera + took some photos of the buildings — then we walked down to the Riverside drive in course of construction along that part of the way — we walked to 147\. S + then took train home — Chase went on. I got in at about 6 45 + rested till near 8 — then had something to eat — Jim got in after 7 — Evef. reading.
