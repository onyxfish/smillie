## Left Page

### Thur. June 18, 1908

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day \- spent on't at studio putting p'ps into their mounts \+ pasting hinge strips along the tops \- This work is now done \+ nothing remains now to be done but to clean them up \- Home to lunch at 2 o'clk feeling pretty tired \- after lunch I had a nap \+ after it, still feeling unfit, I loafed about \- Dinner with the boys at 6.30 \- Soet'f \+ some writing.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 19, 1908

**Weather:** Very fine day, but hot  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day, but hot. To studio before 10 \+ finished up work on the mounting of p'ps. for taking abroad. Nicole came \+ we had a final talk over about the Ark \+ his occupancy of it during July \+ Aug. About noon I went to Second Nat'l Bank \+ cut Coupons due from now to Oct. \+ left them with bank \- home to lunch at 1.30 — To studio again \+ there till 6 getting things in shape to leave. \- Home to dinner with the boys \- after it they went to Coney Island \+ I busied myself with many little things.
