## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 23, 1908

*Jim Ret'd late at night.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very light snow falling all day - More properly it was flying - little of it, not an inch, reached the ground - I had blkp. before 9 - \+ worked on my revision of my papers to accompany Father's sketch - After 12 I went out \+ had a good 3 mile walk on Riverside \+ got home without any sense of fatigue - which, of course, pleases me - dinner alone at 2 o'clk After dinner I indulged in a nap \+ then had another little airing - supper at 6.30 - Evef I went to Cooper Union - Meeting of People's Institute - I heard address by Mr. Alfred W. Martin on The World's Great Religions It was very interesting to me - Martin has a mission - He finds the "essentials" in all of the seven great Religions of the world \+ looks hopefully forward to the time when the human race will have one religion - He finds the Decalogue \+ the Beatitudes, and more, in all the Religions - In social \+ economic conditions there is a growth that must precede the Religious growth but the World's Religion is sure to come eventually -

Jim got home before midnight.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 24, 1908

**Weather:** Clear \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \+ cold - To studio before 10 - Started work on plate "Marble Head Neck" started in 1883 - I cleaned it up - laid an etching ground, drew in a sheep on a surface where another sheep had stood — it I scraped out "hammered up" - \+ I added work to the mass of rocks on the left (plate) - Then I varnished the plate. Then home at 2 \+ had lunch - After 2 went to barbers - hair cut - then to Fine Arts Bldf in 57th S. to see Architectural League Exh'n. just too late - it closed Sat. night - walked home thro' the Park - Dinner with Jim at 6.30 - Evef - Reading -
