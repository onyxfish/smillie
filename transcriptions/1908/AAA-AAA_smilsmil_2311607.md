## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 22, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

3444 miles.

A breezy day — sometimes so strong forward that I could lean up ag.t it without falling — at other times I could not hold my own ag.t it — a few rifts in the clouds gave now \+ then brief gleams of sunshine — there was much more of mist, muggy \+ gusty showers of rain — considerable sea in the m.t — calmer at night. While far from a pleasant day it caused me little discomfort. I ate \+ enjoyed my meals, had my naps \+ exercise in gym. \+ considerable breezy walking on the boat deck \+ elsewhere. The commodiousness of this boat is a constant surprise to me — altho' having a full list of first class passengers — there is no apparent crowding, there are so many comfortable places available for lounging. The smoking room, a large double deck place, foul with fumes of stale tobacco, beer \+ coffee, is the gathering place of the least desirable of the men \+ women. Charl' is so disgusted that he has not loafed there as he usually does on his sea voyages, but sits in the very pleasant reading room — The swell people find their congregating places in other corners \+ spaces on the several decks. Ralph had an upset while at lunch, very unexpected — his digestion seems to be on a strike — he kept quiet during the p.m. \+ went without his dinner —

## Right Page

### Wed. Sept. 23, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

377 miles.

Disagreeable — mist \+ fog making everything damp inside as well as out — the roughness of the sea went down decidedly \+ late in the afternoon there was an attempt at sunshine, but it proved to be a failure. I had my bkf. at 8 as usual \+ much of the day \+ evening was given to reading "The Awakening of Helena Ritchie" by Miss Deland. I had my meals, my naps \+ my gym. exercise as usual. Eve.g I sat in the pleasant reading room till 10. Then to the smoking room, very foul, there I found Charl \+ the boys interested in the auction of pool chances on to-morrows run.
I had to take a breezer on deck to clear my lungs before going to bed.
