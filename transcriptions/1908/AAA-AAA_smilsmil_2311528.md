## Left Page

### Wed. April 15, 1908

Ralph came home for Easter Vacation.

**Weather:** Dull mf.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull mf. \- rain from about noon \- I got to studio at about 9.30 \- changed pfp. in blotter. Man from Heater mft Co. came \+ explained to me what would be required to insure satisfactory heat in the building \- it promises to be a costly undertaking \- I spent much time in grinding mastic gum into powder for aquatinting. \- I got home at 1.30 \+ had lunch \- It was after 4 o'ck \- when Ralph came. Jim came in at 6 \+ we had dinner at 6.30 Ev'f. The boys went out together \- I rec'd a note from Aug'd. J. Conejblan, Manager of Ausable Club at S. Huberts, stating that Mr. Jas. B. Ladd of Phila had written to him, concerning the Ark \- to know if it could be rented. I wrote at once to Mr. Ladd, giving him some information \-

## Right Page

### Thur. April 16, 1908

**Weather:** Brilliant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day \- windy \+ unusually cold. To studio before 10 \- met Chas. Collins on Park Ave. \+ had a little walk with him \- Changed pfp. in blotter \- then got out plates "Chapel near Lugano" \+ "Frauenkirche Dome, Dresden" \- I propose to put an aquatint ground over these plates \- worked with dry-point on dome. Walked to 42d. St. subway to 72d \- walked home \+ had lunch with Ralph before 2 o'ck \- then a rest \- met Ralph at N.A. rooms before 5 \+ looked at pictures till 6 \- met there Meta Gavit Adams \+ had a pleasant little chat with her \- R. \+ I walked home \+ had dinner before 7 \- Jim came in at 8.15 \+ had his dinner alone \-
