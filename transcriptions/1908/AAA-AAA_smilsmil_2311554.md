## Left Page

### Sat. June 6, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day \- breezy + warm. I got to the studio at about 9.30 + to work as soon as possible at my printing from the Frauen K\- plate but there were provoking delays in cutting + preparing the india so that it was near noon before I pulled the first of\- The plate is a disappointment \- it is too evidently a work of deliberation \- it is too correct \- Too proper \- it lacks abandon \-
I went home to lunch + back soon after 3.
I worked till 6\- It was 6.40 when I got home very tired \- I hoped to find Ralph + was sure that Jim would be there to eat the special dinner that Kate had prepared for him \- but neither of the young men were on hand \- there was a note from R\- saying that he would be down next week + at 20 m. before 6 Kate had got a phone message from Jim saying that he was at Coney Island. The disappointment floored me completely + I had to sit down alone to the elaborate dinner prepared to celebrate Jim's birth day \- on his return his explanation of his strange behavior was that he forgot entirely that it was his birthday.
Evep. I wrote a letter to Ralph + to bed early.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 7, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely day, altho' rather warm for comfort in walking \- had my bath + rub down at 6 o'ck then to bed again + slept till after 8 \- Bkfst. at about 9 \- with papers till near noon \- then out \+ loxfed in Park till after 1 o'clk \- then home + had dinner at 2 \- a nap after that \- Charl came at 4.30 + left before 6 on his way to take dinner at George's \- Then I went to the Euclid \+ called on Minn + Walter Pierce \- found her in bed \- I sat with her while Walter had his dinner \- I left at 7.30 \- at 8 I went to Bdway + 75th St. + called on Jas. Carmael \+ wife + had a pleasant time till 10 \- then home + to bed.
