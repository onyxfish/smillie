## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 1, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very mild pleasant day - Ther. 64°. Went to Second Nat'l. Bank after 10 + arranged my matters there - it was after 11 when I got to studio - A very unsatisfactory day so far as character of work is concerned. Again I painted on the Warner lot sketch + at 2 o'clk had to admit to myself that the time had been mis-spent - I a? do better to destroy all of those old things + have done with it - It was near 3 o'clk when I had lunch - The afternoon's work was as unsatisfactory + wasteful as the m'g. From 3.30 to 4.30 I made selections of photo. prints for albums - Then I went + had a walk on Riverside - In before 6 + had a short nap before dinner - Evelf writing out checks for monthly bills + notes to Ralph, Mc[illegible] etc -

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 2, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Winter has come - brilliantly clear day, breezy \+ Ther. down to 32°. To studio after 10 - stopped at lincoln Nat'l. Bk. + made deposit for Jim - Cold at studio - owing to Winton's stupidity in caring for the steam heat, Gus + I had to work at the furnace for half an hour or more before we could get a clear, steam making fire - then the heat soon came up - Then to work painting on "Morning in the Adirondacks" - 20×24 - worked till near 2 o'clk making a number of little changes that have been in contemplation for a number of years - I think my work bettered it. Then I prepared some lunch for myself - It was 3.30 before it was eaten + palette cleaned up - At 4 I went to Charl's + spent more than an hour with Mary + the girls - 6 o'clk when I got home - Dinner - most of the evening fussing with photo. prints.
