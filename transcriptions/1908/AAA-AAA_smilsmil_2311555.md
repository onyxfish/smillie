## Left Page

### Mon. June 8, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The fine weather continues - cloudless, hazy, with warm southerly breeze. I got to studio before 10 \+ spent the m'f working over pfs. with crayon \+ chalk - The india pfs. are not satisfactory - I think the ink was in some way not quite right - Home to lunch at 2 - back at 3.15 \+ worked till 5.30 - Commenced to clear up \+ get the studio in shape to leave for the summer - I shall go away without having accomplished anything of importance of the many things that I had planned to do - my winter has been a dreary failure in every respect - Home at 6 - dinner with Jim - Ev'f I wrote to Mr. Atherton

## Right Page

### Tues. June 9, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still cloudless - strong southerly breeze - Ther. at 80°. Spent the day at studio clearing up \+ stowing away preparations to leaving for the summer. I had lunch at home at 2 - to studio at 3.30 \+ left at 5.45 a long day for me - \+ tiresome - Dinner with Jim - nothing doing in the ev'f \+ to bed early.
