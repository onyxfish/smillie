## Left Page

### Fri. July 10, 1908

Russell Hotel - London - Room 231,

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A wet day - few brief periods when it did not Rain - bkfs. after 8 - I went out & bought a few photos. of Cathedral - then down & around the south side of the building to see the shoring up of the walls & work in progress. Men back to hotel paid bill & at about 10 left in bus for station. Took 10.27 to Waterloo Station & got in at 12.10 Cab to Russell Hotel - To Rooms at once & at 1 o'clk had lunch - after that, to Bank by bus - got a letter from Deutsche Bank from Mary - drew £50 - then left the boys & rel? to hotel by tube - met W. Atherton & arranged to dine with them - About 5 I lay down on my bed & slept till Jim waked me at 7 - down to dinner, sitting with the Athertons. Before the dinner was finished, Holsapple sent in a card to Jim inviting him to join in some sort of "doings" - Jim & Ralph excused themselves & went away with Holsapple - I sat for awhile after dinner with the As - there wrote to Mary - then read newsp. & to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. July 11, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A few gleams of sunshine - a pleasant day for London. - Bkfs. at about 8 & after various detentions we went to the British Museum - the boys looked thro various galleries while I went to the print dept. & was accorded a pleasant interview with Chief Colvin - he made an appointment for Tues. at 12 when I am to take my p[o]s. to him - Then I rejoined the boys & we spent the rest of the wk at the museum - lunch at the hotel at 1.30 - After that the boys went off for a prowl - I had a nap & then a long session with Badeker, laying out a plan for our London Campaign - Then I went out for a prowl - went to Lincoln's Inn Fields & thereabouts - back to hotel at 6.30 & at about 7 had our dinner - immediately after the boys went off to meet Holsapple - I read newspaper for a time & then joined the Athertons & W. their guest, a young man connected with the War Dept. - We chatted till 10 - then to bed.

wrote to Charles in the m'g
