## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 4, 1908

**Weather:** Overcast, m't f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- nasty weather, snow + rain beginning before 1 o'clk. - Bkft. with Jim before 8 - News paper + some writing took my time till 10 o'clk - To 2d. Nat'l. + got new check book - walked up 5th Ave. - stopped at Wunderlich's + had a pleasant chat with Kennedy - then to Knœdlers + saw some fearful portraits by A. Benziger. - I cannot understand how anybody could live with such things about them - then to Montross Gallery + saw a collection of Willard Metcalf paintings - four I could live with - Then to studio + wrapped up three cows - To be given to Emily Cook - Home thro' nasty slush to lunch soon after 1 - lunched alone - Ralph met Jim to lunch + spend the afternoon with him - After 3.30 I devoted Remainder of P.M. to Rest. - Chas. Graham came to dinner at 6.30 - Jim went to his class dinner at that time + Ralph did not come in till 6.50 - then we had a very good dinner provided by Kate. Graham + Ralph left before 8 for the opera - + I loafed till bed time.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 5, 1908

**Weather:** Very cold + clear with high west wind.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkft. at about 9. busied myself in various ways till about noon - By that time Ralph was up + went to Chas. Graham's in Brooklyn - To lunch with him - I walked up West End Ave. To 106th S'. + back, a good 2½ miles The boys got up in time to take dinner with me at 2 - After 3 I took a nap + in about an hour Charles came in and stayed till after 6 - The boys came in in time to take tea with me at 6.30 - Ev'g for a time they worked taking books from a number of the Globe-wernicke sections + readjusting them -
