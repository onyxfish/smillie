## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 25, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull Xmas Day \- a very little struggling sunshine in the morning. We had no Xmas Tree \+ spent little time in exchanging compliments \+ presents \- I felt uncomfortable \+ stupid \- out of sorts in an indefinable way. A good dinner \- much too good for me \- at 2 o'clk \- I had a walk with the boys \- sidewalks wet \+ muddy \- air raw \- no surface \- discomforts increasing till, where I went to bed, my Temp. had got up to 101/5. Then I took a dose of quinine \- passed a restless night.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 26, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The sunshine fell upon my windows but I had to stay in bed \- fever rather less at 7 o'clk I took Castor oil \- at 9 o'clk. progress was reported \- While in bed, sometimes during the m'f, I had a visit from Shuter, our Subº., Ta bug effect. The expert performed incantations \+ exorcisements in Jim's bedroom \+ elsewhere \+ is to return on Monday to perform like exercises in my room. I was in bed till 4 o'clk \- then up to give bed \+ room an airing \- Temp. when I went to bed about 100\. Hopeful that I may get thro' without further trouble.
