## Left Page

### Sat. April 11, 1908

**Weather:** Wet m't  **Temp:** [illegible]

Wet m't but weather soon cleared — a good deal of sunshine \+ high wind — I got up feeling better \+ got to studio at 9.30 — Spent some time with Ryan getting report made by Sifpert yesterday — The matter of heating Tolfres building from my plant is not going to be all plain sailing — I got to work at printing \+ put in a good day's work. I was very careful of myself — not withstanding the long wait the paper was in very good condition. I commenced printing from Port Marie — at 1.30 I prepared my lunch \+ had a rest \+ then to work again \+ printed from Marblehead Neck. finished before 4 — cleaned up — washed up — home at 5 — very tired — I rested at once \+ had a nap before dinner at 6.30 — Alone — Jim dined with some of his acquaintance fellows — I spent the evening reading —

The wind was a gale in the afternoon \+ considerable damage was done.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 12, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

High wind all day — also brilliant sunshine — I had a scrub \+ shave \+ kept after 9 o'clk — I sat till noon with newspapers \+ a book — Then Jim \+ I walked up West End Ave to 101 St. \+ back — Dinner at 2 — from 3.30 to 5 o'clk I slept — Then I found Charl waiting for me — we left after 6 — Jim came in before 7 — I had a glass of synthetic buttermilk — Eve'g. Reading \+ to bed early.
