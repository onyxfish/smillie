## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 24, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snowing all night, all the m't, \+ held up early in the afternoon, clearing at night. Because of the storm I did not go out till late in the afternoon when I took a two mile walk peeling some inconvenience because of the cold. \+ bad walking. I wrote a part of a letter to Katharine \+ fussed about the rooms trying to reduce innumerable little things to some sort of order \+ system. My urinary apparatus is out of order again \+ towards bed time my temp. had gone to 100/10. \+ I went to bed much depressed. Jim went out to a dance with Phoebe Whiting.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 25, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant \- after a restless night I got into feeling depressed \+ seedy \- Temp. somewhat lower, full flow of urine with considerable pus. After consulting with D\. T. over the 'phone decided that it would be better for me to remain quietly in-doors. finished letter to Katharine \+ did a lot of clearing up confusion in drawers desks \+ closets. Eve't Jim went to the Armory for target practice.
