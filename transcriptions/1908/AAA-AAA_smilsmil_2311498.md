## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 15, 1908

**Weather:** Wind + Rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Wind + Rain — everything swimming — a very good thing for the city streets which are in a horrible condition of bumpy ice. To studio at 10 — took some points from face of G. D. + decided that it will be best for me to take out all of the light side check + put it in again — the texture is bad. It was after 2 o'clk. when I got home — Lunch — at 3.30 I felt very sleepy having been engaged in a struggle with a Rattle snake from 3 to 5 o'clk — I had him by the tail + had much difficulty in getting rid of him — so, I slept till 5 o'clk, coming out about even — Dinner with Jim at 6.30 — Katie's younger sister acting as waitress — Jim went to Armory to shoot in the eveg. I fussed about — Read + wrote.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 16, 1908

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant aetho' clouded much of the time. Fine moonlight night — Help — after 8 — most of the mg spent revising M.S. of father's auto biography + my supplemental work Before 1 o'clk. I went out + had a very satisfactory three mile walk on Riverside — Got in just in time for dinner at 2 — Afternoon Charl came in + stayed till 6 — I then had a half hour rest before supper — Eveg I worked over the souvenir album that I gave to Jim on Xmas, writing titles above or under the photos. It involved considerable searching thro' guide books + diary — About 10 I went out + had about a mile of fresh air + exercise — then in + to bed.
