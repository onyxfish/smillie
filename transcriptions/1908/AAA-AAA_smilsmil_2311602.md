## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 12, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright m'f. but cold \- The Ther. in my room has stood at 60 to 64° for some time past \- Afternoon clouded over \- light sprinkles of rain once or twice \- About 10 out with boys going out to the River near the R.R. station \+ getting some snap shots \- then back to Tier garten \+ wandered there till after 12 \- To hotel \- lunch \- at 3 o'clk went out hunting for street scene photos but found very few, even of good post cards, to my surprise \- It was 6 o'clk. when I got to hotel \- a rest before dinner at 7 \- After that sat with the boys in large hall till nearly 10 \- then to my room \+ did some packing before going to bed.

While out this m't we had a fine view of a Zeppelin balloon, circling about in very steady, sedate manner, describing short curves, figures eight \+ other movements that showed that it was under complete control

## Right Page

### Sun. Sept. 13, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hamburg \- Vierjahrszeiten Haus. Room 408.

Overcast with squally showers in the afternoon \+ a cloudless moonlight at night \- after bkfst. while in the reading room I met a young couple, man \+ woman, may have been bro. \+ sister, with whom I had a pleasant \- to me, interesting chat which lasted till after 10 \- they had just come from Russia \- Then I went to my Room \+ looking at my trunk, decided to undo what I did last night \+ do it over again \- It was 12 before I had completed my packing, the boys were ready soon after \- At 1 we had lunch \- then bill \+ tips were paid \+ at 2.15 we left the hotel \+ in a few minutes were at the h[illegible] station, bought tickets, passed baggage \- got our compartment \+ at 2.45 started for Hamburg \- getting there at 6.30 after an uninteresting ride \+ passing thro' a number of gusty showers \- To hotel with little delay \+ shown at once to our Rooms on fourth floor 406 \+ 408 facing the Alster \- I took 403, with balcony. The same room I think that I had four years ago \- The boys tossed up for choice \- Ralph got the one adjoining mine, with bathroom between \- Jim had to go across the hall to a little room \- Dinner at 7.30 \- Ev'n'g I wrote to Mary \- The moonlight over the Alster, as seen from my balcony, delicious \- like an enlarged Venice \- shimmering lights \+ boats moving about \- after 10 had a short walk.
