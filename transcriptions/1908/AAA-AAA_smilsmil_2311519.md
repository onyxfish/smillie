## Left Page

### Sat. March 28, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Act m'g. Bkfs. in bed \- rec'd copy of new Will from Clarence Smith \- Two paragraphs do not meet my approval \- I 'phoned to Smith \+ made tentative appointment for next Monday. Afternoon there was some sunshine \+ mild drying weather \- After having made a good start on a letter to Mary I went out to the Park \+ loitered there for some time among the children \+ nurses. There was nothing inspiriting to me in it \+ I got back home as little pleased to be there. \- Had dinner with Jim, making a very light meal of it \- Eve'g he went to an "Athletic event" \- 7e Regt. \+ with new magazines I worried thro' to bed time in an unsatisfactory way.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 29, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain during the m'g. Late in the afternoon it blew off cold. \- I got up \+ had my bkfs. at about 9 \+ then read news papers \+ looked at the pictures for the rest of the m'g. \- not very inspiriting employment. While feeling very weak I think that there is a slight improvement in my general condition. \- Had dinner with Jim at 2 o'clk. Soon after that Hugh Croswell came \+ Jim went off with him \- he came back at 6.30 \+ we had tea \- Eve'g he read aloud to me for awhile \+ I wrote a little on letter to Mary.
