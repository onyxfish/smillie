## Left Page

### Fri. May 29, 1908

**Weather:** overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast \- Easterly wind \- sunshine for a couple of hours or more during the middle of the day \- muggy \- Then again overcast \- To studio before 10 \+ again at work scouring on overbitten plate \- Home to lunch before 2 \- back to studio at 3'5 \+ worked till 5 \- Then home at 6 after a short walk \- Dinner with Jim \- Well he went out \+ I copied into my mem. book itinerary for summer trip abroad \-

## Right Page

### Sat. May 30, 1908

**Weather:** Very heavy rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very heavy rain \- beginning before 6 \- Then holding up till near 10 \- long enough to let the Decoration Day procession get started \- After\-noon, rain heavy \+ continuous \- A great disappointment to me as I had planned to go to New\-Haven to see the Princeton\-Yale Ball game \- Instead, I went to studio \+ completed the cleaning of overbitten plate \- Chas Collins called \+ sat with me for some time \- He seemed to be depressed \- Home before 2 \+ had lunch \- Too wet \+ dark to go to studio in the afternoon so I just loafed it away \- Jim came in with Hugh Croswell before 4 o'clk \+ went with him to dinner \- I had dinner alone at 6.30 \- Well writing titles \+ dates under photos in 1904 Album.
