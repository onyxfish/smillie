## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 1, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Peasant day aetho' m'g sky was sicklied o'er with a pale cast — but later in the day it cleared finely — I got up before 8 after a fairly good night + feeling better than for sometime past — Temp. still 4/5° below normal Had bkft. then some writing — then out + walked up West End Ave. to 94th St. Soon after my return Phabe Whiting called — The boys were neither of them up yet but they were able to present themselves before she left. Dinner at 2 — At about 4 I went out + walked up Riverside drive to 96th St. Rather cold + windy — on my return found Chas. waiting for me — he stayed till 6t. The boys came in at 6.30 + we had tea. Eve'g. I read some + a little work with my accts.
