## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 9, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Duller than yesterday, dry & quite mild. 56°. To studio at 10.30 — Ther. there at 80°. The new steam heater is too much for such weather — I wasted the m'f with bleaching operations this fussing with such matters of secondary importance, when I have so much serious work laid out to do & so little time & strength to do it is inexcusable \+ I am continually upbraiding myself — but seem to be unable to mend my manners — Home to lunch — & continued the improper use of time during the rest of the day — walked on Riverside from 5. to 6 — The misty gloom — The mystery of Twilight, was very charming to me — Dinner — Eve'f I was unfitted for anything because of a cathartic that I took after lunch — My machine along the canal line is very troublesome just n[illegible]

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 10, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still dull & misty — Went to 2° Nat'l Bank before going to studio — Got to studio soon after 10 & spent the m'f bleaching — Home at 1.30 — lunch — spent the afternoon with my negatives & at last got them in shape to take them to Cellen & give orders to print for the albums for Jim & Ralph — A poor showing for a days work — Had a 2½ mile walk on Riverside before 6 o'clk — Then dinner — Evening I wrote to Ralph.
