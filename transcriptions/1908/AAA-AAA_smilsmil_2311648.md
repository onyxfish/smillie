## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 15, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph home for the Holidays.

My head \+ throat gave me much trouble last night — disturbing my rest — but I got up \+ had my bkfs'. at 8 — The weather was overcast \+ my nose \+ throat were too sensitive for me to dare to go out — I stayed in-doors pacing back \+ forth thro' my small rooms — I worked on Jamaica \+ Costa Rica negatives \+ prints, numbering them \+ writing titles on the backs of the mounts. I wrote several notes \+ spent some time over Frank Weitenkampf's book, just out, "How to Appreciate Prints" — I shall not yet commit myself to any criticism, but so far as I have penetrated it is exactly what I w'd. expect as the outcome of such a man as I know F. W. to be. Jim came early \+ dressed to go out to dinner — Ralph came before he got away — R. \+ I had dinner together — Eve'g R. went to Charles' to call on Mary \+ her girls. I spent the eve'g Reading.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 16, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some pleasant sunny weather in the m'g but the afternoon was clouded over — I had trouble towards m'g — after 3 o'clk. — with mucus membranes of head \+ throat — \+ my rest was much disturbed — I got up at 7 \+ had bkfs'. I kept indoors till near noon — then out for a little while but found the breezes too brisk \+ played out for my comfort \+ I soon got home again — Lunch with Ralph at 1 — during the p.m. I made up for some of the sleep that I lost last night — During the day I busied myself with the negatives \+ prints of the Jamaica \+ Costa Rica trip in 1907 — I also read some in Weitenkampf's new book without increasing my respect for it — Dinner with Jim \+ Ralph at 6.30 — Swe'f Holsafple, Jim's college chum, came in for a little while — After 8 he, Jim \+ Ralph, went to the Yale Club \+ I continued my vigil.
