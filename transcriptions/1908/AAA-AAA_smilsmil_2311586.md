## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 9, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day - I did not get up till late + I let the boys lie - I ate very little for bfst. - my digestive apparatus seems to have almost stopped working - A lower down the machinery is working overtime - at about noon I strolled out with my camera - to Madelaine + theu across the Champs to the Two Art Palaces where I got a number of snaps shots - find it difficult to get the pictures I wish, of these bldgs as the extent of the edifices + the limits of points of view do not come within the lines of my camera. Back to hotel + had lunch with the boys - then a rest till near 4 o'clk. then took cab + drove out to the Bois de Boulougne - making the entire circuit - a lovely afternoon + an interesting drive - a vast number of people all over the grounds + great cavalcades of vehicles. Back to hotel after 6 - Dinner at 7 - Evg. in the Reading Room + in our bed rooms.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 10, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charles arrived.

A very pleasant day, altho rather hot - I was routed out of my bed at 4 A.M. - call imperative - I did not get up till after 8, feeling very slim + seedy - I had coffee + a roll - about half an hour after that I had a singular turn - I was just able to get to my room + lay there for some time in a sort of half swoon - cold sweat breaking all over me - + I did not know whether I was going to vomit or purge. It was a horrible experience - but after a long time, as it seemed to me, it slowly passed away - then the boys went off on a prowl - + I lay in my chair till noon - then feeling better I had courage enough to saunter with my camera to the Madelaine - a crowd there - a daughter of Pres. Fallieres to be married - I went to Rue Rivoli + around by Place Vendome + in at 1 o'clk + had a little to eat - After a rest we took cab to the Eiffel Tower + by ascenseur reached the top - a wonderful view altho very hazy over the extreme distance - 5 o'clk when we got to hotel - I rested till 6 - We went to the Gare d'azare at 6.20 the hour we were told that the Special we get in from Cherbourg. It did not come till about 7.30. In the mean time we went to a Restaurant near - the boys got a dinner - I had a couple of eggs - in spite of crowds + confusion we spotted Charles + after some not unreasonable delay we got into a cab with him + his hand baggage - He was furious + contemptuous by turns at the miss management of the R.R. - waiting 1½ hours at Carbourg after passengers were all in the train - he says it could have been here at 4 o'clk. His two rooms at the Chatham are quite palatial - 40f per day - He cleaned up a bit + had a late dinner - then in our Room till 10.30
