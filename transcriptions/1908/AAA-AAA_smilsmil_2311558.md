## Left Page

### Sun. June 14, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still pleasant weather — I had my bath soon after 6 — then to bed again — a nap — up after 8 \+ bkfs. at 9 — after looking over the m'f papers I got to work upon my wardrobe — got out woolen clothes. brushed them \+ then packed them away in tar paper \+ with camphor balls — Had dinner at 2 — then a nap. After that I went to Sam'. Colman's to see his baby — he had gone to the country — then to Dielmanns — they too were in the country — then I went away up to Manhattanville \+ called upon Meta Gavit (Mr. Chas. Adams) there till 6.30 — then home — found Jim \+ Willie Cook at supper — later they went out. I wrote to Katie Elkin \+ W. Tho. Atherton.

## Right Page

### Mon. June 15, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

At last, a wet day — overcast m'f — Rain began falling at about noon \+ kept on steadily, with thunderstorm attachments during the rest of the day \+ ev'f. I went to Clausen's before going to studio, saw his maker of mounts \+ gave him directions to cut mounts for some of the larger pfs — then got a dozen 15×20 boards \+ went to studio \+ spent the rest of the morning in marking upon them lines for openings for the various prints — Home \+ had lunch at 2. Then a rest — Had a 'phone conversation with Mr. Heymann in reply to a note that I mailed to him yesterday — he reiterates promise to let me have the apartment I wish as soon as possible — Wrote to Ernest Roth — \+ worked in a desultory way at preparations for our voyage. Dinner — Ev'f was mainly given to getting the drug shop, that I expect to take with me, into order.
