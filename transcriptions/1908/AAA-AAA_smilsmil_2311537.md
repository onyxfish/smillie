## Left Page

### Sun. May 3, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant day but unreasonably cool. Up at 6 o'clk — had my bath, went to bed again + slept till near 9 — then up + had bkfs. Some time loafing + reading — then out to Riverside — gall bladder or something else. Troublesome but I was able to walk slowly without much discomfort — Home in time for dinner at 1.45, a little earlier than usual because Jim has to join his Regiment in its annual attendance upon Divine service — Reading + sleeping till after 4 — Chat, came in + after a while we went to the Park — the beautiful afternoon + the length of the day betrayed us + it was after 7 when I got home — something to eat + a loafing evening till early bed time.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 4, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day but I was not able to enjoy it — my combination of Kidney + liver, very good as a haslet, but very bad as a "combine" against internal interests, made it impossible — I could only drag about the house in mental + physical depression. I spent the m[?] on my lounge with a hot-water bag under my vest. Afternoon I sat at open windows for more than an hour — Dinner with Jim — Well Gil Kinney came to borrow fishing tackle as he is going off on a fishing vacation.
