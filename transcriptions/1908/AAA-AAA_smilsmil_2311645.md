## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 9, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

After an uncomfortable night of pain \+ fever I was unable to get up in the m't \+ passed the day in bed \- my ailing seems to be a composite affair \- primarily a cold affecting the kidneys \+ other organs \- Dr. Tuttle came in the evening \+ stayed with me for about an hour helping me along "mixed cure" lines \-

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 10, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day \- clear \+ sunny \- I could not describe last night as refreshing or altogether restful \- but in the m't fever was gone \+ I had but little headache \- Castor oil cleared the bowels \+ copious drinking cleared the/[illegible] plug in the right Kidney \- I got up before 12 \+ had a short walk out of doors \- I busied myself as best I could \+ ate with Jim, a light dinner Eve't he went to drill \+ I was alone.
