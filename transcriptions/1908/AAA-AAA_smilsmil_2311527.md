## Left Page

### Mon. April 13, 1908

**Weather:** Overcast n'th  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast n'th - then clouds + sunshine. Pkfo. at 8 - wrote note to Howard Kingsbury. After 10 when I got to studio. I spent some time in clearing up - then in cleaning the pfs. Taken on Sat - then in damping them + getting them under press between blotters - It was 1.30 when I started for home - lunch at 2 o'clk - then a nap - after 4 I went to 42nd. St. to a large heating furnace place + saw types of heaters such as may be used in my studio's bldg - then to Century - met Chas. Huntington - Wm. Alexander - Roelker Smedley etc - I wrote in Candidatis Book name etc of Howard Thayer Kingsbury + my sig. as Seconder - Men to 42nd. S' Subway + home at 6.30 - dinner - Eve'g Reading.

## Right Page

### Tues. April 14, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day - To studio at 9.30 - took pfs. that were put between blotters yesterday out \+ replaced blotters with dry ones - then I got out the gum Mastic, mortar + pestle + commenced pulverizing it preparatory to some aqua-tint work. About 1 o'clk I walked up to 42nd S'. Station - to 72nd. S' by 4pp. + then walked home - lunch at 1.30 - a bit of a rest - then to Rogers Peet with summer hat + left it to be cleaned - then to Wanamaker's + bought Towels, sheeting, pillow-cases, sugar Tongs, neck-ties + all sorts of things - then to Iden + Co. + bought an electric lamp + shade for my Table - altho' it was bulky + heavy I carried it home. getting there at about 6 - Dinner alone at 6.30 - June came in after 8 - overwork.
