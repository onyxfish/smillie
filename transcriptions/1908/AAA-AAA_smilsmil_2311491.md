## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 1, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A horrible day — sleet, rain + wind. I did not go out at all — Finished writing a resume of father's most notable work. Ralph did not go out until late in the afternoon — we amused ourselves + one another as best we could. Jim lost his half holiday by having to remain at the office + work. Dinner at 6.30 Eve'g he got an invitation from Alice Ireland to escort her to the opera — the went — I wrote titles on the mounts of European photos, of which I have a great number.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 2, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph Rel? OK. H. in the eve'g.

After a night of howling wind the day was exceedingly cold + squally — some light snow at night — I did not wake till 9 o'clk — bkfst before 10 — Jim + Ralph got up later + after their bkfst. went out for a walk — about noon I went out + in about 20 min. got as much as I could stand — Dinner at 2 — the boys again went out + made a call upon a young lady. a steamer acquaintance — about four o'clk I went out + did better than in the m'g. being out about half an hour — Chat. came in about 5 + left at 6 — Supper at 6.30 — soon after Jim went with Ralph to the station.
