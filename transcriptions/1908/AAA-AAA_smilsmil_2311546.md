## Left Page

### Thur. May 21, 1908

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast except for an hour or two in the afternoon when the sun shone out — then I sat at the open window — my early m't experience much less trying than yesterday I got up \+ had my bkfs'. at about 8 o'clk. I wrote some notes — sent birth-day congratulations thro' Emma to Aunt Jessie who will reach her 89° mile stone on Sun. In the afternoon a pkg came from Cullen's with my camera \+ a lot of prints from negatives sent to him some time ago. Prints from 1904 negatives to extend my own collection on white mounts \+ to complete the Souvenir Albums for the '04 trip that have been so long underway for the boys — Dinner with Jim. Eve't at work with photo. prints.

## Right Page

### Fri. May 22, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain this m't early — ceased during the m't — more or less fog during the day. I had bkfs'. with Jim before 8 o'clk — Trouble in head \+ throat much lessened — For about an hour after 3 o'clk. I sauntered in Riverside Park — the air was damp \+ mild — D. T. called me up on 'phone to learn how I was getting along — I spent most of the m't assorting photo. prints rec'd. yesterday from Cullen \+ in trimming them preparatory to mounting — I also gave some time to reading R. L. Stevenson — Afternoon a repetition of the m't. Geo. called to see me at about 5 o'clk — Dinner — Eve't with photos \+ reading.
