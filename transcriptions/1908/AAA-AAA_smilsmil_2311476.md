## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 2, 1908

**Weather:** A brilliant winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A brilliant winter day — After 10 o'clk I went to The Ansonia to find H. A. Allen The dentist who used to do work for me — he was gone \+ in his place I found D. Thos M. Grace — I talked with him about retaining an ulcer set for me — Then I went to 2° Nat. \+ attended to fam. business there. Then I walked to studio \+ was there for a short time — Then home at a few min. after 1 \+ had lunch with Ralph — After that, feeling tired I had a nap — Then out \+ had a short walk — Dinner at 6.30 with Jim \+ Ralph — Eve. Jim went to drill \+ Ralph went to make a party call on The Woodins — I loafed about till 10.

Temp. mt. 97°
" night 98°

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 3, 1908

**Weather:** Bright day but cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright day but cold \+ windy — Bkf. with Jim before 8 o'clk — Mt. had a number of letters to write — it was nearly 2 before I left the house — went to studio — picked out the sky on painting that I intend to give to Emily (Mr. Geo.) Cook — Then home soon after 1 o'clk. I had lunch with Ralph — later, at about 2.30 we went to the Mus. of Nat. History \+ were there till near 5 o'clk — Then home \+ I Rested till dinner at 6.30 — Eve. The boys went to hear the Merry Widow \+ I spent the eve. alone

Temp. normal to-day.
