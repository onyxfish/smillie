## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 12, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable day- wet with some very heavy showers - I was unusually long in waking this m'f - I did not get fairly awake \+ up till after 9 - I had a curious succession of dreams that fit in with other dreams that I have had ever since Annies death. For a considerable time I dreamed at intervals that A - was with me but in a sort of France, seeming unconscious of my existance - This sort of dream continued for months - then there was a change - she seemed to be alive but ignored me - did not in any way Recognize me — Later in my dreams she showed aversion \+ refused my pleadings - these dreams were depressing of course - After a time conditions were mitigated \+ Annie had agreeable communication with me - we lived together but there was always the unsolved mystery as to why there had been a separation - I never dared to probe it. Last night good Relations were fully established \+ active preparations were under way for a return to our ordinary life together - it was a question whether another marriage ceremony would be necessary \+ that had been decided against when I wake up - It will be 18 years on the 27 ins. since she died.
I used hte p. before 10 o'clk - did little else than fool over newspapers till dinner at 2. After that I read the feeling sleepy I gave way to it \+ had a nap from which I was roused by pelting of hail on my window. More reading till 6.30 - then a light supper - Even I commenced a long delayed letter to Robert Roth.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 13, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day - Got to studio at about 10 \+ spent the m'f getting ready to begin reworking my plate "The Goldsmith's Daughter" after Daut. Huntington, done twenty years ago - I had a long hunt for p'p., photos from painting \+ the plate - these were all hidden away in different drawers \+ closets. I found my choice p'f - also one carefully retouched two or three years ago - I hope to get more tone \+ mellowness in the flesh, reduce hard lines \+ quiet the sky background - The plate had a deep tone over it \+ that I removed by bloting \+ oil \+ by Putr-Pomade Had lunch at home before 2 o'clk. After that had my hair cut; then went to Wanamakers \+ ordered curtains for the dining room windows - Home at 5 - Dinner at 6.30 evening, reading.
