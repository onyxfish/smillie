## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 21, 1908

**Weather:** Sunshine + cloud — cold + windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim went to New-Haven. — Sunshine + cloud — cold + windy — Jim was up unusually early to make preparations for his trip to New-Haven this afternoon to go to a fraternity dinner there to-night — I got to studio before 10 + until 1.30 I worked at clearing out the dark room — Then home + it was after 2 o'clk before I had my lunch — The remainder of afternoon was spent with newspapers + magazines + a 2 mile walk before dinner — dined alone — eve'g reading + a little writing.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 22, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day, but the wind almost too wintry for me. To studio at 10 — I got out etching plate "Marblehead Neck" — done many years ago for a Collection of Am. etchings pub. for the etchers by Cassele + Co. — A sheep is in the foreground that was always to me a weak spot + failure — I had it taken out + now plan to put another in its place + make other changes that I hope may be improvements — while hunting for studies of sheep I became interested in my big drawer full of photos, + in consequence spent the rest of the m'f'g in re-arranging them — Home + had lunch before 2 o'clk. Some writing in the afternoon — a walk before dinner — Eve'g I went to Fred. Ireland's — E. 40th St. — Saw him for a few minutes — Alice's father + step-mother have died within the past fortnight + she is not at home — Then to Mary Cook's — E. 37th St. — all out — Then to Walter Cook's + sat with him + Louise till 10 o'clk — Then home.
