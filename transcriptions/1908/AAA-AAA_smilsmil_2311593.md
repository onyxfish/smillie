## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 23, 1908

**Weather:** Dull m[illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull m[illegible] \- Rain commenced before 11 \+ continued with little intermission all day. I got up at 6.30 \+ spent some time writing to E.N.I \- I did not waken the boys, wishing to give Jim a chance to recuperate in bed. I had bkfs. before 9 \- Then out to Cathedral \+ had another look at the Rubens paintings. Then out \+ wandered about till the chill wind \+ threat of rain drove me in \- We did not, any of us, go out after that owing to the rain \- Later in the day a fire was made in Jim's room to dry the atmosphere for his benefit \- I had a two hours nap in the afternoon. Every all in Jim's room.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 24, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain \+ drizzle till about 4 p.m. when the weather cleared pleasantly \- It was after 10 o'clk. when we went to the Plantin Mus. \+ spent the rest of the m[illegible] there. An exceedingly interesting exhibit of a large printing \+ publishing establishment started about 1650 \+ successfully carried on for 150 yrs. We had lunch at the hotel \+ then went to the Royal Museum \- on the ground floor an amazingly large exhibit of engravings \+ other forms of reproduction after Rubens \- He must have been a tremendous worker. At 5. back to hotel \- tired \+ back hurting. Rested till 7 \- dinner \- Eve[illegible], some writing.
