## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 13, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dismal day, a damp light snow began falling before noon - When I got up at about 10 o'clk I hoped that I w°. be able to go to Mary Cook's to dinner, as invited - \+ dressed accordingly, but soon found that it would be useless to attempt it - so, I wrote a note to her \+ Jim took it when he went - I felt very wretched \+ depressed - I am convinced that I cannot stand our winter weather here -

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 14, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, judging by the sunshine that came in at the windows - last night I was feverish \+ restless \+ did not feel able to get up till noon - Then dressed \+ kept about during the rest of the day - I was able to do something in arranging negatives \+ writing titles on the backs of mounted prints of our journey to Jamaica, Colombia \+ Costa Rica - Dr. Tuttle came in after 3 o'clk - \+ advised Digitalis, Phenacetine \+ Salol - I had dinner with Jim \+ worried thro' a tedious evening.
