## Left Page

### Tues. June 2, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day \- pure + refreshing \- I was detained after bkfs. drawing checks in payment of monthly bills \- got to studio at 10,20 \+ To work with scraper + burnisher on Chapel near Lugano \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- to Ottendorfers at 3 + had my hair cut \- nearly 4 when I got to studio \- I got the Frauen Kirche into the acid once + did some stopping out \- started for home at 5\. \- walked down 72d. S. to Riverside. Then thro' the Park + out at 83d. St. The Park is very lovely now \- Jim + I had dinner at 6.30 \- my eyes troubled me \- lids smarting, so that I was able to do but little.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 3, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- Bkfs. with Jim \- then to D. Tuttle's + he removed from my left eye a small black grain of something that has been troubling me since yesterday eve\. \+ Then to Second Nat. Bank \- walked from there to studio arriving at about 11 \- To work at biting the aqua tint grounds on Frauen Kirche + Port Marie plates \- home to lunch \+ back at 3 o'clk + completed the biting. I think successfully. \- After 5 when I left studio \- had a short walk in Riverside \+ home soon after 6\. Dinner with Jim \- Eve\. I had some writing to do \- \+ I wrote some more titles in photo. Album.
