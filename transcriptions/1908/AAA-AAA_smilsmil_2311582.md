## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 1, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day, considerably cooler than yesterday my kidney discomfort was much relieved during the night \+ was comparatively slight when I got up in the nw't at 7.30\. I waked the boys \+ then went to Covent Garden with my camera hoping to get something in the flower market but found it impossible because the masses of flowers were in a building \+ the crowds of people prevented any chance of a snap even under such discouraging conditions \- back to hotel \+ had bkfs\. with the boys \- then waited about till the Atheltons got away for Dover \- Then went to Cook's \- 122 High Holborn for money \- none there \- then to the Deutsche Bank \- City \- \+ drew £25\. I had £5\. of it put into french \- Then by bus \+ tube to the Hotel at noon \- I took a dose of Castor Oil \+ that kept me busy during the afternoon \- I wrote to Bessie \- a quiet ev'n in the Palm Room.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 2, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day \- clear \+ cool \- like a day in the country \- After a restless night I got up at about 9\. It was not till about 11 that we started out \+ by tube \+ bus went to Cheyne Walk \+ Row \- there I took snap shots a houses that had been occupied by celebrities but my roll of films gave out before I had got all that I wanted \- It was near 2 when we ret'd to the Hotel \+ had lunch \- Immediately after that, having reloaded my camera we returned to Cheyne walk \+ I got two or three more snaps\. Then we went to Hyde Park Cor\. \+ loafed awhile in the Park \- then on to Marble Arch where we went from group to group where Speakers of all kinds were haranguing \- I was most interested in two groups where women suffragists were holding forth \- They were quiet \+ good natured \+ the women we heard spoke well\. Got to hotel at 6 \- Slept till 7 \- dinner at 7.30 in Palm Room Till 9 \- Then to my own Room \+ packed \- To bed soon after 11.
