## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 1, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Splendidly clear day - high wind - \+ coal - Bkf. at about 9 - After newspapers I worked over my photo prints till after 12 - Then out \+ had a three mile walk along Riverside Drive - feeling fresh \+ enjoying it greatly - Dinner at 2 - Afternoon trimming prints \+ a short walk before supper - Eve\. again with photos. Not much to record after such a day.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 2, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph came home to vote.

Another beautiful day - milder \+ quieter than yesterday. To studio at 10 - Saw Winton \+ engaged him to take charge of my studio furnace in place of Jennings - When I got to studio found Collins \+ Nicoll there waiting for me - C. came to enquire about Charl's furnace man - Nicoll had just arrd. in town \+ came to talk about his securities in the ark - It was after 11 when he left - Then I got to work over the "Belgium" illustrated nos arranging them for the binder - After 12 went to Wanamaker's \+ selected new covering for Eider Down spread - After 2 when I got home - lunch - Then, till 5 o'clk. trimming photo prints - Then out \+ walked on my favorite terrace from 86th St. to Soldiers \+ Sailors Mon\. on Riverside Drive, overlooking the river \+ reflections \+ lovely sunset \+ twilight - In at about 6 - Ralph came in soon after - Dinner - Eve\. till 9 o'clk showing to Jim \+ R\. the photos that I have selected \+ trimmed - Then newspapers \+ to bed.
