## Left Page

### Mon. Sept. 28, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain last night \- wind, some rain \+ uncomfortable mugginess throughout the day \- I was engaged all the mfo about many little matters \- It was near noon when I went to Second Nat'l. Bank \+ straightened out some matters there \- Then home to lunch at 1.30 \- After that I got my 35 Roles of films ready to give to Cullen to develop \+ after 3 o'clk I took them to him \- Then went to Charl's office \- Saw him there for a few minutes \- Then home \- wrote part of a letter to Mary \- Dinner alone \- Jim dined at Mary Cook's \- Eve "f" I finished \+ posted letter to Mary \- \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Tues. Sept. 29, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day \- cloudless, cool, inspiring \- Bkf't at 8 \- After that till 11 o'clk. I was busy clearing up things, writing to Scott Brown, drawing checks, &c \- Then I went to Studio \- No one visible but "Gus" \- work on new heating plant behind, as I expected, but the workman assured me that every thing w'd. be completed in a few days \- Maynard not in \- nor was bro. George \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- Putting things away till near 4 o'clk \- then out with Jim \+ walked up Riverside Drive to 108th St. Then home at 6 \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g Reading \+ writing \- Jim out to 7" Reg. Co. meeting.
