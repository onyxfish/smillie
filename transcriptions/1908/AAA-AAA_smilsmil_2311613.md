## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 4, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day - a mild, dreamy fall day - I had bkfp - after 9 - then newspapers \+ arranging photo negative till about noon - then Jim \+ I went out for a walk - we strolled up Riverside Drive but soon found that it was too warm for exercise - even slow walking - so we sat down on a bench \+ loafed for some time - then home to dinner at 2 - afternoon divided between photos, sleep \+ another short walk - About 7 I went to Charls' by appointment \+ soon after 8 we went to the Collingwood, W. 35 street \+ spent the evef with Josephine (bro. Will's widow.)

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 5, 1908

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very pleasant day - Bkfp - at 8 - then till 11, arranging \+ cataloguing negatives of last summer - then to studio - there till after 1 - home to lunch at 2 - then a rest \+ resumed work on negatives - about 5 - out for a walk - very mild - beautiful twilight - Dinner at 6.30 - Evef some writing \+ more work cataloguing.
