## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 21, 1870

Delightful day \- fresh, breezy \+
cool \- Worked on Uncle W's
drawing (2d day) at 11 a.m.
went to Steamer Ocean Queen
to see Ferguson off for South
America \- at 12.30 went to
Snedicor's to see why a parcel
for F. had not been left at St.
\- boy brought parcel back \- I got
"high" because I insisted that the
boy had not done all that he ought
to have done \- Home to lunch
at 1.30 \- By 6 o'clk. P.M. only
finished outline of building.
Tea at 6.30 \+ at 7 went to Central
Park \+ enjoyed the delightful
air \+ every there walking
about \- Got home at 8.30 \-
Read a little \- wrote a
little \+ to bed about 10 ½

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 22, 1870

Another delightful day. My
at 9.15 went to Studio \- then to N.a.
of D \- then rode down to the Tweedys
\+ made engagement to go to Central
Park with them this evef. \- Ret'd
home about 10.15 \+ to work on
Uncle Willie's drawing (3d day)
washing in effect in "India" cut \-
about 5 P.M. got it finished \-
Early tea \- Mary in great agi\-
tation because Dr. who went to
Carmel yesterday did not return \-
He got in at 6.35 P.M. \- went to
Tweedy's at 6.40 \- at 6.45 started
out with Belle \+ Mary \- Geo. joined
us in car at cor. of 41st S. \- Wandered
in the Park \- through the Ramble
\+ about until near 10 o'clk \- Rode
down to Waverly R. \+ home near
11 o'clk \- got to bed at 11.30
