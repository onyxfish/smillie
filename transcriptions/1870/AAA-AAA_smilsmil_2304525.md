## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 19, 1870

Up before 6 after a tolerable night — sky cloudless — I made my first attempt at cooking Hon- -ing for Father's bkfst. not a very brilliant success — after bkfst — Mr. Redfield. two daughters and young Cheesman went in to the Disable Lakes. — I spent the mng about the house, feeling very listless & weak — Father, much to my discomfort seems ditto — ditto. Lunch at about noon — A good part of the p.m. was spent with Father & girl down at the River, near the house, bathing feet, etc — The lake party came in about 6.30, delighted. Immediately had dinner. Bet. 7 & 8 P.M. Father girl & I walked part way over to S Ruth Beede's, then back — got to bed at 10 — nervous during the night about Father.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 20, 1870

Up at 6 & this time, the Horning a pronounced success. After bkfst, the Redfield Party got off for Scott's — and a good part of the Remaining morning I sat dozing at the front door, feeling ever more listless & discouraged than yesterday — Lunch, or dinner at 2.30. The day close and thunderous — breezy too at times. heavy showers over the valley & spitting Rain promises at times; from 6 to 8, heavy Rain & thunder storms — very Refreshing. Wrote to Belle & Meedy. Dibble brought up my Provision box, $3.25 charges. Everf. Father & Egglesfield, had a long talk concerning 2d. advent matters. Got to bed about 10 o'clk.
