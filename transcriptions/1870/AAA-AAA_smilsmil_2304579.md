## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 8 1870

Superb day \- after [illegible] went with Ge\_ and voted \- then to Studio at 9.30 \+ worked on small studies \- after 12. Art critic for N.Y. Times called \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \- Met Corinne Coleman Smith at the door \- called on me \- before she left, Miss Torrey called \- Got my work done about 4.30 \- washed brushes \+ at 5.30, went to Brook Bros\_ about my over coat \- Then to Snedecors \- Home to tea at [illegible] Evening worked on Jcyas Arms drawing until 11 \- To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 9 1870

Foggy, overcast mrg \- brilliant, warm mid-day \- heavy rain from 4 to 7 \- windy, cold \+ clear at 10 P.M \- Got to Studio just before 9 \+ for a couple of hours, clearing up \- then to work on outlineing Building\- B.a.R.N.Co's drawing \- (2½ days) \- Home to lunch at 1.15 \- back at 2.30 and worked as long as I could see, which was until near 4 o'clk \- Lewis B\- Reed called \- then Jos\. Jefferson with a couple of his water color dashes, for me to give him hints \- Home to tea at 6.30 \- evening worked until 10 o'clk on Jcyas arms and to bed at 10.30
