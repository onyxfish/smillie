## Left Page

### June, Wednesday, 29, 1870.

Very hot mng — later in the day, pleasant breeze — got to work on my dag. about 6.30 am. This is the fourth day in preparing for my Locomotive drawing. Got outline finished at noon — Mr. Main came in then — Dinner at 1 — then right down town & had impression of dag. outline on drawing paper & sketched it on board — at 3.45 at boat foot of Cedar St. got not there — home — got not there — Hudson R.R.R. — got on 5 o'clk train — went to Irvington — invitation of the Colmans — Dinner at 6 — Clara Dunham came up in the eveng. which was very cool & spent on the porch — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Thursday, 30, 1870.

Another very hot day, but with a charming breeze from South — the mng spent lounging on piazza, in Colman's studio & looking over his California Sketches — lunch about noon — after lunch, Mr. Colman played some Beethoven Schumann music while I enjoyed the soft breeze and dreamed dreams concerned past & future — About 4 o'clk saunted away up on the hill — back to dinner at 5.30 — About 6.30 Mrs. Colman — Mr. Dunham — gk. & I went out to ride — Had a delightful call on Mr. & Mrs. Williams and a ramble over his new house — back near 8 — soon after Mr. C. Clara D. Mr. D. gk. & I went over to the Tiffany's — visited on their piazza until near 10 — the back & for another ½ hour on our piazza and at 11 o'clk — to bed —
