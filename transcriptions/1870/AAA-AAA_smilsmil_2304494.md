## Left Page

### May Saturday, 14 1870

Delightful day. fresh breeze. Clouds \+ warm sunshine. Up early to hear the birds. B'kfast at 6.45. Between 8 \+ 9 a.m. open wagon, two horses, Belle, Jennie \+ Geo. on back seat, Mary \+ I on front — Eagle Rock — Llewllyn Park \+ S. orange to A.B. Durand's where we had a delightful call and over his new house — home by another Road at 12.45. Lunch P.M. worked on Goldengate drawing until dinner at 5.30. Even'g, full moon \+ cloudless. Bet. 7 \+ 8 o'clk. the girls Geo. \+ I. walked to Mrs. Geo. Z. Haws. about a mile. Surprised them, but had a pleasant call and a wander home by different Road about 9.45. Sitting on piazza until about 10.30 then to bed very sleepy.

## Right Page

### May Sunday, 15 1870

Up early again \+ sitting about until B'kfst. at 8.30. Soon after to Belle, Jenny, Mr. Chas. B. \+ I — John \+ Farted for Mr. Bacon's Cong'l Ch. S. orange. By a Roundabout way, got there at 11.20 \+ heard a sermon in commemoration of the Landing of the Pilgrim Fathers. Psalms 44th 1st v. Out at 12.25 and by another Roundabout way got home about 2 o'clk. Very late lunch \+ dinner between 4 \+ 5 o'clk. Even'g Mr. \+ Mrs. B. Geo. \+ Mary I. went to Ch. Belle, Jenny \+ I at home. Bright moonlight. air fragrant and delightful. on piazza until to bed about 11 o'clk.
