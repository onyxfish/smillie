## Left Page

### October Friday, 21 1870

mixed, sunny + thick — and so [illegible]
my head — At 9 o'clk, went down town
\+ saw Will about a little drawing on
wood that the A.B.N. Co. wish me to do.
Then up to Sauthier's. Schaus'. [illegible]
Kelly's + Brooks Bros where I ordered
a suit of clothes + an overcoat. Then
at 1.45 home to lunch — Afternoon
so used up with my headache
that I went to bed + stayed there
until Tea time — eveg. looking
over my English papers + trying
to make sketch for wood cut —
To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 22 1870

Delightful day — After bkfst. went to
Kahns + had my hair cut + head
shampooed — then to Brooks Bros. to
see about my overcoat — then back
to studio + got some brushes, pumice
etc, to work at home this afternoon.
Miss Conlies had been at studio, to
arrange to call this P.M. — with Miss
Bright, a sister as I understood, of
Mr. Bright, M.P. of England. So Black-
man told me. — Home about 12. P.M.
worked on little drawing on wood
for the A.B.N. Co. finishing it about
5 o'clk — then walked down to studio —
met Fairman + short call in his Room —
eveg. went up to Burggraff's and
ordered pair of boots — The rest
of the eveg until 12.30 pasting
up mounting boards to mount some
of my summer studies —
