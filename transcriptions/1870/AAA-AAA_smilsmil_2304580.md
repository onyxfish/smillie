## Left Page

### November Thursday, 10 1870.

Splendid cold, breezy day — Got
to Studio at 9.15 + to work on drawing
for B.a.B.N.C. — at 12 o'clk. ment to
George's Room — then to Rensett —
found him much engaged —
then to Wm Hart's — there until
12.45 — then home to lunch —
Back at 2 o'clk — Finished out-
line of drawing about 4 o'clk —
near 5 went to Coppee's — The
plaster-man about some cast
of horses legs — then to Brooks
Bros. then to the Tweedys &
there until near 6 — Home to
tea at 6.15 — Went ment to
Sunna Ireland's + had a
very pleasant chat with her
about her Recent trips to Europe —
She has been home about 3 weeks —
At 10.45 stopped at Apollo Rooms
Baptist fair — Matt + Charl there —
Home at 11.15 + to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### November Friday, 11 1870.

Superb day — Got to Studio at 8.45
Surprised by an early visit from
Messrs. Geo. Whitney + Enoch Lewis of
Phila. To work on drawing for
B.a.B.N.Co — (4th day) washing in
the effect — Home to lunch
at 1.15 — Walked down to A. of
Music — Phil. (1st) Rehearsal at
2.30 o'clk — Secured seat — don't
like the position — Programme
excellent — out at 4 o'clk —
Up to Studio with Mary, Belle,
Florence TMV? Tom Tweedy and
are at 5 — walked over to 14th &
+ 2d ave, with Belle — then to
Waverly R — with Mary + Florence —
then to Brooks Bros. — then home
to tea at 6.30 — Went to Burgraff
+ Reading Paper — Will + Jac
called — To bed at 10.45
