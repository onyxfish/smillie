## Left Page

### February Monday, 21 1870

Very cold blustering day — got to Studio at 9.20. Kindled fire & to work on "Twilight" (14th day) painting over middle distance trees, left hand — Very anxious lest I lose some qualities of the first painting — Met Mr. H. again in the hall of our building — answered his greeting as on Sat. & when I got home to lunch, found that he had left Lizzie's wedding cards for the family at our house — Back at 2.30 & painted until 5 o'clk — Two ladies called — don't know who they were — Home to tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to Mary Butler's Maggie Green there — and 20 got to Mr. Norton's 14th St — Mary, Mr. Dr. Matt, Kate & Charl there — left at 11 o'Clk — Home at 11.20 — got to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 22 1870

Very cold windy day — got to Studio at 9.15 — Prepared a piece of tin to paint a little sketch for Laura Hearn's tin wedding — Then at "Twilight" (15th day) working over the middle distance, Right hand — Had a number of callers — J. J. Griffen, Ned Dunning, Mr. & Mrs. Heldman, Snedicor & late in P.M, Dr. Lewis, Mr. Jos. Clay & his son. Home to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.20 Painted until 5 — Home to tea at 6.30 — At 8.30 got to the Tweedy's with Jno. Charl, Matt & Kate — a surprise birthday party to Belle Tweedy — Jennie Tweedy & other members of the family there — a very pleasant [illegible] until 11.15 — then for home and to bed at 12.
