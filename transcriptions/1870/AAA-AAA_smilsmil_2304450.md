## Left Page

### February Sunday, 13 1870.

Bright, cold & windy — morning
went to Mad. ave. Baptist Ch.
Heard Mr. Elder preach from
Paul's saying "By the grace
of God I am what I am."
He preached the absolute sov-
ereignty of God & the doctrine of
election at the same time
declaring that man is a free
agent & accountable. — All
of these doctrines & speculations
are idle to me. — I can get along
without them. — At 12.30. with Geo.
went up to Uncle A's. — After
dinner, a nap. Home to tea at 6
Eve'g, again went to hear Elder.
He preached from the story of
Nathan the seer. — Home at
9.30 and to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### February Monday, 14 1870.

Raw threatening N.E. — got to Pate's
No. 60 Fulton St. by 9.15 but is was
some time before he was ready to
begin proving my plate "Upper
Ausable Lake" — Experimented
with Van Dyke Brown — settling
upon a mixture of it with fine
Frankfort Black. — At 11 went to
Sackett's. Thing Smith's. A.B.M. Co. —
(got $80.00 for my Pacific R.R. drawings)
then back to Pate's — Stretched 2 Pfs.,
took them with me — bought cash box
for Water Color Soc'y — went to Parker's
& lunched, then to Studio, where I
arr. at 2.15 P.M. — Built a fire —
Rain at about 3 o'clk — very dark —
Spent what was left of P.M. in
touching up one of the stretched pfs. —
Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g, steady
Rain — amused myself at home
Reading & writing — to bed at 10.45
