## Left Page

### April Sunday, 10 1870.

Another charming m[or]g, but I
got up about 9.30 with a head-
ache which so far got the
better of me that about 1 o'clk
I Rolled in to my bed and
lay there until 8.30 P.M.
when I got up — eat my
supper & lolled in my
big chair until about
1 o'clk A.M. — when I
went to bed to Roll
about an hour or more
before I got to sleep.

## Right Page

### April Monday, 11 1870.

A horrible storm of N.E. wind
& driving Rain — got to Studio
at 9 until 11 fighting to get
a fire — The draught seemed
to be first-rate but it was
all down the chimney — Was
very much shocked to hear
of the death of Kuntze the
Sculptor, yesterday. Home
to lunch abt. 1.30 — back at
2.15 — Very nervous from smoke
and coal gas. Pottered away
at my water color box. Re-
grinding some of my colors &
experimenting by mixing
white wax & Dextrine gum —
also "touching up" a p[iece] [illegible] "Upper
Ausable". Home to tea soon
after 7 — Evening Reading news-
paper & a little writing —
To bed about 10.30
