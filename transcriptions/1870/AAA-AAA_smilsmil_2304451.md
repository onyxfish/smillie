## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 15, 1870

overcast [illegible], Rain commenced
soon after 1 & continued heavy,
with wind until late at night. A
weak attempt at snow about 11 P.M.
Got to studio at 9.15 & after a little
time again touching up Pf. of Patie's
plate, got to work on 14×24" — Man
"Keepsie" (4th day) — Repainted part
that I worked over on Saturday —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.15 — Very dark — finished painting
foreground & before 4 P.M. gave it
up as I could not see — amused
myself sweeping & washing up —
Home to tea at 6.30 — Evening
at 8.30 at the Smedys there
until near 11 — then home and
to bed about midnight.

## Right Page

### February Wednesay, 16, 1870

Clear, cloudless and mild —
Got to Studio at 9.5 and to
work on 14×24 — (5th day) painting
foreground trees — R.h. corner —
Worked until 1.30 — home to
lunch — back at 2.45 — after
stopping at Burling's and at
Bogardus's — Found Rbt. Dodge
waiting for me — before he went
att Fessenden came in and
had a very pleasant call of
half an hour or more — Worked
as long as I could see — getting
all "in" — Met Miss Thomas in Ogilv[illegible]
Room — she & a friend gave me a
short call — Home to tea at 6.45
Rec'd a note from Pliny Smith. I
made out a Promissory note for
him for $37 & 62 At 8.30 got to
Mary Butler's — not in — went to
Leila Marten's — 155 W. 21st Misses
Blackwell & Miss Sarah Marten
there — pleasant call — left at 10.30
Home & to bed at 11.30
