## Left Page

### October Thursday, 27 1870

Got to studio at 9 o'clk — day dull \+ overcast — began to Rain about noon — kept it up at intervals — night stormy — During the m'g, worked on Uncle W's architectural drawing (2 days) Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30 — Telly brought stretchers for my studies and I spent the p.m. stretching them — Home at 6 o'clk — Then Tea — Charl went to Junic's school for her \+ she spent the evening with us until 9 o'clk. I Read, looked over some acs[illegible], wrote a note or two \+ got to bed at 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### October Friday, 28 1870

Superb day, somewhat overcast late in the afternoon — Got to the studio at 9 o'clk — \+ to work again on drawing (2½ days) Uncle Millie came in about 10 \+ stayed until after 11 — I had other interruptions so that my m'g work did not amount to much — as usual — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 p.m. — made alterations on proof of engine — an old die that I eng'd for the A.B.N. Co. some years ago — Father is now going to modernize the engine \+ add material according to my alterations — Home at 6 — Tea soon after — Father brought me up $60.00 from the A.B.N. Co. — My new suit came home — a failure — disgust — well, worked on die alterations, etc., etc., and got to bed at 10.30
