## Left Page

### November Friday, 18 1870.

Dull — chilly, spitting Rain, commencing to fall about 1 o'clk.
Got to studio about 9 — Spent an hour stretching three pieces
of paper on which to make drawings — then to work out —
lining an dag — until 1 o'clk.
when I went home to lunch.
At 2.30 — at Phil. Rehearsal —
again enjoyed it very much —
After — walked home with Belle
& Mary Tweedy — went in & fed
on grapes — back to studio, after
stopping to see Mr. Chas. Tiffany,
at 6 o'clk — called on Burling
& his two sisters in his studio —
Home to tea at 6.45 — Even —
wrote a letter to Louis Tiffany.
To bed at 10.30 •

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 19 1870.

Nipping cold day, with snow flurries.
Got to studio at 9 o'clk. & to work
outlining dag of building (2 days)
worked until 1 o'clk — then home
to lunch — back at 2 — got my
outline finished & scraped, all
ready to transfer — Home to
tea at 6 o'clk — even. until
10 o'clk. working on drawing
"Kansas Arms" for A.B.N. Co —
To bed at 10.30 —
