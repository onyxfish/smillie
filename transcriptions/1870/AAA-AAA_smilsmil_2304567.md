## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 12, 1870

*The Lewis House, Binghamton*

Waked at 4.30 at Whitehall — Dark & dismal
& rain — glad that we did not wish to come
down the Lake by daylight — Train started
about 6 A.M. got something to eat at Saratoga.
Got next on to N.Y. by way of Troy & H.R. R.R. &
left him at Junction & went on to Albany
getting there at 9.50. Went to the Delevan House
for a few minutes then sauntered out — into
the Gallery of Annsley & Vint, then up State St.
to the Geological that. His. coll — Then to the
courts & back to Watkins' House Restaurant —
bread & pot of Tea — then down to the River &
along the docks — getting to A. & Sus. R.R.
depot som after 1 o'clk. tired & foot-sore —
Bought back papers & read until train
started at 2.15 — nearly 6 clk — nearly mild
headache — arranged myself so
that I was more comfortable & got some
sleep — P.M. cleared off charmingly. Got to
Binghamton at 8.45. Rushed around to get off
& get Bend by Erie train just starting when I
found after my Trunk was on the train — & I had
told my niece that she Del. H. S. trains
started from here — then I gave up and
came to this house — to bed som after 11.
