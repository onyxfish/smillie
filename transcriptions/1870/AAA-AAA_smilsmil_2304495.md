## Left Page

### May, Monday 16, 1870

Another charming day, but very warm — About 9 o'clk.
Belle, Mary, Jenny, Gus + I with horses + wagon started for Little
Falls. Drove all over + got to the falls soon after 11 — then
home by new Road at 12.30
after a hot, but delightful ride.
Lunch — and afternoon spent
working on Occidental drawing,
got it finished about 6 + soon
after, dinner. We'f me all
went to Mr. Sullivan's + found
them very pleasant + the house
quite magnificent — Lemon-
ade with a stick anything but
comfortable — walk home by
moonlight about 11 o'clk and
did not get to bed until
after midnight — Strong wind
blowing + threatening a change.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 17, 1870

Weather changed — cold with
some Rain — Took 8.20 train + got
to ABN. Co at 9.45 — Submitted drawing
but left in midst of discussions to
catch the 11 o'clk train by which
I reached Montclair at 12.5 — the
girls + Gus met me + we walked
about until near 1 — then home
to lunch. Afternoon I made
a water color sketch for Stepheu
Massett on a music title — a
little before 6. Belle, Mr. B. + I
drove recklessly to the depot for
Mr. B — Gus + Miss Mary out walking
for their health — Dinner soon after
6 — We'f Miss Chittenden + their bro.
called — stayed until 10.30; about
10.30 all went out + had a little
breezing before going to bed — the
clouds + moonlight, beautiful —
