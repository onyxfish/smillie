## Left Page

### March Saturday, 5 1870.

Snowing in the mrf, clear in the afternoon — got up to skcps. about 9.30 p.m. — with a miserable headache, which took me to bed before noon where I stayed until 4.30 when I got up \+ had some tea. At 7 o'clk, or soon after I was washed, dressed and fuling quite shaky — but I went to Phil. Concert which after all I enjoyed very much. After the Concert. Gel. and I went home with Belle, Mary \+ Jenny I meeday — there until 11 o'clk — walked home — got to bed about 1.30 am.

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 6 1870.

Dull day threatening snow which came late in the afternoon — Mrf. went \+ heard Dr. Hastings preach a very earnest anti-Hyper. Calvinistic sermon from Hosea 5th ch 4th v. He was interested and interesting \+ I liked very much his straight forward anti-doctrinal views. He had no pet doctrines he said — God was sovereign — and he was accountable \+ free — to make these statements coincide did not disturb him — After Service, with Charle went to Uncle A's to dinner — home at 6.20 — every feeling effects of cold, did not go out — spent the evef. chatting with Dr. Woods until 9 — Got to bed about 11.
