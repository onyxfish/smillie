## Left Page

### December Friday, 30 1870

Dreary w[eather] \+ head feeling very achy \- Did not get to Studio until 10.15 \- Tried to work on drawing of horse but found myself unfit. M. J. Heade made a dancing visit \- in \+ out \- seeing nothing \- saying nothing \- a senseless formality \- J. C. Nicoll also called \- Got home a little before 2 o'clk So used up with my headache that I went to bed \- there until 8 o'clk \- then down stairs, but my aches did not leave me until about 3. A. M \- Did not go to bed at all \- but spent the night on the sofa \- About 5 in. of Snow fell during the night.

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 31 1870

W[eather] overcast \- afternoon bright \- rather mild \+ everything afloat \- got to Studio at 9.30 \- feeling as usual after a headache \- Spent the m[ornin]g regrinding colors in my water color box \- Home to lunch about 2 o'clk \- and after, Rode down to Havris's \+ bought pair of Kid gloves, then to [illegible]'s \- bought mittets \- then to Trosden's \- then to Studio just in time to go in to Perspective class. Out at 11.10 to find the names of "the Misses Sweedy" on my door \- then went to Dunlap's \+ got a "high hat" \- home \+ had a time about it \- Eve[nin]g Reading Grant White's "Words \+ their uses" \- To bed at 11 \-
