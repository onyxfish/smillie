## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 2 1870

Foggy, disagreeable wtf, clearing before noon to an Indian Summer
sort of day — Wtg went to Bro — bro's great tribulation about her
clothes — Got to Studio at 10 o'clk
just in time to give considerable
of my time to Mr. Aikeu of Chicago —
got to work fixing up my studio
& with various interruptions, kept
at it all day — J. W. Bell of Conowingo called called about him —
I got a great load of old sketch-
ers from Brevort — Took my lunch
at 3 o'clk — Gave up work at 4.30
— very fired to spread myself in
lounge from 5.30 to 6 — Then home
to Tea — Welf went to Bur-
ggraff's — but not done —
Head & eyes troubling me — cold
in the head — Read Central
Park Report and to bed
soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 3 1870

Miserable wtf, winding up
with quite a heavy thunder-
storm about 10 o'clk — After that
it cleared into a fine day —
but I suffered very much
with my head — a sort of
catarrhal trouble — not
able to get to the studio —
Spent most of the mtg on
the sofa — P.M. — at
3 o'clk went to studio. fixed
fine & pottered with studies.
Home to Tea about 6 — Well
slowly improving, but unable
to do anything — Sat about
until between 10 & 11 & then
to bed —
