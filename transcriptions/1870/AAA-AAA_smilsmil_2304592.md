## Left Page

### December Saturday, 10 1870

Cloudless \+ breezy \- Got to studio abq \+ at work with graver and burnisher on die (15 days) until 1 o'clk \- then home to lunch \- back at 2 \- when Blackman came in with Miss Blodgett \+ Miss Fessenden \- After they left, Miss Porter came in \+ when she left, at 3 o'clk \- I went into Brevoort to take my first lesson with his class in Perspective \- but was soon called out to see Fan Mulford \- Sat down to my work while she chatted until nearly 4.30 when I walked to 39th St. \+ 8th ave. with her \- then home. Ev'g, went with the girls to Apollo Hall \- Rivard Concert \- Sat back of Fanny Kimball's party \- Home at 11 \- and to bed at 12 \-

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 11 1870

Disagreeable cold \+ cloudy \- In the m'g went to Dr. Hastings \- Heard him give his text \- St. John 1st ch. 23d v. "I am a voice crying in the Wilderness" ate then went sound asleep \+ awoke at the close \- my rest last night was much disturbed. At noon went to A.W.'s to dine Slept on the library sofa from 3 o'clk until 4.15 \- Home at 5.45 \- Ev'g listened to a Chalmers discourse read by Father \- then Read a couple of lectures by Prof Agassiz and to bed about 11 \-
