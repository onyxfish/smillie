## Left Page

### March Thursday, 31 1870

Fine day, altho' the wind blows threaten-
ingly from the N.E. About 8.45 got
to Gauthier's to see about Father's
passe-partout — at 9.15 got to Geo's
studio, to see his picture & at 9.45
at my Room — Spent my making
final preparations to get my pictures
off to academy besides receiving some
calls — lunched in studio about
2 o'clk — During the rest of P.M.
had a pretty steady run of visitors
among them — most of Mr. Beer's Lambs
miss Sterling, the Tweedys, Harveys
Rogers — Van Doren & friend — Day —
Corlies — Nettie Jessup — Pike —
Mr. Blatchford of Chicago — Will
& Woods Kimball — &c — George
came over about 6 & we walked
home together — Eve'g copying
letter to Emma Ireland —
To bed about 11

## Right Page

### April Friday, 1 1870

Fine day, wind still east, dirty & windy
went to N.A.D. to see that things
were all right & at 9.15 got to studio.
not in humor to do much — Geo. W.
Nichols — his father-in-law, Longworth
& son made a short call — Also
Belle Tweedy & Mrs. Julia Benedict,
Dr. Mulford called & at 12.45 me
started out meeting Dr. Mrs. Thompson
saw on the corner — we walked
up together — Home to lunch at 1 —
with Matt & Kate to Phil. Rehearsal
at 1.45 — great crush — Parepa Rosa —
after Rehearsal — Matt Kate, Belle
& Mary Tweedy with Geo — up in my
Room — walked home with the Tweedys
back to studio & home to tea at 7 —
Eve'g at 8.30 went to Emma D's
quite a number of callers — Home
at 11 — took a leg bath and to
bed about midnight —
