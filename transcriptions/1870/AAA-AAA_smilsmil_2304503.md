## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 1 1870.

Muggy day, with clear sky and then Rain \- Got to studio at 8.40 \- Waited for Will who came along at about 10 when we agreed about drawing of R.R. cars for A.B.N.C. \- I pattered away the mng doing more gaping than work \- Home to lunch at 1.30 Back in the Rain at 3 \- P.M. little more successful with my work then in the mng. \+Johnston called late in the P.M. Home to tea at 7.15. Aunt Jessie's piano arrd. to-day. Eveng Reading "Lothair" \+ writing a note to Wm C Bryant To bed about 11 \-

## Right Page

### June Thursday, 2 1870.

Another muggy, enervating day \- Got to studio about 9 o'clk and at work outlining drawing Room car" design for A.B.N.G. worked until shortly after 1 \- then home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 \- P.M. was hing in effect \- soon after 5 o'clk, Belle \+ Mary Tweedy called. walked down to Waverley Pl. with them at 6.30 \- Home to tea at 7 \- at 8.30 went to Madison Ave. Baptist Ch \- a festival \- about left \- 3d ave. car to Pink, 63d St. Saw Weston, finish his fifty mile walk in less than ten hours at a little before 10 o'clk Great enthusiasm. \- He made a very good speech \+ showed fatigue wonderfully little \- was much interested \- I walked home through a sort of nizzle \- getting in abt 1 o'clk \- to bed at 11.30
