## Left Page

### February Saturday, 19 1870.

overcast m[illegible], but soon cleared off bright & breezes — Got to Studio at
9.30, after stopping at Bogardus's to
look at a frame for Ring Smith —
Painted on "Inlight" — (13th day) still
working over the central group of
trees & forms near it — Home to
lunch at 1.45 — meeting W. Hin-
shelwood on the way & putting into
practice my new Facties. Instead of
passing him without noticing his
usual greeting — when he addressed
me "How are you Jimmy?" I answered
"How are you Bobby?" and he passed
on — Back to Studio at 2.30 — worked
until dusk — then painted over cast
of horse that Rogers gave me —
Home to Tea at 7 o'clk — Mr. Cox
with us to-night — at 9 went to
Tweedey's where Gil. was and
there until 11 — walked home
& got to bed soon after 12 —
At noon deposited $113 — Water
Color Money, in 3d Ave. Savings Bk.

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 20 1870.

Up at 9 — Mrs went to Dr
Hastings — He preached an
excellent sermon from Romans
12th Ch. 1st v. Paul's beseeching
that the body be given a living
sacrifice — At 2.30 started
for Uncle A's — Met Miss Portent
& lady friends in the car. She
went to dinner with them — I
went to Uncle A's — P.M. very windy
with threats of change — about
5 o'clk got started with Minni, it
commenced Raining — Returned
with her. Met Miss P. in the Rain
no umbrella — at 5.30 I started
again for home alone — got very
wet — bathed lower extremities
in cold water — better — brisk
toweling — good — Evg at home
Mr. Cox very Talkative — got
to bed at near midnight.
