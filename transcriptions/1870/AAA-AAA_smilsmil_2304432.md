## Left Page

### January, Saturday 8, 1870

Snow in the m'g, but by noon it was bright & clear, windy
and slushy — got to Studio at 9.30 & to work on drawing (10° day)
finishing over foreground Rocks and trees & generally — I am
fully aware that my drawing is a failure, judged by what
I had hoped to do. It is unequal in execution, false
in color & tone and generally heavy & monotonous — the thine
is good, parts of sky, tolerable & the drawing of objects & detail
pretty fair. Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.15 — worked all p.m.
on drawing — M'g took French lesson
Youney Haven called — also Rob't
Dodge — Ed. Bierstadt & Calimau —
Home to tea at 5.30 — Father went
with us to Phil. Concert — to hear
Ole Bull, whom I think something
of a mountebank — I enjoyed
the Raff Symphony, Especially
the 3d movement — Home at 11 o'clk
and Right to Bed.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 9, 1870

Clear, cold & windy — M'g
went to D'. Hastings & heard
him preach from Luke XV &
7. "Joy in Heaven over one
sinner that Repenteth — After
service, home, & abt 2.45 with
Charl. started for Uncle C's
getting there in the midst
of soup. a nap after dinner
& at about 5 o'clk — C & I started
for home, walking down the Bloom-
ingdale R'd to 100th St. thence to the
Park & through the Park to 59th
St & 6th Ave. when we Rode home —
the Sunset, Twilight & moonlight
very delightful — Evening
I did not go out but enjoyed
the fireside — Father and
the Rest of the folks in
extacies over their new
minister M'. Elder —
Got to bed at near 11.
