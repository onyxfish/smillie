## Left Page

### April, Saturday, 16, 1870.

Cloudy m'f — clear p.m. windy
& cold. Got to studio at 9 &
had to kindle a fire, 'twas
so chilly — then to work, painting
on Conway & artist's brook (2nd day)
worked until 1.15, then home
to lunch & back at 2.30 —
worked until near 5. when
I got my canvas covered — It
is scattered & weak, but I am
sanguine of making something
above my average. At 5, went
to N.a. of D — then to Y.M.C.a.
& paid dues, went to gymnasium
then to studio — washed up, &
home to tea about 7 o'clk —
Even looking over catalogue
of N.a. of D. & a little writing
got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Sunday, 17, 1870.

Miserable day — cloudy m'f
winding up with rainy p.m. &
night — M'f went to D. Thos.
Hastings & heard him preach
one of a series on life & times
of Abraham — from Hebrews
II — using the story of the
command to sacrifice Isaac
for an Easter sermon —

Afternoon, went to bed
& got up & lost some tea
soon after 8 o'clk — Even
Reading until near 1
o'clk a.m. when I went to
bed —
