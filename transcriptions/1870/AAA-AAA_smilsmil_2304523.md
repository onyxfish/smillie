## Left Page

### July, Friday, 15, 1870.

Warm day. Up early and began packing before bkfst. Got all finished up, my own trunk \+ Father's valise by about 11 o'clk. Then being tired, I Rested. Lunch about 1 o'clk. Matt arr'd. from Saratoga bet. 2 \+ 3 o'clk. She was full of her adventures \+ lost no time in giving us characteristic descriptions. About 4 took a bath \+ dressed. Tea at 4.45 — at 5 started for the "Drew" foot of Canal S— there at 5.35 — Charl. m\[illegible\]us. (Father \+ me) \+ at 6 o'clk. we were off at last. Met Dr. Hagy on board. Passing Princeton, Red. Roman Candle Salutes from the Tiffany's — Pilot Rve'd with 3 whistles — Turned in at 10.30 in State Room N. \+ slept tolerably well.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday, 16, 1870.

Up at 4.30. Glorious Sunrise — left the boat about 5 \+ got to the "Delevan" about 5.30. Had a first-rate breakfast \+ at 5.45 went over to the train. Found my trunk lock smashed again. Train started at 7.10 — very long. 16 pass't \+ 3 baggage cars at Junction. Ride hot \+ dusty — got to the place on time \+ started on Canada at 11 o'clk. Dinner at 12.30. Seats forward — breezy \+ pleasant — altho' the day was very hot, began to cloud up bet. 2 \+ 3 — at 3.30 Reached West port. At 3.45 started for \+ at 5.45 Reached E-town. a pleasant ride without the sun. Got at Mansion House, lots of company, flattering reception. Did not get to bed until 11 o'clks.
