## Left Page

### July, Sunday 17, 1870

Overcast \+ threatening showers. \- Very hot \+ strong wind from S \- very enervating \- Did nothing during the mng but sit about \- lunch at 1 \- again, sitting about \- at 2, went out with Father \+ Gil \+ sauntered along the "Boquet", back at 4. Then dinner \- \+ yet again, sitting about, mainly with Misses Johnston \+ Ma ma \- under the shadow of "Tumble down Hall" \- Miss Walters sent her card, so at 8.30 I called on her at the "Valley House". Saw also Mr. \+ Mrs. H\+ Marks. back at 9.15 \- Chocolate \+ Claret \- Mr. Howe \+ Harmen, Misses Johnston, Paul. Gil. \+ I \- Singing in parl To bed about midnight \-

## Right Page

### July, Monday 18, 1870

*at Widow Reed's.*

Clouds threatening again but little chance of Rain \- after bfst wrote a few more to Mary \- Father \+ Gil \- got Ready \+ at 8.45 after farewells, we started for Widow Reedie's, where after a very pleasant ride we arr'd at 12.30. Two wagon loads \- the Redfields, Miss Whitney, Mr. Leys, Mr. Cheeseman &on had arr'd just before us \- Dinner, codfish, pork \+ fried eggs at 2 \- Father quite tired \- all of us lying about until near 4 o'clk. Then with some of the party I went back on the hill, saw Giant then through the woods to the Ravine \- too much for Mr. Cheeseman \+ Miss \- Leys \- back at 5.15 \- Some of the party ride to E. town. Tea between bt 7 \- out front of house enjoying every effects until driven in by black flies, punkies \+ mosquetoes \- Got to bed at 9.30 \-
