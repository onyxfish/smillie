## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 1 1870

Beautiful on[?], variable day —
At 8.30 went to studio & from
there walked down to Fred. Durand's
where I had an engagement at 9.15
Met Belle & Mary Tweedy — Had a
hard time of it in Fred's chair until
11 oclk — then went to Schaus' and
8 nedicors & to Laura Hearn's to
see about my little Tin picture
which I was told could not be found
— but it was — back to Studio at
11.45 — Miss Sterling called it [illegible] —
Home to lunch at 1.30 back at
2.15 & painted on "Twilight" until
5 oclk — (19th day). Home at 6.30
Well — at 8.30 Charl and I
called on Miss Annie Tiffany &
Miss Goddard — left at 9.40
Stopped in at Dickels Riding
School for a few minutes, then
home and to bed at 11

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 2 1870

Got to Studio at 9.20 — To work at
once on Rock foreground in "Twilight"
(20th day) and kept at it until
1 oclk when I went home to
lunch at 2 oclk went again
to Fred Durand's, when he finished
me off. Left [illegible] my Patu plate
with Saunier to passe-partout —
called at 8 nedicors & back to
Studio at 3.45 I worked some
but in little humor, not feeling
well — For several days past
I have marked a full day against
"Twilight" which is hardly fair, as
I have really not worked over half
days — Home to tea at 7.15, well
working over an old sketch with
the pencil — Cold — The day has
been quite Marchy — Got to bed
about 11.15
