## Left Page

### June, Sunday 5, 1870

another day of the same miserable sort \- Sticky
Chilly \+ close \- M\[illegible\] went
to Hastings' \+ heard him
preach from Luke, 22°. C¹
15ᵗ. V. After Service went
up to Uncle A's to dinner
Joe \+ Mill \- Ada Thayer \+
\- Hedden there \- After
dinner, lounging and
Reading \- Tea at 6, 30.
Left at 8 \+ at 9, 15 called
at Divens' in 52°. St, Girls
in at Mrs Camps \- Sayme
street \- went there after
them \- Home at 10, 30
Read until 11, 30 and

## Right Page

### June, Monday 6, 1870

and yet another day \- hot and
muggy in the m\[illegible\] \- from 4 to 6 oclk
p.m. charming \- eveng fog. Got
to studio at 9, 15 \- no strength
\+ little work \- what I did was
upon sketch for Noreld Mt. Co\-
mt. T. Cols cult called \+ I promised
to ride with him this p.m. Burling
\+ Louis Tiffany called \- Home to
lunch at 1\- at 2, 30 went to S Louis's
\+ paid carpet bill \- then to Anthony's
to Sarmys \+ to Sauthiers \- then
to studio at 3, 10 \- While away
Rev. Thos. Hastings called \- at
4 oclk. Rode up to \+ around
the Park with W. C \- back
to Studio at 6. after a most
delightful drive \- Home to
tea at 7 \- at 8, 25 called on
Mr \+ Mrs. \+\+ Marks, 550 Fifth ave.

left at 10 \- Home \- mounted
