## Left Page

### December Friday, 16 1870

In bed all day with a splitting nervous headache — a culmination of the days of distress that I have just passed — Fortunately I had a bed to lie in — Got up at 7 o'clk. P.M. — very hungry — Matt broiled a bit of chicken for me which I enjoyed very much — Sabin my bed chair Reading or called up on the sofa until about 1.30 A.M. when I got off to bed — but did not get to sleep until after 3 o'clk —

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 17 1870

Changable day — but cold and windy — Got to studio about 9.30 kindled fire — not feeling well, the m't passed without anything being accomplished — at noon went to the barbers — had hair cut and had shampooed — decidedly Refreshing — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — worked on Alabama Arms drawing until 3 — then went in to Brevoort's \+ mth class took 2d lesson in perspective — until 4 — then back to studio \+ hugged the Stove until near 6 — then home to tea — After tea — mounted drawing of arms — then to work on perspective problems until 11 o'clk — enjoyed it very much — this is a great pleasure to do anything that can be demonstrated to be exactly right — got to bed in my own bed — about 11.30
