## Left Page

### October, Sunday, 23, 1870.

Gloorious day. Up late — a bath &
skfat at 9.30 — We[?] went to hear
Dr. Hastings — He preached from St.
James, 1st Ch — 2d 6th v. — I listened
with a heavy head & vain thoughts
— so I am afraid got but little good.
After services went into the Charl and
looked over the new R.R. depot building
cor. 4th Ave & 42d St. Home to dinner at
12.30 — At 3 started out for a walk.
Stopped at St. Thomas's Ch — 5th Ave St
heard opening service — About [illegible]
went up to Central Park & walked
about there until dusk. Home to tea
at 6. We[?] went to B[illegible] Tabernacle
& heard Dr. Leonard Bacon's 2d lecture
in the Course — on the history of Puri[tan]
Fanism" — very sleepy. walked
up 6th Ave. with Rich[ar]d. Dorman.
To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### October, Monday, 24, 1870.

Charming day — mild & clear — got
to Studio at 9.30 — Some clearing
up & then commenced arrangements
to begin on drawing of a building
for Uncle Willie's company — made
but little progress & at 12 went over
to see George in his Room — We
went home to lunch at 1 —
Back at 2.15 — Reducing with the
Camera-lucida, building from
architect's drawing — Did not
work any better than in the m[orning].
Home to tea at 6.15. After tea, up
to Burggraff's — Uncle & st Junius
every part of ev[ening?] — Joe, Will,
& old Brainerd, later — I worked
until 12.30 mounting drawings
& old sketches — got to bed
about 1 —
