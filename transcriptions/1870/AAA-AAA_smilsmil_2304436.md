## Left Page

### January Sunday, 16 1870

Clear glorious & unseasonable day, being altogether too mild — Mrs., went to Dr.
Hastings & heard him preach from 1st Kings 15th & 17th v. giving a fine summary of the characters of Elijah & making applications to Christ. went up to Uncle Xs to dinner. Spent P.M. enjoying out of doors — at 4.30 I walked over to 3d ave & Rode down to Gavits no 84 8th. Took tea there & at 8.30 Rode down to 31st St stopping at Madison ave Baptist Ch — walked home with Matt — Read Guernsey's Homoeopathic Practice until 11 o'clk and then to bed.

## Right Page

### January Monday, 17 1870

Miserable, muggy, warm & wet day — got to studio at 9.30 and kindled fire — Commenced a 2d drawing for Pacific RR Bonds for a B.N. Co — Much broken in my work, waiting for Russell to come for Water Color drawings — at 12.30 went to Swain Gifford's but he was not in — Ret'd. Abt — Russell came — Took lunch at Studio — at near 3, again went to Gifford's — saw him & got photo of Mt Hood. Met W. Hinshelwood in the Hall & had a few words with him — Father at Studio near 4 — worked as long as I could see. Home to tea at 6 — Every called on Kate Fessenden at Mr. Lewis's No 8 W. 29th St — Pleasant, chatty call until near 10 — then home. Raining steadily — Read until after 11 and to bed about 11.45
