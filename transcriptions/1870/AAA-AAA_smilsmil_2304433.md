## Left Page

### January, Monday, 10, 1870

milder & part of the time, overcast — making paste, etc detained me so that it was 9.45 when I got to Studio — kindled fire & swept up — letters from Dr F.W. Lewis and Alfred Jones, also a drawing from Jones. W. Wood of Utica called — at noon went to Lauthier's got board to mount drawing — then to Snedecor's & at 1.30 home to lunch — back at 2.30 after stopping at Burling's — Stretched paper over mounting board for drawing & wrote a letter to Dr. Lewis. Home to tea at 6 — at 8, with Geo. & Will went to Mr. Camp's — 345 Fifth Ave, where he is boarding — He is Pres. of the Clearing House and is making a little collection of pictures — a pleasant chatting ever until 10.20, then home and to bed soon after 11

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday, 11, 1870

Mild pleasant day, got to Studio at 9.15. Paper that I stretched on mounting board yesterday, slightly marred by an accident, so I stretched another piece & set to work on drawing (11th day) working generally over the whole bit and I think improving it in color and tone — Home to lunch at 1.20 & back at 2.30, worked as long as I could see — Young Teel and a cousin called — Home to tea at 6.15 — Well feeling tired, I spent it reading & looking up some sort of sentiment or quotation suited to my drawing, as I have no title that pleases me, which is rather getting the cart before the horse — Got to bed about 11 o'clk —
