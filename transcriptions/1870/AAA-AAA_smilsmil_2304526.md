## Left Page

### July, Thursday 21, 1870

Early m[illegible], overcast about 10
o'clk, a change, glorious, breezy
day. Father + Geo. went to the
woods beyond Smith Beede's
where I followed them — we
went up over the hill + back
of Phin Beede's + so home about
noon after a very enjoyable
tramps. Dinner about 2.30
After dinner I took a nap.
Then arranged to work on
my locomotive drawing — about
3\. Father + I joined George
on the Ausable River near the
house, there until 5. Then home.
Weather thickening up smoky.
Beautiful sunset effect over
"The Giants" — Tea at 6.30. after
it Father wrote some — took a
short walk — back — more writing
— Everything very cool + to bed at
9.45

## Right Page

### July, Friday 22, 1870

Charming m[illegible], but very warm. Ab.
4 A.M. Mr. Beede + Alma started for
town for supplies + to get Miss Walters.
I spent an hour or two on locomotive
drawing. About 11.30 Father + I went
through the woods to the Ravine near
the house + there met Geo. Father
feels unusually well to-day. We
climbed over the big Rocks and ex-
plored the place generally. Father
lost his spectacles on our way back.
Got in at 1.30 — Beef tea lunch at 2 —
Father took a nap + then went to the
River. I worked again on drawing
about 4 p.m. The Beedes am[illegible] with
Miss Walters. I went to River and
washed. Very smoky. Dinner at
6.30. About 7.30 Father, Geo. + I
walked down to [illegible] sight of
Potter's. Charming effects. Home
about 8.30 — Some chatting with
Miss W. and to bed at 9.30
