## Left Page

### January Thursday, 20 1870

Another mild, spring like day — Headachy — got to studio
about 9.45 — nearly. Rode
down town, went to cor. Dutch
& Fulton St. & got block of box
wood. Then to Pate's — to see
about pfs — the to A.B.N. Co —
then to Continental Pub. Co
— at 1, met Father at Parker's
& lunched — then rode up to
N.A. of D. & at 2.30 back to studio
& there until after 5 — then
over to Y.M.C.A. with Geo —
called on Swain Gifford &
back to studio & home at 6 —
Tea & at 8.30 with Geo —
to "Private Views" — Water Colors
at N.A. of D — Home at 10.30
and to bed 11.15

## Right Page

### January Friday, 21 1870

Beautiful day — Late in
bed, trying to get rested, so
that I did not get to studio
until after 10 o'clk — Worked
on Scott's drawing on wood
until 1.15 (½ day) getting it
transferred & partly outlined.
Home to lunch & at 2.30
at Phil. Rehearsal — good,
Enjoyed "Der mis der Tone"
very much — after, went with
Belle & Mary Tmeedy & George
to Sloane's to select Rugs for
G's studio — Home to Tea at 6 —
at 8 went to Prof. Dodge's
& with him went to No. 3 —
Washington Sq. & called
upon Prof. Mr. Draper Saw —
found him quiet, but very
agreeable & spent the evng
until 10.15 in conversation
chiefly of the Irish & Negro in
this country — Home at 11
& to bed soon after —
