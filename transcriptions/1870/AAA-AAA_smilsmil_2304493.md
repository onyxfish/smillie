## Left Page

### May, Thursday, 12, 1870

Charming day — almost a shower about middle of P.M. — Got to Studio
at 9.15 and commenced drawing
of Golden Gate for Occidental Ins.
Co. — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.30 & worked until
6.45. after some trouble with
the sky, getting it into such
condition that I hope to
made a creditable drawing —
Miss Sterling called late. —
Home to tea soon after 7 —
Eve'g spent reading Mrs.
Mr. Farland — Richardson's acct.
of herself in Tribune of yesterday.
It is well written and all of
my sympathies go with her.
Got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Friday, 13, 1870

Fine day. Got to studio just before
9 o'clk & at work on "Golden Gate"
drawing (2nd day) Stephen Massot
called about noon to beg me to
make a music title ingr. for
him — bet. 12 & 1. Belle & Mary
J meedy called. — I made arrange-
ments to go to Montclair with them
this P.M. — Home to lunch at
2 — & after very hurried fixing
got off at 3 — met Geo. & the Swedish
at foot of Barclay St. at 3.50 just
in time for train — while on the
way out, a heavy shower, and
others about us during rest of
P.M. Mr. Benedict & Jennie [illegible]
met us at depot with carriage —
Pleasant house — dinner at
6 — an evening of chatting
& to bed soon after 10 —
