## Left Page

### April, Saturday, 2, 1870.

miserable day, wind and
Rain — In bed all day
with a horrible headache.
Did not get up until 9.15
P.M. — and so lost my
Philharmonic Concert —

Took food for the first
time since yesterday at 10.14
P.M. — then laying my
big chair until 5 o'clk A.M.
when just in the grey of
the mng I got into bed —

## Right Page

### April, Sunday, 3, 1870.

Another miserable day —
got up shortly after 9 o'clk
A.M. feeling very weak —
Spent the day in-doors,
amused with some reading
and a good deal of lying
about — Got to bed at
about 11 o'clke —
