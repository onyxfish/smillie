## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 19 1870

*In Camp — Sower ausable* [written above heading]

Up at 6 — bkfst soon after 7 — but did not get traps packed to start until near 9 o'clk. — Then went alone, carrying my painting traps — Misses Torrey + Walter, Fred D., Gel. + Orlando + team following. — The day, the color of sky + foliage, everything superb — enjoyed the walk exceedingly — got to lake shortly before 10 and to the top of fall at 10:15 + Right to work on oil sketch of Rocks + top of fall — at 1:15 prepared lunch at 2 o'clk. again at work + at it until near 4 when I gave up, after getting a very encouraging start — Fred D. came up about 3 — m Kell[?] to the shanty a little before 5 — Dinner at 6 — Chatting until 9 when the ladies turned in — m D's tent — then Fred, Gil + I went + called on Myant + Shurtliffe. Back at 10 + then rolled in for the night —

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 20 1870

Up soon after 6 after a rather cold night — but slept pretty well. Bkfst about 7 or later — Partridge — about 8:30 got off to my work at the top of Rainbow Fall — Fred D. followed soon after — all the m'f noticed m study looking of to the Giant — Lunched soon after 1 — at 2 worked over study that I painted in yesterday — worked until 4 then cleaned up + back to camp at 5 o'clk — washed brushes + then cut boughs for bed until 6 o'clk — a late dinner — inconveniently dark — but broiled chicken, canned peaches, very good — Shurtliffe + Myant came up bet. 8 + 9 — after lying around, chatting + star gazing going in for the night.
