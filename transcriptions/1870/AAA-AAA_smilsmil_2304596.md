## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 20 1870.

Beautiful mng, but after cloudy got to studio at 9 o'clk — and to work on drawing of Locomotive No. 2 — A,B,N. Co— (2d. day) — At it until 1 o'clk — then home to dinner — back at 2.15 \+ worked until 4.10, when I could go no longer — then went to Coffie's to see if I could find where he has sent horses legs — notin — then to Snedecor's — then to studio — then to Bogardus's — then home to tea at 6.30 — Uncle a. to tea — Swell at 8.20 — Called on Miss Annie Tiffany — just got nicely started on a cosy eveng, Reading "Lear" aloud when interrupted by Mrs H.4 Dyer — I left at 9.40 — Stopped for a few minutes at Dickel's Riding academy — Home at 10.15 and to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 21 1870.

Clear — breezy — Cold — Got to Bogardus's mth Mother \+ Watt at 8.30 — Mother sat again for photo about 9.30 — we all went to my Room — Soon after 10 I got to work on Locomotive drawing (3d day) \+ at it until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — back at 2.30 \+ worked as long as I could see, until 4.15 — then went to Coffei's — as a liar — he is quite an interesting study. I don't know any other trait of his so well marked — on Union Sq. almost run over by a stage — while abusing the driver — Belle Tweedy came up — she gave me a ticket to Emerson's lecture to-night \+ I went home with her — Home to tea at 6.10 — At W.A.\[illegible\] D at 7.15 — B2 Emerson's 3d. lecture — Fine in hall Y.M.C.A. — After lecture me \+ over to see that Geo's Room was all right — The Emerson lecture postponed — Rel to Social meeting at W.a. \[illegible\] D — there until 10 — walked up mth J.D.E. Green — Home at 10.30 and to bed about 11.
