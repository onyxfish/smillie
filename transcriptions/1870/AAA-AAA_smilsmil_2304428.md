## Right Page

### January, Saturday 1, 1870

Dull mrg. Up about 10. Shfoted and dressed & by 11.30 was ready to make
New Year Calls. For the first time in
about 5 years I felt like calling, so
I had prepared a list of about 20 —
went first to Divens. 52d St. then to Mr.
& Mrs Marks, 550, 5th ave. then to Miss Annie
Day, 48th St. then Miss Corlies with the
Misses Macy at 17 W 49. Then about 1 ock
it began to rain & so continued off & on
during the day, showery & misty. light —
then to Mr. Anthonys & the Rogers, vot 49th 39
at 1.30 home to lunch. Shortly after 2 went
to Nellie Jessup's then to Mrs. Wm Dart 44 Park
ave. then to Mr. A.B. Marks 34th St. Not at home.
No. 53 26th St. & at 5.30 to Susena Melancia's
where I took tea & stayed 1¼ hours. Then
to Mrs. Harper's 259 4th ave. to Harry
Kimball's, to Florence Smith & Rosalie
goodman. 47 West 25th St. to Leila Martins
and left there at 9.15. Tired and with
7 more calls on my list. Rode down
to the Freedmen, getting there at 9.30.
Stayed there until 11.45 then home
getting to bed at 12.40 after a hard
& agreeable day's work.
