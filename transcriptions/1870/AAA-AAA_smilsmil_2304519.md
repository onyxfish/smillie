## Left Page

### July, Thursday 7, 1870

Slight rain early in the mg — then muggy \+ clearing, with heavy showers during the p.m. Everf pleasant — Heavy head and unwilling to work got about half a days work done on Locomotive drawing making about 5½ days on it — got it completely outlined and ready to commence with the India ink — Everf writing a note to Annie Tiffany with pen \+ ink illustrations — Did not get to bed until midnight —

## Right Page

### July, Friday 8, 1870

Up late — quite a heavy Rain during the morning but the afternoon, lovely — \+ moonlight, glorious. Worked hard all day on drawing (6½ days) washing in the effect — letter from Mr. Enoch Lewis, accepting "Lake Placid" enclosing check for $100 — \+ "Indian Pass" Returned by Yp — George got off on the night boat for Albany and E-town — there to wait for me — Everf wrote to Mr. Lewis \+ to Swain Gifford got to bed Som after 10 —
