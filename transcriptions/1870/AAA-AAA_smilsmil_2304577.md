## Left Page

### November Friday, 4 1870

Glorious day — felt very much better when I got up. Got to studio at 9.30 \+ to work on studies — varnishing \+ Retouching — Ret 1 \+ 2 — went out for a walk in the square — back \+ lunched — \+ worked until 4.30. Rogen brought up Jefferson the actor — He wants lessons in drawing — at 5 went to Brooks Bros then to Snedecors back to studio \+ home to tea at 6.30. Eveng. washed in in India ink, effect on drawing of little church for Lexington, by Charl — got to bed at abt 11.

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 5 1870

Disagreeable mf — dark \+ Rainy during the middle of the day — cloudless, fresh moonlight night. Got to studio abt 9 o'clk. and at work again on my studies. Lunched in Studio at 3 o'clk. \+ after working as long as I could see got all my large studies in a presentable condition — my Scrambling \+ glazing \+ occasional touching has improved them very much. At 5.30 — went to Brooks Bros. Home to Tea at 6.45 — Read Tribune until 8.45 — then Rode down to the Tweedys where I met Gel — . Left there at 10.30 — walked home. Washed brushes \+ to bed about 12.30
