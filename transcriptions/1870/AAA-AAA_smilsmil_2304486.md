## Left Page

### April Tuesday, 26 1870.

Delicious day — got to Studio at 8.30 — Pliny Smith called about 9 — Expressed himself as perfectly satisfied with the three paintings for him — Had to go over to see Wyant about sending a picture to Cincinnati — Man at Studio fixing roof & had so that at 11 o'clc I had not got to work — Then mouth had hair cut etc, & at 12 commenced work on 14×24" Adirondack Lake (6th day) painting thinly men part of sky & distance and in R.h. corner — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Worked until 5.30 then home — tea & at 7.15 at Union League Theatre to see Corinne Colman & Nellie Howard. Was very much pleased — met the Colmans Tiffanys and Treedays — Home at 11.30 and Right to bed.

## Right Page

### April Wednesday, 27 1870.

Hot day — Up with a miserable headache — got to Studio about 9.30 — Sat about until 12 when I gave it up & went home to bed — where I stayed until about 10 p.m. — when I got up & took something to eat — Sat in my big chair until 2 o'clc a.m. — then to bed — By my headache I missed Prof. Morse's Reception in the P.M. & Mrs. Greatores's & Avery's entertainments in the eveg.
