## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 19 1870

Fine breezy day. Spent most of
the mng arranging contents of my
Trunk — went to Studio at 11 o'clk.
Called on several of the folks and
home to lunch at 1 — Pretty well
used up with a headache, So
about 4 o'clk, lay down & slept
until near 6 — Evelf spent
in "the bosom of my family"
looking over English papers,
&c — &c — Got to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 20 1870

muggy. Rainy day, with an Earthquake
that I didn't know anything about — got
to the Studio about 9.30 & set to work at
"house cleaning" — piled things up — swept,
washed, dusted and but for interruption
would have finished — Lunched at Studio
at 1 — Jas. M. Hart brought me a letter
he had just rec'd from J. R. Brevoort
announcing the death of his wife
yesterday — Cancer of the womb — after
giving birth to a still born child —
Home at dusk — Raining steadily —
Uncle A. took dinner with us. Evelf
Belle & Mary Tweedy made their ap-
pearance in Mountain costume at 8.30
P.m. Enjoyed a [illegible], altho'
I was suffering from a headache
& at 10.30, [illegible] I escorted them
home — I got to bed at 12 —
