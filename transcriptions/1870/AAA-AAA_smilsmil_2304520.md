## Left Page

### July, Saturday, 9, 1870

Glorious day, hot in the sun but breezy & cool in the shade.
Stomach troubling me. M'f
until after 10 fixing lock on trunk
mess afts. Then got to work
on drawing & stuck to it until
after 6. P.M (7½ days) working
with magnifying glass over the
engine — After Tea went
down to studio & fixed up
some things. Then up to Bur-
ggrof's & got boots & shoes for
m's fixed up. Home and
to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday, 10, 1870

as usual, whenever I get into a
corner, I have a colic — so, as I
am very short for time & hurrying
to get off if possible, next Friday,
I had my colic this morning.
I'm thankful that it did not
come on a work day, for then I
would have lost more time. I
woke about 5 A.M. with anguish
about 6, by vomiting cleared my
stomach of about a gill of greyish,
almost tasteless fluid — not a
particle of solid — great irregularity
of the heart, nervous, until about 8. A.M.
bowels regular — nervous pain in
the head commenced in upper part
of head during m'f & increased until
evg, leaving about midnight. Fed
on Beef Tea & granum. Slept most
of day & night — getting to bed in
the grey of the morning.
