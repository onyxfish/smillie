## Left Page

### August Thursday, 4 1870.

Cloudy mg, but cleared off charmingly — about 9 o'clk, went to work on large Water Color drawing. my study place, quite cold & damp, but worked steadily until 1 o'clk. Then home to dinner. Back and at work on oil study of Running water soon after 2 — finished the painting in — & about 5 o'clk, with George went over the same scramble that I went over yesterday afternoon — got in a little before 7 and had tea at once. Evening, feeling uncomfortable did nothing but wait until 10 o'clk & soon after — to bed.

## Right Page

### August Friday, 5 1870.

Early mg, charming — then a shower about 8 o'clk — after that beautiful skies of torn up clouds. So I sat down upon the doorstep & spent the morning painting a study of the sky & distant mts under sunshine & cloud shadow. Dinner near 1.30 — Soon after 2, went to top of high cliff overlooking fall in Raimie & outlined a view that, then to some old hemlocks & outlined a sketch of them, so that I am ready to go on with them in oil color — back about 6.30 — Then Tea — Evey wrote a note to Miss Johnston at E-town & tried to write some to Emma Ireland, but gave it up.
