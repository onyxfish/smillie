## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 16 1870

Glorious, cloudless day —
got to Studio at 9.15 & spent
the m'f getting my desk
& things about in order to
begin working on B. a B. N. Co's
die — Another visit of W [illegible]
who thinks that he is slowly
improving — Home to lunch
at 1.20 — back at 2.30 — The
P.M. getting tools in order.
This slow work with me to
get again at engraving —
At 5 went to Coffee's,
Snedecor's & then called
for half an hour on the Tweedys.
Back to Studio at 6 — saw
Meuzler — Home to tea at 6.45
Eve'g, mounting photos, for
Emma Ireland — drawings
etc, etc — To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 17 1870

Glorious day again — got to Studio
at 9 o'clk — and to work outlining
dag. of building — slow work — my
head hurting me — a cold
like that of some days ago evi-
dently has hold of me — Home
to lunch at 1 o'clk — after lunch
stretched myself on sofa &
lay there until 4.30 o'clk —
at 5 went to Studio & started
up fine — at 6 — went to the
Tweedys & begged off from
engagement for this eve'g
to call on Miss Torrey at
Columbia College — Back
to Studio — Saw Meuzler — then
went to Emma Ireland's with
photos that I mounted for her.
Home to tea at 8 o'clk — Eve'g,
mounted one or two drawings
& sketches — Will & Joe came
in — To bed at 10.30.
