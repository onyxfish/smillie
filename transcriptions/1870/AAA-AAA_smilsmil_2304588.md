## Left Page

### December Friday, 2 1870.

Another glorious day — Atg. went down to see Emil Seitz about morning board — not in — called on Savony — saw him — then at Snedecor's — met there Mr. Ino. Fraser & J.G. Brown. At 10.30 at Studio — had to kindle a fire as my grate tumbled down last night — then swept & spent rest of m'f. grinding gravers. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.10 all the P.M. at work on die m's. graver (8½ days). Mr. Durand called in the m'f., Mr. Fairman in the afternoon — at 5 went to Y.M.C.A. — worked forth [illegible] hour in gymnasium — then in Reading Room for ½ an hour — home to tea at 6.30 Even'g. worked on drawing of Arms of Alabama & to bed on sofa at 10.30

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 3 1870.

Cloudy & chilly m'f, but bet 10 & 11 it cleared to a superb day — Got to studio at 9 o'clk. & at once at work with graver on die (9½ days). J.M. Falconer came in to see Geo. — some one wishes to buy a picture of his — also a Mr. Davis, to take lessons — I sent him to Geo. — At 12.30 went to Geo.'s room — at 1.15 home to lunch — back at 2 — worked until 4.40 when I could see no longer — stretched myself on lounge & lay there until 6 o'clk. — home to tea at 6.20 — Ev'g. from 7.30 until 11 worked on Arms of Alabama drawing — getting nearly finished — to bed at 11.30
