## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 1 1870.

Beautiful clear m'f. up at 6.30, but did not get bkfst and
off to my work until near 9 o'clk
went to top of Rainbow Fall, but
like yesterday, it soon clouded
over & light showers fell — but I
kept on with my painting.
Lunched at 1 — had peaches.
At near 3 a thunder storm came
up & I beat a retreat, getting to
shanty wet & just as it cleared
off beautifully. Changed boots
& spent Rest of P.M. paddling
about the lake. Charmed with
the eve'g effects. Smith took the
place of Orlando. Tea about 6.30
after tea — a sketch of the ledges
— then a visit from my aunt — then
a moonlight row on the lake with
girl — then Reading newspapers
and to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 2 1870.

up at 6 after a good night's Rest.
morning charming — enjoyed exceedingly
a capital bkfst. of broiled chicken,
toast, potatoes & first Rate tea. Ready
off to my work, a little before 8 o'clk —
at the top of the fall — light & effect
charming until about 11 when it filmed
over — Lunch at 12.30 — dyspeptic — too
much to eat — dizziness in head so that
I cannot work — much discouraged —
all that I have done this season below
my average — could not paint after
2 — cleaned up — tried pencil on
several bits about, no go — at 3 started
up streams & went for an hour. Some
very mild passages — back at 4.45 —
feeling much better — got to shanty
a little after 5 — tea at 6 — Hard work
to get rid of the eve'g, no one on the
lake; my aunt went out this m'f — my
eyes would not let me Read or write
so enjoyed the delightful air and
struggling moonlight until 10 o'clk
