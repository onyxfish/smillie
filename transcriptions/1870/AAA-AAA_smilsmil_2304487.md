## Left Page

### April, Thursday 28, 1870

Clear, hot day. Feel very weak. got to studio a few minutes after 9 \+ set to work on little sketch of grain field for McAdams \- worked on it until . 1 \- then home to lunch \- back at 2.30 \+ worked until 5.30 when a splendid thunder storm came up \- the hurricane of wind \- the darkness and the flying clouds of dust made some grand pictures which I enjoyed looking out of my window \- Home to tea at 7 \- Evely writing to Natt Caler delinquents \- more Rain \- got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 29, 1870

Overcast and as compared with yesterday, a cold day \- Rain during middle of the day. Evely cleared \- my stomach needing a play day I determined to drum up Recruits for an etching club \- Called on Hennessy Linton, V. Kehlig, Falconer (not in) Dana Julien Scott, Head \- De Haas (not in) J.G. Brown, Mr. Hart. Eaton \- La Farge (not in) \+ Gifford (R.S.) Home to lunch at 1.15 \- to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.15 after Rehearsal, with Gel. Many \+ Belle Smeedy. Called on Mr. Tiffany then went to N.A. of D. There met fanny Kimball \+ all had a very pleasant time together until 6 o'cl then walked home with Smeedys \+ walked home myself about 7.30 Evely tired \+ Read My newspapers got to bed about 11 \-
