## Left Page

### January Saturday, 22 1870.

another spring like day. Got to studio at 9.30 \+ at work on Scott's drawing on wood, the mg broken by the moving of George's furniture to his New Room at the Y.M.C.a. \- also, Rec'd a long \+ very abusive letter from Mr. Rob Hinshelwood which, as usual I sent back to him. \- Home to lunch, at 1.15, stopping at Burling's on the way \- back at 2.15, worked on drawing until 4.15 then over to Y.M.C.A building. \- Saw Gifford, (Matt \+ Kate there,) Tiffany \+ George \- Home to tea at 6. \- Evening at home Reading \+ writing to Miss Sterling \+ Alfred Jones. To bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### January Sunday, 23 1870.

mild, muggy \+ disagreeable. \- mg went to Isaac Riley's church to get his address, on my way back stopped at Man's. in 34th St \- \+ with the Dr. walked to Madison Ave. Baptist Ch. where I heard the new pastor Rev. W. Elder. Text Philippians II Chap 5-11 verses. \- It was not an argumentative, or doctrinal sermon \- but custodinal \- Parts I liked \- walked up to 5 ave \+ 49th St. after stopping at home \+ then Rode to 125th St. going to Uncle A's to dinner. \- By 1.30 it had cleared away charmingly with lovely skies \+ effects. I enjoyed myself out of doors until near 5 o'clk, when with Will's Miun I Rd home \- Caught in the Rain. very damp \+ muggy \- did not go out but Read \+ napped in my chair until after 11. getting to bed a little before 12 \-
