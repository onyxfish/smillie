## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 9 1870.

Brilliant day — got to studio at 8.45 + at work on "Lake Mahkeenac"
(5th day) About noon Mrs. Beach —
her daughter Numa + Miss
Bullard — niece of H.W. Beecher
called — Home to lunch at 1.30
at 2.15 went to N.A. of D. to see
to Packing of Phila drawings —
Back to studio at 3.10 and
painted until 5 o'clk — getting
over middle distance, meadows
+ most of near trees — I think it
is improving — at 6 went down
to S. P. Avery's to get my Union
League Club invitation transferred
to next month — Home to tea at
6.45 — Eve'g at 8.30 — Belle,
Mary + Jenny Treedy with Joe
came up — Left at 12 o'clk —
I got to bed at 12.30

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 10 1870.

Weather mixed, and I too — did'nt
get up until 9 o'clk — got to studio
at 10.30 — miserable with a head-
ache. Met W. H. Snudsen in the
street — he went up to studio with
me — there until about 11.30 —
then went with him over to
George's studio — I called on
Kimbze, Tiffany + Gifford — Met
Mr. Putnam & W. C. Bryant — W.C.B.
went over to my Room + spent a
very pleasant hour and a half with
me. He seems disposed to help
me get out my 8 vues of plates of
"Among the Trees" — Home to lunch
at 2.45. Back at 4 o'clk. John
Durand, Walter Brown + Miss
Helen Slade called — Home to
tea at 7 o'clk — Eve'g Doc. + and
Mary. Miss Hayward + Tom Sharmon
+ his wife at the house until 11.45
got to bed about 12.30
