## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 23 1870.

Windy, dirty day — got to Studio at
9 o'clk & to work on "Twilight" —
(23d day) working over the middle —
dis Four Trees — Home to lunch
at 1.15 — back at 2.15 — Painted
until 5 o'clk — cleared up &
home to Tea at 6 — Father
came down to Studio with
his work — Eveng Mr. & Mrs.
Elder & Miss Kimball came to
Tea & spent the evening
until 11 o'clk — got to
bed about 11.¾5

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 24 1870.

Cloudless, but muddier & dirtier
than yesterday — got to Studio
at 9.10 & to work on "Twilight"
(24th day) on foreground Rocks
etc, making them lighter &
bluer & quieting harsh color
& drawing — Mr. Benedict of
Schenectady a cousin of the Tweddies
called about noon — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked
until about 5 o'clk — I am
very much encouraged about
my picture & am hoping that
it will bring me credit — Home
to Tea at 7 — Eveng looking
over old letters — got to bed
about 11 —
