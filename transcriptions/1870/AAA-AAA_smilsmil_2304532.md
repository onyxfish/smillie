## Left Page

### August Tuesday, 2 1870.

Beautiful clear day — fresh breezy and cool — Had a long mornings work on Water Color drawing, but advancing slowly. Home to dinner at 1, surprised to find Mr. & Mrs. (Rev.) Van Dyke, Mrs. Hunt & Mrs. Jas. M. Hart & daughter. A pleasant dinner & after, all went with us to our work & visited about an hour. I painted on oil study, getting in all the water & worked until after 5 — then a made Tea at 6.30 — and at 8, Geo. & I went to Dr. Potter's to get the mail, he to bring it that far. Waited until 9.30, having a pleasant visit with Mrs. & Miss Noble — a letter from Belle Treadway & Sam. Colman, none from home but a lot of papers — until 11 o'clk, looking them over — then to bed —

## Right Page

### August Wednesday, 3 1870.

Clear m'f, but soon thickened up. By 10 o'clk. it was all overcast & slight spittings of Rain — Got up with a miserable headache which stuck to me all day — Skpt at 8 — but did not go to my large Water Color study till after 9 — then worked steadily until 1 — then dinner — until after 3 Read my newspapers then went out to Ravine and followed it up to near the widow Hales — slight showers. Found Father's lost spectacles in the woods. Home to tea about 6 — Heavy Rain and during the night, blowing — Eve'g wrote to J. Frank Reed & S. Verplanck — To bed about 10 o'clk,
