## Left Page

### May, Thursday 26, 1870

Cool, delightful day — on my way to Studio, stopped at Bogardus's & had photo taken
got to Studio at 9.30 and to work on 12×22" "Indian Pass, Adirondacks" (5th day) — Glazed and painted thinly over sky, Mts & middle distances — getting it somewhat better, but I need very much knowledge — more knowledge — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30, worked on painting until 6 — then went into Madison Sq. to hear band play until 7 o'clk — Then back — Washed up — home to Tea at 7.30 — At 8.30 Matt & I went down to call on the Steadys — Home at 11 o'clk — To bed at 12 —
