## Left Page

### January Thursday, 6 1870.

overcast and disagreeable
windy day. Got to Studio at
9 o'clk. and at work on drawing
(8th day) going over the foreground
trees. In spite of me, I
am getting it black and
begin to fear that it will
only be a repetition of
what has gone before.
Home to lunch at 1.15
back at 2.15 & worked as
long as I could see.
Home to tea at 6. Every.
engaged with french
and to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### January Friday, 7 1870.

Cold, & clear. Got to Studio
at 9.10 & at work on drawing
(9th day) trying to give life &
variety to color of foreground
trees, but in spite of me they
are heavy and monotonous
a difficulty that I run into
in spite of myself, especially
when working in Water Colors.
worked until 1.30, then home
to lunch & at 2 o'clk, Rode
down to Phil. Rehearsal. The
Academy crowded to hear Ole
Bull & Mills. Sat in amphi-
theatre. walked to 25th St with
miss Howe. Then to 25th St & 8th ave
& back to her home with Emma
Ireland. Then to Studio. Home
to tea at 7 o'clk. Evening
lazily at home & to bed
about 11 o'clk.
