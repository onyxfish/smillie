## Left Page

### February Saturday, 5 1870

Overcast, with threats of snow.
Got to studio at 9.10. Before
10 o'clk Messrs. Geo. Whitney, Enoch
Sevins + — Justin's called, staying
about ½ an hour. I did nothing
worth counting until 12 o'clk
when I went to Y.M.C.A. Bldg. +
called on T.W. Wood, my old, Tait
+ Louis' [illegible]. I gave him my note
to his sister. While there she came
in with Miss Goddard + another young
lady. Home to lunch at 1.20. back
at 2.15. Afternoon painted a little
sketch about 3×6 in to fit a frame
that I have. P.M. checked over lake
+ mountains — trees + figures in
foreground. Maggie Girnum and
two lady friends + a gentleman called.
Home at 5.15. Went Phil Concert.
Spohn's Die Meader Jone was given
superbly. I think I never heard
the orchestra play so well,
+ altho' at times feeling quite sick
I enjoyed the concert immensely. — To bed
about 2. —

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 6 1870

Overcast + Raw — got up headachy
at about 10.30. Had my [illegible]
about 11.30 + at 12.15 started for
Uncle Aaron's — got there + found
Will, Joe, Minnie + Clare Brainerd,
Miss Porter being at home we had
a table full. After dinner, I
wandered over the place + along
the River, + at 4.20 I started for
home, walking over the Insane
Asylum grounds + meeting a patient
who wished to join me in my walk,
then along aqueduct to 105 St.
into the Park. down 6th ave + home
at 6.20, still feeling my head
uncomfortable. Jas. + the Rest
of the ev'g in my big chair.
To bed at 10.30
