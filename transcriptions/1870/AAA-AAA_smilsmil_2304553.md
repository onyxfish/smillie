## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 13 1870.

another superb day. Up at 5 o'clk to see the Smeedys + Miss Abbott. [illegible]. They got away at 6 o'clk — Geo., too sick to get up — something that has eaten — Regular bkfst — at 7 — about 9. went to study of Hemlock \+ beech — worked until 12. When I got it done after a fashion. I transferred traps to other study of Hemlock Trunks + home to lunch at 1 o'clk — back to work at 2 o'clk managed to get my 2d study done after a fashion just as the sun set, about 4.30 — at 5 down to the house, then up on the hill to enjoy effects for an hour. I [illegible] som after 6 — Geo. much better — eveng chatting with Miss [illegible] \+ Reading newspapers until near 10 o'clk — then to bed —

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 14 1870.

Delicious day — mild + cloudless — Up at near 7. half over with a headache with which I fought until late in the afternoon — about 9 took traps \+ went into the woods — Spent an hour getting a pencilling of a beech tree, then on to the cliff overlooking the fall. worked over background rocks + notwithstanding headache did better work I think than any day yet. I could see what I was doing + worked with actual comfort. Home to lunch ab 1 + after lunch lay down on the bed until 2.15. then off again to same study, but the light had so changed that I did little but finish a mornings work — at 4.30 went into Ravine when Geo. was — at 5.30 home + took a sponge bath \+ changed — Tea + eveng spent mending sketching traps + chatting with Misses Torrey + Walters — To bed at 10.45
