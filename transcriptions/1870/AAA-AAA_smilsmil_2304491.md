## Left Page

### May, Sunday, 8, 1870

In bed all day with a miserable headache — got up about 9 o'clk P.M. and took something to eat — in my big chair until 2 a.m. then to bed + did not get to sleep until daylight, about 5 a.m.

## Right Page

### May, Monday, 9, 1870

After an hour or two of poor sleeping got up — Day overcast + chilly — Went to Sloan's to rectify a mistake about border for new parlor carpet as I did not get up until 9 o'clk was too late to go to funerals of Mr. J.Q.A. Ward. On way up from Sloan's called on Geo. R.S. Gifford, Tiffany + Robbins — got to studio about 12 m. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30. Still very much under the weather + unlike work. Tried to compose design for [illegible] (world) Mr. Co— At 6 o'clk, went to 217 E. 39th St. + called on Fan Vl. for a few minutes — Home to tea at 7 — Evet'g getting my closets on 2d story to rights — got to bed about 11.
