## Left Page

### June, Monday, 27, 1870

muggy overcast muf. Took 8.13 train for city. Stopped at home — then to Bogardus's but yet no dag. Then to studio — mapped up some things — then to Snedecor's & Sauthier's — then home to lunch — got am° from Yonkers — P.M. writing until 5 o'clc — then to Bogardus's — got dag. [illegible] — then to Gilleck's — then to G.P. Putnam's & at 6 a tremendous thunderstorm came up, just able to get to studio, watched it from my window — grand — Home to tea at 7.15. Mr. Main there & all night — Evef W. Chat Breaker — Ollie Treedy — Will & Joe all in — Rec'd invitations to visit Irvington from Colman & Louis Tiffany — To bed som after 11 —

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday, 28, 1870

Intensely hot again all day. Father & Mr. Main got off for West Point this muf — got started outlining dag type of Locomotive for drawing, and worked until after 6 P.M. — Between 5 & 6 o'clc a thunderstorm passed over us, but only seemed to steam us up. — Evef at 8 o'clc went to Kimball's — pleasant visit there until near 9 — then to the Treedy's and there met George — Stayed there until near 11 — then home & to bed at 11.30 —
