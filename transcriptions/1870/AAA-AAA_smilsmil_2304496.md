## Left Page

### May Wednesday, 18 1870

Overcast and quite chilly. After
bkfst, drove with Mr. B, Mary J. & Geo.
over to Orange Junction where Mr.
B. took 8, 7 train — then back and
took Florence to school & drove
around a little with Belle J. —
Worked a little on Massatt's Music
title & from 11 to 12 we all wandered
up the "mountain" — at 12.30, lunch
then napping & a little more
work & ab 4. P.M. Mr. B. Belle
Gel. & I drove over to Orange &
called on the Fessenden, Kate
& Sarah out — then to Setbyllen (?)
Park & called on Mr. Chas. Martin
back to Montclair in time for
6.10 train, but Mr. B. had gone
to Danbury — home about 6.30
Every felt cold & clucky and
consequevitly, very stupid.
We all agreed to be stupid
& stayed up doing it until
11 o'clt — then to bed —

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 19 1870

Glorious day — breezy, clear & cool.
Did not bkfst until near 8 o'clt —
then, with Belle & Mary, took Florence
to school, did marketing & returned
adding miss Jenny & Geo. to our company
we drove about north of Montclair from
9 until 10.30 — then home & I commenced
a water color sketch of Mr. B's house
& worked until lunch, som after
12, worked until 3.45 when with
the three girls, Gel & I drove through
Llewllyn Park out on to the Ridge &
the Mt. & on to Hecker's & McClellan's
places, then Ret. on the back, or west
side of Ridge over a charming road
coming out at Eagle Rock & then down
to Montclair — we left miss Jenny at Mr.
Sweet's & then drove around until the
6.10 train brought Mr. B. — the home and
dinner bet, 7 & 8 o'clt. Eve. very mild.
out on piazza listening to music in
the distance. party at a neighbor's. Belle
Mary, Gel & I walked to the house — back
about 11 & got to bed at 12 —
