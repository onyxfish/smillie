## Left Page

### January Friday, 28, 1870

another beautiful day \- got to studio at 9.20 \+ commenced painting on 14×24 \- Adirondack lake, for Pliny Smith. Worked hard and by 1 o'clk got the sky \+ distance laid in \-
Then home to lunch \+ at 1.30 went with Matt \+ Kate to Phil. Rehearsal \- got good seats after suffering the crush at entering. Belle \+ Many Tren joined us \- After Rehearsal Bill walked with me to the N.A. of D \- there met Feun who told me the father was at the studio waiting for me \- went to studio \- father had drawing done yesterday. Chane wanted \- Home to tea at 6 no \- Evening spent with French verb \- It seems almost impossible for my memory to compass these things \- I must be too old a dog now to learn \- got to bed about a little after 11

## Right Page

### January Saturday, 29, 1870

Steady pouring rain all day. Got to studio at 9 o'clk \+ continued the painting of Adirondack Lake (2d day) got water \+ distant trees painted by 1 o'clk \- Coleman came in \+ me talked Water Color Society matters for ½ or ¾ of an hour \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.15 \- Rode down in car with the Misses Anthony of 42d St \- Worked on painting \- got the hall laid in by 4 o'clk \- treated water \+ washed brushes \- Home to tea at 6.15 \- Evening \- Will gave me tickets to 7th Reg\. Band Concert. I went down early \- Charl, Matt \+ Kate following \- Storm abated \- the concert not interesting to me \- Home at 11 o'clk \- Stars out \- wind blowing up and cold \- got to bed at abt 11.30
