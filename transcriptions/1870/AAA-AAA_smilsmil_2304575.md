## Left Page

### October Monday, 31 1870.

Splendid windy day with fine flying clouds — tried to get to studio, but was unable — throat very sore — head giddy & knees weak — about noon went to Reservoir Park & took an airing for half an hour — in to lunch at 1 — P.M. did not feel so well — evening in the bosom of my family doing nothing with all my might and got to bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 1 1870.

Delicious day — feeling very much better — at 9 o'clk. went & Registered then to studio & lighted fire — went in to see Coleman — at 11 o'clk. went down town to Equitable [illegible] & paid Mr. Prew — then up to Bredecon's — then to Brooks [illegible] — to Knoedle's & Studio at 1.30. Lunch — Saw Coleman — Grannis — Burling — etc — then went in to see Brevort — the first time since the death of Mrs. B. While in there Miss Tiffany & Miss Perkins called — then Belle & Mary & Meedy [illegible] — at 5 o'clk. walked home with the J's — I got home to Tea at 7 o'clk — Welf, Uncle a— & Jimmie with us until 9 — got to bed at 11 —
