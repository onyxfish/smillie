## Left Page

### May Tuesday, 24 1870

Hot, muggy + overcast \- Got to studio at 9.5 and to work on "Sake Placid" (7th day) Painted over middle distance and water \- Home to lunch at 2 \- back at a little before 3 \- Rain commenced about 5 + kept it up until after 7 So dark that I could hardly see to work. Did not get home to Tea until 7.30 \- Will \- Joe + Minn \- there until near 9 o'clk \- I worked on Rough sketch for design for World Ins\- Co\- Got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### May Wednesday, 25 1870

Another hot day \- Went to Snedicors to see about a frame \- on my way back stopped at Knoedlers \- Got to Studio at about 10 o'clk. + then to work on "Lake Placid" (3rd day) painting over foreground. Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30 \- worked until 5. getting the painting in finished \- it now needs another general going over, thin painting and glazing \- At 5.20 went to N. A. of D. + met Fanny Kimball \- there until 6.30. Walked home with her \- Then to Studio, cleaned up \+ home at 7.30 \- Were expecting Newman \- but he didn't come \- Wrote letter to A.W. Warren + notes to Tenn \+ Farren \- To bed at 11.45
