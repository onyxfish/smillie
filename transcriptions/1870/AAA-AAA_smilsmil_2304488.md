## Left Page

### April, Saturday, 30, 1870.

Charming day. Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. & to work on 9×12 — in — "Chappel Pond" (2nd day) the third day in fact as the first day I gave up at noon with a headache, and 2nd day worked about 3 hours — Spent some time with Coleman talking sketching clues. Rec'd. notes from A.A. Warren. Prof. Root & Mr. G.P. Whiting. Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.15 worked until 5.45 & got painting finished in a manner tolerably satisfactory. Cleaned up and commenced fixing up Room when I had a sick turn & was obliged to lie quiet for a time. Home to Tea about 7.30 P.M. Eve'g looked over English News papers & Tribune & got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### May, Sunday, 1, 1870.

Delightful day. Myf went to Rev. Thos. Hastings' & heard him preach from John 14th. 2d. "In my Father's house are many mansions — etc." He gave particular attention to the clause "If it were not so, I would have told you". With Charl went to Uncle a's to dinner — out of doors all the afternoon — me stayed over night — getting to bed about 10.30
