## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 20, 1870

Charming day. Got to studio at 9.30 after having card at Fanny Kimball's \+ stopping at Knoedler's. Commenced painting on 20×24 "Gothic Mts. Adirondack's" — for Mr. Geo. Whitney — Home to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.20 — Mr. Enoch Lewis of Phila. Called. I worked until 6 getting in sky \+ trees in a manner that encourages me — washed up brushes and home to tea shortly after 7. Evening doing nothing largely, so as to be able to do something to-morrow. Got to bed about 10 —

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 21, 1870

Mixed — Some sunshine — some Rain \+ a good deal that was neither. Got to studio at 8.25 \+ to work on 20×24 — (2d. day.) Painted trees on L. h. Side — Rec'd card for Prof. Morse's Recpt'n next Wed. Geo. Percy \+ wife called bet. 1 \+ 2 o'clk. Home to lunch at 1.55 — back at 2.30 — Painted until after 5 o'clk — getting over what I had laid out to do — but not so successfully as yesterday. Home to tea at 7 — Evening as busily employed as last night — got to bed soon after 10 o'clk —
