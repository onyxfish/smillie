## Left Page

### August Monday, 8 1870

Another very hot day. Bkfst about 7.30 + about 8.30 went to work on large water color study — Soon after Magrath, Bryant + Mrs. Svall or Snow passed through. Worked on the water all the mrf + had great difficulty. About noon the girls came down + stayed with us until 1, when we went to dinner — Kate + Charl did not come as we expected — P.M. went to old Hemlocks, studying in oil — got seated, heard thunder — home just as it began to Rain — Heavy showers — Painted sky back of Hemlock trunks + then a small sketch (sky) from the door — Tea at 6.30 — then a walk down the Road to Patters; back about 9 — chatting + Reading and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August Tuesday, 9 1870

mrf, thunderous and cloudy — worked all the mrf on large watercolor, on the foreground rocks + the R.h. side — altho' I had no proper light — about noon Kate + Charl arrived bringing a Mr. Roberts, whom they had met on the way + who ret'd to S-town after dinner. Dinner at 1.30 + after that until after 4 all together jabbering — then, altho' thunder storms were all about, all started for Ravine — through the woods + down the face of the Rocks — funny tumbling + scrambling among the girls — At the fall, caught by a heavy shower — all under a big Rock — very funny. Home at 6 — all wet + jolly — bathed + felt good — After tea, very heavy showers + thunder — Some Reading T. B. Reed's "Sylvia" — and later trying to lay plans for camp — a general puzzle — disappointment does n't express it — To bed at 11.
