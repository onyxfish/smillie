## Left Page

### Saturday 12 —

Very delightful fall day — Got to Studio
at 8.15 with Father, to see my studios —
Had a long talk with him — he left
at 9.45 — Worked on B.a.R.N.Co's
drawing (5th day) until 1 o'clk —
Then home to lunch — back at
2.15 + worked until 4.40 when I
could no longer see — nor could
I finish the drawing — it has been
a Sore Trial to me — tedious, tiresome
and poor pay — At 5 went to Yale
Men's Chr. Ass. — to see if I could
get a gymnasium boy — Saw
Wm E. Dodge Jun. — very pleasant —
There to Fair + Apollo Rooms to get
Matt — Home with her at 6 o'clk —
Ev'g. Reading papers + trying
to think out subject for Academy
picture — a Rushing Torrent —
worked some in charcoal —
To bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### Sunday 13 —

Superb day — M'f, went to D'r Hastings
after eating my sleep — and a big apple —
Heard the text — 11th Thess — 1st Ch. 12th V —
then slept sweetly until the close.
After service, a few pleasant
words with D'r + M'rs Hastings —
At 2.45 — went up to Uncle G's
to dinner with Charl — late —
Miss Porter there — After dinner
walked about with Miss P. — Junie
\+ Charl — until sunset — at
about 5 started for home, getting
in at 6.15 — Ev'g, went with
the family to Madison Ave.
Baptist Ch. — + heard D'r Elder —
Thought him rather prosy —
To bed about 11 —
