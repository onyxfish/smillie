## Left Page

### June, Sunday 19, 1870

Clear \+ very hot — mu\[?\] went to Dr. Hastings \+ heard him preach from the 119th Psalm — verse, forgotten. It may have been 10th or 34th. The thought was somewhat of the Relations of love \+ law — After service, went to Uncle A's to dinner, getting there at 1 o'clk. Afternoon spent quietly on Piazza or in a cherry tree. Tea at 6 — started for home about 8, getting in at 9.20 — To bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Monday 20, 1870

Got to studio at 9.30. Tremendously hot day — Ther. 95°. Belle \+ Mary S. \+ Mrs. Chas. Benedict called bet. 10\+ 11 — Ferguson came along about noon — I worked on drawing for Uncle W. — a Bank Building — Photos have been sent me, but the points of sight so bad that I have to Reconstruct perspective, at much trouble — Home to lunch at 1.30, taking my drawing traps with me, so as to work at home. Shaved my face, all but moustache. P.M. thunderstorms threatening to the North \+ South — At 8.15 went down to Will's in St. Luke's Pl. At 9.15 went to the Tweedy's — there until near 11 o'clk — Magnificent thunder \+ lightning — Home about 11.20 — To bed soon after midnight —
