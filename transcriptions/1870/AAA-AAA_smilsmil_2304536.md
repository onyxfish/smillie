## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 10, 1870

Beautiful morning, but very
warm — a little before 9 — went
to Ravine and worked steadily
all the m'g on large water color
study on Running water, but
got over very little space —
finding a double difficulty in
catching the movement of the
water with water color. To
dinner at one — after dinner
stretched paper & commenced
drawing of Mississippi arms
for A.B.N. Co. Stuck to it until
6 o'clk — then a short walk
alone — tea late — evening
as usual — chattering and
laughing — half past eleven
when I got to bed — Weather
very dubious for the camping
party to-morrow —

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 11, 1870

Early m'g, not promising, but
at 9 o'clk, Mr. Treedy, Belle, Mary,
Florence, Jenny, Sister Kate & Charlie
got off for Camp on Upper Ausable
Lake — m'g improved into a beautiful
but warm day. I went down to
my work on large water color
drawing, hoping to finish it, but
altho' I worked closely until
1 o'clk, I could not do it. Then
home to dinner — P.M. worked
on Mississippi arms, getting
it finished — worked until
tea, shortly after 6. Ev'g
glorious — cloudless & full
moon — wish I was on the
lake, with the party. Got
to bed at 10 o'clk.
