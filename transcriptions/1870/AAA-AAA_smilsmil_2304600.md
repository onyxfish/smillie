## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 28 1870

Up about 9 o'clk, after a miserable night. Got to Studio about 10, 15 — Day dark \+ snowing a moist snow all day — I felt so much under the weather that practically nothing was accomplished beyond stretching a piece of paper for my large Water color drawing — At 2 prepared my lunch \+ eat it — Most of the P.M. was spent on my back on the lounge —
Home at 6, 30 o'clk — Snow stopped — Letter from Uncle Willie enclosing draft for $125.00 — Evng made to Uncle W. and Read Atlantic —
To bed at 10 45 and lay awake until abt. 12 \+ 7 o clk.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 29 1870

[illegible] overcast \+ cold. after part day, clear \+ still cold. About [illegible] o'clk — went to Dickel's Riding Sch with my Charcoal drawing — Two of the bro? D. criticised my horse — then to Studio about 10, 15 and (5th day) Traced first the man in the horse \+ made a separate and corrected drawing of him, on the tracing paper — then traced the horse alone — Reversing \+ working on both sides of the tracing paper. [illegible] all day — Home to lunch at 1, 20 back at 2 20 — At dusk, Wenzler came in — New Mr. \+ Col. Cutt — visited until near [illegible] o'clk. — Home to tea at 6, 20 — Evng at home nursing myself as I feel "played out" — Read Grey's Anatomy \+ made out my list of New Year's calls — Got to bed at 11 —
