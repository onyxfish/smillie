## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 6, 1870

A persistently showery day —
Got to Studio at 9 o'clk, but
used up with "Spring fever" —
so that I am unable to do
anything — I tried to make a
drawing from "Latta Charles", but
she was not good enough — at
12.30 went to Kneidlars — then
home to lunch + back to Studio
at 2.20 — Worked on tinting
on subject for Mr. Jno. Whitney
but did very little — J.C. Nicoll
called between 5 + 6 o'clk —
Home to tea at 7 — Eve'g at
8.30 called on Kate Fessenden
Had a pleasant visit until
10.20 — Gave her a little painting
Home + to bed about 11.15

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 7, 1870

Charming Spring m'g. Clouded
p.m. + eve'g. Got to Studio at
8.45 + finished outlining 20×24
for Mr. Jno. Whitney — at 10.30 started
for A.B.N. Co. to meet a Mr. Walthing-
ham — but he did not come — then
to Mr. Pate's — then Rode home to
lunch at 1 o'clk — back to Studio
at 2 — Young Wells called with
some of his drawings — I finished
up: "A dream of the Adirondacks"
7×12 — "Mr. Lafayette in Oct." 
10×18 — + "Lake Mahkeenack"
14×24 — Home to tea at 7 —
Eve'g with Jno. at the Tweedy's
in Waverly Place — Jenny
Tweedy there — Home at 11.45
To bed at 12 —
