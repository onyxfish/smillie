## Left Page

### July, Monday, 11, 1870

Feeling very slim, Unable to work at my drawing, So made notes to Snedecor \+ Verplank — at 10 went to Studio — arranged a little \+ home about 11.30 — Tried again to work — dinner at 1 — at 2 got to work and worked on locomotive until 3.30 then went to Snedecor's Putnam's, Schmidts, Selleck's Mc. Burney \+ Studio — there until 7.15 — then home to tea — after tea, called on Miss Percy — out — back \+ at work making up accts until 10 o'clk when I went to bed — Soft Rain at night —

About 6 o'clk A.M. called on Mr. Francis Capcutt to congratulate him upon the success of surgical operation on tumor on his eye — Saw him for 15 or 20 minutes —

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday, 12, 1870

Up about daylight with more trouble — pain \+ action of the bowels after a very uneasy night. The result of trying to arrange my money matters — Spent the day in graceful yielding to the situation — doing nothing and, I may say, hoping nothing. About noon, Neuwkon the young Russian called — I saw him — as, by the way, I saw Belle Treacy when she called about 10 or 11 o'clk, a.m. Slept during part of p.m. — very hot about 6 o'clk — fine thunder showers — Sweet Father and mother in my room talking —
