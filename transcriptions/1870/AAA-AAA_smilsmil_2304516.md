## Left Page

### July, Friday 1, 1870

Remarkable change in the weather \- wind from N. \+ E. \- sky overcast with sea scud \- B[illegible] at 7 \- go \+ S. Ril[illegible] to the city in 8 o'clk. boat getting to foot of Cedar S[t] \- ab[ou]t 9.30 \- went to A.B.N. Co \- then to Jersey City R.R. depot \+ ketched them for an hour \- back to A.B.N. Co called on W. Frothingham \- left watch with Hyde Bros then home at 12.30 \- after lunch took a nap \+ then commenced work on locomotive drawing (5 day) worked until after 6 \- then tea \- at 8 called on Rockwell Photo[illegible] \- then to Bur[illegible]groff's \- Ril[illegible] and wrote letter to Meta Gavit Adams \- got to bed about 11 \-

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 2, 1870

Overcast \+ very cool \- M[illegible] until after 10 o'clk \- getting wardrobe in order for visit to the Tiffany's this P.M. \- Then set to work on locomotive drawing, finishing Roof and putting in figures. Worked until near 1 o'clk \- then lunch \- after that dressing \+ putting things away until 3 o'clk, whe[n] Geo and I started for foot of Vesey S[t] \+ took boat for Irvington \- getting here about 5.30 \- walked to the house with Louis \- dinner at 6 \- Mr. \+ Mrs. Stockwell also visiting here \- Ev[en]g spent walking on the piazza with Misses Perkins and Tiffany \- got to bed about 11 o'clk \- night almost cold \+ slept soundly \-
