## Left Page

### May Saturday, 28 1870

Got to the studio about 10 a.m. feeling so much out of sorts that I did not try to work but went off to the Y.M.C.A. studios. Called on Gie, my aut, Wm Hart and Gifford, Wood \+ Kensett, out. Then to N.A. of D. \+ to Schmiett's new cabinet warerooms \+ Illecks gilding shop. Back to studio at 12.30 — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Heavy dash of Rain. Neuribow, the Russian made a long call. I worked in charcoal on design for World Ins. Co. until 6.30 then home to tea — Eveng writing notes to Gifford, Breaker and Reinhart — To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### May Sunday, 29 1870

Overcast \+ threatening Rain — Must went to Dr. Hastings \+ heard an excellent discourse — Text Hebrews 2° Ch. 9" v. At 12.30 with Charl, started for Uncle a's — Heavy Rain came on while in cars — had to walk through \+ to his house. Big dinner — After dinner, dry enough to walk about near the house — at 5 o'clk. home with Charl — again caught in the Rain. Eveng so used up with headache that I did nothing. To bed at 10.45 —
