## Left Page

### June, Monday 13, 1870

Miserable easterly wind \- muggy \+
chilly \- Quite a dash of Rain between
9 \+ 10 a.m. Got to studio at 9.10 \-
found Ferguson there \- and a long
delightful letter from Emma Ireland
Jesse Talbot called \+ then Miss Nellie
Slade with a boquet for me \- Then
Chas Breaker, U.S.N. \+ then Saint
Colman, just in from California.
\+so my m\[illegible\] went that at 1.20 when
I started home for lunch I had
accomplished but little \- back
at 2.45 \+ worked all P.M. on a
little drawing of Bank Bldg for
A.B.N.Co. \- Used up with a cold \-
on lounge from 5 until 6.20 \-
Home to tea at 7.15 \- Eve\[illegible\] wrote
notes to Granniss \+ Townley \+
at 9 Rode down to Tweedys \-
left there at 10 o'clk \+ walked
home with Gel \- Got to bed soon
after 11 \-
Uncle Willie went to Ottawa this
m\[illegible\], \+ Matt to West Point with Uncle [illegible]

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 14, 1870

Muggy \+ depressing to the last degree.
Rain between 10 \+ 11 \- Got to studio
at 9.15 \+ set to work on Uncle Willie's
drawing "Niagara" \+ worked until
1 o'clk, then home to lunch \- went
with Gel to his Room to see his
5 foot picture just laid in \- very
good \- Then to N.a. \[illegible\] D \- for 5 min \-
Then to studio's at 2.45 Day,
clear, hot but breezy \- wind
getting to South \+ west \- worked
on drawing until 6.45 \- finished
\+ home to tea at 7.20 \- Eve\[illegible\]
made paste \+ mounted drawings \-
looked up some Equitable
Ass. business \- Read a little
\+ to bed at 10.45
