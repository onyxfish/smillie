## Left Page

### April, Friday, 22, 1870

Lovely day — Got to studio about
8.45 and at once at work on
20×24 (2½ days) Painted
in trees on R.H. Side. By
12.45 had finished my
"stent" cleaned up and
started home for lunch.
At 2, at Academy of Music
Phil. Rehearsal — Enjoyed
very much my old friend
"Daliam" by Jadé — After
Rehearsal, with Belle &
Many Tweedy, Gel. & I went
to Central Park — there until
6 o'clk — Then home with the
girls — Stopped at Snedicor's
& at Studio & home to tea
at 8 — Evening doing nothing
& to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 23, 1870

Clear & then overcast, two or three
times during the day — Got to
Studio 5 minutes before 9 — and
soon at work on 20×24 (3½ day)
painting in the Rock foreground —
Worked until 1.15 then home
to lunch — back at 2.30 —
& worked until near 6 —
when I got my canvas covered
altogether I think it promises
better than anything I have
yet done. — Home to tea at
7 — Evening wrote a note to
Mr. Geo. Whitney and got
to bed about 10.30
