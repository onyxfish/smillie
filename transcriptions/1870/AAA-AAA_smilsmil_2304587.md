## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 30 1870

Very pleasant day — got to Studio at 9.20 — Felt headachy & unlike attempting to bite in etching, so gave it up for the day & worked on Kansas Arms drawing until 1.30 then home to lunch — back to studio about 3 & until 4 worked on drawing — finishing it — Dr. Yale then came in & we chatted until about 5 when we walked up together, I leaving him at 31st St. — Home & stretched myself on sofa & fell asleep — Woke at 6.30 & took tea — Every, mounted drawing, Read newspaper — called about & got to sofa about 11.30. Uncle Aaron spent part of eveg with us.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 1 1870

Still charming weather — Got to Studio about o'clk — & at work at once biting in etching, (8 days). Closely [illegible] all day, Taking my lunch at the Studio at one o'clk — my biting went very nicely, but I feel timid & do not see as well as I used to — Cleaned off ground at 4 o'clk. & found that in parts it is slightly overbitten & in other parts underbitten, but nothing that I cannot easily remedy — Home to tea at 6. Every feeling tired. Read newspapers & got to bed at 10 o'clk. Joe & Will came up about 9 o'clk. & stayed ½ or ¾ of an hour.
