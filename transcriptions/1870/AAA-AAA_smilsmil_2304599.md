## Left Page

### December Monday, 26 1870

Bleak, cold day. Parker met home in the 10 A.M. train — I got to Studio at 9.20 — Ther. 40°. Made fire \+ got to work on Charcoal drawing — (3d. day) \+ worked until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — back at 2.20 \+ worked as long as I could see. 4.10. About 5 stretched myself on lounges \+ dozed \+ dreamed for an hour — then went to Will's, St. Luke's R. Matt, Kate \+ Uncle Aaron there besides the Brainerd Tribe — a very hot, smoky noisy time. Home with the girls at 9.15 and to bed at 10.45.

Uncle A.V. Suwey \+ Ida Shayer called at Studio —

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 27 1870

Bleat again, getting rather milder towards the eve\[ning\] — got to Studio at 9.15 \+ after a little work on Charcoal drawing (4th day) Set it with fixative — cleared up about the room somewhat and engaged for a time in measuring Water Color drawings \+ inverting frames. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30, \+ after a few minutes went to Silleck's — not in — then to Sauthier's, leaving a drawing \+ order for frame — then to Nehlig's — Showed him my drawing \+ had him \+ Culverhouse criticise it — then to Snedecor's — ordered frame for W.C. drawing — then to see Nicols — not in — then to Knoedler's — met the Riley — then to Silleck's — ordered a frame — then to Studio — fixed fire \+ home at 6.15 — tired \+ sick — Eat a light meal, \+ spent the Rest of the eve\[ning\] in big Chair until 10.30 — to Bed about 11 —
