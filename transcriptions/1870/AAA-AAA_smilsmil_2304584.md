## Left Page

### November Sunday, 20 1870

Superb, cloudless m'f. Went to Dr Hastings & heard him preach
from Matt. 25th Ch. 35th v. "For I was
an hungered" etc — a begging
sermon for the home mission of
the Ch — Went to Uncle a's to
dinner — After dinner a walk
up the new Boulevard with Uncle
a. + charl — Home to tea at 6 —
Eve'f went to hear Rev. Hepworth
— Unitarian, Cor. 34th St. + Park Ave.
Crowded — Subject "Moral courage"
Text, Matt. 26th Ch. 74th v. "Then
began he to curse + swear, saying,
I know not the man" — Can't say
that I was exceedingly pleased
— the music was fine — Home
at 9.30 — Read newspapers
+ got to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### November Monday, 21 1870

Mild, overcast + threatening —
Got to studio at 9.15 — Kindled
fire — Young girl came to be a
model — not 16 yrs old — beautiful
figure + color — a sad picture —
I made an outline drawing from
her to use on Virginia Arms — but
very unsatisfactory to me — At 11.45
started for down town — Got transfer
Uncle W's die — ground etching point —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — Belle +
Mary Tweedy at the house — walked
down to studio with them at 2.15 —
P.M. Worked some on drawing of
Kansas arms correcting drawing of
horses etc — + fixing points for etching —
Home to tea at 6 — at 8.30 P.M. went
to Private view, Fall. Ex. Stu. N.A. of D. —
a very poor affair — Spent the eve'f
pleasantly enough, mainly with
the Irelands + Tweedys — Walked
home with Belle T — at 11 — then
home + to bed at 12 o'clk.
