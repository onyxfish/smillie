## Left Page

### December Saturday, 24 1870.

Very clear, very cold, very windy
\+ consequently, very dirty. Got
to studio soon after 9 and saw
at once changes to be made in
my charcoal sketch of horses —
changing materially proportions
\+ action — At it until 1 when I
went home to dinner — back
at 2.30 — At 3 went to 3d Perspective
lesson by Brevoort — At 5 went
to Tuscalei's to look for Chromo
for Mrs. Wm Mullords — Home to
tea at 6.30 — Between 7+8, Kate
Ret? from Po'keepsie accompanied
by Mr. Ed Parker who visits with
us over Sunday — worked until
11 o'clk on perspective problems
\+ to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 25 1870.

Beautiful day, but very windy.
Up late — Kept late \+ to church
at Hastings's late, so that I did
not hear his text — Before dinner
wrote a note to Mrs. Milford —
After dinner "lay off" on sofa —
about 5.30 went to 205 8th Ave.
to hunt up a widow woman — Mrs.
Sarah Harris, who begged of me on
Fifth Ave. last night — Found
her in a miserable garret \+
apparently very poor — Home
to tea at 6.30 — Evening
went to Madison Ave Baptist
Ch. — Elder preached a sermon
parts of which I liked \+ parts not.
The Sneeders there — Charlie \+ I
went home with them — Home
at 11 o'clk — I sat \+ Read until
12 — to bed at 12.30
