## Left Page

### August, Friday 12, 1870

Cloudy m'f which culminin in a very heavy thunder storm
with torrents of Rain — Almost
used up with a headache —
Touched up some points in Ann
drawing, mate a letter to Will
& got all nicely done up to send
by mail — went to bed with my
headache a little before dinner
then up — Afternoon, glorious.
mate some in letter to Minna
Ireland — about 5 o'clk, walk'd
in towards Ausable Lake a mile
or two, through to the River & down
that way — got into a hornets nest
& badly stung. Took counsel of
my legs and they did all that
could be expected of them —
in at 7 without any hat — Tea —
went finished letter & Minm
& to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 13, 1870

Rather a dismal m'g — wrote a
note to Oscar Ireland & fussed around
until I made up my mind that I could
do nothing out of doors — About 10 o'clc
Orlando Beede came down for the team
our party coming out from camp — So at
10.30 got & I started in — got to lower
Lake at 12.20 — Rowed to upper end,
met all coming down but Charl, Reb —
to lower end — lunched at Shurtleffs
cam & about 3 o'clc, started home —
for a mile & a half or more, Belle
& Mary Tneedy, Geo & I followed
Gill brook & were much delighted.
It did not rain altho' it tried
very hard — went into the woods
& got my hat — in about 5.30
bath & Rub down & felt like a
brick — Heavy dinner — Evening
every body jolly, everything con-
sidered — To bed at 10 —
