## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 4, 1870

Fine day. got to Studio at 9. 5 — Fire out — had to Re-kindle it \+ then to work on drawing (6th day) working over middle distance and onto foreground. Got at Studio until 1 o'clt — Belli \+ Mary Tweedy \+ Clara Dunham called. — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.20 \+ worked as long as I could see — Home to tea at 6 — Evef at 7.30. Monthly M.C. meeting at Burling's Studio — There until 10.30 then home \+ to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 5, 1870

Cold, windy day. Got to Studio at 9.10 \+ at work on drawing (7th day). going over middle distance \+ foreground trees. Young Haven came in about noon — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.20 — Worked over foreground Rocks getting the whole drawing laid in at last — At 5.30 went to Snedicor's to see about my frame — home at 6.45 — Evef writing french Trans. \+ notes to Consort and Jounley — Got to bed about 10.30
