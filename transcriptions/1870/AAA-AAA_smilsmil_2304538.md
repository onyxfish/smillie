## Left Page

### August Sunday, 14 1870.

Delicious day, altho' varying.
All up late and late ok[illegible].
At 10, Mary Belle Jenny & Mr.
Treedy, Geo. & I went to Potters
for letters — a visit there with
Miss Noble — a batch of papers
& letter for Kate from Matt —
Back at noon — late lunch —
Sitting about until 4 o'clk —
Then, with Mary J. — went to
the Ravine & wrote letters to
Father & Saml. Coleman —
back at 6 — dinner later —
then a walk on the hill —
Welf wrote a note to Emma
Beach — Charl am'd. from
Camps about 7 P.M —
got to bed near 11 —

## Right Page

### August Monday, 15 1870.

M'f, early threatening to be thick
but blew off exquisitely clear and
cool — went to Ravine and worked
until 12 o'clk, getting my large
water color study finished at last.
Then, crossed the stream and
climbed the Rock on the west
side of the Ravine, going along the
top of the cliff through the mede-
tangle of dead wood, to the fall,
then across & to dinner abt 1.30 —
Afternoon went to woods on the
hill & commenced painting on
study of Hemlock Tree Trunks.
Worked until about 5.30 when Misses
Bellet Jenny came & I Ret'd to the
house with them. Welf Charl
drove down to Crawford's with
Jenny, Belle, Mary & Florence — Geo.
and I walked down the Road about
9 o'clk. Met them & walked back
with them — Very dark —     Got
to bed about 10.30 — very cold —
