## Left Page

### March, Thursday 3, 1870

Clear, windy & cold — got to Studio
at 9.15 & at work on "I might —"
(2d day) painting over the
immediate foreground. Mr.
Joius Rondel called — I
worked until 1.20 then home
to lunch and back at 2.20
Finished my painting about
3 o'clk — The Rest of the P.M.
painting in cows on "The
Boquet River at Elizabethtown"
24×40 — my last years academy
picture — Home to tea at
6.45 — Eveñg writing biographical
sketch of father at request
of Mr. Wellcome for Phrenological
Journal — got to bed
about 11.30

## Right Page

### March, Friday 4, 1870

Dark day, snow commencing to
fall about noon. & continuing the
Rest of day & eveñg. got to Studio
at 9.5 — Myf worked on little
painting, Adirondack Lake, about
6×3 in — finishing it. & Belle J's
wooden spoon — ab 1.40 home
to lunch — at 2.30 at Phil.
Rehearsal — met there J. & F.
& Mary Butler — After Rehearsal
met mtr Belle & Mary J — to
Mr. Tom. I reedy's in 17th St. then
home with them — then to Snedecor
then to Studio & home at 7 o'clk
Eveñg wrote note to Mr. J.H.
Wood of Utica — Copied biog.
notes for Mr. Wellcome and
straightened some old acct.
books. getting to bed about
midnight —
