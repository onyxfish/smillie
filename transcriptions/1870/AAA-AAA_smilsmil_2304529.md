## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 27, 1870

M\+f clear. After 6 k\[illegible\] went to work at Water Color study, \+ at it all the m\+f until 1 o'clk. getting in sky, distance \+ part of foliage on R.h. Side. I ask myself the question", "Is it Really worth while to work in Water Colors \- are there any qualities that I cannot get in oil?" Dinner delayed by arrival of party from S. town. Yarrows \+ Woodwards. They eat up our dinner \- At 3 P.m again at Ravine \+ commenced by outlining etc, oil study of Running water \- bet. 5 \+ 6. Took a walk with girl. Tea at 7 \- Every chatting and to bed at 10\- M\+f Rec'd a letter from home for Fathr. \+ a "Harper Mag" from Ch[illegible]

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 28, 1870

Pleasant m\+f, About 9 o'clk got to work on Large Water Color. Kept at it until 12.30 when I hastily packed traps and got in just before a Thunder storm \- Dinner at 1. The Yarrow party in at the Ausable Lakes. Thunder showers all the P.M. Read \+ wrote to Father and a note to S\&F. presenting a little painting \- at 6 took a walk and then tea. About 5 P.M. the Yarrow party came in from the lakes pretty thoroughly soaked. Every a little writing \+ more chatting \- to bed soon after 10 \- [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~6 words] [illegible — ~6 words]
