## Left Page

### January Wednesday, 26 1870

Beautiful day, clean + warm — got to studio at 9 o'clk and all the mrng outlinuing with spte Turp. + brown two canvases 14×24 in. subjects that I expect to paint for Ring Smith one a view of Lake Mahkienac the other a Recollection of the Upper Ausable Lake — At 12 went down town + saw Gavit concerning a drawing — went to lunch with father + back to Studio at 2 o'clk — all the P.M. painting over old study 14×24 painted in '65, near Uncle Willie's, Hyde Park — Changed lines + masses + going over it all, got something better in color — I expect to get something out of it for Ring Smith. Home to tea at 6 — Swell, washed brushes — mounted paper — the etc — Frank Pettit with us all night — To bed at a little after 10 —

## Right Page

### January Thursday, 27 1870

Beautiful mild day, got to studio at 8.50 + to work on a drawing for A.B.N. Co. being the 3d one for Jay Cooke + Co. they not seeming to know exactly what they want, but as I am paid for each one, I cannot object — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.15 + worked as long as I could see, getting this drawing finished. Dennis Pardee called very anxious that I should go with him to see some monstrous fat cattle — Frank Pettit left this mrg. Home to tea at 6 — Swell, mounting drawing + hunting up some water color statistics — got to bed at near Midnight —
