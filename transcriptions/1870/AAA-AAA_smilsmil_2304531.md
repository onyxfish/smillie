## Left Page

### July, Sunday 31, 1870

Charming day — Spent the
mng by the side of the River
writing a letter to Emma Ireland
— back at 1 and lunch soon
after — Afternoon in Rocking
chair at east door, enjoying
breeze, sunlight + news papers
until after 4 o'clk. Then mote
note to Matt — + shorter one to
Charl and another to Robert —
son of the Continental — Dinner
soon after 6 — Everf. Gre and
I walked out — cut walking
sticks — half a dozen of them for
the girls to come — In about
8 — Rest of everf. Chatting +
Reading, to bed ab 10 —

## Right Page

### August, Monday 1, 1870

Clouded, but pleasant day — Got
started on Water Color drawing
at Ravine about 9 o'clk — Rather
late, but kept at it until 10 o'cl
getting more hopeful + interested
— I have Regretted doing it in
water colors instead of oils —
Lunch abt o'clk — and then
again to Ravine + commenced
a study in oil of a bit of
Rock and Running water,
got the background of Rock
in + gave up about 5 o'clk —
My watch stopped at 3.30 A.M.
I cannot get it to go and
am lost for time — Everf.
Chatting as usual — Miss
Walters, Gre. + I — and to bed
soon after 10 —
