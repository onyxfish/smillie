## Left Page

### May, Sunday, 22, 1870

Beautiful day \- Mrs, went to Dr. Hastings + heard him preach from Hebrews 6th ch. 11th v. his theme being the "full assurance of hope" \- At 12.45 go + I started for Uncle a's \- got there just after they commenced dinner \- After dinner, sauntering around \- Cha't. Cornish + Son in law called \- tea about 6 \- then out again until after 8 o'clk with Miss Porter, I sat on the piazza until near 10 \- then in and to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### May, Monday, 23, 1870

Up at 6.30. Bkfst at 7.30 \- Wf a little thick \- took 8.19 train \+ got to Studio at 8.50 \- Set to work Repainting again sky on "Lake Placid" 12×22 \- (6th day) about 11 o'clk, a spatter of Rain, about noon, a young Russian, Mr. Nenuskow, called with a note of introduction from the sister Pierce \- now in Rome \- Home to lunch at 2 \- back at 3 \- worked all P.M. \- getting sky + distant mts Repainted \- Mrs. Dinsmore called on \+ eve. \+ gave me an invitation to visit her at Staatsburgh \- Home at 7 \- Eve't at 8.30 called on Miss Annie Tiffany, she goes to Irvington to-morrow \- Home at 10. Fanny + Will Kimel all there walked home with them and then to Studio for a bundle \- Home at 11 and to bed at 12.
