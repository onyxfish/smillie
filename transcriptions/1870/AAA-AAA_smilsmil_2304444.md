## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 1 1870

Clear, crisp day, got to studio
ab't 9 o'clk. & commenced painting
in 14×24 in. "Lake Mahteeenac" —
Alfred Jones surprised me
with a call — Miss Annie Tiffany
proposed that I take her and
miss Goddard as pupils —
Miss Thomas (J. D. a Ward's
niece) & a lady friend called —
Home also Craig & Drumm —
Davenport — Home to lunch
at 1,30 back at 2,30. Drew from
6' National Bank $4.00 — and
deposited it in 3d ave. Savings
Bank — Water Color Money —
P.M. Painted in rest of sky
& distance — Home to tea at
6,30 — Ev'g Annual meeting
Water Color Soc'ty in Miolts
& Darnas. Studio — Wm. Harb-
etded Pres't, Nicoll, Sec't. — I was
Re-elected Treas — Home
ab't 11,30 & to bed ab't
midnight.

## Right Page

### February Wednesday, 2 1870

Overcast M'f got to Studio
ab't 9, 5 & continued painting
in 14×24 (2d day) — Home
to lunch at 1,30 back at 2,15
P.M. Mr. Eliz. Murry called
& spent near an hour of my
time talking about her elf-
& just prevented my
getting the whole of painting
laid in — Home to tea
at 6,30 — Winter, a friend
of Charl's — to tea — Ev'g
I spent until after
11 — writing to Darley, Van-
ungen, Newman & Falconer
& fixing up Water Color
accts. Got to bed at
near midnight, very
tired.
