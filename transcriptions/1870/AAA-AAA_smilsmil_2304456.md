## Left Page

### February Friday, 25 1870.

Clear, cold, windy and dirty —
got to Studio at 8.50 - about 9.30
Mr. Frank Scott came along and
I was until 10.30 making changes
to please him on drawing on
wood - Then he paid me $35.00 and
left. I set to work on sketch —
about 3×6 in. Lake bit, afternoon.
I worked until 1.15, then
home to lunch - at 2.30 at
Academy of Music. Phil. Rehears[al]
got back to Studio at 4.+ worked
until 5 — but failed to get what
I had hoped out of the sketch.
Home to tea at 7 + spent
the evely again Reading
Müller's Science of Language —
parts that I can understand
being very interesting to me, but
there is much that is "too much"
for me — got to bed shortly
after 10 —

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 26 1870.

Clear, milder quieter but still
breezy. Got to Studio at 8.45 +
spent ½ an hour or so touching
up little tin sketch for Laura
Hearn — then at Sunlight (17th day)
worked until 12 — then went to
Fred Durard's + made engagement
with him for next Tues. mf — then
up to Y.M.C.A. Bldg calling on Gifford + Eaton — Home to lunch
abt 1.45 — back at 2.30 — worked
until about 4 getting over the
Ravine part L.H. — Robt. Dodge
bothering me — Then on outside
of wooden spoon for Belle Tweedy
working until 5.30 — Home to
tea at 6.30 — Evely Reading
newspaper + Müller until
about 10 o'clk, when I got
to bed — Beard + Launtdin
called at Studio this A.M.
