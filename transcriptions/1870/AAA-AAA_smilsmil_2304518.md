## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 5, 1870

Mf hazy & at one time quite over-
cast — air delicious — Bkfst. a little
before 8 o'clk — Mr. Stockwell left for
NY. mg o'clk train — Mf, until 12
spent in chatting & croquet, except a
short visit that I made to Colman —
Lunch abt 1 and immediately after
Miss Perkins, Miss Annie, Louis, Geo. &
I started out for a drive, I drove
half. then Louis — we went over to the
Nepperhan Valley & after a charming
ride, returned a little before 4 —
Then to Colman's where he, Louis,
Geo. & I rode up to Bierstadt's
& called upon him — no him, but were
shown into his studio & entertained
by a younger sister of his wife's —
Back about 5 — more croquet —
Dinner about 6.30 — Evef. NW. &
Mr. Colman called — we all Ret'd.
with them — Back about 10 — glorious
moonlight night — walked piazza
made lemonade & to bed soon
after 11 —

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 6, 1870

Another hazy, dreamy day — Headache
not enough to make me unwilling to
go home — so Geo. went in 8.57 a.m. train —
I spent the mf alone on piazza
enjoying the view over the River — the
shade and the breeze — Abt 12. m —
Miss Allie invited me to play croquet
with her which I did until lunch
abt, which was served on piazza
to Louis, Miss Perkins, Miss Annie & me.
After lunch a little more croquet & then
Louis, Miss P., Miss I. & I rode down to Hastings
& called on Clara Dunham. Back at 4.30
more croquet with miss allie, the others
gone to depot for Miss Sue Bishop — a bright
pleasant brunette — Miss Allie, Louis & I
at croquet until dinner — Left the table at
dessert to finish the game — Miss Allie & I
beat Louis in a few shots & back to the
table before dessert was removed — Immed-
iately after dinner Louis, Misses B., P. & I. and
I off for Tarrytown — Philomena — give &
take — I caught all — caught Louis by giving
him a bit of advice which he took — on
getting to Tarrytown — my feelings too
much for me — stopped at drug store — all
took soda water — I took a bottle of Mr. Minsh
[illegible] syrups — drank it from Miss I. & his cups —
8.38 train to NY. Home at 9.45, to bed at 11.
