## Left Page

### February Friday, 11 1870

Splendid day — up with a headache — got to studio at 9.30 + set to work on "view above Po'keepsie" 14×24 — (2nd day) for Mr. Pliny Smith — worked over the sky + distance — About 12.30 Uncle Wm Turrell came in — at 1.30 home to lunch — had to go to bed where I stayed until 6 o'clk — got up, took tea + at 8 o'clk went to studio, fixed fire, cleaned up + abg o'clk went to N.A. of D — back to studio abg 9 + there Reading until near 11, then home + amused myself writing to Dr. Lewis + Reading until bet. 1 + 2 o'clk A.M. when I went to bed.

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 12 1870

Dark mf — overcast until about 10 clk — then cleared warm + sunny, everything afoat — well clean, cold moonlight — got to studio abg 9.15 — To work on 14×24 (3rd day) painting mr middle distance — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.20 + worked until about 8.30 when visitors began to arrive — Tod munter 2 Misses Harvey + another lady came first — then Miss Woodworth then Dr. Wm Thompson + wife + then it was blind man's holiday — Home to tea at 6.45 — at 8 went to 259, 24th ave to call on Mrs. Harper — out — then to Emma Ireland's + there until about 11 o'clk — Home + Read until shortly after 12 — then to bed —
