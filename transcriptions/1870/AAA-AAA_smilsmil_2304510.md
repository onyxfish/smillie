## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 15, 1870

Charming day, clear, very warm but breezy \- Mf, wrote a note to Saml. Coleman \- Took Niagara drawing to Mr Hinshelwood's then went to Hudson R. R.R., 30th St, Saw Mr. Burdett \+ made arrangements to get photo of engine \- then Rode down to Anthony's, having to get out of the 'bus to stop a runaway horse in 9th ave. \- From a's walked up to Snedicor's \- then to Rockwood's \- then to Rehm's \+ had hair cut \- then at 12.20, to studio \- home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2.30 and worked on little painting for Meta Adams \- finishing it \- About 6, very heavy thunderstorm from S.E. \- Home at 7.15 ever more thunder storms \- at 8 o'clk went to Rockwood's in 43d St. nat in \- chatting \+ writing note to Mr. \+ Mr. Chas Benedict \- To bed about 10.15

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 16, 1870

Overcast, muggy and thunderous. got to studio at 9.15 and to work on 12×22 \- "Lake Placid from Whiteface" \- (9th day) Scumbled \+ thin painted over sky \+ distance \- Bet. 10 \+ 11 o'clk Mr. Enoch Lewis in May \+ a young lady called \- Home to lunch abt. \- Uncle Ch. \+ Tunney \+ Matt there just in from West Point. \- Back to studio at 2.30 \+ worked until after 5, getting over the entire painting \- It is not as good as I had hoped to get it \- at 6, went into the Square to listen to the music \- there until 7.15 then called on Mr. Frances Colpcutt \+ left card. \- Home to tea at 7.30 \- Mr. Chas. Breaker called \- Stayed until 10.40, got to bed a few minutes after 11 \-
