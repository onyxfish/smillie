## Left Page

### February Thursday, 3 1870.

Pleasant day, but windy. Got to Studio at 9.45 and painted in remaining corner of 14 × 24 "Late Mahkeenac". Then set to work on large "Twilight" (9½ × [illegible]) Repainting the sky — Got it much grayer + somewhat cooler. This I think now in a better condition than before for the processes I intend using. Home to lunch at 1.45, back at 2.30 + worked as long as I could see — Interrupted by a call from Robt. Dodge — washed brushes + put things to right — + home at 6.45 — Found a note informing me that Fan Mulford was at the St. Nicholas — There at 8.30 — Wm. Jessup + wife + "Vess" + Vaney M. — there — Stayed until 10.30 — home at 11 and to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### February Friday, 4 1870.

Got to Studio at 9.10 — feeling tired + unwilling to work. altho the day is a fine cold, windy + wintry one — Spent some time kicking up + sweeping around. The outlining in brown for a while on 20 × 24. (Mr. Geo. Whitney's) at 12.45 home to lunch + at 1.30 went with Matt + Kate to Phil. Reh. — a tremendous crush — got in and saved seats for all — Ferg. + Miss Gibbs among the number. Prince Arthur in Doremus's box — Orchestra opened with "God Save the Queen" — Spohr's Symphony splendidly given — after Rehearsal, went to Snedicor's then to Tneedy's — there met George — Back to Studio at 6 — there an hour, then home — Sveuf wrote a note to Annie Tiffany + some French Translation — To bed about 10.45.
