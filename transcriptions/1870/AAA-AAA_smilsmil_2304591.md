## Left Page

### December Thursday, 8 1870

Cloudy, blustering day — Superb night — Got to studio at 9.20 and to work on die (13½ days) graven + dry point — until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — back at 2.30 + spent the afternoon burnishing — until 4 o'clk — At 4.30 started for Coffee's — meeting Joe (Will's wife) at the door — went with her to 5th Ave Hotel — then on to C's — Then to Brooks Bros — then to the Tweedys to say that owing to boil on my face, I could not go with them to the Tomy's to-night — then to Snedecor's — then to studio — then home at 7 — tea at 7.30 — Eve'g worked on drawing of Alabama Arms until 10.30 and to bed about a quarter before 12 —

## Right Page

### December Friday, 9 1870

Superb day — cloudless, breezy and cold — Got down town at 9.30 — had a [illegible] of die — got $45.00 for drawing at Kansas Arms + back to studio at 11.15 — worked until 1 o'clk on die (14 days) with graver emphasizing the architecture on the shaded side — Home to lunch at 1.15 — At Phil Rehearsal at 2.30 — not altogether in trim to enjoy the music — After Rehearsal went with Belle + Mary J — to the Eclectic Library — then to Knedecor's then with them home — then to Studio + fixed fire — great pain in my stomach — Rose home at 6.15 + stretched in my big chair until about 8 o'clk — then eat a little — Read a couple of lectures on "Structure of Animal Life" by Agassiz and to bed at 10.45
