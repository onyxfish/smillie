## Left Page

### June, Saturday, 11, 1870.

more Rain + a miserable headache. Found that my Insurance
(Life, Equitable.) premium payable 1st of last May — still unpaid —
So I had to go down + have the
matter settled. Stopped at A.B.N.
Co. + left new design for World
Ins. Co. then home at 11.45
and to bed where I stayed
until after 9 P.M. Then up —
had something to eat + in
my big chair until 4 a.m.
when I went to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday, 12, 1870.

Up at 8 am, feeling very weak
in the knees. MF went to
D. Hastings + heard an admirable sermon from the
text John 4th Ch, 32d v, "I have
meat to eat that ye know not
of." Got up to Uncle C's at 7.30
quiet dinner with Jamey + Uncle
a— strawberries + cherries. The
day perfectly charming at
3.45 went out Riding with
Uncle C. King's bridge + around
by Jerome Park, McComb's Dam
bridge, 8th ave, 125th St + back at 6 P.M.
Tea at 6.30. Miss Porter + Yng. Dowd.
added to our party — soon after 8.
with Miss P. + Yng. D. started for home
from 72d St. + 8th ave walked through the
Park to 59th St + 6th ave, enjoying the
moonlight. They got into car + I
walked home at 10.15 got to bed
at midnight —
