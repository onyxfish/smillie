## Left Page

### January Tuesday, 18 1870

Weather improved, cold + cloudy
until P.M. when it cleared. —
Got to Studio about 9.30. Father
there. — Mr. Phelps could not be there
on acct. of another engagement.
Worked on drawing for A.B.N.Co.
(2d day) until 12.30 when I
went over to N.A. of D. — with Coleman
+ Burling worked at hanging
drawings for an hour. Then
home to lunch + back to Studio
at 2.20. + Worked the Rest of
P.M. on drawing. — Kate Fessenden
called — also Miss Willitts and
Mr. Camps. — Home to tea at
6.15. At 7.30 at N.A. of D. and
with Burling + C.P. Cranch
worked at hanging until
10 o'clk. — Home at 10.30 very
tired + to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### January Wednesday, 19 1870

Birthday. Clear + cold. Got
to Studio at 9.5 + worked on
drawing for A.B.N.Co. (3d day)
until 1.15 then home to lunch
+ back at 2.15 and at it
until about 5 o'clk. — When
I finished the drawing. —
Home about 5.30 very tired.
Sketched myself on my bed
until 7 o'clk. — then Tea and
at 7.40 at N.A. of D. to hang
drawings. There until
8.45. Then home and at
9.5 went to the Rogers' at
106 W 42d St. — a jolly and
little musical party. — home
at 10.30. — mounted drawing
+ got to bed soon after 11 —
very nearly "played out".
