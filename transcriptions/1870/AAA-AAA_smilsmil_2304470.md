## Left Page

### March, Friday, 25, 1870

Charming day — got to Studio at 8.45 + to work on "Twilight" — on central group of old pines — finished them up in about an hour + then set to work on little patchwork drawing for C.B.N. Co — old figure die of Casilearis — put back a ground of Farmstead + morning — finished it by 1 o'clk — Home to lunch at 1.30 — at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal — lat in Upper gallery + tried to understand Listz's Inferno — After Rehearsal, went off with the Threedys — Belle, Mary, Jenny — Florence + Clara Dunham — went to George's Studio — then up the ave to 40th S. — then to Waverley Pl. Then to Snedicor's — then to Studio + at 7.30 home to tea — Slept in my big chair. To bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### March, Saturday, 26, 1870

Wind from N.E. + not altogether agreeable. Got to studio at 9.5 + to work on Mt. Lafayette in Oct. 10×13 in. (3¼ days) Scumbled very slightly the sky and all the distance + brightened the foreground — + now call it finished — this above my average — but poor enough. At 12 went to Sauthier's stopping at Knoedler's + Schaus's — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — worked on "Dream of the Adirondacks" 7×12 (2½ days) — Scumbling + cooling tints generally — this too I call "done" — worked on little Twilight sketch 3×6 in — + at 4.30 with Ferguson went over to Y.M.C.A. Studio Reception. Saw lots of people + had a good time — walked home with Belle + Mary Threeды — back to Studio at 6.45 — Home to tea at 7.30 — Slept a little + sketching + looking over die pefs. with Father + to bed about 10.45
