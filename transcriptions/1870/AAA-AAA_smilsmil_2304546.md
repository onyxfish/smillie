## Left Page

### August Tuesday, 30 1870.

Up early after a night of solid sleep. Mng fresh, breezy \+ very clear \- It was arranged that D. S. should take Orlando \+ go up Mt. Marcy \- He got off at 8.30 \- traps packed \+ Reede's Man Tom. Mr. B \+ Mr. J. took most of them over the camp \- got men to lower lake to work \- I painted on study of camps until 12 \- Then lunched \+ all left camps at 1 ocl each one expressing great Regret \- got over the camp nicely \+ just about getting into the boat when D. S. hailed us, Jubilant \- made ascent of Mt. Marcy from upper lake in 2 hours \+ 20 m \- saw White Mts 120 miles east \- got to lower end about 2.30 Smith Reede's team there \- Our Shanty not quite finished \- with Belle, Florence \- Chas. J. \+ Chas. B. went to tops of Rainbow Fall \- all started for home at 4.30 My aunt me \- thro' Shurtliffe \- Hall's for \+ Mrs. J tow. In Shurtliffe's shanty. Turned in about 9.30 \- Night very cold \+ windy \-

## Right Page

### August Wednesday, 31 1870.

Up about 6.30 \- had an irregular bkfst at 7 \- Some time fixing about shanty \+ at 9.30 went to the tops of Rainbow fall \- Day windy and without a cloud until 11 \- then it began suddenly to bank up \- I worked in oil colors, as hard as I was able, with very severe headache until 1.30 \- then cooked lunch and worked on until about 3 \- Slight shower \- packed traps \+ back to shanty \- worked abit until tea time getting things quite comfortable. The girls at Reede's had sent up a lot of provisions and some peaches. Enjoyed my every meal and ate about 8 o'clk. Geo \+ I called at the Shurtliffe camp where we spent a pleasant hour \- then back to our Shanty \-
