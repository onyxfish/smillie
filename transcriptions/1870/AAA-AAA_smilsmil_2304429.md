## Left Page

### January Sunday, 2 1870

In bed again until 10 o'clk when I got up not feeling
very bright. The day so much
worse than yesterday that
I could not think of our bad
weather then but as pleasant
a blessing as compared
with this — Rains in driving,
gusty showers — afternoon
mind shifted, clouds broke
away to break up again at
night with winds a young
hurricane — About 3 o'clc.,
went to bed, there until near
6 — then tea — Spent some
writing, listening to Father
Read a sermon and to
bed at 11 —

Dr. Hastings — gave as the
text for the year — "God
Knoweth" —

## Right Page

### January Monday, 3 1870

Got to Studio at 9.30 — built
fire and at drawing (5th day)
George spent an hour or two
doing a little work on a
W.C. drawing — Rec'd letters
from Dr. F. W. Lewis & Mr.
Frank Scott — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& worked rest of P.M. — on
drawing over the lake and
middle distance — Mr. Bul
Jessups & her sister Miss Imbridge
called & about half an hour later,
Nettie and a [illegible] lady friend
made a pleasant call — Home
to tea at 6 — At 7.30 called
on Mr. Greatforest at 55 W. 11. [illegible]
& at 8.15 on Miss Annie Cook
& Miss Howe at 192 W. 10. [illegible] —
Left there at 10.15, walked home
stopping at Studio — Home at
11 & to bed soon after —
