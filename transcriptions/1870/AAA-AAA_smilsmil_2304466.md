## Left Page

### March, Thursday 17, 1870

In bed all day with a mild nervous headache — got up
about 8 o'clk. P.M — took
something to eat som after.
Then sat rolled up in my
big chair until 3 o'clk A.M.
when I went to bed. and
got to sleep about 4. A.M —
Slight snow storm during
the night —

## Right Page

### March, Friday 18, 1870

Fine day — Feeling very
ragged after yesterdays ex-
perience — Got to Studio about
10 o'clk & found under my door
letter from Mr. I. H. Wood with
draft for $75.00 for little painting
"Whiteface & Lake Placid" — Unfit
for work. visited Jas. Hart & O'Donné
& at 1 went to Snedicor's & Lawthen's
Home to lunch at 1 — wrote letters
to Mr. Wood & Mrs. Wellcome & at
2.30 went to Phil. Rehearsal. Much
disappointed in Sisto's "Inferno
& Purgatorio" — with Belle & Mary [med.]
Clara Dunham & Geo went over &
through the Rutherfurd Model
Building 18½ — then to Geo.'s Studio
then with Belle & Mary home — then to
Snedicor's — there to Sommerville Gallery
then to Studio & at 8.10 home to tea.
Mother home from Uncle [illegible] —
Evelf did little but chat and
read news paper and at
11.15 to bed —
