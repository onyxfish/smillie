## Left Page

### October, Saturday, 1, 1870

About 8 o'clk a.m. — desperate effort
to clear up, but it was a dismal
failure — fine, drizzly Rain again
most of the mng & afternoon —
worked with water colors making
a study of the Blackberry stalks
that I outlined yesterday p.m. —
about noon went out & took a
walk — afternoon, worked as
long as I could see on blk.berries
then afterwards had a twilight
chat with Miss Newman, upon
subjects that neither of us under-
stood very well — Tea — a little
Reading of Artemus Ward — the
ladies Retired early and I had
a stupid time dozing & nearly
breaking my neck in the Rocking
chair until at about 9.30 — when
I went to bed & lay awake for
some time.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday, 2, 1870

Up at 6 — too early — Up again at
6.30 — made cup of Tea & eat two
crackers & at 7 o'clk. started for
Dibble's — got there at 7.45. got mail
of this morning — enjoyed walk
immensely — back at 9 o'clk —
Took help & letters from Father, Kate
& Uncle Willie — Read papers until
11 o'clk — then wrote to many friends
until lunch time — finished letters
at 3 o'clk — then went out, crossed
the River & climbed the "Cobble"
on the other side — very hard work
covered with muck — back at 5 —
wrote to Uncle M.J.J — late dinner
some Chatting — at 8. left to our-
selves — So I wrote to Sister
Matt — Evening all overcast —
Got to bed about 11.45
