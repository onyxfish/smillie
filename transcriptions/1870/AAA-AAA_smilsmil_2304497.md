## Left Page

### May Friday, 20 1870

Heavy thunderstorm bet. 5 & 6 o'clk
A.M. Rlcp. at 7 — good byes all
said in the tail end of one
shower & with another threatening
Belle, Mary, Jenny, Geo. and I
got off for home in the 8.20 a.m.
train. Into New York at 9.20
all took Bdway car — wives got
out at Waverly R. Geo. & I
went on — got to Studio shortly
after 10 — Set to work finishing
drawing of Benedict's house
that I commenced yesterday.
Home to lunch abt — back
at 2.15 — worked until 6 on
drawing — Finished it — Home
to tea at 7 — Eve'g hanging
my pictures in 2nd story
back Room — Got to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 21 1870

Muggy m'g and feeling headachy
got to Studio at 9.15 — About
10 Miss Corlies & a lady friend
called. About 10.30 sat for
Augero to paint a head in
one of his pictures — an hour.
Then preparing some paintings
for next painting — scraping —
sandpapering etc. Home
to lunch a little before 2 —
back at 2.45 — Joe (Will's wife)
called between 3 & 4. Painted
over lower sky & landscape
of "Sunset" 9×12. improving
it very much, I'm sure —
Between 5 & 6. Heavy thunder
storm. Home about 6.30 — More
thunder storms during the
eve'g. Read & wrote letter
to Mr. Main. To bed at
11 o'clk.
