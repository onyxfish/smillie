## Left Page

### July, Monday, 13, 1874

More rain, spiteful & heavy. Brkfst before 8 — then out to studio. Cotrell & his man there and active operations commenced. Before 11 they left. I worked awhile, starting "Florida Arms". Dinner abt 1 — C. & man again at work — I joined & all faithfully at it until 6 — The floor was cleared after much work, & putting down the "fining" for oak and walnut floor commenced. Tea at 6 — some chatting with our boarders — then a walk with Kate Riley and Bessie Riley — then with Susie, Ida, Uncle A, & Apl — all weighed — &c &c — Rts to W.J.T's & Romped on piazza until after 9 — Then home, read "Trib" & to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday, 14, 1874

Cloudless & very hot. Cotrell & his young man got to work at 7 o'clk. I breakfasted at 7.30, then out and spent the mfg, sorting & selecting lumber as it was laid down on the floor. Dinner at 1.30 — back again to studio, at same work as morning until 6 o'clk. Very satisfactory progress was made — C. & his man worked faithfully & well. At 6 — tea. — very tired, but dressed & with Gel. went to call on Hettie Searle & Miss Snead — there until 9 o'clk. Joe Williams on "high" affectionate demonstrations to do Mr. Searle — Home & to bed soon after 10.
