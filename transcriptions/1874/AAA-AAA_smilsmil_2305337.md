## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 16 1874

Milder & pleasant day — Got to studio at 8.45 & to work on 14×24 — old Study near Po'keep-sie — trying to make it some-what brighter. At 12.30 went to dinner & got back at 1.30 — worked for awhile on 10×14 "Meadow" & at 3.30 went to Selleck's — then to Y.M.C.A. Bl'g & called on Satterlee, Robbins, Eaton, Mr. Culver & Mr. I[illegible]. Back to studio at 4.30 — Nicole & T.H. Smith there — got over some W.C. business — cleared up & to tea at 6.50 — At 8.45 at 139 W. 22 & called on Bessie Riley — with her aunt Mrs. Abbatt — left at 10.10, walked up & to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 17 1874

Disagreeable day — Storm & clear at intervals. Got to studio at 8.45 and to work on little bit 4×6 in (3rd day) Painted solidly over it. troubled by visitors. To dinner at 12.30 — back at 1.45 — Painted until 4 o'clk — when got little painting finished — Washed up & in to tea at 6.15 — Dressed before tea. At 7.30 walked up to Will's — there until 9 — then walked with A.P.V. to his house — Geo. & Kate there. At 10 walked down to 4[illegible] with Geo. To bed at 11 — [illegible — ~2 lines]
