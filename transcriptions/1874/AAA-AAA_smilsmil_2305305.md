## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 13, 1874

Horrible day — snow, wind, rain & cold as Greenland —
Bkfs. at 8. Ab. studio at 9
but coned not get the place
warm — It was after 10 before
I got to work painting in a
twilight sketch. Took dinner
early & at 1.30 Geo. got away
for Elmira. At 2 again at
studio & worked until after
4, getting sketch finished —
this pretty good for me —
S.C.A. came in about 5, but
it snowed so hard that we
coned not go out to walk —
Tea at 6.15 — Eve'g. packed
& ripp'd. Roll of wall paper
to Charl & wrote a note to
Nicoll — Down town at 8.45 —
then went in to Ben M's
& played whist with him,
Will M. & Fred Crandall
until 10.15 — then home &
to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 14, 1874

Yet another horrible, dark
cold day — After bkfs. went
to P.A. Post's to aw deed — then
to studio & until 11 o'clk. fixing
up bay window & closets so that
all the heat of my stove would
be wasted — Then to work
on little twilight sketch that
I painted sometime ago — a
Recollection of Raquette Lake —
worked until 12.30 — Dinner
abt 1.30 — Sue Holt & Hetty
Searee there. To work again
at 2 & I painted until 4, when
I finished sketch — Washed
up & walked with S. until 6 —
Then tea & after that for awhile
with the Lathrop girls — then down
town — met Ben M. & with him
went to Gus S's — Fred C. & Will
M. there. Whist until 10 — then
home & to bed.
