## Left Page

### June, Monday, 1, 1874

Delightful day — Got to studio
at 10 o'clk — Spent an hour
altering + finishing "Quachos" —
then to work making alterations
on old Quachos drawing, made
in '67 — Tom Mulford came in
at 12.30 went to Knoedler's — but
abt — Miss Sterling came in —
Home to lunch at 1.45. After
lunch, Rode down to Devey's
with coat; stopped at [illegible]
for Mounting board — back to
studio at 3.45 — worked until
7 "Charcoaling" "Drops" for
Palmer — Silva called and
we walked up town together.
Home to tea at 7.30 — During
came in — at 8.30 called
on Miss Burdell, 135 W. 42 —
Home at 10.30 + right to bed

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday, 2, 1874

Remarkable weather — Glorious
Got to studio at 9 o'clk. and
commenced painting "Drops" —
20 × 21 in — oval — Marston came
in — worked steadily until
20, then home to lunch —
back at 2.15 — at 2.45, Miss
Scott + her father came and
I gave her a lesson — very
unsatisfactory — She, so like
nervous + I pre-occupied.
She left about 5 o'clk and
then again I pitched for
my painting. Worked until
after 6 — Home to tea at 7.30
Eve'g wrote to Gifford, Magrath
finished long letter to Sam
Colman. Washed brushes
+ bed at 11.15
