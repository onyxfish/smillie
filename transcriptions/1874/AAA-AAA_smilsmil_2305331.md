## Left Page

### December Friday, 4 1874

Clear beautiful day — got to studio at 8.40 +
to work on "Meadows" —
10×14 — Finished painting
in and at 1 o'clk took
lunch in studio. Soon
after 2 went to Phil. Rub
after that went to N.A.
of D. to see Prof. Welunarth
then to Silleckas for frames
there at 4.30 to studio —
there until 6 — to tea
at 6.30 — surprised to
find father at tea table.
He stayed overnight
with Charl. I wrote
some letters and to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 5 1874

Mild pleasant day — got to
studio at 8.30 — father there — took
a look at things before he went
downtown. I started second
painting on little 4½×6 bits,
worked faithfully on it all
day. to dinner at 12.30 — back
at 1.30 — Painted until 4.30
then Mr. Walter Brown came in
+ we talked water color business
for awhile — to tea at 6.30 —
loafed until after 8 — then
dressed & went to 4.59 N. 34th
S. W. Dallas — to the Woman's
Art Assn Reception to Carl Hubner.
C.H. not there. abt 1 walked
home with Clark Bell — to bed
abt 11.45. [illegible — ~4 lines]
