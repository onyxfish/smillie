## Left Page

### November Sunday, 22 1874

Overcast day. Bkfst at 8:30.
Myf. went to church (Baptist)
& heard Rev. W. Kendrick on
"It is finished". After service
went in & saw Matt. Home
at 1 o'clk. & dinner soon after.
About 3 Phare & I went
into town. Ed. joined us &
we walked out to the Water
works. On our return stopped
at Upper Iron works & saw the 5
o'clk. Run. Back & to dinner
shortly after 6, there was a very
wet & now storm that went
into rain & continued all night.
Evg. a sermon by Channing
was read. There was some [illegible]
& a little sing & at 10:30 I got
to bed.

## Right Page

### November Monday, 23 1874

Horrible storm, beginning with
sleet & winding up with wind
& rain. Bkfst. at 8:30. Tried
to work on water color drawing
but my head troubled me &
finally I spent the mtf on the
sofa. Will, Joe & Minnie arrived
about noon, very much out of
temper. Dinner soon after.
The p.m. spent in wandering
about the house. Took a
catch tea about 6 o'clk. Uncle
Gel. Fred. & Aunt Ollie arrived
about 5 p.m. Evg. Uncle Willie,
Aunts Agnes & Jessie, Minnie &
Belle, Matt, Ed, Mr. Parker
& Mr. & Mrs. Kendrick came &
we had a pleasant & quite
old fashioned evening. Will,
Joe, Minm & Fred stayed all
night with us. Just upon
12 when I got to bed.
