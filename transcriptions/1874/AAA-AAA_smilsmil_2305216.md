## Left Page

### April Sunday, 12 1874

Very clear, cold, windy day —
Mf went to Dr. Hastings —
He preached from Rev. 15.
Ch. 3d. v. first clause — After
service walked with Allie D.
Josie J. & her father, to Mad. Av.
& 78th St. Stayed to dinner — Dr.
Kelsey & Mrs. J's Mother &
cousin there — Left at 4.30
stopped a few moments at
Uncle A's — then a walk in
the Park & home to tea at
6 — Evef snooping on
the sofa until the folks
came in from church —
wrote to Sam. Coleman's
letter until 12 o'clock. then
to bed —

## Right Page

### April Monday, 13 1874

Splendid day — got to studio
at 9.25 — after stopping at Duvalps
& Kneedlers — Kindled fire &
then to work planning &
drawing on Vulcan & Winona —
a number of callers — Louis
Tiffany brought me all of the
[illegible] W.C. books, papers
& funds — Home to lunch at
1.30 — back at 2.40 & worked
until afterts — Dr. Haas
called — also Mrs. Greatrex,
Miss Burdell & others —
Home to tea at 7 — at 8.30
at U.A. of D. & met the Jessups
there & Allie Diven — Home
at 10.30 & to bed at 11.30
