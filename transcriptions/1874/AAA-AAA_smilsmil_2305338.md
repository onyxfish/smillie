## Left Page

### December Friday, 18 1874

Very pleasant day — clear + cold.
Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. + to
work on "Meadow near Beede's"
(5th day.) Worked until 12.30
then to dinner — back at 1.30
+ worked until about 4.15
when I got over it all — S2.
came in for a few minutes.
At 4.30 I went down to Inds
with a painting. Then to
Brook's Bros. back to Studio
at 6.15 — Totia at 7 — back
again to Studio at 8.15 +
worked over accts. until
10 o'clk. then walked up
with Ferguson who had
come in — To bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 19 1874

Delightful day — got to Studio
at 8.45 + commenced painting on
a 10×14 "on the Housatonic River" —
Rubbed it in regardless — went
to dinner at 12.30 — Back at
1.45 — worked for an hour or so
+ got painting all in — then washed
up + at 3.30 geo. + I started for
foot of Liberty St — took 4.30
train to Plainfield + got to the
Jmeedy's about 5.45 — Dinner
about 6.30 — Eveg spent in
giddy conversation + to bed
soon after 11.
