## Left Page

### May Sunday, 24 1874.

Very pleasant — Mrs went
to Dr Hastings's — He preached
from Mark. 15th Ch. 31st V. —
After service, walked up
with the Jessups & dined
with them. — Abt 4 o'clk.
walked up 4th Ave to 98th
St. & inspected the R.R. Tunnel
& works — then down to
44th St — & called on F. H.
Smith — Home to tea at
6.45 — Every, lounging —
To bed at 11.15 —
met, heavy mizzle.

## Right Page

### May Monday, 25 1874.

Very heavy rain with thunder
& lightning. Got to studio at
9.25 — Worked all day on A.B.
N. Co. Italian drawings. At
1.30, lunched with Miss Woodward
& Mrs Hawley. — about 3.30 —
Palmer & Marston & [illegible] &
Theatre came in and talked
of a picture for drop curtain.
Worked until 6.30 — then
from hour washing & cleaning
up. Storm cleared away — at
7.30 walked to F. H. Smith's —
126 E. 44th St — not in — then
Rode up town in Mad. Ave. Cars —
met Mrs Jas. Ingersoll & took her home
No. 21 E. 73d St — then walked up to
Jessup's — 78th St — & Ret'd umbrella.
Home at 9 o'clk — Took tea — mounting
a drawing — Father & Charl went
up to P— this mt. — They Ret'd at
10 P.M — with Aunt Jessie —
— To bed at 11.15 —
