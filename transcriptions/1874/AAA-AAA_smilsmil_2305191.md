## Left Page

### February Thursday, 19 1874.

Overcast & threatening,
nizzling rain beginning at
about 3 P.m. Up late,
suffering from a headache
& did not get to studio until
10.30. Worked awhile on
drawing for A.B.N. Co. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.30 — finished etching [illegible]
"Josh" for Graphic — Several
callers, among others, Dr.
Percy — Home to tea late
every, quite used up
on sofa until 10.30 — then
to bed —

## Right Page

### February Friday, 20 1874.

Overcast, but not unpleasant,
Up late & so under the matter
from headache of yesterday that
I spent the morf at home.
After lunch abt. 1.30 went
to studio — the P.m. spent
doing very little — Home
to tea at 7.15 — Every
Reading — Uncle A. came
in — Got to bed at 10.30
