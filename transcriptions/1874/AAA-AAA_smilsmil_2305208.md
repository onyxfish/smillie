## Left Page

### March Friday, 27 1874

Very pleasant day — got to
studio at 9.20 and fussing
again over Raquette, painting
over a considerable portion
of the water — Home to lunch
at 1.15 — back at 2 — gave my
Phil. rehearsal ticket to Miss
Gilbert — a number of callers in
the P.M. Anna Cook called
& accepted the picture — tried
to clear up — Home to tea at 7.15
horribly tired — back at 8.20
camping party and about 9. in
full costume — Anna — Edith W.
(from Phila.) Fanny, Emma & Sib
Howe (from —) Will K., Gelthed
C. & Fred J. — all very much sur-
prised when they found that a.
C.C. owned the picture — About
10 — Cream aw² — made coffee
& spread cakes — all seemed to
be very good — a sudden shower
about 11 o'clk — Party broke up at
11.50 — I got home at 12.15 and
right to bed, but did not get to
sleep until after 1 o'clk.

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 28 1874

Overcast mf, but cleared
into a charming day — Up
early & got to studio at 9.5
mf, repainted little painting
done sometime ago for Jas. Heavis-
tin wedding — also a painting
"October" belonging to Dodworth —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.30 — finished painting for
Sauthier "Among the Thousand
Islands" — also the little painting
on a shell for J. D. A. Ward —
Rossell got picture for H.A.J.P.
in the afternoon. Home to tea
at 7.30 — Swerf, doing nothing
to bed at 9.30
