## Left Page

### February Monday, 9, 1874

Pleasant but overcast.
Got to studio at 9.45 and kindled fire — cleaned up
— Burling called — so did
several others, so that most
of the morning was lost —
Got started on R.R. cent-
sketch for a B. N. G.
Home to lunch at 2 o'clk.
On my way back, stopped
at E. D. E. Greene's — Got
to studio at 3. Painted
placard for W. C., at 4.30
went to Appleton's with
Montreal Block — Got
$45.00 for it. On my way back
stopped at Lauthier's & [illegible].
Home to tea at 7.30. Matt &
Ed, am? from [illegible]. Eve'g at
9 went to N.A. of D. — Home
at 10.30 & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 10, 1874

Superb day. After bkfst wrote
notes to Will, Sackett — Johnston
of the Mail & Rollson of [illegible] Times.
Got to studio at 10.15 — Set
palette & painted on Matt's
picture "Eve'g in the Bernese".
Home to lunch at 1.30, back
at 2.30 & painted as long as
I could see — finishing it
after a fashion — then Ferguson
came in & stayed until
almost 6. Washed brushes,
cleaned up & home to tea
at 7 o'clk — Eve'g, wrote
notes to Clarence Cook &
Granniss & at 9 went to N.
A. of D. Receipts for the day
$86.50 — the best, so far.
Home at 10.30
To bed at 11.20
