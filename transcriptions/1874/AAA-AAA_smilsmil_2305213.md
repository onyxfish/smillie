## Left Page

### April Monday, 6 1874

Very delightful day — got to
studio at 9.15 — kindled fire
& lost most of the mf. over
Darwin's outline & with callers
at 1. went to Silleck's and to
the N.A. of D. — Saw Richards &
got a couple of invitations to P. V. —
at N.A. every. Home to lunch at
1.45 — back to studio at 2.40 —
P.M. — Charcoaling State arms
of N.Y. for A.B. N. Co. — Worked
until 6.45 — Home to tea at
7.15 — Sweif writing notes
to committeemen for W. C. S. —
So bid at 11 —

## Right Page

### April Tuesday, 7 1874

Disagreeable mf., with some
rain — It was 10.30 when I
got to the studio — At 11, went
with Jas. Hart to N.A. of D. "Var-
nishing Day." Everybody in the
painting way, there — a new
leaf has been turned over and
the system is better than usual.
Did not go home to lunch
until 3 o'clk — at 4, went to
Silleck's — then to studio &
charcoaled for A.B. N. Co. —
for a couple of hours. Took
a nap at lounge & home to
tea at 7.45 — At 9 o'clk.
with Matt & Ed. — went to Uncle
A's — grand party — Supper
at 11.45 — Home at 12.45 — to bed
at — but not to sleep until
after 2 —
