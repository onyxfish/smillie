## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 17 1874.

Strong wind & very heavy rain during p.m. & eveff. Got to studio at 9.30 & worked all the [illegible] on "Race" — About 12.30 — feeling very tired went over to my room & lay down upon the bed, getting a few winks of sleep. Dinner at 1.30 — Stewart & Josie B. [illegible]. After dinner, was betrayed into a game of whist which detained me from studio until after 3 — Very dark & stormy — After 4 wrote for about an hour to Emma [illegible] — then to stable & groomed "Jem." Tea at 6.30 — Chatting with Miss Strawbridge until near 8 — then to Ty. Tyler's to see his cousin Miss Beldon — there until 8.45 — then to P. D. & with Gre. to J. Searle's to bid Josie goodbye & at 9.20 to Faris to bid the Dr. goodbye. Home soon after 10 & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 18 1874.

The rain still continues — and this is the last day of the fair. Got to studio before 9 o'clk — Set to work with water colors to try to do something. "Outlined" entrance to Thousand Islands "Lawrence", & started that in color. Dinner at 12.15 — on acct. of the Fair, continual rain. P.M. worked on a "Sunrise on" Long's Peak, from "near Greeley. Col." Elisha Mulford called. Worked until 5 — then wrote to Van Zandt, Kate Druker & Chave until 6 — then tea — Chatted with Miss Strawbridge until 8. Then down town for mall — Frat Faris there until 10.30 — Played a game of whist in which she & I took every trick — To bed at 11, but not to sleep until 1 o'clk.
