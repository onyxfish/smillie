## Left Page

### May Saturday, 30 1874.

Still charming weather — Called on A. M. Baldwin — did not get to the studio until near 11 o'clk. Then to work on Guachos. Home to lunch at 1.30 — Telegram from Mary — all Right + Willie better — Back to studio at 2.30 + worked on drawing until 7 — Home to tea at 7.30 — Eveʼg, mounted "Guachos" — Read newspapers + to bed at 10 45

## Right Page

### May Sunday, 31 1874.

Delightful day, but very warm — Mʼg, went to Dr. Hastings's and after service walked over to St. Bartholomew's — Mad. ave. + 44th St — Service not over there until 12.40 then walked up 5th ave. Willie met me — got into carriage and drove to Garit's — He is reported much better — then to A. N. S. — There to dinner + until 4.30 — Dick Piarse + Sam [illegible] called on the Ave. met Willis Palmer + Hodges — walked with them up to 72nd St — through the Park + down the ave. Home at 6.30 Eveʼg wrote a letter to Sam Colmour + got to bed at 11 — [illegible] ave — from [illegible] —
