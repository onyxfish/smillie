## Left Page

### April, Thursday 16, 1874

Generally overcast & cooler. Got to studio at 9.30. Had to Kindle fire — Worked on Vulcan until 1 o'clk. Then in to Miss Gilbert's Room where we had a Pic-nic. Back at 2.40 & worked until after 6. Mr. Marten Nicalls [illegible] called — also Dr. Holder. [illegible] museum [illegible] at 6.30 went to Na. [illegible] — Home at 7 — Evening reading news papers & a little writing — To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### April, Friday 17, 1874

Horrible stormy day — Got to studio at 10.30, after stopping at a Telegraph Station & getting sketches of Keys. Relays & batteries — Worked all the rest of the day on corrected outline of Vulcan & Minerva. I get along very slowly, but yet cannot help thinking that I am doing better than I have before — Miss Woodward gave me some lunch abt o'clk. At 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal — the last of the season. Back to studio at 5 — Worked until 6.30 — Home at 6.50 — George arr. direct from Washington between 5 & 6 P.M. Every went to Colosseum & saw Paris by Moonlight — Home at 10.20 & to bed at 11 —
