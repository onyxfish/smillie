## Left Page

### August, Saturday, 8, 1874

Muggy & overcast. Had a very good
night's rest & my neck feels better.
Before bkfst. with C, for a short
time. Bkfst. at 8 — All my time
now given up to studio watching
& directing progress. Did not get to
the stable this mfg as I got too
lame. Dinner at 1.30 — Spent
all the p.m. until 6 in studio —
Then tea — Ever & my neck
very lame again — Went down-
town with Flora S., & Miss Manin.
then back again. Played whist
with Miss Mamie Bogep, Miss Mitchell,
& Mr. Gratz, until 10 o'clk —
To bed soon after —

## Right Page

### August, Sunday, 9, 1874

Very warm — Strong breeze from
W.W. & slightly overcast most of the
day. Attended to horses before bkfst.
at 9 o'clk — Then another turn at
grooming — then a bath — then to
Presbt'n Ch. — where I am late,
but heard concluding portions of
Isaac Riley's sermon drawing
a parallel between the Parable of
the Prodigal Son & the Lord's Prayer.
Walked up home with Flora S.
Dinner at 1.30 — Chatting until
near 3 — then to my Room & finished
letter to Fanny K. & note on 3rd
Mormon letter. Tea at 6. More
chat. Eve'g went to Episc'l Ch. —
Sermon from 1st. 2d. Tim. 4c. 7.48 —
Walked home with Jan M. Then
with her & Miss Smith to Gus's —
I got to my Room soon after 10.
To bed at 11 —
