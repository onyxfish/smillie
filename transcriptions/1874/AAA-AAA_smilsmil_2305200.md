## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 11 1874.

Still very cold & blustering —
Dec. & March combined — Go
to studio at 9.40 — Set palette
& to work on sketch" among
the "Thousand Islands" — Second
painting. Sauthier has promised
to give me $50 & print — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.45 — Painted until 5.15
getting through with a tolerable
satisfactory days work. Washed
brushes, cleaned up & then
on lounge until near 7 —
Home to tea at 7.15 — Ed
down from Po'keepsie —
Dressed & at 9 o'clk. went up
to Mr Jessup's — Madison Ave
& 78th St — a very pleasant comp'y
There until 10.45 — Home at
11.15 & to bed at 11.40 —
a very cold, windy night.

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 12 1874.

Colder, windier & dirtier than
ever — Go to studio at 9.30 —
Set palette & again at work
on "Among the Mts and Islands"
Home to lunch at 11.15 — back
at 2.30 — Painted until 5
o'clk — Then finished — cleaned
up & washed brushes — Spent
an hour on the sofa & home
to tea at 7.30 — Went, at
8.30, at [illegible] Stearns — 110 W.
[illegible] — There by invitation
to play a game of whist —
met there Miss Mentz, of
Utica — a very pleasant
young lady — also, a Mr.
Carey — Left there at
11 o'clk — Home & to bed
at midnight —
