## Left Page

### Sept. Friday, 25 1874

Very mild, delicious day — got to studio before 9 — to work on fire engine — Wrote a note to father & at 12.30 took it down to the P.O. — then went to quarry & fair ground to find material that I may introduce into "Sunlight on a Shadowed path." Dinner at 1.30. At 2.15 at studio & on fire eng. My head troubling me very much — so confused — Depressed from thinking of the corner I am in. Soon after 4, went to stable & at 4.45 off with Geo. Horseback. Had a good long ride with a glorious moonrise — In late & tea at 7 — Evy, Geo. & I called at Geo. Beuty's — Mr. & Mrs. Beuf. [illegible] there. At 9.30 went to Faus — had a moonlight walk with her — then in one game of whist — then home & to bed soon after 11 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Saturday, 26 1874

Still most charming weather. Got to studio shortly after 8 — but 9 o'clk before I got to work on fire engine. Worked until about 12.30 — then up on my cot for a little rest as my head has troubled me very much all the week. Dinner at 1.30 — the Beuty's & [illegible] there. After dinner all went over to my shop. At 3 I got to work under the beeches beyond the quarry trying to finish landscape in "Shadowed path." Worked until after 4 o'clk. Then to stable. Had a good ride with Geo. In at 6.15 — Tea at 6.30 — Misses Carries & Emily Boggs and late — At 8.30 went down town — At Faus until 11 — then home & to bed at 11.30
