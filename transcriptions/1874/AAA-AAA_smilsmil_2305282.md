## Left Page

### August Monday, 24 1874.

Very cool, but superb day.
Up & at Studio at 7 a.m. but
Cotrill disappointed me. Found
myself very much under the
weather from molly grubs. The
studio too cool for work and I
in pain, so, altho' I tried to
work on A.B.N. Co. drawing I
made no progress. Mr. Muel[on],
Dan Sayre, Mr & Mrs. Reed & [illegible]
came to studio about 1 o'clk. &
Mr M. carried us off to dine
with them. P.M. did very little
work — same causes as the morn.
At 4 o'clk gave up, went to stable,
cleaned up & at 5 started on Tom
for Five Hill where Geo. & a party
had gone. Rode to foot of hill,
missed the party — back at 7 —
tea at 7.45 — Very very tired &
out of sorts, so, on my bed with
newspaper until 10 o'clk, then
to bed.

## Right Page

### August Tuesday, 25 1874.

Another very cool but pleasant
day — still out of sorts, paint trouble
with bowels — after B/kfst. went
downtown & arranged with Chab
Sayre for a stove for studio. Tau
M. insisted that I should go to
Salt Spring with her party — I
begged off — then to Studio & got
to work — Dinner at 1.15 — In
the afternoon Cotrill came &
worked on mouldings — I worked
in effect on Reaper drawing
for A.B.N. Co. — At 5 went to stable,
groomed Tom & then took a short
ride on his back — Tea at 6.30
Vic Dimock arr. — After tea
rested on my bed — Avg, went
to Jan M's — Home & to bed
abt 11.
