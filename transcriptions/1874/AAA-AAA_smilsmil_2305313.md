## Left Page

### October, Thursday 29, 1874

Up at 7.40 — had [illegible] at 8.15 then went to D[illegible] — then to Eff's Office — then to studio & at 11 o'clk. to A.B.N. Co. — Had a talk with Van [illegible] which resulted in the canceling of my agreement with the Co — sending me out to pick up work where I can find it — Met Mary in B'dway — she went into Willis & me. Went out to lunch — wrote a note to Geo — then to Continental Bank Note Co — Got a small job of etching from them — outlined it & had it transferred & back to studio at 4.30 — Met W. McIlhan at the door — To dinner at my new boarding house at 6 — the is hard for me — wrote a letter to S.C.T — To Kimball's soon after 8 — In at 10 — unpacked trunk & to bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### October, Friday 30, 1874

[illegible] at 7.30 — very unsatisfactory — went up to Uncle A's before 9 o'clk — got there in time to get a real [illegible] — Kate & Mary there. Then on my way down stopped at D's — [illegible] — then to studio at 10 o'clk. worked until near 12, arranging desk so that I could go to work etching. Then to Mrs Blake's & had a talk with her — I gave up the room that Geo. & I had taken & she very kindly consented. Then again went to D's — then at 2.30 — to Phil. Rehearsal — Enjoyed it exceedingly — after walked down P[illegible] way — with Belle Tweedy — called on Sauthier — saw Leavitt — at 5 went up to Uncle A's to dinner. Goeckler — Kensha — Bishop & Viola there — home at 11 & to bed soon after —
