## Left Page

### January Tuesday, 20 1874

Superb day. Got to studio at
9 o'clk. & to work on Calf.
Stuck to it closely all the
m'g working on the fore-
ground. Home to lunch
at 1.15 — back at 2.30 & worked
until near 5. Junie &
Flora came in for a few
minutes. Home to tea at
6.20 — at 7.30 Geo left with
Uncle Wm & Aunt H. for the
South. Matt Ret'd to P.
at 4 o'clk. I went down
to Mr Norton's — got the old
drawing attributed to A. del Sarto
& back to studio at 8.30 — then
met Walter Brown — we went
over to Clarence Cook's — 2.15 [illegible] —
found him in Calvert Vaux's —
Home at 10.45 & to bed at
11.30

## Right Page

### January Wednesday, 21 1874

Another horrible day. Sleet, fog
& rain. Got to studio about
9.10 & much of the m'g lost
through callers. Alfred Jones,
Nicoll & others came. Worked
on Calf — getting things forced
together more than at any
time before. My calf gives
me great trouble though, for
I cannot get the color near
right. Home to lunch at
1.45 — back to studio at 3 —
worked as long as I could see,
then to work fixing the
many drawings into their
frames that I am to send to
the [illegible]. Did not get home
until 7.40 — tea at 8. &
after that lying around
until bed time at 10.15
