## Left Page

### February Sunday, 15 1874

Another superb day. My went to St. Hastings & then a tip to sermon from the 46th Psalm - 10th v. "Be still and know that I am God." Dinner at 12.30 - Mr. John Guy Vassar called & dined with us. Afternoon wrote some & at 4 o'clk. started out for a walk - went first to Will's - then to Uncle A's - then to Mr. Jessup's - 78th & Madison Ave. Then to tea & until near 10 o'clk. walked home & got to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### February Monday, 16 1874

Threatening my - even a few drops of rain - Got to studio at 9.30 - Kindled fire, after doing a little work on a Bank note drawing, started the Glass Etching of my calf for the "Graphic." Spraning called. Home to lunch at 45 - back at 2.35 worked on etching until 4.30 - Then Board meeting of W.C. Socy to consult about a Brooklyn meeting - no go - Graphic man came - Home to tea at 7.15 - Evelf wrote a number of letters & then to No. [illegible] at 9.15 - Home at 10.45 - more writing & to bed at 11.30
Superb P.M. & evef.
