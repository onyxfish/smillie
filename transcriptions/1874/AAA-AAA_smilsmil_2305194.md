## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 25 1874

Very heavy snow storm. Got to studio after 10 o'clk. & to work on R.R. stock certificates. Verplanck & Lancaster called. Got my lunch from Miss Wood. Afternoon worked until 5 — when I could no longer see — but I did not finish my drawing — Charl came in for a few minutes. About 6, spread myself on the lounge — there for an hour. Home at 7.30 — Tea at 8 — Eve'g, mounted Bellows', drawing for Granius. To bed at 10.30

Vincent Colyer called & wishes me to meet the Ass. of the N.A. of D. at his room to-morrow P.M. — I guess not.

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 26 1874

Charming day — got to studio at 9.40 & worked on R.R. cert. until near noon. Verplanck called. Then went down town to A.B.N. Co. & lunched. Borrowed $1.00 & took out previews of the Ins. Policy in Continental Co., from A.B.N.C. Went to Sackett's — then to Hamel & back to studio at 4 o'clk. — Smith's funeral procession passed at 5. Miss Eliza Lorry called — invited me to tea to-morrow eve'g. Home to tea at 7.30. At N.A. of D. at 9.20 — fine crowd — Home at 10.30 — To bed at 11.15
