## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 12 1874.

Up at 6 oclt. at studio at 7 —
then to Republican office [illegible]
M.S. Bkfst at 7.45. Very hot
day — at studio stretching muslin
until near 10.30 — then to stable
& worked until 11.30 — then in and
wrote a note to A.P. Throop & at
12.30 took it to P.O. Up to my
Room at 12.45 on bed, resting
until 2.15 — then dinner — after
that, at studio mixing fresh col[ors]
& painting muslin until after
5 — then downtown & got some
glue — back to tea at 6.15 — after
tea, again on my bed, feeling
very tired — heavy thunderstorm
came up — piped the horses [illegible]
the night, & gave up the idea [of]
going to company at Mr. Mrs. [illegible] —
at 9 went to Mrs. Dud's — had a
couple of games of whist — [illegible]
at 11 —

## Right Page

### August Thursday, 13 1874.

Some rain in the early mfg —
looked like clearing at noon
but again overcast. Wt. [illegible]
at nell until after 10 when I went
to the P.O. Found a letter from
Will ordering a small drawing
of Florida arms to be done instantly —
Hurried home — wrote him a
letter, made an outline of drawing
& sent it off to him by noon mail —
Dinner soon after 1 — All the
P.M. engaged on drawing — tea
at 6 — after tea, cleaned stable
then saddled "Tom" & rode him
for half an hour — Then worked
on drawing until after 10 —
got it finished — wrote a letter
to Charle & at 11 went down the
hill & posted them — Back
& to bed at 11.30
