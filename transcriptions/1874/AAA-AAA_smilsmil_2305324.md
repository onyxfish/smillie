## Left Page

### November Friday, 20 1874

Miserable stormy day —
got to studio at 8.20 but
did not get to my painting
until after 10 — then to work
on 14×24 — an old study
near Poughkeepsie, which I
hope to get into shape to
sell at an auction. Lunched
in studio — worked until
4 — then to Boucher's room
at Y.M.C.A. where the
W.C. Board of Controls met —
back to studio at 5.30 —
washed up & to tea at
6.45 — spent a while — Hat & went to Charlie's —
Back at 10.30 & to bed at 11

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 21 1874

Pleasant until, soon overcast
at times, threatening. Got to
studio at 8.40. Painted on 14×24
old study above Poughkeepsie — to
lunch at 12.30 — back to studio
at 2. Ferguson had his studio
things moved in — I got no chance
to work — cleaned up at 3.20
started for R.R. station — off on
4 o'clk. train for P. — got there
at 6.40 — glorious moonlight —
ground covered with snow.
Tea soon after 7 — for Geo.
Charl. & me. Matt & Ed. at
Father's. General chat until
10 — to bed at 10.30
