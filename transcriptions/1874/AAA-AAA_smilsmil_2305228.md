## Left Page

### May, Wednesday, 6, 1874

Cool & pleasant. Got to studio
at 9.30 — worked on W.C. drawing
of yesterday & got it about done
by 12 o'clk. Then went to Anders.
Lauthier's, Brookes Bro. & Deming
Home to lunch at 2 — back
at 3 — worked until 6 on
new drawing, getting it all
washed in — Home to tea
at 7. About 9 o'clk. went to
Academy of Music — Great
Lotus club reception and
Concert — a splendid array
of dress — Home abt 2 to bed
at 12.30.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday, 7, 1874

Got to studio at 11 o'clk. after
attending funeral of Woods
Kimball. Quite used up
with a headache, but worked
on W.C. drawing of Tuesday,
making changes in compo-
sition, improving it very much
I think. Gave up at 12
o'clk. & spread myself on
lounge. Uncle W. J. T. came
in about 12.30 — Home to lunch
at 2.15. At 3 went to bed —
up at 3.45 — packed bag &
with Matt & Ed. went down
to Propeller J.S. Has brought
met father — all off for S. —
to find a house for father
to live in — Took no tea
— to bed soon after 8 & passed
a miserable night of pain.
