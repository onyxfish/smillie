## Left Page

### June Tuesday, 23 1874.

Very hot day. Got to studio at 9.45 — locked my door & stripped to my work — All the morning clearing up about studio's and finishing packing of big boy. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.40 — Young man came from Union Sq. Theatre to say that Palmer wanted my Drafs° sketch. It was packed in my boy, but I consented to let him have it — a check for $75² was at once sent up to me. Home to tea at 7 o'clk — Even't. at 8.30 — went down to Trimballs & spent the even't there until 10.30 — Then to studio & got big bundle & took it home — Ab't 11, family party took's Lager° — To bed at 11.45 —

## Right Page

### June Wednesday, 24 1874.

Delicious day — About 9.30 got to Eugines House (No. 18) in W. 10th S. — There sketching until near 12 — Then to Studio redf in 10 ½° — Saw Niccolls, J. G. Brown — Whittridge & Perry at A.B.N. Co. at 1 — Hurried — Running around to D.S. & W. RR office, DeVissers — Luce — agent of Steam Fire Engines — West eating up in Park Pl. & College Pl. Then to Suttons's — 594 Bdwy — Then Wilsons — Snedecors, Stewarts & studio at 5 o'clk — Miss Correll made a long call — At 6 went to Rehm's & had hair cut then at 7 to Mrs Despard's — great time — there for an hour — Then to Emma J's — There for an hour — Then again to studio — Misses Gilbert & Woodward gave me Strawberries & Ice Creams — Home at 10.15 — So, did it pt up to A.V.V. & to dinner. To bed at 10.45 —
