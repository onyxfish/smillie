## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 27 1874.

Rain in the m'f — Peculiar weather. Showery — Sunny & very mild — Packing during the m'f but much time lost with trunk — Dinner at 1.30 The afternoon spent fussing & fixing — After 5 o'clk took a walk in the twilight with S. Tea at 6.30 — then to studio — finished picking up there — wrote a note to Fan — downtown at 8.30 — then called on Mrs. Hunt, Jacob at Mr. son. Searle — then at Jno S.s until 10 o'clk — then home & packed trunks until after 11 — to bed at 11.45 —

## Right Page

### October Wednesday, 28 1874.

Dull mild m'f — very delicious in grays — the leaves shimmering gently down in showers. All the m'f flying around doing up odd jobs & bidding folks good bye — at 12.20 had my dinner — gobbled it & suffered the rest of the day & night to pay for it. Got off in stage before 1 o'clk — train about 2.30 — got into N.Y. at 10 o'clk & up to Mrs. Blake's at 10.30 went at once into my new quarters and to bed at 11 —
