## Left Page

### August, Saturday, 22, 1874

Quite cool & overcast after the heavy rain & thunder of last night. After bkfs. went out to the stable, cleaned out, then groomed "Tom" — Ben M. came along & insisted that I should join a party to ride — I went with him & misses Walbridge, Miss Saulsbury & Mrs. Jeucks — Got home about noon. Charming day — all went to my studio — joined there by Mr & Mrs. Elisha M. & Isaac Riley — Dinner at 1.15 — Afternoon, tried to work, but suffered from dyspepsia & confusion in the head — At 4 o'clk went off with Geo. & Gratz & had a horseback ride — Tea at 6.15 — Went, called on Miss Hetty Budd & at 9 went to Gus S's where Jan & her friends were — did not get home until 11.15 — went right to bed —

## Right Page

### August, Sunday, 23, 1874

Very cool day — most of the time overcast — Bkfs. after 9 o'clk — Wf. went to Baptist Ch. and heard our Rev. Dr. Saylor preach a very good sermon from "Come unto me all ye that are heavily laden, etc. — Read my papers until dinner at 1.30 — After dinner went to Mr. Cooper's & got him to go on the hill with him to look at his lot — About 4 o'clk. went to stable & worked there until 5.30 — Then Jan M. & party came for me, & I went on the Fair Ground. There until 6.30 — Then home to tea. Mrs. Dad L. out of her room for the first time in three days — Wf. did not go to church — but in my room writing, to Kate — to bed at 10.45
