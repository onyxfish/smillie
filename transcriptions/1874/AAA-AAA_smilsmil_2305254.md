## Left Page

### June Saturday, 27 1874.

Up early & had to wait around until near 9 for bkfst — Then over to Studio & down-town — Saw Catrill, Strange, Frink & lots of others and back to W. J. J's. at 11,15 & worked until 1 o'clk, unpacking boxes that had am— with Studio truck. Dinner at 1,15 — After 2, finished unpacking. About 5 o'clk, being very much overcome by sleep, I lay down on bed & got a little nap before tea at 6 o'clk. After tea, went to Avery Frink's & he went with me to Studio. He finds much fault with some of the work — I tried to get him to take hold of it for me & we talked to little purpose until 8,45 — Glorious moonlight night — went for a few minutes into Davis's, at 9 went down for my paper — Stopped in at Jan's — went out with her & had a regular lark. Called on Gus Seymour. Home & to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### June Sunday, 28 1874.

Delicious day altho' very warm — Up late & had bkfst — at 9 — Sat under the trees until church time — then Geo. & I went to Presb'n Ch — met Joe Williams & sat with him — Miller preached from 2d. Cor. 5 ch — 10 v — as usual, he was to me very tiresome. After service, went in to Jan's — then home to dinner at 1,30 — Sat chatting under the trees until after 4, then with Leora & went up to fair ground. Back to tea at 6. After tea went to Mr. Cookson's for a few minutes & sat on porch with Bessie C. & Fannie Parson. Walked down to the Episcopal Ch. with the Lathrop girls & Geo. Heard a sermon from — Kirkland. "We must all appear before the judgement seat of Christ." Walked home with Ella & sat on his piazza until 10 o'clk — Then home to bed — Glorious night —
