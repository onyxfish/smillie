## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 24 1874.

Clear, fresh & breezy — [illegible] at 8 — Will, Joe, Minnie & Charle off on the 9.10 train — I got to work on Water Color, Morning on Long's Peak, from near [illegible] — Dinner at 1 — at drawing again until 4.30 — altho' I have worked faithfully I succeeded in getting but little for my pains, I packed my few duds — took tea at 6 & at 7 walked in with Kate & Fred, who went to a Thomas Concert, I took 7.35 train — got to N.Y. at 10. & to bed at 10.35 —

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 25 1874.

Superb mtf — clear & cold — got to studio at 9 o'clk — Spent the mtf. painting on "Macmillan's moods. North Conway" 10×14 — to dinner at 12.45 — back at 1.45 & painted the rest of the day on old 14×24 study near Po'keepsie — Nicoll called — I did some carpentering in my coal box & to tea at 6.45 — Eve'g, in at Renner's & drawing plans for a case of drawers for studio — to bed at 10.45
