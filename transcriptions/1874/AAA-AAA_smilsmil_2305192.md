## Left Page

### February Saturday, 21 1874

Mild & spring-like day —
10 o'clk. when I got to the
studio. Miss Desboryam
came soon after & I set
to work painting small
figures for boats in Amu
Coats painting" Evening on
Racquette Lake" — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& painted as long as I could
see. Bothered with head-
ache — several callers —
Johnston of the Mail — Fergus n—
& several strangers. Arthur
sofa until 7 o'clk. — Home
to tea at 7.15 — At 9 o'clk.
at N.A. & D— back at 10.30
& to bed at 11 —
Matt, Ed — & Charl — went
to Po'keepsie this P.M. —

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 22 1874

Very mild & overcast —
Up late — Mus went to
St Hastings' Heard him
preach from Hebrews, 6th
ch. 5th v. — "the powers of the
world to come."

Dinner at 1.15 — I spent
P.M. until 4.30
writing — then went out
to walk — Called on
H. H. Smilte. 1268. 44th St. —
Home & to tea at 6.15 —
[illegible] did not go out but
did more writing —
To bed at 10.45
