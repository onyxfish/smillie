## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 3 1874.

Very mixed — part of day
overcast with threat of Rain —
Got to studio at 9.45 and
worked on R.R. cent. of st[eam?]
for A.B.N. Co — Home to
lunch at 1.45 — back
at 2.45 — Much time
taken by a call from
Geo. Percy — Mr. & Miss Mun-
son — Miss Avery called —
then E.D.E. Greene —
Home to tea at 7 —
Every writing — Tobc[co?]
at 10.45

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 4 1874.

Paint in the m[ornin]g — very mild —
cleared later in the day —
Got to studio at 10 — [illegible]
palette & to work on
an old painting "View
above Po' Keepsie, [illegible] Hudso[n] —
Lunched at studio about
2 o'clk. Painted as long
as I could see — [illegible]
6 o'clks. — Several callers
here on lounge until 7
then home to tea — [very?]
very tired — writing
up water color business
to bed at 11 —
