## Left Page

### July, Friday, 3, 1874

Sulpest day — Bkfs. at 7.45 and nearly 9 o'clk. when I went to the plains mill — All of the morning there with Geo. Lathrop, sawing oak + blk. walnut. Dinner at 1.30 + back to mills at 2 o'clk. Here until 4.30 — then got a dark buggy had my planed lumber carted up to studio. Uncle A. and [illegible] Mt. at 6 o'clk. Tea + to — After that, with the Vander + Grosvick's went over + inspected studio. About 3.30 went to Agen Lathrop where the Rest of the eveg was spent. To bed about 11 o'clk. ## Right Page

### July, Saturday, 4, 1874

Light Rain at 5 a. m. — Did not get up until near 7 — Bkfs. at 7.30 weather cleared, but heavy showers fell at midday and at intervals during the afternoon. After pulling over lumber until 10 o'clk, I wasted the rest of the mst with the fires. Misses Varick + Wallace went away right after an early dinner. During the p.m. I felt very much hit of sorts — something about my stomach — on the bed + Reading. Tea at 6 o'clk — Down Town in the eveg. Went to Louie's + with her to Gus Seymoure's — then back + played Whist with Elisha + Ben until near 11 o'clk — Then home to bed.
