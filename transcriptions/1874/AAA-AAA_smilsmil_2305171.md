## Left Page

### January Saturday, 10 1874.

Splendid day. Half past 9 again before I got to the studio.
Spent the mf on calf drawing - fussing with sky -
Home to lunch at 1.30. back at 2.30 & worked as long as
I could see - Home to tea at 6 - Suey, at 8 o'clk. I
went to No. 6 E. 43d - to find Mr. Haines - to get Water Colors
- Removed - then went down to H.G. Norton's, 233 W 14th
to see his old W.C. drawing from Mexico - 10.30 before I
got away - 11.15 before I got home & nearly 11.45 before
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January Sunday, 11 1874.

Another glorious day. In the morning went to Dr. Hastings
& heard a very stirring sermon on "going forward" — Exodus
14th ch. 15th v. Dinner at 1.30.
After dinner wrote a letter to Mr. Gel Whitney - then walked
in the park until 5.30 - went to Uncle A's - met Mother &
took her to 6th ave. cars - then rid to Uncle A's & took tea.
Aunt Alphia to go home to-morrow mf. Charlt took
Flora d. to church and I went to Dr. H's & heard another good
sermon from Prov. 4th ch. 23d v.
Home at 9 - Wrote some W.C. business & to bed at 11.20 -
