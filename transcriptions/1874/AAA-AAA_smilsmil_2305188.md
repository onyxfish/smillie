## Left Page

### February Friday, 13 1874

Heavy rain, like a spring day. Got to studio at 10 —
had to kindle fire. Pryor
a dealer from Baltimore
took up much of my morning.
Then, Lancaster of the
Herald — I worked a well
as I could on drawing —
Home to lunch at 1.30
at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal.
Walked up with Fanny
K — At studio at 5. Spread
out on lounge & got to sleep.
Home to tea at 7 o'clk —
Everf. did some writing &
got to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 14 1874

Superb day. Got to studio at
9.40 & spent most of the mfg
fussing over Water Color drawing
for Lautner. Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — so
much out of sorts nervously
that I could not work — I
felt perfectly worn out &
very shaky — in the lounge
for an hour or two. A number
of callers — Young man from
the Graphic with prepared
glass & photo from drawing
"Lost" — also Nicoll about
transferring our W.C. show to
Brooklyn — also Ferguson &
Chard — Home to tea at 7 —
at 8 at Phil. Concert, went
home with the Kimball's
& cousin — there until after
11 — home at 11.40 & to bed
at midnight.
