## Left Page

### January, Thursday 8, 1874

Yet miserable weather. Got to studio at 9.30 & commenced color on my calf drawing. A good deal of a muddle — Between times I worked on some little w.c. sketches of Peruvian Andes by H. Ferguson. At 12.30 went to 10th St. Studio Bldg & called on Whittredge, Nicoll & J.G. Brown — Home to lunch at 1.50 — back to studio at 2.20 & worked until 4.30 — wrote a note to W.T. Richards. Then Louis Tiffany came in — fussed about until 6.20. Then home to tea. Even, arranging mounts on some sketches by Mr. Hart & Ferguson for black & white [illegible]. To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### January, Friday 9, 1874

Charming day. Got to studio at 9.45 — very much interrupted by callers. John Durand & J.M. Falconer spent some time with me. Worked over sky & distant trees on calf drawing. Home to lunch at 1.45 — at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal. After Rehearsal went to Gauthier's. Then to Schau's & got to studio at 5 o'clk. Found invitation to dine at the Ripley's — called there & arranged to call later in the even. Went home, dressed, tea at 6.30. At 8. at Annie Cook's escorted her & Mary Willard to 66. 30th; then to Miss Ripley's at 8.30 — Met there Miss Alice James & Mr. & Mrs. Adams — Home with Miss James at 10.30. Then home & to bed at 11.15
