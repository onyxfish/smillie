## Left Page

### May Tuesday, 12 1874.

Up at 9.30 — but did not get to
studio until near 10 — found
a lot of letters; among them, m[e]
from Alfred Jones finding fault
with my management of the
funds of the W.C. Society. It
quite threw me off my work.
At noon went to Lanthier's — then
to 51 W. 10th. Saw J.W. Wood &
J.G. Brown — Home to lunch at
1.45 — After writing to Jan M &
Mrs. D. Hartholp, back to studio
at 3.30 — J.C. Nicoll & Walter
Brown called — Home at 6.30
— Evef talking Eastman Ho
& mounting W.C. drawings —
got to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### May Wednesday, 13 1874.

Pleasant day, but warm in the
afternoon. Up with a headache,
which I fought with all day —
Got to studio at 9.30 & set to
work on W.C. drawing — "a bit in
Berkshire Co" — & at it until 12
then I stretched on lounge for
an hour & home to lunch at 1.30
— wrote some letters & back to
studio at 3.40 — Worked until
near 7, when I got drawing done
after a fashion. At 7.30 went
over to Union League to take off
from hanging to-night — then
home & tea at 8.15 — Evef,
mounted drawings and
to bed at a little after 11 —

W. B. Wood called at the
Studio.
