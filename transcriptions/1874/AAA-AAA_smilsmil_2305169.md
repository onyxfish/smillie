## Left Page

### January Tuesday, 6 1874

Horrible day of mizzle & rain.
Got to studio at 9.20 & went
in with outlining calf drawing.
At 12.30 went to S. P. Avery's
& got card to Jno. S. Johnston's
gallery for Rumea Beach. Home
to lunch at 1 o'clk — back at
2.30 — outlining — Burling
came in — then Ferguson —
Home to tea at 6.30 after
stopping to see Richards —
Eve'g, at 8.30 went to Mr.
Perkins's — 121 W. 44th St. — and
saw Miss Alice James & Boston
— Spent a very pleasant
eve'g until 11.15 — then
home & to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### January Wednesday, 7 1874

A horrible driving rain all
day. Got to studio at 9.45
& to work outlining on calf
drawing, which at last I
finished. Prepared my
lunch in studio at 2 o'clk.
Vainis very much exercised
over his water color drawing —
in & out many times during
the day. Could not seem to
work after 4.30 — lounged
on the sofa — at 6, still rain
— walked up to P. O. station —
8th Ave, bet. 55 & 56th St. & got
some stamps. In to tea at
6.45 — Eve'g, reading news-
papers & to bed at 10.30.
