## Left Page

### October Thursday, 1 1874.

Fresh, cool day — Up at 7.30
B'kf't + then with Charl to
41 [illegible] St. Worked there pulling
things out + packing until
12 o'clk. — Then to During's
to lunch. After lunch went
up to A.V.V.'s for samples +
then to Finkmauer's + bought
1/2 doz. brushes for Austin
+ expressed them to Montreal.
In Renner's store wrote a
note to Austin + me to go
again at packing + destroying
until 5.30 — then to A.V.V.'s to
dinner — Will + Charl there —
At 8.30 at Sturtevant Ho
with Jan until 10.30 —
Know her + Cornell better
than I did before. — Met
Charl at 54 [illegible] St. + 6th ave —
then to A.V.V.'s + to bed at
11.30

## Right Page

### October Friday, 2 1874.

Took a little stroll in Central
Park before bk'f't. At 9.30 at
the Sturtevant Ho — With J —
until 10 when Miss Walbridge
+ the Dr came. Left them in
B'dway at 10.45. Went to the
Studio. Made several calls
+ then, after a little packing,
lunched at During's at 12.30.
Then down to A+B, N. Co. Had
a business talk with Van Zandt
+ Goodall that was discouraging
+ amounted to little. On my way
up town stopped at Appleton's to
see Bunce but he is in Europe —
At Dodworth's + paid rent at
20 W 41 [illegible] St. at 4.30 + worked
until 6.30, then to tea at During's —
again at 12o ab't 9 o'clk. after
calling on Mrs. Blake, 27 W 38th —
At 10.30 went over to During's
+ got to bed shortly after 11.
