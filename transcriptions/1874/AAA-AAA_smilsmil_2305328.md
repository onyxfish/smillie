## Left Page

### November Saturday, 28 1874.

Overcast — mied & foggy —
got to studio at 8.45. & to work
on little 6×10 — over which I
spent the whole day — I am
much interested in my attempt
to get more color in my work.
To dinner abt 1 o'clk. back at
2. Mrs. Beach & Miss Kittin[illegible]
called — to tea at 6.30 — After
some reading & music went
around to Charl's where he
was entertaining Hodges and
Palmer. Back at 10. I to
bed at 10.45 —

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 29 1874.

Horrible storm in the morning
which blew itself out before 11
o'clk. Geo. & I went to Dr.
Hastings & heard him preach
from the words: "Come & see" —
After service, reto. to the house
dressed & walked in Central
Park until 1 o'clk. Then to
Uncle A's to dinner — Billy
Newhall there — also, Mr. Hall.
After dinner, a short walk. Then
a short nap — at 6.15, tea — at
7.15 I walked down to Dr. H's
& heard him preach lessons
to be learned "from the Christians
of the first 3 centuries and their
life in the Catacombs of Rome."
In abg — to bed at 10.
