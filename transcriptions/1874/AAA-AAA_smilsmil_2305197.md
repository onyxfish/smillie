## Left Page

### March Thursday, 5 1874

Glorious day — feel very tired — all of the mng about the morning running around after frames — to Sirus Tiffanys — Giffords — Mr. Culveis — the N.A. & P. etc. — Did no studio work — Home to lunch at 1.10 — back at 2.15 & worked all the p.m. on an Italian drawing "Ocean Telegraph" for A.B.N. Co — Allie, Anna & Josie came in about 6 o'ck — Home at 7.15 Tea at 8.30 — Sewing all the evng & to bed at 10.20

## Right Page

### March Friday, 6 1874

Cold, raw & overcast — Got to studio at 9.30. All the mng, composing & outlining two drawings in water color for [illegible] Hearn — Shurtleff, Granniss & Tiffany called. Home to lunch at 1.20 & at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal. "Sis" Howe there — also J.C.T. met with Nicoll to N.A. & P. trying to straighten out some of the kinks — then to Tiffany's to Silleck's — Snow & sleet. Got to studio at 5.30 — tried to pack "Old Birch" to send to Mr. Whitney but broke the glass. Home to tea at 6.30. Most of the evng writing a note to Gifford (R.S.) on the subject of some disputed commissions — To bed at 10.50
