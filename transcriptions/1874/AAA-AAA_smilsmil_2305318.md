## Left Page

### November Sunday, 8 1874

Delightful day — B[illegible] at 8.30.
Walked awhile in little park &
at 10.30 went into Dr Hastings[illegible]
half heard (for I was very stupid
& sleepy) a sermon preached by
a very venerable looking old man
— don't know who. After service
walked up with the Jessups &
dined with them. At 3.30
walked down through the park —
met Geo. — went in to Uncle's —
tea there & met Chas. Van[illegible] &
Geo. Eve[illegible], Geo. & I went to the
Dutch Ref[orm]. Ch. 5th Ave. 48[illegible].
After service, strolled through
the Windsor Hotel — M. & wrote
to father — Did not get to
bed until midnight —

## Right Page

### November Monday, 9 1874

Miserable dull day with rain.
Got to studio at 9.15. Kindled
fire & got to work charcoaling
several subjects, preparing
them to paint — To dinner at
D's at 12.45 — back at 1.45 —
worked with charcoal —
very heavy & confused — feel
much discouraged at these
fits of uselessness — To tea
at 6.30 — Eve[ning] & spent sorting
old letters & writing to Henry
Tyler — To bed at 10.45 —
