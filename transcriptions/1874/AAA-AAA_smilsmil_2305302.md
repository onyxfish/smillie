## Left Page

### October, Monday 5, 1874

Most glorious mt. Up at 6 - bkp: at 7. Off for N.Y. on 8.10 train, where
we arr. at 10.30. Went right to 120 W.
41st, & worked there until 12.30
then to Dürings & got my dinner.
All the afternoon worked in the
same mess until 6 o'clk. then
to D's to tea. Chave there. To
work again about 7.30 and staid
until 10 o'clk. Then Chave & I
went again to D's, who played
on piano for us until 11.30
Then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 6, 1874

Overcast most of the day and rather
threatening. Bkp: at 7.45. Avg
went to studio — back & at work
at 120 W. 41st at 10.30. Packed
until 12.15 — then to lunch at D's.
Back to work at 1.30 kept at it
until 6.15 — then over to tea —
After tea went after Rossel,
the carman, 4 24 W. 33d St. from
there, went to the Kimball's.
Found them all in & spent
a very pleasant eveg until
10.20 — Then home to Dürings
& right to bed, exceedingly
tired.
