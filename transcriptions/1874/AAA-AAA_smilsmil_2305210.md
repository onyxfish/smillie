## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 31, 1874

Disagreeable day — snow and
Rain from 12 until after 2 —
Got to studio at 9.20 & worked
all day on "Moat Mt. from the
Saco at North Conway" 16×26 —
for Uncle A.V.V. Painted all over
the sky & got it into tolerable
harmony — It is now a pretty
fair picture, for me — Prepared
my lunch at 1.30 — Worked
until 4 — Miss St. Evay came
in — at 5 commenced a little
sketch of Distant Sierras &
got it all in by 6.15 — Home
to tea at 7.15 — Eve'g spent
trying to clear up accumulated
papers & general muss —
Got to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### April Wednesday, 1, 1874

Cold, Raw day — Got up with
an outrageous headache but
got to studio at 9.5 — Held
up for a little while & worked
with charcoal planning a
view of the High Sierras from
Cloud's Rest. Before 11 o'clk
gave up & the rest of the day
was spent in misery on the
lounge — It was near 9 o'clk
before I could get home —
To bed about 9.30 & passed
a Restless night.
