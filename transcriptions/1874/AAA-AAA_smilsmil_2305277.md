## Left Page

### August Friday, 14 1874.

Cloudy, but very charming.
Hard at work all day at
studio, superintending work
& stretching, sizing & coloring
muslin for the walls. Bkpt
at 8 — dinner at 1.30 — All the
afternoon busy hoping to get
Cotrell through with his
work to-morrow. Tea at 6.30
— Then cleaned stable, then
dressed — at 8.30 went to
Fan M's — "Sister Wickedness"
there — also Mrs. Wm Jessup —
Priscilla Poore & Miss Smith.
Home at 10.30 — Read Beecher
statement until 11.45 and
to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### August Saturday, 15 1874.

Very warm day. All day with
Cotrell & feeling very much
used up. Bkpt at 8 o'clk. I
cannot get the carpenters
out to-day as I had hoped.
Dinner at 1.15 — and again
in studio until 6. Then I
was played out. Tea some
after. Then fixed horses for
the night. After that, spread
myself on my bed with Tribune
& read myself asleep. Got
to bed about 11; very nervous
& miserable — Passed a very
bad night dreaming.
