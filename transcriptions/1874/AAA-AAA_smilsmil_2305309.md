## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 21 1874

Quite a heavy shower in the early
mrg., but very mild, misty
weather. Spent the mrg. painting
over the pool of water in "Wood
Scene" — White Mts. — Weather cleared.
Dinner at 1.30. After dinner
worked about ½ an hour, then
finished painting. At om after
2, went over to [illegible] caused
Ht. J's horse, harnessed him
& about 3 was ready to go off
with S. Riding. That about
6 — tea — then down town for the
mail — up to W. J. S's — there
until 10 o'clk — then walked
the streets for a while enjoying
the glorious moonlight — and
at last to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 22 1874

An ideal, dreaming Oct. day — the
mrg. I spent fixing up odds &
ends & finishing pen sketch
of Sue bolt. Rec'd letters from
Wile with orders for a Whiskey
stamp drawing — Answered
him & then to dinner at 1.30 —
Sue & Hetty S. there — Got to
work on drawing soon after
2 & by 5 o'clk. had my design
outlined. Then went to H. Post's
& got deed, invited to take tea at
Jem. Searles — there a little before
6\. After tea, whisked up the hill
to W. J. S's at 9 o'clk — There until
10 — then to bed at 10.40.
