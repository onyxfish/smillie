## Left Page

### November Monday, 16 1874.

Pleasant day — At Studio
abut 8.40 — Hayek's man &
lumber there — All day with
the carpenter "bossing" the
putting up of shelves, etc in
closets, that I may stow out
of sight a lot of my rubbish.
Did not go out to lunch —
Miss Woodward gave me a
cup of tea abt 1.30 — Tea
at 6.30 — At 8, back to the
studio & worked until 10.30
trying to get some order —
To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 17 1874.

Overcast again — Very tired
& unfit for work — Walked up
to Uncle A's to get umbrella
that I left there on Sunday.
Got to studio at 9.45. To work
on 6×10 — "Adirondacs at North
Conway" — To dinner at 12.30
back at 2 — P.M. Bessie Riley
& her cousin called — Worked
as long as I could see, but
owing to interruptions, could
not finish little painting —
To tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 at
studio — Fall meeting of W. C.
Society — full number out &
business went off briskly &
smoothly — Out at 10.30 —
To bed soon after 11 —
Raining quite heavily —
