## Left Page

### May, Thursday 28, 1874

Still superb weather. Got to studio at 10.10 — About the most (very weary) in planning new drawing for A.B.N. Co. of "Gaucho Lasso" — Home at 1.45, back at 2.45 & worked from a day figure until 6 o'clk — met wrote for an hour & home to tea at 7.30 — Matt arrived from P— late in the p.m. to help Mary get away to Geneva — Willie's fever seems stronger to-day — Evef wrote a letter to Alfred Jones — To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### May, Friday 29, 1874

Beautiful day, but very warm — Did not get to studio until 10.30 — Aunt Ollie at the house — worked on Gaucho until 1.30 — thru home — back at 2.45 & worked until 6 — Horrible paper — Mary got off with the children & nurse on 6 P.M. train — tea at 7.30 — Evef minding some W.C. business — To bed about 11.30
