## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 26, 1874

Pleasant day, altho' at times threatening showers. Got to
studio at 9.15 & to work in A.
B.N. Co's drawing Vulcan & Minerva,
giving it more force. Home to
lunch at 2 o'clk — back at
2.45 — Met Mr. Guel Crane —
Miss Scott came & took a
lesson — I worked all the
P.M. for her on W.C. sketch
"in the old fort. St. Augustine
fla" — Gel called — & so did
McCall — the Auditing Com-
have labored & brought forth
a — louse. Home to tea at
7 o'clk — Mother & Willie
quite sick. Eveʼg, being
very tired, I sat in my big
chair until near 10 — then
to bed — [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 27, 1874

Glorious day — Got to studio at
9.30 & spent the m't working
on Vulcan & Minerva — getting
it at last, Ready to deliver.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.40. Much of the P.M.
taken up by watching the
military display, from Studio
window — Worked on Italian
drawings until 6 o'clk. Home
to tea at 6.30 — Eveʼg, with
Charl went to Union Square
Theatre — on passes from Manager
Palmer. Saw Clara Morris in
"Camille". Marston (Scene painter)
took us on the stage between acts
— saw Palmer & agreed to paint
sketch for his new drop.
Home at 11.30 — mounted a
drawing and to bed at 12.
