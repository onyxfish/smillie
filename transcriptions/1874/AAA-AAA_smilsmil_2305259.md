## Left Page

### July, Thursday 9, 1874

Heavy, muggy day. Up, with nausea & very sharp pains in the region of the liver. Took but a few mouthfuls of breakfast, then back to my room & on my bed, where I lay, curled up, until after 3 o'clk. Then up & walked out, gradually feeling better & doing more. The weather cleared very pleasantly. Went down to the parsonage & saw Frank & Strange. I find that Strange has lied to me — went for Cattuel — he is out of town. Up the hill at 6.30. Junie & Ida Thayer arr'd. Tea at 6.45. A few minutes on piazza chatting with Junie & Ida. Then down town — went to Fan M's & played whist until 10.30. Home & to bed at 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 10, 1874

Overcast & sprinkling. Bkfs. at 8 o'clk, then down town & about doing messages until after 10 o'clk. Arranged things & commenced work on "State Arms of Geo." in room over my J.T.'s back house. Dinner at 1.30. After dinner Uncle A. took me out riding — we were soon turned back by a very heavy thunder storm, so that — got to work again about 3 o'clk. At it until 6 — then tea. After that, down the hill — a few m. at Fan's. Then up & went with Junie, Ida & Gol. to a little Co. at Mr. Jas. Dewitt's. Left there at 11 o'clk. Home & to bed before 12. Heavy Rain during the night. Rev. Dr. Taylor and family of Newark arrived this afternoon.
