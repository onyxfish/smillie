## Left Page

### October Sunday, 11 1874

Cold breezy m't. At first it looked very like clearing. Then followed gusty, spitting showers. M't, went to the Baptist Ch. & heard Mr. Cheshire on "the desire of all nations" — Haggai. After service walked with Sarah to the Methodist Ch. & viewed the Ruins. Met Mr. Mrs. Mc Mul[illegible] then joined by W.J.T. & walked out beyond Warner's. Driven in by a sp't of Rain. Dinner abt 1.30 P.M. In shop writing. Took tea with S.C.T. at W.J.T.'s & spent the every there until 10 o'clk then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### October Monday, 12 1874

Cold, windy & Raw with sputtering rains. Bk'f: at 8. Then saw H't Tyler on my lot business. Then went to A.L. Post's. He agrees to give me the "Realty" to a lane of land provided I get the assent of W.J.T. & H't J. It took me most of the m't to manage it. Abt 2.30 at Studio. Soon after 1 o'clk, dinner at 2 again at Studio. Got out my oil traps, outlined two subjects & got all ready to paint by 3.30 P.M. Too late to begin & my head felt bad so, went off & took a walk with S.C.T. Took tea at W.J.T.'s & spent the every there until near 10 o'clk. Ret. came in about 8 o'clk. To bed at 10.30 after making a settlement with H't Tyler.
