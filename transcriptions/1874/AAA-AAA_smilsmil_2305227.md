## Left Page

### May Monday, 4 1874.

mild, hazy day — got to
the studio at 10 o'clk. after
going to Knoedler's & buying
paper etc — worked on
sketch (water color) of Sentinel
Rock Yosemite — made
a year ago — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and
soon finished drawing
— then commenced on two
others, hoping to finish
them for the League
[illegible] Home to tea at 7.30
— every on Water Color
Report until 11.30 —
To bed at 12 —
First day of the season
without fire —

## Right Page

### May Tuesday, 5 1874.

Rain in the [illegible]. Got
to studio at 9.30 — Had to
kindle fire — then to work
on Water Color drawing —
stuck closely to it until
afternoon — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Weather
cleared up — Worked
until 5.30 — Then a nap
until 6.15 — Home to tea
at [illegible] — at 8.15 at
Tiffany's Room M.W.C.A.
Spring meeting of W. C. Socy
a very long session over
report of Committee on
frames — Out at 11.15 —
Home at 11.45 — To bed
at 12.15 — Did not get
up to Will's Crystal
wedding —
