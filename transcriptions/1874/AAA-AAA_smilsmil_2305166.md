## Right Page

### January Thursday, 1 1874

Overcast, but not unpleasant — quite mild. Up late. Breakfasted, then dressed — nearly 10 o'clk. before I started out to make my calls, a list of which will be found in pocket. About 3 o'clk. when I got home to dinner — off again at 4 & kept diligently at it and quite enjoying the fun. About 7 o'clk P.M., after leaving D.S. Coy's, it began to drizzle. Got to Fanny Kimball's just before 9 — then raining quite hard — stayed there until 10, then started out with the intention of going up to Uncle A's, but wisely gave it up, and instead went home & got to bed shortly after 11.
