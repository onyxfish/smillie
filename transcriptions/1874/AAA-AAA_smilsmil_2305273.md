## Left Page

### August Thursday, 6 1874.

Heavenly day but my head
does not correspond. All the
m'f until 10. with C., then with
gps. in stables until 11 — then
Rested my head for a few
minutes — then again with
C. + went down town before
dinner at 1.15 — Took a light
dinner — again, the blueness
\+ before 3 Had to go to bed
with a horrible headache
— there until 6.30 — then out,
over to studio + attended to
stable chores. Went back to
Mr. Dud's about 8.30 + was sur-
prised to find a number of y my
folks who had a good time
until 11.30 — Home + to bed at
12.30. Night very dark with
threats of Rain —

## Right Page

### August Friday, 7 1874.

overcast day. A sharp feeling
very sore + lame down my back,
having taken cold — probably
while sitting by an open window
at my meals. All the m'f was
given to Cotrell's man — C.
himself did not come until
bet. 3 + 4 o'clk. Nearly 11 when
Ge. + I went to the stable where
we spent an hour with our horses.
At noon (at 2.30) again went to studio
— again worked all over large
sheet of muslin + made it look
worse than before. Dinner at 1.30
All the p.m. again in studio — C.
struck a small piece of wain-
scoting that pleased me very much
— Tea at 6 — We'f; went down
town — at Jan's until 8.45. playing
whist with Miss Emma Smith +
Priscilla — then up to the house +
Miss Gratz. Joined a pepper plaster
for my neck + shoulder — at 9.45 went
to my own Room.
