## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 17, 1874

Raining & Mizzling all day.
Got to studio at 9.40 — Set
palette & to work on Raquette
Lake — Painted over sky
& part of the water — Home
to lunch at 2 o'clk — back
at 2.45 & painted until
after 5 o'clk — Then stretched
myself on lounge & got to
sleep — Up at 7 — Cleaned
palette & washed brushes.
At 7.30 walked up to the
Windsor Hotel & saw a set
of Masonite Photos by [illegible]
bridge of Sant. Home at
8.30 & had my tea — The
Rest of the eveg lounging
about & to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 18, 1874

Muggy mizzling day — out of
sorts altogether — Got to studio
at 10 o'clk. Did not get to work
until afterll. on Raquette Lake
Jack Diveau called, but I
did not see him — Home to lunch
at 1.20 — back at 2.45 — Painted
until after 5. having done very
little — at 5.45 started for Mr
Jessup's — 78th & Madison Ave. —
Dinner at 7 o'clk. Passed
a very pleasant eveg until
10.20 — Home at 10.45 & to
bed at 11.20
