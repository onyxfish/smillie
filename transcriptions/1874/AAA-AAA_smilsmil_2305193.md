## Left Page

### February Monday, 23 1874

Exceedingly mild & muggy — people sitting with windows
open & out of doors. Got to studio at 9.45 — Kindled fire
& got to work on 2nd sketch for city arms for A.B.N. Co. —
Home to lunch at 1.30. back at 2.45 — worked until near
6 — Home to tea at 6.30 — wrote until nearly — then
down to N.A. of D. Met there Geo. Hearn & wife —
The greatest day yet at the Academy — $220 — a
thousand people must have visited the galleries.
Walked up with Green &
Richards. Home at 10.40
To bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 24 1874

Slightly overcast & very much cooler than yesterday. Got
to studio at 10.30 & to work on R. R. Certificate & stock border
for A.B.N. Co. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.20. Worked
until 6 — but had several callers — Miss Parker & Meridian
& her sister, Mr. Day — also [illegible] Johnston — Burling and
others. Home to tea at by 30
at 9 went to N.A. of D. There
until 10 — Home at 10.30 &
to bed at 11.15
