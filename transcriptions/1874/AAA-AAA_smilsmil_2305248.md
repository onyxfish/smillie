## Left Page

### June, Monday, 15, 1874.

Still glorious weather. At 9 rode down to Engine St. in W. 10th St — then walked to 120 Mercer St (near Prince) then to Dewey's in B'way then to Geo. Hearn's — Snedecor's & Studio — then to Villede's — N. A. of D. met young Brown — then with him to Union League — & at 1.30 home to lunch — at 3.30 at A.B.N. Co. all engaged — didn't see any body — then to Beekman St. no one in — then rode up to 131 Mercer St & ordered packing boxes — then to D. Appleton's — then again to Dewby's — then rode up to studio at 6 — stretched paper & home to tea before 7 — every with Kate — Charlt & Miss Peabody went up to Thomas's garden. Home at 11.20 — to bed at 12 —

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday, 16, 1874.

Delightful day — got to studio at 9 o'clk — Palmer called & liked my painting but didn't seem to fancy paying $150 & for it. Young Brown came & paid me W. Cheever's check for $70.00, payment for little Water Color drawing "a memory of the Northern woods" Steam did not come — Hard at work packing in two big boxes — Home to lunch at 1.15 after lunch went to Knoedlers & bought material. Met Silva at Studio at 4 o'clk, worked at my packing until 8 o'clk then home — tea at 8.45 — lounged the rest of the even't & to bed at 11 — Rain at 1 o'clk.
