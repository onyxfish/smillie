## Left Page

### November Thursday, 26 1874.

Charming day — Thanksgiving —
Got to studio at 8.30 — Painted
on old study 14×24 — near $. "
— trying to get it into shape
to put in some sale — At 12 [illegible]
went up to dinner, but as Mary
the Cook had to have a tooth
drawn, dinner was not on the
table until 2.20 — It was near
4 before I got to studio again
and it was 7 before I got back
to tea. Every again drawing
on plans for drawers & to bed
at 10.45.

## Right Page

### November Friday, 27 1874.

Very mild, pleasant day. Got
to studio about 8.30 & to work on
little painting 6×10 — "White House
edge & Saco at North Conway" —
had it already painted, but
went all over it trying to get
an effect luminous, mellow
grey — did not get it done
before I had to lunch at 1.30
— at 2 I went to Phil. Rehearsal
After that went to Hy Cox's —
& to [illegible] & Baway, to see if he would
buy one of my Water Color drawing
— no go. I Neale with me — we
walked up together — at
9.30 — at Miss Mary Willard's
106 W. 49 St., to tea — a very bright
little company of girls — at 11 o'ck
walked to 22d St. with the Cooks &
Kimballs — To bed soon after
12\. — Geo came down from
Poughkeepsie this m'f.
