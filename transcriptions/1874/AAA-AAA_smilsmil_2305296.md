## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 23 1874.

Glorious day — In studio before
9 o'clk — kindled fire & then to
work on fire engine. Altho' feeling
quite tired from ride yesterday, to
worked steadily until 12.30 — then
arranged for a ride in the afternoon.
Dinner at 1.30 — at 2.30 went down
to Fan's — Ben got up his horses for
me & at 3 Fan & I got off, followed
by Gel. & Miss Strawbridge. We
drove out by ma Geo. Walker's place
& got a glorious view — then across to
Elk Lakes — mt past Risely's, Shelfin.
& Shays to Five Hill where we had
a grand sunset. Then home over
Butterfield's Hill by twilight & moon-
light. In at 7 — 16 or 18 miles — Tea
at Fan's — Spent the even there
& home & to bed at 10.30 —
Rec'd a letter from father
enclosing a B. N. Co's check
for $55.00 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 24 1874.

Very pleasant day — to work on
fire engine at 9 o'clk — sat it
quite steadily until 12.30 — then
downtown & got check cashed —
An rainy dinner because Mr. & Mrs.
Strawbridge left in the 1.30 stage —
At 2 o'clk. quite a heavy shower
with thunder — worked on drawing
until 4.15 — then to stable, groomed
Fan & at 4.45 Gel. & I went out —
Rode around the lake & about town
watching a thundershower that
came up about 6.30 — Tea at that
time. Mrs. Blossom & two daughters
and Everf. made a call on [illegible]
Wm Milford — Glorious night — at
8.45 went to Fan's — Home &
to bed at 11 o'clk —
