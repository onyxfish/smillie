## Left Page

### August Monday, 10 1874.

Glorious mng., but later, overcast and
sultry. With Catrell before 8 o'clk. and
ale the mng. at noon went down
the mill to P.O. — Republican offic—
bought more color, etc. — Dinner at
1.20 — about 2 o'clk., a very heavy
shower with a little thunder —
again in studio — C. got up the
first of my Paliastres. Worked
until after 6 o'clk. — then dressed
& went to Mrs. Mr. Mulford's
to tea — Isaac Riley made
merry for us — Home soon
after 11 o'clk. & thue to bed.

## Right Page

### August Tuesday, 11 1874.

Very hot day — P.O. at 8
o'clk — all day hard at work
with Catrell — At noon, went
to Republican office & got
papers — then P.O. & got letters
from D. & Mary — very characteristic —
Mary is evidently worn
out & homesick. — Dinner at
1.15 — In studio until after
6 — then attended to horses —
then dressed & at 7 o'clk
at Fanny M's. — to tea —
Had quite a jolly even't
until 9.30 — then up home
after stopping a short time at
Ager Lattinde's. About 11, got
started writing the end of the
third mormon letter. — Abt
until 1.30 — then to bed.
