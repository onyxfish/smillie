## Left Page

### May Friday, 22 1874

Glorious day. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on Italian drawing of yesterday — Very much interrupted by calls but got along pretty well until 1.30 when I went home to lunch — back at 2.40 & found Miss Scott — her mother & Mrs. Partridge waiting most of P. M. — given to Miss S — & her first lesson in Water Colors. Managed however, to finish Italian drawing — At 6.20 went to Emma Ireland's to dinner — "Sis" Howie there — at 8 o'clk Annie & Geo. Cooke came over & about talking over Adirondack trips — left at 10.20 — Home & to bed at 11.15

S — 1st lesson —

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 23 1874

Still delightful weather — Up with a headache — Got to studio at 9.30 — Nicolle called with another note from Jones — he is bothered & so am I — Miss Abbie Seymour of Buffalo also called & Mr. Marston, Smis artist of [illegible] Sq. Theatre — He wishes to get some material for a new "Drop" — About 12 had to give up — went home & to bed — Got up about 8 o'clk & took some tea. Lay [illegible] until between 2 & 3 o'clk. A.M. then to bed —
