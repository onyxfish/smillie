## Left Page

### August Sunday, 30 1874

Delightful day. My, went to Presbyt Ch. and heard
Isage Riley — left "Our father which art in Heaven." After
service went home with Fan
there until 1.30 — then up to [illegible]
Dud's — Dinner at 2 — Josie
B. Hetty S. & Joe [illegible] Thur.
Afternoon, note to Matt &
to Menger. At 5.45 went up to
fair ground with Fan M's party.
Tea at 7 — Every went to Ch.
(Episcopal) with Fan and
misses Alling & Wallbridge —
Left "What think ye of Christ."
After service at M's until after
10 — Home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August Monday, 31 1874

Very dry & warm. After bkft. got
uncle Wm. to go over that portion
of Hy Sykes's lot that I am thinking
of buying. It was 10.30 before I got
to work on "Thresher" drawing. I
traced the woodcut & transferred
it. Ben M. came & begged me to
join a little pic nic party, but
I stuck to my work. All went —
they had a good time at Wyalusing
Falls. Dinner at 1.15. Worked
until about 4.30 — then nt to
stable & worked over Tom until
near 6 — then in & dressed — tea
at 6.30 — Sue Holt there. After,
went down to Fan's & with her
friends & Mr. & Mrs. My Jessup, went
to the Presbytn Ch. & heard
Judge Wright of S. Ca — a negro,
lecture on Sunday — a day of rest —
a weak & sad exhibition to
me — a failure every way. At
Fan's until near 11 — then home
& to bed.
