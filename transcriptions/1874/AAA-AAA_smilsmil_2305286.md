## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 1 1874

Still very dry — got to studio
about 9 o'clk & to work on "[illegible]"
stuck to it pretty steadily
until 12 o'clk — then went to
Beck's & ordered a panel & stretcher
— then hunting around for a horse
until 1.15 — then up to dinner.
At 2, at studio — At 3.15, Fan
& friends came — at 4 Gil came
with horses & wagon — thus all
off for a ride — back at 6.15 —
Tea at 6.30 — Hy & I brought
back Mrs S— At 7.30 went with
Gil to Mr. Wm Jessup's — met friends
there & spent a pleasant hour —
At 9 went to Fan M's — there
until 10.30 — then home and
to bed shortly after 11 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 2 1874

Hot & dry — Headachy & sort of seedy.
Got to studio a little before 9 &
to work on "Thresher." At 11.30
went down town to bid Misses
Velling & Wallbridge good bye,
then went to Beck's & to Smith's
back to Mulford's & was induced
to take some bread & berries and
then ride over to the R.R. depot
with them — again good byes — the
cars started & we slipped into
the baggage car (Fan, Mr, Wm & I) — at the next station we
surprised the girls by joining
them. We went on to Springville
where there was a Preston pic-
nic under way — Romped with
the young people until 5 o'clk
then R.R. Tea at 6.30 — every-
thing until near 10 at Fan's —
D.M. am? this eve? — Stopped
at a moment at Joe Wm's to see
how his "surprise party" was
getting on — Home & to bed at 10.30
