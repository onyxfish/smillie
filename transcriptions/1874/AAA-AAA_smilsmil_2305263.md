## Left Page

### July, Friday 17, 1874

Glorious day. The morning pretty
much lost in ting to Chase and
running after the felting paper
that arrd by Exp. yesterday eve.
I did a little on the "Genius
of mining", then dinner at 1 o'clk.
It was after 3 o'clk. before I
again got to work when I
kept at it until I got the
drawing finished. Tea at 6 oclk.
After that Dr Taylor & son went to
my studio — then down town for my
mail. Then called on Mr. Isaac Tyler,
notin — then for some time on Taskell's
cor. talking "biz" with different
ones — then to Mr. Jessup's piazza,
then to Mr. Dud's & at 9 to my room.
Read newspaper until near 10 —
then wrote to Kate & to Mama. —
After 11 went down to P.O. — then
home & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 18, 1874

Warmer & weather hazy, but delightful day. After bkfs., some time as a dicker with H & Tyler for a horse —
then wandered off with George
to Cooper's lot on the fair ground
hill — was permeated & saturated
with the loveliness of the day &,
looking away to the blue hills in
the distance, dreamed dreams of
a little home there for my own
fashioning. 11 o'clk. before I
got to work. Outlined "Arms of
Florida" for A.B.N. Co. Dinner
abt. 20 — Did little work in the
afternoon, my stomach & head
giving me great trouble again.
Tea at 6. About 6.30, the Misses
Boggs and Evert Oel & I went
calling — Stopped at Mulford's, then
went to Bessie Riley's & passed
a very pleasant eve there until
10 o'clk — then home & read
paper until after 11 — then
to bed at 11.30.
