## Left Page

### October Saturday, 17 1874.

Still charming weather — Got up very early because I had to — then back to bed. Bkfst. at 8. then to Studio — thick with [illegible] but spent the mst painting on "Tuolumne River + San Joaquin Plains" — Som after 12. went with S. up to the fair grounds. Air, lovely — color, very beautiful — there until 1.15 — then back to dinner. At Studio at 2 — At 2.30 Sue Bolt came + I worked on a pencil ink sketch from her until 4.30 — then washed up + at 5 went downtown — Met Julia Read, Gus, Lillie + Alice out with the horses — had a romp with the girls and a ride down Public [illegible] bareheaded — then to Cottrell's + back to tea at 6.15 — At 7.30 went to W. J. J's — went downtown + got his mail for him — back at 8.30 — Chat until 10.30 — then to bed —

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 18 1874.

Gloomy, roaring day — cold, cloudy + blustering. Up late — Mst went to the Baptist Ch. + heard Cheshire preach a sermon that, owing to my being in just the humor for it, interested me exceedingly — 1st Cor 15th Ch. 19th v. — He dwelt upon the terrible failure of the Almighty in our creation, if we are not immortal — After service walked with S.C.F. + W. J. J — until 2.30 — then to studio + kindled fire + wrote letters until 4 o'clk then with S. C. F., walked around the lake and in at 6 o'clk. — tea — + at 6.30 took S. to the Baptist Ch. Service over at 8 o'clk. went to W. J. J's — there until 11 o'clk — then home + to bed.
