## Left Page

### December Saturday, 12 1874

Clear, cold day — Got up feeling very much out of sorts — at studio at 9 o'clk. Set to work painting on Meadow etc' (2½ days). To lunch at 12.45 — after that felt so used up that I went to bed & slept much of the time until 5.30 — then up, washed, dressed & had my tea. At 7.30 walked down to Phil. Concert. Had a miserable time — after concert walked up home with [illegible] & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 13 1874

Up to brkfst. but felt so miserable that at 10 I went back to bed & stayed there until after 2. Aunt Jane brought up some dinner & insisted upon my eating it. About 3 o'clk. I walked up to the Park & to Uncle A's. Kate & [illegible] there — stayed to tea & took Kate down to Mad. Ave. Ch. I waited until his return, then walked down with him — to bed at 10.45 [illegible — entire passage]
