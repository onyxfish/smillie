## Left Page

### April, Saturday, 18, 1874

Tolerably clear again —
Got to studio at 9.30 —
Spent the day working up
outline of N- + m- [illegible] B[illegible]
John Durand + W. Ford
called — also H.W. Robbins —
Home to lunch at 1.30
— back at 2.45 & worked
until 6.30 — Wrote a letter
before 7 — Tea — dressed,
at 8 o'clk, at Academy of
Music — Last Phil. Concert
of the season — Home at
11 + soon to bed.
   Meanwhile, Rec'd — a
note from [illegible] R. —
announcing the sudden
death of Woods R— in
Cal.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday, 19, 1874

Charming Spring day — Got
up with a headache — but
went to Dr. Hastings + heard
the stranger that is filling
his place — After service
walked up 5th ave — with
Josie + Mr. Jessup — Left
them at the Arsenal — went
into the Park — then to Uncle
A's — Took dinner there —
Was informed by Aunt H. that
Hutton. Wm. J. has ordered
that I am not to be allowed
to sleep over his back house
next Summer — So much
might be expected from this
pattern gentleman — Home
at 3.0 clk used up with
my headache + to bed.
