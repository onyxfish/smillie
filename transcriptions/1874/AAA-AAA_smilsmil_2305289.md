## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 7 1874.

A little, tantalizing rain very early in the morning. After bkfst went down to Cotrell's to see after the card he is making for me. I did not get to studio until near 11 o'clk. Stewart & Josie B came in, also Miss Hylet and [illegible]. Worked until dinner time on Penn'a Arms. Dinner at 1. On drawing until 4 o'clk then to stable & with Geo. Rode until 6. Superb day offered Hy I. $450 for his lot on the hill — he agreed to 475 & so did I. Tea at 6.30. Swelf wrote letters to Uncle Willie & to father — went down to P.O. with them at 9.30 — could hardly get back to my bed, felt so sick — but got there some after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 8 1874.

Very lovely m'f. but I carry a very sore stomach. After bkfst went out with Hy I. to stake out the lot on the hill but we soon ran against a snag. Hy is not satisfied that he has got the last dollar that I am willing to pay, so he makes trouble about the Right of way south of his house. I saw W. J. [illegible] & tried to effect an arrangement with him, but he is unwilling to accommodate. At noon Hy was willing to come to terms if I would pay $25 extra — I would not — & so it stands. Dinner at 1.30 Josie & Stuart B. there. P.M. little feeling unable to work — but I drew a plan for bill of studies furniture that Cotrell is to make for me. Will & Joe are from Geneva. At 5. P.M. Geo came with buggy & we rode until 6.15. Swelf took tea at Hy I's — two or three other elderly people there. From 8 to 9 at W. J's — then down town — letter from father with $70 ? Went to Fan's & had whist until 10.30 — then home & to bed.
