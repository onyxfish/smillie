## Left Page

### June Friday, 19, 1874

Cool & overcast with slight sprinkles of rain — Got to studio at 9.20. Spent the mf, packing and at 12 o'clk. went down to the A.P.N.Co & lunched. After lunch, settled matters with Van then with Charl. went to Hutters P.O. & climbed to the very top of it. Called on Luce — nobody — Back to studio at 3.30 — Found Florence Jaylor, English Model, waiting — Started a drawing "Mining", from her — worked until after 6 — When packed [illegible] horse & home to tea at 7.30 — Eve'g spent with Duning — got to bed shortly before 11. Some rain in the eve'g.

## Right Page

### June Saturday, 20, 1874

Overcast & pleasant. Got to studio at 9.30 and to work on "Mining" — After until afternoon o'clk — Then home to lunch — got back to studio about 2.45 — Miss Jaylor came about 4 o'clk — but I did not work from her. — I worked steadily on "Mining" until near 6.30 — Home to tea about 7 — at 8 o'clk went with Kate to Thomas's Garden — Heard Andante & March from Raff's "Lenore" — got home at 11.30 & to bed about midnight —
P.M. Father & Mother went down to Plainfield to spend Sundays.
