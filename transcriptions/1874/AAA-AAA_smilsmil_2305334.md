## Left Page

### December Thursday, 10 1874

overcast but promising better weather. Got to Studio at 8.30
& to work on 14×24 — the old, old study above Po'keepsie —
& repainted the sky & went over the entire thing — To
lunch at 12.45 — back at 1.45 and painted until
4.15 — Nicoll came in and stayed until 5.30 — I went
to tea at 6.30 — back to Studio at 7.30 — wrote a note to [illegible]
Bently — at 8.30. F.H. Smith,
J.M. Falconer, Walter Brown
& Bircher came & we talked
Water Color Matters until 10
o'clk. then walked up the Ave
with Smith & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December Friday, 11 1874

Mild pleasant day, although for two hours in the m'f, it
looked very like rain. Got to studio at 8.45 & set to work
on "Meadow etc" — 10×14 — and painted over sky & part of the
central group of trees. Mr.
H.H. Cox & a Mrs. Smith called.
I took lunch in studio at 1.30
& at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal —
Kate came down from Po'keepsie.
Then walked down to Snedecor's
& back to Studios — felt sick &
at 5 o'clk. went to Emma Ireland's
& begged off from dinner there — then
in my bed until 8 oclk. Soon after
Emma, Fanny & Anna & [illegible] cook
came — Dominoes played for us
all until near 11 o'clk — To bed
at 11.15
