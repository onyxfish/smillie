## Left Page

### January, Monday 26, 1874

Splendid cold day. Got to
studio at 9.30 — there for a
few minutes then over to
N.A. of D. & at work hanging
until 12 — Home to lunch
at 2.40 — went to Darley's
Mr. J. W. 30s & saw some of his
new outlines, "Scarlet Letter"
He wants me to engrave them
for him — Met Mr. Darley for
the first time. Got to N.A. of
D. at 3 o'clk. & worked until
5 — Home to tea at 6 — back
to Academy at 7 & working
until 10 — all worked together
more harmoniously than
during the day — Home at
10.30 & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 27, 1874

Overcast & milder. Prepared
portions of catalogue for N. C.
& sent them down to Sackett.
Went to Studio — there for a few
minutes & at 9.45 at N.A. of D.
Bellows just on from Boston.
Worked until 12 — Home to lunch
at 2.45 — back at 1.30. Worked
again until 5 — great trouble
to get things into anything
like shape — Home at 6
very tired — Back to N.A. of D
at 9 & worked faithfully
until 10 getting the hanging
almost done. Home at 10.30
& to bed at 11.
