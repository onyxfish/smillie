## Left Page

### March, Sunday, 1, 1874

Delightful day — Did not get up until after 9 o'clk
& got to church late —
Heard Dr. Hastings preach
from 19th Ch. Luke — first
16 verses — his story of Zaccheus.
After service walked up
5th Ave to 50th St — into Allie
Diven — then home. Dinner
at 1.30 — a snooze on the
sofa & at 5.30 went to
Emma Ireland's to tea —
at 7.15 to studio to meet
the young man who is to write
Saguenay article — he did not
come. Amused myself
writing until near 9, then
home — Glorious night —
to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Monday, 2, 1874

Delightful mild day — All
the mts given up to W.C.
distribution at Academy.
At 9 A.M. went up to
[illegible] & saw J. Z. A. Ward,
also Chas. Miller, then
to Tiffany & Gifford. Home
to lunch at 2.15 after
having my head barbered — got to studio at
3 o'clk & found Pickering
from [illegible] followed me & talked
[illegible] P.M. as I
tried to explain things
to him so that he may
write Saguenay article
for P. American — Home
to tea at 7. Matt &
[illegible] arr? from P, went to
Ma J D. & packed Mr.
Geo. Whitney's box — Home
at 10 — wrote to Mr. W. &
to bed at 11.30
