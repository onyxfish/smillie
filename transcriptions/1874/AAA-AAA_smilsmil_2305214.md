## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 8, 1874

April like day, without the showers — Got to studio at 9, 40
Before I got to work, W. T. Richards came in — after him, Metra [illegible] Adams — so
that most of my mf was given to visitors — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.30. Worked until after 4 for A.B.N. Co — Men had to give up, I was so sleepy
Robt. Foote came in, and I engaged him for model —
Home to tea at 7. At 9 —
at N.A. [illegible] D. "Private View" —
Great crowd of people &
saw almost everybody —
Had a good time & home
at 11.15 — Heavy, steady
Rain — To bed at 11.45 —

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 9, 1874

miserable rainy day — Got
to studio at 9.30 & had to
wait until 11 before Jessie
(my model) came. Drew
from her until 1 o'clk —
left her in studio while I went
to Mr. Darcy's with [illegible] etching
— Saw Mrs D — Home to lunch
at 1.45 — back at 2.40 — Drew
again until 4 o'clk, making
very poor work as I feel
tired — [illegible] on it — Through
mismanagement, my fire went
out — Until 6, worked on [illegible] —
proof of Fred's dies, for A.B.N. Co —
Home to tea at 6.45 —
Evef Read newspapers —
Lounged around & to bed
at 10.15
