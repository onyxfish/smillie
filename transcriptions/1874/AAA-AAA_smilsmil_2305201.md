## Left Page

### March Friday, 13 1874

Very cold + the blustering weather conditions unabated.
Up late — got to studio at 10 o'clk. Traced and
transferred to block outline of "Idle Hours" — for Aldine
Almanac. Home to lunch at 1.15 — Rode down to Phil.
Rehearsal with Mr. Perkins.
Back to studio at 5 —
Pitched in + had a regular sweep up. Very tired, so
lay down on lounge until 7 o'clk. Then home + tea
at 7.30 — Evef, lounging.
To bed at 10.15

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 14 1874

Charming day, although yet very blomy + dirty. Up with
a headache + got to studio at 10 o'clk. Set to work on
block for Aldine, "Idle Hours".
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.40. Found Josie Jessup
& Allie Dixen waiting for me. Worked on block
until almost 6 when I got it done. Home to tea
at 6.30 — Evef went with Mam to Phil. In the cars
met Mort, Mortimer + Nellie.
Edith Wright at the Concert.
On our way home, again met
M. M. & N. in the cars —
To bed at 11.30.
