## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 17 1874

Fine wintery day. Got to studio at 9.40 & to work on Graphic Etching — finished it, then on to N.Y. City arms for A.B.
N.C. — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.35. Lauthier called, took water color drawing & gave me two orders at very low figures ($50 TT 75) worked on arms until aftn 5. At 6.15 went to Kimball's to tea. Emma D. Annie C. & Mary Willard there. At 7.30 at T. [illegible]'s — Annual meeting of W. C. Society — a full meeting — was Re-elected Pres. Home & to bed shortly after
12 —

## Right Page

### February Wednesday, 18 1874

Splendid day — Up late & did not get to studio until 10.15 finished City arms for A.B.N.Co & home to lunch at 1.45. back at 3.15 after going to Knoedler's for material — The Rest of P.m. much broken up by callers. Home to tea at 6.30, feeling very tired. Everts, Wills and came at 6.30 — at 9.30 D. went to the N.A. of D. Home again at 10.30 & to bed at
11.15
