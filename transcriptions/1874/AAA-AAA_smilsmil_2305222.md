## Left Page

### April, Friday, 24, 1874

Pleasant, spring-like day —
Feel somewhat improved —
Got to studio at 9.45 — Kindled
fire — At noon Emma Ireland
called — walked over home with
her — then to N.R. f.D. — Home
to lunch at 1.15 — Back at
2.45 after calling at Darley's
— not in — At 4 o'clk. went
to Dewey's to try on a coat, met
there Will Kimball — we walked
together as far as Jno. J. Johnston's
I went in — a Reception to
artists — Had a very pleasant
time among my friends — met
many — Left at 5.40 — Stopped
at Sommerville Gallery — then
at Studio & home to tea at 7.15
Uncle W. & aunt Agnes there —
Evg., on W.C. Sales acct —
To bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 25, 1874

A most horrible day — driving wind
& rain. Got to studio at 10 o'clk.
& to work on Vulcan drawing — At
it until 1 o'clk — then prepared
& ate lunch. Worked until
about 6 o'clk. finding great
trouble with the paper —
Home & tea at 7 — Evg.,
Reading — Water Color accts.
& writing to Saml. Colman.
Got to bed at 12 o'clk —
