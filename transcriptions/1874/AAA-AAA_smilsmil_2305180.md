## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 28, 1874

Nearly ten o'clk when I got to N.A. of D. — The hanging in
showed through, many drawings being thrown out. abt 11 o'clk
first part of catalogue sent to printer — Raising steadily and our prospects
for the night dismal enough. Things were ablast so far
finished that at 1 o'clk I went home to lunch & then to bed
& had a nap of an hour or more.
About 5 again at Academy — In trouble about some hangings —
left at 6 & home to tea — Went at 8 o'clk. back with Charl —
a very pleasant crowd and every thing seemed to go off
very pleasantly — left shortly after 11 — home & to bed at
12.30 —
afternoon cleared charmingly

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 29, 1874

In bed all day with a splitting headache —
filled up with gruel at about 1 p.m. & got up at
9 o'clk — Rested on sofa in blanket until 3 a.m.
then to bed & to sleep.
