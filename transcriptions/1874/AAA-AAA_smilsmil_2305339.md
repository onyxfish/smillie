## Left Page

### December Sunday, 20 1874

Glorious snow storm all day —
It kept me a prisoner in the
house, but from the windows
& piazza I enjoyed it ex-
ceedingly — Bkfst at 8.30
— impossible to say how the
mrg went — Dinner about
2 — and then the rest of
the afternoon went — About
5.30 I went out & shovelled
snow for fun — Tea at 6.30
& then the evng went far
into the night — Blowing
a real guns when I went
to bed at 11, 15 —

## Right Page

### December Monday, 21 1874

Glorious day — Had bkfst about
8 oclk. I took the 9.02 train into
town — Called at A.B.N. Co. and
saw Will, then to studio where
I am shortly after 11 — Will
sent up a boy & by him I sent
two little paintings down town.
To lunch at 12.30 — back before
2 & all the P.M. spent in
hunting up & arranging studies
& sketches that I will want
for my water color work — To
tea at 7 o'clk — Back to studio
at 8.30 &, mistaking the time,
I stayed & bothered over
water color accts until after
11 — To bed at 11.45.
