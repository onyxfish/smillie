## Left Page

### April Tuesday, 28 1874.

A perfectly atrocious combination of snow, sleet
and rain — Got to studio at 9 o'clk, worked badly
on Vulcan until 1 15, then home to lunch — back
at 3 — Gw. Ward Nichols & J.C. Nicoll used up
pretty much the rest of my p.m. — Home to
tea at 7.30 — Evening writing water color biz
& to H.F. Tyler —
To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### April Wednesday, 29 1874.

Horrible slush and snow — then rain — P.M. about 5 o'clk cleared
very cold & windy — Got to studio at 9.45 worked until after 1
on Vulcan — Then for expand lunch & ate it, worked
until 5 o'clk — Then went to Sauthier's & Snedecors —
Back to studio at 6.20
Home to tea at 7 — Evening
writing letters until 10.30
To bed at 10.45
