## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 19 1874.

Still dark & threatening weather at times drizzling — Got to studio at 8.30 & for a time worked on water color drawings — then continued on Steam fire engine for B. H. Co. Dinner at 1.15 — back to work at 2.15 — continued on fire engine until shortly after 4 o'clk — then off with Geo & in spite of wind & weather, had a fine skate ride. Tea at 6.30 — After tea, which with Mr. Post, Flora & [illegible] &, at 8.30 went down town to [illegible] Fairs — There until about 11 — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 20 1874.

Very heavy rain & wind all the mng. Bkft. at 9 o'clk — About 10.30 went to studio. Kindled fire and spent an hour or more writing their will. W. J. T. & Geo came in & we chatted until 1.30 — then dinner — After dinner, again in studio — there until near 5 o'clk — weather cleared beautifully — went over to stable & groomed [illegible] m — Tea at 6 — Chatting until after 7 — then dressed & went to the Episcopal Cha. — great crowd. Sammy Lines preached — Text, Christ stilling the waves. Then confirmation by the Bishop, & 16 candidates. At 9, with Geo went in to Fairs — Home & to bed at 10.30.
