## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 30 1874

Charming day. After bkfst I began to feel very much out of sorts — nausea & pain — I stuck to my drawing & dlettons during the m'f. but was obliged to give up soon after dinner — I went to my bed & stayed there until about 7 o'clk. I did not go down to tea but sat in father's room until about 10.30 — when I crawled off to bed again.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 31 1874

Very cold but subpert day. I got up & took a light bkfst. at 8.30 feeling better. Then fussed with some plumbing work & finally got at my drawing about 10.30 — worked for an hour or so when my sore stomach again bothered me so that I had to stop. Dinner at 1 o'clk. Again tried to work but it was fighting against great odds and what I did was very unsatisfactory. About 4 o'clk Mr. Loomis called. Got & with him until 6 o'clk. Then tea — well spent in family chat and a little reading — Got to bed at 10.30 without paying any particular respect to the old year, now at its last hours.
