## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 3 1874

Very hot & no rain yet — Got to
studio som. afting & to work
on "Thresher" — made slow progress
my head being very heavy and
aching. Dinner at 1.30 — Worked
until 4 o'clks. getting my drawing
almost done — then gave up
& went over to my bed — Soon
got up & went out on horseback
with Ge. Sky suddenly over-
cast & threats of rain — Rode
in a whirlwind of dust — in
at 5.40 — again on bed for a few
minutes before tea at 6.30 — Mr.
Strawbridge & daughter of Phila.
arr. After tea I went to bed
again & did not get up until
12, when I undressed — A party
at Azur[d]'s — Ge. went —

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 4 1874

Cool m'g, & no rain — Got over to studio
about 8 o'clks. — did a little finishing
on "Thresher" — wrote letter to Father
& Vanzandt, then down town, packed
drawing & got it off by 1 o'clk. S.P. —
Dinner at 1.30 — Josie Reutters and
Betty's there — a visit to studio,
after — Cleared to a bright, beautiful
day. Mounted paper on stretcher
— 16 x 14 y4. to commence cartoon
of "Racing in the forenite" — Then
made drawing for Cottrell & [Caske]
that he is to make for me — Soon
after 4 went to stables — groomed
Tom & then went out on him with
Ge. Had a good ride — in at 6 —
Tea at 6.30 — Went down to Cottrell's —
then to Fan's & at G. with Ge. to
[L.] Sear's — Home at 10 & to bed
at 10.30 —
