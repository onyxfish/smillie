## Left Page

### July, Saturday 11, 1874

Continual showers all day. Dismal does not suppress it —
Late &kfo— again. Got to my work, Gel. Arms, about 10 o'clk —
Kept at it until dinner time — after 1 o'clk — After dinner,
went down town to see Cottrell. He will not be able to commence
work for me until a week from Monday. Got to my work again
after 3 o'clk & continued until 6 — then Tea. — Evef, went
down town — at Fau M's — Elisha there — played whist. Julia
Read, Alice Strond & Fanny Jenks came in & had a regular
Romp. Home soon after 10 —
Read Tribune until 11. Han to bed —

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 12, 1874

Still Raining — Did not get bkfp. until 9 o'clts. Myf, went
to the Baptist Ch. Took a seat in the loafer's pew when Mr. Cheshire
came out of the pulpit & led me to a seat in the body of the church —
He preached with much energy a very good & stirring sermon from
1st Samuel. H. Ch, 17:18v. After service as it rained stopped in at Fau's there
met Het Searle — went home with her & saw the Sneads. Home to dinner at
1.30 — P.M. Read newspapers — then walked with Uncle A. & Gel. Soon
driven in by the rain — wrote a letter to Kate. Tea at 6 —
At 7 went with Uncle A. to bid Fau good bye — Het & Sneads there —
went to P.O. walked around by Baptist Ch, met Het & S's — went home with
them — there until 9 — left Uncle A. —
Ret. to Fau's — found Gel. there —
Home at 9.30 — To bed at 10.45
