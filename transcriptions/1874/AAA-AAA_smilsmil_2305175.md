## Left Page

### January, Sunday, 18, 1874

Delightful day — clear & very much milder —
mf. went to Dr. Hastings & heard him preach a tip
top Home Mission sermon from Deut. [illegible] 4th Ch. 5. 6. 7th vs.
Dinner at home at 1.15.
After dinner wrote at note for Mr. Walter Brown to give to Clarence Cooke, giving data for an article on our coming W.C. exhibit that C.C.
has promised to write for the Tribune — at 4 o'clk.
took it to Mr. B. in 23d St. —
Home at 5.30 — tea about 6.30 — went ms of a and writing — pain in stomach & flatulent — To bed abt 15 —

## Right Page

### January, Monday, 19, 1874

Thick, foggy & rainy — Got to studio at 9.30 & spent the mng. finishing Matt's small drawing — "Among the Islands. Lake George" — Home to lunch at 1.15 — Matt there —
came down to see Jo — off for Florida — but he wont start until to-morrow. — Back to studio at 2.30 & worked on Calf drawing until 4 — Tired out & could not see any longer — Nicole came in — at 5 went down to Lauthers — Then to Schaus's — back to Studio at 6 — Home to tea at 6.30 — Werf. lying about until 10 o'clk — To bed soon after.
