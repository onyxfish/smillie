## Left Page

### December Saturday, 26 1874.

Overcast most of this day & rather raw. B[illegible] ab't 30. Soon after 9 got to work preparing to put on paper drawing w'N. C. of "Idle Hours" but feeling the need of exercise at last I went out for a short walk. In & at my work ab't 10.30 — Dinner at 1. About 2, again at my work & by squares enlarged from small pencil sketch & commenced out-linning for W. C. drawing 10×12 in. It was nearly 5 before Father & I got away for our walk, owing to callers. We walked out by the "old farm" & in by the "lower road". Tea at 6. At 7.30 Geo & I called on Mr. & Mrs. Loomis. A pleasant, chatty time until about 9, then we went to Mr. Parker's & brought home Kate & Emma B. at 10.30 — There had been a slight fall of sleet & rain that made very slippery walking. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 27 1874.

Delicious m'f. About 10.30 I walked out to the top of the "Big Hill" & listened to the "ringing in" of the church bells & enjoyed the view to the south — very dreamy & luminous — Walked out to Hooker Ave. & then in to Vassar College & back by college Ave. Before noon the walking was very bad. Home at 1 o'clk. Dinner soon after. The afternoon was spent in snoose and gossip. Tea at 6. Eve'g, more gossip & wrote letters to Belle S. & Fan W. — got to bed ab't 11 o'clk.
