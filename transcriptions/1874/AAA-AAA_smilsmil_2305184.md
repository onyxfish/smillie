## Left Page

### February Thursday, 5 1874.

Very fine day — at 9.30 went to Mr. Walter Brown's — then to N.A. of D. — 10.30 when I got to Studio. Then Nicoll came in at work on Montreal block. Home to lunch at 1.30, back at 2.45 + worked until after 5 — Ferguson came in — fixed up + went to Nicoll's at 6 o'clk. to dinner. Two or three other elderly people there, but had a very good time. At 8.45 excused myself + went down to Mr. Norton's with his old Master — back to Nicoll's at 9.15 — Rev. Wykoof + wife there. Nicoll + I went to N.A. of D. — Home at 10.30 — Dr. + brother, Judson an[d] — [illegible] and at 11.30 —
Father much improved —

## Right Page

### February Friday, 6 1874.

Fine day. 9.30 again when I got to Studio + not until when I got to work on block "Montreal on the bottom view of the fire. Home to lunch at 1.30 — could not go to Phil. Rehearsal, so back to studio at 2.30 and got my block finished by 5 o'clk. altho' hindered by Dyer of Montreal, who made me a long call. Chas. came in after Phil. Rehearsal. Home to tea at 6. Well. Wrote notes to Smiley, Vanplaunde, Falconer + Geo. Rec'd quite a long letter from Geo. at Jacksonville. Fla. got to bed at 11.25 Snowing heavily.
