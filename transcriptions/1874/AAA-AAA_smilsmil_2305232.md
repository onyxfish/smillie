## Left Page

### May, Thursday 14, 1874

Very warm day — got to studio at 9.30 & then went directly to Union League &
hung pictures with Walter Brown & Nicoll until after 12.
Then to studio & worked on my small drawing until
1.30 — then took it to the League & finished hanging —
Home to lunch at 2.15 —
back to studio, after writing
notes to Hy Tyler — Fan M. &
Mrs Lathrop — at 3.30 — Prep'd
paper to start drawings
for A.B.N. Co — 7.30 before
I got home — Tea at 8 —
Father has agreed with East-
man to buy his house, No 1 —
in P— At 9 went to League —
a fine supper given to the
W.C. members — Home at
11 o'clk. & right to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 15, 1874

Pleasant day, but overcast, got
to studio at 9.20 & most of the
day arranging accts. & papers of
W.C. Society to present them to
the Board of Control this P. M. —
Started drawing "Interior of
smelting Furnace" for A.B.N. Co —
Home to dinner at 1.30. Back
about 3 — At 4.30 went to
Walter Brown's Room — Meeting
of B. of C. Submitted books &
accts — all very satisfactory —
Did not get home to tea until
after 7 — At 8.30 — Raining
steadily — went to the Kimball's
— there until 10.45 — then home
& to bed at 11.30
