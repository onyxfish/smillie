## Left Page

### Sept. Sunday, 13 1874.

Some hopes of rain — a strong wind from the S.E. — overcast sky — went out to stable — cleaned up — Rode Tom + led Bill down to the Jail Trough to water — Back at 8.30 — Mr[s]. went to the Baptist ch. + heard Mr. Cheshire preach from Acts 13: & 38th v. After service walked up with Uncle A. Niles + Geo. — stopped in at Janis for a few minutes — then on to the Fair ground — Dinner at 1.30 — Mr. + Mrs. McCartney there — Afternoon until 5 o'clk. in studio, writing — then with Geo. — went out + had a sharp ride on horseback until 5.50 — Tea at 6.30 — Dr. + Elisha Mulford then called about 7.30 — Went to studio + wrote for about an hour.

## Right Page

### Sept. Monday, 14 1874.

Overcast mor. with more hopes of rain — but it cleared before noon. Again at work, charcoaling on "Race" + making very little progress. I am undertaking to do more than I know how to do — Dinner + soon after 1 o'clk. — from 2 until 4.30 worked on drawing then went out on Tom's back while Geo. + Joe rode in the buggy — Got back at 6.30 after a good ride — the colt improves very fast. Tea at 7 — Everyone waited with Mr. + Miss Strawbridge until after 8 — then on W[?] to piazza until 9 — then at [Dean?] Searle's until after 10 — then home + to bed at 10.30.
