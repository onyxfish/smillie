## Left Page

### March, Sunday, 15, 1874

Delightful day. Myf
went to D. Hastings' + heard
him preach a characteristic
study of a sermon on Baalaam
from Numbers 22. ch. 18. v —
Willie sat opposite me — his
first visit to church. After
services, walked with Josie
F. & Allie D. up 5th Ave. to 72d
St. then home to dinner at
1.30 — At 3.30 walked
up to Wills — Josie very sick
then to Uncle A's. There to
tea — Ben Horn there — left
at 8.30 + stopped at Collegiate
Ch., 5th Ave. + 48th S.

To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### March, Monday, 16, 1874

Overcast & threatening bad weather.
Got to studio at 9.10. Kindled
fire — felt so much out of sorts
that I did not dare begin
to paint on "Raquette Lake" —
Put in background on fire
engine die [?] for B.N. Co.
Home to lunch at 1.30 —
Back at 2.40 — Pencilled
up day, sitting of Dog with
Mf, Sub. Treas't Bld. for a
background. Wasted some
time playing myself with
Mr. Browning & Miss Shattuck
in Miss Wordward's Room —
At 6.15 at Mrs. Kimball's to
tea — At 8 we all went down
to Annie Cook's + had a Rehearsal of our Kindersymphony —
Home at 11.30 + right to bed —
Mergling — Edith Wright
called at studio at 10 o'clk
this mf.
