## Left Page

### March, Sunday, 29, 1874

Still charming weather.
Mr. Ed. & Matt came up
from the P. boat — went
to Hastings's & heard him
preach from Job 15. 11.
"Are the consolations of God
small with thee?"
Dinner at 1.15 — P.M. wrote
several notes & at 5 walked
up to Central Park — took
tea at Uncle a's — Every
went to Collegiate Ch. 5th
Ave & 148th S. & heard a
discursive sermon on
Babylon — Home at 9.15
& to bed soon after 11 —

## Right Page

### March, Monday, 30, 1874

Delightful day. Got to studio
at 9.15 — Kindled fire & to work
on painting 14×24 — old Study
news near to keep air — also
fixed up "October" — frame, etc.
for Dodworth — Ab't 6 clk. went
to Dilleck's — at 1.30 at Darling
got a Scarlet Letter outline to
etch one of the figures. Home
to lunch at 2 — back at
2.50 — Got out plates and
traced some of the figures
in Dareing outline — [illegible]
fussed about until 7.30
then home to tea at 8 —
Every. Read news papers
& got to bed at 10.45
