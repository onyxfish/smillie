## Left Page

### May, Monday, 18, 1874

Wet all day — cleared beautifully about 6 P.M. — Got to Studio at 9 and worked steadily on Smelting Furnace until 12.30. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked until near 7 — after making up my mind that I could not make such a drawing as I wished, I then found Siccatif de Haarlem over it, so that to-morrow I may paint it in oil — Home at 7.30 — Tea at 8. Read newspapers — wrote a note or two and got to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday, 19, 1874

Delightful day — Got to Studio at 9.15 and commenced the painting over the drawing of Smelting Furnace — worked steadily until 1 o'clk — then home to lunch. Wrote a note to Falconer, then back to Studio at 2.45 — Painted until 7 o'clk — Cleaned up and went to see McCall. Home to tea at 8 o'clk — Tea at 8.30 — Newspapers — then to bed at 11.15.
