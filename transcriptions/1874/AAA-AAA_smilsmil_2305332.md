## Left Page

### December Sunday, 6 1874

Overcast and mild. My went to young Tyng's and heard a good Sermon upon who is greatest in the kingdom of Heaven. Met R. M. Shurtleff there — After service we took a short walk together & then called upon F. H. Smith — After a pleasant little visit J. walked up to A. N. V.'s — getting there at 1.40 Dinner after 2. Geo. and Charl there — At 3.30 into Park to walk — went over to Will's. Bid Annie Bradly goodbye. Back to Uncle A's at 6.15. Adams & Minnie Raym to tea. At 7.30 walked down with Geo — It began to rain so we hurried to our Room. Wrote two or three notes & to bed at 10.25

## Right Page

### December Monday, 7 1874

Overcast all the mrf. P.m. clear. Got to studio at 9.10 — J.G. Brown soon came in, drumming for pictures to be sold for the benefit of the N. A. of D. A large part of the mrf. spent in discussion with him — then began painting 10×14 in — "Meadow near Smith Belee's" — To dinner at 1 — back at 1.45 — Painted badly during the P.m. accomplishing but little. Will came up about 5. in a very nervous state — sure he is going to die of heart disease. To his at 6.30 — back to studio at 8. & wrote — new constitution for W. C. Soc'y until 10.30 — To my Room & to bed. Shortly after 11.
