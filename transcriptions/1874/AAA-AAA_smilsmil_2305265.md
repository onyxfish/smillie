## Left Page

### July, Tuesday, 21, 1874.

Overcast mng. with slight sprinkle.
Up early & before Bkfst went to studio.
B. there & C. & his boy — Muffon was
finished before 11 o'clk. C. & boy then
did some bussing around — C. fixing
the shingles on W. J. T's barn — Dinner
shortly after 1 o'clk. Then a while
with B — afternoon generally clear —
at 3 o'clk. set to work on Louisiana
arms & washed in effect. Tea
at 6 o'clk. At 7 went out & took a
short ride on Tyler's colt. He is
very awkward, but quick, kind &
intelligent. Soon after 8, washed &
redressed — then spent the evng.
in Mrs. Dud's — Rev. W. Cheshire
called & Mrs. Reid & a Miss Deal.
To bed at 11 o'clk after reading
the newspapers.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday, 22, 1874.

Superb day. After breakfast
went down to Cotrell's shop and
lugged up two sashes for my
windows — Backens finished
papering at noon — Two pkgs.
came for me by exps. — Muslin
for my dralls & large photo.
from Renner & McKeever from a
painting by Wagner — "Chariot
Racing — Colosseum" — Dinner
at 1 o'clk. — P.m. — Austin
did some painting and I
stretched one parcel of muslin —
got glue to make sizing —
Tea at 6 o'clk. — At 7 went
out to ride on "Major" — Tyler's
colt. — He stumbled — burst
his saddle girth & rolled me
off. — He ran for a ways, but
he was caught without trouble.
I was bruised on hip & shoulder.
[illegible] in Mrs. Dud's & for a while
on [illegible] piazza — To bed at
11 — after reading Beecher-Tilton
