## Left Page

### June, Thursday 11, 1874

Heavy showers at intervals during the day — Did not
get to studio until 10.15
The mng. spent working on
A.B.N. Co's Smelting Furnace
& W.C. Sketch f "Old Fort at
St. Augustine, Fla" — very
heavy shower at noon, so that
I could not go to the steamer
to see the Browns & Gifford
off. Miss Woodward gave
me lunch about 2 o'clk.,
P.M. — mounted paper,
patched Uncle Willie's torn
"Casilear" & got things into
shape to leave for the summer
— Ferguson made a long call
— Home at 7 o'clk — tea —
Evg., fussing to get things
fixed up so as to leave
town — To bed at 10.15
nasty, raw night.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 12, 1874

Very warm but delightful day —
got to studio at 9.45. Marston
& 14th St. Theatre called — very
much pleased with my com-
position. Spent the morning
getting things in order to leave.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30. Miss Scott came to
take her cash lesson. Stayed
until after 6 o'clk. Katie
[illegible] & Mather called —
I got home to tea at 8
o'clk. About 9, got down to
Anna Cooke's — hung her
pictures — had a very pleasant
evg. — then got my check for
$240.00 Home at 11.15 and
to bed at 11.45
