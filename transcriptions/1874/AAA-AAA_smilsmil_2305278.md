## Left Page

### August Sunday, 16 1874.

In bed all day with a great variety of entertain-
ments. Headache — back ache
— stomachache — lots of other
aches. In the even't, I took
a cup of tea. Tyler's Sarah
made me a bowl of gruel.
J. was very kind in offering
to do anything. I got to bed
again about 9 o'ck — but it
was almost 11, before
I got to sleep.

## Right Page

### August Monday, 17 1874.

Very warm, but delightful
day. Up at 7 feeling exceed-
ingly flimsy. Soon after 9,
J. M. sent up for me to go with
them to Salt Spring on a Pic Nic.
but I did not feel able. Besides,
I had to be with C. finishing
up in studio. At 12, Rested
in my bed for an hour. Dinner
at 1.30. In studio again
until 6. Then tea. About
7, as added "Jom" & took a
short ride. Then cleaned
stable & fixed it for the night.
At 8.30 went down town — for
a few minutes at Jan's — at
9.30 at Mrs. Dud's. Home
at 10.15 & to bed at 10.45.
