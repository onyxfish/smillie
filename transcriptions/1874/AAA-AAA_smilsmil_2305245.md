## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 9, 1874

Sunday. 7th

Have written Monday & Tuesday on Sunday & Monday pages,
forgetting that Sunday was a headache day & so, blotted
out, for I did not get out of
my bed until about 6.30
P.M. Then up, ate a little
& conversed about until between
2 & 3 A.M. — then to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 10, 1874

Delightful day, altho' very
warm. Got to studio at
9.30 & worked all the m'f
on "New Alabama Arms" for
A.B.N. Co. At 12.30 went to
N.A. of D. Then to Schmitt's —
then to Dunlap's — then home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.30 — Russell took". Racquette
Lake" home to Miss Cook.
Katie Greatorp called — also
Nicoll. Worked on drawing
until after 7 — near 8 when
I got home — then tea —
then, Kate helping me, I
mounted 9 large Watkins
Photos. Got to bed about
11.45 — very tired.

Ed W. Brown called at the
studio & presented me with
a copy of his poems.
