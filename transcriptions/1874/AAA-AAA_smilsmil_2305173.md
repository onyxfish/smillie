## Left Page

### January Wednesday, 14 1874.

Snow storm in the mrf. got
to studio at 9.45 & to worke
on foreground of Calf drawing
getting in my first washes
& scheme of color. At 1.30
I prepared my lunch —
Som. after, Borg came with
drawing, just home from
Fenn, now in England —
Worked until 4.45, getting
all of my paper covered
except the calf — Home to
tea at 6.30 — Evef, in
big chair & wrote a note
to Fenn — got to bed
at 10.45

## Right Page

### January Thursday, 15 1874.

Very cold & windy — Got to studio
at 9.45. & spent the mrf pencilling
small copy of Oertel's Cattle
drawing for B.B.N. Co — Then
worked for an hour on W.C.
studies by Ferguson. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15
& worked on Calf drawing —
Had many callers — Nicole and
Walter Brown called in the mrf
Mrs. Parkins & Walsh & Misses
James & Helen Ripley called
at about 4 o'clk — Later, Louis
Tiffany came in — Home to
tea at 6.15 Evef, mounted
three of Ferguson's drawings
& wrote to Burling & Hackett
about Society printing.
To bed at 10.30
Rec'd. letter from b. C. f.
