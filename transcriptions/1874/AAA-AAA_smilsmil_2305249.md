## Left Page

### June, Wednesday, 17, 1874

Dark + muggy m'f, cleared very hot, but heavy showers bet
12 & 1 o'clk. cooled the air —
Got to studio at 9.30 + started work on "View of Salina, Kansas"
for A.B.N. Co. Salvini, the Italian tragedian came in with
Vaini — Silva called. — Home to lunch at about 2 o'clk — back
at 3 + worked until after 6 —
then took down my curtains
in Miss Woodward's Room and
fussed with my packing until
after 7 — Home + tea at 8 —
Went up to Burgraff's —
Read until 10. To bed at
10.45

## Right Page

### June, Thursday, 18, 1874

Delicious day — got to studio
at 9.35 + worked steadily until
1.5 then home to lunch — back
at 2.35 + again worked until
after 6 o'clk. A.W. Palmer
sent up a note declining
to give $150 00 for "D rolp" — I
sent back word that he
might have it for the $75.
first agreed to. Miss Taylor
a new English model called.
Worked until near 8 o'clk
packing his boxes — then
home — got through my tea
just before 9 o'clk — Made paste
\+ mounting drawings until
11 o'clk — Father + Mother
took dinner at Willis's —
To bed at 11.20
