## Left Page

### October Saturday, 31 1874.

Yet another pleasant autumn day. B'kf't. at 1.30 — then packed things to leave Mrs. Blandge — had a pleasant talk with her. Got to studio about 10 & spent a part of m'g getting to work on etching — About 1 o'clk went out with Ferguson & had oysters — Etched until 3.15 — then to 42d St. & off on 3.40 train. Got to Poughkeepsie abt 6 o'clk — went at once to father's. Tea — Ev'g. Matt & Ed. there — General chat & to bed after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 1 1874.

Clouded & chilly o'v'f. Went with father & mother to Bapt'ist Ch. & heard Mr. Kendricks — His text was the words of Christ from the cross — After service I went to Cong'l Ch. & there met uncle W. & family. Then to Mrs. Parker's & from there walked home with Charlie. Dinner abt 3.30 — After dinner took a long snooze which used up the p.m. Tea abt 6. I spent the even'g in father's Eng. Room, Ruminating. Did not get to bed until 11.45.
