## Left Page

### January, Friday 16, 1874

Enter on my 41st year to-day.
Very clear & cold. Got to studio
abt 9.40 & to work on calf drawing.
Painted in calf — worked until
after 2 o'clk — Then Belle &
Mary Tweedy came in — then
Jamie & Flora Larthrop —
Home to lunch abt 1.15 — at
2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal —
After it, went to Avery's &
talked with him of Walter
Color matters — at studio
at 5.30 — Home to tea at
6.20 — Every wrote a
letter to Mr. J. Richards &
to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 17, 1874

Still cold & clear. Got to studio
at 9 o'clk, & pitched into my
calf. Got along very encouragingly on the foreground — Home
to lunch at 1.15 — back at
2.30, but soon found that I
was very tired. Worked
until a little after 4 —
Then to Y.M.C.A. Gifford's
Room — Board of Control. W.C.
Gifford, Tiffany, Nicoll, Eaton
& Burling present. Walter Brown
came in about 5.30. Broke
up at 6 — Then home to tea.
Dressed & with Kate to
Phil. Concert. Rested while
listening to the music —
Home at 10.30 — To bed at
11.15
