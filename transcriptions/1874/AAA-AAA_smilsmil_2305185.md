## Left Page

### February Saturday, 7 1874.

Stormy mf + overcast day.
Got to studio at 9.30 — Some
water leaking in at my skylight
my french modes — De Bory [illegible]
came about 10 o'clk — It was
after 11 when I got started
upon some studies from him.
Home to lunch at 1.50 — back
at 2.30 — She, in the mean time
having stayed in the studio
worked as long as I could
see, getting something very
tolerable. — Home to tea
at 6.45 — Every, after some
writing went to [illegible] D —
thun, after 10, to Century
Club to see Mr. Collins —
Home at 11 + to bed at
11.45

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 8 1874.

Delicious winter day —
Sate to Church — so that I
did not hear Dr. Hastings'
text; but I heard a tip top
sermon without any text. —
Dinner at 1.30 — after
dinner Took a long nap,
then wrote a note and
after that took a walk up
to uncle A's + back home
to tea at 6.30 — Every, went
to Hastings' with Mary —
Tuft 119th Psalm 59 + 60th v.
Home at 9 — wrote a letter
to W. D. Howells + got to
bed at midnight.
