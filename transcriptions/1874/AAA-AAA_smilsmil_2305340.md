## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 22 1874.

Overcast — Got to studio at 9 — & to work planning my water color work — Head hurt me and I made but little progress. To lunch at 12.30 & back at 1.45 — Afternoon did no better than in the mng — Rain commenced after 4 o'clk. I made [illegible] & mounted W.C. drawings for Miss Gilbert & for Geo — [illegible] Took Miss Woodward to Mrs. Shattuck's — 754. 5th ave & in to tea at 7.20. Charl spent the evng with us. I worried over W.C. accts, until after 11. To bed at 11.20.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 23 1874.

Very mild, pleasant day — Streets all afloat. Got to Studio at 8.45 & again puzzling over W.C. accts, trying to reconcile & understand some discrepancies. Then sketching for placard to be printed in colors. To lunch at 12.30, back at 1.45 — Spent an hour or two trying to plan a Yosemite drawing 14 × 25 in in — little progress. At 4 men over to Gilleck's — then to Snedecor's — then to Leavitt's — back to studio at 6. To tea at 6.30 — Eve'g I felt too tired to go down to the Leavitt sale, where I had three pictures, so, at 9.30 I went to bed. [illegible — entire passage]
