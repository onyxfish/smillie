## Left Page

### November Monday, 2 1874.

*This Record is for Wed. Nov. 4 — owing to a mistake in turning the leaves —*

Up at 9 — foggy — but clearing charmingly. Bkfs. at 7.35 —
Got to studio at 9 — finished etching on $60.00 P.O. Stamps
& home to dinner at 12.30 —
back at 1.30 — Got my die
Ready for biting but it was
so late that I did not dare
to start it. Miss McSame
called — then fan M. at 4 —
I went with her to the Sturtevant
at 5.30 at Mrs. Blake's and
carried alcohol things of mine
away — Tea at 6.30 when
Geo. arr. from P. Chat and
fussing until after 8 — then to
studio & fixed fire — Home at
10.30 & to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 3 1874.

*This record is for Thurs. Nov. 5 — See foot of next page —*

Dull, foggy & chilly. Bkfs.
at 7.40 — to studio at 9 o'clk
and set to work biting in die,
used Spencer Acid & the result
was a decided success — Toward
noon began to suffer from a
headache — Got through with my
biting about 2 o'clk — At 2.30
went to the Sturtevant He. and
dined with fan & the Dr. About
3.45 walked with fan down
to 23d St. then back to studio
& worked with graver until
after 5 — Took no tea & before
8 o'clk on my bed — there
until 11 then undressed &
to bed fairly for the night.
