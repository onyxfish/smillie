## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 8, 1874

Cold, brisk day. Got to studio at
8.45 and to work on 10×14 "Meadow
beyond Beede's" (4th day) — Worked
badly — things didn't go right.
To dinner at 12.30 — back at 1.45
Worked until 4.15 — then could
see no longer. Got a very
short nap on the sofa — Nicoll
came in about 5 & stayed for
an hour. To tea at 6.30 —
Evett, went with Geo. to call
on Miss Scott at Hotel Branting
— Madison ave. & 58th — no him —
went to S.C. yesterday — Mrs.
Tom Tweedy out — then went
to Mrs. P. Townsend's 53 E. 54th.
2d. Pleasant every there until
10 o'clk. Walked down — m
at 10.30 & to bed right away.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 9, 1874

Overcast & rather raw. Got to
studio at 8.40 & to work on 10×14
"Meadows etc" (5th day) — Worked
until 12.30 — then to dinner.
Back at 1.45 & painted until
4.15, then I could see no longer.
At 5 went to Knoedler's and
bought a print to send to
Josie Beatty. Met Satterlee
& D. Johnston. Back to studio,
fixed things for the night &
to tea at 6.30 — At 8.30 called
at the Kimball's — at 9 went
to the Dreland's — met Geo.
there. Left at 10 o'clk — then
listened to Dürring & his piano
until almost 11 — then to bed.
