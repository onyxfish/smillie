## Left Page

### April Tuesday, 14 1874.

Very pleasant day — at studio soon after 9 — at 10 Rob't. Foote (model) came & I drew from him until 12.30 — Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30 & again drew from Foote until 5.30 — Afterwards in worked on the drawing as long as I could sit. Home to tea at 7.30 — at 8.30 at Louis Tiffany's to bid him Goodbye — with his wife and child, he sails for Europe tomorrow. At 9.30 at N.A. of D. met there Vaini & Mr. [illegible] Darenms. Home at 10.20 — to bed at 11.15
Mrs. Brown & daughters called at studio while I was engaged with my model.

## Right Page

### April Wednesday, 15 1874.

Very warm & overcast with some rain — Played out & used up — Did not get to studio until near 10 o'clk — then had to return home for my key — 11 o'clk, when again I got there — Had to kindle fire. Did some work on study of yesterday. Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45 — Did some work but it amounted to nothing. Spent some time on lounge. At 6.45 went with Vaini & was stuffed with an Italian dinner — got through with it at 8.15 — then to Sommerville's Gallery to see Snedecor's pts. Left there at 9.30 — Home & to bed at 10.45
