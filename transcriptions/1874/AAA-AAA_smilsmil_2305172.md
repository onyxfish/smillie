## Left Page

### January, Monday 12, 1874

More gloomy weather — again — waited & did not get to studio
until 9.45 — Kindred first at 10.30 went to Na [illegible] D. & saw
Richards, then to Silleck's —
then downtown to Sackett's at
11.5 — There until 12.45 arranging
printing matters. Then to [illegible]
N. Co & to lunch with father —
then to J. M. Falconer's & at last
at studio at 2.30. Mr. Nickerson
of Boston called with a young
lady — Then Mr. Walter Brown's Bro.
then Ferguson — I got a little
work done on my drawing —
At 6 o'clk — Rushed home, then
back with Sturtevant & dined
with [illegible] M. & the Dr. Home at
9.30 & worked on an Oertel sub.
for father until after 10 — To bed
at 11.15

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 13, 1874

Pleasant w[illegible], but P.m. overcast —
Got to studio at 9.5 & set to work
on calf subject. Nicoll [illegible] others
came in on N. C. business, so
that I was somewhat interrupted.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & worked until a little
after 4 which was as long as I
could see. Then Silva came
& we walked over to Silleck's —
back to studio at 5.30. Home
to tea at 6.30 — Evett, Hy
Ferguson called. I mounted
some Saguenay drawings &
got to bed at 10.30
