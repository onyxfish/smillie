## Left Page

### December, Thursday 24, 1874

Bright day after a cloudy mg with a little Rain —
a sudden snow flurry abt. after bkfo. Packed things
to go to Po'keepsie — About 10
ment down town — called at
Hultatch Lithographic Co, then
on Sackett — then on Charl — then
F.H. Smith, 121 Front — then on Will
at A.B.M. Co — then at 12.45 lunched
then Rode up to Snedecor's +
called on Sauthier. My three
paintings sold for 27. 40. 460 dols
gros — at Leavitt's sale. Will sell
my little up right, "Abrook in Sep."
to Freeland for $35.00. Got to
Studio at 2.40 — packed things
there + got to 41 st 5- at 3.10 —
Swallowed a plate of soup and
at 4 o'clk. with Geo + Charl. Off for
C— got to the house at 6.50 — Soon had
tea — then chatting and to bed
at 10.30

## Right Page

### December, Friday 25, 1874

Superb day — Bkfo. about 9 o'clk.
Between 10 th 11 o'clk. walked out
by the old farm + back by the
lower Road — my head troubling
me so much that I could not
work. Dinner soon after 1.
It was after 3 o'clk. when
Father + I walked out to the
top of the "Big Hill" — then back
through Dwight + Hooker Aves.
and in about 5 o'clk. The
sunset color, exquisite and
very much enjoyed by both of us.
About 5.30 Uncle Willie + his
family am². The Christmas tree
was lighted up + the usual fun-
dance gone through with to the
great delight of Willie, Katie and
other larger children. Tea at 7 —
at 7.30 W.H. Toomis + lady called
+ chatted pleasantly for half an
hour or more — Uncle W.'s family
left at 9.30 — ab't o Charl + I went
out + had a long moonlight tramp —
in ab't 10.45 and right to bed —
