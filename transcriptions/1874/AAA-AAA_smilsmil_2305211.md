## Left Page

### April, Thursday 2, 1874

Up at 9 — Very much from yesterday — Got to studio
at 9.20 — The mff was afoot
with visitors, washing dishes
cleaning up & preparing to
etch Darley's outline —
Davidson called & paid
me $20 for the little Aldine
drawing. Home to lunch at
1.45 — back at 3 — Commenced
etching on figure for Darley
abit until near 6 o'clk —
Home to tea at 9 — Svery
mote to Alfred Jones and
part of a letter to Colman
To bed a little before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 3, 1874

Regular March day with wind &
dirt. Got to studio at 9 o'clk. and
to work etching Darley outline —
Got it finished before I went
home to lunch at 1.30. Detained
by Joe & Junie — Back at 2.45
all the p.m. biting — Darley
called about 4 o'clk. & I lost
considerable time — Worked
until after 6 — my last biting
being a matter of feeling rather
than seeing — Home to tea at
7.15 — Svery, fixing up accts
To bed at 11 —
