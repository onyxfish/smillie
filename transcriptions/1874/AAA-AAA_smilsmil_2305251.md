## Left Page

### June Sunday, 21 1874.

In bed all day with a splitting headache — It seems that a headache is now to be my regular Sunday diversion.

In bed until 8.30 then up & had something to eat. On sofa until 12.30 — then to bed —

## Right Page

### June Monday, 22 1874.

Clear day & very hot. Up late & feeling very slim. Got to studio at 10 o'clk. Florence Taylor came some after & I worked from her face on "Mining" for an hour. Then she went to Kittell & I worked on until 1 o'clk. Went to lunch — back at 2.30 — F. Taylor came again & I worked another hour. I managed to get my drawing finished. At 7, walked up the ave. with Miss Gilbert. Home to tea — Matt & [illegible] am'd from P. [illegible], went over to Duning's — back at 10.30 & to bed at 11.15
