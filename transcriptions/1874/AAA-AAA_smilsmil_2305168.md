## Left Page

### January, Sunday 4, 1874

Muggy and overcast —
Mrs went to Dr. Hastings.
He preached an admirable
New Year Sermon, giving as
the mottoe for the year, the
words "Tender Mercies" — from
Luke 1st Ch. 78th v. — Dinner
at home at 1.30 — At 3, with
Charl, went up to Will's. Thru
across to Uncle A's — Tea at
6 — As it commenced to Rain
did not go to church in the
evng, Home at 10 o'clk — t
Right to bed —

## Right Page

### January, Monday 5, 1874

Overcast and disagreeable
day. Got to studio about
9.45. Kindled fire & to work
on outlining calf drawing.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & worked until after
4 — then prepared and at
4.30 started for Brooklyn.
Reached Mr. Beach's about
5.40 — Had tea soon after
6. About 8, with Emma &
Miss Foote, went to Mr. Healey's
& found the whole family at
home — the daughter, lovely.
Enjoyed my call very much.
Soon after 9, back to Mr. B's —
H.W. Beecher just leaving,
having brought some oranges from
Mr. B. Stowe's Florida home. Found
Tom Sheaman & wife there — Tom
would be funny & very ill mannered
at my expense. Left for home
at about 10 o'clk. Home at 11.15
& Right to bed —
