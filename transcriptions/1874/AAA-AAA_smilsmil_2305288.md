## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 5 1874

Hopes of Rain — a heavy mist
so that the trees dripped — but
it cleared again before noon.
After [illegible] — went out with [illegible]
I measured lot & talked of
price — His ideas are too high
for me — Got to studio at 9.30
& commenced work on Penn
arms drawing — Miss Jewell
called — Dinner at 1.30 — Mr. Read
came in after dinner — worked
until 4.20 — then to stable.
Rode with [illegible] — Tom did
well — he is improving greatly.
In at 6.15 — Tea at 7 — Evening
at 8.30 at Jan's — Whist
with Elisha & the Dr. Home
& to bed ab 10.30.

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 6 1874

Very light rains in the m'f, but
it cleared early & the after-
part of the day was charming —
[illegible] at 9 — went to the Baptist
Ch. & heard Mr. Cheshire. Text
Mat. 5th ch. 18 v. — Luke 16th c. 17th v.
After service to studio & read
part of Prof. Tyndal's lecture, at
Belfast before the Scientific As.
Dinner at 1.30 — Again to
studio — Wrote notes to [illegible] Mr.
Nichols & Menger — At 5 went
to stable & worked over Tom
until 6 — Tea at 6.40 — Evening
again at studio — Wrote to
Father & part of a letter to
Anna J. — At 10.20 went to
the P.O. — then home & to bed.
