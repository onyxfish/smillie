## Left Page

### December. Monday, 14 1874.

Very dark. Heavy rain in the mf. but it cleared to
be a very cold, windy day.
Got to studios at 9.15. Felt
miserable but persisted in
trying to work on "Meadow"
10×14 — (3½ days) — To dinner
at 12.30 — back at 1.45 — felt
better & worked until 3.30 —
then Kate & Fannie came in.
At 4.30 a meeting of the
W. C. Socy Board of Control
was to meet at my room to
take action on the deaths of J.
W. Marshall but it was found
that he had never qualified
as a member. To tea at 6.30.
Evelf. Geo. went with Ferguson
to see Miss. Hitchcock. I
stayed at home & heard During
play — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### December. Tuesday, 15 1874.

Exceedingly cold, windy & clear.
Got to studios at 8.50 & commenced
painting on "Blue Mt. Lake". To
dinner at 12.30 — back at 1.45
& painted until 4 o'clk. when
I got over the whole subject, but
in my desire to get it warm
I got it very red. At 4.30 went
to Snedecors & arranged with
him to put three [illegible] paintings
in his Hubner Sale. Then went
down to Jeavitt's & completed
arrangements with him for the
same thing. Back to studios at
6.15 — cleaned up and to tea
at 6.50. Evelf. with During,
him playing Beethoven until
10.20 — then to bed — [illegible]
