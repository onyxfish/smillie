## Left Page

### November Monday, 30 1874.

Clear, cold day — got to the
Studio at 9 o'clk. made a fin[e]
[attempt] to work on a little
panel. but did not make
a successful start in color.
To dinner at 12.30 — back at
2\. Painted until 4 — then
down to Appleton's — saw
Bunce + Gle + Willie A. —
back to studio at 5.15
\+ tea at 6.30 — at 8, went
up to Will's with Geo —
Charl soon followed us.
Walked down together
\+ got to bed at 11.20

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 1 1874.

Cold & misty with a couple
of attempts at snow squalls —
Got to studio at 8.30 — Charcoaled
a subject on 10×14 — A sort of
Blue Mr. Lake + set to make
painting nit. To dinner at
12.45 — back at 1.45 — During
came in about 2.30 — The
P.M. grew so dark that I
could not see to finish my
sketch. — In to tea at
6.30 — After tea, Charl came
in for half an hour. I
had a head ache — did
a little writing + to bed
at 10.30
