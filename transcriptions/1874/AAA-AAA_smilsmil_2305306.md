## Left Page

### October, Thursday 15, 1874

A glorious day — very cool but
clear & bright. Got to my work
shortly after 9. & commenced
on a sketch suggested by Fitz
Hill — but nothing went right
& finally, before dinner, I took
my knife & scraped off all
that I had done. For a while
picked apples with S. & Carrie.
Dinner soon after 1. At 2 went
down to stables & shortly before 3
S. & I got off on a ride, went
out to the trees beyond Gp. Walcott's
then around by Elk Stakes,
getting in at 6.15. Went at once
to tea. Then for a short time in
at A. Lathrop's — then in studio
& washed up — Spent the evening
with S. & to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### October, Friday 16, 1874

Glorious day. Did not get to breakfast
until about 8.30. Started fire in
studio & to work on small upriver
sketch, Riverview about 9.30. Worked
all the mid morn. About 11 S. came
in with Ketty Beatty, Misses Minty
& [illegible] & a young man. Dinner
at 1.30. W. & Mrs. Havard then. To work
again at 2.30 — finished over sketch
& commenced on another "Insolenn
River & plains & San Joaquin" —
Worked until 5 o'clk. Then S.
came & I walked with her until
after 6. Then tea. Then washing
up in studio — then, at nearly 8,
with S., Aunt H. & Uncle W., went
down to Hy. J's & there until almost
9 — then back to W. J. J.'s — there
until 10 — then to bed at 10.30
