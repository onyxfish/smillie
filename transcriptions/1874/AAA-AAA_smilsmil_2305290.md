## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 9 1874

Very hot & terribly dry. At the
Helps' Table Geo. was taken suddenly
sick — on his back all day — most
of the n[illegible] lost — lots of family callers —
Got to work on drawing (Penna. arms)
about 11.30 — Dinner abt. 1.30 — worked
pretty well during the P.M. until
4.45 — then went out with Will
on horseback — he didn't enjoy it
much — Tea soon after[illegible] — Tyler
secedes & again I have agreed to
take his lot & have at $475.00 —
Evert went down town — after calling
at Lathrop's & bidding Miss Gilbert
good bye — [illegible] until 10 — at Fan's
— then home & to bed — Geo. quiet
but still unable to sit up or
eat.

## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 10 1874

Hotter & dryer than ever — cleaned
out stable before Help's Geo. got up &
dressed but could not go over to his
Help's. I got to studio at 8.30 but
it was afternoon before I got to work
on Penna. arms — worked until
Dinner at 1.30 — afternoon
again at it until about 4.30
when I went over to stable —
gave Tom a clean off — then,
as Geo. was unable — gave
Bill a Rub — cleaned out
stable & by six o'cloc felt
that I did not need any
more exercise — Tea at 6.30
uncle A. & I mine am[illegible] — on the
piazza of the Turrill mansion
until near 9 — then down town
at 9, at Fan's — home soon
after 10 & right to bed.
