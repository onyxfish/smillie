## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 26, 1874

Cloudy & very high wind —
cold too — Mrs. went to
Dr Hastings & heard the man
that is trying to fill his
place preach from the
the words "For all things work
together for good for them that
love the Lord". After service
walked up to 78th St with the
Jessups — Back to Uncle a's
to dinner. After dinner, had
a long talk with Uncle Wm
on the subject of moving my
studio from his loft. At 4.30
went over to Will's — Charl
came & we took tea there —
Walked down home at 8.15 —
To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 27, 1874

Clear, cold & very windy — Up
early & after b'kf's — wrote a note
to Fanny Kimball. It was near
10 o'clk. when I got to the studio.
Kindled fire & spent the m'g
finishing "Young Helpterne" — an
Italian drawing, for the A.B.N.Co.
Home to lunch at 1.45 — back
at 2.45 — Worked on Vulcan
& Minerva all the P.m. altho.
I was much 'nterrupted —
Home to tea at 7 — Evel'g
Read papers & wrote until
10.30 — To bed about 11 —
