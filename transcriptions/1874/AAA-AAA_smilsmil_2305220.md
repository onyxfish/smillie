## Left Page

### April, Monday, 20, 1874.

Did not get up until near
1 o'clk — Then had some
gruel made — rabid —
and spent most of P.M.
on sofa — Tremendous
storm of rain — During
the P.M. — Thunder and
lightning — Eve'g, able
to read newspapers —
got to bed just about
midnight.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday, 21, 1874.

Got to studio at 9.20. Kindled
fire & to work finishing out-
line of Vulcan & Minerva.
The day quite cold — clear
& windy — at 12.30 went to
N.A. of D. to see J.A. Richards.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 — Commenced washes
on drawing — worked until
6 o'clk — Met Emma D.
& walked home with her.
Home to tea at 6.45 — Eve'g
arranging W.C. accts and
writing — To bed at
