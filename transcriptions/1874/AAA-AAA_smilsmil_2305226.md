## Left Page

### May Saturday, 2 1874.

Cloudy but pleasant — got to studio at 9.30 and had another hard day on Vulcan. This figure work taxes me very severely — I cannot work with freedom or rapidity. Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30 — Worked until after 6 — then took a nap until 7 — as I felt very tired. Home at 8 — with drawing my hard work very easily criticised — The drawing is a poor thing — Tea at 8.30 well. From 9 oclk working on W.C. accts until 1 oclk — then to bed —

## Right Page

### May Sunday, 3 1874.

Delicious spring day. Mrs. went to Dr. Hartings. He preached a capital sermon from 1st Epistle of John 5th Ch. 4th V — After service walked up with the Jessups & dined with them. After went into the Park & walked, went over the foundations of the Metropolitan Museum of Nat. History — got to Mills at 5.15 Mr. Sweet and Annie Bradley there — left at 8.30 — Home at 9 — to bed at 10.45 —
