## Left Page

### August Tuesday, 4 1874.

Superb day — After bkfs. awhile in studio with Cattnell, then to stable — groomed colt & cleared up, then again in studio until 12. Then out on horseback with Geo. until 1 o'clk. Dinner soon after — Mr. & Mrs. Mercer & Mrs. Biddle went home at noon. After dinner wrote for awhile — then again to studio — Sayre sent up man & chimney, which was put up. With G. until near 5 o'clk. Then out on horseback with [illegible] & Geo. until 6 — then tea — wrote a letter to father & at 9.15 went to Joe W[m's]. — a little party given to Miss Grow — a niece of Galusha's. Home at 10.45 & to bed at once.

## Right Page

### August Wednesday, 5 1874.

Glorious day again — Before bkfs. for a short time with C., & again after bkfs. Then an hour in stable with Geo., working over the horses & clearing up. Soon after 10 ready to go to work on drawing but instead, spent the a.m. with C., selecting wood & laying out his work — A very little work in my drawing before dinner at 1.5. P.m., went, like the m[orning] until 4.30 — then Geo. came a little before 5 we got away together on horseback. The ride was a very pleasant one. In at 6 — tea soon after — then went down the hill — taken with a strange blindness — my stomach & liver — stopped at Azur S's. & heard Miss Gilbert play — home & to bed at 10 with a miserable headache. My Tyler went to Gr. Bend, to be away until Friday.
