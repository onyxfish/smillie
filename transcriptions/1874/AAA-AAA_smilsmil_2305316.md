## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 4 1874

Another cloudy mng, but the weather cleared charmingly in the afternoon. Bkft. about 8. The mng was spent arranging things about the house & getting ready to hang the pictures. Dinner abt —. Until 6, hanging pictures — Fisher helping — Then tea. Evng, Matt & Ed. came. Hung pictures until 10 o'clk. To bed at 10.30 — very tired.

By a mistake, turned over two leaves — this page is a record of Monday, Nov. 2d.

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 5 1874

Mild & hazy — savey nov.-like day. Bkft. at 7.30 — then took a stroll about the neighborhood about 9.30 towards hanging pics kept closely at it until 1.30 — then dinner. Worked until 4 o'clk, at hanging — at 4.20 started for train. Realizing perhaps more fully than ever what it is to have a home and loving, watchful parents. Walked into town with Charl. & Ed. Took 4.50 train — got to 42d St at 7.20 — went at once to D's & got my tea — then up to Will's — home at 10.30 & to bed at 11 —

This Record is for Tue. Nov. 3d —
