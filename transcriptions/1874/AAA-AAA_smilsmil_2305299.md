## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 29 1874

cold, bleak day winding up with
a blustering norther — Fussed,
bothered & wasted the whole
day over tire engine drawing.
Did not go out to ride on
acct. of the weather. Tea
at 6.15 — then to Mr. Cooker's
for a few minutes — then down
town scouring around getting
ready to leave for N.Y. tomorrow.
In at Jan's — took [illegible] & home
then to Elder Posto — saw Sac.
then to L. Searle's — a small
party — Home at 11 & to bed
about 11.30

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 30 1874

After a disturbed night, up at
5 o'clk. Very dark, cold and
windy — at 5.45 at Mulford's.
Bkfs. at 6 — at 6.30 Train, Bessie
Jessup & I off in Stage, I feeling
very cold & uncomfortable — Train
on time — 8.10 — A very pleasant
ride getting into N.Y. about
11 o'clk, where Dr. M. met us.
The girls rode up to Sturtevant
Ho. I went on to 24th & St.
Took tea with Father, Mother
& Charl at D[illegible]'s — Uncle
a. came in & Charl & I went
with him to his house — Got
to bed about 11 o'clk —
