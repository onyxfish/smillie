## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 10 1874.

Charming mf. After breakfast went to Burgraff's. Had him operate upon my soft corns — then walked over to 10th ave. & gave Hay & directions for covering my big chair — then down to 8th an & 34th. P.O. Station & sent $18 — to Mr. Dud. Harthrops. Did not get to Studio until 10.30. Then started painting of Yosemite fall. To dinner at 12.30 — back at 1.45 & painted until after 4 — then Maud Diven called. At 5.30 went over to Hoffman Ho. with her — to tea at 6.30 — Evet Charl. arrd from P. Very much disgusted at the loss of his room at D's — I wrote two or three letters & got to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 11 1874.

Windy & cooler, but a fine day — got to studio at 9.15 and to work painting on Yosemite fall. Whit until 12.30 — then to lunch — Back at 1.45 & painted as long as I could see, or until about 5 o'clk. Dr. Percy called. Just before 6 went to Hoffman Ho. and for a few minutes saw Eugene Diven & wife — Mrs. Aleck and Maud. To tea at 6.30. Evet Charl. came in for a few minutes. I wrote letters until 10 o'clk — To bed at 10.30.
