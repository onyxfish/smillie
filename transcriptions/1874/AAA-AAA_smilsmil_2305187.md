## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 11 1874.

Splendid winter day — got to studio at 10 o'clk + started work on drawing in W.C. that I started 2 years ago. "View on the Saco near North Conway" — for Sauthier. Home to lunch at 1 30. back at 2.30. Mr Bellows called — then J.G. Johnston & Mr Mach — was introduced to business of a new kind. — Mr Worley of the Times came in — all of my P.M. lost — Home & tea at 6 45 — Swy dressed — went to N.A. [illegible] — met the Jessups there — at 9.15 went to S[illegible] Tiffany's — 3 30 E. 17 S— Reception — Home at 11 5 + to bed at 11 45—

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 12 1874.

Superb day — Nearey 10 when I got to studio. Spent the mng on Water Color drawing — Home to lunch at 1 45 — back at 2.40 but belated in various ways so that I did not work for more than an hour during the P.M. — Home to tea at 6 45. Evg. dressed & with Mary, Matt & Ed went to W.C. [illegible]. There until 10 o'clk — the best day so far. — Then went to Union League Club with Nicoll — there until after 11 — Home at midnight + to bed som after.
