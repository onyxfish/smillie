## Left Page

### October Saturday, 3 1874.

Superb m[or]g. After some fussing about, Father Mother
Charl & I got off at 10, a.m.
Train for Poughkeepsie — enjoyed
the ride & got to P. at 12.
Rode right to No. 1 Eastman
Terrace. Found things in
a very comfortable condition.
Ed, Matt & Kate waiting for
us. Dinner some after 1 —
The afternoon spent all around about. Tea at 6 —
wet, a slight fall of Rain.
A good deal of sitting
about until 10.30 — then
to bed.

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 4 1874.

Superb m[or]g, my head feeling
very uncomfortable. Pkps. about
9 o'clk. Soon after 10 Charl &
I walked up to Main St. — He
was shaved — then we went
through Academy St., out to the
"Big hill", down to the Lower Road
& then to the Terrace. Dinner
abt 6 clks — none of us had been
to church. Matt & Ed at dinner.
P.m. Ed, Charl & I again on a
walk. Spent some time with
Gronps the carpenter. Back
at 5 — tea at 6.15. Every
in family conversation and
to bed at 10.45
