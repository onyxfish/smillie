## Left Page

### April, Thursday, 30, 1874.

Clear, windy day — Got to studio
som after 9 o'clk & worked at
the m[illegible] steadily on Vulcan
drawing — Home to lunch
at 1.15 — back at 2.15. Worked
until near 5, then, feeling
used up I went over to the
N.A. of D. — then to Gifford's
(Y.M.C.Ch.) & bid him & his
wife "good bye" — Saw Wyant,
Wm Hart & Walter Brown —
Back to studio at 6.20 —
Home to tea at 7 — at 8, at
Steinway Hall — 2d. concert of
Vocal Society — Charle joined
me later — Enjoyed concert —
walked up with C. m at
10.45 — To bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### May, Friday, 1, 1874.

Brilliant, breezy day — Got
to studio at 9.30 — To work on
Vulcan & stuck steadily at
it until 1 o'clk — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& again worked hard until
near 6 — Home to tea at 7 —
Every at work on Water
Color accts until near
11 o'clk — To bed at 11.20
