## Left Page

### April Friday, 10 1874.

Very disagreeable m[illegible]. Got to
studio at 9 o'clk. Had to kindle
fire — Some time lost before I
got to work on "Oxford & Dolphins"
an Italian drawing that I am
working over for the A.B.N. Co.
At 1.30 home to lunch. At
2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal —
at 4.30 went down to [illegible] —
then to Snedecor's — and at
5 at Studio — Swept up, took
a nap & home to tea at [illegible] —
Matt & Id. went home this
P.M. — At 8 o'clk. went up
to Mr. Gavit's. — There until
10.45 — Home at 11 — & to bed
at 11.15

## Right Page

### April Saturday, 11 1874.

Very pleasant m[illegible]. Got to
studio at 9 — Spent an hour
drawing with charcoals, then
Jessie came & I drew from
her until 1.15 when I went
home to lunch — back at
2.30 & worked until about
4.30 when I let her off for
the day — A violent storm
came up. Miss Wordward &
her cousin came in — also
Miss Covell & a gentleman —
Home, between showers, at 6.30
At 7.45 — very cold wind & a
snow flurry, at Jno. E. Gavit's —
took Meta down to [illegible] [illegible].
[illegible] Home at 11. 20 & to bed
at once —
