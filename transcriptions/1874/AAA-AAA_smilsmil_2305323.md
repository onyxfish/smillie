## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 18 1874

Very mild & pleasant —
Got to studio at 8.45 — but
used up — nervous exhaustion.
Confused & tired feeling in
the head — Tried to work but
had to give it up — To lunch
at 12.15 — After that — to 4[th] &
B'dway to see Hy T. Cox, but
he was out — To studio at 3 —
got little panel 6×7½ and
started subject to fill a frame
for Lautner. Bought 3 old frames
10×14 from Snedecor for $24.00
At 4.30 again went down to
Cox's — not in — back to Studio
— to tea at 6.30 — Evening
loafed & to bed at 9.30

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 19 1874

Fair weather. Got to studio at
9.30 & to work on "Bit at North
Conway" & worked faithfully
at it all day, but not able to
finish it. Jas. Hart spoke very
well of it. To lunch late and
hurried back to my work which
kept me until dark — In at
5.15 — dressed & then up to
Jessup's — Madison Ave & 78th
St — to dinner. Maud D—
was to be there, but was not.
She called at Studio about 3.30
P.M. Left at 8.15 & got to
Annie Cook's at 9 o'clk —
An Adirondack party there
to listen to some Readings
from "The Survey." Left there
at 10.45 — to bed at 11.15
