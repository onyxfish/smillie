## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 3 1874

Horrible storm of sleet & snow — nearly 10 o'clk when I got to studio. Worked on page block, Montreals, getting in the custom House — Did not go home to lunch — that was furnished me by Miss Woodworth. Afternoon cleared off charmingly. Time very much interfered with by W. C. matters — Home to tea, after a call from Tiffany, Nicolet & Silva, at 6.30 — Evef. at 9 o'clk. went to N.A. of D — there met Louis T. & wife, Chas. Mitchell & wife, Dwight Ripley & Miss Helen — Home at 10.30 — To bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### February Wednesday, 4 1874

Very fine day. Kate & Uncle Wille started for Montreal this mf, got to studio at 9.30. Louis & Nicoll there until 11 — then I got started on Montreal blocks. Worked until 1.15 — home to lunch & back at 2.30 — Swain Gifford called in the p.m — & also Burking came — all on W.C. business. Home to tea at 6.30 — Swf wrote a letter or two. Joe & Will came about 8.45 — went up to Burgraff's & paid all — then to N.A. of D — Home at 10.40 — wrote two letters & to bed at 11.40
