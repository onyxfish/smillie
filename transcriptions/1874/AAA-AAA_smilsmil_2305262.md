## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 15, 1874

I making hot day — Rkfp: at 8, until 10 o'clk, in studio sorting lumber. Then to work on "Italian drawing" female with three children. Worked well until dinner at 1 o'clk. In my Room at Sylee's until 3 o'clk Reading & writing — Then again to work & at 6 o'clk. got the drawing finished — then tea — After tea went over the way & with I wife, Ida and Floral, went up to Fair Ground for ferns. Back about 8 o'clk, and after frolicking with the girls until near 10 o'clk. home. Read until near 11 — to bed soon after —
Uncle A. & the girls are to go to N.Y. to-morrow —

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 16, 1874

Gentle sprinkle of rain with promise of a wet day. But it cleared before noon & been off quite cold — Rkfp: at near 8 o'clk. & until nearly 11 was in my studio assorting my lumber for the different parts & got it finally arranged — then to work on drawing that I made in N.Y. "The Genius of Minute" — Dinner at 1 o'clk. After dinner went down town to see Fordham about getting paper, put and nearly at 4 o'clk. Before I got to work again on drawing — Gave up at 5.30 as my head hurt me and again went down town — Went to Cottrell's shop — Got mail & up to tea at 6.30 — Evelf, with gre called on "Mrs. De Witt" — Mrs. mr Jessup and Fanny Jessup — Home & 10 o'clk. & Right to bed —
