## Left Page

### Sept. Friday, 11 1874.

Intensely hot (for Montrose) the ther. got up to 83° in my studio.. mistakes before bkfs. Got to studio before 9 — but lost much time — family visitors — the most of the mng was given to finishing touches on Pen. Ams. At noon went down to Cotrell's to see why Fales for my mechanical horse had not come up — Back to dinner at 1.20 — P.M. got out canvases & photos & began charcoaling Yosemite picture 24×44 for Uncle A. — Mrs. Wm. Mulford called — At 5½ went over to stable & fixed my horses — Tea shortly after 6 — then with Frank went over the ground to see about moving studio — 8.30 before I got away from him — Abg went to Josie Bently's — at 10 went into Fan's & had one game of whist — then home & to bed shortly before 11 — D. Taylor left this mng.

## Right Page

### Sept. Saturday, 12 1874.

Another scorcher — The heavens are as brass. Worked awhile in the stable before bkfs. at 7.30 — Got to studio at 9.30 & after a time got started on charcoaling Yosemite Horse Race — I got along very badly my head swims & feels tired — my liver is probably at the bottom of it all — Dinner at 1½ P.M. worked until near 5 o'clk. making very little progress then went over to stable, put a saddle on Tom & gave him a hard trot until near 6. Both of us came in very wet. Tea at 6.30 — Went with Lillie Jessup & the Lathrops went to see Mrs. Bullard night Blooming cereus — very beautiful at 9 o'clk. at Fan's — There until after 10 — Home & to bed at 10.45.
