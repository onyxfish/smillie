## Left Page

### July, Sunday, 5, 1874

Cold, mizzling, foggy mf.
Did not get up until late —
[illegible] after 9 o'clk. Mr.
went to Episcopal Ch. +
heard the Regular minister
preach a Regular sermon.
After service wandered
around — caught in a shower
+ went into Jan's — Dinner
at Mr. L's after 1 o'clk, Sat
+ chatted with Mr. Mercer
— saw Uncle Al, V. + Gus + I
went off + had a walk with
him — wrote a letter home
Tea at 6 o'clk. Mr. + Miss
Sneed called — walked down
the hills with them — At 8.30
went to Presbt'n Ch. Walked
home with Gus S. stopping a few
minutes at Jan's — then home
stopping again at Agur S's.
To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Monday, 6, 1874

A Superb day, clear + cool. Bkf-
[illegible] from 9 until 12 o'clk, in
planing mill helping run through
oak + Bk's Walnut — for my studio.
Then went to see Joules. A most
unsatisfactory fellow to deal with
as I can get nothing satisfactory
definite out of him. Dinner at
1.30 — Soon after 2 again at mills —
most of p.m. spent hauling the
lumber from the mills to studio.
Tea at 6 — Mr. + Miss Snead met
me on my way down the hills + with
them I called at Mr. Jacoby's +
saw Lill who has just ret'd this
p.m. from Syria — Then to Gus S. —
Aunt Bsen came + we had whist
until after 11 — It was near
midnight when I got to bed.
