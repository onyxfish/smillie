## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 2 1874

Charming mild day — got to studio at 8.35 & painted on 10×14 (2nd day) "Blue Mt.
Lake." — At 12.45 went to lunch — back at 1.45 & painted again until
4 o'clk, getting the whole subject painted in —
Then to Y.M.C.A. Birkin's Studio — "Meeting of the
W.C. Board of Control" —
Back to Studio at 5.45 & to tea at 6.50 — at 7.30 Charl came and
I went with him to the San Francisco Minstrels —
Met there Will, Joe, Annie, Bradley etc. etc. — In — & to bed at 10.40.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 3 1874

Mild & mixed — on my way to studio met Wm Beard — walked & talked with him — He went up with me — got there at 9.15 — then Nicolé called on W.C. biz — & it was 11 o'clk. before I got to work on 10×14 "meadows" — something praisetion [illegible] — to lunch at 12.45 — back at 1.40 — worked until 4.30 — could not see any longer — Mr. Leonard Scott & daughter called — worked on charcoal sketch until 6 — then to tea. Swell, Charl called for a few minutes — then Dining & music until 9 — then mate to Coleman Fenn — to bed at 10.30
