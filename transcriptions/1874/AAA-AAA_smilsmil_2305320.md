## Left Page

### November Thursday, 12 1874.

Pleasant day — got to studio soon after 9 o'clk — After a little fussing about — hanging pictures, etc — I set palette & commenced little painting 10×14" Autumn — a down — to lunch at 12.45 — back at 2 & worked as long as I could see but could not quite finish first painting in — Tea at 6.30 — at 8.30, at Kimball's — got there — at — Will, Fanny, got, it & went to the Ireland's — a very pleasant time — did not get in until 11.30 — To bed at 11.45 —

## Right Page

### November Friday, 13 1874.

Very charming, but cold — fighting all day with a headache — Could not work but spent the mof., hanging pictures & studies in the studio — at 1.30, Miss Woodwood gave me lunch — Then I went in to Philharmonic rehearsal — My head got worse until at 4.30 I gave it up — Left rehearsal — stopped at studio & fixed fire — got to 4.15. at 5 o'clk almost wild with the pain — went right to bed — Turned in for the night at 11, feeling much better —
