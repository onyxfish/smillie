## Left Page

### January, Saturday, 24, 1874

Clearing weather. My head troubling me. Got to studio
at 9.30 & worked on "Calf"
until after 2 o'clk. Then
prepared tea & eat lunch.
After that, went over to Academy
there until 5 o'clk, almost
used up with headache &
sore throat. Then back
to studio — fussed there
for awhile & spread myself
on lounge until after 8 o'clk
then again over to Academy
& there until 9.30 — then gave
out & home. To bed at 10 o'clk
in great pain.
Uncle Willie arr. from P. &
stays with us.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday, 25, 1874

Very clear, cold day. Up late
— 8.65. At 9.30 after that, felt
so played out that I spent
the rest of the m't on the
sofa. Dinner at 1.15. At 3
I went down to studio, fixed
tablet on frame, then walked
up to Willi's. Took tea there
& left at 8 o'clk. Bringing
home with me Leibler's
drawing on lithographic stone.
Read, wrote & chatted until
after 11. To bed at 11.15
