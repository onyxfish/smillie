## Left Page

### November Saturday, 14 1874.

Charming day — feel much better than I expected.
At studio at 8.45. Spent the mf. finishing painting
in [illegible] — Young Asto[r] &
his cousin Armstrong came
in to see Ferguson — To
lunch at 12.45 — back at
2. P.m. — painting on
"Whit on Artists Brook —
N. Conway." To tea at
6.30 Every went to Phil.
Concert — 1st — to bed at
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 15 1874.

Windy, overcast & threatening.
Mf., went to Dr. Hastings's &
heard him preach. After service
walked in the Park until near
2 o'clk. Then to Uncle A's to
dinner — met there Walter Vant
wife & child. Left at 4.30 &
walked up to Mr. Jessups — then
to tea. Left at 8 o'clk. & rode
down to Ch. [illegible] Messiah cor. 34th
St. & 4th Ave. Heard Mr. Ames —
one of camping party on Raquette
last summer. Met there the
Irelands & Misses Cook & Hare —
walked down with them to
the I's — left about 10.30 —
to bed at 11 —
