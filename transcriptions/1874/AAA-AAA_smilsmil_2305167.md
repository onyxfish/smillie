## Left Page

### January Friday, 2 1874

Up late again — steady rain —
11 o'clk. before I left the house
then rode downtown & fought
with the powers of mud for
some hours. First called on
Roberts, the wood engraver.
then on Parsons, at Harpers. Then
on Sutton of the Aldine. Then
at DeVisser's — saw Charl. Then
to Sackett's, the printers & spent
more than an hour selecting
types & colors for Water Color
printing. Then to A.B.N.Co's
& lunched with Gaink. Nearly
3 when I started for Phil.
Rehearsal — After that, I
walked to 20th. with Emma &
then to studio & wept. Home
to tea at 6 — Everg did a
little writing & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January Saturday, 3 1874

Foggy, muggy day. Went to Mr.
Waller Brown's 115 2. 23°. &c. at
9.30, by appointment. Got to
studio at 6.30. Kindled fire
then to work & finished Cald[?]
tracing. Between 12 & 1 o'clk
Emma Beach & a number of their
girls called at the studio. Soon
after joined by Mr. Beach and
by W. Beecher, who acted in
a very indifferent manner.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30. Matt & Ed. Relle to
Po — at 3.45. Worked on
tracing until 4.30. Stretched
a piece of paper for Jas. Hart
to make a charcoal sketch.
Home to tea at 6.30 Everg
writing W.C. business — To bed
at 11.
