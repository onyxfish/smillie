## Left Page

### July, Friday, 31, 1874

Irregular wt. Overcast & threatening, then clear & beautiful, to be again overcast. Worked on wall muslin in studio until near 11 o'clk. Then went out on the colt in company with Mr. Gratz — the animal behaved admirably — the air was delicious & I had a most enjoyable ride. Home abt 2.30 & dinner soon after 1 o'clk. Afternoon, again in studio working over muslin for the walls. Very heavy showers came up. Tea at 6 o'clk. After tea, took another short ride alone on the colt. Eve, at 9 o'clk, went to Faus. Priscilla, Poor [illegible], and Elisha. Whist until after 10 — then, with Fan, took P. home — a glorious moonlight night — To bed at 1, 20

## Right Page

### August, Saturday, 1, 1874

Overcast wt. Rain commenced about 10 o'clk. Kept it up all day — sometimes very heavily until near 6 p.m. Cottrell sent up door & blinds this A.M. I fooled away most of the mft, altho' I made believe work on a drawing for a B. N. Co. — Penn'a Arms — Dinner abt 1.15. After, blocked out subject from day figure house, made some progress with drawing. At 4 o'clk went to studio &, Geo. helping, stretched & primed a large panel of muslin. Austin came along at near 6 o'clk. & lathered front door with oil (it is a splendid combination of oak) & primed blinds. Tea at 6 o'clk. Mr. Mercer arr. Abt 9 o'clk — down the hill — went to Faus — with her went to Geo. S's — not in — over at Strond's — went there a lot of girls — took Lilly Jessup home — then home with Fan & stayed whist until after 11 — then home & to bed abt 11, 40
