## Left Page

### May, Saturday, 16, 1874

Cool w[eather], passing into a close, muggy day — some heavy showers — Got to studio at 9.25 & worked tolerably well in furnace drawing — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Back at 2.45 Afternoon, several calls — Nicoll came in about the Jones business. About 7, I went over to the League club & saw Mr. Walter Brown. Home to tea at 7.45 — Spent lying off in my big chair — feeling disgusted & quite disheartened — To bed at 10.45. Father, Mother & Mary took dinner at A.V.V.'s —

## Right Page

### May, Sunday, 17, 1874

Delicious day — Up late and late to church, (Dr. Hastings?) walked up to 72d St, with the Jessups — then back through the Park. To A[.V.]V.'s — there to dinner — After dinner had a grand nap or snooze until almost 5. Then Geo. & Charl came. Got & I stayed to tea — After tea Junie & I strolled in the Park for an hour — then I went over to Wells 8.45 — Did not get home until after 10 — & to bed at about 11 —
