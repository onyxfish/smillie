## Left Page

### June, Friday, 5, 1874

Overcast most of the day &
threatening. Got to Studio
at 9 o'clk. & pitched into
"Drop" for Palmer for Union
Square Theatre. Worked until
1.15 then home to lunch &
back at 2.30. Found Miss S.
& her mother waiting. Begged
off from giving lesson; then
went on with my painting
and about 7 o'clk. finished
it. I am very much elated
as I think that my two days
work shows that I have not
yet lost all of my facility, dash
& power. Home at 7.30 and
tea after 8. Evef. tried to
write to Belle Tweedy, but
too tired. Got to bed at
11.20. Weather very muggy
& thick. Matt & 2. went
home this P.m.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday, 6, 1874

A very sticky, muggy day. Got
to studio at 9.30. Fussed
about until 11 o'clk. Then
with Ferguson went over to
the N.A. of D. There met Mrs
Kimball & Fanny. Then went
to Un. Sq. Theatre & saw Palmer.
— then to Snedecor's — then to
Dewey's — then home to lunch
at 1.40 — back to Studio at
2.30, Miss S. & mother already
there — gave the whole of
my P.m. to them. Emma
S. called — also Ferguson
& Gel. & miss Burdell — Home
soon after 7 — Evef. writing
for Circular "Frame circular"
from W.C. Socy. To bed at
11.30
