## Left Page

### April Wednesday, 22 1874

Passed a horrible night of
nightmares & pains —
Unable to get up — In
bed until about 3 o'clk.
at 4 had some gruel
made and ate it —
Eveng sat up & read
some in the news papers.
To bed about 6 o'clk. —

## Right Page

### April Thursday, 23 1874

Rain all day long — I
was unable to get up until
after 9 o'clk. — All day, very
weak — stomach very sore.
Read a little but my
head ached. — Eveng
feeling very much better
worked over Water Color
accts until 10.30 —
To bed at 10.45
