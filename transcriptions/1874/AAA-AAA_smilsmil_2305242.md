## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 3 1874.

Pleasant m't, but with indications of change. Up with a headache —
got to studio at 9 o'clk. but was unable to work. — Burling
called. — Had to give up at 12 o'clk & home to bed. — The
Rain came on heavily during the afternoon. — I
took my meals in bed after 9 o'clk. — Then up &
on the sofa until 2 o'clk —
then to bed —

Ferguson painted in my Room to-day.

### June Thursday, 4 1874.

Pleasant day — Very slim —
on my way to studio met
Magrath — then Baldwin
& so did not get to my Room
until after 10 o'clk. —
Ferguson there. — Could not
work — home to lunch at 1.30
— back at 2.45 — managed to
work on Italian drawing Union
& Progress until 6 o'clk. — Home
to tea at 7.10'clk. — Gel. arr'd
from his visit to Plainfield.

arranging my acc'ts with A.B.N.
