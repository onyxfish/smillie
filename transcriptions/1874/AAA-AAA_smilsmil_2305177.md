## Left Page

### January Thursday, 22 1874.

A horrible, muggy, foggy day —
Got to Studio at 9.15 — all the
mst lost with calls — Mr.
F. O. C. Darley came in — then
D. S. a. Ward & Whitteidge
— then J. M. Falconer & so
on — It was quite noon
before I got to work — at
2.30 I prepared & took
lunch — then worked
until near 5 — Then went
to N.A. & D. from there to
Schaus's & Gouthier's — then
to academy again & unpacked
Mr. Geo. Whitney's boy. Back to
Studio — Home to tea at 8 o'clk.
Eve'g, wrote a note to Mr.
Whitney & to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### January Friday, 23 1874.

Still disagreeable weather —
Got to Studio soon after 9 &
after working awhile on the
calf was disgusted to such
extent that I washed most
of the animal out. Worked
on until near 3 o'clk when
I prepared lunch — went
over to Academy about 5 o'clk.
Home to tea at 6 o'clk — back
to Academy again at 7.30.
Worked there at hanging
until 10 o'clk — Home at
10.30 & to bed about 11. [illegible]
