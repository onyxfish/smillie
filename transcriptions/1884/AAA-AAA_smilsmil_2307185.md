## Left Page

### August, Monday 11, 1884.

Clear mtf. Not t hard, like an American one —
I got part way up the hill to work on sketch
looking over to the cliff d'Aval when I saw
that there was no such color or atmosphere
as I wanted — I had met Bacon on his way to
Bennville, so I bid him good bye & returned.
Got a bright study of the cliff d'Aval from a
point above the washerwomen's ground & went to
that point & worked on it until 1 o'clk — then
home & had lunch — After that, again
worked in study in-doors — bringing it together
& doing things that I could not see to do out-
d doors — then went out with Annie but
found it so hot that we soon returned —
At 6 o'clk. Promptly on hand at the dinner
table to secure our places — no trouble — After
dinner — the tide being very low I went along the
beach to the arch of the cliff d'Amont — after sun-
down — made a light sketch — then through tunnel
& up to the top of cliff — wonderful effect of reflec-
tion of a beautiful eve'g. Sky — very warm — no
air stirring — hottest eve'g yet — To bed soon
after 10 — The boy seems very well — has
improved a very little to-day.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 12, 1884.

Close, overcast mtf. Up, as usual, with Joe, just
at about 4.30 — but unfortunately did not get to
sleep again — Up for the day at 7 & had a short
walk before bkfst. at 8.15 — nearly 9.30 when I
got away with paper & charcoal & went to the
farm gate on the St. Clair Road, of which I made
a small W.C. sketch about a week ago — Spent
the mtf. making a good sized charcoal study —
The sun came out very hot at 12 — home at 1 —
lunch at 1.30 — After that spent a little time
over charcoal study — At 3.30 went out looking
for horse & carriage — after a little trouble, found
one & at 4.15 Annie, Joe & I started off, having
a driver — out on les Ifs Road some way, then back
by the Foret de Loges, & through the Grand Val —
Back at 6.15 having had a delightful ride —
then dinner — a short walk — Tried to
write a note to Father, answering his rec'd today
concerning the Cooper lot — Danforth came in —
then we had some ice cream that Annie
had ordered — The usual duties, then
to bed at 10.30.
