## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 16. 1884.

A grey day with spattering rain in the m'g
— went out on the beach & for awhile worked
on pencil sketch — general view — enough
rain to drive me in — then worked on
same sketch for awhile in-doors with
white chalk & charcoal — Lunch about
1 — After 2' it had so much cleared
that I took my w.c. & went to church
yard & worked on my study there f the
church tower until 5.30 — then home
& with Annie to hotel to dinner —
Eve'g. after 9 I went out on the place
& walked for awhile in the dim
twilight — that, with the sound of
the sea on the pebbles, was very lovely —
to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 17. 1884.

Rain in the m'g, & gray until afternoon,
when it cleared brilliantly — m'g. went
to the beach & worked on my pencil study
of general view until after 12. altho' it was
breezy, almost cold & at times spattering
rain. Lunch at home soon after 1 — and
again on beach & made pencil study
of a group of boats & figures — then, at 5
o'clk. I went with Annie up on South
Cliff & wandered about there wondering at
the beauty of sky & sea. Back & to dinner
soon after 6 — Eve'g quietly at home
doing nothing in particular — Got a very
interesting budget of home letters in the
m'g. — one from father & Mary at Montrose.
