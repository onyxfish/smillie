## Left Page

### September, Friday 12. 1884.

Very pleasant day — About 10 we made
up our minds to the Thames River and
Kew Gardens — So we took cab to the
Cadogan Pier, near Albert Bridge — at 11 o'clk
boat to Kew. Tide running out strong, so
that we got along very slowly — water ex-
cessively dirty — mud banks exposed — got
to K— at 12.30 — half an hour late — walked
up to the Gardens — hurried across to the
Cumberland Gate, having little chance
to enjoy the beauties about us — onto the R.R.
Station & missed the 1.11 Train by about
2 min. Had to wait until 1.44 — got to
Victoria Station at 2.15 — drove right home
& got there at 2.25 — or 25 m. late for dinner —
After that, a rest & at 4.30 took cab & rode
out to Campden Hill Road, Kensington W. West
House. To call on Geo. H. Boughton — out of town — then
to 54 Bedford Gardens where I saw Abbey & A.
Parsons — also, for a few minutes Frank Millet —
Had a very pleasant visit — & at 6.30 we all three
rode to Clarges St. in a cab where I was left
at my door — Tea — evening — very quiet —
resting — after 10 when I got to bed.
Annie having read aloud a chapter from
Village on the Cliff.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 13. 1884.

Very warm, pleasant day. At about 9.30
Annie & I started out; we did a little shop-
ping — she then went off alone & at 10.30 I got
to the National Gallery where I worked
faithfully at the pictures until 1 o'clk —
then home — Found that Geo. & Nellie had
been there — having got to London earlier
than they expected — also F. Seymour Haden.
Dinner after 1.30 — then took a rest &
at 3.30 again started out — went through
Green to St. James Park & on to Westminster
Abbey — took quite a careful survey,
following the verger with my guide book in
my hand — I think it the most interesting
place I have been in — out at 5.30 & did
considerable wandering about in the
neighborhood of Houses of Parliament — St. James's
& the houses of the aristocracy — Home at 6.15
Geo. & Nellie had again been at the house —
Annie saw them — Tea at 6.45 — at 8, A.
& I went to Charing Cross Hotel & spent
an hour with Geo. — very warm — then
home & to bed at 10.30.
