## Left Page

### April, Sunday 13, 1884

Easter —

Delicious day — Bkfst soon after 9 —
Domestic duties kept me until
near 12 — then to barber's & had
hair cut & head shampoo'd.
Then walked up to Junie's &
saw Morthen & Kate for a few
minutes — Then home to dinner
at 2 — Geo. Nellie & Sheldon
came in between 3 & 4 o'clk.
Soon followed by Morthen & Kate
& then Charl. They spent
what remained of the afternoon
here — At 6.30 walked over to
Will's — he was out — home & had
tea soon after 7 — Miss Law, the
nurse, left us — until 8.30 with
fat June — Then wrote to Pattren
& to Esta & Launat — Took letters
to G.C. Depot at 9.30 — To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 14, 1884

Still pleasant weather — wind E. —
Worked all the m'g on a B. N. Co.
drawing of Steamer "Furnessia" —
finished & mounted it & home to
dinner at 1 — Back at 3 & worked
for awhile on W.C. "Causeway from
Pebble Head Neck to Mainland" — as
I want to send it to Mr. Geo. Whiting
subject to his approval. About
4.30 I went to Chas. Willis's Re-
ception at his studio — Then down
to Flint's furniture store in 14th St.
& ordered crib — Then to Bisbee's
in Waverly Pl. & ordered pr. boots.
Then to studio & home at 6.30 — Kate
there — Tea soon after 7 — at 8.15
Kate & I at Chickering's — Henschel
Recital ([illegible]) — very enjoyable —
at 10.30 — left K. at Junie's — at 11,
home — Tried to take a nurse's
place with Annie during the
night & had about 2½ hours
of sleep, altho' the boy behaved
very well.
