## Left Page

### August, Sunday 31. 1884.

Cloudy with occasional showers —
For awhile on the beach, looking at
the boats — about 11 Annie & I walked
over the route that I walked yes-
terday afternoon. Got back to the
house at 12.30 — had lunch at 1.30 —
after that I took a nap — then wrote
letter home — also one to Dalemeyer, in
London & acknowledged receipt of [illegible]
letter of credit to J.S. Morgan & Co. Lond.
About 5.30 Annie & I went to call on
the Danas — not in — at church — we
walked out towards the church & met
them & then to the P.O. with them — then
home & to dinner soon after 6 — then
back & wrote letters to Charle & Mr.
W.M. Basset. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 1. 1884.

Overcast mtf, with threats of rain but
I went up to the crucifix with my ées
to paint the sketch, view to the east,
fought with high wind for an hour to a
half, — tried everything fast that I could,
& scrabbled in as much as I could, then
it came on to rain & I was driven in-
in-doors. I continued work on sketch,
as I could see much of the material of
it from my window & I wished for grey,
sunless effects. Lunch at 1.30 & then
worked until 5 when I went out for
a little airing — rain too heavy & I
came in. To dinner soon after 6.
Evelf. being very tired & feeling
busted about the head I rested —
The sketch that I got this mtf
is I think, the best that I have
done this season — To bed at
10.30.
