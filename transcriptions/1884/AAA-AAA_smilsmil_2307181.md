## Left Page

### August, Sunday 3, 1884

Lovely day — Beeca so sick as to
be in her bed most of the day,
making the duties come rather hard
on Annie. We went together, at
10 o'clk., to High Mass in the old ch.
of Notre Dame — a very prosaic &
uninteresting performance. I thought
so far as the performance itself
was concerned — Home soon after
11 — unable to get our lunch
until 1.30. About 2.30 Annie took
the boy to the beach & I wrote to
Father. I then, at about 4, went to
the beach & met Annie & was with her
& the boy, wheeling him about until 5.30 —
To dinner soon after 6 — after that, again
home — self — wrote to Drexel, Harris &
Co. for $60 — To bed at about 10.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 4, 1884

Beautiful, cloudless day — mild and
quiet. Got to my work on the cliff at
about 10 & stuck to it until 1.15 — then
called my study "done" & went home —
Some delay in getting my lunch, which
rather excited my irritable stomach.
After my lunch I lay down & had a
short nap — then, with water colors,
at about 4, I went to a little, thatched
cottage in town & commenced a sketch
of it. Annie came soon after 5 and
sat with me for awhile — Home before
6 & to dinner — then for awhile on
the beach — washed brushes — did my
"chores" & to bed at 11.
