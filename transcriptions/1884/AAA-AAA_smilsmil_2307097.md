## Left Page

### February, Friday 15, 1884

Charming day — after 10 when
I got to studio — mixed a half
gallon of Dutch Mordant & got
my Heullard Etching ready for
the acid — also worked a little
with pencil — Home at 1 — At
2.15 at Phil. Rehearsal — Kate
Fook dinner with us & we
refreshed at the house, caring
for the boy, while Bella ate
her dinner — After rehearsal
Annie & I went to Cassell & Co.'s
— saw Mr. Dunham, manager,
& had a long talk with him.
Had a copy of "Original Etchings
by American Etchers" sent home.
— Then to N.A. & looked at W.C.
& Etchings for a little while &
at 6.15 — home — tea — at 8 I went
to Etching Club meeting — Nicoli's
studio — 51 W. 10 St. — Home at
11 & then to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 16, 1884

Bright beautiful day — got to
studio before 10 — To work biting
on Heullard etching — & between
times, fussed with pencil on
two old studies from nature and
tracing large W.C. composition.
Home to dinner at 1 — back
before 3 — finished biting and
cleaned the plate — At 5. went
to Am. Art Gallery & saw pictures
by Alex. & Birge Harrison. Then
to N.A. — & at 6.15 home — tea —
& at 8 o'clk. Annie & I went to
Philharmonic Concert. Walked
& got home at 10.30 — sat awhile
& read — To bed at 11.30.
