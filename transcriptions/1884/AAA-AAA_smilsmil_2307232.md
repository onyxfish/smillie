## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 19, 1884.

A nasty day — heavy rain part of
the time — a mizzle at all times —
Got to studio about 10 — Tackled
my big canvas with charcoal
& blocked in my "Cliffs of Norman-
dy" — then had Ge. in to look at
it — he made some suggestions that
led me to make a general change
— home at 1 — Mr. Ashman called
to look at papering at 2 — then I
ret. to studio & resumed work in
charcoal sketch — getting it into
tolerably satisfactory condition —
then a rest & home at 6 — dinner
— at 8 to W.C. [illegible]. Water Color
meeting — wanting one of a quorum
we waited until 9 o'clk — then to
work informally — adjourned at
9.35 — home — Spent some time
in spreading bread with Costar's
Rat exterminator — then for a time
airing & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 20, 1884.

Overcast with. clearing pleasantly.
Got up with a headache. — To
studio about 10 & worked during
the m/f on Lake George study,
giving it some spunk, altho' I
had but little myself — Home
to lunch at 1 & after that went
to bed & did not get up until
after 9 p.m. Spent the rest of
the eveg by the fire until
after 12. — Had some supper at 11.
To bed before 1.
