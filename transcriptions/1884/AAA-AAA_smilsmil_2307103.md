## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 27, 1884

Pleasant day — to studio before 10 — Spent an hour or more painting
in a large canvas for Miss Abbott
— pulling it together in light and
color — then to work for myself
on "View near Marblehead". giving
a few finishing touches — Home
at 1 — dinner — back before
3 — Mary Hinchlewood called —
Put [illegible] of "Etchings" "The King
D[illegible]" & "Admiral DeRuyter"
in their frames & at 5 went to
Collamore's, on Union Sq. to meet
Annie to look for tea cups —
we went to several places —
home at 6.30 — tea — at 8.30
I left Annie at her mother's —
went on up to Charl's — laid
up with a boil on back of his neck.
— back for A. — at 9.30 — home
at 10 — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 28, 1884

Heavy, wet snow during the morning —
clear & very cold at night — got
to studio soon after 10 — Spent the
m[orning]. stretching [illegible] of Rocky Mts
on panel back stretcher — one or two
other odd jobs — home at 1 — dinner
— back at 3.15 — made paste &
mounted pencil studies — got
out Japan paper — copper plates
etc — at 5, went down to [illegible]
& Voigt's with them — made arrange-
ments to go there Sat. m[orning]. to have
them proved — Home at 6.15 — at
6.40, with A. at Kimball's —
Emma & Grace there — tea — at
8. Took them all to their Singing
club — I went to Century — there
until near 10 — then went for
Annie — home at 10.15 — to bed
soon after 11.
