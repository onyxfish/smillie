## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 23, 1884

Cold and overcast — after 10 when I
got to studio. Made charcoal sketch
13×20 — Farm on the cliff near Etretat.
Then Traced it — Home at 1 — back
at 2.30 — Retraced & outlined subject
on the drawing paper and after
4 o'clk. went to Manhattan Gas Co.
miring Place $15'8. Paid bills &
collected $5? that has been lying
there for 20 years as security — got
interest for the past 8 years — then
home & dinner — Loeff wrote note
to Will Cook & sent him a lot of pf.
Postage stamps for Robert — went to
G. C. Depot & mailed it — then to
Grady & McK's about frames — home
before 9 — made some anthology
spoon sketches for Annie & to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 24, 1884

There a fall of snow, continuing most
of the day — After 10 when I got to the
studio commenced work on a sketch
W.C. — 7×11 "Cliffs near Etretat" —
Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30.
Finished the sketch & mounted it.
Then, before 5, went down to Fulton
St. & bought a diary for next year —
Home at 6.20 & had dinner — then
made pen sketch on a card for [illegible]
& Fanny K. About 9 went with Annie
to Walter's for a few minutes, then
to Ed[?] Cook's where I assisted in
dressing the Xmas tree. Mr. & Mrs.
Alex. Oakey there — Home at 11 &
right to bed.
