## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 12, 1884

Spring-like day - bright [illegible]. heavy
rain between 2 & 3 o'clk - clear every.
To studio before 10 - made 3 sketches
of my bond outlet pictures for [illegible]'s
Academy notes, but was not satisfied -
Home at 1 - back before 3 - made
sketch of Nellie's picture and
commenced work for A.B.N. Co.
on a drawing of about an Statue.
About 5.30 went up to Dickie's and
rode there from 6.15 to 6.45 -
Home soon after 7 - then tea -
Nelly, Annie read aloud from
"The Newcomes" - To bed about
10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 13, 1884

Mild & partly overcast - To studio
before 10 & to work on drawing of
S. A. Statue for A.B.N. Co. Home
at 1, after going to N. A. with list of
my exhib. pictures - Kate came in while
we were at dinner to say that she
was going home on Sat. Back to
studio before 3 & worked on drawing
until 5 - then to Dickie's & rode for
¾ of an hour - Home at 7 - tea -
Nelly, for a while Annie read
aloud - To bed at 10.30.
