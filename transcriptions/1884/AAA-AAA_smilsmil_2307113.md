## Left Page

### March, Thursday 20, 1884

Raw, wet day — Got up with
nasal lining membranes & throat
very sore, so that I thought it
wisest to stay in-doors all day —
Spent the mtg. reading and
nalopping. Dinner at 1 —
Afternoons, Nelia came in &
made quite a visit — Then Br.
Yale, & later, mother Cook —
Tea at 6.30 — Evntg. wrote a
letter to Cottrell — Then Annie
read aloud until near 10.
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 21, 1884

Overcast; wind East, but not wet —
got to studio about 10 & altho' still
feeling far from well, I managed
to do some work on A.B.N. Co's
drawing of a Chilian seaport. I
also wrote to Stroud & Smith — Maxim
telling them to add another 4000
dols — to house us — as I had de-
termined to cancel Tyler's policy.
Home to dinner at 1 — Emma
there — after dinner I took a
nap & did not get to studio
until after 3 — again worked
on A.B.N. Co's drawing. Bristol
again came in about 4 or so —
Reps[illegible]. Home soon after 6 —
after a walk about Mad. Square.
Evntg. wrote circular for Bristol
forg. Reds[illegible]. Then Annie read
aloud until 10.30. To bed
about 11.
