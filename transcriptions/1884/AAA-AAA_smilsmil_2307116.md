## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 26. 1884.

Very heavy showers in the m'g.
— then fog & mizzle — Dr. Yale
called before I left — Annie
still has to keep quiet — Got
to Studio's at 10.30 — Cleaned up
my Press — ground ink and
before I went home at 1 — got
one proof Falcon. — Back at
3 and before 6. Took five
more p.p. — stretched them &
them — also stretched two tin-
type outlines of locomotives upon
which I am to make drawings
for C.B.N. Co. — Home at 7,
quite used up with headache.
Hung about during eve'g
& to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 27. 1884.

Beautiful day — Dr. Yale came
before I left home in the m'g.
so that it was after 10 before I got
to Studio's — then to work at my
proving & by 1.15 had taken 3
pp. of my colored neighbor's Hen
yard." Then home — Annie still
on her back & but little encour-
agement from the D'. Got to
N. at about 3 o'clk. & to work
for C. M. Kurtz, making sketches
of pictures for his Catalogue.
Commenced on Ulric's picture
& altho' I worked until 6.15
did not finish it — Then to Studio's
& washed up my printing things
after 7 when I got home — Tea
near 8 — Dr. Yale came in — after
he left I spent some time in
arranging Annie's Diet Kitchen
accounts — To bed about 11.
