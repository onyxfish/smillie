## Left Page

### April, Friday 11, 1884

Charming day — altho' breezy — after
1.30, went to Robt. Gordon's — about
state room on "City of Berlin" of which
he has the disposal — then to studio, &
with Geo. went to the steamer & saw chief
steward — then back — stopped for a few
minutes at N.A. of D. & home to dinner
at 1.15 — did not return to studio
until 3 — then commenced work on a
steamer drawing for B.B.N.Co. After
5 went to Grady & [illegible]'s about
frames — home at 6.30 — tea — Eve.
with Annie — much talk of our
proposed trip to Europe —
Will Park & his friend Welhouse
called at the studio about 4 P.M.
To bed before 11 — Jas. Jun. had
a breezy time during the night.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 12, 1884

Lovely spring day — after 10 where
I got to studio & to work upon steam-
ship drawing for B.B.N.Co. — home
at 1½ dinner — back at 3 and
worked until 4.30 — then to Dickel's
& rode in the Park on Kingfisher
for more than an hour — home at
about 7 — (met Charle at the stable
when I came in he showed me
his new horse & dog-cart.) Tea
at near 8, after having had a good
scrubbing down — Geo. came in
for a few minutes on his way up
to Junie's to see Mother & Kate.
Eveth. spent loafing about —
Annie sitting up part of the time.
To bed at 10.30
