## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 4, 1884.

D'rty. Rainy weather most of the day —
Up soon after 7 — the boy having passed
a comparatively quiet night — Blkb. before
9\. when a note from P. Seymour Haden was
brought to me, inviting me to lunch to-day —
It was nearly 11 before I was ready to start
out; I then went to the Dudley Gall'y Egyptian Hall — expecting to see a general collection of pictures & was disappointed to find
only a small one of uninteresting French ones.
I spent an hour there — then took a hansom
& drove to 38 Hertford St. Mr Haden took me at
once to his own rooms at the top of the house
where we spent an hour chatting — looking at
his proofs (Rembrandt & others) & his large plate
of "Calais Pier" after Turner, upon which he has
a Mezzotint ground — He told me that he has
£37,000 insurance upon his collections — At 1
we had lunch — joining Mr. H. & a little grand-
son — very informal — At 2.30 I left & rid. to
hotel. Abner & went out to Elkington's & others —
to look at silver cups. Tea at 7 — evening
reading newspapers etc. — To bed at 10 —
but the boy would not let us settle to
sleep for an hour or more.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 5, 1884.

Overcast m'fg., heavy rain all the P.M. —
Blkb. at 8.30 — it was nearly 11 before at last
I was able to go to the Royal Soc'y of Painters
in Water Colors. where I labored faithfully
& with some gratification for more than
an hour, finding a good deal that interested me. Then I went to Exhib'n of Society
of British Artists & scampered nastily thro'
800 pictures there. & back to hotel soon
after 1 — went with Annie to Dr. James Res-
faurant, Piccadilly & had a 2/6 Table d'hote
lunch — then with A. to Royal Institute of P.
W.C. & spent an hour or more perusing
previous visit & impressions — then to hotel.
in pouring rain — Then I went to Charing
cross station & bought tickets to Paris for the
party & made all other necessary arrange-
ments — then had my hair cut & back to
hotel — Tea at 7 — then settled bill —
£12. 5. or about $10. P. day — Dr. Wiltshire
but that however, by charging a 6 guineas
for his 3 visits — Tried to get to bed at 10
but Jas. junior was in fractious mood
making our final packing very difficult —
finally got to bed soon after 11.
