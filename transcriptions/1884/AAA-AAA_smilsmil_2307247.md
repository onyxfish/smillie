## Left Page

### December, Friday 19. 1884.

Clear & exceedingly cold — got to
studio about 10 & for awhile worked
on "the Edge of the Wood" & small sketch
"Stray Lambs" — then washed up oil
brushes etc — put them away & got out
water color business — Home at 1.15 —
back at 2.30 — Did not do much else
during the afternoon than make
waste & remount on solid fruit board
back W.C. study in Churchyard at
Tretrat that has buckled in its frame.
Home at 5.30 — dinner at 6 — Everts,
Annie, father & I had a game of word
making & talking — Charle came in
& stayed until 10 — To bed about
11.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 20. 1884.

Still exceedingly cold — below
zero — Father kept in all the mit[h].
I got to studio soon after 10 &
to work planning all. c. 10½
× 15 — from a charcoal sketch
made in an orchard last
summer — outlined it on the
paper — Home at 1 — lunch — Father
did not come in until near 3 —
Geo. came at 2.30 & I worried at
father's non appearance — When
he came he hurriedly ate a little
then he & I went to depot & I saw
him off in 3.30 train — then to studio
& started a little W.C. sketch 7 × 11
Cliffs near Tretrat — Home to dinner
at 6, after going to Grady's about frames
Everts. At G Annie & I went to Mr
Morse's — 43 W. 39th St — company
— then until after 11 — then home
& right to bed —
