## Left Page

### January, Sunday 20, 1884

At about the same cheer-
ful level as yesterday, with
an enlivening sense that I
may stick there indefinitely.
Was able to write a short
note home at night — Had
visits from bro. Ge. & from
Walter, George & Jno. Valentin.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 21, 1884

Feeling better, but not yet
able to go out. Breakfast
in bed, then up for all day.
So far improved that at night
I wrote several letters — To
bed at 11 & passed the most
comfortable night that I have
had for a fortnight.
