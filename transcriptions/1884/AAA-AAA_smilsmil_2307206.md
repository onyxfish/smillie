## Left Page

### September, Friday 26. 1884.

*Oxford. Randolph House. Room 112.*

Up soon after 6 — after a disturbed night.
A day of sunshine & showers — a good deal of flying about getting the last things packed — no key
to the new trunk — boy has lost it — went out twice before
9 o'clk — no store open — bkfst at 8 — bus at the door
at 9 — at last, all things together & we got away at
9.15 & to Paddington Station more than ¾ an hour
before starting time of train 10.10 — but it was not
too much time — had to take with tickets & baggage.
Got to Windsor at about 11 & went at once to White
Hart Hotel — smart shower — soon over — got youngsters fed & to bed & we went out & spent nearly
two hours going over the castle — lingered in the
top of Round Tower — lovely atmospheric effects.
Back to hotel & had lunch before 2. Then rested —
away for Oxford on 4.10 train — due at 5.45 — but it
was fully 6.15 before we got in — vexatious delay in
getting luggage & cab — at last to Randolph Hotel &
got pleasant rooms & had our tea at 7.30 — our
temporary nurse refused to eat with the servants &
we told her she might go back to London. During
part of the way from Windsor it rained. To bed
about 10.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 27. 1884.

*Leamington. Manor House Hotel. Room 28c*

Two or three showers, but much pleasant sunshine. Nurse presented herself early in the mng
very contrite & after hesitation Annie concluded to
try her again — Bkfst before 9 & as soon after as we
could we all started out for a walk & went steadily until 1 going first through Christ's Ch. College —
got into the Cath. Ch. & heard part of the service —
delicious — the interior one of the finest to my mind
that I have seen. Then through cloisters & into dining
hall — out to Broad walk — on to Magdalen College &
through Quad's & cloisters there & to Addison's walk —
met an ostrich & he swallowed for us a horse chestnut
burr & all — then back & into several Quad's of other
colleges & at last to Bodleian Library. Then, at 1, to hotel.
Lunch at 1.30 — we all rode in a hack with the
children from 3 to 4 going all about town.
At 5.25 took train to Leamington (Between
4 & 5 Annie & I walked to New College & around the
beautiful old gardens there — also the cloisters)
Got to L. at 6.30 & right to Manor House Hotel.
After getting the boy's supper for him we had
our tea at about 7.30. I got to bed about
9.30.
