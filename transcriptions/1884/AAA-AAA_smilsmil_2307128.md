## Left Page

### April, Saturday 19. 1884.

Pleasant, varying clear and cloudy — almost a mizzle of rain at night — To studio at 10 + spent most of the morning repainting foreground on little picture of Yosemite Falls. Wett just back from Detroit — Home to dinner abt 1.30 — back at 3.30 finished painting — got out some of my etchings to have Gradey mount them — Home about 6 — Rested for awhile. Tea at 7 — + at 8 Annie + I went to last Piel Concert of the season, which we enjoyed very much — I did not feel strong enough after it to go to The Century + help entertain some members of The St. Botolph Club of Boston but home + to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 20. 1884.

Delightful day — B[illegible] about 9 — Domestic duties + writing letters until noon — out + had a short walk before dinner abt 1.30 — at 3.30 went to studio — Charl. with his camera in George's room. Nellie, Sheldon — her sister Marie + her boy there — some photos taken — Home again at 4.30 — wrote more letters + played with the boy — Tea after 7 — Even. loafing + writing — out with my letters + mailed them after 10 o'clk. To bed soon after 11.
