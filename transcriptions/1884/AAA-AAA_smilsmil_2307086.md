## Left Page

### January, Thursday 24, 1884

Dismal day — pouring rain.
Bkfst between 9 & 10 — Felt
depressed at finding myself
no better — Weather too bad
for me to think of going out.
In the mty, took a walk in
the parlor — & again in the after-
noon — Dinner at 1.40 —
Read some, but my eyes hurt
so that I had to give it up.
Eveg — Annie read aloud
— some of Matthew Arnold's poetry
& two chapters from a light
French novel — To bed before
11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 25, 1884

Cold, clear day — Bkfst in bed —
arose after 9 — Headache — Went
to studio at 12 — To Dr. Yale's at
1 — Home & had dinner at 2.10
Spent most of the rest of this
day on lounge & had a good
sleep — Eveg — was at 7 —
wrote notes to Chas. H. Hart
& S. R. Koehler — Took a glass
of Peptonized milk at 11.
