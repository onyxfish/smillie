## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 2. 1884.

Heavy rain — up late — To
studio at 10 — Worked on
Lake Geo. drawing altho'
at times it was so dark as
to make it very difficult
to see. — Home at 1 o'clk —
back at 2.45 — Worked as
long as I could see —
Signed 54 Jab. [illegible] for Cassell
& Co. Packed Etching No. 7
(the plate) & at 5.30 took
it to Adams Expts — sent it
to Richter 9 E. 13 St. Bills —
Home at 6 — At 7 Annie
& I went to Ed & Cookes to
dinner — Met Dr. J. W. Yale
& wife there. — Good dinner
& pleasant evef. — Home
at 10.15 & Right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 3. 1884.

Clear cold day — got to studio
at 10 — Spent some time fixing
my two drawings — Wht. Head
neck Coast & Lake Geo. — from
the stretcher & cutting them
then got started on drawing
of a hilly road & hill near
Montrose that I have christened
"Stray Lambs" — Markhouse, the
architect came in while I was
at work on the sky. — Home at 1 —
back at 2.30 — Worked until
dark — Home before 6. Emma —
Grace D — Fanny & Will Kimball
to tea — After that they went to
Singing Club rehearsal in 14th St.
& I went to Walter's to consult
about Bay window plans — At
9.45 I went for Annie — home
at 10.45 & to bed at 11.30.
