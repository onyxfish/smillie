## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 25, 1884

Overcast & very cold — Got to studio
at 10 & to work on 41×66 straining
in a suggestion of color effect by the
use of a mixture of turps, oil & sweat
& transparent colors — getting the effect
& not unlike a huge water color —
Home at 1 — Nelie, Katie & Malese. Then
to lunch — back at 2.30 & worked for an
hour, getting my tinting completed.
then home — Will Cook there. He helped
hang pictures in parlor bed room —
Kindled fire in furnace & we all scu-
rried about until 6 when we had dinner
Dr. Adams dining us — after dinner, dressed
Soon after 8 the company began to assem-
to congratulate Mother Cook on her 80th Birth day
the house, just got into order, looked very pretty
masses of flowers, the gifts of friends, everywhere
the largest display from Mr. & J. Stewart, who
was one of the company, adorned with lace &
resplendent in diamonds — more than forty
friends came & seemed to enjoy themselves
very much — Will & Sue from Cambridge — James
[illegible] & other members from Phila. & Dr.
Adams & family from Ridgefield — a fine
supper from Purcell's — with champagne at
10.30 — the company left immediately after
Dr. A. & family stayed with us over night
& we got to bed at midnight —
Heard that Addie Hawes is to marry the
young tutor next spring.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 26, 1884

Overcast n'f. clearing to a charming
winter day — Rkp: a little later than
usual — Nelie unable to get up but she
managed to get away for home by a n'f
train — to studio at 10.30 — Stretched small
canvas & outlined Cliffs of Normandy up a
bit worked a little on Sep. P.M. — with
cows & other odd jobs — Home at 1 — back
at 2.30 — tinted small cliffs & worked
a little on "A fallow field", getting ready
to send 3 pictures to the Brooklyn exhbn —
At 4.30 left studio & went to Grady's and
ordered large W.C. frame — Home to
dinner at 6 — at 7.30 Will came for
Annie — they went to Met. Opera Ho. to hear
Tannhauser — I went to Murray Hill Hotel
to see Chase — not in — hue home & spent
the evef. like a good father by the side of
my boy, giving him such aid & comfort
as I could during his paroxysms of
coughing. Annie came in at 12 and
I got to bed.
