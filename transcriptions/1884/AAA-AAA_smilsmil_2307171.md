## Left Page

### July, Monday 14, 1884.

Brilliant mtf. delightfully fresh. Our
cook & serving woman, Rosalie, a grand
mother, came this mtf. As we had to get
supplies & the experiment was new, there
were delays & misunderstandings, but
we got our bkfp — d chops & potatoes by 9
o'clk. After 10 when I got off for the little
farm at the north cliff where I made a pencil
sketch & got home to lunch soon after 1 o'clk.
The festivities of the day (The National fête —
like our 4th of July) were to begin at 2 — so about
2.30 we went to the Public Square & were
in good time — boys & young men climbed a
greased pole for prizes at the top — then a cart
with driver in Rubber coat was driven under a
frame from which hung a pail of water, to the
bottom of which was hung a ring — now boys
would mount the trail of the cart, some having a
long pole & as the cart passed under the pail
he tried to catch the ring upon it, succeeding
generally in upsetting the pail of water over him-
self & companion. Then children blindfolded
by having an absurd manikin head put over their
own, tried to walk to a ribbon & cut it with
scissors — At 4 o'clk — we adjourned to the beach
where there was a swimming match between
four of the young men of the town & after that a
Duck Race — 15 or 20 ducks being thrown out of a
boat & as many swimmers rushing into the
water after them — it was quite exciting & a full
half hour & a long peine in the surf before they
were all caught. Thunder storms passed around
us, but little rain fell on us until after 6, while
we were at dinner — After 8 I went out to the
square to see the colored lights & the procession
which was a very simple affair — In, &
to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 15. 1884.

Grey day — with a very few drops of rain & a few
faint sunbeams. After bkfp. started out with
my oil traps & went to little farm on top of south
cliff — many vexations in starting my first oil
sketch for the season — among others — forgetting seat
to camp stool. But in spite of everything I worked
until near 1, getting my sketch done. Then
tramped home to lunch. Took a long rest
after that & then out to the beach with large
sheet of paper & commenced large pencil
sketch of south cliff, boats etc — Annie out
with me — in before 6 & then to dinner —
after that marketed for the mtf — then
home — After 8, Annie & I went out on the
beach & sat for awhile enjoying the faint
twilight & the still water. To bed
at about 10.30.
