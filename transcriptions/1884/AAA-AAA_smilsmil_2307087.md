## Left Page

### January, Saturday 26. 1884.

Clear & very cold — got up
with a rheumatic sort of
headache that made me quite
useless until late in the after-
noon — Got to studio about
10.30 — hoping [illegible] on for w?
come about [illegible] p.p. but he
did not. I was not able to do
anything. Home at 1 — dinner
— after that, lay on lounge &
did not go out again. Fanny
K. made a call — Tea
about 7 — [illegible], Annie and
[illegible] — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 27. 1884.

Beautiful winter day, over-
cast in the afternoon. Got
up feeling quite well. B&p.
after 9 — After 11 Rode in Mad.
Ave. cars up to 83d. St. walked
there a little while, then rode
home — Read until dinner
at 2 — then a short nap —
about 4 again rode up to
83d. St. met Charles for a mo-
ment — home in about an hour.
Tea at 6.30 — [illegible] wrote to
father — B.H. Ticknor & J. Thomas
Murphy — At 9 went out and
mailed letters — then read
until 10.30 — To bed about 11.
