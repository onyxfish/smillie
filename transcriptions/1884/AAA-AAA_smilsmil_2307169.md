## Left Page

### July, Thursday 10, 1884.

Quite a heavy shower at 7 A.m. The m'f. clearing — our bkfs. at 8 — then out on
the beach & had a chat with W. Gay &
Knight. As it cleared at 10 I went back
to house & got my W.C. traps & went to the
church yard & worked on that sketch
until 11.45 — then to lunch — about 2
Annie, Nellie, Geo. & I walked to the top
of the hill north of town, hoping to get a
little sketch, but only got there &
found a subject when we were driven
back by a storm of wind & rain that
continued for some time. we got a
little sprinkling before we got in —
there I took a nap as I still feel
very much lassitude — I wrote some upon
letter to Father — Dinner at 6.30 — later in
the ev'g — Annie & I walked through the
town, buying a cheap clock, & then out upon
the hill to the south, passing some of the finest
residences here — back at 9.30 & to bed about
10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 11, 1884.

Another very uncertain day — late when
I got away to my work with little
drawing board — climbed the road way
to the little farm on the hill north of
town & made 6×10 sketch W.C. looking
back over town & beach to the south cliff —
left at 11.45 before I had quite finished
sketch & to hotel at 12.15 & had lunch.
Then a heavy shower — wrote for a while
— H.H. Bacon came in for a few minutes
about 3. but as it was then clearing off
Geo. & I started up the hill with our
traps — I finished m'f. sketch & then went
on to the farm & settled myself to sketch
a bit outside of it — Annie joined me
about 5 — I got through my sketch after
a fashion but another shower caught us
just as we got in — to dinner soon
after 6. Ev'g. not feeling well I
did not go out. Annie engaged giving
Nellie a lesson in French — to bed about
10.
