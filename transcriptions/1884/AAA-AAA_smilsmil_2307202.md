## Left Page

### September, Thursday 18, 1884

Another clear, warm day — Mr. Satchell
spent the day with Annie — After bkfst — I
went to So. Kensington Museum & spent
an hour & a half there, then to museum of
Natural History & used up what little strength
I had left. I went all over the immense
building — Home at 4 & had a half hour
rest before dinner. After dinner, as my
head felt very uncomfortable I went to
bed & slept until 4.30 — At 5 I rode
down to the Tailors — Fickling, & tried on my
clothes — then back, stopping at Swan
& Edgars about my gloves & underclothes —
Tea at about 7 — evng. wrote a short
letter to Charl — did not get to bed
until after 11.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 19, 1884

Yet another very pleasant day — After bkfst. A.
Mr. Satchell & I went to the Health Exhibtn at
Albert Hall — Interested in the model streets of
old London perhaps more than anything else —
We kept steadily at it until 1.30 then home
& had dinner soon after 2. — Mr. S. left to
go to her brother's soon after — At past 4 A. &
I took cab for the Zoological Gardens where
we worked faithfully & systematically until
a little after 6 having done everything but
the Kangaroos who were laid up for re-
pairs — then home & had tea at 7 — wrote
a note to Geo. & just as I was going out
to mail it, he & Nellie came in and
spent the evng. Rec'd a letter from
father — still at Montrose — Sep. 7th.
To bed about 10.
