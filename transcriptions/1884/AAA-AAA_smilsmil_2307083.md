## Left Page

### January, Friday 18, 1884.

Intense headache — could
not lift my head from the
pillow in the mng. Gave
up my milk diet with
speed — Late in the afternoon
was able to get up —
A more comfortable night
with slight return of fever.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 19. 1884.

Heavy & stupid with no
very active symptoms — as
Dr. [illegible] says — with very few
objective & a great many
subjective ones —
Wriggled dreamily through
the day & night.
