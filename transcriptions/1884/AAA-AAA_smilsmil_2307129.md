## Left Page

### April, Monday 21, 1884

Pleasant day — To studio at 10 — Gradey's man there — gave him etchings to mount — then went to No. 1 Broadway — human line — + arranged for passage on May 17" — Then to A.B.N. Co — Home to dinner at 1.30 — Back at 3 — Uncle W. + aunt Agnes came in + made quite a call — I did no work — at 5, with Geo. went to Gradey's + ordered 12 × 22 frame — Home at 6.30 — Tea later — Every [illegible] — + brought proof of Fastin's engraving Rocky Mts in bed room — To bed at 10.30. I did not turn over until after 7 o'clk — next m'g.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 22, 1884

Beautiful day — Got to Studio about 10 + to work on 10 × 17. "A fallow field" — Repainted the sky solidly + made other parts fresher — Home to dinner abt — back at 3 — Minn. Melford + her cousin, Miss Cook — called — I finished 10 × 17 — was led up + at 4.30 went to Exp. Office with drawings for panel in main hall in Montrose — then to Putnam's + bot Blotting paper for my jabs. pp Then to McKeen's shop — then to Gradey's + ordered frame for 10 × 17 — then home at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Rusty wrote letters to H.F. Richter + Claude Contteuden — Near 11 when I got to bed.
