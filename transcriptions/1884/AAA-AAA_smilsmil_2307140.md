## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 13, 1884

Beautiful m'g — overcast afternoon + rain at night. To studio before 10 — wrote two or three letters then began proving "A Lady of Cairo visiting to take to Paris to get Bridgman autograph. Home at 1 — back before 3 + worked until 5.20 — Faking 13 pfs — only one of which was at all like what I wanted — then home — Annie gave up the idea of going to Kitty Stewart's wedding — Tea at 7 — Evening — writing letters — Mrs. Park called at about 9 — Heavy rain — I went to drugstore + got ingredients for "Tonic" — Home + to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 14, 1884

Since a heavy thunderstorm early in the m'g — before daylight — Charming day — To studio at 10 — A run on the 2° Nat. Bank — so that I had to go down town to see if I could get our letters of Credit — went to Brown Brothers without success — to A.B.N. Co. + left drawing of locomotive — + got check for $2.30 — Home at 1 — dinner — at 2.20 at 2° Nat. Bank where I stood on a line for an hour, then abandoned it + went to N.A.D. Annual meeting there until voting was over, then to studio + packed until near 7 — Then to 39' S. + saw Geo. C — about money matters — home to tea at 7.30 — wrote letters etc, until about 10 o'clk. To bed before 11.
