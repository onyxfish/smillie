## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 29. 1884.

Bright day, but cool. To studio at 10 — Dr. [illegible] came in + I assorted some of my etchings. Finally got to work on 10×17 "A Fallow Field" — went over it generally but very lightly. Soon after 12 went to Bank of the Metropolis for a Bank Book for Birnie — Had to get a young man from Dempsey & Carroll's to identify me — Home to dinner at 1.15 — back at 3 — worked until near 5 — with Geo. I walked up to Grady's — we stopped in + saw Jno. Snedicor in his store. — Home at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Eveth — Reading + writing + to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 30. 1884.

Bright + beautiful day — got to studio before 10 — After writing some notes, To work on 12×22 "The Edge of the Wood" — Painting very carefully over it — Belle + Many Tweedy called — Home to dinner at 1 — back at 3 — Finished 12×22 — + painted a change in one of the sheep in a "bit on Marblehead". After 5 went to Flint's and paid bill — Then to Century + wrote two or three notes — Home at 7.30 — Tea — Evening, Read + wrote. To bed before 11.
        Young Ordway called in the eveng.
