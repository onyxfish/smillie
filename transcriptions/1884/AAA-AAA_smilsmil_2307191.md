## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 27. 1884.

Overcast mtg. with some sunlight —
until 10 o'clk. with Geo. who then, with
his family, got off on the desJo' bus,
started on his Holland Journey — Then
I returned to the house & getting paper
& charcoal I went to a farmyard in the
Rue Isabey. So end. — & worked until
1.15 on a sketch there — Then home &
had lunch — after that, finished up my
sketch of the mtf — Then, after 3 — as I
did not feel very bright, I wandered
out with Annie — first to the beach — &
then out to the Grand Val — where, in a
farm yard, I made a little pencil
sketch — little dribbles of rain had been
falling for some time — quite a shower
by the time we got home — 5.40. — To
dinner at 6.15 — home at 7 —
Eve'g. wrote a note to Mr. Wm. Neilson
about my Keen Valley lot. To bed at 10 —
but was more or less up with D. Jun. from
12 to 3 o'clk. The third night of his weaning
from his midnight bottle.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 28. 1884.

A very stormy day — after bkfo., prepared to
work in doors — made outline on canvas of
view from Crucifix looking to the east — Then
took study of clouds that I painted
from window some time ago — & finished
the sketch of landscape, a bit on the
St. Clair road, that I then rubbed in —
twice went out for a little airing, but
it rained more or less heavily almost
all day — Lunch soon after 1 — I wrote
a little note to Addie Strawes on the
death of her two children — Afternoon,
I worked on background of a barrel
study that I painted some time ago
of boats on the beach — about 5, went out
with Annie for another airing — on the
beach met Dana, Bacon & Dr. Oliver —
— back at 6 — To dinner at 6.15 — Then
home — washed up brushes, etc.
To bed at about 10.
