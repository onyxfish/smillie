## Left Page

### November, Monday 17, 1884

Delightful day — some time with the fresco man who finally went away —
did not get to studio until near 11 —
there, by sudden freak, started on the
painting of a little bit; x based on
a study of rocks made at Montrose
some years ago. Home to lunch at 1.
back soon after 2 + managed, before
dark, to get the sketch "all in" — an
experiment in painting upon canvas
without fratte or glaze. Home at
6\. dinner — Annie feeling the
effects of her cold, making her heavy
desired to go out, so we went to her
bro. Ed's in 37th St. found them
at dessert — Mr & Mrs. Aley & Mrs. Sprague
f. Oakes
there — spent the evening until
near 10, then home + to bed —

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 18, 1884

The nicest pleasant weather still
holds on — Three fresco painters who
finished the cornice & style of ceiling
in parlor had come — I spent some time
with them after bkfst — to studio at
about 10.30 — to work on a study
painted at Lake Geo. in 80 — trying to
get it into shape to be 24th at the
Am Art Galleries Sketch Exhib. — lunch
at home as usual — worked on sketch
as long as I could see — then rested
for awhile in big chair — home before
6 — dressed, + at 7 with Annie, went
to Walter's — his 8th Annual Wedding
celebration dinner — Mother Cook. Aunt
Anna Ireland — Two Misses Ireland of
Orange — Ed W. & wife — Geo & Valentine
there — a good dinner, of course —
Home at 11.30 — a nasty sleet storm —
the breaking up of our long spell
of delightful weather.
