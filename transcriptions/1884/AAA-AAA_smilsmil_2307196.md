## Left Page

### September, Saturday 6, 1884

*Southampton — South Western Hotel — Room 110.*

Dismal day — one or two brief & sickly
efforts at sunshine — but for the most part
mizzle & rain — Got to dock about 6 and
went through a Customs Ex. — Trunks searched
to the bottom in an aggravating way. At last
we got through & by R.R. went up to S.W. Hotel
& depot — At once took a room for the day —
the boy had his bath & bkft. & we had ours — then
went to Opdyke, the banker & got some money —
then with Annie wandered awhile in rain
& mud, then to hotel & soon after 11 — Annie,
the boy & I went to sleep on one bed & slept
until 1.15 — nearly 3 before we could get
our lunch — At 5.5 we started for Salisbury
expecting to get there at 6.20 but it was
almost 7 before we got in — miserable wet
afternoon & night — The boy quite fagged out
but we got him his supper at once & then
his bed — then we had our supper &
it was quite 10 before we were able to
get to bed — The boy behaved admi-
rably all night.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 7, 1884

*Salisbury — White Hart Inn — Room 57.*

Breezy, cool day with clouds & sun-
shine — B&b. at 8.30 — got through with
our duties so that at 10 we started out
for sight-seeing — find that we are very near the
cathedral — wandered around it admiring
the beautiful trees as much as the building
itself — At 10.30 we went in to m'g. service & sat
there until nearly 12. — not particularly impres-
sive a beautiful — interminably long but interesting.
Left just before the sermon & wandered about the
streets for near an hour — then home — got our lunch
at — & at 2.15 started for Stonehenge — about
2 m. out, stopped at Old Sarum, the site of a Saxon
city, now only an immense mound — extremely
interesting — then on through Amesbury getting to the
henge at about 4.30 — the drive over Salisbury
plain, bleak — the henge, immensely interesting &
me — drove back for a mile or two over the turf,
then onto a road passing the Lake House — very
interesting & charming with hedges & noble trees.
Back to hotel before 6 — tea at 7 —
