## Left Page

### October, Friday 24, 1884

Charming, clear, fresh day, but got up with a stupid headache that annoyed me all day — The Prescors came at 8 o'clk & commenced operations on front parlor ceiling by washing the centre field of the ceiling, at which they worked all day — Wm. Douglas called to get his orders about the painting — It was after 10 when I got to studio — so cool that I kindled a fire — not in a condition to work, so I fooled away the m'f — home to lunch at 1 — with the workmen for some time & back to studio at 3 — Telly came & stretched my oil studiess and I did a little at mounting my water color studies — Ferguson called — Home to dinner at 6 — went with Annie & spent part of eve'g. with her Mother & Mrs. Valentine — Home before 10 — gave my poor feet some attention & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 25, 1884

Dubious looking morning early, but clear by 10 — Spent some time with Fresco Painters — Plumbers & carpenters — To studio at 11 — mounted a sketch or two & home to lunch at 1 — At 2.30 Annie & I went to 6th Ave. & 42nd St. by Elevated R.R. to 72nd St. where we met Will & Fanny K. — Emma D. & Chas. Collins — we walked down to Riverside Park & through it up to 125th St. Thence to R.R. Station at 125th St. & 8th Ave. Exceedingly strong wind & very cold — could hardly be called comfortable — Took the K's & Chas. C. home with us at 6 — Emma would not go with us — I went to 110 Mad. Ave. & got Elizabeth Howe & at 6.30 we had dinner — after that made ourselves comfortable by the dining room fire — trouble with the tank overflowing — could not be stopped — too many faucets — went to the plumbers — could not find him — let water run in bath tub all night — Gave Chas. C. briarwood [pipe?] that I brought from [Donders?] — went to 3rd Ave. got one for W.K. & they smoked with fun. Jal. just gave a concert for half an hour at 11 — to bed after that.
