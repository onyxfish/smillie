## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 31. 1884.

Mild & muggy — overcast, but sun
struggling to shine, a very enervating
day — Will Cook took b'kf't with us.
Nearly 10 when I got to studio &
commenced work on W.C. 14×20
"Outside of a Farm yard, near Étretat"
Slashed away & got the paper
covered soon after 12 — Abt 2.30 I
went to Miss Abbott's Room & worked
on a drawing of hers until 1.15, th[en]
home to lunch — back at 2.30 &
after working awhile on sky — I
washed it nearly all off — Before
4 Miss A. brought a flower drawing
up to me & I worked over background,
then with Geo. to Grady's — To shop
& got some back boards — home &
6 & had dinner — Evng. pasted
into neat f'kl'p some unbound
Vols of Punch. Fixed escutcheons
on doors in parlor & bed room —
To bed soon after 10.
