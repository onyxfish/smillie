## Left Page

### November, Saturday 15, 1884

*At home again.*

Wakened at about 6.30 from car
window saw beautiful sunrise + am
delicious November pictures — Got in
to N.Y. at 7.10 + home at about 7.45 —
quite unexpected — Had bk fo: some
after 8 — went to Warren, Fuller + [illegible]
about paper hanging — It was 11 before
I got to studio — startled by my big
canvas just brought in by Kelly — It
is the biggest one I have ever tackled
41 × 66 in. Home at 12 — lunch at
1 — At 3, Annie went to the Josephine
Ireland Wedding + Reception while
I went to bed to "catch up" + slept
soundly until 5 — Dinner at 6 —
Before 8 we were at the Academy
of music — first Phil. concert of the season —
afterward walked up home in company
with Fred & Alice Ireland — Sat
& read news papers — To bed at
12.30.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 16, 1884

Mild, lovely day — Bkfst at 9 — were
asked by Mr. Haven Putnam to join
a party for a walk & then lunch,
but we excused ourselves — for some
times reading — at near noon Annie
& I rose up to 72d St. + then went
into the park & sauntered about for
an hour, finding it very delightful,
then home to dinner at 2 — After
that, again reading & writing — went
with A. + boy to Mother C's but finding
the other children there did not go in —
the whooping cough is progressing —
about dusk. Oscar Ireland called for
a few minutes — wrote letter home + at
6 went with it to G.C. Depot — then took
a short walk — tea at 7. Evg. Reading
— the boy very restless —
