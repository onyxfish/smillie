## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 30, 1884

Very mild day, without wind —
mts. overcast — went to the beach
— sky cleared almost without a
cloud. very warm — I made a
w.c. tinted sketch of boat with
fishermen getting ready for a start
— Annie & Nellie went in bathing
— Rec'd a letter from Father — 15" in.
Home at 12.30 — lunch at 1.30 —
worked on my mts. sketch for
awhile — about 4, feeling unfit for
any more work I went out and
sauntered about with Annie —
on the beach until near 6 —
To dinner soon after 6 — After
it I went up to the Crucifix to
study Evg. effects — home at
about 8 — Evg. — in chatter and
usual duties — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 31, 1884

A very warm, still day — Got up with a
headache — about 10 got away to my work
on path part way up south cliff & painting
until 1 o'clk. I got the foreground painted
in after a fashion — am much discouraged
at my continued inability to get qualities
of color that I very distinctly feel. Annie
came up & sat with me for awhile — The
sea perfectly calm — atmosphere very
hazy. Lunch soon after I got home
& then I gave up to my headache &
went to bed where I stayed until 5
— Evg. I prepared my tea instead
of going to the hotel to dinner — Later,
with Annie I walked for half an hour
on the Terrace, enjoying the quiet sea, the
twilight & moonlight — Then in & made
a charcoal sketch on canvas from one in
pencil that I made a few days ago.
To bed late & passed a restless
night.
