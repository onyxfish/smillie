## Left Page

### November, Friday 7, 1884

Clear, breezy & cool — Spent some time after bkfst with Douglas mixing colors for the woodwork in parlor bed rooms — Nearly 11 when I got to studio — there to work "View near Marblehead." Sand papered it & then repainted the sky solidly. Home at — fresco man on the cornice of bed room — Near 3 when I got to studio — went over the rest of Whittst? pictures — Lib. Home & Miss Phillips called — I went in to see Tom Smith for a few minutes — Home before 6 — Aunt Jessie there — She took dinner with us & at 8 I took her to the Hrusheluood? gives — Home before 9 — Read alone for a little while — The boy's cough is increasing — so bed Before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 8, 1884

Very pleasant day — got up with a headache — Spent some time with painter & fresco man in parlor bed room — then to studio for a few minutes — then down town to Chase's — then to Drexel, Morgan & Co's & got bal. ($250) on 2nd letter of credit, used last summer — then to A.B.N. Co & saw Will — then home abt. 2 & had lunch — took 3.30 train & got to Po'k at 5.45 — walked right home, getting in at 6 — had tea — found all very well — Father having nearly recovered from his recent neuralgic attack — His bruised eye is much discolored & of course disfigures him very much — we sat & chatted until after 10 o'clk, then to bed.
