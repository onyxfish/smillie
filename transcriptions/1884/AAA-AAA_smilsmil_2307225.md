## Left Page

### November, Monday 3, 1884.

Charming day — nearly 10 before
I got to studio — Pitched into the
repainting of "Evening Shadows" —
33×44 — commenced on foreground,
pitching it much higher in key
& cooler. Rec'd. note from Miss Hall
orwell — enclosing $400 — payment
for "Pond's Onettet" (250) & "Crisp Oct." (150)
went to Bank with it — then to [illegible] —
then home to lunch at 1.30 — back
to studio at 2.30 & worked until
5 — then cleaned up — Home to
dinner at 6 — Eveg. writing &
marking linen & underclothes —
The boy's cough continues to be very
harassing. After 10 went out to
post my letters & walked to 48
St. & 5th ave. —     To bed at
11.30.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 4, 1884.

*Election Day —*

Overcast — light showers — heavy
rain after 5 P.m. — Got to studio
about 10 & right to work on "Evening
Shadows" — on left hand trees all
day — Home to lunch at 1 —
Annie had gone off with Collins
to meet others by appointment —
& have a walk — The weather
spoiled it & they (A & C.) went to
Met. Museum of Art — Annie
ret'd at 2 — I got to studio at 2.30
& worked until after 4 — although
it was very dark. — Charl came
in — Home to dinner at 6 —
Eveg. decided between the
boy — Reading & writing —
To bed soon after 10.

The carpenters finished at noon.
