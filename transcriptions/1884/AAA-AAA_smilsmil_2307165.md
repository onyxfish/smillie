## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 2. 1884.

Hot day — got up feeling perfect
used up — help- afting — at 10.30
Geo. & I went by bus down to the
rag market. where I spent some
time but found nothing — Saunter
ed back part of the way along
the Boulevards & at 1.30 took a
roll & cup of tea — After that
a nap — Annie & the boy also
slept soundly — I wrote notes
to W.f. Bacon & Mr. Wm Cooker
of Montrose — At 4 o'clk I went
to the Louvre & spent an hour
making notes of pictures — at 5
along the Rue Revoli & stopped
at the Continental Hotel to see
Mr. Wintherbee — not in — to hotel
at 6 & had dinner — After that
went with Annie & she ordered for
herself a hat — every quiet.
but did not get to bed until
11 — boy behaved better but badly
enough.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 3. 1884.

A very hot day — About 10 o'clk —
Annie, Nellie, Geo. & I Rode over
to Ecole des Beaux Arts & to our great
disgust found that all the attendants
being engaged. we could not get
through the building before 2 o'clk.
Annie & I then went to the Luxem-
bourg & took a look at the pictures
there — then to the Pantheon, walked
through it & then into St. Etienne —
Back to hotel at 12.30 & had lunch.
after that, a nap. About 2.30 Annie,
Nellie, Geo. & I went over to Ecole des Beaux Arts —
when we promptly got an attendant & went through
with the buildings — out at about 4 — I took voiture
& rode to Orleans R.R. station & by 4.55 train out
to Juvisy, about 30 min. where Mr. Jo. de Mare met
me & drove to his son's house "Chateau de la Fontaine" —
a beautiful establishment. delightfully situated
on a nice side — we strolled about the grounds till
dinner time — the family in Holland, except an English
gov — & two children — more strolling after dinner
To station for 8.52 train — Mr. de M — made a mistake & we
missed it; I sent him home & took 9.30 train in to Lyons
station — in at 10.15 — very slow — moonlight & delicious
effects over the river — got to hotel soon after 11.
