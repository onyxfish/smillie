## Left Page

### January, Thursday 10. 1884.

Clear, beautiful winter day.
Got to studio about 10 o'clk —
Worked for a little while on "Stray
Lambs" — then mounted my
two other drawings & pasted
labels on framed etching pts.
Home at 1.15 — found Miss Helen
Butler there (who stayed to dinner)
& Mrs. Birney — After dinner I
went to Knordeis — got some paper
& had a look at the pictures then
thru to Miss Abbott's to look at
her W.C. work in their frames. Thru
to my own room where I made
part of a sketch of Stray Lambs for
W.C. cat. At 5.15 went to Ditson's
B'dway & 18th S— thru to Grady's Shop
42nd S. & 7th Ave — thru to the store. Thru
to Thomas's photo. gallery — & at 6.30
home — Tea at 7 — Self. lounging
& putting Master James to sleep
after his ½ hour naps — wrote a note
to Cottrell.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 11. 1884.

Nasty, dark day with rain &
sleet. To studio at 10 — got my
etching pts off to Century Club
& did some general fussing
about. Frame for "Stray Lambs"
came & I put it in. Weldon
called with proof of a plate
in trouble — Told him to come
this p.m. — Dinner at home at
1.30 — To studio at 3 — W. came
with plate & I layed a ground
on it — Home at 6.30 — Tea — at
7.30 with Annie, went to Worther
cook, got Kachttm & we went to
Metropolitan Opera House. There
met Fanny K. Emma & Grace J —
Elgth Howe, Chas. Collins — Geo. C. —
& Jno. Valentine — Opera of Gioconda
Heard Nilsson — Scalchi — etc. —
about 12 when we got out —
12.30 when we got home —
1.30 when James just let
us go to bed.
