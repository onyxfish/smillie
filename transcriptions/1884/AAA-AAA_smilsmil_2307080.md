## Left Page

### January, Saturday 12. 1884.

Pleasant & cold — feel pretty well used up after last night's
festivities — Got to studio about
10 — made sketch d "Stray lambs
for W.C. Cat. Before noon, Roull
Took the W.C. to the Century Club.
Home to dinner at 1.30 Annie
out to a lunch party at Mary
Willard's — After dinner I
Took a nap until 3.30 — Then to
Studio — did little — Home at 6.
Tea — at 8 went to see the Clark
pictures n [illegible] at Am Art Gallery —
Then to Artists Fund pictures at N.A.
Then to Annual meeting of Century
at 9.15 — Excitement over the Newton-
Heaven squabble — very full meeting.
Speeches by Robinson — Judge Daly —
Tracy — Nash & Judge Choate — I
exhibited 10 etchings — Home
at 12.30 — To bed about.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 13. 1884.

Cold, raw & overcast — got up
feeling played out — after b[rea]kf[as]t,
on lounge until 12 — at 12.30
Annie & I went to Century and
saw the pictures — walked home.
Elizabeth Howe took dinner
with us at 2 — again, much
time on the lounge — at 5.30
went to Will's to learn that
Minn & her boy are getting along
but not without trouble — Tea
at 6.45 — Eve[ning] wrote letter
home. Took Irto G.C.D. at 10
— To bed about 10.30
