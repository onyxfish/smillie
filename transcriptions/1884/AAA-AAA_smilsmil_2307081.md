## Left Page

### January, Monday 14, 1884

Stayed at home all day in
a generally used up condition
— Pain in throat & bronchia
J— just also pretty well
used up by a cough &
stuffing up & head. Sent
for Dr. Yale who came to
minister to us.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 15, 1884

Pain in chest so increased
that I was in bed nearly
all day. miserably un-
comfortable & feverish nights
