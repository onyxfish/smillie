## Left Page

### January, Monday 28. 1884.

Overcast with light snow later in the day — Got to studio at about 10.30 & spent some time picking up things & trying to get myself into trim for work. Before noon, J. N. [illegible] me sent over my drawing of "The Narrows, Lake Geo." at my request, as I wish to work upon it. I studied over it for some times — Home abt. Rosalie Butler took dinner with us — After she left I took a nap & got to studio at 3.45 — Worked for about an hour on Lake George. Home soon after 5 — Tea before 7 — Early part of evng. Annie read aloud — then I wrote awhile. — To bed at 11.

Geo. & family went to Po'k this P.M. for a short visit.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 29. 1884.

Dull day — a little struggling sun light in the afternoon. Got to studio soon after 10 & to work on "Narrows Lake Geo." While at dinner, had it taken again to the N.A. of D. Home at 1 — back at 3 — Young Bellows (Son of A.F.) came in — selling his father's effects — I bought 9 copper plates for $10.50 — I outlined subject for new W.C. 10×14 & worked awhile on old drawing 10×14 of a [illegible] near Saratoga. Home soon after 5 — Tea at 7 — Eve'g Mr. Herbert Morse called to get Annie's subscription for purchase of a portrait of Mrs. Gibbons by O.J. Lay. Annie read aloud. — To bed about 11.
