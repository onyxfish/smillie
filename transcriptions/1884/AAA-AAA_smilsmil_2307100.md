## Left Page

### February, Thursday 21. 1884.

A really charming day — to studio before 10 & to work on 23×40" "The Lake's outlet" — painting sky — after working until near noon, scraped it all off — abt 1 o clk. scraped it off again & then home to dinner — Fanny F. took dinner with us — back at 2.30 & again painted in the sky — & which I remain not because it pleased me, but because I thought it was about as good as I could do. Soon after 5 went to Century & looked at some papers — home at 6.30 — tea — eveg. wrote several notes — one to [illegible] Fra of Cin. concerning etchnuip

## Right Page

### February, Friday 22. 1884.

Delightful day — mild & clear until toward eveg when it became overcast — To studio at 10 & spent the mfg in morning work over the middle distance "The ponds neter" — It seems to me that my painting work has never before worried & exhausted me as it does not — I find it so impossible to do what I so clearly know that I want to do. It's a cut & relief after I went to barber's had my hair cut — home at 1 — dinner — To studio at 2.30 & worked as long as I could stand up — then home — sick — had an uncomfortable eveg — indigestion — for awhile, a. read aloud — To bed before 11.
