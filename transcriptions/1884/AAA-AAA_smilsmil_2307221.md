## Left Page

### October, Sunday 26, 1884

Brilliant & beautiful day — Bkf: before 9 — Reading until after 11 — then walked up to Central Park — home to dinner at 2 — From 3.30 until after 5, asleep — then went to studio & attended to my fire — Home before 7 & had tea —

Ev'g.. wrote letter home — also one to Bella. Tuesday. Took letter to depot at 10 — home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 27, 1884

Mild cloudy day — after bkf. went with Annie to Warren Fuller & Lange in 42d. S. to look at wall papers — there for an hour without finding anything we liked. — To studio at about 11 — set palette & tried to fix up some of my last summer's studies — a hopeless job — they look to me too bad to be saved — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 and continued the work of the m'g. Got a letter from Wm. Jessup, Montrose about the sale of the Cosper lot. Home to dinner at 6 — went in to our neighbor Edwards' to enquire about an opening discovered by the carpenters in the wall between us — Ev'g.. wrote notes to Wm. Jessup, Wm. H. Boyd & Wm. Smillie — to bed before 11.
