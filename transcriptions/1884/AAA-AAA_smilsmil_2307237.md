## Left Page

### November, Saturday 29, 1884

Pleasant day — after o when I got to studio & still feeling unlike going on with my color sketch for the Cliffs I spent the m'g. Tracing for W.C. & doing other routine work. A messenger came from Will for the drawing & touched [illegible] for $loco — but it was not ready. Home at 1 — back at 2.30 — spent an hour & a half touching on [illegible] loco die. Then at about 5 went to Reichard's & saw Prang's designs for Xmas cards — much interested in the one by Dewi[ng] — then to Newman's & get knobs for the parlor & bed room doors. Home at 6 — dinner — Spent some time in the ev'g putting brass hooks on window sashes and trying to put knobs on but they do not fit the doors — Read aloud for a little whi[le] to Annie. To bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 30, 1884

overcast, mild & very quiet. Not feeling well. I did not get up early — About 11.30 I started out for a walk & went up through Central Park, around upper Reservoir & down to 60th S — where, as it was late, I took car home & got in just in time for dinner at 2 o'clk. About 3 mingled rain & snow began falling — Spent the afternoon with the boy — at 5.30 went to studio — back in an hour — tea at 7 but as I was out of sorts I ate nothing. W'g. wrote letter to Father & took it to G.C.D — at 9.30 — To bed about 10.30
