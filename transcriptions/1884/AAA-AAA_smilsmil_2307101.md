## Left Page

### February, Saturday 23. 1884.

Horrible day — at first rain, then
snow — late in the afternoon it blew
up clear & cool. — Did not get
to studio until after 2.30 — head
uncomfortable — did little work
on the Pond's outlet — Home at 1 —
back before 3 & worked for an
hour or more — home at 6 —
Tea before 7 — at 8 Annie & I
went to Star Theatre & saw —
"Twelfth Night", Modjeska as
Viola — She did not impress me
very favorably — her support was
fairey good & on the whole, the
presentation was entertaining —
out at 10.45 — home at a few
minutes after 11 — Jas. Jun.
waiting — to bed before 12.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 24. 1884.

Charming day — R.[illegible]. at 9 —
Sat about & read & did various
things until 12 when I walked
up to Central Park & out at 72°.
3. gate — on my way down, stopped
at Junie's & caught air for a few
minutes — then home to dinner
at 2 — Eliz. Home with us —
about 4 Kate came in to see
the boy — at the same time,
Sneedon & his nurse came.
From 5 to 7 I had a long nap.
Tea at 7.30 — Werf.-Co. Read
aloud awhile — also I wrote
my letter home & before 11
took it to the G.C. Depot.
