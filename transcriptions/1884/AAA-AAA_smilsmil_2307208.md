## Left Page

*Queen's Hotel, Chester, Room 28 —*

### September, Tuesday 30, 1884.

Clear & crisp mtf — Bkfs. at 9 & at 9.55
we got nicely away for Chester, which
place we reached after a comfortable
& rather uninteresting ride of four hours,
getting into C. at 2.03 — we went at once to
the Queen's Hotel — a R.R. establishment, adjoining the station — got the boy's dinner ready
for him & at about 3 got our own — While
we were waiting for the nurse to get hers,
lay down upon the bed & got a few moments
nap — At 4 Annie & I started out and
by tram car rode to the center of the city —
went into the Cathedral — Service — could not
walk about — a dignified sort of place —
on to the walls at the North gate & walked
all around the city — met Geo. & Nellie — they went
part way — After returning to N. gate — walked
through some of the principal streets & under
the arcades — Returned to hotel at 6.15 —
Had our tea at 8 & got to bed before 10.

## Right Page

*Liverpool — Adelphi Hotel, Room 57.*

### October, Wednesday 1, 1884.

Charming day — Up before 7 & hurried all
things as much as I could to get away by the
8.41 train, — might have done it but for stupidity of Porter, who took us to the wrong train, two
trains starting from same station for same place
at same time over two Roads — Gt. Western & North
Western — just missed our train & took 8.50 which
got us into L. 20 m. later than the other — Got
to Birkenhead at 9.40 & when crossing over the
ferry discovered that one of our bags had been
left in car — so I went back for it & got it — On
my return, went to Dunran Co's — then to Gt. W —
Luggage Depot & then, at about 11, to hotel —
Where the party had got settled, at 12 I went
to Bank of Liverpool — drew £15 — then to Cunard
Office & bought Bella's ticket — Back at 1 — Annie
not yet in — Bella at hotel before we arr'd in
the mtf. The boy considerably out of sorts —
quite a budget of letters awaiting us — Dinner
at 2 — Afternoon, repacking trunks — Tea
at 7 — Rested for an hour or so on lounge —
Took a short stroll in the streets — To
bed at 10.30.
