## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 9, 1884

Heavy Rain nearly all day, with wind — After 10 when I got to studio
to work Repainting "Cedar Meadows"
making great change in the color
— Home to dinner abt — back
at 3 & painted until after 5
in spite of the darkness, but
did not get the Repainting finished. Home at 7 — tea —
eveg. spent with the boy —
Annie — & Reading — To bed
abt 10.30.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 10, 1884

Fine day — Up with a headache
which I struggled with all day —
To studio abt 10 — & to work again
on the Repainting of "Cedar Meadows"
At 12 to Bank of the Metropolis — Cor.
Bdway & 15th St — & deposited Diet Kitchen
money for Annie — Then to 2d Nat. Bk.
& deposited money for Annie & self —
Then home abt to dinner — after
that gave up to my headache &
went to bed & to sleep — Up before
5 — Annie having quite a levee
of her family — To studio & painted
as long as I could see — Then Took
Dr. Herrick's pf. "My Col. Neighbor's
new yard" to him — (his etching has been
just rejected at Boston Art Club exhibit)
Home at 7.30 after stopping to pay
some bills — tea — eveg. for a time
troubling over some Diet Kitchen accts
& money that Mr. White has brought in.
To bed soon after 10.30.
