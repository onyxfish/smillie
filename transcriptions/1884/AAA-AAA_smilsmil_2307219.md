## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 22. 1884.

Another warm day — After [illegible]
went with Annie to Warren Fuller
& Lange's — in at 2°. 3° to select paper
for parlor ceiling — met Oakey — then
— no selection — To studio at 11 — made
paste & got out water color sketches
to mount — also wrapped & pasted
up some papers & books for binding
— To lunch at 1 — back at 2.30
& resumed work — Charl surprised
ed me with a call — just in from
St. Louis & his grand western trip —
Home at 6 & had dinner — just
in time to escape a heavy rain
that kept up most of the night —
Charl came in & got his camera
lens that I brought for him from
London & spent the evef with
us — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 23. 1884.

Lovely day — After 10 when I got to
studio, having waited at home some time
for the wall papering man — Spent
the m'f in mounting some of my
last summer's w.c. sketches — Home
to lunch at 1 — Mr. Cashman — of Warren,
Fuller & Lange called — to consult with
us about the parlor ceiling & we decided
to patch it with fresco work — Back to
studio at 3 & did more mounting work.
Went to Champney's with a lot of
Charl's small instantaneous photo
work — Home at 6, by 5th Ave., stopping
at [illegible] Douglas's, asking him to call
to-morrow to see about some painting
that we wish to have done —
Evef Will Kimball called and
spent the evef. To bed at about
11 o'clk.
