## Left Page

### May, Saturday 3, 1884.

Beautiful day, but my head very uncomfortable — To studio at 9.30 — To
Bank at 10 — then to Mr. [illegible]'s — then
home at 10.40 — with Annie rode to
City Hall — Comptroller's office — got Diet
Kitchen money — then we rode across the
bridge & back — A. went home & I went
to Mivan Co. & paid $4.10 — for our tickets
there to Seveon Baché's in Greenwich S. — t
ordered glass for Montrose Studio Bay
window, then home at 1.30 to dinner —
After that, gave up to my headache for
awhile — Dr. Y — came — I got to studio
before 5 & signed a number of mounted
p[illegible] — home at 6.30 — dressed & with A.
at 7.20 to Ed. Cook's to dinner — Mr. &
Mrs. Brownell, W. & Mrs. Ales. Oakey & Dr. Y —
& Mrs. Sprague — left then with Dr. Y —
soon after 10 — Took a. home, then we
went to Century — there until a little
after 11 — home before 12 & right to
bed.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 4, 1884.

Pleasant mfg. — but soon clouded over.
Light rain by 11 o'clk — when Annie & I
went to N.A. to take a quiet look at
the pictures — Ferguson there painting —
at 1 — to Century — Alden Weir there —
introduced his wife to us — Home to
dinner at 2 — Walter came in after
3 — then Miss Gibbons — then Geo. C. —
& Valentine — Tea at 7 — Eve[ning]
wrote letter home telling Father
he could use Montrose house next
summer — Took letter to G.C.D. —
soon after 10 — then home & to bed
before 11.30
