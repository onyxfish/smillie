## Left Page

### December, Monday 29, 1884

Overcast, mild & nasty — clearing at night.
To studio before 10 & to work on W.C.
"In an Orchard" — Home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2.30 & worked until 4, when
I could see no longer. Then made
paste & prepared mounting board
for my drawing & put it in press.
At 5 went to Newman's about books.
Then to Grady's & home at 6 & had
dinner — Evef. in parlor, Annie
reading aloud from Julian Hawthorne
N.H. & his wife — Then we amused our-
selves for a little while with Barique[?].
To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 30, 1884

Nasty day — very mild, muddy
& overcast. Got to studio about
10 & fussed for a couple of hours
over "In an Orchard" on a couple
of little, unimportant bits. Then
mounted it on a strong backer.
Home to lunch at 1 & back at
2.30 — Fussed over several
things — among others, took out
Roof & Tower of Erretat Church
study & corrected the drawing
of it — a very nice job to do and
successfully done. After 5 went
to Grady's & ordered 2 W.C. frames
& 3 etching frames — Home to dinner
at 6.30 — Evef. at about 8, went
with Annie to Aunt Anna's in E.
17th St — Kitty & Emma were out, but
we spent the evef. with Aunt A.
& Mary — Home a few minutes after
10 & found Will Cook there — come
to stay over night. We got
to bed about 11.
