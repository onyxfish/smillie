## Left Page

### April, Friday 25, 1884

Pleasant mrg — then overcast — showery — & at night, Rain — To studio
before 10 — worked for awhile on 6×[illegible]
& then on "A fallow field", putting in
some sheep — also fussed over some
etchings getting them ready for
Grady to mount — Sent my Yosemite
Fall down to Pate & the Falcis it at
$95.00 Home to dinner at 1 — After
it Kate called, then Mr. Coleman, then
Fanny K. — so that it was 3.30 before
I got to studio — Mounted Geo's W.C.
sketch that he is going to give to [illegible]
Irving — & other chores — Home at
7.15 — tea over at 8 — wrote a letter
to Cotrell about his occupying Morton
houses — & a note to Kate. To bed
before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 26, 1884

Cloudy day — my head cloudy
too — Rain in the mrg — Clear at
night — Went to McKeever's shop
about mounting etchings — then to
Hunti[illegible] about glasses — then to
Knordles & got mounting board & to
studio shortly after 11 — Mounted
amount on Geo's drawing for H[illegible]
Irving — then home to dinner at 1 —
At 2, with Annie, went to Wagner
Matinee at Met. Opera Ho. Seats
very poor — went all over the house
& was disappointed in musical
effects generally — the orchestra
was not so rich, altho much larger,
as at Academy of Music — Home at
4.30 — head hurt — so I went to
bed & had a nap — Tea at 7 —
Eveng at home — To bed at
11.30.
