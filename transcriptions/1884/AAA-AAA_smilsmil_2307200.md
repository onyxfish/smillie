## Left Page

### September, Sunday 14, 1884

mild, pleasant day, altho' the sun did not shine — Got up with a stupid headache — [illegible] about 9 — about 11 Annie & I went out to Hyde Park & inspected the Albert Memorial — then through Kensington Gardens into Hyde Park & along the banks of the Serpentine and home soon after — then had dinner — salmon & venison — sounds well — but I did not enjoy it — after it went to bed & stayed there all the afternoon — Up & had some tea — early — wrote letter to father — then on sofa until 12.30 — then to bed & had a bad night owing to the setting in of a regular headache.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 15, 1884

was not able to get out of bed until about 3.30 p.m. — then had a cup of tea & bit of bread. Between 5 & 6 went out & had a short walk on Piccadilly — tea before 7 — at 8 went to Charing Cross Hotel & found that [illegible] had gone to either lodgings leaving no address — on my return home found a note from him saying that he had gone to Burns — 11 Queens Square — Bloomsbury — wrote him a note & posted it — then wrote a letter to mother concerning Jim Jr. Got to bed about 11 — early, quite warm.
