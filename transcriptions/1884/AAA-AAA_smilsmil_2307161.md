## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 24, 1884.

Another charming day — warmer —
At 10 I went to Drexel's & drew some money
Annie met me there with Bella & Boy — we
went to Mag.ᵉ du Louvre & bought, hat, dress
& shoes for the boy — then sent him home while
A & I continued shopping. She bought a blk.
silk dress & then we got some curtains —
At 12.30 we went to Palais Royal & had
dejouner. At 1.30 to hotel — after a rest
I went to the Louvre where I met Jo.
& Nellie & then Wm Sartain — we went
through the marine museum & at
last found two galleries with comparatively
modern French pictures — Particularly in-
terested in Regnault's Jew. Prim & Delacroix
Davie — out at 5. Ret? to hotel & had a
few minutes rest before dinner —
Evelf being very tired. I did nothing
and got to bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 25, 1884.

Pleasant day — getting to be quite warm —
After many delays, at about 10, Annie & I
went to Drexel's & got more money — then to the
Opera house & got tickets for Friday evelf —
then to Mag.ᵉ du Louvre where we met Jo.
& Nellie — there the nulf was wasted, so far
as I was concerned, the time being given
to the dressmaking dept. — a few minutes
was spent looking at more hangings and
two more curtains like those of yesterday
were bought — To hotel for lunch — then took
a nap & at about 3 we all again started
for the Trocadero — went to Folp of Tower, then
to 5 Rue de Bern — Detaille's & de Neuville's
panorama of the Battle of Champigny, where
we spent half an hour — then at 5.30 back
to hotel — I went around to W. Cram's
& saw Lippencott for a few minutes —
then to hotel to dinner — Evelf in
our rooms quietly. To bed at 10.
