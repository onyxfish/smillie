## Left Page

### April, Sunday 27. 1884.

Brilliant, beautiful day — wind East — After help: t domestic duties, wrote one or two letters. About 11.30 went up to Central Park — beautiful effects of color — songs of birds, etc. — There until 1 — home at 1.30 & took #5, with a — went to Mother Cook's & had dinner. There until after 4 — then home — Geo came & made a call — wrote note to W. Com. Bassett & letter home — Tea at 7 — Then went to G.C.D. & mailed letters — then to 26's & 44 [illegible]. Ave. & called on Louis Tiffany — Mrs. Sam. Coleman then — Harry Gray came in — Home at 9.45 — to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 28. 1884.

Bright & beautiful day — To studio before 10 — helped Geo get his W.C. Sketche off for Irving — then to work on "S elp" afternoon — wrote "cows" — repainting generally — To bank & deposited Patri's check — then home to dinner at[illegible] — back at 3 — Painted until 5 — then cleaned up & went to McKeeve's to see about etchings that are being mounted — then to Grady's & home at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Eveg, Read "Queena" articles — to bed about 10.30.
