## Left Page

### November, Thursday 13, 1884

*Montrose, Montrose House, Room 37*

Up before 7 — Bkfs. at 7.30 — left home at 8.15 — Train at 9 — Glorious day — delicious Indian Summer effects — Had the parlor car, "Scranton" pretty much all to myself. A good dinner at Scranton at 2. Got to M. Station at 3.25 & to town at 5 after a pretty cold ride, as it had clouded over & was windy — Went at once to see Cotrell — then to J. S. Tarbell's — then to hotel & had supper — after that met Courtright & went to Skating Rink with him — there for a few minutes — then we went to his house & spent the evening there — Near 10 when I got to Hotel — wrote a letter to Annie & took it to P.O. — To bed about 11.

Saw Agur Hathrob after tea & had a talk with him about Cooper lot.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 14, 1884

Brilliant day — up at 9 after somewhat disturbed night — Bkfs. before 8 — about 9 went up to house with Cotrell — met Tarbell there — later Herrick — went over the house and grounds — then back to town & did some hurrying about until noon when I went to Jno. Courtright's & had a very good & acceptable dinner — After we went to the Court House where I saw Wm. Jessup who told me that he would bid on lot up to $600 for the estate — I got the lot at $605. Out at 2 — Teleg. to Annie — went up on the hill & spent an hour or more going over the house & grounds — then a few messages & at 5 started for Deposit, young Mitchell driving — got there at 6.15 — went to a little hotel & by 7 had supper — then to a room & went to bed until 11. — Rested if I did not sleep — the hotel near then wanted to go to bed, so I went up to the depot & sat there until 12 o'clk. when the train came along — my berth was ready for me & I turned right in & had some sleep.
