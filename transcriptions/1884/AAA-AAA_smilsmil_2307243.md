## Left Page

### December, Thursday 11, 1884

a little rain in the morning
then clearing — late at night
bad weather again. Got to studio
at 10 & to work painting in the
distance on big picture — much
interrupted by Bolang who came
& put up the window curtains
that I brought from Paris. Home
at 1 — back at 2.30 — managed to
get in part of distance without
the freshness that I had hoped
for — Kate lunched with us
but we dined alone. About
9 o'clk. I went to Union League
Club Monthly meeting — Water-
Color Show — fairey good. met
a number of friends — Cyrus
Butler detailed his grand
Southern R.R. scheme & enter-
tained Ge° & me at supper —
It was after 11 when I got
home & nearly 12 when I got
to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 12, 1884

Nasty day — Rain & sleet — got
to studio at 10 & in spite of the
darkness painted in the cliffs
& beach of the distance of big
picture — Home at 1 — at 2, with
Annie to Phil. Rehearsal which
was very enjoyable — Kate in
Charl's seat — after, with A. —
went to Tiffany's & Gorham Co.
& looked at spoons — To studio
at 5 — cleaned up — Mr. Champney
paid me $14.50 for etching kfs.
sold at a fair — Home to dinner
at 6 — Kate there. Charl came
in later in the evng & took
K. to the Hines Helwood's in 37th St.
at about 9.30 — To bed before 11.
