## Left Page

### April, Monday 7. 1884.

Lovely day. To studio before 10 o'clk. & commenced painting on 12 × 22 "The edge of the wood" I painted in & scraped off one or two skies — then, abt. home to dinner — Durfaee called on Annie soon after 2 — She still improves rapidly, altho' I am sure that the question "to make or not" disturbs her — To studio at 3 & painted until 6 — scraping my foregrounds off a number of times — did not get the canvas all covered — Selstedt of Buffalo called — home at 7 — tea soon after — Even'g lounging — out for a short walk at about 10. Met F.S. Church & walked from 42° 634' S. with him — home & to bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 8. 1884.

Beautiful spring day until the afternoon, when the east wind brought a heavy, steady rain — Got to studio at 10 & to work on 12 × 22. Shruinger & [illegible] called & I did but little work — Home at 1 — dinner — back to studio at 3 — worked until after 5 getting my "first painting" all in — Home at 6. Mother & Kate there. Geo. & Charl came in after tea — carriage came at 9 and Charl took Mother & Kate up to Junie's. I got to bed about 10.30.

In the mtg. the cook informed me that she was so sick that she must leave at once — So I went to Mother Oost's after help & got her to take the matter in hand.
