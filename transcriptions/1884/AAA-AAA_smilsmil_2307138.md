## Left Page

### May, Friday 9, 1884

Pleasant day — feeling very much like a boiled owl — Spent most of the mng at 11 a.m. D- with Father a short time at studio — then home to dinner at 1 — after that, went to bed for awhile to recover some of my lost sleep — back to studio at about 4 — Engaged there until near 7 — During the afternoon, in Geo's studio, Char[lie] photo'd in a group, Father, Will, Minnie & her boy —
When I got home found a ticket from Charlie for Wagner Concert at Met. Opera house — tea & immediately after, went there & met [illegible]. Between 10 & 11, heavy showers — out at 11 & right home & to bed —
Father stayed with Geo overnight.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 10, 1884

Still cloudy weather — To studio at 10 & commenced work on drawing of a locomotive for a B.N. Co — At it all the mng — home at 1 — back at 3.30 & worked until 5 — At 3, went with Father, who had dined with us, to G.C.D — where he & Geo. took the 3.30 train to Po'k — at 5 I went to McKeeve's & then to Grady's — then up to LaRoche's — then to Dickee's & got riding suit & home at about 6.45 — Tea soon after then dressed — at 8.30 with Annie, her Mother & Geo. C — went to Reception at Judge Mitchell's Co — W.9 4 S — there until 11 then home & right to bed.
