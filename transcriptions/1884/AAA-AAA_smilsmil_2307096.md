## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 13, 1884

Fog & Rain again — very mild —
To studio soon after 10 — Sketching
mtf. Re entering portions of New
Yard Etching — No. 30 — in preference
to rebiting — & also adding more
work preparatory to biting. Home
to dinner at 1 — back at 2 —
Finished work on No. 30 & then
for some time fussed over the
mounting of some Etching pts.
— then a rest — then a walk
about Madison Square in the
fog — home before 7 — tea —
Evenly., Annie again read
aloud from The Newcomes —
To bed at usual time.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 14, 1884

More fog in the mtf — some heavy
rain — then west wind with promise
of clearing. Got to studio before 10
& to work on two old pencil studies
that I think I can make good
subjects for Etching — Home at 1 —
back before 3 & worked until 5 —
Then to Sup. Office with [illegible] for
Chat [illegible] Hart & [illegible] a proof of
Street in Port Arabia — Then to cor.
8th St & 5th Ave. & got Acid & bottles —
then to studio with them — Then
to Dist. Mess. office — 5th Ave. near
37th St — paid bill & scolded —
Home a little before 7 — tea —
Evelf wrote to Mr. Hart & to
Cabrée. To bed at 1.
