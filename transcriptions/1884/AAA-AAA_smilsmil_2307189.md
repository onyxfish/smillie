## Left Page

### August, Thursday 21, 1884.

Another delicious day — m'f., worked on
study from road, part way up to farm on
top of cliff d'Amont — The light & hazy
atmosphere was very favorable, & I made
progress that gave me some satisfaction,
altho' I could not get over all that I
wished to — Home at 1 & had lunch —
then Annie read aloud to me & I fell
asleep — Soon after 3 we went together
to the d'Amont farm & found the yard
full of sketchers & their umbrellas — I
found one group of apple trees disengaged
& made charcoal sketch of it —
At 5.30 we ret'd by way of the cliff —
Dinner soon after 6 — then some messages
& home — Even'g — Reading Belgian Guide
Book & newspaper — then washed brushes
& to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 22, 1884.

Another beautiful day, but quite breezy.
morning, worked on same study as yesterday
m'f., until 1.30 getting very
nearly over the entire surface — then
home & had lunch before 2 o'clk.
then had a nap — After that, at
about 4, Annie & I went out on the
cliffs south of town & wandered as
far as the remains of an old fort —
I took a sketch of the Mannupore
from the south side. Home at 6 &
as I did not feel comfortable in
the stomach region I did not go to the
hotel to dinner with Annie — I prepared
the boys supper, then my own —
Even'g. Reading — etc — To bed before
11.
