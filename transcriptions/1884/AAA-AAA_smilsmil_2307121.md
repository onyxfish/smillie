## Left Page

### April, Saturday 5, 1884.

Still unpleasant, altho' not quite so windy & dirty — To studio at 10, feeling out of sorts — so did not begin painting on 12×22 — but worked over an old A.B.N.Co. drawing, made by a young man of "Electricity" — made a new drawing of it — Home at 1 — back at 3 finished Electricity & monuted a few more etchings & rubbed in a glaze of transparent color on 12×22 — Near 7 when I got home — tea — after 8, went into N.A. Reception for a few minutes then on to Century — Rob. Gordon, Yale & others urged me to go to France with Geo this summer. Home at midnight & to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 6, 1884.

Charming day — B'kf't at 9 — Domestic duties kept me in until 12 — then to N.A. & to Century where I met Geo. & Nellie — then walked up home & had dinner at 2 — afternoon went as the m'f did — wrote some letters, tea after 7 — went to G.C. depot with letter home after 10 o'clk, then to bed.
