## Left Page

### December, Saturday 27, 1884

Dull day — snow fell during night. Late as usual & after 10 when I got to studio — To work on 9×14 W.C. "In an Orchard — Normandy" & rubbed or washed in first color. Home at 1 — Emma J. there & lunched with us. Back to studio at 2.30 & worked as long as I could see — M. Farish came in just at dusk — he does not yet give up his desire to take lessons of me — Charl also came in — After 5, walked with Geo down to Broadway & 10th St., then up home & had dinner at 6.30 — Evelf., a fire in parlor & Annie Read aloud to me from Julian Hawthorne's biography of his Parents. It was after 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 28, 1884

A wretched day — Cold & sleety in [illegible] the m'g — mieder, wet & foggy at night — B&p. about 9 o'clk — m'g spent reading & fussing — did not go out of doors — Dinner at 2 — In the afternoon wrote my letter to Father & played with the boy. At 6.30 went to G.C.D. with letter and then took a short walk. In to tea at 7.15 — near 9 o'clk. before the boy was got to sleep — then wrote a letter to J.D. Payne of Monroe. To bed before 11.
