## Left Page

### May, Saturday 31. 1884.

Cheery, raw & overcast — after bkfst —
& the morning duties I got on the top
of an omnibus & rode down to 22 Old Broad.
J. S. Morgan & Co. Bankers — & got some money —
then I walked back, stopping at the Bank
of England, the Exchange — St. Pauls — the Temple
courts — & then out on the embankment to
beyond Somerset Ho, where I ret'd to the Strand
again took 'bus & back to Hotel at 1.30 when
Annie & I had dinner — After that A.
Bellie & the boy went off for a ride in
a cab & to try to find Dr. Certshire whom
Dr. Yale recommended — To consult him
about J's [illegible] who continues much out of
sorts — I went to the Royal Academy
& worked faithfully until 6.30 when
I ret'd to hotel — Tea after 7 — Annie
did not find Dr. C— in — will have to
go again — got rid of the evening
& to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 1. 1884.

A beautiful day — Up late & had
bkfst between 9 & 10 — Having a number of
things to attend to it was after 11 before A.
& I were ready to saunter down to West-
minster Abbey where we got in in time to
hear the close of the m'g. Service. The effect
of the music & the place was to me exceedingly
solemn in spite of the shocking bad colored glass
in some of the windows — After service we took
a hansom and drove along the embankment
& back, around St. James' Park, to Hyde Park,
on up Piccadilly to our Hotel. & had dinner
after 2 o'clk. The afternoon was spent
writing letters — Tea at 7 — evening
more writing & to bed at 10.30 — The
boy has been so sick to day as to
make us very uneasy —
