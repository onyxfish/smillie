## Left Page

### November, Friday 21, 1884

Very pleasant day — Worked for a little while on Lake Geo. study — then on an old sketch, motif for "Cedar Meadow" painting — Home at 1 — Mr. Hervey there — lunched with us — back at 2.15 — finished Cedar Meadow sketch & at 4 went to Decorative Art Soc'y Rooms in 2.12 8[?] to meet Annie who was made late by the tumbling of her house cleaning woman from the step ladder — Then we went to Johnston & Faulkner's to look at some stuff for covering chairs — did not find what we wanted — then to Newman's & got some wooden knobs for bed room doors — Home at 6 & had dinner. Eve'g. Took Annie to her mothers — I returned & wrote letter to Cottrell. The boy woke at 8 & was sleepless & cross until nearly 11 — also I went for A. 11.30 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 22, 1884

Charming day — To studio before 10 & to work on an old sketch. 11×14, painted some years ago as a motif for my 33×44 "Adirondacks" — Repainted it hoping to make something saleable for Am. Art Ass'n Sketch Exhib. At 12.15 went to 7th St. Johnston & Faulkner's — to get something to cover bed room chairs — Home at 1.5 — lunch — back at 3, having to stop for many messages — worked as long as I could see — then a short rest. The plasterers are making a horrid mess throughout the building, the hallways being almost impassable — went to Grady's to enquire about my W. C. old Ice Houses. Home & dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g. Reading & writing. To bed at 11.
