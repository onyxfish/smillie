## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 7. 1884.

A worse day than yesterday — cold, Raw — wet & windy —
got to studio at 10.30 — Had to build a fire in stove —
then putting away studies etchings — paper etc — etc
until 1 — then home to dinner —
after that wrote some notes
& at 3 o'clk — lay down and
had a good sleep for near
two hours — then more writing.
Dressed & at 7 o'clk went to
Mottier Coale's who gave us
a dinner to celebrate our third
wedding anniversary — NW. Spray,
Miss Williams — Mr. & Mrs. Ed [illegible] &
Mr. & Mrs. Walter Coale — Geo. & Valentin[e] —
Home at 10.45 & to bed —

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 8. 1884.

Still wretched wet weather.
After bkf. went over to Grady's
— then to Newman's & at 10.30 to
studio — Spent some time writing
letters — Father came in about
noon — Home to dinner with
me at 1.30 — back soon after
3 — more putting away of things
until 5 — then to Besbee's —
Scribner's — Donnelly's — Tibbals
— the Tailors & at 6.30, home —
Tea at 7 — about 8 Father &
Geo. came in — soon after 9
Geo. & I went to Union League
Club & saw collection of etchings & engravings — Had a
pleasant chat with W. Lawrence
Sec'y of Art Com — home at 10.30
& to bed — up with Jas. Jun.
from 2 until near 5 — Teeth & vaccination.

Father stayed with us overnight.
