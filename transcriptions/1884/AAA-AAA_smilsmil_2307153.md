## Left Page

### June, Sunday 8. 1884.

Still showery weather — late in
getting up — had our coffee at about
9 — it was near 11 before Geo. & I were
ready to start out — we went to the
Madelaine & heard some music, then
across the Place de la Concorde — & back to
hotel at 12 — Bkfs. at 12.30 — after
that I had quite a nap — about 4
we all went out hoping to get voitures
to ride on the Champs Elysées — but
the Grand Prix Race made it im-
possible to get more than one, in
which Jes. & Nellie went while A & I
went to hotel — a smart shower fell,
after which we walked to the Champs
E— & saw the returning crowds — back
to our dinner at 6.30 — Rvell., writing
letter home — did not get to bed
until near 11. Fortunately the
boy slept like a top all night
so that we were not disturbed.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 9. 1884.

Still cold & threatening — Bkfs. about
8.30 — It was after 10 before Geo. & I
went to Drexel, Harjes & Co., 31, Boule-
vard Haussmann — where we met Wm. Lippin-
cott & had quite a visit with him — in
the mean time, Annie & Nellie went
off shopping — G. & I got back to hotel
after 11 & superintended our change
of quarters, both of us going a floor be-
low into larger rooms with saloon attached
— lunch at 12.30 — About 2 I went to the
Salon & went through every gallery of
paintings in a very superficial way,
but it required hard & constant work
until nearly 5.30. There are 2488
paintings & about 1500 pieces of sculpture.
Back to hotel at 6, exceedingly tired —
on sofa for half an hour before dinner —
— After dinner went out with Annie &
did a little shopping — then in and
studied maps of Paris — wrote to Charley
Pratt etc. & to bed soon after 10.
