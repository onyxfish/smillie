## Left Page

### May, Sunday 11, 1884

Beautiful day but quite cool — B[illegible] late — still feeling
quite used up — after 11 went to studio & spent some time
looking over old documents in my tin case — Home before
2 — then dinner — after that Annie, I & the boy, went to
Geo's studios where we met Charl — Home at about 5 —
slight showers — Walter came in — then I took a nap during
which Will & Joe called. — At 6.30, wrote letter to Mother
took it to G.C. Depot — Then Annie & I went to Aunt Annie's
to tea — there until near 10 — then home.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 12, 1884

Charming day — got to studio before 10 & to work on locomotive
drawing — home at 1 — back at 3 — a little more work &
then I mounted it & put it in press — Mr. H. P. Gray came
in — at 5 I went to Pier 36 & on to the Bad City of Berlin
where I saw the Purser Mr. Kinsey — on my way home
stopped at Duncap's — Emma I — to tea — about 8 we were
invaded by a surprise party headed by Mother Cook — there
were about 30 — who had a very pleasant time until
about 10.30 when they left & we got to bed.
