## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 16, 1884

Pain in chest better but nearly cured with pain
in head — inflamed nasal mucus membranes — again
sent for Yale. — In bed most of the day — At
night had a slight chill followed by a mild fever
that was enough to make the night one of misery to me.

By birthday + Water-Color Socy meeting — Sorry that I
could not attend the latter as Hopkinson Smith put through
his plans of opening the exhi-
on Sunday for full fare.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 17, 1884

Very much out of sorts yet.
Started a diet last night
of milk & [illegible] Water exclusive-
ly — At night more chill
