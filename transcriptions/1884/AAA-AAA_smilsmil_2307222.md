## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 28, 1884

Dark wtf. Rain beginning to fall at about 10 — Some time spent looking after the men — carpenters painters & plumbers — nearly 11 when I got to studio — went on with the fixing up of my oil studies of last summer — home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked all the afternoon — stretched some common paper to make charcoal compositions upon — Home at 6 — dinner — weather clearing — Evtf. Read for awhile "Queen" — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 29, 1884

Charming wtf. — Rain in the afternoon & at night — With Annie went to Fakenhauge's & selected papers for bed room & the parlor — got to studio about 11.30 — fussed with last summer's sketches and home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 & again worked over sketches until 6 — then home to dinner — Evtf. Walter & Valentine called — left at 10 — got to bed at about 11.
