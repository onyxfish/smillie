## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 22. 1884.

Convalescent, I hope. — A
very pleasant winter day —
Up soon after 8 & ate V¼p —
by bedroom fires — At 12 went
out & had a short & refresh-
ing walk. Took dinner in
dining room at 1 — Then
sat & read Tourgenef's
story "Smoke" until 3 — then
out again & had quite a
lone walk — Then a nap on
the sofa — Evef. — felt a
slight return of fever — enough
to influence my sleep during
the night unfavorably.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 23. 1884.

Half clear day — not so well.
overdid things yesterday —
lying on lounge most of the a.f.
After 12 rode in car up to 70th
St then back to Dr. Yale's — then
until after 1 — Home to dinner
at 1.45 — Afternoon Mary Willard
& Miss Abbatt called — Annie
went over to Will's — saw only
Minnie's nurse — brought home
disheartening accts — from both
mother & boy. M's right breast
has gathered & the boy cannot
retain anything on his stomach.
Tea at 7 — Evef. — writing &
reading — Alf. came in for
a few minutes — To bed at
[illegible] — again feverish during
the night —
