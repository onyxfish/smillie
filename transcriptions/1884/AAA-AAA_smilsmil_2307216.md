## Left Page

### October, Thursday 16, 1884

Chilly, overcast w'th., growing milder during
the day. Again blp. at Mother C's — on my way
to studio, stopped at Carpenter's, plumber's —
furnace man's — grocer's, butcher's — back —
on my return, went in to Am. Art Galleries &
had a pleasant interview with Messrs Sutton
& Kerby — saw the new galleries — About 11 —
got to studio — fussed — some time taken
up with callers — to 39th S. for lunch at 1.30 —
then home & spent some time with carpen-
ters who are tearing up kitchen floor &
Clark, the plumber, on tank business — got
to studio at 3.20 — again fussing with
my last summer's work — Champney
called — Home at 6.30 — with Annie to her
mother's to dinner — again home at 8
& spent the ev'g — in arranging contents
of closet — bought of O. Neil a pair of
ready made shoes.     To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### October, Friday 17, 1884

Very pleasant day, altho' cloudy — milder
& at night, some rain — Blkp. at Mother C's —
lunch & dinner — 4 men at work in kitchen
laying floor — to studio at about 10 & fussed
about cleaning & clearing out truck — Rec'd
note from Miss Hallowell informing me that
she had sold "Crisp Oct." for $150 — wrote in
reply — lunch at 1.30 — after a call from
Champney & Miss D urfer — Back to studio
at 3 — found men there cleaning my stone —
after they left had floor washed & then
took the books from one of my cases and
dusted them — to dinner at 6.30 — home
at 8 & spent the ev'g — in arranging
small things in my closet —     To bed
at about 10.30
