## Left Page

### February, Monday 25, 1884

Pleasant day — To studio before 10 &
to work on "the Pond's outlet" generally
over middle distance & foreground —
much wondering whether the work
I am doing is fairly good or inexcus-
ably bad. Kelly came with panel
back stretchers & stretched two of my
canvases. Home at 1 to dinner — back
at 3 & worked until 5 — Thum[illegible]
came in for a while — in trouble
because Clark is laid up in his
lodgings with a boil (or carbuncle)
on the back of his neck — Washed
up — lighted up & took a look
at my work by gas light — Home
at 6.30 — tea — Evef. Annie
read aloud from "The Newcomes".
To bed before 11. Rain.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 26, 1884

Overcast & threatening. Light rain
& snow in the afternoon & evening.
To studio before 10 & to work on the
Pond's outlet, repainting the water.
Home to dinner at 1 — back before
3 — worked until 4. Went to
Dickie's at 4.30 & rode from
5 to 5.30 — walked down with
[illegible] Horces — Home soon after 6.
& had a good grooming —
then tea — Evef. Annie
again read aloud — To bed
about 10.30.
