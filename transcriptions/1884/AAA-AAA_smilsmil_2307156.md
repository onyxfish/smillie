## Left Page

### June, Saturday 14. 1884.

Another beautiful do — So used up that I did not attempt to do anything in the mtf. Annie & Nellie went out & bought a shawl for mother & I tried to get a little sleep — another rest after lunch & at 3 I went to the Salon — where I worked faithfully until 6 — met Geo. & Nellie, Eugene Vail, Blashfield, Sprague Pierce and Magrath — Dinner as usual. Eve'g. studying cat. of the Salon. To bed soon after 10 — in a room by myself

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 15. 1884.

Partly overcast — growing much cooler. At 8 went to market for my berries and was too early — After bkfp — we got the boy off for a ride & then A. & I went to the Pettit gallery in the Rue de Seze. & saw the Messonier collection of paintings — The gallery is a very charming one but the collection of paintings (a hundred a more) has no really important pictures by M. I was most interested by two or three of his early pictures — 1852–60 — & by bits of study for the large A. T. Stewart painting. Back to lunch at 12 — Mr. Rumb called — About 3, J. Junt. was sent off to the Champs Elysees & A. & I went to the Parc Monceau — near there saw Bartholdi's statue of Liberty, now temporarily set up — could see it only imperfectly. On our way back, stopped in the church of St. Augustin & saw part of ceremony of confirmation & first communion — quite impressive. back to hotel at 5.30 — dinner — eve'g. reading & writing until 9.30 — To my little bed room
