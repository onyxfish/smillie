## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 15, 1884

Dismal rainy day — did not get up until after 8.30 — very much jagged — Wife at 9.30 — & to studio at 11. Worked on "Rk &white." View of Lake Geo. from top of Rogge's Rock" — an old ill.? made for Harper Bros until 2 o'clk — then home to dinner — after that had an hour of sleep & did not go to studio until 6 — Tea at 7.30 evening loafing — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 16, 1884

Very mild, beautiful day — After 10 when I got to studio — To work on 23×40 "An old wood Road" — making changes in the composition — worked until meant — then home to dinner. About the house until 3.30, then up to La Roche's, the dentist in 54th St., with whom I had an appointment — There only 15 minutes — back to studio at 5 & worked for an hour on 23×40 — Then home — Found men from Fleise & Co. moving Bedstead & Bureau from 2d story front to back parlor — the work of an hour — about 8 when I had tea — Dwelt with Annie — the boy in his new crib & very restless — his throat seeming to trouble him.
