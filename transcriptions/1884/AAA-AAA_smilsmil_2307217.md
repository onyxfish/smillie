## Left Page

### October, Saturday 18. 1884.

Pleasant day — Bkfp. in 39° S. men still
at work on kitchen floor — Studio at 10 +
to work cleaning — stove men came about
11 + until 1.45 they were at work cleaning
the stove + putting up new heke — I got
to lunch at 39° S. at 2 o'clk — back to
Studio at 3.30 + until near 6 working
at book case + contents — then to 39° S.
where we had dinner + home at 8 —
Margaret, our waitress, am- from Ridgefield at about that time. Doing
nothing in particular until 10 o'clk
then to bed —

Geo. + his family went up to Po'k,
to spend Sunday, this afternoon.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 19. 1884.

Delightful day — Bkfp. at 8.30 — After that
walked over to 6° ave. for a paper — then
went in to see Grady — 41° S. + 6° Ave. for
a moment about picture that is to go to
The American Gallery — then home + read
my paper — About 12 went to Studio
for about an hour — then home to dinner
at 2 — It was 4.30 before I started
out for a walk — went up to 80° S. +
5° ave — lovely evely. Rode home —
in at 6 — Tea soon after 7 — Read
awhile — then wrote a note to father
\+ one to Wm. Boyd at Montrose —
To bed before 11.
