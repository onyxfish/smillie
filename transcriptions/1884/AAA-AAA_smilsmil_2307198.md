## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 10, 1884

*46 Clarges St. 2° floor - apartments*
*Mr. Cratts -*

Bkfst about 9 — after 10 I went to 22 Old Bond to see about our trunks & paid 7/8 charges on them — then to Mr. Cracken's — 38 Queen St., where they were stored & paid 6/ more — in all, about $3.40 that might as well as not have been saved — to say nothing of vexation & ill temper — Back to hotel at 11.30 — & soon A. & I went out for a prowl through Lincoln's Inn Fields & the Courts of the Temple — The weather was very sunny & pleasant — found the Courts of Justice closed — changing boilers for heating apparatus — Temple church also closed — we went through to the embankment — back — got the boy's dinner & then about 2 got our own — Packed up & at 3.30 we left the First Avenue Hotel & drove to our new quarters in Clarges St. — very soon got to rights & quite pleased — Tea with chops & potatoes at 7 — The evening passed in attending to various duties — To bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 11, 1884

Beautiful day — up unwillingly at 8 after a restless night — Bkfst very satisfactorily, at 8.30 — At 10 Annie, Bella & the boy off on a shopping expedition & I, to So. Kensington Mᵘˢ where I spent the m'g in a general survey — most of my attention given to the pictures which, to my mind, are a poor lot, all the many of them. For one reason or another are very interesting — Very much interested by the Dyce & Foster collections, especially the autographs & Dickens M.SS. Home at 1.30 — before Annie — out again & did a little shopping for the boy's dinner — Had our own at about 2 — Haddock — heels of beef — Roast chicken — ham — Potatoes — tomatoes & plumb tart — all very nicely served — After dinner proposed to take a steamboat excursion on the Thames but was so extremely tired from want of sleep that at 4 I went to bed & slept until after 6 — Tea about 7 — Letters from home — Some of A's shopping came in — She read aloud a chapter from Miss Mackery's Village on the cliff — Got to bed at about 10.30.
