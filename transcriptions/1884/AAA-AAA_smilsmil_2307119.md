## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 1. 1884.

Mild & quiet day — B&p. soon after 8 — Annie quite bright after her nights rest. I got to studio about 9.30 & got Boston Water Colors & Etchings ready — then spent rest of the m'f signing 105 pf. for Cassell & Co. Home at 1 — after dinner, took a nap & felt better. To studio at 3 & for two hours, at work mounting etchings. At 5 went out to look for furniture especially, a crib — went to two or three places, & saw what pleased me best at Ward's in 20° 8? Home at about 6.30. Tea at 7 — Yace came in & pronounced A's condition very satisfactory. Ev'g — working for Annie on her Diet Kitchen accounts —
To bed at 10.30 —

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 2. 1884.

Horrible day — driving rain all the m'f — & snow in the afternoon. Got to studio after 10 — soon went down town to Continental Ins. Co. to enquire further about canceling Montrose Policy — Then to A.B.N.Co. & saw Will — then to Day 8. & bought some black for printing. Then home at 1 o'clk — dinner — at studio at 3 — wrote to Tyler & Burchard about insurance — & then, until after 6, mounting large etchings. Home at 7 — tea — ev'g — part of it spent with Wm. Bliss Baker who made us a call. To bed at 11.
