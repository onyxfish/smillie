## Left Page

### May, Thursday 15. 1884.

Beautiful day — to studio at 10 — after doing several "chors"
thru to K. + V. with Estis' plates —
thru to Geo. C's — 49 Walker + got
his certified check for $19.00 — thm
down to Charl's, where I met
Geo. C — went with us to Drexel
Morgan & Co's & we got our letters
of Credit, went to Ch. B. N. Co. + [illegible]
will — Home at 1.40 — dinner —
Back to studio before 4 —
wrote letters to Bellows, Tanner,
Coleman & Gifford, enclosing
checks for last des. & Poets & Trehus —
Packed up things — Home at
7 — after going to Substation P.O. —
tea — every — writing notes
& packing. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 16. 1884.

Very bright, pleasant day — about
10 started out on a series of messages
— to Simpson Macauley's — Fingerhut,
Nooney — the Studio — 2°. Nat. Bank —
Dumeats's — Knordler's — Century
Magazine. Nash, Wilson's — Wilmuth's —
Donnelly's, Tibbal's — Renna Bakery —
then to studio again & home to
dinner at 1 o'clk. where I found
sister Mary & Katie. They stayed
to dinner with us. It was near
4 o'clk. when I went up to Yale's
with two [illegible] of etchings for him, then
to Grady's & settled up — then to Jack-
son's Exp. & Pool's Stables — Home at
5.45 — found Dr. Y — there — some packing
before tea at 7.30 — Charl, E. Parker,
Julie Spring & Emma J — called &
at 9.45, Frank Waller & wife — I
finished packing at 10.30 & then
to bed —
