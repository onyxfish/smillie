## Left Page

### January, Friday 4, 1884

Clear, windy & very cold — got to studio at 9.35 — suffering with a headache against which I struggled & worked all day on my drawing "Stray Lambs" —
Home to dinner abt — back at 2.45 & worked until 4.30 when I gave up for an hour & rested in my big chair. Then home — tea about 7 — the rest of the eveng on lounge most of the time, Annie reading aloud some of the time. —
To bed, as usual, about 11.30 having spent an uninteresting hour with James just before turning in.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 5, 1884

Very cold, raw day — overcast in the mng — clear afternoon. Out of sorts — after bkft — went to Gradu's & ordered three frames for my W. C's — got to studio after 11 & to work on "Stray Lambs" — Home abt & back at 2.30 & worked until after 4.30 — About that time, a little excitement caused by fire in Tiffany's establishment next door. Engines on hand promptly — plenty of stir — no damage. — Home at 6 — tea — eveng — quietly on lounge most of the time — a little reading aloud and to bed before 11.
