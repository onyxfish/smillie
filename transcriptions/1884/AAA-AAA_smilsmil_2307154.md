## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 10. 1884.

Showery & Sunny, by turns — Bkft. near
9 — it was after 10 Before Geo & I started
out for a stroll — Annie & Nellie going
off on a shopping spree to the Bon Marché —
we walked through the Tuileries gardens
along the Seine — around the Louvre and
at last back to hotel, being caught in
two quite heavy showers. Luncheon at
12.30 — then a rest until near 3, when
Geo & I went to the Salon & worked in
detail over several galleries untill
5.15 when, feeling pretty well used
up we got to hotel — met Mr. Plumb
& party in the exposition. Dinner at
6.30 — in the eveg. Mr. Wm. Lippincott
called — it was 11 o'clk when I got
to bed — Jat. Junr. behaved badly
through the night in an amiable
way that kept me awake most of
the time.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 11. 1884.

A pleasant day, altho' cool & cloudy
at times almost threatening — got up
with a bad head ache — which modified
during the day — After 10 — Geo & I started
for F. A. Bridgman's — 144 Boul. Malesherbes —
there we were directed to his studio
46 Rue Joufroy which proved to be the
rear of his house — an old stable, charmingly
rebuilt & fitted up with garden between
it & house, were well rec'd by B. & spent
an hour or more with him — then back on
the tops of an omnibus — Prepared a lunch
for myself of Rolls, strawberries & granum.
Then a rest & at 3 o'clk. Annie & I rode
out to the Bois de Boulogne, making the
circuit, sun shining bright & warm — the
air delightful — back at 5 — dinner after
6 — then A. & I went out & did a
little family shopping — in at 9 —
