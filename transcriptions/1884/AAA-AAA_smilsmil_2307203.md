## Left Page

### September, Saturday 20, 1884

Beautiful weather still — A little before
10 o'clk. Annie & I started out for a day of
work. Drove first to the houses of Parliament —
& in a little less than an hour got through —
then across to Westminster Abbey where A. & I
went through without a guide, having written
my name & address in a book — we went
through very deliberately & enjoyed it immensely — out just in time to walk over to the
Westminster Station of the District R.R. & took
12.44 train getting to Richmond at 1.20 — we
took cab to Roebuck Hotel where we sat at a
window with charming outlook over Park & river
& ate our lunch at about 2 — at 3 took cab &
drove through Richmond Park — out at Sheen
gate & to southern gate of Kew Gardens, getting
there a little before 4 — We wandered through
the grounds — over the grass & under the trees — the
afternoon light was superb — went through all
of the plant houses & one of the museums — then out
at Cumberland gate & took 5.44 train in — got to
Victoria Station at 6.15 & home at 6.30 — Tea at
once — note from Seymour Haden making appointment for to-morrow. To bed before 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 21, 1884

overcast m'g. but clearing to be very
fresh & beautiful before noon. Took
a short walk for an airing, then spent
the rest of the m'g writing my letter to
Father. Dinner at 1 — a very good specimen of English sole & a pair of grouse
spoiled to my taste, by cooking with hare
or pork of some kind — they are no better
than our Prairie chickens but much smaller & our dinner cost us $2.75 — At 2 o'clk
went by appointment to Seymour Haden's —
was well rec'd by him & spent 2½ hours
looking over a part of his remarkable
collection of Rembrandt etchings — the
finest I ever saw — & most interesting —
the etchings with a few other art works
in his house are insured for £35 000 —
I saw a number of his P[late]. marked
£250 — £300 each — He gave me a
copy of his monograph on Rembrandt.
Tea at 7 — even'g, some more writing —
To bed after 11 as I got to reading
Haden's book — Showers during
the night. Went to 11 Queen's Sq.
(Bern's) at 8 o'clk. & spent an hour
with Geo. & Nellie.
