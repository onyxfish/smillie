## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 30. 1884.

Dull day, with rain that froze as it
fell before 6 P.M. Got to Studio
before 10 & to work on 10×14 W.C.
Sketch near Montrose — a study
in grey greens — an attempt to get
out of a color rut in which I
have worked for some time. Annie
came about 12.30 — we were detained
by man bringing in coal — so we did
not get home to dinner until 1.30.
It was 3.30 before I got back to
Studio — worked on 10×14 as long as
I could see — Geo. came in from
Polk — all were there. — Before going
home I went to Mother Cook's for a few
minutes — saw Nellie — home at 6.50, ten
before 7 — Eveg. Annie read aloud
until after 9 — To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 31. 1884.

A nasty day — dark & foggy —
Got to Studio at about 10.30 —
To work on old drawing "Crisp
October" & washed in a new
foreground — an undoubted im-
provement upon the old one,
being much simpler in every
way — At noon went to N.A. f.D.
& saw Farner. Thue to 2: Nat. Bt
then to Kneedler's & bought a
little outfit for Katie Adams to
begin oil-painting — Home at
1.40 — dinner — back at 3 — went
to N.A. f.D. & put in wooden
backs to my frames there — then
to Studio & began making Tracing
of charcoal sketch for large W.C.
drawing. Home soon after 6 —
Tea — Eveg. wrote note home
— one to Tom Shearman & one to
Mr. J.E. Morrison of Phila.
