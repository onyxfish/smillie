## Left Page

### February, Saturday 9, 1884

Another day of fog or rain — got to
studio soon after 10 — laid a ground
over all No. 30 — carefully filling in
the lines — then to work on bay-window
plans — drew floor — & wrote letter
to Cottrell, explanatory — varnished
plate & home to dinner at 1 —
back at 3 — got out an old canvas
24×40 — washed it & smeared
it with Frames parent glazes. Then
rubbed opaque color over the
foreground preparatory to a change
of composition & repainting —
then a rest & home at 6.45 —
Tea soon after 7 — Eveg. wrote
at note to Chas. Stef. Hart. &
got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 10, 1884

Some sunshine at last — Pleasant
day — but overcast again at night —
Bkft. before 9 — at about 11.30
went out for a walk — to Central
Park — back about 1.30 & had a
short nap before 2 — Dinner
soon after — Mr. Ordway, a young
man from Boston, recommended
to Annie's tender mercies by her
bro. Will — to dinner — also Emma
J — pleasant — Mr. O. left at about
5 — then A. & J. went to Mother
cook's & at 5.45 A. & J went over
to see Will — Minnie's baby was
shown to us — poor little person —
with a pained, pathetic face —
Home at 7 — Tea 7.30 — Eveg.
A. read aloud a little — J wrote
home letter & took it to depot
before 11 — To bed about 11.30
