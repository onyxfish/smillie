## Left Page

### June, Friday 20, 1884

Pleasant day — cool — overcast in the after
noon with threats of a shower — Bkfst
& morning duties as usual — Annie not
feeling well enough to go out I spent the
mf with her — Lunch at noon — About
2.30 Geo & I went to the Louvre and
went in a general way through
the galleries of Painting & Sculpture
& the museum. We came out at
4.30 very tired & went over to the
gardens of the Palais Royale where
for awhile we listened to the music
of a very fine band — Then to hotel
& had dinner at 6. Evg. quietly
with Annie & got to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 21, 1884

Sunny mf, but before 10, overcast and so
continued all day — To market & Bkfst
as usual — After 10 went to Louvre and
for more than an hour among the
ancient greek Statuary — Then to hotel
to lunch — At 2.30 again went to
Louvre with Geo & Nellie & I spent the
afternoon mainly among the old Italian
paintings, with a little exploration
among Egyptian Antiquities and into
the Marine museum — Back to
hotel at 5.30 — dinner at 6 — Evg.
out for a little while on a few
messages — Then with Annie until
near 10, when, being very tired, I
went to bed.
