## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 24, 1884

Pleasant, altho' most of the time overcast.
After bkfs. - with Annie in cab to her dress-
maker on Regent's St. Then I went by bus
to Paddington Station to make enquiries as
to tickets & luggage to Liverpool via Windsor
Oxford, Leamington & Chester. Satisfactory —
Then to British Museum at 11 & made general
circuit of everything but the Print Rooms —
Home to dinner at 1.45 — after that, much
confusion — Trunks — all sorts of goods — packing
took a rest for an hour — At 3.30 Bella
got off on a trip to the North of Ireland to
see her Mother — Tea at 7 — Eve'g I amt
a note to Abbey & got to bed about 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 25, 1884

Very pleasant day — After bkfs — went to Charing
Cross for bus & found I would have to go to Paddington
Station — then on to Fickling's & tried on my clothes —
To Boulcin's & drew £35 — on new letter of credit —
then by underground R.R. to Paddington Station &
engaged bus for to-morrow mt'g. Then bus to Bloom-
sbury St & called on Dallmeyer about Charle's lens —
then, at 12, to British Museum & spent 1¼ hour in
study of Niello & the sulphur imks. Then home to
dinner at 1.40 — just after that Abbey came
in & spent half an hour or more — invited me
to dine with him that eve'g — I rested for an
hour then to work packing — near 7 — A. came
for me & we went to Hotel Continental — Waterloo
Place. Alma Tadema declined A's invitation
being worried & tired over a painting — but
Comyns Carr came & we three dined — a
swell champagne dinner which, of course,
was wasted upon me — Part of the conversation
was interesting & entertaining & part of it did
not come up to my ideal — we left table at 10
Abbey & Carr to go to Prince's Theatre where C's play of
"Called Back" is having a long run — I to my rooms
where I packed until after 11 — Then to bed —
Annie's temporary nurse came this evening
from Nellie.
