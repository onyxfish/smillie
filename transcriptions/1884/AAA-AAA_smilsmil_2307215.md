## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 14, 1884

Still bright weather & much cooler —
Again to 39° S°. to b&p° at 8.30 — home again
at 9.30 — Nelie came down from Ridgefield.
Old Mr. Gibbons called — I took a lot of
things to studio & stopped at plumbing
builders & range mans — To dinner at
39° S° at 1.30 — before we left, Dr. Yale
came in & went with me to the house —
Plumber came & carpenter about the
kitchen floor — also range man — It
was nearly 5 when I got to studio
with another lot of things, & then found
that my stranger acquaintance, Mr. Den-
man had called on me — To dinner
in 39° S° — home at 8. Even'g putting
things to rights" & to bed at a little
after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 15, 1884

Cloudless & very cool — to Mother Cook's
to b&p° again at 8.30 — then home again —
Clark, the plumber, came & went over the
house with me to see about getting water
into the big tank on the upper floor — also
man from Mulligan's to see about necessity
of taking up kitchen floor & putting concrete
under it — Fanny K. called — It was 12 o'clk
before I got to studio — to Mother C's to lunch
at 1.30 — Mary Sprague C. there — then home
& waited until men came to clean cellar —
to studio about 3 — in George's Room — Miss
Durfee made a long call — went to Grady
& Mr. K's to get track of some of my pictures
that are out — To Mother C's for dinner
at 6.30 — after that, home & spent even'g at
Lounge — The new cook came this m'g.
To bed at 10.30.
