## Left Page

### November, Thursday 27, 1884

*Thanksgiving —*

Dull day with promise of snow — which promise was not kept. Got to
studio before 10 & right to work on the
small sketch for "Cliffs of Normandy" — I
spent most of the m'f'g. Trying to get
what I wanted in the sky — Home to
lunch at 1 — back soon after 2 & spent
most of the p.m. Trying to get what I
wanted in the water — At 4.30 could
see no longer & had not painted in
the foreground — Took a rest — then went
to Murray Hill Hotel — saw Charl — then
home — Champney soon came — then
Charl — & at 6.20 we sat down to a
very nice dinner — Champney & Charl
talked photos until 9 — then we adjourned
to his "dark Room" 41st S. & Park ave — there
until 10.15 — when I left them — very tired
& went home to go to bed, but Jas. Jun.
ordered otherwise & it was after 1 before
at last I got settled to sleep.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 28, 1884

Dull wet & after 11 a persistent rain.
Feeling very stale after last night's
experience — Got to studio soon after
10 — Traced charcoal sketch for W.C.
20×29 " Cliffs near Étretat." Annie
came in about noon after having
bought Irving tickets for next Tues.
night — Home to lunch — heavy
rain & very dark — I gave myself
up to resting & spent most of the
afternoon on the bed. At 5 went
to studio & attended to fire — home
at 6 — Evng arranging files of
Punch & London News — Annie quite
used up — head troubling her — I
got to bed shortly after 11.
