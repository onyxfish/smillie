## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 13, 1884

overcast most of the day & warm — got up feeling very miserable all over — sore head — sore throat — sore back — Helped to get out to my work but at 10 o'clk was obliged to give up & go to bed quite played out — Up at 1 & had some lunch & then spent most of the afternoon on my bed — Up again about 5 — had a little to eat after 6 — [illegible] sat about until 11 then to bed — still feeling very generally used up.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 14, 1884

Beautiful day — after a fairly good night's rest got up feeling better than yesterday — About 10 o'clk. went out with Annie — Took money for our Ball tickets up to Madam Boissaye at the Villa Georgette — then we sauntered to the Chambre Demoiselle at the top of the cliff d'Aval — enjoying very much the glorious air & sunlight & the views over cliff & ocean — back home soon after 12 — lunch at 1.30 — then a rest and at 3.15 we went out to ride — up the Petit Val to Benouvral — thence to Vattetot-sur-mer — thence to les Loges & back home by les Its road, having had a charming ride over a country rich in grain fields now in harvest — Annie went to her dinner at 6 — but I took a light tea at home — About 7.15 A. & I went out for a few minutes to do marketing & the like — Evening loafing quietly — to bed before 11.
