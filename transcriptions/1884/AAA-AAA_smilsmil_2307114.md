## Left Page

### March, Saturday 22. 1884.

Pleasant day — went to Y.M.C.A. & called on Binstols with draft of Gordon letter for Reception —
then called on Ferguson & got to studio after 11 — I commenced washes on So. Am. drawing for A.B.N. Co. Home at 1 — dinner —
Nap, a nap & at 3.30 went up to Mr. H.f. Villards & there met Annie — at the close of a Diet Kitchen meeting — listened to home music in the music hall & then spent more than an hour going over the house from garret to cellar — thoroughly worn out, so that when I got home, I again stretched myself on the bed — Tea at 7 — Eveg. Annie discovered that she had over tired herself & had warnings that in her condition could not be overlooked. Bella, the nurse, took up her quarters with her for the night — I got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 23. 1884.

Pleasant mg. — light rain in the afternoon — heavy showers at night — Bkfst. alone about 9 —
About 10.30 went up to [illegible]'s & saw him — He came to the house about noon — Says Annie must keep absolutely quiet for two or three days — At 1, I went to studio — Sent Continental policy to Typert Birchand, instructing them to cancel it on the 26 inst. Home & dined alone at 2 —
a nap in the afternoon and some reading — Tea soon after 7 — Eveg. wrote home — & a note to Parrish — went to Y.C.D. at 10 in a heavy shower — To bed at 11.
