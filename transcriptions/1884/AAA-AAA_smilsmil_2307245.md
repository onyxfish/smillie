## Left Page

### December, Monday 15, 1884

Rain in the mtg, but cleared pleasantly later — Got to studio at 10 — last heat in the laying in of my big picture — Met F.H. Smith on my way to studio — went up with me & gave half an hour of amiable criticism — I pitched into foreground & worked with an approach to frenzy — Home at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked until after 4 — then cleaned up my laying in complete, & altho' far from what I want, still it is in a condition that encourages me. To Grady's about a frame — then home at 6 & had dinner — At 7.30 went to Charl's room at Murray Hill Hotel & at about 8 went with him to his dark room. Geo. joined us there. We looked over several dozen of his western negatives & then I watched him develop a tray of four small negatives — left at 10.20 — home & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 16, 1884

Brilliant day — Annie went with her mother to Bridgeport by 9 a.m. train & returned at 6 p.m. Got to studio at 9.30 — Painted in a new sky on "Bit on Marblehead Neck" — then to work on "Edge of the Wood — near Montrose" — Ogilvie came in at 12.45 & spent half an hour with me — Home at 1.30 when Father arr'd. He took dinner with me then he went down town & I went to studio where I worked for some time on "Edge of Wood" — About 5 walked down to 14th St. then by way of B'dway up home — then to G.C. Depot to meet Annie but missed her — then home — had tea bfor 7 — We'f gave Father a lesson in dominoes — Geo. came in & spent the ev'g with us — to bed before 11.
