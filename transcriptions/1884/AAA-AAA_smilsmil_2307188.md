## Left Page

### August, Sunday 17, 1884

In bed all day with a beastly headache, that at times made me almost wild — soon after 5 P.M. was able to take a little to eat — got up before 7 — lounged about, most of the time pillowed in big chair until 2 A.M. Quite a heavy shower about 1 A.M. — Thunder storms near us.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 18, 1884

Beautiful day — deliciously clear & fine. Got up feeling rather light about the head. Bkfst about 8.30 — after that, with Geo. to the P.O. & other messages — in soon after 10 & outlined on canvas the crucifix on the cliff looking to N.W. & prepared it for painting — then, soon after 12, to the beach with Annie & sat there sunning myself until 1.15 — then home to lunch — At 3 we all went out to ride — Annie's treat — went to Gonneville & saw Isabelle Ernestine & her museum of paintings, sketches & scraps by artistic & literary celebrities & friends — from there we were to drive over to St. Joauns on the sea, but our driver was so stupidly drunk & sleepy that he could not make the horse go; so we had to go directly home & got in at 5.40 — the afternoon was perfect: if we had only had a good driver, nothing more could have been desired — I went to dinner with Annie at 6.15 — then a few messages & home — early. a little reading & newspaper & to bed about 10.30.
