## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 18. 1884.

Pleasant day, but still quite cool —
to market as usual & get my supply
of straw berries, eggs & cream cheese —
B'kf at 8.30 — After 10 went to the Louvre
& spent the rest there among pictures.
Back to hotel at 12 & had lunch —
then a nap — at 2.30 — Annie went
out with the two nurses — Took tram
to Notre Dame Cathedral — I rode out
to the Trocadero — went to top of tower &
enjoyed a superb view of Paris — got lost
in coming down by stairway but came
out all right at last — went into Ethnologi-
cal museum & studied Aztec inscriptions
wandered about the grounds & the building
& at last took tram car via Haussmann Boul.
& got to hotel in time for dinner —
Evef. wrote a note to Hy. Bacon at Trouhat
to bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 19. 1884.

Pleasant, altho' much of the time overcast
& cool — B'kf as usual. — Soon after 10 [illegible]
& I set out, went through the Parc Monceau
& out to the works of Gaget Gauthier & Co. 25
Rue de Chazelles, where we saw Bartholdi's
Statue of Liberty, set up temporarily — we climbed
through the body to the head & looked out through
the little windows in her diadem — went through
the ateliers & had the processes of the manufacture
of the image explained to us — Back to hotel at
12 — at 1.30 prepared a cup of tea & had a roll —
at 2.30 Annie & I went to the exposition and
were met near 6 taking a review of the [illegible]
To-morrow is the last day & an immense crowd
was there. — To hotel & had dinner at 6 —
Evef. doing nothing in particular
until 10.30 when I went to bed.
