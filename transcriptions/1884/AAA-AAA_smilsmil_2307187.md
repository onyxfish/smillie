## Left Page

### August, Friday 15, 1884

Charming day — Feeling better, at
10 o'clk. I went up to my study
at the gate of the Jaumé and on the
cliff d'Amont — Annie went up
with me. I found it rather cool
sitting at my work. Home at 1 —
but did not get my lunch until
1.45 — Then a short rest & soon
after 3 Annie & I again went
on the cliff. I taking charcoal
& paper with me, but failed to
find just what I expected —
walked out to Bernivilles & made
a slight sketch — Clambered down
the steps at the cliff there — Ex-
ceedingly wild — we got home
at 6. very warm & tired — I took
a bath & changed my under-
clothes — A. went to the hotel to
dinner but I made a meal
for myself at home — Read the
paper for awhile — got to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 16, 1884

Delightful day — m[illegible]. went up to farm
on cliff d'amont & finished my study
in a manner not satisfactory by working
until after 1 — Then home to lunch —
After that, a short rest & then, taking
paper & charcoal I went out on cliff
near signal station & made a char-
coal sketch, enjoying very much the
delights of breeze, temperature & view —
Home at 6 & had a light supper —
Every — after attending to my regular
duties I dressed & at 10, with Annie
Nellie & Ge°. went to a "Grand Bal"
at the Casino for a Charity — Found it
exceedingly crowded & hot, with little
of anything to interest — At 11 Annie & I
had enough fit & came home, leaving
Ge° & Nellie. To bed at once —
