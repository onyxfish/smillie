## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 1. 1884

Miserable dark, raw day —
Up late — got to studio at 10
& to work on Lake Geo. drawing.
Began by washing out part
of yesterdays work. I am
not going to be able to bring
it out at all what I at first
intended — Home to dinner
at 1 — Annie went off with the
young folks to a Matinee at
Havigans & Hart's Theatre —
I ret. to studio at 2.30 & worked
as long as I could see — made
calls on Mr. Champney — Polpe
McKnight — Then, at 6.30, to
Fanny Kimball's — there until
9 — then home to tea. eve'g
Annie read aloud to me
for awhile — to bed before
11.
