## Left Page

### March, Friday 28, 1884

Charming day — after going to studio went to N.A. & to work there on pen-sketches after Ulriche — then made a small one of Jas. Hart & outlined one after J.M. Turner — then, abt, home to dinner, taking Gifford with me. He returned at 2 & I waited with Annie, who is still on her back, for half an hour longer — when I went to N.A. I took colors, etc &, under Gifford's suggestions, I went all over my painting, intensifying it. I worked as long as I could see — & I think, improved it greatly — At 6, to studio — then to up-town Tribune office & got #3 00. Refunded to Diet Kitchen Home at 7 — tea — [illegible], wrote a letter home congratulating Father & mother on their 52d wedding anniversary — at 10 took it to G.C. Delp[illegible] — to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 29, 1884

Still pleasant weather — Got to Studio at about 10 & to work on a sketch after Huntington picture photo — all the rest on that — Dinner as usual abt — back to Studios before 3 & worked on the Turner sketch from photo when I finished that took my sketches over to Kurtz, at N.A. After that to cor. of 10th S. & 4th ave. — then walked to School for Trained nurses opposite Bellevue Hospital — 2.26 S — Saw Miss Perkins — I wanted to engage Miss Wilcox but she was out of town — Miss P. gave me address of Miss Law. After) when I got home — tea every with Annie — to bed before 11.
