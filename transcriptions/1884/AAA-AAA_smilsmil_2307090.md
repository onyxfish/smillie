## Left Page

### February, Friday 1. 1884.

Cold, clear & windy. Got to
studio soon after 10 — to work
on Tracing of subject for large W.C.
Landscape & Sheep — near Montrose.
After a time got about canvas &
commenced charcoaling "The
Pond's outlet, near Montrose" for
Academy picture — Home abt —
back at 3 — Continued work on
charcoal & after consulting with
Geo., set it — Home before 6.
About 8 went to N.A. Private
view — Water-color & Etching
Exh'n. Found all of my works
very well placed & had a
generally agreeable time
until 10.15 — then home &
to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 2. 1884.

Very pleasant with. , clouded p.m.
clear even'g. Went to N.A. to look
after etchings — met friends there
& did not get to studio until near
11 — Rubbed a pastel over canvas
with charcoal drawing on it for
Academy picture & soon after 12
went again to N.A. (Burgio's Day) &
met Annie, mother Cook — Many (Mrs)
misses Williams, Toshay, etc. Home
to dinner at 1.30 — back to studio
at 3.30 & made charcoal sketches
of locomotive right for A.B.M. Co —
Mrs. Janvier called — also Miss Rom.
Home to tea at 6.45 — At 8.30, to
Century — gave Ernest Longfellow
a ticket — Pleasant even'g. Home
at 11.15 — To bed before midnight.
