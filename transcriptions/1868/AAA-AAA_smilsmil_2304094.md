## Left Page

### March Monday, 16 1868.

Paw, miserable day. Mist + drizzle going into Rain about 4 o'clk P.M.
Got to Studio a little before 9. a.m. Kindled fire, then went to Dechamp's + bought some brushes + colors. Ret'd at 10.30 got to work again on Moat Mt. (23d day.) commencing on 3d painting. Worked over the Sky + distant Mt. down to the middle line, painting everything much cooler + broader. At 1 o'clk. had a call from a Miss Van Boskirk, a gentleman + small boy. At 1.30 home to lunch, at 2.30 back again. Rec'd a letter from Chas. H. Moore, Catskill, enclosing $12.50 Water Color Soc'ty dues. Home to tea at 6.30. Even'g spent Reading + writing, wrote to Chas. Moore, studied some French + got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### March Tuesday, 17 1868.

Up at 6.30. Dull, muggy morning — Before going to Studio, outlined on daj. plate, for father, horses + driver to a moving machine. Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. Set palette + commenced my 24th days work on Moat Mt., worked over the middle mt., bringing cloud forms + gray yet more down over it. Home to lunch at one, stopping at Bank + making a deposit of Society (W.C.) money. Back at 2.10 + worked pretty steadily all the p.m. Mrs. J.R. Breevort gave us a call. Rec'd a note from Sallie [illegible] inviting me to take tea with her next Thursday. Home at 6.45 Even'g spent over French Exercises to bed at 10.30.
Middle of the day, very warm + sunny. p.m. cloudy. Even'g, heavy showers.
