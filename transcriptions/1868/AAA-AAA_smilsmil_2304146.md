## Left Page

### June Tuesday, 30 1868.

Weather still fine. Up at 6 —
Got to Ritchie's at 9.15 — at once
at work burnishing — at it
until 1 o'clc — Then to lunch
with Father — Back at 1.40
While away [illegible] taken of sky
The Ribbing of the Ruling shows
badly, the whole thing is out
of keeping & generally it
looks unsound, I am re-
duced to desperation — my
time & work is getting into a
perfect muddle. Nothing goes
right — worked faithfully until
6.15 — Then up to studio — there
until 7.25, then home to tea.
Spent the eve'g Reading &
writing 2 or 3 notes —
Got to bed about 11.

15 days on Ritchie's sky.
Full time to-day.

## Right Page

### July Wednesday, 1 1868.

Morning cloudy — Up at 7.30,
& headachy — Got down to R's
at 9.30 — Set to work at once
burnishing — at 1 went to
lunch with Father — back
at 1.40 & again at work
& did not stop until 6.15
My head has troubled me a
good deal to-day — Told Mr.
R— that I would not hold him
to the $20 — per diem arrange-
ment — I could not conscien-
ciously charge it — Home at
7.15 — stopping at Schmitts
for my water-color tile box —
Eve'g, Mr. Gavit called — &
helped Gio. mount some
sketches & got to bed at
midnight.

16 days on Ritchie's sky.
Full time to-day.
