## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 29, 1868

Up at 6 o'clk. Bkfst at 7.15 went to Hayck's in Hester St., selected Rfps to cover my chairs, then Rode to H.N. R.R. depot. There met Geo + took 10 o'clk train for Po'keepsie, where we arr? at 12.55. Day pleasant, but weather signs bad. Walked to Morgan House. no Room — went to Rutger's + got dinner — perfect swindle — nap after dinner — took 4 o'clk stage to Vassar College — got there at 4.25. Charming Reception — Kate Fessenden + Van Ingen — got keys to picture gallery + amused ourselves there alone for an hour — At 6. M. Vassar arr? grand celebration of his birth day — 76 yrs old. After song of welcome, all went to chapel — Recitations + Singing — At 8.40 that was over + until 9.30, promenading with Kate + suffering introductions — Then the "Collation" — suffocation + plenty to eat — played the gallant to a No. of yng ladies — more promenading + at 11, in a drizzling Rain Rode in to town — got to bed at Rutger House at 12 o'clk. Mf Rec'd letter from Mr. Main.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 30, 1868

Up at 5 o'clk. Mf overcast + threatening — so gave up idea of a plain day + started home in 6.30 train — in N.Y. at 9.20. home at 9.30 got my breakfast + at 10.15 at Studio. Kind[d]ed a fire as it was quite cold + got to work on 20×36 — (8 day) by hard work painting all day + taking lunch at Studio. I got my stunt finished, under the circumstances, I feel rather proud of the achievement. the work I think is up to my average. the weather cleared off cloudless + warm during the P.M. — Ogilvie called at Studio at 5.30. the first time I have met him since his boorish behavior at the N.A. of D. — treated him civilly —
Home at 7. — after tea — some fixing up of accts — some writing — to bed at 9.30
