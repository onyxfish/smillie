## Left Page

### May Thursday, 21 1868.

Wonderful weather. very heavy Rain last night, & this
m'f. Rain & wind until near
11 o'clk. Then cleared ff, cloudy
& windy. Got to Studio at 9 o'clk
& commenced biting in Brazilian
die for A.B.N.Co. after Kuidling
a fire. — Fitz-E. Jones of Cinn.
called at about 11 & stayed
until near 1. Retarding my
operations very much. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15
& worked until 7 o'clk. getting
a very good bite, much to my
Relief — as I had fears concerning
acids, steel, my want of experience
etc, etc. Home to tea at 7.30
Even'g spent. Reading. Chatting
writing at note to Bellows, & c
Got to bed about —

## Right Page

### May Friday, 22 1868.

Clear day, for a wonder — got down
to Ritchie's at 9. 20. a.m. and set
to work at once etching cross line
over a part of big sky, of which I
have about half an acre to do.
Worked until 4.30 p.m. when
as my fingers & wrist were too
tired to continue. I went over
to cars & rode up to H.R.R.R.
dep't where I met Uncle m.
& aunt H. Went with them up to
Manhattanville in 5.30 train.
Met Gel. at Uncle a's — S.C.T. at
the Studio to-day. Spent the
even'g playing billiards with
uncles a. & m. Got to bed
just before 11 —
Charming evening & twilight —

4½ days on Ritchie's sky
Short of time to-day — 1½ hour.
