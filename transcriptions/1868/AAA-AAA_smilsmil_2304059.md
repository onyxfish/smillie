## Left Page

### January, Monday 6, 1868

Another clear, cold winter day —
Did not get to Studio until 9.30. While
I was making the fire. Mr. Dodworth
paid me a visit. Commenced on
my Academy picture by finishing
up with pen & ink the outline sketch
that I took from the top of White Hor[se]
Ridge, looking across to Moat [illegible] M[t].
This subject I intend trying to compose
into shape & desire first to finish
up my literal sketch, that I may be
more familiar with the subject at
my command. Home to lunch at
1 o'clk — back at 2 & spent the rest
of p.m. on pen sketch. At 5 o'clk, went
to Gymnasium & worked until 6 —
After tea, a little Reading, then went
to Mary Butler's & spent a pleasant
even[ing], meeting there Maggie Gunn,
Gilbert Pettit & one of his wife's sisters —
J.P. Williams & Rich.d Dorman called
at the house during the even[ing]
Shortly after 11 o'clk. I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 7, 1868

Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. Worked
all day on pen-sketch, commenced
yesterday. It is a more awkward
subject than I thought it. At
1 went home to lunch — back at 2.30
and until 5 P.m. Continued at
pen sketch. After tea, wrote
a "Regret" to Mr. & Mrs. Phelps — No.
6 E. 17th St for J.P. & self. Horace
Robbins's grand wedding party,
altho' he has been married
about 2 yrs. but they have been
living most of the time in Paris.
Walked down to Studio Bldg
at 7.45 & gave the note to Mulvany
to deliver for me. Then back to
Burling's where the Water Color Soc[iety]
held their monthly meeting. Quite
a lively time. Considerable general
discussion & waking up. Left
at 10 o'clk. & Ran home through
a drizzling Rain.
