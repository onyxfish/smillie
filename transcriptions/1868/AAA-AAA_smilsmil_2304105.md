## Left Page

### April, Tuesday, 7, 1868

All day a down pouring
Rain — and all day I was
in bed with a miserable
headache — Got up at 6 o'clk
p.m. & took some tea —.
Rolled myself in my big
chair until 10 o'clk A.M.
Monthly meeting of Water
Color Society, but no quorum
on acct. of the weather —
Charl. still continues
very much under the weather
— I think that he is getting
worse.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday, 8, 1868

Windy Squally day, with
spittings of Rain and some
wonderfully blue sky — At Studio
at 9 o'clk feeling unfit for work, at
11 went over to N.A. of D. Finding that I
could get in Sul. of Rocky Mts, Rode
down town to Fathers & got a p.f. —
Back at 1 — at 1.30 went to Studio, Mr.
Young of Boston Atheneum called. At
2\. home to lunch — at 3 back to
N.A.of D & spent rest of p.m. getting
P. of Rocky Mts right in + frame &
also the cups & etchings — Home
at 6. At about 8, again went
to N.A.of D. There until 10.15. Many
sky-scrapers were being + con-
siderable Revision of hanging
already done. I was successful
in securing some changes to the
advantage of my constituents.
the outsiders + scallyways in
Art — Got to bed a little after 11 —
Charl in much pain to day
from acute Rheumatism.
