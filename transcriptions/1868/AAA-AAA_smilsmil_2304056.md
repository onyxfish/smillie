## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 1, 1868

Up Rather late - about 8.30 a.m. A dismal sort of day. Raining & blowing quite furiously. Got to Studio about 10 a.m. Could not walk - streets in such a slushy condition. Worked at various odds & ends, finishing "Mt. Kiaarsarge from the Meadows" that I painted over yesterday. finished outlining 10×14 "Words back of McMillan's North Conway." Rode home to dinner at 1, back at 2.30. P.M. painted in a little twilight bit about 3×6 in. Think that it is pretty good for me. About 5 o'clk. got things cleared up & with Joe went over to Miss Gould's No. 5. E. 26 St. The G's live in very fine style - we had a pleasant call of ½ an hour or more. Then I went alone to the Ireland's, & was there more than an hour. Then went to the Kimball's - there until 8.30. By that time it had cleared off overhead charmingly - but it was something fearful under foot. Went to Mr. Marten's. 133 W. 21 St. — there until 9 o'clk - then Rode to 38 St & had a very pleasant call at Munroe's - there until 10.30. On my way home stopped in at Will's - They were going to bed. Home at 10.45. Got something to eat. finished Reading in About "Broken Ear" and got to bed at midnight.
