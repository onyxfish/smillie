## Left Page

### August, Saturday, 15, 1868

an unpromising mf for work, but as the chances seemed about even for Rain or clear, about 9 o'clk went to sketching ground — It soon began to Rain & Rained heavily for an hour or more. I worked through it all, laying in a part of the R.H. foreground. About 1 it cleared off beautifully when I had a chance to finish up my laying in — I worked until 1 o'clk. After dinner had one game of Croquet and then set to work indoors on mf study trying to finish yet more what I had done — Another shower in the P.M. At 5.30 went to where Colman, Tiffany, Lambdin, Wood & Ge. are sketching on Buck Hill — we made quite a procession through town, with all our sketching traps & escorted by ½ a dozen young ladies — on our Return home. Evening, very up Roarious — with dancing and games — much fun caused by Wood's attempt to blow out a candle — he blindfolded and the candle being constantly moved — Rec'd a letter from Claire — got to bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### August, Sunday, 16, 1868

Up late, after a very windy night — Sky overcast with heavy clouds and a very fresh breeze blowing — Mountains, strong blue & purple — went to church in the mf & heard a sermon upon the text — "pressing on to that which is before" — Lunch about 1 — then walked awhile with a lot of the girls — Came back and made a letter to Mary — Dinner & tea about 5 — another walk along the River bank — Returned & wrote a letter to Charl — About 9 had a little sacred music — about 11 o'clk when I got to bed —
