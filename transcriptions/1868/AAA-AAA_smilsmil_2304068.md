## Left Page

### January, Friday, 24, 1868

A delightful day again overhead.
Feel pretty well played out after
yesterdays experience. — Galt to
the Studio about 9.30 & worked on
Academy picture, outlining, until
noon — W. P. W. Dana made us a
pleasant visit — at 1. I went to
Uncle George's — He was not ready for
me — went to Sarmys. — He was not
ready for me — back to Uncle G's
got teeth set in position on platina
plate — Then to Phil. Rehearsal,
stopping at Snedicor's on the way —
After listening to Symphony — went
to Studio & completed the outlining
of "Moat Mt. from White Horse Ledge" —
At 5. went to gymnasium — at
6.20 home to tea — Spent the
evening writing up accts of Water
Color Socty & to delinquent
members — To bed a little
after 10 —

## Right Page

### January, Saturday, 25, 1868

A charming day, clear, breezy & cool,
walking again good. Got to Studio
at 9 & made a second start on
7×9 sketch of my academy picture;
but for some reason, could not get
things to go right — & before noon, again
gave it up — Maggy Quinn, her cousin
& Amelia Butler called between 11 & 12
o'clk — Home to lunch at 1, back at 2.15
— Jonny Bell waiting on my return —
During the P.M. he nearly finished the
best copy he has yet made of one of my
color studies — I have not touched it and
he is very much pleased — I painted in
a sketch on the half of a broken palette —
a view on the Saco at N. Conway. I am
rather pleased with it — partly on accto
of some tones in it & partly because I
produced it so Rapidly — From 5 until
after 6, worked in gymnasium — Went
with Jel went to N.A. of D. by invitation
The artists to meet members of the Press in
the Library — pretty good turn out of artists
but few members of the press — Got there
at 9 & left at 10.30 — Home & to bed at 11.30
