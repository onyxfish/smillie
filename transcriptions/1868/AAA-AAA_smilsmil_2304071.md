## Left Page

### January Thursday, 30 1868.

Bright, sunny day, thawing freely in the sun. Got to Studio at 9 —
Set to work on "Moat Mt." At 1 o'clk
home to lunch & back at 2 —
At 5 o'clk got painted in the
right-hand side, comprising
the large evergreen trees and
Rock behind — While disappoin-
ed in many things I find
much encouragement as I
go on with this painting. At
5.30 went to Gymnasium & worked
for an hour — getting home to tea
at 7 o'clk. Spent the evening
Reading & looking over some
of my English papers. To bed
at 10.30

## Right Page

### January Friday, 31 1868.

Brilliant winter day & quite cold.
At Studio at 9.20 & to work at once
on "Moat Mt." Having lunch at
Studio I worked away until 2.30
getting all painted in but the lower
right-hand corner of Rock — at
2.30 I went to Phil. Rehearsal &
heard Beethoven's Choral (9th) by symphony
with all the choruses. Whether the
chorus was imperfect or no, I must
admit that I do not appreciate the
choral beauties of the 9th. At 5 o'clk
back to Studio & at 5.30 to Gymnasium
worked there 3/4 of an hour. Then home —
At 7.30, back to Studio — Mr Hart,
J. Guy Lamsdin Fredericks, Burling
Perry & Lumley met me there to
organise a Sketch Club. After
talking it over we agreed to meet
once a fortnight. (Friday Ev'g) at
Mr Hart's Room — Broke up at 9.30
at 10 met with Burling to his Room
at 10.45 home — at 11.30 in bed.
