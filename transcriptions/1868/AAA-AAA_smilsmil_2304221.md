## Left Page

### December Friday, 25 1868.

a Raw cold disagreeable Christmas day — got to studio about 9.30 and spent most of the m'f getting my Tracing of Charcoal drawing finished — Commenced to re-trace it upon the drawing paper using Charcoal to paper \+ out-lining it with pen \+ Prout's Brown. Home to dinner at 1.30. eat a good dinner \+ got back to studio at 2.45 — Worked heavily during the P.M. until 4.30 feeling the effects of a heavy cold coming on. Ev'g immediately after tea, got my feet into hot water \+ shortly after, got to bed —
Passed a miserable enough night — 

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 26 1868.

not able to get up in the m'f — In bed all day. Heavy Snow storm — Got up about 5 o'clk — washed dressed \+ took tea like a Christian but carried, a very heavy read Ev'g sat in easy chair until 11 o'clk then to bed — another, miserable night, suffering with my throat —
