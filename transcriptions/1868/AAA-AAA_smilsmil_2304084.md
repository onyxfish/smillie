## Left Page

### February Tuesday, 25, 1868.

another miserable overcast day
snowing + blowing, but by no means
so cold as yesterday. At Studio
at 9.30 - throat done up in a sling.
Set palette + to work at once on
Moat Mt. (13th day) commencing on
the Mt. peak — Home to lunch at
1 - back at 2, + worked until 3.45
— very much puzzled, of course, for
the subject is "ahead of me" — that
is, I have not study or experience en-
ough to paint such a picture. Got
over the Mt. + ledge for my days
work. At 3.45, with Saml. Coleman
went to Mr. Hart's Studio to attend
a meeting of the Council of Water Color
Society. Business concerning our
Botany Exhibition — Back to Studio
at 5.30 - home at 7 - Evening spent
studying French. To bed at
11.

## Right Page

### February Wednesday, 26, 1868.

Dull, overcast + milder. Got to
Studio at 9 o'clk + commenced
work at once on Moat Mt. (14th day)
Painting the middle-distance
mt. as it comes to the foreground.
Home to lunch at 1 - back at 2.15
Painted until 3.30 when Geo.
\+ I went to Brevoort's Room +
took our French lesson. Back
at 4.45 + continued work until
5.30 when I could see no longer.
My days work is satisfactory
to me, on the whole — I am already
very impatient to get at the next,
or third painting over — I am
almost bewildered with what I
see to do, even in what I have pre-
painted over — Did not get home
to tea until 7 o'clk - Evening
spent Reading — to bed at
10.45.
