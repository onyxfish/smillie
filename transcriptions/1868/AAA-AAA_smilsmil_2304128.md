## Left Page

### May Monday, 25 1868.

For a wonder, the sun shone
this m'g, early, but it soon tired
of the effort. Got to Ritchie's at
9 o'clk — & set to work, etching on
sky. The plate is 25×40 in. at
11 o'clk — a shower, & in the p.m.
several more. Worked very
steadily until 6.20 p.m. — then
very tired, started to walk home,
where I arr'd, quite played out
at about 7.30 — took tea at 8 —
& spent the ev'g in my big
chair & chatting with Father.
Got to bed shortly after 10.
Uncle Wm & aunt H. got off for
Montrose to-day.

6½ days on Ritchie's sky.
Full time to-day.

## Right Page

### May Tuesday, 26 1868.

Up at 6 o'clk — Pleasant m'g — Day
cloudy, at times threatening — Got to
Ritchie's 5 m. before 9. Etched
until 4.30 when, thankfully, I
finished, for I was about "used up" —
Came back — neck — shoulders — wrist —
fingers. Varnished plate & got
things ready for biting and at
5.45 started for home. Tea
at 7 & after that amused my-
self cutting the grass in the yard,
making my lame back lamer.
Spent my ev'g pottering with
bench, but being very tired, I
accomplished but little — To bed
at 10.30

7½ days on Ritchie's sky.
Full time to-day.
