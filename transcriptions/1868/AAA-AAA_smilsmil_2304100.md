## Left Page

### March Saturday, 28 1868.

overcast day, with several spittings
sprinklings + showerings of Rain —
Got to studio at 9.20. Mlle d'Asnoy
Ready + took french lesson. at 10.30
back to Studio, set palette + worked a
little on the foliage back + to left of
large evergreen in R.h. cor. of foreground — Moat Mt. + added my flying
eagle. Chas. Burt called before noon.
Addie Dunning, Minna Ireland and
Fanny Timbalt came at 1 tne had
a Royal, annual lunch, Brown
Bread from Dunning's kitchen, Beef
Tongue, Jelly + fixings — the girls
stayed until near 3 o'clk. Misses
Korn + Rankin called later. I
amused myself, Rather than worked.
I call the picture finished, and
tis a great Relief to me. I think
it the best that I have done so far
+ feel that it shows as much advance
as I could Reasonably effect —
Every, Aunt H. at the house. I spent
the even. looking over my English papers.
got to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 29 1868.

Glorious, cloudless day. Mrs went to Rev.
thos. Hastings's + heard him Repeat his
2° sermon on "Self-Examination", first
delivered 2 or 3 Sundays ago. Text
in 139 Psalm — "Search me" — etc —
after Service, with Charl, went up to
Uncle Aaron's to dinner — Spent the
p.m. until 5 o'clk, on the bank by the
River, in the sun + out of the wind.
Home to Tea at 6.15 — all of the
family except Mother down at Mad.
Ave. Bpt. Ch. to hear young Tyng
preach before the Yng Men's Christian
Ass'n. Church jammed full +
running over. Emma Bourne came
from Jersey City to hear him — she stayed
at our house all night. Aunt Mary G.
also came over. I was her escort —
all of us much pleased with Tyng.
Text — "They saw no man save Jesus,
only" — Matt. XVII. 8th V.
Got to bed at 11 o'clk.
