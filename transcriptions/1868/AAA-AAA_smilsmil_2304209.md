## Left Page

### November Monday, 9 1868

Very mild, enervating day —
got to Studio at 9 & after
kindling a small fire got to
work outlining bridge open on
canvas 24×38 in — Louis Alman,
the Photo. & he often called in,
& so did Menger — Home to
lunch at 1, back at 2 and
outlined my charcoal [illegible]
in Apts, Turfs, & Brown — Miss
Haines called, with two
young ladies, wishing me to
take them as pupils, but I
could not consent — Spent
part of even. Reading Darwin
1st book of Geology — and
got to bed before 10 o'clk —
Uncle Willie came about
10 30 P.M.

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 10 1868

Disagreeable day, threatening
rain, but not able to get any thing
very wet before even. — Got to
Studio about 9 o'clk & at once
set to work painting on Drew's
open. By noon had the sky
painted in — home to lunch
at 1 o'clk — back at 2 — worked
as long as I could see — but
could not get groups of trees
finished — afternoon very dark —
Home to tea at 6 — at 8, Jel.
Clark & I went to Colman's
Studio & there met our Camping
"Adirondack Party", with
Misses Anna Tiffany & Howard &
Mestro Cleveland & Oliver Tiffany
& Albert Burling in addition — we
had a jolly time in C's studio
on our own — with Coffee & cakes
& "name games" until 11 o'clk —
then home through a very wet
rain & to bed at midnight.
