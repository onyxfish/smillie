## Left Page

### April Friday, 3 1868.

Glorious day — Got to studio at about 8.30 & set to work at once
(6"day) upon 16×26. By ne o'clk
had finished the first painting
in, working over L. hand water
and all of the foreground —
Crook, Grannis & Haven called —
Took lunch at Studio — Made
arrangements to have studio swept out & at 2.20 went to Phil. Rehearsal
Charmed with the programme.
After Rehearsal took a short walk
with Addie D. Emma J. & Fanny R —
Then to Lawtiers, Snedecor's. The
studio & home at 6.30 — Went to
N.A. of D. at 7.30 & hard at work
hanging until 10 — Walked
home — very tired — Splendid
night & got to bed shortly after
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April Saturday, 4 1868.

Overcast m[orning]. A little work for father
& writing a note to S.C.T. kept me from
the Studio until 9.30. After kindling
a fire went into Brevoort's Room &
took French lesson. At 11 got to work
clearing up Room & by 12.30 had it
in order for Reception — Took
lunch as I could catch it at Studio.
Many calls & pleasant, but
not such a crush as we had
last month. At 6.40 started
for home — tea at once & at 7.15
off for the N.A. of D. Another
evening of trying work — Many
men of many minds and more
pictures of many and perverse
sizes & dispositions it seeming
almost impossible to reduce
them to any order — For home
at 10.15 through a rain & snow
storm. Took a foot bath as hot
as I could stand it & to bed about
midnight — At that time, a full
blown snow-storm under way.
