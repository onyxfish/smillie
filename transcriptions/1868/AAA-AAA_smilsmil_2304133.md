## Left Page

### June, Thursday 4, 1868

Up a little after 6 o'clk. Most glorious mng — the same all
day. Clear, breezy & bracing — at night, moonlight glorious —
Down at the River side until breakfast at 8 — at 8.30 took stage
& got to studio at 10, after stopping at home to brush up a little —
Spent the day working up a large study (pencil) of Hemlocks —
Commenced at Saranac. Aug./66 —
Home to lunch at 1 — at 2.30
went to Lizzie Munroe's Wedding
Reception — at 3. got to studio
worked on drawing until 6 — then
home to tea — Uncle a. there —
Meta Gaird & her father spent
part of eveng with us — Meta is
out of humor with me & complicates
the matter by being, at the same time
evidently out of humor with herself —
After a short walk in the glorious
moonlight — to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### June, Friday 5, 1868

Overcast all day with a sprinkle
at about 4.30 — Got to studio at 845,
worked a while on Hemlock drawing
& then commenced graver work on
a B.N. Co's die, of which I have a 2d
[illegible] — Home to lunch at 1 — on my
way back, went to Pike's Opera house,
25th St & 8 ave — there saw Augero at
work on the drop curtain — went
with him, all over the establishment —
under & over the stage & all
around — back to studio at 3 worked
until after 6 — very generally thin
every thing with graver, adding
much color — at 7.15 home to tea
Spent the eveng studying French —
got to bed a little after 10 o'clk
