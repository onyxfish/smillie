## Left Page

### May Sunday, 17 1868.

Overcast m'g. After a good bath & Rubbing down with horse-
hair mittens & Rough towels. had my bkfst — At 10 o'clk went for Dr
Throop. Mother suddenly ill with some trouble in her stomach — brought
him to the house at 10.30 & then went to Hastings' Ch. Heard an excellent
Sermon on Moral Courage & training —
Text, Ep. III — 16. & Coll. I — 11. —
At 1.30, with Charl, went up to Uncle
Aarons to dinner — Rain came
on before we got there & continued
all p.m. & night, so we stayed.
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May Monday, 18 1868.

Up a little after 6. Still Raining
heavily. From 6.30 until 8 o'clk in
the Billiard Room amusing my-
self & again after bkfst. At 9.30
drove over to 8th Ave. & Uncle A, Charl
& I came together to the City — got to
Studio about 11 — Found young Bell
there taking a drawing lesson.
Got to work on my Brazil etching
for A.B.N.Co — Home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2.15 — weather changed &
clearing up in showers — Was
not able to finish my etching
as I had hoped to.
Home to tea at 7 — eveng.
at home Reading & writing.
To bed at 10.30
