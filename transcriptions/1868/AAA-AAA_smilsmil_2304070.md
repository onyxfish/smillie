## Left Page

### January Tuesday, 28 1868

Snowing nearly all day — Got to
Studio at 9. & to work at once
on "Moat Mt." — worked all day
like a good fellow — going home
to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30.
and got painted in all the
remainder of mountain and
middle distance. It is very
much "cut up" in spite of me &
rather "coppery" in tone. These
defects I think I can overcome.
Mr. Seely called in the morning —
At 5.15 went to gymnasium &
worked for an hour. then home
to tea & at 8.30 Ge. & I made our
party call on Mrs. Phelps. 6 E.17 St.
Had a very chatty, pleasant visit
— Left at 9.30 & on our way home stopped
at N.a. & D. There until 10 — then
home & to bed about 11

## Right Page

### January Wednesday, 29 1868

Snowing all the mrg., sleety Rain all
the afternoon & snow again in the
evening. Got to Studio at 9.20 & to
work upon Moat Mt. Rec'd a note
from Mrs. Eliz. Murray — Boston, enclosing
her dues to Water Color Society, & another from Haseltine enclosing
check for $75.00, for the five sketches
that I sent him a month ago. Home
to lunch at 1 — wrote Regret to Miss
Bliven for this eveg & notes to Mrs.
Murray & Haseltine. Back to Studio
at 2.45 & painted as long as I could
see. getting in middle ground Rocks
& considerable foliage. Uncle George
called to try my teeth — also J. W. Patti-
son. also a poor Artistico-literary beggar.
went to gymnasium at 5.30 & worked
for an hour — then home to tea. At 8.
went with "Regret" to Bliven's 146 W.34 St.
On my way home bought some horse hair gloves
for Bath Room purposes. Got to bed
at 10.15 after a sponge bath & rubdown
with my hair gloves.
