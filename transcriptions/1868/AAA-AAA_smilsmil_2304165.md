## Left Page

### August, Sunday, 9, 1868.

Cool, breezy day. Up at 6 — took a
bottle of Empire water & then to bed
again until 8 o'clk. Up. dressed &
went to bkfst. Symptoms in my
bowel trouble very much improved
but suffering from a headache, so
that I lay on the bed from 10 until 3
when I went to the seat that I
fixed yesterday on the River bank
& enjoyed for an hour or more the
air, the color & especially cloud
effects & color over smooth shallow
water. In the parlor with Misses
Tweedy & Mrs. Tiffany until dinner
time at 5.30 Eveff., wrote to
Father — from 9 until 10.30 again
in parlor — After dinner walked
out to meadow back of the house to see
the sun set — glorious evening
Cool & Crisp like October —
got to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### August, Monday, 10, 1868.

Overcast and quite breezy, worked
all the mff., getting stretcher
18×24 in. made, stretching the
canvas & making a camera-lucida
pencil outline of the subject that
I have selected for my new study.
It is on the River right back of the
house & for material has a bit of
distant mt. shallow water, perfectly
placid reflecting trees & sky, yet
showing in places the bottom,
overhanging trees, foreground tree
trunks, driftwood, grass & tangle.
Enough to keep me hard at work
for the rest of the summer —
P.m. outlined the subject with
[illegible] Turps. & Brown — Finished
letter to Father & got it off — Rec'd one
from him, telling us that Charrl is
again very sick — Eveff. felt very
tired & out of sorts — I am sorer and
more crippled than yesterday —
Spent an hour or so, with the ladies
in the parlor & got to bed after 11.
