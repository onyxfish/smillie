## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 10 1868.

Up a little before 7 — Another warm, wilting spring day — got to
studio before 9 o'clk — & set to work
upon Moat Mt. (20th day) Painted
over the bushes & foliage in the
centre of foreground — made but
little progress, as I was much
interrupted one way & another,
besides feeling very starchless.
Went to N.A. of D. at 12.30. to see
Ordway, from there walked home
to lunch — back at 2 — Tom
Fessenden called about 4.30 o'clk.
Home to tea at 7 — Evening
spent, newspaper reading, writing
& french — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 11 1868.

Up at 6.30 & had a study at my french
before I went to studio. Got there at
9 o'clk — Mild day, but wind easterly
threatening. Painted on the left hand
foreground Rocks — Moat Mt. (21st day)
Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — back at
2 — Isaac & Kate Riley called at
studio at about 2.30 o'clk — Mlle.
d'Asnoi at 4 o'clk — gave Geo. & me
our french lesson in our own studio
as Prevost was home, sick — Home
to tea at 6.30 — With Father, Mother
Mary, Matt, Kate & Geo. I went to Mr.
Benj. Jessup's in 39th St. Found him
& Nittie at home — back at 9.30, wrote
Water Color Society letters to Moore, Sewtge
& Rawson — To bed at 11 o'clk.
