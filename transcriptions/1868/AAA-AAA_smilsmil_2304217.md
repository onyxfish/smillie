## Left Page

### November Friday, 27 1868

Fine day. Got to Studio at 9.10 Had to kindle a fire which took some time — then our Water tank of 400 galls. burst, taking more time. So that my m't was much broken. Tried unsuccessfully to draw out a figure of a "Gaucho" playing on a guitar, for A.B.N.Co. — at 2.30 went down town to get the printing of our Studio Reception plate stamped — unsuccessful. By much driving to get Phil. Rehearsal just in time. after it, went to Killey's \+ ordered a 24×38 Canvas — then to Studio, tat 6 o'clk — home. Spent the most of the evening directing envelopes for May's wedding cards — got to bed bet. 11 \+ 12 o'clk —

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 28 1868

Fine day, but cloudy, \+ every almost threatening — Headachy — did not get to Studio until 9.30 — then set to work on "Gaucho \+ guitar" for A.B.N.Co. — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Lenny Bell called \+ desires to take lessons in Water Color. I agreed — Worked on drawing until 4 o'clk — getting it nearly finished — then home at 5 — tea at 6 — \+ at Phil. Concert at 6.45 — House crowded — enjoyed very much the Eroica Symphony. Adelaide Phillips pleased me in many Respects, but not altogether. Gad's "Hamlet" was very good. But the Berlio "Symphonie fantasique" I do not think comes up to his mark. Walked home after the concert with Fanny Kimball. — In at 10.45 — Read Pope for an hour, then to bed —
