## Left Page

### May, Friday 29, 1868

Weather as usual — wind E, N, E —
Raw sea mist — every thing & every-
body disagreeable — Got to Studio
at 9, 5 Mr. Lloyd called to see me
about some drawings on wood —
Laid Rebiting ground on die &
Rebit — then worked with graver.
Joining Bell, his mother, sister and
aunt called at Studio at 1 o'clk.
At 1,30 I went home to lunch — back
at 2,30 — worked until near 7 on
my die — then home to Tea.
Evelf; spent hanging one or
two new pictures — pegging at french
etc — etc — Got to bed about
10,30

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 30, 1868

A miserable day, fog
and wet — Got up with
a headache — had my
breakfast, then went to bed
again where I stayed until
11 o'clk at night — then got
up feeling better & sat up
until 1,30 a.m. when I went
to bed again.
