## Left Page

### June, Saturday 6, 1868

Dull mt again — up at 6 and rather headachy — at Studio at
9 o'clk. dry pointed on die-
distance and water — at 10.
took french lesson — at 11.30
back to Studio almost used up
with headache — at 12 went home
& to bed — used up — woke at
3 P.m. much better. went up
to Central Park to meet Uncle A.
by appointment at 4 — failed.
Went to the Mall & heard music
— by this time, weather lovely —
Met Julia Riley & two Misses Arti[?]
after music — Rambled with them
in the Ramble — left them at home
at 8 — I got home & had tea at 8.50
after 9 o'clk. Meta Gavit called —
after 11 I took her home through a
pelting Rain — had a long talk &
something of a walk — I hope that
she understands me better now, as
I feel sure that I do her — home at
12.30 and to bed at 1 —

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 7, 1868

Stiftingly close & muggy day — Rain
at intervals all the a.m. at 10 o'clk
Meta Gavit came to the house — I went
with her to the Madison Ave. Baptist Ch.
where Sister Kate was baptized — She
(Meta) at our house to dinner. at
3 o'clk I took her up to 44th St. and
then walked down to Studio to get
die — there, as I felt much over-
come, I went to sleep, & did not
get home to Tea until 6.30. Went
after a few minutes at J. P. Thompson's,
went in & heard Miss Burns hold
forth as a progressive Spiritualist.
Soon tired of her and went to Osgood's
cor. 34th Park ave. — Enjoyed very
much the Vesper Service — I think
it a very artistic, aesthetical
& moral entertainment — walked
home with Mr. B. T. Jessup, stopping
to hear organ at 35th St. & Madison ave.
Home at 10 o'clk. & to bed at 12.
