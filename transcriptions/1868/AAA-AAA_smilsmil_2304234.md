## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 15 1868

a beautiful day — Clear but very cold — At Studio at 9.15 — worked on Charcoale drawing until noon — after getting the views of Hart + Colman — Set it. Miss Sarah Martin + Holt called at 1, so did Havens — At 1.15 home to lunch — at 2.30 went to [illegible] with Joe, to buy a print — then down town, Paid Roberts + boy [illegible] 3 Reams of paper to send to Doc Gavit — Called at A.B.N. Co. to see about my "Gaucho + Guitar" drawing — Gavit is not pleased with it but does not know why — at 5. walked up — Stopped at the S. N. Fan came in from a drive with Mam They called at the Studio for me — Took dinner with Fan — then until 9.30 walked home — got to bed about 12.

Mary came to stay home for a few days, to be nursed until her neck gets better.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 16 1868

Very disagreeable wf. Rain + sleet setting in at about 11 + going into a Regular storm about 3 P.m — Got to Studio at 9.15 — Set to work Charcoaling [illegible] No. 3 — a bit all the Wf. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Most of the afternoon spent mounting paper for my large drawing — for George's drawing + for Louis Tiffany and giving him som hints in washing flat tints. Young Bell came + spent an hour or so drawing, but had not time to give him a lesson — Home to tea at 6.30, feeling very tired I gave up did not return to the Studio I had intended to outline an [illegible] — Rested myself and got to bed shortly after 9 —
