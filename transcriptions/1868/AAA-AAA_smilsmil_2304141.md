## Left Page

### June, Saturday 20, 1868

Up at 6 — wrote a letter to Matt.
& Kate at Hill Side. Studied a
little French, got my bkfst. and
to Studio at 9 o'clk. Fearfully
hot. Ther. 92° in Studio. made French
exercise until 10 o'clk. then went
into Brevoort's Room & took French
lesson — back at 11 & to work touching
on photos of H.F. Fessenden. Home
to lunch at 1 — back at 2. then went
to J. Q. A. Ward's — 22 S. & 5th Ave. to see
if we could get the use of his bro. Ed's
Room for a few days, it being cooler
than ours — failed. Back to Studio
& to work at 3 — on photos. Stuck to it
(& to pretty much everything in fact, it
was so hot) until 7 o'clk. then
home to tea — Eve'g spent
looking over pfs. — writing &
fixing up things — so bed
at near 11 —

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 21, 1868

The worst sort of a hot sticky day —
did not get through with my bathing
& operations & bkfst. until 10.30 and
then, feeling lothe to move, did not
get to Mr. Hasting's Ch. until 11.15.
Heard a good Sermon from the
text "Lord I believe, help thou my
unbelief" — Got home at 12.10
just about 10 m. before a heavy
shower. Dinner at 1 — after dinner
a short nap — most of the P.M.
spent sitting on back piazza.
Very soft showers from time
to time culminating in quite
a wet one bet. 6 & 7. Mr. & Mrs. Noad
up to tea — they spent the evening
with us, also, D. A. [illegible] — also
from about 9 o'clk. M. Chocoroo
Reed. Got to bed at about
11 o'clk.
