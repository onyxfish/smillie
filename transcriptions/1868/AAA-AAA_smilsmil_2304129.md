## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 27, 1868

A very charming day — got to
studio just before 9 o'clk, and
worked closely all day on die
for A.B.N. Co — trimming up the
etching with graver — at noon
Burling called — gave him a
check for $11.15 on Water Color
Soct'y funds — Home to lunch
at 1 — back at 2.15 — Took French
lesson at 4 — back to work
at 5 & stuck to it until near
7 — Then went to Emma Ireland's
& took a cup of tea — Met her cousin
Miss    & gave her season ticket
to N.A. of D. Fanny Kimball then &re
Will came in before I left at 8.30
Aunt Mary & Tom g— about house to
tea — Spent what Remained of
evef looking over 11th Ann. Report
of Central Park Comm'n — got
to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 28, 1868

Disagreeable day — threatening &
late in the p.m. getting into a wet
mizzle — at 9.5 got to Ritchie's &
bitin the etched crossing that I
had at work upon for some days past.
At noon went to Parker's to lunch,
then to A.B.N. Co. & got etching [illegible] of
my die — at 2 — back to Ritchie's
& spent the p.m. burnishing on
the sky — which is patterned like
moire-antique — It seems to go
under the burnisher — a good view
of Review of 7th Regt in front of City
Hall — Stopped work at 6.15 — walked
up home — Tea at 7.30 — Evef
reading & writing a note to Fan
Mulford. George spent evef
at Diven's — To bed at 10.30

8½ days on Ritchie's sky,
Full time to day.
