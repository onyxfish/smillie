## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 17 1868.

Dull, disagreeable day — at Studio at 9.30 & tried to
paint the figure in of picture —
after bothering with it for some
time got Auders to come in &
help me out with it — Finished
up everything about it by 10 cl —
then home to lunch — back
at 2 — Spent the p.m. fixing
up the Remainder of my out
studies & sketching Ruby etc
for A.B.N. Co. drawings — Howard
of the Every Mail gave us a call —
Bought some fine photographs
of Ancient Armor, Spent the
evening fixing them up, also
engaged addressing Man's
wedding cards — Did not
get to bed until midnight.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 18 1868.

Miserable Rainy day — Left
home before 9 o'clk — First stopped
to see Kaulbach's Cartoon "Era of
the Reformation" — deeply impressed —
wish that I was a master — then
went to Knoedler's — saw a fine
Bouguereau & again wished to be
a master — Called in at Snedecor's,
Schaus', & to Putnam's to see
Bierstadt's "Vesuvius" — a very
poor picture I think — I walked
on down to the A.B.N. Co — bothered
there with Gavit & Goodall until
after 12 — Lunch at Rudolph's &
to Studio at 2 — Spent the p.m.
outlining A.B.N. Co drawing of
a horse — Home to tea at 6 —
Evg. mounted Armor photos —
Got to bed shortly after 11 —
