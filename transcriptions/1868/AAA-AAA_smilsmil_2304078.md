## Left Page

### February Thursday, 13 1868

clear, and mild — Got to Studio a
little before 9 — Mr F spent
stretching small canvases and
outlining two subjects, On the Saco
7×9 in. Woods Kimball called at
noon — Home at lunch at 1 o'clk,
at 2, went down to Uncle George, but
he was not ready for me — went to
Sarony's — he was not ready for me —
Called to see Fred Durand — he
was not in — went in to Schans's,
Snedicor's & [illegible]'s to see the
pictures — made a long call on
C. Ogilvie — went to S. P. Avery's, Cor.
[illegible] to see his French pictur[es].
Then walked up to Gymnasium. Got
there at 5.30 & worked for an hour —
home to tea at 7 — Found Uncle
Wm & aunt Huldah, just in from
Montrose. Spent the evening
chatting with them — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### February Friday, 14 1868

Still clear & somewhat colder than
yesterday. At Studio by 9 o'clk &
spent the morning outlining two
6×10 in subjects, one "On the Saco"
the other "White Mts from top of White Horse
Ridge" — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.30 & resumed outlining
got a third subject, 7×9 in, a
Reduction of my Academy picture,
Ready to paint — Rodney Kimball
made us quite a call & Mr. Prevost
dropped in — Home to tea at 6 —
after much fussing in getting skates
in order, at 9 o'clk, went to Central
Park with Charl & skated until
after 10 o'clk, when the policemen
cleared the pond. Enjoyed the
fun & think that I'm beginning my
winter amusements late — my first
sleigh ride & skate for the season
this week — walked home,
getting in at 10.45 — went at once
to bed.
