## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 24 1868

Pleasant day, with a shower at 11
o'clk. Got to Ritchie's at 9.15 & found
things all ready for me to begin
to bite. Stopping out & biting all
the m.f. Ab't took lunch with
father at Parker's. Took ground
off from plate about 3 o'clk —
and burnished until 5.15
Have added much color and
feel encouraged. At 5.15 a
heavy storm coming up — took
car & while on my way up a
tremendous storm — Thunder
& lightning & hail & rain —
nearly over when I reached
home — Joe & Will home from P. I.
Every Mr. Batters by with us
until after 10 o'clk —
I got to bed shortly after
11
10½ days on Ritchie's sky
full time to-day —

## Right Page

### June Thursday, 25 1868

Delightful day — At Ritchie's at
9.15 — am m.f. — had just been taken
from sky — Very black & a long
ugly pattern in the ruling. Set
to work burnishing & stuck to
it until 12.30 — then got lunch
& went to the foot of James St. to
see if I could find some flannel
shirts for out of door wear — Back
at 1.30 & resumed burnishing
which I kept up until 6 o'clk —
then home — At 8 o'clk P.M.
went down to 73 W. 21 St. and saw
E.C.T. — Her mother has just under-
gone an operation for cataract
at the hands of Dr. Agnew and is
now doing remarkably well.
Left at 10 o'clk — Home & to bed
at 11 —

11½ days on Ritchie's sky —
full time to-day.
