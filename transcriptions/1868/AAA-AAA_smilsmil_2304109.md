## Left Page

### April, Wednesday, 15, 1868

Up, as I have written at 9.30 after a night of no Rest. After washing dressing &bkfst., got off to Studio at 11. A.M. going first to Uncle George's, then to Kelley's — Sarony's — Schnutt & getting to Studio at 12.30. Had lunch at Studio — then tried to work on drawing, but found myself more tired than I thought. Miss Percy & Gel. P's wife née Crocker made us a long visit — Gel. went in to Brevorte & took French lesson. I was not prepared so, worked on my drawing — Mrs. B. made us quite a visit — Home at 6.45. The mng, muggy & overcast — p.m. very warm & clear — evng. high wind — Every thing upside down at home moving about, to accommodate Bro. will & his wife — Part of my furniture down stairs — part in 3d Story — Char. moved from 2d Story front to 3d Story back — to bed in my new bedroom at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Thursday, 16, 1868

was wakened in the mng by a clap of thunder — muggy & warm with Rain all day & at intervals sporadic thunder-claps. Got to Studio at 9.30. Worked on sheep drawing — Rode home to lunch, carrying a Lemon marangue pie presented by Mr. Culver — Found Aaron McCabe at the house — He is on his way home — Back at 2 — At 3.30 Rode down to Bellows Studio, Astor R. to attend meeting of Council of Water Color Society. Met Bellows. Wm Hart. Coleman and Burling. Called on Hicks, for a few minutes — Stopped at Snedecor's — walked up to Studio with Jos. Hart. at Studio until 6.45 — then home. Rain stopped — Evng spent arranging books in cases etc. that have been moved down to-day. Charl. still continues to improve. to bed at 10.
