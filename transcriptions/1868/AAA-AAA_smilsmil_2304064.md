## Left Page

### January, Thursday 16, 1868

A delightful day, clear as a bell
& almost mild. Got up heavily
& did not get to Studio until 10 oclk.
Then looked over Delnoce's die of
Boston for a little while & after,
Retouched upon a photo. of one
of Gavit's children, for him. —
Then I began outlining with
Sp.P. Turp. & Brown on Academy
picture — Took lunch at Studio
& at 2.15 went to Uncle Geo's to keep
an appointment — He is making me
a set of teeth. He was not Ready so
I Ret'd at once to Studio & Resumed
work on my Moat Mt. Sent Sprits
to Louise Dia. for to-morrow night.
Home to tea at 6.15. At 7.30 went
with Charl to Armory to see Battal-
ion drill 20th & 9th Cos. There until 9.
Stopped at Studio on way up & made
for half an hour. Got to bed
shortly after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 17, 1868

Clear & cold again. Up early &
after bkfst went down town to A.
B.N. Co. where I worked for an hour
or two on Beckwith's die — Drew my
money ($50.00) & at 11.45 started for
up town. Went to Uncle George's, after
getting my lunch & had my new
plate fitted — then went to Sarony
& sat for a photograph. From there
to Snedecor's & then to Philhar-
monic Rehearsal, getting there
at 2.30. At 4.30, up to Studio
got letters from Ritchie & Hasel-
tine. At 5.15 went to gymnasium
met Capt. Van Wyck & worked until
near 7 o'clk. Aunt Mary Gillelan to tea
— Fanny Tintall & her br. Mill
called during the evening —
Got to bed at 11 o'clk.

Taib & Tramp called at the Studio
to-day, but I did not see them.
