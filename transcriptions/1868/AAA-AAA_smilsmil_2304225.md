## Left Page

### December Sunday, 27 1868.

In bed again all day. Heavy snow storm — Got up between 4 & 5 o'clk in the afternoon, washed \+ dressed \+ went down to tea — Still suffering with my throat \+ head — Sat up until 11 o'clk \+ then to bed — Slept well during the night —

## Right Page

### December Monday, 28 1868.

My head still very heavy — kept my bed until 11 o'clk — then got up — feeling better. Dinner at 1 \+ at 2 Rode to the studio — Found that Gavit (Jos) had at last sent p\+g $500 Reception Circulars — Spent the Rest of the P.M. distributing them — to the fellows in the building — Home about 5.30 — Sent a regret to Mr. Chas. Culver who celebrates her "tin" wedding to-night. Kept up until about 11 o'clk — then to bed —
