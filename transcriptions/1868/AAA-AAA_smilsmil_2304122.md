## Left Page

### May, Monday 11, 1868

Up at 6. Magnificent mrg.
at 7 Uncle a. made his appearance
& together we drove up to Carmans-
ville. Back at 8. Bkfst at 8.30
at 9.15 drove over to 8th av, Cors,
& at 10.30 got to Studio. Set
to work on "Wool Drying" drawing
at 1 home to lunch — Uncle Wm
& aunt H. there — back at 2.30
& worked until near 7 o'clk. but
did not succeed in getting my
drawing finished — at 7. went
to Emma Ireland's with passe-partout
for her. Home to tea at 7.30. feeling
very tired spent the evening
in my big chair. — To bed at 10.50

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 12, 1868

Fine day. Up at 6 — At Studio at 8.30
to work at once on A.B.N. Co drawing.
got it done at about 11 am, & then
went to A.H. Ritchie's — Park Row, to
see about the large sky that I am
engraving for him — left him at 12,
at 12.30 stopped at 10th St Studios
& called on Sanford Gifford, J.G. Brown
& S. Guy — Home to lunch at 1.30
back to Studio at 2.30 — Spent
the Rest of the P.M. getting things
Ready to begin engraving die
for A. B. N. Co — Home to tea
at 7.15. Wee brought up to me,
$100.00. — $160 for sheep drawing
& $40 for [illegible] Coffee, Tobacco &
Palm. — Spent the even. on
French exercises & verbs.
To bed at 10.45
