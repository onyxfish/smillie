## Left Page

### July, Monday 20, 1868

Feeling rather better to-day but yet very Ragged — The weather changed. Wind E.N.E. About 1 o'clk — Thunder. Soon after Rain which kept up pretty constantly during the p.m. —
Got to Studio at 9 15. a.m. Set to work at once biting in die for A.B.N.Co. — & stuck to it all day until near 7 o'clk. P.M. getting a good bite with almost over much color. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Evening thoroughly tired out & very nervous — So did nothing but spread myself in my big chair until bed time. Mother still improving — Uncle W. at our house to tea — he stayed all night at Düring's. Raining steadily when I went to bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 21, 1868

Rain during most of the m'g. Got to Studio at 9 25 feeling very much as though I must soon give up to a headache — but I stuck to my die, working with the graver, trimming the etching — Ther. 78° which by comparison is comfort itself. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 & worked on die until 5.15 when I went down to Billeck's with Alfred Jones's W.C. drawing to order a frame for it, & from there down to Lauthier's. Home to tea at 6.30 very tired & head aching. Uncle Willie with us to tea. Every passed in my big chair, resting. To bed about 10. Weather still cool — no rain, but wind from the E. [illegible]
