## Left Page

### June Tuesday, 2 1868.

Again, dismal Rain, My
Raining quite hard until 10 or
11 o'clk — at intervals during the
Rest of the day, Clearing off
gloriously at 7. Got down town
at 9.15 + Ruled sky on die at
A.B.N.Co — made a call on Mr.
Lloyd + on father + got back to
the Studio at 12. Home to lunch
at 1, back at 2.15 Bit in sky
+ worked with graver + burnisher
during Rest of p.m. Home
to tea at 7. The girls all off
during the eveg to see Nellie
Baker married — Young Haven
called at the house — I
wrote a note to the Pierce
Sisters in Boston + got to
bed at 10.15

## Right Page

### June Wednesday, 3 1868.

Clear weather, though hardly promising.
Got to studio at 9. Soon after saw
Sam. Colman + some of his water-
color sketches that he has just made
among the Highlands on the Hudson
so fired me that I started right off
to select papers + order sketching
apparatus. my die being downtown
to prove. went to Knœdler's + bought
specimens of drawing paper — then
to Schmitt's to get up the drawing
board arrangement — but did not settle
upon anything — Home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2 — Spent Rest of p.m. until
3.30 o'clk studying French — At 5.30
took French lesson — then, with Gie
walked over to H.R.R.R. and took
5.15 train to Manhattanville — took
tea + stayed all night at Uncle
Aaron's — Spent most of the eveg
at the Billiard table with Miss
Porter. Evening, overcast and
threatening Rain — to bed at 10.30
