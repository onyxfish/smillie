## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 19, 1868.

Day rather overcast + quite
cool. Did not get up until
9 o'clk, bkfst at 10. Shortly
after which I was so much
pained in the stomach that
I went to bed + stayed there
until after 3 o'clk. P.m —
Then got up, + sat around
until tea time — Every-
spent comfortably in my
new quarters, back parlor.
Got to bed at about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 20, 1868.

miserable, Cold, Rainy day —
Uncle Willie started for Poughkeepsie
at 8 am. I got to Studio at 9 o'clk —
worked all the m'g on drawing that
I made for A.B.N. Co some time ago —
"Camping on the Pampas" — Home to
lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 — Afternoon
used up, between finishing another
drawing "Lassoing Wild Cattle" and
touching up photo. copy of the head
I finished of N4 Fessenden.
Home at 6.45. At 7.45 with Matt,
Kate + Gel went around to Harvard
Rooms to see some of Prof. Marten's
class practice calisthenics —
Home at 9.30 — Some Reading, a
little writing + to bed at 11.15
