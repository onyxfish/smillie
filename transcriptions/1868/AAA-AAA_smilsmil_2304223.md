## Left Page

### December Friday, 25 1868.

A Raw cold disagreeable Christmas day — Got to studio about 9.30 and spent most of the m'f getting my Tracing & Charcoal drawing finished — Commenced to re-trace it up on the drawing paper using Charcoal for paper & out-lining it with pen \+ Bront's Brown. Home to dinner at 1.30. Eat a good dinner \+ got back to studio at 2.45 — Worked heavily during the P.M. until 4.30 feeling the effects of a heavy cold coming on. Ever immediately after tea, got my feet into hot water \+ shortly after got to bed — Passed a miserable enough night —

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 26 1868.

not able to get up in the m'f — In bed all day. Heavy Snow storm — Got up about 5 o'clk — washed \+ dressed \+ took tea like a Christian but carried a very heavy head \+ Sat in easy chair until 11 o'clk then to bed — another, miserable, night, suffering with my throat —
