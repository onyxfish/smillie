## Left Page

### November Monday, 23, 1868

Mary married — Father's 61st and
Milli's 34th Birth day —

a glorious, mild & cloudless day — About
9 o'clk when I got to the studio — Laid a
ground on Studio Recto card plate &
etched in palette & brushes — then worked
on Iowa State Arms drawing trying to get
it finished, — but much interrupted with
callers. S.S. Conapt of "The Times" called —
Alman came & took alway his picture —
Ed Peele & his Bro-in-law Dougherty called
& Mr. Pate called — I promised to engrave
two plates for him next Spring — About
11.30 Dr. T. came & I went with him to the
Fifth Ave. Hotel & was introduced to his
aunt, Mr. Blackmar & her daughter, Mrs.
Sternberg — back at 12.15 — Took lunch
at the Studio — at 4 o'clk bitin "palette
& brushes" — Francis Coppelt Esq. called
& gave me a copy of his "ascent of Mont Bl[anc]"
Charley Adams called & walked up home
with me — Got home at 4.20. Dr. Army Fox
in a great hurry to catch 5 o'clk train
as he had been summoned as a witness —
Joe late & for a few moments, a flum[?] —
all right soon & Mary married to us
at P. Throofs at 4.50 P.M — 30 or 40 members
of the family present — at 7.30 I escorted
Mrs. R. & Mrs. S. to the 5 Ave. Hotel — at 8 I
went to Minnie Ireland's — saw her and
Mrs. Spring — at 9. went to Private view
at N.A. of D — There met Gue and the
Freedy girls — did not get away
until near 11 — & got to bed just before
We had a capital supper between
6 & 7 o'clk. P.M. —

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 24, 1868

Another superb day — Rather
tired from the rush of yesterday —
nearly 10 o'clk. when I got to the
Studio — Found that my biting
of palette & brushes yesterday
was too light — went through it
again with the graver — at it until
1 o'clk — Then on Iowa State Arms
at 12.30 went to The St. James and
called on Mary. As she has a
swelled neck from cold, Dr. does
not think it prudent to go off on
a trip, so for the present it is
postponed — Home to lunch at [illegible]
back at 2 — Finished Arms &
horse — Home to tea at 6 —
self mounted drawing — made
a note to Pate & went down to the
James — Vedder called at the
Studio today — Uncle Milli
started for Canada this M[orning]
