## Left Page

### November, Thursday 19, 1868

Charming day \- Got to Studio at 9.20 \- Father walked down with me \+ sat to Augers for his portrait until about 11.30 \- I sat with him, watching progress for nearly an hour, then, shortly after getting to work on my Horse drawing, Dr Clinesley came in \+ shortly after that Mr. Tiffany \+ Belle \+ Mary Tweedy \- They left at 1.20 \+ I went home to lunch \- Back at 2.30 \+ worked as long as I could see on drawing \- Early part of Eve'g, hunting up authorities \+ materials concerning Water Color painting \- at 8.30 called at Mary Butler's \- not in \- went to Kimball's \- Will \+ Fanny Ready were to go out to a party, but they stayed with me until 10 o'clk \- walked to the Cor. with them \- I walked home \- Did not get to bed until 11.15 \-

## Right Page

### November, Friday 20, 1868

Dull \+ overcast, Immediately after slept got a sketch of Charl. in his military Rig, to aid me in a drawing of Iowa State arms that I have to make for the A.B.N. Co \- did not get to Studio until 10 o'clk \- found Alman waiting for me \- He expressed himself satisfied with the Cattle \+ made but one or two trifling suggestions, which in half an hour I had complied with \- but I think that he wanted more finish for his $100 \- Maud \+ Ida Diven called \- I worked on drawing of Horse \- got lunch at Studio at 1 o'clk \- At 2.15 went to Phil. Reh. \- heard the Eroica Symphony \- back to Studio at 4 \+ managed to get Home some after a fashion at dark \- Home at 6 \- Eve'g, mounted a number of proofs that Alman gave me \+ my Horse \- it does not seem to give satisfaction, \+ I am rather discouraged for I do not see how to Remedy it \- Got to bed about midnight \-
