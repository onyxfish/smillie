## Left Page

### June, Friday, 12, 1868.

A day not quite so bad as yesterday,
but still, miserable enough — Mist
mizzle & rain all day until about
6 o'clk P.M. when there was a sort
of clear up. At Studio at 8.45 — gave
a general sweep & dustup. At
10 o'clk went to 5th Ave. Hotel — Met Mrs
Annie Peirce & walked up to the cor. of
26 St. with her. Meta Gavish came to
Studio at 11 o'clk — at 12.30 I went to
N.A. of D. with her. There until after 2 —
Then went with her to Bergman's & had
lunch — then put her in a Fulton Ferry
'bus — at 3 Ret'd to Studio to find the
Sisters Peirce, Mr. Hill & an elderly lady.
At near 4 went with them to N.A. of D. —
There until 5. Then with them to 5th Ave. Hotel
& there left them. — Home to tea at 6.
Ev'g. Reed of Boston called — left at
9 o'clk. — Wrote french exercises
& got to bed shortly after 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday, 13, 1868.

Clear & tremendously hot — Before
going to Studio, with Gel., at 9 o'clk.
went to 5th Ave. Hotel & bid the Sisters
Peirce "Good-bye" — They sail for
Brest at noon. At Studio at 9.30
found J.J. Griffin waiting — He wants
again my 3 foot picture — thinks
he can sell it — am sure he was
not pleased, because my upper
lips were so stiff — Took french
lesson until 11 — worked on photo
of H.Y. P.F. until I went home to
lunch at 1 — back at 2 — worked again
on photos. Then 82° in our Room —
at 3.30 Gel. & I took car for Central
Park — met Matt & Kate — heard music
in Park, but heat so intense, that
it was not enjoyable — Saw the
animals & home at 6.30 — After
tea Mr. & Mrs. Node came in — they
have taken Rooms at Mr. Blaney's.
Gel & I went up to Diven's —
there until 10.30 — then home
and to bed at 11.30
