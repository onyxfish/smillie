## Left Page

### August Friday, 7 1868.

Got started this m'f. with my
water-color traps up Wood Hill —
Atmospheric effects — Cloud & sun [illegible]
over the mts. most delicious. Se-
lected a point & made a Camera-lucida
outline — got home just before dinner.
Spent the p.m. stretching canvas
& making outline of Camera-lucida
sketch on 15×26 in canvas — Abt.
5 o'clk. went to top of Buck Hill
to see the Sun set — Box arr'd.
from Home with Grits, crackers,
beef & tea — now I have something
to eat. Ev'ef., Mr. Darrow & miss
Morris got up a series of 6 tableaux
vivants — I was betrayed into presenting
myself as a sort of dark Earl in
an Effie Deans Scene — my debut
upon the stage — Everything & every-
body very gay. Mr. D. & miss M. have
been up Hurricane to-day — & have
yet to pack, to start to-morrow m'f.
They are tremendous drivers —
Got to bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### August Saturday, 8 1868.

Overcast & showery m'f. After Mr. D. &
miss M. got off — took oil colors, went
to the River (Boquet) Right back of the
house & commenced a sketch about
7½×15 in — Became quite in love
with the subject & have determined
to make a careful study 15×22.
Home to dinner — Some time after on
piazza with the ladies — then to
my m'f. sketching ground & built
up a seat, as the ground is very
sloping, & fixed things to commence
work in earnest. Rained quite heavily
while I was at work — Thunder showers
passing to the E. & W. of us, all the
p.m. & Ev'ef. Ev'ef., entertained
with Planchette and games —
got to bed about 11.30.
