## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 1 1868.

Quite a cold mistry day — did not get to Studio until 9.30 — Commenced Charcoaling fat open (2d) 24×38 in. for Drew — at 12.30 went down-town to C.B.N. Co. with drawing of Gaucho + Guitar — Drew $100 + for "Horse" + Iowa State arme". Then went to Roberts, the printer + got all of the Studio Reception Circulars that he had printed, only about 50 — Back to Studio about 3 — Worked as long as I could see, on open, but could not get outline complete — Got it so that I can begin on sky to-morrow — Called on Many on my way home — Tea at 6.30 — A. + with Gif went to Water Color Society meeting at Swain Gifford's an entirely informal meeting — Some Sketches to be seen, Chatting, Smoking, etc. — Introduced [illegible] Howard of the Mail — Home at 10.30 + to bed at once.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 2 1868.

Overcast, mild + feeling like snow — Got to the Studio at 9.30 — Finished continuing open + then commenced painting in the sky. — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.15. Found Tony Bell waiting for me — as I was hurried with my sky I met him at copying in pencil a study of Beech Trunks by Geo. Howard also called — I worked as long as I could see — getting my sky + a small bit of distance in. — Home to tea at 5.30 — Well, Gif. went down to the Tuesdays — but as I felt tired I stayed at home + wrote to B.F. + notices to the press for our Studio Reception next Sat. Got to bed at about 11.
