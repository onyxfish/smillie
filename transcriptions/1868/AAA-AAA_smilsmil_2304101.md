## Left Page

### March, Monday 30, 1868

Marchlike day — windy & raw. Got
to Studio at 8.40 AM. Got fair under
way & started (2d day) on 16×26.
"Moat Mt. from the Saco River" —
Painted in the sky, but so many
callers came in that I could get
no farther. A Mr. Johnston of Boston,
native of Norway called in the m'f.
Home to lunch at 1, back at 2 —
Maud Mrs. Diven called — then
Amelia Sent & her friend Miss
Brower, a y'g lady that I took
a great fancy to. About 6 P.M.
went down to Sauthier's paste portrait
maker — home to tea at 7 — Settled
myself to my french when Reed
of Boston called — he spent the
ev'g in my Room until 10 o'clk
Matt & Kate entertaining.
To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 31, 1868

Very mild clear day. Father went
with me to the studio — Got there at
9.30 o'clt — Father was much pleased
with our pictures — I worked (3d day)
on 16×26. Moat Mt. painting in the
mountains — day very much
broken by getting off pictures for
Academy. I find that if I don't
get excited, others do. Mother came
in just too late to see our paintings.
Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2. Found
Aunt H. at Studio — Miss Pardee also
called & Young Havens, bringing with
him the Son of Gov. Morgan. At
5 o'clk. took [illegible] of "City of Hartford" to
N.A.& D. then home to tea — Ev'g
spent writing french exercise.
First meeting of hanging Com. to-night
but I thought it best not to go.
To bed at 10.45
