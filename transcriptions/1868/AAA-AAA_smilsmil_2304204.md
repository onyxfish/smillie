## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 28 1868

What some people call a "Catching sort of day" — Showery
& uncertain — After some time spent in trying to get things to rights, about the middle of the day, went with Geo. to the Studio —
Saw for James & p.? Rent for the month of Sep't. Saw about having the fallen Ceiling repaired & the Carpet taken up — Geo. to "boss the job". Then went to the Barber's & enjoyed a shampoo —
Home to lunch at 1 — Worked on sketch of drawing for A.B.N. Co. & finished large dog's head that I drew in Montrose — Evening set drawings & until 10 o'clk getting drawers of my table in order — Joe & Mille, who are spending the week at Uncle A's home to-night to attend the wedding of Joe Brown.

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 29 1868

Delightful day — Immediately after bkfst. to work & completed sketch of St. A. Mr. Saladers about 10 o'clk. Then went down to A.B.N. Co. with it — and for a wonder neither Mr. Gordall, nor Mr. E. had any objections to make —
Then went to Cohen's & got mounting board & at just 1 o'clk, got home entirely used up with a headache — Took a cup of tea & got to bed immediately where I stayed until 6 o'clk, when feeling much better I got up & took tea like a Christian —
Spent the evg. stretching paper for drawing & mounting some of the drawings that I made last summer — Did not get to bed until after midnight.
