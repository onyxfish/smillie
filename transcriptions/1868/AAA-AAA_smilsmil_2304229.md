## Left Page

### December Thursday, 31 1868.

Raw, overcast \+ disagreeable \- Up about 9 o'clk. after a tolerable night. Got to the Studio shortly after 10 o'clk. \+ set to work outlining drawing \- worked until 1 \- then walked home to lunch \- back at 2.30 \+ continued during Rest of p.m. to 5 o'clk. at outlining \- a pkg. of Reception Circulars arr'd. Call from Jo°. Gavit \+ I worked folding them until 6.30 when I went home to Tea. Spent most of the eveg. in Will's Room \- our hospital up stairs, being full. Mary continues about the same, of course losing flesh \+ strength. For two or three days she has been fretting about a sore spot on her knee. Mat is under the weather with a Cold. I got to bed about 11.
