## Left Page

### May Friday, 15 1868.

Up at 7.30, overcast day. Got to studio at 9.15. Before I got to work M. Whittridge came in & before he left Mary Fessenden came in & made a long & very pleasant call. Got to work soon after 10 — outlined figures on White's die & went home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.45. In the mean time Kitty Mapes & a friend had called. I etched all the Rest of the p.m. on A.B.N.Co's die — Find my hand all Right, which quite astonishes me. Worked until after 6 — Home to tea at 6.30. Eveg spent writing French exercises, etc — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 16 1868.

Up at a little after 6 — M[illegible] overcast & showery. At Studio about 9 o'clk & etched on Brazil die, foreground effects. At 10 went in to Brevoort's Room & took French lesson. Returning to my etching at 11.30. At 1, went home to lunch. at 2.15 back again. Etched until 6 o'clk — getting in the distant mtys & water. It pleases as well as surprises me, to find that after the long time that I have done no engraving, my hand is as fresh in practice, as though but three weeks, instead of 3 years had passed since I did any engraving — Home to tea at 7. The Eveg passed studying French — To bed at about 10.
