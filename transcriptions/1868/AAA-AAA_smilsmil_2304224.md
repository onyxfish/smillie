## Left Page

### December Saturday, 5 1868

About as miserable a day as could be made — Snow & sleet thail all day long — Got to Studio at 9.30 — Almau came in soon after & insisted upon my painting a 3d fat of subject until 12 o'clk. Gil & I engaged clearing up as to-day has been announced for our first Studio Reception for the season. Took lunch about 1 — Commenced Charcoaling West Point subject for Meuger — got it somewhat into shape, but had to give it up as quite a number of callers came in spite of the weather. Home at 6 — After tea, wrote to Joseph Gavit concerning the printing of our Reception Circulars — at 9 o'clk felt uncomfortable & until 11 took it easy in my big chair — then to bed —

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 6 1868

Brilliant day overhead — but bad enough under foot — 8 in — of snow must have fallen —
Myf went to Hastings. Communion service — He preached from Paul to Titus 2d chap. 14 V —
Home to dinner — Still rather under the weather & spent the p.m. on Sofa in front basement. Evef — wrote a letter to Fau M & a note enclosed to Sydia Harrington with whom she is at present staying at Wilmington. Del — At 11.30 got to bed —
