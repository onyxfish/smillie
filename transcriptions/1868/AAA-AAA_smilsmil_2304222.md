## Left Page

### December Thursday, 3 1868.

Cold, windy day — got to Studio at 9.25 — Set to mk at once painting in ground on "Ofen" — At noon. Circular for Studio Receptions came up from the printers, only 100 of them \+ shockingly printed. The printer has given up the job. Did not get home to lunch until 2 o'clk — back before 3 \+ worked as long as I could see, getting the ground finished, after a fashions. Home to tea at 6 — wrote a note to 7an W. then called my Mary Butler — There until 10.15. On my way home I stopped at Mary's in 34 St. There met Will Joe Geo Matt \+ Charl — all walked home — To bed abt 11.15

## Right Page

### December Friday, 4 1868.

Dull day, threatening snow. Got to Studio at 9.20 — Painted all day on trees back of Ofen \+ Feuchsel called in the. Home to lunch at 1 o'clk — back at 2 — worked on painting until 4 — Then set with Shellac — Auger's charcoal drawing of Andromeda — 5 feet high — worked about Studio until near 6. getting things somewhat to rights for our Studio Receptions to come off to-morrow — Mr. Carroll called for a few moments, this A.M. He owns two or more of Geo's paintings. Out \+ about with Meta \+ Charley Adams — Home at 10.20 — To bed at 11.30 commenced snowing about 11 —
