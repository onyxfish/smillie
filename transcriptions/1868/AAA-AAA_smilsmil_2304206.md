## Left Page

### November Sunday, 1 1868

Dark & gloomy, with Steady ra[in]
all day. Up early and enjoye[d]
my bath before breakfast —
went to Rev. Thos. Hastings' ch[urch]
and heard him after a very
poor fashion — Spent weak[ly]
all the afternoon in a stupen[d-]
kind of dozing — Evening,
did not go out, but stayed
at home and Read — g[ot]
to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### November Monday, 2 1868

Cold, Raw & cloudy with occasional
flakes of snow — Worked on drawing
for A.B.N. Co until 2.30 P.M. then
went to Wm. Hart's Studio to
attend a meeting of the Board of
Control of Am. Socy of Painters in
Water Colors, met there Colman
Fredericks & Burling. There until
about 5 when I went to Snedecor's
there until 6 — then home to tea —
At 7.45 went to Gavity, in 45th [St.]
Saw him on business, then visited
with Meta, now Mrs. Adams &
her Charley until 10.45 — a
glorious, clear, cold, moonlight
night — S.L.F. called at the
house this P.M. — got to bed
shortly after midnight —
