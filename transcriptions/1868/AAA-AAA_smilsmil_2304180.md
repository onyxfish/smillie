## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 8 1868.

Up at 7 - weather slowly clearing at 9, everything clear & superb. Wrote a note to the Tweedy girls at E. town & at 9 got off to my work. 11×19 — Giant from the hill back of Reede's — Charl came up at 12 o'clk to bid me good bye — he goes to E. town this p.m. — To remain there until next Monday — Stuck to my work until near 2 o'clk when I got my canvas covered & went to the house to lunch — at 2½ off for my p.m. subject — the Gothic & Ausable Mts — worked until 6 — sky then overcast & quite cold. Got my canvas covered on returning to the house found Lambdin there — The Durand party mention to the ponds this mng. Saw the Coleman party at 6 o'clk for a few minutes — [illegible] was [illegible] brushes, cleaning palette, etc. etc. Got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 9 1868.

after a blustering night a blowy rainy morning — Soon after noon it lifted & was quite pleasant toward [illegible]. Worked on water color study from the tops of Giant's Leap, looking E. giving it some finishing touches — also on little sketches in my pocket sketch book — About 4 o'clk commenced getting things ready to go in to the Ausable Ponds to-morrow — outlined on canvas, Painton Falls so that I may be ready to go right to work — Coleman came up about 5 o'clk. to arrange about to-morrow — went with him over to Smith Reede's — & then a short walk with him & Gel. [illegible] chatting with Lambdin & misses Sculls, Lewis & Blodgett — Got to bed about 10.30.
