## Left Page

### August Saturday, 1 1868

Awake at 6 — Steady Rain — up at
7 & down to Hotel at 7.30 — Rain over —
Took steps with the party with them
until they got off at 9 o'clk. for [illegible]
Pat. Pet. to my work feeling very
lonesome. Worked steadily all
day until about 6 o'clk. P.M.
when I got my drawing into
a shape that I thought I could
send it off. Wrote a letter to Will
& at 7 Sent pkg and letter
to my great Relief. Spent the
evening in Wood's Room looking
over his sketches, showing much of
the country about here.
Got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### August Sunday, 2 1868

Woke up at 6 with an unmistak-
able headache and stayed in
bed with it until 6 o'clk P.M.
The Result probably, jointly
of close work & fretting over my
drawing and the hard feeding
that I get here.

At twilight walked with [illegible]
out on the Keene Road a ways.
Returning, wrote home & to Lazarus
concerning Studio Rent. Sat
a while on piazza dis cussing
proposed trip to Hurricane Mt.
to-morrow — Wrote a letter
home and one to Lazarus.
To bed about midnight.
