## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 25 1868.

Glorious morning — Up a little after 6 o'clk — Bkfst at 7.15 — Stopped at Studio & got sketching traps & on the 7.15 Harlem train started for Croton Falls, only 48 miles, but did not get there until 11.7. Alman met me with a carriage — Started at once for Brew's, big oxen, but mistaking the Road we were delayed — Found them & got to work about 12.30. Very soon the sky overcast & I had no sun. I worked until near 3, when almost stiffened with cold I gave up, at getting a poor sketch from one of the animals — Walked to Croton Falls, 3½ miles — getting warmed up again. Alman rode & couldn't understand why I should walk — got to C. Falls at 4. A. left me at 5 — at 6 I took tea at the Hotel — at 7.37 Up. train to N.Y. at 9.10 left traps at studio & home at 10.10 pretty well tired out when I got to bed about 11 o'clk. —

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 26 1868.

Thanksgiving Day. Raining torrents & flowing quite hard — after bkfst got a sketch of Charl. to aid me in making a drawing for A.B.N. Co — as he was up late I did not get it until after 11 — Dr. & Mary stayed at our house last night & took dinner with us to day — At 2, as it had ceased raining, went to studio — Found when I got there, that girl had my key, could not get in, made calls in other Studios — home to dinner at 1.30 — Back to studio at 3 — found the fire out — Worked away for a while at "Gauchos & Guitar" drawing for A.B.N. Co — but darkness soon overtook me — Home at 5.15 — tea at 6 — Many & the Dr. at their own quarters in 34th St. to-night — Spent most of the eveg. arranging Mary's wedding cards — Got to bed at 10.45
