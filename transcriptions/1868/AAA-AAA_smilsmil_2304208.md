## Right Page

### November Friday, 6 1868

Up about 6 — Clear and almost
cloudless — Hurried up things and
got off for West Point on the 8 o'clk
up train — Reached W.P. about
10.30 — Cold, windy & clouded over —
First walked over to take a look at Jaut
Thompsons Statue of Genl. Sedgwick, then
climbed to Fort Putnam — I worked
under almost every disadvantage
of wind and weather, being nearly blown
off the fort & frozen stiff — I found it im-
possible to use my colors, but got 4
or 5 pencil sketches — Left the Fort at 2.30
stopped & made another sketch back of
the barracks & at 3.20 was on the dock —
The 4 o'clk train was 40 m. late — got to
the city about 6.50 — Home to tea at 7.10 —
tired & hungry — Wife worked
on my sketches until after 9 o'clk —
Got to bed at 10 —
