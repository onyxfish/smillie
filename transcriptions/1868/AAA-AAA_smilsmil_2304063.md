## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 14, 1868

Day slightly overcast, milder and
threatening a snow storm — at Studio
at 9.10 — set to work again trying to lay
a Rebiting ground — at 11 — gave it up
& started for down town — went to
A.B.N. Co's & got p.fo — then at 12.45 to
N.B.N. Co's with father's aid, at last
succeeded in getting a ground that
will have to answer — at 1.30 me went
to lunch — about 2.30 — started for
up-town — Stopped at Sarony's —
Schaus's & Snedecor's — got to Studio
at 4.30 — with just time to varnish my
die — at 5 went to gymnasium &
worked until 6 — home to tea & at 7.30
went with Will to Annual meeting of
Am. Microscopical Society. Mr. E. [illegible]
Pres't. He Read an address — we then
had an opportunity to examine many
microscopes — preparations & photos
we left about 9 — before the supper, walked
home through a thin snow storm. Some
Reading & writing & got to bed about
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 15, 1868

In the m'g, at 7 o'clk, snowing as
though in for a three days storm,
but it broke away by noon & during
the p.m. it was quite delightful.
M'g, Rebit Beckwith's die very
generally, getting as clean a line
as I expected — the line is not
always sound, consequently it
only comes up broken — Took lunch
at the Studio & during the after-
noon, burnished on the die, making
it look quite beautiful — Now I
want to see a proof. At 5 o'clk went
to gymnasium & had a long and
hard work — Home at 6.30. W. [illegible]
sen to tea — After tea Reubin to
Will's & from there over to Düring's
to hear him play some Chopin
music. There met young Rob't H —
& Miss Jenkins — Home at 9.30
did a little writing for Mary
& did not get home to bed until
shortly after 11 o'clk.
