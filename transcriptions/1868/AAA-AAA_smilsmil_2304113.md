## Left Page

### April, Thursday, 23, 1868

Up at 6.30 — Beautiful, clear
m'f. Got to studio just before
9 & commenced painting in
sky on 20×36 (4th day) worked
hard all the m'f & succeeded
in getting my sky very even;
Rather sweet & certainly weak —
Home to lunch at 1. back at 2.15.
Painted in mountains & part
of middle distance. Showers
from 3 until 6 o'clk. Sudden
change in the weather to quite
cool. Home to tea at 7. all
of the ev'g, until after 10 o'clk
diligently at work mounting
Sheep drawing, stretching paper
& putting on strainers a photog'h
for Emma Ireland and print
of Fetcham Park. Great hubbub
at home. Will moving in his traps.
Charl so much improved that he took
a little walk out at noon.
Got to bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Friday, 24, 1868

Up at 6.30. Bright m'f, but quite cold.
Rode down to Brooks Bro's about clothes.
Got to Studio at 9.30 pretty much
used up with a headache. Rondel,
Ed. Owen & Miss Pettingill called —
Worked on 20×36 (5th day) Home
to lunch at 1. back at 2. Head
somewhat better, but succeeded
only in painting in line of trees
in middle distance & meadow. At
4.30 cleared up & at 5. went with
Emma Ireland's picture to Sauthen's
ordered passepartout. Cold,
windy & overcast — home at 7.
Spent the ev'g until near
11 studying my French.
