## Left Page

### January, Monday 20, 1868

Dull, heavy m'f. — snowing about
10 o'clk., mizzling during the p. m.
& Raining at night, freezing upon
the pavement as it falls. Got to
studio at 9.15 & lighted the fire &
started 7×9 sketch of my Academy
subject — pattered with it until noon
when I gave it up & worked upon
sketch for Grannis — Home to lunch
at 1 & back at 2 — a little more work
& finished the Grannis sketch. At
3.30, with Ferguson went to N.A. R.D. —
there until 4.30 — then went to Snedecors
with 2 — 6×10's for Utica. "White Mts.
from Randolph Hill" & "After a Stormy
day, on the Hudson" — Home at 6.30 —
worked for an hour or so on sketch
of a drawing I am to make for N.B.N. Co.
Then went with Geo. to Diving's —
there, listening to his music until
after 11 — About midnight before I
got to bed — M.E. Parker spent
the ev'g with Matt.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 21, 1868

Up at 7.30. Strong wind & Rain — a shocking
day — went down town, stopping at Studio
& got to Ritchie's. 39 Park Row — at 9.30 am.
found him all Ready for me to proceed
with the "biting in" of his sky — a great
thing in, Long — after a drawing by
Darley. I have been dragged
into doing this thing — R. assures
me that he will not hold me Respon-
sible for any mishaps & promises me
$20 — p. day. Unfortunately for me,
every thing has been done in a very
slovenly manner — bad ground, bad
Ruling — all bad — Result, after a
very hard days work, an imperfect
sky, great batches, owing probably
to vibrations of plate or Ruling bar
when Ruling — biting very badly — At
home at 7 — very tired. Stretched
paper to make drawing for the
N.B.N. Co.
