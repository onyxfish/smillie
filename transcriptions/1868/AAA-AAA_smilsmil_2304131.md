## Left Page

### May, Sunday, 31, 1868.

Charming day. Rather dragged
out from yesterdays performance.
Mr. went to Hastings Ch. he
preached from Fift St Luke
20 Ch. 13 V. Ed Parker at our
home, to dinner. At 12 30, I went
to Uncle Aaron's. Afternoon
& evef, glorious, Spent the
time sauntering about.
Evef, some Reading and
chatting — got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Monday, 1, 1868.

Woke at 5 o'clk. Glorious morning.
But as I had not passed a very
good night I dozed, enjoying it, until
near 7 when I got up. Bkfs. at 8.
About 9.30 Uncle A & I started for the
City in the carriage — got to 26 St at
10.15 — went into Keim's, had my hear
cut & head shampoo'd — got to Studio
at 11. Ged down-town trying to get
clear of jury duty — at 12 Jomng Bell
came & took lesson — paid me $20.
Home to lunch at 1. back at 2 — worked
until after 5 on die — graver work &
laid ground to rule sky — Water Color
drawings Ret'd from Phila. at 5.45 went
to N.A. of D. met there Emma Ireland —
walked home with her. Ret'd to Studio —
got die, etc. & at 7.15 started for home.
Brevoort walked up with me — came in
& stayed for an hour. Kate before the
church to night — Toomy at our house
all night — my evef spent between
times trying to write — About 8 o'clk
a severe squall of wind & Rain. To bed at 10.30
