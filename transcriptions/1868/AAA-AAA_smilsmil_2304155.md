## Left Page

### July Saturday, 18 1868

Up late & feeling miserable
— pains all over me, severe
headache & fever. Mother
also, very unwell. Dr. J—
called bet. 11 & 12. In a few
minutes after my feet were
in hot water & altho' the
Ther. was about 90°. I was
seeking for an old fashioned
"Sweat" — I got it & rolled in
big blankets until near 6 P.M.
Before 7. P.M. I got up &
until midnight was in
my big chair. Lizzy
[illegible] came in during the
evng.

## Right Page

### July Sunday, 19 1868

The flies got me up about 5.30
— I retreated to back parlor
& slept in my big chair until
8.30 when I got up & had
some bkfst. — Feel "cruel weak"
& very uncomfortable about
the head — Kept my chair
most of the day, sleeping in
it from about 2 until 5.30.
P.M. About 6.30 we got the
edge of a Thunder storm that
passed west of us.
Evng I wrote a note to S. C. T.
Mother is much better.
