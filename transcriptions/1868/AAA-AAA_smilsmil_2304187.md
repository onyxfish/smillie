## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 24 1868.

And yet another dull, overcast and
miserable sort of a day. After Bkfst.
got palette set & commenced to
work on R.H. foreground of oil study
of "Giant" — but about 9 o'clk the Mudys
& Colemans came along & I went to a
country redding with them at the
Church — The girls had a high frolic
& did not get through with it until
11 o'clk. I worked on until dinner
time when I took a short walk with
Burling — after dinner at Giant
again for a time — at 4, went to
Burling's Room to look over his
Canadian sketches & at 5 went
out with Wood & climbed part way
up Cobble — commencing to rain in —
Red in time for tea — After tea
wrote a letter to Will & at 9 o'clk
went to the parlor where general
jolly times were under way. Played
"Muggins" with much noise and
confusion until after 10 — got to
bed about 11 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 25 1868.

Dismal, pouring Rain — Bkfst.
at 8 & from then until 9.30 all the
confusion & disorder incident to
the setting off of a large party — Mr. &
Mrs. Clay — Mr. & Mrs. & Miss Coleman —
Misses Belle & Mary & Frank Tweedy
& Messrs Burling & Fuechsel went
to NY this mng — a serious loss to
our numbers — I set at once to work
upon an oil sketch 9½×14½ in.
from a pencil outline that I
got while at the upper Ausable
Lake, looking up a Slide — I
was much impressed by the
place & desire to catch something of the spirit. Worked hard
all day getting my sketch about
half done — Evening, still
raining heavily — sat in the
parlor, hugging the stove, altho
it was not cold, & chatting with
Dr. Lewis — Got to bed at about
10 o'clk.
