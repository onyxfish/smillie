## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 5 1868.

Dull overcast m'g with snow before
noon which continued falling heavily
the rest of the day — Got up between
8 & 9 o'clk & had part of a beef tender-
loin for bkfst. Feel so well that I
hope soon to be myself again. I
have dreaded the Army slow fever
again. Dr. called in the m'g. Did
but little during the day — mounted
& touched up photo. pf. by Sarony &
laid out plans of N. B. N. Co's drawing
Ev'g busy Reading newspapers
until after 9 o'clk — am pleased
to hear that the exhibit of my accts
to Water Color Soc'ty last night
was satisfactory & that I am
Re-elected Treas'. for another
year. Got to bed after
10 o'clk, but not to sleep
until after midnight.

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 6 1868.

Snowing & overcast during the m'g
but in the afternoon it cleared off
clear & quite cold. About 11 o'clk
a.m. Young Bill called at the house
to see me — he stayed about ½ an hour.
I got to work upon N.B.N. Co's drawing
but did not feel very brisk nor in
working trim, altho' during the p.m.
I stuck to it pretty closely & got
one section of the drawing well
advanced. Ev'g, did little,
Read some in the papers and
worked some on drawing —
did not get to bed until
after 10 o'clk —
