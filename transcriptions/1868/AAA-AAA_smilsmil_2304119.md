## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 5, 1868.

Chilly, overcast w[ea]t[her], got to
studio before 9 o'clk. & set to
work (2nd day) on drawing for
A.B.N. Co. — S. A.M. Subject, Coffee
Tobacco & Palm — At 1, went home
to lunch, through quite a heavy
Rain — back at 2 & worked until
near 6 o'clk — but could not finish
drawing. Home to tea at 6.30
At 7.30 back to Studio, where I
entertained Water Color Society
at its regular quarterly meeting.
Nothing of special interest.
a bare quorum present, a
miserable even[ing]. Broke up
about 11 o'clk — Home at 11.15
& Right to bed.
Uncle Willie started for
home this p.m.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 6, 1868.

Another disagreeable day — Feel
about half under the weather with
a headache. Got to studio at 9.
Had to sweep up — cigar ashes, etc,
etc — To work at 10, on drawing,
Finished it at 12.30. Home to
lunch at 1, back at 2 — Zimmerman
a model came by appointment
& I made some sketches from
him for a drawing that I am to
make for A.B.N. Co. At 3.30 went
in to Brevoort's & took French lesson.
Home to tea at 7 — at 8 went to
Mr. E. Gavit's with drawing — He
approves it — Rit[?], mounted it
& stretched paper for another
drawing. D. T. Albertson called,
to see Will. He was out — spent
half an hour with me — Will was
today, elected one of the board of
directors of A.B.N. Co. — To bed
at 11.45
