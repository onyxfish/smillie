## Left Page

### March Thursday, 26, 1868

Lovely day, up early & got to Studio
before 9, but with a heavy head —
Sat about unwilling & unable
to begin work. Emma Ireland
came with some passe-partouts
that I am to have framed for her,
at 10 o'clk — with her, went to Fanny
Kimball's & with them walked to a
"Conservatory of Music" & there left
them. Returning to Studio. Found
Mother there — Miss Hawes & a friend
called. At 12 I was obliged to give
up & go home to bed — Miss Masses &
a friend called after I left. I
stayed in bed until 9 o'clk
when I got up, & spread myself
in my big chair until 1.30 a.m.
when again I went to bed.

## Right Page

### March Friday, 27, 1868

Up at 6, feeling pretty well — got to
Studio at 8.30. Washing up dishes &
fixing up for lunch to-morrow. At
about 10 started again upon Moat
Mt. (27th day) oiling out the sky and
[illegible] & painting on foliage at the base
of middle ground Rock. Mr. & Ella
Owen called, So did S.C.F. & Mr. Warrin-
er, leaving photo of 44 F. to be copied —
Took lunch at Studio. Fanny Kimball,
Nettie Jessup & a friend called at 1.30
p.d. a long letter from Meta Gavit —
Walked down to Phil. Rehearsal with
Fanny K. Found Addie D. in box with
Sister Mary — Sat awhile with S.C.F.
& Mr. Sharpless — then Ret'd to box —
Enjoyed the music very much —
After Rehearsal, walked with Addie &
Miss Masses to 14th St. & 8 Ave — then with
Miss M. to 22d St. & 5th Ave, then to Studio &
there found Gay, of Albany. Mr. H. Pardee
& Mr. Thompson had called during p.m.
[illegible]. Raining — at home touching die [illegible]
writing, & studying French — To bed
at 11.
