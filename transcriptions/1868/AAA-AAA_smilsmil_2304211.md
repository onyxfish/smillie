## Left Page

### November Friday, 13 1868.

Beautiful day — got to Studio at 8.30 & at once set to work on [illegible].
By noon had the R.h. anomal & the man painted in — As I
was in a hurry to get to the first Phil. Rehearsal for the season,
I could not finish them as I varnished — Mr. Pate called to see
about my paintings & [illegible] for his new work. Took lunch at
the Studio & at 2 o'clk. went to the Academy of Music & heard
the Phils. Rehearse the Eroica Symphony — a Berlioz & a
[illegible] — Saw a number of friends speak to — back to Studio at 5 —
cleaned up for the day — home to tea at 6 & at 7. 45. at [illegible]'s Opera
House by appointment with [illegible] to see his drop curtain & La Belle
Helene — was not overwhelmingly absorbed in either — out at 10.30 —
home before 11 & to bed soon after —

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 14 1868.

Still fine weather — got to Studio before
9 o'clk — walked down with Charlie Adams — Spent the day fixing
up my Summer Studies, varnishing some, painting out the edges of others
& putting strips of wood about them.
Leila Martin, her Cousin Sarah [illegible] called at about 10 — Home to
lunch at 1 — back at 2 — walking down with Mr. Charlie Adams —
P.M. Calls from Mr. Morse (Ruth Morse)
Diven & others — Home at
6.15, feeling pretty well tired out — Spent the evening Reading & got to bed at 10.30 —
