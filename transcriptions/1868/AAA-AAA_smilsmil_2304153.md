## Left Page

### July Tuesday, 14 1868.

As hot as ever. While at breakfast Uncle Willie + aunt a. arr'd, just in from a trip through the Provinces. Got to Studio about 9 o'clk + set to work etching on sky. Succeeded better almost than I had hoped so that I determined to etch in the whole of it, at first I intended to Rule it. Home to lunch at 1, back at 2.15 + tried my best to finish the etching but could not do it, altho I worked faithfully until 7 o'clk. Charley Adams called bet. 5 + 6 o'clk + took away some large Yosemite photos that he lent to me some days ago. The eve'g spent at home hard at work grinding up my moist Colors + getting my Color box Ready — To bed at 11.30

4 days on A.M.B.N. Co's die —

## Right Page

### July Wednesday, 15 1868.

No mitigation of heat. Ther 89° when I got up in the m'g, 97° at the Studio + 88° now, at bed time, with a little air stirring, which is positive refreshment. M'g got up with a headache — On my way to Studio, stopped at a hardware store + got all of the necessary fixings to complete my sketching board. Got to studio at 10.30 + by sticking to it, got my etching finished at 4.15 o'clk — Then Right home + to bed, where I stayed until near 9 o'clk P.M. Sat in my chair until after 12 — To bed about 1 o'clk A.M. Charl quite sick owing to exposure to the heat and imprudence —

5 days on A.B.N. Co's die

Uncle W. + aunt a. went to Co. to-day.
