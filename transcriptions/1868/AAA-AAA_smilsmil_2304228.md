## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 9 1868

Gusty, cold, fickle sort of day. Some beautiful skies \+ snow squalls \- Got to Studio at 9 o'clk \+ set to work painting in man standing by one of the oxen \- [illegible] came in for a little while in the m[illegible]. Tom Mulford came in with Mr. Eloise Post just before 12 o'clk \+ took us quite by surprise I went home to lunch at 1.30 \- back at 2.30 \+ by working closely about 4 o'clk \- Called my 2nd fat oxen painting finished \- During the P.M., I glazed \+ dragged fresh color over all the ground. Every, about 8 o'clk went to the St. Nicholas Hotel \+ stayed with [illegible] M. until after 11 o'clk. \- Then walked up home, stopping in at Snedicor's to have a "private view" of Mayer's new picture "Good words" \- Found a lot of the fellows, mostly "boss" men quite jolly with punch \+ cigars. Home at 12.15 \+ to bed at once.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 10 1868

Up about 7.30 \- About 8.30 went to Brooks Bros. and ordered a suit of clothes. then, at 9.30 went to the St. N. \+ found Faw. waiting for me. as agreed, we went right in to bk fo\- had a jolly good one, Venison Steaks &c. \- at 10.30, we started up B'dway, stopping at Schaus's, Snedicor's \+ Knoedler's \- then rode up to Manje leaving her as we passed the Studio at 11.30 \- I determined the size \+ character of the water Color drawing that I intend to make for the coming [illegible] Charcoaling it out very roughly 25½ × 30½ in \- Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 2.15 \+ started upon a more careful Charcoal drawing for my water color \- Mr. Knoedler \+ Mr. Coleman made us calls \- Every at Home \- Worked on some old pencil studies \+ got to bed about 10
