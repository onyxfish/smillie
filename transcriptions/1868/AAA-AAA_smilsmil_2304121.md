## Left Page

### May Saturday, 9 1868.

Up at 6 o'clk — glorious day. Got to studio at 8.45 — looked over my french lesson. Rec'd a call from Mlle d'Asnoy at 9.30 went into Brevort's Room & took french lesson — Back at 11 & set to work on wood drawing for A.B.N. Co — Young Bell came soon after 12 to take a lesson & J.H. Dolph called. Home to lunch abt 1. Uncle Wm Turrill — Tommy & Aunt Toley at lunch — back at 2.15. Young Bell at studio until 4 o'clk. I worked on drawing until 6.30 — Home to tea at 7 — Eveg spent reading until 9.30 — got to bed before 10.
Mary went to Uncle A's this p.m. to make a visit.

## Right Page

### May Sunday, 10 1868.

Lovely m'g, but April like — Just at church time quite a shower came up. With Charl, went to Hastings ch. D.H. preached an excellent sermon from Matt. 27 Ch. 22? v. "What shall I do then with Jesus, which is called Christ" — At 12.30 started for Uncle Aaron's, getting there at about 1.45. Mary, Uncle Wm & Aunt H — there, dinner at 2 — sauntering over the grounds until 4.30, then walked to 8th Ave. cars with Mary & Aunt H. Mary went home. Aunt H & I rec'd to Uncle A's — got caught in slight shower — Tea at 6 — Beautiful Sunset — out until quite dark — then spent remainder of eveg in general chatting & got to bed at 10 o'clk.
