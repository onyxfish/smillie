## Left Page

### May, Saturday, 23, 1868

Awakened this mrg by
Rain up on the window. The
Regular weather back again.
Went down to City in 8.24 train
got to Pitchie's at 9.20 and
again set to work on etching
but under a cloud with threatening headache. Worked until
3.30 when I gave up & started
for home, getting there a little
after 4. Tumbled at once
into bed & lay there until 11
o'clk p.m. when I got up
& went to bed properly.
Rain & wind still driving
like mad.

5½ days on Pitchie's stay
short of time to-day, 2½ hours.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday, 24, 1868

Still misgling & misting.
Feel a little bright in the upper
story from yesterday's experience
mrg, went & heard Dr. Hastings
text, 119 Psalm 2º. v. The discourse
I know was excellent, but I
did not half hear it — Home
to dinner. Afternoon, wrote
a letter to Meta Gavit — At
5 o'clk. walked up to Central
Park. About there until after
7 o'clk. Enjoying the birds and
their singing — A drizzle set
in just before I left — Home
& took tea alone at 8 o'clk.
Had the Company of Mary &
Dr. I. until I went to bed about
10.30.
