## Left Page

### June Friday, 26 1868.

Got up with a miserable headache.
After bkfst, worked a little for
father, outlining on a dag.ª from one
of Schussele's drawings. at 8,45
Started for down-town & got to
Pitchie's at 9,30. Set to work burn-
ing & managed to keep up until
1, when I gave up, went home, and
to bed where I stayed until 5.
Then up & had tea at 6 — The girls
off all to-day to Staten Island on
a S.S. Pic-nic. I spent the evng
seeing to getting sketching traps
Ready for work. — went up to Bur-
ggrafs etc etc — Glorious evening —
Cool, breezy & clear — the moonlight
& odor of Roses & honeysuckle made
my Room particularly delightful.
Got to bed about midnight.

12 days on Pitchie's sky.
½ a day to-day.

## Right Page

### June Saturday, 27 1868.

Up at 6 o'clk — glorious day, hot
but breezy. Got paint & painting
sketching board & frame — had
bkfst. at 7 — Mother & Geo went riding
with Mr. & Mrs. Dr. Thompson. I
went down town to Pitchie's, got
there at 9 o'clk — burnishing again
until 1 — then went to Parker's to
lunch — Met father — then to A.B.N. Co
at 2, back to Pitchie's & worked
closely, burnishing until near 6.
Then home — at 7,30 went to Schmitt's
& ordered box for water-color tiles —
then to Kimball's in 22d. St. House
shut up — then to Zabriskies in
21st St. Saw S.C.7, & Kate 7—
Pleasant every. Until 9,45 then
home. — Did not get to bed
until near midnight.

13 days on Pitchie's sky.
Full time to-day.
