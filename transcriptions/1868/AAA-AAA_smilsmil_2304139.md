## Left Page

### June Tuesday, 16 1868

Up before 6 — sky overcast and day warm — Spent most of the time before bkfst. catching Bumble bees for Mr.
Beers — She is painting apple blossoms
+ wants some bees for models. Up
at 7.45 — At 8.30, Geo. + I took stage and
I got to studio at 9.40 — Set to work
etching on Ottawa die (4th day) At
12 very heavy Rain — home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — more very
heavy Rain + thunder + lightning between
4+6 — Finished my etching + stopped
ont. Ready for biting to-morrow. by
6 o'clk — Home to Tea at 6.30 — Every
Mr. Node + Harry in for a little while —
Wrote a letter to Mr. Thompson of
N. Conway in answer to a note of his —
Got my french lesson + wrote my
exercise + got to bed at 10.30.
Terribly close, sticky weather.

## Right Page

### June Wednesday, 17 1868

Suffocatingly hot + muggy day — got
to studio at 9.15 + at once set to
work biting in die for Ottawa folks.
Closely at it all day with the
exception of french lesson from
4.30 to 4.30 P.m. — Home to lunch
at 1 — back at 2 — too hot to walk.
Got through with my biting about
6 o'clk — Everything apparently
very successful — good line
+ judicious stopping out —
Home to Tea before 7 — At 8 o'clk
Geo. + I went up to Mr. Harris Pardee's
on 47th St., somewhat to see if he
intends buying our Academy
pictures, as he has spoken of it —
Mr. P. + daughter out of town. Saw
M. P. + Mr. + Mrs. Dr. Thompson —
Had a pleasant chatty call —
left at 10 o'clk — Stopped at Haffy's
— had some cream — home and
to bed at 11.
