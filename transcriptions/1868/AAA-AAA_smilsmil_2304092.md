## Left Page

### March, Thursday 12, 1868

Up at 6.30 — after bkfst wrote
a letter to Tom Shearman, got
to Studio at 9.15 and set to work
on the R.h. cor. of Moat Mt. (22d day)
Painted the rocks a a little
over the pine tree above — the
finishing up of the 2d painting.
I am pretty well satisfied, but
new difficulties present themselves
as I bring it nearer a finish. I
am now fearful that I cannot
avoid the effect of a general jumble
in the foreground — Home to lunch
at 1 — back at 2 — Rain commenced
soon after — Some excitement among
our Studios on Report that Lazarus
has hired the upper part of our
building — Evening at home —
wrote a long letter to Tom M.
and got to bed just before
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 13, 1868

Dull & disagreeable day — got
to studio at 9 o'clk — and for the
day, devoted myself to working
up a photo of H.Y. Fessenden —
This I have undertaken to do
for Sarah — If I do not succeed
in pleasing her, I will at least
have some practice in face
drawing — Home to lunch at
1 — back at 2 — and worked
faithfully all the rest of the
day on photo — Home to tea
at 7 — Evening spent studying
French & writing exercises —
To bed at 11.
