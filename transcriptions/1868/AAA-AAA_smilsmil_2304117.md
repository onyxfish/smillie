## Left Page

### May Friday, 1 1868.

Up at 6.30 — Clear day & rather cool — Got to Studio a little before 9 o'clk & at once got to work on 20×26 — (9th day) and by close work — at 5.40 P.M. got the first painting in finished — I have been very careful. too careful — am in some respects pleased, but in general disappointed — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Many & Amelia Butler, Maggie Gunn & 3 others in Fairman's Studio sewing on a S.S. Banner — they gave us a call — Jno. Innes moved into Studio on our floor to-day. Home to tea at 7.20 Spent the eve'g scanning Buchin's "Norwood". Uncle Willie arr'd from Phila — Got to bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 2 1868.

Miserable cold day, with heavy pouring Rain — Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. Lighted fire & studied French lesson until about 10 o'clk. when I went to Brevoort's Room & took lesson — there until 11.30. Home to lunch through the Rain and back at 2 o'clk — Worked as long as I could see Retouching Photo. of H.F. Fessenden — Home to tea at 7 — Will brought material for a Bank note drawing. I made a Rough Sketch & run up with it to Jno. E. Gavit, 127 W. 48th. He told me to go ahead & wants me to engrave the die — As I'm very hard up, I delightedly gave a Reluctant consent —
To bed shortly after 10 —
