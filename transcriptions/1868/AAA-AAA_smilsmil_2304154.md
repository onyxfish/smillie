## Left Page

### July, Thursday, 16, 1868.

Routed out of bed at 5. a.m. by the
flies, went down to back parlor —
& slept in my big chair until
7.30. Then dressed for depot, but
not feeling well. At 10. a.m. went
to studio got a few things & home
again, as I did not feel able to
bite in my etching to-day.
Spent the day fixing up my
water color sketching board —
finished & stretched paper
up on it — all right, but it is
unnecessarily heavy. The
weather is still wonderfully hot
& we are all pretty well played
out —
Every, lying about until
about 9 when I commenced
lying about less promiscuously.

## Right Page

### July, Friday, 17, 1868.

Did not get up until near 8. a.m.
my bowel troubles are no better
& again I decided that it would
be safer to stay at home — So
spent the day trying my new
arrangements of water colors
& stretching, sketching board —
got off a couple of slight
sketches that encourage me
very much — I am now all
impatience to get at my work
out of doors — I am afraid that
I will break down before I can
get my engraving not finished
Well quietly doing nothing &
got to bed before 10.
