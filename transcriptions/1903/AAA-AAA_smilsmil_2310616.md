## Left Page

### Wed. May 6, 1903

**Weather:** Very pleasant & mild  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant & mild - To studio about 10 after going to Douglas's, & Miller's - I worked on Borden plate but was much interrupted by call of Douglas & Carpenter who was putting in new door - At 12.45 I went to bank & made deposit - then home to lunch - & to studio at 2 worked on Borden plate till after 4 - then went to Kimmel & Voigt - Canal St. & saw prints that H\. V— is taking from plates by Etching class - after 5 walked up Elm St. to note the shameful condition of the street & took car at 10th St. & Rode home Dinner at 6.30 - W. Goring dined with us & spent the eveg. I worried over accts & wrote checks.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 7, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Our wedding anniversary.

Some sunshine - some shadow & a very little shower - To studio about 10 & worked on Borden plate till ) - home to lunch & back soon after 2 - worked till near 5 - then a short walk - dinner at 6.30 - Eveg reading newspaper & writing notes
my work during the day was interrupted at different times by requirements of workmen on new partition etc - in the Maynard studio.

Twenty two years ago to-day Annie & I were married.
