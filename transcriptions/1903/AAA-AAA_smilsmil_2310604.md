## Left Page

### Sun. April 12, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day — o'clk. at 9.30 It was about 11 where Jim, Ralph + I rode to 100 "St. + Mad. Ave. — Took a look at the outside of W. Sena hospital, then to the green houses + saw the flower show — then thro' the Park — out at 90" St. + down Fifth Ave, looking at the houses Jim. left us at 85 "St. + rode home — We went on a number of blocks further + then we took a car — went to Charlie's + got correct time — then home to dinner at 2 — after that I read paper till 5 — then Geo. + I walked to Mad. Ave + 43d St. — met Champney — Geo. left us — C + I went on to Century Club — I got 't 4 0 — Taylor's "Ancient Ideals" + I read in it for some time — It was 9 o'clk. when I got home — Charlie there —

## Right Page

### Mon. April 13, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Katharine + Bessie went to Po'k.

Pleasant but threatening — I had some messages to attend to, so it was after 10 when I got to studio — Worked on Borden plate till 1 o'clk. — Weitenkampf came in for about half an hour — is anxious to have me arrange a set of pfs. from my complete works for Print Dep't. of Public Library — Home to lunch — after that went to N.A. of D. — Etching Class — just started on Prize competition work — found 1 it work making preliminary sketch from model — old woman — I was with them for more than an hour — then rode down to the Carnegie Bldg. in 57 'St. + saw large mural decorations just completed by Blashfield + Cox for a Savings Bank in Cleveland — Ohio. I like the Blashfield one — that by Cox I do not like altho' there is some good work on it — then to Geo.'s studio — met Mr. Messerole there — walked home — 6 o'clk when I got there — dinner at 6.30 — after 8 I went to Edw. Cooks — 37 "St. met there, Geo., Walter + Louise Cook — Mrs. S Prague — Miss Williams + Jno. Valentine — I was there for an hour or more — then home. Charlie there —

Katharine + Bessie went to Po'K — 3.30 — to visit for two or three days.
