## Left Page

### Tues. May 12, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still exceeding fine weather — to studio at 10 \+ worked on Borden plate, but as on preceding days, subject to considerable disturbance — home to lunch at 1 \+ back at 2 \+ spent a couple of hours taking prints from Borden plate the result being, on the whole, satisfactory. I think that now there are no difficulties that cannot be overcome in a few days — the plate will, after all my work out, never be a thing to be proud of — Had a short walk with Geo. after \+ then home — dinner — Mary ret. from Po K. during the afternoon — Miss Abbott called at studio at about 3. P.M. Evelf. Chael came in — I wrote a letter to Jim.

B. 4 h.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 13, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

our dreadful good weather continues — what shall we do for rain? To studio at 9.30 \+ worked till 12 on Borden plate — then home \+ soon after went to N.A. of D — Amsterdam Ave. \+ 109th getting there about 1 — at lunch oat [illegible] D. aud. French \+ had a very pleasant time — Meeting called at about 2 — Saml. Calman came \+ we sat together.

B 2½ h.
