## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 30, 1903

**Weather:** Snow in the early a.m. clearing to bright sunshine + cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at usual time + spent the m't painting on little "Stormy Eve't. Fall" - Painted in Po'keepsie many years ago - Home to lunch - To N.A. étching class at 2. 30 + worked there till near 5 - Then to studio - cleaned palette - washed brushes - home at 6 - dinner - Evening loafing - wrote note to Emma J - + sent it with tickets to N.a.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 31, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day - Got to studio at about 10 + painted all the m't on "Passing Showers". I Tackled the big cumulus cloud on the left end of picture, repainted it solidly and changed its shape - Then modified or suppressed many minor forms making it in effect + color broader + quieter. At 12 went to Scribner's + had a pleasant call on Mr. H\[illegible\] Smith - Then to 2d. Nat'l. Bk + deposited $100 check from Maynard + $30 chk. from Geo - Home to lunch soon after 1 - o after lunch, to studio + again painted on Showers - going over to right hand end of sky - cleaned up at dusk + had a rest - Home to dinner - Eve't. \[illegible\] went to a dance - Saw him, for the first time, in his dress suit in which he looked very well - Then Ralph + I went to studio - he had been there in the afternoon + in my dark room developed a couple of dozen films - In the eve't we tacked them on a board to dry - To bed at about 11.
