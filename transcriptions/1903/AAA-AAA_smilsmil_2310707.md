## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 4, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still beautiful weather - To studio before 10 \+ painted all the m'f. on I getting to the end of this painting. It was 1.30 when I got home to lunch. Miss Kate Vanstorne there - At 2.30 I went down to Wall St. to Winter Smillie where young Winter very civilly sent to Custom Ho. \+ got pass onto Pier, so that when Charl. arrives I can get to him at once. Then to Findler Wiber \+ got Diary for 1904 - Then to 22d. St. to the Kenny W.C. Co. where I saw the value from my W.C. \+ gave directions. Then to studio \+ cleaned palette. Home about 5.30 - dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g Reading Harper for Nov.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 5, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain last night \+ in the m'f. - clearing for awhile about mid-day - more rain in the afternoon - high wind \+ cold at night. To studio about 9.30 - Painted on 14×18 \- home to lunch \+ back to studio for a little while. \+ home again about 4 - Troubled with giddiness \+ distress in head - I lay on my bed Till 6 - dinner - Eve'g - went with Katharine to Carnegie Hall, Concert of Boston Symphony Soct). - where I heard some old friends. - Home soon after 10.
