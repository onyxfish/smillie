## Left Page

### Tues. May 26, 1903

**Weather:** A clouded & cooler day but no sign of rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- After a troublous night I got up feeling pretty seedy - it was 11 o'clk before I went to studio - Met Zimmerman who had called to see me about etching - After he left I fussed awhile about the studio - then went to Scribner's - saw Mr. J. Smith for a little while - then to "The Times" (London) office - 2.25 Fourth Ave about settling finally for Melf Bird's New Edt. - Then Totton's + paid Ice Cream bill, then home to lunch - back to studio before 3 - very soon Fred'. Stahr came with a lot of plates + proofs + he took up all that remained of the afternoon - I had a short walk before going home to dinner at 6.30 - Katharine + Bessie went to Charl's with Miss Helen Howells to dine at 9 o'clk - Mary + I followed them - Miss it - sang very pleasantly + Charl gave us some music on the Orchestrelle - It was midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 27, 1903

**Weather:** Clouds + a very little rain in the early m'f - growing warmer  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- Wrote some notes in the m'f after b'f't. Then to studio - got Ryan's man started on changing gas pipe in lower hall, Studio + then commenced seriously the clearing out of Studio's closet where I have my files of Catalogues, Pamphlets etc. - Home to lunch at 1 - back soon after + continued the work till near 5, then home + had a rest before dinner time - Eve'f. at 8.30 went to Century - last meeting for the season of Com. on Admissions - It was midnight when I got home.

Mr. Fred'. Crang lunched with us
