## Left Page

### Fri. June 5, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Less smoke than yesterday - cool + overcast. While at bkfst: a telegram came from J.H. Maghee saying - Dont come to Keene - fires all about - have moved family + furniture to Tahawus House - Stories pub'd. in the newspapers added to our consternation - after bkfst: a note came from Charl asking us to go to him - he had Rec'd. a telephonic message from Mr. Hardenburgh - Jersey City - Repeating a message Rec'd. from Maghee, same in effect as we had Rec'd. I went to Gr'd. Cent. Station with message for M - leter, the Operator sent me word that the message could not get farther than Saratoga. I wrote a letter to M- also one to F.W. Morton + one to Mrs. Barhydt - At 12 I went to studio - Miss Ellen Rosenfeles came - I Talked with her + promised to write letters for her to R.W. Gilder - Ge. Haven Putnam - Ripley Hitchcock \+ My L. Smith - She wishes to get Position as Reader + Proof Reader in some Publishing house. Home to lunch at 1 - after that, to Gr'd. Cent. St. met Nellie (Geo's wife) there - Then home + wrote to Ralph - + a second note to Maghee - Then a walk in the streets + to the Century + looked over the eve'g papers to see if there was any thing new about Keene fires - Then home - dinner at 6.30 - eve'g wrote to Jim - his birth-day to-morrow - at 9.30 went to Charl's - Mary + the girls there - home at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Sat. June 6, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim's birthday.

Overcast - growing warmer - some veiled sunshine about the middle of the day - light shower about 9.30 p.m. I got to Himmel + Voigt's about 9.30 + was there till about 11 while 5H V. took four p.p. from the Borden place - I am not satisfied either with the p.p. or the place - to studio \+ Tacked the p.p. to stretching boards - home to lunch at 1 - all alone - Mary + the girls having gone with Charl to the Bron. Zoo, + Botanical Gardens - To studio at 3 + worked on B - place till near 6 - then home - dressed - + at 7.15 went to Charl's where I joined the afternoon party + enjoyed a swell dinner - at 9 o'clk I went to Century - monthly meeting - + had an agreeable time till near midnight - Then I walked home with Avery + Collens -

B. 5h.
