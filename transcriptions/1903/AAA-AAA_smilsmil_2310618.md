## Left Page

### Sun. May 10, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another fine day \- Bkfs\. at 9.30 after a good scrub \- Rub\-down \+ exercise \- After bkfs\. went to studio, opened up windows \+ then rode up to N.A. f.D. Meeting of Council \- Instructor \+ School Committee \- Miss Nathalie Chadwick got First Hon. Mention \- no silver medal was given \- one Bronze med. First Prize \- Scholles, Second \- Sandrescgki got Hon. Mention \- In painting O'Malley got p\. \+ the new Scholarships \- 2 yrs. in Europe \- $750 per an. went to Maurice Steerne. All of these awards interested me because I know the young fellows \- at about 12.30 I walked down to Riverside Ave. I called at Mrs. Absalynnis to leave my card \- met at the door her sister, Miss Josephine Goldmark \- I walked down Riverside Drive to 87th S\. Then to Amsterdam Ave. \+ took car \- went in to Charles\! \- a few minutes before dinner \- Had a good nap after dinner \- at 6.30 went to studio for awhile. Home \- Fred Young at tea \- Ev\. f. D\. S\. Clair Smith was called to see if he could tell what is the matter with Emma B. who is confined to her bed with swelling and pain in the Region of her stomach \- Smith is puzzled \+ will call to\-morrow with his stethiscope \- I wrote notes to Mrs. Chadwick \+ her daughter Nathalie.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 11, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very fine day \- To studio's at 10. but had to return home to get Maynard's key for electrician \- Went up on roof to see oil\-tank boat on fire on East River \- Remarkable sights of smoke \- Worked on Borden plate. Maurice Steerne came to hear about the Etching Class awards. Home to lunch at 1 \+ then to dock foot of E. 35th S\. \+ for half an hour watched the still burning tank boat \- the burning oil running over the water \- \+ ten fire boats pumping masses of water upon it \+ drowning it with difficulty \- It was a fierce fight \+ intensely interesting \- got to studio at 2.30 \+ worked till 5 \- then out \+ walked the streets \- down to Mad. Sq\. \+ home at 6 \- Jene Atwater lunched with us \- dinner at 6.30 \- Ev\. f. \- Charl came in \-
