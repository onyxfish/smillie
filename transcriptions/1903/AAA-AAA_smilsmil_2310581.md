## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 25, 1903

**Weather:** Clear \- growing milder  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \- growing milder \- got to studio about 9.30 I worked all the m'f on B. plate \- Katharine T\- had a model \+ worked \- I had a short walk before lunch \- back to studio about 2.30 \+ worked till 4 \- these feeling the discomfort in my chest, which is much agravated by my position while sitting at my work \- arms raised on desk \- head down \- it may be Rheumatism, neuralgia or something of that kind \- I went out and walked awhile \- then home \+ had a nap before dinner \- Josephine B. Smillie dropped in \+ dined with us \- Every I went to Century \- Com. on Admissions \- Had an interesting \+ protracted meeting \- M. \[illegible\]. Edmunds passed the Com. without trouble \+ after the meeting I wrote notes informing &c, Chas \+ Maitland Armstrong \- It was 12.30 before I got to bed.

B. 4.30 h.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 26, 1903

**Weather:** Clear \+ very pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \+ very pleasant barring the chill in the air from melting snow. I got to studio a little before 10 \+ till 12.30 etched on Borden plate \- Then I went out \+ sauntered on the sunny side of Park Ave. till after 1 \- then home to lunch \- After that I feel the effects of last night's late hours so heavily that I lay down on my bed \+ slept till 4 o'clk. I could not go to studio very much to my regret for another hour of work would have finished the etching stage on the Borden plate \- but I can no longer do as I wish \- I can work only as I have strength. At 4 o'clk I went to H. D. Allen's as per appointment \+ he put the new set in my mouth to determine "bite" \+ length of teeth. walked down thro' Park to 59th St \+ 5th Ave \+ thence by car to studio \- varnished the back of my plate \- home at 6 \- dinner at 6.30 Carlson came soon after 9 \- I gave him check for $24.\- being payment in full for 12 sessions \- Nov. 1st to 26th inclusive.

B. 2.30 h.
