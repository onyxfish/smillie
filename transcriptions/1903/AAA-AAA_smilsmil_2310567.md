## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 28, 1903

**Weather:** Dull, foggy + damp  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, foggy + damp - for awhile during the middle of the day there was an appearance of clearing, but it was a failure. After bkft. wrote a note to Mr. Barhy & acknowledging her note of thanks to me for what I had done to establish the Dept. of Etching in Mafd - Schools - To studio later than usual - Repainted an oil sketch 9×16 - mountain valley view - intended for wedding present for Sally Cook. - At 12.30 went to bank + borrowed $500. hopeless work, fighting to keep my head above water financially - To lunch at 1.30 - To studio soon after 2 + painted till after 4. - Then to the barbers + had hair cut - Home at 6 - dinner - Every at 8.30 to Con. on Admissions - Century - first meeting of the year - some new members - Mr. Sidney Smits. Dr. Herrick - Bolton Jones - [illegible] Smith. - Mr. Prentice made Chairman + Prof Goodenough Sect. - Social atmosphere seemed to me a little less chilly - a very long session - did not get home till midnight.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 29, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another dull, damp day - much nearer rain than yesterday. Spent some time after bkft. in writing checks in payment of bills - did not get to studio till about 11. Then painted awhile on little pictures intended for Catharine Cook - + a little while on an old interior wood scene near the Huddle, Lake George - Home to lunch at 1 - at 2 went down town - first to Tax office + found that valuation on 110 E. 38. has been raised from $21000 to $44000 - + studio from $7500 to $11000 - This means a raising of my Tax on house - under most favorable rate promised - 50% - Then to a B.N. Co - 86 Truely Place - saw Freeland and enquired about calletbers - saw Aug. Shepherd. Then to 21 State St. + had conference with Clarence Smith about my Will - Then to studio about 4.30 - cleaned palette and washed brushes - Dinner at 6.30 - Working with accts. till Carlson came - drew check for him - ten visits from Jan. 7. to this every inclusive @ $2.00
