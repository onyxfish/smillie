## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 17, 1903

**Weather:** A wet day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A wet day - I spent it at studio finishing the work of the cleaning woman of yesterday. Home to lunch at 1. \+ back at about 3 \+ continued my work till near 6 - Then home. Miss Mg't Buck, of Po'k. came to spend Sunday with Bessie. \+ Mr. Fred'n Young dined with us \+ spent the ev'g. I read papers in my room.

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 18, 1903

**Weather:** High wind. Clouds \+ sunshine  **Temp:** [illegible]

up early, had my bath \+ bkf't. \+ at 10.02 started for New-Haven, getting there at about noon. Trolley car up to Chapel St. cor. High No. 1076 Chapel - There I found Jim in his room \+ after a few minutes we walked up the hill to 477 Prospect St. - Wm. \+ Katie Elkin's. There we stayed to dinner \+ till near 4 o'clk - Then walked back to Jim's room with \+ for the best part of two hours I looked over his text \+ note books in his various studies. I was much gratified at the evidence of interest in, \+ understanding of the subjects he is studying - about 6.30 we walked down to R. R. station \+ there he left me - The 7.10 (Boston Exp.) was 15 minutes late on leaving New Haven \+ 20 m. late in getting to Grand Cen'l. Station I went directly home - had some supper \+ to bed.
