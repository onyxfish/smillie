## Left Page

### Sun. March 15, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather rather sullen after a pleasant m'f. — getting ready for a change. Bkf. at 9.30 — After bkf. I wrote for a while on biographical sketch of father — then to studio, opened windows — then walked on Park Ave — then to Charl's — he is bunged up with a cold — then home to dinner at 2 — Had my regular Sunday afternoon nap — tea at 6.30 — eve'f finished sketch of father's life — went to Charl's where Mary, Kate \+ Bessie were — Home at 10 —

## Right Page

### Mon. March 16, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

miss Buck ret. to Po'K.

A dull day with raw, N.E. wind — to studio before 10 \+ worked till near 1 o'clk on Borden place — then home to lunch — back at 2 — soon after that Miss Grace Buck \+ Katharine T. came to studio \+ for half an hour I showed my paintings \+ sketches — then Miss B. — went to station \+ back to Po'K. — I worked on B. place till 5 o'clk — then walked on Park Ave. till (counting the blocks) I had walked three miles — then home at 6.15 — dinner 6.30 — wrote anote to Sallie Munroe accepting invitation to dinner Thurs. eve'f. Carson came at 9.5 \+ gave me special treatment for constipation — this is at his request — because he says he has never failed before —

B. 5 h.
