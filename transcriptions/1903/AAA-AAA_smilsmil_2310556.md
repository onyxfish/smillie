## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 6, 1903

Boys returned to school.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day \- dark, raw, wet. At night it cleared quite cold \- After bkfs. I wrote a letter to Clarence Smith about my Will \- it delayed me + I did not get to studio till nearly 11 \- worked on Borden pl. which goes dreadfully slow. Carlson called + offered me a considerable discount on regular terms if I would again take massage treatment \- I agreed to try it, he to come to me every other mng at 8 \- commencing to-morrow. Home to lunch after having gone to Bank + paid several small bills \- to studio at 2 + worked till after 4 \- Then went to Kleppel's + took him two pls. Morton + Morgan \- + presented them to him \- there I met Saml. Hollyer \- I had not seen him for many years \- seems to be a hale old fellow. Home at 6 \- dinner \- a good one, as a send off for the boys \- Soon after 8 \- we went to Gr. Central Station Ralph left for Tarrytown on the 8.35 + Jim went to New Haven on the 9 o'clk. Train \- then home + toasted myself in front of the fire \-

B. 3 h.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 7, 1903

Carson.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another dull day with weak + watery attempts at snow \- Carson came at 8 + rubbed + pummeled me till 9 \- At 9.30 I had bkfs. \- + at 10.30 got to studio. I am not altogether satisfied with this morning experiment \- It upsets things very much \-. Worked on Borden plate till 1 o'clk. Steene came in \- lunch \- back at 2.30 \- worked till 4 \- could not see any longer \- went to Ryan's + Talmenfell's, Katze' + Miller's \+ paid bills \- whole oin part \- Then home \- dinner \- Sue f. Charl came in \- I worked awhile over my accts.

B. 4 h.
