## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 1, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Carlson.

A nasty day, dark, wet + raw — I awoke at 6 o'clk. with severe headache but was able to get up at 9 + get thro' with my scrub + have a light bkft. after 10 — I read newspaper + did a little writing till 1 o'clk — then went to Charl's + he went with me to studio — saw the picture that I have just repainted for him + approved it — Home to dinner at 2 — my headache Ret? after 4 + I went to bed + was there till 8 — then up + prepared for Carlson who came at 9.15 + gave me massage for an hour.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 2, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unpleasant m't. clearing towards night — head still troubling me — I wrote some notes after bkft. It was after 11 when I got to studio — two pictures for Barnard Club were called for — "Morning over the Gothics, Adirondacks" — "little sketch — Welf on Raquette lake." — I commenced work again on Borden etching after a week of vacation painting in oil — I worked a little before lunch at 1 — back soon after 2 — Young Staho called — I gave up work soon after 4 + wandered in the streets for a while then home — dinner — Ret? Read newspaper for a time, then worried over the bank's balancing of my pass book — but my head was too heavy to make any progress — so, I went to bed.
