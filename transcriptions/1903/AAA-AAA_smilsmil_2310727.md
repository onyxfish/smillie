## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 14, 1903

**Weather:** Bright, cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold day \- After bkfs'. went to Miller's in 40th S! \+ got two back boards for paintings \- was detained there some time \+ got to Studio late \- The day was spent in putting the last touches on those small paintings that I intend to send to N.A. in 24th." A Pool in the woods" "Gathering Clouds" \+ An Adirondack Lake". Home to lunch \- back at 2. \+ worked till 4. Then to Clausen's \+ ordered frame for 16 × 20 \- unchristened \- then to Ochme's to see some Lenbach's. Thence a short walk \+ home at 6. dinner at 6.30 \- Evell, added a few words to Jane's letter to Mary \+ posted it. Charl came in \+ spent the eveg with us.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 15, 1903

**Weather:** Clear \+ cold \- Strong NW. wind  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \+ cold \- Strong NW. wind \- After bkfs'. I wrote a letter to Will Parker, of Scranton which delayed my getting to the studio till about 10.30 \- my work for the day was desultory \- Glazed \+ painted on Hollyhocks \- the one that was original for my Megg tint plate \- also on my old painting "Hazel-brook \- Apl'e." I finished my days work soon after 4 \+ then took a little Twilight time to think of what my effort at painting has so far accomplished The result of the thinking was discouraging \- I looked at the three specimens that I expect to send to N.A. 24th \+ had to admit to myself that they were not unlike colored Photographs \- heavy, monotonous, unimaginative \- They are not works of art, or sentiment or, in its best sense, color \- The conclusion was depressive because I feel sure it was just \- so I went home \+ tried to get a little nap before dinner \- Evell. at 8.30 I went to Mendelssohn Hall in W. 40th S! \+ heard Mr. Cook tell how he failed to get to the top of Mt. Mc. Kinley last summer \- the Route \+ its difficulties illustrated by a great number of fairly good Photo stereopticon slides \- He is not an interesting speaker, but his subject was to me \- out at 9.45 \- home \+ to bed.
