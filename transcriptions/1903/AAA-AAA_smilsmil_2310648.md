## Left Page

### Sat. July 11, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The laundress left us.

Very close + muggy — turning to showers before noon — change, p.m. clearing to cool. Delightful moonlight night. Ther. in my bedroom 65°. Miss Carlson left us on the stage at 7.15 — an expensive experiment — she goes because she is love sick + cannot live away from her "fellow" — otherwise she is inefficient + unsatisfactory. After bkft. I helped Moody move furniture into his room in little new bldg. Then finished letter to Jim + at 11 took it to P.O. \+ got mail — then to Ferd. Deeds's + ordered cobble stones to line road with in front of house. Home to dinner at 1 — had a small mess of moderately good peas from my garden — also a second mess of spinach — To hotel at 3 — at 4 I walked into Reserve to the Gill brook Reservoir that has been broken out + beyond to where men were repairing the washed out road. Home at 6 — Evg., rolled in my blanket, I lounged + slept in the north Piazza — enjoying the cool night air.

Got a card from Jim — 6" vis. — he is having a good time at Rainbow lake — camping.

## Right Page

### Sun. July 12, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cloudy day — Rather warm in the mfg. but cooler in the afternoon — Yelp: at 9 — I accomplished but little during the mfg. went to hotel at noon — got mail — dinner at 1.30 — a head ache sent me to bed in the afternoon — Evg. — walking grounds about the house + doing a little reading —
Mr. Walker + Mr. Rockwell (Alfred Jones' daughter) called in the afternoon.

Moody slept in his new quarters + will begin to-morrow to take his meals in the kitchen.
