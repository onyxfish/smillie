## Left Page

### Tues. June 9, 1903

**Weather:** overcast \- muggy \- veiled sunshine later \+ dull, copper moon at night  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast \- muggy \- veiled sunshine later \+ dull, copper moon at night. Woke early with severe headache that disabled me to a considerable extent all day \- After b'fst \- wrote to Mr. Maghn then to bank \+ got to studio at 11 \- worked on B\. plate till 1 \- home to lunch \- Fred's coming there \- to studio at 2 \- Douglas came \+ I gave general directions for fixing hall walls \+ stairs then worked on plates till 4 \- then to Gilli's Printing Co\. in 25" \[illegible\] \+ got Jmb. Jap\. paper \- then to a place in 23 st\. to get stuff to cover studio hall wall \- without success \- then to K\. \+ V\. \+ made arrangements to get pfp\. of B\. plate on Friday \- back to studio at 5.30 \- worked on B\. plate till 6'5 \- home to dinner \- Evely went to Charl's \- Geo., Gordon, Mary \- the girls \- \+ Mr. Fred. coming there \- Charl played on his orchestrelle \+ I read Helen Keller till 10 o'clk \- then I came home, leaving the others

B. 3½ h.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 10, 1903

**Weather:** overcast, muggy, foggy \- Rain in the afternoon with some thunder  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast, muggy, foggy \- Rain in the afternoon with some thunder \- To studio about 10 \+ worked on Bordeu plate till 1 o'clk \- then home to lunch \- all alone \- Mary \+ the girls having gone to Newark \- to studio at 2.30 \+ worked till 4.30 \- then went to Dr. Allen's \- the Ansonia \- B'dway \+ 74' S'. He says my mouth is all right \+ advises waiting till next face before any further change is made. Walked to the Park \+ down to Mad. Ave. \+ 57\. S'. The walk in the Park, in spite of light Rain, was delightful \- Home at 6.20 \- dinner \- Eve'g. loafing with newspaper.

B. 5 h.
