## Left Page

### Mon. May 18, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Exceedingly dry, hot \+ uncomfortable Ther. in Weather Bureau 87°. in street 90°. I went down to Wanamakers after bkfp. \+ then back to Studio at 10.30 \- Worked on Borden plate \- laid ground \+ opened some lines that I wished to bite \- varnished plate \+ home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2.30 \- bit during the afternoon \- Ernest Roth came in (Etching Student, N.A.) to get some hints. I worked till 6 \- finishing the biting \+ cleaning off plate \- Home to dinner at 6.30.
Evef writing checks \+ arranging accts.

To-day I Rec'd. a note from Halsey Ives Art Director of St. Louis Expos'n \- asking me to take a place on Com. on Etchings

B. 5 h.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 19, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very uncomfortable day \- quite as hot as yesterday \- After bkfp'. writing etc \- getting Mr. Kane at work scrubbing Geo's studio's floor, etc \- so that it was 11 before I got to work on Borden pl. Home to lunch at 1 \- After that went to barber's \+ had my hair cut \- to studio at 3 \+ worked till 5 \- then home. dinner \- Evef Lizzie \+ Jessie Smillie came in \- also George \- I wrote to Ralph \+ sent him check.

At 12.30 I went to Maurice Stern's studio in W. 34th St \- not in \- then home to lunch.

B. 4 h.
