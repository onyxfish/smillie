## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 13, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very lovely day - To studio at 9.30 \+ worked all the m'f on Borden plate \- \+ home to lunch \+ usual time \+ back after two \+ then worked till 4. Then at mounting etching &c till 5. Then to Alexander's - 6' Ave. \+ 17 ½ \+ to O'neil's - McCutcheon's - Sterns \+ Putnams \+ home at 6 - feeling quite used up altho' the sum total of walking done inside of an hour did not amt. to more than 2 ½ miles - I am much discouraged at my rapid loss of strength.
Eve'f. Charl came in. I wrote to Jim \+ Ralph - \+ a note to Dr. Allen asking him to change date of appointment given for next Tues P.m.

B 5 h.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 14, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day - To studio at 9.30 - got prints given by Mr. Barhydt (Rembrandt \+ Vandyk) \+ took them to NA of D - Amsterdam Ave. \+ 109 S. \+ had them put up in Etching Room. Found the class at work, from model, under direction of Maurice Stern - my first visit to the class this season - there till 12 o'clk - then to D.H. D'Alean at the Ansonia - Bdway \+ 74th St. He examined my mouth \+ made appt. for Monday. Feb. 23? Then I walked across the Park, finding it very pleasant to Mad. Ave. \+ 59th E., took car home - lunch - \+ to studio at 2 - Etched on Borden plate till 4. Walked on Park Ave. for a time, home soon after 5 \+ rested till dinner at 6 - Eve'f. Carlson came - usual treatment.

B. 2 h.
