## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 21, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another lovely day, sunny & mild \- Bkfst. at usual time \- after that went to studio \- no word from Higgins \- I went to his place, 430 4th Ave. \- boy there \- found my water gauge on his desk \- nothing done to it \- I took it to Roberts & left it there to be put in order \- then to studio \- there Higgins called me up on 'phone & explained \- I took studio keys to Thorne & ordered duplicate set, went on studio roof with Ryan to look for leaks where water got in during the big storm some days ago, \- did not find it \- home soon after 12 feeling quite done up \- after lunch I went to Yale's \- saw him but did not get much satisfaction \- says that probably I overtaxed myself when I climbed Noon mark a month or two ago \- a nervous strain \- a post mortem again. Would reveal nothing \- nerve tonic & patient waiting will probably effect a cure \- then went to Borrmann's & bought some photo. supplies then home & did a little photo printing. Dinner at 6.30 \- Every fooled around.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 22, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day \- up early & wrote part of a letter to Mr. Kent \- before bkfst. \- after that I went to studio for a while \- then home & amused myself printing photos. from some of Ralph's last summer negatives \- a little more writing on Mr. K's letter. Patrick Conefoy objects to doing most everything that is asked of him \- I have decided to ask him to "get out" \- just before I went to Chas. Collins'. To enquire about Jennings \- maid said he was not be in till to-morrow mfg. Home to lunch \- more photo. printing. \- finished letter to Mr. K. At 5 went out for air & exercise \- walked up to 5th Ave. & 58th St. \- then back \- dinner at 6.30 \- At 8 went to Chas. Collins' & spent the eve'g till 9.30 with him & his sister \- a nephew came in but I did not get his name \- home before 10.
