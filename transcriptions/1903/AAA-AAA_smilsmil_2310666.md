## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 16, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful weather in the m'f. Clouding gradually — Rain in the afternoon — I got up with severe headache but managed to keep up till after dinner. I picked peas \+ worked in the garden after 6kf.
\+Ralph \+ I had a pleasant chat with Lawrence Ates who called to tell us of a desirable nurse for Kate. who passed a quiet, comfortable night \+ continued to improve all day — Dr. Lombard, who came four times yesterday, came but three times to-day — I gave up to my headache after dinner \+ went to bed where I spent a wretched afternoon \+ night.

Kate continued to improve during the day. The Dr. came 3 times.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 17, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, rather cool \+ breezy. Thunder in the afternoon with no indication of electrical disturbance main or temperature. A heavy shower at 6.30 p.m. At 6 a.m. after a most uncomfortable night, I got up \+ took a dose of Castor oil, with much the same results as followed my taking it less than four weeks ago (July 24) except that its action was much more rapid. The last movement bring it about 4 p.m. I was able to dress late in the afternoon \+ was rolled in my blankets till tea time at about 7 p.m. Spent much of the eve'g in the same way.

Kate improves slowly. Miss Mage did not stay with her to-night — the Dr. came twice.
