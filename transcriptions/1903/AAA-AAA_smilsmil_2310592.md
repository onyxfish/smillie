## Left Page

### Thur. March 19, 1903

**Weather:** Dull mtf., clearing to a cloudless day after 6hfs.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I wrote to Mr. Potter Palmer \+ to Mr. Thurber — It was 10.30 before I got to studio — Graver work on Borden plate till 12.45 — then to Davies Coal Yd. foot of 9.37 E — ordered coal — home to lunch — back to studio at 2 — worked on plate till 3 — Carlson came \+ gave me abdominal treatment for half an hour then I worked till a little after 4 — then home — had a rest — dressed — at 7 at Mr. Vernon Munroe's (Sally Cook's) Walter and Louise came soon after \+ we had a good dinner \+ a pleasant ev'f. till near 10 — then Walter, Louise \+ I walked down Lex. Ave. home at 10.30.

B. 5 h
C. \-1/2

## Right Page

### Fri. March 20, 1903

**Weather:** Carlson.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Light rain in the early mtf. — clearing to a mixed, sunny day — to studio before 9 \+ worked on Borden plate till 1.15 — home to lunch \+ back at 2 — worked till 4 — then home — wrote letter to Ralph — dinner at 6.30 — wrote letter to Jim — mailed letters at 9 — Carlson came at 9.15 \+ gave me treatment giving especial attention to abdomen on acc't of constipation.

B. 5 h
C. 1
