## Left Page

### Thur. July 23, 1903

**Weather:** Pleasant m't. some sunshine, but close \& warm.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouded afternoon \& showers towards eve't. I got up feeling very much out of sorts but was able to keep about \- went to hotel for mail \& then down to feed Beades \- dinner \& then I gave up \& went to bed \- there all the afternoon \- no supper \- but wrapped in rugs spent the eve't on north piazza \- miserable with headache \& more or less discomfort all over.

## Right Page

### Fri. July 24, 1903

**Weather:** Glorious day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph \& Elizabeth went to the top of Noonmark \- Mary \& Kate went down the valley on a calling tour \- After a bad night, my head distressing me so that I could not sleep, I got up at about 6 a.m. \& took a short oz. of Castor oil \- then to bed again where I spent the day \- severe headache still persistent. The oil did not begin to operate till about 2 o'clk \- the first movement was a clearing out of fecal contents of the bowels \- three others follow \- the last being at 10 p.m. They were very peculiar in character \- a clear, saffron colored fluid \- saffron stained mucus (very tough) \& quantities of shreds, unstained, that looked like dead mucus membrane, such as sometimes peels from the inside of the mouth \- from the character of pains that I have had during the afternoon, sharp, tearing pains, I think it not unlikely that these shreds, some of them ½" long \& ⅛" wide, may have come from the duodenum (possibly the stomach) \& the small intestines \- The bright saffron color may have been bile (it scalded the anus in passing) \- The whole may indicate chronic catarrh of stomach \& bowels with some irregularity in action of liver \- my bowels do not move of their own unless stimulated by some drug \- every night I have to take Cascara in some form or Soda Phos. \- liver pills or some thing of that kind \- The condition in which I now am has been coming on for three years. Probably more \- but no doctor seems to have been able to stay it \- no one of them showed any understanding of it, so far as I could know \- If the trouble cannot be stayed it is a question of time only as to when the end will come \- Headache passed away about 5 p.m. then I had a cup of coffee \& some bread \& butter after fasting about 36 hours \- Eve't. got up \- extremely weak \- spent eve't on north piazza \- Rolled in rugs \-
