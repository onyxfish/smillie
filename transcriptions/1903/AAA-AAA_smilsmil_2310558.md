## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 10, 1903

**Weather:** Clear, very windy & very cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear, very windy & very cold — To studio about 10 & worked all day on the Borden plate, drawing in the little pattern on the carpet, avery [illegible] work to take the time & energies of a sane man Soon after 12 went to Wallack's Theatre — Bdway & 30th, & bought six tickets for Sultan of Sulu, next Friday night — then home to lunch — back to studio at 2 & continued work till 4 — then to Church — 35th St. & Lex. Ave — thinking it was Ch. of the Incarnation 35th St. & Madison Ave — where Dek. Tiffany was to be married to daughter of Juliane Scoti there being no wedding there, I Ret. to studio Home early & had a Rest before dinner — Eve'g went to Century — Annual Meeting After a pleasant eve'g, home at midnight

B. 4 h.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 11, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A most dismal day — snow in the m'g which soon turned to rain which fell heavily all day — I had a headache so severe that I did not get up till near 1 o'clk. when I was able to take my scoul then my dinner at 2 — I loafed through what remained of the afternoon grubbing through the dusty contents of closets that had not been disturbed for years looking to find some things that I have not found Tea at 6.30 — Eve'l — Read till Carlson came & gave me massage from about 9 till nearly 10.30
