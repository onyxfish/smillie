## Left Page

### Tues. April 28, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A bright, warm day — my bowels again gave me trouble in the early m'g \+ gave me constant dis comfort thro' the day — It was 11 before I got to work at the studio on Jordon plate — worked till 12.30 — then to bank \+ got a loan of $300 to pay bills. Home at 1.30 — found company at lunch — misses Buck \+ [illegible] o'k. Back to studio at 2.30 — worked till 4 — then home — more trouble with bowels \+ then I took an ounce of Castor Oil which made things lively till 11 o'clk —
I was much desappointed at not being able to go to Norman Park's wedding — Charl, Mary \+ Bessie went to the reception in the ev'g.

B — 3 h.

## Right Page

### Wed. April 29, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** 84°

Bright \+ very warm — said to be the warmest April day here in seven years. I did not get out of bed all day. I passed a quiet night till after 4 am. — then had a painful time — \+ again at 8 o'clk — I was uncomfortable \+ slept a good deal till noon — then began to feel easier — Geo came in during the afternoon.

Bessie went to Adolph Oppenhuym's funeral at 10 this m'g — at his late residence — Felix Adler presided — very affecting.
