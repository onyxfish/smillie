## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 18, 1903

**Weather:** Cloudy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy day - clearing towards night \+ growing much colder - To studio at 9.30 \+ painted all the m'f on a little sketch x made years ago at Po'k - a nov. gloaming - clearing after a day of storm. There was to me something good in the crude sketch but I am afraid that the little good in it was lost in my attempts to make it better. Home to lunch \+ after that I went to Narf D. School. 109" S \+ Amsterdam Ave. \+ met candidates for Etching class - arranged for a meeting next Sat. m'f. walked down, across the Park to Lex. Ave. \+ 90th St. Took car \+ because I was cold got out at 50th S. \+ walked the rest of the way to studio - cleaned up - home at 6. Mr. J.H. Maghee came home with Bessie \+ stayed to dinner - he left at 9 \+ then Chare came with a great lot of photo negatives, just developed by Colle[illegible] of views taken by C - during his European trip this last season.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 19, 1903

**Weather:** Fine cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine cold day. To studio at 9.30. Painted in "Eve'g after Storm. Nov.", that I worked on yesterday - I struggled with a headache all the m'f - went home at lunch time \+ to bed - my headache developing into one of the worst that I have suffered for along time - I did not get up to undress \+ go properly to bed till 2. o'clk in the m'f.
