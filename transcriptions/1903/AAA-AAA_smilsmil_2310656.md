## Left Page

### Mon. July 27, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

High wind + very cool all day with much bright sunshine — after a night of little rest I got up feeling much below par — It was so cold that I kindled a fire in studio stove before I went down to bkfst — After bkfst. for some time in garden gathering vegetables — got a large mess of very fine wax (string) beans — also Spinach — Then to studio to work on painting 16" x 20" — The motif I got last evef. passing from Anney to house — Cumulus in the east. Rosy at top — sombre at base — landscapes in shade — I worked till about 12.30 — then a rest till dinner soon after 1 o'clk — Afternoon, painted till 5 — then being very tired I wrapped myself in rugs + rested on north Piazza till 6.30 — then supper — guest. in front of fire till near 9 — then to studio + did a little writing —

## Right Page

### Tues. July 28, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — some bright sunshine in the mf. — a thin film covering the sky later. k overcast afternoon — After bkft. in garden with Moody for awhile — then painting on 16 x 20 that I began yesterday — Before 12 went with Ralph to Hotel for mail + then had a stroll in Reserve around the "ladies mile" + home to dinner — K. + B. away — one at Lower Park — the other at Noon Mark. Afternoon I painted till 4 o'clk + got into a mess on the landscape portion of my picture + went off in disgust. Rev. Bulcom Shaw + Rev. called — + so did Mr. Shurtleff — I did not see them — With Ralph walked over the wood Road from Reserve Entrance over to Openheim's + thence home over Golf links + Neilson place. Mr. Hardenburg took tea with us — later N. Maghee + Eliz.t came in — I read my newspapers Part of evef. in my Studio.
