## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 4, 1903

**Weather:** Dull, Raw day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After a very uncomfortable night I got up before 9 \- had my bath, \+ bkfs. at 10 \- About noon I went to Dr. Cilley's \+ left with him specimens of my urine thinking it about time to have another test of the condition of my Kidneys \- To studio \- opened windows \- Then to Charl's \- home at 2 \- dinner \- Afternoon Ge. \+ Val \+ Charles came in \+ I loafed with them \- Eve'g \- Charl came in again \- I managed to do a little writing.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 5, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another dull day \- I got up in the m'g. still feeling the effects of my recent spree. I wrote for awhile after bkfs. \+ had a talk with Mary about my struggle to live within my income \- She urges me to rent my houses, in town \+ out, for the next two or three years \+ take a rest \- but I cannot bring my mind to accept a change that would mean loss of home for self \+ boys \- the only home that the boys have ever known \- the home that Annie chose \+ where she died \- I feel that to leave it now would mean not to return \+ I cannot think of that \- I could not live alone in apartments or a boarding house \- Mary is tired of the responsibilities of house keeping \+ would be glad to escape \- She would like to go abroad again with her girls \- the situation is a very trying one \+ depresses me greatly \- It was near 11 when I got to studio \- worked on Borden plate till 1 \- home to lunch \- back at 2 \- worked till 4 \- home at 5 \- dinner \- Eve'g Chase came in \+ I did little else than loaf \- I tried to write to Clarence Smith about the new codicil to my Will but could not \- feeling confused in mind

B. 4 h.
