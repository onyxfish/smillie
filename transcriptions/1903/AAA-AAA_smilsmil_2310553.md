## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 1, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant winter day. I began the year badly, getting up much out of sorts after a trying night. Nevertheless I put in a good days work on Worden place. Got to studio after 10 \+ worked till 12.30 — then sauntered on Park Ave. Till 1.15, then into lunch. To studio at 2 \+ worked till 4 — then a half hour or more on Park Ave — then home \+ rested till 6. D. W\. Elkin \+ Katie dined with us \+ later took Bessie \+ Ralph \+ went out for a "random scout" — first to Proctor's 23d St. Matine then to the Eden Musee. Jim \+ Katharine went to Pompton. N.J. carrying in the m\[illegible\]. to visit Katchen \+ Willie Cook. Char came in the evefg. I wrote to A.A. Adams sending him a check.

B. 4/2.
