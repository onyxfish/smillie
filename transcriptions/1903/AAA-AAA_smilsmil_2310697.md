## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 15, 1903

**Weather:** Pleasant altho' overcast during latter part  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant altho' overcast during latter part - Gas man finished his job with Gas Range \+ after blkp. I went to Ryan's to hurry up Charley to get at trouble in butler's pantry - He did not come till about 11 \+ then he had to go for George to tell him of location of pipes - during the p.m. stoppage was found in soil pipe under dumbwaiter. I was going about attending to little matters - to studio about noon \+ home to lunch at 1 - after that, feeling weak \+ tired I slept awhile. Then Jale came in \+ advised with me - He advises me to try Russell's Emulsion (Beef Mutton fat) \+ Elex. Glycerophosphates - \+ da \+ [illegible] - I went to Hress's \+ got them - dinner before 7. Every read Post awhile \+ again fell asleep - Then I remembered that I should write to Jim - I did so, hurriedly - also a short note to Katie Selcin saying that I hoped to be able to dine with her next Sunday - At 10 went to G.C.St. \+ mailed notes - then home \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 16, 1903

**Weather:** A cloudy day - threatening - some sickly sunshine  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cloudy day - threatening - some sickly sunshine - Got up with headache which finally floored me at 5 p.m. A colored woman, Mrs. Bond, came to clean my studio \+ at 8 I went there with her \+ started her on the work then home \+ had my blkp. - back after 9 - with her most of the m'g - had a walk in the streets before 1 - then home to lunch - \+ again to studio \+ superintended the work there till 5 o'clk then I had to give up to my headache - I went home - to bed - \+ did not get up to undress till 1 A.M.
