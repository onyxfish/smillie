## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 2, 1903

**Weather:** mild, dull day — wet night, light rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

beginning to fall about 6 P. M. — To Studio
at 10 — worked on Borden etching — I
mounted on small sheets of plate paper
Two india prints from very early work of
father's — one a print from top of [illegible?] Snuff
box — the other, view of Mont morenci falls.
These I cut from the Hillemonrcan Scrap
Book that father gave me \+ shall add them
to the collection of father's work already
given to Print Dep. Public Library — Went
to Second Nat. Bank — then home to lunch
— to studio at 2 \+ etched till 4 — Then home,
changed dress \+ went to Private View of the
N. A. of D. [illegible] — Fine Arts Bldg. 57. S. Rode
up town with P. Putnam — The exbn
seems to me to be fairly good \+ makes
me itch to try to do a little more painting.
Home soon after 6 — dinner — evening
feeling very lame \+ stupid — To bed
early.

B. 3 h.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 3, 1903

**Weather:** Dull, raw \+ disagreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

— so was my
head, so that I was unfit to work — went
to Clauson's \+ had specimens of three kinds
of mounting boards sent to studio — it
was after 10 when I got to studio \+ for the
rest of the day I was preparing mounts
for etching plt — lunch at home — at
studio till 4 — then I gave up \+ went
home \+ to bed \+ there I stayed till
2.30 a. m. — then I got up, undressed
\+ went to bed like a Christian.
