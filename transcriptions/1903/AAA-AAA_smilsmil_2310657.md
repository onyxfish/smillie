## Left Page

### Wed. July 29, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very wet day \- some of the showers were unusually heavy \- a great deal of Rain fell \+ the roads were badly washed \- So the mt. was very cool I started a fire in studio stove \+ worked all day painting on 16" x 20". that I started on Monday \+ worked on yesterday \- Cumulus clouds in the east lighted by setting sun \- moon almost full, seen in clear sky over the clouds \- landscape in quiet grey tones. \- a very difficult subject, depending upon both strength \+ delicacy \- I wrestled with it almost hopelessly \- Before noon Ralph \+ I went to hotel, between showers, for mail I painted till after 4 \- then cleaned up \+ after that I rolled in my rugs on one of the lounging chairs on north Piazza till tea time \+ again, after it \- I enjoy the fresh air sure when protected agt. chilliness by rugs \+ cushions.

## Right Page

### Thur. July 30, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very heavy rain before 10 a.m. then clearing began \+ progressed slowly thro' the day which was very close \+ warm \- I had a headache which handicapped me for the day \- I was in the garden for some time after it began to clear \- Otis came with his tools to fix the basin in Mary's wash-closet \- I could find but one of the two detached clamps, so he could not complete the job \- At 11 I went to hotel for mail \+ stopped there to read my papers \- Dinner at 1. I was with Moody giving him instructions about placing cobble stones in regular order along the drive-way edge up to house \- Ralph went with others to the Country Club where there was an entertainment \- Scenes from Alice in Wonderland \- About 4 I walked up River Trail, west side \- almost to the bridge at the Canon falls. I turned back at 5 o'clk \- because it was too late for me to complete the circuit, crossing at the falls \+ thence over to the Reserve Road \- I found the walk very interesting \- the woods were wet \+ dark \+ solemn \- the River was in fine fettle \+ making a roaring time about it. \- home at 6 \- had a bath \+ change \- a rest for half an hour \+ supper at 7 or a little later by myself \- alone \- Cosy in my rugs on the north Piazza till after 9.
