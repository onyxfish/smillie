## Left Page

### Thur. June 11, 1903

**Weather:** Pleasant, altho' warm \+ clouded part  **Temp:** [illegible]

of the day \- Got up at 6 a.m. \+ wrote copying portion of my Will before bkfs. To studio at 9.30 \+ worked faithfully on Borden plates till 1 o'clk \- To get it ready for pfs. to-morrow M'f. Home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 \+ worked till after 6\- Then home to dinner, pretty thoroughly tired \- Evey. lounged with news paper till near 9 \- Then a little writing \+ to-bed.

B. 7 h. Last entry of Borden Time. From Nov. 12, 1902. to date \- 526 hours \- Price paid for Borden plates No 1 \- $495.50

## Right Page

### Fri. June 12, 1903

**Weather:** Heavy Rain beginning about 10 p.m.  **Temp:** [illegible]

last night \+ continuing till after 10.4 m. Then holding up awhile to commence again later in the day \- Weather bureau reports a fall of nearly 2". I got up early \+ reached K. \+ V. at 9.30 \- Was there till after 11 getting M'f V \- Started on the proving of the Borden Place \- After he had taken four or five pfs. I ret. to Studio \- Taking one wet pf. which I tacked down to dry \- Then home to lunch \- After that I wrote to Jim \+ after 4 o'clk. returned to K. \+ V's \+ got five more pfs. \+ took them to studio \+ tacked them all down. It was 7, #5 before I finished. The proofs look somewhat better than I had expected \- I would like to work a couple more days on the plates. Of course it was very late when I got home to dinner \- the family having already had theirs. Evey \+ Charl. came in \- I finished my letter to Jim \+ mailed it.
