## Left Page

### Mon. May 4, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day, but not sunny — Got up with severe headache which stuck to me all day — To studio at 10 \+ put in a hard days work in going over 42 plates of Etching class work (10 of them competition) getting them ready for the Printer. Well I was so much used up by headache that I had to go to bed before 8 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 5, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day with some threatened showers that did'nt come. To Studio at 10 — worked till 1 o'clk cleaning the etching class plates — some of them filled with ink dried hard had to be soaked in hot lye. Arranged them, with instructions for printer. Back to studio at 2 — worked on Borden plate — Geo. painted in my studio — Carpenters have not yet finished putting in partition on third floor. Walked awhile with Geo. \+ home soon after 6 — dinner — Well. Charl came in.
