## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 24, 1903

**Weather:** Cold, windy day — m't. cloudy \+ dark — afternoon, clear.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at about ten \+ set to work painting on "A Pool in the woods" × — a painting commenced more than a year ago — an upright — the top of a mt. lighted by last rays of sun — the rest of picture in shadow — pool in foreground. At 12.30 I went to bank — then to 83 Duane St. \+ got back No. of Punch — then home to lunch — to studio at 2.30 — Kost, the artist, called — I painted till 4 — then cleaned up — went to Miller's, the carpenter — home at 5 — dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g went to studio, got portrait of W. Geo. Ireland \+ took it to Alice Ireland's in 40th St. Home soon after 9 — Chase there.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 25, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim \+ Ralph came home for Thanksgiving. Jane \+ Mary Hinshelwood came —

Fine day with strong \+ very cold N.W. wind — I got to studio about 10. It was nearly 11 when I left. went to Calverly's \+ was much surprised to learn that he was stricken with paralysis some time last summer — had given up his studio \+ was now with his daughter in New Jersey — then to 308 W. 26th St. to enquire about printing blankets for Etching class — man not in — then to Stern's in 23d St. \+ bought cheese cloth, mosquito net \+ pongee silk for class work — then to Studio \+ home at 1 — lunch — then to Millers \+ then to N.A. to meet my class — but four of them there besides Stahr — two young women \+ two young men, all novices — had a discouraging time till 4 o'clk — when I left \+ walked across the Park to Madison ave. \+ 95th St. thence home by car \+ had a little rest before dinner at 6.30. Jim \+ Ralph came home during the afternoon for Thanksgiving — \+ Mary \+ Jane Hinshelwood came \+ commence their labor of taking care of me while Mary is away. Consequently we had an extended table — Eve'g at 8 o'clk I went to Century to Com. admissions — we had a long session — the last meeting before annual meeting — I excused myself after 11 \+ got home \+ to bed at about midnight.
