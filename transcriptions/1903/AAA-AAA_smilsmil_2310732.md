## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 24, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull day giving poor promise for Xmas. I went to studio at usual hour \+ painted all the m[orning] on right hand end (distance \+ water) of "Passing Showers", changing it much \+ improving it I think. Then home to lunch — then to bank \+ got gold pieces for xmas presents — hunted for napkin rings for the boys without finding what I wanted — then to studio cleaned up. Dinner at usual time \+ eve\[ning\] quietly at home.

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 25, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dark, wet day. Had b[rea]k[fas]t. at usual time — after that, when Table was cleared, presents were brought out \+ b[rea]k[fas]t. attended. It was a pleasant, informal time \+ did not consume much time. Then I went to studio \+ worked till 1 o'clk. Painting on left hand, foreground end of "Passing Showers". Home before 2. Dinner soon after 2 — Chave dined with us — Jane Banks spread herself \+ presented a capital Xmas dinner. Between 5 \+ 7 o'clk. I wrote a long letter to Bessie \+ before 8 o'clk. mailed it to catch S.S. St. Paul early to-morrow out[?]. At 6 the boys went to Mary Cook's to supper. I went there at 8 o'clk — a family gathering. The boys \+ I left for home at about 10.
