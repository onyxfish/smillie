## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 24, 1903

**Weather:** Very cold, cloudy \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkfst. I went into see Dr. Putnam \+ had quite a long talk with him after which he decided to defer the washing out of the stomach set for to-morrow m'g because I am feeling so much better \- He will try a new arrangement of drugs looking to supplying various digestive energies \- To studio at 10 \- Katharine there with a model \- I worked on Borden plate until near 1 \- a short walk on Park Ave. then home to lunch \- to studio about 2 \+ 1\. am dreadfully tired of the mechanical grind of the Borden plate. I amused myself by painting on two oil sketches \- At 4.30 I walked to Horton's \- 23° S\. \+ 4th Ave. Home abt 6 \- dinner \- Eve'g Carlson came at 9.15 \+ left at 10.30.

B. 3 h.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 25, 1903

**Weather:** Snow fell all the m'g  **Temp:** [illegible]

I had bkfst. at 9.30 \- newspaper \+ writing till near 1 o'clk \- Then to studio for a few minutes \- Then had a promenade on Park Ave. in the snow \- Then to Charles for a few minutes \+ home at 2 to dinner \- had a long nap in the afternoon \- Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g wrote to Wm. G. Neilson, to D. Wm. L. Elkin \+ to Frank Jones \- out at 10, mailed letters \- had a short walk \- home \+ to bed.
