## Left Page

### Thur. April 2, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*[header note: Jim has mumps.]*

A day with a little sunshine in the m't. - ending with rain at night. D. Putnam came soon after bkfst. and examined Jim - is puzzled \+ asked for a consultation with Dr. Janeway - I assented I got up \+ dressed before noon \+ had a short walk on Park Ave. Dr. P. came in about 1 to say that he had an appointment with Dr. J. to meet him here at 4 - He came again at 4 \+ Dr. J. - followed soon after. He saw Jim \+ said that he had misplaced mumps - that is, the parotid glands were not affected but the salivary \+ lymphatics down the side of the neck \+ under the chin He spoke as tho' it might be a protracted case - every effort is made to quarantine Jim properly without impairing his comfort - He complains of dry mouth \+ pain in chewing \+ swallowing. I have kept about all day - bowels obstinate finished Oliver Home - old Richard is a very warning character - the latter part of the second vol. makes amends for the prolixity all that precedes it. The Swedish new comers seem to give great satisfaction \+ to be satisfies To bed about 11.

D. P. 3 times?
D. Janeway.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 3, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*[header note: Ralph came home.]*

A very mild, balmy day. After a night that was not altogether restful I got up \+ had bkfst. at 9.30 - all the m't. we waited very impatiently for Dr. Putnam but he did not come till near 2 o'clk - No marked change in Jim's condition or appearance - I walked for awhile on the street for air \+ exercise - my bowels continue obdurate in spite of the cathartic taken yesterday - they did finally take action late in the afternoon \+ during the ev'g. Ralph came home at about 2 o'clk - then I went to the 2d Nat'l. Bank \+ made deposit - then to Scribner's - saw Mr. Smith \+ got Jor's "Gerome" - a work that bro. Will paid more than $200 for about 20 years ago - by pub's Record of auction sales Mr. Smith showed me that copies had been sold recently for $37 - \+ $40. - then home - out with Ralph just before dinner - dinner at 6.30 - Charl came in the ev'g.

Dr. P. once.
