## Left Page

### Thur. April 16, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A leaden sky \+ cold wind getting around to the north all day \- Got to studio about 10.30 after going to Davis Bro's Coal yard, to the Butcher's \+ grocer's \+ to Thorne's, to have him fix lock of studio front door \- I worked till 1 on Borden place \- then home to lunch \- at 2 I went to NA of D\- where etching class members were biting their competition plates \- I was with them till near 5\. \- Saw Miss Chadwick for a few minutes at her work in Antique class \- Walked thro' Central Park to Lex. Ave. \+ 75 St\- not less than 3 miles, I think, then Rode to studio \- Home at 6 \- dinner at 6.30 at 9 I went to Charl's, being unfit to write, as I should have done \- Mary \+ Kate there looking at foreign Photo. views \- Home at 10.

B 2h.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 17, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Kath. \+ Eliz. Ret. from Po'k.

Brilliant, beautiful day \- to studio before 10 \+ worked till 1 on Borden place \- Home to lunch \- after that went to Clausen's \+ got a lot of pulp boards for mounting prints \- to studio at 2.45 \+ worked on plate till 5\. finishing the drawing in of work on second ground \+ varnishing plate ready for acid \- then did some cutting of mounts \- home at 6 \- dinner at 6.30 Mr. Young \+ Walter Thrash unexpectedly to dinner with us \- Katharine \+ Bessie Ret. from Po'k. visit this mth. Eve'g I wrote letters to Jim \+ Ralph.

B 5h.
