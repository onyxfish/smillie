## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 4, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day overhead — shocking underfoot. I was belated in getting to studio. It was aft 11 o when I got to work on "Evening before full moon" x Wingate came \+ got three of his etched plates that I have. I had a very short stroll in sunshine in Park Ave. before lunch — \+ it was 2 o'clk. when I went back to studio \+ painted till about 3.30 then I cleaned up \+ washed brushes. I had another stroll on the Ave before I went home at 5.15 \+ rested till dinner at 6.30 — Well. I wrote letters to Jim \+ Ralph — posted them at 10 p.m.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 5, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day \+ milder — Immediately after bkft. I went to Etching class at N.A. \+ worked there with acid for students till aft 12 — then walked part way home — lunch — spent afternoon at studio clearing up things — home at about 5 \+ rested till dinner. Evef. went to Century. Monthly meeting — there till midnight — then home \+ to bed.
