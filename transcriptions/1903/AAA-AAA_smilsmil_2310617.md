## Left Page

### Fri. May 8, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary went to Po'k.

A beautiful day - to studio after 10 - after going to Davies Bros. Coal Yard \+ ordering coal - worked on Borden plate till 12.30 - a short walk before lunch - to studio soon after 2 and worked till 4.30 - Connolly came in to ask if he may submit some of his etched work for criticism to-morro Mary went to Po'k - this afternoon to be gone for a few days. At 5 I Rode up to Central Park - 8th Ave. \+ 68th St., walked across the Park to 5th Ave. \+ 59th St - delightful walk - \+ took Mad. Ave. cars - getting home at 6.30 dinner - Eve'g - Chas came in - I wrote to the boys.

B. 5h.

## Right Page

### Sat. May 9, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, breezy \+ warm. To studio at 10 - got to work on Borden plate but was interrupted at various times \+ in divers ways. Connolly came \+ brought some inks, from his etchings - Then Bessie came to tell me that the Craton has broken loose in the kitchen - went home - fussed awhile - stopped flow - Then back \+ got plumber - Electric bell man at work running a bell wire to Maynard's door - Home to lunch at 1 - after that went to N.A. of D - met there Mauvel \+ Frank Jones \+ spent a little time looking over acct. for past year - then to Etching Class Room - Aronson helped me put up inks. of class work for inspection - members of the class all interested. At about 5 walked thro' upper part of Park \+ wondered at the number of babies \+ children - concluded that the language of the country I was in is German. \+ the Race, Hebrew - walked to 96th St \+ Mad. Ave. took Car - home to dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g. with newspaper.

B. 1h.
