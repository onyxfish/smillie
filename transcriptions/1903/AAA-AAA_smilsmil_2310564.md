## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 22, 1903

**Weather:** Variable - bright - \+ then overcast.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 10. Katharine had a model there, I decided to take a half holiday. Went to Marquand 54 6[illegible] \+ was there till noon - then to Nat'l. Arts Club, 37-9 W. 34 St \+ saw a La Farge window just completed for W. C. Whitney. Then to Montross gallery \+ saw Horatio Walker pictures - then home to lunch at 1 - After that, \+ writing a note to the Ed'r. of Brush \+ Pencil - W. F. W. Morton. I got to studio soon after 2 \+ worked on Borden Pl. till 4. Then to Davies Bro' - foot of E. 37 S. Then to Horton's - 4th Ave. \+ 23. St. - then home at 5.30 \+ read until dinner at 6.30. Even'g. Clark came us - Carson came at 9 \+ left at 10.30 -

B. 2 h.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 23, 1903

**Weather:** Changeable day - at times clear - at other times overcast with threats of snow flurries.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio about 10 - Borden Place till 12.30 - walked n'ark Ave. - lunch at 1 - to studio at 2 \+ etched till after 4 - then I went to Sam't. Colman's - 6th Ave \+ 40th St. \+ chatted with him for the best part of an hour - then to Lewis \+ Conger's - got a spirit lamp \+ home at 6 - Dinner - Margaret Buck - a friend of Bessie's came from Po'k - this m'g. for a visit of a few days - She had company in the ev'g. I was engaged writing letters to the boys - Went to G. C. Station before 11 - then to bed -

B. 4½ h.
