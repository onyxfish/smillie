## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 18, 1903

**Weather:** Cold \- Partly cloudy \- a light snowflurry during the middle of the day  **Temp:** [illegible]

at 10 \- at noon to studio for a few minutes, then to Charl's \- met Ms. Aug. Meyer there \- Home to dinner at 2 \- fooled away the afternoon feeling much out of sorts. Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g I decided that the time had come for me to consult a new doctor \- so I went in to B. Chas Putnam's \+ had an hour \+ a half with him \- He made a careful examination but did not express himself \- gave me a triple prescription which I took to Frees \+ had filled \- then home, read awhile \+ to bed \-

Afternoon I wrote W.G. Neilson about bit of Reserve land that St. Elkin w'd. like to buy.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 19, 1903

**Weather:** Still very cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

to studio at 10. Maurice Stern came in for a few moments followed by Katharine T \- \+ soon after that Jessie Edgar came with her little boy Jamie \+ M.P. Dau. Sayre \- K.T. made a Water-Color Sketch of the boy, taking all the m'g at 4 \- I worked on Borden place till 12.30 \- then I walked on Park Ave. till 1 o'clk \- then home to lunch \+ to Studio at 2 \+ on B\- Plate till 4 \- at 4.30 I went to Saml. Coleman's Studio \- not in \- then to Century \- met Collins \+ others there \+ had a pleasant time till 6 \- Got notes (biographies) of Thos. Doughty \- Home to dinner \- Eve'g, Charl came in \-

P.o. 4½h.
