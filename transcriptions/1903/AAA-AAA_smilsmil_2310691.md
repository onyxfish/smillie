## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 3, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day \- before bkfs. I printed some photos, for Mary to send with letter that she has written to S.C.F. I gardened as usual \+ gave more time to printing photos. Then the work of doing up pkgs. \+ other preparations \- dinner \+ after that went to hotel \+ got mail \-
Afternoon, the work of the mf. continued. Supper \- Evef. most of it given to going over Keene accts. of last summer \+ this \- tho' incomplete I find that the expenses of this summer have been from $500 to $700 less than last year \- food \+ help have cost a little less. The main saving being on "improvements" \- the Road wall costing $250 \+ the Tool house (with mains bed room) quite as much more.

Rec'd note from Edna Cook announcing her engagement to Henry Murphy.

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 4, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

High southerly winds all day. Sunny for a time in the mf. Heavy shower about 11 \- distant thunder, sunshine \+ cloud during the p.m. After bkfs. in garden \- gathered a small mess of belated peas \+ string beans \- I put in a full days work of all sorts, all tending to clearing up \+ in preparation for town getting. Dinner at 1.30 \- About 3 I walked over to hotel \+ then back \+ down the hill \+ saw ferd Beede Albert Winch \+ Harry Beede in business then walked down to the iron bridge by the big rock that has just been replaced on new foundations \- Then home \- About 6 P.M. team came with cold frame sashes from Montrose. Man let one fall on others \- from the sound think that all of the glass must be broken. Supper \- Evef. assorting papers in my writing desk \+ tying them up in pkgs.
