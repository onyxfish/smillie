## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 23, 1903

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - After bkft. went to Beyerderfer's \+ left my watch with him for Repairs - Then to studio at 10 \+ until 12.30 worked on Borden etching then walked on Park Ave. for about half an hour - then home to lunch - to studio soon after 2 \+ worked till 3.30 - then to D. Allen's by appointment - He took imp. of lower part of mouth preparatory to making tempot set of teeth - After that I walked across the Park \+ down to 54. S. \+ Mad. Ave. \+ then rode home - Dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g reading.

B. 4 h.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 24, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull most of the day - Raw air \+ thawing. To studio soon after 9 - Etched on Borden plate till 12.30 - then had a short Park Ave. stroll before lunch at 1 - Katharine T. at the studio with model all the m'f. - also during the afternoon. To studio after lunch soon after 2 \+ etched till 4 then I went to St. D. Allen's - 74th St. \+ Bdway "The Ausonia" \+ gave him my "bite" for the new lower set - Walked through the Park \+ down Mad. Ave. to 52d S. \+ there took car home - Dinner at 6.30 Charl came in the eve'g. Carlson came at 9.30.

B. 4.30 h.
