## Left Page

### Fri. March 13, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

miss Grace Buck came [illegible] Parlstone

Milder than yesterday — very pleasant. To studio at 9.30 — to work at once. printing — took two or three impts. from Borden plate — none of them really good, but sufficient to show that I have been over anxious and pains taking \+ most of the work on the plate is too close to give a proper amt. of texture — The ceiling especially fails in comparison with plate No. 1 — altho' on that I made the mistake of putting crosslines over the work \+ biting both at one time — Portions of the etching on No. 2 has bitten badly in a way that is quite unaccountable to me, the line being rough \+ in some cases two parallel lines would be unequal as tho drawn with different sized points. To lunch as usual — worked with graver till about 5 — alone at 5.30 rested till dinner at 6.30 — Evef. Charl came in — About 10 p.m. Grace Buck came to spend Saturday \+ Sunday with the girls — I spent the evef. writing to Jim \+ Ralph.

B. 5½ h.

## Right Page

### Sat. March 14, 1903

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Carlson.

A warm, sunny Spring day — to studio about 10 — worked with dry point \+ graver till near o'clk — lunch — back about 2 \+ worked till 4 — Then I went to Barnard Club Carnegie Bldg. to hear recitation by Miss Agnes Donaldson — Getting there at 4.30 I was told that Miss D. was not expected till 5 — then I went out \+ tried to see H.R. Butler \+ F.S. Church — both out. wandered up to Central Park — back, \+ at 5.15 found Miss D. receiving — listened to two presentations — French Canadian stories given with fluency \+ expression — but her voice did not carry well to where I stood — \+ I could not hear well. I gave up the attempt \+ walked home getting in at 6 — dinner at 6.30 — Evef. Carlson came at usual time — he is much chagrined because he has not been able to subdue my constipated habit — He wishes to give me treatment every night next week as a test — says he will not charge for the extra times — he wishes to satisfy himself.

B. 5h.
