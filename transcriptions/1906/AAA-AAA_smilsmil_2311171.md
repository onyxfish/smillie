## Left Page

### Wed. May 16, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably warm day. I had bkfs'- soon after 8 \+ got to studio about 10 I put in a small addition to etched plate sketch of Father, To'Keepsie, 1842 \+ bit it in \+ I put other plates, the Quebec portrait, Study of old man's head, etc, in order to be printed from. Then, at about 1 o'clk. I went out \+ sauntered about taking note of some of the building operations in the neighborhood. At about 2 o'clk, prepared lunch After that I cleared up press corner, got out blankets, wet down paper \+ prepared for printing to-morrow. It was after 6 o'clk when I got to my rooms. Dinner with Jim at 7 — no letter from Ralph. I am worried about Jim. I telegraphed \+ at 10.30 got Reply from him that he was "fine." Eve'g with news papers \+ writing a short letter to Ralph about whom I still worry — got a letter from Mary mailed at Gibraltar.

This m'g paper announced that Carnegie had given a million dollars towards a united Arts bldg. I wrote him a note referring to H. R. Butler's plan \+ confederation eleven years ago.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 17, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably hot day — after bkfs'. which was late owing to a headache rather more severe than usual, I wrote a letter to Ralph \+ a note to H. R. Butler — It was near 11 when I got to studio \+ at once set to work at printing. It did not take long to get ink etc. ready as I had made preparations yesterday P. M. — My first essay was on the Quebec portrait of Father \+ I was greatly pleased to find that my first print was good — This gives me hope that I may still be able to do something. I then took up my study of an old man's head \+ took p's. \+ worked on it for the rest of the day till at 4 o'clk. when I got the plate to give me something near what I wished. Then I was held up \+ wet down more papers for to-morrow. Caught by a thunder shower on my way to my rooms at 5 o'clk — took shelter in Clare's — (he'd now in Boston.) Then went on at 6 o'clk — dinner alone at 7 — Jim having sent word that he was going off with Starred Grove on an auto mobile ride. — At 8.30 he called me up on phone saying that he was at Coney Island \+ might not be home to-night. Later — He came in at 12.30
