## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 25, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temps. 98°-99°. Pulse 84 to 96. Head ache \+ cough — very deaf — Roaring sounds in the head. Ears carefully irrigated several times during the day — Some evidences of very slight discharge in left ear — most pain \+ discomfort in right ear.

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 26, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temps. 99°. Head ache \+ cough continued — Cotton in left ear still shows slight moisture \+ discoloration — irrigated several times during the day. deafness continues greatly to my discomfort as I dread to becoming chronic.
