## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 18, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable damp, clouded day — some weak attempts at both sunshine + rain. In answer to the demands of my towels I got up soon after 7 — a demand that was repeated after 11. Bkfs' in bed + then my bath — about 10.30 when I came out of my bed room — I read newspaper + loafed away the mf expecting D. T. but he didnt come. Dinner soon after 1 o'clk — After 3 I had a short nap then Chad came in + he stayed till near 6 — Supper at 6.30. I have been extremely careful in my selection of food to day + my stomach has been quiet — I am still about a degree in temp. below normal — my hearing varies from Tolerable to bad — Well, reading writing + loafing —

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 19, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull + mild — a few attempts at sunshine — awoke at 7 — had my bkfs' in bed as usual. After 10 before I was dressed — then spent most of the mf studying over papers rec'd. from Ham. Am. S.S. line giving information concerning Attas Line service to Jamaica, Columbia \+ Costa Rica — with schedules + maps I planned a possible trip for Dr. Tuttle + myself sometime before the end of the winter — Dinner at 1 — I dared to eat a small chop hoping for the best. Then Miss D. got Jessie Smillie on the phone + arranged with her that she + I should go to the Presb'n Hospital — we started at 2 + by surface R.R. got there in a round about way — found Jessie + had cordial greeting — then a short call on Miss Hicks the young nurse who visited Mrs. Wainit at St. Hubuts last summer — Then we went to the room where Florence Tweedy is lying — having had an ovarian tumor removed — She is very weak + looks badly — Belle is with her — we stayed but a few minutes — then Jessie, Bellee + I Rel'd to J's Room — Miss D. was making visits — \+ there I stayed till after 4 o'clk. + got very tired — Then Miss D. Rel'd — a courier was ordered + we Rel'd. home at 5 — Then I parted with Miss D — she leaving me after five weeks of service — went to bed + had a nap. I att 6.30 I had a light meal while Jim had his dinner — Jim spent the eveg at home + read aloud to me. I wrote to Dr. Tuttle — my stomach has done fairly well + my hearing has improved a little.
