## Left Page

### Mon. May 28, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beastly day \- wet, raw + cold \- I wore my light over coat on going out + at night Steam was on in our rooms \- I got up soon after 7, had bkfs. at 8 + got to studio before 10 \- I found more work to do on "Study of an old man's head + then made preparations for printing to-morrow \- looked over my paper + made selections \- Some of the fafs. paper needed much clearing of specks. I had my lunch in studio at about 2 \- + got back to Touraine about 5 \- Jim went to Yale Club to dine + meet some of his fellows. I dined alone after 7 \- Evef I got a pleasant note from Katie Elkin inviting me to stay at her house when I go to the ball game next Sat. I wrote her a Reply.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 29, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Splendid day \- brilliantly clear + pleasantly cool \- Bkfs. at 8 + got to studio at 10 \- F.H. Smith came in to get list of colors for his oil palette \- He is going to pitch into Painting in oils during the coming summer \- I got to work at printing as soon as possible + worked very steadily till near 2 o'clk \- The work being largely experimental to get paper + ink best suited to the plates in hand \- I tried some old bleached india, without pasting, of father's \- I was not successful + ate my lunch discontentedly \- Then I went out + had a short stroll in the sunshine \- then back + took more pfs. till after 4 \- There I cleaned up + at last got to my rooms about 5.30 \- Jim came in \- took a bath + then went out \- I had my dinner alone \- my stomach misbehaving badly \- Evef got from Cullen a great lot of my photos, bought abroad mounted \- The evef. was spent looking over them
