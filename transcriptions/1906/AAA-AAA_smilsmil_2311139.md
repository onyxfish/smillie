## Left Page

### Tues. March 13, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An exceedingly disagreeable day - B&lp. at 8.30 - to studio about 10 - clouded air raw - snow flurries - beastly - Stopped at Charles for a few minutes - at studio busy all the m't. with photos - trimming \+ assorting prints - worked till almost 2 - then to Century - met Collins there and spent some time with him - 4 o'clk when I got to studio - \+ dark. Took photos \+ cutter to Rooms at Touraine \+ worked there - Jim \+ I dined at 7 o'clk - The eve't passed in unprofitable napping \+ reading news papers.

## Right Page

### Wed. March 14, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold \+ raw - overcast, but clear at night. Up earlier than usual \+ had b&lp. soon after 8 o'clk. I worked till after 11 in my room trimming photo prints \+ numbering \+ cataloguing the negatives of photos taken on my recent trip. Then I went to Man. Storage Co. - To have error in bill corrected - then to studio - then to bank - then to 246th of Stephen Parish paintings - then to see the Brandus paintings - then to Century at about 2 \+ had lunch - It was after 3 when I set to my rooms \+ resumed the work of the m't. - At 5 young Gould came to shave himself in Jim's room - Jim dined with him at the Yale Club - I dined a 6.30 \+ then went to the Special meeting of N. A. of D. to ratify the changes in the constitution, after which the absorption of the Soc'y of Am. Artists will be completed - I expected a full meeting but there were few more than a quorum present - meeting called for 8.15 did not come to order till nearly - The proposed changes were taken up section by section - there was very little discussion - Perry leading - the main contention was to secure a definite constitutional right of each N.A. to one good place in each ex'b'n - It was pointed out that there is no such right assured in the present constitution \+ it would not be wise to attempt to force it at this time - it would require another conference with the Soc'y of Am. Artists \+ probably an indefinite postponement of the union — after the failure of that contention the reading of sections went along without interruption \+ at the end all of the proposed changes were adopted - After adjournment of special meeting a regular meeting was held - I left at 10 o'clk \+ ret'd to my rooms.
