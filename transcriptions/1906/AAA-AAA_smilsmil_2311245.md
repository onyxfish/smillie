## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 9, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charles arrived —

Beautiful mf., about 1 o'clk. a very heavy Rain storm came up \+ continued, with intermissions, for the rest of the day \+ evef. At 7 got first notice by Tel. that the S.S. Kaiser Wm 11 was off Nantucket — at 8 o'clk. Rec'd. 2d. notice. S.S. wd dock about 10 45 Bkpt. before 8 — 'phoned to Geo. got to dock at 10 40. vessel in sight — passengers did not get ashore till 11 45 — immense crowd — got Chas. without trouble — but there was delay in getting his 6 pkgs together — passed without difficulty \+ at 12.45 we all got away together — Rain came very suddenly while we were on ferry boat — we rode directly to 2d S. 38th — I got home between showers after 2 o'clk \+ had lunch — Then a rest — After that some desultory work — most of it looking for mislaid articles — Dinner at 6 30 — Evef. Jim went out \+ I went to Charls' — spent remainder of Evef. talking about his summer vacation — Home \+ to bed at 11 30

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 10, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Douglas's man came —

Overcast mf. — clearing to very pleasant day — Bkft. before 8 — Douglas's man came with material \+ commenced operations on walls — sized those of parlor \+ library \+ a sort of dining room wall — did not put in a full day — I went to butcher \+ grocer \+ then to Wanamaker's — there till about 11.30 — then to Studio — Home at 1 \+ had lunch — Then to 2d. Natl. Bk before 3 — Then walked up 5th Ave. \+ to studio — worried there for awhile — then home at 4 40 — had a rest — Dinner Evef. Jim went to theatre with David Harris — I loafed — Jim came in at 2 40 a.m.
