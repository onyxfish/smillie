## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 17, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. slightly increased - got up to 102°. urine clear - showing that right kidney may be plugged - Drugged with Benzoate of soda - acetanelide - strychnia etc \+ fed on gruel \+ broth.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 18, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. up to 103°. \+ down to 100°. urine still running clear, making Dr. Tuttle anxious - Pulse up \+ down from 84 to 116 - all mucus membranes in head, throat \+ chest very sore. I was much of the time in a confused semi-conscious condition owing to pain \+ fever. my eyes were so sensitive to light that I could not keep them open. Neuralgic pain in face \+ ears. Benzoate of soda - strychnia - acetanelide \+ Codeine gruel, clam broth \+ malted milk.
