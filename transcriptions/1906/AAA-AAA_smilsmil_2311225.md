## Left Page

### Thur. Aug. 30, 1906

**Weather:** A fine day, after a dubious outf.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Miss Kathryn + Master Harry Bradly went to the lower Ausable for a days outing. Nearly the whole of my day was spent with Moody gathering small spruces + setting them along the gore by roadway in front of house. At 11.30 I took a short recess. Ike long expected bath tub am? + at noon I went to Club ho. + saw Scott B—, enquiring why the metal fixings had not come with tub as ordered — no explanation — Got P.O. cases, Zurich. 19 inst. announcing that Chase, Mary + the girls were there — Dinner at 1 — work resumed with Fres. — Kept at it till near 5 — unusually lovely atmospheric effect over valley to north — R. + I sat on piazza till 6 enjoying it — R. cut out the top of a white birch, enlarging view of Giant from bay window — a short East before tea at 6.30 — Eve'g reading as usual.

. Adams did not work.

Rec'd. letter from Mr. Marquand, accepting my terms for the Ark next Summer — He has an "option" till March 1st. If for any reason he fails to perfect his lease at that time, he forfeits $50.

## Right Page

### Fri. Aug. 31, 1906

**Weather:** A rather cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

with very high wind — again most of my time was given to transplanting evergreens — + watching Adams — I went with Moody to the north side of W.Q.K's hill, windy brow + got a number of very small spruces + then left Moody to get more while I went to the library + hunted thro' the catalogue securing four books by noon. — Then to P.O. I tried to learn something concerning my bath fixtures that did not come with the tub — but without success. Dinner at 1 — Afternoon went to Hardenburg (or Sandstone) hill with Moody + Harry + got some cedars — one very fine clump that I had seen on a paint of grass between roads on load to the house about 4 Mr. Vivante + her sister + bro.-in-law came (I did not get the latters name) + we had quite a visit — he brought a couple of his oil sketches (one, of Mr. V's house) for criticism. Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g

Adams + Don worked.
