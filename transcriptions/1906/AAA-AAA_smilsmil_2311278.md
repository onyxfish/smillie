## Left Page

### Wed. 12. Dec. 14, 1906

*(turned over two pages instead of one.)*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another fine day - did not rest well last night - up later than usual. This m't had blkfi. after 9 - After 10 went to studio \+ till after 1 o'clk. assorting the Hinshelwood p\[o\]s. There are some good specimens of me: among them - if judged by the best he w\[oul\]d be given a high place among Am. Landscape engravers - He falls short because he had no noble ambition - Home to lunch before 2 o'clk - a rest - At 3.30 I went to Edw\[ar\]d. Cook's - 154 E. 37th St - Annual meeting of Ireland Real Estate Co - Edw\[ar\]d. Geo\[rge\]., Walter \+ I present - unanimous in action - I got home after 6 - frightful jam in subway cars - Dinner with Jim at 6.30 - Every he helped me in arranging the books.

Free discharge of pus in urine last night.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 15, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day - mild, streets not slimy - Up soon after 7 - blkfi. at 8.30 \+ at 9.30 started for studio - I spent the m't there working most of the time over Hinshelwood p\[o\]s. trying to get satisfactory results with my punch, J.D. Smillie collection. The punch is not a good one giving an impression unequal in its parts - Geo. Green came \+ we went to cellar, opened a box there \+ took from it Ralph's fine sizable rope \+ two cloisonné vases - the Rope I took to Halmenfeld's \+ had it put into a mace box to be expressed to R\[alph\]. at New-Haven. It was after 2 o'clk before I started for home \+ 2.45 before I got a light lunch, after which I rested till Miss Anna B. Murph[y] came at 4 o'clk. She is a stenographer \+ type writer sent to me by Charl. I read to her from Father's autobiography till 5.30 omitting such parts as I do not wish to see printed. She took stenographic notes \+ will bring them to me next Sat - in such shape that I can easily revise it. It will require a number of sessions to complete the work if I go on with it - Dinner at 6.30 - Jim with me but early in the ev't he went to a 7th Reg\[imen\]t. Athletic expts - there to meet the [illegible] \+ go with them to their Yonkers home to spend Sunday with them - I spent the ev't browsing over newspapers \+ books.
