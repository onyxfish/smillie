## Left Page

### Tues. July 17, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lowering m'f. that promised a wet day — then some sunshine — then showers that continued till the afternoon, when it cleared rather cool — During part of the m'f. I wrote to Jim Shearman \+ finished the letter in the afternoon — At 11 R. \+ I went to Library, Club house \+ P.O. Dinner at usual time — After 5 we went to Fred. Beede's, saw Orrin \+ arranged to have some work done — as, in the m'f. I had rec'd. note from Earl Moody saying that he could not work for me — On our way up we stopped at "The shanty" \+ saw Kath'n. Hardenburg \+ Miss Nicolls — there for a few minutes then went on leaving R — I went without supper having eaten more than enough already — Eve'g we sat in-doors \+ read Jack London Stories — which did not move me greatly, perhaps because I was chilly — As we had no fire I went to bed soon after 10 to get warm.

## Right Page

### Wed. July 18, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely day — Spent much of the m'g trimming Arbor Vitæ's near the house, set out in front of East piazza — \+ following Orrin Beede \+ man John, gathering up grass left by Nye's men \+ cutting with scythe over spaces rolled down or left by the machine — about noon went to P.O. — mail late — R. \+ I walked a short way into the reserve \+ on return got mail — Charl forwarded letter from K.P.T. Then home to dinner — After that Beede's man came with half a load of very poor manure — His R. \+ I got under \+ about the Arbor Vitæs \+ then, with hose, saturated it with water — I hope that this will bring up some of those transplanted last year \+ doing poorly — We uncovered piano, taking off winter wrappings to give it air \+ found two mouse nests in the old soft comforters where the families had evidently spent the winter — After supper had a megaphone call from the Magbees to come over to a "Camp-fire" — R. went at once — I lighted up, kindled a little fire \+ read a story by Jack London — At 10 I went to the "camp-fire" \+ spent an hour there — R. \+ I back to the ark at 11.
