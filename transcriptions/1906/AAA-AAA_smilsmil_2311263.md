## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 14, 1906

**Weather:** Brilliant \+ cloudless  **Temp:** [illegible]

my routine over at about 10 - soon after that I went out alone \+ walked on the sunny side of Bdwy for half an hour - Then sat at open windows till D. T. came before 12 - we had a long Talk about some southern Trip, possibly about the middle of Feby - Dinner at 1.50 - After that Miss D- \+ I walked for a good half hour in Riverside Park - Then I ret'd to the house \+ had a nap - After that I fin-ished letter to Mary \+ mailed it - I took my evef mail with the family - Jim spent the evef at home - Bella Tanner Jim's nurse who went abroad with us in 1892 came in to see him - From 9 to 10 I played cards with Miss D -

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 15, 1906

**Weather:** A very disagreeable day - snow in the  **Temp:** [illegible]

mf - slush in the afternoon - It was after 10 before I got thro' with my routine after 10 \+ until noon, as the air was raw \+ my stomach still keeps up a grumbling I kept pretty closely to my big chair and awake - After 1 I had a very light meal, mainly of Rennet milk - a nap between 3 04 - then some moping about as my stomach condition is depressing - The condition of my hearing to-day has been better altho still far from satisfactory - a light meal at 6 30 \+ more discomfort in the evef which I tried to forget in playing cards with Miss D -
