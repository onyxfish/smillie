## Left Page

### Fri. Aug. 24, 1906

*[Written above header: Mr. \+ Mrs Marquand went thro' the Ark &c to dependencies \-]*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A glorious day, beautifully clear with cool north wind \- Adams came late \+ did a part of a days work. \- The mt'f was spent putting things in shape for the Marquand visit. Before 12 I went over to Club Ho. \+ saw Scott Brown \- got mail \+ back home to dinner at 1. About 2.30 P. Terry \+ wife \+ Mr. Marquand came \+ a few minutes later Mr. M. \+ daughter. They all went thro' the house enthusiastically \- then Mr. \+ Mr. M. went thro' the Annex where everything was in good shape, Kate \+ Katy having taken hold of clearing up in good spirit. After they left, with Ralph's help the water-pike where it crosses the drive way was found so that we know not the location of a "union" \- meas[urements] were made for pipes to tennis court \+ then R. went to the village \+ ordered 7 lengths \- I went to Mrs. W. A. Nelson \+ saw her about getting some small spruces \- while there Mr. Jno. Hawes \+ daughter called \- from there I walked down to find Beede's \- to see him about manure \+ hauling stone \- did not see him \- back home \- Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g commenced early as it was too cold to sit out of doors \- we read a couple of stories \- then, as R. had trouble with one of his eyes \+ I was very sleepy, we went to bed early.

Adams at work.

## Right Page

### Sat. Aug. 25, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Glorious day. Brilliant \+ cool. wind increased in afternoon \+ clouds came up from S.W. suggestive of weather change \- About 10 o'clk. R. \+ I went to the top of Roaring Brook fall, he took his camera \+ I loafed \- got home about 1 o'clk \+ had dinner delayed for half an hour. About 3 o'clk. went to P.O. \- after that called on Miss Miller at her cottage \+ had a chatty time for half an hour or more \- then went to cottage nearby to call on the misses Putnam \- no one at home \- then home \- Tea at 6.30 Eve'g. worked till 10 o'clk. Taking [illegible] prints from negatives recently developed but got nothing that was quite satisfactory \+ went to bed quite discontented \-

Adams put in a short day.
