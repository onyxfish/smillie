## Left Page

### Tues. May 22, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still bright \+ so cold that I wore my light overcoat — a delightful day but rather severe following such hot days as last friday \+ Sat. I had a number of things to do in my room after bkfs. so that it was 11 o'clk before I got to studio — worked till 1.30 on Quebec portrait with graver bringing the new body form together — then prepared my lunch — At 4.30 I went to liner \+ Arneuds — 3d Ave. \+ 18. S: Then to Bridgemans 19o 5. near Bdway \+ bought grassseed for S. Huberts \+ left locks to be sent with it to Albert Winch — then walked up to Touraine \+ at near 8 Jim \+ I dined — About 9 the Wellesley Sup. called me up to say that the man who could it make up his mind had about decided to stay \+ that a possible tenant for the other suite was to call to-morrow — So, it was up to me to do something decisive \+ rather unwillingly I said that I would take the apartment that I least like — The chance of not getting the other one is, as I surmised from the first, are exceedingly slim —

## Right Page

### Wed. May 23, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Signed lease for Wellesley apt.

A very fine day — Got up pretty early \+ before bkfs. wrote a note to Meta Zavit. (Mrs. Chas. P. Adams) to accompany two photos. of her father that I have had for many years — After bkfs. I was called up by E. A. Seardon, Sup. of the Wellesley — said he was ready to sign lease for apt. on 5th floor, not the one I most wanted on 4th fl. occupied by Mr. Young — I was disappointed, but as it is to my mind, the most desirable apt. I have yet seen I told him I w'd be up after 4 P.M. \+ sign — It was late before I got to studio. I laid ground upon plate for portrait of father. I also spent some time on photos. that I was to send to Cullen to mount — had my lunch at 2 — went to Touraine at 3 \+ at 4.30 went to the Wellesley \+ signed lease — I hope that I have not made a mistake — find that the house is run by a few son-in-law of the owner — After a pretty thorough inspection \+ going to the roof to see laundry arrangements, I went to the Riverside park. \+ then to Touraine at 6.20 Jim came in late \+ we had dinner together — later he went out \+ I went to Charls \+ there met Eddie Zabriske \+ his wife — Co. was laying out for them a short trip abroad during the coming summer — I got back to my rooms before 11.
