## Left Page

### Wed. Sept. 5, 1906

**Weather:** Bright, breezy & cool  **Temp:** Ther. fell to 44°

in my room last night & heavy frost down the valley is reported. I was up early & had a good fire in my bedroom. After 4½p. I set to work at writing — got off a number of bills with checks & wrote a note to Mr. Marquand in reply to his of Aug. 29 accepting lease of the Art. for summer of 1907. At noon R. & I went to P.O. & from there over to Wes. Otis's & thence home. — passing Grace Neilson's "studio" cabin, when we stopped a few moments to speak to her. Dinner at 1 — after that, with Earl on the road or hill to gate where, at a turn, he started under my direction, a wall to be built into the bank on one side — about 4 o'clk. I went over to the Hemlocks where I saw Mrs. Vauthorn. She told me that Mrs. Maghee was well enough to see me. I found her in her hammock on piazza. Miss Davis, who was here four years ago, was there & we chatted — I held the cat while a remarkably tame chipmunk came & took pea nuts from the others — it was a job to hold the cat. Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g I felt stupid & uncomfortable & napped on one of the box seats while R. read.

Adams worked alone for half a day.

## Right Page

### Thur. Sept. 6, 1906

**Weather:** Pleasant & much milder after a night when the  **Temp:** Ther. in my bedroom got down to 45°

This m't there was evidence of fires in my voine, passed during the night more than I have seen in a long time. Bkf't at 8. & later I went out with Earl & gave directions about cutting down some trees — then to house & fussing with Studio curtains free after 12 — then to P.O. — letter from Mary to Ralph & a lot of picture postal cards from Charl. from Sils Maria — Engadin — Aug. 27. Dinner at 1 — again with Sara — then a rest — then painted Ralph's snake for the Scott Brown entertainment to-morrow night — out among the trees again to see what Earl had accomplished. — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g Reading — Commenced on W.D. Howells' "A Shadow of a Dream" — sorry to find that R. has no taste for that kind of reading.
