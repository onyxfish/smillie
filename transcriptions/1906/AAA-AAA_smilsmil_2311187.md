### Fri. June 15, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very warm day \- Up at 7. b'clp' at 8.15 \+ got to studio before 10 \- I drew in the lines, on Etching, on Father's head before noon \- That completes the drawing in \- Lunch in studio after 1 \- Then for some time, getting plate ready for the acid \- Then I developed an 8×10 spool \- Then wrote a letter to Raeffen \- To my rooms before 5 \+ had a nap \- Jim came in late \+ we had dinner at 7.30 \- As we came out of the dining room we found Charl n one of the lounges waiting for us. We spent the evef together looking over a lot of photos. ## Right Page

### Sat. June 16, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day \- wet, humid, raw \- I got to studio at 10 \+ as soon as possible got things in shape to commence biting the portrait of Father \- my mordant, that is old did not act satisfactorily so I made up a new lot, but for some reason the progress was very slow \+ when I gave up at 4.30 I had not got far on with the work \- I had my lunch in studio between 1 \+ 2 o'clk. At about 5 o'clk I stopped at Charl's \+ was there for an hour \- went to my rooms at 6 \- Jim called me up before 7 on 'phone, from Yale Club saying he was there with Gould \+ would not be in till late \- I dined alone at 7.30 \- Evef passed stupidly \+ I went to bed at 10.
