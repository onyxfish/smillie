## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 16, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very disagreeable day — neither snow or rain but enough of both to keep everything wet + nasty — Up at 7.30 but was so long about my bath that I did not get my bkfst. till 9.30 — much of the rest of the mfg. was spent dawdling over news papers — At 2 o'clk I had a fine dinner Soles, fine drive in Yonkers — The afternoon + eveg. was spent in worrimcnt over the arrangement of my books — but I am gradually getting them into an approach to order — which brings me some comfort.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 17, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another nasty day, wet + slimy — Just enough snow + rain falling to keep the streets in a mess — Bkfst. at about 9 o'clk. did not go out of the house all day — I gave considerable time to writing a first draft for note or preface to go with Father's Autobiographic "Pilgrim-age". Lunch at 7.30 — more work on the re-arrangement of books — Dinner at 7 — fine coming in late. Eveg. — some reading and some more work among the books
