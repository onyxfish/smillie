## Left Page

### Tues. April 10, 1906

**Weather:** Bright m'g but soon clouded over  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ by 11 o'clk. Rain began to fall \+ in light showers it fell at intervals for the rest of the day. Ralph and I had b'kf'n at 9 - \+ after that he went to Dr. Tuttle about his eye. At 10 I went to Charl's for a few min., then back to my rooms \+ phoned to W. C. Cullen who said he could get a set of lantern slides made from my negatives if I would let him have them before noon. I selected 19 negatives \+ at once went to his place in W[illegible] street \+ left the negatives with him - then back to rooms where I found Ralph \+ we had lunch at 1 o'clk - then I had a rest for an hour - Later R \+ I went to studio. Back after 5 - Jim came \+ we dined at 7 - Eve'g Jim went to Harold Hows. Ralph went to Kneisel Quartett at Mendelssohn Hall - ticket from Mary Ireland - \+ I went for a little while to Charl's where I learned that Uncle Willie, 94 years old, had fallen a night or two ago \+ broken his hip.

## Right Page

### Wed. April 11, 1906

**Weather:** An April day - pleasant sunshine \+ several light showers  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph \+ I had b'kf. together at a little after 8 - then he went to see Dr. Tuttle. I went to studio before 10 \+ commenced work on plate. "Road side chapel near Lugano" - worked steadily till after 12 - then to lunch \+ back - prepared my lunch - Exp. man came \+ said that box of Antantics taken for Mr. Murphy a day or two ago was directed to Bradford, Conn. - No Bradford in Conn. - I went to Edw. Cook's \+ from Mary \+ Hy Murphy - learned correct address - Branford - here to studio \+ worked till after 4 - to my rooms at about 5 \+ had a rest - Jim, Ralph \+ I had dinner at 7. The boys have decided that it will be more satisfactory to them to share Jim's room together - so Jim has given up the Yale Club room. They went to Diet Kitchen concert at Waldorf - on tickets given them by Emma S—. Evening I wrote letters to Rob't. Neilson - J.M. Howell \+ Scott Brown with reference to S't. Hubert's matters - To Rob't. Neilson - Pres. of Ausable \+ Mt. Club - I sent $100 - initiation fee \+ annual dues - After 10 I went to studio \+ got a pillow.
