## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 21, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. 98°- 101°. Trouble seems now to be mostly in my face \+ ears \- P. T. is evidently much relieved by improved appearance of urine \- Trouble with bowels \- enema \- Irrigation of ears continued Strychnia, Acetanilid, Codeine \- Whisky \+ Bengoate of Soda \- I was bolstered up in my bed for awhile.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 22, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. 98°- 100°. Pain in ears \+ deafness the greatest discomfort \- much pain in frontal sinuses \- irrigation of ears, oil \+ glycerine continued. Considerable coughing \+ expectoration \- I had a mouthful of leaf steak at midday drugs continued.
