## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 24, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very cold day \- Ther. down to 9°. clear m'f \- clouded afternoon \- B'kf'. at 9 \- at 10 started for studio \- stopping at Miller's \- set Palette and fill 1 o'clk. Painted on sky of Cliffs of Normandy. a ticklish job \- there is too much paint on the sky to begin with \- if I scour it down then I will lose the toning of age \+ compared with the rest of the painting it will look Raw \- As it is I must do something with it \- so, I worked very carefully \+ timidly \- I think I bettered it. Geo. thought so too \- I feel it is not at all what I wish \- If I had more time before sending it to Washington I'd work at it with more courage \- at 1 o'clk I ate the lunch I took to studio with me \- Then I went to 2 Nat. Bank \- then to Putnam's \- then to Riker's then, by Elevated R.R., down to Wale St. to No. 11 \- Zoo \- office to see if I could get back No. 2 of Bulletin \- then to Charls \- there for half an hour \- Then to Cullen's \+ got Photo Supplies \- Then to Fussler \+ Weikels \- had inst'r for Excelsior Diary \- did not have the X. 2 I wanted \- Went by 3d Ave. Elevated to 23d St. got the Diary I wanted at a store there \- then to Studio \+ cleaned palette \- then home after 5 \- Cold \+ tired \- Took a stimulant \+ lay in my lounge till dinner at 6.30 [illegible]. In easy chair by my imitation coal fire and Read \- The boys at home \- Jim. played att and asleep \- Ralph Reading with now \+ then a bit of chat between us \-

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 25, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unusually cold, windy m'f. It was after 9 when I had b'kf'. The boys were later \+ then they went out \- I read newspapers \+ wrote for awhile on Preface to Father's autobiography \- Then, at about 1 o'clk \- Ralph \+ I had a half hours walk in Riverside Park \+ did not find it at all pleasant \- Kate made a mistake \+ we did not get our lunch till near 2 o'clk. Between 3 \+ 4 o'clk. I had a nap \- some Reading (R.L. Stevenson) till after 5 \- at 6, with the boys, went to Charl's \+, of course, had a very good dinner handsomely served \- After dinner C \- showed us many of the photos. that he took last summer \- \+ then he set up his Lantern \+ showed us a lot of slides. It was about 11 when we left for home \+ near midnight when we got to bed.
