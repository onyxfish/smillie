## Left Page

### Sun. May 6, 1906

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast day & rather cool — I got up feeling so much out of sorts that I had to give up my usual Sunday on't scrub — I am now passing more pus in my urine than at any time since I was at the hospital. & There is discomfort everywhere in my right lumbar region to attract my attention most of the time. I had bkfs. alone before 10. Read awhile, before noon went to studio, then went to 124 E. 57th S — looking for the Bradley's. They had moved — 230 E. 54th S — went there, saw them — They are not the Bradley's I'm looking for — don't know Kate Leonard — then I retraced my steps & went to Alice Ireland's in E. 40th S. Alice was in & had the True Bradley address — 956 Third Ave. near 58th S. I was invited to stay to lunch — Fred came in & lunch was served. Then I walked up to Mrs. B's & found her at home. There are two Bradleys employed at the Cathedral. When I told her what I wanted of Kate, her sister, she was much pleased & said she was sure Kate wd. be glad to come to me — She is employed at Saml. Sloane's 7 E. 38th St. & I can look into her kitchen from my Touraine window. Then I went to the Century — wrote a note to Emma Ireland — then to my rooms & at 7 Jim & I dined — Eve'g loafing stupidly.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 7, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather continues overcast. I got to studio about 10 & put in a fair days work on Study of an old mans head — bring a portrait of myself from enlargement of a photo. taken by Raefsn at St. Huberts in 1903. It was about 1 o'clk when I went to 2nd Nat'l. Band, & then back to studio where I had my lunch \+ suffered afterwards with flatulency and general misery. I worked on etching till 5 o'clk. Then tried to walk but was too much discomforted — then sought the seclusion & such comfort as I could get in my rooms at the Touraine. Dinner with Jim at 7. At 8 Kate Leonard came & we had a long talk — she is employed now by Mr. Saml. Sloane 7 E. 38th St. but is not altogether satisfied there — She promised to come to me in Oct. if I can find a suitable apartment — the prospect is not without promise — After she left Clark came to make further enquiries concerning whales, he is making notes for his Talk at the Century next Sat.
