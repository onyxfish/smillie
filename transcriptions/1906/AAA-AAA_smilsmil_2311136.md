## Left Page

### Wed. March 7, 1906

**Weather:** Dull  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day - some light sprinkling of rain - more at night - As Jim has to get away from the house before 7 - taking bkft in 6th Ave. I do not see him in the mng & have my bkft alone, I wrote to Ralph & Hattie Elkin - I propose to spend next Sunday in New Haven. Other letters detained me till near noon & then I went to studio for awhile - It was after 2 when I got to the Centurion & there had lunch - it was 3 when I got to my rooms - Dr. Yale came in later & spent some time with me - at 6 I thought it time to take my before dinner nap which was necessarily brief - Jim & I had dinner before 7 - he went to theatre with Harold Howe - I remained in my rooms, writing & fixing up matters.

## Right Page

### Thur. March 8, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pale day, very thinly veiled at times and quite mild - After a disturbed night I got up late & had my bkft after 9 - It was 11 when I got away from the house & went to Brooks Bros taking a coat that does not quite fit me - Then to Scribner's & had a very pleasant chat with Mr. H. L. Smith concerning Jim - I forgot to record in its place that Clarence Smith came while I was yet at bkft - & told me that Pease & Elliman are not living up to their agreement with me & that he Smithack claims has taken such shape & P.S. have "crabbed" it in such way, refusing to divide their com. with S - but expressing a readiness to compromise - they to give up $200 of the com. if I will pay the other $100. That Smith thinks I had better pay the $100 & close the matter. The course of P&E has been sharp & unscrupulous - I consented unwillingly - From Scribners I went to the vaults - then to 44 W. 25th S. & saw Bassie T - then to Wundersmith's to see Whistler etchings - then to Rund[illegible]'s to saw Shannon portraits - a great disappointment; don't come up to work I have seen of his in London - Then to Yale's & had him fix a troublesome corn so that I could walk with a little comfort - 2:30 when I got to Century & had lunch - a pleasant chat with Janvier - It was after 3 when I got to my rooms & I did not again go to the studio - Jim dined with friends - I dined alone & did a little writing in the evg.
