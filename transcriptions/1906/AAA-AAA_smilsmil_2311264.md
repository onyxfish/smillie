## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 16, 1906

**Weather:** Clouded m"f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

by noon it had cleared delightfully. - After a quiet night I awoke at 7 - stomach quiet - a cup of junket - a regular routine over at 10 - a bowl of gruel at 11 - m overcoat + cap I sat at open window till after 3 - at 2 30 had an egg - stomach still quiet - I moved about very little - at 3.30 a nap - Soon after 4 Chas. Collins came \+ I had a pleasant call of an hour or more - at 6 30 a light fish dinner. Eve was mostly given to cards with Miss D-

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 17, 1906

**Weather:** Some sunshine in the m"f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

but the weather gradually passed into a quiet misty, cloudy sort. - awake at 7 after a quiet night a light sleep, no discomfort in stomach. 10 30 when I got up after my bath. - Before noon I went to a barbers on B'dway - bet 80° \+ 81° St. \+ had my hair cut - then a little walk on Broadway. \+ back home to dinner soon after 1 o'elk. - After 2 miss D- \+ I went out to Riverside Park \+ walked there for half an hour or more - then home \+ had a little nap when Geo. came in. After he left I got up \+ wrote some notes. at 6 30 I ate a meal with some fear as my stomach is not as it should be, tho' not actually troubling. - Eve with newspaper \+ cards - Jim dined with the Dielmans - Charl ret. from Boston \+ I got a telephone message from him. My hearing to day is better than usual, but generally, I feel very weak \+ discouraged - I continue sub-normal - 97° to 98° - my vital force very low.
