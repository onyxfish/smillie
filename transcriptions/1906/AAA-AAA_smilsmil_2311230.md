## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 9, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very uncomfortably warm day \- Ther. in large Room getting up to 86°. A breeze made it bearable in doors but in the sunshine it was scorching \- Thunderstorms all about us in the eve'g \- \+ a sprinkle of rain \- Bkfs. at 9 \- skunk in trap \- after bkfs. R \- shot it \- Earl got infected to some extent \- Most of the m'g was spent getting photos. in large Room \- R \- developed them \- At 1.30 went to P.O. back to dinner at 2 \- After that loafing \+ anaps \- At about 5 R. \+ I went to Maghus met Miss Cockroft there \+ others \- Then we tramped over to Wes. Otis's \- but he was not at home \- Then home late to tea \- Eve'g, we read aloud for a while, finishing Howell's "The Shadow of a Dream" \+ "doing" a part of W. J. Bryan's Reply to letters from a Chinese Official.

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 10, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day \- brilliant, breezy \+ moderately warm \- After bkfs. got to work with R \- \+ tried nearly all the m'g to get a satisfactory photo. of interior of large Room (No. it was the afternoon when when R. \+ I worked at photographing \- in the m'g I worked at taking prints from the three photos. made yesterday and taking those prints as guides in making corrections \+ improvements. we tried flash lights \+ modifications of lighting, but the result was less favorable than the ones taken yesterday.) Went to P.O. at noon \- then dinner \+ after that the photographing as stated above \- After that I did some work in getting things together \+ making an inventory \- Tea at 6.30 \- a large part of the eve'g was given to a brush fire, managed by Earl \+ Harry in burning a great quantity of brush that has been accumulating for the past two or three years.
