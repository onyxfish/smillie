## Left Page

### Mon. April 16, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, altho' there was not much sunshine. Bkfst. with Ralph soon after 8 — then he went to Dr. Tuttle about his eye \+ later, with Alice Iraland \+ some others, to the Zoological Bronx Park where the day was spent — I got to studio about 10 \+ spent much of the mt. looking over a great pile of Annie's music — I had lunch in studio — worked on Lugano Chapel till 6 o'clk — Ralph came in \+ we had a walk up 5th Ave. — back to Touraine \+ had dinner with Jim at 7.15 Eve't Jim went somewhere — Ralph to theatn \+ at 9 I went to Charl's \+ was there till 11 looking over his slides \+ making a selection for the Century show.

Jim brought Harry Gould home with him about midnight \+ the three, Jim, Ralph \+ Gould, managed to get through the night someway.

## Right Page

### Tues. April 17, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day — Ralph \+ I had bkfst. after 8 o'clk. \+ later he \+ I went to Dr. Tuttle's — the Dr. examined R's eye carefully \+ said that it was improving very slowly \+ doubled the strength of his prescription of Zinc sulphi. Boric Acid \+ Camphor. \+ advised him not to tax his eye, either by reading or exposure to too much light. It was 11 when I got to studio — I got at my press with emery cloth \+ was surprised to find how much it had rusted — I tired myself out scrubbing at it \+ did not half finish the work. — Had my lunch in studio at 1.30 — After that I found frames for two of my etchings for W. C. Joclk show — About 4 o'clk. Mrs. Meyer came \+ stayed till 6 o'clk talking about our early landscape painters. She says she is gathering material to write a book in which she will show that due credit has not been given to the landscapists from Cole to Kensett. Jim \+ Ralph dined at the Yale Club. I dined alone. Charl came in at about 9 o'clk — he selected about half a dozen of my negatives to have lantern slides made from them — he left at 11 \+ it was near midnight when I got to bed.
