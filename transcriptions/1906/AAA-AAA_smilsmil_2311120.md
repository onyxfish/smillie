## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 3, 1906

**Weather:** Brilliant day \- cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day \- cold, but not so cold as yesterday. After Hkp'. wrote some notes \- then to studio gathering things to take with me on my trip. Just before 12 Supp. man came for steamer trunk, he took it to the Touraine \+ I followed him \- there the work of gathering things together went on preparatory to packing. At 1 had a light lunch \- after that a Rest. \- Then to the Palette in 23d. St. \+ bought some pens for sketching \- then to studio \+ back to my rooms at 6. Alice Ireland called me up on phone to say that Mollie Adams was there \- I promised to call in the eve'g. Dinner with Jim at 7 \- He went to some Architectural League function with Miss Dielmann on her invitation \- I went to Alice J's. \+ Talked with Mollie about the trip to West Indies \- She took same trip a year ago on same boat \- Back at 10, got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 4, 1906

**Weather:** Very fine day \- sunny \+ milder  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day \- sunny \+ milder \- up at 7 \+ had a good rub down \+ bath \+ down breakfast at 9 \- from that till 12 I was writing notes \+ getting things in shape to pack \- then to studio \- back at 1 \+ went with Jim to George's when we had dinner \- I left there about 3 \+ walked back to the Touraine where I podged around till near 5 \- then I had a nap \+ was wakened after 6 by a call from Saw?. \+ Mary Cook \+ Edna Murphy. They stayed till after 7 \- then Jim, who had come in with them \- went away \- I had a light supper \+ then till 10 was gathering things alongside of my steamer trunk for packing \-
