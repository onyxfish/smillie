## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 16, 1906

*my birthday, entering my 73d year*

**Weather:** Clouded m'fg. clearing in the afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

woke at 4 am. \+ took half an oz. \- 1 Castor oil \-
then to sleep again \- at 7 o'clk. \+ reported
progress \- I had my Wlfo. alone about 9 \- \+
then another report, much diluted \- feeling
weak \+ uncomfortable I spent most of the
mt on my lounge \- At 2 o'clk. I had a final
\+ interesting report consisting wholly of strips
of tenacious mucus like semi-transparent
skin \- some being 8 or 10 inches long \+ 2 in. wide
I wonder if my intestines have been lined
with that stuff \- it looks like it \- Then I
had my lunch in the restaurant \- Later I
promenaded for awhile on Park Ave. Eve'g
Jim \+ I dined at our Rst'nt \- At 8.30 I went
to Charl's \+ discussed events \+ investments
Back to Touraine at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 17, 1906

**Weather:** Clouded day. Wlfs. at usual time \- at  **Temp:** [illegible]

a little after 10 o'clk. I went down-town to
25 Broad St. \+, with Charl's card of introduction
called on Toby \+ Lamarche \- saw Mr. T \- had
a pleasant interview at which I learned some-
thing. \- was disappointed to find that the firm
made a specialty of trading in traction, electric light \+
gas stocks \+ could give me but little information con-
cerning N.Y. City municipal bonds \- without coming
to any definite conclusion I went to Charl's and
there met his Electric Mule man, Mr. Thompson,
who insisted upon taking me to Smith \+
at 20 Broad St. \- whose specialty is municipal
securities \- so, I went there \+ Smith talked us
almost wild but I escaped with only a confused
sense as to what will be the best thing for me to do
in making investments \- I went up town at 1 o'clk
\+ then had lunch at the Touraine \- I spent the
afternoon in my rooms asssorting \+ making up
into packages all the family photos, from both
sides of the house, \+ filing them away on closet
shelves \- at 7 had dinner with Jim \+ at 8 went
to Dr. Tuttle's \- 48 W. 49th St. \- Was there till 10
Talking over our projected trip to the West Indies
etc \- etc \- Then back to my Rooms \+ to bed, still
feeling unpleasant remnants of vertigo \-
