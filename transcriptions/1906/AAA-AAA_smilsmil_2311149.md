## Left Page

### Mon. April 2, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day, but still pretty sharp. To studio at 10 \+ spent the am in unpacking a case there \+ getting out the silver \+ many small articles of value to transfer to the safe I have in my apartment at the Touraine — At 12 I went to the bank \+ made deposit — on my return to studio I called Dr. Yale on the phone \+ asked him if he would go with me to Wmsburg. to see Henry Voigt — he agreed — I had lunch in studio \+ at 2.30 met him at the Manhatttan Hotel — took car \+ crossed Wmbg bridge \+ from the Plaza walked to V's house — Found him in — I think he is a broken down man \+ nothing can be expected of him He looks to me like a great big blister. We ret. to N.Y. as we came — I went to studio for a little while — then to Rooms \+ dined alone — Jim went to Yale Club — Evef with my photo-negatives —

Rec. a long \+ tart reply from Pease \+ Silliman to my note of yesterday — sent it to Clarence Smiith.

## Right Page

### Tues. April 3, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming, spring-like day — To studio at 10 \+ spent the am. Packing remaining books in two cases at studio — worked till 1.30 — then had lunch in studio — then took to Touraine Rooms the silver that I had packed yesterday to put it in the safe that I have there — then to barber's \+ had hair cut. There I went to Met. Mus. of Art \+ spent time till 5 o'clk. among the paintings — then back to Rooms \+ rested till 7 there Jim \+ I went to Murray Hill Hotel \+ had dinner — Evef I continued the numbering of negatives \+ cataloguing
