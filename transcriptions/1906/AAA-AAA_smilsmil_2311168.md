## Left Page

### Thur. May 10, 1906

**Weather:** clouded during the m'f. \+ unusually cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

clouded during the m'f. \+ unusually cold. The Ther. falling as low as 42°. I had an unusual flow of urine during the night with much pus \- at 9.30 a.m. I went to D'. Tuttle taking a specimen. He says that he does not believe that the kidney is yet diseased \- thinks irritation is in the sac along the ureter \+ probably, to some extent, in the bladder \- There is some discomfort, almost constant, in the region of the right kidney. I am much discouraged. It was after 10 when I got to studio \- worked awhile on study of old man's head \+ on trimming photos for mounting \- had my lunch in studio \- afternoon, I got out copies of Cat. of Etching Club to prepare them for binding \- owing to the pulling of damp prints into the cats of 1892 they were much wrinkled, These I put in water, to be put in press later \- after 5 I went to Wills' in 27th S. about the Studio Roof \- Store closed \- To Touraine after 6 \- Dinner with Jim at 7 \- Evg I went over assets since last Sels. to learn where I stand \+ to be able to follow for the next year with approach to sureness \-

## Right Page

### Fri. May 11, 1906

**Weather:** Brilliant cool m'f., clouded afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant cool m'f., clouded afternoon Thunderstorm at about 8 p.m. After bkft. at 9.30 I went to Wills, S. 27th S. \+ arranged that his men shall be at studio Monday m'f. to Repair Roof. Then to studio \+ spent the day, practically, in damping \+ pressing Etching pfs. \+ Photo. gms. portraits of Etchers in two copies of Etching Club Cats for that were badly wrinkled because they had been bound up before they were dry. I am going to have two sets bound half mo. I prepared my lunch at about 2 after having been to Manhattan Storage Warehouse \+ having arranged to have my goods there packed into a smaller room. I worked at studio till near 5 \- Then to Touraine \- Rested \- dinner with Jim at 7 \- Evg at 8.30 went to Charl's to have a Rehearsal of his Camera Talk for to-morrow evg \- but he did not feel well enough to set up his lantern, but we went over his notes.
