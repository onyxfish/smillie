## Left Page

### Fri. May 18, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very uncomfortably hot day - up above 80° somewhere - After bkfs. I went to Herald office \+ had an ad, put in for Sunday \+ mo. Wed. of next week offering the Ark for rent - then to studio about 11 \+ to work again at proving I did as well as could be effected having no weak oil to reduce my ink - I took pfo. of the Lippincott head \+ a couple of old man's head - I had lunch in studio - cleaned up before 5 o'clk - Then to my rooms Dinner with Jim about 7 - Evef. at my desk -

## Right Page

### Sat. May 19, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another withering hot day with a sudden change in the early evef. to very refreshingly cool - Up a little earlier than usual \+ got to studio before 10. Got to work at my printing hoping to be able to overcome the oil difficulty by using some of the old nitried ones that I have - worked without success getting some very hard specimens from the Lippincott head \+ the Chapell near Lugano \+ gave up further attempts about noon - cleaned up \+ prepared my lunch about 2 - about 4 I went to Water Color exbtn in W. 57. St. \+ there saw some etchings that discouraged me, because showing me how far short I come of the mark set by younger men - I was much pleased by some pft of Ernest Roth's recent work - I got to my rooms 8 at 7 Jim \+ I had dinner. Evef. I had appointed to meet Clare at the Century but Clare came in \+ spent the evef. with us tée after 10.
