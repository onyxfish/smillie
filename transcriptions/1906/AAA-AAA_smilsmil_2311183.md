## Left Page

### Thur. June 7, 1906

**Weather:** Dull day & quite cool  **Temp:**

Dull day & quite cool \- Bkfs\. at 8.30 \+ at about 9.30 I left the house, D. S\- accompanying me to the Winchester Ave. car \+ insisting upon carrying my suit case, which was fairly heavy. Part of the way\- I got to R.R. station much too early for to go train \+ reached N.Y. at 12.30 \+ went direct to my rooms\- Had lunch at 1 o'clk\. soon after Geo\. came in \+ then Kate Leonard \- she is evidently well pleased with the prospect before her \+ offered to go to St. Huberts, saying that she is now twenty years younger than when she was there last\- She may get a niece to go with her\- This proposition I at once accepted with satisfaction\- It presents the possibility of one more summer in the Adks. It was after 3 o'clk when I got to studio \- I cleaned up things preparatory to beginning work on etching portrait of father on his 75th birth day \- Back to Touraine about 6 \- Jim dined out \+ I dined alone at 7.30 Eve\# I was good for nothing. Indigestion [illegible — ~8 lines]

## Right Page

### Fri. June 8, 1906

**Weather:** Dull day again, quite cool & Raw  **Temp:**

Dull day again, quite cool \+ Raw till the afternoon when it cleared partially \- Up early \+ did some writing before Bkfs\' \- at 10 o clk\. I went to Second Nat\. Bank \+ on my way to studio stopped at Bayerdorfer's \+ ordered a watch for present to Jim \- Then to Lock's to arrange about work that I am to leave with him during the summer. To studio after 11 \- made gelatine tracing from photo\. of Father taken by Chas\. on Father's 75th birth day \- scraped, filled with vermilion \+ ran \+ thro\' press damped \+ on damp paper \- Had lunch in studio \+ at about 3 clk went down town to W. C. Cullen's \+ took tracing to him to have an enlargement of it made to a size to correspond to the reduction of my painting that I am to etch \- By this plan I shall use for the head the tracing made direct from the photo\. by this means I hope to get a more exact likeness. Returned to studio at about 4\. \+ busied myself there till after 5 \- Then to Touraine \+ there met Josephine (Will's widow) \- she had been waiting for me for some time \- She was with me in Reception Room for more than half an hour \- Jim \+ I dined together after 7\. Evenly under the spell of a severe indigestion which prevented me from doing any thing \+ at last I went to bed to get rid of myself.
