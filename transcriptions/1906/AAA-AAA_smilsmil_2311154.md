## Left Page

### Thur. April 12, 1906

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - made a mistake + got up an hour earlier than I thought - Bkfs. alone before 8 - I went to Douglas's + ordered a small sign to put on alley end of studio's bldg., threatening prosecution for any one leaving ashes or garbage there - It was after 10 before I got to studio - Finished work with point on Chapel near Lugano + had some trouble in getting Asph. varnish in such shape that I could use it, but at last got plate ready for the acid - Prepared lunch before 2 - then got glass for some frames cut from old, damaged glass that I had - + got some Rfs. - Ready for Water-Color, etching &c - It was after 5 when I got to my rooms - \+ had a rest - Jim dined at Yale Club - Raeker had two College fellows dine with him + they went to theatre after - I read news papers + wrote titles on mounted photos.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 13, 1906

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day. To studio at about 10 + spent the mf in the very dirty work of clearing out the stuff of all sorts that has been cluttering up the "dark Room" so that I might "bite in" the new work just kept upon the Chapel near Lugano plate - It was a dreadful Task - It is now nearly a year since it was last cleaned - At last I got it into good shape + ready for work - I had my lunch in studio + worked with acid on the Lugano Chapel till near 5 o'clk then to Furraine - had a rest - Dinner with the boys - "we" I rec. P&P from Carleen with about 30 lantern slides from negatives of my west Indiana trip + a couple of enlargements with these, at about 8 o'clk I went to Chares' to meet the Century Art Com. to whom he is to show a lot of his lantern slides with a view to an invitation to show them at the Century - Wil Low came first, just before 9 - Frank Millet followed a little after - Bielman did not come at all + did not send any words - Chare commenced at once + in an hour + a quarter ran off about 140 slides - \+ all expressed themselves greatly pleased + said they would be glad to have him show a lot of his slides at a date as early as could be fixed - Then Cigars + something wet took up the time till midnight when we dispersed.
