## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 4, 1906

**Weather:** Cloudy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy most of the day \+ unseasonably mild \- Ralph \+ I spent most of the m't at the studio \- we got his trunk from the cellar up to my studio \+ he worked \+ unpacking \+ sorting contents \- I packed a lot of the books, given to the boys as presents, in a box to be eventually given away \- we rel'd. To Touraine after 1 \- R\- had his lunch in Restaurant \- I prepared a bowl of junket for myself \+ again went to studio \+ continued my work there as long as I could see \- then to Touraine \- had a rest \- J, R, \+ self dined at the Murray Hill Hotel \- After that we went to Charles' \- Later in the evg. W. Heinck of Po'keepsie, called with note of introduction from Uncle Willie \- He spent about an hour in chatting with Eliz'th. \+ the boys \- Back to our quarters at 11.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 5, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day with many changes from sunshine to shadow. Because Jim forgot to set his Alarm clock, we all slept till near 8, consequently had a late b'kf't. \- I got to studio at 10.30 \- there till 12.30 \- most of the time arranging contents of trunks that are to be put in my studio cellar \- then to bank \+ made deposit \- then to Touraine \- prepared my lunch \+ ate it \- At 3 o'clk. went to Charles' \+ saw Mary \+ Katharine \+ bid them good bye as they go to Po'keepsie this p.m. \- Then I went to Century \+ got visitors cards for the Twelfth Night fake picture show Sunday \+ Monday p.m. Then again to studio \- there till near 5 \- then to Touraine \+ rested till dinner \- At 7 o'clk. Jim, Ralph \+ I went to Manhattan Hotel \+ dined \- After that Jim went his way \+ R\- \+ I went to Needlession Hall in 40th S\-, meeting of the Geographical Soc'y where we heard Prof. Nordenskjöeld, Prof of Geology \+ Metalurgy at the Swedish University at Upsala give an acct. of his experiences in the Antarche \- He showed some interesting pictures \- his english composition was good but his pronunciation was so wild that I understood but little of what he said \- we met Charl \+ Basie there \+ sat by them
