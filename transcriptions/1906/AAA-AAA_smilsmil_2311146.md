## Left Page

### Tues. March 27, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another horrible day — I got up feeling on a par with the weather — my misery increased as the day wore on. I got to studio at 10.30 \+ worked at Etching mounts — Mary Cook came in to say that two of her invited guests for the dinner to-night would not be able to come on acct of illness \+ said that the dinner wd. be an informal family affair — I had lunch at studio — at 3 o'clk I gave up \+ started for my Rooms intending to go to bed — on my way met Yale \+ Gerster coming to see me — I ret. to studio \+ for sometime they looked at pfps. I had mounted — Then they accompanied me to my Rooms at the Touraine. It was after 5 when I lay down — at 6 I was so completely used up that I asked Jim to 'phone Ed. Cook that I was too ill to dine there — after 7 I rallied \+ had a cup of tea — at 9 I wobbled around to Charles to show him prints from last Roll, just Rec'd from Cullen — There for a short time then back \+ to my bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. March 28, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant, almost spring-like day. I awoke very early with a heavy head \+ general misery — So. I determined to take a Castor oil clearing out — \+ I did — blfp. after \+ for some time after was engaged paying penalties — to studio after 11 \+ completed the mounting of pfp. for Pub. [illegible] $[illegible]. At 1.30 I had a simple lunch in studio — worked till about 4 — then walked down to 25th St. \+ called on Mary — It was after 6 when I got to Touraine — after 7 when Jim \+ I had dinner — Eve'g he went out \+ at 8 I went to Edw. Cook's to apologize for my misbehavior of the eve'g before — Saw Edw. Jack. Edna, [illegible] Murphy \+ his father — left there at 8.30 \+ went to Chares. to show him some interesting photos just rec'd from Mo. F. Victor taken while on our West Indian trip — back to Rooms \+ to bed at 11 —
