## Left Page

### Wed. Sept. 19, 1906

**Weather:** Warm, pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Warm, pleasant day — Chase \+ Edmonds came \+ worked all day finishing the carpenters work except putting in the rod to support the bath-tub — I worked all day too at many things, getting very tired but without unpleasant sense of exhaustion. After 12 went to Club house — wrote notes to P.M. Station 7 — to Kellogg \+ Dort to Jim — To- dinner late — after that I had a rest for ¾ of an hour — Then at it again. Beede sent up three loads of manure most of which was put on the tennis court — Tea at 6.30 — Eveng. in a big bare room — a little reading \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Thur. Sept. 20, 1906

**Weather:** Very warm m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm m'f. — Showers in the early afternoon — then clearing — I got up at 6 o'clk. \+ to work — bkfs. at 8 — Adams \+ his son came \+ got to work but were driven off by the showers — R. \+ I had some photo- portrait work that took up an hour or more of precious time — at noon I went to P. O. Dinner at 1 — after that a short rest — Ferd Beede came \+ I paid his bill. R. \+ I packed box that is to be sent to N.Y. Robt. Nielson with a friend called — I had a short rest before tea at 6.30 — Evef. looked over news paper — then making out checks in paymts. of bills till aftng. — then some packing.
