## Left Page

### Wed. April 4, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant mild \+ spring-like day — To studio at 10 \+ packed books in two cases during the m'f. At noon went to bank \+ then back to studio \+ had my lunch there \+ at about two o'clk went to Touraine taking the last of my silver with me — There I made a complete inventory of all that was already in my apartment safe \+ what I had taken from the studio — I carefully rewrapped the silver in tissue \+ packed it all in safe — This took me till near 5 o'clk, then I went to Lotos Club on ticket from Geo. \+ spent an hour looking at the W. T. Evans collection of paintings — It had a fine Winslow Homer — Surf \+ Rocks \+ several charming Wyants — The bulk of his collection I don't care for. Had dinner with Jim at 7 — Spent the eve catalogueing my West Indian negatives [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~10 words]

## Right Page

### Thur. April 5, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast, with threats of coming storm. Got up with a very heavy head which grew better as the day went on — I got to studio about 10 30 \+ spent the m'f getting things ready to go to work Etch-ing — I went to Lincoln Bank \+ deposited $90 to Co. of Jim's acct. there — I had my lunch in studio \+ after that worked with crayon on a p[illegible] of Chapell by roadside near Lugano — To my rooms after 5 \+ had a rest before dinner at 7 with Jim. I worked upon the catalogueing of my west Indian negatives.
