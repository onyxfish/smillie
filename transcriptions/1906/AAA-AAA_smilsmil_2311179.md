## Left Page

### Wed. May 30, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Decoration Day.

A lovely day. I did not get up till about 8 - consequently was later than usual in getting to the studio - I trimmed some photo. prints sent to me by Cullen + then got to work, taking up first Study of an old man's Head + scraped down parts of the face shading that is yet too strong - After 12 I went out for a sunning - went to Bryant Park having my 4A Camera in my pocket - \+ took snaps at various unconscious posers - then to Touraine + had lunch - then to studio + worked on zinc plate, Chapel near Lugano, scraping down over bitten portions - at 4.30 I started out for an airing - walked up 5th Ave. to Park - immense crowds of all sorts of people - the vegetation exceedingly beautiful - I walked up to the wall then out to Mad. Ave. + 70th St. + then a car - I went to the Manhattan at 7.30 + had a Table d'hote dinner, Jim having gone to West Point to see a ball game. Eve. in my rooms - wrote to Meta Gavit Adams enquiring if she had Rec'd. the photo's of her father that I sent to her a week ago.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 31, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Light shower in early m'f. - overcast during the rest of the day with some light showers - Up at 7.30 + got to studio + got to work as soon as I could at printing - I had considerable print experimenting to do with oils, ræp, paper etc. but managed to get some fairly good prints from Father, Quebec 1829 + from study of an old man's head - It was after 1 o'clk when I washed up for lunch + went to Douglas's \+ got oil + turpentine. I continued my printing work till after 4 - then feeling tired I finished for the day + after 5 got to the Touraine - When Jim came in we went to the Manhattan + had a table d'hote dinner there. Eve'g. I wrote to Ralph + Cousin Katey about my proposed visit to New Haven.
