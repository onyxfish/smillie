## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 28, 1906

**Weather:** overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast - with some threats of Rain in the m%. Up at 8 after an unrestful night — wakeful \+ dreaming — consequent feeling very seedy — After bkfs: walked to P.O. sub station W. Cor. Columbus Ave \+ 84th S. \+ got stamps — did not go out again during the day. On my Return called up Cons. Gas Co., Branch 4 W. 42d, St to enquire why I had not yet rec'd a bill for gas burned since Oct. 1st. Was told that there was no application for service on file from me. I wrote a note at once explaining that I had made personal application at the office 112 W. 42d St on the afternoon of Sept. 26th — \+ stating that I was prepared to pay for all gas burned since Oct. 1st but not for any before that date. Lunch after 1 o'clk. During a part of the afternoon I arranged the books, for which I have no shelf room, on the floor according to sizes so that I may know what order to give to the Globe — Wernicke for their "Elastic" book cases. Jim did not come home to dinner — so, I had mine alone — The work in his office is now driving the employees very hard.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 29, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Thanksgiving Day.

Brilliant day. Cold windy m%. — quieter afternoon — beautiful, clear, cold moonlight night — Up at about 8 — Jim \+ I had bkfs. at about 9 — we had a rather long walk in Riverside Park \+ I came in perishing with the cold — my temp. has not yet got up to normal — Jim \+ I worked for some time in arranging a mass of books so that I could measure the shelf length required \+ the different heights as I propose (or purpose) to buy "Elastic" book cases — At 2 o'clk. we had our Thanksgiving dinner — a very good one \+ was. Kathryn had decorated the centre of the table with a bunch of immense yellow \+ white tissue Chrysanthemums — they were very effective. Chad came at about 3 \+ spent the afternoon with us — about 7 o'clk. Jim \+ I went out \+ had a moonlight walk on Riverside drive — At 9 o'clk. I had some Toast \+ Malted Milk.
