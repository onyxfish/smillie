## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 6, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Trumbull's carpenters came.

A muggy mf. clearing very hot - at noon a brisk thunder-shower of short duration. I was caught mit on my way to P.O. \+ had to stop for some minutes at Miss Mitchell's cottage - She was not at home. Steaming hot home to dinner - another heavy shower at 4 after which it cleared cooler. I had a full days work following the two carpenters that Trumbull sent up - They were waiting for me when I went to bkf. - I got them at work at once \+ they got the partition between rooms that were occu-pied by Sister Kate \+ Katharine T. down \+ moved north, enlarging the corner room 2' 6" \+ lessening the other room by that much - which is to be a lined closet, while the old linen closet is to be a bath room. They got the partition set up again in very good shape with far less marring than I had expected. After they left I moved furniture about and took contents of linen room to S.W. Room to wait till new closet is ready. I really did a pretty good days work - I had Moody cut out a lot of the young pines that were crowding at the north of the house, opening up a wood view that is very pleasing to me. Tea at 6.30 - Evef R. went over to the Club Ho. with Kath. H. \+ I had a lone watch - on N. piazza till 9 - Then in doors \+ read till 10 when R. came home - fine moonlight.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 7, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Early mf. cloudy but the weather soon cleared to be delicious - The carpenters were late in coming - 8 o'clk. - Ralph went on a picnic with the Hardenbergs soon after bkf. \+ was gone till seven. I took the carpenters into his room \+ had the partition on north side of it torn down, to move it about 2 feet to enlarge - it made a wreck of his room but he was able to sleep in it at night. At noon I went to the Howell cottage \+ had a pleasant chat with him \+ his sister - saw his new bath room \+ made notes. Had lunch at 1.30 - I directed Moody in the cutting down of a number of well grown pine trees to S.W. of house - The opening up being a great improvement - It was a busy day for me. R. came home at 7 \+ then we had dinner - After the usual lounge on N. piazza till 9 - then some reading - \+ to bed.
