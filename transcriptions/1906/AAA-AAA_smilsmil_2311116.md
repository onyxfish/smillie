## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 26, 1906

**Weather:** Pleasant but with a raw tang in the air.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got to studio soon after 10 \+ fixed my table so that I can now etch at it if I will — An expressman came from Miss Decker of the Corlies branch of Charity Orgn. Socty \+ I gave him four packages of books — Then Yale came in \+ I gave him the three or four plates of Swain Gifford's that I got from Voigt a day or two ago. Then I went to bank — then to P.O. 23d St \+ 4th ave. \+ got money order for $25 — to send to Roth in Florence — then to Touraine \+ had my lunch at 2 o'clk — Men a Rest \+ did not go out till near 6 — then had quite a walk \+ did some shopping — Jim \+ I had our dinner at 7.30 — Evef I finished my letter to Roth \+ posted it.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 27, 1906

*Eleventh anniversary of Annie's death.*

**Weather:** A fine day \+ milder than yesterday —  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got down to the Tax office, Bdway \+ Chambers St. at 10 o'clk \+ had no trouble in getting admittance at once to the officials — I was surprised to find them really helpful — one said to me "You need not tell us of the injustice \+ hardships of personal Tax as it now stands — we have to do with it every day \+ know it all" but as they have no discretion I shall have to pay Tax on $10000 that I had in the Trust Co on Jany 8th the day set by law as the test day of the year — A few days later that money was invested in non-taxable stock — Too late to escape — Then to Ham. Ave. office \+ got more baggage tags — then to Charl's office — not in — then to Cullen's \+ back to Touraine at 12 — As my digestives are behaving badly I took no lunch — passed about till 4 o'clk — then to studio — Back to my rooms at 6 — dinner with Jim at 7 — after 8 went to Charl's \+ at 9 at Century where Mr. Gorham P. Stevens lectured for nearly an hour giving photo. views \+ architectural drawings of the Mekhtium at Athens — to me an exceedingly interesting, prosaic presentation of an interesting \+ romantic subject — got to my rooms about 10.30 \+ being out of sorts I tried to set myself right by taking a bottle of Citrate of Magnesia —

I wrote to Ralph in the afternoon.
