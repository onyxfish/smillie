## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 30, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. 97° to 98°. Had a very severe attack of pain early in the mte which for a few minutes alarmed — the chest was rubbed \+ heat applied \+ in an hour or two it had entirely disappeared. I was wrapped in blankets \+ sat in my chair for a couple of hours.
