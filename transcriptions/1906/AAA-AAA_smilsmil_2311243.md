## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 5, 1906

**Weather:** Rain in the m'f., clearing to be pleasant in the afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain in the m'f., clearing to be pleasant in the afternoon — Awoke at about 5 a.m. with headache — Took a dose of Castor oil which made me sick \+ unhappy about 7-8 o'clk. I did not go out all day but worked with little spirit about the house — Lunch at 1 o'clk — feeling better in the afternoon I wrote a short letter to Mary. Just before 6 o'clk. Harris, a friend of Jim's called — J— came in at 6 \+H. left shortly after — then J. \+ I had dinner. Every we were each engaged in our own quarters "clearing up" —

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 6, 1906

**Weather:** Some sunshine in the early m'f., then clouds \+ about noon a smart shower  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some sunshine in the early m'f., then clouds \+ about noon a smart shower. After bkfp — I went to Butcher's \+ grocers then down town to Winter \+ Smillie's in Wall St. Jouis Calpon got Custom House permits to go within Customs lines on the pier on arrival of "Kaiser Wm. II." Then to W.U. Tel. Co. Bdway \+ Day St. \+ arranged for Tel. notice of arrival of S.S. — Then to Tax Office — \+fo°. taxes — Personal \+ on Studio — [illegible] the assessed value raised to $14500. Then home. Had lunch — Afternoon I did some good work arranging \+ packing away things — The last of my things at Socks was delivered. I dined alone, Jim dined with Harris \+ went to theatre with him \+ came in after 2. a.m. I worked at putting away things till after 10.
