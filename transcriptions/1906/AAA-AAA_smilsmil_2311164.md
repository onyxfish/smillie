## Left Page

### Wed. May 2, 1906

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast m't with some light rain. Before noon it was brighter but about the middle of the afternoon there was a light shower \+ at 10 p.m. a thunder storm passed south of us. I got up at about 8 feeling seedy, but better than for some days past — after bkfst I wrote notes to Helen Butler \+ Emma J — It was after 11 when I went to Bayerd orfer taking the pendulum of Fall clock for repair. Then to studio where I spent a little time working on one of Charl's lantern slides with little success. So troubled by indigestion that I had no lunch — Otto Sandresczki came in to tell me that he was again giving all of his time to painting. I got back to rooms at about 5 — Had dinner with Jim at 7 — Eve'g he slept in his chair \+ I pottered about doing odds \+ ends of work.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 3, 1906

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant — I got up about 4.30 \+ partook of a Castoroil cocktail — on acct. of which I had to rise hurriedly at about 6 \+ again at 8 — I then prepared for bkfst. which I had at 9 \+ got to studio at 10 — at 11 Wills, the roofer came \+ we went to the roof which was found to be in bad condition. I worried around without accomplishing any thing till near 2 o'clk when I had lunch in my studio. I ret'd to Touraine at 5 with a lost day on my conscience — Jim had his friend Kinney to dine with him — we dined together at 7 — Later, they went out \+ I passed the eve'g reading magazines.
