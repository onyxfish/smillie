## Left Page

### Sun. June 17, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, muggy day \- east wind \- I was up two or three times during the night to see if Jim had come in \- at 4.30 I put out his light that I had left burning for him \- I got up at 7 o'clk \+ had my bath \- Help \- after 8 \- If the day had been fine Charl \+ I were to go to Bronxville to see George's place in Lawrence Park. But as the weather was unfit I decided to go to the studio \+ continue the biting of portrait of father \- no word from Jim \- to studio at 10 \- worked till 1 o'clk \- then lunch \- then went to Charl's \+ sat here till near 3 \- again to studio \+ worked till after 4 \- then a stroll \+ to Tourance about 5 \- no word from Jim \- I waited till 7.30 \- then had dinner \- after 8 I called up Gould in Yonkers \+ found that Jim was there \- no reason given why he had not informed me of his whereabouts. Charl came in \+ stayed till 9, \- Jim came in at about 10 \- sorry, but could not I see that there was any cause for worrying on my part \- I might have guessed where he was.

## Right Page

### Mon. June 18, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable day \- dark \+ at times wet. up at 7 \- to studio at 10 \- continued the biting of etched portrait of father till it was completed after 1 o'clk \- then had my lunch \- then cleaned the plate \- I think the biting is successful \- in view of my plan of work in finishing it. worked on it with graver till near 5 o'clk \- cleaned up \+ had a saunter on the streets till about 6.30 \- Jim came in at 7 \+ we had dinner at 7.30 \- He went out later \+ I got thro' with a stupid evening. early \+ went to bed \- Rec'd. an interesting letter from Bessie from Fusole June 6".
