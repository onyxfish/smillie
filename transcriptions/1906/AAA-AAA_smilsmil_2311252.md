## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 23, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. 99°. Pulse running at 84. Codein \+ Acetanilid to reduce pain. Dr. Tuttle took a considerable quantity of old, hardened wax from both ears, using a soda solution — the treatment for the past few days had prepared \+ softened the wax for removal. — The neuralgic pain in ears was much reduced but there seemed to be no lessening of the deafness. Coughed \+ expectorated freely.
