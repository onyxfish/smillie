## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 25, 1906

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast m.f. clearing later — I got up with severe headache \+ had a glass of malted milk for bkfs. I was able to be about but unfit for anything — as sunshine came out before noon I took some more olio prints from Ark negatives. Had a light lunch in Touraine Restaurant — then on my lounge till 3 o'clk — then went to Wellesley \+ saw Deardon, the Supt. The apts. having been vacated yesterday we went thro' it — the dirt \+ confusion made everything look worse than it really is \+ I was much depressed by it. It is very common when compared with the Touraine — I regret that I took at three year lease altho' my reasons for doing so seemed good at the time. I walked to 5 ave. \+ across the Park to Di Gaston's — saw caretaker \+ to her grave — receipt bill of lading from Westport for printing press, with instructions. Then rode back to Touraine — used up — lay upon my lounge till near 9 o'clk — then to Cutting \+ had a cup of tea — then back as I saw no one, but to call Farragut. To bed before 11 — after writing to Kate Deardon instructing her to go to Wellesley, take a look at the Kitchen, then select \+ order from Waanamakers a Kitchen outfit.

## Right Page

### Wed. Sept. 26, 1906

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — partly clouded — Bkfs. alone at 8 — It was after 9 when I went to storage warehouse \+ arranged to be there at 2 o'clk tomorrow to get such things out as are to be sent to The Wellesley — these to be sent up in a van on Friday — then I went to Lochs \+ saw father \+ son \+ arranged to have them send such furniture of mine as they have to the W. on Sat. \+ to get my belongings in the Touraine \+ take them too so that I may make the change from T. to W. on Sat. if Kate can get my kitchen outfit there on that day as I wrote her last night — Then to studio for a short time — To Touraine at 1 o'clk \+ had lunch — then some more olio printing \+ after 3 o'clk I went to Con. Gas. — 112 W. 42d St. \+ made application for gas at the Wellesley — then to the Algonquin — W. 45th St. to see Alice Ireland, but she had left — then walked around the streets till after 5 — then to 14 I — Jim came in at 6 — dinner at 7 — Evening lying around — trouble with stomach \+ bowels.
