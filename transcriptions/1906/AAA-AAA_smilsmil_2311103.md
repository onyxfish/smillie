## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 1, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine, clear, cold winter day. I had bffs alone at about 8.30 + an hour later went to the studio — too cold — got milk at Hahnenfetetts + then ret. to the Touraine — wrote a note to Kitchen Cook relative to the books I sent her on Sat. About 12 went to studio with Jim + Ralph + they sorted the gift books of many years that they have there. — Back to Touraine soon after 1 o'clk. The boys then had their breakfasts + I had my Junket. They went off soon after to call on Katharine Hardenburgh + did not return till after I got to bed. At 4.30 Charl + Katharine Throat came + made a call. At 5.30 I had a nap. At 7.15 got a telephone from Jim that they were going to stay where they were. At 7.30 I had dinner at the Murray Hill Hotel + at 8.30 called on Fred + Alice Ireland. Met Miss [illegible] there. Fred left to call on his sisters + I had a pleasant eveg with Alice + her friend.
