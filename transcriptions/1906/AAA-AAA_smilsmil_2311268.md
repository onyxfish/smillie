## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 24, 1906

**Weather:** Cold + windy - cloudy mt.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold + windy - cloudy mt. - clearing afternoon - Bkf. in bed at 8 - then up - feeling rather more than usual played-out - no doubt I did too much yesterday in walking across from Presb.tn. Hospital - I went out for a walk in the mt. but between the disagreeable weather + my slim feelings I stayed out for only 15 min. I did a great deal of loafing in-doors. my bowels are giving me trouble, action causes discomfort. Jim left for New H. soon after bkf. Taking my place at the Yale-Harvard foot ball game. I greatly regret my inability to be there - the young people + their excitement is to me an inspiration.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 25, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day - breezy + cool in the mt. but by noon it was much quieter + milder - I woke late + did not get my bkf. till after 9 - in bed as usual - It was late before I got up + was dressed - it was near noon before I went out to the Drive + Park - I was out for an hour + a half - found some seats sheltered from the wind + in the sunshine where I lounged - I enjoyed it greatly altho' I still feel miserably weak + uncomfortable. Home at 1.30 + had a light lunch + at 2.30 went to Char's + spent the rest of the afternoon with him - most of the time looking over his last summer photos. He came up town with me before 5 o'clk - but did not come in - he was out for exercise - I had dinner at 6.30 - Jim came in at about 8 - + had a cold dinner - It did not take him long to tell all that he had to tell about the great game yesterday - my boys are not gushers.
