## Left Page

### Thur. July 5, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Glorious day, brilliant \+ cool. Ther. in-doors at 64° last night it fell to 44°. Up at about 7. a short walk before bkfs. soon after 8 \+ at 9.20 we started on our way to the Ark. Ford. Budi man John driving. baggage left to follow. At B.B. Istes stopped \+ got a lot of food stores. To Saunders \+ got a beef steak. At Kelly's got Butter, milk, eggs \+ potatoes. Then onto the Ark, getting there at about 1 o'clk. Found Albert Winch waiting for us. water turned on \+ fire in the range. It was 2.30 when at last Ralph \+ I got our dinner. After that there was lots to do \+ I kept dodging about till so tired that I lay down upon my bed \+ had a dozy kind of rest till after 6 when Besse came with our trunks \+ R's bicycle. Then we spent an hour or more unpacking. It was after 8 when we went over to the Reserve dining hall \+ got a loaf of bread \+ some matches from Mrs. [illegible] in charge. Then back \+ had a light supper before 9. Glorious moonlight but too cool to be enjoyable. I kindled a fire in my bedroom \+ we sat there for a while.

Winch told me a long story concerning the hold-up of Water. It is entirely at variance with Brown's statement to me over the 'phone.

## Right Page

### Fri. July 6, 1906

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day after a cold night. Ther. fell to 40°. I got up at 7. R. \+ I had bkfs. after 8 o'clk. Then we wandered over the place till near 10 o'clk. Then I went to the Van Horn-Maghee place. Saw Mr. Hardenburg \+ then to the Maghee cottage. Mr. burned his face badly last night with fire works. he was lying on a lounge, his face bandaged, his right eye covered. I chatted with him \+ Mrs. M. till after 11. then I went to Club House (hotel) \+ saw Robt. Neilson, Mr. Openhrym. Mr. Booraim \+ others. At P.O. got letters. a short one from Bessie at Rivau Garda a short note from Mary \+ an interesting type-written letter from Scott Brown. an olive branch. Went to the Ark \+ wrote to Jim \+ Charl. \+ sent the notes to P.O. by the boy. Had a frugal meal. no meat. at 1.30. There worrying with telephone to Arney. unpacking trunks \+ store rooms till I was so tired that at 5 o'clk. I lay down on my bed \+ had a nap. Supper at 7. Self R. \+ I had a quiet time. enjoyed a fire on our big hearth.
