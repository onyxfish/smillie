## Left Page

### March, Friday 19, 1875

Bleak & dismal, but nothing worse than threatenings. Mrs went to Burgdoff's — then to Hayek's — 10th ave + 44th St. Did not get to studio until 11. Tried to do some work — no success — To dinner at 12.45 — to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30 — at [illegible] went to Dewey's — Gauthier's — Snedecor's + then to tea at 7 — At 8, again at studio — did a little writing — at 9 — at Nicoll's — W.C. business — then until 10.30 again to studio — then to bed at 11.45 —

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 20, 1875

Sleet and snow — After bkfst went to F. N. Smith's house — there until after 10. Talking W.C. business — then down to 4th M C.A. Bldg + a long call on Mrs Hart — then in to Birche's, Thompson's + Mrs. [illegible] — To dinner at 1 — at 2, at studio + the afternoon spent in trying to cypher out some water color averages + writing on the new constitution. To tea at 6.30 — To fifth Phil. concert at 8 — Enjoyed it very much — walked to 41st St alone + to bed about 11.
