## Left Page

### October, Sunday 3, 1875

Charming day — up to a late bkfst. Then to studio
there until church time then to Baptist Ch. + heard
M. C. preach from the text,
"And I, if I be lifted up from
the earth will draw all men
after me." Again to studio
there until 1.30 — then to dinn
After dinner Ella + [illegible],
Courtwright, Gus + I wandered
about my place until after
3 — then I wrote to Gocbp[?],
A. Sturgis + Charl — to tea
at 6 — Evett went to Baptist
Ch. M. C. preached an excellent
discourse from 73 Psalms. 23 + 4
v. After service took Mrs +
Miss C. to Mr. Bullard's to see
the Night Blooming Cereus — at
9, to studio. mote for awhile
there to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 4, 1875

Pleasant day. Got to studio
about 9. Spent some time
passing + finally got to work
on 9×15. Painted some time ago,
a glimpse of the Hudson from
back of "Peaks Kill". I repainted
much of it — To dinner abt 1.5
back soon after 2 and worked
as long as I could see — informing
the painting very much, I
think. Tea at 6 — again
to studio to fix fires — then
at 7.15 went to take Elora
to Gus E's. A sudden
shower surprised us, but
at 8 we went — Fred C —
there — 4 games of whist, the
girls against Fred + me —
had a very good time —
left abt 10.30 — To bed before
11. [illegible — ~3 words]
