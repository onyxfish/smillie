## Left Page

### June, Friday 11, 1875

Very warm day — at 9.30 started out, went to Toy's lot & commenced new sketch but my head troubled me so that I got very little done. Although very warm, it was breezy & charming under the trees. At 12 returned to the house, dinner at 1 and until 4 gave way to my headache — At 5 Kate & I went out in Mr. Nogue's phaeton — drove down to Barnegat — back at 7 after a delightful ride — bolted my supper & then hurried to Main St. dock — got there at 7.30, just as the Mary Powell came up — Belle & Mary & ready on board — Rode home. Tea for them. Matt & E. came — a short moonlight stroll at 9 o'clk — then chatting over ale — & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 12, 1875

Clear breezy m'f. Bkfs. at 8 o'clk then in town — back at 9.30. Horses & wagon from livery — Belle, Mary, Geo. & I went out — down to Barnegat then back & out on North Road to beyond Clew's place. Returning, stopped a few minutes at Uncle W's. Home a few minutes before 1 o'clk. Dinner soon after — Then all went to their rooms — I read newspapers after 5, the S's. Geo. & I walked until 6 — then tea — Music by the band in the Park — all over the house & out in the park, enjoying it, from 8 until 9 — out with the horse — Matt & E. came up — To bed at 10.45 — very heavy rain at midnight.
