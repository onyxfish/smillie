## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 1. 1875.

Warm, cloudless m'f. Got to
studio at 8.30. Fau. Vic. & Bun M.
came & invited me to dinner.
I wrote a note to Will, giving
him a piece of my mind con-
cerning Wright & agreeing to sell
a little picture to him for $70 —
Then I got out paper & canvas
& prepared for work. To dinner
at Fau's at 1.10 after bidding
Mrs. Tweedy & Tiffany good bye.
Back again to studio at 3 o'clk.
Stretched a canvas & went
out to a Beech tree & commenced
a study. My cellar occupies much
of my attention. Tea at 6. Eve. &
another short call on the Deal's —
then down town, stopping to bid the
Taycon's good bye. To P.O. then to
see Holts. nobody out. Mrs. Ike. was.
then to Scard's — there until near
10. there to Azur Lattinobs — to bed
at nearly 11 —

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 2. 1875.

Yet another hot day. Got to
shop early. Superintending the
cellar matter until about 10.30
when Sliver (boy) came with
Bill (horse) & until 12 o'clk, I
painted from the horse; the first
of my studies from life for "a minute
horse race". At 12.30, bid the
Deal's good bye & went down town
to see Fink. Dinner at 1.20.
Most of P.M. lost while directing
Brady how to make my coral &
wood shutes. It was 6.40
before I got to tea at Mrs. Hurds.
Clouded swif with thunder storm
to with [illegible] us. At 8.30 went home
with Fau. At 9 went down to Scard's
(met Mrs. Ike Riley on the way.) &
at 9.45 went to Surprise Party at
Azur L's — Finally got to bed at
11 o'clk.
