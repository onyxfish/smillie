## Left Page

### September, Saturday 11, 1875

Clear as a bell & as cold as ice — Ther. in my studio 45°. Kindled a fire and got to work at my painting but got along very slowly. Fan M. & Mary Samesbury called for a few minutes. Dinner at 1½ — Painted during the afternoon until near 6 — then went out with Hy Tyler & Talked about adding to my land. Tea at 6.20 — then back to studio — washed up — sunote awhile. Then down to P.O. — met Wm Mulfor walked home with him & in for a few minutes — then to Azur Lathrop's — there until 9.30 & finally to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 12, 1875

Overcast day — wind, S.E. — Up to studio after bkf.? — Fire had kept in — blew it up — Sat & Read until church time — then to Presbyt. Ch. — heard Mr. Miller preach from Acts, 19th Ch, 19th V. — concerning the burning of the books of the soothsayers — After service walked home with Fan — again to Studio & Read until 1.30 — then to dinner — then again to studio & wrote until after 4 — then calls from Laura Austin — tea with Flora. Fan M. Mr M. & Azur. Finally, went walking with Fan until 6 — then tea — after that again to studio — Rain commenced about 7 — wrote until 9.30 — letters to Oscar D. & to father — then down to P.O. — stopped at Fan's & got her letters. To bed about 11.
