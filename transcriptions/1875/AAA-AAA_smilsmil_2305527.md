## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 14, 1875

Disagreeable day — Cold &
blustering — Got to studio
at 9.10 — To work on drawing
which I finished about
noon — Then Rode down
town — got lunch & went to
F.H. Smith's to transfer B.W.
acct water color funds, but
he was not in — Back at 3.
made tint washes over my
big drawing — Dom about 4
Nicoll came — The N.A. of D.
have let the big Smith Gallery
to the Artists fund to hold
their sale while we are ar-
ranging our exhibit — J.W. Wood
J.G. Brown & Birchur came in,
able went to dinner with
Coleman — at 8.30 saw J.A.
Richards at Coleman Hd. — at
Studio at 9.30 — at 10.30
& started for 41 [illegible] & to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 15, 1875

Very pleasant day — About
9.20 when I got to studio.
Commenced washing color
on to my big sky. There
seemed to be a tendency on the
part of the color to "crawl" and
the immense surfaces to be
worked over gave me no end
of trouble — About 2, went down
to Easton Rd. & saw Hicks about
the Artists fund reemploying
the N.A. while we are arranging
the W.C. Exhibit — My efforts to effect
an arrangement without result.
To dinner in 41 [illegible] & back to studio
at 2.30 — Worked on drawing until
4.30 — Then Board of Control met
& went to tea at 6.30 — back at
[illegible] wrote a letter to Hicks, & at
9.15 went to Louis Tiffany's studio
a few friends to see Tapestries —
To 41 [illegible] at 10.45 & to bed
about 11.
