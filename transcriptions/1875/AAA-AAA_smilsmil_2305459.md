## Left Page

### July, Saturday 31, 1875

Delightful day, but still somewhat under the influence of yesterday's headache — I did messages & played with myself until near 11 — then to Studio — Ther. 60°, 50. I kindled a fire — worked on "Cathedral Spire" charcoal. Dinner at 1 — Back at 2.30. Finished charcoal & then commenced another, near the same size. "The Southern gate to the Valley, from foot of Sentinel Fall" — Tea at 6. I spent the even. with Allie Stroud & Ben M. — at Gus S's — Whist, until 10. then home & to bed at 10.45. Mr. Gillman arr'd this P.M. — from "The Woods" in answer to telegram from Mr. G. on acct. of sickness of infant.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 1, 1875

Overcast & very cool, with rain after 3 o'clk. B'kfst at 8.20 — wandered about until church time — then to Baptist Ch. & heard Mr. Chuskin preach from John — 4th Ch. 5.6.7–13.14 v. — Christ & the woman of Samaria — After service, more wandering — Dinner at 1.30 — then a walk out to the lake — back to shop at 3 — wrote a note to Miss Abbott. Tea at 6 — Evng. with George — went again to Baptist Ch. Mr. [illegible] preached from Mark. 10th Ch. 15–22 v. "Christ & the young man" — doing the right thing at the right time — "one thing thou lackest" — being the first — an excellent sermon — To bed soon after 10.
