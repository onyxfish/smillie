## Left Page

### May, Friday 28, 1875.

Wild — cloudless — delicious — Up to bkfst at 7.30 — New W. W. ordered up a carriage & we drove along the beach to the inlet where he hired a boat & we set out for a mix of sailing &
fishing — terribly pestered with gnats where we first stopped to fish —
then sailed up back of Brigantine Islanu. First rate sailing breeze — came to anchor in a new place & fished for nearly an hour — W. W. caught a shark about 3 feet long but was unable to land him — I caught a sea spider — that was all of our sport — sailed again — then ashore at 11.30 & rode to hotel — I sketched an old cedar — Dinner at 1.30 — another sketch on the beach until 3 — At 4.15 all off for Phil where we arr. at 7 — carriage waiting — Tea at 8 — all sunburned — tired &
happy — Eve'g spent chatting & telling Mr. W. of our trip — Young Gratz called for a few minutes — to bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 29. 1875.

Charming day, getting to be overcast during the eve'g. Bkfst at 8 o'clk. At 9.45 I started out & went to new bldg of Penn'a Academy of Fine Arts — then to new City Hall. Then took Walnut St. Car & rode out to West Phila — 3811 Chestnut St & called on W. Wm. Maun — Found the old gentleman very feeble & a perfect mental wreck — Net get. There. Took cars back & at 12 called on Miss Gratz — then to the Bogg's — there until 2.40 — then to W. W's at 3. Dinner — After that, as I felt decidedly unwell — I lay down & did not get out before tea at 6.30 About 8 — all of us went up in the carriage to W. Lewis's — a very pleasant little company there, but I was unfit to enjoy it — back at 10.45 & right to bed — Splitting headache and pulse 110 —
