## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 17, 1875

Bright, breezy day growing rapidly colder. Got up early & at 8.45 at Dr Yale's office —
too early for him — then to studio at 9.00 — To work at once on 3? painting of Sky of Sierras —
Dr Yale came in & looked at my eas. Worked until 12.30
then to dinner — back at 2 —
worked until 6 o'clk — got
over Sky & most of the distant
mts. Misses Conuree & Burdell
called — To tea at 7 — at
8.30 went to Renner's shop &
worked for an hour cutting my
name in Water Color on my frame.
For an hour worked on Water
Color Report & got to bed at
10.45. Harry seems to be
somewhat improved.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 18, 1875

Bright day but very windy and
cold. At 9 o'clk. went to Renner's
gilding shop & finished lettering
on my frame. Got to studio at
10 o'clk. & found a letter from Gd.
Whitney enclosing check for $250 —
on acct. of painting that I am
to do for him. Worked on "Sierras"
but made up my mind to give
up trying to get it done for the
Academy. Just as I was going to
dinner Misses Fanshaw & Colgate
came in. Fargeon was out, so
I entertained them until 1.30 —
then to dinner — back at 2.30 —
Painted on Sierras & finished little
album sketch for Mrs. Abden.
Annesley of Albany called — To
tea at 7 — Eve'g: writing, but
Dr Throop came in — did not do
much. Harry During continues
about the same — To bed at
10.15
