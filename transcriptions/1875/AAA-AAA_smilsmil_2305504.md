## Left Page

### October, Friday 29, 1875

Delightful Oct. day — got to studio at 9 o'clk. To
work on High Sierra sketch
but had many callers —
Josie Jessup & Miss Watson
called — Was much pleased
to see Josie much improved
in health. To dinner at 1 —
back at 2 — Several callers
in the afternoon — so that
I made but little progress
with my work. To tea at
6.30 — at 8 at Miss Watson's
75 W. 45 th where Josie Jessup is
visiting — there until nearly
11 — then to 41 [illegible] & to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 30, 1875

Miserable stormy day — wind
& rain — Got to studio shortly
after 9 & to work on High Sierra
painting in the foreground.
To dinner at 2.30 — back
at 1.30 — Painted until
near 5 — much of the time
so dark that I could hardly
see to work. Finished the
painting in & think it the
best bit of color handling
I have done — Of course, it is
a subject without popular
interest — To tea at 7 — at
6.30 Dining & I went to the
Colosseum. Private View
of the Siege of Paris — very
poor thing — Met a lot of
the painter fellows —
To bed at 10.30.
