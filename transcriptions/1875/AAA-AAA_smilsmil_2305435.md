## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 9, 1875

Charming m'f, but threats of a change again in the matter m'f went to old Fox's place on Livingston St. & worked on a bit in the woods. Very soon got it into a mess, but worked until after 12 — then to dinner. Ale made me very sleepy & I took a nap. As the sky was overcast I did not go out sketching but wrote to Saml. Coleman. At 5 it commenced to rain at 5.30 went to Rev. Loomis croquet party. Elpis! minister & wife — S². Winslow & wife & Miss Nick — tea about 6.30. Evet'f, music on piano — cream at g — home at 10.20 — still raining — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 10, 1875

Delightful breezy day — so cold that I sat all the m'f sketching (same place as yesterday a.m.) with my overcoat over my shoulders. In to dinner at 12.45 and did not go out again until near 3 o'clk — then to sketch of Livingston Lane that I commenced on Tues. got it done by 5 — then at another and finished it slap dash by 6.30 — then to tea — Evet'f. Gen. Maccabe called to see Aunt A — I called on Mrs. Thorne — at g took a short walk — then in & wrote to Saml. Coleman until after 10 — to bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
