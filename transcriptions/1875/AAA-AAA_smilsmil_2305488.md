## Left Page

### September, Monday 27, 1875

Charming day, but very breezy. After bkfp., went to Dewitt Ford-
ham's & ordered a lot of lumber for cellar — had it Falcon Right
up. Then to Zulphas & Boyd &
Corwin's — again to shop — &
again down town at 12. Then
to Sherman's & at 12.30 at Fan's
to bid her good bye — She & Ben
started at 2.50 for Elmira —
To dinner soon after 1. Then
to studio — Strange & started
at work & Johnston & his boy
had Sherman's open plough
& scraper & worked all p.m.
on my path. I could do no
work but oversee Strange &
Johnston. To tea at 6.20. Down
to P.O. at 7.30 — in Mr. Mulford's
store — Abg Quest I went in to
Ben M.'s — Young folks had a
clam bake — at 10 went to studio
& fixed fire & about 10.30 to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 28, 1875

Mild pleasant day with indications
of rain or change — Bkfp. at 7.45
then to Mr. Mulford's & looked at
the furnace that he offers me for $40.
Then to studio & until 12, bossing
Strange, Brady, Johnston & boy.
Then to house & bid Hgt & Mrs. J. —
good bye; they leave for a few
days. Then down town — went
to Sayre's about furnace, did
other little chores & to dinner
at 1.15. After dinner again to
studio & the whole p.m. was
consumed with Strange in
arranging cellar, stairs, etc.
Tea at 6.15 — then to studio &
fixed fire for the night. A
shower of rain — then to house and
dressed myself — then in to Azur
— then down to P.O. & back to
Azur's & played whist until
ten o'clk. To bed at 10.30.
