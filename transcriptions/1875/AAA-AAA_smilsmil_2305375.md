## Left Page

### February, Thursday 11, 1875

Pouring rain with tornados & wind. Got to studio at — & to work on 24×44. Painting in foreground & middle distance. Miss Abbatt came in spite of the storm but Miss Sautelle did not but in an appearance. I worked until after 1 o'clk when I lunched with Miss Woodward. Then painted until after 5. Getting things roughed in after a fashion. Rec'd note from Will Kimball announcing the death of his father. To tea at 7 o'clk — very clear & very cold. Went to N.A. at 9 o'clk. & then met Mr. & Mrs. Saunt. Chew of Germantown. Abt 9.15. Went to Union League Club and at 11.30 got to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 12, 1875

Clear, very windy and cold. Got to studio at 9.15 and to work on 24×44 painting in group of trees — much interrupted. Rosalie Goodman — the Misses Jane & Mrs. [illegible] called. Went to dinner abt 6 o'clk. Kate there — then to Phil. Rehearsal, then to Kurtz Gall. & saw Knoedler's collection of water colors. Then to studio at 5 o'clk. & did a little more work painting. To tea at abt 6.30 — [illegible] dined at A.V.V.'s — abt 9.15 again at studio. Wrote until 9.30 — then to N.A. back at 10.30 wrote notes to Nicoll & Sackett — then to 41 [illegible] & to bed at 11.30.
