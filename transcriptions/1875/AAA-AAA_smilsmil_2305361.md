## Left Page

### January, Thursday 14, 1875

Very pleasant day — got to studio at 9 o'clk. Got to work on Idle Hours &
pottered away all the mf.
To lunch at 12.45 — back at 1.45 — much of this p.m.
lost with callers — Misses Beach, Bullard, Niles m
+ Nellie Jacobs called — I worked on Idle Hours
+ got it finished for the present. At 6.30 went to T. N. Smith's with W. C.
Boy + Books. To my own tea at 7. Spent writing
letters on W. C. business until after 10 — to bed about 10.30
Simon de Nisser, Chas's employer died this night.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 15, 1875

After a night very much disturbed, awoke with a horrible
pains in my head — to bed all day, the pain continuing without cessation, so that late in the afternoon I thought that I would go wild — I did not get up at night but continued in bed — got no sleep + nothing to eat until near mf, feeling somewhat easier I took a crust of bread.
