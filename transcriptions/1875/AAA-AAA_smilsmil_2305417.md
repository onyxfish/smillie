## Left Page

### May, Thursday 6, 1875

Rainy mng. & overcast all day.
Got to studio at 9.15 & to work
on charcoal "Cathedral Spires".
Did not get to dinner until 1.30
— back at 2.30 & most of the
P.m. on same study, getting
it finished. Worked up on
a little pen sketch and
to tea at 7.30. Mother still
at Divinne's — Father in
Plainfield. Spent the
evg. with Mother — very
stupid with a headache
— to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 7, 1875

Clouded but pleasant. In bed
with a head ache until almost
3 o'clk. P.m. Then up and had
a lunch — then down to studio.
Loafed about there until 8 o'clk.
Then fixed up some tea for
myself — At 8.30 went to Fifth
ave. Hotel to call on Miss
Buggs but she had ret'd. to
Pier. Then rode up to
Will's where Father & Mother
are — at 9.15 — Go° & Charl
there — Left with them
at 10 o'clk — In at 10.30
To bed before 11 —
