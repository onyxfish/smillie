## Left Page

### April, Thursday 22, 1875

Bright & clear — a little warmer & less wind — got to
studio at 9.15 — Misses J & C
took their last lesson.
I spent the m'f with th
until near 1 — To dinner
at 1.20 — back at 2.30
& painted on 10×16. on
the Saco. getting it finished
before 5 — then set to work
& gave studio a regular
sweeping — did not get
to tea until 8 o'clk —
Evng. wrote a letter
to Jan Mr — & to bed at
11. [illegible — ~2 lines]

## Right Page

### April, Friday 23, 1875

Still very pleasant weather.
10.30 before I got to studio. The
m'f was spent in shopping
at Knordlein & looking at the
pictures. Then charcoaling
on a subject, "Bridal Veil fall"
that I intend to give to the N.A.
y D. Jonthier Mortgage Fund".
To dinner at 1.15 & at 2.30, at
the last Phil. Rehearsal —
then to Chapman's (Heartt)
& saw my picture in place —
then to studio & again worked
on charcoal — To tea at 7.15.
At 8.30 went down to Hormor's
fair in 14th St. At 9.15 went to Booth's
theater to see "Henry V." A series
of spectacular effects until 11.15.
Immense crowd — fine art & hist.
Glad I saw it, but not sufficiently
interested to forget personal dis-
comforts. To bed at 11.45.
Miss V. Boggs & friend called
at studio this m'f.
