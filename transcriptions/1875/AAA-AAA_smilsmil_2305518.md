## Left Page

### November, Friday 26, 1875

Pretty steady Rain all day —
Got to studio at 9.15 and
to work on cartoon, getting
along very slowly — To Dinner
at 1.30 back at 2.45 — but
the afternoon was so dark
that I accomplished
but little — About 4.30
Nicoll — J.G. Brown F.N. Smith
& Bobbins came & we held
a Board Meeting & arranged
details of next Ex[hibition] —
To tea at 7 — Between
8 & 9 in at Renner's, trying
to work out a frame pattern
for my big drawing — Then
in to write letter to Fenn
& part of one to Ferguson &
to bed at 10.20

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 27, 1875

Pleasant day — Got to studio at
9.20 feeling very shakey — The
whole morning lost with callers.
I tried to work on large cartoon —
To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.45
Worked pretty well during the
afternoon — At 6 o'clk. walked
up 5th Ave. with funeral procession
of Vice Pres. Wilson — Got to
tea at 6.50 — At 8. again
at studio & worked on cartoon
until after 9 — Then wrote
2° letter to H.C. Tyler & one
to Mr. J. Tunnell — To 41°
S. som after 10 — & to bed
at about 11. [illegible — ~3 lines]
