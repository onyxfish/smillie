## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 18. 1875.

Charming day but breezy &
cool. Got to studio shortly
after 9 & worked on Dautmis
14×22. Upper Ausable Lake.
Louis 1 [illegible] our surprised me
by a call — just Rec'd. from
Europe — at 11.15 left studio
Got to foot of 9. St. S.R. at 1.40
[illegible] boat came along at
12.20. Got to M. 12.55 — Down 11
on R.R. & at 1.20 got to Timpsons
Miss Abbratt there to meet me.
Went right up to the house &
had dinner — at 2.30 — off into
Miss A. & sister & Rose until
near 5 — took 5.38 train to M
walked across to Harlem St.
& took 6.10 train — 42d. St. at [illegible]
Tea at 6.30 — wrote a letter to
Geo. Then went to 40 W. 57. St —
called on Maud D — at 9
went over to Will's & saw Mary
20 41 [illegible]. at 10.30 & right to
bed.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 19. 1875.

Cloudy & threatening — Heavy
shower about 2 — 9.30 when
I got to studio. Painted
on Bridal Veil. Maud
D — called about 11.30 and
with her I went to Kurtz's
then to Bogardus's. as
she is desirous of getting a
crayon likeness of Allie.
To dinner at 1.15 — back
at 2.15 & got caught in
the shower — Painted
until after 6 — Stopped
& saw fan at 7 — then to
tea & back to Stuyvesant
[illegible]. Hetty Searle is
with fan. They had callers
Riley & Cap. coles —
Stayed until near 11 — then
to 41 [illegible] & to bed in
my new Room — the little
entry bed room.
