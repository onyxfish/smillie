## Left Page

### August, Sunday 22, 1875

Raw miserable day, with rain beginning at noon. Up late & breakfast at 9 — Mrs went to the Baptist Ch. & heard Mr C. preach upon "Christ, the Bread of Life." Then to the Presb'n Ch. & caught in the rain — to dinner at 1.30 — then ran & for awhile in W.J.'s [illegible] — & at 4 o'clk at studio — kindled a fire & wrote, but made little progress as I gave up too much time to a nap. Tea at 6 — very tired again wrote but did not finish letters to Amanda Diven & to Father. Went to bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 23, 1875

Cold, disagreeable day; at 8.30 went up to studio and kindled a fire & got to work giving final touches to Bridal Veil Fall & Hudson River from Sunny Brook Farm. Surprised by a visit from Hon. Wm. J. S— and lady. Diner at 1 — again at studio at 2 — soon followed by Mr. Reed & party, before they left Fam. Bam. & Mrs. Wm. [illegible] called — & before they left, Agur Nathrop's family came — all to see my pictures — at 5.15 went to Baptist Ch. & finished chalk sketch commenced in No. 2. Tea at 6.15 — went for a few minutes in at Faris — then to Mr. Coabci's — there until 9.40 — then to my room and wrote until midnight on a letter to Maud P— Then to bed & had a very bad night.
