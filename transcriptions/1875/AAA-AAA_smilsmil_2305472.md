## Left Page

### August, Thursday 26, 1875

Glorious day — Got to studio at 8.30
8am afting. Ben M. drove up with
Fam. Josie, Vic. & Hetty & insisted
that I should ride with them
. We rode until near 11 — Then
I got to work on Peekskill Huds.
— Dinner abt — back at 2 + worked
faithfully until 6. Then cleaned
up — Tea at 6.30 — at 7 went
down the hill & at 7.30. Fam.
Josie, Hetty, Vic., Ben, Ge. & I
went to Court House & heard
Root's Cantata "Haymakers"
given by the Montrose Musical
Union — a fair performance.
Out at 10 — all went to Fam's
— there until 11 — then to
my Room & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 27, 1875

Another delightful morning.
After 9 o'clk — Ge. hitched up and
we rode until 10 o'clk — then
to studio, worked on Peekskill
Hudson — until about 1.30, then
Mr. Comstock's little girl
came & I spent the rest of
the mtg. sketching from her.
Dinner abt — back at 2 +
worked on the P.M. on P.H. —
Tea at 6 — very tired — leg hurts.
Svef. made a short call on
Mr. Sweedy — then down town
to P.O. then to Fam's — Whist
with Josie B. Vic. D. & Azunt —
Mr. Elisha M. came in late —
To my Room at 10.30 & put a
red flannel bandage on leg
wet with Pond's Extract — To bed
at 11 — after 12 before I got to sleep
— Serenades about town by
young Scranton fellows.
