## Left Page

### January, Saturday 16, 1875

Got up about 8.30 & went downstairs — very shaky — eat
a light bkfst. but did not dare go out as my throat &
head troubled me & the weather was raw & disagreeable. Dr. Throop came in to
see me in the mrf. Aunt Jessie took dinner with us
& went to Po'keepsie at 4 P.M. Tea at 6.30. Evrf.
Rec'd a boy of Graham's gems from Belle. S. Drawing [plan]
ed for me & I got to bed at 10.30, but did not get to sleep until sometime in the my. My birthday — don't know how many — & who cares?

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 17, 1875

Very clear, breezy, brisk and cold — Head better — mrf. went
to Dr. Hastings's & heard him preach what he called "a character study" — Joshua —
text Numbers 27th ch. 18 v. — and Deut. 34th ch. 9th v. After service walked up with Josie and
Mr. Charlie Jessup — Dined with them. Miss Dave, a fellow graduate of Katie's, there
also — a pleasant time until 4 o'clk — then walked down to A.V.V.'s — got there — tea
at 6 — got went to church & I stayed until 9.30 — then walked to H. 48s. Sat with my feet in hot water &
mustard & got to bed about 11.
