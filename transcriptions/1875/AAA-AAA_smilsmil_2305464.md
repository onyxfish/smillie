## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 10, 1875

Delightful day. Bkfst. as usual. Between 9 & 10 a.m.
D. Mulford called & gave my
knee careful examination.
He says there is no serious
injury. From 10.15 until near
1 o'clk. Rode in the gel., then
dinner. Near 3 o'clk. hobbled
up to studio — the first time since
my fall. Elisha Mulford came
up. Then Sue & Mr. Ths. Post
with carriage. I rode with
them until 6.45 — then tea.
Called on Azur Lathrop's
family — then wrote two notes
& at 8.45 hobbled down town.
— Beautiful moonlight — back
in front of court house. went
in to Mulford's — Whist —
until 10 — then up the hill
& to bed at 10.50.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 11, 1875

Very heavy rain all the mtg.
Bkfst. at 7.45 — then spent the
mtg. writing letters to Father,
Nicoll & Claghorn. Dinner at
1 o'clk. At 2, called at Mr. S.
Sayre's & saw Mr. Sackett about
Nellie Jacobs coming here. Then
went to Court House, there until
near 4 o'clk. listening to trial of
New Milford Bank Robbers — then
to Tyler's No. & wrote a letter to
Nellie Jacobs. Tea & Son after
6 P.M. very warm & clearing.
Evtg. went to see Mr. Sackett
again — then to W. J. D.'s & bid
aunt Alphia good-bye as she is
to start for home tomorrow mtg.
& also went to J. Searle's. Joe,
Mrs & Taylor there. Whist until
10 — then back to room & got
to bed a little before 11.
