## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 6, 1875

Charming day. Got to studio soon after 9. Misses Abbatt Conwell and Jacobs came & I devoted the m'f to them. Got to my dinner about 1.15 back at 2.30 & worked on Trellis walk. all the P.M. Stopped at the Brunswick at 6.15 & saw Col. Domis for a few moments — then to tea at 7— About 8.30 at Union League Theatre Tableaux & vaites — I was to help young Riley in fitting up "The Artist's Dream" — Amused myself back of scenes until 9.30 — then went men to the Hippodrome & saw the "Amazon March" — then to studio & used fire then to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 7, 1875

Horrible. Raw storm of Rain and snow. after part of the day almost pleasant. All of the m'f. was spent getting water color notes ready to send to Union League. 1.30 before I got to dinner. back at 2.30. Then sent to work planning a little oil picture for Uncle Willie. Belle Tweedy called & later father came in — He spent the night at Will's. To tea at 7. Dressed & with Geo. at 8 o'clk. went down to [illegible] Private View at N.A. of D. There until 11.20 — To bed at 12.
