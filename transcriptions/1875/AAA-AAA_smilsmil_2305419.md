## Left Page

### May, Monday 10, 1875

Awake soon after 3. Glorious mf. Got up at 6.30 by mistake. Bkfst at 8. Took 9.12 train in to NY. Sky overcast. Got to city at 10.20 — went to A.B.N. Co. to Continental Life Ins. and to Jas. Nightis. He bought my little painting "Mc. Nilleans nr. North Conway. N.H." for $75 — To dinner in H[illegible] at 12.30 — one or two smart showers in the middle of the day. Got to studio at 2 o'clk. Set palette & commenced painting 10×14 "A Sketch" — Stuck to it until 6.45 — To tea at 7.20 — very tired — on sofa listening to Düring & his piano until 10.40 — There wrote to Chapman & Jam. M. & got to bed at 11. [illegible — ~2 words] [illegible — ~3 words].

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 11, 1875

A very warm day — Stopped at D. Percy's & got to studio at 9.30. Painted all the mf on sketch that I began yesterday P.M. Misses Cornwell & Jacobs [illegible] called at 12.30 — To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.30 and spent the P.M. Sketching in charcoal & in making several little sketches in Miss J's book. At 7 called at Sturtevant. Ho. Jam. M. Sillie Jessups, Billings, Stroud & daughter there. To tea at 8 — at 8.30 called on Mary Willard — out — then at 9, called on Miss Burdell. There until near 11 — home in & to bed right away.
