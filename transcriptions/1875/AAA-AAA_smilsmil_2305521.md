## Left Page

### December, Thursday 2, 1875

Charming winter day. Got to Studio about 9 — with uncle Willie. Found my linen on stretcher all in a pucker, pulled it off & then stretched it dry — went to Kuordle's & got common paper. Then made paste — To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.15, all the p.m. spent fussing over stretching linen & paper. Another Board meeting at 4.30 — until near 6. To tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 again to Studio — engaged miting until near 10 — then to 41 "b" & to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### December, Friday 3, 1875

Very pleasant day. Got to Studio at 9 o'clk. At 10.30 Norris (model) came and I made a half figure study of him in a red shirt, working as rapidly as I could until 1 o'clk. At 1.30 took lunch in Miss Woodward's studio with Misses Jacobs & DenBleyker. At 2.30 at Phil. Matinee. After that, Belle S. walked with me to Studio — I went to tea at 6.30 — at 8. again at Studio where I worked until near 11 o'clk, making paste & mounting paper for big drawing. Then to 41 "b" & to bed at 11.40.
