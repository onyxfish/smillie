## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 5, 1875

Very pleasant until after 3 P.M. — then overcast and
threatening — To studio before
9 — again fussing with muslin
on the wall — At last commenced
washes on Smock Davis's W.C.
"Cathedral Spires — Yosemite."
At 11.30 went down town to
the blacksmith shop — then
to Zerpha's — then to Beck's
& on my way back at D.V.'s
Dinner at 1.15 — Studio at 2
& worked in drawing until 4
Then gave Geo's horse a brush
& went out on horseback —
Rode until 5.15 — Then a bath
— a few minutes of rest — Tea
at 6 — Again to studio — fixed
fire, then called on Mrs. Cross man
to see if she wd. feed Geo. & me for a
short time — would not. Then to P.O.
Called on Fanny Jessup — Then to Ager
No. — & at 10.30 — to bed —

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 6, 1875

Overcast & blowing all day with
threats of a storm — Got to studio
at 9, but went back to Mrs. Sayre,
Sayre's & made arrangements
to get board with her. Mrs. Dud
has her auction sale on Sat. Again
to studio — Johnston with Gilmore —
horse & stone boat hauling stone
off my lot. I got started at last
on W.C. Yosemite, Cathedral Spire
& did [illegible] ) — Then to last dinner
at Mrs. Dud's — Ager's wife there —
Back at 2 & with interruptions
worked in drawing until most
6 — Austin painting blinds etc.
No tea at Mrs S's at 6 — At 7 back
to studio — Rain & blowing great
guns — fixed fire, wrote awhile,
then down town with Geo. to P.O. —
Then to Farr's, who has just ret'd from
Elmira — There until 10.15 — Then
on the hill & to bed at 10.45
