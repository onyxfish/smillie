## Left Page

### February, Thursday 25, 1875

Miserable day. Up earlier than usual & got to studio
about 8.45. Soon after 9 —
Messrs Whitney, Lewis and
Richards came alone — Mr.
Lewis commissioned me to
make him two Yosemite
water color drawings for
$300 # Misses Jacobs and
Conwell came — Miss Abbott
still unwell & Miss Sawtelle
non est. Gave the mfg to
pupils — unfit for much
as I was fighting against a
headache. To dinner at 1.15
back at 2.15. Little done
during the P.M. Heavy Rain
yet & I took tea with Misses
Gilbert & Woodward. Rest of
spent trying to clean up my
shop — In at 10.30 and
to bed right away.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 26, 1875

Very pleasant day, although
windy. Got to studio at 9 o'clk.
and to work on Saco. No. 1 —
Miss Degolier came, but I
gave her no lesson — to dinner
at 1 o'clk — back at 2.15 — worked
as long as I could see, getting
No. 1 pretty near finished.
Reimer sent down my frames
Put "Frost" into his new frame and
he looks better — Dr. Yale came
in with an etched [illegible] and
worked at my desk for some
time. To tea at 7 — wrote a
note or two — then to studio at
8.30 — made another note or two —
home at 9.30 over to N.A. A great
rush again — met [illegible] me at
10.45 & to bed at 11.30
