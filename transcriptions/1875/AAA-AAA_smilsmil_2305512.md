### November, Sunday 14, 1875

Overcast & disagreeable —
Mrs. went to Dr. Hastings &
heard him preach. After
service went around to Smith's
— there for an hour. Then
walked up to A.N.V.'s when
at 2.30 I had dinner —
Kate & Charl there — Took
a nap — up to tea at 6. +
at 7 walked down, stopping
again at Smith's for a few
minutes — Then to Dr. Hastings
Kate & Charl there — went
to Ditine's — Wrote a letter
home — Posted it & to bed at

## Right Page

### November, Monday 15, 1875

Still overcast & threatening —
Got to studio about 9 — Kindled
fire & fussed around — I
got out a canvas 12 × 22
& outlined "Horse Racing
in the Yosemite" that I
may paint a color sketch
in oil. To dinner at 1.15
— back at 2.30 — worked
until near dark — then to
Harris's & bought a pair of
gloves for Josie Beuty — then
to Stewart's & got canvas to
mount paper for big drawing
then to studio & wrote a letter —
To tea at 6.30 — Ev. & Mrs. went
to Mary's (sister) and
back & to bed soon after 10.
