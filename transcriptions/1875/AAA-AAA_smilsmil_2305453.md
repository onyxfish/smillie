## Left Page

### July, Saturday 17, 1875

Very pleasant, but warm —
Bkfst. before 8 — then went to
my building & worked hard
all the m'f. clearing up in-
side & out. Dinner at 6 o'clk.
— after that, went down town
before again going to bed. [illegible]
Sarah went up with me &
washed the floor. I then got
my cases to the building and
unpacked them — Tea at 6
30, — again unpacking — there
until almost dark. — then
down town — Saw Cottrell &
others — had shellac varnish
made up & attended to the
things — carried up box from
Student's Lamp, a present
from S.C.F. — Glorious moonlight
night at last, got to bed at
11 o'clk., very tired.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 18, 1875

Like a Selpi day — Heavy Rain
during the m'f., changing to
a heavy blow of wind and
no breaking away of clouds.
Bkfst. — about 9 — Feeling very
tired I spent nearly all the
m'f. on my studio floor, mapp'd
in my big blanket, my head
on Mr. I's sofa bollow — Dinner
at 1.30 — Talk in doors until
3.30 — then a short walk —
in at 4, & did a little miting.
Tea soon after 6 — Eve'g. I
went to the Presby. Ch. and
heard Miller — Christ's miracle
of turning water into wine —
after service walked home
with Dan Sayre & Priscilla B.
— Stopped at Sam'. S's & talked
"boarding" — Got to bed
about 10 o'clk.
