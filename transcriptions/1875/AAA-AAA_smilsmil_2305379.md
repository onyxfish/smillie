## Left Page

### February, Friday 19, 1875

Overcast, with light fall of snow & sleet. Got to Studio
about 9 — Miss De Galien
there ahead of me — gave
her a short lesson & got
to my painting on "Sierras"
about 10 o clk. At it until
12.30 — then to dinner —
at 2.30, at Phil. Pleased
Kate there — Back to
Studio at 5 — to tea at 7 —
at 8 called on Miss Burdell
at 9, on the Kimball's —
Had a very quiet and
pleasant visit — at 9.50
at [illegible] a — at 10.15 at Studio
at 11 at 23 W 41 st & at
11.30 to bed —

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 20, 1875

Horrible day of rain & sleet.
Went to Ranney's & ordered a
new frame for "Soot" — then went
to Hayek's — cor. of 44th & 10th ave. —
he not in — then to studio at 10.30.
Set palette & to work on Sierras.
To dinner at 1 o clk — back at
2.15 & painted until about 5.
when I got the canvas covered.
The Tweedy's called late in
the afternoon. To tea at 6.30
Kate took tea with us and
went with Gel. & me to Phil.
concert in the evening. Will
K. having given me his
ticket. Enjoyed the concert
very much — especially 2d
movement of a [illegible] symphony
No 3 — After concert, brilliant
moonlight — Took Kate up
to A.V.V's — walked down and
to bed at 11.30
