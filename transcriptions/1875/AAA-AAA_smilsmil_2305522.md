## Left Page

### December, Saturday 4, 1875

Much milder & overcast. Got to studio at 9 — at 10.
Mr. Donald (model) came & I worked from him until 1 o'clk. getting half-figure
study. To dinner at 1.30
— back at 2.30 — Spent some time with Miss Abbott, then commenced Tracing on
big drawing — To tea at
6.30 — At 8 at Steinway
Hall. 2° Thomas Concert.
Enjoyed it exceedingly.
Got to studio at 10.45 —
fixed fire — then to 41 [illegible]
S; & to bed at about 11.45.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 5, 1875

Dull overcast day — Got up very late — 9.30 — & after bathing & dressing did not get down to bkfst until 10.30 and 11 o'clk —
Then had to read a long acct
of the escape of "Bill Tweed"
so that — I did not get to Dr
Hastings's until service was
almost over — Then to Mam's for
a few minutes — walked down to
studio & back — dinner at 1 —
at 3 again at studio & spent
the p.m. in some clearing up
— at 5.45 walked up to 41 [illegible]
in a rain storm — tea at 6.
Every[?] went to Dr. Jay [illegible]'s.
(Broadway Tabernacle) & heard
from him a sort of Bible lesson
from Peter's denial of Christ —
Very heavy Rain — To bed
at 10.45
