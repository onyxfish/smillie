## Left Page

### January, Friday 22, 1875

Horrible snow & rain —
got to studio soon after 9
almost used up with a
headache — fought along,
working for Geo. & Miss Wood-
ward until noon when I
gave up. I gave my Phil.
rehearsal ticket to Miss Gillis
& lay up on the lounge until
about 3 — The storm passed
& sky cloudless — I went to
123 — & to bed — did not
get up again until next
mrf. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 23, 1875

Very fine day — breezy & clear.
got to studio at 9 — then went
over to N.A.D. — there about
an hour — then to Snedeco's
& to Avery's — back to Studio
at 11 — made a note to Hon.
A. Borie repping the loan for
drawing by Gimt Grant — also
one to Earle & son — to dinner
at 12.45 — back at 2 & all the
P.m. worked on my drawings
getting them at last ready
to go over to the Academy — at
6.15 to tea — at 7.20 called
at Colman's & with Geo. &
Escorted Belle & Jenny J. to the
3d Phil. Concert. After that
took Belle to her Aunts in
44th St. To bed at 11.20
O'Kelly called at Studio
in the mng. Miss Taylor in
the afternoon.
