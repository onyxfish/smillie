## Left Page

### February, Saturday 13, 1875

Peasant day — got to studio
shortly before 9 — Miss Degulin
came & I gave her a short lesson
in Water Color — was no — at 10
went to [illegible] of Mr.
Kimball — At 11, back to
studio & painted until
12.30 on 24×44 — Trees — &
got it painted in. Mr. J. J.
Astor called — to dinner
at 1 — back at 2 — very little
work — at 3 I went to N.A.
crowded — met many friends.
To studio at 5 — to tea at 6.30
again went to N.A. at 8.30
Music until near 11. Took
down & put up flag staff.
To bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 14, 1875

Charming winter day, growing
very windy & colder at night.
Mrs., went to Dr. Hastings' &
heard him preach from II Cor.
2d ch. 12v. 15. After service
rode up with Mrs. Jessup &
Josie. Took a brisk walk
for half an hour then back
to the Jessup's & dined
with them — at 4.30 went
down to A.N.V.'s — Billy Newhall
there — tea at 6.30 — Eve'g
I made one or two notes.
Charl came in with Kate
at 9.30 — at 10 I walked
down with him getting in
at 10.30 — Sat by basement
fire until 11.30 — then to
bed.
