### May, Wednesday 12, 1875

Delightful day — Showers at noon or later — Got to Studio
at 9 o'clk. & to work in "Ausable
Lake" 12×22. Painted on
sky & distance — about 2.30
went around to Nellie Jacob's
Saw her & her mother and
chatted & looked at work
until 1 — Then to dinner
back at 2.30 & to Studio
until 6.30 — After 7 when
I got to tea — a little after
8 when I got to Steinburnt
Ho. Saw Fau — There until
near 11 — Then in and

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 13, 1875

Charming day. At 9 o'clk. at D. Noyes
engaged — then to Kurtz & ordered
copy of a photo of Nellie Jacobs —
Then to D. N's again — he swabbed
back of my palette with Carmin —
To Studio at 10.20 — Set palette
& commenced work on "Ausable
Lake". 12×22 — Maud Dixon
surprised us with a call — at
11.30 went down-town to Continental
Ins Co. & arranged for a new
policy to take the place of the
old one — Then to Charl's F.H. Smith's
& Nile's. Lunch at Restaurant —
To Studio at 3.40 — worked on
12×22 until after 6 — Stopped
& saw Fau M. — at 7 — To tea at
7.30 — at 8.30 called on the
Kemp's in 39th St. Pleasant
chatty time until 10.20 — Then
in & to bed at 11.
