## Left Page

### April, Monday 12, 1875

Overcast & struggling to be clear all the mf. Finally giving it up. I got up &
ate day felt very much "played out". Got to studio
at 9.45 & worked until 12.30
on Mearine for C.B.N. Co — then
to dinner — Funeral of Harry
at 2. a very interesting service
with extraordinary display
of flowers. At 3.30 went back
to studio & worked until 6
on drawing — It commenced
storming so G[illegible] & I stayed &
took tea with Miss Woodum —
& I worked at cleaning out
closets until near 11 —
Then to H[illegible] S — & to bed at
11.15.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 13, 1875

The most horrible day so far
this winter — Snow — Slush —
Slosh — Got to studio at 9.30
Miss Jacobs was the only lamb
that came & I devoted myself
to her until 12 — To dinner
at 1 — Aunt Agnes staying
with the D's. Back at
2 & commenced a little
painting 10 × 16 "On the Saco" —
worked as long as I could
see — To tea at 7 — Every
note to Thomson — Mdus.
Efp., Art Delp: Cincinnati.
Got to bed shortly after
