## Left Page

### December, Saturday 18. 1875.

Got to studio at 9.20. Very cold & windy. Set to work washing in big horses. Found some fun in it. Mrs Hawly & bro called. Then, Grannis to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.15. Worked as long as I could see. Mr Reed called. Got a P.O. card from Father saying that Geo. is better. To tea at 6.30 — Evett. at 8.30 at 57 W. 10th S. Social meeting in W.C. Soct[?] Room — "Pipes, Pictures & Music." — A keg of lager, pretzels & cheese — singing, recitations & comic stories — on the walls, a lot of W.C. sketches — left at 11.15 walked to 41 [illegible]S. & to bed at 12. ## Right Page

### December, Sunday 19. 1875.

Very horribly cold, windy & dirty — Up very late — to Hastings late — 1st. Vi. Serm. 20th. 39th S. — "unquestioning, implicit obedience" — After service walked down to studio and, walked up to 57th S. Dined with Charl & spent part of P.M. with Mr. Rosa. At 4.30 walked down to studio — at 6, walked up to Mr Rogers's 23 W. 48th S — Tea about 7 — Evett., chatting, & at 9.30 walked down to studio — wrote a letter home & walked up to 44th S. & Grand Central depot & mailed it — then to 41 [illegible] S., warmed my feet at the front basement fire & to bed at 11.
