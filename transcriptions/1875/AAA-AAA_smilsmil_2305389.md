## Left Page

### March, Thursday 11, 1875

Very charming day. About
9.30 again before I got to
Studio. To work on Sierra
painting over the trees in
middle ground. Getting
a good deal of the coal hole
in the way of color. To
dinner at 2.45 — back at
2 & painted until 4.
Mr. S. W. Stoughton called
to see if I would give her
lessons in W. C. drawing.
At 4.30 I went to Knoedler's
Moore's, Devry's, Gauthier &
Snedecors & bought gloves &
hdkfs besides. To tea at 7.15
At 8.30 again at Studio —
cleaned up — fixed fires —
did a little writing and
then to 41 [illegible] & to bed at
about 10.45.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 12, 1875

Mild & pleasant. 9.30 again
before I got to Studio. Painted
all the mf on nearest trees
in "Sierras". To dinner at 1.o
& at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal
which I enjoyed exceedingly.
Walked up to Studio at with
Miss Helen Ripley Mr. Walsh.
In at 4.45 — to tea at 7. At
8.30 Geo. & I called at the K's
22d S. Saw only Carrie. At
9.15 at Studio — worked
awhile on W.C. annual Report.
In at 10.45 and Right to bed.

For some time past I have
been troubled with ringing in my
ears. Dr. Yale told me there was
wax there. For two or three nights
I have put glycerine in my
ears & this mft. in using a syringe
I was amazed at what I
dislodged — but the ringing
was not yet ceased.
