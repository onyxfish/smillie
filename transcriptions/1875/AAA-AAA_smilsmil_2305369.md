## Left Page

### January, Saturday 30, 1875

Brilliant day, but very bad under foot — Dressed carefully
went up to Bierngraft & had him
operate on my corns, then went
to N.A. of D., getting there about
10.30. Not many people, but
soon a host of buyers came in
& a number of pictures were sold
including my "Deldtouis" — At
1 oclk. I went to Truma Di
& had lunch — the sisters Miller
there — back to N.A. soon after
2 — A lively crowd during the
P.M. — After b. arranging supper —
to tea until 8 — at 7.15 — back
to N.A. at 8.30 — a very fine
show — at 10 oclk. Had Table
set & I think had a very good
supper — Wine, Punch, whisky, salad
& sandwiches — Everything seemed
to go very nicely — Left the N.A.
at 12 — To bed at 12.40. So
judged the first Day Views and
the Press Views of the 8th Am. [illegible] —

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 31, 1875

Heavy Snow storm all day —
but I did it get out of my
bed — Totally used up with
a nervous headache —
got up at 7 P.M. — Took some
tea & then up on sofa in
basement until 11.30 — then
to bed — [illegible — entire passage]
