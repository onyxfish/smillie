## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 2, 1875

Bright, breezy day — got to
studio at 9 o'clk. & to work
on "Eve of etc." Painted over
middle distant mts. in sun-
shine. Fiddled away with
a little brush until Colman
came in & started me off
with a big one. To dinner at
12.30 — back at 1.45 — Painted
until 5 — Dr Percy. Nicoll,
Charley Smith & Corinne called
at 6 o'clk. went to the Kimball's
to tea — there until 9 o'clk.
then to studio & wrote a letter
to Jno. Sartain — to 41st St. &
to bed at 11 o'clk. [illegible — ~4 lines]
[illegible — ~3 lines]

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 3, 1875

Very pleasant day — got to
studio a little before 9 — & to
work on Sierras — Painted
over middle distance. Mr.
Saml. Colman called & made
me a very pleasant visit — To
dinner at 12.30 — back at 1.45 —
worked until about 4.30
then Nicoll came & before 5
F.H. Smith, J.G. Brown & Bircher
then we held a meeting of
the B'd of Control & transacted
business with regard to the
Centennial. I am to go
on to Phila — to arrange with
Sartain & Bossilee — To tea
at 7 — wrote a letter to Jno.
& at 8.45 went to Mary's —
there until 10.40, seeing
Mrs. Bell & the Hertzgels — then
to 41st St. & to bed at 11. [illegible]
Rain.
