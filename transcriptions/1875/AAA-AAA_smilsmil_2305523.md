## Left Page

### December, Monday 6, 1875

Rain in the m'f, overcast all day. Got to Studio at
9.15 — At 10, Norms came &
I worked from him in M. C.
until near 1 o'clk — making
the strongest sketch yet —
To dinner at 1.30 — back at
2.45. Got a letter from
Curgen — he has a son. All
the P.M. I continued on
big drawing — when I lost
light — I commenced little
historical sketch of W.C.
Docty for S.S. Conant — To tea
at 6.45. At 7.30 went to Man's —
Father there — at 8.30 he & I
went up to Charl's in 51st St.
— then down to Studio. I wrote
until 10.30 — Then to 41st St. —
& to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 7, 1875

Overcast day — After bkfp. went
to Man's & saw Father — then
to Burgraff's — Got to Studio at
10 — McDonald (model) there
before me — I worked from him
until 1 o'clk — then Father came
& I walked to 44th St. with him.
He is to go up to P. on 4 P.M.
train. Back to Studio at 2.30
— Rain & very dark — worked
in morning study — C.S. Welles
spent most of P.M. with me —
Nicoll came in at 5 & announced
action of N.A.D. Council last night
— To tea at 6.15 — heavy Rain —
back to Studio at 8.30 — worked
at mounting a number of W.C.
Studies until near 11 — then
to 41st St. — & to bed at 11.40
