## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 29. 1875.

Mild cloudy m'f, with two or three smart showers between 10,30 & 12 o'clk. Then cleared with out a cloud. All the morning with Johnston & Carpenters, drawing design for front door casings etc. Invited to dine at Agur H's at 12,30 — dressed & went there — & at 2 went down town, back to Studio at 3 & worked about until 4 when Geo. P prevailed upon me to go out riding with him. Rode until 6 — tea at 6,30 — Rec'd a note from Ellea Diven asking us to visit Elmira — went up to Studio & made answer to her & a note to W. Whitridge — Then down town at 9 o'clk — Saw Chap Dayre about furnace — To bed at 10,30.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 30. 1875.

Overcast day, threatening showers that did not come — Got up with a headache but spent the m'f with carpenters — was not able to take my dinner — held up until 3 o'clk — then tumbled into bed with my boots and overcoat on — Was not able to get up & undress until near 2 A. M. — then slept the rest of the night.
