## Left Page

### December, Thursday 30, 1875

Miserable. Raw foggy [illegible] when I got up found left side of nose & face much swelled & Red, shewing some symptoms of Erysipelas? Went to Dr. J— He did not seem to know what it was — then to Dr. Yale — He did not seem to know much more — At 11 got to studio. Did a little work — then to dinner at 1.30 & as the weather was so damp & Raw, I did not go out again but spent the rest of the day on sofa in basement. Tea at 6. Even'g Dr. J. called — seems satisfied that it is not E— got to bed soon after 10 & passed a comfortable night [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### December, Friday 31, 1875

No change in the weather — face still much swollen. Dr. J— called while I was at my [illegible]. I got to studio about 10.30. Had to kindle fire — then wrote a note to Hon. H. E— Howland. Laurie saw him & says he will attend to my jury summons. Then to work on big drawing — finished brown horse. Lunched about 2 o'clk — then worked as long as I could see. Miss Abbatt made me a present of an exceedingly fine worsted Rug. Bundled my face up & went to tea at 6.30. Mary & the Dr. came in at 8 o'clk. for a few minutes — I got to bed about 11 — Soon went to sleep & forgot all about the old or the new year.
