## Left Page

### July, Monday 5, 1875

Overcast, warm & muggy — but
finally a fine day. The
mtf. I spent on piazza
Reading & swinging in hammock.
Mr. Beach arrd. from NY, in the
mtf. — dinner early — Mr. B.
arrd. later in p.m. Played
croquet on H.W.B's ground
with Emma & Ella until
after 5. Tea at 6. At 6.20
the mare Maggie was saddled
for me & I went at once to Mr.
K's — Miss Sallie was ready
& waiting — we rode until
after 8.30 — Sauntering and
found it very cool and
pleasant. Both of us
came back to Mr. B's house
where we waited a display
of juvenile fine works. Then
at 10, ice cream, when the
little company of neighbors
broke up and we got to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 6, 1875

Very warm mtf. Hf[?] at 8 o'clk.
atq went over to H.W.B's house
and commenced a sketch in
W.C. Worked until 12. when
I was driven off by a thunder
storm — Dinner soon after —
at 2.15 Mr. B. & Dea. F. started for
NY. Several storms passed
around us — I worked in-doors
on sketch of the mtf. until
at about 4 o'clk. when Emma,
Ella, Geo. & I went out for a
ride, although the sky was all
overcast & the air was filled
with mutterings of thunder.
We had a very pleasant ride
but at last were driven in
by a tremendous storm that
broke grandly over us about
6.30 — Tea at 7 — then walked
out back of the house to see effects
driven in by more rain. Spent
the evng. Reading. To bed at
10 — gentle Rain. [illegible]
