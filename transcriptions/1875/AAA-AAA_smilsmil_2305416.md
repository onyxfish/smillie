## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 4, 1875

Heavy Rain all the morning. At 9.15 at Dr. Noyes — he made a careful examination of my ears — up my nose and down my throat but came to no conclusion as to what caused the ringing — Got to Studio at 10.30. Got out Sierra picture but could not make up my mind to tackle it. To dinner at 1 — back at 2.15 & worked on charcoal study "Cathedral Spires in Yosemite". I hope to make a Water Color of it for such things. Geo. Whitney came in at 4.30 — Geo & I went to tea with him at 6 — at 7, all at Avenjo. Short call. — At 8.30 I went to Kimball's — There until 10.30, then to Studio — In at 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 5, 1875

Charming Spring day — Surprised when I came down to the [illegible] table to find Mother there. She & Father came down by boat last night. Got to Studio at 9.30 & to work on Charcoal Study "Cathedral Spires — Yosemite" — To dinner at 1 — back at 2.20 & worked until after 6. Father came in. To tea at 7 — at 8 again at Studio. Spring meeting of the Water Color Socy. Nicoll had to go away sick — Smith was in Baltimore but we had a quorum & transacted biz. adjourned at 9.30 — In at 10.30 & right to bed.
