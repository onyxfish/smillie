## Left Page

### May, Sunday 2, 1875

Cold, clear & very windy. Mrs. went to Dr Hastings
& heard him preach from 23 Psalms. 1-2.3²- v- After
service walked to 48 [illegible] -
with Miss Rogers & Mr. Griffith then on up to the Park &
at 30 to th. V.V.'s — there to dinner — then at 4.30
a nap — Tunie waked me at 5.30 — Julian Hedden
& — Fessenden there —
Tea at 7.00 — After 8 —
[illegible] & I went over to Will —
Walked down to 41 St —
at 9.15. Wrote notes to
Hy Tyler — Mrs. Tattinalps
& Mrs. Stoughton — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 3, 1875

Clear, cold day. Up late
& did not get to studio until
almost 10 o'clk. Put second
painting on 9×16 " on the Saco"
To dinner at 1 after leaving
academy tickets for Kitty ? —
Back to studio at 2.40 and
painted until after 5. getting
my picture done, after a fashion
Bob Ives came in & stayed
until after 6 — at 7. went
to D. Noyes in Madison ave.
& made appointment with
him for to-morrow. To tea
at 7.30 — at 8.30 called
on the Blacks in 38th St —
& had a very pleasant
time until about 10.30 —
To bed about 11. [illegible — ~4 words]
