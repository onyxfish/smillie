## Left Page

### September, Sunday 19, 1875

Cold windy rainy mfg; and I suffering with headache so that I did not get up until late — then took a very light bkfst — mfg to studio. Sat most of the mfg trying my feet to the fire trying to get warm — and did not — Dinner abt 3.30 — ate lightly — Joe Williams — Jancaster Burlin — Ella d. to dinner with us — Again to studio — unfit for anything, so I spent the afternoon putting up nail holes — Tea at 6 and then to bed — gave it up.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 20, 1875

Did not get out of bed until about 1 o clk — headache — then to Mr. Dud's & got something to eat — then to studio & fussed there all the P.M. — putting and oiling things — sweeping up & generally putting things to rights — unable to do anything else. To tea at 6.15 and after that got — I went to Mr Jessup's to call — quite a little party of young folks — feeling miserable I excused myself about 9 & went down to Fan's where I toasted my feet until 10 — then to bed.
