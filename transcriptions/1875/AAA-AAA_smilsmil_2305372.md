## Left Page

### February, Friday 5. 1875.

Very clear & cold — got to studio at 9.15 & worked all the mng. on W.C. drawing making but little progress — to tell the truth I did not feel like work — at 12.30 to 44 [illegible] — to dinner — at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal Kate came in late — detained on R.R. — after Rehearsal went to Siegman, then to N.A. — in to tea at 7 — at 8.30 back to studio & worked until 9.30 — then to N.A. — to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 6. 1875.

Cold, cloudy & windy. Got to studio at 9.30 — Set oil palette & worked all day on 10×14 — "Macmillan's Woods. North Conway." To dinner at 12.30 — back at 2 & worked until 5 — To tea at 6.50 & at 8.15 again at studio — work until 9.30 then to U.A. — met there Bellows, Perry, Homer and W. Jno. Sherwood. Again at studio at 10.20 — there until near 11 — then to 44 [illegible] & to bed at 11.30
