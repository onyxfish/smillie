## Left Page

### April, Saturday 24, 1875

A very pleasant morf, but late
in the P.m. it changed to Rain.
About 10 P.M. a horrible snow +
sleet storm. Got to studio
soon after & set to work painting
on 12×22 — also able take my
a picture for [illegible] — To
dinner at 1 — back at 2.
& worked as late as I could
see, getting over all but the
foreground. Delayed by Rain
so that I did not get to tea
until after 7. [illegible]. went to
last Phil. Concert — at 10.30.
went to Sommervillee's Gallery
Private views of [illegible] coll.
ate his oysters etc. Looked
at his pictures — then to
bed at 12 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 25, 1875

Dissipation too much for me
Did it — got out of my bed
until near 9 o'clk all night.
Bad headache all day —
Then had some gruel and
got to bed again at mid-
night. [illegible — ~3 lines]

Fine wake at the Union
League Club House this
mng + nearly destroyed
it. The W.C. [illegible] is still
on its walls. [illegible — ~2 lines]
