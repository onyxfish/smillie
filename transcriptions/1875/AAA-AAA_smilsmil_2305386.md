## Left Page

### March, Friday 5, 1875.

Dirty weather again and more snow. Got to studio very soon after 9 o'clk — but before I got to work on "P in s" Johnston of the Mail & T.H. Smilth & J.C. Nicoll came in. It was nearly 11 o'clk before I could get at my painting. Worked until nearly, then prepared my lunch — at 1. At 2.30 at Philharmonic Rehearsal. After that, went to Devry's & ordered a coat — then to Austin & other places on my way back to studio, meeting Belle S. on the way. To tea at 7 — at 8.10. at A.H. Baldwin's Studio — a Board Meeting, W.C. Socy. So at 11 o'clk. & right to bed. Still snowing &

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 6, 1875.

Overcast until late in the afternoon — Got to studio at 9. To work on 10×14." On the "Housatonic" with an idea of finishing it for a sale of Eaton's works next week. Although my head hurt me I stuck closely to it. To dinner at 1 o'clk — back at 1.45 and worked until after 5 — when I got pretty nearly finished then tried to do a little cleaning up & sweeping — To tea at 7 — went until 9.30 writing — then washed brushes & to bed shortly after 10 o'clk.
