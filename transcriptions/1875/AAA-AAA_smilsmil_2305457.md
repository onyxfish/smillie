## Left Page

### July, Sunday 25, 1875

Charming day but pretty warm. B'kf't at 8.30 - then to studio's & wrote to father - then to Baptist ch. & heard Mr. Cheshire preach from 11 Cor. 9: 15 v. After service walked down with the Drinker's - then again to studio's. Dinner at 1.30. on W. J. S's piazza until 3.30 - then to studio's. Finished letter to father and one to Charl. Tea at 6 - at 7 at Studio's. Did Nathaniel's am't at 1.30 this morning. Evett. went to Baptist Ch. & heard a sermon from Rev. 21st C. 1-4 v. "The new Jerusalem" on our way home from ch. stopped at Mrs Jessup's until near 10. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 26, 1875

Charming day with showers. Got to studio's before 9 & commenced a composition suggested by sketches at Peaks kill near H. W. Beecher's. Painted on an old piece of mill board with a surface like sand paper. The result was that I made slow progress. Dinner at 1 - again at work at 2. - worked slowly. Resting & painting until 6 - then tea, another shower after a splendid sunset - on Tyler's piazza until near 8 - then to P.O. & at 8.30 Geo. & I went to Bessie Riley's - there until a few minutes after 9 - then to Mrs Mulford's - there until 10 - then to our room & to bed at 10.45 [illegible — ~3 words]
