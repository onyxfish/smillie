## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 10, 1875

Horrible day of down pouring
rain. D. T. came again
in the morn. Also did many.
I got up & had some
beef tea about 8 o'clk.
I spent the afternoon
in the front basement.
Kate came down from
Co'. & called on me about
4 o'clk. Took a bowl
of gruel at 6.30 — We tf.
Charl came in — Sat with
me from 9 until 10.15, then
I went to bed — Passed
a very comfortable night.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 11, 1875

Felt quite well when I
got up — Very pleasant
mild morning. About 10
Will called to see me — then
I went to [illegible] Throop's. Walked
down to Studio and got a
lot of letters — then to dinner
at 12.30 Kate came in &
took a "nasty plate of soup" &
got ticket to Thomas's Reh.
I paced the deck — wrote
a note to Josie Bentley —
Kate came in at dusk —
stayed to tea — During played
most of the week & also 45 —
Charl. took Kate to Mary's
where she stayed all night.
