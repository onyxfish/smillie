## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 26, 1875

Glorious m[or]g. Got up with a headache. [illegible] at 8 - Som[e time] 9. with Miss Ely. Miss Mary + [illegible] Rode out to the Park - visited the centennial Buildings + Return[ed] at 1 o'clk. After a most charm[ing] ride, but suffering with my hea[d]. Lay down + had quite a sle[ep] before dinner at 3 o'clk. Got up again before 5 o'clk. and went to bed. Did not get [to sleep] until about 8.30 - very warm, close even[ing]. To bed again before 10 -

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 27, 1875

*Atlantic City — Clarendon — Room 6 —*

Up at 6 o'clk. [Left] at 6.45. Mr. Whitney. Miss Mary. Miss Ely. Geo. + I got off on 8 o'clk. Train for Atlantic City where we arrived at 11 o'clk. Went to Hotel + got our Rooms, then to the beach + walked up to the lighthouse + about before dinner at 1.30. Then rode from before 3 until near 6 - up + down the beach - Glorious day for sunlight, air + color. Tea at 6.30 - Then to Train hoping to meet the Lewis's but they did not come - telegrams instead. Walked to beach + enjoyed sunset + twilight. Even[ing] spent in chatting. To bed just before 10. Day very cool — hardly 65° in the shade.
