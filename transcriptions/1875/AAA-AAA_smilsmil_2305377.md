## Left Page

### February, Monday 15, 1875

Very cold again & windy, but clear. Got to studio at 9.15 and to work on 24×44 Merced group. High Sierra with charcoal. Set palette & got ready to paint — then to dinner at 12.45 — back at 2 o'clk. and pitched into the paint but I made a good deal of a mess of it — much to my disappointment. Soon after, Father surprised me by coming in — walked up to tea together at 6.30. At 8.30 I went to N.A. — back at 10.30. Father stayed with me all night. To bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 16, 1875

Beautiful winter day. Bkfst. at 8.30 — at 9 Father & I walked down to N.A. — there until 10 o'clk. Then to Studio — Misses H. & S. came & spent the time with them until 1 o'clk. Then to dinner — back at 2.15 — Set palette and worked all over the sky & mountains that I painted yesterday — getting them somewhat better but yet far from what I wish. — To tea at 6.45 — at 8.30 at Studio there until 9.30 — then to N.A. Glorious moonlight night. Father & Kate Red. to P. in the 4 P.M. Train — got in bed all day with severe cold. I got to bed about 11 o'clk.
