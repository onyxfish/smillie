## Left Page

### October, Monday 25, 1875

Dream — dream — dream —
After bkfst. wrote a note to Fan
M — then in town — back & at
about 10.30, Fanny, Kate & I
went to Livingston's woods —
down to the River & sauntered
about until after 12. Then
home — Dinner at 1 — ab. 2.
Fanny, Kate, Ge. & I went
on a drive down to Barnegat
& enjoyed it exceedingly —
back before 5 — Tea before 6 —
Matt there. At 6.50. Fanny, Matt
& I walked in to town — left
Matt then door & then on to the
dépôt & took 7.35 Train to N.Y.
where we arr'd at 10 — Went
with Fanny to her home &
then to 141 [illegible] & to bed at
11.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 26, 1875

Overcast & threatening — Got to
studio's soon after 9 — but could not
get in as Coleman had my key.
Waited about until after 10 —
then started for his house —
Belle Sweedy met me in the
street & we went to C's 81. 5th
ave. Mary J. there — a jolly little
call. Bella had three Phil.
tickets — sold me one — we then
went to Dedan's — met C. there.
I went to studio & commenced
clearing out — To dinner at 12.30
back at 2 — worked hard until
near 6 — To tea at 6.30 — At 8
at Kimball's — there until 10
— then out in a heavy shower.
To 41 [illegible] & to bed at 10.30.
