## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 8, 1875

Horrible day, sleet & rain —
got to studio at 9.15 and
got started on a W. C. sketch
9¼×13¾ to give me an idea
of what I am to do on my large
picture. To dinner at 1.30
— back at 2.30 — worked until
4.15 — could see no longer —
a bad muddle of a sketch —
B° of Control meeting — W.C.S.
to accept terms of the N.A.
J.D. To tea at 6.40 — at
8.30 called on the Rogers's
23 W. 48° S: Them until 10.15
Then to 41 S: & to bed at
10.45

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 9, 1875

Still disagreeable weather.
got to studio at 9.20 & again
at work on yesterday's sketch
meant to dinner at 1.15 — back
at 2.30 & worked over the
same sketch until 4.30
when I could see no longer.
Charé came in about 5.45
we walked up the avenue
together. Tea at 6.30. At
8.30 — at Anna Cook's where
I found a party at work
dressing dolls. Stayed
until 9.40, when I started
for 41° S: stopping at the
studio — got to bed a little
before 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
