## Left Page

### April, Monday 26, 1875

Delightful day — At 9 o'clk
went to the Union League
& took a look over the W.C.
pictures & found them very
little damaged — To studio
at 10 & to work on 12×22 —
got very little done as I was
much interrupted. Frank Reid
& some Boston friends called —
To dinner at 1 — back at 2
but at 3 had to go again to the
club to meet insurance & other
people — back at 5. Painted
a little — more calls — To tea
at 7 — at 8.30 called at 5th Ave
Hotel. Miss Boggs & friends not
in — then I went to Chapmanś
sale — there until 11 — my
two pictures, that I sold in the
Snedecor Sale last Dec. for $40.
& $67.50 — sold in this sale for
$100 + $180 — Rough on me —
Walked up & to bed at 12 —
