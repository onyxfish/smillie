## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 23, 1875

Overcast m'g. — threatening rain.
Uncle W. & Aunt A. went to Vassar
College Commencement. I worked
in doors (after walking about
until 9) until dinner at [illegible].
The weather cleared & the p.
m. was very warm & beautiful.
Worked out of doors from 3 until
6.40 — then tea — after tea
Emma, Aunt Jessie & I went
down to the River — back to
the house at 8.30 — to bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 24, 1875

Up at 5.30 after a miserable night.
Took a cup of tea & at 6 drove in
town with Aunt Agnes — just in
time for the Mary Powell — there.
at 6.45 went to Matt's and had
help — at 8 drove down to Terrace
& got Kate & Geo. Then Matt and
at 9.15 out at Uncle Willie's — after
trying to work, gave it up & went
to bed for an hour or more — Had
dinner soon after 1. About 3 I
took my work out of doors &
kept at it until after 6 — then
tea — & having dined in for Ed.
After tea, all went down to the
River — there until 8.20 — then
as David had gone, I harnessed
the horse & about 9 took Matt, Kate
Geo. & Ed. into town — Drove
out alone after 10 — Charming
night after a very hot day —
got to bed at 10.40 but did
not get to sleep until 2
o'clk. A.M.
