## Left Page

### August, Thursday 12, 1875

Heavy rain again during the mrg. After bkfo? wrote a letter to Coleman, then up to studio about 10 o'clk. At once more got started at work. I painted on foreground trees on "Bridal Veil Fall" — Early dinner (12.30) on acc't of W. Gilman's return to NY — again to studio & painted — heavy showers — Joe Mrs [illegible] Will Tayler came up — Tea at 6.20. About 8 went down to Fan's, Mr. Isaac Riley there — got came in later. Whist until 10 — Knee growing gradually stronger — To bed at 10.45. Still raining.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 13, 1875

Rain — Rain — still Rain — Bkfo? at 7.45 — After bkfp? I wrote a couple of notes — to Sander & Fleming, then to studio at 9.30. To work on "Bridal Veil" — wind changed to west — heavy showers — Dinner abt — back to work at 2 — very heavy shower at 4 — so dark that I could not see to work for some time. Kept at it until 6 — then to tea — Down to P.O. — at 8 went to Court House — heard Mr. Burnett read for the benefit of Episcopal Ch. At 9.30 went to Fan's with [illegible] B. & Hetty S. There until a little after 10 — then to my room & to bed shortly after [illegible]
