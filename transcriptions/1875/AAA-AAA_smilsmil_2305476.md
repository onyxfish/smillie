## Left Page

### September, Friday 3, 1875

The warmest day this seas[on].
Ther. in my studio got up to 80°.
Soon after Elmer got to studio
I wrote letters to Mulvaney &
Coleman concerning Varin's stud[io]
sent them to P.O. by him & in
½ an hour he ret'd — with Bill
the horse — I painted from him
until 12 o'clk — then in my
Room until dinner abt 1 — &
back at 2. Kate Riley som[e]
came with Dr. Vail — She
remained all the P.m. &
I did very little. Tea ab[t]
6\. Went to P.O. then to
J.V.'s & saw Kate R. & the Dr.'s
family — at 9 went up to Fain's
— there until 10 — then to
bed.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 4, 1875

Clear cool day with high wind
from S.W. Got to studio about
8.30 & to work on 11×15. Hudson
near Peekskill — Jessie Comstock
came in & I painted from her
for an hour. At 12 she left —
Fink came to advise — at 12.45
to dinner — the sisters Butler
ret'd to their home. Back to studio
at 1.30 & Painted until 3.30
on H.P. picture, then went out
to ride with George — Sky very
much overcast, but effects
were fine — In at 5.30 — tea at
6 — washed up brushes — dressed
— went to W.J.J.'s — Cheshire family
there by invitation — stayed until
8.30 — then to P.O. & to Jen'. Searle's
family party, celebrating birth-
day of Hettys — & the chrystal
wedding of Josie B — There
until nearly 11 — then to my Room
& to bed.
