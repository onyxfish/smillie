## Left Page

### March, Thursday 25, 1875

Very pleasant day — got to studio about 9. Miss Abbatt came at 10 & until 12.30 I worked on a little water color sketch that she might see the method. Then Mr. Stafford of Chicago came in & it was near 2 before I got to my dinner — Back to studio at 3 & found there Dr. & Mrs. Durrock & Mabel & Col. & Mrs. Somnis & son — we went into Jas. Hart's & then did all down to Jno. T. Johnston's — then at 4.30 to Avery's — then to Bradleys & table I left them. To studio & at 6.30 walked up with Joe and Charl. Tea at 7 — at 8.30 at Anna Cook's — there until 10 — to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 26, 1875

Milky mtf. — cold, slitting rain during P.M. & tried damp night; at 8.30 at the Brunswick Hotel, about 9.30 B'kft. with the Somnis party — Did not get over to studio until 11 o'clk. Then did little else than write a letter. E. Brown called. To dinner at 1.30 — feeling very tired I lay upon the sofa until 3 o'clk then down to Dewey's — tailor — got to studio at 4 o'clk — there was J.C. Nicoll — until near 6 — Did nothing — there to tea at 7. at 8.15 at the Rogers. 23 W. 48 St. There until 10.15 — then to bed at 10.45.
