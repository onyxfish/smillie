## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 7, 1875

Warm and overcast — Bkf. at
8 — then went to the B' house &
worked there for an hour — the
entire B. family expected, but
only a portion came — introduced
to Mrs. Col. Beecher. At 10.30 got
away in carriage with Emma,
Nea. Violet & Geo. Reached
the top of Anthony's nose soon
after 12 — took a couple of slight
sketches — the road very rough
until we crossed the mt & then
by comparison it was like a
garden — got to the Highland
House abt. 30 — put out the horses
& went to the church yard & got
another sketch — then dinner
at 2.20 — Soon after 3 we got
under way again & after taking
a turn through Cold Spring, at a
little before 5 we reached Mrs.
Healy's — out on the hill with
Miss H. Emma B. & Geo — another
sketch — Tea at 6 — & at 6.30 [illegible]
again — Drove right down to
Garrisons — then a little after 7 —
Maggie had cast a shoe — got a
blacksmith to set one. At 7.25 the
girls bid us good bye & started alone
over the mt. for their home & at 7.42
we took train for our home where
we arr. at a little before 9 —
all well — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 8, 1875

Delicious day — very warm, but
with cooling breeze from the N.
Bkf. before 8 — then to work &
spent most of the m'g going
over & working up a little the
sketches that I brought back —
also worked on the W.C. sketch
of the Beecher house — Uncle
W. came in — Dinner at 2.30
P.M. worked on W.C. until
6 o'clk. Then out with Nose
until after 7 — tea between
times — then washed & dressed
& at 8.30, with Geo & Father
called at Eastman's — found
Mr. & Mrs. E. in & had a very
pleasant time until near
10 o'clk. Then walked to the
P.O.    Back & to bed at 10.30
