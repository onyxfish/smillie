## Left Page

### June, Monday 21, 1875

Very warm day — Up at 6.30. Richp:
at 7.15 & at 8 o'clk. Father & I walked
up to town — I kept on and at 9.30
got out to Uncle W's — I commenced
work at once. washing in sky, in
view of house. Rested awhile
in bed — Dinner abt — Soon
after 2 set up umbrella out
of doors & worked until 5.30
Tea at 6 Suef, Emma &
I went down to the River —
joined by uncle W. & aunt
A. wandered about until
after 9 — then in & to bed at
10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 22, 1875

Very pleasant day. Mother &
Aunt Alphia were brought out
by Uncle W. & spent the day
with us. I worked in doors
all the m'f on drawing —
Dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon
I worked out of doors until 6
then tea — Soon after 7
I went in town with Mother
Aunt Alp. & Emma, & went
to Terrace & to Matts — Back
to Hill Side at 8.30, and to
bed at 10.45.
