## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 1, 1875

Still clear, beautiful weather. B[illegible]
at 8 oclk. Then, until after 10 packing
trunk — then to Studio — Miss Bond
called — found card from Will
so, at 12 started for down-town. Saw
Goodall & declined to make a
drawing for him. Lunch downtown
got to studio again at 2 — packed
up — went to Knoedlers & D[illegible]
at 3.30 found myself very short of
time — hired a cab — drove up
to the house & then over to R.R.
depot getting there just 5 min.
before 4 — Found Geo. there. Got
to Po' at 6.25 — Rode right up to
the house. Aunt Alphea arr'd
but a short time before us —
Tea at 7. Eveng passed in
general confabulation & to bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 2, 1875

Clear & charming but my head
all out of sorts. Took a walk
with Father after B[illegible] and
during the m[illegible], got some [illegible]
things put away. Dinner at
12.30 and all this afternoon was
spent in misery on the sofa
in Father's Room. Tea at 6
when I got up. Father did
not come in until 7. Then
we had a confab with Eastman
Mr. Nick called. After
some newspaper reading
got to bed about 10.
