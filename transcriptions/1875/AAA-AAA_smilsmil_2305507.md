## Left Page

### November, Thursday 4, 1875

Rain nearly all day — at all day I fought with a headache. Got to studio at 9 & painted on Trees through middle distance of "Sierras". To dinner at 12 clk — back at 2.15 and painted but little during the P.M. — At 5.30 went to the Kimball's to tea by appointment, as we were all to go down to St. Paul's to hear the choirs of Trinity Parish. I managed to get through tea but begged off from going to St. Paul's & instead at 8 went up to 41.45. Sat & listened to Diwing until 9, then to bed. Louis Tiffany called in the m[orning].

## Right Page

### November, Friday 5, 1875

Pleasant day. Got to studio at 9. & Painted over foreground of "Sierras", getting the whole thing into much better shape. Miss Abbatt called — went to dinner at 12.30 — back to studio for a few minutes at 1.45 — then to Academy of Music. 1st. Philharmonic matinee — a new arrangement — don't like it — very few out. Bella & Florence already in their seats. After matinee went to Faulons (Dewey) — back to studio at 5.45 & to tea at 6.30. Spent the eveng until nearly 11 writing to Bellows. Santrain & prints — To bed at 10.30.
