## Left Page

### February, Sunday 7, 1875

Very cold + overcast — mvf
went to D. Hastings + heard
him preach from Matt. 8th ch
2 + 3° v. Church after lelpser,
After services, Rode with mrs
Jessup + Josie up to H.P.R;
— there to dinner at 2.30 —
Charl also + Kate — at 4.30
Kate, Charl + I took a short
walk to look at the 4th ave
tunnel — then in + Kate cut
my hair — then I took a short
nap — Tea at 6.30 — evng
wrote some notes — walked
down to 41st at 9.30 + to bed
about 10.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 8, 1875

Very cold day — some fine snow in
the mt, but soon cleared, cloudless
The mt papers announced the
death of Gilbert Burling + J. O.
Eaton — I wrote after bkfst until
near 10 o'clk — then to studios, then
to N.A. met there Nicoll, Smith
Walter Brown, Bellows, Hubbard
+ Silva — arranged to dralse the
pictures by Burling + Eaton. Back
to studios at 11 — at 1.45 a man
came from Miss Sawtite + I went
with him to Knoedler's + bought a
brush for her. To dinner at 2.45.
Back at 1.45 — Pedc. Secty of Chicago
Academy called + wanted to buy
some pictures cheap — Kate called.
I worked on 10×14, Keene Valley
as long as I could see — To tea
at 6.45, at 8.15 at N.A. meeting
of B. of C. Resolutions were
passed — To studios — then to
bed at 11.30
