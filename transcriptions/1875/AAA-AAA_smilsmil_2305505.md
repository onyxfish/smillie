## Left Page

### October, Sunday 31. 1875.

Cold, blustering day — Up
& off — at 9 — Mrs. went
to Dr. Hastings's — Heard him
preach from Acts 1st ch.
11th v. Charl came in &
after service I walked with
him to his vo. house, 22.
W. 51st S. Then up to A.
V.V's — I stayed there to
dinner — then walked
in the Park until about
5 — then to Will's — took
tea there & stayed until
9 o'clk — then to 41,,
& to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### November, Monday 1. 1875.

Cloudy & cold — Apto Studio
at 9 — Kindled fire & then
commenced painting "Eve'g
among the High Sierras" ×
Painted over the distant
mts & have hopes that I
will be able to make some-
thing out of it — To dinner
at 1.20 — back at 2.30 &
Painted until 5 o'clk —
Then did some writing &
to tea at 6.30 — At 7 Di & I
& I went to Dr Jno. Hall's church
5th ave. & 55th St. to hear the new
organ — great crowd — met
Charl & Miles P. After the
playing went all through
the organ — much pleased &
interested. To bed at 0.30.

Got a letter from Jno — he's
in the dumps & does not know
when he will come to the city
