## Left Page

### September, Sunday 5, 1875

A perfect day — cool, clear and
delicious. Up late for bkfst 9 o'clk.
Mrs. went to Baptist Ch. &
heard Mr. C. preach from Stph.
6: Ch. 19: V. "The Relations of the
ministry to the mysteries of Re-
demption." Walked up the hill
with Bessie Coelper. Dinner
about 1.30 — Stuart & Josie B.
there. After dinner, under the tree
for a time, there a number of us
went up to Studio's — there until
4.15. Then tried to do some
writing — failed. Tea at 6 —
there down the hill — met
Fan — took a short walk
with her to to Studio's and
wrote to Fanny Kimball
& to Kate. To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### September, Monday 6, 1875

Superb Sep. day — Rain very
early in the mng. Then strong
N.W. wind, beautiful clouds
& glorious cool, sweet air —
Very much interested in the
setting of wood shute in my
clear so that little work
was done in-doors. Started
Charcoal design! Come down
busy! Dinner at 1, again
more work out of doors than
in doors — the day continuing
superb to the end. Sea at 6.
Cvef at 7.30 went into [illegible]
down to P.O. Called in at
Fan's but she was out. Ab-
about 9.30 went to my room,
Read the Trib. & to bed at
10.30.
