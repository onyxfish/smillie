## Left Page

### May, Sunday 30. 1875.

Unable to get up in the m[orning]
after a miserable, feverish
night. About 4 o'clk P.M.
got up & washed & dressed
then went out to the square
& sat there until near 6 —
W. Lewis came there & met
me. — Tea at 6.30 — felt
very much revived in the
evg. which was spent in
general chatting until 10
o'clk — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 31. 1875.

Lovely day. B[reakfast] at 8 & at 9.15 I
went over to Claghorn's house to see
him — not in — followed him up to
the Centennial Loan & [at] Rittenhouse
[S]q. — had left there, so I called at
J. W. Lewis — 2016 Spruce S[t]. — Not
in — then rode to 3[rd] & Walnut &
went to Bank — Claghorn not there
so I went to 524 Walnut S[t]. and
called on Kate Drinkcer & Col. B[ush]
wood jun[ior]. Then again to Bank
found C. — Had a long talk
with him on Academy & Centennial
art business — Introduced to several
gentlemen. — After 12 when I began
my saunter up Chestnut S[t]. Stopped
at Earle's & other places — visited
again the buildings on Penn. Sq[uare].
In the office of Claghorn's we[nt to]
the Academy [illegible]. — At 2.10 at
Mr. Whitney's — found in — he had
gone to dinner — Then to the house —
found there W. W[m]. J. Richards who
had come to dine with us — Gen[l].
Cram called just as we sat down
to dinner — short call — dinner at 3 —
After that with R. — Willcox called
[in] the Rest of the P.M. soon got away
from us. — Tea at 6 — At 6.30
Nel & I bid our friends good bye
& went to the W. Phila. Train[s] &
[ar]r[ive]d at 7 o'clk. — Got to 123 W. 41[st]
at just 11 and Ret[ired] to bed.
