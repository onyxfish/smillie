## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 13, 1875

Bkf. at 7.40 — then out to Hunt —
& had him send for my Trunk
to mend it — gave orders to
Renner & got to studio at
9.30 — at 10.30 started for down
town — stop[p]ing in Bapfers[?] —
to order packing boxes — then
to A.B.N. Co. — to Charl's — 70
Wall S[t]; then to N.J.C.R.R
& at 1.10 to dinner at D's —
again at studio at 2.30
a couple of showers during
the p.m. I worked until
6 gathering things up and
getting ready to pack —
To tea at Dinning's at 7 —
Soon after 8, at Anna Cook's
not in — a few minutes with
her mother — then at 9
back to studio — note[s] note
to Geo. & to Ty. Tyler — then
to 41 at 9 — & to bed at 11, but
not to sleep until near 1.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 14, 1875

Cool breeze but hot — Spent
the m'g in 41. 4 St. packing
up all of my things there —
Dinner at 12.10 — at 1.30
Hunt came for trunk &
parcels & carted them to
studio. I followed at once
& worked at Re-packing
until near 7 — then to tea.
After tea, at 7.45 went
to Charl's — No 14 E. 48,
there until 8.30 — then
again to studio — there
until 10.30 — then to
41 at S[t]. — & to bed at 11.
