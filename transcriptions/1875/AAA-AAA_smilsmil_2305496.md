## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 13, 1875

Superb day — a hard freeze last night — ice this morning 5/16 of an inch thick. B'kfast at 7.45 — then to studios and worked until nearly 12 on Cartoon of Horse Race — then down to Bank & up to dinner at 12.30. George left for Po— at 12.45 by way of Mauch Chunk, where he intends to stop over night. I went down town to look for a boy those — Got one of [illegible] & at 2.10 got up to studios & right to work & stuck to it until 5.30. I can work with great comfort from my model out of doors. A short, sharp walk & tea at 6.30 — To studio at 7 cleaned up, then took P. from Sam Say[illegible]s to [illegible] Beuity's. Great goings on — opening things & displaying presents — went with the whole bridal party to the church and rehearsed — then again to the house — then with P. & Fan to F's house & to bed on the hill at 11.15.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 14, 1875

Another superb day — Got to studio before 9 — ice 3/8 & 1/2 in. thick. For a time worked on cartoon of Horse Race — then on W.C. S[illegible] but felt something of the old confusion about the head that so unfits me for work. Dinner at 1 — after a short walk. After dinner got Strange to help me about the building — He finished the covering of coal shoots & hung cellar door — To tea at 6 — then dressed for the wedding — went to studios for a few minutes, then down town & stopped at Fan's — went with them (Fan, Minnie Smith & Bu M.) to the Ref[ormed] Ch. where Kitty Beuity was married at 8.6. At 8.30 went to the B. house — grand times until about 11 o'clk — the Bride & Groom went at 9.30 to D.L.& W. depot to meet the midnight train. I met the midnight train in bed.
