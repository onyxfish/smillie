## Left Page

### November, Monday 8, 1875

Bright & pleasant day — got up feeling very well.
— to Studio at 9 — kindred fine. — Then got Coleman
to superintend setting my palette & starting repainting
sky on "The Spires of the Yosemite"
To dinner abt — P.M. — I got into a mess with my
work — then came distress in my head & I was obliged
to give up in the worst possible place. To tea at 6.30 — very
nervous & depressed. — At 8 called at Tiffany's. Mad. av.
at 8.30 down to Studio. I cli[mbed]
to Sartain that I winged meet
him No. M. at [illegible]. Phila.
Then to 41 [illegible]. & to bed at 10.30
— fevered & sick — passed a
horrible night without sleep
or rest.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 9, 1875

Unable to get up. Sent around for Dr. Throals. He
came about 9 a.m. — found me suffering from combined
effects of cold & dyspepsia
— throat very sore & head feeling as though the whole
scalp had been crushed in. — Many came about
10.30 & spent an hour with
me. — In the evng. I was able to take some gruel. —
my second night was
hardly more refreshing
than the first. — Every
part of me hurting so that
I could lie in no position
with any comfort.
