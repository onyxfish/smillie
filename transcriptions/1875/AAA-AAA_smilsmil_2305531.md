## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 22, 1875

A mild, april-like day — got to studio soon after [illegible].
To work on distance & middle
distance figures — after I had
been to the barbers & had
my hair cut. To dinner at
1.30 — back at 2.15, worked
until 4.45. Then went
to Gauthier's — went into
Jalosa's new Art Room
that is to be opened to-night.
Stopped at Stewarts, bought
under shirts — To tea at
6.45 — at 8.45 again to
studio — Read newspapers
& wrote a note to Miss
Sawtelle — To 41st. about 9
& to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 23, 1875

Another very mild day — Got
to studio at 9 — Spent some time
with Miss Abbatt — Then to my
work — washed out a lot of
fore-ground green, replacing
it with orange & ochres — also
finished several of the figures
in middle distance. To dinner
at 1.30 — back at 2.20 — worked
as long as I cared see — Miss
Sallie Kittredge & aunt, and
others called — To tea at 6.30
— Every. about 8.30, called on
Mrs. Dav. Morrison (Alice
Buckingham), at 69 W. 47th.
Very pleasant visit until 10.
Stopped at Manje (4[illegible]). Pea-
nut party in Mrs. Belle's Room.
Left at 10.40 — & then to 41st.
& to bed at 11. [illegible — ~2 words]
