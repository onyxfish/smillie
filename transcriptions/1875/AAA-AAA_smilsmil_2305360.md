## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 12, 1875

Pleasant day, but gradually overcast. Got to studio about
9 am. my pupil. Miss Abbot
made her first essay. Most
of the mng was spent with her
altho' neither fus accomplished
much — To lunch at 1 o'clk.
Back at 2 — Miss Woodward
sat for an hour for "Idle
Hours" — then Geo. posed +
I worked as long as I could
see — Tea at 7 o'clk. Soon
after 8 I called upon Mr.
Hart, 118 Madison Ave — He
has splendid pictures — am to
call again — Then went to
the Rilpleys + had a very
pleasant call — There
until 10 — Dry snow —
To bed at 10.45 —
Father + Mother Rel'd to
P. this P.M. — [illegible — ~4 words]

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 13, 1875

Dark, dismal + storming
9 when I got to studio.
Got to work on "Idle Hours"
and fussed in a weak and
dribbling way — perfectly
disgusting. Afraid of color
+ too ignorant to know what
is wanted. To dinner at 1 —
back at 2 + worked as long
as I could see — To tea
at 7 feeling "played out"
but I spent the evef until
11 o'clk. fussing over Daris
Tiffany's W.C. accts before
I could straighten them
out to understand them.
To bed about 11.20
