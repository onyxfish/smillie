## Left Page

### April, Sunday 4, 1875

Heavy rain in the m'g. Went to Dr. Hastings's & heard him preach from St. Luke - 17th ch.
5th v. After service walked up to Mr. Jessup's - 78th St. and Mad. Ave. Josie very unwell — did not see her — Stayed to dinner — about 3.30 walked down to Uncle Ce's — found them yet at the dinner table — Young Gunther there. Took a nap & awoke too late to go to tea at the Rogers's — so took tea at A.V.V.'s & walked down with Geo. to 48th St. At 7.10 stopped at the Rogers's — they were yet at tea table — stayed until 10 o'clk — then to 41 2nd St. and to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 5, 1875

Bright, breezy day — 9 ot to shop about 9 o'clk. — Tire out & altered, miled, & thought it better to light it. Halberton called — No work on "Trellis Walk" until 12.30 — then to dinner — back at 2 — & worked until 6 when I got it somewhat like finished — but it is a very poor thing. Mr. Jas. M. Hart made a very pleasant call — Nicole came in — To tea at 7 — at 8 went to Charl's — back at 8.30 — wrote notes to Peck of Chicago & to Uncle Willie — To bed at 0.
