## Left Page

### April, Friday 2, 1875

Overcast m[orn] — very mixed w[eath]
no rain. Got to studio at
9.30 & to work on "Trellis walk"
but very much bothered by
my head, so that I worked
with difficulty. To dinner
at 1 o'clk — back at 2.15 and
worked until near 6 — To
tea at 7 — Then on my bed
& quite gave up going out —
but at 9 got up, dressed &
went to W. Robt. Gordon's. No 7
W. 33d. — a gathering of artists
& connoisseurs — Sutsken ab[ou]t 11 —
left at 11.30 & then to Saxon's
5 W. 38th. Clraut's club — & a
little of private theatricals" —
left there at 12 — to bed about
12.30 — Rain during the m[orn].

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 3, 1875

Raw, chilly rain — Up late, as
I felt the dissipation of last
night — U[p] to: at 9.30 and got
to studio som after 10 — Then to
work on "Trellis walk" but I
made no progress — It is a
very poor drawing, even for
me. Wm H art called — To
dinner at [1] — back at 2 —
worked until 5.30 — To tea
at 7 — at 8.30 went to Charl's —
back at 9.30 — wrote notes
to Mary Tweedy & H[?] Tyler —
got to bed about 11.
