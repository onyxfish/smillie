## Left Page

### August, Saturday 28. 1875.

Beautiful mfg. Johnston began digging at my cellar.
& I worked for awhile on Peekskill. Huds on until Jus.
Comstock came — Painted for her until 12 o'clk. Dinner
at 1 — back at 2 & worked all the P.M. on P.N. picture
getting second painting done.
Tea at 6 — very tired &
out of sorts — took a ride
with Geo until 8 o'clk —
then to the Searl's — fount
Vic D. there. Stayed until
10.15 — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 29. 1875.

Very warm day. Fighting
with a headache — Bkfst. at
8.40 — Up to studio for a few
minutes — then to Chap. in Ch.
Dr. Jaugen preaching. Subject
"Victory — more than conquerors" —
Romans. 8th Ch. 36th v. Walked
home with Lillie Joss up — on piazza
until 1.15 — then to dinner —
afternoon in studio — wrote
to Matt — Will — & Renner —
Tea at 6 — Merf. went with
Geo. to Presbt. Ch. and heard
Isaac Riley preach from
Rev. 2d Ch. 10th v. "The Crown —
stedfast to the end" — I was
so sick that I could hardly
wait until the close of the
service — Right to bed at
9.30 & passed a very Restless
night.
