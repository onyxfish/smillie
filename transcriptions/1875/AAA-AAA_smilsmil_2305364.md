## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 20, 1875

Brilliant day & cold. Went down to studio about 10 o'clk.
the mrng was spent in general water color work.
Back to dinner abt 6 o'clk
& again to studio at 2 —
More letters to write &
did a little with brush
on one of my drawings.
Mounted "Idle Hours" &
some oil sketches for
Ferguson — To tea at 7 —
Eveng spent chatting
& wrote a note to Susan
Lewis — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 21, 1875

Disagreeable & overcast — got
to studio at about 9.30 — Miss
Abbatt came about 10 — then
a long call from Nicole about
W. C. business. Then Josie Beauty
& Hetty called — Got but little
work done before 1 o'clk. When
Miss Woodward fixed up a
lunch for me — Worked steadily
during P.M. on "Idle Hours" —
after closing right, put some
lettering on a drawing for
Miss Woodward — To tea at
6.30 — note a note to Emma
Beach & at 8.40 met [illegible] at
Martin's 155 W. 21 — Saw W. [illegible] —
thru to Mr. Brom's — br W. 20c —
Saw W. B — thru at 9.25 — to
Kimball's — saw them all —
there until 10.25 — To bed
at 11.15
