## Left Page

### July, Thursday 1, 1875.

Mrs. Slightly overcast + very cool. Got to work before 9 o'clk. on drawing "Baby Bacchus in a basket" for A.B.N. Co. At it until near 12. Walked up to town — bought Strawberries — back to dinner at 1 — then again at work on drawing — at it until tea time — after 6. Rec'd a note from Imma Beach inviting us to Peekskill to-morrow. At 8 went in town & called on Fonda Gives, then on Matt & home at 10 o'clk. To bed about 10.30 — very cool nights. [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### July, Friday 2, 1875.

*Peekskill — at Mr. M. S. Beach's —*

Glorious morning — clear and cool. Did not go out for a walk, but to work on drawing. About 10.30 Mr. M. S. Beach called & drove out to Sunny Brook Farm with her. Sketched a bit down at the mouth of the brook for her. About 1.30 we drove down to Hill Side — there got dinner, then to the Terrace and there got ice, then to R.R. depot and took 3.20 Train for Peekskill — where we arrived, very hot and dirty at 4.36. Ella Beach met us & drove us right to their house — wandered over the hills until tea time, after 6. Ev'g on the piazza — Miss Sallie Kittredge, a neighbor, came in. Soon after, I escorted her to her home. To bed at 10.30 & had a very mean night.
