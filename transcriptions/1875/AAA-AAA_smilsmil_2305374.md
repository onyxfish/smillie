## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 9. 1875.

A horrible windy day and intensely cold. Got to Studio at 9.10 — Miss Abbatt came about 10.30 — I overlooked her & commenced charcoal work on 24 × 44" "Over the hills in Oil". To dinner at 1 — back at 2 — Suffered with headache & worked with trouble. To tea at 7 — Eve'g did not go out — wrote a few notes and got to bed ab't 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 10. 1875.

Charming winter day. Got to studio at 9.10 — Set palette and commenced work on 24 × 44 — "Over the hills in Oct". Did not get to work with color until about 11 — then pitched into my sky. To dinner at 12.30 back at 1.30 & painted in my distance. Before 4 o'clk felt very tired & gave up for the day. Mr. Beach — Miss Foote & friend called — Miss F brought her drawings from the Round to make application to N.A. School. Miss Degoliér called — she wishes me to give her lessons. To tea at 6.45 — back to studio's at 8.30 & wrote a note — then to N.A. at 9.30 to bed at 10.45.
