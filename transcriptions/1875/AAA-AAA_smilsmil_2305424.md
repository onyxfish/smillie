## Left Page

### May, Thursday 20, 1875

Mrf. overcast & rather chilly, but later it got to be very close, smoky and hot. Got to studio at g 2.0 & to work on "Bridal Veil". To dinner at 1 & back at 2.30 — Painted until near 6. Father surprised me then — at 7 me walked up together but he went to a Restaurant to tea — at 9 I called on Mrs. Rd: Browning, 54 W. 55th St — There until about 11 — then down & to bed — Father slept with me. ## Right Page

### May, Friday 21, 1875

Horribly hot — got to studio at 9.10 but unfitted for work. Don't know what to do about picture that I intend for the N.A.Q.D. I can't finish it now & have no money to give instead of the picture. To dinner at 1 — back at 2.15 & to work on a little sketch 10×14 that I want to finish for a sale by Schenck. Worked until near 5 — then Father came in & at 6.30 me walked up to 41st & took tea at 7 — Soon after 8 went to Tiffany's — Saw all the family. Ferguson came in — Misled by the clock, I left at 9.30 — went to bed about 10.30 — Father with me again —
