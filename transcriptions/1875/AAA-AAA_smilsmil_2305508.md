## Left Page

### November, Saturday 6, 1875

Pleasant day — m/f went to Devere's — thro to Magraths
& to Knoedler's, getting to my studio at 10.30 — To work on fore
ground of "Sierra's" — Miss Abbott
Alex. Craig & others called — It
was after 1 before I got to
dinner at 2.30 when I Ret'd
to studio. Got Sierras finished
for this painting. To tea
at 6.50 — at 8.30 at Emma's
& at 9.45 at studio — thine
until 10.30 washing up
things — thro to 41 [illegible] — and
to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 7, 1875

Very pleasant day — Did not
get up until 9 — Bkfst at 9.30
at 10.20 at F.N. Smith's to see
if he would be Ready to start
with me for Phila tomorrow
m/f. he will not — He kept me
looking at things until noon
— To dinner in 41 [illegible] — at 12.30
— until after 2 with Dining
talking of organs & organ music.
About 3 o'clk. made a short call
at Louis Tiffany — then walked
to St. Thomas's ch. & heard D. M—
preach — thro to Wile's to tea —
& at 7.30 at Dr. Hastings's and
heard him preach from Hebrews
12th ch — 22-24 v — Home at 9 o'clk
& wrote a short letter to Fain
Mulford — To bed soon after 10.
