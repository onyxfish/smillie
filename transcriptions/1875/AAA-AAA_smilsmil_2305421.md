## Right Page

### May, Saturday 15, 1875

Slight Rain in early mrf.
Then cleared very warm. Got
to studio at 9.30 & commenced
2° painting on "Bridal Veil
Fall. To dinner at 1.15. Ge.
at last all ready to start
for Po'keepsie — He is to go in
4 o'clk. Train — Bid him good
Bye & back to studio at 3 —
Painted until after 5 — Then
washed up & at 6.15 at the
Sturtevant Ho. Took dinner
with Fan & Dr. W. & Maud D.
Had a good time — another
shower — over at 9.30 when
I went with Maud to Mrs.
Bishop's — 10 W. 57 — Then
walked down to 41st. and
to bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
