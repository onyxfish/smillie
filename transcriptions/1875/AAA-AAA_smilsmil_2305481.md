## Left Page

### September, Monday 13. 1875.

Overcast mtg., clearing later in the day — Got to studio at 8.30 — Started up fire & then to work on 23×44. "Cathedral Spires" — Painted until 1 o'clk — dinner — back at 2. Mr. Jewell came up then from 2. She cleaned up my studio lamps. Charming afternoon — I painted until 6. Tea soon after — Wef, Geo. & I called at Mrs. Jessup's — the young folks all out — at 8.30 we went down to Jen. Jean's there until 10 — then Josie B. & I walked up on the hill to studio's & fixed fire for night. Glorious moonlight — then home with her — took Mrs. Miller home then & did not get to bed until 11.30.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 14. 1875.

Pleasant mtg., after breakfast went down to Hugh Mitchell's and arranged to have his letters to my come up the hill — Horace Allen in charge — Commenced painting up a view about 10 — in less than an hour it clouded over so that I was obliged to give it up — I wrote a letter to Sam Coleman & took it down to the P.O. Rode up town with Josie B. — dinner abt. 15 — To studio som after 2 — out of sorts — Painted in two foreground figures on 23×44. Tea at 6.15 — Wef, Geo. & I called on Lillie & Mina Richards about 9 to P.O. — letter from Ferg. — then down to darkie conference. A lot of the young white people there — met Fam & Gus — home with Fan — there until after 10 — then to bed at 11.
