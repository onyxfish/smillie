## Left Page

### March, Monday 29, 1875

Delicious day. Up at 7. Hlpt. at
7.30. Gel. Cook, Haven P. & W. [illegible]
left immediately after Hlpt. In the
music Room until about 10.30 &
enjoyed some duetts on Piano &
piano & violin. Then, Kid good
byes & with Ruma & Miss Howe
Rode to Junction, started them
for NY. & was then driven over to M.
W. T. Richards's — almost missed him
but had a very pleasant visit until
1.25. Then started on train, but an
accident to engine kept me — did
not get to Geo. Whitney's until 3.10. They
had Hlpt dinner waiting for me. Mr.
under the weather but very kind about
4. with Miss Mary & Miss Burnham, Rode
up to W. Mochdewig's — Mr. L. not in but
had a good time with other members of
the family & Ret'd to W. W's at 5.30.
Tea at 6 — at 6.30 the carriage again
at the door and I was driven over
to the depot & took 7 o'clk train
for NY. — Got in to Hoboken abt 9.20
into 6th ave cars at 10.40 & to 41 [illegible]
abt 11 o'clk. — Lost no time in getting
into bed —

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 30, 1875

Up early, mild, hazy Spring
day — got to studio about 9 o'clk.
& at 9.30 Mr. Stoughton came &
took her first lesson — Miss A. —
also came but none of the Rest
of my class. I worked with the
two until 12.30. To dinner at 1.
& back at 2.15 but feeling
uneasy about the head & very
unlike work. I tried to get
started on several things
but was uniformly unsuccessful.
To tea at 7 — at 8.30, with Gel.
went to the Kimball's — there
until 10.15 — then to Studio,
but my fire was out — then
to 41 [illegible] — at 10.45 & to bed
at 11. a little of patter
of rains.

Rec'd present [illegible] of a [illegible]
from Plainfield to-day.
