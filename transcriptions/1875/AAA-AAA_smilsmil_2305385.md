## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 3, 1875

A most horrible day of driving snow. Got to studio at 9.20
and immediately set to work on sky of Sierra picture
24×44. Got my new sky painted in by 12 o'clk — a
very great change and for the better. Mr. Goelpken & a
friend called on Cincinnati exposition business — To dinner
at 1 — back at 2 — Worked on N.E until 5 — then fixed
up — washed brushes etc. To tea at 7 — Evening spent
writing out charter for W.C.
Society & notes. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 4, 1875

Charming day, but all afloat underfoot. Before 9 a.m. went
to F.H. Smith's in 44th St. then to Union league — then to N.A. and
at 10.15 got to studio. Misses Abbatt, Jacobs & Converse there
waiting for me. All the afternoon given to them; altho' very
much interrupted by callers. To dinner at 1.30 — very much
tired out — back at 3 o'clk. Begged my class to give me
three weeks that I may work without interruption
in my Academy picture. Did nothing during the
rest of the afternoon. Miss Burdell called late and at
6 o'clk I walked up 5th ave with her. Tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 went
down to Averys — Miss Mary Whitney there — In at 11
o'clk. & right to bed.
