## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 28, 1875

Miserable cold Rain & [illegible].
Got to studio at 9 o'clk. Miss
Conwell & son came & until
10 o'clk. I worked on a little
water color sketch. She watching
me. Then to work painting
a 12×22 "Ausable Lake" —
To dinner at 1 — back at 2
& painted until after 5 —
when I got over the first
painting — Then some writing
To tea at 7.15 — back to studio
at 8.15 — Re-wrote a Report
of Nicole's concerning the [illegible]
in Brooklyn — To 41 × 82
at 10.45 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 29, 1875

Clear & pleasant until the
P.M. about 4 o'clk — W[illegible]
called on Nicole — then to
Knoedler's — To studio at 10
& at once set to work on 19×
21" "Bridal Veil Fall" — for
presentation to W.A. of D —
To dinner at 1 — after that
dressed for "Palette" Dinner —
at Studio at 2.45 — Painted
until 5.30 — getting, I think,
a good [illegible] — W. Smock
Lewis called — Abt. 30 went
to "Palette" club — annual Dinner
under Dohauis ring — Had a very
good time until Clark Bell
called upon me to speak for
the Water Color Socy. He had
promised me that he would not
do so — I begged off in very few
words — left soon after 11 — in
a pouring Rain — To bed at
11.45
