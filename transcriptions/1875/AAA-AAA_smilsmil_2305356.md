## Left Page

### January, Monday 4, 1875

Dull and overcast — after
Hfo. walked to town & ordered
S4b. man to call for valise
then back & packed it and
got to work on my drawing
som after 10.30. Kate dressed
& sat for the figure — About
12 Mrs. Thorne came in — up
to father's Room & saw our rdc.
Dinner soon after — again
worked until 3.30 — Packed
up drawing materials — went
into Mrs. J. for a little while
then walked to town — back
to tea at 6 — at 7 — good byes
& started for depot — took a
short cut — lost my way — got
onto R.R. Track — had two heavy
tumbles & got to depot at last
5 m. ahead of time but very
much bruised — got the last
chair in the D.R. Car — into N.Y.
at 10.03 — Right to D's & to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 5, 1875

Still dull & overcast. Got to
Studio at 9.30 — Then for awhile
then started out for a day of it
on W.C. "biz" — went to Knoedles
& then to Avery's — at 12.30 Rode
up to 41 4S & got dinner — at
1.30 Rode down town — called
at Hatch Co. Then to A.B.W. Co —
called on Camp — not in — then
on Falconer — then up to Brooks
Bros. & ordered suit for father —
then to Snedecor's — then to tea
at 6.45 — Back to Studio at
8.30 & wrote until 10.20 —
then home & to bed at 11.
