## Left Page

### April, Thursday 8, 1875

Harry Dining died this m'g about 6 o'clk. Overcast with a sprinkle of rain but clear charmingly. — b'kf't at 7.30 at the table Jane Dining was taken with violent Hysteria & convulsions — a terrible sight. She had not recovered when the undertaker came. I saw them lay out the body and at 9.15 went to Charle's — then to studio. Misses A., C., & J. came to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.45 — Hayek sent my sets of drawings & I spent the P.M. Raking things out of the closets & getting everything upside down. To tea at 7 — & at 8.45 with Ges went to the Union League — a collection of Water Colors in view — a little Supper was given to our Socy men. In at 11.30 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 9, 1875

Overcast with a smart bit of rain late in the afternoon. — Got to studio about 10 & spent all the m'g, arranging my drds, traps & confusion of trash. Made little progress. To dinner at 1.20 & at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal. — After rehearsal, went to [illegible] Studio Bldg with Nicoll and called on Gel Hall — also Hubbard & Perry — got to my own studio at 6 — to tea at 7.15 — Swe'g, as I felt tired & unwell, did not go out — did a little writing — To bed at 10.30.
