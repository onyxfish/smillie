## Left Page

### June, Sunday 13, 1875

Gloriously clear & very cool.
Bkfst at 8.30 — The J's, Kate &
I walked in to St. Paul's Epis.
Ch. Enjoyed the service & after
it walked out on Hooker Ave.
& to the top ofMund's Hill — The
view, superb. To dinner at 1.
Chatting until 3 — then I went
to letter writing — wrote to D.C.F.
& finished a long letter to Dan'
Coleman — About 6 went out
& met the J's & Geo — in to tea.
Eveft. on the Roof enjoying
the twilight — About 9 — the J's
Kate, Geo. & I went out and
walked for more than an
hour — The moonlight was
simply wonderful — Air
very cool — In at 10.30 — To bed
at 11.30.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 14, 1875

Another very clear crisp day.
After bkfst went to town & got
carriage & drove home at 9.30 —
The J's, Kate & I then started
out & the m'g was spent driving
to Vassar College & about it — out
to College Hill — never saw the
m'ts to better advantage — then
to the insane asylum and at
12, home — half an hour for
lunch — then we drove down
the back streets to Main St. dock.
At 1.15 the Vibbard came and
the J's left us. Back home at
1.50 — quite used up with head
ache — Uncle W. came about
4 & with Father, Geo. & I drove
out to Mr. Forbes — very pleasant
time there until 6 — then home.
Eveft went into Mr. Thorne's —
Mr. & Mrs. Davies there — some
duett singing — much enjoyed
— home at 10.30 — To bed at 11.
