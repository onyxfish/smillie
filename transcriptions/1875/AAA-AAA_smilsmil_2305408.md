## Left Page

### April, Sunday 18, 1875

Cold, breezy day with snow squalls. My. went to D.N.,
& heard him preach a sermon from the H. v. of the
37th Psalm. After service walked up to the Jessup's
— 78th St. — Josie J. improved
in health. Got to A.S.V.'s
at about 2. Dinner at 2.30
Günther, Newhall & Charle
mere — a great spread.
Took my nap — a cup of tea
at 7, & at 8.30 walked over
to Will's — there until after
10, then walked down to
H. us. — & to bed about 11.
D. Unrals went up to P.
last night — Mother is
reported better.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 19, 1875

Cold, March-like day. Got
to studio at 9.15 & to work
on Chapman's 14×24. To
dinner at 12.45. Aunt Jessie
at D's — back at 2 and
painted until about 4.30
when I got through with the
first painting. Did not
feel well — on lounge for
a while — then worked on
a charcoal sketch of
Fergus's until 6.30
Charl. came in. To tea
at 7. Wrote a letter to
Harry Fenn and a note to
A. M. Palmer, Union Sq.
Theatre — To bed soon
after 10.
