## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 19, 1875.

Dark, dismal day — got to
studio at 9 & set right to
work cleaning away things
& preparing to pack. Frank
Beutty called — then Fam
& Simmons. At 12 45 I went
to dinner — back at 2.30
after having looked over
a lot of Mrs. Comstock's [illegible]
worked & packing until
5 — then down town & ordered
[illegible] box — Tea at 6.15 — at
7 again down town — at 8
went up to studio — fixed
fire & at 9 went down to Fam
& played whist with Elisha,
Ben & Miss Smith until near
11 then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 20. 1875.

Charming day — to studio
at 8.30. A part of the m[?] I
laid out plans for Sprout to
set trees by. — Down town at 11
& settled a variety of matters.
To dinner at 12 45 — & again
to studio at 2 — Spent the
A.m. packing a box & doing
lots of little things about.
Austin at work painting —
at 6.15 at Fan's to tea — a
little party there — Down after
9 members to studio; there
until after 10. Stowing things.
Then to house & until after
12. Packing trunk — then to
bed.
