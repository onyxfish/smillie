## Left Page

### June, Thursday 17, 1875

Very pleasant m'f, but signs of
change. Geo. Pet'd home. Aunt
Jessie & Emma, at same time, went
in to spend the day with Matt.
I continued the outlining for view
of the house until dinner time
after 1 — after dinner again
at work until near 3 when it
began to sprinkle & I had to
give up work — then rest of the
p.m. I spent wandering over
the place — the rain did not
begin to fall with any steadiness
until after 6. Then tea at about
7 Uncle N. drove in town for
Emma. Aunt J. & little Jessie, watching
dined — they got back about 9.
To bed at 10.30. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### June, Friday 18, 1875

Delightful rain at last. Up
a little before 7 — no help — until
near 8.30 — Uncle N. unwell. Spent
the m'f writing. Rain ceased
at 11 but commenced again
about 2 & continued steadily
until 6 — I wrote to Nicoll —
Josie Beutty & part of a
letter to Auers. Dinner
at 1 & tea at 6.30 — Evening
I walked back piazza until
after 8 — To bed about 10.
