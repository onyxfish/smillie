## Left Page

### December, Sunday 26. 1875.

Dull, Raw day — Philp.: —
at 8.45 — went to Christo Ch.
in the mrf with Charl, [illegible]
& heard a very short sermon
by the Rector of St. Paul's from
Christo's answer to his mother
when found in the temple
with the elders. After service
we walked to the new Bapt
bed & in mile S., then home —
Dinner soon after 1. After
3 walked with Charl. to Matts.
with E2 went to the Mill Store
in — back home at 5.40. Tea
at 6. Swerf. all singing
until after 8. Men Vr & E2 —
went home — Chat until 10 —
then I wrote for an hour
& to bed at 11.15 — Rainy
studing

## Right Page

### December, Monday 27. 1875.

Up at 7 — Dark, cloudy mrf. Philp.: —
at 8 & at 8.30. started for R.R. —
depot — Train left at 9.5 — Weather
cleared charmingly soon after. Got
to N.Y. at 11.40 — Right to studio
kindeed fine & then to dinner
in 41st St. — at 1.15 — back at 2.15 &
spent the P.M. until 4.30
working over distance in big
drawing — At 5, went to Y.M.S.A.
to see Harl and — not in — back
to studio's. At 6.30, to tea, after,
took' wash' to Peggy Mentley
& then made a call on Mrs S.
Thorne — there until 10 o'clk. Then
to studio, long enough there to
fix five then to 41st St. — and
to bed at 11 o'clk. [illegible — ~3 words]
