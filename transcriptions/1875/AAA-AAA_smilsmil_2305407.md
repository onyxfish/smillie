## Left Page

### April, Friday 16, 1875

Miserable stormy day — got to studio about 9.30 — and then to work Re-composing & re-painting on the picture belonging to H[illegible] Chapman. To dinner at 1 — at 2.30 with Geo. at Phil. Rehearsal. Out at 4.30 — walked up to studio with Mary & weedy — Painted until 6 — To tea at 7.20 — Swef, still raining — wrote a letter to Father. and got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 17, 1875

Cold, breezy day — went up to Burgraff's — he operated on my corns — then to studio at 9.45 & to work on Chapman's picture. He came in during the m[illegible]. To dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked all the p.m. on painting, but did not get along nicely as I felt out of sorts — To tea at 7.20 — glorious night. News from home that Mother is better — Swef, for a while in at Renner's with Charl & young Townsend — wrote a letter to Maud D. & got to bed about 11.
