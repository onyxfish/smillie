## Left Page

### May, Saturday 8, 1875

Overcast and cold. Stopped at D. Percy's on way to Studio which I reached at about 9.30. Most of my absent in one way or another over charcoal study "The Spires etc." Went to Knoedles, got fixative etc. To dinner about 1. Mother then dressed & back to studio at 3. At 4.30 started alone for Plainfield as Geo. was only so so & would not go. Father & Mother Reid to Po'keepsie. I took 5 o'clk train & got to P. field at 6.10 — went right to the Sweedy's. Dinner about 7. Eve'g spent in general gabberation & to bed at 11.40. Passed a miserable night of unrest owing to dyspepsia etc. Some Rain during the night.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 9, 1875

A very warm, delicious Spring day. Slept about 9 o'clk — went to church with Belle & Mary — heard Rev. W. Vincent, a Methodist — a good straight-forward talk upon a section of the Lord's Prayer. Matt. 6 ch. 10 v. Dinner about 2. On piazza for awhile, but about 4 gave up & took a short nap. At 5 went with Mary & met Belle & Florence returning from S.S. — Then a short walk together. Tea at 6.30. Eve'g. went with the girls to church in 5th. 8 — left them there & went to Uncle P's — only he & aunt O. in. There until 8.30 — then Reid to ch. met the girls & had a moonlight walk — m and after some chatting, to bed shortly after 11.
