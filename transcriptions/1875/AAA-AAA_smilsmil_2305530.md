## Left Page

### December, Monday 20, 1875

Still very cold & windy — To studio at 9.20 — Theu to work on big drawing putting in the large figures — F.N. Smith called. Went to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.15 & worked as long as I could see. Did not quite get all of the paper covered. Nicoll called — To tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 went to Mamy's with a letter from Father giving particulars about Geo. & his condition. I also got a few words from Ge. At 8.30 called on Mrs. Sam. [illegible] at No. 11 E. 22nd St — There until 9.40 then to Studios, made awhile & at 10.45 to [illegible] St. Martin very much milder — To bed at 11.15 — Sleep broken

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 21, 1875

Up late — Very pleasant day & mild — Bkf. at 8.45 — Studio at 9.40 — Spent the morning working over sky on big drwg. At 1 o'clk. walked to [illegible] — 8— with Miss Burdell — dinner back at 2.15 & worked over distances until 4.40 — Tiffany came in — He sails for Algeria to-morrow. Nicoll came in with a communication from N.A. [illegible]. To tea at 6.45 — Sueff. felt dragged & tired. Started out to call on Louis Tiffany, but Reid. Marked a lot of my under clothes & got to bed some after 10.
