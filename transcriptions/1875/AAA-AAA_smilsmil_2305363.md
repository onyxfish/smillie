## Left Page

### January, Monday 18. 1875.

After a night of struggle for
the breath of life, awoke in
the [illegible] with a head all
stuffed up & full of pain.
I managed to get up to my
[illegible] — but could not go out
— the day was spent mostly
on the sofa. After dinner
I wrote a note to Nicole
that Geo. took down to him
& one to Miss Abbatt. The
Eve'g. it seemed to me that
I felt worse than ever. I
got to bed about 10 feeling
very feverish.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 19. 1875.

Got through the night better
than I expected, but had
my [illegible] at my bedside —
did not get up until near
12 — The afternoon was spent
in the basement, my cold
much better but my head
very light. Wrote to Kate
& to Parker. Tea at 5.30
Eve'g. with Drawing & music.
Dr. [illegible] came in for a while.
I got to bed about 10.45.
The day very clear, cold &
bright.
