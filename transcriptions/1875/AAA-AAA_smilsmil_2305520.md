## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 30, 1875

Still a very cold, windy day. Got to studio at 9 — About 10 Worms, a model came & all the m[orning], until 1 o'clk. I drew hands for my picture. To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30, but got little chance to work as Florence Lee & miss Adams, friends of Ch's came in with Nellie Jacob. Charl came in at 3.30 — At 4.30 Nicolls, Brown, Wood, Robbins & Bricher came & m[ade] a Board meeting. The N.A. will let us have their galleries for $1200.00. To tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to 25 W 44th St. & saw Whittredge — there until 9 — then to studio — finished a letter to [illegible]. To 41 [illegible] — at 11 & to bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 1, 1875

Bright, cold day. Got to studio at 9.15 — Nicoll came in, then T.H. Smith and W.C. affairs were talked over until 11 o'clk. Then Mr. Knudsen & Dr. Green came & finally my whole m[orning] was lost. To dinner at 1.30 — found uncle Willie at Dunning's. Back at 2.45, finished my tracing during the afternoon — commenced stretching paper & linen on stretcher for big drawing — To tea at 7 o'clk — at 8.30 at 10 W. 57. Spent the evening with Maud D. & May [illegible]. To 41 45 — at 10.20 & to bed at 10.50
