## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 7, 1875

another strong, breezy, sweet-smelling day — got
to studio's at 8.30 — Kindled
fire & cleared up — Set
palette & painted at Hudson
Peekskill picture until
12 o'clk — Then down to the
house to bid Vic D. good
bye — back at 2 & to work —
painted until almost 4 —
then to the house — quite
a cavalcade — 8 horses — Hom
& Ella Lathrop, Bessie Cooker, Mis.
Jewell & Miss Cooker & Geo.
Courtright & D — Ella & ms
soon scared & did not start.
We had a very pleasant ride
altho' all the ladies were" green"
Tea at 6. Flora & Miss Jewell to
Studio's with me to clean up. Then
called on Miss C. at Crossmans —
then on Bessie C — Then down
town — Letter from Will with 95 —
shew to Paris — got there — met
miss Chapman for the first tim

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 8, 1875

Yet another delightful day,
altho' very cool in the mf.
Ther. in Studio's 58°. Kindled
fire — then got sketching traps
& commenced study under
the apple trees at UW. Dud
D's — worked until dinner
time — After dinner went
down town — got check cashed
back to studio at 3 — went
out under the beeches &
commenced painting on
study of distant hills and
tree trunks — Josie B. came
for me at 4 o'clk to take a
ride, but I could not go.
Painted until 6 — then tea —
Then to Studio's — washed up &
made a letter to Will. Called
on Kate Riley — too late — then
again up the hill — a little
frolic at Crossmans — Flora
— Miss Jewell & t— — finally
to bed at 10.45.
