## Right Page

### January, Friday 1, 1875

The new year opened brilliantly — glorious day, but very
cold. I got up at 8 feeling
better, but all day suffered
more or less. I worked on
my "Idle Hours" without inter-
ruption until dinnertime,
at 1 o'clk — except when uncle
W.'s man David came with
and pew wagon to take father
get — true out to Hillside — we
could not go. I worked all
the P.m. until I "lost light."
Matt spent the day with us
& had about a dozen callers.
"Bob Palmer" called at to — at 6.30
we had tea — At 7.30 Dr. A. P. [illegible] —
surprised us while we were
yet at the table. Every pacing
the parlors & Reading. To bed
at 10.30
