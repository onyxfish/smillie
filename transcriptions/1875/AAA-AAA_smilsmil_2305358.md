## Left Page

### January, Friday 8, 1875

Very pleasant day — Got to studio at 9 and to work on little drawing made last Spring", a Bit in Berkshire Co" — an hour or so on that, then on drawing 16 × 16 — my head troubled me but I worked until 12.30 — then up to H1 "[illegible]" — to dinner — then walked down to Phil. Rehearsal — can't say that I found much to enjoy in the music — went to Snedecor's & back to Studio at 5.30 — found Miss Taylor waiting for me — in very amiable mood — To tea at 7 o'clk — Then called on Mr. G.M. Warren — 145 Mad. ave. to beg W.C.'s favour [illegible]. He, very pleasant, but I got no paintings — at 9.30 found G[illegible] at Corinne Colmant's [illegible] — there until 10.40 — then to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 9, 1875

Overcast — got to studio at 8.45 — the place so horribly dirty that I had to do some clearing & sweeping — then Miss Abbatt called — quite insisted upon my giving her lessons in Water Colors — and I yielded — About 11 o'clk got at my drawing & worked until 12.45 — then to lunch — Surprised to find mother at the Fable — father down town — Back to studio at 2 — I worked until after 4. Then could see no longer — at odd jobs until 6.45 — then to tea — father & mother there. At 8.30 took them to Mad. ave. car & they went up to Uncle C.V.V's. — Very clear & very cold — after writing several letters, to bed at 11.
