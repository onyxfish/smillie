## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 17. 1875.

Very fine day, but yet windy & cold. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on 24×44 "Sierras" — painting down the mountains. To dinner at 12.45 — back at 2 & painted as long as I could see — Mr. A.F. Bellows, Miss Beach & Miss Jacobs & sister called. — also Nicoll. Also Kate (who did not go to P. with father yesterday) and Sunnie to tea at 6.45 — at 8 went to H.P. Throop's — at 8.30, at 126 E. 28th — to Mr. Jacobs' — at 9.15 at N.a. In at 10.46 & to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 18. 1875.

Charming day. Got to studio soon after 9 — Misses Abbatt & Nellie Jacobs came & I spent the mf with them & water colors until 1 o'clk. — then hurried to dinner & back at 2.15 — Sch[ool] Palette and painted until 5 o'clk. on middle distance of 24×44 "High Sierras". To tea at 7 — at 8 called on Miss Burdell — notion — at 9.30 at N.a. — In at 10.30 & to bed at 11.
