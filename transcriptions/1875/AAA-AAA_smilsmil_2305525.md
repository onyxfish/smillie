## Left Page

### December, Friday 10, 1875

Bright, pleasant day — got to studio at 9.30 and to work tracing & outlining on big drawing — To dinner at 1.30 — at 2.30 — at Phil. Rehearsal. After that went to Sauntrees — He bought the 9½×15. that I sent him last summer for $35.00 — I asked $75.00 but did not get it — To tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 called on Mrs. Paul. Thorne at 11. E. 22d. St. She was unwell & I did not see her. Met Mr. Jessups in the street & had a long talk with him then to studios — stretched paper — made a little — at 10.30 started for 41. 8. & got to bid a little bfor 11.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 11, 1875

Bright wf but soon after, overcast. Got to studio at 9.30 — Sam McCleeam (muscular models) came & I made a half nude study from him, working until 10'clk — To dinner abt. 30, back at 2.30 & worked as long as I could see ruling on large drawing — also went to Bugnoff Mme to 41 [illegible]. & dressed — Tea at 6.30 — at 7 went up to Charl's with ticket that I got from Dinning & together, we went to Philharmonic Concert. Enjoyed it very much — I escorted Belle I to 16 St — Charl took Mary J. to 13 St — we met again at studios — after 11 when I got to 40. — & 11.50 when I got to bid [illegible — ~3 words]
