## Left Page

### June, Saturday 19. 1875.

Charming mng. B[illegible] — at 7.30 — Sauntered about the place until near 9 o'clk — then got at my drawing — outlining the building — found it rather breezy. Dinner at 1.30. Again at my work at 2.30 — the wind had so increased as to be very troublesome — worked until 4.30 when I got the outline finished. at 5.15 walked into town & got home about 6.20 just in time for tea — Music in the Park — very bad — about 8.30 Geo & I went up to Rev. Mr. Loomis's — there until near 10 — then home & to bed —

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 20. 1875.

Pleasant day, but very smoky — wandered about until it was church time — then, with Father & Mother went to the Baptist Ch. Mr. Kendrick preached from Romans, 3rd ch. 4th v. I don't like him — went in to Matt's after service — then to dinner soon after 1 — P.M. spent chatting with Mother & Reading — Tea at 6.30 — Evg., all went in to church — I took Mother to the Methodist Ch. & heard Rev. Mr. McClellan — a very spirited discourse from the words "What is that to thee; follow thou me" — after service, introduced ourselves to Mr. McC — Then to Matt's — Mr. Forbey took Mother home in his carriage — Home & to bed at 10.30
