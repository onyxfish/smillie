## Left Page

### October, Thursday 7, 1875

Dark, cloudy day until p.m. then it cleared splendidly, got to studio before 9 — After some time with Johnston, I finished a letter to S. C. F. that has been lying unfinished for a fortnight. Then to Aunt It's — then down town — went to Zelphas's — Back's — Sattrup's &
to Devitt Fordham furniture. I bought material for my fence. Then to dinner at 12.45 — At 2 at studio & to work on Lewis's W. C. Hon. W. J. J. & the Madams called — Worked badly & with many interruptions until 6 — Then to tea — at 7 at studio fixed fire & started a letter to G[illegible]. Whitney when Bessie C. & Flora J. came in dressed in old style. Went off with them & had a frolic & also to P.O. Then in to Fan's — there until 10 — then to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 8, 1875

Cold, dark, dreary day. B[illegible] — at 7.30 — Then up on the hill to see Johnston started — then down the hill to find Langdon & make arrangements with him to put up my fence — It was after 10 before I got back to my studio — Then started on Lewis's W. C. but got very little work done, Fanny Drinker & a party calling — Dinner at 12.45 — back to studio & worked well all the P.M. on drawing — a smart shower also — Tea at 7 again to studio — fixed fire. At about 8 o'clk. went with to Mr. Wm. Cookper's — left 9[illegible]. Then 8½ o'clk. & I went to P.O. Then to Fan's — there until after 10 — to bed shortly before 11.
