### January, Sunday 24. 1875

Snow during the m[orning]. went
to Dr. Hasting's & heard a
good sermon on eternal
life — Hebrews 16th ch — 7th v.
After service, walked to the
corner with Mr. Griffits & Miss
Rogers — then to A.N.A. to
dinner — arnit H. there —
at 4 o'clk took a snooze
— Horrible Rains — Tea
at 6.30 — time jogged on &
at 9 que & I walked to
41 [illegible] — Rain stopped. The
slush under foot terrible.
Home until near 11 —
then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 25. 1875

Fine day — Cold & breezy —
went to studio at 9 — then
to N.A. & pitched into the
hanging. Silva, Robbins
& Smurtiffe Hanging com[mittee]
worked until 12 — To dinner
at 12.45 — back at 1.30 and
stick until 4 — then a
Bd of Control meeting in the
Lecture Room, lasting for
an hour. About 5.30 called
on Hart, Madison Ave & 30th
S — but he would not lend us
his Fortuny — To tea at 6.15
back to N.A. at 7.30 and
worked until 10 — To bed
at 10.45 feeling completely
worn out.
