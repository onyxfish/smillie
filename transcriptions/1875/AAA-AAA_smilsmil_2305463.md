## Left Page

### August, Sunday 8, 1875

Delicious day — got up at about 8 & over to bl[illegible] — a little late. Do not feel as brisk as I had hoped to. Spent the m[illegible] at Mrs. Dud's & on Sylee's p[illegible]. Tried to write but made no headway. Dinner at 1.30 — Loafing in front of the house until about 3 — then over to W. J. J's piazza — there until near 5 — then to Dan Sayre's — Sam. Sayre's & Mrs. Mulford's. To tea at 6.15 — W. J. J. came over & sat with me for awhile. At 8. Ret. to Sylee's — wrote part of a letter to Father — fixed "wash" & my leg — & to bed at a little before ten o'clk.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 9, 1875

Glorious day — [illegible] — at 8 o'clk. after that wandered about from piazza to piazza on my two crutches until after 10 — then to my bed room and wrote for awhile. A little before 12 Gs° came with buggy & I rode with him for half an hour — then to Jan Mulford's — there to dinner & until 5 o'clk. when I went up to bed room again — wrote — Tea at 6.15 — Eveg. spent at Mrs. Dud's with Sue. Half & Reading Tribune until 9 o'clk. Then to bed room, dressed my lame baby for the night & to bed at about 10.
