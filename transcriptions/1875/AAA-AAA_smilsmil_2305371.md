## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 3, 1875

A horrible day of pouring Rain — Streets almost impassable. Got to Studio at 9 & to work on W.C. drawing "Saco" — ab't o'clk — took lun't with Miss Woodward & then worked until about 5 o'clk — F.N. Smith called in the m'f. 92. Brown came in with Wm. Layton of St. Louis to look at "Josh" & Nicoll came about 5 — to tea at 7 — Rain ceased — at 8.15 again at Studio and mounted oil sketch for Ferguson & paper for Miss Sawtell, my new pupil. at 9.30 at N.A. there met Frank Smith & wife & Mrs. J.J. Astor & son & Nicoll & wife & Avery & Bellows —
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 4, 1875

Cold, overcast & windy — got to Studio at 9 & prepared for pupils. Miss C. came first, then miss Sawtette for her first lesson — my whole m'f was devoted to them — at 12.45, I went over to N.A. f. to dinner — back at 2 & worked until near 5 on new drawing commenced in m'f. "Near Conway m Saco — then to 41 n S. and dressed — at 6.30 at Frank Smith's to dinner "to meet W. A.F. Bellows". Tillinghast, Isham Cortaudt Palmer, Page David Johnston & Bellows there — 9 steps at table — a very pleasant little dinner. Did not leave the table until 10.30 — To bed at 11.15
