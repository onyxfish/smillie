## Left Page

### August, Monday 2, 1875

Miserable, cold Rainy day — like a Sept. equinox. Went up to studio Right after bkfst — lost some time sawing hard wood knots to burn in Franklin stove — then to work on "Bridal Veil Fall" & repainted the foreground. Dinner abt — back at 2 — Read until 3 — Kindled a coal fire over my wood fire & every thing was cosy. Worked until after 5 — then went out & had a splash in the Rain. To tea at 6 — At 7, Gillman's baby died — at 8, went to studio and cleaned up — at 9 to Mrs. [illegible] & met Gel — at 10 to bed — Still Raining & strong wind during the night.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 3, 1875

Still cold, cheerless rain — Bkfst about 8 — & at 8.30 at studio. Got started again on "Bridal Veil Fall" when, about 10 o'clk Gel. pointed out water on the floor when I at once started to climb under the roof by way of the east closet, to find the leak. I got to a height of 6 or 8 feet from the floor, lost foothold & fell backwards. Trying to regain my balance that I might free on my feet I twisted myself around & fell to the floor driving my head against the door & giving my left knee a very heavy blow. Was much stunned but soon Rallied — Gel. brought my dinner up to me. Hetty S. & Josie B. called — At 6 H. & J. came up with a wagon & carried me to the house — at once to bed. Tea brought to me. Owing to pain in neck & head had very little sleep.
