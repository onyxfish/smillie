## Left Page

### March, Sunday 21, 1875

Bright breezy day. Myf went to D. Hastings and heard him preach from Matt. 11 ch 15 v. "He That hath ears" etc — a dissertation on the different ways of listing learning. After service walked up to 78 "8; & called on the Jessups — then to Uncle A's to dinner. Charl. [illegible] — at 4.30, walked down to studio — wrote a little while — To tea at 6.30 in 41 "8 — then to evening service in Hastings met [illegible] there — Glorious moonlight night — Read awhile — made a note to Will & to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### March, Monday 22, 1875

Pleasant day — Stopped at Charl's — he walked down to studio with me. I gave up the day to fussing over water color matters. De-Gordon Dimock called. To dinner at 1 — back at 2.15 — Still over my accts & papers. T.H. Smith & Symington called a little before 6 — To tea at 7 — at 8, back to studio & to work writing on my [illegible] J.S. Nicoll came in about 9 & stayed until near 10 — I wrote on until near 11 — then to 41 "8; & to bed at 11.30. [illegible — entire passage]
