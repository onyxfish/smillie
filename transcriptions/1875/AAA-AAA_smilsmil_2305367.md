## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 26, 1875

Delightful mg. Very tired when I got up —
B[illegible] about 8 — at studio 9 [illegible]
aftn g & at N.C. at 9.30 —
Miss Abbatt came to studio
& amused herself alone —
at Academy, Silva got very
mad & talked a good deal
because he could not have
every thing his own way —
To dinner at 12.45 — back
at 1.30 & worked until 5.
Then with Boldevine Falling
"barnner" until near 6 — 7.
Tea at 6.30 — back at 7.30
& worked until 10 —
To bed at 10.45, but
not to sleep until after
midnight.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 27, 1875

Pleasant day, but during
the mg. it became over-
cast & threatening. Got
up feeling very tired — had
B[illegible] at 8 o'clk — at studio
at 9 & at N.A. [illegible] at 9.30 —
General Revision of color
hanging & pushing ahead.
Belt & White — F. N. Smith
came in & kicked up a
Regular Rumpus because
so many drawings of flowers
by girls had been hung.
To dinner at 1 — back at 2
& worked until 5 at B & W.
Tn at 6 — Rolled in my blanket
on my bed until near 7 — then
tea — at N.A. at 7.30. Finished
hanging at 10 — then Catalogued
with Nicoll & Silva until 12.
To bed at 12.30.
