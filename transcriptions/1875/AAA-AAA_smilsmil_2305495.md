## Left Page

### October, Monday 11, 1875

One inch of snow — strange sight as it crested the plums of brilliant autumn colors; after help. to studios and to work on "Spires" — my work goes badly and discourages me. To dinner at 12.45 — back at 2.30 after getting Flora J. to sew some curtain stuff for me — Worked on drawing until after 5 — then fussed on window curtains until 6 — then to tea — At 7 back & boxed painting 9×15 "Glimpse of Hudson near [illegible]" — wrote a letter to Gauthier. Ab't 8.45 took box & letter down [illegible] & ab't went to Paris. Priscillea P. there — nearly 11 before I left to go on the hill — then right to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 12, 1875

Dull, disagreeable day, with much wind & snow flurries — measured the ice on water out of doors — 1/16 of an inch thick — Got to studios at 8.45 & spent some time stretching stuff on window screens — then to work on "Spires" until 12.45 — then to dinner — after that took a look at Mr. Baynes' rooms & talked "business" for next summer. Then at 2.30 to studios & worked on drawing until 5 — then could see no longer & went out & took a "constitutional" — Tea at 6 — at 7.30 for a few minutes at studios, then down the hill and at 8.40 at Paris — Gus S. there — whist until near 10 — then J. Bean & I went home with Gus — & played another game of whist there — I finally made up the hill & to bed at 11.15
