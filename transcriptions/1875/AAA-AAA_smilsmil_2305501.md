## Left Page

### October, Saturday 23, 1875

The dreamiest sort of an Oct.
Indian Summer day — Did not
get up until 9 — then very heavy
with a headache. Fanny K. —
came up to the house, just for
a moment, with Matt — For
an hour or more I hung up on
a fence & about outside of the
house — then had to give it
up & went to bed — there I
stayed until ev'g. F.K.
came to the house sometime
during the day. Much of the
ev'g spent with music —
Fanny at the piano — Matt &
Ed here — After 11 when all
got to bed — I was up
until near 2 a.m. — then
to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 24, 1875

A few drops of Rain at 8.30 but
that was all. Another dreamy day
mrs. Fanny, Kate & I went to
the Episcopal Ch. on Market
St. & heard Mr. Ziegenfuss. We
all enjoyed the services very
much — Then went to Matt's
& had our dinner there. It was
about 4 when we Ret'd home — then
Fanny & I sauntered out to Fox's
place — to the River & back to the
Emmonsville & home through
S. Liberty St. in the dusk — Tea
about 6 — Matt & Ed. in the
ev'g which was spent in
reading & singing until 11
when all went to bed.
