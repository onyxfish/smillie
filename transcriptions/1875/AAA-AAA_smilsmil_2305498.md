## Left Page

### October, Sunday 17, 1875

Up at about 8 o'clk — Brilliant & cloudless mntf. Snow about 6 ins. on the level — many trees broken by the weight of it — The effect of brilliant foliage, cmmn by snow & lighted by sunshine was like fairy land. Bkf= between 8 & 9 — then on the hill & up to studio — snow drift 22 in. deep in front of the door, another not far off, 36 in. Went to Prestn Ch. Late — home with t— To studio at 1.30, wrote part of a letter to Gel. To dinner at 2.30 but had to wait until after 3 for it — Then again to studio, then until near 6 — Then to tea — Then to Avery Fmdc's — with him until after 8 — Then to P.O. Stopped at Fan's — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 18, 1875

Dull day with rain at 4.30 — Go to studio at 8.30 — Set palette & finished painting "Old Bill" at 10 went down town to see Mulford's furnace. Got it outside & found it so old & broken that I declined to take it. Most of the mntf. spent in running after Sayre & Mulford. H— Tyler backs down from his promise to sell me another acre of land for $200 — I got at bnke $245.º on my note. Dinner at 12.45 — To studio at 2.15 & painted a 6×10 for Hetty Searl's wedding present. Tea at 6 — Evntf. at 8 o'clk went to Searl's — Fan & miss Amiith. tume — left at 11.15 — went to Fan's & the two girls tried to get a piece of pepper candy in my mouth — general confusion — To bed in stubill at 12 —
