## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 27, 1875

Bright [illegible], but changeable day. Wrote to get before going to studio where I am at 9.30 — most of the mg. spent cleaning my stove — Dinner at 12.40 — back at 2 and spent P.M. dusting + fussing — got things in such shape that I can now go to work. Colman + Jas. Hart saw cartoon for Horse Race + spoke very encouragingly. To tea at 6.30 — at 8 at [illegible] Colman's — 81 Fifth ave — + spent the eveg until [illegible] 10 20 — Then to H + S. + to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 28, 1875

Bright, pleasant day — got to studio at 9.30 after going to Peggy Murphy's + Burgraf's — Set palette + commenced painting on × "The High Sierras from Cloud's Rest" — a bold work undertaken with a view of keeping me up to the "level among the High Sierras" which I wish to go at + finish. To dinner at 12.50 — back at 2 Painted all the P.M. until after 5 — then could see no longer. For a short time on the lounge — then to tea at 6.30 — Eveg until after 10 listening to Dinky [illegible] play on the piano — To bed at 11.
