### November, Saturday 20, 1875

Clear, pleasant m't, afternoon
cloudy. Got to studio at abt
9 — set palette & commenced
on 12×22 a study in color
of "Horse Race in the Yosemite"
I passed all the morning
accomplishing but little.
Abt 1.30 walked up the avenue
with Miss Bisdell. Dinner
at 1.45 — back at 2.30 — Then
Colman came in & made
a dashing little sketch for
me, giving me some new
ideas & I went on much
encouraged. To tea at 6.30
At 8, called at Nicoll's — not
in — Then went to Century Club
& paid George's dues — then
to Munna's — not in — then
to studio — Read newspapers
& wrote until 10.15 — then
up to 41 4th St. & to bed just
about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 21, 1875

Miserable rainy morning.
Bkfst at 9 — at 10.30 went to
Dr. Hastings's & heard him
preach from 3° V, 135° Psalms.
After service, walked up to
Uncle A's — dinner at 2.30 —
abt 4, spread myself on the
lounge & slept until 6 —
then tea — after 7, Kate,
Uncle A. & I went over to
Will's — there until 9 o'clk
then walked back — perfectly
clear overhead, but excessively
damp. Walked down to 41st
