## Left Page

### July, Thursday 15, 1875

Hot day with cool breeze —
B↑↓p° at 7, 45 — attended
to some messages & got to
Studio at 9, 45 — Worked
hard & at last, got every
thing done by 1 o'clk, then
to Exp. Office & to dinner
at 1.30 — at 2.30 Rode to
Hauthues — not in — left a
note for him — did some
shopping & to Studio at 4,
at 4.30 Exp. man came &
carried off things. Then
to Hoffman Ho., had hair cut and
read Shampooed. Skew to Millers
on 6th Ave & 22nd St. Then Rode up to
Nile's — 4 et at dinner Table — Then
until near 8 — then to Charo's &
48 St. & at 9 to Gilmore's Garden —
met Fred Drulese there — at 11,
went to Studio got [illegible], gave
Mulvaney my key, bid Chard
good bye, went to 41 &c and
to bed at 11, 45.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 16, 1875

*Montrose.*

Up at 6 o'clk — Heavy Rain —
B↑↓p° at 7 — all good byes said
& away at 7.20 — made close
meets with cars & boat and
landed in Hoboken before 8.
Geo joined me in the cars,
he having came down the Riv.
in a boat last night. We got
away at 8.25 — Heavy Rain
until after we passed the
water gap. — very cool — then
— passed a dry belt — got to
Scranton before 2 — lunchi. — The
Post & two of Hand's boys came
from there with us — got to Mt.
Station at 3.34 — very hot — got
Mt. Town at 6, & Reached Tyler's
door just as a sharp shower
came up. — Bed at once at Mrs.
[illegible] — Surf about town until
near 10 — then to bed.
