## Left Page

### February, Sunday 21, 1875

Charming day — [illegible] at
9 o'clk — m'f, went as usual
to Dr. Hasting's & heard him
preach from 91st Psalm
near — After service, I
walked up 5th ave with
Charley Jessup & wife. Jo in
& D. Kelsey. After a short
time among the animals in
the Park I went over to
A.V.V.'s — dinner at 2.30.
M. Adams there — at 4.30
I lay down on the lounge
in library — Tea at 6.30 —
Minn. Raymon there.
Kate & Geo went to church
I waited until they Ret'd —
bid Kate good bye, as she
expects to go home in the
m'f. Then walked down
with Geo — & to bed at near
11 o'clk. [illegible — ~4 words]

## Right Page

### February, Monday 22, 1875

Very fine day. Did not get
to the studio until near 10
o'clk. Went to Kneedler's to
buy some material & had a
couple of business notes to write
so that I accomplished very
little during the m'f. Johnston
of the mail came in just as
I was preparing to go to dinner
To dinner at 1.15 — back at
2.30 — Worked all the p.m.
(until near 5) on Water Color —
"Saco, near Conway" — To tea
at 7 o'clk — At 8.30 — with Geo.
called on the Ireland's. Emma
not in — went to N.A. — great
day — $362.00. In at 10.30
& to bed at 11.30 —
Kate Ret'd to P. this m'f.
