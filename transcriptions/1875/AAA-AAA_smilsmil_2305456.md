## Left Page

### July, Friday 23, 1875

Cloudy & windy. Bkfst at 7.30
To studio at 8.30, but too tired
really to work. Podged about
putting shellac varnish on the
woodwork & writing letters
to Mrs. Beach & to W. J. ready.
Dinner some after 1 — P.M.
did no better than in the
m'f. Mersey got a little
9×12 charcoaled "a view
n the Hudson from Sunny
Brook Farm". Tea at 6.
Went down town for mail
then up town & dressed & at
8.45 got to Hunt Jessup's — a
little party — there until
near 11.30 — then to bed
at 12.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 24, 1875

Clear, cool, breezy morning.
Bkfst at 7.30 — at Studio at 8.30
Ther. 62°. Started painting
m'g 9×12 "View on the Hudson
from Sunny Brook Farm" —
Dinner at [illegible] then
after that & did not get
fairly at work again until
3.30. Then painted until
6 getting the sketch into
tolerable shape, but parts
of it are discouragingly
bad. Tea at 6.15 — Went
for mail — Ret'd to studio &
washed brushes & cleaned up.
At 8.15 went to Hetty Searles
Gus. L. & Ben M. joined us
Whist until 10 — got came
in about 9 — To bed at 11.
