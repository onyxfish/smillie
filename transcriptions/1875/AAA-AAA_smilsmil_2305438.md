## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 15. 1875.

*Hill Side —*

Beautiful day — Rec'd from
NY, letters, from Fenn & from
Schenck, auctioneer, enclosing
check for $148.07 being payment
for little picture 10×14. Early during
Willie Cummins came & spent
the day, about 11 o'clk. Uncle
W. & aunt A. came — Geo. & I
went out to Hill Side with them
Dinner at 1.30. The p.m. was
spent in Roaming around —
Tea between 6 & 7 — out of doors
until 9 o'clk — To bed at O.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 16. 1875.

A beautiful day, but everything
cries for rain. B'kft soon after
7 — after g. got to work on a little
sketch (W.C.) looking out of the
woods to the "Greenhouse" — made
nothing of it & gave up at 12.
Dinner about 1 — about 4 o'clk
pitched upon position for large
drawing & commenced the
outline — about until tea time.
Some after t — about 7. Uncle W, Summa
Belle & S started for in-town to go to
Commencement Exercises of Cooke's
School. I drove down home & had
a little visit with Mother — then to
the Opera House but exercises so very
uninteresting that I went out. Went
to Sevin's & paid livery bill — back
to O.house at 9.15 — over at 10.15 —
then drove to Hill Side — Glorious
moonlight night.
