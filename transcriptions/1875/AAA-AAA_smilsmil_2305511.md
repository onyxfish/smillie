## Left Page

### November, Friday 12, 1875

Charming, mild day — do. after a light blcfo. I went around to Dr. A.P.S's — about 10.30 — then down to studio &
lighted fire — my head in such a state as to unfit me for anything. To dinner abt. 15 — Kate came in at 2 — I gave her my Phil. ticket & she went to the Rehearsal. I started out & went first to Whittredge's 51 W. 10" — Saw him on Centennial matters — then to Y.M.C.A. & called on Bellows — same business — then to studio and fixed fire & at 5, in 41 sts 8.
Had my throat & chest rubbed with camphorated oil — Tea at 6.30 — Swlf on sofa — head in much pain — also throat — again rubbed at 9.30 & at 10, to bed — Had a good night's rest.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 13, 1875

Very mild, pleasant mg. — overcast afternoon. Gahnlp feeling very bad in the olspen story. It was 10.30 before I could go around to the D's. Then I Rode down to Dewey's — walked up to Studio — to dinner abt. 15 — back at 2 — but spent rest of the p.m. on sofa. To tea before 6 — Swlf at 8 at the Academy of Music — 1st Phil. Concert. A poor affair and very thinly attended. Wachtel did not sing. After the concert walked a couple of blocks with Leila Martens & her father. Mary & Florence J. — at Phil. Chant & Kate at Thomas's — came in to Phil. & heard the close of the "Eroica" — To bed at 11.
