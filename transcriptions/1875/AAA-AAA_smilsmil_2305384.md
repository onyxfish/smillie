## Left Page

### March, Monday 1, 1875

A most horrible day of snow followed by sleet & Rain —
got to studio at about 9.30. Miss Jacobs came soon after and
most of my mttg was spent with her. To dinner at 1 —
back at 2.15 but did not accomplish much in the
way of work as I feel fagged. I got at arranging notices
of exhibition etc. — Ape. and I took tea with Misses Woodward & Gilbert at 8 o'clk.
I worked at my pasting until 10 — Then to 44th
St. & to bed at 11 —
Mr. Wm. Kendall & Griscored
of Fifth Ave Hotel called in
the mttg. Mr. K. took W.C.
drawing.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 2, 1875

Overcast mttg but cleared very bright & mild — Slush Reigns —
got to studio at 9.30 — Misses Abbatt, Jacobs & Cornwell came
all the mttg I gave to them.
To dinner at 1.20 — Played out — went back to shop
at 3 — Rec'd check from Mr.
Kendall for $100 — Could
not work — at 4 went to
N.A. Nearly all the pictures
down — At 5.30 back to shop
on lounge until 6.30 — To
tea at 7 — Every did not
go out but to bed soon
after 10.
