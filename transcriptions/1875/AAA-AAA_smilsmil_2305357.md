## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 6, 1875

Splendid day — after going up to Burggrafs, got to Studio at 9 o'clk. Commenced a new drawing in Water color, trying to loosen out my style going in composition & first washes on the sky before I went to dinner at 12.30 — Back at 1.45 & worked as long as I could see. I think there is promise in my days work. Snedecor came in at 4.30 — Nicolle came later & stayed until 6 — tea at 6.45 — I made an effort to get ready & go out & make some calls, but the whole evening was spent with Daving & his piano. — to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 7, 1875

A horrible day of driving wind and rain — Head very much out of sorts — but got to studio before 9 o'clk & to work on my new drawing 10×16 — I worked hard all day, but I'm afraid that there is some radical defect in my intellect or temperament, for while I see how very poor my work is, I cannot by any effort seem to do better. Went to dinner at 12.30 — back at 1.45 & was able to keep up as long as I could see — then gave up, rolled on my back on the lounge & stayed there until near 9 o'clk — Then up to 41st & — & to bed, my head aching very badly — [illegible — ~2 words] [illegible — ~4 words]
