## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 21, 1875

Cold, cloudy, windy day — I am beginning to think that all days are windy on my side hill. Got up a new, big stove in place of the little Open Franklin and by 10 o'clk. had a roaring fire in it — then seated myself to begin W.C. drawing 14×25. "Cathedral Pines" for Enoch Lewis — same as the 23×44 that I have just painted in. At 12.30, down town to buy Turb's & coal — Josie B. went home to dinner at 1.15 — at 2, at studio worked on outline all P.M. — W.J.T. paid me a visit — Tea at 6. At 7.30 called on the Chandlers — at 8.30 on Miss Cheshire at 9 at Fan's & with her to Allie Stroud[?] then to Gus Seymours — also, back to Fan's & at 10.30, to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 22, 1875

Cloudy mrf, with light snow squall at 8.30 went to Fan's, who was ready to start for Elisha's, but did not think it prudent. Ben & me said "o" to at 9.50 we started with Ben's horse & buggy well swaddled in clothes & robes & rugs. Enjoyed the ride & got to Elisha's at 12.05 — walked in the fields with him for an hour or so. Dinner about 2 o'clk and at 3, E. & I walked across lots to [illegible] Carmult's — Charlotte only at home — visited with her until 4.15 then back to E's — & at 5.10 got under way for home which we reached at 7.40 — very dark but of course a pleasant ride — Then an oyster supper — Geo. came in Fan went to bed with a headache. Bent & I sat by fire until 9.15 — then up on the hill & to Studio & fixed fire. To bed soon after 10 — Heavy frost.
