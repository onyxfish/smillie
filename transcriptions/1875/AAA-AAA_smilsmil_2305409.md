## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 20, 1875

Clear, cold, blustering & dirty.
Got to studio at 9.30. Misses
J. & C. soon followed and I
gave the entire m'f. to them.
To dinner at 1.10 — back at
2.15 and all the afternoon
worked on a little watercolor
that I began for Miss C.
to copy — to tea at 7. &
at 7.45. back to studio
where was held a Board
meeting of W. C. Soc'y.
Out at 10 — glorious moon-
light night — in at 10 +
& to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 21, 1875

Another clear & benny day, but
not so cold. Got to studio at
9.30 and right to work finish-
ing Chapman's "In the Studio"
to dinner at 1 — back at
2.20 and at 5 I had the
Chapman where I could
call it done. Then went
down to Brooks Bros. and
bought a p'r. of pantaloons.
Then to Deveny's & p'd. bill —
then to Leavitt's & saw Chapman
& at 6.40 back to studio —
got to tea at 7.20. Even'g
went up to Burgruffs — wrote
a letter home — washed up
my brushes & got to bed
at about 10.45.
