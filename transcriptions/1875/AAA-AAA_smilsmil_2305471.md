## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 24, 1875

Cold mrf. & not very promising — Up late & blefs — near 8 —
feeling slim — Got up to shop
about 8.30 & kindled fire —
W.J.T. & Prof. came in —
then Kate Riley & Mr. Pictur
— Got Kate started on a study
out of doors, then to my own
work — Began to Repaint
on Hudson R. — from back of
Peekskill — worked until 12.
Then our boarding house family
called — To dinner at 1 —
Josie B — there — back at 3 —
worked until 4.30 — then the
Biddles, Rileys — Sue N. & Mrs.
Ike P. called — all to see Bridal
V.F. — To tea at 6.30 — back &
washed up — more until 8.30 —
then down the hill — at 9
went in to Fan's — Vic D. &
Josie B — there — Whist until
10 — then up the hill & to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 25, 1875

Charming day — but I did not
feel like it — Got to shop at 8.30
— D. Taylor & Mrs. T — called & saw
my picture. Then I hoped it but
after that, Mrs. Jessup & others called
Mrs. Comstock, Mrs. Dan. Sayre, &c —
made letters to Wright & Clare & at
12.30 went down town & mailed
them — Dinner at 1 — Bothered
with a headache. Back to shop
at 2 & pressed to my head until
4 — then, mixed putty & color
& for an hour, filled nail holes
in the oak work — at 5 went out
& hunted up a study — Studied
by observation until 6 — then tea
— after that, down town & finally
got my box & picture [illegible] — called
at Sears's — out — then on Tweedy's
— then on Mrs. Mulford — Company
at Judge [illegible]'s — not invited —
then to Azur & with Flora & at
last, to bed at 10.40.
