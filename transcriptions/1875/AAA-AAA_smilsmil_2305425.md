## Left Page

### May, Saturday 22. 1875.

Very close & hot. Now & then looking like rain — at one time a slight sprinkle. Got to studio at 9.50 & painted all m'f on little 10×14 sketch "Early Autumn" — got it done — To dinner at 1 — at 2 went to 51 W. 10th & called on J.G. Brown, Casilear, Hubbard & Gifford. Then to Gauthier's. Then shopping at Stewart's — in at Leavitt's gallery & back to studio at 5. Fan & Hetty had been there — To tea at 7 — near 9. At Sturtevant Ho. Sam Harrington there — Hetty, Fan, Sam & I went to Thomas's garden — back to Hotel at 11.30 left them at 11.45 — To bed at 12 — [illegible] method — of all [illegible] [illegible] [illegible] [illegible] [illegible]

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 23. 1875.

Very hot — did not get up until 9.30 — Mcf[illegible] at 10.45 — wrote letters until 12.30. About 1 o'clk. went up to M.V.V.'s & had dinner abt 2.30 — Between 4 & 5.30 had a nap. At 6. went up to Mr. Jessup's — there (at 78th & Madison Ave.) for a short time, then across to Wills 61th St. & 9th Ave. Bid Mary goodbye & at 8.10 went down to Sturtevant Ho. Took Fan up to see Maud D — 10 W. 57th St — there until near 10 — then [illegible] to hotel — Bid Fan goodbye at 10.30 (she goes home tomorrow) & then I went to bed but got no sleep all night — owing to heat & noise in the street.
