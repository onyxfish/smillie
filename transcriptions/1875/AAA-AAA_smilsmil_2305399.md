## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 31, 1875

Mild & overcast all day — got to studio at 9.30 & commenced on a W.C. drawing 9½ × 13¾ — "The Nellis walt" — a bit in Uncle W's place at Hillside somewhat composed. At it until 12.45 then to dinner — back at 2.15 and worked until nearly 6 o'clc., getting most of my washes in — but all day I fought through a stupifying & depressing confusion in my head. Chase came in & at 6.30 I walked up with him & Geo. Tea at 7. Svelf, on sofa for an hour, then to Burg raff's — thru wrote a note to Geo. Whitney. To bed at 10.30.

Allie Diven died today at Fordlyon — Colorado.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 1, 1875

Mild & overcast, but cleared before noon — Misses Abbatt & Conwell took lessons in the m'f. I devoting my entire time to them. To dinner at 1 o'clc. & back at 2.15 — then to work on W.C. "The Nellis walk" and worked until 6 in spite of a stupid head to which was added a sneezing cold. To tea at 7 — after that, dressed, & with Geo. went to F.H. Smith's — a Bohemian shindy. A very good lot of fellows and a jolly time but I was not in condition, so I cleared out soon after 11 — to bed at 11.30.

Rec'd note enclosing $225 — from Sales in Brooklyn —
