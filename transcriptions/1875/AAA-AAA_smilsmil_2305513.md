## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 16, 1875

Overcast with rain during the mng. Up before 7 — hlf p. at
7.30 & at 8.30 at J. N. Smiths — m
went together to foot of Des[illegible]
D. & took 9.30 train for Phil'a —
got there about 12.30. Mr. Sartain
met us at depot — Had dinner
in depot Restaurant — then took
cars for Centennial Bldgs. Spent
the rest of p. m. there wandering
all over — no rain — but mud
ankle deep. Saw plans of bldgs
& agreed upon a gallery — every-
thing lovely. Weather cleared
magnificently before sunset —
Up 5.30 I left Messrs Sartain
& Smith at West Phil'a — left at &
went to Mr. G. O. Whitney's —
was very warmly Rec'd — and
invited to stay all night,
which I did — Tea at 6.30
every, chatting until 10,
then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 17, 1875

Clear, crisp breezy day. Up
at 7 — hlf p. at 8 — & about 9
Mr. W. & I went to his office —
then I looked at some new
pictures, then went to Penn'a
Academy of Fine Arts — all
over that — then to new bldg.
in Penn Sq. then to Merch. News.
15th S. & Market — then to Sned's
sale in Chestnut St & then with
[illegible] to Earle's where W. Whitney
met me. Went with him and
[illegible] Reached his home for
dinner at 2.30 — Dinner at 3
& at 4.45, good byes — then in
Mr. W.'s carriage, rode up to the
Devins's & called on them for
an hour — then to W. P. depot —
took tea & sandwich — then
to 3.5 Train — New York at 9.5
and to bed at 10.30.
