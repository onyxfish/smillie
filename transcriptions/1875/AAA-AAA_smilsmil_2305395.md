## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 23, 1875

Superbly clean & quite cold. About 9.30 called at the Bonuswick H° & saw Dr. Dimock wife & daughter, also Col. Loonic. Wife & son. About 10.30 went to barbers & had my hair cut— got to Studio at 11.30 — to dinner at 42. #5 1.30 having met Mr. Jas. M. Hart on our way out. Back at 2.45 and until 6 I was engaged in writing Annual Report. Then fixed up Room — to tea at 7 — back at 7.45 — Annual meeting & Am. Socy of P. in Water Colors — great crowd out — very good humored — I was Re-elected Pres. — 3° term — unanimously. Meeting adjourned at 10.15 — after 11 before all got away — then to bed at 11.45.
Anna Cook & Miss Howe called.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 24, 1875

The sloppiest sort of a sloppy day — Rain, wet snow, slosh — got to studio shortly after 9. F.H. Smith came in — then Walter Brown — the result was that the morning slipped away and no work was done. Went up to dinner at — back at 2.20 — a little fussing about, as I felt tired & used up. Mr. Geo. Whitney surprised me between 3 & 4 o'clk. After a long & pleasant visit, got & I took tea with him at 6 — at the Albemarle — 14 & 7. He walked down to Avery's with him & then to the Sommerille Gallery. I waited until 8.40 the Goudy auction under way — then to Studio. 10.41 & S. at 10.15 + right to bed.
