## Left Page

### December, Friday 24, 1875

Overcast & Raw. Did not get to studio until 9.40 — then to work on big drawing on foreground. About 1.20 met into Miss W's Room — lunched with her & Miss Jacobs. Snow began to fall. Worked until 3 —) has gathered my duds, met [illegible] 41 [illegible] & got arctics & heavy gloves — then to Grand Central Depot. Met Charl there — & took special train (3.55) to Po'— where we arr'd at 6.08. Went right to Terrace & found all in usual health. Geo. looks bad enough but not so bad as I had feared to find him. Tea about 7. Eveng. spent in general chat & got to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 25, 1875

Dull, mild day — B'kfs'. at a little after 8. With Geo until almost 11. Soon after, went out to walk — went over "big hills.", past the "old farm" to the South Road, then into Kingston woods — wandered there, then home at 12.30. Mary & Willie came from W'l. before 1. Dinner about 1.30. The Parkers & folks from Hill Side arr'd — soon after the Christmas tree was illuminated about 4.30 & abt. 30, 18 took tea. The eveng was spent singing until after 9 — when the Parkers left & [illegible] when the Hill Siders got away. It was a little after 11 when I got to bed.
