## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 28. 1875

Miserable day — Up late &
got to studio at 9.30 — worked
on distance back of middle
group of figures, making it
very much stronger — Sleet
& Rain with wind — To
dinner at 1. o'clk — back
at 2.15 P.M. worked on
large L.h. figure — Could
not see until 4.30 — Mr.
Abbatt came in. To tea at
6.30 — back to studio at 8 —
for a short time in Mr. Bees'
Room — Then made paste
& mounted drawing paper
for Miss Jacobs — then some
work for myself & fixed geo's
drawing for Mr. Whitnew —
11 o'clk when I finished —
then to 41 [illegible]; & to bed ab
11.20 .

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 29. 1875

Another bad day — Got up
with a headache against
which I fought all day —
Got to studio at 9.20 — found
a defect in the paper I
mounted for Miss J — so, had
to put another piece over it —
then to work on big drawing —
worked up left hand horse
& rider in central group —
To dinner at 1.0, back at
2.25 & worked until 4.
when I could see no longer,
gave some time to Miss Abbatt
who, with a friend called in
to see my work. Then to
[illegible] P. Co. & to Ruordle's — back
at 6 — P. tea at 6.30 — at 7
went to 300 Lexington Ave — Hom.
H.E. Howland to meet Lawrie &
try to arrange about my jury duty —
missed them — called on the Black[illegible]
in 38'S; left at 9 — Raining — left side
of my nose much swelled — to bed at 10
