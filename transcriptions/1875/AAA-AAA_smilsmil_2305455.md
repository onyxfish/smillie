## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 21, 1875

Clear, breezy [illegible]. Trunk had me out before [illegible] —
& set Brady to work putting
up my temporary piazza.
[illegible] at 7.45 — Down town
to buy nails etc — Rode up
to Fair ground with Mr. [illegible]
Post & Sue P. — At Studio until
near 1 — then to H & Tyle's.
Dinner about 1.30. Priscilla
& Dutton & Kitty Beeuty then
At 2.30 back to shop and
bossed Brady until after 5.
Then to Larthrop's & got some
more lumber. Tea at 6.15 —
Then again to shop and
wrote to Renner & S.C.T.
Soon after 10 went down to
P.O. Then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 22, 1875

Warm day & overcast. Brady
again to work for me. After
[illegible] went down town to see
why my lumber had not been
delivered. Found that it had
been left at Tyle's barn — from
there I carried it to my shop.
By working hard, Brady got
done at 12 — Dinner at 1.15 —
During the P.M. I got things
finally to rights & am now
ready to go to work. Tea
soon after 6 — then down town
very tired — went with Ben.
Mr. to Gus S. — left before 9 &
on my way up stopped at M.
[illegible]'s — Sprinkle of rain —
at [illegible]'s until 10 o'clk — then to
bed at 10.30 — Heavy Rain
during the night.
