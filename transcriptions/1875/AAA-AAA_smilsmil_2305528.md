## Left Page

### December, Thursday 16, 1875

Mild + overcast — Got to
studio's at 9.30 — Feel rather
played out — Continued
washes on sky — very few
flat or successful washes —
To dinner at 1.15 — After
that, went to Robbins in
Y.M.C.A. also saw Johnston
on fund business — Then went
to N.A. to see the donated [illegible]
Then to studio's at 3.30 + worked
for an hour. Nicoll + D'Silva
came in. The fund will not
yield — walked up with
Silva — Tea at 6.30 — Charl
came in + gave me tickets to
Glee + Madrigal concert at
Chickering's Hall — Dunlop +
I went — much pleased — To
studio's at 10 — Heard bad news
from George — Got to bed
about 11 + had a bad night.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 17, 1875

Cold + windy — Got to studio's at
9.15 — When I tried to go to work
found myself very much unstrung.
Put in some small figures on the
extreme left of my big drawing —
At 2.15 started for down-town.
Got my lunch + then went to T.
\+ Smith's — with him to Seamen's
Savings Bank + transferred W.C.
fund to him as Treas. Then in to
Charl's — then to Will's — attended
to some messages + got back to
studio at 3.30. Did a very little
on my drawing — Nicoll came in —
The Artist's Fund refuse to accommodate the W.C. Soc'ty by holding their
sale a week earlier. Silva came
in — at 6 walked up with him —
Tea at 6.30 — at 8 went [illegible]
to Mary's — Will + Minn there.
Left at 9.30 — To bed at 10.30
\+ had a fair night's Rest.
