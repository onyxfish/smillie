## Left Page

### August, Saturday 14, 1875

Close, muggy & thunderous weath. Mr. Dud. got away very early this mng. to go to Bernice to visit her husband. Bkfs. as usual & got to studio before 9 o'clk — Set palette & to work on 2°. Painting in 4" view in Hudson from Sunny Brook Farm — Dinn at 1 — back at 2 — after 3 o'clk fan M. Mrs. Morehead & Josie B. called at studio. I worked until near 5 — then washed up & oiled outside of my front door then went to my Room, bathed, dressed, & at 6:45 went to Fan M's to tea — At 8 went to A. Dartinp's with Flora called on Miss Sackett. At 9.30 Ret'd. to Fan M's had two games of whist — at 10.30 left & to bed at 11.
The Misses Butter arr'd this P.m. from Wilkesbarre.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 15, 1875

Weather still anything but pleasant — wind from east & most of time, sky overcast. Bkfs. at 8.45 — for a short time went to studio — then to Baptst Ch. Mr. C. preached a Missionary begging sermon from James V ch [illegible] 20th v. — After service walked home with Josie Bentley & sat upon piazza until 1 o'clk. Mr. Miller, Hetty's intended, there — then to dinner at 1:30 — then to studio. There until about 5 — wrote a letter to Kate — Tea at 6 — for a while on A. Dartinp's piazza — then to Presbyt Ch. & heard Dr. Taylor preach from Isaiah 32 ca. 3d v. — unrest and rest. Stopped at Jan's until after 10 — then to bed.
