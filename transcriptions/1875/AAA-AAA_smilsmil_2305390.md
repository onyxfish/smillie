## Left Page

### March, Saturday 13, 1875

Horrible day of sleet & rain —
came in the mrñ and
mizzle during the rest of
the day. Got to Studio at
9.40 — for awhile painted
in foreground objects on 12
×20 "Ausable Lake" — then
on "Sierra" foreground rod.
at 12.45. to dinner — back
at 1.15 and painted as
long as I could see, very
much bothered by the want
of light. To tea at 7 —
Snerp did some Society
writing and got to bed
shortly after 10. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 14, 1875

Miserable, raw, overcast day.
Mrs. went to Dr. Hastings's &
heard him preach from John.
13" ch. 7" v. After service, I
walked up 5th ave. with Josie
Jessup & Mr. Charlie. Then
ret? to 41st S — got rubbers
& umbrella & walked up to
A.V.V.'s. Geo. & Chave there
to dinner & tea. From 4.30
to 6.30 — napping. Tea about
7\. Norman Reale added to
the party. At 8 o'clk. Lucee
and I walked over to Will's
there until about 9.30 — then
I walked down to 44 St.
to bed about 10.45.
