## Left Page

### March, Monday 15, 1875

A horrible day of rain and mizzle. Did not get to studio until 9.45 — T.H. Smith & J.C.
Nicoll cons ummed some funny time. Stretched "Icemas" on new stretcher — scraped it
down & oiled out sky & distance. So dark a day that I would not paint on it. To dinner at 12.45 — Harry Dining very sick — a great scare. Back at 1.45. Painted a little sketch for Mrs. Alden for Home of artists fair Album. To tea at 7 — Uncle Willie at the house. Everg — Prof Smith called. I wrote from 9.30 until 10.30 then to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 16, 1875

Dull foggy morf. and I got up with a head to match. Uncle W. after sleeping in the chair, took [illegible] with us — at 9 he walked down to studio with me. I was unable to work. To dinner at 12.30 — after that, to bed. Did not get up until 6. Then walked down to studio & fixed fine. To tea at 7 — weather, mild & clear. Harry, less feverish but not any better. Everg I wrote 4 or 5 notes & got to bed about 11 o'clk. [illegible — entire passage]
