## Left Page

### October, Friday 15, 1875

Overcast, mild morn. Thought that surely our bad weather had come at last but it cleared charmingly before noon — Blf. very late — after 8 — got to studio about 9 and to work on W.C. drawing "Cathedral Spires" — at it until 12.45 — then to dinner — got to studio at 2.30 — Strange at work on my blind door — Jerry came up to tell me that Kane's nose was at the blacksmiths. I went right down & brought him up, with a little larkies to hold him — worked from 3 until 5.30 like fun — then very much tired out — tea at 6 — at 7 at studio at 7.30 went to Fairs & with her, Emma Smith & B. m. went to Court House & heard Anna Dickinson give her lecture "Joan of Arc" — was very much pleased. also went to Mulfords here until 11 — then to bed on the hill about 11.30. Rain.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 16, 1875

Horrible day — Rain & wind all day until about 5 P.M. when the rain changed to snow. Got to studio at abt 8.45 — set palette to work on studies of horses, putting in backgrounds and finishing bits that in my hurry I had left undone. Dinner abt — back at 2 and worked until about 4.45 when I could see no longer. Sprunt came to see me about setting out trees — then M. came to invite me to Mary M's to tea — there at 6.30 Mrs. Wm. Jessups, Fau. Mima S, Ben, Frank Bentley the guests — at 8 went down to the P.O. the storm around sight — Again to M's & took the girls down and then received invitation to stay all night. After 11 before I got to bed.
