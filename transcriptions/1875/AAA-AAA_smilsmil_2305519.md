## Left Page

### November, Sunday 28, 1875

Weather very much mixed but generally disagreeable feeling very much out of & at &
I made up my mind to see some Doctor so, after bfst at 9.
I went to Dr. Yale's in 49 "S" —
but he was not in — Then to Chas's
in 51 "S" — then to D. Hastings's &
heard him preach from John
3° ch. 4° v. After service went
to Mary's — then to A.V.V.'s. took
dinner there — P.m. walked in
the Park — took tea at Wills
& at 7.30 went to Mr. Bishop's.
10 W. 57. & spent the evg
with Maud D. & May J.
to 41 [illegible]; at 10 — to bed at 0.30

In the mfg lost Phil. ticket
& at night, before I discovered
the loss they were ret'd with
card of Mrs. J.J. Oakley, 59
W. 57° S. I wrote a little
note of thanks.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 29, 1875

Clear cold windy day. Got
to Studio at 9.25. Kindled fire
& tried to clear up a little
then got Water colors Ready
to go to work. Maud & Way
came in about 11.30. I
went to dinner at 1.15 &
back at 3 after going to
Knödlers & doing some
art shopping. Worked
on Tracing big cartoons —
R. Swain Gifford came in
late. At 6.30 to tea and
at 8 back again. Until
10.30 writing letters. Then
to 41 [illegible] & to bed at 11.
High wind & very cold.
