## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 15, 1875

Mild overcast mng. Got up to Studio's at 8.30 — Josie B. came up on horseback & I got a little sketch of her. Then to work on 23×44 until 1 o'clk. when I went to dinner. Back at 2. & Painted until 4. then to stable & saddled Go's horse — swept out — met Jos. Benty & Rode Round the Lake with her — had a very good ride — slight spatter of Rain — In at 5.45 — a bath and scrub then dressed for the evng & tea at 6.30 — Down town to P.O. & at about 8 went to Wm. Jessup by invitation. Found them at [illegible] — so took another one. A [illegible] time until after 11 — to bed about midnight — very tired.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 16, 1875

Dismal day — Rain commencing about noon — Up late — did not get bk fo: until 9 o'clk — then to studio & to work on big Tree on 23×44 — until 12 — then to the house to bid good byes the Jewells heads. Fanny Payson, Hetty Biddle, Mary Salesbury & others leaving — Mr. Gillman & family left in the mng. Dinner & back at 1 o'clk. Painted until 5.30 — then, as I could see no longer I cleaned up, went to the house, dressed up & at 7 at Fam M's — Ike P. & wife. Hy Syer[?] & wife, Fanny Dinker[?], Miss Gouel & Gus S. There to tea. Left there soon after 10 & to bed about 10.45. [illegible — ~3 words]
