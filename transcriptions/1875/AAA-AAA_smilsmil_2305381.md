## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 23, 1875

Mild, pleasant day, but
showing a disposition to thicken
up & be unpleasant. Got
to studio at 9.20 afin going
over to N.A. Miss Jacobs came
for her lesson but Miss Abbatt
was sick. Spent the rest with
Miss J. working on "Saco" no. 2.
Miss Conwell called, but did
not take a lesson. To dinner
at 1. Back at 2 ¼ — all the
P.M. on Saco. To tea at
7. At 8.30 called on Miss
Burdell. At 9.30 at N.A.
In at 10.30 & to bed at
11.15.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 24, 1875

Dense fog, mild & threatening
rain all day. Got to studio from
afting and to work on "Saco"
no. 2. At 12.30, to lunch, back
at 2. About 3 Mr. Merch. Lewis's
came in & invited Gel. & me to
dine with him & Mr. Gel. Whitney
at the Albemarle at 6. P.M.
Will Shearman called and
we talked Mormon etc. Miss
Conwell came to say that she
would commence lessons with
me to-morrow. Att. went over
to the Hotel, met Messrs. Whitney,
Lewis & Avery. Had a very
pleasant time & at 8 went to
the N.A., all together. About
9.30 W.T. Richards joined us
there. Parted at 10.30,
to bed at 11.30.
