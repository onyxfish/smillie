## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 14, 1875

Beautiful bright day — got to
studio at 9 & to work on 10×16
"On the Paco" — So dinner at 1.20
— back at 2.20 and painted
until 6.30 — cleaned up and
to tea at 7.15. Wille called
at studio about 5 o'clk. and
complained of the marine
drawing I have just done
for the A.B.N. Co — thinks it smoky.
At 8.30 went into Remairs — there
met the father & brother of C.S. Coleman
abq. Went down to Chapman's
Very fine old Views. Found one of
very old pictures there all painted
over & changed in composition. I
protested to C. & he offered me
$100. to publish in shape at once.
Met Camls & had a long talk
straightening out old misunder-
standings. A little supper
& champagne, but I did not
partake. About 11 when I left.
Walked up & to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 15, 1875

Pleasant day — Hardly able to
get up. Had my bkfst. about
9 & did not get to studio until
10.20 — Misses Jacobs & Conwell
there — I begged off & they went
away. I then worked on the
A.B.N. Co. marine until
near 1 o'clk. when Fred. Smillie
came for it. Then I gave up
& went right to my bed.
Got up again at 7 & took
tea — "Bill Earle" there —
at 9 I walked down to the
studio — fixed fire — back
at 10.30 and to bed at 11.30
