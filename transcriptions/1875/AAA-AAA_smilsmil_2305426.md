## Left Page

### May, Monday 24, 1875

*at Mr. Geo. Whitney's, Logan Square — Philadelphia.*

All the hot mud — spent [illegible] doing little messages — went to Studio at 10 — made some notes — went to N. A. & to Kuntry's & there to 44 [illegible] at 12 — [illegible] and — from [illegible] soon at [illegible] Dinner at 12.30 — at 2 we left the house — got to foot of [illegible] St. at 2.40 — off on 3 o'clk train for Phila. where we are — at 6 — got to W. W.'s at 6.25 and had tea at 7 — an evening of pleasaunt chatting in [illegible] — also a call from Mr. & Miss [illegible] & then to bed at 10.30 —

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 25, 1875

Up soon after 7. Rain falling steadily — Bkfst. at 8 — W. W. spent a short time with us among the pictures — at 9.30, left to ourselves — feeling very stupid & stretched myself on the bed & slept until 12 — Dinner at 3 o'clk. Soon after 4 got & went to Miss Gratz's. 245 S. 18th St — there until after 5 — then to Misses Boggs — there until after 6 — then to W. W.'s at 6.40 — tea at once — Quite a very pleasaunt little company that did not break up until near midnight — to bed at 12.
