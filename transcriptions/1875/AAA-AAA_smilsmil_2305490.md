## Left Page

### October, Friday 1, 1875

Horrible day of driving wind & rain. Up soon after 7. Mdlf. at 8. All the mrf. about with carpenters & running messages up and down town. Dinner at 1. After dinner the carpenters did not work. I went to Foster's Fannery & got a calf skin — then to studio & stretched it over stretchtolps — also did some varnishing about the wood work of my Room. Tea abt. 5 — at 7 down town to P.O. — then to studio & wrote.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 2, 1875

Cold, breezy day — another mrf. lost bothering with Brady & Johnston, superintending little odds & ends of work. Dinner at 1 — much of the p.m. went as did the mrf., but at last I got to work with charcoal on the big Race drawing & worked until 6, when I could see no longer — then to tea — again to studio — found that I could arrange student lamps so that I could see to work on my drawing & so I worked until near 9 — then down to P.O. & abt 15 to Gus Seymours — there for a few minutes — to bed abt. 10.30.

Rec'd. pkg. from Charl with English papers & letters.
