## Left Page

### July, Thursday 29, 1875

Cold, Rainy day — no use to
try to work in studio without
a fire — After b'kfst — went down
to Sayre's — Picked out an
open stove — 2ⁿᵈ hand stove
pipes & two men. Got all up
to studio; then set stove and
rigged the pipe outside of the
house, abt 6 o'clk. Started fire,
then to dinner. Afternoon
I started charcoaling on
22×44" Cathedral Spires —
"Yosemite" (Same subject as Mr.
Enoch Lewis's Water color.) — In
at 6. Eveg. Geo. & I called
on Fanny Jessup — then to Mrs.
DeWitt's — there met Dutton &
Mr. Mr. Mulford — went home abt
stumnt abt 9 o'clk. To Mrs. Tyler's.
Before we went up to bed, a
short visit in the parlor, as
Mrs. Dyer leaves for home
early to-morrow mng. Then
to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 30, 1875

Did not get out of bed until
9 o'clk. — miserable head ache.
Then went to Mrs. Dud's & had
tea & boiled Rice — From 4 until
6 I was rolled in my blanket,
stretched in my chair under
the maples back of studio —
The air was delicious and I
found sweetest refreshment.
Tea at 6. Eveg. I went
with Geo. & called on the
Biddles — then to the Beutly's.
Home at 10 o'clk — sat and
read Tribune until after
11 — then to bed.
