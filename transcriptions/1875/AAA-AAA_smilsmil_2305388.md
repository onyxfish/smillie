## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 9, 1875

Bright mrg. but day overcast. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on Sierras — Painted over middle distance. To dinner at 1 o'clk — back at 2.15 — Painted as long as I could see getting over the sun-lighted part of mts. De-fale & Huntleff came in to tea at 7 — Lucy did some writing & got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 10, 1875

Pleasant day — Got to studio about 9.30 and to work on "Sierras". Painted over the shadow mts. in middle-distance but did not get the color at all what I wanted. To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2 and painted as long as I could see — To tea at 7. Lucy & I spent over W.S. catalogue classifying pictures & painting for my Report. To bed at 10.30.
