## Left Page

### January, Sunday 10, 1875

Extremely cold day — went ment to Dr. Hastings & heard him preach a sermon upon the proper way of Reading the Bible — Text. 15th Ch. 4th V. After service walked up to Uncle A's & took dinner with father & Mother. At 4 o'clk. went over to Will's — back to A.V.'s at 6 — tea at 6.30 & chatting with father & mother until 10 o'clk. Walked down with Geo. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 11, 1875

Still very cold but moderate up at night. Got to Studio's at 9.20 — so cold that I could not get to work until 11 o'clk — then at 10×15 sketch and worked until 12.30 — then to dinner — back at 2 o'clk. Went with Ferguson to Mrs. Bridge's in 22nd St. and saw some water color drawings that she will loan to our exhibition — back to Studio's at 2.30 & worked until after 4, getting my drawing pretty near done — to tea at 6.30 — at 8 called on W. Smith 23 W 47. Saw Mr. S. & water Colors. — then to Mr. Haines 54 W. 57 — He was very chatty — took me over his house & promised 3 drawings for our exhibition. At 9.30 at A.V.'s — there until 10 — bid father & mother good bye — Geo. & I walked to 41st St. — a little writing & to bed at 11.15
