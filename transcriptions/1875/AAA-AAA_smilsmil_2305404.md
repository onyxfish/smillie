## Left Page

### April, Saturday 10, 1875

Charming Spring day — Got
to studio at 9.30 & worked on
a steamer drawing for the a.
B.N. Co — To dinner at 1 —
back at 2.15 & worked on
drawing until 5.30 — Misses
Beach — foote & Many Tweedy
called — worked at my clot[hes]
until after 7 — To tea at 7.30
We th. shortly after 9. with
geo went to Misses [illegible] my-
in 44th St. a pleasant little
company — Spent an hour
chatting with Herman White's
daughters — then back and
to bed at 11.30 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 11, 1875

A charming day out of
doors but I was in bed
all day with a vile head
ache — Did not get up
until near 9 o'clk. I
sat about until 11.30 and
then again to bed, but
got little sleep & rested
very badly.
