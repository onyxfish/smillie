## Left Page

### November, Monday 22, 1875

Bright, cold, winter-like day — got to Studio at 9.15 Kindled fire & then to work dashing the little group of racing horses on 10×17 sketch. Colman does not like it — advises different treatment of sky — at 12.30 went to Knoedler's — then in to see Snedecor's new gallery, & to dinner at 1.20 — back at 2.30 & started a 3° sketch for my "Race" 9½×18 in — Rushed it all in but the group of figures & Colman says I have hit it at last. To tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to Mary's — Matt there. Char came in & at 10.30 I walked up to 57°. S. with him — then to 41°. J. & to bed about 1 — Had a nervous, half-awake night-mare for an hour or two before I got to sleep.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 23, 1875

Miserable Rain storm all day — got up feeling out of sorts — Did not get to Studio until nearly 9.30 — Messrs Geo. Whitney — Murch Lewis & Mr. S. Richards came in at 10 & stayed for an hour. Then I set to work & tried to paint on group in 9½×18. To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.30 — Very dark at afternoon & very little done. To tea at 6.30 — at 7.45 at Studio Bldg. 51 W. 10°. S. & all meeting of A.S. of P. in W.C. In spite of the storm a very full one & much good feeling concerning Centennial matters. Adjourned at 10.30 — walked up and stopped at Studio — piped for then to 41°. S. & to bed about 11.40. Still raining. 05.01
