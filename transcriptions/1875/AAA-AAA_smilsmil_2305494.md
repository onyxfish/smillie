## Left Page

### October, Saturday 9, 1875

Wonderful day — of course it is a "weather breeder" — The sky & foliage color marvelous.
After [illegible] — detained a long time with [illegible] Tyler, trying to come to some sort of arrangement as to value of an acre of land that I want. Saunders started putting up my fence.
[illegible] — I got started on my Cathedral Spire drawing.
Dinner at 1 — Back at 2 & at my work with many interruptions — I cared not been to stay in doors such a lovely day. Aunt Priscilla came up & invited us to tea to-night — worked until near 6 — then to Fan's — There until after 10.
Then [illegible] & I went with [illegible] P. to Mr. [illegible] Bentley's — then up to bed just before 11.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 10, 1875

Miserable, overcast day — a tumble contrast to yesterday.
[illegible] — at 8.30 — then to studio —
Feeling a little out of sorts, I sat in easy chair until after 11 — then wrote a letter to Mrs. [illegible] Whitney — worked awhile outside & then to dinner at 2 — then Rained — At 3 back to studio — Wrote letters to Father & Mr. [illegible] Whitney.
Tea at 6 — went for a few moments into A. Harknolp's — then down town — heavy Rain — instead of going to church as at first I intended, I went in to Fan's & did not leave there until 10 o'clk — then up the hill & to bed.
