## Left Page

### October, Thursday 21, 1875

Up soon after 6 & at trunk
called about 7 to see Fred —
Shipman who ached work on
the ploughing of my lot. Up
at 8 — all the mf on a great
drive, winding up matters —
Langdon at work, finishing from
Stone. Taken down & [illegible] packed
Trunk packed & at noon down
the hill to Hetty S's with her
little painting — Dinner at 1 —
a few goodbyes & at 1.30 rode
over to depot — into Ben — Jan
& Emily Smiths — (New Milford) —
Train at 3.40 — to Binghampton
about 4.45 — went right to
Miss S's home — Tea about 6 —
Ew[illegible], some neighbors came
in & we had whist until about
10 — At 10.30 I bid the ladies
(Fan & Miss S) goodbye & all
started for N.Y. Went right
to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 22, 1875

Got in to Jersey City about 7.15
and 8 when I reached Devine's.
Had a good blf[illegible] — Father came
in soon after — my trunk came
& I unpacked it — About 10.30
I went around to Mam's. Found
mother there — then down to
studio stopping to see D. Mulford —
Saw Saml. Coleman & the girls of
212 — After 1 again in 41st St. Then
unpacked trunk — At 2.30 went
to Mam's. 105 W. 44th. Got a cup
of tea — a little [illegible], met
Father in H.R.R.R. cars & went up
to [illegible] — Ella Beach in seat back
of us — Got to Eastman Terrace
about 7 — had tea — very tired
& to bed at 9. The twilight
ride through the Hudson
Highlands, very delicious.
