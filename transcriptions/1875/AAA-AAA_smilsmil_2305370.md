## Left Page

### February, Monday 1. 1875.

Glorious day — B[illegible] at
8 — I felt rather groggy
after yesterdays experience
but got to studio about 9.30
mote a couple of letters —
At 11 went to N.A. from thun
down to 12 S[t] & B[roa]d way to
Devry & Conger, sign framers
& ordered some signs for
W.C. then to Magrath's &
Silva's — then to Snedecor's
then to dinner at 1.30 — got
to studio at 2.30 & began
to lay out things for my
Academy picture — in
to tea at 6.30 — mote a
letter to Father — at 8.30.
at Geo. Hearn's — at 9 — at
the N.A. sold my other
two pictures there — Hurrah
for me — in at 10.35 — to
bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 2. 1875.

Overcast & raw — bad underfoot,
well clear. B[illegible] at 8 — Studio
at 9 — Miss N. at 9.30 — I spent the
m[orn]g starting a drawing with [her],
she coloring as I went on. — Miss
Burdell called at 12 & went with
me to the N.A. we walked together
to 41[st] St. Dinner at 1 — back to
Studio at 2 & all the P.M. — I
worked on the drawing commenced
in the m[orn]t. "A view on the Saco —
near Conway, N.H." At 5.30 I went
to Devry's & ordered blk pantaloons.
then walked over to 94 2[n]d ave,
& left little picture for O'Kelly —
then up to studio & to tea at 7 —
At 8.30 called on Miss Burdell —
at 9.10 at N.A. — met Geo. — at
10, stopped at studio — for a
few minutes in Miss Freeborn's
room — to bed at 11.15 —
