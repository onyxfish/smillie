## Left Page

### November, Saturday 20th, 1875

Still overcast. At 9 o'clk. went to Mary's for Father — we walked down to studio where he looked over my work until 10 o'clk. Then set to work & dashed in my groups of horses in 12×22 sketch — then began another sketch on another scheme — that is, without the Yosemite background. 10×17 — To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30 Mrs. Noble — Bryan Smith — Husted — Fathers called — so that I did not get sketch finished — At 5.30 must down to Nicoll's studio — To Tea at 8 — at 8.30 at Mama J's — paid for Thomas ticket — at 9.15 at studio — wrote until 10.30 — Then to 41 [illegible] & Right to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 19, 1875

Overcast, but not unpleasant day. Got to studio at 9 o'clk. Set palette & to work on sketch of Horse Racing, 12×22 — went to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked as long as I could see. I think I can manage the ideas I have, but Colman insists that I must give up the attempt to make a Yosemite background to my groups of figures — I am in some trouble. Nicoll came in & stayed until after 6. To tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to Mary's — Father there — at 9 Father, D. & I went up to Charl's — there until 10.15 — then back — I got to bed at 11 — [illegible — ~4 words] at [illegible — ~3 words] — asked a [illegible] — [illegible] then home — [illegible] bed at [illegible] 1.45 at [illegible] — [illegible] 15 [illegible]
