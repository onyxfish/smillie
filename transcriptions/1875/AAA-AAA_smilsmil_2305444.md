## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 29, 1875

Overcast and comparatively cool. Got out my work in the mɣ. to commence a drawing for the A.B.N. Co — "Bacchus in a Basket" — but did very little — dinner about 1, the afternoon was spent to as little purpose as the mɣ. About 5 o'clk. Mr. Loomis called. Tea at 6.30. Some short, sharp showers. Much of the day was spent "bossing" the job of making connections from gas main to street lamps. Every spent in family chat. To bed about 10 & fine rain during the night. [illegible — ~3 words] of. ## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 30, 1875

A very warm but charming day — after bkfst. went to town — had glasses mended — then on to Goup's & to Ed. P.'s mill. Home at 10.30 — but did not get to work until a little before dinner at 12.30 — After dinner, suffering from headache — found water almost out of tank — emptied & cleaned it — then took a bath & change — Ready to go to work at 4.30 but my head then like to split. Matt & Ed. to tea — made a short note to father — music in the park. Spent the twilight on the roof — glorious. Suffered from headache until went to bed at 10.40 —
