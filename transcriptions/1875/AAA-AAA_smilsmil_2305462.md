## Left Page

### August, Friday 6, 1875

Breezy day — very anxious to get out but my knee is not yet enough better to warrant the use of "Aunt Hannah's" crutches, offered to me by Moley Sayre —
Dr. Taylor & Mr. Tweedy called, otherwise my day was without incident —
very stupid — I am getting impatient. Invited to Uncle Mr. Jessup's this evef. Uncle Mr. & Aunt H. called in evening.
Heavy thunderstorm & Rain until late at night. Got to bed about 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 7, 1875

Very pleasant day. Up at 7.30 — washed & dressed — Geo. brought my "Help" — Went down to piazza for a while, then again to my room & wrote — about noon Fam M. & Mrs. Morhead (Smiley Black) called, & I went to Mr. Lathrop's to dinner — my crutches very much in my way — After dinner went down to Dan Sayre's — on piazza there until 3.30 — then back to H. Tyler's — Mrs. Tiffany, Miss Mr. & Mrs. Tweedy came and visited me until 6 — then to tea. Sue Holt — Josie B. & Hetty S. called in the evef. Read Tribune until after 9 — then to Tyler's & to bed at 10.
