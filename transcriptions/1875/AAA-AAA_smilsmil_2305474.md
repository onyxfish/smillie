## Left Page

### August, Monday 30, 1875

Another very warm day —
Bkfst. at 7.45 — feeling quite
as well as could be expected
under the circumstances.
Spent the mf overseeing
two darkies at work at my
cellar — doing a little light
work & tracing for much
Lewis's Water Color drawing
"Cathedral Spires — Yosemite
Valley". Isaac Riley called
about noon. Dinner at 1 —
back at 2.30 — a little more
work on tracing & some loaf-
ing outside, working with
the hoe & picking up stones.
Tea at 6 — down town soon after
7 — called on Isaac R. — not in —
went to P.O. then to Fan's —
there until 9.15 — then called
on the Comstocks at Dan Dayris —
Mr. & mrs. W.J.S. there — away at
9.45 & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 31, 1875

Another clear, fresh morning.
To studio about 8.30 & worked
with the hoe until 10 — then
in & to work on Tracing. By
12.15 had finished it & then, as I
felt much out of sorts, I went to
my bed until dinner at 1.15 —
To studio at 2.30 — Jessie Comstock
came up about 3 & until 5. I was
engaged painting a sketch from
her. To tea at 6 — Evef. for a
time on d. Darhrds's piazza with
a number of callers — then to Dan
Dayris & bid the Sackett's good bye
— they leave for home to-morrow mf
at 5 — then to P.O. — then to Fan's —
Stewart & Josie B. there — one game
of whist — then at 9.20 to Mrs.
Crossman's & until 10 o'clk. with
the Deal girls — then to Mr. Dud
Darhrds's & finally to bid at 10
45.
