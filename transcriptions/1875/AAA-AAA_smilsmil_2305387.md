## Left Page

### March, Sunday 7, 1875

Dull disagreeable m'f. heavy snow storm during the p.m., winding up with wind & sleet at night. Wy went to Dr. Hastings' & heard him preach a doctrinal sermon on infant baptism from Jeremiah's 30th ch. 20th v. — a very weak presentation to my mind. After service walked to 55th St. & Mad. Ave. with Mr. Jessups & Josie, thru to 7. H. Smillie's, 44th St — then walked to A.V.S.'s at 1.45 — dinner at 2 — got there. At 3.30 took a nap — at 6, tea — at 8.15 walked down to 41 [illegible] St — wrote some letters & to bed abt 11.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 8, 1875

Glorious day overhead, but a marvel of slush underfoot. Did not get to Studio until 9.45. Then set to work on distant mts in "Sierra" picture. Painted until 12.45 — did not get to lunch until 1.20 — back at 2. Painted until 4.30 — then gave up, very tired — Dr. Yale came in. At 5.30 at Deery's (Tailor) — walked up to tea at 7 — Sweetf felt too tired to go to Brooklyn Water-Color opening. So loafed & to bed at 10.30
