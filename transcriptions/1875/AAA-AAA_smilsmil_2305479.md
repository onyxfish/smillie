## Left Page

### September, Thursday 9, 1875

Very cool clear mg. After bkfp. went down town and arranged with Hugh Mitchell for a horse "Calp. Jack" — a little Canadean — Baldwin brought him up to studio & from 9.30 until 12 I painted from him — Then worked in-doors on the study until dinner time — 1 o'clk. Afternoon, much time lost with screwing up my building & getting the floor level. Worked a little on "Peekskill Hudson" painting. A little before 7 — at Gus Seymour's to tea — a few of the "young" folks — considerable romping & a little whist. Then some moonlight strolling with Fan M. & Hetty S. — To bed soon after 11. ## Right Page

### September, Friday 10, 1875

Very heavy wind & Rain — with cold wind from N.W. Got up to studio, kindled fire & fought the water that drove in under the door — thought almost the bldg would be blown off its temporary foundations. Started painting on 23×44 "The Cathedral Spires — Yosemite" — Rain ceased so that abt I went to dinner — P.M. — cold, windy & clearing. Painted on until near 6 — then dressed & went to Fan M's to tea — a real, frolicking romp — quite ashamed of myself — Got to bed at 12 o'clk — almost.
