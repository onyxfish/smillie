## Left Page

### Thur. June 4, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still pleasant but not so cool. To studio + commenced the work of putting things up for the summer. I went to Central Iron Co. to order iron grating for silt basin in Sneffeu Court. Dinner at 1 - It was after 2 when Central Iron man came + got my order with diagrams - Then I Ret. home + took the boys out with their two cameras - It was cloudy + the sunshine was fitful, but they got a few shots under my direction - very unsatisfactory, of course, because they could not see immediate results. Then to Borrmans for R-wheel but it was not yet Repaired - Then home + finished the afternoon writing letters + doing up over accounts - Tea at 6.30. Some reading to the boys - Charl came in at 8.30 but left early - at about 9 I went to Walters + saw there Mary Cook who leaves for Buffalo with her family to-morrow night — There until 10.30 - Then home.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 5, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

growing very warm + close - all day at the studio at first getting things into general order, then sorting out the things to be taken to Montrose + doing them up into pkgs. Immediately after pkg. I took Jim to Dr. Noyes to have his eyes carefully examined, as he has been complaining of them for some time past. Dr. Noyes + Hunter gave him a careful examination + then said that his eyes were entirely normal but that he gave evidences of being run down. To-day was celebrated as his birth day altho the Almanac w. not permit it before to-morrow. Dinner in the middle of the day + a celebration at night - Katie T. celebrating her birth-day too - cake with candles - ice-cream, strawberries etc. Evett. Charl came + took many + the girls to see + hear The Mikado. I struggled for awhile trying to make my check book bal. write the bank's acct + then, at 9.15 I got on my wheel + made a circuit of Central Park - getting in at 10.30 — It was a very pleasant trip. The frog music in the lakes was particularly amusing — a Rub down + to bed.
