## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 28, 1896

**Weather:** Bright & beautiful  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright & beautiful — To studio before 10, completed mezzo. gr. on 7"x9" plate then filled it in with etching gr. and smoked it black & then made upon it a red Retracing of the little oil sketch that I painted the other day. "A memory of Raquette lake". Then I commenced scraping upon it. Home to lunch at 1 — After lunch went with R. to the barber's & we had our hair cut — it was 3 o'clk. before I got to studio — I worked on mezzo. as long as I could see. Dr. Gerster came in for a few minutes, late, tired out from a severe operation. I had a short walk before dinner. Swelf.. Charl came & took the P. gives to the Olympia — a Music Hall. I wrote a couple of notes.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 29, 1896

**Weather:** Beautiful day — cloudless, brilliant & mild  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day — cloudless, brilliant & mild. To studio at 9.30 — Had the carpenter come — explained to him the drawings that I had made for a device to aid me in laying mezzo. grounds. a Table revolving on a centre set in a board, sliding in grooves, at Right angles to the face of my rocker. Then I set to work scraping on my 7" x 9" memory of Lake Raquette. until 12 — Men to Carpenter's to see that he was going on correctly — then to [illegible] to get an Int. Policy [illegible] for Farrington, [illegible] to 2. Nat. Bk. & drew a little money. Then home to lunch at 1.30 to studio at 2 & worked on mezzo. until 5 — Then walked up to 62. St. home to dinner at 6.30. I loafed through the evelf.. Charl came in.
