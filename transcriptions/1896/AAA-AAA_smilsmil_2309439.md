## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 17, 1896

**Weather:** Pleasant \+ almost uncomfortably warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

After [illegible]. I worked for some time writing a paragraph or two to be added to my Academy letter, the out-come of some of the conversation last night at N.A. council table — then sent galley proof of Report \+ letter to J. C. Beckwith. At 11 went to studio, market, \+ two or three other places \+ later met Mary at Sloan's where we selected carpet for the 3d story front — left her \+ to 2d Nat. Bk. to N.A. f. [illegible] — to C. Brien's \+ then home to lunch — to Studio at 2 \+ spent rest of afternoon over my bicycle \+ finally completed to cleaning \+ setting up \+ took it home with me at 5.30 — Ev'g. went to [illegible] Ireland's in 40th St. — out — to Century Club Peny. out — to [illegible] Fitzgerald's in 44th [illegible] — Nicole, out — to Dr. Yale's — 49th [illegible] — out. Stopped in to see the [illegible] of the new Manhattan hotel — it disappointed me — met Mallory there \+ he kept me for some time explaining some past project of a combination of N.A. \+ [illegible] theatre — Home at 10.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 18, 1896

**Weather:** Pleasant, hazy, mild  **Temp:** [illegible]

When I got to studio I found a note from Beckwith with [illegible] of my report. I liked a portion of his note so much that I at once went up to his studio \+ got his permission to embody it in my printed letter. Back soon after \+ until 1 getting things in readiness to begin real studio work. Then to lunch. After lunch a little more writing \+ then to B. Brien's — then to Arnold \+ Constable's — met Mary \+ selected a pair of curtains for library — then left her \+ went to 2 W. 18th St. to see some pieces of sculpture by Barnard — said to have made a sensation in Paris. I doubt it. Then to Champney's — 15th [illegible] \+ 5th Ave. — there until dark — then to studio — fixed his \+ home at 5.30 — Dinner. Ev'g. to Water-Color meeting — nothing of special interest — had some pleasant chats with a number of the fellows — home at about 11.
