## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 1, 1896

**Weather:** Brilliant cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant cold day — To studio at usual time — Room cold — satisfied that the heater in cellar was clogged with soot \+ dust — I went down to it \+ spent an hour clearing it out — dirty work but it bore good fruit — Then I went on with Vesuvius — painting it in in a very slight, sketchy was as a basis for a first painting proper — As far as I got my success was not very encouraging — Ralph came for me about 5 o'clk \+ walk up 5th Ave. to 50th S— Then home — Eva \+. Charl came in — after which I puzzled for an hour over my bank acct — just balanced — I was out 1.28 but discovered where the error was before I went to bed. [illegible — ~8 words]

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 2, 1896

**Weather:** Dull, cold \+ raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, cold \+ raw — So cold in studio that I could not commence painting at once. So, for a time, I was engaged in arranging some of the confusion that still exists in corners that are out of sight. Then I got to work upon my little Vesuvius painting — I do not make very satisfactory visible progress but the subject grows upon me \+ I have begun to dream of a 33×44 canvas for it — Home at 1 — after lunch to Bank \+ thro to studio — Ralph came \+ spent the afternoon with me — I painted as long as I could see — Then R \+ I had a short walk — After dinner I went to see about my fire which I had left in bad shape — it was out — \+ the weather being so cold I decided to take the trouble to kindle it, which I did — Home at 9 — but to had to go again to studio — Wrote to Lee \+ Lee concerning N.a. matters.
