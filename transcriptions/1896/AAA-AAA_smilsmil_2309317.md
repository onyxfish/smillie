## Left Page

### Wed. March 18, 1896

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — got up feeling rather stale after last night's experience — To Studio at 10 \+ painted languidly on Oct p.m. lunch — \+ back it 2 o\+ painted until 4 when I completed the repainting of "Oct. afternoon" — Home at 5 \+ had a rest until dinner time — Acquit evef. writing notes.

## Right Page

### Thur. March 19, 1896

**Weather:** Heavy Rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy Rain all day with wind — It was after 10 when I got to studio — Part of the mf was given to scraping \+ sandpapering over some old canvases — The Rest of the mf I spent in working with crayon over a photo. print from an old dag. type of Father Cook. It is for Alice Ireland who is to give it to Kathanie Adams for a Wedding present. Home to lunch — Heavy Rain \+ high wind, so that the boys could not go to dancing school — Jim complained this mf so much of shiverings, headache \+ sore throat that I allowed him to stay at home — during the day he seemed to recover rapidly — Afternoon I worked on drawings for closet finish in one of my studio closets — After 5 went to Carpenters (Millers) in 40th S. gave him drawings \+ arranged to have a man at the studio to-morrow. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Rain ceased in the evef. Did a little Reading \+ to bed early.
