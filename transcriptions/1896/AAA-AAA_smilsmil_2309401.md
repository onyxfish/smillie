## Left Page

### Wed. Sept. 2, 1896

**Weather:** Cloudy with indications of rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy with indications of rain \- wind from the south & milder \- A good days work on dis[illegible]cate head \- altho' I did not get started very early \- I spent some time preparing some ground for Transplanting lettuce to it \- & at 4.30 I Transplanted about 50 heads. Eve'f., while the others amused themselves with cards I read.

## Right Page

### Thur. Sept. 3, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

No rain during the night as I had hoped \- Mild w'f. with strong So. wind which increased going around to the west with showers & some thunder bt. 3 & 6 o'clk. Clearing at night. Worked on place all day \- Before 5 o'clk. I went down town and attended to some matters. Eve'f. Read awhile to Ralph. \+ Then an hour with newspaper.
