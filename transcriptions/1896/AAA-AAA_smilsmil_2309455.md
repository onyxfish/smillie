## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 19, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine cold day - rather windy. To studio before 10 - Painted until 7 o'clk. on Vesuvius - Trees in foreground. etc - it is now ready to begin the second painting upon it. I hope to be in trim to start upon it on Monday m'f. Just before 1 o'clk. Mr. Fowler The advertising agent, came in \+ got from me my scrap book giving notes of earliest history of the Water Color - ot. He is to prepare an article for the su edition of the London Ill. News. Home to lunch. At 2.30 Ralph \+ I went to Calveria - P. - was very little impressed - apparently by the bust of his mother - he said he thought it could be improved - Jim was off all day with Mr. Delano \+ von Cook - went to aquarium Butterfly show \+ theatre \+ had lunch at Dorlan P. - I went to Denton's Butterfly show at Am. Art. Ass'n galleries - To studio about 4 - washed brushes - home at 6 - Dinner at 6.30 - fooled the even. away \+ to bed [illegible]

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 20, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day. After 11 when fine Ralph came in from S.S.- we went for a walk, going up to Central Park where we took a look at Metro Valley [illegible] - Sotami - Home to dinner at 2 - After dinner I went to Fine Arts Gallery in 57th S.- \+ saw the prize Architectural drawings of several young men, among them, young Palse, upon which he got prize that sent him abroad. On my way home I stopped at Sam Colman's - 60 W.57 S.- not in. After tea I went to Walter Cook's \+ was there for less than an hour - Evely Geo. \+ Nellie \+ Charl came in.
