## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 6, 1896

**Weather:** Showers in the mf  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ breezy \- BkP. after 9 \-
After getting fires in studio \+ etching
room going, the rest of the morning
was spent in the dark room developing
up a lot of films from Raelph's camera.
Then dinner \- all of the afternoon
was spent loafing about enjoying
out of doors \- Evely. I wrote some
notes while sitting in my corner in
the studio.

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 7, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant but cloudy during much of the
day. Peter Radeker \+ Jill came before I
had finished with my bkP. \+ much of
the mf was given to them directing
how catches should be fixed up on window
blinds \+ some other small jobs. I
worked with graver on Lippincott plate
for about an hour before dinner. At
2.30 I went out for a drive taking Ge. \+
Emily, Mary \+ her two girls \+ Raelph.
Had a pleasant ride down Meshakken
way. Back before 6 \+ worked for about
half an hour preparing a bed for
lettuce \- To bed early after the usual
newspaper reading.
