## Left Page

### Thur. July 2, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm day - Got to my studio work at about 10 o'clk. \+ in the course of the m't finished my Rhododendron dry-point. Dinner at 1. after that I wrote some letters of business \+ before 5 o'clk went down to P.O. \+ while down town attended to some matters. Tea at 6.30 \+ after that, with Katie, Bessie \+ the two boys, went to the station \+ met Charl who has come up from N.Y. to spend the Fourth. Eve'g on the Piazza. ## Right Page

### Fri. July 3, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day - worked for awhile finishing dry-point Rhododendrons. Then wrote several notes \+ drew checks to pay small bills that have come from N.Y. Dinner at 1. After that I lost some time owing to discomfort - a sort of wind colic. When I got to work again it was to fuss on the miniature of Mr. Comstock. After a time I commenced a new one - At 5 went to P.O. on my way home stopped for awhile at Agur Lathrop's - After tea I read awhile to R— Jim went down town with Charl \+ the girls.
