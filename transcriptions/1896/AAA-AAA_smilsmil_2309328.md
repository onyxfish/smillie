## Left Page

### Thur. April 9, 1896

de Bisschop died at 3.35 A.M.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — weather getting streaked towards the eve'g. Telegram from Charl while at bkfs', saying that the Doctors had little hope that de Bisschops w'd live — in the afternoon mother telegram came stating that he died at 3.35 This m'g. Charl went on to Boston. I have been much distressed by this event. I went to Miller-carpenter — Then to barbers \+ had hair cut — To studio at 11 \+ to work on Oct. brook painting — Will Parke, wife \+ daughter called at studio before I got there — Katie Adams came at about noon — lunch — back at 2.30 — Painted until 5 o'clk. Then walked to 34th S. — 7th \+ 8th Ave's — to see Ja's. Hart's new site for N.A. fD — a miserable place — Home at 6.15 Dinner — Read to R. until 8.30 — Then engaged upon the task of making list of garden seeds for Montrose until almost 11. Kate \+ the T. girls went to Carnegie Hall — a Princeton Glee Club concert.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 10, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — all day fighting a headache. It was 10.30 when I got to studio — set palette \+ did a little work on painting — some time engaged in going over a drawerful of old pencil sketches. A messenger came from Pro't. Low saying that he w'd like to see me this afternoon. Soon after 12 went to 2d. Nat. Bk. — deposited my Will \+ some Insurance papers in Vault — Saw Cash't. Case \+ got Rate on loan cut down from 6% to 5%. Then home to lunch. After that lay down an hour \+ got up feeling a little better in the head. Saw Mama J. as she gave Jim his lesson. Then to Columbia College \+ saw Pro't. Low. \+ he gave me \+ one of the latest information concerning site matters about Columbia's new grounds. Then to 5th Ave \+ 55th S. \+ called on Mr. W.H. Beach — not in — then to Fine Arts Socty Bl'g. — 57th s. — to exhib'n of Socty d'Am. Painters where I stayed until after 6. I met Gilman there \+ after leaving the exhib'n we walked together down to 39th St. Talking my headache still very severely I went to Mary's to offer her my tickets to the Diet Kitchen concert but she had other engagements so, I decided to waste them \+ not to go. Dinner before 7. Eve'g, Chas. Collins \+ Jno. Valentine came in \+ stayed until near 10. Then I went to studio \+ fixed up for the night — then home \+ to bed.
