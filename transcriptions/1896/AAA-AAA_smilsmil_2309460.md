## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 29, 1896

**Weather:** Overcast \+ milder  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 10 \+ spent most of the mng getting my w.c. box prepared for work. To lunch at 1 — after that to 2°. Nat. Bnk. To studio at 3 \+ worked as long as I could see on an old W.C. painting, done 12 or 15 years ago — Winter Moonlight. Then to Butters with frame to be regilt. Evng Bessie went to party at Morse's — I wrote invitations to up-town wing of S.te Com. To meet at my house on Thurs. evng next.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 30, 1896

**Weather:** Cloudy \+ milder  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 10 \+ commenced water color work. I put a couple of Rabbits into the "Winter Moonlight" drawing — Home to lunch. Afternoon I took up old drawing "Tower of Cathedral at Ravello". washed out a considerable portion of foreground then washed it in, making radical changes. Dinner at 6.30. Evng I went with the boys to S.S. festival — ch. of the Messiah — Jim took part by reciting a few verses, part of a little entertainment. Then there was ice-cream, cake \+ lemonade. Home at about 10.
