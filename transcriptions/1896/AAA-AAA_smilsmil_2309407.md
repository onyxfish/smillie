## Left Page

### Mon. Sept. 14, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

After a headache that lasted thro' the night I was in bed with it all day until 5 P.m. Then I got up, feeling very shaky, \+ had a light meal. I kept wandering about until dusk \+ then promenaded the piazzas until nearly, then to bed, [illegible — ~50 words]

## Right Page

### Tues. Sept. 15, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy day, with now \+ then a gleam of sunlight. Clear at night \+ much cooler. I felt very much out of sorts all day but managed to do a little work on my etching "On the Road to the Lake". I also spent some time trying to do some printing from my film negatives but there was so little strength in the light that I did not get anything ready to put into the toning bath. Eve'g in my cosy corner in studio Reading.
