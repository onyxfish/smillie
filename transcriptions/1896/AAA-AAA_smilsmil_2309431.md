## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 1, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Grey, mild + quiet m'f — brilliant afternoon. — Gave R. his bath before an open fire. Then had my own sponge bath + Rub down. — P&lp: at 9 — After that I pitched into my work + stuck to it all day — Having a good fire in bed room I gathered all my clothing etc. + prepared to pack my trunk. Went about with Mary + put things in shape to leave. — I also made my final gathering + assorting of things in studio — at 2 we had dinner — Mr. Van Aaken [illegible] it — After dinner I spent a couple of hours trimming the piazza vines — Then marked the various P&lp for freight + R.p. — at 6 had tea — After tea finished studio packing + screwed tops on case — Then worked for awhile on trunk + feeling very tired I went to bed at 10

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 2, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary went to Washington D. C.

Brilliant, superb day. Up at 6 — glorious eastern sky — sun not yet up — old moon — Mr. Van Aaken got our meals — K.T., R. + I went to train with Mary, then home to bkf: After that I commenced to pack the last barrel — At 10.30 went downtown + attended to various matters + home at 11.30 — Finished packing barrel — made list of Seeds (garden) on hand — Pope's team came before 4 + all freight and S+p. matter was taken away — Then I set to work on my trunk — at 5.30 I lay down + rested until 6 — Then tea — After, went down town + finished up business — Home at 8 — Cleaned out "dark Room", emptying all salutions — Then finished the last of the packing at about 9.30 — Chas. Sayre + Wm. Warner, spending the ev'g with K. T. — I had to wait.
