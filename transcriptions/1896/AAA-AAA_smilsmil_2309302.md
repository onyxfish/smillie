## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 13, 1896

*(Note above entry reads: "This is Tuesday. Feb 11th")*

**Weather:** Very windy & growing colder  **Temp:**

Got to studio about 9.30 \+ spent such portions of the day as I was allowed to work, in laying mezzo. ground on few. Green plate. I finished the laying of the fine ground over the face (125 tool) and commenced a general gro. over the whole plate with a 60 tool. It is a very hard, mechanical sort of work, but there is much that is interesting in working out the many possible textures resulting from the use of different rockers \+ the different ways of rocking. Damone sent his boy with some die proofs to sign \- also three notes of $100 each at 30-60-90 days. Home to lunch. my afternoon's work was interfered with by calls from Board of Health Inspectors \+ a man representing the owner of the property adjoining studio on the east \- all the trouble about water in the cellar has been renewed since the big storm of last Thurs. again flooded my cellar. The water now comes in all along the south wall \+ along part of the north wall also. Worked as long as I could see then walked up to 57th S. \+ 5th Ave. \+ back home getting in a little late for dinner \- A quiet eve'g at home.

grease 1 day.

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 14, 1896

**Weather:** Bright day but rather disagreeably breezy  **Temp:**

I stopped at Walter's house for a few minutes \- When I got to studio a little after 9 I was surprised to waiting for me Three men who had with them a great lot of material for waterproofing the studio cellar \- They set to work promptly \+ soon had the cellar in the greatest confusion piling \+ packing things all to one side so that their operations could be carried on over one half of the floor at a time \- The plan is to spread down tarred felt \+ mop boiling hot asphaltum over it, placing layer upon layer until there are 6 or 8 thicknesses. Then concrete 3 in. \+ a finishing coat of cement \- 1 inch \- 4 in. brick walls are to built all around the cellar 2'. 6" high. I put in a fair day's work in the studio on my green mezzo ground \- aetho' I had several interruptions. Stom came \+ got some items concerning early history of Water-Color Socy. for a newspaper article \- Eve'g at home.

grease \- 1 day.
