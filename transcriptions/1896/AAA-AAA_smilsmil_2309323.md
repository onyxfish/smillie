## Left Page

### Mon. March 30, 1896

**Weather:** Dull, mild w.f. threatening rain clearing in the afternoon quite warm. muggy + overcast at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got to studio at usual time but as I did not feel like painting I spent an hour or two on some pen sketching. At 12 went to Bank + deposited a check from Ch. Danmore — + got documents from Safe Deposit. Home to lunch at 1 — at 2 to Chickering Hall to "Vocal Recital" of Mr. + Mrs. Georg Henschel. I was not moved to the extent I had expected — Their singing fell cold upon me + did not awaken the warmness, or cause certain chords to vibrate as I had expected. While I could not quite control myself at the Recital of Miss Dawson's on the 17th — here I was almost entirely unmoved — It is inexplicable to me. Met Sally Akers + her father + had a pleasant chat with them — for the latter part of the Recital sat with Mary Ireland — + after it walked the B.S. with her. Went to Kurty + saw miniatures of Mrs. Comstock in progress — then in to see Savony collection — saw him — has sadly changed — Then to studio + then home. + had a short rest before dinner. Walter not in rough around plans + estimates for temporary steps. based upon some crude ideas of mine in connection with an uptown site found a f.d — to N.A. at 8.15. Cornell meeting which, during latter part of session proved to be refreshingly breezy — the subject of discussion being sites — We did not adjourn until near midnight — It was nearly when I got to bed

## Right Page

### Tues. March 31, 1896

**Weather:** Mild + pleasant Spring day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up without a headache but when I got to studio I found I could not work. I went to a florist's + bought an Easter lily in pot + took it to studio + made a poor slight pencil sketch. To lunch at 1 — after that, feeling exceedingly sleepy I lay down upon my bed + had a sound sleep — after 4 — Joz. + Ninn called — as I was the only one in, I saw them — Bessie + Jim came in before they left — I went to studio + put things in shape for over night — dinner at 6.30 — Charl came in for a few minutes immediately after dinner — later in the w.f. I did a little writing + got to bed early.
