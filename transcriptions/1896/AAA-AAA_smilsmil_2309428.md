## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 26, 1896

**Weather:** Pleasant day. much milder  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day. much milder — I spent the m'f in studio's at the almost endless task of assorting \+ packing the many things that have to be taken back \+ forth. Dinner at 1 — after that, feeling much out of sorts I lay down upon my bed \+ slept until almost 5 o'clk. \+ then got up feeling much better. — Charl had been printing from some of the negatives recently developed \+ I tried them — In the ev'g. in studio sorting bills, letters \+ other papers. Wrote to Clark \+ to Higgins to turn on water \+ clean flues next Tues. m'f \+ took letters to P.O. at 11.

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 27, 1896

**Weather:** Delicious day — mild \+ cloudless  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delicious day — mild \+ cloudless — About 11 o'clk I went downtown — telegraphed to Jim — had hair cut \+ attended to other small matters. I went over the place with Stone \+ Spense \+ explained to them the work that I wish to be done between this time \+ next Spring — The afternoon was wholly given up to out of door work — most of it among the trees, applying tar — etc. etc Downtown at 8 — Eve'g some reading \+ R — Thus my regular chores, and newspapers.
