## Left Page

### Tues. May 19, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably hot day — clouded, with shower, about 3 p. m. clearing much cooler. met Mary Cook at the butcher's \+ she invited Sister Mary, the boys \+ me to dine at her house to-morrow. To studio at about 10 \+ spent the day writing letters in reply to acumulations. To lunch, as usual \+ back. Returning at 5.20 to bid Kate good bye, as she was to go to Po'k. on 6 o'clk. train, but she had already gone — so, Ralph \+ I went out on our wheels — we were detained by some repairs that R. had to stop at Borrmann's for — but we had a 4¼ mile ride over As- phalt pavements \+ practiced dodging cable \+ horse cars, teams, pedestrians \+ bicyclists. Ralph does very well. Home at 6.30 — dinner delayed a little — Eve'g — stupid again \+ to bed early — I have been ex- permenting with lagerbier — it is refreshing \+ palatable but makes me very heavy.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 20, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cool m'g. Up early \+ had a short ride on bicycle before bkfst. — Afternoon overcast with sprinkling of rain — about 6.30 it rained as tho' starting in for a night of it — but it did'nt. Spent the m'g at studio's regularly clearing up \+ getting such paintings as I have, in shape to show to Ja. to. Morse Jim's teacher, who has expressed his willingness to accept a part. — first all of his pay from me in that form. Home to lunch at 1 — at 3 went to Columbia to see Pres. Low, but he was engaged — then to Century \+ wrote a note to Mr. Jno. Bigelow concerning Reservoir Site for library — his bill securing that having been signed by the Governor. Then to studio \+ soon after 4 again to Columbia \+ saw Pres. Low for a few minutes. He wishes me to meet him again early in June when he hopes to be able to talk with more certainty on site matters in 16" 5— Then home — had a rest before dinner — at 6.45 went to Ed. W. Cook's where Sister Mary, the boys \+ I had been invited — we had a very pleasant dinner \+ chatty eve'g until after 9.30 — then home.
