## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 4, 1896

**Weather:** Very cold. partially clouded.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got to studio soon after 9 \- \+ down to K. \+ V's \+ got proof of Lee head \- also a close wipe on white paper for catalogue reproduction \- back to studio at 11.30 \+ worked on plate until 1. then home to lunch. To studio at 2 \- Dr. Gerster came in at 4 \+ soon after I went to Grand Central Station \+ met Jim as he came in from Boston at 4.30 \- in good shape after having had a very good time. \+ Rest before dinner. Evelf at 8 went to studio \- Jared Flagg and Fredc Dielman only came \- my humiliation is complete. I feel that I have been very severely snubbed \- Home at about 10.

## Right Page
