## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 9, 1896

Mary arr. from Washington

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — After bkfp. went with R. to his school + saw Miss Miller — Then to Dr. Allen's. W. 47 + 5 — He took imp. from lower jaw — Then home — to market + studio + then down town — went to see Mr. W. Lee — he was not in — back to studio + home to lunch at 1 — After that a out until 3 — Then to Am. Art Ass'n. + had a conference with Sutton + Kirby as to terms upon which N.A. of D. could exhibit in AAA galleries if an up town site is bought + building deferred for some years — Then to Dr. Allen's at 4.30 — Then home where I found Mary, just in from her visit to the Cromwells at Washington, D.C. Dinner + then to N.A. Council meeting — all present but T.W. Wood — Short uninteresting meeting — I left with F.S. Church at 9.30 we walked up town together — I went to studio — then home.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 10, 1896

Uncle Willie + Aunt Agnes with us

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — I got to studio before 10 + until 1 o'clk was engaged writing a Report on site for N.A. f.d. in favor of plot south side of Columbia University Sq. — Perry has got up one in opposition — both are to be printed — a special meeting has been called for Wed. eve'g. 25th ins. After lunch I went to Dr. Allen's. W. 47 + 5 + had a short session with him — To studio soon after 3 + worked for nearly two hours on Report — Then home + worked for awhile unpacking freight from Montrose. Uncle W. + Aunt A — arr. from Po'k and dined + stayed over night with us — I spent a good part of the eve'g chatting with Uncle W — Then resumed my unpacking + worked until near 11.
