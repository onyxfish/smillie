## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 11, 1896

**Weather:** Quite mild & pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Quite mild & pleasant — I spent the day with color on my large Vesuvius, daubing crude indications upon the charcoal sketch, of the scheme that I hope to be able to carry it. Evatt Clark came to the house — as the night was fine I proposed a ride on our wheels. It was considerably cltng before we got started — went down Mad. Ave. to 23d St — then across thro' 26 t S: to 8 t Ave — down 8t to Hudson St, back up 8 t Ave. to 40 t St —, thro' to Park Ave — \+ after some more riding up \+ down the Ave — S turned in

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 12, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — after getting thro' with my mtf duties — making out J. P. Ryan's lease, among other things — I went to Cleverly's studio in 27t St \+ spent more than an hour with him while he made little changes in the clay model of portrait bust (small) of Louise — He has smoothed \+ refined away the character that he had at first, but I yet hope that he will succeed in getting a satisfactory likeness. Then I went to the N.A. Then to Safe Deposit Co — Then to Am. Art Ass'n, where I saw the wonderful Denton Butterfly syph. Then home to lunch — Afternoon I worked on my big Vesuvius as long as I could see — then, at 4.30, home \+ had a rest before dinner — Aty went to Century Club — there until near 12. Then home.
