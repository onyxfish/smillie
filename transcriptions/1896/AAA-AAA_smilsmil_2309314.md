## Left Page

### Thur. March 12, 1896

**Weather:** Bright winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright winter day \- a charming contrast to yesterday. Lovisas Cook \+ Miss Jennison (?) called at studio soon after I got there. Then Mary Cook came in for a few minutes to offer me a ticket for the meeting of the 19th century club at Sherry's to-night. I got to work Painting on an old picture 14 × 24" "Oct. p.m., with sheep". Home to lunch \+ back at usual time \+ Painted until 4:30, when I was too tired to continue Masons at work in the cellar, repairing portions of the cellar floor, where the cement had not hardened properly. I went over to Knoedler's, 5th Ave. \+ 34th S. Saw Roos. \+ Sonder \- also some paintings by Wm T. Richards \+ his daughter \- English coast. Suet I felt too tired to go to the 19th Century Club meeting, so, I stayed quietly at home, Reading to the boys as usual \+ getting to bed tolerably early. Charl was in for a time.

## Right Page

### Fri. March 13, 1896

**Weather:** Cold mt[illegible]  **Temp:** down to 15°

Ther. down to 15°. After bfst. I went to studio, grocery market \- then to Davies. Coal in 42°. St. Then down town \- went to Geo. R. Read's in Pine St. \+ talked with Van Pelt about Real Estate \- then to see thee. where my patience was worn out waiting for W.H.L.in who was engaged with a client. At last I saw him \- arranged some Academy business \+ then some of my own estate business. I got the Wills (+ executed them) that are to protect the legacies left by Annie to the boys. It was after 1 when I left there \- then to 84 Chambers St. Yale Lock Co. about a part for a lock at the studio \- then to Miller the Carpenter in 40th S. \- to the Studio \+ home at 2.30 \- Had a very late lunch \- At 4 to studio \- Miller came at about 5 and measured for some cases in one of the closets. It was after 6 when I got home \- Dinner. Tom Cook dined with us, taking Bessie's place who dines at Tom Cook's \- After dinner I went to Geo. Cook's \+ he complesed with me, the assignment of the Cornman mtf for protection of Annie's legacy to the boys. Home at about 9. Charl there.
