## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 7, 1896

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — after bkfs. I went up to Dr. Allen's — W. 47th S. about my teet \+ made an appointment with him for Monday m'f. Then to studio — spent the rest of the m'f. setting up bicycles of K. T. \+ Ralph — Took K. T.'s home when I went to lunch at 1 — on returning to studio at 2 R— went with me \+ got his \+ he \+ Jim went off together \+ had a ride. The main part of my afternoon was spent resting preparatory to an ev'f at the Century, Monthly meeting — I got there before 9 o'clk \+ left after 12 having seen \+ chatted with many acquaintances. most of the times with [illegible] Beckwith \+ other N. A.'s preparing for the campaign that is to occur with the special meeting called for Nov. 18 — After 1 o'clk. when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 8, 1896

**Weather:** Cloudy  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ Cloudy day \+ altho' not cold it was rather raw. Bkfs. at 9 — about 10 I went to studio \+ got to work on my bicycle, intending to give it a rub up, but instead, finding that it really needed a thorough cleaning I worked at it until nearly 2 o'clk. \+ then did not finish it. Charl came with his bicycle but I did not go out with him — After dinner I read newspaper for awhile but soon feeling sleepy I lay down on my bed \+ slept until near 6 — Tea at 6.30 Sev'f. went to Mary Cook's for a few minutes — then to studio — there for a short time \+ then home. Ge e. \+ Charl came in \+ stayed until 10 — I got to bed early.
