## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 15, 1896

*Miss Buck & Taylor left us -*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day inspite of considerable heat — a refreshing breeze all day. Up soon after 6 — having been awake from 4 with headache. Bkf. at 7 — bus. \+ hyp. at 7.30 — Kathenie, Carleton, Ralph \+ I went to Station \+ saw the girls started, the Bucks for P'k. \+ Miss Taylor for Quogue. Then home \+ podged about until 12 when I gave up to my headache \+ had a nap before dinner. Quite useless during the afternoon. At about 5 R. \+ I, on our wheels, went to H.H. Beach's with a note, then down town to P.O. \+ after a short ride, home. Tea Ev'g., read awhile to R. before getting him to bed. Capt. \+ Mr. Cromwell came up for a short time.

Three Italians worked filling ditches all day — Hibbard \+ Putnam finished after working 3 hours — I paid them off. Paun \+ Shermann finished — I paid $—

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 16, 1896

*Gretie left on her vacation.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cool, with threats of a storm, the wind changed \+ in the early P.m. There were two showers with some thunder — at the rest of the afternoon the weather was clearing — fine moonlight at night. Bkf. at 9.30 — I spent what remained of the m'f. in the "dark room" developing a lot of photo's taken by Ralph, a few of them being tolerably good. Dinner at 2. Afternoon I wrote letters to Emily \+ Mary Cook — Ev'g. I wrote until near 10 — then to P.O. \+ to bed.
