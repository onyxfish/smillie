## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 3, 1896

**Weather:** Very cold day \- cloudy most of the time.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got to studio at usual time and worked all day on Vesuvius, fussing over what I painted yesterday \+ getting all except foreground on the left painting in \- At 1.30 I went home \+ at about 5 I went with Katherine \+ Bessie to the hotel in 44th St. where Nicoll is staying \+ we called on his daughter to bring one of her regular afternoons. Nicoll came in before we left. Home at a few minutes after 6 \- Eve'g Charlie came in \+ I did not accomplish much.

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 4, 1896

**Weather:** Fine winter day \- milder.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio about 10. Jerry came \+ got the pt. of Rocky Mts. belonging to Century, that I have cleaned \+ reframed. I got to work on Vesuvius \+ painted in the foreground feeling quite encouraged \+ enjoying my work. Soon after 12 Miss Abbatt came \+ as she stayed until 1 I asked to to go home with me \+ have lunch \- which she did. Back to studio at 2 \+ painted until after 4 \- Then I went to Knoedler's and saw Sonden \+ from him got address of a man who makes canvas \+ panel-back stretchers \- I am going to paint Vesuvius on a 33×44 canvas \- Then a short walk \- dinner \- Eve'g Read to R until 9 \- Then for an hour \+ a half writing notes \+ making out checks and such like business.
