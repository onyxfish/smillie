## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 18, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A charming, bright \+ mild day \- After bkfp. went to studio \+ then home \+ with Jim \+ Ralph I went to Dr. Allen's at 10.30 by appointment to have their teeth looked after. Both of them, it seemed, need some work \- I left them there \+ I went to Mr. Jas. H. Morse's \- Jim's teacher, \+ had a long talk with him concerning the treatment most advisable for Jim in his studies at his time \- There are some things in his school treatment that I do not like \- M. was very pleasant \+ promised that the matter should have his most serious attention \- Then home \- lunch \- at 2 went to studio \- Spent some time clearing up \- Then made a start to commence work on head of Gen. Green. At about 4 went to Con. Gas. Co. 4th Ave. \+ complained of meter \- Then to Hallie's in Broadway near 12th S \- Shoes \- Then home \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Mallie Adams still with us \- A very quiet eve'g at home

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 19, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dismal, dark day with light snow \- I got up late with a headache that increased as the day went on until at night \- I had to go to bed with Ralph \- I did some writing \+ some newspaper reading during the day \- but it was very wearisome \-
