## Left Page

### Thur. Nov. 5, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A spiteful, windy, rainy day — I got up with a headache & staggered thro' the day with it, incapacitated for work of any kind — Immediately after bkfst I went out & attended to marketing but returned at once & did not go out of the house again.

## Right Page

### Fri. Nov. 6, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant day — After bkfst I went to Millers (carpenter) in 40th S. & then to Davies (coal) in 42d S. & hurried up the order I left there on Wed. — Then to grocer's & butcher's — Then to studio — Mrs. Long came to clean & I gave her directions — Men I uncrated the three bicycles that have been in Ryan's shop for two or three days. Took crates into cellar & bicycles into my part of the bldg. — Men home at 12. — Lunch at 1 — At 2 Ralph & I started out — went to H.A., Then to 2d. Nat'l Bk. & dep'd. & drew out money — Then to Am. Art Ass'n. & saw Kirby — Then to Danielle's. Bdway near 9th S. & exchanged umbrella bought for Ralph — Then to Cammeyer's, 6th Ave. & 20th S. & bought R. shoes & rubbers — bought for him a rubber coat — Then to Hunter's. Bdwy & 28th S. & had spectacles rep'd. Then to Hankinson's — 2. 27th S. — Then to studio, & home at 5.30 — Dinner at 6.30 Ev'g. a little writing. reading newspaper. Charl came in for a few minutes bet. 9 & 10 & to & I went to studio — back & to bed soon after 11.
