## Left Page

### Sat. April 25, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

huled all day with a beastly headache
Got up at about 7 \+ had something to
eat \- Then lolled in my chair until
midnight, when I ate a trifle more \-
Then again in my chair reading
newspaper until 1.30 A.M. Then to
bed \-

## Right Page

### Sun. April 26, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy m'f. clearing to be a delightful
day \- I got up feeling the worse for
wear \+ had bkfs. at 10 \- Then with news-
paper, before a pleasant fire, for the rest
of the m'f. After 1 I went to studio \+
opened windows etc \- to give the place
an airing \- Home at 2. Roger Adams
There \- me stayed to dinner \- At 3.30 Raelph
\+ J. by elevated R.R. went to 104th St. 9th Ave \-
Thence walked up to 110th St \+ up Morningside to
116th St \+ through to Columbia University \+ took another
good look at the site suggested for the N.A. of D.
\- square of 200' on The Boulevard \- did the block
at 115th \+ M16th St \- thence to Riverside Drive near
Grant's Tomb, where for a time we watched the gay
crowds of pedestrians, cyclists, riders \+ drivers \-
Then we walked down Riverside Drive to 82d
St. Tho' to The Boulevard where we took a car and
got home at 6.40 after a walk of more than three
miles \- Tea \- after that, to Walters \- not
at home \- then to studio \- then to Colman's
121 E. 39th St not at home \- then I went
home \- a little writing \+ a little reading
\+ then to bed \-
