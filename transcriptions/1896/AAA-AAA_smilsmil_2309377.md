## Left Page

### Thur. July 16, 1896

**Weather:** Brilliant, cool day  **Temp:** [illegible]

My "chores" consumed the m'fg., nor could I object, the weather was so glorious. It was 12 o'clk when I went into studio + worked on drawing of "Tower, Mad. Sq. Garden." After dinner I continued the work. Rev. W. Beuton + wife called. About 4 o'clk, I stopped work + prepared myself for a bicycle ride — I was out until 5.30 + when I came in my cyclometer registered 500 miles. Tea at 6.30. After that worked for awhile in garden setting out lettuce plants — then to P.O. — then read awhile to Ralph before he went to bed — then read newspaper for an hour — + to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. July 17, 1896

Mollie Adams + Eugene Atwater arr?

**Weather:** Very cool day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very cool day — cloudy most of the time — beautiful afternoon + cloudless night. Ge. Walker sent two men who commenced digging down in the swamps, reopening the ditches dug there nearly two years ago. I propose now to finishing the draining + have the lot plowed over. I spent some time overseeing them — also worked on the "Tower" — at 12 went out on bicycle + had a short ride — dinner — more work on "Tower" until near 5 — then out in garden transplanting lettuce until tea time — After tea went to R.R. St. Train late 20 m. Mollie + Gene arr'd. in good shape. After a supper they spent an hour on Piazza —

Two Italians commenced the work of reopening the old ditches + draining swamps in Tyler lot.
