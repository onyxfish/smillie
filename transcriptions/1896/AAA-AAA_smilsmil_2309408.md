## Left Page

### Wed. Sept. 16, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant, beautiful day \- Took a number of photo's with Jim's camera after 4½p. \+ then to work in studio dividing my time between etching \+ printing photo's. I commenced the etching of Mad. Sq. Tower, \+ before the close of the day began to feel encouraged to believe that I was "getting the hang of it" \- I worked until 5 o'clk. then went out bicycling with Katherine, Bessie \+ Ralph \- We went out on some back of "little R.R. station" \- a short 3 m. ride \- Tea at about 7. A quiet, humdrum evg.
Gretta came back to-day \+ her niece, who has been taking her place, left.

## Right Page

### Thur. Sept. 17, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The day opened with rain but the weather cleared before noon \- Thunder showers near us in the early afternoon \- Fine moonlight night. I worked on the etching of Mad. Sq. Garden Tower, trying hard to lift myself above my own level, but in spite of me the old manners \+ methods prevail \- the work is no better, if as good, as I have done before. I did also some photo. printing. Dinner at 1\. Worked until 4.30, then down town \+ did some shopping \- back \+ read newspapers until tea. Fine \- Evg some magazine reading \+ to bed early.
