## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 31, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fairly pleasant day — some sunshine, very bright + beautiful + some shadow. To studio at usual time + to work upon my drawing of Cathedral Tower, Ranello, exhibited two years ago. I changed it materially working very much crude + inharmonious color out of it + clearing up the shadows. After lunch I went to 2°. Nat' But— on my way back stopped at N.A. + for a few minutes + saw the genl. Exhibition made by the students. On the whole I think it a mistake to make public exhibition such crude + callous work as much of it is. To studio at 3 — worked until 4.30 — home + rested — Diggie Smillie dined with us. Evg. by invitation — Wood, Brown, Nicole, Beckwith + bro. Geo., of N.A. + S. Eles came to the house to confer together over the situation — J. S. Church has joined the up-town party which makes us 7 — divide the Com. of 14 equally — part down-town + the chair without vote, would make the com. on a vote stand 7 up-town, 6 down-town — up-town men are divided in favor of two sites — 116° 5'— + 116° 5'— it was to insure unanimity of action agt down-town, or 5th site, that I called the meeting — we were very harmonious + separated at about 11 olk. with a general understanding as to our tactics for the immediate future.
