## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 7, 1896

**Weather:** Overcast with threats of Rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast with threats of Rain — I went down town — first to Tax Office \+ spent much time running from one dep. to another to get my Tax bills for the year — exasperating work — taxes on studio more than doubled — Then to Lee \+ Lee. Mclee lost his wife abt 3rd of Nov. I learned it from him \+ was much moved. Then to Geo R Read's — saw him \+ Van Pelt for a few moments — Then to Fredericks P40 Sullivans \+ ordered 33×44 [illegible] for Resourias from Sorrento — Then home to lunch. After that, to studio — clearing drawers \+ hunting for desired studies. Home at 5 \+ had a rest. T. S. Church dined with us — the T. gives took a great fancy to him. They to council meeting — That meeting adjo — at 9 — Nicoll, Perry \+ Watrous came in \+ the Site com. held its 2d session. Perry soon opened his attack \+ made a long harangue lauding the 57th St. site along side of Am. Art Socy. It amused me to hear him repeat all of the arguments that I had used in its favor — He got into the realms of imagination \+ took [illegible] time so I called him down to cold facts and figures — after doing some cyphering \+ discussing upon lower levels he promised to bring some exact information next Monday night \+ then, at about 11 o'clk, the com. adjo.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 8, 1896

**Weather:** Dull day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day — To studio at 10 — Nicoll came in \+ spent some time talking over the situation in Academy matters. Then I worked on compilations from I.S.C. Academy Annals. Home to lunch. After that, feeling the loss of sleep last night, I had a good nap. Then to studio \+ again worked on compilations. Evef Mary \+ Bessia went to Mendelssohn Club Concert on Charl's tickets. I read until after 9 — Then until 11 worked on compilations.
