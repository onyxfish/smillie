## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 21, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day, dark \+ mizzling wet. To studio at about 10 \+ got out remainder of letters to N a s \+ posted them \- Carpenter in studio cellar putting up a partition that will give me a corner exclusively. About 12 I went to Dr. Seew\- W. 47 S. \+ he gave me some alleviation from the soreness in my mouth caused by new plate. To lunch at 1 \- to studio at 2.30 \- fooling around \+ accomplishing nothing of importance \- home at 5. Dinner at 6.30 \- at 8.30 went to [illegible] Century \- had a long talk with Perry during which he argued his side of the site question to his own satisfaction \- Home before 11.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 22, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Not altogether pleasant in weather \- A wretched headache kept me in bed until noon \+ made me wretched for the rest of the day. I managed to take a walk in the Park with the boys in the afternoon but gave up after that \+ kept on the sofa until I went to bed for the night.
