## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 29, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Much cooler and wet, rain beginning to fall about 3 o'clk. B'kfs. at 9 — read newspapers until 11 — then to studio — there until after 12 — then home \+ had a short walk with the boys before dinner at 2 — After that, as it was raining so hard that I could not go out I had a long nap. Tea at 6.30. Ev'g. arranging papers etc about Jimmie's desk. Chas came in about 9 P. m. — Snow followed the rain.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 30, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Six or more inches of wet snow fell last night. Cloudy until ev'g. air very raw \+ cold — Got to studio before 10 \+ for some time, as the room was chilly, I scrubbed at my printing press with a wet cloth — then I made a slight tracing of charcoal sketch "Vesuvius from Sorrento" \+ retraced it on to a panel \+ with a little more care, made pencil outline. In the m'g went to Bank \+ attended to some of my business there. On my way back to studio stopped in at O'Brian's (Printers) \+ upbraided him for printing site map in my report upside down. Ev'g. Council meeting — got through with routine business at 9 o'clk — then Nicoll, Perry \+ Watrous were introduced \+ the new Site Com. consisting of the Council \+ the three gentlemen named organized by electing Jas.
