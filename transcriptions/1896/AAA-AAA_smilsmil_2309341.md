## Left Page

### Tues. May 5, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A true April day — Brilliant m'f quite warm — a brief Thunder storm at noon \+ a better specimen between 1 \+ 2 o'clk. I worked pretty well all the m'f writing a new Report to be presented at the Special meeting next Sat. ev'g. Whittredge called about 12.30 expecting to see Jas. from whom he wishes to learn something about summer places on the New England Coast — Home at 1. Elie. Howe at lunch — I got back to Studio before 3 \+ worked for about an hour \+ a half on Report. Then home \+ with Jim went out on our bicycles in the park The roads were very muddy \+ I twisted my front wheel so very badly that I had to return. I left my wheel for repairs in Jay. Ave. I should have written before that Jim, Ralph \+ I had a half hours ride on our wheels before bl'f. This m'f. Ev'g with boys until 9 — then some reading —

## Right Page

### Wed. May 6, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day — growing quite cool at night — After bl'f: I went to Bormann's \+ had an interview with him concerning the injury to my bicycle yesterday — He says I will have to get a new, Tangent spoked wheel finished my Site Report for the meeting of N.A.'s next Sat. After lunch went to back \+ got to Studio at 3 — Shirlaw came in for a few minutes — About 4.30 I went to 57th St. \+ saw Beckwith in his studio \+ consulted with him concerning my report \+ got some excellent suggestions. Walked home — Jim \+ Ralph on their bicycles in the street — I had a short rest before dinner \+ a very quiet ev'g after it. To bed a little earlier than usual.
