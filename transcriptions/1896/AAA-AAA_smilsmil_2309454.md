## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 17, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day, growing milder — To studio before 10 — worked all day on Vesuvius, painting in the lower R. h. corner. At 12 I went to Tho. Locke's \+ saw chairs that he is repairing for me — Then to Calverly's \+ saw the finished bust of Annie with which I am entirely satisfied — Then to 2? Nat Buk — Met Eleg. Howe in Mad. Sq. — Home to lunch at 1 — To studio at 2 \+ worked until 4.30 — a short walk — home at 6 — dinner at 6.30 Bessie went to Po'k at 3.30 this p.m. on a visit — Swetf. Charl came in.

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 18, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy day \+ much milder — Put in a good day of hard work on Vesuvius. Painting in all of the l. h. foreground — of course, very broadly — there being many details that I expect to complete to-morrow. Home to lunch \+ back as usual. After work at 5 o'clk I went to barber's \+ had my hair cut — then home — dinner — Swetf — reading \+ loafing \+ to bed early. K. T. went to Po'k. this m'g.
