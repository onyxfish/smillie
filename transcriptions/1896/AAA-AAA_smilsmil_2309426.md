## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 22, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charles ret? to us.

Sulperb day \- brilliant + coal. Soon after bkft. Charc came in having come from Dingman's Ferry via Erie R.R. + Binghampton. I gave the whose day to gathering things together for packing + doing some things out of doors to get the place in shape to leave for the winter \- Cooley + Rutan came up + I explained to them how I wished to have the house cisterns connected so that their contents could be forced into the barn Tanks. I did some photo. printing from the negatives that I developed last hight. At 2, Charl, Ralph + I went to the fair ground + rode 5 miles on the track on our wheels \- About 6, C. + I went to P.O. Evtf Wm. Warner toat K. + E. to hear a lecture by Will Carleton.

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 23, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very lovely m'f. \- thickening up at noon rain commencing after 3 \- very heavy rain at night. \- I was very busy all day \+ during part of the m'f I worked at cutting back the tall hedge of Maple trees that hide the unsightly fair ground fence + wall in the m'f I also made some photo. prints \- in the afternoon I toned them \- I did some work in the way of gathering things together for packing. Went downtown at about 6 \- Evtf, Mr. + Mrs. Dav'd. Sayre took tea with us \- At about 8.30 I amused myself + went to my "dark room" + developed a dozen films, getting through at about 10.30. \- Then sat + chatted with Charc until midnight.
