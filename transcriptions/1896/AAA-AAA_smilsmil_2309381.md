## Left Page

### Fri. July 24, 1896

*Kate arr. from Po'keepsie.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy & continuous Rain all day. I made fine in studio & took pencil sketch that I mounted yesterday act of press & worked upon it with pencil & chalk. After dinner I continued my work & made tracing preparatory to etching. At 5 went to P.O. Got some candies etc. to solace the family who have been prisoners indoors all day. Tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 went to Station & met Kate — then I went to P.O. a quiet evey & newspaper as usual.

## Right Page

### Sat. July 25, 1896

*Jim went to spend Sunday with Greer.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Glorious day. Very breezy & cloudy in the mf. clearer & quieter in the afternoon — cloudless & quiet moonlight. I wrote to Chas in the mf. sending him key to No 2.38" S- as Kate tells me that Ch. Reported to her that had seen one of the upper windows open a day or two ago. \+ so, after going to the P.O. the mtf went with to work in studio — After dinner Norman Lake came & took Jim to spend Sunday with Greer on the farm. I rec'd. letter from J.W. Maynard enclosing check for $229.75 Rent & heating studio. I Replied to him & wrote some other letters & went to P.O. about 5. Giles fitted the big blind to Jim's Dormer window. After tea again went to P.O. We spent some time with Harper and St Nicholas pr. Aug.
