## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 25, 1896

**Weather:** fine clear, cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkfst. at 8, as usual. Boys in a great hurry to get at their Xmas presents in the parlor — All hands assembled at 8.45 & the pkgs. of presents were opened. They made quite an exhibition — Jim was greatly excited over his aquarium — I was surprised by getting entirely unexpected presents from Mary & her gives. Went to studio at 10 — a big fire in 33d. S: E. of 3d. Av. I spent half an hour there — then to my work. Another day spent pothereing over the [illegible] nts & other portions, painted upon yesterday & the day before. — I know what I want — I know where I am at fault — but I am powerless to do better. I need the years of incessant practice that I have not given. Home to lunch at 1 — while at Table Geo. & Emily came in — They lunched with us. I got to studio soon after 2 & worked as long as I could see. Geo. Smillie came in about 4.30 — I cleaned up & had a rest before I went home to dinner at 6 — Charle dined with us & stayed until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 26, 1896

**Weather:** Clouded most of the day — clear at night  **Temp:** very cold

I got to studio at about 10 & started at once upon the work of painting the water in my Vesuvius picture — a piece of work over which I am quite nervous. I got it done, but of course far short of what I had hoped. With a very large brush I worked back over the mountains & clouds. Home to lunch — after losing light, washed my brushes & home at 6. Evening at home & did none of the things that I should have done.
