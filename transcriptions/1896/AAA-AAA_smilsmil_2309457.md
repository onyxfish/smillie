## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 23, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow all day \- high wind \+ cold, afternoon \+ at night \- To studio before 10 \+ painted on Vesuvius all the m'f \- going over much of yesterday's work on sky \+ distance \- Home to lunch at 1 \- At 2, to 2? Nat'l. Buk \- then to Putnam's \+ brought some more books \- then to 39 Park Pl \- \+bat \- an aquarieme for Jim \- then to MacRaris, 41 Wall \- not in \- then to Cullen's \- Wm St. Photo. supplies \- then to G. R. Read's \+ saw W. Van Pelt \- then by elevated RR to 6th Ave. \+ 33rd S. \+ went thro' 32d. S. to 8th Ave. \+ back looking for Decamp the plaster figure man whose shop is in 33d. S. \- Then to Studio, \+has a small blizzard \- cleaned palette \+ fixed fire \+ got home to dinner a little late \- Svey. Charl came in.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 24, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear cold day \- After bkf. I wrote a note to Sue Cook \+ then went to P.O. Station 44th S. \+ lex. Ave. \+ mailed two books to her for Eleanor \+ Willie. It was 10.30 when I got to Studio Painted on Vesuvius \- fussing all day over sky \+ distance \- trying to work out the muddy color \- Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2 \- Painted until 4.30 \- then cleaned up \+ went to 33d. S. west of 7th Ave. \- Decamp, the plaster figure man \- but he did not have what I was looking for \- so, K. P. must go without her Xmas present for the present \- Ev'g, after the boys got to bed Charl came in \+ some time was spent arranging the presents for the mornng. To bed before 11 \-
