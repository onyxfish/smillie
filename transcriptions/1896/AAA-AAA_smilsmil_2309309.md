## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 29, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Steady, down pouring rain all day. After disposing of my own duties I went down town to Lee & Lee's to see M. W. Lee about N.A. & Estate matters. I had to wait until nearly 11 o'clk for him & then was with him for about an hour & a half. He is to me a provokingly slow man & I do not get from him a clear impression of what I should do in my estate tribulations. Home at 1 — Spent the p.m. at home, writing notes & clearing up matters on my desk at home. Went to Studio about 5 to attend to things for the night. Everything so wet that I did not go to Cemetery as I had intended — Charl came in — just in from Boston.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 1, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, wet day, rain falling quietly most of the time. I did not have bkfst. until 10 as I got up with a headache. I went to the studio before 12 — Spent an hour or more there — Maynard came in — Ret. from Washington last night. Home at 1.30. Dinner at 2 — lay down on my bed at 3.30 & dozed & slept until 6 — Then to studio & fixed things there for the night — Home to tea before 7 — Found Katie T — there — she having just Ret. from her brief visit to Po'k. where she went to some Vassar class function. Read to Ralph until 8.30 Evng., later, worked on Estate accts for '95 — Charl came in.
