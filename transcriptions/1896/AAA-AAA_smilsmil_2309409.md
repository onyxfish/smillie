## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 18, 1896

**Weather:** Brilliant m.f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant m.f. — overcast later in the day. Gill came after b'kf. \+ for an hour I was with him planning improvements by which some habitable rooms will be "done off" in the large barn. Then to work on etching "Mad. Sq. Tower" — with no better result than yesterday. Dinner at 1 — Some photo. printing going on at same time as etching. Worked until 3.30 — then feeling wearied of my work I spent some time cleaning bicycle. Then I had a 5 mile ride on it — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g., newspaper \+ writing letter to Farrington — To P. O. before 10 —

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 19, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Exceedingly heavy rain with thunder \+ lightning during the night — continuing thro' the m'g. in gusty showers. Wind rose and shifted to west — clouds broke late in the afternoon — high wind at night. After b'kf. I wrote draft of letter to Pres. Seth Low — Then I spent an hour toning \+ fixing photo. prints — found that of the 15 prints all are under printed but two that I thought spoiled because they were forgotten \+ left too long in the frame. Then I got to work on etching, Mad. Sq. Garden Tower" — Dinner. After that I spent some time working over a pen drawing by K. T. — she standing beside me. — no other studio work — About 6 Ralph \+ I walked to the head of Jones' lake hill — Tea — Eve'g. reading \+ walking piazza — very gusty, fresh air \+ moonlight \+ shadow —
