## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 27, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day, uncomfortably muggy — To studio at 9.30 + spent the m't painting on the Ehninger picture — Lunch at 1 — back at 2 — Afternoon, painted on October Afternoon — a meadow brooke with ducks + think I improved it by adding grey + light. Home at 6. "Gogo" Cook took dinner with us — Eve'g I fooled around, reading in desultory fashion + to bed early. Charl came with his wheel + with the girls + gogo went out riding —

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 28, 1896

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The warmest day yet in the mth. but growing cooler at night — Dull until late in the afternoon when heavy rain began to fall. To studio at 10 — looking over my sketches I determined to make a small painting X to fit a frame that I have, + chose as the subject "Vesuvius from Sorrento" — a bit that I got from a high cliff near Sorrento looking across the bay to Vesuvius. I spent the day making a charcoal study. At the dinner table surprised to find that Mary had gone to P'ok. to spend several days — Evening Charl came in — chatting until 10 —
