## Left Page

### Tues. Feb. 25, 1896

**Weather:** much colder again — clear & windy  **Temp:** 70

studio at about 9.30 & worked all the m'f
on Jany. G — scraping backgrounds, a very
tedious, uninteresting piece of work. Home
at 1 — Ralph out of sorts — indigestion — did
not go to school this m'f. On my way
back to studio stopped & saw Mary
Cook — asked that Louise might play with
Ralph at home, as he could not go out.
Worked until near 5 & quit. G —
Then out for a walk — went to Avery
gallery & saw Mosler pictures — & then
to Ortgies gallery & saw the Reichard
collection — Home before 6.30 — The
usual quiet ev'ef at home.
After the boys got to bed, put in
some time writing up acc'ts for
last year.

Greene 1 day. [illegible — ~3 words]
