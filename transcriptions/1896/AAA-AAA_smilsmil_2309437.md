## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 13, 1896

**Weather:** Sunshine \+ shadow \- some bright sunshine and about 4 p.m. a very sharp, heavy shower \- clearing colder at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I did little else during the day than write a letter giving my reasons for approving the 116th S. site for N.A. \+ D. This I purpose to have printed \+ sent to N.A.s with Report. At 12 I went up to Dr. Allen's in 47th St. \+ had him make some changes form \+ then walked home to lunch, stopping for a few moments at Miller-Small School. 2d \& 42nd St. where I had a short talk with Miss M. To studio after lunch \+ there until 5. Mu home \+ had a rest before dinner. Rory Lizzie Smillie dined with us. Uncle Willie took Bessie to Boston Symphony Concert \- Charles' ticket. J.C. Nicoll came before 8 \+ we went to 57th St. \+ 6th Ave. \- Beckwith's studio \- \+ we got Blashfield to join us \- I read to them my Report and letter \- They were discussed and criticised \+ amendments suggested \- Academy matters were talked over and plans to thwart Perry at the Academy meeting, 25th inst. where he is determined to force the A\- to commit itself against any site above 59th St. were considered. It was about 11 when Nicoll \+ I walked down Fifth Ave. together.

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 14, 1896

Uncle W. \+ aunt A. \- Ret\. to Po'k.
Jim went up to Mr. Walton's over Sunday.

**Weather:** A bright, cold day \- it was the day that was bright, not me, for I got up with a headache that discomforted me throughout the day besides having a sore attack of indigestion at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got to studio soon after 10 \+ to work puzzling over the revision of my Report and letter to embody the suggestions \+ criticisms that I got last night. Home to lunch at 1 \+ after it was detained somewhat chatting with Uncle Willie. He \+ aunt A. \- Ret\. to Poughkeepsie on 3.30 train. It was not until near 4 that I completed revision \- then to O'Brien's printing establishment in 23rd St. \+ left copy \- to have galley pfs. monday evg. Back to studio \- cleaned chain of bicycle. Home at 6 \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Geo. came \+ took Bessie to Philharmonic Concert \- Charles' ticket. I did not feel well enough to go to Century, much to my disappointment. I wished to have a bout with Perry \- It was more comfortable to toast my toes in front of a grate fire.
