## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 8, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — to school with R
then to work upon water-color sketch
of Campainile Lugano — stuck to it
until after 12 — then for R — Lunch
& again upon sketch until 3 o'clk
when I thought that I had finished
it — Then I went down towns to
a leather manuf'g house — 77 Beekman
St. to see if I could get leather to
match that on dining room chairs
— not made now. Then to Charles
saw him for a few minutes — Then
walked to So. Ferry & took 3rd Ave. cars
— out at 14th St — walked up to 84 D —
stopped there — then walked home.
Dinner — evef. Annie took Ck. Collins
to the Opera on tickets from Rosalie But-
ler — I went to meeting of the Etching
Club at Meclatys & studio in 15th St —
we discussed the desperate con-
dition, financially, of the Club — also
the very poor prospects of an exbn —
nothing finally decided — I walked
up home, getting in at 11.20 — waited
until after 12 for A — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 9, 1893

**Weather:** Dull m'f  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull m'f — heavy rain in the afternoon
& evef. I worked again upon sketch
of Campainile, Lugano, making changes
& additions that came to my mind last
night & think the whole thing is much
improved — Then I got to work at
the framing of my Morgan proof which
is wonderfully improved by its dressing
up. Lunch at 1 — after that I
sketched Parchment proof of the Morgan
head — Then I took a nap to recover
some of last night's loss — At 6.30
Fred, Alice & Mollie Adams came
to dinner — In the evef I played
showman, putting my sketches up
upon an easel & discoursing more
or less discursively.
