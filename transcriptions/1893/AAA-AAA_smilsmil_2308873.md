## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 17, 1893

**Weather:** Brilliant & lovely day — mild & cloudless  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant & lovely day - mild & cloud-
less — heavy frost last night. I spent
most of the m'f with Stone, setting out
plants. The lead pipe for our water work
was delivered — Pink & Stone worked
during the m'f hauling dirt — Hill &
a darkie digging trenches — now & then
I ran into the studio & did a little
work on some of my sketches, manag-
ing to give the life out of "Cloisters of
Capucin Convent at Amalfi."
I went down town after 5 o'clk. met
Will Parke who walked up town
with me — stayed to tea & spent
the ev'g with us. I showed him
some of my mounted sketches —
also, did a little work on plans
for frame work for water tank.

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 18, 1893

**Weather:** A lovely day — cloudless, very warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely day — cloudless, very warm
& perfectly quiet. Spent part of the
m'f with Herrick us going over the
plans for frame timbers for tanks &
piazza bath room. Took Stone with
me to school house & to Court House
& set pegs where he is to set out nine
Pyrus japonicas — he was nearly all
day about it. I then went after Oakley
& Rutaur & found them — They promised
to start work for me on Sat. It was
2.30 when I got home — Dinner before
2 — again at work on plans & gave
H— a list of timbers wanted — Then I
went down town & had my hair cut.
Annie went out riding with W. &
Mr. Christman & Mr. Ben. Mulford —
Ev'g, Blessing came up to talk to
me about a Windmill, but I don't
want any — Wrote to Little, sending
him check & complaints.
