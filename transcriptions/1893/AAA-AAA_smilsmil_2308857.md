## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 15, 1893

**Weather:** Heavy rain — very violent thunder storm  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain — very violent thunder storm, deluges of rain, & hail, about 1 o'clk, coming up ago' the easterly storm of the mtf. Clearing late in the afternoon — pleasant evef. I did some writing in the mtf — wrote to Mr. Kant — 2 o'P.O. at noon. Evef at 6.30, Annie, Kate & I went down to Dud Dartrakis's to tea — quite a little tea party, which Mrs. L. made pleasant in her usual successful way — Mr. Christianan Took A — in to the Fable &, turn about, I took in Mrs. C — We left before 10 o'clk & had an agreeable walk up the hill.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 16, 1893

**Weather:** Brilliant, breezy mtf  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant, breezy mtf — cloudy in the middle of the day with some light showers — raw, windy evef & later, some rain. I went down town in the mtf & attended to a number of little things, among them I made arrangements with the Bank for an advance to tide us over our Chicago trip & until we can get our check books that were left in Mtf, so locked up that Geo Cook has not yet been able to get at them. Then I took a walk out to the pumping station at the lake, then home at 1 o'clk. Dinner. In the afternoon I tied up some Cos. Then I took another walk & got in after 6. Tea — Katie T. went to Sayra's for the evef.
