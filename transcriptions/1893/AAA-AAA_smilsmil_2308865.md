## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 1, 1893

**Weather:** Brilliant cloudless day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful kind of day for our first one at home. Bkf. at 9. Spent a part of the morning prowling about. At noon, feeling very sleepy I took a nap before dinner at 1. After that I prepared some notes for Char's guidance at his fair. After 5 o'clk. I had a tramp on the Piazza & a romp with the boys. Tea after 6. Eve'g, still looking over fair catalogues & talking of our experiences.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 2, 1893

**Weather:** Exquisite day — brilliant atmosphere &  **Temp:** [illegible]

wonderful color. After b'kf't. I spent most of the m'g out of doors, looking about & planning work & improvements, the introduction of more extended water privileges being the most important. Went down town before 11 o'clk. & back in time to walk with Kate & Katherine to the train which started at 12.15 carrying them on their way to Chicago. They are to meet Char., Mary & Bessie at Buffalo to-night. Dinner soon after 1. Jule came to the house & is to start digging to-morrow. I had Herrick work at putting up wire netting for the vines on balcony out of Cherry bed. Room. I went down town at 6 — tea on return. Eve'g. looking over back news papers. To bed early as Annie & June are to start for N.Y. soon after 7 to-morrow m'g.
