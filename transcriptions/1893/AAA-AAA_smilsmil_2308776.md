## Left Page

### Sat. April 8, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

much pain in right sided face and head — complicated with a strong streak of bilious headache that prevented me eating any bffo. — & kept me very flat on my back until noon, after which it was much modified. This am f. I sent a specimen of my urine to D. Scotti who came at about noon. He will not swerve from his Caffeine & Salicilate of Soda Treatment & evidently thinks me foolishly mfs about over the pain in my head, for which he has nothing to recommend.

At last the long day was finished. Evely came & Annie read aloud from the Marion Crawford book.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 9, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Feeling much weaker & more hopeless & depressed. Discomfort in my head very persistent, of an entirely different character from that of preceding days — extends all over the head from base of skull, up. — with neuralgia in right cheek & socket of jaw — right side of scalp very sore to touch — all of the catarrhal symptoms have disappeared. Bowels were moved at 11. much skin-like mucus passed. Stomach begins to growl in a portentous manner. Nervous weakness, I suppose. I now sleep a good deal during the day — at 6.30 P.M. I had a remarkably good bit of beef steak, a portion of which I chewed, rejecting the fibre.

Annie wrote to Kate telling her of my sickness — Louisa Burkhardt, the Swiss woman engaged by A — came in the w'f & took the boys out. — Jim is complaining in all sorts of ways, continued from yesterday.
