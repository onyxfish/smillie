## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 2, 1893

**Weather:** Clear, cold & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear, cold & windy — my arm has troubled me much all day, but I have kept on, much discomforted at times by the cold. In the m'f I went to the N.A. p.d & spent some time looking through the loan coll'n. Also with Farrington & got some Ins. policies from him. & with him went to the 2nd Nat. B'k, where he made his deposit. I then went to Safe Deposit — got Annie's coupons & made deposit for her — Then to Hunter's where he fussed for some time with my spectacles — then home to lunch. At 3 went with A. to Panning's & made selections of some furniture covering. Then to Vautnes — to Brockaway's & to Conover's & then home at 5.30. Eve't. A. went to Edw. Cook's while I stayed at home with my lame arm & mounted some old photos. that I took in June '92. A. has rec'd Dr. Valk's bill for treating Jim's eye — $160 — we think it an imposition — Century meeting to night — very sorry to lose it.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 3, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very disagreeable day — snow in the early m'f — then rain & slush — rain at intervals & falling heavily at night. I was glad of the chance to nurse my rheumatic arm & kept warm & quiet all day, accomplishing very little — I commenced to number & make a list of the photos. we brought home with us & to prepare them for mounting.
