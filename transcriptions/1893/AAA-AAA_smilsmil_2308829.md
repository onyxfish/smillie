## Left Page

### Sun. July 23, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, chilly day ending in copious rain
up late - bkfst after 9 - Then I went out
in the park with the boys for a walk.
in at noon & had a nap - Heavy lunch
at 1.30 - After that A - & the boys went to
Hampstead to see Mr. Satchell - I
wrote to Charl until 4.30 - then out &
walked in the park until 6 - then
in & had another nap before Anni's
return at 7 — then we had dinner
Evef. I finished letter to Charl.

## Right Page

### Mon. July 24, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very little sunshine, but much
warmer than yesterday - Bkfst at
8.30 - I went to the museum &
got to work upon early aqua-tints
trying to glean something from a fresh
biography of de Prince & studying a
portfolio of his works - then Maris
Prestel & Paul Sandby - I did not
get thro' with the works of the latter
when the Print Room was closed at
5 o'clk. At 1 o'clk. I made a short
call on Dr. Garnett - After leaving
the museum I walked down Shaftesbury
Ave to Piccadilly & then up Regents St -
Tried to get some magnifying glasses
for my work but could not find what
I wanted - but I bought a pair of hair
brushes - Home it 6.30 - dinner at 7 -
Annie as well as Jim & Ralph are
somewhat out of sorts
