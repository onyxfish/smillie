## Left Page

### Sat. June 17, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Lovely day, but very warm. After bkfs. I went to the Rialto & spent the mng there finishing a few sketches from the bridge looking over the fruit market. I stopped at 12.30 and walked back to hotel — A. & E. had been out shopping for lace & had visited the day and hours on the Grand Canal — lunch — after that I rested until 3 — then again to Rialto & commenced a new sketch — the bridge seen from under arches at N. end — worked until 5 & then walked around the N. & W. end of the city doing a good deal of tramping but without finding much that was very picturesque — Took boat at the Jesuitas landing & got to hotel at 6.30 — dinner — at 7.30 we all went out in a gondola for an hour. Glided around S. Giorgio & then a little way up the Grand Canals. Evening very mild & quiet — water still and color reflections very beautiful — saw lots of pictures to paint — but never, never will my — later I spent sometime on our balcony & saw, in the deepening twilight, nocturnes that would knock Jimminy Whistler galley west.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 18, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A brilliant hot day in spite of a pleasant breeze. After bkfs. I went to the 9 o'clk. high mass at S. Marcos & spent a delightful hour wafting about there listening to the music — chorus of men's voices — very good, but too much ornamentation to give fine musical effects — and studying the interior — the marble illustrations & the various & many splendid compositions from & color seen from different points — after the service I went into the Ducal Palace & then met E., A. & the boys — after some time there, the boys went back to the hotel while E., A. & I went to the steamboat & to the Accademia, which to our disgust we found closed because to-day there is a municipal election. Then back to our hotel, stopping nowhere. Lunch at 1 — then a rest — In the course of the afternoon I went to a barber's & had my hair cut — then I prowled about the streets & canals until dinner time, then back to hotel — Immediately after dinner on our swgga listening to the music of the band on the us not far from us until — then until 2. A. went to the Piazza to listen to the band then we the Grays, if possible, to get some ice cream. I was fooled & got some miserable lemon water-ice instead — We lemonaded about until 10.30 admiring the very juvenile moon then to our hotel & to bed.
