## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 24, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A charming day, altho' quite cold —
Had at 20 min. walk before bkfp —
Charl, Annie & I Ret'd. by 9.27 Train, getting
into NY. at 11.30 — Went for Ralph at 12 ½ —
Jim met with another accident yesterday
injuring his right hand in some play in
the street — Lunch at 1 — I worked on my
pen sketches as long as I could see, then
Ralph & I took a walk up town —
Dinner at 6.30 — Evg. I mounted
six more of my sketches & did not
get to bed until near midnight.

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 25, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, breezy & cold. I worked on my
pen sketches all day & late in the after-
noon I went to N.A. of D. & saw Farring-
ton about making a proper schedule of
accts & papers transferred to me by
Alfred Jones. Evg. — Dinner at 7 —
George Cook & Emily with us. The
eighth anniversary of their wedding.
Later — Jno. Valentine came in.
