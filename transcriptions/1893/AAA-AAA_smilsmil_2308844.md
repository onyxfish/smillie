## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 20, 1893

**Weather:** Cool, fresh  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cool, fresh day, most of the time cloudy
— a shower in the afternoon. Spent the
m'f indoors — wrote part of a letter to Sam
Colman — It was after 1 o'clk. when I
went out into Kensington gardens for a walk.
Back to dinner at 2 — Immediately after
it Annie & the boys went to Hampstead.
I wrote for a while but was troubled very
much by a severe headache — Rheumatic
it seems to me in character — Between 5 &
6 I walked — Tea — wrote a little
while in the ev'g.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 21, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day after near two showers
in the m'f before 10 — I got up feeling very
seedy — at about 10 I started for Mrs. M—
but felt so out of sorts that I returned to the
house & stretching myself on my bed I spent
the rest of the n'f there, sleeping most of
the time — a little before 1 I took a light
lunch & feeling better I went to the museum
getting there at 1.30 & until four o'clk. I
puzzled over the Prince Rupert prints trying
to form a theory as to how they were done.
Then I went to Newman's, Soho Square
& was there until near 6 selecting water
colors, paper etc — Then I walked home
& had a short rest before dinner — Ev'g
I finished letter to Colman.
