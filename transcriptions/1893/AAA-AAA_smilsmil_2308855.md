### Tues. Sept. 12, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day. Got up in the eve'g, washed & dressed like a Christian & came down to my steps. Feeling weakish I loafed about all day — interviewed Herrick & gave him further directions concerning the swing he is putting up for the boys — also, Mr Stone, with whom I discussed the silver question without settling it.
