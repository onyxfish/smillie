## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 6, 1893

**Weather:** Bright, pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkft. I went up to Dr. Yale's — saw him for a few minutes & made an appointment to call on him in the eve'g. Then walked down 4th Ave. as far as 26th S. went into D. B. Butler's — to 3d. Ave. to Station "7" — met Nellie in the street — Home before 12. Then to Mrs. Walton's Kindergarten in 42d. S. & got R — lunch after 1 — + after that I went to Geo. Flint's. W. 14th S. met H — , bought bureau — left him & went to 51 W. 10th S. & made a long call on J. C. Nicoll & talked over the Treas. ship of N.A.D. Then went to two places to find Deelman — & did not — then to Washington Sq. & took a look at the Arch by Twilight — Walked all the way home — dinner before 7 — and immediately after I went to Dr. Yale's & spent two hours with him — most of the time discussing my physical condition — He is going to try a course of treatment for gen. catarrh — upon m Home at 10.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 7, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkft. took Ralph to his school in 42d. S. Then to Century Club in 43d. S. — there met Shirlaw & with him had a confabulation as we looked at the meagre show of pictures in the Gallery. As I left I met Linton, hale, hearty & enthusiastic. I went around by way of 6th Ave. Home after 10 o'clk — worked a while at Reducing Chaos — then Walter, Louise & little Mary came and stayed until after 12 — then I went for R — more work on return & before lunch at 1 o — The same work continued during the afternoon — about 4.30 I went out for a walk — on my return Geo. & Emily at the house — (Charl. called for a few minutes about 1 o'clk.) Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g Chas. Collins called — & spent a pleasant two hours.
