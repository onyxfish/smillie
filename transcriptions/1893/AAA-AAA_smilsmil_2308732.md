## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 10, 1893

*Lyons, Hotel Univers. Rooms 20 · 21.*

**Weather:** Rainy m[orning]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rainy m[orning]. we spent the whole of it indoors getting ready to leave. lunch at 11.30 — Eli H. + Mrs. K. came at that time. also the family bus. we got away from the house at 12.15 + got to the Gare de Lyons at about 1. Then we had 45 m. to get baggage weighed etc. etc. — we got away on time — just before we left a poor inner very far gone with some pulmonary trouble, got into our compartment + made us more or less [illegible] — uncomfortable all of the rest of the way — The journey was entirely without interest of any kind — we got to Dijon on time at 6/43 + [illegible] 25 m. for dinner — fairly good, but I got two [illegible] having [illegible] enjoy it. Then on to Lyons where we am. on time at 10.23 — having got through with the 8¾ hours from Paris very comfortably — we did not suffer at all from cold — but, owing to the presence of our sick fellow traveller, we did suffer for want of fresh air — A 'bus took us at once to the hotel and a very short distance from the R.R. station — we found everything in readiness for us — fine large room + [illegible] one of it — plain wood fires in both — [illegible] also had a good room — just across the hall + a good fire — we toasted ourselves for a few minutes — had some lemonade — good tea + [illegible] ones to bed + then followed ourselves —

### Wed. Jan. 11, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very little blue sky at rare intervals — a cold, gray sky with a strong bitter wind + clouds of [illegible] — [illegible] with our early [illegible]. at 9.30 + at about 10.30 we started out to see some sights through the principal [illegible] + squares — to the Bourse + other [illegible] buildings — the church of S. Nizier — the City Hall + to the Palais S[t.] Pierre or des Arts where we took a rain [illegible]. some of the galleries — Then out to the quais along the Saone + back to the hotel at 1 when we had a good lunch — After that we toasted ourselves for a while + then again out — we crossed the Pont de Tilsit + by a short cable R.R. got nearly to the top of the Fourviére, a height overlooking the city (400 f.) + crowned by a couple of churches — an older, Chapelle de N.D. de Fourviére + the yet incomplete church of N.D. de F. — the two adjoin — the church is an immense overly ugly building upon which great sums of money are being expended — we went into the interior where strong cutting + gilding are now going briskly on — There is much beautiful mechanical work + there are some effects of color from that can be commended, but as a whole it is artistically to be deplored — we climbed to the roof [illegible] despite of wind + bleakness were much interested in the fine view over the heights, the rivers + the city + the plains beyond — but we could not see Mt. Blanc, less than 100 m. east of us — Then we sauntered down through gardens full of old Roman remains — The palace is full of them — this was a very important Roman stronghold at one time — We got down on the wrong side of the hill + had to return by a long, circuitous route to the Cathedral of S[t.] Jean where we got when the light of day was so far gone that we could see but little of the interior — The rest of the party here took a cab + rode back to the hotel while I walked — at a little before [illegible] we had a good dinner — after that, chatted [illegible] a while before our cheery wood fire — Then I wrote awhile + to bed at 10.
