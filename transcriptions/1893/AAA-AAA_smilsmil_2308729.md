## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 4, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A change — thick, foggy weather — a light fall of snow & then — aloft — altho' the Ther. was a little higher the air was very raw & unpleasant. Yesterday I left Durand's, forgetting to take with me a proof that he gave me of Jones's etched portrait of his (D's) father. So I walked out there again, getting there at noon — got proof — & then walked thro' Luxembourg gardens down to Boul. S. Germain — then after waiting a long time for tram car I took a cab — lunch — After that, Ralph stayed with the Kimball boy O'delay — & Annie, Julie & I took cab to the dentist's Dr. Davenport — Ave. de L'Opera — then I went to Drexel, Harjes & Co & drew some money — then back to D's — where I left Annie — took Jim & we went down to the Louvre & wandered thro' the sculptures & picture galleries until they closed at 4 — we could not see anything it was so dark — Then we walked home — Annie getting in at about the same time — Dinner — Eve. — I wrote a note to Mielatz concerning the clue etchings for the British Museum —

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 5, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still overcast, with efforts at clearing. The air exceedingly chilly & Ther. down about freezing. The usual mlf. experiences & then about 10, Annie & I walked down to the Place de la Concorde where we separated — she going to the dentist's — I going on, thro' the gardens of the Tuileries to the Louvre where I wandered thro' the galleries until after 12 — then home by tram car — A. did not get in until late, having had a hard time at the dentist's — I went in to see the Stevens Panorama of the Century's great Frenchmen — a fine work — if there had been light enough to see it — After lunch — A., J. & R. went to see [illegible] K. while I wandered over the Champs de Mars, looking at the old Exposition buildings & the Eiffel Tower — then to the Trocadero — thence home just as A. & the boys got near, when we had dinner — Eve. — reading guide books & a little writing —
