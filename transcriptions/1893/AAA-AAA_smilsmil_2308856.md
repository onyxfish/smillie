## Left Page

### Wed. Sept. 13, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant & very mild but with threats of a change of weather.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fireman's Parade — Lots of brass-
band & other noise — I did little
else than loaf about being with-
out ambition. In the after-
noon Mary & Kate came up —
Mary stayed to tea. All came
in the evef & stayed until after
10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Thur. Sept. 14, 1893

**Weather:** Dull, cloudy day — east wind & occasional showers.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Many & Bessie left early
this mf for N.Y. & Po'keepsie & in the
afternoon Kate & Katherine came to
the house & took up their abode.
I wandered about, downtown in
the mf & again in the afternoon
Towards evef I walked out to
the lake to see the pumping station
of the new waterworks & then up
on the hill to the Reservoir — Every
thing connected with these water-
works looks to me very mean and
slip-slop. Evef, general
desultory chat.
