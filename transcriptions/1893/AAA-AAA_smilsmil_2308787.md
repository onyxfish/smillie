## Left Page

### Sun. April 30, 1893

**Weather:** Heavy rain during the night, overcast morning  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain during the night, overcast morning
— sultry — beautiful afternoon — much more
life in the air — Had a walk on the Terrace
before bkp. & a good long one afterwards.
Then worked for a little while with pen &
ink on a Terrace sketch — another walk
before lunch — After that, with A. & boys
went to a point above hotel & got a
sketch taking in hotel, Terraces & cliffs
to the west — After 4 o'clk. came down
& had an hours walk on Terrace, making
a pretty good sum total of walking for
the day. Had a rest before dinner with
the boys at 6.30 — Suelf. A. & I did
some moonlight sauntering on the
Terrace.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 1, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day, altho' at times somewhat
clouded in the m'f. S'r Yozzi telephoned
down for the old Capuchin monk. Padre
Antonio, & he came up to pose for me
before 10 o'clk. He took his position
(sitting upon a stone seat) & he did it
budge for an hour. I got a few study
of him that I think is pretty good for
me. I had a short walk on the Terrace
before bkp. & for nearly an hour before
lunch — After that went to rock over-
looking convent & finished the sketch I
commenced yesterday — then, before
5 again on Terrace & walked until
about 6 — glorious afternoon. Dinner
with the boys at 6·15 Suelf wrote
a letter to Herrick in answer to one
rec'd. from him to-day.
