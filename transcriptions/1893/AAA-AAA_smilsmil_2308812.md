## Left Page

### Mon. June 19, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another cloudless, hot day — Ther. in our room 88°. (78°) At 10 o'clk. took steamboat to the Rialto & selected a position for a new sketch — from the Flower Market, looking up the Rialto steps. I made myself agreeable to a young woman who let me sit where I got some shade from her awning. I worked faithfully until 12.30 & then walked back to the hotel — lunch — Owing to the heat of the day I did not feel like going out to work again in the afternoon, so I took a rest & then worked on my morning sketch until near 5 when I went out with Annie to look at photos — we found them very poor & cheap and unsatisfactory — bought some & back to hotel in time for dinner at 6.30 — After that E., A. & the boys went out for an hour in a gondola but as I have been bothered to-day by rheumatic pains in my left wrist I decided to stay at home — Evening, a band played on the Riva very near our hotel. =

At 9 A. + E. went to the Piazza to get ice or cream — I begged off as I am not well enough to be able to enjoy seeing others do what I cannot do.

On the Piazza this afternoon A. & I met E. A. Abbey, who has been with his wife for the past six weeks at the Grand Hotel.

## Right Page

### Tues. June 20, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The warmest day yet — Ther. in our room up to 80° in the afternoon. a strong, warm breeze — sirocco, probably — After 11½°. I worked for some time on pen sketch — Rialto Bridge from Flower Market, working up parts that I could do without the original before me — it was about 11 when I got to the market & commenced work — then, for an hour & half I jotted down the figures as I saw them, some with pencil, some with pen — walked to hotel at 12.30 — lunch at 1 — Then a rest — from 3 to 5 worked on morning's sketch, filling in the figures etc. — Then with Annie went to the S.M. Salute & from there to the picturesque Court de S. Gregorio — found it used as a stone cutters yard for a big building in course of erection near it — on our way back I bought some more photos — Dinner before 7 — Evg. after 9 — E., A. & I went to Piazza S. Marco — they had ice cream — I had Vienna Beer.
