## Left Page

### Thur. March 23, 1893

**Weather:** Dull, clouded m/f, threatening rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

to our great disappointment for this was the day we had planned for a trip to [illegible], Lago di Nemi, Frascati, [illegible] places — As we had to start from the Hotel at 8.45 & the weather even then unpromising we gave it up — later the weather cleared very pleasantly in a most aggravating way! I went out and did a little shopping, buying a number of photos. Lunch at 1 — Afternoon I began to feel the effects of a coming influenza — I kept it down — did some sorting of my things preparatory to a rearrangement of our trunks for the Naples trip — Reg. & Annie went off for the afternoon. A, bot. some imitation bronzes — a cupid & a door knocker. Evef. a little musical play & a dance at the Pension Michel — I was not able to go out, my nose needing my entire attention. So Annie went alone, by cab, there & back at 11.30.

## Right Page

### Fri. March 24, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — Up early after a fevered and restless night — splitting headache — To-day we were to start for Naples but my condition makes it impossible — my disquiet & my discomfort were about equal — I loafed about the room all the m/f — sometimes on the bed — then in an easy chair — Temp. 100°. Ely came in & after a talk with Annie, at my suggestion, at 11.30 they started for Frascatti taking the boys with them. I had my b/fo. & my lunch in my room — I was able in the course of the m/f to do some packing — After lunch I wrote part of a letter to Geo. A & the boys came back at 5.30 having had a very satisfactory pic nic. At 6.30 I went down to dinner with them — Spent the evef. reading & at 10, to bed.
