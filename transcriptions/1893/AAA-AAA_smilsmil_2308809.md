## Left Page

### Tues. June 13, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day — cloudless, fresh & cool. Had an early [illegible] & before 9 o'clk were at S. marks to see the procession forming for the festa of S. Anthony. It was funny to see the old fellows, candles & banner bearers, adorning themselves in different colored shirts while in the lobby — they were a rum lot, with their old hats & caps on & smoking — some after the procession formed & made a very pleasiug spectacle with its richly colored banners, the massive candles, trucks, instruments, etc. etc. — we preceded it across the Piazza, through the narrow streets, over the temporary boat bridge to S. M. della Salute, where we straggled than the greater part of a high mass, sung by a choir of men. While sitting in the ch. listening to the music & where I could look out of the great doors over the canal crouded with gondolas I was much impressed by the kneeling figure of a bald headed & kneeling man, saying his prayers in most devout fashion & holding a great bunch of white lilies that he had for sale. He was a picture. I left A. & Z. & spent what remained of the m'f wandering thro the S.W. portion of the city. I got and made a sketch & went into a number of churches but found no material that made me willing to get at it. Lunch at 1 — after that a visit — Ms. Appleton & Miss Parsons called & at their urgent request I showed them my new sketches. Then A. Z. the boys & I went to the Campanile of S. marks & climbed to the top & had a beautiful view of everything except a portion of the mis. to the N.W. They were partially hidden by thunder clouds — after that I again wandered along in the S.E. portion of the city until dinner at 6.30 — Evening Reading newspapers & guide Book —

While on the G. canal to-day A. met Mr. & Miss Gray & della Martine — They are at the Grand Hotel.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 14, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day — It was about 10 o'clk when we all got started upon our days work — went first to S. Zaccaria, just back of our hotel and there I was very much interested in the painting "an altar" "Virgin enthroned" by Giov. Bellini — charming in color & composition — a surprise & treat to me — Then around by S. Giovanna [illegible], but as it was not open we did not try to get in, but went on to S. M. Formosa to see the Santa Barbara by Palma Vecchio — a very noble painting but in color it did not appeal to me as the Bellini Virgin did — Then we went to look at the quaint old Porta de Paradiso nearby & gave a youngster a few soldi because he stripped like a circus rider & jumped into the canal from the bridge — Then on to S. Giovanni e Paolo where we spent some time looking at the Tombs of the Doges etc. We went into the Cappella del Rosario, destroyed by fire in 1867 & now being "Restored" in a very decorative way — a sad wreck of what must have been a very fine example of its style, in the Campo, the celebrated statue of Colleone — Then we walked to the north part of the city, the Fondamenta Nuove & from there saw the island Campo Santo — Munch around to the great church I dei Santi, lined by encrusted & inlaid marble in set pattern giving the appearance of being covered with chintone or other textile stuff — we saw the Titian martyr [illegible] of S. Laurenlius, exceeding darkened by age — Often by gondola, we returned to hotel getting in a little before lunch — after that a rest; at 3 I went to Zaccaria & began a new sketch, working until 5 — Then walked about Rialto, until 6.30 — Dinner — every at 8.30. Z. A. & I went out in a gondola — lovely effects from S. Giorgio looking towards the city & the west — then up Grand Canal — Singing parties with instruments & gondola with many Chinese lanternes — an audience in gondolas about them — palaces, domes, campaniles, lights & shimmering reflections — music — delightful combination. In the deepening twilight — Came as home at 9.30 & went to Piazza S. Marco — Band playing — a great, gay crowd — A. & S. had ices — I looked on — Back to hotel at 10.30.
