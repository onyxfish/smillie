## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 26, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright & beautiful day — most of the
m't spent hanging pictures, fixing window-
shades, etc — Walter & Louise came in for a
few minutes, then Paul. Colman & Mr. C. —
then Edw'd & Mary — At 4½ Ralph & I took
a run up to 44th St & Mad. Ave — then home.
At 2 we dined — Sligth Howe. Eugene. [illegible]
& Jack Downs with us — At 5 o'clk, I
walked up to George's — met Charl then
& we walked down together — Tea at 7 —
Eve'g, Charl came in & we talked
together over George's affairs until near
11 o'clk — 12 where I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 27, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day with heavy rain at night.
After taking R. to school I got at my
work on pen sketches & kept at it until I
went for R. at 12½ — after lunch I
worked until 3 — then feeling loss of
sleep I had a short nap before going
down to Chas. W. Nichols's — 153 E. 15th St —
where I also met Alex. I chilling at
4.30 — Supposed to be a meeting of
the Cat. Com. of the N.Y. Etching Club —
we talked over the bankrupt condition
of the Club — left them about 5 — walked
home — dinner — then to K.A. of D. —
Council meeting — there until near
11 — Ge° walked up with me in the
rain that was just beginning to fall —
midnight when I got to bed.
