## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 24, 1893

**Weather:** Cloudy most of the day — air chill  **Temp:** [illegible]

my finger discharged a little pus at the root of the nail. This mff + was much better — I spent part of the mff making pencil sketch looking up a narrow street in the old town. Annie & Jim went to dentist. Dejeuner at 12.30 — The new French teacher came in the afternoon & took the boys out walking. I also took a short walk before I set to work with color on the sketch I commenced this mff. When I could see no longer to work, I took another walk — Dinner at 6 ½. Evef wrote a letter to Kate.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 25, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day — cloudless & very mild. I, however, was not in tune with the day, having a discomfort under the belt, so that I spent most of the morning arranging colors on my W. C. palette. I rec'd letters from Miss Abbatt, Mr. Davis, Sect'y of Soc'ty of Eng'd. a Word & from Farrer. I went out to the Promenade before noon & sat for some time sunning myself.
