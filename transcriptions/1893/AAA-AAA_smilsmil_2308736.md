## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 18, 1893

**Weather:** Brilliant & cloudless - warm in the sunshine very cool in the shade  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant & cloudless - warm in the sunshine very cool in the shade - 9 o'clk. before we had [illegible] music again - man with violin - woman with trained voice, operatic manner & much gesticulation - Her yodel was very good, so we dropped sons to her from our balcony. It was 10 o'clk. when I started out with my sketch book & went to the flower market where I found a very good subject & stood for an hour making a careful outline of it - then, being both tired & cold I started walking through the old town & found some other subjects that I wished to get sketches of - then out into the Quai du Midi along to the Promenade - home at 12 15 - Sat for awhile & listened to Mlle Calcus the new French teacher for the boys - She cannot speak a word of English - Jim struggled with good intent & even stole Ralph's made efforts — it will not be long before they "catch on." Pajanen before 1 2. After that about some time getting my sketching materials in order to begin work — A— had gone out with Jim as he wished to do some sketching and Ralph had stayed at home with me - we went out at about 3 o'clk. to the Public Garden where an excellent band was playing - we walked up & down the Promenade des Anglaises - before the sun got low, as it was growing chilly. Ralph, as well as A— & J—, has a bad cold, we ret. to the hotel where I worked as long as I could see on my sketch of the [illegible] — At 5.30 I took my tea with the boys, not feeling equal to a Table d' hote — Evng, wrestling with French verbs & a little writing.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 19, 1893

**Weather:** Another bright, beautiful day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another bright, beautiful day — Ralph at 9 — out soon after 10 — Annia went with Jim to the beach that he might do some sketching in his new sketch book. I went again to the flower garden & worked there — saw the [illegible] with fence — a brisk walk — to warm up & then to hotel at 12 15. Rest soon after — Then I worked awhile on sketches & after 3 went to Pub. Garden & listened awhile to the band — then walked down the Quai du Midi, around the cafe to the point where I met Annia & the boys on their return — A. took R— with her to the hotel & J— & I went onto the basin of the port & spent some time looking at Norfolk boats & steam yachts — then to hotel, getting in soon after 5 o'clk. — Dinner after 6 — Evng, reading and napping in our rooms - Eliz. with us —

Ther. now varies bet. 38° & 55°
