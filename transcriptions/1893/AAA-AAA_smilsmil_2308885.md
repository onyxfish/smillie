## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 10, 1893

**Weather:** Brilliant, beautiful winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got up with a stupid headache after a very uncomfortable & restless night. After taking R. to school went down town, stopping at N & V's Canal St. & arranging to take pfp. on one of their presses as I need them. Then walked the rest of the way — got Diary for '94 — then to Dept. of Commerce — then to Drexel, Morgan & Co — then to 52 Wall St. — then to Charles & saw him — then to A.D.N. Co. & saw Jones & [illegible] Doe — then to 23rd St. & left bonds in Safe Deposit — then walked home. Lunch at 1.30 — not good for much during the p.m. — had a troubled nap. After 5 o'clk. had a call from Dr. Gerster who was very enthusiastic over pt. of Morgan head & few sketches. 7 o'clk. when we had dinner. Well, wrote note to W. K. Vickery — sent his pfp. of Sandrias Ray pfp. to him today by Wells, Fargo & Co.

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 11, 1893

**Weather:** Beautiful day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Attended to marketing after bkfp. then to work on Fauk plans — after 12 I went to barber's & enjoyed the luxury of a first class shampoo — home for lunch at 1.30 — after lunch I worked on plans for outside finish of bath-room, Montrose. Until about 4.30 — then I went out for a walk, choked by heart-burn & almost bursting with flatulence. It seems to me that I am suffering now more functional disturbance along the digestive tract than ever before. The trouble commenced in London, more than a year ago. I walked up to 59th St. & went in to see George's new quarters for a very few minutes — home in time for dinner at 6.30. Katie Adams there — after it, she & Annie went to see Irving at Abbey's new theatre — I spent part of the evef. looking over Montrose accts. covering the time while we were abroad. Annie & Katie came in after 11 — A. & I went with K. to the door of the Fred. Melaud apartment house. It was 12 o'clk. when we got to bed.
