## Left Page

### Wed. May 10, 1893

*Sorrento - Villa Mayo -*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain + wind in the early m'f., but fine weath
er when I got up soon after 6. Bklp. alone at
7 - then I went to Piazza + made two sketches
+ got back to Hotel before A + the boys had finished
their bklpt. Settled accts. + stopped at Albeyo
Pagano to see the paintings by different visiting
artists - Then to Piazza before 9 o'elk - + rode down
to the marina where we engaged about 4 four
rowers + made the "giro" of the island - we got
away before 10 - brilliantly clear - strong breeze
from S. E., water quite rough - very pleasant to
me but A. was uncomfortable + poor Ralph
had to give up all of his bklp. about an hour
after we started - The motion + color of the water
on the S. side of the island exceedingly fine, but
the two grottos, white + green, don't just - to
much. The Faraglioni Rocks are very fine
out much of the stone is forbidding without
being grand or picturesque - much is sug-
gestive of nothing but sterility - when we got
to the N. side of the island into smooth water
Ralph recovered promptly. We made another
visit to the Blue Grotto + saw more than we
did yesterday. Then completed our journey
+ got to the Marina at 12.30 - after a hard tug
for the boatmen + a very interesting trip for
us. Got our lunch at a subcaroale of [illegible]
Suissana - at 1.30 we drove up to Anacapri,
a wonderful drive part of the way along the
face of a tremendous cliff. The air and the
atmosphere were delicious - we had only a
glimpse of the little old town then turned
about + rattled down the hill to the Marina
getting there in one hour + 5 m. after our start
- in ample time for our steamer at 3 o'clk -
crossing the afen but. The island + point of
Sorrento for tomorrow she rolled very heavily
- got to Sorrento at 4 - + at once to Villa Mayo.
Then to Mr. Banker's + drew $50 - Then to
our room + rested for an hour before dinner.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 11, 1893

*Pompeii - Pension Suisse -*
*Rooms 2 + 3.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - clouds hanging on the mt. Tofas + at
times spreading extensively - I got up early
+ before bklp. wrote part of a letter to Kate -
after bklp. finished it - then out + wandered
about - pleased at some parts just outside the
old city walls - looked over some photos + but
a few - Then in + did a little packing before
lunch - after lunch, finished pklp. + started
but at about 2.10 started in laudan for Pompeii
- trouble with the horses at starting - balking -
horses breaking - Great festa - road thronged
with people - on foot + in vag[?]s covered with
green boughs, white sheets + wild flowers - men
+ women - old + young, decked out in their best,
many of them promenading in harmonious and
singing lustily. At one place, in front of a
wine shop, dancing was going on with great
vigor. The road was terribly dusty + every-
thing was disfigured by the white powder -
The portion around the face of the cliff east
of Castellamare Meta, then on past Vico Equense
is full of very picturesque material but on a
hazy, sirocco day, after a dry spell of fourteen
weeks the conditions for seeing it were not favor-
able. The approach to Castellamare, through
+ on to Pompeii, is uninteresting - altho' under
some lights the mts. to the SE might be fine - we
got to Pompeii at about 4.45 + stopped at the
Pension Suisse, a mean, prowsy, dirty little place
but as good as any other of the three houses here
at any rate it is exceedingly cheap — we
had dinner before 7 + managed to dawdle
away the every + got to bed early.
