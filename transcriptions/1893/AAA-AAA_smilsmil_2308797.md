## Left Page

### Sat. May 20, 1893

**Weather:** Bright & breezy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright & breezy - at 10.30. we all started out in cab & drove first to the Cappuccini & saw the ghastly & amusing decorations made of human bones & shrivelled corpses of deceased Capuchins - then to S.M. Maggim - then to S. Clementine & went down into the ancient ch., underneath - then to the Colosseum & climbed to the upper galleries - then to the Borghese Palace & went through the series of rooms where there is a large amount of all sorts of truck & frequent auction sales. The original Borghese collection has all been sold - then we returned to hotel just in time for lunch - While at lunch Mary on her husband made us a brief call & Annie gave to M. the wedding present that she had for her the other day - At 3 o'clk. I went up on the Pincio with my man to hold an umbrella over me & I worked on a sketch from the top looking over the Piazza del Popolo - The wind was so strong that I could not work & the man could hardly control the umbrella - I stuck to it until 4 o'clk & then gave it up & returned to the hotel where I continued the work until 5.30 when I went up on the Pincio & listened to the music - Annie had gone riding taking Miss Joshay & the boys with her - they met me at 6.15 & after strolling about for awhile we all went to the hotel. Dinner at 7 - Eve. & I worked on [a] sketch.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 21, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Sunshine & showers - hot & close in the middle fresher in the afternoon - Showers could be seen about us in the mts., but none fell upon us until the afternoon, then we had two or three light ones - at 10 I walked down to the Piazza di Spagna & up the steps & along to the Pincio where I took my last, loving look - Back to hotel soon after 11 & then set to work to pack by taking everything out of my trunk & assorting things - Shower at 1 - Lunch - Annie had been with the boys to S. John Lateran to hear the music of the morning service & was much pleased - After lunch commenced to put things into my trunk - Two or three showers prevented us from starting on a ride until 3 o'clk - then we went to San Pietro in Montorio, hoping to get the view that so charmed us when first we came to Rome, but showers hung in veils over the distant hills & so that the effect was spoiled - however, it was bright, sunny & fresh & the ride in itself was a pleasure - We drove back by San Onofrio & S. Peters - Dinner at 6.30 after which I had a nap - then Annie & I finished our packing, getting through at 10.30 -
