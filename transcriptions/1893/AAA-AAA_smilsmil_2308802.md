## Left Page

### Tues. May 30, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely day — Soon after help. E., A. & I
Rose down to the Piazza d'Signoria & then
walked to the Michael Angelo house when
we looked at the few interesting things to
be seen there — Then to Saute Croce & took
a second look at Church & cloisters. In
the latter I was much impressed by a
"subject" & determined to return to it in
the afternoon — Then to the Palazzo Vecchio
& went through the halls & museum of
that building — then took another look
at the statues on the outside of S. Michele
Then home — Lunch — After that I got
my traps together & got to S. Croce cloisters
about 2 & worked until 5 upon pen sketch
then walked home, meeting by the way
Eliz. H — & doing some shopping with her.
Dinner at 7 — It was near 10 when E—
A. & I walked to the Cascine or Park
not far from the house. Annie was
seeking for a nightingale — but nary a
nightingale appeared, but the moon
light & the dark & even the yellow Arno
were very lovely & we strolled until
10.30 — Then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 31, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast in mf. that by 1 o'clk developed into
thunder showers that kept up heavily & almost
continuously until evening — The thunder was
exceedingly heavy at times — About 10 A., E., & I
went to the Uffizi Pal. & spent the mf. There
got home about 12.30 just in time to escape
a shower — It was 4 o'clk before the rain
ceased so that I could go out. Then I went to
Alinari's, the [illegible] & spent
nearly two hours looking over photos — &
finally selected 15 — I had considerable
trouble in getting home as more showers
came up & it Rivers [illegible] — I got from
place to place — part of the way in a bus
at last got home before 7 — Then Dinner.
Eve'g in Salon, reading.
