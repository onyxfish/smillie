## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 8, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant m'g, growing overcast & rain with rain at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Took Ralph to his school after d'f'o, then to Manhattan Storage Co. & got tin box from my cell there. Home & then out again to market etc. — Then to 2°. Nat. Bk., on my way stopped in to see Dr. Gerster & had a very pleasant welcome. Then to bank & safe deposit — Got through just in time to go for R. — at 12.30 — Lunch at 1.30 — among other things, in the afternoon I looked over the Santuis Rey p.p. sent to me by Vickery — 34 of them — I signed 15. & retained one & then fixed them up again to return — Before I went to 3°. Ave. & bat. arable to work on & some other things — Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g wrote a letter to Herrick.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 9, 1893

**Weather:** grey day, clearing towards ev'g.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Took R. to school in the m'g & on my way stopped at Chase's & handed to him five gre-[at] thousand dollar bonds Toledo, S. Dories & Kansas City R.R. that road now being in trouble — Got a letter from Herrick asking for certain instructions — added them to my letter written last night & mailed it — Worked on plan for water tank frame. Went for R. at 12.30 — Lunch at 1.30 — again at work on plans — At 4.30 with R., walked to Barrey & 23°. S., Wells Fargo & Co's Exff'o. — arranged to have them call for Vickery's p.f. To-morrow. Then walked up Bdway — looking at the new buildings — Herald — Abbey's Theatre — etc — Home at 6 — Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g, quite useless for some unknown reason — to bed soon after 9.
