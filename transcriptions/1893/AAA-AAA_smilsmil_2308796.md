## Left Page

### Thur. May 18, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant, altho' overcast, until about 4 o'clk.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant, altho' overcast, until about 4 o'clk. when we had some light showers. Before bkfp- wrote awhile on letter to Ge. after bkp- Annie, the boys & I went to the G. Hotel de Rome on the Corso & were successful in finding Marjory Spring Ramsey & her brown husband — After a half hour call on them we went to some photo places & on the Piazza d' Spagna Annie bought a gold pin as a present for Marjory — then the boys went to the Pincian Hill & we went to the Cappuccini Ch. but being closed we could not get entrance — to lunch at one — after that we went to the Colonna Palace & spent a short time in its marble & historic halls — then leaving A. to take Jim to the dentist's I rel'd to hotel, got my sketching things & went on the Pincian Hill, but the wind & a shower drove me in before I had accomplished anything — Dinner at 6.30 — Evey, finished letter to George.

## Right Page

### Fri. May 19, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant — At 10 o'clk. Annie & I [and] Ralph got to the Vatican Library & had to wait a quarter of an hour for the doors to open — then, with a guide, we made the extensive tour & saw all, more than is set down in Baedecker — The guide delivered us safely at the gate / entrance at about 11, then we got tickets for the museum & spent another hour in making a hasty review — then back to hotel, I stopping at the Piazza di Spagne to make enquiries about sending a trunk to Southampton — Lunch — at 1 — after that I made a pen sketchy out of our hotel window over the Piazza del Popalo — Soon after 3 I went up to the terrace on the Pincian to make a sketch but, altho' the day was warm, a cool breeze was blowing so hard that I did not dare stand still in it for any length of time & very reluctantly I returned to the Hotel — & worked on sketch taken from our windows — Dinner at 6.30 — Evey sat with open windows waiting for Marjory & her husband.
