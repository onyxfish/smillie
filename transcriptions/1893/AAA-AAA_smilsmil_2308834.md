## Left Page

### Mon. July 31, 1893

**Weather:** Raw & chilly  **Temp:** [illegible]

Raw & chilly — a little sunshine and
a little rain — In the m'f wrote a note
to Mr. J. Pierpoint Morgan telling him that I
expected to be in N.Y. next Nov. & that I was
anxious to get to work upon the etched por-
trait of his father with the original painting
before me. Walked down to the museum
& about the m'f studying the mezzatints of
Tarlow — especially the two plates of "Fruit" &
"Flowers" after Van Huysum, which du B.M.
have two splendid series of working proofs —
lunched at about 1.30 & then walked to
the gallery of the Royal Soc't of Painters in
Water Colors. Pale work, East — a comfortable
little collection that I was able to get thro'
fairly & comfortably in about two hours —
Then I went to Cockspur'd — office of the
no. German Lloyd S.S. Co. — to enquire about
the possibility of getting desirable State Room
on board ship sailing a week later than
Aug. 27 — Then walked to St. James' Park along
the Mall to Green Park, across to Hyde Park
Cor. & across the Park to Victoria Gate & home
at 5 o'clk. & then rested until near 7 — Then
dinner — Ev'g Reading.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 1, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Really quite a pleasant day — some sunshine
& no rain — but there were clouds & cool wind.
Walked to the museum, getting there at 10.30 —
engaged upon aqua-tints all day — lunch
at 1.30 & after that walked about until I got
somewhat warmed, then back in Print Room
until 4 o'clk. at which hour now it is closed
— after Aug. 1 — Then I went down into the
Reading Room & got some of Layard's Nineveh
books & studied up concerning the bronze
bowls, the ornamentation of which has
so much interested me. After carefully
studying the character & position of the incised
lines I am led to believe that they were
etched — some form of Mordant was known
also a
protecting varnish or ground — but this
purely technical destruction was not touched
upon by Layard — Left museum at 5.30 walked
home — a short rest before dinner at 7 —
Ev'g some news(paper Reading.
