## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 12, 1893

**Weather:** Dull  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day. Up late, help. after 9 o'clk
it was after 12 when Ralph & I started
out for a walk — went up to Central
Park & took a look at some of the
animals — Home at 2 o'clk. Then
dinner — after that I spent a little
time over some Montrose plans —
It was after 4 when Annie & I went
out for a walk — up 5th Ave. to 65th
St — then to George's rooms, 57 E. 59th —
Had a little visit with him & his
family — then walked home. Tea
at 6.30 — Evetg. Charl came in
& stayed until 10 — Then we went
to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 13, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable day with some light
rain — On my way back, having left R
at school, I stopped at Charl's to see the
work of Cullen, as a number of photos. Then
home & finished some plans for Montrose
& wrote a letter to Herrick. Mailed
plans & letter to him — In the afternoon
I went to 10th St. & saw T. W. Wood in his
studio & we talked over the Treasures-
ship of the N.A. of D. I agreed to act as
Treas. He thanked me. Walked home
dinner — Evetg. at 9 o'clk. I went
to Council meeting of N.A. & was prompt-
ly installed — nothing exciting about it.
After the business, Maynard cooked
a mess in a chafing dish — Chopped
beef — chopped green peppers, butter
& a lot of eggs — This mixture was served
hot on crackers with whiskey & seltzer —
walked up with Gel — just 12 when
I got to bed.
