## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 4, 1893

**Weather:** Overcast m'f — cold & raw — clear afternoon, very cold & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

After taking Ralph to school I got to work fussing over Onion skin [illegible] of the Jan Luis Rey plate. After lunch I went to Dr. Yale's — found him in but gave way to two ladies (!) who kept me waiting 3/4 of an hour — then I would wait no longer & hurried home & tried to get a little work done in what remained of a short afternoon. Well, just as I was preparing to go to Council meeting Mrs. Smith sent in two Mendelssohn Glee Club tickets (rehearsal) — Molly Adams, staying at Fred Ireland's also had two tickets & had arranged to come for A. & take her, so, having 4 tickets, Mollie, Annie & I went to the Rehearsal &, of course, enjoyed it very much. I had not before seen their new club he in W. 40th St. It does not excite in me any great amount of enthusiastic admiration.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 5, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*Mary came from Po'keepsie*

Very disagreeable day — snow beginning in the m'f — something like a blizzard in the afternoon — Yale came in to see me in the m'f & spent some time — while he was with me Mary came in, just from Po'k — to stay with us for a night or two — while talking with her I forgot to go for Ralph until almost 1 o'clk. In the afternoon I went to the Bank & until 4.30 was engaged with accts. of Annie & the N.A. of D — Then to N.A. f.D. & worked for an hour over contents of safe there. I find confusion. Home after 6 — Mrs. Smith, from across the way, sent us tickets for concert of Mendelssohn Glee Club — (U.O. — Charle sent us his tickets, but the night was so bad that we set them to Ed Cook — Ralph had suddenly developed a high fever — 104.75°  so I went up to Yale's & spent an hour with him & then returned home with a great deal of advice & a little medicine.
