## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 26, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A mixed & pleasant day, altho' cloudy — I got up at 7 o'clk. after another uncomfortable night and administered to myself a dose of Castor oil from which I was more or less uncomfortable all day long. I did not go out until sundown when I took a short walk with the boys. I finished my letter to Kate & in the eve'g wrote one to Chase.

R.R. fares etc. on the P.d.S.M.R.R. from Paris to Lyons & Marseilles, the time table gives fine train directions — first class with Wagon Restaurant (Dining Car) Rapide — Express — Direct & Omnibus — The fast runs from Paris to Marseilles - 540 Eng. miles in 13h. & 33 m. — exclusive of 39 m. in 6 stops — actual running 45 miles an hour — fare $19.53 — on the N.Y. Central, with Parlor car it would be $12.80 — Difference $6.53 —
The Rapide has 1st cl. only — runs the 540 m. in 14h. 56m. including 6 stops. 38 m. — actual running 38 m. — an hour.
Express has 1st & 2d cl. & sometimes with 3 —

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 27, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mild, quiet, cloudy day — another wretched night — discomfort, but not pain, in the bowels generally — when I got up at 7, Temp 3/5° above normal & I feel very shaky — ate very light bkfst. — Annie & Liz. the boys & I went out at 10.30. I walked down to the Promenade — At 11 A. took J. to the dentist's — & soon after I ret? to the Hotel — Had an infée dejuner in my bed room — & another short walk in the afternoon. Legs very weak & alarm feeling in stomach & bowels — Spent my time reading — Sat at hrs' tables d'hote & ate a very few mouthfuls — Annie & Eliz. went to the Jetty Casino in the afternoon — a concert & Ballet — They did not return until after 6, having stopped to watch at the gambling Tables.
