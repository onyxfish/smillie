## Left Page

### Tues. May 16, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day — very warm in the sunshine but a delightful breeze blowing. At 10 A — t I got to the museum & spent the mt'g in reviewing — Back to hotel just in time for lunch — after that some news paper reading then A — the boys & I took cab down to Piazza dei Martiri where we parted, she going with the boys to the Aquarium while I gave myself up to seeking photos. Found the best collection at Sommers. Largo Vittorio where I spent along time in making a selection of a dozen — Then I started upon an expedition of discovery on the Pizzofalcone height — & failed — Back to hotel at 6 — very tired — Dinner at 7.30 — a tub id every nabs king most of the time until finally I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 17, 1893

*Rome. Hotel de Russie. Room 59 & 49.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day — Divided the mt'g between writing a letter to George & packing up things — had lunch at 12, paid all hands & at 1 o'clk. left Parker's Hotel & went to R.R. Station & at 2 o'clk started for Rome. The ride thro' the valley with high mts. on either hand most of the way recalled our travel over the road in the opposite direction 6 or 8 weeks ago. We got to Rome at 6.45 and got to the hotel at 7.30 — as soon as we could clean up a little we went down to dinner — only 9 persons besides ourselves — warmly greeted by head waiter who leaves to-morrow — He got us an excellent dinner for us & then amused us by telling us something of his experiences — He sails for N.Y. on the Fulda next week — going to Chicago on pleasure — to return to Cairo professionally next Oct.
