## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 22, 1893

**Weather:** mild, but overcast & raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild, but overcast & raw — In the m'f, feeling very shaky, I went to Knoedler's to get some plate paper to mount a couple [illegible] sketching [illegible] — or Mr. Parke — was obliged to take Whatman paper. Met F.H. Smith & had a chat with him about Venice — then home. Afternoon, I mounted [illegible] — took them to D. B. Butler's to have them packed & expressed as directed. Dinner — Ev'f, Charl came in it was very late before I got to [illegible].

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 23, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very mild day — bright m'f. clouding over about noon with light rain — clearing again before 2 o'clk — cloudless. m'f. I went down town — first to Charl's office — then in to Moses Smith, about N.A. Ins. Then to Continental Inst Co. + got [illegible] certif. of deposit in exchange for Toledo, St. Louis + Kansas City R.R. Bonds. Then to Hamann & Koch + got a watch for Annie — then to Fulton St. + got awnings for Montrose — then home before lunch — After that worked for awhile mount-ing some drawings — then to Barker's and spent nearly an hour there — on Return to the house, Mary, her fires & Kate there, met in from P.K. Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'f Charl came in for awhile.
