## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 30, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very nasty day - snow + sleet +
rain - Annie used up by a cold
that has, mold style, taken me
by the throat - We all breakfasted
late - During what was left of the
m'f. I cut some mounts - after lunch
Annie & Kate went to the opera -
Katie Adams went home on the 3 p.m.
train & many the girls went to Po'k-
at 3.30 - then I retired & had a
rather restless nap. Dinner at 6.30
Eve'g. A. + K. got off to bed very
early & I was preparing to follow
so good an example when Charl
came in - He was amiable &
didn't stay long, so that I got
to bed at 10.30.

And so ends the year 1893 -
a year of many sunny memories
as well as some very gloomy ones.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 31, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow in the m'f. - Before noon clear-
ed sunny & mild. Got up with a
very sore throat which did not get
to be any better during the day - I
remained closely indoors & nursed
myself.          In the eve'g Annie
& Kate went to Church -  Charl
came in & waited until they
returned.

Got to bed before 11 & was awakened
at midnight by the din of steam
whistles & bells.
