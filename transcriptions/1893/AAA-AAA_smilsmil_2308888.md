## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 18, 1893

**Weather:** Charming m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming m'f. — early afternoon clouded & for a few moments there was a light drizzle of rain — before sundown all was again clear — cold & cloudless moonlight. Setting my easel up in third story front room & having a fire in the grate. I got to work & put in a good days work on my pen sketches. Bef'm 5 o'clk. I went out for a walk — up 5th Ave. nearly to 80th St. and then back, just in time for dinner at 6.30 — Evelf., wrote notes to Jno. Durand & Chas. Nielatz.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 19, 1893

**Weather:** Very pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day — 9 o'clk. when we had bkfst. I spent much of the m'f bussing with pictures & things in room 2d. story, front. Dinner at 2. Late in the afternoon, that is, about 4 o'clk. I went out for a walk. Taking Jim and Ralph — we tramped thro' the park to about 80th St. on our way back stopped at 59th St. & saw Geo. & his'm & met Charl there. Home at a little before 7 — tea — Evelf. ( our first one in the parlor. Charl came in — it was late when we got to bed.
