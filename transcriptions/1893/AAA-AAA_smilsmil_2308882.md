## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 4, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A steady down-pouring rain all day — with considerable wind & chilly most of the day. I was unpacking my things & distributing them as best I could — I went out before noon & did some marketing — About 7 p.m. I went with Jim on his daily visit to Dr. Valk in E. 37th St. He examines & bandages his eye every evening — I had quite a lengthy & interesting discourse from Dr. V. upon the subject of eyes & eye-surgery. Jim went again to Mary's to spend the night. I did some marketing for Sunday — then to Mary's, where, besides her, I met her mother, Edw[ard]., Walter & Louise — after a short visit, home

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 5, 1893

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy most but cleared about the middle of the day. Protracted toilette — late bkfst — afting — I loafed about with newspapers & after 12 I went to Dr. Yates's, but he was not in — then went to 5th Ave. & walked up to about 70th St. interested in the new buildings — It was 2 o'clk. when I got back to dinner — after 3 o'clk & Charl came in & we sat & chatted until after dark — tea at 6.30 — Eve'g a little reading & a little writing.
