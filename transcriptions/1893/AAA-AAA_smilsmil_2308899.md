## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 10, 1893

**Weather:** Very pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day - up late - bkfst. at about 10 o'clk. Before we got away from the table Yale came in bringing his boy Leroy to visit awhile with Ralph - I - visited for about an hour with me & then went out to make a visit, returning at about 12. Nearl, he & I, & Jim, Leroy & Ralph, started out for a walk, leaving Y. & his boy at their home while we went on to 59th St. & around by 5th Ave. I feeling much out of sorts. Home at 2.15. - Dinner - loafing about in the afternoon, feeling unequal to anything. Tea at 6.30. Evef. Charl, Geo. & Nellie came in and again I was showman of my sketches.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 11, 1893

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day. After taking R. to school & attending to my other m'f duties I spent the rest of the m'f. in working upon Annie's accts. mainly to clear up the Central Trust Co. of St. Louis (Wilson & Toms) mortgage acct. as I have to write to Carl Sprague about them. In the afternoon I went out with A. to Associated Artists in 23d St. to Kurtz, to Stern's & to McGibbons' looking for furniture covering for parlor chairs - not widely happy over anything we have seen. Home by 5.30 - Dinner at 6.30. Evef. I went to N.A. Council meeting where I was, as usual, a good deal bored by the inept manner of transacting business. At 11 o'clk. I bid them good night & left them still talking
