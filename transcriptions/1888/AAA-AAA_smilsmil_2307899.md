## Left Page

### July, Monday 2, 1888

Delightful day — Bit in etching of Burns Statue + had much trouble doing it — there were so many preliminary things to be done and so many temptations to enjoy the day outside — Curtains for N.E. Cherry Room came from Lord & Taylor + two framed Water Color studies from Grady & Mckeen. In the afternoon we had many callers — Mr. W. Jessup + daughter Mr. Mr. Courtright — D. Richardson + Gardner — Mr. Christiansen + Miss Keene — Did not go out for a ride as Selim's neck is galled — Tea about 7 — Went downtown with Charl. Home at 8.30 — Read until after 10. About 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 3, 1888

A delightful day — all the m[ornin]g. finishing the biting of Burns + cleaning plate — so many interruptions — Mr. + Mrs. Parke Rode up on horseback between 10 + 11 o'clk. Dinner at 1 — Fixed up printing table + press — ground ink + took impressions of Burns. Charl. at work with his camera taking views in dark room developing them. Mr. + Mrs. Husted called — about 5 o'clk Annie, Charl and I went out + Rode until 6.30 the horses going very well in spite of Selim's galled shoulder. On our Return Charl took a photo. of our Turnout. Tea — Sat on Piazza until about 9 — there Read newspapers until near 10, — to bed soon after.
