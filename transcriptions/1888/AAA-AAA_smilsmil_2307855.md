## Left Page

### April. Tuesday 3. 1888.

Bright & mild — To studio at 10. Stop-
ping out on Tadema plate meet men
then to 2°. Nat. Bk., made deposit to
my C°. Got C & O. receipts from Safe Dep°.
& went to Drexel Morgan & Co's in Wall
S°. & had them stamped — & got up
home at 1.20 — lunch — To studio
at 2.30 — biting & stopping out on
Tadema plate with now & then a
little work on "What o'clk. is it."
until 6 — then home — dinner —
Evef. Annie read aloud a few
chapters from Crawford's "A Roman
Singer" — then I wrote a long
letter of instructions to Herrick and
posted it at 10 E. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 4. 1888.

Another pleasant day — Got to studio
about 10 & spent the m'g. biting the
Tadema plate — at near 1 went to
Safe deposit & then home to lunch.
Before I left to return to studio.
Mary & then two girls arr'd from Co' K.
After lunch I went to the Carpenter
Moran's & then to Volkening, the
marble cutter — It was after 3
when I got to studio. I finished
biting the Tadema & cleaned off
the plate — also cleaned off "What
o'clk is it?" At 5.30 I started for
McKeever's — met Pt. Mulford & chatted
with him — then to McK's shop & left
directions concerning a Water-color frame
then home — dinner before 7. Evef.
Al. Smillie came in for a while —
Later Geo. Cook & Minly came in —
To bed soon after 10.
