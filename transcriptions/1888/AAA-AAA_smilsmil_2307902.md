## Left Page

### July, Sunday 8, 1888

So cool in the mtf that we had a little fire in the h[illegible], cherry room.
I spent the mtf about the place,
most of the time pulling away at
weeds. Dinner at 2 — a short
nap after it & wrote a note to
Marvin (of Scribner's) & an 8 page
letter to Mary, sending it with
a note to Charl. Later, Henrick
brought the horses to the door — It
was discovered that Selim had
cast a shoe — we started, but
at once saw that he limped, so
we turned about at once. Eve &
chatted until after 10, then to
bed.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 9, 1888

A gentle rain in the mtf
that continued for a couple
of hours — we hoped for more but
it cleared, though most of the
day we had the mitigation of
clouds. Carey came with stones
in the mtf for culvert at entrance
to lane from main street. I spent
a good part of the mtf. bossing
the job as I had something of a
headache — In the afternoon I
tried for a little while to work
on the text part of the Burns etching —
About 5 went out with Sue, Katchen
& Annie for a drive. The horses
pulled like fury & quite tired me
out. In at 6:15 — after that went
down town, Robt. with me, & inquired
for hay — To bed at 10.
