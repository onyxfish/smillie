## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 13, 1888

Beautiful day — after 10 when I
got to studio — To work on So. Am.
A.B.N. Co drawing until 1 —
then home — Helen Butler took
lunch with us — 2.45 when I
got back to studio — worked on
drawing until 4.30 — could see
no longer — A young western
man, with Sherk Bros, Brooklyn
called to solicit pictures for a
Brooklyn Exhib. I put some
more rough dry etching felt in
press — Home soon after 6 — dinner
at 6.30 — Evef. some writing —
Chas. Collins called. To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 14, 1888

Another lovely day — nearly 10.30
when I got to studio — worked on
A.B.N. Co. drawing until 1 — then
home to lunch — back at 2.45 &
worked until almost 4.45 — getting
it very nearly finished — then
walked up town to 59th St — after
stopping at Hale's (not in) to see
him about the hoarseness that
has bothered me ever since my
return to town. Dinner at 6.30.
Evef. wrote notes accompanying
letters from Kate & Mary — to Emma B —
& to Lizzie Smillie, 10 Stratford Pl.
Newark N.J. Then hung some
pictures in Reception & dining
room. — To bed at 11.
