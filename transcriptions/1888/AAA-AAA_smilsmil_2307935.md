## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 12, 1888

Very peculiar weather — clear
mtf. — then clouds — a shower
at 2 — again clear — cloudy ovf.
After my usual visit to the garden
I got to work at my dry-point
& then took three proofs, trying
different inks without satisfying
myself — Dinner — out of doors
with the men at work clearing
the ground adjoining the straw-
berry bed — At 4 o'clk. Edith's
cousin Chas. Wright called — at
4.30 we went out for a ride —
back at 6 — Evef. as usual —
a little reading & some chatting.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 13, 1888

Quite cool, cloudy & windy — Spent
some time in the garden & then
with Decker & [illegible] as they
worked over the ground adjacent
to the new Strawberry bed. Went
to the Fair looking for geese from
which to make studies — Met Perry
Conklin who promised to bring a
couple to me next Monday —
Home before dinner. After
that, at 2.30, again went to
the Fair with Annie — Edith —
Jim, Gretta & Ralph — Retd. to the
house at 3.30 & we rode to Elk
Lake & Retd. at 6.30 — Tea — Evef.
very cool & we sat in the warmth
of a wood fire & talked
