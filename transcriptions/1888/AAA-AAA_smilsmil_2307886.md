## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 6, 1888.

*at Wm E. Downes's — New Haven —*

Beautiful day — After bkfo — we determined upon a short visit to New Haven, So, we took the 9.30 train, Annie, Jim & I, leaving Greta & Ralph & got to New Haven at about noon. We Rose by horse car out to the Downes Place on Whitney Ave. & Edwards St. Found Mr D. at home his sons Wm & Jack — Mrs. D., Mrs. Whiting & her children — Mrs. D. complaining & Jack Recovering from the measles. Lunch about 1 and later Wm D. Jun, Annie, Jim & I were taken out Riding, went to the Tops of East Rock, very fine Road & view — Back to house before 5. W.D. Jun. departed — we looked about the grounds & at the new house — one f Walter Cook's designs. Dinner about 6 — Evef. chatting until 10 — then to bed — very warm night.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 7, 1888.

*Bridgeport — Ridgefield.*

Cooler mf. Up early & about the place until 8 when we had bkfo — At 10.30 we went out Riding. Jennie Whiting, Annie, Jim & I — going thro' the Hillhouse place & Ave, & Prospect & other very charming Aves & finally to depart at 11.50 — got to Bridgeport soon after 12 & by Street car to Mr. Jos. Ireland's where we saw Mr. & Mrs. J. & "cousin Julia" — We had dinner & were pleasantly entertained & a little before 4 left the house with Mr. J. who went to the depot with us, we took 4.20 Train to So. Norwalk thence to Ridgefield, Riding also with Mr. P. Louisbury. In at 6 and had tea — Evef. it blew up quite cold — chatting until 10 — then to bed, glad to get into a warm places.
