## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 21, 1888

A dull mf developing into a soaking wet day — until after 11 I was about the place, most of the time fixing the wire screens for vines on the west front of the house — then wrote awhile on letter to Shninger — Dinner at 1 — Rain — then — made fine in studio & finished E's letter & wrote a note to J.Q.A. Ward & one to Drexel, Morgan & Co about Che. & his matters. About 6 o'clk. went down town — got mail etc — Tea — Evef. reading newspaper & walking on piazza until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 22, 1888

Tremendous high wind with spiteful showers until noon, then clearing & growing colder — I spent much time in the vegetable garden — Coupe being sick it requires attention — then I wrote to Yale & also to Geo. giving him & family a formal invitation to visit us here. Dinner & after that made a start at A.B.U. Co. drawing "Place Dom Pedro II." Then took a nap & soon after 4 — bundled up as though it were winter we went out & had a very enjoyable ride, getting home a little before 6 — Tea — Evef. newspaper awhile — then Annie read aloud to us Kennan's last Siberian article in "the Century" mag. To bed at 10.30 — very cold. Thes. fell during the night to 38°.
