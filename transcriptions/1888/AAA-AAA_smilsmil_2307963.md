## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 7. 1888.

Bright & pleasant out of doors — in bed all day with severe headache resulting from my cold — Got up late in the afternoon & was able to go down to dinner — Mr. Theo. Justice came to make Annie a short visit — Eveʼg. Geo. & Nellie came in for a time. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 8. 1888.

Disagreeable day — overcast with some rain about the middle of the day & at night — Felt well enough to go to studio but did no work except cleaning out drawers & corners — At 12 went to Knoedler's — then to 2ᵒ. Nat. Bk. & home to lunch at 1 — Masons began work on the chimneys & Kate (the cook) raised a row — I retᵈ. to studio at 3 & worked in closets until 5 — then home — Eveʼg. Mr. Justice, Annie & I spent until 10 o'clk. looking over back Nos. of Lond. Ill. News & Punch — then to bed.
