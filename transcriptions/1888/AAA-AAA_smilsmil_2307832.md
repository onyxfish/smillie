## Left Page

### February, Friday 17, 1888

Routed out at 6 A.M., & again at
8 o'clk. Bowel trouble — much pain
— did not get up until 1 —
Felt so shaky that most of the
afternoon was spent on lounge.
Evening read until 10 o'clk —
then to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 18, 1888

Disagreeable day — a slight
return of my trouble in the n[ight]
so that I determined to remain
quietly at home — Spent most of
the m[orning] reading & looking over old
vols of "Punch" with Jim. In the
afternoon I went to studio and
after an hour or more, there I
returned home. Dinner as
usual. Even[ing] finished, with
Annie, the addressing of the
D. K. circulars & to bed at 11.
