## Left Page

### May, Monday 21, 1888

Charming day — an early start. After bkfs; went over to Grady & McKeever's &
spent some time there ordering frames
& arranging to send a man to Po'k. to
pack the pictures & frames. To studio
soon after 10 — To work on "What o'clk is
it?" took a[illegible] before going home to
lunch — back at 2.30 — all the after-
noon fussing over the face & hands of
Teddy — I can't get him to blow — his
hands are very badly drawn. It
was 7 before I got home to dinner.
Mary, the waitress, still sick — Yale
came to see her in the [illegible]. So
much used up by my headache that
I was unfit for anything — spent
most of the eve'g on the bed —
To bed after 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 22, 1888

Lowery day — after a most uncomfortable
night I was unable to get up until after
8 — struggled through a headache that
finally passed off — After 9 went to Grady
& McK's & arranged to have a picture packed
to go to Po'k. tomorrow m'g. also attended
to the ordering of some frames — to studio
at 10.30 — wrote note to Mary — Got out
a[illegible] of "Pate Plain." "Upper Ausable Lake"
& did some retouching upon it — At 12.30
went to McKeever's shop with it — Home
soon after 1 — lunch — again to studio
at 3.15 & worked until after 5 on "What
o'clk is it?" Then, with Geo. walked
down to the Century — There until 6.15 —
true home to dinner — Eve'g sat
reading in the nursery, with Annie —
Chase came in for a few minutes
before 9.
