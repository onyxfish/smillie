## Left Page

### May, Sunday 27, 1888

A dubious m'f., clearing in the afternoon — B'kf't at 9 — Indoors all day — feeling tired — took short naps m'f and afternoon — 9e° came in about 1 o'clk. Dinner at 2 — wrote to Herrick, Parke. & Tarbell — (sent Tarbell Annie's check for $500 — Pay'm't for horses & harness) Annie & I went out & took a walk up to 50th St & back about 6 o'clk — Tea at 7 — Eve'f. Reading & writing until about 10 — took note to Kate up to G.S. Depot, then home & to bed

## Right Page

### May, Monday 28, 1888

overcast & disagreeable — Rain, or mizzle in the early eve'f. Later, heavy thunderstorms. Got to studio at 9.30 — most of the m'f spent in getting some Tadema plate proofs into order by lamp-black touching upon them, they being very defective — At noon went to Renner's shop with order for frame for etching for Mary Sprague Cook — then to Grady's — it was 1.40 when I got home to lunch & 2.45 when I got back to studio — Miss Verdenal came & drew from cast while I went on with taking some large paper imp's from "What o'clk is it?". I think the plate is done — Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eve'f at 8 o'clk went to C.F. Turner's studio — 35 W. 14th St. meeting of Soct'y of Am. Etchers — an organization to flank the publishers & get some of their profits. I doubt it. Left there at 10 o'clk — heavy shower. home & to bed.
