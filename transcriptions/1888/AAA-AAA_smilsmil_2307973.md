## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 27, 1888

A raw, dark day, part of the
time wet. Got to studio at 10
& to work painting on "Huddle
Woods — Lake Geo". 11×18 — an
old canvas laid in years ago
& smeared with glazes very low
in tone — worked until 1 — then
home to lunch. Back at 2.30 —
tried for a time to paint but it
was too dark — so I gave up to
my Translation into blk. & white of
the Rainbow Spectrum — mounting pf.
& entertaining Mr Gebbie the publisher of Phila. Took pf copies of
"Ports & Etchers" to Sarah Leggett — many
— then home at 6 — dinner.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 28, 1888

A pleasant mf. but clouded
afternoon. To studio at 10 & painted
all the mf on "Huddle woods" getting
it all painted in — Home at 1.40
after having gone to the bank for
Annie — It was about 3 when I
returned to Studio — The rest of the
afternoon I touched on a number
of my pf. "What o'clk is it?" correcting the drawing of the mouth of the child
in white apron — & did a number of
little odd jobs about — At 5 went
down to Disbrow's about my boots & to
Keating's & got a button in my overcoat — Home to dinner at 6, to
Evef. wrote to Herrick — Then
Eliz. Horne came in & spent the
evef with us until 10 — when
Annie & I walked with her to
her lodgings — 110 Madison Ave —
then home & to bed at 11.
