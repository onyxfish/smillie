## Left Page

### September, Sunday 30, 1888

*First snow.*

Cloudy & very cold day. When
I got up first at about 6 o'clk
2 inches of snow clung to the
full foliaged trees & lay upon
the ground. About 8 I got up
& took my camera out & made
some exposures, but I was late
as the snow had already begun
to melt & a little breeze arising
shook some of it down. The Ther.
fell last night to 32°. Having
a troublesome headache I spent
the mt out of doors, most of the
time pruning the grafted apple
trees. After dinner I went to
bed & had a long nap but did
not feel relieved when I got
up. Evg. lounged about until
after 10 — then to bed. Passed
a very uncomfortable night.
