## Left Page

### November, Thursday 15, 1888

A very disagreeable day of rain &
wind — 10 o'clk. when I got to the
studio — worked for a little while
on the Don Pedro Statue drawing —
then, calling it finished, commenced on the Albright vigt. drawing
of the Statue of Liberty — tracing my
sketch — Retracing & continuing it
before I went home to lunch at
1 — wrote notes to Alfred Jones &
Nicole about W. C. Soct. Auditing
Com. business. To studio at 3 &
worked until 4.30 putting in
first washes on Liberty — There
some more cutting of etching pt.
Home before 6 — at 6.30 went
with Annie to Mary Cook's & had
a quiet dinner, Ed. dining out
with the Twilight Club — Eve.
Annie, Mary, Miss Williams & I
played Whist — Home & to bed
at 10.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 16, 1888

A delightful m'f. but rain commenced again at about 1 o'clk. & fell
light showers at intervals during
the rest of the afternoon — To studio
before 10 o'clk. & continued work on
A.B.N. Co. drawing of Statue of Liberty.
Young Piegatto called to get the promise of a painting for the Ohio Club
at its meeting on 26th ins'. Home
to lunch at 1 — at 2 o'clk. to Met.
Opera house — 1st Phil. Rehearsal —
sat in Charl's seat — got in the other
one — Enjoyed the Wagner selections
immensely — out at 4 — went to
Grady's & looked at patterns for frames
Then walked up to 59th S. Then home.
Dinner at 6.30 — wrote a letter to
Herrick — Sat for a while by fire in the
parlor — the first this fall — then with
Annie to Geo. Cook's in 37th S. and to
Walter's — Home before 10 & to bed
soon after.
