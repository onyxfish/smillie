## Left Page

### June, Thursday 28, 1888

A raw, cold day — wind strong from N. E., rain at times falling heavily — Wood fires in studio —
Dining room & back bed Room —
Bkfs at 8 — much of the day spent
in etching room & studio getting
things in shape for work —
Went down town in the mf &
attended to messages — Dinner
at 1 — 3 cases from home arr-
som after dinner & contents
were unpacked — Tea at
6.30 — After that went down-
town for the mail — back som
after 8. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 29, 1888

A very cool, cloudy day, at times
mizzling wet — afternoon, pleasant.
Did not have bkfs. until after 8
Some time spent getting fires
made & warming up the house &
studio. Some time lost by a call
from Mr. [illegible] a German gentleman
who occupies the Geo. Beniity house
for the summer. He showed me
a lot of his photographing. Got
to work at last tracing Calversley's
Burns — Dinner at 1 — laid ground
& prepared gelatine tracing for
transfer — then put press in order
for use — It was after 5 o'clk. when
I went out for a ride with my family.
In at 6.30 — tea at 7. Every-
about out of doors until 8 — then
reading & writing until 10 — then
dressed Annie's breast —
