## Left Page

### December, Monday 31, 1888

Disagreeable wet day clearing at
night. To studio at 10 & spent the
day working on front cow in "A Group
of Cows". Getting it into much better
shape generally. Home to lunch
at 1 — on my return, went to Bank
& then to studio at 3 o'clk — worked
as long as I could see. — Then to
the Century & had an interview with
Ferguson & others — Home at about
6.30 — dinner — at about 8 — as we
had settled ourselves before the parlor
fire Annie noticed in the Tribune that
Fidelio was to be given at the Met. opera
Ho. She & Edith at once determined to
go over & I escorted them — got tickets
— saw them going & then returned home.
About 9 Charles came in — a headache
had increased upon me making me
very uncomfortable — Annie & Edith
ret'd. at 10⁴⁵, earlier than expected, they
had not waited for the ballet.
To bed soon after 11 —
And so ends the year of our Lord
1888.
