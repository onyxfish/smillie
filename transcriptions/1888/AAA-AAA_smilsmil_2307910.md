## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 24, 1888.

Brilliant day — spent a good part of the m'f. putting up wire screen on west front of the house for vines to run up on. Parke came up bringing his friend Wm. Allen. I did some work on Tadema plate with graver — Dinner as usual a little more work on my plate & at 4.30 Annie & I took Mr. & Mrs. Husted (Baptist Minister) out for a ride — back at 6.30 — At 7.30 Annie & I went to the Parkes to tea. Mr. Allen & Jas. Carmalt. — we had a very pleasant time until after 10 — then Annie & I had a moonlight walk home & to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 25. 1888.

Ed⁴ + Mary Cook with Catherine, Edna + Tom arr⁴. from Buffalo

A very pleasant day — Spent the m'f., or a good part of it, putting up poultry wire netting for vines. Then to work up on Tadema plate until dinner at 1 o'ck. After that put up lanterns & some other things to get the house ready for company — then a very short nap & soon after & started for New Milford, getting there at about 5.45. Put up horses at Hotel Stable — gave them oats & then waited for train which arr. on time at 6.22, the expected party being aboard — we soon got under way for home, arriving at about 8.30 — a light supper then chatting until after 10 — we got to bed about 11.
