## Left Page

### March, Saturday 24, 1888

Milder & pleasanter day. Before
9 o'clk Dr. Blackwell & Cushier came.
put Annie under the influence of chloroform
& lanced her breast, finding deep seated
burrowing pus. Ace was over & I got away
to studio before 11. I etched on the [illegible]
until 1. much exercised over the marks —
not knowing what treatment of line to
use, determining at last upon a perfectly
mechanical under line upon which
I will work freely with dry point. Home
to lunch at 1. Back at 2.30 & worked
until 5.30 when my eye troubled me
so much that I went home. Dinner
— Evett, first part with Annie. later,
Charl came in for a little while. I
got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 25, 1888

Bright mtg. — overcast p.m. — threatening.
Got up at 8 with a very heavy head &
a sore throat, hardly thinking that I w'd
be able to keep up through the day. Annie
kept her bed & I played the part of ass't.
nurse to her & the children. I had a
rest between 11 & 12 — then Dr. Blackwell
came & dressed A's breast. D.B. threatens
me with conjunctivitis, or something of that
sort, if I persist in working my lame eye
& insists upon my giving it a rest for a
day or two. Geo came in in the mtg &
so did Marie & many red. Dinner about
2\. Afternoon wrote to Mary — S.A. Schaff &
Herrick. Charl came in & Emily & Geo —
about 7 I went out to G.C.D. with letters —
to Druggist's for sundries — & for a short walk.
A cup of tea before 8 — then spent the
rest of the evng on lounge in the nursery
attending to the demands of Master Ralph
from time to time. To bed about 11.
