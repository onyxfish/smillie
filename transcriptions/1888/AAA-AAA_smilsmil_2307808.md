### January, Sunday 1, 1888

Very dark, windy & wet — heavy rain all day & at night, showers — Up late — bkfs — about 9 — Read & taken & then spent some time drawing some plans for small closets etc or my studio here, in town, that I expect Herrick to make for me — Dinner at a little after 2 — about 3 Char came in & stayed until near 5 — I wrote letter to Mary & Kate — had a cup of tea after 7 — Itself. Read — Earnest Wright called for a few minutes — at 10 I went up to G. C. D. with letters — on my return read until 11 — then to bed.
