## Left Page

### January, Friday 20, 1888.

*Brother Will died —*

Bright, with a slight snow flurry about 1 o'clk. To studio at 10 o'clk feeling very seedy after a night of headache — but I got out a large sheet of mounted paper × + commenced to charcoal another cattle subject. I worked until near 1 — then home to lunch — at 1.30 Charl came in + announced the sudden death of Will — We soon went up to 73d St. Joe was like one wild — but from her I learned that at 9.30 this m'g. he had suddenly complained of a pain in his chest — He caught her hand + said "Oh! Josie" + then sank down + with a slight struggle it was all over — Doctor said, disease of the heart — Ge. soon joined us — We left at about 3.30 + went to Up-town news paper offices — then I went to Grolier Club on my way to studio from studio, home at 5.30 — Dinner at 6.30 — Charl joining us — He took Kate to his rooms later — I wrote a letter to Mary.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 21, 1888.

Bright, windy + cold — got to studio at about 10.30 — I tried to concentrate myself upon my work and worked with pencil outlining the new cattle drawing — Home to lunch at 1 — back to studio at 2.30 — but did not do anything — Frank Waller came in + made quite a visit — Home at about 6 — Dinner before 7 — Eve'g., Charl came in at about 9 — To bed soon after 10.
