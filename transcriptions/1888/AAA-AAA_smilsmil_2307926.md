## Left Page

### August, Saturday 25, 1888

A delightful day — as usual,
it was after 1 before I got to my
studio to commence work on the
A.B.N. Co. drawing — Dinner at
1 — more work until 4 — then out
for a ride with Annie, Sophie
& Jim — Went to South Pond again
by way of Giles Watrous Road — up
the hill to Brooklyn & around
to East & South of the Pond & home
by new wood road & over Brew-
ster's hill getting in, after a
delightful drive, at 7 o'clk —
Tea — Eve'g with news papers
& magazine — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 26, 1888

Cloudless — the ther. says, very warm
but this was flatly contradicted by a
delightfully fresh breeze that blew stead-
ily all day — Bkfst at 8.30 — It was near
11 when I got at my letter to Kate — &
finished it before dinner at 2 — after
that wrote a letter to Charl — & at
4 we got off for a ride — Annie
(taking Ralph) — Sophie & Jim —
we got back to town at 5.15 and
stopped at the Darke's & called upon
them — Home at 6, Tea at 7 —
Eve'g. I made a start upon a
letter to Jas. A. Shearman, in answer
to one from him rec'd late last April.
At 9 o'clk. went to P.O. with letters.
