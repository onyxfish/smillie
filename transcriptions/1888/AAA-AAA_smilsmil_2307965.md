## Left Page

### November, Sunday 11, 1888

Bright & very pleasant day — as
a result of the demoralizing with
of yesterday & the excitement of
last evef. I was pretty much used
up with a headache but I managed
to force through — Geo. called with a
letter for me from Katie Throops. I
walked between 12 & 2 — then
dinner — before evef. managed
to write to Charl & Katie. — Jim
was kept in bed until the middle
of the aft. but seems to be quite
himself again — After a light tea
Annie & I walked down to Aunt
Annie's in 17th St— found Caswell there
we stayed until near 10 & then
walked home again — & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 12, 1888

A bright day & March like. After
a very uncomfortable night got up
with a very disagreeable head which
got better as the day wore on — Got to
studio after 10. did a little work on
A.B.N. Co. drawing — home at 1 — back
at 3 — the masons making awfully
dirty work at home in tearing down the
back chimney — worked on drawing
until 4.30 — Then walked down 4 Ave
to 6th St — then to Bisbee's — the boot maker
in Waverly Place — Home at 6 — dinner
at 6.30 — Evef. Geo. Cook came in
for awhile & helped hang pictures
in the parlor — away at 9 — I did
some writing & to bed at 10.
