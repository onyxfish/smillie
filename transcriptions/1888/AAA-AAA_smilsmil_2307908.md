## Left Page

### July, Friday 20, 1888.

A pleasant day — cloudy in the m'g
with, at one time, a few drops of
rain — bright p.m. & cloudless moon-
light. Worked with graver during
the m't on Tadema plate — Dinner
at usual time — again worked on
plate until after 4 — then out
riding until 6. Annie — Sue —
Eleanor, Willie & Jim — our return
was hastened by the sickness of W.
whose dinner had disagreed with
him. Rec'd letter from Charl. at
Lake Geo. enclosing a long & en-
thusiastic letter from Kate — written
at Rolandseck, on the Rhine. —
Ev'g. I wrote to Geo. enclosing
Kate's letter & took it to the P.O.
at 9.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 21, 1888.

A very beautiful day — feeling
headachy & out of sorts I did not
try to do any work in the studio
but spent all the m'g fixing up
some of the new vine netting for
vines to run up on in front of the
piazza. In the afternoon I
again worked about out of doors
until 4.30 — when, with Sue —
Annie — Jim & Willie — I went
off to ride — got back about 6.30.
Ev'g. The rest of the party,
after a lovely twilight on the
piazza — played cards while I
read my newspaper. — It was
11.15 when I got to bed.
