## Left Page

### July, Saturday 14, 1888

A dry & cloudless day with wind still persistent from the N.E. I worked about among plants & vines until after 10 — then to work proving the Burns plate. took 8 pf. Then dinner — after that made up pkf. & plate pf. & photos — wrote a note to Calverley. Soon after 4 Annie, Hatcheu, Willie & Jim went out with me riding. we came around by Spencer's & got butter — Home at 6.30 — then tea — Evef. Sue felt so unwell (pain in her back) that she went to bed. I read newspaper awhile — then took a hand at whist with Annie, Hatcheu & Rob't. — To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 15, 1888

overcast & cool in the mf., coming off very dry & warm in the afternoon — Dan. Saupe & W. Joy of Lawonda called in the mf. before church time — I wandered about the place — then wrote a letter to Kate & at 12 went down town & got some Mellin's food — dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon again wrote — It was after 5 when Annie, Hatcheu Rob't & I went out to ride & about 7 when we got home — so late tea at which Miss Kate Seaord rebelled — we well have to take our rides at times to suit the pleasure of our cook. Evef. I worked hard watering trees [illegible] our last fall — It was just 11 when I got to bed.
