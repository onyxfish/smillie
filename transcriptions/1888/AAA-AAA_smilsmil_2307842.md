## Left Page

### March, Thursday 8, 1888

Bright, breezy & dirty — Got to studio at 10.15. There for a few minutes, then to Bank — got a little money — then down town to A.B.N. Co — Saw Freeland who has just ret. from Brazil. Gave lef. of etching to Jas. Conklin & got address of Fuchs & Lang. Then to Chas's — then to Clayton's (Stationer) in [illegible] St., & ordered an address book for Annie for Rich. Kitchen — then to Fuchs & Lang, 29 Warren St., & got some gelatine for tracing. Then home at 1.30 — lunch — It was 3 o'clk. when I got to studio — Mr. H.H.T. Clark, of Knoedler's came — They are all much stirred up about the unnecessary delay that there has been over the Alma-Tadema. The large photo. was delivered to me to-day. I got things ready for a start. Frames came from Brady & Wk. 3 small etching frames & a large one for my big Water Color — I spent some time in getting Mr. Woodin's etchings mounted & into their frames. At 6 o'clk. Geo. & I walked up town together — It was near 7 when I had my dinner — Evey — felt too tired to write an answer to S.R. Koehler — so I sat in front of the fire until 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 9, 1888

Delightful day — still a little too cool for storing — Got to studio at 10.5. Finished getting Mr. Woodin's etchings into their frames & sent them to her by Selleck — Then, at 11.30 — Took my large photo. of the Alma-Tadema & went up to the Met. Museum with it & compared it carefully with the painting — walked part way home — lunch after 1 — Annie went to Phil. Rehearsal — I wrote a note to Kurtz expressing my admiration for his photo — To studio at 3. Miss Verdenal came & drew until 5.30 — I got my etching table cleaned up & ready to start the new plate — Then I mounted some of the [illegible] J.D. Durand's portrait — Cut Jno. Durand's two phs. into a [illegible], directed them to Chas. H.T. Hart & took them to Lipp.'s office at 6 o'clk. Then home to dinner. Evey wrote to Schoff about etching Mr. Rines' plate — a long letter to Koehler descriptive of my Durand plate working pfs. Geo. & Emily came in for a little while — Dr. Kellam came & bandaged Annie's breast. Gretta tried her first night in the nursery with the children — To bed at 11.
