## Left Page

### November, Thursday 1. 1888.

Very pleasant & mild — Ther. got up to 70° out of doors, in the shade
in the afternoon — It was after 11
when I got to Studio — unpacked
my big case — Home to lunch at 1.30
— back to studio about 3 — Put up
some of the curtains in studio —
got home about 6. Evening
Annie & I worked until 10 at
dusting books & getting them into
book cases — after that I unpacked
big case from Montrose & got to
bed, very tired, after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 2. 1888.

Another pleasant & very warm day — Ther.
in the shade, out of doors, 74°. I worked
about the house until after 10 — then to
Douglass' — Moran's — Davis's — on the east side
crossed over & went to Wood carpet man's
to Grady & Tuck Eeven's shop & then to their
store & got to studio about noon. Then
to Lord & Taylor's with curtain pole &
home to lunch at 1 — It was 3 o'clk.
when I again got to studio — Spent what
remained of the afternoon in cleaning
up & putting things to rights — It was
about 6.30 when I got home to dinner —
— About 8 Annie & I went to Walter's
with photos of Montrose house & after
9 when we went to Edw. C's where Mr.
& Mrs. Cooker were dining — there until
10.30 — then home & to bed after 11.
