## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 20. 1888.

A day that began delightfully cool & wound
up red hot. Up early & at 7 was at Moran's
carpenter shop at 7 o'clk — ordered packing
box — then to Douglass & got small signs — then
to Crandall's & got "Excelsior". then did my
marketing & back home to blk — After that,
at 9 o'clk, went to cor. of Mad. Ave. & 37th St, &
looked over a collection of paintings there.
then to Safe Deposit & arranged Rinnes coupons
for collection thro' the summer — then to
Knorales — to Lord & Taylors — to D. Blackwells
to Eimer & Amend & got drugs — then to
studio — settled with Gardner — then got
some ice cream & got home with it at
12.30 — lunch at 1 — Soon after 2 out
again — to Gurney's Exp. to Lord & Taylor again
— to 2nd nat. Bk. — three downtown — to Charls
— then to Rich's Dorman's — got Rubber gloves in
Maiden Lane. Telegraphed to Herrick that we
would not leave town until Friday — then
to Boyle's about awnings — then to Park Place
& engaged Parlor car seats for Friday — then to 47
Chambers St. to see Store Lawn, Movers —
then up Broadway to Sargent's & bought an
earthen pail for urinal closet — then home
at 5.30 & rested until dinner time — After
that wrote to Tho. Kirby.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 21. 1888.

A very hot day — Up early and
to work at home packing — at about
11 went to livery stables — then had
my hair cut — some messages in 3rd &
4th Aves — then to studio — got Burlaps —
then to Horton's — got some ice cream
& came home with it — lunch —
then more packing — Started on
my trunk — dinner at 6.30 —
Eve. if Charl came in for a
little while — I finished trunk
soon after 9 — then went out &
got a cool drink — to bed at
about 10 o'clk.
