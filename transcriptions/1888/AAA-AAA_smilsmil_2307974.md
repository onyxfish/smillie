## Left Page

### November, Thursday 29, 1888

*Thanksgiving Day —*

Disagreeable cloudy day, at times
wet. Got to studio at 10.15 & to
work painting on study that I paint-
ed at Montrose last summer — a year
ago — × "A Pool in the Woods —
Oct." I took a light lunch with
me & ate it in the studio at 1.30 —
Chas. Baker called — I cleaned up
things & at 4.15 went to Charl's
Rooms & left letters for him as
he is expected home to night. —
Then home — a little dressing & at
5 o'clk. Annie, Jim & I went to
Edwd Cook's where we took our
Thanksgiving dinner, a party of
18 counting the children — Henry
Sprague & his wife — Walter & 3 children
Arthur Williams etc — we had a
very good dinner & a very good
time — home at 10. Annie read
aloud a chapter in Rob't. Elsm ere
& then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 30, 1888

A bright, pleasant day — After bfo.
went to Charl's Rooms & spent half
an hour with him — It was 10.15
when I got to the studio. I painted
all the m'f'n. "A Pool in Oct. woods"
home at 1 — back at 2.30 — Painted
on "the Huddle Woods. Lake Gee." until
4 o'clk — cleaned up & at 4.30
met Annie at Denning's — Bdway —
& 10th St. — & look at some carpets — Then
to Sloan's & after that to Arnold.
Constable & Co's — then home at 6.
Dinner — Ev'e'g — Annie & I
read aloud from Rob't. Elsmere
until 10 — To bed at about 10.30
