## Left Page

### September, Friday 14, 1888

A delicious day, brilliant and
cloudless. I spent the m'f. out
of doors — in the garden & then,
for some time trying to get a
phot° of Ralph — Dinner at 1 —
& at 3 we started for a drive
to South Pond which we enjoyed
exceedingly — home at 6 —
Ev'g. I spent some time
in dark room developing the
four exposures of the m'f.
They seem to be pretty good
but the Carbutt plates are too
slow for instantaneous work.
— To bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 15, 1888

A bright m'f. but with sure
indications of a change of weather
— wind southerly & a suspicious
haze in the air. At 10 o'clk. we
got away on our trip to Ballyhack
& Lakeside — Annie, Edith, Jine
& J. It was about 12.15 when we
got to Ballyhack (Elisha Mulford's
old home) where Mrs. Wright & her
daughter Mrs. Newhall & children are
(Mr. W. an Aunt of Edith's) We left E —
there & drove on to Lakeside, getting
there before 1 — later Mr. W — Miss W —
& Edith joined us there & at 2.30 we
had dinner — Ja° C — was not at
home but his wife, daughter & son Chur-
chill were — we had a pleasant time
until after 4 — then started for home —
Sky overcast & the smell of rain in
the air — home before 7 — tea — Ev'g.
before a pleasant fire with burning
faces & heavy eyes until 10 — then
to bed.
