## Left Page

### October, Thursday 18, 1888

A fine day aetho' cloudy & cold
but there was some brilliant
color pictures from the windows.
Out about the place until 11 — then,
for some time, mounting a number
of photos of groups of Geo's & our
boys. Afternoon taking down
awnings, screens, curtains etc
etc — went down town before 6.
Eve'f., brilliant moonlight —
still & cold — I spent some time
in going over accumulated bills
& letters — saving & destroying —
after 10 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 19, 1888

Rain, rain. Fog & rain all day
long & heavy fog at night — Awoke in
the m't with a very severe head-
ache — at 6 o'clk. Took Bromo-Caffiene
& was able to get up at 8 & made
a good days work after all — finishing
several plain drawings for [illegible]
& getting all my studio packing
very nearly finished. Went down
town before 6 — got mail & a pair
of very wet feet — Eve'f. Read
newspaper for awhile — then
had a game of cards with Annie
& soon after 10 tried to go to bed
but Master Ralph had other plans
& petta being still away he cuts
up shindies at night — it was about
12.30 when at last I settled for
the night.
