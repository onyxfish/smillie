## Left Page

### November, Saturday 3. 1888.

Unpleasant m'f — some rain but soon after 2 o'clk it cleared charmingly — Worked at hanging pictures etc until 10 — then to studio & spent the rest of the m'f. with a carpenter fixing up the closets — Home at 12.30 — Annie had been with Jim to Am. Institute Fair & in the afternoon went to the memorial services at Normal College of Miss Wadleigh — again with carpenter until 3 — he left & I cleared up his mess — after 5 went to Hunters & got my new spectacles — saw a part of Rep. Procession — Dinner before 7 — Ev'f went to Century monthly meeting — Home before midnight & saw part of the Dem. Torch light procession.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 4. 1888.

A delightful bright day — B'kf. late worked about for awhile — some time with the children & some time with my newspaper. Annie went to church & about 11.30 Jim & I walked up to 55th St. & 6th Ave. & called upon Lippincott who is doing some elaborate etching — It was 1.30 before we got home — dinner at 2 — after that a nap — then wrote letter to Mary — Tea about 7 — Ev'f Dr. & Mr. Yale called — after 9 o'clk again writing — Montrose bills etc — etc. It was 12 o'clk before I got to bed.
