## Left Page

### May, Sunday 13. 1888.

Overcast, with light, spattery showers
from time to time throughout the day —
clearing at ev'g. Bkfs. at 9 — Ralph
passed a tolerably quiet night & in the
m'g. was evidently himself again and
continued to improve all day. — I spent
the m'g. with him — reading newspaper
& correcting some short biographical notices
for Frank Weitenkampf. (of Father & self) who
is preparing them for D. Appleton & Co —
Dr. Yall came in about 1 o'clk & was
quite a visit. — I went out for a few min-
utes before dinner at 2.15. After that, with
children — After 4 went to studio — then
to George's & home before 7 — a cup of tea —
Ev'g. wrote a short note to Mary —
Charl came in after 9 — we walked up
to 9th St. Depot together & mailed M's letter —
then home & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 14. 1888.

A bright m'g. with heavy rains in the
afternoon & at night. Got to studio about
10 — & to work repainting the old picture
20×24 that I commenced 12 or 15 years
ago for Geo. Whitney — a view in the Adiron-
dacks, from Roaring Brook, high up on the Giant
looking over to the Gothic s. Went to Bank
at 1 — then home to lunch — near 3 when
back to studio & painted as long as I
could see. — Home to dinner & found
Wm. G. Parke at the house — He stayed
to dinner & spent the ev'g. Charl
came in about 9 o'clk. To bed
just before 11.
