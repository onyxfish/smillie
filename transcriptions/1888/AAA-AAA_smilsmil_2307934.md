## Left Page

### September, Monday 10, 1888

A mild, dull, milky day. I got through with my duties in the garden soon after 10 — & then until dinner, &c. worked on my dry point "Poppies" after dinner I took two pt. and worked until 4 — I did not get in my pt. all that I had hoped, but, being my first experiment in this line that was not surprising — nor was I discouraged — At 4, went out riding — back at 6 — Tea at 6.30 — Evg. Read papers — Wrote a short note to Geo. at N.Y. & sent him Katie's & Mamp's letters — Took it to the P.O. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 11, 1888

A pleasant day, clouded much of the time — Rain commenced falling at 6 p.m. & continued this much of the night — After bfst. went to the garden — At 10 — with Annie & Jim, drove over to New Milford & met Edith Wright who came on the 11.53 train — just from the White Mts. We had a pleasant drive back, getting home at about 1.40 — dinner at 2 — Afternoon, I took a nap & then worked about the place until 6. The masons worked in the cellar changing the opening into the flue for stove in lower hall — I used cement that was left in "painting" part of the foundation of workshop. Evg. Reading papers & chatting until 10 o'clk. Annie's breast to-night presented again a healed surface — How long will it stay so?
