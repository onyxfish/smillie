## Left Page

### December, Saturday 29, 1888

Very fine day — Got to studio at 10.15 & soon got to work on Water color drawing of "Fronts of Coins" — Concluded that I must make a radical change in the central color — a white one — tried to wash in color but found that the Chinese white used upon it last spring would not hold a wash — so, had to scrub the whole cow down to white paper — a hard job. Home to lunch as usual — then to 2° Nat. Bank & paid $750 loan & replaced the collateral bonds in the deposit box — To Studio at 3 & worked on cow as long as I could see — Dinner at 6.30 — Everf — soon after went down to Century — there until about 11.30 — chatted with Nicoll, J.J. [illegible] ward — Perry — Collins & others & had very pleasant & instructive conversation with Dr. W.H. Thompson — walked home & to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 30, 1888

A delightful day — B&b at 9.30 — Before noon Annie, Jim & I rode up to the 72° S. gate of Central Park & walked up to the Art Museum — to the obelisk — around the upper Reservoir & out at the 65th S. gate at 8th Ave — then to Elevated R. R. station & home at 1.40 — Dinner at 2 — afternoon with the children — Edith Wright came in before dark and stayed with us over night. — I wrote notes to Barnabas Carey & John Raemsford — Tea at about 7. Everf wrote to Herrick — posted letters before 10 — Annie read aloud for awhile — To bed soon after 11.
