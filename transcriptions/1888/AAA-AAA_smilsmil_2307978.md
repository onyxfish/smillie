## Left Page

### December, Friday 7, 1888

Bright, cold day — b.l.f.p. late — got
to studio's at 10.40 & Painted again
on The River's Source, getting his second
painting finished — To lunch and
back as usual — In the afternoon
put a little more work in the Crown
in shield Point Painting — Home
at 5 o'clk — Doll dressing. — I
carved cold turkey & tongue before
I presented myself to the working
company, some 12 or 13 ladies — By
some accident Avery Brown strayed
in before high tea time, & so relieved
the embarrassment of my situation
as the only man — Our company
left us soon after 10 when I was
glad to get to bed, having a
large sized pain under my
apron.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 8, 1888

A pleasant day — much milder —
Got up with a mean headache
was able to go to studio's & did a
little work at Painting — was able
to keep up until 1 o'clk. Then I
went home & gave myself up to
my tormenter — Even I was
able to be up & did a little
reading as we sat in the parlor
— To bed early.

Today the carpet men put down
the last of the carpets (except the
dining room) & with carpets & cur-
tains the house begins to look
very home like — The wood car-
pet man came & laid the oak
flooring in the dining room at come.
