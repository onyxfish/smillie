## Left Page

### January, Monday 30. 1888.

Mieder, but still cold. & promising
a change — To Studio at 10 — and
worked on my big Water Color drawing
putting in big white cow. Home
at 1 — back soon after 2 — worked
as long as I could see. — Then
went over to N.A. & spent near
an hour looking at the Water Color
show — Home at 6.30 — dinner —
Evetf. Chare took Kate to his
room — I read aloud awhile to
Annie — Then wrote up some old
accounts — Chare came in with
Kate at 10.30 — I got to bed about
11.30.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 31. 1888.

Snow during the night — dull day and
thawing — To Studio at 10.15 & to work on
large drawing — working over foreground.
Home at 1 — back at 2 ½ & worked as
long as I could see — Then walked
down to Union Sq. — up Bdway & 5th Ave.
& 42d St & home soon after 6. Dinner
at 6.30 — Evetf. — wrote for awhile
on old acts — Chare came in &
spent the evtf. with us. To bed
at 11.
