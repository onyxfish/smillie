## Left Page

### August, Friday 3, 1888.

Very warm & dry — the m'f cloudy as though for Rain but
it cleared before noon — I
absent the m'f until 12.
Putting up the wire netting
for vines to run upon, over
or in front of Piazza. Dinner
at 1, loafing about until
after 4 — at 4.30 took the
horses — drove Annie & Mary
to Tarkes for a call & took F.
out for a little ride — He fixed
the harness over more to ruin
his ideas & loaned me a collar
for Selim — Then, a short drive.
Home at 6.20 — Tea — Eve'g
a little writing after which
we had some Iliad — Annie
reading aloud. To bed at 11.
The healing process is no going
on very slowly on Annie's breast
which looks to me more en-
couraging to night.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 4, 1888.

A day of thunderous mugginess
opening with a thunder storm at
6 A.M. Cloudy & threatening all
day, heavy storms passing all about
us. Worked all day. Faking a
few last pfp. from the Alma-Tadema
plate — 2 plain — 2 India — 1 heavy
fab & 4 thin fabs. pfp. Just as I
put the paper upon the [illegible] for one
of the thin fabs. pfp. a fly got under it
& I took an impression of the wretch
to the destruction of the pfp. As the
weather looked so threatening in
the afternoon we did not go out
to ride — I went down town after
Tea & "did up" all my "chores".
Eve'g, each one for himself — in
reading & writing — To bed a
little before 11.
