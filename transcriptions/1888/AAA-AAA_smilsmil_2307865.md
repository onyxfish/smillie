## Left Page

### April, Monday 23, 1888

Cold rain in the m'f — clouds + wind for the rest of the day — To studio about 10.30 — worked on Tadema plate until a little after 11 — then took some [illegible]. It was 1.30 when I got home to lunch & found that Annie has had a chill in the m'f & had sent for Dr. Emily Blackwell. I was much alarmed — Dr. came after 2 o'clk. & evidently did not understand the root of the trouble. I got A. to bed, during which process she had a violent fit of vomiting. It was after 3 when I got to studio — Burnished on plate until 6.30 — then home — Dinner — Ev'f — Annie in bed — I in nursery while Gretta got a sleep. I wrote to Herrick — It was after 11 when Gretta came to take her place & I got to bed at 11.30 — Annie feeling easier.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 24, 1888

Bright, windy & cold — After bkfst, waited for the Dr. to come to see Annie. She came before 10 — state seems uncertain — she seems to think that more trouble is brewing in the breast. To studio at about 10.30 — worked on Tadema plate with burnisher until noon — then took a proof — Miss Hallowell & Mr. Dale, of Chicago, came in — Home to lunch at 1.30 — after lunch went down to Kimmel & Voigt with my Tadema plate & was there for an hour & a half getting [illegible] [illegible]. Young Voigt gave me a very brilliant India [illegible]. There seems to me to be but little more work required. At 5 o'clk back to studio & stretched [illegible] — Home at 6.30 — dinner — Ev'f in the nursery with Annie who is still a little feverish — Edw'd Cook came in for a little while. To bed at about 11.
