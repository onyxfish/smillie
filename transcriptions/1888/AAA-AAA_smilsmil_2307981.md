## Left Page

### December, Thursday 13, 1888.

Bleak, blustering & dirty — To
studio after 10 — Got bf. of Alma Tadema
for Mr. Woodin into frame which
is becoming to it — Took it back to
Butler's — Got some dry point bf.
mounted — Home to lunch & back
as usual — Made some final touches
on India ink Portrait of Annie's father
& selected some small negatives of
last summer's work & before 5 o'clk
went to O'Neils — Cor. No. 8 & University Pl.
to have proofs — also 5 copies of the
drawing of A's father — Went into Kelpels
& saw some of his late importations — then
home to dinner at 6.30 — At 8 took A. to 24
St. to Rehearse with the Choral Club — left
him & went on to Century Club — Nominating
Committee meeting — D. Fessenden otis
& Mr. Jos. W. Harper oney, present — Messrs.
W.W. Appleton & Stephen H. Olin absent.
I was asked to act as Secy & we worked
very faithfully over unpleasant and
knotty questions until 11 o'clk — when
we parted to meet next Monday. Eve,
the three of us got along very harmoniously
together — In this wretched up town on
me. I found Annie & Mr. Williams on
the doorstep trying to wake the girls &
get in — they had not waited. Come
fortunately — About 12 o'clk, when
we got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 14. 1888.

A very cold windy day with dust
flying like March. It was late
when I got to the studio — about
10.40 & I felt very little like work.
I spent some time with Geo. talking
of Century matters — then Yale came
in & more time was taken so that
when 1 o'clk. came I had accomplished
nothing — Home to lunch & back before
3 — got frame for Dry Point bf. for Mr.
Woodin — put bf. in it — mounted some
Alma Tadema bf. working at it
until nearly. Then home — dinner.
Eve'g I set myself to the delicate task
of answering a bad tempered & clownish
letter from Merrick — He is so unreasonable
& touchy that I have about given up
hope of being able to get along with him
much longer — Before I had finished
Charl came in & stayed until 10 — then
by hurrying I finished my letter &
got it into the box by 11 — It was
11.45 when I got to bed.
