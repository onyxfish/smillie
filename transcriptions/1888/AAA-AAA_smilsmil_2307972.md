## Left Page

### November, Sunday 25, 1888

Almost a blizzard — strong wind & snow — I did not go
out of the house all day, my cold being troublesome. The
day was spent with the children — writing to Mary & Reading —
in the mtf. Annie went away up to W. 86th S. to see Miss Joshay
who has just Ret'd. from abroad —
Eve'g spent in desultory work & to bed after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 26, 1888

A worse day even than yesterday with a sleety rain instead of snow —
Very much used up with a headache & did not go outside of the
house — wrote a note to Geo. Butler & sent it with W. Hives' note, up to
him by messenger — It was Ret'd.
about noon — W. B. not in — Then I
sent it to him by mail — I
also wrote some other notes and
between times spread myself
on the bed — also, hung some
pictures — Eve'g. Annie read
aloud from Robt. Elsmere until
after 10 — then to bed.
