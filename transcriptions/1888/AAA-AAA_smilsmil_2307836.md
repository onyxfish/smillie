## Left Page

### February, Saturday 25. 1888.

A horrible day of wind & driving
rain — To Studio before 10. To work
forwarding my etchings — made a
tracing from my drawing "Competitive
Blowers" for Stokes' Etching — Edith
made me a visit on her way down
town & home. I went home to
lunch at 1 & back at 2.45 — I
fixed up press & transferred to
a plate my tracing made in the
m't. Then I got Geo's Invitation
plate upon which he had drawn in
a subject & I worked over it as long as
I could see — then home — dinner —
Evef. at 8 — rain having ceased, Annie
& I went to Met. Opera House & heard
a Symphony concert led by young
Damrosch — Tickets for box 38 given to
Geo. & by him to us — there we met Geo.
& Miss Buckingham & Charl — Enjoyed
the concert — home at 10.30 — then to
bed.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 26. 1888.

A fresh, pleasant, clearing day — up
late — b'kf't after 9 — After some domestic
duties I wrote a long letter to Herrick
on the subject of his duties to me and
raising his wages — At 1 walked up
Madison Ave, to 64th S't into the Park
& home at 2 — dinner — Gretta's
afternoon out — Annie not feeling very
well — more domestic duties — then
I finished writing a long letter
to Sam'l Colman, now at Santa Barbara
Cal — then more domestic duties —
a light tea at about 7.30 — At 9
went out to G.C.D. — & posted letters —
then home & read.
