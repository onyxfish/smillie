## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 18. 1888.

Bright & cold — To studio at 10 15
& to work on Cattle drawing — Put
in the subordinate figures & home
to lunch at 1 — back at 2 15 — most
of the afternoon spent worrying
over the foreground — getting every-
thing laid in except the central
white cow — Took a walk down to
8th St. across to 5 ave. & then home
before 6 — Dinner. Sweet, at 8 15
went to Water Color Soct meeting
at the A. of D — out at 9.30 & then home.
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 19. 1888.

Bright, cold day — To studio at
10 & to work on Water Color, put-
ting in the white, central cow —
Home at 1 — Kate had arrived
from 50k. Back to studio
at 2 15 — D. Yale called — I
got in my big cow & spent
the rest of the afternoon fussing
over the foreground & fighting
with a headache — Home
before 6 — Dinner at about
7 — Geo & Emily — Mr. Valentine
— & Charl. & Kate sat down with
us — before we had finished
— at about 8.30 Geo & Nellie
came — I got to bed about
11 very much used up by
my headache.
