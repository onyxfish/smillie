## Left Page

### September, Saturday 22, 1888

cloudy wt. Some pleasant, clearing weather about the
middle of the day but heavy clouds + a raw air in the later afternoon.
As usual I spent some time in
the garden after bkfo — then to
work on my biting which was
continued with many interruptions until 4 o'clk. — Mr.
Gant called just before we
went out for our ride — home
at 6 — Rec'd an amusing letter
from Ehninger at Munich — Evelf
Annie + I went down to the
de Schweinitz's where we met
a pleasant little company —
Home at 11.30 + to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 23, 1888

A cloudy wt. promising a sour
day but it cleared pleasantly, the
afternoon being delightfully bright and
fresh. After bkfo I spent some
time in the garden among the to-
mato vines — then some photographing — then a little writing — then
dinner at 1.45 After that again
tried some photographing of the
children — Soon after 3 commenced
to harness the horses — Henrick came
+ finished it + we got away at 3.45
driving to the Colpe place + around
by Elk Lake, getting in at 6.45 —
tea — Evelf until 9.30. attending
to the horses + developing the
photos taken during the day.
To bed at 10.30 — Had a scare on
finding that A— has a lump in
her breast, about the size of a small
horse chestnut — developed very recently.
To bed at 10.30.
