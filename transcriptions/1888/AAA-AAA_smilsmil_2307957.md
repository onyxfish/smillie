## Left Page

### October, Friday 26, 1888

Pleasant day — At 9 o'clk went to Dr. Yale's & consulted with him about my eye & he agreed with me that it was best for me to go to Dr. Noyes. I then went to him — the trouble in my eye interested him very much as it is rare. He first pricked the cyst filled with serum & then finding the cyst very tough he extirpated it with scissors, a delicate operation very deftly performed. His assistant immediately prepared it for microscopic observation & in a few minutes I was examining what had been upon my eye. With Dr. Noyes to point out the interesting parts & explain them it was quite interesting — I got home at about 1.30 feeling no inconvenience in my eye — Bandaging it up I went to market then to the grocers & to studio, where I sat awhile with Geo. Home at 1 — lunch — In the afternoon my eye was considerably inflamed & painful — the effect of the cocaine having passed off — so I spent it & the even'g on the bed — turning in finally for the night at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 27, 1888

A dismal wet day — I spent the whole of it in-doors as my eye still continued inflamed & somewhat painful. — I rec'd a letter from Charl at Banff, Province of Alberta, away up in Manitoba, on the Canadian Pacific R.R., enclosing a long letter to Katie Throop describing his journeyings — after reading it I sent it by messenger to Geo. together with Katie's letter from Dresden (to me). I did considerable work in the way of cleaning out & putting away things. Eve'g Hunie was alone to me & we got to bed soon after 10 o'clk.
