## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 18, 1888

Rain, fog & mizzle during much of the m'f. - very warm & muggy -
Clearing & warm afternoon & ev'f.
After b'kf's, I went to the garden & spent some time there - It was
10.30 when I got to the house -
quite tired — I lay down for a
time & rested - Then to Stchuing
room & made last preparations
for the biting of "A Goldsmith's
Daughter". Dinner a't. After that
I got my plates into the acid
twice — everything working nicely.
About 4 went out with the horse
& had a promenade in the mud -
Home before 6. Tea - Before 8. went
with Annie to Wm Jessup's - left her
& went to P.O. mails delayed by
a "wash out" — had my hair cut — then
to Wm Jessup's & completed my call.
Home about 9 - read newspaper —
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 19, 1888

Geo. & family arr'd

Fog & muggy weather all the m'f.
Pleasant afternoon & ev'f. After
my usual visit to the garden I
got to work again at the biting
of "The Goldsmith's Daughter". Dinner
at 12.30 — soon after I started
with the group for Montrose Depot
- Train 20 m. late — about 3.15. Geo.
& family arr'd. a pleasant drive,
but slow, getting home about 5.15.
Tea at 6. went down town
after 7 - Ev'f. Reading paper
& general chatting until about
10 — To bed soon after.
