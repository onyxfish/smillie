## Left Page

### May, Saturday 5, 1888

Rather a raw m'f. changing into a warm, rather muggy day. To studio soon after 10. Tried to get to work upon large W.C. drawing "The passing herd" but was much interrupted —
Welles called + Laurie came in to borrow more money. I went to Adam's Exp. and Branding Iron for Herrick — then to 2° Nat. Bk. + put Annie's new bonds into her box.
Home at 1.30 — D°. Stinkson + Blackwell called at 2 + held consultation — Annie pronounced to be getting along as well as could be expected. After 3 when I got to studio, found Geo. there with a California man — Mr. [illegible] — who represents a wealthy ranch man who wants $10000 worth of pictures, with cowboys, bronhos + cattle — we had a talk for some time that was very amusing to me as it was so characteristic of the demands of border or new country wealth — but there was nothing in it for me alto' he urged me to go to Cal. + make the necessary studies. I worked on "The passing herd" until 6 o'clk — then home — dinner — Read to Annie until 9 then Charl came in + we went to Century monthly meeting — his first visit as a member — There until about 11.30 — then walked up home + to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 6, 1888

A very pleasant day — Bkfo. soon after 9. Spent the m'f with Annie + the children — Helen Butler + Geo. + Nellie came in + saw A. for a few minutes about noon — Then St. Black well came + as Miss Church had gone out for an airing I had to assist at the dressing of Annie's breast — a very bad looking thing — extending over a surface perhaps 7" long by 3" broad + sloughing at each end but going no deeper than the subcellular tissue — all but the two sloughing ends having commenced to repair. Between 1 + 2 o'clk. I went out for a short walk with Jim — then home to dinner — In the afternoon took nap of about an hour — Geo. Cook came in with some wild flowers — out for an airing from 5.45 to 6.30 — Gretta's night out + Ralph [illegible] perfectly, but he settled down between 8 + 9 + we had a quiet even'f. I read to Annie until about 10 — To bed soon after.
