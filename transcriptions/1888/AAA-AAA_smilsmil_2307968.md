## Left Page

### November, Saturday 17, 1888

A very fine day. Annie & Jim spent the day in Orange making calls. I got to studio at 10 and worked the rest of the mth on vig. drawing "Statue of Liberty" — about 1 went to 2° Nat. Bak. & Safe. depos I— Co— Home to lunch at 1 ¼  to studio at 2.30 — Soon finished drawing & then, until near 5, mounting pt. [illegible] etchings — then home & found that Annie & Jim had just got in. Dinner at 6.30 — Evef., Annie & I went to Phil. concert, occupying Charlie's seats. I enjoyed more than ever before the Wagner selection Ride of the Valkyries "Wotan's farewell & Magic fire scene." On our way home, stopped at the Casino & bought tickets for "the Women of the Guard" next Friday evef. — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 18, 1888

A fine wintry day. B[illegible] at 9 ³⁰ after that reading & caring for the children until near 12 when I went out for a short walk, getting to the church of the Messiah at about 12 ²⁰ — heard conclusion of Collyer's sermon on the new Religious novel — Rob. Elsmere— met Annie — Jim also joined us & then we went to 9 o's in 2. 28' S— Saw Nellie & the children Home to dinner at 2 — Sat about & played with the baby until after 4 — then Jim & I took a short walk. on my return, more baby & a little writing. Evef. I wrote an 8 page letter to Kate. To bed at 11.
