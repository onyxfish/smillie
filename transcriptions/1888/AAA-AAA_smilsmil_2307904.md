## Left Page

### July, Thursday 12, 1888.

Chilly, overcast m'f, so that we built fires — Wind blew strong & before noon a few drops of Rain fell — we hoped for rain altho' the wind has been steady from the N.W. for several days — The p.m. was beautifully bright & the night clear & cold — I worked on Burns plate by jerks & at last got it bitten in — that is, the lettering & coat of arms — At 5 Annie, Sue, Jim, Willie & Eleanor went with me out riding — a very pleasant ride — back at 6.30 — tea — after that, went down Town, Robt. with me — back at 8.30 — another eve'g of card playing & newspaper reading.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 13, 1888.

Ther. went down to 44° last night. Cold, cloudy m'f — clearing into a delightfully cool, breezy day. I spent most of the m'f finishing text & etching on Burns plate & making a bevel as well. Dinner at 1 — Prepared for proving plate to-morrow. Had stone put up in Etching Room. Soon after 4 out riding, taking Annie, Katchen, Eleanor & Robt. The day very lovely, the horses well behaved & the road selected picturesque & good — It was a very enjoyable ride & we got home at 7 — then tea — over some reading aloud by Sue — some card playing — etc. About 10 o'clk. Jim had a sudden vomiting attack after which he promptly went to sleep again & we went to bed.
