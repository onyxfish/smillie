## Left Page

### April, Thursday 5. 1888.

Very wet, disagreeable day — thunder
storm about 6 p.m. Got to studio at
about 10 — worked closely on Tadema
plate until 1 — then home to lunch
finding Kate there, just arr'd from
Po'k. Back to studio soon after 2
& again worked with dry-point &
graver. Miss Hallowell came in
to secure pictures for Chicago —
Home at 6.30 — dinner — Elf.
Charl came in & there was general
chatter until 10.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 6. 1888.

Pleasant day — after an unpromising
rainy m'g. To studio after 10. all
the m'g at work on Tadema plate.
Home at 1. Emma Ireland lunched
with us — after lunch I went to
Moran's, the carpenter & then to
Rackemann's — after 3 o'clk. when
I got to studio, my printing table
hind frame & marble slab came
at last. I worked on plate until
about 5.30 — then to Knordle's — Home
at 6.30 — dinner — Elf. Annie
& I went to Fred. Ireland's in 41st S.
& made a call there — after 9 o'clk
we went to Charl's — where we met
& Kate, Mary & the children —
Home about 10 o'clk — & right to bed.
