## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 29, 1888.

Another delightful day. Spent the m'g finishing A.B.N. Co.
drawing "Place Dom Pedro II" —
After dinner I wrote letter to
Freeland & got [illegible] ready
with drawing for [illegible]. Took
letter & [illegible] to office soon
after 4 when we went out
to ride — Sophine Ireland
did not go with us — Annie,
Jim, Ralph & Greta made the
party — on our return, at 6.30,
got from P.O. a letter from Nellie
enclosing Mary's letter from Venice
which we read with much en-
joyment — After tea I wrote a
letter to Chas., enclosing Mary's
letter in it & before I took it to
the P.O. Then home — Annie read
the end of the mag. story that
she commenced for us last
night — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 30, 1888.

Very pleasant day but my head
ached from my getting up in the
morning till my going to bed at
night — I worked about in a
desultory way — in the gardens &
about the house — I worked on
big acid tray, making it tight in
preparation for the biting in of the
Huntington etching & I made a
[illegible] of the 4" "finisters" Proctor proof
that I have signed for J. D. Waring —
wrote him a letter — & when we
went out riding at 4·45, took
them to the office — took a
short ride — home at 6 — Even if
my head was so uncomfortable
that I lounged about or paced
the piazza untill we went to
bed at 10.
