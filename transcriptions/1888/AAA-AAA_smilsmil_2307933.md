## Left Page

### September, Saturday 8, 1888

Much heavy rain during the
night — awakened before 11 A.m. —
by alarm of fire — a row of small
frame buildings, stores & dwellings —
on the main St. in town were burned. —
Up at 8 o'clk. heavy rain until
after 10 — heavy clouds all day —
growing warmer — fog at night —
Spent some time in the garden
at about 10. 30 got started on my
dry-point of Poppies & stuck to it
until dinner at 1 o'clk. After
that, went down town — & again
before 6. doing a little studio
work in the afternoon — Rec'd.
a letter from Charles, enclosing
a very interesting one from Mary
at Jeintet on Lake Geneva — Evef
Read newspaper — walked on
piazza, & wrote notes to [illegible] —
& to Kimmel & Voigt. To
bed about 11.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 9, 1888

Heavy fog until near 9 Am. Grey
foggy clouds all day — weather growing
much warmer. Up late — bothered
with headache. After bkfst — went to
the garden — after gathering vegetables
wrote a long letter to Mary — At 1 o'clk
took a nap — dinner at 2 — at
4 went out riding taking Annie
Jim, Gretia & Ralph. Back at
5.30 — tea at 6.30 — Welf wrote
a note to Charl & at 8 went to
P.O. with letters — Later, Annie
read aloud a Magazine article —
To bed soon after 10.
