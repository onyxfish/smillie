## Left Page

### January, Thursday 12, 1888

Bright, cold day — To studio at 10 — Worked for an hour on
7×13, getting it into to frame
with final touches — then
commenced a new sketch
about 6×13. "A hill side with
cattle — Before I went to N. A.
f. D. with 7×13 drawing — then
to Bank for Annie — then to
Knoedlers & home to lunch
at 1.20 — back at 2.15. worked
as long as I could see, getting
my new sketch all in — then
about 5 — Started out for a walk.
Went down to 14th S. around
Union Sq. — up B'dway to 5th Ave
— up to Central Park, then home
Dinner at 6.30.    Every
Read — To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 13, 1888

Horrible day — Snow in the early
mf., followed by heavy Rain — In
the evening it cleared.   Got to
studio at 10 & to work on "Hill side
with cattle" 5½×13 —    Mr. Rives
came in in the mf with India-
ink sketch of the late Mr. R — and
urged me to take charge of the work
of getting up a portrait & Re pro-
duction —    Home at 1 — back at
2.30 — Afternoon so dark that I could
not finish my drawing —  After
5 went to O'Neil, photographer —
16th S. & B'dway — & ordered two plain
[illegible] of Mr. Rives's.  Then home, getting
in very much used up.     After
dinner spent the eve'g on lounge
& big chair — so drowsy that I
could not keep awake —  To bed at
10 tho awake until near 1.
