## Left Page

### February, Sunday 19, 1888

A pleasant day — Got up feeling better — The mt'f. spent with my family — at about 12. Jim & I went out for a walk — To 6th Ave & 42d S. & by Elevated R.R. to 72d — Then to 73d S. & called on Joe. Found her in much the same condition as when I saw her last. Home at 1.30 — dinner soon after 2 — after that, Charl came in about 3.30 went with him to his Room — There for an hour — then home. Tea after 7 — Evef. Bro. Geo. came in for a little while. Wrote to Mary & to Chas. Hy. Hart. To J.C.D. before 10.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 20, 1888

A nasty day & very wet. It was 10.30 when I got to the studio and I worked on Mr. Rives' portrait. Geo. brought in Col. Elliott Shepard who is to draw up the A.B.N.Co memorial Resolutions concerning Bro. Will — He wanted some points. At 6 o'clk this mt'f. Georgi's third boy arrived — with blue eyes — a little brown hair & 9 lbs of flesh to begin with — all well at latest dates — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.45. Mr. Greer came in & I sold him a proof "Voice from the Cliffs" for $6.00. Then Annie came in with Jim & we went to the W.C. Exhbn. N.A.d.d. There met Louis Tiffany & wife — Home at 6 — Dinner at 6.30 — Evef. wrote part of a long letter to Saml. Colman — now in Cal.
