## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 28, 1888

Wet day — In bed all day with a
wild headache — a catarrhal cold, con-
gested throat & general cussedness.
The pain was less in the afternoon
& even & at about 9 I got up — went
into the nursery & had bed made
& room aired — Then we got to bed
at about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 29, 1888

Disagreeable m[orning] with rain but
in the afternoon it cleared charmingly.
I got up but did not dare go out of
the house all day — [illegible] my head — eyes,
nose, throat — all feeling exceedingly
uncomfortable — all of the strength
that I had was devoted to the baby,
Annie being so crippled that she
cannot take him — as usual me,
I spent the ev'g with him, to give
Annie a rest. Chas. came in for
a few minutes — Annie read
aloud a couple of chapters from
Marion Crawford's "A Roman Singer,"
to bed before 11.
