## Left Page

### January, Saturday 28, 1888.

Bright, cold day — To studio at 10
to work on big Water-Color. Put
in all the cattle except the
large, central white cow — Home
at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked as
long as I could see — Then
walked down to 16th St. & got from
O'neil Two prints of enlarged phot?
of Mr. Rives. Then up to Private
View of Water Color & spent an hour
there looking at the pictures — Home
at 6.40 — dinner — Evg. Read
aloud until Charl came in —
He stayed until near 11 — Then
I went to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 29, 1888.

Bright, crisp mt. — overcast and
mieder afternoon. Bkf about 9 —
read paper for a while — It was about
12 before I started out & walked up
5th Ave., through the Park, out at 72d
St. — I left a note for W. Verdenal
at the "Dakota" — then to 43 W. 73d St.
where I spent nearly an hour with
Joe & Minn — Joe very quiet and yet
freely yielding to her feelings — walked
home getting in at 2 — Dinner at once.
After that by fire in dining room
wrote to Mary — to h.S. [illegible] all — Two
Robertson — Then with the children —
Tea at about 7 — Evg. Charl came
in — I wrote a note to Jas. Macdonough —
After 10 went to G.C.D. — Then home
to bed.
