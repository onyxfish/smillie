## Left Page

### July, Monday 30. 1888.

Overcast mt — clearing before
noon — showers passed south
of us about 4 o'clt. I took
some proofs from my Tadema
plate — Dinner at 1 — and
then more printing until
4 o'clt- when I washed up
things & at 5 went out for a
short drive with the family.
Back at 6.30 — tea — Eve'g
Edw'd read two political
articles from the "Forum" &
they were discussed with some
spirit by many things — I
signed proofs of Etcteruil
"what o'clk is it" — 11 o'clk
when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 31. 1888.

*Edw'd left for Vt. by 12.30 Stage.*

Again, an overcast mt. followed
by clearing & a hot, drying wind
from the S. W. A delightful rain
free from 6.30 until after 9 p.m.
I spent the mt. taking five
Tadema pf. Dinner at 1 —
then, feeling very tired I took
a rest & nap. Cleaned up
in printing room & at 4.30 we
went out for a drive — Had
to drive home pretty fast to
escape the rains, which we
did by a very few minutes.
Tea — Eve'g Annie & I
read aloud, alternately, the
3d & 4th books of the "Iliad" —
to bed before 11.
more rain during the night
