## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 17. 1888.

Pleasant wtf., lightly overcast in
the afternoon — To Studio at 9.30 —
& worked all the wtf. with graver
& dry-point on Tadema plate —
At 12.30 went to Safe deposit Co.
& made transfer of keys & got larger
box — then home to lunch — back
at 2.30 & worked until 4.30 — then
went to 153 E. 15th S. to call on
Laurie, as I promised, but he was
out — (H.W. Ranger called on me in
the mtf.) — then to Gas. Co. 18th S. & Irving
Place & saw Treas'. about Bonds that will
mature May 1st — then to Century &
wrote out a list of garden seeds — then
to Bridgman's & ordered the seeds
— then home — Evtf., dinner at 7.
Geo. & Emily — Mary Willard & John
Valentine — They spent the evtf.
with us until 10 — we got to
bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 18. 1888.

[illegible] day — overcast with little
showers — To studio at 9.30 — worked
on the Tadema plate — graver &
dry point on foreground figure.
Home to lunch & back as usual —
worked until 5 — then cleared
up studio — a short walk — home
at 6.30 — dinner — Evtf. — wrote
a letter to Herrick. Charl came
in aftn'g & stayed for about an
hour. To bed at about 11.
