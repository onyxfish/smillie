## Left Page

### April, Friday 27, 1888

Lovely day - got to studio at 9.30 -
t worked as closely as I could on the
Tadema plate - At 12 I took two [illegible]
t at 12.30 started for Kimmel & Voigt -
Soon after young Voight got at the
Proving of the plate, Taking 3 on thin
Jats & 3 on India, when I was satisfied
that I could leave him to finish the
Proving - The plate is still far from
finish, but I must give it up to my
great sorrow. I ate a light lunch of
bread & butter while I stood by the press.
About 3 I started back to the studio
where I put things a little in order -
I wrote a letter to the Boston Photo-gravure
Co. & sent them McRhive's drawing to Re-pro-
duce. Home at 6.30 - dinner - Eve.
again with Annie, who has less fever.
Charl came in & spent a little time
with us - It was about 11 when I
got to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 28, 1888

Exceedingly warm day and clear.
After bkfst - went to Brady's & saw new
frame for Water Color - then to "The Chelsea"
222 W. 23d. St. & saw Weldon & Murphy -
then to Safe Deposit 2d. Nat. Bk. & got
Bonds - Municipal Gas Light Co - due May
[illegible] & deposited them to Annie's Ct. Then
soon after 11, to studio. Worked about
awhile until boy came with Tadema
plate from Kimmel & Voigt - then scrubbed
out remarque & home to lunch - waited
in there until Dr. Blackwell came - Annie
seems to be better To-day, having no fever -
but it is evident that her case is not clear
to the Dr. About 3 o'clk. Rode down to Scribner's -
but was surprised to find them closed - half
holiday - then again to studio & spent what
remained of the day working on the Tadema
plate, which, after all, has proved to be another
of my disappointments - I might be able to
finish it to my satisfaction if I had lots more
time - Home to dinner at 6.30 - Eve.
with Annie.
