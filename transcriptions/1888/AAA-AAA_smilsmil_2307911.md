## Left Page

### July, Thursday 26, 1888

A very pleasant day, clouding up in the afternoon, we pray for rain but without hope. In the m't Mr. deSchevernitz & Miss Dwight came up & we spent some time in the studio — At last I got to work upon my Tadema plate with graver — Dinner at 1 — a little more work & at 5 we went out riding — Edw'd, many — Edna & Annie — Back before 7 — Great excitement over the appearance of a skunk that seems to have domeciled himself under the house & parades about with unlimited impudence — I went to Azur Larthrop's for a gun — got one at Jane Austin's — then determined to try poison & with Ed we went down town & got Arsenic & returning set poisoned bait. — It was 11 o'clk. when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 27, 1888

A very small shower about 5 or 6 A.m. Overcast during the m'g, but warm & close — clearing in the afternoon — with thundershowers to the south of us — I spent the m'g taking proofs & working on the Tadema plate — did not get a chance to do much in the afternoon before we went out riding — from 4.30 to 6.30 — Tea about 7 — Eve'g the Skunk excitement broke out again — In spite of arsenic, his lordship appeared again in the early eve'g upon the lawn. Edw'd & I went down town — got shop key from Herrick & Returning got box trap from the shop & set it. It was 9 o'clk. before we got through with it. It was 11 o'clk before we got to bed.
