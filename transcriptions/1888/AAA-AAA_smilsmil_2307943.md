## Left Page

### September, Friday 28, 1888

Much cooder & windy — cloudy —
Some time in the m'g was spent
getting fires under way — in furnace
in studio — studio fire place — N. E. —
cherry room — etching room etc — et c —
then to garden — To work at my
biting at 10.30 — Dinner — again
at work until 3.30 when we went
out riding — Annie, Nellie — Geo. & I
to Paullin Forks & back — home
at 6 — then went to garden &
picked all the tomatoes that
were near ripe — fearing a frost
tonight — then tea — even't much
taken up with house chores —
a little reading after 9 — To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 29, 1888

Bleak, cloudy, cold & windy —
Ther. fell last night to 32°.
Some beautiful sky & atmospheric
effects about the middle of the day —
I worked on my biting until
about 4 o'clk — when, with Annie,
Nellie & Geo. we started out to
make calls — I acting as driver.
We went to Mr. Wm. Jessup's —
Mr. Wm. Parke's — Mrs. B. de Schweinétz
& Mrs. Dud. Lathrop's — then we
took a short drive around the
lake & got in before 6, all pretty
cold — After tea Geo. & I went
down town & spent some time
shopping — home at about 8.30 —
some time with our newspapers
— to bed before 11.
