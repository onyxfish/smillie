## Left Page

### February, Saturday 11. 1888.

Horrible day of sleet ice, rain,
snow & all such things in alternation
& jumble. Much annoyed by a sore
throat. It was 10.30 when I got to
studio. Found that my chest with
the two additional drawers had
been sent home, so I set to work
to get my west closet into order.
I worked at carpentering all the
mg. Home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2.30 & worked as long as I
could see, getting things quite ship
shape — but it will be the work of a
long time to get all of my drawers,
shelves & corners into a cleanly order.
Dinner at 6.30. Eveg. at home
thanking my stars that I did not
have to go out. All of us more or
less under the influences of colds.
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 12. 1888.

In bed all day with a very sore
throat & headache. Got up about
6 o'clk p.m. & spent the evening
before a pleasant fire in the parlor.
Charé with us. To bed again
about 11.30.
