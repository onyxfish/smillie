## Left Page

### February, Monday 13. 1888.

Very bright & mild — Got up after a quiet, restful night feeling much better — Kate left for Po'K. on the 10.30 A.M. train — D. Yale came in at about 10 in the m'g & we spent some time together — It was after 11 when, with him I went to Market, then to Plumbe's & grocers & to Studio — Saw Bernau for a moment. Gathered material for A.B.N.Co. work & home at 12.30. Lunch at 1 — Afternoon, Traced photo & Trant. Tracing to Bristol B°. of Ox team with two negroes. S°. Ave. worked until 5 — then took charge of baby — G°. came in for a few minutes — Dinner at 6.30 — Even'g, with London Ill. News & Punch. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 14. 1888.

Another bright, mild day — To studio at about 10.30 & spent the rest of the m'g fixing up Etching pl's — At 12.30 went to A. G. Newmans & got hardware for printing table off to Herrick — Then home to lunch at 1 — Back at 2.30 — to work on A.B.N.Co. drawing "Ox Team — the Halt"; worked until 4.30 — then to N.A. of D. for a few minutes & saw Mr. Greer of the Etching Club. Even'g, Home at about 6 — Dinner at 6.30 — Even'g read news papers for awhile. Then addressed D.K. circulars for Annie — It was after 12 when I got to bed owing to the humors of Master Ralph — up agains after 1. He did not sleep quietly more than one hour at a time during the night.
