## Left Page

### April, Sunday 15, 1888

A pleasant day altho' cloudy —
up late — bothered with a headache —
took Annie to Collyer's church +
then I went on to the barber's and
had my hair cut — Home before
12 — took Jim up to Central Park
— Showed him the animals + gave
him a breezing — Home at 1.30 —
Dinner at 2. — after that gave up to
my headache + went to bed where
I stayed until after 6 — then
up — Gretta's evef. out, so Annie
\+ I were nurses — In spite of head-
ache I wrote to Mary + to Wm. Parke —
after 10 went out + mailed letters —
To bed after 11 + passed an un-
comfortable night.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 16, 1888

Bright pleasant day — To studio at 10.
All day under the influence of a
mild but sufficiently unpleasant
form of headache — worked on Tadema
plate with graver on draperies — Hex
Sauine made a call + borrowed $2.50.
Chy went to Safe Deposit + put in the box
the mortgage given by Geo. Cook + two bonds
given by J.d. W. Cook — then home to lunch.
Back to studio before 2.30 — worked
until almost 5.30 — Miss Verdenal
came + drew from plaster heads — At
5.30 I went to Bindguain's — then to
Wunderlich's + saw the Rembrandt +
etchings on exhibn. there — Home at 6.30 —
dinner — Evef. with Annie made
out a list of garden seeds — flower +
vegetable — then she read aloud
a portion of one of Minnie Putnam Jacobs'
articles descriptive of her education of
her daughter — To bed soon after 10.
