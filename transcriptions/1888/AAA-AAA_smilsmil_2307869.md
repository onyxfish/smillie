## Left Page

### May, Thursday 3, 1888.

Dull day — I got into feeling out of sorts
Waited about until after Dr Blackwell
had made her morning call & Reported
that Annie had made slight progress —
then walked down Eimer & Amend's —
Cor. 3rd Ave & 18th S. & bought a lot of Anti-
septic gauze & Absorbent Cotton — then
across to Bridgman's. & ordered plants
sent to Montrose — then to Ashland
house & Telephoned to Charl to come
& dine with us To-night & Fake half
to Choral Club Concert — then to Studio
& wrote short letter to Herrick — then
to Geo's Studio & insisted upon his
taking my ticket to the Water-Color
Dinner at the Hotel Brunswick tonight
while I went home & to bed with a
headache —

## Right Page

### May, Friday 4, 1888.

Another dull day — waited about
until after 10 waiting for Dr. B—
who did not come — After 11 when
I got to Studio — wrote to Herrick —
Home to lunch at 1 — near 3
when Dr. B — came — almost 4
when I got to Studio — I fussed
about a while — then to Prize-Fund
Exhib & there met Louis Tiffany &
wife, F. S. Church & others — Home
at 6 — Evng — Read aloud for a
little while to Annie who has
suffered considerable pain this
afternoon although the Dr. Reports
progress — Charl came in a
little before 9 with some bonds that
he has bought for Annie —
To bed about 10.30 —
Late Rel'd. to Po'k on 3.30 P.m. Train
