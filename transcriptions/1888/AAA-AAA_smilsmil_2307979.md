## Left Page

### December, Sunday 9, 1888

Milds, very wet + very dark —
I was a good deal under the
weather — head ache + cold
— head stuffed up + sore throat
I lay about, useless, much of
the time — In the evtf. I
managed, at last, to write
a letter to Geo. Diven — one
of condolence on the death of
Eugene + his daughter-in-law. Mrs.
Geo. Diven — last Sept. — Charl
came in before dinner was over.
Evtf. Annie read aloud some
more Robt. Elsmere — + then
I wrote a short letter to Mary.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 10, 1888

A dark, raw + generally disagreeable
day — It was about 10.30 when I got to
the studio feeling entirely unfit for
work + used up by my cold — Geo.
soon came in + seemed in no better
plight, having passed a bad night
with his sick children — Before noon
I was able to make some progress
in arranging for distribution my
ph. of "Reading Homer" after Alma Tadema.
Home to lunch at 1 — Katie + Wallie
Adams ret. home by 3 oclt. Travis —
To studio at 2.30 — about 3 Yale came
in — he had some plates + did some
work — I did little — Then Saml.
Coleman came in some time after
4 — He + Mrs. C. are in town on a visit.
I got home at 6 — dinner — Evtf.
Annie read aloud a chapter
of Robt. Elsmere + we got to bed
soon after 10.
