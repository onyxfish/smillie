## Left Page

### February, Thursday 23, 1888

Pleasant day — after 10 when I got to studio — then to E.P.P. with drawing for N.W. Rines — then to A.B.N. Co — with "Six-Ox-Team at Rest" — near noon when I got to Drexel, Morgan + Co's — there for almost an hour arranging for a transfer of Annie's cheese cake + Ohio Securities for a Reorganization — then to Charl's for a few moments + home at 1.45 — lunch — after it walked to studio with Annie — she went on to W.C. Sifton + I went in to studio — accomplished nothing beyond some fussing with Etching plt. Priscilla Bently made a pleasant call — Home + dinner at 6.30 — Edith dined with Geo. + Emiley. Annie + I went there after 8 — + before 9 to Ed.wd Cook's in 37th St. Home at 10 —

## Right Page

### February, Friday 24, 1888

Very pleasant day — To Studio at 9.30 — about 11 went down town — first to Receiver of Taxes at 57 Chambers St. then to 31 Chambers St. + [illegible] Croton Amears — then on to the East River Bridge + sauntered Park way over and back enjoying very much the sunshine + fresh breeze. At 12 Rose up to 23d St — went to [illegible] + saw negative after Alma-Tadema's painting that I am to etch for the Scribners — Clarence Cook there. Then to Parkinson's + got photos. of the children — then home to lunch at 1 [illegible]. Wrote note to Marvin. To studio at 2.45. Annie — Mary Cook + Miss Williams called at the studio about 4 o'clk — After 5 I went to McKeever's shop in 42d St. to see some frames under way — Home at 6.30 — dinner Edith with us — Eve'g in parlor, in front of a big fire + feeling [illegible].
