## Left Page

### August, Sunday 19, 1888

A delightful day altho' warm
I spent most of my spare
time writing a letter to Elm-
inger — but as I did not feel
well it was a hard pump.
Annie & Dolphie went to the
Episcopal church & at 12
Jim & I went down to meet
there as they came out, but
missing them we visited with
the Parkes — Kate Riley & family
& Mrs. Dan. Sayre & her family
getting home after 1. Dinner
at 2 — It was before 4 when
we started out for a ride &
just 6 when we ret'd. Tea —
Evef I felt so miserably
out of sorts that I went to
bed soon after tea.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 20, 1888

A pleasant day altho' cloudy
in the afternoon with more
threats of wet — I did not
feel well enough to work so I
dawdled about — after dinner
I wrote a letter to Mary & sent
it to Charl & from Charl I rec'd
a long letter from Kate — At 4
we went out to ride — called
for Mrs. Kate Riley but she had
other engagements — Home
before 6. Paid Tarbell for
his hay — laid in for horses &
cow — $40.33. Evef. most
of it devoted to newspapers —
To bed at 10.30.
