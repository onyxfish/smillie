## Left Page

### June, Monday 4, 1888

*To Ridgefield —*

Delightful day. Up early + at 9
o'clk was at Kimmel & Voegt's in Canal
St — Too early for them — had to wait
almost an hour before Young Voegt
commenced the proving of my plate
"What o'clk is it?" He took [illegible]
on India + [illegible] Imperial. It was
about noon when I got to studio —
Went to Brooks Bros. + bought a pair
of pantaloons — to J. & C. Johnston's +
bought some underclothes. Home to
lunch at 1 — after that, packed bag
ready to start — At 2.30 went to
McKeeve's shop + gave further directions
about repairing old rug — back to the
house at 3.30 — got bag + things + then to
G. C. depot + took 4 o'clk. Boston Exp.
+ got to Ridgefield at 6 — meeting
Annie, Jim + Dr Adams at the depot.
Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g. chatting
until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 5, 1888

A very pleasant day — fine out of
sorts — wakened very early, coughing
+ complaining — Baby at 7.45 — out with
Ralph in a borrowed baby carriage —
After 11 I got to be so uncontrollably
sleepy that I took a good long nap —
Dinner at 2 — after that, Randolph
([illegible]) got a team of horses + at
3.30 we started out. Annie, Jim + I +
Rogen, Grete + Ralph; we went first
south of town — Ralph got to be so uneasy
that we ret'd + left him + Grete at the
house + then drove north — going to
the site for Dr Emmet's new house —
got back at 6. Then Tea — Eve'g.
again spent in general chatting
until 9.30 — To bed about 10.
