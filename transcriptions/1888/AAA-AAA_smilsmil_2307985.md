## Left Page

### December, Sunday 23, 1888

A very fine day — clear & crisp —
with the children & their writing
until 12 — then out for a walk —
met Annie at St. College's & walked
with her to Walter's — then on to studio
Dinner at 2.30 — again with my
family & writing. went through the
form of taking tea at 6.30 — Continued
to write in dining room until
Chas. Collins came to talk Century
matters at my request. We talked
until after 10 — I wanted his counsel
to guide me in my work on the Home
Com. To bed at 11, after having
gone to J.C.D. with some letters.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 24, 1888

A delightful day, cloudless & quite
mild — To studio about 10 & spent
most of the day finishing with india
ink the photo. proofs from my drawing
Annie's father. Home at 1 — lunch —
to bank at 2.30, cut some coupons & made
a heavy deposit for Annie — To studio
at 3 — feeling quite out of sorts I did
very little — At 5 walked up to Jale's
& left a bt. of my Alma-Tadema.
Home to an early dinner at 6 — Eve. &
much night-before Christmas preparation
taking pkg & receiving them — Charl.
came, got up his sheet & rehearsed
with his magic lantern. I hung
pictures in parlor, bed room — after 11
when we got to bed.
