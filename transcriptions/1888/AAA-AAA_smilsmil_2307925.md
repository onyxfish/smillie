## Left Page

### August, Thursday 23, 1888

Clear cool mf. Ther. 49° when we
got up — having been down to 38°.
during the night. I spent some
time in the vegetable garden
trimming things there — It was
nearly 11 o'clk. before I got to
work in Studio tracing photo
for A.B.N. drawing — Dium
at — a little more work &
soon after 3 we went out
riding — called for Mrs. Kate
Riley & she went with us —
altho' the sky was overcast
with clouds we had a very
pleasant ride & got home a
little after 6. Eve'g with
newspaper mint near 9 — then
a walk on piazza for half an
hour — then read a magazine
story & to bid before 11.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 24, 1888

Brilliant, cloudless & cool
mf. Rec'd by Exp. Two parchment
proofs from Stokes Bros. f "What
o'clk is it?" for signature — one
was so bad that I would not
sign it — Ret'd them immediately.
Late in the afternoon I Rec'd a letter
& in the eve'g wrote one in reply —
Spent some time in vegetable garden
& then about the barn directing Herrick.
It was after 11 when I got to work on
A.B.N. Co. drawing — had a hunt
without being able to find the Bristol
board that I brought up with me —
It was after 4 when we started
up on our ride — Drove to South Pond —
a new drive, & were very much pleased —
the afternoon delicious & the horses in
good spirits — Home at 6.30 — Tea —
