## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 7, 1888

Dull day — Rain commenced at about 6 p.m. + fell heavily during the night. To studio at 10.30 — I got hold of cattle W.C. drawing +
worked on it for an hour or more crowding pure color into parts of it. Then to work on Banco de Cubico drawing for H.B.N. Co, Home at after going to water color club.
1.30 — back at 2.30 — found there Mulligan, two carpenters' men — had shelves put into large, dark room closet and sent to the shop my largest chest to have more drawers put into it — Then worked on drawing as long as I could see — should have said that at 12.30 went to H.d. + D. + there met Kate, Annie and Jim. Kate went to lunch at George's + Jim walked up home with me.

Evelf. Annie, Kate + I went to Charls — he has been kept in-doors all day by a severe cold — we stayed there until 10 then home in a pelting rain — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 8, 1888

Wet + Rainy most of the day — At night, cooler + clear. To studio at 10 + to work on H.B.N. Co. drawing "Banco de Cubico" — Home at 1 — Kate lunched with Fanny Kimball — back at 2.30 + after a little more work on drawing finished it — then gave up to a headache that had bothered me all day + stretched myself in big chair for an hour. Then wrote a note to Macdonough and one to Herrick — Home soon after 6. dinner at 6.30 — Evelf. too much used up to go to Water-Color Society meeting to vote on the two prizes. Sat in my chair by the fire until after 10 —
