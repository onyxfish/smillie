## Left Page

### August, Friday 31, 1888

Overcast wtf. with S.E. wind.
Rain commenced about 4 P.M.
& feel, at times, heavily during the
night. I got up with a head
ache that prevented me working.
Between 10 & 11 Jim & I walked
over to Mr. Roger Searle's to get his
advice in the matter of setting out
a Strawberry patch, but he was
not at home. I stayed with Jim
for an hour while he amused him-
self by the lake side. Then home —
made up some photographic sol-
utions — Dinner — then for awhile
gave up to my headache — did
some weeding until the Rain
came — At 6 went down town —
messages — tea at 7 — Evef
for some time in dark room —
no luck so far with Carbutt
plates — then read newspaper,

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 1, 1888

A cloudy day — I spent much
time in the garden in the wtf
& did not get to work in studio
at all — In the afternoon, worked
among the Raspberry bushes setting
out the Runners — did not go out
riding because the weather was
too threatening — went down town
twice in the afternoon — tea early —
Evef, reading & chatting & to bed
after 10.
