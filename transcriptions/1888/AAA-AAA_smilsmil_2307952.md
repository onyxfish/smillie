## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 16, 1888

Again heavy fog in the mtf. By the middle of the mtf — the fog
had lifted into clouds — a part of the afternoon was beautiful & clear
before 5 o'clk. there was a thunder storm with heavy rain — clouded
evef. Most of the mtf was spent in studio packing up. About 12
Jim & I went down town — back at 1 — dinner — After that, out on
the lawn photographing geese — in dark room from 4 to 5.30 — went
down town before 6. Evef
wrote some notes — one to miss Hallowell accepting an offer of
$125 for Water Color "Feeding by the way side." To the P.O. at 9 o'clk — to bed
at 10.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 17, 1888

Considerable rain during the night — a nasty wet morning —
clearing pleasantly about noon —
a shower in the afternoon & a breezy, cool moonlight night — I
spent the mtf about attending to many things — About 11 went
with Jim down town — back before 1 — much of the afternoon
varnishing with asphaltum var. the wire fire place screens — a
troublesome, dirty job — Rec'd a letter from Geo. enclosing a
long one from Mary. Evef wrote a note to Chiart enclosing
Mary's letter to him. Wrote a note to Kettle — condolence on the
death of his wife.
