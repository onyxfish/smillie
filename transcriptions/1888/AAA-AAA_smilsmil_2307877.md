## Left Page

### May, Saturday 19, 1888

A dull day with some struggling sunlight about noon. To studio before 10 — after a time got to work on etching "What o'clk is it?" I hacked and scratched all over it with dry point — then, wet some paper & took a proof — Have to lunch soon after 1. & back about 2.30 — worked & proved until 6.45 then home to dinner — After a visit, about 8.30 I returned to studio & spent the rest of the evng cleaning up. It was near 10 o'clk. when I went home & then I got to bed as quickly as possible.

Chas. Collins called at the studio in the afternoon.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 20, 1888

A pleasant day but it took all the morning to clear — The wf. until noon was spent in family cares; then Annie & I left the children & went out, rode up to 72°. S— crossed the Park which was exceedingly delightful — & went to Jo's in W. 73°. S— There we saw her, Minn & Walter — Four months to-day since Will died. Then we walked back across the Park & took car at 72°. Home at 1 — dinner at 2 — Mary, the Waitress taken sick — Dr. [illegible] sent for — came in the evng. Follicular Tonsiltis — Nreta's day out, so Annie & I devoted ourselves again to the children — a cup of tea at 7 — Jo & Nellie came in for a while in the evng. I wrote to Mary & to Herrick at 10 to G.C.D. & posted letters — To bed at 11.
