## Left Page

### Sun. March 25, 1894

**Weather:** A nasty Easter sunday — cold, raw \+ threatening all the w[ay] — rain coming in the afternoon \+ night. Late bkfs.
It was after 12 when Jim, Ralph \+
I went out for a walk in the patting
rain. We went into the St. Patrick's
Cathedral \+ heard the end of an orchestral piece of music — then home. Dinner
a little before two, to give A. \+ the boys
time to get to a children's service at
Collyer's ch. I did not go out
again but wrote a number of letters
\+ notes. Tea at 6.30 — Evef Char
came in.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 26, 1894

**Weather:** Bright, cold \+ windy. After bkfs. went out \+
attended to usual domestic matters \+ had a
short walk — then to Mr. Morgan's with the
Terra Cotta colored proof — he is the most ag-
gravatingly indifferent man that I have yet
met — takes no interest in the work he has ordered
— hardly looks at it \+ will give no definite instructions — the plate is practically accepted but he
keeps me dawdling along getting proof after proof
\+ spending a half day at a time in following some
ignorant Slim of his. I got him to say that
he would want a hundred pfs. Then home
\+ worked awhile on head of ul— dry painting
on some broad parts of forehead — [illegible] —
Howe came to lunch — not fairly ins two
forming a good sized party — Jim out \+ sorts.
Fever \+ sore throat — probably indigestion \+ cold —
worked for an hour on [illegible] head — ? Geo. Sharps,
the Collotype plate man, called to see me in answer to a note from me asking prices for gelatin
forming etching classes — At 4 went to N. A. \+ so
annoyed that Hffy. should give all $250. to
proving the bl. plate —. then walked home —
very cold \+ windy — Dinner at 6.30 — every
Annie, Mary \+ Bessie went to the Opera —
Walküre. I went to Council meeting —
The Hanging com. Rec'd the Council in the
galleries at about 10 o'clk. \+ the Staring Life and
inspected \+ hanging approved — then in
council room the Hanging com. were entertained
with welch Rarebit, New Jersey champagne \+ stories
until near 12 o'clk — then home — Annie \+ the
others came in soon after — To bed at [illegible].
