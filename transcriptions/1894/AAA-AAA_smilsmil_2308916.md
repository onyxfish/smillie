## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 6, 1894

**Weather:** Pleasant mlg. — Rain later in the day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant mlg. — Rain later in the day — Dead busted — In bed all day — Influenza, I suppose — sore throat — pain in head and general misery — Got up in the evef and was in parlor for some time. Charl came in & took Kate to Lyceum.
Gretta took note to Pierpont Morgan.

overcast mlg., clearing afternoon. I had my bkfst in bed, after which I got up — nursed myself carefully as I am fearful that I cannot get ready for the Century Club show next Sat. evef.
