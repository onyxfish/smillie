## Left Page

### Thur. June 7, 1894

Jas. wright died at 3.30 this mg. \-

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouds to sunshine & wind. Boy with a Telegram at the door about 6 o'clk. After 6&p I wrote to Herrick, then, with R - went to Grocer etc - Then to Mad. Sq. Garden & took some photo. snap shots at it — then to N.A. — then to fountain, Mad Ave & 23d S. More snap shots then to 2d Nat. Bnk — then home to lunch — Roger A. lunched with us & afterwards went with A. & the boys to the Navy Yard. I went to Wall S. met Charl & to Cullen's with European Photos to be mounted — then to Boyle's in Fulton S. then to Heywood's in Canal S. & bought Table & seat for Montrose — then to Sharp's 13, Baxter S. & bought more copper plates then home at 5 & had a short rest before Annie & the boys rele. At 6.30 with A. went to Geo. Grimley's in W. 42d S. met there Muma D — & John Valentine & had a very good dinner every we amused ourselves playing Fan Tan — supposed to be a Chinese game of cards — Muma & Valentine walked home with us — In before 11.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 8, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant & beautiful day. After Bkfst. I went down to Geo. B. Sharp's, 13 Baxter S. with a lot of his copper plates to have a finer finish put upon them — thence to K. & V's & there I spent the rest of the morning signing the Morgan portrait pf. Home to lunch a little after 1 o'clk — after that I went to Newman's in U. 29th S. & got my pole rocker fixture — of course, it was not finished exactly as I directed — then home with R — went to the barber's & each of us had our hair cut & our heads shampoo'd — then I went up to 155 W. 40th S. & Dr. H. D. Allen pulled out one of R's teeth, very deftly, to make place for a second tooth now crowding in behind. R. stood it very well & we got home at about 6 — Dinner before 7 — Evening I wrote a note to Mrs. Silas H. Witherbee asking her, in as pleasant a manner as I knew how, if she would be willing to have a studio built on the top of her stable in 36th S. near 3d Ave.
