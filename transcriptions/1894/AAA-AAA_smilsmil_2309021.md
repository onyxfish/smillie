## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 4, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful, brilliant, September like day - so cool in the m'f that a fire was desirable, \+ we had one in dining room \+ in studio. I got up before 7 \+ went out for half an hour on horseback before bkfs. After bkfs - I got to work upon a pen sketch, based upon view from studio's bay window \+ spent the rest of the m'f at it. After dinner to work on sketch of the house from the lawn, south. The wind was so high \+ it was so cool that I could not continue to work out of doors; had to go in to studio. neither of the sketches gave me any satisfaction - I do not have sufficient practice. They are for Quarterly Illustrator - Harry C. Jones. Eve'f, went to P.O. after tea - Mail late - had to wait until 8.45 - then home \+ read awhile newspaper \+ Century for Aug.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 5, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another day, beautiful \+ cool - almost cold. Got up with a headache that troubled me all day. Bkfs at 9 - some time in the garden. Then, in studio where I revised sketches made yesterday. Dinner before 2. Afternoon Annie, Ralph \+ I went to fair ground at 4 o'clk. Negro open-air meeting with some jubilee singers - singing over when we got there. N. Barney came back with me to explain a catch on a saddle - then I took him through the house. Mary, Kate \+ the girls came before 5 \+ took tea with us at about 6.30. Eve'f, I wrote to Harry C. Jones. After 9, took Mary \+ others to Sayres \+ then I went on to P.O. Then home \+ soon to bed.
