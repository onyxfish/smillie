## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 1, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A charming day — I got up feeling
much better about the throat but
did not think it safe to go out be-
fore the middle of the day when I
took a walk up Mad. Ave. to 52d. St.
Lunch soon after 1 — Sheldon came
in late with Jim & Ralph, lunched
with us, then Annie, Kate & the three
boys went off to a Menagerie-Circus
where they met a lot of the Cooks and
Cook youngsters, the children all
being on "a treat" from Grace Ireland.
I worked on my Ballerino Campanile
as long as I could see, getting it finished.
Charl came in for a little while.
Dinner at 6.30 — Evef, reading in
magazine until Charl came in.
Got to bed early.
