## Left Page

### Sun. July 15, 1894

**Weather:** Bright mtf. clouded before noon  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ light shower — In the afternoon two more showers, close together, after which the air was decidedly cool. I had time before the first shower to look over the three gardens, pick some peas, etc. etc. Then, after the shower + before dinner I did some office work, that is, I drew some checks to pay bills \+ answered a note or two. After dinner I spent some time oling + coloring some of the oak that Henick is to put up in the new bath room. Evg. I wrote to M. Orue [illegible] M.C. a. in reference to studio, + to S.S. Farrington, took them to P.O. after 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Mon. July 16, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delicious day altho' very warm in the sunshine — lovely clouds — cloudless late P.M. + moonlight night. I got up with a headache + fought agt. it all day — mtf. with Henick + about generally — Role of Tapestrolea for covering wall of lower bath room arm — and I spent some time with it — trying to stretch it on the wall, but there is so much cockle in it that I shall have to have it pasted down solid. for some time watching + some grinding + fixing tools that have been neglected or abused during our absence — Evg., after tea, before 8 o'clk. went with A. to call on Mr. Gilbert + his daughter, Mrs. Winant, + her husband, now occupying the Beardsley cottage opposite the end of our lane. There for half an hour and then ret'd. home + sat upon piazza enjoying the wonderful beauty of the night until near 10 o'clk.
