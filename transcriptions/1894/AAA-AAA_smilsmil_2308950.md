## Left Page

### Thur. March 15, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Raw, disagreeable m'f — snow flurry about 2 o'clk — sleet, hail, rain following — I went down town, getting to the office of W. Phillips at 11 o'clk. He promptly agreed to raise N. A. loan from $5000 to $8000 — then to Carter & Ledyard's at 54 Wall S. \+ arranged to have the m'f sent to me to-morrow for signatures — then to Charl's — \+ then home at 12.30. Lunch at 1. I wrote letters until 5 o'clk. Then I took a nap — Dinner at 6.30 — Em'f my cold came down on me again \+ I feel horribly stupid. Charl came in at about 9.

## Right Page

### Fri. March 16, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful bright day — air fresh and enjoyable. Went to school with R — then down 5 Ave. stopping at Ortgies' Gallery to look at some fine p'fs — for sale. Then on to 2? Nat. Bk. where I attended to some N. A. business — then to Kurtz — then to N. A. \+ then back to 42? S. \+ got R — Lunch at 1 — Afternoon I wrote to Koshler — \+ some alternates. Twilight, went out \+ had a walk — rather dragy — Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g. Katie \+ I had a sketch class, both of us making a sketch from Annie.
