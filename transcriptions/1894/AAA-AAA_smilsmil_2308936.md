## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 15, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day of sleet \+ slush, clearing windy \+ cold late in the afternoon. As soon as I could get away after b\[illegible\]. I went to N. \+ V. \+ got three pfs of the Morgan head. \- a great disappointment to me. I cannot get exactly the sort of proof I want \- will have to take hold of the printing myself \- then, since touching up a proof after the painting I have cut loose from my moorings to the photo. \+ I now feel very much at sea \- After getting pf. V \- talked to me about a project he has of getting out a fine folio of 12 plates in Edition de luxe. I could not encourage him \- Home to lunch at 1 \- after that spent some time tracking down pfs and \+ then worrying over them \- wrote note to Herrick about bath-room floor \- \+ then worked until 5.30 writing out part of a statement of the financial affairs of the N.A. of D \- then I walked up to Central Park \+ back, getting in just in time for dinner \- Every wrote note to Lee \+ Lee concerning Doré dyon mtg.

Morganhead \- 5 h.

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 16, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very cold day with high wind. Took R. to school \- Then I went to V. Pierpont Morgan's \+ spent the rest of the mtg there \- had the painting taken down \+ placed where I could see it \+ then worked with crayon upon the proof. left at 12.20 \+ went for \[illegible\] \- lunch at 1 \- Immediately after, I went to Safe Deposit Co. \+ got from the f.d. box the Doré Lyon Bond \+ mtg., took them to Farrington \+ asked him to take them to Lee \+ Lee, lawyers, 20 Nassau St. as they are to commence foreclosure proceeding. Then walked up to Morgan's and until nearly 4 o'clk. continued my crayon work on proof \- then home \+ looked over pfs. as long as I could see \- Dinner at 6.30. At 3.30 this afternoon Katie T. went up to P'k. to attend "Vig. ceremonies" at Vassar College tomorrow. Every very quiet, Reading.

Morganhead \- 5 h.
