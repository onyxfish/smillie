## Left Page

### Wed. April 18, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day — Took R. to school — etc — then wrote a note or two — thru to 2? Nat. Buck. deposited the Morgan Check for $1.000 \+ paid off $500 loan with which, five years ago, I bought lot in Greenwood. Home at 1. lunch at 1.30 — R \+ I went to barbers \+ had our hairs cut \+ heads shampoo'd. Then, as I had a wretched headache I lay on the bed for an hour. Up at 6 \+ dressed — Dinner at about 7 — Walter \+ Louise — Chas. Collins — \+ Dr. Churchill Carmalt in addition to ourselves. Jim \+ Ralph had their dinner at Marys — The party seemed to have a very good time altho' Emma, by having her escort come for her at 9.30, broke it up at a very early hour.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 19, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some clouds, some rain bet. 9 \+ 10 A.M. \+ later, some sunshine — \+ a light spatter of rain at night — generally raw. Took R. to school, etc — Then wrote a long letter of instructions to Herrick — Then, as my head troubled me very much I spent an hour on the lounge before lunch. About 3 o'clk I walked up town \+ wandered about the 8th Ave. entrance to Central Park looking for a site for the new N.A.f.D. building. At 5 went to Art Student's league \+ met Etching class — had an hour with them — half of that time spent biting in a clever little Sketch by W. Young — I was amused by hearing, \+ seeing, a part of the orchestral rehearsal, a part of the entertainment of the Socy. of Am. Fakirs — Home to dinner at 7. Tickets for Choral Club Concert. Madison Sq. Garden Concert room — Annie, Katie \+ I went \+ heard a very good Concert, \+ after it, walked up home — I went to bed feeling very uncomfortable.
