## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 10, 1894

**Weather:** Bright & breezy — pretty cold wind  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkfs. I got thro' with my usual domestic [illegible] \+ then went down town. First to 9 Pine S. \+ spent some time with Mr. Vaudset over N.A. Real estate matters — then to Mr. Lee's 20 Nassau S: some time with him on N.A. matters — then to Mr. Wm Phillips — 54 Wall S: — more N.A. business — then to 54 Broad S. \+ saw Ernest Flagg and had him explain to me his Tilden \+ Historical library \+ N.Y.P.L. \+ [illegible] scheme — The three corporations, none grand [illegible], to occupy the site of the Reservoir — Fifth Ave. 40–42? S: — with some rough sketches, he presents an exciting \+ alluring project. After 1 o'clk. went to Charles \+ we lunched together — we then went to Cullen (photo) in low S. I got some European photo views that had been mounted for me — Then I went to Home Ins. Co — then to Mr. [illegible] office well 45 S — \+ after 3 o'clk. I went home \+ got a rest before dinner — after that until near 9 o'clk. — unpacking boxes just rec'd this afternoon from Montrose. Then I went to Century Club — off night, but very full attendance — among others, Mr. Rob. Gordon who am. this afternoon on Cunard S. [illegible] I was at the club until 11 o'clk — nicely but tried, unsuccessfully, to get hold of Mr. [illegible] Bigelow to learn how far he approves of his interested in the Flagg Project referred to above. It was so surrounded all the evg. \+ so gave it up.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 11, 1894

**Weather:** Bright \+ pleasant — up late  **Temp:** [illegible]

after to where we finished Hfs. [illegible] Then I spent some time, completing the unpacking of the Montrose boxes — At o 1 o'clk. I went to Union League Club \+ by appointment met Charl \+ we spent an hour looking at the collection of Illustration's work, some of which is of a very high order. At 2, home to dinner — Before that was over, Geo. \+ Nellie came in. They sat \+ talked for some time — After 4 Geo. \+ I walked up 5th Ave., he left me at 48 S. \+ I went on up to the Park \+ then home. At 6, Annie \+ I went to the Ireland's in 17th S. to celebrate Emma's Jubilee Birth Day — It is hard to realize that she is 50 years old. Mr. \+ Mrs. Paul Jones (Constance Andrews) came in \+ talked about their baby — It was nearly 11 o'clk. when we got home.
