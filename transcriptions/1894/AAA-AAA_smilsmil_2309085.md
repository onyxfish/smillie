## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 10, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still cloudy + saw during the day. At night, a feeble attempt at snow. To studio on f. + afternoon — worked on Crécy drawing + hope that I have got it finished. Had Farrington go down town to Lee + Lee's for me. Mr. VanPelt called at the house in the ev'g. before dinner, to tell me of the progress of negotiations for stable property in 36th S. for studio. Ev'g + went to Council meeting which was interesting + kept me late as I did not get home until 11.30

\* VanP.'s call was on Tues. ev'g. — not on Monday.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 11, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable, wet day. Mary was expected to arrive from Po'k. but, owing to the weather, did not come. I did not go to studio but spent the day at home at work upon Report on Sites to be presented at regular N. A. meeting to-morrow night. All of the time spent in arranging material which is voluminous and chaotic. Ev'g. I went to a print sale at Ortgies' gallery — a change had been made so that what I wanted did not come in until to-morrow at? home. Annie read aloud a M. S. written by Mr. Percival Chubb. "Toleration in Art, Music + Literature". Read before the Unitarian League (Woman's — Then I got to work upon my site Report + wrote until 11.30
