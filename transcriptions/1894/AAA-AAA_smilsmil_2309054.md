## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 9, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant for a little while in the early mg. then cloudy + bleak + cold. —
Katy, the cook, went to Binghampton on the early train + Ret? on the late one —
After looking after Herrick + the painter I got to work with my water-colors +
made a little opaque color sketch of the lion at entrance of Lindan har[bor] one of four that I have planned for 5×7 experimental dry-ground, aquatint plates. I went down town in the mg + again in the afternoon with Ralph. — Eveg I wrote titles to a number of the mounted European photos.

Tremendously heavy rain during the night.

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 10, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Floods of rain thro' most of last night + continuing during the morning. At one time in the afternoon the clouds broke away + the sunshine but again the clouds came down + everything was wet. I worked steadily in Studio all day upon the four 5"× 7" water color sketches started a day or two ago. — I finished "The lion at Lindan" + carried the other three on part way to finish. — Bet. 5 + 6 I went down — Annie going with me to Mr. Daut. Sayres. — Eveg. newspaper + a note to Farrington.
