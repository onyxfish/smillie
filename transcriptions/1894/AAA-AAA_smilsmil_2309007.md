## Left Page

### Sat. July 7, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cool, cloudy, windy day with a smart shower after 1 o'clk. \+ again, in the evef. Had a fine in dining room in the m'f \+ again at night. Ther. on Piazza in the afternoon 54°. I was out of doors most \+ the day, altho' a good part of the afternoon I was at work in the shop making an experimental box for the laying of dry-aquatint grounds. About 6 - Raelph \+ I went down Town \+ I got my second awning from Newcomb. After Tea Annie was going to call on Florence darling \+ Mrs. Danl. Sayre but was prevented by a shower — I went down to P.O. \+ butcher's at 8 o'clk. Then home \+ read newspapers.

## Right Page

### Sun. July 8, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph's Birth day.

Clouded \+ very cool — like a Sep'. day — 55°. before 9. After that I was out and about the place until just before dinner at a little before 2 — out again after dinner — went with Annie \+ the boys out N.W. from the house, thro' pastures, lots \+ woods over to the Sayre farm and then back, thro' woods to the Reservoir \+ home by the quarry at about 5 o'clk. Then I bussed myself potting Rimmes \+ mulWhite Strawberry plants \+ tying up lettuces until tea time — after 6 — Evef. Reading Waring's book "Modern Methods of Sewage Disposal".
