## Left Page

### Sun. May 20, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day with much wind & rain growing quite cold at night. Continued my clearing up thro' all the m'f. It was 2.25 when we sat down to dinner. Geo. & Smiley Cook dined with us — Tea at 7 — after that I went to Walters for a short time to enquire about the condition of Louise who underwent an operation some weeks ago & has been very miserable since — Then home. Annie packed her trunk preparatory to starting for Cambridge to-morrow m'f Charl came in — He starts for Boston to-morrow p.m.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 21, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Annie went to Cambridge.

Dismal m'f, followed by cold wind & rain. Bkfs. early — Mrs A. had some Diet Kitchen business to attend to & at 9.30, in a heavy rain, we went to G.C. Station & A. got away on 10.23 train. I spent the most of the day working upon a number of proofs of McMorgan head trying to bring them up to standard so that I could sign & turn them in with other p.p. to Mr. J.S.M — For a time the rain held up in the afternoon & just before 6 I went to N.A., got something from the etching room & ret'd to dinner. Eve'g. at 8.15 I went to Council meeting — made interesting by the presence of the new members elect for the coming council — Walter Shirlaw — Olin Warner & Jas. M. Hart. There was not much business & some reminissing over men & times that were past. Home at 11.
