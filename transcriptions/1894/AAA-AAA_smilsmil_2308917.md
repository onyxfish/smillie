## Left Page

### Mon. Jan. 8, 1894

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — Got up feeling somewhat better — About the middle of the m'f I got to work on Ravells Cathedral Tower & stuck to it steadily for about a couple of hours — lunch at 1.30 — gave another hour & a half to the work in the afternoon & think that I have forwarded the drawing satisfactorily — At 5 I lay down & had a little nap — Before 6 Geo. came in & stayed to dinner. — on his way to N.A. Council meeting — of course I could not go — much to my chagrin. Ev'f Elwyn Waller came in with some books for Annie & spent an hour or more enlightening her as to the chemical products of Petroleum & coal tar.

## Right Page

### Tues. Jan. 9, 1894

**Weather:** overcast & disagreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast & disagreeable. I got up in a very bad temper having been nearly strangled during the night, it being impossible for me to breathe thro' my nose. I could not get to work at my drawing in the m'f — but in the afternoon I worked for more than two hours on it. Ev'f T. W. Wood came in for about half an hour to have a talk with me upon Academy affairs that are now in very bad shape. I do not envy him his position as Pres't. & wish that I were out of office as Treas't.
