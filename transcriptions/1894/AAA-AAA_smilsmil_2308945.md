## Left Page

### Mon. March 5, 1894

**Weather:** Very pleasant \+ very mild  **Temp:** —

To school with R — then to work on Morgan head until 12.15 when I went for R — lunch at 1.30 — Miss Joshay came \+ stayed overnight. worked on head until near 4 — then gave it up \+ went out — to Kurtz — nat. in — then to Hunters and left glasses there to be repaired — home at 6 — dinner. Every Council Meeting at the N. A. — Rel was out \+ reports that Val seems to be improving — the meeting was full \+ active \+ it was after 11 before I got away \+ after midnight when I got to bed.

morgan head 4 h.

## Right Page

### Tues. March 6, 1894

**Weather:** A bright \+ very warm day  **Temp:** — at bfks. I rec'd a note from Haber Bishops refusing me permission to sell pl. from jade plate \+ asking me to send to him such pls. as I had in my possession, asserting that all belong to him — I wrote a reply as temperately as I knew how. Then I went to N.Y. \+ had pls. of Morgan head, over which I am very unhappy — I cannot get the effects that I wish — I can never finish it I'm afraid — Home soon after 12 — lunch after 1 — Miss Joshay did not come in to lunch — She left us in the afternoon. I mounted the three proofs \+ before it went to Hunter, the optician, \+ arranged to have my working glasses straightened a little. Then to Kurtz — \+ got directions for the preparation of Asphaltum varnish — then to N.A. \+ got my jade pl. and carried it home. Stopped at Butler's \+ ordered passe partout for Morgan's head — Had a short rest before dinner. Eve'g I went to Siley's \+ saw him for a moment to ask him if he knew anything concerning the Rights of such etchers to pl. of their own work — he had no positive information out — and that usage undoubtedly allowed them to keep a reasonable number of pls. Then to Walter Cook's — company — did not go in — then home \+ wrote to Lee \+ Lee concerning something on mf. to Symington about the jade plate sale fiasco \+ to Walter Cook.

Recd news of the death of old Mr. Knudsen at St. Norwalk [illegible] —

morgan head. 3 h.
