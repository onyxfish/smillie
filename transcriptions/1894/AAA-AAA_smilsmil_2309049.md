## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 29, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day \- I attended to many small matters \- wrote a letter to Robbins \+ at about 10.30, with horse \+ buggy, took Fanny down on Wyalusing Creek Road \- we carried a large basket of provisions, shawls, jackets, wraps \+ overshoes, for the rest of the family who had walked on ahead. They got to the Rendezvous just as we did \- there I left F. \+ ret\- home. Had a very quiet dinner alone \- At 2.30 I went to A. Lathrop's \- got his team of greys \- hitched them to our platform wagon \+ went after our party. Picked them up \+ driving further down the creek turned to our left \+ went over the hill to Sc montrose \+ thence across \+ around by Brewster's \+ Harker's ponds \+ so, home, getting in at 5 o'clk. Some writing at night \+ more European photos \-

## Right Page

### Sun. Sept. 30, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delicious day \- very warm \- 9.30 when I had my bkfs\- The rest of the morning was spent out of doors \- in garden, etc \- A little writing before dinner at 2 o'clk. After dinner I wrote a letter to Charl \+ a note to Chas. Mac Rae, Real Estate agent \- Then Annie, Fanny \+ I went wandering over the place \- down to swamp in pasture lot \+ I examined the work of ditching now going on. Eve\+ I wrote in answer to ticket agent D.L.\+W. R.R\- complaining of his treatment \- Annie read aloud for a little while.
