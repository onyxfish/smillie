## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 23, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- Easterly & southerly wind changing in the afternoon to S.W. \- with a light shower at about 6.30 \- Blcf t\- after 9 \- after that I spent the m't in the garden & about the place \- Dinner at 2 \- In studio for an hour or so getting my papers together to take with me to N.Y. to-morrow \- Then, until dark, pruning young fruit trees & flowering shrubs \- After tea I made studio & Etching room a little more Tidy \-

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 24, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** 114 W. 73° 5·

Started for N.Y. Stayed over night at J. C. Nicoll's

Up at 6 o'clk. Clear, crisp & breezy m'g. After an early bf't. we all went to the R.R. Station \- Annie & the four children went as far as Hart Lake to enjoy a Pic nic & back at 2.30 \- Hope they won't freeze \- after an uneventful journey I got to N.Y. on time & by Barclay S't ferry went downtown & direct to Mr. Lee's office \- met him there \- Talked over matters concerning sale of N. A. appointment to meet Mr. Read next N.Y. \- Then to Chas' office \- he's down east \- Then I went up town \- To Walter's house \- no one at home but servants \- W. & L. to return to-morrow \- Edw't. not in town \- Charl \- [illegible] not in town \- I was alone with N.Y. Then I went to the Century \- washed up \- Rested & had a very good dinner \- afterwards \- Seven N. A's [illegible] in appearances \- J. G. Brown \- Maynard, Nicoll, Shirlaw \- Guy \- E. N. Ward & [illegible] \- a large [illegible] did not remain after dinner & apparently had no interest in common with the others. In the small library room I explained the transaction & the sale of the N.A. of D \- as far as it had gone, in detail \- , we were busy answering questions & asking them, until towards 11 o'clk \- Then Nicoll was kind enough to take me home with him \- 114 W. 73° 5·. Being a country man the noise, thro' the night, from elevated Railway & cable cars, disturbed me very much and the little sleep I got was not very restful
