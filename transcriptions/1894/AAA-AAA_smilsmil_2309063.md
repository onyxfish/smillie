## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 27, 1894

**Weather:** Delightful day \- clear \- sunny, \+ very mild.  **Temp:** [illegible]

It was 11 o'clk. before I got to work in studio, continuing the biting of the Ravello Cathedral Tower \- very slow work \- I do not get absorbed in it as I would if I did not have so many other things demanding more or less attention. \- After dinner had a little sunning on Piazza with Annie \- then again to work on my plate until abt 4:30 \- then Annie \+ I went to P.O. \+ Ret'd. by way of Jail Road \+ thro' our swamp lot where Giorgio Vaccari is digging ditches \- captured Ralph there \- then spent a little time in the twilight in tree tops near the house looking for dead wood. \- Evef. Reading newspaper \+ Harper for Nov.

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 28, 1894

**Weather:** Brilliant, cloudless, \+ very warm day \- a basking day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pkft. at 9 \- after that I absent [illegible] until 1.30, clipping \+ pruning the hedge of maples that hide the fair-ground wall on our Northern bound[ary]. Then dinner \- immediately after that, Annie, Ralph \+ I went out for a ride \- \+ had a very pleasant one, \- back just before 5 \- worked as long as I could see and finished the clipping of the maples. Then a promenade in the Piazza, enjoying the dusky twilight \+ the church bells. \- Evef. wrote titles on a number of mounted European photos.
