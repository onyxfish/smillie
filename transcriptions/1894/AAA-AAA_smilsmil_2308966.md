## Left Page

### Mon. April 16, 1894

**Weather:** Beautiful, mild day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After taking R. to school \+ attending to other domestic duties, I went to K. T V's — in Canal 8 — \+ took eight morgan f[?]s from the boards where they had been tacked to stretch — Then home — and until lunch time I was at work cutting margins of p[l]ps \+ getting them ready to deliver to J. P. m — This P. m — after lunch I completed the work \+ also got some things ready for the league etching class — It was 3.30 before I could get ready to start for N.A. f[?] — where I met etching class \+ spent the rest of the P. m — hitting in two etchings by Misses Hunt \+ Levick It was 6.40 before I got home — Dinner — Katie 7 — Tel[?] from F'k — Reporting that sister Kate is very sick with some form of stomach trouble — Evening I went to N.A. Council meeting and was there until near 11 o'clk —

## Right Page

### Tues. April 17, 1894

**Weather:** Still very mild \+ sunny  **Temp:** [illegible]

Took R. to school — Then, with Annie, went to Tiffany \& Co's Glass Establishment \+ saw Mr. Mitchell \+ arranged with him to have, in the "Tiffany Chapel" \+ some other parts of their establishment, a sweet entertainment for the benefit of the W. Dist Kitchen Ass'n — next Sat. It was 11 o'clk when I got home — worked awhile on the etchings by Misses Hunt \+ Levick — then on portrait of Reed — completed erasure of work (tint) at top \+ bottom — then laid an etching ground over all — After lunch I etched closely \+ freely over all the background \+ coat \+ bit it in, finishing by 5 o'clk — Then to N.A. f[?] D. taking plates of p[?]s — to matt, prease \+ finish — walked home with Katie P. Dinner at 6.45. Every Katie went with Charl to Mendelssohn Glee Club Concert — I was stupefied \+ didn't do much of anything
