## Left Page

### Sun. March 11, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull day with some heavy showers about 6 p.m. - Then a brief clearing. I did not go out of the house all day \+ my throat gave me little trouble. I spent much of the m'f pasting a Morgan pf- into a passe-partout to be ready to send to W. J. P. Morgan To-morrow. Dinner at 2. Loafed \+ read until after 4 - then I had a nap - Up at 6 \+ had a walk in the parlor until tea time. 6.30 - Even. Clare came in \+ stayed until 10.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 12, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day, but I did not feel quite well enough about the throat to go with R. to school but I went after him at 12.30 - I wrote several notes \+ letters in the m'f - Got Morgan pf. ready \+ sent it with a note, by Jaird to Mr. Morgan's house. I also got our Pratt his. pp. ready \+ after lunch I went down to B.B. Butler's (taking R. with me) \+ gave B. directions \+ sent them to Dr. Jas. Mac Alister - Pres. Drexel Ins. Phila. Then I went to N.A. \+ spent some time with Nicoll there - Then home - Had a nap before dinner - as soon thereafter as I could I went to Council meeting - N.A. f D. got thro' business \+ home \+ to bed before 11.
