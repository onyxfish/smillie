## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 28, 1894

**Weather:** Clear m'f. grey p.m.  **Temp:** very cold.

It was nearly 11 when I got to studio Mr. Montgomery having caught me at the house before I left. I commenced work upon a careful charcoal sketch preparatory to all ater-color, based upon the pen study that I made from our balcony at Venice, overlooking the Run. Home to lunch \+ back, continuing work on sketch. W.J. Van Pelt came in to say that the 36' S'. property is hampered by a 6 year lease \- he will enquire to find out if it can be bought. Home before 6. Katie \+ Bessie T. came down from Po'k. this P.M. They went to a dance in the ev'g. Ed'w \+ Henry came \+ spent the ev'g with Sue. I wrote to Herrick.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 29, 1894

**Weather:** Very clear \+ very cold.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I was detained at N.a. for a time, so it was about 11 when I got to studio \- Then Nicoll came in \+ took nearly all that remained of the m'f. Home to lunch at 1 \- before 2 I took Sue, Katchen \+ Eleanor to Station of Elevated R.R. at 6th Ave. \+ 42d S'. \+ started them on their way to Germantown. Then I walked down to studio but I did not make satisfactory progress with my work of laying out the outline of the Run view, Venice. Home before 6. Roger Adams came from New Haven to stay overnight on his way to Buffalo. Charl came \+ took Katie \+ Bessie to the Theatre \- later Rob't Cook came in. I worked on my Scrap book \+ S'tes \- when Annie \+ I got ready we left Rob'. \+ Roger in the parlor waiting for K. \+ B.
