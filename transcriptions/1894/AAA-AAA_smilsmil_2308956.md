## Left Page

### Tues. March 27, 1894

**Weather:** Bright, cold, windy & dirty with some very light snow flurries in the early afternoon.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkfs. I went out to my messages - then went on down 5th Ave. to Einert Ameub where I spent some time getting outfit for my Etching classes. Then to N.A. - then to 2d Nat. Buk. & back to N.A. & then up home, having been gone all the mf. Mr. Parker & Geo (bro.) joined our lunch party - Then I laid some etching grounds - Then W. Duncan Morgan - Real estate man from Liberty S. (No 51) called to see me about possible sale of N. H. fd. property. Then, at 4 I went to N.A., met my Etching clu & showed them how to lay grounds & sharpen points - There until after 6 - Dinner at 6.30 - Every Annie, Kate, Katie & Bessie went to the German theatre.

## Right Page

### Wed. March 28, 1894

**Weather:** Clear & not so cold - after bkfs.  **Temp:** [illegible]

laid some grounds on small plates for etching class - Then my domestic duties & messages - then to K. & V's where I spent the rest of the mf. superintending Hf Voigt who is taking in on a dozen fine hub. jap. paper bkfs. out which I am to make selections & present as final pfs. to Mr. Morgan. Home to lunch at 1 & after it (by the way, Fanny K. lunched with us) I again went to K. H's & got more pfs. & tacked them down upon stretching boards - then went to N.A. & met my Etching class & spent the rest of the afternoon with them. All were present & all worked upon their plates - the result was somewhat dis appointing to me - none of them really knew what they were working for. I but tis for them the etching by Fulw. Did not leave them until near 6.30. Then home to dinner - Every Annie, Kate & Katy T. went to Getter [illegible] at met. Opera Ho. & I got to bed early -
