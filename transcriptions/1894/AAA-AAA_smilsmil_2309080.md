## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 30, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very dark day, with some snow. I got up feeling tolerably well but had trouble with bowels that continued through the day, \+ before 12 o'clk a half hour of very severe pain. Yale came in the afternoon \+ prescribed Hydrastis can. \+ Salol. My head hurt me so much \+ so constantly that it made a very gloomy day for me. I was in the bed most of the time. In the even'g I managed to write short notes to Jas. C. Taylor of Montrose Republican \+ Herrick. Charles Collins called \+ after 9 o'clk. I went to the parlor \+ had a half hours chat with him.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 1, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another dark \+ dismal day, with some sleety rain \+ some rainy snow. I got up to blkfst. feeling very shaky but much freer from pain than yesterday. I spent the ev'g in dining room \+ library. I was much disappointed that I could not get out to the regular monthly Century meeting in the even'g, but had to content myself with a quiet even'g at home reading about Bacteria.
