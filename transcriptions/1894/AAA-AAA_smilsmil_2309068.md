## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 6, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Election Day, + of course, nothing in the way of business could be accomplished. The day was bright, brisk + breezy. I went to butchers + grocers after blkft — There were some things that could be done about the house - I wrote part of a long letter of instructions to Herrick. In the afternoon I got a restful nap. In the even'g I got through some more writing + to bed early.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 7, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold + windy - Soon after blkfst, in spite of a wretched headache, I got away with Annie, going first to Douglass, the painter's + from there to Warren, Fuller + Lange's, paper makers in 42? S. where we selected paper for dining-room wall - then I went to butchers + grocers - then to 2° Nat. Bk + arranged financial matters in Safe deposit - then to Brooks Bros. + bought a last year's, shop worn $45 overcoat for $20 - then home + being entirely used up by my headache I lay down upon a sofa + did not get up again until I went to bed. I had engagements to meet Nicoll + Ernest Flagg's in Broad S. but Rec'd a telegram stating that F. was out of town. I had made engagements with Mr. Lee + W. Vandelt. Sue ff. Annie + Mary Cook went to a swell concert at the Waldorf
