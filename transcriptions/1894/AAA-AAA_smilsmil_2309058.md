## Left Page

### Mon. Wed. Oct. 15, 1894

**Weather:** mon[?] Wed.  **Temp:** Ther. fell to 29° last night.

Brilliant, cold day, very windy until evett., when it became perfectly quiet — brilliantly cold moonlight night.
I got up feeling much better — It took considerable time, after bkfs., to get all the fires going — Then I got to work in studio \+ worked on water col. sketch "Tower at Constance, Rhine out-let". At noon I went to R.R. station \+ got mileage book — then to P.O. \+ to Stebbins \+ got celery — then to J. Harts \+ home to dinner. P.m — finished W.C. sketch \+ after 4 went down town with Annie \+ Ralph \+ had a walk. — Air very crisp — Evett. — my head \+ eyes hurt me. \+ after reading news papers, I lounged on big chair.

This should read for Monday — Oct. 15. Turned over two pages instead of one.

## Right Page

### Tues. Thur. Oct. 16, 1894

**Weather:** Tues.  **Temp:**

Rain in the early m[or]t. but, after a long struggle the weather cleared in the afternoon — wind at night. Cloudless moonlight. I worked in studio, finished "Tower at Constance — Rhine outlet." I painted large aqua-tint box. I had considerable work to do out of doors in looking after Hart who came with his team \+ man \+ ploughed up barn yard, cutting down the bank \+ carting portions to Hamels H\+. \+ other places — also, looking after Walker digging in the swamp — the Dimitru and Herrick. — I went down town about 5 o'clk. with A —, Eliz. \+ Ralph. Evett., after news paper I worked for some time over accounts.

Tues. Oct. 16 —
