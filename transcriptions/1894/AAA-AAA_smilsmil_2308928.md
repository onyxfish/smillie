## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 30, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

In bed all day — frightful head ache, nowell marked febrile symptoms until night — Jim was in all day with aches and pains — sore throats \+ things. Tried to get Yale in the well, but the Messenger service didn't work. I had a feverish \+ miserable night. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 31, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still bad weather — To-day my disorder is unmistakable grip — I have fever — severe pain in the head \+ a pounded feeling all over. Yale came in the course of the m'f \+ gave more advice than he did medicine — My pain gradually decreased as night came on, but I did not sleep as I usually do — In the afternoon Annie went to the Wed. P.m. Club \+ read her paper on "The uses of Waste". She is evidently pleased with the way in which it was rec'd — Sueff Charl came in for a little while.

Last night \+ to-night are the two nights of sale at Ortgies' Gallery of the 12×12 pictures. 12 paintings by 12 painters = 144 — Geo. had 12 paintings in the combination.
