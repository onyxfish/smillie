## Left Page

### Mon. March 19, 1894

**Weather:** Overcast, with some sunshine in the m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ Rain in the afternoon, clearing at night — Got to K. + V.'s soon after 10 \+ young V — Took four p'fs of Morganhead. 1 dark olive, or bronze-green India — 1 snuff brown India — 1 black, India on Etching paper backer \+ 1 Toronoco f'alp. V. Took p'f more to my liking than any preceding ones — He used the damp lawn rag on the face exclusively, as I desired, \+ so got more delicacy getting the modulations that I have so carefully put into the plate — The India p'fs look better than I expected \+ the Toronoco is the best one yet taken. Home to lunch at 1.20 — Kept part of the afternoon given to backing down \+ Stretching p'fs — Dinner at 6.30 Svelf Council meeting at Academy — There until near 11, then home.

morganhead — 3 h.

## Right Page

### Tues. March 20, 1894

**Weather:** Pleasant m'f. — overcast afternoon.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Went down to Wall St. immediately after bkfp — To Carter \+ Ledyard's with Bond \+ Mortgage that was perfected at Council meeting last night. Then to Lee \+ Lee, 20 Nassau St. — concerning Dorelyon m'f. Then to Devoe's in Fulton street \+ got a dozen of small copper plates for my N.A. of D. Etching class. Then again to C. \+ L., 54 Wall — \+ got check for $8000 — Then to N.A. \+ with Farrington went to 2d. Nat. Buk. \+ deposited $8000 \+ wiped out back loan of $5000 — Then to Stern Bro., \+ bot. a yard of blk. silk for dabbers \+ covering grounds. Then home to lunch at 1.15 — Alice dined there — Didn't accomplish much during the afternoon — Dr. Gerster came in few \+ o'clk. to see p'f of Morganhead \+ made a long call — Dinner at 6.30 — Ely. House. Eugene Atwater, Eliz. \+ Salley Oakley Cook \+ Catherine Cook dined with us — After 9 I took I gene to her home in 47th S. near 6th Ave — Then gel 2 \+ took Sly. Home to her home in Mad. Ave. near 30th S. — Then I took myself home \+ to bed.
