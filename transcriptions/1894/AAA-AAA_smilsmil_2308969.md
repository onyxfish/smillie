## Left Page

### Sun. April 22, 1894

**Weather:** Dull  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull wtf again, \+ even a sprinkling of rain followed by a delicious Spring day. Between 12 & 2 I had a walk \- then dinner \- what remained of the afternoon was divided between callers \+ writing a letter of condolence \+, I hope, of comfort to Kate who is miserably dyspeptic \+ dejected. At 6 I went out \+ walked for half an hour \- then home \- tea at about 7 \- Roelf Chase came in.

Louise Sprague Cook went through a surgical operation to-day.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 23, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day with one or two April showers. Took R \- to school, etc \- and then to work on Reed portrait \- finishing with dry-point \+ graver until 12.5 then went for R \- lunch. A little more work on Reed \+ at 3 o'clk. I went to the K. A. Etching class. Misses Hunt and Levick \+ Borford, Julius, Fangel \+ Meth- russell there. I'm afraid that I have made a mistake in undertaking this work \- my scholars are not sufficiently advanced in drawing \- they know nothing for what has been done in etching \+ there is no Public cole[ge] here in Nft. to which I can send them \- the task of teaching there is too great \- yet \- some of them are promising. I am very sanguine in my hopes of Young of the league \+ I think that Fulles of the d.a. has good stuff in him. I got home at 6.30. dinner. Roelf, went to K.A. Full Council meeting. but not an uninteresting one. I stayed until it broke up \- the first time I think that I have stopped so late. walked up with Geo. To bed at 12.
