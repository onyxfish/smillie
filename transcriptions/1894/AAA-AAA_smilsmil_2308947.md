## Left Page

### Fri. March 9, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A Raw, disagreeable, east wind day with rain at night — took R. to school — then marketing etc — then to N.A. of D — where I saw Pennell who has rec'd a letter from Ryle insisting that he shall deliver to him proof of Jade Place — home after 11 — went for R — at 12 't' at 12.30, man from Century Co. called to get information about the Inving Etching that I made after Darley's pencil sketch 34 years ago — lunch after 1 — spent the afternoon writing notes \+ letters — after 5 walked up to George's to enquire about Val — J — not in — Saw Sheldon \+ Gordon — they report that Val is progressing favorably — Home — dinner at near 7 — Edw'd Mary \+ Catherine Cook dined with us — R — who has been under the weather this afternoon \+ slept much of the time seemed much brighter \+ better — Every one C's left at 9.30 —

## Right Page

### Sat. March 10, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

not really unpleasant but overcast \+ raw with some sunshine in the afternoon. After a restless uncomfortable night I did not get up to bkfst. in the mrt. but had it brought to my bed \+ I lay there until after 10 — signed some N.A. checks sent to me by Farrington \+ returned them to him by messenger. I fussed, making Asphaltum Varnish. lunch at 1 — after that, most of the afternoon was spent making up a lot of etching ground — Annie \+ Katie went to Private View of Socy of Am. Artists in 5yr S. of course I was not able to go out — wrote notes to Robbins \+ Nicoles whom I was to meet at The Century — Dinner at 6.30 — Charl came \+ took Katie to Star Theatre — I spent some time preparing two Morgan [illegible] — one of them to be submitted to J. P. M — on Monday.
