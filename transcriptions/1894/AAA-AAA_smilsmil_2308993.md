## Left Page

### Sat. June 9, 1894

*Annie went to Germantown to Jas. Wright's funeral.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Muggy, close, hazy day — went down town right after bkfst — first to M. Siebold 44 Vesey, where I got a lot of dry colors to do some experimental color printing during the summer — then to Day St. to see W. H. Gibbs but he was not in — then to 84 Beekman St. — f.s. Hiatt works to make enquiries concerning water closets \+ bath tubs for Montrose — then to Siner \+ Amend's — 3d Ave \+ 18th St. \+ got Acids, gums \+ other materials — delayed there 3/4 of an hour — then to Stetson "7" \+ home to lunch at a little before 2 — R.-t I lunched alone as Annie had gone to Germantown before noon \+ Jim had gone to Rockaway to spend Sunday \+ Monday with Edwd Cook's family. I worked all the afternoon \+ packing up things — a half hour rest before dinner — after that Chas came in for a little while — I put R.-to bed \+ Annie ret'd at 9.15.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 10, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** 96°

A blistering hot day — up earlier than usual \+ turned the time to acct. by packing into bundles the many things that are to go in the big box — At 12 I went to Dr. Allen's in 47th St \+ spent nearly an hour with him — He looked at my teeth \+ thought that I would keep until fall — Dinner at 2 — more work in the afternoon — Walter came in, \+ we had a talk about Architectural matters \+ U.A. fls — I had a rest before tea at 7, \+ after Geo. \+ Minly \+ Mr. \+ Mrs. Brownell had called — In the everg Geo. \+ Nellie Smillie came in — stayed until 9.30 — \+ we got to bed early.
