## Left Page

### Sat. March 3, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day. As I did not feel like work upon the Morgan plate I determined to take a day off, so I engaged in a number of little jobs in the way of writing overdue answers to notes etc, etc. In the afternoon Raefsh \+ I went to the Water Color show for a little while \+ there I saw W. Pennell \+ rather upbraided him for his haste in making sale of jade proof before I got consent of Mr. Bishops \- Mr. Wm Kyle has already paid the $75 to Mielste. Chas came \+ took dinner with us then took Katie to a students entertainment at the N.A. Tableaux, Recitations, music etc. followed by dancing \- I went down later but could not get in, so I went up to the Century monthly meeting \+ had a very pleasant time getting home at about midnight.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 4, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- Annie went to church in the mtf \+ there the boys \+ I met her at 12.30 \- \+ then we went to The Water Color Show at the N.A. of D. \+ had a last look around. Back to dinner at 2. \- Katie at the Ballards \- In the afternoon I went up to George's \+ saw him \- Val has progressed favorably with his scarlet fever so far. Evelf I went to Walter Cook's \+ put some rough sketches \+ measurements into his hands \- and his employees will make drawings for finish of Montrose bath room. Then I went over to Edw Cook's where Annie was \+ the many talking over some business. It was 10.30 when we got away from there \- \+ an hour later when we got to bed.
