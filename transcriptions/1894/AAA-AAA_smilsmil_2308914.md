## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 2, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still beautiful weather. I fussed a little while on Belluno, then preparing sketches for exhib. At 12 went to Knoedler's & then to D. B. Butler's, getting home to lunch at 1.30 — After that, with the boys, I went to Grand Central Station to Treas. office to see about payment to be made on a stock issue that Annie has. Then to butcher's and home where I spent the rest of the day mounting drawings & etching p[late]s. Eliz Howe came late and stayed to dinner — I took her to her lodgings — 110 Mad. Ave. soon after 10. Charl spent the ev'g with us.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 3, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast & Raw — clearing after sun set — clear, starlight night. — My, him again commenced going to Mr. Morse's School. I took Ralph to his school — then home & commenced drawing outline of "Cathedral Tower, Ravello". 12×18" — At 12.30 went for R. — on the way home stopped at Gd. Central Station, Treas's office — & paid amt. due on some stock that Annie subscribed for sometimes last summer. — After lunch I went to 2d Nat. Bk., saw Cash. Case & made a loan of $500 for Annie. Then to D. B. Butler's & spent some time in selecting patterns for Water color frames. — 4 o'clk. when I got home — worked for an hour getting drawings into mounts. Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g. Charl came in —
