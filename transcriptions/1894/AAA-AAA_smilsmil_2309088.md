## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 16, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day altho' a portion of the p.m. was overcast. Late okfo. as usual then newspaper — then I got to work cleaning up my portfolios of prints in the little attic room. At 10'clk Jim Raelkh \+ u went out \+ had a short walk. Dinner at 2. after that Chr came in — + then Robt. Cook who took Katie to Church — I got some more work done on portfolios before tea time at 6.30. Evely Mr. \+ Mrs. Wm Justice of Germantown came in \+ made a pleasant call until 9.30.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 17, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day, sunny \+ mild, but I was not able to get up in the m'f. Trouble in larynx \+ bronchial tubes — \+ headache, somerons dreading me of my old bronchial attacks that Annie sent for Mr. Papenor of Topeka, Kansas, came to consult with Annie about her Western mortgages — he is recommended by H'f Progue. He came up to our bed room and Annie, he \+ I conferred together. The three migs. that A. holds were but into his hands to look after. After 12 Yale came in, but that did not cure me. At 10'clk I got up \+ dressed. Mary came from P'ok in the afternoon. Evely, Geo. \+ Nellie came to dinner — after that, Geo. left to go to N.A. council meeting — I was not able to go out of course — Nellie left later.
