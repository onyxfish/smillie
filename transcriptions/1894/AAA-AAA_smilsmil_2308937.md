## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 17, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

milder than yesterday \+ not so clear. Worked on Morganhead morning \+ afternoon adding work with dry-point \+ graver. Had a walk for exercise in the afternoon \+ then a rest before dinner. Eve'g I went to the Century where Jno. J. Weir read a paper on the Architecture of the Chicago Fair illustrated by a mass of Stereoptican views. A large number were present. I was not in a very happy frame of mind \+ left for home soon after 11 o'clk.

morganhead. 5 n. [illegible]

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 18, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

In bed all day with a wild and nasty headache. I did not get up until near 9 P. m. Men had a light repast in the parlor \+ got to bed again soon after midnight. A thunder storm consisting of two good explosions passed over us at about 12.30 at night. P.S. This thunderstorm occurred tomorrow night.
