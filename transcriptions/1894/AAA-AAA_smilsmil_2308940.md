## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 23, 1894

**Weather:** Rather Raw \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rather Raw \+ windy \- took R. to school \- then to work at home on correspondence, etc \- wrote to Herrick \+ for a time was engaged upon N A acct. Lunch \- at about 3 o'clk I went to 36 E. 31° S. \+ saw Jos. Evans Pres. of Art Students League \+ laid before him my plan for recruiting among students for Etchers \- He takes hold \+ says that he will give all the help he can in the league classes \- then to Na \+ worked with Farrington over the Academy books making myself acquainted to some extent \- then a walk \+ home \- Dinner \- Even altho' it had come off extremely cold I walked down to 15° S. Salmagundi Club meeting at Niglati's Studio \- Few there but an important meeting \- Farrer, Wood Niglati \- Blood good \- Lander \+ my self \- we gave a new turn to things in the club voting to drop the names of all who did not pay the assessment \- A letter from Thos. Moran was read to which S was requested to write a reply \- Then I explained my plan for recruiting among the students of the Na and the league \+ it was heartily approved \- then we had "mordant" crackers and cheese \- Splendid Aurora from 10 to 10.30 \- I walked home and got to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 24, 1894

**Weather:** A very cold day with high wind \+ much dirt  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very cold day with high wind \+ much dirt \- After 4[illegible] \- went down to N.Y.C. \+ had a couple of proofs taken \- one on a thin Jap. paper. I am much gratified \+ think that now I am nearing the end \- the elder [illegible] detained me to have a long talk with me about his project for a fine art work \- Pub°. in edition de luxe only \- I have no faith in it \- home soon after 12 \+ stretched my 2 pl°. Lunch \- then I moored for awhile over my pl°. \- at 4 went to market \- then worked for awhile getting plans for bath room at Montrose in shape to put them into Watter's hands \- then a nap \- then dinner. Evef. Annie went with Alice J \- to some entertainment at Sherry's \+ Katie went with Charl to the Lyceum \- I stayed at home \+ amused my self in front of a fine fire \-

morgan head, 3 h.
