## Left Page

### Tues. May 22, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A persistently wet day. Showers so heavy that I did not go out in the mf. I worked hard at cutting mounts for some Morgan pf., one for Koehler \+ one for myself, etc. — After lunch I went to Mr. Walten's in 42d. St \+ got R's books \+ a list of Kindergarten material that he is to take to Montrose with him this summer. I got pretty wet. — Then to butcher's \+ grocer's. — \+ then back to my fussing over pf. \+ trying to reduce my scattered possessions to order. Dinner at 7. — Swef very stupid \+ after much effort succeeded in doing nothing.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 23, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A sulky mf. passing into a humid, sunny, sultry day — overcast again before night — wind East. Got a letter from Annie — had her pocket picked bet. Boston \+ Cambridge, lost $8. I continued my work of clearing up my confusion, cutting mounts for pf., etc. About 12 I took Ralph out — went to grocers etc. \+ then to D. B. Butler's. Home to lunch at 1 — at 2.30 took Morgan \+ Reed pf. \+ left them at Dr. Gerster's in 25th St — then to Bauer — to Kurtz's \+ to N.A. Farrington not in — then home — continued my fussing until 6 o'clk — then took Morgan \+ Reed pf. \+ left them at Dr. Yale's — Then home to dinner — Swef, wrote to Annie — also a short note to Koehler. Took them to G.C. Station to mail —
