## Left Page

### Mon. May 28, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

at Saml. Colman's. Red Cross Lane, Newport.

A lovely day - Bkfs. at 7.30 - much of the evf. spent loitering on the verandah under the awning, part of the time reading aloud to Sue & Annie. Then I walked to Mt. Auburn Cemetery, my first visit. Everything in beautiful order & looking its best - Back in time for lunch at 1 - Said our good byes & left at 2 - car to Harvard Sq - Then East Camb. & car at the bridge another change that, after a very long & round about ride, took us to the Old Colony R.R." door at 3.5 our train left at 3.40 & we got to Newport at 6 - Mr. C. met us at depot & we at once rode to the house. Dinner at 7 - Evef. chatting until 10 - then, to bed.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 29, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain during the night & mf. - clearing abt. 2 o'clk to a cloudless afternoon. - Bkfs. at 8 - after that spent some time with Colman in his studio looking over his Mexican sketches & photos. It was after 11 when he & I started out for a walk - went down to the beach & along the cliff walk away - caught in a light shower - back about 1 - lunch at 1.30 - after that I had a light nap on lounge \+ after 4 C. \+ I started out for a walk - we went into town where some sort of holiday was in progress - a very considerable military procession - then down to the docks & about there until 6 - Then Ret. to the house with C. left him at the door & back again to Steamboat office & engaged stateroom for To-morrow evef. Then to C's just before 7 - in a few minutes had dinner - Evef. chatting until 10 - then to bed.
