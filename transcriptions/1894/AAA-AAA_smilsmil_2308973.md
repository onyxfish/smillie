## Left Page

### Mon. April 30, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — very much cooler. In the m'f went to K. \+ V's to get p'f. of Reed plate, hoping to find it finished so that it may be sent to M'. Durand in Paris by Wsd. steamer. Also there are a number of things that I still wish to do on the plate I got 8 p'fs — also a number of prints from to party plates — It was 11 o'clk when I got home to lunch. After that, sometime tracking down my p'fs. It was 3.30 before I was able to go to N.A. where I met Stehnil class \+ was with them until 6.30 — then home — dinner at 7 — We'f went to N.A. — Council meeting — min attendance — M'. Rogers — Blashfield, Murphy \+ Nicole, not there — home at 11.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 1, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm \+ enervating. After b'kf. I went to Butchers \+ grocers — R— now goes to school in the afternoon instead of the m'f — then home \+ to work for 3. h on Reed portrait trying to remedy some minor defects — also gave a little time to a small plate by Fuhrs — Lunch at 1 — After that went to Bank \+ attended to A's business — then to K. \+ V's \+ had some prints from the Fuhrs' plate and arranged to have a few p'fs. from Reed portrait taken to-morrow m'f. Then to N.A. \+ had Davis this new put up the Baldwin printing press \+ I got the some into some degree of order — also, some business with Farrington — Davis's man Gardiner, the accountant \+ assistant, have been at work on the N.A. books all day — Home at 6.30 — dinner — We'f, Charl came in — has been having protracted times with Alex Kleinwort who is now in N.Y. with his wife —
