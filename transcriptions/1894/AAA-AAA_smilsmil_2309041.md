## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 13, 1894

**Weather:** Pleasant m'f., with a shower in the afternoon \+ a cloudy ev'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

The m'f was spent going about the place \+ directing matters — I stood over Herrick for some time while he made a smaller aqua-tint gr° laying bap. At 2 I got to work in studio \+ painted until dinner time upon the Capri picture — again, in the afternoon I painted until after 5 — Then went down town — after tea the young ones had a good time over a new game — a sort of auction — after they went to bed I wrote a letter to Robbins \+ took it to P.O. ## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 14, 1894

**Weather:** Shower in the m'f before 10 — muggy and cloudy — afternoon, clearer, warmer \+ very humid — beautiful moonlight night and still very humid.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Before I got through with my bkfst. Geo. Vaccham presented himself — Then I went down to swamp in old Tyler lot \+ showed him where I wished him to commence drain digging — Then it commenced to shower and we were both driven off. There was a very heavy shower at 3 A.M. Sometime in studio planning sketches for aqua-tint experiments, based upon Venetian photo's. Before dinner, wrote to Clare — After dinner went down to swamp to see how Vaccham was getting on — Then wrote letter to Nicoll, after 4 went down town \+ did not get back until tea time — To P.O. again at 7 — on my return, Mrs. Schaffer \+ her daughter at the house, making a parting call at Kate who is to go to Buffalo to-morrow — Later, I read a story in the Century, \+ then to bed.
