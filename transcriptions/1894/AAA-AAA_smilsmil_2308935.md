## Left Page

### Tues. Feb. 13, 1894

**Weather:** T  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow most of the day - went with R. to school - Spent most of the rest of the m'g working on M. head - then ready for a proof - Began to get things ready to begin on Durand Reed head went for R - lunch - made tracing on gelatine from Reed photo - & then made a rough pen sketch - at 5 walked up to Central Park & back - dinner at 6.30 - Ruef fell asleep in my chair in front of fire & then went to bed.

morganhead 3 h. Reed head 3 h.

## Right Page

### Wed. Feb. 14, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day, & raw - After taking R. to school \+ attending to other domestic matters, I went down Town to Equitable Ins. to pay my premium due on 1st ins'. \+ forgotten until now by me. I had no trouble \+ went thro' some necessary formalities without loss of time - then up town & went to 2d Nat. Bk. where I saw Cashier Case & arranged with him to extend the N.A. $5000 loan for 30 days - then to Knordlie's - then to 9 W. 17th S. Goslet's office to see Wm. Berrian about Real estate matters looking to N.A. business. Then I walked up to 42d S. - stopping at picture galleries, & got R - Home to lunch - afternoon, I worked for some time on a copper plate preparing it for the Durand-Reed head - then a nap before dinner. Suelf Dunne read aloud for awhile - then Charl came in -

Reed head - 2 h.
