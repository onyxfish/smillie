## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 26, 1894

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — the same work at Studio, sorting contents of drawers \+ cases, cleaning \+ dusting. Clark's man came in the m'f \+ made measurements for gas pipe — Home to lunch \+ back — Clark's men came \+ put up gas pipe — now I can get gas to use at my work Table \+ writing desk. Soon after 5 I went to N.A. \+ Katie T. walked uptown with her as far as 86th S'. where we [illegible] and I went to Mr. W'm. Lee's home 32 W. 36 S'. \+ we discussed Academy matters concerning the closing our sale, as per contract, to the Met. Life Ins. Co. \+ the lease of the Academy bldg. by the N.A. from the Met. Ins. Co. It has been thought that to save payment of taxes (there, being [illegible] the delivery of the property might be delayed as long as we arranged to occupy it. I think it better to close at May 1", as agreed, take our pay \+ invest it, thus avoiding many risks. Then home to dinner. Council meeting — I was instructed to negotiate a lease of N.A. bldg. from Met. Co. for one year for a sum not to exceed $10000. — Home at 11.30 —

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 27, 1894

**Weather:** Very pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day — it was after 10 when I got to Studio. Very soon after that Mr. Seymour, of Harnett \+ Co., came in to urge upon me the various beauties \+ advantages to be found in a site upon 5th Ave., 90th \+ 91st S's., that his firm has to sell. Soon after he left me I went down Town — first to Pine S'. to saw Mr. Van Elt, then to Nassau S'. \+ gave to W. W'm. Lee a check for $27,000 amt. loaned by N.A. f'd, on a piece of property on 7th Ave. above the Park. Then home to lunch — after that, back to studio \+ finished the work of going through all drawers, cases, etc. thoroughly cleaning them out \+ putting away contents. — At 4.30 went to 2 [illegible] Commerce \+ [illegible] to see on W. Jno. Bigelow about Ernest [illegible]'s Reservoirs. plans for suit for Tildden library \+ N.A. f'd — not in — then to Mitchell Vance \+ Co. B'dway \+ 13th S'. to look at gas-light gas fixtures — home at 6. Very [illegible] until 8.30 when feeling out of sorts I went to bed. Charl came in later —
