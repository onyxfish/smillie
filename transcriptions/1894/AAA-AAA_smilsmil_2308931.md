## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 5, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Blfs. in bed. — then got up and hung about in an unhappy frame of mind + body all day. The weather a match for every thing else. I accomplished absolutely nothing except the writing of a letter of instruction to Jarring ton concerning Carter + Hedy and business (the Phillipes Mortgage.) Liz. Howe took dinner with us + in the eve'f, with Katie Adams went to Mendlessohn Glee Club Rehearsal on tickets given to Louie by Emma J —

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 6, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day. Clear + crisp. — I got up feeling tolerably well after a good nights rest. It was after 11 when I walked to the Ortgie's Gallery + saw a portion of the Wyant Collection, many of which, being pictures in progress + entirely unfinished, should never have been offered for sale. — Then up to Avery's Gall. \+ saw a small collection of Geo's oil paintings, Water-colors + pencil sketches. — a better showing of work that he made in the 12 × 12 sale last week — Then I went for R— Lunch at 1 — About 3 I went out + had another short walk. Then wrote to Lee + Lee concerning the foreclosure of the Doré d[illegible] on Mortgage. Eve'f, Charl Took Katie P— to the Concert of the Mendelssohn Glee Club — I read aloud a sketch by Howells.
