## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 24, 1894

**Weather:** A nasty day, raw fog in the mng.  **Temp:** [illegible]

wind & rain in the afternoon, cooler with heavy rain & wind at night. About 10 I went with Katherine to N.A. School & got her started in the Antique Class — then I went to Kurtz's — then to Knordler's & there met Mr. Brom-seth & we had a long chat about the Beache's & of old times — then I went to Champoney's & got a lot of Pastel crayons then walked up home & then went for lunch at 1 — pretty nearly used up with a headache — on sofa in dining room until near 4 — then again to N.A. — to see Etching Club hangers — missed them then to Kurtz's & got photos of Durand por. of Dunace Reed. then home — Dinner at 6.30 — just as we sat down W. Parke came in & took dinner with us — Rosalin Butler had sent Opera tickets to A. — so she & K. — went to the Opera together — Parke walk-ing over with them — I stayed at home & studied T.S. Cumming's History of [illegible] & to learn something of the financial struggles of the institution.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 25, 1894

**Weather:** Clear & cold. After h[alf] p[ast].  **Temp:** [illegible]

I went to Mr. Pierpont Morgan's house with a proof of my Etched portrait to compare it with the painting — It was a revelation to me I cannot understand how, after having had a chance to compare the photo, with the painting when I began the etching, I could have so forgotten the inaccuracies of the photo — I have been led entirely astray by them, have wasted much precious time & patience & have nearly injured the plate. I spent more than an hour toning down the cold, hard light on the face, working with crayon upon an India proof. It is now getting into real, artistic shape — then to N.A. & got Ravello Cath. Tower drawing down into the Council Room to work upon it — then home to lunch — after that, again to N.A. & worked upon Ravello, also Belluno, with pastels to the very great betterment of both of them — worked as long as I could see, then home — Molly & Adams are from Germantown — Every Annie, Katie T. & I went to Choral concert at Madison Sq. Concert Hall & later were joined by Charle — The concert was a very enjoyable one. Mallie Adams would not take any place there, preferring to go to bed as she was tired.

morgan head. 2 h.
