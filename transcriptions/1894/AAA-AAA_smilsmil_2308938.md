## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 19, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Read head \- 3 h \-

mild & wet \- of course I feel very shaky after yesterdays experience. I took "R." to school & went to market then home & laid ground on Durand plate for Demnan Reed plate \- then I walked down to 2°. Nat. Bk. & extended NA f.S. loan for another 30 days \- then went forth \- & home to lunch \- After that I went down to R & V with Durand plate & got a gelatine tracing \- not very satisfactory I tried, without much success, to get a set before dinner. Katie T\- ret. from to 'k. Eve. f. I went to Comicil meeting. N.A. f.S. with Ward went into Life Class & saw students & said a few words about forming an Etching Class. They were enthusiastic Ge°., Robbins & I left Comicil meeting at 10.30 & walked up. Rain came on before I got home. \- after which it rained very heavily for some time. Men came the Thunder storm that I have already described as having taken place last night.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 20, 1894

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild, pleasant day \- After bff: went to Kimmel & Voigts, getting there about 10 o'clk I wiped the head part of three pls \- & then young. Took a fourth one, a compromise between his manner of wiping & mine & it is, I think, the best one yet taken here. Home at 12.30 \- lunch at 1 \- Sat for awhile study. ing my pls. at 3.30 walked down to W. 17. 5: and saw Wm. Berrian, Gorlet's agent about property values in the NA. f.S. \- neighbourhood \- then to Keppel's & saw the Serner sketches \- then down Fourth Ave. below 17. 5 & bought some Asphaltum \- also some Grease Paint to experiment with Herkomer's place of etching thro' a white ground., Then I went up Bdway \- toNeumans Hardware Store & got some brass fittings for Montrose. \- Then home \- Before dinner a notary came from Lee & Lee with a paper for me to sign in suit for foreclosure of the Predyon mtg. of the NA. f.S.\-.
Every Chas. Collins came in and made a chatty, pleasant call \- we were illuminated by a new lamp, with wonderous "shade" bought by Annie at an auction sale this afternoon.

morgan head 4 h.
