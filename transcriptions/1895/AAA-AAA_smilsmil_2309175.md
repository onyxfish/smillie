## Left Page

### Fri. June 7, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another delightful bright breezy day \- I spent the m't about the house, getting trunks down stairs \+ making a start for packing. I also did some Academy writing \- At just before 12 I went to Dr. Allen's \+ he eased a little more some of the hard bearing parts in my lower teeth \- Then home \- After lunch I sent Jim up to J.Q.A. Ward's for the dee document that I left with him last Tues. \- \+ got it \- Soon after 2 Farrington came in with the two checks \- one for Central Trust Co \- $10,000 \- the other for Fisk \+ Sons $24,000 I signed them \- more N.a. writing. At 4,30 I went to Walters about plans for studio \- Then to Arnold \+ Constable's \+ bought underclothes \- Then to N.a. \+ had a talk with Farrington telling him some things that he must do \+ some things that he must not do \- Then home \- Dinner at 7.

## Right Page

### Sat. June 8, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still pleasant weather \- In the m't rec'd a note from W.C.N. Lee with reference to some N.a. N't business which obliged me to give up much of my m't to it \- I took Ralph with me \- went to N.a., then to 2d. Nat. Bk. \- to Safe Deposit \- back to N.a. \- to Telephone \- to Clark the Plumbers \- to grocers and butchers \+ home after 12 \- The afternoon was given up to writing \+ getting things together \- While we were at dinner Mary \+ Bessie came in \- just down from Po'k \- Bessie having graduated yesterday with flying colors \- Later, Charle came in \- bet. 10 \+ 11 p.m. \- we used up some bottled lager that was in the refrigerator.
