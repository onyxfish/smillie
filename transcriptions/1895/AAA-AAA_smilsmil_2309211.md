## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 18, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A superb day \- Bkfs \- before 9 \- It was noon before I got through with my routine duties \- Then I wrote on letter to Geo \- that I commenced last Sunday \+ took a little spin on my wheel \- Dinner at 2. About 4 the A \- \+ T \- girls, the boys, \+ I started out for a walk \- we went across lots to the Sayre hill \+ thro a portion of the woods \+ loitered by by the way \- It was a little after 6 when we sat \- Then tea \- After that I went to P.O. with letters \- Another piazza eve\+ with Chinese lantern accompaniment

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 19, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Katie \+ Mollie Adams went to N.Y.
Aunt Jessie \+ Emma Bourne arrive \-

A superb day \- brilliant, breezy \+ very cool. Ther. below 60° in the m\+ Bkfs \- at 7. at 7.30 most of us walked to the station \+ gave the girls a send off \- Raedaker \+ Tyler made an opening in the roof for the dormer window \+ most of the day I was with them \- They worked very persistently \+ got the work so well advanced that they got the frame up \+ so well covered that it was fairly protected in case of rain \- We\+ just before 8 \- we went over to the train \+ met Aunt Jessie \+ Emma, just in from Po'keepsie \- They being tired \+ hungry had a cup of tea \+ got to bed early.
