## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 4, 1895

**Weather:** Very pleasant day, cloudless, mild & quiet.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkft. I worked over my accounts until after 11 — then to Grocei's, butcher's & studio — after seeing the carpenter at work. there I went to Walter's, Bdway & 18th. \+ from there to 2d. Nat. Bk. \+ thence home to lunch — After that to studio. Then to Ryan's \+ left order to have gas meter set — then home \+ at 3.30 went to Met. Museum of Art, Fall opening — and was there until near 5 — then home \+ had a rest until dinner at 6.30 Evef. went to Corneil meeting of N.A. of D. — short session — Home at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 5, 1895

**Weather:** mild & pleasant.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkft I spent some time at work over accounts, then, with Ralph, went to the studio where the rest of the mf was given to cleaning up the carpenter's shavings \+ dirt \+ stowing things away in the closets. Home to lunch at 1 and did not go back until after 3 as I had writing to do at the house. As it was election day \+ a holi- day, I had some callers — among them Chas. Isham \+ Loyal Farragut. Chas. Diggs came to talk about caring for the heating of the bed \+ I went down to the cellar with him, closing studio's front door after me and leaving my keys inside — I was locked out. Maynard let me in thro' the side door — After consultation I went to Hahnenfell's \[illegible\] \+ coffee — Maynard let me down the ville hole from his bath room window to my closet window \+ then I got in to my keys. Home at 5 — Evef. Mary \+ her two girls \+ Sheldon Smillie, went to the Mad. garden Theatre \+ saw Jefferson as Rip Van Winkle — I had a quiet evening at home \+ went to bed before they got?
