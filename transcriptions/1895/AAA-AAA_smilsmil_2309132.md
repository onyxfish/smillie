## Left Page

### Thur. March 7, 1895

**Weather:** mild, overcast m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild, overcast m'f. heavy rain about 3 o'clk continuing during the rest f p.m. — went down town before 10 o'clk to complete yesterday's work of getting stock \+ bond transfers — went to Charles' — then to Mil. L.S. \&Western \& had bonds transf'. then to Morgan's \+ to Gaines, in Mills' Bldg with New, ot news \& Miss. Valley — then a walk about Trinity Church — then to 15 Courtland \+ [illegible] with N.f. Sus. \& West'n — then to studio at nearly 1 — B.N.A.f.D. \+ then home at 1.30 \+ had lunch at 2.30 at 2'. Nat. \+ in a heavy shower then to studio — A young illustrator (I forget his name) came in \+ offered to take my studio if I could let him occupy it by the 15th in. — I agreed to do so, very joyfully — then Mr. Jas. H. Morse came in \+ we had a long talk concerning Jim \+ his studies — I have feared that he was laboring under too great a strain. I made some progress in straightening out the Western Mtg. \+ M.f. Sprague — Palence business. Home to dinner at 6.30 — wea'f, a little writing before Charl came in —

## Right Page

### Fri. March 8, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day after a night of heavy rain — I spent most of the m'f at home writing \+ after 11 went to Howard St., I saw George Cook \+ get his signature added to mine on Nesquehoning stock for transfer — then to studio — went to office of M. M.C.A. — to see Mr. Orne \+ arrange with him to turn my lease of studio to Mr. Wexall (or some such name) but Mr. O. was not in — then home to lunch — Emma, Mary, and Louise spent part of the m'f, arranging \+ disposing of Annie's belongings — After lunch I wrote some letters — then took a nap as a preparation for to-night's work — after that I went out, taking R. with me for a little walk — Dinner at 6.30 — went to meeting of Com. on Site, N.A.f.D. All present — The Howard Russell Butler paper was then read \+ carefully discussed without reaching any conclusion — Home at 11.30 in a raw, nasty rain —
