## Left Page

### Tues. June 11, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather getting to be oppressively hot again. Thunderous clouds, but no storm. After bkfs. went to Century by app't with H.R. Butler. He telephoned to Mr. Ed. D. Adams & made appointment to meet him there at 2 P.m. Then I went to Dr. Allen's & had a little more easing to my mouth. It was near 12 when I got home. I did some packing & then down town to Mr. Lee's office where I settled some matters concerning new mortgages. Then at 1, went to Café Savarin & met Butler & had lunch \- & at 2 we went to Mr. Adams in the Mills Bldg. He rec'd us pleasantly & opened the business by reading a letter that he had rec'd \- " signed by several N.A.'s he said \- It warned him agt. hazarding any capital in securing a site in 57 St. under the Butler plan, saying that the majority of the com. on site did not represent the majority of the N.A. It was plainly the work of Wood & to my mind was cowardly & [illegible]. B. & I parted \- having had no discussion with Mr. A. We accepted the situation without objection \- I went to Hoboken by Barclay St. ferry that did & W. Office secured seats to Montrose for Thursday P.m. Then home getting in soon after 4.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 12, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Muggy \- warm, overcast, easterly, chilly wind. In the mlf. went to Calverley's studio with medallion of Annie's profile & some photos. Then to N.A. \- then to Bank & settled my business there with Case the cashr. Then home by way of 3rd Ave., butcher's & grocer. Proceeded with pkg. & pf. of trunk & cases \- also spent some time writing a letter to Nicoll concerning the minority letter to Mr. Edw. Adams \- After lunch I went to Jackson's Sup. met H.R. Butler & had a short talk with him concerning yesterday's N.A. business \- Continued packing until dinner time with some interruptions. Walter came in & I ordered outside winter doors to be put up while we are away \- Evely. Charl came in \- I wrote to Mr. Lee concerning the boundaries & measurements of the 36' 5" lot \- To bed at 11.
