## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 12, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day — with heavy clouds in the mtfs. that cleared at about noon. The day passed like many others, with much dawdling over the dormer window plans the construction of which — problems of strength + stiffness — water-tightness etc. — etc. — bother me very much.
I had a little riding on my wheel when I went down town + attended to various little matters. A quiet eveff on piazza + to bed early.
