## Left Page

### Tues. Aug. 20, 1895

**Weather:** Clouded m'g.  **Temp:** [illegible]

At times looking very like rain — very cool — clearing beautifully, \+ very cool, in the afternoon. I went down town in the m'g \+ spent some time at Cooley's watching the overhauling of my bicycle. The two carpenters made good progress in covering in the dormer \+ I spent much time with them — no matter of interest, day or ev'g.

## Right Page

### Wed. Aug. 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Seven Counties Veteran's Encampment.

Another superb day, but, in the m'g at night it was really cold. The carpenters came at 6.20 \+ worked until noon — then they joined the crowds that flocked to the Fair Grounds — Before!! I went to the grounds with the boys \+ after wandering about there for some time I left them there \+ went down town — went to R.R. Station \+ was interested in watching the handling of the unusual numbers of cars \+ passengers — 54 car loads — 6 engines were up in the tracks while I was there. Dinner at 12.30 — then all went to the grounds \+ for some time were amused at the dancing floor — the many booths \+ games — etc. etc — winding up with the reception of the Gov. of Pa. — Again I went down town \+ found some amusement in watching the uncouth crowds. Tea at 6.30 — quiet ev'g at home.
