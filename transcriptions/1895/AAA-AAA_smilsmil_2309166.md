## Left Page

### Mon. May 20, 1895

**Weather:** Charming  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming mt'g., overcast p.m. — Mollie A. Ret'd to New Haven at 10 A.m. I took R- to school \+ then to Dr. Allen's — then but a few minutes — then home. after my marketing etc. I wrote some notes \+ part of a letter to Mr. Wm. See concerning N.A. com. business. went for R- at 12.15 — very warm walk home — lunch — then I had my usual naps — after that, completed my letter to Mr. dee \+ wrote a Resolution, with preambles, that I intend to present to N.A. council to-night — at 6 went to Cycle A.t. and had a lesson in mounting \+ dismounting — a good deal of work. Home before 7. dinner — then to N.A. — new members of Council met with the old ones — very little business transacted. — Home at 9.45, Geo. coming in with me — Charl there. G. \+ C. left at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A steady rain all day until about 6 P.m. — our visit to New Haven was postponed on acct. of it. At 9.30 at Allen's in 47' S. \+ had two lower pieces put into my mouth — much to my discomfort, of course. Then home \+ wrote letters — mostly N.A. business. Went to grocer's etc. at about noon — lunch at 1 — That nap after it — then more N.A. business notes — after 5 I went out — went to Cycle A.t. \+ had another lesson in mounting \+ dismounting — make progress very slowly — I have so little spring left in me. Home soon after 6 — sponged off \+ then dinner — Swell. Charl came in for a little while. I wrote three more N.A. notes, mailed them \+ to bed before 11.
