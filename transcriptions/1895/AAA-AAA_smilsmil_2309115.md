## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 3, 1895

**Weather:** Very bright, crisp mtf., overcast p.m. cold wind getting around to N.E. very raw \+ disagreeable.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I spent the mtf arranging papers connected with Anim business \+ did so without depression. I cannot understand myself. It seems to me at times that I have lost my feeling — am blunted in my sensibilities — as though already beginning to forget — then would follow such a sickening sense of the truth — such a realization of the fact that the light \+ strength has gone out that everything about me, \+ life itself is hateful — then, the great fear that I shall break down, physically \+ mentally — Gel Cooke came in for a few minutes at about 7 ( — at 12, as Kate T—, Ralph \+ I were going out, Edw C. came in \+ he walked with us down to the N.A. where we looked over the Water Color Show we met bro Gel there — Before 2 we walked up town — Dinner at 2 — After that Home \+ Walter came in \+ we sat \+ talked for an hour or more — at 4.30. Jim, Ralph \+ I walked up to Central Park \+ back. Tea before 7. Evatf. Chark came in.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 4, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Last night was the first night that I have slept naturally since a week ago, yesterday. Horrible mtf., raw air with wet snow flurries — clearing in the afternoon — brilliant \+ very cold night. I went to Mr. Conklin's with [illegible] — but being too late to catch Kennedy \+ his nurse I had to take him up to his home, 167 W. 47' S° — then home, \+ to butchers grocers \+ then to 5th Ave. Safe Deposit, where I made copy of the contracts by which I hold the box there. Then to Lee \+ Lee in Nassam S° to see if they wd. advise seeking an order of court giving me access to the safe. Mr. Lee wd. not advise it altho' he said the action of the Co. was arbitrary \+ without foundation in law. He said that no time wd. be saved \+ money would be needlessly spent. Home to lunch at 1.30 — then to studio where I commenced work on aqua tint plate, Cathedral Tower. Ravello. I managed to get somewhat interested in my work — but it is dreadfully trying to sit still \+ alone \+ hold my mind to my work. Evtf. I went to Council Meeting at N.A.A.D. determined to face the work — I was really very considerably \+ managed to get through with less suffering than I had expected — I left a soon after 9 o'clk. \+ Re[illegible]. home.
