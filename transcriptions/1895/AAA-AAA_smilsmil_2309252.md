## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 10, 1895

**Weather:** Rain during the n'f, clearing \+ much cooler in the afternoon.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up feeling not much worse for my dissipation last night. Bkfst late \- at about 11 went to studio \- there until 1 \- then home. Maynard came just before 2 \+ dined with us \+ left after 3. about 4 \- men \- made up a little of the sleep that I lost last night \- After tea I went to studio \+ looked after the fire \- then to Mary Cook's \+ made arrangements with her about dancing class lessons for the boys \+ some other matters. Home at 9 \- then attending to business matters until 10.30 —

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 11, 1895

**Weather:** Dull n'f, clearing cold \+ cloudless.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got to 2° Nat. But. \+ attended to business there then went down town \- first to 4th Dis. Note Co \+ made arrangements with Conklin to put up my printing press in studio \- then to Mr. H.L. Cole's office, 59 Wall \- then to Winter \+ Smillie's \- then to M \- check [illegible] R.R. office \+ got nos. int. on bonds \- then to Lee's \- Then, soon after I got lunch at Café Savarin \- then to Maritime Tel. Co. \+ arranged to be notified of arrival of Fuerst Bismark upon which Chorel. is supposed to have sailed from Halifax on 7th ins. Then to Fay office \+ got bill of Fayes on new studio bldg in 36 St. Then to 2°. Nat. But. \+ made deposit \+ negotiated a loan to make the final payments on studio Mtg. Then to N.A. \+ some time with Farrington \- then to studio \- then home. Ev'g. Council meeting of N.A. of D. J.C. Nicoll's last ev'g with us prior to his start upon a journey to extend, perhaps, to Japan. After the meeting Geo., F. R.A. Ward \+ I walked up town together.
