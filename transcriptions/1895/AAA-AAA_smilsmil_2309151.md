## Left Page

### Sat. April 20, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another delightful day. I went down to bkfs. \+ after a rest I went to Grocers \+ Market. Then home \+ while resting Yale came \+ asked me to take a short walk with him - we sauntered to B'dway \+ 38th S. Then he took car \+ I got home - meeting Tom Fessenden, \+ learning that he too is just recovering from the grip - got in at about 12. It was 1/30 before we had lunch - Jim, Tom \+ Edna went off on their bicycles, with lunch, getting back at 3 after having had a very good time - At 3.30 Ralph \+ I rode up to 80th S. \+ went into the Park - I found the air chilly \+ myself very weak - so, we made a short turn \+ rode down 5th Ave. in a bus \+ got home before 5 - Dinner late - every felt very tired, restless, nervous - I chatted with the two Katie's until about 10, then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A heavenly day - after a tolerably good night I awoke before 5 \+ then had naps until I got up at 8 \+ dressed. Bkfs. at 9 - I spent some time in the yard with Jim \+ Ralph superintending some of their gardening operations - but I feel very weak \+ wretched - Afterwards I sauntered up \+ down Park Ave for half an hour or more before dinner at 2. met Jno. Valentine - About noon Emma Ireland \+ Marjorie Spring called \+ spent half an hour with me - After dinner I had calls from Walter \+ Louise, Dr. Yale \+ bro. Geo. I got to be very tired by 6 o'clk. all day Temp. 97 3/5 - until at night, as I went to bed, it got nearly up to normal. I read awhile to R - in the evg. \+ then chatted with he K's until 9 - then all to bed.
