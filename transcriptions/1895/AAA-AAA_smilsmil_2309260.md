## Left Page

### Thur. Nov. 28, 1895

**Weather:** Beautiful day \- clear \+ quite mild.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkfo. soon after 8 \- It was a little after 9 when, with Jim\+ Ralph, I went to [Birrman?] in dep. Ave \- about bicycles \- when I ret\- to the house I had such a severe headache that I took a dose of Caffiene \+ lay down upon my bed for nearly an hour \- then got up feeling better \+ went to studio \+ until 1.30 passed over the cleaning of my bicycle \- At 2, dinner \- Geo. \+ his three boys (Nellie did not come) \+ Charle were added to our family, making 12 at table. It was about 4 o'clk. when we got thro'. Then Geo. \+ I went to my studio for a little while \- then I walked up to 70th S\. \+ back Home at about 6 \- Nellie had joined her family there \- all away at about 7 \- It was almost 8.30 when we had a cup of tea \+ a bite of bread \- Some reading \+ notes writing in the eve\+ \+ to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### Fri. Nov. 29, 1895

**Weather:** A pleasant day \-  **Temp:** [illegible]

when I got to Studio found man digging in alley as I had directed, to discover if possible the leak that is flooding my cellar. The chief inspector of the district was on hand \+ much of my time during the day was spent bossing the job. I divided the remainder of the day between putting paper on my new, large blind frames \+ cleaning my bicycle \- Before 5 o'clk I went to Century Club to find Dielman \+ H. Butler, if possible \- Then to B's house \- back to Club, where I found him \+ made an appointment to meet him at 9 \- then home to dinner \- just before 9 Dielman came in \+ I read to him my rough draft of the Com. Report \- Then we walked to 6th Ave. \+ 42d. S\. \+ I went on to Butler's, 135 W. 47th. Saw Mr. B \- \+ then spent an hour with B \- going over the matter of the report. At 11 he walked with me to the cor. of 5th Ave. \+ 47th S\. \- a glorious moonlight night.
