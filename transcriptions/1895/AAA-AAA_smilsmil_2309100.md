## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 4, 1895

**Weather:** Bright + beautiful winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got to studio at 10 + to work on plan of Riva drawing. Mr. Rathbone took considerable of my time talking about N.A. sides. Home to lunch. Annie away — Unitarian league meeting. Back to studio at 2.30 + worked until 5, finishing my "plan" — establishing outline, values, etc preparatory to beginning work with color. Then I walked up to 57th S. + 5th Ave. + thence home — a 3 mile walk — very cold. Dinner — evef. — a little reading — then wrote a couple of notes + at 10. ilk went to Edw'd Cook's for Katherine T. — who had taken tea there. Rabt. Cook came home with us + came in for a few minutes.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 5, 1895

**Weather:** A very cold, clear m'f. becoming overcast + milder in the afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

Vand'set called soon after 6hf. To say that he could get, for me, the stable + lot, 20×41. on Sniffen Court, 35th S. for $9.500, subject to a lease that has 6 years yet to run. After consultation with Annie, I told him to get it. Here I hope to get a permanent studio. Then I went to Hoboken + saw S. R. Koehler off on the "Normannia", going direct to the Azores to rest awhile, then on to Europe for a year of work + study. Home at 1 — lunch — much of the p.m. was spent writing — did not go to studio. Dinner at 6.30 — Evef. Charl took Katie to the theatre. At 8 I went to Century where I had an appointment to meet J. Z. A. Ward. We talked Academy matters until after 10 o'clk. I found him full of strong, practical ideas + energetic almost to enthusiasm, but he would not promise to take any part in work — said he was getting lazy. Then I went home.
