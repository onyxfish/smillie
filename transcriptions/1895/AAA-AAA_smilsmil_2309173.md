## Left Page

### Mon. June 3, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Even hotter than before, until late in the afternoon when a delightful cool breeze came up — Thunderstorm having passed near us. At 10 I went to Century + met H. R. Butler + was in consultation with him until 11 — then home — got documents — then to N.A. got $340 oo check — took it to 2° Nat. + deposited it — then to Century at 12 and was with Butler until 1 — then home to lunch — almost used up by headache after lunch I had a short rest — then to Century at 3 + with B. for another hour — our conference resulting in the preparation of a letter to Mr. 2d° D. Adams concerning action of N.A. Site Com. + the formation of a Syndicate to purchase lots — we went to Stenographers in 42° S. then to Barrett Ho. where B. has a room — then I went to Dielman's Studio — but, he was not there — To Stenographers — got 3 copies, typewritten, of letter to Mr. Adams — then home — writing letters to Wood, Brown + Nicole — Dinner at 7 — at 8.30 went to Special Council meeting — had a Finance Com. appointed — Robbins — Brown + Self — At 10.30 Geo + I walked up to 38° S. together — I found Charl at work tinkering on Hattie's Bicycle.

## Right Page

### Tues. June 4, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast, with chilly, north easterly winds — The m'f was spent in attending to small matters out of doors — getting to Mr. Allcott's at a little before 2 — then home, after making an effort to find Dielman at his studio. Lunch at 1.30 — after 2 Nicole came in + we talked N.A. business for about an hour — Then I rested awhile — then to 2.2.a. Ward's — 119 W. 52° S. just missed him — wrote a note + left it for him with W. H. L. Lee's Revised opinion concerning H.R. Butler's plan — home again Dinner at 6.30 — Wm f. Wile Parks came in with his youngster Helen — He + Norman are to sail on the St. Louis to-morrow — To Century at 9 to meet Brown + Robbins (Wood not there) Finance Com. for an hour discussed the question; what disposition shall we make of the $340 oo just pd. to the N.A. ? I got some instructions for to-morrow — Home at 10.15 — Charl there until 11.
