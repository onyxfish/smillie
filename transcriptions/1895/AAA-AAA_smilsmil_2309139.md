## Left Page

### Thur. March 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day - immediately after bkfs- I went to Columbia college + after a little delay I saw Prof. W. R. Ware concerning a form of plan for a college of Fine Arts - he showed me thro' the rooms of his classes in Architecture which I found to be very interesting - + after about 15 m- of conversation he passed me on to Pres. Low. Who really is the head of the movement, which, so far as I know, had its beginning in my interview with Pres. Low at his house Feb. 14th - I had a very pleasant interview with Pres. Low - he gave me a type written paper, "Scheme for a proposed College of Fine Arts to be established as a part of the University of Columbia College." This I am to submit to the Com. on Site. - Then I went to City Hall + got certified copy of Annie's will to be sent to Thea. Then to Walter's office 874 Bdway. cor. 18th S.- to consult over plans for 36th S. Studio - Then home to lunch. After that to Treas's Office - N.Y. Cent.- to show copy of Annie's will as declined before transfer of stock + found that no seal had been attached to certification. Had Ralph with me + we sauntered for awhile on bridge over G.C.R.R. yard at 42d S. then home. Wrote part of a letter to S.R. Kohler -
Dinner at 6.30 - a part of the evef. spent at Walter's - Katie + Dennis now there. Home again before 10 - found Charl there.

## Right Page

### Fri. March 22, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day - after bkfs: attended to regular duties + then went to City Hall + had the negligent clerk add the Surrogate's seal to the attested copy of Annie's Will, omitted by him yesterday - then to Henderson + Co's in Courtlandt St. to get a list of seeds for Montrose - then to Grand Central Station to Treas' office N.Y.C. - then home - soon after 12 -
Lunch soon after 1 - Katie T. went to Po'k. on the 3.30 train - I finished letter to S.R. Kohler - In the afternoon I had calls from Mr. Will Parke - Miss A.D. Abbatt + from Mr. Sidney Smite - Dinner at 6.30 - Evef. I went to N.A. meeting of Com. on Site - had an animated time - all present but J. Q. A. Ward. Reports were made by Wood + myself of our interviews with Carnegie, Bigelow + Seth Low - The Com. begins to comprehend the shape of the work ahead of it + got hold of threads of some of the strings that form the tangle. The character of the schools of the New Academy was discussed + a meeting of N.A.'s who have studied in the schools abroad was arranged for next Thurs. evef. Home at 11.
