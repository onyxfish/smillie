## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 4, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A little rain during last night \+ a slight spatter at about 7 p.m. \- Otherwise the day was fair \+ beautiful much of the m'f. I wrote upon a letter for the Republican upon the subject of sewers \- Then out for more than an hour went about on the place on a tour of inspection. Dinner at 2. After that some more writing \- to Mrs. Rachel Mufford \+ two notes on U.A.f.S. business. At 5 got on my bicycle \+ had an hours exercise \- went out to the lake \+ tried some coasting on the road \+ found it very enjoyable. After tea went to P.O. with letters.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 5, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- warm but breezy. All of my spare time during the day given to Town sewerage \- In the m'f. I wrote a paper to be presented to the Town Council this eve'g. urging a vote for or against inviting Mr. Whitemore to come here as a Sanitary Engineer to report \+ advise a plan of sewerage \+ of sewage disposal \- Took a ride down town on my wheel at noon. Dinner at 1. Afternoon, completed letter to Republican \+ at 5 o'clk. went down to the office where I met Taylor, Dr. Halsey \+ Lott, members of the Town Council, to whom I read my letters \- Immediately after tea went down town to see the bicycle parade \- 23 or 4 turned out, fine bringing up the rear \- All out on the Piazza until 10 o'clk. beautiful moonlight night.
