### Sun. April 14, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Easter Sunday — the boys went to Sunday School exercises at Dr. Collyer's Park Ave. & 34th S. — brought home for me a fine Easter lily plant in pot having a card & my name upon it — in the afternoon, Sheldon came from Dr. Williams' Church 4th Ave. & 20th S., bringing a great lily plant & pot, also bearing a card with my name.
