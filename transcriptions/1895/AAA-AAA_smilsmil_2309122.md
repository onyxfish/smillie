## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 15, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another delightful day. After my home duties I went to 2° Nat. Bk. \+ made a loan of $8500 with which to make final payment on 36° S. (Sniffen Court) property. Then to Studio \+ worked awhile on plate Tower on the Rhine. Nicoll came in at 12 to tell me what he had learned from most Flagg concerning his Tilden Trust, N.A. of D. combination for Reservoir Square. Home at 1 \+ had a hurried lunch \- then to Mr. Lee's office \+ thence to lawyers of estate of Julia Jerome where title was passed \+ I paid $8500 dollars for the Stable in 2. 36° S\- making $9500 which with $1500 to P.J. Byrnes for cancellation of a 6 year lease makes a total of $11,000 as cost of the property. It was 3.30 before we got thro' with the transaction. Then I went to Charl's for a moment then uptown to studio. Signed lease of that \+ paid rent up to May 1, '95. Home about 5 \- tried to get a rest before dinner. Evef. Katie Adams spent with Fred \+ Alice Ireland while I went to N.A. \- Meeting of Com. on Stat \+ Plan \- full meeting \- Ward, Dielman, Nicoll, J.G. Brown \+, ex-off. T.W. Wood. The discussion was animated \+ very general upon the several general propositions before the com. \- The Tilden Trust Plan \- The H.R. Butler Plan \- The Artists' gate plan \- The Columbia University Plan \- etc. etc. We adjourned at 10.30 until next Weds. eveg. I got home \+ to bed at 11.30. Oh, how lonesome to come to a dark \+, to me, empty house. My boys \- our boys were there, but there is a void into which they do not enter at all. Another restless night.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 16, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant mf. but growing overcast and raw later in the day. After my morning duties R. \+ I went to barbers \+ had our hair cut. Then I ret'd. to the house \+ spent the rest of the mf. in going over some of Annie's private papers \- an ordeal that intensified the dull, persistent pain that now has possession of me. Lunch at 1. After that, resumed my work until I went to meet Mary \+ Katie T. who got to Grand Cent. Station at 3.25. Mary Ireland \+ Gene Atwater at the house. Katie Adams left us late in the afternoon and went to Fred Ireland's. R. \+ I had a short walk before 6. Dinner at 6.30 Evef. Charl came in \+ stayed until after 10. Then to bed \+ had a fair night's rest. — but oh, the hopelessness to me of that empty bed.
