## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 24, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A sultry, uncomfortable m'f. with an enervating south wind blowing — soon after midday a change came \+ about 5 o'clk a heavy shower came continuing for more than an hour — then clearing \+ much cooler. A skunk was caught in a trap near the house — Dana Austen came \+ shot it \+ we had perfume as well as sultry heat. I commenced another crayon sketch portrait of Gen'l. Lee, working over the photo. enlargement left by Mr. Darnore — From him I Rec'd to day a photo. of a painting of three confederate generals, Lee being one of them. It will be of no service to me. The shower made some trouble about the new dormer window — I went to P.O. at 8 o'clk — then some magazine reading.

Bessie Troops went to Silver Lake in the m'f. to be gone for some days.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 25, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day — I was out of doors all the m'f. going about over the place \+ looking after garden matters. It was after 1 o'clk. before I finished. The I took a half hour bicycle ride. Dinner at 2 — had a rest — some reading, \+ a nap after that. Then out with the boys — each of us had our wheels \+ we had half an hour of riding over the grass \+ roads on the place.
