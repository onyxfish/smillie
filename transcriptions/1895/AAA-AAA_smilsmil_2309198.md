## Left Page

### Tues. July 23, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cool day + somewhat overcast much of the time — Got to work upon plans for Katie's room as early as I could + in the course of the mf. Jill came to get them preparatory to beginning his carpenter work — Before dinner went down town on my wheel to P.O. Dinner at 1 — + then again to work on plans. Jill came + took some of the Chestnut lumber, stuck up in the barn, down to his shop. At 5 I went there, on my wheel, with drawings — then rode out Depot St. to Harrington's miles + out on back road — back, on the D.L. + W. Depot Road — Home at 6 — Cyclometer showing a ride of about 6 miles — I rested + had tea after the others — Gretta went to her sisters for a visit + her sister Bessie takes her place. Eve'g. Mr. Cromwell came in for a call. I escorted her to her home at about 9 o'clk. Read news paper until after 10.

## Right Page

### Wed. July 24, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

comparatively cool — more half clouded, hazy weather. Warner came in the mf. + completed the painting of canvas on the attic bed rooms — I was with him for some time — worked a little while on plan for Katie's ceiling — then a short ride on bicycle — + dinner — After dinner I indulged in a nap — then I finished ceiling plans — Rev. Mr. Denton + his wife called upon Mary + Kate at about 4 P.m — Men, with Katie T. + Jim, I went out for a bicycle ride — we went out on the road running back of "the little depot" to the old Post road thence around to the turnpike + home in less than an hour + a half, having ridden 4½ miles + walked one — The roads are execrable. A rest, a bath + change of clothing — then tea — wrote a note to Katie + Adams + downtown to P.O. etc — Eve'g. with newspaper.
