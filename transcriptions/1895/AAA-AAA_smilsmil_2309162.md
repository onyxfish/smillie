## Left Page

### Sun. May 12, 1895

**Weather:** Rain. cold \+ raw, during the m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

In the afternoon it cleared fresher and colder. Bkfp. at 9 — then Reading papers \+ writing some notes in connection with Montrose sewer matters — getting drowsy, I took a nap at about noon \+ more writing before dinner. Part of a long letter to Jas. P. Taylor, Editor \+ Ind. Rep. of Montrose, about sewers. Dinner at 2 — then finished letter to Taylor — also notices to Site Com. \+ a short letter to Nealie Adams. Then out for a walk with the boys — had on my Spring overcoat but found the air so cold that we shortened our walk. Tea at 6.30. Roger came in for a short time \+ Charl — I had to put R. to bed — so I did not get to call on the Irelands in 17th S. as I had expected to. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 13, 1895

**Weather:** Very cold \+ raw m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Took R. to school \+ spent part of m'f. in arranging my disordered letters \+ papers and writing notes — went for R. at 12.30 \+ lunch at 1 — after that, a nap — At 3.30 I went by appointment to the Century \+ met there T.R. Butler \+ had a talk with him concerning his plan for Site Com. He had prepared a plan for Procedure — meeting some of my objections to the plan as originally presented we had a long conversation — then I went to Dr. Deeds o in 45th S. \+ made further arrangements with him as to operation on Jim's nose, set down for next Friday \+ not in — left a note for him. Then home \+ got off — we met Fanny R. \+ Mrs. Reddington as we went to 34th S. to Bicycle School, where R. \+ I took a lesson — R's 4th \+ my first — I got along fairly well. Dinner at 7. Sisy \+ Conudie meeting N.A. Home at 11.
