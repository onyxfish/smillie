## Left Page

### Tues. May 28, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightfully fresh, breezy day. Remained to when Mr. Bennett came — then he, Mollie A., Katie T., Jim \+ I started off on our wheels \+ had a very funny time — we N. B. is not always at home upon his wheel — Jim was the best of the lot — but we managed to climb about half way up East Rock on the N. side — walking most of the way up the incline — then we branched off to [illegible] — road pretty rough — on one steep bit my wheel ran away with me and I [illegible] into K— general upset, some thing learned \+ no great damage done — we had a very varied ride \+ got home very hot \+ jolly well tired out — then a sponge bath — change \+ rest before lunch at 1 — After that I had a nap \+ feeling much fresher took a good walk with Ralph along [illegible] \+ beyond the city confines. Dinner at usual time — Eve. in Music Rooms — with a very little music.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 29, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day, but warm. I rode on my bicycle in the Park, alone, from 10 to 12 \+ had a very good time practising many different things. I really enjoyed it very much. The usual precautions after getting home — bath — change \+ rest — After lunch Frank went off on a fishing excursion, to be gone several days. Katie A. Kate Mollie A. — Jim \+ Ralph went on an excursion to Savin Rock, \+ of course had a very good time. — after my nap — I sauntered down town — about the College buildings \+ then to Station, where I bought tickets to N.Y. Then home at 6 — meeting Roger, who has just come from N.Y. to spend Decoration Day \+ Sunday at home. — All being tired in the eve'g. we went to bed early.
