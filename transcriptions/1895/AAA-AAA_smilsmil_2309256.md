## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 20, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day — wet, growing colder — Got to studio soon after 9 — worked until near noon on Site Com. Report — then took hold of lee plate until 1 — then to lunch — After lunch went to 2°. Nat. Bk. — Then to Calverly's — he is on Jury duty — Then to Tiffany's — T. not in, but saw Glass panels for front door — I don't like them much — New to Hawkins — and paid bill $4.75 — Then to studio \+ worked as long as I could see on lee plate — Dinner at 6.30 — Eng could not make up my mind to go to W. C. Soc'y meeting — Charl came in \+ spent the evey, beginning late.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cold, windy day — After bkf. called at Walter's — Carl Sprague \+ wife there — learned that Mary's Louise's down with diptheria — but not supposed to be dangerously sick — When I got to studio the Ther. was 55° — but before noon it got above 70°. I worked until noon on Site Com. Report, \+ on lee plate until 1 — then home to lunch \+ again to studio at 2 — on lee plate until 4.30 — then a rest — Dinner before 7 — Fred \+ Alice Ireland \+ Katie Adams dined with us. They had to leave about 9 because Alice's asthma troubled her so much — Later I wrote a couple of notes \+ twice went to studio carrying things.
