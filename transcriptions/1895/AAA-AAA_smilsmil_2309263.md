## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 4, 1895

**Weather:** Bright \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ cold \- again, more time given to the draining of the cellar. Some of the tile laid \- I am hopeful that I shall eventually get through the mess. I worked for some time on Site Com. Report, trying to get it into harmony with Butler's paper which will form a very considerable part of it. Afternoon, Jim came to studio at about 3 \+ with him went to Brooks Bros. \+ bot. him a "reefer" \- then I went to Putnams to see about getting Site Com. Report printed \- then again to studio \- At 5.30 to Century to see Butler \- not there \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve\[ning\] writing notes.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 5, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very cold, wintry day \- at times threatening snow \- clear at night. Another day showing but little advance many direction. The men worked in the cellar \+ also did a little outside. Of course, I was running after them all the time. \- I went to H.R. Butler's in 47\. S\- but he was not at home \- at about 12 \- Saw Mr. B \- ourate a note for b \- making an appointment for this week \- Then home to lunch \+ Taylor's man came \+ put up some outline pictures in studio \- I worked meanwhile on dea plate. Dinner at 6.30 \- at \+ H.W. Butler came \+ until 11 we talked over the material that is to go to the make up of the Site Com. Report. I am encouraged to believe that everything is getting into very good shape. \+ B \- seems to be hopeful. He spoke warmly in praise of my part of the report.
