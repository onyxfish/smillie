## Left Page

### Sat. March 23, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another delightful day - much of the mg. spent writing - got copy of Annie's will off to Howell The Treas. of Susquehanna Valley R.R. to secure transfer of stock - After lunch Nicoll came on Site business together we went to Private View of the Socy. of Am. Artists - 57th St. \+ spent an hour or more looking at the paintings. Then I went to J. 2. A. Ward's for a few minutes \+ from there to Dr. Nevins, artist in W. 47th St. \+ to Dielmans, Bldg, 44th \+ then home - Dinner at 6.30 Charl came in soon after 7 to talk Bicycle \- I gave a conditional order for a 2nd hand one that has been offered him - I want to ride with Jim \+ Ralph \+ keep in touch with them in spite of my age - at 9 I went to Century \+ talked with a number of the younger artists — an amusing \+ in some respects, interesting controversial discussion as to technique in art was carried on in one group — Howard R. Butler, Lockwood De Forest, Walter Shirlaw - I. Ward Perry \+ others taking part - Butler is well read on the subject, is fluent, \+ when not away beyond my comprehension was very interesting to me.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 24, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some weak sunshine in the mtg. - then overcast - light rain after 2 o'clk. \+ clearing at 6. Bkfst. at about 9 - near 12, Jim, Ralph \+ I went up to Riverside Park \+ up the Drive to 70th St. - then to the Boulevard \+ home by car - Dinner at 2 - wrote to Herrick \+ notes on H.H. business It was nearly 6 when I went around to Mary (ned) Cook's, she having ret. from Buffalo last night, but did not see her as she was dressing to go out. I took a short walk - tea before 7 - Suety. Harry Watson's came in for half an hour to enquire for some early Am. paintings for Albion Club system. Later, Geo. \+ then Charl came in \+ they stayed until nearly 11.
