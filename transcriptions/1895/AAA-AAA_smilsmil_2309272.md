## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 24, 1895

**Weather:** mild \+ overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild \+ overcast \- Immediately after bkfs. I wrote a note to th R. Butler \+ sent it to him by messenger \- He came to studio before 11 o'clk \+ stayed until after 12 discussing the situation with me. Lunch at 1 \- after that to Kimmuel \& Voigts \+ got two pfp from dee portrait \- \+ to studio at 3.30 \- Tacked down cut pfp. Did a little more Xmas shopping \+ had a rest before dinner \- Some active preparations for Xmas in the eve'g, brought a sense of loss very painfully to me. Charl came in, just home from Boston.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 25, 1895

**Weather:** mild, quiet day, overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkfs. at 8 \+ after it the boys had a great time in emptying their stockings \+ opening their many Xmas pfp. They were ecstatic over their steam engines \- it was a sore, sore trial to me \- memories of our last Xmas could not be driven away. Before 11 O went to studio \+ spent the rest of the with there, studying new pfp. taken yesterday, \+ trying to clear up some of the confusion caused by the piles of truck still foiled about \- Lunch at 1 \- The boys ran their engines for some time after it. Then I went out for a walk \- left a Water color, in frame, at D. Male's \- went to 72 S. \+ back \- wrote letter to Robbins \+ sent him S.te Com. Report. Home at 6. Dinner before 7 \- Geo., Nellie \+ their boys in bet. 5.30 \+ 6.30 \- Charl dined with us \- nothing particular done in the eve'g. Geo. came in for a moment at about 10 \+ Charl went away with him.
