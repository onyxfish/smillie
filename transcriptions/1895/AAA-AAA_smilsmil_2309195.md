## Left Page

### Wed. July 17, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very uncomfortably hot day. Thunder storms passed very near us in the afternoon but we got only a light sprinkle. Wm Spence, the old darky that I have hired to take Herrick's place, put in his first days work on the place \+ did fairly well. I worked at tacking up canvas in Kate's room, attic, the work that Herrick left unfinished, but found it so severe a strain on me in various ways that I did not get it finished as I had hoped to. In the evef I went to Warner's house \+ told him that I wd not be ready for him until Friday. I met Herrick at the P.O. \+ told him that I wished him to find another house for his use \+ that while he was in mine I should expect him to pay rent. Sat on piazza until after 10.

## Right Page

### Thur. July 18, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very much cooler day \- sunny without being very bright. After bkfs: the usual garden \+ other duties \- then to work in attic tacking canvas to ceiling \- finished it soon after 12 oclt. Took a short bicycle ride to the top of the hill by Jones' lake \- then dinner \- then a rest \- then made up a pail of sizing \+ worked faithfully until near 7 oclt. before I finished re-sizing the canvas tacked upon Kate's wall \- Tea after 7 \- \+ at 8 went down town for the mail etc. \- Evef, reading news paper until 10.
