## Left Page

### Sat. July 13, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, but not agreeable, to my notion - being very warm with strong, cool breeze. Exertion overheated + the breeze chilled. The m'f. was divided between Herrick + his work in the attic + writing letters until nearly - Then I went down town on my wheel + after going to P.O. + transacting other business, I had a little ride, then home - a rest - + my dinner. After that, again with Herrick and re-sized some of the canvases on the wall. After 3 again down town on my wheel + spent some time at Cooley's over-hauling it. Then another short, but rather hard ride home + tasted tea at 6.30. Priscilla Poore + daughter came in just after + at 8 I walked to Dan. Sayre's with them. Then on to P.O. A peripatetic vendor of nostrums had his wagon in front of the Exchange Hotel + had a great crowd. He had two confederates, another man who, like himself, was dressed as a cowboy, + a young woman - not to forget a performing dog. The entertainment was characterized - in a way it was amusing + I was a watcher for a few minutes while warts + corns were removed + a blacksmith's hands were made "white as snow + soft as kid" — Then home + to bed early.

## Right Page

### Sun. July 14, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day - bkf's. soon after 8 o'clk. Mrs. Risley presiding in the kitchen as Katie + Hetta have been given a holiday + went early to G's home near Silver Lake. They eat? at 8.45 My m'f., until 12 was given to domestic duties. The rest of the time before dinner at 2 was given to writing - planning finish of the attic + riding my bicycle. After dinner I worked again upon attic plans until I began to feel very stupid. About 4.30 I got upon my bicycle + by my cyclometer I rode 6½ miles, + walked up two hills only. I am sure that I am improving in lung + heart action as well as in muscular strength. The roads are shockingly rough - I may get strength + confidence by such practice but I can never get grace or "good form." I got in at 6.30 very warm + tired, so I lay down on my bed for an hour + when cooled down + rested I took my tea alone. Many other girls, just Ralph having gone to church. They eat? just before + went right to bed.
