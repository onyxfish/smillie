## Left Page

### Thur. April 4, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some pleasant sunshine came in at the window. I passed a wretched night of high fever + pain — as I burned + coughed I could not but think of Anim who a few weeks ago had occupied the vacant place beside me + had burned + coughed in the same way. A night passed in such manner is not inspiring. I kept my bed all day, not getting up even for a change. Yale came before 4 P. m. + made an effort to cheer me. The great pain, thro' my chest, under R. shoulder blade was so much modified that I suffered very little inconvenience from it. Chase came in the eve + for a time sat by my bed — my chill commenced before he left.

A cot was made up in the nursery + Ralph slept there, leaving me alone.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 5, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant, beautiful day, at least as I see it from my bed. I passed a sort of night-mare night. a chill came upon me before 11 after which I burned up. I took my temp. at 1 A.m., almost 104°. Nothing could be more dismal than the watches of such a night. The fever, under drugs administered, began to abate after 2 o'clk. + before 6 I was comparatively comfortable + got some sleep. Yale came before 10 A.m. + put me on a quinine diet. The day was one of great pain + misery — fever continuing high — great distress in head which was much added to by the increasing effects of the quinine. By night time I got quite iced. \+ when Yale came in the eve, I told him that I did not want to be alone another night. I had not nerve enough to contemplate without much disquietude, another such experience as last night. Chase, who was present, immediately scurried about + by 11 o'clk. he returned with Miss Ida Strickland (she nursed Annie) whom he had captured in her bed. The summons was so short that she could not be supplied with a bed so she passed the rest of the night in a chair. I had a repetition of chilliness + high temp., with great pain in the head, getting a little feverish + troublesome sleep.
