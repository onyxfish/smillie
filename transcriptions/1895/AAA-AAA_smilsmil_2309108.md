## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 22, 1895

**Weather:** Bright & rather mild for awhile in the mf. but grew colder, overcast and squally.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Annies condition does not improve — the same may be said of the other invalids. After 8½. I was interviewed & detained by Crosby — Real estate man. It was considerably after 10 when I got to A. A. V. I had. 4 pts. Taken from my Corona Study — I but one each from my Aqua-tint experiments. To Studio just about 12. 30 — thru home to lunch. Afternoon, I took Ralph with me & walked down to T.W. Wood's studio at W. 10 St. where I had a talk with him concerning the N.A. Com. on Site and the vacancy caused by resignation of Jas. Hart — Took R. home & at 5 went to Clinton & met Howard R. Butler by appointment & talked of his plan of an enlarged Fine Arts Socy embracing the N.A. I told him that I would like him to meet the Site Com. & explain his plan to them — to which he assented. Home to dinner & spent the eve. & waiting on Annie.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 23, 1895

**Weather:** Beautiful winter day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Before 9 I went to Falls to Report very high temperature — I was not able to get to Studio until after 12 o'clk — there for a moment only — thru home to lunch — my duties as a nurse consumed so much time that I did not get back in the afternoon. I managed to get a short nap before dinner.

Soon after 8 J.A. Ward came in & later, Fol — Dielman, we three, with Nicole, who is out of town, constitute the Site Com. of N.A. After much discussion we elected J.G. Brown to fill the vacancy. Then the list of sites was gone over & some of them were discussed — I grew very wild as the time went on & it grew late.

At a little after 11 I excused myself & went up to Annie's room — Katie Troop had gone to bed & A. was alone — fever still high — She had taken medicine to move her bowels & when I got to the bed room I found her up & waiting upon herself — for this, I can never forgive myself — I should have sent W— & D. away before 11 & have devoted myself to A — She insisted however that she was fully protected & refused to use the bed pan that I had got for her — After getting her back to bed, I returned to W. & D. & brought the conference to a close before 12 & then to bed.
