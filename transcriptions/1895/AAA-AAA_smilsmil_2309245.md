## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 27, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day \- up late, of course, \+ had bkfs \- at about 9 \- It was aftr 10 when I went to Walther's \+ after a little visit there he \+ I went to the new studio in 36" S\. \- There we saw Maynard who appears to be quite settled in his new quarters \- a very much pleas\- I am disappointed in the effect of the proportions of my room \- It seems so very narrow in proportion to height \+ length. I wish I could have built it by days work \+ superintended the whole of it. I would not have had many of the extravagances now embodied in the building \+ I would have had many little conveniences \+ economies of space that are not to be found there now. The appearance of the outside of the building was a surprise to me, it is so large \+ important looking \- Home to dinner at 2 \- after dinner I went again to studio with Mary, Kittie, Bessie, Jim \+ Raefor \- after that I went to the Century \+ saw Maynard for a moment \- Tea at 6.30 \- a quiet evng \+ early to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 28, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rather cold \+ windy but bright \+ clear. Bkfs \- at 8 \- Geo\. came in soon after we had finished \- At about 9.30 I took Ralph to Mr. Walton's school wher new rooms at 3 24 E. 42d St\. Then back to the house \+ with Geo\. to studio in 36d St\. There for some time \+ in Maynard's studio. When Geo\. left I went to Davis in 42d St\. \+ ordered coal \- Then to Hugh Douglass \- then to Kyle \+ Son in 3d Ave \- Real estate agents, \+ placed the letting future under studio in their hands \- Then to Union Transfer Co\. in 21st St\. \+ arranged with them to move some of my studio traps to\-morrow. Then to Calurary's studio \+ home to lunch at 1 \- After lunch, feeling tired I took a little nap \- Then to market \+ grocers and again to studio \- Home at about 5 \+ unpacking bicycles until dinner time \- At 8 went to Council meeting N.A. of D\. \- There until 10.40 \- Geo\. \+ I walked up together \-
