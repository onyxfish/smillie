## Left Page

### Tues. May 14, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very wet day \- a succession of heavy showers with much intermediate drip. Took R\- up to 47th S\- from there rode to 10th S\- \+ spent nearly two hours with J.G. Brown \+ Nicoll \- discussing the Butler plan before the Site Com. Then, at 11.40, I went to NA \+ got some documents \+ then again to 47th S\- \+ got R\- on our way home I stopped at Hotel \+ telephoned to Mr. Wm Lee, making an app't. to meet him at 4 at his office \- lunch. Nate arr'd. from Po'k. I had a nap. \+ at 4 was at Mr. Lee's \- we discussed the papers of the Howard Russell Butler plan of Site in W. 57th S, embracing [illegible] until 5 15 left the papers with him \- Then home \- Mollie Adams just in from New Haven \- Dinner before). [illegible] Roger Adams came in for a little while \- Chase came, bringing a lot of things to add to Jim's Bicycle Kit \- a cyclometer \- foot pumps \- new kit \- etc \- contributions from Mary, Nate \+ Katherine to his birth day in advance \- my head troubling me \+ I went to bed early \-

## Right Page

### Wed. May 15, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant spring like, but crisp, day \- I took R\- to 47th S\- Mill home \+ rested for a while \- then to market \+ grocers \- To Daires [Coal] in 42nd S\- out 11 to Dr. Allens in 47th S\- He got through with me at 11.30 \- I went to J.Q.A. Ward's in 52d S\- \+ talked N.A. \+ Site matters \+ at 12.30 went for R\- \+ home at 1 \- Lunch after that, a nap \- Then to Walter's office \+ looked over his plans \+ specifications for Studio \+ the several bids Rec'd. \+ the need to accept one by Lamb \- It was 6.30 when I got home \- found Nicoll and Byrnes waiting for me \- got rid of B\- my soon \- Nicoll stayed to dinner \- after which we read over a letter from Mr. Lee concerning the Butler plan sub\- mitted to Site Com. Then we went to N.A. f[or] D\- \+ had a Sites Com. meeting that was without result \- Home at 11 \-
