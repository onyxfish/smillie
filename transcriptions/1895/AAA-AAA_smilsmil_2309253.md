## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 12, 1895

**Weather:** Fine clear, cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine clear, cold day — Mr. Barmore called immediately after bkfst. \+ detained me for an hour. I spent the m'f getting a great many little things assorted into groups \+ into various places. About 1 went to Dock's \+ arranged to have a cabinet maker come to the studio at 2 \+ fix up my big book case in Maynard's studio. Then home to lunch. To studio at 2 — men there to move the case from my room up to Maynard's — the cabinet maker fussed about most of the aftr'noon — Dielman came in late to see me about my lent. Lee etching — Ev'g. To Century club at about 9.30 — there for only a few minutes. Walked to 34th St. \+ 5 Ave. with Nicoll \+ there we parted.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 13, 1895

**Weather:** Overcast, damp  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast, damp, raw \+ disagreeable m'f. clearing in the afternoon. I went to the butcher's \+ grocers as usual. Stopped at Walter's \+ had a talk with him — then to studio where the day was spent in sifting \+ sorting the many things that have not yet found a place. In the even'g I went back \+ forth several times between house \+ studio carrying rugs, etc. \+ at last to bed, very tired.
