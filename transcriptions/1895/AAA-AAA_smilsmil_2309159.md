## Left Page

### Mon. May 6, 1895

**Weather:** Dull, raw m'f., clearing to bright & warm  **Temp:**

day. Went with R. to Mrs. Conklin's \+ as Kennedy did not go to school I took R. up to 47' S. — Then to N.A. — met Gardner, the accountant, \+ went very carefully over figures — then I went to Jones Tiffanys. \+ then to Ja'. R. Brevoorl \+ talked with both of them on N.A. affairs — there again to 47' S. — got R. \+ home. — Lunch — after that to market etc. Then to 54 Wall S'. called on S. D. Cournan who is delinquent in W. due on a co'. of Nits on the 1st inst. He was not in — then to Mr. Lee \+ saw him about Academy business — Then to Charl \+ saw him for a moment — then home \+ had a nap before dinner — Tom cook is quartered upon us, Jonise being down with "german measles". While at dinner many came in from Po'k. — left Katharine there at her neighbors, Mrs. Dave, too sick to travel — Evely. went to Council meeting at N.A. — There untill 10. — Home at 10.30 — Charl had just gone.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 7, 1895

**Weather:** A very warm day  **Temp:**

— After bkf. took R. to 47' S. Then to market, etc. \+ home at 10 commenced to write Treas'? Report for a — about noon Mr. Pakenoe of Topeka A.C. Co. called \+ we talked of the Wilson \+ Tom western Nitts — with the planet that together we went to Safe Deposit Co. where I gave him all the papers connected with the Nelson \+ Chapman loans \+ abstract of title to Wall loan — he to get title for me to Wall game by giving up the Nelson \+ Chapman claims if possible. — Then I went to N.A. — thence home to lunch — at 2 o'clk. young man from Lee \+ Lee came with final papers \+ [illegible] met. Life \+ N.A. — to be signed by Wood \+ self at Met. \+ . O'd. — at 3.30 — I went to 54 W. \+ 10' S. \+ saw Wood \+ he agreed to be at the place on time — then I went alone \+ having a little time I bought hat \+ \+ neck tie — \+ got to Met. Bldg. in time — I was sent up to Vice Pres Fiske's office where Wood was — Fiske came on time — but tea was half an hour late — the papers were duly signed — we were shown the palatial room of the Directors \+ left at 4.30. I went home \+ rested before I again began work on my Treas' Report — Dinner at 6.30 — Every I did some more work on my Treas' Report — Charl came in for a few moments — To bed soon after 10.
