## Left Page

### Wed. April 24, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another charming day \- \+ another day of depressing weakness \- I suppose that I am improving in health but the improvement is so slow as to be imperceptible \- When I awoke Temp. 4/5° below normal \- normal at noon, \+ 3/10° below normal at 11 p.m. \- a poor showing but an impovt. on yesterday went out before 11 \+ attended to my household matters \- went to Grand Central office \+ got 50 shares of stock that had been transf. to my name \- on my way home, near 1 o'clk., met Nicoll \- she went in with me \+ we talked over NA affairs for a while \- Lunch at 1 \- after that I had a Rest \- at 3.30 went with Jim \+ Ralph to O'Neill's \+ got Jim a pair of shoes \- Then home \- Jim gave R\. a lesson on his Bicycle \- Then I wrote some notes \- Dinner before 7. \- after, it \+ some games until the boys' bed time \- one of the young fellows called on Katie T — but he left early \- lights out in the parlor at 10 \- but I did not get to bed until after 11.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 25, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** 64° at 4 p.m.

Very uncomfortably warm day. Hard work to drag about. Ralph went to school \+ promptly etc., reporting that Gilbert Jones was down with Scarlet fever \+ that there would be no school. It was after 10 when I got out \+ attended to my usual duties \- Then I tried to give to R\. a lesson on his Bicycle in the street \- Then with him I went to call at Mr. Conklins in 36th S. \- R\. lunched there with her son Kennedy. Nicoll sent to me a letter from Mr. Chas. T. Barney concerning NA \+ S. etc matters \- I immediately wrote to him requesting an interview. Lunch at 1. Then a Rest \- about 3 Nicoll came in \+ I got all the latest NA news \- There is a great tempest brewing \- I hope that I may get upon my feet to take a part in it \- Then I wrote to Ward \- went out to post some notes \+ met Yale \+ walked a short way with him. About 5 went with the two Katies, Jim \+ Ralph to the Cycling Academy in 34th S. The two K's \+ R\. Rode \- Dinner at 6.45 \- every Charlk came in, just from Boston \- he left at 10.30 Then to bed \-
