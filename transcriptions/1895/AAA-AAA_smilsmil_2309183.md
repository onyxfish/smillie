## Left Page

### Sun. June 23, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day \- Bkf. at 8.30
I spent some time in studio \+
wrote a letter to Norma Dreland.
Dinner at 2 \- Afternoon about
the place looking after things

to bed early \- a better nights
rest than the night before

## Right Page

### Mon. June 24, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant mf \- overcast before
noon \- very light rain, p.m \- \+ pleasant
evef. Got up early intending to take
a bicycle ride but found that the pedals
thro' carelessness on my part, had worn
in places, needing attention \- after Bkf.
I went down town taking wheel to
Conley's where I waited nearly an hour
while it was being fixed \- It was about
1 when I got home. There Katie, Jim,
Ralph \+ I went to the upper track at
Fair Ground, 3/16 of a mile around \- \+
we rode for nearly an hour \- then home
washed \+ changed. Kate Parker Riley
having spent much of the mf on
our Piazza dined with us \- Aftern
I spent much of it in the garden
transplanting lettuce etc. After
tea I went to P.O. etc \- Evef.
with newspaper \-
