## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 20, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very mild, depressing day — I got up with a distressing headache which clung to me all day unfitting me for work of any kind. I spent a good part of the m'g in my big chair at studio — I did a little work on lee plate. Home to lunch at 1 — after that I went to Putrames \+ gave Stu Report f/p a last revision \+ at 3 went to H.R. Butler's, 47th S. \+ conferred with him for half an hour — then to studio \+ managed to do about another half hour work on lee plate. Then home. Dielman came in at 5.30 \+ stayed until 6. Then I rested until dinner time. Suet f, Read aloud to the boys for awhile — then fooled with newspapers — before 10 went to studio — then home \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very mild, some rain bet 9 \+ 10 a.m. Then clearing — high wind \+ showers at night. I worked on the m'g. on lee plate, finishing with graver \+ dry point about the face. Miss Hannah, Bessie Throop's Kindergarten teacher lunched with us — At 2.30 Ralph went back to studio with me \+ stayed an hour or more. Later Dr. Gerster came in — I put in a fair amt. of work, with burnisher, on lee portrait — Had a walk up 5th Ave. to 60th S. \+ home before dinner. Suet f, some reading aloud to the boys — \+ then awhile with the j'l f Harper. To bed early.
