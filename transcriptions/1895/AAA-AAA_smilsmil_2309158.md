## Left Page

### Sat. May 4, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm day, cloudless, shimmering at times. Up early \+ wrote part of Report of Com. on Site \& Plan to be presented at N.A. annual meeting. After bkfst - went with Jim \& Ralph to a Bicycle shop in Lex. Ave — They taking their wheels for repairs — then home \+ completed Report and after 11 went to N.A. \+ with Mr. Gardner, the accountant, went to 5th Ave. Safe Deposit Co. \+ checked the W.A. Securities — We parted at 1 \+ I went to Farragut Status, Mad. Sq., having Charl.'s camera \+ made four exposures — then home to lunch at 1.45 taking a brick of ice cream with me — after lunch I had a nap, as is my custom now, \+ they revised Site Report — at 3 went with R— to Bicycle man again — Dinner at 6.30 at 8 Charl. came up for a moment — I rode over to 5th Ave. with him in his Cab — then to Century Club, monthly meeting — at 8.30, by appointment, met members of Site Com. except J.D. Ward — Read my Report to them \+ after — one amendments it was approved — after that, a very short business meeting of the club — after that some buzzing about N.A. — in preparation for the fight on Wed. which promises to be a hot one — Two tickets have been printed — it is not pleasing to me to find that I am on both of them. It suggests a two-horse act. I have no sympathy whatever with the old fogy party.
At 11 o'clk — I thought it was time for me to get home — to bed before 12.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 5, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast most of the day — some sunshine in the afternoon — Up unusually early. got up about 6 o'clk. \+ gave myself a grooming \+ a spongebath — then back to bed \+ had a nap before I got up at 7.30 — After bkfst — read for awhile — then got to work upon Report of Site Com. approved last night \+ I entirely recast it. Dinner at 2 — after that had a nap — then a short walk on Park Ave. — called at Murray Hill Hotel to see W. Mrs. Coleman but they had left yesterday — I had a call from Mary Coale in the mts'. She broached the matter of a visit from Tom during the summer at Montrose — Tea at 6.30 — Evg. I spent an hour at Walter Coale's — on my return home found Joe \+ Nellie \+ Charl — They left at about 10.30 — then to bed.
