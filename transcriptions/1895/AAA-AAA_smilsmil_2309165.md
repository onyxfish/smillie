## Left Page

### Sat. May 18, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain during the night + in the morning clearing pleasantly in the afternoon. Emma J- came at 8 to give m- a lesson - visit noted. I went to Dr. Allen's at 9.30 + was there only a few minutes - then to Safe Dep. Co. 5th Ave. + 23d. S- + made copy of Act exempting na f D. from taxation - then to carpet cleaner's + to Clark's, plumber. + home about 1- By mes. the carpenter, came, very anxious to get contract to put up studio Bldg in 36th S- about 12 I went to market, etc. - then to Cycle Ach. in 34th S- + met there Mollie H. + Katie T- we walked up 3d Ave. - but having messages I parted from them soon - but some mins fur- nishing for self - some drugs at Schmely - + then to Higgins' + gave orders to have the flues cleaned next Wed. - Then home + had lunch, late. About 2, P. Lewis called + saw Jim who seems to be getting along finely + already much improved in breathing thro nose + in hearing - after 3 I had a long nap. - Then wrote a note to Miss Truesdaff asking her to make arrangement some as last summer to go to Montrose. - At 6 went to Cycle Academy in 34th S- Took my wheel + had a ride on it - Dinner at 7 - I was 9 o'clk before I went to Century (9 o'clk Cook came in before I left the house.) - all members of 5th the Com. there but Nicole - we threshed the old straw of the Butter Scheme until 11 o'clk - without making any apparent advance. Then home + to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 19, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day - B'kfst. at 9 - newspapers awhile. - Then I wrote a note or two + at about noon, after Yale had made us a visit, Ralph + I went out for a walk - we went to G. C. Station + I set my watch - then to 5th Ave. + to Mad. Sq. + to na f D. There for a few min. + then home - Dinner at 2 - Roger Adams with us. At 4 I went to Cycle Ach by app't with Robbins, my teacher - we had the entire place to ourselves + I quite enjoyed my ride - Then to Walter's - there for half an hour - and home at 6 - Tea at 6.30 - Sue + Charl came in - Mollie A. took her tea at Ed W. Cook's + came in early - To bed before 11.
