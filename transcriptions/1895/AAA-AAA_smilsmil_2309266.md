## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 10, 1895

**Weather:** Miserably raw, overcast & disagreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very much out of sorts with sore throat + the other discomforts of a cold. Most of the rest was spent at studio in finishing the 9 post diagram of 57th & 58th St. property that I shall need to illustrate my Report to be read at N.A. for D. to-morrow night. After lunch I thought it wise to remain at home \+ I had a nap in the afternoon which was disturbed by a messenger from H.R. Butler with note informing me that there was a hitch in getting the dotions for the 57th & 58th St. property + that, under the circumstances it wd. not be wise to present the report at N.A. to-morrow eveg. — Later, wrote him a letter stating that I intended to be too unwell to go to the N.A. to-morrow evg. — Charl came in the eveg. He is suffering from severe cold — About 10 I went to studio + found furnace fire out — I was half an hour relighting it —

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 11, 1895

**Weather:** Bright, cold & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

My throat somewhat better. Immediately after bkfst. to studio, then downtown — to Royal Ins. & got Moses Smith — went to the manager of the Fine Ins. Tariff [illegible] — where I was able to have the Ins. Rate in my studio old materially reduced — to 2 Nassau St. — G.W. Dougherty, arranged about buying a small amt. of scrip to the amt. of $1,000 for an Ann Arbor R.R. bond — Then to Messrs Lee & Lee & saw Mr. W.H.L. Lee — Then to 74 Park Place. Ed. Cook's Office. Stockholders meeting to-day Ireland Real Estate Co. — Ell. C. was away on jury duty — Then to 23rd St. Putnam & Co. — Then to Tiffany & Co's & approved of glass for front door — Then to D.B. Butler & paid bill — then to Studio & then home. Lunch — Numa Ireland came in — Later G.W. Maynard came — + after he left I had a nap — Dinner about 7 — Eveg. Charl came in at 9 —
