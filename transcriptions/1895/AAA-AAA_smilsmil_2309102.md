## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 8, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Shocking day — dark \+ rainy — waited until 10 o'clk for Sam P. but he did not come. Then to Studio — Spent the remainder of the mf. mounting a fine old sheet of 1826 "Whatman" up on a backer for the "River" drawing but unfortunately I injured it so that I was obliged to cut it into two pieces. Home to lunch \+ back. Set W.C. Palette \+ started transf. W.C. on one of the pieces of paper mounted this mf. Subject "A clearing by the river" — the sketch of which I made in charcoal, based up on a photo. by Yale. Didnt make much progress. Home at 5 — Evef. loafed about, not in humor for work of any kind
\- To bed early.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 9, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold, raw \+ overcast. After 10 when I got to Studio — worked on Crecy en Brie drawing — more real estate men came to bother me. A young man came from Ge \+ R Read's with contract of sale, No. 2 Sniffeu Court, for me to sign — I made appt. to be at R's office bet. 3 \+ 4 o'clk. Then to bank \+ safe deposit — walked over with Wordsworth Thomson \+ his wife. Home to lunch — Then to Lee \+ Lee's — 20 Nassau St. — At 4 we went to Read's \+ signed contract. Then up town to N.A. of D. — met Nicole there — Home at 6.30 — Kate came down from P'k — dinner — Eve'f Charlie came in for awhile —
I did some N.A. writing.
