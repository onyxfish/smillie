## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 9, 1895

**Weather:** Clear, most of the day  **Temp:** not quite so cold as yesterday.

Emily Cook left very soon after bkfst., returning to her apartments. Katie Adams came in the afternoon, late, and took the place of Geo. & Emily. Rec'd. Telegram from Mary saying that she & Katie T. could not return to N.Y. to-day. I got to studio at to work on aqua-tint — Cathedral Tower at Ravello — laid a biting ground & prepared to bite — Nicoll came in at 12 & stayed until 1 — then I went home to lunch — back to studio & at work, burnishing up on aqua-tint "Tower at Constance, on the Rhine." Yale came in soon after 4 & prevailed upon me to go with him to see the mins paintings at the Ortgies Galleries — there we met Mr. Walker & after looking thro' the galleries very superficially we walked up town together — I got home at about 5.30. Dinner at 6.30 — Katie Adams taking her place at the head of the table. I felt much depressed. The Reality, the breadth & the depth of my loss seems to force itself upon me now with an increased sense of my helplessness & hopelessness. Andella was able to give the boys their baths & it was late, for them, before they got to bed. I was exceedingly heavy & I got to bed early, for me.
I rec'd. to-day a Postal card from S.R. Koehler, dated Jany. 11 — announcing his safe arrival at Porta Delgrada — Azores —
Plumbers, in throwing out gas & water by their carelessness, came near setting the house on fire this afternoon. I cannot get a clear statement but it appears that some alcohol that they were using took fire down in water closet in lower hall. When I got home the house was full of stench & smoke & most of the windows were open. In the mng. I went to 5th Ave. Safe Dep't. Co. with letter prep'd. by [illegible] & [illegible] & was permitted to open my box.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 10, 1895

**Weather:** Dull most of the day, not very cold, but below freezing.  **Temp:** some wind — clearing at night. Brilliant, cloudless moonlight night.

Bkfst. after 9 — Read newspapers — about noon went out for a walk — went to Mr. Jno. Bigelow's, 21 Gramercy Park & spent half an hour with him — then a round about walk home, getting in before 2 o'clk. Many (nee) there — a talk with her. Dinner. After that Charl came in — then Valentine, then Geo. About 5 I walked up to Yale's — Jim's cough troubles me. Tea at 6.30 — About 7 Y— came in & looked Jim over but suggested no new treatment — simply palliatives itself. Charl came in again and stayed until 10. To bed soon after.
