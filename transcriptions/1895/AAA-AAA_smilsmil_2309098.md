## Right Page

### Tues. Jan. 1, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, charming day — Ralph passed a quiet night, had a little fever in the n'th, \+ as he had no passage yet from bowels I gave him an enema after which he improved steadily. It was after 11 when I got to studio — Spent the rest of the day making a charcoal sketch after a small photo. Taken by Yale. I wish to make an etching after it. Home to lunch at 1 — Annie out to lunch at Alice Ireland's — R — well enough to eat at table with us. Back at 2.30 — home before 6. Geo., Nellie, \+ their three boys dined with us — also Chas. They all left early — Dr. Yale came in for a few minutes — I again spent some time upon my N. A. D. Site Scrap Book.
