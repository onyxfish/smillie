## Left Page

### Sat. April 6, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

For a number of the succeeding days I was utterly unable to keep any account of what transpired. It was but one story — Temp. not very high — rarely reaching 102° — but excessing great & persistent pain all over the head — bones of the face very sore — back of the head & neck the seat of pain — very tender even to the touch. A painful, bronchial cough with considerable expectoration — the first experience of my life of such trouble — racked me a good deal.
