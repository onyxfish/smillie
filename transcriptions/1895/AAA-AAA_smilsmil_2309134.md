## Left Page

### Mon. March 11, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unpleasant day without overt acts. After bkfp. went to 42d St. E. of 3d Ave. to Davies + ordered Kindling wood — then to Neumann's — the butcher + the grocer — then to N.A. + at last, got to studio after 11. Commenced picking up things preparatory to moving out as I have agreed to give Mr. Wochsler possession on Friday. At the N.A. I saw Hovenden + gabbed awhile with him. Home to lunch at 1 — then a rest + back to studio + worked until 6. Then home — dinner — wife, to Council meeting — an interesting discussion as to the advantages (if any) + the evils of having nude female models in the N.A. schools. This arose from some scandals that have just come to light.

## Right Page

### Tues. March 12, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Raw + disagreeable day with weak efforts at snow + drizzle. Got to studio at about 10 and to work preparing to move. Packing all the small things into drawers, etc. Had two Real-Estate callers. Home to lunch at 1 — after that, a little rest + then again to studio after 8 — got through with my work at 4.30. Then went to Am. Art Galleries + spent some time looking at the Raffaelli works. They are entirely beyond the line of my comprehension + too ugly, to my eyes, to give me any pleasure. Fred. Viton, of Boston, was there + tried to point out to me some charms of "blonde tonality" and "color notes" + the "intricacy of the streets" etc. Tried very hard to "catch on" but am sorry to note that I am getting too old to be plastic. Then to Union Transfer Co. + to Gas Co. 15th St. — moving place — once more to studio. Then home. Found Ralph out of sorts — Mar. gave him [illegible] as 100°. Indigestion probably. He ate very little + had Rhubarb + Soda when he went to bed at 9. every. I went to Am. Art Galleries + heard out new French teacher, Jean François Raffaelli lecture an American audience. It was all amusing exhibition of gaulish gall + assurance. He has a very meagre knowledge of our language — Some one had made for him a good English translation of his lecture + he read it in an exceedingly funny way. I don't think many who heard him were able to understand him. I was disappointed because he said nothing that was new + much that was trite as tho' it were a discovery of his — Very few of his statements could have provoked discussion. Beneath an affable exterior it is not hard to see that he is wholly + aggressively French. A very brief newspaper notice with portrait sketch by Abbey will be found in pocket.
