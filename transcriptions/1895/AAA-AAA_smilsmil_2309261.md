## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 30, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary \+ Katie went to Po'k,

Still fine weather. On my way to studio I stopped at Walter's \+ had him come to 36 S. with me \+ look at the excavation \- Walter has been found at the bottom of the foundation wall \- drainage \- many plans proposed to control it \- The ass. inspector came along \+ proved to be very amiably tipsy. Again most of my [illegible] was taken up with this matter. I got home to lunch at 12.50 \- Mary \+ Katie had gone to Po'k \- to be gone for a week \- After lunch again to studio \- wasted some good time over bicycle \- finished cleaning it \+ at 5 took it to Borrmaan's to have the tire of hind wheel made tight \- A short rest before dinner \- Suet[?], after some reading aloud to the boys I wrote several notes

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 1, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant n[illegible], overcast \+ saw later in the day \+ clear moonlight at night. B[illegible]. at 9 \- then newspaper for an hour \- then to studio \+ looked after fire \- met Maynard \- at about 1 went out with the boys \+ walked up 5th Ave \- Returning in time for dinner at 2 \- After dinner, feeling very sleepy, I lay down \+ had two hours of solid sleep \- Tea at 6.30 \- at 8 went to studio to look after fire \- back \+ read aloud to the boys until near 9 \- Charl came in then \+ the boys went to bed. I wrote for an hour or more on Ste. Com. Report \- after 11.30 when I got to bed.
