## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 6, 1895

**Weather:** Clear \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \+ cold. After bkfs. went to studio for a few minutes — then to Putnams in 23. S. \+ saw Bishop P. \+ left with him part of Ste. Com. report to set up in type. Then to Lee \+ Lee in Nassau St. \+ saw W. [illegible] We read over together, his opinions \+ after a number of suggestions he promised to rewrite it to form a part of the Ste. Com. report \+ to let me have it to-morrow. Then I went to Geo. B. Read's \+ saw him \+ W. [illegible] for a few minutes, then home to lunch at 1 o'lk. Did a little work on the plate in the afternoon. Evef. Chas. came in \+ talked freely of his business affairs \+ his trouble with Winter. It was 11.30 before he left.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 7, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary, Katie \+ Bessie ret. from Pok.

Pleasant in the main, with some raw, disagreeable streaks — I put in some work on the mt\. on the lee etching — \+ also some bossing of the work in studio's cellar which is not being done to my satisfaction. Lunch at 1 — R. went back with me to studio \+ spent the p. m. there. I had a rest before dinner — At 8.30 went to Century — Monthly meeting — was there until nearly midnight — Walked home with Walter.
