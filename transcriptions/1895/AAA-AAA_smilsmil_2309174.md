## Left Page

### Wed. June 5, 1895

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast \+ much of the time quite raw \+ chilly \- After bkfs\. went with the boys to O'Neill's 5th Ave\+ 42\. S\. \+ bot them some shoes \- Then to Dielmann's \- not in \- then to Allen's \- 47\. S\. \+ he worked at my teeth a little \- Then by Bdwy\. R\.R\. to see \+ see \+ talked N\.a\. money affairs \- Then with Mr\. Wm\. Lee to the Central Trust Co\. \+ had a pleasant interview with Mr\. Hyde \- then to the office of Sweet \+ Co\. in Broad S\. \+ talked of Municipal Bonds \+ such matters\. \- Then I went home getting there in time for lunch \- Then a long Rest as my head has been very troublesome for some days\. Josephine Braunerd \+ Mattie called\. For awhile in the afternoon \- Eveng\. at 8.30 I went to Cantine \+ met Wood, Brown \+ Robbins \- full com\. on finance \- made a Report of what I had learned while down town \+ after discussion it was decided to put \$100000 with the Central Trust Co\. \+ the Rest into Gov't 4s \- 1925 \- adjourned at 10 \- There had been a heavy thunder shower \+ as the Rain held up I started for home but before I got there the Rain came down suddenly \+ I got somewhat wet

To day the contractor commenced pulling down the building 2 Griffen court\.

## Right Page

### Thur. June 6, 1895

**Weather:** Delicious day \- brilliant \+ cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delicious day \- brilliant \+ cool\. After bkfs\. I went to N\.a\. hoping to see Farrington \- gone to Phil\. Then to Wall S\. 54 \- Central Trust \- \+ at 10.30, by appt\. met T\.W\. Wood \- With Mr\. Hyde 2\. V\.P\. we soon made arrangements to deposit \$100000 of N\.a\. funds \- then to Harvey Fisk \+ Sons \+ made agreement to purchase \$240000 W\.S\. Govt\. 4s \- 1925 \- Then we rode up to 42\. S\. \- Lincoln Safe Deposit Co\. \+ there we hired a box to put the N\.a\. bonds in\. Then we parted \+ I got home before 1\. Kate went to Pok\. on the Durell, leaving the house soon after 2\. I took my usual nap then out with the boys \- went to N\.a\. to write a note for Farrington \- Then to Walter Cook's Bdwy\. \+ 18\. S\. where I signed contract with builder to put up Studio in 36\. S\. Then we went to Best's \+ got Ralph a pair of trousers \+ Jim a hat \- Then to another Store \+ got Jim a dark lantern \- This being his 12\. birth day \- We went in to Mad\. Sq\. garden for awhile \- it is now a Bicycle Academy \- home at 6.45 dinner \- Jim, Ralph \+ I sat down alone \- the first time since Annie was taken from us \- Eveng\. Dielmann came in for awhile \+ \[illegible\] business\. Then Chas\. came in \- Then Alice Ireland \+ Roger Adams \- Chas\. stayed until after 10 \- I got to bed after 11\.
