## Left Page

### Sun. March 17, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, but I was not in condition to enjoy it, a sore spot under the belt making me unhappy. I took a very short walk with Ralph about the middle of the day — Jim is still visiting at Walter's — I wish he were at home — Katie T. unable to get up — fever, pain \+ sore throat. Kate went for S. Clair Smith who says it is Tonsillitis. Dinner at 2. It was after 4 when I went to Union League Club to see the pictures and bas-reliefs — met many acquaintances \- had a confab with Clarence Cook and a long talk of N.A. matters with Howard Russell Butler whose proposition is before the Site Com — It was after 6 when I got home, tea a little later — Evg. Charl came in — we spent some time looking over a lot of photos that he took while abroad

During the night I dreamed of Annie for the first time since her death. Parts of the dream were very vague — other parts were clear — She was restored to me, I could not tell how, but we sat upon a lounge together — she was pleased — impassive — did not say a word. I gathered her to me, \+ embraced her in an agony of joy, crying out "it is a miracle". The intensity of the emotion awoke me \+ once more I felt the pain of living alone.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 18, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day — after bfst. took R. to 36th S. then home \+ spent the mfg. working over accts. \+ writing two or three notes. At 12 went out for some fresh air \+ exercise — went to Century \+ spent a little while among books. Home to lunch at 1 — at 2 took R. to Mr. Conklin's to go with Kennedy to see S. Patrick's Parade \- at 4 I went to 52d S. \+ saw J. I. A. — and we talked for nearly an hour of N.A. \+ \+ matters. On my way home I stopped at Jph. Morse's. \+ saw him \+ Mr. M. \+ talked of Jim's schooling — Home at 6.30. dinner. Katie T. still in bed but better. Evg. to Council meeting — late in the evg. the Council were sec'd. in the galleries by two members of the Hanging Com. Will H. Lno \+ Geo. Nues jun. who handed over the Spring Welm. After 11 when I got home.
