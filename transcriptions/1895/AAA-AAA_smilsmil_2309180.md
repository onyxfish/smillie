## Left Page

### Mon. June 17, 1895

**Weather:** Still clear & warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still clear & warm - my time fully occupied about the place. Mary had a scrubber & followed her about & cleared out corners & made things shine generally. I went down town late in the aftf. Some time given to Titman who is dickering for the double harness that was used on Sant Selim - I am willing to exchange it for furniture -
I worked until late in the evef transplanting lettuce, of which Stone has got enough under way to supply the town - To bed early -

## Right Page

### Tues. June 18, 1895

**Weather:** Clear & hot  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear & hot - The day passed without incident - working about, out of doors & in-doors - Sometime in the garden - then putting something to rights in Studio or Stabling room - In the twilight I transplanted a lot of lettuce - The evef being nevertheless hugely warm we all got to bed early.
