## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 8, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Thick & foggy & very mild with some light rain in the afternoon. To studio at 9. m gas men at work there — They did not get thro' until 4.30 p. m — The meter was set in the afternoon & the gas lighted in the ev'g. I worked about the place until after 12 — home at 12. 30 — lunch at 1 — back at 2 & then set to work to do some rough carpentering — I covered the hole cut in the floor for gas & heater men & moved my heavy printing table over it. This I gave a hard rubbing & made it look like a noble piece of furniture — I pulled my big screen into place & for the first time my studio began to assume an appearance of proportion & possibilities. I went home before 6 very tired & had a rest before dinner — Read the latest magazines — uncomfortable warm & close in the house. Katie T— quite used up with her cold —

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 9, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very disagreeable day, improving somewhat towards night — Rain threatened but it didn't come. Very warm — Ther. above 70°. out of doors. To studio at 9 — soon went to Union Transfer Co. in 21st S. to arrange to have my Standing Press bought up to studio — then to N. A. — then to Calverly's & saw the bust that he has just started of Annie — already a suggestive head. We arranged the pose of the head — then to Clark the plumber — then to Locke, the Upholsterer, then to studio — Man came to arrange for hoisting in at the big window my standing press — Maynard not in so arrangements could not be completed — m — came in soon after 12 & again I went to the Transfer Co — then home to lunch — Soon after 2 the press was brought — Tackle attached to beam across m's window & the press, weighing very nearly half a ton was hoisted into my big window and the outfit knocked off it in just one hour — then I worked & clean things up — Diggs came to care for furnace & [illegible] gave it up — Locke came to retouch [illegible] bookcase — Home at 5.30 & had a rest before dinner — Ev'g. at 9 o'clk — went to Cutting & got into Academy business with Ward, Nibble, Dielman & Butter & it was 1 o'clk. before I got home.
