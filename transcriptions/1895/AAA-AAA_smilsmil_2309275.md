## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 30, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable day \- light rain in the m't. commencing again at about 3 \+ rising to a gale of wind with floods of rain continuing thro' most of the night \- I was in bed all day with a cyclonic headache. I did not get up until 10.30 pm \+ went to bed again at 2.30 A.m. A very hard day for me.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 31, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A brilliant day, breezy \+ cool. I got up a little late, feeling the effects of yesterdays experience of course. About 10 I went to Saml. Colman's at 2, 39e S. \+ saw him for a few moments. It was about 11 when I got to studio. I was in no condition to work \+ moped about trying to get a little order in the confusion of the place. Home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 \- It was about 4 when I went home \+ to bed \+ had a two hours nap, after which I got up feeling better \- Dinner \- After that went to H.R. Butler's \+ had an hours talk with him the various modifications of his plan, possible, when it is discussed by the N.A.s \- Before 10 we walked down to the Century where I left him \+ went home. Chare there \- It was midnight before I got to bed \- A glorious moonlight night \- the train whistles made a tremendous did at 12 o'clk. Speeding the old year \+ welcoming the new.
