## Left Page

### Tues. March 19, 1895

**Weather:** Charming day, bright \+ breezy  **Temp:**

Got to N.A. fd. at 10 o'clk \- met there Howard R. Butler \- went with him to 5th Ave. Safe Deposit Co. \+ gave him a copy of Act of Legislature granting exemption from taxation to N.A. Then together we went to Chamber S\- where I left him \+ went to the City Hall to get additional Surrogates and a copy of Annie's will authenticated. Then to Tel. \+ Cable Co. \+ got shares of that stock in my name \- Then to Sus. \+ Western to get that stock \- then to Geo. Read's office. By him was sent to Dutcher \+ Dunlster, 58 W. S where I arranged some fire ins. business between the N.A. \+ Met. life. \- Then to the office of McGates in the Mills Bldg. in Broad S\- \+ then by R.R. home to lunch at 1.15 \- at 2 o'clk. Nicoll came and we talked \+ \[illegible\] com. matters \+ decided to call a meeting of the Com. for Friday every \- wrote the notices \- also some other letters

Evef. \- after dinner went to Geo. Cook's \+ spent some time with him going over Annie's book \+ making out a list of Annie's securities for him to have as my co-executor \- It was nearly 11 when I got home \-

In the afternoon Walter came in with rough sketches for my studio building. I was disappointed to find that he had arranged it so symmetrically \- institutional appearance \- it is a very prosaic, three story house \- I knocked some parts of it along by my criticisms \+ suggestions \+ he is to make some radical changes.

## Right Page

### Wed. March 20, 1895

**Weather:** milky sort of day, growing thicker towards night  **Temp:**

Got to Fifth Ave. Safe Deposit Co. at 9.45 \+ met Geo. Cook by appointment \- we spent an hour in going over securities in Annie's box \- Then I went to Scribner's \+ saw Mr. Marvin about the possibility of getting work to do. Met here Mr. Jno. Bigelow \+ had a talk with him about Library consolidation \+ N.A. site \- Then to Am. Art Ass\- \+ saw the caricature sets of pictures \- very poor \- not to my mind \- Then to 37 W. 22? S\- to call on Miss Abbatt \- she was out \- then to Y.M.C.A. office to see Mr. True \- to have him give me a release on my lease. Then to N.A., then to Grand Cent. Station to N.Y. Cent. Treas' office to get transfer to me of Annie's stock. They must see A's will. Home to lunch at 1 \- most of the P.M. engaged straightening out estate accts. of Western Mtgs. Dinner at 6.30 \- Evef. at 8 went to Annual meeting of Water-Color Socy. which was dull \+ uninteresting. Home at 10.30 \-
