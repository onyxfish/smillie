## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 28, 1895

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — got to studio at about 9.30 — got Heumann to do a little carpenter work to fix gas fixture in closet. The night night. So many matters to get into working shape, as this is the first printing that I have done for perhaps three more years, that I was not until now that I pulled my first proof. Polished + slept until after 6 — Tea at 1 — Gerster came = + then [illegible] 6.30 — Evng. Chase came in — \+ much time was consumed — Daylight was almost gone when I pulled my third pl. — not at all satisfactory — but it might have been if I had been undisturbed. After dinner I did not feel equal to going to the Century as I had intended to, but at about 9 I went to Saml. Colmaris — 121 2. 39¢ — spent an hour or more there, then home.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 29, 1895

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast, with a very weak attempt at rain in the mlfg. — Brilliant morning night. I got up with a headache that rendered me nearly useless all day. I went to studio at about 11. \+ to N.A. Looked at Autumn Exbt — met W. Thomson there — Home in time for dinner at 2 — After that I went to [illegible] + slept until after 6 — Tea at 1 — [illegible] \+ then [illegible] 6.30 — Evng. Chase came in — \+ went to Century at about 10, after writing a short letter to Jim — Home at 11.
