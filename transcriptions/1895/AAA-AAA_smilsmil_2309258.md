## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 24, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Wet [illegible] \- P.M. dry but overcast chilly & raw \- after looking over our paper I went to studio & spent some time revising my com. on Site Report. About 1 went to Fred. Ireland's in 2. 40th S. He was not in but I saw his other friend Miss Sturgis (?) & Katie & came home to dinner at 2 \- Charl came in with a great lot of photos. that he brought home with him & we looked over them until after 5 \- Then I walked up to Central Park & back. Swelf. Charl & Geo \- Spent with us.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 25, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very wet & disagreeable \- heavy showers at night. To studio soon after 9 \- Young Dettars called to ask me to act as pall bearer to his father at the funeral at the N.A. to-morrow. I promised that I would if certain possible engagements did not interfere \- Then the Health Dept. Inspector came & some time was given to him \- then Haumann came & put up pale & curtain \- then I wrote a letter to Mr. S.R. Koshler. Home at 1. at 2 I went to Kimmel & Voigt's & had three proofs taken from the Gen. Lee Etching \- So far as I could see (for the day was very dark) the condition of the plate is encouraging. It was about 4 when I got to studio \- stretched two of the portrait proofs. Home at 6 \- Eveg council meeting N.A. \- Beckwith & Blum, the new council men there. Home at about 10 o'clk. Charl there \- to bed at 11.
