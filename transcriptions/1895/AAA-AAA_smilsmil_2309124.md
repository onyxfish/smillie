## Left Page

### Tues. Feb. 19, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day - clear & mild - after 11 when I get to studio, having written several Siti Com. notes before I left home. At studio I continued my work on Montrose plans Nicoll came in for a little while after 12. Home to lunch at 1 15 at 2.30 walked back with Katie T- she going to N.a. school. Most of my afternoon was taken by two callers, J. R. Brevoort \+ Dr. Arpad Gerster. I walked down to 14th S. \+ up Bdway to 34th S-, thru home to dinner at 6.30. Eve'g. I went to Fred. Ireland's \+ had a talk with Fred. \+ Alice concerning Jim \+ his studies. He worries me, he takes his work so seriously that life must be a burden to him. I do not wish to press him too severely. He is not robust \+ his temperament is nervous \+ irritable. He is intelligent, conscientious \+ ambitious - a splendid composition if not over-worked. Home before 9.30. To bed early.

Mary has been under the weather again to-day! a return of her bowel trouble.

## Right Page

### Wed. Feb. 20, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant \+ beautiful day. Had to go to 2d. Nat. Bank - do. it was 11 before I got to studio. I rec'd. in the m'g from lawyer dee letter Testamentary as Executor of Amice's Will. At studio I continued the work of yesterday - plans for Herrick's work at Montrose. Nicoll came in for awhile after 12. Home to lunch at 1 - back at 2.30. Minor came in for a short time. I made drawing for frame of a looking-glass at Montrose. At 5 o'clk. I went to Mr. Jno. Bigelow's 21 Gramercy Park \+ had a talk with him about Mr Tilden Trust \+ N.a. affiliation. Home at 6.15 - Dinner - Eve'g at 8.30 went to Walter's - They have just Ret'd from Buffalo. met there Miss Kent and Ed Mrs Cook - Home before 10 - Charl there with a lot of fine mr. papers.
