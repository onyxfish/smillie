## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 7, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another horrid, dry day — a very pleasant one under ordinary conditions, but at this time when rain is prayed for by everybody, a dry day, no matter how agreeable, is unwelcome — a thunderstorm muttered to the south of us about 10 P. m. but it didn't materialize. I was up, dressed \+ ready for bkfst. at usual times, but Gretta brought my bkfst. to me before I got down. My bowels being troublesome I had to keep quiet during the day. I felt very weak \+ hung about a good deal, giving some attention to the carpenters at work in Jim's room. Dr. Gardener came in for a chat, he said, about noon —

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]
