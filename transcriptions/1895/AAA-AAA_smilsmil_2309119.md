## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 11, 1895

Annie's Will Probated.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day + quite mild. - after my regular m'f duties I got to N.A. of D. soon after 10 - then to studio + worked a short time on Ravello Tower plate - then down town - went first to W. Lee's, then to Geo. Read's + saw him + Van Pelt. Then to the Café Savarin + had lunch - then to the Surrogate's office where I met W. Lee + we went over to the Troy office but had not time to arrange that business - too many ahead of us + we had to be back at the Surrogate's at 2 - then Jel. Cook + other witnesses to prove the will + signatures came but it was after 3 o'clk. before I got through - then I rode to studio + a bit on Ravello Tower - Home about 5.30 - feeling very tired + much depressed - my grief seems now to be more constantly + persistently present than before + my struggle against it less effectual - a realizing sense seems to have taken hold of me as not before. I lay down before dinner + dozed restlessly. Ev'g at 8 o'clk. Jel. came in + together we walked down to N.A. Council meeting. I found it a relief to me but left early getting home about 10 o'clk. - found Charl there entertaining Katie Adams - He left at about 11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 12, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright m'f but it soon clouded over \+ during the day there were some very slight snow flurries + the air, tho' not cold, was disagreeably raw. It was 11 before I got to studio + to work again with acid on Ravello Tower - At 1, went to 2d Nat. Bk. - then home to lunch. It was 3 when I got back to studio - I worked until 5 on plate.

This m'f. on my way to studio I stopped at W. F. P. Church's - 46 E. 30th St. + saw him + arranged with him to make an appointment for me to meet Pres. Seth Low of Columbia College that we may confer upon the subject of a higher art education + possible harmonious or co-operative action between the College + the N.A. in school matters.

Home to dinner at 6.30 — a very quiet ev'g.
