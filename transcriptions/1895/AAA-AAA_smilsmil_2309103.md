## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 10, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A horrible day of wind + rain, kept up continuously from m't to late at night — Clark, the plumber, came with Higgins' man, the roofer, just after b'kft. to confer about getting the rain water away from extension roof — at present it discharges thro' a pipe built into the wall which is defective. We went into Mr. Edwards' to see if we could make an opening into his drain — Got to studio late, of course, + about the m't working on Craeg en Brie, much to its advantage, I think. Home at 1.30 — Annie lunching at the Putnams' in 58th S— I did not return to studio as it was so cold there — I found enough to do at home with t.a. affairs + my own. Dinner before — Charl dined with us + after it, Kate, Katherine + he went to a concert — I remained at home + worked on a letter of instructions to Herrick — To-night is Twelfth Night celebration at the Century, but I thought it more prudent to stay at home.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 11, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable — it might have been worse + could easily have been a great deal better. Spent much of the m't fussing over a small, body-color W.C. sketch — Entrance towers — Cairn brook Castle — commenced at Montrose last fall — also, on a Ravello sketch — Home at 1.30 — Mary Sprague Coat + Miss Kent, of Buffalo, to lunch — got back to studio about 3 — It was after 4 when I went to Mr. Danl. Huntington's — 49 2.20 — saw him about serving on Com. on Stte. He declines finally. Then home — Eve'g Edith Wright McCarthy at dinner — after that she + Annie went to a concert. And I wrote a letter to Herrick — Charl was with K. + K. — Part of eve'g — After 11 I began to get uneasy because Annie did not come in — At 11.30 I started out to look for her — brilliant moonlight: went to Mendelssohn Club Hall — 40th S't near 6th ave — closed — Home a little before 12 — woke Kate who told me that A. + E. had spoken of the possibility of their getting seats at the Opera — with that que to quiet me I went to bed — A. + E. came in soon after having been to the Opera House + seen Don Giovanni.
