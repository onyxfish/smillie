## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very disagreeable m'f., slushy snow until near noon, then the weather cleared delightfully \+ continued pleasant. As usual, near 11 when I got to studio — again working at plans. Home to lunch at 1 (after a call from Benedict, who has charge of N.A. f.S. advertising) — Back at 3 \+ worked on plans, finishing them at 4.30 — Then went to see J. Q. A. Ward. 119 W. 52d S't — out — found him in the riding school, 59d S't \+ 7th Ave. \+ we walked back to his studio in 52d. S't. Talking about N.A. matters. Home to dinner at 6.30. Eve'g given to writing N.A. notices to Com. on Site — also several other matters of business, \+ to bed at 11.

Mr. Sam'l. Colman called upon Mary.

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 22, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ breezy but my throat troubled me so much, my head ached \+ I was so much out of sorts that I did not go out of the house. I wrote letters \+ attended to some business matters — It being holiday, Mr. Valentine \+ bro. Chas. called in the afternoon. Mrs. Brownell called upon Mary —

A quiet eve'g — some reading \+ to bed early —
