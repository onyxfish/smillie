## Left Page

### Fri. April 26, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very warm day — slightly overcast most of the time — I got up feeling much better than usual altho. Temp. is still 3/5° below normal — After 6 t.p. I went to Mrs. Jones's in Mad. Ave. to enquire concerning her boy Gilbert who has a light attack of Scarlet fever — then to butcher & grocer & then to Cycling Hall in 34th St. where the two Katies & Ralph were already at work — This is R's third lesson but he rides off alone as tho' to the manner born — After waiting awhile I went to 20 Nassau St. & to see Mr. Wm. H. Lee abot my own business & that of N.A. [illegible] — Had an hour's session & much complex (to me) figuring concerning N.A. mtg — lease, port payments etc etc — at 11.30 started for N.A. where I spent some time with Farrington over the books — Home to lunch at 1 — found that Mr. Chas. Barney had called at 9.30 — left word that he would be in after 6 this evng — After lunch, being tired, I took a long nap — Then, for some time was puzzling over garden shipperies for Montrose — at 6.15 went to Mr. Barney's but found he was taking mass all — left word that I wd. call in the mrg. Took a short walk — Dinner at 6.30 — She & Fred Ireland came & took K.A. to the Opera — Don Giovanni — Later Alice S. came & took K.T. to the same opera — Gogo & Tom Cook came & took Jim to a Glee Club concert at Carnegie Hall.

## Right Page

### Sat. April 27, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Nasty wet day, rain beginning to fall about 9. A.M. New Yale came bringing his "boy Roy" & left him to play with Ralph — At 9.30 I went over to Mr. Chas. T. Barney's & spent half an hour with him talking over N.A. business matters — Then home & it was near noon before I went to grocers & butchers — I had a sound nap before lunch — a good part of the p.m. I spent in arranging Montrose garden matters which have got into something of a muddle — Mollie Adams down to N.Y. for the day — called, but I did not see her. The two K.'s out to a "Trust" Matinee all the p.m. Dinner at 6.30 — Evey & Charle came in — had a number of Bicycle sundries that he had got for me. He left at [illegible] .30. To bed at 11 —

When I awoke this mrg at 6 — I temp. was 97 7/5 — pulse 60 — I felt very seedy all the mrg but was better after nap. Temp. got to be near normal about the middle of the day — but down to 97 3/5° on going to bed.
