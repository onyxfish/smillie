## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 2, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — got away from the house early enough, but 'twas later than usual before I got to studio — went to Bank \+ safe deposit \+ arranged Annie's a/c for Jany, then to N.A. \+ wrote to Lee \+ Dee \+ to J. 2. A. Ward \+ was interviewed by a Real Estate man — After 11 when I got to studio \+ then Nicoll came in — I worked for a little while upon a charcoal sketch after photo. by Yale. then home to lunch — back at 2.30 \+ finished charcoal sketch. — Home at 6. Bessie T. Ret'd. to Po'k — this m'f. Dinner at 6.30 — Wel'f. went to meeting of Sketching Club, studio of Nicolatz — 13½ × 2.15" — Eight members present — The "stand-bys" now of the Club. After discussion it was decided to try to make an exhib'n at the N.A., with the W. C. Soc'y. Home soon after 11.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 3, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day, grey \+ raw, with a horrible mess under foot, making the vastness above \+ below, complete. Detained at home writing N.A. letters etc, so that it was after 11 when I got to studio. Resumed work upon "Riva, Venice" outline study for Water Color drawing. Karrman called for 3 W.C. \+ to be submitted to Cleveland Com. for their great Inaugural exhib'n — Later in the afternoon two of them were Ret'd. rejected. Alas, alas. I have outlived myself. Home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 \+ worked until 5. Dinner at 6.30 Wel'f. finished the scraps book.
