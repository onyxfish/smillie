## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 13, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant, breezy day growing positively cold in the afternoon. It was about 11 when I went out for a little turn on my wheel to the top of the hill overlooking the lake — then back, feeling that during the past week or ten days I have lost a good deal of strength. — I went down town about noon. Dinner at 1. Then wrote a couple of notes \+ at about 4 started down town. I met Ruan \+ we ret'd. to the house \+ looked over soil for pipes, traps \+ ventilating pipes, much of which I propose to change. I made a slight scale sketch of the opening from Kate's closet onto the roof. Then down town \+ attended to a number of small matters. Walked up the hill with Dact. Sayre but as I did not "warm up" I got onto my bicycle \+ rode for some time about the place. Then tea — while at table M. H. [illegible] Beach \+ his son came \+ made notes \+ measurements for a duplicate of my standing press made for use by Beach — Part of eve'g in studio by a pleasant fire \+ good light.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 14, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Tom Cook. arr'd from Buffalo —

No frost last night \+ a very cool, breezy day, growing stiller \+ colder at night. After 11 the boys went out with pony \+ cart \+ I accompanied them down town \+ thro' darkie-town to the Wyalusing road when we turned about \+ retraced our steps. The pony had more than she could do to keep up with me on my wheel. On my way back had to leave my wheel at Cooley's for repairs. Peter commenced on little window in Kate's closet this m'f \+ made an opening thro' the roof. I made drawing for the outside detail. Spence's wife came \+ with him cleaned out Jim's room \+ set up his furniture so that he \+ Tom Cook can go into it this eve'g. I worked on plans. Tea at 6 — at 6.20 Jim, Ralph \+ I took train for Alvord \+ met Tom who started from Buffalo at 9 his m'f. The 1 o'clk train from N.Y. was 25 m. late, but we ran from A. to N. in 23 m. including the two stops. The boys went up to the house. I went down town to P.O. etc — \+ got my wheel from Cooley's — The boys got to bed before 9 \+ after reading my paper I followed them.
