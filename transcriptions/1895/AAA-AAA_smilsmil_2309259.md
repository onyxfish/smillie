## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 26, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very dark day \- Rain commenced falling in the n't \+ continued in heavy showers all day \+ a good part of the night. Got to studio soon after 9 — Jo. Conklin \+ an assistant came at 9. 30 \+ were a couple of hours at work putting up my printing press. I finished my letter to Mr. Kohler \+ attended to other matters \- Home to lunch at 1 \+ back at 2. Dr. Gerster came in at about 3 \+ stayed for more than an hour. He wishes me to etch the portrait of his wife. Evest at home. Reading aloud to the boys \+ after they went to bed, doing some reading for myself \+ a little writing \-

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 27, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day, following yesterday's storm. Immediately after bft. I went to Health Dpt. in Centre S. \+ had an interview with Chief Inspector Lucas \+ the 36 S. inspector \- the result being that a new inspection is ordered for Friday n't. I think things are getting into good shape \- Then to 32 Howard S. \+ saw Geo. Cook \+ Jno. Valentine \- Then to Fourth Ave. \+ 25th S. \+ saw Jones Tiffany. Saw glass for front doors and he ordered important changes. Then to Dr. Gerster's \+ saw him for a few minutes \- then to grocer's \+ butcher's \+ did my Thanksgiving shopping \+ home to lunch. Back to Studio at 2 — I worked for a little while with crayon on lee proof \- then I wasted a lot of precious time over my bicycle \- taking it all to pieces to clean it \- Evest. after getting R\- to bed I felt heavy \+ bit of sorts \- so I lay down beside him \+ had a very refreshing nap. Charl came later in the evest.
