## Left Page

### Sun. July 7, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day without clouds. R[illegible] before 9 — While the others went to church I picked peas & got other garden stuff.
\+ so the m'f went. — Charl & I had several little exercise spurts, in spite of the heat, on bicycles. Dinner at 2. Getting to feel very heavy, at last I succumbed and had a nap. About 5 o'clk the bicycles came into play again.
\+ within an hour I had considerable exercise. Tea before 7. Eve'g until 8.30 hard at work watering lettuce, sweet peas & pansies — Put R— to bed & then sat on piazza with the others until 11 —

## Right Page

### Mon. July 8, 1895

*Ralph's Birthday*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very hot day tempered by a strong South easterly breeze. — When Ralph awakened in the m'g I gave him the little watch that I had bought for him, much to his delight — Later he had a plane, saw & hammer for the girls & at tea a birth day cake with 8 candles. I went down town in the m'g & spent much of it at Broadhead's blacksmith shop having changes & repairs upon my Bicycle saddle & braces for spark under for Dining Room. Dinner at 1. after that for a time reading — then I had a nap & later a Bicycles ride with Charl when I was able to do bitter work than at any time before. A bath & change & tea before 7 — Eve'g Mr. Daw. Dayre, Priscilla Poor & her daughter & Mr. Kate Parker Riley called — I went down town — back at 8.30 but they had gone. The family sat upon the piazza until 11 — then to bed —
