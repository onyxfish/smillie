## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 14, 1895

**Weather:** Cold & clear  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold & clear — To studio at 9.30 — cleaned a lee plate & laid a strong ground over it then laid a new series of lines over the entire back ground — varnished the back & went home to lunch —
Afternoon, varnished the face part & put it aside — Executed a five year lease with Maynard for the studio he occupies — fussed about curtains & shades & used up the remaining daylight. Home at 6 — dinner —
Eve'g. E.W. Perry came & we talked Academy matters until 9.30 Ge. had come in to see the girls and together, he & Perry went to the Club + I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 15, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day — cloudless & quiet — not more than pleasantly cold — I had a bad night — headache & sore throat, but I got up at 7 so as to get Jim & R. off to New Rochelle to spend the day with Mr. Walton — Bkfs. at 8 +, with Tom Edna Cook we went to Station & I started them off on the 9 o'clk train. Then I went to studio, got fire started & opened windows for ventilation — Then out for a walk — & got as far as north end of [illegible] in Central Park — on my way home met Ge. — & went to his studio with him & after looking over his work, home to dinner at 2 — The boys Ret. after 3, laden with hickory nuts — I had a good nap before tea at 7 — Eve'g, my head being heavy I loafed about & fooled with newspapers.
