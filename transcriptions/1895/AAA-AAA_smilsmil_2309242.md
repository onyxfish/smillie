## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 21, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Two surprises on getting up this mng. The first was to find a half inch of snow, like a white sheet, over everything out of doors — the other was the burning out of our big chimney at about 7 o'clk. — There was a mighty roar \+ a great volume of black smoke but no damage was done. Tyler came \+ worked at boxing the two clob hoppers. I had many little things to attend to before going downtown at 10.30 \+ so many other things to attend to that it was 12.30 before I got back. The day cleared to be beautiful, altho cold. After dinner I worked awhile with Tyler patching some places on piazza floor with canvas \+ white lead — then for some time in studio packing things. at 5 went down town, R. with me, there until about 6 — Evef. working over accts — making out checks, etc.

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 22, 1895

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** fell last night to 27°

Unsettled weather — bright mng with strong cold easterly wind — clouding about the middle of the day — very light showers bet. 4 \+ 6 — quiet evef \+ milder. I was about all day, directing Tyler who got Hopper casings finished. The wooden shutters up at Studio's windows \+ water turned off from the cisterns, etc. Warner came \+ did some painting. I did alot of work indoors selecting \+ arranging things in etching room. At about 3.30 R. \+ I went down town \+ attended to various matters there. Evef. for some time in studio arranging letters \+ papers.
