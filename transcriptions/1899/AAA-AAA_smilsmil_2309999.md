## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 13, 1899

By running over two pages instead of one I have got the days of the week out of order. Fine day - very mild - Got to studio som aftn - Jennings had got it insufferably hot - I wrote a note or two \+ then went out - to Wanamaker's to have an error in Receipt corrected. Then to Pach's Photo. estbt. Bway \+ 22d - with order from J. T. White \+ Co - \+ sat for photo - treated with much consideration by operators - then to Secon Natl Bank \+ home before 1 o'clk. After lunch I did little \+ later had a long rest preparatory to the long journey to N.A. [illegible] in the Avel. Regular quarterly meeting. Having agreed to de Forest's proposition that I should not attempt a summarized report I contented myself that they [illegible] Report had been prepared last spring [illegible] \+ had been in the hands of the auditing Com. since then - It was announced that the Report wd be printed \+ sent to the members. There was no opposition but Miller \+ [illegible] Jones made themselves disagreeable [illegible] after the meeting in discussing with Perry audits \+ Reports - implying that my Report if presented would have required explanation that I could not give - Beckwith brought before the meeting the matter of a municipal site for the N.A. \+ It was referred to the council - After 11 I came down with George \+ got to bed after midnight -

## Right Page

### Thursday - 14th

Dined at Edwd Cook's.

A fine day - much cooler - Stg o'clk went to Edw. Cook's by appointment - we walked to 2, 23d St. Ferry, crossing to Grand St. Brooklyn - \+ then walked up to the property on the riverfront between the ferry \+ 1st St. owned by Inland Real Estate Co. \+ occupied by Bal. \+ Ohio R.R. The lease expires next April \+ we went to look over the ground to learn in a general way "how things stood." we tramped along the docks up to where P. \+ R. - N. J. Central \+ R.R. have piers \+ yard room - I got home soon after 12 pretty tired \+ lay down until lunch - after that to studio \+ was called until dark by business callers - Miller came in about work at N.A. - Etching Studio - de Forest came in to arrange about my Audit Report \+ to submit to me, as member of the Finance Com., an offer of 3 doe. property as Security for loan of $2700 - A short walk \+ home at 5 - rested an hour \+ at 7 at Ed. Cook's to meet Mr. \+ Mrs. Donaldson at dinner - Mr. \+ Mrs. Thurston - Mr. \+ Mrs. Nichols - Miss Donaldson (sister of W. D.) \+ a gentleman whose name I did not get, were there - a pleasant company \+ a good dinner. It was 11 before I the last one got away - too late to accept Mr. Avery's invitation to meeting of Grolier Club - after midnight when I got to bed.
