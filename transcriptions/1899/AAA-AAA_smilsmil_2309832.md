## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 6, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very disagreeable day - dark \+ wet - Snow \+ sleet fell last night \+ much rain to-day - I spent the m\[orn\]g at the studio working on Magrath's ex libris plate \+ think I have it finished - Home to lunch at 1.30 - After that it was so dark \+ the rain was falling so heavily \+ I feel so sleepy that I gave up to it for an hour or more - Then I went to the N.A. \+ from there to Keppel's where I saw Allison about Wood, The delinquent purchaser of Fowler's \+ Lippincott's pictures in the last fall Aubin Then Home - dinner. Evey I read to Ralph \+ he read to me Then I wrote to Wm. Warner \+ Apollos Stone in Montrose, Pa.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 7, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold \+ windy - cloudless - After 6\[??\]: went to Charl's for a few minutes \+ saw him on business - Men out with Jim \+ Ralph - first to studio - then to N.A. \+ S. - then to 2d. Nat'l Bank - took them down to Safe deposit meets, showed them bonds etc \+ explained such matters to them - then to "Nat'l" for savings of Merchants Clerks - 15th S\[t\]. \+ 11th Ave. - where Jim deposited $10 to his ct. on the acct. that Amice \+ Susan there for him years ago - By this time it was nearly 12 o'clk - they went home \+ I went to studio \+ worked on my Keene planes meet after 1 o'clk - then home to lunch - back to studio and worked on plans until near 5 - then to Mrs. Yates's - did not see her but was told that Mrs [illegible] was improving - having been moved from the hospsital to his own rooms yesterday. Dinner at 7 - Ivcie Buck \+ Edith Hoet were guests. Charl came in after dinner -
