## Left Page

### Sun. June 11, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasantly cool day \- breeze from south east \+ a thin skim of cloud nearly all day \- Breakfasted with Adams at 9 \- after 10 went to house \+ wrote to the boys \- to Roland Mulford \+ others \+ then went to C's \+ lunched with Adams at 1.30 \- At 3 o'clk. C \- Adams \+ I went out on our bicycles \- up 5th Ave. to 130th St. then to St Nicholas Ave to [illegible] St. \+ then west to the Suburban Club. of which C. is a member \- stopped there for a while then down Boulevard \- Cycle path to 104 St. Boulevard to 58th \+ 5th Ave \- home at 6 o'clk \- 17 miles \- I went at once to 110 \- lay down on my bed \- had a short nap \- Then changed after a rub down \+ to C's at 7 for dinner \- George came in in the everg.
