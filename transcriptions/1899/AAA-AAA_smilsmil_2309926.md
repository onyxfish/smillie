## Left Page

### Sat. July 15, 1899

**Weather:** A lousley m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lousley m'f. — overcast afternoon looking like rain. Rain at night. I worked for some time during the day upon working drawings for the frame \+ casing of the 4×5 foot plate glass that is to fill the opening in the N. E. cor. of the main room in Keene house. There was some general work in preparing Jim for his start for the Nuleford camps. He got away before 3 on what is called The Frendville stage — a one seat old buckboard. — Evff. a deputation of 2 members of Hose Co. No. 2 called on me for a subscription to clear of Co. debt. I gave them $5.00 — later I went to Dan. Sayre's where there was a meeting of Executive Com. \+ Trustees of Village Improvement Co. under direction of Edgar Tirrell to dispose of the $1600 in hand from insurance of the burned Village Hall. Mr. \+ Mrs. Dan't Sayre — Mrs. Judge Searl — Mr. Harry Beach — Mr. Henry Tirrell, Edgar Tirrell \+ def were present. The business relating to V. Imp't affairs has been shamefully neglected for the past two or three years \+ has got into a complete snarl — Edgar T— has never taken to unsnarl it \+ make up for what is wanting in the Records — I was made chairman — T. T. Bacty \+ a new Record was made — Things that should have been done were declared done \+ approved and a contract was approved with Adams to Rebuild the burned hall. — Home at 10.

Five weeks since my accident \+ I am still hobbling about on a crutch \+ suffering much discomfort.

## Right Page

### Sun. July 16, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

much rain during last night. a close muggy m't. — cloudy throughout the day with a soft west wind — I had a fine rub \+ bath \+ did not get down to bkf. until 10. The character of the day was not conducive to work — \+ I did it do much. The most serious work of the day was going over my check book \+ bringing it into harmony with The Bank's balance just rec'd. The acct. has run since Nov. 4th. There was one error \+ it took me some time to find it. Evff. some reading to Ralph \+ to bed early.
