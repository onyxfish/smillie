## Left Page

### Sun. June 25, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A close muggy day - light showers in the afternoon - Jennings came at about 8 a.m. \+ assisted me thro' my bath and dressing - then I went downstairs to bkft. I did some work on M.A. cash book \+ had a nap in the mtf. Before 2 I had lunch - sat at front parlor window \+ listened to the music in the church cor. 38th S. \+ Mad Ave. I had two naps in the afternoon \+ wrote a letter to Bessie Throop \+ one to Jim - Dinner at 7 - After it Charl \+ I walked around the block - it was hard work for me \+ I had to stop two or three times. After coming in Charl went off for a walk alone \+ I wrote a letter to Haef. I took it to the mail box myself. Charl came in after 10 \+ we chatted until after 11 \+ then I went to bed \+ found that I was able to get thro' the operation quite alone - The usual disturbances arising from pain - but in all I got a fair amt. of sleep.

## Right Page

### Mon. June 26, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ pleasant altho' very warm at midday - I spent the mtf. in my room writing up the last of the Record of N.A. of D. fiscal year 1898-9 - It was not until after lunch that with Jennings I went to 110 \+ spent an hour \+ a half there getting my things into shape to pack into my trunk \+ brought my trunk down to dining room \+ then I gathered my wardrobe etc \+ he took them down to the dining room - About 11.30 I was very tired \+ decided to stop - on getting to Charl's I found Mr. Monroe waiting for me. I made an appointment for him to meet me \+ de Forest at M.A., 109 \+ 5th on Wed. at 11.30 - Then I wrote to de Forest. Then I had a nap before dinner - While at dinner Uncle W. \+ Aunt A. came in for a short time - just back from Providence where they had been to see Lizzie Smillie - after having been to Plainfield to funeral of Fred Smillie's wife -
