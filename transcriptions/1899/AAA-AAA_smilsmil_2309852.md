## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 15, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ pleasant day \- a fine specimen of a winter day. At 2.30 a.m. I was aroused by severe pain \- epigastric region \- probably gas \- I had to get up \- labored with myself for two hours \+ at last, hugging hot-water bag, got to sleep. much discomfort after I awoke at 7.30 \- was not able to get up until after 9 \- Sat by dining room fire \- Nicoll came in to see me \- about noon went to Dr. Yale's \- to my surprise found that he was out visiting his patients \- left word for him to call on us \- Home to lunch \- afternoon worked on my personal accts until Yale came \- told him that he must find some drug or regimen that will correct my acid habit \- he prescribed a drug new to me \- name forgotten \- but it tastes like soot. Evelf quietly at home \- did not go to the "smoker" at the N.A. but got to bed early \- Mr. Mayon spent two hours with him in the evelf.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 16, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beastly day \- dull \+ Raw in the morning with heavy sleet \+ Rain in the afternoon \+ evelf. I went to studio in the mt \+ washed brushes etc. etc. \- \+ had a short work before lunch at 1.30 \- to studio again \+ the rest of the afternoon I spent in getting out old frames suitable for the etching pfs. that I wish to hang in the etching class studio. I found that I had 23 that I could use \- light movedings that I had had made for pen sketches, etc. \- I cleaned the glasses \+ got some of the pfs. into the frames \- Yale's drug did not prove effective in the afternoon \- so I had to supplement \+ with bi-carbonate of Soda \- Dinner at 6.30 Evelf Reading \- Mr. Mayon again with him until 9.30.
