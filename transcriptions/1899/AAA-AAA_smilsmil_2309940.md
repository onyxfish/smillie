## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 12, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bit etching of Jacobi.

Day opened with fog-like mist clearing to a typical dog-day — intensely hot sunshine, humid, sticky atmosphere — a vail over all the distance — At 5 P.M. a heavy thunderstorm came from the N.W. — heavy rain fell for a time — Showers following until late in the evening. I got to work on Jacobi etching immediately after bkft — Got my acids, hot water, etc — all ready & then began biting — it went slowly — things were unfamiliar. At noon went to P.O. with letters for Jim & Ralph. Continued biting in the afternoon — but timidly & too slow. About 4-30 went down town & got back to the house just before the storm broke. So dark that I could not see to do any thing. Unwell — as usual.

Eight weeks since my misadventure — The pain in my hip joint has nearly gone but I cannot walk with any comfort owing to a disability that I cannot exactly describe or locate — it is somewhere in my hips & both legs seem to be affected — I am always quite stiff after sitting for a few minutes — The pain in my left side remains more definitely but still hurts a little when I inflate my lungs fully.
