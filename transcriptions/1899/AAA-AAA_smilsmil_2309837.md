## Left Page

### Mon. Jan. 16, 1899

**Weather:** Dull day, steady rain beginning  **Temp:** [illegible]

bet. 5 \+ 6 o'clk. p.m. To studio, where I did a little N.A. work on accts. \+ then to N.A., a little more work \- \+ then to 2°. Nat. bank where some time was spent in finally adjusting the difference between the bank \+ academy accts. Home to lunch soon after 1 \- then again to N.A., drew two checks \+ went to 2°. Nat. \+ closed the transaction as agreed this mf with Case, the Cashᵗ. Ret. to studio \- took proof \+ etching for Rev. doris Benson to Exp. office \- then, at about 4.30, home \+ rested until dinner time. Evf at N.A. Council meeting. \- a quiet session \- low tempᵗ. between the Pres. \+ Treas. \- no business of especial importance transacted. Home \+ to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### Tues. Jan. 17, 1899

**Weather:** Bright day  **Temp:** [illegible]

The mf at studio \- a roofer at work on leader \+ roof that is in bad state \- Home to lunch at 1 \- Back \+ worked awhile on Keene house plans \- at 4.30 went to N.A., there until 6 \- home to dinner \- Evf \- Read awhile to Ralph \- then Clark came in \+ we had an interesting time as I explained to the family the plans as far as they have progressed, for the Keene house.
