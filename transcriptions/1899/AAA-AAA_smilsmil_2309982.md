## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 4, 1899

*[Above entry, handwritten notes:] Kate Ret? to Polly / Katharine + Bessie Ret. from New Haven.*

**Weather:** Clear part of the time - bleak, windy + cloudy at other times.

Immediately after bkf. went to Chase's taking with me check to complete the sale of Cent Pacific Scrip. + four. Chase of Union Pacific Pref. Stock - ten shares. Then to studio + had a h[alf] + of hard work printing from Etched Plates by N.A. students (Stralio + Stone) It was nearly 2 o'clk. when I got home to lunch after cleaning up at studio + spreading out and paper + [illegible] to dry - It was after 3 when I walked up to Geo's studio, 60 St. + Madison Ave. I was there for some time - Then home + had a rest before dinner - Eve'g, a little reading to Ralph + then, after 9. went to Century monthly meeting - met a number of friends + had pleasant chats - had quite a long talk with Cutler about Ralph + his studies - It was decided to draft his history for the present. It was after 12 when I got home.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 5, 1899

**Weather:** Bright day, but windy + rather cold.

Had my bath at 7 + an hour in bed after it before bkfst. at 9.30 - When Ralph Ret? from S.S. at 11 we got our wheels ready + at 11.30 we went out - thro' Central Park, up 110th St. to Cycle Path - to Grant's Tomb + back by Boulevard + 72d St. - The Park + 5th Ave. A very pleasant ride of 11 miles in spite of the wind that was at times very strong - Home at 1 - dinner at 2. Loafed until after 5 - then to Walter Cook's + to Elwell's making family visits until 6.30 - then home + had tea - Eve'g some reading to Ralph - some writing + to bed.
