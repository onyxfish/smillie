## Left Page

### Sun. May 14, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary Ret'd from Po'K. at 6.25 p.m.

Brilliant, no painting day - Bkfst. at 9 - Ralph went to S.S. + Ret'd. at 11. Very soon after that we went out on our bicycles - the air was delightfully fresh but the wind was rather strong. We went to Cantrae Park + up to 110th St. - then home, stopping to enjoy the wonderful display of Wistaria in bloom Lilacs etc. - Home at 1 + rested until Dinner at 2. - Late in the afternoon I called at Walter Cook's + saw Louise + then to Ed. W. Cook's + saw him + his daughter Catharine - then walked up to G.C. Station + there, with other members of the family, met Mary as she Ret'd from Po'K - Tea before 7 - Eve'g. I wrote to Jim + took the letter to G.C. Station after 10 - then to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 15, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still clear + cool - Immediately after Bkfst. went to Etching class - only Sterue + Thunder Clona there - I waited for half an hour - then I Told Sterue to pose Thunder C. - go ahead with his drawing - not to wait for the others - Then I went to Lee + Lee in Nassau St. + waited there from 10.30 to 11 - went out to Stock Exchange + was full about until 11.30 - then to L.d. - with Wm. Lee went to Tax Com. Office + saw Tax Ct. Salmon with reference to tax for this year on new site - found him agreeable - but he did not commit himself - so, the matter remains undecided - Then to Comptroller's office to learn the status of the petition of N.A. concerning the claim for taxes on the N.A. property for 98 - were referred to office of Corporation Counsel - Chief clerk Campshell - I stated [illegible] [illegible] there found that the clerk in charge of that matter was not in - made appt. to call to-morrow at 12 well. Home to lunch - About 4 went to N.A. Francis too busy to work with me - I took home with me check books + papers to work there.
Dinner at 6.30 - about 8 went to N.A. Council meeting - all members present also J.G. Brown + B. Forest + [illegible] Turner of the new Council - A good deal of business was harmoniously transacted. Beckwith Read the Deed of Trust prepared by Mr. Jennings in consultation with B- Nicoll + Self. Church Read a plan of study for the school next season - based upon a plan by Beckwith, improved by Bolton + Frank Jones - a long step in the right direction. It was midnight when I got home.
