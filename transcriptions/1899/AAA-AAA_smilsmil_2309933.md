## Left Page

### Sat. July 29, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A peculiar day — a few drops \+ rain bet. 7 \+ 8 A.m., cloudy \+ humid — Between 2 \+ 3 o'clk \+ later, Thunderstorm \+ heavy showers — at 6 p.m. — heavy storm passed north of us — very near. Evef. — light rain. Herrick finished his work before 10 \+ I paid him — I worked until 12 with crayon, upon Jacobi portrait — Then to Post Office — Afternoon I continued work on portrait until 5 — then again down town. Evef. sauntering about Piazzas — Reading news papers \+ magazines.

## Right Page

### Sun. July 30, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very heavy rain during the night followed by high wind — Superb day — brilliant — beautiful clouds \+ cool north westerly wind — I got up bet. 7 \+ 8 \+ as the sunshine pee into the bath Room very pleasantly I gave much time to my m[illegible]. exercise \+ bath — then off — then I wandered over the place inspecting — Then wrote letter to Maegnard. At 12.30 I went to P.O. Dinner at 2 — After \+ Read for awhile \+ then feeling pretty well I went off for a walk — over Dunton's hill \+ back by the Road — more than two miles — pretty good for me — Tea at 6.30 — again to P.O. Evef. Reading.
