## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 4, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day — damp \+ raw, but not cold. clouds broke in the afternoon, melting snow — horrible — After bkfs: wrote to Mr. H.Y. Parish — To studio soon after 10 — Some writing there. Then I got my material together \+ commenced the laying out of the plans for the Keene house — Home to lunch at 1 — after that went to Harvey Allen's — W. 49c S. \+ spent an hour in his chair — then walked down to N.A. \+ was with Grounnis for more than an hour selecting passages from Minutes with reference to new site \+ building of N.A. for use of Dee \+ Lee — then walked home — Dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g read aloud to Ralph —

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 5, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull muggy day — every thing wet without rain — snow almost entirely gone — After bkfs: attended to my accts. for a time — then to studio — Maynard came in \+ for some time I worked on his ex libris plate — then for awhile on plans for Keene house — About 2 went to N.A. Dee \+ Lee business — Home to lunch at 1.30 — To studio at 2.30 — more work on Maynard's plate \+ a little work on Keane plans. At 5 went up to George's to see how he was getting on — he was at his studio — then to Mr. Yale's — The Dr. has got so impatient at the Hospital that Dr. Gerster has consented to have him removed to-morrow to his own home. This seems to me a bad sign. Dinner at 6.30 Ev'g read a little to Ralph — wrote a letter to Frank Fowler.
