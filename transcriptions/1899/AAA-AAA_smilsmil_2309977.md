## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 25, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A mild, pleasant day - 10 o'clk. when I got to studio - got out plates by etching class students \+ furnished them up preparatory to taking pfs. from them - one of Stern's plates was badly foul-bitten in patrons of girl's face \+ I stoned \+ scraped it nearly clean. Home to lunch at 1.30 - Dr. Gerster came in \+ went to studio with me \+ stayed, looking over etchings \+ talking about his trip to Europe last summer until near 4 o'clk. Then I went home, changed my clothes \+ went out on my bicycle. It was very lovely in the Park at twilight much like what I have seen in London Parks in Oct - very tender, hazy \+ dreamy. I had a half hours rest before dinner at 9.30 - Eve'g for awhile with Ralph - much puzzled over one of his problems in arithmetic - Charl came in with some bonds for me.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 26, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm \+ hazy - all the m'g spent over my accts. getting Cent. Pacific Readjustment bonds properly recorded in my books - making out checks in payment of bills etc - Before noon went to safe Deposit \+ bank - Then paid several bills \+ got home before 1 - It was near 2 when we had lunch - got a note from Cutter with reference to Ralph \+ the school - stating that Bronson Cutting was to enter the same class with R - next Monday - then a note from Mr. Cutting inviting Ralph to spend Sat. \+ Sunday with Bronson \+ go to school with him Monday. After answering it was after 3 when I got to studio - for two hours I worked over Etching class plates getting old ink out of the lines \+ otherwise preparing them for printing. Home before 5.30 - Read my Eve'g Post before dinner - Eve'g until after 9 with R - his studies - Charl came in for a while.
