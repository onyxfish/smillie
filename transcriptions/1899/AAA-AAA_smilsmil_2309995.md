## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 4, 1899

**Weather:** Rain last night — change in weather — cold \+ windy —  **Temp:** [illegible]

went to Millers in 40° 8': — to studio at 9.30 — laid rebit[ing] ground on a plate by Stene \+ varnished it — laid etching ground on Mr. G — plate, to make contemplated changes in background — Then spent some time rearranging a part of my stock of printing paper — Home to lunch — after it went to see Dr. [illegible] — spent a good deal of time there — about 4 o'clk — went to Millers in 40° St — but he was not in — Then to studio — little daylight left — got out pfs \+ original Lippincatt W.C. Sketch \+ pfs of Plate — Girl's head — preparation to finishing that — Home at 6, — dinner — Evef. Charl came in for awhile — I attended to some business matters \+ got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 5, 1899

**Weather:** Cold, cloudy \+ windy —  **Temp:** [illegible]

went to Schoonmaker's \+ had two [illegible] prescriptions done up — Then to studio — Got water \+ acid hot — rebit the small plate by Stene \+ prepared to etch in new lines in background of Mr. G — but found that I had smoked the ground overmuch — so, I cleared it off, laid another \+ did not smoke it — I also gave some time to the Lippincatt Girl's Head — studying it with proof \+ original — About 12 went to Second Nat'l Bk — Then to Calverly's \+ spent half an hour with him — then home to lunch — to studio at 2.30 — continued the work of the etf until 4.5 then to N.A. — Wx Miller met me there after 5 \+ together we studied over my plans for the Falles \+ iting sink \+ made modifications of my plans that were based upon measurements in the architects drawings that proved to be incorrect. Home at 6.30 — dinner — Evef quiet with newspaper — at 9 went to studio to see to things there —
