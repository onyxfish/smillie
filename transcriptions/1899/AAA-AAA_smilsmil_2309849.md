## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 9, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Exceedingly cold day — Ther. 2°. below zero at 8 A.M., a wind 44 miles an hour. I went to studio before 10 \+ found the Ther. there a little above 50° — too cold to do anything so I ret'd. home \+ spent the m'f writing up back acct. of N.A. Before lunch went to studio to look after the heater — After lunch, feeling very much out of sorts I rolled myself on blankets on my bed \+ lay there until 4 o'clk — went to Carrere \+ Hastings at 4.30 — meeting of Bldg Com. to open bids for temporary School bldg on new site. The Lowest, over $21000 — the architects estimate, at first given, was $17500. These will be submitted to Council at next meeting — Dinner at 6.30 Mr. Mayon spent two hours with Jim in the ev'g. I loafed \+ Ralph read awhile to me.

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 10, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

One of the coldest days on record. Very bright \+ clear with high wind — I did not get out of bed until 6 p.m. — on going to bed last night I took a cathartic dose of Cascara Sagrada Cordial \+ was routed out of bed before 3 o'clk — \+ had 'ell to pay thro' the rest of the night — too much pain \+ weakness to allow me to get up until dinner time, when feeling easier in the epigastric regions \+ dressed \+ went to table — very severe head ache during the ev'g — \+ in fact, all night. Charle \+ Mr. Adams came in for a few minutes in the ev'g.
