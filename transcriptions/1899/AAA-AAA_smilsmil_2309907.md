## Left Page

### Wed. June 7, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still tremendously hot — but a little more breeze is stirring — a sudden change came on at about 8 p.m. when a delightfully cool N.E. breeze blew in \+ the ther. fell out of doors almost to 70°. M\+ after bkf: I went to Studio \+ saw Ryan about Sanitas W.C. — The boys went with me \+ spent some time Rummaging in the cellar — Then home \+ sometime was spent writing to defrost about na matters — payment on new bldg etc — but I got some work done on copying accts. into volume — Lunch at 1 — after it, with Jim went to 2° Nat.l Bk — Then to Arnold \+ Constable \+ bought 3 suits of thin underclothes — Jim went further to continue purchases — I ret'd home \+ worked on my Na writing until after 6 — Then tea — Rudy, Charl \+ Mr. Tho\+ Adams came in. I wrote to Lee \+ Lee \+ got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Thur. June 8, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

No mitigation of the heat which is simply terrific — After bkf\+, before 9 I went to Ryan's about Sanitas W.C. for Montrose \+ learned that none are to be had — then to Miller's \+ conferred with him about plate glass for Keene house — then home \+ worried for some time over check book of N.a. getting the winding up of the three accts into such shape as will be satisfactory to the bank. Then to 2°. Nat.l \+ left checks \+ pass books — Then to Arnold \+ Constable's \+ changed underwear got there yesterday — Home — dinner at 1.30 — after that I worked pretty steadily on copying Na accts until 5 — Then I went to Spalding's, saw Ball \+ got a Hunt bicycle saddle — Tea before 7 — Eve'\+ worked awhile with Ralph packing his trunk — Charl came in \+ Geo\+ \+ Kellie. I met Charl about 6 at his house \+ saw the big Elk's head, just set up in his library — it is too big \+ injures the effect of things generally — to bed early — Ther. in my bed room as I write, 87°.
