## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 22, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day — mild + sunny, altho' for a little time between 10 + 11 A.M. it looked as tho' it might turn showery — enough rain fell to wet the sidewalks — It was near noon when Ralph + I walked up to Dr. Yale's to see how he was getting on — Saw Mrs. Y — who reported favorably — Ralph missed Roy who had just gone out — Then we went to studio + opened windows etc for ventilation — Then home — I wrote a number of notes, wrote checks, etc — Dinner at 2 — after it wrote again — until about 5 when I lay down + had a little nap before tea time at 6.30 — At 7.30 I went to Walter's to learn if he had made some promised enquiries about tutors + schools for Jim — He gave me the name of a Mr. Edmonds for tutor + had some expectation of getting school circulars etc — Then home + read to Ralph until 9. when my throat gave out — Jim had gone as escort with the other members of the family (except Kate) to church.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 23, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day — clear moonlight at night. As I have fallen much in arrears in my correspondence I spent the mth at the studio writing letters. Soon after 12 walked to the Con. Gas Co's office Cor. 4th Ave + 26th St. + made inquiries about gas stove for Etching studio in 22d St. Then to N.A. + home at 1.20 + had lunch. Then to studio + more writing until after 4 — Then home + rested until 6 — dinner at 6.30 — After dinner Mr. Warren A. Mayon called with a note of introduction from Fred Ireland who recommends him as a tutor for Jim — as Jim had gone to bed with a headache + I had to go to N.A. Council meeting I asked him to call again — Then I went to Council where I gave acct. of Court experiences of last week. Adjourned early + home + to bed at 11 o'clk.
