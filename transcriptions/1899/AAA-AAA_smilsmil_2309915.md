## Left Page

### Fri. June 23, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still pleasant weather. Charl says the Ther. went as low as 59° last night. I had a tolerably painful nights rest. Soon after 7 I went to bath room \+ Jennings gave me a full bath, much to my comfort. After the bath I was well rubbed over the sore spots with lotion. Then dressed \+ went to bed where I had my bkfst. \+ stayed until about 10. Manuel came \+ about the mlf \+ analyzed the several accts that completes all he can do for me. I got a note from Perry in the mlf agreeing to accept M. as auditor. Was able at last to arrange at 2° nat. Bk. for the closing of the three accts of expense. Syptr \+ Schools, merging them into one again as they were before. The experiment was a failure. It was near 2 when I had my lunch \+ later when Dr. Allen made his call which, he says, shall be his last. I worked for awhile finishing up my accts. in harmony with Manuel's figures. About 5 I lay down \+ had an hours rest. Evelyn Adams came in from Summit \+ dined with us, leaving early in the eveg. About 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. June 24, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant mlf. but soon grew very hot \+ close. Late in the afternoon Thunderstorms came up. There was a little rain. no commotion. \+ but little lowering of temp. Full bath again \+ then dressed. Bkfst. at my bed. Then I worked on h.a. accts. getting my Cash Book to conform to Manuel's arrangement \+ figures. Charl came home \+ we had lunch at about 2 o'clk Dr. Gerster came in at that time \+ had a cigar with Charles. Manuel did not come to-day. I continued writing in the afternoon. Charl ordered a hansom at 5 that we might have a ride in the Park, but the threatened storm interfered with that. A nap before dinner — down to dinner at 7. Evely I loafed about painfully until 10.30 When Jennings came \+ helped put me to bed. \+ then he left me. I enjoyed the release. Of course I had some trouble thro' the night but not more than was expected.
