## Left Page

### Tues. Aug. 22, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightfully breezy day \+ just uncomfortably hot. When the plumbers came I commenced an investigation \+ was satisfied that a pipe could be extended from the Faulk over my dressing room to the W. C. Faulk in bath room \- so I set Reuan at it \+ by Eve'g it was done \- but there is a leak in the earthen part of the closet, where it was broken 3 years ago or more. I worked on Jacobi \- Taking p\[?\] \+ reworking \- It seems to me that I am nearing the end. Went to P.O. before 6. Eve'g again went to P.O. at 8.
Eve'g \- as usual. To bed with a headache.

## Right Page

### Wed. Aug. 23, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Headache made me uncomfortable all night. Cool, clouded day \- with a fine mist that, late in the day, resembled the rain \- After bkft. wandered about the place on a tour of observation \- Then got to work on Jacobi until 1 o'clk. Then dinner. After dinner I took p\[?\]. \+ worked on the plates. I shall lay it aside now for a time \- Knale, the mason, came \+ looked at my chimnies that need re-topping \- After 5 I went to P.O. \+ then walked past Presbyterian Ch. to Debat st. and so around home. After tea Mr. Beuf. Comstock called \- I escorted her home. Eve'g, coal \+ saw \- had a little fire on hearth \- Read newspaper.
