## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 20, 1899

**Weather:** Clear + cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear + cold - I got to studio at about 10 + to work on Portrait etching of Jacobi. I worked steadily all the m'f, correcting bad drawing on the nose + giving points of emphasis on various parts of face. Then I got nt plate, p.p. + drawing of Mr. Gester. Had a short walk before lunch. After lunch, again to studio + worked on Mr. G— plate with graver until 4 o'clk. Then home + rested until 6 — Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g at 7.45 went to N.A. of D. Amsterdam Ave + 109th St — "House Warming" — N.A's + Ass. — a moderately full gathering. Huntington, Whittridge, Eastman Johnson, T.A. Richards + some other "veterans" were set in a row before the assembly — Dielman made a speech at them + Huntington + Whittridge responded — Then a procession was formed that went thro' the building — They ended their march in the etching room where the display of etchings took some attention — but very few of them really appreciated them. As soon as I could I got Dielman, Bleim + Church together + they examined the exhibits of Storne + Staho + awarded first Prize to Storne — Second to Staho — At 10.30 I started for home — got into car with H.R. Butler + Frank Fowler — Rode to 59th St. with them.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 21, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — milder + quieter than yesterday — After bkf. wrote to Sterne — To studio about 10 + worked all the m'f on Mr. J. plate — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 + worked until 4 — About 5 o'clk. went to lamp store in 4th Ave. near 20th St. with portion of Montrose student lamps to have it fixed — Then to Brooks Bros. and bought suit of ready made clothes — Then walked up 5th Ave. + stopped at Tooth's Gallery + saw a number of "Jungle Sketches" Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g read aloud to Ralph from Stevenson + to bed early — I feel fairly well during the day, but during the eve'g I was extremely uncomfortable — I am getting into a very morbid + low spirited condition.
