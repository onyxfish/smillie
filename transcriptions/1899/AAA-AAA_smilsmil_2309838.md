## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 18, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant, cloudless, cold day \- After bkft. Nicoll came in \+ we had a talk concerning N.A. matters \- after 10 I went up to Yale's \+ saw him for about 20 minutes \- he sits up \+ moves about his room. Then I walked to 2d. Nat'l. Bank \+ deposited the $7683.75 check that Charl brought to me last night, proceeds of sale of N.K.\&T. 2ds. \- then to N.A. \+ home to lunch at 1. \- It was 2.30 when I got to Lee \& Lee's \- Nicoll there \- then we had a long consultation \- the two Lees \- Kingsbury \- Nicoll \+ self, concerning the case to come before the Supreme Court of the State of N.Y. to-morrow. It was near 5 when I got away \- went to N.A., got Central Trust check book \- wrote note to G.H.S. saying that I could not attend Water Color meeting to-night \- then walked to Century \+ saw Maynard \+ arranged with him to be at Court to-morrow to take place of Nicoll \- Then home to dinner \- pretty well tired out \- Lucy \+ Charl came in for a few minutes \- Ralph read a little to me and I got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 19, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Near \+ cold \- after a restless night owing to pain in head \+ sore throat I got up feeling hardly able to get through the day but as I had to do so I did it \- I got to Supreme Court Special Term, Part III, in the new Court house, City Hall Park soon after 10, joined later by Meurs B.F. Lee, Kingsbury, Maynard, Mr. Brainerd \+ bro. Gea \- At 11 we were dismissed until 2 \- I went home at once \+ got a little nap before lunch \- Back to Court at 2 \- in 15 minutes thereafter we were dismissed for the rest of the day \- Maynard, Gea \+ I came up town \- I went to studio \+ worked for an hour on plans of Keene house \- then to Wunderlich's \+ looked at the mezzotints on view there \- then to Keppel's \- met Robt. Blum, Dielman did not come \- we were at K's until 6 o'clk \- with Keppel \+ Allison selecting etching p/p. for the use of students in the etching class \- to be paid for from the Bathgett-Baldwin fund \- I walked up home \+ saw Uncle Willie for a few minutes before he went with Mary \+ Kate to Charl's to dinner \- we had dinner at the usual time \- Katherine \+ Bessie had their french class \+ the boys \+ I had a quiet eve'g \- to bed early \-
