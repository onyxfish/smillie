## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 28, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day \+ milder \- looks like a snow storm. The w\*f at studio \- finished plans for Keene Aunney \+ took them to Carrene \+ Hastings \- then to Dr. Janeway's where I saw his son Theodore \+ made an appointment for Monday m\*f \- then home to lunch. Afternoon at studio \- got out some of my old canvasses out \+ sandpapered them \+ made other preparations for beginning to do some painting.
Evefy at home \-

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 29, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

a fine winter's day \- enjoyed my morning scrub \+ had bkft. at 9.30 \- Before noon Fred Ireland came in \+ we talked of his friend, Mr. Mayou, as a tutor for Jim \- About noon, Jim, Ralph \+ I went out for a walk, stopped at Dr. Yale's \- saw him for a few minutes, \+ Dr. Gerster. Then we continued our walk to the Lake Terrace in Central Park \- then home to dinner at 2. After dinner I had a nap \- Evefy, Read awhile to Ralph \- At 8.30 he \+ I went to Charlie's \- at 9\. Jim \+ the girls came in from Church \+ then J. \+ R. went home to bed. I remained with Chase \+ Adams until near 11 \- then home \+ to bed.
