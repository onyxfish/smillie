## Left Page

### Mon. Sept. 25, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouded day with some warm sunshine \+ a strong so. east breeze. Up at 7.30 — went to Hahnsenfeld's \+ ordered milk, milk, butter eggs \+ other things — Returned home with a pkg of wheaten grits \+ while I was preparing to cook them Jennings came in \+ took the job off my hands. He got up a very nice bkfs. for us — then I wrote — about 11 we went out. Jim \+ I to barber's \+ had hair cut — Then to trunk place \+ bought large bag for Jim — to watch maker \+ left his watch — to umbrella place \+ left his umbrella — Then we walked thro' Bryant Park \+ looked at the Reservoir in process of demolition. At 1 o'clk. Jennings served us a lunch of chops \+ potatoes \+ plenty of fruit — at 2.30 we went out again — Jim went to bank to draw his allowance — R. \+ I walked down 5th Ave. \+ wondered at the Arch — great crowds of people — met Dünn in the throng. he told me of the mental wreck of his wife. We met Jim by appointment at Alexander's shoe store \+ made purchases for him \+ his wife Ralph — (I was thinking of something else as I wrote) then to Rogers \+ Peet's \+ fitted Jim out with winter clothes to take with him to Pomfret — It was 5 o'clk. when we got home — met Sam Plummer who took Jim to dinner with him. Ralph \+ I dined alone at 7, Jennings serving us —

## Right Page

### Tues. Sept. 26, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

muggy, mild, clouded m.f. — wind S.E. a heavy shower at about 10 — clearing after that — wind changed — bright, breezy, cool p.m. Up at 7.30 bkfs. about 8.30 — waited until the shower was over then went to G.C. station \+ got time table for Pomfret. Then to 2d. Nat'l. Bank \+ arranged matters there — my neck on left side is so stiff \+ painful that I am much inconvenienced by it — Lunch at home at 1.30 — after that a nap — then I went to Douglas's — Lex. Ave. to Miller's — E. 40th St. \+ then to Alice Ireland's \+ had a talk with her about Ralph's school — Then to Putnam's \+ bought for Jim a fine copy of Prayer Book \+ Hymnal — Home after 6 — a very good dinner about 7 — At 8.30 went to Jackson's \+ ordered Exps. for to-morrow — Then to Charl's — looked over pfs. from his negatives taken while at Keene Heights — just ace? Home at 9.30 \+ to bed.
