### Sun. April 2, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim \+ Ralph went to New Haven.

A blustering day of cold wind. I got up with a headache \+ kept about until I got the boys off on the 3 o'clk — Train — then I went to bed \+ stayed there until 12 o'clk then up \+ undressed \+ went to bed for the night.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 3, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright but very windy \+ raw — after a very uncomfortable night I got up with a continuation of yesterdays headache. I went to studio after 10 \+ found that Wilson had let the fire go out. The ther. at 52°. I kindled the fire, went to N.A. \+ returning at 12 went to 555. 3. dre — Saw Jennings \+ engaged him to care for Studio fire etc — then home to lunch — After that, my head still troubling me, I rested awhile — Imma came to give Jim his music lesson at 3.30 \+ I talked with her about Jim — to studio at 7\+ — Jennings came \+ I took him to the cellar \+ explained how he is to manage the furnace — \+ arranged that he was to come to the house at 7.45 as he did, \+ I took him to Charles'. I found that Wilson has taken "french leave" of both of us — so we engaged Jennings to do our work — Then I went to Council meeting at N.A. — As Vautine's show is on in the Council Room \+ Library, we had our meeting in the Portrait class room — Home at 11 o'clk.
