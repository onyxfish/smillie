## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 25, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day \- After bkf. went to C.'s \- saw W. Ford \+ drawings for the Keene house \- they are now complete with the exception of near two small details \- when these are attended to, duplicate blue prints will be made, one set of which I hope to send to Branch about the middle of next week \- Then to Studio \- Painted on "a winter Twilight" \+ finished This \+ trace of it \- To lunch \- Then to N.A. \+ to 2\. Nat. Bank \+ found where the error was that has made a difference of 6 c. bet. the acc'o of the Bank \+ the N.A. \- Graham's addition on a deposit slip. Then to 4 W. 22\. S. \+ spent the rest of the P.M. there arranging \+ hanging the framed Etching pfs. for the class \- I hope to be able to get them to work next week \- Then to Studio at about 6 \+ cleaned up \- To dinner at 6.30 \- Mr. Thos. Adams dined with us \- After dinner I went to Ed Coaks to enquire about Tom who is sick with Pneumonia \- not seriously as yet \- Walter \+ Louise there \- general talk about Boarding Schools \+ Jim \- Home \+ Read a St. Nicholas Story to the boys before they went to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 26, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very nasty day \- dull m'f \- sleet beginning to fall at about 11 A.M. and incoming a steady Rain soon after.
Bkf. after 9 o'clk \- about noon I felt so heavy with sleep that I lay down upon my bed \+ slept until near 2 o'clk \- Then dinner \- Afternoon, wrote to Mr. Olmsted, master at Pomfret School for Cat. etc \- also to Wm Speuse \+ Wm Warner at Montrose \+ a note to Cox, Sales man at Brooks Bro's. Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g some reading to the boys \- \+ some more writing.
