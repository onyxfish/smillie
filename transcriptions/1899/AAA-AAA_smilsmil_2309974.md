## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 19, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day \- clear with pleasant breeze \- up early \- after bkfs. went to the carpenter (miller) about letter just Rec'd from Beede about return of part of hardware. Then to studio \- saw Maynard \- in high spirits \- just got com's for two panels for Appellate Court Bldg \- $2500 each \- Then home \- fixed up my bicycle \+ at about 11.30 left the house. Rode up to the Park \+ to the 90th St. gate, the scene of my accident June 17. I have not been on a wheel since until to-day \- was pleased to find that I rode with much ease \+ comfort \- home by 12.30 \+ had a rest. Lunch at 1.30 \- had a nap after that. Then wrote a long letter to Jim \- Hastis to J. B. Caledwell \+ Fred Manuel. Then out \+ had a short walk \- Dinner at 6.30 \- awhile with my Post \- Then with Ralph \+ his lessons until 9.30 \- to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 20, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright breezy day after some dubious weather in the mf. Mrs. Kane came while I was eating my bkfs. As soon as I could I got to work with her taking every thing out of one of my closets preparatory to cleaning it \- Then I wrote one or two notes \- Then to studio where Mrs. K. followed me \+ I superintended her cleaning of closets there \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- again to studio \+ worked with the cleaning woman as long as I could see \- Then out \+ had a short walk \- on my way home passed Charl's house just as he got in from Boston \- stopped for a few minutes. Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g. Charl came in for a few minutes \- Val came to see Ralph.
