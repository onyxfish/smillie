## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 17, 1899

**Weather:** Cloudless, breezy from south \+ very warm \- a smoky haze over all.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up at 7 \+ had a sponge bath \- it was 9 o'clk before we had bkfs. \- soon after that Reede sent for me \- Brauch was with him \- we went over to the Knoll \+ spent much of the m'f going over the buildings \- Brauch seems to be a very intelligent fellow. I wish that he could boss the finish of his work at once but he is now in the midst of a contract putting up a large bl'f in Burlington \- He promises to take hold here again about the middle of Oct. \+ carry the work to a finish at once. To hotel \+ wrote to Charl. \+ to Mary. Dinner at 2.30 \- loafed on piazza until 4.30 \- then went to the White Cottage \+ saw Mr. W. \+ thereon \- Prof. Van Amringe. there. Then wandered about for awhile \+ to hotel after 6 \- Tea after 7 \- lovely, brilliant moonlight \- with the boys, went to the house to study moonlight there \- much pleased with some of the effects there \- then we wandered over Windy Brow \+ back to hotel. Had a long talk with Wm. Elkin \+ Katie about Jim \+ education \- to bed after 10 \-

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 18, 1899

**Weather:** Very warm \- hazy \- some signs of rain in the sky \+ the warm so. wind \-  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up rather early \+ did some writing before bkfs. Went over to the house expecting to find the two carpenters promised me by Brauch \- also one to come with Cox \- but Cox was the only carpenter there \- another man with a horse \+ light sled (jumper) was with the two at work clearing up outside \- much of the stuff was hauled out to the open part of the lot \+ burned \- I worked pretty hard sawing out dead wood, \+ branches that impeded the view, on the pine trees in front of the house. Mid-day meal at 1.30 \- wrote to Brauch \- Got a Telephone call from Callanan plumber at Heaseville \- To house again \- Rosenthal \+ wife came over \+ I took them over the buildings \- back to hotel after 6 \- dinner after 7 \- lovely \- porra time on piazza with the Edkins \- beautiful moonlight night \+ cooler \- to my room soon after 10 \-
