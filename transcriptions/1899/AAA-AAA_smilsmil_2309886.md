## Left Page

### Wed. April 26, 1899

**Weather:** very warm & enervating \- hazy & misty \- after  **Temp:** [illegible]

bkfs\. I went to C.&N. \+ saw M. Brainerd about putting a quick drying cement finish on the interior walls of N.A. school \- then to Miller's \+ arranged to meet him at 3 P.M. \- then to studios \+ wrote on a Report giving a history of the deGrand Benedict business with the N.A. until 12 o'clk \- then to N.A. \+ home to noon soon after 1 \- after lunch I went to studios \+ passed man up on the roof to fix wires connecting with telephone in Ryan's office \- left Ralph with him \+ I went to barbers \+ had hair cut, \+ a shampoo \- then to Miller's \- he went with me to studios \+ got plans of house for Keene to make specifications for hardware \- I again worked on Benedict business until after 5 \- then to N.A. to get further information from the books there \- Home at 6.30 \- every all went to Charks at about 8 \- Ralph \+ I returned home at 9.30

## Right Page

### Thur. April 27, 1899

**Weather:** Brilliant, breezy \+ much cooler \-  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio early \+ spent much of the m'g on the Benedict business, getting as far as I can go in making a history of it from the Academy books \- I must now have his assistance to show the development of the note now held by the N.A. \- The Gen'l Agent of the United Electric Light, Heat \+ Power Co. called \+ so modified the contract he desires the N.A. to make for its new school bldg. that I approved it \- Home after 12 \- packed his summer bicycle suit \+ took it to Adams Exp. in 40th St. \- then home to lunch \- immediately after I went down town \- first to U.S. Trust Co. about Wielifes mtg for N.A. \- then to Lee \+ Lee \- there for more than an hour \- then to 23 Park Row to see Benedict \- not in \- then to N.A. \- there until near 6 \- then home \- dinner. a short ev'g with Ralph \+ to bed early.
