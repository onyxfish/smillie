## Left Page

### Thur. June 15, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An exceedingly hot day - Thunderous atmosphere - heavy shower at 5 p.m. - In spite of the heat last night I had a fairly restful night + enjoyed my bath at 7 o'clk + my bkfst. at 8 - went to house at 9 - Lockwood deForest came at 10 30 + was with me until 12 - I went over N.A. matters as thoroughly as I could - Then to [illegible] Second Nat. Bk. - with him + introduced him to Mr. Pabst. Then to Safe Deposit + together we went over the contents of the NA safe + made a careful inventory - I gave him the keys + he receipted to me - then we parted - I went to Century + got my lunch - Telephoned to Chas. + Mr. Adams about an expert to help me get my NA accts into order - To the house about 2 - Mr. Underhill of McKnight + [illegible] Pavement Co. came - took measurements + will send me an estimate on Mond. or Tues. I wrote a letter to many + then worked awhile on NA Building acct. The storm came on + kept me in doors until near 7 - then to Charls to dinner - Every the air was close + heavy + neither Charl or I cared to avail ourselves of our cards of invitation to the Play given by the "Strollers' Club" at Weber + Fields' - Bdway + 29th St. - I loafed until 10 30 + then to bed

## Right Page

### Fri. June 16, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A superb day - Crisp, cool + breezy - Mr. Adams' son arr. on the Germanic early this m'g, getting to Charles' house at about 8.30 making considerable confusion as A. had rushed to the steamer dock to meet him + had missed him. I got to house about 10 + gave time to the involvements of N.A. accts + under the influence of the weather I made progress. I got up with a severe headache that gradually passed away. To Century at 1 + took a light lunch - Then to house + worked until after 3 - Then out on my bicycle. In spite of strong westerly winds I had a very pleasant ride in the Park. In at 6 - Rested - changed dress + to dinner at 7 - Evg. Mr. Wm. Sturgis came to see Charl. Geo. Cook came + I went with him to 110 to put there some pkgs. of his on storage.
