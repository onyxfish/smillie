## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 6, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day \- brilliant, cloudless \+ cool. I had an unpleasant half hour or so last night \- a sort of dream hysteria \- I thought I was losing my mind \- Up early \+ enjoyed my sunny bath room. I wandered over the place after bkfs \- a tour of inspection \- Then back \+ wrote a letter to Emma Ireland. Dinner at 2 \- Afternoon I walked out past the pumping station \+ on the lake \+ back by depot street \- .
Eve'g. with Sunday Tribune.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 7, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another glorious day \- cool \+ breezy with beautiful clouds. Went down town to Main's to see about the Jacobi photos. had W[illegible] fix my press preparatory to work \+ I trimmed the vines over the etching room windows to give me more light \- I also spent some time in getting my tools that have become corroded \+ otherwise out of condition, back to working order. After dinner, with lawn mower, I mowed the Tennis court \+ found it rather heavy work for me. Then I made my first effort at Tennis playing with Bessie \- I only practiced delivering balls, so, my game leg was not very sorely tried \- went down to P.O. at 5 \- picked black berries until tea time \- Eve'g. all hands went to the concert given at [illegible] in aid of Self fund for Baptist Ch. There was a full house \+ the entertainment lasted until 10.30 \-
