## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 27, 1899

**Weather:** Dull, disagreeable  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, disagreeable day - clearing at night. After bkft. went to Tax office + dwore off the Commissioners guess at my Personal property — Then to Am. Art. Ass'n. \+ saw the Bewest pictures - then to N.A. \+ to studio at about noon - Set Palette \+ did a little work on winter twilight before going to lunch - after lunch, worked at painting until after 4 - Then to Carrère \+ Hastings - meeting of Building Com. The bids for Temporary School Bldg. were finally \+ formally passed upon - award made + the work ordered started at once - then home \+ had a half hours rest before dinner. Evg. - Mr. Mayon came + spent two hours with Jim - I went to N.A. - Council meeting - Informal discussion as to the best plan to secure the permanent setting apart of a certain percentage of all sums given to the N.A. as Endowment - led by Carroll Beckwith. Home at 11.

Mr. Mayon gave Jim two hours.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 28, 1899

**Weather:** Bright, pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant mg. The afternoon raw windy + clouded - a taste of March. To Studio at 10, painted on Winter twilight until 1 - Then had a short walk before lunch - again to studio + painted for about an hour. Young Stahr, a student at the N.A. \+ a member of my etching class came to show me some of the work that he did during last summer. I arranged to meet him at the 22d S. Studio - Then went to Brooks Bro's - \+ from there to 22d S. \+ outlet near 6 - Stahr \+ I were picking over a lot of rubbish that had been sent from the N.A. \+ throwing on what was of no use - Then home - Dinner at 6.30 - Evg. read a little while to the boys \+ to bed early.
