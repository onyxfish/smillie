## Left Page

### Tues. Feb. 21, 1899

**Weather:** mild, filmy + foggy sort of day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After bkfst. I went to Carrere + Hastings + spent some time with Messrs Carrere, Brainerd + Ford — about N.A. affairs + the Fleene house. Then to studio — awhile fixing etching pls in frames — Then to N.A. — again, found so much work that it was 2 before I got home to lunch. To studio at 3 — finished framing + etching pls. at 4.30 Then went to 5th Ave. Galleries + saw the W. J. Havemeyer pictures — There to studio 4 W. 22nd Then to Brook's bro's + bought a suit of clothes — Then home to dinner — Eve'g. Reading to the boys.

## Right Page

### Wed. Feb. 22, 1899

**Weather:** dull m'f. clearing to a very mild + spring-like mid-day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Thickening up again towards night + looking like Rain — I went to studio + spent the day clearing up, mending frames + such like work — Before lunch sauntered down to Madison Park, Then up 5th Ave. + stopped in at Fifth Ave. Galleries + took a look at the Havemeyer pictures — my painting does not look as well as I had hoped — Lunch — Then to studio — p.m. spent as the m'g. was. At 5 walked up 5th Ave. to Central Park but rode home as my legs complained. Dinner — a quiet eve'g. Reading to the boys.
