## Left Page

### Sun. July 9, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A September day — Heavy showers last night \+ early this m'f. Strong westerly wind \+ light, flying showers. Ther. about 64°. About 4 A.m. my leg \+ side hurt me so that I could not sleep — I got up \+ taking my hair belt I rubbed myself from neck to heels as severely as in my sore condition I could — then to bed \+ had a good nap. I had another rub \+ a bath about 9 o'clk. \+ then blf. It was comforting to have a fine on the [illegible]. The day was spent loafing about — some reading — some writing \+ some sleeping — During the afternoon the light showers were very frequent — At sunset it cleared finely \+ the ev'f was almost cold.

## Right Page

### Mon. July 10, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A glorious day — cool m'f. growing milder — I got up quite refreshed after the least uncomfortable night since my accident — I wrote a number of business letters — to Case of 2° Nat'l Bank — to Orlando Beede, sending him a check for $1250.00, \+ to others. About 11.30 Ralph harnessed Trixy \+ took me down to the P.O. Rather an uncomfortable ride — while R— was in the P.O. Trixy got uneasy, turned and bolted uphill \+ gave my lame side \+ leg something of a hoist — a broken shaft hurt her side — we got home. I had a long nap after dinner — wrote to Chase \+ to Jennings — Ev'f with newspapers \+ to bed at about 10.30
