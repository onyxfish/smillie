## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 19, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant m'f. but it soon clouded over. Between 1 \+ 2 o'clk there was thunder \+ a heavy shower — this was followed by showers during the afternoon \+ steady rain at night. When I went to the house after 6hfs. I found a second carpenter at work — one of Branch's men — I was with them (the two) most of the m'f. superintending the hanging of the outside doors — It was after 4 when, between showers, I went to the house — there until 6 o'clk trying to distribute the hardware properly.
Dinner at 7 — much of the ev'f. in my room looking over newspapers.

## Right Page

### Wed. Sept. 20, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain in the m'f — sunshine came out very warm during the middle of the day — there were some beautiful cloud effects. Heavy rain in the afternoon — low clouds at night. Bkfs. soon after 8 — went to house after 9 — with Cox \+ [illegible] while they were worrying over the thin doors \+ the thick locks. Callanan, the plumber came in the n'f \+ we went over the ground together \+ I explained what I wanted — Then the Elkins came — It was near 2 o'clk when we had lunch — again to house at 3 — there until 6 — Callanan came \+ told me that he had made his calculations \+ he proposed to put in what I wanted — 2 bath tubs, 4 W.C.s — 2 wash basins — a cooking range — water back \+ boiler — laundry stove — soil pipes \+ cesspool, etc, etc — for $720.
Dinner at 7 — ev'f. with the Elkins in parlor.
