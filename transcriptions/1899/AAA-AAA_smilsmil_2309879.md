## Left Page

### Mon. April 10, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day. Worked most of the day at Studio on zinc plate portrait of self — Printing or then working — Time wasted — I know — After lunch Yale came in \+ made appointment to come on Wed? p.m. to sit for me to make a sketch of his head in crayon \+ then a sketch on a plates — Before I went to Studio in m'g I packed Jim's dressing case with his things \+ took it to Adams' Exp. at 42d. St \+ sent it to him — I did not get washed up \+ home to dinner until 6.30 — Eve'g went to Council Meeting, N.A. — Home after 11 — Beckwith wanted to know who is to be our next Treas'n. There was no response — nor any evidence of interest.

## Right Page

### Tues. April 11, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Lovely day — Got up with a beast of a headache that stuck to me all day \+ finally sent me to bed at night with misery until morning — To Studio at 10.30 — Maurice Sterne there with two newly etched plates — very promising — I told him to leave them \+ I would take prints from them — I went to N.A. at 11 \+ was there until 1 at work on Check book — Lunch — Then to Mr. Fred. Jennings — Mills Bldg. 15 Broad \+ spent half an hour with him talking of a Trust for the N.A. — He made another appointment for Thurs. at 2.30. Then to Lee \+ Lee's — then walked to Kimmel \+ Voegts — Canal S' — got some Boiled oil for printing — then to N.A. — more work — then to studio \+ wet down paper for printing to-morrow — \+ home at 6.30 very tired \+ head aching badly — dinner — Eve'g Ralph read to me for a little while \+ then to bed before 10 — a hard night.
