## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 10, 1899

**Weather:** overcast + rather raw  **Temp:** Bkf's at 9.30

My eyes are troubling me very much. considerable inflamation + discomfort. About 11 Ralph + I went to G.C. Station + I set my watch - then to studio where I made paste - then home - worked over the files of Punch back to '92, verifying them preparatory to pasting them up to wait until the day of binding comes. Dinner at 2 - Afternoon I pasted wrappers about the Punches + marked the pkgs + 2 came in + then Charl. Tea at 6.30 - Self, Ralph + I worked at arranging back Nos of Harpers + Century mag. It was after 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 11, 1899

**Weather:** muggy + damp - a little sprinkling + some clear sunshine  **Temp:** At 9 o'clk

Muggy + damp - a little sprinkling + some clear sunshine - At 9 o'clk. I got to the office of Dr. Noyes + spent an hour with him + Dr. Hunter. My eyes were being examined nearly all that time for change of glasses. Then to studio + worked until 1 in head mus. g. Lunch at home - then went to Meyrovitz - 123 W. 42d St + left Noyes' formula + an order for new glasses. Then to N.A., 109 St. Grannis not in - but I got cash back with my accts. for last year, so that I may make up my complete report for the fiscal year ending June 1st. Then to studio + home at 5.30. Mable Cooley came to stay overnight on a visit to the girls - Her brother, a theological student, dined with us - Evening I pasted in pkg. back vols. of Harper's Round Table, + St. Nicholas -
To bed at 11.
