## Left Page

### Mon. June 5, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph came home from Oakdale

An infernally hot day - at 5 A.m. my headache was very severe but by breakfast time it was much better \+ I managed to get through a good amt of work - Went to studio \+ then to 2° Nat'l. Bank - Then to Brooks Bros. \+ bought a suit of clothes - then to Met. Life, \+ saw Mr. Eckert, Supt. of Bond \+ Mtg Dept. He went with me to the N.A. Taking Kellogg, Supt of Bldg with him - we went all over the bldg \+ considered in a very general way the possibility of getting it into some shape that will give promise of some revenue - I left them \+ went to studio \+ tackled the problem of getting my check book into harmony with the Bank's statement \+ then merging the Expenses Exptin \+ School Accts. into one. Home to lunch soon after 1 - Back to studio \+ worked until 6 then home - Ralph had come in a little before from his visit to the Cuttings at Oakdale, looking very brown \+ healthy - Tea at about 7 - Eve'g, loafed about trying to find a breath of air - I read a short story to Ralph.

## Right Page

### Tues. June 6, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** Official 97°

Katharine \+ Bessie went to Montrose.

Hotter than yesterday - After bkft. went to Walter's to enquire about Saintas W.C, Had a talk with him about the management of the Ireland Real Estate Co. Then home \+ worked on copying NA accts. into large vol. until 11 - Dockwood deforest was to come to the house before 11 \+ I was to meet Dielwan at 2° Nat'l. Bank at 11 - def. did not come - then I went to bank \+ waited until 11.30 \+ D - did not come - then rode down to Bowry \+ white \+ to P. L. \+ Co. Co. office \+ got car seats for many \+ boys next friday - then home - just in time to bid K. \+ B. good bye - they go to Montrose to prepare house for as \+ will stay at Chas. Sayre's - d. def. prest came at 11.30 \+ left card with message - Dinner at 1 - Afternoon I worked on copying accts. until after 6 - Dielwan came in later to explain just he got to the bank just after I left - Tea before 7 in Eve'g lounged about suffering very much from the enervating heat - wrote a note to deforest - To bed early.
