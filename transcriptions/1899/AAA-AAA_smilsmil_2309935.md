## Left Page

### Wed. Aug. 2, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Singular weather - cloud \+ sunshine until late in the afternoon when the day ended with a furious thunderstorm \- At 10 o'clk. went down town \- first to Maile's Photo\-gallery where I spent some time getting a photo. enlargement of the Jacobi photo. Then to Dr. Smith's \+ was in his chair until near 12 \- had two fillings \- put in teeth. Men home \+ had a short rest before dinner \- After that, tinkering until after 4\- Then down town again \- back just before the thunderstorm got to us. Had the hot air pumps in the cellar going \- sending water from house cisterns up to barn tanks \- Evef. \- as usual.

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 3, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A muggy day \- overcast in the m'g. About 10 went to photographers \+ got enlargement of Jacobi photo. Wrote to Katie Elkin \- to Jim \- to Jennings \- etc. Dinner \- more writing \- running hot air pumps \- picking black berries etc etc \- at 6 o'clk. went down town \- Evef. Miss Mc Intosh was with the girls until after 9 \- Then, the girls, a chinese lantern \+ I escorted her to her lodgings at Chas. Sargres.
