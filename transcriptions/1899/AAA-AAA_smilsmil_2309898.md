## Left Page

### Sat. May 20, 1899

**Weather:** Cloudy, breezy day, clearing beautifully late in the afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

Uncle W. \+ aunt a. went to Phila.

After 6h\[p\]. I went to Ed\[w\]. Cook's to see Tom. To get Seney Plummer's address — Saw his mother instead — then to Waeters — he went with me to my house to advise about new pavement in front of it — Then I worked for awhile \+ N.A. acct\[.\] after 11 went to Canère \+ H — about wall finish in new school bl\[d\]g \+ N.A. — then to 2\[d\] Nat\[l\] Bk \+ made loan of $500 to pay Reeve bills it. Then to Am. Art Am\[.\] to see collection of Jap. dwarf trees — Home to lunch at \[1\]. 20 Uncle W. \+ aunt A. left immediately after — I worked on N.A. accts until after 4 — Then I went out on my bicycle \+ altho' it was quite windy I had a very enjoyable ride thro' the Park \+ got home at 6.30 — The family dined while I rested — it was nearly 7.30 when I took my dinner — Then Eve\[n\]. Post for an hour or more \+ to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 21, 1899

**Weather:** A fresh, pleasant day of sunshine \+ shadow  **Temp:** [illegible]

After a restful night up at 7 feeling unusually well. had my rub \+ bath \+ a rest before 8h\[p\]. at 9 o'clk. After 6h\[p\]., newspaper — then wrote to Bielman — then to J. H. Mayhee — then out \+ had a little walk — went to Century — met Ju\[.\] Cauldwell \+ had a talk with him about the Paris Exposition. He asked for my assistance in the Dep\[t\]. of Etchings. Dinner at 2 — After that went to Etching class where Sterne, Stahr \+ Oliusky were completing their drawing on the plate from the indian, Thunder Cloud. There until 4.30 — Then to Studio — home at 6 — George there. Tea at 6.30 — Eve\[n\]. wrote a long letter to him \+ mailed it at G. C. Station.
