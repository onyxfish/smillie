## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 20, 1899

**Weather:** A very fine day — bright, quiet \+ cool.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio about 9.30 — for a while worked with scraper \+ crayon on pl. of "Mr. Q" trying to get the expression that Dr. Q. wishes — Then I wrote a letter to Scott Brown at Keene Heights \+ some short notes — At 12 went to Con. Gas Co — then to Tach's \+ ordered print for J. T. White \& Co. from one of the negatives taken the other day — then to 2d Nat. \+ got another loan — #800 — then walked up 5th Ave. \+ stopped at some of the picture places — Home at 1.30 lunch — then to studio — scraped out two or three places on face of head — \+ repaired with graver — worked as long as I could see — then walked up 5th Ave. to 58th St. \+ then home —
Dinner at 6.30 — every I wrote to Callanan Bro. sending check for $600 — also to Spencer \+ sent him a check — also to C. Dana Gibson com. n admissions, Strollers Club, declining an invitation to become a member — Charle came in — also Frank Bauity.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 21, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day — To studio at 9.30 \+ worked all the m'g making changes on the Mr. Q — head — I took a couple of prints at noon — having to mix new ink \+ with clean washed printing rags the prints were dismal failures — This place is becoming a nightmare to me \+ if I do not soon get it off my hands it will drive me wild — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Jim drags about, limp \+ coughing, in a most discouraging way — he was not able to go with Ralphe \+ me to the new Appellate Court Bldg — Mad. Ave. \+ 25th St. We got in on Maynard's invitation — found him up on a scaffold at work. The Court Room \+ lobby are exceedingly rich \+ the mural decorations by Blashfield, Scimmonds Walker, Cox, Reid, Turner \+ others are effective \+ harmonious with the surroundings — Back to studio after 3 — \+ experimented, grinding more ink, new combinations etc \+ took 2 or 3 more prints getting better but not entirely successful results — washed up — Rested until 6 — Home to dinner — Quiet eve \+ to bed early —
