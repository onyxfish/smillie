## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 8, 1899

**Weather:** Superbly clear — cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkfs at 9 — read news paper awhile — then went to Walter Cook's \+ spent an hour or more with him, Louise \+ Sally — then to Dr. [illegible]'s — saw only the servant who said that the Dr. was improving nicely — then home \+ before dinner wrote to W. Scott Brown \+ sent him a check in payment of taxes on Keene plot. Dinner at 2 — after that a short nap — then I walked down to Emma Ireland's 2, 17th St. \+ spent an hour or more with her. Then home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g read awhile to Ralph — then wrote a note to Mollie Adams.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 9, 1899

Mary went to Po'keepsie.

**Weather:** Milder \+ semi-overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio \+ worked for awhile on Keene house plans — much puzzled as to where to put stairs to reach second story. About noon went to Higgins' \+ arranged with him to come to studio this p.m. to look at my roof \+ at the leak that is wetting all of the N.E. corner wall of my studio. Then to N.A. where I spent some time worrying over the word suit from 2d Nat. Bk — that one of the three N.A. accts. there is overdrawn — there is a tangle but not an error — I went to Bank but was not able to reconcile the two methods — the Bank's online — Home to lunch — then to studio — Higgins came \+ I went on the roof with him finding many things that needed repairing — I did some writing — Home about 5 \+ rested until dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g to Council meeting — things ran smoothly until Wood commenced his lines for enlightenment as to my new \+ complicated way of keeping N.A. accts — obliging him to sign three checks in paying janitor's salary. He showed a dense stupidity that to me was enraging — his cry being against a waste of revenue charges \+ a deficit of $400 a year — I explained that the waste he complained of amounted to just one dollar a year \+ that the deficit was not $400 a year — But he would not be pacified and the wrangle went on for ~~a year~~ an hour — I was disgusted \+ went home mad clear through — I got permission from the Council to spend $125 for studio rent \+ for moving press etc. for Etching Class — To bed at 11.
