## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 27, 1899

Ralph went to the Cuttings.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Muggy, enervating day — overcast with now \+ then some cabbey sunshine — To studio before 10 \+ spent the m'f cleaning up a lot of Etching class plates — most of them had been left filled in with Printer's ink which had hardened so completely that I had to clean the plates with a brass scratch brush in hot lye. Home at 1.30 \+ had lunch. After that got Ralph ready for his visit to Bronson Cutting until Monday m'g. At 3.45 we went to foot of E. 34th St. \+ crossed to Long Island City \+ in the station there waited for W. W'ms Bay and Cutting who came at 4.30 \+ at once went on taking Ralph — I walked back to studio — then home \+ read my Even'g Post before dinner at 6.30. Even'g. Mary \+ I went to Charle's where we looked at photos of Keene house \+ surroundings taken last Sep'. \+ listened to some orchestral music — Home at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 28, 1899

Katharine \+ Bessie went to New Haven.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very mild day, grey \+ at times heavily overcast. Lockwood de Forest called soon after \+ bringing with him bills of See \+ See ag't. N.A. f'd — covering business as far back as '96. He asks me to audit them. It was after 10 when I got to studio — until 1.30 I worked over old plates of etching students — boiling them clean in strong lye and scraping \+ burnishing portions of surface where the surface was scratched or foul bitten — many of these etchings are made on the backs of plates that have been already used. Lunch before 2. Immediately after it Kath'e \+ Bessie went to New Haven on invitation of 'gene Atwater — after waiting awhile I got away on my wheel \+ made the circuit of Central Park in a manner very satisfactory to myself. in that I found it all comparatively easy work \+ I got home soon after 5 o'clk. without feeling at all tired — I had a call from Walter Cook as I got in — after that an hours Rest — Then Mary \+ I had our dinner alone. Even'g — Reading \+ to bed at 10 —
