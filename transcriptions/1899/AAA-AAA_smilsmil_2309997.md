## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 8, 1899

Jane Düring's funeral.
Kate Ret'd. to Po'k.

**Weather:** Bright, pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant day — After bkft. to studio \+ wrote a long letter to him also one to W\+ Russell Mray \% U.S. Senate — Washington, D.C. in answer to one from him on the subject of Etching — \+ I wrote a long letter to W.H. Warner outlining a plan for the fitting up of the village Hall at Montrose for entertainments. At 12 o'clk I went home as it was arranged That we should lunch at that time — but I could not eat — At 1 o'clk. went to Düring's — 123 W. 41 St. The funeral service opened anew the unhealed wound of five years ago. My heart was full of sympathetic pity for poor Düring. After the service I came home at about 2.15 had some lunch. Kate went to Po'k at 3.30 — I went to studio \+ continued my work until 4.30. Then I walked up to Beckwith's \+ had a talk with him about Academy matters. While there a Sun Reporter came in \+ had an interview with him on N.A. affairs — Home at 6.30 — Uncle Willie \+ Aunt Agnes came in for awhile — also Geo. \+ chat.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 9, 1899

**Weather:** fine clear \+ cold winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine clear \+ cold winter day. To studio at 9.30 — all the m'g writing — a long letter to Geo. Cook on business matters — To Mrs. Burhydt \+ others — Lunch at home — afternoon I worked until 4.30 on head of Mr. G. — Then I walked to Keppel's in 16th St to see the collection of dry-point k\[illegible\] from plates by Paul Helleu — There were some good specimens of dry-point — his hand has but one string — a rather graceful but feeble string over which there is an awful lot of foolish gush of which the most is made by those who can turn it into dollars — it disgusts me. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g. at 8 went to Walter Cook's — meeting of Trustees of Ireland Real Estate Co. Ed'd, Geo. \+ Walter were the other Trustees present — The main subject under discussion was the expiration of the B.\+O. R.R. Co's lease of the Wysburg dock property — Ed'd made annual Report which was discouraging. Home at about 10.
