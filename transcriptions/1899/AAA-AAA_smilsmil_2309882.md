## Left Page

### Sun. April 16, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dismal day — rain almost constant until late afternoon — breaking away windy + quite cold — Up at 7 + had my rub + bath — after bkfs. I got Jim's Trunk into my room + brought his things there to pack into it — I became interested in the contents of an old trunk filled with old letters + papers + spent a good part of the mf. assorting them. Dinner at 2 — after that continued the work of the mf. doing but little however in Jim's trunk —
About 5 Ralph + I went to Mr. Moses — saw him — pleasant interview — gave him check for $200 — got Jim's books + rec. — home — tea at 6.30 — after it, the family including Ralph went to Charls and I wrote letter to Jim — took it to G. C. station + then to C's at 9.30 — George there — C. played his orchestrelle until almost 10.30 — then, all home + to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 17, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant mf. Immediately after bkfs. went to NA + got some figures relating to the Baumann suit — then to City Court, Chambers St. for the trial — but B's lawyer sent an affidavit that he was sick in bed with grip + case was adj. until wed. next. Then to Comptroller's office with Warrant for sum not mentioned + I don't know what for. The clerks could not find any thing for me and no one appeared to know anything about it — the Warrant was sent to me last Sepr. while at St. Hubert's — it is probably some matter connected with Diet Kitchen + Excise — then home + finished packing Jim's trunk — After lunch, a rest — then to Adams Exp. 42d St. Am. 24th — 45th St. — to Bornneau's — where I got my rejuvenated recycle — then to studio where I met [illegible] from B's — took him down to cellar + set him to work fixing Jim's recycle — Then to J. C. Nicoll's studio, 51 W. 10 — Ways + Means Com. — to consider matters in connection with NA junction next Sat — after that had a talk with Lockwood de Forest about Academy matters + the Treasurerships — Told him that I was sick of the whole thing + must get out. He said he had had a talk with Wood + W. Revealed himself in such a way that he determined not to consent to accept the nomination for Treas. —
Every at 8.30 went to Council meeting — home at 11 —
