## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 10, 1899

**Weather:** Very mild - cloudy at times - cloudless at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After 6kp. Wm. miller came with estimate of $85.00 for putting fixtures - sink, etching \+ printing tables - blinds, etc in Etching Room at N.A. of D. school - It was after 10. 'dk when I got to studio \+ started at once taking p\[illegible\]. from Stahr \+ Sterne plates on large paper - I got some very satisfactory p\[illegible\] \+ some that for some inexplicable reason - Reasons were not so satisfactory - At 12.30 washed up \+ went to 2\. Nat'l. Bk. - lunch at home at 1.30 - to studio at 2.30 \+ printed until near 5 - about 4 o'clk - a criek in my back caught me suddenly \+ after that I had difficulty in moving about. After washing \+ cleaning up I rested in my big chair until 6.30 - then home to dinner - Mary, Ralfen \+ I alone. - the girls having gone to Charl's to dine - we followed them soon after 8 - C - gave us some old time music on his Orchestrelle - Ralph \+ I returned home at 9.30 \+ soon got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 11, 1899

**Weather:** Light Rain in the early m'g - overcast \+ Raw all day - Rain again after 6 p.m. \+ through the eve't.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After an uneasy night my back was so stiff \+ sore that I was prevailed upon to take my 6kp. in bed - Ralph got a plaster \+ I put it upon my back - I got up after 10 \+ about 11 went to studio - I found that I could do a little printing \+ work that interests me now very much as I have got ink, Rags, paper, press \+ everything in good working order. Home to lunch at 1.30 \+ to studio again at 2.30 \+ worked until near 5, the condition of my back improving all the time. Dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g. news paper \+ reading to Ralph \+ to bed early.
