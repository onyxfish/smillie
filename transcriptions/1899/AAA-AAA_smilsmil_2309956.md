## Left Page

### Wed. Sept. 13, 1899

**Weather:** Cold, cloudy day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold, cloudy day \- Bkf't about 8.30 \- went to house \- Cox at work \- also man sent by Scott Brown to clear up \- He worked most of the m'f \- clearing up \+ sweeping out auney \- my head ached so badly that after 12 I went to my room \+ lay down for awhile \- Then I had a cup of tea \+ some bread, \+ wrote to W. Miller about the finding of the missing hardware \- Then again to house \+ made measurements, and accorded them, of various parts of interior for my guidance next winter \- About 6 o'clk Charl \+ I took a walk out toward Chappel Pond \- Dinner after 7 \- Eve \+ lounged about until 9.30

## Right Page

### Thur. Sept. 14, 1899

*Dr. Elkins \+ Katey arr?*

**Weather:** Cloudy, windy \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy, windy \+ cold, Bkf't soon after 8 \- Then to house \- Cox at work on flooring \- but the job man, to clear up, off for the day. At 10 we started in a two horse, two seated vehicle, Charl, the boys, the driver, a guide \+ I, for the ausable Ponds. The clouds were then breaking away \+ there was promise of a brisk, breezy \+ charming Sep't day \- The ride in was delightful \- Charl had his camera \+ took a number of snap shots \- The clouds closed over \+ the wind blew hard \+ cold as we were rowed up the lower Pond \- Owen was the guide we took with us \+ at the Pond we got Otis \- We were warmed up as we tramped over the carry between the Ponds \- again a cold time as we were rowed to Owen's camp, getting there at just 1 o'clk. While the guides prepared dinner we took the boats \+ rowed up \+ in to the inlet \+ back at 2 \- dinner was ready \+ we ate it with a Relish \- the guides then changed up \+ we started back at 3.35 \- we got to the lower end of lower Pond at 4.45 \+ started to walk on the road \- In a few minutes we were overtaken by the vehicle that was coming out forces \- got in \+ rode until we came to bridge over Gill brook I then got out \+ walked to the hotel \- about a mile \+ a half, getting in at about 5.45 I went up to my room \+ rested for half an hour \- Dinner about 7 \- after dinner met Katie Elkin \+ Perkins band who have just come over from Adirondack Lodge \- chatted for some time with them \- The parlor as furiously hot as the atmosphere outside was cold \- The tops of the Grant This m'f was white with frost.
