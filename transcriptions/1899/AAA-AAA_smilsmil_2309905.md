## Left Page

### Sat. June 3, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim returned from New Haven.

A very uncomfortably hot day altho' there was more vitality in the air. After 6 o'clk. I went to Millers. Then to Camera \+ H. Then to Spauldings — to studio — then to 2d Nat. Bank — Got N.A. pass books, bal. to June 1 — then to N.A. Davis there — every thing now rit but some of the casts in plaster — the big safe \+ the statue of Fogg. Then to studio \+ worked for some time over the balancing of accts — Home to lunch at 1 — After lunch I rode up to new beds — 109 s. \+ Amsterdam Ave \+ arranged some matters with Grannis — then I went carefully over the bl\[??\] \+ gave some directions for details in etching studio. Then rode down to 42 S. — went to Spauldings but Bell has got no saddle for me yet — then home \+ found Jim there — just in from New Haven — He has been there just nine weeks — He looks very well — I rested until after 6 — then dressed \+ with the family went to Charles' where we celebrated one of his birth-days — I don't remember which one. Had a very pleasant time until 9.30 — then home — changed dress \+ went to Century where I stayed until 11.30 — To bed at midnight.

I went to Wood's studio

## Right Page

### Sun. June 4, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day, but tempered slightly by a breeze like that of yesterday — At night the breeze was really refreshing. Etch's. at the usual time — feeling a little out of sorts — too much dissipation last night perhaps — headache increased during the day until at night I was quite used up by it — In spite of it I wrote some notes \+ letters, but not all that I had planned to do — Dinner at 2 — Geo. came in during the afternoon — later Nellie came — in the eveg. Geo. again — Charles \+ Mr. Thos. Adams — I wrote to Katie Elkin \+ sent her check in payment for her advances to Curtis Walker, Jim's tutor — To bed at 10.
