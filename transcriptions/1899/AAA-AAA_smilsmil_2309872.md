## Left Page

### Mon. March 27, 1899

**Weather:** Mixed weather \- overcast m'f  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mixed weather \- overcast m'f \- very pleasant afterpart of the day \- clouded at night \-
I worked at the studio all day \+ made
two crayon sketches of myself as I sat in
front of a looking glass \- the first, an abso-
lute failure \- the second, a little better. I
wish to make a study sketch from which
I can make an off hand etching\- at
noon I went to Fifth Ave. Trust Co. \+ saw
Mr. Dandell \+ gave to him a list of the
securities of the MA from which income is
derived \- From this he will decide the rate
the Trust would charge for collecting income.
Then home to lunch \- after lunch \- to N.A. \-
then to 2° Nat'l Bk \+ then to Met. Life Mr. \+ saw
Mr. Haley Fisk \- we talked for a few minutes
about the plans of the MA \- he professed to have
thought that possibly the MA would retain
possession of the old premises for another year.
I asked him if the Co. had any plans bearing
up on the disposition of the old bldg. he
said they had no plans \+ would be pleased
to have a proposition from the Academy \-
I went to studio \+ worked until after 5 \-
then home \+ had a rest before dinner.
Ev'g \- Council meeting \- Saw the Spring
Exh'n \- nothing very interesting to me \-
it was late when I got home \+ near
midnight when I got to bed.
Mr. Mayon came \+ gave Jim hours
I saw him \+ told him that I had decided
to make a change at once, taking Jim from
Morse's school \- That would bring only
relations to him to a close \-

## Right Page

### Tues. March 28, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very nasty day \- wet with a raw
east wind \- Several things delayed me
after bkf't \- among others, Walter came \+
we had a talk about Jim \- like Fred, he
advises me to make the change at once.
It was after 10 when I got to studio \- I
spent the m'f very unprofitably writing
out a biographical sketch of myself at the
urgent request of Jno. T. White \+ Co. Publishers.
Home to lunch \- after that I went to Etching
Class \- only Stahr, Stone \+ Kelly there \- They
each "bit" their plates \+ then took prints
from them \- Stahr \+ Stone were enthusiastic
but Kelly was downcast because he was
not very successful \- I left them at 5.30
\+ went home \- Feeling the loss of sleep last
night I rested until dinner \- Very
quiet \- going to my room soon after
9 o'clk.
