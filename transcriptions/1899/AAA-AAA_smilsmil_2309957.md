## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 15, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charl Returned to N.Y.

Glorious day. Up at 7. Bkf't at 8. Charl got away at 9, going to West Post by private conveyance by way of Yuba mill. After bkf't with Dr. S. \+ Katie went over to the house \+ spent most of the mf there. At 11.30 went to Mr. Maghee's \+ bid him \+ his wife good bye. They start for Canada at 12.30. Pleasant call \+ much pleased with his reconstructed house. Mid day meal at 1.30. After that, feeling very heavy I had a nap. Then again to house. Then to Scott Brown's \+ learned that some of my missing hardware had been found in a barn by the old Thin. Buede house by a man name of Slater. I went for Slater, found him \+ got a lot of sash lifts, bronze butts, Push button plates, etc, etc. These I took at once to Black, the book keeper, as Otlaw is away from home. After 7 where we had dinner. Ralph went with the Elkins to the top of Grant's Leap. Both boys have trouble with their bowels. An epidemic here. I got some drops from Dr. Hobbs. Cloudless moonlight, \+ cald [illegible]. To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 16, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

much milder, cloudless \+ smoky. Bkf't late. Elkin \+ Katie went to the Ponds for the day. I spent most of the mf at the house as usual. Cop worked until noon \+ then went home promising to return on monday \+ bring another carpenter with him. The man who is clearing up about the house brought another man with him \+ the two worked all day. I worried over the hardware trying to disentangle the puzzle of finding the place for each article. I find that fine bronze knobs \+ escutcheons have been sent for the kitchen \+ laundry doors \+ that all of the hardware is really better than is needed. Midday meal at 1.30. Prof. Van Iuuinge came to the house in the mf \+ made a call. I did not go to the house until after 4 in the afternoon. Ralph has been almost used up to day by the trouble with his bowels. He has been taking medicine prescribed by Dr. Hobbs. Back to hotel at 6.40. Dinner at 7.15. Eve'g chatting with the Elkins.
