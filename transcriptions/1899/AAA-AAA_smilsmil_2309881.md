## Left Page

### Fri. April 14, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very mild overcast day, with a slight attempt at rain about 6 p.m. I did not get down to bk\[s\]. until after 9 — after 10 when I got to studio — worked for a few minutes — then to N.A. Grannis has made a sort of commencement at making up Annual Report. I went to Am. Sct. As. \+ saw Kerby about the possibility of holding the M.A. Exhib. in the A.N.A. galleries — He was rather offish — Home at 1 — lunch — after that I lay down until 3 — then to Etching class — Sterne, Kelly \+ Perrine there drawing from chiea of Janitor Davis — I stayed with them until after 4 — then to studio worked a little while on head — wrote a letter to uncle Willie \+ home at 6 — Charl there — learned from him that there would be Trouble in getting int. on Kansas Smelting stock — due to-morrow — Dinner at 6.30 — Eve\[.?] a little reading \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Sat. April 15, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast mild day, clearing towards evening — working over my accts made me rather late at the studio — there I wrote two or three notes \+ at about 11 went to 2d Nat'l Bk \+ arranged for a loan of $400 — then to N.A. — Grannis making very slow progress with accts for the year. Then to Avery's \+ saw some pretty poor paintings by C. C. Coleman of Capri — then to Durand-Ruel's \+ had another look at the works of "nine American Artists" — To lunch at 1 — did not get to studio until 3 o'clk — Commenced to write out Instructions for Branch in building Keene house — Yale came in with Ray — I worked upon an Attemp. at a likeness of Yale — crayon — until near 6 — not a success as a likeness. Then to Borrman's about Ralph's broken bicycle — home to dinner at 6.30 — Eve\[?] with Ralph — checks \+ accts.
