## Left Page

### Wed. March 1, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — After bkfst. went to C. \+ H. — and got drawings \+ blue prints of plans for Keene house — then to 2? Nat'l. Bk. \+ made deposit — then to 140 Sullivan St. Fredericks \+ ordered three panel-back stretchers \+ to studio at 11.30 — Painted on 20×24 M'f among the Adirondacks — a work that I commenced more than 20 years ago — home to lunch at 1.30 — to studio at 3 \+ painted until 5 — then to 5th Ave Art Galleries \+ saw a lot of paintings by an Englishman — then went down 7th Ave — about 28" \+ 30" \+ 8to looking for Ag. Newman's Hardware works but could not find there — Home to dinner at 6.30 — a quiet even. at home. M. Mayor gave Jim an hour \+ a half.

## Right Page

### Thur. March 2, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Nasty day — wet \+ windy — To studio after 10 \+ painted until 1 on 20×24 "M'f among the Adirondacks". S Fahr there came with some plates to have etching grounds laid on them — Home to lunch — letter from Ch. Stewart stating that he cannot collect rent from Baumann, 143 S. Nicholas Pl. I wrote to him in answer \+ sent his note to Lee \+ Lee with a note from me — To studio at 3 — S Fahr came again \+ waited until I had laid the grounds — as it was too dark for me to see to paint I devoted my time to clearing up in preparation for Building Com. Meeting at my studio to-morrow eve'f. I hung the big Cameron \+ Hastings comprehensive plans on the wall — Johnson came to see me that he had got a situation as porter \+ gave up his place with me — He brought a young fellow to take his place — Home to dinner at 6.30 The boys \+ I dined alone — Mary, Kate \+ the girls dined at Charles — We had a quiet time alone — Mrs. Mayor came \+ gave Jim an hour —
