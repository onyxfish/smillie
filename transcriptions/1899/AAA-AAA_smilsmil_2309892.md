## Left Page

### Mon. May 8, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, chilly day \- a slight spatter of rain
bet. 2 & 3 o'clk \- After bkfs. went to Nicoll's
273 Mad. Ave. we walked down to N.A. together
He agrees with me that it would be well to have
printed \+ sent to the N.A's a ticket for officers \+
council that might have a good influence as
a steener in the election \- we selected names \+ he
will attend to the rest. I worked over the accts
all the mf., getting little helps from Grannis who
is very easily phased by figures \- Home to lunch
at 1.30 \- back at 3 \- worked without helps until
4.30 when the Auditing Com. came \- Miller,
Ward \+ Frank Jones. Miller brought with him
a man named Snyder, as an expert auditor.
We retired to the Portrait class Room \+ there
miller \+ the Auditor kept me under fire as tho
I were a culprit until near 7 o'clk \- then I
went home, very tired \+ dejected \- found Mary
Kate \+ the girls just going out to dine with Adams
at Charl's \- C. being still away from home. Ralph
\+ I dined alone, but I could not eat \- After 8
I left Ralph at Charl's \+ I went to Council
meeting at N.A. A very quiet time. Hart
seemed to have regained his composure,
Dewitt Wood seemed to be in very good spirits.
I called up my letter of Oct. 3? Resigning the
Treas. hip \+ asked that it be acted upon \- My
Resignation was accepted \- Home at 10.30 \+
to bed.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 9, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, threatening mf., which soon cleared into a
bright \+ pleasant day \- I went to studio soon
after bkfs \+ set myself to writing my report
for the Annual meeting of the N.A. to-morrow \- At
about 11.30 I went to N.A., to get some needed
figures \- back to studio at 12.30 \- wrote until
after 1 \- then home to lunch \- Mary went to
Po'keepsie to stay there a few days \- Ret? to
studio abot 3 \- completed report \+ at 4.30
went to N.A. there until 6 writing checks
for School Prizes \+ arranging that account.
Then a short walk \+ home at 6.30 \-
Dinner \- Sue \+ Read to Ralph until 9.
Then to my room.
