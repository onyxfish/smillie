## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 28, 1899

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — Nole \+ his helper came in the m'f \+ commenced work on the chimnies — relaid upper courses on Studio's chimney \+ pointed two chimnies on house. I worked on Mrs. G. [illegible] biting, rebiting \+ with graver \+ dry-point. I wet down paper for prints to-morrow. About 5 o'clk. I played my first games of Tennis with Bessie. I hobped about in a very sorry manner for a man of 66 \+ won two games out of eight enter. Then went to P.O. for ane \+ more —
Evening, as usual.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 29, 1899

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — out of sorts after a restless night — Took two prints from plate of Mrs. [illegible] head — worked n it with graver. Knole \+ his helper finished the chimnies \+ partly finished setting the tiles about my bed room fire place. I wrote a note to the boys — \+ one to Chaol telling him that I would meet him at Elizabeth town next week \+ took them to P.O. before noon — From 5 to 6 o'clk. made believe play tennis with Katherine. At 8 o'clk. I went for mail — Evening in much discomfort — \+ got to bed early.
