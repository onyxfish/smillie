## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 21, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The Elkins went this m'f.

Showery \+ growing colder — at times in the m'f it seemed as tho' it w'd clear — The sunshine broke out at times but frequent \+ sometimes heavy showers followed. a very fine moonlight night. Bkfp. at 8. Katie \+ her husband got away at about 9.30 — I spent the m'f,, as usual, at the house directing the carpenters — Adams the painter kept podging around painting sashes etc — It was 1.30 when I got back to hotel to lunch — after that wrote notes to Cauldwill \+ to sister Mary — then to the house \+ was there until after 6 — Then to hotel \+ had dinner soon after 7 — Eve'f in parlor reading — Between 9.30 \+ 10 walked Scarza — very fine \+ suggestive cloud effects. Then to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 22, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another showery day with some passages of sunshine \+ fine cloud effects. The m'f was spent at the house making my last arrangements there with the carpenters \+ Adams the painter. I was kept fully occupied until after 1 — there lunch — then to Scott Brown \+ paid him $10 for firewood \+ about 4 made my last visit to the house for this season — It was after 6 o'clk. when I locked it up \+ in almost darkness went across lots to the hotel — dinner after 7 — I sat in the parlor for some time chatting with the Janeway's \+ others — then to our room \+ we packed our trunks which were taken away by the porter at 10 o'clk. Then to office \+ got my bill — items in it that I could not get satisfactory explanations for — left it until I could see Beede in the m'f.
