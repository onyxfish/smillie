## Left Page

### Fri. March 3, 1899

**Weather:** Very pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day — Spent most of the m'f. painting on 20×24' — cleared up my Studio — cleaned Palette etc before I went home to lunch at 1. After lunch went to Roome's in 34th St. \+ paid rent of Etching Studio for March — then to 2d Nat'l Bank — then to Etching Class at No 4 W. 22d St. Two young men there — only three of last years class. A model was there \+ they had got started upon their work — I waited about for nearly an hour with them, overlooking their work as they progressed then I went to MMA intending to return to them but at the A— I was detained by a meeting so many that before I was aware of the time one of them came to tell me that the session for the day was over. Home at 5 \+ rested until dinner time. — Every at 8 went to Studio — meeting of Building Com. to consult about Revision of plans for the 110th St. portion of the new Academy Bld. Dielman, Jones \+ Maynard came — Blum did not. The large competition plans were hung upon the wall \+ we talked about Requirements — \+ of objections to the plans as they were — the other members made no suggestions — at last I ventured one, or asked upon requirements that were not disputed but the others said it would destroy the architectural symmetry of the building — I intend to make the suggestion to C.S.H — this is to change entrance to Amsterdam Ave. \+ give the entire N. front on first floor to the Historical Gallery — It was 11 o'dk before I got home.

## Right Page

### Sat. March 4, 1899

**Weather:** Disagreeable day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day — dull m'f. — wet afternoon — heavy showers during the night. I got up with a dismal headache \+ was just able to get through the m'f. at Studio. I varnished the boys plates to have them ready to bite on Tues. Mr. Kingsbury called at the house before 10 with complaint ag't. Baumann, which I signed, preparatory to suit for — paid rent $43 St. Nicholas R. Home to lunch — Miss Anne Smillie \+ Mr. Thos. Adams there — I got through lunch \+ then went to bed where I stayed until dinner at 6.30 — then I got up \+ ate a light meal, altho' my head still ached badly — I lounged in my chair after dinner until 8.30 — when I again went to bed without undressing \+ lay there until 1.30 A.M. — then undressed \+ to bed for the night.

Regular Century Club meeting to-night.
