## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 23, 1899

**Weather:** mild & overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild & overcast \- After bkfp. I spent an hour or two fixing a closet that has been emptied & cleaned \- Then to studio & for most of the time for the rest of the m't. I was cleaning my printing press. Home to lunch at 1.30 \- 3 when I got to studio & fussed there until about 5 \- Then home & wrote a long letter of instructions to Spouse \-
Dinner at 6.30 \- Ev'g with Ralph & his studies until 9 \- Then Charl came in for a few minutes \- I got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 24, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

light rain, early m't. a little sunshine middle of day \- dull again & mild \- After bkfp. to Jackson's e'xp. with notice from D. & W. freight \- Then to Dr. Yale's about my nose & upper lip \- an hour with him \- Then to studio & completed my clearing up there. About noon I went to 2°. Nat'l. & drew money on two checks for the two servants \- Then home \- lunch at 1.30 \- To studio at 3 \- stretched tissue paper on blinds & got out my work to commence to-morrow \- Dinner at 6.30 Ev'g with Ralph & his lessons until after 9 \-
