## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 10, 1899

**Weather:** Very clear & very cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very clear & very cold - at 9 went to Charl's & gave him $11000 N.K. & T, 2°. s to sell. Got to studio about 9.30 - Cut mount for pf. Cedars by moonlight, that I am going to send to Geo. Mr. Benson, Phila. \+ I got it into press. About 11.30 I went to N.A. & then to 2°. Nat'l. where I was with Mr. Pabst, Ass't. Casht. trying to untangle my Bank acct. suare until 2 o'clk. This is caused by my ignorance of book keeping. As I have it on my check stubs it is right but except in aggregate bal. it does not agree with the balances of the several accts. I see where my amateur work has made the muddle but I shall have to get the aid of a book keeper to help me out. Home to lunch at 2.30 - to studio at 3.30 - for about an hour worked on Maynards ex-libris plate - then to Dr. Yale's - saw Mrs. Y. for a moment - Dr. getting along satisfactorily - Home before 6 - dinner - Every. Reading. Charl came in for a little while.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 11, 1899

**Weather:** Brilliantly clear, bitterly cold & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliantly clear, bitterly cold & windy - To studio for a short time - there to N.A. - then to 2°. Nat'l. & spent some time with Case & Pabst when it was arranged that there were to be no N.A. bank transactions before Monday next - Then there was to be a balance struck and proved & a distribution of the aggregate sum in accordance with my accts. Then went to Sect. Sec. - 20 Nassau St. & had a protracted interview with reference to many items, especially to the case of last years Taxes to come before court next week & the bill for Remission that is to go to Albany. It was 2.30 before I got home to lunch. Emma J. came and arranged to give Jim music lessons - About 4 I went to N.A. - there met Vander Penne & we went to see studios for Etching class - went one in 22°. St. & to another in 14th S°. - decided to take the one in 22°. S°. Called on Champney - cor. 5th Ave. & 15th S°. - Home at 6.15 - dinner - Every. Reading until 9.30 - to bed early.
