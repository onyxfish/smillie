## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 28, 1899

**Weather:** Pleasant day - after bkf.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day - after bkf: I copied onto fine N.A. paper my letter to Mr. Babu asking her consent to use a portion of Baldwins fund income to fit up etching studios in N.A. school belf. To studio at 10 - to work for awhile on Jacobi head - there on Mr. G. head, taking pfs. as I worked - a short walk before lunch at 1.30 - P.M. worked until 4.30 - then home - at 5.45 went to Mr. Cadwalader's house, 13 E. 35th by appointment \+ met there members of the Ways \+ Means Com. of N.A., Beckwith - Wood - Nicoll - Brown \+ de Forest. F.D. Millett, who recently arr. from England \+ is now a guest of Mr. C's, came in -
The subject discussed was: "Shall the N.A. try to get a site for its new belf from the city." Mr. C. - who has had much experience in such matters laid the subject before us very clearly, pro \+ con. It was not debated but questions were asked \+ Mr. C. answered them - The conference was over at 6.30 - Home to dinner — [illegible] wrote a short note to W.fs, Warner - Montrose -
Today a box of Thanksgiving goodies was made up \+ sent by [illegible] to Jane -

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 29, 1899

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - to studio at 9.30 - All day spent taking proofs from plates of Jacobi \+ Mr. G. - on thin Jap. paper. Home to lunch as usual - back and worked until 4.30 - Had a short walk bet. 5 \+ 6 o'clk - Just before dinner I had an attack of nausea that prevented me eating my dinner. most of the eveg. I spent on my bd - later, Ralph read aloud to me from "Kidnapped".

Kate came from Po'k. in the mrf.
