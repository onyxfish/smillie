## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 18, 1899

**Weather:** Foggy, raw not f. clearing to sunshine — quiet day — fog at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 9.30 worked all day on head of Mr. G., Taking prints from time to time — am encouraged — home to lunch \+ back in an hour — Cleared up \+ left before 5 — walked down to 18th St \+ bought Salicine \+ capsules — then to J. T. White \+ Co. 5 \+ 7 E. 16th St. — saw Mr. Bisler — \+ I am to make pen sketch of my head for the Am. Cy: of Biography — then to Merk's — cor. University Pl. \+ 8th St — after 6 — closed — then to Lockwood de Forests — E. 10th St — about Na business — saw him — home at 7 o'clk — dinner — Eve \+ f Chad came in for a few minutes. To bed early.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 19, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim came from Pomfret.

Stormy day — opening fair but before noon strong wind \+ heavy rain, continuing until eve'g. It was about 10 when I got to studio. Worked again on Mr. G. head, fussing with the delicate shadings of the face \+ trying to get an expression about the mouth that eludes me — I took prints as I worked. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 — Took a small paper india pf. It does not yet come quite as I want it — Dr. Gester came at about 3 o'clk \+ stayed as long as there was day light. After he left I boiled some of my printing rag in strong lye \+ cleaned up. It was 6 o'clk when I got home — found a telegram from Jim stating that he would be home at 6 \+ found him in the parlor, barking with a severe cold — Dinner — Eve \+ f with family in parlor — To bed early.
