## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 1, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast \+ muggy \- a little spatter of rain about noon but the rain did not come untit about 6 p.m \- then it fell heavily for a time \- In the m'f I wrote to Reede, Callanan Bro's \+ to Mr. J.H. Maghee that I expected to be at St Hubert's next week Sat. I did no serious work but pottered about. After five o'clk. I had a little Tennis play with Ralph \- Jim was off all the afternoon fishing \+ came in at 6.30 wet. Ev'tf. I fell into my old habit of Reading aloud to the boys.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 2, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy, muggy day \- At times when the sun shone out it was very hot but temper. by a breeze. I got up with a headache which bothered me all day \- most of the m'f I spent out of doors pruning trees \- After dinner I gave up to my headache \+ was on my bed for a couple of hours \- then I worked on drawing a plan for the frame work to support a revolving box for laying dry aqua\-tint grounds. At 6 went to P.O \- to R.R. station \+ to N. Warner's \- Ev'tf. went to Y.M.C.A. \- where, after much difficulty, the Miller Bro's \+ Jas. McCausland were brought in to join Mr. \+ Mrs. Danl. Sayre, Edgar Turrell \+ self in forming an annual meeting of the Village Imp't. Soc'y. An entire new ticket was elected \- so I am out as 1st Vice Pres. Home at 9.30.
