## Left Page

### Sun. March 19, 1899

**Weather:** Rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain all last night \+ thro' the day until late in the p.m. when it cleared for a time — heavy shower about 6 — high wind at night — I had a very bad headache all night — at 4 Sun. I got up \+ took Phenacatine \+ Caffiene — got relief — at 6 I got up \+ had my exercise \+ scrub \+ went back to bed at about 7.30 \+ had a nap — Up to bkfs. at 9 — The rain bring somewhat light at about noon I went out \+ had a little foggy air \+ exercise — Dinner at 2 — a nap later — I wrote some notes during the day — after 5 went out for a walk \+ was caught in the shower — Tea at 6.30 — Evey. some Reading to Ralph then wrote to Spense \+ Saegre.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 20, 1899

**Weather:** Clear  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear — cold — east wind — I started for Kimmel \+ Voigt's with Goldsmith Daughter" plate \+ took Elevated R.R. M. W. E. Dodge met me at Station \+ at once we engaged in discussing NA matters. He told me that he had talked with Mr. Carnegie \+ was sorry to find him at variance with the NA. He said that is Butler's proposition had been accepted, C. would have given the NA $10,000 — I was so much interested that I went onto Fulton St. with him — Through him I was directed to Sandford \& Co. 26 Vandewater St. \+ there I sought a lot of Zinc plates for the Etching class — then to Kimmel \+ Voigt \+ left plate \+ engaged to be there for p.m. on Wed. at 2 p.m. There to 2. Nat. — \+ home to lunch at 1.30 — to studio in the afternoon — wrote some letters — got Reports of the Com. majority \+ minority, with Reference to the Butler joint stock plan for N.A. \+ Fine Arts Soc. \+ sent them to M. Dodge thinking that if he was trying to influence Carnegie in favor of the NA already had information of the Butler plan, it would be a help in dealing with C — for him to understand the proposition \+ the opposition to it — Then to Carrere \+ Hastings — took set of large NA plans — Saw C — \+ told him that the Council was not Ready to instruct the Bldg Com. — to go on with work on plans on the 110 St. part of the Bldg. Home after 5 — Rested until dinner at 6.30 — Evey — Council NA there until near 11 —

Evey Mr. Mayon gave Jim 2 hours.
