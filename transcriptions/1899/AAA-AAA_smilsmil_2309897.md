## Left Page

### Thur. May 18, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day with chilly east wind. After bkfs. wrote to D. Reads + to Fang Branch — then to N.A. - then to Etching class + after awhile back to NA where I worked on checks for the artists until 1.20 — then home to lunch — At 3 met Beckwith + Nicoll at the office of W. Jennings — Mills Bldg. We executed the Deed of Trust acting for subscribers to fund. for N.A. f.d. — Then I Ret'd. to NA but was not able to accomplish anything — so I walked up to Carrere + Hastings + saw C. + Brainerd about work on the new School Bldg. Then home before 6 \+ rested until dinner at 6.30 — Cue¥. Charl came in —

## Right Page

### Fri. May 19, 1899

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph went with Bronson Cutting —

A dull day with a little writing. I went to NA after bkfs. — there to Etching class + found Stone, Stahr + Clinshy at work upon their etchings — they will not complete the drawing in to-day as was planned. — Then to 2d. Nat'l. Bk — Then to Institution for the Savings of Merchant's Clerks + deposited Jim's $15.00. Then to N.A. and issued a lot of checks — These took check books \+ cash acct. home to analyze + worked until lunch at 1 — Mrs. Paulk Hasbrouck of Pok. called — Nellie Casey took lunch with us — I went out \+ got a nice Dress suit case for Ralph — It was packed with his things out at about 2.45 Bronson Cutting in charge of housekeeper came in coupe for Ralph + took him to West Brook — Oakdale N.Y. — to remain Sat. + Sunday with him at his Country home there — Then Katie Adams Elkin came and told me about Jim. She has just Ret'd. from Roger's wedding at Buffalo. Then I got to work on N.A. Check books until 40 o'clk — I took my cycle to Bormann's + got another Saddle to experiment on — I rode thro' the Park to N.A. new bldg. Amsterdam Ave. + 109th St. — went over the [illegible] + home by way of Boulevard 72d St. thro' Park — 57th St. + 8th Ave. — in at 6.10 — Dinner at 7 — after that Uncle W. + Aunt A. — went to call on [illegible]. I had a stupid eve'g + got to bed about 10h.
