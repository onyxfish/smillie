## Left Page

### Sun. May 6, 1900

**Weather:** Beautiful day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up at 7.30 \+ had my bath \- bkfp. at 9 \- at 10.30, as agreed with Ed. Cook, I went to Walter's \- he was not yet dressed \- I went away for half an hour or back \- chatted with Walter for half an hour or more \- Then we went to Edward's \+ there we three discussed financial matters for the family until 1.30 \- the Ireland Real Estate Co. will have to pass its June dividend as it has no money \- This will be a hardship to Sue \+ Geo. At last it was agreed that some money that Edw. has from a sale of property, the last of the undivided estate of mother Cook, sh. be advanced to Sue \+ Geo. instead of being divided among the heirs \+ subsequent dividends of the Real Estate Co \- as will as further payments to be made upon the Mother Cook property shall reimburse the heirs who now consent to this diversion of funds.

Home to dinner at 2 \- at 3 Rachel \+ I went to Greenwood Cemetery \+ visited Annie's grave \- It was a lovely spring afternoon with tender shimmering greens \- misty skies \+ melting distances \- The birds sang \+ the dog wood was in bloom \- everything was to me badly beautiful. \- After leaving our plot we wandered for some time about the grounds \+ reached home at 5.40 \- tea \-

Eve. at 8.30 \- R. \+ I went to Charlie's for an hour \- we met there Walter \+ Minnie Pierce \+ their boy.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 7, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

[illegible] day \- crisp \+ brilliant \- Spent the day at studio biting etching of Century book plate \- to lunch \+ back as usual \- the biting was successful but not quite as vigorous as I intended to [illegible] \- I am getting to be too timid in my old age. A headache developed in the forenoon that increased until at night I was totally used up. As I left the studio at about 5 p.m. I met Dr. Gerster. we returned. \+ he finally approved the etching of Mr. Gerster's head \- at 5.30 I walked down to J. T. White \+ Co's \- C. 16th St. \- young W. White was not in \- so I walked home \- I was not able to eat any dinner \+ I went to bed early \-

Got a reply from Chs. Miller to my letter of last week \- quite sane \+ reasonable — more so than I expected.
