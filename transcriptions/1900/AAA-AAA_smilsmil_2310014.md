## Left Page

### Mon. Jan. 8, 1900

**Weather:** Early mtg clouded, soon clearing to a bright, cold \+ windy day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim left for New Haven \+ Pomfret.

Early mtg clouded, soon clearing to a bright, cold \+ windy day — Got up under the influence of a headache that sent me to bed later — At 9.30 I went with Jim to G. Cent. Station \+ started him on his way to New Haven to spend a couple of days with the Elkins — then to go on to Pomfret. Then I went to Carree \+ Hastings \+ was there with both of them talking of the proposed bldg at Artists gate for b.a. of n. until near 12 — They both became very enthusiastic \+ Hastings promised to make some sketches — Then I went to studio — got amended [illegible] of Treas. Report \+ took it to O'Brien — Printer. Then home to lunch — after that I went to bed \+ was there until 5 — then I walked down to Lockwood de Forest's — 7. 2-10\. 5 — and talked with him \+ Treas's business for half an hour — Then home \+ had dinner at 6.30 \+ spent the remainder of the evening in a sort of loafing — my head \+ my eyes troubling me a good deal.

## Right Page

### Tues. Jan. 9, 1900

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day. After bkft — some writing kept me so that I did not get to studio until after 10 — There I commenced a new pen sketch — portrait of self with new pens \+ new ink — I worked until after 12 — Then to 2\. Nat. bk \+ deposited Swelter div. Home to lunch at 1.30 to studio at 2.30 — worked on pen portrait until 4.30 — Then to O'Brien's with [illegible] of Treas. Report bearing last corrections. Home at 6 — dinner — Eve'g wrote to Hibbard \+ Roberts \+ sent them payment for laying wall on Montrose property — Chase came bringing with him Mr. Aug. Meyer to call on the girls —
