## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 13, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull day \- with one or two attempts at rain. Up late \- bkf't. about 8.30 \- trunks came before I was up \- after bkft. sometime unpacking \- it was about 11 when I went to studio \- Saw Ryan \- then to 2d. Nat'l. Bk. \+ deposited checks of Mrs. Kent \+ Ryan \- then to W. S. Miller's \- 141 E. 40th S. found him at lunch but I talked to him about Keene hardware matters \- It was after 1 when I got home \- lunch at 1.30 \- About 3 I went out with Ralph \- to Alexander's \+ got shoes \- then to Rogers Peet \+ Co \- \+ got clothes \- then home \+ had a rest before dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g. reading newspapers. Rain commenced in earnest about 10.

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 14, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty wet day \- Up late \+ had my bath \- it was 10 before I had bkft. \- It was so disagreeable that I did not go out of the house. Some time getting my trunk cleared out \- dinner at 2 \- a day of loafing \- Charl came in after 4 o'clk \- with a lot of prints from the Panoramic photos he took at Keene \- Then Geo. came in \- Then Emily Taylor Kellogg came \- She is to stay with the girls over night \- We had tea before 7 \- Eve'g. Reading \+ getting some of my accts. into shape.
