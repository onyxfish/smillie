## Left Page

### Tues. May 22, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day — Spent the m't until 12 o'clk. at studio at work on VanDyke ex. lib. \+ got it ready for first proof — Then went to Richter Mf't Co. in 23°. St. \+ ordered Burlaks for Studio \+ large Room at Keene — Then to Chairty organisation Socy. 22°. St. \+ paid sub't to "Charities" — Home to lunch at 1 — At 2, with Ralph went to Murray Hill Theatre \+ saw "She Stoops to Conquer" — It was given well \+ was amusing. out before 5 — went to studio \+ worked till after 6 squaring VanDyke plate \+ filing bevel — Had a little walk on Park Av. before dinner at 6.30. Eve't. Read my newspaper — Sat with Mary — heard R. recite some of his lessons — wrote a note to Kimmel \+ Voigt \+ Then to bed — Mary continues to improve.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 23, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Close \+ uncomfortably warm — To studio at 9.30 — got printing things ready \+ then took first proof from the VanDyke ex. lib. The nitric acid ran away with me in the biting of that plate \+ the proof, when compared with the original looks very strong. Geo. came in \+ put his Meyer painting in its frame \+ then we held a discourse over it — I kept on with my printing \+ got 6 india p'f's, all in very good shape. It was after 1 when I got home to lunch — found a telegram from H.R. Butler making appointment for me to meet him at Century Club at lunch — It was a little after 2 when I got there \+ met him coming out — we went in \+ talked over the K.A. \+ Carnegie business for nearly an hour — I got a little encouragement out of it. Then I went to studio — there until near 6 working over Keene house matters — planning casing for large Room \+ laying out burlaps requirements for the attic part of studio — a short walk before dinner at 6.30 — after dinner. Read Post \+ then sat with Mary who has improved rapidly in the past two days — Chase came in for an hour.
