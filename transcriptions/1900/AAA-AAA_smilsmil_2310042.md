## Left Page

### Mon. March 5, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Katie Elkin arr. from New Haven.

Beautiful day - Temp. at 8 Am. 9/5° above normal \+ generally I was comparatively comfortable. I sat propped up in bed \+ was able to read newspapers Yale came before 12 \+ thinks I am out of the woods. - had a bowl of clam broth for lunch - I got up before 3 \+ made a complete change of under clothing \+ had a sponge bath - then retired to Mary's room \+ rolled in comforter in her lounge I had a nap. In the mean time my room was sunned \+ aired \+ the bed linen changed. About 5 o'clk. I ret. to my own room Katie Elkin came quite unexpectedly to stay two or three days.

Temp. at 10 p.m. just 100° - one [illegible] higher than this m'g.

## Right Page

### Tues. March 6, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day - down again - perhaps I was too entertaining yesterday. - headache all day \+ temp. at 100° - except for a little while about 1 o'clk. mid-day, when it fell to normal. - I clung to my bed all day.
