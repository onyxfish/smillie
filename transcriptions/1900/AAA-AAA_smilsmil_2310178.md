## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 8, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day - to studio soon after 9 - the labors there all the aft. filling in the hole by the wall + replacing the flag stones - all of which was done in a sloppy, unworkmanlike manner. I painted a "Capri from near Naples" - then got out a smaller panel of nearly the same subject + laid out some radical changes to be made on it - my time much interfered with by electrician looking for cause of trouble with my studio door bell - Home to lunch at 2. After lunch went with Ralph to Met. Mus. of Nat. Hist + heard one of Chatsman's lectures on birds - acoustics of hall so bad that I had difficulty in understanding parts - I felt very uncomfortable - headache + chilly - after the lecture we walked home but I did not get warmed up at all + was so out of sorts that I lay upon my bed wrapped in a blanket until I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 9, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very raw, blustering + disagreeable day with a flurry about 11 A.m. that was neither rain or snow. I got up feeling better than I did the night before + had bkfst. at 9 o'clk. At 11 I went to studio for a little while + about 1 I went to Chare's - had a very short walk on Park ave. - during a lucid moment of weather before dinner at 2 - Had a nap in my chair after dinner then wrote some notes - Geo. Millie came in before 6 + a little later Chare came in - Supper at 6.30 - Eve'g I devoted to making a draft of changes that I intend to make in my Will of Apl. 3° 1896,
