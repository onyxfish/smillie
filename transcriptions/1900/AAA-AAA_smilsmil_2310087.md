## Left Page

### Thur. June 7, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright day with cool east wind \- Got to Kimmel & Voigts a few minutes after 10 \- Mr Voigt had commenced taking plt. from Head of Mr. G \- He took 7 india plt. then he took some prints from book plates to determine color of ink \- It was noon before I left there \- On my way up town bought a Virgil crib for Jim \- To studios and tracked down two of the seven plt., then home to lunch \- To studios at 2 \- finished tracking down plt. \- Then B. G. came \- Expressed himself as entirely satisfied \+ paid me $125.00 He left at 4 o'clk \+ I went home. Fixed up plt. for Jim \+ sent it to Exp. Then Ralph \+ I went to Alexander's \- 6 Ave \+ 18th St. \- bot. 3 pairs of shoes \- Home at 6. diner at 6.30 \- Spent writing out checks \+ getting accts. written up.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 8, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Kate \+ Bessie arr'd. from Po. K.

Very humid \+ hot \- showers \- thunderstorm \+ tremendous down pour of rain from 5 to 7 p.m. The m'f at studio \- arranging in my mind the furnishing of the Keene house; plans of the rooms before me, I wrote the furnishing of all kinds that I would like to have gone there \+ wrote out a list. I made time to work with crayon on one of the india plt. of Mr. G \- head taken yesterday \- I lowered all of the high lights much to its advantage \- Then wrote a note to B. G. telling him that I was not done with the plate yet \- at 12 went to 2d Nat. Then to 12 W. 18 \+ left print for W. Bispham \- caught by shower \- then to 6th Ave \- Rode to 34th St. bought acct. book \- Then 34th St. to 3d Ave. Grocer \+ butcher \- home to lunch at 1 \- Sister Kate there \- Bessie came afterwards \- To studios at 2.30 \+ by 4 had completed my lists \- Then to 54\-6 Bleecker Marvin Safe Co. bought small safe \- $58.50 \- Then walked up as far as Union Sq. \- Heavy storm threatening \- got into car just in time \- but had to pass thro' a waterfall from the cor. to 110 \-
Dinner at 6.30 \- Edw'f. Chord came in \- In the confusion of new comers \+ visitor \+ storm I forgot to write to Jim until it was too late.
