## Left Page

### Sun. March 11, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Temp. at 9 A.m. 97½°. at 9.30 p.m. 98°. A fine day so far as I could see by looking out of my window but those who went out said it was very cold + windy + I was advised to keep in-doors. After a fairly good nights rest I awake after 8 o'clk. with discomfort still in the head. I got up at about 11 + had a little refreshment from ablutions + change of clothes. I went into Marys room + rested there for awhile. At 2 o'clk. my dinner was brought up to my room. I kept wandering about feeling exceedingly dismal + uncomfortable. pretty late in the afternoon I went down to the parlor. + there I saw Ys.? + Charl. + had my tea. Soon after 9 I went to bed but it was after midnight before I got to sleep.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 12, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charl. started for Florida.

Bright day — windy + cold — acting under advice I did not go out of doors. I got up at about 10.30 + dressed. Charl. came in at about noon + bid me good bye before starting on his trip south — he expects to be gone for about a month. I went down to the dining room where there was a good fire. I long to get out of doors. I am very weak + feel wretchedly uncomfortable. After the sunshine went out of the dining room I ret. to my bed room. About 5. Geo. W. Maynard came in + made a short call my tea was brought up to my bedroom. I was in parlor for awhile + got to bed for the night before 10 p.m.
