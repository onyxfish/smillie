## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 12, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A great change in the weather - bright breezy \+ almost cold. After a restless night I got up \+ had my regular scrub \+ bath - Then Tackled Beedes Statement for building house \+ set to work to analyze it - at 1 until bkft. at 9 - \+ again till 11 - Then to hotel - got letter from Mr. Kent say drought is very severe \+ little water in the Tanks. I wrote at once to Cooley to see if he could run a 1/2" pipe from Street main to one of the cisterns. Home at 2 - dinner - about 3.30 Jim Ralph \+ I started on Trail by Chapel Pond brook, was followed it alongside of the brook for some distance - then Jim gave out \+ said he would go back the way he came. Ralph \+ I went on - there were many delicious bits of mountain forest \+ brook - we came out on the road not far from the pond \+ started on our way back - then turned off on a trail over the burned sunset ledge \+ again out upon the Road \+ home about 6\. Tea at 6.30. Eveg. quite cool - fine in main Room \+ a family circle. To bed about 10

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 13, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy \+ continuous Rain all day - So cool that fires were kept up indoors - I wrote notes to different Wanamaker employees hoping to get from them some response - I cannot get any from the firm - about 11 Jim \+ I went to hotel - met Dr. Hobbs there \+ with him went to his cottage where I had a consultation with him concerning Jim's condition - after some questioning he said that Jim's trouble arises mainly from too much thinking about himself - he confirms my fears that the boy is temperamentally and physically very much like his father - poor boy - I had hoped for better things for him - the loss of his mother, with her sweet healthy nature, was the greatest loss that could have befallen him. I worked on plans for ceiling for main Room - \+ got it finished at about 5 p.m. I wrote a note to Mr. Kent \+ it was taken to the P.O. before 3 o'clk. Tea at 6.30 - Eveg. it was so comfortable in my little bed Room - with fire \+ students lamp \+ that I spent my eveg. there with news paper.
