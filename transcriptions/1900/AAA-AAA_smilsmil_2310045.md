## Left Page

### Tues. March 13, 1900

**Weather:** Dull, grey m'f, clearing later to sunny  **Temp:** [illegible]

Last night was a fairly restful one — had my bhlp'. in bed — up at 10 & then down to the dining room where there was a good fire — about 12.30 I wrapped myself & went out into the yard where I sunned myself for half an hour & felt greatly benefitted by it. Dinner at 1.30 — afternoon I had a nap on my bed Telegram from Charl — arr'. at St. Augustine. Alc came in at about 6. Tea at 6.30 — Even' in parlor & to bed about 10.
Temp. 9 A.M. 97⅘ — at 10 p.m. in shade above 98°

## Right Page

### Wed. March 14, 1900

**Weather:** Bright, sunny day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up before 9 & had my morning wash & then to bed again where I had my bhlp'. Got up at about 10.30 & went to the dining room — I had more or less discomfort in both head & stomach & generally did not feel as well as I did yesterday — I went out after 11 & walked for some time up & down Park Ave. on the west side where I was in the sunshine & out of the wind. I came in after 12 without having found the Refreshment in out of doors that I had hoped for. Trielly Cook came in for a little while — Lunch at 1 o'clk. I tried going out again about 3 o'clk — but soon came in finding it unpleasantly breezy. I had an afternoon nap on my bed — Had my tea with the family — Early even' in parlor but a caller on the girls sent me to my own room —
Temp. 9 A.m. 97° — at 10 p.m. 98°
