## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 23, 1900

**Weather:** Dull, cloudy day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, cloudy day — To studio before 10. Spent the day — working on the two plates — "the Road to the Lake" — "Mad. Sq. Garden Tower" — drawing in the skies \+ varnishing ready to bite — home to lunch — about 4 o'clk. I went to Eimer \+ Amends \+ got some fresh acid \+ chlorate of Potash \+ to Central Iron works \+ got a stand for Acid heater for H.A. — then to studio \+ made up a lot of fresh mordant. Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eve'g. wrote a long letter to Jim — took it to G.C. Station — then home \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 24, 1900

**Weather:** Cloudy day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy day — Rain at night followed by snow \+ high wind — Got to studio before 10 \+ the day was spent biting the skies on the two plates — "the Road to the Lake" \+ "Mad. Sq. Garden Tower" \+ working on the plates after the biting — Home to lunch as usual — Maynard paid me bal. of studio rent due to Jan'y 1. Between 5 \+ 6 I walked for exercise, then home to dinner — Eve'g.
