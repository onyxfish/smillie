## Left Page

### Tues. Aug. 28, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hot yet but with a breeze that made it bearable if you sat quiet. I was still a good deal out of sorts but, as there were some fine cloud effects I commenced an oil sketch \+ stuck to it pretty closely through the day. I am not sure that the result paid for the effort — the sketch was cold, colorless \+ formal — but maybe I learned something.

## Right Page

### Wed. Aug. 29, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Warm \+ mf. Thunderstorms were about, north \+ south of us, but did not wet us. The afternoon was a delightful change, clear \+ breezy \+ almost cool. Wells came \+ worked most of the day correcting the errors made in putting up the seat on north piazza. Me Otis men worked most of the day on the road up to the front door. I was completely useless owing to a headache of unusual character. Thinking that the trouble might be tracable to my liver I devoted myself to a course of calomel. At night, felt somewhat better \+ got to bed at 10.
