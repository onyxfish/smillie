## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 12, 1900

**Weather:** Bright cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

after b.b.f. I went to the M.A.f.D. \+ met the etching class \- The first day of the season \- I got there after 10 \+ found four young men making pen studies with old Duffy for model \- later several applicants came in from Antique class \- I told them to make pen drawings \+ submit them to me on Sat. next \- Strone came in later \+ brought with him another applicant an ex-member of U.A. school who has been abroad I did not get his name \- I prepared some etching ground but did not have time to finish it by kneedling \- it will have to be melted over again \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- after lunch went to S. W. Cook's office \- 150 Nassau St. to meeting of stockholders of Oakland Real Estate Co \- Ed. \+ Walter are the new stockholders, beside myself, resident in the city \- Ed. \+ Walter were in Phila., so Ed. \+ I constituted the meeting. We went over the accts. with me \- a very bad showing for this year \- no dividend this half \- but as there is some money lying idle in bank to Cr. of the Co. he amiably offered to advance me $500 \- on my demand note \- Home before 5 \- Rested till dinner \- Evert Chase came in \+ gave me a note explaining that I had 200 shares in his Smelter Pool, bot. at 54 \- for which he holds as margin two C.\+O. bonds of $1000 each \- Then I went to N.A.f.D. meeting \- Am. Ave. \+ 109th S. \- Routine business \- I got home at 11 \-

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 13, 1900

**Weather:** overcast \+ milder  **Temp:** [illegible]

got up with a severe headache \+ feel much out of sorts all day \- To studio before 10 \+ spent the m'f painting on "Capri from outskirts of Naples" \- Home to lunch at 1.30 \- at 2.30 went to 2nd Nat. Bk \+ then stopped at Calverly's studio \+ saw him \- he is modeling a small female figure, seated "Meditation" \- preparatory to beginning work upon one life size \- for a monument \- he has bitten off more than he can chew \- To studio about 3.30 \- in no humor for work \- cleaned up \- home at 5 \- on my bed till dinner \- suet \- lounging.
