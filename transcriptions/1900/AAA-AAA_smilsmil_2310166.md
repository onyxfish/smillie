## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 12, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day \- to studio at 9.30. worked on "Summer Showers \- Evening" all day got down out of the sky \+ struck for a good while at the transition to distant mountains \+ for the rest of the day I floundered in the water \+ upon the farshore. \- Home to lunch \- back at 2 30 \- out at 5 \+ walked up 5th Ave. to the Park \+ back, home. \- Dinner \- after that went to Charles for an hour, am anxious about stock operations. There is wild work in Wall St'. \+ Smelters are jumping \- I had expected to get some common to hold for a Rise \- but they got ahead of me \- went up 12 points \+ now I dare not buy \- at 49. for common \- Then home, read newspaper \+ made a list for W. Magher of hotels \+ pensions in Italy that I knew favorably.

In the m'f. wrote a note of condolence to W. Jas. Gifford of Hudson N.Y.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 13, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day \- another days work on "d summer Showers \- Eve'g" \- without much progress. Painting over water \+ landscape on left hand end of picture \- To lunch \+ back at usual time \- At 5 o'clk. I went to Brook Bros. \+ Snuold \+ Constables \- Home to dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g after reading my paper. I spent an hour trying to put my writing desk in order.
