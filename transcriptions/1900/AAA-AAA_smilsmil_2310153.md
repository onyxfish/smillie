## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 17, 1900

Mary \+ girls went to Scarboro.

**Weather:** Bright \+ cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

for some time after bkfp: in my bed room fixing things there - Mary, Kath: \+ Bessie went on early train to Scarboro' to make Minnie \+ Walter Pierce a visit. I went to studio with a number of things - did some fixing up on the few studies I made last summer - Maynard came in - the first I've seen of him since my return. Home to lunch at 1.30. Afternoon - wrote to S. W. Buck \+ sent him check for $104.00 paym't. in full - wrote a letter to Jim. Mr. Alex Delmu[t] of Cambridge Eng'g. Co. came \+ we spent some time talking of the plans of the Co. in republishing S.R. Koehler's "Etching". Mr[s]. not altogether agreeable. Dinner at 6.30 - Ralph \+ I alone - we had a good dinner - Ev'g. newspaper \+ some sums with R— but he got the better of me.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 18, 1900

Mary \+ girls ret'd.

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- went to W. S. Miller's - saw him about expensive locks sent from Russell \+ Erwin's - he agrees to take from me all of the hardware that I don't use at Keene. Then to studio set palette \+ spent the m'fg. repairing an old painting by Agate - a view in a palm grove on one of the South sea islands - I am going to give it to the N.A. f'd. At 12.30 - went to 2. Nat'l. Bank - got two C.\+O. bonds ($2000) from Safe Deposit \+ took them home. Mary \+ the girls had just ret'd. from Scarboro' where they had had a very pleasant visit. To studio \+ did a little work on one of the sketches I painted at Keene Heights last month. Dinner at 6.30 - Ev'g. Chase came in - I gave him the C.\+O. bonds to use for me in a venture with Am. Smelters. Later we all went with him to his house \+ heard a lot of his new music on his piano (Pianola attachment) Ralph \+ I ret'd. home soon after 10.
