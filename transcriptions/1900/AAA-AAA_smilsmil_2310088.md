## Left Page

### Sat. June 9, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Day opened muggy \+ hot but it cleared to be a delightfully fresh \+ breezy summer day. After bkft. I went to Charle's — then to Miller's then to DiHale's \+ then to Wanamaker's when I spent the rest of the mt'f going thro' the furniture dep't. making enquiries \+ selections I got home at 12.30 — lunch at 1 — then I had a good nap \+ after 3 again went to Wanamaker's where I rummaged in the rug \+ upholstery dep't's until near 5 — then walked up Broadway but found Vautine's closed — then to studio \+ home at 6 — dinner at 6.30 — Katherine \+ Bisu dined at Charls' — a quiet ev'f. \+ to bed early — Mary is getting about very slowly — she has been down to the parlor two or three times but has got no further from her room than that.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 10, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

After a night of headache I was unable to get up in the m'f. \+ kept my bed until near 2 o'clk. Then I got up \+ had something to eat. The day was very lovely — even cool — with pleasant breeze — about 4 o'clk. I went out with Ralph but was able to take only a very short walk. Tea at 6.30 — Ev'f. loafed over Sunday papers \+ to bed at 10.30.
