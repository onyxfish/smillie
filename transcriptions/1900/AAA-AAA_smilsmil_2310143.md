## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 27, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A warm day with charming cloud effect in the mt. \- Rain last night \- showers in the afternoon + at night. Buck + Webb finished the ceiling wood work + commenced on seats \- In detail of design the room is a happy-go-lucky hodge podge \- nothing about it is studied or digested \- nevertheless the general effect is good + it is comfortable. Before bkf. I ran down to the road and bid the Nelsons good bye as they drove out. Ray continued his agricultural work + sowed grass seed over the place generally. The sunshine + shadows over the valley were so fine that I set my palette + spent most of the day trying to paint another study \- which I did not get what I tried for I think it will help me in my studio work next winter \- Lawrence Adler came to bid Ralph good bye at about 5 P.M. We went over to the hotel where I got my mail. Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g. with newspaper.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 28, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day \- Buck + Webb at work on seats in main room \- Ray digging out earth from under north piazza \- After a while I set palette + painted over one of the studies looking up the valley \- painted some time ago \- Before noon I went to hotel + got mail \- it looks very forlorn + deserted there now. Dinner at 1.30 \- Afternoon painted until near 4 \- again to hotel + by telephone secured Parlor car seat for Mary + Katharine for next Tues. Then to W. Naghee's \- he was not at home \- Tea at 6.30. after an hour of hard work carrying lumber up to attic + cleaning up main room for our use in the eve'g. \- The usual quiet time \- except for the flies which are an exasperating + unmitigated nuisance.
