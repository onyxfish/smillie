## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 21, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A Fing in the air from meeting snow modified the mildness \+ pleasantness of the day that was sunny until late in the afternoon when it became overcast. Jennings reported that he had two forgetfulness, let the pickles at studio fell with cold water, consequently the studio was not warm \- business of various kinds kept me until after 10 \- then I walked to St. Allen's in W. 47a S. \- made an appointment with him for to-morrow. Met Yale there. Then I walked down to 29. Nat. Bk \- Fifth Ave. \+ 23. S. \+ to studio at 12. Home to lunch at 1.30 Back at 3 \- worked on M.G. \- head until 3. \- then to Central Iron Co. \+ gave an order for an iron for N.A. etching studio. Home before 6. Dinner at 6.30. \- West loafing about. At 9. \+ Charl came in \- but I went to my Room at 9.15.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 22, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouded, mild \+ moist until noon when the sun shone out \- To studio at 9.30 \- worked on etching of "The Road to the Lake" \- drawing upon it cloud forms in the sky \- laid ground on the plate \+ transferred a tracing \- At 12 I went to St. H.D. Allen in W. 47. S. again I met Yale there \- I had a tooth filled \- home to lunch at 1.30 \- to studio at 2.30 \+ drew in the lines for sky, ready for the acid. Walked up to Central Park \+ back \- dinner at 6.30 \- West. I went over bank acct. \+ balanced check book \-
