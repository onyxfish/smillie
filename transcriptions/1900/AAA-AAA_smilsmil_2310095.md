## Left Page

### Sat. June 23, 1900

**Weather:** Hot, muggy + cloudy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hot, muggy + cloudy on't. with strong breeze blowing — clearing in the afternoon + very cool at night. Not long after bkfst. Ralph + I got away — went to Arnold + Constables — bought things for both of us — then to Smith + Grey's + bought caps for Ralph — then to Rogers + Peet — then home at about 11 — went to studio — there until after 12. then to Miller's + to Jackson's Exp. + home to lund at 1. After lunch I had a nap — then to work getting things together preparations to packing my trunk — I found a thousand + one things to look after + provide for — as there are no stores at Keene Heights — so I made but little progress apparently. Dinner at 6.30 — Evening given over to the same sort of work — little items of no especial importance — + yet, each having its bearing upon some comfort for the coming summer.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 24, 1900

**Weather:** Hot, muggy + overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hot, muggy + overcast with S.E. breeze. — Up at 4.30, had a bath + shampoo. Then to bed again + slept until 7 — then up for all day + packed trunk until bkfst. at 9. — newspaper — after 11 went to Central Park with Ralph, taking his big grey squirrel. He liberated it in the Ramble — it seemed to be having a very good time when we left it. Home to dinner at 2. — After that I took a nap — + then completed the packing of trunk. Tea at 6.30 — we[illegible] loafed about until 10 — then to bed.
