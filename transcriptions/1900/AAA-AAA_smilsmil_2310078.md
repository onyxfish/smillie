## Left Page

### Sun. May 20, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A glorious day \- ups at 8 \- bath \- bkfs \- at 9 \- with newspapers \+ Mary until 11 \- Miss Thompson (the nurse) left at 10 a.m. Mary is much better, without pain but very weak. Soon after 11 Ralph \+ I went to 6th Ave. \+ 42nd S. \+ by elevated road to 155th St. \- from there we walked up the speedway by the Riverside to Washing Bridge at 182nd S. \- then to bridge level where we took 3rd Ave. Trolley car at 1.15 \+ got home at 2 o'clk. Dinner \- after 3 I gave up to sleepiness \+ had a good nap in my bed \- about 6 Ralph \+ I went out for a short walk \- went to Century \- wrote a note to H.R. Butler \- met Watrous \+ we walked home together \-. Supper at 6.30 \- Evef. read to K \- an article on the coming Eclipse of the sun \- At 8.30 went to Geo. Cook \+ talked with him about the ledger that he is writing up for me \- also about his books that I have told him I am willing to buy at the prices given by Jno. Ireland \- Emily came in just as I was going at 10 o'clk \- home \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 21, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fresh day with alternations of bright sunshine \+ heavy showers. It was 10 o'clk. before I got to studio \- but in VanDyke ex-lib. \- Nitric \+ water, equal parts \- very prompt by \- but little stopping out \- worked with graver until near 1 o'clk \- then a short walk \+ home to lunch at 1 \- showers kept me \+ it was 2.30 before I got back to my work \- graver until near 5 \- then to Richter Co. \- in 23rd St. for Biolabs for Reese house \- Place closed at 5 \- went to Am. Art Ass'n. and saw the paintings by 12 Am. Landscape Painters. I was not much inspired \- Home soon after 6 \- dinner at 6.30 Evef. wrote a short letter to Jim, advising him not to buy a second hand camera offered him by one of his school fellows. Took it to G.C.S. \- Mary has got along very nicely to-day.
