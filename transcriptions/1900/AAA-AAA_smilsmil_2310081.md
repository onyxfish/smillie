## Left Page

### Sat. May 26, 1900

**Weather:** Bright for awhile in the m'g  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright for awhile in the m'g \- soon clouded over \- rain in the afternoon \- At studio after 10, having gone to D'. Yale's immediately after bkf'. to consult him about my condition. I fear that I have started upon the same down hill course that brought me to grief in '98-'99. At studio I wrote letter to Siddons Mowbray about the VanDyke ex.-lib. \+ sent him proof \+ photos. Then I worked on plan of large Room at Keene until 1 \- home to lunch after that went to Charl's \- we had planned to go to the Speedway this p.m. with cameras but the weather was unsuitable. Then to Studio \- G.° There, painting \- D'. H.f. VanDyke came in \+ we had a chat of 3/4 of an hour. He expresses himself much pleased with proof of his ex.-lib.-plate. I worked on Keene plans until after 5 \- home before 6 \- dinner at 6.30 \- At 8.30 went to Century \+ by appointment met Mr. Comp. Bispham we Talked over the matter of a plate of smaller size \- to be reduced by photogravure I am printing \- \+ it was agreed that I should make enquiries, get estimates \+ samples of paper \+ printing \+ have the small photo, etc\- plate made. The pf. was shown to a number of members \+ was much praised. \- Home at 10.30

The hospital part of the est\[illegible\] progresses \- Mary is easier in some respects \- less so in others \- Bessie is reported as improving \+ Katherine kept her bad all day. Miss Page is kept busy.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 27, 1900

**Weather:** Brilliant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day \- up at 6 \- bath \- to bed at 7 \- naps \- bkf'. at 9 \- At 11.30 \- I went to Charl's \- got his new camera \+ with Ralph went to 23'. 5'. \+ 5th Ave. \+ got a number of snaps at the Navy Arch, Mad. Sq. \+ Garden Tower \- out \+ which I hope to evolve a picture \- Home after 12 and read until dinner at 2 \- At about 4, R. \+ I started out again with our cameras. \+ by Elevated \+ R went to 155\[illegible\] St. \+ walked up the Speedway to High Bridge, each \+ us taking several snaps. Then climbed to R.R. \+ had a coush time riding home in a surface car. Got in a little before 7. \+ had tea. \- Nell R. \+ I had a quiet time in the parlor \-

The invalids are all progressing \- Mary says she is much better to-day \- Bessie was able, with aid, to come down to the lounge in Mary's room \+ Kathy was up \+ about all day \- In fact, in the m'g she went to Roosevelt Hospital \+ saw Mable Cooley who has just come thro' another operation.
