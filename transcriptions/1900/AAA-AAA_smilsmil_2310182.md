## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 16, 1900

**Weather:** Still clear & cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still clear & cold — Bkfst. about 9.30 Part of m't was given to tinkering with Pneumatic buffers on storm-doors — + to reading newspapers. About noon Ralph + I went out + had a short walk — to 23d. St. around Mad. Sq. + then to Chaves'. — Dinner at 2 — Slept from 4 till 6 — Then wrote letter to Mr. Pessier Charleston S.C. about getting up Fine Arts Exbn for Interstate Exbn to be observed a year from now — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g wrote a long letter to Jim — mailed it at 10 then to Chaves — met Minn + Walter Pierce + son at his door — they were just leaving — I went in — Mary, Kate, Katharine + Ralph there — they soon went home + I stayed until after 11 — then home + to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 17, 1900

**Weather:** Clear & cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear & cold. after bkfst. went to Wm S. Miller's, the carpenter + arranged to return at 1 to straighten out our acct. Then to studio + was at work all the m't painting in an old cart + some chickens in the centre of "Normandy Back-Yard" under the trees — for some time past I have been dissatisfied with the centre of the composition — the two trees stood estranged + the pond beyond was taking up too much space + spreading in an uninteresting way — The thought came to me that a cart would fill that gap — giving me a play of light + shade + a spot of color — so I put it in + I think it is a very great improvement. Went to Miller's at 1 + was there till 1.45 without coming to the end of the business — then home to lunch — to studio before 3 + painted for awhile upon a small, single sitting, study that I painted on the fair ground at Montrose, Oct. about six years ago — I shall christen it "Oct, a study from Nature" + shall then send it to some exbn. At 5 went down to 23d. St. to do some shopping but got nothing. In Putnam's book store for some time — Home to dinner at 6.30 — no gas, owing to stoppage in pipes — poor gas + cold weather. We had lamps that supplied the deficiency. I read + did a little writing.
