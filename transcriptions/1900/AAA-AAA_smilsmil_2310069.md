## Left Page

### Wed. May 2, 1900

Funeral of Alfred Jones.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very mild pleasant day - After bkfst spent some time writing notes at home. - After 10 when I got to studio the men still at work on studio gutter I spent what remained of the mf tracing on gelatine Maynard's design for Century Ex-libris - home at 1 - lunch - Mary used up by severe attack of Tonsilitis - I took 2.05 train to Yonkers to attend funeral of Alfred Jones who was killed last Sat. p.m. in 5th Ave. at 34th St. by being run over by a cab - a large deputation of N.A.'s \+ Am. Bank Note people went up on the train - got to Y. in 35 min. - rode to house - funeral at 3 - service by a clergyman - then the assemblage was dismissed - I walked to train with Nicoll - 3.54 \+ got to G. C. Station at 4.30 - went home \+ wrote a letter to Chas. Miller who is very anxious, I am told, to assail the correctness of my printed Report as Treas. of N.A. - then out \+ had a short walk before dinner. Evg. writing until 10 o'clk - then to bed

## Right Page

### Thur. May 3, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain during the night - pleasant mf \+ warm day - heavy showers about 8 P.m. Mf at studio - scraped gelatine tracing of Century book plate - laid ground and transferred it successfully - then varnished the plate - This completed my mornings work - home at 12.30 - lunch at 1 - after that went to N.A. p.s. school \+ until near 5 was with my etching class giving them practical lessons in printing - I ground ink \+ took a lot of imps. from their etched work — pretty rocky, most of it — Home at 5.30 - dinner at 6.30 - Eve mf at 8 went to Ed Wm Cooke's - meeting of Directors - Ireland Real Estate Co. - Walter detained at home - severe Tonsilitis - Geo \+ Edw'd \+ Mrs. Edw'd present - The latter is not one of the directors - but there is a vacancy \+ no doubt she will be elected to fill it. A very poor showing - Wyss burg \+ Cleveland docks without tenants - The June div. will have to be passed - Many doubts some arrangement made by which a div. will be advanced to Sue. Look who will be much embarassed if she is deprived of it — not a very business like proposition — home at 10.30
