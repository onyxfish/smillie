## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 20, 1900

**Weather:** Rain last night — clear + muggy in the  **Temp:** [illegible]

m't. — soon clouded over — Rain afternoon
\+ ev'f. After bkfst. went to Charl's about
selling my Smelters Common stock — Then
to studio + worked all day on "Adirondack
Valley" — painting over portions of near
distant mts. I'm afraid that I wnt
be able to get it above my common place
\+ uninteresting average. I am floundering
After lunch I went to bank + got cert.
of Smelter Common to bring home. — Ev'f
Charl came to the house + got it —
many + gives Ret? with him to his
house — I looked over newspapers +
then worried for an hour with my
accounts.

In the n'tf I wrote to Mrs. Alfred
Mitchell congratulating her upon the
marriage of her daughter to-day.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 21, 1900

**Weather:** Rain in the n'tf — but it cleared cloudy  **Temp:** [illegible]

before noon — high wind in the afternoon
\+ ev'f. Sudden dash of Rain after 4. P.m. —
To Charl's studio before 10 — very dark, so that
I had trouble to see — Got out canvas —
"Road to Wood lot" + made a radical
change in large + central group of trees —
as the light improved I worked with more
comfort + stuck to it all day — At 12.30
went to Second Nat'l. Bank + got loan of
$800 to meet Orlando Deeds note due on
Friday — as I am not sure that something
may not interfere with sale of Smelters stock —
Then home to lunch — Then to studio — Painted
until D. Ferster came soon after 4. about
+ I cleaned up — walked up to Central
Park + back — Dinner at 6.30 — Evening
Charl came in — he sold my 70 shares of
Smelter stock at 52 —
