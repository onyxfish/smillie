## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 19, 1900

**Weather:** Bright & very pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright & very pleasant — Mr. Lang came early — got studio keys & went there to clean up — I followed, after my bkft. & remained there doing my share of the work untit 1.30 — then home to lunch — back again & found Jenning cleaning the windows — after a time I found it not agreeable & ret. home & found clearing up work to do there. After 4 I went back to studio & was there untit 6.40 when Mr. L got through & I went to my dinner. In the afternoon I met Mary (Ed.) Cook & her Catharine. She surprised me by telling of the engagement of C. to Smith a young lawyer — all parties are said to be very much pleased & I congratulated C. heartily & sincerely. Quiet eve & at home.

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 20, 1900

**Weather:** Charming day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day — after bkft. went to Charles' had a few minutes talk with him about money matters — then to studio. Large part fmf. covering the various "blinds" with tissue paper. Before 1 o'clk. went to Dr. Chas Alcus — 30 E. 33d. St. — to learn from him fully what he thought of Jim (physically) after his examination of him — The D. had an engagement at 1 o'clk — so he could not give me the time necessary — but made engagement & cut me for Monday next — bet. 5 & 6 o'clk. p.m. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back to studio. There untit 5 o'clk — it looks quite fixed up but there are many little things yet to be done in the way of clearing up — Walked for nearly an hour — then home to dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g Post took most of my ruck.
