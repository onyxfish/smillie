## Left Page

### Wed. April 4, 1900

**Weather:** An April day - bright + sunny - warm in the m'g but sharp wind in the after.  **Temp:** [illegible]

noon. At home writing until 10 - then to studio + worked until near 1 on Lippincott head - then out + walked until 1.30 - home to lunch - After that went to studio - made paste + taking tissue paper, cheese cloth, etc I went to N.A. of D. - There until 6 o'clk. pasting paper on blinds etc - etc. Home to dinner. Eve'g adjusting Jim's accts, + other matters

## Right Page

### Thur. April 5, 1900

**Weather:** Beautiful day - about 9.30 when I got  **Temp:** [illegible]

to studio - scraped + burnished on the face - Lippincott head - wet down paper + ground ink for printing to-morrow. Before 12 I went to Kurtz + paid bill - then in to A.A.A. galleries + saw the Bonner + other pictures - Curios etc - then to 2° Nat'l Bank - then to Hegger's - 5th Ave. + got three Paris photos. Home to lunch at 1.45 Gene + Betty Atwater there to lunch. After that to studio - then to N.A. of D. - 109° S. + Amsterdam Ave., getting there at 3.40 spent the rest of the afternoon putting up paper + cheese cloth under the glass of [illegible] cases + then tacking up etching p[illegible]. Home to dinner at 6.40 - Eve'g feel stupid + uncomfortable about the head - got an ill tempered letter from Mrs. Barhydt. She is not pleased because I have not formed + taught an etching class during the past winter + seems to forget that all that I have done so far has been for love of it + without reward.
