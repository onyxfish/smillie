## Left Page

### Wed. June 13, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day — warm but with cool breezes — After bkf. I went to studio \+ then to Wanamakers where I was engaged until after 12 — home at 12.30 — lunch at 1 — After that I went to Ostermoor's \+ Co. 116 Elizabeth Street \+ ordered 3 elastic felt mattresses — then again to Wanamakers — there until 5 — then to studio — home at 6. dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g wrote notes to Miss Kent \+ Skeuse concerning the cow question at Montrose —

## Right Page

### Thur. June 14, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Showery m'f — some muggy sunshine during the early afternoon \+ heavy thunderstorm between 5 \+ 6 o'clk. Katharine went to New Haven by a m'f train. I spent the m'f at Wanamakers — home to lunch \+ at about 3 Bessie went with me to Wanamakers \+ we spent some time selecting cretonnes \+ deniins for curtainings for Keese house — we left W's before 5 — B. walked home \+ was caught in the storm — I went to Keep's — bought shirts \+ was caught there — I got home at 6 o'clk without getting wet. Dinner at 6.30 Eve'g Charl came in bringing prints from photo — Taken about the Navy arch \+ on the Speedway about a fortnight ago.
