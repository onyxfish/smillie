## Left Page

### Wed. Aug. 22, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely day - the w'f. spent largely with the men - Buck & Webb still at work on cold room - Gilbs part of m'f on the plumbers work - from him I get some new ideas on the subject of getting hot water from annex up to my bedroom. He says the boiler from range can, with little trouble, be put down in laundry, insuring a circulation thro' the hot water pipes. The Otis men worked on the Road up to the house. I had Ray among the trees on the west slope of the hill cutting out undesirable growths so that the silver birches would have a better chance. Mrs. Lorms called with her friends Mr. & Mrs. King - Kate Hillard drove up with her friend Miss — nearly it being not so much to make a call as to get Gilbs who is wanted at her sisters. Mrs. White's. I went to hotel at noon - after dinner the girls went riding down the valley with Mr. Maghee - Jim went fishing - \+ Ralph \+ I went up the River trail, west side, to the outlook down the canon, N.E. to the Giant. I made a slight pencil mem. \+ then home soon after 6. I rested \+ took my supper with Jim after the others. Ev'g. I read newspaper \+ got to bed early -

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 23, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ida Holt left for Mf.

A very pleasant day - up soon after 6. Ida Holt had her bkf't. at 6.30 \+ took 7 o'clk. Stage for E. Town, expecting to get to N.Y. to night at 9.30 - Bkf't. at 8 - then with Moody setting out trees on old road bed - Buck & Webb finishing about cold room - Otis men on Road - Adams, painting part of day - At 11 I went to hotel - then to Maghee's - on Piazza there until near 12 - left a request with Mr. M. that he buy a safe for me at the auction to-day of the late W. Barker's effects - Dinner at 1 - a nap, then worked on charcoal sketch, a memory of the Giant \+ Ausable. Mr. Howell's made a call at about 6 P.M. Five screens \+ curtain poles came from Wanamaker's. Tea, 6.30 - Ev'g. Charl took the girls and Jim \+ Ralph to an entertainment at the hotel - they ret'd. at about 10.30 - I spent the ev'g. reading.
