## Left Page

### Fri. July 13, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Peculiar weather - cloudy - light showers in the mt - then a heavy shower - then clearing + in the afternoon another sudden + heavy showers with showers about - everything quite cool + breezy. To house after 9 o'clk carpenters on outside work - I flitted about giving directions - Wrote a letter to Miller about the plate glass + other things - sent him a check - sent Branch a check. Lunch - went to Mr. Sidney Smith's cottage to see some orchids that she had got in the woods - shower caught us there - then to house - ticked off a part of The Wanamaker list - ordered more lumber. To hotel to dinner after 7. Evg. in parlor with newspaper. Then a chat with Mr. Lorbenstein on religious matters + to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Sat. July 14, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cloudy mt, clearing to be a delightful day - Such a day as made me wish that the other members of the family might be here to me. I am quite in the dark as to their intentions having rec'd no word from them for two days. A letter from Charl came to me this mt more truculent + unfeeling even than the first - he is a rhinoceros + does not know what sensitiveness is. I went to the house at 10 - Mr. Ducharme + her two mates were there making believe clean things. Adams + his bro. were daubing joists melling glue size over the ceiling + walls of main room + the carpenters were cutting sticks in the linen closet while the man of all work was grabbing up old pine roots. He left at 12 + at 3 I engaged Ray Moody provisionally - The women got through at about 5 + took their discharge. At 5 30. R + I took a ride on our wheels down the valley for a mile or two - It was almost as much trouble to get our wheels down the hill as it was to get them up - we got back to the house at 6 10 - + to the hotel before 7 - meeting W. C. M. Nelson + urging him to make an appointment when we could talk about locating my road up the hill - but could get nothing from him. Dinner at 7.30 - Eve. loitering about. Stops at Casino - went in for a little while.
