## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 14, 1900

**Weather:** Clear, cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio and painted on little Eve\[?] sketch, made 3 or 4 yrs. ago — "Eve\[?] on Raquette" 9×12.
Will Parke \+ Miss Kingman came in \+ visited for an hour or more. Home to lunch \+ back as usual. After a little more work on sketch I finished it. Then I got out the Normandy Barnyard \+ sand papered it and oiled it out preparatory to getting it ready to N.A. exb\[?]. Then to Clausen's \+ ordered frame for sketch just finished. Then a walk for half an hour \+ home.
Eve\[?], lounging in front of fire, reading newspaper \+ writing.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 15, 1900

**Weather:** Fine clear, cold wintry day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After 6\[illegible] I went to Charles taking to him his 4×5 camera that has been in my keeping for some time. It was a little after 10 when I got to studio. I rubbed up the big frame for Normandy Barnyard, then set things to rights a little \+ putting the barnyard into to frame I sat down to take a good look at it — after a time I became satisfied that some object was needed under the two central trees — after a little more thought I got one of my old charcoal studies \+ from it drew in a farm cart \+ this I propose to paint in the first thing on Monday moff. Home to lunch at 1 and after that I went to N.a. f.d. \+ found a full class of embryotic etchers writing [illegible]. I completed the making of a batch of talking ground then I had eight grounds on eight 6×9 plates. I left at about 4. 30 \+ walked down 5th ave. to 97. The Paulico \+ called on Sam. Calman. I was permitted to see Mr. C — who was remarkably bright \+ pleasant — after half an hour I went on \+ stopped at B'way \+ 55. S. \+ had five minutes with McVill who is convalescing from an attack of pneumonia — then home to dinner — Rec\[?]. a letter from Mr. Kent of Buffalo offering me $700 for the Montrose [illegible]. I had offered it for $800 — I think I shall accept it. Ther\[?]. — cold out of doors — warm in doors — big fire \+ newspaper
