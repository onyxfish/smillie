## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 1, 1900

**Weather:** At Upper Ausable Lake.  **Temp:**

Glorious day \- Bkfst. at 8 \- about 9.30 all hands started for Ausable Ponds in Munroe Holt's three seat wagon preceded by Guides Jno. Brown \- Ed. Beede, Hegrand Hale \- Mary, Kate, Katharin, Bessie, Charl, Jim, Ralph \+ self. The ride over the Reserve Road was exceedingly pleasant. Got to lower lake soon after 10 \- Delicious atmospheric effect \+ reflections \- Charl took a panorama snap shot \- The walk over the camp was a delight. About 11.30 when we got to upper lake \+ before 12 when we got to Ved. Beede camp, at the upper end of it \- I went out in boat with Jim \+ Ralph \- back before 1 \+ then had dinner which was much enjoyed by all \- then W. \+ Mrs. Sidney Smith, with their friends Mr. \+ Mrs. Zabriskie, called \- then I went out in boat with many of Kate \+ took a look at some of the camps \- on my return the boys went in swimming. It was after 4.30 when Jim, Ralph \+ I, with Deg. Hale, left camp for our return \- the others remaining over night \- a pleasant walk over the camp \- The sun of the lower lake \- rather cool \- at 5.50 we started on our walk over the Reserve Road \+ got to the entrance at 7.05 \- just 1¼ hour \- a walk that gave me no discomfort \- Stopped at hotel \+ got shod \- Home at 7.30 \- changed under clothes \+ had a rub. Supper at 8 \- Read newspaper \+ we went to bed at 9.30 \- I was a little lame from rowing \- in the shoulders \- otherwise S was not tired \-

## Right Page

### Sun. Sept. 2, 1900

**Weather:** Rest of family Ret. from lake.  **Temp:**

An exceedingly hot \+ oppressive day. Air thick \+ hazy \- strong southerly breeze \- Up at 8 \+ had very satisfactory bath, followed by a good Bkf. Jim slept until after 10 \- I did some writing \+ read newspaper \- About 11.30 I walked over to Mr. Weston's cottage \+ made a call on Mr. W \- then up to the Openhein cottage \+ made a long call meeting Mr. \+ Mrs. O \- Miss (didn't get name), Miss Weston, Mr. Cohn, architect \- \+ Alex. Schilling \- left there at 1, \+ went to Dr. Hobbs \+ arranged with him to come to house \+ see Kate Leonard after dinner \- trouble with bowels. Then to the Howell's cottage \+ saw bro. \+ sister \- Then to hotel \+ got mail \+ home at 2 \- 5, dinner \- much loafing after that \- young Dr. Janeway \+ wife called \- D.J \- Jr. \+ wife am. at hotel \- this m'g. just from Liverpool \- Our family arrived from lake at a little before 6 \- two of them well up \- Katharine sick \+ Charl suffering from a heavy fall that he had from tripping on a root in camp at dinner time \- He fell upon his back \+ was much shaken up \+ tea at 6.30 \- We \+ on Piagra until 9.30 when Kate \+ I got Charl to bed \-
