## Left Page

### Sun. July 29, 1900

*Mollie Adams & Miss Davies spent the night with us.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day - atmosphere so hazy that distant mts. could not be seen. Up at 6.30 \+ had bath \+ scrub in the sun shine. Went out \+ prospected over the new road across Mr. Nelson's corner. Bkf. at 9 - almost all the m'f. was given to close work writing a letter to Wanamaker revising his bill and giving orders for wire five screens. It was nearly 2 o'clk. when I went to the hotel with my letter. Home at once. dinner - after that some loafing - About 4 we were surprised by the unexpected appearance of Mollie Adams \+ Miss Davies her companion - They had just tramferd over the Giant from their camps - about 14 m. They were a very rough looking couple with their bare heads - short skirts \+ packs on their backs - Mr. Maghee, Mr. Hardenburgh \+ her grand daughter called \+ we had a pleasant, neighborly chat - After tea Mollie \+ Miss D. went to the Whites \+ made a call. We \+ on piazza.

## Right Page

### Mon. July 30, 1900

*Mollie \+ friend ret? to camp.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The day opened with showers \+ was quite unsettled in weather all day - between 7\+8 p.m. there was a fine thunder shower - \+ an eve'g of brilliant starlight. Bkf. at 8 - Mollie \+ her friend got away after some heavy rain at 10.30 - to return to their camp by way of Chappel Pond Road - 16 miles. Buck \+ West did not come to work to-day. Adams \+ his bro. came after 10 o'clk. We worked together in the studio putting up burlaps. Dinner at 1 - Katherine \+ Bessie went to Mrs. Coffins' Informal Musicale at 2 o'clk. \+ returned bringing with them Mrs. Hardenburgh \+ a young friend \+ Mrs. Sidney Smith - At the time Mr. Borbenstein was making his parting call. Ray Moody worked a part of the day \+ I had him cut down a number of the young pines about the house. Tea at 6.30 - then on piazza for an hour watching the storm cross the valley \+ the eve'g close in. - Then lighted up \+ started a small fire \+ had a reading circle until 9.30
