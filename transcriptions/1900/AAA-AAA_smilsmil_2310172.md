## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 24, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day — dark + drizzly wet. I got to studio after 10 + painted on Road to woodlot picture making some considerable changes in the foreground. Home to lunch + painted as long as I could see which was but little after 3. Home before 5 + rested until dinner. Ev'f at 8 o'clk. I went to Century + heard a very interesting Talk by Dr. W.A.P. Martin who was in Pekin during the late siege — He was appointed some years ago head of The University of China established at Pekin by the young Successor to educate chinamen in western Science, Philosophy + International Law. He told his story graphically + with some humor at times — He is 70 years old + has spent 50 of them in China + has the credit of being very learned in Chinese matters + fluent in the use of their language. He described the outbreak as the result of Political jealousies among the Chinese — Trade jealousies owing to encroachment of foreigners + least of all — Religious animosity — The Boxers are a new new sect having given trouble as much as a 100 years ago — They may be described in a very general way as Hypnotists or Spiritualists taking fanatically opposed to all foreign innovations claiming to be directed, by some occult means, by the supreme Power. The Dowager Empress found it convenient to use them ago. The young Emperor who, having come under outside influences was too progressive having established not only the university but decreed a system of popular education, the temples to be used as school buildings — I remained chatting with others after the lecture until 11.30 — then home.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 25, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dark + dismal rainy day — Bkft. at 9.30 — Spent the m'f reading — at about 10'clk — I ventured out between showers but was soon driven in — Dinner at 2.30 — lost part of the afternoon by sleeping through it — Supper at 6.30 — Ev'f Charl came in for awhile — I did some writing on my accts also some preliminary revision of my Will which I have decided to amend — I shall drop out of it all of the involved provisions that I had inserted to secure to my boys a home in their 38th St. house until they reached majority — They have now reached an age that they are capable of deciding for themselves as to how they wish to live + provisions or plans that I might make would be quite likely to be distasteful or irksome to them. So, I shall provide only for executors to take care of the property until the boys come of age.
