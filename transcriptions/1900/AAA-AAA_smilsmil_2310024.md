## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 28, 1900

**Weather:** Bright + sunny in the m'f. clouding over later — Rain falling in the afternoon + continuously at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I awake at 4 A.m. with a headache that clung to me all day making me perfectly useless. I got up + dressed but hung about, much of the time on my bed.
I Revived somewhat in the eve'g and wrote two or three notes.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 29, 1900

**Weather:** clear, cold + unpleasantly windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- got to studio before 10 + etched in small head of self - from tracing of Pach photo. Varnished to get it ready for biting — after lunch I bit it in — bitre — half + half. I was so anxious to get it done before having to go to Com. meeting at Carrere + Hastings at 4.30 that I hurried it in parts — so that my success with the acid was not brilliant — but I hope to make a good thing of it nevertheless — Got to C. + H. on time. Beckwith there, Brown, Nicoll \+ Dielman came in the order named. J. Q. a. Ward did not put in an appearance at all. There was a good deal of talk but no real progress. The absence of Ward was accepted as an explanation. I think it was because no one had a clear plan for action. I advise a cautious exploration to find just what opposition is to be expected from the press + City + Park officials — Home at 6. dinner. Ev'g Chaol came in for a time — Later, I did some more work in pasting up + packing away back nos. of Harpers Mag. to be bound.
