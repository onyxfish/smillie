## Left Page

### Thur. May 24, 1900

**Weather:** Dull swarm  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull swarm — rain commencing about 10 p.m. To studio \+ worked on some plans for Keene house — Gel. came, set his palette \+ painted on W. Meyer's picture. — At 12 I went to Richter Mft Co. \+ gave final order for burlaps for Keene ho. Lunch at 1 — then to K.V. \+ had four pfs. taken from M.P. of. head. — It all comes very well but the shaded cheek — that work is too close \+ has been fussed over until it is unsound. — To studio at 4 \+ tracked down the four pfs. — then stretched some paper to make drawings for plans for piazza on south end \+ east side of Keene house — Walked up Fifth Ave. with Gel. \+ home to dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g went to Charl's for a little while — then to Murray Hill Hotel \+ saw Mr. Kent \+ her daughters, from Buffalo. Home at 9.30 — wrote some notes — mailed them \+ to old at 11.15

## Right Page

### Fri. May 25, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Much rain thro' the night, continuing until the middle of the day. To studio \+ worked all the a.m. on plan of piazza for south end \+ east side of Keene house. — home to lunch at 1 — at 2 at D. Yale's — out of town — then to studio — the afternoon spent writing along letter of instruction to Franz Branch \+ planning further improvements for Keene house — out at 5 — sent drawings \+ letter to Branch — went again to Yale's — found him just starting to catch a train for Peekskill — walked to station with him — then home. — Mary continues to improve but to-day Bessie is down with tonsillitis \+ under instruction from D. Smith, Miss Page, a trained nurse was at once secured. — With Miss Page \+ Miss Fullwood (dress-maker) at table. \+ Mary \+ Bessie, \+ perhaps, Katharine not there, I look about \+ wonder what family I am in. Eve'g. wrote a letter to Jim. Charl came in for a few minutes.
