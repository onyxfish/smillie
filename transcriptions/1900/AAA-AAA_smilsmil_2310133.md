## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 7, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

After a windy night a cold \+ cloudy m'f that cleared into a brilliant, cloudless \+ cool day followed by a still, cold \+ wonderfully bright moonlight night. I spent much f the m'f with Webb laying out the steps to the Anney bedroom balcony, Running over ice bo. \+ butler's pantry \- \+ in finishing the work on the large window of studio, closing joints when water came in when Rain came from the north. I went to hotel at noon \- Mary, Kate, Katherine \+ Bessie walked up River Trail, west side, to where There is a fine view looking N. E. to Giant. They got back at 10 o'lk. After a pretty hard walk \+ climb. Raelph was out of sorts all day \- vomiting \+ purging. Better at night \- Jim seemed better. Late in the afternoon Mrs. White made her first call on us \- Very pleasant \+ neighborly.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 8, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very much milder than yesterday \- thin clouds much of the time \+ indications of change of weather. It was full moon at night with short periods of brightness. The m'f went as many others have gone in attending to small matters. looking after Webb \- \+ Ray \- putting up curtain rods. etc. Went to hotel before noon \- worked on plan of last side of Main Room \- Otis came \+ I paid him $75.00 on acct. Dinner at 1 \- afternoon, got Plan of E. wall almost finished \- Webb got his stairway to Anney bed Rooms almost done. Eve'f. no lamps were lighted \+ we sat about on the Piazzas \- the upper one \- the north one \+ the south one \- at last all hands went to bed before 10 o'lk.
