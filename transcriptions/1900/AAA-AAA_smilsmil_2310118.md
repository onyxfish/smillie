## Left Page

### Wed. Aug. 8, 1900

**Weather:** Hot, humid \& exceedingly uncomfortable  **Temp:** [illegible]

The Ther. in my room did not get above 83°. but the discomfort was that of 93°. There were some heavy showers in the early m'f, and again in the afternoon - one bet. 4.55 o'clk \+ another while we were at tea, about 7 - This one deluged my studio \& Ralph's bed Room - coming in at the open doors \& windows to the north - Wonderful effects of moonlight in the ev'g, clouds overhead \& mists filling the valleys. Mollie \& friend left to go over the Giant at about 10.30 - Miss Holt arr'd about noon by stage from E-town. Buck \+ Webb came \+ worked all day on cold store room. Adams came alone \+ fussed about all day - Ray Moody came up for awhile in the m'f but is still unable to work. I wrote some letters \+ worked awhile on plans but had little energy for anything - I directed the work of Buck \& Webb \+ had Adams restretch burlaps on studio wall that was hanging very loose owing to the damp weather.
Ev'g on piazza watching moonlight effects \+ reading Van Dyke's "Fisherman's Luck."

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 9, 1900

**Weather:** fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

When Buck was coming to his work this m'f, up the hill \+ thro' the thick woods north of the house he found a bag containing all of the hardware that was stolen from my house more than a year ago. The bag was so rotten that it w'd. not hold together. The metal was more or less marred by wet. I gave a lot of it to Buck. Work went on on cold room until afternoon when B.\+W. put up window blinds on bedrooms. The plumber who did the work here came late in the afternoon with two bags of mineral wool to pack about hot water pipes between house \+ annex. It surprised him to find how completely he had got the hot \+ cold water pipes mixed up. At 6 I went to Scott Brown to get Otis to dig for me to-morrow. The work on the new road is standing still for some reason unknown to me. Ev'g a sickly moonlight. The family went to entrance of Ghost's walk about 9 o'clk but did not enter as the quality of moonlight was unsatisfactory.
