## Left Page

### Wed. Thur. Jan. 4, 1900

*(noted at top: "Turned over two pages — Tues 2°")*

**Weather:** Very cold & windy  **Temp:**

Very cold & windy — to studio about 9.30 — made a camera-lucida reduction in outline on tracing paper & transferred it in vermilion to the etching ground that I had laid over the mezzotint ground on the old steel plate x  That I got many years ago from Father — Spent the rest of the day scraping it — Home to lunch as usual. About 5 went to O'Brien's in 23d St. & got pf- of my long delayed Treas't Report — Then home — dinner — Wea. & some writing & some reading — Charl came in for awhile.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 5, 1900

*(noted at top: "Wm & Katharine Elkin arr. from New Haven.")*

**Weather:** Very pleasant m/f- clouded p.m.  **Temp:**

Very pleasant m/f- clouded p.m. - with a slight sprinkle of rain for a few minutes. Having some writing I did not get to studio until about 10. Then worked on mezzotint until noon — then began to arrange to re-enlarge photo. of self (by Pach) to make new sketch for J. T. White's Am. Enc. of Biography. a short walk before lunch — did not get to studio until nearly 3 — missing Manuel by only a few minutes, as I learned afterwards. I waited at studio for him until 6. when I got home found that he had been there at 5.45 — At studio I arranged my square with Threads over photo. & Bristol board — but it was too dark for me to see to work —
The Elkins got to the house a little before 6. After dinner they, with Katharine \+ Bessie, went to the German Theatre. Charl came in for awhile — I got to bed early.
