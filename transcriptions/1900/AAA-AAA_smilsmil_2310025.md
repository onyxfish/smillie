## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 30, 1900

**Weather:** Cold & clear - quieter & rather milder than yesterday  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold & clear - quieter & rather milder than yesterday. To studio before 10 - Took prints from little etching of yesterday & found that in my hurry it had not been bitten enough in parts - I laid a sebiting ground that looked all right but it was too weak + promptly began to foul bite - then I stopped without accomplishing anything but the foul biting which took me some time to clean off. I spent the rest of the day bringing the work up with the graver instead of acid. Home to lunch as usual - After 4 o'clk. I took two more prints. I am satisfied now that the etching cannot be used as I had hoped it might, for photo-reg. reproduction for J. T. White & Co. About 5 o'clk. Difk[illegible] came in with sketches & estimates for stage setting for Village Hall - nearly $800 - I have it much hope that the Montrose people can come to such terms - It was nearly 6 when I got home. - dressed - & at 7 went to Walters to dinner to meet Mr. Corning of Albany - a sprightly lady by no means young. Ed & Cook & Mary together with Sallie Cook constituted the dinner party. Home at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 31, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast day until late in the afternoon when it cleared cold & windy again. I worked all day on little portrait of self, taking two prints in the m'g & again two the last thing in the afternoon - & yet it is not finished - it is a disappointment - I had hoped that I could rush it right through without any fussing over it, making it simple, direct & free - I am not actually ashamed of it, but it is not what I set out to do. Kerby cut up the photo. Reduction of Mr. Q - head - now I have that problem before me again. I wish it were well out of the way. - D. Yale & Mr. Walker paid me a visit in the afternoon. After 5 I went to a store on B'dway near 26th St. to get gas heaters for printing outfit at N.A. of D. - did not find just what I wanted but got one small burner. Dinner at 6.30 - We felt very stupid but having many things that I should do I did none of them but went to bed early.
