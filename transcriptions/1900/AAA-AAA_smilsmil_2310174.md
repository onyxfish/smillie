## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 28, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day — at 9.30 went to Safe Deposit Co. 5th Ave. & 23d. St. — met Lockwood de Forest & felt manuel & together we went over the N.A. securities & I checked them off. After that was done I went to studio — set palette & found something to do on Woodlot Road until after 12 — then home & until lunch time was engaged packing a box with stuff more or less indigestible for Jim's Thanksgiving — I hope it won't prove to be a Pandora's box for him. After lunch to studio & found Sterue waiting for me — I absent sometime with him showing him how to use dry-pt. & graver in finishing his etchings — then he worked at my desk while I painted a frotté over sketch that I rubbed in a few days ago — that is, I smeared thin & transparent colors so as to get the relations & color values in a crude way — Home soon after 5 & before dinner wrote a letter to Jim — took it to Grand Cent. Sta. — Dinner at 6.30 — after 8 went to Water-Color Socty. A Special meeting at Salmagundi Club — 12th S. — near 5th Ave. Voted to hold annual exhn in galleries of old Academy Bldg. Walked up town with Geo. & got home soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 29, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Thanksgiving Day.

A mean sort of day — cloudy & damp with light rain at night. I got up with a headache which finally floored me about 4.30 p.m. I went to studio in the m't, but was not able to paint — but I worked over my Montrose check books for the past five years to extract answers to certain questions put by Mr. Kent who may possibly be willing to open negotiations for the purchase of the Mt-house. — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Ralph had jabbed an old & rusted pair of scissors deeply into the palm of his left hand — I took him at once in to Dr. Putmans & had the wound properly cleansed & dressed with antiseptics — $5.00 — then I had lunch & back to studio — Sterue came & I gave the rest of the afternoon to him & was able to hold up till 4 o'clk — then home & to bed — & there stayed — losing the fine Thanksgiving dinner at Charles — it was a great disappointment to me.
