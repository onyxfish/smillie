## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 25, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An extremely cold \+ windy day \- Bkfp. between 9 \+ 10 o'clk \- after reading newspaper I spent the rest of the m'f writing an auto biographical sketch for J. T. White \+ Co. I went out for a short walk but very soon returned because of the cold \+ wind \- Dinner at 2 \- in the afternoon I had a long nap \- then I went to studio to look after the fire \- Tea at 6.30 \- Evening I read aloud awhile to Ralph \+ then wrote to Mr. W.m Beach Olmsted, Head Master at Pomfret School \- \+ to London Punch, enquiring if it is possible to get vols 20\-31 to complete my set.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 26, 1900

**Weather:** clear, cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- to studio at 9.30 \+ spent the m'f. working on plates preparing to print to-morrow \- most of the time filing bevels on margins or edges. After 12 I went to 2d Nat'l. \+ deposited Maynard's check \- on my way home stopped at Avery's \+ saw Vedder's exhibit of Paintings, bas Reliefs etc \- strong work but excessively mannered \+ conventionalized Lunch \- then to studio \+ continued my work on Mad. Sq. Tower \+ other plates until after 5 \- Home at 6 \- dinner \+ a loafing evening in front of a pleasant fire.
