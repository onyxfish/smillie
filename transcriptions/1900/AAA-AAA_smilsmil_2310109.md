## Left Page

### Sat. July 21, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very humid with showers but clearing delightfully in the afternoon — Lue f. still, cool + cloudless. I got up late after a night of indifferent sleep. We had a satisfactory bkft. at about 9 — for the rest of the m't I was kept busy interviewing sellers of meats, vegetables, etc — + looking after the carpenters + painters — After 1 we had a good dinner — then I had a nap. About 5 Katherine, Jim, Ralph + I went through Ghost's Walk + around by the mails — supper after 6.30 — Lue f. on piazza + in large room until 10 —

## Right Page

### Sun. July 22, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day, clear, breezy, not overwarm my watch being on a spree I don't know at what time I got up — probably about 7, had my bath — bath sun + water — but it was after 9 when I had bkft., Kate having trouble with the coal + range. Hyer + his wife came with milk about 8 o'clk. I had some exercise up a pine tree cutting out dead wood — then I wrote a lot of letters — among them one to Ryan telling him to go ahead + fix up the 2d. story water-closet + connections according to his specifications + estimate for $245. I did not get thro' with my letter writing until after 2 — then I hurried to hotel + mailed them at 2.30 + back to a late dinner. After that I had a nap + at about 5 o'clk Katherine + Bessie, the boys + I went on a walk, to Windy Row, Ghost's Walk + back over road \+ golf links. Supper after 7 — Lue f. I did some more writing, business that has accumulated. To bed at about 10.
