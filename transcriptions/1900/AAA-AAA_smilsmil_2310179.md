## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 10, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cold winter day but clear \+ quiet. To studio before 10 \+ painted all day on a small panel, "Catskri from near Napls" same subject as yesterdays work, but smaller \+ different in composition \+ color scheme \- if it can claim to have a color scheme. I am not sure that, in its best sense, I know what color is \- I am not sure that my color efforts are intelligent \+ in the right direction \- I have found amusement \+ encouragement in my work of the past two days but as I took a last look at it this eve before leaving the studio the thought came to me that I was tricky \+ amaries cent only of the clever work of others \+ not an outcome of perception \+ truth \- \+ that, after two days of fun, was depressing. I had a half hour back before lunch on the sunny side of Park ave. \- at 6 I went to Liners [illegible] \-18' S. \+ 3' Ave. \+ bought some material for etching class \- then home \- dinner at 6.30 \- Evetg at 8 \- for a few minutes at Edw Cooke's \- he kindly [illegible] to advance $5.00 from Ireland Real Estate fund \- now in back \- but not enough there from which to pay a dividend. \- Then home \-

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 11, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Nasty raw day ending in light snow. After bkfs. I went to Dr. Yale's \+ was vaccinated \- got to studio soon after 10 \+ for some time painted on the little Catskri panel 8×15". It is not as good as I thought it was yesterday \- there is little to commend in color or composition \- Home to lunch as usual \- P. m. painted awhile on sketch 9¼×11⅞. Evetg. Raquette Lake \- a rough thing that dashed off three of four years ago \+ from which I scraped a mezzotint plate that I cannot find now. It is shinted \+, I think, has some good color. I shall try to finish it with as little change as possible. \- Dinner at 6.30 Evetg little else besides reading news papers \- a useless waste of time.
