## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 11, 1900

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

It was 10 o'clk. before I got my bkft- \+ after 11 when R- \+ I went up to Centrale Park - we wandered about there \+ got home at 2 - just in time for dinner - I had a long nap in the afternoon \+ tea at 6.30 - Eve'g. after Reading aloud awhiles to Ralph I wrote a note to Mr. Olmsted asking him for some information concerning Jim's progress at school - \+ then I wrote part of an autobiographical sketch for J. T. White \+ Co.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 12, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day with steady rain after 6. p.m. I spent most of the m'f making two small pen sketches, portrait of self, for J. T. White \+ Co. \+ then worked for an hour more on plate of Mr. A. head \+ then home to lunch - Men at work on fire escape altering it as I wished - Back to studio at 2.30 \+ worked on plate till near 5. I had thought of going to annual meeting \+ Weet. Mus. of Art but when the time came, 4 P.M., preferred to stick to my work. At 5 I went to the Exstn. of hus? Paeement Bldg at the old Sherry Belf, 5th Ave, \+ 37th 5t. \+ found it very interesting - Home after 6 - had to run - caught in the rain - Dinner at 6.30 Eve'g until 9. with newspapers \+ loafing
