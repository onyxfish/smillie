## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 9, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A charming day - followed by a beautiful moonlight night. I awoke before 6 o'clk. with an exceedingly sore head - Rheumatic, I think - but it passed away during the day - last night again I dreamed of Annie + that she was estranged from me - I have dreamed something of like character several times since I came here + it is depressing to me. I had my bath - + then my bkft. at 9 - The family seem to be demoralized - Mary + Kate are under the weather with stomach + bowel troubles + Charl complains of stomach pains + unusual pain + stiffness in his back. I think he took cold on the piazza last night. I worked part of the m'f on plan of East wall, main room on making up a bill of lumber needed to finish the main room + studio. About 1 - with Jim T Ralph went to hotel for mail. Dinner at 2. About 3.30 Jim, Ralph + I went to the old Dry trail + by it to the top of a ledge overlooking the Otis house - I had one of Charl's cameras + took a shot at Giant + Roaring Brook fall - Then I climbed a small Hemlock + took a snap shot at Pine Knoll - home before 6 - Every. much time on south piazza - moonlight effects very lovely - the vague misty mountains - the sombre pines - the ghostly white birches - I could hear plainly the congregation at D'. Dubois' sing + when a dear old hymn, sung here years ago by Mr. Colman, Cortena + the [illegible] girls - came floating through the shimmering birches to me, it made me profoundly sad.

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 10, 1900

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A bright m'g, but growing sultrier every hour, until night - a few drops of rain about 4.30 P.m. See hands late in the m'g. - Mary quite under the weather with the prevailing bowel trouble. Charl, very stiff in the back + complaining. I was busy all the m'f. writing out my order for lumber to Prescott + son. It covers all the material needed to finish main room - studio - extra doors, a sash, window blinds, etc - etc. Dinner at 1 - went to hotel at 2 o'clk. on my return wandered about awhile with Jim + Ralph - Every had a fine in main room - Reading till 10 o'clk.
