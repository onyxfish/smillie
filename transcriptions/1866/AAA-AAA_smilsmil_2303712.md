## Left Page

### Friday, February 23, 1866.

Fine day — Painted (14th day)
on academy picture — painted
in the meadows in the middle
distance for the second time
and the distant trees about
the middle of the picture.
Think that I have much
improved the color, though
now on the other hand Running
to brown. Eveᵞ went to
Mr. Greatorep's & joined in
Reading 2ᵈ part, Henry VI.
I took the part of Suffolk.
After the Reading a little
Singing. — G. & I got home
after 11 o'clk —

## Right Page

### Saturday, February 24, 1866.

Disagreeable change in the
weather — Threatening all the
mᵍ. Raining in earnest all the
afternoon & night. Painted
(15th day) on Academy picture, on
middle trees in middle ground
but did little as I was much
interrupted. Mrs. Choate came
to take her first lesson. She
commenced copying a painting
of mine 6×10 in. Katy G. took
her 11th lesson. Afternoon
went to Philharmonic's Rehearsal
there met Miss Dunning and
after Rehearsal went to see
the Brady war Photographs.
Eveᵞ it Rained so heavily
that we did not go out to the
Bellevue Hospital Med. School
commencement at Academy
of music. I stayed at Studio &
made charcoal sketch from
Georgi's head.
