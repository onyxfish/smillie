## Left Page

### Wednesday, December 12, 1866.

Clear cold winty day
Mrng, went to Schmitt's, to
see some furniture & then
to the Studio, Returning home
to lunch — At 2.45 P.m Rode
down to Walker St. to see Mary
M C Slvane — to get her to come
to the house to do some scrubbing.
From there Rode on down town
& called at A.B.N.Co. Saw Gavit
& got my $60.00 for drawing Check
Head. A little talk about
drawings & engraving but nothing
accomplished. Saw Will &
Josie for a few minutes.
Got home just about dusk.
Pretty well tired & chilled.

## Right Page

### Thursday, December 13, 1866.

Another cold day — feel quite
sore, as though I had been
hammered, from head to foot.
Took cold yesterday. Went
to Studio in mf, set palette
& painted over distance on 10×14
"October" — trying to get it more
melting — Afternoon — went down
to Durning's to see Fred. Durand
to make an appointment with him
to see to my Remaining teeth. — Saw
him — he set Next Tues. at 3.15 P.M. —
Called in at Schauss's — Knoedecker's
Knoedler's & Lied's — Saw Lied's
& made an appointment to meet
him at the Studio next Sat. mf. to
arrange for the sale by auction of
my 10×14, "October".Ped home
tired out again — Eve. Mr Morrell
upon Farker's invitation spent the
evef at the house.
