## Left Page

### Monday, May 14, 1866

Very fine day, clear & cool.
Worked all day (2nd day)
on little picture for Mr.
Lewis B. Reed. I think
he will like this one. — It
is made up upon the old
Recipe. A bit of distance,
a Rock in the Right hand
Cor. with two silver birches
Catching a spanoing light
— I hope to get beyond
doing such things presently.
Eve[ning] at home.
Miss Kate Drinker called
at the Studio with two
lady friends.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, May 15, 1866

Splendid clear day, but
windy — Mng. Painted a little
sketch about 9½ × 7 in. "On
the Delaware — Near Easton".
Afternoon painted sky
(— day) upon Mr. Harris's
picture "On the Delaware"
10½ × 18 — This is painted
over a thin buff painting
that I put over the entire
surface — So that it is almost
the same as beginning it anew.
Ella Bourne & Louisa Dua.
called at the Studio in
the afternoon.
Eve[ning] at home.
