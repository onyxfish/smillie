## Left Page

### Wednesday, April 18, 1866

Overcast with a little
spit of Rain toward every
Still continue "powerfully
weak" and Spring-like —
Commenced with the
brush, upon the Guano
drawing making but
little Progress.
Father Still continues
quite unwell, with a
Strange eruption upon
his back, under his
left arm + upon his
breast. The Dr. Calls
it Tetter.

## Right Page

### Thursday, April 19, 1866

Mild day — one of those
pleasant weakeners that
draws the Starch from
every joint. Worked
a little again upon the
Guano drawing — I
have not energy enough
to do half work —
Father Still continues
unable to go down-town,
+ complaining of Soreness
in his back + irritation
from the eruption upon
his back and Side.
Every at home, mounted
Guano Drawing.
