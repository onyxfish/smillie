## Left Page

### Tuesday, May 22, 1866

Clear, warm day — worked
(2nd day) on Mr. Harris's pic.
finishing the painting in.
Evef., Geo. and I called
upon Miss Ida Ogilvie at
No. 378 W. 32d St. The Father,
Mother, Brother and Sister
Sail for Europe a week from
next Sat. to Stay an indefinite
length of time.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, May 23, 1866

Cloudy and Chilly — also
windy — quite Marchy. Worked
(1st day) on drawing of Denver
City, Colorado. Reduced it by
Camera-lucida from large one.

Afternoon, finished up little
Sketch 5½ × 7 in. for Miss Gordon
+ worked awhile on M. Smith's
morning — Kate Drinker + Mr.
Holt called.

Father + Geo. went up by barge
to Pok eepsie this p.m. Father,
by invitation of Aunt Agnes, to
recuperate for a week, or ten
days. Geo. to Return to morrow
or the day after —

Evef. at home, writing to
Miss Dunning.
