## Left Page

### Tuesday, May 8, 1866

Fine day — worked (8th day) upon Ottawa Bldgs
with India Ink — which works but indifferently
upon the Bristol Board.
In the eveng Mr. Wm
Olana Bourne called at
the House with her daughter
Ella & Mr. McMaxwell to bid
Uncle Frank good-bye,
as he starts for Ottawa
to-morrow mrg.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, May 9, 1866

Chilly mrg and threatening
Rain — Uncle Aaron called
at the Studio between 9 and
10 o'clk. AM & hurried me off
down-town to attend Court,
a Suit being expected that
might reap orders a $2500.00
Check that I Rec. last Janth.
Went down & found the case
postponed for one week — Did
up some "chores" & Ret. to the
Studio about noon — Worked
upon Ottawa Bldgs. (9th day.)
About 5 o'clk — Fairman
came in — we stripped to
the Skin, down to the waist
& had at least an hour with
"the gloves" — Geo went Riding
with Mr. Capscut. Spent the
eveng with George at Mr.
Tracy Harris's No. 46 W. 46th St.
Mr. H. has just Ret. from
South Carolina.
Uncle Frank got off this mrg.
