## Left Page

### Monday, September 17, 1866

Very fine day. In bed with a miserable headache
until between two three o'clk. Every. Mr. Wm Main spent
with us — and all night —
Every thing in turmoil and confusion. Joe Thill &
Aunt Margaret came in also, for a time Dorman.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, September 18, 1866

Muggy, threatening — went to Studio — then to the
barbers & enjoyed barberizing &
shampooing. Then down-town
stopping on many messages.
Called at Jouis [illegible] Meusei's and
got check for $72.00 being
$36 each for Jel. & self, for
two 7×12's that me [illegible] painted
for him last Spring. Saw Father
at N.B.N. Co — he took dinner with me.
Called upon A.B.N. Co in their new
quarters & was shown about.
Returning home at 4 o'clk commenced
packing to get off to-morrow evely,
but in the evely. Jel. Saw Mr. Harris
who is going to Montrose Thursday, so
it was determined to wait over one
day for him. I Regret it.
