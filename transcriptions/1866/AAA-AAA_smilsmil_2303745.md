## Left Page

### Sunday, May 6, 1866

Superb day. We went
to Dr. Armytage's in 46th St.
and saw 8 young girls &
two women baptized. —
One of the girls was but 11
years of age — D. B's sermon
seemed to me to be but a
labored excuse for baptizing
very young persons. That
it may be wise sometimes, I
have no doubt — but as a general
thing it must be dangerous.
Afternoon, took a stroll
away up to the foot of 70th St.
& Hudson River — enjoyed
it very much. Were at
home.

## Right Page

### Monday, May 7, 1866

Weather, a fair continuation
of yesterday. Worked (9th day)
& finished finally the out-
line of Ottawa bldg.
