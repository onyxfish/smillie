## Left Page

### Tuesday, February 27, 1866.

Every thing white with snow when I got up this mng.
Unpleasant nearly all day, but cleared away late with
delightful moonlight night —
Worked my Sky in the mng
(17th day) with a will — Repainting
it all Solid & think it decidedly
better, although it inclines to
Red and is too full of drawing
but these defects I think I
easily Remedied — Every
at Studio,

## Right Page

### Wednesday, February 28, 1866.

Threatening weather in the mng.
but cleared toward evng with
a glorious moonlight night.

Worked, glazing and painting
over my foreground, Academy
picture (18th day) which I have
made very much stronger. I
am now Rather pleased with the
quality of the color. The general
effect of the picture very disjointed,
but on the whole, to me encouraging.

Evng Gel and I spent very
pleasantly with Miss Fanny
Kimball — 46. W. 22d. St.
