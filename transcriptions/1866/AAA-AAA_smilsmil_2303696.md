## Left Page

### Monday, January 22, 1866

Fine day. Worked all day (3d day) on "Smash Ups" drawing
with the Coarse grained, low-toned Creswick paper
that I have, my drawings turn out rather sooty and
want clearness. I am disappointed in this last
drawing — as a Composition it is badly constructed. I
have to get ups all of these things "to pay" & with too much
haste. Every Geo. & I went to Mr G.P. Putnam's, it
being one of his Reception evenings to meet a certain live Baron
who has been making some very interesting experiments &
discoveries in Photographic Engraving — We left at 10
o'clk — he arrived after.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, January 23, 1866

Dull day, towards every evening.
Mr F worked a little on "Smash Up"
with white — also on outlining
day of "Beaver" for Uncle Willie
also, a little on Sheep die for
Continental Bk. Note Co — &
late in the afternoon char-
coaled in a little subject
6×10 in. for Mr. Gratorex.
Between 5 & 6 o'clk Mr. Shepard
called to see the drawing.
Every went to see Carry about
mattrass for Father to sleep
upon at the Studio — as we
expect to furnish him a
shelter, probably until next
May.
