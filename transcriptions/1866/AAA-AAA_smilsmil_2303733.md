## Left Page

### Tuesday, April 10, 1866

Another great day — worked on "Under the tough" — 7×12 in.
(3rd day) Graying the whole
thing, it is now much
more delicate in color,
but monotonous — green —
green — green — &still tending
to heaviness.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, April 11, 1866

warm and Spring-like
still — Painted (2nd day) on
6×10 in. for S. R. Menger —
I called it finished — all but
a little scumble on this
painting over sky or distance.
Eveg. at home.
