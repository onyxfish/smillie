## Left Page

### Saturday, May 12, 1866.

And yet again. Some
more not. I worked (12th day)
most of the day finishing
up the Ottawa Building.
I hope that at last I
am done with the drawing.
It has been extremely
tedious to me. Katy J.
took her 21st lesson.
Lewis B. Reed Jun. called
during the p.m. About
5.30 o'clk went to N.A.R.D.
Evey, found Kate at
home, just Rec'd from Montrea[l]
— with Geo. Spent an hour
at Mr. Harris Pardee's, No. 10
W. 47° St.

## Right Page

### Sunday, May 13, 1866.

Very warm &sultry — Went
to near Hastings — 42d Street.
Text I Kings. 20th Chap. 40th verse.
"And as thy servant was busy
here and there, he was gone."
Lesson — not to busy ourselves
with too many objects, lest our
main duty be neglected. The
discourse was good, but H —
does not impress me favorably.
He seems to me very intellectual
but unsympathetic & undevotional.
Afternoon went to bed with a
headache — at about 5.30
o'clk a grand wind & thunder
storm came up — half a dozen
fine trees in Madison Square
were upset — Evey at home
singing & chatting.
