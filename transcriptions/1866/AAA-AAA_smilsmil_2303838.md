## Left Page

### Wednesday, November 14, 1866

Up at a little after 7 — Paced the piazza until 8.15, then bkft.
mry quite mild & slightly overcast. With Charl, took 7/4 before
train — got to studio at 9.25 —
Worked all day (2d day) on Locomotive drawing — Home to
dinner at one. Between 5 & 6 o'clk. walked down to Snedecor
& got my little 9×12 pic. "Eagles
Nest" & took it to the studio —
Eveg. Geo went to Mr Martins
21st St. I spent the eveg between
home & Fleming's — Dr. Throop's
called — also Dan Sayre and
Ben Richards, Reporting that
S. C. F. came from Montrose
with them yesterday.

## Right Page

### Thursday, November 15, 1866

Miserable day, Raw, windy, Rainy
day — Commenced work again on
"October" 10×14 in (5th day) Scumbled
& painted over all the distance, glazed
over portions of foreground & Central
tree — fancy that now it is worth
twice as much as before & has more
& better color in it than anything
that I have before done. Eveg —
took home, on the drawing boards
my Locomotive & Sheep's head to
show to Father — Spent the eveg
taking down my big book case and
transferring books & all into front
room so as to give a chance to
scrub out my stable — Painters
& frescoes being now done with
my Room, I am beginning to look
forward to a Return of order —
S. C. F. took lunch at home
with many.
