## Left Page

### Tuesday, November 20, 1866

Pain at last, but it could not keep it up through the day —
night a glorious moonlight.

Worked all day (2nd day) on 10×18 "October Afternoon", for Mr. Mivans —
Putted Rich heavy glaze of Mars Yellow, Cadmium + Madder Lake over sky + distance, then painted solid right into it. Got the sky and distance in color + aerial effect better than anything that I have done yet. Eve'g, Ge must up to the Diven's. With the help of Kate I got my file of newspapers during the Rebellion nearly sorted + arranged. Worked at it until after 11 o'clk. There is now a prospect of getting my Room in order. Mary + Mother got home during the day. Mother says that now she is home for the winter.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, November 21, 1866

Pleasant day. At home until after 10 o'clk, misty fresco men.
At Studio, worked again (3rd day) on 10/×18 — finishing 2nd painting.
I am now quite pleased. The whole thing is so grey + yet has so much color, that, altho' far from satisfied, I feel sure that I am making progress.

Eve'g found Room at home scrubbed, so set to work to put down my carpet. George gave me some help, their Charl. but I could not master one corner, got it all down but that + at after 11 o'clk got to bed.
