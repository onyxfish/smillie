## Left Page

### Sunday, June 3, 1866

Heavy Rain all day. So kept the house, except
in the m'g, when I went
in to the little Congregational
Church on our block + heard
young Abbott, for the first
time — not very favorably
impressed — Subject —
Prayer — the necessity of
Prayer + how to Pray —

Wrote to Uncle Willie
Josie Bentley + Fanny Mulford

## Right Page

### Monday, June 4, 1866

Some Rain in the morning
+ so Chilly in the Studio that
I kindled a small fire —
By noon however, the weather
had much modified. Worked
half a day (2½ days) on the
little wood bit — + spent the
Rest of the day finishing up
Mr Harris's "On the Delaware"
and on a Sketch by Warren,
(giving the Sky) that belongs
to Mrs Greathex. Between
1 & 2 o'clk. Dr Throop called
+ I went with him to see an
operation (dental) performed
by Dr White, using Nitrous
Oxide Gas — but the patient
did n't come — but was
interested looking about —
Every. had a confounded
sick turn.
