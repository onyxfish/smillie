## Left Page

### Monday, November 12, 1866

Up before 7 o'clk. Pleasant m'f. Charl + I played Billiards. My Sunday ev'g lesson did me good service, as on the first game that I ever played I stood 96 to his 100. B'kft a few minutes past 8 at ¼ before 9. Charl + I took cars at [illegible] Riv. R.R. got into Station at 30 [illegible]. at 9 o'clk 8 m. walked over to studio in 10 m. got fire kindled + Room swept before [illegible] arr. Spent m'f in looking over my pencil studies. Getting them etc, etc. Home to dinner at 1.
P.M. Roughly blocked in a drawing of Locomotive, Train + Etching for Uncle Willie — Ev'g Gee and I went to see Mr. + Mrs. Jas. R. Brevoort. Car. B'd way + 28 [illegible]. Spent the evening until 10 o'clk. Arrangements made at home to see the "shooting stars" if they "go off" — Glorious clear night.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, November 13, 1866

Fine day — no meteors seen by us last night. On my way to Studio bought a copy of Don Quixott. Ill. by G. Doré — Spent most of the morning studying up my purchase. — Home to dinner at 1 o'clk. All the afternoon finishing outline of Locomotive vict drawing (2 half days, or one day) Ev'g on going home to tea found Mother late. Mother has been looking for carpets all day. — Charl + I met up with them in the ¼ before 8 stage to Manhattanville, arriving there at 8.30. Ev'g glorious with young moon. By 9 o'clk Charl + I got to work at Billiards, and, keeping watch of course, for the shower of Meteors, we kept it up until one o'clk — then to bed —
