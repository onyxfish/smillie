## Left Page

### Wednesday, April 4, 1866

Clear, warm, Spring like.
Quite used up with a
headache — Spent the m'g
on my back — afternoon
Rallied & charcoaled upon
16×22 in. "View on the Hudson"
intended for Mr. Dodge, and
on one or two other little things.

## Right Page

### Thursday, April 5, 1866

Mng overcast & threatening,
but soon cleared to be an-
other warm day — Still
very much under the weather
& unfit for work — Got out
a little. Canvas on stretcher
5¾×8 in., the half of an old
8×13 painting — the other
half Uncle Willie has. This
half I worked on during
the day, intending if successful
to present it to [illegible] Will.
Meta Gavit called at Studio
inviting us to her house to-
morrow eve'g. Had quite
a jolly call —
Evening at home.
