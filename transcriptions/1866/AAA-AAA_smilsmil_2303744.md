## Left Page

### Friday, May 4, 1866

Fine day — 5th day, commenced outlining buildings upon the board. After handling the brush off-hand this sort of work is extremely irksome & tedious. Did not get it more than a quarter outlined —
Evening at home —

## Right Page

### Saturday, May 5, 1866

Another fine day. Worked all day, & quite closely (6th day) outlining Ottawa Bldg!, but did not succeed in getting it finished — A charming job, pecuniarily, artistically, & any other way — Aunt Huldah started for Montrose this mg.
Eveg at home.
Katy J. took her 20th lesson
