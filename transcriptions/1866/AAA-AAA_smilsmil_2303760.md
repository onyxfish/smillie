## Left Page

### Monday, June 11, 1866.

Clear & very warm — a real live Summer day — Painted for the last time over sky on Mr. Harris's picture —
I, now call it done —
oiled out Mr. Earle's 10×14
& his 9×15 — working a little on each — Mrs. Choate
called to bid us good bye
& Katy G. came in the p.m
for a few minutes to finish
her last drawing — at
4.15 went down town to see
Mr. Menger — but failed —
did up some little
messages — Every after
9 o'clk went down to Mr.
Mr. Marten's. 155. W. 21 st.

## Right Page

### Tuesday, June 12, 1866.

Very fine day — clear & warm.
Uncle Willie and his mrs from
Ottawa & Pkeepsie — Went with
him to the Studio — he saw his
drawing of the Parliament Bldg
& expressed himself pleased.
At about 1 o'clk I was obliged
to give up with a sick headache
& go home to bed, where I spent
all the afternoon asleep —
In the mrf. Mr. Greatorey
agreed to buy my little wood
bit that I finished a few days
ago — 7½ × 10¾ for a birth-day
present to Katy G. She will
pay me $40.00 for it next
Sept.
