## Left Page

### Wednesday, March 21, 1866.

Miserable day — breaking away
in a warm, foggy even —
Painted (2nd day) on Acad. pic.
finishing the opposite bank
— Think that it looks tolerably
sunny.

Eveng at the Studio.

## Right Page

### Thursday, March 22, 1866.

Beautiful day — Went went
to No. 3 Chambers St. to see
Mr. Jay Con, Commissioner of
Jurors, to make arrangements
with him about paying my $100 —
fine, as a delinquent — On
my swearing that no summons
had been served up on me —
that I was a poor devil artist
— sick, at the time summoned
& not worth $250 — I was excused.
On Returning to the Studio a
headache got the better of
me, and for the rest of the day
I was on my back.
