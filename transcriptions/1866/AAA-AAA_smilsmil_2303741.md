## Left Page

### Saturday, April 28, 1866.

Beautiful day — Found aunt Huldah at the breakfast table — arr'd this morn on the boat from Catskill. Mr. Choate took her 9th lesson + Katy 9 —, her 19th. I did little but attend to them until the afternoon when I got out an old canvas 6½×9 with a Catskill Mt Cascade subject upon it, which I fixed up almost good enough to give away.

## Right Page

### Sunday, April 29, 1866.

Cloudless very high wind, utterly used up with a tremendous tearing nervous headache.
