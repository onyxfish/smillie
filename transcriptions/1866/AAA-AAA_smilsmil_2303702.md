## Left Page

### Saturday, February 3, 1866.

Still a fine day with April
characteristics overhead,
fitful & squally. Pitched
into big picture again (10th day)
painting foreground trees
on left. Very anxious to work,
but fighting with a headache
at about noon. Mother & Kate
came in from Poughkeepsie —
I kept up until about 2 o'clk.
when the headache floored
me. Suffered a teaser
until after 10 o'clk P.M.
P. C. F. sent in tickets to
22? Reg't Band Concert, but
could not use them & [illegible]
failed to find any one else that
could. Ev'g, was told that
Adelaide Dunning has broken
off her engagement with Mr.
Effinger.
Katy G— took 9th lesson.

## Right Page

### Sunday, February 4, 1866.

Clear, cold very windy day —
Did not go to bed last night
until 4 o'clk this morning.
So to-day feel used up from
yesterday's excessive indulgence
in headache — Took breakfast
& dinner with Mrs Myckoff —
Father, Mother & Kate are
staying here (No. 20 W. 4th) by
invitation, so that we have quite
a family party. Ev'g, started
to go out to tea but was met and
detained by Mrs W. So took
my three meals & did not
stir out of the house. — Ev'g
wrote to P. C. F.
