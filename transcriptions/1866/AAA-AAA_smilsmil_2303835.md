## Left Page

### Thursday, November 8, 1866

Another delightful mild day — after some time with fresco [illegible] went to Studio, worked with tolerable faithfulness all day (3d day) on Sheep's head drawing for A.B.N. [illegible] got it nearly done + think that it will be creditable to me. Home to dinner at 1 o'clk — to the Studio at 2.50. Everf Charl, Mary + Matt went to hear Parepa sing at Steinway's new Hall — I spent the evening writing to Uncle Wm Turrill.

## Right Page

### Friday, November 9, 1866

Still mild but threatening rain — a little dash of which we had towards noon — Set my palette for the first time since coming home + worked (3d day) on 10×14 "October" that I painted in, in Montrose. Rubbed a very warm scumble into the sky, glazed the distance + painted into it — worked feeling very unlike it; stupid + headachy — Home to dinner at 1 o'clk — P.M. still at work on 10×14 — George went up to Uncle Aaron's to stay all night at 11 o'clk — Everf I stayed at home, + wrote a letter to Sarah Fessenden.
