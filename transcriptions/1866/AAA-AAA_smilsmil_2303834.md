## Left Page

### Tuesday, November 6, 1866

Father waked us before day light — almost to get up & go with him to vote. Up, & a little after 7 — Geo. & I went with him to 511 7th ave — he, to cast his virgin vote — beautiful mrng, but cold — kicked & stamped for more than half an hour, before our turn came. Went to Studio at about 9 o'clk — with Fergus, went to Artists Fund [Elp?] — Varnishing day — there until 11 o'clk — to Studio & stretched two pieces of paper for drawing — Mr. Breevort called in the mrng. Home to dinner at 1 — Studio at 2.30 — Pottering around, in & out of the Studio. About 4.30, Amanda Dixon, Sister Mary & her friend called. At 5.30, after Maud, had mended a rip in the carpet, walked up with them, leaving Mary & friend at Mr. Corey's in 33d St. Went to 52? with Geo & Maud, then home to tea. — Evng spent at home, writing. Father went up to Uncle Aaron's & stayed all night.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, November 7, 1866

A glorious Indian Summer day. Late again at Studio owing to fresco painter at home. Morning made Camera-lucida outline of Lamb's head, of which I am to make a drawing for A.B.N. Co. — Went home to dinner at 1 o'clk. — P.M. worked with pencil over drawing, getting it ready for the India Ink. A very small day's work — not more than half a day. Mr. Breevort called and so did Jeel —

Evng. Went at 8 o'clk to Harvard Rooms Cor. 43d St & 6th ave, to see some light Gymnastics — at 9 o'clk went with Geo to private view of Artists Fund Socty. — Quite a brilliant affair — Met Cunning Ireland, Addie Dunning & her Father — young ladies surrendered to us & we took charge of them the rest of the evng. Geo. having Addie & I, Emma — Left at 11 o'clk among the very last. Mother came down from Uncle A's in the mrng. Returning again in the afternoon.
