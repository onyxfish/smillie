## Left Page

### Wednesday, March 7, 1866.

Another high windy day. Worked about an hour in
the mrf on the little 6×10 in
picture that I painted for
Mrs Greatorex. Then painted
in (first day) "Under the Bough"
7× 12¼ in. Commissioned by
Mr Meuger. Am rather
pleased with my days work.
Evelf Charl came up
& took tea at the Studio
with Father. Spent
the eveng at Studio,
Reading

## Right Page

### Thursday, March 8, 1866.

Still blustering weather and
quite cold. Painted all
day (2d day) on little picture
Round top = Wood bit for Mr.
Lewis B Reed — In some
respects am pleased, but the
color of the whole verges
too closely upon a blackish
tinge — Evening at Studio
fixing up, writing to Mother
etc, etc.
