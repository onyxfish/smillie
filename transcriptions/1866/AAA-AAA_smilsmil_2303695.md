## Left Page

### Saturday, January 20, 1866

Very disagreeable day, mist
mizzle & rain. Drew (2° day)
on Smash Up drawing.
Katy 9 — took her 7 th lesson.
Eveng — at home.

## Right Page

### Sunday, January 21, 1866

Clear, cold & windy. In the
mrg went to J. P. Thompson's
& heard a beggar in behalf of
the Seamen — Either he was
intolerably prosy, or I was un-
usually sleepy. At 12.15 he
closed his discourse — Took
dinner by invitation with
Mr & Mrs Myckoff — Afternoon
& evening wrote — one letter
to Sister Mary & one to b. C. F.
