## Left Page

### Friday, August 24, 1866.

Cold, blue, blustering day,
mf, walking chopping wind + stfst.
until 9 o'clk — Too cold, cloudy, wet
\+ windy to work out of doors, so set to
work + painted a new sky on oat
field study. Am not pleased — it
is not delicate enough in form & color.
Just after dinner went over to Dr. D's
to get Addie + Emma to go with us
this p.m. to Rattlesnake Mt. Found them
just beginning a game of Croquet —
took a hand + understood that at the
end of the game they would go with
me — before the game was over they
announced their determination to Ride —
whereat I was veyed — + they Rode —
game left unfinished — P[l]P home,
got India Ink Sketch + took it to Miss
Ellen Tappan — got colt + Rode from
5.15 until nearly 6. After tea, by invitation
we went to Mr. Packermans to hear
music — The Misses Loop + the Dummys
\+ their guests there — Very informal — Music
violin + piano — Two of Mozarts Sonatas +
some Chopins + Schuberts — very good —
jolly walk home — glorious moonlight — bid 12. m[?]

## Right Page

### Saturday, August 25, 1866.

Clear, cold, cloudless mf — Ther.
47° at 6. a.m. On the Road walking
at 6.30 Met Addie, Emma + Charley D.
on horseback. Between 8 + 9 o'clk got to
work again upon 14×24, Elms (16" mf) painted
over distant trees, l.h. side. A little before 12.
Addie, Emma, Rosalie Goodman, Miss Cheney, Mr +
Mr. Ireland, Georgy S. + Geo. Hawes made us a
visit — Geo. was at work near me under the Hemlocks —
we had a very pleasant visit of half an hour.
Afternoon cold + cloudy again. Took pencils
\+ paper + went over to Higginson's hill on Mt
Stockbridge Road + made outline sketch
of Lake Mahkeenac from the North. Suffered
from wind + cold. Eve[?], as it promised
fine 'twas night of the full moon. at 8.30 went
with Geo. over to the Dr's to get the girls to go
out with us for a moonlight walk as agreed
upon in the mf. At nearly started out —
night glorious, cold + clear with a few clouds.
Walked over the hill, past Mr. Smith's + on down
to the Lake — then home — a walk for the girls
of nearly 3 miles + for us, over 4. Enjoyed it
all — walked out with Emma. Returned
home with Addie. — Girls in first rate
humor.
