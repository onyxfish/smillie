## Left Page

### Wednesday, July 25, 1866.

Warm morning and cloudy — went to work upon Elms (14×24) + painted in the Chestnut tree on the extreme right hand side — worked with comparative comfort, as the sun troubled me but little — a thunder storm came up and drove me off a few minutes before 12. We got however, but a few drops — While at dinner Addie + Emma drove up + invited Geo + me to tea at their house this evng + all to go together to spend the evng at Mr. Goodman's as p. invitation — Heavy storm in the afternoon, so I worked indoors upon a small sketch that I intend making a Philopena present to Emma. at 5 o'clk the storm had passed, but was very muddy — Prepared + Reached the D's a little after 6 — tea, after 7 — then Addie excused herself from going, + Geo, Hawes + Emma lead, followed by Oscar D, Ned D, Geo + I — passed a very pleasant evng at Mr. G's — Enjoying the company of Miss Rosalie — with no dog to share it — Had a conversation with Emma that tended to improve my humor — The Kimball's, besides ourselves constituted the invited company. Left for home at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Thursday, July 26, 1866.

Fine day, but sick — Confoundedly sick — sick all night, sick in the mrng and sick all day — bah — boiled veal likely, with gooseberries — Spent part of the mrng in bed — at dinner, Mrs. P. had kindly prepared for me a little chicken broth — Afternoon, very weak uncomfortable — hung about, + sat about in the neighborhood of the house, slight showers with cloud effects making charming pictures. Evng, still improving, but not able to straighten myself out.

This mrng, early — Addie, Emma + Fanny K, with Ned, Oscar, + Geo. Hawes started for Worcester, Mass, to attend a college boat race and ball to-morrow — Charley D is stroke in the "Scientific" crew — Joy be with them.
