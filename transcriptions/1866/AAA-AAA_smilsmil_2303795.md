## Left Page

### MONDAY, AUGUST 20, 1866.

Bleak and windy morning —
Up early — walked & chopped wood
before bkfst. After — finished letter
to Mary — At about 8.30 o'clk Addie
& Rosalie Goodman called — Miss G.
invited Geo. & me to play Croquet with
her to-morrow P.M. at 4.30 o'clk —
Addie invited us to take tea with her
this eve'g. — both invitations graciously
accepted — After 10 ventured out
with pencils & paper — took outline of
an upright lake glimpse near Mr.
Bullard's barn — at 12 — Returning met
Addie under the Elms, at work —
P.M. — as it was tolerably clear, worked
again upon my outline shading in
distance — At 4 o'clk got colt & took
a short ride — he behaved wilfully —
Ret'd a little after 5. Geo. went with
Mr. Smith to Stockbridge — he had a call of
an hour or two upon "Emma J." — Enjoyed it.
At about 6 we prepared & went to the Dr's
— tea after 7 — then some good Beethoven
music — Singing by Geo. Hawes & the Dr.
and at 9.45 we started for home.
Charming moonlight night, but Cool.

## Right Page

### TUESDAY, AUGUST 21, 1866.

Up again bright & early & out ever since
before bkfast. Charming mist effects
& fog clearing off from the lake. At
8.45 under the Elms, again at work on
14×24 (15× mf) Between 9 & 10. Addie & Mr.
Davis came along. Addie to work. Finished
my elms — now have the Chestnut on R.h.
& distance on L.h. to finish — worked faithfully
all the m'g. — Glorious, bright, breezy & cloudless.
P.M. Clouded over — windy & sprinkling Rain.
Took out large Hemlock study & commenced
penciling on it (2d day) worked until 4 —
Ret'd to house & "fixed up" — At about 4.30
Geo. & I started for Mr. Goodman's, across
lots — Reached there in about 20 minutes.
Miss Rosalie & Miss Chevy commenced a
game with us at once. Before getting half
through, Miss Mackenzie & another Lady
joined us. We commenced a new game,
before we finished it Rain came up
& we scattered — we had one umbrella —
borrowed another & walked home, 2 miles,
through the Rain — did not get much
wet however — Hungry & tired, took
tea at 7.30 — Spent the evening Rocking
& talking & at near 10 o'clk went to bed.
