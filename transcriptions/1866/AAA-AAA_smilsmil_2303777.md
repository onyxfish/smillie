## Left Page

### Sunday, July 15, 1866.

An almost insufferable
day — heat intense, but a
delightful breeze blowing.
Threatened with a headache.
Spent the mf. lying at ease
with pillows etc, on the
front piazza. — Afternoon
in bed, used up — Every
upon my feet again and
enjoying the beautiful sun
set — Moon set, etc, etc —
Got to bed along towards
morning.

## Right Page

### Monday, July 16, 1866.

Ehuinger visited us this mf. while
we were sketching —

Another piping hot day. Up
at half past 5 o'clk. After
breakfast wrote a letter to
Father — About 9. went to work
at my elms. before getting fairly
started Addie & Emma came
up — As I had got Addie's study
into a sort of mess for her last
Friday — I spent the mf. getting
it into shape again for her.
Dr. D. went to N.Y. in the afternoon.
P.M. worked (5 day) on 12 × 18.
again painted over the sky.
— The clouds splendid — quite
considerable showers passing
around me & over me, so that I had
to take Refuge under my umbrella.
Am better pleased with today's work.
At about 6 o'clk Addie, Emma,
Geo. Hawes, Ned, the dog & I took a
jolly good bath in the lake.
— thinks I rather liked it —
Every, Geo. & I went over to
the Dr's. — Singing on the piazza
— Sky without a cloud — Every
Lovely —
