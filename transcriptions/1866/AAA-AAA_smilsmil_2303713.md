## Left Page

### Sunday, February 25, 1866.

A good winter day, cold
and breezy — Spent the
m'g. Rubbing, Scrubbing
\+ bathing — With Father +
Geo took dinner at Mr.
Tracy Harris' 46 W. 46 St.
Had a very pleasant time.
Afternoon, took a long walk
in the Park, alone — Eveg
went to Cooper Institute to
hear Frothingham the
Unitarian discourse upon
"The devout life" — He devoted
considerable time to lam-
pooning the Evangelical
Ideas of to-day — + wound up
by declaring Wendell Phillips
to be his pattern saint of to-day.
Walked home with Miss
Dunning,

## Right Page

### Monday, February 26, 1866.

Splendid day — Miss Dunning
came to draw in the morning
\+ took lunch with us. Am
in distress over my Academy
pic. Spent my 16th day over it —
The sky is so chalky and I
have not the pluck to repaint
it — Did little else than oil
out sky, Scrape it + rub it —
I have determined, if I feel
equal to it to-morrow — to
repaint it — Eveg, Jas.
\+ I spent with Miss Parson
Miss Mollie Biddle, Mrs. Lillie
Gillman very pleasantly —
Left a little after 10 o'clk.
