## Left Page

### Thursday, June 7, 1866.

a stifling sort of day —
Sunshine & heavy showers.
Mrng. painted again over
Mr. Harris's "Delawarore" sky
tearing away clouds and
opening up the blue. Then
took hold of Mr. Earle's
9×15 — put a thin painting
or scumble over sky &
distance, working down
towards the foreground —
(4th day) P.M. Went to Brook
Bros and ordered a suit of
blk clothes & bought a spring
overcoat. Jerusha Vant.
married at 3.15 o'clk P.M.
to — Grckler (?) mother
Mary, Kate, Will & wife went
over to attend the operation.
Evng. at home finishing
letter to Fanny Mulford.

## Right Page

### Friday, June 8, 1866.

Very warm. Mrng promising
to be clear — but it clouded
over & showered like an April
day — Ther. at the Studio 80° —
Worked all day (5th day) on
9×15 in for Mr. Earle — over
the Right hand piece and
foreground objects —
Evng. in at Willis,
cyphering up old Stock
accounts between us.
At 10 o'clk. P.M. more Rain.
