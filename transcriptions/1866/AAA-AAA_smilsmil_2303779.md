## Left Page

### Thursday, July 19, 1866.

Dull & lowering m'g. Ther. at 8 o'clk 58°. Spent ent'y m'g upon Lamb's Head
Study, working at it indoors, after having walked over to the D's with the umbrella
borrowed last ev'g. Found them all off on an Excursion to Mill River, for
Hay caps, a drive there & back of 44 miles.
Practiced up a little on Croquet, all
alone. By noon it cleared up — after
3 o'clk, not a cloud to be seen, except
in the far East. Gel & I took a tramp
down the Road to the Stockbridge
end of the Lake — every thing charming.
On Returning, went down to the pond
& gathered a lot of Lilies for the girls.
Ev'g. Mr. S. kindly offered me
the use of one of his horses & buggy —
accepted — Gel & I drove in to
town, leaving some lilies at D'r D's — taking
Remainder to Fanny K— Spent a very
pleasant ev'g with them until 9.30.
Returning, stopped again at D'r D's.
Left mail — found Party just in
from Mill River — 10 o'clk — all fagged
out — but gave us a good natured lemonade

## Right Page

### Friday, July 20, 1866.

Cloudy & pleasantly cool — Finished
Lamb's head Study — at 10. Addie came along
with a contribution from her garden — went
with her down to Sketching ground & there
found Emma & Gel. Did not get out my
oil study, as there was little or no sunlight.
Afternoon, entirely overcast. Took my
pencils & went out but accomplished
nothing. Returning washed up brushes
& things, fixed up, & at 5 — went by invitation
to D'r D's there to meet the Kimball's and
the Goodmans — Croquet — Fanny K. Oscar
J. Mr. Brown & self against Rosalie G. Rich.
G. Emma J. & Ned D— beaten, of course.
At a little before 8. took tea — pleasantly seated, flanked by Rosalie G. & Emma J.
— 17 at the table — After tea, Singing
"by the band." — Shared Miss Rosalie's
attention with the dog "Flush" — Met
Mr. Gel. Cheney, the engraver — half an
hour's chat with him — Company broke
up amid assurances of general, mutual
and individual happiness at 10.30 —
A very pleasant ev'g. have forgotten to note
several things in my diary, particularized by
Emma J —
