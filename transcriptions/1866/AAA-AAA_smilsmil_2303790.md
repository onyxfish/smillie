## Left Page

### Friday, August 10, 1866.

Clouds breaking away and a strident disposition to clear
up — even if it takes all day to
do it — Ther. at 7 o'clk, A.M.
56°. Too cold & wet to work out
of doors — finished cloud study
8×12 in. commenced sometime
ago — also, the little sketch of Mon-
ument Mt. for Addie. Afternoon
delightful — went over to the Dr. at
about 5 o'clk, but saw no one. Addie
off driving with Fred Durand, who came
from N.Y. with the Dr. this mtg. Early
in the eveg. (the invitation named
7.30) Geo. & I went over to Mrs. R.'s —
Met there Misses Loop (2). Carey (2)
Hooper, Haggerty & Sedgwick & Misses
C.A.B. risted — Ehninger — [illegible] Seay me
& one or two others — later in the eveg.
Addie then Bro. Charles & Geo. Hawes
came — a little after 10 — ices and
mices — then some light music —
duetts, W. R. & Miss Loop — then a walk
home with Geo. Hawes — then on to our
home & to bed a little before
midnight.

## Right Page

### Saturday, August 11, 1866.

Splendid day for study, altho' a little
breezy & cool. Mf. went at Elms 14×24.
(3: mf.) Painted over the l. h. foreground elm,
effect of sunlight marvelous — of course
did not get the first sentiment of it —
Addie & Fred D. came along near 10 o'clk
Addie worked the rest of the mfg. up on her
oil study until about 12, when Geo. joined
us & we all went up to the house & took
a look over the work that we have ac-
complished so far this season — After
dinner took my oatfield study & went to the
ground, found men at work binding and
gathering into "shocks" what was cut yesterday.
as the oatfield is the "joke" of the study, set
to work at once upon that & had it painted
in quite to my satisfaction by 4.30 o'clk. Feel
as though a good days work had been done.
& inclined to stay here through the season —
Fred D. came for me to go in swimming with
him & Geo. Hawes — went in — had a good time.
Tea early — went over with Geo. to the Dr.'s by
invitation — had a game of Croquet. Fred D. &
me ago. Geo. R. Geo. S. & Addie — Eveg. by a
fire in the Library — talking & toasting our toes,
