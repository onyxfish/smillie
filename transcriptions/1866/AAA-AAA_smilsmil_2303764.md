## Left Page

### Tuesday, June 19, 1866

Clear, Rather windy and comparatively cool —
Finished drawing (2d day) in the m[illegible] of Ohio Arms.
Rubbed out the heads of the Black Smith & Farmer and put them in again, Smaller — Afternoon buying up things about the Studio — Evety spent at Driving's in Company with Miss Pendleton.

All day, Mother, Momy, Matt & Kate have been at Uncle Aaron's at Mannattanville — Mary & Charl. stayed all night.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, June 20, 1866

Delightful day — Clear and breezy — not warm — At the Studio, getting things ready to leave until noon — Mr. Jacob Myckoff called by appointment about 1 o'clk — was not on the buy — Used up with a headache so went home to bed and slept until every.
Early every Mary returned home with Uncle A —
My Father got a proof of his Bierstadt plate, but have not seen it yet — He is, of course, I believe disappointed with it.
