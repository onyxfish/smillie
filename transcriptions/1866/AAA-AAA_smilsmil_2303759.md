## Left Page

### Saturday, June 9, 1866

Clear, cool day — worked all day (6th day) upon Earle's 9×15 picture, painting upon the foreground + figures. It is now Ready to lay away for a few days — Katy G took her 23d lesson. Eveng went with Father to Dr. Wm Thompson's to get his professional advice for Father's trouble — he pronounces it Herpes [illegible], a very, Rare trouble, but in no wise alarming. — Eveng fresh and very chilly, so that I put on my light overcoat when I went out.

## Right Page

### Sunday, June 10, 1866

Overcast + [illegible]. Rather chilly. Started with Charl. at about 10.30 A.M. Walked through and about in Central Park, out at 110th St and 8th ave — across to Bloomingdale Road + up to Uncle A's at 122d St. Took a bath immediately (1 o'clk) + after, a cup of coffee — Then sauntered off to the River about — dinner at 3 — then more sauntering. In the mean time it had cleared away charmingly — At 6 o'clk took tea, when Uncle A. drove Charl Geo. (who joined us at 1.30) and myself over to 8th ave + 125th St where we took 8 ave. Car and Rode down, Reaching home at 7.45 o'clk — Eveng tired, so did not go out.
