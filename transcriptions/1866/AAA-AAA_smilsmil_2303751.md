## Right Page

### Monday May 21, 1866.

Cloudless and warm. Worked
a day on Mr. Tracy Harris's
picture. "On the Delaware, near
Easton" — Worked somewhat over
the sky, then down up on the
middle distance & all but the
left hand bank & trees. The
whole tone & treatment are
radically different now from
the first painting. I hope
to make it as good as any
thing that I have yet done —
Every at home,
about Midnight. the Academy
of Music — College of Surgeons —
a Lutheran Church & several
houses burned down.
