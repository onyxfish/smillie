## Left Page

### Friday, December 28, 1866

Blowing a hurricane and cold as Greenland — wind rifting in at every crack — Feel dumpish after last nights experience — am afraid that I took heavy cold.
Spent the mrf wrapped in a heavy overcoat + his blanket sitting in front of the fire —
Visited somewhat in the north side of the house where it is warmer. Read Hunt's abstract of Dante's Inferno — + considerable in Don Quixote. Dore's Ill. Edition. Anything that is spicy or has pictures amuses me now, but not for any length of time — Every D.T. did not come as he promised — Gilbert Pettit and wife were here to tea + spent the eveng.
Got to bed at near 12 o'clk — this time between flannel blankets + with pillows at my back. Slept through the night tolerably well.

## Right Page

### Saturday, December 29, 1866

wind not yet gone down — but overhead clear as a bell —
Did not wake up to get up until near 10 o'clk — Feel better after my quiet night. Spent a part of the mrf writing to Mr Brevoort + making a pen + ink sketch to accomp[any] —
was indulged with 5 oysters at dinner — About 3 o'clk D.T. came along — pleased with my condition — much amused with my pen sketch. Represents me, very sick — lying off in my big chair — asked for a duplicate — I afternoon — made a duplicate better than the original — will give T. the original. Every quite quiet + mild again —
Will in + all hand engaged hanging pictures in the parlors.
