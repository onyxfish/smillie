## Left Page

### Tuesday, July 3, 1866

Delightful day, but very warm. Took [illegible] at about 8 o'clk and soon after Addie D, Emma S, and I started out horse back. Went to Belden's and tried by a wood Road to Reach Bald top. Failed but by pulling down fences, etc, got across lots & came out on the West Stockbridge Road at Reuben Ford's — went on to the top of the Mt. & then took another Road (wood) that brought us back again upon the Curtisville Road by Ch[?] Bristed's & then home by 12 o'clk. But a Bristed made the Dr. a call, at about 1 o'clk, thereby delaying dinner whereat the Dr. waxed wroth. Before dinner was over Ehquiner came in & took dessert with us. About 6 o'clk, packed trunk & drove with it to Gideon Bristo's. Bd. & with the girls & Ge. walked to the P.O. Bd. & took tea at the Dr. & spent the evng until near 9 o'clk — when Ge. & I walked to I mistr[?] there to pass our first night.

## Right Page

### Wednesday, July 4, 1866

The glorious fourth — after a miserable, almost sleepless night, up and had breakfast shortly after 6 o'clk A.M. Mr. F breezy & clear, but with many signs of rain — huntered about looking for sketches until nearly 9 o'clk when we went over to D. D's. — Girls picking over strawberries to can — waited nearly an noon, then Addie, Emma, Ge. & I went down to the bower under the Hemlock to finish our studies. Emma & the D[?] joined us later — worked until near noon. when Ge. & I left to go to dinner at our new place. About two o'clk a sharp, short shower with hail came up with thunder storms all around. After 4 o'clk we went again, by appointment to the Dr. — Girls gone to the Furnace on a message — too wet to keep our engagement with Miss Rosalie Goodman to play croquet. — Girls back shortly after 5 — tea after 6 — night dark, gloomy, & blustering — comfortable with doors & windows all closed. — Sat in the Library from twilight into deep dark — engaged in conversation upon a subject where words I think, generally fail to convey one's meaning or else, mock us with a sense of the crudety & imperfectness of our ideas. At 9.45 clk walked home over a very dark, lonesome & muddy Road.
