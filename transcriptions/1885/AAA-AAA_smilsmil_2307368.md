## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 18. 1885.

Up at 11.30 Am. & made experiment at tries to my new earth closet. Up for all day at 7.30 — misty, clearing to be a breezy, cloudy day — Before bkfs. went to see Father who passed another miserable night. This mtg he is very weak. Until 11 o'clk at work fixing Virginia Creeper Vine on big Bass wood Tree — the vine having been blown down — Near noon I went down town & back home, after stopping to see Father, at 1 o'clk — dinner soon after — with Bevick at odd jobs — again at work on the vine. Mrs. W.H. Jessups called. Annie & I went again to Father's — then I went on down Town & having many messages did not get back until 7 — Tea — Me every a broken one — the boy did not get to sleep until after we went to bed at 10.30. A Shower between 7 & 8 P.m.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 19. 1885.

Bright, breezy mtg. Spent it in working about the place. went to see Father about 10.30 — In some respects he seems rather better but he is extremely weak and suffers very much in his breathing. Then went down Town — back about 12 — Dinner at 1 — at 1.30 with Mary, started for the depot. Heavy thunder showers most of the way — the Train with Mills Abbott came at 3 ½ +, as the showers had passed, we had a cool, pleasant drive home — went again to see Father — then down town — tea at 6.30 — Every so cool that we had a fire — chatting until 9.30 when Miss A. went to her room — To bed about 10.
