## Left Page

### May, Monday 18, 1885

*Harley — Meesm Post Office, Talbot Co. Md*

Awake at 4, up at 5. Blep. at 6 +
left Jas. Wright's in carriage at 6.30. Got
to Broad St. Station at 7.15 — Very pleasant
m't — Bought tickets to Wapke. Del. & Chs. &
$3 35 each. Got baggage & had it checked &
then to Parlor Car Office & was told there was no
7.32 Train — none before 8.56 — mad — Took Annie
Bella & Boy up stairs & started for further infor-
mation — soon found that there was at 7.32
Train which we came near missing — could
not change Parlor car tickets for want of time.
The ride uninteresting — did not change
cars — got to Wapke at 12 — Preston at the
depot — a few minutes ride got us to his
house where we had a pleasant welcome.
Dinner about 2.30 — at 4.30 I gave up;
went to bed & had a nap — After that I
walked about the place with Preston.
Had tea about 7 — We all enjoyed a
pleasant wood fire indoors — general
chatting & a little music until 10
o'clk. when we went to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 19, 1885

Clear, milky day, the ther. getting
above 80° — with a pleasant breeze in
the afternoon — Blep. about 7.30
about 10 I went to boat house & got out
a light rowing boat & with Annie rowed
to a point about a couple of miles away where
we landed & stretched ourselves awhile in
the shadow of the pine woods. Got back about
noon & lay down upon the bed & had a
good nap. Dinner at 2.30 — After that
loafing in the breeze on the shady side
of the house until 5.30. Then Annie
went out for a sail with Preston. and I
did a little writing. Tea at about 7 —
Eve'g in parlor chatting until 10. Then
to bed.
