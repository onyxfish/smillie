## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 14. 1885.

Bright but cold — to studio at 10 — made haste & spent most of the m'f in carefully pasting into its frame "Coming Shower" — & writing some notes — Home to lunch as usual & back before 3 — Spent part of afternoon in arranging portfolio with pffs. from some of the oldest of Father's plates — Home before 6 — Kate there — later came Wm. & Fanny K. & Emma J. & last of all Ele. Home then we had dinner. After that we amused ourselves by playing a child's game styled "Fish pond" where much depends upon firmness of hand, & after that — a game of Bagatelle — Party broke up soon after 10.30 — I took Kate home & then did. Home down to Mad. Ave & 30' S. Home & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 15. 1885.

Cold, raw day — Rain beginning to fall about 11 — occasionally snow with it — Heavy rain at 1 — ceasing about 3 with signs of clearing at night. To studio at 10.30 Spent the m'f in mounting the six autograph pffs. I have from the Bridgman plate — Home at 1.30 — back to studio before 3 — Feeling very headachy I did little else than rest — went home again about 5. Did what I could to get things ready for dinner then dressed. Swain Gifford & wife came first soon after 6.30 — then Mr. & Mrs. Ehninger, a few minutes before 7 — Walter & wife exactly on time & Alden Weir & wife 15 m. late — At 7.20 we sat down to dinner & I think we had a very good time — Every one was jolly & informal. It was quite 9.45 when we rid to parlor & at 10.30 our guests left us & we went to bed.
