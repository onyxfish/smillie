## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 25, 1885

Quite a fall of snow in the m'f —
clearing brightly by 10 a.m. and
before night snow nearly all gone —
To studio at 10.30 — To work on big
picture, painting over far distance
& cliffs — making more grey & tender-
ness. Home at 1 — Emma at lunch
with us — back at 3 & painted
over distant seas & a little on
the sky — Home at 6 — dinner at
6.30 — Eve'g Sketched plans
for some Montrose wood boys
about 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 26, 1885

Pleasant m'f — but soon overcast
& raw — Blels — late & got to studio
at 10.30 but could not work. I
lounged about — until after 12 —
then to N.A. of D. — W.C. & [illegible] &
there met Annie. Home to lunch
at about 1.30 — Sat by the fire until
near 4 — then to McKeever's shop &
saw my big frame, just [illegible] — in
the white — Gave directions con-
cerning the gilding — then walked
down to studio & fixed up things
for the night, then home & had
dinner — Eve'g in dining room
reading until 9 — then Annie
& I had a game of Bazique &
before 11, to bed.
