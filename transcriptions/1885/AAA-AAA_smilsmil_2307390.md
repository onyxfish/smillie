## Left Page

### October, Thursday 1. 1885.

Still delightful weather, altho wind from East & other indications of a change — about 11 got to my study on Fairground & painted until 1.30, getting all essential work finished — then home &
had dinner about 2 o'clk —
It was 4 before I again got off to woods back of Fair ground where I undertook to scrub in a sketch of foreground material — Rocks, naked bushes & dead leaves — worked until almost 6 — then home — tea — after that, down town & attended to messages — back — washed brushes & wrote awhile — then with Belle, Eliz^th & Fanny in our bed room — Annie again feels encouraged to hope — to-day — nearly 11 when we got to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 2. 1885.

A mild, dull, brooding sort of day — Preparatory to a storm — Late when we got up & had Bkf^t — The Holleys finished Stone wall. We had an early dinner — 12 o'clk — on acct. of Belle's going away — Carriage late — I went down to stable — broken tire — at last we got off at 1 o'clk — hurried the horses & got over to New Milford, after a pleasant ride, in 1 hour & 20 m. — train on time — 2.41 — Belle goes to Canandaigua — will reach her friends at 8 P.M. — we (Eliz^th & Fanny) came back by old Road & through the woods on Cross Road to Donn-erville Road — Home at 5 — Tea at 6 — Swett, felt very tired — been bothered with Trouble in head & bowels all day — was routed out twice during the night — at 2 — & at 4 A.M. — [illegible — ~3 words] do — [illegible — ~4 words]
