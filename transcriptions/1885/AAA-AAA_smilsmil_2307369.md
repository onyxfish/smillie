## Left Page

### August, Thursday 20, 1885

Very pleasant day — no shower. Before 6&6" — went down to see Father — another sleepless night — much distress in his stomach — Spent the m'g. in Etching Room & studio, getting things into shape to go to work. Mother came up & made us a visit — about noon I went down again with her — then on down town — back, & had dinner at 1.30. Lenfus came with [illegible] to put in studio cistern — Jauced in the Etching room — he boggled over it for all the afternoon, but finally after 6 o'clc. got it done. Herrick came up & helped for awhile. Miss Abbott had to amuse herself most of the day — Tea at 6.30 — About 8 again went to see Father — he seems to be easier — down town — back before 9 — the rest of the even'g chatting — to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 21, 1885

Wind from [illegible] — bringing clouds & by noon, rain — then it brightened &
o'clc. P.M.
came warm — between 4 & 5, heavy thunderstorm — up at 6.30. 8&6" at 8 — after it went down to see Father who seems but little improved since yesterday. Then home & for a time with [illegible] Coyle — again went to Father's after 11 & then down town — Home to dinner soon after 1 — Afternoon worked on a die subject for Father — accessories to a Jones figure — I traced the subject on gelatine & transferred it — wrote a short letter to [illegible] at Father's request — again went to see him about 6. Then down town — Home about 7 & had tea — Even'g. chatting, reading, paper & making slight sketch in a book for Kate Riley — To bed about 10.30.
