## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 15. 1885.

Delightful day, altho' quite warm — While at the bkf. Table many's children came & startled me by saying that Father was unwell & Mary wanted me to go there — I did so — Father had had a bad night & was troubled with his breathing — but was not seriously indisposed — I ret'd to the house at about 10 went down town — it was nearly noon when I ret'd. Found the McCarty sisters waiting for me — had been for at least an hour — One of them had some of her drawings to show me.
Dinner at 1.30 — At 4.30 carriage came & a., the boy & I went & took Father & Mother out for a ride — back in less than an hour & then took Mary & Kate out for an hour — Tea after 7 — Eve'g we got to writing letters concerning our projected trip to Saratoga & the Adirondacks — It was 11 when we went to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 16. 1885.

A very hot day — did not have bkf. until 8.30 — then spent some time with the men about 10.30 went to see Father & found him quite bright. Then down town and back about noon. Dinner at 1.30 — again out with Cuny to show him about screening earth for the lawn & using the lawn mower. Finished putting in the glass in the front door. Tea about 7 & after that, Annie, the boy & I went to see the old folks — I went into P.O. etc. & on my way back sat on piazza for some time at Aunt [illegible]'s — then — before 9 — a. & I ret'd home. on piazza there for some time.
