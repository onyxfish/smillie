## Left Page

### November, Saturday 14, 1885

Pleasant day — delayed by various things in benefit. A note from Fanny K. saying that Mrs. Green of W. 31 S. would take our Phil. tickets — on my way to studio I left them there. — Painted on one of my old sketches made from Nature last fall, a foreground bit — twilighty effect — Home to lunch as usual then back to studio and painted as long as I could see. Then went to Thel. Starr's to look for Quinnie, at something suitable for a wedding present for Geo. Cook. Dinner at 6. Evg. Read aloud to Annie until she fell asleep — then I became interested looking over old letters & papers & it was 11 before I went to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 15, 1885

Charming day — BkBk. about 9 — m. to m.f. Mr. Saml. Coleman & many Willards called to see Annie, who [illegible] them in bed — It was about noon before I could get out for a walk — then up to Central Park to head of mall & back at 2. Had dinner — the rest of the afternoon with Annie & the boy — wrote my letter to Father — Tea at 7 — about 8.30 went for a few minutes to Murray Hill Hotel & bid the C's goodbye — they start for Cal. at [illegible] to-morrow m.f. Mailed my letters & home a few minutes after 9 — looking over old letters and papers until 10.30 — about 11 when I got to bed.
