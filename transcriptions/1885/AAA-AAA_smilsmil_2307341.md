## Left Page

### June. Thursday 25. 1885.

Very pleasant day — after some work at home, to studio at 10 & then in to Tiffany's where I completed selection of glass for front door & transom & had it cut — back at 12 & worked in studio until after 1 — then home to lunch — Finished packing big case at home & about 3.30 back to studio where I worked until near 7 — then home & had a late dinner. After that, got cover on big case, putting blisters on both hands — then wrote notes to Cottrell — Mr. Park & Miss Abbatt — At 10 went out, mailed letters — had a glass of Vichy with acid phosphates — then in and to bed.

## Right Page

### June. Friday 26. 1885.

Very warm but with a pleasant breeze. Up at usual time — after bkf. got down some empty trunks & did some preliminary work — Got to studio about 10 & then to work packing my big box which I got done with soon after 12 — then putting pictures in order & labeling them for the summer [illegible] 1.30 before I finished — Stopped at Clark's & Quincy's Exp. on my way home — To lunch at 2 — then a rest — At about 3.30 went to foot of Christopher St. to enquire about 9 Exp. Train stopping at Montrose. Had to go to Hoboken — saw Ass. Dep.; very civil — arranged to have Train stop there at the [illegible] down on time Table. Saw Ticket agent — bought $20. mileage ticket — then back & stopped at Sarony's — too late — then to Wunderlich's & got $7.64 for etchings sold. Then to Newman's & Sloan's — then home at 6 & had dinner — Packed trunk and valise — out at 10.30 & had another Vichy [illegible] — To bed about 11.
