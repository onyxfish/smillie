## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 1. 1885.

A milder day, but still very far
from a warm one. Enjoyed a brisk
fire while at [illegible]. Soon after 8 —
Herrick put in an appearance this
mf. & I kept him at work on odd
jobs. By a combination of carelessness
& accident yesterday I broke one of my
pieces of Tiffany glass — so, this mf. I
spent some time putting it & other things
together with Stratina — Went down town
at 12. Gave Fordham stuff to make
some window cushions f. Got marketing
& other supplies & home before 1 — Dinner
about 1.30. Spent the afternoon putting
glass into door sash & transom — The effect
of color is very delicate & very agreeable to
me — near 7 when I had tea — Annie
went down town for mail etc. —
Evf., a little writing & some quiet
enjoyment of the good things sur-
rounding us — & to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 2. 1885.

Still cold & windy — All day about
running after the men & at odd
times working on the front door —
giving it a coat of umber (antique)
rubbing it into the grain of the oak.
& putting the glass into place, which
I find to be a serious job — Had the
fence between us & the old Cooper
lot knocked down this mf. It
gives us space in a very satisfactory
manner — Went down town about 11
& back at noon — dinner at 1.30 —
tea at 7 — Evf. writing letters —
a game of Bazique & to bed before
11.
