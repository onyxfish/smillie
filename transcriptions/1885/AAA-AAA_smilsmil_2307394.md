## Left Page

### October, Friday 9, 1885.

Another dull m*t*, but milder.
In the afternoon it cleared charm-
ingly. B*lc*b. about 6.30 — then left
our guests to themselves & spent most
of the m*t* with the men directing
the grading. Dinner at 1.30 & again
out with the men most of the
afternoon. Payne put a blast in a
big rock — near 6 o'clk — went
down town & took Jim with
me. Had tea about 7. S*we*4
I wrote a letter to Kate — news
from the Terrace still continues
good. — There is no change for
better or worse that Annie can
report in her case. Every
all of us in Annie's Room
& to bed about 11.

About noon Mr. Eldred
called with deed for gone
land that he has sold
me.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 10. 1885.

Beautiful day — heavy frost
last night. Spent a good part
of m*t* with the men. Finished
scratching on etching 34 — went
down town — dinner about 1.30.
Before 3 carriage came and
Mother C. D*r*. & Nellie went out
riding with me & Jim Jim.
Went over Butterfield's hill &
back on Wyalusing Road — in
at 5.30 having had a charming
ride. Tea before 7 — went
down town after that. On
my return played Bezique
with Mother Cook D*r*. & Nellie
until 10. To bed about 11
after having worried for some
time about fire place in
main room — took panel
over mantle piece out but
found no place where thin
should be none.

M*rs*. Husted, her Mother & bro
came up in the m*t*. He to
take photos.
