## Left Page

### December, Thursday 10, 1885

Overcast most of the day — To
studio at 10 — After a little fussing
over my Etching Club Cat. plate I
went to K.&V.'s which & got a
couple of proofs. — back soon after
12 — To lunch at home & back
as usual — Yale came in and
made a long call & I had
hardly settled to my work
when Manley & his friend Bailey
came — I laid a ground for
M. & then got a chance to
put in a few minutes of work
before dark — Dinner as usual.
Chase came in, just down
from Po'k. at 8 P.M. — I wrote
a note to Kate & took it to the
G.C.D. — about 10 — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 11, 1885

Bright, pleasant day — I work in
the m'f. on reduction of Pasini
painting which I completed —
Belle & Mary Tweedie called — I
gave my Phil. Rehearsal ticket
to Miss Abbratt — Home at 1 & back
at 2.30 — made tracing of sketch
at Montrose of Studio & corner of
house — three fixed press & transferred it & the Pasini reduction
to respective plates — About 5 went
to Newmans & bought a spring scale
to weigh ice at home — Dinner at
6 — About 8 walked down to J.C.
Nicoll's Studio — Etching Club —
There until near 10 — walked
up most of the way with Dr.
Yale — To bed about 11 — Annie
continues to improve, but slowly.
Chase came in about 6 and
told me of some of the troubles
he has already encountered, in
unexpected places, as executor
under Father's will.
