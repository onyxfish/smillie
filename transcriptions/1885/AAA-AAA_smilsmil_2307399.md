## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 21, 1885

Dismal, down pouring rain —
got fire started in sketching
Room & in Studio furnace.
Cumy worked half a day, cleaning
Studio cellar & S. W. cistern. I wrote
a note to Mr. Brewster & one to Natu.
Then, about 11, went down town —
Among other places went to Brewster's
with my note — Saw him & found
him about as petty a man as Coon.
Dinner after & then spent the
afternoon picking up about the
house. At 6 again went down
town — went to Payne & told
him of Coon's suit — In my way
home stopped at Aunt H's — Tea
after 7 — Evelf, being tired, after
Reading newspaper, went to
bed before 10 — was routed
out by trouble in lower regions
soon about A.M.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 22, 1885

Dull, overcast & rather cold —
Kept rather quiet owing to my
experience of last night. In the
m't — Payne came up & told
me that he had been Coon and
settled with him — telling him that
he had spoiled all chances for ever
having any further dealings with
me. Pettus, all day hauling
four loads of dirt — 3 from Cooleys
& 1 from near the lake. I objected.
most of the afternoon I spent
on the bid.

Evelf had part of a game
of cards with Annie — then to
bed about 10 —
