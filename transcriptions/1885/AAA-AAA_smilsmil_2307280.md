## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 17, 1885

Very cold clear day — to studio soon after 10 — Found that I did not feel like attacking big pictures, so, after awhile I set to work with charcoal on a canvas X from Study of cliffs near Etretat — "French Coast — etc Home at 1 — back at 3 — tinted my charcoal sketch — & then did some chores about studio getting things into some order. A young German scamp came in & tried to play the Confidence game on me — pretending that I had forgotten him — wanted me to make him an advance on some boxes he said contained cigars — no go. Before 6 walked to 7th St. & up 5th Ave. then home to Dinner — Eve'g Chas. Collins called — spent most of the eve'g with us in the dining Room. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 18, 1885

Snow storm lasting until near noon — then it cleared brightly — got to Studio at about 10 & commenced the painting of "French Coast near Etretat" — all the wt. on sky — Home at 1 — back at 2.30 — Revised sky & painted in sea — worked until 5, then washed ups — Went to Sub-oration P.O. — & got money order for $2.15 & sent it to d. H. Lincoln at Montrose — charges on deed for the Cosper lot. Home at 6 — Dinner — Eve'g — feeling heavy headed — From 9 to 10 played Bazique with Annie — To bed about 11.
