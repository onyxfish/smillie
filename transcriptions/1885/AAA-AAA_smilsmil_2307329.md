## Left Page

### June, Monday 1, 1885

Bright, pleasant day — Got to studio
before 10 & to work on pen sketch of
Pagoda — a nasty headache interfering
— At 1 went to Bank & made deposits
for Annie & myself — home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — had to give
up work part of the afternoon owing
to a swimming headache — managed
to finish sketch — heated glue and
fixed Palette & sketching box — Home
at 6.30 — Emma & Geo. Ireland
took dinner with us & spent the
evef until 9.30 — To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 2, 1885

Delightful day, but got up with a mean
headache that had troubled me all
night — It passed away by noon —
Got to studio by 10 o'clk — & spent part
of the m'f in revising the Saratoga
sketches already done. Made paste &
mounted Toboggan Sketch, carefully cutting
at the Toboggan & setting in a new piece.
Rec'd note from Col. Ingraham enclosing
check for $100 — Mr. [illegible] Pate called to see
if I had any Yellowstone material — I sent
him to Tom. Moran. Home at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & worked until near 4.30 on second
sketch of top of Mt. McGregor — Then went
to Grolier Club — 64 Mad. Ave — met Annie
there & we saw a collection of book illus'ns —
Then at 5 went to Am Art Ass'n & for about
an hour looked at Prize finders — Then
I went to barber's & had my hair cut,
Dinner before 7 — Evef wrote letter
to Ehninger & several notes. At
10.30 went to G.C. Depot & mailed
Ed's letter — home & to bed at 11.
