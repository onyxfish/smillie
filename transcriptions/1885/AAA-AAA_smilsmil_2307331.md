## Left Page

### June, Friday 5. 1885.

A very hot day with very heavy shower about 3 — Weth. quite cool. Bought a summer hat in 3rd Ave. & got to studio at 10.30 — To work on "Race course" — Home at 1.30 — after a call from Mr. Ely of Milwaukee & Wm. Bemian — Back at 3. just in time to escape a ducking — again worked on Race course — cut out a portion that I did not like, set in a new piece of board & redrew the object upon it. Again at 5 worked at backing back vols of Ill. news — did not get home until 7.30 — then had dinner — Weth for about an hour, cards with Annie. To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 6. 1885.

Delightful day, clear, breezy & cool. Soon after 10 when I got to studio — worked for awhile on "Saratoga Race course" — then commenced on sketch of conical formation over one of the Springs — with figures about. Bemian Home at 1.30 — after lunch went to A.B.N.Co. with all the drawings so far finished — Got there at 3.10 — Closed at 3 — caught there by accident. waited but a few minutes — then to studio at 4. Consulted with Miss Abbatt about flowers for Montrose — Worked awhile on Sp'ng. At 5, C.M. Kuertz came in with a photo. of Walter Damrosch & while he waited I made a few sketch to be electrotyped, for Alitories Palser — Done at 6.40 with $5.00 more in my pocket. Home at 7 — dinner — to Century at 8.30 — a slim meeting — open windows & little smoke — a very enjoyable meeting — pleasant chats with members until 11, then walked up town with Geo. Home & to bed just before 12.
