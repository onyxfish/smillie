## Left Page

### May, Monday 4, 1885.

Cool day with considerable variety of weather — a shower in the afternoon — to studio at 10 & found Col. Prentiss Ingraham of Saratoga waiting for me in Geo's studio — we soon came to an agreement by which I am to make him 20 or more sketches of places in & about Saratoga, for Reproductive process for $10.00 each. I was somewhat startled by getting note from Equitable Life Ins. Ass. returning check for May premium informing me that my policy was void owing to non payment of [illegible] premium. Rode down with Col. D. in his cab to Ms. Co. Saw W.W. Alexander & son had the troubles righted — Stopped in at Chase's for a moment — Home at 1 — lunch — at 2 at 2d Nat. Bk. — then to Knordeis & at studio at 3 — Commenced work on Ingraham sketches — Vichy Spring — Worked until 6 — Annie came in — walked home with her — Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g. Chase came in with prints of the photos. Taken yesterday — of course, we had a warm discussion on the subject of art & photo &c. I read awhile — then our usual game & then to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 5, 1885.

Pleasant day — After 10 when I got to studio — Finished sketch with pen commenced yesterday p.m. with few interruptions from calls by C.W. Kurtz & Mrs. McCartney — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 — made another Saratoga pen sketch — Col. Ingraham called about 3.30 — About 5.30 I left my work — met Miss Mblatt at the door & walked with her to 3d. ave. & 28th St — then on to Wm. Douglas' 3d ave. & 34th St — spent some time discussing floor finish for the Montrose House. Nearly 7 o'clk. when I got home to dinner. Eve'g. until 9.30 writing to Ehninger — & Kimmee & Vogt — then a game of cards with Annie — To bed about 11 — During the night the boy made some howl from about 12 to 3 for no reason that could be discovered.
