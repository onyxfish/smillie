## Left Page

### September, Thursday 3, 1885.

Bright, windy & smoky. Bkft.
late — mt cold — big fire. After
bkft — attending to small matters
until about 10 — then down town
where I was engaged until
noon — then to Warner's lot —
where I saw Miss A & her work.
Home at 2.30 — went to bed &
had a nap — slp in 3/4 of an
hour — dinner soon after —
At 3 Jessie Comstock & Mary
Sayre came & took Marion &
Miss Clark riding — while
Annie, Misses A. & L. with Uncle
& Aunt A. went off for a ride.
Back at 5.30 — hurried by signs
of a storm — bits of showers
soon after — tea about 7 —
Ev'rybody in general chatting
& reading — To bed about
10.45.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 4, 1885.

Pleasant day — Before bkft — went
to Aunt H's & bid Uncle L. &
Aunt A. good bye — They started
for N.Y. by 9 o'clk stage. Downtown
about 10.30 attending to various
little matters. Dinner at 1.30 —
afternoon cleared out closet
in studio — directing Lenfus, who
fixed furnace in studio cellar
& register in bay window and
derrick — very much used
up with a cold in the head.
By afternoon mail got good
news from Kate of Father —
Tea as usual — Ev'rybody in
general chatting — I, most
of the time, sniffling, sneezing
& blowing my nose. — To bed
at 10.
