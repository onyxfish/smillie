## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 17, 1885

Delightfully cool & pleasant day —
Slept late — bkft. after 8 — Wrote notes
to Cantwell & Curtis of Syracuse Art Club
& Hall of R.I. School of Design — nearly 12
when I went to market — Studio & Bank
— also to Knoedle's & did some shopping
at Johnston's — Home at 1, but near 2
when I had lunch — After that, to work
on U.S. Hotel illustration & worked until
after 5 — then to studio & worked there
at getting things in order to leave
until near 7 — then home & had
dinner — Evg. Read awhile —
then wrote to Father, Koehlen & Co.
International News Co. Also went
to G.C.D. with letters — Then home
& to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 18, 1885

Very warm — Did not go to studio
but worked on my last pen sketch
at home — I finished it a little
before 11 — then packed up things
& rushed for the A.B.N. Co. getting
there at 11.30 — Saw Will & the head
of the Photo-Eng. dept. — & delivered
my last & completed work; much
to my relief — one thing & another
kept me until after 5 when I
rode up town with Will — Got
home at 6 — Took Annie & Mother
C. down to Kimball's — Lib Howe
there — also Misses Haines & Clark —
but Will was not there — We
had tea at about 7.30 — I, doing
the honors of the carving knife —
but felt very stupid — General
chatteration until after 10 when
Mother C., Annie, Eleg. & I left —
we stopped in Bdway & had Ice-
Cream Soda — Then put A & mother
into 4th ave car — & took Eleg. home —
I got home & to bed after 11.
