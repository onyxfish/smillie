## Left Page

### February, Saturday 7, 1885

Bright cold day — to studio
at usual time — Before I got
started on my work Van Bos kvd
came in — I wrote a note nom-
inating him candidate for membe-
rship in W. C. Soct'y — then to work
on Sea — Cliffs of Normandy — Miss
Hallowell + Mr. Larned (?) one
of the Chicago Efp. Art Com. called
+ we had a pleasant chat. Home
to lunch + back as usual and
worked as long as I could see —
then washed up + had a nap
in my chair — Home to dinner
at 6.30 — At 8.30 went to Century
monthly meeting — stopping for a
few minutes at W. C. club —
Left Century with Re° at
11.30 — walked home + got
to bed shortly after midnight.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 8, 1885

Dull m'f. clearing beautifully late
in the afternoon — got up with a
heavy head which cleared with
the weather — Bkfs at usual time
Read until near noon — then with
Annie walked down to the Century.
Home again soon after 1 — About
2, Jenny Downs + her son Jack am-
+ dined with us — the left bet-
ween 3 + 4 — I took a nap in a
chair — Between 6 + 7 wrote
letter to Father — Tea at 7 — at
8.30 took letter to G. C. D. — then
a walk of a couple of miles +
in at 9 — Read until 11 — then
to bed.
