## Left Page

### November, Friday 20, 1885

Bright pleasant w'f., but I got up with a nasty headache. Got to studio before 10 & spent the m'm until near 12 at work with graver on Father's Lion die — then Bela on die — came with Jim Junt — & we went out & shopped to get him a winter suit — got a coat at Lord & Taylor's & leggings & cab at Best's; in 23°. i. Home at 1.45 & had lunch — Then had to give up & go to bed with a head ache — did not get up until near 9 — then had some toast & tea — lay down on bed beside Annie — fell asleep & did not get up until 12 — then to bed for the rest of the night.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 21, 1885

Overcast day — stupid head — got to studio before 10 and worked on Lion die — Home about 1 & back at 3 — worked until about 4.45 on die — then to Am Art Ass'n. & saw Kirby about etching after Fasini for Morgan Cat. Home at 6. Annie does not seem so well — Today & has about given up all hope — Eve'g I wrote notes to Gifford — Nicola & Mr. Gregory. To bed about 11. [illegible — ~4 lines]
