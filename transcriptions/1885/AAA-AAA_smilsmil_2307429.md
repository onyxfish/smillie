## Left Page

### December, Sunday 20, 1885

Brilliant, cold & windy. Up for bkf. at 8.30 — the fire in parlor was so comforting that it was 12 o'clk. before I could leave to go out for my walk. Went up to Central Park — as far as the Obelisk then back — getting in at 1.45. Then dinner — afternoon, Nellie Sneddon — Charles — Ed & Cook — Mary & three children called. Tea at 6.30 — After that, Read aloud to Annie for awhile. Then wrote to Mother, Nicoll, Freelan & Ehninger. Mr. & Mrs. Geo. J. Cook called — Emma J. was among the callers in the afternoon. About 10 went to G. C. desk with letters, then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 21, 1885

Overcast in the m'f., clearing in the afternoon — Glorious moon-light at night. Got to Studio before 10 & right to work on Etching of Parini — Mr. Koehler came in about noon — nothing settled about the Johnson & Bridgeman plates. I got the acid on my additional Etching about 12.30 — puzzled to know if the acid was acting all right — delayed — At 1 went to Bank & deposited money for Annie & self — then home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45 — finished biting & then did some experimental dry-pointing — went to G. P. Putnam's & looked at some new Etching Ill. works — home & had dinner soon after 6 — Well. Read awhile &  then wrote awhile — Rec'd a request from Mrs. Jno. C. Draper to act as pall bearer at the funeral of her husband on Wed. — To bed before 11.
