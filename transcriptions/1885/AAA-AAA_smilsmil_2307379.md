## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 9. 1885.

Pleasant day but with high
wind — The girls went away in
the 9 o'clk. Stage — I worked in
Etching room, Tracing pencil study
made in 1860. At 10.30 went down Town
& did not get back until near noon.
Then went out & hunted about
for an Etching subject unsuccessfully
— back at 1 — Dinner at 1.30 — P.M.
felt so heavy with sleep that I took
a nap — Then wrote a letter to
Kate — Lib Howe am — by 5 o'clk
Stage — About 6 I went down
Town — Tea about 7 — Rest &
Reading. Patsen & a game of
Bazique — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 10. 1885.

A dull day with muggling rain
that came down a little freely at
night — I worked in Etching Room
part of the time Tracing old pencil
study & part of the time Etching
on A.B.N. Co. die — Went downtown
before noon — Dinner as usual.
After some work in Etching Room
I climbed a beech tree near the
house & sawed away at the dead
top of it until I was thoroughly
tired & wet with perspiration —
Then in & had a sponge bath —
Did not go down town — Tea
before 7 — Rest. Annie read
aloud for awhile — To bed
about 10.30.
