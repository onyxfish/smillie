## Left Page

### February, Monday 9, 1885

Dark mtf. drizzle in the afternoon that froze as it fell and heavy rain at night — got up with a heavy head — after 10 when I got to studio — worked awhile on big picture, then gave it up — I felt so unlike working. Home to lunch at 1.30 — Soon after 3 went over to Gradey's & spent the rest of the afternoon in trying to decide upon frame pattern for my big picture — At last ordered a section of one to be made. Home before 6 — Streets hardly passable on acct. of ice — Dinner at 6. Evef. in dining room — one game of Bazique just as I was going to bed, was told that water was pouring into Kitchen from back area — found there a pool of water rapidly increasing & cesspool vent choked — donned big boots & Rubber coat & with furnace poker cleared it — gave feet a warm bath & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 10, 1885

overcast with light rain, clearing windy & cold. Hale came in to see & just & I did not get to studio until 10.30 — then to work on big picture, glazing & painting on the left hand foreground. Spent the day working over the grass slope. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back before 3 Charl came in about 5 o'clk. with very discouraging accounts of Father's condition — he having spent last Sunday with him — we walked into town together — Dinner at 6.30 — Evef. read awhile — then drew awhile on [illegible] of drawers for side board then a game of Bazique with Annie & to bed about 11.
