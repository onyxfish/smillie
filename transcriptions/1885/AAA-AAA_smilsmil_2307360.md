## Left Page

### August, Sunday 2, 1885

Up before 8 but did not have bkf't until 9. A very beautiful day but I felt so generally tired that I was not inclined to do anything but "loaf". It was afternoon before I went to see Father — all are much exercised over the news just rec'd of the Robbery of the Terrace House & the capture of the thief. Kate will go to Po'k to-morrow. Ev'g. I did a little reading & we got to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 3, 1885

Overcast m'g, which soon turned into a heavy, steady rain — Bkf't at 7.45 — then went down town and telegraphed to 2d. Parker to meet Kate at Hoboken — Kate left at 9.30 in the stage to go to Po'k to look after things there. The house has been broken into & robbed — the thief & some of the plunder caught. Feeling very uncomfortable about the head I found that I could not work as I wished to, so I wasted much time in hanging about. The Studio bay window is nearly finished and looks very well — Before tea time, on my way down town, I stopped awhile to see Father — the folks — Tea, after 7 — Ev'g. Reading papers for awhile then Annie & I had a short game of cards & to bed soon after 10.
