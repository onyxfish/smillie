## Left Page

### January, Thursday 8, 1885

Fine Day. While at bkfst. rec'd telegram saying that Mother & Kate would visit us to day. To studio at 10 & worked all the mng on "Coming Shower" — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Geo. with me — Mother & Kate there - got in from Po'k about 11 — Back to studio at 3 — got Aquarelle varnish & sprayed my drawings, much to their benefit in brightness — did some general work on them & home soon after 5 — Joe, Minn & her baby had been there — Will was there when I got in — Charl & Ed came soon after — Mother having had a gay time rec'd to Po'k with Kate & Ed on the 6. P.M. Train — Dinner at 6.30 — wrote & read until 9, then went to Union League Club. Fine collection of paintings — annual meeting — there until 10.30 — met Geo. & he walked home with me — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 9, 1885

Very mild pleasant day — To studio at 9.45. Did some work on "In the Orchard" — also on "Coming Shower" — Sent "Wayfarers & Orchard" to Century — also a little oil sketch of "Stray Lambs" — Home to lunch at 1 — met Wm. Hudsen in Park Ave & took him in to lunch with us — At 2, Annie & I at Phil. Rehearsal — Enjoyed it very much. Belle & Mary Tweedy there — Saw Ella Beach after Rehearsal — To studio at 11.30 — At 5 went to Barker's & had my hair cut — home before 6 — dinner at the usual time — before 8, with A — went to Mother Cook's — Walter & his wife came in — The announcement of Geo. Cook's engagement to Miss Hill is made, creating great surprise. Home at 9.30. To bed soon after 10.
