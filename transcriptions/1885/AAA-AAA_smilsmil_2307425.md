## Left Page

### December, Saturday 12, 1885

Brilliant, beautiful winter day. Got to studio at about 10 & spent a little time on Pasini plate, then to work on Etching Club. Cat. [illegible] until about 11.30 when I got done all that I could without a [illegible] — then, until 12.45, putting things in order about the studio — Home to lunch — back before 2.30. Will sent up for drawings — sent him Patterson Iron Mill & Mexican [illegible] — then to work on drawing of Mill in Bennington, Vt. Worked as long as I could see — walked down to Wunderlich's & saw the Haig etchings — Home to dinner before 6 — Mr. de Schrevenitz & Mrs. Parker called on Annie to-day. Eve'g I worked until 10 on Annie's Annual Report for Diet Kitchen. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 13, 1885

A miserable dark day, with heavy rain & howling wind — All day in-doors — some writing in the m'f & revising Diet Kitchen work of last night — Before 1 o'clk Yale came in & made a long call. Dinner at 2 — Annie on lounge in parlor — we worked on her Diet Kitchen Annual Report for some time — Tea between 6 & 7 — Eve'g Geo. Cook & his new wife called. After 9 I got at my letter for mother & it was near 11 before I could get up to G.C.D. with it, then to bed.
