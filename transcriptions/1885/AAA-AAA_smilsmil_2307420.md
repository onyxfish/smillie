## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 2, 1885.

Bright day — to studios at 9.30 —
tried to make camera-lucida re-
duction of Pasini's picture for the
Morgan Cat. etching, but for some
reason could not see, so etched
on small plate for Etching Club
Cat. Went over to Br[other]'s for Annie
abt — then home to lunch & with
Kate went up to Po'k on 2.30 train —
got to the Terrace at 5.15 & found
Father in a most depressing
condition — our coming did not
seem to rouse him — as he lay
with his head almost over upon
his knees, on his bed, panting for
breath & apparently not knowing
what was going on about him —
He seemed to recognize me
though, when I bid him good-
bye at 7 — I then walked
to Station — took 7.21 train
& got to 42nd St. at 9.20 — Home
& right to bed —

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 3, 1885.

Overcast w[ith] f[og], then clearing —
a smart shower about 11 — again
clear — flurries of snow in the
afternoon — got up with a heavy
head — to studios before 10 — too dark
to see to make Pasini reduction —
again worked on Etching Club
Cat. plate — Home abt — back at
2.30 — finished plate — worked
on reduction of Pasini but did
not finish it — then mounted
a sheet of drawing paper on a
heavy trunk board — Home
soon after 5 — found Mary Sprague
Cook & Cathenie there — took them
home — then dinner — well
Annie got up & walked into
parlor where we sat until 10 —
had a game of cards — then
I went to G.C.D. & posted a
letter to Kate & also one to Mr.
Whitney.
