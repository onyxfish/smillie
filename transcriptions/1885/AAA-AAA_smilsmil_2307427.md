## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 16, 1885

Beautiful day — After [illegible] went to Kimmel & Voigt's, Canal S. & had [illegible] of Etching Club Cat. [illegible] — It does not look as I wish it to but I left it there for finished. There to studio & got to work on Pasini Etching — home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked as long as I could see on Pasini — only 4.15 — about 4.30 Chas. Collins came in & we chatted for half an hour — then walked up town together — I went to barber's & had my hair cut — home to dinner at 6 — Met [illegible] — Read aloud awhile to Annie — Charle then came in & stayed until after 10 — It was near 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 17, 1885

Bright & pleasant — To studio at usual time & to work on Pasini etching — R. Swain Gifford came in a little before & made quite a call — It was near 2 before I got home to lunch — back about 2.45 & finished Pasini laying in — varnished & got everything ready for the acid — then, having still a little daylight I worked awhile on etching from studio from the North, at Montrose — then took a walk to 14th St. & around by Bway & 5th ave. — home — Dinner at 6 — Before 8 went up to Charl's — J. E. 47? — by appointment and spent a couple of hours with him. Talking over estate matters & looking over his statements of Father's assets — home before 10. Collins had called, but had went after Annie had gone to bed — I got to bed at 10.30.
