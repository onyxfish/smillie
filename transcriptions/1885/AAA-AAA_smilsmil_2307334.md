## Left Page

### June, Thursday 11. 1885.

Still pleasant — hot but tempered by breezes. Annie with her Mother
& Marie spent the day with Many (Sprague) at their place for the summer at
Crawford, N.J. I got to studio before 9.30 — bothered with a head ache — commenced sketch of Grand Union —
Charley Platt came in — just back from
home & spent much of the mtf. Home
to lunch at 1.40 — back at 3 — much
of the afternoon my head ached so that
I could not work. About 6. went to
Herald, Tribune & Word agencies & put
in an ad. for Margaret, our waitress —
went to Newman's & left bill for Col.
Ingraham at Stuertevant. Home at 7 —
Dinner — Eve'g wrote to Koehler
(sent him 5 of my C.P. this p.m.) to Will
Cook & to J.N. Cottrell — Soon after 10
went to G.C.D. & mailed them —
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 12. 1885.

*Poughkeepsie —*

A hot day — Got to studio before
10. Found letters from R.I. School of Design
& Syracuse promising to make good the
damage to my frames & pictures — also
a letter from Syracuse enclosing check
for $145.00 for my W.C. drawing "Recollection
of a Cal. River" — Purchased for the University.
I worked all the mtf on "Grand Union
Hotel" but could not get it finished — At
1:15 I went to Bank with Syracuse check
& home to lunch at 1.45 Annie went
to the dentists to keep a 2 o'clk. engagement
& I deliberately packed — At
3.30 A. [illegible] & we took the 4 o'clk
train, A. B. & the boy & I, getting to
Pok at 6.20 & home at about 6.30 — and
had dinner — Found Father & Mother
very well — Mary quite broken down
under her trials with the Doctor who
now comes to the house & insists that
he is master — After an evening of
chatting we got to bed soon after
10 o'clk.
