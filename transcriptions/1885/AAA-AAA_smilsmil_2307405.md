## Left Page

### November, Monday 2, 1885.

Pleasant weather again. Spent the mf. about matters
at the house & studio — after lunch went to A.B.N.Co. & got
3 pt. from Father's lion die. On my way back to studio stopped
at Private View, Am. Art Galleries — saw Kirby who asked me to do two
Etchings for the Morgan Catalogue. Then to studio & tracked down pt.
— then to Station 7, & got new Postal note for Gifford for $2.20. Geo.
walked up home with me —
Dinner about 6.15 — Mother Cook with us — Ev'f., a game of
Basque — Wrote a short note to Father — Took Mother C. home
& Father's note to G.C. depot —
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 3, 1885.

*Election Day.*

Got to studio about 9.30 Bright
mf. — breezy — Two or three showers
with snow & hail between 11 & 1 —
A woman at studio cleaning for
me — we tackled the Etching
corner first — did all the mf.
home and back soon after 2 —
worked until 5 — then home
my tired — Dinner soon after 6 —
Ev'f. wrote a note to Gifford —
Chas. Collins called — To bed
before 11.
