## Left Page

### October, Monday 5, 1885

Ther. went down to 32°. last night — cold bleak day, with some sunshine — Routid out at 6 A. M — my trouble seems obstinate — As I had to get up so early I made good fires & then went back to bed, chilled, in consequence, I was out of sorts all day — Bkfst soon after 8. Fanny & Eley [illegible] left us for N.Y. on 9 A.M. Stage. I made fire in etching room & spent a good part of the day there at work on Etching No. 34. a group of Maples on the new lot. Took my dinner & tea in bed room with Annie who Rec'd. from Male this m't, a reassuring letter. Ev'g spent Reading & feeling lonely & to bed before 10, after having done some Diet Kitchen work for Annie.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 6, 1885

Bleak & rather dismal day — was routid out again during the night — to-day feel under the weather — did not go out until the afternoon — Rec'd a short letter from Father to my great joy — it is like a return to life — for I had not dared to hope that he ever again could write — I spent the day in bed room with Annie — had my meals there — & in the m't. Etched on No. 34 — In the afternoon went out to look after the men — Curry cutting dead wood out of the trees back of the house. About 6 I went down town for my return had tea — Ev'g read newspapers — Annie is encouraged to believe that she is better. To bed at 10.
