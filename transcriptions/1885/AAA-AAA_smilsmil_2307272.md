## Left Page

### February, Sunday 1, 1885

Dull with rain + snow during the day — B[illegible]. Late — then for
some time reading papers —
About noon went with Annie
to W.C. [illegible] at U.A. — + having
the galleries to ourselves we had
a good quiet look at the pictures.
I think the exhib[ition] below the average.
Home at 2 — dinner — afternoon
did not go out — Read — tea at
7 — then wrote letter home
+ after 8 went to Murray Hill H[otel]
to see Charl who has been laid
up for some days with boils on
his neck. Found Ge[o]. + Nellie there
— stayed until after 10 — then went
to G.C.D with letter — then home
+ to bed about 11, 20.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 2, 1885

Bright day, growing colder with
light snow at night — at Studio
all day working over the last of
the first painting — in of the Meadow
Pond — or, as I now think of calling
it "a waste lot." Home to lunch
as usual — worked until about
5 — then a rest — not feeling well.
Home to dinner at 6 — Eve'g
took Annie to her b[rother] Edwards
+ I went to Star Theatre + saw
Laurence Barrett as Cassius in
Julius Caesar. Barrett had
given to Frank Nilletti a number
of tickets to distribute among the
artists — + many of them came —
a very slim house — J.C. Nicoll
sat with me — the performance
was tame to me, + Barrett was a
disappointment being conventional
+ stagey — out at 11 — walked up
with Walter Cook + Willard after
stopping to let them have a glass
of beer with Babb + Jos. Hartley —
Home + to bed at midnight.
