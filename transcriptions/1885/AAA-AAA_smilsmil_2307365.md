## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 12. 1885.

A very hot day — Father came
up soon after 9 with his dies of
"liens" — Spent the rest of the m'f with
us & took dinner — Mother also came
up & Kate & Mary's children. I
spent most of the m'f. grinding
ink & taking p's. for Father, one
which pleased him very much.
It was nearly 3 when he put to
Aunt H's — I was after the putties
& Herrick & Coyle all day — I
went down town about 6 & back
to tea at 7 — Mied. Pleasant —
ev'f — for some time swinging
in hammock — Then read
newspaper — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 13. 1885.

Very close & muggy m'f. Rain
commenced about noon & fell nearly
most of the afternoon. Damp ev'f.
Did not go down town in the m'f.
but spent the time looking after the
men who are culling the wood
work in the hall with oil & a little
staining of [illegible] lumber — Dinner at 1.30
In the afternoon I completed the
cleaning out of the studio — also
the Cherry bed Room — Tea about
7 — Then went down to see Father
who had a bad night last night.
Home again about 8.30 after
having gone down town — Then
read paper — had a game &
to bed between 10 & 11.
