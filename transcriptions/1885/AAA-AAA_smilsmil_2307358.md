## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 29, 1885.

very heavy shower during the night — bright
mng. — then clouds & cool breeze — in the
afternoon some very light showers. Did
not feel like exercising & spent
most of the mng with Annie on
Pine Knoll. Finished the mng
by taking a nap — then dinner
abt — after dinner, another nap
& between 4 & 5 a. & I went up
noon-made Trail a little ways
— Tea at 6 — Evng — with a
number of others, we went up
to Mrs. White's Piazza to see
the moon rise — after that
back to hotel & to bed abt
10.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 30. 1885.

muggy, hot day, much of the time
overcast — some very light showers —
Bkfst at 8 — at 9 went to Pine Knoll &
spent some time getting a map of the
positions of trees — then Annie & I
went to Chappel Pond Brook and
followed it up some ways — then climbed
the mt. to the new Port Henry Road — Ret'd
part way on that — back to hotel at
12.30. Dinner at 1 — Assisted Dr. Bull
in constructing a tripod sundge &
climbed a tree for Miss Kate Hilland's
parrot "Pickles" — After 4 went with
Annie into the Beede Ravine & had
quite a hard climb — Back in time
for tea at 6 — Evng on Piazza until
near 10 — then in & packed Trunk —
near 11 where I got to bed —
Brilliant moonlight night.
