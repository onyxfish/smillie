## Left Page

### March, Monday 23. 1885.

Still cold & Raw — grey day — After bkfst.
went to 37' S. with some bkfst. for Father
that I had prepared myself. Found
both Father & Mother better — Then to Grady's —
about Tablet for "Stranger" — to N. A. at about
10.30 — made drawing after Wm Hart's
"Modern Cinderella" & a picture by Nicoll.
Almost frozen by 1 o'clk. Then home to
lunch — back to studio at 2.30 & spent
the Rest of the afternoon putting sheep
into foreground fld. C. "A Bluff on Marble-
head Neck". At 6.15 called for a min-
ute at 21 W. 37 S. Found Mother down
in basement at tea. Father up & about.
Home & had dinner about 6.45 —
Eve'g soon after 8. with A. to Mother
Cole's — 104 E. 39' — Played Bezique
with her, Geo. & Valentine until 10 —
then home & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 24. 1885.

Bright — much milder but windy & dirty.
After bkfst. went over to Father's with some
steak sandwich for him — Both he & Mother
seem to be much improved — Then on to
studio shortly after 10 — Found my Obelisk
picture so bagged on the stretcher that I
could do nothing with it & at last took it
down to Kelley & had him re-stretch it —
back to studio at 12 & commenced painting
a sky — Home at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked
on picture until about 6 — then went
to 21 W. 37 S. where I saw Father for
a few minutes — then home to dinner at
6.30 — At 8 went with A. to Miss Rodgers'
38 W. 22° S. left her there & went to
studio — washed up some brushes and
worked on a ~~trade~~ mark sort of design
for Kurtz (Chas. Mr.) until 10 o'clk — then
went for Annie — met there Miss Ella
White of Phila. Home at about 11 & right
to bed.
