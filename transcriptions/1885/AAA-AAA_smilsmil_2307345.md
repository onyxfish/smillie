## Left Page

### July, Friday 3, 1885.

A very charming day, in spite of some showers, overcast at first, then clearing. Showers before 10 & again before 5 — Evg perfectly clear & nicer — the day spent in hard work, at a great variety of little things — went down town about 11 & back about noon — dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon most of the time hanging curtains with Annie — Tea at 7 — Eveg waiting for Geo. & Valentine & Miss Hunt who left W[ilkes?]-B[arre?] on the 1 P.m. Train, D.L.&W. We sent a conveyance to the depot for them & they did not get to the house until after 9 — Then chatting for an hour & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 4, 1885.

A hot day with a shower, or series of them, after 4 P.m. All up to bkft. about 8.30 — after that, sauntering about — Geo. helped me to put up some of the bamboo curtains on the piazza — He, Valentine & I walked down to D[r]. Nattralp's & back, to see something of the town — Dinner about 1.30, by which we lost the parade & the fun on the Fair Ground where we could hear the shouts of the beholders. There was to have been a balloon ascension but the showers & other things spoiled it. After tea, on upper piazza for some time — later, we had a game of Bajaue — & to bed before 11.
