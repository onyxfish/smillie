## Left Page

### September, Thursday 17. 1885.

Beautiful day — After going down town & looking after the men about 11 o'clk. Annie, Lib, Collins & I, with Jim Junt went to the fair where we roamed about until after 1, then home to dinner. About 3 we went off for a ride out past Saml. Brewster's around to top of Well's Hill on South Road — no view of distance owing to many fires. Then on to Elk Lake Road & through to Fair Dale & then home, a very long ride — got in at 6.15 — Tea about 7 — Brilliant moonlight night — walked Piazza with Collins from 9 to 10 — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 18. 1885.

Very mild — overcast afternoon with a shower between 5 & 6 o'clk. Pkfp. late — much time in the m'g spent with the men ploughing & picking up stones — About noon I took my etching traps & went to fair ground. Started an etching of a subject that was not what I wanted & found it much more work than I expected. Back to dinner at 1.30 — After that again with men, getting out big stone to mark Eldred's corner. Heated tar & climbed a beech tree from which I had cut dead wood and tarred the cut ends — then the rain went down town soon after 6 — Tea about 7 — Ev'g. Reading & writing — Annie, Hy. & Collins playing cribbage — Remarkable effects of mist & moonlight between 10 & 11 o'clk — To bed soon after 11.
