## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 11. 1885.

Clear, wintery day — to studio about
10 & to work on "Breezy Mt. on the
french coast" — Painted all the mf
on the foreground sea — At 2.30
went to 37th St. & saw the folks —
father seems to be better — At 1.15
home to lunch — back at 2.30
& continued work on sea, then on
foreground. Frank Waller & thru
his wife called to see my big picture
which was taken over to the N. A.
at 6 o'clc. 6.30 when I got
home to my dinner — Busy
until near 10, at work with a.
over invitation cards to a recep[tion]
that she intends giving next
Weds. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 12. 1885.

Pleasant day & milder — to studio
at 10 & got started on my obelisk
painting — 21 × 31 — altho' I did not
feel at all like it — head hurting me.
A canvasser from Stoddards — with
portfolios of Darley's Shakespeare
came in & offered to exchange a proof
set ($100) for a painting, & I agreed
to. Mr. Worman of Nashville Art Ass[oc]
called with a young lady & I promised
3 pictures to him — Home to lunch
soon after 1 & back at 2.30 — worked
on obelisk, getting in only sky.
About 5 walked up to 37th St. with
Geo & called on Father & Mother. found
Ed. there — Will came in also & Mrs.
Winans was there — Father is very
feeble & I cannot but feel very
apprehensive as I look at him.
Home to dinner at 6.30 — Bella
out & Annie & I struggled with
the boy to get him to sleep until
after 9 o'clc. To bed at 11.
