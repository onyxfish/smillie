## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 22, 1885

Bright mng., overcast later & some light, sprinkling rain at night. Got to studio at 10.30
after having been to K. & V. for pk
for Pascui — Spent most of the mng. & part of the afternoon experimenting and trying aquatint grounds — to lunch as usual & back — dry painted on my plate as long as I could see — then, with Chauncey, went to Century to consult Hamerton's Graphic arts to learn what I could of aquatint processes — met Gifford & learned more from him than I did from the books — Home soon after 6 — Annie did not feel well enough to go down to dinner. Evett — a little reading — a game of cards — & then to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 23, 1885

Very nice, pleasant day — To studio before 10 & spent the mng dry painting on Pascui plate. At 12.30 went to Houghton's Ch. 29th St. bet. 5th & Mad. Ave. — Dr. Jno. C. Draper's funeral — Quite an impressive ceremony — processions — boy-choir chanting etc. etc — At 2, with body to Harlem depot, the pall bearers leaving the body in the car. Home at a little after 2 — lunch — then took Annie out for a walk to cor. of 37th St. & Park Ave. then back — & to studio at 3.20 — worked with dry paint until 4.45 — then to Reichard's to see W. Louis series of illustrations to Samia — Excellent — Home soon after 6 — dinner — Evett, some reading — some writing — then Walter & Mary C. came in and visited until 10 — To bed soon after 11.
