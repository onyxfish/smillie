## Left Page

### May, Friday 22, 1885

Heavy rain in the mng. with showers during the day — Got up
with a nasty headache with which I struggled all day —
worked for an hour or two in the mng on Saratoga Mon'. Then gave
up & for awhile on bed — Mail came — letters from Father Ingraham
Dana Austin & Kurtz — with a set
f pf. from Watts Cat. drawings.
Dinner at 2.15 — After that wrote letters to Kurtz and
Dana Austin. Tea as usual
Evg. Reading & then a
game of "Stop" — To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 23, 1885

Muggy & overcast — Parts of the
day very pleasant. In the mng
worked on pen sketch of Saratoga
Mon'. & finished it — commenced
one of a battle ground — don't ex-
actly know which one. Dinner
after 2 — Finished Battle ground
about 5 Annie & Preston went
off for a drive & I took Jessie
under saddle & had an hours
ride — went beyond Trappe Station
& along a wood road in a pine
wood. Showers threatening — on
my return, had a scrub and
a short rest before tea — Evg.
after reading awhile. Julie
Lilian & Lizzie Pratt played
with me a game of Bazique —
To bed at 10.45.
