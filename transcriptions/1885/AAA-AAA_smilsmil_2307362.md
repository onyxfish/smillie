## Left Page

### August, Thursday 6, 1885

Another beautiful cool day —
Ther. went down to 46° last night.
Went down town before bkfst —
the n'f spent with Coyle
on the lawn & with the car-
penters — Went with Annie to
Father's before noon & then down
town again. Dinner at 1.30
Worked on Earth Closet plans
— met wagon cane — Then, at
4, with A. & Jim went to Father's
& took him, Mary & her children
out — Rode over the Meshopken
Hill & back through the Brewster
woods — Home with Father at 5
— then we went around the lake
being out an hour longer — Tea
after 7 — Flora Lathrop's called —
When I went home with her
again went in to see Father —
Eve'g, read paper & wrote
a little, Nearly 11 when we
went to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 7, 1885

overcast n'f that gradually drew
to rain late in the afternoon — the
day passed in superintending the
men — old Coyle dodged about. Payn
hauled a couple of loads of earth &
stone to the roadway — & Cottrell &
Herrick are drawing their work
to a close — I went down town
in the n'f, & twice in the afternoon
Father seemed brighter in the
afternoon — a letter from Kate —
she gives little to hope in the way
of recovery of stolen goods. Eve'g
close enough to have a fire —
we read until after 9 — then
a game of Bezique & to bed about
10.30.
