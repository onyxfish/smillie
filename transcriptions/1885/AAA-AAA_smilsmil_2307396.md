## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 13, 1885

Blustering, strong wind from
East all day — Some rain in
the early mf. & a little again
at night. No outside work done.
Soon after bkfs. I got to work
at the biting of my etching on
Jones's die, begun some time ago
for Father — Things went along
very nicely altho' I found that
I had to be lively in my movements
as compared with acid working
on copper — All my spare time
during the day was given to the
die — after biting I worked with
the graver as long as I could see.
Then down town & attended to
chores — Home & tea before 7 —
Evef. writing letters & getting
bills & accts into order.

To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 14, 1885

Brilliant mf., clouding over later,
but on the whole, a fine day. & for
me, a very busy one. Bkfs. soon after
8. & at 9.30 Mother Cook. D. H. and
Nellie started for Wf. Mother C.
not well — She had a fainting turn
last night & again at the bkfs. table this
mf. Only Pettus & Rhinevault at
work to-day. Herrick came up in
the afternoon with the shutters that
are to be put up for winter & fitted
them on — I worked all day on
proving No. 34 with which I had
trouble & a depressing questioning
as to whether it was worth the
trouble — I am disappointed in the
plate: it is fussy, commonplace &
conventional & in some parts, clever.
Went down town after 6 & did not
get back to tea until after 7 —
About 8 went to Aunt H's & marked
some furniture pkgs. for her & put
up proof of Lions (large) after Rosa Bonheur
to go by 2¢ to Father — Home at 9 —
Minnie continues to improve — news
from Father not encouraging.
