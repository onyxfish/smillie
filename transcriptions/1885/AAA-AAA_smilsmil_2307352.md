## Left Page

### July, Friday 17, 1885

A very hot day — Ther. last night
did not get below 70° & to-day
up to 90° but a pleasant
breeze blew all day. Did a
good deal of loafing. Called
on Father about 10.30 — then down
town — messages — Called on the
McCartney Sisters — saw their
drawings & gave them some
instructions — Home after 12.
Dinner after 1. Worked on
plans in the afternoon for
improvements in Etching
Room — At 6.30 light wagon —
Took Father out for 40 m. —
There at 7.30 Took a. & the boy
around the lake — Refreshing —
had tea about 8.30 & then in
hammock on piazza until
near 10 — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 18, 1885

Delightfully cool mt. B[e]d
at near 9 — When I came down
stairs found a Tax collector waiting
for me. Wrote notes to Wm Neilson
& Walter Cook. — After 11 when I
went to see Father — then down town
— home soon after 12 — dinner at 1.30
Afternoon, completed drawings
for sink & closets in Etching Room
& gave final directions to Cottrell
& Herrick about work to be done while
Annie & I are away — With Cuny
for a time superintending him —
After 5 went again to Father's —
then, after 6, down town — did
not get home until after 7 — Tea
late. — Eve'g quite cool —
Annie read awhile from Adam
Bede — then we had a game
of cards — almost 11 when we
got to bed.
