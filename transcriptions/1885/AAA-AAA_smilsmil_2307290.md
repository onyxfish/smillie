## Left Page

### March, Sunday 15, 1885

Rain nearly all day with S. E.
wind — B[illegible] at [illegible] — It was 12 o'clk.
before I went to 37 S. Found Father
in bed + [illegible] sale there — Father having
had trouble during the night with
his stomach + bowels — I fixed some
cracker + hot water — which he ate
a little. Home to dinner at 2 —
Mother Cook, Geo. Emma J. and
Lib Home there — They stayed until
5 — Then again I went to Father's
\+ stayed with him until nearly
9.30, getting him up + dressed
\+ coaxing him to eat some farina
gruel — He seems brighter, in
spite of stomach trouble, than for
several days. Had tea on getting
home at 7.30 — Evening Parker
with Annie until 10 — Reading.
Then to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 16, 1885

Very bright; pleasant m[illegible]. Saw solar
eclipse through a piece of smoked glass
at 1.30 — overcast p.m. + snow at
night. Got to Studio at 10.15 — on my
way stopped at Mulligan's — carpenter.
To see Van Riper — by appointment, but
he was not in — Miss Hattie Gibson
called with note from Charl. + I gave
her a note to Mrs. Susan N. Carter —
she wishes a scholarship at Cooper
Institute. Half ton of coal came —
I worked on 21 × 31" "Stranger etc" —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 —
continued painting as long as I could
see — Cha. McKurtz came in after 6
so that it was 6.40 when I got home
to dinner — At 8 took Annie to her
mother's + I went to see Father —
Found him very much better — Geo.
\+ Chave there — at 9.30 went for a —
all had a game of Bazique —
Home at 10.30 — To bed about 11.30
