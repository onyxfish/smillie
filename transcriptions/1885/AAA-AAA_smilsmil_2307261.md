## Left Page

### January, Saturday 10. 1885.

Pleasant day — to when I got to Studio — frames for coming Storm there — felt mat, heavy & unbecoming, so that I spent most of mf cutting heavy white mat & trying to concoct something that would be an improvement on what I had — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — Did some work on Sketch. w W.S of Cliffs near Etretat — After I went to Grady & McKeever's — gave them a blowing up — & they got out of temper too. Home to dinner at 6, at 8, with A — at Phil sonata — a very fine one — A went home with her br. Ed W. & I went to the Annual meeting at Century there until midnight — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 11. 1885.

Charming mf. with a nasty afternoon of rain & wind — strong wind & heavy rain at night. In the mf — Annie & I went to All Souls Church — Sabin Aunt Anna's pew. C. Y. Turner there — Heard Ed W. Everett Hale preach an interesting sermon on the sermon on the mount. After service, went to Century with Annie — Mary Sprague Cook & Miss Williams — there met & was introduced to E. E. Hale & his son — walked up home — dinner at 2 — About 4 A I went to the Chelsea — in W. 23d. S — to call on Mr. & Mrs. Moschelles — they were out of town — caught in the rain — tea at 7 — about 9 went to G. C. Depot with my letter home — near 11 when I got to bed.
