## Left Page

### November, Saturday 28, 1885

A very fine winters day — got up
with a very sore throat & chest — so
that I did not dare go to Co'lc.
for fear of adding to my cold — so, I
telegraphed to Father. To studio at
10 & worked all day strengthening
a drawing of trip hammer at work
in some Patterson Foundry, for the
A.B.N. Co — don't know who made
the drawing — Charle came to
studio about 4.30 & walked up
home with me. — Evelf got
Annie on lounge & wheeled her
into Parlor before the fire — To
bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 29, 1885

A pleasant day, altho' at times
overcast. Throat & chest feeling
somewhat better than yesterday —
It was after noon before I got
out for a walk. Stopped at B.
Hale's — saw him for a few min-
utes — Altho' the bad symptoms
in Annie's case have ceased for
three days, he does not give us
any encouragement. I walked
up to Central Park & back at 1.30
Dinner at 2. Got Annie into
the Parlor, on lounge before the
fire where she stayed until
10 p.m. — I wrote letter to Father
— Evelf, most of it spent reading
old letters — After 10.30 went out
to mail Father's letter at G.C. Depot
thence to bed.
