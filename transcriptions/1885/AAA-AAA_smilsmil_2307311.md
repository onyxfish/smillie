## Left Page

### April, Sunday 26, 1885

Heavy rain most of the day — Up late
& spent the m'g lying about and
reading & sleeping — Dr. [illegible] came
in to ask for news from Father, & to say
that Katherine (Mary's) is down with the
measles. Dinner at 2 — Afternoon
wrote letter home & one to Uncle Willie
Geo. came in about 6 — walked with
me up to Depot where I mailed
my letters — then with him to 28' S—
tree on, around Wall. Sq. — up 5' Ave
home to tea at 7 — Ev'g, Reading
until 10, then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 27, 1885

Charming day — After bkf'. went to
Grandy's & ordered frame for "Recollection
of a California River" — 10.30 when I
got to studio — Most of the m'g used up
in getting my stock of mounting board
into order — Sent note & expressed two
autograph Bragman R.P. to Estes &
Lauriat — then home to lunch at 1 —
back at 2.30 & spent the afternoon
Charcoaling "Normandy Farmyard"
on 35 × 60 canvas. Did it make much
progress. Ed Parker came in with good
news from P. O'K. — Surprised by a visit
from St. Thompson (bro. in law of Harris Pardee)
Have not seen him for many years & did
not know him at first. He stayed until
6.30 — then I went home to dinner & found
a most encouraging letter from Father.
Ev'g — altho' suffering from headache,
I read aloud to the. until 9 — then we
had a game of cards & to bed at 10.30.
