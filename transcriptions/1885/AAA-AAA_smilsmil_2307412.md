## Left Page

### November, Monday 16. 1885.

Cold & at times overcast — to
studio before 10 & spent the
morn painting over a part of the
sketch upon which I worked
on Saturday — E.M. Ward
called just as I was about
to start for home, for lunch —
I got there at 1.30 — back at
3 — out of sorts during the
afternoon with an old time
neuralgic sort of distress in
about my stomach — I was
not able to do anything —
home about 5 — did not
take a mouthful of dinner
spent the evety on the bed
sharing it with Annie
who dare not yet get up —
to bed for the night
about 10.30, getting through
without disturbance.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 17. 1885.

Charming day — feeling de-
pressed by the discomfort in
my stomach as it recalls to
memory the miseries of 6 years
ago. Got to studio soon after
10 — Will came in, bringing
Father's lion die & explaining
changes that he wishes to have
made in the West Shore vigt.
I worked awhile on a Mexican
figure group. Home to lunch
at 1, after writing a note to Ehninger
back at 2.30 — Will sent up
West Shore drawing & I made
a new sketch — Yale came
in about dusk — Home at 5.15
Dinner at 6 — About 8 Yale
came in — at 9 went out to
druggist's with him — Read
aloud to Annie until she
fell asleep — to bed at 10.30
