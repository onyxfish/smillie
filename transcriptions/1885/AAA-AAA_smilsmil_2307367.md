## Left Page

### August, Sunday 16. 1885.

Another beautiful day —
After bkfst. went to see father
just as he & mother were
about to start for church —
they have a carriage every
Sunday mf. Then I went
up to Fair Ground where a
Darkey Camp Meeting has just
started — after remaining there
a while, ret'd home & spent
the rest of the mf. enjoying
out of doors under the beech
trees north of the house —
Dinner at 1.30 — After that
had a nap in hammock
under the trees — Mother &
Kate came up — I went again
to see father about 6 — Annie
joined me — Tea at 7 —
Evef. wrote some letters
took them to the P.O. at
9.30 — Home & to bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 17. 1885.

Overcast, with two or three brief
showers between 10 & 11 o'clk —
Bkfst. later than usual, then out
& after the men — most of my time
given to the Earth Closet. Kate
Riley, her daughter Kate & Fanny
Jessup called — Annie had gone
out riding with the Misses Leggett
so I entertained them — At noon
I went down town with part of
Earth Closet & reduced its capacity
as it throws down too much earth.
Stopped to see father — his night
was alarming one & to-day he seems
more prostrated than at any other
time since he came here — Dinner
at usual time — again went down
town about E.C. & at last got it to
work — altho' it is still too liberal
The painters got away — Payne
at work on the road — At 6
went again to see father, who
seems easier — Annie there —
back & Tea at 7 — Evef. some
writing — To bed soon after 10.
