## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 15, 1885

Another very mild & pleasant day — spent the mff. about the place — Eliz. H. under the weather so that she could not go down to depot with me — went down town before 11. Dinner at 12.45 — At 1.20 went to depot in buggy & back at 4.45 with Chas. Collins, just arr'd from N.Y. Then down town for the mail — so stiff from a lame back, suddeney developed, that I could hardly get about — A glorious sunset & lovely sky — Tea about 7 — Evely, Mr. & Mrs. Danl. Sayre, Mr. Comstock — Mr. Jones & miss Sayre called — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 16, 1885

Cold, cloudy, windy — Bkfst. at usual time — Spent the mff. about among the men, ploughing etc — went down town before noon hoping to find a laborer there without success — Dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon Derrick came & put catches on the transoms in main room. About 5.30 walked down to P.O. with Chas. Collins — Mr. & Mrs. Husted & Mr. H's mother called before we left. Tea before 7 — Evely chatting until near 10. Still favorable acct. from Father.
