## Left Page

### February, Sunday 15, 1885

Brilliant day. Spent the
mng indoors — at 1 walked up
to 57th St. & back by 5th ave. The
air mild & still — Dinner at 2.
Annie went out in the afternoon
& while I had charge of the boy
between 5 & 6 o'clk. Edith Wright
came in. She stayed to tea at
[illegible] — I've f. I wrote a long
letter to Father — at 10 Took
Edith to Mother Cook's & then
to G. C. D. with letter — home
at 10.30 & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 16, 1885

Torrents of rain & hurricane of wind.
Got to studio soon after 10 & found
it so dark that I determined
not to work on my big picture — I
temporarily stretched a canvas 21
× 31 & blocked out my old idea of
"A Stranger in a Strange Land." — The
obelisk in Central Park with snow
& moonlight — at 11.40 I got to work
with color — Home to lunch at 1.30
— nearly wrecked on my way — back
at 3 — scraped painting all off
in two hours had covered the
canvas, but not as I had hoped
to — cleaned up & home at 6 —
weather cleared — dinner —
Read for awhile — a game of
Bezique — then gave my feet
a hot bath & to bed about 11.
Charl called in for a little
while early in the eveng.
