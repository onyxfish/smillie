## Left Page

### August, Sunday 30. 1885.

Rain' in the m'f, clearing later to a hot afternoon. Up at 8 & had blkf. with Mother & Kate about 9.
Father very lethargic. Hel'd. came in to see him & brought a leg of mutton. Will also came in. About 11 went over to Mother Cook's & was invited to stay there — accepted — then to 110 E. 38 & went through the house. Then again to hotel. At 2 back to 39' S. & had dinner. To Hotel about 3. Will came down again. I wrote to Ge°. & to Annie. At 6.45 again to 39' S. & had tea. Hotel at 8, Ed Parker & Will there. I wrote to Mary before 9 — then read awhile. Returned to 39° S. before 10 — sat awhile with Mother Cook & Ge°, then to bed. Rain during the night.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 31. 1885.

A muggy & stifling m'f, clearing into a breezy, pleasant day. Street noises awakened me about 5, but I got naps after that. Up & 8 & blkf. About 9.30 when I got to S'. Cloud. Father much debilitated altho' he had passed a comparatively quiet night. About 10.30 I rode down town with Mother in a B. way car. She desired to see the Battery — we loitered there some time & then took 3d Ave. Elevated R.R. that she might see the Brooklyn Bridge. Got to hotel at 12.30 Then I went to A.Q. Newman's & spent some time selecting hardware — then to Studio — then to 39' S. & then to hotel. Back to 39' S. to dinner at 6.30. Ge°. Valentine, Walter & Ge°. J. there. To Hotel again at 8.30. Father has taken considerable nourishment but shows no increase of strength — is drowsy much of the time. Back to 39' S. at 10. Chatted with Walter until 11 — then to bed after having suffered all day with [illegible] at Smith & Green's at 3 o'ck a beastly headache.
