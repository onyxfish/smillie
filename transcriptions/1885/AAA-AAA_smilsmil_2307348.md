## Left Page

### July, Thursday 9, 1885

Very hot day with strong breeze. Thunder storms passed very near us in the afternoon but we got only a slight sprinkle on the N.f. W. Park drove up with Mr. P. + her baby — He then took me down Town (where I attended to chores) + off on a short ride while Mr. P. visited with Annie. The carpenters continued the putting up of ward robe in S.W. bd. room. Dinner soon after — Battte's boy came + I set him to work weeding lawn — Lawn mower came in the afternoon + I worked for half an hour with that. Then a bath + change + tea at 7 — Eve'g, very mild — in hammock until after 9 — Annie giving me a synopsis of Wallace's "Ben Hur" — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 10, 1885

Heavy wind + rain last night — breezy cloudy out with delightful effects of color + temperature — went down Town between 10 + 11 + attended to various matters. Then following after the carpenters + planning new work — Spent some time laying out plans for ex— of my pictures at the various fall ex— out west — Dinner after 1, more work until 5 when Annie + I went to Aunt K's and found Father, Mother + Kate there, they having come from N.f. to-day — They bring stories of tremendous heat in N.f. Ther. there 96° yesterday — fully 10° higher than I get here. Father + Mother seem to have come through very comfortably, altho' they were tired — Eve'g, a little reading + a short game of cards + to bed about 10.30.
