## Left Page

### October, Saturday 3, 1885

Rain during most part of the m'f. Then it suddenly cleared bright & mild — 9 o'clk bfo we had bkfs — found enough to occupy me about the house & spent part of m'f in big chair — feeling the effects of being routed out three last night by trouble in the bowels. Dinner at 2 — after that went out & made a camera-lucida sketch for etching of big maples N.E. of the house on new lot — Curry, Coyle & Rheinvault at work after 1 o'clk. After 4 until 6 I worked about the roots of beech trees near the house — Tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 went down town — back in an hour — all sat in our bed room, where there was a pleasant fire, until 10 o'clk — then to bed — again up during the night — at 1 A.M. — same trouble as night before.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 4, 1885

A heavy, driving rain from the east until about 3 o'clk — then the weather began clearing — bkfs at 9 — During the m'f wrote letter to Father — had dinner after 2 — About 6 I went down town & as Annie felt discouraged I went for Dr. Richardson who came up soon after — the result of his visit was to give us both hope & confidence, both much needed — Fanny & Elligts spent the ev'f with us in our bed room — They have spent much of the day in preparation for their departure to-morrow m'f. To bed about 10.30
