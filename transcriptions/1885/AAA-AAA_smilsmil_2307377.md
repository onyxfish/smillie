## Left Page

### September, Saturday 5, 1885

Dubious mt., gentle rain in the afternoon & brilliant sunset & eve't. Bkf't about 8. Carriage at 9 & soon after, Miss Abbatt started for home — Drove over to D.L. & W. Depot, taking Marin & misses Clark & Fogbaum along for a ride — waited for trains on time — then back by old depot road — gathering ferns etc. — got home soon after 1 — dinner. Tried to stake out work for Coyle but rain prevented — Tea before 7 — Eve't the girls amused themselves with games — I read part of the time — To bed soon after 10.

P.c. — at 6 o'clk. encouraging letter from Kate concerning Father — about 8, a telegram from Chas announcing the safe & comfortable arrival home of Father.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 6, 1885

Delightful day, but my cold in the head very much marred my enjoyment of it — Bkf't soon after 8 — The mist off over the hills very beautiful. Spent most of the mt. about the place — part of the time with Annie setting out the ferns got yesterday mt. Dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon until about 5 razing about the place & around the hill & fair grounds. Then in & wrote to Father — Will & Ehringer. Tea before 7 — about 8 went to P.O. with mail — back & rest of eve't holding my head. To bed about 10.30.
