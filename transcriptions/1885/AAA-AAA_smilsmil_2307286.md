## Left Page

### March, Saturday 7, 1885

Snow & nastiness generally — Got
to studio about 10 — The whole
mf spent in getting & stretching
in frames for a Toronto exhib—
& writing notes connected therein
with — Home about 1.30 — back
about 3 — took a rest — About
4 went in to Frank Waller's —
he & his wife having a studio
Reception. Home before 6 — Charl
took dinner with us — After it
I went to 37 S. & sat awhile
with Father & Mother — About
9 got to Century — my big picture
looked unexpectedly well &
many pleasant things were said
to me by bro-artists & others con-
cerning it — walked up town
with Geo — Got home just about
midnight & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 8, 1885

Some flying snow in the mf., but it
soon cleared — a cold, raw, March-like
day — Bkfs. soon after 9 — 10.30 when
I went to Dr. Yale's — had to wait for
him — he told me that he considered
Father's illness a grave matter owing
to his age, but he did not think there
was yet foundation for alarm — Got to
37 S. at 12 o'clk — found Father yet in
bed but he got up & dressed with Kate's
aid — I went home at 12.30 & went with
C. to Century & took a look at pictures —
we walked up — I took cheap cab at the
G.C. Depot & went after Father & got him
to the house at about 2. Dinner soon
after — Father sat in big chair before the
fire & ate his dinner there — Charl &
Geo came — he did not leave the chair
until 5 when I got a cab & he was
taken back to 37 S — Charl & I with
him there until near 6 — then home
tea after 7 — wrote a letter to Mary
concerning Father & atq went to the
P.O. with it —
