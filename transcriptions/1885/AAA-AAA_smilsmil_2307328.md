## Left Page

### May, Saturday 30, 1885

*Decoration Day.*

Cool overcast [illegible], some showers
in the early afternoon + rain at night.
Got to studio about 10 + soon to work on
Pagoda over — [illegible] Saratoga — Home
at 1. 30 after going to telegraph office +
sending message to Syracuse accepting
offer of $50 for Recollection of California
River — Park + wife, of Montrose, came
in, at home, for a few minutes — lunch —
back at 3 — then Mr + Mrs. P— called at
the studio — I worked on pen sketch
next to — then Annie came in +
we walked home together. Dinner
Mother Cook shaving it with us —
Evef. we had a game of Bazique —
nearly 10 when I took Mother C.
home — Raining — Back + to bed
before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 31, 1885

Misty, muggy day — some sunshine and
some strinkling in the afternoon — [illegible]
about 9 — after it, reading for awhile,
then got to my writing + wrote notes to
Austin, Herrick, Cottrell — Koehler — Pate +
Jones — then, at 12.30, out with Annie for a
walk — stopped + saw Yale to ask him
some questions about Father — Home at 2 —
Elizabeth Horne came to dinner with us.
Afternoon, Geo., Nellie + Sheldon came,
also Charl, who brought me a photo proof
of Normandy Cliffs — About 5 wrote notes
to Rob., Barry, Phil. about Pullman Car tickets
to Jesse Metcalf, Prov. R.I, acknowledging check
for Bluff on Marblehead Neck. To Col. Prentiss
Ingrahm + letter to Father — Tea at 7 —
Evef. wrote a note of sympathy to Wm
Bassett on the death of his wife. Went to
depot with letters at 10.30 — then home
+ to bed soon after 11.
