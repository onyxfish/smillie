## Left Page

### March, Friday 13. 1885.

Clear + very cold, windy day. Got to studio after 10 + to work on "a Breezy Day etc" — working generally over sea + land. The picture looks fresh + bright + technically, I think it about as good as I have done. Home at 1 — a bowl of clam broth was sent over to father — had lunch + at 2 Annie + I went to Phil. Rehearsal. Jennie Tweedy there with Florence. — did not get much pleasure out of the rehearsal. After it walked up to studio with Annie + Sid. Home — went in for a few minutes — then went up to 37th S. + sat awhile with Father whose stomach is somewhat rebellious — where there he took some Beef Et. but it would not stay down. I went out + got some raw oysters for him — Home at 6.30 to dinner — Evett. Diiing came in + made a call — after 9 Annie + I had a game of Bazique — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 14. 1885.

Very pleasant day + much milder. To studio at 10 + spent [illegible] painting on 21×31" "Stranger etc" — worked on groups of trees + snow — Home to lunch — 1.15 — back at 2.30 — worked until 4 — washed up — Annie came before 4.30 — we went to Am. Art Ass'n. Private View of the Geo. I Seeny Paintings — met there Chas. Smith (Corinne Colman's husband.) at 6 walked up to 37th S. + I made a 15 m. call on Father — home at 6.30 — dinner — at 8, at Phil. Concert — Cannot say that I enjoyed the concert but was strangely interested by the Berlioz "Fantaisie" Episode in the life of an Artist — we walked home — in at 10.30 + to bed at 11.
