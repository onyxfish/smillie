## Left Page

### September, Sunday 13, 1885

Pleasant day with strong, warm south
wind — In bed with head ache
until near 1 o'clk. Then up & had
dinner — The afternoon was spent
sauntering around. Near 6 o'clk
went down town & met Payne coming
up to see me — then to Cumy's &
arranged with him to come to
work to-morrow m'f. Tea at
6.30 Evef. wrote to Father,
& Dr. Yale & a few words to ac-
company Annie's invitation to
ge: & Nellie to visit us — To bed
about 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 14, 1885

Charming mild day. Up before
7 to see to the starting of Petters at the
ploughing of the Cooper lot & Cumy
at cutting down two big beech trees.
Before b'kf. I climbed a tree & cut
out some dead wood. — Before 11
I went to fair ground with charcoal
sketching materials & worked until
1 upon a sketch there. — Annie and
Elizabeth came up for a while.
Dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon, for a
while, worked in studio on the
sketch of the m'f. Tea at usual
time — Evef. reading papers,
chatting & to bed about 10.30.
