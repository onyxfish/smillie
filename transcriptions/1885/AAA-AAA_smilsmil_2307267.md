## Left Page

### January, Thursday 22, 1885

A very cold day with more wind +
dish. To studio at 10 + worked on
large W.C. all the mtf. scrobbing in
general washes of color — Home at
back soon after 2 + worked until
4.30, getting my paper almost
covered. Home at 6 — dinner —
Evef. went to Choral Club concert
at Chickering Hall. — Enjoyed it
only fairly well. — Robt. minuor
sat across the aisle from me. —
Annie + I walked up home —
in at 11.20 + right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 23, 1885

Not quite so cold + slightly overcast.
To studio at 10 — A Mr. Braun called to
see me about selling some English W.C.
paintings — then Neeley came + took up all
the rest of the mtf. in fixing Panel
stretcher + restretching "Evef. Shadows"
+ some other jobs — Home to lunch + back
as usual. Fine — Afternoon, worked as
long as I could see — over sky, distance
+ sea of big W.C. Home at 6 — dinner
Evef. with Annie in dining room
Reading + commenting on Mr. Barrington's
Prefaces to Met. Museum Cat. of Watts'
pictures + a game of Bezique.
To bed about 11.
