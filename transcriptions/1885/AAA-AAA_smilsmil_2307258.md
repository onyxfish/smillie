## Left Page

### January, Sunday 4, 1885

A dismal day, with a little snow being much disturbed the night before we did not get up until late — hlp. a little before 10 — then lounged about until near 12 — then to bed & had a nap — about 1.30 Charl came in with some prints from Recent Photos. Dinner soon after 2 — Afternoon played with the boy & wrote letters — At 6 went out to post them — met br. Ge. & after going to G.C. Depot, I walked with him down 4th ave — leaving him at 28th St — then to 25th St. & home by 5th ave. — In & had tea at 7. Evef. in dining Room — made two autographs with bits of pen sketches in two albums for Champney & Pope [illegible] — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 5, 1885

Charming day, much milder — overcast toward evef. Up late — did not get to studio until after 10 — then to work on my "Coming Shower" & slashed in a foreground fairly well. — Home at 1 — back at 2.30 & made pen & ink sketch for W.C. cat. Miss Kate Hillard & her friend Miss Shutter called — then some young people from Art Students League, thro' Frank Walker to get a transfer on my press — So, my afternoon was fully occupied as long as I had daylight. After 5 went to Knoedler's & took a look at the pictures there — Home at 6 — dinner — Annie had been out making calls — among others, on Mrs. [illegible] — not in. Evef. we sat in dining Room & I wrote up my list of Water-color works.
