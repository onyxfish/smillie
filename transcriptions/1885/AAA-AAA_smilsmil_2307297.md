## Left Page

### March, Sunday 29, 1885

A fall of about 4 in. of snow during
the night. The weather clearing.
Up late — b'kfst. after 9 — Read
awhile, then feeling sleepy I
lay down on the bed & had a
half hour nap. Dr. Yale came
in on his way home from church
— before he left, R.C. — a note from
Kate saying that neither was out
of sorts again & they would not
be able to get to our house to
dinner to celebrate the 53d
anniversary of Father's & mother's
wedding — I went around at once
— one o'clk — back at 2. & Annie
& I sat down to our big dinner
alone. About 4 we went to 37"
5'. Found Geo. & Jakey there. Yale
came in later — Father seemed
very much brighter when we left
at 5 for our walk. We got home
at 6.15 — Will & his family called
at 5.20 — Tea at 7 — evng.
I read until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 30, 1885

Clear day — rather cold & windy — To Studio
soon after 10 & commenced Painting on
20 × 24" "The Gothic Range. Adirondacks. from
the East" — a picture begun years ago
for Mr. Geo. Whitney & never finished because
the thought of his gallery of fine paintings
always paralyzed every effort to do my best.
Now that he is dead I must finish it to
discharge my indebtedness. I worked in
the sky with the palette knife & in the after-
noon painted the distant mountains but
cannot yet tell with what success, the
rest of the painting being so very much out
of key. Miss Buffon & friend (from Lynn) called.
Pictures for Am. Art Ass'n. prize exhib. called for —
Home to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.30 — worked
until 5 — washed up & at 6 at 21 W. 37°. Found
Annie there & Charl. — Father & mother very well.
Was told that Aunt Agnes is to undergo surgical
operation to-morrow for Ovarian tumor — Home
to dinner at 6.30 — Before 8 again went to see
Father & mother as Kate is to be with Geo. this
evng. at [illegible] Theatre (Ellen Terry — Twelfth Night.)
I played dominoes with him until 9.30 — then
home — To bed before 11.
