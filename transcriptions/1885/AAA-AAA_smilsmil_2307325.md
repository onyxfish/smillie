## Left Page

### May, Sunday 24, 1885

Rain during the night — overcast most of the day — Ther. about 70°. Bkfs. later than usual. About 10 went out with Lillian & Lizzie Platt in boat & rowed up to the very end of a creek & home at 12 — then lay down & had a nap. Dinner after 2 — For awhile with Annie & Jim on the lawn — then in & wrote a letter to Mother congratulating her on this, her 76th Birthday. Tea at 7. Spent quietly in-doors & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 25, 1885

Cool & overcast mtf — a mist that was almost a mizzle came up about 9 o'ck — by noon it had cleared — the afternoon was charming. I spent the mtf at work. Finished sketch of the lake & traced Garden Party. Dinner soon after 2 and almost immediately after dinner Lillian & Lizzie Platt, Annie & I drove over to Cloras Point where we saw the "Broad Water." The roads were muddy & I had a moments trouble with the horses in passing a traction engine on the road but the sky was lovely — we got back about 6.30 — Tea — Evelf in piazza & reading newspapers. To bed before 11.

Between 1 & 2 o'clk I took a. ntf for a row — we pulled up to & under the R.R. bridge — many picturesque bits by this water side.
