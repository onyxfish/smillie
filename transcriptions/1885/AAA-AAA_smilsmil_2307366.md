## Left Page

### August, Friday 14, 1885

Bright & breezy — looking showery at first, then clearing cool & September like. Old Coyle could not work — the ground so wet. I went down town about 10.30 — many messages — at 11.30 got to foundry & started black smiths on fixtures for Earth Closet. Stopped to see Father & mother some after noon — Father feeling very well. Home & worked putting new chords on bamboo screens. Dinner at 30 — with painters & Herrick until 3 — then to foundry and until 6 with machienist, who finished fixtures — then to P.O. — on way home stopped again at father's — Annie, Mary & Kate out making calls — glorious after noon — tea about 7 — Evening reading — a game — and to bed at a little before 11. A very light rain as we went to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 15, 1885

Brilliant cool day — Aea the day busily engaged in looking after the men. Spent much of the mf with Zerfus in tin shop getting made parts of the metal works for Earth Closet. — Dinner at 1.30 — the afternoon, worked with Zerfus, with Heinicle, with Coyle at the making of a path, with the painters (wood finishing) & with the scrub woman who labored with my studio's floor. It was not until after tea that I got down to see Father for about an hour — then home — Read paper — had our game & got to bed about 11. Ther. went down to 48° during the night.
