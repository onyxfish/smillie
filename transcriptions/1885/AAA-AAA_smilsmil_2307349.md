## Left Page

### July, Saturday 11, 1885

Beautiful day — cool & clear — after bkf. A. the boy & I went to see Father &
mother — mild excitement caused by inflation of the balloon back of the old
court house — when nearly full a gust of wind caused it to collapse — then
A. & I went on down town & transacted our business — on our way back about
noon, we were just in time to be within a few feet of the balloon when
it went up — maybe 4 or 500 feet & in 5 or 6 minutes came down on Jessup's
farm — the young man landing safely —
Dinner at 1.30 — Got a new man to work in the afternoon, on the [illegible] —
Mary, the children & a servant girl
arr. on the stage about 5 p.m. — having
come through from N.Y. all right —
Tea near 7. Eve'g Annie read
aloud from Adam Bede — to bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 12, 1885

Delightfully cool, clear day — Bkf. at 9 —
it was after 10 when I went down to see
Father & mother — Found Father out in the
garden in an easy chair, wrapped in a
shawl, sunning himself — After being with
him some time I returned home & sat
under the Beeches with Annie & Jim where
Kate joined us afternoon. I wrote several
notes before dinner at 2 — After that, in
hammock where A. read to me a
Chap. of Adam Bede — then I had a nab —
then more writing — Miss Leggett & her
sister-in-law called — After 6, with A. &
the boy, went to see "the folks" — then on
with the children (Mary's) & posted letters —
Home to tea soon after 7 — Walked for
some time on piazza with Annie — so
cool that we had a little fire. A
chapter from Adam Bede & to bed
soon after 10.
