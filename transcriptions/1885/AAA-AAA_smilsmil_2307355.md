## Left Page

### July, Thursday 23, 1885

*At Hulett's — Lake George — with Addie Hawes Wilson*
*Gent. Grant died.*

Up early. bkft at 7. Said our good
byes & with E. walked to the station.
Took 8.15 train — got to Caldwell
about 9.30 & then p. 3V. Ticonderoga —
Delicious mts. got to Huletts at
11.45 — met Addie & nearly all her
family on the dock — we set. to
the cottage, a procession of three
mainsal, most of the party took a swim —
boats — had dinner about 1.30 —
after that, some chatting, then
A. & J. took a nap — About 5, all
hands rowed over to Harbor Islands
— picked huckleberries & then had
tea — later, when the others returned
to the cottage, A. & J., in the canoe
paddled about in the moonlight —
delicious effects of sky & water —
— at times, absolutely still — we
were delighted — in before, or
soon after 9 — sat awhile on
piazza, chatting, then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 24, 1885

*Elizabeth Town — Windsor Cottage — Room 46.*

A change in the weather — pleasant
when we got up but — before 10, overcast
with a few drops of rain — After bkft —
called on Mr. Chas. Kimball for a few
minutes. At 11 we started for the landing
most of the way in a heavy pelting shower,
lighter showers before we got to Baldwin —
no rain as we went across to Ti — but
again heavy showers on Champlain while
we were at our dinner on the "Vermont" —
Pleasant sail — got to West Port at 3.30
& by stage to Kellogg's at E. Town getting
there about 5.50. Old Mr. Simmonds died
very suddenly about an hour before we
got there. I felt so headachy and
out of sorts that I took no supper
& before 10 we went to bed — We
could have driven right on to
Beadie's but thought it best to rest
one night.
