## Left Page

### November, Sunday 22, 1885

Dull + overcast — B[illegible]. at q -
fire in parlor — very comfortable
— fixed myself to write — callers
came in — Charl — Lib Howe —
Mary Sprague Cook — etc — So I
did not get out or accomplish
much before dinner at 2. From
4 to 5 I walked — Rain begin-
ning to fall — tea at 6. Eve'g
finished my letters — one to
Father for his Birthday to-
morrow. About 10 went out
to post them — Raining hard
— Annie shows no improvement
— to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 23, 1885

*Father's Birthday — 78 years old*

The nastiest sort of a nasty day —
wind, rain + sleet — to studio
before 10 — finished burnishing on
Father's die — then to work on 2d
West Shore vigt. Calversy spent
much of the m'f with me. Home
to lunch at 1 — at 2 to Grady's
then down town to A.B.N. Co. +
got p[illegible] from Father's die — think
it very near finished — to studio
at 4 — home at 5.30 — again
fire in parlor — very cheerful
— wrote notes to Father + to Fred
Smillie, went out + posted them
about 10 — to bed before 11.
