## Left Page

### September, Monday 21, 1885

Very mild & pleasant — after bkfs. Spent some time with the men, then took plate &
went to Fair Ground & finished drawing in the subject — back to Etching Room & varnished it
& made for the acid, but dinner — some time getting all things ready for biting — 3 30 when
at last I commenced — had the acid on three times — 5 — of &10 min. — Then cleaned the
ground — 6 30 when I got through — then tea — Even. Reading
papers — wet paper for printing to-morrow — about 11 when
we got to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 22, 1885

Struggling day — over cast most of the time — Thunder storm after & with heavy rain & a wind storm that howled all night —
after bkfs. To work in Etching room preparing ink & press — it was nearly noon before I took the first impression — While at dinner a young man from the A.B.N. Co. came with some photos, subjects that W'll wants me to make drawings of — Again I worked at my proofs until 6 o'clk. when I washed up —
about 7 when we had tea —
Evef. Reading papers — some cards & more chatting until
11 — then to bed.
