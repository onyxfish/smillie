## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 22, 1885

Clear & hotter than yesterday by about 2°. Waited some time after bkfst hoping that the Dr. would come — got to studio a little before 11 — Did no work — wrote some notes — Home at 1.15 — Annie went to spend the night with Edith Wright, who gives a Musical entertainment at Hazelbrook — by 1.30 train — I went back to studio at 3.15 — my sparrow spent the day with me — I did no work in the afternoon — Miss Abbatt came in to tell of the distribution of Prizes at the N.A. Home at 6.30 — dinner — Charl came in at 8 — at 8.30 I went up to Yale's — & did not get home until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 23, 1885

Still clear & very warm — after 10 when I got to studio, much of the mng spent mounting a [illegible] of Dr. [illegible]'s large etching and other chores — but no real work — home soon after 1 — before we had finished our lunch Annie came in — just arr? from Hazelbrook — Back to studio after 3 — Ranger came in about [illegible] of the Syracuse Art Club — the first time that I have met him — Home at 6 — dinner. At 7.30 Lippincott called — after a few minutes we excused ourselves & Annie, Kate & I went to the Berkeley [illegible] Cor. 5th Ave. & 9.45. To a Parlor Concert under direction of Mr. Raymond in aid of Diet Kitchen — We were late & got seats at extreme rear by an open window — The entertainment was a very good one & we enjoyed it — out at 10.45 — home at 11.15 & right to bed.
