## Left Page

### February, Monday 23, 1885

Pleasant day — Did not get out of bed until about 4 P.m —
feeling very stuff from my fall & generally wretched from my
cold. While on the lounge in the afternoon, Mr. Hale called
on Annie. I went down to dinner about 6 — & spent
the evef in dining Room.
Had a game of Bazique —
& to bed about 11 — Wt. [illegible].
Kicking up rather ugly.

Charl came in for a few minutes about 5 o'clk.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 24, 1885

Pleasant wf — afternoon overcast. Got to Studio about
10.30 — did not feel much like work — but got out big
picture & sand & glazed over
the water & other portions — Also
went to Bank & deposited Cassell
& Co's check — wrote a note to
Father — home to lunch at 1.30 —
back soon after 3 — worked on
big picture with glazes — on
foreground — Home at 6 — dinner
at 6.30 — Evef Charl came
in & we talked about
cattle ranching. to bed at
11.
