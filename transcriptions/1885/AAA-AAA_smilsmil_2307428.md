## Left Page

### December, Friday 18, 1885

Dark, dismal & muggy — but cleared by nightfall. To studio before 10 & spent sometimes in getting acids ready & other things in shape for my biting — Koehler called — wants me to do two plates for him & we talked of Eastman Johnson's "Stage Coach" & Bridgman's Salon picture of last year. Turks bargaining for a horse. So lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 and kept on with my biting until after 4 — then cleaned off the ground — Young Watrous called — then walked to No. 5 East 14th St. & looked at some gas stoves — home at 6.10 — Dinner Annie & I — both [illegible] quite used up with headache — so that we struggled thro' the evef. & to bed before 10. Charl came in for a few minutes just as I was preparing to go to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 19, 1885

Bright mtf — snow flurries in the afternoon — again clear at night — To Kimmel & Vogt's & got Pf. of Pasini etching & then to studio at 10.30 — laid around over plate & commenced additional etching — Home to lunch back at 2.30 — continued the etching as long as I could see — then to Eimer & Amend & got a pound of muriatic acid — again to studio & fixed up for the night, then home at 6 and had dinner — Evef. read aloud a little while to Annie — then read to myself — It was nearly 11 when I got to bed. Mother, Cook & John Valentine came in between 9 & 10 P.M.
