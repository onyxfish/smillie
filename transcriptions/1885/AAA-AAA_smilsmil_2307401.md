## Left Page

### October, Sunday 25, 1885

A delicious day — mild & clear. Spent some time after bkf. going about — then looking over bills. At 11.30 Annie said she would like to take a ride — I got a buggy — Kate & I carried A. down stairs & with Jim, we rode from 12 ¹⁵ to 7 ³⁰, very gently — & A. came in much refreshed. Called at Payne's while out — Dinner at 2 — Annie took dinner with me in the etching room. Then Pettus came & then Park & Riley — It was after 3 when A. was carried up stairs — I wrote letter to Father — then rested. Before 8. To work in Studio's packing big cases — until 9.30

## Right Page

### October, Monday 26, 1885

Another delicious day — All hands at work — Payne & son taking up trees & Cerny, Coyle & Rhinewalt, under my direction, botany planting them — Pettus came about 11 o'clk — with four very good trees — & about 5 P.m. he came with five more. Herrick worked all day at putting up storm shutters etc. Two of the small party spent the day at oiling wood work in main room — I did not go down town until after tea time. — Spent some time reading papers & got to bed at 10.30.
