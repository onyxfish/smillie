## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 10. 1885.

Breezy - cloudy - cool - By 9
o'clk. was at Lincoln Storage Room
42°. S. & met Kirby & with him
spent 2 hours in looking over the
pictures etc. of the late Mr. Cha. Mor[gan]
with a view to making selections
to be etched for a fine catalogue
— then back home for awhile —
11.30 when I got to studio —
made a commencement on West
Shore drawing — Home at 1 — Yale
had been there — gave Annie little
hope of weathering the storm & saving
her baby — she completely broken up —
Mother Cook came in — Back to Stu[dio]
before 3 & worked on drawing as lon[g]
as I could see — Home before 6 — din[ner] —
Yale came in before 8 — I dodged [illegible]
during the rest of the evn'g. Put[ting]
up hanging shelves in our wash [room or]
room — to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 11. 1885.

Pleasant day & milder — to studio
about 9.30 — worked on West Shore
drawing all day. making fair
progress — Evn'g. I took home a
great bundle of letters — half a bushel
or more — that I have been bothered
with for years — got interested in
looking them over — they proved to
be compositions that I wrote in Po'k
when 10 & 11 years old — & letters from
almost everybody bearing date as
far back as 1852 — I amused my-
self until after 11 — looking them
over & burning very nearly all of
them that I read. — then to bed
