## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 25. 1885.

Beautiful day. To studio soon after 10 & got to work on obelisk — going generally over all the painting — nearly 1.30 when I got home to lunch & 3 when I got back to studio. Ferguson in, anxious about a sketch of his big picture for the ill. cat. I painted until 5 o'clk — then to work on pen sketch for Kurtz — design for his title page — letter head — trade mark. He came in with a young lady before I got it finished — waited & took it away with him — It was 6.30 before I could get away from the studio — then home & had dinner. Evelf., with Carrie, looked over my Bank books & got them straightened out — then had a game of Bezique & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 26. 1885.

Very pleasant day — Pclp. late — went to Father with some roast beef sandwiches at 10 — on my way to studio. I stopped at the barber's & had my hair cut getting to work about 11.30 on 18 × 28 "Coast of France — near Etretat" — home to lunch at 1.30 — back about 3 & continued work — made parts of the sky more tender — repainted the horizon line — & worked over near water — Home to dinner a little before 6 — had a half hours nap before dinner then, with Carrie to Star Theatre — where she met Grace Ireland party & sat with them. I bought a dollar ticket & had a very good seat on the floor in balcony — heard & saw to my entire satisfaction — was intensely interested — Irving rises above his undeniable faults & disabilities & grows upon me as a really great actor. It was just about 12 when we got home — right to bed but some time before I got to sleep.
