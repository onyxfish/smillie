## Left Page

### November, Monday 30, 1885

Overcast, but clearing to-
wards night. To studio at 9.30
& spent the rest working on a
drawing for A.B.M. Co. — Mexican
woman — infant & boy — Finished
it by 1 o'clk. Then home to
lunch. Emma came in before
I left the house — back to studio
at 2.30 — Mounted drawing of
Patterson Iron works — & several
A.B.M. Co. photos. — Laid grounds
on three copper plates & put
things in order to begin etch-
ing — Skinner of Brooklyn Eagle
called — Before 5 went to Am.
Art Ass'n. & saw Kirby. Then
home — Kate came down from
Po K this afternoon — dinner
at 6 — Evely all in parlor —
Chare came in — To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 1, 1885

Overcast, cold & raw. Got to studio
about 9.30 — made reduction of part of
"Rough Spot in the Yosemite" & after
putting my press in order. Transferred
into a small plate for Etching club
[illegible] Cat. & commenced the etching
[illegible] — Home at 1 — Ed. called soon after
2 to tell us how Father is — Back to
studio at 2.30 — Kirby sent the Paini
that I am to etch for the Morgan
Cat. I etched on small plate
until 4 — Home soon after 5 —
Dinner soon after 6 — Chare
came & dined with Kate & me —
Annie on lounge in parlor —
Evely, Geo. & Nellie came —
all left about 10 — To bed about
10.30.
