## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 4. 1885.

Brilliant, beautiful day. Spent the day at the Studio clearing out closets — a horribly dusty, tiresome work. Got in coal — boy from Po'k. with Rosa Bonheur "Lions" & case from Montrose am². Home to lunch at 1.30 — Annie out — back at 2.30 — young woman there cleaning for me. Home at 6. — dinner — Evef. unpacked case from Montrose — Geo. Cook came in for a little while — I wrote a note to Father, took it to G.C. Depot. — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 5. 1885.

Very mixed beautiful day — All day at Studio putting back things into the closets & throwing away things that I did not want — got very tired — Home, after taking in to Tiffany & Co. some prints of Sheep to aid their draughtsman. — Back at 2.30 — did not get things all back into their places. — Walter came in & showed me photos of his house building at Cookerstown — Charl came in. — Home — walked up with Collins — dinner at 6 — Evef. Charl came in to arrange about a trip to Po'k. Had a game with Annie. To bed soon after 10.
