## Left Page

### January, Monday 26, 1885

Clear, windy & growing colder — Got to studio at 10. Spent the mfg working on large water color drawing — then came to the conclusion that I had made an entire mistake — in subject — treatment manipulation, so, I did not but my foot through it but I Folded paper over its face & put it carefully away — then went home to my lunch at 1 — Fanny K. there — to studio at 2,15 and worked with charcoal on canvas "The meadow Pond" — preparatory to the beginning of some work in oil — Home to dinner at 6. Eveg. at 8,30 Annie & I called on Mrs. Sharkless at her mother's, 151 E. 37th S. — saw her alone — At 9 we went to S. R. Avery's Recto[ms] at his new house in 38th S. Were shown over the house & had a sight at some of his possessions with an invitation to come & see more — Home at 10.45

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 27, 1885

Clear mfg — afternoon overcast and raw — To studio at 10 & to work with charcoal on "Meadow Pond" — finished, then tried in effect with Transparent color & home to lunch at 1 — on my return at 2.30 set palette & commenced on the sky — Nicole came in with pf. of Etching Club Cat. for me to read over — then Mrs. Saml. Spring & her daughter Anna came & talked & talked while my poor sky waited & I went almost wild — at last they went and soon after 4 I finished the laying in of my sky — at 4.30 went to Star Theatre, Bdway & 13th S. to buy tickets for Booth's Hamlet & found that he was playing at the 5th Ave. cor Bdway & 28th S. — went there & bought seats for Thurs. eveg — of speculators — then home — dinner —
