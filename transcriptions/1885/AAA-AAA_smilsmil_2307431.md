## Left Page

### December, Thursday 24, 1885

Charming, mild day. To studio before 10 — Said a Relisteing ground on parts of Pasini etching but spent most of the m[orning] experimenting trying to get an aquatint covering that would granulate satisfactorily. Home at 1:15 & back at 2:45 & resumed experiments with unsatisfactory & bewildering results — worked awhile at stopping out and varnishing on my plate — Home at 6 — dinner — Kate (the Cook) & Margaret (waitress) have gone (by permission) on a Xmas eve spree, to be out all night.

We read a little — I wrote a little — had a game of cards & went to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 25, 1885

Clear & cold — Got to studio at about 9.30 & right to work on the Pasini etching — on central group of figures with strong drypointing — also, continued experiments, trying to get an aquatint granulation — Home to lunch soon after 1 — there had a rest till near 3 — when with Annie & Jim I went to George's where a Xmas tree had been prepared for Held on — Dr. & Mrs. Swift & their little one — Dr. S's No. 2 wife & his mother — Mr. & Mrs. Harper & a child, & Mr. & Mrs. Babcock & child — Miss Sackett & the several nurses were there — all hands amused until about 4 o'clk. Then I went to studio — Home at 5:15 — dressed — & at 6:30 with Annie went to Mother Cook's & had dinner — Geo. & Miley — Walter & Marie there — a quiet evef. until near 10 when Annie & I went home & right to bed.
