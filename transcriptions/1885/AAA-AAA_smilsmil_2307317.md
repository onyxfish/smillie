## Left Page

### May, Friday 8, 1885

Wind + rain pretty much all day —
Got to studio at about 10.30 — finished
Toboggan Slide — Put the five Saratoga
sketches, with photos in a package —
wrote a letter to Col. Ingraham —
Put up a package with such things
as I will want at the Met. museum —
Put studio in order to leave it for
some days — then went to Nat. Exp.
with Saratoga pkg. + home at 1
Lunch — at 2.30 went up to Met.
museum + after an interview with
di Cesnola got to work on the Watts
pictures — Drew the Browning read and
then the Carlyle, getting both done
by 6 o'clk — Home at 6.30 — Dinner.
Evg. wrote notes to Kurtz, Geo. B-
Wood + Dana Estes. a game with
Annie + to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 9, 1885

Bright, cool day — threats of shower
about 4 — again at 6.30 — a sprinkle and
at night some rain. To studio at
10. Found note from Providence that I
answered — other matters kept me until
10.35 — then to Met. museum, getting
there about 11 + right to work. — First, on
Uldra — then on Calderon — at 1.30 went
out + ate my lunch by the side of the
reservoir — Back in about an hour +
a little rest then again to work —
Drew Mottey + Burne-Jones — Annoyed
by the crowds of people. In the m[useum] had
a few minutes pleasant conversation
with H.P. Vinton + wife from Boston. At
6 o'clk. Packed up + washed my dirty
hands — home at 6.30 — dinner.
Evg. being very tired we had a
game of cards + to bed. at 10.
