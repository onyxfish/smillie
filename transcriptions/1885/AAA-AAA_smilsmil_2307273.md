## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 3, 1885

Bright mtg .. overcast afternoon
After 10 when I got to Studio —
Spent the day repainting "It
fallow field" — much to its ad-
vantage, I think — a more lum-
inous sky & better foreground —
Removed some of the foxy color
replacing it with a pea greens —
To lunch at 1.30 — back before
3 — at 5 to Grady's & spent
an hour trying to find such
frame mouldings as I wished —
walked part way with J. Z. A
Ward. Home to dinner at 6 —
Every in dining Room
Reading — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 4, 1885

Pleasant mtg — but nasty p.m.
Got to Studio at usual time,
but out of sorts — head troubling
me — Spent the mtg painting
over foreground of a "But on
Marblehead Neck" — Home
at 1 — after that went to bed
& stayed there until 4 — then
up & went over to Grady's &
ordered a frame for my 20×30
"waste lot" — then down to Studio
& fixed things there — 6.20
when I got home — dinner.
Every in dining Room —
Balm headache — Played
Bazique with Annie for a
while — to bed soon after
11.
