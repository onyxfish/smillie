## Left Page

### April, Monday 6, 1885

Bright, breezy day — B[illegible] late —
afterwards Rep aired paper on the
dining room wall — To studio at
10.30 & to work on "View near
Crowninshield's Point, Marblehead" —
Rep ainting it for the 4th or 5th time
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.45 & worked until 5.30
then to Twordle's & bought
some lith. prints — paper, etc
for Katy Throop — back to Studio
& cleaned up & at 6.40 home
to dinner — Evelf Charl
came in for a little while —
I heated glue & fixed some
broken screens, easles etc —
then had a game of Bazique
with Annie & to bed before
11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 7, 1885

Mild, pleasant day — overcast in the after-
noon with light rain at night — Got to
studio at 10.30 — feeling very unlike work
but I started charcoal sketch "Normandy
Barnyard" 11¼ × 18¼ for W.C. Many.
the Children & Kate came in to studio
for half an hour — About 1 went to 21
W. 37th & arranged to have a carriage
sent at 3 that Father, Mother & Kate
might take a ride in the Park — then
home to lunch at 1 — Many returned
to her home by 3.30 train — Back
to studio at 3 — tried to work on the
charcoal sketch — made but little
progress — gave it up & spent some
time in my big chair — After 5 went
again to see Father & Mother — met
Annie there — Home to dinner at
6.30 — after that did some home mending
— read until afterly — a game of
Bazique & to bed soon after 11.
