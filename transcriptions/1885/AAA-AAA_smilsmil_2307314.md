## Left Page

### May, Saturday 2, 1885.

Pleasant day — to studio about 10 +
spent the mng trying to improve the
rubin of transparent color on my
35×60 canvas, but found that I was
only mussing + destroying freshness &
suggestion. W. Frank Carpenter
called + so did Ferguson — Home at
1.30 — back before 3 + spent the after-
noon repainting on "View back of
Marblehead Neck — near Crowninshields
Point" — think it is a very little better
— Chare came in about 6 & we
walked up together — Dinner before
7 — Eveg. feeling tired I did not
go to the Century — Annie read
aloud for awhile — then we had
a game of cards + to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 3, 1885.

Delightful day — Bkfst. at usual time.
Sat reading until after 11 — with Annie
went around to Walter's + he went with
us to the N.A. of D. where, all alone, we
spent an hour + a half in the galleries —
then home. Dinner at 2. — At 3.30
off to studio — met Chare there — I
arranged easel with my big picture
and portfolio rack — portfolio — studio
sketches etc. — then struck an attitude
myself in front of the easel + Chare fired
at me four times through his camera —
then I went with him to his dark room
— let him — walked up to Central Park —
back to Chare at 5.45 — saw negatives —
then home + had tea at 7 — Eveg.
with the boys + reading until 9, then
took Mother Cook, who had taken tea
with us, home — back + dro a commen-
ced letter to Father — near 11 when I
took it to depot — then home and
to bed.
