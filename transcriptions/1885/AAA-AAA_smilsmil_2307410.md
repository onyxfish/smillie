## Left Page

### November, Thursday 12. 1885.

Very mild pleasant day — Got to
studio before 10 & to work on West Shore
bond drawing — Home to lunch about
1.30 — did not get back to studio
until near 3 — mounted drawing
on heavy board & put it under weight
— About 5 went to Apelier Club for
a few minutes & saw a collection of
specimens of "process" reproductions —
Then to Murray Hill Hotel & there
saw Mr. & Mrs. Saml. Colman, who are
in town on their way to Cal[iforni]a — to start
next Monday — Colman troubled with
his lungs — Emma J. there — Home to
dinner at 6 — Eve[nin]g interested in
looking over old letters etc. — until
11 — then to bed.

Geo. & Charle went to P.o.k. to-day —
no change evident in Father — The
same may be said of Annie who
seems to puzzle the Dr. She keeps
quietly on her back in bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 13. 1885.

Very mild — pleasant m[ornin]g — but rain
in the afternoon — Got up with a head-
ache which was more or less troublesome
all day — Got to studio at 9.30 — Walter
Cook came in soon after — I worked
for awhile tracing photo. of Mexican
well & water-carriers — for drawing for
A.B.N. Co. — About noon went to A.B.
N. Co. & found that Will had unexpectedly gone to Phil[adelphia]. — Left my West
Point drawing — Then out to Restaurant
& got lunch — then to Char's — then
to Academy of Music — N. Phil. Rehearsal — At 4.30 to studio — Night came
home before 5 — dinner at 6 — Eve[nin]g
again spent some time looking over
the bundle of old letters.
