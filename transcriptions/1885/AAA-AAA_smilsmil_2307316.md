## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 6, 1885

Pleasant day with strong, cool east
wind — overcast after 5 P.M. with a
wet wind — almost drizzle — To studio
at 10 + to work on Saratoga pen work.
C.M. Kurtz came with list of the Watts
pictures that he wishes me to do for the
cat. Mr. McCartney called again —
About 1 I went to Bank, deposited
a check for her + drew a little money
for her — Then home to lunch — back
at 2.30 + made another Saratoga —
ill, with crayon on Process paper — At
3.45 went out to Mad. Sq. for a walk,
met Sam. Colman who is in town
with Mrs C. for a day or two — Home to
dinner at 6.30. Eveg with
writing + Reading + the usual
game — to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 7, 1885

Driving wind + rain with little
intermission all day — Got to studio
at usual time, about 10.30 + Spent
all the m'g on a Pen Sketch of the
Congress Hall hotel — Home at 1.30
back before 3 — finished sketch +
at a late hour fetched into another,
"the Toboggan Slide" — by working
until nearly 6.30 I got it almost
finished — Then home to dinner,
feeling rather out of sorts — Eveg
after some reading we had our
usual game of cards + got to
bed before 11 having had nothing
to celebrate the 14th anniversary
of our wedding day but a bunch
of roses from Mother Cook.
