## Left Page

### April, Friday 10, 1885

Pleasant day, altho' rather raw & at times overcast — got to Studio at 10.30 + spent the mrf- making a tracing from the charcoal sketch of Normandy "Barn Yard"; Home to lunch at 1 — at 2 at Phil. Rehearsal — out at 4 — to Studio — feeling exceeding stupid — At 5.20 started for 37th S. to see Father + Mother but changed my mind + went home + lay down upon the bed + had an uneasy nap — up at 6.40 + with Annie, Mr. Justice + daughters went to Mother Cook's to dinner — Nehe + her two daughters there — Mr. Jennie Downs came in after dinner — we left at 9.30 — had a game of Basique at home + to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 11, 1885

Bright, raw mrf — with snowflurries later in the day — Got to Studio about 10. + then to Am. Art Ass'n — with Craig me + made sketch of Alex. Harrison's "moon— rise over the sea" for Catalogue of the coming "Prize Exhibition" — Home to lunch at 1 — back to galleries about 2.30 + worked until 5 on a Sketch after Cons'. Mayer's "Knitting Lesson" — Then to 37th S. for a few minutes + home to dinner before 6.30 — At 8 — Annie + I at the last Phil. concert of the season — Walked home at 11 + to bed at once —
