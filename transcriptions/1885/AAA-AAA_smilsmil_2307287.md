## Left Page

### March, Monday 9, 1885

Cold, breezy + march-like. Got up with sore head + throat — To studio at about 10.30 + spent the rest of the m'f in my big chair unable to work. Soon after 12 went up to 37 & S'. + saw Father who had just got up — he seemed to be feeling better — Minn was there — home at 1.30 + had lunch — about 3 went to bed + slept soundly until 6 — then got up feeling better — had dinner — Charl called in for a moment on his way to see Father — Anin + I had a game of Bazique — I lay up on lounge until 12 — then to bed + about 1 o'clk to sleep.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 10, 1885

Bright, cold, windy + very dirty. To studio about 10 + painted all the m'f on "Breezy day on French Coast." — working over sky + distant sea — Home to lunch at 1.30 back at 3 — Century Picture not yet Ret'd — went over to N.A. of D. + found it there — Jerry brought it right over + man from McKeever's put on oak outside — I painted some upon the line of distant surf — also added a few points of emphasis among the grass tufts of foreground — Geo. + Nellie gave a recp'n in their studios — I had no callers + at 5.15 I went up to see Father, found him much depressed — went out + got some raw oysters for him which he ate with much relish — Home at 6.30 + had dinner — Evng wrote a note to Wm T. Richards in answer to one from him on the deaths of Geo. Whitney — To bed before 11.
