## Left Page

### October, Sunday 11, 1885

Brilliant beautiful day. Ther. no lower than 41° last night — rises to 76° in our bed Room during the day — Jim much troubled during the night with a barking cough — much fret. Spent loitering. About 1 went to Drug Store & got some Ipecac. for Jim — on my way stopped at Will Mulford's & saw his 6 mo. boy — also Mrs. Jepaning fathers — Dinner at 2. — Got Annie out on piazza in her big chair — at 4 walked around the hill with St. & Nellie — then wrote letter to Father — Dan Daype & wife came in before 7 to make a call.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 12, 1885

Hazy day with unmistakable threats of change — much of my time given to the men — part time up in trees sawing out the dead wood — when not at that work biting in etching No. 34 — my day was a very busy one. Did not get down town until 4.30 — Eve'g. Reading papers & chatting with the family who gathered in our bed room — to bed at about 11 — high east wind and rain before m'f.

Annie for the past three days seems to be improving.
