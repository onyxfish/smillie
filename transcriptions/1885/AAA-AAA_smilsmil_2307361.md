## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 4, 1885

Charming day. with a dash of a shower between 5 & 6 P.m. — Bkf.
at 8. Coyle started work at 9 on Road borders — I went downtown at 10. stopping to see Father on the way back in about an hour — then put up awnings, put [illegible] in studio bay window — Dinner at 1.30 —
Fussing about the house & after Coyle most of the afternoon — my head troubled me less than yesterday but I had many sharp twinges of pain — After the shower I went down town again stopping to see Father — Home to tea about 7 — Evelf, quite cool — wrote a note to Walter Cook & sent him check in payment for Rattan Web. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 5, 1885

Brilliantly beautiful mtf. with showers between 12 & 1 & a cloudy & almost cold afternoon — Spent the m'f writing letters & went down town about noon, after the first shower — Dinner at 1.30 — Spent the afternoon looking after Coyle & trying to finish plans for an Earth Closet — Down town again before tea — going with H to see Father & the folks — Got a letter from Kate, but her account from P'k, being written when she was tired & hurried was not very lucid or altogether satisfactory. Tea after 7 — Evelf my head bothered me a good deal. Played a game of cards with Annie & got to bed about 10.30
