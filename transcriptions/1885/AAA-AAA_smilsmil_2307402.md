## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 27. 1885.

Yet another delicious day —
out all day — Payne spent half
a day, taking up soft maple
trees on the Warner lot — Pettus
spent half a day hauling five
loads of manure — Cuny, Coupe
& Rhinevault, helping with
the trees & spreading manure.
I got a lot of work done &
felt much pleased — the brick
workers oiled main room floor
& sashes — Eve'g. I wrote notes
to Gifford, Yale, Hamblin,
Cassele & Co., Poule — ticket agent
at Binghampton etc — After
tea went down Town — back
soon after 8 — Had a game
of cards with Annie, & to bed
about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 28. 1885.

Very mild & pleasant but por-
tentous in a weather way — I
set to work in earnest on the packing
of the two big chests but was
much interrupted — Cuny start-
ed to cut dead wood out of trees but
had a cramp, so I set him to work
cleaning out cisterns — I having to
watch & direct him — about noon
went down town — dinner after —
more work on the cisterns, Coupe
helping Cuny — I got in more work
on cases — About 5 Zerfus came
& uncoupled valves & outlets and
cleaned them. Tea at 6.30 after
that down Town & about 8 went to
J.D. Payne's & settled with him
near 9 when I got home — then
finished the cases & tried to pack
my trunk but was too tired — It
was about 10.30 when I went to
bed.
