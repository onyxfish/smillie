## Left Page

### November, Thursday 26, 1885

*Thanksgiving Day.*

Dite overcast & raw — Got to
studio at usual time & worked
on West Shore No. 2 — finishing it
before I went home to lunch
at 1 o'clk. — It was near 2
when I went around to Ed W.
Cook's (154 E. 37 St.) to see the young-
sters there — ten of them — with
a number of grown folks — Jim
was there, enjoying himself
very much — I got to studio
before 3 & then worked on Pattie's
lion die — between 5 & 6. at
the N.A. looking hurriedly thro'
the Fall Exbn. Then home — Had
our Thanksgiving Turkey dinner
in our parlor bed room — Annie
of course, in bed — The dinner table
being a small stand on bureau —
From 9 to 10 I was at Mother Cook's
where she had a family gathering
Then home & sat by fire looking
over old letters until near mid-
night — Then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 27, 1885

At last, a bright day — quite
cool — To studio before 10 — to work
on several upright A.B.N. Co. draw-
ing — mexican figures — woman — boy —
baby — Marion Wright & Miss Clark
called — At 12.30 went to A.B.N. Co.
to see West Shore No. 2 — accepted
(& so was No. 1) — took lunch with
Wells — There had p.p. Faker from
Pattie's lion die — not satisfactory —
The man is not a good prover —
Brought away with me a poor
drawing — Patterson from Mill — intend
to try to make something of it — got
to studio at 3.30 — Stretched poor
drawing — mounted p.p. & home
at 5.30 — home — dinner — Evening
wrote notes & at 9 went out and
mailed them — to bed before
11.
