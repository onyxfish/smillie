## Left Page

### July, Monday 13, 1885

overcast & cool — thea got down to
52°. last night & up to 75°. during the
day — was surprised by a visit
from Father before 10 o'clk — but he was
evidently very feeble. At about 10.30
I walked back with him & then on
down town where I attended to my
"chores" — about noon, back, & stopped
at Aunt H's. Annie there. Father
suffering from shortness of breath — so
much that he could not stay in-doors.
Dinner at 1.30 — after that again
went down to see Father who seemed
better — while sitting at my work
had a call from Mr. Munons, a book
agent who wished to meet W.J.D.S. the
artist & see his home. About with
Curry, the laborer, & carpenters — tea
about 7 — again went down to see
Father — found him quite bright —
Dan Sayne & wife calling there —
home in an hour. Read news paper —
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 14, 1885

Heavy rain during the night — rain
during the m'f. until after 10 — then it
cleared — about 1, a change again —
with thunder showers — Showers again
about 5 — charming eve'g. After bkft.
at my work as usual — Bugling over
plans for earth closet. Also looking after
carpenters — About 11 went with Annie
"to see" the folks — I went on down town to
the P.O. etc — back for it. & home at 1 — dinner
half an hour later. Afternoon again at
my earth closet work. — Curry & his boy
at work in the afternoon. After tea, Mary
Kate & the children came up & spent
some time going over the house. Dr.
Mulford & Elisha also came up for
a visit. Eve'g Read & wrote for a
while.
