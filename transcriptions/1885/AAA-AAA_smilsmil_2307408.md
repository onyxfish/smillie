## Left Page

### November, Sunday 8. 1885.

Dull day — rain in the after-
noon & heavy rain at night.
B&p. late — spent the m'f. about
the house with a heavy headache.
Geo., Nellie & Sheldon came to
dinner about 2. Stayed until
after 4 & then went home in the
rain. I wrote to Father & to
Herrick. Very heavy rain with
wind between 6 & 7. I went to
G. C. D. with Father's letter, then
back in a cab, got Annie & we
went to Mrs. Gibbons' — 55 W. 47th St.
to tea & to meet Miss Dewey —
Spent the eve'g pleasantly
until about 10. Then home — by
mad. ave. cars — still raining
— to bed by 11.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 9. 1885.

Pleasant day — cooler & breezy.
To studio about 9.30 — wasted part
of m'f. looking over old letters —
mounted large sheet of paper
on heavy board for water-color.
Home at 1.30 — Annie on her back
again — Male came to see her at
3 o'clk. Can say nothing definite
— went to studio — mounted paper
for West Shore drawing for A.B.N. Co.
Charl came in — walked up
with him — home to dinner at 6.
Eve'g with Annie — Read to her
until she fell asleep. I went
to bed soon after 10.
