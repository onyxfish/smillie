## Left Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 17 1873.

Another glorious day and more frost last night. Got up very unwillingly & Tom had a short currying — Bkfst soon after & after bkfst wrote a letter to [illegible] — then went to Leu Sears [illegible] meddling with the Episcopalian Ch. — back at 11 & the rest of the mng was spent with the Carpenters laying foundation stones & getting down the floor frames and beams. Dinner at 1.30 — Between 3 & 4 o'clk. got sketching traps, saddled Tom & Rode out about 2 miles on Salt Spring Road & sketched some Sunflowers — Back at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Eveng at 8 went down to Sears's — there until 10.45 — Home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### Sept. Thursday, 18 1873.

Overcast day, at times, threatening — sent all the mng — drawing at the planing mill, &c, pushing on my studio! Dinner at 1.30. After dinner the Crams & Boggs and Blossoms & Lathrops came over & spent an hour or more, looking over my work — all very agreeable and lovely. Out with the Carpenters until near 5 o'clk — then off on Tom — In at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Chatting until near 8.30 — then Joe Williams called — went with him up to the Coopers — all out — Ret'd to Mrs. Lathrop's & played whist until 10.15 — then home & to bed at 10.30
