## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 18 1873.

Disagreeable day — cold, Raw
& dirty. Very much under the
weather with sore throat & general
cold. Did not get to studio
until after 2 o'clk. All the mfg
washing and cleaning up. Had
lunch at the studio. Afternoon
worked on "Jake & Louis" block.
Took tea in the studio, so as to
avoid exposure to the raw
air. About 8 o'clk, members
of Water Color Soct'y began to
arrive for fall meeting of the
W.C. Soct'y. But a quorum
did not come. Broke up at
10 o'clk. Home at 10.30 and
to bed at 11. To sleep about
2 o'clk. A.M.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 19 1873.

Up at 9 o'clk. 10 o'clk. before I got
to the studio — weather still dis-
agreeable. To work on block
for Appleton. Home to lunch
abt 1.30 — back to studio at 2.30
& with many interruptions
worked as long as I could see.
W.H. Morse, Engr, came with block
"Spartan", to get my ideas about
engraving it. Home to tea at
6.30 — Throat still very sore.
Duett. Uncle A. & Susie at
the house until 9 o'clk.
