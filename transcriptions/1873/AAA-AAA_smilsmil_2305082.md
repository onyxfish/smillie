## Left Page

### July, Sunday 27, 1873

Very muggy & close. Thunderstorms occasionally — Up at 8.30. Help —
and then lay about all the morning quite useless. Quite a heavy storm
at 2 o'clk — Dinner at one — another heavier storm just before 3 o'clk.
— Commenced a letter to Will Kimball but napped until
near 5 — Air much cooler and refreshing — Evef. finished
letter to Will K. and cut to bed soon after 11 + [illegible]
pretty much all night —

## Right Page

### July, Monday 28, 1873

Up at 7 — Weather extremely hot & sticky — Help — about
8 — Worked on my sketches very unwillingly during
the mf. — Feeling very much fagged. Dinner soon after
13 clk — Worked better during the p.m. — gathering material
for Appleton article into some shape. Tea near
7 o'clk. Evef. went with
Charl. to Thomas's garden —
Enjoyed the concert very
much — especially the Andante
to Beethoven's 5th Symphony —
[illegible] — "Rhapsodic Hongroise"
No. 2 was given with a great
spirit — Home soon after
11 and to bed at 11.30.
