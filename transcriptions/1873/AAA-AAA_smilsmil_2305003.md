## Left Page

### February Thursday, 13 1873

Mf very clear — overcast aftnoon.
Got to Studio at 9.45. Kindled
fire — then went to N.A. [illegible] D —
then to Geo's Room and saw
Bellows — then to Knoedlers
and at noon, back to studio.
Set palette & worked on
10½×20 for Matt — Home to
lunch at 2 — back at 2.40.
P.M. very much broken up.
Johnston, art man of the
Mail made a long call.
Home to tea at 6 ½ —
Ev'g at work on water-
color accts and general
business — To bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### February Friday, 14 1873

Charming day. Got to Studio at
9.20 & commenced painting on
20×24" Tenaya Canon & Half Dome —
worked hard until after 1 o'clk
getting in sky & distance — then
home to lunch — Matt + Ed, there
— late — hurried down to Phil.
Rehearsal — After — went with
Belle & Mary S. to N.A. [illegible] D —
— there until 5.30 — left them
& went to studio — cleaned
up & at 6.30 went to Kimball's
to tea — Sinna [illegible] & Miss Howe
there also — pleasant time.
Ev'g. Singing club — had
a very good time — Home
at 11.20 & to bed at 11.45
