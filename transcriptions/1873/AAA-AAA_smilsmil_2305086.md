## Left Page

### August, Monday 4, 1873

By comparison, a delightfully cool day — B[illegible] at B — Cb[illegible]
g Aitken of Chicago, called —
about 10, Moore, art dealer,
called, — anxious to have me
engrave a plate after Eastman
Johnston — About 11.30 — S.R. T[illegible]
called — nearly an hour —
very bright + chatty — abt had
lunch and after that, gave
up to headache — on the sofa
all the P.M. — Tea at 6.30 —
aunt Jessie + Jane Dinning in
in the eve'g — I sat up until
after midnight. Reading and
writing. Posted four letters
& to bed abt 6 o'clk.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 5, 1873

By comparison, a delightfully cool day —
Matt, Kate + Charl. aw[illegible] from Po[illegible] about
10.30 AM, abt 11 went to Moore's, Union
Sq. He is very anxious to get me to eng.
after a painting by E. Johnston — then
to Appleton's — Saw Bunce — agreed to
most of my sketches + gave me lots
of wood. Also $250.00 for my trip.
Then to A.B.N.Co. Got my lunch there
then to studio about 3.30 — packing
things until nearly — then home
to tea. — Eve'g at 8.30 — called on
the Misses Ripley — 13[illegible], 32[illegible] — Met
there Mr. Perkins + her bro. W. Walcott
— also their father — passed a very
pleasant eve'g until 10.30 — Then
to studio + home abt 11 with two
heavy [pkgs]. To bed abt 11.45
