## Left Page

### October Saturday, 25 1873.

Clear, but windy & cold. After Bkf. went after Austen to Ile about my Painting (of studio) then down town to get off a barrel of apples. Then as it was too cold to think of painting out of doors, I determined to go after my raft lumber man. Got Sattrup's horse & buggy & with Ella, lunch & plenty of robes, started at 11 del[ivered] about 2½ miles out, met the man & his load of lumber. Turned about & home at 12:15. Lumber arr. just before 1. Dinner at 1.20 from 2 until 3. Unloading — measuring etc. Then worked alone "Sticking up" the lumber until 5 o'clk — Mm tired out, in bed until 6. Je[?] soon after. At Austen's until near[?]. Uncle Wm arr. at 6:30 from Phila. Chatting until after 10. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 26 1873.

Dull, leaden & Novemberish looking day. Bkf. at 8.30. At Church time went for Fan Mr. stiff neck — could not go. Gus Seymour went. Miller preached — 1st Kings 20: Ch. 28 V — God is the king of the hills & the valleys & the Mt & the plain. Dinner at 1.30. After dinner chatting with Mm Cooker. Mrs. Sattrup & Flora — Gus's family have Rid. to their own house & Wm C. has come to board. Walked to Fairground with Flora — back at 4. Then to Fan's — short walk with Gus — left her & took longer one alone — in at 6 — Tea — Flora &. came in — took her to Pres. Ch. — caught in driving storm — home at 8.20 more. Short letter to Father. Took it down town & stopped in at Fan's — left at 10. Home and to bed at 10.45. A night of very heavy wind & rain.
