## Left Page

### Sept. Friday, 5 1873.

A marvellous day — so cool — so [illegible]
— so bright — Up some after 6 and
gave my horse a good grooming.
It was after 10 before I could get to
my work — Finished "Descending the
Rapids" — Dinner at 1.30 — Uncle —
Junic — Uncle M — Sauntoff all off to
Salt Spring — Balston Pic Nic — I
ate too much dinner + was unable
to work. Tried until 4 — then gave
it up — Saddled Tonic + rode until
6.30 — Bath — Tea at 7.15 — Walked down
the hill with Hettie S. + Josie B. — a
wonderful moonlight — A [illegible]
party called (about 20) to celebrate
Josie's 13th wedding anniversary —
They had a jolly time — I left early
+ got to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Sept. Saturday, 6 1873.

Overcast mg — clearing — glorious
a.m. and eveg. Up early and
groomed my horse — B'kfs't at 8 o'ell.
to work on "Tracing of Breakneck's
Brans, Quebec". Uncle A. and
Junic left for N.Y. at 6.30 a.m.
Dinner at 1.30 — worked until
near 4 — then saddled and went
down town + met Josie B., Hetty
S. and Joe [illegible] on horseback —
Rode with them until 6. Took
a little ride by myself, then
in. Scrubble + tea at 7 — The
Boggs.es arr'd this eveg — Some
conversation with them — then
down town — in at Searle's for
a few minutes — then home +
bed at 10.
