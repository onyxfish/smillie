## Left Page

### December Friday, 12, 1873

Dark & threatening — Got down
to Appleton's at 9.30 & gave in
my last blocks to Runce and
got $75.00. B. wants me to make
a full page combination of views
about Montreal. Got to studio
at 10.30. Blocked out page
for B. Painted a background
for portrait by Miss Richards m-.
Home to lunch at 1.15 at 2.30
at Phil. Rehearsal. Back
to studio at 5 o'clk. Home
to tea at 6. At 8.10 at
Kimball's — Spent the even.
until 10.45 — Home & to bed
at 11.30 —

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 13, 1873

Horrible day — Steady down
pouring rain — Got to studio
at 9.30. Set palette to finish
small picture for Sauthier
but it was too dark to dare
so I started on a 14×16
to fill another frame that
I have from Sauthier. I
pitched in & painted a
sunset among the Thousand
Islands. Misses Woodward
& Richardson invited me & I
lunched with them. P.M.
so dark that I could not
work, but I got my sketch
all in. Home to tea at 5.45.
Uncle Geo. there. At 7.30
Kate & I went to Phil. Concert.
Sat next to Miss "Lib" Home
who was there with Will K.
Had a very enjoyable con-
cert — Home at 10.30 — Rain
stopped — to bed at 11 —
