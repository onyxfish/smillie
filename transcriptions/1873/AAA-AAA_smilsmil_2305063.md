## Left Page

### June, Thursday 19, 1873

Having a miserable headache, did not get up until after 10.
Tremendously hot day — after [illegible]
H[illegible], rode to studio, got dinner
& went to A.B.N. Co., stopping
at Harley's — he wants me to do
some drawing on wood — at
A.B.N. Co's at 4 o'clk — got [illegible]
[illegible] die. Back to studio at
3.30 — worked on study for drawing
until 6.30 — Home to tea at 7.15 —
father arr'd — home last night
while I was at Thos. garden.
Every. loafing about until
ten, then a bath & to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 20, 1873

Very hot and some rain — got
to studio at 9.15 after going to
Dimealp's — Emma I's (and
writing a note for her) and to
Telegraph office — Augers
said my study for Bank Note
drawing was very bad, so he
worked on it for quite 2 hours
improving it wonderfully.
Took lunch in studio as I
was waiting for Steiner, who
was to drive me out to Jerome
Park this p.m. — He did not
come — one of his horses used
up — worked on drawing
until after 6 — Home to tea
at 7 — Even'g doing nothing —
to bed at 10.45
