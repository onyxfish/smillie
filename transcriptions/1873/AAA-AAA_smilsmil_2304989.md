## Left Page

### January Sunday, 12 1873

Pleasant day — Wt, went to
Dr Hastings's & heard him preach
from Acts 1st ch. 8th v. — dwelling
particularly upon being witnesses.
A very old man assisted — I
don't know who he was — Walked
home with Mr & Mrs Mc. Harg —
Dinner at 1.30 — About 3.30,
with Charl, walked up to Uncle
Al's — went over the new house —
then on up to Mr Jessup's, on
5th ave. & 85th St. — There at 5 o'clk.
Tea at 7 & general, pleasant
chatting until 9 — Walked down
stopping again at Uncle Al's and
at the church 5th Ave & 45th St. Home
at 9.40 — Read and to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### January Monday, 13 1873

Mild and overcast day — got to
Studio at 9.45 — Kindled fire &
spent most of the morning
preparing paper for my new
Water Color — Mr Kingdon called
with eng. — block "Indians making
Chemuck" — from my approval —
Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at
2.40 — Worked on block "Tenaya
Cañon" until near 5 — Then
went to U.A. for D. — with drawing
by Fred & Millie — then to Sauthiers
thru to Snedecor's — then Studio
& home to tea at 7 o'clk —
Evelf wrote a note to S.C.F.
To bed at 10.45
