## Left Page

### April Saturday, 26 1873.

Clear but very windy & dirty.
Got to studio at 9. and set
to work painting on a view
on Artist's Brook — North Country
that I painted in a number
of years ago. Mr. McArthur
& two daughters called at about
10.30 — Abt 2.30. Rode down to
Dewey's (Tailor) to try on coat
— home to lunch at 1.30 — back
to studio at 2.20 & painted
until 6 — Home to tea at
7 o'clk — Went very tired
& got to bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### April Sunday, 27 1873.

A charming day overhead but
windy & very dirty. Mrs. went to
Hastings's & heard him preach
from John 2d. ch. 17th v. a
sermon begging for $5,000 to pay
for repairs & decoration on the
church building — Home to
dinner — Between 3 & 4 o'clk
went up to the Park and
wandered about there until
after 5 — Then went to Wills
— there until 8.30 — then
went to Uncle Q's — He
& Junie were in bed — Saw
Uncle W. & Aunt H. — Home
a little before 10 — To bed
at 10.40.
