## Left Page

### November Thursday, 6 1873

Still glorious weather. Bkft. at 7.30 — To work early
on Appletons drawing until
I went out to walk at 11.45.
Lunch at 12.30 — again
worked steadily as long as
I could see — until 4.45 —
Then walked down to
studio — Met Ferguson and
walked up the Ave. with
him — then into [illegible]
& back home. — Tea at 6.
Evef. at 8. with Matt, K[illegible]
& Charle, went up to Will's.
Home at 10.30 — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November Friday, 7 1873

Dull morning — Bkft. at 7.30 and
then to work on Yosemite drawing —
Stuck to it faithfully all the m'f.
Uncle Wm. J. came in at 11 o'clk, and
stayed to lunch with us — at 12.30.
At 2.30 went to Phil. Rehearsal —
It then commenced raining — the
Rehearsal very short — Raining hard
— went to Brooks Bros. & ordered
coat & pantaloons — then called on
Moore. Then walked home, getting
pretty wet — Tea at 6. Evef.,
Read Verne's 20,000 Leagues under
the Sea & mounted Yosemite
drawing. To bed shortly after
11.
