## Left Page

### January Thursday, 30 1873.

Splendid day and very cold.
Got to studio at 9.30, morning
spent working on an Italian
drawing, Retouching it for
the A.B.N. Co — also entertaining
lots of callers — answering notes
etc. etc. — Wm. Cox called and
I engaged him as salesman for
our Water Color Soc. — At noon
got to work on my Water Color.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & worked until 5 —
then went to Smillie's, Hewitt's
& Snedecors. — Home at 7.30 — tea
at 8. — Surf, feeling tired
& Rested myself — To bed
at 10.20

## Right Page

### January Friday, 31 1873.

Another fine day — Matt and
Mother went to Po'keepsie in the
10.30 A.M. Train. — I got to studio
at 9.15 & to work on W.C. drawing.
I hardly know what to think of
it, but am hopeful — tho' from
day to day new defects & weak-
nesses make themselves more
& more apparent to me. — Home
to lunch at 1.50 — back at 2 to
although it was Phil. Rehearsal —
worked as long as I could see.
About 5 Geo. came in — then
Belle & Mary Tweedy, Kate & Charl.
A breezy time, of course. — At
5.45 went to N.A. [illegible] — At 6.30
to the Tintball's & excused my-
self from the Singing club for the
evef. Home to tea at 7.15 — after
made two or three notes & then down
to N.A. [illegible] — at 8.30 — there, hanging
until 10 — Home at 10.40 and
to bed at 11.15
