## Left Page

### June, Sunday, 29, 1873

Another very hot day —
Did not get up until near
9 o'clk — Ed. Parker. arr.
before that time — Myf.
went to Dr Hastings and
heard him preach from
Rev. 3° Ch. S. v. Home to
dinner at 1.30 — afternoon
got a number of things ready
for my departure — Tea at 6 —
At 7.30 went to the Kimball's
— bid them good bye and
at 8.30 went to the Strands —
found Ned Cook & Miss Parker
there — Will K — came in after-
wards — It was 10.45 before
I got away and 12 when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Monday, 30, 1873

*On board Str. St. John —*

Very hot & very breezy — all
the mft. Running around
doing messages, etc. etc. Went
to Studio — had my hair
cut & had shave &ood —
etc — Home to lunch at 1.15
At 2 Rode down-town to 75
Broadway & bought tickets for
Father & self — Home at 3.30 —
Packed our bags — at 4.45
took tea — bid good bye
all round — on board
St. John at 5.30 & before
we left the dock, we were
established in Stateroom
C — The eve[ning] very breezy
overcast with storm scud
& chilling.
