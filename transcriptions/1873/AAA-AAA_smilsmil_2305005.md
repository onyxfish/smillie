## Left Page

### February Monday, 17 1873.

Superb day overhead —
very shakey after yesterday's
spree. Got to studio at
9.40 — Kindled fire and to
work on drawing for B. N. Co —
"Arms of Penn" — worked
until 1.30 — home to lunch
back at 2.30 — worked
until 5.40 — Rec'd a good
natured note from Thos. Rast
— also a very pleasant note
from Annie Coak commissioning
me a Racquette Lake painting.
Home to tea at 6.40 — Eve'g
with english Illustrated
Papers. To bed at 9.30

## Right Page

### February Tuesday, 18 1873.

Mild and rather mixed. Got
to studio at 9.25 and worked
faithfully — Arms of Penn until
1.30. Then home to lunch.
Matt & Kate came in a few
minutes before 1 — Back to
studio at 2.30 & worked until
near 3.30 — Then, with drawing
went down to A. B. N. Co. Walked
up to Appleton's — Saw portfolio
— all my Yosemite blocks and
arranged with Bunce about
the manuscript. Back to
studio at 6.20 — Fixed fire
& home to tea about 7 —
Eve'g at 8.30 at Wr. McHarg's
36th St until 10.15 — Home &
to bed at 11.
