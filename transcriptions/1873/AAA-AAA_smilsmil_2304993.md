## Left Page

### January, Monday 20, 1873

Overcast & mild — snow fell in
the afternoon — 9.25 a.m. when
I got to the studio — lighted fire
& dusted up — Then commenced
the washing in my large water
color — About noon Misses Cook
Edith Wright, Numa Ireland &
Mr. Jas. Wright made a pleasant
call — Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.45 & worked until
5 o'clk — Home to tea at 5.45
At 8.30 called at Mrs. A. G. Clark's
7 W. 20° St — cards — then on to
Sommersville Gallery. Private
view Artists Fund — there
until after 10 — Home at 10.50
to bed at 11.20

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 21, 1873

a detestable day — dark and
rainy — and a detestable head-
ache. After writing some notes
got to studio at 9.30 — Shortly
before 10, my model (Brunswig)
came — he posed for me for
an hour & a half, but I could
not get what I wanted. Mr.
Walter Brown called — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — so used
up that I did not return
to studio but went to bed
up at 6 — Evening spent
on sofa & reading news-
papers — got to bed about
10 o'clk. A. M.
