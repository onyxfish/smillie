## Left Page

### June, Monday, 9, 1873.

Up bet. 6&7 — glorious morning —
took 8.30 a.m. train to NY, Into
the city at 9.30 & went to A.B. N.
Co. Reached home about 11 —
The rest spent visiting —
Lunch at 1 — then a nap —
Joe came soon after. Rode
with her down to studio —
visited about there, doing
no work so that my day
has been lost. Home to tea
at 6.30 — at 8.30 went up to
uncle A.V.V.'s — Aunt Alpina
goes to Montrose tomorrow.
Home at near 11 after a
delightful moonlight walk
in the park — To bed soon
after 11 —

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday, 10, 1873.

Up at 7.30 dressed & washed
but then gave up to head-
ache & spent the day on
the sofa — Up & took tea
at 9 o'clk. Rolled myself
in my blanket a midnight
& got to sleep between 3 and
4 o'clk. a.m.
