## Left Page

### May, Wednesday, 28, 1873

Very hot day — got to the studio soon after 9 — could not work on drawing, so spent the mtf. cleaning up my room. Home to lunch at 1.30. Went to 64 Nat. Bank & drew out val. of Water color money — Rec'd home & at 4.30 Rode down to Appleton's. Bunce saw & approved Sketches on Lower Ausable — Back to Studio at 5.30. Went to Bogardus's with Alfred Jones's drawing & at 6.15 went to the Kimball. There to tea — home at 7.45. Packed, preparatory to a start on the morrow and got to bed at a little after 11 o'clk — when Father started on something like a flatulent cholic which was very violent — I got to bed at 1 o'clk. Father got quiet by 3 —

## Right Page

### May, Thursday, 29, 1873

Hotter yet — Father complaining so that I gave up thought of going to Montrose. Got to studio at 9.30 — Reduced & partially outlined drawing for Appletons — View on the lower Ausable Pond. Home to lunch at 1 — Had a long sound nap in the afternoon — Geo & Willie went out to ride with Will & Joe. Evg. with Father who seemed to get relief at last from hot fomentations of Whiskey & Camphor. Got to bed at 11.45
