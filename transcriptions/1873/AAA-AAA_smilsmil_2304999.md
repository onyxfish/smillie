## Left Page

### February Saturday, 1 1873

Still splendid weather. Got to studio soon after 9. Rev. E.S. Wagoon called — half an hour — then to work on my drawing with many interruptions. I worked all over it getting it together — forcing or intensifying it. etc. Home to lunch at 1.50 — back at 2.40. Consultations in my Room — Hart, Beard, Burling & Holberton — got along slowly with the drawing — Tiffany & a friend called about 5 — Got drawing into frame — then Falconer called — Home to tea at 7.15 — at 8.30 — at Dodworth's — Carried drawing over to N.A. of D. Advised tearing down the hanging already done on two walls of North Room — done. We worked hard until 10.30 — Home at 11 + to bed at 11.30 —

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 2 1873

Still clear & very cold. Wife went to Dr. Hastings'. & heard him preach from 1st Timothy 4th ch. 10th v. So much engrossed with other thoughts, that I'm sorry to say that I didn't hear the sermon as I should. Home to dinner. Read & napped until 5 — Then walked up to Will's — took tea and at 8 o'clk. went over to Uncle A's. Charlie there — at 9.45 we started for home. At 11.30 I went to bed.
