## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 5 1873.

Up late — got to studio about
10.15 — Spent the mf cleaning
up — about 1 o clk Mr. Hunt
& Mrs. Harmer called — quite
a chatty time for an hour —
Home to lunch at 2.15 —
After lunch went to Y.M.C.A
Bldg & called on Wm. Hart —
Spoke very frankly to him of
his course in Water Color Socty.
Then to Bevoort's — then G. Myant.
— over to N.A. f.D. — at 4.30 — met
there Messrs Clarence Cook and
Walter Brown. To studio at 5.45
& tried to get a nap — Home at
6.45 — at N.A. f.D. at 8.45 — A
very crowded & warm place
— but everything seemed to
go off lovely — Home at
11.30 and to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 6 1873.

Up late again — did not get
to studio until 10.30 — Mrs.
Diven had called — Spent
the mf writing letters —
Home to lunch at 1.45 —
After lunch at N.A. f.D. —
there until 4.30 — then to
studio — took a nap and
at nearly went over to the
Hoffman Ho & to see the Jevl.
& Mrs. Diven & Mr. & Mrs. Dickenson —
Home to tea a 6.45 — At
9.30 at Black's — 9 W. 35 St. —
— tried to leave at 10.30, but
detained until about 12 —
then home & to bed at
