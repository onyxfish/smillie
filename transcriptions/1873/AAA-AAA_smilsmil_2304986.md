## Left Page

### January Monday, 6 1873.

Glorious day — Got to Studio at
9.30 — Kindled fire — swept and
dusted somewhat, then to work
on Water color — washing out
tin & making very slow pro-
gress. Sandford Gifford called
about noon — Preps and lunch
at 1 o'clk — Fred came at 1.30
I went to Kuorzdei's — back in
a few minutes — & worked as well
as I could through many interruptions
Nichol came in about 4.30 on
Water Color business & Remained
until 6 — Home to tea at 6.30
Evety — Read aloud an account
of Everts's wanderings for 37 days
alone in the Yellowstone Country
To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January Tuesday, 7 1873.

Pleasant day — Got to studio at 9.30
to work on water color. About 11.
Kate came & sat as a model
for head & hands until 1 o'clk
when we walked home to
lunch — back together at 2.30
Fred. was there before us — he
has not yet finished drawing
of foot. I worked away at
head making horrible work
of it until 4.30 — Then Kate
went home — At 5 — went over
to M.M.C.A. about gymnasium
closet — then called on Cy. Brodle
home to tea at 6.30 — Evety —
wrote a note to R.H. Stoddard
& worked over large photos,
until after 11 — Rec'd a note
from Wm. Hy. Blackburn — To bed
at 11.30.
