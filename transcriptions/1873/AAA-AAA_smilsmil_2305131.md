## Left Page

### November, Sunday, 2, 1873

Charming day, but got up with
a headache — About 9 when I
had [illegible] — Walked about until
11, when I went to Dr. Hastings
& heard him preach on walking —
sermon up on Prov. 1¥ ch 9th v.
Home at 12.30 — was just able to
carve at dinner, then gave
up to my headache — came
at fit about 9 p.m. — when
Uncle A. N. V. called — Got
to bed at 12.30 —

## Right Page

### November, Monday, 3, 1873

Clear, bright day, but very windy
& dirty — Very dyspeptic and
somewhat rheumatic. Started
on drawing for Appleton's "The
descent into the Yosemite by the
Mariposa trail" — At noon went
to studio & got photos — back at
1.30 — lunched — P.m. worked
until near 4 then gave up
& walked to Y.M.C.A. [illegible] —
called on Louis Tiffany & Wm. Hart
— then to 126 W. 23d St. to see J.C.
McCall — not in — then home to
tea at 6 — Evef. Robt. Swenson
called with [illegible] for dies — wrote
notes to [illegible] — Jno. Durand
& Dr. Satterlee — To bed at
10 o'clk —
