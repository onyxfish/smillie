## Left Page

### November Monday, 10 1873.

Cold, but pleasant. After bkft. prepared page block for new drawing of Montmorenci Falls &
made sketch on paper. About 10 o'clk. went to Carpenter's & had a board made to fit blocks while drawing — then to Astor Library, stopping at Kurtz's & Leavitt's on the way. At Library, Reading & making notes for St. Lawrence & Saguenay article until 1.30 — Then home to lunch at 2.15 — At 3.30 went to Studio & swept it — Home at 5.45 — Tea at 6. Evelf. until 9 o'clk. Reading Verne — then fussing with drawing board until 10 o'clk. To bed soon after —

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 11 1873.

Delightful day. After bkft. made several notes on W.C. business and at about 10 o'clk. started for Astor Library. Snedecor met me and I had to go into his place to see some of his pictures — Got to Library at 11 o'clk. Read until after 1 — Home to lunch at 2 — Then to Studio shortly after 3 — Met Mrs. E. Moran & had a visit with her at the [illegible] head. Worked in Studio, cleaning up, until 5.30 — then to Brooks Bros. Home to Tea at 6.30 — Evelf. Read Verne until near 10 — To bed at 10.30 — Raining & blowing.
