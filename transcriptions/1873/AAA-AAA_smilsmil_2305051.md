## Left Page

### May, Monday 26, 1873

Delightful day — got to
studio at 9.15 — Jessie came
& I worked from her — Dr
Pavey called & with him I
walked up home to lunch
at 1.45 — back at 2.45 — My
"Lambs" all on hand — their
last lesson. I tried to work
on my A.B.N. Co. drawing
Dr. P. & two ladies came about
4 o'clk. I went with them
into Calverly's rooms & he
took a cast of the hand of
one of them. I worked until
7.30 — Home to tea about
8 — evef, mounted drawings
& to bed at 10.30
Father went up to Po'[keepsie] m[?]
propeller with Matt &
Ed, this evef.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 27, 1873

Still delightful weather — got
to studio at 9 o'clk. & spent
the mf on A.B.N. Co's drawing
Home to lunch at 1.15 — back
at 2.15 — went to App. Co. with [illegible]
case for Mr. Bellows — then to A.B.N.
Co. my drawing did not take very
well, to my great disappointment,
for I thought it one of my best. Then
to Appleton's — Prince, not in —
then to Snedecors — to Moore's &
to studio at 6 o'clk. Wrote
a letter to G.F. Bellows &
home to tea soon after 7 —
evef, Reading news papers
& wrote a note to W.S. Baker
of Phila & to bed about 11.
