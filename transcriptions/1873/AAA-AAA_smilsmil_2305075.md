## Left Page

### July Sunday, 13 1873

*Mt. Washington Summit Ho. Room 13*

Up late. 9.30 when we had our
[illegible] — felt rather headachy —
Father & I walked down the Road
& sauntered about until near 1 o'clk
Then to our Room & napped until
dinner time 2 o'clk. at 3. Mr. &
Mrs N. Father & I started for the top of
Mt. Washington — we reached the
top in 3½ hours. Altho' the horizon
was very hazy, the views all about
were very grand. We had Tea
soon after & then out wandered
about the [illegible] — The sunset
was very fine in color — but had a
still covered the horizon — We
spent the every chatting with
Mr. & Mrs. D [illegible] & going over the new
house. The night was very thick &
until the moon Rose — dark. Father
felt the wild influence of the place
especially when we went out about
10 o'clk into the blinding, soaking
fog. When we arrived the wind blew
at the Rate of 40 miles p² hour
& increased until 11, when it
reached 76 miles — so Recorded at
the Signal Station — To bed in the
new house, a little before 11 —

## Right Page

### July Monday, 14 1873

*On Str. John Brooks — Room 59*

Up about 7 — Heavy driving fog. Had
[illegible] at 8 o'clk & then during the rest
of the mfg wandered about over the
Mt. tops, Rewarded by occasional
views into the glories of cloud land
through momentary rifts down
to the valleys below — but most of
the time the fog was so thick that,
even the distance of a few feet we
looked spectral to one another.
About noon over 70 people arrived
by RR & a [illegible] stages — we got
into a furor because our driver
did not arrive & at last, at quarter
past 1 o'clk, we took one of the drivers —
& without any dinner, drove for the
glen. The views as we descended
were very impressive & [illegible]. We
got to the Ho. at 2.40 & found the 2.30
stage still waiting for us — Rushed
on, without dinner & got to Gorham
in time for 4.15 train — Got into
Portland at 9 & went at once on
board a Boston boat & secured the
last stateroom, a very nice one —
Father turned in at 9, but I
enjoyed the salt air & motion
until 11 o'clk —
