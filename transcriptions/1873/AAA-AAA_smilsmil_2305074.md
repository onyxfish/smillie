## Left Page

### July Friday, 11 1873.

*At St. Lawrence Hall — Room 136*

Mng. overcast — Up at 6.30
& got to Hotel about 8 oclk. and
had bkfst. at once — Father +
I very tired. Saunged up Beaver
Hall St — To Noltman's in Bleury
St. & around until 11 oclk —
Heavy shower at 1 — Wrote
to Kate — Dinner at 2 — & at
3, started out & spent the p.m.
on the wharves — Climbed
the tower of the French Cath[edral]
& back to Hotel at 6 — Rested
for an hour & after much
vexation, determined to give
up the Ottawa trip & start
Portlandward tomorrow mng.
Tea at 8 oclk. Hung about
until after 9 & to bed about
10 — Beautiful cool evng.

## Right Page

### July Saturday, 12 1873.

*Glenette, White Mts Room 107*

Up at 5 oclk. Charming mng. clear & cool
Bkfst. at 6. & at 6.40 off for Station & at 7, off for
Gorham by R.R. For a time the country very flat
& well cultivated, then still flat — very
bare & ragged — nearing the boundary it bec[ame]
more hilly — Reached Island Pond at 1.15 —
dinner. Ran along side of Connecticut Riv[er]
some distance — Mts. increasing in size
until we reached Gorham at 4 oclk. —
Stage at once to the Glenhouse, where
we arr. about 6 oclk. — The ride was
very charming — but cool — Ther. 55°.
Tea at 7 and after that a short
walk — Then in the parlor with the
Nickerson's until about 10 — Then to
bed.
