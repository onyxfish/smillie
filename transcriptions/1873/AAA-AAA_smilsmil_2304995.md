## Left Page

### January, Friday, 24, 1873.

Horrible day of sleet & rain & snow. Got to studio at 9.15.
Retouched Italian drawing
No 2 (Caves) and got to work
on Water Color at 11.30 —
Prepared lunch at 1.30 —
and after, worked until
5 — in spite of the dark day.
Wrote a note to Will Kimball
& left it at his house. Home
at 6.45 — Eveg. Read
papers — prepared gargle
for my sore throat — & wrote
notes to Filmer — J. W. Wood
& Geo. Whitney — To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday, 25, 1873.

Clear, cold day. Got to the
studio a few minutes after 9 —
To work on water-color — somewhat
interrupted by calls. Prepared
lunch at 1.30 — & worked on
after it, until 5 o'clk. — Dr.
Percy called — also Miss Steking
and one of her brothers — at 5
went down to Gauthier's — then
to Schaus's — then to Snedecor's —
then to W. Gilbert's — 142 E.
16th — an interminable talker.
It was 7 o'clk. before I could
get away from him — Home
to tea at once — Eveg.
spent in a wasteful sort
of way and to bed at 11.
