## Left Page

### January Tuesday, 28 1873.

Filmy & slightly overcast —
Got to studio at 9.15. Worked
on drawing until about 2 o'clk
when I prepared lunch
and again at work at 2.30
Louis Tiffany called at
1 o'clk. Worked until 5 —
then went to Lauthier's —
— back to studio & home to
tea at 7 o'clk — at 8 o'clk
at N.A. of D — Annual Meeting
of Water Color Socty. A
good working quorum present.
Found myself elected
President — Louis Tiffany
taking my place as Pres't.
Walked up with Tiffany
& F. Hopkinson Smith — Home
at 10.30 & to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### January Wednesday, 29 1873.

Superb winter day — Clear and
very cold — After stopping at
Bogardus's, got to studio at
9.25 — Worked on drawing
until 1.15 — home to lunch
and back at 2.30 — Worked
until 5 o'clk. Calverley gave
me a sitting as a model —
Home to tea at 6.30 — Swell
battered over Water Color
money matters — wrote notes
to F.H. Smith & W.T. Richards
and got to bed about 10.45.
