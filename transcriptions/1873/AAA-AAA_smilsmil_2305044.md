## Left Page

### May, Monday, 12, 1873

Delightful day. Got to the studio at 9 o'clk. & to work on Adirondack Camp drawing for Appletons. Worked until 1.30 — Fred Smillie brought up old drawing of "Havana" — Home to lunch at 2 o'clk — Nette Sutphen there — back at 2.45 — Misses Sterling, Bond & Jas. Slade there — Worked on a sketch in oils for Miss B. & then painted a little one "Sunlight" — Did not get home to tea until nearly 7.30 — At 8.30 went to Dr. Hastings — 27 — W. 46th. Home at 9.50 — mounted Adirondack drawing washed brushes & to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday, 13, 1873

Overcast m'f, threatening rain, but it soon cleared off. Got to studio at 9 & worked on Adirondack drawing — Some time lost by visit from Augers & Garibaldi — a little before 2 went to Y.M.C.A. Bldg. to George's old room & got a few things — Saw Robbins — back to studio & home to lunch abt. 1.5. Back at 2.15 Thunder shower between 3 & 4 o'clk. Set palette & Repainted a little Twilight sketch 6×10 — painted some years ago & owned by Bleede the frame maker. Finished soon after 6. Home to tea at 7.15 — Eve'g Reading and to bed at 10 o'clk.
