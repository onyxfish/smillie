## Left Page

### May, Thursday 8, 1873

Miserable stormy weather again — headachy — got to studio at 9.30.
Kindled fire + mounted several pieces of paper for drawings.
Wrote a note to Will K. Went on a message for [illegible] + home to lunch at 1.15 — Matt [illegible?]
to [illegible] at 3.30 — I gave up to my headaches until I remembered that I had forgotten my [illegible].
Got to studio at 3.20 + found all three waiting for me. I tried to give them a lesson. —
Home to tea at 6.40. During sent over two tickets to Concert of Church Music Ass'n. Kate +
I went. I was delighted. —
Haydn's Symphony in C minor. Weber's mass in C + Mendelssohn's Walpurgis Night — The Chorus singing was very fine. The solo not so good. — Home at 10.50 — to bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### May, Friday 9, 1873

Furious wind + Rain — got to studio at 9.15 + to work on drawing for Appleton's. "A camping party in the Adirondacks." Worked until 7.15 — home to lunch — dressed + back at 2.30 —
worked until 5.45 — then to Kimball's — tea + with Fanny, her mother + [illegible], went to the Madrigal concert at Steinway Hall (In Memoriam, Dr. Brown) + was delighted — The Cookes and Haun's sat with us. Enjoyed particularly "The Wish" Kücken and a bit for female voices from Mendelssohn's "Lorelei" —
Out at 10.30. Raw + disagreeable but no Rain — Home at 11.15
to bed at 11.45
