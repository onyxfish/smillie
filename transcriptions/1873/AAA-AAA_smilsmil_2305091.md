## Left Page

### August Thursday, 14 1873.

Still Raining hard nearly all day — [illegible] as usual about 7.30 Then all the mrf at work on studio plans — Walked for half an hour on piazza, before dinner abt 6 clk — Worked again on my plans from 3 o [illegible] until near 5 — Then to Carpent[er] & brought home drawing boar[ds]. Tea at 6 — Soon after[,] went to Court House & heard McCart[hy] (Kate Searle's husband) & Fitch sum up a case — The Room was crowded & foul — Before 9 I went out & walked about for 3/4 of an hour to air myself then up & to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### August Friday, 15 1873.

Mild, & pretty much all day clearing up — Abt 10.30 Dr. Mulford called with Judge Porcw— [illegible] & wife & took me out to Jas. Sarmalt's where at 11.30 we had a lunch — Elisha M. & wife. Bessie Henry & Kate Parker Riley. Fanny Drinker. Mr. Price, son & daughter — After a pleasant time, was driven back at 2.30 — dinner at 1.15 — a long chat after with Rev. Dr. Taylor — from 3 to 5 again on plans — Then Elisha M. came & we went to the M. home & I hung pictures until 6 then up, to tea — After tea went to [illegible] Lathrop's house — then with the young people until near 8 — then home — letters from Matt & [illegible]. At 8.40 went to Wm Mulford's & visited there until near 10 — then home again — Read paper & to bed at 11 o clk.
