## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 26 1873

Very clear, cold & blowy — Got to
studio at 9.30 and to work on
block." Out of the Thousand Islands"
worked until 12.30 — Wrote to
Mrs. A. Fraser — Home to lunch at
1.00 — back at 2.45 — Mary Swady
called at the house at 1.30 —
worked on block — Annie
Cook called to see about her
picture — could not see to
work after 4.45 — Wrote notes
to Dr. Satterlee & Jones — Then
walked down to Miss MacDonald's
Bdway & 10th St — Then to Coleman —
He & saw J.A. Richards — Home
to tea at 7 — Ev'g, finished
letter to Coleman & got to
bed at 10.15

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 27 1873

Overcast day, but cloudless
moonlight night — Got to studio at
9.15 & finished block that I drew
upon yesterday, then commenced
large block "View from top of Mont-
morenci's falls" — worked steadily
until 1 — Then home to lunch,
a rather, Thanksgiving dinner —
Back at 2.30 & worked until
4.30 when I could see no
longer. Took a nap and at
5.30 started for home — Walked
up 5th Ave to 48th St & home to
tea at 6.20 —

Ev'g, Read newspapers
& wrote to Fenn & Bellows —
To bed at 10 —

Old Mrs. Mulford of Montrose
died this m'g.
