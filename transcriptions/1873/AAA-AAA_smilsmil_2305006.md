## Left Page

### February Wednesday, 19 1873

Dull [illegible], with heavy Rain at noon — cleared of clouds after 3 P.M. Got to studio at 9.45 and settled myself to try to write Yosemite article — hard work — and called myself hard names many times — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45 — wrote until 5, then over to N.A. D — then to Y.M.C.A. — called on Gifford — then down to gymnasium — worked short time, back to studio at 6.30 made tea — & between 7 & 8, again at work — abit until 10 o'clk — Home at 10.30 and to bed at 11 — Matt, Kate & Ed. to Po'keepsie on 10.30 A.M. Train.

## Right Page

### February Thursday, 20 1873

Uplate — gloomy day — got to studio a little before 10 — very much interrupted — Tried to write "Yosemite" — at 12.30 went over to N.A.D. to see Richards — out — Home to lunch at 1.30. Back to studio at 2.45 and wrote until 4.20 — then to Appleton's with part of my MS. — Stopped on my way back at Sauthier's & Snedecor's. At studio at 6.15 — Home to tea at 7 — Well, clearing out closet and to bed at 10.30.
