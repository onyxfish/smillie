## Left Page

### May Wednesday, 14 1873.

Very pleasant day — went to Knoelle
to buy paints & palette. There met
Geo. Whitney — 10 o'clk. when I
got to studio — To work on
Wery on Racquette Lake (3d day)
at 1 o clk. W.W. came in and
I went over to the Albemarle
& lunched with him — back
at 2.30 & worked until 6 —
getting over sky & mountain.
Home to tea at 6.45 —
Wery wrote a short sketch
of Father's life for N.S. Baker
of Phila & note to S.S. Conant.
To bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 15 1873.

Another delightful day. Got
to studio at 8.30 & to work on
Racquette Lake, but very much
interrupted by callers, among
them, Elisha Mulford. Home
to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.30
are three pupils at work.
I tried also to go on with
my painting, with no great
success. Converse Smieth called
for a few moments. I worked
as long as I could see, but
did not get my painting fin-
ished — The whole picture now
looks very like a custard.
Home to tea at 7.30 — Wery
went up to Burgralf's with boys
— Read newspapers & to bed at
10.45
