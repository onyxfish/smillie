## Left Page

### May, Friday, 16, 1873.

My, a little threatening, but soon
cleared charmingly — Got to studio
at 9 + spent the my between
Adirondack camp drawing +
Havana drawing for A.B.N. Co.
At 12.15 went to A.B.N. Co +
lunched there — Camp, + the
party. Got instructions for more
drawing — on my way back I
stopped at Appleton's. Bunce
does not like my drawing —
it is not what he fancied
camping in the Adirondacks to
be — Got to studio at 3.30 Miss
Bond then came + painted —
Brewer called. I worked for
A.B.N. Co. At 6.30 went to
the Kimball's to tea — every
all went to the Haws's to sing.
Home at 11.15 — To bed abt 12.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday, 17, 1873.

Delightful day. Got to studio at
9.15. All the my. Designing
new viet. (upright) to National
Currency V's. Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30. Dr. Percy
called. Jessie Hayland came
+ I drew from her from 4.30
until 6 o'clk — Home to tea
at 7.30. At 8.45. at Mrs.
Chas. Smith's (Cornice Columns)
in [illegible]. Helped hang pictures
+ had a pleasant visit until
11 o'clk — Mew home and
to bed at midnight.
