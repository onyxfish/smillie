## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 11 1873.

Up at 7 — grooming — Bkfst. at 8 —
got to work at 10 — Glorious
mtt. Commenced on page block
"Saguenay River" — getting it
transferred — outlined and sky
washed in. Josie R. & Hetty [illegible]
called — invited me to tea this evg
& we made arrangements to ride
this P.M. — Dinner at 1.30 — Dres[sed]
& soon after 3 at the Searles — Jac[ob]
was Fos[t] in his buggy — I took Josie
went over Butterfield's Hill to Five
Hill — Delicious day — but milky so
that extreme distance was alm[ost]
lost. Back by Wyalusing Road — in
a little before 7 — then tea — su[pper]
"Cal. Jack" & "Pitch". Home abou[t]
10.30 and to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 12 1873.

Another delightful day — out of
sorts — caught cold — slept badly —
nervous. Got through my home duties
& bkfst. & to work about 10 o'clk —
Did not feel like going on with
large block so commenced on
"Dunham Terrace" — Dinner at
1.15 — nearly 3 o'clk — before I got
to work again & gave up at 4.30
Saddled Tom & off for my P.M.
ride which I enjoyed exceedingly —
Tom being very tractable and
learning what I want of him —
in at 6.30 — Well very tired but
at 7.30 called on Jan. Mulford
who has just arr[ived] from Chicago.
At 8.45 went to Baptist Church
Festival — met there Jos. Mrs. — Hettie
& Josie — went home with them —
there until 10.30 — Home & to bed
at 11.
