## Left Page

### March, Friday 7, 1873

Clear, nice day. Up early and at Appleton's ab't 9 o'clk. with corrected pf. got to studio at 9.30 & wrote all the morning — produced very little. Had a long call from young Stephen H. Parker. Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.15 and wrote again, all the p.m. — Forgot to say that Jerusha V.V. Goockler was at lunch — also that Mrs. McHarg and sister called at the house — Did not go to Phil. Rehearsal. Home to tea at 6.40 — at 8.15 at Hawes, No. 21 Bond — Singing. Rode up in cars with Fanny & Willt. Emma & Katie Greatory. Home at 11.5 — to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 8, 1873

Very nice & pleasant w.f. Got up with a headache — Stirred myself on with coffee — Wrote checks & letters for Water Color So'ty until near 10 — then to bank — Called on Greene & got to studio at 10.30 — Wrote until 12.30 — Then had to give up — Clouded up & commenced to shower — home and right to bed — did not get up until after 8 o'clk. Sat in my chair and rolled on the sofa all night.
