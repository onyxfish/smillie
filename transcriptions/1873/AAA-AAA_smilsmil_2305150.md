## Left Page

### December Sunday, 14 1873

Splendid m[orning]. Breezy +
cold. Matt + Ed. am[?] from P.
M[?], went to Dr. Hastings[?]
He preached from 27th Psalm
4th v. — after service walked
home with Mr. Griffis & Miss
Rogers. 23 W. 48th St. Dinner
at 1.30 — Slept in my chair
most of the p.m. — Tea at 6 —
Even[ing] went again to Dr.
Hastings + heard a very
spirited discourse — In Ch.
16th, Ch. 13th v. — After service
walked awhile with Mr.
Ed. Anthony — Home at 9.30
Nearly 11.30 when I got
to bed.

## Right Page

### December Monday, 15 1873

Delightful day. Got to studio
at 9.30 — Kindled fire, set
palette + started painting on
small picture for Gauthier —
"View on the Saco at North
Conway, N.H." Things worked
nicely. Home to lunch at 1.30
just in time to see Matt + Ed.
on their way home to P. Back
to studio at 2.30 + worked
until 4.30, when I got this
painting finished. It looks
quite bright — for me. Home to
tea at 6 — Evell[?] at 8.30
at Mr. Ripley's — 13 E. 32nd St. —
ladies at home — met their
bro. Dwight — Whist until
11 o'clk — Home + to bed
a few minutes before 12.
