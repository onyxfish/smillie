## Left Page

### April, Wednesday, 2, 1873

Muggy day — part of the time over cast & threatening —
at noon, clear & very hot. About
4.30, heavy showers. At 9, went
up to Station "9" — then rode
down to the coats & left umbrellas
Stopped at 10.15 Studios & made
a number of calls — then to Amerelli
gallery & saw the Brudecor pictures
Got to studio at 11 — had to kindle
fire — unable to work — played
out & springy. Home to lunch
at 2 — back at 3.15 — at 5. at
Appleton's — Bunce paid me
$80 for my Yosemite article.
Went down to Dewey's — paid
bill and ordered Spring varnish
Back to Studio at 6.15 — Home
at 7 — Evng wrote several
notes & got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Thursday, 3, 1873

Threatening, April-like day —
sunshine & shade. Got to
studio at 9.15 — Had to kindle
fire again. Spent the mng.
on W.C. sketch, "A clearing in
the Adirondacks" — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Misses S.
& B. & [illegible] Slade there. Hard
at work with them until 6 o'clk.
Home at 6.45, calling at the K's
on my way — At 8.30 at Annie
Coats's — an informale, musical
party — with piano — singing, etc
very pleasant — About 10.30, mr.
Kinder symphony was given.
Ran away at 11 — Home and
to bed at 11.45.
