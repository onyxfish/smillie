## Left Page

### June Saturday, 21 1873

Extremely hot & dry — work at home — A.B.N. Co's drawing —
Had to go to studio abt 1 o'clk —
Took Willie with me — back
at noon. Lunch at 1.30 and
at 3 again at Studio, to meet
Mr. Richardson, who had
made an appointment to
meet me, but he did not do
it. Took 5.30 boat from
foot of 24th St for Pleasant Valley
— Met Mattie Rivard & her
three children. Found Geo.
a little dumpy — After dinner
went out to row with Mr & Mrs.
Umans, Mrs. Rivard & their children
& Fred joined — back in an hour —
11 o'clk when I got to bed —

## Right Page

### June Sunday, 22 1873

Overcast day which gave us
hopes of Rain — but they were
very scantily realized. During
the a.m., I took a [illegible] for an
hour, taking Geo with me —
after that, a nap and dinner
at 2.30 — occasional extremely
delicate showers. General
chatting on piazza & about
until tea time — near 7 o'clk —
Everg. quite chilly. walked
the piazza with Mr. Brown.
There was some singing of
sacred music — to bed abt
11.15
