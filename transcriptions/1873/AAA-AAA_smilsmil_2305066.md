## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 25, 1873

Charming day — at 11 o'clk
a.m., went to A.B.N. Co., My
new drawing gives great
satisfaction — lunched there,
got $256 [illegible]. Ran around,
— to Charl's — to National
Bank Note Co. + saw Jones —
and to other places. At
3.30 to Appleton's + saw
Bunce. — At 5, at studio,
Miss Bond there — at 6.30
home to tea — at 8.30
at Mr. Tiffany's, Madison
Ave. — pleasant [illegible] —
home at 10.15 + to bed
at 10.45 —

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 26, 1873

Another superb day. Mrf.
spent upon the two figure
drawings that I have just
finished for the A.B.N. Co. At
12.30, lunch + then down town.
Made deposit in Savings Bank —
two or three messages and at
2.30 Stayner, Van Zandt and I
crossed Wall St. Ferry — there
met Stayner's carriage, his wife,
his sister + we all rode down
to Coney Island — got there
at 4 o'clk — walked for an hour
on the beach — at 5.30 dinner
at Beneder's — Soup, fish, crabs, beef,
pigeons, etc — etc + champagne — Van
Zandt was very funny. About
7 started back — got to Stayner's
house about 8 — a look through
then V. and I took cars to Fulton
Ferry — walked up to the Astor[?]
V. took 3d ave. + I Bdway cars — got
home at 10 — to bed at 10.30.
[illegible] came down from Pleasant V. to day
