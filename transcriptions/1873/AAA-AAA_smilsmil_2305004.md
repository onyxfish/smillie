## Left Page

### February Saturday, 15 1873.

Overcast — got to studio at
9.30 & to work painting on 20×
24\. "Senaya Cañon" — worked
very hard — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & painted
as long as I could see. Got
all the canvases covered but
the foreground corner. Home
at 6.15 — at 8. at H Phil. Concert
— enjoyed it very much. Walked
home with Miss Howe & Fanny
Kimball. Home at 10.45 and
to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### February Sunday, 16 1873.

Snow all the morning and
rain all the P.M. and a
good part of the night.
Got up with a headache
and fought through until
I carved the dinner. Then
to bed and did not get up
until nearly 9 o'clk. P.M.
Read news papers and
sat about until near 3 o'clk
A.M. Then to bed and
to sleep shortly after 5
A.M.
