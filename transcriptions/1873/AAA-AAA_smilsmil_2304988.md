## Left Page

### January Friday, 10 1873.

Another glorious day. Up soon after 8 — at Studio at 9.30 and at charcoal drawing — Misses Whitney & Avery called at 12.30 — Home to lunch at 1.30 P.M. at Philharmonia — Sadie Dunning Hawes there. Very anxious that we should make up a party to go up to Montclair "coasting". After Rehearsal, went to Snedeco's Appleton's — Schaus's & Sauthins — Home at 6.30 — A few minutes after 8 at Fanny Kimball's — First meeting of Singing Club — Home soon after 11 & to bed at 12.

## Right Page

### January Saturday, 11 1873.

Still glorious weather — Did not make until about 9 o'clk. — and it was 10.15 before I got to the studio — Still worked on the charcoal sketch for water color & commenced full page block "Tenaya Canon" from Glacier Rock. Prepared lunch at 2. Worked on block until near 5 — then went to Sauthins — then to Snedeco's & ordered water color frame. Stopped at the Kimball's & home to tea at 6.30. Swelf lolling in big chair & to bed at 9.45.
