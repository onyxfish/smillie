## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 15 1873

And yet another day of this same sort — afternoon stable
work — Help — at 8 — Nearly 10
before I got off to my sketching
worked away with Water Colors
for an hour, then determined
to go home, get my oil Colors
& go to work with that medium
— did so & sketched in until
12.45, then back to dinner —
back to work at 2.30, very
chilly & uncomfortable in the
shade but kept at it until
4 o'clk — then home and off
on "Patch" when I somewhat
warmed up again — [illegible]
at 5.40 — Tea at 6. Evening
letter from Will Kimball.
Spent the Evefg at [illegible]
Home at 10. — To bed at
11 —

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 16 1873

Up at 6.30 & gave "Patch" rather more
of a scouring than usual — Help
at 8.15 It was 10 o'clk. when I
got to my study ground and
commenced a sketch of Mullens
& other foreground stuff — Worked
faithfully until 12.45 — then home
to dinner — back at 2 & worked
on yesterdays sketch until 4 —
then home, saddled "Patch"
& gave him a hard trot
until 5.30. Washed up — tea
at 6 — made Aquas until 7 —
home & wrote to Father and
Will Kimball — down to P.O.
at 8.30 — quite a shower of rain
— too late to go to [illegible]
Richards, so I went in to
Jan's — there until 10.30 —
then home & to bed at 11 —
