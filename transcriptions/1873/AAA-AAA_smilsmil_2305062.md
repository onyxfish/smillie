## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 17, 1873

Still pleasant weather — got to studio at 9.15 — worked with graver through circular Ruling on Ginsck's die — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Then rode down to A.B.N.C. got [illegible] — ground laid and Ruled sky — back to studio at 5 o'clk — "Stopped art" — Home to tea — dressed and at Kimball's at 7.15 — Went with Emma D, Fanny + Will. to Thomas's garden. Enjoyed the every [illegible]. Home at 12.15 + to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 18, 1873

Still dry but breezy + pleasant at studio som after 9 and bitin Ruling on die — then graver + burnisher — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45 + worked all the p.m. until 6 o'clk on dies. Home to tea + in the even. took Kate to Thomas's, on our way we met Wild Joe + Hatch + his wife — Home at 11.20 + to bed at 12.15
