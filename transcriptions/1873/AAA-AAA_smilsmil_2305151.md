## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 16 1873

Very mild charming day. Did not get to studio until
10 o'clk. Then to work on
Matt's painting (2d day)
worked with much pleasure.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & stopped a little
afore 4 — Did some cleaning
up & at 5 went down to
Lauthier's with his little
picture — he expressed
himself very much pleased
with it. Home to tea at 6.30
Some after 8. at Mr Cook's
192 W 10th St — There until
10 — home at 10.30 — To bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 17 1873

Dense fog — went over to
Mayeck's — 10th Ave. & 44th St —
& could hardly find my
way. Got to studio at 10.30
to work on Matt's "Yosemite"
(3d day). Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and
painted until about 4 —
The fog still continuing.
Had a short nap on the
lounge — Ferguson came
in & at 5.30 I walked up
with him. At 7.30 Charle
& I went to F's in 36th St —
are walked down to Academy
of Music & saw Salvini in
Sampson. F. had the
use of 3 of Kingsland's seats
for the eveg. Home at 11
& Right to bed.
