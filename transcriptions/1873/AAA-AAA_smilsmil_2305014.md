## Left Page

### March Tuesday, 11 1873

Very severe storm of wind and
rain during the m'f. — cleared
charmingly during the p.m —
Rode down to Appleton's with pf —
at studio at 9.30 & wrote pretty
steadily until 5 p.m — had lunch
of crackers and tea in studio —
Went down to A's with two
pages of M.S. that had slipped
from pkg of yesterday. Home
at 6.45 — Out at 8.30 went
to Louis Tiffany's — also to
Mr. Jessup's — entertainment
given to members of Y.M.C.A. —
Took a look around and then
home at 10 o'clk — To bed at
11.

## Right Page

### March Wednesday, 12 1873

Glorious day — Got to studio at 10. Ill
and wrote. Farrer called — then
Harry Fenn — then Louis Tiffany
with whom I walked up the Avenue.
Home to lunch at 2 — Back at 3
& again at writing — Mr. Henshaw
called — much time lost. Home
to tea at 6.30 — Went down from
Po'keepsie. At 8.30 — called on
the Black's in 38th St. Pleasant
time until 10 o'clk — Home at
10.15 and to bed at 11.20.
