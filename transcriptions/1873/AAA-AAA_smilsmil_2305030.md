## Left Page

### April, Saturday 12, 1873

Snow & sleet all the mng —
a most horrible & depressing
state of weather & things.
My head aches & my nose
runs. I am now thoroughly
demoralized and am
wondering how long my con-
stitution will hold out —
Storm held up later in the
P.M. Uncle A. came in.
Slept on the sofa — and as
it was my regular night
in bed, I went there at about
11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 13, 1873

Fresh and cloudy with wind
from the west. Woke up with a
headache again, after a miserable
night of unrest, so kept my bed
until near noon. Then up with
a sore head and a very lame
spot in my chest. Still very
[illegible] — having to fight for my
breath. The sore spot in or
under my breast bone got so
much worse that on going
to bed, found myself fairly
crippled & have had to help
me take off my clothes. Got
into bed about 11 with my first
mustard plaster. Could not
breathe & so, fought & blew my
cussed nose until near midnight
when tired nature could stand
it no longer & I slept, nose
and no more.
