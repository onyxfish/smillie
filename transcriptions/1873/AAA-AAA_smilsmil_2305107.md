## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 15 1873.

Glorious mrf. Swapping frost last night. Up early — groomed my horse + hlcp. at 8.15 — to clk before I got to my work on page block" on the Saguenay" Dinner at 1.30 — The carpenters commenced work on my studio building this afternoon. At 4 o clk gave up work — Saddled Tom + shortly before 5. Rode over to the Saw mill to hurry up some lumber — Had a tip top ride in at 6.30. 7, before I had tea — Chat, as usual, afterwards. 8.30 when I made a short call on Jan — nearly when I got to Searles — Joe Mrs brought in oysters — cooked them + had them with Cal. Jack. Home at 11 — to bed at once — Uncle Wm went to Phil. at noon.

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 16 1873.

Another glorious day. The usual mrf exercises in the stable. Bkfp. at 8.15 — To work about 10. Finished "on the Saguenay" in the mrf. Dinner at 1.30 — To work again at 2.30 on page block "Under Trinity Rock" commenced some time ago. Made but little progress on it as this is a mess. Stopped work some time off. Got away in Tom at 4.45 — again went to Saw mill + ordered additional lumber. Took a longer + harder ride than usual in the west felt quite banged up. In the mill, had Tom's Shoes re-set. Every tea at 7 — downtown messages — a short call at Mr. Searles — Home + to bed at 10, sick.
