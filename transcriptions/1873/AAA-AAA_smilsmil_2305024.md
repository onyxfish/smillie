## Left Page

### March, Monday 31, 1873

A Regular April day — Sunshine & shower — Got to
studio at 9.20 — Kindled fire & then worked on Water
Color view of Uncle A's house — About 11, Fanny K., came
& invited me to tea to meet Rosalie Goodman. Home
to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2 — Misses Stirling & Bond & young
Slade took lessons. At 6.30 went to the K's — Miss Helen
Nelson also there — & cousin in — Alf. Kimball. Had a very
jolly time until 10.45 — Home at 11 — Found Matt there.
Mrs. McHarg's infant died this p.m. —
To bed at 11.45

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 1, 1873

Delicious Spring day. Mild and hazy. Got to Studio at 9.20 and
had to kindle fire — then to work on View of Uncle A's house — W.C. —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked on drawing until
4.30 — Then discovered that my little painting "Moonlight over the
High Sierras," had been blown out of the window into the street and
could not find it anywhere.
— at 5.30 went to Fanny K's — not
in — then went right on to Miss
Cook's — dinner soon after 6 —
every, Rehearsal of the Kinder
Symphonie — Home at 11 —
To bed at 11.20 — Heavy showers
during the eveg. — Thunder
storm at night.
