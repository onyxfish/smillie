## Left Page

### February Sunday, 9 1873

Clear, windy & severely cold.
Myf — went to Dr. Hastings — He
preached an excellent discour —
I have no doubt, but conscienc
demands that again I put on
record, against myself, that
I did not hear all that was
uttered in my hearing — After
service walked home with Ms.
McHarg & her sister — Dinner
at 1.30 — P.M. in big chair
Reading, writing & napping —
Tea at 6 — Welf wrote a
note to Thos. Nast, explaining
why two of his drawings were
not hung in our exhibit.
To bed at 7 1,

## Right Page

### February Monday, 10 1873

Got to studio at 9.30 — very cold
myf, but clear — Kindled fire
& got to work painting on X
"Sunset & Moonrise — Whiteface from
Lake Placid" — Worked until 12.30
then went to N.A. of D. Then to
George's Room to see Bellows, but
he was out — Home to lunch at
1.50 — back at 2.45 and worked
until 5 o'clk — then again over
to N.A.of D. & back to Studio
at 6. Cleaned up & home to
tea at 6.45 — Welf, loafing
& fooling — anything but work —
to bed at 11. 15
