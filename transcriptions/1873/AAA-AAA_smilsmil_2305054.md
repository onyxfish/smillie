## Left Page

### June, Sunday 1, 1873

Superb day — Up at 7 & paced the walk at rear end of lot, listening to the birds & gazing at the blue distance until 9 o'clk — when I had bkfst. At 10.45 went to the Episcopal Ch. & heard Elisha Mulford preach from 1st Cor. 11. ch. 23. 24 & 25 v. After service, wandered to the burying ground with Fan M — Home at 1 — lunched — made a letter to Kate & at 3.45 went to Fan's to dinner. Walked with her to the fair ground to see the sunset. — Back at 7. Home at 8 — wrote a letter to Josie Beatty — to bed at 10.30. Beautiful moonlight night.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 2, 1873

*At the Girardtte. Phila. Room 159*

Up at 7 & had an early bkfst. Weather still glorious. Spent the mff wandering about until 11 o'clk & soon after that had dinner — At 12, Uncle Wm and I rode down to the present terminus of the Montrose R.R., a baby affair of 3 foot gauge. Started at 1.30 & at 3.15 got to Tunkhannock — at 3.55 took cars on Lehigh Valley R.R. & at 10.30 arr. at Phila — after a very exciting & interesting ride. Enjoyed especially from Wilkes barre to Mauch Chunk. — Went with Uncle W. to the Girard Ho. — washed up & got to bed a little after midnight.
