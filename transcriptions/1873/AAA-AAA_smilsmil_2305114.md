## Left Page

### Sept. Monday, 29 1873.

Cloudy, but breezy & delightful.
Up at 6.30 — Mr Tables & [illegible]
after carpenters until 8 o'clk.
Bl[illegible] — at 8.20 — work interfered
with very much — full page
block "Quebec" — a [illegible] went
out to "Studio" — at 12.45 went
down town with Uncle Willie —
at 1.20, dinner — Interested
very much in progress of
ceiling studio — there until
3 o'clk — Very heavy shower
& rain — Worked on "Quebec"
until near 5 o'clk. Then [illegible]
on "Tom" — Carrie on "Polkey" —
Rode until 6.30. Then went
to Jarbell's & back with a great
bag of oats before me on the saddle
— Caught in another heavy shower.
Tea at 8 — Then down town — letter
from Father enclosing $100? — [illegible]
at Fan Mulford's for half an hour —
Home & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 30 1873.

Cleared very cool, but charming.
Usual [illegible] duties attended to
[illegible] at 8 — Then Carpenters
at drawing on "Quebec" until 12.
Then wrote a letter home —
Uncle W. & Aunt A. — by mistake
did not get away at noon, as
intended — Dinner at 1 —
with carpenters until 3 —
At last, finished "Quebec"
at 5 o'clk. Went out for a
ride on "Sam" — Ever very
brisk & had a first rate
rides — in at 7 o'clk. bright
moonlight. Tea at 7.30 —
Then a Romp until 8.30
with Ella & Flora Lathrop —
Then down to Fan Mc's —
Home at 10.30 & right
to bed.
