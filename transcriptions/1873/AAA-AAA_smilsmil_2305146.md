## Left Page

### December Saturday, 6 1873

In bed until after 4 o'cl
P.M. with headache and
general distress — Then
up & cleaned up a little
& took some nourishment
in my big chair, rolled
in my blanket until about
2.30 a.m. then I went to
bed & got some sleep.

## Right Page

### December Sunday, 7 1873

Very pleasant day, but still feeling
so shaky that I did not go out
out of the house until between
3 & 4 oclo. when I took a
short walk in Reservoir
Sq. Dinner at 1.30. Tea
at 6. From 7.30 to 8.30
walked in the parlor — then
made notes to S.S. Conant
& Thos. Moran.
