## Left Page

### November Monday, 24 1873.

A horrible day, wind & rain driving furiously. Got to studio at 9.30 & found that Dodun th[e] Solomon & a carpenter were in ahead of me & had turned things generally topsy turvy preparing to fixing ceiling where the leak last summer came in — Tried to work through it — succeeded indifferently — outlined & transferred two or three subjects to blocks — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — Home to tea at 5.30 — tea at 6, in the kitchen — Every — a family breakdown to celebrate Father's & Will's birth day — Mary's 5th and Matt's 1[4th] wedding anniversary. The weather very much improved — Quite a number of the family out — I got to bed about 11.25 —

## Right Page

### November Tuesday, 25 1873.

Weather yet unsettled, and so was I head — At 9.30 went up to 49th St. J2A Ward's. Found him in — just home from a Buffalo hunt — He entertained me for an hour & half with descriptions of his experiences — Then rode down to City Hall & saw Douglas Jauger — Comm. of Jurors. He will let me off for $50 — Got to studio at 2.30 — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back to studio at 3 — Did nothing but lie on sofa — Home to tea at 6 — Quite a snowflurry — then clear — Back to studio at 7.40 — Every had an adjourned Water Color meeting — Good business meeting — through at 9.30 — left at 10.20 — got to bed about 11 —

Geo went down to St. Nicholas & saw Maud D'wen who has just ret'd from Europe on St. Nevada —
