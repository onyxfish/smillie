## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 23 1873.

Disagreeable day, with threats &
rain, & finally, at night, a deluge.
After 10 o'clk. when I got started at
my legitimate work on page block
"Quebec from the River" Reduced
it, Traced & commenced to outline
it, much bothered by perspective
& the many buildings. Dinner sometime
after. Afternoon, worked until
near 5 — then Rigged me by boots
& Rubber Coat & walked out to
Jim Carmalt's — back at 6.30 — Tea
at 7 — Eve'g, went to fam Md's [illegible]
Tea — Cards with fam. Quasday morn
& the Dr. until after 10 — Intensely
dark night & Rain pouring down —
To bed at 11.15 —

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 24 1873.

Rained out last night, for a pleasant
day followed. Charming effects of
cloud & shadow. Ovesle & myself
"Tom" got off with a short cunning
after bkfs. Time lost with the
carpenters — To work outlining
page block "Quebec &c." At
noon the Misses Boggs got
away for Wilkes barre. Dinner
at 1.30 — worked until near 5 —
then "saddled" Tom & went to
the new depot. Trains came
in at 6.20 — got on it — Carrie
had gone down for Uncle Wm
but Uncle W. did n't come —
so Joe rode in with her. Tea
about 8. Stuart & Josie & Hetty
& Mrs. Dud's — [illegible] — visited
with them — Home & to bed
soon after 10.
