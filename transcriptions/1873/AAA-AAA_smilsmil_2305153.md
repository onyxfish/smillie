## Left Page

### December Monday, 22 1873

Clear, cold day — Got to
studio at 9.30 — Kindled fire
& got to work on water color
drawing 5×6½ as a mate
to one by Geo. Soon after 12
went into Miss Richard's [illegible]
Room & painted a background
for one of her heads. Home
to lunch at 1.45 — back at
2.30 & worked as long as I
could see — Home to tea
at 5.30 — Every. at 8 o'clk
went to Mrs. Perkins's
121 W. 44th & called on Mr.
& Mrs. Walsh — Enjoyed a
very chatty eveet until
10.30 — then home and
to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 23 1873

Dull until about 3 o'clk. when
snow commenced & kept it
up until night. Got to studio
at 9.30 — got started on char-
coal sketches of water color
subject "Lost" — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& worked as long as I could
see — Ferguson came in,
thru Nicoll & I was kept
until about 6.45 — then
home to tea — Eveng
wrote at note to Mr. Chas
Laumnan & got to bed
at 10.30
