## Left Page

### May Sunday, 4 1873

Delicious day — went to Dr. Hastings's and heard
a capital sermon from the 84th
Psalm 6th v. After service walked
with Charl. up to 51st St. & around
by Grand Central Depot. Home to
dinner at 1 o'clk. At 3 started
out with Charl — went to Jno. P.
Towns and's — there for half an
hour — then to A.N.V.'s & while there
Mr. S. went over to Will's — back
to A.N.V.'s at 7 — tea — Sverf.
wrote composition for Suniie
Charl & Millie Palmer came
in — all walked down home
at 9.30 — to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### May Monday, 5 1873

Clear & very warm. Got to studio
at 9.10 & spent an hour arranging
water color accounts. Col. Jas.
Fairman, just arr. from Germany
took another hour, then Mr.
Braisland & one of Mr. Corell's daughter
took more time — then Miss
Richardson & her mother — until
at 12 o'clk — I found my morning
gone — then over to Y.M.C.A. Bed &
called on Gifford — then to Laurtheis &
ordered new glass for A.N.V.'s frame.
Then to 6th Nat. Bank. & home to
lunch at 1.30. Aunt H., Suniie, & Jac.
at the house — back at 2.30. Misses
Bond, Sterling & Jas. Slade there.
I worked on A&S, N. Co. drawing for
N. Nat. currency. Then put the
A.N.V. drawing in frame — Home
at 7.30 — tea at 8. Sverf in big
chair & to bed at 10.45
