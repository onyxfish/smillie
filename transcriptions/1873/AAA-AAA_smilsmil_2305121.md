## Left Page

### October, Monday 13, 1873

Clouded up, but cleared — Cold & Raw — Still feeling very much under the weather. Up at 7.15 — Bkfst at 8 — about 10, after Ansten came to paint went down town — Spent time myself until 12 with Cotrill selecting lumber — Boyd & others — Dinner at 1 — After 3, out on horseback — Called on Mrs. Carmalt — back 4 or after 5 — Jean & 6 — Evg. went with Flora Lathrop to Court House to hear some bell Ringers — a funny lot of Bohemians — Back at 9.45 — To bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 14, 1873

Still superb Oct. weather — Very mild, cloudless & hazy — After bkfst at 8 — Some time with Mitchell, who is painting my studio — About 10, out with water color traps to a lot beyond the Fair ground & made a sketch — Blackberry & Thistle — Dinner at 1 — Back to my work at 2.30 — & worked on a water sketch until 4. Then home, quite chilled — Saddled "Patch" & had a fine ride — in at 5.30 — Tea at 6 — Cotrill called at 7 with his bill — At 8.30 went down town — Rec'd letter from Charl enclosing $200 — In at Jans — Met Mrs. Jeucks — took her home — Home myself at 9.45 — and to bed at 10.30
