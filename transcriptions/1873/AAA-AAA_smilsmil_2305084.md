## Left Page

### July Thursday, 31 1873.

Still very hot. B[illegible]. at 8 — at work again on Saguenay material until lunch at 1 o'clk. — at 3 o'clk. Rode down town to Harley's and from him got block and Chromo "Yosemite" that he wishes me to draw for him. — Back at 4.30 + worked until tea time — 6 o'clk — Chare at Winter's (Brooklyn) all night — Suey at 8 o'clk went to Thomas's Garden. — Heavy night — 5th Symphony, Wagner + Liszt — Enjoyed it very much — Home at 11.15 and to bed at 11.30 —

## Right Page

### August Friday, 1 1873.

Very hot & muggy — Shower bet. 8 + 9 a.m. — all day again on sketches for wood drawings — lunch abt 1 o'clk — worked until between 5 + 6 — Then another shower made it too dark to see — Tea at 6 — Suey, spent evening and to bed at about 1. —
