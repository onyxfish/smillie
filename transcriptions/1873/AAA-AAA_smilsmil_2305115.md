### October Thursday, 2 1873

Glorious weather still continues.
Tired & up so late that I am"
went without grooming. B[reak]k[fast]
at 8 o'clk — Near 10 again when
I got to work on drawing. Dinner
about — 3 when I got to work —
made good progress & away
on "Tom" at 5.15 — Had a short
ride (7 miles) and in at 6.30
Tea at 7.30 — another romp
with the Lathrop girls and
with them & [illegible] — went up
to Mr. Wm. Coolfacts — Home
at 8.30 — then went down
to Fain's — there until near
10 — then home & to bed at
