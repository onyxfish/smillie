## Left Page

### March, Sunday, 23, 1873

A mild, Spring-like day —
went to Dr. Hastings's &
heard him preach a very
good sermon from Rev. 2° Ch
4th v. Home at 1.15 — wrote a
note to Meta Adams — Dinner
about 1— then wrote a note
to Darley. At 3.45 walked
up to Uncle A's — a short
visit, then up to Mr. Jessup's
— took tea and visited
until 9 o'clk — walked
home and to bed at 10.20

## Right Page

### March, Monday, 24, 1873

Overcast and growing colder
until night, then a sleeting,
sleety storm setting. Got to Studio
at 9.20 — kindled fire & worked
awhile on Shasta drawing — then
finished water-color sketch
that I made Sat. p.m.
Commenced another and
became so much interested
that it was 2 o'clk before I
knew where I was — Miss Bound
[illegible] Slade came — At 2.30
I rode home & got lunch —
back at 3.5. At 5, at Appleton,
got pay for my drawing — and
agreed to get up a Dagueway
article for them. Home to tea
at 6.30 — At 8, at Annie Cook's
rehearsing Romberg's Kinder
Simphonie — I'm a Wachtel.
Home at 11.45 and to bed at
12 —
