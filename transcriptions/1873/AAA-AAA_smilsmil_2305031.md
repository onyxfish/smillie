## Left Page

### April, Monday, 14, 1873.

Cloudy breezy day. Up at about
8.30, after some hours of sleep and
on the whole, feeling better; but
that doesn't amt to much.
My head is still a mussy
mess and the pains in my
chest, altho' abating, is still
no inconsiderable matter.
The day finally passed, between
blowing my nose and small
attempts at reading, a dis[mal]
addition to the fellows that have
preceded it.
Got to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday, 15, 1873.

Charming day, but I have been
in no condition to enjoy it. Up
about 7, still very heavy & mussy
in the head. About the house all
day unable to do anything but
growl. After sucking water
through my nostrils, got to bed
at 11.30.
