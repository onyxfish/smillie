## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 5 1873

Delightful day — Got to studio at 9.20 — After a bad night — I don't feel much better about the head — Tried hard to write but made very little progress. Home to lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.20 and had very little time to do anything, so much interrupted by callers — among others Bellows, Pease & on came in — Home to tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 at N.A. of D. the last night of the Water Color Exh[ibition]. There until 10 o'clk. — Home at 9.30 — Uncle Willie arr'd from Po'keepsie som after — To bed at 11. Uncle Willie arr'd from Po'keepsie —

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 6 1873

Delicious day. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work writing — Elisha Mulford came in some after and the morning was spent revising M.S. — Home to lunch at 1.30 back to studio, after stopping at the bank, at 2.30 — afternoon again very much interrupted, by callers — at 4.30 went down to Appleton's — saw Bunce — got [proof] of M.S. & walked up town — Stopped at Schaus's & [proof] for Water Color sold from nier [illegible]. At studio at 6 — at 6.30 at Sturtevant Ho. and waited until 7.15 for Elisha M. to get his dinner — then with him to 31st & Read [illegible] — had terrible stomach ache — Home at 8.45 tea &c. & to bed at 10.45 Kate arr'd from Po'keepsie —
