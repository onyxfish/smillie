## Left Page

### October Monday, 27 1873

Very severe storm of wind and rain — [illegible] at 8 — then went down town to Sathrok's to see if I could get a man to help me "stick up" my oak — could not, so [illegible] & worked a bit myself until 12 o'clk — then rested until dinner at 1 — again at work after dinner but gave up, tired out at 4 — on bed until 5 — then up and took an alcohol bath and hard rubbing — felt greatly benefitted — at 6, Ben, Fan & Gus came and took me over to Jos Carmack — Fanny Drinker there — Tea, then cards — Started for home a little before 9 — storm had cleared — bright moonlight — got to [illegible]'s at 9.20 — Stayed there all night as A. is away from home & the girls alone — Sat chatting until 11 — Then to bed —

## Right Page

### October Tuesday, 28 1873

Up at 7.30 — [illegible] at 8 — Played about until 9 — then over to studio — finished sticking up oak and then in & to work clearing up & preparing to pack trunk — Dinner at 1.15 — again at work on trunk matters & from 3 until 4 with Robb — rest in studio Re-sticking the Hemlock lumber — gave a finals clearing up, then in and on the bids from 5 until 6, when I got up and went to tea — Storm commenced about 5 o'clk — Snow flurry at 6 — for awhile in at [illegible]'s — at 8 went down to Fan's — there until 10.30 — then home and to bed soon after 11 — [illegible]
