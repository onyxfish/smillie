## Right Page

### January Wednesday, 1 1873

A superb day overhead and better than could be expected
underfoot. Got to studio at 9.20
Spent the mng Reading C. King
"mountaineering among the High Sierras"
trying to write article for [illegible]
america. Home to lunch abt
1.30 — Dressed & at about 3.45
started out — called on the Rodgers,
Mr. H.H. Marks (cards) the Torrey's (there
met Prof Tyndall). then Tiffany's. Black's
(a very pleasant call) Munsee's — Hyde's
(Sneddy's — there met Soris Tiffany, Gifford
& my Jale — had dinner) Miss Ripley — the
Schuyler's (cards) Walter Brown — Leila
Martin — Avery's (Miss Mary Whitney)
Fanny Turnball and at 10.15 my last
call was at the Ireland's — winding
up a very agreeable evening's
entertainment. Home about
11.15 & to bed at midnight.
