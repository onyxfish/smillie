## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 24 1873.

Splendid day. Got to
studio at 9.30 & to work
on Charcoal "Lost" — Abt
until near 2 — then home
to lunch — back at 3 &
worked until 4.30 — then
went to Silleck's — from then
to Greene St looking for
Mr D'Oremieux — did not
find him. Then to Southum
— back to studio at 6 and
home to tea at 6.45 — even
spent in a hurly-burly
of preparing Christmas
tree — Got to bed & some
after 10 —

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 25 1873.

Dull & overcast. B[illegible]: at
9 — then the Christmas tree
was lighted & there was great
hubub — Went to studio at
10 o'clk. Worked on Char-
coal sketch for "Lost".
until 1.45 — then home
to dinner. Mounted little
drawing that father has
given to Matt. Father & Mother
offered, as a Christmas present,
to wipe out my indebtedness
to them. Back to Studio
at 5. Piped fire — Home
at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Uncle
a & Surie came in &
stayed until 9.30 — To bed
at 10.30
