## Left Page

### November Saturday, 8 1873

Heavy, overcast day — Spent most of the mng, fussing & finishing our Yosemite drawing. Then to work arranging designs for [illegible] drawings for Saguenay article. Lunch at 1 — then went down to Appleton's. Saw Bruce — Got $50 — for my drawing — agreed to it for $4.00. Arranged for blocks to complete my Saguenay set of drawings — walked up & stopped in at Astor Library — then to Snedecor's — then to 10th Building & saw Geo. Hall & J. Brown. Stopped for a moment at Brooks's — then at Studio — then home at nearly — Tea soon after. Every Reading Verne & chatting. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 9 1873

Weather still unsettled. Myself went to Dr. Hastings's and heard a most admirable discourse on hard times. A spatter of rain as we came out of church. Went home and stayed until 12:40 — then walked up to Uncle's to dinner. Got Charl & Uncle Wm S. there — After dinner Uncles A. & Mr. & I walked over to 4th ave. R.R. cutting — up to 74th St — about for an hour or so — then back. Home at 5.30 — Tea at 6. Myself, very headachy and on sofa until after 9 — Read some Verne and to bed at 11.15.
