## Left Page

### July Tuesday, 1 1873.

*Cataract Hto. Room No. 16*

Up at 5 oclk. after a poor night —
ashore at 6 & rode to the D[illegible] —
had bkfst & then to NY, Central
Depot, secured seats, telegraphed
ahead for rooms, etc, etc — then
owing to a change in time table
waited until 9 oclk. for the train
to start. Weather overcast & before
10 it began to rain. Very heavy
showers until after 1 oclk, then
began to clear & came off a superb
afternoon. The ride tolerably in-
teresting, but very dirty — Dinner
at 2 oclk, at Syracuse. Very good,
made acquaintance of Mr & Mrs
Howard, of Boston. Got in to the
Cataract Hto. about 9 oclk. Had
tea, spent an hour on piazza
with Mr. & Mrs. H. & to bed at
11.30

## Right Page

### July Wednesday, 2 1873.

Delightful mnt. Up at 6.30 and
bkfst at 8.15. It was after 9.30
when Mr. & Mrs. H., Father & I started
on our rounds. A few drops of rain
fell, but in the main it was sunny
until noon, when it clouded to
break away again about 3. We
did things generally in Prospect
Park & on the islands to our
great enjoyment. Back to hotel
at 2.30 — wrote a letter home —
Dinner at 3.15 — after, met Mr.
W. E. Williams on piazza & had
a long chat. At 5.15 we all started
again & crossed the bridge, went
at the tower, etc, etc, & back
at 8 oclk. Tea at 8.15 — then on
piazza listening to the band —
at 10.30, took last looks with Mrs
H. — then started alone — went to
Prospect Park — then crossed over to Goat
Island and had some coming views
of the falls by moonlight — the effects
very grand and solemn — the woods
dark & full of roaring. Back and
to bed at midnight.
