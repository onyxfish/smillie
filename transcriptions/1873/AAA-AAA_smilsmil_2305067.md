## Left Page

### June Friday, 27 1873.

Up early & with a headache — no
wonder, after my Coney Island dinner
of yesterday. Set to work on drawing
on block of "Yellowstone" for Mr. Richardson
Glorious day & regretting exceedingly
that I could not go sailing in Cook's
yacht on Emma. J's invitation.
Rec'd last night on my Return —
About 11 Uncle A. came with his
carriage & Mary, Willie & I went
up to the Park. About 12.45 when
we ret'd, lunch abt. 30 and I
napped until 2.30 — Then started
again on my drawing & worked
until 6. Tea abt. 6.30 — At 7.30
went with Matt & Kate to Thomas'
garden. Miles Palmer met and
came home with us at 11.30 —
I got to bed a few minutes
before 12.

## Right Page

### June Saturday, 28 1873.

Very warm day. Got to work
at once, after bkft. on Yellowstone
drawing & at it steadily until
after one o'clk. Then lunch.
At 2.15 started for Mr. Richardson's
son, in Crosby, near Bleecker
St. He was pleased with the
drawing & is to send me a
check for $20 on Monday —
On my way up town, Emma J.
got into the car & I went with
her to her glove cleaners. We
had a jolly visit & I got home
between 4 & 5 P.M. At work
getting things to rights generally
until tea time, 6.30. At 8,
went with Matt & Kate over to
Devings by invitation. Father
& Charl joined us afterwards.
We had music and ice cream.
I got home at 11. To bed
a little before 12.
