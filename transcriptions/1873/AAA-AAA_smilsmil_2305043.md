## Left Page

### May, Saturday, 10, 1873

Mild + overcast. Got to studio
ab't. 40 + worked on drawing for
Appleton's. Home to lunch
at 1.45 — back at 2.30 — Found
man with note from Josie
Jessup— At 3.30 Rode up there
+ about 4.30 we got away on
horseback. Had a delightful
ride + back to the house at
6.15— Had a wash + brush
up, then to dinner — every
chatting until 9.45— Then
started for home — where I
am? at 10.30 — Tobed at
12.45
Thunderstorm at 2 a.m.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday, 11, 1873

Mild + overcast, with more or
less rain during the day +
evng. Up late and late to
church — Dr. Hastings's — no
time for his text but heard
a good sermon concerning
conscience as affecting judgment
actions of men to fellow men.
Dinner at 1 o'clk — At 3.15
walked up to A.V.V.'s with
Charl. At 5, with A.V.V., walked
over to Will's — back at 6.30 —
caught in a shower — Tea —
uncle Wm, aunts H. + Alphia, her
angel, Charl + I. making the
guests. C. + I. went to the
Collegiate Ch. After service,
caught in a shower — Stopped
at Dr. Chapin's — home ab't 9.30
+ to bed at 10.30
