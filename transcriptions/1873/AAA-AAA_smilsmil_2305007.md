## Left Page

### February Sunday, 23 1873

A mild headache kept me
in bed until noon — then
up & dressed, but about 2
o'clk. went to bed again
& did not get up until
6 — In my big chair until
1 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### February Monday, 24 1873

A bitter cold day — the coldest of the
season — Got to studio at 9.20 —
Kindled fire & then tried to write
— head very thick — then tried
to charcoal on "Racquette" 18×
30 — about noon, Mrs. Wilder and
three ladies called — Home to lunch
abt. 30 — back at 2.30 — Painted
in foreground on "Tenya Cañon"
20×24 and about 4.30 went
over to N.A. [illegible] — back at 5.30
washed up & home to tea at 6.30
Ever't in big chair — went over
to Diving's — & engaged with his
singing club — back and
to bed at 10.20.
