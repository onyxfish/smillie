## Left Page

### December Monday, 8 1873.

Dark & threatening weather again — Went to J. 2 a [illegible]
about W.C. [illegible] matters &
got to studio at 10.30.
Kindled fire & then found
myself quite unable to
work owing to the uselessness
of my head. Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.45.
Tried to work on page
block but gave it up —
Ferguson came in at about
5 o'clk. — Walked up town
with him at 5.30. Tea at 6.
Swent, with Charl went to
see Jno. E. Owens. Had a
laugh, but it did'nt brush
away the cobwebs. Home
in the Rains at 11 — To bed
at once. —

## Right Page

### December Tuesday, 9 1873.

wet, mizzling, misty day. Got
to studio about 5 30 clk. after
having met J. A. Richards and
walked with him to the [illegible]. [illegible].
Tried to work on my block
but only spoiled whatever I
touched. Home to lunch at
1 — back at 2.30. Did a
little better in the afternoon
in the way of work. At 4.30
went to 4 W.C. [illegible] & called
on Louis Tiffany. Then to
Nicoll's on Water Color business.
On my way home, stopped at the
gallery of J. C. [illegible] in 34 [illegible]. Home
to tea at 6.30. Katt & Charl up to
Uncle A's to dinner — Swent
lying around — wrote a note
to Fredericks & got to bed at
10.45
