## Left Page

### August Wednesday, 6 1873.

Still charming weather — To work
on block for Harley "Yosemite" —
Stuck to it closely all day and
finished that at 5.30 P.M. — Had
lunch at 1 o'clk — Matt left
for Po'keepsie at 2.30 — Tea
at 6.30 — Ruey, I went to
studio — got together a number
of things & at 9.30, carried
them home — Glorious morning
night — To bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### August Thursday, 7 1873.

Still clear & cool — Up soon
after 7 — Help. at 8, and all
the morning gathering and
arranging things preparatory
to starting for Montrose. Lunch
about 1, and som after 2.
Rode down town with Yosemite
block for Harley but found that
he was not in. Then up town
again & went to Snedlei's, where
I got a lot of materials — then
to studio, for one or two last
things & home som after 5 —
Tea at 6 — an hour or two
swinging in hammock —
then until after 10, gathering
things & to bed about 10.45.
Eveg cloudy with a few
drops of rain.
