## Left Page

### May Sunday, 18 1873.

Still delightful weather — my went to Dr Hastings's + heard him preach from Matt 11¼ 11⅝ — Headachy + got asleep. Home to dinner. At 3.30 went up to Wills — at 5, went over to A.V.S.'s + at 6, up to Mr Jessup's — Cor. 78th St. + Madison Ave. Mr + Mrs Mc Devlin there — Stayed to tea + until 10 o'clk — then walked home — To bed at 11.15

## Right Page

### May Monday, 19 1873.

Very warm + delightful. Got to studio with Father at 8.20. He took away Havana drawing, which was Ret'd. in the P.M. Jessett came at 9.30 + I worked from her until 12.15 — Study for a new Bank Note drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — At 2.15 Gee Mary + Willie went to Ride in the Park — Junnie called soon after — Got to studio at 2.30 — ale my lambs there. Besides attending to them, worked on a study of drapery. Miss Barbera Mortheim called — Worked until 7 — home at 7.30 — eve'g news papers + loafing — To bed som. after 10 —
