## Left Page

### March Saturday, 15 1873.

A streaky day. It was 9.45 when
I got to studio. Spent the
mg clearing up studio and
getting in trim for new work.
Home to lunch at 1.15 — back
at 2.30 — Soon after, went over
to the [illegible]'s and made a
short call — then to Appleton's
— back to studio at 5.30 — Home
at 6.30 — Raining — at 7.30
went to St. Cloud Hotel for
Mary Tweedy and took her to
Phil. Concert — Gee did not
go — Uncle W. took Kate —
Did not enjoy the concert
very much. Took M. J. to 43
St. Home at 11, and to bed
soon after — no Rain in
the night.

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 16 1873.

A regular March day, very
high winds. Mrs. went to Dr.
Hastings' & heard him preach a
missionary sermon from Romans
10th ch. 12th–15th v. After service
walked down to studio and back
with uncle Willie. Dinner at
1 o'clk. At 2.30 went for Dr.
Burdick, as Gee got into one
of his mild, nervous streaks — did
not get back until near 4. —
Dr. B. came at 5.30 — Tea at
6 — Evening spent at home — to
bed at 11.
