## Left Page

### April, Thursday, 10, 1873

Charming day — but my cold
no better — I gave up to it &
loafed about until noon, when
I went out to Reservoir Squm
with Willie — out until after
1 — then in to lunch —
P.M. made paste & prepared
to mount my fly away p'ntg
"Moonlight over the High Sierras" —
Tea, bet. 6 & 7. Got to bed,
a little before 11, feeling
very miserable.

## Right Page

### April, Friday, 11, 1873

A perfect Hell-day for me —
Up about 9 a.m., washed &
dressed after a very poor fashion
then unable to take any bkft.
Spent the day in Rocking chair,
unable to move & entertaining
within my one skull, no less
than three well organized
headaches, to say nothing of
the beastly catarrh. I do
not remember ever to have
suffered such an almost-
unbearable combination of pain
that continued without cessation
until about 8 p.m., then gradual
relief came & I spent the night
on the Sofa. About 4 a.m. got
up for variety & cooked a little
bkft.
