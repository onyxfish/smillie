## Left Page

### January, Thursday 16, 1873

Slush storm last night — All day foggy, over cast, mild &
threatening — Got to studio at
9.20 — Part of day, working
on pencil drawing "Crest of
Monument Mt." Then commenced
tracing & transferring subject
for Water Color — Home to
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
and worked until near
5 — Then took a nap. Home
to tea at 6.30 — Old Dr. Stearns
at the house over night — Just
before 9 went to Albert Monroe's
Rec'ptn 35¢ &. There until
9.30 — Then to Clara Dunham
Kirkham's Rec'ptn 7¢.43¢ &—
Very hot & crowded — left
there soon after 10 — Home &
to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 17, 1873

Disagreeable day — but only
threatening — Got to studio at 9.25
worked returning my drawing, but
did it very languidly — feeling very
heavy & stupid — Home to lunch
abt. 30 — walked up with Series J—
who called at the Studio — At 2.30
at Phil. Rehearsal — gave Addie
Dunning Hawes my seat — left
before Rehearsal was over & went
down to Appleton's with Senora
Canon block — Then to Dewey's &
paid him $40 & on acc't. — Stopped at
Gauthier's — at Studio at 6 — Home
to tea at 6.50 — at 8.45 at private
view at Leavitt's — Home at 10.
30 — bed at 11.
