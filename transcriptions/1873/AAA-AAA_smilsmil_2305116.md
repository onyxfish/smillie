## Left Page

### October Friday, 3 1873

Still sulpert weather — After
stable duties, We fo't at 8 o'clk —
Near 10 when I got started
on Opening cut — Worked
until noon, Then wrote a
little letter to Willie Throop &
mailed it abt. Dinner at 1.30 —
Did not get to my work
until 3.30 — at it then until
near 5 — Then at on "Tom" —
in at 7.30 — Tea at 8 — About
8.30 went to Fan M's —
There until 10 — Home — Read
news paper & to bed at 10 [illegible]

## Right Page

### October Saturday, 4 1873

Overcast and threatening — Up late —
but groomed Tom before 8 — Up at 8 —
Got to my work about 10 — Spent
the m'f working over several
blocks, sharpening them up with
the pen. Mr. Joseph Drinkm called
about noon. Dinner at [illegible] — With
carpenters until 2.30 — Then
commenced work again on my
last block "Under Trinity Rock" —
It got to be so dark that I gave
up at about 4.30 — At 5 I went
out on Tom — Came with me an
[illegible] — We had a very good
ride and got in a few minutes
after 6, just as it began to rain.
Tea at 6.30 — Stayed at Mr. Lattin's
until after 8 — Then home and
fussed about until after 9 — but
it was just upon 10 o'clk. when
I got into bed.
