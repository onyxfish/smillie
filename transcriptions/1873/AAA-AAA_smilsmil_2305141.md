## Left Page

### November Saturday, 22 1873

Pleasant day — It was 9.30 when
I got to the studio — finished the
"Saguenay near its mouth" block
and home to lunch abt 1 o'clk.
At 2.30 at D. Appleton's with
my blocks. Got $65.00 —
Walked up. Stopping at
Dewey's — paid him — then
to Ferguson's — then to Brooks
Bros. then to studio at 4.30
Home at 5.30 — Tea about 6
— Suef wandering about the
house and to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### November Sunday, 23 1873

Up at 7. Overcast day. Ed.
Parker arr'd. in time for bkfst —
M[illegible], went to Dr Hastings and
heard him preach from Prov.
22d ch. 21st v. Dinner about
1 o'clk — After dinner, a
nap — About 4 o'clk, commenced
a letter to Sam. Colman.
Coued it see after 4.30 —
Tea at 6 — Suef wrote on
Colman's letter until 9 —
General family chatting
until after 10 o'clk — Rain
— To bed at 11 —
