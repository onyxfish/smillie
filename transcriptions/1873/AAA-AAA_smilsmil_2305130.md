## Left Page

### October Friday, 31 1873.

Dull, overcast. m[illegible] 10 o'clk. before I got away from the house for downtown. At A.B N. Co — Lunched with father at 1 o'clk. 2.45 before I got to Academy of Music to first Phil. Rehearsal of the season — Met all of the old friends found that Will R. had secured for me my old seat last year. Walked home with Nicoll & wife. Back to studio, where, by the way, I had called in the m[illegible], & saw Ferguson — for the first time since his return from South America. — Home to tea at 6 o'clk. — Even[ing]. Passed in the bosom of my family and to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 1 1873.

Beautiful day. Up before 7 — after some cleaning up, got to studio about 10. There all this a[illegible], most of the time looking over Ferguson's sketches & studies; — called on several in the building. Home to lunch abt 30 — Afternoon at home — Sketched papers & sketched some vations for additional Illustrations for St. Lawrence & Saguenay article. Tea at 6 — [illegible] spent as the even[ing] before — getting to bed soon after 10 o'clk.
