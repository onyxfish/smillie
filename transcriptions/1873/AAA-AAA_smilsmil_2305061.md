## Left Page

### June Sunday, 15 1873

Raw & damp & very undecided whether
to rain or clear. Bkfst. about 8 o'clk.
Sat about until 10, then climbed
the hills to the Presbt'n Ch. & counted
one of the congregation of 33 in the
plain little stone church. Subject of
the preacher, 144th Psalm - 15th v.
Praising the cause of Sunday Schools.
After service, walked with Mr Nimans
along the crest of the ridge, through
the woods, then down and upon the
road some distance below the house
making a very pleasant walk. Had
dinner about 2 o'clk — a nap after
it & then wrote to Amb. H — Tea
about 6.30 — chatting on the
piazza — then singing in the
parlor while I walked outside —
nearly 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### June Monday, 16 1873

Awoke very early — cloudless and
very warm. Bkfst. at 7 & took
7.30 boat to the city — Home at
8.30 & to studio at 9.15 — B't hn's
etching on Girochi's die. Home
to lunch at 1.30 — Article in
the Herald of to-day on "Models"
— my name appearing among
the workers from the nude —
wrote a note to the Herald in
reply. Back to studio at 2
& worked on die with graver.
Home to tea at 7 — at 8.15
at Josie Jessup's — they sail
for Europe on Wednesday.
Quite a number of friends
there — left at 10 o'clk —
To bed at 11.45
