## Left Page

### August, Friday, 22, 1873

A close, muggy morning — clear hot afternoon & warm evening.
Spent the mrng. trying to draw on the block "Under Trinity Rock" but worked very badly — Dinner at 1.5 — after dinner, Read for awhile "Hard Cash" — Headache — at last, gave up efforts to work — at 3.30 went down town — Loafed about until 5 o'clk — then walked over lots adjoining fairground hoping to find a building place. Tea at 6.15 — After tea, Josie P. came in, went down town with her & Vic D. Called on Priscilla V — did n't see her — met Hetty S — all wandered about until som after 8 — then to Mother Searles — Looked over Katie's photos. Then Cal, Jack until 10 — Home at 10.30 and to bed at 11 — Passed a very uncomfortable night.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday, 23, 1873

Pleasant day — After bkfst. got to work on block "Montmorenci Falls, from the River" and worked closely all the morning. Dinner soon after and then again at my drawing, but in spite of my best endeavors, I cannot do even up to my average of work. It seems as though I had lost all that I have acquired of skill or experience — I am very much discouraged and the drawing is a poor thing. I don't believe that I will be able to make any thing of the page block "Under Trinity Rock". About 5 o'clk gave [illegible] and out. Tea soon after 6. Went with Well wm & aunt H — went to Entertainment given at Court House for benefit Episcopal Ch. B. F. Duane, a character that tried to be amusing by singing songs, appearing in character etc — etc — After getting home, Read Hard Cash for an hour & to bed at 11.30
