## Left Page

### January Wednesday, 22 1873

Clear, crisp day — got to studio at 9.10 — Kindled fire and set to work on Water Color drawing — after writing & mailing a note to Mr. Harral. Worked until 12 — then Rode down-town, calling first upon Harley the wood engraver — then to A.B.N. Co. abt 8 clk — lunched there — Transacted my business & set out to Return, but owing to detentions on street cars it was 3.30 before I got to studio. Jas. Cole of Stockton, Cal. had called — worked on my drawing until 5 o'clk — Mortimer Smith called about 5.30 — Home at 6.15 Eveg. Reading — got to bed about 9.45.

## Right Page

### January Thursday, 23 1873

Overcast & threatening until about 4 o'clk, when a heavy snow storm set in. Got to studio at 8.45 and until after 10, worked on one of the Italian drawings (Contadina) that I got yesterday. Then to work on my Water Color drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and worked as long as I could see, which was but little after 4 — Dr. Percy called — then Miss Covell — Home at 6 —
Eveg. most of it spent with Kate putting up her book shelves for her — To bed at 10.20
