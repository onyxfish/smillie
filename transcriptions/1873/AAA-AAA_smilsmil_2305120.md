## Left Page

### October Saturday, 11 1873.

Yet another glorious day —
but got up feeling tired and
sore — gave an hour to my linen
had b'kfp — at 8 — All the m[orning]
until noon running about
for painters & carpenters — a
load of oat was left at Sath[illegible]
for me, but I refused it as this
not all sound — Dana Austin
promises to begin painting for
me next Monday — Dinner
at 1 — 3 o'clk. Before I got out
beyond Fair Ground to make
some foreground studies in Water
color. At 4, gave it up & went
home to bed — Up at 6 + went
over to tea — went down to P.O.
at 7.30 — Stopped in at Fan's for
a few minutes — then came
Near 11 when I got to bed —
a little Rain fell about
10.30
Mr. Tower of Pottsville called
in the eveng.

## Right Page

### October Sunday, 12 1873.

Cold, Raw mus[ky], threatening
rain. Up at 7.30 very unwell[ing]
& spent an hour over my horse[s]
B'kfp at 8.30 — At 10, fixed
some medicine for myself
& went to bed. Up again at noon —
after noon — Dinner at 1.30.
The afternoon was "loafed"
away — Kindled a fire in
my room — Tea at 6 —
Eveg, wrote a note to Will
Kimball — Went down the
hill at 8.20 to P.O. with letters —
very dark & slight mizzle —
Went to Presb'n Ch. Found
Aunt H. & Mr. Tower waiting
in carriage — Home before
[illegible] & to bed after Reading
[illegible]'s "John Endicott"
at 11 o'clk.
