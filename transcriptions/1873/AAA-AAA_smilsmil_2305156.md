## Left Page

### December Sunday, 28 1873.

Up very late — Bkfst at 10.30 — didn't go to
church — Dinner at 1.30
— a nap in my chair
& at 4.30 went to walk
in the Park — Enjoyed
immensely late afternoon
& even. effects — Before
6, at Uncle C's — there
to tea — Flora Satterly &
Aunts H. & Sophia also
there — Everf went to
Collegiate Ch. Cor. 48 &
4th Ave. & heard Mr. Hitchcock
preach on the Unity of the
Race from Acts 17 ch. 26 v.
— Of course, a very interesting
discourse. Home at 9.40
To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### December Monday, 29 1873.

Overcast & horrible underfoot. Got to studio at 9.45 &
kindled fire — Verplanck
came in — then Matt and
Kate — went into Rogers' studio
& selected a group — worked
on calf on Tracing paper,
trying to correct the drawing
— Servis of Phila. the middle
one of the three sisters, came
in about 12.30, with Miss Jacobs
& her friend Miss Sackett —
Home to lunch at 1.45 —
back at 2.45 & worked on
calf again until 4.30 —
Nicoll came in — Home
to tea at 6.30 Joe joined
to tea — mounted paper
for calf drawing & to bed
at 11.15 [illegible — ~2 lines]
