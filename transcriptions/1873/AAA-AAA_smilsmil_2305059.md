## Left Page

### June Wednesday, 11 1873

Very hot muggy day. George & mother went up the River to
Mr. Winant's about 10 o'clk. this
a.m. Mother Ret'd in the p.m.
after leaving Geo. comfortably
settled. Chear. is to go up this
evef. Father went up to Po on
the Miller. I wrote to [illegible]
Whitney in the muf. Then, with
pencil worked for a while on [illegible]
of Vt. being eng'd by Girsch. at
11.30 went down town with it.
Back to lunch at 2. All the
remainder of p.m. worked
on Ausable Lake drawing for
the Appletons. ab at the all
Evef. Spent loafing. Tried
very hard to Rain, but could it.
To bed ab 10.40

## Right Page

### June Thursday, 12 1873

Delightful day. Myf, wrote
a short letter to uncle Mrs
then to work on drawing of
lower Ausable. Lunch at
1.30, and again at work.
Finished drawing about 5
o'clk — made paste and
mounted it. Tea at 6.30
at 7.15 went to studio. Got
letter from Colman and
die+[illegible] from Girsch. At
8 went to the Kimball's
Fanny & her Mother out —
until 9 o'clk. Left at
10.20 — Home & to bed
ab 11.15
