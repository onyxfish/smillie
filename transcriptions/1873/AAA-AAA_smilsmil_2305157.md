## Left Page

### December Tuesday, 30 1873

Superb, cold day. Up late again & did not get to studio until 10.30. Set to work tracing calf drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — wrote letter to Miss Whitney & re-dressed myself & back to studio at 3 o'clk. Mrs. Way said Hoyt had just called — worked in tracing until 4 o'clk. Then the Misses Convers called with Mrs. S.S. Coe. Geo. came in while they were there.

At 5 o'clk. walked up to Uncle A's — got there just in time for dinner — at 7.30 took Flora Lathrop to Perkins's concert at Madison Ave. Bap. Ch. Met Matt, Kate & Charl. there — Ch. went home with Flora & I, went to the girls. To bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### December Wednesday, 31 1873

Pleasant winter day. Got to studio late again, as is so usual now. Suffering from confusion in my head worked on tracing of calf drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — afternoon, feeling unfit for work, walked down to Sauthier's called on Ferguson — did shopping — then on Mr. D'Oremieup. [illegible] Greene presenting Gavit's letter of introduction. Back to studio at 5 — Home to tea at 6 — We'll all hands — including Will, & Minn, went over to Düring's & had a New Year Justification. Home about 2 & to bed right away —
