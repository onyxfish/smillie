## Left Page

### July Wednesday, 9 1873.

Up at 5.30. Sky still overcast but giving promise of a fine day. Bkfs at about 7 — a walk on shore & at 9 got away from Ha Ha Bay — a series of exceedingly fine atmospherical effects followed — all of his points were duly showed off & I sketched away for dear life. At & cl— the Sagueny was done & we came to Tadousac. With Misses Reddl & James, walked to the old church & back in 3/4 of an hour. Dinner at 2 as we crossed to Riviere du Loup — a wonderful afternoon — water like oil, reflecting clouds etc. — I at about 7 — Reached Murray Bay soon after. With the ladies walked ashore & marvelled at the moonrise and then the Aurora — a grand night. Ret'd to the boat about 9 & did not stir until 11. Then we made our adieux and I got to bed at about 11.45.

## Right Page

### July Thursday, 10 1873.

*on Str. Quebec —*
*Stateroom 136-*

After a sound sleep, up at 7 and got bkfst on the boat at about 8. — Very tired & Father quite used up. Went over to Montreal Str & secured stateroom — then to Pt. Levis. Exceedingly hot & after a long tramp got only a very rough sketch — back to boat at about 1 & got into our room — Father lay down & I left him. Lunched on crackers at noon, went up to the Terrace, to the Governor's garden & to the Citadel. — Then over to the boys' dales & got littles from home — then to St. Lewis hotel. & at last back to steamer — very tired with headache from extreme heat. It was 4.30 before the str. got away — and had tea. Every showery — [illegible] To bed at 10.30.
