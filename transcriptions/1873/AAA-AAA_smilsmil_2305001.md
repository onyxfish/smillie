## Left Page

### February Friday, 7 1873.

A horrible day — Rain beginning during the forenoon — wind & Rain increasing during p.m. Got to studio at 9.30 — In no humor for work — pottered about all the m[or]n. — Roughly charcoaled "Every m Racquette Lake" — Home to lunch at 1.30 — At 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal. Walked up with Louis J. to N.A. of D — there until near 5 — went over to Y.M.C.A. Reading Room & to studio at 5.45 — Home to tea at 6.30 — Frank Pettit & the D's Uncle at our house overnight — At 8. at Emma Ireland's — Singing club — Home at 10.45 & to bed shortly after 11.

## Right Page

### February Saturday, 8 1873.

Delightful day. Got to the studio at 9.45. Have not got rested yet, so it was after 11 before at last I started painting on "Moonrise over the High Sierras". Worked until 1.30 — then home to lunch — back at 2.30 & worked all the p.m. Angero very much frightened by Manara's threats to sue him for libel. Louis J. & A. Anderson came in at 6 — very jubilant over sales at N.A. of D. At 7 left Academy ticket at Emma D's then to Academy — saw Cox — then to Fanny K's — left ticket — home at 7.30 — tea at 8. Eve[ning] loafing and to bed at 10.30
