## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday, 27 1873.

Another glorious day, but very warm. It went as may days before I have gone. Blkps. at 8, after grooming my horse. Then some time with the Carpenters — to work on "Quebec" at 10 — Dinner at 1 — Carpenters, taking more or less time during the p.m. Worked on drawing until 4.30 — then out on Tom and had a first rate ride — in at 6.30 — after a superb twilight on Butterfield's Hill — found Uncle Willie & Auntie just arr. Tea at 7 o'ck, sat with new arrivals until 9.15 then to bed at 9.30 —
Strange did not work for me today —

## Right Page

### Sept. Sunday, 28 1873.

Thick foggy mt. — did not lift until 10 o'clk — then came off clear & very hot, felt headachy so, spent most of the mt. on my bed — after 1 o'clk at 9.30 — Dinner at 3 — at 4.30 went off on Tom to corners beyond "Lathrop's Lakes" — back at 7.30 — the moonlight, delicious — Sea at about 8 — Ev'g with the folks until near 10 — then to bed —
