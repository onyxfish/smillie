## Left Page

### May, Tuesday, 6, 1873

Another delightful day. Got to studio at 8.45. Spent some time getting A.V. V's drawing ready to send home — then to work on A.B.N. Co. drawing. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked until 4 o'clk, then went to Church Music Ass'n Rehearsals, at Steinway Hall and Walpurgis's Wacht. Back to stud. at 5.30 & worked as long as I could see — Home at 7.30 & back at 7.45. Spring meeting of Water Color Soct. — no especial interest — home at 10.15. To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday, 7, 1873

Pleasant day, but threatening a change. Got to studio at 9.10 & to work on A.B.N. Co. drawing. At 11 Jessie came & I worked from her until 12.30, improving my drawing very much. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked faithfully as long as I could see, when I got drawing finished after a fashion. This hard woodeny, but afterall, is to me encouraging. Home to tea at 7 — Matt arr'd from P. this a.m. At 8.30 went with a note to Miss Watson in 45th St. Saw herself & had a pleasant little call — back at 9. Uncle A, Jucie & Aunt H. at the house also, Miles Palmer — visited until 10 — then mounted my drawing & to bed at 11.
