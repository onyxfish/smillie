## Left Page

### November Friday, 14 1873

Up early — another cold and gusty day. Father went to
Phila at $8.40 to see Mr. Main.
I spent the mf. drawing
on paper sketches for some
of my Saguenay illustrations
& preparing some new blocks.
Lunch at 12.30 at 2.30
at Phil. Rehearsal — after
that went down to Davey's
the Sailors. Then walked
home — tea at 6 o'clk — [illegible]
went up to Burgraff's — [illegible]
at home cleaning up closet —
Bed at 11.15 —
Father Ret'd at 11.30 P.m.
with sad news from Mr. M.
who seems thoroughly broken
down & his mind much
affected —

## Right Page

### November Saturday, 15 1873

Up at 7 — cold day. At work
on sketches, tracings and
outlines on blocks. Lunch
at 1 o'clk. P.M. drew on
the block. "Lighthouses
among the Thousand Islands
& St. Lawrence." — Evening
went to 1st Phil. concert
of the season. Headache
& sore throat interfered
very seriously with my
enjoyment. Walked home
with Kate and got to bed
about 11 o'clk.
