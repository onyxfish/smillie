## Left Page

### April, Friday, 4, 1873.

In bed all day with an outrageous headache —
missed Phil. Rehearsal —
Dinner at [illegible] J's and
the evng in the Every.
Got up & took my tea at
10 o'clk P.M. Got to sleep
about 4 a.m.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday, 5, 1873.

Got to Studio at 8.50 — feel groggy —
before I got my fire lighted, John
Townsend came in. He agrees
to pay $275.00 for my drawing "Horse
Racing in the Yosemite" — I asked
$375.00 for it. Uncle a, & Tunis
also called — and a Mr. Walker,
who told me where to find my
painting that blew out of my window
last Tues. Home to lunch at 1.30
— back at 2.30 — Many callers —
among them, a small boy with
my painting — gave him $2.00
— worked all the P.M. — on W.C.
sketch to give Mr. Walker.
Mr. Edw. Anthony, Nemiko & Meta
Adams, among others, called — I
worked as long as I could see &
at 6.45 left note for [illegible] J—
at her door — Home & tea at 8 —
Everg, loafing about. To bed
at 10.15    Quite a heavy thunder-
storm during the eveng.
