## Left Page

### November Sunday, 16, 1873

In bed all day with a miserable headache &
sore throat. Some Rain in the morning — clear
afternoon — Got up about 6 o'clk & took tea —
music, chair & m s fa until 4.30 A.M. — then
to bed.

## Right Page

### November Monday, 17, 1873

Dull, mizzling day — Did not get up until 9 o'clk and it
was 11 before I got to the studio. Until 1.30 — clearing up —
then home to lunch —
back at 2.45. Hart [illegible]
Dr. Greave & others called —
so that I did not make
much progress — Home
to tea at 7. Very sore throat
— devoted myself to it and
to bed at 10.30 — Horrible
storm of sleet, rain & snow —
