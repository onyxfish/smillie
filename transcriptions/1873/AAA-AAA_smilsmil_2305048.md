## Left Page

### May, Tuesday, 20, 1873

Still charming weather. Got to studio at 9.30 & worked all the mrng on sketch for drawing for A.B.N. Co. Mr. Knudsen called at 1 o'clk. Bunce sent up adirondack camp drawing. Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45 —
Belle Sweedy called and after a pleasant visit, me walked down to 14th St. I went on to Appleton's & settled Bunce's dissatisfaction by agreeing to take half price for camp scene & make another drawing, he selected view on lower Ausable Lake. Back to studio at 5.45, after stopping at Snedecor's & Lauthier's — worked on charcoal sketch for Bunce, until 7 — Then Miss Woodward invited me into her studio to tea with Miss Whitney — at 8 called on Emma Ireland — Home at 10. + to bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday, 21, 1873

Overcast. Rain after 3 o'clk P.M. Got to studio at 9.20 & spent most of the mrng on charcoal sketch, view of lower Ausable Lake. Then at work on old "Havana" drawing — Had model from noon. Home to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45 & found Miss Bond waiting. I worked on Havana drawing as long as I could see — Home to tea at 7.30 — Evening squaring up Water Color books — to bed at 10.45
