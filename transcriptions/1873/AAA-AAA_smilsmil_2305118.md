## Left Page

### October Tuesday, 7 1873

Up at 7 + found 2 in. of snow over everything — wintery in feeling and appearance — Storm continued all the o[ut]f. Soon after went down to Blacksmith's with Patch. Then to hardware + lumberman — back for Patch + rode him up home carrying 2 bushels oats. In at 11.30 — Worked on designs for mouldings for studio until 1 — Then dinner — P.m. again at it until 4 — Then as it had cleared charmingly overhead, but still windy and cold — I went out on the colt — went better than yesterday. In at 5.40 — Tea at 6.30 — Eve at Mrs. Lathrop's until 9 — Then home + to bed at 10. [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### October Wednesday, 8 1873

Superb day. Up before 7 — Stable duties + help — at 8.30 — After that went down town + among other things, had my hair cut — After "bossing" the winter covering for my big window, got to work on "Under Trinity Rock" at about 11.30 — Dinner at 1 — managed to get in about an hours work in the afternoon, but much of my time was taken with Austen — as I expect to morrow will be his last day with me — At 5 o'clk went out on "Patch" with Carrie + Fletcher Warner's daughter Jo — with fan M — I got in at 6.15 — Tea at 6.45 — Glorious moonlight night — at 8.30 went down to Foley's — met there Mr. Jeuckes — Home at 10.20 + right to bed.
