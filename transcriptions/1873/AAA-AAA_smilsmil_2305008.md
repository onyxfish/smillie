## Left Page

### February Thursday, 27, 1873

Another of the horrible days for which this winter must be famous. Sleety snow all day long. Up late & got to studio at 10 — most of the mng spent upon a painting by Miss Woodworth — a lesson to her. At 2 made my lunch upon tea & graham crackers — and then wrote from 3 until 5.30. Home to tea at 6.30. Did some reading and mending and to bed at 10.45 —

## Right Page

### February Friday, 28, 1873

Cold and raw — walked down to Appleton's — met Belle & Mary J. at Schram's — took Grand St. cars to home — South. & mng bg. Then to Appleton's printing establishment on Kent Ave. & [illegible] St. There saw first page of Yosemite article in the press — back to A's & saw Bruce — then home to lunch and at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal. Did n't enjoy it — after, went to N.A. of D. then to studio — home to tea at 6.30 & at Annie Cooke's at 8. Singing until 10 — walked up with Fannie & Will K. Miss Howe & Emma J. — Home & to bed at 12 o'clk.
