## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 14, 1873

Very mild day — Horrible underfoot — Got to studio at 9.30 —
Worked closely all day on page block "Senaya Canon
from Glacier Point" — Miss Redfield & brother called in the mof. — Home to lunch at 2
o'clk — back at 2.30 worked until 5, Then went over to Y.M.C.A. & after considerable Trouble succeeded in getting new gymnasium closet —
Home to tea at 6.30 — Even Reading newspapers and wrote note to Mr. Townley.
To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 15, 1873

Cold, Raw and overcast — Got to studio at 9.20 & to work on block "Senaya Canon" — and got it finished shortly after 1 o'clk — Then home to lunch — back at 2.30 — Commenced Tracing Charcoal Sketch for Large Water Color drawing. A little before 5 Nicoll called on water color matters & was detained until 6 o'clk. On my way home stopped at Coleman H.Q. & saw Richards.
Home to tea at 6.45 — Even. Reading newspapers and writing little notes etc —
To bed at 10.30
