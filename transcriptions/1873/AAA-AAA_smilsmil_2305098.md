## Left Page

### August Thursday, 28 1873

Partially overcast, but on the whole, very pleasant day.
After bkfst made to Mary and to Ferguson. Then worked
until dinner time and sketch "Old Church at Jadonsac."
After dinner had a miserable headache so that I could not
work — gave up & spent most of the afternoon at Lathrop's.
at 5.30 gave up, went to bed &
slept for an hour, then got a feeling better. Dr & Mrs. Jaye[?]
came over to bid us goodbye.
Mrs. Bard & Clark called.
I got to bed about 11.
Rec'd letter from Kate enclosing a long one from Mary
to Matt. Also a paper from Martha's Vineyard.

## Right Page

### August Friday, 29 1873

Pleasant m'g. Immediately after bkfst Jas. Carmalt came & asked
me to go with him to Lakeside, to get Tom Treadles, the horse that he is
to let me have. I went with him
starting soon after 10 — we got to
widow Carmalt's at abt 12.30. Dinner
about 2. Then looked over the "Turner Gallery" & chatted until
after 4 — then with C. out over
the farm — caught the horse &
got started back at 5.30. Sky
overcast & threatening rain — before
we got home (8.30) had a slight shower. Quite dark, but C. ran
his horse over the road, regardless.
Mrs. Lathrop prepared tea for me
at 9 o'clk. Met Genl. Crane and
daughter. Mrs. Genl. C. Retired
without honoring me. Got to
bed at 10.30
