## Left Page

### March, Thursday 13, 1873

Still charming weather — got
to studio at 9.20 and fell to
writing. Home to lunch at 1.30
& back at 2.45 — For some time
at work on Water Color business
& at last, again "Ulpos emitti" —
and at last, about five o'clk.
the nightmare ended. The
work is done, I can only hope
that it will not prove actually
discreditable. Home to tea at
6.30 — at 7.30 at Elisha Mulford's
& he looked over the last pages
of M.M.S., at 9, at N.A. [illegible] —
Private View of Tenselt's pictures.
Looked about until 10. Home
at 10.30 & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 14, 1873

Delightful day. After getting my
last batch of M.S. ready, went
down with it to the Appletons.
Bunce tells me that it is too long
and must be "cut" — Then went to
A.B.N.Co — must make drawings
for them — Home to lunch at 1.30
and at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal
— then to studio & wrote letters
until 6, then to Minna Ireland's
— dined there. Took Minna and
Miss Miller to Fanny Kimball's,
stopping for Katie Greatoreip —
At the singing, Russell Bellows
took part. It was after 11 when
we got away and 12 when I got
home and 12.30 when I got
to bed.
