## Left Page

### November Wednesday, 12 1873

Dull & overcast with occasional Rain — After o'clk — at 7.30 got to work on page sketch "Falls of Montmorenci from the River" — I traced it & transferred it to the block — then went on with the drawing — Lunch Sam at 1 o'clk. Then as long as I could see, worked on the block — gave up at 5 o'clk & walked down to 18th St. — back to studio — Home to tea at 6.15 — Every one on Water Color business — To bed at 10.45 —

Emma Bourns arr'd. from Po'keepsie before o'clk — this mng, & left about 10 a.m —

## Right Page

### November Thursday, 13 1873

Cold day — gusty & with snow squalls — Got up headachy & after o'clk, got to work on "Falls blocks," "Falls A.M. from the River" but nothing went right — was too clouded & the whole thing looked so distressed that at 12 o'clk I gave up & spread myself out in my big chair. Part of the time I was able to be in the cellar, to watch progress of getting in new furnace — Tied on the Swerf on sofa and to bed about 10 o'clk —
