## Left Page

### March, Thursday 27, 1873

Bright & breezy — got to studio at 9.20 and commenced painting on "Evening on Racquette" (2nd day) Pitched into it like a good fellow until 1 o'clk — then prepared lunch — at 2 — Miss B— & [illegible] Slade came — I attended to them & painted too — At 6 o'clk got the canvas covered — tolerably successful, I think. Washed & cleaned up & home a little before 7 — At 8 o'clk — at Annie C's — 2° Rehearsal of Tinder Symp. Home abt 11.15 & to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### March, Friday 28, 1873

Delightful day — Got to studio at 9.20 & to work painting on "Moonrise over the Sierras" (2nd day) Between 11 & 12 o'clk. Elisha Mulford came & took me over to Carpenter's Studio to see portraits of his father & mother — on my return, found Meta Adams waiting in Angora's studio. At 1.45 went home to lunch & got down to Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30. out at 4.30 — went to Snedecor's — Back to Studio at 5.15 & worked as long as I could see — At 6.30 went to Kut[illegible]imball's, to tea — at 8. at Katie Greatoney's — our singing night — went home with Emma S— Stopped at Studio & home abt 11.15 — To bed abt 11.30.
