## Left Page

### May Thursday, 22 1873.

Rain nearly all day long —
got to studio at 9.15 & to work
on A.B.N.Co's drawing (upright)
only outlining for India ink work.
Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.30 — See my pupils
in the afternoon while I
worked on my drawing.
Home to tea at 7.30 —
Svetf. loafing & to bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### May Friday, 23 1873.

Very warm, muggy and dubious.
Got to studio at 8.45 & to work
on A.B.N.Co. drawing — About
10.30, Jessie Hayland came
in, Then Uncle Wm S. for a
moment. Jessie sat for me
until 12 — Home to lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Miss
Sterling came & worked
most of the P.M. — Augusto
also visited with me — I
worked on drawing until
6 — at 6.20 at [illegible] D's
to dinner — Svetf. Singing
club — Escort to Fanny K. —
got home at 11.30 — to
bed at 11.45
