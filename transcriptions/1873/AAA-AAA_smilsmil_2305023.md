## Left Page

### March Saturday, 29 1873.

A most horrible day of rain & hurricane. Got up with a headache & did not leave the house until 10.15 — then went to N.A. & D — then to Y.M.C.A. — called on Wm. Hart, S. W. Wood — R. S. Gifford and Louis Tiffany. — Home at nit- 1 o'clk — & gave up to my headache — the rest of the day in my big chair and all night on the sofa —

## Right Page

### March Sunday, 30 1873.

A bright breezy day. morning went to Dr. Hastings's and heard him preach from 1st Cor. 11 ch — 15th v. Home at noon — after dinner, wrote to Maud D[illegible] Did not go out during the afternoon — Tea at 6.30 — Evening wrote a long letter to Saml. Coleman — To bed at 11 o'clk.
