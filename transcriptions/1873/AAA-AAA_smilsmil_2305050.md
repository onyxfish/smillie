## Left Page

### May, Saturday, 24, 1873.

Muggy mrg, clearing off very warm — Wrote a note to
Mr. Huredson explaining that I cannot go to South Norwalk
next Tues. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on A.B.N. Co's
drawing — about 11.30 Dr. Percy called & brought a young woman
with him — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.40. Worked
all the p.m. on drawing making slow & timid progress. Glorious
cloud effects in the evening.
Waited until 7.30 to see the
last of them. Home at 7.45. Tea
at 8.30 — Anniversary of Father
& Mother's wedding — Ice-cream.
Matt arr'd from Po, this a.m.
Got to bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday, 25, 1873.

Delightful day, altho' very warm.
W. P. arr'd early in the morning.
Between 9 & 10 a.m., took Willie
out to the square for an airing.
Went to Dr. Hastings's & heard
him preach from 1st Peter,
V ch. 6th v. — Home to dinner.
At 3.30 — went up to Will's —
went over to Uncle A's &
had tea — Home at 9.30
& to bed at 10.30
