## Left Page

### Sept. Friday, 19 1873

Overcast mng & threatening Rain.
Bkfst. about 8 — Spent all of the
mng with the Carpenters getting
up the Rafters for my Studio
Roof. The weather changed —
the sky cleared & the sun
came out very hot — Had dinner
very early — at 2 o'clk — on acct
of the Crayons going away —
In the afternoon again at work
until after 4 — when a violent
storm of Rain drove us off
before all of the Rafters were up.
— Did not ride — Tea at [illegible]
over, playing cards with
Mrs. Post & the Blossoms
until 10, 15 — Home & to bed
at 10, 30. Storm then very [illegible]

## Right Page

### Sept. Saturday, 20 1873

Cold, cloudy & windy — All day
again spent on my building
fussing with the big windows,
which I now believe to be too
big, and the Roof Rafters —
Dinner about 20, then worked
until after 4, when we got
the Rafters all up — Austin
not at work — About 5 o'clk
got away on "Tom" & although
I was very tired, had a good
ride — In at 6, & soon after
had tea — Read news paper
until 8 — then down to Fau-
m's — Met there Julia Read,
Metty S., Mr. Dr. (Mrs. [illegible]) and
the family. The old lady is
failing rapidly — Home a little
before 10 — To bed at 10, 30
