## Left Page

### March, Monday, 17, 1873

Clear, windy & dirty. Got to studio ab't 9.15. It indeed fine — put studio in order — worked awhile on charcoal "Racquette Lake" & ab't 2 went to Sielford's for gel, then to his studio. While there Bales & Wang S. & two, Ch. Benedick came in. Home to lunch at 1.15 — back to Gel's studio at 2.15 & marched his pupils over to my room, where they took their first lesson under my charge; I am to take them until Gel feels physically better. I did not get home until 6.30 — felt tired & headachy. So, spent the even'g reading. To bed at 10.45. Uncle Willie went home at 10.30 a.m.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday, 18, 1873

Still breezy & March-like, but not so clear. Felt tired & sick & did not get to studio until 10 o'clk. Spent the m'g working over some sketches & ab't 2 went down to Palette after stopping at Knoedler's. On my way back, stopped at Alman's Photographer. Home to lunch at 1.15. back at 2.30 — set palette & painted on Tonis Tiffany's 10×18. Shurtleff came in at 4 o'clk — & t 5, at Appleton's — agreed to engrave a Josaniti plate for $400 & on way back, stopped at Gauthier's & at Chavitt's & saw the Beaumont collection of pictures, met the Kimball's there. After stopping at Studio, home to tea at 6.45 — Even'g, reading "Atlantic" to bed at 10.30.
