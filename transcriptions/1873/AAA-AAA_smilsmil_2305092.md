## Left Page

### August, Saturday, 16, 1873

Rainy weather not yet over — all day, overcast with occasional light showers. Bkft. at 7.45. Spent the mf varnishing & fixing up drawing boards — Dinner at 1.30. Afternoon, calculating amount of lumber required (with Mr. Catrill) — Reading & walking until tea time at 6 o'clk — After that, slight attempt at charades at Bud. Lathrop's — At 8.30 went to Sen. Searle's. Met McCartney (Kate's husband.) Josie Beatty and the Rush family — There until 10.30 then home & to bed at 11 o'clk. Glorious, cloudless night.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday, 17, 1873

Superb morning and overcast p.m. went to mf service at Baptist Ch. with Aunt & Uncle Wm. Heard a very good sermon from Rev. Mr. Cheshire on the Regality of Christ — "and on his head there were many crowns." After service went with uncle Wm. to his mother's. At 1.30 took a light dinner at Mr. Lathrop's — then wrote a letter home — At 4, went down to Leonard Searle's, by invitation, & eat another very light dinner. At the time, suffering with a headache. Joe Williams also there — Walked from 6 until after 7 with Josie B. Hetty S. & Vic Dimock — Mrs left Hetty at Episcopal Ch. & ret'd home & wrote a letter to George — Between 9 & 10 o'clk, posted my letters — Ret'd with increased headache and to bed about 10.30
