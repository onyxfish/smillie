## Left Page

### May Friday, 2 1873.

An atrocious day — Rain and wind — got to studio at 9.45 &
spent some time clearing things up — Med. Augera's girls came
in & waited for him until near 12 o'clk — On my way home to
lunch, stopped at B. & Percy's. Home at 2 — back to studio at
2.45 — Misses Sterling, Bond &
Jas. Shade there. At 6.30
went to Fanny K's to [illegible] —
Every, Singing club —
Home at 10.30 & to bed at
11.15 — Still Raining —

## Right Page

### May Saturday, 3 1873.

Another horrible Rainy day —
got to studio at 9.15 & to work
on study for A.B.N. Co vigt. It
was 10.30 when Jessie came &
under Augera's direction she
posed & a corrected my study,
then drapery was arranged
upon her & work progressed
until near 1 o'clk — In trying
to put A.N.V.'s drawing into the
frame, I broke the glass. Home
to lunch at 2 — back at 2.45
Commenced drawing and got
it outlined — Home to tea at
7 o'clk — Every, Ditring
came over & we had music —
to bed at 11.
