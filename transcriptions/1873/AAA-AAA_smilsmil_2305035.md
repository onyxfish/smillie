## Left Page

### April, Thursday 24, 1873

Lovely day — got to studio at 9.20
& to work on A.B.N. Co's drawing —
Finished it at 1 o'clk. Miss
Sterling then came to say
that she could take her
lesson, but she did — Miss
Bond came at 2, & then I
prepared my lunch — Then
I painted a sketch — "on
the Saco at North Conway", while
Miss Bond watched me — I
painted steadily & finished
it at 6 — Then to N.A.A.D. to
see about Word's drawings — then
to Silleck's — back to studio &
until 7.30 cleaning & washing
up things — Then to Kimball's &
home at 8 — Tea at 8.30 —
newspapers & to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### April, Friday 25, 1873

Pleasant day — Got to the studio
at 9.20 & spent the mng. Re-
painting a picture that I
gave to Augero some years
ago. About 1 o'clk. Mr. [illegible]
& Mr. Moran came in and
visited until after 2 o'clk.
This Miss Bond came to
paint & at 2.30 I went
home to lunch & Returned
at 3.15 — Spent the Rest of
the p.m. working on "Moon
Rise over the High Sierras" —
at 6.30 went to Kimball's
& begged my tea — Every
singing club at Emma
J's — Oscar J. there — Every
body more or less under the
weather — Home abt 11.15 &
right to bed —
