## Left Page

### January, Sunday 26, 1873

Delicious mg. for winter —
cloudia p.m — Mg. went
to Dr Hastings's & heard him
preach from the well worn
text Prov. 22° ch. 6° v. "Train
up a child" etc — It was
an excellent discourse —
After service walked home
with Mr & Mrs. McHarg — Home
to dinner — Afternoon at
4.30 went to studio & fixed
fire for to-morrow mg. Home
at 6 — Surely did not go
out, but after some writing
got to bed at a little
before 11 —

## Right Page

### January, Monday 27, 1873

miserable snow storm all day
long. Got to studio at 9.30 —
Kindled fire and tried to
work on Water Color — Abit
until about 1 o'clk — Then
went over to Y.M.C.A. with
some notes for W.C. members.
Back & prepared lunch
at 1.30 — again to work, but
had to give up som after
4 o'clk — It was so dark —
washing & cleaning up and
home at 6.30 — At 8 went to
Mr Staines's No. 6 E. 43° — to get
him to lend a drawing to
W.C. 46th — pleasant call —
Home at 9 — & worked on W.C
accts until after 10 — Matt
& Charl. arr°. from Po'keepsie
about 7.30 P.M — To bed at 11.
