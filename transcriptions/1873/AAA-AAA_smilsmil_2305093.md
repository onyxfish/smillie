## Left Page

### August Monday, 18 1873.

Heavy Rain and cold — In
bed all day with a miserable
headache. Got up about 4.30.
went over to Mr. Loutrop's to
tea at 6 o'clk — having already
had a cup of tea and piece
of bread + butter at Dud H's —
Spent the evey until 9 o'clk
at Mr. Dud's — then until 11 o'clk
with Uncle Wm — Read "Hard
Cash" until after 12 + finally
got settled in bed at abt 1.30

## Right Page

### August Tuesday, 19 1873.

Dull, overcast day — afternoon trying
to clear and warmer. Morning,
worked between two + three hours
trying to improve upon some of my
Canadian Sketches — Walked from
12.15 until after 1 — then dinner —
After dinner, two or three games of
croquet — Rain away with an
hour or more — when I got at my
work again, could not do anything
to please one — about 5 o'clk feeling
exceedingly heavy and sleepy, I
lay down for half an hour —
Tea at 6 — Some croquet again
for awhile on A. Loutrop's
piazza — Home at 8 — then went
to Mr. Coolper's — Mr. C. unwell,
Fanny Payson out — Then to Mr.
Judge Jessup's — there until 9 o'clk
— then to the Biddle's — there
until 10 — then home + with
Uncle Wm until near 11. then
to bed + didn't get asleep until
after midnight —
