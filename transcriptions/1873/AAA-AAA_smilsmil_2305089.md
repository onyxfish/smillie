## Left Page

### August, Sunday, 10, 1873

Up at 6.30 & had a regular shampooing & scrubbing to get
rid of some of the dirt from yesterday's ride. Bkft about &
sat on piazza until church
time — Ther. 68°. Then to Presbn
Ch. & heard Rv. Mr. Taylor of Newark.
Had dinner about 1 o'clk — the
afternoon spent on piazza and
napping — Tea about 6.30 — Every
went to Episcopalian Ch. and
heard the minister from Great
Bend — then to Mrs. Dud. Lathm
& made arrangements to get my
meals there — Looked over
Constitutional Convention photos
with Uncle Wm & to bed at
11 —

## Right Page

### August, Monday, 11, 1873

Very cool & pleasant — took bkft at
Mr. Dud. Lathyp's at 7.30 — then for
some time with Rev. Mr. Taylor & other
of the boarders. About 10 o'clk — went
down town to the Cabinet makers, and
ordered drawing boards, etc — Sat
about — on Cartmalt's stoop chatting
with different ones until 11.30,
feeling lazy, stupid & dyspeptic.
Then up town & took a nap before
dinner at 1 — after dinner, croquet
until 3 o'clk — then to my room — tried
to do something — scrubbed some of
my old clothes — then gave up
and lay down on the bed & napped
again — a little after 5.30 went out
to side with Carrie — caught in a
sprinkle of rain — back to Mrs.'s
to tea at 6.15 — Well, saw Cottrell,
the carpenter in Mulford's store —
at 8.30 went to Mr. Riley's. Saw Ike
Julia & other members of the family —
there until 9.30 — then home & to bed
at 10.15
