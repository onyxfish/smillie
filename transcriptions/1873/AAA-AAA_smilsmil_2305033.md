## Left Page

### April, Sunday, 20, 1873

Charming day. Morning walked up to St. Thomas's with Belle & Mary J. — left them then & walked on to the park — at 11.40 went in to Uncle a's — Home at 12.30. Dinner soon after — full table — Ed. P. arr'd this mg. Afternoon chatting & Reading. At 5.30 went up to Central Park with Belle — back at 6.30 — Tea. Evey, Mary J. went to church with "the folks" — I stayed at home. Got to bed about 11.15

## Right Page

### April, Monday, 21, 1873

More miserable weather. Snow during the mg — then mizzle. Cleared during the p.m. Got to studio at 9.30. Kindled fire. Mr. McArthur & daughter, of Montreal called — got sub-letter work done on [illegible] N. Co's drawing for German Belgian Rio Peatte Bank. Home to lunch at 1.15. McTwedy gone — Matt & Ed. went up the River at night. Back to studio at 2.30. Miss Bond there — alone. Gave her a lesson. Mr & Mrs. Hawley & Miss Woodward called. Worked on drawing until 6.15 — Home at 7 — Eve'g. Read newspaper and to bed at 11.
