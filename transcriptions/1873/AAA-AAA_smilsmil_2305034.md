## Left Page

### April, Tuesday, 22, 1873.

Another horrible day of raw, cold rain. Got to studio at 9 o'clk — + worked closely all the m'fg on A.B.N. Co. drawing. Burling called at about [illegible]. At 1 o'clk I was obliged to give up to a headache + without lunch, I spent the rest of the p.m. on the lounge. Rec'd check from J.P. Townsend for my W.C. drawing "Horse Racing in the Yosemite". $275.00. Home to tea at 6.20 — Evey, pasting newspaper scraps in book — To bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday, 23, 1873.

A streaked sort of a day, but on the whole, pleasant. Got to the studio at 9 o'clk — + set to work on Bank note drawing + stuck to it faithfully — Frank Reed + two friends came in about 11 o'clk. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45 + worked until 5.45 — Then went to N.A. of D. to see about G.B. Woods' drawings — then to Snieder's + paid bill — back to studio + home at 7.20 — Evey, arranging slips in scrap book — To bed at 10.45
