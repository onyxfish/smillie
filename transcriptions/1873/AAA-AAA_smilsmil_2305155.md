## Left Page

### December Friday, 26 1873

Horrible storm all day & Rain
& now — slush. Up at 9
with a headache — 11 bef m
I got to studio — Worked all
day on a little W.C. sketch
5×6½ that [illegible] had out-
lined. Had hard work
to fight through my head-
ache — Made a cup of
tea at abt 1.30 & worked
until nearly 4.30 — then
spread myself in the
lounge. Rec'd a letter
from Harry & Emma. Louis
Tiffany came in about
5\. Home to tea at 6.30.
Eveng. mounted drawings
& Read news paper —
To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### December Saturday, 27 1873

Still very disagreeable weather.
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on "page block", views in Montreal
At 12 o'clk. went over to Y.M.
C.A. Bldg & called on Tiffany
Gifford, Brischer, Wyant and
Thompson. Home to lunch
at 1.45 — back at 3 — at 5.30
went down to Sauthiers —
miserable Rain — Home
in driving storm at 7 o'clk.
Eveng. pasting mounting
boards for my big drawing.
To bed at 12.30.
