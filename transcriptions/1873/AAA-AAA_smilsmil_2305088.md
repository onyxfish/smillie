## Left Page

### August Friday, 8 1873

Overcast mng, with a shower and thunder about 9 o'clc —
Cleared soon after — charming day — Spent all the morning
clearing out + packing up
the Truck in my Table Drawer
so that it may be sent to Ha[rris]
for repairs — Lunch at 1 o'cl
Afternoon, packed my trun[k]
— finished at 4 o'clk — at
5 went to Westcatts — bought
R.R. tickets, etc — Home at
6 — Tea at about 7 — Every
loafing about — looking over
English papers — etc —
a glorious moonlight night —
To bed at 10.45 —

## Right Page

### August Saturday, 9 1873

*Montrose. Pa.*

Up at 5.30 — glorious mng, Rlcp: at 6.30
Good byes Jeff at 7.15 — Crossed at the
Christopher's ferry — had to wait — off
at 8.30 — air delightful but much dust
& cinders — no particular interest in
passengers — got to Scranton at
[illegible] — had a cup of tea — Montrose
depot at 3.30 — Started in Stage
a little before 4 + reached Uncle
Wm's about 5.45 — found all well
+ was amiably Rec'd — Tea about
6.30 — Every Mr. Clark and
daughter + Mr. Bard called —
Got to bed at 10.30 —
The night really cold —
