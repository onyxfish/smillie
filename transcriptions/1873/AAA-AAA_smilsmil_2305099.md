## Left Page

### August Saturday, 30 1873.

Delicious day, but rather warm.
After 1 o'clk I went down town to see
about the horse that Carmalt
has for me — Then back & to work
on block of "Old Church Tadms[?]"
Elisha Mulford called at noon —
Dinner at 1.15 o'clk — Between
2 & 3 Carmalt came with horse
& I spent some time riding
him about. He is an immense
shackle of bones and is absurdly
awkward. Took him into the
stable & tried to set to work when
Isaac Riley & then Sam Jessup
called — near 6 when they left —
After tea went down town for
some oats — Fixed my horse up
for the night, then went down
to the Searles'. Vic Dimock
there — Cal, Jack — Home &
to bed at 11 o'clk —

## Right Page

### August Sunday, 31 1873.

Overcast morning. Before service, wrote
a letter to Maud Dixon — Went to
the Presbt. Ch. and heard Mr. Munger
preach from Mark 9th Ch. 2d v. concerning
personal recognitions in Heaven. After
service it rained very hard — walked
home with Joe Williams — Dinner
at 1.30 — Afternoon, finished letter
to Maud and wrote to Father. At
4 o'clk took my horse and went
out for a short ride — very muddy
& spattering rain now & then. Went
to Jas Carmalt's around the lake and
out by Sayre's farm — Back at 5.75 and
had a good bath and dress up —
Tea at 6. Eve'g in Lathrop's pew —
posted my letters and to bed at
about 10 o'clk.
