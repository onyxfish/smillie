## Left Page

### March Friday, 21 1873.

March day, with gusty snow squalls — got to studio at
9.20 & set to work on drawing
of Mt. Shasta for Appleton's.
Home to lunch at 1.50 —
Joe walked down to studio
with me at 2.45. I worked
until 6 o'clk, but did not
get my drawing finished.
Home to tea at 6.30 — Mr.
Vansinger, (Dr's aunt) there —
at 8. at Emma Ireland's
singing — nearly 12 when
I got home + 12.15 when
I got to bed —

## Right Page

### March Saturday, 22 1873.

Another squally day. Got to
studio at 9.20, feeling headachy.
Worked on drawing of Mt. Shasta.
At noon, stretched myself on
the sofa and rested my head
for nearly an hour. Home to
lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.50
& set to work desperately on
a water-color sketch; "The
Path to the Lake" — 6½ × 8½ in.
Worked until 5.15, getting
something that looked like a
sketch. at 5.30 went down to
Gauthier's — then to Snedecor's.
Back to studio & home at 7.15
Even't. mounted Shasta drawing
& got to bed at 11.15
