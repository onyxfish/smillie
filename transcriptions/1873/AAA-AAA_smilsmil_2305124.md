## Left Page

### October Sunday, 19 1873.

Stormy day, growing worse until at night it was almost a gale of wind and rain. Up late — no horse to attend to. B[illegible] at 8.30 went with Floral to Baptist Ch. Mother & Gel there. Mr. Cheshire preached from 1st Peter 2d Ch — 7th V.
Dinner at 1.30 — about 3 went to bed — up at 5.30 — Tea at 6 — at Mr. La[illegible] until after 8 — then home — Read news papers until after 10 & to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### October Monday, 20 1873.

Tremendous storm of wind & rain all day — After bkfst spent the m[illegible], until 11 o'clk, in studio building, clearing the [illegible] from out of the rain and otherwise fixing up. Tired out & on the bed for an hour before dinner. Dinner at 1 — a good part of p.m. fixing up apples to send to N[illegible] & settling bills. Tea at 6 — at 7.30 wrote a letter home — went down to P.O. — at 8.15 — then in to Lau's — there until near 11 — then home through tremendous wind & rain & to bed at 11.30.
