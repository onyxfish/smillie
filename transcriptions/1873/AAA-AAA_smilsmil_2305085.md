## Left Page

### August Saturday, 2 1873.

Hotter than ever, if possible —
Bkf't — ab't and again at work
on my sketches for Bunce —
Am getting along so slowly
that I must do something to
hasten matters & find a short
cut someway to Montrose —
Lunch ab't 1 o'clk — and a
shower soon after — At
about 4 o'clk — went to 4th M'c
a. to see about bill for Rent of
Studio No 4 — Sent to Geo. Went
in & looked at his furniture — Then
to my own studio — Letter from H4
Ferguson — Saw Lawrie & James
Hart — back at 6 o'clk — Tea
at 7 — Charle went to Po— at
3.30 P.M. — Eve'g swinging
in hammock & moonlight
until 10 o'clk — Then to bed —

## Right Page

### August Sunday, 3 1873.

Hot, Hot, Hot!!! — Bath — Bkf't —
at 10 — went to Baptist Ch — in the
S — heard most of the sermon
— then to St Chrysostom — cor. 7 av
& 49th St — tail end of service — then
home — Will there for half an hour —
Dinner ab't 1 o'clk — P.M. — wrote
to Geo & swung in hammock —
Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g Aunt
Jessie came over from Dunns
& visited until after 9 —
& got to bed about 10 —
