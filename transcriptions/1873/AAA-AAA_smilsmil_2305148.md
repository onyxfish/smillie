## Left Page

### December Wednesday, 10 1873.

Weather mixed, but in the afternoon it cleared quite
pleasantly. Got to studio
at 10 o'clk. — Fire out — while
kindling it, Emma D. came
in + invited to dinner to night.
Managed to do about a couple
of hours work on block — Home
to lunch at 1.45 — back at 2 —
found Mary Willard waiting —
before she left Dr. Percy came
+ before he left Ferguson
came in — At 6 went to
Emma D's — Preston D's mother
+ sister to dinner, — also Miss
"Lil" Howe. — A pleasant and
chatty evening followed —
Left at 10.45 — Home at 11
+ right to bed.

## Right Page

### December Thursday, 11 1873.

Another atrocious day —
drizzle, mud + rain. — Got
to studio at 9.20 — Found fire
out — so had to kindle it — then
to work on page block of
Montmorenci falls, from the St.
Lawrence River and got
it finished before I went
to lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.40 + touched [illegible] of a die
for Uncle Hellie + charged
drawing on block of Montreal
wharf. — Lost light early —
Home to tea at 6 — At 8
went with Charl up to
Wills — left Charl there
+ home at 10.10 — To bed
at 10.45 —

old Mrs. Fessenden died
to day.
