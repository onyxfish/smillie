## Left Page

### November Thursday, 20 1873

Mixed, as to weather — & under
the weather — as to my physical.
Near 10 when I got to the studio
to work on block "Islands
of the Saguenay" & finished it
before noon. Home to lunch
abt — back at 2.30. Commenced
on block "On the Saguenay
near its mouth" — Could not
see after 4.30. Tried to
sketch out something for a
steel plate subject for [illegible].
Home to tea at 6 — Eveng
made a number of notes — to
Geo. Whitney, W. J. Schuton — etc.
&c — Geo went to visit the
Sweedy's at Plainfield this
P.M. — Got to bed at 10.
Still suffering from a cold.

## Right Page

### November Friday, 21 1873

Weather not altogether settled
yet. Got to studio at 9.30 &
to work on "Saguenay, near
its mouth" — Interrupted.
Carpenter fixing ceiling.
Home to lunch at 12.30 —
Many Petjens & Hurrua Brime
at the house. Back to
studio at 1.30 — Finished
block about 4.30 — as
friend of Fergusons, a Mr.
Barnes called about a
peculiar postal card
of his own invention. Matt
am° from P. at noon —
Home to tea at 6.30 —
Eveg everybody gone to
church — Uncle Aaron
with Mother & me —
To bed at 10.45
