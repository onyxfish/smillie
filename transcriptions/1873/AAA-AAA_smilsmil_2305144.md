## Left Page

### November, Sunday 30, 1873

Pleasant day, but overcast part
of the time. Myf went to Dr.
Hastings' & heard him preach
from II Peter 1st chyrs. Home
to dinner at 1 o'clk. Men
a nap on sofa. At 4.30
went out for a walk —
Stopped at Will's — Took
tea — Chris. Bradley there
— Left at 8.45 — Home and
wrote a letter to [illegible] Mulford
To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### December, Monday 1, 1873

Dreary Snowy day — Got to Studio
at 9 o'clk. Very Cold. Kindled
fire & commenced a block —
"River front — Montreal." At it
until 1 o'clk. then home to
lunch. Emma Bourne there —
back at 2.30 — much trouble
to see, so dark. Geo. Spent
most of P.M. with me — so did
Ferguson. Home to tea at
6.30, after stopping at Nicoll
[illegible], wrote to Darley and
to Mr. Wm. Mains — Got to
bed about 11.30
