## Right Page

### April Monday, 7, 1873

Hardly able to hold up my
head, but went to studio.
Kindled fire + spent most
of the erly in lounge. Brewer
made a long call. Took
a lunch of Graham crackers
+ tea in studio. At 2. Misses
Bond + Sterling + yf Slade
came — I went through the
forms of giving them their
lessons and after much
distress got home near 7
o'clk. On the sofa until
nearly — then to bed.
