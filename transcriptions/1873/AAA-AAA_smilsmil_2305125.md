## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 21, 1873

A Regular NW. day. Cold
cloudy + windy. All the m[orning]
working on a Water Color
Sketch to give to Mr. Carmul[?]
Dinner at 12. Afternoon, at
work again on Sketch.
At 5 went out to walk.
At 6. at Fam M's to Tea.
Fanny Jenks there. also
Elisha Im. Home with
F.J. at 10.30. Read paper
until after 11. To bed
at 11.30.
At [?] 7.30 went to Dearks
with Hetty + saw [?] att[?]
baby.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 22, 1873

Rather dubious looking mg.
but determined to go over
to Stem's mill, beyond the
D.S.+W. Montrose depot, two
miles, and select 1000 feet of
red oak. Got started at 9.30
\+ took Cotrell with me. Owing
to bad Roads + poor horse, It was
near 12 when we got over there.
Stem not there, so Cotrell, another
man + I set to work tearing over
the lumber. By 2.30 had sorted
out 100 ft. + left it, offering $30.
for it. The weather had cleared
charmingly. Set out for home.
Stopped at depot + bought crackers
\+ cheese. Home about 5.15. Tea
at 6. Every went to P.O. at 8.
men in to Fan's. Elisha there.
Home at 10 + to bed at 10.30.
