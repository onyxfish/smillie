## Left Page

### November Tuesday, 4, 1873

Bright m[or]n. Up early — more [illegible] to Aunt H., Dana Dusten & Cotrell.
Bkfst at 7.30 Then to work for
A.B.N. Co on medallion head
(Italian drawing) "Roma" —
Diner soon af[ter] — worked
steadily on head until 5 o'clk.
At 5.30 called on Nicole, walked
around by studio & stopped there.
Home to tea at 6.15 — Eveg.
Uncle A. + Junia called and
stayed until 10.15 — To bed
at 10.45.

## Right Page

### November Wednesday, 5, 1873

[A]ttle pleasant weather. Up at 6.30
Matt am — from Po'keepsie about 7.30
+ [illegible] from Syracuse, soon after.
I worked all the m[or]n on drawing
for Appleton's "Yosemite" and took
a walk at about 11 — Lunch at
1 o'clk. P.M. worked steadily
on drawing until near 5 o'clk
when I could see no longer.
Then went out and walked
to the Park + away up to 72d
St + down 5th Ave — home.
Tea at 6 — Eveg. at home
in general mixture —
To bed at 10.30.
