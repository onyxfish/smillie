## Left Page

### October Thursday, 23 1873

Delightful day & quite mild. Bkft. at 8 & until 11, Running around after painters & carpenters. Then, with sketching Folios on my back, started for Jas. Carmalts. Got there at noon. C. & wife not at home. Pat caught the calf & got it into the barn for me while the girls gave me a cup of tea & bread & butter. Then out to barn & painted until near 5 o'clk getting something, but not much. Mr & Mrs. C. arr'd home before I set out. Home at 5.30. Dressed & at 6.30, after a light tea at Mrs. Dud's — went to Dunbayn's — Fau, Ben, H.H. Tyler & wife. Fanny Drinker, Gre. & S there — until 10 o'clk. — Took Fanny D. home — In at 10.30 — Washed brushes & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### October Friday, 24 1873

Gloomy w'd — looking like Nov. Bkft. at 8. Soon after, to work painting calf's head depending upon some slight outlines & Recollections of yesterday's work. Mother and Ree got off at 12.15 for home — they intend to go as far as Wilkesbarre to night. Dinner at 1 o'clk. Worked on head until about 4.30 — Then Jan & Gus came along and we walked out to Jas. Carmalts and back. Just in time for tea at 6. After tea walked down the hill & back, with Acora. At 8.45 went to Jan & Mrs — Left at 10.15 & to bed at 10.30
