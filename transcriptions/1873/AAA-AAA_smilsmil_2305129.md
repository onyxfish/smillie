## Left Page

### October Wednesday, 29 1873

Cold, blustering & wintry — snow squalls during the m[orning].
[illegible] at 8.30 — Mrs Spent picking up things & packing
trunk. Dinner at 1 o'clk. Then finished packing and
between 4 & 5 o'clk started out to make some calls. The
weather had cleared. Went down to the Searle's — there
until 6 o'clk. Called in on Fanny Jessup on my way
up to tea. After tea, an hour with the Lathrop girls — then
to Smyrna Richards' — then to the Biddle's — then, at 45
to Jan M's — Home at 7 o'yds & to bed at 11.

Chas. Lathrop's died at 3. a.m. Thursday —

## Right Page

### October Thursday, 30 1873

Bright, cold morning. After [break]fast at 8 o'clk. Swept & generally
cleaned up my rooms, then went out & spent the rest of the m[orning],
bidding my good byes. At 12
Mrs. L. had my dinner ready
for me. It was 12.30 before the
stage left. Got to Montrose depot
in time for train at 2.10. Had
a very pleasant trip home. Had
tea at Delaware at 6.10 —
Should have been at foot of
Barclay St. N.Y. at 9.25, but
about 15 m. late. Reached
home at 10.30. Fanny waiting
for me — all well. To bed
at midnight. [illegible — ~3 words]
