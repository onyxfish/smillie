## Left Page

### December Thursday, 18 1873.

Still mild & foggy — Got to
studio at 10 o'clk — & to work
on Matts painting (4th day)
Home to lunch at 1.30 —
back at 2.30 — & finished
2° painting about 3.30.
fussed about until
near 6 — Then miss [illegible]
Woodward & Gilbert
came in & it was 6.30
before I got started for
home. Tea at 7 —
Every writing notes
& letters & to bed
at 10.

## Right Page

### December Friday, 19 1873.

Dull m[illegible] then a downpouring
rain until night. near 10 o'clk
again before I got to studio —
Set palette & painted over the
cloud background on panel of
Cupids that Angers gave me.
Then painted a little bit of
sea coast on a shell for J. D.
[illegible] Ward. It was about 2 o'clk
when I went home to lunch
and after 3 when I got back.
Did some fussing and fixing
up — washed brushes & home
to tea at 6. Every, made
a note of Condolence to
[illegible] C. T. — and got to bed
shortly after 10.
