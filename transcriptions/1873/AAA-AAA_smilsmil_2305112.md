## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday, 25 1873

Miserable weather again —
overcast & threatening all day —
Heavy Rain after 3 o'clk P.M —
Bkfs at 8.30 — Myf very much
broken in upon in many ways.
Did but little work on "Quebec" —
At noon wrote letters to Father
& to Ferguson — mailed them —
Dinner at 1.30 — P.M. worked
until 4 — Then saddled Jim
& took a joley splashing
Ride of 8 miles or so, on Jim —
Stuart & Josie went home
this myf.; went down & bid
them good bye. Tea at
7.15 — at 8.30, over home —
Read newspaper and
to bed at 10 o'clk —
Bright starlight —
Three men at work part of the
day, shingling studio —

## Right Page

### Sept. Friday, 26 1873

Glorious day — Very mild and
bright. Overslept myself so
that "Jim" was again put off
with a lick & a promise —
Only Austen to work for me to-day
— 6 o'clk, when I got to work
on "Quebec" — Elisha M. called
about noon — Dinner at 1 o'clk
until 3 o'clk. with Austen
then worked until near 8
on drawing — A little after
5, out on "Jim" & had a joley
good Ride — In at 6.30 &
Tea at 7 — Evef at 8 o'clk
went down to Mulford's —
Cards with fam. Elisha &
Sylvanus until 10 — Then
home & to bed at 10.30 but
did not get to sleep until
near 1 o'clk. A.M. —
Robt Strange did not work
for me to-day —
