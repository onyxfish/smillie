## Left Page

### Sept. Tuesday, 9 1873.

Superb mef. Genl. Cram came
over to see me groom my horse —
Uncle Wm came out and met
him. Bkfs. at 8. After 10 before
I got to my work on "Ponakneek Stairs"
at it until 1 oclk. Dinner at 1.30
Rec'd. letter from Ella Dwen.
To work again at 2.30 & until
near 5 — Then & saddled "Tom" &
had a good ride until near
9. Tea soon after. Learned that
Mr. Azur Lathrop & Miss Allerston have
been filling Mrs. Cram with a hash
of all the stories they have heard concerning
her — mine included — with embellishments. Shall "go" for them.
Everf. about with uncle Wm. to
order paint. To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### Sept. Wednesday, 10 1873.

Up som after 6 — out of sorts — but
gave my horse his morning cleaning.
Bkfs. at 8.10 — Chatting until 9.15
Got to work on "Breakneck Stairs" at
10. and finished that 1 oclock. My
horse was attended to & dinner at 1.30
P.m., worked on Tracing of full page
"Saguenay River" & transferring until
4.30 — Then out to stable. Started
about 5 o'clk & had a delightful
ride — "Tom" getting to understand
what I want of him — In at 6.15.
Tea soon after. Everf. wrote
letters to Father & Geo. Posted
them and to bed at 10.30
