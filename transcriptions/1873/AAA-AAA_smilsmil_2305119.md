## Left Page

### October Thursday, 9 1873

Glorious day — cloudless & mild —
Alsstin finished his work on
studio about 11 A. M. — I did
a little work on "Under Sandy
Rock" then determined to cut
about 1½ inches of the top, which
I did after dinner — Worked
about on all the other blocks —
& at last at 4.30 got away on
my colt. Had a trip to [illegible]
Ride & in at 6.30 — Mother
arrived from N.Y. — about 6 P.M. —
Early to part & in every with
her — Then downtown and
at Fan's until 10 o'clk — Then
home & to bed at 10.30 —

## Right Page

### October Friday, 10 1873

Still superb weather — Up at
6.45 & spent a full hour in
my "Colt's Patch" — Bed at 8 —
met with mother until 10 —
Then down to Smiths & had
packing boy made for my
blocks — Back at 12 — Went
to Bunce — Dinner at 1
P.M. — The Lathrop gives Fan
Mrs & gus, & looking over my
drawings until after 3 — then
packed them in bed — On
"Patch" at 4.30 — in at
6.20 — Sea at 7 — Until
8. at Azur Lathrop's — Then
home until 9 — then down to
[illegible] office — In to Fan Mrs
at 9.30 — Bant Baldwin and
wife there — Stayed until
10.30 — Then home and to bed
at 11 —
