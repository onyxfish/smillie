## Left Page

### April Wednesday, 30 1873

Charming day — Got to studio at 9.15 & set to work trying to arrange lay figure & drapery for A.B.N. Co. drawing — about all the mf. At noon rode down to Sutphen's & bought underclothes. Home to lunch at 2. At studio at 2.45 — Juniee & Minn waiting for me. Juniee stood & I drew her figure in Water Color view of Manhattanville house. At 5.30 went to Lawthiers — At 6.30 at St. Nicholas Hotel & saw Mr. W.E. Brown & daughters — brought them up to Bryant's, in 23d St. Then to Studio & home at 6.40. Tea at 9 o'clk and to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May Thursday, 1 1873

Delightful mf. and very warm. Got to Studio at 9. It was 10 o'clk before Jessie came — & at 10.30 got to work studying from her. Worked until 12 — Then went over to Y.M.C. a. delivered Georgi's Studio Keys to Howland & packed up loose things until 2 o'clk — Then home to lunch — Dressed & back to studio at 3.15 — fussed about & pasted H.V. drawing into Passepartout. At 5 started for Mr. Jessie's — there at 5.15 — overcast. Dinner at 7 — Miss Watson & a german lady the other guests — a very pleasant evef. It was 11.30 before we started for home — young Watson & sister & the german lady under my charge — no madison ave. cars — walked over to 3d ave. Horrible crowd — got out at 45th St. & walked to 6th Ave. Rails — borrowed the Watson's umbrella & with my lady rode down to 32d St. and at last got home pretty thoroughly wet; at 1 o'clk — ague — to bed at once.
