## Left Page

### March Wednesday, 19 1873

Pleasant day — got to studio at
9.20 — Very much under the weather —
Pains all over — Went over to N. a
of D. Back to studio about 11 —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 — Bunce sent up the
last of my M.S. in Pf. with a
Request that I cut it down
another page — my nightmare
not yet over. If there was any
unity in my story, this sure to
lose it now. At 4.30 went down
there. Saw Bunce — Promised
to make him a drawing of
Mt Shasta. Back to studio
about 6 — Home at 6.30 — Swt.
over Water color acts —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March Thursday, 20 1873

Horrible storm that commenced about
10 a.m — Got to studio at 9.40 and
set to work mounting paper when
uncles A. + Mr came in — then
Miss Rosalie Goodman called for
a moment; and soon after, was
surprised by Messrs. Geo. Whitney,
Enoch Lewis and W.T. Richards.
Before they left, Miss Bound, one
of George's pupils came in, at
2.30 went over to the Albemarle
+ lunched with the Whitney party.
Back to studio at 4 o'clk. and
labored with Miss B. and young
Slade until nearly — Rec'd a
letter from Ferguson. Home
at 6.45 — Swel[l?], Rummaging
among old scraps and drawings
and to bed at 11.
