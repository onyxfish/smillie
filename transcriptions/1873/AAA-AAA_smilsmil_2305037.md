## Left Page

### April, Monday 28, 1873

Pleasant & springlike — No fire at Studio — got there at
9.15 & spent the mng char-
coaling design for $5.00 Nat.
Currency — At 1 o'clk went
to see Mr. Sherwood's pictures.
Home to lunch abt 30 — back
to Studio at 2.30 — Misses Bond
Sterling & Jos Slade there — I
gave them some attention &
also sketched on Bull note skt.
Mr. Jessup came & invited
me to dine with them next
Thurs. Home to tea at 7.30
at 8.30 went up to Willie's —
home at 10.30 & to bed at
11.20 —

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 29, 1873

Mildly pleasant day — got to
studio at 9.30 — Kindled a fire
& cleared up a little. Then
some charcoal drawing — At
12 o'clk. went to Howland's
Studio & paid George's rent.
Then to N.A. f D — then to Arnold's
& bought flannel for my lay figure
then to C.C. Ward's & Magraths —
neither of them in — then to Brook's
Bros & bought a suit of ready
made clothes — Home to lunch at
2 o'clk — back to studio at 3 —
Miss Bond painted during the
P.M. I worked on sketch of
Camp Scene in Racquette, for
Bunce — Uncle A.N.V. called
in hurry & paid me $70 —
Home to tea at 7 — Wet,
trying to rain — arranging
Water color accounts —
To bed at 10.30
