## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 5, 1905

**Weather:** Clear + somewhat milder, but hardly enough so to make it quite pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Blkp. at 8.30 — while sitting with my newspaper, at 10.30, Will Parke, Mr. P— + daughter Helen came in — on their way to church — P. made engagement to meet me at studio at 12.30 — I went to Charl's just before 12 — then to studio + at 12.30 — the P's, Miss Cunningham + two Messrs Cunningham came + spent half an hour or less in looking at Vesuvius + the River's Source — After that I walked on the sunny side of Park Ave. Till 1.45 — then in to dinner — from 4 to 5.30 I slept — then wrote to Miss Koshler + to Miss Nathalie Chadwick. Eve'g. I walked down to Emma S's — E. 17 "S. + spent the eve'g there till 10 — walked up home in a driving snow storm — cold + cutting.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 6, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

One of the worst snow + sleet storms that I remember. It was raining steadily when I got up. a most unexpected change after the fine, cold snow of last night — The leader from Rear extension was choked with ice + soon after blkp! The water set back, came in at the window at stair head + down thro' the wall into bath-Room off from parlor bedroom — I was the only available man so I went out on the Roof + shovelled snow + bailed water till I was tired out — then, after a rest, at it again — Before 11 Tony came along + completed the work — It was some time before I was able to go to studio — there I finished Tracing of Front Marie — Home to lunch — after that I spent an hour + two on my bed, getting up in time to go over to Mrs. Sidney Smith's + hear Rev. T. C. Williams read Milton, with Remarks — Then home — Many other girls have sent out cards for Mondays — this being the first — Mr. Woolsey Carmalt was the devoted one — Emma J. + Helen Butler came in from the Reading at Mrs. Smith's. Mrs. Emily Taylor Kellogg came + later went with K. + E. — to Charl's to dine — Mary + I dined alone — Eve'g I wrote notes to Mr. Seaver Buck — Acting Head Master at Hackney + to Raepm.
