## Left Page

### Fri. April 14, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day, winding up with a brief thunderstorm \+ some heavy rain between 8 \+ 9 p.m. To studio at 10. my days work was printing from the three plates just prepared. "Ponte Marie" Paris, "Frauenkirch dome, Dresden" \+ "Chafel near Lugano". The results were reasonably successful — no parts proved to be under bitten while some parts were over bitten, but not seriously. Home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 — Printed till near 5 — then home \+ rested till dinner at 6.30 Eve'f I wrote brief letters to the boys \+ got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Sat. April 15, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant mf. To studio at 10. Put two plates in my holder \+ then with camera \+ tripod went to 42d. St. bet. 5th \+ 6th Aves, \+ took two photo-shots at the "New Times" Tower building to give me exact data from which to correct my pen sketch from which I purpose to make an etching — Then back to studio \+ developed them — 12 o'clk — till then I worked on European negatives — then home to dinner lunch — To studio at 2.30 \+ again worked on negatives — home after 5. used up by an attack of indigestion — was not able to take any dinner — on my bed till 11 — Then up \+ prepared for bed for all night.
