## Left Page

### Mon. Jan. 16, 1905

my birthday .
it passed without notice of any kind.

**Weather:** Clear, cold, windy \+ dirty  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear, cold, windy \+ dirty \- I got to studio after 10 o'clk. Found Jennings there sweeping \+ the Ther. at 50°. In explanation he said he was late \- I told him that I agreed to that \- he asked me if I w. return at about 11, when it w. be more comfortable. So I went to Kimmel \+ Voigt's \+ was there till 12.30 getting ph- of Borden Photo grav to submit to W. P. \- Then to studio. Packed them down \+ then home. Miss Margaret Buck there to lunch \- To studio again soon after 2 \+ found that, all draughts having been left open, the furnace fire was out \- Before I could rekindle it, A.C.B. \+ Maynard were after me to fix the electric bells at front door. That attended to I kindled the fire \+ then in studio till 4 o'clk \- Then downtown to W. C. Cullen's \+ got my deco roll of developed films. Also some glass plates \- Back to studio made up some velox developer \- then home at 6.30 \- Dinner \- Well. after reading my newspaper I fixed myself for work when Clark came in \- he stayed till after 10 \- Then I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Tues. Jan. 17, 1905

R. Swain Gifford's funeral.

**Weather:** Very fine day, clear \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day, clear \+ cold \- To studio at 9.30 \+ spent the day unpacking more boxes of unbound books \+ clearing up generally \- Emma J. came to studio to see about having something done to the portrait of Grandfather Ireland \- I said I thought a panelback stretcher was all that it be best to do now \- At 11 o'clk. Maynard \+ I went to Church \- 39th St. \+ Mad. Ave. \- to funeral of R. S. Gifford \- The service was to me very formal \+ unimpressive \- Talked with Yale of G's illness \- he had a very poor heart \- Saml. Colman walked to studio with me \+ made a pleasant call but did not tell me where he lived nor did he ask me to call on him \- Home to lunch \- back at 2 \- worked at putting away unbound books till after 5 \- a short walk until after 6 \- dinner at 6.30 \- Well. fussed for a while with photo-printing.
