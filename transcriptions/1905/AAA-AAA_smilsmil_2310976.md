## Left Page

### Tues. April 18, 1905

**Weather:** Cold, cloudy & windy — disagreeable  **Temp:**

To studio about 10 — then to grocers & butchers — then to Kimmel & Voigt's, in Canal St. about printing of Miss Roelker's book plates — then to studio — took photo. prints out of blotters. Home to lunch — back about 2 — most of the afternoon cutting & mounting photo. prints. Had a long, pleasant call from Nathalie Chadwick & her cousin Mr. Anderson — Nathalie told me of her engagement to be married to a young electrical engineer — name forgotten — home at 6.30 — dinner — Eva & Chard came in — to bed late.

## Right Page

### Wed. April 19, 1905

**Weather:** Jim came home. Easter holiday.  **Temp:**

Milder — overcast most of the day. After bkft. — went to Douglas's & left word for him to call at the house — then to Millers — saw him & arranged for him to come to the house after lunch — Then, after marketing, went to studio at about 10.30 — Set to work at house cleaning & at the same time looking for & gathering together the sketches, half finished plates & prints that I wish to work upon and finish — home to lunch — Miller came after it & I gave him order to make glass doors for the book cases in library — There was considerable talk & it was late before I got to studio & resumed clearing up work — I was very tired when I got home at 5 & I rested till dinner at 6 — Jim had come some time during the afternoon — After dinner I went to Chard's & left with him negative to take to Cullen to be enlarged — Then I went to Alice Ireland's & met Jim there — Mollie Adams has just come from her long trip — Canadian Pacific — Cal. & Mexico. She showed us a remarkable lot of photos, that she had taken — quite late Edwa Cook & Henry Murphy came in — Jim & I left at 10.30.
