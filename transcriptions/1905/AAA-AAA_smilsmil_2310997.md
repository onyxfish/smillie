## Left Page

### Tues. May 30, 1905

*[written above header: Emma J \+ Chas. Collins dined with us.]*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day \- up early \- but it was 10
before I got to studio \- worked on 5×7 \-
Aqua-tint, (Oct. afternoon) with roulette
giving tone (covering grain) \+ emphasis
\+ then, on damp paper from yesterday
I took some pr. \+ think the plate is
much improved \- Home to lunch \+
back \+ continued the proving till
the paper was all used \- washed
up \+ home at 4.30 \- Had a nap before
dinner \- Emma Ireland came \+
then Chas. Collins whom I invited
this m'g. We had a very pleasant
eve'g till 10 \- then E \- left in her cab.
\+ C. followed \- Chas ret.
from his Schenectady demonstration
but I did not see him.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 31, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant \- cool, cloudy \- rain somewhere not
far off. To studio \+ worked on 7×9 \- Campoing
Road side Chapel \- Maggiore \- with roulette,
scraper \+ burnisher \- at 12.30 went to bank
then home to lunch \- after that went to la
Touraine in 39 St. Manager not in but clerk told
me that the suite on 10th floor, that I had spoken
for, had been let \- then I looked at suite on 5th
floor \- then home \+ wrote a letter to Ralph.
Then to studio \- got 7×9 into condition to
print \+ prepared paper for to-morrow \-
A headache that discomforted me all
day quite used me up at night after my
dinner \-
