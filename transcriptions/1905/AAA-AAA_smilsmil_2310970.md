## Left Page

### Thur. April 6, 1905

**Weather:** Cloudy m[illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The H's went to Jamesburg. N.J.

Cloudy m[illegible]., windy & cool, clearing to fine. To studio as usual & fussed away the morning over little odds & ends of work. At 12.30 I went to Century — the clerk told me that W. Edgar Van Winkle of E. 70's — had told him he wd. give $100 for my painting "Chapel near Lugano" — I wrote him a note accepting offer — Lunch at home — The H's left after bkfst. to go to the little house they have lived near Jamesburg N.J. — At studio I fooled away most of the afternoon over photo-films — Went to tile man in E, 23d. St. & selected tile for bath room in extension — then to Standard Co. mill, 31st St. but was too late to select pattern of bath tub — Home after 6 — dinner — Swell all went to Charles to see the working of his new lantern for slides — Show did not come off because condenser of lantern had not been sent home with lantern — I took photos etc. to Miss Roelker & left them at her home —

## Right Page

### Fri. April 7, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — To studio at 10 & commenced the biting-in of "Fraueu Kirche dome" — much bothered at beginning by presence of a lady pupil of Georges who waited for more than an hour for the coming of Geo. Home to lunch — continued & finished biting \+ cleaned plate at about 5 — The result appears to be successful — Home after 6 — dinner — Evely. Ralph & I went to Charles & saw his lantern work —
