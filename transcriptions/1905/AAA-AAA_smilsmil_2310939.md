## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 1, 1905

**Weather:** Overcast & Raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast & Raw — Snow late in the afternoon. To studio at 10 & began to put things in shape to do some etching. I got out plates & sketches made last summer — made up some soft ground & then had a mess in cleaning the casseroles. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Laid grounds on Two zinc plates & one on copper & cut gelatine for tracing. At 4.30 I went to gas stove store. 4th Ave. & 26th St. to have laundry gas stove fixed. Home at 5.30 — dinner with Mary alone (K. & B. dining with Charl.) at 6.30. At 8 took M. — to Charl's & then Returned home & spent Rest of evg. in posting accts of Jim & Ralph.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 2, 1905

**Weather:** Very clear & very cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very clear & very cold — after 10 when I got to studio. To work making tracing on gelatine of the pen sketch of the dome of Kirche & picturesque bldg. in foreground, made in Dresden, last summer — at about 12.30 went to 42d St. & 5th Ave. to get another photo. of Times Bldg. Found that the sun-light had gone from the east side — did not get any thing — home to lunch — To studio at 2. Selleck had been there with pictures from N.A. of D. — had taken portrait of Geo. Ireland. — My Dresden pen sketch had the dome so crooked that I got into a mess & consequently worked till 4.30 before I got it straight. Walked for awhile on Park Ave. — but it was far too cold for comfort. — Home, before 6 — dinner at 6.30 — Evg. continued the work of Posting accts. of Jim & Ralph.
