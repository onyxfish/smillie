## Left Page

### Fri. March 17, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day - bright + almost mild - a letter just rec'd. from Charl at Palm Beach says that the Ther. there is at 71° at midnight + 84° at noon. And here we are just getting the ice out of our streets. I went to studio + commenced work, in a small way, on the Roelker book plate - went out for a sunning + walk at 12.30. crowds on 5th ave. to see Pres. Roosevelt + the Irish Parade - Two separate shows - Home to lunch at 1 - after that wrote a short letter to Kate introductory to the one rec'd. from Charl. Then to studio - there I wrote a letter to Ralph. Then out + had another walk - home at 5.30 + rested till 6.30 - then dinner. Ev'g wrote to Jim + mailed it + Ralph's letter at 10 - then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. March 18, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudless - mild with spring-like haze. I got up with headache - to studio + did a little work on Roelker book-plate, redrawing the design with some modifications, upon Bristol board. At 12.30 out for a walk - went to Butlers + enquired about frame he is making for me - then home to lunch - headache got the better of me so that I went to my bed - slept till 3 - then feeling better I got up + went to studio. Worked awhile cutting margin mounts for crooked negatives - then to Brooks Bros. with Spring overcoat to be renovated - then walked up 5th Ave. + home at 6.15 - dinner. Ev'g. loafed with news paper + to bed early.
