## Left Page

### Wed. Aug. 30, 1905

**Weather:** Day opened cloudy & with showers — clearing later beautifully, clouding over again late in the afternoon  **Temp:** Heavy thunderstorms between 6 & 8

o'clk. with torrents of rain. Otis came & put in a part of a day & Moody helped him a part of the time. Bashaw came also & worked at laying wall near gate on road for a short time. He came again in the afternoon — do not know how long he worked. I spent some time in doors working with pencil on negatives trying to work in some sky effects. I was out of doors a good deal with the men & weeding over the top-dressed hillside. Evef. I read as long as my eyes would allow.

This afternoon Ralph, while Bowling at the hotel alley, managed to bang his right hip severely with one of the balls that slipped in his hand. He was very lame after it & at night I fixed him with a hot water bag.

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 31, 1905

**Weather:** A delightful day — beautiful atmosphere — clear cool breezy.  **Temp:** [illegible]

The boys spent the day at the lakes with young folks from Maghees. I was out of doors a large part of the day — first, in garden after Help. where I gathered a large quantity of corn. Then with Earl directing repairs on road that was badly washed by the torrents of yesterday eve'f. & with Otis & Bashaw at work on the wall at the gate. Eve'f. I had a pleasant little fire in my room & was very comfortable. I think my Rheumatism is slowly passing away.
