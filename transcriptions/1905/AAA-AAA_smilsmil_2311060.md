## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 3, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine weather continues — In bed all day — a fairly comfortable night — Temp. in m't only 99° which made me hopeful — Yale came in the m't \+ made a long call but it did not seem to me that he took much interest in my case — Told a lot of stories \+ said I'd pull through — \+ that discouraged me. Dreadful upsetting of things in the house owing to our coming \+ condition Mary seems to be easier, \+ quieter, but she has to remain here while Geo sleeps at Chars — Chard was very attentive — \+ the \+ Nellie did all that they could —

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 4, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still fine weather, rather warm — my Temp. a little higher than yesterday but I got up after bkfs', dressed myself \+ went down to my easy chair in parlor \+ lounged there — about noon I went out for a short walk on Park Ave. — I went but a short distance \+ finding that I had no strength I returned — I kept to my chair for the rest of the day \+ eve'g — Chard came in — To bed after 10.

Jossie Smillie who is Matron in Presb'n. Hosp'tne called \+ urged me to go there — I have already almost decided to do so —
