## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 12, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful day — It was after 9 when we had our bkft. — am discouraged again this m't. — feeling weak \+ out of sorts, but cannot tell just how, specifically — It was near 11 before Jim \+ I went to studio — There we overhauled some trunks \+ found sheets, pillowcases, towels \+ a lot of my under clothes. Jim had a suit case \+ I had a bag — we filled them \+ rel'd. to Touraine — Dinner at about 2 o'clk. After that we went, by subway, to 110th St. \+ thence down to Riverside Drive — we had not gone far enough up town to get a good view of the American end of the line of 18 battleships, so we walked up to 120th St. \+ farther till we found a stairway down to the walks in Riverside Park — There was an immense throng of people out to see the two fleets — it was with difficulty that we walked, the crowd was so great \+ slow moving — The display on the River was wonderfull — The 18 vessels, lying in mid stream, formed a line from 42d St. to above Grant's tomb — The River was alive with small craft, from Row boats to excursion steamers, all crowded with sight-seers — I walked down to 95th St. \+ felt so tired that I started for our rooms — Jim pushed on to 72d St. — When I got to my Room I stretched myself on my bed \+ had just fallen asleep when Chas came — he stayed till after 6 — Jim came in \+ we had dinner at about 7 — Ev'f he went to Ed'd Cook's — Val \+ Gordon made me a short call — I Read awhile \+ got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 13, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A little sunshine in the m'f. but soon changed to clouded, bleak \+ cold weather — After bkft. was in my room some time arranging my laundry — then to laundry cor. 41st \+ Lex. Ave — then to Douglass \+ P't. his bill — then to Miller's \+ P't. his bill — then to studio \+ got out from two trunks all of the curtainings of one kind or another that I could find \+ then took one to Lock's to see if it could be cleaned — then back to Touraine \+ lunched — At 3 o'clk. back to studio — There for a short time — for a while in George's studio then to 39th St. rooms \+ worked till near dinner time assorting books \+ putting them on shelves — Dinner at 7 — Ev'f. Jim went to theatre with a friend — I continued my work with the books till 9 — then Read till I went to bed at near 11 o'clk.
