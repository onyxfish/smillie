## Left Page

### Sun. April 2, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Wonderfully fine day in spite of wind — much cooler — breakfast at 10 — It was not long after 11 when R. \+ I started out for exploration — By subway we went to 157 th St. Broadway. Then walked north \+ across to pumping station at High Bridge — Then across the bridge \+ down to H.R.R.R. station on the platform met Isham (the artist) with party going north, also Mr. \+ Mrs Diekman \+ youngest son returning to city from Tramps — They took Putnam div. Train — 1.25 — R. \+ I took H.R. Train at 1.04 \+ got to Grand Central Station at 1.21 — Then home — dinner at 2 — About 5 I went to studio \+ changed p.fs. to dry blotters after I had been to Century \+ seen the picture there — My Lugano Chapel looks common place to me — It was not well placed — maybe it looked common place to the Com, Geo. walked home with me — after returning from studio I rested till 6 — Quiet eveg at home, readi[illegible]

## Right Page

### Mon. April 3, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — To studio at usual time — Spent the day biting-in the plate of Pont Marie — Paris — drawn some weeks ago — Home to lunch \+ back — Cleaned plate at 5 o'clk. \+ consider the biting fairly successful under the circumstances — I feel timid \+ very uncertain about this work now — I suppose it's loss of nerve owing to age \+ want of constant practice — A short walk — home to dinner at 6.30 — a rather stupid eveg at home which I regret for Ralph's sake.
