## Left Page

### Sun. July 9, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

I think this has been the hottest day so far this season — at any rate, I have suffered more to day — Ther. in my room got to 84° did not go below 74° last night — A light thunder storm with a heavy dash of Rain after 10th no let up in the heat — I spent the best part of two hours in the m.f. on a scrub \+ shampoo. bkp. at 9 — Then I worked in the attic turning over the Rubbish \+ getting an idea of the contents of the store room — I had a nap before dinner at 1.30 — afternoon I worked in my own room \+ cleared up there somewhat — \+ again I had a nap — a light supper at about 7 — at near nine R. \+ I went to studio with a lot of fragile bric-abac —

## Right Page

### Mon. July 10, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

To day has been a coaster \+ I have suffered very much — in my bed room the Ther. did not fall below 80° last night \+ has stood at 86° all day — as I write, at 9.30 p.m. it is 83°. I have given out generally \+ have had great discomfort in dragging about. After bkp. I went to Ryan's \+ telephoned to Selleck asking him to send men \+ wagon as soon as he could — they came at about 2 — When I came back Ralph pitched into the work of cleaning out the attic store room \+ he did a power of work — I was able to do but little in assisting him, but before noon he had almost every thing out \+ in trunks. I had to lie on my bed part of the time — lunch after 1 o'dk. All hands worked, in the face of a coming thunder storm to load Selleck's wagon — it was hardly done when the rain came \+ for nearly an hour that wagon was in front of the house in torrents of rain — it was 4 o'clk. before I held up so that we could go to the Studio where we unloaded. It was about 5 when R. \+ I ret. to the house — Then again I took to my bed \+ he emptied the linen closet — Letitia Kelley, the colored cleaner cleared up some of the rooms — The paper hanger finished 2d Story front. Dinner at 6.30 — Gordon ret. while we were at table — We all I was in my bed, except for a brief space.
