## Left Page

### Wed. May 24, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day - Mary, Katharine + I started for Po'keepsie on the 8.45 AM Train + got there at about 10.30 - + went directly to 39. Hooker Ave - Uncle Willie's - Found them in a sort of hospital condition - all three of the old people are very feeble - This is Aunt Jessie's 86° birth-day - Uncle W- is in his 93. year + Aunt A - is 81. Aunt J- seemed quite bright but Emma told us that she had been so ill the day before that she (E-) was alarmed - Uncle W- suffers constantly from bladder trouble + was so feeble + in such pain that he did not come down to dinner - Harry Smillie was there + so was Walter's daughter Mable who seems to have arr'd from So. America to be in at the death. Before dinner I went out to Vassar College - I had about an hour on the grounds + went the round of the grounds + buildings - The flower beds about the athletic field made an exceeding fine display - I spent a large share of my time looking at the collection of pictures in the Art-gallery, a singular jumble - many little examples of early American - Cole, Church, Cropsey, Kensett, Innis - Doughton - Homer - Martin - Whattuck, Colm m + others - a good sketch by Mr. Ruskin - three little W-C. vignettes by Turner, for engraving - etc- etc - I got back to Uncle W's a few minutes after 1 + had dinner - We left soon after 4 - took 4.50 train + got to N.Y. in time - 7.22 Home + had dinner at once - Charl came in later + to him we told the story of our journeying.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 25, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - still unusually cool weather - It was not till 10.30 that I got to studio + to work, again trying to get a satisfactory aqua-tint grain on lower portion of "Constance - Tower on Rhine" - At last, getting a fairly good one + that seemed to match nearly the other portions of the plate, I set to work on the biting quite determined to be brutal + get snap + intensity. Home to lunch - Afternoon, continued my biting, taking too much time over it. Cleaned plate at about 6 - Then home to dinner - Evert. Charl came in.
