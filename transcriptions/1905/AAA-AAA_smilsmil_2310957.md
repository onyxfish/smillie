## Left Page

### Sat. March 11, 1905

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - to studio at 10 - another day spent between photos. \+ getting things in etching room in shape for work. Then the photo work is now a waste of good time \+ I cannot excuse myself for giving so much up to it - Lockwood de Forest came to consult me, as one of the finance com. about investments of N. A. Funds. At 12.30 went to bank \+ deposited Parkin's check. Home to lunch late - 1.30 - then to studio - home about 5 - had a rest before dinner - Eve'g. went to Century \+ heard Edw. S. Curtis of Seattle, Wash. Talk of the Athapascan Indians of the south west. He has been with them a great deal during the past six years \+ has taken a great number of photos - many of which he showed in the course of his Talk - they were interesting - some were very good as photos, but many were faked to produce startling effects - evening - night, moonlit, etc He is not an educated man \+ is an aggravating speaker - does not tell what you most want to know \+ got thro' an hour \+ a half without giving any specific or valuable information. Home before 11.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 12, 1905

**Weather:** Dull, raw day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, raw day - Up at 7 - had a good scout \+ [illegible] at 9 - after 11 I went to studio at [illegible] 1.30 I worked making a frame to hold gelatin film masks against a printing frame; a devise by which I hope to be able to retard the printing of thinner parts of negatives - a short walk before dinner at 2 - another walk between 4 \+ 5 - then a rest till 6 o'clk when I commenced Velox Printing \+ continued till 10 - then cleared up \+ to bed at 11.
