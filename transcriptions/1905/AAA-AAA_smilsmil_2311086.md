## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 26, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day after the light rain of last night up after 8 \- had a rub down \+ bkfs. after 9. I did some writing \- then Mary \+ Bessie came \+ stayed till after 1 o'clk \- then I went to George's \+ had a comfortable dinner there \- Jim dined alone at the Touraine \+ later went over to Brooklyn on a girl hunt. At about 3.30 I went to the fine Arts bldg \+ saw La Farge's Confuscius for the Minneapolis State-House. I do not \- cannot enjoy his work \- then to Central Park \- 8th Ave. entrance \+ walked thro' \+ out at 5th Ave. \+ rode down by Mad. Ave. cars \- Had a nap \- a light meal with Jim at 7 o'clk \+ at about 9 went to Charles \- Mary, the girls \+ Geo. there \- looking at photos \- C. played on his Orchestra \- back at 10.30 \- light rains \-

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 27, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day \- clear \+ quite cold \- Bkfs. as usual \- I went around by way of Victoria laundry to give the girl there a scolding \+ tell her that I have arranged to give my "wash" to Lavinia Nash. Then to studio after 10 \+ worked at re-arranging curtains \+ hangings \+ trunks to send to the Touraine where I shall have dock select the fittest to put up. Katharine \+ Bessie came after 11 \+ selected a number of German, French \+ Italian books of Annie's. I returned to my rooms before 1 o'clk \- ate a raw egg \+ some grape nuts \+ then went to Presbn. Hospital \+ saw D. Brown for a few moments \- then went to the Lenox library \+ spent an hour looking at the old Am. paintings \+ the collection of Menzel prints. Then walked a short way down the Ave. but feeling weak \+ out of sorts I rode down to my rooms \+ then slept for an hour or more. On getting up I was less inclined than ever to move \+ so, sat around \+ read newspapers \+ magazines till Jim came in \- then had dinner at 7 \+ after that spent the ev'g reading.
