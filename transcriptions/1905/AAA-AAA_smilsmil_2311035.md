## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 14, 1905

**Weather:** Clear & cold m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

The Ther. fell to 54° in my bedroom last night & my rheumatic arm pained me so greatly that my sleep was much broken — I went to Scott Brown's & ordered stovewood for laundry — then to P.O & got mail — The boys went up Noon mark & Ralph Ret. somewhat under the weather — dinner delayed for them — afternoon, between loss of sleep last night & a miserable headache I gave up, went to bed & had a long nap — the headache did not yield & I spent an uncomfortable eve'f. — Went to bed early with a hot water bag at my lame shoulder.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 15, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

In bed all day with a most uproarious & damnable headache that started yesterday afternoon & lasted all night. The weather was horrid — heavy cold rain that fell most of the day & all night. Mr. Taylor arrived at about noon. I began to revive at about the close of day & was able to take something to eat at about 8 o'clk — The boys came in & read aloud to me, first Ralph & then Jim.
