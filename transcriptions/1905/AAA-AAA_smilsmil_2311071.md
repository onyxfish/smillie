## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 25, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day - overcast \+ Raw, with at times enough of snow or mizzle to make the sidewalks damp but not wet. When I woke at 6.30 I did not feel any more comfortable than I did yesterday but breakfast improved matters - Dr. Brown made his rounds later than usual - he considered my case carefully \+ then ordered an enema which R. administered at 11 o'clk. \+ I got a satisfactory wash out. After that a rest - then my dinner - cream tomato soup roast chicken - potatoes, Rice, spinach - a bisque cake \+ grapes. Then another Rest - then up and dressed soon after 2 - Jessie at once took me off to her room \+ I spent some time there - then back \+ sat in the doctors parlor \+ read till Dr. Tuthill \+ Dr. Mason came in - They talked encouragingly of my condition \+ Dr. T- said if there were no drawbacks he thought I might be in shape to leave the hospital early next week - I got back to bed before 5 \+ soon after Mary \+ Emma Ireland came \+ I had a very pleasant visit - I rec'd from Clarence Smith [illegible] bills for 110 \+ 3 studios - amounting to $815 - I drew checks for taxes \+ for Pease \+ Elliman com. $600 - also $200 to Ralph \+ $30 to Century \+ $31 to Moody at St. Huberts - Jim did not come in the evef.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 26, 1905

**Weather:** Variable  **Temp:** [illegible]

a cold Raw wind - The sunshine about the middle of the day, very pleasant - The cathartics given me made my m't. from 7 o'clk. to noon very uncomfortable - Before noon I got up \+ dressed, but found the parlor \+ corridors chilly \+ I was rather disconsolate as I wandered about - after my dinner Jessie came \+ together we went up on the roof \+ walked about there for a few minutes - I am getting stronger rapidly \+ enjoyed the brief outing - Feeling tired I undressed after 4 o'clk. \+ got into bed again - Jessie made me another cheery visit - Eve'g. I felt dumpey - my head ached \+ I was out of sorts generally - Jim came after 9 o'clk - stayed till 10 \+ then Miss Mosely \+ Collins fixed me for the night.
