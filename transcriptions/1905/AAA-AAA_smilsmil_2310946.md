## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 15, 1905

**Weather:** fair & somewhat milder but still cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

fair & somewhat milder but still cold — To studio after 10 — Nellie came in saying that Gel. was sick — unable to come to studio & wished me to paste his two Water-Colors for Rotary Exhtn in their frames & let carman have them when he came — That took some time — & I made up a lot of Acid fixing solution — Then to work on Port Marie Etching — Again my work was interfered with — drain pipe from my dark room sink stopped up — plumber at work out in cellar & I had to go down & see to it — then to Davies Bros coal yard foot of E. 37th St. to hurry up coal for studio — Then home to lunch — Miss Van Stone there — Back to studio — finished Port Marie — worked again on Done of Frauen Kirche — Before 5 I went out & walked 2½ miles by block measure — then home — Mr. F.H. Maglee there — he dined with us & spent part of evng — Then left to call on Chase — I loafed altho' I have several letters to write.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 16, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fairly pleasant day — It was 10.30 before I got to studio — Ryan has a part of drain pipes in cellar, from my dark-room sink pulled out because of a stoppage — so I could not bite my etchings — but I cd. not have done so even if I had had a free drain pipe, feeling too unfit to attempt any work of importance — I fooled away much of the time experimenting upon negative films having their spots & trying with Retouching varnish & lead pencil to obliterate them — After 5 p.m. I went to Georgie's — 20 E. 47th St. He has been sick for a couple of days — Mary was there — we came home together at 6.30 — dinner — Evng, altho' feeling wretchedly sick, I did a little work printing from my experimenting during the day — When I went to bed soon after 10 I was burning with fever, stomach in a ferment & with severe Rheumatic pains all over — I had a night of extreme wretchedness
