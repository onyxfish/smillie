## Left Page

### Sun. May 14, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim ret. to New-Haven

Some very heavy rain in the m't. but before noon it ceased + became very uncomfortably warm + muggy - I got up at 7 + scrubled, rubbed + dressed till 9 - then had H'p't - Read my news paper after that till 11 - Then to studio + till near 1 was engaged in clearing + cleaning - the place is in dreadful disorder. Then a short walk + to Charl's. There till 2 - home to dinner - some reading - Then a nap - At 8. went to station with Jim + saw him off for New-Haven - Then to 3d Ave + rode down to 17th S. - I called on Emma D— + spent a pleasant eve't with her till 10.30 - Then walked home

## Right Page

### Mon. May 15, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable day - overcast, close and muggy - The work of denudation + packing goes steadily on - Mary has nearly everything in boxes or barrels - a lot of it has gone to the storage ware-house - + many bare spots are left. I have to realize that I am now close to the end of a very interesting chapter in my life - I am soon to leave the home of my married life, where I have passed 24 years - + it is very probable that I shall not return to it - I do not, dare not, think of it.
To studio at 10.30 - spent the day biting on Bavaro Chapel plate The liquid aqua-tint grounds spread on Sat - on sky + foreground - Then I burnished + fussed over various parts of the plate - To lunch + back as usual - Dinner - Eve't at home.
