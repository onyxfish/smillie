## Left Page

### Sat. June 17, 1905

**Weather:** Hot \+ humid but with pleasant air stirring  **Temp:**

Woke at 3 A.m. Splitting headache \- able to get up to bkft. \+ gradually the pain of it passed away \- At 10. to Manhatttan Storage \+ arranged to have two China Packers come to the house monday m'f. then to Laundry \+ got wash \- then to Miller's \- then home \+ worked sorting books till lunch at 1 \- to studio before 2 to meet Mr. Gaffney who was to help Hahneufeld's boy Take Packing boxes from Studio to house \- Mr. Gould did nt put in an appearance \- H's boy managed alone to Take them \- I collected my oil painting things \+ went home at about 4. Getting unbound books together till after 5. Then I Rested \- to Charl's at 7 \- dinner \- Ge\. came in for a little while bringing 3 paintings for C \- To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 18, 1905

Charl left for Phila at 8 P.m.

**Weather:** Very hot \+ humid \- a trying day in spite of a little breeze \- some clouding \+ threat of a storm but nothing came of it.  **Temp:**

Up at 7 \+ had a very satisfactory bath \+ rub-down \- ableft. alone at 9 \- At 10 I went to 110 \+ there I worked at clearing out closets till 1 \- Then Geo., Nellie \+ Gordon had dinner \+ I had some lunch \- At 3 o'clk. I went to Emma J.'s with little blocks, stretcher keys screws, glue etc \+ was successful in getting Grand Father Ireland stretched out flat. Emma was there \- left at 5 \+ then to Charl's \- Dinner at 6.30 \- at 8 he left for Phila expecting to meet two German "Ural-men" to go on with him. I remained to spend the night \+ went to my room before 10 \-
