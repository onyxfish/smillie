## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 10, 1905

**Weather:** Beautiful m't. but growing colder  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful m't. but growing colder \+ windy in the afternoon — until nearly 11 ok I was engaged in my rooms at writing or one thing \+ another — Then I rode down to Wannamaker's \+ tried shopping for various things but succeeded only in getting a laundry bag — Then I went to Pomroy's to have my truss adjusted but after waiting for a long time I gave it up \+ left without attention — Then to Vantine's \+ bought a smoking jacket — Then to Brook's Bro's. but Cox was out to lunch — Then walked up to 39th. \+ prepared my own lunch after 1 o'clk. Then I had a nap \+ following that, as I found it unpleasantly cold \+ windy out of doors I busied myself indoors, having some of the furniture shifted around \+ cleaning some of it up. Jim came in at 6.20 \+ later, Harold Howe. He dined with us \+ with Jim went to the theatre — Sweth. I wrote to Ralph \+ Edw. Cook paid me a pleasant call leaving before 10 — It was after 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 11, 1905

**Weather:** Remarkably fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Remarkably fine day — but I got up after 7 o'clk. having been much disturbed since about 4 a.m., consequently I was not feeling very fresh — Had bkfst. with Jim — Men sat in my room for awhile — then to Mauth's. Storage \+ superintended the getting out of bureau \+ bookcase for Jim's room — then to barber's \+ had hair cut — then to Brooks Bros. \+ ordered an overcoat \+ was measured for a suit — Then back to Tomaino's \+ had lunch — After that, feeling the loss of sleep I lay down on my bed \+ had a good nap. It was after 4 when I went to Freese's \+ got Lithia water etc. Jim came in at 6.20 \+ we had dinner — Sweth. he went out for awhile \+ I worked at arranging books — he helped me when he came in — after 11 when I got to bed.
