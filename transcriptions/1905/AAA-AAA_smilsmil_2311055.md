## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 23, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A disagreeable day — heavy clouds with a blustering N.W. wind. When I got up at 7 o'clk. it was after a night of little rest \+ my headached as usual — the pain in my right side was nagging \+ the irritation of the ureter showed no abatement — alto-gether I started the day much depressed. Bashaw did not come in the m'f, but he came in the afternoon, completed the decline east side of Arvey \+ Adams came in the afternoon \+ primed it. I was about with Earl for a time marking trees to be cut down near the house — K. \+ E. went off to Indian head \+ Mary \+ I had dinner alone. I wrote to Nye \+ Ferd Beede to keep their cows \+ horses of my grounds — Nye's cow has been chased off a no. of times \+ B's horses cut up me and [illegible] needed tennis court with their tracks. Afternoon I wandered about a good deal — fixed camera in large room to take a flash light picture at night, which I did at about 8.30 —

## Right Page

### Sun. Sept. 24, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, clear m'f till about 11 o'clk — then it began to cloud over — the afternoon was gloomy \+ a little rain at night. I got up feeling better \+ went to my off. quite hopeful — After it I went out toward the w'p of Windy Brow \+ took two photo-shots at the house — on my return met B. Garvin on his way to make a call — we were on the so. porch for a long time till I felt very tired \+ chilly \+ asked him indoors — there he stayed till almost noon chatting about pictures \+ artists till I was completely tired out — then Mooby came for letters \+ I told him I w'd be ready in a few minutes — then g — went \+ got at my letters — at 12.15 Mr. Howell came \+ as I was the only one at home I sat with him till Mary \+ Katharine came — then again at my letters — mooby waited till 1 o'clk. when I finished — then I lay down \+ rested till dinner at 1.30 — after that I made up developer \+ developed two plates — one, the flash light picture taken last night — failure because the flash was insufficient — the other, a south piazza picture — failure because my tripod is so flimsy that in shoving plate holder into place twisted it out of focus. Supper at 6.30 — Eve'f spent comfortably by the fire in large room.
