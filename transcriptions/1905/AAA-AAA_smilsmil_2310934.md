## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 22, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather trying hard to be pleasant after sleety storm last night. I got up, after an uncomfortable night with so much headache that I was unable to take my usual bath. Help - at 9.30 Before noon went to studio + was busy for some time there with photo prints - then walked for a while on Park Ave. Dinner at 2 - Afternoon was engaged going over photo-negatives, numbering + recording them - After 5 o'clk. was obliged to give up to my headache - was unable to get up till after midnight when I undressed + went to bed properly for the night.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 23, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold day - to studio after 10 - divided my time between work on Cumulus after Sunset photo. + pen sketch of Times Bldg. - Think I improved "Cumulus" greatly by glazing - Before lunch went to Millers + engaged carpenter to come to studio + fit panel stretcher to the canvas of Geo. Ireland portrait - then home - back to studio at 2 - carpenter came + fitted stretcher + I tacked portrait onto it - worked awhile on pen sketch - also more fussing with photo - a short walk - then home at 6 - Dinner at 6.30 - Eveg. with newspapers \+ photo printing - till midnight.
