## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 5, 1905

**Weather:** Cloudy, mild - a little sunshine + some very light showers during the day + a heavy one between 8 + 9. p.m.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I was in the garden for a time - then on Tennis Ct. working to get a level by driving stakes + using spirit level. Dinner at 1 - + again in the afternoon the boys + I continued the work till sunset. Otis + Sam Moody under direction - Eve'g. miss Rose was Oracle + by a trick using figures rec'd. questions + made answers with knowing (?) either question or answer. After the others had gone to bed Ralph + I had a sort of jaunty chat as we sat + nursed the remnants of a fire.

Cablegram from Charl - London. Thu date - is to sail for home on Friday next.

## Right Page

### Wed. Sept. 6, 1905

**Weather:** Various showers during the day with bits of sunshine, very few of them. Otis came at bkfs'. time to report that the bridge by which he got to ledge for stone was washed away - so he went to Adlers for a job. Bashaw came + worked, when the rain w'd. permit, at levelling the Tennis Court. Moody helping him. I doubt if he was able to put in a half day. I managed to gather garden truck for dinner between showers - I wrote to Miller + to Eastman Kodak Co. + went to P.O. after 12. Mr. Maghee came to dine with us at 1 - She rode up in the midst of a shower, sitting + devise on the low truck upon which Frank, her man, hauls stone. She held an umbrella over her + was protected by a rubber blanket. Miss Rose sang after dinner. I was on Tennis court for a time - it was driven off by showers. Supper before 7 - Eve'g Maghee brought Elizabeth home - Miss Rose sang. I was in my Room + did some writing under difficulty.
