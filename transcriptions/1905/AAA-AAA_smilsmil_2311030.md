## Left Page

### Fri. Aug. 4, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant summer day - Very soon after bkf. I got started on photographic work in the studio - commencing with a reduction from a photo. of The great street in Innsbruck. I had Trouble in arranging properly my original \+ getting exact reduction size \+ focus I made Three experiments \+ the first was the best - It was after 12 when I went to P.O. \+ got back at 1 in time for dinner - Afternoon I wrote again to Wanamaker \+ sent him a check for what I owe him - but not for full amt. of his bill - The boys went out to Chapel Pond hoping to find a good bathing place - At 5 I started out to meet them \+ stopped at Otis's to learn why Rob. Otis had not put in an appearance to-day - he was not at home - Then on \+ met the boys \+ we set? together getting in at about 6 - The boys were disappointed in finding a bathing place. Supper at about 7. Evef. Jim \+ the girls went to Maghee's - Mary, Ralph \+ I Remained at home.
Rec'd. letter from Kate.

## Right Page

### Sat. Aug. 5, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Peculiar weather - Sunny misty o'f. with strong southerly wind - mild \+ enervating - After bkf. Tied up some Cos lettuce in the garden. Rob't. Otis came with Team \+ mower \+ cut the grass standing on the cor. of my land N.E. of the road up to the house - then went to place on Chapel Pond Road near W. Otis's \+ got a load of Rotten stone - in the afternoon he brought two more loads, Moody helping him with one - Ralph \+ I worked nearly all the m'f. on photo. work - Reducing some 8×10" photos. bought abroad to 4×5 for album - At 12 I went to P.O. \+ back at 1 - dinner - afternoon, more photographing \+ out superintending work on road - supper before 7 - Evef. reading "Asia \+ Europe" by Meredith Townsend - A very interesting work to me, especially, his Two chapters on the Negro Problem in the United States.
