## Left Page

### Sun. May 28, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Uncomfortably muggy + warm — a sort of misty + thunderous day — at one time in the afternoon we heard the rumble of distant thunder — but nothing came of it. Up at 7 + scrubbed till 9 — then bkft. About 11 went to studio + attended things up for an airing — went to Charles — not in — off with his French Engineer. Girard. Home — dinner at 1.30 — at 4 again went to Charles — he was just about to start for Schenectady where there is to be an interesting demonstration on the Erie Canal, before experts, of the Electric mule — then to studio — there till 6 o'clk getting out paper, preparing + + getting ready for printing to-morrow — then wandered up Park Ave. from 50 to 57 St. to see the progress of the work of sinking the N.Y. Central R.R. tracks — home at 7 — Lucy Lizzie + Jessie Smillie called + we had an interesting time in going over the Düring idiocy

## Right Page

### Mon. May 29, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm but pleasant — After bkft. I wrote a letter to Jim asking him to read Prot. Hadley's paper in the June Harper concerning Mental Fruits in the matter of Education + asking him to comment thereon — It has puzzled me sorely in directing his education — Hadley's theory seems to me excellent but it requires too much of the teacher — I want comments now from the stand point of the boy — It was after 10 when I got to studio — I got to work at proving the "Constance, Tower on the Rhine" — as promptly as possible — but delays necessary at the beginning made it possible to get only three Chinese India + one French India before going home to lunch at 1 o'clk. — back at 2.30 — I labored (working at the press is labor) till after 5 having taken half a dozen large paper India pts — They make an appearance that is quite encouraging to me — Washed up + home at 6.30 — dinner — Sue + I read with enthusiasm the news paper acts. of Admiral Togo's defeat of the Russian fleet in the Corean Straits — as yet we have no details — To bed early — very tired —
