## Left Page

### Thur. June 29, 1905

**Weather:** Beautiful day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day \- Up at 6.30 \- packed valise \+ wrote a couple of notes before bkfst \- Cab came before 10 \- Rode to station \- 10.30 Sup. to N.Y. \- got in on time, about 12.30 \- went to 110 \- had some lunch \+ then to 2° Nat'l bank \- Cut \+ coupons to leave with the bank for the summer \- It was about 4 when I got to studio \- then home \- dinner with Geo. \+ Nellie \+ later went to Char's \- Home \+ to bed soon after 10 \-

Miss A.D. Abbatt was at 110 when I got there \+ lunched with us.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 30, 1905

**Weather:** Still pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still pleasant altho' the Ther. got up to 85°. After bkfst. wrote to Mary \+ some other matters \- the mng passed without my having made much visible progress. Lunch at 1.30 \- Will Parke came in at about 2 \- surprised me by giving me a check for $350 \- payment on painting "Vesuvius from near Sorrento" \- Then to bank \+ deposited it \- then to Wanamaker's \+ bought the Kitchen utensils on list Rec'd. from Mary \- then home \+ worked awhile upon the disorder in my room \- At 6.30 went to Char's \- telephoned to Ralph at Hackley to learn how he is \- I have been uneasy about him \- he responded all right. Dinner at 7 \- Well, Charl showed some remarkable work done by his new 5×7 camera \- home before 10.
