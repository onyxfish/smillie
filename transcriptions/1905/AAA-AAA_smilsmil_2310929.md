## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 12, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dreadful day — after nuzzling \+ freezing all night it Rained heavily all day — very dark so that I could not see to paint — I spent the m'f developing a couple of Roles of films for Ralph.
Home to lunch — back — afternoon cutting masks for photo printing \+ working on negatives — After 5 went to Borrmauris \+ bought Velox paper — Dinner at 6\. Mr. J. Holme Maghee dined with Katharin \+ Elizabeth \+ afterwards took them to the opera — Mary dined with Charl \+ went with him to Symphony Concert. After dinner I spent the eve'f printing from the films that I developed for R — so that I may send him copies to morrow eve'f. To bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 13, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy — very cold at night — to studio at usual time — I prepared to revarnish the portrait of old W. Ge? Ireland but upon careful examination found that the coat I gave it a fortnight or more ago had not dried in — the trouble was "bloom" that disappeared when wiped very lightly with a soft rag \+ dampened with double distilled turpentine — Then the whole work appeared as bright as ever — Then painted on Vesuvius free after 1 — then home to lunch — back soon after 2 \+ painted till after 4 — cleaned up \+ went to 23d st. to get colors — then to Butters — then to Bayerdorfer \+ to get crystal for watch — then home at 5.30 — Rested till dinner — Eve'f wrote to Jim \+ Ralph — mailed letters at 10 30 —
