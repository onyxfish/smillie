## Left Page

### Fri. April 28, 1905

**Weather:** Overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast — with some sickly attempts at sunshine — After ten when I got to studio — until 12 o'clk. arranging the contents of a drawerfull of old pen \+ pencil studies \+ looking for a certain one that I needed —
Then I worked awhile on "A Terrace at Mentone" \+ home to lunch at 1 — back soon after 2 \+ worked for the rest of p.m. on Terrace — now I want to stick it — I think it is an interesting subject.
George came in late with the astounding news that Sheldon married a young woman out west sometime early last Feby, the matter being kept secret from parents \+ every one else till now — To my mind the fellow is a fool — graceless \+ thoughtless — a cruel blow to his parents — forgetting them, it may possibly make a man of him — I hope so. Evely I wrote to my boys.

## Right Page

### Sat. April 29, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, dark mt. with some heavy rain in the afternoon — I worked on some old pensketches — one, an old Paris sketch on the Seine near Pont Neuf, showing Pantheon dome — Home to lunch \+ after that went to Bank — Then to studio — fooled away some time on an obdurate photo negative — home to dinner — Evely Charc came in \+ the Sheldon matter was fully discussed. —
