## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 4, 1905

**Weather:** weather cleared bright \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather cleared bright \+ cold — The heavy fall of snow last night is beautiful to look at but paralyzing to street traffic — To studio \+ then went to 2° Nat'l Bank — arranged my business there, then back to studio \+ Resumed my relox printing — very slow, discouraging work — I must give it up. Home to lunch at 1.30 Jim had a friend, Cor, to lunch — Back to studio at 2.15 \+ continued printing till after 6 — then home to dinner — Evelf. newspaper — then Charl came in — Jim went to a dance with Louise Cook after having gone to Humpty Dumpty Matinée in the afternoon \+ dining with Bordman at the Yale Club.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 5, 1905

**Weather:** Bright \+ pleasant during the day — change about night fall  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ pleasant during the day — change about night fall — I went to studio intending to do some painting, \+ preparatory to that I did some clearing up — When I stood before my painting "Cumulus, after Sunset", I could not feel sure that I wanted to do anything on it, \+ so, being in doubt, I took the trick of photo printing — Home to lunch \+ back — In the afternoon I mounted a number of prints \+ worked till 6.30 — then home to dinner — Evelf. K. \+ B. went to Opera with Mr. Maghee — Mary, Jim, Ralph \+ I went to Charl's \+ he showed us a lot of new lantern slides that he has just had made — home soon after 10 —
