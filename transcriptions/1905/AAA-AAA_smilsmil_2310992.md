## Left Page

### Sat. May 20, 1905

Ralph came from Hackley

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

fine day - breezy + rather cool - To studio at 10.30 Still unsuccessful in my efforts on Constantine Tower - Took prints from it + found that the biting had deceived me, it was far too light to harmonize with the older parts of the plate - So I laid that aside + took a couple of prints from the Bavano Chapel - That too failed to meet my expectations + I went home to my lunch rather low in my mind - Found Ralph there - just down from Hackley - after lunch we went to de Marginis - apartment hotel in 31st St. + looked at the apartments that I have about made up my mind to take - then to studio at 3 - laid another dry-ground on Constance Tower, hoping to be able to bite up a strong grain - It is very difficult to see what grain is formed when it is over one already bitten - I got one that seemed to me promising but I cannot tell till the acid by its discoloration reveals the texture - About 4.30 I went up to Central Park + wandered about there till after 6 o'clk - a great display of childrens maypole parties + processions - Dinner at 6.30 - Evef. Ralph met Bundy - they went to a theatre + R - brought B - back to the house + gave him a bed - I spent the evef. at home.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 21, 1905

Ralph + Bundy ret. to Hackley.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day - Bkfst. at 9 - after reading newspaper I went to studio - then to Charl's + home to dinner at 1.30 - R. + B. took a 3 o'clk train to Hackley so as to be in time for Chapel to hear the new Head Master, Rev. Eells. At 3 o'clk. Charl came + together we went to Greenwood Cemetery + there I took two photos of our lot, to monument + head stones - light unfavorable, sun in wrong direction - but everything, so far as light, color + temperature were concerned, was lovely - Home at 6 - I went directly to studio and developed the two negatives - They came out as well as could be expected - Home at 7.30 - Maghea there - Charl came in later - To bed early.
