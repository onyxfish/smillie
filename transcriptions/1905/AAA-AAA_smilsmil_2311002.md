## Left Page

### Fri. June 9, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day — growing warmer — after bkft. went to 110 for a few minutes, then to Kimmel \+ Voigt's \+ started the printing of the Borden photo-gravure plate — there till after 11 — then to Scribner's — saw Mr. Smith who approved sample submitted — Stopped at Butler's — to have him send to studio for "a pool in the wood" — I shall send it to Eleanor Cook as a wedding present — to studio soon after 12 — At I went to 110 \+ had a cup of tea with Ge°. \+ Nellie — then to studio wrote to Mary — had a long call from O'Donovan the Sculptor — Butler sent for picture — then I worked hard till after 5 o'clk — Taking down curtains \+ piling things together — went to 110 \+ had a rest — to Charl's at 7 — dinner at 8.30 Prof. Girard came — very enthusiastic — just from a visit with the engineers to the Tunnel under the east River — He \+ Charl went out for a prowl \+ I went to bed at 10

## Right Page

### Sat. June 10, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant \+ warmer — Bkft. at 9 — bothers me headache still continues but passed off later in the afternoon — after bkfst. signed lease for apt. No. 51 — in La Touraine — then to Wallpaper shop then to Touraine — made plan of apt. \+ disposition of furniture — then to 110 \+ wrote to Eleanor Cook \+ also to her mother — lunched with Ge°. \+ his family — Vae helping me I worked all the afternoon, till after 5, Took down every picture in the house, removing wire \+ screw eyes \+ piled them up for Selleck on Monday m'g. Then lay down on my bed \+ had a nap. At 7 went to Charl's — dinner — Mr. Girard with us \+ spent the eve'g. I excused myself at 10 \+ [illegible]
