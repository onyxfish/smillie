## Left Page

### Wed. April 12, 1905

**Weather:** clouded m'g.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Katharine \+ Bessie went to Annapolis \-

clouded m'g. \- a few hours about mid-day delightfully clear \+ fresh \- overcast later \- K. \+ B. left the house at 9 a.m. to go to Annapolis to visit Mrs. L't. Emily W. Taylor \+ Kellogg. I went to studio \+ worked on plate "Chapel near Dugano" \- Had a walk before lunch looking for Hotel Walcott. \- Found it in W. 31st \+ Back to studio soon after 2 \- worked on plate till near 5 \- Voigt came with some prints of Roelker took plate \- unsatisfactory \- counts too much for indifferent printing, I think \- At 5 at Hotel Walcott to see Mrs Kent. not in \- waited half an hour \- then to studio \- home \+ to Am. Exp. office about Ralph's trunk. Then home to dinner \- After that wrote notes to Jim \+ Ralph \- 8 o'clk \- at 53 W. 47th S. \+ Saw Miss Roelker, her father \+ a daughter of Col. Armstrong of Hampton Va. fame \- I showed Miss R. \+ one of the prints that Voigt brought me \+ got her instructions \- at 8.30 I was at Hotel Walcott \+ until 10 was with Mrs. Hy M. Kent \- Saw her daughter Charlotte for a few minutes.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 13, 1905

**Weather:** clouded  **Temp:** [illegible]

Emma Bourne came from Po'Keepsie.

Clouded most of the day \+ rather cool \- To studio \+ rebit on etching "Chapel near Dugano" \- cleaned up a lot of things in my laboratory etc \- before 1 o'clk. went to Davis Bros. \+ ordered ton of coal for studio \- then home to lunch \- Ryan's men commenced work tearing out the inside of extension bath-room \- Emma Bourne arr'd from Po'k. for a few days visit \- To studio \+ got my three plates in order for printing to-morrow \- Got paper ready \+ wet down \+ home in time for dinner at 6.30 \- Well, Mary \+ Emma went to Charlie's \- he is at home used up by a rheumatic attack. I remained at home studying the problem of last years receipts and expenses with a view to plans for the future.
