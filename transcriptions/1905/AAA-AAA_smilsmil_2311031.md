## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 6, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

After heavy southerly wind during the night rain commenced in the m'g \+ fell heavily at times, about the middle of the day there was a thunder storm \+ a brief clearing - then came rain again, another thunderstorm \+ clearing before evening. Had a good Sunday soul before bkft. I had afire in my bed room \+ wrote some notes - to Nellie - to Parke \+ others - Dinner at 1.30 - after that, more writing - Ralph busy in Studio \+ dark room - after sunset, when rain had ceased I wandered about the place for some time - did not have any supper. Eve'g. walked on north piazza for awhile - then a little reading \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 7, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still peculiar weather - sunshine in early m'g then cloudy with light sprinkle of rain - one sudden \+ heavy dash for a moment - then clearing dog-day hot - cooler eve'g. Otis brought three, \+ possibly four loads of rotted stone for the road. I do not know whether or no Moody helped him as I was too busy at the house doing nothing of importance. At 11.30 Ralph \+ I went to P. O. back - dinner at 1 - Rheumatism, or what ever is the matter with me now, made me so uncomfortable that I lay down on my bed \+ had a nap - after that I wrote part of a letter to Kate then prepared to do some Velox printing tonight. Supper at 6.30 - Eve'g. printed till 10. but got no satisfactory results - the negatives developed by R. in his Contrast developer proving to be too black \+ white to please me.
