## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 10, 1905

**Weather:** Cleared slowly during the m'g till about 3 in.  **Temp:** [illegible]

of snow fell last night, it was a beautiful winter day — P&p.
before 9 — we sat chatting till after 11. Then
Ralph called me up on phone + I went
at once down to his room 537 Pierson, when
I found him + his mate Bundy — About
1 o'clk. we started to walk up to the Elkins
a car overtook us + we got upon it. Just
before lunch I went to W.C. to urinate + had
got nearly thro' when there was an intense
pain in the urethra as tho' fire was passing
through — after that, a couple of drops of blood.
I was greatly shocked + depressed — Men
came lunch — the two Murphey boys there — sons
of William's cousin — After lunch my discomfort
increased with severe pain in the urethra + swell-
ing of penis + I determined to start for N.Y. on the
5.12 Train — a surprise to the Elkins as well as my-
self — as I could not give explicit + public ex-
planations — a cab was called + Ralph + I
went to the station — train on time + very full
but I got a seat beside a talkative fellow who
kept my mind off from myself — Got to Touraine
at 7.30 found St. Gauvin dining with Jim —
I telephoned to Dr. Tuttle, then had my dinner.
Dr. T. came after 8 + to some extent relieved
pain caused by swelling of penis — He said
what he could to comfort me + I promptly
got to bed — a restless night as I had some
fever + intense pain when I had to urinate

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 11, 1905

**Weather:** A fine, sunshiny day — but I spent it  **Temp:** [illegible]

in bed — I had a wretched night —
feverish thro' most of it + suffering great
pain each of the three or four times that
I had to make water — Dr. Tuttle came
soon after 10 + said that I was making
satisfactory progress — but my feelings
gave me no such testimony except
that the passing of urine became less
painful each time till at night there
was very little discomfort; the local
swellings were much reduced — At
noon I took a dose of castor oil + in
due time was cleaned out — I got
up late in the evg + sat in my chair
rolled in my rugs — about midnight
I had a glass of milk treated with
Rennet.
