## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 22, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still wonderfully fine weather — but I do not respond to it — It has been all I could to to drag about to-day. Bkfp. with Jim at 8 — then I waited till 10.30 for young Bayendorfer who came \+ started the Fall clock in the hall — Then I went to Mauli's Storage \+ had men get out five boxes of books to unpack hoping to find a number of missing books that may have been packed in them by mistake — There till after 11 — Then to studio. Maynard came in \+ surprised me by handing me a check for $150. Then I went to bank with it — Then to Cantring \+ had my lunch. After that read till near 3 o'clk — then to Man Storage \+ had two more cases unpacked but found none of the missing books. Then to Douglas's \+ got check for $20. from sale of small safe that stood in dining room — Then to Touraine \+ slept till 6 o'clk. Jim \+ I dined at 7 — Evening reading.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 23, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Father's \+ bro. Will's birth day.

A lovely day — hazy \+ mild. Bkfp. with Jim at 8 — About 9.30 I went to Man "Storage \+ had two more boxes of books opened but found none of the missing ones — then to studio \+ to my disqust found the books I have been looking for piled away in big bookcase that I thought was full of unbound books — then to bank \+ dep. Douglas's chk of $20 for safe — then to Scribner's \+ saw Jim \+ some rare old books — then walked to Touraine \+ fixed up some lunch for self. It was about 3 o'clk when I went to studio \+ fussed there till after 4 o'clk — then back to my rooms. Jim came in at 6.30 with his friend David Harris \+ then went with him to the Murray Hill hotel to dinner. I dined alone \+ at 8.30 went to Chars — Jim came in at about 9.30 \+ we ret to our rooms at 10.30.
