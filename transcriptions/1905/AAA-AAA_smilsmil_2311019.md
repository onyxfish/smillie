## Left Page

### Thur. July 13, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still very hot, the breeze possibly having a bit more of life in it than yesterday — humidity still reported at 86° (has been 89°) \+ showers in the afternoon. I got up at 7 after a quiet night feeling weak but otherwise fairly well — just after 6hrs. Parke \+ Mr. Atherton called for a few minutes — intown to meet Mrs A — on her return from Europe — Then walked to 42d St very slowly \+ bought a hat — Then down 42d St to Bdway, then to 40th St \+ bought R.R. tickets \+ seals for to-morrow — then home \+ then worked at picking up last things — lunch at 1 — afternoon I kept very quiet doing but little — dinner at 6.30 — Eve\. Ralph \+ I made several journeys between house \+ studio carrying loads of things, returning from the last \+ getting to bed after 10 — my bowels behaving singularly well.

## Right Page

### Fri. July 14, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Scranton. W. G. Parke's — 1601 Adams Ave.

Very hot in the sunshine — pleasantly cool breeze but atmosphere still very humid — every effort causing profuse perspiration. — Thunder storms threatened — we ran thro' two, late in the afternoon \+ evel\., Up at about 6.30 \+ set to work on my trunk \+ other things till 6hrs: about 8. Then continued the work till after 11 when that was finished — Ralph in the meantime doing all the running necessary out of doors — Then I went to studio — to bank got a lot of money, all sorts \+ sizes for Bessie at home — Then to Storie's \+ Cammeyers for Bessie — Then to Simpson \+ Crawford's \+ bought socks for self — \+ home before 1 — lunch at 1.30 Then I rested for awhile — about 3 we said our good byes to Ge. \+ Nellie \+ by cars, went to foot of Christopher St. \+ took 4 o'clk. train to Scranton — Drawing room car for us — intolerably hot at first — gradual improvement as the sun went down \+ we got farther from the city — Ran thro' showers at about 5 — at 6.30 we had a fairly good dinner in Dining car — later we ran thro' a heavier storm \+ got to Scranton about 9, behind time \+ after the shower was over — thro' a misunderstanding no one was at station to meet us — we rode to Park's in a car which passed the door — getting there at about 10 — cordially greeted \+ to bed at 11.
