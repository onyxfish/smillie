## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 12, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful day to look at thro' protecting windows but windy \+ dirty in the streets. I had a fairly restful night \+ my bkfs. with Jim, or rather, after him, because he had finished before I got down. All the m'f was spent in my rooms writing \+ arranging laundry matters together with some loafing as I found myself rather light in the head \+ weak in the knees. D'. Tuttle called me up, asked how I was getting along \+ said he wished me to go to the Presby'n Hos'pital \+ have some X Ray photos taken of Kidney \+ Bladder. I phoned to Dr. Brown \+ \+ arranged to be at Hospital at 2 o'clk. I wrote to Hamburg-Am. line \+ sent check for $150. securing stateroom 88 for D'. T. to self. Lunch at Doraine \+ then to Hospital — there at 2 — Dr. Brown sent me to the X Ray Room with instructions \+ two negatives were taken — one of the Right Kidney, the other of the Bladder — I was placed upon a table — abdomen stripped — \+ something like an inverted search light was screwed down upon my belly — then the current was turned on for one minute — I left before 3 o'clk. \+ walked down Fifth Ave. for some distance — then Mad. Ave. cars. Had a nap after I got in — Dinner with Jim at 7 o'clk. — Eve'g.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 13, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very mild, sunny, hazy day. Bkfs. as usual with Jim — soon after bkfs. an old time discomfort at the "pit of the stomach" — a sort of neuralgia, began to manifest itself — It is six years or more since I have had an attack coming now, with my other troubles, it made me very blue. I took ⅛ gr. Morphia \+ lay upon my couch until 12 — then the trouble passed \+ I walked down to the bank — then to Brooks Bros \+ tried on a grey suit — not yet quite satisfactory. Then into Dorlan's \+ had some oysters. Then walked up to Studio — then to T Reed's — then to my rooms — there till 4.30 — then to Walter's house \+ met him, Edw'. Theo. — we four representing the Ireland Real Estate Ass'n \+ we held an Annual meeting, with little to rejoice over. Back to my rooms before 6 — Jim came in at 6.30 — we had dinner at 7 \+ then he went to Katharine Hardenburg's having Rec'd. a hurry call by 'phone — I podged about all the eve'g accomplishing very little. Rec'd a note from Southack's Lawyers, Cardozo \+ Nathan, 128 B'way — inviting me to pay S. \+ Co., without litigation, $600 com. for trying to sell 110 E. 38th \[illegible\]. I sent the note to Clarence Smith. about 10 p.m. while hanging a picture \+ bkfs. was on a rug that slid over the slippery floor \+ was thrown heavily striking my right side up on the end of a large chair arm — the pain was intense.
