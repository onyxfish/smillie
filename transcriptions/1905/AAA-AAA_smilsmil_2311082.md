## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 18, 1905

**Weather:** fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim went to New-Haven

fine day \- ufo \+ bkfst. with Jim at 8.
He took 9:15 Train to New-Haven to see the
Yale-Princeton foot ball game over which
there is just now intense interest. After bkfst.
I wrote several notes which took so
much time that it was near noon when
I went to studio. I finished getting all
books for Touraine into box \+ mailing it up.
Then went to Anderson's Eff. in 3rd Ave. \+
arranged to have wagon come at 3 o'clk.
Then to Touraine \+ had lunch. To Studio
at 3 \- Sup. on time. Two boxes taken
to my rooms \- Porter got there ufo after
some delay \- I opened boxes \+ took out the
books \- quite a chore for me as my ureter
trouble was causing me considerable discom-
fort. As time got along towards 6 I got uneasy
\+ at 6.30 went to Charl's to beg an invitation
to dinner \- found Thompson, his Electrical
mule man, there \- just back from Europe
with a stack of documents. He is a great talker
\+ many of his experiences, as related, were
very interesting. We had dinner at 7. I
returned to my rooms at about 11.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 19, 1905

**Weather:** A very fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim Ret'd. sometime during the night

A very fine day \- I did not get out of
bed till after 8 \- \+ had a very light bkfst.
after 9 \- then I assorted books for awhile \+
at 10.30 went to Charl's to see Katharine
concerning several matters \- They came
down to bkfst. at 11 \+ I sat with them till
near 12 \- then to studio \- there till near
1 o'clk. Then to my rooms \- I fussed about
there till near 3 o'clk \- Then I had a
long nap \- after that killed time till 7.
I am so weak \+ my hip joints have been
so creaky that it is painful for me to walk.
Soon after 7 I went down to my dinner
after 8 Charl 'phoned me that Mary was
at his house \+ asked me to come around.
I did so before 9. Looked at many photos.
C. played on his orchestrelle \- I left at 10:30.
