## Left Page

### Wed. April 26, 1905

**Weather:** mild & overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jine left this afternoon.

mild & overcast — Rain at night. To Studio at usual time — found that Jennings had done nothing in the way of clearing up the mess left by the carpenter so much of the [illegible] was spent in that way & in looking at printing rags. I worked for a time on the plate. chapel near Bavano, a soft ground etching made some time ago — I am going to put an aqua-tint ground over it. Home at 1 \+ back at about 3 — Jine left us — is going to Yonkers with Gould — Take dinner with him — Then to a dance — Return to city some time after midnight — Take 4 A.M. train to New Haven, getting there before 7 — in fine condition to commence his recitations — I am coming to the conclusion that, too generally, a college education is not learning — Jine's motto is "Don't let your studies interfere with yr College Courses". At Studio I finished with pen a slight pencil sketch of Tower of Ravello Cathedral made in May '93. — I quite pleased myself because I think it is as good as the sketches that I made 12 years ago. Home at 6.30 — Mary & I dined alone. Evely Charl came in, evidently in very good spirits. Every thing seems to be going his way now.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 27, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

K.T.E. arr? from Annapolis.

Rain till about noon when it cleared mild \+ muggy. Wrote to Katie Elkin \& W. Edw. Paris Sec. of Barnard Club after [illegible]. \+ did not get to studio till near 11 — worked on pen sketch that was partly finished at Mentone in 1892 — on a "Terrace". Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 \+ worked till near 5 — Then to Eincer Ament's \+ got acid — Then to Bridgeman's \+ ordered garden seeds for St. Hubert's. Then to Studio \+ was engaged there till 6.30 — Then home to dinner — Evely Charl came in \+ at about 9.30 Katharine \& Bessie arr? from Annapolis after a ticket that gave them immense pleasure.
