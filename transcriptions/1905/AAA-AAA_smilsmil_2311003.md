## Left Page

### Sun. June 11, 1905

**Weather:** misty \- cloudy with a shower about 6 P.M.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up at 7 \- Rub-down \+ bath \- bkfst. at 9 \- at 10⁴⁵ train to Tarrytown \- got to Hackley at about 12.30 Ralph out, but he soon came in \+ we chatted till dinner, at about 1 \- I sat beside Mr. Buck \- after dinner we had coffee on the terrace \- until after 4 wandered about with R \- air humid but soft \+ cool \- at 4.30 we went to Good hue Hall where Rev. Robt. Collyer preached a sermon to the graduating class \- but I could not wait to hear him thro' as I had to catch the 6.º² Train \- He took an old testament story of the Journey to Canaan but before he got to its application to the young of to-day I journeyd \- walked a considerable portion of the distance to station before I was overtaken by Trolley car \- a shower that came down smartly as we got to station \- Train 5 m. late \- Reached 9th Cent St. 8 m. late \- Excitement \- 48" water main in Park Ave bet. 41st \+ 42nd S \- burst \+ a veritable flood was running down the hill \+ into sub-way \- Got to Charl's at 7.15 \- part way thro' dinner \- Prof. Girard there \- brim full of enthusiasm as usual \- I excused my self before 10 \+ went to bed \-

## Right Page

### Mon. June 12, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain commenced at about 10 o'clk \- \+ continued smartly till after 6 P.M. \- I was up at 7 \+ down at 8 \- went to 110. gathered weeks wash \- took it to laundry \+ then to Charl's before 9 \+ had bkfst. Before 10 went to the house \- Selleck \+ two men there \- I superintended removing all the pictures to studio \+ at the studio it was 11.30 before they were all carried up. Then I had to arrange \+ pile them compactly away to remain till my return in the fall \- To 110 at 1 and Geo gave me a lunch \- wrote to Ralph \+ sent him $20 \- also to Mary \- To studio at 3 \- finished putting away pictures \- then gathering all photo materials to take to Keene \- As Charl dines out this evef with Prof G \- I had dinner at Georges (110) \- wrote to Jane Hinshelwood \- gathered photo. materials at home \+ at 9.30 took them to studio \+ after that to Charl's \+ to bed.
