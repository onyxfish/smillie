## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 6, 1905

**Weather:** Dull, rainy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, rainy m't. - clearing gradually till it was sunshine + pleasant in the afternoon. - I got up + had bkft. with Jim + then Rel. to my Rooms + was there till after 11 - then as the weather promised fair I went to the studio + unpacked the box that came down with us from St. Huberts. It was pretty hard work for me + when I Rel. to my Rooms, carrying a heavy bag, I was very tired - I rested for nearly an hour + then prepared some junket + had a light lunch about 2 o'clk. Then more resting - + at about 4 I went to Triplers in 42d St. to buy a hat - none to fit - Then to Raggio's, [illegible] + bought one there. Then to Buskirk's - 6th Ave. + 34th St., + bought stationery - then to Townsend - tired again - Rested - Jim came in late - dinner after 7 - Every Jim + I went to Charles + were there till 10 - looking at his Norway + Spitzbergen photos - I was exceedingly uncomfortable being in pain all over + when we got to our Rooms I got into bed as soon as possible.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 7, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Election day.

A fine day - cloudy at times - after an uncomfortable night I got up late - headache - weak + uncomfortable all over + temp. just short of 100°. Being election day + no business doing at Scribners Jim had a day off - we bkft. at 9 o'clk. After that I fixed myself with blanket + pillows on sofa + spent the m't there trying to do some reading - At 1 o'clk - still feeling much out of sorts I thought best to call up Dr. Geo. A. Tuttle on the phone - Mary + Katharine came in for a little while - About 2 o'clk I sent down to restaurant for milk + a raw egg + I made junket + had a light lunch - After 3 o'clk Dr. Tuttle came + made a pleasant + reassuring call - thinks my trouble is mainly with digestion + assimilation - He said that sometime in Jan'y or Feb'y, he + Dr. Fisher of [illegible] Hospital might take a west Indian cruise, as they have done before, + suggested that I take same trip with them. I would like it very much. Afternoon on sofa - Jim + I had dinner at 7 - mine was a very light meal - Every Jim went off to join the crowds that filled Broadway from 42d to 23d St. Geo. + Emily Cook came in for half an hour - Charl came + stayed till 10 o'clk.
