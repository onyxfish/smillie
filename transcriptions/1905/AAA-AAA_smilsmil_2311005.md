## Left Page

### Thur. June 15, 1905

Hackley Commencement.
Ralph graduated.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day — up early — about 8 went to 110 \+ dressed with some care. To G.C.S. — bought ticket — to Charls \+ had bkfst. before 9. to station \+ took 9.50 train to Tarrytown — got to Hackley about 11.30 found R— after a little trouble — I got a very good seat in Goodhue Hall — Exercises commenced about 12. an excellent address to the grad. class was made by Dean Briggs of Harvard. Carroll D. Wright spoke briefly — Minot Savage aroused enthusiasm by waving a check for $9000 that he had from Mrs. Hackley — the presentation of diplomas came last — Collyer \+ Erls, the new head master prayed at opening \+ close — at a little after 1 went to R's Room — then to lunch. after that — wandering about — very delightful under the trees — about 3 went to athletic field — The ball game between graduates \+ under class, the event — R. Took a very inconspicuous part — grads won. There were other athletic sports — It was after 5 when I left — leaving R— for his fortnight of study preparatory to Yale Exams — at station met Slicer \+ others \+ passed the time while waiting for belated train, in pleasant chat we got to N.Y. City a little before 7— Dinner at Charl's at 7.15 — Every, being tired \+ sleepy I got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 16, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A hot day but with mitigating breeze — Bkfst. before 9 — at about 10 I went to the house, then to Bornnams — then to Douglas's — some time there with Hugh, then we went to Miller's — met him in the street — more time lost — then to the house \+ there Douglas this nephew made an estimate as to cost of Repapereing \+ painting parlor floor — then I spent rest of mng selecting books — some to go to Torraine apartment. The rest to store house — Had lunch at 1 — after that, with books till 3 — then to Studio — Mr. Gaffney came \+ worked in cellar, getting out packing boxes for an hour — then to Wanamaker's \+ got shirts — Then to Pomroy's \+ got Truss pads — then to Marks \+ got vest — then to studio — then home after 5 \+ rested till 6.30 — to Charl's at 7 — dinner — Even with newspaper \+ chatting — to bed soon after 10
