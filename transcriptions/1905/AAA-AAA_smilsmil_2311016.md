## Left Page

### Fri. July 7, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A comfortably hot day — up at 6 o'clock a letter to Jim before bkfst. at 8 — After bkfst. went to Man'n Storage \+ arranged to have van \+ men sent to the house — they came at about 10 \+ worked till 12.30 \+ got a great lot of the furniture out of the house — I worked hard going from bottom to top of house many times carrying \+ lifting — I rested a little before lunch at 1 — men \+ van returned before 2 \+ carried away the remnants before 3 — The furniture of my room being gone I brought Ralph's bed \+ bedstead to my room — I wrote a short note to Mary — then Ralph \+ I went to Lock's \+ had him send up for half a dozen cane chairs \+ after he got them I went again to consult with him as to their needs. Gordon left to be away over Sunday \+ Val came to stay a couple of days — Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g in parlor in easy chairs, dozing \+ talking with Ralph about English literature.

## Right Page

### Sat. July 8, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hot, muggy, sticky, depressing — as for several days past the m't was overcast — then clearing hot (Thes. in my room 82°) Thunderstorms in the middle of the day — the edge of one passed over us abt. 2 o'3 o'clk — short shower. About 6.30 I got up \+ wrote two or three notes before bkfst. — one, a note to Jeanette Adams, presenting painting \+ of art folio of etchings to her — At 10 Ralph \+ I went to Lock's — then to bank — then to Brooks Bros. where I bought a suit of clothes — then to Arnold \+ Constable's where I bought summer underclothing — \+ back to 110 about noon — lunch at 1 — Val. left immediately after to go to the Columbia School of Mines camp — Gordon is spending Sunday at Ossining — Ralph \+ I went to studio \+ were there till 5 o'clk. packing box for Keene, Studio \+ photo. supplies — then to 110 feeling quite used up but rested before dinner \+ then felt better — Eve'g Ralph \+ I discussed education \+ athletics till about 10 — then to bed.
