## Left Page

### Tues. Aug. 22, 1905

Boys went fishing.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming \+ seasonable day — very warm with an. w. breeze that was delightful. The Ther got up to 80° in doors. The boys went fishing on Maefee's permit — they started at 5 o'clk. a.m. \+ got back at 6 p.m. with 45 handsome trout. The family breakfasted at usual time — after it I worked for more than an hour to put together the shutter mechanism that Ralph had opened after my cameras fall — I did the whole wreck up in a package (3½ lbs.) wrote a letter to W. C. Cullen \+ went to Post Office — Dinner at 1 — afternoon I worked awhile on the three negatives that I developed yesterday — I was out awhile with Adams, who returned to his painting \+ with Otis \+ Mookey who hauled four loads of road filling — Supper at 7 — Eve'g I made some prints from the three negatives — all more or less disappointing.

## Right Page

### Wed. Aug. 23, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day — a hot sunshine \+ refreshingly cool breeze — In garden for awhile after b'kf. then at work with photos — \+ first panels of pyragraphic work, stained with color — in place on my bedroom manete piece — I stained with little success, the rebbon with mattos under panels — At noon went to P.O. with Ralph — dinner about 1 — immediately after it the boys started for the valley to go to Circus \+ there have a swim — I was about with Adams who finished his work here for the present. Otis too has finished his Road filling job, bringing two loads of gravel \+ placing it at the front door — About 4 I went out along for a tramp, went to Roaring Brook fall — taking trail that led to pool about two thirds of the way to the top — there I sunned myself on the rocks for about half an hour — Then home at 6 — had a sponge off \+ rub down \+ a rest — Supper at 7 — Maefee joined — Eve'g. Bessie played piano — I read.
