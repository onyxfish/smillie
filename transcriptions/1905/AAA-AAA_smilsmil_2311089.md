## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 4, 1905

**Weather:** Dull, cold & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, cold & windy — At about 10 went to Dahlen's, 629 Third Ave. he promised to send for the Olsen Trunk this m'f. Then to studio + worked there till 1 o'clk. — Sorting books + packing a couple of boxes that I got from Hahneufeld's — Dahlen's man came + got the Olsen Trunk — I went to my Room + got lunch at the Touraine — Loafed + read for awhile after that I had a nap + then to studio + worked there as long as I could see — Then to Touraine — Read my Post — had my dinner + later Chase + Katie Throop came + spent the ev'g with me.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 5, 1905

**Weather:** Very fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day — after b'f'st I wrote to Katie Elkin + to Ralph expressing my desire to go to New Haven to spend next Sunday. Then to studio + packed away book + other things + got four trunks + two boxes ready to transfer from studio to the Touraine. I went to dock's + arranged to have his wagon make the transfer — + I went to D. B. Butler's + gave an order for a 9×16 frame — George is to paint a little picture that size for me to give as a belated wedding present on Xmas day to Carleton Cook's wife. Feeling tired, as I usuaeey do, about 3.30 or 4 o'clk. I had a nap + then a walk up 5th Ave. Dinner at the usual time + the ev'g was spent in an unsuccessful effort to get my check stub + my pass book to harmonize
