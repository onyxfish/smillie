## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 23, 1905

**Weather:** fair day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fair day - I decided that I must do something diverting or there would be little hope of my getting out of the physical hole that I am now in \+ so, the best I could think of was to make myself miserable by trying to paint a picture. I spent the day making a sketch on a 12×18 milboard of a very picturesque Roadside Chapel near Lugano, using some photos that I made last summer. To lunch at home as usual - After finishing my work in the afternoon I went to Pub. Office of Photo-miniature - 287 Fourth Ave. \+ got No. 12 - on Retouching negatives - Home at 6 - dinner - Eve'g all hands went to Charl's - Geo. \+ Gordon there - we had some music - home soon after 10 -

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 24, 1905

**Weather:** Very fine day - clear, bright \+ Reasonably cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

It was after 10 when I got to studio \+ as promptly as possible set palette and started work on 12'×18"- Chapel near Lugano - Worked steadily till 12.30 - then had a short promenade on Park Ave. before going home to lunch. Back to studio at 2 \+ painted till after 4 - then cleaned up - a gilder came from D.B. Butts \+ took inside member out of 33" × 44" to repair it. Then out for a walk - W. Messerole overtook me \+ together we walked up 5 Ave. to 53d St - then I ret'd - \+ had a short rest at home before dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g. Charl came in but I managed to get my letters to the boys written.
