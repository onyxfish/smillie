## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 26, 1905

**Weather:** Fine clear cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine clear cold day — Jim + I had bkfs. at usual time — Ralph took his time about getting up — It was about 11.30 when he + I went to Wanamakers + bought a cot bedstead + mattress for his use here when with us on vacations — from W's we went to Manhattan Hotel café + had lunch there — + then to our Rooms after 2 o'clk — Between trouble with my feet + trouble with stomach, following lunch, I did not go out in the afternoon but busied myself with little matters indoors — Jim came in before 6.30 + at about 7 we three went to the Murray Hill Hotel + had a Table d'hote dinner there — good dinner, good service, but I was in no condition to enjoy it — after dinner Jim went his way + R. + I went to bro. Charls — there till near 11 — indigestion distressing — can I continue to lead such a life? Can I live without our home comforts?

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 27, 1905

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — streaks of sunshine and streaks of shadows — Jim + I had bkfs. at 8 — Ralph, as usual, kept his bed till the m'f was well passed — I went to studio at about 11 + worked there till near 1 o'clk — clearing a portion of middle closet — making inventory of old studies + packing away large illustrated books then to Touraine — then with Ralph to Manhattan Hotel where we had our lunch — back to Touraine — at about 3.30 to studio + continued work of m'f. as long as I could see — then to my Rooms + rested for half an hour — at 7 Jim, Ralph + I went to Manhattan + had a Table d'hote dinner — from there at 8.30 we went to Charl's — he got out his lantern with its new Girard's Electric light + showed us a number of lantern slides — to our Rooms at 11. Ralph's new cot + mattress was set up.
