### Tues. Feb. 21, 1905

*Jim came home.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A bright, comparatively mild day I got up a little later than usual - Still unfit for work - my right eye in very bad condition - much of the mt I sat with an iced bandage on my eyes - At noon I walked down to the 2?. Nat'l. Bank \+ back, with my right eye closed - I felt it imperative to deposit Maynard's check - Lunch at 1 - much of afternoon, like mt., on my back with ice water on eye - Jim came from New Haven to take Miss Stoughton (Brooklyn) to a dance - He took dinner with us \+ left immediately after - Bro. ## Right Page

### Wed. Feb. 22, 1905

*Ralph came home.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A mean sort of day - overcast, raw and windy - I got up to my bkfs- as usual - my eyes somewhat better - but generally feeling still much out of sorts - Jim left to New-Haven because he had an exam. set for this p.m. - Ralph came from Hackley just before he left - R- is down with his hockey team that is to play a match game at the Jefferson Rink this evef. Lunch at 1 - after that R- went to theatre with his chum Bundy. I slept during a good part of the afternoon - dinner at 6 o'clk - to suit R's convenience as he wished to get away early to meet his team at the Rink - after the game they are all to return to Hackley. Evef. Charl came in.
