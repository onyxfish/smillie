## Left Page

### Thur. March 9, 1905

**Weather:** Dull day - Rain in afternoon & at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio after 10 - days work divided between photos. & getting etching-room sink in order for a campaign - a split in the bottom of it had to be caulked, the old asphaltine varnish scraped off & a new coat of varnish added. Home to lunch - a headache caused me to lie off for about an hour. Home at 6.30 - dinner - Welf, Mary & Katharine went to Charles - He did not start on his Southern Trip as intended owing to Rheumatism. I wrote to Ernest Roth, now in Rome. & to Mary Cook.

## Right Page

### Fri. March 10, 1905

**Weather:** Disagreeable day - dark, damp & Raw.  **Temp:** 70

To studio after 10 - day spent working over photo negatives (a great waste of time, I know) & repairing large sink in acid room - the bottom of which is cracked & leaks. Home to lunch - at studio till near 6 p.m. - dinner. Welf & H. Maghee came & took K. & E. to theatre - I wrote to Jim & Ralph & to W. G. Parke from whom I rec'd. a check this m'f (on First Nat'l, Montrose, Pa.) for $100 - on acc't for Painting "Vesuvius."
