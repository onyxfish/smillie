## Left Page

### Fri. June 23, 1905

**Weather:** Overcast, humid & warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast, humid & warm — a light shower fell after 2 o'clk — Bkfst. at 8 — Read newspapers for a short time — then to work assorting books & going over magazine files to make sure they are complete — It was after 11 when I went to Dode's & left order for him to clean flues & Repair Range — then to studio & continued work there Till near 1 — then home & had lunch — Before 2 o'clk. I went to Kimmel & Voigt's & signed 32 Jap. & 26 India pp. of the Borden photo-gravure plate — then to Wanamaker's & bought some flannel — then to Electric supplies & got four carbon batteries for Keene — then to studio, then home & struggled for a little rest before dinner — wrote to Ralph & in Reply to a note from Jno. N. Golding, Real estate broker — dinner Ever. I read to a finish

## Right Page

### Sat. June 24, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable weather — muggy & overcast with several light showers — but when I got to New Haven there was a delightful change — to clear & pleasantly cool — After bkfst. I went to Y.C. Stat. & bought ticket to N.H. then to Charl's — he Ret'd from Boston last night — home at 10 — got out valise & commenced to pack it, but many things to interfere promptly turned up — an agent from Mr. Golding to look over the house — the men to clean the flues, the boy to carry things from upstairs down, etc. etc., all had to be looked after — I had about 10 m. before lunch for a sit — while at lunch rec'd Telegram from him saying the Commons bill ($50.°) must be pd. before Monday noon. I went at once to 2d Nat'l. to make a loan as I had but a small amt. in bank — but on getting there found it closed — Sat. Half holiday — Keld. home tried to complete packing & then to Charl's — got to R.R. station at 3.45 & on train a few minutes before starting — a dirty ride to N'ot. getting there 15 m. late — Jim there to meet me but I missed him & took cab to Wm. Elkins getting there at 6.30 just in time for dinner — Ever. Chatting till about 10, then to bed.
