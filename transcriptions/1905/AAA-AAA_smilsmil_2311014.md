## Left Page

### Mon. July 3, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably hot day - up early - okf. soon after 8 - assorting books - Selleck came at about 10 + from that time till 12 we worked hard, taking a load to studio + bringing back a lot of the unbound Ill. Lond. News, Punch etc - etc - Then I had a rest after that till Lunch - After lunch Ralph + I went to Wanamakers - he to buy shirts - I to get China for Keene. I got back after 3 + continued my work among books + contents of drawers till near 6 - Then I rested till Charl's dinner at 7. Ralph + I dining with him - He is to sail on the Kaiser Wilhelm der Grosse at 10 o'clk. to-morrow. After a brief stop at Antwerp he will go on up the coast of Norway + from the North Cape across to Spitzbergen. We had a quiet ev'g. till after 9 - then home + to bed.

## Right Page

### Tues. July 4, 1905

**Weather:** clear  **Temp:** [illegible]

clear, hot day, but pleasantly cool at night. Up at 6 + started upon my day's work. At 7.45 I went to Charl's - Geo. + Ralph followed soon after - we bid C. good-bye as he left his house at 8.15 to go to the S.S. Kaiser Wilhelm der Grosse the plans to get to Antwerp as soon as possible - transact some business, then leave Hamburg, July 18. on the Prinzessin Victoria Luise to sail for Norway, the North Cape + Spitzbergen + back to Hamburg. Aug. 8 - He will then meet Kate somewhere + have a jaunt with her before returning here. Then back to 110 + had bkf. Ralph + I spent the m'g. writing a list of titles of books that I am to put in storage. Lunch after 1 - I worked all the afternoon among my books - Ralph + Gordon went to a ball game - The day was singularly quiet for Fourth of July - the old fashioned "fourth" has passed away. Dinner at 6.30 - Ev'g. I took some things to studio on my return read awhile some of Geo. Ade's Foul Fables -
