## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 21, 1905

Kate's funeral \-
Spent some time on roof.

**Weather:** Glorious day, brilliant + breezy \- not too cool \- day began as usual at 7 \- bkfs. at 8 \- at 9 Clarence Smith came + I told him just what had been done yesterday between Elliman + self + he told me of some of the possible disagreeable consequences that may follow if Master contests my right to sell to any one but him. now I wish that I had not so promptly fallen victim to that $3000 check \- it was that that was my undoing \- Smith left promising to try to arrange things between the contending brokers \- He thinks my verbal promise to Harron to sell at $60000 gives to Martin the foundation for a claim agt. me \- Harron came before 11, much disturbed but he did not abuse me \- I wrote to Ralph \- up at about noon \- was dressed \- seated in a wheel chair \- had chicken dinner \- after two o'clk. was taken in my chair, thoroughly bundled in blankets, to the Roof where Roe got me into a sunny corner, with a wide view over the city. Jessie went up with us \- then I was left \- It was a joy to be in the sunshine + fill my lungs with the bracing air \- I felt greatly benefited by it. I had a nap as I sat there \- Roe came + took me down before 4 clk \- I sat in my chair for awhile in my Room \- Dr. Trethill came + expressed himself much pleased with my progress \- Jessie had attended the funeral + came in to tell me how impressive the service was \- George came in also \- About 9 P.M. Jim came + stayed till 10 \-

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 22, 1905

Not so pleasant a day as yesterday, sunshine in the mf \- a slight cloudiness, growing more dense till the sunshine was gone + the air was cold + raw \- Roe came in somewhat late in great excitement, his face badly cut, result of being thrown from rear platform of 8th Ave. car. The surgeon dressed his wounds + he was sent away for the day much to my regret + discomfort for he has grown to be very necessary to me \- Miss Crandall did her best to supply his loss \- helped me dress + got me into my chair \- then got me + chair on the Roof \- this was before 11 \- the sun was shining feebly + there was little wind \- by 12 the sunshine was gone + the breeze was stronger + cold \- about 12.30 I came down + sat in my chair in my room \- Miss Stebbins got my dinner for me at 1.30 \- when I came down I found Mary + Bessie in my room waiting for me \- I had a good visit with them \- It was after 3 when I went to bed \- when Charl came he waked me \- I had a good visit from him. He stayed till 6.30 \- Jessie came in after her dinner + gave me a pleasant chat. Jim did not come up this evg.
