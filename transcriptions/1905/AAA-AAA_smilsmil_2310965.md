## Left Page

### Mon. March 27, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

miss Buck ret. to Po'k.

Pleasant day. To studio about 10 - painted all day on "Chapel near dugans" \+ think I have it now nearly finished - a short walk before lunch at 1 - painted till 4.50 - then washed brushes \+ cleaned up - At 5.50 went to Butters \+ arranged to \+ have flat on "Chapel" frame burnished Then home - dinner - Eveg. newspaper \+ to bed early.

## Right Page

### Tues. March 28, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \+ very warm. Official report 73° max. To studio at usual time - continued work on R-book plate - Home to lunch - P.M. worked till near 5 - Then home - made a change in dress - \+ with Katharine went to 147 E. 38th St. \+ called on Ella \+ Violet Beach. Ella was on her way to train to go to their place back of Peekskill - we had a pleasant call - Then home soon after 6 - Dinner - Eveg J went to 40 E. 70th St. \+ called on Mr. Barbyet - a chatty eveg till near 10 - Then home.
