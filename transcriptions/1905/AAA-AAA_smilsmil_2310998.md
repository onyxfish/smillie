## Left Page

### Thur. June 1, 1905

**Weather:** Pleasant but with evidences that showers are all about us  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant but with evidences that showers are all about us — Got up with severe headache \+ very uncomfortable swimming sensation in the head. I went to studio \+ pulled some prints from the Campino plate — after lunch the swimming sensation had increased so much that I thought best to go to Yale — He advised a calomel clearing out \+ an analysis of urine — Then to studio after 3 o'clk \+ pulled two or three more Campino pp. \+ home at 6 — Eveg, quite collapsed \+ head hurting. Charl \+ George came in for awhile.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 2, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

In bed all day with violent head ache — Thunderstorm between 3 \+ 4 o'clk p.m — I got up at about 7. \+ had a bowl of gruel. Geo. \+ Chase came in the Eveg. Chase had his contracts with Malinkrodt just perfected.
