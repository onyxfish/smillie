## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 9, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beast of a day — Snow, sleet, rain \+ glare ice — but I did not go out — I was barely able to get up — aching from head to foot \+ my eyelids inflamed — I spent most of the day on my bed with wet cloths over my eyes — Eve'g I felt better but did not go down to dinner — I got to work at Velox printing from my Quoakpean films Later Charle came in with an interesting letter from Kate — I did not get to bed till midnight.

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 10, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A gusty, raw day with some light snow flurries. I got up feeling the evil effects of yesterday's storm \+ was not good for much all day. I went to studio's and busied myself mounting photos. making asphaltum varnish \+ varnishing the three plates that I have in hand to etch. Home to lunch \+ back as usual — Then home at about 4.30 \+ rested till dinner — Eve'g I wrote to Jim \+ Ralph — mailed letters \+ to bed.
