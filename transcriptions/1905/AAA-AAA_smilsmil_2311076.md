## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 4, 1905

**Weather:** Superb day \- cloudless, breezy, cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Had a bad night owing to indigestion, the result, I think, of drinking a bottle of "White Rock" water at dinner. I was not able to get up + take bkfst. with Jim \- so I lay in bed + took Apenta. I was able to get up + bkfst. after 9 o'clk. During the m'f I was much out of sorts. I got over to studio + got some things + then to Hahnenfald's + bought milk, junket tablets + grape nuts \- Then to my rooms + made a good junket + ate it, with grapeuts at about 2 o'clk \- After that I had an hours nap + on waking felt much better \- After four o'clk I went to Lewis + Conger's + bought some crockery to help in preparing such food as I must have + cannot get in the restaurant. \- I read till Jim came + we had dinner before 7 \- I was careful this time to be very mindful in food \- drink no sparkling water \- my palititis seems to stick by me with little change \- A stupid evg. with a short telephone chat with Charl.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 5, 1905

**Weather:** Beautiful m'f. but clouded after 3 p.m.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I did not get up till about 8 \- then a bath + tub down + bkfst. at 9.30 \- It was nearly when Jim + I went to studio \- there he opened box from St. Huberts but I did not find some of the things that I was looking for \- Between Ralph packing things last July, possessions of mine to go to studio, + Katharine packing my trunk + box at St. Huberts, it is hard for me to find some of my smaller belongings, like shoes, gloves + some of my underwear. After 12 we acc'd to Touraine + at 1 o'clk. went to George's. 57th St. + Park Ave. I sat in my big chair on my old parlor carpet, had my dinner on my dining table + at my sideboard beef cooked on my gas range + ice-cream frozen in my freezer \- it was good but I was in no humor to eat \- Jim went to Jersey City, I sauntered part way by Park Ave. R. R. car \- then to 39th St. by Mad. Ave. car. At my rooms arranging closets + drawers \- Charl came in for a few minutes before 6 \- then I went to Freese's + got ½ gal. Londonderry lithu \- I am disturbed because the flow of pus in the urine seems to have ceased \- Jim came in at 6.30 + at 7 we had something to eat. I wrote a letter to Ralph.
