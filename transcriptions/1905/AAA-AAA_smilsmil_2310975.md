## Left Page

### Sun. April 16, 1905

**Weather:** Cold, cloudy & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold, cloudy & windy — an unpleasant kind of Spring day — several very light snow flurries instead of April showers — [illegible] at 9 after a good scrub. After 11 when I went out for a short walk — to studio — then to Charl's & home to dinner at 2 — It was about 4 when I commenced Velox printing — at about 6 I took a recess for a few minutes & walked on Park Ave. — then home & resumed work \+ continued till near 9 — then cleaned up after having made about three doz. prints — The remainder of ev'g. spent by a pleasant little fire in parlor.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 17, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Lizzie Smillie dined with us.

Another fitful, cold day — brilliant sunshine at times — then flurries of snow — a light skim of snow lay on the roof under my bed room window when I opened it in the m'g. To studio at 10 — But Velox prints between platens — the rest of m'g. preparing for aqua-tint work — reducing mastic gum to powder etc. — Before 1 o'clk. went to Century — saw Librarian & prints from book-plate — I do not like the prints of Roecker plate that Voigt has showed me or the prices he has named — Then home to lunch — to studio at 2.30 — a little work with gum mastic — Then commenced writing letter to Kate — home at 5.30 & rested till dinner at 6.30 — Lizzie Smillie with us — again at Kate's letter from 8.30 till 10 — Then took it to P. O. station H. to catch steamer early to-morrow m'g.
