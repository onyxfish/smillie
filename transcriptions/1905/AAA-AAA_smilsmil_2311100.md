## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 28, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The day opened with a pale yellow fog + tho' not cold, rather raw. Jim + I [illegible] at usual time + Ralph kept his bed as usual — I went to studio after 10 + put the mf. continuing the work of clearing out + re-packing the middle closet — Ret. to Tourraine at 1 oclk — Beautiful clear sunlight + very nice — R. was up but I did not lunch with him — I prepared a bowl of junket for myself — about 3 I went to barbers + had hair cut — then again to studio + worked there as long as I could see. Then to Tourraine. Jim dined at the Dielmanns + took Miss D— on her invitation to the Neighborhood Cotillon. Ralph + I went to Manhattaen Hotel + had our dinner — Returning to Tourraine we spent a part of the evg. hanging more pictures — the oil paintings in their old frames look dark + dingy as they have against the light buckram covered wall.

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 29, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy storm of wind + rain at about 3 a.m. The mf. misty + damp — late in the afternoon it cleared to fresher + cooler weather. All three of us breakfasted together — It was about 10 30 when I went to studio + continued my work of sorting, packing + clearing up. To Tourraine at 1 oclk + prepared my lunch — Ralph went to Mary's + had lunch with her — I ret. to studio soon after 2 + worked till 4.30 — then back, feeling very tired — I rested + had a nalp before dinner — Welf Jim dined with a Pomfret Club — Ralph + I went to Murray Hill + had a satisfactory dinner there. I ret. to our rooms — read awhile + wrote a note to Emma J.—
