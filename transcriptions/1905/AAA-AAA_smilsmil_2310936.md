## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 26, 1905

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some remnants of the storm overhead, they soon disappeared — but underfoot \+ clinging to the houses there was a fine wintry display of snow — the streets were again an interesting sight with their snow drifts \+ the men every where struggling to clear the sidewalks — After blef! I went to Claussen's, got my little painting in frame, Adirondack lake, \+ brt. it home for the Johnson Springfield exbn. Then to studio — again divided my day between photo's \+ Times bldg pen sketch — the latter I think I finished — Home to lunch — Dinner at 6 — Mr. Maghee dined with us \+ later took Bessie to the Opera. Katharine in bed with Tonsillitis — I was offered her ticket but feeling in no humor for opera I declined — Stayed home \+ struggled unsuccessfully in attempts to print from some photo negatives — to bed at 11.30 sick \+ disgusted.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 27, 1905

*The tenth anniversary of Annie's death.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unable to get up in the mtg. I was in bed all day — some fever — racking, bursting headache \+ pains all over — Sent for Yale — he gave me Heroin \+ Phenacetine to relieve pain — it was a miserable day for me. The pain lessened in the afternoon \+ I got into dozing condition which was helpful in passing time — I got through the night fairly well.
