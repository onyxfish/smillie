## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 11, 1878

Rain in the mrf. Some time getting my etching traps in order — then to work laying grounds & experimenting with acids on zinc — Dinnn at 12.30, then more biting & ground laying — wrote letters to Am. Exp. Co. & to the Hirschel — wood fires — my trunk came at last at 11.30 a.m. — Evef at 5 walked with Father to Mary's & then to Matt's — home to tea at 6.30. Evef, as usual — lounging around until 10 — then to bed — passed an unrefreshing night.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 12, 1878

Heavy fog in the mrf. clearing later — thunder showers in the afternoon — heavy hail to the north. — Still very much under the weather from in-fluenza — In big chair & blanket until about 11 — then out and loitered about until 12. Dinner at 12.45 — again in big chair until near 4 then wrote a letter to Sam. Coleman. Mother spent the p.m. at Mary's — Tea at 6.30. Aunt A. came to invite Ge. & me to dinner to-morrow — Evef. Matt & Ed came down — To bed at 10 — another night of hard breathing & broken rest.
