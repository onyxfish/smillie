## Left Page

### August, Friday 30, 1878

Beautiful but very warm
my. Invited by Miss Hilland
to go to Clifford falls with party
but had to stick to my work —
Went across the River & commenced
a foreground study of Rock with
pools of standing water — At it
until 1 o'clk — then to dinner.
After that again across the River
& finished up right study of
Rocks & overhanging trees — worked
until 6 — then to tea — Every
felt exceedingly stupid and
tired — on Piazza until about
10 then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 31, 1878

Hot & close. Clouds threatening
a shower. About 9.30 crossed
the river to continue sketch
that I commenced yesterday
my. Changed my position &
lost some time in getting stones
piled in by the riverside so
that I could place my easel
upon them. Then painted view
up the stream upon bare canvas
without drawing in or frotté. At
it until 1 o'clk. then to dinner.
At 2.30 again across the River
but could not work. Sky overcast
— spent rest of p.m. rummaging
in the woods — Tea at 6.30 —
Every on Piazza — soft, moist
breeze from S.W. presaging rain
for to-morrow — After 11 before
I got to bed.
