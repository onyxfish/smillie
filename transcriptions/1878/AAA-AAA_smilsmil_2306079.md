## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 29, 1878

Glorious day — up late — b/lp: at 8.30
made a tend to Geo. & took it & P.O.
then back & to work — finished
sketch on Jefferson's — for etching
& made a tracing of it — then out
for a short walk — dinner at 12.30
then Transferred Tracing through
press — then commenced Proving
etching that I bit in (& etched) yes-
terday — at it from near 3 until 4.30
& took & p/p — quite pleased with
appearance of etching — Soon after
5 went up to Mary's with Father —
Kate & Matt there — took Matt home
then to tea at 6.15 — Evef, making
small pencil drawings for etchings
until near 9. at 9.30 went
to P.O. & at 10, to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 30, 1878

Heavy rain in the mf. After
b/p, kindled fire in 3rd story front
room & at 10 got started on etching
No 6 " Hay Ricks near Peekskill — stuck
to it until 12.30 — then dinner —
Emma Bourne with us — at 2
again at my etching — cleaned
the plate at 4 — having worked
nit 4½ hours — think it shows
progress — about 5 out walked
until 6 — Evef, very tired &
the S.W. sky very beautiful —
enjoyed my walk exceedingly
Tea at 6.30. At 7.30 went to
Wallace Bruce's with four pen' ill.
for his "Land of Burns" — about
8.30, to Mary's — there until
9, then to Matt's — there until
9.30 — then home & to bed.
