## Left Page

### August, Thursday 22, 1878

Charming day but I am not in corresponding humor.
The miserable food we get here is playing the mischief
with my digestive machinery.
After mending broken strokes
etc — got to my work at 10.30
but managed to do but little.
Dinner at 1. Near 3 o'clk
when again I got to my study
worked very faithfully until
near 6 getting quite a satisfactory piece of work done —
then to tea — had very little
to eat & suffered considerably
pain — After tea went down
to the Widow Beede's — saw her
— Alma out — back soon after
8 — on piazza awhile —
then to bed about 10 o'clk —

## Right Page

### August, Friday 23, 1878

Charming day — in better
humor than I was last night —
It was 10 o'clk. before I went
to my work across the River —
Spent the morning on 1st study
getting in 2d painting on foliage
Soon after 12 took a little
rush through the woods to
the old Beede Ravine — then
to dinner soon after 1 — After
that again to my work across
the River — finished the painting
in on my 2d study by sticking
to it until almost 6 o'clk.
then to tea. Evef as usual
on piazza & to bed between
10 & 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
