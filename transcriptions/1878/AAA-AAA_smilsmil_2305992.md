## Left Page

### April, Friday 26, 1878

Still overcast & unpleasant — all day quite used up with a headache — Father bkfst'd with us at 8 — got to studio after visiting mother at Mr. Herts's about 9.30 — did some work on a Harper block, but could not continue long at it — Lunch in studio after going to the [illegible] — Visited the School to see how the prize drawings are getting on — found a live chorus hard at work — singing — as we came in I was almost wild with the pain in the back of my head — Took something to eat about 9, but was obliged to give up going down to see "Sleeping Beauty" at Academy of Music — Sabin my big chair until after 10 — then my headache cleared off & I went to 41 S. & to bed —

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 27, 1878

Still unpleasant weather — Father took bkfst with us — Got to studio at 9.30 — to work on block "Eaux Bonnes" — Harper stuck to it & got it done by eve'g. Lunch about 2 o'clk. a short walk — worked until after 6 — another short walk — took flowers in to Muma — Tea about 7.15 — Painted for me on lounge until 8.30 — then in to Mr. Columbus's & heard some Mozart Quartettes by Markoz (violin) Mosenthal (Viola). Fabri (Violoncello) & Mr. C (piano) Muma — Julie Spring & Miss Blake the audience — add Colman & myself — to 41 S. at 11 & to bed —
