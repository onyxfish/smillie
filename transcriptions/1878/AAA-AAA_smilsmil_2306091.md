## Left Page

### November, Sunday 24, 1878

A struggling, pleasant sort of day — BkIp. at 10 & about 1 went to Dr. Hastings' — a good sermon but must confess that I slept through part of it — after service went to studio — then to Hotel Royale & about 1.45 had dinner — after that again to studio, where Geo. & I wrote congratulatory letters to Father, who passed his 71st Birthday yesterday — after that out & walked for some time — back to studio & had tea about 7 — later went into Colmans' & chatted with them until 9 — then to 37 S[t] & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 25, 1878

Mild, overcast day — got to shop at 9.30 — finished "Two Dogs" & got at Field of Bannockburn — Bruce came in about 11 & sat for some time — I got lunch about 2 — worked as long as I could & finished Bannockburn — then fussed over mounting etchings — out for a short walk — & had tea in studio at 7 — after 8 o'cl. when I went to Anna Cook's — Ned & wife there — heavy rain. To 37 S[t] at 10.15 & right to bed.
