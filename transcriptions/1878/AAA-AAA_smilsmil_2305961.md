## Left Page

### February, Saturday 23, 1878

Dark, foggy day — got to
studio about 10 o'clk — part
of my, mounting some
half and paper stretching
pf of "Farm Lane" — Then
painted on 6×10". Shaded
"Spot" to give to Horace Rusch
as a wedding present —
Lunch about 2 — Mr.
Thos. G. Hunt called at
4 o'clk — a little later I
went to Y.M.C.A. & called
on Gifford. Satterlee & Robbins
electioneering. Walked
until near 4 — then to
studio & had tea. Mot
awhile — then to N.A. &
shortly after to "Century"
there until near midnight
then to 44th St — & to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 24, 1878

Cloudy windy & wintry — Up late
& barely got through by 1 p — in
time to go to Hastings' church.
Heard him preach from a
number of texts in the old & new
testaments — After service, walked
down to studio — then to Gifford's.
3rd ave. & 23rd St — did not see him —
then to "Century" with Stoddard's
hat (he wore mine away last night)
then again to studio — note to father
& at 4 o'clk went to Mr. Thos. G. Hunt's
116 E. 39th St — Met Geo. there. Dinner
about 5 — at table until after
7 — then Mr. & Mrs. H. Miss Rawson
& Geo. went to Dr. Taylor's & I went
to Horace Robbins' — 137 E. 60th St.
a very pleasant evening there
until after 10 — then walked
down to 44th St — & got to bed
about 11.
