## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 26, 1878

Cold, windy, overcast day — to studio at 9.30 & to work on "Burns' monument at Alloway". Got along with it very slowly — went out to lunch — at 2 & had a walk — worked most of p.m. on pen sketches — then mounting etchings — then down to Centtiny — back at 7 & had tea — then read papers — mailed to Mr. Whitney & at 9 again mounting etchings. Worked until 10 — then to 37th St. & got to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 27, 1878

Wind & heavy rain from about 11 o'clk. until after I went to bed at night. Got to studio about 9.30 after stopping at Seenie's about etchings to go to Boston & at Miss Durfee's about etchings to go to Cleveland. Then to work mounting etchings & did nothing else until after 2, when I got them ready & took them over to Miss D — then got my lunch & to studio — tried to do a little work on Burns' Mon. at Alloway — but could not see — then planned in charcoal for awhile as "Mon was made to mourn" — Tea about 7 — after that worked awhile mounting more etchings — then read & at 9.45 as there was a let up in the rain I went to 34th St. & got to bed at 10.15.
