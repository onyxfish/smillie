## Left Page

### December, Thursday 12, 1878

Breezy with disposition to snow squalls — At 9.30, at Steins
in unsuccessful attempt to do some shopping for Matt — then
to studio & from there down to Harpers —
Spent the rest of the mf there with
Parsons talking over Lake fee drawings —
Had my bill arranged & got bal. of
$48 due me — To studio at near 1 —
then out to lunch — Back and
commenced a sketch of Paradise
Bay — Kate Parker made a long call
the first time I have seen her since
the death of her husband — At 6 went
to Anna Cook's to tea — Fanny & Will K.
there — the first night this season of Miss
Arnolds singing club — At 8.9el. & I attended
May Hall — Miss Degretti's concert in and
of her woman's home — met there Emma &
I & her mother — went home with Mrs —
then with Emma — then, at near 11
to Union League Club — there for about
half an hour — then to 37th St & to bed
at midnight.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 13, 1878

Very pleasant day — about 9.45 when
I got to studio — In the mf finished
Paradise Bay — out to lunch at 1.30
after it, a walk around Madison
Sq — then again to work — commenced
combined 3 drawings — By 4 o'clock had
finished to give me view of Torque Mt —
At 4.15 Gee & I started for Roselle
by invitation of Miss Vick Howe — On 3d St
met Mrs. Doc. Tiffany on her way to
Plainfield & took her in charge — in a
hurry to catch the early boat we missed
Emma & Fanny who were waiting at the
ferry house — they saw us & giving us the
slip — took 4.45 train to Roselle while we,
waiting for them, took the 5 train — and
found them waiting for us when we got
to the house — Tea near 8 — then I made
starch paste & pasted some photo's in
Miss H's album — At 9.30 we all went
down to depot — glorious moonlight
night — Anna — Fanny — Gee & I took
7.53 train — & by elevated R.R. got
up town about 11 — I got to bed at
11.30.
