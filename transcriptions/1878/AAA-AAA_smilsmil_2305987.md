## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 16, 1878

Overcast at times, but not unpleasant — got to studio
at 9.45 & spent the whole day in making a finish
of my cleaning up which extended into every hole
& corner — Lunch at 2 —
At 5.30 I went out to make some purchases, returning
at 6.30 — met Anna Cooke at the door — soon after followed
by Nell & Fanny K — & [illegible] —
then commenced a jolly time
we had tea about 7 — everything
altogether informal — about 8 —
or soon after, Mr. & Mrs. Coleman
came in & until after 9, the
group sat in the shadow of
the gas-light reflection and
chatted — then the window
was opened showing the moon-
light — then & there the ice-
cream was served — after
that songs, duetts & trios —
then dish-washing until
about 11 — then walked
home with Anna C — then
to 41 S = & to bed before 12.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 17, 1878

Clear & very nice — got
to Studio about 9.45 feeling
very headachy — much of
the m[orning] was spent fussing
over the stretching of a 33 × 44
canvas — Lunch about 2 —
then set palette & with pal.
knife painted a little sketch
a travestie on the Broad School
for Emma J — Painted over
the frame to make it harmonize
with the picture — I call it
"a Spasm in Dark Blue" —
was so much pleased with
my success with the knife
that I smeared over a 10 × 14
canvas, first with the knife,
then worked over it with
the brush — I think that I got
some effects worth getting —
Tea about 7 — Every did
some mending & a little before
10 went to N.A. — walked home
with Miss Durfee & then
on to 41 S = & to bed before
11.
