## Left Page

### November, Monday 18, 1878

Another miserable day — Raw +
rainy — Up late, with a very sore
stomach — [illegible] at the Hotel Royal
at 8.30 — 10 when I got to Studio.
sometime before I got to work
on Alloway Kirk — then Bruce came
about 2 o'clk — I prepared for my-
self some gruel + beef tea — worked
awhile on Kirk but gave up about
4 as I could no longer see. Bruce
again came in with later photos,
engᵍˢ etc etc — About 9 had a
light tea — Remained about
studio until nearly — then to
Louis Tiffany's — there until
after 10 — then to 37ᵗ Sᵗ + to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 19, 1878

Pleasant m[illegible], overcast later in
the day — again about 10 before I
got to studio + to work on Alloway
Kirk — interrupted by callers —
about 1 went out + walked around
Madison Sq. — got an oyster stew +
returned to my work + at it as
long as I could see — then with
[illegible] to Century Club — there until
then to Studio + had tea —
after that I studied out accts.
Finishing W.C. Ill. Cat. — mats
until 9.30 — then to 37ᵗ Sᵗ + did
not get to bed until nearly 11.
