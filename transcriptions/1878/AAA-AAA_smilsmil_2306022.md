## Left Page

### June, Thursday 27, 1878

A very warm day — aftn
bkfst — took a walk &
then, about 10 o'clk. got
to work, mounted paper,
prepared the Anthony block
& commenced a device
sketch of subject that is
to go on it — Dinner at 1.
Matt spending the day
with us. Afternoon, finished
sketch & undertaking it,
commenced another —
About 5, heavy thunder
storms — over soon after 6.
Sea at 6.30 — at 8 went
to Gonip's & got my sketch
box — at home, gabbed,
& read & made a little
& got to bed about [illegible]
