## Left Page

### February, Sunday 17, 1878

Pleasant day — Blbs — at
9.30 — went to Mr's service
at Dr. Hastings' & heard him
preach from Isaiah 21st ch
10th + 11th v — I must confess that
I was so tired that I went
to sleep during the sermon
Walked down to studio — met
Chas. Isham & took a short
walk with him — To the ping
at 2 & took dinner with them
at 3.30 again to studio — at
6.30 had a light tea — at
7.30 a great fire broke out in
23d St near the 7th ave — went
there — grand sight — tremen-
dous crowd — there until 9 —
then to studio & made slight
charcoal sketch on canvas
24 × 40 — then to 41st St &
to bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 18, 1878

Pleasant winter day —
After going to Knoedle's, buying
colors & looking at pictures, got
to studio & at 11 commenced
painting sketch, first night's
[illegible] upon a canvas 24 × 40 — I
used big brushes & slapped on
the color at a great rate — Did
not stop for lunch until after
2 & about 6 got my sketch
finished — I think it has been
a very good practice for me.
Tea at 6.30 — washed brushes,
wrote a couple of notes & got to
N.A. Council meeting at 8.30 —
at 10.15 again at studio —
wrote a note to Mr. Adams
of Utica & a Regret with sketch
for Miss Aitken — Got to 41st
abt 1.30 & right to bed.
