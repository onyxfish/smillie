## Left Page

### May, Monday 20, 1878

Raw, wet day — Got to studio a little before 10 & to work on "Thompson block," S. Philips well" — outlined on gelatin & transferred it — My head bothers me — worked badly. A short walk in the mizzle then back to lunch about 2 — Did but little work in the afternoon — Some on block of [illegible] & some on S. P's well — Made an outline pen sketch of West Haven games for Annie C — Tea at 4 — at 8 to N.A., no council meeting for want of quorum — In school & gallery & council room until 10 — then to studio. Finished long letter to Bellows — then to 37° S. & to bed just before 12 —

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 21, 1878

Rain in the mf — clear off, before 11 — with clouds & sunshine the rest of the day — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on Thompson block for Harper — S. Philip's well. Did it work with much spunk — Lunch at 2 — then worked until after 6 — then out and walked until near 8 — charming twilight — Tea at 8 — every in studio — wrote a note to Jno. J. Meir — accepting his invitation to New Haven next we? — at 9.45 to N.A. — To 37° S. at 10.30 & right to bed.
