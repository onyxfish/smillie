## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 16, 1878

Not able to get out of bed owing to a headache until
11 — then to Studio fixed up a little — for myself &
eat it about 12.30 — in
the afternoon tried to
work but was unable.
Roughly planned a
water color picture 10×14.
Had tea about 6.30 &
at 9.30, with Coleman &
geo walked down to 51 W.
18th St. where the Water Color
Socty held its Janᵞ meeting.
Very full. 28 out, much
interest in the matter of
salesman (or woman). T.H.
Smistn played an underhand
part but he gained nothing
by it. Adjourned at 10 —
C. Ge°. A.C. Anderson & I
walked up town together.
Talked about 10.45.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 17, 1878

Partially overcast. Studio
at 9.45. Commenced out-
lining 10×14 "Self: in the
Chemung Valley". Many
interruptions — a short
walk about — but after
2 before m had lunch.
After 3 before I began to
wash in my sky — lost
light about 4.30 — tea
at 7 — It was 8.30 before
I got away from Studio, then
to Renners — then to 127 &
dressed — It was 9.20 when
I got to W. 58th. Huntg — 116 E.
39 — a Reception — Miss Redfield
of Phila there — 11 o'clk before
I left — at 11.30 when I got
to bed. Slight fall of snow
during the night.
