## Left Page

### May, Sunday 26, 1878

Charming day — Bkfs at 8 — at 9.20 went over to 9th ave elevated R. R. & got to Battery at 10.15 — Geo & I loitered there until 11 o'clk — then boat to New Brighton — Heard Geo. W. Curtis Read a Sermon on the observance of Truth — then walked home with the Jessups — Dinner about 2 — after that we went across lots to a place where we overlooked the kills — Jersey flats & the Orange Mts — Delightful breeze — back to tea at 6.15 — thence by 7.05 boat (which did not start until 7.20) back to N.Y. at 8.30 I got to studio — first went in to see Emma for a few minutes — then wrote a letter home — got to 37° S— about 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 27, 1878

Pleasant but quite warm. Got to studio at 9.45 & to work on laying in an 33×44 — lower Ausable lake — Painted in sky [illegible] & down to middle distance — very much interested and as much encouraged. Lunch after 2 o'clk — Several callers but continued painting until 6 — then cleaned up — Tea at 7 — at 8.30 — N.A. Council — there until 10 — then to Saml. Colman's Piano Quartette — at 10.45 home with Anna & Geo C — then to 39° S— & to bed at 11.30.
