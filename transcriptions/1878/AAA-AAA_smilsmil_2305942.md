## Left Page

### January, Monday 14, 1878

Very foggy & dark mng. Got to Studio after 10, having stopped to see about [illegible]. Worked a little while on the Orchard — then, with oil, a little on 4½×10½" "Solomons River etc" — then commenced tracing large drawing — weather cleared like a Spring day. Out for a short walk — stopped at R. M. Pratt's house to see a drawing by Inghram. Lunch at 1.45 — worked on tracing — Board Meeting W.C. at 4.30 continued until 6.30. Then out for a short walk — tea at 7 — at 7.45 went to N.C. with Wood & Melinart for a short time — then Council — abq m all went to Artists Fund there until 10.15 — then to Studio fixed fire — to 41st St. at 11 & to bed abt 11.40.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 15, 1878

Pleasant day — grumbling headache all day. Got to Studio at 10. Finished tracing & commenced outlining with pencil — a hopeless job for me to get finished in time for coming W.C. Subjects. So I worked badly — much time lost by calls — lunch about 2 — then worked at outline as long as I could see — at 5 went to Renner's — then to Burrall's then to 127 — dressed & at 6.30 to Annie Cook's to dinner — Fanny K. & [illegible] there — at 8 took them to Miss Arnold's 40th St. & Mad. Ave — there as my headache dismissed me I [illegible] to 127 & at 9.30 went to bed. [illegible — ~2 words]
