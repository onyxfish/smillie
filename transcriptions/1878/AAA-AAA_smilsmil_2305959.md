## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 19, 1878

Clear & cold, got to studio
at 9.45 — W. J. Turrell gave
us a call — he & aunt to go south
to-morrow. S Falford of Chicago
& Word Mr Thompson called — I
got to work on Placid Lake
& fought all day over the sky.
Lunch at 2. In the afternoon
Sutton of the Aldine called —
Tea at 6.30 at the Kimball's
There until 9.45 — then to
N.A. 1 — studios about 10.20
wrote awhile — to 41st St at
11.30 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 20, 1878

Snow in the m'g — but it all
disappeared before 11 — some
spitting rain during the day.
Got to studio at 10 & to work
on Placid — going over part
of sky — distance & water —
A number of visitors took my
time in the m'g — Father
surprised us with a flying
call — After 1 I went out for
a short walk — lunch at 2.
Worked until 4 — then painted
on a little sketch that I gave
to Emma D. years ago — At 6
took a rest on lounge — tea at
7 — at 7.30 went for Fanny K.
& took her to Coleman's house
where we had a musicale —
Exceedingly enjoyable —
After the entertainment took
Fanny home — then home — it
was 11.45 when I got to 41st
St — Right to bed —
