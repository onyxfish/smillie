## Left Page

### September, Thursday 5, 1878

Overcast m'f. Soon af [illegible]
& crossed River & spent
the m'f. Repainting study
of Running water — I did
not succeed in making
much of it but bettered it.
Back to dinner at 1 o'clc
After that sat for awhile
on piazza chatting about
Walt Whitman — then
to my work at River, the
first painting — Evergreens
& moss topped Rock, worked
until near 6 — Tea about
4 — Charming moonlight.
Some excitement because
young Anderson was in
the woods until nearly —
Every on piazza and
to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 6, 1878

Clouded m'f — Crossed River
& to work on study looking up,
while in the midst of my work
ab't 11 o'clc. was surprised by
a very sudden & heavy shower
that fairly ducked me — Soon
followed by clear sunshine, when
I repaired damaged & again
got to work but at 12.30 another
shower warned me off & I barely
got my things packed up when
it came down heavily — went up
to hotel in a pouring rain which
lasted for an hour, then clouds
came down to the valley & hung
so for the rest of the afternoon & very
Dinner about 1.30 — After that Miss
Hillard Read some of Walt Whitman
to me — very much interested — then
on piazza holding my head until
5 — then wrote to Benjamin — Tea
at 6.30 — Every on piazza until
after 11 — To bed about 11.30.
