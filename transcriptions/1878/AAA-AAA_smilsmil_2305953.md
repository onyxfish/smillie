## Left Page

### February, Thursday 7, 1878

Beautiful day — Father breakfasted with us at 8 —
got to studio about 9.20 and to work on cow picture — Paint
ed over landscape, trying to get some freshness of color
& freedom of touch. Lunch in studio at 2 — in the
afternoon later had several callers & at 4.45 geo. & I
got away — At foot of Deberry St. at 5.30 & on ferry boat found
Geo. & Anna Cook — Walter & wife — Mary Chas. Collins
Millard, & Valentine — on the train
Will & Fanny K. & Anna D. — all
went to Roselle to Miss Howe's
where about 7.30 we had tea,
Singing & chatting until near
10 — then to 10.13 train — to N.Y.
at 11 — all Agus rose up in 6th air.
car — to 41st St. at near 12 and
to bed right away.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 8, 1878

Dull mth. Rain beginning
about 11 & continuing during
the day — Got to studio at 10
feeling so unlike work that
I could do nothing — so I
just fussed about until
12.30 — then prepared lunch
ate it at 1 and at 2 at Phil.
Rehearsal — Jennie & Florence
I weedy there — After Rehearsal
walked up 4th Ave. with Mrs.
K., Fanny — Miss Howe & Anna
Cook — went to N.A. with A.C.;
Sumner J. joining us — There
until 6 — then to studio — tea
at 6.30 — on lounge until 8 —
Sat and made until after
9 — then to 41st St. & to bed
before 11. From 9.20 to 10 —
at N.A. galleries. [illegible — ~3 words]
