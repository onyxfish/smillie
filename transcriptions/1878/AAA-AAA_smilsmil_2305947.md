## Left Page

### January, Thursday 24, 1878

Crisp day — milder than yesterday, with less wind. Nearly 10
when I got to studios — then to
N.A.D — there until near 1 — the
Hanging Com., Magrath, Church
& Baldwin getting pictures
into place — To studios to lunch
father came in about 1 and
stayed until after 3 — when
we rel[?] to Co — I then went
to N.A. — there until dark.
Took a short walk — then
to studios — Rested for awhile
— tea at 7 — then wrote a note
to J.W. Pinchot, stating that I
had not yet painted the picture
that I would wish to go to Paris
to Represent me — 8.30 when
I got to N.A. — there until
10 — to 44 S. & to bed at 10.30
Fred & wife occupied their
new quarters there — [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### January, Friday 25, 1878

Pleasant m'f — mild & overcast
later in the day & a mizzle of rain
at night — To studios a little
before 10 — then to N.A. of D. There
until after 12 — Messrs Pinchot
& Jno. Taylor Johnston called there
to see me about getting together
a W.C. Exhbn for Paris. About 12.30
to Swain Gifford's & Abbey's — to
studios at 1.15 — lunch — then
to Scribner's — Avery's & Knoedler's
about blk. & white work for the Exhbn
got to N.A.D. at 5 — soon to studios
— on Lounge for an hour — tea at 7.
About 8 went to N.A. there
until 10 — then again to studios
— there until 11 — then to
41 S. & right to bed.
