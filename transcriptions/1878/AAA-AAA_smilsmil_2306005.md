## Left Page

### May, Friday 24, 1878

Pleasant day — got to studio's about 9.30 — after 10 went to Scribner's & saw Mr. Drake — no work for me at present — then to Appleton's & saw Bunce — no work for me at present — then to Harper's with blocks, stopping on my way at Mr. S. Jessup's — saw Mr. Parsons & got my money $157.00 — then to studio's about 1.45 & lunched — loafed about, feeling unlike work until 4.30 — then to Da Rochi's & he burnished up my teeth. Overcast, raw & windy — back to studio's at 6.30 — tea at 7. about 8.30 to Anna C's — Quartette piano playing until after 10 — Anna — Emma Mrs. Coleman & Miss Stone — then to 37: 8= & to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 25, 1878

Pleasant day — got to studio's at 10 — Commenced painting 9×12 sketch from 33×44 "Andover, unsuitable lake" — I worked very closely — lunch at 2 — Mr. Sanford Gifford called — Painted until 7 — then got my sketch finished and I am not to begin the big one — Tea at 7.30 — cleaning up until near 9 — at 9.15 went to Gilmore's Garden opening with Thomas's orchestra — hoped to meet Emma & Anna there — did not — Concert over at 11 — then to 39: 8= & to bed at 11.30.
