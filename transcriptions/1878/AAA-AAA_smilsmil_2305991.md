## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 24, 1878

Very disagreeable m'f.
Raw & misty — improving
somewhat during the day —
Got to Studio at 9.45 & to work
on W.C. Worked over sky
& battered until lunch
abt 1.30 — then again at
it getting the paper
covered — Tea at 7 after
a walk — At 8, with Geo.
went to W.f. Hotel & spent
the evef until 10.30 with
Mr. & Mrs. Wm. Hammond
(S.C.A.) of Portland Me — then
walked up to 41' S: & to
bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 25, 1878

Rain commenced about 9
& fell heavily during most of
the m'f., Cloudy & misty after
noon & evef. About 10 when
I got to Studio — McEntee there
for an hour — worked on W.C.
Lunch abt 1.30 — continued
W.C. work — Father came in
about 3 o'clk — just down from
Po. with Mother & Kate — Spent
the rest of the P.m. with us
& took tea in Studio. Scribner
came in & made a long call
— anxious concerning annual
meeting of N.A. Short walk
before tea at 7 — To 41' S:
at 8 o'clk. & soon after 9, Geo.
& I at W. Rob'. Gordon's — 7 E. 38' S.
— stag party for artists etc —
