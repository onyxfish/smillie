## Left Page

### December, Friday 20, 1878

Very fine day — got to studio at to 30 — worked on sketch in oil 10×14 — Burning leaves in Nov. Hoped to finish it before lunch but did not — Lunch at 1.20 — after that walked to Phil. Rehearsal — after that, did some shopping then to Tenth St. Studio bldg & called on J. G. Brown to learn what I could concerning the rejection of George's pictures by the Artists' Fund — Then to find Durands + Deweys. To studio at 6.30 — then tea Every, note at note to Mr. Adams of Utica, telling him of the feeling aroused by the report that he had arranged to have a number of foreign pictures in the coming Utica exbn. Read it to Bellows, my aunt, Smth & Huntleff — to 37th St at 11 to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 21, 1878

Snow all the mof. After bkfst went down town — to A.B.M. Co — to copperplate man (Shark) to Queen's printing house & got 2 copies Island of Burns & then to Harper's — there until noon — got $50 — then to studio — found card from Mr. Geo. Whitney — at 1.30 went to Albemarle & lunched with him — back at 2.30 — snow changed to heavy rain — tried to work on drawing for A.B.M.Co, Amy of Sandwich Islands — too dark — did not go to Mr. Rich Butler's lecto. At 6 went to the Kimballs. Ls Howe there. + Evef went to Phil. Concert — thine home — good concert. went home with the K's + in for a few minutes — at 11 walked up to 37th St — no rain — To bed at 12.
