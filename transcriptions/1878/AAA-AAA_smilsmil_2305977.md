## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 27, 1878

Threatening weather with some light, spotting rain.
Up early & went down home to bkfst. After that I got
to my printing — things did not go right at all & I
could not find the cause of trouble — at 11.30 went up
town & bought tubes of Raw Umber & Van D. Brown — back
mixed them with my black ink
& found an improvement —
Dinner at 1 — again at my
work — about 5 Uncle W. aunt
A. Emma & aunt Jessie came in.
I was so used up with headache
that I could take no tea & went
to bed & was unable to get up
again — Mr. Meltsie & Kw. W.
Lawrence came in the every.
All had a good time celebrating
the 47th anniversary of the wedding
of father & Mother — which does not
really take place however until
Friday night the 29th ins.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 28, 1878

Very mixed with fog and occasional heavy rains during
the day — after a very bad night
I got up & had my bkfst — soon
after the others and shortly after
9 was able to get to work on my
printing — the difficulties of
yesterday seemed to be overcome
and I took much better proofs.
Dinner at 12.30 — worked
faithfully at my proving
& at 6 o'clc. for 2½ days
work could show just 26
India & Japan paper proofs
& not half of them what I
wished — this does not include
half a dozen or more that I
destroyed — Tea at 6 — every
lounged in Mother's room &
at 9.30 went to the Post Office
— back & to bed at 10.15.
