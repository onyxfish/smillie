## Left Page

### June, Sunday 23, 1878

Overcast until late afternoon but charmingly fresh — My
went with Ge° to the Baptist
Ch, & heard Rev. Mr Hull, of
the Baptist Tabernacle Ch, in
N.Y, Eph. 1' ch. 6° v. Dinner
at 1 — after that a short
nap — then wrote to Parsons —
Anthony & Calverey & got
written part of a letter to
Ferguson — At 5 went out
& walked until 6 — then
in to tea — Matt & Ed came
All went off to Ch. & I was
left alone in the house
with "our new girl" — I
finished my letter to Ferguson
& to bed at about 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Monday 24, 1878

Pleasant April-like day
with some heavy showers.
After b'kfst — walked out to
Arrola's — Rec'd letter from
J. W. Richards & block from
Anthony — To work soon after
10, finishing up an etching
of which there is no drawing
for father to work from. Got
it done by dinner time
at 1 o'clk. Afternoon
worked on Harper block,
"View from Anthony's Nose" —
Tea at 6.30 — Werf. —
Walked up town with father
& Ge°. Went to a man in
Garden St; & ordered brass
fixings for sketch box that
Tombs is making — then to
Mamie's & walked home
with Kate & Ge° — to bed
at 10 very much out of sorts.
