## Left Page

### December, Thursday 26, 1878

Very cold & windy, fine day —
Charle left early for N.Y. "Bkfp"
at 8.30. I got started on etching
altho' feeling somewhat headachy.
At 10.30 walked to P.O. Right back
& at work until 12.30. Then dinner.
Got my plate in bath & had
first bite — then etched until
near 4 o'clk — could not see
& very tired — so stopped work
— Walked from 4.30 — until
near 6 — called on Mary —
Home to tea soon after 6 —
Eve'g. Rec'd formidable looking
Reply from Mr. G.W. Adams of
Utica — Spent some time
writing an answer to him.
So bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 27, 1878

Snow in the m'g — very mild
sunshine at 10 o'clk — 8.30 when
I had bkfp — after that fixed the
parlor fire — at 10 went up town
to Matt's with Geo. Posted Adams'
letter to Renner & Co — also my answer
to him — Back home & at etching
by 11 o'clk. Dinner at 12.30.
Laid Rebiting ground for Geo —
coached him & attended to my
own work until 4 — at 4.30 went
out & walked until 6 — then in
to tea — beautiful fire in the
parlor — all enjoyed it — latter
had a lace on the ice this m'g &
is quite lame. At 7.45 I went
to Baptist Ch. Children's X mas
festival for Kate & brought her
home — Pleasant fire in parlor —
wrote answer to Nellie Jacobs
& got to bed a little before 11.
