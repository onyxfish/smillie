## Left Page

### February, Saturday 9, 1878

Miserable sad of a day.
Rain in the m'f. In the
afternoon a sad of clear up
followed by thick weather
again. During the m'f I
was perfectly unfit for work
— altho' my head did not
ache it was useless — so after
many trials I gave up and
spread myself upon the lounge.
Belle & Mary J. called —
Lunch at 1.30. After that I
forced myself to work & painted
over an old study that I made
at Hillside years ago — Tea
at 6.30 after a walk around
the Park — at 7 went to Mr. Hydes
for the J's & took them to Phil.
Anna C. had given 9e. a ticket —
he took the S's home — I went
to studio — fixed fire & read
paper until near 12 — then
to 41st St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 10, 1878

More miserable weather.
Cold & wet — Up late & help=
about 10 — went to Dr. Hastings'
& heard him repeat an old
sermon — Ex. 29° c. 34° v. Re-
garding the shining of Moses'
face on his Return from the
mount. After service 9e. & I
went with Charl. to his lodgings
— then 9 & I to Hertzels. Found
them at the table — Came not
there but a Miss Poole — Item
until 3 — then to Studio — I
read a letter — then on lounge
until 6 — At 6.30 went in to
Emma J's & took tea — At
8 took her to Boorman's & I
ret'd to studio — made until
10.30 — near 12 when I
got to 41st St. & to bed.
