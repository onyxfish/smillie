## Left Page

### October, Sunday 13, 1878

Glorious day — clear as a bell
& cloudless — Wrote awhile after
b[reakfast] —, then out for a walk &
did not get to Baptist Church
until just about 12 — Walked
home with Kate & had dinner
at 1 — After that again
writing — & then a saunter
out of doors — Tea at 6 — But
interested reading Kismet
so that it was late before
I got to church — Walked
home with Kate & Father —
Read a little more Kismet
& to bed soon after 10 —

Wallace Bruce called on
me in the early eveg.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 14, 1878

Still very pleasant, but variable —
at times overcast — been clear &
very warm — Got my Lake George
designs off to Parsons and then
about 11, commenced on tinted
board a pen sketch of Caldwell
with washes of opaque white.
Dinner at 12.30 — Worked all
the afternoon on drawing
& got it finished — Took a
short walk with Father before
tea — Tea at 6 — Read Kismet
until 9.30 — Finished it —
then out & walked for ¾
of an hour — then in & to
bed.

At 9 a.m. I called on Wallace
Bruce.
