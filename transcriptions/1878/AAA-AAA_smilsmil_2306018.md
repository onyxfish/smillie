## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 19, 1878

Glorious mng. Up at 7 and went home before 8 had bkfp -
After that transferred etching
subject to zinc - About 10, went
to Fox's lot & commenced work
on a study of some oaks (water col.
15×22). Will & his family came
to me during the mng - To Dinner
at 12.30 but did not get it until
1.30 - After that anab - then
again to mng study & finished
outlines - then into Livingston's
woods & found a foreground -
To tea at 6.30 - About 8 Will &
I called on Bob. Palmer for half
an hour, then back home. Matt
Ed. & Mary there - Some singing
& at 10 Father had an old fashioned
"family worship" - At 10.30 Geo. &
I went home with Mary & stayed
there overnight - The Dr. had
Rd.? from N.Y. by Mary Powell -
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 20, 1878

Clear & very warm - Up at 6 & got
home at 7 & had bkfp - alone before
8 - During the mng. at work
in studio on various things, but
most of the time making a
slight drawing for R.R. subject
to be patched on to a locomotive
already eng.? by father -
Dinner soon after 12 & at 12.50
Will & party got away from the
house to return to N.Y. by the
"day boat" - Soon after, Charl.
arr -, having come up at the boat.
I finished letter to B.N. co.
drawing - With father, Geo.
& Mary went up to Mary's
before tea - Home & had tea
a little before 7 - & with
Matt & Ed. came in - lounging
about until near 10 - then
to bed.
