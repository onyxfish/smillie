## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 3, 1878

Another hot day, but with a pleasant breeze later in the day. The m[orning] was spent between M. C. sketch & photographing. Dinner at 1 — a nap after then reading of papers &
a little writing. At near 5 we started out again for a ride; W. T. M. P. & G[e]t & J. We drove by Halman's Mills & Lake Mahausic, through Yorktown & home about 7 — a storm in the west gave us some very welcome shade. After tea, on piazza until about 10, then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 4, 1878

The ever glorious fourth & hotter than Tophet. Up soon after 6 — Bkft. at 7. 30 & at 9 at work on M. C. sketch & stuck very closely to it for the rest of A. M. Dinner at 1 — before we left the table a very heavy thunder storm passed over us — & during the rest of the P. M. — storms kept ale about us, now & then giving us a dash. I finished sketch, used a lawn-mower & fussed about with cats & chickens all the rest of P. M. Tea soon after 6 — were on piazza until after 10 — the place being pleasantly lighted with Chinese lanterns, these, with the burning of some magnesium wire, constituting our 4th of July celebration — To bed at 10.30.
