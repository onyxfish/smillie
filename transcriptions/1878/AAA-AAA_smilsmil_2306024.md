## Left Page

### July, Monday 1. 1878.

*Shrub Oak — at J. C. Nicoll's —*

Very hot day — after an early
b[r]kft — a walk — then worked
on Anthon[y']s block with color.
Dinner at 1 — Did little
after that but waits for train
time — Packed small bag —
hack came at 4.30 then
Geo & I went to depot & found
a new Fine Table just in force
tho train for us before 7.35 —
Telegraphed to Nicele — then
after some wandering, went to
Matt's where Geo. & I had tea.
At 7.35 off for Peekskill — got there
at 8.35, found N. waiting & had a
charmingly cool ride out to
his house, 6 miles, mostly up
hill — got there about 9.45 —
Had a sangarea & chatted
until after 11 — then to bed.

going north.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 2. 1878.

Another very hot day — Up at
6.30 — brkft — before 8 — then much
of the mf spent with Nicoll
photographing — I commenced
a water color sketch — Dinner
at 1 — then a nap — then
laying off on piazza — About
4.30 Mr. & Mrs. N — Geo & I — got
off for Lake Mahopac — Drove
over very quietly, suffering some-
what from heat & dust — Drove
around the lake — wild, unused
road — sunset soon after — charming
cool ride home — got in after 8 —
then tea — then looking over
photos — & chat until 10, then
to bed.

going south
