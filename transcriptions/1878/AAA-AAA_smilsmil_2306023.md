## Left Page

### June, Saturday 29, 1878

Yet warmer weather — a walk after b'kf't. Then to work on 2° design to ill. 1' v. of Longfellow's Preludes. Finished it soon after dinner — Then traced & continued it on the block. Uncle W — aunty J — Emma came in during the p.m — Emma stayed overnight. Tea soon after 6 — about 8.30 went up to Wallace Bruce's — There until 10. He gave me the order to illustrate his Poem of the Land of Burns — Home & to bed before 10.30.
