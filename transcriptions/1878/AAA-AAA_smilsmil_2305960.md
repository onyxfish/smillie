## Left Page

### February, Thursday 21, 1878

Very disagreeable day —
Rain at intervals all day
long & very heavy Rain with
wind at night — Got to
studio a little before 10 —
After some delay — To work
on Placid — Painted over
several h. corner — & spread
the light somewhat — I
went out after t for a short
walk & at 1.45 had lunch
Worked as long as I could
see — a walk after 6 — &
tea about 7 — then until
10\. for preparing pts for my
Etchings by monday, &
also, went to N.A. — & at
10.30 to 41st S — Horrible
driving wind & Rain.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 22, 1878

A mild day & wind & floods
of Rain — Got to studio at 9.40
& had a gone quiet day on
my Placid — Painted over
the foreground, trying to
save all the dash of the first
laying in, adding solidity
& only so much finish as was
a necessity — Lunch alone
at 1.30 — Got lunched with
Waller & Co. — I worked until
near 5 — there for an hour
clearing the Room — About
6.30, Smillie & wife came
in & we had tea with an
accompaniment of Rain at
the sky light — they left
at 8. I made awhile —
went to N.A. at 9.30 — Saw
Reinhart & had a talk
with him — To 41st S: at
10.30 to bed abt 11.
