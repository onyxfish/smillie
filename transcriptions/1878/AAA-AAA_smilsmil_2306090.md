## Left Page

### November, Friday 22, 1878

Horrible driving wind & Rain got
to studio at 9.30 found Rob. [illegible]
waiting for me. I drew from him
for 1½ hour for illustration for the
Mountain Daisy — and by 1.30 had
completed the drawing. At 2
went out to lunch. Dec. Tiffany
came in during the morn with
my Phil. tickets from the [illegible].
I gave rehearsal ticket to Miss
Abbatt — At 3 went to Stern Bros
to do some shopping for Matt — then
downtown to Roto Ing. Co & to Skelton
the die cutters & to Charl's — wrote
a note to Matt — Back to studio
soon after 6 — after calling at Colman's
to see how he was getting along —
Tea at 7 — Wyant came in to stay
until 8 — then I wrote to Col. Clark
about etching club. Got to 34th
St. & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 23, 1878

Still disagreeable weather —
got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on "The Meadows" for Bruce — it
was after 2 o'clk. before I went
out to lunch — back & worked
as long as I could see — Camp
came in at twilight & made a
call — At 6 o'clk. Ge° & I went
to Kimball's to tea — driving
rain — Lib Home there — they
fanny "Lib" — Will K. — & I went to
First Phil. Concert. much
interested in new conductor —
reverend of — Remenyi on the
violin was very wonderful —
Galassi's voice, very robust
& rich — the storm had abated
so that we walked to & from the
concert with comfort. It was
after 11 before I got to 37th St. and
midnight when I got to bed.
