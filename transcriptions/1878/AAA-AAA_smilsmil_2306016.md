## Left Page

### June, Saturday 15, 1878

Pleasant, but very warm — out during [part] of the [morning]. made some [new]
sketches for Chas. Parsons — Dinner before 1 — then finished three sketches —
On half Hudson — Lake Raquitte
& on the St. Lawrence —
Renumerate my letter & sent
to P— as he desired me to
that a selection might
be made for [illegible] for Harper's
Weekly — Out again for a
walk — made a note to
Miss Abbott — Tea at 6.30
Charl expected by 8 ocle
but telegram came instead
saying explanations would
come by mail — [Evet?] went
to Wallace Bruce's — not in —
then a walk & home —
Matt & Ed at the house —
To bed about 10 —

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 16, 1878

mild, overcast day — myself
went to Christ's Ch., [illegible], &
heard Rv. Mr. [Jeirgeufus] — a very
good sermon from Job 11 ch.
7th v. & also part of Luke 11th 2d —
enjoyed the service — Dinner
at 1 — After that, a nap, then
spent the rest of the p.m. in
writing letters — to W.T. Richards,
J.C. Nicoll, Dr. Mulford, Smiley,
Smith & Emma Cook — Tea
at 6.30 — [Evef], after taking
my letters to P.O. went to
Baptist Ch — walked home
with Father & Kate & [part] of
the way with Mary & the Dr —
To bed at 10.15. [illegible — entire passage]
