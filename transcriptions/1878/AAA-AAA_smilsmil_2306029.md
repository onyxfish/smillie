## Left Page

### July, Thursday 11. 1878.

*New York City*

Charming fresh mng. Got to H.
R. R. R. depot for 8.30 train — 40 m.
late — had to take 8.35 slow train,
got to N.Y. at 11.10 — went to da Roche's
— he was out — waited for him 25 m
I then was obliged to go without
seeing him — then by Elevated R.
R. to Park Place & to Harper Bros —
Parsais just gone to his lunch —
waited half an hour for him, then
went & got my own lunch — back
& found P. Paid for page block —
gave me a com. to get up a
large geo article — then to Gag-
nebien's with my watch — Main
Spring broken — then to Anthony's
& hunted over a lot of photos for
subjects to ill. The land of Burns —
found but 2 out of 15 — then to
Brooks Bros — to Johnston's — to
Knoedlers — to Putreans & to barber at 5.20 when I had hair
cut & head shampooed — then at
6 to studio — there for ½ an hour —
then to Mrs. Jacobs in 28th St — &
had some tea & got to depot
just in time for 7.30 train — got
to Po. at 9.40 — Rain as me
passed up the River — got
home a few min. after 10 — &
to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 12. 1878.

Cool, rainy day — In the
mng. went up town & until
after 10, attending to messages.
Feeling tired I did some
loafing & wrote a letter to
Horace Robbins — Dinner
at 1.30 — After that wrote
to Colligan. Picked up
some things with a view
to packing & getting away.
Tea soon after 6 — Wet, still
raining — went to Bruce's, not
at home — then to R. R. depot
to learn about trains to take
geo — got home about 9 — &
to bed at 10.15.
