## Left Page

### July, Sunday 21, 1878

Charming mf. but I got up about 10 o'clc. with a vanilla headache. had a light help — a Sacred picnic party went on the meteor to Paradise Bay — I did not feel able but about 11.30 went to bed again for an hour or so & had a refreshing little nap — Dinner about 3 — heavy thunder shower passed over us; one earlier had gone just to the north of us & which we got a sprinkle — other dashes followed later — I wrote a letter to Nellie Jacobs — Tea about 8 — the Paradise Bay party returned unanimous that they had had a good time — Eve'g. in the parlor & semi-sacred singing & playing on the piano until after 10, then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 22, 1878

Up at 6 — Bkfp. at 7.15 — air cool — sky clouded & delicious looking, but at 8.30 I got away (92° too) on the Meteor, having a small boat in tow, after several stops, about 11.30 we got to Blk. Mt. then the Meteor ran & left me to an island off the South end of Torque Mt. & left me in my little boat — I stopped for about an hour in the lee of the island & got sketches — then pulled out, the wind so heavy that at times I had very hard work — had my lunch about 2 under shade of Turtle island — Rain me in light showers, but I got quite a number of bits that will be useful — enjoyed exceedingly the quiet and freedom — at 4.30 the Meteor came over to French Point & I pulled up to her — went over to Blk. Mr. Point & got geo. then down the lake — very squally with rain — got to R.R. Ho. dock about 7.30 — Tea right away — after that, dressed myself and the even'g was spent in the parlor, the young folks playing games — at 10 o'clk. left there dancing & went to bed.
