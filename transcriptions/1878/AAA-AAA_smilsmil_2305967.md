## Left Page

### March, Thursday 7, 1878

Very mild & spring like —
Got to Studio at 10 & spent
the rest of the mtg minting
copies of my note to nominating
com. & letters to press men —
At 1.15 at Anna Cook's — 14
W. 38' S — & took lunch — then
until 2.30 — then to Studio —
Tried to paint on "Placid"
but my head troubled me
& I spent some time in the
lounge — At 7 went in to
Preston Spring's & saw Fred J.
for a few minutes — Spring
shower with lightening —
Tea at 7.40 — at 8.30 went
to Kurtz Gallery — first exhibit.
Am Artists Ass'n. Met a
number of friends there —
dft also the Studio — fixed
fine.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 8, 1878

Still beautiful spring weather.
Goes to Studio about 9.30 —
to work on Placid — painting
over what I did yesterday
in the lower l. h. corner —
worked abt all the mtg.
Lunch at 1 — & at 2 went
to Phil. Rehearsal and
enjoyed it exceedingly —
Walked up 4th ave. with
Belle & Mary J — went in
to N.A. to see how the distrib-
ution of pictures was getting
on — then to Y.M.C.A. — & at
last to Studio & had tea about
6.30 — near where I went
in to Mr. Colman's — Tiffanys
there — old & young — Mr. & Mrs.
Mitchell — Mr. & Mrs. Kirtcham — the
Tweedys & Miss Reynolds — all
went up to Colman's Studio
for a little while — then came
at 11.30 — waited on Miss Reynolds
to Miss Haines' — then to 41 · S =
& to bed about 12.30.
