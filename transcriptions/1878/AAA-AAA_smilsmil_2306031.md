## Left Page

### July, Monday 15, 1878

Very smoky day — after bkfst —
got to work on Anthony block
giving final touches and
trying to get it a little nearer
what I want — gave it up
at 11 o'clk & went into town
& attended to a number of
little things. Dinner
at 1 — after that puzzled
for some time over a letter
to Anthony — didn't want
to tell him my block was bad
& at the same time wanted
to let him know that I was
not satisfied. Packed the
block carefully & took it to [illegible]
before 6. Tea about 7 —
Judson Throops & bride to tea,
evety — some time spent dropping
the house for Eastman's funeral.
After 10 brought all my things
down to parlor — To bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 16, 1878

Fort William Henry Hotel.
Lake George, Room 86.

Bkfst — at 7.15 & before 8 got to
work packing trunk which I
finished just as the Exps. man
came for it — Bid goodbyes &
got to 11.15 train — uninteresting
ride — so smoky that we could
not see the Catskill Mts — got
to Saratoga at 2.25 — after eating a lunch & prepared at home
35 m. to wait for train — went through
grounds & verandas of U.S. & other
hotels then to Congress Sp. & drank
then to R.R. & off at 3. Arrives tales
at 4 — pleasant stage ride of 9
miles to the Lake — then at 5.45
Tea at 7 — after that a short walk
— met W. Jas. Harker — spent the
evety with him on piazza —
evety very cool — To bed at
10.30.
