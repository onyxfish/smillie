## Left Page

### April, Monday 8, 1878

Clean, bright day, but my head, very uncomfortable —
got to studio at 9.30 — tried to begin work on large Water
Color — a view in Chemung Valley but had to give help — then
Rode down town to Harper's
& saw Mr. Parsons — got from him
$36.00 & half a dozen subjects
& blocks — got back to studio
about 1 o'clk — after stopping at
Juant's galleries & had lunch —
During the afternoon — Traced
on gelatine one of the subjects
Had tea at 4 — at 7.30 — Ex-
com. of Etching club met in
my studio — at 8 — the club —
at 8.45 went to N.C. [illegible]
there until 9.30 — then again
to studio — another speedily
pleasant, informal conversation
Gifford, Coleman, Miller —
Stilleans & Reinhart taking
part in it — Broke up at
11 o'clk — then to 41 S. and
to bed shortly before midnight

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 9, 1878

Pleasant but — gradually
changing until eve'g — Rain
about 9 P.m. got to studio
about 10 o'clk — & to work on Harper
block — at 1, lunch — W. Hurd
Lewis called & invited us to
dine with him & W. Whitney
at the Albemarle at 5.30 —
worked on block until then —
a very troublesome architectural
affair — there to the Albemarle
& had a very pleasant dinner
& visit — at 7.30 Messrs Lewis &
Whitney went to the Avery Sale
& I went to the K's — found
them still at table — a C.C.
there — a pleasant evening
followed — at 9.30, I went
to studio — fixed fire & then
[illegible] to the K's — at 10.30
walked home with a C.C. —
Raining quite hard — then
to 41 St — & to bed at 11.
