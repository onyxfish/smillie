## Left Page

### June, Monday 3, 1878

Raw & overcast, clearing later to be very warm —
After 4½p — to Reusci's & spent some time overhauling acct —
then to Diering's — then to Burggrafs — then, at 11 — to
studio — Much time spent in doing very little in the
way of getting things together & in order to leave town for
the summer. Lunch after 2 — then to Kneedleis & made
some purchases — back soon after 4 — Mr. Mrs. Cambs
came in — Tea at 7 — at 8.30 — last Academy Council
meeting — Pleasant evening until 10 — then to studio —
carried some of my duds up to 37° S. at 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 4, 1878

Muggy, thick mf. followed by clear, very warm weather — nitta
afternoon, again overcast. After
4½p — Ape & I went to Diering's
clear & spent some time repacking two trunks of J's studies
etc, to be cared for by Reusner.
Then to Burggrafs & to studio
soon after 10.30 — then to work
picking up & packing — very
slow about it — Academy [illegible]
set? — Anna C & fanny [illegible] —
called about noon. Ran in
to E. J's for a moment at 1.30 —
Lunch after 2 — Cambs came
in soon after 4 — Tea at 7 —
at 8 called on the Shuttleffs
in their room — then to 37° S.
with a lot of things — then to 41's.
(D's) & to studio again at 10.
To 37° S. at 11.15 & right to bed.
