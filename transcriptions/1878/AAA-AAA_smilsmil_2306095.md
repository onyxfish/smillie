## Left Page

### December, Monday 2, 1878

Beastly, wicked day of wind
& rain — Bkfst at 8 o'clk — tht's
had a hack at the door & we took
the 8.46 train to NY. Got there about
10 — I went at once to Photo. Eng.
Co — then to studio — got there
after 11, very wet. Worked on
Burns' mon. Edinburgh until
1.30 — then to lunch — About 3,
weather suddenly cleared —
I lighted gas at 5 & worked until
near 6 — then out for a walk —
back at near 7 & had tea —
evely wrote to father et —
At 9 meeting to Colman's — Mr.
& Mrs. Pinchot there — left at
10 — got to 37t St at 11 — after a
stop at studio & then to bed —

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 3, 1878

charming mtf, got to studio
at 9.30. Finished Burns' mon — at
Edinburgh & then to work on design
to "ill." There are grander fields to fight
where man proclaims a brother's right.
To lunch abt 1.30 — got design
done about 3 o'clk — then to Photo.
Eng. Co — then to Greene's Printing
Establishment — very much dis-
satisfied with the printing — then
to Charles — & to Willis — back to
studio about 6 & tea at 6.30 —
most of the evng spent amusing
etching pls. to varnit so that
I may get them out for sale —
To 37t St — about 10.30 — at tut 11
when I got to bed — Rain
during the night.
