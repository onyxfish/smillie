## Left Page

### April, Saturday 6, 1878.

Beautiful day — afterbl[oo]p-
went to Met. Academy of Fine Arts
in Central Park near 80th S. &
there met Mr. Calvert Vaux and
son — climbed over the entire
building & was immensely in-
terested — got to studio about
11.30 & found card from A.S.S.
inviting me to join a walking
party for this P.M. — at 1 o'clk
went in to Triton Spring's and
lunched — Minna not there —
At 2.30 at Harlem R. R. Depot &
there met Minna — Helen Butler
Anna Gee & Wallin Cook & M.P.W.-
C. — & Valentine & Chas. Collins —
went up to Fordham & from
there walked to Spuyten Duyvil
& took 5.43 train down to 30th S. —
got to studio at 6 & had a rest
on lounge for an hour — then tea
at 8 o'clk — at the last Phil. Con.
for the season — grand affair —
[illegible] & Edith [illegible] there alone —
Put them on their way home, &
then to Century — Reception
to J. G. Chapman — walked
up with Colman — Tiffany —
Saltau & Ge — about midnight
stopped at studio a few minutes
then to 41th S. & to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 7, 1878.

Blowing & overcast — up earlier
than usual, expecting to go to
Pawkabo to spend the day,
but not quite early enough —
B[illegible] — at 8.30 — After that walked
down to studio, after a call at
Anna C's — Being very tired
spread myself on the lounge
& fell asleep — waked at 11.30 —
went then to Dr. Hastings' and
heard part of his sermon —
then again to studio and had
lunch there. — at 2.45 at A.S.S.
took her & Edith W. to "Century"
then to N.A. leaving there at
5 o'clk — then to studio — another
nap — tea about 7 — then read
some Stopford Brooke's Sermons —
to 41th S. — about 10.45 and
right to bed —
