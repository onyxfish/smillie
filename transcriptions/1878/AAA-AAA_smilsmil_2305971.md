## Left Page

### March, Friday 15, 1878

Pleasant, but overcast —
got to studio before 10 &
after a multitude of little
things got to work on 9×12
sketch "from Owl's Head" &
painted over the middle
& foreground. Did not
lunch until after 2 — then
learned, through Ge., that
J.J. Williams does not like
"Placid" & will not take it —
Quite upset by the news —
went to Anderson's — not in —
then to South St. & called on
Homer — then to 41 St. and
dressed & at 6.30 at J. Shaw's
— 5th ave. — a party of 12 to tea —
I at the end of the table —
Miss Scrypser on L & Miss
Mullen on R — ho — pleasant
time — very games —
with letters &c — to 41 St.
soon after 11 — very near
12 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 16, 1878

Charming, fresh, Spring day.
At 9.30 at 171 W. 10 — called on
Clarence Cook — very graciously
rec'd — an hour with him talking
very generally over art matters
& particularly of my relations
to the W.C. Socy. Then to
Avery's & spent some time among
his pictures — then to Cottier's —
then to Knoedler's & at 12 to
Studio, having made a mfg.
of it — Lunch at 1.30 & the
p.m. — was passed in writing
on Society matters — the near
approach of the election & the
coming conflict with Smith quite
unfits me for my work — Tea
at 5 — about 9 went to Century
— then to 171 W. 10 with a letter
then to Davite's — cor. Bdway &
12th St. — where Avery "opens" his
Latham collection of paintings —
large company — Delmonico
supper — at 11.30 I walked up
to 41 St. & to bed at 12.
