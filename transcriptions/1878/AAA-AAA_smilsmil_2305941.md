## Left Page

### January, Saturday 12, 1878

Charming day — late, as usual —
did not get to studio until
near 10 — Ella & Maud D. Bu
Mulford & Mary Tweedy called
I worked some on Orchard.
Taking out most of what I
put in yesterday P.M. Mary
J. gave us her Pie, tickets — I
went to Fanny N. with one — then
lunch about 2.30 — Nellie J. —
posed for an apron for me —
At dusk. Harper of The Graphic
called to see about illustrating
our N.C. System — Tea at 4 —
At 8. at Phil. Enjoyed the
concert exceedingly — Fanny
sat with us — Geo. & I walked
home with her & W. ell K. — then
I went to studio & fixed
fire . then to 41st St. &
to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 13, 1878

Mild, spring-like day. overcast
at times — heavy rain at night.
Blch. at 9.30 — then to studio —
back to Hastings's & heard him
preach from Cor. 13th ch. 11th v. —
After service, walked with Ge.
up to Char's — ate there back
to Hotel Royal — Dinner at 2.30
near 4 when I got to studio
again — napped on sofa &
felt homely out of sorts — went
to Dr. Bellows & heard him
preach from 1st Cor.
2d. c. 9th v. "Eye hath not seen,
neither hath it entered into
the heart of man" — etc. etc. —
Very few out — a very dolorous
service — back to studio at
9 — there until 10.30 —
then to 41st St. & to bed.
