## Left Page

### January, Saturday 26, 1878

Pleasant day — after going to Studio, at about 10, went to N.A., There until after 1 making pen sketches for Ill. Cat. To studio & lunch at 2 — back at 3 & worked as long as I could see. Then walked up to 41st S. — Changed linen & dressed & to studio & tea at 7 — at 8.30 to N.A., & at 9.30 with Nicole, went to Century — there until 11, then to Lotos — there until after midnight — walked up to 41st S. with Clark Bell and to bed about 1.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 27, 1878

Pleasant m[or]n., overcast after 11 o'clk. Rain after 8 P.m. Up late & had bl[ea]k? ab[ou]t 10 o'clk. At 11 went to Dr. Hastings' & heard Rev. D. Sheldon, a western missionary — from a text somewhere in Joshua, I think, "There remains much land yet to be possessed", or something of that kind, he gave us a very interesting lesson in geography; especially concerning Alaska — after service walked with Chas to 48th S. Then to studio — then to Hotel Royal & had dinner at 2.30 — at 3.30 again to studio & had a long nap — Tea at 7 — then note to Father & at 8.30 went in to the Dreland's, there until 10.30 — Chas. Collins there — then to Studio — fixed fire & at 11.15 to 41st S. and right to bed.
