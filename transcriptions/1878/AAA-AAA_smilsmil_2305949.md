## Left Page

### January, Monday 28, 1878

Rain in the mng but cleared about 10 — growing colder +
windier all day — to studio at 10, then to N.A. at a general
work but especially making pen
outlines for catalogue — Ed. Megon
+ others called — I did not get
to studio to lunch until 2 o'c
Back to N.A. after 3 — there as
long as I could see — then to
studio — then to Knoedler's —
then a walk + to studio at
6.30 — wrote to Darley — tea
at 7 — at 8 at N.A. Council
meeting — after g ment up
to the galleries — there until
10 — then again to studio,
wrote until 11 — then to
41 St. + right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 29, 1878

Pleasant, but cold — to studio
about 9.30 — to N.A. at 10 + found
that Church + Magrath had put
up much of the work in the Black
+ White Room altho' I asked
them to leave that until I was
present. I feel it to be an intentional insult to me. I insisted
upon some changes — but the
situation between Church +
myself was made very dis-
agreeable — at 12.15 Rushed
over to studio + I walked part
of a role — then back to N.A. —
there until dark, working
part of time in pen sketches
for catalogue — Abbey last this
one I did after P. Moran on his
way to Brooklyn last night —
at 15 to Kimball's — tea
at 6.40 — at 8 — 9 o'c + I took
[illegible] C + Fanny K. to then Club.
I went to N.A. meeting for a
little while to anatomical lects
+ Mr. Wood went to my studio
with me at 9.30 — at 10.30 I
went to 41 St. + to bed.
