## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 22, 1878

Glorious day — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on "how [illegible] in block" — "Sycamores" — worked until 12 — Uncle W. & Aunt a came in — took a light lunch & at 1 o'clk at da Roche's .65 W. 54° S. — In his chair until 3 o'clk — 3 cavities filled. Then felt as though the whole family had been in my mouth — walked down to studio — Tried to work but much distressed by my mouth — Tea at 7 — Evert in studio looking over photos — wrote a note to Wash. Nelson — Affuring to W. Earle "Rough Sport in the Yosemite" for $250 — Then to N.A. for a few minutes — 9 37° S. at 10.30 — Uncle W. & Aunt A. there over night — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 23, 1878

Another glorious day — got to studio at 10 & to work in blocks for Harper — Finished all before lunch at 2 — After lunch, in Coleman's Room about etchings — Miss Abbatt called & with Miss Jacobs spent part of afternoon in my Room — I charcoaled a 33×44 — Adirondacks — "Lower Ausable Lake" — very much interested & kept at it as long as I could see — Tea at 8 o'clk — Evert wrote a letter to Mother congratulating her on her 69th birthday which takes place to-morrow — Then, at 10.30, with Geo. went to Grand Central dépôt — mailed letters — Then to 37° S. & to bed before 11.
