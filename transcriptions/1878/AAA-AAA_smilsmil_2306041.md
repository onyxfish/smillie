## Left Page

### August, Sunday 4, 1878

warm day, with a shower that lasted from 3 to 5 - B.H.
at 8.30, the mvy. Slipped away, studying Adirondack
Routes with Mr. & Mrs. Eastman Johnston - who decided to go
over to Schroon Lake to-morrow. Dinner about 3 -
more wasted time & about 5 o'clk. Mr. Cyrus B. took
his bro. Richard, Mr. & Mrs. Johnston, Mrs. Watrous & son
Geo. & I over to Ticonderoga &
back by 7 o'clk. Then tea
every on piazza & in parlor
Clowe and [illegible] Reggie
again - then I packed my
Harper drawing & wrote
a note to Parsons - To bed
at midnight -

## Right Page

### August, Monday 5, 1878

Very fine day - Up early & about
9 got away on the meteor - Mr. Cyrus
- Mr. & Mrs. Rich'd. Butler, Mr. Schuyler
& Watrous & Mr. Smith & Geo. the party.
Stopped at Meadow & Belc Mt.
points, went into Paradise Bay
& nosed about among the islands
& about noon got to Little Green
Island where the Manhattan
chapter boys of the Alpha Delta
Phi have their camp - was rec.
by Wheeler Edwards & H.f. Mann
very cordially & sketched about
the camp until about 3.30
when the meteor came for me.
Thunderstorms all about but none
came over us - Ran over to Bolton
& N.W. Bay - to French Point &
Belk Mt.; then to Rogers Rock
at 7 - Tea at 7.30. Pretty, a
dance in the parlor - Herman Butler's
birthday - cake presentation - To bed
at 11 - Tremendous thunderstorm with
wind & hail at midnight -
Johnston went to Schroon Lake in the mng.
