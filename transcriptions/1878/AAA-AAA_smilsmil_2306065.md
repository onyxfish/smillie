## Left Page

### September, Friday 27, 1878

Clear, crisp m'g. Took a rub
down with alcohol & salt bath.
B'kf't at 8.30 — at 9 went out
& walked until 10. Then
worked with little purpose
or result over Lake George
sketches — Dinner at 12.15 —
again at work on plans
for Harper Lake Geo. Ill's
until about 5 — then to
Mary's — she is preparing
to go to the Dr's home to-night
on a short visit — Home to
tea at 6.30 — again at Mary's
about 8.30 — with Matt & the Dr.
went to R.R. Station with her
& the children & saw them
off in 9.35 Train — to get to
Geneva at 7.30 a.m. She
has more pluck than I
have, to start alone on such
a night's ride with two little
ones. Home & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 28, 1878

Still beautifully clear & cool.
After B'kf't walked out to
Goule's — then down to River
& gave Yelverton, the turner, an
order to turn for me a pair of
Indian clubs — then along the
Riverfront to Blast Furnace,
& home at 11 o'clk. Worked
on pl. of "Goucha" for a B.N. Co.
The Dr. came to Dinner at 1.
Telegram from Mary — arr'd.
at Geneva, all Right — After
dinner worked upon design
of Arms for Va — for a B.N. Co —
until near 5 — then walked
with father until 6. Tea
soon after — Ev'g. at 8 —
went to Matt's for Kate.
Home at 10 & right to bed.
