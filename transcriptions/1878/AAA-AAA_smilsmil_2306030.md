## Left Page

### July, Saturday 13, 1878

Overcast mtf., clearing ff very pleasantly — very much used up with a head ache. Part of the mtf. writing to Walter Bruce — Parsons — etc — then to P.O. + other chores — back about noon — Dinner at 1, again up town + about 4 gave up to my headache + on bed for an hour — news rec'd of the death of Mayor Easthvan at Denver — Col. — Uncle W., aunt a., Mr. + Mrs. Daig came to tea — Mang + the Pl — his br. Judson + bride came later + after them Mrma B. + Mr. + Mrs. Dudley — they stayed until about 10 — lovely moonlight night — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 14, 1878

Very warm day, mitigated by a pleasant breeze — mtf., went to Grace (Epis.) Church + heard a young man who mumbled his words so that he could not be understood. — Dinner at 1 — then spent some time sorting things + getting them together to pack my trunk — Som after 4 took my book + stool and went to the woods near Fox's. There until near 6 — then home to tea — Svelf alone with mother — Sat up on the balcony + did nothing until I went to bed soon after 10. [illegible — entire passage]
