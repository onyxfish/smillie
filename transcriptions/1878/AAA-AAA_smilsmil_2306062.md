## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 17, 1878

Charming day — such a horrible headache that I did not get
up until 12 o'clk. — Then up to eat a light bkfst — then to Piney
Knoll, sat there awhile, then to Stetson's, then to Beede's just
in time to interfere in a three sided dog fight, then to my
Room & packed trunk, a very trying job to me in my
condition — got through before 6 — then on piazza — Tea about
7 — Mr. Cole & Meyer arr'd from Lake Placid about 8 — Sat
& spent on piazza until near 10 — Settled bill, said good
byes & to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 18, 1878

*Hewlett's Landing — Lake George*

Up at 5 o'clk, bkfst at 5.30 & soon after 6 got away for E-town. Enjoyed
the ride exceedingly, mt being partially overcast — got to E-town at
10 & took Kellogg's stage to R.R. & at 11.30 took train to Fort Ti — thence to
Lake Geo. at 1 & on to [illegible] to Hewlett's soon after 2. — Met Ned Dunning &
wife on board & at Roger Rock Dock other friends — At H. met Fred Durand
& Dr. Kimball & wife — went out in Fred's sail boat — to Sabbath day pt.
& to Davis's leap — Climbed below the cliff, very hard work & got a sketch
looking S — then to Harbor Islands &
around by Dr. Straws — He was at his
dock & shouted an invitation to us
all to take tea at his house — which
was accepted — had an exceedingly
pleasant evening in his new house
until about 8.40 — then rowed up
to the landing — got there soon
after 9 & at 9.30 went to our Room —
smoked awhile & then to bed
before 10 — Glorious, clear night.
