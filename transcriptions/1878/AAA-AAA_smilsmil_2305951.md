## Left Page

### February, Friday 1, 1878

More snow during the day — M[illegible] got to Studio at 10 o'clk. In no humor to work. Set palette &
painted over sky on Oscar Ireland's "Cress Brook" —
Lunch about 2 — in the afternoon went over to N.A.,
met Clarence Cook there — back to Studio at dark after taking Wills Durfee to 31. [illegible]
Tea in Studio at 6.30. At 8.30 to N.A. "Press View" — very full in spite of bad weather — did not get away until near midnight — then to 41 [illegible] and to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 2, 1878

Fine day but very bad underfoot. Did not get to N.A. until 10 o'clk. "Buyers Day" — very few bought — a dismal sort of time — I walked about until about 2 — then to Studio & lunched — back to N.A. until near 6 — then to Studio & rested until 7 — then tea & at 8 again at Academy — there until near 10 — when Micelle & I went to the "Ceneung" — there until 11.30 — then to 41 [illegible] and to bed after midnight.
