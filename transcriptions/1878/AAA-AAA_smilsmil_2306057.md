## Left Page

### September, Saturday 7, 1878

Hazy, overcast mrf., inside of my head very sympathetic —
after 4Up. Spent some time on piazza — then to my room
& wrote part of a letter to Emma J. my head hurting me very
much, I took a short walk &
then a rest before dinner —
After dinner I went to bed
& slept soundly until after
4\. then up feeling much
better, walked to the brow of
hill back of Stetson's, then to
tea after 4 — clouds lifting
very slowly. Everg in
parlor playing whist with
Miss Bates — then out on the
piazza until 11 — then to
bed at about 11.30.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 8, 1878

Up early, for Sunday, & walked
around "the Square" before breakfast.
After that went with Mr. White &
Miss Hillard to the foot of Roaring
Brook fall & while they waited
I climbed to the top on the R.h.t
down on the N. side. It was then
12, so we ret'd to hotel & I wrote a
letter to Emma J. until dinner,
about 2.15. — At about 4.15, the
Jacobs', W. Hadsord, Geo & I, started
for Chalcbpce Ponds, the day being
very beautiful, the walk was
charming — on way back stopped
at Sunset ledge & met Hillard, the
Whites & Miss Bates — 7.30 when we
reached hotel — beautiful moon-
light — sea — a big a number of us
went to Knoll back of Stetson's &
made a brush fire & some stuff
there — effects with moonlight
very fine — there until after 10 —
then on piazza, mooning alone
until after 11 — then to bed. —
Miss Bates, the Whites & others
leave early to-morrow mrg.
