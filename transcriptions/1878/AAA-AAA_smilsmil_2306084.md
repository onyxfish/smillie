## Left Page

### November, Sunday 10, 1878

Beautiful mrng., after bkft. wrote a letter to Geo. — then, as the Church bells began ringing, I went out to the top of Emmott's hill & there enjoyed a few minutes of exaltation as I looked off to the Hudson Highlands & listened to the church bells behind me — when they ceased ringing I went to the Baptist Ch. Heard Kendrick then home to dinner at one — after that, fussed awhile in father's Room, then stretched in big chair & just as the sun went down found that I had been taking a nap. Wrote awhile on letter to Geo. Whitney. Tea at 6. Matt & Ll. with us. Evrf. went to Opera House & heard Abbott on the Brotherhood of Man — back at 9 — some more writing & to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### November, Monday 11, 1878

Pleasant & growing milder — after a short mrng. walk, got at Bruce's work & spent the mrng. going over all the drawings, finishing them a little more closely — dinner at 12.30 — after it, another short walk — then at little etching No. 8, on Jefferson st. Bruce called about 3 & I gave him the drawings — I worked at etching & biting until about 4.30 then gave up as I could see no longer — wet down paper to prove to-morrow — then went to Gronlp's — met father at Wilson's Book Store — home to tea soon after 6. Evrf. spent picking up duds & preparing to pack trunk. Rain commenced soon after 9. I got to bed soon after 10.
