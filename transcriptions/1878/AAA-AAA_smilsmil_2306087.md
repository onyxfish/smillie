## Left Page

### November, Saturday 16, 1878

Pleasant day, but overcast — Got
to studio about 9.30 — Spent some
little time in getting things into
shape, then to work on Beems'
Birth place — About 1, went to
Hotel Royal + had lunch — back
to studio + finished Birth place
geo — so had tea in the studio
at about 6.30 + about 8 we went
to the Kimball's — Spent the eve'g
with Will + Fanny + at 10 — to
37 St + to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 17, 1878

Miserable day — Rain + wind
by jerks — B[illegible]; about 10 o'clk at
Hotel Royal. At 11 went in to
Geo to Hastings' + heard him
give me of his fine line Jimmy
sermons from Genesis 42 ch. 36 v —
where Jacob says, "Me these things
are against me" — After service
went to studio — then back to
Hotel Royal to dinner at 1.30 —
About 3 — to studio — wrote a letter
home — + at 6 went in to Springs'
— very nervous + miserable — had
tea + as the eve'g wore on gained
my equilibrium — left at 10.20
then to studio + fixed fire —
did not get to 37 St until
about 11.30 + right to bed
feeling exceeding sore in the
region of the stomach.
