## Left Page

### April, Thursday 18, 1878

Very warm & spring like.
After 6&[illegible] — went up to see Dr.
Yale — not in — walked very
slowly down to Studio, getting
there at 10 o'clk & feeling
very weak & much pained
in the Region of the Stomach —
Stretched at 33×44 canvas —
made a note to Emma J. to
accompany my little painting
"A Splash in Dark Blue" — Took the
[illegible] picture & note, left them at
her door — Returned & gave
myself up to my pain —
Was able to take some tea
but could not go to Dr. Mr. Thomp-
son's in the evely as I had
proposed to — on the lounge
until near 11 — then to 4½
S: & to bed — Had a bad
night with pain in Stomach
in character very like Neuralg[ia].

## Right Page

### April, Friday 19, 1878

Very warm day — feel very slim
after my experience last night
and so, was unable to do any-
thing. — Emma Beach, Mr. Baudwith —
[illegible] little one called — 9o. Amis
called concerning his project to
increase facilities for the selling
of am. pictures — At 12 lunched
with Coleman & at 12.30 we (W.+
Mrs. C. & D.) went to foot of 23d St. met
a party of 12 & all went to Sewaley
9o. & Anna C — Walter + wife — Valentine +
Collins — Emma J — Fanny K. — Helen
Butter, Mary Millard & Miss. Wood —
Walked east from the Station on
about 15 m. Reached a pleasant place
in the woods where the road forked &
was crossed by a small stream —
There a lunch was served and then
the party went on to the top of the Pal-
isades — I remained, seated up on
the stump of a tree & made a pen
sketch — a slight shower made variety —
About 5.30 party got? then on to Station
to catch 5.53 train — ½ a minute too
late, but by screaming, waving hats &
halts & all Running we got the
conductor to stop the train — much fun.
Got to studio about 7 — very tired —
Rested & took tea — then on lounge
until after 9 — then worked for
awhile on pen sketch & at 10.30
Rode up to 41·5= To bed at 11.
