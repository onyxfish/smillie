## Left Page

### October, Thursday 17, 1878

Very delightful day but with a strong South wind blowing in a way that means something. After my walk got to work about 10.30 o'clk. on Harpers drawings & finished a little more on "Huddle" — then on views of the Narrows from Blt. Mr. Trail — Dinner at 12.30. P.M. washed in effect on drawing — done on tinted board — like the working of it — at near 5 went out for a walk & got a sketch or two — Tea at 6 — Svefy. Read Burns' poems until 9 — then walked about out of doors & about 10 met father & Kate as they left church — then home & to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 18, 1878

Overcast — Rain began about 10 a.m. (it had rained during the night) & continued to increase until at night it was a very heavy storm — Short walk bet. 9 & 10 — then to work & spent the w[hole] over three Harper drawings — Caldwell — Huddle — & from Blt. Mr. Trail — Dinner at 12.30. afternoon laid varnish on backs of plates — & commenced to etch on a little fellow but mordant would not work — as my hand was in & etched a zinc plate & bit it with nitric [?] — at it all the p.m. — worked by gas light before tea — tea at 6. [illegible] — I got out my printing makes & took three [?] of my etching — not a very satisfactory work after all my trouble — Mother & Lee were expected but they did not come. Got to bed soon after 10 —
