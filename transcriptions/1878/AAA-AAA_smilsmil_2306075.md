## Left Page

### October, Saturday 19, 1878

Cold, windy day — got up with
a miserable headache — after
hlf° went back to bed with it
& did not get up until near
noon — then laid Re-biting ap°.
on zinc plate etched yesterday.
Dinner about 1. Soon after
Mother & Ge° arr° from their Catskill
Trip. After dinner I varnished &
"Stopped out" plate & at 3.30 went
with Kate & Matt to Organ Concert
at 2°. Dutch, from 4 to 5. Was
thoroughly chilled — went to Mary's
& after that a brisk walk home
to tea at 6.15. At 9.30 at Wallace
Bruce's — there until 9.30 making
final arrangements to illustrate
his poem "The Land of Burns" —
then home & to bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 20, 1878

Brilliant, cloudless day —
in the mrf. — after a walk, went
to Christ's Ch. (Ep.) & heard an
old, querulous voiced man
preach a very fair sermon — out
at 12.25 — then home to dinner.
After that, took a nap & felt
none the better for it — About
4 went out for a walk — went
almost to the southern limit
of Morse's place — about 2 m —
then back after sunset by
way of the "big hill" & Academy St.
Tea at 6. Matt & Ed. there —
every. Father & Kate went to
Ch. — the Rest of us visited
for awhile — then Matt & Ed.
went home, Ge°. going with
them. I stayed with Mother
& Read "Thoreau's Excursions"
— don't like them — he is an
illogical, incoherent writer
to me — got to bed at 10.
