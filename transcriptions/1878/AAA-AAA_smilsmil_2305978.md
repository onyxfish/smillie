## Left Page

### March, Friday 29, 1878

High wind + cloudy mtf. claring
to a windy but glorious afternoon.
Up late — b+lp. about 10 — Ge.
went to Hill Side — my mrs Falcon
up clearing away my printing
mess and putting things in
order to go to N.Y. to-night.
Dinner at 12.45. Afternoon
writing + packing until 4
then to Mamy's — Natt's +
walking, enjoying the glorious
day until 6 — then home to
tea — left soon after 7 and
by 7.35 train reached N.Y.
at 10 — Right to 41' S. — and
to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 30, 1878

Very pleasant day — on my
way to studio, stopped at Sturt
evant H. + saw Jan M. Connell
to studio at 10 — by 11 at N.A.
— Varnishing Day — there until
1 — then home with Colmant
lunched with him — his niece
Miss Howard there — after lunch
Mr. H. + Miss Parker came in —
about 2 Ret. to studio — then
again to N.A. Made sketch of
Placid painting — Ret. to studio
+ finished it with pen + ink for
"The Graphic" — Dielmant Wallen
called — Tea at 7 — went to Kunio's
+ got some money — then to Sturt.
H. + spent rest of evening with
Jan — Dr. Ben. + Ge. — to
41 S. + to bed about 11.
