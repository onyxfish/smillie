## Left Page

### December, Monday 16, 1878

Fine day, altho' rather cold
& windy — Got to studio at
9.45 & spent the m'f. Re-
enforcing drawings for Harper
to lunch at 1.30 & after a
short walk — Spent the
afternoon fixing up one of
my oil studies of last summer.
After 5 went to Rennie's and
ordered some W. C. frames —
to studio before 7 & had tea —
At 8.45 met Geo. at Mr. Chas.
Watrous' cor. Lex. Ave. & 40th
St. Ev'g passed pleasantly
until 10.15 then to 37th St. &
to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 17, 1878

Charming day — overcast for
a while during p.m. — Got to
studio at 9.45 & again at work
on oil studies of last summer.
Mr. Lord of Orange called to
enquire about lessons — out to
lunch at 1.30 — afternoon
worked on another study —
after dark, went to Miss Dufe's
did not get to studio to tea
until after 7 — Ev'g until
11 o'clk — fussing about studio
mounting etchings etc —
then to 37th St. & to bed at
11.30.
