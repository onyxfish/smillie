## Left Page

### April, Friday 12, 1878

Charming Spring day —
but I. soured up with
a heavy headache that
I could not work — Podging
about the studio all day,
trying to get the better of
the disorder & dirt that
threatens to crowd me out.
Lunch after 2 — most of
the p.m. at same work as
in the m'f — At 4.30 Board
meeting of W. C. Soc'y. Members
of the new board also met
with us — Everybody exceedingly
amiable — but my headache
increased so that immediately
after they left I threw myself
on the lounge & did not get
up until about midnight —
then staggered up to 41 S.
& to bed

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 13, 1878

Another charming day and
another day of heavy headache
— Part of the day on the lounge
& the rest of the time cleaning
out closets & dusting studios.
Lunch at 1.30 — A walking
party went over to Woodbury,
L.I. but I was not able to
join them — between 3 & 4 P.m.
I thought I would have to give
up, but at 6, feeling much
better, I went in to Munna's
to dinner & at 8 o'clc. to Thomas's
last Symphony Concert — Enjoyed
it exceedingly — After the
concert went home with Munna —
there for awhile, then to studio
& wrote a letter to father — Got
to 41 S. & to bed a little
before 12.
