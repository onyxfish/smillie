## Left Page

### February, Friday 15, 1878

Mild, & somewhat overcast promising a change that
did not come — After bkfs.
went up to Dr. Yale's to ask
his professional advice —
there until 11. Then to studio
Painted a sketch 9 × 12
of the view from Owe's Head
on Jungsalee looking over
the Wilderness & Raquette dale
Trying to shake my hand
free & get some freshness of
color — Lunch at 2 — at 3
went to the N.A. for a few
minutes & into the schools.
Painted until after 5 — then
a walk up to 36th St & back —
Tea at 7 — Eve — writing a
note to Chas. deBray of The Times
at 9.45 to N.A. Met Gifford &
had a street walk & talk with
him about the W.C. Presidency
then again to studio — near
12 when I finished my note
To 41st St — & to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 16, 1878

Another pleasant day after
some mild threatenings — Got
to studio at 8.30 after having
spent all the mng at Dunbar's
printing establishment, 121
Liberty St, trying to teach him
how to prove my free hand
etchings — a very hard job.
Lunched at studio — then
set palette & about 3.30
commenced a sketch 10 × 14
"The Giant Indian Head & Devil's
Pulpit on lower Ausable Lake"
Got it done in less than 2 hours.
Delacroix & Hirsch came in
at dusk — a short walk, then
tea at 6.30 — wrote awhile
& at 8.30 went to Emma's
— there until near 10 — then
again to studio — after 11
when I got to 41st St — Right
to bed.
