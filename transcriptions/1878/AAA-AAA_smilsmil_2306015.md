## Left Page

### June, Thursday 13, 1878

Charming my but my troubles
seem to be increasing — my head
feels as though on fire inside of
it — I spent the my, rolled
up in my blanket in big chair
Emma came & took get out to
Hillside at 12.30 — I rec'd notes
from Anna C. & J.C. Nicole —
Dinner at 12.40 — Matt spent
the day with us — a shower
before t — slight, spitting rain
at intervals during p.m. — Felt
better & wrote a note to Falconer —
Tea before 6 that father might
get off to N.Y. by every boat —
We all got to bed by
half past nine.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 14, 1878

another charming day —
got up with a furious head-
ache & for some time was
exceedingly miserable but
later in the my felt so much
better that I sauntered out
& loafed about in the shade of
trees for some time — Dinner
at about 1 — again out in
the afternoon & by evening
feel quite like myself
again — wrote a note to
father. Father rec'd — from M.f.
at 6.30. Beautiful moon-
light night — nearly 11
when I got to bed. [illegible]
