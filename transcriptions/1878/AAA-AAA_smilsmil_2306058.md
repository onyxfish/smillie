## Left Page

### September, Monday 9, 1878

Beautiful day, but sort of sorts — head & bowels. Did not feel equal to going out alone for work. So I remained in my room & finished letter to Emma J. Dinner about 1.30. About 3 p.m. went to River & painted on study looking across it — until near 6 — feeling chilly & uncomfortable — Tea bet. 6 & 7 — on bed for awhile after it, then on piazza. Met Misses Putnam and Nesselhock. Went over to the Putnam cottage with them & Miss Streane & Mr. Coes — After leaving them we went to the foot of Boating Brook Falls — Glorious moonlight effects in the woods — Back to house about 11 — Right to bed. ## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 10, 1878

Delicious day, but rather breezy — Still feel quite out of sorts — but about 10, when the light was right, went across the log over River & finished the study looking up stream — back abt 4 — had dinner, at 2.30 down to River & painted on study of Evergreens & on Rock tops until 5.30 — then to house feeling very much used up in my head — Tea about 7 — then on bed until near 9 — then wrapped in big blanket went on piazza & stayed there until 10.30 — Lovely moonlight night — but with promise of a change to morrow. The Jacobs' to leave abt. 6.30 a.m. for home. To bed about 10.45.
