## Left Page

### November, Thursday 14, 1878

Charming day — after bkfs — worked on Nat's flower pots —
painting silhouettes in black on them
— then putting my eng. + printing
things away — At 11.30 went up-
town — went to Mam's — back to
dinner at 12.30. The afternoon
spent in putting things away
& finishing packing. Tea
about 6 as usual — Every
went to Nat's + Mam's + bid
them good bye — home again
soon after 9 + to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 15, 1878

Up at 6.30 — bkfs. at 7.30 + to N.Y.
R.R. depot in time for 8.25 train
which was 1 hr + 35 m. late — So, walked
to the river — the haze color + light
over the river + its banks was as
lovely + tender as softest summer
effect — took 9.05 way train + got
to N.Y. at 11.35 — went right to studio
— soon after 1 went with Geo. to
a Bdway Restaurant + had a
dinner that troubled me all the
afternoon — tried to get started
on some of Bruce's work but found
too many other things to do — At
5 went with Geo. to Century, back
to studio + had tea between 6 + 7.
About 8 we went in + spent an
hour or more with the Colemans —
then to 34th St. — for an hour I was
unpacking my trunk + fetching
things to my room — to bed
about 11.
