## Left Page

### December, Saturday 28, 1878

Mild pleasant day, altho' run-
cast. One thing + another preventing
I did not get at my etching
until 10.45 — then to work on
sky — went all over it, gave it
two light bites + at 12.30 cleaned
it off in find that it was a much
lighter bite than I had expected.
Dinner about 1 — All the P.m.
I was fussing over my etching
Faking pf- + laughing Rebiting
ground — I proved two plates —
for [illegible] + one for Nicoll — Altogether
I had a diverting time — At 5.45 got
out for a short walk — To tea at
6. Emma Ro- with us — after tea
went to Wallace Bruce's — not in —
then with Emma to Lawrence's —
So Liberty S-. there until 9.15 — then
home — again — 11 o'clk — before
I got to bed —

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 29, 1878

Mild — Charming mf. — over-
cast to. m. — mf. went to Baptist
Ch. + heard Dr. K — after service
walked home with Kate. Dinner
at 1 — then a short walk in front
of parlor fire — after that, made
notes to Louis Tiffany — Smirsean,
Resainicks + Miss Anderson —
made a from 5 to 6 o'clk — very
beautiful evef. Matt + Ed.
at tea — Evef. in front of big
fire in parlor — much chat +
a little reading — To bed at
10.45 —
