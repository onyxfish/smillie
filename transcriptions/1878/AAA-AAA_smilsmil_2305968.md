## Left Page

### March, Saturday 9, 1878

Still pleasant weather. Got to studio about 9.30 — to work on 12×18 "Boy still waiting" — Ret'd from Utica — Painted over most of it — very tight & overworked — never will be any better. Lunch at 2 — Painted until after 5 — washed up — tea at 6.30 — At 7.30 went in to Colman's and walked down to Phil. with them & the Tweedys — Very delightful concert — Geo. came in at the end & walked up with the girls — I went to Century — there met Gifford, Robbins & Moore & we went to the mat room up stairs & we consulted together concerning Water Color matters — Home after 12 before I got to 4[?] 3[?] — to bed right away — [illegible — ~2 lines]

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 10, 1878

In bed all day with a miserable headache. Got up about 8 & had some gruel & beef tea. At 9.30 walked down to the Gilsey Ho. & had a glass of Geyser water — then back & lay on lounge until about 1 — then in to bed, [illegible — ~20 lines]
