## Left Page

### March, Sunday 17, 1878

Cold rain with wind &
some snow — Bkfo. at
9.30 — At 11 went to D. H's
& heard him preach a
good sermon for the times
from

After service went to 127
& had a nap on lounge
before dinner at Hotel Royal
at 1.30 — About 2.30 went
to Studio — M. Cyrus Butler
& M. E. J. Phelps called —
I wrote to father & other notes
then on lounge & took another
nap — Got foot tea at the
Jacobs'. I tea'd in Studio
at 7.30 — at 8.30 went in to
Preston Spring's — Emma & aunt
gone to Germantown to gather
Hepatica. At 9, in to Colman's
there until 10.30 — To 4. S.
at 11 & right to bed —

## Right Page

### March, Monday 18, 1878

Overcast, raw & windy — After
bkfo. rode down town & went
first to J. M. Falconer's — then to Harper
Bros. & saw Parsons — Perkins & Frau
is [illegible] — then to A. B. M. Co — and
bought blankets for printing press.
Lunch at 1 — in office — then to 35
B. way — T. H. Smillie's, who was as mild
as a May mf., & there for nearly an
hour over W. C. S. accts — then to Duby
then to Pati's & bought india & plate
paper — & about 5 got to Studio very
tired. Nearby 9 when I had tea
at 8.15 to K. A — no quorum of
Council — Chatted until after
9 — then to Studio & wrote until
after 10 — To 4. S. & to bed
at 11.
