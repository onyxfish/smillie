## Left Page

### April, Sunday 28, 1878

Still unpleasant with considerable Rain —
Again used up with a headache — went to
Hotel Royal & ate a light breakfast, then back to 41
S. & spent the rest of the day on the lounge until
after 5 — then to Hutterzels — took tea there with
father, Mother & Kate —
Spent the evng until 9.30 — then walked with
father up to Charl's — then on up to Central Park &
back to 41st S. & to bed about 11. Raw, misty night.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 29, 1878

Still overcast — in the afternoon it quite cleared up
but at night, Raw fog again.
Father visited with us — Studio
at 9.30 — to work on block —
"Near Pau" — Lunch at 2 —
after that went to N.A.D.
School — Reid & finished
block & commenced tracing
another subject. Walked
with Miss Abbatt & back to tea
at 6.30 — Father took tea with
us — at 8 went to N.A.
Council, chatting after
council until 11 o'clk — then
to 41st S. & to bed at 11.30.
