## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 5, 1878

Beautiful day — Got to
studio about 9.30 — To work
on my Japan paper etching
Pfs — to get them ready to
deliver — At 1 o'clk, went
to Louis Tiffany's & lunched
with him — Back to studio
at 2.30 — then to N.A. & again
to studio & finished working
over etchings — about dusk
W. F. A. Silva came in —
At 6.30 to Fanny K's to tea —
found there, Emma & Mary J.
but Matt & Kate had Rel'd to Po.
in the mrf as Matt was unwell
At 8.30 all started for Mr. Colman's
I went also to W. J. T. Williams' — but
he was not in, then to Colmans
& in addition to the party, found
the Cooks — there until 10.30
then Raced home with
Emma — then to 41. 3 — &
to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 6, 1878

Charming day. Got to studio
about 10 o'clk — & to work after
some delay on "Lake Placid" —
Painted in some large tree
forms in the foreground &
improved it very much —
About 1 o'clk, W. J. T. Williams
came in — he liked the pick
but did not say any thing
positively. Lunch, after 2 —
worked on painting until
5 — then Nicole Colman and
J. G. Brown came — Bd. of Control
meeting until 6 — Tea at 7 —
wrote letters until 8.30 — then
to N.A. — Special Meeting to
amend Constitution — Did it.
Back to studio at 10 — made
until 10.45 — to 41 S. at 11.30
& right to bed.
