## Left Page

### January, Thursday 10. 1878

Heavy rain nearly all day — Got to studio's at 9.45 —
Nicole came in then
Abbey came & I did but
little work before 2 o'clk —
2.30 when I got lunch —
then Pratt came in &
then Robbins came in &
then I worked for awhile
on Miss Jacli's drawing. I
got comparatively little
of my own work done —
Left after 4 — wrote
notes to Yale — Charl —
Bunce & a letter to Genl
Diven. After 10 — met a
Union League Club — met a
lot of fellows, but got away
with Charl soon after 11 —
walked up to 41st S. & to bed.
Addie Dunning Hawes surprised us with a call this
morning.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 11. 1878

Still very nasty weather —
9.45 when I got to studio's —
to work on my figure in the
orchard — very much disgusted.
Abby called — heard of the
death of Mrs. Saml. Sayre of Montrose
by Typhoid fever. Yale called —
a short walk, then lunch at
about 2 — Did not go to Phil.
Rehearsal but worked on my
drawing — At 5.30 Geo. & I went
to Century — looked over papers
then walked — Tea at studio
at 4 — wrote notices for next
Etching club meeting & other
things until 10 — there to
44th St. & to bed about
10.45.
