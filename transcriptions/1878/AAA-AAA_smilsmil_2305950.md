## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 30, 1878

Splendid cold, clear & windy
day — Bkfp. before 8 — then down
town went first to Graphic —
then to Harpers — Saw Fletcher
W. If the Graphic publishes for
Mr. Hatt's will not touch our
Society illustrations — back to
Graphic & they yielded — a very
graceful thing on the part of young
W. St. John Harper of the Graphic —
then again to Harpers & arranged
for a page in the Weekly & another
in the Bazaar — then to Rowell's
advertising place — then to lunch
again to Graphic, each time
climbing four long flights of stairs
then to Gagnebins for my watch
then to Sackets — then to A.P.S.
N. Co. — There to Dunbar's place
printing place — 21 Liberty S —
& at 4.30 to N.A. of D. — There for half
an hour, then to Studio — a.a.
Anderson interviewed me then
a little rest on lounge — Tea at
7.30 — At 8 Granville Perkins
came & also Sackett curb proof.
Perkins & I worked on the Ill. Cat
until 11.30 — then I made a note
to Anderson — to 41st St. — at 12.15
& Right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 31, 1878

Tremendous storm of wind
& snow — continuing all day until
after 8 P.M. Bkfp. about 9 — to
Studio at 10 — very tired after
yesterday's work — Until 12
looking over catalogue pf. & other
work of like kind — Between
12 & 1 — a boy from Harpers — over
to N.A. with him — there met
Conant & Miss Durfee — Back
at 2.30 & had lunch — The
rest of P.M. N.W.C. business —
Another messenger from Sackett
Tea at 6.30 — fussing — note
to Darley — etc. Did not
get to bed until 11.30.
