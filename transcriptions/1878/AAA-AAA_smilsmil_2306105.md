## Left Page

### December, Sunday 22, 1878

Windy & cloudy. B[illegible]. at 9.30 after that, went to studio and having fixed fire, went to Dr. Hastings' & heard him. After service, again went to studio & at 1.30, to Miss Abbatts to dinner — met Geo there — at 3.30 again to studio & mat for W. Lawrence Hutton, for his book to be published by Hurd & Houghton, a sketch of Water Color movements in this country, and a history of W.C. Socy. At 6, with Geo went to Miss Jacobs' to tea. At 8, escorted her to studio & back, then went to Cyrus Butler's but missed him & daughter — saw his sister — then to studio to 37 4 St — soon after 10 and right to bed —

## Right Page

### December, Monday 23, 1878

Splendid & very cold day. Got to studio at 9.45 after giving my fingers a severe frost-nibbing because I had to carry a bundle. Worked all the m[illegible] on drawing of Sandwich Islands Arms for A.B.N. Co — out to lunch about 2 — & at 3 or a little later Marion Butler & Wm Newhall came & made quite a visit — I tried to start a charcoal sketch of "The Belated Gallant" — Tea at 6.30 — Every I mounted my W.C. "Butternut Bend" — a small sketch, also a W.C. & an old sketch by B[illegible] — hard at work until 10.30 — then to 37 4 St — & did not get to bed until 11.30.
