## Left Page

### December, Friday 6, 1878

Very pleasant day — but with some slight snow flurries about mid-day.
Got to studio at 9.45 & to work on Harper's blocks — worked over block
photo from view back A Bolton —
So lunch at 1.30 — a short walk
& back — Afternoon, worked over
drawings of "The Huddle" and
"Caldwell" — about 5.30 went
out for a walk — back at 6.15 —
Tea at 7 — Eve 9 — Sketching
last summer's studies & mounting
more etchings — to 37° 8° at
10.30 — to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 7, 1878

Charming day — Got to studio
about 9.30 & to work retouching
Harper drawings made while
in P! — worked over Huddle &
Caldwell — To lunch at
1 — In the afternoon, feeling
very unélike work I fussed
over the mounting of some etching
until Miss Vel Howe came in —
Then visited with her — At dusk
went out & had a walk. —
Tea at abt 7 — fussed in
studio until 9 — then down
to Century — monthly meeting —
There until after 11 — Then
walked up town with J. G.
Brown — Glorious moonlight.
To bed soon after midnight.
