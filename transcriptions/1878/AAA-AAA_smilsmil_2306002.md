## Left Page

### May, Saturday 18, 1878

Bright & beautiful mng. got to
studio at 9.30. M. Loomis & Co.
surprised us at the [illegible]. Fable —
I got to work on Harper block
f "Lourdes" worked until near
1 then Emma D & Anna C —
came in & we quickly arranged
a trip to Mott Haven to see the
Athletic Sports of the College Boys.
I fixed & ate my lunch & at 2
o'clk went to Emma's — then
with her & Grace went to Harlem
depot — there met Anna Cook
& by 2.30 train went to Mott Haven —
walked over to the grounds & were
very much interested in the sports.
Returned by 6 o'clk train & went
to Anna C's to dinner — at 8.30 to
studio & atq to Centery — there
saw J.W. Wood & Gifford about
etchings — walked up with
Wood — got to 39th St. soon after
11 & to bed by 11.30.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 19, 1878

Very warm day — B[illegible] — atq —
Yod. Hastings at 11 & heard
him preach from Jer. 24th Ch.
2d Cor 7th V. After service went
to Shetterzel's and dined
with them. Carrie sails
next Mr.? for Europe — Got
to studio about 4. Tried to
get a nap — then wrote a
letter home — at 6.30 went
to Emma's to tea — there
until 9 — then to studio.
wrote on Bellows's letter
until near 11 o'clk — then
to 39th St. & to bed at 11.30.
