## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 20. 1878.

Again wet, miserable weather.
got to studio a little before 10
& worked nearly all the [illegible]
on Alloway Kirk — then went
to Buchanan's, the florist in 17
S; & got slight sketches of Thistle,
Hare bell & Heather — then at 1.30
to studio — fought hard for a
couple of hours to get my lunch
nicole, Hinds & others calling.
got it at last & to work on
design for Cover of Bruce's book.
worked as long as I could see.
There cleared up the room as
a preparation for meeting of
W.C. Soctÿ in the eve'g. Took
tea about 6.30 — Eve'g full mee-
ing — adjourned about 10 — all got
away about 10.30 — then I went
to 37 E — & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 21. 1878.

Rain in the morning — later in the
day it ceased but about 10 a.m.
it came down again harder than
ever — got to studio at 9.30 to work
again on cover design for Bruce —
commenced a new one — then I
penned a new corner for frontes-
piece — went to lunch with Ge-
at 2 o'clk. — During p.m. worked
on pen work — About 4 o'clk.
T.W. Wood & Nicoll came in, then
Coleman — held an informal W.
C. Board meeting. About 5 I
went out to walk — went to cutting
Rooms & wrote a note to Miss Hilland
then to studio to tea at 4 — Wrote
note to Col. Clarke acct. of the
Water Color Soctÿ for Educational
Bureau Report — To 37 E — at
10.30 & to bed before 11.
