## Left Page

### May, Thursday 2. 1878.

Overcast & muggy — with streaks of hot sunshine —
got to studio about 9.45 & altho' bothered with a mild
sort of headache, stuck to my work all day on large W.C.
and making considerable advance on it in spite of
discomforts — a walk about Mad. Sq. with Emma J. then
into tea at f— father with us — Soon after 8 — went to
D. Thompson's — W. 43° S. — and saw Mrs. J. & the D's younger bro.
At 9.15 to Mr. Hertzel's & saw father mother Kate & Geo. At
10 o'clk, left with father & Geo.
to 37' S. at 10.30 & right to
bed. [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~4 words]

## Right Page

### May, Friday 3. 1878.

Very warm day — partially overcast. Got to studio at 9.45
& to work on W.C. "Butternut's — Chemung Valley" — Anna C.
called — to make up a walking party for to-morrow. By working
faithfully, managed to get my W.C. to where, for the present,
at any rate, I called it done —
Then, at 6.30, went out for a short walk — called for a moment
at Emma J.'s — Tea at f— father with us — At 8.20 at
Anna C.'s — at 9.20 at 45 W. 47. Father, Mother, Kate & Charl
there — at 10 went with father & Charl to 597 Fifth Ave & at 10.50
to 21 W. 34' S. & to bed before 11.
