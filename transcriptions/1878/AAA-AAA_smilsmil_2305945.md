## Left Page

### January, Sunday 20, 1878

Dull mng. Bhp. at 9.30 then to studio, fixed fire then to Dr Hastings's & heard him preach a very spirited sermon from St. John, 3° ch. 9° v. "How can these things be" — After service went with Charl to his lodging — then to Miss Jacobs', where it began to rain. Dinner about 2 — there until near 4, then to studio — a nap on sofa — then wrote a letter to father — had tea about 8 — until near 11 clearing up the mess of material that had collected on book cases & in every corner — then to Hl'S. & to bed —
Heavy Rain.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 21, 1878

Dismal day — dark & wet. Got to studio at 10 — to work on pen sketches for W.C. Cat. after "In the Orchard" — Abt went to lunch with Coleman. On my return to studio, W. Mirror came in & turns near 3 o'clk. before I could get to work on my drawing "Solo. in Chemung Valley" — worked until near 5 when I cut it off the board & put in in frame. Went out for a walk. Tea at 6.30. Soon after 8, members of the Etching Club began to come in. At 8.30 went to N.A. Council meeting — at 9.15 Ret'd with J.W. Wood to Etching club — At 10.15 adjourned. Fixed fin. at 12° to 41° S. — after writing a note to the Post in answer to an editorial of this sort. To bed about 1.
