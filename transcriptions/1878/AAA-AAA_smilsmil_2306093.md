## Left Page

### November, Thursday 28, 1878

Thanksgiving Day — Up late &
got to studio a little before 10 —
overcast & windy with a little
spattering rain — To work on Bum
men at Alloway & by sticking to
it got it done by 1 o'clk — then
at 1.30, to Miss Abbatt's to dinner
with Ge. — 161 W. 23d St — After a very
pleasantly served dinner, went
to parlor — there, looking over
"Eureka (Picturesque)" until after
4 — then to studio — at 5.30, went
out for a walk & to Century — at
7 — back to studios & at 7.30 had
tea, about 8.30 went to U.A.D.
Art Decorative loan affairs — there
until 10, then to studio — to 37
St. at 11 & to bed right away.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 29, 1878

Cleared to be a very pleasant
day — After bkfst, by elevated RR. to
Park Place — to Photo. Eng. Co. — with two
designs — then to Harper's — there until
near 11, detained by a number of
things — got to studio at 11.30 and
commenced design "Man was made
to mourn" — got my lunch at 2,
again to work on drawing — at 4.30
went to Dewey's — then to Century —
met Vaux & Collins & Benjamine &
had a pleasant time until 6.30 —
then to studios & had tea — at 8.15
with Ge. called on Miss Hilliard
at 33 m. 37 — not in — then to Mrs.
Longstreet's — 122 W. 42d — had a
very pleasant call — met there
Bronson Howard — left at 10.20
to 37. 8d & to bed at 11.
