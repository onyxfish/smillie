## Left Page

### July, Sunday 7, 1878

Altho' last night was very cool, to-day has been very hot. Between hlf't church time wrote to nicole & Miss Durfee — then to Episcopal Ch. & heard a sermon from Rev. Mr Geigenf[illegible]. then home & wrote to Robbins & Harris 3.¢4 f[illegible]: Louis Elp[illegible]. Dinner some after 1 — then a nap — then more writing — wrote part of a letter to Metra [illegible] Adams. Tea ab 6 — at 7 walked by So. Hamilton St. to Mary's — then to Baptist Ch. & heard a very wordy but meag. sermon by D. Kendrick — then with father, Mary & the D. went to Church St. & stopped there for a few minutes — then home & finished letter to Metra — to bed ab 11.

Heard to-day of the death of Wm night on friday P.M. at Litchfield Conn —

## Right Page

### July, Monday 8, 1878

Very warm, close & hazy. After blo[illegible]: took Anthony block & washed it clean. then put a new preparation on it and went out to walk. on return commenced new drawing — Dinner about 1. Mother spending the day at Matt's. worked all the P.M. on block. About 5, or a little later, Uncle W. & aunt a. came & stayed to tea — which was served about 6. Thunderstorms about, slight shower ab 6 — gorgeous sunset. Uncle W. & aunt a. went to the Mary Powell to meet Mr. & Mrs. Düring. About 8, after I had written a note to Hunt. Sayre, Father & I went to Matt's for mother — a very heavy shower kept us there for some time — home about 9, to bed ab 10 — more showers during the night.
