## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 13, 1878

Pleasant day — got to studio a little before 10 — set palette & painted a little sketch about 3×5 in — trying for a bit of freedom of hand & color — painted him about two hours then on to Lake Placid and painted over middle distance with & water, left hand — Lunch at 2 — worked until 5 — Then Nicoll Coleman & Smith of B°. of Central came in — session until 6 — had a little passage with Smith to add to our unpleasantness — at 6.30 went to Century & looked over papers — at 7.45 went to Nichols to see him about water colors for Paris &p — very pleasant call — at 8.15 to studio & had my tea — after that made until 9.30, then to Academy — after 10, back again to Studio made awhile — to 41 S — about 11, then to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 14, 1878

Very pleasant all day with promise of change — on my way to studio, stopped at Mrs Jonathan Sturgis — cor. Park Ave & 36' S — Had a very pleasant call for 3/4 of an hour, seeing pictures & chatting with many Whitney — got to Studio soon after 10.30 — headached so that I could not work — got a very clever Valentine from E.M.D. Replied with a pen sketch — Lunched with the Colemans — after that went & had my hair cut — at 3 — to N.A — ab't 4 to studio on lounge until 5.30 — then to 41 S — at 6.30 at Mr. Chas. Isham's to dinner — met there Mr. & Mrs. Moore & Mr. & Mrs. Seaver Page — Sat down to dinner at 7 — Up at 9.30 — to N.A. at 10 — then to studio — fixed fire & made until 11.15 — then to 41 S — [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~4 words] [illegible — ~5 words] of S'ho — [illegible — ~3 words]
