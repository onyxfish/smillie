## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 2. 1878.

Overcast & threatening all day — 9.40 when I got to studio — then for some time helping Miss Jacobs — then at my own work on 12×18 — Painted over cows & fore g'd — at 1 o'clk. Mrs. Coleman came in & with her I went to lunch two ladies & Mrs. C. completing the party. Back at 2 & putting aside the 12×18 as finished I took up large charcoal sketch, for Mrs. C.'s drawing — Ogilvie & Mrs. O. made us a pleasant [illegible] call — Nicole came in — at 5.30 walked up 5th ave. & met Anna C — with her to Lu K's — tea — at 8 Fanny went to Teachers meeting with Nell — Geo went to studio & I took Anna to her Singing Club — Sleety snow falling — back to studio — note to J. M. Wood — Shut up shop at 10.30 — went to 41st S't & to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 3. 1878.

Brilliant milky windy day — 9.40 when I got to studio. Set to work on charcoal sketch 13× 28 in — View on Newtown Creek and finished it before lunch which I took in Miss A's room with her sister & friend Mrs. Hoffmann — Nellie J. & Geo. To work again at 2 upon 10×14 in charcoal sketch" Under the Apple Trees — getting ready studies of figure etc. etc. At 5.40 out for a walk went to Knoedle's — then to Century Club for a few minutes, then to studio & had tea after 7 — Wk until 10 o'clk — cleaning drawing boards & mounting & stretching paper — then to 41st S't & at 10.45 to bed.
