## Left Page

### December, Saturday 14, 1878

Charming day — got to studio at 9.45 & commenced middle one of 3 combined drawings — "The Dawn at Bolton" — Worked until 1 — then out to lunch & a walk around Mad. Sq. Then in & finished drawing & rubbed in bottom one of 3 — "Rogers Rock & Anthony's nose" — then, into cabs, I did not get out until 5.40 — then walked up to Charl's — 5th Ave. 148th S — Saw him for a few minutes — then to 37th S — got bundle & got to Studio's at 7.15 — a slight snow fall — had tea and the eve'g was spent passing down among sketches & writing. To 37th S — & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 15, 1878

Miserable day. Rain & darkness — my head in sympathy with only ↑ down — Had help? at 9.30 — At 10.30 went to studio & found John mopping the floor, as I did not dare to leave him alone, I stayed & "bossed" him until 12.30 — then to Hotel Royale & with Pet — went to the Hertzel's where we had dinner there until after 3 — then to Charl's there until after 4 — then to studio — note to father & at 6 went to Miss Abfatt's & had tea — there until 9 — then again to studio — there until after 10 — then to 37th S — & to bed at 11.
