## Right Page

### February, Monday 4, 1878

Beautiful day — feeling
the effects of yesterday's
headache — To studio at 10 —
"most of the mg" biting in
an etching of Bellows —
Lunch at 2 — again at
biting until 3 — then set
palette & painted over
"Swere on Placid & Mount
Whiteface" — about 8 × 16 — got
it all covered — Tea at
6.30 — At A. Council meeting
— after that in the galleries
for a short time — then
to H: 82 & to bed at 11.
