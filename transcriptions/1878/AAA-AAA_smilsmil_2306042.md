## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 6, 1878

Very heavy showers all day.
I spent the mng. until 11.30.
writing — then joined in a
general goodbye to young
Milburn who left for his home
at 11.30. Then play until dinner
time at 1.30 — after that, more
play, then a nap, then more
writing, some work with the
pencil & tea at 7 — Mr. Culver
arrived at 1.30 — Evely. had
whist & games, then dancing
music & singing & to bed
at 10.30. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 7, 1878

Charming mr. About 9.30 almost
every one in the hotel got away on
the "Meteor" for a day of pleasuring.
I could not go because I have
so much to do before I can be
ready to leave to-morrow — I
wrote until near 11 then went
into Marion Butler's room and
commenced making a water
color sketch from her window.
Thunder storms came up &
very much interfered with
my work. Dinner about 1.30.
Worked on drawing until
after 5 — then to dock. Meteor
arrived before 6 — Geo. & I took
a row under Roger's Rock & to
Juniper Island — Sea soon
after 7 — a very quiet every
lovely moonlight — Commenced
packing trunk about 9.30 &
kept at it until 11 — then to
bed.
