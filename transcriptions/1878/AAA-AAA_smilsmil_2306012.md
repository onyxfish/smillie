## Left Page

### June, Friday 7, 1878

Po'keepsie —

Glorious day — but suffering from a headache — B'klo —
at Hotel Royal — then to 40th S-
& Park Ave. & left for Mr. Ned Couls
her "Etching & Etchers" — then to Grand
Central Depot & bought ticket —
then to 37th S— & finished packing
then to Renner's & had a box made
to take what I could not get
into my trunk & valise — then
to 37th S— & packed that — then bid
good bye to 37th S— went to Grand
Central at 12.30 & gave orders to
AM S'lp. & to Westcott's — then to
Studio — bid my good byes & then
finding that Renner had used
my order for Chicago water colors
that he should not have used
until next Aug. I went there
again & directed the return
of the pictures to the Studio — then
to Station & off at 4 oclk — train
Mr. M.S. Beach with me as far as
Peekskill — got to Po' — at 6.35 —
no trunk or box — but my little valise
brought up by Westcott instead of
my trunk — Right home to tea —
Every spent in chat & about
10 o'clk — got to bed —
