## Left Page

### October, Saturday 5, 1878

*Peekskill — visiting Mr. M. S. Beach —*

And yet another glorious day — Up with a headache — made a note to Emma after bkfs —, there out & walked until 11 — fresh breeze from the north, there in until dinnertime. at 12.30, put my traps to go to Peekskill & Wf. city.

By 3.50 train got to P— at 5.07 — Chas. B. & wife — Emma B. & Miss Bullard came up from Wf. on 5.18 train — all rode up to M. Beach's — Tea about 6. Mr. M.S.B. and before we finished it — later, Mrs. Col. Beecher came in — pleasant evening general chatting — to bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 6, 1878

overcast myself — with traces of a few drops of rain, but it soon cleared charmingly. Bkfs. at 8 — after that, spent the mf. sauntering over the place — a pleasant rest in summerhouse by the grotto — Dinner at 1.30 — most of the p.m. spent going over H.W. Beecher's new house & sitting up in his piazza — there a walk over the hill & there to tea at 6 — About 8, went out for moonlight walk — Emma, Miss Bullard & I kept it up until about 10 — the night was wonderfully beautiful — then to bed —
