## Left Page

### January, Friday 18, 1878

Charming day — Go to studio.
at 9.35 & to work on "Selp. in
Chemung Valley" — finishing
sky & running midday
over the rest of the picture.
At 12.30 went to Y.M.C.A
Bldg. called on Tiffany & Robbin.
Then to Artists' fund [illegible] &
back to studio & lunch at 2.
Worked as long as I could
see making slow progress.
Cat lo went out & walked —
Tea in studio at 7. Evening
in studio writing & fussing.
To 41° S. at 10.30 & to bed at
11.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 19, 1878

Overcast with, clearing to be a
charming day — After bkfst — I
went to Mrs. Perkins's in 44th S.
to see a W.C. by Miss Eliza Ripley.
Did not get to studio until after
10 o'clk. There to work on 10×14.
About 12.45 went out for a walk.
Lunch about 1.30 — Geo downtown
for fun — I worked until 5 —
went out for a walk — back &
had tea at 5.30 — After that
settling acts with Geo. Then
some writing. Went to Century
org. — there until 11 — there
to 41° S. — & to bed before
midnight.
