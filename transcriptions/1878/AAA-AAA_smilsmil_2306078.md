## Left Page

### October, Friday 25, 1878

Charming day — Up early, had my work & comb — then blkp. &
after that my walk — then to work on pen sketch of the two
brigs o' Ayr — Dinner at 12 &
at 12.45 George left for the city —
he can't stand it here any
longer — I took a short walk
then again at work & finished
my "brigs" — Then got out my
acids & experimented on a
copperplate — about 5 took a
walk — tea at 6 & at 7 with
Kate went to Matt's & with her
to the Opera House & saw some
Tableaux arranged by Mr.
Bartlett of Concord — they did not
amount to much — home at
10½ to bed at o¼.
Rec'd a note from Ella Diven
telling me that Maud was
married to Mr. Silsbee on Oct. 7.
also one from Chas. Parsons with
a Harpers check for $75.00

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 26, 1878

Charming day, but up late with
a headache, owing to dissipation
last night. Bkfo. at 9 o'ck — went
up town after that, to P.O. & to Matt's
& did not get home until 10.30 —
Then to work on "Helen's Sea" for
Wallace Bruce. Dinner at 12.30 —
worked on pen drawing until 3 —
then out with pencils & Baker —
Sketched old pollard willows on So.
Ave — then to Dwight S. — made a
sketch there — then to Champ't. &
made a sketch there — delicious
twilight. To tea at 6 — at J. Chase
am? — part of evening together in
[illegible] room — Matt with us to tea —
Chase & walked up with them
& it was near 11 before we left
& 11.30 before I got to bed.
