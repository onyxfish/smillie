## Left Page

### February, Monday 11, 1878

Clear, winter-like day — got to studio about 9.30 to work on "Lake Placid from Whiteface" 23×40. Painted over sky and distance. About 2 went out & had a short walk — went to N.A. about 2.30 had lunch in studio. Several callers — worked until after 5 — then put studio in order for Etching Club meeting — & walked down to 74th St. & back. Tea at 6.30 — nearly half a dozen of the Etchers came — at 8.30 I went to N.A. — stayed there until 9.15 — at 9.30, with Wood (J.W.) again to studio. Found the Etchers just going away — had a talk with [illegible] — again to N.A. there until 10. Then to studio — fixed fire & to 41 E 8th St. about 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 12, 1878

Pleasant w[eather] — overcast P.M. Got to studio at 9.40 & to work on 23×40 — painting over the water & middle distance. About 1 o'clk. went over to N.A. my "picture" "In the Orchard" marked sold. Back to lunch at 2 — Belle & Mary J—, Mr. Hertzel & Miss Powea — Mary Whitney & Miss Ely called — I painted as long as I could see — at 6.30 went to Kimball's to tea — Geo. & Anna C. & Emma J. — there — after tea Geo. & Anna & I went to N.A., met there Walter C. & his wife — at 6.15 to studio — fixed fire — 7 to 41 E 8th St. about 11 & right to bed.
