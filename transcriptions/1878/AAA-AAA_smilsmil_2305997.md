## Left Page

### May, Monday 6, 1878

Charming day — to studio at 9.45 & to work on W.C. sketch 9×14 — based upon an old Adirondack study I call it "Digging Potatoes" — worked closely and made some progress, altho' bothered by my paper, French Egg Shell for crayons & charcoal. Lunch at 2 — at 4.30 went with Calverly to N.A. & met other members of Council & until 6.30, engaged in judgement upon drawings by scholars in Antique School — an exceedingly difficult task — Tea at Studio at 7 — at 8 again at N.A. & at 8.30 Council in judgement upon work of Life class — at 10.30 to studio, then to 37 S— & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 7, 1878

*at Geo. Hawes's —*

Very pleasant day, partly overcast. Got to studio about 9.30 & to work on "Digging Potatoes" — very soon got it finished & mounted it, then to work on another sketch — Lunch about 2 — got my drawings ready for the League and at 3 started for the foot of Christopher S— met Geo— there & we took the 3.40 train for Montclair — there about 4.30 — Addie waiting for us at depot — we rode about until about 6 — then to her house — soon after Emma J. & Geo. Hawes came & about 7 we had dinner — a very pleasant evening then followed until 11 o'clk. when we went to bed.
