## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 1, 1878

Very fine day altho' cold & windy. Up late,Helpi at
9 — got to studio about 10 —
Some delay because the
Room was so cold — then
to work on 12×18 " By Still
waters" & painted over the
distance & foliage, greatly
improving it. It was after
2 before I took a short walk
& light lunch — then painted
as long as I could see —
Johnston — the old critic of
the Mail called — At 6 o'clk
went to Fanny K's — took tea
& at 8 get & I went to Emma
J's — nearly went to Nellie J's
& almost 10 when we got
to Anna C's — there until
10.40 — then to 41 St. then
saw "Liz" Ely — nearly 12
when finally I got to bed
