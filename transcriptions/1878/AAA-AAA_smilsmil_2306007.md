## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 28. 1878.

Warm with a shower about mid-day — Got to studio
before 10.30 — after going to
da Roche's (dentist) & Knoedler's —
To work on 33 × 44 & painted
in middle distance & water
Geo. down-town — Miss Jacobs
lunched with me at 2.30.
I painted until after
6 — W. Hetzel of Pittsburgh
called — I was so tired
that I did not go to tea
at Kimball's with Geo. —
Had a nap on lounge — then
at 8 had my own tea —
cleaning up until 9 —
then Geo. called — so I did not
go to Holt's but made —
on my way to 34th S. at 11 — met
the Columbia students with band
& torches on their way to bury "the
ancient" — went with them up
to the College — 49th S. & Mad. ave. —
waited through part of the serenades
there to 37th S. — & to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 29. 1878.

Splendid day — up with a mis-
erable headache — just enough
to prevent me working or en-
joying myself — Prof. J.F. Weir's
day of entertainment — at Yale —
Got to studio before 10 & spent
the a.f. in my big chair —
Lunch at 2. During the
afternoon fussed around but
did not accomplish any-
thing of importance — About
went to 37th S. & dressed — then
to Anna C's for a few minutes
then to studio & tea at 7.30 —
At 8.30, Geo. & I at Dr. Bellows' —
no one in — then to "Century" —
an hour with Stoddard — at
10 walked up with S.W. Wood
to 37th S. — & to bed at 10.30.
