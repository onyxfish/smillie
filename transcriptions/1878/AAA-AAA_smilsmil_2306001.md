## Left Page

### May, Thursday 16, 1878

Delightful day — clear &
fresh — 9.45 when I got to
Studio — fussed over mixing
Dutch Mordant according
to Haussment's formula —
three alt Harper beocks until
after 1 — then to Kimball's
& down to Vienna Bakery.
Back to Studio at 2.30 —
Lunch at 3 — then worked
in Miss Abbatt's album —
finishing her sketches —
at 4 met in to Emma J's
I had expected to have her
the K.'s & Anna C. — to tea
in the Studio — but Anna &
Fanny are still out of town —
Back to Studio to tea — then
again in to Emma's — back
to Studio at 9 — made letter
to Farmer (J. C.) on behalf of
Etching Club — To 39° S°
at 11.15 — to bed at [illegible] 45.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 17, 1878

Charming mf. Partially overcast
later — cloudless, moonlight night.
After [illegible] — went to LaRoche's — 65
w. 54". had him look at my teeth
& made an appointment for next
me, from 1 to 3 — walked down the
5 ave. to the St. James & had my hair
cut — 11 30 when I got to Studio —
Spent the rest of the mf biting
in Wood's Etching — "The Man of War" —
the one upon which I failed the
other day — the Dutch Mordant
works favorably — Lunch at 2 —
the rest of p.m. spent biting
a head etched by Wood & another
plate by Nicole — between times
I did a little work for myself out-
lining the block "City of [illegible]" —
went out for a walk at 6.30 — back
soon after 7 — near 8 when we had
tea — went most to Bellows
until 9.30 — then to N. A. of D —
To 34° S. — at 10.30 — to bed before 11.
