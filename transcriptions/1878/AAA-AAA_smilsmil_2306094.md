## Left Page

### November, Saturday 30, 1878

Bright, beautiful day — Got to
studio at 9.30 — Newton waiting for
me — I drew from him for old man
1½ hour — then I got McManus, a
young man & drew from him an
an hour — then at 2 I went to
lunch — back at 3 & finished
drawing — at 4.30 got & I went
by M.E.R.R. to Liberty St. & took 5 o'clk
train to Plainfield — got there just
in time for the Smeedy dinner &
were warmly welcomed — Every
Mr. — & Mrs. Massett, daughter
of Burton, the actor, spent the eveng
at the S's — got to playing whist
at 11 o'clk. & to bed about 2. —

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 1, 1878

Pleasant day, but slightly over-
cast — Help about 9 — after that I
walked down to Uncle J's — met him
& aunt Ollie just leaving for church
so I walked with them & shared their
pew & heard — Ketchum preach a
temperance sermon — walked back
with Uncle J — then to S's — Dinner
soon after 1 — the P.M. until
about 4 — spent in reading, chatting
& napping — then had a little
walk with Florence — Tea about
6 — We, Belle, Mary, Florence, got &
I went to Episcopal Ch. After
service, added Mrs. Massett to our
number & escorted her home —
chatted until after 11 — & then
to bed.
