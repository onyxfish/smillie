### September, Sunday 15, 1878

Raw dismal m[or]n[ing] — wind getting around to the west & clouds lifting — After b[reakfast] for awhile over soap stone stove in parlor — Then to my room & wrote a letter to Father — also, edited a part of the novel written last night then out for a short walk. ## Right Page

### September, Monday 16, 1878

Very fine day — After b[rea]kf[ast] went to Stetson's & with him chained off four acres on the top of Puny Knoll — then to Beede's and fixing my traps went to the river to finish my last study. It was thin about 11 — got color rubbed in & to dinner and back at 2.30 & by close work got study finished at 5.30 — then to house — Tea at 6.30 — After that went to Stetson's & arranged to buy Puny Knoll — back to B's at 8.30 & spent a little while on piazza with Miss H — Mr. Coee & Krueger went to Lake Placid this m[or]n[ing], to return to-morrow — I went to bed with a bad headache at a little after 9.
