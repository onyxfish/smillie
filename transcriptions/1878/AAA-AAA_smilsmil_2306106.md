## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 24, 1878

Very clear, cold day — Got to studio at 9.30 + in spite of two fires, suffered from the cold. Got to studio at 9.30 + commenced seriously to bother in charcoal, 10×14, with sketch for "the Belated Gallant." Dinner at 1.30 — After that, did little but get ready to take 4 o'clk. train for Po. Got to dépôt at 3.40 — very heavy train — Geo. Charl + I went up. Reached home a little after 7 + Jom had a good warm supper — then, a great time unpacking presents — about 9, Charl, Kate + I went to Mary's — I got home about 10, but it was after 11 before I got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 25, 1878

Very cold + windy — Routed out of my bed three times last night + so feel rather seedy to day — Did not get up till after 10 — Spent rest of mt in getting a big fire in back parlor grate — About 1.30, the clans being assembled, we went down to dinner — D. Kendrick + our own family, twelve altogether — we had a very good time — During p.m. — I got ground on my 7×9 plate + made tracing of "Cedars by moonlight" + transferred it to plate — Did not go out for ever a walk — Tea at of D. K. + all hands again at table — a very jolly even't. until 10 — after 11, when I got to bed.
