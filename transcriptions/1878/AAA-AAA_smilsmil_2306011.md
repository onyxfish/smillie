## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 5, 1878

Thick muf., muggy, warm day — breezy cool even't — By at 8.30 — my back so stiff & sore that I could hardly move. At studio worked on block "City of Lourdes" — About 11.30 went to Kelly's & got some canvas, then to Brook's Bro. & got a pair of trousers — Back to studio at 1.30 & had lunch at 2 — on sofa for awhile & at 4 to da. Roche's & had a filling put in one of my teeth — Dom after 5. 5] 37° S— at 6.30, with Geo. to Kimball's to tea, Minna's, then Fanny & Mee went to an engagement after tea — Minna, Geo. & I to send Colmans — met there Mrs. Borie — very pleasant call — at 9.30 went home with Minna — at 10.15 at studio — there until after 11 packing up things & to 34° S— at 11.30 — to bed at once.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 6, 1878

Glorious day — cool, fresh & clear — At house packing trunk until 10.30 — then down town by 5yth Ave. Elevated R.R. to Charle's niel's — Sharp's & Harper's — then to studio at 1 — went with Geo. in to Colman's to lunch — He & Mrs. S— go to Greenwich, Conn. this P.M. to visit Foster Higgins — Back to studio about 2.15 & all the afternoon spent in getting things ready to leave for the summer. Took my tea in studio and at 8.15 to 37° S— with [illegible] — then before 9 to Anna Coale's in 38th S— a little musical party — was chilly & out of sorts & at 10.30 excused myself — went to 37th S— & worked over my trunk until 11.30 — then to bed — had but a poor night's rest —
