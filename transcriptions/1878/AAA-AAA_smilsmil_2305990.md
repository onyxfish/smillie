## Left Page

### April, Monday 22, 1878

Pleasant day, but overcast with considerable wind
got to Studio at 9.45 — set
palette & went over the
two Colgate Riverdale views
glazing & touching on higher
lights. Lunch at 1.30 —
Afternoon finished the
little sketch that I intend
giving to Mr. Geo. Whitney.
Jet went to Brooklyn to
dine with the Sackets — J
took a short walk & tea
at Studio at 7 — at 8 to N.A.
Council meeting — then
until 10 — then to Studio
until 10.45 — then to 41"
St. & to bed. —

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 23, 1878

overcast but not unpleasant
Up late. Bkft. after 9 — then
to the Cottier Coll. of pictures
at Leavitt's — met W. Geo. Whitney
got to Studio at 12 — Lunch
abt — then commenced
work on Water Color "Butternuts
in Chemung Valley" — fussed
with sky washes until aft.
6 — then to Fanny K's to tea
at 8 — went to Cottier Sale
at As'n. Hall — at 9 to Studio
— at 10 o'clk. to N.A. — then
to 41st St. & to bed at 11.
