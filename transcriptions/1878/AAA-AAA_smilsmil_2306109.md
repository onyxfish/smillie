## Left Page

### December, Monday 30. 1878.

Glorious day — slight fall of snow during the night. After bkfst. to work retouching etching. At noon, walked to P.O. & back. Dinner at 12.45 — finished biting — pulled a proof & laid ground over entire plate — dismayed by appearance of pf. — Pulled several pfts. for Gee from his first etching — then cleaned ups mess — fixed my fire in the parlor & at 5.15 went out for my walk. Delicious evef. — clear & cold — bewitching effects of twilight over snow — stars twinkling in clear sky & young moon giving just enough light to make everything look mysterious. A short call on many — to tea at 6.15 — Evef. in parlor — a little reading — a little writing & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 31. 1878.

Glorious day — After bkfst. — got my plate varnished & all ready to begin my cross etching — then made notes to W. Jo. Lord & Rob. S. Hale — about 10 went to P.O. then to Matt's — then to my work — all the o' bothered with a nasty headache. Soon after 12 went out to hill on Livingstone St. near Toy's & made a couple of hasty sketches — then home to dinner at 12.45 — Crossed & hatched over all the sky of etching & most of the rest of it — then bit & at 4 cleaned plate — hardly know yet how it looks — Gee went to N.Y. on 4.50 train — I walked from 5 to 6 — delicious evef. Tea at 6.30. Evef. made cards to Misses Waller & Bullard & at 8.30 took them to P.O. At 9.30 gave up to my headache & went to bed where I spent a miserable night —
So ends the year 1878.
