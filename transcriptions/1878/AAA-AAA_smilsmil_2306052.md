## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 28, 1878

Got up in the mf. with an outrageous headache — ate a light bkfst — but before 9 was obliged to give up & go back to bed again where I stayed until after 8 P.m. — then got up & had a little to eat — quite a change in the social atmosphere of the place — the noisy & roistering party has gone & in its place has come a musical element that — resulted in quite a concert during the evng. During the day some strolling musicians came along with harps & violins but they left in the afternoon. Pleasant but warm morning — heavy rain in the afternoon — Dark night — to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 29, 1878

Very warm & overcast. Up soon after 6 — Bkft. about 7.30. Feeling the effects of yesterday's headache I did little else than lie about during the morning. Did some final visiting with the Colemans & Tweedys who leave this afternoon. Dinner soon after 1 — then quite a heavy shower — before it was fairly over the C.s & J.'s got away for E-town. It rained most of the P.m. — wetly charmingly clear. Sea about 7 — Pretty on Piazza as usual — to bed soon after 10.
