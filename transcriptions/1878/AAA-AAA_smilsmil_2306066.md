## Left Page

### September, Sunday 29, 1878

Beautiful mng. B[illegible] at 8 — wrote a letter to
Robbins & then to church
(Christs) & heard Rev. W.
Feigenfuss on angels —
Then home to dinner
at 1 — Between 2 & 3
went out with father
& had a stroll — Mat 4
wrote a note to Miss Durfe
Tea at 6 — Eveg, walked
up & down in front of the
house & finally walked
with mother to church
St & back — to bed at
10.30 —

## Right Page

### September, Monday 30, 1878

Very mild, slightly overcast
day — Rec'd a note from Miss
Durfee & had to write an-
other note to her — Walked
until near 10 — about 11
when I got to work on
arms of Va. for C.B.N. Co.
Dinner at 12.30, walked
to P.O. again at work
at 3 & stuck closely at
it until 5.30 — then my
head & eyes felt very tired.
Took a short walk & had
tea a little after 6 —
Eveg, went up to Matt's
for Kate & brought her
home about 9.30 & to
bed at 10.
