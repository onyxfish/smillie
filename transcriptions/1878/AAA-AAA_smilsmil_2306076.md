## Left Page

### October, Monday 21, 1878

Supreme october — balmy,
dreamy — delicious — all day
helping that Coleman & wife
would come up — but they did not.
After bkfst. went out & sketched
on Jefferson St. until near 11, then
in & rebit zinc plate — Dinner
at 12.30 — after that went up town
& to the hotels to enquire after
the C's — back at 2 & got to
work on pen drawing of Brig o'
Doon for Wallace Bruce — At
it until 5 — then out with Fath[er]
went to Mary's — there found
mother & geo. I walked out by
Ch. St. to Cherry — thence by Seminary
home at 6 & had tea — Rec'd a note
from Mrs C — saying that they had
given up the idea of coming to
P.O. — also from Ella Devon — one
dated Sep. 6 — been in Montrose P.O.
ever since — note to see Mrs. C.
— at 9 o'clk. Read aloud to the family
a Stopford Brooks sermon on
Expenditures — then to P.O. with
my letters — to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 22, 1878

another intoxicating day —
After bkfst. went up town on an
errand — then to work on pen
view of Brig o' Doon — At 11 o'clk.
went out to Jefferson St. & worked
on pencil sketch until 12.30
then home to dinner — After
that, a little general work
on Harper drawings & at 3
commenced proving etchings
& worked at that as long as I
could see — Tea at 6 — Well
fasten Mother & Kate went to Mr.
Stull's, 80 Liberty St. to call — I
wrote a letter to Parsons & put
my drawings in a pkg — to send
to the Harpers. went to [illegible] office
& P.O. before 9 — then to Mary's
visited with her until near
10 — then home & to bed at
10.30.
