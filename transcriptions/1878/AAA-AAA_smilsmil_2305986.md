## Left Page

### April, Sunday 14. 1878

Exquisite day — Up early
Bkfst before 8 — Elevated R.R.
at 42d. 8t. & 9t ave. at 8.46 to liberty
5t — there at 9.10 — then, Jersey
Central at 9.30 to Pamurapo,
wandered about with Charley,
& admired his chickens until
2 o'clk — then dinner — at 3,
out again — the air, intoxicating
— the color, beyond description —
especially so, half an hour before
sundown — then the trees up
the bay to N.Y. city Realized
the richest dream of Venice
& the Adriatic — Tea about 4 &
at 8.8 took train in to N.Y.
glorious moonlight — at 9.30
got to 41. 8= & to bed before 10.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 15. 1878

Very pleasant day — Got to
Studio at 9.45 & to work on out-
line of "Butternuts — Newtown Creek"
which I finished before lunch
between 1 & 2 . Then to work
outlining on gelatine, photograph
for a Harper block which kept
me out of mischief until near
6 — then out, did some messages
& Rid? to Tea about 7 — at
8 went to N.A. Council meeting
visited the Schools & then
the principal work was to
make up the list of candidates
for Academy honors — Adjourned
at 10.30 — then to Studio & at
11.30 to 41. 8= & to bed.
