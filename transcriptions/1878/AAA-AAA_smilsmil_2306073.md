## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 15, 1878

Beautiful day — After bkfs —
writing until near 10. then
to P.O. & back — help photos of
Scotch Scenery for Wallace Bruce
were sent to me this mng, they
are very beautiful. To work
on pen sketch of "the Huddle —
Lake Geo." — dinner at 12.30 —
finished Huddle & made
pen sketch of Cottage near
Hewlett's Landing — finished
it before 5 — then out and
walked with Father until
6 — then tea — Evening
reading until about 9 — then
out & walked for half an hour
then in & to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 16, 1878

Beautiful day — After bkfs —
mate awhile — then out for
a walk — nearly where I got
to my work — came to the
conclusion that I did not like
pen sketch of "Huddle", so I
made a new one on tinted
board & touched it with
white. Dinner at 12.30 —
worked until near 5 finishing
Huddle — then out for a walk.
Tea at 6 — Even't. Set to work
to compile a dummy for
Wallace Bruce's poem — at
it until 9 o'clk — then out
& walked for nearly an hour
then in & to bed soon after
10.
