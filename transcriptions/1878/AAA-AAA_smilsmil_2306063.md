## Left Page

### September, Saturday 21, 1878

Home, Po'Keepsie

Up soon after 6 — overcast. Bkfp — about 7 — after that, to Day boat "Drew" — leaving Geo.
+pkfp— I went to see the new State Hse an unartistic but expensive thing — back to boat which left at 8.40 — soon, a light spatter of Rain, but the trip was altogether pleasant. altho' somewhat tedious — Got to Po' at 1.20 + right to the house — found Fanny K. Then Dinner at once — After that, ins iting trunk packing trunk — Fanny + Kate went out riding with Matt + Ed. Late to tea near 7 — Heavy shower early in the eveg + light Rain after — Matt + Ed went home som after 9 + I got to bed som after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 22, 1878

Clear, crisp, cold day. Bkfp — at 8.30 — at 10 went to Matt's with Fanny + from there to Baptist 8.5 — then in to service — Dr. R. preached on the kingdom of Christ — after service went to Matt's — here until near 1 then home just in time for dinner — About 3.30 gave up to a nap which lasted until after 4 — at 5 Fanny, Geo + I went out + walked until 6 — then in to tea — Eveg, all went to church except Mother + I — we stayed at home + gossiped — To bed at 10.15.
