## Left Page

### February, Monday 25, 1878

Rather cold & blowy — Got to Studio's a little before 10 & to work again Repainting sky on "Placid" — Very hard work for me to get color & light and grey — In spite of a number of callers I got over all the sky & distance — Lunch about 2 — 9 M. Carleton called — About 6 o'clk — I went to Louis T. Pfauv's to see him about Water Colors for Paris $46 — Stayed to dinner at 8 o'clk — to N.A. School Con. with Castevecchi (Plaster-man) until 9 o'clk — arranging to get the Antique School casts into perfect order — then Council meeting until 11 o'clk — then to Studios — there until after 12 — then to 41. 82 & to bed about 12.30.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 26, 1878

Fine winter day — Up late and did not get to Studio's until almost 10 — Anna Cook had already been there with invitation for to-night. To work on "Placid" and painted over middle distance, water & a little in foreground — Lunch at 2 — no walk as usual. More callers again in the afternoon — It was nearly 6 when I got away & went to Mr. Chas. T. Pfauv's — 255 Mad. ave. Kept in a pleasant call for a few minutes — then to Kimball's at 6.30 to tea — Matt & Kate there just down from Po'. — At 8.30, with Alf. K. went to N.A. — there until 9 — then to Anna C's & found there A. C., Emma D., Mary Willard, Miss Wood & Helen Butler & Mrs. Ned Cook — no gentlemen — Later, Med. Geo. & Valentine came in — About 11 I went to 41. 82. & to bed.
