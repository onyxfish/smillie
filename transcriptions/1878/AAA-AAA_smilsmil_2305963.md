## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 27, 1878

Charming Day — Got to studio at 9.30 & got out 11×17 "Sep-day — with Cows" — Matt, Kate & Fanny came in & spent much of the mg — I worked over sky — improved it — Lunch at 2 o'clk — no walk — then on white cow — callers — cleaned up & about 5.30 went over to N.A. — then to "Century" and got my hat — then walked until after 4 — then to studio & tea — Mrs. Shurcleff came in & visited until after 8 — then I made & made paste, jiped some funny etchings & at 9.30 went to N.A. to 4H' S. at 10.30 & right to bed:

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 28, 1878

Another charming day — Near 10 before I got to studio — To work on 11×17 & painted over standing cow — did little work owing to interruptions from callers — Just as we were finishing lunch, about 2.30 Stoddard came in & made a long call — I finished my cow when Mr. Jeirde, a model, came in & for an hour I drew from her — Chase called at a little before 6 — I walked down to "Century" & about — back to studio & tea at 7 — After that studying my "Raid" by gaslight — some writing etc — & also went to Union League Club to see the my fine collection of foreign pictures — there until near 10 — then to N.A. & at 10.30 to 4H' S — to bed about 11 —
