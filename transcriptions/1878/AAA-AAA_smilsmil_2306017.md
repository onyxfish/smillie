## Left Page

### June, Monday 17, 1878

Shower in early myf — close, & mostly overcast day — aftn
blfp. went to Wallace Bruce's
got myf. f "I and of Burns" — tun
a walk & got home at 10.30 —
Read & wrote — Uncle W. &
Aunt A. called at 11.45 — dinner
at 1 — after that finished
letter to Maud D. — Drew plans
of new sketch-box & after tea
at 6 went to Group's with
it. Back home at 8 — about
8.30 Will, Joe, Minn., Miss Shaffer
& Mr. Walter Pierce came — just
from Myf. by "Mary Powell" — they
were served with a hearty
meal about 9 — at 10.30 I left
there & camped out on the
sofa in father's engraving
room — did not get much
sleep.
[illegible] to bed & back [illegible].

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 18, 1878

overcast myf. Blfp. at 8 —
after that until 10, about the
house & on the Roof. The Rest
of the myf. soon got away &
nothing done — Dinner soon
after 1 — & at 2.15 Uncle Willie
am. with his turn out & took the
party off to drive — they took tea
at Hill Side & did not return
until near 6 p.m. I made
a note to Jo. F. Meir — then
took sketch book & went out
to Fox's lot — got sketches of
cedars — tea at 6.30 — then
made sketch from my sketch
for etching — about 8 went to
Mary's with Mother — back with
her soon after 9 — found the
party ret'd & Matt & Ed. also
there — at 9.30 with Geo. went
to Mary's & stayed there over
night — to bed at 10. The
Dr. with Myf. [illegible].
