## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 8. 1878.

Up soon after 6 — charming m'f — B'kf's — about ¼ — after that some wandering & at 8 went down the road to see the pony get a lesson he refusing to carry Addie's eldest to school — then to the home & all went out for a ride — Had a delightful time, gathering wild Azalias and filling our lungs with the sweetest of air. At 10 we were left at the depot — got to studio about 11.30 — Rested awhile — got water colors ready for H.D.C. — then lunch & about 1.30 to N.A. 1D — The rest of the afternoon was spent going through the routine of the annual meeting with its endless balloting — No leader appeared & everything went by chance — through no objection to me, but nearest chance. I was dropped from the council — I was very nearly elected then upon the Hanging Com. — but positively refused to serve thinking that under the circumstances it was an imposition upon my good nature. As a member of the dinner committee I was active until 7 o'clk. when the dinner was served — then until near 11 the dinner was in progress with all sorts of fun & some speech making — I responded very briefly for the water color body — To 39th St at 11.15 — to bed at 11.30 & to sleep soon after 12.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 9. 1878.

Heavy Rain in the m'f which continued until about noon — heavily overcast during the rest of the day. B'kf's — at 8.30 — then to studio, then to Union League club about 10 & to work with Nicole hanging water colors — ab't o'clk. lunched with N. — in the Restaurant of the club at expense of Art Com. Finished the hanging at 3 — then to studio & had a rest on lounge — then mtg. Tea about 7 — bet'n 8 & 9 went to Union League club — There until near 11 o'clk. As the Club had an exciting business meeting that did not break up until 11. I did not wait for the Art Com. & the Supper but went to 39th S. — & to bed by 11.30.
