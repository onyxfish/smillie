## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 3, 1878

Warm m[illegible] with beautiful clouds that threatened a storm
that did not come. Got to work at 9.15 on study by River side —
the water having risen makes it difficult for me to keep my
place & see my work. Miss Way crossed my log without help.
Dinner about 1.30. Afternoon overcast. Spent some time
on top of cut old studying nts— then went to River & cut out
a place for a new study and charcoaled it — then 6 o'clk —
then to tea. Dark night with low, oppressive clouds.
On piazza part of time. Miss Kate Hillard gave some ballad
recitations in the parlor —
Washed brushes & got to bed about 11 —
[illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 4, 1878

Heavily overcast m[illegible]. Got up with a headache. After b[rea]kf[as]t,
about 9.45 got away with Miss Hillard & Mr. Cole & Nuper for
a tramp, up Gill Brook — a slight drizzling rain falling
most of the time. Followed the road as long as we could
see or hear the brook, then took the brook & followed it up until
we came to some beautiful falls & cascades. Went until after 12
when dinner obliged us turn back. Got to hotel at 1.40 very
dirty, bedraggled & tired. Dinner at 2 — then read & had a nap
as my head still felt badly.
Then a little reading & writing.
Tea at 7. Even[ing] on piazza —
to bed between 10 & 11.
