## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 30, 1878

Overcast mng. Got to shop at 9.30 & to work on block.
"Roman Viaduct near Nismes" — About 1.30 went
to the Kimball's to tell them that we would not be there
to tea — Took lunch with them — back to studio
after a visit to the school at 2.45 & by sticking to
my work, finished block —
Abbey — T.H. Smith — Chase
father & others came in —
At 6 went out for a walk
— went again to [illegible]'s —
back at 4 — Tea at 4.30 —
Father read until 8.30
then he went up to Chase's —
I wrote until about 9 —
then to N.A., met a number
of acquaintances — Minnie Smillie
& others — to 41st St at 10 & to
bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 1, 1878

Up early & packed trunk
as the Hins[illegible]wood gives move
to-day to 21 W. 34th St. To help —
at 8. Overcast & raw omng.
Clearing later — Rain after 5
P.M. Stopped at 37th St house,
then to studio at 9.45 & to
work on W.C. At 6 o'clk. went
out for a walk — Lunch after
2 — Robbins called — worked
until nearly on W.C. — Miss
Abbott left for her Westchester
home to-day — Chase came
in — Mr. Jno. G. Chapmen called.
At 6.30 went out & sauntered
about Mad. Sq. in the rain —
Tea at 4 — Father with us.
Every mate on Etching Club
matters until a little after
10 — then to 37th St & to bed in
our new quarters.
