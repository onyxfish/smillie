## Left Page

### August, Saturday 24, 1878

Pleasant m'f. About 9 got to work on 1'i Rock study across the River & pegged away at it until about 2.30. By this time a haze spread over the sky — went up to the house — dinner soon after — at 2.30 again to my study. Coleman made me a call — weather getting rapidly thicker about 4.30 Rain began to fall & at 5 I gave it up, but got my study into such shape that I will do no more on it — Before 6. Bellet Jenny Tweedy, Mr. Meyers May Tiffany & Eastman Johnston arr. from Lake Placid in a pouring Rain — but all jolly — Tea soon after 6 — most of evng. indoors as the Rain drove us from the piazza — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 25, 1878

Heavy Rain during the night and most of the morning — Got up with a mean headache which did not entirely leave me all day. Some beautiful cloud effects over the Giant — wrote to father & Dewey — Dinner at 2. Sat 4 went with Eastman Johnston down to my aunt's Studio, then to Chas. Dudley Warner's. Had a very pleasant call there, met Mr. Lounans as well as Mr. thus Warner. The wind getting to the North it was very cool & had cleared charmingly. Back to Beede's about 7.30 — Tea at once — Evng. between parlor & piazza — To bed about 11.
