## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 10, 1878

Disagreeable day — got to
studio at 9.45 & to work on
Harper block — Many interruptions — Wilson called — then
Harrison — then Nicole — and
Gifford & Coleman — & [illegible]
Duryee — & later Wallace
Bruce & a P. Thorup — At
1 o'clk I went to Sauthier
to see him about a rug
that he is to give me to art
payment for my painting
by Patron's — back to studio
and lunch at 2.30 — Rain
heavy — worked on block
as long as I could see —
then an lounge for a rest
to my aching head — Tea
at 4 — Every in studio
Reading & writing — To 41 S.
about 10.45 after going over to
N.A. for a few minutes —
to bed at 11 —

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 11, 1878

Dismal rainy day — got to
studio at 9.30 and to work on
Harper block, street scene in a
German City — worked until
11 — then to foot of Christopher
St. where I met the Cooks, De-
lands & Kimballs in a party to
bid Miss [illegible] Home goodbye —
as she sailed for Liverpool on
the "Baltic". The clerk of the weather
was for the moment, accomodating &
miss H— got away in fine style — then
to lunch with Elenia — very heavy
rain — back to studio soon after 2
& finished block — Tea about
6.30 — About 8.30 — Jee & I went
to Union League for half an hour
then to Anna Cook's — a piano
quartette — there until near
11 — then to 41 S. & to bed.
