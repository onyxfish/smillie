## Left Page

### May, Friday 10, 1878

Fresh & cool day — After
bkfs — went to Studio, then
about 10.30 — went downtown
to the Harper's — saw [illegible] —
had a talk about work —
got two blocks for "The Land &
the Book" — then to Scovill
Manuf & Co. in Broome St —
& bought India Rubber
Tray for "biting" in — then
walked up to Studio,
getting there about 2 —
there lunch — then out.
Lined on gelatine a Harper
subject — Tea near 7 — at
8.30, at Geo. Comstocks until
10.30 — Priscilla Poore there —
then walked down to 37th
St — & to bed soon after 11 —
Several heavy showers
fell between 3 & 6 —

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 11, 1878

*Plainfield — N.J.*
*at O. B. Tweedy's —*

Charming m[or]f, but quite
cool — Suffering from a
headache, against which I
was barely able to hold up
all day — Got to studio bef[ore]
10 — Tried to do some of Harper
wood drawing but could not.
A lunch of Graham [illegible] at
2 o'clk & about 3, with Geo.
started for foot of Liberty St —
Caught 4 o'clk train & got
to the Tweedy's soon after
5 — before 6 I was obliged
to give up & go to bed —
Passed a miserable
night.
Mrs Geo. Comstock — Jessie,
Priscilla & her little girl
called in the m[or]f.
