## Left Page

### September, Sunday 1. 1878.

Heavy Rain during the night — also the mts. Mainly overcast —
Up at 7. Bkf. about 8 — on piazza
until 11, then wrote letters
until near dinner time at 2
again on piazza until about
4, then started out for a
walk with Nellie & Marie Jacob
& Gel but before we got over to
Smith Reed's old house a
shower overtook us — Waited
there for about an hour, then
to hotel — Tea at 7 — Evef
spent as usual on piazza
and to bed. Soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 2. 1878.

Overcast with — unfit for work
out of doors & my head felt
equally unlike it — after some
time on top of cupola studying
the mts I started into the woods
S.E. from the house — went over first
ridge to Sandy Gap brook, followed
that for a time — very fine rocks &
moss — then crossed next ridge
& to cliffs opposite — sens[ible?]
— then down into that ravine
& out up on Beaver Meadow and
chapel Pond brook — Heavy Rain
came up & I ret'd. to hotel in
the midst of a drencher at 1 o'clk.
Dinner soon after. on piazza
most of the afternoon thinking
about doing something — Some
clearing weather — Tea after 6 —
clear evef with first sight
of our new moon — a very wet one
Evef on piazza & to bed at
about 11 —
