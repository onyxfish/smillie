## Left Page

### November, Monday 4, 1878

Splendid winter day. Ther. 26° at sunrise. After my walk I started work on my etching No. 1, view cor. Reade & Jefferson St — worked away until 12.15, then had a short walk — dinner at 12.45 — & again at etching, had acid on twice during the day — stopped work soon after 4 — Before 5 Mr. & Mrs. Peck & young P— called. I went out to walk & had a brisk & exceedingly enjoyable one — Tea at 6 — Evetf went to Hultpen's House & saw the Florences in "The Mighty Dollar" — a half filled row slow to take points & a decided second rate performance — I was amused between audience, actors & play until near 11 o'clk in spite of draughts & cold feet then home & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 5, 1878

Another clear wintry day — Brkf late — then a hurried walk up town & back & to work at my etching — Spent the mf getting ready for the acid — at 12.15 went out & had a short walk — then dinner — then I put my plate in the acid & it started off brankly — then again etching until about 3.30 when I gave it the last touch of acid & cleared my plate — altho' the acid has been on in the darkest places 40 minutes — it is too light — At 5 I went to Mary's for Mother, brought her home & then had a sharp walk alone — Beautiful effects of evening & moonlight — In to tea 6.15 — Evetf made a letter to J.C. — Nicole & made some slight sketches — took letter to P.O. & to bed at 10.30.
