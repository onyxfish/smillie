## Left Page

### March, Sunday 31, 1878

Charming spring day, with sunlight & shadow - ending
with rain late in the p.m.
& [illegible]. B[illegible] at 9.30 - at
10.30 at studio - went to N.a.
to give my sketch to Graphic
man - found so many artists
there that my n[illegible] was gone
almost before I knew it -
All went to Hertzel's to dine
Miss Watrons there — left at 3.
Stopped at Anna Cook's -
Got to studio soon after 4 -
Read & mate - tea about
4 - Evef went to Dr. Crosby's
& heard from him a sort
of Bible Reading - Part of one
of Paul's Epistles to Huttebrews.
Back to studio at 9 - there
until 10.30 — then to 41'
S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 1, 1878

Cloudy m[illegible], clearing to be a
beautiful day — Got to N.A.
(Buyer's Day) about 10 o'clk and
waited about there until 1 o'clk.
hoping to interest some one
enough to induce them to buy
a picture, but, altho' many rich
men were there only few more
pictures were sold - the failure
was dismal — Lunch at studio -
There until after 4 o'clk -
Then with Dr. Yale to N.A. -
There until near 6 - There again
to studio & took a rest on lounge
- tea about 4 - then to 41' S. &
dressed — at 9 at N.A. and
submitted to the crush until
about 11 — then to 41' S. and
to bed —
