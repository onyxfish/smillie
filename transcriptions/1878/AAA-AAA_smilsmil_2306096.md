## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 4, 1878

Rain in the m'f— but cleared before 10 — up late — bkf'. later & did not get to Studio until 10 — Spent the m'f clearing up about my easel, then to work with charcoal, planning hirleight 10×20 — at 2, went out to lunch & met Wallace Bruce — Ret'd to Studio with him & made my score about the printing of his book — he paid tal, due me — $112.50 — got lunch about 3 — worked on Charcoal until 4.30 — a short rest on lounge — then to Bur[illegible]'s & back to tea at 6.45 — Wet'f mounting etchings until 10 — then to 37t' St. & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 5, 1878

Fine winter day — quite cold & muddy — got to studio at 9.30 — very much interfered with by callers before I got to my work on blocks for Harpers that have been photos' — upon afternoon designs that I made last Oct — worked over "Cliff near the summit of Blk. Mt." At 1.30 Anna Cook came with a ticket for Symphony Rehearsal — I rushed out, got my lunch & at 2.30 was at Steinway Hall — Enjoyed the Rehearsal very much — sat for a time with Fanny Payson — after — went with h.c. — then to studio — there to Dewry's Gauthier's — to Century & to Studio at 6.30 — had Tea — Wet'f mounting etchings — In the m'f Yale called — P.M. Kate Hillard called to 37t' St. at 10.30 — To bed at 11.
