## Left Page

### April, Thursday 4, 1878

Pleasant day — 9.30 min I got to Studio — set palette
& to work on little odds &
ends — Finished Emma J's little
sketch — then on another
little 4×6 — Annie Cook
came in & surprised me
in a 6 inching match with
N.S.J. Lunch at 2.30 — after
a protracted call from Mr.
Calvert Vaux. P.M., finished
foreground of Owl's Head —
Father came in about 5.30
& took tea with us. at 7.45
He & Jee went off to the Aquarium
& I went in to S. Coleman's & met
with them & Emma J. up to
Anna Cook's where Emma —
Anna — Fanny K. & Mrs. C —
played Schubert's Symphony
& Tannhauser overture — 8 hand.
At 11 walked down with Emma
— back to 41: 8= at 11.45 — and
right to bed —

## Right Page

### April, Friday 5, 1878

Overcast but pleasant —
Father breakfasted with us
at the Hotel Royal soon after
8 — He goes home this P.M. —
Got to Studio at 9.30 — very
much bothered with a head
ache — Mr. Vaux came in
I fussed over foreground on
Owl's Head — but did not
manage to do much — at
1 oclock. had lunch — at 2
at Phil. Rehearsal — after
that went to Leavitt's — saw
Lauthier about a Rug — he has
promised to make exchanges
for my Patron's picture — then
to see Avery's collection — met
Mr. French of Boston, there —
To Studio at 6 — tea at 7 — at
8.15 to Albemarle & called on
Miss McGraw of Ithica — at 9.30
to 41: a. at 9.30 to Studio —
wrote. and at 11 to 41: 8=
& to bed —
