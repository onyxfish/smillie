## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 25, 1878

Milky sort of day with high N.W. wind. Got up feeling very shaky — don't know what's the matter — I say I think I'm malariated. After b'kf't — went to Gronp's by a round about way & did not get home until 11.45. Dinner soon after 1 — then to work on Harper block & kept at it until 4.30 — progress very unsatisfactory. Aunt Jessie & Emma spent part of p.m. & took tea with us — I walked out to Gronp's — back at 5.45 — Tea at 6. Everyf, mth ge called on Mrs. Hull — She excused herself — Then I called on Carrie Fondan — There until near 10 — then home and to bed at 10.30 [illegible — ~2 words]

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 26, 1878

Charming day — took a walk after b'kf't — & about 10 to work on Harper's block. Don't yet feel well enough to work with any sp'rits. Dinner at 1 — Worked on block until 4.30 — then took sketching traps & went to Academy Newington — & made a pencil sketch — then to Livingston's woods & made some slight sketches there. Home at 6.30 & found Uncle W. & Aunt A. there — they took tea with us. After tea I went to Gronp's — then to Matt's — then to Mary's & home at 9.30 — Wrote a letter to Nicole & to bed about 10.45.
