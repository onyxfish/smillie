## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 18. 1878.

Another fine day, mist a snow flurry in the afternoon — Got out
with a headache which increased during the day — Got to studio
about 10 after calling on Miss [illegible] but was not able to work — Kept
fussing about until after 1 — then out & took a light lunch — then
to Brevoortt's studio in 2½ S —
there for half an hour, then
to Ferguson's — then had to give up & return to studio — able
to take some tea about 4 — [illegible]
complaining too — on sofa until about 10 — then feeling
better got up & wrote a couple
of notes — Abel went to 37 S
& to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 19. 1878.

Still very pleasant weather —
to studio at 9.30 & commenced
a sketch in monotone of Cedars
by moonlight 6¼ × 8½ for etching.
Much bothered by callers, but
got it done before I went to lunch
at 1.30 — on my return began
a sketch in color, 10 × 14 — Nov,
wet — "Burning leaves" — By dark
at 4.30, I had got it rubbed
in — at 6.15 at Anna Cook's
to tea — at 8, took her & Fanny M.
to Miss Arnold's & then went to
studio. At 10.15 again at Miss
[illegible] — waited for the girls
until 10.45 — then home with
H. C. & walked with Will and
Fanny down to 28th S — then back
to 37th & to bed about 11.30
