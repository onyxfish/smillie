## Left Page

### March, Monday 25, 1878

Cold wind all day — overcast sky & snow flurries on the Catskiee mts. After bkfst — mate letters until 10 o'clk — then to P.O. — back home & prepared to print — Dinner abt — then to work printing three small plates etched along time ago by Sam. Coleman — Tea time when I finished — after tea went up town — at Mary's until aftnq — then to Watts — Kate there — Home at 10.30 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 26, 1878

Charming day — After bkfst — went up town — back about 10 & rebit a small portion of fam. Jane — then arranged to print my large paper pfs — Dinner at 1 — then to printing — all the afternoon hard at it — things working pretty well after a time — got about half a dozen India & Japan paper pfs. Then tea — Well, met down more to taken, fussed about until aftnq — then with Ge. went up to Mary's & stayed there all night as the Dr. has gone home to see his father who is reported dying — Got to bed about 10.30.
