## Left Page

### May, Thursday 30, 1878

*Decoration Day —*

Dull mrf. Rain commuing about 11 & falling heavily in showers through the day. Got to Studio at 9.30 & to work on middle foreground on 33×44. Col. Jas. Fairman came in & made a long call, not at all to the advantage of my work. Lunch at 2 — worked until 6.30 — getting my "Tent" done — Tea at 7.30 & about 8.30 in to Coleman's where we had a delightful evef of music. Mosenthal played on violin & piano & there were duetts & quartetts — Mrs. C — Emma — Anna C. & Miss Blake — Mosenthal, Gifford, Col. que & I all that were present until later when Geo. Cook came — To 39's — at 11.30 — To bed a little before 12 —

## Right Page

### May, Friday 31, 1878

Horrible day of wind & heavy rain — Got to Studio before 10 & to work on 33×44 laying in the foreground Rocks — I felt the influence of the weather but managed to get my canvas covered & my first painting in finished — It is, I think, the best I have yet done & encourages me. Lunch at 2 — & Tea at 4.30 — Evefg in studio — washing brushes, clearing up and writing. To 39ths St — & to bed about 11 o'clk. [illegible — ~3 lines]
