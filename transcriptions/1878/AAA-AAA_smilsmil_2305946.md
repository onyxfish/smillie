## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 22. 1878.

Clearing off day — Alright —
very high wind & cold —
got to Studio's about 9.40.
& to work on "S. cp. in Cherry
Valley" — Father came before
noon from Po. Lunch about
2 — I worked as late
as I could see & got my
picture finished after a
fashion — At 6 went to
Fanny K's — Tea — Messrs
Gregory there — At 8, with
go —, Took Fanny & Annie to
their singing club — Then to
Studio's where I worked
until after 10 cutting
mounting board & fixing
my etchings — To 44.
about 11 & Right to bed

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 23. 1878.

Cold day with very high wind.
Farther Took Help — with us at
Hotel Royal — I got to Studio's
soon after 9 — Mr. G. P. Whitney
came in for a few minutes in
the mg — my time was given
up to finishing up drawings
& etchings & getting them
into their frames — Rossell
carted all the W.C.'s out of the
building — Lunch after 2 —
Tried to start a small W.C.
— too much hurry-scurry — went
to N.A. & to Abbey — then
a short walk — Tea at Studio's
about 6.30 — Evening writing
notes to Richards, Wilcox, etc
& mounting drawings — to
41 & 8; ab't 10.40 — & Right to
bed.
Frd. Smillie married Miss Ward
at Newark this P.M. Father
went down there.
