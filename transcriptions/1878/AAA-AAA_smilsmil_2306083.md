## Left Page

### November, Friday 8, 1878

Windy & cold - after bkfp - Took a walk
then in & at work on etchings, laid
re-biting grounds on three plates,
then to work on Bruce's work - Re-
touching some of the designs almost
done - after 12 a short walk, then
in to dinner - after that again
biting plates, varnishing & working
with the point until 3 - then
commenced on [illegible] to Kelvin in Burns'
mausoleum at Dumfries & worked
as long as I could see - walked
from 5 to 6 - stopped at Mamp's
for a few minutes - Tea - then
read for awhile. Then bit in
some etching on W. H. (line.) & rebit
foregrounds, the two grounds being on
different parts of the same plate.
After that went out & had a
walk in magnificent moonlight
- back about 10.30 & to bed before
11.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 9, 1878

Windy, but a charming day. After
bkfp - Took my walk as usual -
then a little work over my etching
plates, then on Bruce's work - Tomb of
Burns in Mausoleum at Dumfries -
At 12, a short, brisk walk - then
dinner & again at pen work -
About 3.30 rebit on No. 6 (Hay
ricks) & at 5 out to walk - Paid
bills of Butcher & Grocer & went to
Matts - Tea at 6.30 - until 8 I was
engaged cutting paper, preparing
to prove my etchings - then to
Wallace B's with my pen work -
He had a couple of the photo engs. &
from what I learned from them I might
back the entire lot to revise & add a
little more work. Glorious night & at
10.30 I went to bed - Bruce gave
me a check for $100. 00.
