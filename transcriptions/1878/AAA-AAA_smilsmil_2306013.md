## Left Page

### June, Sunday 9, 1878

Clouds & wind in the mntf — the afternoon very charming — very slim after yesterdays experience
took a short walk in the mntf — Dinner at home at 1 — In the afternoon, Reading and lounging about — Tea at 6.30 — as my trunk has not yet arrived, I wrote to Am. Exps. Co — to the P.O. about 8.30 walking up town with Matt & Ed — then to Baptist Ch — & heard the winding up of Mr. VanMeter concerning his Roman Mission. Then home & to bed at 10 o'clk —

## Right Page

### June, Monday 10, 1878

Overcast mntf — after bkfst — took a long walk going out to Mansion St — & so around to Main — across, east of town to Hooker Ave — & so home about 11 o'clk — Dinner at 12.30 — Rain in the afternoon — went in town and after some trouble got an acid bottle — back, arranged things to get to work — Tea at 6.30 — loitered about the house until 10, then to bed.
