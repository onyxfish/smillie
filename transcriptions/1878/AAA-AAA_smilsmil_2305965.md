## Left Page

### March, Sunday 3, 1878

An April day with sunshine & showers — Up late — Olis: at o
& at 11 to Dr Hastings' & heard him preach a sermon on
Strength out of weakness, from Hebrews 11 ch. 34 v. Then
to 41st S— there until 1.15 — to Hotel Royal & dined at 1.45.
At 3 I went to Emma J's &
at 3.30 went to N.A. with Emma,
Mary — Oscar & wife — Preston &
Fred — & Anna Cook — at N.A.
for an hour or so — then all,
except Fred, to "Century Club"
there until 5.30 — then walked
up 4th ave — there to Huet's &
took tea there — about 9
to Studio & fixed fire and
list of water colors for the
Paris art Com — to 41st S.
about 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 4, 1878

Cold & windy — At 10 o'clk. at
625 B'way to meet the Paris
art Com — there until after 11 —
then to Reinhart's — then to
Magrath's — not in — then to N.A.
Arranged pictures in west galley
& about noon several of the Com.
came to see them and made
selections — I went to Studio
& had lunch — then I took
a little rest on the lounge —
Later, W. T. Richards came in &
made a long call — About 6
went to N.A. met Prof. Wilmarth —
he told me J. W. Wood was sick
so I went to his Hotel "The Glenheim"
& saw him — Back to tea at 7 —
at 8.30 to Council at N.A. —
there until near 11 o'clk — then
with Mr. Huntington went to Huntg
gallery — an art ass'n but it was
closed — I was in there for a
few minutes about 10 o'clk —
to 41st S— & to bed at 11.30.
