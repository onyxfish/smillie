## Left Page

### March, Thursday 21, 1878

Very pleasant altho' cloudy —
got to studio at 9.30 — After putting
things a little to rights, to work
on a pen sketch in Mr.
(Sister of W. a. Camp) album —
Lunch about 2 — after finishing
pen sketch, packed things to
take to Po — with me — It was
near 4 when we took tea —
At 8.15 went to Nicole's — not in —
there to 41 St., dressed — then
to Camp's — 39 St. & 5 ave. — not in —
left album — there to Colman's
at 9.15 & heard conclusion of
a musical entertainment —
nearly 11 when Ge. & I walked
up with Annie Cook — we went
in to her house for a few minutes
thrice to 41 St. — & to bed about
midnight.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 22, 1878

*Poughkeepsie.*

Glorious day — B[illegible] about 9 —
at 10.30 Ge. & I took train for
Po — where we arr'd at 12.40 —
went right home where we
found father, mother & Kate
waiting for us — Dinner at once —
after dinner, some chat, then
a nap & at 5 I went to Grunp's
& ordered some fixings for father's
printing press — got to Mam's some
after 6, found Carrie Hertzel
there — stayed to tea — Kate
there too, & later, Matt, Ed. &
Ge — Home before 10 & to bed
at 10.30.
