## Left Page

### December, Sunday 8, 1878

Pleasant day, but presaging
a change — Mrs. went to
D. Hastings & heard a good
sermon — Matt. 26 ch. 22° v —
"Lord! is it I?" — After sermon
Geo. & I walked about until
near 1 — then to Mr. Payne's — cor.
Park Ave & 40° St. Had a very
pleasant dinner & visited
there until about 4.30 — then
Geo. & I walked down to studio
& had got quite chilled — so I
went down to 74° St. & back — At
6 I went to Emma J's — Took
tea there & later in the evef.
went with her to her mother's
in 17° St — Mrs. Downs & her daughter
& Mr. Chas. Collins came in —
I got to 37° St. at 11 — to bed
at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 9, 1878

Miserable wet day after all —
attempt at & now in the mf.
got to studio about 9.30 & to work
on Harper drawing — view from top
of Rogers' Rock — Lake George —
to lunch about 1.30 — then
worked as long as I could see.
Then packed up about studio
preparing for Etching Club in
the evef. Feeling very tired
& took a rest on lounge before
tea at 6.30 — altho' the evef.
was very unpleasant a quorum
came & business was trans-
acted — to 37° St. at [illegible] & to
bed at 1.30.
