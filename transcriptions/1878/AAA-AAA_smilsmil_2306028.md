## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 9, 1878

Slightly overcast mrf, but very
warm — a walk after help; then
back & to work on Anthony block
— unfit for work, so made but
poor progress. At 10.30 father
went to Mr. Wright's funeral —
He was drowned & by some it is
feared that it was his own act
while suffering great nervous
excitement. Emma Bourne
Fosk dinner with us — Kate
spending the day with Matt.
About 2 a heavy thunderstorm
came up & continued for more
than an hour. During that
time I suffered from neuralgic
pains in face — worked on block
until 6 — tea at 6.30. At
8.30 went to Matt's for Kate —
G.P. already there — we came
home at 9 & to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 10, 1878

Muggy, overcast mrf. Thunder
storms about — cleared before noon
very hot — then a strong blow of wind
P.m. cooler — delightful even. —
Up at 6.30 — Help before 8 — then a
walk — To work on my Anthony
block before 10 — & stuck to it
until 12.30 — then dinner &
worked on block until near 5 —
then, as my head hurt, gave it
up & went to Livingston's woods
& around until 6 — then home.
Tea at 6.30. After 8 went up
town with father; then to Mary's
— home at 9.30 & to bed about
10. Glorious moonlight night.
