## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 31, 1878

Heavily overcast all day with some light rain. Blf. at 8.30 — about 9.30 all the young ladies got away on the Meteor for a trip up the lake & dinner at the fort Mr. N.M. I Resisted the temptation & stayed & worked on block. Wrote a letter to Saml. Colmans — Dinner at 1.30 — a walk on piazza before & after — Then to work washing in effect on block — a half headache made my work very unsatisfactory. At 5 o'clk. Geo. & I took a boat & pulling beyond Anthony's nose, met the Meteor & returned with it at 6.15 — Tea at 7 — Evng. in parlor — Marie Butler playing & Cobb singing until near 11 o'clk. Then I went to bed with a very bad headache.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 1, 1878

Another overcast day with showers. Brkft. at q — then to work on block — at 1 to wharf to see Mrs. Cobb & others off — Dinner at 1.30 — after a walk on piazza again at my work & stuck to it faithfully until after 6. Tea at 7 — Evng. walking on piazza, listening to singing & playing games until 10.30 — then to bed —
