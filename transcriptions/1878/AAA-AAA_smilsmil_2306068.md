## Left Page

### October, Thursday 3, 1878

Very warm, clear day —
Oct. seems to be doing
its very prettiest now —
After b.k.p. — made a letter
to Mr. Sam. Coluane, then
to P.O. & back to work at
10.30. Worked on Harper
work — upon pen & sketch
& india ink drawing, view
on Recluse Islands — Diner
at 12.30 — after that a short
walk — again at work
& at 5.30 went out with
Father for a short walk —
went to Elt's Office with
Va. Arms drawing & a slight
pencil sketch that I made
for Will to-day — tea at 6 —
Evey, with Geo called on
Miss Nicks — not in — then
on Mr. Peter Helm — there
until 9.30 — then home
& to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 4, 1878

Another glorious Oct. day.
After b.k.p. — made a note to
Ferguson who has just ret'd
from Europe. To P.O. & then
at 10.30 to work on Recluse
Island drawing — Dinner
at 12.30 — a short walk &
again at work — finished
the drawing & two pen
sketches about 4.30. a
little rest on my bed, then
a little walk — & at 6, tea —
At about 7 Father, Geo. & I
went to lawyer Saleuais &
had a very pleasant, chatty
call with his wife — at 8.15
we called on Mr. Jas. Night
50 Liberty St. — there for an hour
then home — made notes to
Benjamin & Parsons — then
to bed shortly after 10.
