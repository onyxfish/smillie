## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 23, 1878

A tremendous squally day —
heavy rain & wind in gusts
that brought down nearly all
the remaining leaves — Did
not go out to walk after Hlpf
but got to work on small
drawing for A.B.N. Co. of Hoosac
Tunnel entrance — Dinner
at 12.30 — finished drawing
before 3 — then packed it —
made a letter to Will & took
them to R4fo. & P.O — Walked
out to Groulps — Rain ceased
clouds broke away & fine
effects of light & color — on
my way back stopped at
Mary's — Tea at 6 — Read
awhile — then made notes
to M.P. Colman & Parsons — P. has
sent me a drawing & photo
by Graham as a specimen of
the work wanted for wood
engravers. Took them to P.O. —
& back to bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 24, 1878

Clear, crisp, breezy day — After
Hlpf took a short breezing —
would have liked to be out all
day — Got to work on pen drawing
of Dumbarton Castle for Wallace
Bruce — Dinner near 1 — Mary,
the Dr. & family with us spending
the day — After dinner a short
walk — then finished Dumbarton
& commenced on two bigs o'Agr-
a little after 4. Ferguson and
Pumpelly called — Shortly before
5 father & I went out & took
quite a walk — glorious evening.
Tea at 6 — about 7, Mary & her
family started for home — I
read aloud an article in
Appleton's Journal "Consolation
for the Nervous" — Got to bed
at 10 o'clk.
