## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 25, 1878

Overcast mrt, but soon cleared
to be a very charming day —
I, however, still under the weather.
Spent with my Lake Geo.
sketches — Dinner at 12. at 12.45
carriage came & I went to boat
with Fanny — went with her to
West Point, delightful sail —
there bid her good bye, went
ashore — 2.45 & walked about for
an hour, then crossed river &
took 4 o'clk train to Po. getting
there at 5 — home at 5.20, tea
at 6 — after tea, walked to
Mary's — there for half an
hour — then home & just
hung about until 10. when
I went to bed. [illegible — ~2 lines]

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 26, 1878

Mild, overcast mrt. with
occasional struggling sun-
light — B[ro]k. at 7.45 — at 8.45
went out & walked for more
than an hour — then to work
on small Lake George drawings
for Harpers — Dinner at 12.30.
a shower with distant thun-
der passed at about 3. after
that, clearing from the N.W.
Worked on Lake Geo. subjects
until my head troubled
me so that I could work
no longer. a short walk
with Geo. before tea at 6.30
Every eve Rocking chair
until 9.30 when I went to
bed very much out of sorts.
