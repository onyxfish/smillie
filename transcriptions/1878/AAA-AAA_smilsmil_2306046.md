## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 14, 1878

Very fine mt. for a time the Coleman party seemed quite determined to start into camp on the Upper Ausable but gave it up before breakfast was over — About 10 I crossed the River back of the house on a fallen Arborvitae, very risky — Sat down to work on a study of Running water, extremely beautiful in color. Painted until 1 — then to dinner — at it again about 3 and managed to cover my canvas before tea, but a very poor thing. The Hillard Party returned from camp — a very charming moonlight night, much milder than the night before — on piazza until after 10 & got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 15, 1878

Bright, beautiful morning — about 9.30 went across the River on my Arborvitae bridge — started a new subject, about 14×28 — a study of Rocks and shallow running water — Drew in & painted until near 1 — then to dinner — At 2.30 back again — about 4 — Nellie Jacobs came & insisted upon my taking her over my bridge, & I did it — in about an hour Geo. came bringing her sister Maine — I painted under difficulties until after 5 — then a short climb about the Rocks — Sky overcast — clouds down on Colvin & the Giant — Tea at 6.30 — very mild eveg. — a long walk on the piazza with Miss Cushing — to bed abt 9.30.
