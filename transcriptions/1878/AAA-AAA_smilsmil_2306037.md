## Left Page

### July, Saturday 27, 1878

Fresh, cloudy mng, clearing to a beautiful day — Got to work on sketch from Blk. Mtn top but was soon started off by a call that the "Meteor" was coming — went down to dock — hired small boat & waited until after 11 — no meteor — then up to Hotel — dinner at 1 — Soon after 2 — the Meteor came having a big boat with lumber, &c in tow — got off at once for Blk. Mn. — slow work — cast off among islands south of Hewlett's & made sketches of Birch Bark hut on one of the islands & was picked up on return of M— Beautiful Eveg. Got to R. R. H° bet. 7 & 8 — then had tea — after that, dressed — Eveg spent in parlor — Singing by Cobb — dancing by the company. Quite a jolly time — chatting with Mr. Payne until after 12 o'clk — then to bed —

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 28, 1878

Superb day — Up late & did not get up until after 10 — then on piazza chatting until after 11. Then went out on the lake with Miss — Rowed about the foot of Roger's Rock until near 2 — then in. Dinner at 2.30 — again on piazza until near 4 — then more, then took a nap — then got up feeling very useless — on piazza until after 7 — then tea — then again on piazza until after 10 — then to bed.
