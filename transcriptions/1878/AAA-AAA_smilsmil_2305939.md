## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 8. 1878.

Still fine weather. Up at the usual time & got
to studio about 9.40 — To
work on figure in Orchard.
Things dont go right in the
color way — Lunch about
2\. At dusk, Miss Dunfee
came in with Mr. Dole of Chi:
Ill. At 5.20 I went to 19 W. 24
S. & met W.D' — Huntington Miss
Deming & saw two very bad
W.C.'s of flowers — At 5.40 walked
up to 34' S — then to Fanny
K's — Tea — At 8 — Ge. P. & I took
Fanny & Anna to singing club.
Then I went to N.C. & heard
Dr. Moffatt's first lecture on
anatomy to the students —
At 9.30, at Student's art league
monthly Reckon. There until
10 — then to 4th ave. & walked
up to 38' S. with Geo. & Anna
Cook — near midnight when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 9. 1878.

Overcast day. 10 o'clk when
I got to studio. Miss Jacobs
posed for me during the
mfg & I got in face & figure
in Orchard. It was late
when I got my lunch —
after 2 — Very tired when
I got done my day's work
— and Anderson came in
& made a call — then Mr.
Coleman, so that I had
to hurry to get to Wallis's
in 15th S. (No. 31 W.) to dinner.
Had a very pleasant time
until after 8 — Nasty night.
For a few minutes at Century
Club — & soon after 9 at Colemans
where he had a charming
musical entertainment —
About 11 took Anna Cook home
There to 41st S. & to bed &
[illegible — ~3 words] leaving
— [illegible]
