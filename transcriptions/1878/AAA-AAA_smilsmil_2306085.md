## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 12, 1878

Very mild & overcast — Rain
fell at noon — cleaner during
p.m. After b'kf't did not take
my walk, but right at etching
No. 8, took a proof about 11 &
was disappointed — it is skinny
& cut up — Then to work proving
No. 6" Hay Ricks" — at noon went to
Talkee Bank & drew Bruce's check
got tissue paper etc & back to dinner
Mary & children & Matt spending
the day with us — after dinner
laid a ground on No. 8 & touched
over parts p'ct & bit it in but
don't think I improved it much —
worked at proving & cleaning up
until 6 o'clk — Sea & son after.
Between 8 & 9 went to P.O. & to
Matt's — then home & packing
trunk until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 13, 1878

Windy day, with a dashing, March
like shower about 11 a.m. After b'kf't
went up town & attended to some
little messages — back at 9.30 & to work
on my proving to which I stuck
steadily until 5 o'clk — then had
6 pieces of wet paper, but I gave it
up & lay down on the bed &
rested — Tea at 6 — & again
rested — at 7.30 went to Baptist
Ch. Grand Concert — very few
people there — after, walked
home with Mr. Peck — Splendid
moonlight night — rather breezy.
home before 10 & to bed at 10.30.
