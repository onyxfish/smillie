## Left Page

### September, Friday 13, 1878

The worst day yet, heavy
S. winds & heavy showers,
with one or two glints of
sunlight. Spent all the
m'f on piazza — head very
heavy & stomach very sore —
unable to exercise —
Dinner about 1 — After
that again on piazza —
driven in by very heavy
rain — a short time
on bed, then some
reading & writing — Tea
between 6 & 7 — Every
Mr. Cole, Meyer, Gee & I
went into Miss Hillard's
room where she read to
us several scenes from
Shakespeare's plays —
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 14, 1878

Weather more promising in the
morning & I felt better — About
9.30 went to the Widow Beed's
— saw Alma, sick in bed — got
to talking about buying land —
I was offered 4 acres on the knole
back of the house for $100 — I think
I will buy it. Then walked
up on the knole, over & about —
Spent the m'f. wandering —
got to hotel about noon —
Dinner ab't 1 — after that, Rain
again, the whole afternoon
being about as dismal and
wet as any that we have had.
Tea after 7 — most of every
in Miss Hillard's room, part
of the time writing a joint
novel, Miss H. Mr. Cole, Meyer,
Gee & I — Title: If so, why not? If not,
why so — A dark & bloody mystery!
To bed about 11, cold night.
