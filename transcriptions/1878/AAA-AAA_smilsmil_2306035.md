## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 23, 1878

*On board Steam Yacht "Meteor"*

Glorious, cloudless, crisp mrg —
so cool that fires were lighted —
Bkfst at 8.15 & at 9 Mr. Butler &
I got away on the Meteor for a tour
of the lake. A long delay at Hewletts
moving Mr. Searl's family over to Pine
Point — Stopped at every landing —
& to 14 mile island at 1 o'clk and
had a very good dinner there —
then to 100 island & Bolton — got to
Caldwell at 3.20 — at 3.45 Mr. Butler
started for Glen's Falls & I went to
the Ruins of Fort George & I sketched
there until 5.30 — then a sketch
of Caldwell & at a little after 6
to the Fort pmy Hy Hotel & sat on piazza
until 7.15 when I went in & had
my tea. At 7.45 Mr. Butler came
I had a pleasant chat with Mr.
Jos. Abner Harper — At 8.15 we got
away & ran over to Crosby Side — I
was very much pleased with the
house & grounds — Negely 10 when
we reached the narrows — water very
smooth — Reflections deceptive —
Small islands could not be seen
until we were close on them — Ran
very slow — had hard work to find Belle
Mr. Point — got Delnoce's boy — left
him at Hewletts — then, having passed
the dangerous part of the lake —
Mr. Butler & I turned in & had quite
a nap in the cabin — got to R. R. at
12 o'clk — the last of the old moon —

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 24, 1878

Very pleasant mrg, but somewhat
smoky. Late bkfst 9 o'clk. Aftn 10 o'clk
when in a small boat I pulled
around under Roger's Rock — then to
the Waltonian Islands — found a
charming little bay & had a good
swim as well as a sketch or two.
After waiting until 1 o'clk. for the
Meteor to come along I pulled
all the 4 miles back to the house
& got there just as the party were
leaving — I took my dinner from the
Table & carried down to the boat &
before 2.30 we were off — rowed Row
boat below Huetts — cast off there
& got & I pulled to Gel. Haines's
Cottage — visited them for an hour &
a half when the Meteor came along
& we boarded her. Got up to R—
Rotto before 8 — then tea — then
dressed — Every in parlor &
soon after 10, to bed —
