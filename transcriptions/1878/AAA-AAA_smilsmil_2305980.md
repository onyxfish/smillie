## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 2, 1878

Very fine day — got to studio at 9.30 & to work on block for Harpers "The Parade — Tunbridge Wells" — worked pretty faithfully — Lunch at 1.30 — and before dark got the block finished — At 6.30 — after a very short walk — went to Kimball's to tea — there until 9 — then to Art Students League — Annual Reception. Very crowded & close — There until 10.30 — then to studio for a few minutes — Ther. to 41° 8° — & to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 3, 1878

Still pleasant weather — soon after I got to studio — then to work on block of "Heu's Castle — England" — for Harpers — Stuck closely to it — Lunch in [illegible] at 1.30 — and finished the drawing about 4.30 — had some callers — went out for a very short walk before tea which I had in studio — At 8.15 — at Miss Aitken's, 31 W. 52° 8° — & called upon her & Miss Alling — left at 9.15 & rode down to [illegible] 4to & played Whist with Fau, Ben & the Dr. until near 11 — Fau & the Dr. start for Chicago to-morrow mng. Ther. to 41° 8° & to bed at 11.30.
