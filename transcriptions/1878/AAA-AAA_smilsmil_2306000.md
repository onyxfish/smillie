## Left Page

### May, Sunday 12, 1878

Pain in my head continu-
ed till 10 o'clk — then up
feeling very miserable &
had bt fp. Lounged about
during the rest of the day
& had dinner at 2 — the
day very chilly with
short showers in the after-
noon following each other
at short intervals — walked
for a time on piazza — Started
out for a walk with Belle
but had to return on acct
of showers — Sea bet. 6 & 7 —
Evef, until after 10 chatting —
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 13, 1878

Overcast & very chilly. Bfp
at 8 — Soon after 10 — Belle,
Mary, Geo & I went out riding
— found it cold work & got
back before noon — Lunch
at 12.30 — took 1.25 train in
to N.Y. — Charley [illegible] came in
with us — got to Studio about
3 & had callers during the
rest of the afternoon — among
others, young Thompson, with
a lot of Eastern Photos — Sea
at 6.30 — with the Colemasins —
Soon after 7 to Studio and
prepared for Etching Club
annual meeting — Yale, re-
elected Prest. — in spite of me
I was re-elected Secty — & then
Beer & Pretzels followed —
Broke up at 11 — then to
37 St. & to bed.
