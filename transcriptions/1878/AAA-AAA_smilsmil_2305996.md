## Left Page

### May, Saturday 4, 1878

Very warm & overcast m'f —
got to studio at 9 45 & commenced
W. C. sketch, 5½×9½ — upright,
but had calls that interrupted
me. Miss James called about
etchings — she is writing an article.
Could not get away in time
to join a walking party to
Tenafley — lunch at 2 — then
got seriously to work on W. C.
sketch & sketched in until
5.30 when I got it done.
Tea after 7 — at 8.30 went in
to Mr. Coleman's for a few
minutes — saw Miss Wilde —
Then to Conkling — there
until near 11 — then walked
up to 37 S. in the Rain
& to bed before 12 — Quite
a heavy shower fell before
6 P. m.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 5, 1878

After heavy wind & rain in the n'f
clear, fresh & lovely day —
B[illegible] — at 10 o'clk. & at 11 at
D. Hastings' & heard him
preach a strong sermon from
1\. 9 in. 2° ch; 7 v — After
service, Ge° & I went to
Wash. Nelsons — 348 W 21 St.
to dinner — a very pleasant
time there until after 3 —
Then I went to studio —
took a nap, did some
reading & writing & tea
at 4.30 — then to Anna
C's — nothing — then to Ned
C's — 40° St. & Park ave. — found
him in, his mother there &
his wife gone to bed. Made
a short call, borrowed Hammerton's
"Etching & Etchers" — then took M[illegible] C.
home — went to Divings & got
my two coats — then to 37° S.
at 10 o'clk — Read Hammerton
until after 11, then to bed.
