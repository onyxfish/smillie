## Left Page

### April, Saturday 20, 1878

overcast with a shower or two. After 6 clk went up to Dr.
Yale's — just gone out — Rested for
awhile — then to Studio at 10 —
Mr. Lynch called — I, so much out
of sorts from the pain in stomach
that I could do nothing. was
unable to take my lunch
but spread myself on the
lounge — took something
to eat about 3 o'clk — continu-
ing to feel better — ate a little
about 7 & then went to Aca-
d'Music — Dress Rehearsal
of a fairy Pantomime to be
given next week — Charle
takes part in it — a very
brilliant affair — stayed
until 11.15 — Not yet chosen but
I was very tired — then to
41 S. & to bed shortly before
12 —

Anna Lewis & Miss Sackett
called this p.m.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 21, 1878

Very warm clear day, with
strong southerly breeze — Still
feel very weak and out of
sorts — at 11 o'clk. went to Dr.
Hastings' — the Easter music
very fine but the sermon
by a stranger was a bore —
after service walked down to
Studio — then to Miss Jacobs'
where we took dinner at 1.45
At 3.30 again at Studio —
on lounge until after 5 —
Wrote a letter to father and
at 6.30 went to Emma J's to
tea — there until about 9 —
then again to Studio —
there until after 10, then
to 41 S. & to bed.
