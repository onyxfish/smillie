## Left Page

### August, Friday 16, 1878

Pleasant mrg. — at 9.30 went to my work across the River &
painted until 1 o'clk. then to dinner — at 2.30 back &
painted until almost 6. when I got my canvas covered. the work of to-day
encourages me — Every overcast again — Tea at 6.30
Eve'g on piazza until about 10.30. then to bed.
Heavy Rain during the night. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 17, 1878

Pleasant mrg. At 9.30 crossed my log & commenced repainting study of water that I painted in on Wednesday — but I could make no progress, color and motion it seemed impossible for me to catch — A heavy thunder shower came up soon after 12 & I was driven in — Dinner at 1.30 — Between 3 & 4 o'clk the Jacobs girls, Mrs. Meyers, Geo. & I went to Bryant's Studio — after a visit there climbed to some rocks then over a spur, through the woods down on to the meadows. then at 6 to Bedee's — had a wash & change before tea at about 7 — Eve'g on piazza — Charming moonlight between 10 & 11 — then to bed — Heavy Rain during the night.
