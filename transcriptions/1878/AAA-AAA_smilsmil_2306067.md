## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 1, 1878

Mied & pleas ant, but at times seeming to threaten rain. After my walk I set to work again on Va. arms & all the mtf was given to it. Uncle Willie came in about noon. Dinner at 12.30. After dinner, a short walk & then spent an hour finishing up drawing — then went to Yelverts [?] turning shop & from there to Groulp's — at Wood & J's met Jenny Wright. Home to tea soon after 6 — Every spent in big rocking chair doing nothing — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 2, 1878

Very mied day, opening overcast but clearing before noon. After bkfs. made a note to Emma Beach & went to the P.O. with it, then back & commenced work on Harper sake [?] drawings, drawing up on Bristol Bd, two views on Recluse Island. Dinner at 12.30 — a short walk and again at work which I stuck to until 5.30. Then a short walk & tea at 6. Every. with Geo. called on Mr. Hull & Mrs. Williams — there until 9 — then to Matt's & brought Kate home — To bed about 10.
