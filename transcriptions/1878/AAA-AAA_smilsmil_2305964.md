## Left Page

### March, Friday 1, 1878

Pleasant day — got to
studio about 9.45 — To work
on Self- P.M. with cows &
painted over the big Red
cow. Lunch at 2 —
Painted until after 5
when I got my picture
finished for the present.
A walk in the twilight
up to 41st St — very tired
— Rested — dressed — to
studio about 8 — had tea
— then to N.A. for a short
time — then to D. Bellows
Reception — very pleasant
affair — met the Irelands
& Oscar & his wife — to
41st St about midnight
& Right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 2, 1878

Pleasant day — overcast P.M.
& Rain at night. Got to shop
at 9.30 — Russell came & took
away our pictures for Century.
Tried to get to work at something
but with callers & interruptions
the whole day passed — with
lots of all sorts of work but
painting — Lunch after 2.
The P.M. went as did the
A.M. until after 5 when I
went in to Proto[n] Springs when
an afternoon Reception was given
to Mr. & Mrs. Oscar B. Ireland.
There until nearly — then tea
in Studio — mate until 8 — then
to N.A. Last night of Watercolor
[illegible] — at 9 to Thomas' Symphony
Concert — at 10.30 — to Century
there until near 12 — my
pictures looked better than
I had hoped — walked up
with Louis Tiffany — to bed
about 12.30.
