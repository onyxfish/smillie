### Fri. Jan. 1, 1909

**Weather:** Bright, cloudy, cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cloudy, cold \+ windy — Up at 8 \+ had bkfst. alone — The boys slept till 10'clk — They came in at 11 A.M. — thinking that they had had \+ were having a "good time" I found many things to do till 11 o'clk — then out \+ had a breezing on Riverside — Dinner with the boys at 2 o'clk — Then a Symposium till 4 — Then I went to Chase's \+ made a New-Year call of on Mary — Charl \+ George who happened to be there — Home at 6.30 — a light supper — The boys at home — a very quiet even.
