## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 25, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A gale of wind — 50 m. an hour the weather man says — clouds + sunshine — To studio soon after 10 + to work on "Lifting of the Clouds" — at it till after 1 o'clk — have now made all the changes that I think advisable on the landscape. There is a portion of blue in the sky that is hard + lacking in atmosphere — It will be risky to attempt to correct it but if I feel well to-morro I think I shall try it — Home to lunch at 2
Afternoon given to some judicious loafing —
About 5 o'clk. I went to W. Stewart's apartment house Riverside Drive + 84th S. + called on Mr. Squires I was very nearly carried off my feet by the wind at 84th S. + the Drive — For a minute or two I was really afraid that I w't lose my breath + be carried away. I had to hold on to a railing — Mrs. Squires took me thro' the rooms again — second view is not so satisfactory — servants quarters + kitchen all that could be desired but the arrangement of other rooms w't make it impossible for me to find wall space for pictures or books — There is no adequate closet room — I think I shall give up the idea of trying to get it — Dinner with Jim — Evef. he went to drill — I read Will Lows Chronicles —

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 26, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — still breezy — To studio before 10 + painted on Lifting of the Clouds, changing materially the color of blue in the sky + modifying some of the cloud forms + colors. I soon got thro' with this + cleaned up — Then I had a job recharging electric battery in cellar + putting in new zincs — After 2 when I got home to lunch — After that I went house hunting — went to 127th S. + Riverside — Sheridan, agent about an apartment cor. 83d S. — walked down Riverside + stopped at two or three houses that had desirable south + west exposures. One Cor. 116th S. — another cor. of 110th S. — Saw the Superintendents — got general information + figures — but was told that nothing definite could be known or done for Oct. renting before June — What I did learn was encouraging —
Home at 6 — Dinner — Evef. wrote to Ralph.
