## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 15, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day. wet, but not rainy. foggy \+ raw — I did not go to studio but busied my self at home in many ways — In spite of weather I was able to get a short walk before noon. After lunch I went to Met. Mus. of Art — to the Annual meeting of the Corporation — not a stirring occasion — held in the Gallery or balcony of one of the new unoccupied halls. Very few present, as usual — After it went to the German Exbn. Gallery — sometime there with Beckwith \+ Huntington — then walked home. About 6 Mr. Fredk. Dielman called \+ invited me to lunch with her at the Schuylers, on Wed. next. I had a short rest before dinner at 7 — Gil Kinny dined with us \+ in the evg. he \+ Jim went to a 7th Regt. Hockey game. I read —

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 16, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very heavy showers during the early morning thro' the day \+ into the night — I did not go to the studio but worked at home on a new revision of Father's autobiography, preparing to show it to Geo. Haven Putnam for his criticism \+ advice. I think I have now eliminated such passages as other members of the family have objected to putting into print — I expect now to follow the advice of G. H. P.     Between showers I got out for two moderate walking \+ breathing spells — but found poor atmosphere to breathe in. I had dinner alone as Jim dined with the Dielmans \+ then to a dance — Evg. I continued my work of revision.
