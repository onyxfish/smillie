## Left Page

### Tues. June 1, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful weather still holds - \+ my physical condition continues to improve, but very slowly I have had more peace to-day than for a fortnight past - Bkf\. at 8 - To Dr. Tuttle's before 10 - after an examination he says that my kidney is resuming its normal proportions - walked from his office, 49 S. \+ 6th Ave. to studio - found it too warm - There till after 12, working with die pf\., then to 2d Nat'l. bank \+ attended to 1st of the month business - then home by subway \+ had lunch at 1.30 - After that, writing, reading, resting \+ loafing in Riverside Park - In at 6 - dinner at 6.30 Eve'g Reading - wrote a note to Jas. S. Tolfree putting into writing my refusal to renew the verbal agreement of last winter to furnish his garage with steam for heating.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 2, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some rain in the m't - stopped before noon - Some sunshine about the middle of the afternoon - Bkf\. at 8 - To studio at 10.30 - worked till 1.30 at cataloging my die pfs - \+ separating the works of other bank note engravers into groups - then home to lunch after 2 - Some time writing notes, checks etc - then a rest - then out in Riverside park till after 6 - dinner at 6.30 - Evening stupid -
