## Left Page

### Sat. July 3, 1909

**Weather:** A Chill \- Temp. 103¾°  **Temp:** [illegible]

A wonderfully fine day \- actually cool in breeze \+ shade \- Got up feeling well \+ had bkfs. at about 8 \- Saw Charl before R\- \+ I started for 440 \- We set to work on final bout in packing \- He helped me in lifting heavy trays etc \- By 11.30 my trunk was packed \+ locked \- The towels etc. bought for the Ark were put into my trunk \+ the surface was jammed into an old suit case \- R\- rolled the rugs \- Then we ret'd to 29 \+ had lunch with Charl. who has engaged rooms at the Oriental \- Coney Island \- for over Sunday \- Then I bid him good bye, as we expect to leave with the 9.34 train to night \- Then to 440 with R\- I soon began to have very severe stomach pains \- they did not pass away in a few minutes as on previous occasions recently \- but increased in severity so that I was nearly floored \- Gt.'s phoned from 29 \- then he came up to bid us good bye \- R\- went off to buy a pair of shoes \- I was barely able to move about \- I did manage the much pain to get some clothes folded \+ put away in boxes \- At 5 Geo. accompanied me to 29 \- I phoned to Dt. Little \- He was off on a little auto golf trip but he got to me at about 7.30 in time to see me have a shattering chill \+ into a fever that ran up to 103¾° in a few minutes \- it may have gone higher during the night \- it felt so \- Ralph went to a station \+ gave up our section \- The Dt. stayed with me for some time \- The night was a dreary one of feverish dreams \- Tossing \+ drinking cold water with [illegible] of S. Nitre \-

## Right Page

### Sun. July 4, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The same wonderful weather \- Ther. fell to 58° last night \- I felt much quieter in the m'f \+ when the Dt. came my temp. was but one degree above normal \- I remained in bed till 1 o'clk \- Then up, dressed \+ had a light lunch \- loafed about during the afternoon \- Ralph went with me to the studio \- which was about as much as I was able to do \- After 7 o'clk we went to the Manhattan \- R\- had a good dinner \+ I imbibed a soft boiled egg \+ a cup of tea \- The eve'g till 10 discussed many grave \+ important matters with Ralph \- Then to bed.
