## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 24, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Nasty day — wet misty \+ foggy — I got up after 8 — had bath \+ bkfst after 9 — I did not go out of the house all day but just loafed about feeling unnerved \+ despondent — some reading Ralph went to church in the mf. Jim was waked in time to get dressed for dinner at 2 Afternoon R. went out \+ made calls — Supper at 6.30 — he took the 8.30 Train for N.H — Jim went with him to station \+ then to Charls where he spent the evef.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 25, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mild pleasant day — got up reluctantly after 8 — feeling very seedy — Got a note from Wm Selkin saying that they had just rec'd a cablegram from Kobe Japan from Mr. Shaffer announcing the death of Mollie Adams — no particulars — He asked me to have notices in Tribune \+ Times — I attended to that on my way to the studio getting there at about 11 — I fussed around — Had lunch — \+ then went to Clauson's with frame that was on the little Noughty painting \+ gave orders to have it re-gilt — ordered frames for little paintings by S. R. Gifford \+ Saml. Coleman — then back to studio till 5 — then home — dinner — evef until 11 trying to get satisfactory prints from some of last summer's negatives
