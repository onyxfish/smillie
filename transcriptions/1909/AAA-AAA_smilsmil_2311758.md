## Left Page

### Fri. July 23, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A howling dismal day — Storm started last night & continued thro' the day with but little intermission — I kept to my bed till near 8 P.M. when Ralph helped me into an easy chair in my bedroom, so giving my bed a chance to get an airing — I slept & read most of the day — Rus passing freely with the wine —

## Right Page

### Sat. July 24, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Stormy day till afternoon when there was clearing — after a restful night I was hopeful that I had started on a road of improvement — I had my b'kft. in my room — then up & dressed — Katie made up my bed after an airing — I finished reading Haggard's Stella Fregelius without a lively sense of satisfaction — an impossible story carrying much metaphysical mysticism & speculation some of which interested me as it was along lines of my own experience & speculation — Just as my dinner was brought to me a spasm of pain that I think is neuralgia, gripped my stomach & for an hour held me in pain that caused cold sweat to break out all over me — 2 drams of paragoric had no effect — at last I fell into a sleep of exhaustion & lay on my bed for an hour or more — then, as threats of a return were made I had a hot water bag — comfort & more sleep following — The attack has completely demoralized me — I see nothing before me now but a harrowing by fits of pain till at last, I succumb worn out — The Kidney has given me no trouble to day.
