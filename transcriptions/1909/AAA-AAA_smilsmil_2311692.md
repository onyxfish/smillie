## Left Page

### Sat. March 13, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very disagreeable wet day - Rain or mizzle all the time - 10.30 when I got to studio - until 1 o'clk I was selecting pfs. To put into mounts for Washington Nat'l. Print Dept - The W. Library \+ Boston Mus. Then I went to Charl's \+ had lunch with Mary \+ her girls - back to studio at 2.30 \+ until 4 o'clk at work taking the Geo. Hearn painting off from its old stretcher \+ putting it on a new panel back that I have had made - The transfer was made successfully - I got home after 5 o'clk. \+ rested till dinner with Jim - He went out in the evef. I got rid of the time some way.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 14, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A clouded m'f., with some pleasant sunshine about the middle of the day - more clouds in the afternoon - I had hlfs. at about 10 - then with newspapers till near noon, then out \+ had a comfortable walk in Riverside Park till near 2 o'clk - then home to dinner - The remainder of the afternoon was loafed away by various methods more or less commendable Tea at 6.30 - Jim tea'd at the Hardenburgs \+ Took Catharine to concert at Met. Opera Ho. I got busy enlarging a photo of self taken at St. Hubert's by Ralph about six years ago - a good negative, well lighted \+ focussed but an absurd, roosterlike pose - To bed at about midnight.
