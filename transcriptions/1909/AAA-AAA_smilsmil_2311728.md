## Left Page

### Mon. May 24, 1909

100th Anniversary of Mother's birth.

**Weather:** Bright \+ sunny  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ sunny. I was awakened at 4.30 A.M. by water dripping in the hall - It was over the bath room door \+ coming from the apt. above - I phoned down to engineer - he went to the apt. above \+ the dripping promptly ceased - I soon fell asleep again \+ did not get up till 8.30 I still have some fever - Pulse 86 - less pain in Kidney \+ passing pus freely - but something seems to be generally wrong with all my insides. I have strange feelings in my chest as well as stomach. liver \+ bowels - B. T. called me up to know how I was getting along. I spent some time in the Riverside Park sunning myself - glorious mt\. but I could not walk - sat \+ watched children \+ young people who could walk \+ run. - light lunch at 1.30 - a nap - more park - feeling better but still very decrepit - dinner on hash (a very little) in solitary grandeur - Eve\+ I did some house cleaning - very much needed.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 25, 1909

**Weather:** Charming weather  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming weather - It was after 8 before I got up \+ an hour later when I had bkfs. Had a comfortable night \+ got up feeling much better than yesterday - After bkfs. I went to Dr. Tuttle's - 49 St. - Faking a full specimen of the pus I have passed in the last 18 hours - he seemed to be much gratified \+ gave me a ray of hope by saying that it was possible that the secretive portions of the kidney may waste away leaving an innocent capsule that will cease to cause trouble - says he has found such cases in autopsies. From the doctor's I went slowly over to Mad. Ave. down to 45th St. - where I inspected the vast work progressing for new station of N.Y. Central - Then to Douglas's - young D. went with me to studio \+ together we looked over portions of the bld\. needing care \+ paint. Then home \+ had lunch at 2 - after that, a rest. \+ then to Brooks Bros. \+ tried on summer suit just made for me - then to 72d St. \+ Riverside Park \+ loafed there till after 6. Then home - dinner - Evening, vegetated.
