## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 28, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Almost a hurricane last night. 50 mile wind. A clear, cold windy day. I awoke at 3.30 A. M. \- took Cocktail as prescribed last night \- got busy at 5 \- Took my gruel in bed at 8 \- up + dressed before 11 \- feeling shaky in the knees \- about 1 o'clk. went out for a few min \- but found the weather too strong for me. Had a very light lunch \- + after 3 o'clk \- again went out + was able to stay out for about half an hour. When I came in I tried to do something useful but spirit + flesh were too weak. Geo. came before 5 o'clk \- dreadfully hard up. I gave him a check. \- Had a light dinner with Jim \- Ev'f he went to drill. \- I worked at arranging the photos, bought in Eurofse last summer \- mounted by Cullen \- \+ packing them away in my photo-case which is now full.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 29, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, raw day with snow + than sleet in the afternoon. I got to studio at 10.30 + was there till 4.30 sorting the contents of one of my closets where, in confusion, I have a lot of prints of many kinds \- my own works + others \- I find many things that have been missing + some so long lost as to be forgotten \- I am trying to reduce the collection to something like order from consideration for my executors. \- There is a singular contradiction in my make-up, in that I admire order \- have a bump for it theoretically \- but do not practice it \- a lapse that costs me much time that might be better used. Had lunch in the studio. Home before 5 \- Dinner alone. \- Jim dined with the Dielmans + then to the "Studio" dance. I spent the ev'f at home much as I had spent the day at the studio.
