## Left Page

### Fri. June 25, 1909

*Ralph left for New-Haven.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The hottest day yet. Up a little earlier than usual \+ had a very satisfactory scrub. Bkft. at 8.30 \- Ralph went to New-Haven on 10th train \- Charl did not go down Town on acct. of heat. I went to D. \+ H. office. Bdwy near 36th St. \+ bought mileage ticket \+ there learned that my train goes thro' without break getting to West port at 6 a.m. Then up to the Wellesley \+ worked there till near 1 o'clk. \- then to Charl's \+ had lunch \- back to 4.40 later \- had a nap \- then some work \+ thunderstorm threatening, I left for 38th St. about 5.30 when I got to Gt. Central rain had commenced \- went into Belmont Hall \- got Post \+ read till the rain ceased \- then to Charl's \+ had dinner at 7 \- Evely with him \+ Bessie \- I wrote to Mr. Mc. Alpin 2° concerning Bella Tanner \- Tired \+ went to bed early \- [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### Sat. June 26, 1909

*Bessie started for St. Hubert's.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Exceedingly hot, slightly tempered by breezes \- Bkft. at 7.45 \+ at 8.30 went to Gt. Cnt. Station with Elizabeth \+ got her started for St. Hubert's. Then to the Wellesley \+ at work there till 10.30 \- then to 85 Duane St. Internat'l. News Co. \+ arranged to have Punch held for me till my return to Town in Oct. Then to Palette in 23rd St. about \+ Colors \- then to Studio \+ wound up matters there. To Charl's at 1 \+ had lunch with him \- then there was nothing to do but wait for my train time. Had a talk with C. about domestic matters \- de pressing \- To train at 4 \- very hot before we started \- but when we got out into the country, with windows open we had lots of breezes \+ dirt \- an unexpectedly pleasant trip. To N.H. on time \+ got Winchester Ave. car promptly \- \+ to 477 Prospect St. at about 6 o'clk \- Found Katie \+ Wm. under the trees \- cordial greetings \- dinner 6.30 \- Evely. Wm. went to see his cousin Edwd. Murphy who is ill \- Katie \+ I sat on piazza till after 9 \- lovely quiet moonlight night \- heat not at all excessive but the mosquitoes were far too busy \- I was tired \+ went to bed early.
