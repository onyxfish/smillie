## Left Page

### Thur. July 1, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Excessively hot weather again \- Up at 7. packed my suit case \- Bkfs. at 8.30. \- left the Pelins at 10 o clk \- drove directly to station \- the 10 40 for N.Y. started on time \- hot, dirty ride of course \- slow train, many stops, got to Grand Central at 1 o clk \- 20 m. late \- To 29 E. 38th \+ the maid served a lunch for me in the library To 440 West 2nd after 2\* the work of the P.M. was very general \+ not very effective \- I felt very much out of sorts with discomfort in the stomach \- To Charls \+ dinner at 7. \- Eve\[ning\] chatting \- discomfort continued to increase till at 10 I was obliged to go to my room \- I hoped that the spasm w\[oul\]d. pass as others had in about 20 m. but it went on without abatement till I took 1/8 gr. of Morphine \- not long after I had relief \+ fell asleep \- woke about midnight \+ then to bed in proper manner.

Correction \-

\*After lunch I went to 2d. Nat\[iona\]l. Bank \+ arranged my financial matters there for the Summer \- Then to Palette in 23d. St. \+ got some colors \- then to Wellesley at 3.30.

## Right Page

### Fri. July 2, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A hot day, pleasant breeze \+ more vitality in the air than for some time past. Up soon after 6 \- did considerable painting before 4kfs. at 8 \- after bkfs. went to Altman's \+ bought some very thin underclothing \- then back to 29 \+ then to 440 \- there I spent the m\[ornin\]g in weak efforts at packing \- I find that I am no longer able, in mind or body, to grapple with the problem of packing I got my watch from Jorgen's \+ he set up the works in the old Fall clock \- To lunch at 29 \- at 1 o clk \- after 2 ret\[urne\]d. to 440 \+ continued my work there till after 6. Then to Charls \+ found Ralph there \- Dinner at 7 \- Eve\[ning\] I was so completely tired that I slept on his lounge till after 10 \- then to bed.
