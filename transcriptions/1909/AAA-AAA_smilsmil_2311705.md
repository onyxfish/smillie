## Left Page

### Thur. April 8, 1909

**Weather:** Grey skyed day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Grey skyed day - Up at 7. Bkft. at 8 - Coupé left with Ralph + self at 9:10 + got to Penn. R.R. foot of 23d. St. at 9.40 giving us time to catch 9.55 boat - we were after the 10.25 - got good Pullman seats + had a very comfortable ride over an uninteresting country as seen at this time of year - at 1.30 we had a reasonably good dinner + got to Washington a little before 4 - 10 m. late - The first marked change in foliage was seen at Havre de Grace - The new Union Station is a very fine + imposing building standing alone in the squalid outskirts of the city - thence by Taxicab auto to the New Willard - no single rooms for us - we were given a good room, two single beds with bathroom - facing west, with view of Potomac - White House Grounds, Statue of Gen. Sherman - Corcoran Gallery - Army + Navy Bldg and top of the White House - we went out + inspite of wind + cold tramped around the White House + Lafayette Park, then south to the monument, around it + back on east side to Hotel - Both of us complaining of headache - We got our eve'g meal in the Grill Room - I ate very sparingly - headache increased - Sat in the large room - fine in proportion + tone - low light, few people there - Dining rooms + corridors crowded with showy people - Ralph found a friend + went off with him - I went to bed before 10 o'clk. ### Fri. April 9, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

rest of the night suffering intensely - my eyes extremely sore + feeling as tho' swollen + like to bulp out of my head - cramps in my legs + nausea that increased till the early mng when I had a spell of vomiting - no undigested food - only a browne bitter liquid - This was repeated later + also a movement of the bowels - altogether a combination of miseries unusual even in my experience. At about 10 o'clk I called for tea + toast, but when it came could not take it. The day was fine but cold with high wind - Ralph went to the Capitol - could not get in to Ho. of Representatives - great crowds to hear debate over Tariff Bill - He got into gallery of Senate - my headache slowly disappeared + at 5 o'clk. I got up feeling extremely slim.
