## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 7, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant weather still continues - I got some sleep in the early mng. hours \+ awoke feeling quite fresh - I had bkfst. of hot chicken broth with Albumen in it - Then I had my bath \+ rub-down. Then a slow development of the stomach pain - not so severe as its predecessors - When D. Tuttle came it was at its maximum \+ advised a hypodermic with whiffs of chloroform - The pain soon subsided - The attack was the lightest so far \+ yielded to the smallest dose of morphine yet administered to me, all of which I find comforting.
