## Left Page

### Wed. March 3, 1909

*Jim left for Washington.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A cold, raw, windy, overcast day, giving poor promise for To-morrow for Inauguration Ceremonies at Washington. To studio at 10 \+ painted till after 1 o'clk on Chartres Panel, finishing the first painting — The effect is not what I hoped for. Many effort to get fresh \+ vivacious color I have got something that is only a rather raw green \+ brown — but I am not yet quite discouraged — I hope to better it at the next painting — I worked till 1.30 — home to lunch at 2 — After that, a rest \+ then to the Century Club. There I wrote a note to Will H-dow \+ one to Geo. A. Hearn — asking him if he cared to accept my invitation to the Century Sat. Evef. 5.30 when I got home. Jim left immediately after dinner to go to the Armory — The Reg't is scheduled to leave on a special Train for Washington — at 9. 20 Pullman Cars — To get to W. in the early mof. The men are to be quartered in the cars \+ have meals served in the Train sheds — Cooks \+ 100 waiters go with them — They are to return friday night getting here at about 6 o'clk. Eat. mof. I am anxious for Jim's safety as troubles may come from so many quarters —

## Right Page

### Thur. March 4, 1909

*Inauguration Day. Jaffs at Washington*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An indescribably beastly sort of a day — Four or five inches of snow fell last night \+ almost everything in the way of bad weather followed To-day. The news from Washington is of a rather startling kind — a blizzard, severer than than here, has paralyzed W. \+ to as far north as Baltimore — Everything is confusion worse confounded. I got to studio after 10 \+ got "Lifting clouds" into an old frame of mine \+ ready for the Century Monthly [illegible] I got home after 2 o'clk — lunch — I got from Geo. Haven Putnam The M.S. of Selections from Father's autobiography with an approving letter — \+ from Cullen I got the 2d. photo. album of our last summers trip — I had a lonesome dinner \+ alone-some evening.
