## Left Page

### Sat. July 31, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The day opened. muggy with some misty clouds hanging about the Giant? I felt sufficiently strong to go over to the Club Ho. where I had a long \+ pleasant interview with Mr. Scott Brown — saw Scott B— also — Got back to the house a little before 1 o'clk — just in time to escape a heavy shower that came up suddenly Dinner — pleasant afternoon during which I was more active, wandering about the place Another shower bet. 6 to 7 o'clk. clearing cooler — Evef. before the fire as usual — lanks not lighted.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 1, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day — quite cool when sitting quiet — but warm enough while exercising — Feeling out of sorts I passed the m't on lounge on South piazza — I wanted to write to D'. Tuttle but had'nt sufficient energy. I gave myself up to the enjoyment of the sweet fresh balsam laden air — to the sunshine — the sigh of the pines \+ the ripple of the aspens \+ birches. Afternoon — some loitering about the place — James Hoft came \+ agreed to go to work for me on Tues. with his team, hauling ledge rock to build retaining wall on west side of my road on the hill. Evef. a wonderful full moon — but I did not enjoy it — could not get properly warmed altho' I sat in front of fire. It really was cold out of doors.
