## Left Page

### Thur. May 6, 1909

**Weather:** Hot \+ enervating  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ther. got up to nearly 80° in the afternoon — I spent the rest at the studio continuing the work of arranging my proofs — Home \+ had lunch before 2 — Had a long distance 'phone call from Ella Beach at Peekskill inviting me to attend Sunday there as our old time friend Mr. Broudvett is to be with her then. I had to beg off as I no longer have confidence in myself — I spent some time in Riverside Park lounging — it was too warm for exercise — Dinner alone at 6.30 — Jime dined with a lot of his 7" Reg't. fellows — I wrote to Ella Beach in the eveng.

## Right Page

### Fri. May 7, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

28th Anniversary of my marriage
Ralph came this p.m.

A lovely spring day — Delayed in getting to studio by going to Mr. Kane's room. 706 Third Ave. to see how she is getting along. She is recovering from severe attack of grip. I went to the new P. O. Station — H.R. R.R. Station Corley. Ave. \+ 145th St. At studio worked till 1 o'clk. Then finished the numbering \+ putting away in compact shape my etching pfs. Lunch at home at 2 — Some reading after, but practically the afternoon was spent in Riverside park, lounging in the sunshine or loitering — It was after 5 when I got home. Rested till 6 — Raelph came — He left N.H. yesterday with Prof. "Bill" Bailey's "slumming" party of students \+ spent yesterday \+ to-day going about seeing the seamy side of things — last night he slept in a "Mills Hotel" — Dinner — Evef. Jim went out — Ralph \+ I spent the evef. together.

Light showers in the evef.
