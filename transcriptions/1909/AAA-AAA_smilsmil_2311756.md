## Left Page

### Mon. July 19, 1909

**Weather:** Raw, windy & cloudy with showers.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Raw, windy & cloudy with showers. Every where about the house was so cold that I did not get up to dress till about 9 o'clk — then had bkft. in my room — feeling very weak & seady — Temp. 2/5° below normal — Pulse thin & 82. When the big room got warmed I went down there & sat in front of fire — wrote notes to Janu, To Preesse & Co. & to Scorbner's. ordering a copy of Prof. Marquand's "Greek Architecture" — Dinner with the family at 1 o'clk — Feeling a little stronger I walked about on piazzas & out on the lawn — but heart action is not right & there is an unpleasant feeling in my head. Ev'g meal at 6.30. Ev'g gossiking before the fire till after 9 — then, a little writing & to bed. Had an attack of stomach pain before getting into bed that was severe for about 3/4 of an hour. I took a dram of Paregoric & at last got rest.

Ther. fell to 56° in my bed room during the night.

## Right Page

### Tues. July 20, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day — but breezy & cool — I got up feeling much better than yesterday — Spent the m'g on south porch in the sunshine watching Ralph trimming the big pine trees, continuing to feel better as the day advanced — dinner at 1. Afternoon I went about with Eare & directed the cutting down of a number of trees in the neighborhood of his house — Mick that by night time I was overtired — Fred't. Young arrived & spent much of the afternoon at the house — Tea at 6.30 — Ev'g before the fire without lamp light.
