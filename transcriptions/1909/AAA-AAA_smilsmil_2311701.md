## Left Page

### Wed. March 31, 1909

Special meeting - N.A. of D -
Ralph home for Easter holidays

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A brilliant day - I got to studio at 10. \+ till 1 o'clk was busy with my prints - It is a great mess - I cannot get farther till I have some kind of a closet properly shelved where I can place the separate pts. in proper shape. I got home to lunch at 1.30 - The day was too fine to be spent in doors so the greater part of the afternoon I was loafing, always on my feet, in Riverside Park - Home after 5 \+ had a rest - Ralph came in before 6 - dinner soon after - Welf Jim \+ Ralph went to the Armory - Battalion drill - I went to N.A. of D.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 1, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day - It was 9 o'clk before I got up \+ nearly 11 before I got to studio - Some time was lost discussing last night's N.A. of D. meeting with Maynard \+ Geo. A little later Miss A.D. Abbott came \+ we had a visit \+ then it was time for me to go home for lunch - Ralph was up \+ we lunched together \+ after that we went to the new Queensboro bridge formally opened yesterday - The 59th St. crosstown cars taking us to the entrance - an immense crowd \+ a long walk over the bridge \+ back - more than two miles - 6 o'clk. when we got home - dinner at 6.30 - Welf Jim went to a lecture \+ Ralph \+ I had a quiet time at home.
