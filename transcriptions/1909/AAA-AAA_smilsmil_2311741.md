## Left Page

### Sat. June 19, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still very fine weather — cool \+ pleasant — up soon after 7 \+ down for my bkft — awaited till 8.30 when Bessie came down — sat \+ read paper awhile after my bkft — until Charl came down for his soon after 9 — then to studio — worked on mounts to be put in press when a spell of cramping pain in stomach came on — first attack since the last one in New Haven — It passed off in about 20 m — but left me lame for the rest of the mg — Having finished with the mounting work to be left in press for the summer I started on general clearing up before closing studio for the season. At 1 o'clk — went to Charl's \+ there had lunch. While at the Table Charl came in — short day down Town — \+ he sat with us \+ had a lunch — chatting with him for some time after — It was nearly 3 o'clk when I got to the Wellesley — had the engineer up \+ he cleaned out the freezing box — then the porter \+ he took away Water bottles \+ bread that had been left — then a rest \+ again at work stowing things away for the summer — To Charl's after 6 — found Jim there — he had arranged with Ralph — that he, Jim, would stop at the Yale Club \+ R — should share the double bedded Room at C's — Dinner — Eve'g chatting \+ looking over some of C's very old photos — taken 20 years ago — Ralph came at 9.30 — It was 12 o'clk before we got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 20, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably hot day — bkft: at 9.30 At about 10.30 Ralph \+ I went to the Wellesley \+ soon after Jim joined us and we worked till near 1 o'clk taking down pictures \+ stowing things away — then to Charl's \+ had a lunch that, to me, was equal to a dinner — chatting till 3 — then I ret'd to the Wellesley \+ puttered about there till 6.30 — then to Grand Cent. Station \+ saw Jim off for Ansonia on 7 o'clk Train — Then to Charl's in time to join them at a cold supper — Eve'g, orchestrelle by Charl \+ chatting till a late hour — midnight when I got to bed.
