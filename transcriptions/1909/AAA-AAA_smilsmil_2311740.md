## Left Page

### Thur. June 17, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fairly pleasant day preparing for rain which came after 8 in the eve'g. \- Bkfs. at 8 \- Then to Exp. \+ left orders to call for Kate's trunk and sewing machine \- Then to Charl's \- found him in bed \- severe attack of nausea without vomiting \- Perfected plans for the start for the Ark to-morrow. Then to studio at 11 \- Prefoared panels \+ trimmed half a dozen oil studies on canvas to mount on the panels \- This took the time Till 1 o'clk \- Then home \- lunch. Shuter fixing up things in the apartment \- Keys in doors, catches on windows \+ such like \- At 5 o'clk Westcott had not come for trunk \- rushed to office in wrath \- Stupid boy had not accorded order given in m'g. I insisted that this must be made a "rush order" \- It was \- Dinner at 6.30 \- No word from Lucy Kane \- I made arrangements with Katie that she sh'd. hunt the stupid woman up \- get her Exp. check \+ wait for me at a given place \- 42d. Cent. Station \- I went to Charl's \- found him up \- had been down town \- got mileage books \+ Jacksons Exp. checks \- went to Grand Cent. Station \- waited till after 9 \- when she came had Mr. Kane's Exp. chk \- trunk at Lex. Ave. Station \- after some searching was able to find all of the pieces of baggage except the Kane trunk \- had trunk checked then went on hunt \+ found the trunk \- Then nearly 10 \+ I was mad \+ tired \- Then home \- Raining hard \- to bed

## Right Page

### Fri. June 18, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** at 65°

At bro. Charles \- 29 E. 38th St.

Superb day \- Ther. at 65° Up at 6.30 \- Bkfs. at 7 \- left the house at 8° having in tow \- old Kate, Katie B. \+ Lucy Kane. Got to 42d. Cent. Station at 8.20 \+ soon had them in their places in train \- Then got Mary \+ Katharine in Ladies writing room \+ got them into their Parlor car seats \+ just before 8.45 bid them good bye \+ they were off for the Ark \- a great relief to me that the exodus so far was a thing accomplished. To studio at 9 \+ till near 1 o'clk was busy at mounting some oil studies on panel backers, using a stiff flour \+ glue paste \- Then to Charl's \+ had lunch with Bessie \- after that to Wellesley \- as I had strained myself lifting heavy trunk boards \+ in working the standing press my stomach was very lame \+ I spent some time on my lounge resting it \- At 5 I packed my bag \+ was at Charl's by 6 \- my room is third story front \- two single beds \+ bath room \- many elegancies \+ full supply of comforts \- Dinner at 7 \- Evening chatting with Bessie \+ Charl \- then he played for us \- To bed at about 11.
