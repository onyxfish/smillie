## Left Page

### Tues. July 13, 1909

**Weather:** mild day with many clouds \- showers north of us at even't.  **Temp:** nearly 2° above normal

Up after 7. after a restless night. headache \+ temp. nearly 2° above normal. \- I felt very shaky during the m'f. but improved as the day went on. \- Some time with Katie in the Butler's pantry going over crockery \+ making list of pieces needed. \- also over kitchen stores to make up orders for Park \+ Tilford but was not able to get the orders off in time for the mail. \- Spent a good part of m'f. on lounge on North piazza. \- Dinner at 1. afternoon given over wholly to lounging feeling more normal as eve't came on. \- Made exaggerated sketch of Mt. Giant from my bedroom window. \- I shall try to paint it some event as the shadows are creeping from the bottom up. Even't. chatteration till 9 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Wed. July 14, 1909

**Weather:** A lovely day \- mild, breezy, sunshiny, shadowy by turns.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Up at 7 \- glad to find myself in normal condition as to temp. \+ heart action. \- Young Crawford came \+ started work carpentering \- many things being required. \- After 6\. h'f. I began to feel uncomfortable \+ by the middle of the m'f I had a temp 1½° above normal \+ accelerated heart action \- very depressing to me. \- I cannot understand the peculiar condition I am in \- it unfits me for work of any kind. \- I wrote an order for goods to Wanamaker but did not get it ready in time for mail. \- Dinner after 1. \- afternoon wrote order to Park \+ Tilford for groceries. \- my temp. kept up at about 100° till late in the afternoon when it seemed to abate. \- Buttermilk diet at night. \- Eve'f in ruse on North verandah till near 10. \- Then to bed.
