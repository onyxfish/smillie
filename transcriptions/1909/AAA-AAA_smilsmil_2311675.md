## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 7, 1909

Jim Ret. from Yonkers at 10 p.m.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather rather streaked out, on the whole fine. \- I had my bkft. before 10 \- \+ had an airing on Riverside before dinner at 2. much of my time was given to inspection of my photos. \- \+ then I had a little nap. \- When I got home from the Century last night I found a letter from Geo. Hearn who, rather curtly, says that I may have my "Lifting of the Clouds" for what he paid for it \- $375. I wrote a reply hoping to smooth troubled waters \- I am glad to get possession of the painting which has, I think, the elements from which something much better can be made \- It has worried me that Hearn should have this painting \- the only Representation of my work in his collection \+ should refuse to let me correct a one changes of color the result of too free use of bitumen \- Of course he was wholly within his rights. In the evening I got to be lonesome \+ went to Charls \+ spent a couple of hours with him, Mary \+ her girls. Katharine has a remarkable lot of photos, taken by her in various parts of Europe within the past two years.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 8, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant \- to studio at 10 \- Vernon Mem. or came \+ some time was taken up with fire ins. When I got to work it was upon a panel about 11 x 14 with charcoal working out a composition based upon photos, that I took last summer at Chartres \- an upright \- distant view of Cathedral with intervening buildings \+ foliage \+ water in the foreground \- I am much interested in it. Lunch in studio at 2 \- then to bank \- on my way back stopped at the Grolier Club \+ after a pleasant chat with Mr. Morton \+ Miss Grannis in charge, I spent some time in going thro' David Mc. K. Stauffer's 2 vol. work on Am. Engravers on Copper \+ Steel \- He has done a prodigeous amt. of work \+ gathered a remarkably full mass of information concerning many men not worth the consideration \- It seems to me to be a remarkably careful \+ conscientious work \- Home before 6 \- Dinner \- Evef. business in connection with Taxes \+ insurance.
