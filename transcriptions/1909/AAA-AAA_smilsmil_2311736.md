## Left Page

### Wed. June 9, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beastly weather - cold \+ rainy - [illegible] after 8 - wrote to Mr. Scott Brown - About 11 when I got to studio - continued work of arranging my collection of die pfs. Home to lunch before 2 - after that a rest, then wrote a number of notes - to Nye, Moody \+ others, making preparations for Mary \+ others to start for St. Hubert's on Wed. 16th - Dinner - Even', more writing -

## Right Page

### Thur. June 10, 1909

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull day but no moisture till rather late in the afternoon when at intervals there would be mizzle enough to wet the walks - but no rain. To studio at 10 \+ got to work on my collection of Father's die pfs. worked till after 1 o'clk. sorting the several pkgs. into one collection with sub-divisions - etching pf. \+ finished pfs put together - \+ see duplicates in proofs - I hope to finish the arrangement to-morrow. Home before 2 - lunch - a rest - then I became interested in reports of Tariff discussions in the Senate at Washington \+ spent nearly all of the rest of the afternoon reading them - A great pkg. came from Wanamakers - towels etc purchased at Wanamakers to re-stock the desk - an alarming display - dinner - Even' podging about pretending to prepare for departure. wrote a letter to Katie Elkin describing the muddle of the Ireland Real Estate Co \+ the lease of the Wms burg dock to N.Y., N.H. \+ H. R.R.
