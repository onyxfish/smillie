## Left Page

### Wed. Aug. 4, 1909

**Weather:** A pleasant m'f  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant m'f \- the afternoon presaged a coming storm \- Before getting up I wrote in bed a brief resumé of my development from early training as an engraver \- it has the character of an estimate as just + modest as I knew how to make it of what I have done under the adverse conditions that best me from the start \- I have nearly completed the notes from which to compile a catalogue of my engraved + etched work \- This 'estimate' is to precede the cat. as a foreword or Preface \- It is for my boys that I prepare it \- We had a pleasant call from Mr. Frank Dodd in the m'f. He is with his family at the Club \- Holt commenced hauling stone for wall on lower side of my road \- When I saw the first two loads I was disgusted \- small stone fit only for "chinking." I expressed myself freely \- Ralph then went over to the ledge with him where his helper was at work \- He reports that there will be difficulty in getting such stone as is wanted \- I arranged my assets with Mary + then went to bed with a pain + lay there two hours hugging a hot water bag. The pain was distressing, depressing, rather than severe \- I was able to sit with the family in the evening.

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 5, 1909

**Weather:** Unpleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unpleasant \- the m'f opened with promise of rain which came liberally in the afternoon \- I wrote in bed for some time before getting up on what I term "An Estimate" \- Then up + to bkfs. feeling very seedy \- no strength + little hope \- I think I am losing strength constantly \- my stomach is getting more + more out of tone \- the cause, I think, is the uatropine + Benzoate of Soda diet \- It is now a question between stomach + kidney \- I shall take the side of the stomach, stop the u. + B.of S. + let the kidney take care of itself. I spent part of the m'f. + again, part of the afternoon on my bed \- Between times I did nothing of any importance. Holt hauled stone \- do not know whether he + his helper put in a full day or not. Ev'f before the fire comparing foreign notes \-

Ralph went to Scott Brown + interviewed him as to possibility of finding other ledge stone + getting help.
