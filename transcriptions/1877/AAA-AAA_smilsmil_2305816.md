## Left Page

### June, Thursday 7, 1877

Horrible weather — Raw &
muggy, hot & cold — Impos-
ible to move without getting
heated or to be quiet without
chilling — occasional Rain
during the day. Got to
studio at 9.15 & to work
on "Herder" painting in the
figure — To lunch soon
after 1 & back about 2.15.
Worked wearily during
the P.M. & finally got
my figure all in. To
tea at 7.30 — Went at
about 8.45 — went to [illegible]
J's — met Will K. there —
left at 10.30 — got to bed
soon after 11.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 8, 1877

Very heavy, muggy morning.
So threatening that I did not go
to Riverdale as I had arranged
to. Got to studio soon after 9 &
tried to work on A.B. N. Co drawing
"Coal mine — At the foot of the Shaft"
but felt so weak & dragged that
I hardly accomplished anything.
Weather cleared very warm —
To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& still fussed over outline of
drawing — Father came in about
4.30 — on his way to Po' by 6 o'clk trin.
To tea at 7.30 — Went, called
on D. Kelsey 48 & 2.30 S: not in —
Then to studio & wrote to W. Snoch
Lewis — (Sent his "Cathedral" pictures
to him by 2.46. to-day.) At 10.30
to 41° S2 & at 11 to bed.
