## Left Page

### May, Thursday 24, 1877

A very cold, bleak day. Got to studio at 9 & kindled fire.
Then commenced repainting Colgate view from the South
& before noon got over the sky. To lunch at 1.15 — back
at 2.30 & painted distance & part of middle distance —
To tea at 7.30 — at 8.30 went to Mami's — there until after
10, then to 4H [illegible] & to bed before 11.

Cold, spitting rain at intervals during the day, but hardly enough at any one time to wet the sidewalks.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 25, 1877

Charming mild & much milder. Got to studio at 9. Got to work on Colgate picture & spent the day on lawn & detail painting being, as usual, very much interrupted — Very heavy shower kept me from my lunch until 1.30 & while at my lunch another fell with hail — Back to studio at 2.45 & worked until 6.30. To tea at 7.30 — Read my papers until about 9 — then walked up 6 Ave. to 53d St. & back by 5 Ave. and to bed about 10.30.
