## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 3, 1877

Bright day, breezy, cool &
with promise of a change —
Bkfst. at 7.45 then a walk &
to work belonging on Concepcion
R.R. Station, worked until
12.30 — then another walk on
the little gate walk — dinner
abt. 1.5 — Col. Means & Towanda
there. Again at drawing
& at it until after 5, then
to Barnes and after that
another walk — Sea about
6.45 — Euchre, for a while
Euchre with Ella against
Genl. D. & the Mad.m. Eugene
wife & daughter came later —
He took my "hand" — About
9 went up to Maud's room
& visited there until 10.15
then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 4, 1877

The rain has come at last,
during most of the day it
fell heavily, stopping at last
about 5 p.m. when the wind
got to the N. & blew strongly — the
clouds broke away & there was a
strong yellow & orange sunset. I
got to work right after Bkfst. &
stuck closely to Concepcion R.R.
depot subject until dinner —
soon after 1 — again at work at
2.30 & worked until near 5 — then
a walk on piazza until about 6 —
Tea soon after — Euchre between
Maud's room & the library —
an hour of euchre with Ella
agt. the Genl. & Mad.m. — To
bed at 11 — Wind strong &
cold from the north.
