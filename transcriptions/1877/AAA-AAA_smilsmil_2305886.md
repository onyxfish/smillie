## Left Page

### October, Saturday 27. 1877.

Dull, Raw day, after Bkfp:
went to Burgrafts - then to Di-
gale's, then to studio at about
10.30 - much of the mf spent
overlooking men at work on
my studio's floor but finally
managed to do a little work
myself on drawing for A.B.N.
Co., figure for a label. To lunch
at 1 - back at 2.15 - after 3
before I again got to work.
At 5 called on Maud at Hoffman
for a moment. Tea at 6 - very
with Ge°. went to Mrs. Hertzel's
145 W. 47th S:- Soon after to Charles's
59 7, 5th Ave.- Left there shortly
after 10 - walked down to 26th
S:- & to bed a little before
11.
