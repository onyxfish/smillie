## Left Page

### July, Sunday 1, 1877

Rain very early in the myt, a hot, muggy day following.
I was awakened by pain in my right eye which
soon after 11p. developed into a very irritating form
of inflamed lid which kept me all day in the
dark with a wet cloth over my eye — Late in the after-
noon a very heavy storm of rain freshened the atmosphere
— heavy & continuous thunder all about. To bed at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 2, 1877

Glorious day, cloudy & breezy —
my eyes somewhat better but
still so weak as to keep me
from doing anything — Sat
about & paced the parlors
all day — my being so caged
naturally enough resulted
in a very disagreeable sort
of headache in spite of all
my care in eating. A little
before 6 went into Geo. up
to Mary's — back to tea
at 6.30 — Well. Took an-
other walk into town &
back & to bed at 10.
