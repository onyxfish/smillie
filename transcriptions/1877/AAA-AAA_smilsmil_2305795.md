## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 24, 1877

Very pleasant & very warm. Blefts — about 7.30 — much of the
miff spent running around get men at work — About 10
Rode over to Tarbell's farm with Ht Tyler & looked at
some trees — back at noon Dinner at 1 — After dinner
walked down to Scott's and with him looked at some
elms — then back up to Wm J.J.'s & hitched up his nose
& took Avery Frink over to his farm & looked at some maples
He kept me tramping until after 6. 7.30 when I got to tea
Fred Crandall & Gus there — all good naturedly waiting, then
had tea — heavy showers in the every — also whist —
got to bed about 11.
Bun Rid? from his fishing trips before 10 p.m.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 25, 1877

Superb day — clear and fresh
Blefts — at 8 — It was 10 o'clock before Sprout & team got
under way for Frink's lot when by 12.30 we got 5 small maples.
It was 1.30 before I got to my lot & nearly 2 when I got
to dinner — found them all still waiting, hungry as hawks
but wonderfully good natured.
About 3 again on hill & with Sprout until near 5 when he
got trees set, then with the other men until 6 — then
to tea & sat down exceedingly tired — felt very uncomfortable
for some time after it. Jas.
Carmalt came in and we had whist until 10 — to
bed about 10.30.
