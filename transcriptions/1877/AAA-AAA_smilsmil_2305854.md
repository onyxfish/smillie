## Left Page

### August, Sunday 26, 1877

Glorious day. Clean, cool
& crisp — In the mng. [illegible] &
& I went to Epis. Ch. &
heard Mr. Warriner preach
a sermon begging for the
ministry — Then to [illegible]
turnto. Dinner at 2.
Again to Studio at 3.30
& note to Emma J —
Tea soon after 6 — but I
did not take any as I
did not feel well — My
went to Presbn. Ch. &
after service stopped at
Fani's — She had just Ret?
from Lake Side — went
with her to Mrs. Seymour's
where she is to act as nurse
to-night. Nearly 11
when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 27, 1877

Very warm day — waited
about studio until near 11 o'clk
for Laypie's men to come. Then
they used up the rest of my
morning. Dinner at 6 o'clk
at 2 again at Studio — the men
came & fussed & fooled in a
provoking way until after
5 then they left to come
again to-morrow — I tried
to use some of my time
by writing to Emma J —
Tea abt. 6.30 — Well, soon
after 8, I went to Fani's, as
I promised Elisha to do —
Josie B. & Hetty M — there —
which until near 10. Then
took Hetty home — Ret?
again to Fani's — there until
10.30 — then to bed — [illegible — ~5 words]
