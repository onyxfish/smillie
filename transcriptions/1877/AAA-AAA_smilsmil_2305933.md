## Left Page

### Memoranda

Young fellows at a batchelors
breakfast party — Pool betting
as to who will next be married.
Parties in a pool have to answer
whether they are engaged or not.
One said "Could'nt exactly
say I'm not engaged — as I have
the Refusal of three girls; that
is, I asked them to have me
& they said No."

The Boston Sexton who was always
complainant — He always did this
just right & nobody ever found fault.
He officiated at a funeral. Stranger
finds him at church door & asks
him what is going on — a funeral
— ah! who is it? — Mr. Jones —
Hm! — what was the complaint?
Complaint! no complaint —
everybody is perfectly satis-
fied.

Gentlemen, I have been at a good
deal of pains to prepare this little
impromptu speech & thank you
for your kind attention.
