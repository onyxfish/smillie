## Left Page

### October, Sunday 21, 1877

Off the same piece as yesterday — Steady Rain nearly all
day — Did not go to church
but spent the time Reading
astronomy of Rollwyn (don't
like him — confusing English,
altho' his title is "Astronomy
Simplified" + has too much
worded poetry + too little
Real prose. — also picking
up things preparatory to
packing trunk — Dinner
at 12.30 — Afternoon, the
same as the morning —
Tea at Mary's + spent
the evening there until
10 o'clk — then home +
to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 22, 1877

Very heavy wind + Rain continuing, so that we gave up
the idea of going to N.Y. this
A.M. Got out my etching +
set to work on that with dry
point + worked until noon.
Then prepared paper + got
ready for printing — Dinner
at 12.45 — Storm cleared away
+ gave us a wonderfully clear
afternoon + night. Most of the
P.M. spent taking proofs
Then cleared up my mind.
Tea at 6. Dwert went to
Mary's with father + after
that we walked together to
the top of Emmott's Hill. Home
about 8.45 — then for an hour
packed my trunk + got
to bed about 10.30.
