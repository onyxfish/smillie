## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 13, 1877

Clear & cold with very high wind — got to studio
soon after 9 — To work on
Bridal Veie & finished
by about 11 — Father came
in soon after — he went to
N.A. of D. & I soon followed
at about 12.15 — there until
after 1 — then to 41st S. to
lunch — back to studio
at 2.45. W. Abner Colgate
came & took his first lesson
in drawing — I painted
in some sheep in little
painting for S.C.F. To tea
at 6.45 — back to studio
at 8.30. Wrote a note to
Every Post. To N.A. at 9.15
To 41 N.S. at 10.30 & right
to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 14, 1877

Beautiful matter growing to
be decidedly milder at night.
Got to studio at 9 & after a time
got to work on the Remington
arms factory drawing for B.M. Co.
To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.45
all day suffering with a mean
sort of headache. P.M. worked
until 5 o'clk. Then some
writing — To tea at 7 — at 8.30
at studio, wrote for 3/4 of an
hour, then to N.A." Stated
meeting of N.A.'s — my first
appearance among them —
Left at 9.45 — To 41st S. at
10.30 & to bed before 11.
