## Left Page

### March, Friday 23, 1877

Very muddy, mixed & enervating day — got to Studio a few minutes after 9 finished letter to Geo. Mentions, sent it off with boy & little painting of "Hills of Northern Penna.", then set to work planning ill. for Bryant's Song Sparrow to be published by Miss Sarah Segrits. To lunch ab 1.15 & at 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal left before it was over and went to Lawtiers & Stewarts Back to Studio before 5 — a little more work on Song Sparrow & at 6.15 to H's to tea — back to Studio soon after 8. wrote until after 10 — to 41st S. ab 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 24, 1877

Very pleasant day — got to about 9 — Mr. Lord, Ferguson's friend called — Worked all the mrf on V's ill. of Song Sparrow. To lunch at 1.30 — after that, Rode down town & spent remainder of p.m. in F.H. Smith's office with his [illegible] man Lyman looking over W.C. accts at about 5 Rode up town with Lyman ten — Stopped at Studio, Anna Coal & Miss May Justice of Phil. had been there. To tea at 6 — dressed & at 7.30 stopped & Colman's & took Belle & Mary J. to Phil. Concert. After that, walked up to 40th S. with them — then to 41st S. & to bed ab 1.30.
