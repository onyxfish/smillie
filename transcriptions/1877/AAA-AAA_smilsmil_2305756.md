## Left Page

### February, Saturday 3, 1877

Overcast, but no rain.
Bkb. at 8.30 — then to
Rennie's — met S. W. Wood —
then to 44th S. — Saw Mary. Je—
gone — Then to Hoffmanns & t
saw Maud D — Got to studio
at 10.45 — There awhile, then
to N.a. & at 12 again to Hoff —
He & lunched with Ella —
Maud & Jack D — They start
for Fla this p.m. Back to
studio at 1 — There until 5.30
Jeo there — wrote my last letter
to S.C.F. in answer to hers telling
me that she is to be married
next Mr 2 — at 11.30 a.m. Then
to N.a. — there to 41st S. — Tea at
6.30 — Event to Thomas Concert —
Enjoyed it hugely — walked
home with Emma J — then
to studio & wrote to father
& to D. Yale — to 41st S. & to
bed at 12.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 4, 1877

Clear, breezy day — Up at 7 —
Bkb. at 7. 45 & at 8.30 started
for foot of Carleton S. — no ferry on
Sunday — took Elevated R.R. to [illegible]
S. & was in ample time for 9.30 fr. —
Got to Pamrapo at a little before
10 — Charley Gunther at depot —
Walked to house & saw Junie &
baby & A.N.V. Wandered about
until dinner at 1.30 — almost
used up with headache. After
dinner on piazza & on sofa —
Headache increasing — Tea at
5.45 — at 6.10 left the house &
took 6.20 fr. to N.Y. In horrible
discomfort from head — got to
41st S. about 7.30 — warmed
my feet then tumbled into
bed & slept pretty well all
night.
