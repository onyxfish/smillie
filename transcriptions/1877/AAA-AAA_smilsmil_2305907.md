## Left Page

### December, Thursday 6, 1877

Overcast mrfs — late blfs —
& again half past 9 when
I got to Studio & still later
before I got to work on "Cross
Brook". My eyes bothering
me — feeling dry & as though
sand was in them. As
the weather was windy
did not go out for mid
day walk. — Lunch in
Studio at 1.30 — After that
worked as long as I could
see — Then a walk —
Tea between 6 & 7 &
the evef, spent getting
out etchings & engraving
& putting my etching
corner into some shape
After 11 when I got to
41° 8° & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 7, 1877

Pleasant day — 9.30 when
I got to Studio — Then to work
on Cross Brook & [illegible]
over the brook — Went out
& walked about noon &
had lunch at 1 o'clk —
Afternoon, finished the
painting — About 3, Miss
Jacobs took [illegible] — over to
Brooklyn to dine with
W Sackett — I had several
callers, among them Mr.
Cambs — went out & had
a walk about & tea
at 4 — Then spent the
evef until 11 fixing
up etchings & hanging
them in my Etcher's corner.
Then to 41° 8° & to bed.
