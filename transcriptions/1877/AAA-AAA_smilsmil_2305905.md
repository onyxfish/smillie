## Left Page

### December, Sunday 2, 1877

In bed all day with a beastly headache.
About 9.30 Geo. had some gruel & had tea
made up for me which I took but did not get
out of bed until after 9 o'clk. — Then washed
& dressed & lay on sofa in front basement until
12 o'clk — then crawled into bed again —

## Right Page

### December, Monday 3, 1877

Up late — Did not get to studio until 10 o'clk. —
my eyes troubled me so that I could not work. —
Got out an India ink drawing of "Camping Party at Lake
Raquette", made two years ago, & determined to improve
it as much as I could & then give it to Oscar Ireland for
a wedding present, as he was one of our camping party on
Raquette — Scrubbed out the foreground figures — Did
not go out to walk at noon as I did not care to expose my
eyes to wind & dirt — Lunch abt 1.30 — Then Miss Abbott posed
for me for my Raquette drawing. Then I worked on the India
ink as long as I could see —
at 5.30 walked up to Valtrenius's grocery store & back — tea at
6.30 — at 8 to N.A.D. Council —
Opening night Decorative Art. Exh. —
at 9 o'clk — & went up to the galleries — there until near 10 —
then to Emma J's — J.° there —
to 41. 82 about 11 & to bed.
