## Right Page

### Bills Payable

Enthusiastic fisherman after being out all day Returns with half a dozen tiny, little fish —
He spreads them out for inspection.
One who is not enthusiastically
piscatorial turns them over
with the blade of his penknife
& says: "Would'nt you have
been about as well off if you
had saved your bait and
cooked it?"

Sot, somewhat muddled — makes
a grand leap to mount his horse
which stands perfectly quiet —
He clears the horse & sprawls upon
the ground on the other side —
his valet runs to him, picks him
up & asks if he is hurt — "No,
not at all hurt, but did ever
any one see a horse behave
like that before — He did'nt
know that he had any such
bad tricks — never had done
so before."

To the bereaved husband: "Did
she die suddenly?" — "Yes, pretty
suddenly for her."
