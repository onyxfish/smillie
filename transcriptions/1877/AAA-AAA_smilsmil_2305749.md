## Left Page

### January, Monday 22, 1877

Miserable snow & storm,
perfectly Ruining our Private
View Day — Got to N.A. at
about 9 o'clk — on my feet
all the time until 1 o'clk
when I went to Muma's
& got lunch — Rosalie Butler
there — back at 2 & at N.A.
until near 6 — at studios for a
moment — to tea in 41 [illegible] at
6.30 — Soon after 8 Back to N.A.
Storm over — took my station
at the head of the Stair & there
until near 10 o'clk. Many friends
& many congratulations. Did
not leave the N.A. until about
midnight, took Miss D. home
— then to studios for a moment
then to 41 [illegible] & to bed at 1 —

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 23, 1877

Up at 9 — head level — Splendid
day — Went to see Artists' Fund
pictures — then to N.A. Got to
studio near noon. — To lunch
at 1.15 — at 2 went downtown
called at several places — at
the Harper's & at A.B.N. Co —
there, after much talk, told to
go on with coal mining subject —
got back to studio about 5.30
To tea at 6.30 — Went, went
to N.A. Met there Washington
Wilson — he bought my upright
out-of-door study for $275.00 —
To 41 [illegible] at about 10.30 and
to bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
