## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 27, 1877

Charming day — got to
studio at 9.30 — much of the
mrf spent fussing over
Gurnee's Water Color paintings
at noon went to Kurz gallery
& saw "the American Coll."
Then to Knoedler's & to lunch
at 1.30 — back to studio at
2.30 & the rest of P.M. on
Gurnee's damaged W.C.'s.
So tea at 6.30 — back at
7.30 — N. & B. came & m
worked on Constitution
until 9.45 — then to N.a.
Met Miss S.B. Gilbert then
escorted her & Miss Durf[illegible]
home to 74 Mad. ave. — much
in — stayed until near 11 —
Then to 41 st S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 28, 1877

Charming day — got to studio
at 9.30 — much of mrf. lost
in discussing with W. Depnch
& others the plan of adding
another story to our studio Bldf.
Worked over sky of Gaucho &
to dinner at 1.30 — back at
2.30. In no humor for work.
Called on Louis Tiffany — also
R.S. Gifford — not in. Worked
over foreground of Gaucho &
got to tea at 4 — Back to
studio at 8.30 — wrote awhile
then to N.a. Then to 41 st
& to bed about 11.
