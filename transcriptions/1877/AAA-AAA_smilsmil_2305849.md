## Left Page

### August, Thursday 16, 1877

Glorious mtt., deliciously sweet, cool air — After 9.30
when I got to studio. Then went out to fair ground
Rocks & painted on study of Rocks until 12.30 — Had
a very hard time to see what I did on account
of Reflections. Storm threatened at noon but did not
quite reach — Dinner at 1 — At 2.45 started to
outline a study of Rocks & woods beyond — Kate
Drinker & Betty B. called when again I got at my
work a shower drove me in — then I wrote a letter
to Nellie J — Tea at 6.30.
At 8 went with Fan M
& made a call on Vic D.
To Fan's at 9 — there until
10 — Then on the hill &
to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 17, 1877

Heavy fog in the mtt. overcast
until near 11 — beautiful clouds
& delicious air — After 4½ pt.
went down town & did several
messages — went to Cahill's
shop — back to my studio
about 10 — went to the edge
of my wood & outlined a
study of Rocks with glimpse
into the grove beyond — got
through at 11.30 — then took
cave & saw & worked for one
hour, then had a rest of
15 m. before dinner — At
2.45 back again to study &
painted until 5.30 — most
of the time without sunlight.
Gus came up with Flora &
& Allie Foster — Tea at 6.30.
At 8 called on the Rileys & at
9.15 at Fan's — there until 10 —
then to Wm Mulford's for a little
while — a lot of very young folks
there — So bed a little before 11.
