## Left Page

### December, Thursday 20, 1877

Very mild, pleasant day. Got up with a headache which so bothered me all day that I did very little. Got to Studio at 10 o'clk. Put a group of three little figures in a Gay Head Water Color sketch that I am going to give to F. K. & then put it in frame. Lunch about 2 — much of afternoon spent on sofa — About 6 walked up to 41st St., dined & then to Studio, where I took tea at about 9.30 — fussed, about & at 9, at Anna Cook's — nothing but spent an hour with her mother — then to 41st St. & to bed at 6.30.

Young W. Sturgis & Mary W— made us a very pleasant call at Studio in the m[orning].

## Right Page

### December, Friday 21, 1877

Still very pleasant weather. Got up with a headache again but it gradually wore off during the day. Got to Studio about 9.30. Uncle Willie came in & soon after — Rose Morris came and I drew from her from 10 to 12. Had lunch at 1 & at 2 went to Phil. Rehearsal. — After walked up to the Irelands with Anna Cook (whose check I rec'd today for "Cress Brook") then to Barber's & had hair cut — at 6.30 had tea in Studio. At 8 in 41st St. dressed carefully & at 9.30, with Geo., at Union League Club — Recep'n to Pres't Hayes & wife — very grand affair — immense crowd — gay dress — generous supper & every body sweltering hot — met many friends — had a pleasant time — left at 11.30 — to bed at 2.
