## Left Page

### March, Thursday 1, 1877

Charming day — got to
studio at 9 o'clk. Spent
the mf painting on
Gaucho — To lunch at
1.30 — back at 2.30 and
all the p. m. worked on
foreground of "Study" — Mr.
Colgate came about 4 o'clk
+ worked awhile, but I
gave him no attention.
To tea at 6.30 — back to
Studio at 7.30 — met B —
+ N. + worked on N. C.
constitution until 9.45
Then to N. A. After 10 —
again members to see
Miss Gilbert with Miss
Dunfee — To 41's + to bed
at 11.30

## Right Page

### March. Friday 2. 1877.

Horrible day of wind and
Rain — Got to Studio at 9.30
+ commenced painting on
"Brush Fire, Moonlight, on
Upper Ausable Lake" 14×20
a subject that I outlined
on the canvas 4 or 5 years
ago. To lunch at 1.30 and
back at 2.30 — worked as
long as I could see + got
this painting "in" done. W.
Abbey + one of the young
Harpers called — At 6.30
to tea at Fanny K's and in
spite of Rain took her Anna
C. to club — Then to Studio + wrote
a letter to Sheldon of the Post.
Then to N. A. Then to Sale at
Kurtz — Matthews — Galaxy of
am. art gems. Then to Studio
+ at near midnight to
41' S. + to bed.
