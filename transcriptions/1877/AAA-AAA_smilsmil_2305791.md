## Left Page

### April, Monday 16, 1877

Beautiful day — got to Studio's abq with good heart to start painting on Colgate's ho. (40. view) but all the mtf was taken by calls. Haven Putnam called + is very anxious that I should start some ell's of "The Flood of years" — to the N.A. at 12.45 — met several friends there until 1.30 — then to Emma J's — to lunch — Ann C. + Edith Wright there — about 3 when I got to Studio's Vail waiting — he worked all the P.M. but as I gave him no attention, did not call it a lesson — I painted steadily on Colgate's ho. to tea at 7.20 — at 8.20 called on Maud D. at 10 W. 57 St — to 41 St. at 10.30 — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 17, 1877

Beautiful day. Up early + got to Studio's at 9 — Had a good long day on Colgate's ho. — D. Bellows called soon after noon + made a very pleasant chat about some of his memories of Thackeray. To lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.15 + painted until 6. Matt Kate came to town this mt — are staying with Fanny K. who came with them to studio about 4 o'clk. To tea at 7.15 — 9 o'clk. before I got to the K's — gee there — at 10 we walked up town together + before 11 I was in bed.
