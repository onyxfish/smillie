## Left Page

### October, Monday 1. 1877

Still beautiful weather. B'k'f't at 9.30 — after it, Cap't
Read & daughter came, he asked me to ride his mare.
I did & was Fallen a violent pain in the head — had to
go to bed altho' the weather was so glorious & a fine cow
was waiting for me in the yard — vexations hurt as
much as my head. About 5 o'clk got up — at tea time
had above 1 gruel. Hbd & wife, Eugene & wife, me[s]ton
Washburne & bro. John & Bray Hall came in the eveg. I
had to give up again before 8 o'clk. Went to
bed.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 2. 1877

Glorious day — Head still very shaky — B'k'f't shortly
before 8 — Bray Hall & the Washburns there — Soon after 9
went out to Barnyard and labored with cow until 11
30 o'clk — then in to bed until 12.30 — Then worked
on A.B.N. Co. drawing — R.R.
Station at Concelpción —
Chile — Dinner at 2.30. The
Washburns, Bray Hall & Wt.
Bennett — About 3.30 again
at A.B.N. Co. drawing. At
4.30 out to cowyard — there
until 5.30 — then walked
until 6.30 — tea at 7 —
Euchre with Ella against
[illegible] & Mr. D — beat
them 5 G8. Stopped at
9.20 — to bed before 10.
