## Left Page

### February, Friday 9, 1877

Still beautiful weather —
got to studio at 9.20 — After
some fussing got started
on drawing of Condor for A.
B.N.Co. Then Mr. Lynch our
new landlord came in with
a friend & used up an hour
& a half — Gave Miss A. her
first lesson in oils & got to
41 St. Lunch at 1.45 — Then
to Phil. Rel. at 2.30 — Enjoyed
that very much & at 4.30 went
to Tenth St. & saw Perry — Then
to N.A. Met Snirtt & Symington
— Talked biz — to studio — at 6.15
to K's to tea — took Fanny & Anni
to their club — to N.A. — to studio
mate to Ferguson & Sackett
& at 9.45 again to N.A.
Got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 10, 1877

Still beautiful weather.
got to studio at 8.40 but my
time was lost by a call on De
luce — Then Deck came and
did some cabinet jobbing &
I started on Condor — worked
until 1.30 — then to dinner —
back at 2.45 & by 4 o'clock
finished condor — then gave
Miss A. a lesson — Abner
Colgate called & I agreed
to give him some lessons —
got to tea about 7 o'clock after
that in Rennie's & got to N.A.
about 9 — there until 10.20
then to studio & to 41 St. &
to bed at 11.20.
