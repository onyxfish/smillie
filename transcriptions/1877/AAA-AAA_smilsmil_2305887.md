## Left Page

### October, Monday 29, 1877

Overcast mrg. clearing off pleasant before noon and
clouding up again before night — very mild. Bkfst.
at 8.15 — about 9 went to the Hotel Royal — Cor. 40th St. & 6th
Ave. & arranged to take a Room on the 2d Story — very
clean & pleasant — at $9 00
p. meek — Then to Studio &
worked on drawing for a
B. N. Co. To lunch abt —
back at 2 — Finished a
B. N. Co's drawing. Yale
called. at 5.20 went to
34th St. & 3d Ave. to see Douglas.
Store closed — Then to Hoffman
& saw Maud for a few minutes
at 6.15 to dinner. At 8 at N.A.
J.D. Council meeting — my
first night with them — Some
discussion on the subject of terms
& rent of galleries to be used
by Water Color Society — I made an
appeal in behalf of W.C.S. but no
impression was made — adjourned
at 10 o'clk — To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 30, 1877

Disagreeable mrg. — Heavy
rain between 8 & 10 — then a
sudden clear up & beautiful
day. After bkfst. went to 34th &
3d ave. to see Douglas about
staining floor — back to Studio
at 9.30 & worked for a while
in Coleman's Room on the
little figure drawing that
I did for B. N. Co. To lunch
at 1 — back at 2.30 after a
short call on Maud at the
Hoffman — All the afternoon
spent fussing in closets &
putting things away and in
waiting for man to come to
finish my floor with oil &
shellac — So Degars at 5.30
then to Tessalated Floor Co. and
found a young man to finish
my floor — To dinner at 6 —
at 8.00 — I went to Hoffman
& spent every until 10 with
Maud — Then to Bath House
& right to bed.
