## Left Page

### September, Saturday 1. 1877

Overcas so that I could not work out of doors —
at 9 at studio's & to work on 22 × 44 — painted over along
— about 12, went out & had a little wood chopping
Dinner at 1 — at 2 again at studio's. Spatter of Rain
about 4 o'clk — after 5 again out with my axe — Tea
at 7 — at 8.30 90° & I went down town & walked
about, back at 9.30 &
as I feel out of sorts I went to bed at 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 2. 1877

Glorious m't but quite cool after 6&p — took a walk — went
to studio's for a few minutes then to Rest° Ch & heard Chesshire
the baptist preach a very good sermon from 1st Cor. 1st ch. 22 & 23rd v
& 2d. ch. 2. v. Paul preaching Christ
& the Cross — & descriptive of Athens & Corinth. Walked home with dis —
Jessup & Miss Dougherty — went
in left at 1 — went up to Studio
made fire in furnace — Dinner at 2 — about 3, back & wrote to
Maud D. Ben M. & Gee came
so did a Rainstorm about 4.30
Tea at 6 — Rain over — but little wetting — Wrote note
to Father — down town with letters at 9 — then in to
Paris for half an hour — to nd at 10 o'clk.
