## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 9, 1877

*[top of page, above printed header:]* at Po'Keepsie —

Nearly 9 when I got up.
Rain still pouring — Got
to Studio at about 8.30 — then
to Ashland Ho. & had blcf —
After that the storm suddenly
cleared — About 9.30 went to
Miller's Water Cure & Faber[i]sh-
ment to enquire about board
for the winter — Mr. Staines
showed us the Rooms — were very
much disappointed — they looked
so dirty — then to Kelmis & had
my hair cut & head shampoo'd
at 12.30 went to Saml. Coleman's
Everything in confusion with
paper hangery — Had a very
pleasant & informal lunch
in the back parlor. About
3 stopped in at Miss Leppets Book
Store — then to Diemer's — there
for 15 m — then to Grand Central
Depot — met Geo. & took 4 ock
train for Po — Reached home
about 9 o'clk — had tea &
soon after & walked up to
Mamy's with father. Saw her
& her daughter & aunt Jessie —
Home again about 9 & to bed
very soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 10, 1877

Up with a headache & hardly
helped to keep up all day — Un-
packed my trunk. Genl. Custer's
body carried in state past the house
abg a.m. on its way to West Point —
Got out my Conception fountain
drawing but could not work on
it — Soon after 12 went out & had
a walk — charming day. Dinner
at 12.30. Afternoon, worked
with pencils on Cow sketches
until after 4 — then out and
walked until near 6 — Tea
at 6.30 — Well, tried to
mite but was interrupted.
Uncle Willie called from
8 to 9.30 — To bed soon after
10.
