## Left Page

### July, Friday 13, 1877

Still charming weather — altho' warmer than yesterday.
Bkfp: late. Rec'd letter from Miss Leggett with "Fire Fly"
poem by J. R. Lowell & part of a poem by Bayard
Taylor. Blocked out subject for Jan M. & did some
writing at 11.40 went to Matt's. The 3's — Kate, Geo.
& I took lunch at 12 & at 12.40 went to R.R. depot.
The 3's took 1.15 train to N.Y. Then right home
& worked all the p.m. on an "ill." to Lowell's "Fire Fly."
Tea at 6.30 — After that Father & I walked out
to the Bailey place — then by W. Hamilton S — to May's —
them for awhile — then home soon after 9 and
to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 14, 1877

Singularly cool day — Ther.
59° soon after sunrise &
69° at 9 o'clk — After bkfp — I
got to work on Jan M's painting (wedding present) and
painted until noon — The
subject is from a slight
sketch that I caught at
Woodburn — on Montrose R.R.
as we stopped to "wood up"
Oct. 1876. Dinner at 12.30.
Afternoon, worked until
near 5 — then finished the
painting in — washed up &
took a short walk with Geo. before tea — Tea soon
after 6 — Music in Eagtman
Park from 7 to 8.15 — After
that Father, Geo. & I went
up town — back soon after 9 —
to bed at 10.
