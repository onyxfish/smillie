## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 10, 1877

Very fine day — but I don't
feel to match — heavy head.
Got to studio at 9.30. Did n't
do much during the
m'f. Wrote to Jan M. +
S. Sayre at Montrose — about
planting trees — To lunch
with S- at 1.30 — back
at 2.45 + spent the p.m
fooling around + fixing
mill boards + preparing
to painting Colgate picture.
To tea at 7.15 — Meaney
+ where I got to Dan. Solway
— a pleasant call until
10.15 — thru to 41. 18 +
to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 11, 1877

Beautiful day — Got to studio
at 9.30 + got to work tracing
+ outlining Colgate pictures
(10½ × 19.) Interrupted by
callers very much + quite
out of sorts for work owing to
a heavy head — To lunch
at 1.15 — back at 2.30 + worked
on outlinings until 6.30 —
thru to tea — Aunt Jessie
there. At 8.30 at Miss Abatt's
14 S: near 2d. ave. Took her
up to Mr. Hawk's — 5 E. 48 S—
(he is in S.C.) + saw his pictures,
Michael, his man, lighting up
for us — At 9.30 walked all
the way down with Miss A. +
mentine for a little call — at
10.45 to 41. S- + Right to bed
