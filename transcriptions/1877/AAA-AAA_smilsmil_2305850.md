## Left Page

### August, Saturday 18. 1877.

Early mf overcast — glooming cool day — at 9 went to studio & started study of Beach from Francts very near to my shop. worked until 12.30. Dinn at. At 2.30 again at shop — worked on study of Rocks under the hill — Painted faithfully until 6.30. Then to tea very tired. At 7.30 went with Geo. to call on Mrs. H.H. Tyler — not in, but for ½ an hour visited Mr. Wilcox then down town & at 8.30 at Faris, Stewart B. & Josie, Hetty, & ke Post & wife — Dan Sawyer wife there — whist — on the hill — to bed at 10.30 — [illegible — ~8 lines]

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 19. 1877.

Beautiful day, but warm. After help. Sat on W.J.T's piazza until church time — then went to Presb'n ch. & heard Mr. Miller preach & saw this birth right bargain. Walked home with Lillie Jess up & sat on her piazza & chatted with her friend Miss Butter until 1 o'clk. — Then on the hill — Dinner at 2 — at 3.30 went to studio. Did some Reading & writing — tea at 6, again to studio after & took a walk up the road met the Foster girls — walked down to Church with them — went to Elpis. Ch., heard Mr. Warner preach upon the Prayer of the Publican — walked home with Fam — there away out to Warner's with Allie Foster & at 9 back to Faris — there until 10 — then to bed. Judge Streeter died at 11 a.m.
