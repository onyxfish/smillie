## Left Page

### May, Saturday 12, 1877

Pleasanter weather — up
with a horrid headache.
Did not get to studio until
about 10.30 — out of all patience
— everything seeming to go
wrong — Hung about until
near 1 — then to lunch and
after that, to bed where I lay
until 5 o'clk. Then down to
Studio to see how things were
going — Geo. & I were to go to
spend Sunday with Miss
Abbott but I was entirely
unable to send word to her
father. Did not get my tea
until about 8.30 — then on
sofa until I went to bed
at 11.30.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 13, 1877

Very warm, pleasant day —
up at 8.30 — Myf went to Dr.
Hastings's & heard him preach
from Jeremiah 23rd ch. 28th v —
After service walked down
to Studio & to Mary's to dinner
at 1.30 — There until 4.15, then
with Chase walked down
to studio — there I wrote awhile
at 5.30 went to Preston Spring's
to tea — Emma J. not at home.
Spent the eveng in chatting
until 10.15 then to 41 St. &
to bed at 11.
