## Left Page

### December, Monday 10, 1877

Pleasant day — 9.30 when I got to studio — All of the mfg was spent in packing pictures for Oscar in boy. About went to Exp. Co. Lunch in studio after 2. During the p.m. — working on copperplates, filing bevels & laying grounds. Took a short walk before tea which I had at studio about 7 — At 8, Yale & others came to attend Etching Club meeting & I went over to Council meeting at N.A. of D. There until 9, then back to studio — 9 or 10 of the fellows there — Talked etching & laid grounds until after 10. meeting then adjo. Gifford & Farrer showed two excellent etchings — Got to 41 S. S. am after 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 11, 1877

Pleasant day, altho rather threatening. Got to studio at 9.45 — Tried to work. Wrote a letter to Oscar J. — Took it in to his mother. The party starts for Springfield, mass. to attend O's wedding to-night at 11 o'clk. A.M. The mfg passed & I did but little. Did not prepare lunch until near 2 — Gifford came in with a zinc plate and after some hard work I managed to get a ground laid for him. During the afternoon again filing copperplates — At 6 o'clk. with Jelf went to Century Club — there until 7 — then again to studio & had tea — Ruef, Jussing our etchings until 10.30. To 41 S. at 11 & to bed.
