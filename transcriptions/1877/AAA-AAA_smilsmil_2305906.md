## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 4, 1877

Milder & overcast — Got to Studio at 9.30 — worked for awhile on India ink "Camping party" — then on "Cress Brook" — Lunch at Studio at 1.30 — Painted until a little after 4 then had to give up as I could see no longer — fussed about until near 6 — then a short walk & at 6.15 at Fanny K's — tea at 6.30 — At 8, Jed & D. took Fanny K & Fannie C. to their club — Rain commenced — then to Studio & at 8.30 I went to Social Reunion of Students Art League — 5th Ave & cor. 16th — spent an hour pleasantly — Kd. Mr. Corning there to spoke a few words to the company — At 10 went for Fanny and at 10.45 took her home — then to 41st S. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 5, 1877

Horrible dark, Rainy day — Up late — Bkft — after 9 — Met Chas. Millen who insisted upon my calling at his Studio — then looking over etchings etc etc — until near 11 — Got to Studio at 11.30 — So dark that I could hardly see to work but painted over foreground of "Cress Brook" — Lunch at 2 — Painted as well as I could in the dark until 3.30 — Took a short walk at 6.30 — tea at 7 & immediately after went to Coleman's with a specimen of our peaches & cream — There until 8.30 then again to Studio — made notices for next etching club meeting — did not get to 41st S. & to bed until after 11.
