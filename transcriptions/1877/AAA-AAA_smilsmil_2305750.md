## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 24, 1877

Got to studio at 10 — Did a little at picking up things
& made paste & wrote one or two notes. The day very pleasant, To lunch about 1 o'cl. Back soon after 2 and most of the p.m. spent mounting a drawing of Heidelberg by some foreign artist that has been badly injured by a broken glass — To tea at 6.30 — At 8.30 at Miss Abbott with her, her sister & brother went to Reception at Wm M. Evart's — got to N.a. at 9.50 — & at just about 11 got to Ned Cook's Rec'p'
There until 12 — had a very good time — There to 41' J'd — & to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 25, 1877

Fine day — again very late in getting to studio — much of the m't given to writing & to Mr. Geo. Hearn & Miss Mentz of Phila. To lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.45 and the rest of day light was given to Restoration of the damaged drawing of Heidelberg — To tea at 6.30 — At 8.30 at N.a. — There until after 10 — To 41' St & to bed about 11.
