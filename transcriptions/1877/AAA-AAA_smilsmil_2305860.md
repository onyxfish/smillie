## Left Page

### September, Friday 7, 1877

Rains, soft + steady all day long. Got to studio at
8.30 — could not bring myself to work on Yosemite's picture
altho' I set palette for it — finally worked an effect
with Charcoal on a sketch that I made at the foot
of the Sentinel Fall — the basis of my 44 in. composition. It
made my painting look very sick. To dinner at 1 — again
at studio at 2.30. Off the hooks, could do nothing, so
at 4 o'clk. went to the foundry door, + around Town until Tea at
6.30 — In eff. Read [illegible] until 8.30 — washed brushes
+ to bed before 10.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 8, 1877

Overcast m'f. followed by a mild, pleasant day — Got to studio
before 9 + to work on Yosemite. Remodeled + repainted the Rocks
of the left hand side in foreground. Dinner at 1 o'clk. Again
at work soon after 2 — darting off girls + Miss Causland came
for a few minutes — George painted beside me — I
worked until 6 o'clk — Tea at 6.30 — Wetf. called on
my Coolser's — no one at home, so left our cards sticking in the
door, then to Alice Stroud's where we found Miss Shemard
+ Lillie Jessup — left at 10 o'clk
+ right to bed.
