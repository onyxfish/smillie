## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 5, 1877

Very mild + pleasant. Most of the day slightly overcast. Got to studio at 8.30 & to work on "Yosemite" — painting in the foreground. Old W. Jot Drinker made a call about 11 — At 12 went out + shopped around for ¾ and hour — Dinner about — Soon after 2. again at work but feeling nervous & [illegible] unlike exertions — Stuck to it until nearly — Tea at 6.30 at 9.30 went for Allie Foster + took her to Repetition of Parlor Entertainment at Dau Sayre — invited to meet W.P. Hurd + Josie Bentley at Fan's — At 9.30 went there visitors gone, so Ret? to Sayre's — at 10.30 Took Allie home — then to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 6, 1877

Mild + overcast. Got up to studio before 9 but not feeling well determined to give myself a holiday — Read my newspaper — did a little miting & about 10.30 went down town. Met Fan. Josie B. + Hore — was invited to take a ride with them + did so — At 11.30 was left at Fordham's shop. At 2. went to Smith's + had him examine my teeth — Dinner at — light spattering rain — at 2.30 again at Fordham's — he made straps for my sketching traps — at 4.30 to Foundery — At 5 wrote a letter to Ella D'wou. Tea at 6.30 Raining sweetly. wrote to Maud D— about 9 went to Fan's with Geo. Dau Mollie S— there — Fan goes to N.Y. to-morrow — on the hill at 10 + Right to bed.
