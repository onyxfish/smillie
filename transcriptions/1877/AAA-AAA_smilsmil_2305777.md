## Left Page

### March, Saturday 17. 1877

Quite four inches of snow — a unpleasant day — Got to studio at 9.10 + soon started painting in 13×17 sketch of "Cathedral Rocks, with Spires". Bothered by interruptions but stuck to my work as well as I could until 1.15 — then to 41 S. to lunch — back at 2.15 + painted as long as I could see. Darsey came in — Gee went to Plainfield to stay over Sunday. Got sketch Rubbed in + went to tea at 6.45 — back to studio at 8.45 — Charcoaled some more Yosemite Sketches — then to 41 S. about 11 and right to bed — Horribly cold with wind, [illegible — ~3 words] I must and sleep the most dull [illegible] displeasingly dread my budget [illegible] stones to [illegible] as, and woke [illegible] [illegible] 2, [illegible]

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 18. 1877

Clear, cold day. Up before 8. B'kf't at 9 — went to Dr. Hastings' — heard him preach from Jer. 15° ch — . After service walked home with Mrs. Mary Rogers Griffits — then over to F. H. Smith's and had a free talk with him concerning some disagreements we have had — He persists that I have done him an injustice, have inflicted a social + official discourtesy + consequently owe him an apology. At 1.40 to Mary's to dinner — at 3.30 to studio and made until 6. at 6.15 at Kimball's took tea + stayed until 9 o'clk then to Studio. Wrote a letter to Father. To 41 S. about 10.30 + to bed at 11.
