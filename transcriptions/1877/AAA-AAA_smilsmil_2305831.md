## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 11, 1877

Warm — at times overcast with at light sprinkle of Rain about noon. Up early & went up town before Help — After Help — went with the girls down to the River to see the caisson & loaf on toy's Point. Back at 10.30 — then I charcoaled sketch for Fau Mulford's wedding present. Uncle Willie came at noon & invited us all out to dinner at Hillside to-morrow — a short walk alone — Dinner at 1 — then a nap — then wrote to Fau M. W. E. J. Phelps & part of a letter to W. Williams — Tea at 6.30 — then Belle, Mary, Kate, get & I walked to the old Bailey place to see the view — then to Mary's — there until afting — then home & after 11 before we got to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 12, 1877

Superb mnt. — again up town before Help — After Help — I mate awhile. At 1.20 went to P.O. & posted a letter to Julie Spring — then to Stable & got 3 seat wagon & horses — then to Matt's — got here, then home & got the Sweeney — Kate & Geo. I drove up to N. Hamilton St. & out by Parker Ave. & East Road to Uncle Willie's — got there at just 1. Dinner at 1.30 — The P.M. spent in wandering over the place — Tea at 6 — At 6.30 took horses & wagon — added Emma B. to our load, drove up East Road, out to Union Corners, back by Hyde Park Road to Hillside — there at 8.20 — then in town — got to the Terrace about 9 o'ck — back to Stable with horses — Really cool on the Road — then to Matt's — nearly 10 when I got back home — all to bed by 10.30.
