## Left Page

### November, Sunday 4. 1877

Very fine day — but in bed all day with miserable headache.
Got up about 9 o'clk.
After having had my tea served to me in bed.
About 10 walked down the avenue + Bdeway
to 23°. S. + back. getting
to bed again before midnight.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 5. 1877

Dull wtf. Before noon, a Tornado of Rain which continued during most of the night. After 11.fo — got a note from Jane Hinshelwood — So Geo. + I went to see their back parlor — they want us to take it — we promised to if they can be assured possession of the house until May 1st. Then I went to Douglass + he promised to send his bro. to studio to paint on wall. at 9 o'clk — got to studio after 10 + found man pumicing the floor. Douglass came abt before floor man was done + then there was confusion — at 1 o'clk, went to Sumner D.'s to meet Ascart his bride elect, Miss Gordon, but they were not happening + probably detained by the storm — so I got lunch + left without seeing them.
P.m. worked a while on charcoal — watched progress in my Room until 9.p. — to took tea at Ashland Ho. then I went to N.A. Saw Trilement there to council meeting — no [illegible] — at 10 — to H.R. in heavy Rain + to bed before 11.
