## Left Page

### July, Thursday 5, 1877

Very lovely m'f. Mary & the Dr. finally left for their own house. Af'n
bldp'. Geo. + I went down to the River — back bef'n
10 — I felt very tired — Tried to work on Whittier
ill. + at 12 went to town + saw florist to learn that Kalinia is Laurel —
Dinner at 1 — At 3 went to foot of Main St. + by steam launch crossed the River — Rummaged the woods — found lots of Laurel, just out of bloom, at last found one blossom + two buds — home with my prize at 6 15 — Tea — Evening too lazy to do any thing but Rock myself in big chair — to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 6, 1877

Still deliciously cool weather. After bldp'. went in town with Geo. + did family marketing, then to Mary's + about 10 home again. Then to work on Miss Leggett's pen ill. of Whittier until dinner at 12.45 and again during p.m. until after 5 when I went out + walked to the Widow's Bailey's place — Tea soon after 6 — about 7.30 Father, Geo. + I went to Mary's + hung pictures + otherwise helped get things in order until 10 o'clk — Then home + to bed about 10.30
