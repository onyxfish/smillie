## Left Page

### January, Thursday 18, 1877

Charming day — got to studio at 10 — there until after 11 — then to N.a. there at all sorts of work until about 1 then to 41·S:, lunched — back at 2.30 — at 4.30 to studio. Meeting of B. of C. to settle Salesman matter & start things for an Illustrated catalogue for W.C. [illegible]. To tea at 6.45. Back to N.a. There until near 11. Then to 41·S: & to bed at 11.30.

### January, Friday 19, 1877

Mixed, [illegible] & Raw. feeling up late — went first to studio — 9.30 — then to N.a. back about 11 — [illegible] waiting for me — S.M.
