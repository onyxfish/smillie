## Left Page

### April, Thursday 12, 1877

Beautiful day — got to studio
a few minutes after 9 & to work
continuing on Milleboard. Mr.
Colgate's views — To lunch at
1 — Aunt Jessie there — back
at 2.15 & spent the P.m.
between Colgate (15) & Vail
(9) To tea at 7 — & soon
after 8 went to 43 W. 52 S: —
& spent the every. with D.
Yale writing out a constitution
for a proposed. E. Etching Club
was kept until after 11 —
to 41: S: & to bed at 1.45.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 13, 1877

Very pleasant day — On my way
to studio called upon Miss Woodward
got to shop at 9.30 — undertook
to make a little M.C. sketch before
beginning my days work and it
was 11.30 before I finished it —
then worked on outline of Colgate
No. No. 2. until 1 o'clk — then to
41: S: to lunch — At 2.30 at
Phil. Rehearsal — after that, went
to Dawcy's & ordered a suit of
clothes — then to Stewart's & to
Dunlalp's & at 6.30 to K's — 22° S: —
Took tea there & at 8 took F. &
A.C. to their club — there to M.A.
there until 9.30 — then to Studio
to 41: S: at about 11 & right
to bed.
