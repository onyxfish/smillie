## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 27, 1877

Very hot day in spite of a pleasant air stirring — got to studio soon after 9 and did spend the most of it in putting studies to rights to leave for the summer — Bid those in the building good bye — called on Miss Durfee about 8 clk — then to Grand Central to Am. Exp. Co — & to 41·S· to dinner at nearly 2 — 3 o'clk I went to Mamy's & packed barrels of kitchen stuff until 5.30 — then to 41·S· Tea at 6.30 — after that went up to 52·S· to see Dr. Yale who sails for Liverpool on Sat. out — then again to Mamy's. Chase there — I worked until 10.30 (Chase until 1.) Then to 41·S· & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 28, 1877

*Po'keepsie.*

Up early — bkft· before 8 — then to work packing trunk & valise — got through soon after 11 — then dressed — at 12 Exp. man came to get baggage. Dinner at 12.45. At 2 went to Mamy's & for half an hour did what I could to help her off — At 2.30 Mary, Matt, Katie & I started for the Mary Powell — I stopping on the way down to do some messages — At 3.30 off on the M.P. Aunt Jessie also — Dr. following with the baggage in the propeller — I soon began to experience discomfort from my dinner (salmon) which increased until near 6 when I was violently sick — purging & vomiting — got to Po· about 8 still extremely uncomfortable — Rode Right home & tumbled into bed without delay when I passed a very miserable night. Rec'd from Rob't Colgate $250 — which I distribute pretty generally.
