### May. Sunday 20. 1877.

[illegible — ~4 words] long warm — Up soon after

## Right Page

### May, Monday 21. 1877.

Quite a cool mfg. Ther.
66°. Up at 6.30 — [illegible]. at 7.30
at 8.50, with M. & Miss A. &[illegible].
took Train then boat to [illegible]
city — a slight spatter of Rain
landed at foot of 23 [illegible] St. & [illegible] to
Studio about 10. About noon
went to N.A. & there met Mr. Chas.
Martin & daughter & Genl. Ducat.
Left them at 1 & went to Kneedlers
& finally at 1.45, through a spatter
of Rain to lunch — back to Studio
at about 3 in a heavy shower
& the rest of p.m. was spent
fixing up one of my closets —
To tea at 7 — at 8.30 went to
Stunt, [illegible]. & spent the eve'g
with Jam M. 10.41. S: at
11 & right to bed.
