## Left Page

### April, Sunday 8, 1877

Very charming mrng.
p.m. thickened up with
raw wind from the East.
Mrng, went to Dr. Hastings +
heard a strong sermon from
S. Suice. 15th Ch, 17 v. After serm
walked down to studio + then
up to Mamy's to dinner at 3.30
About 4 o'clk — walked up
to Park + about there until
near 6 — then to Will's — met
Mrs. Sweet of Syracuse there
crackers + tea — then took
Mrs. S — Joe. Niems + Mrs. Reynolds
down to Hepworth's — left them
there + went to young Tyng's
— a good deal of Moody and
Sanky — Mr. Perkins doing
the Sanky — stayed to Prayer
meeting after the service —
out a letter before 10 — then
to 41 St + right to bed —

## Right Page

### April, Monday 9, 1877

Blustering, but not unpleas-
ant day — Soon after 9 when
I got to studio — worked
all the mrng enlarging the
boundaries of Colgate pictures
+ at 1 to lunch — back at
2 — Miss Boyd Ret'd — very study
+ Mr. Colgate (14) + Hail (8)
came for a Regular lesson —
worked with them until
after 6 — after 7 when I got
to tea — At 8.30 called on
Miss Carpenter at 40th St — out —
then on Annie Cook — there
until 10.15 — then to 41 st +
heard Diving play until
11 — then to bed —
Maud + Ella D — called
in the mrng — just arrived from
Florida — I lunched with
them at Hoffman.
