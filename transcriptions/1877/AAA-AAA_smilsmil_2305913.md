## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 18, 1877

Clear, beautiful day, with more of winter's snap in it than before — Up early & got to bkfst before 8 — Father met us at the Hotel Royal and Frank bkfst with us — To Studio at 9.15. Right to work on lake placid & painted in lake & middle distance. At 1.30 went to Tweedlie's & then met Reinhart & Abbey — back to Studio at 2.15 — lunched & painted as long as I could see — Mr. Adams & two other gentlemen called in behalf of the Utica [illegible] — at 5 made a[illegible] — at 5.30 walked up Fifth Ave — Met Anna Cook & Ret? with her to the K's where Ge. already was — Tea at 6.30 at 8, took the girls to their club — then to Studio — in until

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 19, 1877

Pleasant day, altho' at times overcast. Got to Studio soon after 9 & to work on lake placid & slashed away at the foreground. W. Geo. Whitney and W. T. Richards called for a moment to arrange with us to dine with them at 3 o'clk. at the Albemarle — I worked away & soon had my whole canvas covered — at 3 o'clk with Ge. went to hotel & had dinner — back to Studio at 4.30 — Tea at 6.30 — then to 41st St., dressed, & at near 9 Ge. & I called on Mary Whitney at Mrs. Jonathan Sturges where we were very pleasantly Rec'd — Met Miss Buckingham of Yosemite memory — Did not get away until 10.30 — caught in a shower on our way there — Glorious moonlight on our way back. To bed at 11.
