## Right Page

### January, Monday 1, 1877

Pleasant morn. Got to Studio at 9.15 & right to work on big architectural drawing. Mr. Harrison came at about 10.30 & stayed until after 1. I finished the thing about noon and to work on "The Truants" — Geo. came in for awhile. At 1.30 I went in to Emma J's & got my lunch — back in an hour & worked as long as I could see — about 4.30 — Frank Scott & Mr. Buell called — Heavy snow storm. At 6.15 went to Nellie Jacob's & got my supper — then again to Emma J — Kitty J — then — then for a moment in at Fanny K's — then to Lela Marten's & at 10.30 to 41 8 — got to bed soon after 11 —
