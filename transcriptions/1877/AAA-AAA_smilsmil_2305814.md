## Left Page

### June, Sunday 3, 1877

Very hot day — unable to get up, owing to headache, until 12.30 — Then dressed & went to Nellie Jacob's to dinner — Had a very hard time of it — I was so uncomfortable. At 3.30 excused myself & went right back to bed — got up at 9 o'clk & had a cup of tea & to bed again about midnight. [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### June, Monday 4, 1877

Very hot & very breezy — got to studio about 9.30 after going to the, & getting "Gaucho" — Chas. Miller called to learn something about etching — & then J. W. Wood. I felt very much used up from yesterday's headache & did not try to paint — Lots to do "podging" about my Room — got my etching corner in order & went to lunch soon after 1 — back shortly after 2 & had a call from Belle & Mary Tweed. About 3.30 or 4 o'clk. Wood came & then Louis Tiffany & I laid grounds for them until 6 o'clk — to tea at 7.30 — Read my Post until 9.30 then to bed. Wood & Tiffany told me that I was elected unanimously on Sat. night a member of the Century Club.
