## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 19, 1877

Glorious day — at 8.20 a.m.
Rec'd a Telegram from father
announcing that Mary has
a second daughter. To studio —
Kindled fire & freed the mtf
packing & arranging things
to leave — Dinner at 1 —
After dinner went downtown
& went to Cotrell's — Sattrap's
& a number of other places
on little errands & got to
studio about 5. Did a
little picking up then
Tea at 6.30 — About 8.30
went with Gu. & Priscilla
to Fau's — Connell then
played whist with him
against Ben & the Dr —
beat them — [illegible]
before 11.

Mary had a daughter born
this evening.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 20, 1877

Charming day — After bkfs.
went to studio & swept
out. Returned & dressed
& at 9.45 went to Fanny
M's — Soon after 10 the
bridal party came in to
the parlor & Fau & Connell
were made man & wife —
There was much fun &
I was very generally con-
doled with — The bride &
groom got away at 12.30
as I had eaten none of the
wedding breakfast I went
to Mr. Sayre's & got my dinner
after that went to studio
& prepared for my leaving
About 4.30 went with Wheaton
to Sayre's farm to pick out
trees to transplant — a long
tiresome walk — Tea at 6.30
& went to Mr. Casper's —
Mr. Jessups & Ben Mulford —
to bed at 10.30. [illegible]
