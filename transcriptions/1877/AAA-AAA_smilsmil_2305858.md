## Left Page

### September, Monday 3, 1877

Cold, cloudy, blustering day. Kind of a new fire in my furnace & had my shots in delightful atmos[phere]. Then commenced painting "the Yosemite Valley from the foot of Sentinel Fall" 22×44 — Josie B., Hetty M., Summa S. & Son B. called about noon — then chopped some wood — Dinner at 1 — At work some after 2 & painted until 6 — got in sky & part of [illegible] — Tea at 6.30 — At 7.45 [illegible] — I called at Mr. Cooper's to see Fanny Payson — all out but Bessie C. — There until near 9 — then down to [Tanis] — there until 10 — to bed at 0.30 [illegible — ~4 words]

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 4, 1877

Pleasant day — partially overcast & still somewhat breezy. Got to studio before 9 & to work on "Yosemite" painting in the middle distance — half a head acher & did not work well — At 12 went out & chopped wood — Dinner at 1 — At 2 again at studio — Scrubbed more color into what I had done in the m[orning] & laid in [El Capitan] — At 4.15 went out riding with [Geo]. Delicous afternoon — in at 6.30 — tea — [W. H.?] wrote a letter to Ferguson, mailed it at 9 — there to [Tanis] — then until 10 — then on the hills & to bed — [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible — ~5 words]
