## Left Page

### November, Friday 16, 1877

Rains during the night, but cleared very muggy & mild in the morning — after bkfp— went up to Charl's — got some money from him — $15 in all — I felt tired & weak, I walked down to Studio, getting there about 10 — A.N.V., Jack D., & others called — I yielded to my feelings & did nothing. At 12.30 — Geo. & I went out and walked — in to Threede's & saw pictures — back & had lunch in Studio at 1.30. Afternoon painted on 12×18 — getting in foreground — the Cows & some of the tree trunks — Troubled again with colicy pains, so that I did not go out & take any walk in the evey, before tea. Ate a graham cracker at 6.30 — about 8.30 started with Geo. for the hotel which I reached after much trouble about 9 — at once to bed — Geo. applied hot fomentations with Capsicum & camphon to stomach and bowels & got some relief but about 9 o'clk grew worsened by extreme pains — a hard night was purgatory. This was hell — lay until near 5 o'clk, much of the time in a real agony — then Geo. got for me some Brjonia which seemed to help me for the pain soon left & I fell asleep.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 17, 1877

Up at 8.30 feeling considerably the effects of my hard night — took a very light bkfp — got to Studio soon after 10 — on the lounge most of the my. About 1 fixed for myself a lunch of gruel and beef tea — Francis & Miss Durfee called — the afternoon went like the mf. Jack Diven came in with invitation from the Jessups to dine with them to-morrow — At 5.30 went out with Geo. — lovely moonlight night — went in to Adolph Rahn's & looked at his pictures — Back to Studio & had tea at 6.30. At 8 o'clk Geo. & I went to Kimball's & played "word making & taking" until after 10 — then to H.R. & to bed at 11.
