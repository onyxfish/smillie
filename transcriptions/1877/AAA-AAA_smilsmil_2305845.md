## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 8. 1877.

Very warm day — B'kf't. at 9.30 —
All the m'f spent loafing
over my lot, sitting under the
trees & enjoying a very delightful
breeze — At 12.30 took a little nap
& at 1.15 dinner — After that a
little chattering & then, as thunder storms threatened, Ge— &
I went to studio — I got my big
window open & spent some time
in getting some things straightened
to begin work — also, did some
minting — The Storms went provokingly around us — Tea at 6.30
After that, for a time on piazza
Kate Drinker came up — then
I went off in carriage with Josie
Beatty, went home with her &
at 9 o'cl. Ge. Vie D. Josie B. &
I went to fairs — there until
10.30 with Priscilla & Jal. Carmalt
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 9. 1877.

Very hot day — After b'kf't, I
loafed on different piazzas
until after'n — then until after
11, hunting for a good beech
trunk to study — got hot and
tired without finding one but
found two good morning
rock studies — then to studio
& read until 1 — then to my
dinner — At 2 back to studio
but I could not get to work —
tried various things — then went
out under my hemlocks and
sought for a convenient study
& so the p.m. went — nothing
done — Threatening storms late in the
afternoon & every, for which we got but
slight sprinkles — Tea at 6.30 — Every
went to fairs — there until 10.30
To bed at 11.
