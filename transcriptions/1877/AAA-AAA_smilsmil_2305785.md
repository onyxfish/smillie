## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 4, 1877

Rather Raw & dubious — got to studio's at 9 & spent
the m'f over several trifling
jobs — finishing the last of
gurnee's Repairing — being
the one (water color) with two
holes through the sky — To
lunch at 1 — back at 2.45 after
stopping at Tinkemann's and
Knoedler's — Spent the rest
of the p.m. finishing "On the
Saco" — think I have got it
about done at last. At 6.30
stopped at Miss Leggett's — To
tea at 7.20 — at 8.30 went
to Mary's & saw Mother —
at 9.40 at studio's — made
until 10.30 — then to 41 S. —
& to bed soon after 11. [illegible — ~2 words]

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 5, 1877

cloudy but not unpleasant —
got up with a headache again
& was able to get to studio's at 9
but was not able to do anything.
Spent most of m'f on lounge and
about 1 o'ck. gave up & went to
41\. S. took some lunch and about
3 o'ck. went to bed — there until
7 when, feeling better, got up &
had my tea — At 8.30 went to
Mary's to see Mother, but she was up
at Will's — then at 9.30 to 41 S. & at
10 to studio's, there until near mid-
night, then to 41 S. & to bed at
12.30
V. 7. C. 13.
