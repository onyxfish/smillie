## Left Page

### January, Saturday 6, 1877

Overcast, mied & threatening
a storm. Nearly 10 when I
got to studio — eyes better
but uncomfortable. Set to
work on "the little fisherman"
study from nature & worked
all the morning on it —
W. Harrison came in & gave
me $20.00. To lunch at
1.45 — back at 2.30 — all
the P.M. fussing over a
margin about "Study" of
the mf. To tea at 6.15
at 8 went to Thomas's [illegible]
Concert — Rain & sleet, horrible
Enjoyed the concert very
much — took Emma. I
home. Stopped with her
at studio & had a pleasant
private view of Water Colors —
To 14th St; at midnight in
a pouring rain — Side
walks like glass — Streets
like Rivers.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 7, 1877

Overcast mf. blowing off
clear. Late to Dr. Hastings
motto text for the year
James 1 Ch. 12 v. "A crown
of life" — After service, down
to studio — then to Mani's
at 1.40 & had dinner — at
4 o'clk. to T. H. Smith's — at
5 to studio — at 6.30 fixed
up a cup of tea & ate a
stale Roll — at 7.45 went
to Belloni's Ch. & heard
Rev. Everett on the verse
in Gen. 1st describing the
creation of sun & moon etc
& divisions of days — a very
good discourse — at 9 called
on E. Moran. not in — then
to studios. Went into Shuitleff's
Room — Heaton there — then
all to my Room & at 10.15
to 14th St. & to bed at 11.
