## Left Page

### May, Friday 4. 1877.

Pleasant day — Up early with a mean sort of headache — To studio before 9 o'clk — tried to work — Painted a little on Chili Arms — Alterations going on in the building causing tremendous confusion & noise so that it is almost impossible to work. About 11 o'clk, the water color pictures arr'd — from Boston immediately followed by Mr. Cole & Cleveland. O. Miss Durfee Mr. C. very soon decided to take my "Mounts" and she bought it & paid for it on the spot, very much to my gratification of course. At 1 o'clk I went to 41st S. & after taking a cup of tea went to bed where I stayed until 5 o'clk — getting some sleep & feeling much better — then I went to studio & back to tea at 6.45 — Every dressed comfortably at 9 o'clk was at Chickering Hall & heard the concert of Miss Arnold's singing class under the leadership of Hermann. A very enjoyable affair given to a very well company — Concert was not over until 11 — walked up town with Charl & got to bed at 12 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 5. 1877.

Still pleasant weather — after bkfst — went to Mary's & saw Aunt H — walked down to Studio with Geo., getting there at 9.45 Painted on Chili arms but made little progress owing to the horrible confusion through the building from carpenters & masons in their work of pulling down & building up. To lunch at — back at 2.30 and worked until after 6 — To tea at 7, and at 8 went to Barnum's Circus to see the famous black stallions — was disappointed — not so graceful as I had expected — To 41st in a drizzling rain at 11 o'clk. & right to bed.
