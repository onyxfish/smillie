## Left Page

### December, Saturday 22. 1877.

Overcast until evng but mild & pleasant. Got to studio
about 9.30. Washed in background to Miss Abbatt's big River
picture then painted out two Dam Mace portraits in Horse
pictures & put in doors notice — promised Renner I would do
it for $5.00 Lunch at 2. after a short walk — During the
p.m. fussed about. Tried to finish "Ausable" [p?] for Anna
Conk but did not finish it. Had a long walk hunting for
diaries — Tea at 6.45 & at 8 at Academy of Music — 2d
Phil. concert. Had a fit of nervous dyspepsia. Took the
Tweedy's to 2, 12" S. & then to "Century" where I stayed
until 11 — then to 41 S. & to bed abt 1.45.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 23. 1877.

Overcast but charming day. Late H[elp?] — then to Dr. Hastings &
heard him preach from 25 P[salms?]. After service to 41 S. & at
120 to W. Hertzel's 145 W 44 S.
where we had dinner — At
3.15 Geo. & I left — I walked
up to St. Thomas's & stopped
there awhile — could not get
a seat where I could hear
the service so I left and
walked in Central Park until
near 5 — then to Will's where
I met Geo. at 6 we walked
down, he going to 41 S. & I
to the Studio — there at 6.30
about & had tea — then picked
up things to take to Po'[keepsie?] with
me. To 41 St. at 10.30 — there
packed valise & to bed at 11.
