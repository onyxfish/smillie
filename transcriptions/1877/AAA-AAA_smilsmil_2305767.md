## Left Page

### February, Sunday 25, 1877

Sp'itting, sputtering m'f —
clearing in the afternoon &
evening. M'f went to E —
Hastings & heard his 3?—
sermon from the text "I
am the way, the truth &
the life". After service
walked to studios — at 1.30
to Mary's to dinner — at
4 again to studios — then
until near 7 — then to
142 E. 18' St — & made a call
on W. & Mrs. Bay and Taylor,
then on Charley Smith
& Corinne — Back to studios
at 9 & wrote until after 11.
— a letter to Maud D. among
others — Then to 41' S= &
to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 26, 1877

Overcast day but glorious
night — Got to studios at 9.15
feeling tired & used up — Most
of the m'f was spent fussing
over Reduction of "Gaucho" for
Ill. Cat. of N.A. To lunch at
1.30 — back at 2.30 & spent
the p.m. at work on Gurnee's
damaged water color drawings —
Abner Colgate came at 3 and
took a lesson — Dr. Yale came
in — To tea at 6.45 — at 8
at studios — met Nicoll & Baldwin
& we worked on Constitution
until near 10 — then to N.A.
again to studios — there
until 11 — then to 41' S=
& to bed at 1.30.
