## Left Page

### July, Sunday 15, 1877

Very pleasant day — Blck. between 9 & 10 — To
Christ's Ch. — Up is². — and heard the Rev. Leigenfuss.
Dinner at 1 o'clk —
P.m. wrote a note to Miss Hobatt. Took a
walk — tea after 6 and in the eveng went to
the Baptish Ch. & heard S. Kendrick. — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 16, 1877

Very warm day — went up town before bkfst —
After that, got to work on ill. of Howell's fire fly and
stuck to it until dinner at 12.30 — About 2 got to
work again & continued closely until near 6 —
Soon after 6 had tea —
Every about 8. went with
Geo. to So. Liberty St. Called on W. & Mrs. Loomis & then
on Mr. & Mrs. Wright — Home at 10 o'clk. & Right to bed.
