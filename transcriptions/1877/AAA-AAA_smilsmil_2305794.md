## Left Page

### April, Sunday 22, 1877

Charming spring day but I did not get out of my bed until 11 o'clt owing to an abominable headache — then up & at 12 had my bkfst — at 2 o'clt. got to studio. Did a little writing & then on sofa until about 5 o'clt — headache still on — then rode up to Will's — 66th St — there for a little while, then walked down to Horace Robbins' 137 E. 60th St; There met T. E. Church & we took tea at about 7 o'clt — Spent the evening in pleasant chatting until som after 9 when I went down to Mary's — then until a little after 10 — then to 41st St & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 23, 1877

*Montrose.*

Charming mt. up at 6. bkfst, before 7 — left the house at 7:20 — got to foot of Christopher St, at 7.45 — Train left Hoboken at 8:30 so that I had 20 m. to wait. Was the only passenger in the drawing room car all the way — very hot & dusty. Qo. Comstock went up to M. on same train for his horses — Got to depot at 3.34 — by stage got to Fau Mo's at 5.30 — Charming ride some snow still lying under hill slopes. Tea at 6.30. Ben off on a fishing excursion — After tea went out & saw Wheatly & others & made arrangements to begin work to-morrow — Every chatted with Fau & wrote a letter to Geo. Took it to P.O. at 11 — glorious moonlight, to bed som after 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
