## Left Page

### March, Thursday 29, 1877

Overcast, bleak & cold with
high wind — got to studio
ab 9. & spent the m'f
working on "The Saco" — W.C.
To lunch at 1 — back at 2.15
Miss Boyd (2) W. Vail (5)
& W. Colgate (11) came. —
They kept me busy until
6 — ab 6.30 — went to K's to
tea & at 8 took Fanny to
her singing club, then at
8.30 — to Emma J's — her mother
is now in town — a very pleasant
evening until 10.45 — then to
studio, fixed fire etc. &
got to 41. 8? ab 11.35 and
right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 30, 1877

Up very late; brilliant m'f,
B'lp. at 9 & got to studio at 10.
After some delay got to work
first painting "Cathedral Spires"
Yosemite (×) Did not feel
like the work a bit. Silva
came in about 12.30 & for an hour
talked of W.C. matters & Smith's
'unpleasantness' with me. In
the Smith matter I carried a very
stiff upper lip. To lunch at 2 &
back at 3. Worked on painting
until 6 when I got it all in.
To tea at 7.15 — after that
read newspapers until 8.45
then to studio, there until
10.15 — then to 41. 8?, & to bed
before 11. [illegible — ~4 words]
