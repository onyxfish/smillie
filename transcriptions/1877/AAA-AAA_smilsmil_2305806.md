## Left Page

### May, Friday 18, 1877

Hotter than ever altho' a very pleasant westery breeze was blowing all day — Ther. in my studio got up to 88°. I got to shop at 9.30 & commenced painting again on Chilé arms for A.B.N. Co — worked all day on the mearth, mainly on the oak leaves — besides all the interruptions incident to the work going on in the bldg., setting hearth etc — had several calls, among them Jos. Jessup & Mr. Ch[illegible] J — To lunch at 2.30 — back at 3.30 — painted until 6.30 — to tea at 7.30 — to bed at 10.30 after looking over English papers.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 19, 1877

*at Aries Abbatt's.*

Still very hot, tempered by a delicious breeze — Got to studio a few minutes after 9. To work on Chilé arms. & painted under many difficulties all the a.m. — to lunch soon after 1 — then dressed & packed bag & got to studio about 3 — worked until 4 — cleaned palette & with Geo. went to foot of E. 23d St. & took 4.35 boat to Morris-ania & then cars to Simpson's. Mr. Abbatt & Son going up with us. Got to the Abbatts about 4.30. Lounged & sauntered about until tea time, between 6 & 7 — Every, chatting in doors until 10.30 — then to bed.
