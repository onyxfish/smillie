## Left Page

### September, Saturday 29, 1877

Foggy mtg as usual but before it cleared cloudless
& warm. At 9.30 went to barn yard where a blt. & white cow
was waiting for me. She was very uneasy & would not stand tied so I had unusual trouble in getting my study.
Worked until 1 — then to dinner which was served at 1.30 — at 2.30 again at my con. study & painted until 5 — then cleaned up. Think this study proves to be the best so far in spite of trouble. Tea near 7 — Every in Maud's Room. Ella, Maya, Jack & I at Euchre until near 10 —
To bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 30, 1877

Cloudless & warm. Spent the mtg writing — finished a long letter to Emma J —
Dinner at 1.30 — Soon after the gent. Aleck & I went up on the hill, circling around by the farm Road & coming down over the new fallow just sowed to wheat.
Very breezy & hazy — Took up a pocket barometer of Jacks that showed 571 feet — he says it is out of order as it was on other occasions showed near 700 ft. Tea at 6.30 Eugene & wife there — Every wrote a letter to father — most of the time in Maud's Room — To bed soon after 10 —
