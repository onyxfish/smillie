## Left Page

### February, Thursday 1, 1877

Still pleasant, but with dubious symptoms. After
bkfs. walked to P.O. then
home & to work on etching
scratching with dry-point
digging with graver and
finally laying ground &
filling in parts with more
etching. also took proofs.
Dinner about 1 — went out
for a short time after dinner
very nice — worked on etching
until dark — Tea at 6 — at
7.30 went to Mr. Chas. Williams
there until 8.40 — then to
Mrs. Peter Helman's — there
until near 10 — Then home
Rev. J. 2. Adams, a sort of Evan-
stayed with us over night

## Right Page

### February, Friday 2, 1877

Gloomy day. Rain commencing
about 6.30 a.m. & falling heavy
all day & most of the night.
Father quite under the weather
— stomach trouble — Immediately
after Bkfs. got at my etching
biting, proving, rebiting &
proving again. Dinner at
1 oclock. Tried to get ready
to go to N.Y. on the 4.40 train
but had to give it up to
finish proving — Tea at 6 —
left the house at 6.45 — &
to N.Y. on 7.30 train — Got
to G.C. Depot at 10 — Right
to 61st St. & to bed at 10.45 —
Rev. J. 2. A. — again at the
P. — home over night —
