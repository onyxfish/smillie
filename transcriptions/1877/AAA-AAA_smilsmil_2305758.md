## Left Page

### February, Monday 5, 1877

fine day — Up at 7.30 — Got
to studio, after going to Mary
at 10 o'clk. The whole m'f
lost between callers & trying
to get in order for work — To
dinner at 1 — back at 2.15
& Farrer called — Finally
set palette & painted
for a couple of hours on little
"Penna. Hills" for wedding R.:
Jard. C. f. — To tea at 6.30 —
m'f. went to Mary's — then
to studio & at 9 to N.a. —
Met J.W. Wood & J.g. Brown
— Walked up town with
J.g.B. & agreed to run
again for Pres't of N.C. Soc'y
— to bed at 11.

Owing to mistake in turning
the leaves, this is
Monday, Feb 5th.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 6, 1877

fine day — got to studio
soon after 9 — The m'f
all got away from me without
my doing anything else than
get out all my old work that
is under way or unfinished —
& plan to go on with it. To
dinner at 1.30 — after that
went to Knordle's — there to
his Water Color exbn at Kurtz's
called on Kurtz — then to N.a.
& to studio about 3.30 — Got
some paper stretched to
begin A.B.N. Co. drawing —
Nicole came with [illegible] from
Harper's & the rest of P.m. was
used up discussing Ill. cab.
Tea at 6.45 — m'f with gel.
at Avery's at 8.30 — Mary Whitney
there at M.a. fD. at 10 — then to
studio — then to 41 S. — to bed at 11.20
Tuesday. Feb 6.
