## Left Page

### October, Friday 19, 1877

was not able to get out of bed until near 11 o'clk. owing to headache — then had a bowl of gruel and to work on my etching. Laid a ground & scratched and etched all over it — stuck to my work in spite of my headache as long as I could see — then paid up for it with more pain for an hour or two — Tea at 6. Kate at Watt's — mother at Mary's. Uncle W. & aunt A. in their places — on their way to Vassar Coll. to hear Corning's 3d lec. on Art — Geo. & father went to Ch. to hear Gallagher — I sat in big chair, my head ache clearing away until 8.30 — then I walked up to Ch. & home with the folks — Rain began to fall before we got in — To bed at 9.20

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 20, 1877

Heavy Rain all day long. Enjoyed it exceedingly — think I never saw autumn foliage more beautifully sober — worked all day on etching — 2d biting in the mf, then some graver and dry point work — Dinner at 12.30 — After that, fixed paper & press & the P.M. was spent proving & working on my etching — burnishing or scratching or doing some little thing after almost every bf. Gave up when I could see no longer — Tea at 6 — At 9.30, although the weather was horrid, went to Wright's, 8o Liberty St. there until 8.20 — then to Bisby's — there until 9.30 — then home & to bed by 10.30.
