## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 3, 1877

Overcast mf. clearing before 10 o'clk. Walked with
Geo. my eyes giving me much trouble — on my back
with a lotion on my eyes. Tried to do some letter
writing — but gave it up —
Dinner about 1 o'clk. Loafed about during
the P.M. doing a little Reading & writing — a
very lovely shower bet.
6 & 7 o'clk. Later, Father
Geo. & I went up to
Matt's — back ab't 9.30
& to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 4, 1877

Overcast & Remarkably cool mf. After b'kfp. took
a short walk with Geo.
Then wrote a note to
Flora Lathrop — also other
notes — About noon W. Loomis
called — dinner near 1.
After that got to work on
1st illustration of Whittier's
poem "June on the Merrimac"
for Miss Leggett. Wrote her
a note on the subject.
About 5.30 walked up to
town with Geo. & on way back
stopped at Robt. Palmer's
& Mr. Loomis's. Tea ab't 6.15.
Lovely evg. on the Roof
for some time after Sunset.
An exceedingly quiet "fourth."
Matt & Ed. came down — ab't
9.30 walked up with them
— back & to bed soon after
10.
