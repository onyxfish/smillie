## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 31, 1877

Glorious day — cloudless with fresh breeze from north. Blks —
at near 8 — Geo. & I went up town
I did a number of messages, then
went to Mary's — Spent a good
part of the mttg there — then home.
Half past me before we had dinner
Nellie, the servant girl, having been
sick for more than a week, all of
the house work comes up on Mother
& Kate. During the a.f. & p.m. folded
& addressed a number of papers
having notices of Willie's death.
Wrote letters to W. E. J. Phelps &
Miss Leggett. Matt came to tea.
After sunset, Mary, Kate & the Dr.
stopped awhile at the house, they
having been out for a ride. Ab.
& I walked up town with father
& Matt — went to P.O. then to Mary's
where I met Geo. We stayed
until 9.45 — then home & I got
to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 1, 1877

Again overcast, close & muggy with
tendency to showers during the p.m.
A late bkfs. after which Geo. & I
went up town — I went out to
Parker's mill — about 11 back to Mary's
& at 12, home. Dinner at hoclk
after that, a nap, then wrote
some notes, among others to
Mr. S. H. Sayre at Montrose &
to Miss Abbott, walked up
to P.O. before tea — nearly 7
before we had tea — About
8 I went to Mary's where Kate
was — Abg went to Dr. Otis's
with her — home at 10 and
right to bed.
