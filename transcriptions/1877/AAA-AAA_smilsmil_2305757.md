## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 7, 1877

Charming day — got to
studio soon after 9 and to
work Repainting "Bridal Veil"
for Matthews sale. Mr. Etten
& Co. & another lady called &
spent 1½ hour — To lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and
painted on Bridal Veil —
Yale came with Bf. of my sitting
To tea at 6.30 & soon after 8 at
W.C. Rooms. 51 W. 10 S. Informal
meeting — Jagar Bier etc. — New.
Balper men invited — Burbank
& Smith gave us recitations —
Got to 41 S. & bed about
12.30.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 8, 1877

Splendid day — weather could
hardly be finer. A little used
up about the head — After bf.
went to Mary's — & then down town
— to Harpers — saw Parsons & finally
arranged for use of cuts in catalogue
of W.C. Socy. Then to Sackett's — met
F.H. Smith there — then to A.B.N. Co.
— then to Menger's — again to Harber's
then lunch — then, at 1.45 rode up
to N.A. of D. Wrote 4 notes & sent
them off by Alfred, — then to studio
& busied about little things
until 6.30 — Then to tea — After
that to Mary's — Jee went to Po-
this P.m. with Willie & Watt &
Kate came down — To studio at
8.30 — at 9.15 to N.A. of D. There
until 10.15 — then to 41 S. & to
bed at 10.45. [illegible — ~2 words]
