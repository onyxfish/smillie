## Left Page

### June, Friday 15, 1877

Another very hot day — got to Studio's at 9 o'clk. and to work on Colgate No. 2. Stuck to it until 12 o'clk — Thence a Rest — To lunch at 1 — feeling very much used up, Took a nap, & did not get to Studio until about 3 — worked well during the Rest of P.M. & got a good days work done. To tea as usual about 8 — near 9 when I went to Mamp's. found her alone & sat with her until near 11 — D. Rid[?] from Po'k this P.M. — has signed a lease for a house in Chrerchl S. — Po[illegible] To 41 S. & to bed soon after 11. Rain at night.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 16, 1877

Very muggy & close with some heavy showers — So dark at times that I could hardly paint. Got to Studio's about 9 & to work on Colgate No. 2, to finish up the last corner — Got it almost done when I went to lunch at 12.45 — After that changed dress and prepared to go to Riverdale — back to Studio at 2.45 & finished my painting, washed up my brushes & took 4.15 train from 42d St. with Charl. Dinner about 6 — Evening cleared very pleasantly so that it was delightful on the broad piazza. Was generally introduced & the every was got rid of in general chatting & lounging. It was nearly 11 o'clk when I got to bed.
