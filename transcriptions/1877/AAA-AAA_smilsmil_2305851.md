## Left Page

### August, Monday 20, 1877

Clear day, beautiful in
the woods but hot in the
sunshine. Spent the
m'f under the trees in the
fair ground outlining a
study of Beech Trees — At
12 went down town & did
several chores — Dinner
at 1 — Afternoon worked
on Study of Rocks under
the hill & got it all painted
in — Wheatley & Johnston's
boy at work for me all day
digging my cellar deeper —
Tea at 6.30 — About 8, went
to Mr. Judge Jessup's — Fanny
not in — Half an hour with his
mother then to P.O. and to
Fan's — The Searles there —
whist until 10.30 — then
on the hill — after 11 when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 21, 1877

Another clear, hot day. Got
to studio at 8.30 — Wheatley
& Johnston at work for me —
Painted in fair ground until
12.30 on Study continued yes-
turday — Dinner at 1 — then
again to studio — Painted
on Study of Beech Trunk
and dis-gusted myself
with my poor work — Tea
at about 7 — At 8 went to
Mr. Coulper's with Mr. Hollins-
worth & Miss H. & &c — There
for half an hour — then down
to Searle's — Fan & Ben there —
whist until 10.30 — then took
Fan home — on the hill
before 11 o'ck. & right to
bed. [illegible — ~4 words] [illegible — ~3 words]
