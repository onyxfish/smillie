## Left Page

### November, Monday 26. 1877

Very warm & showery — got to Studio at 9.30 & to work painting on 13×16". Willow or "Cress Brook". Much bothered with varying light — Lunch in Studio between 1 & 2 — Gaslight early so that the day's work amounted to but little — Took a walk with Geo. between 5 & 6 — then tea in Studio — At 8.15 went over to N.A. No quorum of Council but sat & chatted until 10 — there to 41. St. & to bed just at 11. Cloudless night & very warm —

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 27. 1877

Cloudless mng. but quickly overcast & altho' threatening no rain fell until after 10 o'clk P.m — went up to see Charl after 9½ — met J.D.A. nard & went into his 49th St. Studio — Did not get to my own Studio until 10.15 — then to work on "Cress Brook". At 12.30 went out & had a short walk — went in to Knoedler's & met Mrs. Jessup & Josie — lunch at 1.30 — Mary Perrine & Miss Burling called — did not get much work done — At 6. went to Kimball's — Kate there. Geo. took her to see Rip Van Winkle & took Fanny to her club & then to Studio where I fussed over etchings, trying to arrange them in frames until after 10 — to 41. St. & to bed about 11.
