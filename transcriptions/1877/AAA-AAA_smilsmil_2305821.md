## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 19, 1877

Sweltering hot day — Got to studio soon after 9 and to work on "Herder" — but my head was so heavy & stupid that I did but little work — what I did was painting on the figure. Miss Abbatt sent me a lot of flowers & strawberries — Emma J. came up & invited me to lunch — went there at 1 o'clk. Rosalie Butler there — at 2 took Emma & Ketty J. to Union League club — there until 3.15 — Then to studio — about 4 — Miss Colgate & Miss Stone came — The pictures were pronounced satisfactory & will be sent home to-morrow. To tea at 8 — nearly 9 when I got to Sturtevant Ho. Spent what remained of the even with Fan M. who came from Montrose yesterday — Did not get to bed until 12 o'clk.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 20, 1877

Delightful day by comparison — clear with fresh breeze — Up late & did not get to studio until almost 10. Then to work on "Herder" & painted over horse — To lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 & finished horse — J. C. Nicoll & Mary (big) Cook called — I fussed over boy & saw Coleman's plate until 8 o'clk. Then to tea — 9 o'clk. when I got to Sturt. Ho. — with Fan & the Dr. until 10.30 — W. & Mrs. Joy there part of the time — To bed. S— & to bed at 11.
