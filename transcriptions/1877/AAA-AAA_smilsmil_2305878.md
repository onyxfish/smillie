## Left Page

### October, Thursday 11, 1877

Pleasant day, altho' at times quite overcast. Got to work on A.B.N. Co's drawing of fountains at Concepcion & stuck to it until 12 when I went out & took a little walk. Dinner at 12.30 — worked until near 5 and got drawing finished. Had another walk but 5 ob — Tea soon after 6 — Well. Made paste &
menueis drawing. Got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 12, 1877

Cold, cloudy & windy. After bkfst. made to get. Corliss — Actuary of Penn. Aca. of Fine Arts — then to P.O. — then to Wam's — then home, set palette and to work touching up one of my Pluniera cow studies. Dinner at 12.30 — again worked on cow until about 4.30 & at 5 out for another walk. Uncle W. called & I went up town with him. Tea at 6.30 — Father went to N.Y. this mth & K[illegible] at 7 — I went out to Vassar College & heard Rev. W. Corning deliver one of his course of art lectures — "Early art in the East" — I was pleased that I went but learned nothing new. Met a number of friends. Reached home at 9.50 — I read awhile & to bed at 11.
