## Left Page

### September, Friday 21, 1877

*Towanda. Ward House. Room 41.*

Up a little before 6 o'clk &
Packed trunk until hlf 2
tine at 7.30 — at 8.30 went
to Studio — Lyp. Man came
for box & Cottrell came to
fix front door — Soon after
9 went about bidding good
bye & attending to last
messages. Back to dinner
at 11.30 & at 11.45 off in stage
for depot. Away at 2, with
Priscilla P— Mr. R. Ri'en
& her Mother — got to Junk
hammock about 1.30 Priscilla
got away at 2.25 & me
at 3.15 & Reached Towanda
at 5 — Rode right to Hotel —
Got & I Foot. a short walk
& a with Azur [illegible]
at 6 — another short walk
glorious moonlight — then
in my Room & Read news
paper. Another short walk
& to bed at 9 o'clk.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 22, 1877

*Bernice.*

Up at 5.30 & at 6.15 off by Rail
for Bernice — Sharp, cold & clear
mrf — 29 m. to B — got there at
8.15, very hungry & found Dud.
& — waiting for us. Went right
to the house & soon had a good
hlf2 — then out & wandered
about — took Sketches in the
Breaker — to the house soon after
12 & as I felt headachy, lay
on the bed until dinner time
at 1 — At the dinner table —
introduced to Mr. Blight & 3
daughters & Miss Humbs hueys.
P.M. again wandering about
until about 4, when with Dud
Ben & Gel I rode towards
Deshore & had a beautiful
view — in at 6.30 — then tea —
Intro'd to Mr. Blight, Subs. of the
Royal Sock Coal mining Co —
Eve'g in parlor chatting
until 10 — then to bed.
