## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 28, 1877

Pleasant day — after bf.
went up to Charl's — walked
down with him & got to shop
about 10 — then to work on
Cress Brook — At 2.30 Mrs.
Geo. Whitney & Enoch Lewis
came in & Geo. & I went over
with them to the Albemarle
& had lunch — at 2.30 I went
to Fanny K's to see Kate but
she had gone to the Thomas
Rehearsal — as Fanny K. had
given tickets to Geo. & me —
went down there & met Kate
who left at 3.15 to go to depot —
Charl is to go home with her
to spend Thanksgiving — I
stayed to the end of Rehearsal
then to studio — While there
at tea, W. & Mrs. Coleman came
in bringing some pumpkin pie
Rev. Dr. Bellows made a very
pleasant & chatty visit from about
5 to 6.30 o'clk — feeling tired
I determined not to go to the
Reception at Met. Mus. Part —
on my way up town called on
Miss Lippitt — 1164 Bdway — then
at Renner's & at 9.30 — to 127 —
Gave myself a good rubbing
with hair mittens & then with salt
water & alcohol & to bed abt 0.

## Right Page

### Thanksgiving.

### November, Thursday 29, 1877

Miserable rainy day — and
my head as thick as the weather —
Got to studio at 9.30 but was
not able to set palette until
noon — Lunch in studio at
1.30 — Finished 2° painting
over of "Cress Brook" & then
lounged in big chair till
6 — Made a call on Jom.
Smith & had tea at 7 —
Storm cleared away &
bright starlight — at
8.30 went in to Coleman's
— he was in bed used up
with a headache — Geo.
& I sat & chatted with
Mrs. C. until 10 — then
to studio & fixed fire —
& to 41. 8. & to bed before
11.
