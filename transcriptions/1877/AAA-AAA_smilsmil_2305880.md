## Left Page

### October, Monday 15, 1877

Charming fall day — at times clear, then overcast, but very nice — After bkfst —
wrote a short letter to Ferg. —
then a short walk — then with pencil made small
sketch of cattle to etch from —
Dinner at 12.30 — After
dinner, finished sketch &
commenced a second one —
worked upon it until 4
30 — or 5 — then Geo. & I sauntered
to the Bailey place & took notes
of the west effects — back to
tea at 6 — at 7 we went in
to the Warren Organ concert
at the New Baptist Ch. — It
was crowded — the organ music
was heavy, being too scholarly
& scientific — the programme
very long — it was 11 before
we got home — Right to
bed.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 16, 1877

Another charming day. After
bkfst. I had several delays
so that I did not get out to walk
and it was nearly before I
got to my work, tracing the
sketch for etching & finishing
2nd sketch, commenced yesterday.
Dinner at 12.30 — again to
work — got poor transfer in press
& retraced to apt with Red, then
to work etching — lost light
early — 4.30 — & at 5 went out
to walk. I have arranged a
temporary truss that gives me
all the support I need now —
Walked until near 6 — then tea
at 4.30 — out with Geo. called on
Mrs. Loomis — saw Miss Douglas — then
at 8.15 at Miss Nicks — out — then to
Mary's & to Mr. Douglas (next door)
then to Mr. Wheeler (Rev.) — not
in — then to Mr. Hull — not in —
near 9 — Geo. went home and I
went to the Baptist Ch. Dedication
Services — begging worse than beggars
so I cleared out — went home
did some writing & to bed at
10.30.
