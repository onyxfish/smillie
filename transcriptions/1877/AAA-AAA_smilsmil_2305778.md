## Left Page

### March, Monday 19, 1877

Clear mf, overcast p.m. &
snow again at night — Got
to studios soon after 9 — Got
Miss Abbatt started on her copy
of my sketch — painted a little —
dusted a little. Then made a
slight charcoal scheme 10×18
of W. Colgate's house at Riverdale.
To lunch at 1 — back at 2. W.
Vail came (2°) also W. Colgate
(8°) gave up my p.m. to them.
About 6 W. Lynch came in &
sat with us for half an hour.
To tea at 7 — Uncle Willie
came down from Ro'thro a.m.
Back to studios at 8.30 and
more until after 10 — then to
41 S. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 20, 1877

Overcast mf, pleasanter afternoon
& beautiful evef. Got to studios
at 9 o'clk. & to work on W.C. on
"the Saco" laying in anew the
half that had been washed out.
To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& continued in drawing until
6 — then to tea — Uncle W. there.
& at 8 o'clk. at 51 W. 10th. Meeting
of W.C. Socy. Got at our work
in the new Constitution at 8.30
& stuck steadily to it until
10.40 — adjourned at 10.45
after approval of entire Contu-
& By-laws. Room very close —
was perfectly saturated with
the horrible tobacco smoke —
walked up town with J.G.,
Brown & Shurtleff & to bed at
11.45.
