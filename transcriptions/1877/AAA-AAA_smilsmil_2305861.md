## Left Page

### September, Sunday 9, 1877

Charming day — After bkfst —
wandered about my place
until church times, then
to Presbn Ch. & heard Mr.
Miller. After service, walked
home with fanny Jessup — at
her gate met fanny Payson and
walked home with her and
sat upon piazza until near
1 o'clk. Dinner before 2.
Sat about, chatted, read &
had a short walk — at 4 wnt
to studio's. Read awhile —
then a walk & to tea about
6 — at 7 took another walk —
stopped at Epis. Ch. — too cool.
Ret'd to Sayres & until 10 —
reading astronomy & taking
notes. Then W. & Mrs. Hollings[worth]
came in & we had a discussion
up on Beecher & the duties of law-
yers towards clients they know to
be guilty — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 10, 1877

overcast wth., clearing miled —
To studio before 9. Ther. 60°, 80.
I kindled fires — consequently
all day with doors & windows
open. Did not get to work
until after 10 — Painting in
lower, R. h. cor. Yosemite —
Did not feel at all like
work — at 11.45. took axe and
chopped wood until 12.30 —
Then rested until dinner
at 1 — after dinner talked
with Wm Coolsen about build-
ing a summer hotel here —
did not get to work until
near 3 — Finished painting
in Yosemite at 4.15 — then
went out & rode with Geo
until 6.30 — then tea. Even
Read newspaper & wrote
until 10 o'clk — To bed at
10.30.
