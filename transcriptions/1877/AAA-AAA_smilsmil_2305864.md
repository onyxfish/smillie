## Left Page

### September, Saturday 15, 1877

Overcast, mild & breezy —
got to studio before 9 quite
used up, aching & lame all
over — probably having caught a
cold — After a time, took
paper & pencil, went out
back of the fair ground and
got three outline sketches.
Dinner at 1 — again to shop
fine my chair until near
4\. Then Hunt Sayre came
with my lumber from Cottrells.
Then Elisha & D. Ne. came —
Then Aunt H & Mr. Bard —
Tea at 6.30 — Soon after 8 at
fan's — Mr. Grannis of Chicago —
Mr. & Mrs. Riley Reid of Elmira
& Emma Smith of Binghamton
there — the girls all went to bed
at 10 while Elisha — Ben,
the Dr. & I had a Rubber at
whist — through at 11. when
I went on the hill & to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 16, 1877

Warm day, but delightful air
stirring. Mrp. went to Presby.
Ch. & heard Mr. Muneger preach
a very good sermon from the
6th v. of the 51st Psalm. After
service, awhile at fan's stoop
with Mr. Grannis & Emma Smith
then to studio & read until
1.45 — Then dinner — At 3 again
to studio — Read awhile & wrote
a letter to father. Took a walk
between 5 & 6 — delicious evening.
Tea soon after 6 — to Presby.
Ch. in the evening & heard —
[illegible] — After service —
went to fan's — there
until 10 o'clk — then on
the hill & to bed.
