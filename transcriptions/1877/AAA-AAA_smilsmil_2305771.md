## Left Page

### March, Monday 5, 1877

Pleasant day — got to studio about 9 & soon to work on 9×10, Repainting "an Elmira grain field" — went to lunch at 41. S. at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & finished the Repg. Mr. Colgate came and I gave him a lesson. — To tea at 6.45 — hurried back to studio to meet Baldwin. (Nicole could not come) and worked on Constitution until near 11 — then walked up with B. — not far from midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 6, 1877

Fine day. Up with mean sort of headache — went to studio & started Repainting "Oct. Sketch, Elk. Mt," 10×13 — to 41. S. at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked until about 4 when I was obliged to knock under. Rolled on lounge — Nicole came in for awhile — I kept still until 7.30 when Baldwin came, he soon went away & about 8.30 I got up and cleaned my palette and stumbled along to 41. S. and then tumbled into bed where I rolled all night with a headache worse than that of the day.
