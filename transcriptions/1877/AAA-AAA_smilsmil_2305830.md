## Left Page

### July, Monday 9, 1877

Milky murf & very cloudy & muggy — Did not go
out for a walk — but got to work on Whittier
illus". Worked until dinner at 12.45. About
1.30 Gilbert Pettit came & stayed until 2.30 — In
that time a very heavy thunderstorm after which
it was cooler. Worked until after 5 — lea sometime
after 6 — At 7.30 with Gel. called on Mr. Hull,
there until near 9. then to Mary's — met Father
there — Home just before 10 — To bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 10, 1877

Very warm day — went out & gathered "glad-eyed flowers
for illus". Father & Mother went by 12 train, with Uncle
M. to New Hamburgh to see the Cummings's — Gel. went
out to Hillside to spend the afternoon — I got my
Whittier illus done and at 5 o'clk. went to Sup. Co.
with pics — & to P.O. with letter to Miss Leggett — then to Watts
& met carriage — Rode down to R.R. depot — 5.40 train — on
which Belle & Mary Tweedy came. Drove home — nearly
when we had tea, Father & mother came in near 8, Emma
Bourne to tea also — Eve. all in general chatting until
11 o'clk — then to bed.
