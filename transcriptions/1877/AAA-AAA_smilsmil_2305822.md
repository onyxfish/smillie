## Left Page

### June, Thursday 21, 1877

Close, muggy day — latter part overcast — Got to studio at 9 — Heavy head — unfit to do anything — Tried to work on Mining drawing but could do nothing — note to [illegible] Coleman — To lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.30 — note to father + to Filmer — but did not do any thing that paid. To tea at 8; just escaping a very heavy thunder storm — before it was over went to Mamp's — Matt + Charle there helping in general packing up. To 41 S. at 10.30 and right to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 22, 1877

Delightful day — clean + crisp — Before going to studio I stopped at the barber's and had my hair cut, so did not get to studio until near 10 — worked some on A.B.N. Co's drawing but much troubled by my aching head. To lunch at 1 — after getting "Herder" finished to send to Burlington Vt this P.M. — To studio at 2.30 after a call on J. Haven Putnam — much of P.M. I could not work, so, slept or lounged — then worked on plans for my new floor + got to tea at 8 o'clk — at 9.15 at Emma [illegible] — two or three friends there — Fred starts with Sennuline for Paris to-morrow. Left soon after 11 — To 41 S. + to bed about 11.30.
