## Left Page

### June, Friday 1. 1877.

Steaming hot with a relentless, coppery sky — Got to studio a little before 9 — to work on Colgate No. 2 — Painted over foreground trees. Fred. Smillie came up with points & rollers for Yachting club. To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — calls from Miss Durfee & J.W. Wood. Painted until after 6, making some head way. To tea at 7.30 to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 2. 1877.

Very hot — got to studio about 9 & tried to get to work on Colgate No. 2, but it was very hard to get anything out of me. Managed to go over much of the picture, but in a very unsatisfactory manner. To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked until after 6. Then washed up my brushes & to tea at 7.45 — About 9 o'clk got to N.A. of D — last night & looked as well as I could at the pictures until 10 — Then walked home with Miss Durfee — then to 41 S; & to bed at 11.
