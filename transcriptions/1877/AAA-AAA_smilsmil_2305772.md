## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 7, 1877

Fine day — unable to get out of my bed until near 2 o'clc — Then up & had something to eat — went to studio — Baldwin & Nicoll came at 4.30 and about we finished up Constitution colpy. I got to tea before 7 — & about 8.30 again to studio — wrote a note or two & about 11.15 got to 41 S. & right to bed — Tossed until near 2 before I got to sleep — [illegible] few bad ones [illegible] helping [illegible] & [illegible] sorry [illegible] is this — prat [illegible] Jo [illegible] mark

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 8, 1877

East wind & threatening — 9.30 when I got to studio — very much demoralized — tried to work on several things but unfit to do anything — fussed my time away & at 1.30 to lunch — back at 2.30 — Mr. Colgate came at 3 — gave him a lesson — Miss Vander and a friend called — Miss [illegible] wants a W.C. with "a Red cow & amber colored water" — To tea at 6.45 — at 8.30 went to Joris & Tiffanys — pleasant call there until after 10 — Then to studio — There until after 11 — Then to 41. S. & to bed at midnight — Rain & high wind during the night — I had a Fantrum.
