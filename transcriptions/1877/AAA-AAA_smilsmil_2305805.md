## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 16. 1877.

Muggy & hot after a light fall of rain in the early morning.
Feeling very much the oppression of the heat did not get to Studio
until near 10. John had got the floor scrubbed & everything
dusted so that the place looked quite orderly. Got to work on
block of "Scub Race" but made little progress owing to noise
& confusion outside from my Room.
Just as I went out to lunch at 1 o'clk. met Wash. Wilson & as we
walked over to 5th ave. he agreed to
take my "Scub Race" at $1000 — $250 cash
the fall, next fall & Spring. Provided if he
Repents of the bargain before next fall,
will take it back & paint a small picture
for the $250. Then to lunch & at 2.30
back again — Worked on Scub Race
block & wrote to MrUrch Sewing —
To tea at 4.30 — on Sofa Reading Post
until 9.30 — then to bed.
