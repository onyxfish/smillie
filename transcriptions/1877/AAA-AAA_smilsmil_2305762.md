## Left Page

### February, Thursday 15, 1877

Beautiful day — Got to
studio at 9 — Some time
writing W.C. letters before
I got to work on A.B.M. Co.
drawing — Extremely disagreeable work — Perspective
& continuing best to lunch
at 1.45 — back at 2.45 &
continued work in outline —
Wm. Colgate came at 3.15
but went away at 4.30 when
Nicoll came. Bellows & J.J.
Brown followed — Discussed
plan of advertising until 6 —
To tea at 6.45 — Every
at studio from 8.30 until
near 9.30 — then to N.A. —
To 41 & 8 — at 10.30 & right
to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 16, 1877

Overcast & rather threatening
late in the day — Got to studio
at 9 — T.H. Smith came in &
some time was lost with W.
C. advertising matters before I
got to work on A.B.M. Co. drawing —
At 1 o'clk — went to Emma S.
& took lunch — At 2.30 to
Phil. Rehearsal — After that
went to Desaints & H.J. Dewey's —
There again to studio & at 6.15
to Fanny K's to tea — Took her
& Anna C. to their club — & at
8.30 to Nicoll's — There until
9.45 working on Constitution
for W.C. Socy — Then to N.A. —
then to studio — There until
11 — To 41 & 8 — at 11.30 and
Right to bed.

Matt & Kate at Mrs. Daning's
to stay over Sunday.
