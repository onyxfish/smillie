## Left Page

### March, Saturday 3, 1877

Cloudy but pleasant — got to studio at 9 o'clk — worked so hard yesterday that I found myself used up to-day but fussed over the furnace drawings & other things about the studio — went over to N.Ch. Last day of W.S. Spts. To lunch abt 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Late in the afternoon had quite a wind colic — went to 41 °S. at 6.45 & at about 9.30 got to Mary Willard's, 106 W 49th. To tea — very much used up — did not get to table until near 8 — nor away from it until near 9 — Then to N.Ch. Left there about 10.30 & to studio — did not get to 41 °S. until midnight — Right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 4, 1877

Overcast & windy — have not recovered from indisposition of yesterday — BLCp: late, then to Dr. Hastings'. Rev. W. Conkling preached — after service, to studio — Took a short walk about 1.30 — then back — ate a few crackers & wrote most of p.m. — At 6.30 went up to Mary's to tea, there until near 9 o'clk — then again to studio, piqued fine, did a little more writing & got to 41 °S. soon after 11 — & right to bed.
