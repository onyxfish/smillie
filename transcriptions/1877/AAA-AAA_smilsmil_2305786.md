## Left Page

### April, Friday 6, 1877

Still cloudy + windy — got to
studio at 9. feeling effects of
my two headaches this week.
Did nothing but fuss about
to lunch at [illegible] — Emma P — at
the house — back at 2.30 and
spent the afternoon on [illegible] +
into ill. A Pinyant's "Song Sparrow"
for Miss Leggett. At 6.30 to K's
to tea + at [illegible] took Fanny + Emma
to their club. Then to U.A. [illegible] D.
+ at 9.20 to studio — then for
an hour + to 41 S. at 11 +
to bed at once.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 7, 1877

Mild pleasant day — got
to studio at 9 — Spent part
of [illegible] working over 2° ill.
to "Song Sparrow" then some
work on Colgate's Charcoal
Sketches, arranging them for
the enlarged size 10½ × 19.
To lunch at 1.30 — back at
2.30 — worked on sketches
but felt very much under
the weather — To tea at 6.45
at K at Thomas's Symphony
concert — Steinway Hall — last
of the season — very nervous
+ uncomfortable — the 2°
Beethoven Symph. — delicious —
Largo by Handel very large —
Faust by Liszt too much
for me — took Emma
home — then myself +
to bed about 11.30.
