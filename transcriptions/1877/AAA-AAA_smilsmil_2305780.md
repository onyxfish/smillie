## Left Page

### March, Sunday 25, 1877

Pleasant day with promise of a change — N.Y., went to D Hastings's & heard him preach from Hebrews 12'. 2d "The joy that I have set before you". After service walked down to studio and back to Mamp's to dinner at 1.30. At 3.20 again at studio. Did a little writing. Geo. there also — Did not go out to tea but ate some crackers & wrote upon Annual Report until 10 o'clk. To 41' S'. in a mizzling rain at 10.45 — & to bed soon after 11 — Wind & storm all night.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 26, 1877

Horrible day 1 wind & Rain. Got to studio after calling at Miss Harman. at 30 then went at 10 to Bayard Tayler's, 142 E, 18' S'. the revised a copy of N.C. Constitution. To studio again at 10.30 — worked on "Song Sparrow" &c. until near 2 — then, as it was raining floods & blowing great guns, I ate a lunch of crackers — Mr. Vail came (4½) and Mr. Colgate (10½). Haven Putnam called to see me about ill." The Flood of Years". At 6.30 walked to 41' S'. in a pelting rain. Left me "Song Sparrow" &c. at Miss Leggatt's — Back to studio at 8.20, work until after 10 — To 41' S'. at 11 & then light to bed.
