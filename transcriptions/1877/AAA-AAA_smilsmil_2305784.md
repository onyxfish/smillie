## Left Page

### April, Monday 2, 1877

Heavy Rain all day. Got to studio at 9 and spent the mrf. Charcoaling view of Mr. Rob. Colgate's Ho. from the north side. Went to lunch abt - back at 2. Miss Boyd came (3) also Vail (6) and W. Colgate (12) and spent the afternoon working and seeing me work. To tea at 7. About 9 o'clk. got to N.A.D. Reception. 52 around suptū — great crush — all sorts — walked up to 41st with E. M. Ward abt 11.30 to bed before midnight.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 3, 1877

Pleasant mrf. got up with unpleasant premonitions of a coming headache — got to studio soon aftr 9 — Had several callers, among them Misses Emma Beach + — Bullard. Worked in Water Colors on a small sketch — Morf among Humboldt Mts" — About one o'clk. was obliged to give up — wrote a note to Mrs. R & B. Spring saying that it was impossible for me to accept her invitation to lunch + meet friends — then spread myself on lounge and lay there until aftr 7 — then to 41st S: got some tea and at 8.45 went to Mary's and visited with Mother (she came down this mrf) until after 10. Then to 41st S: + to bed before 11.
