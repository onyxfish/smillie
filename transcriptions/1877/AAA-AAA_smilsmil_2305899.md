## Left Page

### November, Thursday 22, 1877

Overcast & rain — up late
B1p: as usual at Hotel
Royal — nearly 10 when I
got to studio — very much
work — accomplished nothing —
at 12 went to Na of D. School
& saw Prof. Meinwarth on the
subject of forming a sketch
class — a short walk & back
to studio — to work on 11 × 17 —
Did not have lunch until
after 2 — could not see to
paint after 4 — but got in
all called on 11 × 17 — Nicoll
came in at dusk — at 5
went to Snedecor's & ordered a
frame — then walked — back
to studio & wrote until 6.30
then tea — then framing &
framing until 8.30 — then to
Renner's & ordered two frames —
at 9.30 went to Divings &
got some of my trades old clothes
& things, then to 127 — & at
10.30 Ge: came in & we went
to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 23, 1877

Matter still threatening —
got to studio at 9.20 & spent
the most working in charcoal
on "Placid & the Adirondack Mts.
from Whiteface" 22 × 40 — at
1 o'clc. had lunch in studio.
at 2 Mrs. Colman came in
& with Ge: we went to the
Academy of Music — first
Phil. Rehearsal of the Season.
The: Thomas conductor — the
Pastoral Symphony was given
splendidly — Back to studio
at 5 — tea at 6 — I spent
the eveg until 10.30 trying
to arrange my corner for
etchings — got very tired
without accomplishing
much — to 44: S: & to bed
at 11.
