## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 22, 1877

Fine mef. got up with a mean sort of headache &
to studio at 9 — Unable to
do anything — about 11 went
to N.A. & from there at 12
to 41st St. and Right to bed
Slight showers all the
P.m. & evef. Did not
get up until after 8 — had
a bowl of gruel about 9
— went up to my Room
at 11 & Read Post & looked
over English papers until
12.30 — to bed at 12.45 &
to sleep about 2. —

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 23, 1877

Charming mef. Up at 9 —
got to studio before 11 — Set
palette & at last finished
Chili arms for A.B.N. Co.
To lunch at 1 — after that
Rode down to Devreej's (Failor)
Paid him, then up to studio
at 3 o'clk. — "Outlined" Western
Herder", a Replica of "Gaucho"
for Mr. Phelps of Burlington
Vt. Got things in order to
begin Repainting to morrow
on the Colgate pictures. To
tea at 7.30 — At 8.30 called on
Mr. J.T. Williams. 18 W. 36th St —
there until near 10 — then to 41st
& to bed at 10.30.
