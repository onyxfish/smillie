## Left Page

### January, Friday 12, 1877

Dull day — with dry, sleety storm in the eve'g. Got to
N.A. of D. about 9.20 — there until 10.30 — then to studio.
The Tweeday Girls came soon after & made a call — Worked
on "the Truants" until about 1, then prepared lunch —
then worked until after 4 — Rossel came & took
all my drawings except "the Truants" — At 5 went
over to N.A. of D — back at 6 —
there to the K's to tea —
Took Fanny & Harriet to their
club — then to N.A. then until
8.45 — then to studio. Fixed
fire, did some constr[uing]
& got to 41 [illegible] S. about 1
& right to bed. [illegible]
[illegible] — 11 [illegible]
0.5. 10 [illegible] bed at 0 — E[illegible] 1.1

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 13, 1877

Overcast — Got to studio
soon after 9 — then to N.A. of D.
There until after 11, passing
pictures — Got to studio about
noon very tired — To lunch
with S. at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& worked on "the Truants" as
long as I could see — then
to N.A. & at 6.20 to 41 S. —
Dressed — had tea — at 7.30
went to Coleman Ho. & from
there with Belles & Mary P. —
to Phil. Concert. After that
with them to 43? S. — then
to 41 S. & to bed shortly
before midnight.
