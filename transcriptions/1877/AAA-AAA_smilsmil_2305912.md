## Left Page

### December, Sunday 16, 1877

Like an alpine day —
Up late, Bkf. at 10 —
a saunter in Reservoir
Sq. — then to Dr. Hastrup's
& heard Prof. Howard Crosby
"Luke 8 ch. 25 v." "Where
is thy faith"? After service
walked down to Studio.
There until 2 — then to
Hotel Royal & had dinner
at 3 — Soon after 4, again
to Studio & had a nap on
Sofa — At 7 went in to Tura
J's — there until nearly —
again to Studio — then
until 10.45 — then to
41 S. & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 17, 1877

Very nice — part of time
overcast — Got to Studio at
9.45 — Set palette & began
painting on 25×40. "Lake
Placid from Whiteface" —
Pitched into the sky and
worked like a house on fire.
Lunch at 2 & painted as
long as I could see — getting
in sky & mountains. Father
stopped in in the morning
& returned in the eveg
& took tea with us at 7 —
at 6 I took a walk — at
8 I went to N.A. Council
meeting — back to Studio
at 10 — [illegible] & Father there
Had been down to "Century"
at 11, in at Remein's for
a few minutes — Got to
bed at 11.30.
