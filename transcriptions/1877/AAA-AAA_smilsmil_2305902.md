## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 20, 1877

Beautiful day — Got to
Studio's soon after 9 — found
it very cold — fire in stove
burning poorly — so, kindled
fire in grate & commenced
painting on 11×17 Cattle
dozing. Father came in about
11 — just from Po[?]. on his way
down town — At 1 went out
& had a walk — Nearly 2
when lunch was served in
Studio. Then on with painting.
Miss Nellie Jacobs made her
appearance when Studio to-day.
Father came up again about
5 — thru [illegible] — Tea in Studio
at 6 after I had had a walk.
Father seemed to enjoy it
very much — At 7.30 I went to
Fanny K's & took her to her club.
Then to N.A. School to see about
forming a Sketch class. Wilmarth
not in — Then to Studio — looked
over etchings & grate and
10 o'clk. Got to H.R. and
to bed at just about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 21, 1877

Pleasant day. Father came
to Hotel Royal & breakfasted with
us — Then to see the Hinshelwood
girls & to studio about 10 — Painted
on 11×17. About 12 went out
& had a walk. Lunch in
Studio at 1 — Afternoon, the
weather overcast — could not
see to paint much after 4.
A short walk & tea at 6 —
At 7.15 walked down to 10th
S: Studio Bldg. — Water Color
Socty Meeting. Board Meeting
at 7.30 — Socty meeting at 8.30
The main business was push-
ing through an amendment to
our new constitution changing
the titles of our classes of
membership — Adjourned
at 10 — walked up with
Colman, Bellows & Tiffany
to bed at 10 in our new quarters
at Hinshelwood's — 127 W. 41st.
Back Parlor.
