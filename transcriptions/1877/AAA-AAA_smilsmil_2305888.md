## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 31. 1877.

*Hotel Royal Room 52.
cor. Sixth Ave. 440 S.*

Dull mist has developed into a very wet day. Got to studio about 9 + worked awhile in my closets — about 11 Maud + Mrs. Gramus of Chicago called — then the young man to finish [illegible] came + went again. At 1 I went to lunch in a heavy shower — Back at 2 — found Father — Fred S. — + Jack D. were waiting for me — found that Mother came to town yesterday my + Is to Return with Father today — Father left at 3.30 — Jack went off with George + I spent the rest of p.m. overseeing young man orl[illegible] my floor. To Bath House got washing + went up to Hotel Royal — very soon settled in our Room — then had tea in the Restaurant — then to Colmans + before 9 to the Irelands + there rejoined Geo. Summa not in — at 10 o'clk — left — + got to bed a little before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 1. 1877.

Charming day — at 8 o'clk had a first rate [illegible] — before 9 got to Studio — Made quite a long visit in Wyant's Room + Miss Durfee made a long call on me bringing me $4500 Result of sale in Chicago of my Water Color "Cedars" 6×10 in. At 1.30 went to Dolan's + got some raw oysters — then climbed to the top of that building near going up for 4th Ave + 26th St. to see Louis T. [illegible]'s new Studio + apartments — just saying save of the floors — back to Studio at 2.30 + worked on composition with Charcoal. Cattle in pasture × . Belce, Mam + Florence [illegible] came in — at 5.30 I went to Arnold's with Mam + Florence + then to 44th St. + 1 day ave. + went with Bulee — Tea at Hotel Royal at 7 — went to Hoffman H[ouse]. Maud + Jack gone there to Colmans for 3¼ of an hour — then to Kimball's for a few minutes then to Marten's — 155 W. 21 S — where I rejoined George — there until 10.20 — then to 140th St — + to bed a little before 11.
