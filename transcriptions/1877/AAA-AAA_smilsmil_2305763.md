## Left Page

### February, Saturday 17, 1877

High wind & cold with a snow fleemy or two. Got to studios at 9 — will & om came — worked on drawing until 12.30 — then to lunch — in 41' S: Geo has Ret'd from Po. & was in studio — Mr. Page (Miss Blaewelt) called. Back at 2.15 & worked on drawing until 5.15 when I got it finished — Mary & Belle J — called — To tea at 6.30 — at 7.30 called at Colmoulte for M & B & took them to Philharmonic — after that walked up to studios (the girls went to Plainfield with Nellie Cock) there until after 11 — then to 41' S. & to bed about midnight.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 18, 1877

Clear, windy & cold — In the mvg; took Matt & Kate to D'. Hastings & heard him preach his 2d sermon from the text John. 14 ch. 6 v. "I am the way, the truth & the light" — after service, Rid'd with the girls to 41' S: & had dinner soon after 1 — at 2.30 went to studios & spent the P.M. cleaning up & hunting to Lavee & Miss McDonael. To tea at Mary's at 7 — Geo, Charl. Matt, Kate & I went again to Hastings — after service to 41' S: got to bed at 10.30.
