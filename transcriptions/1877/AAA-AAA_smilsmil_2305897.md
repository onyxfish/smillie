## Left Page

### November, Sunday 18, 1877

Clear, cold & windy — Got up with a headache that promised to grow worse instead of better — This was a great disappointment to me as I had hoped to go down to Staten Island with Maud, Jack & Ge² to dine with the Jessups — M. & J. came to hotel just before 10 & with Ge² went to foot of Dey St. for 10 o'clk boat. I went to studio soon had my fire roaring & in my big chair waited to see which way my headache would jump — By 12 o'clk I felt much better — went out & walked on Madison Sq. until near 1 — then to Columnus & invited myself to dinner — Had a very pleasant time until near 3 — then to studio — wrote a little — fussed a little — read a little — Ge² & Re² about 5.30 — Tea some after 6 — at 7.30 we went to Howard Crosby's & heard him on "the blessings of justification by faith" — I was much interested & pleased — Before 9 o'clk — again at studio — finished letter to father — to Hotel Royal at 10 o'clk. & right to bed —

## Right Page

### November, Monday 19, 1877

Bright clear & windy — Bkf². at 8 — Got to Studio at 9.30 & to work on 12×18 and painted in foliage. About 1 o'clk Ge² & I went out & walked a mile or more, then in & had lunch in Studio. Then painted until 4.30 when I could see no longer. Ge² & I walked with Miss Abbatt to her lodgings 14 E. 32. Then I went on to Station E. 8th Ave. near 33rd St. & got a money order for $13¹³⁄₁₀₀ to pay Wheatley of Montrose — Back to Studio & had tea at 6.30. At 8 to N.A. of D. council meeting — There until 10 — then to Studio — fixed fire — Got to bed at 11.
