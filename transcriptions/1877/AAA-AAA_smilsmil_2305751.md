## Left Page

### January, Friday 26, 1877

Charming day — got to
Studio about 10.15 after making
a call upon Mary in 44 8 —
made for awhile, when Abner
Colgate came in — he made
quite a long visit — then worked
on some W.C. accts — At 2.30
Misses Abbatt & Channing came
in & we went to the Union
League & saw pictures — got to
my lunch in 41 S. at 2 — back
to Studios at 3 to find that Misses
Beach & Bullard had been there.
The rest of P.M. absent in
writing letters etc — At 5.30 a
B. of C. meeting — at 6.15 to
Fanny K's — tea — Anna C. not
there — took F. to singing club —
there to N.A. At 10.20 went for
F. & took her home — glorious night.
Soon after 11 when I got to
41 S. — right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 27, 1877

*Po'keepsie.*

Still charming weather —
got to Studios about 10 & spent
the m'f in receiving calls —
writing letters & picking up
things preparatory to my trip
home to Po'keepsie — About
2 went to lunch — after that
packed bag — went to Mary's
there to R.R. Depot at 3.10 &
at 3.30 ff. for P. Charle in a
Drawing Room Car — I — only
an o'nary mortal — enjoyed
wonderful P.M. effects over the
frozen River & in the beautifull
clouded sky. Got home soon
after 6 — tea & then an evening
& family chat. Got to bed
about 10.30 in big north room
2d. story — all alone.
