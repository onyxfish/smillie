## Left Page

### August, Saturday 4, 1877

Delicious mf. Got up feeling weak & depressed after last night's tantrum. Had bkfp = at 1.30. About 9 went to foot of Main S. & to H.R.R.R. depot to enquire about the D.L.&W. R.R. to find out if we will be allowed by the grace of the Strikens to go to Montrose on Tues. ngt. Then to Mary's — there until after 11 — then home — W. Loomis called at the house at 12.30 — Dinner at 1 — At 3. Reached my study ground & painted until near 6 getting study all painted in — Then to tea. At 8 went to Loomis's with the Miss Goodrich sketches that he had left at the house — there until 9 — then to Mary's & joined Kate & Geo. There until a little after 10 — then home & right to bed — a glorious twilight with music that did not match in Eastman Park.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 5, 1877

Another delicious morning. Nearly 9 o'clk. when I had my bkfp. Went to Christ (?) church & heard a very good sermon from Rev. W. Jeisenfus on Christ driving the money changers from the Temple. After service went to Mary's & then home to dinner at 1. After that, a nap. Then fixed some funny things ready to pack my trunk & at 5.30 Geo. & I went out for an hour & enjoyed the view from the Bailey Place. Sea at 6.30 — Matt & Ed. there. Evf. all went to Mary's — there until 10 — then home at 10.45 when got to bed.
