## Left Page

### October, Thursday 25, 1877

Pleasant day. Got to shop about 9 with Geo. & Janitor
to help. Set to work carrying furniture from my room to
vacant rooms across the hall.
At 8.30 I went to Burgraff's — then
to Düring's — (Saw only Mary) & to
Renners — then to Deurys. By
way & 24' S. — & to Pomeroy's, who
again adjusted my truss. Then
to Studio & to the work described.
To lunch at 1 — back at 2.30
after calling at the Hoffman to
see Maud D — who was not in.
— Put my books into closet &
cases in other Room & my
place was cleared. Ready for
the carpenters — To tea at 6 —
about 7.30 Geo. & I called on
Maud D — with her until 8.
30 — then to Colman's & spent
the rest of the eveng. with them —
Corinne & Charley Smith sail
for England on Saturday to be
gone 7 years — Mr. & Mrs. Kirk-
ham & Edws Dunham there —
Back to 26' S. — & to bed at
10.30 —

## Right Page

### October, Friday 26, 1877

Bleak miserable day — got to
studio about 8.30 — Three car-
penters there at work upon the
laying of the floor. Charl came
soon after & we went to Schaad's
& 2. 3' S. — & arranged to get two
extra Phil. seats — walked back
to studio & the mtf was spent
between it & Colman's room
where I have arranged to do
some work. To lunch at 1 —
after that went down to Nicol,
Cowlishaw & Reid's — Saw Charley
Smith & lots of beautiful goods.
To studio at 3.30 the rest of p.
m. spent overlooking the new.
Louis Tiffany came in late & we
talked over art matters. To tea
at 6. drizzling Rain — at 8.
with Geo. went to Anna Cook's
I had a pleasant evening
there until 10 o'clk. — Then
to 26' S. — & to bed at 10.30
