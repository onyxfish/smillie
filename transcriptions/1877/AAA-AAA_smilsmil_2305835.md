## Left Page

### July, Thursday 19. 1877.

Rain commenced about 10 + fell heavily until noon — overcast + muggy all day — After 6 o'clp — did a little finishing work on Pen' Illus [illegible] then up town with Geo — went to Manny's + several other places — back to dinner at 12.30 — The afternoon was lost in a very lazy way — Tea about 6.30 — Every Mr. Loomis called + stayed until after 9, looking over Poems + Illustrations in progress. After that a walk up town — for a few minutes listened to Rehearsal of 22. Reg. Band — then home + to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 20. 1877.

Steamer "Providence" —
Stateroom 65.

Still muggy + overcast. After 6 o'clpt. packed valise + from 10 to 12 Read. then lunch + at 12.40 rode down to R.R. depot + took 1.15 Train to N.Y.; got there at 3.45 — Rain commenced falling — took cab + drove over to Renner's — then to Miss Suggett's — with latest pen illustrations — there until 7.20 — then through heavy Rain to St. Providence. Just of Murray St. 4 minutes to spare — In spite of the Rain enjoyed the sail up the East River — rand on deck — took tea about 7.30. Wakeman Halberton + wife on board — Went in grand Saloon — Concert — the selections + the performance alike indifferently good. After 10, walked on deck for about an hour, then turned in — first state room, lower tier on Starboard side — had no sleep.
