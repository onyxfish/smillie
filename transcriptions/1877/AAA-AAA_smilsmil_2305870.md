## Left Page

### September, Thursday 27, 1877

So warm as to be oppressive
& enervating. After b'kf't
got my oil sketching materials
together + at 9.30 went to the
Barnyard where Waters had
a cow waiting for me. I
labored faithfully until
1 o'clk. when I gave her a
rest. Dinner about 1.30 —
nearly 3 when again I got
to work — Painted until 4.30
when I got the study done.
Then into the milking yard
& worked with pencils until
6 — then in to tea — Mr. Wood
there — also Eugene & his wife
Two entertainments, one up
the other down stairs — later
in the evening Geo. D. came in
Euchre in Maud's Room.
11 o'clk. before I got to bed

## Right Page

### September, Friday 28, 1877

Still close + hot but somewhat
overcast. About 9 got to work
in barn yard with another
subject — not so docile as the
one of yesterday — managed
to fix her up & made some
progress — worked until 1 —
Dinner at 1.30 — Maud + the
Gent. went to Watkins at noon.
To work again at 2.30 — a
Thunder Storm came up about
3.30 + I had to go under the
shed. Managed to get my
study finished — In at 4.30
washed brushes + cleaned
generally — Thunder Storms
all about during the evening —
Tea at 6 — Maud + the Gent.
back at 6.30 — most of the
evening spent in Maud's
Room — To bed soon after
10.
