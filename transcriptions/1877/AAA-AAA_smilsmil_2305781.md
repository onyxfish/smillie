## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 27, 1877

Heavy Rain nearly all day. Got to studio about 10 o'clk my head feeling very heavy. Tried to work but did not accomplish anything. Then made some on W.C. Report. So lunch at 1 — back at 2.15 — and during the afternoon finished Report. To tea at 6.30 — at 8 — at 57 — W. 10%, adjourned Annual Meeting — for Reports & Elections W.C. Society — In spite of the horrible weather, 22 members turned out — I was Re-elected Pres. — At 11 walked up with J.G. Brown — got to bed a little before 12. Rain stopped wind blowing great guns.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 28, 1877

Cold & snow — winter come again — On my way to studio stopped at Miss Seegett's and saw her about my long Spanno[?] — got to studio about 10 o'clk cleared up things to get to work, but all my way was Taken up with Colman, Shurtleff & Magrath discussing W.C. affairs. To lunch at 1.30 back at 2.30. Dr. Bellows called to see [illegible] — had a very delightful call from him — then Gay & others came, so that all I accomplished was to work over a study of Ferguson's for Millard to see. To tea at 7 o'clk — went on sofa in Diering's basement — very cold, windy & snowy out of doors.
