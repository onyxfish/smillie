## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 16, 1877

overcast m.f., clearing later in the day — Got to studio at 9.30 — about 10 when I went to N.A. — worked theres until near 1 — then to dinner in 41" St. back at 2.30 — then at all sorts of work until near 5.30 — then with T.H. Smith to Arnold's & Constable & to Johnston's, shopping for something to cover lounges in N.A. galleries — then at 6 to Reitzel's — 330 E. 18. Engaged music for Mon. evef — At 7.15 to tea 41 St. — then at 8.30 to studio, wrote awhile & at 9.30 went to Private view of Artists Fund — Buzzed with "the fellows" & walked up 5' Ave with J.P. Townsend — To bed at 11.30. Thawing all the m.f. Freezing hard at night.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 17, 1877

Overcast again — In at studio for a moment, then to N.A. at 9.45 — there at all sorts of work until near 2 — at 12.30 Ch. Parsons & S.S. Conant came about Ill. for Harper's weekly — with them until I went to my lunch at 2.15 — got back to studio soon after 3 — get there worked over invitations until about 4.30, then on lounge for awhile to rest — at 5.30 went to W. Alex. Majors — 142 2d. Ave. about Darley's drawing. Was very cordially Rec'd — sat at dessert with them — almost 7 when I got away — to tea in 41 St. at 7.30 — at 8.40 at N.A. There until 10 — then to studio there until 11. Then to 41 St. & to bed about 11.30.
