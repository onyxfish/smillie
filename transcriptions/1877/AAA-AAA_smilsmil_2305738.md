## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 2, 1877

Brilliant day. Got up with something of a head-
ache. Got to studio men through mountains of
snow about 9.30 & to work on "The Truants" — worked
until 1.15 — then to lunch
back at 2.20 & worked as
long as I could see. At
4.30 the B². [illegible] met at
my studio & transacted
business connected with the
coming [illegible]. Especially
interested in the matter
of salesman. At 6 started
portrait Nicoll. To tea at
6.30 — at 8 called on Anna
Cook — at 9 at the studio,
made entries & at
10.30 to 41 8th — to bed
soon after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 3, 1877

Bright cold day — got
to studio at 9.30 — Soon after
Renner's man came and
commenced putting up
moulding up on which to
hang pictures — under great
difficulties I kept on with
my work on "The Truants"
until 1.15 — then to lunch
— back at 2.20 — could not
work owing to the confusion
so devoted myself to
hanging pictures & clearing
up — At 4.30, B². [illegible] Central
met in my room, adj[ourned]
from yesterday. At 6.30
to tea. Down to studio
soon after 8. Hung pictures
until 11 o'clk — then to
41.75 & to bed at 1.30.
