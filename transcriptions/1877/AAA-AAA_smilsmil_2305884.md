## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 23, 1877

at Miller's Home. Room 32
No. 37 W. 26th S-.

Superb day. Bkfp. before 8. then finished packing
trunk & at 10.30 went to
Matt's — walked with her
to her dress makers & in
the street met Kate & Emma
B. & Uncle M. Bid them all
good bye & went to Mam's.
Bid her & the Dr. good bye
& then home at 11.30. Dinner
at 12 & at 12.40 bid the last
of our good byes for the day
to Fath. & Mother — walked
to the depot & took 1.15 p.m
train to N.Y. — detained by a
broken down freight train
& did not get there until
about 4. — then to studio &
read letters — at 5 went
to Dr. Yale's — found him at
his press proving etchings — him
had some consultation with him
concerning Hernia — at 6.15 at the
Miller Home — Geo. & Mr. Bellows
at the dinner table — feel very
much dissatisfied with our
quarters & general appearance
of things about the house — Soon
after 8 went to Emma J's — not
in but saw her Mother — Julie &
Preston — back at 10 & to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 24, 1877

Charming day — bkfp. at 8 then
to studio — made a short call
on Mr. & Mrs. Coleman — then to car-
penter Deegan's — not in — then
down town — called first on Chapel
then some time — then on Will
" " " then rode up town
stopping at 615 to see Elastic
Trusses & then to Wash. Wilson's
then lunch at St. Denis Res't.
then to Pomeron's — cor. as ton R.
& B-way — after much talking
he fitted a truss to me & I paid
him $26.00 for it — then rode
up town — 4 o'clk when I got
to Deegan's — then to studio
wrote a letter to the Dr. and
to dinner at 6 — After that
in parlor with Bellows until
near 8 — Then out with Geo.
ment to St. Omar Hotel & enq'd
about Rooms — then to Kimball's
& there until 10 — then to 26th
St. & to bed at 10.30
