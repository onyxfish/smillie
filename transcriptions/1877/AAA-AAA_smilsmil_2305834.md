## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 17. 1877

Very close, muggy day. Thunderstorms commenced about 10.30 and all about north & south of us, they continued until after 4 o'clk. Some very heavy rain. After blfp. wrote a letter to Meta Adams. Rec'd. from Mrs. Spring & Maud D. About 11 got to work on Fire Fly ill. No. 3. Dinner at 12.30 at 2 again at work until 5 but did not get ill. quite done. Father, mother & Kate went to Walt's to tea, then to Mr. Parken's. Geo. & I took tea alone — then out for a walk — wrote a note to Mr. Spring & at 10 o'clk went to P.O. with it — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 18. 1877

Foggy, muggy, miserable morning — just Kate weather for grass & toad poles. Blfp. at 7.30. After blfp. to work on Fire Fly ill. — Dinner at 1 — then worked on ill. for Barnard and Taylor's "Lost Caryatid" — finished that about 4.30 — then went into town & returned to tea at 6.30 — at about 8.30 went to call on Carrie Tonda — there until 10 — then home & right to bed.
