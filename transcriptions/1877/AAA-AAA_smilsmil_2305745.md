In bed all day with

some dinner but had
to go back to bed — Up
at 9 o'clk & had a bowl
of gruel which I enjoyed
got to bed about midnight

## Right Page

### January, Monday 15, 1877

Snow in the m'f which
changed to Rain that
froze as it fell — Got to shop
soon after & "curbing" to
finish" the "mounts" — worked
until about 1 — then to
41st S. to dinner — back
at 2.15 & worked until
about 4.30 — then bundled
up my picture & sent it
over to the N.A. — followed
it — To tea at 6.45 —
back to N.A. about 8.30
& worked with Hanging
Com. until 10 — got to
41st S. & bed. Soon after
