## Left Page

### May, Monday 28, 1877

Delightful day, but growing warmer. Got to studio at
9 & tried to get to work on Colgate picture but between
carpenters & callers I did not do half an hours work
before I went to lunch at 1. — back at 2.30 & did a
little better during the p.m. painting the details of the
house & about it — did not get to tea until 7.30
& it was 9 o'clk. before I got down to Wash. Wilson's
where Renner & assistant had gone to hang my "Scrus
Race" — I think it looks exceedingly well on his
walls — there until 10.30 — walked up to 41st St. and
to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 29, 1877

Very fine day, but a little too warm — Up late & did not
get to studio until 9.20 —
Then to work on Colgate Pic
& painted on foliage about
the house. Miss Dunfee sent
me word that she had sold
my Study in N.Q. [illegible] for $100 —
Went there at 12.45 — to 41st St.
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 and
finished 2d painting on Colgate
No. 1 — Did not get to tea
until almost 8 o'clk — and
at 9 back to studio — worked
until 10.30 — then went into
Gilmore's garden for a few min-
utes & saw the flowers now &
to 41st St. & to bed soon after
11.
