## Left Page

### November, Friday 30, 1877

Clear, crisp & cold — Got to
Studio at 9. 30 & to work
on 2° painting on 12×18". By
"the still waters" — Painted
over distance & trees. Lunch
in Studios at about 1.30 —
Annie Cook called —
Painted as long as I could
see — then lounged by
my grate fire until near
6 before I took a short
walk — Then tea in Studio
& soon after 8. Geo. & I
went up to Annie Cook's
& spent the rest of the
evening there with her —
At 9.15 to 41. 8= & at
10.30, to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 1, 1877

Milder, but still wintry.
Got to Studios at 10 — & to work
on 12×18 — Painting over fore-
ground & back again over parts
that I worked upon yesterday
and that were not yet quite
dry. Lunch between 1 & 2 —
Emma J — came in for
a few minutes — I worked as
long as I could see. & got all
of my 2° painting in except
the cows. Went out & took
a walk & at 6 had tea in
the Studios — at 7.15 went for
Emma & at 7.30. with her & Grace
went to Fanny K's & then took
all three down to Steinways —
Thomas's 2° Symphony Concert —
not caring particularly for the
middle part of the programme
I ran over to the Century Club's
monthly meeting for half an hour —
back in time to hear Schumann's
Symphony — after the Concert the three
girls came up to my Studio & we
had a regular lark.) — I spread
all my dainties before them and
they ate while I fixed my fire.
Then took Emma & Grace home.
Then Fanny — just 12 when I got
to her house and 12.30 when
I got to my own bed.
