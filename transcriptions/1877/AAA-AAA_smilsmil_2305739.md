## Left Page

### January, Thursday 4, 1877

Bright, cold, windy day. After a restless night I got up with my eyes as though full of sand — Got to studio about 10 o'clk — totally unable to work. I bathed my eyes hung up pictures — fixed lunch at 2 o'clk — the P.M. passed as the morning except that I had a numbered callers. Hines, J. Haven Putnam — Miss Leggett, etc — To tea at [illegible] — spent on sofa, bathing eyes with warm milk — to bed at 10.30 [illegible — ~3 words] at [illegible] — there's 11 letters [illegible — ~2 words] at [illegible]

## Right Page

### January, Friday 5, 1877

Brilliant, beautiful day. Feel much better sorts when I got up — at 9.30 went to Dr. J's there an hour — gave me a lotion for my eyes — thence to studio — Mrs. Age Hawes had called. I spent what remained of the mfg in cleaning up — fixed lunch at 1 — at 2.15 went to Phil. rehearsal — at 4.30 ret'd to studio — at 6 went to K's — tea — and Foul Fanny Fauna C. to their singing club — then to studio & note to Mama Beach & to father, To H.S. at 11 & right to bed.
