## Left Page

### April, Saturday 28, 1877

Cloudy & Raw, threatening more.
Pitch's son after 8 — about 9.30
got Ben's horse & buggy & took
Wheatley over to Fink's farm
& after considerable tramping
picked out four fine maples
for him to transplant — then
back to town at 11 — gave final
orders & prepared to start
for W.Y. Dinner at 12. at 2.50
started in stage — train by
D.L.&W. at 2.30 — Rain began
falling about noon — Had for
a travelling companion an
insurance adjuster who
proved a very agreeable
fellow in whiling away the
dismal time until 9.40 at
night — Got to 4th S. at 10.30
& to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 29, 1877

Overcast — Myf went to
Dr. Hastings — He preached
from Matt. 28th Ch 20th V. but as
my head was very thick and
full of trees I did not hear
much of it — After service
walked down to studio — Read
letters, then to Mary's to dinner.
There until after 4 — then with
M° walked up to 59th S. & called
for a moment on Maud D — then
alone I went on to Will's — got
tea there & walked with him
down to 52nd S. — Called on Dr.
Male — not in — walked over to
41st S. & got umbrella — then to
47 E. 34th S. & called on Mrs. Dr.
[illegible] — there until near 10 then
to studio — quite a heavy thunder
storm — to 4th S. at 11 & to bed
at once. [illegible — ~4 words]
