## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 26, 1877

Still overcast in the morning & promising snow. After bkfp at 8 chase Ret'd to Nif, & I went with Emma to Matt's then left Fergusons letter for Dr. Carton at his office & got back home soon after 10 — Absence to work on small Salt Lake etching — my first experiment of biting, in a "bath" (soap dish) no stopping out but etching & biting progresses lively. At 12.20 went to town & bought turpentine — back & dinner at 2.45 — Finished my etching & biting about 3 o'c. Then fixed press & ink & until dark baking pts — at 5 o'c went up to Matt's with some cream — 2° hard — she was not in — back to tea — soon after 6 — evening in chatting & writing until 10 o'clk. then to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 27, 1877

Overcast until late in the afternoon — then cleared charmingly from the north. Bkfp — about 8.30 then to work laying grounds & at about 11 went out & had a short walk — in, & etching on little Salt Lake city plate, also on "farm lane" — Dinner at 12.30 after that, biting, burnishing & proving little plate until near dark — then walked down the South Road, around by Spring Side, up to Col. Hamilton, to Church St. & to Mary's after — a very charming walk — Met farther there & all Ret'd home with him & had tea. Most of the evng. writing notes to family K. & Annie C. — at 10 o'clk. walked up to P.O. — Back & to bed at 11.
