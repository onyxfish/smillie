## Left Page

### April, Monday 30, 1877

Pleasant day — Got to studio soon after 9 and undertook to start work on a B.A. drawing for A.B.N. Co — but my head was so heavy, in that aching, that I could do nothing — made notes to Wheatley + Saml. Sayre — Dr. Yale came in and we arranged for a meeting in my studio next Wed. evef. to organize an etching club — To 41 S. — to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45 — Vail came + so did Colgate but I excused myself — At 5 o'clk. last meeting for season of B.d of Control — all present but Bellows — I dine at 7.15 — After that to Mary's — At 8.45 to studio, wrote letter to W. J. Richards — at 9.50 to N.A., Esc t? — Miss Durfee home — Stopped in to see Dr. Mulford + got to 41 S. soon after 11 — Right to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 1, 1877

Delightful m'f. B'kf't at 7.30 — but after going to Mary's & Barber's did not get to studio until 9.45 — Then to work on A.B.N. Co's drawing & stuck to it until after 1, then to 41 S. to lunch (after a short call from Fanny [K.]) — back at 2.45 & worked until after 6 — getting drawing done & making my $40 in one day. The subject is a charming architectural one taken from a very good photo. The result is satisfactory to me. Maud D. came & soon after a shower of Rain that kept up, off to the rest of P.M. & evef — to tea at 7.20 — back to studio at 8.45 & wrote a letter to Father — to 41 S. & to bed at 10.30 —

Aunt H. just arr'd from South — went with Mary to Po. this P.M. — [illegible — ~2 lines]
