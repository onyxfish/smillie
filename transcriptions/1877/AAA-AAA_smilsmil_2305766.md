## Left Page

### February, Friday 23, 1877

Over cast but not dis agre-
able — Got to Studio & to work
on Study painting our foreground
finished it before noon —
then Wilson & young W. Earle
came in — I went with them
to see water colors at N. A. —
there until 1.45 — then to 41st
to lunch — back to Studio at
2.45 — What Remained of
the P.M. was fussed away —
At 6.30 went to Fanny K's —
took her & Anna C. to their club
then to Y.M.C.A. auction of the
Smieth pictures — then to N. A. &
at 10 back to Y.M.C.A. Then to
Studio — & to 41· St. soon of the
11 & Right to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 24, 1877

Horrible day, starting with
snow & sleet & keeping it up
with wind & rain — Got to
Studio at 9.30 & spent the day
in working over some water color
paintings belonging to W. Gurnee
(foreign) that have been badly
damaged — Wilson & Earle called
about 1 o'clk — Earle could buy
nothing at the Smieth sale, so he
went to N.A. & spent $11.00 then
I helped to sell him my "Scrub
Race" — went to N.A. at 1.30 — back
& had lunch about 2 in Studio —
at work on various things until
6 — then to tea — To N.A. at
9.30 after spending some time
at Studio — To 41· St. at 10.30
& to bed.
