## Left Page

### June, Saturday 9, 1877

Still hot & muggy with some light rain — Got to
studio about 9 & tried to do something on coal-mining
subject for A.B.N. Co. Got the outline finished all
but the figures — waiting for a model — To lunch at
1.30 — back at 2.45 — After some more work on mine
& doing all that I could without model, I set to
work on the confusion &
dirt in my studio, arranged
my closets & part of the confusion in engraving corner.
To tea at 7.45 — Evening
about 9 went to Mam's —
Bidwell & Palmer with Cha[rle]s
— with them until after 10
then to 41 S. & to bed at
11.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 10, 1877

Up at 7 — very muggy & cloudy.
B'kf. at 8 — at 8.40 went to Mama's
& with Charl went to Elevated
R.R. at 42nd S. & took 9 2 train to
Liberty S. & just caught 9.30
train out to Pamrapo — getting
there about 10 — Charley Gunther
met us with Miss [illegible] 9 — who
came out to meet a Mr. S[illegible]
who was on the train with us.
Several slight showers & two
heavy ones — we lounged — & at
had a dinner that kept us
at Tabee until 4 — leaden sky
& very much cooler — lounged
until 7 — then tea — glorious
sunset — Took 8.08 train in to
town & got up to 44th S. about
9.45 — there until about 10.15 —
then to 41st S. & to bed.
