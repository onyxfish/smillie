## Left Page

### May. Sunday 6. 1877

Very pleasant day. Muf went to D. Hastings and
heard him preach from 63rd
Psalm 8 v. After service
walked down to studio. made
a note + back to Mam's +
had dinner at 1.30 — About
3.30 again down to studio,
tried to write but was in
no humor for it, so at 6
went into Emma J's and
had tea there + visited until
10.15 — then to 41 83 and
to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 7. 1877

Pleasant day — Got to studio
about 8.30 — Everything in the
greatest hubbub + confusion,
— impossible to work owing
to alterations being made in the
building — masons & carpenters tearing
everything to pieces — Started to
make drawing on block f. "Scrub
Race" for Filmer — made but little
progress. To lunch at 1.15 — back
at 2.30 ( Vail waiting for his
(12") lesson — He got but little
attention altho' he was good enough
to accept it as a lesson — As Messrs
Abbatt & Jacobs had to move out of their
room, many of their things were
brought into mine, increasing the
confusion — Louis Tiffany came +
laid etching grounds for him —
To tea at 7.15 — Uncle Millie then
spent the evening with him and
with Renner, going over his bill
with him — To bed at 11.
