## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 30, 1877

Very hot day — Got to studio about 9.20 & to work on Colgate No. 2 — View of N.S. of house — Decoration Day festivities interfered with me as at 11 (after writing a note to Maud D.) I went out to see the procession — To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & painted over the house — To tea at 4.45 — on 3 of a Reading papers until 9 — then to Mam's — there until 10.30 — then to 74th St. and to bed at 11.

Very fine thunderstorm about 4.30 P.M.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 31, 1877

Still very hot — altho' tempered with breezes. Got to studio soon after 9 & again at Colgate No. 2. I don't work with enthusiasm & it is hard to keep up to the back. Miss Abbatt came in town — To lunch at 1 — back at 2 — & painted over the ground — very tired from being on my feet — To tea about 7.15 — a little before 9 went to 44th St. & called upon Miss Ripley at Mrs. Perkins's — met there Mr. many Rogers Griffits & miss Rogers — Sat upon front door steps until about 10.20 — then walked briskly down to 34th St. & back — then to bed at 11.
