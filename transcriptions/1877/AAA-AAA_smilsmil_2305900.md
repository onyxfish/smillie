## Left Page

### November, Saturday 24, 1877

Cold, windy & threatening —
Got to studio at 9.30 - Tried
to work on charcoal sketch
15×22 for Water Color drawing
but feeling under the weather
made but little progress.
At 12 stretched out on lounge
& had a nap. Lunch in
Studio soon after 1 — Near
3 Belle & Mary & Weedy came
in for a few minutes — I worked
on charcoal sketch — about 4.
30 - Nicolle came in & we progressed
pt. of W.C. Constitution for the
printer — Had tea soon after
6 — then the rain commenced.
At 8 o'clk. went to Phil Concert
1st of the season — Met there
Belle & Mary — Enjoyed the
concert immensely — At
10.30 took the girls to W. Scon-
dert's in 12th St — near 2nd av.
& then to 41st St — to bed
at 11.15 — Horrible Storm

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 25, 1877

Storm seems to have blown it-
self out — Dull, raw & windy —
B'kfst at 9.30 — At 10.30 at Studio
& as my head & eyes hurt, I
spent the mtg in my big chair.
At 12.45 walked with Shurtleff
up to Hotel Royal — there met Jo-
& Charl — went with S. to 127. 6 see
the rooms & at 1.30 back to Hotel
when all four of us dined together.
At 3 I reld to Studio — Being
headachy I spent the rest of
the p.m on lounge. W. & Mrs.
Jacobs & Nellie came in at dusk
& made a short call — Then
I wrote a letter to Miss Sterling
— at 7 took tea and at 7.45 Jo
& I went over & heard P[illegible]
Howard Crosby preach upon
how sin & death came into this
world followed by Redemption
at 9 back to Studio & wrote
a letter to Sackett. Then
to 41st St & to bed at 10.45
