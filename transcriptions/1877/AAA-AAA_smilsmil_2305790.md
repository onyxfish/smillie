## Left Page

### April, Saturday 14, 1877

Pleasant m'f. Got to studio at 9 o'clk. + finished outting 2° Colgate picture — A little before 12 went out + got some oysters — then down to foot of Charleston St. + met Anna Cook Rosalie Rueter — met Geo. C. + Valentine on Jersey side + took 1 o'clk. train for Fanwood. At Roselle, Miss Lil. Home + Cousin Miss Perry, joined us. Got to Fanwood at about 2 — walked back to the mt. about 3 m. — at 3 o'clk. came for lunch. After that started through the wood looking for Hepatica blossoms — found lots of them — also Blood Root — Dutchman's breeches + a few anemones — wandered towards station — delicious effects in the woods — part of party being late we missed 6 o'clk. train — then hired a conveyance to all. Rode to Roselle in the twilight getting him 20 m. ahead of train — Don trotted up to Miss House's. Swallowed a glass of wine — snatched a bit of bread and dog-trotted back just in time to catch the 8 train — got to Hy about 8.45. Rode up town in b'aue cars — took A.C. + Miss Butter to their homes — then to 4th at 9.45 — arranged flowers + got to bed at 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 15, 1877

Very mixed, delicious day — M'f went to Dr. Hastings — He was as vigorous as a December morning. After service, went to house, got Hepaticas, took half of them to Fanny K., the rest to Emma J. — was invited to dinner at 2 — To studio for a few minutes, then back to Sprug's to dinner. At about 4.30 Emma + I went up to the park + wandered about until after sundown — then out at 72° St. gate + rode down to 25th St. Got there a little before 8 — Took tea with Emma — W. H. [illegible] Ned Cook there — At 9.30 went to Studio. — very soon to 41st — + to bed about 10.30.
