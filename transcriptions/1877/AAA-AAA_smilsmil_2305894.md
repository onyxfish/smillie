## Left Page

### November, Monday 12, 1877

Mild & partially overcast. Got to studio soon after 9.
& to work planning small pictures about 12×18 — for
Anna Cook — She came in before noon — then Maud
D— came — then man from Renner's with mouldings
to put about ceiling — It was 3 o'clk before I got my lunch
then to Park Theatre & bought tickets for Thurs. Night — then
to studio & charcoaled a sketch from an Elmira study — "The
brook 13×16" — Got home in studio — about 8 o'clk. members
of Etching club began to come — At 8.30 I went to N.A.D. Council
meeting — back to studio at 9.15 — in or a dozen etchers
present & quite a number of etchings submitted — m—
buzzed until about 10.30 —
then to Hotel Royale and to bed shortly after 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 13, 1877

Charming day — At Studio soon after 9 — Stretched a canvas
temporarily 13×16 & commenced "the brook" for Anna C. — Maud
came in for a few minutes.
To lunch at 1.30 — on return found Mrs Jessup & Josie. had
a very pleasant visit — then tried to rub in a color effect
in 13×16 — Did not make much of it — Ge. went to funeral of
H.P. Gray — I was taken with a sudden & painful sort of
Colic — but was able to go to Kimball's with Ge. to tea —
After that, took Fanny to her club & then I went to studio.
Fussed there until 10 o'clk
got to Hotel Royal & to bed at 11 —
from 2 until 4.30 in great pain with a colic — no one was
disturbed but myself.
