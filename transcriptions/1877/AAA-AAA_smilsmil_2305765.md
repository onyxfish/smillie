## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 21, 1877

Charming day — got to studio at 9.15 — gave lesson to Miss Abbatt — then Nicoll came but finally got to work on "study" — Painted until after 12 — then wrote a letter to Bay and Taylor — To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — the rest of p.m. nearly lost with calls — Sanford Gifford & Mr. Canfield — Wash. Wilson — Harrison, the architect & others — Talking with Mr. Peter Lepuch until after 9 so that I did not get to tea until near 8 — after 9 when I got to N.a. To studio after 10 — wrote until 11 — then to 41st St. & right to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 22, 1877

Mild, beautiful day — At 9 a.m. met Wash. Wilson by apt. at Kurtz Gallery & looked over the Smith coll'n — then to N.a. — there until 10.30 — then to studio. & after some further delay, started work on "study" — To lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.30 — after again stopping at N.a. Finished working over group of trees — To tea at 6.45 — at studio at 8.30 — there until after 9 — then to N.a. — to 41st at 10.30 & to bed before 11.
