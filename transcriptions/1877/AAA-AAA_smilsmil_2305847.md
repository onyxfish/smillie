## Left Page

### August, Sunday 12, 1877

Cool, overcast day — was routed out of my bed at daylight this mng — bowels on a rampage — weakened me for the day — B'kf't at 8.30. After that went to Uncle M's & spent the mng with Uncle A. — we went to my lot and around it. Geo. & I took dinner at Uncle A's — there until near 4 — then to my bed & had a short nap — then to my shack, wrote a letter to father — to tea at 6 — soon after 7 went to P.O. with letters — thunder storm came up — were b'in to Jas's — there until near 10 — then on the hill & to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 13, 1877

Overcast & cool — got to studio about 9.30 & after some reading set palette & got to work on picture for Fau M. (2d day.) Painted over the sky & distance — Dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 — Uncle A. spent an hour or more with me. Painted over the middle distance — cleaned up at 5.30. Took axe & went out & chopped down a wild cherry tree & improved the landscape. Elisha M. came to studio & stayed until 6.30 — then to tea — Swell. went to Searle's & called on Betty (Mr. Miller) & the others — along to Jas's — The Post & wife there & Priscella & Poore — Company left at 10 — then Ben, Elisha, Fau & I had whist until 11 — then to bed.
