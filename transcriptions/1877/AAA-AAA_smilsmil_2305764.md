## Left Page

### February, Monday 19, 1877

Snow until near noon —
then blew off cold — went
clear & frosty — Got to studio
at 9 o'clk & to work painting
a B. N. Co. ing. "Gaucho" not
to an oblong 10½ × 14 in. I
have borrowed the painting
to finish definitly at the N.A.
as I cannot finish other work
as I wish to — To dinner at
1 o'clk — back at 2.15 & worked
as long as I could see, getting
all painted in — Mr. Colgate
came at 3 o'clk. & worked
until 5.30 Did not get to
tea until near 7. at 8.30
at Nicole's — worked at W.C. Con[mittee]
until 9.30 — then to N.A. —
to studio at 10.20 & to 41 [St.]
at 11 — to bed at 11.30.

Matt & Kate went to Fanny
K's this mg to stay over night

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 20, 1877

Charming day — bothered with
a headache — got to studio at
9.10, to work painting on study
of last Summer 10½ × 13½ — that
I propose to finish for N.A. — [illegible]
Mr. Rob. Goodman & Denoy
called & made quite a visit.
Have not seen him since I
was in Jersey 8 or 9 years ago.
To lunch at 1 — back at 2.15
& painted on study. Every
at 7.45 at 51 W. 10th S — a
Special W.C. meeting for
discussion of new constitution
— broke up at 10.30 — walked
up with J.G. Brown — In
at 11.15 and right to bed.

During gave his "Organ
Recital" at Odell's factory.
