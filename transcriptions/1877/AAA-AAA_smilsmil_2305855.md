## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 28, 1877

Very warm day — got to
studio before 9 — near to when
furnace men came & it
was 11 before they went away
finally getting the furnace
"set". Jas. Carmalt came —
what time was left to me, I
used in writing a letter to
Mary. Dinner at 1 — at
studio at 2.30 — Painted out
of doors on study of Beach from
mule. Elisha M. called —
Did not feel like work a
bit — Heavy clouds obscured
the sun & threatened a storm
but it passed to the north. —
After 6, carried into the studio
a lot of lumber to [illegible]
it. At 7.30 went down for Fant &
took him to "Pastor entertainment"
at Dan Sayre's — for benefit of the
Presbyterian Ch. — "Die en parte Francis"
was given & Tableaux & songs —
At 10.15 went home with Fant —
had three games of whist with
Fant, Ben & Elisha — At 11 — on
the hill & to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 29, 1877

Got up with slight headache
but pleasantly cool. About 30
overcast & about 10.30, shower
of rain. Feeling unlike
work at painting I went
into my cellar & put things
to rights there. — At 11.30 went
down-town & attended to some
errands. Dinner at 1.15 —
At 2.30, again at studio, more
showers coming — went all
around & finally, about 4.44
struck us. I Frans planted
a lot of Horse Chestnuts —
Finished my letter to
Mary — Tea at 7.30 — Lucy
at 8 called on Fanny Jessup-
not in — then about Jessups
— not in — & at 8.45 went
to Fant's — There until
a little after 10 — then
on the hill & to bed.
