## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 13, 1877

Overcast much of the day & considerably cooler — To
Manip's after h'lp — walked over to 9th Central depot with Chan
& then to Studio's at 9.35. To work on Colgate, No. 1 — going generally
over everything & improving in a way that gratified me.
To lunch at 1.15 — after that to Louis Tiffany's studio to see
about plates for Coleman — got to Studio's at 3.30 — Wash. Mils m
there — paid me first installment on "Jones Race" of $250.00 — worked
on Colgate & got it where I call it done — did not get to tea
until 8 o'clk. — Read Post— until 9.30 — then chatting with
Uncle Willie & Diming until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 14, 1877

After h'lp — to Manip & paid Chase, for father, $250 — Then
to studio at 9.30 — To work on "Western Herder", 2° painting —
Did not get my alley to suit me — too cold & wooley — To
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.15 —
Painted over foreground —
quite a shower between 4 & 5 — Gay & Bolmer
sought shelter — Painting stopped for awhile — To
tea at 7.30 — About 8.30 — to
Manip's — Wile & Joe there —
Some excitement over the
suicide of a Mr. Brock — an
employee of the A.B.N. Co —
To 41 S — at 10.30 & Right to bed.
