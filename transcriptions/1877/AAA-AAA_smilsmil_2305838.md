## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 25, 1877

Very warm day — Bkfst at 8 —
Soon after 10 again took S's
ponies & with Ge drove around
by the Fort (Adams) & ocean
drive. Returning through Belle-
vue Ave. Reaching the house
about 12.30 — Dinner at 2.30
Took 4.30 stage with Mr. J —
Emma, Grace & Ge. Rode to
southern end of Bellevue Ave
& then walked to Spouting Rock
& the cliffs beyond. Enjoyed
immensely an hour or more
of lounging & loitering on the
Rocks & watching the surf —
then back by 7 o'clk bus — Tea
at 8 — Later Emma, Ge. & I
went to Mrs. Bigelow's where
we sat upon a piazza overlooking
the water towards the west —
watched twilight fade from the
sky & the moon make ghosts
of the sails — Exquisite pictures.
Late at about 10 — then Emma, Grace
& I went out & took a moonlight
row for an hour. To bed soon
after 11 —

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 26, 1877

Warmer than yesterday — At 10
Rode in Beach bus over to Gibbs —
when Emma, Ge. & I walked
to W. T. Richards's — Spent a very
pleasant hour & a half there
looking over his recent work &
visiting with Mary Whitney & Clara
Kidfield — went to the beach and
left by 12 o'clk bus — Dinner soon
after 2 — By hurrying Emma Ge. &
I got to the foot of Mill St. where we
met Mr. & Mrs. W. T. Richards, W. & Mrs.
Grace Robbins, Mary W. Clara R. &
W. Warren. Went at once on board
a cat boat & sailed over to Conanicut
near Fort Dumplings — all of the
party but Mrs. R. & Clara R. strolled
across to a charming cove where we
found a charming place on the rocks
and camped for about an hour then
back along the cliffs to our landing
place on Bull Point — after sunset
Mrs. R. had cloth spread & all the
crockery, eatables & drinkcakes &c —
all sat down & had a glorious time
with twilight and ocean for attendants
upon our meal — a brisk sail back
in the fading light & at 8.30 were
landed at Smith's wharf — almost
at Preston Spruce's door — There, at
once Emma & I went to Mrs.
Bigelow's — were just in time to join
their party & again went out sailing —
the night was glorious & we had
some wonderful moonlight pictures —
had also some loafing in a calm —
in at about 11 — To bed at once.
