## Left Page

### January, Saturday 20, 1877

Fog, Rain & slush —
Did not get up until
near 9 — took things easy —
went to police & got detail
of men for Mon'd. ev'g — To
studio about 11 & worked
on Inmates until after 2.
Improving it 100 p. cent —
then to dinner in 41 S—
the Misses Mulford there —
back at 3 & worked for
an hour — there to N.A.
Hung up "Inmates" — every
thing looking well — To tea
at 6.30. At the N.A. soon
after 8, there until 12 —
many congratulations and
some grumblings — altogether
a success — walked up
with Miles C. & Charle —
To bed about 12.30.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 21, 1877

Overcast & much colder —
got up at 9 o'clc — took
things easy & at 11 went to
studio — at 1.30 to Mary's
to dinner — at 3.30 again
at studio — there until 6.30
then in to Emma D's to
tea — there until 8.30 —
again at studio for a few
minutes — About 9, called
on Miss Durfee — found there
Mr Beard & wife — there until
after 10 — then to 41 S. & to
bed at 10.45.
