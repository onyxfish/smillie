## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 10, 1877

Did not wake until 8.15 —
got to studio at 10 — and
to work on "The Truants" —
Did not get into the spirit
of the thing at all — to my
great disgust — To lunch
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — had
a call from Fanny K. & Miss
Howe — Did not get to tea
until 6.45 — there found
Mother there — Geo. came
in after — left them at 8 &
went to 25 W. 47 to call on
W. Chas. Smith to borrow a
Villegas for our exhibition. He
kept me until after 9 —
then back to 41 E S. — Snevo
again — at 9.30, with Geo.
took Mother to Mary's —
— back & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 11, 1877

Dull day — at 9.30 got to
N.A.D. — there until 11
with B. & C. passing upon
the pictures — then to studio
& to work on "The Truants" —
Mr. J. J. Williams came in.
At 1.30 had my lunch in
the studio — after that again
to work — Geo. & D. [illegible]
came in — Helmuth also —
but he did not come — at
4.45 set to work pasting
paper over the backs of my
drawings — To tea at 7.30 —
at 8.30 went to Nicoll's and
found him horribly behind-
hand with his work — at 9.15
Y.M.C.A. engaged two young
men to help him — then
to studio — there writing
until 11 — then to
41, 8 — & to bed at 11.30
