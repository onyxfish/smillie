## Left Page

### June, Monday 25, 1877

Pleasant day — but still growing warmer — Got to studio soon after 9 & put finishing touches on "foot & skalt" & took it down to A.B.N. Co. Got paid for it — Stopped at Flumers no money there — there at Wash. Wilson's — there at Dawsey's & ordered a suit of summer clothes — 2.30 when I got to 41st St to lunch — To studio at 3.30 Began packing up things. To tea soon after 7 — Mrs. Brown (Mary Hartly) and sisters Mary & Walt spent the evey. by invitation at Mrs. During's — Refreshments at 10.30 — about 11 took Mary & Walt home — then back & to bed about 1.34 very hot night.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 26, 1877

Sweltering hot day. Got to studio soon after 9 and spent the m'f sorting out such traps as I will take with me. To lunch abt — back at 2.30 — Spent p.m. in doing my traps up into small p'kgs. To tea a little after 4 — Approaching storm. — at 8 went to Mary's from there to studio — very pleasant shower between 9 & 10. Packed up the last of my traps and to 41st St at 10.30.
