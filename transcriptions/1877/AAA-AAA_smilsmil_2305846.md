## Left Page

### August, Friday 10, 1877

Very pleasant mng. B[illegible] at
4.30 — got to studio soon after 9.
Did a little mntng then went
out to fair ground Rocks and
until after 12 o'clk. worked on
drawing (for oil study) of some
of the Rocks — Then down town
to P.O. & got letters — To dinner
abt — After that again to studio
& painted in sky on old canvas
24×44 "on the Boquet, Elizabeth-
town" — Trouble with my bowels
compelled me to stop before
5 o'clk — Tea abt 5.30 — all the
evng very much out of sorts.
Provoking showers came very
near us — about 9 p.m. a little
dash & during the night, quite
a shower — To bed at 9.45

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 11, 1877

Delicious day — To studio at 9
& then to fair ground Rocks. I
Painted until after 12, much
inconvenienced by the wind.
Also quite cool — in my studio
at 10 a.m., 64°. Dinner abt 1 —
Soon after 2, again at studio.
Painted in mountains "on the
Boquet" — 24×44. Cleaned
up at 5 o'clk, wandered around
until 6.30 — then to tea. Evng
walked down home with fanny
Jessup — then in to Mrs Jessup's
for a few minutes — met Alice
[illegible], went home with her —
then to the P.O. then to fan M's.
Priscilla & her husband came
at 9 o'clk. On the hill & to
bed at 10.30 — 4 nice [illegible]
& Uncle C — are? this evng.
