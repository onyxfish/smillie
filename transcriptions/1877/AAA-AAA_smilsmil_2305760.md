## Left Page

### February, Sunday 11, 1877

Very pleasant day — my went to D. Hastings & heard him preach from "I am the way, the truth & the light" —
walked down to studio — then to A Kerkhanis 67 m, 50¢. where at 2 o'clk. had dinner — M st m?
Colman there — At 4.15 walked down to studio — at 6.45 up to Mam's — 44 S. then to Ken. mydlpp?
64 E, 54 S. where Matt & Kate were
Charle here — walked down to
Hepworths — after service walked down to studio & at 11 up to
41 S. & to bed having walked about 9 or 10 miles.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 12, 1877

Peasant day with a storm of wind & rain between 5 & 6.
P.M. ev'ry clear & very cold —
got to studio before 9 o'clk. —
nice there — I got to work on Bridal Veil Fall Re-painting.
To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.45
& continued painting as long as I could see — To tea at
6.45 — at 8 went to Mam's & saw father, who came down this way. Got to
N.A. soon after 9 — Had an offer of $125.00 for my cattle
— at 10.20 to studio, cleaned palette — & to 44 S. at 11.10
& right to bed.
