## Left Page

### June, Monday 11, 1877

Cool overcast m'f. Up at 7.30 — on Ph'p'. Fable found telegram from Miss C. saying that I was expected on 9 10 train, so I took it, getting to Riverdale about 9.45 — went right to the house & spent the m'g sketching — found that I would have to make Radical changes on No. 2 (The view from the north.) Ret. to NY. by 12 48 train — jumped at 42. S. & 11 ave. & got to lunch at 1.30 — To studio at 2.30 did some work on No. 2 preparatory to a re-painting to-morrow. Got a scare from men, ladders & paint-pots falling in the hall — no one seriously hurt. To tea at 7.30 — Until after 9 Reading "Post" — Then listening to Dining on piano & to bed a little before 11.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 12, 1877

Overcast most of the day &, quiet cool. After Ph'p'. went to Mary's — then to studio at 9.20 — To work on Colgate No. 2 & painted out & in according to notes & sketches that I was yesterday m'f. Miss Abbatt came in town & brought me a basket of magnificent Straw- berries — To lunch at 1.15 — back at 2.15 & painted until after 6 — To tea at 7.30 — Uncle Willie came in at 8 — At 8.30 I called on Denis Tiffany — not in — Then to studio & wrote until 10. Then to 41. S. & to bed.
