## Left Page

### July, Saturday 21. 1877

At Preston B.S. Spring's —
cor. Washington & Poplar S.
At Fall River.

Up at 5. Had a cup of coffee & some bread on board boat & at 6 o'clk. off by Old Colony R.R. to Newport — Providence where we arr'd. about 6.35 — then walked to Mr. S's — getting there before 7 — Mr. Ireland up to greet us. I went out & walked about for nearly an hour — then back & had bkf't. The mf't proved close & overcast, so I was spent about the house which is very quaint & picturesque, close to the water & overlooking the bay, or harbor, or river or whatever it is. Dinner about 2. Spitting showers all the P.M. with fog variations. After 4. Mr. & Mrs. Ned Cook, Emma & Gee & I started out & walked through some of the most fashionable streets. After 6 when we Ret'd. at the house found Mr. White, a friend of Ned C's from Phila — Tea — Evn'g in Sitting Room in general chatting until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 22. 1877

Very close & hot — Bkf't. near 9 — Preston Spring arr'd. from N.Y. on boat at 2.30 A.M. About 10 several of us with books & umbrellas went to a Rock on Mr. Meyer's place on the water's edge — Emma & I found seats in a bit of cob docking where the m'f't was spent, she with her botanical book pulling to pieces a little flower of the [illegible] family while I held an umbrella, looked out over the water, sniffed the salt air, fragrant with sea weed, fish & lobsters — accepting the fact as a guarantee of health — giving properties & dreamily yielding to the ocean wave. — Stayed for some time with a sea anemone. — To dinner at 2 — after dinner — Mr. & Mrs. Ned C. — Mr. White. Emma & I. went in a flat boat over to the Dumpling Fort — a very charming sail — with ashore & clambered to the fort. Back about of sea — After that on the flat Roof of the house & about part of an hour there — then on Piazza until near 10 o'clk. Ned C. invited me to go on a 4 or 5 day's cruise with him, his wife & Mr. White. I could not do it as for that time I was a guest of Mrs. Spring — Go to bed about 10.30.
