## Left Page

### December, Friday 14, 1877

Charming day — again late
9.45 when I got to Studio.
Spent the day painting &
mussing over the cows in
12×18 " By still waters" — Took
a short walk at 1.30. Took
lunch in Studio — Painted
as long as I could see —
Deluoce called — so did
Mr. Abbatt & daughter — &
Bellows & Nicoll — Took a
walk at 6 — Tea at 7.15 —
Eveff again in Studio
hanging & arranging
pictures until nearly 11,
then to 41 St. & to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 15, 1877

Still mild, pleasant
weather — Got to Studio
at 9.30 & spent much of
the mf making one of my
red cows a black one —
then worked awhile on
a little sketch painted
some time ago" a bit on
the Tuolumne River — Cal".
A short walk & lunch at
2 — then painted as long
as I could see on 6×10 "A
Shady Spot" — walked at
6 o'clk — Tea near 8 & at
9, with Gee went to "the
Century" — a pleasant
evening until near
midnight — then to
41 St. & to bed at
12.30.
