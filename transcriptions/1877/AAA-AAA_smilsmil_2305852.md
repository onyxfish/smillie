## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 22, 1877

Hot mtf but found it very comfortable under the trees on the fair g— where I worked until 12.30 on study of Beeches. My head troubled me very much — Dinner at 1 then on my bed until nearly 4 when I went out & rode with ge—. Did not take any tea as my headache continued to grow worse — Eve'g went to Gus Crandall's — Mother very sick, could not see her. At 9.20 went to fan's — there until 10 — glorious moonlight — met walking about alone — met the Searles on their way home from Wm Jessup's — walked home with Josie B — on porch until 10.45 — then off again walking about alone — turned in at 11.30

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 23, 1877

Not able to get up in the mtf owing to my headache — in bed until 1 ock. Then up & had a cup of beef tea & some rice — At 2 went up to studio to see how Wheatley was getting on with my cellar — Paid him $10 as he was just finishing up — At 4 went out & rode again with ge—. In at 6.30 — then tea — went down town at 8.30 to see Chas. Sayre — then to fan's — there until 10 — then on the hill & to bed at 10.30. Heavy Rain during the night.
