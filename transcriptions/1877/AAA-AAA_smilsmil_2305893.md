## Left Page

### November, Saturday 10, 1877

Beastly day. Raw & wet —
Blk p — about 8 — to studio soon
after & the whole m'f spent
in fixing up — To lunch at
1 o'clk. & in the afternoon
got out my work when W. W.
Hturds came in & made a
long visit — after he went
I blocked out on a canvas
about 22×40 a composition
based on study from Whiteface
overlooking Lake Placid &
away to the mountains south.
It is a subject that W. J. T.
Williams wishes me to try —
Shortly after 6 had tea in
the studio. Rain pouring on
the sky light —. At 8 got a — went
in to the Springs — I wrote a
letter to Nicoll & at 9 o'clk. joined
him — played whist with Busti[n]
against Julie & Philip Wilson —
imma played a little. Geo.
& at 10.30 we retired carrying
between us a glass dish presented
by Mr. Ireland. To the studio
to see after my fire — then to
the Hotel Royal & to bed at
11.15 — Rain ceased — Raw
& windy.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 11, 1877

Mean. Raw sort of overcast
day — Up late — Blk p — about to
a short walk & then to church.
Heard D' Hastings preach from
1 Cor. 7 ch. 19 v. — a warning
against new forms. After
service walked down to studio
wrote a letter to father & then
back to Hotel — Dinner at 2.30
near H where I walked to
57 S. & called on Maud D — out.
Then on up to Will's — Geo. there
me stayed to tea — Walked as
far as 54 S. with Will — then in
to see Maud — here until 8.15
then walked down to 42 S., in
to Hastings's — met Geo. then to
Hotel — then walked to studio
& fixed fire at 9.30 — on my
way out met B [illegible] — 10.30
before I left the building &
almost 11 where I got to bed
