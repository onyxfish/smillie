## Left Page

### July, Saturday 7, 1877

Glorious mtf. after bkfst — went out & got sketch for a Whittier ill — in at 10 —
& worked the rest of the mtf on "Heavy boots" — a another illustration for
the same Poem. — Dinnr abt 12.30 — Again abt work on "Stately towns" until after 4, when, my head hurting me, I went out with Geo & lounged under a tree on the Bailey hill.
Tea soon after 6 — Music in the park from 7 to 8 —
enjoyed general effect from father's windows —
matt[e]d at house — at 9 walked up town with them — back & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 8, 1877

Pleasant, breezy mtf, but rather warm walking. Up late. After bkfst — mate until 10.20, then with Geo walked to Episc. Ch. on Madison Sq. Heard a very good sermon — on being armed while we tried at work — from the story of the Jews rebuilding the walls of Jerusalem. On our way back stopped at Mary's. To dinner abt 1 — After that a nap — at 4 o'clk. again began to mate. Tea at 6.30 — Evg, all went out so I stayed with Mother &
finished a long letter to Miss Abbatt. About 10 I went out & took a walk — then in & to bed abt 10.30.
