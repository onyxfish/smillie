## Left Page

### October, Saturday 13, 1877

Clear, cold & windy — a beautiful fall day — After
bkfst. walked up town with
father. Spent part of the
mft with pencil making
up some cow-sketches from
nature. Dinner at 12.30.
again at work, planning
some small sketches for
freehand copperplate etchy.
About 4 o clk. Father, Geo.
& I walked down to & over a
part of the "old farm" — Back
to tea before 6 — Kate Ret?
from N.Y. about 6.30 — Evng.
spent in chat & reading
astronomy — to bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 14, 1877

Charming day. Had a good
wash & scrub & while at it
made the alarming discovery
of what seemed a hernial
protrusion above the right
groin — After bkfst. went up &
saw Dr. [illegible] — He examined
me & pronounced my fears
correct. I have not — do not
suffer the least pain or discomfort
— it was altogether accidental
that I discovered the swelling
but I feel completely prostrated
by the knowledge of this new
weakness, to be added to my
old ones — went to Christ's Chr.
& heard Rev. Mr. Feigenquis —
After service to dinner at home
at 1 — Afternoon note to J. T. Wms
& lunch — & Z. D. Reeks — About
5 walked up to P.O. with letters —
Tea at 6 — Matt & Ed. with us —
About 9 I went home with
them & there met Father &
together we Ret? at about
9.30 — got to bed soon after
10 —
