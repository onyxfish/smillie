## Left Page

### March, Sunday 11, 1877

Very pleasant day — M[r]f. went to D. Hastings & heard him preach from Romans 11 ch 36 v. After service walked down to studio — wrote a note to Henry Tyler & sent him $150.00. Then to Mary's to dinner at 1.30 — There until near 4 — to studio at 4.15 and more. At 6 o'cl went to Nellie J's in 28 S[t] — Tea at 6.30 — Chatting until 8.30 — Then again to studio & wrote until 10. Then to 41. S. & to bed at about 10.30 [illegible — ~8 lines]

## Right Page

### March, Monday 12, 1877

Disagreeable day. Rain commencing about 4 o'clk — got to studio at 9 o'clk & spent the m[orning] in making two pen sketches of "Gaucho" for M[a]g. ill. car. To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — At 3 after Colgate came & the rest of the p.m. I divided between him & outlining cows in "The Saco" W.C. picture. To tea at 6.30 — Eve[ning] at 8 went to Mary's — feeling very stupid I spent the evening on chaise lounge — To 41. S. & to bed before 11.
