## Left Page

### September, Monday 17, 1877

mild, overcast mtf, at studio until about 10 o'clk. Then to old maple back of Fairground with Peucie & Baker & spent the rest of the mtf there. dreaming & sketching — the Sep. quiet of the woods — the grey day with occasional glints of sunlight making the hours very short & delightful. Dinner at 1. At 2.30 Geo. & J started with horse & buggy for Jas. Carmalts — Elisha M. is to celebrate his crystal mdday to-night — J. M., Calp. Reid, Mrs. grannis & Emma Smith followed. When we had been about an hour on the road it began to Rain & before we got to Carmalts it poured heavily, the wind driving it into our faces. We got to our journeys end a little before 5 — built in front of a wood fire soon lost out of doors — tea at 4.30. Every, Miss Morris joined the party — a large number of children enjoyed a Romp — we had chatting & Whist. Bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 18, 1877

Clear, crisp mtf, wind from the north. Slefo — about 8 and then getting woodbine & Bitter sweet roots. About 10.30 got started for Montrose, but stopped at new church needing to be dedicated this mtf. Finally away at 11 & after a delightful drive got in to M. abt. 20 and had dinner — Priscilla P— and at Mrs. Sayre's — At 3 went to studio & worked for some time packing box with pictures, then took wheelbarrow, shovel & hoe & went to the quarry & got a barrow full of leaf mould, then planted vines on the So. side of Studio's — Tea at 6.30. About 8.45 Priscilla, Geo. & t went to Jarvis — there until 10.30 — To bed at 11.
