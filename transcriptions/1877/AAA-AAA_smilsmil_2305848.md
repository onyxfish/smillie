## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 14, 1877

overcast with. Heavy Rain
at 1.30 — light showers during
the p.m. Got to studio at
9.30 — wrote a letter to Mary
then to work on Fan's picture
Elisha M. came in & spent
most of the m/f. with me, so
that I made little progress
with my work. After 12, I
went with axe & saw and
cut down a couple of dead
hemlocks & trimmed many
branches. Dinner at 1.15.
During the Rain lay down
& took a nap. Then to studio
& finished picture. Cleaned
up shortly after 5.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 15, 1877

overcast with brisk Rain about
8 o'clk. Got to studio at 9.30
& to work on Boquet River
(24×44) & painted foliage
on large central oak tree —
At 12 went to my grove
with saw & worked at trimming
trees until 1, then to dinner
To studio again at 3 & worked
until 5 — cleaned up —
Storms to southwest of us —
superb cloud effects. Light
Rain about 6 o'clk. At 8
went to Mr. Coolson's & called
on Mrs. C. & Mrs. Gilleman. At 9
went to Fan's — Josie B. and
Vic. D. there — Whist until
10.15 — took Josie B. home.
Moon on the hill — after 11
when I got to bed.
