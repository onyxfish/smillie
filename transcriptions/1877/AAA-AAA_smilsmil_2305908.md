## Left Page

### December, Saturday 8, 1877

Pleasant day — near 10 when I got to studio — A.C.C. had been there & left a dainty dish for our larder — Spent the mf. house cleaning — Mr. J. T. Williams called — Then a.c.c. — she expressed her entire satisfaction with the picture painted for Osia Ireland. Lunch at 2 o'cl. afternoon — fussed with frames & fixing up etchings — out and had a walk at 6 — Tea at 6.30 — at 8 Yale, Ino, Gifford & Baldwin came & until 10 we discussed the Constitution that I drew up for Etching Club — Then Giff. & I went to Century Club — had a very pleasant time there until near 12 — Met Judge Daly for the first time — To 41. St. & bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 9, 1877

Bleak & windy — up late. Bkft. about 10 — a short walk — then to Dr. Hastings & heard him on Joy — he was as usual, devout — After service, walked with Geo down to studio — there until near 2 — then to Hotel Royal & near 3, had dinner. Then back to studio — read awhile — took a nap — & at 7 went for Imma J. who came with me to the studio to see painting for Oscar — took her home at 8 & back to studio — wrote a letter to father — got to 41. St. about 11 & right to bed.
