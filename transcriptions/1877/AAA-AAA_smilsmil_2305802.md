## Left Page

### May, Thursday 10, 1877

Overcast day with light Rain
at times. After bkfst. went to Mary's
& with Geo. walked down to studio
About 9.45 went to Union League
there with Nicole until 2 o'clk
hanging Water Colors — then to H.S.
had a good dinner at 2.45
Got to studio about 4 — every
thing in & about the building
still in horrible confusion —
did a little miting. To
dinner at 6.30 at Anna Cook's
Fanny K. there — General chat
until about 9 — then to H.S.
and got my umbrella as
there was a drizzling Rain
& at 9.30 to Union League
Club — a very pleasant
gathering — at little supper
to the Water Colorists given by
the Art Com. to H.S. at 11
& to bed at 11.30. [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### May, Friday 11, 1877

Still cloudy & quite cool —
on my way to studio stopped
at Sturtevant H.o. & called on
Fanny M. Mrs. Jas. Carnalt & Connell
Found St. M. & Geo. there. Got to
studio a little before 10 — men
set right to work & my chimney
breast was soon torn down
making a fearful mess. To
lunch at 1 — back at 2 & bossed
the job until 6 — to tea at 7 —
about 8.30 at Abbey & Reinhart's
Studio, 58 E. 13 S. — informal
meeting of the etching club —
there until about 10.45 —
walked up town with Dr.
Yale & got to bed about 11.30
