## Left Page

### October, Sunday 7, 1877

Beautiful day — Spent
the mrf in wandering about
+ in Maud's room — Did a
little writing, a little
gathering of my things
together and some walk-
ing — Dinner about 1.30
Afternoon, wrote to Belle
Tweedie — most of the time
was loafed away. Aleck
Harriey + Eugene + family
came up. Riley Reed
came + took me out
riding — he made his
horses spin for about
two hours — Tea at 6.30 —
Rest in library — all the
family were there, the
new madame having gone
to church. To bed about
10.30

## Right Page

### October, Monday 8, 1877

*Erie R.R. Sleeper Jas. Fiske Jun.*
*Section 10.*

Overcast, windy mrf. Up
about 8 — wrote note to Charl,
did some packing, visiting
+ walking — Mr. Eugene spent
most of the mrf with us —
Dinner at 1.30 — Rain and
wind made a dark after-
noon. Things all packed
before 5 o'clk — Tea at 6.30.
Eugene, wife + daughter came
up through the storm to bid
us goodbye. Parting speeches
were made by all + at 8.20 we
got off in a driving heavy storm
train late — left Elvira at
about 9.30 + soon after 10
had our bed made up +
got into it — Rain roared
on the roof — the great car
thundered along, I bounced
about, shivered and got
little sleep.
