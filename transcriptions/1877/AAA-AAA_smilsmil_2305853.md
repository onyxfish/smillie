## Left Page

### August, Friday 24. 1877.

Threatening more rain but we did not get it.
About 9 went to Studio to work on Bosquet Pic
24×44 — Painting in R.h. lower corner from
study of Rocks that I have under way — am
very much pleased with my progress & have strong
hopes that I will yet make a good picture (for me) out
of the old condemned one.
Dinner abt — at 2.30 again at Studio — very hot — thunderstorms all around — at 5.30 got part of one — finishing up morning's work. Mrs.
Wm Muelford, Mr. Cook & Mr.
Dreiman
— Called. Then a shower —
Tea at 6.30. Wef called on Allie
Stroud — not in — There on W. Sm.
(Dominic) Miller — met Mrs. D. Sayre
there — abt took her to Faris's — There
until 10.30 — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 25. 1877.

Heavy Rain early in the
mf. Got to Studio abt 10 to work on 24×44 — Painted in
Beech Trunk & Foliage — To
dinner at 1 oclt — Back
at 2.30 & all afternoon
lost in fussing with
Chaps Sayre's men who
were putting in my furnace.
Fan & Mrs. Gauymbau called
about 5 — at 6.30 to Tea —
The weather had cleared
gloriously. Wef went
to Gus Crandall's — from there
took her home — 9.30 — there
— stayed until 10.30, then
on the hill & to bed at 11.
