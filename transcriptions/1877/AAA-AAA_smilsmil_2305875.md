## Left Page

### October, Friday 5, 1877

Windy, cloudy & chilly — at work after blkfst — on Concepcion
R.R. depot & fussed over it until dinner. Fine when
finally I pronounced it done.
Dinner soon after one. Before
3 Aharnes Foak drawing in
To [illegible] Office. I traced &
retraced Concepcion Fountain
for Q. B. N. Co — Then went
out & walked for awhile.
Tea at 6.30 & at 7.30 Ella,
Maud, Jack, Jee & I rode
to Sugges's where we spent
the even. I played whist
with Mr. Smiths (Lewis M.) ag.
Mr. J.M.S. & Ella with success.
Oysters & champagne after 10.
All back to Willow Brook &
to bed just at midnight.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 6, 1877

Beautiful mrg. But breezy
& cool — after blkfst — set palette
& commenced painting on a
white cow that I outlined on
Tues. last. She was in the barn
yard & I went to look at her
now & then — At 12.30 went out
& walked until 1.15 — then had
dinner — At 2.30 went out to
barnyard with my colors and
worked there until 4.30 — It
was quite cold, wind blowing
& sky overcast — from 5 to 6 I
walked briskly to get my blood
circulating freely again. Tea at
6.30 — Every Sugge & wife came
up — time divided between
Maud's Room, the library & the
dining Room until about 10.30
when I went to bed.
