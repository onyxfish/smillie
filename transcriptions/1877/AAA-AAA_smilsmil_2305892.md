## Left Page

### November, Thursday 8, 1877

Dismal wet with rain about 3 oclk. & a [illegible],
driving storm at night.
Got to studio about 9.30 +
found [illegible] at work. putting
in my grate — fussed with
them & moving my furniture
all the m[orning] & besides had
several calls — It was 3 oclk
before the great men got away
& about 3.30 I walked over
to Bdway with Miss Woodward
went to Overton's & had a bowl
of clam soup — Then to Y.M.C.
A & called on Tiffany & Robbins
to meet Claghorn at the 5th ave
tonight — Saw also Thompson
& Satterlee — Back to studio
after 4 & invited Coleman &
Bellows for to-night — Had tea
in studio for first time — Sent
out notices for Etching Club
meeting for next mond — every
& at 8.20 went with Bellows
to 5th Ave. Hotel — Parlor No. 1 where
were Coleman, Robbins, Tiffany,
[illegible] Snedecor & (Swiss) [illegible]
Knowdeer & Co. Claghorn was
working up a Water Color Show
to come off next month in the Penn.
a. fine arts — as a general sort
of meeting, in spite of Champ. &c.
& a horrible rain went back to C's
for geo & at 10.30 we got to Hotel Roy[al]
& right to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 9, 1877

Very nice, delicious day after
a rain that did not cease
until after 9. a.m. After bkfst. went
to see Jane Hushelwood & gave
warning at the Hotel that we
might give up our Room — Then
to studio — The unsery day
made we feel very weak &
tired. So, much of the m[orning] I
spent in writing letters, among
others to Oscar Freeland — At
1.45 went to lunch — then back
took a short nap & more writing
& fussing in the confusions of my
studio — Every good sub. my
supper of wheat, peaches cream
Vienna Rolls & tea & enjoyed
it very much. A little before
8 rode up to 57th St. with Geo. and
called at Bishop's to see Maud &
Jack — they had gone to Opera with
Mr & Mrs. Graceking — we then walked
down the ave. & stopped at Charl's
house — he was out — there on to Dr.
Yale's — 2 2.47 5 — He was in and
we spent the even there — had a
etching — he on a big zinc plate.
I on a little copper one, a portrait
[illegible] as he sat at work — very bad —
about 11 oclk we started for the
Hotel Royal & got to bed about 11.30.
& got ours.
