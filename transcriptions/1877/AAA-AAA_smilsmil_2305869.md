## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 25, 1877

*Elmira. At Genl. Dixon's*

Beautiful day. Rkfp. at 7.30. Good byes said & at 8.30 got away. W. Bligh & family went to Towanda with us. From below D ushore to Munroe. 13 miles, we ran without an engine, in 26 m. average grade 50 ft. to the mile highest 85 ft. I sat on the front platform & found it very exhilarating. Part of the way was through the woods which were still full of the cool, sweet mist — Reached Towanda at 10.30 caught 10.05 train & got to Elmira at 12.40. Maud at the depot waiting for us — drove right to Willowbrook — washed up & had dinner at 2. Introduced to Mad. Dixon — Afternoon got out my sketching traps & out with pencils & paper went after the cows — Tea at 6.30 Every at Genl. D's request played Whist — with Mad D against him & Ella — got badly beaten — Then retired to Maud's Room & played Euchre with her with no better success — finally to bed soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 26, 1877

Very warm day — Rkfp. about 8. After that, Ella, Maud, Genl. & I rode over to the Cemetery & then a little farther & back before 11 — then I took pencils & paper & went after the cows. Dinner at 1 — Again after the cows, following them into the milking yard at 3.30 where I got some fair sketches & worked until 6 o'clk then in to tea — Every about 8, Ella, Maud, Genl. & I went by invitation to Jut. Reed's — Mrs. Grannis, Falt & Alex. Dixon there — cards — Supper at 10. home about 11 & to bed at 11.30.
