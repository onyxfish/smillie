## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 13, 1877

Rain in the mist, clearing later in the afternoon. Got to studio before 9 o'clk. Miss Abbatt posed for me & I painted a sketch of a figure. To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & painted figure of mist. W. Darsey called. To tea at 7 and about 9 got to W. Poet Gordon's — No. 4 E. 38th. Reception given to artists etc — Sulpers house & a gay stag party. I left a little before 11 when they went in to supper. Got to bed very soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 14, 1877

Miserable day — Up with a headache against which I fought all day long. Got to studio about 10, unable to work so I fussed about. Miss Boyd called. To lunch at 1 — my way back called on Dalton & got to studio about 3 o'clk. Feeling better later in the afternoon, charcoaled sketch of "Cathedral Rocks & Spires, Yosemite" — 13×17. Miss Durfee came in late — To tea at 7 — After that went to Pennie's shop — there called on Miss Sarah H. Heggelt about a sketch or two I saw to make for her & got to studio about 9 o'clk. Worked at "cleaning up" for an hour — to 41 S. at 10.30 & to bed at 11.
