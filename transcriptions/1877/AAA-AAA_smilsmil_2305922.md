## Left Page

### December, Friday 28, 1877

Superb day — Charming effect at sunrise. — Help. at 8 — at 9
went out + walked for near an hour, then in + to work on
etching "A Farm Lane" — putting in parts that I have had
taken out + biting — then some work with dry point + joining
the old work with graver. [illegible]
I when I had dinner — At [illegible]
that etching cross work on "Cows" until after 4 — then
up Four for card board on my way back met Father +
geo, walked with them + went to Mary's — tea at 8
at home — went to repainting palette, etc. for printing [illegible] —
Out 9 o'clk. went up to matts for Hats — back before 10.30.
To bed before 11.
