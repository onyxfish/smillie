## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 14, 1877

Got up feeling very weak after last night's experience.
After a light bkfst — went up to
Dr. Yale's — there until 10.30
chatting of myself a little + of
etchings more — as it was a
very lovely mng, I sauntered
down to Studio — getting in about
11.15 — Maud D. came in — at
1.30 — Johnis wife (Janitor) cooked
a steak for us + we took our first
lunch in the Studio — In the
afternoon set palette + painted
over proofs to picture that I
pushed in yesterday — Tea
in Studio about 6.30 — After
that, with [illegible] — went down to
Century Club — No one there —
met Nicoll — at 9.30 sauntered
up 4th Ave — Went into N.A.
D. "Stated Meeting" of Academicians — there for a few minutes —
then over to Studio + fixed fire —
was kept waiting so that it
was 11 o'clk. when I got to
bed —
