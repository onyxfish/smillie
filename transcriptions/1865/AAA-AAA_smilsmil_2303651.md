## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 18, 1865

Spiteful, windy & rainy. Gave
Study No. 2 a coat of Siccatif
& retouched some bits in
sky — on the water in foreground.
Ev'g. at home.

### Thursday 19.

Steady, heavy storm of wind &
rain — Spent nearly all the
m'g at the Photo.° Gallery of
Lyons'. Had a likeness
taken that pleases me very
much — Afternoon painted
in, in very rough manner a
little up right autumn sketch.
Ev'g. at home.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 20, 1865

Blustering windy day — p.m. heavy
squalls & rain — M'g went to Lyons'
Photo.° Gallery with J.C.F. She sat once.
Got a print from negative that I had
taken yesterday — flatters me — of
course I like it. Took a long walk
through Darkey Valley over Butterfield's Hill.
P.m. met with J.C.F. & go to exercises
at Graded School. Ev'g spent
half an hour at Fam M's. Dan S. & Jos. W.
called at the house.

### Saturday 21.

Very heavy wind, cold & cloudy.
Spent all the m'g with J.C.F. at
Photo.° Gallery — she sat three times, getting
an excellent negative. P.m.
wrote out a series of toasts (Regular) for
the Rough & Ready Engine Co (No 1) Committee
on toasts. Supper to be next Tuesday
ev'g. Went calling with Sarah
& go on Mr. Chas. Sayre and
Priscilla Beatty.
Ev'g knocking around. at 10 p.m.
got prints from Sarah's negatives.
