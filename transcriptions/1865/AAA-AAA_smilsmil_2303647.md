## Left Page

### October, Monday 2, 1865

Quite cool and very windy —
tried to work out of doors on No. 9
but it was no use — Every thing
blown over + off in spite of me.
So worked in-doors on No. 9 —
coming to the end of it — I
think that it would take a week
or ten days to finish it —
Eve'g packed up things, to be
off to N.Y. to-morrow m'g.

### Tuesday 3.

Started for town at 6.30 A.M. Cold
as Greenland — off by Rail at 8 o'clk
Reached N.Y. at 10.30. All day doing
up messages. Got $45.00 from Snedecor
$25.00 from Myckoff. Got Study
Ready for Artists Fund Exhibition
etc, etc. At night got every
thing packed + ready to be
off for Montrose in the m'g.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 4, 1865

Bleak, cloudy m'g — Started
from N.Y. by N.J. Central R.R. at
9 o'clk A.M. for Montrose —
Reached M. Station at 7.20 o'clk
P.M. — very cold, windy + raining.
Disagreeable Ride in Stage
reaching Uncle M's at about 9.30.
Found all well + waiting for
me —

### Thursday 5.

Cold, cloudy, windy — 2d day
of Susy. Co. Agri. Fair — S. C. F. —
used up with boil on her face —
G. & Mary — Fanny Mulford + Mrs.
Salome Watrous spent a couple
of hours on fair ground in the
afternoon — Mrs. W. took tea
+ spent the eve'g with us —
Eve'g Illumination + Band at
fair ground — Did not go —
Dan. S. + Jos. M. called + took
S. C. F. + Mary up to the grounds.
