## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 28, 1865

Painted (28½ day) on Haines
Foreground pool + Rocks.
In eveng. Read last Act
of Tempest + 1st & 2d of Merch.
of Venice at Miss Dunnings.
At 9½ o'clk went to 10th St. Studio
Bldg to Private view of Whittredge's
Twilight among the Shawangunk Mts.
Took Miss D. + Miss Emma Deland.
W. J. S. + Aunt H. arrivd from Phil.
+ Harrisburg —

### Wednesday 29.

Painted (29½ day) on Haines
Foreground Fall + Rocks.
L. Meyer took his 20th lesson.
In evng went with Gel.
to call on Miss Menny Pleasants
at the Parsons in 15th St. But
found them all out.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 30, 1865

Heavy Rain — Painted
(30½ day) on Haines, finishing
about — Genl. Danl. Butterfield
called at Studio.

Spent the evng at Tom
Fessendens. with D.C.F. — 82
W. 48th St.

### Friday 31.

Heavy Rain — Pitched into
Haines (31½) + last day —
To-morrow is the last day for
Receiving at the N.A.D. Made
great improvement, I think,
in branching + drawing of foreground
trees — Glazed pool and
finished about generally.
The picture however is very
monotonous in color.
D.C.F. Spent the evng at
our house — She looks, to
me, very miserable.
