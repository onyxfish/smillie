## Left Page

### July, Saturday 22, 1865

Heavy Rain in m'f. Cleared off at about 4 o'clk P.M. Washed out the drawn
foreground of "Fountain". Am very much
dissatisfied with the drawing — Commenced
"Roughman" with as little prospect
of pleasing myself.

### Sunday 23.

Warm overcast morning. Father and I
walked in to Poughkeepsie to Church —
went to the Mill St. Baptist Ch. & heard
Rufus Babcock. Called on Mary & Matt.
Geo. Father & I walked out home again.
Spent the p.m. on high Rock overlooking
the River, reading Swedenborg's Heaven & Hell.

## Right Page

### July, Monday 24, 1865

Warm & semi-overcast. Commenced
my first Study in oil this season.
Found things generally go more
as was to be expected. Worked
m'f & p.m. on the same subject,
some old, misshapen cedars
on a rocky knoll overlooking
the River.

### Tuesday 25.

M'f at 6 o'clk drove into town
taking Uncle N., Charl. & Miss Dining
to the boat. Father rode in with us.
Geo. walked & we met him at the
girls in Main St. Muggy, hot
m'f with threatening rain, got
out into the woods but did nothing.
Afternoon rained very heavily
clearing up beautifully in the eve'g
— Spent most of p.m. in bed
with a sick headache.
