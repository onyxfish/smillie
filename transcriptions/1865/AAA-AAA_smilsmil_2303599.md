## Left Page

### March, FRIDAY 24, 1865

Painted (25½ day) on Haines
Glazing in more strength &
breadth in foliage. Foreground
Rocks — much encouraged.
Rec'd $30.00 from J. H. Wood,
Utica for little "Rep." afternoon
went riding horseback bet.
3 to 5 o'clk with Miss Dunning
went out to the Park. Raw,
windy & showery.

### SATURDAY 25.

Painted (26½ day) on Haines'.
Put in groupse of small figures
on right hand side & painted out
figure on C. R. — filling in with small
Evergreen & stuff. — Am now quite
encouraged, & am almost out of the
blues. S. Meyer took his 19th lesson.
P.M. Phil. Rehearsal. 1st for last
Concert. D.C.F. in company with
us. Took her part way home. Spent
eve'g with her at her bro. Tom's
No 82 W. 48th St.

## Right Page

### March, SUNDAY 26, 1865

All day used up with
a sick headache.

In the eve'g Cy. Bradley came
to tea & Geo. went up for D.C.F.
& took her to near Dr. Thompson.

### MONDAY 27.

Glorious day. Painted (27½ day)
in foreground of Haines' — Miss
Dunning drew at studio all the mo'g.
Miss D. & Miss Goresuch took lunch with
us. At 2 o'clk went horseback
riding with Miss D. Splendid —
great — Bully — anything. Rode
until 4¾ o'clk — Evening went
to Mr. Ed'w'd Anthony's, in 42d
St. Pleasant little company
of about 25 — Staid until about
1 o'clk — waited upon D.C.F.
