## Left Page

### July, Sunday 2, 1865

In bed all day with
an infernaly headache
and other things.

### Monday 3.

Glorious day — find myself
powerfully weak — but am
quite able to lie about and
do nothing very quite hard.
Evening sent in to P.O. for
trunk, but got no word of it.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 4, 1865

Glorious fourth, and glorious
day — Drove into town in the AM
with Charl. Succeeded in getting
milk & various other things —
Afternoon sauntered off looking
for sketches — Every went to a
little out of door entertainment —
for some young ones, given by Mr.
Beach on his grounds — Ice cream
berries, cherries — fireworks etc, etc.

### Wednesday 5.

Clear, cool & breezy. Packed valise
& drove into town about noon. Took the Str.
"Daniel Drew" at 12.20 & against strong wind
tide Reached Hudson at a little after 3 o'clk
just too late for the 2.45 train on the
Boston & Hudson R.R. No way out to wait until the
6.20 A.M. train. So put up at the Hudson Hotel
a miserable place — Walked about & saw enough
of Hudson — think it a very poor place. Took
tea at the North House, a clean, spacious
& expensive place — After tea enjoyed
sunset over the Catskill Mts. Discovered
a sort of esplanade overlooking River, mts —
a public place — voted a "good thing"!
