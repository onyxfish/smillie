## Left Page

### February, SUNDAY 12, 1865

Miserable headache all day again, did not go out of the house.

### MONDAY 13.

Finished figures in "West Brook." 10½ days. H.H. Leeds. auctioneer, is to sell a private collection of pictures in a few days. He assures me $100.00 for picture + frame, if I agree to give him all that he can get over that — agreed. Outlined "Going Home" 6×10 in — Twilight + sheep wending down a hill side.

## Right Page

### February, TUESDAY 14, 1865

Painted 1st day on "Going Home." Did not succeed altogether in getting what I wanted, but came near enough to keep me in good heart. One day to a half.

### WEDNESDAY 15.

Painted 1st day "On the Hudson, Opposite Po'keepsie" 10¾×14¾. Got something strong in color + effect but I have yet to see, whether the strength is of the right kind. In eve. worked until 11.30 o'clk on Sanitary maps for D. Heth. Smith. Sent picture down to Leeds. L. Meyer took 10th lesson.
