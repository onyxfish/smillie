## Left Page

### April, Saturday 1, 1865

Bright morning. Threatening afternoon — Our pictures
were sent to the Academy
this P.m. about 3 o'clk — Now
I tremble to see mine on
the walls of the N.A.D. — Went
down to Knoedlers to see the
"Jeromes" there. "Prayer in Desert"
"Market Day" & "Dancing Girl" —
Eveg at home.

### Sunday 2.

Glorious day — In mrg
heard J.P. Thompson —
Afternoon, exciting news
from Grant — His army all
in motion — The north holds
its breath — walked in
the Park — with Uncle Wm. — met
Mr. Dunning & daughter, Miss
Mabes & Miss Hathaway —
a very pleasant walk —
Eveg went to Thompson's ch.
S.S. anniversary —

## Right Page

### April, Monday 3, 1865

Commenced painting again
on "From the top of Buttermilk"
(5½ days) About the middle
of the day news came of the fall
of Petersburg & Richmond — Every
body is wild — In the eveg
went over to Brooklyn — Clinton
St. 200 — with Miss Dunning, Geo.
Mary, Matt & Mr. & Mrs. Brevoort
to visit Miss Gorsuch

### Tuesday 4.

Richmond fever unbroken —
nobody tires of talking & asking
questions — Painted (6½ days)
on "Buttermilk" — Got the sky &
distance in yesterday — worked
to day over foreground — In
eveg Mary, Matt, George,
Dornan & myself spent the
eveg at Fanny Kimball's.
