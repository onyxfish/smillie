## Left Page

### Nov. Thursday 23, 1865

Disagreeable day — Raw & Raining — At home all the m'g finishing 1st Condor drawing & fixing up some of my sketches (pencil) in the sketch book — P.M. at studio doing little or nothing.

### Friday 24.

Glorious day — Started for down town at 10.30. Presented to A.B.N. Co my drawing of 1st Condor — accepted — and until 6 o'clk P.M. Running around doing up chores & messages — Well tired but feel much better for the day's exercise — went am to W. Gavit's & spent about an hour with Meta.

## Right Page

### Nov Saturday 25, 1865

Fine day. Went up to Central Park in the m'g to look at the eagles, to help me in making Condor drawing No. 2. Charcoaled away at the subject, but failed to get the action that I want. At 4 o'clk arr'd at the Studio a box from miss Dunning, containing 3 jars of fruit (3 broken) two large loaves of brown bread & a lot of apples. Ev'g at home.

### Sunday 26.

Out to D'Thompson's & heard Rev. D. Bushnell of Hartford on "our property, or possessions in the souls that are about us" from Paul's expression "I seek not that which is yours, but you" — very much pleased. Afternoon Geo. & I took a walk up to Central Park. Wrote a letter to Miss Dunning — Ev'g went to D. Thompson's & heard him on luxurious living.
