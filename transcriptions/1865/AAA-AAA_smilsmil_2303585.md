## Left Page

### January, Friday 27, 1865

Finished charcoaling & outlining on "Haines
Fall" —
2nd day.

### Saturday 28.

Charcoaling on small
canvases — View on the
Hudson, above Pokeepsée.
(upright) 10½ × 14½ in.
Also, The Catskill mts
from opposite Pokeepsie.
(Horizontal 10 × 14 in.)

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 29, 1865

[illegible — ~4 words]

### Monday 30.

Commenced painting on
Haines fall — Very soon found
that I had more than I
could do. — Started at the
upper left hand corner,
— gave up & started again
at the large Rocks in
the foreground — got
somewhat into the dash
of it, and quite enjoyed
it — To night felt hopeful.
