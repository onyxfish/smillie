## Left Page

### August, Monday 7, 1865

Drove into town with the Bourn Girls to meet the "Mary Powell" —
missed her by about 5 minutes.
Sister Mary went down on her —
got the girls off on the 8 o'clk train
for N.Y. P.M. finished drawing of
"Ploughman" on wood for [illegible],
Heavy Rain in afternoon. —
glorious moonlight walk. — bright.

### Tuesday 8

Clear + Cool. — Just at work on
Study No. 2. Pre-Raphaelitising
it to death — P.M. Painted
in sky + distance to No. 7. —
Eve'g Rec'd a note from Chas. informing
me that he had enclosed $11.74 in
his letter of last Jue. — So it has
been taken from the letter — Drove
in town to see VanVleck the P.M.
of P — He promises to start engraving.
Matt drove out with Parker
in the even'g.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 9, 1865

Very cool m'g, hot enough at
noon — My work disturbed by
Father Rebiting large plate — my
help needed. M'g worked an
hour or two on Study No. 2 — also
painted sky on a Canvas 14×26.
P.M. painted in R.h. trees on
Study No. 3. Geo + self walked
until after 10 o'clk on Hyde Park
Road — Superb night.

### Thursday 10.

M'g threatening Rain, worked
on Study No. 2 — There seems to
be no end to my work on this
Study. I am patient, because I
know that now I am forming a
"manner" + I desire it to be based
upon knowledge — P.M. Rained,
Geo + I walkéd out upon the Hyde
Park Road — I got a couple of
pencil sketches of tree groups.
