## Left Page

### May, Friday 19, 1865

Raw & misty. Bit in etching
of Ferry boat successfully —
worked a little with the graver.
Afternoon went to N.A. of D. & met
Meta Gaint — at dusk she stopped at
the Studio, & then Geo. & I went home with
her. At 9 o'clk got dinner & at
10 Reached St. Nicholas Hotel. Visited
with Fan Mulford & Misses Tuthill
& Diven — Messrs Waldron, Lee &
Mulford until 11.30 o'clk.

### Saturday 20.

Muggy, damp & windy — worked
all day (3d day) on "Ferry boat."
Meta Gaint called at Studio
in the afternoon.
Evening writing letters to
Kate & Matt.

Lloyd Mayer took his 26th lesson.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 21, 1865

Heavy Rain — Spent my
afternoon writing. Eveng
went to Thompson's Church
& heard a Stranger, without
much either of pleasure
or edification, I fear —

### Monday 22.

Wet Mrng — dry in afternoon.
All day outlining with Turp.
"Meadow & Grove" 10½ × 18
and "From Under Sunselt Rock"
12 × 20 in. Spent the eveng
with Meta Gaint —
