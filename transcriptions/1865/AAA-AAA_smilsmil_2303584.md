## Left Page

### January, Monday 23, 1865

Painting 9th day on
West Brook.

### Tuesday 24

Finishing up pictures
& getting Studio's prepared
for 1st Artists' Reception of
the Season. Very
brilliant affair. + Geo
\+ I Rec'd much praise,
my pencil studies and
sketches took much
attention in our own
Room.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 25, 1865

Making little camera-
lucida outlines of my
paintings to keep as
notes of what I have done.
Little done, as day
after Reception seems
to be a "blue day".

Seoyd Meyer took 9th lesson

### Thursday 26.

Commenced charcoaling
on my Academy picture,
"Haine's Fall, Catskill Mts"
24 × 32 in.
