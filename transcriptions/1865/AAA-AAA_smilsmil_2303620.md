## Left Page

### June, Friday 16, 1865

Warm again. Painted (8½ days) on "From Sunset Rock" worked again on the sky — Some of the clouds are moving north — some south — worked over the foreground rocks. Geo went up to [illegible] his afternoon.
Sloyd Mayer took his 29th lesson.

### Saturday 17

Another smoking hot day — Glazed & painted all over sky & distance (3½ day) on "From Under Sunset Rock" — Have got a great deal of glow & color in the sky, without being I think, "painty" — It makes my upright Sunset look color less. I have quite determined to paint that over again. 4 o'clk went up to the Park & heard the music — Everf writing to Fau. Mulford.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 18, 1865

Sweltering hot — In the m[orning] went into Mattison's church heard Rowland Bourne — Spiritual but not spirited. Everf went to Epis Ch. Cor 39th & Madison Ave. After ch. stopped in to Mills — met Frank Petit — Capt — a Lieut — justin from Baltimore bound for Albany to-morrow m[orning] — I liked the Capt much — on their way home to be mustered out.

### Monday 19

Moist, muggy hot. Got to work in earnest upon "From Sunset Rock" (8½ days) & glazing strongly all over the sky, Repainted the clouds, changing the color radically — Also dragged a grey green over the Rt. hand middle distance mt. — The whole thing now I think, is vastly improved — As I compare it with the little "evef" 5×10 painted 2 months ago — I am pleased to note progress.
