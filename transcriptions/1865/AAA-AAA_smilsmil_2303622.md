## Left Page

### June, Saturday 24, 1865

Very hot in the sun, but a delicious
breeze blowing. Did little else
than sit by the Studio's window &
keep cool. Worked a little on one
of my old "Continental Park Note-b[ook]
drawings & made an outline mem.
of "From under Sunset Rock".
Afternoon went up to the Park and
enjoyed it exceedingly.

### Sunday 25.

Mng. hot — afternoon cloudie
& delightfully breezy. Did
not go out of the house — but to
dinner at the Broadway Hotel,
wrote letters to Father and
Miss Dunning — D⁰ Throop
came over & spent the mng.
with me.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 26, 1865

Hard Rain all day — Set
my palette & tried to work on
"From under Sunset Rock" but
gave up at noon to a miserable
bilious headache — Lay helpless
at the Studio until after 9 o'clk
p.m. I am thoroughly miserable.

### Tuesday 27.

Delicious day — clear & breezy —
but I am quite under the weather.
Feel very bilious feverish. Tried
to work on "From Sunset Rock"
but had to give it up. Afternoon
saw Dr. Dunning the paid me
$101.00 for picture & frame — I
wish all who owe me money would
be as considerate. Went to see
D. Bartlette — He has given me
medicine, warns me to be careful
& keep out of that hot Studio.
Lloyd Mayer took his 30th Lesson.
