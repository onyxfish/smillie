rather muggy — afternoon beyond comp[are]

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 8, 1865

The greatest day yet. Cool,
breezy & bracing — after dinner, morning, made believe
sketch a little — part of m[orn]f writing.
At 5 o'clk started out on a drive,
went to Mr. Chas. E. Butler's near Stock-
bridge — & with misses D. & I. & two misses
Butler went rowing on the Housatonic.
"words is wantin'." — Took tea at Mr B's
delighted. — drove home by moon-
light, closing a charming day.

### SUNDAY 9.

Cooler, fresher & breezier — Spent
the m[orn]f in the lime Groves with
misses D. & I. Reading 2nd chapter of
Dewey's "Problem of Human Destiny"
