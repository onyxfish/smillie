## Left Page

### Nov. Saturday 11, 1865

Delightful day — This m'g about daylight had a bilious turn that made me rather slim for the day — Worked in a very quiet manner about the studio & at last have everything ready to go to work — I am very anxious to get at it & feel like a dog in leash. I have ref't drawings to do for Am. Bk. Note Co — for Continental Eve'g at home.

### Sunday 12.

Up before daylight with a worse attack than yesterday m'g. Spent nearly 3 hours of indescribable misery & then passed from my stomach nearly ½ pint of greenish, muddy looking bile. Suffered so much pain, that after about an hour more, sent over to D'r Throop. He applied hot fomentations, which gave relief. D'r Bartlett called about 12.30. I kept my bed all day, with little suffering about the stomach but a racking headache — D. T. McGavit many. Mill & Carl called in the evening.

## Right Page

### Nov. Monday 13, 1865

Very pleasant day. Had a good night's rest. Went down to D. T's in the m'g, and then found how weak I am — He does not speak very encouragingly, and advises me to be very quiet & careful. He has given me Senna, Dandelion and Podaphyllin. Through the night they worked as desired — Spent the Eve'g all alone. Geo went to D'r Beakley to consult about the cold that seems to have settled on his lungs.

### Tuesday 14.

Still warm, hazy, delightful weather. Some call it the Indian Summer — I keep in doors. D'r B. called to see me this m'g. Evidently he & D'r Throop fear some sort of a fever. I think that I am improving, but I am powerfully weak. Afternoon went to Mrs. Ireland's & got a couple of sketches of the wings of her stuffed eagle to use in some Condor drawings that I have to make for the A.B.N. Co — D'r Throop spent part of the every with us.
