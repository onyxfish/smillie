## Left Page

### Sept. FRIDAY 8, 1865

Light Rains in the mrng. Steady Rain all the afternoon.
My foot troubles me again, being much inflamed & pain. I
have applied a poultice, there has been a gathering & I opened
it — Have done little else than lie about all day.

### SATURDAY 9.

Raining, & threatening Rain all day. Father went down to N.Y. early this
mrng at the Call of the National [illegible]. Painted all day on 10¼×18. working
through the middle distance, Right & left. Evng. Uncle Frank arrived
with Uncle Willie from N.Y.

## Right Page

### Sept. SUNDAY 10, 1865

Dull Rainy day — Foot much better, but nursed it all day on lounge or bed, while Reading.

### MONDAY 11.

Cloudy & warm — Worked all day on 10¼×18. (6th day.) & think
that I have finished it. Can not till now it will look though
among other pictures — being painted in all sorts of lights and
at all sorts of times — But I feel pretty well pleased with
it, think it the best that I have done so far — Call it "September"
Foot healing up, nicely —
Uncles F. & W. went down to the city this evng.
