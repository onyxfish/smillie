## Left Page

### Dec. Tuesday 5, 1865

Fine day. Finished eagle
at noon took it to Nationals
— approved. Afternoon
gave Loops the 3d sitting
for my Academy portrait.
He completed his work with
the crayon. Every spent
an hour at Artists Fund
Exhibition.
Mr. Rogers & daughter & Mrs
Griffis called.

### Wednesday 6.

Fine winter day. Outlined
day. of Florida Arms and got
transfer on die to take up
to Father. Afternoon at
a little after 5 o'clk P.M.
Started on "Barge" "Republic"
for Poughkeepsie — by invitation
of Aunt Agnes, to eat my
Thanksgiving dinner with
Father & Brother.

## Right Page

### Dec. Thursday 7, 1865

and at Poughkeepsie all right, before
daylight. Found it snowing heavily but
very quietly — nearly 6 in. of snow on
the ground. Got a hack & drove
out to Hill Side — in time for breakfast. Found
all well. Wandered all about. — the effects
of snow in the woods like magic —
Dinner bet. 2 & 3 o'clk — tea at 5.30
Sleigh ride into town with Uncle W.
at 8 o'clk took the "Jas. W. Baldwin" for
New York. Have enjoyed the day exceedingly.

### Friday 8.

Clear, cold wintry. Got up at 6
clk. Reached the Studio. Kindled fire
got to breakfast by 7.30. Spent all
the mng between sleeping & sitting
for Loops, who rubbed in with Frans.
color my portrait. Stretched
paper for "Oil Well" drawing for
A.B.N.Co & traced Photo of well
furnished by Co for drawing —
Every in at Wills — Mary
has just ret'd from Orange
is staying at Wills.
