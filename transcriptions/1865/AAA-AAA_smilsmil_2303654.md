## Left Page

### October, Monday 30, 1865

Clouded day — All mrg.
at Studio, trying to bring order
out of the chaos of dirt — went
down town at noon, calling on
A.B.N. Co — Meuger — Bobbelt & Hoops
etc, etc. got up town late
Spent most of the evening in
Fleming's book store.

### Tuesday 31

Very heavy Rain — All mrg.
sweeping up & fixing about studio
waiting for plaster man to come
to fix ceiling — so that after, the
washing & scrubbing may be done.
No plaster-man came; so at
noon went home & spent p.m.
touching up pencil sketches
taken last wk at Easton.
Even'g in at Will's — met Mr.
Gavit, Meta & three other of
his (Mr. G's) children.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 1, 1865

Clear, cloudless day — Spent
the mrg about the studio — P.m.
varnishing & fixing up summer studies
Father arrived from Po'Keepsie —
at 2.30 P.m. at a church in Brooklyn
Mr. M. Hart was married to miss
Theresa Gorsuch. They started at
once for Flint, Mich. I did not
do myself the honor of attending the
wedding. Even'g with Father at
home.

### Thursday 2

Disagreeable Rainy day. Spent
the mrg pencilling up Sketches.
At 2 o'clk. went to the studio to
superintend some plastering on
the ceiling. About 7 o'clk P.m.
Gel & Mary arrived from Montrose.
Spent the even'g between Will's
& at home. Mary staying at
Will's, & Father at During's.
