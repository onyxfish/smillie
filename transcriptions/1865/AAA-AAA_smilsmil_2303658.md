## Left Page

### Nov. Wednesday 15, 1865

Another day like yesterday and the day before. I still improve. D.H. says he will not call again. Commenced drawing of "Condor & Lama" for the A.B.N.Co — getting it merely pencilled in — feel to listless to do any work — Dr. Throop & sister Mary (our sister — my sister) spent the eveg with us.

### Thursday 16.

Yet another Indian Summer day. How I long for out in the country & dream over the glorious Oct. days & the horseback rides about Montrose. Uncle Millie & Aunt A— gave me a call this mng — Mary goes to Newark, to visit Nette Sutphen this p.m. Worked all day on my Condor. Eveg went over to Diving's & from there with Uncle Millie & Aunt A— to Mills — Spent from 7.30 to nearly 9 o'clk at Mr. Gavit's.

## Right Page

### Nov. Friday 17, 1865

Overcast in the eveg but clear later in the day. Mounted the Condor drawing & fussed over it somewhat — Stretched paper for two other drawings. Mr. Gavit called in the afternoon with a phot? of Salto in Chili, for me to make a drawing of it. It is a fearful task.
Eveg at home.

### Saturday 18.

Again overcast with slight rain, afternoon very pleasant. Spent the mng getting part of the outline of Salto upon the paper. It looks more like excavated Pompeii than a living city. Afternoon went to 4th Phil. rehearsal. & then to Dr. I am still miserably weak & do not sleep well. Spent part of eveg in at Mills — Met Mr. & Mrs. Porter (Em. Powers.)
