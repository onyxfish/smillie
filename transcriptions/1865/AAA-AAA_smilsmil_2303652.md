## Left Page

### October, Sunday 22, 1865

Glorious day, bright, quiet &
cloudless. Myf went to the Pres.
Ch. & heard Elder Albert Post.
After ch. a walk around the
lake with Sarah & Geo. Dinner
at 4 — Then writing to Father,
Will, etc, etc.

### Monday 23.

Myf cloudy and quite Rain.
& cold. Out of doors most of
the myf. P.M. at about 3.30 started
out with Sarah horseback riding. She
rode a horse of Tarbell's that had never
had a side-saddle on before — got along
on a big trot splendidly — went out on
the New Milford & Sommerville Road about
5 miles then across through a piece
of wood to the New Milford Road again
then home, a ride of about 11 or 12 miles.
Evef spent about an hour with
Fan Mulford.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 24, 1865

Clear, cold day — Myf
walked down South Road, by
Myalusing & Darkey Valley Road home.
Afternoon, knocking about loose.
Evef. Attended Supper given by
Bright Ready Fire Engine Co. No. 1 — at
Searle's Hotel. Quite a gay time.
about 65 took supper. I had the honor
of supplying the Committe with Regular Toasts.
Was called on for a "Speech" & got off with a
few words, professing that elf Smillie they
called for must be "the other one."

### Wednesday 25

Another glorious day. Myf rode
horseback with Sarah down the South
Road out towards Lathrop's Lake and
back by Butterfield's Hill. Afternoon
running around paying bills etc.
Evef took tea at Dan Sayres
with Mr. Chap. Sayre, Miss —
Sarah, Mary, Priscilla, Emma Richards,
& Dan. & Joe. Mrs. Rt? home
at 10 o'clk & packed valise, getting
to bed just about midnight.
