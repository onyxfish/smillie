## Right Page

### January, Sunday 1, 1865

Painting on little oil sketch for Miss Sarah Marten until 2.30 o'clk. Then started from Studio and made 21 calls. Clear, cold, windy day.

### Monday. Jan'y. 2°.

### Monday 2.

Henry M. Rutter died of Consumption — His death was very quiet. Complained of being tired, — stretched himself out upon the sofa, — rested his head in his hand, seemed to sleep & so died, watched by Father, Mother & sisters.
Painted on Miss M's sketch & charcoaling Fall on West Brook.
