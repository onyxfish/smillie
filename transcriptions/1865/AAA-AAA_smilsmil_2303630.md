## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 26, 1865

Very breezy & brisk m'g, so breezy as to make it impossible to
work out of doors — so spent the m'g about on the place. & the
afternoon Geo & I took a stroll
up, back of Mr. Beachis, through
his grounds to the River, to home.
Sunny Brook, by the Recent Rains
has become a regular Roarer.

### Thursday 27.

Clear & very warm — worked
all the m'g on 2d Study, catching
a bit of the River & west bank —
p.m. on 1st Study of jagged
old cedars — made very un-
satisfactory progress —
Even had a pleasant walk
with Father — Uncle Willie
etc from N.Y. city.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 28, 1865

Very clear & hot, worked all the
m'g on 2d Study, very unhappy.
Worked all the afternoon,
quite as unhappily on 3d
Study — It seems that every
thing, paints, oils & brushes have
conspired against me — 3d
Study is from the Lawn, looking
over circular pond to the
other side of the River.

### Saturday 29.

This m'g started out with
new board — to make a new
& simple start, found a
Butternut trunk & went to
work at that — p.m. started,
on the same board some old
hemlock tree trunks. Things
worked more happily and
now I feel quite encouraged
by my success.
