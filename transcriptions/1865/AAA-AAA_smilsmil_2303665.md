## Left Page

### Dec. Wednesday 13, 1865

Dismal sort of a day. Commenced to finish up
Hinshelwood's large die of Locomotive, Continental Bank
Note Co. I have stoned part of it down & am now Re-cutting
it. Steel, hard & gritty — Eve. at home, Many with us.

### Thursday 14.

Father arrived in town this mng to have pfo taken of his
Rocky Mt. plate. Pleasant day, good clear cold winter weather.
Worked all day on Locomotive die — Every at home, Father
& many with us. The Artists Reception also held their
Annual meeting at Edwin White's Studio, this Evg., to determine whether
to Continue, or abandon their organization — nothing settled. Adjourned without
date — did not attend.

## Right Page

### Dec. Friday 15, 1865

Clear, Cold day. Went down town at 11 o'clk to see Proving
of Father's plate. This is the 2d proving, and of course the whole
thing looks heavy & dead, but it is in a very satisfactory condition. Father
is beginning to worry for fear that he will
not have time enough to finish it. Got
pf of Locomotive die, did many
messages & did not reach Studio
again until 3 o'clk P.M.

### Saturday 16.

Another fine day — Worked on Locomotive — most Provoking
never-ending sort of work — D.C.F.
called at Studio. Came up with
Jeo. from Phil. Rehearsal — Katy
Greatorex took 3d drawing lesson.
Eve. went to Phil. Concert.
Mr. & Mrs. Jos. R. Brevoort occupied
box with Geo. & self. Enjoyed
Concert, especially passages
from Prometheus & selections
Midsummer's Night's Dream Overture.
