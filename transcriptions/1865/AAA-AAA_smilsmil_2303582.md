### Monday 16.

Painted 5th day on "From
the Top of West Brook — Catskill
Clove" — 14×20.
Repainted all the sky,
distance & middle distance.

My birth day — was born at
No. 1 King St. N.Y. City. Jany.
16th 1833. How old am
I? I dare n't cypher
it up.
