## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 10, 1865

Great day — mild & breezy. Worked a little during the mng on drawing for R.B.N. Co —
Afternoon took a long horseback ride with Sarah, down the Silver Lake Road & back by L. Searle's farm — Every. Sarah, Mary, Geo. & I called at Joseph Drinker's & after upon old Mr. Turrell.
After, Geo. & I went by invitation to Mrs. Wm. Mulford's. Met Mollie G. & her Eaton. Fam. M., Priscilla B. Emma R. J. Dutton Colt etc. etc.

### Wednesday 11.

Weather still fine, not so warm as yesterday — Some threatenings of change.
Mng worked again a little on drawing.
P.M. took another ride with Sarah out upon the Meshoppen Road & back by Judge Jessup's farm, then past Wm. Post's farm & around by the lake.
Every. tea party at Geo. Beatty's — at 9.30 went to Bacon's & met 22 fellows who took an oyster supper at the expense of H. Y. Foster who starts for Wisconsin to-morrow to meet his bride elect & be married.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 12, 1865

Cold, windy & blustering day — fired up in-doors & tried to work upon my drawing. Made but little progress. — Walked in the afternoon at 5 & 6 o'clk. Every. at home.
J. S. Williams, Fam. Mulford & Mrs. R. Watrous came up to the house dressed in ridiculous costume & joined them in Uncle Wms. wedding suit. Irish Mary went visiting. Dud. Lathrop's — Leonard Searle's &c.

### Friday 13

Remarkably clear, but cold &
wintry — Mng again worked on
Bank note drawing. & took one
or two blood circulators in the
shape of walks. — Afternoon,
took grey horse from Goodwin's
under saddle & went off alone,
riding out upon the Sommersville
Road, around by Hart Lake and
the Depot Road — found a
new bit of wood road —
Every., wrote to Dr. Throop.
