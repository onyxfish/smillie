## Left Page

### Sept. Sunday 24, 1865

A Real Autumn day — cloudy,
breezy and warm — Spent
most of the mf & part of the
afternoon wandering in the
woods — Enjoyed it hugely —
wrote a long letter to
Miss Dunning.

### Monday 25.

Mf lowering with heavy showers.
Rowed in Rev. Corning's shell-boat
from near 10 until near 12 o'clk. in
Company with Mr. Roosevelt. Great
Sport — Crossed the River — water
rough — Rain falling — all that I
could do to keep my balance —
A bath — dry clothes, then dinner
+ an afternoon flute acc't.
Cleared off bright.

## Right Page

### Sept. Tuesday 26, 1865

Glorious cool breezy day —
Spent mf wandering about, as it
was impossible to do any work
out of doors — After dinner, took
horse & buggy, drove into town, visited
Vassar College — Saw Kate Fessenqui
the Pictures — Miss Mitchell & the
observatory etc, etc. Called on Kate
& Matt — also on Lydia Parmateer — not
in — brought mother home.

### Wednesday 27

Ther. 48° at 7 o'clk A.M. Clear,
Cold & windy — worked on No. 9
from 9¾ to 12.30 A.M. finishing
the "painting in". Afternoon
out getting pencil outlines —
Got two on East Road, & two
from Pell's Hill — one of the
Catskills & one of the Fishkills.
