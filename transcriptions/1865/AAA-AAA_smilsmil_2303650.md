## Left Page

### October, Saturday 14, 1865

Clear mrng but very soon clouded over, making a raw, disagreeable sort of a day — mrng & afternoon worked on Post-Note drawing & think that I have finished it. — Can't claim a success. Evening at home.

### Sunday 15.

Cloudy, cold, raw, windy, forlorn and desolate. Went to the Presb't Ch. with Sarah in the mrng. and then, with Mary & Gle, a walk after church — Dinner at 4 & letter writing — evng cold, blustering & wet. Spent half an hour with Fan M. Got my drawing for A.B.N. Co. into Express Office — to go to N.Y. to-morrow mrng.

## Right Page

### October Monday 16, 1865

Cold and windy — mrng set palette, outlined a small upright autumn subject, varnished out study No. 3, painting in some ducks etc. —
Jos. Mrs had got up a Surprise party for this evng at Uncle Mrs, but as it was to be a compliment to Capt. M. Eaton, Miss Mollie Groesbeck, & Capt hc was sick, it was postponed until to-morrow evng when we will assume it & give out the invitations.

### Tuesday 17.

Splendid clear mrng. Mary, Sarah, Mr. Watrous & herself spent until dinner time wandering about out of doors — p.m. took a horseback ride alone — Evening, about 25 friends, spent the evng with us, by invitation, to express their admiration for Capt. hc. Eaton by eating oysters & dancing until one o'clk in the mrng. By two o'clk got to bed all right — Think that the friends managed to enjoy themselves enough to be willing to come again.
