## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 23, 1865

Disagreeable mrg but cleared during day — Nearly all the
mrg painting on sky & over trees
etc, "On the Hudson, above [Pokepsie?]"
(6 days) much improved I think
& finished — Painted the rest of
the day on "Meadows & Groves"
10½×18 — (1 day) without pleasing
myself in any one particular

### Wednesday 24.

Glorious day, making ample
amends for our late disagreeable
weather. Painted all day on
"From Under Sunset Rock" 12×20
(1½ day) — got in the Sky and
all of the middle distance.
Leoya Mayer took his 25th lesson.
Eveg called upon Emma Ireland —
not in — Spent remainder of
eveg at Mrs. Kimball's — 22d St,
Fanny not in.
Commenced feeding in at
Mrs. Blaney's.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 25, 1865

Another fine day — Finished
painting in "From Under Sunset
Rock" (2½ days) Feel pretty
well satisfied —
Spent the evening at home
writing to Miss Dunning.

### Friday 26.

Pleasant — Painted all
day on "From Sunset Rock"
14×20 in. (3d day) Glazed
over the sky & got much
more strength & color —
painted in all but the fore
ground rocks — Mortimer
Smith called & paid me
$20.00 — Spent the
eveg at home writing.
