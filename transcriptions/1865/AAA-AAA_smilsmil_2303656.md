## Left Page

### Nov. Tuesday 7, 1865

another clear, cold, windy day.
myself went around to the N.A. of D.
varnishing day for Artists Fund Coll.
Afternoon until 4.30 o'clk
at the Studio sweeping &
dusting around — Took tea at
the Irelands. At 9.30 — Miss
Emma, Mr. Spring, Miss Dunning
George Haws, bro. G. & self went to
the private View, Artists
Fund Exhibition.

### Wednesday 8.

Fine day — fussed at Studio
with sky light & other things
until after two o'clk when
I knocked under & went
home with a headache.
Myself got & Mary spent
by invitation with Mr.
& Mrs. Myckoff.

## Right Page

### Nov. Thursday 9, 1865

glorious day — most of the
day used up looking for the
right sort of pullies & cord
for sky light curtains — had
to go down town for them.
All the p.m. at work trying to
rig up the curtains — fagged out.
It seems as though I never
would get things so far righted
in the Studio that I may get to
work — Myself at home.

### Friday 10.

another fine day. Got my
curtains fixed — my screens
up & things generally looking
more ship-shape. In the
myself had a pleasant call
from Miss Emma Ireland &
Miss Dunning.
