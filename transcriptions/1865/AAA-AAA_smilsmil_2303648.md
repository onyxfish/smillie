## Left Page

### October, Friday 6, 1865

Still cold & windy. Spent mrg stretching paper on frame, to make drawing for A.B.N. Co. Most of the afternoon devoted to a stroll with S.C.F. Fine moonlight eveg — another walk and a call upon Fanny Mulford.

### Saturday 7.

Fine day. Cloudy but much warmer. The whole day wasted, to no purpose.
Eveg Priscilla B. took tea with us. Later in the eveg Ad. Watrous & wife — Jos. W. & Dan S. came in — Stayed until 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 8, 1865

Still cloudy, but quite mild. Mrg went to Pres. Ch. & after service Geo. & I took a walk, returning home took Mary & Sarah — then dinner & another walk with uncle Wm. Eveg p.m. — went to Ch. with Dan & Jos. — I went again to Pres. Ch.

### Monday 9.
