## Left Page

### July, Sunday 30, 1865

Delightful day. Father, Ge[o]
+ I walked in to town to hear
H[enry] W. Beecher, at the Congregation[al]
Ch. — Crowded to its utmost. — Hy.
Mark 8 — 34. Took dinner + tea with
the girls at Mrs. Parkers. 6.30 called +
spent an hour with Frank + Carrie
Fonda. — Eve'g went again to hear
H.W.B. — Text Matt. 6. 31. 32. 33. Walked
out with Father + Ge[o] all much
pleased with the services.

### Monday 31.

Mf. Cool like Sep[tember] noon, not
like July. Mf. worked on 4 in.
Study (Butternut trunk) At noon
Uncle W. started for Montreal. —
Emma + Ella Bourne arr[ived]. at
1 o'clk. Afternoon worked
on 5 in. Study of Hemlock Trunk.
Have not accomplished
much to-day. —

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 1, 1865

Pleasant day — Mf., finished
Butternut tree Trunk. — In my
anxiety to have it literal, I
have made it hard in Color
+ manipulation — P.M. as there
was no sun, outlined Study No.
6. "Among the Hemlocks", and
painted over Sky etc. on Study
No. 2 — "over the River".

### Wednesday 2.

All day fighting with a headache.
Mf. cloudy. Commenced charcoal
sketch 12×18 from top of high Rock, look-
ing South. Mary + Matt walked out
from town to spend the day with us. —
P.m. in bed + around — genuinely
clear out of doors — Eve'g. Mf. +
took Mary, Matt, Emma + Ella Bourne
in to town — driving down to
Spring Side + around. Magnificent
moonlight night.
