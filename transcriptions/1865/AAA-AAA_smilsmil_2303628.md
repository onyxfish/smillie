## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 18, 1865

warm, breezy + clear. Drove auto town
this mrf with Father + got valise from
R.R. Station, etc etc. Saw Mary Mat—
at their boarding house — drove down to
\+ about Springside. About Sunset
saddled horse + rode up to near the
top of "Elle's Hill" — fastened horse + clambered
up the Rest. Sunset was charming, over
Catskills, Fishkills, etc etc. Great
4 oared boat race at Po'keepsie.
Charl arrived from Catskill.

### WEDNESDAY 19.

Quite windy + threatening Rain
tried to draw on mood Lowell's
"Fountain" but could not get into
the spirit of it. Charl got
our old boat from Mr. Beach + 
we had a fine time on the
Rough water of the River.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 20, 1865

Still fooling with "Fountain"
drawing — Got it outlined
upon the block but that was
all — In the mrf Mary drove,
in from town with Charl — In the
evf at about 7 o'clk. Maft, Miss
Perkins + Messrs Parker, Peck and
Vassar rowed up from town —
Kate Rt? with them leaving Mary
in her place —

### FRIDAY 21.

Worked steadily all day drawing
on the "Fountain" — am not at
all pleased —. Mother got off
this mrf for the Catskill Mts.
Kate is to join her in Po'keepsie
— they expect to spend 6 weeks
among their Relations and the
mountains.
