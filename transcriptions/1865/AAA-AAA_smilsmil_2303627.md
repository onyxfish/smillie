## Left Page

### July, Friday 14, 1865

Delicious day. Spent the m'f with
Misses D. & I. [illegible] on Pinnacle Rock.
Afternoon Charley Dunning arr'd. —
Took a drive out to the depot or,
near it, then up the River over a very
Rough Road to New [illegible], then a-
round home.

### Saturday 15.

At 8 o'clk drove over to W. Stockbridge
with Miss D. & met her father & Fred.
Durand. Returning at about 9.30
Spent a part of the m'f on a large rock
after dinner, packed valise &c. — at
5 o'clk took stage to depot — & at about
6 — Reached Stockbridge. Putting at the
Stockbridge House, no Room in the house, had
to lodge in a Cottage near by. Spent the
ev'f at Mr. Gavit's. At 10 went to depot
to meet train from N.Y. — in Company with
Meta G. Miss Goodrich.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 16, 1865

Pouring, steady Rain — George & I kept
indoors all day — I was writing letters
& moving — Attended part of service
at 5 o'clk P.M. at Episcopal Church.
Spent the evening pleasantly at
Mr. Gavit's — some singing &c &c.

### Monday 17.

At 7 o'clk, in the Rain, started for Pittsfield
after paying Hotel Bill of $3.50. Reached P. at
8 o'clk — Took breakfast & R.R. Saloon —
walked & waited about until 11 o'clk, took
train for Hudson, changing at Chatam —
Reached Hudson at 1 o'lk. Took dinner
at [illegible] Hotel — Sketched Catskill Mts. from
[illegible] (Rock)
Public height, near town. It having cleared
off delightfully. Took 3.30 train, freight & pass.
to Bleeksie, and at 7.10 walked out on the
track to Uncle R's. A charming ev'f
walk of about 3/4 of an hour.
Thus ended our trip to Lenox
