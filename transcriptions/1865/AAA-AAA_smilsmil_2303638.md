## Left Page

### August, SUNDAY 27, 1865

Used up with a shocking bad Headache.

### MONDAY 28

Glorious day. Clear atmosphere with Remarkably fine color —
Charcoaled + outlined study
No. 9. (14×26) a fine composition
of distance + middle distance —
P.M. worked upon study No. 7.
accomplishing very little however.

## Right Page

### August, TUESDAY 29, 1865

Fine day, but rather hazy —
commenced in color on No. 9 —
made quite a successful start
I think — The sky being already
painted upon the canvas, got
over distant mts + half of the hills
on East side of River — P.M. on No. 7.
Struck a new vein. We attended
a very pleasant surprise party at
Mr. Beach's. By him to her, on her 20th
birthday in wedlock.

### WEDNESDAY 30

Paid penalty for last night's dissipation.
In bed with headache until 4 o'clk.
Then went to Mr. Roosevelt's to see a
52½ lb. bass caught by him with Rod
line + Reel. Night of excitement —
Domestic troubles "takings on" — etc.
In bed at last at 12 M... at 1 was
started again — Jas. Roosevelt's
splendid house burned to the
ground — nothing saved — sad —
but as for effect — a glorious
spectacle. Glorious old trees,
fine lawns — books + pictures.
