## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 11, 1865

At Studio. Painted
2nd day on "Recollections
of Northern Penna."

### Thursday 12.

All day wasted down
town in attending to the
Sale of my lot in 56th St.
All parties did not
come to time, So a new
engagement was made
for to-morrow at noon.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 13, 1865

At home with headache
until noon — then went
down town about my lot.
Parties met and Sale
was consumated, at a
loss to me of about $1200.00
So much for my Real-
estate Speculation.

### Saturday 14.

Painting 3d day on "Recoll.
of Northern Penna."

Sloyd Meyer took his
6th lesson.
