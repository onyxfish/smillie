## Left Page

### February, Friday 24, 1865

Brilliant Spring day. Painted (2nd day) on 'Haines' — Got along better with my foliage, working over the middle distance + distant fall. Joe. Williams + sister Matt took lunch with us at Studio Meta Gavit + Miss Niles called also Mr. Despard + friend and Mr. Rogers + two Ladies.

### Saturday 25.

Painted (13th day) on Haines, on middle distance trees. Last night H.H. Leeds, sold at public auction my painting "From the top of West Brook" for $240 00.!!! Last monday he paid me $100 00 for it. It is very flattering to me + is much talked of in our building (Dodworth's — new) Mr. Menzies, the purchaser called. In the afternoon Phil. Rehearsal — Commenced to Rain. Pouring Rain at night; went to the Commencement of Bellevue Med. Coll. at Academy of Music.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 26, 1865

Rainy — clearing in the p.m. In all day with headache. Frank Petit came at noon + staid all p.m. + eve't — is on his way to Camp Parole — Has been home 70 days and gained 30 lbs of flesh — looks well. He understands that his Rgt. has just been ordered to garrison Ft. McHenry, Baltimore. Went to hear Dr. J. P. Thompson we't. Subject, "Him that knew no sin."

### Monday 27.

Painted (14th day) on Haines' Fall Still fussing over middle distance trees — am getting them too full of drawing — all cut up + wanting quiet.
