## Left Page

### January, Saturday 7, 1865

Painted in, first day, vigt illustration to
Bryant's "Song of the Sower."
"The maples Redden in the sun;
In Autumn gold the beeches stand;
Rest, faithful plough, thy work is done
Upon the teeming land."

Geyd Meyer. 5th lesson.

*(no entry)*

## Right Page

### January, Monday 9, 1865

Down Town with Continental
Bank Note Co. overlooking
various dies — "Hebe feeding
the Eagle." Large Locomotive
etc. etc. —

### Tuesday 10.

At work down town with
Con. Bnk Note Co. Engl
on R. Hinshelwood's die of
Large Locomotive —
Scouring it down, preparation
to Re-engraving it — as the
line was very uneven, and
in parts, unsound.
