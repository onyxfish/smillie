## Left Page

### Dec. Thursday 21, 1865

April day overhead, March day about the ears (ie — very mild) and all that is bad underfoot.
Worked a little during m[orning on] oil well drawing for R.B.N. Co.
At a little before 3 o'clk P.M.
took Mr. Jacob Wyckoff's horse
under Saddle and had a very
satisfactory Ride in the Park.
Doc[tor] at home. Dan Sayer call[ed].

### Friday 22.

Fine, clear, crisp, wintry day.
Went down-town in the City, to
sign a new deed for H.Y. Volkening,
Altho' by appointment, did not
Ready — time wasted. Ret'd to
Studio + worked upon outline of
Petroleum drawing for R.B.N. Co.
Eve[ning] took "book" Festival of Song
to D. Dunning. He is to give it to his
daughter as a Christmas present
from Jo. + myself.

## Right Page

### Dec. Saturday 23, 1865

Fine day. In bed all day
with Racking headache.

### Sunday 24.

All that is disagreeable over-
head and underfoot — Snow
in the City by 9 o'clk, heavy,
constant Rain. Did not go
out of the house, but to my
breakfast. Took dinner
by invitation with Mr. Wyckoff,
Sister Mary being with us.
Took tea also with Mr. W.
