## Left Page

### August, FRIDAY 11, 1865

Map on No. 2 again — this m[orning].
Rather lost heart, because I
think that the study now is not
as good as it was yesterday.
P.M. on No. 3 — painted in
the ground — found grass
under sunlight something else
than green paint — Started
to walk in town with Geo. to visit
Matt, but feeling unwell gave out
halfway there —

### SATURDAY 12.

A most superb day — cool, clear
& bracing like Sept. Morning too
breezy for umbrella & easel, so Geo.
& I went up the Hyde Park Road to the
old Clou place — I made 4 pencil
outline sketches — garden subjects.
P.M. finished Study No. 5 —

## Right Page

### August, SUNDAY 13, 1865

Wonderful day — Clear, cool
& bright — Geo. & I walked in to town
to church & back again to dinner.
I went to the Methodist Ch. P.M.
on the "Big Rock" writing letters. Wrote
to Addie Dunning — [illegible] wrote to
Tom Mulford & Charl.

### MONDAY 14.

Bright, warm m[orning]. Worked on study
No. 2 — without bettering it much.
on foliage in R.h. Corner. P.M.
on Study No. 3 — had but about
an hour of sunlight — as it clouded
over — Put a strong Mars yellow
& Bitumen glaze over all the
foliage & grass — like the effect,
think it good to work into.
