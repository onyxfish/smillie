## Left Page

### May, Monday 15, 1865

another June like day. Down town helping father bit in his sky on the large Rocky Mt. plate. This was the second time & was to all appearances a perfect success.

Spent the Ev'g at No. 83 r. 43d St. Meta Gavit.

### Tuesday 16

a day like unto the former one. Father started for Poughkeepsie to-day. Painted all day (3d day) "Near Montrose". Have come dangerously near pleasing myself. At 6 o'clk took dinner at Mr. Marlen's. 111 W. 21st.
Had a pleasant, informal time.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 17, 1865

Excessively warm & cloudless. D.Wm. Thompson gave his Bible class of yng. ladies, connected with Dr. Jos. P. Thompson's Church a Pic Nic. About 20 of us started by Str'gt. at 8.15. AM. & reached the top of the Palisades. Opposite Hastings at 11.30. Dinner at 2.30. Returned by cars at 6.15. P.M. A not hard worked, but very pleasant day.

### Thursday 18.

Blustering & cold. a wonderful change from yesterday. Etched in "Ferry Boat" for A.B.N. Co. I find that I have still at my command the mechanics of engraving. So if forced to drop the brush & take to the graver, I will have lost nothing, but perhaps have gained much by my temporary change of base. But far be the time.

In the Ev'g attended Great Organ Concert at [illegible] Tabernacle Ch.
