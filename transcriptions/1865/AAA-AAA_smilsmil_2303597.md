## Left Page

### March, Thursday 16, 1865

mild day — under the weather & did out little.
Hazed sky & painted on distance of "Sheep — Evening" 6×10.
Miss Dunning spent a couple of hours at the Studio drawing.

### Friday 17

Sick headache all day —

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 18, 1865

Painted all day on "Sheep" 6×10 (3d day) Think it the best thing that I have yet done in Color. Miss Dunning spent an hour or two, drawing. Lloyd Meyer took his 1st lesson. In Eveng went with Mary to 22d Reg. Band Concert.

### Sunday 19.

Glorious Spring day. In m[orning] went to hear Dr. Thompson. P.M. in the Park.
In the evening went again to Dr. T's & heard an excellent sermon on the Pantheism of to-day; delivered at the request of the Y.M.C. Ass. After service walked home "& more too" with meta gavit.
