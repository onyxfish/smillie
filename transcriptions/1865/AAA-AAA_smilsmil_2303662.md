## Left Page

### December, Friday 1, 1865

All day used up with a miserable headache.
At the Studio's, but unable to do anything.

### Saturday 2.

All the m't charcoaling &
outlining Eagle — "Florida Arms"
for National Bank Note Co.
Miss Katey Greatorey took
her first drawing lesson from
me at the Studio. Afternoon
at the Phil. Rehearsal. Jno
Snedecor, repaid to me $20.00
that I had paid him for a
frame for Mortimer Smith, &
transferred the charge to
Smith's account. David
Father's eldest bro died at Mont[real]
aged 60 yrs.

## Right Page

### Dec. Sunday 3, 1865

Mild, spring-like day. My[self]
heard J. P. Thompson again
upon the subject of Riches. The
true spirit for acquiring and
distributing them. Took a two
hours snooze during the afternoon.
Eve'g went to J. P. T's.

### Monday 4.

All day at work upon
Florida Eagle, but failed to
finish it. Eve'g took it
home & mounted it.
Mrs. D. T. Albertson died.
— Consumption — She
was a cousin of bro.
Will's wife.
