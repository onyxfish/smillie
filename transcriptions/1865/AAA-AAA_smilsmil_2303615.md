## Left Page

### May, Saturday 27, 1865

Disagreeable Rainy day —
Finished painting in "From
Dunset Rock" 14×20. (4th day)

Spent the eveg at home, writing —

### Sunday 28.

Still Raining — Went in the
mng to hear Miss Lizzie Doten
Speak before the "Friends of Progress"
at Ebbitt Hall — 33d St. Was
taught anew the Heathen doctrine
of Pythagoras, with some of the
flummery diddle of modern
Spiritualism — It is wonderful that people in this 19th Century
calling for more light Can be
satisfied with the dark-lantern of
a pagan teacher of 2400 years
ago — Eveg went to Dr. J. P. Thompson
& heard a Son of Dr. Bacon of Conn.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 29, 1865

Rainy — Frightful day with a sick
headache — Painted through it,
on "Fall on Westbrook" 12×18 (4th day)
Eveg called for an hour
on Miss Emma Ireland —

### Tuesday 30.

Dreary, hot day — worked all day
(5th day) on "Fall on Westbrook" on the foliage
and tree trunks —

Spent the eveg with Miss
Maggie Gunn. 153 W. 24th St.
