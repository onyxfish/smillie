## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 25, 1865

A bright and glorious day —
The day has been given up
entirely to the funeral procession
of Mr. Lincoln — It was a
magnificent display of popular
feeling — After going the length of
Bdway I took my position on the
tops of our Studio Bldg Cor. 26 St. and
5th ave. The sight up & down the
avenue, & over the city was grand —

### Wednesday 26

Very warm & June like —
a lost day for me, fighting
all day with a headache —
In the mrg went over to the
N.A.D. — having a Supplemental
varnishing day — The opening
night having been postponed
on acct of the death of the Pres't
(U.S.A) from Monday 17th to Thurs.
27th ins. —

Uncle Wm & Aunt H. started
for home this mrg.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 27, 1865

Another sultry day — Spent
the morning sketching about the
Jersey City Ferry for the A.S.N. Co.
Went to Tate's & saw the etching
Ps that he is taking (25) of the "Rocky
Mt." Plate — They look first rate.
Great excitement over the capture of Booth,
he webs. Private view & inaugural ceremonies, new bldg. N.A.D. Crowded
to suffocation & very hot. Took
charge of Miss Dunning —

### Friday 28.

Warm day. Still good for
nothing — Did but little, and
that little was outlining upon
the canvas (14×20) "View from
Sunset rock — Katskill Clove" —

This afternoon Mother and
Kate started for Poughkeepsie
where, at Uncle Willis's place,
Father expects to join them
next week — We are to try
the experiment (as a family)
of living separated for a year,
