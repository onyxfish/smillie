## Left Page

### May, Thursday 11, 1865

Cloudy, Rainy threatening.
Finished painting in "Falls on
nest brook" — 3d. day — Under
the circumstances, consider
myself tolerably successful.
Rec'd. notification from
N. A. of D. of my election
as associate.

### Friday 12.

Rainy mf., clear afternoon.
Painted all day (3d. day) on
"Looking out of the Valley" 6½×9.
Made considerable changes
in lines of composition, with
advantage to the picture, I
think.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 13, 1865

Glorious day. Painted all
day on "View near Montrose"
6×10 (2d. day) Clouds + distance
Afternoon Mr. Copcutt
called & I drove with him in
style through Central Park.
Black Horses in front, black
man behind — nothing
like money.
Leord Mdyer took 25th lesson

### Sunday 14.

Another fine day. Attended
church in mf. & heard Dr.
Neston. At 2 o'clk took
Dinner with Miss Gorsuch at
Mr. Grant's. Brooklyn — Stayed
to tea & was caught in the
Rain on my return home.
Had a very pleasant time
with some very pleasant people.
