## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 5, 1865

Painted (7½ days) in "Buttermilk" —
worked on foreground trees
& Rocks & Scumbled the Sky
distance & middle distance.
Lloyd Meyer took his 21st lesson.
Ev'g. Gilbert Pettit, uncle Willie, [illegible]
& others here to tea. Went to Miss
Dunning's & Read with others the
3d. 4th. 5th. acts of "Merchant of
Venice."

### Thursday 6.

Rainy day — under the weather —
did out little. Painted
(8½ days) the branches and
foliage in upper left hand
Cor. "Buttermilk." —
Ev'g. Spent at home.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 7, 1865

Still under the weather —
Spent the day Charcoaling
& outlining subjects on
6 × 10, and 6½ × 9 in. Canvases.
Getting several ready to
Push in at odd times.

### Saturday 8.

Painted in small subject —
6½ × 9 — for Mortimer Think.
A little Ravine bit. Something like the 14 × 20 "From
the top of Westbrook". Subject
by request of M. T. —
Lloyd Meyer took his 22d lesson
Afternoon. Phil. Rehearsal.
After Rehearsal went
about among the galleries.
