## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 3, 1865

Fine day — Dinner yesterday too much for me.
Used up all day with a headache — miserable —

Miss Carrie Ward &
a cousin called in
the afternoon.

### Thursday 4

Fine day — Painted all day on "View near Montrose,
Pa." 6×10 in — 1st day —
Think that I was pretty
successful in getting a tender
color & effect — as usual however — sky & distance was too
strong & foreground weak.
In Ev'g had our last
reading for the season at
Miss Dannings — Present: Miss
D — Ireland & Kimball — myself
& a part of the time Dr. R —

## Right Page

### May, Friday 5, 1865

Threatening, with fine heavy
shower in P.M. Went down
town to aid father in biting
in his sky on large plate —
owing probably to want of
pressure in Ruling — biting failed
In Ev'g took tea at Mrs
Rutherford's — cor 1st. St & 2d. ave.
Very stylish, — old Stuyvesant
family —

### Saturday 6

Rainy day, very dark — worked
on little upright 6½×9 "Looking
out of the Valley" — but dark
& dreary — made little progress.
2d day on the subject —

Boyd Mayer took his
23d lesson.
