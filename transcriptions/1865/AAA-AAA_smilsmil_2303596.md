## Left Page

### March, Sunday 12, 1865

Superb day. In the
mng went to Dr. Thompson's.
All the afternoon in
bed, sick headache.

### Monday 13

Commenced painting again
(19½ day) on Haines' Fall,
working over foreground.
Rocks — Schauss called
at the studio. In the
eveng went into Br. Mills.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 14, 1865

Painted (20½ day) on Haines.
Repainted sky & distance
giving more aerial effect.
Have had to paint out a
great deal of careful painting
but it's better. Read Shakesp.
at Mr Dunning's in eveng. Finished
Hamlet & commenced the Tempest.
Willet Fanny Kimball at the house,
also Gilbert Tittyt just in from a
year in Southern prisons.

### Wednesday 15.

Mild, muggy day. Commenced
raining in the afternoon.
Felt miserably under the
weather & unlike work — Painted
& fussed away with sky & distance
of Buttermilk (4½ days). Better
but still very unsatisfactory.
Lloyd Meyer took his 16" lesson.
In eveng Dorman & Temple at
home, also Mary Tietjen & husband.
