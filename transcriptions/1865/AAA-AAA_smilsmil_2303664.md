## Left Page

### Dec. Saturday 9, 1865

Pleasant mng, sleety, stormy every. All day hard at work
on die of South Am. Go-cart. Engraving by Beckwith after a drawing of
mine. I am trying to finish it up in short order.
Eveg at home. Mary with us.
1 Katy Greatones took 2d. [illegible]

### Sunday 10.

Very disagreeable underfoot
& unpromising overhead.
Mng attended Tabernacle Ch.
Stranger preached — had headache
& slept through the whole sermon.
Slept during afternoon. Eveg
went to "grand Memorial service
for the dead" at Thompson's Tab.
Ch. Services commenced at 7 o'clk
& continued until ¾ to 10. Jos
Duryea assisted. J.P.H.'s discourse
was about 1¼ hour. Music by Chorus
male, female & boys, organ & trumpets.

## Right Page

### Dec. Monday 11, 1865

Fine clear day. Worked with
out stopping until nearly 2 o'clk
upon S. Am. die — Then went
down town & got proofs. Find
it improved, but still needing
more work. Geo & I spent
the eveg at Mr. Gavit's — He
interested us very much
with his Microscope. Meta
spent about half an hour with
us.

### Tuesday 12.

Heavy, easterly storm of
wind & rain. Worked all
day on S. Am. die — Re biting,
graver & burnisher. In the
eveg gave it to Mr. Gavit —
Wrote part of a letter to
Miss Dunning.
