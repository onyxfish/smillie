## Left Page

### Nov. Monday 27, 1865

Fine day. Commenced at [illegible] new feeding place, cor of 18[illegible]
& 28[illegible] — Mr. & Mrs. J.R. Brevoorт board there. Commenced drawing on Condor No. 2. Spent an hour with Brevoort in his Room after dinner, then went to Artists Fund [illegible] for another hour, then home.

### Tuesday 28.

Fine day. Finished drawing of Condor No. 2. Am tolerably well pleased. Eveff until 9 o'clk nearly with Mr. & Mrs. Brevoort then went home.

## Right Page

### Nov. Wednesday 29, 1865

Clouded over threatening snow. Tinkered a little on Condor drawing & then attended to a number of little things about the Studio. At 2 o'clk went down town & signed deed of 4 lots in 55[illegible] to Hy. Dolkeeing. Sat to Loop for my Academy portrait about half an hour before going down town.

### Thursday 30.

Got paper stretched, ready to commence drawing for National Bank Note Co. Sat a full hour for Loop in the [illegible]. He has the head only roughly blocked in. Did some charcoaling & sketching during the p.m. Eveff took dinner at Mr. Wm. Martin's, 155 W 21st St. Mrs. Chas. Martin & daughter Sarah, were there. Music & conversation made the eveff pass very pleasantly.
