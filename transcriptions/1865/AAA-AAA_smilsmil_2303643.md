## Left Page

### Sept. Saturday 16, 1865

Superb day — all the mng. on
Study No. 9. Painted over left
hand middle distance — am
quite pleased with my work.
P.M. on No. 7. Repainted distance
+ water + painted out all of the
middle-distance trees, preparing
along to Repainting again.
Mother Returned from her
visit of a week to the City
of [illegible].

### Sunday 17.

Another great day, only a trifle
windy — Spent the mng. with Chas.
on Peli's Hill — The view and the
atmosphere most glorious —
Afternoon wandering through
the woods by the River —

## Right Page

### Sept. Monday 18, 1865

Cold, heavy Rain — Ther.
this mng. 18° lower than last
night. Worked over water
+ scraped down + glazed over
foreground trees on Study No. 7.
Aunts Mary + Jessie Rill. to
N.Y. by 9 o'clk train — Chas.
Rid to N.Y. by night barge

### Tuesday 19.

Cleared off bright + cold — 6°
colder than yesterday, being
50° at 6 o'clk this mng. —
Uncle W. went to the city this mng.
Worked in doors all mng. [illegible]
Study No. 1 — P.M. Painted
out of doors on Study No. 7.
Glorious afternoon — feel
encouraged again by my
progress.
Put shoe on crippled foot
for the first time since it
began to trouble me.
