## Left Page

### February, Saturday 4, 1865

In bed with Racking
headache. bilious upset,
\+ heavy cold.

Leoya Meyer called at
Studio, but did not stay.

### Sunday 5.

Somewhat better, but
on my back all day.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 6, 1865

At the Studio. Painted
in figures. "From the top
of Westbrook — 9½ days.
Made some alterations
in foreground "Oct in the
mts,          4 days.

### Tuesday 7.

In bed again. big
thing in headache my
Influenza and bile —
Very unhappy.
