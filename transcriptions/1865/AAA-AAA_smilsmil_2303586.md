## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 31, 1865

Continued painting on Haines Fall. Found
new troubles — Cannot find those terrible neutral
greens that are so all important.
Don't feel quite so hopeful to-night.

### February, Wednesday 1.

Down Town. Paid $27.25 Policy of Life ass.
Bought stove for Studio $28.00

Stoeyed Meyer was at the Studio, & drew for an
hour or so, but as I was not there, cannot call it
a lesson.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 2, 1865

Started again on Haines Fall — the further in
the deeper " — the thing now looks like distress
& I feel in sympathy with it.

[illegible]

### Friday 3.

At Haines again — looking & doing no better.
P.M. New stove arrived, great improvement — I
set to work & had a grand sweep up and
house cleaning time —
Studio now looks highly respectable.
