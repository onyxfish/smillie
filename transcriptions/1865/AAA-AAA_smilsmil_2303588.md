## February, Wednesday 8, 1865

At home — Powerful weak — Pencilled on
Summer Study, Old Birch
(No. 2) Haines Ravine —
& finished pen sketch
"On the Hill side —
Dr. Bartlett vaccinated ½ doz
of us — myself among the rest.
S. Meyer at the Studio
again — did not stay.

## Thursday 9.

At home — Drawing
(India ink) "The Sword
and the Olive Branch"
for Con. Bnk Note Co.
Mr. Jno. Taylor Johnston —
No. 8. 5th Ave. Invited artists
& others to view his collection
of pictures — Fine affair — but
could not go. Mrs. Jno.
Endicott of No. 2 W. 13th St.
invited George himself to party
to-night — Did not go.

## February, Friday 10, 1865

Finished drawing "The
Sword & the Olive Branch

Mortimer Smith left
$34.00 at the Studio.
Payment for two pictures

## Saturday 11.

At Studio, stretching canvases,
outlining notes from my paintings
etc — little done. P.M.
1st Phil. Rehearsal for it"
Concert. In the Evening
went to Miss Dunnings. No.
11 Waverly Pl. — to Read "As
You Like It" — with Miss
Ireland. Mrs. Spring — Mrs.
Despard. Mrs. Greatorex &
Mr. & Miss Dunning —
Pleasant Evening.
