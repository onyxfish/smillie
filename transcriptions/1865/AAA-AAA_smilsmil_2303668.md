## Left Page

### Dec. Monday 25, 1865

Christmas — Glorious overhead, fearful underfoot. Mf [morning?] went
to studio & outlined upon oil
drawing for A.B.N. Co. At 1 o'clk to
dinner with Br. Will at No. 6 N. 41st St. —
G.W. Brainerd. 3 Sons. & Miss Lucretia Brain-
erd were of the party. also Br. Geo. & Sister Mary.
Splendid affair — ½ doz. or more courses —
at the table nearly 3 hours. D. T.
Albertson dined with us too. Evef. at
home.

### Tuesday 26.

Clouded over & disagreeable
afternoon with Rain at night.
Worked all day on oil
drawing for A.B.N. Co.
Evef. at home.

## Right Page

### Dec. Wednesday 27, 1865

Rain in the mf., and
disagreeable all day —
Worked on A.B.N. Co.'s drawing,
finished it, — at night
mounted it to deliver next
mf.

Cleared off at night.

### Thursday 28.

Another disagreeable day.
Left drawing with Will in
the mf. All day at
work charcoaling (1st day)
on "From under the Ledge,
Kaaterskill Fall" — 24×52 in.
Am now all alive and
enthusiastic to get at my
painting.
Evef. at home.
