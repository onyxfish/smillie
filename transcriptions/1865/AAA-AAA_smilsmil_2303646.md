## Left Page

### Sept. Thursday 28, 1865

Superb, cloudless day — worked on No. 9. but almost useless, fighting
with a headache — am discouraged to-day — P.M. got an outline
sketch from Trellis walk looking over the River. Matt and
Kate walked in from town this mf. and spent the day with us.
Walter driving them in at 9.30 in the evg.

### Friday 29.

Another clear, warm day — worked from 9.30 until 10 o'clk. upon No. 9.
with comfort, making gratifying progress. Afternoon out in
Roosevelt's "Hog Lot" sketching Chestnut trees — This
glorious weather, & the invitations that I am daily receiving
from Geo. Many + J. C. F. at Many are making me quite unsettled
and almost unhappy at my work. Uncle W. arrived
from N.Y.

## Right Page

### Sept. Saturday 30, 1865

Still another great day, but with signs of a change — a
warm South wind blew so strong as to keep everything upon an
uncomfortable niggle. All the mf. on No. 9 — Still unfinished —
progress unsatisfactory —
P.m. On More Chestnut trees upon Roosevelt's.

### October, Sunday 1.

And yet another great day,
with a night beyond all for loveliness — Clear, bright,
breezy moderately cool.
Did not go in town to-day.
mf. writing to Many —
afternoon twelf. wandering about.
