## Left Page

### March, Monday 20, 1865

Painted (2½ days) on Haines.
The sky again & distant rock
& fall etc — Better, I think.
Misses Fanny Payson — Nanny
Pleasants — — Gillman & Mrs.
Illy Gillman called. Later P.C.F.
her sister Kate & Miss Kidder
called. walked up to 42d St with P.C.F.
Read 2d 3d & 4th acts "Tempest" at Dunning's
then took Miss Dunning Miss Emily Ireland
& Miss Fanny Kimball's — a little company.

### Tuesday 21

2 2½ days on Haines' — working
over the foliage, upper part of the
picture. — Miss Smith & mother
called — also Miss Ella Rogers & 2
sisters — also Miss Meta Gavit &
friend — walked down to 42d St
& back again with Miss G — com-
pared notes, & found that we were
both human & "subject to like passions."
In eveng — called on the Lents'
found both Amelia & Sarah at
home. —

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 22, 1865

An April day. Sunshiny & showery.
Painted (2 3/4 day) on Haines' Fall.
Still working over the trees. Lloyd
Meyer took his 18th lesson. —
In evening called on
Miss Blanche Martin with
Mary. Matt & George.

### Thursday 23.

Cold windy. Painted (2 4½ [illegible]
day) on Haines' Still on trees.
Mr. Jas. Hart worked for a few
minutes on my picture to its
very great advantage — giving
very much more sunlight.
Miss Dunning spent the
mf drawing & took lunch with
us. Meta Gavit & two friends
called late. I walked home
with her.
