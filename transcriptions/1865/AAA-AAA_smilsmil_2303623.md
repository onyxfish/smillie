## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 28, 1865

Another delightful day. Felt better but miserably weak. Worked about an hour glazing on "From Moun[t]
Sunset Rock". Now I call it finished, but I'm so out of sorts that I don't know how it looks. Worked two hours or more on "From Sunset Rock" — Jos. M. N. helped me much with the foreground. The 12×20 has taken me 6½ days; the 14×20, 10 days.
Cleared up about the Studio, preparing to leave.

### Thursday 29.

Delightful day — Spent the m[orn]ing at the Studio. Taking up carpet, etc. up. Got come[?] down from [?]; about noon.
Afternoon running messages down town.
Early every called at Miss Ireland's.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 30, 1865

Intensely hot, with pleasant breeze. Every, heavy thunder storm. P.M. again down town messages, etc. All day fixing up about Studio to get off — at night arranging things at home to the same end —

### July, Saturday 1.

Cloudy & close day — All m[orn]ing packing up — at 8.30 p.m. started on the Mary [illegible] for Pkeepsie — Had a very pleasant trip — arrived at Pkeepsie — found our trunk non est come at it us — Every body quite civil, but the trunk, nowhere.
