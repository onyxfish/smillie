## Left Page

### October, Thursday 26, 1865

Up at 5.30 A.M., got [illegible] and off in Stage for depot at 6.10, with Sarch.
At 8.10 left Montrose depot reaching Mauch Chunk at about 1.30 P.M. Took cars at once on Belvidere RR, reaching Phillipsburg at 2.45. Crossed the River to Easton, putting up at the Franklin House.
Between 3.30 & 5 o'clk P.M. walked about seeing what was to be seen.
Am delighted with the scenery from Mauch Chunk to Easton.

### Friday 27

Up at 6. Bkfst. at 7 — at 9 o'clk met men at Central RR. Station & at 9.30 got [illegible] — started on her way to York Road Station by Lehigh Valley RR.
Then climbed a rock back of Station & took two sketches, one looking up the Lehigh valley, the other, up the Delaware. Day cloudy & chilly.
Then crossed the River & Easton & climbed the 220 steps to Lafayette College. Then down to the river Road & out along the Delaware for about a mile, taking another sketch. Took train on N.J. Central RR at 12.15. Reaching NY. at 3.15;
called upon S.B.N. Co. & got [illegible] Am. Go. card drawing to mount & retouch. Suey at home.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 28, 1865

Day much like april, warm, cloudy, and showery — [illegible] about among the studios, took dinner with Mrs. Nyckoff. Afternoon about at home trying to Right things — Evening, in at Will's.

### Sunday 29

Bright, clear, windy day —
Mrng went to Thompson's, but he did not preach — then went to studio & from there to the Madison Ave. Baptist Ch. Took dinner at the Broadway Hotel. Afternoon writing to George and Mother.
Evg went to J.P. Thompson's Tab. Ch., heard a [illegible] stranger. Walked home with Meta Gavit — found ourselves locked out, so walked about until after 10 o'clk — Lovely moonlight night.
