## Left Page

### July, Monday 10, 1865

Delightful day. Spent the m[orn]g
trimming trees. Afternoon
went up on Bald top & took a
outline sketch.

### Tuesday 11.

Rainy m[orn]g. In doors drawing.
Afternoon it cleared charmingly.
Took the horses & Misses D & I. G[et] & all
went to "Icy Glen" near Stockbridge.
Had a good tramp through
a very rough place.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 12, 1865

Overcast, warm & very little breeze. Took stage
at Lenox at 8.30 AM. & with Misses D. & I. and G[?].
went to the depot. Took 9.15 train to Stockbridge —
Reached S. at 9.45. Found Mr. Chas. Butler's carriage
waiting & Misses Rosalie & Helen Butler. Drove directly
to Monument Mt. 3½ miles. Reached foot & left carriage
at 10.30. Reached the top by wood road & path at
about 11.30 — Glorious view & day just right for color.
Took dinner after 1 o'clk, then 3 sketches & scrambling.
Rain threatened in the afternoon, but about 5 o'clk
it cleared delightfully. Left the top at 5.30 & met Dr. Dur-
ton B's carriages at the foot at 6 o'clk — Drove
directly home finishing a charming day at 8 o'clk.

### Thursday 13.

Cool & breezy, quite breezy —
see the m[orn]g. Spent doing nothing
at 4 o'clk P.M. Miss Ireland
& self went horseback to Lenox Furnace
then to Lee centre, & then around
Laurel Lake to Lenox, a
distance of about 12 miles.
