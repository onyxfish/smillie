## Left Page

### June, Monday 12, 1865

Still comparatively cool. Painted for an hour or two
in the mng on "From Sunset Rock"
At noon D. L. ? called & I
spent the p.m. with her at the
N.A. of D— Mary & Matt
arrived from Phila this evng
Spent the evng with them
& mother in at Will's —

### Tuesday 13.

Another of the old sort holidays. Tried to work, but did
little on "From Sunset Rock"
(6th day) Painted trees against
sky on R. hand side — It
is awfully hard work —
Mother went home to [?] this
P.M — Went with [Pratt] to the
Ferris Female Institute to act
as judges upon drawings & paintings
— Spent part of the evng
writing the Report.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 14, 1865

Pleasant, cloudy day. Painted
½ day (6½ days) on "From Sunset
Rock" working over distance etc.
Afternoon took the 4.30 train
to Orange — to stay all night with
the Fessendeus. Took tea with
them & after, Gen. Sarah & I went
to the Cushmans & heard singing
by quite a family, on the tribe of
Bush — men & women as they are called.

### Thursday 15.

[Rid] to N.Y. by the 9 o'clk A.M.
train — Reached the Studio
by 10.30 o'clk. Painted (7½ days)
on "From Sunset Rock" — Made a
change in my sky — Painted clouds
on the horizon, & the Sun behind
them — not set as before — I think
it much better as it allows light to
fall more strongly upon the landscape.
But the sky is bad — cut off by
cloud forms & untrue in its effect
of light — The upper part being a
sunset sky — the lower, 20 m. or
more before sunset.
