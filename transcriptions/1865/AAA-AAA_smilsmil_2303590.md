## Left Page

### February, Thursday 16, 1865

Great show. Painted
5th day, (7th since starting) on
Haines' Fall. Put in sky
anew & distant rocks. thing
looks better & again I am
hopeful.
(5 days painting. 2 days
charcoaling.)

### Friday 17.

Painted (8th day) on "Haines"
worked on trees on right
hand side & some of the
middle Rocks & tones —

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 18, 1865

Painted (9th day) on "Haines" —
got my canvas entirely covered
painting in the tumble of
Rocks, stones etc. in middle
ground.

My large easel came to
the Studio.

Lloyd & Meyer took 11th lesson.

### Sunday 19.

In the m'g, heard D. Thompson
preach on the desirableness
of consecrating sons to the
Gospel Ministry. Walked
in the Park in the p.m.
In the even'g Heard — Abbott
of Terre Haut. Son of Jacob
Abbott of this city. Subject
"The Power of God in us" —
was much pleased.
