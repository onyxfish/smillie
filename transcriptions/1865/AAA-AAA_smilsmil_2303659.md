## Left Page

### Nov. Sunday 19, 1865

Clouded + threatening in a
still, mild + quiet November way.
Geo. is complaining in an un-
accountable way + I myself
am all out of sorts. So both stayed
at home. Wrote to father in the
mng, walked with Geo. up to Central
Park in the afternoon. Spent
eveg at home. Dr. Throop
called + spent half an hour
with us.

### Monday 20.

Dull day with drizzle at intervals
+ rain at night. Mr. Jno. E Gavit
spent the mng with me, turning
over my portfolios + books —
Afternoon worked at an hour
or two outlining "Salto" but felt
most miserably heavy + sleepy,
gave up at about 3 o'clk —
Eveg at home. Dr. Throop
spent an hour with us.

## Right Page

### Nov. Tuesday 21, 1865

Heavy Rain all day long.
Worked steadily upon Salto.
The material is extremely
unpicturesque — low, flat
horizon — square block houses +
tumble down fences — no sign
of life, animal or vegetable.
Eveg in at Will's.

### Wednesday 22

Raw, windy day with rain
at times — Worked very steadily
on Salto + had the satisfaction
of finishing it + showing it to
Mr. Gavit, at Will's, in the eveg.
He expressed himself in a
very flattering manner +
finds no fault with my price
$40.00.
