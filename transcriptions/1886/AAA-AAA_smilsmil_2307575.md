## Left Page

### October, Sunday 3, 1886

Superb day — up late — at 10 went to the butchers, as I had forgotten to order dinner yesterday. About noon Annie, Jim, Eliz., Jack Downs & I went out & sauntered around the top of the hill — I spent some time among the cows with my camera — Home at 2 & had dinner — After that in dark room for nearly two hours developing the 13 negatives taken in the mtf. Then a walk on Piazza — wonderfully beautiful afternoon — tea — wrote my letter home to Kate & at 8 went to P.O. with it — then home. Annie read aloud until near 10. To bed at about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 4, 1886

Very pleasant mtf. — overcast afternoon beautifully clear night — the mtf. until 11 down town attending to messages — then went over to Sayre farm & among the cows made eight exposures with camera — home soon after noon & in dark room until shortly after — then dinner — At 2.30 — went riding with Annie, Eliz. & Jack Downs — back at 5 — tea — Evening reading papers until 9 — then cards until 10.30 — to bed.
