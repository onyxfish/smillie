## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 14, 1886

A pleasant day — 10 o'clk when I got to Studio — For some time at work upon Crécy 'mill. Col-man & Champney came in & the morning passed. To lunch as usual. Back & put Studio in a little order then to work on large drawing Etrêtat from the height and spent the rest of the afternoon working upon it with bristle brush & pure water — W. & Mr. Parke came in, also Champney & Sewell — Home at 5.30. After dinner Chare came in & took the P's up to his room in 47 S. To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 15, 1886

A nasty, wet day with some slety snow at night — All day at work on big Etrêtat drawing, most of the time with clean water & bristle brush — Weth. W. — Beeen came & took Mr. Parke & Bessie to the Casino — Parke himself went to Scranton this afternoon. Annie & I read aloud from "Two years before the mast" & then had a game of cards. I wrote a letter to the Porlets telling them that I must move my studio next May and asking them if they had any property near present Studio that they would be willing to remodel into a studio bldg —
