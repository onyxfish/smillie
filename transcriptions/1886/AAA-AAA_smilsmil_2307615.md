## Left Page

### December, Sunday 26, 1886

Overcast & raw with light snow
after 5 o'clk P.M. Up late — [illegible].
After 9 — Sat with news paper until
about 11, then took Jims and [illegible]
walked down to studio where
told James to have fire for me
morrow — Then we went to George's
126 E. 28th S. Saw Geo. Millie. Shedd
& the Baby — There until 12.30 — Then
walked home — Dinner at 2 —
afternoon, wrote letter to Mother.
Between 4 & 5 took Annie & Jim
to Mother Cook's — Then mailed my
letter & walked on up to Madison Av
& 58th S — back, stopping for H & J.
Tea before 7 — Eveg. looking over
old letters, especially a file of
Kochler's letters written town during
last summer while engaged upon
the Homer-Bridgman-Johnson on
etchings. Wrote to Stroud about
bit, permit for Herrick & Morris to work
in Studio at Montrose.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 27, 1886

Dull, overcast m'f, clearing to a
charming day before noon. Got to
studio soon after 10 — Some little time
before I could settle to my work — Then
made some changes in the L.H. foreground on Old Mill — Crécy — About noon
Ferguson came in & I worked on a
Venetian Street Scene of his in Water Color
until aftn — Then to Bank for Annie &
home at 1.45 — just 3 when again I got
to studio — worked awhile on Ferguson's
drawing, finishing it — making a bright,
sunny bit — Then commenced work on
original study sketch for Fallow Field
making liberal changes & washing
it with clean water — unpacked case
from Hanna & Ives — Detroit — 3 paintings
that should have been ret'd last June —
a frame & a glass broken — Home at
6 — dinner — Eveg wrote to J.W.
Wood & to Hanna & Ives — Then
read — To bed about 10.30
