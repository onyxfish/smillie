## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 30, 1886

overcast — some rain falling in the afternoon + ev'g. 9.30 when I got to studio. Spent the day in mounting etchings. Ferguson came in the m'f. with a Water color new in Venice for advice + a stranger came with a large copper plate to know if his ground would stand acid. In the afternoon Pate came in to ask for changes in our agreement concerning the etching of a large plate. These visits took up considerable of my short day — Just after dinner I went to Dr. Yale's to consult him concerning myself — Home before 9 — Charl came in between 9 & 10 — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 1, 1886

A raw day with slight snowy drizzle in the afternoon — all the morning framing etching p'f. to send to the Century on Sat. ev'g. Went to Bank at 12.30 + deposited money there for Annie + self — thence home to lunch — all the afternoon spent in fruitless search in the studio for a p'f of Jab. proofs of my Jasini etching — There are probably about 25 p'f. in the p'f. + the search made me half wild — I cannot imagine where they have been mislaid — At 5 went to Aveni's taking one copy of Wilson Cat. + my check for $25 — pay mt. for the other. On my way home stopped in to Knordlein's + saw the Rajon etchings. Dinner soon after 6. About 8 p? Nellie came + with Kate went to call on Mr. Watson. Charl came in a little later + stayed awhile with Mother. To bed soon after 10.
