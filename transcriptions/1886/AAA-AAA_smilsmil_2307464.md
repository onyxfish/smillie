## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 23, 1886

Pleasant day — to studio before 9.30
& to work getting plt. from Father's
lion die, using a French paper.
Home to lunch at 1.30 — Kate
got in from Bo'k. at about 2.30 —
It was near 3 when I got back
to studio. I worked all the
afternoon at my Proving, Taking
13 plt. in all, & unaccountably
spoiling the last three — Of the
10, perhaps half were really
good, the others being marred in
one way or another — Home to
dinner at 6.30 — Rul't signed
between 50 & 60 plt. of pigt. for
West Shore Bonds, wrote a note
to Will. To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 24, 1886

Brilliant, beautiful day — to
studio about 9.30 & spent the
m'f. most of it, trying to get a
good plt. of Father's lion die on
parchment — did not succeed —
gave the die up to messenger
at noon. Then home to lunch.
The afternoon was spent in
getting things cleared away
in the studio — dinner as usual.
Annie had a lunch party but
I was not present — in the eve'g
she went to a Theatre Party —
Mad. Sq. Theatre "Engaged" —
I went up to 16 W. 53 & called
on Mr. Geo. N. Curtis about getting
permission to etch his Eastman
Johnson — "A Glass with the Squire".
Spent an hour pleasantly with him
then home — Geo. with Kate —
I sat writing until Annie came
in soon after 11 — To bed before
midnight.
