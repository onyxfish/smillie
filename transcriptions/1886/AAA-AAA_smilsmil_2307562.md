## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 7, 1886

Glorious day from beginning to
end. Some work & varnishing on
Breton plate — then made drawing
of photo wash box & about 11 went
to Ferfers with it — noon when I
got home — worked for an hour
on Breton plate — dinner — all
the afternoon hard at work with
Herrick & Smith the painter,
putting down canvas east second
story piazza deck, near 7 when
we had tea — glorious moonlight
night, air mild & dry — in hammock
for some time — then finished letter
to Koehler, & to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 8, 1886

Another superb day — went
down town to Will & other messages — did not get back until 11 —
found Smith the painter waiting
for me — Got canvas ready to
put down on upper decking and
by that time it was near 12 o'clk
Herrick & Smith went to dinner —
I got things ready for the biting
of sky etc — on Breton plate —
Dinner at 1 — then looking after
H & S. for awhile — three to work
& my biting & finished it — I
find it very hard to work at my
etching having quite lost interest
again with the men — lovely
eve'g & night — Spent some
time on piazza — then worked
for awhile on design for ceiling
of S. W. ash bedroom — To bed
about 10.30.
