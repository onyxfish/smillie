## Left Page

### July, Thursday 15, 1886

Heavy rain in the early mg. All day overcast, windy & threatening — I started my printing about after bkfs. & after a couple of imps — set to work with graver & worked steadily — after 4 o'clk I again took a couple of pfs. & was much pleased to find my plate very nearly finished. Coyle did not come to work — Herrick was at the mill nearly all day getting out lumber — The duck for ceiling & piazza floors got here to-day — I went down town before 8 p.m. evef. wet down paper for printing to-morrow — read paper & to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 16, 1886

A very pleasant day with a heavy shower in the mf. I got to my work printing in the mf. but had much interruption as I had to direct & even help Herrick much of the time in getting up ceiling stuff — In the afternoon I worked until after 6. getting 5 jap. pfs. & two plain imps. for my days work — I was exceedingly tired & did but little in the evef. Before going to bed had a game of Bezique with Annie & at 10 fo turned in.
