## Left Page

### July, Friday 23. 1886.

Glorious day - the most of the
day sketching preparing pf.
& getting off by Wfs. Bridgeman
painting to Am. Art Ass". The
plate pf. to Kimmel & Voigt
& pf. to Koehler. Down town
between 11 & 12 - Herrick &
Smith finished ceiling of
N.E. bed room & started on the
Cherry room. My camera
& "dark room" outfit arrived
in the nt. Well. I felt
very much tired & did
not write the letters I should
to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 24. 1886.

Another charming day - Having
got the Bridgeman painting &
plate off my hands I cleared up
a little in the studio & got the
Johnson painting up on the easel
& the plate on my table. Before
dinner I etched in all of the
floor & in the afternoon put in
the remainder of the second etch-
ing — Herrick & Smith putting up
the canvas ceiling in Cherry Room.
Mr. & Mrs. Husted (Baptist cler-
gyman here) called in the
afternoon. I went down town
after tea — Well. wrote notes
to Kimmel & Voigt, Jas. Sutton
& Mrs. Gardner — To bed soon
after 10.
