## Left Page

### May, Sunday 30. 1886.

Very hot day — Spent the m'f
in doors — rehung some engravings
in 2°. Story bed room — wrote part
of my letter to mother — At
1.30 Annine, Jim + I went to
Mother Cook's to dinner — Jul.
Valentine the only other one —
Home at about 4, took a short
nap + then finished my letter to
mother — also, wrote notes to Homer,
Stedman + Thomas. Tea at 6.30 —
Eve'g. Chark came in with
untoned p'fs from plates developed
Sat. night. Walked up home
with him at 10 — home + to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 31. 1886.

*Decoration Day —*

Bright m'g., clouding over soon
Shower about 10 — Dry but overcast
during the rest of the day. Got
to studio about 9.30 + worked on
Bridgman etching until 12. Then
over to Mad. Sq. + took a look at
Grover Cleveland + other celebrities.
Home at 1 — Telegram stating that
Mother + Kate would come down
from Po'k on 12.10 Train — At 2.10
went up to depot with Jim +
met them — To studio at 2.45 and
etched on Bridgman getting it
nearly drawn in. Home at 6.30.
Eve'g. Geo. + Nellie came. At
9 I went up to Chark's but he
was not in — Home + to bed a
few minutes after 10.
