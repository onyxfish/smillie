## Left Page

### June, Sunday 13. 1886.

A very warm day — in bed all
day with an old fashioned
head ache with the added
misery of a cattunal cold in
my head — I managed to
get up about dusk & sat
about during the remainder
of the evening. It was just
midnight when I transferred
myself from the big chair to
the bed.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 14. 1886.

overcast day & quite chilly — Bkfst.
about 8, feeling very shaky after the
experience of yesterday. Podged about
the place until after 10. then went
down town with W. & Mrs. Park to spend
an hour or more in shopping etc etc.
Dinner about 1 — wrote a letter to
mother & took it to P.O. at 4 —
Tea at 6.30 — Herrick came in
later & arranged to go to work
for us day after to-morrow.
To bed at 10.
