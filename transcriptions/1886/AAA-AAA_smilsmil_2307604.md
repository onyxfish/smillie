## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 24, 1886

A pleasant day — To studio at 9.30
& to work on Pelque Gatherer. At
10.45 Buffalo Bill's Cavalcade "Wild
West" — Indians — Cowboys — etc. etc.
passed out of Madison Sq. Garden —
I went out & up to 41st St. to get to
the head of the column — an amusing
show. On my way back stopped at
Keating's & paid for my new overcoat.
Home at 1.20 — Katie, Nellie, & Adams
to lunch — To studio at 2.30 — Geo. went
up to G.C. Depot & met Mother &
Kate & then brought them around
to our house — I worked as long as I
could see — Then walked to Union
Sq. up B'way to 42nd St. & stopped at
wood flooring place to complain
of exorbitant bill — Home at 6 —
dinner — Evg. Geo. & Charl
came & spent the evg. with
Mother & Kate — To bed about
10.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 25, 1886

A dark, nasty day — driving wind &
rain in the afternoon — Got to studio
before 10 & spent the mg. finishing
A.B.N. Co's drawing of Pelque Gatherer.
Charl came in — Home soon after
1 — It was 3 before I got back to
studio — so dark that did not try
to draw — Got some of my etching
plate in press & cut paper to mount
them. Home soon after 5 — Had
a Thanksgiving dinner at 6.30 —
Besides Mother & Kate, we had
Geo. Nellie & Charles — After dinner
Ed. Cook & Mary came in & spent
the evg. looking over photos of
Montrose house & some of C's Ranche
views — To bed about 11.
