## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 26, 1886

A very mild, bright day — to studio about 9.30 + spent the mfg. biting in on big etching — Home at 1 + back at 2.30 — cleaned off plates + then spent the afternoon in general work over it. Mr Hammond (?) now of Cleveland O. late of Boston — now an architect, late a teacher of Water-Color painting called — About 5.30 I went to Knoedle's + bought some imitation Creswick paper then back to studio + wet it down for printing to-morro It was 6.30 when I got home to dinner — Evetf. Read awhile in Koehler's book (Etching) — then Walter + his wife came in and stayed until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 27, 1886

Rain most of the day + night. To studio at 9.30 — Spent an hour making further changes on the Bennington Mill drawing — then got to work taking p[f]s. on Creswick paper of big Etching — ink two black + strong — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Took four p[f]s. in the afternoon without any one being satisfactory. I cannot mix an ink to my liking — I worked very hard + got home at 6.30 very tired — then had dinner — Evett. Chave came in + stayed until 10.30. Then Annie read aloud a chap. from Adam Bede + we got to bed about 11.30.
