## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 15, 1886.

Overcast much of the time —
when the sun shone out it was
very hot. Spent much time
working about the vines, es-
pecially the new ones set
out in the Spring by Payne.
Some time spent in getting
Etching room in order for
work. Evef. Annie & I
wrote letters — I wrote a
long one to Mr. Koehler. To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 16, 1886.

Overcast & cool in the m'f. clear-
ing for awhile about noon, when it
was very hot. Afternoon again over-
cast with quite a shower between
3 & 4 P. M. The m'f. spent in
general work about the place
& messages down town — among
other things, looking for a copy to
buy. Dinner abt — Soon after
2 went to Dr. Smith's — the dentist
& had him work awhile in my
mouth — then home & until near
6 unpacking the two cases just
am'd & distributing the contents.
Tea before 7 — Evef. Annie read
aloud awhile.
Old Peter Coyle worked for one hour
& then left.
