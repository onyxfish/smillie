## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 23, 1886

Bleak day — To studio at usual time — but did not feels equals to painting on my "Cliff Farm" — Had many callers — Addie Denning Hawes Wilson came in for a few minutes the "Voice from the Cliffs" was brot. in — I sent back etchings to Grady — the Insurance policy on Homer picture was brot. in + the boy with Scrap Books + chair from Po'k. arr'd. Home at 1 — To N.A. at 2.30 + spent the afternoon making a process drawing after Innis for Kurtz — Home at 6.30 — Rosalie Butler + Mollie + Kitty Adams to dinner — took them to Thalia Theatre, Bowery + Canal St. + left them — then home + wrote about etching until 10 — then to Thalia — saw ½ an hour of the German [illegible] play — then home Rosalie staying over night — and to bed at about midnight.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 24, 1886

clear, cold + windy — By an odd mistake, got up an hour earlier than we intended — B'kf't. at 8.15 I got to studio soon after 9. Spent the m'f. painting middle groups of trees on Cliff Farm — Home at 1 — To N.A. at 2.30 + spent the afternoon making drawing after Eastman Johnson's portrait of Dr. Dalton — did not finish it — Home at 6.30 — dinner — Even'g for an hour Annie read aloud from "Strange Case of Dr. Jekyll + Mr. Hyde" — Then, until 10.30 I wrote on my etching paper — To bed about 11.
