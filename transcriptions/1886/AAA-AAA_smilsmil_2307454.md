## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 3, 1886

Very cold, with snow falling nearly all day — near 0 when I got to studio — mounted last Ph. f big plate & at 11 took it to Etching club subtr & put it into frame in place of the one already there. Met Koehler there & spent some time with him & Wünderlich — then to Am Art Ass'n — some time with Kirby — then to 2° Nat. Bank. & got money for Huine. Home at 1 — lunch, back to studio at 2.30 & spent an hour or so working on big Etching. Home to dinner at 6. Every wrote a note to Wm Pate telling him that I would Etch my picture of Yosemite Fall, owned by him — 14×18 in. for $500. Annie then read aloud from Adam Bede until near 10.30 — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 4, 1886

Fine snow & bitter cold wind until near noon. To studio before 10. & by close work finished sketch of one of the Washburn's Co's mills for the A.B.N. Co — by 1 o'clk — then home to lunch — back at 2.30 & in less than an hour Koehler came in & after that I did not get much done on the Lincoln Mill — another sketch for the A.B.N. Co. Home at 6 & had dinner — Avery Brown called for a few minutes in the early eve. I wrote notes to E.C. Stedman & Mrs. Hicks about pictures for the Koehler works — until after 10 Annie read aloud from Adam Bede — To bed before 11.
