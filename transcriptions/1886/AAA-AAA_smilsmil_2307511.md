## Left Page

### May, Friday 28, 1886

Bright day — to studio at 9.30. Before
I got to work at my etching I took
Charl's camera — went to the Mt
ave. horse fountain & made half
a dozen exposures — It was about
10.30 when I got to work. Upon
my Bridgewater etching to which
I stuck closely until after 1 o'cl.
Home to lunch & back at 2.30 —
again worked on etching until
after 5 — It was after 6.30 when
I got home to dinner. Calverley
brought in the masks of Miles
face & mine, made in 1847 & I
gave them a coat of oil —
Evett. Annie & I went to
Walters — there until near
10 — then home & to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 29, 1886

Warmer & cloudless — feeling
so uncomfortable about the head
that at this I tried hard to work
after about an hour & a half I
had to give it up. I may have
strained my eyes & head by my
hard work yesterday — I wrote
to Herrick & Paigne, Montrose &
to Payne the Eisey Co. treasurer,
ending to each of them check.
Home to lunch at 1.45 — Annie
& Jim started for Flushing soon
after 2 — to meet Fanny K — I took
a nap — at 4 to barber's & had
hair cut — then to studio & did
some fussing with my pts.
Home before 7 — Annie & Jim
ret'd from their trip all right —
Dinner — just before 9 went
with Charl to his dark room &
together we developed a dozen
plates of my exposure — got
some very good pictures — Home
at 11 & right to bed.
