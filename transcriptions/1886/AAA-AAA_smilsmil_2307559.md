## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 1, 1886

Charming day, but I was not
up to my privileges — Up late —
Charlie insisted upon getting
a doctor, so, about 9 o'clk. he
went off & brought Dr. Richardson —
I hung about all day, feeling
very weak & much of the time
in pain — getting to be very
much disheartened — Evelf
got a letter from Annie with
some particulars as to Will.
Cook's death — Read awhile
Sef. "Century" — To bed soon after
10.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 2, 1886

Glorious day — Ther. down to 47°
last night & up to 70° to-day. So
much pains in the with, that I did
not get up until nearly 10 — The
rest of the day spent rather mis-
erably, most of it in the hammock
— late in the afternoon I felt
better — got a letter from Annie
but she does not say when she
will return — wrote her a short
note letting her know that I
was sick — Evelf. in big
chair —
