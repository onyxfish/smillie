## Left Page

### June, Monday 7, 1886

*Home again.*

Jim behaved like the old scratch.
Roaming before 4 o'clk. & keeping it
up at intervals. Up & help: at 8 - on
piazza until 10, then Annie & I went
to Telegraph Office & Livery Stables —
making arrangements to return home
at 2.20. Heavy rain all the m'f.
I took a nap before dinner
at 1.15 — About 2 the stage came
Train at 2.20 — clearing pleasantly
— got into N.Y. city at 5.30 and
home at 6 — Dinner at 6.30 —
Charles came in soon after 8 —
I went with him to his dark
room & developed 18 photo.
negatives — 14 appear to be
good — Home soon after 11
& right to bed.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 8, 1886

Brilliant day — after 9 when
I got to studio — Spent most
of the m'f preparing Homer
pfs. for E.C. Stedman & Koehler
— Took K's to H.P. at noon.
Home at 1.30 — to studio about
3 & worked at packing until
after 6 — Dinner before 7 —
Everts wrote letters. Chat.
came in with my photo.
negatives. To bed at 10.30
