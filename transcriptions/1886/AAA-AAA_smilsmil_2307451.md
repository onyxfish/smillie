## Left Page

### January, Thursday 28, 1886

A nasty day — soaking wet without rain & no end of gloom
& mud — To studio about 9.30 —
stopping at O'Neill's & getting a
pair of new boots — Spent rest at
studio working on big etching &
taking two proofs — Then washed
up & at home a few minutes after
1 — lunch — & at 2 went down town
to Com. of Jurors — Chambers St. & Bdway
took Yale's certificate of inability
& was excused from enrollment.
Then to Kimmell & Voigt's — met there
Mr. Chase — publisher of Boston — got
50 large sheets Jap. paper — Then to
N.A.D. at 3.30 — Board Meeting. To Rec.
the [illegible] from Hanging Com. — Then
to studio about 4.30 — made paste
& stretched a Jap. paper pl. of big
etching — Home at 5.30 & had a rest
before dinner at 6.30 — We & Annie
& I took turns at reading Adam
Bede aloud — then a game of
cards & to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 29, 1886

A nasty, wet day — To studio and
spent some time mounting three
pfs. of big etching & about noon
took the best one over to N.A. of D.
& put it in frame already hung
for it. There were near 2 — Then
home to lunch — Back to studio
at 3 & worked on a pf. of big etch-
ing & then laid a ground over
the plate — About 5 tried to get
a nap in my big chair — It was
a little after 6 when I got home
to dinner — about 8 took Annie
to her mother's & left her there
while I went to Press View of the
Water Color Soct'y. A bright &
very pleasant show — had quite
a good time & generally until
midnight — then with Charl.
went to Hoffman House & left
card for to-morrow for Mr. Bement of
Phila. Then walked up 5th ave. with
him — To bed at 12.30. During
the night, Jim Restless, with a
"croupy" cough.
