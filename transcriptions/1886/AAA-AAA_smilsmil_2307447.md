## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 20, 1886

Unpleasant day — near 10 when I got to studio. Spent the day working with dry-point on big plates and at 4 took a couple of proofs to see what I had accomplished before. I laid a rebiting ground. It was after 5 when they were taken and then I had to wash up — after 6 when I got home — dinner — Soon after I took Annie to her mother's — then I went to Water-color Society meeting — Made Report for Auditing Com. Home soon after 10 — then Chase came with leases of Garfield Place house for me to sign — It has been let from Feb. 1st to a W. Maynard — Mary is soon to go to the Terraces with her children & join Mother & Kate — It was after 11 when Chase left — there to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 21, 1886

Uncle Willie called at the house, after 6½p. & went with me to studio — wants me to make a drawing.

Heavy rain during the m'f. at night, simply nasty — To studio about 10 — Most of the m'f spent touching up a p.f. of big plates & putting it in frame to send to Sybt'n to serve as dummy until I can give finished p.f. Will send up p.f. of Skinner die & some other things for my attention — Took big etching to N.A. at noon. Home to lunch & back as usual. Afternoon laid rebiting ground over the most of big etching — At 5 went to N.A.A. & saw Sutton & Kirby. They broached to me the matter of my making a large reproductive etching after Eastman Johnson's "Stage Coach". Home at 6 — Avery Brown to tea (dinner rather) Eve'g, he took Emma Satchell & Adams gals to Cortada Choral Club Concert & I took Annie — a very good concert — Geo & Miss Van Amminge sat near us. Home before 11 and to bed towards midnight.
