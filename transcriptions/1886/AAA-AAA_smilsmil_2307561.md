## Left Page

### September, Sunday 5, 1886

An unpromising mtg. clearing to be a charming day. I
felt somewhat better than yes-
terday & spent much of the mt
with Charl in getting three or
four photos. interior views in
the house. The rest of the time
in the Hammock. Dinner after
2 — again in hammock until
near 4 when Azur & [illegible] and
delschewianitz called. Before
tea wrote about part of my letter
to Kate. Charl spent the eve
at Dant. Sayre's. I wrote part
of a letter to Koehler & got to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 6, 1886

Another dubious mtg. clearing to
a charming day. Bkft. at 8 & about
9 Wm Parke & his bro. came & took
Charl & his baggage over to the
little R.R. to start homewards at
9.25 — Then I kindled fine in
studio — laid around in Breton
place & after much coaxing of my-
self I managed to get to work.
I etched in the sky & some small
bits of cross work. Dinner — more
work & stopping out — at 4 — horse
& buggy — out with Annie & Jim —
taking a long ride & getting in
about 6.45 — Eve. tried to
finish Koehler letter. but too tired
to bed promptly at 10. Glorious
moonlight night.
