## Left Page

### January, Saturday 2, 1886

Brilliant day — to studio at 9.30 — Prepared for a day of printing — then went to [illegible] — got 3 plain imps. then back to studio & took [illegible] until 1 — home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & proved as long as I could see, but did not get half a dozen satisfactory [illegible] owing to the mysteries of ink. Washed up & home before 6. Dinner — Whist — Reading — after 9 o'clk had our little game & into bed as the clock struck 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 3, 1886

A dismal day — Ther. at 43° with a mizzle that did not at any time amount to rain. Sat about until near noon, then went down to studio & spent a little time picking up, as for some days past I have left everything in confusion. Home & dinner at 2 — Read awhile — then had a long nap — Tea before 7 — again some reading then wrote letter to mother — Took it to G. C. D. about 10. Then home & after reading awhile, to bed about 11.30
