## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 19, 1886

Rather a dismal day — cold &
misty — Grass seed & pruning
saw & cutter came — I worked
with cutter until I pinched my
finger so badly that I could not
continue — Arranged brick for
the chimney — afternoon, went
to foundry & had irons forged to
hold chimney to the house —
Eve'g. For some time signing
E. Johnson etchings — then a
game with Annie & to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 20, 1886

Another dull m'f. but before noon
it was cloudless, hazy & warm — I
spent the m'f. as usual about the
place trying to hurry up the men.
went down town before noon &
hired a darkey to dig — he came
in the afternoon — After dinner
I got Eastman Johnson painting
boxed & sent to [illegible] Curtis. 16 W. 53.
(insured) by R.R. I also did
some photographing — the developing
seeming to be successful —
Early eve'g. wrote a note to Mr.
Curtis & took it to P.O.
