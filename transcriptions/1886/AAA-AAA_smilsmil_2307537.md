## Left Page

### July, Monday 19, 1886

A very cool day — almost cold
& cloudy — Up early — most of the
mft. with Smith supervmitending
the painting of ceiling in N.E.
bed room. Down town before 11 —
most of the afternoon with Heinde
putting up ceiling in 2d story
hall — Evef. fussing over
plans for cherry bed- room
ceiling until 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 20, 1886

Very cool day, not actually
over cast, but dull. Spent the
mft. with Smith who painted
2d coat on N.E. bed room ceiling &
also in hall — Laid a beautiful
rebiting ground on Bridgman
plate & in the afternoon bit
it — much interrupted by
having to look after [illegible]
who put in the asbestos & gal-
vanized iron protection against
fire in chimney in main Room
together with a little appliance
that I hope will cure a ten-
dency to smoking. Evef.
after some reading had a
game of cards with Annie
& to bed at 10.30.
