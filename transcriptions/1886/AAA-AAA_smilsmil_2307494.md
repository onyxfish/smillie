## Left Page

### April, Saturday 24, 1886

The hottest day yet. Ther. getting up to 87°. To studio at
9.30 — To work on Homer
plate with graver & dry point
To lunch as usual & back
to my work. S.R. Koehler
came in about 3 & we talked
business — strikes & socialism
& french impressionists until
5 o'clk. At 5.30 I went to
a barber's & had my hair cut
& a refreshing shampoo —
then home to dinner. Evef
just as A. & I had settled
ourselves for a game of cards
Ge° C. & wife came in —
there charm came — It
was nearly when we got
to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 25, 1886

Quite a cool day — most
of the time overcast — B.b.f.:
about 9 — the m'f spent in
reading & a little writing. Went
out about 1 — Enquired about
Mary Sprague C's condition — She
suffered a miscarriage last
night. Went to 41st & 8th; & 1st Ave
& saw an interesting fire to good
advantage — home at 2 — dinner
afternoon, more writing and
lounging & a nap between
5 & 6. Evef. Ge° & Nellie
came in & there chark —
I wrote several notes &
after 10.30 my regular letter
to mother — after 11 where I
went to G.C.D. & mailed it
then to bed.
