## Left Page

### May, Monday 10, 1886

Overcast mtf — Rain beginning to fall by 10 o'clk — a wet, cool day.
Breakfast before 8 — Spent most of the mtf. in chair by the fire &
had a good nap before dinner.
Afternoon, had a tramp on piazza
with Jim — my head continually
very swimming. Even't. Spent
in chatting until about 10, then
to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 11, 1886

Another dismal day — my
head still very heavy — Sat about
until near 11, then Annie, Jim &
I walked up to "the Ridge" & took
a look at the new houses, then
came by a back road getting in
about noon — Took a nap before
dinner at 1 o'clk. Afternoon in
doors reading until after 4 o'clk
when again Annie, Jim & I
went out for a walk, going out
in the direction of [illegible] — Back
in an hour — Tea soon after 6 —
Even't, some of us played a new
game of cards known as "Hearts"
To bed soon after 10.
