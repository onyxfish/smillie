## Left Page

### June, Sunday 27, 1886

Brilliant day, but quite windy.
Bkfst soon after 8 — about 10 Annie,
Jim & I wandered out by the dummy
to Warner's lot & sat there for some time
then & around to the West of the fair
ground, stopping to visit with "Jessie"
our new cow. Home at noon. Dinner
at 2 — then a long nap. Payne
came in the afternoon, to arrange
about work. I wrote my letter to
mother & took it to P.O. before 7 —
then tea — Eve'g, made sketch
of plan for ceiling of N.S. Bed-
room. To bed about 10.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 28, 1886

Charming day — I experimented
with some heavy paper, trying to put
it up on the ceilings by tacking
but without success — Herrick
had to podge about while I
worked on the figures on plate
putting in the faces & other im-
portant parts, getting in a good
day's work. Went downtown
after tea — then watered plants —
near 9 o'clk when I got dressed
& went with Annie to call on the
Woodins, whom we found at
home. near 10 when we Ret-
ned our paper & to bed at
11.
