## Left Page

### February, Monday 1. 1886.

At last, a clean, cold day —
Ten o'clk when I got to studio
to-day Annie takes charge of
the marketing etc. — a work
that heretofore I have attended
to. Spent the rest at work
on big etching; scratching
in background work — then
home to lunch at 1 — back
at 2.30 & bill in the morning
work. Dr. Mulford came &
talked for some time of his
bro. Elisha's death. It
was after 6 when I got home
to dinner — Evett. Clark
came in with the Cargill
lease for me to sign. He
left before 10 — then I read
awhile aloud from Adam
Bede. It was after 11
when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 2. 1886.

Bright, cold day — after [illegible], to
K. & V., Canal St. & had two proofs
of big etching — in an ink that
proves very much softer than
those I took — To studio about 11.
Just missed Will who is anxious
to have me do some work for him
— had to send him a telegram.
Worked on plate until after 1 —
home to lunch at 1.30 — Annie
at Miss Parton's — at 2.30 again
to K. & V's & had 3 pp. Fakes — I
am encouraged & now consider
the plate practically finished —
To studio before 4 — & toasted down
the three pp. to stretch them.
Home at 6. Will & Fanny K. came
to dinner & at 8.30 went to Geo.
Cook's — the rest of the evening
Annie & I occupied ourselves
with various duties — at 10 had
a game of cards & to bed at 11.
