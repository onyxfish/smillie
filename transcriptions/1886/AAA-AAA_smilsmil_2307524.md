## Left Page

### June, Friday 25, 1886

Overcast w[ith] f., clearing hot.
Thunder showers in the afternoon.
Coley finished his mason work —
I put in a good morning on
the Johnson etching — after
dinner I worked about until
4 o'clk. when Dan Saupre came
up & we drove over to Mattius
Harrington's where I dickered
for a cow & finally bought one
for thirty dollars. It was after
7 when I got home to tea —
Eveft. after reading newspapers
wrote a letter to Charl. — & to
Kimmel & Voight. To bed
at 10.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 26, 1886

Windy day with many showers —
Harrington came before 9 o'clk — with
the little cow & we put her in the
lot where she spent the day crying
& wandering about. In the evening
Herrick milked her & she gave 5 qts.
I put in a good day's work on
the Johnson plate — getting nearly
all of it drawn in — Aunt H. came
up with some large strawberries —
After tea I went to Frank's to get
permission to have stuff from the
creamy on to our lane — He referred
me to Mr. Isaac Post. I went down
to Mr. Dud. Lathrop's & saw her
but got no definite answer. Saw
there Mr. Stewart Beatty & others.
After getting through with my
messages it was after 8 when I
got home — To bed soon after
10.
