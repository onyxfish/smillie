## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 17, 1886

Overcast mtf. clearing to bright day — to studio at 10 — Spent the whole mtf. writing letters, mostly on business pertaining to etching. To W.H. Hamilton — E.C. Stedman — Geo. W. Curtis — Eastman Johnson — Thus. Hicks — T.W. Wood — Jno. L. Fitch — S.R. Koehler — etc — etc. Home to lunch at 1. Fanny K. there & lunched with us — back at 2.30 — all the afternoon working over 2° West Shore Bond vigt. correcting details in Morgan's Yacht & mounting it on heavy board. Home at 6 — dinner — Evely Reading until after 9 — then a game of cards & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 18, 1886

Brilliant, beautiful day — to studio before 10. Very nearly the whole day was spent in mounting a lot of Rough S/port [illegible] — preparation to sending them to Kelpkel to make some business arrangement with him if possible. Home to lunch after going to 2° Nat Bank & depositing $35. Rec'd. from Canada Bank Note Co. for a drawing — back at 2.30 Home before 6.30 — dinner — Evely, Annie & I went to Aunt Anna's — 331 E. 17th St. and spent the evef there — Home about 11 — it was after 11.30 when we got to bed.
