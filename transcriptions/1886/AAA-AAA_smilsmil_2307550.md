## Left Page

### August, Saturday 14, 1886

Cool, breezy day — very cloudy in the
mf. the afternoon cloudless but hazy
— night, brilliant moonlight. Spent
the mf. Taking pf. from Johnson Pl.
working slowly. Dinner at 1 — then
a rest & again at my [illegible] —
finishing up at about 5 o'clk —
having taken only 8 pf. Tea
at 6.30 — At 7.30 went down town
& spent an hour shopping —
Eveg. finished news paper
before 9 — then with Margaret
Parker White & Annie got into a
discussion of Religious topics
covering a wide range & did
not get to bed until near
midnight.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 15, 1886

Glorious day, especially cool
& brilliant — but I got up with
a miserable headache that
stuck to me all day, keeping
me in bed a good part of the
time. Mf. Margaret & Annie
& Howard White went to Negro Ch.
I loitered about the place. Mr.
& Mrs. Miller (Hetty Searle) Dimock Searle
& a lady came in from the Fair Ground
by my invitation & enjoyed the view from
piazza. Dinner about 2 — afterwhich
I spent the rest of the afternoon in
bed. Up for tea — Eveg. general
chatteraion — Margaret very much
used up by her cold. To bed
soon after 10.
