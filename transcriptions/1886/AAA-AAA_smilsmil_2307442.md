## Left Page

### January, Sunday 10, 1886

Dismal day & cold. Spent most of the m'f. writing letters — one to Mary & along one to S.C.F. Out for a short walk before dinner at 2 — In the afternoon Annie took Jim around to her mother's for a little while — I took a nap. About 5 Charl came in for a few minutes — Tea at 6.30 Eve'g. Annie read aloud for a little while — Then I made out my bill against the A.B.N.Co. for finishing Father's die — dating it Dec. 31. This I will hand over to Charl for mother. Then I wrote my letter to mother & afterward took it to G.C.D.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 11, 1886

Cold day & clear. To studio before 10. Spent some time on Bennington mill drawing making some changes — then to work on big etching & worked closely until 1 — then home — Mr. Satchell & daughter there. Back at 2.30 & worked as long as I could see — 4.15 — I was after 5 when I left the studio. Went to Berry's — Notary — swore to my copy of Father's mem — that accompanied his will — Then to P.O. station, 3d. ave & 28th S. & sent it to Charl — & a lf. of small etching. Rough spot to Geo. J. Medale — Home before 6. Dinner — Eve'g read awhile — then Mr. Randolph & daughter called to see Mr. Satchell after they left wrote notes to Symington, Dielman & Baldwin about auditing W.C. accts. Took them out at 10.30 & mailed them — Ranin [illegible] & to bed.
