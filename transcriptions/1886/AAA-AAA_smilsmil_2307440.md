## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 6, 1886

Bright mg — clouded afternoon
with a snow flurry — bright, cold
night. Got to studio before 10 — tried
to begin the etching of large plate
9½ × 16. Rough Sketch in the Yosemite"
but after some trial was forced to
conclude that I cannot see to make
the etching on an unsmoked ground
— this obliges me to clean from the
plate a remarkably beautiful ground
& transferred tracing — I commenced
a new tracing at once — Gifford came
in — much interested in my Pasini
etching — at 12.30 went to Bank —
home at 1 — lunch — soon after 2. went
to K. & V's & had some [illegible]. Taken
from my Pasini — got to studio before 4
cleaned ground & tracing from my
plate — home before 6. dinner —
Mr. Satchell & daughter with us
Evef — spent by each of us reading & writing as we willed —
To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 7, 1886

Clear & very cold — To studio about
9.30 & right to work on Tracing of
Rough Sketch — got it done by noon.
Then finished cleaning plate — a
young man from Bink. Note Co. with
a bundle of stuff for corrections —
Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30
Laid a ground & got a beautiful
transfer — Tried a bit with my
point & to my dismay found it
chipped quite badly — I'm afraid
I chilled the ground by cooling it too
suddenly. Tried the remedy of
gently warming it again. Before
6 went to Dr. Yale's with [illegible] of my
Pasini etching & a set of working proofs
nearly 6.30 when I got home to
dinner — Evef spent writing
notes to Gebbie & Co. — Frank Heride
— Dr. Yale & W.M.S. To bed before
11.
