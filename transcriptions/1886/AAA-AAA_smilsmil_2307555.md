## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 24, 1886.

Warm & very pleasant. Up late —
up to about 9 — after that Charl. &
I went to Parke's to consult about
changes under way in dining room
of his house. It was after 1 when
I got back to the house & to work
on Breton Etching. Dinner after
1 — worked on etching until I —
then made two or three experi-
ments in photographing — Tea
at 7 — after that Charl. & I went
down town — I spent some time
with Cooley, settling up bills —
near 9 when we got home — very
warm eve'g. — Read newspaper
& to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 25. 1886.

Overcast m'f., clearing to be warm &
pleasant. Got up with a head ache
& was more or less under the weather
all day. Charl. found a defect in my
Soda mixture which explained much
of my photographing trouble so far. Some
time was spent in experiments. Mr.
& Mrs. Parke called — I took a short
ride with Mr. P. — & got to my etching
at about 11. Worked with little spirit
spoiling part of what I did. Dinner
after 1 — again some etching — & about
4 Charl. & I took our cameras & went
up to Warner's lot & fired away at some
cows — then home & I developed some
of the plates & proved more failures —
Tea about 7 — later Parke came up &
took Annie, Mr. White & Charl. to S°. Montrose
creamery — I went to P.O. then home & had
a cutter game with Mr. W. — The others
came home about 9 o'clk — To bed
soon after 10.

Coyle did not work.
