## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 16. 1886.

Mild, pleasant day — Again late
in getting to studio — tried to do
something in the way of getting
etching plt. in press — Home to
lunch — went to Bank for Annie.
After 3 when I got to studio —
Nettie's feeling far from well. I
got some of my last summer's
etching plt. into press — At 5.30
walked to 74th St. & back by B'way
stopping in at Wunderlich's — saw
Parish's etchings. To dinner at
6 — Wetf. mounting photographs
— last summer's work — Abt 9.30
went to Charl's dark room — met
him there & "burnished" the plt. —
mounted — then home & worked
until 12 mounting more —

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 17. 1886.

Nasty day — muggy & wet — In
the mtg. after working awhile
with etching plt. I got started
on a drawing for A.B.N.Co. — A
well, with figures, city of Mexico —
from photos. Afternoon, worked
on it until 3.30 — then walked
down to 57 W. 10th S. & attended
Bd. of Control — Water Color Socy —
meeting — There until 5.30 — then
walked up home. Raining —
Wetf. did not go to Water Color
meeting — Annie had a fall on
the stair this p.m. — & under
the circumstances we are worried.
I fussed with photos until 10
— then to bed.
