## Left Page

### November, Thursday 18. 1886.

A m't of high wind, driving showers
& some thunder — At home until near
10 — then went to Dr. Blackwell's in 20'
s. To consult her about Annie's condition.
She was in bed, unwell but I waited for
half an hour until she could see me —
after some time with her (when she advised
that for the present A. give up her proposed
visit to Cambridge) I had to wait longer
until the storm of rain should lift a little.
It was near noon when I got to Studio.
Worked on A.B.N. Co. drawing — "Well in
the City of Mexico". Home at 1 — then town
cleared — p.m. bright & breezy. Worked on
A.B.N. drawing as long as I could see —
then to the Private View of Munkaczy's
"Christ before Pilate" — a large crowd
before an impressive picture but it is
not a pleasant one to me — black, opaque,
heavy. Eve'g. hung up pictures
in the parlor — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 19. 1886.

Breezy day. Worked at Studio
on Mexican Well — feeling better —
the first day since my return from
Montrose that I have enjoyed
working. Got my composition
of figures metered ready for india
ink — Before 5 went down to Century
& wrote notes to Parke. Champlin.
Wile — then home to dinner.
Eve'g. until 11 o'clk mounting
Photos. Then to bed.
