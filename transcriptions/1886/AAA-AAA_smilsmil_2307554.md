## Left Page

### August, Sunday 22, 1886

Brilliant day — we all, old & young — Whites, Smillies and
childrens, spent the morning sauntering over the hills. I
took my camera with me &
took half a dozen snaps of
the childrens, some cows &
some wood bits — Home at
1.30 — dinner soon after 2
another telegram from Charl
dated Buffalo. Afternoon
wrote to mother at 7 throus &
to Aunt H. at 6 o'k. Eve'g
in quiet with the Whites —
to bed at 10.30 —

## Right Page

### August, Monday 23, 1886

Still charming weather — worked
on Breton etching — After dinner
a 3°. telegram from Charl. from
Elmira — asking me to send to M-
depot for him at 3 o'clk. Sent Bree
after him at once. Worked on
etching until after 6 — Charl did
not arrive as we had hoped he
might before tea time — Eve'g,
spent some time pacing the Piazza
expecting Charl to arrive about 9
o'clk. p. 7.32 train — As he did not
come at 10 o'clk. I went down town
& while there he came on New
Milford Stage — We talked over
his trip, photography & my dark
room until 11.30 — then to bed
