## Left Page

### August, Friday 6, 1886

Pleasant day opening with a dense fog. Seifus & Herrick again most
of the day at work upon studio cistern. Coyle not at work. I got
to work early on Johnson plate
& put in a long days work —
In the afternoon I took a proof
& was encouraged altho' there
is much work yet to be done on
it. Went down town at 6. Tea
before 7 — Every. about 9 o'clk
all joined in a game of
Bazique — To bed at —

Sent to Koehler the K. F. Y. p[illegible].
of Bridgman plate.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 7, 1886

Heavy fog & dubious weather in
the m[illegible] much to the disappointment of the girls who had hoped
to go on a Picnic to Silver Lake.
As it did not rain Parker
came & they compromised on
a long ride that took all of the
m[illegible]. I started after 12½ on
my plate, printing & working.
Taking in the course of the day
11 plain prints & 4 fals. p[illegible]. I
think the plate so near finished
that I shall send one of the p[illegible]
to Koehler. It comes much
coarser than I thought it would
my line is too clear & strong —
Went down town after tea — on
my way up stopped at Hunt
H's — She had her auction sale
this p.m — This m[illegible]. I suppose
[illegible] started for his Ranches in
Wyoming — To bed before 10.
