## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 27, 1886.

unt morning mts. Morning dull
& rainy — but by noon it cleared
charmingly — my time divided
between my work & the carpenters.
A telegram was rec'd from the girls
saying that they had started on the
1 o'clk. Train from NY. much of the
afternoon was spent with Smith
getting second coat of paint on the
cherry room ceiling & with Annie
getting the rooms in order for the
girls — At about 14 before 5 started
for the depot — Katie & Mollie Adams
— Catherine Cook arr'd on the 7.32
train. Wm Park went west on the
same train. We had a pleasant
ride getting home a little after
9 — a light supper & to bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 28, 1886.

Very warm clear day — for some time
after bkfst — with carpenters — After 9
when I got started down town &
after 10 when I got back to my
work with graver on the Johnson
etching. Dinner about 1 & soon
after 2 with Smith putting a 2d. coat
of paint on ceiling of cherry room —
about 4 when I got back to my
work — dry-point on faces — stuck to
it until about 6.30 — then tea —
Annie used up with a head ache
Geo. Lathrop's called with Mrs. Morse
& her daughter who brought a note
of introduction from Walter Cook —
Evg. very still & tried — on piazza
for some time with the girls.
To bed at 10.30.
