## Left Page

### August, Monday 30, 1886

*Annie went to N.Y.*

Up at 4.30 & at 5.30 started with A.
to Montrose depot where she took
the 6.55 train to N.Y. expecting to go on
to Boston to-night. The mty. was quite
cool & cloudy — Back home before 9 —
feeling very much used up — I was
unwell during the night — The
Whites got away at 9.30 — I was
then so sick that I went to bed
where I spent most of the rest
of the day. Heavy rain about
7 P.M. which continued thro'
most of the night. Chard &
I passed the evg. reading
newspapers — To bed soon
after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 31, 1886

A cool, foggy, smoky day. the
ther. getting down to 62°. Up late
Help — after 8 o'clk — Spent most
of the mty. in big chair unable
to do anything. Part of the
afternoon on my bed after which
I felt somewhat better — Got
my pkg of Johnson [illegible] — from
K. & V. off to Kashler. After tea
I was again very uncomfortable.
wrote a note to Annie & after
it was mailed rec'd one from
Annie. To bed about 10.
