## Left Page

### October, Friday 15, 1886

Bleak — high winds & spitting rain with intervals of sunshine — Growing colder at evef. Spent much of the day with the man at work on fence & stone walls — Herrick working at board fence. Then worked on drawing of north chimney awhile. Down town at noon — showed Dolittle the best of Char's photos which I ret. to him by 4½ in the afternoon. Scott & a helper came with team and commenced work on new ploughed ground gathering stones. I walked down town with Annie at 4.30 — getting caught in a light, spiteful shower — Evef. — looking over old letters —

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 16, 1886

Very cold, cloudy & windy — flurries of snow in the mf. Scott & his man would not work — it was so cold. The Ther. went down to 30° last night & was at 32° at 8 o'clk. I was with Herrick part of mf. in S.W. bed room putting in mouldings & making arch for setting the brick arch of new fire place. Box of tiles for new fire place came from Walter & I unpacked them — after dinner worked on drawings for new chimney — about 5 went out with Annie & had a walk — back at 5.30 — Evef. very quiet, cold & brilliant moonlight. Mended some things with Straliria — Read newspaper — a game of cards and to bed before 10.
