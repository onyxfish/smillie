## Left Page

### September, Friday 17, 1886

A muggy, warm mtg. Heavy thunder
showers in the afternoon - at one
time, a deluge of rain that came
in at my open studio bay window
doing some damage. I had
a day of close work in the mtg
on Breton plates. During the
darkness of the showers I did
some work in the photo - dark
room - I hoped that I had
finished the plates but my last
imp. at 5 o'clk. discouraged me.
Eveg. after newspaper reading
Annie, Eliz, & I had a game
of cards - To bed soon after
10.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 18, 1886

Delightful day - Spent most
of the mtg. out of doors with the
men as I did not feel able to
work. Old Mr Hotchkiss hanging
walk on the Hef. Tyler line - went
down town in the mtg & spent
some time in Dolittle's gallery
seeing some of my negatives
printed - Dinner at 1. After
that made a little sketch in
blk & white of a derrick for F.B.
N. Co. Finished it at 5 o'clk. &
went down to F.P. office and
sent it to N.Y. Then went on a
walk over the Beebe hill with
Annie & Eliz. Tea at 6.30 -
Eveg. a little work in dark room
- then reading - then a game
of cards with A. & E. - To bed
soon after 10.
