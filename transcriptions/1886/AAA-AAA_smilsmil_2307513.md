## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 1. 1886.

Bright & pleasant. Got to studio
about 9.30 & finished etching in
an hour or two — then prepared
acid & got my plate into it. At
about 1 o'clk. Dr. Martin came in
to ask me to put a little sketch
in his Austin Dobson book — then
to Bank for Annie & home ab't 1.45 —
Emma J. & mother Cook to lunch —
Fanny K. came in before we had
finished & later, Belle & Many [illegible]
came. It was after 3 before I
got to studio — biting the rest of
the afternoon — Home at 6.45 —
dinner — Welf. Geo. came in —
To bed soon after 10..

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 2. 1886.

Overcast — It was about 10 when
I got to studio — Worked on Bridg-
man etching until 1 o'clk. — when
I finished the biting — then home
to lunch — back to studio at 3 —
cleaned plate & soon after 4, at
Kimmel & Voigt's & got imp's. from
plate — much encouraged by its
appearance — Back to studio at
5 — tacked down imp's. Home to
dinner at 6.30 — Welf. Mr. &
Mrs. Saml. Colman came in —
— also Edw'd Cook & bro. Charles.
All left before 10 & we got to
bed soon after.
