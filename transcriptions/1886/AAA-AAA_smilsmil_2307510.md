## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 26, 1886.

A march & April day — cold, windy
& showery — Got up with a wretched
headache that made me good for
but little all day — To Studio
before 10 & fussed there after
making up a fire — the ther. being
at 60°. Uncle Willie made a long
call — about 1. went to horsefountain
in Mad Sq. with Charle's Camera &
made two exposures — then home
to lunch — back to studio at 2.30
& to K.+V.'s soon after 4. when I
had some more satisfactory pfs
for the Homer [illegible] — To Studio at
6 — then home — to dinner —
Books from P.'k. am². Evef
Charl came in & at 8.30 went
with him to dark-Room, but
my plates developed nothing —
the fault of the plates, Charl said.
Home at 10 & to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 27, 1886.

Rain until after 10 — cloudy after
that. Got to studio at 9.30 — Went
out with camera to get two rain views.
Then to work on the Bridgman etching
until 12.30 — then to Bank & deposited
Cassell check for Homer plate — Home
to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Etched
until after 5 — then to Newman's — and
a new building cor. Bdway & 33d. St, to see
about studios — then to Station 7 in 3d
Ave. & then home to dinner. Evef
Will. Park & his br. Max. called —
later, Charl came in — they all
went away at 10 — To bed before 11.
