## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 15, 1886

A lovely m'f., clear & mild —
after b'k'f't. I spent some time
with the men who are plough-
ing the cow pasture — Trouble in
getting the stone around to build
a wall on line between Tyler
& self — then with Smith, painting
ceiling of S.W. bed room — at last
got to my own work — rebiting
a Breton plate — It was nearly
6 when I cleaned the plate.
Afternoon overcast — rain at
night — Annte gave up to
a headache just before tea
time & I put him to bed —
S'elf. amused myself for a
time looking over m'ts. from my
photos. Taken by Dolette & rec-
this m'f. Then wrote a letter
to Charl — to bed before 10.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 16, 1886

Heavy rain during the night — the
m'f was misty until 11 when again
heavy rain for an hour — Pettus worked
at ploughing part of the day — Coyle
worked in cellar part of the day —
I had fire in the etching room
& put in a good long days work
on Breton plate — Burnished all
the m'f. — & took a nip. at noon.
Afternoon, worked until 6 o'clk.
with dry point. Elig. Howe
came on stage about 5 o'clk.
After tea I went down town —
Ev'e't. Chatting until 10 o'clk.
