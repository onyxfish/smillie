## Left Page

### April, Friday 16, 1886

Tried hard to be a pleasant or unpleasant day — I don't know which — & did not succeed. It was after 10 o'clk when I got to studio. Annie having had a severe colic & sickness before she had her [illegible] from which she recovered about 9.30. I etched on the Homer. Meta paint Adams made a long call at the studio — Home at 1.30 — 3 when I got back — etched closely until after 5 — then to Gradey's & ordered frame for Cleff Farmilland — also, frame for Park's [illegible] of "Rough Sport" — Home at 6.30 — dinner — Evey wrote to Koehler. Got to bed soon after 9.30.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 17, 1886

Overcast [illegible], clearing very pleasantly — Ten o'clk. when I got to studio — got to biting in the Homer etching as soon as I could & stuck to it closely all the [illegible] — Home at 1.45 — about 3 when I got back — Worked until 6 but did not get the biting finished — Charl came in bet. 5 & 6 with inventories of Father's estate for me to sign. I got home to dinner at 6.45 every. Read awhile to Annie & wrote some Diet Kitchen Receipts.
