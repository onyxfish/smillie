## Left Page

### December, Monday 20, 1886

Splitting headache in the
mrg. — less fever which gradually
subsided during the day — about
11 I took half a bottle of Citrate
of Magnesia — the result it
about 3 & later rec'd reports —
was able to sit in chair for
a little while in the eveg

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 21, 1886

mrg. Yale once more pronounced
temperature as normal — but
I suffered a good deal from
headache — About 4 o'clk p.m.
I got into easy chair & passed
the rest of day & eveg. there
before the parlor fire.
