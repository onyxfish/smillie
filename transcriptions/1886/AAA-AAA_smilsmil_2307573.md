## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 29, 1886

Brilliant & very cool day — All the
m't. with Herrick superintending
cutting out on north side of house
for the new chimney — Mr B
commenced work as helper to Mr.
Hobchkiss — Down town before noon
early dinner — Then Annie & Elis.
went to D.L.&W. Depot for John
Downs. I worked upon chimney
plans — went down town & learned
that the chimney brick had been
sent by D.L.&W. R.R. on to Scranton
from Binghamton & around to Tunkhan-
nock & thence by little road to town
with a bill for freight of $28.80. I
declined the brick. When I got to the
house Downs had arr'd. Ev'g. for a
time I worked on chimney plans —
then a game of dominos which —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 30, 1886

Another very bright day. Went
down town about 10 a.m. after
some messages got at house of
Smith, a big horse & a little pie-
bald pony & at 11 went up to the
house & with Annie, Jim, Sid &
Jack Downs we went off & rode
until afternoon — Dinner at 1.30 —
then a rest — Cogle came in the
m't. — had him dig down to the
drain at the cor. of the house. Mr.
& Mrs. Parke came — P. & I walked
over our cow lot, he talking about
putting up a new building — At
6 went down town. Ev'g. fin-
ished plans for drainage im-
provements to be sent to Mr.
Howard White — three cards
& to bed at 10.30.
