## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 21, 1886.

Charming day altho' rather cool
my head still roaring — much
of the day occupied with my
Breton proofs & getting a [illegible]
made up with pfs & photo-
which in the afternoon I sent
by Exp. to Mr. Marion. I spent
some time with the man at
work clearing in cow pasture &
laying stone walls. I went
down town twice. In the after-
noon, while we were all away
Mr. & Mrs. Jas. Carmalt called —
then Parke brought a carriage
load of ministers — Mr. Reed &
Miss Hyle called. Evety; more
cards & to bed about 10.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 22, 1886.

Raw & chilly — most of the time
overcast. All the a.f. at work
taking ten pfs from the Breton
plate — the afternoon I spent
about the place with Pettus,
Hotchkiss & couple. Paerne played
me a trick again & did not come
about 5 o'clk. I went down
town & attended to messages
saw Gile & made preliminary
arrangements towards putting
up a chimney on the north side
of the house. Evety, meets
8.30 signing Bridgman pfs.
for Cassell & Co. Rec'd telegram
from Scribners approving Breton
plate, except sky, which is
thought to be too flat.

Coyle worked.
