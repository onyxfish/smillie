## Left Page

### August, Thursday 12, 1886

A delightful day — warm but breezy — Spent the mtf. Taking
pts from Johnson Plate & am
much gratified to find it so
greatly improved. Dinner
a little before 1 — Abt. 30 Annie
went to Montrose Depot & at
4.30 Ret'd with Mr. &Mrs. Howard
White & their two childrens —
Dr. Muelford made a call on
me in the afternoons — Still
feeling out of sorts I did some
loafing — Tea before 7 — After
it W. White & I went down
town & soon back — on Piazza
until 9 — wrote to [illegible] &
to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 13, 1886

Pleasant day — Spent the day
proving Johnson Plate — feeling
pretty well used up — I do not
wait to hear from Eastman Johnson
because time is too short and
I hope that he will approve.
At 4:30 o'clk Mr. &Mrs. White, Annie
& I went out riding — the wind
blew briskly & a blue haze hung
over the hills so that we could
not see far — but withal, we had
a pleasant ride — Home at 6.30,
tea about 7 — Even'g — while Annie
read aloud to us part of a Stockton
story I finished the signing of
the Homer proofs — then a walk
at the Piazza — to bed at 10.30 —

A short & very heavy rain storm
abt. 1.30 A.M.
