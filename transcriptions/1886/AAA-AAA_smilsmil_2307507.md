## Left Page

### May, Thursday 20, 1886

Pleasant mtg., then overcast &
clear again at evg. Got to
studies before 10 & spent the
mrg. with my etching plp., in
attending to bus. matters and
writing notes. It was after 1
when I went to the Bank & 1.45
when I got home to lunch
— 2.45 when I got to K. & V.'s —
where I was for an hour & a half
superintending the taking of
some plp. of the Homer plate.
Then to studio & for an hour
tacking down wet plp. Home
to dinner at 6.45. Our new
waitress has got through her
first day nicely. Eve 4, some
sweat showers — wrote to
Herrick — Cooley & Treas.
of Essex Co. N.Y. To bed at
10.30. About 12.30 June
bloomed as a Riot.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 21, 1886

Brilliant day — about 10 when
I got to studios — wasted more time
over etching plp. Koehler came
in & spent some time. & then
insurance agent — with policies
for Bridgeman & Johnston pictures.
It was 1 o'clk. when I went
to Haas's drug store — & 1.30 when
I got home to lunch — 2.45 when
I got back to studio — 4.30 when
I went to K & V's to arrange for
another visit to experiment with
proving Homer plate — then to 13
Baxter St. & ordered copper plates
for Geo. Shark. Home at 6.15 — dinner
— wrote a note to Belle & Tuesday —
Charl came in for a few minutes —
went with Annie to her Mother's —
walked up to 47th St. with him.
Ret'd for Annie — Saw Dr. Adams
& Nelie — Home at 10 —
