## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 4, 1886.

Bright wulf., hazy & overcast afternoon. Got to studio before
10 & spent the wulf. working
on Homer plate. Some graver
& dry point, but mostly with
burnisher — Many came in,
having spent Sunday with
Nette Sutphen & Monday night
with Tom Shearman & wife.
Home to lunch at 2 — About
3.30 Rose up to Met. Museum
of Art with Annie & Mary — A.
did not go in but after sitting
awhile in the Park. Ed came home.
Mary & I went in — Private View —
8° [illegible]. Soct of Am. Artists. We
left at 5 — I went to studio &
wet down some paper — home
at 6.45 — dinner — Wulf. a
little reading & to bed before
10.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 5, 1886.

Bright & very warm — In the
afternoon, a heavy shower with
thunder — Many went home in the
10.30 A.M. train. I got to studio
about 9.30 & spent the wulf.
taking up plt from Homer plate
the condition of which discourages
me — One portion has too much
color — the other portion has not
enough. 1.30 when I got home
to lunch — Annie out with her
mother & did not get in until
after 3 — I waited for her, considerably
worried. Then to studio — Charl
& Ed came in — I worked until
after 6. with graver, dry point and
charcoal. To dinner at 6.45
Wulf. Read a little. To bed
at 10.
