## Left Page

### September, Saturday 25, 1886

A very warm, pleasant day —
up late feeling wretched after
my night's experiences — very
lame in the back & all over —
Went down town about 10 — to
Doolittle's & got photos. imps. then
got teams — drove up to the house
with Eliz., Annie & Jim —
went off & rode until 1 o'cl.
Then dinner — after that I
had a nap — In the afternoon
I tried a little photographing
but the light is getting to be
too weak. Eveg. Mr. & Mrs.
Parker called. I read aloud
a Harker article — the noise
in my head continues very
distressing.

Payne's bro. worked with the
team — Patters & team & Hotchkiss
at work. Coyne not at work.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 26, 1886

Overcast, windy & warm with
frequent showers of fine driving
rain — I spent the day in
lounging about feeling far from
well — In the afternoon I wrote
to Kate & to Charl. Evening
I pursed awhile with a plan
of detail for mouldings for ceiling
[illegible] S.W. bed room — To bed
about 10.30.
