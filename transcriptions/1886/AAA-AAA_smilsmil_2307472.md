## Left Page

### March, Thursday 11, 1886

Pleasant day — to studio at 9. 30
& frittered away the rest trying
to get things straightened up a
little — fussed for some time
with the Rough Spot [illegible] that
I took yesterday — Stretched
outline on paper, Union Stone Co's
Bldg. (sent up by Will). Uncle
Willie came in — just from Montreal
Home at 1.30 & back at 2.30 —
Worked closely on Stone Bldg
until after 5 — then a short walk.
Home at 6.30 — dinner — Svey
Annie & I went to Morgandale [illegible]
books — I wanted a copy of Wilson's
catalogue — worth from $15 to $20 — it
sold for $1.00 — an example of
the way many of the books sold.
Home soon after 10 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 12, 1886

overcast with some showers in the
middle of the day — to Studio at 9.30
& to work on Union Stone Co's bldg, Cen.
6. & finished it before 12 — then I
worked on my Rough Spot etching
for nearly an hour with burnisher
& rough emery paper, Reducing all
the distance & much of the central
group to give greater brilliancy to
the black horse. Home to lunch at
1 — at 2 a. & I went to Phil. [illegible]
— did it enjoy it much — back to
studio at 4.30 & again worked for
an hour on Rough Spot — then home
— rested for half an hour — then,
with Annie, to the Cooks where
we dined with W. & Mrs. Howard
White. Home soon after 10 &
right to bed.
