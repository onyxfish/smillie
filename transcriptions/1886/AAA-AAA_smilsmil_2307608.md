## Left Page

### November, Sunday 28, 1886

Very pleasant day — sat about at home until after 11. Then went out with Jim — first to the R. C. Cathedral — Fifth Ave. 450′. S. where we listened awhile to music then on to 55th S. Spent some time on the bridges at G. C. Depot watching the engines — Dinner at 2 — Charl came in about 4 + wish him I walked down to studios — Tea at 6.30 — Charl with us. Evely, Geo. & Nellie came in — I went to Walter's with rough plans of the Montrose Studio's interior for him to make drawings — home at 9.30. Wrote to Mary — Geo. & Nellie came in to see Mother. I went to Walter's with my diagrams of interior of Montrose Studio — back at 9.30 — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 29, 1886

Very pleasant day — To studio at 9.30 — worked awhile on A.B.N. Co's drawings — then put them aside as finished + fussed the rest of the m'f away over lots of little things of no importance — Home to lunch — Mother & Kate at Geo's. Back at 2.30 — Young man from A.B.N. Co. for drawings — until after 4 cutting mats & mounting etching [illegible] — then a rest — then to 4th S. & home at 6 by way of Bdwy & 34th S. Evely, Charl came in & stayed until near 9 — I read awhile & then for some time getting back files of Lond. Ill. News — Punch — Harper's & Century Mag. in order & into place — To bed at 10.30.
