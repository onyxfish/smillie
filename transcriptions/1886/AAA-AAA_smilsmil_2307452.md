## Left Page

### January, Saturday 30, 1886

The wonderful nasty weather continues with slight variation. To-day there has been an almost continuous fall of slushy snow. Got up with a headache that grew a little better — To studio at 10 — Then to N.A. where I spent the rest of the m'f. Annie, the Adams girls & Emily Cook came soon after — I met many acquaintances — Home at 1 & after lunch went to bed & had a sound sleep until 4. Then to Studio & worked there until 6 — Then home — Eve'g Annie read aloud from Adam Bede until 10 — Then a game of cards — After 11 when I got to bid.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 31, 1886

And yet another disagreeable day — a little struggling sunlight but more of rain & cloudy weather — Late [illegible] — and the m'f spent in lounging — about noon went out for a walk — brought up at studio & got some paste & brushes & then home before 2 — Then dinner — Between 3 & 5 o'clk. had a sound nap. Edw'd & Mary Cook came in for a little while about 6 — at 6.30 had tea — Eve'g wrote note of condolence to A. A. Anderson — a note to S. P. Avery & my letter to mother — Took letter to G. C. D. about 10.30 — Then read Koehler's "Etching" until after 11, & to bed a little before midnight.
