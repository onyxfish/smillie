## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 28. 1886.

Very pleasant day — To studio before
10 & all day on Homer etching
with graver & dry point — To lunch
& back as usual — worked until
5 — Then to A. A. A. & saw Kirby —
arranged with him to have my
battered frame repaired & got
copy of Ill. Morgan Cat. — Home at
6.30 — Fred Smillie & Chaре with
us at dinner — Even'g. with Fred
looked over the portfolio of Jattin's
engravings left to Fred & which
he took away with him soon after
10 — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 29. 1886.

Overcast & rather raw w'f.
Bright afternoon — To studio before
10 — laid ground on Homer plate
& got ready to etch in background
of cliff but my head felt so very
wretched that at last I gave it
up & went over to N.A. where I
met Mary & the children — In
about half an hour ret'd — To studio
with them — A call from Mr.
Gilbert of Portland Me. who kept
me until 1.30 — Then home to lunch
— After that I gave up to my head-
ache & went to bed & slept until
6 — Ate a little at dinner & in
the even'g. went with Mary to
the Choral Club concert which
we enjoyed very much —
Home & to bed at 11 o'clk.
