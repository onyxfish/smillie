## Left Page

### January, Monday 18, 1886.

Bright for a short time in the
mfg — then overcast — Suffered
headache all day — To Studio
at usual time & worked on my
ph. of big etching — touching it
with india ink after the original
drawing — then at work on the
plate with drypoint — At noon
went to N.A. taking my drawing of
Rough Sport — then with Wood to
Safe Deposit — cor. 6th Ave & 23rd S. +
there met Elliman & Baldwin —
we looked over W.C. Socy Bank books
& securities — Then at 1 I went to
2d. Nat. Bank & made a deposit for
Annie — home to lunch — Back at
2.30 & worked with dry point on
big plate until near 5 — Took
a walk up to 50th St & 5 ave & home
to dinner at 6.15 — Eve'g. Annie
Read aloud from Adam Bede —
I suffering from my stupefying
headache — To bed before
10.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 19, 1886.

Several inches of snow fell
during the night followed by
heavy rain during the day —
& sudden clearing about sun-
down — Got up in the morning
after an uneasy night with a
heavy head that so increased
in discomfort that at last I
gave up & went back to my
bed where I passed the rest
of the mfg. Got up at 1 o'clk
& had a little to eat — on sofa
until 3 when I went to N.A.
& saw Farrer for a few minutes
then to Studio — found there W.
Elliman's books, papers & Report.
Worked for awhile on my big
etching — then home before 6.
Will & Fanny K. did not come
to dinner as expected — Eve'g.
until 9 analyzing W.C. Society
accts with a view to economies.
Then Annie read aloud Adam
Bede until 10.25 — then to bed.
