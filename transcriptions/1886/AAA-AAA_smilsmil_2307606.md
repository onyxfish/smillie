## Left Page

### November, Friday 26, 1886

Bright, breezy & cool — 9.30 when
I got to studio — after a little time
spent with pf. of etchings I got to
work on drawing for A. B. N. Co. — A
mexican woman in boat with market
vegetables. Enlarged it by squares
from small photo. — Home to lunch
soon after 1. Marion Wright then Jamie
Mr. Jelke & Miss Toshay lunched
with us. Back to studio at 2.30
& worked on woman in boat as
long as I could see. Then to [illegible]
to leave something for some missing
pictures — then to a furniture place
in 23d S. & looked at some parlor
tables. Home to dinner at 6 —
Eve'g., worked for awhile on rough
plans of Montrose studio for Walter.
Charl was in for an hour or more.
To bed before 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 27, 1886

Very pleasant day — To studio
at usual time — after a little time
working with etching pf. I set to
work on Mexican woman with gar-
den Truck in boat, for A. B. N. Co. —
To lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 &
worked as long as I could see —
nearly finishing drawing. Then
walked down to 14th S. — then by
Bowery to 45th S. & home — dinner
Eve'g. Kate went out with
Charl. — I finished the rough
diagrams of Montrose studio
interior — To bed about 10.30.
