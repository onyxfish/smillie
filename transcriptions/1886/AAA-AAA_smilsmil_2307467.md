## Left Page

### March, Monday 1, 1886

Again, very clear, cold & windy.
To studio about 9.30 — then to N.A. &
got W.C. "Rough Sport". Ret. to studio
& made changes in white horse's
head — then took it & a pkg. of etching
to frame makers & had him fix
them up to send to their respective
purchasers — Back to studio & made
pen sketches after "In Harbor Islands,
Lake George" for Chas. Kurtz's notes —
then to Bank & deposited A.B.N.
Co's check for $75 — Home to lunch
at 1.20 — back at 2.30 — Finished pen
sketch — wrote notes — boxed big
Rosa Bonheur print "Lions at Home"
etc — etc — Home at 6.30 — dinner —
Evef. Kate went to bed early
& Chas. Collins came & chatted
with us until 10.30 — To bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 2, 1886

Still very cold with high wind —
the amt. of dirt that flies through
the streets is maddening — To studio
before 10 — Painted in two small
figures in 15×24 "on Harbor Islands"
— then to work on "Summer Sea on
the coast of France" — painting delicately
inner sky & sea — Home to lunch
after going to Bank & depositing
(no I did it — that was done yes-
terday) Annie out to cooking class.
Back to studio & again worked
over a portion of 15×24 — Home at
6 — dinner — at 8, Annie, Kate
& J went to Thomas Popular Concert
at Academy of Music — Home at
10.30 & to bed at 11.
