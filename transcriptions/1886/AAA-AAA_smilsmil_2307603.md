## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 8, 1886

Beautiful day — a few minutes after 10 when I got to Studio — To work at once on Old Mill at Crécy —
Home at 1 — back at 2.30 — worked until after 4 — then Yewell came & until 5 I worked on a plate for him showing him how to use the dry point. Geo. gave me Mr. Walter D.F. Day's check for $10 — for a france p[late] of "Glass with the Savin." At 5 went to Newman's & bought a coffee pot — home at 6.
About 6.30 Mr. Wm. Parke & her daughter Bessie arr. from Montrose —
Dinner at near 7 — Lively in yawning conversation — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 9, 1886

Another charming day overhead & horrible underfoot. To studio at 10.
Found Chas. Collins there awaiting for me — came to buy a [illegible] of the Winslow Homer etching — Spent part of m[orning] fixing up some of the Homer [plates]. To lunch at 1 —
back at 2.30 — nearly all of the afternoon working on some Blk. & white drawings of Lake Geo. — made for the Harpers some years ago — Sent ph. of Johnston plate to Mr. Walter DeForrest Day —
Home at 6. Wm. Parke arr. soon after — Then Charl — dinner at 7 — Lib. Home sick — [illegible] Mr. Sprague & Mary came in —
To bed before 11.
