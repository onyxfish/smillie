## Left Page

### February, Thursday 11, 1886

Heavy wind & rain all day —
Edith set? to Phila by 4 P.m. From
I got to studio before 10 & spent
a little time stretching more
Rough S/pot p/p. Mr. Bertie Beecher
came in with some of her Oregon oil
sketches — I worked on Uncle
Willie's Int. Vrgf. Home abt 30
back at 2.45 & worked as long
as I could see, altho' I was
interrupted by a great leak that
developed in the S.E. cor. of my
room & obliged me to move
everything away. Home at
6 — dinner — Eve'g. Read awhile
then wrote notes to W.H. Hamilton
Eastman, Johnson — con Pate &
my neighbor Mr. Rob't F. Shepard.
It was after 11 when I got
to bed..

## Right Page

### February, Friday 12, 1886

A nasty day, drizzle & mist with
occasional heavy showers — Got to
studio before 10 & found that a
new leak had come in at the cor-
ner, dripping upon the corner of my
book case & running in up on the
books — my drawing for Uncle ll.
was spattered so that I had to
spend some time working over
it — At first, thought it was ruined —
one of my best "Colored Neighbor" p/p
was ruined. Wrote letter & made
the Montreal Vrgf. into a p/stg. & took
it to Nat. Illo. Co. Home at 1 — lunch
at 2. A + I went to Phil. Rehearsal —
I enjoyed the Wagner immensely —
To studio at 4.15. Stretched 3 p/p. /
Rough S/pot — Home soon after 6 —
Eve'g Read Hocbles "Etching"
To bed soon after 10.
