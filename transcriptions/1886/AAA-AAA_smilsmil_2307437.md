## Right Page

### January, Friday 1, 1886

A beautiful day in brilliant contrast to yesterday — Jim again out of sorts this m't. but better later —
got to studio at 10 & commenced taking p'ps of Pasini — Ink that
I got from H. & V. would not work right on fab. paper — & so I wasted
the m't. Chas. Collins came in & at 1 I walked up town with
him — Bro. Chas. also came to lunch — back at 2.30 — ground
ink which proved satisfactory & I worked all the afternoon until
4.30 — getting 5 p'ps. Then washed up — home — dinner at 6 — Early
before we went to bed Annie & I indulged in the novelty
of a game of cards — To bed at 10.30.
