## Left Page

### December, Thursday 2, 1886

Windy — growing colder until at night it was a nipper. To studio before 10 — Another day wasted over the etchings — all the m'f spent getting them into the frames & making cards to put into the corners — It was 2 o'clk. before I got home to lunch. Afternoon, wrote a note to M. Geo. U. Curtis — at 5 went to his house 16 W. 53. & left note & pf. of the Johnson plate — then to Eastman Johnson's — 65 W. 55 — & got his autograph up on three of the pls. Saw Mrs. J. Had a pleasant chatty call. — Home at 6.30. Eve. tried to write a letter to Uncle Willie — felt very stupid — Kate went out with Charl — I got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 3, 1886

Very cold, raw & disagreeable — Got up suffering from a headache. To studio at 10 — Some time spent putting room in order, then more time looking for sketches made some time ago from Governor's Island — Got material together for A.B.U. Co's drawing Statue of Liberty & Hff. City — Home at 1 — after lunch so used up by my headache that I went to bed & slept for the rest of the p.m. while Kate went with Annie to Phil. Rehearsal. — Swelf lying about, Geo. & Charl came in and chat. Collins called — To bed about 11.
