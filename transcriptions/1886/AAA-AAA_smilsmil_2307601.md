## Left Page

### December, Monday 6, 1886

Peasant day — altho' still cold
a + Raw — nearly 10 when I got to
studio — For some time looking
over Sketch books — Selected sketch
of Old Mill at Crécy en Brie and
made rapid outline upon paper
× + at 11.30 after wetting up
my box of colors, commenced the
washes of color upon it — Home
at 1 — Matthew + Kate returned
to Po'k. on 3.30 train, Charl seeing
them off — I got to studio at 2.45
+ worked on my drawing until
after 4, getting it all washed in —
Then Jewell came in + stayed for
some time — wrote note to Mr. Pinch[illegible]
Home at 6 — Evelf. Read aloud
for awhile from Dana's "Two Years
before the Mast" — To bed about
10.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 7, 1886

Another storm of snow + wind —
To studio a little before 10 and
right to work on my old Mill
Crécy — stuck to it until 1 —
Then home to lunch + back at
2.45 + worked as long as I
could see — leaving the picture
in an unsatisfactory condition —
cold + hard in color — walked
up to Grady's at 5 + Paid the
bal against me — $22.90 — Then
home to dinner — Evelf. wrote
some notes + looked over English
papers — a game of cards with
Annie + to bed about.
