## Left Page

### November, Saturday 20, 1886

Charming day - 10 o'clk. before I
got to Studio - then to work on
"Well in Mexico". Annie lunched
with Mary Sprague Cook - to meet
Margaret Parker White - I lunched
solus at home. Nearly finished draw-
ing in the afternoon - about 5 Charl
came in - we found Piepotto & two
sons in George's Studio - then with Charl
to Keating's where I got new overcoat.
Then other messages & shoppings -
Home at 6 - Charl dined with us -
About 8 we went to his dark room
where he "burnished" a lot of my
mounted photos. At 8 I went to
"Century" where a reception was given
to Munkacsy - an informal affair -
A short welcome - speech from Hunting[ton]
& no reply - then hand shakings - a few
etchings after his paintings & three of his
pictures on the walls - a sudden - at
11 I left for home & got to bed before
12.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 21, 1886

Very nice & pleasant day.
A late b'k'f't - & Reading during
the m't. Just as Annie, Jim
& I were starting to go up to St.
Gale's he came in & we walked
up to his house with him - Saw
his baby - Mrs. Y - just recovering from
a serious attack of Pneumonia -
Dinner at 2 - Loafing until
near 5 - Charl came in - went
with A & J - to Mother Cook's -
Then Charl & I walked to 64th
St. & home by 5th Ave. Did
not take any supper. Wrote
home - & to Ehninger &
Herrick - To bed at 10.30.
