## Left Page

### October, Saturday 9, 1886

Continued delightful weather
B.kp. about 8 at 9. with the
Ehningers went over to the little R.R.
depot & saw them started on their
way to Geneva. Then home — with the
men for awhile — then, with Emma, Annie
& Jim, took painting traps — went out to a
place a short distance back of the house
& worked closely until after 4 on a
sketch in oil — after dinner, a rest
then again with colors went to the
air grounds sketch that I made
yesterday — worked until after 5.
Eve'g, some times in dark room
developing two photos. Taken in the
afternoon — & washing brushes. To bed
about 10.30. Very mild night.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 10, 1886

Still delicious weather — A late
b.kp. — about the grounds for some
time with Annie — then wrote a
letter to Howard White about our
drainage — also one to Chas. —
awhile in hammock before dinner
at 2. Mr. Parke dined with us —
his wife & children are now from home
— he left at 4 — I wrote part of a
letter to Koehler — then to fair ground
with Emma, Annie & Jim — in at sun-
set — tea — finished Koehler letter
& wrote to Kate — took letters to P.O.
at 8.15 — To bed at 10.30 — Up at
1 o'clk. — a darkey shanty burned
down in the valley.
