## Left Page

### January, Friday 8, 1886

Beautiful morf. but overcast afternoon. To studio about 9.30 &
worked for awhile making changes
on the Bennington mill drawing.
Then at my big etching — Soon
found that the ground chips so
badly that I could not go on with
it — Promptly made up my mind
& cleared off the plate again —
Had about an hour's hard work
with water of ayr stone & emery
paper — Home at 1 — lunch. Annie
went to Phil. Rehearsal — I gave
my ticket to Mr. Champney —
& spent the afternoon fighting to
get a ground & at last, about 4
succeeded only tolerably well.
Experimented & found that I
could get a 2d transfer from a
Tracing — Home before 6 — dinner
— Evg. Struck with headache.
— a little reading — a little
writing — a game of cards &
to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 9, 1886

Heavy fall of snow during the
night — Storm from the South — an-
other snowstorm in the afternoon
— that one from the North west.
Got to studio before 10. Soon after,
Will came along & I worked for an
hour for him. Touching up a proof
of a Los Angeles die, which he took
away with him — Then I succeeded
in getting a very good Transfer
of my big Rough's part — the second
pulling from the one Tracing — I
commenced at once to etch —
Home at 1 & back at 2.30 & etched
until 4. Home soon after 5 —
Dinner — Evg. until near
10 spent writing letters — Then
had a short game with A. —
& to bed about 11.
