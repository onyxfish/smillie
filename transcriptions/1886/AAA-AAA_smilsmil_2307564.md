## Left Page

### September, Saturday 11, 1886

A wonderfully beautiful day.
Worked all the m'f closely
on Breton plates, dry pointing
& burnishing on the sky & taking
impressions. After dinner I
laid a Rabitting ground
covering about all of the landscape
& figures. I had some trouble
with it so that it was 4 o'clk
before I got done with it —
then the horse & buggy was at
the door & Annie & I went
off & had a very pleasant
ride getting home at 6. 15
Ev'f. after reading newspapers
we had a game of cards and
to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 12, 1886

Overcast m'f. Rains beginning
about noon & falling heavily at
times — all got up late — brkf.
about 9 o'clk. As the light was
steady & the air still I tho't it a very
good chance to experiment a
little with my camera, which
I did with a tolerable amt. of
success. Dinner after 2 —
afternoon, wrote to [illegible] & to Kate
& to Sonder for some dm. Creswick
baker. Ev'f. got to work
upon some plans for change
in our drainage which kept
me busy until after 10, 11
o'clk. when I got to bed.
Brilliant moonlight night.
