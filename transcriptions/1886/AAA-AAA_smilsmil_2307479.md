## Left Page

### March, Thursday 25, 1886

mild & pleasant. In the m't. went to N.A. & finished the drawing after Eastman Johnson's portrait of Prof. Dalton. To studio at 11.30 — Set palette & to work painting foreground of Cliff Farm. — To lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & worked until 5 — then cleaned up — rested for awhile — then to 5th Ave. theatre & bought tickets for next Tues. evg. Home — dinner — Evg. to work on "Etching" paper — Chas. Collins came in & stayed until 10.30 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 26, 1886

Overcast m't. clearing into a mild pleasant day — got up feeling very shabby — After bkfs. went to barber's & had hair cut & head shampooed — got to studio at about 10.30 — Could not paint. Did some working up on Etching paper to be read at Art Students League to-morrow evg. Home to lunch at 1.30 — about 3 when I got back to studio — headachy. A little more work on the Etching paper — Dr. Yale came in — I went out about 5 & for some time walked about in Mad. Sq. Then home & had a nap before dinner — Evg. did a little writing — Yale came in between 9 & 10 — To bed about 10.30.
