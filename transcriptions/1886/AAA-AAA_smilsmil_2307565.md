## Left Page

### September, Monday 13, 1886

Bright, breezy, cool day. Up with a head ache which kept me under a cloud all day — Smieth came at 9 o'clk. & I spent some time with him directing the painting of ceiling in S. W. bed room. Worked a little with varnish on Breton plate — went down town at noon. Afternoon a little more work — with Smith in "dark Room". At 4 went to Dolittle's with 18 of my negatives to have him Falke prints from them — then home & took a walk over & around the crest of Jarbell's hill with Annie, enjoying it very much. Home at 6 — Well wrote a note to Gale congratulating him on the birth of his son. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 14, 1886

over cast — Trawan Payne came up at 8 o'clk. with Scott & a team of horses & commenced ploughing the cow pasture. Pettus came with his team at 1 o'clk. & went to work ploughing. Herfus came at 3 & managed to stop the bad leak in studio cistern. Naylor came & picked up etching room stove — with all these interruptions I did not make much progress with my etching — I [illegible] stopped out into the acid once & a second stopping out. Well worked on plans for draw pipe for Howard white.
