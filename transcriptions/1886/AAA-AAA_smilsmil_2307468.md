## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 3, 1886

Bright, cold & rather more than breezy
— but wintral, an improvement on yes-
terday — to studio at 9.30 & to work on
"Summer sea" & spent the m.f. fussing
over the foreground fish — To bank at
1, then home & had lunch — back
at 2.30 — Did but little work in the
afternoon & that was on "Summer sea"
Home to dinner at 6.30 Eve'g
Read Matthew Arnold's "Numbers"
— then drew some lettering for
Kate — & a little more reading of
Ruskin. To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 4, 1886

Beautiful day — to studio at
about 10, after having gone into
the Tiffany Glass Co. with Mr. Mitchell
to see a new window after designs
by Dora Wheeler — Worked all the
m.f. on a charcoal sketch to be
submitted to Wm. Pate for his
approval for etching — Subject,
a brook & lane near Eluiera —
Home to lunch soon after — then,
after lunch, to Bank & deposited
check from sale of Rough Sport drawing,
transferring most of it to Annie's
acct — then to Studio — Waller wants
me to address Art Students League
on "Etching" — at 4 went to Sher-
wood Studios — 57th St. Reception —
met Annie there — through mis-
take, missed Kate — home at 6 —
dinner — eve'g some writing —
some reading — & to bed at 10.30
