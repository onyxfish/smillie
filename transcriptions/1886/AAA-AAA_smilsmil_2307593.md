## Left Page

### November, Monday 8, 1886

Another bright, cold day — nearly
10 when I left the house — went to 4:
5., to Douglass the painter — then
to Gurney's Sups — then to grocers —
then to butchers & after 11 got to studio.
Fussed there until near 1 — then
home to lunch — after 3 when I went
again to the grocers — then to Keating's
& ordered an overcoat, then to Newman
— then to Jackson's the grate man — &
then to studio — called on Calverley
& hung some curtains — more little
messages then home to dinner at 6
— Ev'f. writing. Sent off 1st section
of a letter of instruction to Herrick.
Charl came in & made us a visit —
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 9, 1886

Charming day — To bank & drew
some money for A. & self — then to
Dunlap's & bought me a hat — then
to Knordlein's & looked at a lot of
uninteresting pictures, then to studio.
Feeling much out of sorts I did nothing
home [illegible] — back at 2:30 — Put
up curtains & did some other
things in the way of getting into
order for work. It was 6 o'clk
when I got home to dinner —
Ev'f. wrote a note to Belle
Tweedy — condolence — her bro-
ther Chad died last Sat. About 9
A. & I went to Walter's — there
until near 10 — then home &
to bed.
