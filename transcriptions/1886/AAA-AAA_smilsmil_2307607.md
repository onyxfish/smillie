## Left Page

### December, Sunday 12, 1886

A nasty wet day — Last blfs.
about noon went to F. H.
Smiths & spent an hour with
him talking of Water-Color
Society matters — Then home.
Emma J — There — Dinner at
2. Chas. Parke came in late
& left early. Before 5 I took
Emma to call on Eleg. Howe
who is confined to her Room.
Spent an hour with her. Then
walked up to Central Park
& back. to supper — Eve'g
Mr. Wm. Helen, a friend of the
Parkins called & spent the
eve'g. After he left I wrote
to Mother — Koehler & bro. Will.
Went up to G.C.D. with letter.
To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 13, 1886

Another nasty wet day. 10 o'clk.
when I got to studio — To work
on Crécy mill — joining & washing
out & getting tones into harmony.
Home to lunch & back at 2.30
Very dark afternoon so that I
had hard work to see — Yewell
came in soon after 3 — about
4 I went down town to Walton
to correct figures on plans
for Montrose studio. We came
up town together — Dinner at
6.30. At 8.30 went to 2d W.
Cook's to enquire about condition
of the baby who has been very
sick — There until after 9 —
To bed at 11.
