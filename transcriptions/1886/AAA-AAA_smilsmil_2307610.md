## Left Page

### December, Thursday 16, 1886

Indoors all day — sick in
the mng. headache in the
afternoon & generally used
up. Mr. Parks & Bessie got
away for Easton some time
in the afternoon.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 17, 1886

A grudgingly pleasant
day — Got out though feeling
very much out of sorts — To
studio about 10 — Find that
I had forgotten my working
glasses I made paste & mounted
my Crécy Mill drawing — then
busied myself about etching
[illegible] until lunch time — then
home & back — Cassell came
in but I was unable to do
anything for him before I
went to W.C. Board Meeting at
Wood's studio at 4 o'clk. I
was miserable — on my way home I
stopped at A.A.A. — saw Sutton & Kirby who
urged me to etch a plate for Stewart Cat.
I declined — then home & sent for Dale.
Temperature 102°. Pulse 110 — Got to
bed, after a short visit from Charl.
& passed a wretched night.
