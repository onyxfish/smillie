## Left Page

### March, Saturday 13, 1886

Unpleasant. Rain part of the day —
clear at night. 10 o'clk. when I got
to studio & to work on Argentine
Arms. Stuck to it until after 1 —
then home to lunch. Saw Ogilvie
in Geo's studio & promised that I w'd
write a note in his behalf to Com. on
Admissions — Century. Back at 2.30
& by 5 had finished Arms — 6 when
I left studio. Took a short walk
home to dinner at 6.30 — W'th a
little reading — then a game of
cards with a — who, feeling very
tired, for b and for bed before
10 — I followed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 14, 1886

Brilliant day. Bkfst about 9 — most
of the m'g transferring some of my
belongings from old quarters up stairs
to the newer ones down stairs — brought
Annie's desk down from the nursery
— after 12 o'clk went out & had a short
walk — Dinner at 2. Mr. Downs
& her son Jack & Helic Adams took
dinner with us — After 4 I went
to studio & set down some Baker
to take proofs to-morrow — there
for an hour or more putting things
into the places from which they
had been taken when the storm
some weeks ago came through
the roof, to my discomfort and
detriment. Then home & had
tea about 6.30 — W'th. Read
awhile — wrote to Mother &
to Koshler — also took letters
to G.C.D — To bed at 11.30
