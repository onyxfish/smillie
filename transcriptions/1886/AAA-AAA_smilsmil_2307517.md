## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 9. 1886.

overcast — S.E. wind — Rain
commencing before 5 — heavy
Rain at night — Bkft. at 7.30
Worked like a good fellow
all the m'f. To Knoedler's
& 2° Nat. Bank. Home at 1.30.
At 2.30 went to Grady's — then
to Hoboken & bought 1000 mile
ticket, D.L. &W — & arranged for
Parlor Car seats on Sat — then, by
Barclay S'. Ferry to N.Y. & to 85
Chambers S'. Geo. Kralf, where I
bought some Gelatine sheets —
then to Studios at 5 & finished
packing big box — Rec'd a very
flattering letter from E.C. Stedman
in relation to Homer Etching. Home
to dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g wrote
notes & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 10. 1886.

warm day — Bkft — at 7.30 — got
to Studios at 9 & talked boy with
Johnson & Bridgman paintings —
then finished up big box & other
things about the Studio — Home to
lunch at 1.15 — Spent the p.m.
at home getting things there in
order for going — Emma J. &
Emily Coote came in — Dinner
at 6.30 — Ev'g. making out
lists & arranging for the fall
western picture [illegible] To bed
soon after 10.
