## Left Page

### February, Friday 19, 1886

Bright mtf, but showers before noon — continuing muggy & damp.
To studio at 9.30 & for about two hours worked on S. A. lion arms
putting in more black. — Then set palette & until I painted
on Chemung Co. Pasture, putting in an entire new sky & improv-
ing it generally — Then home to lunch — Annie at her mother's
to lunch — back at 2.30. Stupid headache & did not work well.
Sent my framed [illegible] Rough Spot
& 7 other [illegible]. same plate. to Keppel's
& I cleared up about my room —
Home at 6.30 — Dinner — Sweet
Read awhile — Then Charl
came in to bid us good bye
before he starts for Chayenne
on Monday mtf. After 9 A.
& I had a game of cards —
To bed about 11.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 20, 1886

Pleasant day, but saw & windy growing colder —
at night very cold & windy — Got
to studio about 9.30. Then went
to Keppel's & saw him — hoped to
make some business arrangement with
him concerning "Rough Spot" — but, as
he was much engaged with his [illegible]
[illegible] on etchings, got no farther than
criticisms — Then back to studio and
until I worked on father's lion die —
stoning & burnishing the extremities
of the [illegible]. Then home to lunch, back
at 2.30 — until near 4 worked with dry-
point putting a "Remarque" — See all head
of father on the lion die — Then Annie
having come for me, we went to Am.
Art Ass'n & spent a couple of hours
looking at the Morgan collection.
Then home to dinner when my
headache, after grumbling all
day, got the better of me and
both Annie & I went to bed
before 9 o'clk. My headache
making the night a very un-
comfortable one for me.
