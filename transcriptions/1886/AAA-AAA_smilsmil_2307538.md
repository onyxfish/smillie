## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 21, 1886.

Overcast wtf. with rain —
clearing in the afternoon. Tried
our fire place in main Room —
after the new safeguards put
in yesterday & fied that it
smokes worse than ever. R.—
Smith commenced work this
mtf. with Hamick on ceiling of
N.E. bed Room. I worked all
day at proving Bridgman
etching without being able to
please myself. Took 15 japs.
paper p/p. & two plain imps.
I did not get my work done
until after 8 o'clk. A game
of cards — then a nice along
promenade with Annie on
the piazza — 11 o'clks. when
we got to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 22, 1886.

Cool, overcast wtf., but before
noon it cleared charmingly — a
glorious afternoon. I got to printing
again in the mtf. & took more p/p.
of Bridgman plate. I had a bad scare
with Kerosene stove in printing table
that at one time seemed very near
to setting the place on fire. Fanny
Conner & Mrs. Ben. Muelford called
in the mtf. Mr. Htf. Tigler in the
afternoon. The ceiling of N.E. Room
progressed but not to a finish as
I had hoped. Smith painted
ceiling in hall second time.
Coyle set out a lot of Smith's
white Raspberry bushes.
Eve tf., wrote a letter to Charl.
& got to bed at about 11.
