## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 14, 1886

Very mild & charming day. Up soon after 7 — took a short walk before bkfst at 8 o'clk. Worked hard all the m'f assorting things & getting out whatever is to be sent to N.Y. Uncle Willie spent some time with us. Dinner about 1 — Headache beginning to develop which continued slowly to gain ground until I went to bed at night — Charl & I, getting thro' our work after 4 o'clk. I lay down on bed & got a little rest & sleep. In the mean time Mother had quite a severe attack of colic — tea about 6.30 — & at 7 left the house. Taking 7.21 train & getting into N.Y. at 9.18 — Charl went home with me where I found everything all right — to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 15, 1886

Bright warm day — after 10 when I got to studio — Etched on Homer plate until near 1 — then to Bank for Annie — then to Bierstadt's & found him laid up with boils. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 & etched until after 5 — Home to dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g wrote to Mother — Ticknor & Co. — sent a dollar to Miss Durfee & went to Mother Cook's — Home at 9.30 Read awhile & to bed about 11.
