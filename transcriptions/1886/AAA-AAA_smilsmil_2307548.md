## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 10, 1886

Glorious day. Bkfs. at 7.30 — at
8 the Talley ho came & the three
gives mounted to the top of it &
started for New Milford & home —
I got to work at my proving of
Johnson plate & took things
easily. Find the plate much
improved in tone. The crudeness
is much reduced — now it needs
careful burnishing. Dinner at
1.30 — fooled away all the time
over 8 pfs. (more properly 6)
in stretching them. — Evef
reading & writing — a little
of each — another delightful
moonlight night.
Herrick at work in Chemy
Room — Coyle fooling about the
grass & Road.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 11, 1886

Morning cloudy & threatening.
afternoon clear & very warm. I
spent part of the mtf. working
with burnisher over the Johnson
plate — wrote a note to E.J. &
in the afternoon sent proof by
exp. to him for his criticism.
I felt a good deal out of sorts
generally used up & so, did
not work much during the
day. Most of the evef. at the
piazza. Soon after 9 sudden
threats of a thunder shower —
wind blew — clouds flew & a
few drops of rain fell — then,
as suddenly, all cleared away
& at 10 we went to bed.
