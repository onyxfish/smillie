## Left Page

### July, Monday 5, 1886

Still very hot. So that I was demoralized for work. Spent the m'g
with Annie & Herrick on Studio piazza
fixing & putting up bamboo screens.
I tried to do a little work & in the
course of the afternoon I nearly finished
tracing on gelatine the Jules Breton
"Evening at Finisterre". I also worked
on the ceiling problem, sizing a
panel but without success —
After tea I went down town and
attended to little matters & back
about 8.30 — Sat on piazza with
Annie until near 10 — then lighted
up. Read news paper & to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 6, 1886

Horribly hot & dry — Had to go
down town before [illegible] to get butter.
Finished Breton tracing — Tried to
get a transfer & failed — then to work
with dry point on the Bridgman
& stuck to it pretty well thro' the
day in spite of interruptions. Got
one panel up on N. E. bedroom
ceiling — but cannot get it to stretch
as I want it to — Smith painting a
new fence — After tea, down town,
then on piazza with Annie enjoy-
ing the breeze & the young moon until
after 9 — To bed at 10.
