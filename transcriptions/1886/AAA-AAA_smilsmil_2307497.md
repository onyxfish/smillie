## Left Page

### April, Friday 30, 1886.

Bright cool day — about 10 when
I got to studio — Etched in the
cliff on right hand end of the
Homer plate & then bit it in —
Home to lunch soon after —
back at 2.30 — & worked on Homer
until 5 — Then to Bridgman's
& paid bill for grass seed. Then
again to Studio & cut down
paper for proving to-morrow —
Home at 6.30 — dinner — Evely.
Chark came in — then Jo. & 
Nellie — Mary & Katie went
with Chark to his Rooms — I
wrote a note to Clarence Stirling
& read awhile.

## Right Page

### Annie's Birth day.

### May, Saturday 1. 1886.

Still pleasant, altho' part of the
time overcast. Katie & Bessie
went home to Co'k at 3.30 while
Mary went to Newark to spend
Sunday with Nette Sutphen. I
got to studio about 10 & to work
taking [illegible] of my Homer plate.
Mr. Chas. Tolspaw called — Before
I went home to lunch I managed
to take 3 working [illegible]. After lunch
went to Knœdler's & then on down
to J.W. Wood's Studio — 51 W. 10th S: when
an adjourned meeting of W.C. Board of
control was held — Ret. to studio
before 5 — Miss Parker came and I
laid an etching ground for her.
Home at 6.30 — dinner — Evely.
Some reading — 11 when I got
to bed.
