## Left Page

### May, Friday 14, 1886

Pleasant day — sunshine & shadow by turns. Chas. Collins came before we got down to [illegible] — & Annie went with him to So. Orange & met Margaret Parker White & they had a little Picnic — I went to studio — worked on Homer plates & then prepared for proving it. Went to A.A.A. & took a look at the Prize Fund Pictures then to Bank for Annie — Home to lunch at 2 — back at 3 — until 6 Faking plt. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Eveth. wrote letters to Frank Herrick & J.D. Payne. Geo. Cook & wife came in for awhile. To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 15, 1886

A nasty day — mizzling, raw & foggy. To the studio at about 9.30 & to work Faking plt from Homer plate — Agent from Detroit Art museum came in — I did not get home to lunch until 1.45 — near 3 when I got back to studio. worked at press until almost 6 — getting only 10 or 11 plt. Falcon. I have not been able to get the brilliancy that I had hoped for — the thin paper gives me too much delicacy — & the heavy paper too much crudeness. Home at 6.30 — dinner — Eveth. Read awhile then had a game of cards with Annie & to bed at 10.45.
