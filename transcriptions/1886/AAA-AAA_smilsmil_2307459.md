## Left Page

### February, Saturday 13, 1886

more dismal rain in the m'f.
Got to studio before 10 & spent
a little time fussing with [illegible]
of Rough Start — then to work on
an arms vigñ (S.A.) for Brill.
Home soon after 1 — the Rain
had ceased & there were some
signs of a possible clearing —
So, Annie & I decided to go to
Po'k — which we did by the
3.30 Francie getting to the terrace
a little before 6 — all glad to
see us — Kate quite under the
weather — Bessie also, but [illegible] to.
Every passed in chatting
until after 10, then to bed —
the moon making efforts to
be seen.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 14, 1886

*[heading note: Poughkeepsie.]*

a very bright, pleasant m'f.
B'k f's at 8. I spent the m'f
in Father's room trying to get
things in shape to move them
about noon Annie & I walked
up town & met Mr. Grosse. I
asked him for the M.S. "A Pil-
grimage" that I loaned to him
then we went to Uncle Willie's
but no one was at home. I left a
pile of documents for him & then
home — dinner at 1 — afternoon
again at work — Aunt Agnes, Jenie
& Emma called — Ed. came in later.
Tea at 6 & a little before 7, the
hack came & we started home
took 7.21 Train & got to NY. at 9.25.
— home & to bed soon after 10.
