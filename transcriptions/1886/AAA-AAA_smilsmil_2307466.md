## Left Page

### February, Saturday 27, 1886

Still very cold. & windy. Got to studio before 10 & spent the mrt. working on Lake Ge. Harbor Islands. Home abt 1.30 — after lunch walked down to J.W. Wood's studio. W.C. Board of Control meeting at 3 o'clk — through at 4.30 — called on Nicoll — then to Am. Art Gallery & saw Sutton about Bridgeman picture to etch for Koehler. Then to W.C. [illegible] for a moment. Then to studio — cleaned up there — then home soon after 6 — Dinner — Evett. D'el all came in for a few minutes — I wrote a note to Koehler — Annie & I had a game & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 28, 1886

Weather improved — Bright & cold day. B'klp'? after 9 — Annie & Kate went to Dr. Payton's church. About 11 I went to Am. Art Ass'n — by invitation of Kirby & spent an hour & a half among the Morgan art books — then to N.A. where I was joined by Annie & Kate — we looked at the pictures until 1.45 — then home to dinner — At 3.45 we went to the Morgan Collection — closed to the public — but found the galleries comfortably full. There until after 5 — home before 6 — tea — Evett. wrote letter home — to G.C. Depot at 9.30 — To bed about 10.30
