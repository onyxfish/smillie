## Left Page

### July, Saturday 17, 1886

A very pleasant day with a thunder shower about 5. P.M.
After working with Herrick on ceiling for some time I went
down town, having a number of messages to attend to. It
was after 2 when I returned — Dinner — & then again with
Herrick. It was just about 4 o'clk when I got my Bridgeman Ph[oto?] & [illegible] put up for
Koehler & sent it to the [illegible]
office — Then made up my
mind that I must rebiite
portions of the plate — was to
take a ride but the shower
spoiled that — Evtf. wrote
a letter to Koehler — Had a
game of cards with Annie
& to bed at 10.30. after a walk
on the piazza.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 18, 1886

Overcast mtf, clearing pleasantly
before 10 — Thunder showers in
the afternoon & evtf. Some
heavy hail. Much of the mtf
was spent fussing about the
ceiling in the N.E. bed room
Dinner at 2 — then a Rest —
then letter writing until after
6 — Annie & I sent 12 letters
to P.O. by Herrick — Walking
on piazza for some time. The
rain effects between 6 & 7
very beautiful — Evtf. again
writing, & to bed soon after 10
