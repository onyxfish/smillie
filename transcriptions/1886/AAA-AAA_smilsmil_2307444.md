## Left Page

### January, Thursday 14, 1886

Beautiful day — growing milder.
After bkfs — man came & put up
gas stove in bath room — To studio
at 10 & to work biting on big plate.
At 10.45, Mr. Elliman came & spent
the rest of the day going over Symington's
W.C. books — I got my plate in the
acid only once in the m'f. To lunch
& back — at 2.30 went to N.A. for half
an hour to excuse myself for non-
appearance as one of W.C. jury of
acceptance — then to studio — Baldwin
as member of Auditing com. Spent
part of P.M. with us — again got my
plate in the acid but once — At 5
went to N.A. then to A.A.A. to see
Salmagundis pictures — Home at
6.30 — Dinner soon after 7. Emma
& Mary J — with Mrs. Satchell & daughter.
Later, Geo. Cook came in — At 9.30
I took Emma & Mary to cars —
To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 15, 1886

Brilliant, beautiful day — To
studio at usual time & right
to work on my biting — had my
plate in the acid twice & then
ready to clean it off — before
doing so, home to lunch & back
about 2.30. Cleaned my plate
& papered it. Then over to
N.A. & D. Trust members of the
Board of Control & passed upon
a lot of Water Colors — After 5 went
to 18 W. 23d St. & got extra length of
flexible gas pipe — then home before
6 — dinner — About 8 Annie & I
went to Mother Cooks & half an
hour later, up to "the Madrid" — Cor.
7th Ave. & 59th St. — where we called
upon the Randolphs — where Mrs.
Satchell & daughter dined. We
spent the evening pleasantly, near 10 where we returned
home bringing Mrs. S. & daughter
with us — To bed soon after.
