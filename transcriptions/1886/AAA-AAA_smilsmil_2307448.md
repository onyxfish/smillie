## Left Page

### January, Friday 22. 1886.

a nasty day, altho' it tried to
clear in the middle of it — more of
rain + slush than anything else.
Early 6 klo. Catherine & Mary Adams
came so as to go with Annie & Emma
Satchell to the Normal College immedi-
ately after. To studio at 9.30 — did
some stopping out on big plate +
then made pencil sketch for A.B.N.
Co. for a "Remarque" on West Shore
Bond die. W.S.R. Koehler came in
& spent some time talking about
a work for which I have promised
him two plates — Home at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & spent the rest of the p.m.
varnishing & rebiting ground with
transparent varnish. Koehler's
History of Etching was sent to me from
Cassell's for $10.00 — thanks to K — Home
at 5.30 — Emma Satchell went to Bridge
part this p.m. — Dinner — every.
Signed 58 India pfs. Pasini
etching — and got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 23. 1886.

Cold day — partly clear — partly cloudy.
To studio before 10 + to work rebiting
on big etching. Home at 1.30 — back
before 3. finished rebiting and
then worked with dry-point — and
a little with a graver on black
horse, where I found the lines
so big that I could do nothing
with the dry-point. Stuck to
my work as long as I could see
— a little after 6 when I got
home — dinner — every. For some
time looking over Koehler's book
with Annie. Then she read aloud
for some time from "Adam Bede" —
then we had a game of cards
& to bed soon after 11.
