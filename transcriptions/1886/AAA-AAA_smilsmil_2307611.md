## Left Page

### December, Saturday 18. 1886.

Dismal, rainy day — my fever
less in the m'f, with a gradual
improvement during the day.
Very hot throat & tender chest.
Dr. Y — came in the m'f.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 19. 1886.

Got through the night tolerably
in the m'f was without pain
but feeling weak — Yale made
a long call in the m'f. Reported
temperature as normal — pulse
90 — Geo also came in, making
a long call — In the afternoon
I got up & crawled to my
easy chair — wrapped in blankets
& growing every hour more un-
comfortable — Early eve'g, Charl
came in & I asked him to stop
at Yale's & ask him to come down
which he did about 9 o'clk — my
temperature was then 103° — pulse
100 — I passed a wretched
night of fevered dreaming.
