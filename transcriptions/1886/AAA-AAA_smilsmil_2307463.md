## Left Page

### February, Sunday 21, 1886

Half overcast and pretty cold.
Belfo- after 9 o'clk. — Sat reading
paper until nearly — then Annie
& I went to the M.S. & we spent
an hour looking at the water. then
then home — I wrote until dinner
time at 2 — again in the after-
noon I wrote & at 5 went out &
posted my letters — then up to
Central Park — back about 6.15
Tea at 6.30 — Kate, the cook, &
Bella, up to Harlems — a party
Ev'lg. Read Winklemans
& to bed at 10

## Right Page

### February, Monday 22, 1886

To studio at 9.30 & prepared things
for printing — then dry-pointed Father's
autograph onto his leon die & then
took a proof. The little likeness
that I put in on Sat. Proves to be
very fair & after a little touching I
went on with the proving. Home
to lunch at 1.30 & back at 2.30. I
tried to take a pl. on parchment
but the ink was too strong. At 6 o'clk
I had used up my last piece of
paper having taken 8 india — one
on japan paper & 1 (failure) on
parchment. Then I washed up
& cut & wet paper for to-morrow.
It was 7.20, to my surprise and
horror, before I was ready to start
for home — Ran nearly all the way —
Annie was not worried — dinner
was not spoiled — Ev'lg. a — read
aloud first chapters of Howell's Indian
Summer & we got to bed soon after
10.
