## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 8, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot m'f. partially overcast and tempered by a breeze - clouded afternoon with a very light sprinkle of rain about 6 o'clk. - M'f. at 5 o'clk - commenced my course of Carles bad - I had no pain yesterday \+ none to-day - Bkf. at 8 - It was after 10 when Bessie, Mable, Jim, Ralph \+ self got away on buck board - Greer riding a his horse - We went down town with laundry etc. etc. \+ then drove out on some new park roads \+ boulevards to the great new Reservoir now in course of construction It was after 12 when we got to Park's office \+ near't where we got home - Then dinner - afternoon on porch writing letters - also wrote one for Bessie P- to a young Greek who had sent her his photo - About 6 I took a short walk - Tea at 6.30 - Every. loafing about until near 9 - then to bed - Light Rain during the night.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 9, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy day - cooler - up at 6.30 - bkf. at 7.15 Rode into town with Greer, Jim \+ Ralph \+ took the 8.08 train for Wyoming - met at station by conveyance \+ driven to Mt. Hope mine - Everything had been prepared by Parke \+ two bosses went down the mine with us - we were shown through very thoroughly \+ were much interested in the use of electricity in the mine - Rode on a Motor etc. etc. - on reaching the upper air again we went to the electrical plant air pumping stations \+ then to the top \+ down to the bottom of the breaker - Then washed up \+ were driven to Station \+ took 11.32 train back to Scranton getting there at 12.15 to the house at about 12.30 - Rev. Mr. Benton There \+ he took dinner with us after dinner I read Tribune \+ then had a nap. After that I rode into town with Parke \+ one. Worthing - I did some shopping - Then to P's office - met him \+ back by Trolley car to his house Then went to Mrs. Townsend Pome's (Priscilla Benity) \+ made a call on her \+ her daughter. To P's at 6.30 - tea - a quiet even'g chatting with him \+ to bed at about 10.30
