## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 31, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The same old story of quinine \+ discomfort, but no neuralgia \+ no fever. Yale came as usual. I managed to get a Report written which Geo. called for \+ took to the Council meeting. I had a bath, which has been discontinued for the past few days

## Right Page

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]
