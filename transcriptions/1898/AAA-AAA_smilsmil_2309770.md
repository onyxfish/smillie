## Left Page

### Mon. Sept. 5, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

M\+f opened brightly, clouding with a thin skim, increasing until noon when it was quite overcast — light showers in the afternoon. Rffs\. about 8 — wrote to Charl and Treas\. Shields — About 11 went down to the River to the pool where Jim + other boys were swimming — Ralph joined me — went to Scott Brown \+ made an appointment with him — Then R\. \+ I went through the woods by road from Reserve Gate over by Noon Mark trail, \+ back by Deybrook Cottages. Lunch at 1.30 — At 3 I went with Brown over the old Road \+ the line of the proposed road that Simonds found distances \+ grades for me on Friday \+ Saturday — on my Return to hotel wrote a note to W.G. Nelson giving him figures the result of Simond's Survey. About 6 I went out \+ had a short walk, returning in a gentle Rain — Rec\. from Charl a letter from Kate \+ from Nellie two letters from Gel — very enthusiastic \+ interesting. Every I sat in parlor until 9 — then to bed —

no pain to-day, altho' I was not wholly comfortable — I feel stronger than yesterday

## Right Page

### Tues. Sept. 6, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant m\+f, with skim-milk atmosphere. That grew denser until late p.m. it was leaden \+ at night rain. To day rec\. two Nellie very interesting letters from Gel\., also letters from N.A. that required immediate attention — After Hkp\. went to my Knoll \+ ruminated there for awhile — then ret\. to hotel \+ wrote to Nellie — to Switzer — 2\. Nat\. Buk \+ to Grannis — About 1 o'clk. Hatcher Cook arr\., as invited, \+ lunched with us. After lunch we went to the Knoll \+ from thence we walked over to the Nielsen's where K — made a call on Miss N — While there Dr. Hobbs \+ lady came — At 4.30 I got horse \+ drove down to Estes House with K — I got back to hotel at 6 — Dinner at 7 — in parlor until 9 — then to my room \+ soon to bed.

no pain to day — but I am not strong \+ cannot tramp with any pleasure.
