## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 30, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day - snow for awhile in the mg - an inch or two fee - then melted into slush. Bkfs. as usual - I got to the NA about 10.30 + at about 11 went to Bank + was there until 12, most of the time getting my Safe Deposit box contents in order + making a list - Then again to N.A. + there until near 1 o'clk - making arrangements to begin a new plan of separating the 2d. Natl. Bank acct. into three accts., Expense, Exhibition + School - The accts. of the Academy have been kept in its own books that way for some time, but the bank has not before kept the accounts separated - Home to lunch at 1 - I kept pretty steadily at work during the afternoon - I had a note from Geo. H. Watrous of Montrose enquiring if I was willing to sell my place there + if so, w? I let him know my lowest price - I replied that I was willing to sell, but not anxious - The place had cost nearly $20,000 - I would sell for $10000 -
Dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g I did some N.A. writing + got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 1, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day - bright + sunny overhead - but horrible underfoot. Bkfs. at usual time + soon after I got to work on N.A. work, but before I had been long at it Spe? came in, + then Nicoll - N- Remained for some time talking over Ways + Means + Building Com. business. After he left I got the N.A. bank books in shape to make a change, under which the 2d. Natl. Bank will subdivide its acct. with the Academy as the N.A. books are kept - that is, into Expense, Exhibition + School Accts - so that all three of the accts will at all times be kept entirely separate. Then I went to N.A. + from there to the Bank where I arranged with Cash. Case + made the first deposits under the new plan - Home at 1 - lunch - Afternoon I spent some time stamping the ends of checks in the book with rubber stamps - distinguishing the three different sets - I also did some writing + then with Ralph, went to the barbers where we had our heads barberized - Then I went to studio + find that at last Douglas has got the floor into very good shape - Dinner at 6.30 - Evening Ge. + Nellie + Charl came in + stayed until 11 o'clk - I got to bed at 11.30
