## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 5, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A "beast" of a day — overcast & muggy, with heavy rain in the afternoon. I got up after a troubled night feeling very weak. Bkfs. soon after 8 - it was 10.30 before I went to Carnere & Hastings. Saw Mr. C— for a few minutes. Then did my marketing — went to Lock, the upholsterer — then to 2° Nat° Bk. \+ saw Mr. Case \+ arranged with him to have N.A. acct. divided into three, Expense Exp'se \+ School accts. Then to N.A. \+ worked for an hour on accts. \+ home to lunch at 1. After that I had a rest. Then the rain came on \+ I did a little getting my bed room in order. Dinner at 6.30. Ev'g. G.2° came in \+ stayed until after 9 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 6, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day. breezy \+ cool — Got up feeling pretty well, encouraged by the fine day. It was 10.30 before I left the house \+ after marketing went down Town — to Lee \+ Lee's first, where I was engaged with Mr. W— until noon. Then I went to 72 Trinity Pl. The Kenny Co. \+ arranged to have a man sent up to see if the W.C. that Ryan has set in my bathroom is properly set — then home at 1.15 to lunch. The Kenny man came \+ said that the closet was correctly set. That does not mean however that it is set in a workmanlike manner — it is so crooked that I shall insist upon Ryan's taking it down. After 3 clk. I went with Ralph to Cammayer's \+ got a pair of shoes for him — then to N.A. \+ he \+ I spent some time in going through the show there given by the American Institute. When we came out I was so tired \+ in so much pain that we rode home. Dinner at 6.30. Ev'g. Read aloud to the boys until about 9. To bed after 10 with a rapidly rising Temp. 102° when I took it — don't know how much higher it went — had a horrible night of pain and misery — just before getting into bed a window shade fell cutting my scalp \+ bleeding so freely that I went to bed with my head done up in a gory bandg.
