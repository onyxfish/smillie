## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 15, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day, but cool, breezy \+ at times, cloudy. I got up feeling unusually well \+ under direction of Miss Thompson ate a large bkf'. Then up, dressed, \+ down stairs. Dr. Yale came later in the m'f \+ was much pleased at my apparent progress. It was agreed that Miss T— was no longer necessary to me \+ she left me in the afternoon. Lunch at 1 \- After it, Ralph went to Dr. Y's \+ with him \+ Roy spent the afternoon in Bronx Park. I went out \+ walked for a time on Park Ave. I had a return of my old enemy, the pain in the stomach, \+ was discouraged \- Later in the afternoon I was very uncomfortable. Ate a very light meal at 6.30 after doing considerable N.A.f.d. work. Eve'g the boys beds were removed to their usual quarters on the floor with me. Eve'g. Jas. Herbert Morse made a call on me.

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 16, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Breezy, cool day \- I was aroused at about 8.30 by Jane bringing my bkf'. to my bedside. I dressed \+ got to Dr. Yale's a few minutes after 10 \- This was as per agreement. I reported to date \- not altogether encouraging. He talked hopefully to me \+ I returned home \+ tried to comfort myself by the fire. My discomfort in the region of the stomach commenced to assert itself at about noon \+ continued to increase thereafter. Dinner at 2. About 3 I went to druggist \+ got some Muriate of Quinine \+ then to studio \+ home. About 4 Ed W' \+ Mary Cook \- then Geo. \+ Emily, \+ then Geo., Nellie, Val. \+ Gordon came \- I was pretty wretched then \- Tea at 6.30 \- I was able to eat very little \- About 8 I sent Jim to Dr. Yale asking him to come to see me \- Y\- had not yet ret? from his Sunday outing. He came at 9 o'clk \+ I got relief from Bismuth \+ morphine \+ a hot water bag \+ soon fell asleep.
