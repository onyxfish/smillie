## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 14, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull gray \+ rather disagreeable mg., but it cleared pleasantly in the afternoon \- I had my bkft. in bed \+ got up \+ dressed about 10.30 \+ went to the parlor where I passed the day. I felt much better than yesterday \+ spent some time over N.a. f.i. \- check-book. Lunch at 1 \- Then I wrote report for Council This eveg. Rested on my bed from 4 to 5.30 \- Then Gee came \- had my dinner in parlor at 6.30 \+ spent the eveg there until 9 \- then to bed.
Today, Yale has granted my request to be released from Quinine \- Now I am to feed on Warburg for a few days. \+ then take to Arsenic.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 15, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ pleasant mg. \- overcast afternoon clearing again before eveg. Bkft. in bed. Nicoll called before I got up. \- I got up at about 10.30 \+ went to parlor \- about noon I went out \+ walked to studio \- then up 36th S. to Park Ave. \- 38th S. \+ home, without feeling much tired. \- Lunch at 1 o'clk. Not feeling so well in the afternoon I lounged about until then lay down on my bed \+ had a nap. Yale came in after 5 o'clk \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Eveg. Harry Watrous came \+ made report on property offered as security for loan \- 143d S. \- Then Charl came \+ took Nathenie \+ Elizabeth to his house. \- I got to bed before 10.
