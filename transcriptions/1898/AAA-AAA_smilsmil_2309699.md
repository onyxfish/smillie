## Left Page

### Sat. April 16, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

more rain last night \+ light rain this the m'f. After noon the weather cleared delightfully. Again the most of the m'f was taken with writing letters - At 12 with the boys I walked to the P.O. then to R.R. station, bought tickets \+ arranged for trunk to go on 7.10 train to-morrow eve'f. Then we walked home, getting in just in time for lunch. After lunch the boys went out on their bicycles \+ with their cameras to lake Whitney. Having a great desire to go boating Jim returned to the house to get my permission - The result being that Molly \+ I returned with him to the lake \+ the boys, Molly \+ I rowed with them from 4 to 6 o'clk., going as far as we could up the stream that runs into the lake. The afternoon was mild \+ the sky \+ light beautiful. The two boys rowed all the way up \+ part of the way back. Then Molly \+ I took the oars to the end. Home just in time for dinner. Eve'f Molly \+ I went in to Mrs. Atwalers, next door - Jene at home, with his visitor friend, Miss Powell \+ Jack Downs - Mrs. Atwater came in later \+ we had a chatty, pleasant time until almost 10 o'clk. Just as we were leaving Mr. Atwater came in. Home \+ right to bed -

## Right Page

### Sun. April 17, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Returned to New-York -

A very lovely day. Ralph \+ I got up before 6\. \+ had a 20 min. ride on our bicycles. After 6½\. there was reading of the big Sunday newspaper. Packing of trunk \+ general confabulation. Mr. Downs, senior, came in \+ made a short call on me. With the two boys I spent most of the m'f in Molly's dark room developing the films that they exposed yesterday. I got time to have a short bicycle ride just before lunch about 1 o'clk. After lunch I finished the trunk packing just as the exp. man came for it at 4 o'clk. Then we loafed until tea time at 6 o'clk. At 6.35 we mounted our wheels \+ in a very leisurely way jogged down to the station getting there before 7 \+ having no trouble or hurry in getting our wheels checked and aboard the 7.10 train which came in on time - we got into N.Y. on time, went right home \+ very soon to bed.
