## Left Page

### Thur. June 23, 1898

*Jim ret'd from Orange*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day, brilliant, breezy + cool. After my mᵒʳⁿ duties, soon after 10, I got to N.A. f᷊. + spent the rest of the mᵒʳⁿ there arranging myself as to the "Balance Sheet" that Hungerford + Bullock prepared yesterday eve'g. I also worked at some analyzing of acc'ts that I have not yet completed, in preparation for my Report — Home to lunch — Jime there — has had a fine time at Orange. much time again given to plumbers + carpenters. I commenced to clean my wheel at about 3 o'clk + it was after 4 when at last f[illegible] — + I got off on a ride — the ride through the Park + around Grant's Tomb was delicious Home at 6.30. Dinner at 7.15 m᷊ᵒ. White not well enough to come to table — Paddy, her younger boy — am᷊ from Cal. + it seemed to upset her. Eve'g with my paper — + a little writing, + then to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 24, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright day + breezy, but uncomfortably hot. After getting through with my regular duties, + the plumber, the carpenter + the mason, I set to work + finished up some little ends of the analyzing of N.A. acc't preparatory to meeting the Audit Com. this p.m. Soon after 12 I went to Camene + Hastings + saw Mr. C — about relaying pavement on Hm'ˢ Ave, 109' + 10ᶠˢ. Home to lunch at 1 — at 3 o'clk — to N.A. to meet Audit Com. — Dielman, Jones (Frank) + Perry at Bank Safe Deposit + ticked off the securities there — then to N.A. 4 weeks came in + securities there — mortgages, etc., were ticked off + some attention was given to my books — Part of the time I was very uncomfortable owing to severe pain in my stomach. It was near 6 when I got home + I rolled over into my bed at once + stayed there until after 11 when I got up, relieved of my pain + went to bed properly after writing notes to Dielman, Sewitger + Hungerford.
