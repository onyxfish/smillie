## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 8, 1898

**Weather:** fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day \- It was 10 before I got to studio. Spent the mᵒʳⁿ working on Water-Color "Evelf.", using bristle + sable brushes \+ water only, blending + slopping and washing away \- To lunch at 1. After lunch I went to Chaimy organization Socty to see if any thing could be done there for a case like Famington's I found that F\- was known there, having been employed in the office before he came to Me Kd. I think he left the employ of the Socty somewhat under a cloud. To studio after 3 \+ worked on drawing for an hour or more. Then home \+ tried to get a rest before dinner, but it would not come. Dinner at 6.30 \- at 7.45 Emma J. came \+ took Bessie to Phil. concert \- at 9. I went to Century \- Annual meeting \- \+ was there until near midnight.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 9, 1898

**Weather:** charming day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day. Bkf. at 9 \- newspaper as usual. Went to studio \+ back at 11 \+ with Ralph walked to 57. S. to George's \+ then with him to his Studio in 59. S. where we spent some time looking over his work \+ talking of his project of trying to find out what he can do in London. Then R. \+ I walked up to, \+ into, Central Park \- on our way home stopped at Dr. Yale's \- not in \- while there Ed. \+ Mary Cook came \- we walked home together \- Dinner at 2. Afternoon, prepared my work for to-morrow evey's Council meeting. At dusk had a walk \+ a short rest before tea. Welf, read to R \- \+ later, some writing \- Charl came in.
