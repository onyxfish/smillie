## Left Page

### Thur. May 12, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Don't know what sort of weather it was out of doors as I was in bed all day with a horrible headache. I know that there was a thunder storm in the afternoon. The pain in my head was excessive \+ I had also some neuralgia pain in my stomach that made the night as miserable as the day.
This is, no doubt, the price I pay for the strain that I have been under for such a length of time \+ the mortifying relaxation of yesterday.

## Right Page

### Fri. May 13, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — with shower in the afternoon — I got up at about the middle of the eve. in an exceedingly shaky condition — neuralgia in stomach set in again \+ I sent for Yale. He came in the afternoon \+ comforted me somewhat. I felt much better in the eve. altho' I had "temperature" in the eve. that increased during the night so that I had a pretty dismal time from 11 to 3 A.m.
Margaret Parker White came in for a few minutes at 6.30 \+ made an appointment for Sunday eve. at 9 o'clk in the early eve. after dinner Watsons came in \+ made a pleasant call.
