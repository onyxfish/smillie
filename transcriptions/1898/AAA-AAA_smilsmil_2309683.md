## Left Page

### Tues. March 15, 1898

**Weather:** Pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — another days work on Mad. Sq. Tower — fussing with the tower all day. Before 12 I went to N.A. \+ put in an hour or more of work there \+ then went to 2. Nat. Bk. Home to lunch soon after 1 — Fanny Kimball gave us a surprise by lunching with us — Afternoon painted until near 5 — then walked down to Wilmurts to make complaint of the 24×50 frame that they have just made for me — then to dinner. Eveg. at Home — Reading to Ralph \+ writing some notes — Charle came in.

## Right Page

### Wed. March 16, 1898

**Weather:** Overcast during part of day — pleasant p.m. — Rain at 11 p.m.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at usual time \+ worked with difficulty painting on Mad. Sq. Tower — until 1 o'clk — then to lunch \+ after that to Met. Opera House — Thro. Thomas Concert — on a ticket of Charls' — he occupying the seat next to me — Schumann's 4th Symphony was given — it was a trying ordeal to me, bringing back to me so vividly memories of many years ago when Annie \+ her young companions sat before me in their seats in the old Academy of music in 14th S. — In the Romanza one passage seemed to me to picture so perfectly the dying moments of Annie that tears coursed down my face \+ I felt that I was making a spectacle of myself — It was a very painful sort of enjoyment. After the concert I went to Camera \+ Hastings \+ there met the other members of the Building Com. — we discussed the details of school plans until after 5 — Then home to dinner — Emma Dreland there — after dinner I was obliged to go to Annual Meeting of Water Color Socy — A full meeting with the usual confusion in general discussion consequent upon incompetence of Presiding officer — Brown is easily rattled and cannot direct an assemblage if there is any degree of heat or animation — I wound up the O.W. Baker episode by the introduction \+ passage of a Resolution commending the paper \+ approving the purpose of the Co — My head ached so badly that I left before the meeting was over and walked up home at about 11.
