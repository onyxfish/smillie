## Left Page

### Wed. July 13, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The boys ret'd from New-Haven.

Heavy rain all the m'f. overcast P.M. I was engaged in doors all the morning got trunks out of store-room — big basket trunk down to the parlor \+ I got a lot of my things down in the parlor — lunch at 1 — after that went to Jas. Hart's studio 11 E. 14th St. but he was not in. Then I went to Calverly's \+ gave him a sitting until after 4 — then home \+ wrote until it was time for me to go to station \+ meet the boys — They came in on time at 5.40, all right, looking well, Home at once — Dinner at 6.30 — Mrs. White in orange — Eve'g I read to the boys until 9 — then I did some sorting of clothes etc — wrote a note to Katey. Elkin \+ took it to the G. C. Station \+ mailed it — To bed at 11 — \+ after I had a moderate Colic which lasted maybe half an hour.

## Right Page

### Thur. July 14, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Half clouded, humid \+ enervating day — After 6½ f't. I went in to Dr. Putnam's \+ tried to get some comfort from him — then to studio — grocer's, butcher's \+ h.a. From there I went to Jas Hart's — E. 14th S. but he was not in — back to h.a. \+ wrote business letters until 10'clk — then home \+ had lunch. After going to Dr. P's this m'f. I went to 42d S. \+ bought two trunks for Jim \+ Ralph. Afternoon I went to Calverly's \+ sat for him until near 5 o'clk. Jim went to him this m'f \+ he worked some time on his mask, making a very interesting thing of it. Mr. Kingsbury, of Lee \+ Lee, came about 6 with papers concerning Taxation of new site. I signed affidavit. Dinner at 6.30 — Mrs. White with us. I read awhile to the boys but my stomach discomforts stopped me. Later Charl came in — after he left I had a talk with Mrs. White \+ it was agreed that she is to return to her 34th S. quarters on Sat. next.
