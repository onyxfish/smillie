## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 29, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delicious day, cloudless + very mild. I got up feeling quite well after a quiet restful sleep + ate a good bkfst. I felt rather weak on my legs. Before 10 o'clk. Beede + Branch went with me to the Knoll + I showed them my stakes + explained to them in a general way the buildings that I proposed to put up. I also explained my plan of paying all their bills as presented with 10% in addition as their profit + they agreed - it was after 11 when I got back to the hotel + I found that I was very weak + tired - my stomach pained not with the neuralgic pain, but a different kind. I took Bismuth + Morphia + lay upon the bed for an hour + was much easier - Lunch at about 1.30 - wrote a lot of N.A. + S. letters + then sunned myself on the upper piazza for an hour or two feeling much better as time went on - A very light meal at 7 - then an hour of pleasant chatting with Miss Miller and Mr. Loring in parlor - Then to my Room and did some packing - To bed at about 10.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 30, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Elizabethtown.
Windsor Cottage.

Warm, cloudless day - up at about 7 + commenced packing my trunk - Bkfst. at 8 - after that the m'g was spent in intermittent packing, reading Tribune - interview with Scott Brown, etc, etc. Our three trunks were packed before 12 - Lunch at 1 - then hurried over to W. White's + to Mr. Maghee's + bid them good bye - then to Hotel to take stage at 2.30 but it proved to be one of Beede's nicest + easiest road wagons with a fine team - Mr. Loring + Miss March the boys + self were the passengers - It was 3 o'clk. before we got away + Beede told the driver to take us to Elizabethtown by way of Chapel Pond, Yuba Mill + New Russia - The drive was interesting the road better than over Spruce Hill + the time was 2 h. 50 m. I got through the day's work with but little inconvenience - with the boys I went to the Windsor. Had tea at 6.30. Every a cloudless, hazy moonlight and I walked on the Road for some time - To bed at 10.

There was no movement of the bowels, but I secured one by use of Suppository - The result was very scant + quite enveloped in mucus -
