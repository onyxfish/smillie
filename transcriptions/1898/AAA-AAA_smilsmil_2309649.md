## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 4, 1898

**Weather:** Dull \+ Raw.  **Temp:** [illegible]

last act in the Farmington drama.

Bel[illegible]. Com.

Dull \+ Raw. some snow flew in the after-noon. To studio for a few minutes - Then to Criminal Courts Bel[illegible]. Gen[illegible]. Sessions. Part III. I got there soon after 10 but had to wait until nearly before I got hold of McLaughlin \+ the Ass[illegible]. who was to dispose of his case. Then Kingsbury came along - we had to sit thro' a trial of a petty thief before the papers were ready. At a few minutes after 12, we were called up - bewith very little ceremony 7 was discharged. The clerk addressed me as 7 - \+ was disposed to discharge me. outside of the court I gave to 7 who had his daughter with him, a few kind words of caution \+ advice - he seemed to be extremely humble. I got home at about 1. \+ had lunch - w studio - \+ at 4.30 to N.A. of D. Beg. Com. Carnes \+ Hastings there - again we had an interesting time discussing possibilities \+ at last, in a general way, agreed upon a plan to begin upon the 110' S. facade \+ provide accomodations for the schools - two galleries for permanent [illegible] \+ three galleries for current exhibits - all at a cost of about 250 000 dollars.
Home to dinner at 6.30 - Every. Charl came in for awhile - to bed early!

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 5, 1898

**Weather:** Beautiful day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day. To studio at 9.30 \+ until 11.15 I worked at various things about. Then I went downtown under the mistake that the W.C. Socy. Auditing Com. were to meet at Pres[illegible]. Symington's office 16, 18 Exchange Place. Then to N.a. \+ to 2[illegible]. Nat[illegible]. \+ home to lunch at 1.30 - to studio at 2.30 and pasted some W.C. mounts \+ got them in press. After 4 I walked down to Keeps, B[illegible]. near 11[illegible]. \+ bought some pajamas for night wear - Then to N.a., then home to dinner at 6.30 - a quiet ev[illegible]. at home - reading a part of the trine to Ralph. Charl came in, having been out on his bicycle.
