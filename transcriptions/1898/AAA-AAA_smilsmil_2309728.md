## Left Page

### Mon. June 13, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An intolerable sort of day — fiercely hot \+ humid almost to saturation. Got to N.A. at 10.30 \+ started, with Frannis to help me, to go over my detailed Statement of the N.A. accts \+ make them agree with certain basic figures that have been demonstrated to be correct. The proposition is a very simple one but it seems to be very difficult to accomplish. We went over the check-book figures two or three times, getting different results each time — of course the trouble is not with the figures but with the figurers. I got home to lunch at 1.30 tired \+ disgusted — back to studio at 2.30. Frannis \+ I resumed our work \+ continued it without much success until 4.30 when, in answer to a call for a special meeting of the Council — Jas. Hart. Fredk. Dielman, F.S. Church \+ R. Blum came — Our meeting was in session until 6 o'clk. \+ altho' there was no quorum, business was transacted subject to subsequent Ratification. Then I walked up Fourth Ave. with F.S. Church. Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g just loafed about, doing as little as possible. Wrote out minutes of special meeting of Council, held this p.m.

## Right Page

### Tues. June 14, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very uncomfortably hot \+ humid day. Heavy thunder clouds, hung about \+ two or three times there were slight sprinkles of rain. Up early and before bkfst. wrote part of a letter to George. I got to N.A. at about 10.30 \+ at once set to work with Frannis \+ before lunch time had cleared up the income side of our accts. Home to lunch \+ after that we commenced work on the other side \+ by 4.30 had that within less than 5.00 of making a balance. This was very encouraging to me. After 5 I went to Mitchell Vance \+ Co. \+ left order to repair dining Room Chandelier. Then to Keeps \+ Co. — Then to Studio \+ Ryan's. Home at 6 — Dinner — Eve'g so hot. Then just short of 90° in my bed room with every door \+ window open — That for some time I sprawled upon my bed \+ later I rallied — finished my letter to George — wrote a short one to Kate, to accompany one written by Jnne, \+ mailed them before 11 — Then to bed.
