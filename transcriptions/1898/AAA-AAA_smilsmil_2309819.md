## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 18, 1898

**Weather:** Bright mf. \+ pleasant throughout the day  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up at 7 \+ had my [illegible] \+ then went back to bed for a half-hour rest — Bkfs. at 9 — After 10 I went to Dr. Yale's but he was out — then to Century hoping to see Maynard \+ from him get some acct. of what was done at the Bld'g Com. meeting last night — He was not in — then to Charl's where I met Dr. Allen who was making a professional visit — C. bring in bed with what appears to be an attack of grip — Home at 2 — dinner — Read until 4, then had an hours nap — Gal. came in at 5 — Tea at 6.30. A quiet evg.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 19, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day. Rain fell freely at night. Charl is now in bed with an attack of grip. I went to see him for a few minutes about 10 o'clk. Then to Studio — Johnston came before noon \+ commenced cleaning the windows. I left him \+ went to N.A. Taking a bundle of fire Ins. policies — Home to lunch soon after 1. Some writing \+ then I rested for an hour on my bed — At 4.30 I went to Cameron \+ Hastings' — meeting of Building Com. \+ we discussed plans for temporary School Bld'g until near 6 — then, with Dielman \+ Blum I walked down Madison Ave. until we met Nicoll — I walked back a few blocks with him — then went in to Charl's for a few minutes — then home to dinner — Evg. Council meeting, but the weather was so stormy that I did not dare to go out. Geo. came in on his way to the N.A. \+ I passed my papers over to him \+ he took them. I had a game of Halma with Ralph \+ he beat me as usual.
