## Left Page

### Tues. May 24, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A mean sort of day foggy \+ wet, with a very little humid sunshine. After bfkfs. I went to Carrere \+ Hastings \+ Saw C — \+ to him I told the set-back that the Counciel had given to building operations last night — Then to studio \+ wrote out minutes of last night's meeting — at 12 went to N.A. Grannis not there — Home to lunch at 1.30 — At 2.30 went to Butlers to order a frame — met the 69th Regt, as it came up 34th St. from the ferry \+ Hempstead — Then was a large crowd of people \+ some enthusiasm but it didnt impress me with the solemnity that I felt 35 or 36 years ago when I saw the same organization \+ others march down 5th Ave \+ Broadway on their way to the South and war — Later, Ralph joined me in the studio \+ we worked until 5 o'clk on his ants nest — Then I went to Canteny \+ attended meeting of Ways \+ Means Com. Arranging for meeting to take place Sat. p.m. next at his Marquand's house. Over at 6 — home — dinner — Eve read awhile to R — Charles came in \+ to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 25, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Raw \+ overcast — a chill east wind \+ some times a scotch mist \+ niggle. After 9 o'clk. a man from Fuller \+ Co, the paperhangers came to look at the papering in library \+ dining room to see if he wd. advise cleaning it — He does not approve of cleaning — advises new paper, of course. To studio at 10 \+ spent the m'f arranging files of catalogues \+ publications in my closet — Home to lunch at 1 — it was 3 before I got back to studio where I worked at the work of the m'f. until 6. A telephone man came \+ begged permission to fix two wires in my bl'f. Miller came in at the time — after fostering I at length agreed. Just as I got home I met Bessie Parke, who had been to the house, I asked her to return \+ dine with us \+ she did so, leaving immediately after dinner. Eve \+ I read awhile to Ralph — then my paper — I went to studio \+ got mounted pf. of "Goldsmith's Daughter" to put in frame to give to Tinie's daughter on her wedding next week Wed\.
