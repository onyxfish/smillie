## Left Page

### Fri. July 29, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fresh, pleasant mg. - The day was hot with a S.W. breeze - Thunderstorms passed north of us after 6 p.m. I went up to the house soon after 6kß - I wrote a letter to Mary Mr. \+ Mrs. Edgar spent most of the mg making an inventory of dishes etc. and linen - Before 12 I went to the P.O. \+ I called on Dr. Gardner to tell him that I was progressing favorably \+ that I wanted more medicine - He told me to come for it at 8 o'clk - back to the house - had a Talk with Morris about his drain that Austin has complained of - Dinner at 1.30 - after that, to house, feeling tired as my rest was much broken last night - I lay down on a bed \+ slept until after 5 o'clk - Then wrote note to Charl. - took it to P.O. Then to tea - At 8 went to P.O. \+ to Dr. Gardners - Then to Daw. Sayres \+ sat on piazza there until after 9 - Then to Chas. Sayres \+ read news paper until after 10 - Then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. July 30, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather variable - clouded with pleasant breeze - \+ blazing sunshine with muggy heat. Thundershowers after 4 o'clk. Rain at night - To the house soon after [illegible] \+ spent them \+ there. Mr. Fliminaudt scrubbing Warner \+ assistant painting kitchen \+ oiling floor - I did some moving of things \+ wrote letters - To dinner at 1.30 \+ again to house, wrote to Ryan, Miller, Douglass \+ Kate Leonard \+ took letters to P.O. before 6 - Tea at 6.30 - a number of our boarders off on a pic-nic to Salt Spring - Some little stomach discomfort at tea time but none of the pain that bothered me so much some days ago. A quiet evening reading newspaper.
