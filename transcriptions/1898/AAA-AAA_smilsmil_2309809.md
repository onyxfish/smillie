## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 26, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull \+ disagreeable until about noon, then snow began to fall, increasing until night when it was a real, old fashioned winter storm \- I got up at about 10 and spent the day in the parlor, writing letters \+ on N.A. work. Got a letter from Orlando Beede informing me that he had cut a road through to haul timber for my house to the top of the hill \- This is contrary to what I understood, because I was unwilling to trust him to cut a Tree \- he was to haul over the snow, going in at Neilson's entrance. \- I wrote to him \+ to Scott Brown. Got a letter from Beckwith enclosing check from Henry Farish for N.A. building fund \- $1000 \- Lunch at 1 \- nap between 3.30 \+ 4.30 \- dinner at 6.30 \- a quiet eveg with eveg Post, feeling uncomfortable \+ stupoid.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 27, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant, cold winter day. Heavy snow storm last night \- an old fashioned, blizzard-looking snowstorm \- clouds all cleared away in the mg, with cold bright sunshine. I got up and had a rub-down \+ sponge bath before I had my bkft. in bed at 9 o'clk. Then I read my newspaper \+ got up for the day before 11. Then down to parlor \+ spent the rest of the day there, doing considerable writing as well as bear-like pacing up \+ down the floor. Dinner at 2. at which I did the carving. Charl came in while we were at Table. Tea at 6.30 \- Katherine \+ Bessie were at Charl's \- where Kate went later in the eveg. George came in and spent the eveg with us.
