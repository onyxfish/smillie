## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 26, 1898

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day. To studio at about 10 \+ worked on my Water-Color which is now so teazed \+ worked over \+ fussed with that the last spark of life has gone out of it. It depresses me when I find that I can come no nearer to what I wish \+ try to do — but it is unreasonable for me to expect to do much better when I do so little work in painting — for the past two years I have really given more time to Academy work than I have to art work. Home to lunch at 1.30 — at 3 I walked up to 60th \+ Mad. Ave. to George's studio \+ spent an hour with him looking over his summer sketches \+ a lot of new work that he has under way — Then I walked down home, stopping at Yale's. He was asleep \+ I did not see him — Mr. Yo's acct. of his condition was not very encouraging. Home at 6 — Dinner before? — Gene Atwater with us — came down to spend the night with the girls \+ go to Catherine Cook's coming out party to-night. Jim acted as their escort to the door of the house at 9 o'clk — I read aloud to Ralph \+ had a game of Halma with him.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 27, 1898

**Weather:** Slightly overcast \+ rather raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

After 6 p.s. finished the letter, commenced last night, to S.R. Koehler — then I went to Acker, Merrill \+ Condit, 42d St. near Broadway \+ got bottle of wine, then to Lewis \+ Congers \+ got Burgundy Basket — then home — \+ then to studio, late. I worked on Water-Color until after 1 — then home to lunch — Again to studio \+ worked until near 4. Then I went down town to 150 Nassau St., So. Me. Cook's office. Annual meeting of the Trustees of the Ireland Real Estate Co. E.M. Cook — Walter \+ George — Jas. Wright Jun. \+ Self. The affairs of the Co. were discussed until after 6 o'clk. — then I hurried home, getting in late for dinner. Wh. The boys went to a S.S. Xmas entertainment at the Ch. cor. Park Ave \+ 34th St. — I was too tired to go with them \+ stayed at home in my easy chair in front of a pleasant fire.
