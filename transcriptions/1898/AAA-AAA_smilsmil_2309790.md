## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 17, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Awakened at 12 o'clk by a slight chill \- immediately made a tumblerful of hot Horlick \+ took 9 grs of Quinine \- little sleep during the rest of the night \- fever reached its turning point about 4 A.M. at 103¾° \- \+by 10 A.M. I was again normal. Yale came \+ brought Miss Thompson with him. I had a quiet day \+ slept much of the time \- Gel came in the early evening \- I passed a tolerably comfortable night

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 18, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold, raw day \- storm brewing apparently. I got up at 10.30 \+ dressed myself. Yale came about noon while Ge° was with me \+ gave us a spirited lecture on politics. Lunch at 1.30 \+ then Frannis came \+ I got through with a lot of Academy business. I got very tired in the afternoon \+ spent an uncomfortable eveg. altho' we were in the parlor which has been cleaned \+ looked cheerful with a bright fire \- Had a game of Halma with Ralph \+ to bed at 10. \- I lay awake until 2 A.M. then napped until 5. again awake for an hour or more \+ finally was wakened for the day at 8 \-

Chas. Collins \+ Jno. Valentine called to see me in the eveg.
