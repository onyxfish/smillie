## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 14, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very fine day - beautiful clouds and shadows with fresh breeze. It was nearly 9 before we had breakfast - Later, with the boys, I climbed Wood Hill to the point of Rock on the top from which I made a study nearly 30 years ago - a fair path now runs to the top \+ it was not at all a hard climb - It is a long time since I attempted any climbing \+, under the circumstances, I started with some misgivings. It was only a pleasant little jaunt - the views from the top were very delightful \+ we loafed about there until 12.30. The boys had their cameras \+ took snap shots. Got to hotel at about 1.20 Dinner at 2 - after that a nap \+ later, the Sunday Tribune - It was near 5 when with the boys we sauntered along the banks of the Bosquet River until 6.30 - Tea at 7 - Every Ralph \+ Jim packed their trunks - Kellogg has several times talked with me about my Keene 4 acres - to-night he offered me $1000 for it - To bed at 9.30 no pain to-day.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 15, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

St Hubert's Inn. Room 224.

Pleasant day - cool mtf. clear middle of day - cloudy mtf \+ afternoon - Up at 6.30 - bkf. soon after 7 \+ away on Stage at 8 - The ride over the mt. has never interested me - it was made harder to-day by the weak hind Spring over which I rode \+ that banged my weak head \+ stomach wickedly - We got to St H's Inn at 12 o'clk. \+ found that rooms had been given us on the top floor on the rear looking out to Kitchen \+ laundry - For two such Rooms \+ board for boys \+ self I am to pay $49 per week - stop. at 1 o'clk - we had luncheon \+ soon after that, with the boys, I went to my Knoll \+ spent some time there looking over the ground - It is 13 years since Amie \+ I were here - In that time a dense growth of pine \+ various deciduous woods have grown shut out all views - I asked Orlando B- to get labor for me to have it cut off - Then to the River with me boys - back to Hotel \+ Rested until dinner - near 7 - after that, on Piazza for a while - Men in Parlor listening to three ladies - piano, violin \+ cello. To bed at 9.30

Some pain during the hard ride of the mty - took Bis. \+ Morph. powder.
