## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 15, 1898

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - After b.k.f. Robt. Neilson came to me + said that W.G. Neilson + he would go over the proposed Road with me - I went to Mr. Maghee's Gate + there met W.G. N. + Mr. N. - also × × Otis, the game warden of the Reserve, who has a local Reputation as a Road builder - later, we were joined by Orlando Beede - We went over the line + it was discussed from all points showing me that there are so many interests + they are so opposed that I doubt if the project is carried through. One thing was made plain to me - if the Road is made it will Require strenuous Resistance to preserve a number of fine large trees - The practical men said at once they must go - Another thing was that if that Road is brought up as proposed I must get from W.G.N. The gore 18 Rods long + 2 Rods wide at base, or I cannot get such a Road up my hill as I wish. I got back to hotel at 11.30 - letter from deGrand Benedict - I wrote a sketch of Reply - Lunch at 1.30 - after that I used a glycerine Suppository after which I passed a small amt. of solid matter enveloped in mucus - I am now satisfied that the larger part of my trouble, if not all of it, is catarrhal - Later I took a tramp over my hill + windy brow + then to Maghee's Cottage to see how the work of his extension is getting on - to hotel at 6 - Dinner at 7 - in Parlor until 9 + then to my room.

I have felt weak + very uncomfortable all day with a discomfort in stomach + bowels which made walking a trial.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 16, 1898

**Weather:** A pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, growing milder - a shower came up about 4.30 p.m. with one or two thunder booms + soon passed over. I commenced my Carlsbad course in the m'f. Mr. Janeway + her family left soon after b.k.f. There is now quite a thinning out among the hotel guests.
I took a tramp over Windy brow + down through the woods towards The Putnam Camp + then back to the hotel at about 11. Wrote letter to deGrand Benedict + several business notes. Sent Mary's letter to Ralphe to Clear. - Lunch at 1.30 - Tried to finish letter to Mary Cook that I commenced a day or two ago, but I felt too stupid + uncomfortable - Just before the shower I went out + had a short walk + after the shower I went around the block feeling better after the exercise - Dinner at 7 - Evening in parlor until near 9 - then out on the Piazza + had a walk with Prof. Van Amrynge + a German, a member of the Century. To my Room at 9.30 -
A beautifully perfect Rainbow over The Giant after the shower.

Somewhat better to-day - a good deal of discomfort about the middle of the day making walking difficult.
