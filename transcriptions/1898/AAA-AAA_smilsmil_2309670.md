## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 17, 1898

**Weather:** Clear & windy - not quite so cold as yesterday.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Katherine & Bessie ret? from Washington.
Very soon after 8&p. I went to Allens - W. 47"
St. & was in his chair from 9.30 for about an hour
having a tooth filled. Then I walked down
Broadway to 23d. St. \+ went into Am. Art Ass'n
\+ had a hurried look at the Fuller Early
English pictures & the Dana Ceramics. Then
to N.A. \+ was there until 1 o'clk. attending
to business connected with mtg. of Central
Trust Co. Then home to lunch - To studio
at 2 - some more N.A. letters to write. Then
I took 24"×50" canvas, "Showers," from its
old stretcher \+ stretched it upon the new
panel-back. By this time it was 4.30.
So I went home \+ then to Century \+ attended
meeting of Ways \+ means Com. Nothing
of interest. Home to dinner. Katherine
\+ Bessie ret? from Washington, where,
of course, they have had a splendid time.
Read to Ralph for awhile. At 9.40 went out
on my wheel - rode down 5th Ave. to Washington
arch & back in half an hour - about 3½ miles -
Didn't find it very inspiriting - all alone -
streets too dull & wind too cold -

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 18, 1898

**Weather:** Early mt. clear - Rain began about noon - wet the rest of the day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio before 9.30 \+ worked
in a half-hearted way on Arno Valley - At noon went
to Safe Deposit, 5 Ave & 43d S. \+ made copy of Act of [illegible]
latine exempting N.A. from taxation. Then to N.A. \+
wrote a letter to W. Lee asking him to prepare a
[illegible] new site
bill for Beckwith \+ his com. or delegation to take to
Albany next Thurs. \+ I sent him copy of Act
now in force. Home to lunch - To studio \+ con-
tinued my work - Home at 5 \+ rested until
dinner time - Eve'g. with me boys until 9 -
Then to Chars' where Mary \+ Kate already
were. He played his orchestrelle until about
11 - Then we went home.
