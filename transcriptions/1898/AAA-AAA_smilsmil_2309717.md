## Left Page

### Sun. May 22, 1898

**Weather:** Brilliant mrf.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant mrf. — clouded later in the day, with cool east wind. Got up feeling very well \+ had bkfst. at the usual time. At 10.30 went to studio \+ then to Chas. Calverly's studio in E. 27th St. where I met him \+ his son-in-law. The latter, being an amateur photographer took five photo-shots at my head — back, front, \+ \+ profiles \+ a ¾ front — These are preparatory to the modeling of a bust that is to correspond to the one of Annie. After leaving them my head ached so hard that I returned home \+ was on my bed until dinner at 2 — I got through that \+ felt better — later in the afternoon I went to George's studio, 51 E. 59th St. \+ saw Nellie and the boys. Home at 6.45 — feeling very much dragged \+ ate a light supper. Every The boys were amused for awhile by Mr. White. I read for a time to Ralph \+ before I communicated to write to Wm Spense \+ to Apollos Stone — At 10 I went out \+ mailed the letters, \+on coming in was seized with a sudden, violent colic — I took remedies without effect on \!\! Kalodinard went for sale — Before he came the pain passed away more quickly than it came. He could do nothing but sympathise — \+slept at midnight \+ \+ soon fell asleep to be awakened by a return of the pain at 1.30 — again I took Codea without effect — The pain continued most distressingly until 3 o'clk — where it gradually passed away \+ I went to sleep exhausted.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 23, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain more or less all day — sometimes heavily — After bkfst. I went to Carriere and Hastings — but did not see them — saw H — for a moment \+ promised to return at 2 o'clk. Then home \+ feeling unfit for work I spent an hour or two on my bed reading. Lunch at 1 — after that, when the rain let up a little, I went to C. H's \+ spent an hour or more with them discussing certain changes that I wish to have made in some of the studio's windows to carry them up to the very ceiling — Then home — Ralph went with me to the studio \+ I worked with him until after 6 in making his Darwin nest for ants — Then home \+ had dinner — It was after 8 when I got to the council meeting. The meeting was a long one — Watrous brought in a Resolution stating that the Council did not, in view of the times, think it wl. be wise for the Bldg Com. to enter into any contracts prevention of new bldg. ... It was fully discussed — I favored the Resolution but not for the reasons given by W — He thinks the site is a mistake. I have not heartily approved of the plan of building the 109 S. Bldg., because I wished if possible to begin by building the best part of the building on the best part of the site — Then if we used up all of our money \+ could get no more we would have something to show for it — If we got stuck in the back part of the lot we would be in a bad way — The ways \+ means com. must accomplish something liberal \+ tangible before it will be safe for us to commence building. Home after 11 and right to bed.
