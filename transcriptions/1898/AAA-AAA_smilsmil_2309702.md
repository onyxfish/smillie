## Left Page

### Fri. April 22, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine April day - clouds, sunshine + a few very light spittings of rain. - I got to studio before 10 + worked until 11.30 on N.A. accts. Then until 1 o'clk. getting things ready for my etching class this p.m. - They are to bite + print. Back from lunch at 2. Six of the class in hand + they kept me going at full speed until after 6 o'clk. They are so very impatient + enthusiastic. It was all that I could do to attend first to the acid work + then to the printing. Geo. came in at about 5.30. Dinner at 6.30. Welf. Jim went to his school entertainment at W. Morses' + got in at 10.30. Charl came in at 9 + Geo. about 9.30 - C. had his letters of introductions, letter of Cr., + steamer ticket for George who is now booked to sail next Wed. at noon.

## Right Page

### Sat. April 23, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dr. Wm. Selkin + Katy arr. from Washington

Overcast most of the day - rain at night. To studio at 9.30 + worked awhile on M. A. accts. Then to N.A. + worked there the rest of the mng. Home to lunch at 1. To studio at 2 + worked until 5. Then home + got my wheel, rode down Mad. Ave. to 23d 55. Then up to the Park - into the Park to the Mall - then home at 6.15. Found that Katy + Geo. had arr. just a little before from Washington. I had to strip + change, so, it was 7 o'clk before we had dinner. The rain gave us a quiet + undisturbed evng. at home. The E's retired at 9 \+ I followed not long after.
