## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 27, 1898

**Weather:** Bright, rather cold + breezy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, rather cold + breezy day. I woke about 8 feeling refreshed — after my mt toilette + bkft. in bed I got up + went down stairs to dining room taking my N.A. papers prepared to do some work if able. Dr. Yale came about 11 — I tried to intimate that my progress down hill, as I looked back over the road, had been steadily \+ consistently increasing in ratio. I was in considerable pain while talking to him I spoke as I did because he seems to me to be still seeking for proof that my stomach pain is neuralgia in character. — It has seemed to me from the beginning last May that this was hardly open for discussion — He modified somewhat his line of treatment \+ prescribed the remedy (to be taken regularly) that has given me relief during the past summer — 10 gr. Bismuth ½ gr. Morphine — This, while not curative, gives my sore stomach a rest from pain — Miss T. had some ⅛ gr. tablets of Morphine I took one at 1 o'clk — + was under the influence of it all the afternoon — Grannis came in at about 4 with N.A. check book + I worked with him for about half an hour — Then Katie Elkin came in \+ made a very sympathetic call — At 6.30 while the others had dinner I ate a chop + nothing else \+ drank a little Burgundy + Vichy — about 8. I had considerable pain!

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 28, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

The day passed as the other miserable days before it. I was on my bed a good part of the time. Swe T. had a fire in the parlor + sat there for a time — George came in a little later Mr. Wm. H. L. Lee to consult with me concerning some new loans for the N.A. to take the place of a mtg that has matured + is to be paid off — Later yet I had a good deal of pain + got to bed soon after 9 — about midnight I had a slight chill followed by Temp. that reached 104° at 4 A.M. + then slowly fell, normal at 9. The pain seemed to be an intercostal neuralgia encircling the body over the short ribs.
