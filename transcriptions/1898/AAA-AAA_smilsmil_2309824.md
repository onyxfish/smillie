## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 28, 1898

**Weather:** Very cold, windy day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very cold, windy day - I got up with a headache which threatened to undo me for the day - after bkft., at about 10 o'clk I went up to Dr. Yale's \+ saw Mrs. Y. for a moment Y's condition is not so encouraging - then to Caron \+ Hastings \+ saw the drawings of new temporary bldgs for N.A. schools - learned that a call was out for a meeting of the Bedf. com. at 11.30 at C.\+H's - went to studio \+ then back to C.\+H's just in time for the com. meeting - we gave contract for excavating to Crimmins - the work to be commenced immediately and we made several changes in details of the bldg - notably, the enlargement of studio for Dept. of Etching. To lunch at 1 - after that I went to N.A. \+ spent some time there making out checks for instructors, etc. - Then to Sloanes - p? bill - then to Dist. Tel. Co. - Paid bill - then home - Dinner at 6. Jean Atwater had gone. - Evtf. Read aloud to Ralph from one of his books -

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 29, 1898

**Weather:** A very pleasant day \+ much milder than yesterday  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day \+ much milder than yesterday - Nicoll came to the house before I left. I got to studio about 10 after doing some N.A. writing - Maynard came in with a copperplate \+ I spent some time laying an etching ground \+ getting out my etching outfit \+ starting him on a "book-plate" for which he has a commission - He worked all day at my desk - About 12 I went to N.A. Home to lunch at 1.30 - It was about 2, or later, when the boys came in, having been to Van Cortlandt Park skating - They had had a very good time. To studio at 3 - much troubled because I cannot find the rough plan that I made in Keene for the house that I propose to build on the Pricknoll. - I daubed some pt. (four) "Cedars by Moonlight" \+ put them in press - About 5 I went to Yale's - Mrs. Y. sent out word that there was no change. - Home at 6 - dinner at 6.30 - Evtf. I read awhile to Ralph and then we had a game of Halma - Charl came in for a few minutes - Vander Perrine came to see me about the N.A. etching class \+ to tell me what he had learned of the chance of finding a desirable studio for the use of the class.
