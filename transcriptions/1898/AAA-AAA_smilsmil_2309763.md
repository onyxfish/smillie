## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 22, 1898

**Weather:** Bright mtf. but grew cloudy + muggy  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- In the afternoon there was a smart shower followed by mist like fog. Immediately after bkft. I went with Mr. Powell to my hill top to show him the views - back to hotel - got some paper + set to work trying to arrange my plans - a difficult piece of work - Then I wrote awhile in letter to Kate. Lunch at 1.30 - I did not feel well during the afternoon + kept to my room, working on plans + finishing letter to Kate. Dinner at 6.30 - my stomach discomfort culminated while at table - it was not really severe, but it made me miserable - then it passed away, but a sense of uneasiness remained. I sat in parlor with the boys for awhile - when they went to bed I went to my room - read my paper - then to bed.

Jim got his cleek + played golf all the afternoon - getting very wet, of course.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 23, 1898

**Weather:** Dull mtf., weather uncertain  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- a little sunshine struggled out but at last rain prevailed + soon after 2 there was a heavy fall of it; light showers afterwards. I developed a headache soon after bkft. that increased so that at noon I gave up + was on my bed until after 5. Then I got up, dressed + at 6.45 went down to dinner + ate very simply + sparingly. Before noon I went down to Scott Brown's + had a talk with him about payment of Fayes, etc. I wrote a note to A.W. Shields - County Treas'r asking him to send me Receipt for my taxes, paid on the 10" inst. I wrote to Kate Leonard asking her to bake a loaf of bran bread + send it to me by Exp. + I wrote a letter to Nellie + returned to her two letters from George. Eve'g. There was some sort of an entertainment in the Casino in aid of Red Cross work - the boys went but I did not. I walked a little on piazza + then to my room + to bed before 10.

No real pain in stomach to-day but lots of it in my head + a great deal of general discomfort.
