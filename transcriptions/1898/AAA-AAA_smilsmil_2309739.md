## Left Page

### Tues. July 5, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delicious day. Ther. at 68° where I got up in the mt at 7.30. I took a half hour walk before bkft. After bkft. with the boys, cleaned up bicycles, \+ soon after 11 we went off \+ rode about until near 1 - we went through the Park \+ down Orange St. - we called at Dr. Adams' \+ found that Nelie \+ Miss Joshay were off on a ride. We did not meet Mr. Downs \+ hurday Mr. Atwater, riding in the Park \+ stopped \+ talked with them. Mr. D., who is now blind, is very much broken. After lunch \+ a rest, I went with the boys to Lake Whitney where we rowed for a couple of hours \+ then home at 6 - dinner at 6.30 - well the boys set off their remaining fireworks back of the observatory where Dr. A., Nelie \+ Mils [illegible] were an audience - Nelie had walked up the hill with the others \+ seemed quite well. After the fireworks, Dr. Elkin took Miss J—, the boys \+ me up into the observatory where we spent an hour gazing thro' the 8½ inch telescope at Jupiter \+ Saturn - the definition was not good altho' the eveg. was very clear but Saturn presented himself at an admirable angle showing his rings at their best -
To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Wed. July 6, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day - had a half hour walk before bkft. At 10.20 the boys \+ I started on our wheels for East Rock having been told how to go around the East end fit to State St. \+ by Bishop's Gate enter the Trowbridge Road. It was a delightful ride - a very easy grade, a good road and charming woods \+ distant views - we got to the top of the Rock without trouble \+ there had a good rest \+ then down by the Farnham Rd. \+ home at 12.30 - I was delighted with the trip some portions of the ride reminded me strongly of European Roads - At lunch, as at bkft. I took a large dose of Thos. of Soda, acting on Dr. Putnam's advice - my bowels have been very sluggish. In an hour or more I was in pain charged full of gas - I was obliged to give up my trip to Saline Rock with the boys \+ they went alone \+ I went to bed \+ stayed there for a couple of hours - I was very uncomfortable all the rest of the afternoon \+ eveg. but I rode on my wheel for a little while before dinner - The boys got back soon after 6 \+ of course had had a good time -
Eveg. Mr. Bennett called \+ stayed until near 10 - then to bed.
