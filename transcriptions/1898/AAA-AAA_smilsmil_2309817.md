## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 14, 1898

**Weather:** Bright, very cold, with strong wind  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, very cold, with strong wind — about 10 o'clk. I started for the N. A. — There for a short time — then to Bank — to Brooks Bros — back to N.A. \+ home at 12. Having got material for my Report to be presented at the Academy meeting to-night I set to work at once. Lunch at 1.30 — after that finished Report — Had an hours rest before dinner at 6.30 — Katie \+ Bessie went to Opera — Gel came in at 8 on his way to N.A. meeting — I did not go because I did not think it wise to expose myself — Ralph \+ I had Halma, and he beat me badly.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 15, 1898

**Weather:** A fine winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine winter day. I had some N.A. writing to do so that it was about 11 when I got to the Academy — from there I went to the bank — on my way home stopped at Calverly's for a few minutes. Lunch at 1.30 — wrote for an hour after that \+ then rested on my bed for an hour. I went to Meade's studio, 51 W. 10:S, meeting of Ways \+ Means Com. Beckwith not there, owing to sudden death of his bro-in-law — Porter, deForest, J.F. Brown \+ Nicolle. Brown in excitement over letter sent to him by Andrew Carnegie in answer to B's appeal to him on behalf of the N.A. B. was one of C's guests at Pittsburgh a few days ago \+ thought that this was his opportunity. The gist of C's reply was that artists, being geniuses and not business men, were incapable of managing affairs of the magnitude of the enterprise now before the Academy \+ the sooner they learned this — took the advice of business men, called them to their aid \+ took them into business fellowship with them, the better it would be for them \+ the sooner they could attain the eminence that gave the Pittsburgh gallery its leading position. B. wished to give C. a "particular h—" but we all advised him, as the most dignified way, to pay no attention to it any further — Home at 6.45 — dinner — Ivory Halma with Ralph, who beats me now every time.
