## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 24, 1898

**Weather:** Pleasant, altho' not bright  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant, altho' not bright. I got to studio after 9 at once got to work on W.C. drawing — at it until after 11 — then to N.A. to Putnam's \+ to Flint's \+ home to lunch after 1 — Seeing Plummer lunched with Jim \+ Ralph lunched with Bronson Cutting. After lunch went again to studio \+ worked until after 4. then, to Putnam's \+ bought my last Xmas present \+ home at 6. Dinner sometime after — Charl dined with us — After dinner we had our Xmas — see the presents having been arranged in the parlor in groups — Ralph had his opening first, the others following by age. After 9 I went to studio — got the painting of the valley of the Arno from the Boboli Gardens \+ took it to Walter's house, leaving it for the young married couple as a wedding present — then home \+ to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 25, 1898

**Weather:** Bright  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright m'f. for awhile then a thin skein spread over the sky which seemed to promise a coming storm — Up at 7.30 \+ had my rub \+ scrub. Bkfst. at 9 — after that read the [illegible] paper for awhile — After 10 went to studio \+ from there to Dr. Yale's. I found him sitting up in a chair, evidently a weak, sick man. I chatted with him for about half an hour \+ then home. napped on the dining-room sofa before dinner — after dinner I had another nap — not feeling very comfortable. After 4 o'clk. I went to Ed.s Cook's — saw the family \+ more too, for the young Coffins were there — the first time I have seen them since their wedding — after that, to studio \+ then a walk — home at 6.30 but took no supper. Eveff. Read aloud to the boys for awhile. Geo. came in later.
