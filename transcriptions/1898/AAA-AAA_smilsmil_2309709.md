## Left Page

### Fri. May 6, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Sunshine for awhile in the m'f, then the sullen clouds came again \+ in the evening, rain. I got up with a severe headache that kept me very miserable all day - my right ear troubled me yesterday \+ last night, so, immediately after b'f'- I went to Yale. The trouble with the ear is a collection of old wax \+ eczema - Then to studio - to Pates - to the N.A. \+ to the Second Nat. Bank \+ home after 1 - I was able to get to studio at 2 - four Stothers came \+ with them a young girl, a model. I took them up to Maynard's room \+ there they worked until 5 - Then I went to Carrene \+ Hastings \+ there met the other members of the Bldg. Com. - we had an interesting session until 6.30 I asked for modifications of the lighting of the studios on the upper floor, one of which I have preempted for my etching class - My suggestions were favorably rec'd - Home at 6.30 - I found a note from Mrs P. H. Bathydt, enclosing a check for $200 for the Albert H. Baldwin fund - Etching class of the Na. - Ev'f I wrote to her -

## Right Page

### Sat. May 7, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another dismal, dull day - wind easterly and raw - heavy rain at night - Horace Winans called at the house this m'f \+ under the fiction of selling me an old painting of mine that he bought about 30 years ago, borrowed $50 - of me - It is a sad case \+ I parted with the money without regret except for his sake - When I got to Studio I found a letter from Chicago Art Institute offering me $50m for Water-Color "The Ford" - half price - but I took it. I worked about the studio until 12 - then to N.A. Home at 1 - lunch - to Studio at 2 - One of the Etching class there by appointment. He drew a head in the class yesterday \+ I told him that I w'd bite it for him to-day - I did so \+ then worked on it with drypoint \+ graver - It was about 5 when he left \+ 6 when I got home. Dinner at 6.30 - Ev'f Charl came in for a few minutes. I did not go to Centeiny because of the heavy shower - with accompaniment of distant thunder. Heavy thunder storm during the night.
