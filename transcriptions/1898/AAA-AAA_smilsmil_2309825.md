## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 30, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very mild, pleasant day — the effect upon me being weakening — I got to studio at usual time after making a search at home for the missing ground plans of the Keene house. At the Studio I searched — the disappearance is inexplicable to me. I wrote to O. Reede asking him to have Brauch get the measurements from the stakes on Pine Knoll — then I can reconstruct my plan — Then to work in Water Color \+ drew in \+ washed out, a lot of sheep. Home to lunch at 1 — at 2.45 at Rufus. Morton's office in Nassau St. meeting of Sub. Advisory Com. N.A. Mr. Morton \+ Mr. Schiff there, also T.W. Wood. Beckwith, Brown, Nicoll \+ self — out soon after 3 — I got to studio at 3.30 \+ worked until 4.30 washing out a large portion of immediate foreground — Then I walked up to Dr. Yale's saw Mrs. Y — \+ she told me that the Dr. went to Mt. Sinai Hospital. This Mr. \+ that Dr. Gerster operated upon him this afternoon removing his appendix — a very successful operation as reported by Dr. G. Then I walked up to Georgie's 139 E. 57 St. \+ found him in bed with grip and suffering a good deal — Home to dinner — Eve'g. Reading aloud to Ralph \+ some writing [illegible — ~10 words]

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 31, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A wet, disagreeable day, winding up with a cold, windy snow storm at night. As I got to my studio door in the mfg., Mr. Bishop met me \+, coming in, made a short call — Maynard was at my etching table at work upon his "ex libris". I set to work \+ etched in sky \+ water in an oval, the principal part of the work, so far as technical difficulties are concerned — About 11, Katherine \+ [illegible] \+ Jack Downs came for a call — It was after 12 when I went to the N.A. \+ arranged some bank business with Grannis — Home to lunch at 1.30 — then to studio \+ most of what remained of day light I gave to Maynard's plate getting it ready for the acid — At about 5 I went to Yale's \+ got a good acct. of him from Mrs. Y — who had spent most of the day at the hospital with him. Then home to dinner. Eve'g. Some reading to Ralph — \+ my newspaper.
