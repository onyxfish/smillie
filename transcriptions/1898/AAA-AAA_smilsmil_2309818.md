## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 16, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very fine day - Mrs. Saug was all day clearing my studio - I got around there about 10 o'clk to see how she was getting along - then I went to N.A. \+ gave Grannis some directions about making extracts from minutes for dee \+ dee having reference to the preparatory work done on plans for new bldg \+ work done - Then to bank - Then to Calverly's - got masks of self (the old one) \+ Jim \+ took them home - Lunch at 1.30 - Mrs. Whitley \+ her daughter lunched with us on Mary's invitation - Chicago ladies, met abroad, last summer. At 2 I went to studio \+ for two hours assisted "Mrs. Saug - then home very tired \+ rested until dinner at 6.30 -
Evg. very quiet, with newspaper.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 17, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, mild day until the afternoon when rain fell heavily for awhile - Evg. damp, but no rain - I got to studio at about 10 \+ spent the mg there, putting up curtains \+ arranging things generally - Home to lunch at 1 o'clk. After that, feeling very tired I lay down upon my bed \+ took the heart out of the afternoon, sleeping - Dinner at 6.30 - Evg. important meeting of Building Com. at Century - I prepared myself \+ got as far as Park Ave. cor. when I made up my mind that I would be safer at home, so I returned - Had another game of Halma with Ralph \+ his nimble wit beat me again - It is some time since I was able to get a game from him.
