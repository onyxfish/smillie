## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 30, 1898

**Weather:** Very cold, clear day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very cold, clear day - after bkfst., to studio for a few minutes \+ get water-color box \+ some brushes - went to N.A. \+ left them there - then to Am. Art Ass'n. (to which Kirby had given me a card) arrived near 1 o'clk. I looked at the Stewart collection of pictures. Gen Hearn introduced me to the wife \+ daughter of ex-mayor Hewitt. - Then to N.A. \+ spent the best part of an hour reducing with clean water a portion of the foreground of "The Ford - Svet'f." Then home to dinner - I had a long nap in the afternoon. - Svet'f. after reading to Naefsh I went to Walter Cook's \+ There was Told by Walter \+ Louise of the engagement of Elizabeth to Mr. Coffin. A matter to them of mingled hope \+ fear - pain \+ pleasure - The young man is very highly spoken of - For the present the matter is to be kept secret - Home at 10 - Geo. \+ Charl there. - To bed about 11.30

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 31, 1898

**Weather:** Snow all day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow all day. Before I went to studio I wrote several notes - after getting to studio I painted on Jan Luis Ray fussing \+ worrying over the middle distance trying to get a grey tone \+ color too - Home at 5 and had a rest before dinner - Svet'f to N.A. Council meeting - several absentees - Wood, Hart, Bolton Jones \+ Dielman - D- came in after adjournment - J.G. Brown presided - I spent some time after meeting with Grannis \+ Collins - Walked up with Beckwith.
