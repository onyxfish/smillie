## Left Page

### Mon. Nov. 28, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Went to N.A. of D. council meeting.

Pleasant day overhead but very nasty under foot — Had my bkfst in bed, then up very soon \+ to work writing \+ preparing for Council meeting in the eveg. I walked to the N.A. getting there about 11 o'clk — Saw Mr. Ball, who wishes to solicit for funds, representing the Ways \+ Means Com. I was not able to get through with all the work that I had laid out to do. Went to O'Brien's \+ ordered a half dozen Rubber stamps in preparation for a division of 2d Nat'l Bank Acct into three separate accts — Then walked home \+ had lunch at 1 o'clk — Some writing after that — Maynard came in bet. 3 \+ 4 o'clk — Then I had a nap, in preparation for the excitement of the eveg — Dinner at 6.30 — At 8 Gel came \+ together we went to the N.A. where my coming was a surprise to the Council — I was cordially welcomed — I got through with my part of the business soon after 9 — Then walked up home. Found Charl there — To bed at about 10.30 — feeling very well after my days work.

## Right Page

### Tues. Nov. 29, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Sickly looking mt., clearing before noon to a mild sunshine to be overcast later. I got up \+ went down stairs to breakfast — I feel that now I am getting my strength so very rapidly that I must not coddle myself any longer — I had N.A. letters to write \+ as soon as they were finished I went out — first to Hugh Douglas's — Then to Mauchattan storage — then to studio \+ Ryan's — Then to Bayerdorfer's — and at last to the N.A. Grannis was not there \+ I could do no work without him — so I went up to the galleries \+ spent half an hour looking over the very dismal collection of paintings — Home to lunch at one o'clk — Then a young man, a bro. of Baumann, the tenant of N.A.'s bldg. \#3 5. Nicholas Pl — came with a very impudent message because certain improvements in the house were not being made fast enough — \+he threatened to leave the house (S.N.R.?) which I told him he might do — I was very short with him — Then Douglas came \+ I explained what I wanted done to my studio floor — Then Ryan came \+ I showed him what I wanted changed in the bath room — Then I had a nap — \+ then dinner at 6.30 — After reading eveg paper I had a game of Halma with Ralph — Snow at 10 P.M.!
