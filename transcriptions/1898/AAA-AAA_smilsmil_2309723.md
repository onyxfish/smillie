## Left Page

### Fri. June 3, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day, altho clouded part of the time. slight rain at noon Douglass + his men came while we were at bkft. to commence work upon the parlor ceiling. I worked with them for a while getting things stowed out of the way. It was after 10 when I got to studio. There I worked most for one, biting the plates of some of my etching class. Home to lunch + back soon after 2. Perrine, Kelly, Sterne, Stahr + D. There I was kept hard at work at my press printing proofs of their plates until 6 o'clk. + then I went home very tired. Dinner. Eve'g a little reading to Ralph then I tried to write a letter to Geo. but was too tired. Chase came in for a few minutes.

## Right Page

### Sat. June 4, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy day with strong northerly winds. After bkft. Douglass came — we discovered a slight leak coming thro' the ceiling that is now being fixed. I went to Ryan to have him look after it. It was a few minutes after 10 when I got to N.A. Attended to some business — then to 2d. Nat'l Bank — back to N.A. then to Con. Gas Co. about Gas Range — then to Calverly's. He has made a start on my bust + a mistake in making it 5/8 scale instead of 3/4. Home to lunch. At 1.30 Ryan came + spent an hour in planning improvements in 2d Story Bath Room. Then I took bust of Annie to Calverly's that he may model a bust of me to correspond in size. Home at 4.30 + feeling tired I rested. Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g at about 9 went to Century — Rather a slim meeting — a fairly pleasant eve'g until 10.30 — then left with Drs Yale + Gerster. Home + to bed.
