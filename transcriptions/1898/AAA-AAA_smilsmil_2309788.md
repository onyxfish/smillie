## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 14, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, chilly mtg — some sunshine a little later \+ then rain. Yale came \+ found me down stairs where I had a fire in the dining room. I feel better in spite of a feeling of great discouragement — I did considerable work in the way of writing business letters — Charl came in for a few moments on his way to R.R. station for Boston. Maynard called in the afternoon. Evett in the dining room until after 9. men came \+ put down carpet in my room \+ on the stairs to-day.
