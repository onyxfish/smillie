## Left Page

### Sun. March 13, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, mild + weather depressing sort of day with a shower late in the afternoon after which the weather cleared in the evef. In the evef, as the boys were not inclined to go on a tramp we all stayed at home + after dinner I indulged in a nap. Walter + Louise called, + later, Liz. Cook + her Coffin. He is a bright, pleasant spoken fellow + makes an agreeable impression — Evef. fixing hanging shelves in R's room. Charl came in for awhile still much under the influence of a severe cold.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 14, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A charming day — bright + cooler — Got to studio about 9.30 + put in a long + hard days work painting on 24×40. Mad. Sq. Garden Tower by moonlight — I dont know yet whether I shall make a spoon or only spoil a horn, but so far, I feel encouraged. Home at 5.30 very tired — I had an hours rest before dinner. At 8 went to Council meeting — routine business except a resolution that was passed at my request appointing a committee N.A.s, Mesd. Dielman, Frank Jones, Alfred Jones, S.W. Perry + Geo. Yewell — to take charge of the audit at the end of the fiscal year — also to make a thoro' reexamination of the books of acts., the method + manner of keeping them — to enquire + learn the methods pursued by Farrington in his defalcations — + to report to the Annual Meeting May 11. '98. Home at 11.
