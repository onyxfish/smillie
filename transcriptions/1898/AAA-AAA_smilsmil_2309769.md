## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 3, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An exceedingly hot day — a very high winds in the very early m'f. but most of it died away + there were but few clouds. After b'kf. I went to the Knoll + found Simonds, helper + Young at work laying out the lines of the proposed road to take the place of the old road that comes up a very steep grade to the hotel through a sand gulley — I was with them all the m'f as a path was cut through the dense growth on the north side of my Knoll — after lunch at 2 it was so hot that I did not go down to the finish. — It was after 5 o'clk when Simonds reported that all the work was done + I paid him by check. Then I went to the Knoll + also over the line of the proposed road + copied the readings that he had put on the various stakes + then to hotel. It was after 7 o'clk. when we had dinner — Eve'g. the ladies were out in full force for the dance in light + gauzy attire — I was on the piazza with the boys until 9 — then to our rooms which were very warm.

The 75 gr. Aloin with Bell's + Strych. acted very thoroughly + gave a complete evacuation in two movements — felt stronger + had no pain —

## Right Page

### Sun. Sept. 4, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Atrociously hot m'f. — Thunderstorm a good part of the afternoon — pleasantly cool, clear eve'g. Up at 8. bath + rub-down — b'kf. at 9 — after that, the day was so hot that I could do nothing — sat about, read awhile + wrote out results of surveys on roads, old + proposed, to send to W.G. Neilson. Dinner at 2. After that read until the thunderstorm had passed + then took short stroll with Ralph. Returning, he stopped at hotel + I went on to Mr. McGee's where I had a pleasant visit until almost 6.30, seeing Mr + Mrs. McG — the two sisters. Then to hotel + had tea at 7. Eve'g. on piazza + in parlor listening to the trio (piano, violin + cello) and reading newspaper until about 9 — then had a half-hours walk on the Tennis Court + watched the moon come up over the Giant.

Had a slight attack of pain about 8 p.m.
