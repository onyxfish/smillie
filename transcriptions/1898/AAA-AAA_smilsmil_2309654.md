## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 16, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

my 65th birthday.

A bright winter day- with a cold wind from the north west. Bkfst at 9.30 - soon after 11 I went out with the boys - we rode up Columbus Ave. to 110° S. - then walked to the N.A. site \+ looked about there - then to S. Luke's Hospital \+ the Cathedral of S. John - out upon the Morningside Terraces \+ then to Columbia \+ made a circuit of the grounds - then to the Central Park, across to 5th Ave. \+ down to 102° S. \+ Mad. Ave. where we took a car. By the time I got home I was quite used up by a severe \+ peculiar headache. I was able to take some dinner but was obliged to go to bed soon after where I celebrated my birth day in misery. I got up at about 9 p.m. \+ was able to go to studio \+ close the windows that have been open all day - Then back \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 17, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** 3-

Mary went to P'k at 3.30
Katherine \+ Elizabeth went to New Haven at 3.

Pleasant; to studio at about the usual time \+ altho' I felt very slim after yesterday's performance I managed to do some work on Susquehanna drawing. It is doomed \+ cannot now be made anything but a very commonplace piece of work. Home to lunch after lunch, feeling the need of rest, I lay up on my bed untill 3 - then I went to Allen, dentist in W. 47° S. Had half an hours work upon one of my teeth - then I walked to the NA \+ was in time for meeting of the building com. met at request of Conese \+ Hastings to re consider the suggestion made by C. H. previously, to build the facade first. They have had a builders estimate \+ it exceeds the amt. they had given. We discussed the matter very seriously untill 6 o'clk - without any definite conclusion. Then home to dinner. To NA at 8.15 - Council meeting an interesting meeting but not protracted. The subject of a free Sunday resort was brought up - as part of arrangement of coming spring exhibit \+ every member of the council was in favor of it.
