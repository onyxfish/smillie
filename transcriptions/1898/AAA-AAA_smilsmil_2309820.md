## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 20, 1898

**Weather:** Disagreeable, nizzling, raw day  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ I have a head to match it — After bkfs. I attempted to write something that Nicoll wishes to be added to the NA pamphlet prepared by Ways + Means Com. — but I could not do it — Nicoll came + I was not ready for him — Then I went to Charl's — Still in bed — then to studio — all the m[e]n hauling out pictures + frames + making the place look like business — Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 — most of the p.m. in my big chair — unfitted for anything by headache — At 5.30 went to Charl's — up and dressed, in library — Gp.? There + W. Tom Adams — the first time I have met him. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Quiet eve[ning] — Ralph read aloud from Dickens.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 21, 1898

*Got to work again*

**Weather:** A nasty day, muggy, foggy, wet + raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

— everybody is down with the grip — After bkf. I wrote a new paragraph to be inserted in the Treas[urer']s. Statement in the little NA. pamphlet issued by the Ways + Means Com. to take the place of the one in the first edition that stated that the School Bld[g] to cost $200000 was to be built at once on 109th S[t]. The new paragraph states that a temporary Bl[d]g is to be put up at once + the main Bl[d]g on Cathedral Boulevard is to be commenced very soon. Nicoll came + waited until I finished writing + then he took it away with him. Then I went to Charl's — saw him for a few minutes — He is getting along slowly — then to Spaldings' + bought Caddie bags for him + Ralph — for Xmas. Then to Charles's + bought a bottle of wine — Then to Douglass — then to studio + set to work almost before I knew it, on a Water Color that I commenced last Spring" On the Susquehanna near Binghamton". Home to lunch at 1.30 — back before 3 + worked until 4.30 — at 5 went to Charl's — there until 6.30 then home to dinner — Eve[ning] with newspapers + then a game with Ralph, when I succeeded in beating him because he did not play as well as usual.
