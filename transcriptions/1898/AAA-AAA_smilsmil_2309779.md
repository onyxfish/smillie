## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 25, 1898

**Weather:** Cloudy & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy & windy day - quite cool & not over pleasant, I got up at 7.30 & had the exercise that I take now regularly, my hair mitten & rub & a sponge bath - feeling very well, altho' there was just the least possible trace of uneasiness or tenderness in the region of the stomach - Bkfst. at 9. Loafed about until near 11. Then the boys & I walked down the valley going to Munroe Holt about 3½ miles - I walked the distance without fatigue but feel a little heaviness under my waist band - We got there about 12 - Holt was at home & recognized me without trouble. I enquired about my taxes & saw his roll as Assessor - My Pine Knoll is down to Geo. Smiley - 1 Acre - lot 34 - name, acreage & lot no. being wrong - correction was made - He told me that altho' as Assessor he had given a valuation he did not know just where my lot was. As a precaution apt. getting over tired, I had him hitch up a team of horses & take us to the foot of the Reade Hill - We walked up it, getting in at 1.15 - Rested & had dinner at 2. I felt considerable discomfort from 3 to 4 - Then feeling better, with the boys I went to the clearing of Mr. Maghee - about 3/4 of a mile away & a little climb - I should not have done it - back at 5.30 feeling quite tired & discomfort returned - Rested until 6.45 - Then had a light meal - felt better as I sat in the parlor after it, doing nothing - later, wrote a letter to Charl. & then it was evident to me that I was over tired - & the stomach discomfort returned - To bed at about 10.

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 26, 1898

**Weather:** Clouded  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouded m'g. milder - the middle of the day sunny & warm - later, clouded over & rain about 6 p.m. Bkfst. at 8.30 - It was nearly 9.30 when I went with Young to the Knoll & set him to work at chopping out the space where I have decided to put the dining room & kitchen - I was with him some time & then returned to the Hotel. I was surprised to meet Alex. Schilling - we sat & talked until near 12 - Then we walked over to the Knoll - Discomfort began to come upon me about 12.30 increasing until after 1.30 when I had lunch - It passed away entirely after 3 when I went to the Knoll & had Young help me make measurements for the foundations & drive stakes - It was after 5 where I was driven off by a shower. Then to Hotel - Dinner at 7 - Eveg. I wrote part of a letter to Uncle Willie - To bed at 10.
