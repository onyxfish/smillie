## Left Page

### Sun. April 24, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull, mild day with heavy thunderstorm in the afternoon + wet evening. Bkft at 9 - loafed about chatting with the Elkins + reading newspaper until noon - Then I went to studio - wrote a note to Hungerford + had a short walk before dinner at 2 - I called on Walter Cook before dinner + he called on me after it- apropos of a dinner next Tues. eve'g. I took a short walk with the boys after the storm + before tea at 6.45 - Evelf. Katey, Wm + I, looking over European photos. Wm retired before 9 o'clk. feeling out of sorts - Chase + Geo. came in later + went away at 10. — To bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 25, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still dull raw weather, with now + then a very little spitting rain - I worked most of the day on N.A. accts Afternoon, about 5 o'dk. I went to Carriere + Hastings and spent about an hour going over the drawings now in progress for the School part of Nella building - then home + had a half hours rest before dinner - Katey + ours had been invited by Mary Cook to dine with her, but Cl -, having taken cold, begged off - so they dined with me. Evelf. Council meeting - got there at 9.30 — nothing of importance - George bid the members "goodbye", as he is to sail on Wed. + we walked up to 38th S. together.
