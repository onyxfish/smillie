## Left Page

### Wed. June 1, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another charming day, altho' very warm. Went to Douglass' \+ to Millers before going to studio - a floor finisher came to see about finishing the floor \+ gave me estimates - will plane the floor for $55.00 or will scrape it for $40. Then with Ralph went to Butlers - The N.A. to 2d Nat. Bk \+ to Britelliers where I bought two umbrellas - one for self, the other for Ralph. Home to lunch at 1.20. To studio at 2.30. Dr. Gerster came in soon after \+ stayed for nearly two hours - He says he wants to buy my painting "After a Storm" - He was much interested looking over the etched plates by my etching class. I partly bit the plate by Santee. Then to 23d S\. \+ bought some cheese cloth for printing. Home at 6.30 - got a very pleasant note from H.G. Marquand \+ acknowledgment of my plf. etching after Alma-Tleema - left at his house last over\[illegible\]. Read awhile to Ralph. About 9 I went out on my wheel \+ up Fifth Ave. to the Park \+ made a circuit of it, and then home at a little before 11 - a glorious moonlight night - wonderfully fresh, fragrant \+ beautiful in the Park.

## Right Page

### Thur. June 2, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine day, altho' cloudy \+ at times looking very like showers - clear moonlight - night. At studio I laid rebiting grounds on four or five of the plates of the students - varnished them \+ then went to Richter Mf\. Co. in 23d S\. \+ made selection of burlaps to cover hall wall at home. Then to Patis \+ made final payment of $50. for plate of "Goldsmiths Daughter." Then to N.A. Then to Con. Gas Co. \+ inquired about small gas stoves - then home to lunch. To studio at 2.30 \+ spent the afternoon biting upon the plates on which I had laid rebiting grounds in the m\[illegible\]. Out at 6 \+ walked until 6.30 - then home to dinner. Eve\[illegible\] read a short time to Ralph \+ Jim. Charlie came in for a few minutes. I wrote a note to Tinie whose daughter was married yesterday) \+ to Mr. Barhyat - It was after 10 that I went out on my wheel \+ rode down 5th Ave. to 23d S\. \+ around. In at 11 \+ to bed.
