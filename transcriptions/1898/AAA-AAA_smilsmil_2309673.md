## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 23, 1898

**Weather:** Sunshine at last — a pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ got up feeling very much out of sorts and did not feel able to work all day. I got to studio at 9.30 \+ fooled around until 12 — Then I sauntered down to N.A. Collins has not made his appearance — Then home to lunch — to studio — frame for San Luis Rey came \+ for an hour I worked at that painting, varnishing it out, glazing \+ adding a few touches — About 4.30 Barse, Faxon \+ Maynard came in \+ we talked over the arrangements for the Friday eveg meeting to be held at the N.A. until after 5 — Home — had hard work to get through dinner — a sudden movement of bowels drove me away once. Eveg. Mary \+ Mollie Adams went to Charle's. I worried for a time near my personal [illegible] \+ then went to bed.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 24, 1898

**Weather:** Very pleasant day  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- to studio at 9.30 but feeling very much out of sorts, so that I could not do any work — at last I went to N.A. there found Collins at work upon the books. I spent the rest of the mg there, supervising him \+ working myself to get a clear statement of the condition of the Building Fund \+ the Maintanance Fund — Home to lunch at 1 \+ at 3 again I went to N.A. There until 6 \+ there my head was awhirl from the complexity of the accts. which have not been kept as intelligently in the matter of separation of funds \+ their incomes as they should have been. I hope now to be able to straighten the tangle out. Dinner at 6.30 — Eveg. Diring \+ his wife called to see Mary \+ the girls \+ stayed for an hour — Then Charl came in \+ spent the rest of the eveg.
