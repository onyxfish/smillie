## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 22, 1898

**Weather:** Dark [illegible] all day \- high winds \+  **Temp:** [illegible]

very heavy rain at night. The wet did not really come until afternoon. In the w\+ Uncle W. \- Kate \+ Ralph made an excursion up to Morningside Heights to see the sights up there \- The new Academy Site, among others. I went to Studio \+ taking an old canvas on which I had spread a lot of palette cleanings on top of an old painting I sketched a view from a Belvidere in the Boboli Gardens, looking down the valley of the Arno. In the afternoon I practised a slight color scheme sketch. Sp\+. \+ m\+p\+. for medium. Every I was glad enough to stay at home. I finished reading Evangeline to Ralph who really seems to enjoy it.
