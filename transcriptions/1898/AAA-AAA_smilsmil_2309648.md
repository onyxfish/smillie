## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 1, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ bright, cold day, but disagreeable under foot because after the rain last night There was sleet \+ then a little snow. I got to studio at 10 \+ for a time engaged arranging N.a. papers, letters etc. \- Then I made tracing with pen of Susy, charcoal sketch, preparatory to getting outline on the Water-Color panel. Had a short walk on the sunny side of Park Ave. before going home to lunch at 1\- Back to studio at 2.30 \- At 3.30 went to N.A. and wrote a business letter or two before the meeting of the Bldg Com. met at 4.30. Dielmann, Maynard \+ Jones There \- we had an interesting discussion over the plans until 6\. Home to dinner at 6.30 \- quiet eve at home. Jim went to a boy's party at Rob. Putnam's.
