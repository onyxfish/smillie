## Left Page

### Thur. Nov. 10, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain most of the day. Yalle came in the m'g \+ runged me not to be discouraged — nevertheless, I am much discouraged. I had a complete evacuation of the bowels before noon — \+ soon after my temp. was normal. I had little headache \+ slept much during the day. It was about 5 when I got up, dressed \+ went into the front room while my bed \+ room had an airing. I was there until 9 o'clk. when I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. Nov. 11, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy \+ windy for the most part of the day — cloudless afternoon \+ wet. Awake early after a night much troubled with headache. I feel better in the m'g. Dr. Yalle came before 11 \+ said what he could to give me hope. After he left I had a satisfactory clearing out of the bowels, thanks to a combination of Comp. liquorice Powder — Carter's little liver pills \+ glycerine. After resting awhile I get up at. 12 or 1 — \+ had my lunch in my room. A little later I went to the Parlor, kindled a fire \+ spent the p.m. \+ wet. there, dozing \+ reading. Maynard came in — then Bolton Jones, as member of the School Com. to inquire about Etching class — then W'. Wm \+ Lee with $1600 from Auston M'g. Then I had my dinner — wrote to Frannis \+ Lee \+ Lee \+ worked awhile over my personal bank act. Charl came in for awhile. To bed at 9.30.
