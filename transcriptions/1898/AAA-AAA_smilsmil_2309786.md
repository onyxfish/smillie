## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 9, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delicious day as I could see from my windows but, altho' I got up & dressed, I was not able to go out & enjoy it. I got thro' the mtf very comfortably but about 4 o'clk. Stomach discomforts began \+ continued until about 6 o'clk. when they passed away. I was in bed until near 2. Then, Taking advantage of the absence of Miss Thompson I got up and dressed \+ at 2. o'clk. ate very carefully a simple dinner, but that, or something else, disagreed with me as already stated. Charl. came in for a while \+ then Geo. came, \+Geo. Cook \+ Emily \- by 6 o'clk I was very tired \+ was glad to get back to bed again. Later Miss T. gave me a rubbing, massage \+ a sponge bath, after which I felt much better. Dr. Yale made a long call about 11 A.M. I had a quiet eveg. and fairly comfortable night's rest.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 10, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A bright \+ very pleasant day \- After a fair night's rest I had my bkft. at about 8. Granniss came with check book \+ I made out some No. checks \- Then Dr. Yale came \+ it was 12 o'clk before he left \- I got up \+ dressed \+ after one o'clk. had a substantial meal. About 3, Ralph \+ I went out to Park Ave. \+ I walked slowly down to 34. S. \+ then up to 39\. S. \+ home \- no actual pain but a feeling of tenderness \+ distension over the stomach with weakness. On my bed until 6 \- more to eat at 6.30 \- George came in about 8 on his way to Council meeting \+ Charl. came in half an hour later.
