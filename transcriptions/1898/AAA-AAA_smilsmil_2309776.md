## Left Page

### Mon. Sept. 19, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very disagreeable day, heavy smoke filled the air which made the sun look like a copper ball — the air had an unpleasant chill — afternoon There was a heavy shower. Bkfp: at 9 — great times with an excursion party — Mr. Wicks had another party going his new Chapel Brook trail \+ asked me to go — I wrote some notes \+ business letters \+ then out for a walk going over the wood road that runs on the base of noon mark. Then, at last I got out paper \+ drawing materials \+ commenced to plot out the ground on Pine Knall \+ make plans for the house. Lunch at 1.30 — more work — Then out for another walk but the Rain drove me in. I got some exercise on the piazza. Dinner at 7. Eve'g in parlor —

I got up feeling very well after an unrestful night — no soreness in stomach or bowels — had a good rub down \+ felt much encouraged — but, as yesterday — discomfort commenced about 12 \+ increased until sometime after I had lunch to reappear again about 5 to continue until a little before dinner — Eve'g. comparatively comfortable — I am discouraged, \+ would like to start for home to-morrow. I would if it were not that some things here, about the plans must first be settled.

## Right Page

### Tues. Sept. 20, 1898

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very clear atmosphere, cloudy \+ windy \+ cold. I got up at 7.30 feeling very well \+ went through with my light exercise \+ rubbing from neck to heels with hair mittens — but I took no Carlsbad salts — Bkfp: at 8.30 I had a good, brisk walk after bkfp. \+ then got to work up on plans of house for Pine Knall — another short walk before lunch at 1.30 — again at work on drawings but made slow progress — This morning, after bkfp., I had an interview with Brauch, Orlando Beede's carpenter — It was after 4.30 when Jim, Ralph \+ I went to the top of Roaring Brook fall — back at 6.25. I was not able to eat any dinner but got some bread \+ butter \+ Budding \+ took it to my Room — After 8 made some Beef tea \+ ate my bread \+ Budding — At 9 o'clk went down to Public Room \+ at 10 had a brisk walk on the Tennis platform — Then to my Room —

Had a talk with Dr. Hobbs this m'f. I explained fully to him my symptoms \+ the progress of my trouble \+ asked him if he thought I might be doing myself harm by staying here instead of putting myself under treatment at home without delay — He advised me to try Castor oil, 10 m. in capsule, 4 times a day — \+ 10 gr Tablet of Salol after meals — I took the Salol after lunch \+ it distressed me so much that I could not eat any dinner — Eve'g, before going to bed I had some Diam, but I took Salol but once.
