## Left Page

### February, Sunday 9, 1890

Bright, windy & cold — a good winter day — Up late — bkfst. bet.
9 & 10 — then read newspaper —
After 11 went out with Jim & Ralph
walked up to Grand Central Depot
& over two of the bridges north of the
depot where they could see the cars
& engines — Back at 12.30 — Dinner
at 2 — At 4.30 I went out alone
& walked up to 72°.5 - & 5th Ave.
then through the Park & out at 59:
5: & home at 5.45 — Tea at 6.30.
Swelf. Chase came in & stayed
until after 10 — To bed before
11 —

Kate, the cook, in bed all day —
Dr. K— says that she is improving.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 10, 1890

Overcast [illegible]. Some flying snow —
about the middle of the forenoon 't clear-
ed beautifully — after which the day was
bright & cold. Got to studio about 9.30
Got out canvas 20×30 upon which I had
painted two or three years ago an evening
effect, based upon sketches on Jones' lake
at Montrose — very low toned & very crude.
I set palette & by hard work succeeded
in painting over very nearly the whole
thing getting some pretty good color &
handling forme. The composition, as is
usual with me, is fundamentally bad.
Home to lunch as usual & back — Home
at 6.30 — dinner — Swelf. Spent a
short time looking over Punch & Ill.
Bond. News — then wrote a letter to
Kate & got to bed at 10.30.
