## Left Page

### February, Monday 17. 1890.

A niedly overcast day — got to studio about 10.30 — Fixed frame f etchings & hung it back of screen — Fixed proof of Goldsmith Daughter & took it to Butler & put it in frame for him to send to Louise & Walter as our Wedding present. Then got out 20 x 30 canvas & with charcoal blocked out a subject from a last summer's sketch "A view of brook with ducks". Home to lunch as usual — After that did not go to studio again but staid at home & took a nap — then wrote a little on letter to Mary — dinner — Eve'g the Villard Party — Emsley & Geo. Cook & Chas. Smillie came in & saw Annie in her party dress.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 18. 1890.

Mild, foggy m'f. with a little spring shower about 9 o'clk. After that a delightfully warm, sunny day feeling very unlike work I did my little beside getting things into a more orderly shape in the studio. It was after 1 when I got home to lunch & near 3 when I got back to studio — For an hour I wrote on letter to Mary — then, at 4 o'clk. to N.A.f S. meeting of N.A's to satisfy purchase by Council of a second house on 23d S. west of the N.A. bldg. Back to studio at 5 — finished Mary's letter — then home — dinner — Eve'g. at 8 took Annie, Mr. Green & the Bosé girls to Club Rehearsal — then to Century where Lockwood deForest gave an informal talk upon Indian Architecture — with photo. illus. on a screen — very interesting — then to club — home with my four ladies — heavy rain just as I got home — To bed at 11
