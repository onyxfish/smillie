## Left Page

### September, Saturday 13, 1890

Very warm day. After very heavy rain last night the m[?] was very wet until after 9 o'clk. where there were signs of clearing — very hot in the sunshine at noon — about 3 a heavy shower, lasting about half an hour — then another blaze of hot sunshine — Another shower before 7 P.m., a very dark & windy night. Got to studio before 11 and before dinner took proofs from 7×9 etching & the soft ground experiment tried yesterday — Results satisfactory. Hotchkiss got to work before 9 and continued with interruptions during the day. Foster, Judson & Hill worked in the afternoon, losing some time from the [shower]. Went down town before 6 o'clk. Parke came up & took tea with us — Eve'g we had a rubber of whist — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 14, 1890

again, an unpromising morning, but speedily a clearing broke in the western sky & for a few hours we had bright warm sunshine. Before 2 o'clk. the wind got back to the east & there were several spatterings of rain & before eve'g some heavy showers. I spent the m'f. out of doors — Dinner at 2 — then more out of doors — Built a fire in studio & wrote some letters — Eve'g. Retired to studio & wrote long letter to Geo. giving instructions for planting the seeds and cultivating several flowering plants. Took letters to P.O. at about 10 P.m. About 6 P.m. had a brisk walk to the lower end of Town & return with Jim & Ralph. To bed at about 11.
