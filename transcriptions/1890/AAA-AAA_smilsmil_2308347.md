## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 16, 1890

Overcast & raw — Got to studio at 9.30 — some time fussing with p[?].
aqua-tint — then got to work upon a sketch 7"×8½" after C.H. Platt's oil
study of an [illegible] corner — Home to lunch at 1 & back at 2.30 — then
worked until after 4 on pen sketch —
then for about an hour working in etching p[?]. Soon after 5 went down
town to Fudler & [illegible]'s, 146 Nassau
St. & got Diary for '91 — then back and stopped at Art-Guild to see Mr. Mill[?]
about a charge against me of $50 p[?] — then
home soon after 6 — dinner — [illegible]
before parlor fire — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 17, 1890

Horrible day — N.S.asterly gale with heavy
rain — Got to studio after 10 & by 12
had finished pen sketch after Platt's
oil painting — Home to lunch at 1.15
& back at 2.30 being nearly blown away —
worked on mezzatint trying to carry out
a number of Shirlaw's suggestions —
wet paper for printing to-morrow and
then took a rest in my chair. Home
just before 6, dinner at 6.30. [illegible]
before parlor fire — Chase came in
& stayed until after 10 — To bed at 11.
