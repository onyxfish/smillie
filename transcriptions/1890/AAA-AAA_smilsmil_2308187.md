## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 22, 1890

At last, a genuine winter day —
clear, with high wind & very cold —
some snow flew last night. I
got to studio at 9.30 — very soon to
N.A. & then to Kurtz's with Ranger ph.
& tint sketches — back to N.A. & worked
until 1.30 on two tint sketches after
Jno. t Edw. Moran, supporting the
Maynard photo. Home to lunch
soon after 2 — back to studio at 3.15
& worked as long as I could see getting
pen & ink sketches ready to take to
the Moss Eng. Co — I went with them
at 5.15, delivered them — got half-
frame first lot & home at 6.30 — then
dinner — Evelf. Annie & I loafed —
both being very tired — She took
part in a discussion on "Single Tax"
at the Wed. p.m. Club today — We
got to bed before 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 23, 1890

Overcast, very bleak, windy & cold —
with some snow flying — Got to
studio soon after 9 — after a little
work there went to N.A., then to
Kurtz's — back to N.A. & worked a few
sketching until 1 — then home to
lunch — back to N.A. at 2.15 & worked
as long as I could see. After 5 o'clk
I went down to Moss Eng. Co
with the last lot of drawings —
Home at 6.30 — dinner — Evelf.
sat in front of fire until 9. Then
wrote a note to Uncle Willie —
Then Geo. & Charl. came in —
Charl. stayed until after 10 o'clk.
— then we went to bed.
