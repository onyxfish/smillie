## Left Page

### January, Saturday 18, 1890

Charming day — bright & sunny — got to studio about 9.30 after taking Jim to Geo's & leaving him there. To N.A. f.s. at about 10 — then to Kurtz's. It seems to me he is not doing his part of the Cat. work — Ret'd. to studio with some pts. of photos & mounted them — Home to lunch at about 1.30 back at 2.30 — finished up photos after Walker, Brown, Comus & Murphy, & took them to Kurtz to carry on to the next stage — there again to N.A. & to studio — At 5.15 went to a furniture store in 23d S; Annie came soon after & we selected a corner cupboard in oak, for dining room — then to Stern's & took a lamp shade — then home — before dinner a young man from Hearman & Stettings came with head of Lennie Homer in P'k — to Begg. I signed it — dinner — at 8 Annie went with the Bose girls to her Choral Club rehearsals — being very tired & at in front of fire until 9 — To bed before 10.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 19, 1890

A delightful day, mild & sunny until about 3 o'clk. when gradually the sky became overcast — wind east — at night wet sidewalks. B&fo. earlier than usual. Soon after 10 I went to studio, mounted photos Rec'd yesterday P.M. for W.C. Cat. & put them in press — then took a walk, went in to the Union League Club — met Geo. there & saw Charl. — Then home — dinner at 2 — Geo. & Emily dined with us — I tried to get a nap after dinner & about half succeeded — after that I wrote part of a long letter to Herrick — tea at 6.30 — Gretta's night out — so Annie fed the little animals & put them to bed — I finished Herrick's letter & wrote notes to H.R. Bishop & Chase — About 9 o'clk. Edw'd & Mary — Mr. Sprague her son Henry & Walter Cook came in & spent an hour with us. To bed about 11.
