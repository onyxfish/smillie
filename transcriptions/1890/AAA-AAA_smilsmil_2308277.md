## Left Page

### July, Friday 25, 1890

Overcast heavily until about 10.30 when rain began to fall & continued until after noon — Eve'g clouds broke away. I was out in garden until after 11 — then in to Etching Room & worked with graver on "Pansy" until dinner, soon after 1. After dinner worked among vines & training Tomato plants until 5; then again worked with graver until tea, after 6. Eve'g Read newspaper awhile — To bed at a little after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 26, 1890

Foggy & irresolute — from 10 to 12 a mizzling rain fell — afternoon the weather cleared very pleasantly. In spite of the rain I was in the garden all the m'f. I worked with my graver on "Pansies" from noon until dinner at 1 — & after that again for an hour or more. At 5 o'clk had the horses hitched up and we rode until after 6. Tea. Eve'g Reading until 10 — To bed at 10.30.
