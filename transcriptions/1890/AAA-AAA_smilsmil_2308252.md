## Left Page

### June, Sunday 1. 1890

A very warm but beautiful day —
Ther. got up to 84°. I got up at
6.30 — had a bath, a rub & a comb and
back to bed at about 7 — there for an
hour — then up for all day — breakfast
comparatively early — Went to studio
soon after 10 & wrote several notes &
arranged lists of my pictures to be
exhibited during the summer & fall —
Home at 12.30 — Geo. & Emily called —
dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon S. Yale,
wife & boy called — also, Young Carmalt
— also, Charles. About 6 o'clk. I went
out & sauntered about for an hour —
At 7 had a cup of tea — Elly, Fred
& Alice called — then A & I went to
Mary's for a few minutes — then I
wrote a short note to Geo. & mailed
it, with Kate's letter, to him — To bed
about 10.30.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 2. 1890

Clean & hot. B'kp. at 7.30 — After that
some time making a final sorting
of my die proofs & then put them away.
Then to 5th Ave. Art galleries — to Bank, to
Kurtz's — to Am. Art Ass'n — to 15 Art Quied
Then to studio & worked until 1 o'clk
arranging things preparatories to
packing. Home to lunch & back
at 2.30 & until 6 o'clk, writing letter
to Kate — arranging things & enter-
taining St. Mulford — Home — and
at 6.30 Annie & I went to Fred &
Alice Ireland's to dinner, to meet
misses Miller & Small. A very
pleasant eve'g until 10 — Then
home & to bed.
