## Left Page

### October, Sunday 19, 1890

Very heavy rain last night &
dark, lowlying clouds all day
with some light rain — Up late —
b'kfst — after g — then to garden —
spent the day out of doors, pruning,
tying up vines, etc, etc — Dinner
at 2 — after that a nap — then
out of doors until dark — Eve —
wrote a note to Kate & at 9.30
took it to P.O. through the rain —
To bed at 10.30 [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### October, Monday 20, 1890

A dark, heavily clouded day with
a number of light, mizzling showers.
I worked all day at my Printing
getting a number of [illegible] from dry-
point "Hollyhocks — dark, single." No
work was done out of doors by any
of the men. I went downtown
about 6 P. m. Eve — Reading —
