## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 19. 1890.

A heavy snow storm all day long — clearing in the early eve — to studio's about ten. finalized for then went to Eaton's with W. C. A. Belf. & got material for Cat. Com. Report — then to studio abt 11 & finished 1st painting in of Charles' picture 20×30. Home soon after — Stayed at home for the afternoon — wrote out cat. com. Report. dinner at 6.30 — Suelf, Annie & the Adams girls went to Met. Opera Ho. & heard Gotterdammerung — I went to the Annual meeting of the W.C. Soct & took an active part — arranged a compromise by which the Etching club will exhibit with W.C. Soct next year — Etchings to be hung in the corridor — Home at 11. to bed — Annie came in after midnight —

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 20. 1890.

A bright day & so mild that yesterdays snow had small chance of making a stay — At studio's I spent the day mounting drawings & paper & making preparations to do some drawing for A.B.N. Co. Home to lunch at 1 — Miss Poshay took lunch with us. Back at 2.30. Shirlaw called about magazine after a picture of his, from which I have a photo — the place to be for the Pratt Inst. collection — Brooklyn — he spent some time with me & then Champney came in for some time — so, most of the afternoon went. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Suelf at home — I wrote a letter to Herrick — Posted it at 10.30 & to bed at 11.
