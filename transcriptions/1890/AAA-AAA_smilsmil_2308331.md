## Left Page

### November, Friday 14, 1890

Another delightful day — got to studio at 10 + to work on Shirlaw megatint — a number of callers but not so many nor so staying as yesterday. Home at — back at 2.30 + worked as long as I could see — then prepared paper for printing to-morrow, then walked down to 14th St. + up Bdwy & 5th Ave home. Dinner at 6.30. Evening looking over back Nos. of Ill. London News & Punch — Annie & Kate at Piano strumming at some opera music — To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 15, 1890

Overcast day — a very slight sprinkling of rain at night. Got to studio at 9.30 + commenced at once preparations to take first working pf. from the Shirlaw megatint. It took some time to get my press in order — ink ground, etc. etc. — but by 12 o'clk. I had two satisfactory pf. + then washed up. As I expected all of the lighter parts of the plate are still much too dark. Home to lunch at 1 back, with Annie at 2.45. She went to call upon Mrs. Coleman & I made a pf. with aquatint proof for [illegible] Schilling came in — then A. having [illegible] we went to Adam's [illegible] & from there to Mad. Sq. Garden — Horse Show — at 3.30, we were there, without excitement until 6, then home. Dinner at 6.30 — Charl, being sick, sent us his Phil. tickets — we gave them to Miss Van-Anminge & her sister & at 7.45 Annie, Kate & I escorted them to the Phil. concert at Mtl. Opera Ho. where we, A.K.&I, sat in our new Balcony seats for the first time. A delightful concert — home at 10.30.
