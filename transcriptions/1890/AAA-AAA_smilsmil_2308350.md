## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 24, 1890

Bright, beautiful day — Soon after 9 I went to J. Pierpont Morgan's with a large sized photo. after his father's portrait. Saw him & left the photo. with him. Then to studio — To work taking some large paper india [illegible] from the Shirlaw mezzotint. At 12 went to 2° Nat. Bk. Then home to dinner — It was nearly 3 o'ck. when I got back to studio — finished [illegible] for the day — cleaned up & at 5.30 went to Saml. Colman's — saw Mr. C. — C. quite sick — severe cold — so that I did not see him. Home soon after 6 — many, Katie & Bessie there. — just down from B.k. to spend Xmas with us. Supper at 6.30 — Poor Ralph was taken violently ill at the table & had a severe time but was at once put to bed & had a quiet night. Charlie came in and spent the eve'g with us — Annie for a little while was at Walter's helping dress the Xmas Tree — It was after 11 when we got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 25, 1890

Overcast, but not unpleasant. Bkfs. before 9 — after which a couple of hours or so were spent in distributing the presents, then I sat keeping Ralph in my lap much of the time. as he still is out of sorts. We were to have had our dinner at 1, but Ge. & Nellie & children did not come — At 1.45 Geo. came with Sheldon & Val. Nellie remained behind with Gordon who was indisposed. It was about 2 when we sat down to dinner with several vacant chairs — but those of us at the table had a very good time. It was about 5 when Annie, Jem, Ralph & I went to Walter's where Edw. & Walter's children were to have a supper & a good time — Ralph soon fell asleep in my arms & I carried him home without waking him — I ret. with Mary, Kate, Katie & Bessie — the children having had their meal — the grown people sat down to their dinner after which the youngsters had an entertainment of singing illustrations — playing upon the piano — Kindergartnie & dancing — About 9.30 we returned to the house — I read awhile & got to bed before 11.
