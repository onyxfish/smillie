## Left Page

### March, Thursday 13, 1890

Overcast with now & then a little, nice sunshine — at night, Rain. Feeling very limb after yesterday's attack of midsummer. I did not feel equal to work — I was enough behind hand with correspondence of one kind & another to give me a days work at that, so, I spent the day writing letters. Home to lunch & back to studio as usual — home to dinner about 6. Evef. wrote my usual bulletin to Annie & took it to G.C.S. about 10 — Evef. I got out all my old die [illegible] and commenced a systematic arrangement of them — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 14, 1890

Beastly day — dark & rainy, all day & all night. After a good night's rest I got up feeling very well — Got to studio about 10 — set Palette & to work painting in, over an old Yosemite picture, 20×24" "A bit of brook with Sheep" — Trying to get something fresh in color & direct in manner — The color of the smearing over the old painting is against me — Home to lunch & back as usual — managed, before dark to finish the painting except the sheep in the foreground — went down to Health Food Co. 10th S. & 4th Ave. — to find it closed — walked up in all the rain — home at 6.30 — dinner — Gretta wanted to go to church — I assented — Ralph waked up & I had to put him to sleep & him to bed — then to Edward's — saw him & got latest news from Mr. Sprague 9 o'clk. when I got home. Then wrote a letter to Annie — one to Herrick — & one to Koehler — At 10.30 went to G.C.S. with them — then home & to bed at 11.
