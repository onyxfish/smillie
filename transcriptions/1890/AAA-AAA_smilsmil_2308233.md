## Left Page

### April, Thursday 24, 1890

Pleasant until about 2 when we had a smart shower — Rain got started after 8 p.m. To studio after 10 a little — Got out canvas 18×30 — Another brook subject, and started the painting in of it. It was a very warm & enervating day & I found it hard work. To lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 & painted until 5.30 — washed up — Sat down in big chair — fell asleep & did not wake until 6.30 — Home at 6.50 — dinner Emma J. Sent tickets for Musurgia Choral Club concert to Annie, so at 7.40 we started — met Emma & Mary — too late to get good seats — A. E. & M. — sat down stairs. I sat up stairs — At 10 Emma & Mary had to go — Concert not over until near 11 — Home in a pouring rain & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 25, 1890

A raw rain all the morning — dry in the afternoon & bright at night. Got to studio after 10 — Spent the mf writing letters — most of the time given to Herrick's Montrose letter — & preparing place of "A Lady of Cairo Visiting" to be sent to Estes & Lauriat — Took it to Adams Esp. at 1 then home to lunch. Back to studio at 2.30 & fussed about putting things a little in order. Went in & called on Dolph who is now in Chauncey's Rooms — then went up to call on Dolph, but he was not in. Home — dinner at 6.30. Emma & Annie & I went to Chickering and heard Vladamir Pachmann's Chopin Recital on piano — a house half full of very enthusiastic listeners — we were much interested — Concert over at 9.45 we walked home — To bed at 10.30
