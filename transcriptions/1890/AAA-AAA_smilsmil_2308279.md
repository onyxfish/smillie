## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 29. 1890.

Another pleasant day in spite of clouds & Easterly winds — again in the garden — again at work on "Graver Pansies" from 12 to 1 & from 2.30 to 4 — Then Rode until 6.30 — Hotchkiss worked on foundation wall for small building for storing cold frame sashes. Eve'g Read news paper & to bed at about 11 — After midnight Ralph was roused by trouble with his bowels & did not get quieted until nearly 4 o'clk — Too many Raspberries probably — He has been wonderfully well up to this time.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 30. 1890.

A very hot day with all the indications of a "dry spell" — I was out of doors until near noon — Then wasted some time in trying to get my Photo-plate holders in order. After dinner again at the same work — unable to get time to work satisfactorily — At 4 o'clk we went out riding — back at 6.30 — Tea — Eve'g very warm & still we sat out on the Piazza — Chinese lanterns lighted — about 10 Peggy, the donkey, was found browsing in our upper garden having managed to work the gate at the barn open — I bounced her & pummeled her & then went to bed.
