## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 2. 1890.

Bright & pleasant day — got to
studio at 10 & after writing a letter
to Beach in Montrose — at 11 went
to Stengel's — 7.10 Bdway to see about
his forwarding Water Color & pf. of [illegible]
to Kaiser of St. Louis — then to Keppel's &
looked at the Classon collection — pf.
of wood cuts & his new process — then to
Wunderlich's & saw pf. of the grande-
roufs plates — then home at 1 — lunch —
back to studio at 2.30 — until 4.30
getting proofs in order & packing
there with a framed Water Color &
1 unframed one by Geo. for Stengel to
send to Kaiser. Home at 5.0, dressed
& got up to the Clinch's — Yosemite flat
Bldg Cor. 62d St. & Park Ave. at 6.20 there
found Annie — Helen Butler also there.
Had high tea & spent the evef. in general
chatting went a little after 9 — then A.
& I walked down home & to bed at
about 10.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 3. 1890.

Mild & hazy spring day — got to studio
about 9.30 — met Chas. Platt & went to
N.A. with him — Varnishing Day —
There until bet. 11 & 12 o'clk — then
to studio & studied Henderson's Secd
Cat. until it was time for me to
go home to lunch — after that went
to barber's & had hair cut & head
shampooed — Home at 4.15, dressed,
& went with Annie to met. Mrs. [illegible]
[illegible] — Central Park — met Chas. Platt
there — wandered about for some
time — then walked down to 72d St.
& called upon the Tiffany's — out — then
on Mrs. Mitchell (Annie Tiffany) saw her —
left cards for Mrs. Villard & home at 6.20
dinner — evef. Fred & Alice came
in — & about 9, S.R. Koehler — we looked
over father's die [illegible] until after 10 —
it was after 11 when I got to bed.
