## Left Page

### December, Friday 12, 1890

Clear, cold & windy — a dirty day in the streets. Got to studio at about 10. Chas. & Pratt came in for a few minutes — After a time got to work on 5×7" etching for the Pratt Ins. & scrubbed parts & rewritten Water of Aqua Stone — then laid a ground over parts & put in some new work — Home at 1 — back at 2¹⁵. Mary Willards lunched with us — Mary Hook returned to P o k on 3.30 train — I varnished etching plates & then to 51 W. 10' St — saw J.W. Casilean & got his autograph to a proof of "A Sybil's Head" — then to 3 No. Wash— 39 — took mezzo. pf to Shiplaw & got his criticisms — Called on Platt — then to studio & from there dinner at 6.30 — Evely so cold that I did not think the worse for to go to Etching Club meeting — Mr. Chapman's Studio — instead I stayed by the fire until near 10 — to be soon after.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 13, 1890

Another clear, cold, windy day. At 9 o'clk. went to Mr. Morgan's & saw him about Portrait — I want to etch the head much larger, leaving out the hands — then to Chas. Platt's — got framing of an Etcher's corner & took it to studio — I purpose to make a pen & ink sketch for Etching Club pub. then to Kurtz's — he is still sick — back to studio & to work biting in extra work on 5×7" Pratt Ins. Etching — Charl. came in & after 1 o'clk. we walked up home together & he lunched with us. At 2.30 went in to Grolier club to see Rajon's Etchings — met Wm. J. Andrews there — he took me over the club house & I talked to him in favor of myself — then to studio — Coleman came in & St. Genter & his carpenter — I worked on etching & on mezzatint until dusk. Then Kate & Geo. came in & we went in to Champney's "tea" for a few minutes — then Kate & I went home at 5.30 — High tea at 6 — the girls from Walter's & Edwd families have been dressing dolls for Annie, helped by misses Foshay & Williams — they took tea later. Edwd & Gogo came in & the children rehearsed awhile upon a Xmas Carol — Then Walter & Louise & Mary (ned) came in — all out at 8.30 — then I wrote three notes to bed before 11.
