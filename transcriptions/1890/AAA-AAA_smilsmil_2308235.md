## Left Page

### April, Monday 28, 1890

Pleasant day. I got to studio about
10.15 & to work on second painting
of 20×24 — "A bit of brook with some
sheep" Abt 12.30 S went to 2° Nat.
Bk. to arrange A's May income.
Home to lunch at 1.20 — Feeling very
much out of sorts — sore throat & all
that sort of thing. I did not go to
studio until 3 — was able to paint
for an hour or more but of course
what I did was not what I wanted
to do. About 5 Chas. Collins came in
& a little later Fred. Dielman came
to audit G.W. Shelton's Etching Club
acct. with me — It was after 6 when
we got through & 6.45 when I got
home to dinner — Even't I was
quite used up by my throat trouble
& headache but could not get to bed
until 11 — it was about midnight when
A. got to bed — During the night Jim
was up several times crying with
his "growing pains".

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 29, 1890

In bed until about 5 p.m. suffering
from headache, sore throat and general
cussedness — Then got up, was able to
dress & take my place at the dinner
table. Even't. Annie & Emily went to
the Berkeley Lyceum where the Choral
Club sang as part of an entertainment.
Geo. Cook took them & called for them.
I remained at home — wrote my letter
to Mary & got to bed soon after 10.
