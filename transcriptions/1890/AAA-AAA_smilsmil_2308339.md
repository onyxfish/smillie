## Left Page

### November, Sunday 30, 1890

A mild day, a mixture of cloud &
sunshine. I did not get out of
bed until near 2 o'clk. Then
up in time for dinner. George
called & saw me about 1 o'clk.
I spent the afternoon lounging
in the dining room, Charl
having come in — Edw & Mary
& Geo. & Emily called. Kate
took tea with the H[illegible]wood
girls who brought her home at
9 o'clk. & came in for a few
minutes. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 1, 1890

Pleasant morning — Mr. Watrous
called at the house & I saw her
before I left. Got to studio about
10 o'clk & to work on Mezzatint.
Carlton Chapman came in with
proof of his new plate for the Etching
Club Publication — Then Yale brought
in Dr. Gerstner. About noon went over
to Kuntz's & saw [illegible] from photo, after
painting of J.S. Morgan — Then to 2. Nat.
Bk. It was nearly 2 o'clk. when I
got home to lunch. Shortly after
3 when I got back to studio. Again
at work on Mezzatint. Attended
new ground over the entire hands.
The afternoon very grey with threats
of snow — some of which fell in the
eve'g. At 5.30 went up to Yale's &
left with Mr. Yale proof of "Goldsmith's
Daughter". Then home — Dinner late.
Eve'g reading until Charl came
in. Later Geo. came — They left
about 10.
