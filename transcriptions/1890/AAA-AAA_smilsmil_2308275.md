## Left Page

### July, Monday 21, 1890

... Ther. fell to 46° - last night.

milder but still delightfully cool.
At about 10 I got to work biting
on "Old house etc" + proceeded very
deliberately being many times in-
terrupted — dinner at 1 — again
"biting" until 3.45 when with
Mary, Miss W., Annie + Ralph
I went riding — The horses went
admirably but Selim coughed
a good deal. On our way home I
stopped at Tarbell's + had Selim
cough for him — T. says it is "heaves"
Evg. Mr. + Mrs. Wm. Parke + Mr.
Chas. Parke called "to see the sun
set" — Later I wrote letter to
Mary — Took it to P. O. at 10 and
to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 22, 1890

Delightful day — growing warmer —
The usual routine of int. of door
morning work — then to etching room
+ finished the biting of "Old house" —
Dinner at 1 — after that, cleaned
my plates + soon after 3 o'clk. Mary,
Miss Williams Annie Ralph + I
Rode over to Brooklyn + around,
back home, by way of South Land.
It was 7 o'clk. before we got in.
Evg. all very tired + yawning.
I read news paper + got to bed
before 10 o'clk.
