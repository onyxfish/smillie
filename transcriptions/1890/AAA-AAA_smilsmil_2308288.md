## Left Page

### August, Saturday 16, 1890

Pleasant day — Bkfst. at 8.30 — then in garden with Uncle W. & aunt a —
out of doors until after 10 — wrote a note to Dimock Searle telling him
of my agreement with Lozier — then to work on my aquatint with brush.
Dinner — after that, a little more work on my plate & at 4 o'clk. we
went out riding. Back at 6 —
Tea — Eve'g. we read our news
papers & got to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 17, 1890

A warm day with strong, drying wind from the south. Annie went
with Uncle W. & aunt a — to the Presb'n ch. I spent the a'ft. with
the children, in the garden & out of
doors. I wrote a letter to George.
Dinner at 2.30 — afternoon, more
writing — a heavy storm of rain with
a little thunder, came up at about
3.30 — showers followed and the
eve'g. was wet. It was spent
in reading & chatting. Near 11
o'clk when we got to bed.
