## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 14, 1890.

A charming winter day. After going to studio, got to N.A. f.S. about 9 45. Eaton there, Platt soon after — the whole mf spent on the cat. work. Platt went to studio for a time with me — Home to lunch at 1 — back to N.A. f.S. at 2.30 — worked until 4 o'clk. There to Kurts's — Then home at 5 & had a rest before dinner. Miss Foshay with us over night. Dinner at 6.30 — Evf. Annie went to her Choral Club Rehearsal with the Dose's — after that, to an entertainment given to the club by Mr. Nutchle. A. came home in a carriage at 12.30 — I tried to write to Nate but made a poor affair. as Walter Cook came in & then Charles I managed to cover my paper & gave it to Charles to mail when he left soon after 10 — I got to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 15, 1890.

Very dark — heavy rain all the morning. clouded afternoon — warm, wet & foggy night. Got to studio at about 10 — then to N.A. — there for some time at work with Platt to studio again before 12 & worked at the laying out & planning for Cat. until 1 — then home to lunch — back to N.A. at 2d worked with the pen as long as I could see — or until 4.30 — then to studio & home at 5.30 — Dinner at 6.30 — Annie did not come in until near 7 — engaged on some of her committee business — Evf. at 8 15 I went to Water-Color Socy meeting — F.H. Smith presented his plan for a Socy supper in the N.A. galleries on the night of Feb'y 14th — did not general favor — Home at 10 — to bed before 11.
