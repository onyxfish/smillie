## Left Page

### March, Monday 31. 1890.

A bleak, disagreeable day, with promise of snow which came before dark. I was nearly useless from a headache all day — got to studio a little before 10 — Spent some time working on an old wash drawing, sketch of Geo. made at Po'k. in 1863 — & then got to work on Union Trust Tracing — Home to lunch and back at 2.30 — worked in great discomfort until after 4 — then gave up & went home & to bed & did not get up again until to-morrow mf.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 1. 1890.

A bright day but windy & raw — I feel rather shaky after yesterday's headache — Got to studio about 10 — wasted some time but finally got to work upon the Union Trust Co. blof Tracing & nearly completed it. Home at 1 & back before 3. S. R. Koehler came in soon after & made quite a long call — & soon after he left Arnie & Carston came in having started for Erie depot with Miss Williams who arr. this p.m. on a french steamer & starts at once for Buffalo — W. Sprague still very ill. Went home with Arnie before 6 — dinner — Eve'g. She went to her choral club rehearsal & I wrote my letter to Mary — Chare came in bet. 9 & 10 — I got to bed about 11.
