## Left Page

### May, Monday 12, 1890

A cloudless day. Steel feeling out of sorts — got to studio at 10 & finding it chilly stayed there but for a short time — Went to Bridgman's & ordered some plants — then to Wunderlich's to see the Pastel exhtn. Met there Wm M Chase & Oscar Weir & had a long chat with them — then to a carriage mart in 13 [illegible] to look at some donkey carts. Home to lunch at 1 — after that — at about 2, went to Dr. Yale's & was there until about 3.30 — then to Socy of Am. Artists — met Miss —    & her Mother, just in from Bermuda — & again Chase. To studio after 5 — made a 5×7 pencil sketch after "A bit of brook & some sheep" — & then home at 6 — dinner — Suet. Charl came in with accts from Bermuda Steamship — this useless for me to stem the tide of Annie, Yale & Charl. I wrote notes to Holden (Parks architect) Schilling — Amos Nichols & Henick. To bed about 10.30.

Mkr my. went to McQuieid & saw Mr. Harrison Milles about some missing water-color pictures of mine.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 13, 1890

After a very bad night, owing to stoppage in nasal passages, I woke with a wretched headache & unable to get up. Annie sent for Dr. Y. & he came & through the day I was dosed with Citrate of Mag., Belladonna, Camphor & Quinine — in spite of which I was able to get up at 8 p.m. & write a short letter to Mary — Miss Pashay came in the early evtg. & left at 9 — then Charl came — suffering from severe cold in the head — & a hotel on the ocean beach near Norfolk, Va. was discussed. It was after 11 before I got to bed.
