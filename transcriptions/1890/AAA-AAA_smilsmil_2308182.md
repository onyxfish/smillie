## Left Page

### January, Sunday 12, 1890

Hazy, sunny m'f. — overcast afternoon —
clear night — very mild — Ther. on the N.
side of house 66° at 2 o'clk. Sidewalks
wet & dirty. B'k f't at about 9 — at 10.30
went to studio — found Saml. Coleman
there — soon after ll went to N.A. f.D.
Eaton, & later, Chas. Platt there — Spent
nearly an hour looking over pictures.
at 12 Annie came & we went to the
Am. Art. Galleries & saw the Barye coll.
— The Angelus & the other pictures — It was
a little before 2 when we got home —
Then dinner — after that Clark came in
for a little while — then young Carmalt —
at 5, Geo. came & together we went to the
Union League Club to see the pictures —
There until after 6 — then home — Tea
— Read newspaper — wrote to Koehler.
Took letter to P.C.D. at 10 o'clk —
To bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 13, 1890

A very mild, overcast day — at times,
very dark — streets all wet — late in the
afternoon, wind changed & it blew up
quite fresh & dry. Got to studio a
little after 10 — engaged upon one kind &
another of work for about an hour — then
to Kurtz — then to N.A. f.D. — Took Walker
& Coleman pictures to Kurtz & to studio
soon after 12. — Worked on W.C. Cat. work
until 1.15 then home to lunch. Back
to N.A. f.D. at 2.30 — little to be done. then
to studio — to N.A. again at about 4 &
there until dark — then to Kurtz — then
to Cantiny — met Geo. there — started to
walk up home together, but soon I
took a car & rode home — in soon after
6 — dinner at 6.30 — Geo. Cook spent
some time with Annie going over her
Diet Kitchen acct.
