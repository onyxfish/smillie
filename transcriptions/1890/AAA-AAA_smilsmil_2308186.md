## Left Page

### January, Monday 20, 1890

Dismal, dark, rainy day, very mild
Up earlier than usual & was able to
get to studio's about 9.30 — worked for a
time getting Kurtz's photos ready
for his process — Chas. Parsons & Symington
came in — then to N.A. & to Kurtz with
photos — again to N.A. & sent Maynard's
drawing to Kurtz — worked at N.A. till
1½ then home to lunch — after that
Annie read to me notes she has pre
pared for Wed. Club — on Jingle Jay, again
& in reply to Mr. Brownell — To studio's
& then to N.A. before 3 — made sketch of
Nellie's Pansies — then to Kurtz — got
more photo helps — to studio's at 4.30 &
at work mounting them until 6 —
then home — dinner — Self wrote
my weekly letter to Mary — To bed
soon after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 21, 1890

Fine day — cloudy in the mts. with
a few flakes of snow — the afternoon
was very pleasant — Got to studio's at
9.30 & for a time was engaged prepar-
ing photos for Kurtz's process — It was
nearly when I got to K's & for an
hour & a half I worked there on the
negatives — then to studio's & mounted
two photos. Annie met me near the
studio's & we walked up home together.
Lunch at 1.30 — back to studio's & then
to N.A. at 2.30 — made two wash sketches
of Washington & Leon Moran — To studio's
at 5 — Colman painted for a little
while in the afternoon — home at 5.30 —
dinner at 6. After that dressed &
went with Annie to Chickering Hall
to the first Choral Club concert of the season.
During intermission went on to the plat-
form & had a short chat with Julia Gavit
— Met Netta afterwards — Saw many
other people whom I knew & spoke to
several of them. The concert itself
was an enjoyable affair — Home
& to bed at 11 o'clk.
