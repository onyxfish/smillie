## Left Page

### September, Monday 29, 1890

[illegible] frost again last night.

A beautiful day — weather growing milder. After looking after the men & getting Oscar to attend to my garden duties I got to work in etching room & burnished on sky of soft ground etching and took impressions — At last, I got a satisfactory one, just before dinner. After dinner I tried to get some [illegible] of my line etching & took two or three [illegible] — soon after 3 we went out riding — back at 5.30 — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g. Mr. Jeff Griffiths called to invite us to tea at her house on Wed eve'g. I wrote the last of my series of letters to Mary & at 9.30 took it to P.O. It was near 11 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 30, 1890

Bright, warm day — The Ther. did not fall below 40° last night — It was 11 o'clk. before I got to my work proving — commenced with my line etching plate & was not successful in taking two large-paper India [illegible] but did well with some on Jap. paper. After 1 o'clk. when I washed up, Dinner at 1.30 — there with the men at their work — At 3.30 we went out riding — home before 5.30 — Eve'g. loafing, as my head bothered me — Annie read a short story aloud. To bed early.
