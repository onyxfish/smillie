## Left Page

### January, Friday 24, 1890

Clear most of the day — overcast part of the time about the middle of the day — All day given over to Catalogue work — most of the time at Kurtz's where I worked upon negatives, plates & prints in any way that I could to advance the work which is now so behind hand as to worry me a good deal — At about 1 o'clk. went to Bank & Safe Deposit for Annie. met T.W. Wood there & went with him to N.A. Near 2 o'clk. when I got home to lunch — there again to Kurtz's & worked as long as I could see — Home at about 6 — dinner. Eve tf, Annie & I sat in front of the fire & dozed until 9 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 25, 1890

Disagreeable day — windy, raw & very dirty. We got to bed so early last night that we got up very late this m'g — B'kf't about 8.30 — I took Jim with me & left him at G's. I got to studio at about 10 — then to N.A. — then to Kurtz's & worked there until after 1 — Home to lunch at 1.30 — after2 went to studio & put an effect into a die proof f "Moose's Head" for Uncle Willie & mailed it to him — there again to K's and worked until after 5 — K. is not as much worried as I am — Before I left Clarence [illegible] came in & seeing some of the Cat. Ille. expressed a desire to have some for his "Studio". Home at 6 — dinner — Eve tf, a little newspaper reading — to bed before 9 o'clk.
