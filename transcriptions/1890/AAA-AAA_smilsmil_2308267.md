## Left Page

### July, Saturday 5, 1890

Very cool day — cloudy until late in the afternoon when it cleared perfectly. Bkfst. after 8 — I spent the entire mng. out of doors — in garden & going about with Geo. & Valentine. Parke & his bro. came up for a short time. Dinner at 1 — About 2.30 Geo., Emily, Valentine, Annie, Jim, Ralph & I started for Ball Spring having borrowed Parke's big, open, 3 seat wagon. Altho' very cool, we had a delightful drive. & had a very pleasant climb to the falls — we did not get home until 7 o'clk. Then — tea — Swatg. Reading news-papers — later, some of the party played whist. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 6, 1890

Brilliant day — very warm in the sunshine — cool in the shade. Bkfst. after 8.30 — until near 11 out of doors in the garden — Annie & company went off for a stroll & I wrote to George, Charlie & Wilson & Tims. Dinner at 1 — At 3.30 Geo., Emily, Valentine, Ralph & I went off for a ride, back at 6 — Tea — then with my vines & flowering plants as long as I could see — At 9 sat down to write a little. Wrote a note to Barrett Bro. & Binghamton telling them to send along their piano. Bet. 9 & 10 o'clk. went to P.O. with it — To bed before 11.
