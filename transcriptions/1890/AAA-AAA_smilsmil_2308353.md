## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 30, 1890

Cold & raw, but otherwise pleasant. Got to studio about 10 — started work on background, dry points of George's head (portrait) but finding my hands very tender & sore I determined to paint — it was after 11 when I started, repainting an old canvas — 20×30 — "Evening — The Pond's Outlet" — At 12.45 went to 2° Nat. Bk & cut Annie's coupons — then to Krusty's — saw him and got estimate for Etche's portraits for the Etching Club Publication. Home to lunch at about 2 — it was 3 when I got back to studio & continued painting as long as I could see, taking out dark places — on my way home stopped at Butler's and ordered small frames for etchings — Dinner at 6.30 — Evg. Reading — Charlie came in before 8 o'clk. much chatter — I went to bed some after 10.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 31, 1890

A nasty day — raw & cold with some fine snow falling. Got to studio at 10.30 & painted on 20×30 going over the foreground making it much lighter & with brighter color, improving general effect. Home at 1 o'clk — lunch — Mary, Katie & Bessie went back to Po'Keepsie on 3.30 train — At 2.30 I went to DeVinne's & saw the old gentleman & was pleased to find that he is interested in the work — after some talking & planning he handed me over to his subordinate W. H was with him for nearly an hour — then up to studio — got pen sketch after Ritt & went down to Moss Eng. Co. 535 Pearl St — then again to DeVinne's & got a visited that I had dropped piece there — then to studio & cleaned palette. Home at 6 — Dinner at 7 — Evg read Aztec Treasure House — Janvier until after 9 — then Charlie came in. It was about 10.30 when I got to bed.
