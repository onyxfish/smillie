## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 3, 1890

Delightful, warm day — the air full of Sept. haze. About 11 when
I got to my work in Studio — altho much interrupted. I finished the
drawing in a 5"×7" plate "A bit of brook etc." Dinner at 1 — It was
nearly 2.30 when with Annie & the boys I started for a watering
trough on a cross road about two miles west of us. Unhitched the
horses on arriving there — Annie & the boys pic-nicked while I
made an oil sketch of the trough to aid me in finishing Charl's
picture — Worked until near 5 —
Started for home at 5.30 — got
there at 6.25 — Tea — Eve'g after
washing brushes & attending to
chores I read newspaper —
To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 4, 1890

A very warm, hazy day — a delightful
specimen of a Sept. day — my enjoyment
of it was much lessened by the fact
that my rest last night was much
disturbed, & all day my head was
very uncomfortable, so that I could not
work or even loaf with comfort. I
"podged" about all day, directing the
men & otherwise trying to accomplish
something. About 4.30 we went
out for a ride & got home a little
after 6. Eve'g, wrote a couple
of notes — made out some checks
— read my newspaper & got to
bed at about 10.
