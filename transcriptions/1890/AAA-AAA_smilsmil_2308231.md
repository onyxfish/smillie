## Left Page

### April, Sunday 20, 1890

Delightful day — cloudless & fresh —
B'kfo. at 9 — after that some reading
& a great deal of loafing until
dinner time at 2. Geo. & Emily
dined at Mary's — At 3 's' Annie
& I — Jim, Ralph, Catherine & Edna
went by Boulevard car up to 108'
St. & there entered Riverside Park
& wandered up to Grant's tomb —
& then over to Boulevard & by car
home, getting in a few minutes
after 6 — Geo. & Emily came in & we
had tea at 6.30 — Eve'g. wrote
out my order for some flower seeds
to Fel. S. Childs of Queens Co. W.I. also
circular note for Etching Club —
Then a note to Case — Went out &
posted it & then to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 21, 1890

Still delightful weather — got to bed
studio about 10 & prepared to go down
to A.B.N. Co. with Union Trust Co. drawing —
Miss Abbatt came in with a Miss [illegible]
so that I did not get away until 11.
Went to bank (2nd Nat.) & then to A.B.N.
Co. & saw MacDonough & others — my
drawing gave great satisfaction — then
to P.O. to J.K. Campbell with Koehler
plates for Reg. [illegible] ground — & to Fuchs
& Lange — then home to lunch about
2 — To studio at 3 — cleared up & got
ready to do some printing to-morrow.
Before 5 went to Kimmel & Voigt's &
got some ink & took the Estes & Hannah
plate "A Lady of Cairo visiting" which K & V
say is an electrotype & not the original.
Home at 6.30 — Dr. Adams & Nelia there —
dinner about 7 — Eve'g. Charl
came in for a little while about photo
plate holders. I wrote letter to Kate —
to bed at about 11.
