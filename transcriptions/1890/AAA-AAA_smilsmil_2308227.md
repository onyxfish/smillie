## Left Page

### April, Saturday 12, 1890

Delightful day — To studio about 10
& at last started upon Union Trust
Co. drawing washing in the effect —
I think I have made a good
start. Mr. Wm. Pate came in to
offer me a mezzotint plate to do —
but I could not undertake to do
it as he wants it before next fall.
Home at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked
until after 5 — Then went into Mr.
Whittemore's Studio to her reception —
Annie came in soon after. On
our way up we stopped in at the
Grolier club & took a look at the
queer Whistler stuff that is there.
Dinner at 6.30 — Evelf. to Phil.
Concert — the last of the season — a
very fine one — The 9th Symphony with
full choral accompaniment by
the Metropolitan Choral Society was a
great success — Home at 10.30
& to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 13, 1890

An uncomfortably warm day with
some very slight showers in the after-
noon — In the m'f I worked at getting
Father's die proofs into order & finished
with them — I have yet to get into
order proofs of my own & miscellaneous
dies — Dinner a little before 2 o'clk.
Soon after 3 Annie, Jim, Ralph & I
went by Boulevard cars up to 95th
St. called on Kate Hillard & then to
Riverside Park — down to 86th S. & then
back by cars just escaping a light
shower — Tea at 6.30 — Evelf. some
reading — wrote to Macdonough &
a second note to Estes & Lauriat
asking to be permitted to take 3 or 4
[illegible] from Bridgman's "Lady of Cairo" visit —
Geo. Cook came in at 9.30 with some
small matters — He & huey are to
begin their visit with us to-morrow.
