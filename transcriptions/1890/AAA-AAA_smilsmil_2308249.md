## Left Page

### May, Monday 26, 1890

A nasty, raw, wet day — Rain in the m'f
& again in the afternoon with a dry period
between — Did not get to studio until
after 10.30 — Then some cleaning & wrote
notes to Schilling — J.K. Campbell & Jas.
D. Gile. Made pencil sketch of portion
of "A bit of brook" etc upon 5"×7" upright —
for an etching — Geo. came in after 12
having just arr. from Ridgefield. At
1 I went to Knoedle's — Then home to lunch.
Back to studio at 3 — worked on Water-
color Sketch "A Virginia Mill" until
after 6 — Then home — dinner at 6.30.
Ev'g. Reading newspapers & a
little writing. wrote to Herrick
& Macdonough — To bed at 10.30

At 3 o'clk. this m'g. Ralph had an attack
of vomiting & again about 5 — He was dosed
with Castor Oil & seemed well through
the day.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 27, 1890

A wet m'f. clearing before noon — clear
night. After bkfs. Annie & I went to
Flandreau's in Broom St. & looked at
Donkey carts — then we parted & I went
to Bridgman's in 94th St — then to studio
where I finished W.C. Sketch of old
Windmill in Virginia — Home at 1.30 —
lunch — back to studio at 3 — Tried
to work on Tracing of Pernambuco
drawing for A.B.N. Co. but out of sorts
& did but little — Home before 6 —
dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g wrote to Mary
& to Herrick — Mailed them at 10.

Ralph has been languid all day &
is very evidently out of sorts.
