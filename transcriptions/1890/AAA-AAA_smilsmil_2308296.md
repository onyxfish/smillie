## Left Page

### September, Monday 1, 1890

Very beautiful day — after my
mrt, garden duties I got to my
work in studio — made tracing
of "a bit of brook" — etc — laid ground
on plate + made transfer — I
spent some time with the men
in arranging levels about the
excavation for the new sheds —
I also trimmed about the berry
bushes. Dinner at 1 — more out
of door work until 3.30 when
we went out riding — called for
Florence Lathrop + took her out
with us — Home at 6.30 — Tea —
Evefg. wrote a letter to Mary +
soon after 10 took it to P.O, To bed
after 11.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 2, 1890

Delightful day — cloudless + hazy —
very warm in the sunshine with pleasant
breeze. Got to work in studio at 10.30
Etching No. 2 5"×7" for Pratt's Mrs.
Dinner soon after 1 — again worked
on etching until 4 o'clk. getting
it nearly all drawn in — Then
we went out riding — called for
Mr. Garst — she went with us —
back at 6.30 — Tea — Evefg. Annie
+ I went down to Judge Jessup's
where we saw the Judge — Mrs.
J— Louise + May. A little before
9 we went to Wm. Mueford's and
made a short call there — home
at 9.30 — Read news paper + to bed
about 10.30.

Mr Hotchkiss commenced laying
wall for foundation for new shed.
