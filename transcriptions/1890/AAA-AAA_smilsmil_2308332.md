## Left Page

### November, Sunday 16, 1890

A beautiful day. Bkfs. about
9 o'clk. Then with papers until
near 11. Then took Jim & Ralph
down to studio — there for nearly
an hour. Then we walked up
to Charl's quarters where I found
him up & dressed but still com-
plaining. Home at 1.30. Geo.
& Emiely called. Dinner at 2.
Kate dined with Walter and
Minnie Pierce. After dinner
I had a nap. Then Ed. & Mary
& Jos. Valentines called & at 6
Annie & I went to Walter's for
a few minutes. Tea at 6.30.
Evet. wrote notes & letters
until after 10. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### November, Monday 17, 1890

A nasty, wet day. Got to studio
at 9.30. Chas. A. Platt came in
soon after & stayed for some time.
I then got to work upon Sherlaw
mezzotint until after 12. Then
Annie came in. At 12.45 I went
to Bank & then home to lunch.
Back to studio at 2.45 & worked
until 4.30 when I could see no
longer. About 5 I went to Saml.
Colman's & sat chatting with him
& Mrs. C. until after 6. Then
home to dinner. Evet.
Chas. Collins spent with us.
To bed at 10.30.
