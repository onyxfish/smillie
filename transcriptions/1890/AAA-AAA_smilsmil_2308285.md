## Left Page

### August, Sunday 10, 1890

A wet morning but clouds lifted — air growing cooler until night fall. I was about, out of doors most of the day accomplishing very little. Every I wrote a letter to J. R. Koehler & at 10 o'clk. went to P.O. with letters. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Monday 11, 1890

Ther. fell last night to 48°.

Cold, cloudy & showery — like an Oct. day — Spent the m'f podging about out of doors. Miss Williams, Catherine, Edna & Tom left for Buffalo in the stage at 12 o'clk. Just before they left I took a couple of shots at the children in the donkey cart. We had our dinner soon after I. My head was so uncomfortable that I did not try to work in the studio but continued my out of door podging. I mended my blinds in the etching room & in other ways prepared to get to work to-morrow. Eve'g at 7.30 I went down town to see Dimock Searle, but he had left town — then home — went to dark room & tried for the first time Eikonogen, the new developer — made a complete failure. Then tried to write to Kate — another failure.
