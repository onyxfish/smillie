## Left Page

### October, Friday 3, 1890

Heavy clouds, some mizzling rain & a drenching shower just before noon — very mud — a beautiful clear up with rainbow late in the afternoon. After b[rea]kf[ast] in the garden — then went down towns with him & Ralph who had the donkey & cart — went to black[smith] with & had a shoe reset — then to Barney's with the saddle — the youngones were caught in the heavy shower on their way home & got very wet. I followed later walking up the hill with Miss Mc-Cartney who brought up the two por[t]folios she has made for Mr. Parke to hold some of my etchings for wedding presents. Afternoon I worked awhile on small Pansy Dry Point, made for experimental printing — Spent some time out of doors with the man — In at 6.30 Ev[enin]g[.] after newspaper I wrote notes to Alex. Schilling & S.P. Koehler. To bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 4, 1890

Heavy fog in the mtf. — lifting to be cloudy & later very bright & mild. I worked a little while immediately after b[rea]kf[ast] — on my small dry-Point "A bunch of Pansies" — then to the garden — again at my Pansies, getting the drawing all in before dinner —
About noon there was excitement over a green snake who tumbled from the fair ground wall into our pasture & cut his head very badly — he was a gory subject — Annie washed him and took him home in her lap in the donkey cart & the Dr. was sent for. In the afternoon bet. 3 & 4 o'clk. Messrs. Gruiser-Post, Beardsley & — called — a committee of the Board of Trade working up the subject of a Summer Hotel for this place. They called to talk with me on the subject. I took them over a part of the house — worked for a while on dry Point "Single Dark Hollyhocks" & got it ready for f[irst] [proo]f. Poles Stone worked with the other men. At 6 o'clk. the excavation of the cellar & much of the grading about the new barn was completed. — Hotchkiss has almost finished the wall for the manure pit. Tea at 6.30. Before 8 went down town. Evn[in]g[.] Read newspaper. Looked over Century for Oct — had a short nap and got to bed soon after 10.
