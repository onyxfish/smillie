## Left Page

### November, Friday 28, 1890

Another brilliant cold day — In
the m'f Jim & I went out for a
walk, wandering over country road
& through the woods until noon — The
back — Jenny Downs, her daughter
& 3 of her children came to lunch
leaving afterwards at 2.30 — Then I
went to my bed room & had quite
a solid nap — after which, about
dusk, I went to Mr. Downs's for [illegible]
who had spent a part of the aftnoon
there playing with Jack Whiting —
Tea after 6. Eve'g chatting with
the Dr. & to end at 10.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 29, 1890

*Home again.*

Ralph proved again to be a very
disturbing element through the
night & got into my bed at 6 o'clk.
in the m'f. I got up at 7.30 — the
Early m'f was perfectly clear — then
followed cloudiness & between 10 & 11
again it was clear & nice; the Ther.
getting up to 50°. Jim & I went out for
a walk, to the Pumping House & to the big
water wheel & then home by Whitney Ave.
Dinner at 1.30 — At about 3, carriage
came for us & after our good byes (leaving
Nellie in bed with a headache) we
went to Station for 3.30 train — It was 16m
late — very long & crowded — hot & stuffy —
Ralph slept in my arms nearly all the
way to N.Y. Gotten at 6:2, 40 m. late.
Headache, sore throat & general discomfort
had been getting the better of me rapidly —
ate a light dinner & after sitting for
awhile went to bed, completely de-
moralized at 8.30.
