## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 28. 1890.

Splendid day — clear & bright, but rather cool — To studio about 10 &
got fairly to work on drawing for
N.B.N. Co. "Pernambuco" — About
11 Chakurau & Schilling came to
consult about the N.Y. Etching Club
Cat. publication for next year. They
stayed for about an hour. Home
to lunch about 1.30 & back at 3 —
Worked on drawing until near
6 — Chase then came in & we
walked up town together — I
dressed & at 7 Annie & I went
to Mary's where we met W. & Mrs.
Sprague. Walter & Louise & dined.
Edw. was not at home — The
eve'g passed in chatting & home
at 10 & to bed.

Ralph seems to be quite him-
self & now Jim is complaining of
a "tired feeling."

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 29. 1890.

Delightful day, bright & cool — Got to studio
before 10 & to work on Pernambuco drawing.
S.R. Koehler came in about noon & Annie
came before 1 o'clk. Annie & I walked
up home to lunch — After that she took
Jim & Ralph, with Grettie, to the Battery
& Bedloe's Island — I went back to
studio soon after 2 & finished Pernam-
buco & made the changes required
on the drawing of Union Trust Co's Bldg.
Left a note at Chas. Pratt's door for him —
Then up 6th Ave. to Cherry's trunk store.
Home at 6.15. Dinner before 7. Eve'g
John Valentine called — left at 9 — I
read newspapers & wrote a letter.
To bed at about 10.30
