## Left Page

### June, Friday 13, 1890

Clouds hung very low until near
10 o'clk. then the sky cleared. Very
heavy rain between 5 & 6 o'clk P.M.
continuing at intervals during the
eve'g. — distant thunder. I spent
most of the m'f with Herrick putting
up curtain rods in Studio bay win-
dow & writing letter to Mary that
should have gone in Wed'. mail —
the first omission since they left ten
years ago. Dinner abt. After this
finished up my letter & worked about
plants & vines — at 4 o'clk went out
for a drive, watching showers in the
west — got back at 5.10 just in time
to escape a ducking — tea at 6 —
After it, walked piazza for an hour
or more.

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 14, 1890

Heavily clouded m'f — some sun-
shine later but in the afternoon
showers fell all about us & threat-
ened but little rain fell. I
spent the day with Herrick lay-
ing out work for him — time putting
away my things & getting Studio
in order for work. Also did some
work out of doors — woodchucks &
water are making havoc in the
garden. After tea I went down
town & attended to various little
matters. Home, read newspapers
& to bed before 10.
