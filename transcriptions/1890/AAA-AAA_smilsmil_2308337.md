## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 26, 1890

*To New-Haven.*

Pleasant day. Got to studio before 10
o'clk. & to work on mezzotint plate
with Rocker completing the work
commenced yesterday of getting a
texture over the curtain back [illegible]
& skish foregrounds — also, laying a
new ground over the hands, [illegible]
a little after 11 I wet down Rocker
& took one impression to show the
condition of the plate which proved
to be satisfactory — Home before 1. [illegible]
lunch. Before 3 o'clk. Annie, the
boys & I went to G. C. Station & took
3 o'clk train for New Haven, getting
there at 5 o'clk. Hack to Dr. Adam's
house — dinner about 6. Eve.
toasting toes in front of fire & chatting
until a little before 10. Annie & [illegible]
slept in one room & Ralph & I in another.
Ralph, snuffling with a cold in his head
disturbed me very much.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 27, 1890

*Thanksgiving Day.*

Very fine, clear, cold winter day —
Brkfo. about 8 — my head bothering
me with a stupid headache — We
all went down to Mr C[illegible] Downs's
& back home before noon — Then
Katie, Mollie, Jim & I walked to
the Reservoir & back & I lay down
for a rest before lunch at 1 — At
about 2.30 I started out aimlessly
for a walk — went over to West Rock
passing around the face of it & climbing
the west end & descending by the
path on the east end — home at 5 o'clk
& lay down until 6, my head still
very uncomfortable — took a scant
dinner — another eve'g of toe-
toasting & chatting & to bed at 9.30 —
a very cold night — a better night
than last night altho' disturbed
by my aching head.
