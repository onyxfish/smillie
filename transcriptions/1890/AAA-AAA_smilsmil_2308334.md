## Left Page

### November, Thursday 20, 1890

Blustering, windy, march-like day —
very dusty & dirty — growing colder — In
the mtf., started out with Annie —
after marketing & other messages we went
to Co. 28th St. & 5th Ave. to a Nacery Etching
Co. to see about getting a memorial table
for the Wadleigh Histological Room in
Womans College — met there Lockwood
DeForrest. I got to studio about 11 &
worked on megatint until 7 — then
home to lunch — wrote to Herrick &
sent him a lot of checks — Before
I got to work this mtf. I called on [illegible]
Tiffany to learn about the absorption of
our studio's bldg. by the Tiffany Glass Co. He
says I will not be disturbed but that the
part of the building where gel is will be
pulled down & rebuilt. — Worked from
2.45 until 4.45 on megatint — Male came
in — At 5.30 called on Geo. Haven Putnam —
too late — then to Salmagundi Club with
Sharpened scraper for Schilling — met
Smith there & he showed me the new rooms.
Home to dinner at 6.30. Ev'g at 8.30 —
went down to Century Club & heard
Dellenbaugh tell of his adventures in making
a boat journey with Major Powell down the
Colorado River Canon — ill. in a very satisfying
way by Stereopticon photos, views — out at about
10.45 — I walked up home & to bed at 11. [illegible]

## Right Page

### November, Friday 21, 1890

Still fine weather, altho' windy and
very dirty. It was 10 o'clk. before I
got to my work on megatint — Stuck
to it until I then home to lunch —
back at 2.30 & printed all the afternoon — getting two bfp. from my mego-
tint & 3 or 4 from Colman's small plate
"Kew" for Etching Club. Cat. It was
dark before I got everything cleaned
up — then, being very tired, I sat
down in my big chair & had a nap —
went home soon after 6 o'clk —
dinner — Ev'g. I had intended to
go to the Salmagundi Club House
warming but when I got my feet in
front of a pleasant fire I concluded
that the house warming at 10 E. 38th St.
was a more agreeable entertainment.
Ro. & Emily & Jno. Valentine spent
a part of the ev'g. with us — to bed
before 11.
