## Left Page

### April, Sunday 6, 1890

A very bright, beautiful day —
Bkfo. at 9 — after that, news-
paper & writing long letter of
garden instructions to Herrick —
At 11.30 Annie & I Rode up to
Central Park + 72° — stopping at
Mr. Villard's to enquire after her
sick boy — Saw her for a moment —
boy said to be out of danger — Then
we walked through the Ramble &
out at 5th Ave & 59th St — Rode home
1.30 — a rest before dinner at 2 —
At 3.30 I went to Collyer's church
& there met Annie & the boys who
were at the Easter service & flowers
we walked home bearing our pots
with us — Tea at 6.30 — Evelf.
I completed my long letter to
Herrick & posted it at 10.25 —
to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 7, 1890

Rain in the m'f. clearing about
noon — heavy showers between 2
& 4 o'clk — & again clear — Thunder
in the afternoon — To studio at 10 and
worked at retracing [illegible] Trust Co
Bldg — Home at 1.15 back at 2.15. Gave
my head a rest for awhile, as I hurt
me — then worked at my tracing until
after 5, when I finished it — Home at
6 — dinner — Evelf. wrote letter
to Kate & to bed at 10 o'clk.
