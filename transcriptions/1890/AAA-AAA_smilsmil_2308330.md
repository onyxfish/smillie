## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 12, 1890

Rain in the m'f., dull all day. After 10 when I got to studio &
I worked diligently on the Shirlaw plate. Disturbed by arrival
of coal that had to be carried &
dumped into my coal box —
Home at 1.30 — Kate had arrived
from Po'k. Back to studio before
3 & worked as long as I could
see — that was only until 4.35
Went to Coleman's apartments
in 25th S. Saw Mr. C. who described
in detail to me the runaway
accident by which C. was con-
siderably injured. Home at 6.
dinner at 6.30 — Evelf. Geo. &
Nellie & Charl came — they
stayed until 10.30 — We got
to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 13, 1890

Bright & beautiful day — Sitting too
long by a pleasant fire after breakfast,
chatting with Kate, it was 10 o'clk before
I got to the studio — I fell to work on
the Shirlaw mezzotint but was soon inter-
rupted by a call from Irving who stayed
for some time — then Wm. Tate called, &
he too stayed for some time — with one
or two other short calls my m'f. was quite
destroyed — At 1 went to bank, then home
at 1.30 — lunch — back to studio at 2.30.
my evil genius still pursued me — Miss
Oakey, an etching, called & spent some
time — Mr. Metcalf called — Nellie, Calverly,
Miss Abbott & one or two others came in —
but, in spite of it I managed to get
some work done. Soon after 5 walked
down to 14th S. & then up home. Dinner
at 6.30. Evelf. Miss Bosé called for A. —
they went to Choral Club Rehearsal. —
Charl. came & took Kate to his room.
— I had a nice little fire in the parlor
all to myself. I read & wrote —
The family came in at about 10.30 —
it was 11.30 when I got to bed.
