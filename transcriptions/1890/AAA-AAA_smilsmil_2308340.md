## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 2. 1890.

Pleasant, but with a snowy hue in the air — overcast afternoon & the m'f. I worked on hands in Mezzotint & then got everything ready for printing in the afternoon. Home to lunch — Mary & Aunt Jessie had arr. from Po'k. Emma B. came & lunched with us. It was after 2.30 when I got back to studio & took 3 p[illegible] from Mezzotint. Sam. Colman came in & gave me one ticket to Mendelssohn Glee Club Concert this eve'g. Home to dinner. Eve'g at 8 I took Annie to Chickering Hall — M. Glee Club — & having tickets, I went to the Eden Musee where I saw & heard, Otero the Spanish dancer & singer — amused but not charmed — met there Geo. & Nelly W. Thompson & his wife & sat with them until 10.30 — Then went for Annie — Home at 11 & to bed soon after, sure that I have done a foolish thing in exposing myself by going out in such an eve'g & to such places.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 3. 1890.

A bleak, snowy morning & a rainy afternoon & evening — my throat being very troublesome I thought it unwise to go out so I sat at home & wrote, scrappily getting together material for a paper to be published in the annuae of the N.Y. Etching Club. Miss Williams took dinner with us — I felt very heavy & quite used up in the eve'g & got to bed at about 10.
