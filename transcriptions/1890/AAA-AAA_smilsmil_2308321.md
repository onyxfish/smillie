## Left Page

### October, Saturday 25, 1890

Still dull & grey with a moderate
breeze from the N. W. — not very cold.
I was not in good working order
& so did not accomplish much
in the way of getting things ready
for our departure. I worked for
some time in the etching room
getting things into order there &
for awhile before dinner tying up
berry vines. After dinner I went
down town to the Bank & then had
my hair cut — back & for a time
pruning & tying the Va. creeper
vines about the house. Went
to P.O. at 6. Evef reading
news papers & Harpers for Nov.
There for a time in etching room.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 26, 1890

Another dismal, depressing day —
Heavy clouds & more or less nizzling
rain all day — After bkfs. I made
a fire in Studio furnace & then, after
my regular visit to the garden — I put
the studio into general order pre-
parations to packing. I got con-
siderable work done before dinner
at about 2 o'ck — & after dinner
I was busy until dark — There,
as it was not raining & the boys
had been in doors all day, Arnie,
Jim, Ralph & I went down to W.9. Parkes
for a few minutes — I took with me
the 2d. port folio containing five pt.
I ea on our return home. Evef
I wrote to Mary & Charles & to Clark
the plumber. After 9 I went to P.O.
