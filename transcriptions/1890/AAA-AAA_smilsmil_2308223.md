## Left Page

### April, Friday 4, 1890

A warm & heavy rain most of
the day until about 5 p.m. when
it cleared suddenly — To studio
about 10 — took palette etc & went
to N.A. & fussed a little over some
of my work there & had pleasant
chats with a number of the fellows —
To studio after 12 — wrote a note or
two — then home to lunch — Annie
away off in Brooklyn somewhere —
Woman's Unitarian League — Back to
studio at 2.30 — for a time studied
Henderson's seed catalogue — then
finished Trust Co's Bldg. Tracing —
Watrous & Dr. Purdy called for Water
Colors for Union League. Home at 6 —
dinner — Evng completed my
seed list — wrote letter to Herrick
— posted it — To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 5, 1890

Bright day, but rather windy, dirty
& cool — To studio about 10 — worked
at retracing Union Trust Co. Bldg.
Spent some time making out list
of Vegetables & Flower seeds with
which I went to Henderson & Co's
35 Cortlandt St — at 12 o'clk. Home
to lunch at 1 — Ella Diman called
while I was out — To studio at 2.30
& again, retracing Bldg — Home
at 6 — dinner — Evng at 8.30
walked down to Centery — Had
a very pleasant evng until
11.45 — walked up home with
Geo & Chas & to bed at 12.30
