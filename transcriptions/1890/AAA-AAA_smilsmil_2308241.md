## Left Page

### May, Saturday 10, 1890

much of the m'f. overcast with sprinkles of rain — rain, quite heavy, at night. Got to studio about 9.15 — very heavy head and generally uncomfortable, so that I had to drive myself to work on Charl's painting. Alex. Schilling came in & spent some time — Home to lunch & back — I worked until about 4.30 getting a part of foreground painted — then washed brushes — Charl came in about 5 — Home at 6 — dinner — Eve'g. used up on sofa — Charl came & spent eve'g with Annie laying out details on the Swiss portion of his projected summer trip — To bed at about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 11, 1890

Rain in the m'f., but cleared in the afternoon — I was in bed all day, pretty wretched generally — Annie took advantage of it & bestirred herself to work out a plan she has long had in mind. She went to Yale's & consulted with him & later she went to see Miss Kent & W. & Mrs. Akins & the result was a well defined plan to start for her muda next Thursday, to be gone ten days. I got up at 7 P.M. & then the plan was unfolded. I cannot feel that I am in such desperate straits as to call for such an outlay of time & money.
