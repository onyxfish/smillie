## Left Page

### November, Sunday 2, 1890

A very pleasant day spent in a very lazy way — It was after 9 when we had bkfst, and then I lounged about until 1 when I went to to Dr. Gale's & saw him for a few minutes. Home to dinner at 2. Calls from various members of the fam — Geo. & Lucy Cook — Jas. Wright, Jno. Valentine, Ed [illegible] & Mary Cook & most of the children — Charles also came in — Tea at about 7 — Every did some reading — about 11 when we got to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 3, 1890

Pleasant day. Got away from the house at about 9.30 — then to Market & grocers etc — & to studio where I met Geo. It was 10.30 when I got to the 2nd Nat. Bk. where I spent some time arranging A's money matters — then to Union Transfer Co & down town to Commercial Bank where I got Int. on M.L.S.&W. Inc. Bonds — then to Tunnel & Waists in Canal St — then again to 2nd Nat. Bk. & home to dinner at 1.30 about 3.30 rode up town with A — she got out at 61st St — & I rode on to Met. Mus. of Art. Fall opening — wandered about until 5 — then walked down with Champney & Waller. Tea before 7 — Every Geo. came in for awhile. To bed at about 10.
