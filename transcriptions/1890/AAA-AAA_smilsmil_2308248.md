## Left Page

### May, Saturday 24, 1890

At Home.

Overcast with sunshine before 9 o'clk — after which, a very pleasant
day — B[k]f[s]t about 9 — after that Annine & I started out & went to the
State, War & Navy Dept— then to the White House — then to Treasf— then
to Corcoran Art Gallery where we stayed until near 1 — then to the
hotel, doing a little shopping on the way — Dinner about 1.30 — square
acct— & at 3 went up to the Capitol where we spent an hour in the House
& Senate Chamber — then out & rode in a carriage through the handsomest
part of the city, the driver pointing out the residences of well known people.
A very delightful hour — at 4.45 to hotel — got hand bags & then to R.R.
Station & took "Congressional Limited" at 5 o'clk — An excellent dinner
in dining Room car at 6.30 — Got to N.Y. at 10 & home a little before 11 —
The girls were yet up — & Kate got me a cup of tea — It was near
midnight when we got to bed.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 25, 1890

Overcast much of the day & quite
cool — late B[k]f[s]t — & spent the day
indoors until after 4 P. m. when
I went to the studio for letters — back
before 6 — Various members of the
family called, among others, Mary
& her father, Mr. Sprague. Evelf
Chase came in & stayed until
after 10.30 — To bed about 11.
