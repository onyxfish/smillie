## Left Page

### May, Friday 30, 1890

*Decoration Day —*

A little rain in the early m't
but dry after that with sunshine
& threatening shadow — winds veering
from E. - S.S. Got to studio before 10
& spent the m't writing notes and
getting things into shape preparing
to packing & moving for the summer.
After 12. Jim, Edw'd — & Tom Cook came
in, amused themselves for awhile
& soon after I we all walked up
town together. Lunch — Back to
studio at 3 & spent the afternoon
mounting proofs & getting ready a
pkg of many p'ps that I will send
to Mr. Koehler for the Boston Museum
of Fine Arts — the National Museum &
Pratt's Institute — Home soon after 6 —
dinner — eve'g some reading &
more loafing. To bed soon after
10.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 31, 1890

Very pleasant day — To studio before
10 — then to 2d. Nat. Bank where I spent
some time arranging Annie's money
matters for the summer — At 11.30 went
down town to A.B. N. Co. with corrected
Union Trust Co. drawing & "Pernambuco"
for which I got $75— Got more finished
die p'p to go with my etching p'p. Went
to J.K. Campbell's — Warren S. out — Home
at 2 o'clk & had lunch — It was after
3 when I got to Studio — Q'p had gone to
Ridgefield — Charl had been there with a
letter from Kate — I finished packing
a bundle of proofs — 36 in all — Recent
etchings — p'ps from old plates & from dies
by Father & myself — for Boston Museum
— National Museum & Pratt's Institute &
at 5 o'clk. Took them to Adams Exp —
addressed to S.R. Koehler. Ret'd & wrote
notes to S.R.K. & Mr. Chas. Clark of Boston.
Home at 6.30 — dinner — eve'g about
9 Annie & I went to Walter's. Louise
played for us on her new piano — home
after 10 & to bed.
