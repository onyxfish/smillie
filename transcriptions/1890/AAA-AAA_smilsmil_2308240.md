## Left Page

### May, Thursday 8, 1890

Raine meet about 2 oclt - then
it cleared coal & very pleasant —
felt the effects of yesterday's head
ache very much — did not get to
studio until after 11 — did nothing.
Home to lunch at 1 — After lunch
Annie walked with me to Starn's &
I exchanged the fish knife that I
bought for her for a small chatelaine
silver watche — she went down town
& I went to see the da Farge pictures at
Richard's — then to studio & smeared
away on Charé's canvas — washed dishes
& cleaned up generally — After 5 went
to N.A. f.D. — there until 6 — then home.
Evelf. Annie went with Emma to a
concert given by the Metropolitan Soct.
I read until 9 — then wrote to Herrick.
Posted it before 10 & to bed. But
Charé came in & I was prevented
from finishing it — I got to bed
at about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 9, 1890

Bright & beautiful day — to studio at
9.30 & commenced painting on Charé's
picture 20 x 30. I made a long day
of it, going home to lunch as usual,
but was not able to cover the canvas.
I got over the sky pretty well, but
stuck on the distant trees, then I
boggled forward & stopped on the
middle distance & when I got
home was exceedingly tired —
dinner — then finished my letter
to Herrick & about 8.30 took Annie
to Walter's & then went on to the
Century Club where from 9 until
after 10 I heard a "talk" on the
Hawaiian Islands & their volcanoes.
Charé was there & before 11 we rode
up town together.
