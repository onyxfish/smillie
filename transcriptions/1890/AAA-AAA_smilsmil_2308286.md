## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 12. 1890.

Delightful day — brilliant + cool.
I was much out of sorts from a headache. Uncle Willie + Aunt Agnes were
expected this evef., but a telegram
announced that they were to go on to
Athens first. I spent the whole
day out of doors — in garden + among
berry bushes — at 4 o'clk. we went
Riding — back at 6 — dressed, +
Annie + I went to Judge [illegible]
Jessup's to tea where we met a
number of elderly young people
+ spent the evef. pleasantly until
after 10 — then home + to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 13. 1890.

A very pleasant day — late blfo — 8.30
— after that out of doors + in garden until
near 11, then to etching room + worked
on Aqua-tint "Old House" at it until dinner
abt 1 o'clk. After dinner I worked
again with burnisher until 4 o'clk
when we went out Riding, exploring
a new road toward Park Vale — A
shower menaced us most of the
way. We got back at 7 o'clk +
found that there had been a smart
shower in town, but it had not
reached us. Tea — after that, in dark
room, demonstrating that my new Eikronger
developer is defective + that my plate
holders, with which I have taken such
great pains, leak worse than ever.
Read + wrote + to bed about 10 :30.
Herrick went to funeral of his
wife's sister at Great Bend.
