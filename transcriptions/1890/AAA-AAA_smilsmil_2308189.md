## Left Page

### January, Sunday 26, 1890

Very mild day — most of the time overcast. Bkfo before 9 o'clk. for a time read newspapers. Then, as Gretta's sprained foot makes it impossible for her to walk much, Annie & I went out with Jim & Ralph. walked down to 25th S. & called on the Colmans. Mr. C. not in but saw Sam. He went to studio with me while Annie Ret'd home with the children. I was at studio until after noon. Then walked up to 37th St. & called on Jane Hinshelwood to enquire about Mary who is very sick but now improving. Then up to 43d S. & back home. Dinner at 2. — then a nap. Near 5 went with A. to Washington Pl. or Square — to call on Mrs. Shinneger — She was out. Then walked up to 22d S. & called on the Kimballs — Saw Carrie & Fanny — then home — had a cup of tea — Evef. wrote to Herrick & some other notes, & got to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 27, 1890

A very pleasant day & mild withal. I got to studio soon after 9. — then to N.A. Then to Kurtz's where I almost lost hope everything was going so badly for the cat. At 10.30 I went to Rankin, the printers, in Cortlandt St. made some enquiries and arrangements there — got some paper & back to K's before noon — took a plate home to lunch at 1 — to studio at 2.15 worked awhile on plate — then to K's. Chas. Parsons came & took away, for the Harpers, a set of pf. I stayed at K's until 4.30 — then to N.A. To studio at 5 — Saw Jo. & Nellie & got letter from Kate — Jany 13. Home at 6 — dinner — Evef. went to U. S. Club saw Chase & gave him Katie's letter — then home — wrote a letter to Kate & got to bed about 10.30.
