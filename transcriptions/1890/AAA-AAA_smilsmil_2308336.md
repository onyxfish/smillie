## Left Page

### November, Monday 24, 1890

Pleasant weather still continues at 9 o'clk. I went by appointment to W. J. Pierpont Morgan's - 219 Mad. Ave., was greeted pleasantly & after a few minutes conversation concerning the portrait painting of his father & the etching after it that he wishes to make he told me that he placed the whole thing in my hands. So, I left, quite elated, did my marketing & got to studio before 10 — worked on Shirlaw's mezzotint until 12. Then to Kurtz's & to 2°. Nat. Bk. Home to lunch at 1. Mr. Atherton then two children to lunch with us — At 2.30 I went to [illegible] & Voigt's with Colman's. Kee etching until 4 o'clk. labored with young Voigt trying to teach him how to take proofs such as I had taken — gave up in despair. Then to C. A. Platt's studio. 3 Wash. Sq. in — then to studio — Home at 5.15. Dinner at 7. Lizzie Smillie dined with us — did Emelita Boyne. At 9.30 I escorted them to their homes — Lizzie to the N.Y. Hospital in W. 15th S — & Minna to the cor. of Mad. ave. & 28th S — Home & to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 25, 1890

A light rain in the early m'f threatening an unpleasant day, but 'h cleared hazy & very mild — At 9 o'clk went to E. S. Morgan's & got permission to send Kurtz for the painting I am to etch — then to market — then to Kurtz & back to studio soon after 10. Then, until 1 o'clk. printing from the Shirlaw mezzotint — Home at 1.5. Lunch — back to studio a little before 3 & to work with mezzotint rocker experimenting. I rocked, with large toothed rocker, a key line over parts of background. Mr. S. R. Koehler came in at about 4.30. Home at 6. Elig. Howe joined us at dinner. Eve'g, at 8. Auline, Ely. & Mallie Adams went to Geo. Coaks — Kate went to see Charl, who is still laid up, & I completed dressing & about 8.30 went to Charl's, got Kate & went to Georges where the other members of the family were celebrating Fith's 5th wedding anniversary — Beer & crackers — left at 10.30 — Mallie & I took Elig. down to her lodgings in Mad Ave. near 30th S — Then home & to bed at 11.30.
