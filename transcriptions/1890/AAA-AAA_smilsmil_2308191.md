## Left Page

### January, Thursday 30, 1890

Wet + overcast until about 9 —
after that, gradual clearing, until
at times the weather was sunny
\+ almost warm — Raw + overcast
again before night. To studio at
9.30 — Soon went over to N.A. Saw
there J.F. Brown — Eaton — Symington
\+ others — They were not favorably
impressed by the [illegible] fills for cat.
Hitchcock of Tribune came in + I
spent some time going through
the galleries with him — Home to
lunch — To studio at 3.30 — M. Donett
came in + stayed some time — I
did something in the way of cleaning
up. Home at 4.30 — dressed + waited
for Annie until after 5 — then we went
to Rich'd Dorman's Reception — 31 W. 52d S —
a pleasant half hour — then home —
dinner — Eve'g I went to Geo. Cook's with
tickets for to-morrow night — then to Edson
where I saw Walter [illegible] for the first time
since their engagement, his fiancee, Mrs.
Dalcy, to whom I gave my blessing —
then home — Geo. [illegible] nicely came in
\+ spent the eve'g with us — Robed
before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 31, 1890

Wet in [illegible] — nearly clearing afterward —
To studio + spent some time in
putting away the things that have
been thrown down anywhere during
the past two weeks, waiting
for a cleaning woman who did not
come.

Eve'g. Private View — (Stag
Party) Water-Color Exhib'n — on my
way stopped in 25th St. Saw W.
\+ Mrs. Colman — C. went with
me to the N.A. — a very jolly
time — Home + to bed after 12.
