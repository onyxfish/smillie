## Left Page

### September, Friday 5, 1890

Mild m't, with threats of rain. a very little fell, bet. 10 & 11 Am. a light shower, with distant thunder from 2 to 3 P.m. After 7 P.m. a quite rain for an hour or two. — felt better than I did yesterday but did not attempt to work with much energy — I mixed new mordant, studied up the formulas for the new photo. developer, Eikonogen & made a ball of "soft ground" & laid a ground with it for experimenting — at 4 o'clk. I went to the "Richards" cottage & made pencil sketch of a bit that I think will be of use to me. Eve'g, sauntering on piazza & reading newspapers.

The men lost one hour.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 6, 1890

Foggy m't clearing to leaden clouds with threats of rain — misgivings turned into rain & at 10 the diggers gave up work — but the weather soon cleared & they came back at 1. I spent some time charcoaling a sketch based upon the slight pencil sketch that I got in front of the Richards' cottage yesterday — rubbed in a fatte of color — also continued my experiments with soft ground. I went down to Burns' drug store & got some inferior nitric acid as my muriatic mordant doesn't seem to work satisfactorily on soft ground work. The men were at work with the team hauling stones & dirt, so we did not ride. Eve'g — newspapers mostly. To bed at 10.

The men lost two hours.
