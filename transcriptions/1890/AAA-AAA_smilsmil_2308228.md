## Left Page

### April, Monday 14, 1890

Pleasant, altho' rather chilly —
To studio at usual time & spent
the m'f at work on Union Trust
Co. bldg. Home to lunch soon after
1 — Emily commenced her visit —
back to studio at 2.30 & spent
the afternoon repainting sky on
12"×16" View on the coast near Marblehead's Point, back of Marblehead.
Home about 6 — dinner — Geo. and
Smiley with us. Well. I wrote my
regular letter to Mary — To bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 15, 1890

A pleasant day — To Studio at 10
& to work upon Union Trust & bldg
P.M. in a full day — DeKnoce came
in for awhile in the m'f. He says
this about 10 years since his last
visit — Manly came in for a few
minutes — Home to lunch & back
as usual. Worked until after
5 — then a short rest before going
home — Dinner at 6.30 — At 8 I
went to Chickering Hall & met,
by appointment, Emma & Grace J.
in the lobby — Concert of Mendelssohn
Glee Club — very charming — out
at a little before 11 — Took Emma
& Grace home — then home & to bed
at midnight.
