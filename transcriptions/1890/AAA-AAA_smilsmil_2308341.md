## Left Page

### December, Thursday 4, 1890

Pleasant day out of doors, but I did not get there — I was in bed
all day, very sore about the throat & chest. Yale came to see me in
the m'f & advised me to keep quick in-doors until all soreness
passed away — I got up in the early ev'g & sat in the parlor,
Annie having gone to her Choral Club rehearsal — Kate & Mary at
home & Charlie with us for a short time — It was about 11.30 when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 5, 1890

A raw, cold day — overcast. In doors all day with little to vary
the monotony — I sat in the dining room & in the m'f wrote notes — to
T.S. church, among others, in relation to his portrait that I wish to have
in Etching club Publication next — they & he refuses. Lunch at one —
Annie not at home — woman's Unitarian League in Brooklyn — but she managed
to get to Phil. Rehearsal in the afternoon.
Afternoon I commenced rewriting my Composition for Etching Club Pub.
Dinner before 6.30 — Mary & Kate at Divings to tea. To bed soon after 10.
