## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 25. 1882.

A hot day but so breezy that in
the shade there was no discomfort.
Spent some of the m[orning] in fading
duck onto the [wall] of S.W. bed
Room. Hamlin put in an
appearance & got to work on
the chimney again. — I
made measurements &
then a careful drawing
of First or Lower Hall. Trains
it which I straightened out
things for Burgess — Dinner
as usual. — & P.M. as usual
looking after everything & every
body — Tea at 6.30 — again to
the house — There until after 8 —
thence to P.O. & after 9 — back
to C's where we sat on piazza
until after 10, enjoying the
moonlight — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 26. 1882.

Another very hot day, but with
strong S.W. breeze — most of the
m[orning] was spent in S.W. bed Room
fading duck to the wall. Dinner
as usual — & after it again to the
house where I spent the P.M.
in all sorts of work — Tea at 6.30
Mr. Cortis's brother arr[ived] — [illegible].
After tea at the house for some
time — then down town — stopped
at De Schweinitz's — left Annie
there while I went on to Payne's —
on my return stopped again
at De S's & sat on front porch
until 9.30 — then we went to
our quarters — I spent some
time in a hammock under
the trees — To bed after 11.
