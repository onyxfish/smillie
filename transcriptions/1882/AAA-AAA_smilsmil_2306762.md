## Left Page

### May, Friday 12. 1882.

A horrible driving storm of wind & rain all day, so that I did not dare to go out — In the m'g. I wrote a letter to Cotville & sent him check. The rest of the day was spent in meas'ly going about the house. My head troubling me with a rheumatic and neuralgic sort of pain. Eve'g. Geo. came & spent some time with us. Got to bed about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 13. 1882.

Hardly a pleasant day. Rain began to fall at night.

In bed all day with a headache. Dr. Yale came at about 3 o'clk. when I was beginning to recover. Between 5 & 6 I got up & shortly after 6 had tea. Mary came in the afternoon to stay over a day or two with us. Charl came about 8 & took her to his rooms & near 11 Geo. came back with her. I sat up after the others had gone to bed — at 12.30 I began to feel tired and then I went to bed too.
