## Left Page

### January, Thursday 12, 1882

Delightful day — got to studio at 9.30 & worked all the m'f
on Black Mt. Home to dinner at 1.30 — Back at 3 & worked
as long as I could see — at 6 went to Reumer's shop — then
home to tea. Emma & Grace there — took them & Annie to
singing club at 8 — then to studio — Colman came in.
I fussed — fixed pictures, studied them by gas light
& made until 10 — then went for a — & then home — sat
& read for awhile — midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 13, 1882

The nastiest possible sort of weather — snow at night, rain in the
m'f. & slush & splash all day.
Got to studio at 9.30 — after writing
some notes & getting off my weekly
letter & check to Montrose I set to
work on Black Mt. & stuck to it
until 12.30 — then to Bank & home to
dinner at 1 — Annie lunched at
Mary (née) Cook's with Miss Louise Alcott
& some others — I went to Phil. Re-
hearsal & enjoyed it — then, at
4.30 & until 6.30 at work putting
pictures into frames, then home.
Tea at 7 — Read for an hour, then
for an hour made cat. Preface
article on etching. Then, as it had
cleared overhead, I walked up to
5th ave & 53rd St. — then back & to bed
soon after midnight.
