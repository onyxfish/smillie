## Left Page

### May, Sunday 28, 1882

Very warm pleasant day with quite a heavy shower and one solitary thunder-clap between 3 & 4 P.m. Mrg. as I was still dyspeptic I spent much time on the bed — about 1 Annie & I went to 6th ave. & rode up to Centrals Park — walked about for half an hour, then home at 2 & soon after had dinner — after that some reading. Nearly 5 when Annie & I went to George's & saw Nellie & the boy. Still very threatening clouds on W. horizon, but took 3rd ave. Elevated R.R. & rode up to 59th St — then to 5th ave, walked in Park & up to 75th St — then over to Mad ave cars — & home at 7. Eveg., heavy rain — reading & writing to Father — Thuringer & others — at 10.30 took letters to depot — back & to bed about 11

## Right Page

### May, Monday 29, 1882

*[top margin note: Mother & Kate came from P'o'K]*

Brilliant, beautiful day — to studio at 9.30 & worked all the day on Cuban Sugar Plantation drawing for A.B.N. Co. Home to dinner at 1.30 — 3.30 when I got back to studio — worked until 5.30 — then to Dewey & Keating's — then to Lincrusta-Walton Co. — then to Wunderlich's & at 7 home to tea. Mother & Kate arr'd from P's at about 4.30 — just to see George's boy — Eveg. Geo came and visited with Mother — to bed at 11.
