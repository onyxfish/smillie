## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 31. 1882.

foggy, muggy day — got to
studio after 10 — Mr. C. J. Halle
called; from Estes & Lauriat —
at noon went to Academy of
Music to see Sohst — not then
— then home to dinner — got it
at 1.30 — then again to see
Sohst & find that he has not only
failed to get for me the seat
I want (244) but that owing to
Charl's way of executing my
commission, he has sold my
rehearsal seat — I am sick
with vexation — again to Stud —
much time taken up about Etching
matters — Home to tea at 6 —
writing to Koehler — Estes & Lauriat
& to Osgood until near 9 — then
to Dr. Yale's — Com. from Etching
Club to consider a proposition
to manage a course of 3 lectures by
Seymour Haden — Geo & Thos Morrow
there — Walked home at about
10.30 — & to bed —

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 1. 1882.

Very mild, clear day — Having
had a miserable night of pain
& discomfort I did not get up
until about 9 & had my bkfst. brot
up to me — At 10.30 went to bank
& deposited a check that Geo had
given me — then down town to
Equitable Ass. & paid prem — to
Charl's — not in — to Will's & got
materials for work — to Rumsey
Pump Co. — & then home to dinner
at 1.30 very tired — After that
had a nap & at 4 went to Studio —
found draft for $300 from Chicago —
sale of W. C. "Bek Mt. from Harborss —
Lake Geo." wrote a couple of notes —
called on Miss Abbatt & congratulated
Miss Canning on her engagement to
Frank Waller — then called & saw
Geo. who is at home with sore throat.
Home to tea at 6.30.
