## Left Page

### January, Saturday 28, 1882

A horrible combination of snow and rain all day long — After bkfs. went to Union League Club, trying to get Catalogue that I left there last night, then to Market with Annie, then, together, we went to Private View of Water Colors at N.A. — there all the m'f. carefully looking for something for Annie to buy — found only two little sketches, by Dewey & Ratt. To studio for a moment, then home to dinner at 1.30 — After that being very tired, I had a nap — At 4.30, Coursé came — A. & I went to Haven Turnam's & saw him for a few minutes — then to Mrs. Chas. Schans's Reception — then to the Receptions of Mrs. Chas. Adams in 36th St. & Mrs. Sharbless in 37th St. Home to tea at 6.15 — At 7.15 went to Dr. Thomson's — there half an hour — then home — then rest of the ev'g reading — to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 29, 1882

Very cold, clear & windy — Bkfs. after 9 — after 11 Annie & I went out for a walk, being too late for church, we went to 106 Madison Ave. to call on Mrs. Downs — not in — then to N.A. & D. & looked about there for a time, then again to 106 and saw Mrs. D. & her son John. Then home — dinner about 2.30 — Gel. & Nellie — Walter Cook & Valentine called — Mary Ireland, also. Mary (ned) & children called in the m'f. I went to Colman's for a few minutes about 6 — then a walk & home — tea at 7 — Eve'g, Annie Redd aloud for awhile — I wrote a letter home & at 10 went to depot with it. To bed soon after 11.
