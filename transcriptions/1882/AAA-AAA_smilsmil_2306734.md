## Left Page

### March, Friday 17, 1882

Charming day. Spent the m[orning]
at studio's working on pen sketch
after Hovenden's "Elaine" and
in writing to Cottrell. Home
to dinner at 1.30 - feeling tired
I rested after dinner — got to
studio at 3.15 + finished "Elaine"
Mr. Dart + Mr. Walker called.
mounted two P.P. of Lady of
Cairo visiting for W. Galt. Home
to tea at 6.30 but did not get
to until 7. At 8. Annie, Matt
Kate + I went to Haverly's 5th,
Ave. Theatre + saw Olivette
(Catherine Lewis) Poor house,
play poorly given — Home
at 11.20 — Chave there with
mother — father went to
P'k. at 4 this p.m. —
To bed about midnight.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 18, 1882

Fine day — growing overcast before
night — Got to Renner's about 10
o'clk. There for an hour supervis-
ing the opening of 2°. case of
Etchings from London — then to
studio — Finished "Elaine" — Mr.
Kurtz called — Home to dinner
at 1.15 — Fanny K. there — back
to studio at 2.30 — at about 3 went
with W.K. to N.A. & there worked
in the galleries making sketches
after Eastman Johnson — Bridgman
\+ Dolph. After 6 to studio & Home
at 6.30 — Tea about 7 — Eve'g
spent quietly & Restfully —
Got to bed at 10.30 —
Matt Ret? home at 4.
