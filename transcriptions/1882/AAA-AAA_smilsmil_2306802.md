## Left Page

### July, Thursday 27, 1882

A very hot day made almost pleasant by a persistent breeze. Spent the mf. about among the men &
with Annie in S.D. bed room —
tacking & stretching duck up on
the walls — Dinner as usual
& the afternoon spent in general
oversight of carpenter's & mason's
— Tried to do something at plan
drawing but was too tired —
Tea at 6.30 — again to the house
wrote a couple of notes — watched
some of the trees & worked about
their roots — At 8.30 Annie & I
went to P.O. & to Boyd's — back
to C's at 9 — & for an hour swung
in a hammock — beautiful
moonlight night — to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 28, 1882

Warm day, but very breezy — threatening
at times to rain — Spent most of the
mf. with A. in S.CO. Room tacking
duck to the walls — Tried also to
do some drawing up on stair
plans — Dinner at 1 — Afternoon
overcast & quite threatening —
General oversight until
tea at 6 — again to studio
& worked about as long as
I could see — cleared to a
beautiful moonlight night —
A. & I went to P.O. — on our
way back, stopped at [illegible]
Coopers — the family on the
piazza. Sat there until after
9.30 — then to C's & to bed
at about 10.30.
