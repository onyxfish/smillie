## Left Page

### March, Sunday 19. 1882.

Lovely Spring day — Rain in
the early morning — B k p. at
9 — went to church door with
Annie & her Mother — then to
studio & wrote letters to Osgood
& Boston & Bates of Providence — at
12.30 walked up town & saun-
tered about until 2 — then
home & had dinner about
2.30. After that Will & Charl
came — Mother came with A. V. Y.
about 4 — I had a good nap —
& at 6.30 went to studio for
some letters — Home at 7 — then
tea — Charl. came later &
spent the even't until 10.30
to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 20. 1882.

Pleasant day, growing gradually
more overcast & threatening came —
9.30 got to N. A. & to work making
pen & crayon sketches for [illegible]
Academy notes. Worked until
1 o'clk — then home to dinner.
Back at 2.30 and worked as
long as I could see — then to
studio & fixed things there for
the night — then went to George's
to see how he was getting along
with his cold — and at near 7
got home to tea — Mother &
Kate were to have gone home to-
day, but changed the plan — Soon
after 8, Annie & I went to Mrs.
Coleman's — Mr & Mrs. Rokker, Mr & Mrs.
Lewis, Mr & Mrs. Rob't Hoe Jnr. Mr.
Havemeyer & Fanny there. Emma
at home sick — Home at 11.15
& right to bed.
