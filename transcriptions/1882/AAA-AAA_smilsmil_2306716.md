## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 7, 1882

Still mild and clear — Streets
all afloat — after bkfst. a. + I
went to Arnold & Constable's +
spent much of the m'g there
planning over parlor curtain
that had been sent there to be
remade + upon which we had
many experiments. Rummaging
over the goods to do so — back to
studio at 12.15 — wrote letter to
Osgood — Home to dinner at 1.20
More time given to China —
To studio's after 3. Mr. Koehler tun.
At 5 went to Tiffany's — then to
41 Union Sq. Lincrusta — Home at
6.15 — tea — wrote a note to Mr. Rob't.
Gordon on behalf of Mr. Koehler, took
it to the house. Mr. G. not at home —
left note — back — made drawing for
parlor curtain — to bed at
11.30.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 8, 1882

Pleasant day got to studio
about 10 + spent the morning
mounting etchings and other
fussings — Home to dinner at
1 — back at 2.30 — Mr. Koehler
came in + made quite a call.
I accomplished nothing in
the way of work — About 5
went to Tiffany's about China
dinner set — then to see the
Lincrusta-Walton material
for wall covering — Home soon
after 6 — Mr. Koehler came
about 6.30 + soon after had
tea — Every passed in pleas-
ant general conversation —
Mr. K. left at 9.30 — to bed
as usual about 11 or later.
