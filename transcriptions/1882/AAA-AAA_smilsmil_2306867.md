## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 6, 1882

Bright, clear & moderately cold. Spent the m'g in charcoaling Charc's picture — a view of Bel Mt. also planning W.C. of causeway from Marblehead neck to Mainland —. Home to dinner at 1 — back about 3 & continued charcoaling. After work went to Grad's then home at 6.30 — Geo. Valentine there to dinner to eat up the ducks brought home from the shooting expedition — Dr. Adams came in while we were at table — at 8.30 I called on Herbert Herkomer by invitation at his rooms in the Rembrandt W. 57th S. — & spent a chatty every until near 11 o'clk — then home & to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 7, 1882

Overcast, dreary & very cold — to Tailor's after bkft. & tried on coat — then to studio's & commenced painting on Charc's picture — a view of Bel Mt. Lake Geo? Home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 and painted as long as I could see; Interrupted to fix up an Itching "Bold White Rose" — Published a frame & wrote the verse on the margin. Had a rest during twilight — at 6.30 to Aunt Anna's where I met Annie & Fanny — had tea — there all to Singing club — I then went for awhile to Salmagundi [illegible] to see Falt, then to studio where I worked until 10 then to club for Annie — then home & to bed by 11.
