## Right Page

### January, Sunday 1, 1882

Bright m'f, gradually growing sour & overcast. B'kf't at 9 — after
that, lighted a wood fire in the
parlor, very pleasant — Spent much
time in front of it — At 11.30 went
to studio — there for a little while,
then walked up to Central Park
& home at 2. Dinner soon after.
After that, again before the fire.
Walter came in — then Valentine,
about 5, after writing a short
note home, Annie & I went
around to Ned's for a little
while — at 6, back home to
tea — At 8 Annie & I went
to Aunt Anna's in E. 17th St —
Very cold & fine, driving
snow — Kitty & Emma, just
Ret'd from Harley — Mr. Collins
came in — Home at 10.30 —
To bed at 11.30.
