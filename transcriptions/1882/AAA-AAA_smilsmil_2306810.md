## Left Page

### August, Saturday 12. 1882.

charming day & very cool — the
mrg was spent in making
drawings for blinds & in sup-
erintending the correcting of the
errors in the S.W. Room — Dinn
abt 1 — afternoon like mrg —
some drawing on blinds and
a good deal of following up the
men — feel out of sorts — tired
& depressed — abuse the day
when the building was commenc-
ed — I alone am to blame & I have
proved that in some things I
have but little judgement —
Annie too is under this weathe
— a very unusual thing for
her — Tea at 6 — about 7 I
went to P.O. left my order
for blinds with W. Boyd —
then back to C's — read
paper etc until 10 — to bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 13. 1882.

Very cool & overcast — S.W. wind. Brf:
at 8.30 — miserable headache — Annie
still quite out of sorts — About 9.30
we went to studio & kindled a fire
that crackled pleasantly — wrote
some letters — About 11 I started
out & for an hour & a half wan-
dered over the hill enjoying
out of doors — Dinner soon
after 1 — again to studio, more
writing — Tea at 6 — again
to studio — wrote to Geo & Oscar
Ireland — Abt 9 Took Annie
to C's & with Camp Emmons
walked down to P.O. — deposited
our letters, then back & to bed
before 11 —
