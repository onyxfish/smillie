## Left Page

### February, Monday 13, 1882

Dismal rainy day — Edith went home at 11 — I got to studio at 10 — then to N.A. met them Mr. Benedict & daughter, of Montclair — her father, mother & sister — I made sketch of Dec. moonlight for F.S. Church — to be sent to "L'Art" — then to studio — Rennie's man at work on Ports & Etcher [illegible] — Home to dinner at 1.30 — back at 3 — did little else than get up a Valentine for Mima J. — Home to tea at 6.30 — Eve'g, did not go to Mr. Colman's as usual, she having a headache — Chat — Collins called & spent part of the eveg with us — About 11.30 when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 14, 1882

Mild beautiful day — 9.45 when I got to studio — my whole m'f was spent in mounting & framing etchings for Mr. Gall at the N.A. Etching Club ex'n. Home to lunch at 1.30 — back at 3 — The afternoon as unprofitably spent as the m'f. After 5 again went to the N.A. with framed etchings — then to Mr. Colman's & met Annie & then walked home together — Mr. & Mrs. Mr. Justice & daughter Cicilia came to tea at about 6.45 — & spent the eveg, Ned & many coming in after tea — After they left, at 10, I made pen sketches ill. some lines written by Annie & then posted them, lines & sketches to Fanny K. — in acknowledgement of a bouquet of postage stamps Rec'd to-day. To bed after 11.30.
