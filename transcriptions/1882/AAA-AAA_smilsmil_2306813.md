## Left Page

### August, Friday 18, 1882

Very cool cloudy m'f., ther. at
dep't time 57°. To studio at 9 o'clk
& built a fine fire — Had to
go down towns for brick to pan
vestibule. C. went with me
to Coopers & Thos. Warners about
some slates — W. went to basement
of Episcopal Ch. with us & picked
at 40 — back at 12 — Dinner
at 1 — afternoon worked on
drawings for our bed room —
Tea soon after 6, about 9
C. & I went to Daniels Hall
to a sort of fair for Pres. Pn
Ch. — there for about an hour
then to our quarters & to
bed.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 19, 1882

Very bright, breezy cool m'f
Kept busy all day following
up Hamelin, who is preparing
marble vestibule for tiles
& Backus who is stretching duck
on M.E. bed room, 2° story. To
dinner at 1 — afternoon, tried
to work some on details for
our bed room — Burgess at
last got the stairway so
that we can go to 2° story —
Tea at 6 — Ret'd. to house and
walked piazza until 8 —
then to C's — company — a
few — dressed & came down
before 9 — Had some whist —
C. & Mrs. chat. Read ago-amusement
time — nearly 12 when we
got to bed.
