## Left Page

### March, Saturday 11, 1882

Windy but clear & very March
like day — Mr. [illegible] to Studio soon
after 10 — Mr. Dart & others
called — I worked on Montrose
drawings for house — Home
to dinner at 1.30 — back at
3 & worked until 6 on the
drawing of Piazza balustrade
Then home, spending half an
hour on the way, watching the
formation of Barnum's Grand
After Dark Procession — After tea, a
& I went to Phil. concert, where
we had a combination of Wagner
& Beethoven. & met Jas. Wright,
Edith Marion who returned
home with us. To bed soon
after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 12, 1882

Some rain in the early morning —
Bkft. soon after 9 — all went to
church with Annie & heard Dr.
Collyer preach from the text:
"Trust in God & do good works" —
one of the most satisfactory dis-
courses to me that I have yet
heard from him — After service
I took a— to her br. Ed [illegible] & I went
on for a walk to 5th Ave. & 71st —
back at 2 — dinner soon after —
then a short nap. Jas. Wright
Edith & Marion left for home at
about 5 — Walter & [illegible] Howe called
Tea at about 7 — after 8, Annie
& I went to "the Berkeley" 9th
St. & 5th Ave. & called on Mr.
& Mr. & Mrs. Ehninger
were out — Home about 10 &
to bed before 11.
