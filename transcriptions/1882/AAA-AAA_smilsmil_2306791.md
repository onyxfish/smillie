## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 5, 1882

Pouring rain all day. Bkf.
as usual a little before 8 — to
the house before 9 & spent
the mf there, most of the
time with Austin, Cooly &
Glass. Dinner abt 1 —
then a nap. At 3.30 went to
Mr. Sherolle's but she was not
at home. Then down town,
then to house — there until
6 — then to tea — after that
wrote to Semon Bache for more
glass — Took letter to P.O. &
kid. at about 9 — then read
paper until 10 & to bed
about 10.30, after sitting with
great comfort in front of a coal
fire all the evening.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 6, 1882

Very pleasant day, clearing weather
with a lively little shower about 4·o'cl
p.m. Bkf. as usual, & as usual,
went to the house before 9. Opened
box of Tiffany glass & spent the mf
in fitting it into sashes. Dinner
at 1 — then a nap. At 2.30,
again went to house. Second
load of furniture arrived in very
good shape just before the shower.
A box of colored cathedral glass
from Tiffany's was opened, found
a number of glasses broken (5).
At 6. to tea — after, Annie &
I went down town — to P.O. &c
on our way back, stopped and
called for half an hour upon Aunt
H — then to our quarters — had
the house to ourselves, as Mr. & Mrs.
C. were at a rehearsal for a
"Broom Drill" — To bed at 10.30
