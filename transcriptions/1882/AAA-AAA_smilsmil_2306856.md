## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 14, 1882

Cold, overcast & squally — Got to
studio at 9.30 — Did some work
on sketch for A.B.N. Co — Kelly
came with stretchers & I mounted
some of my summer studies.
Home to dinner at 1.30 — back
at 3 — made a Water Color sketch
experimenting with some colors
brought by Mr. Roeloff — a German
article, new in this market —
I think my little sketch is a
pretty fair one. After 5, out for
a walk — went in to Matthews'
in 23° S. to see if he had any-
thing in his sale that I wanted.
Saw a Rug that took my fancy.
Home to tea at 6.30 — Read
paper until 9 — then wrote
a letter to Ehringer, in answer
to one Rec'd this m'f. commending
Miss Walworth to the kindly
consideration of Annie & self.
To bed at 10.45

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 15, 1882

Delightful day — Mrs. Sprague
her son Henry & E.M. Cook's children
arr'd before help. from Buffalo. Got
to studio at 9.30 worked on A.B.
N. Co. drawing until noon — Then
J.W. Wood came in & talked
W.C. Soc'ty matters until 1.30
I agreed to run on Hanging
Com. Then to dinner — back
soon after 3 — worked until
after 4 — at 5 went to J. Morain's
studio & there met Fale & Chase —
Etching Club Com. on Haden Reck'g.
Home to tea at 6.30 — at 8, to
N.A. of D. W.C. Soc'ty meeting —
Hule — T.H. Smith very violent
on the subject of election of mem-
bers — I was elected on Hanging
Com. with Hovenden & H. P. Smith.
Home about 11 — to bed —
