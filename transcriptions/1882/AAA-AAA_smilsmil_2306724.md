## Left Page

### February, Saturday 25. 1882.

fine day — To studio at
10 — another day busily en-
gaged, as it seems in doing
nothing for at the end I
can only say that I have
again done nothing but
fuss over some etching p/p.
Home to dinner as usual
& back before 3 — Home
again at 6 — Annie's first
dinner party — Mr. Downs &
her son John — Deb Home — Ned
& wife Henry Sprague, Mrs.
Williams, Miss Williams, making
with the family, one dozen —
Every thing passed off very
nicely — friends left at 10 —
me did not get to bed until
11 & having to talk matters
over it was after midnight
before we got to sleep.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 26. 1882.

Beautiful day — up late —
had bkfast at 9.30 — late when
I got started for church — went
to Dr. Jno. Hall's — would not
let me in — so, walked up to
the park & there abouts until
after 1 — home at 1.30 — Preston
Spring came to dinner which
was served at 2.30 — he left
before 4 — then I had a good
nap — at 6.30, after writing
a short letter to father, Annie
& I went to Mrs. Gibbons' in
47th St. to tea — Miss Fidelia
Bridges, O. J. Day — Chas. & John
Patt there — later, Mr. & Mrs.
Marquand — home about
10.30 & to bed at 11.
