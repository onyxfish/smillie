## Left Page

### December, Monday 4, 1882

Clear & very cold day — after
bkfo- sat by a fire & wrote letter
to Mr. Koehler about Haden's lectures
in Boston etc — to studio about 10
& spent the day making careful
outline of Rough Sport, of size of
large etching — correcting & changing
in places the composition — Home
to dinner at — back at 2.30 —
Farner called & spent an hour
or more discussing Water Colors
Socy & Etching club matters —
Before 5 I went to Davey & Heaty
& ordered frock coat — then to Bish
to order a pair of boots — then to Tiffany
& left A's Ring to have a new Ruby
set in it — then to Wunderlich's &
bot pf. of a Weber etching — then to
studio, made a lot of paste & home
at 7 — Nelie there — tea — every
pasted letters of Canton flannel on
background of Turkey Red for some
hospital work — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 5, 1882

Mild & partially overcast —
Went to Standard Theatre &
bought 7 tickets for next Monday
eveg. Then to studio — the day
spent cleaning up, drawing boards
— stretching drawing paper and
cartoon paper & Jap. paper pf.
of etching — Home to dinner at
1 — back at 2.30 — Home to tea
at 6 — about 7 sat down — 13 of
us — a doll dressing festival.
Aunt Anna, Emma & Grace — Sis. Howe
Miss Willard, Nelie, Edw. C. & Mary,
Helen & Virginia Butler, Mother
C — Anna & self.
