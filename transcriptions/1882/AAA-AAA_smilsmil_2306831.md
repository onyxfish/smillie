## Left Page

### September, Monday 25, 1882

Yet another dismal, leaden-skied day with now & then a few spattering drops of rain. My trouble has changed character & is now in the stomach, making me very wretched — Indoors all the m'f. amusing myself reading Boswell's "Tour of the Hebrides" B. is a funny toady — Dinner as usual — Afternoon much like the morning, except that I took a very short walk with A — Eve'g. lying about until near 10, then to bed where I passed a miserable night.

## Right Page

### September, Tuesday 26, 1882

More leaden sky — feeling very much demoralized by the continuous dull weather. My pains & want of rest last night. The m'f spent in reading & napping. Dinner at 1 — The sun struggled out feebly during the afternoon & the wind blew hard from the N.E. I kept out of doors for some time. Annie & Nellie went to Marblehead. Eve'g. read newspapers and discussed schools of medicine. To bed at 10.30
