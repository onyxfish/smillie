## Left Page

### December, Friday 8, 1882

Very clear & very cold — Got to studio at 9.45 & to work & completed painting in d Charl's picture. Mr. Brewster of Boston called. — I spent some time hunting for lost etchings & fussing over others that I had found. Home to dinner at 12.50 — dressed — at 2.15 to Phil Rehearsal — after that went with Annie to Faulkner & Solomon's for curtain stuff — then to Mr. Robt. DeForrest's Reception of Washington Sq. N. — After that home at 6.30 — tea — Evelf. fussing over plans for Montrose — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 9, 1882

Overcast and milder. To Studio at 9.30 & to work upon a view of Mt. Washington — 10 in. circle, in oil — monotone — for Van Antwerp & Bragg of Cincinnati — to be eng'd on wood. Home to dinner as usual — worked as long as I could see on Mt. W. & did not quite finish it. — bothered by callers. Home to tea at 6.30 — Evelf. with Annie to Phil. Concert which I enjoyed very much — especially Raff's in Walde. Home & to bed about 11.30
