## Left Page

### March, Monday 27, 1882

Dismal rainy day — Up late got to studio about 10 & spent
the m'f. on Piazza drawings for Montrose House. Home
to dinner at 1 — Back at 3 after going first to the Bank.
Worked until about 5. Then Rode home & Rested for
half an hour before dressing at 7 — Annie & I at Ned Coats'
to meet at dinner Mr. & Mrs.
Hoffman of 57th S. — found
them very pleasant — Ran
away at 9.30 to the Colmans
where Belle & Mary Tweedy
were & a few friends — There
until after 11 — Home & to bed
about midnight —

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 28, 1882

Overcast m'f. but cleared
into a charming day — Geo.
went to Po'k. this m'f. I got to
studio at 10, & would have finished
Piazza drawing but for a long call
from Farrer. Home to dinner at
1 — Back at 2.30 — Finished drawing
& written directions & at 4.30 sent
them off by D.L.&W. Ex'p. — Then
went to 24 E.10th & saw old Prof
R.W. Weir & got from him a little
oil sketch that he painted of
father more than 50 years ago
— then to studio. Home at 6.30 —
Tea — m'f. I worked until
near 11 finishing some cards
with mottoes or sentiments
for the Golden Wedding —
