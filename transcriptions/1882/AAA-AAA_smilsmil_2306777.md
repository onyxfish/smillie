## Left Page

### June, Sunday 11, 1882

Overcast m[or]g, & much cooler —
but the sun shone out in a
muggy sort of way before noon —
I, rather stupid with headache
much of m[or]g in big chair — did
some writing. About 1 o'clk went
to Studio — Home to dinner abt
2 — again in chair until
after 4 — Then Geo & Nellie
came — Annie & I walked down
4th ave. with them — delightfully
cool — we went on to Annie —
Anna Ireland's — saw her & many —
Home to tea soon after 7 —
Evelf wrote home — also notes
to Coleman, Bellows, Farmer,
Mr. R. S. Gifford & Dr. Stephey
Smith — then out to depot &
to post letters. Then home
& to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 12, 1882

Delightfully cool breeze blowing
all day — Got to studio about 10
after having been in to Tiffany's
for a little while — worked on
Shetland O— still pegging at the
detail of buildings — Home to dinner
at 1.15 — Met Col B. Wood on Studio
stair — After dinner A. & her mother
went to Flushing for the afternoon.
I went to G.C. depot AM Exp. with a
pkg of mourning bds & linen for Po'k, —
then to Treas'r Office & left Annie's
certificate of C. H. R. RR. Stock to be
transferred to Mrs. J. D. S — then to 3d ave
& 34th St. to see Cm Douglass — not in,
then to P. O. Station, 3d ave 29th S, — then to
studio & at 4 o'clk in to Tiffany's then
with man cutting glass until near 6
then to studio & sent checks to Boyd
& Griffis in Montrose & home to tea
after 7 — Eve'g quietly at home
until 10.30 — then to bed.
