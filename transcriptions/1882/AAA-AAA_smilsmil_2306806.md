## Left Page

### August, Friday 4, 1882

Delightfully clear day —
breezy & very warm — Spent
all the m[orning] & much of the
afternoon sizing the duck in
S.W. bedroom. Dinner as
usual — more work on the
duck walls — also directing
the work on bedroom floors.
W. Lewis finished floor in the
washroom of our bed room
& has made a very good job
of it. I oiled it before tea
time — After tea again to
house — Mr. C — Florence L. &
Alice Foster came over after us —
After I had watered the trees
we set off to C's with them and
played Whist until 10 o'clk —
Mr. C. & Jno. C. Harriie ago. Fla.
tme. — To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 5, 1882

A very hot day with a droughty
dryness that is very trying. To
the house before breakfast for a
few minutes — there again at
about 9 — on making arrange-
ments to begin on main hall
stairway found that I had
made a fatal mistake in
my elaborate calculations
& plan drawings of day before
yesterday, so that I had to
go all over the work again.
I found that a beam was
6 in. out of place in my draw-
ing. Dinner as usual — in
the afternoon finished plans.
Bed Room floors progressing.
Tea at 6.30, after that again to
house — to P.O. about 8 — then
to Courtright's where I swung
in hammock until after 10 —
then to bed.
