## Left Page

### January, Friday 6, 1882

Slight fall of snow in the m'g, then warmer — a fog & at last, rains — Got to studio at 9.45 & to work on Icehouse finishing & toning — It is no longer the fresh work that I had hoped to make it, but a regular studio picture — Home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 — Little work as it was so dark — Rec'd Mr. P.C. Donnsbury's check for $500 — payment for "Adirondacks" — Home at 6 — all dark — in trouble with the gas — men at work — Tea at 6.30 in half light — light about 7.30 — Spent writing prefatory article for Etching Catalogue for & at Farrer's request — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 7, 1882

overcast & disagreeable until night, when it cleared brightly. Got to studio soon before 10 o'clk. & to work, first a little on "Icehouse" & then put it aside as finished — then on "Crisp Oct" — & then put that aside — then on "Dec Moonlight" — also finished — Home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.15 — then over to bank & deposited Donnsbury's check for $500 — then went into Putnam's store for a few minutes & saw Geo. Haven & Bishols — then to studio & spent the rest of daylight working over "old Niell" which I did not quite finish — About 6, went to Renner's shop — no one there, then to store with like success — then home to tea — Well; feeling out of sorts, I was on lounge until 9 o'clk — then read awhile & to bed at 11.20 — we had intended calling on Walter to-night.
