## Left Page

### January, Monday 16, 1882

*My Birthday -*

Overcast + slight spatter of rain between 1 + 2 o'clk. Got to studio at 9.45 + worked on Black Mt. Water Color until near 1 o'clk. Then home to dinner + back before 3 + again worked as long as I could see. Rec. a letter from Jas. Bourlet, London, saying that the missing case of Etchings had turned up there again, having been sent back to him. At 5. o'clk Annie came + we went to a Plumbers in 17th S. Then to a chair place in 14th S. and bought a fold-up chair — At 6 home — Vases Rec'd. from Mr. & Mrs. Louis C. Tiffany — wedding presents. At 8 went to Coleman's with A. — Rain falling in showers. I went up to my studio for an hour, then back to [illegible] — Quartette playing — home at 10.30 — To bed by 11.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 17, 1882

Rain, early, then snow + about noon clearing cold. Got to studio a little before 10 + by working closely got Black Mt. finished after a fashion. Home to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.45 + then put Blk. Mt. in frame + sent it over to Academy — At 5. went with Annie to Mrs. Danl. Huntington's Reception. Before 6 Ret'd. To studio + made a note — home to tea at 6.30 — At 8, Rode with A. down to an extra Rehearsal of her Singing club + then walked home — dressed myself + at 9.45 walked down for A. — met Compé there + we Rode to Mr. Jos. W. Drexel's 165 Madison Ave — Grand ball — music, supper + great display of dress — about 11.30 Rode home + to bed shortly after midnight.
