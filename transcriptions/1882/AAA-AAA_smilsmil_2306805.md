## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 2, 1882

Threats of rain all day — quite cool — built a fire in our new
Studio's fire place — the effect any
charming — Much time given
to starting attic & Fairs & to the
planning of our bed room floor —
Dinner soon after 1 — Drawing
details for Post casings etc in
lower hall way — Austin oiling
painting. a misgling rain set
in after 5 — promising something
wet, but nothing came of it, moon
& stars out by 10 o'clk. Went
to tea at 6 — again to house, to
P.O. after 8 — found letters from
Emma J. (in Bavaria) from Mary
J. enclosing two of Emma's French
letters, one from Emma's mother
fathers (one from Mr. Thruinger) — To
C's & read them — then our pap-
& to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 3, 1882

Overcast most of the day, but
warm & giving us little hope of
rain — much activity at the house
Cotrell & Herrick at work on attic
stairs, Burgess & Morris on basement
ones Lewis laying floor in our bath
room & Peters fell at work ceiling the
little earth closets — most of the
mf. I was at work on the problem
of staircase in main hall. Dinner
at 1.30 & again to the house — did
some tacking — also some oiling
& some drawing — To tea at 6.30
& again to house — prepared sying
for tomorrow — watered trees, then
trimmed one of the student lamps
lighted it & sat down to my plans
while Annie wrote to Fanny K. —
we worked & wrote until near
to three I cleaned up [illegible]
lantern & as we wished some
exercise we walked out on
the north road for about ½
or 20 m — then to C's & to bed
at 10.30
