## Left Page

### November, Monday 20, 1882

Delightful day — very clear and cool — Got to studio at 9.45 —
Some time spent in getting a press transfer of Rough Sport m[ountain?]
small, or catalogue plate — then to work at the etching + got it
almost all drawn in before I went to dinner at 12.45. Rosalie
Bruster called unexpectedly + dined with us — Back at 2.30 +
to work with Water Colors. The Misses Walworth called — went
to Waller's studio with them — also Mr. — Representing Van Antwerp +
Bragg, of Cin. O., for whom I promised to make an ill. view of Mt. Washington.
Left studio after 5 — went to Renner's, then up 5th Ave. to 51st S. for a walk —
then home — tea before 7 — eve'g Geo. + Nellie called. I did a
little work on Montrose plans — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 21, 1882

A very pleasant day — Got to studio at about 9.30 — Divided
my time between etching and a small W.C. sketch — At 12
went over to Bank — then down to Scoville Manuf. & Co. in Broome St.
to get large Rubber tray for acid — at a place nearby got asphalt. varnish
— home to dinner at 1.30 — found there Junie + aunt H. they made
a short call — back to studio. Miss Walworth called + I started her on
drawing from a bust for application to Student's Art League — At dusk put
for a walk — went to Wunderlich's + bought 3 Whistler Etchings — at 6.15 to
Fanny K.'s — Annie there — had tea — At 8.30 F. A. + I went to Mad. Sq.
theatre + saw "Young Mr. Wrunthrof" — a pretty theatre — play well set —
some good acting, especially Agnes Booth + Whiffin — Took F. home at
10.30 — to our own home soon after 11 + to bed at about 11.30.
