## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 27, 1882

Wind still persistently N.E. and sky not clear — ugly clouds threatening from the east during most of the m[orning]. Hung about, Read, & walked Piazza until near 11, then out with Traks to try to make a pen sketch but it was of no use — the wind blew so hard & cold that I had to give back. Dinner as usual — Weather much pleasanter — clearing but wind still blowing — Annie & I went out & had a little walk — then penned ourselves on the Piazza. W. Bassett & Son came & made quite a long call, making arrangements for us to visit them at Lynn — Eve[ning] as usual lying about & Reading newspapers — bright moonlight — to bed about 10 —

At noon, very high tide & fine surf that I went out to see.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 28, 1882

Another beastly windy day with lowering clouds & occasional spits of rain — Gel & family got off at 10 a.m. to stay with W. Bassett in Lynn over night — Before dinner A & I went out & saw some fine surf — Dinner as usual. About 4 I went out with sketch book & got in spite of high wind, a couple of hurried sketches — then up to Castle Rock & got some detail for finish of my pen sketch — very wild over the ocean & wind cold — Back at 5.30 — Dust[?] for Rain & at night very heavy wind & Rain — Chatting with W. & Mrs Rice of Sidonis & W. Lewis until near 10 — then to bed —
