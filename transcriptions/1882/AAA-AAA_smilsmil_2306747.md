## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 12. 1882.

Bright, but blustering & sharp —
got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on old Apple Orchard for Miss E.
Sartain & by noon had repainted
it & made the changes she desires
then wrote her a letter & took
to W.P. Office my sale of drawings
for Cottrell — Near 1.30 when I
got home to dinner — After that
went down town to A.B.N. Co —
then to Rumsey & Co., Pumps, and
bought pumps & [illegible] for Montrose
then to studio soon after 4 — A —
came in for a few minutes. I
packed Sartain sketch & took
it to Exp. Office — Home about 6 —
Tea at 6.45 — then dressed & at
8.30 A & I went to Mr. Gibbons' at
57 W 47 S. — a pleasant little
company — stayed until 11.45
then home & to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 13. 1882.

Windy & cold — got to studio
at 9.30 — got out material for view
of Portland. O. for A.B.N. Co — Began
to study it & found that I did
not have a very necessary letter
of detailed instructions — After hunting
fruitlessly I determined to go to
A.B.N. Co. — Delay there, but at
last the missing letter was found —
Then to International News Co. in
Beekman St — then home at 1 —
Dinner — back to studio at 2.30 —
Tried to work on drawing — made
no progress — finally, felt so ill
that at 5 I went home & lay on the
bed until 6 — Fanny & Valentine to
tea — Took A & F. to singing club —
then to Colman's — met Mrs. Gray
& Terrence there — at 9 took them
to their home — 28 S — then to studio
there until 10.15 — then went
for Annie — we walked up
home — got in soon after 11
& to bed soon after that.
