## Left Page

### September, Thursday 21. 1882

most of the night spent in a successful effort to wake early in
the mt'f. Up before 6 — thick and unpromising weather. Bkfs: at 6.30.
Very heavy Rain — no yacht trip of course. Wind soon got around to the
N.E. & the Equinoctial Storm set in.
a walk on piazza. then to work on drawings & plans for lower hall.
montrose. Before noon had a short walk with Annie — then a nap.
Dinner at 1 — Tremendous down pour — P.M. worked on drawings.
About 5, dressed in rubber suit & went out on the rocks & enjoyed storm & surf. Tea at 6 — Another stupid evef. feeling dyspeptic.
Annie & I had a couple of games at cards & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### September, Friday 22. 1882

mild, quiet, foggy mt'f. After
Up — wrote a letter to [illegible] &
sent to Wm H. Cooper & Co — Annie's
check for $500 — Mr. & Mrs. Davis went
into Marblehead at 11 o'clk. & took letters
to P.O. Then wrote to W.M. Post & to
work on montrose plans — about noon
went out & had a walk with A. in the
fog on the causeway beach — Dinner at 1.
After that again at plans when the
fog broke away & showed some fine
cloud effects — At 3 I sketched in
to an oil sketch from window — A.
went over to Marblehead & in a
few minutes Mary Ireland came —
She went right after her but failed to
overtake her — I finished sketch by
5 o'clk — then out for walk — met A —
Tea at 6 — Evef. cleaning brushes
& heavy Rain fell that continued —
cards with A — & to beds soon
after 10 — Rain still pouring down
