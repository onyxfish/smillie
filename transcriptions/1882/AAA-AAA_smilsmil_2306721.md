## Left Page

### February, Sunday 19, 1882

Sleety snow in the m't — steady rain all the afternoon — B&p: at 9.15 — at 1 went to ch. cor. 39th St. & Park Ave. & heard Rev. Mr. Peddie. Apologized for not preaching a regular sermon because he had been vaccinated & was used up — After service went to see Will — Home at 1.15 — dinner at 2 — read until 4 — then to studio — wrote a letter to Father & home at 6.30 Tea at 7 — Read until 9 — Took Father's letter to dep't at about 10 — home & to bid about 11.30.

Sent letter to Mr. Stetson — Keene flats — enclosing one dollar — Taxes on my four acres there.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 20, 1882

Pleasant day until after 3 o'clk, when the weather thickened — Rain about 8 p.m. Got to studio at 10 & to work trying to "prove" "Lady of Cairo" — Home to dinner at 1.15 after 3 when I got back as I had to go to Knodler's to get a muller. Tried 2 or 3 more p[roof]s but without success — all hard & heavy or dry & light — gave up in disgust at 4.30 — then to da Rochi's — got there at 5.30 — left at 6.10 — home to tea — Eve'g finished reading Amy as Sleigh & got to bed a little before 11.
