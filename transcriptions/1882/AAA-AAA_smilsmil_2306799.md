## Left Page

### July, Friday 21, 1882

Delicious day, very clear & cool
with beautiful cloud effects —
Ther. last night went down to 60° —
& to-day no higher than 78° at
the house until 10.30. Run down
Town & soon back — tacked up on
one of the walls some cotton duck
& wet it — To dinner at 1, back
at 2.30 — Clifford & Hubbard
finished wall at about 4. P.M.
Had a lively time dancing
about after all hands — The
work goes on — To tea soon after
6 — at 7.15 again to house —
after a short call on Florence
Lathrop — called at Hy Tyler's
but no one was at home —
the rest of the evef. Read news-
paper & to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 22, 1882

Still delightful weather — the
air is very exhilarating & the views
from piazzas at the house quite
enchanting — Made up a little
fire in our bed room. Fine place
& prepared some sizing with
which I covered a couple of pieces
of duck that I had tacked to the
wall — experimental — In the after-
noon, besides drawing some up-
stair & hall way designs, I oiled
a part of the Etching room —
Tea at 6.30 — again to house — en-
joyed sunset from Western balcony.
About 8 — to P.O. Band playing exe-
crably — To C's at about 9 — Read
newspaper & to bed soon after
10.
