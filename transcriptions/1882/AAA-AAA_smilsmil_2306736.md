## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 21. 1882.

A nasty wet day — Mother & Kate
got away for home at 4 P.m —
after intending to go at 10.30 A.M —
I got to N.A. a little before 10
to spend the m'f, making sketches.
Home to dinner at 1.30 — 2.45
when I got back to N.A. Then
worked for about an hour and
finished up. Then to studio —
then to Kroedler's — then to Mr.
J. Walker's — 18 E. 10th St — & delivered
pf. of "Up the Hill" + saw the lady
herself for a few minutes — got
home very tired & wet — soon
after 7 — & had tea — in the ev'g
rested while Annie read
aloud to me from Prescott's
Conquest of Mexico — To bed
about 10.30.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 22. 1882.

Blustering day — sun & wind & snow
flurry — got to studio about 9.30
on my way stopping to see Geo.
who has been used up with a
cold — Spent the m'f, writing &
preparing to make some drawings
for the A.B.N. Co. Home to dinner
at 1.15 — back at 3, having gone to
Bank to deposit Kurtz check —
worked on drawing of Elevator
Soon after 5 went up to la Roche's
& had him fuss about my mouth
— Home at 6.30. Tea soon after
— Ev'g, some general reading
then again Annie read aloud
from Prescott — To bed about
10.30.
