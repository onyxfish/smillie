## Left Page

### October, Saturday 7, 1882

*Montrose.*

Up soon after 6 — Raw & foggy —
bkfst at 7 — at 7.40 left the house
& by Chris. St. ferry took 8.30 train
for Montrose — Sun shone brightly
by 10 o'clk — after which the day
was hot & the ride dirty —
Enjoyed our home lunch at
1 — New chairs in Parlor car very
uncomfortable — got to M. Station
at 3.30 — buggy in waiting for
us & we had a charming drive
into town, going direct to the
house at 5 o'clk — Saw Cotrell,
took a walk through & had a
general view — then took horse
to stable & at 6, to Courtright,
where we had tea — Every
being tired and sleepy, we
went to bed at 9.30. Annie
has stood the journey re-
markably well, much to my
comfort.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 8, 1882

Charming day, but very warm.
Bfst at 8 — very soon after, went
to house & lodged there all
the m'f. Annie in studio — to
dinner at 1 — back again to
house — many visitors, so
that the whole P.m. was
taken up by them. Tea
at 6 — Every again, Annie
& I went to studio where
we wrote
