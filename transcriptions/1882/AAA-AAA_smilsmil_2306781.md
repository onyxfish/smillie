## Left Page

### June, Monday 19, 1882

*at Home.*

Up at 6.30 - very moist, muggy
+hot. B.l.b. at 7.15 + at 8 - a. +
I started for home - Pleasant
company with the Weirs + Mr.
Atwater - R.R. Ride much more
comfortable than on last. Less
heat, smoke + dirt - Into N.Y. late
at 10.30 - Went home - had a
little wash up + after!! Annie +
I went to 6th ave., marketed, then
to a store + looked at Refrigerators
then to Cheeny's + ordered a
Trunk + at 1 o'clk. home again.
Storm threatening - Sophie Ireland
at the house - near 2 when we had
dinner - gas lighted - heavy Thunder
Storm that continued with inter-
missions all the afternoon + wet —
at 3 went to Studio. Putting up
things. at 5 went to [illegible] - Walton
there until 6 - then again to Studio.
at 6.30 went to Georges - saw him +
Nellie - bid them goodbye - then
home to tea — Joey — Walton
Cook + Geo. Smillie called — To
bed about 11.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 20, 1882

Delicious day, bracing cool +
bright — About 9.30 went down town
\+ spent the m'f Running about — to
Mace's, Houston's; for Refrigerator, to Keogh's
Canal S. for Ash Doors - to Charls - not in —
to D.L.+W. R.R. Freight office - Exchange
R. + hired car - to A.B.N. Co. + got
$235.00 to Day S. for ash doors + home
to dinner at 1.30 — Soon after 3 went
to Tiffany's + spent rest of afternoon
there selecting glass — home soon
after 6 — Rested until 6.30 — Then
dressed + at 7 Annie + I went to
Walter's where we met Dr. +
Mrs. Yale + had a quiet little
dinner party. Home at 11
very tired + Right to bed.
