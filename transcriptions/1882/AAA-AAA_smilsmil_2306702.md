## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 10, 1882

Unsettled weather, striving to clear — some pleasant sunlight — Got to studio at 9.30 & slowly worked my self into an interest in my large unfinished W. C. of last year "Sunset on Black Mt., Lake George" — Home to dinner at 1.20 — & then a Rest, as my wretched stomach is still misbehaving. Back to studio about 3 and worked as long as I could see — home about 6 — tea as usual at 6.30 — every until 9 quietly on sofa — then tried some Reading. Did not get to bed until after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 11, 1882

Dismal mtf. Snow fell last night followed by rain — about noon it cleared & at night it was fresh & clear. Got to studio before 9.30 & to work on "Blk. Mt." Had a pleasant call from Mr. Earnest Longfellow & a friend — Home to dinner at 1.30 — near 3 when I got back — worked as long as I could see — Miss James & Mr. Harlan (?) a publisher called. Annie sent up word from Mrs. Colman's for me to stop there for her, which I did about 6 o'clk. Then home — tea at near 7 — I did not feel well enough to go to Dr. Yale's Reception — but took a walk to the Buckingham to see Will who is used up with inflammatory rheumatism in one of his legs — home at 9.30 — found that Ferguson had called at the house — To bed at 11.
