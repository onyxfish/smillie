## Left Page

### October, Friday 13, 1882

Overcast — some sunlight came out before noon, promising better weather, but the wind still held in the East. Clouded p.m. Rain & wind at night. Bkfs. at C's at 8 — then back to house — wrote a note to Farrer, also one to Geo. Lertuss came, made screen to go back of stove in etching Room & worked on furnace in studio cellar. Payne also at work — Dinner at 1. Again supervising work all around — then took a rest — At 5.30 went to P.O. — to Boyd's & to McWhirnie's to get him to dig for me. Then to butcher & bought meat for to-morrow, as we expect to begin to take our meals at home — To tea at C's at 6.30 — then home — passed the evening reading newspapers & listening to one of Cable's Old Creole Sketches read aloud by Annie — To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 14, 1882

Cloudy m'f. clearing to a glorious day — Bkfs. in our own house — the first meal eaten under our own vine & fig tree here — delightful — All the m'f. as usual. Running after the men. Herkness again at work on furnace — A Royal dinner at 1 o'clk. Clifford drilled holes in coping stone of stairway by well. Pettus came & promised to plow on Monday. etc — etc — I worked until I felt very tired — had a short rest before tea time — Tea at 6. After that, went down town & marketed — back before 8 feeling out of sorts — the rest of the ev'g on my bed — to bed for the night soon after 10.
