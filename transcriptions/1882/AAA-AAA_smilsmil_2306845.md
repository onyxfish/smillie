## Left Page

### October, Monday 23, 1882

Disagreeable day — Bkfs. at near 9 — the m'f almost wholly spent superintending grading operations on the north side of the house where the ground requires careful management to induce surface water to run in desirable directions. Down town before noon. Dinner at 1 — again spent afternoon with graders. At 5.30 took a rest. Tea after 6 — Before 8 went down to P.O. & market — back home & spent evening reading newspapers. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 24, 1882

Bleak & windy — sometimes bright & sunny — again dark with heavy clouds — cold & wintry. Bkfs. at 8.30 — all the m'f with graders. McWhinny dug holes for Spruce trees expected now daily — I was out of doors all day — Dinner soon after 1 — Directing generally — & fussing with tiles for bedroom fireplace — Tea at 6.30 — at 7.30 to P.O. then home — Dan Sayre & wife called for half an hour — after that — Annie read aloud to Fanny & Elizabeth — I read newspapers, etc.
