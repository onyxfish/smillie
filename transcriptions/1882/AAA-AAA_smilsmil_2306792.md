## Left Page

### July, Friday 7, 1882

Beautiful day — Up early &
to house before bkfs — Selected
some oak & had it sent to the
mill to be "watched" — then to
bkfs — After that again to house
& worked all the rest, most
of the time over glass, arranying
combinations of Tiffany glass in
the large Room windows, as I
find it best to change some-
what the plans formed in NY.
Dinner as usual — then a
rest — to house at 3 — another
load of goods — a little sun-
shower — To tea at 6 — very tired
Down to P.O. at 7 to see Hamlin
about building studio chimney —
Then up the hill & at 8 went in
Annie to Daniel's Hall — Montrose
Broom Dire for Episcopal Ch. After
it, auction of brooms & dust pans —
musicians from Scranton. Two ladies
cornet & violin. man and piano — out
at 10.15 — nearly when to bed.

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 8, 1882

Delicious mfg. — Shower between
3 & 4 P.M. — overcast after that.
After bkfs — went to house. There
an hour. Then down town &
got horse & buggy & with Annie
started out about 9.45 — Drove
to Colpe's new house & went all
over it — we like our own much
better — the view is too panoramic —
the atmosphere & effects were
heavenly — Back to dinner at 1.15
— then a nap — our last load of
furniture arrived abt. 1.5 — At
2.45 again went to the house
& there until 6 — much of time
spent over glass. Tea soon
after 6 — Then down town to P.O.
etc. — then up to house & sat
on piazza until after 8 — then
to Courmquis & read Baker until
10 — To bed at 10.30.
