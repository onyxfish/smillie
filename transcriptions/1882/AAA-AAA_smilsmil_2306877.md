## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 26, 1882

Rather mild & sometimes overcast —
a shower between 7 & 8 P. m. —
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on W. C. "Rough Sport" — I was out
of sorts & things did not go right —
Made up piece of fatp. paper for
Ehninger & put copy right on
small Rough Sport plate. Home
to dinner as usual. After
3 Champney & I went down
to Kimmel & Voight's — no printers
at work — back to studio. Home
at 6 — tea before 7. Katie & the
Adams came down from Richfield. I wrote letters to Condit,
Ehninger & Oscar Ireland & at
9.30 went to G. C. depot & mailed
them — home & to bed before 10.30.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 27, 1882

Pleasant day — To studio at 9.30
& to work on Rough Sport W. C.
Dinner as usual — to studio
as usual. Batterlee came with
2 plates for advice but as I was
hurried, asked him to come to-morrow
— at 4.30 went to Century,
where Haden com. had a meeting
& transacted much business piling
a lot of work upon me that terrifies
me — Home at 6.40 found Matt
& Kate there — Matt has come to the
city for treatment of a tumor in her
left breast. Tea at 7 — Evening
hanging pictures in parlor —
To bed about 10.30.
