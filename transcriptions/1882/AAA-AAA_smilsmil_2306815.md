## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 22, 1882

Very pleasant day with slight premonitions of rain — Hamlin came & fixed five places & hearth for tiles & finished up. I worked around with him. Dr. Mulford & Priscilla P. called — Dinner as usual. Afternoon chasing around, giving Backus lessons etc — etc — I did some more sizing which kept me until 6.30 — then to tea — after that watered trees — then to P.O. & to Boyd's — back to C's and spent what remained of the evef. in reading papers — All out to Miss Alice Foster's party — we rec- the cut direct — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 23, 1882

Overcast & moist — a little rain fell at noon. I spent the morning giving to N.E. bedroom a second coat of sizing — Dinner at 1 — very heavy rain in the afternoon — continued my fussing over drawings — to C's at about 5.45 pretty well tired to dress — at 6.30 — between two very heavy showers a & I went to Mrs Jessup's to tea — Dau. Sayre & wife — Comstock & wife. Priscilla P. — Mrs. Jaquelin — Dr. M. & the young folks made a party of over 20 at table — a very good supper — at 8 went to P.O. — back to J's — chatting until after 10.30 — then to C's & to bed at 11.
