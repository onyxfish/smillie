## Left Page

### March, Thursday 9. 1882.

Merched, stormy day, opening
with sleet & winding up with fog
& rain — Bkfs. at 8.15 & then up to
daRoche's where I spent an hour &
a half — under his tinkering — then
home again very much used up
with heavy cold — had a good time
while Annie read aloud to me
Prescott's Conquest of Mexico — Dinner
at 1.30 — Kindled fire in parlor
& worked there much of the p.m.
on W. Elevation of Montrose house —
Annie went to Fanny K's to tea —
I not able to go — I spent the evg.
on Elevation — Geo. C. went to
Singing Club for Annie — I
lost W. Robt. Gordon's Reckm. by
staying at home. To bed at
11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 10. 1882.

Overcast & breezy most of the
day finally clearing before night.
Got to studio after 10 & spent
what remained of the mfg.
busily engaged writing letters
mainly about Montrose & money
matters — After 12 went to Bank
& made deposits on my own and
Annie's accts — then home to lunch
& at 2 we were at Academy [Music]
Phil. Rehearsal — out at 4 — went
to Park & Tilford's & brought some cigars
home about 5 — at 6.30 Chas. Ames
of Boston, Chas. Collins, Jas. Nicole, jul.
Valentine, Misses Abbatt, Canning —
Emma & Mary Ireland came & we
had a pleasant little dinner
party — They left at about 11 —
Nicole & I took Misses Abbatt and
Canning home — I got home and
to bed about midnight.
