## Left Page

### September, Sunday 17, 1882

Wind less violent, growing quieter as the day wore on until eve'g which was delicious. B[illegible] near 9 — About 11 Annie & I started off & sauntered about until after 1, especially delighted in a cranberry bog. Dinner at 1.30. After dinner a nap — about 5 Annie, Geo. & I walked over to the P.O. and then to Castle Rock & around by the coast — lovely sunset — wonderfully quiet sea, with rose & opal colored reflections. In to tea soon after 6. Every sitting about & chatting until 10 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 18, 1882

A very pleasant day, altho' still windy & hazy — About 9.30 I started for the far end of the neck where I worked until after 11 on [illegible] sketch of the rock study (Castle Rock) that I commenced on Sat. Then I took water colors & finished the study of Sat. Then to dinner — A. had spent the mtf in Marblehead — After dinner, at about 3, took oils colors & went to study east of the house & worked as hard as I could until after 5, when the sun went off my subject & I gave up, very much dissatisfied with my work. Took a short walk with A. — tea at 6 — After that, again walking with A. by moonlight — To bed as usual at 10.
