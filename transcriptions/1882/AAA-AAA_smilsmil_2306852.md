## Left Page

### November, Monday 6, 1882

Beautiful day — got to studio at 9.30 — Soon went down town to Stavin's, foot of Cortlandt St. to learn why my Montrose boy has not yet been delivered. Found that it was owing to stupid misspelling of name by clerk — It came late in the afternoon. Home to dinner about — half an hour too early. Had quite a nap after dinner — then to Tiffam 334 4th ave to see De Forrest's wood carvings, etc brought by him from India. was not much impressed. Home to tea at 6. Evelf. Ed[?], Ma[?], Walter, Marie, Annie & I went to Union R. Theatre, joined there by Collins, Julie Spring, Valentine & Mary J. & Geo C. & Emma J. All sat together. Wyndham & Co. from Criterion Theatre, London. Played "Ruth's Romance" & "14 days" — Laughable but neither very funny or very witty to my mind. English was execrably spoken — much could not be understood. Out at 11 — and I right home & to bed before midnight.

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 7, 1882

Overcast. Spitting rain about 6 P.m & no more. Got to studio about 10 — Spent the m[?] putting away the contents of box from Montrose & in writing copy of letter to Johst to send to Mr. Drexel. Home at — Dinner after 1.30 — After that, Read awhile & got to studio at 3.30 — Charl came in with Miss After 5 I started for home & walked up 5th ave. to 57th St — there back at 6.20 — Tea before 7 — Evelf. passed in Reading. To bed soon after 10.
