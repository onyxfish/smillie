## Left Page

### April, Monday 10, 1882

Snow in the early mtg. — & now
in flurries during the day — a
raw & disagreeable one — Got
to studio at 9.30 & worked on Mon-
trose window drawing. To bank
at 1, then home to dinner —
After that, to Newman's, about
locks — then to Knoedler's. Met
E. M. Ward & Witt there — long
talk — got to studio at 4 —
all sorts of work until 6 — a
call from Maitland Armstrong —
Home to tea at 7 — After that
wrote awhile — at 9 Annie &
I went to Ned's — W. & Mrs. Sprague
just rid? from Eurakee — There
until 10 — then home — To bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 11, 1882

Cold, blustering, marching — got
to studio at 9.30 & spent the mtg
working over drawings for window
sashes for Montrose — Home to lunch
at 1 — Solos — Annie & Mother took
lunching with the Spragues —
Back to studio & worked until
near 7 getting my drawings done
& put into ready to 9.45 to M. —
Then home to tea, very tired.
After tea, in front of a wood fire
sat in an easy chair & listened
to Annie reading Prescott's Mexico
until 10 o'clk — To bed at 10.30.
