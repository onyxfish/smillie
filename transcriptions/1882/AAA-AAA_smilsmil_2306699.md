## Left Page

### January, Monday 2. 1882.

Clear & very cold — Got to
studio at 10 o'clk — & started
drawing 10½ × 22 — old Ice
house near Saratoga —
worked faithfully until
2 — then home to dinner —
The matter of New Year calling
going on so slowly that at 3
I went back to studio and
worked as long as I could see —
then to Mrs. Saml. Coleman's
— she did not receive calls
— then to Fanny K's — there
for a short time — then home
— Tea about 7 — Everg. by
a cheerful wood fire — a
very few friends came in —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 3. 1882.

Still cold, but not so cold as
yesterday — To studio at 9 45
& went on with old Icehouse —
stone to dinner at 1.15 — back
at 2 30 & worked until near
5 — then to Reeve's shop &
from there walked up to
Central Park — Home at
6.15 — & soon after had tea —
Everg. with Annie writing
out a new book in which
to keep accts — To bed at
11 —
