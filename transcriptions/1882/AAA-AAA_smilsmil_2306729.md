## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 7. 1882.

Lovely day — got to studio at
10 — Worked awhile on Brandt's
catalogue sketch & delivered
it to Kurtz — Then some letter
writing to at 1 o'clk — home to
dinner — Had some Montros-
work to do at home & at about
4.45 went up to Wm H. Vanderbilt
grand House opening art Resp[onse] —
Went all over the house, full
of evidences of boundless wealth
& very limited taste — band &
collections — no end of notables —
splendid pictures — poor gallery —
great confusion in getting coats
& hats — I added to my cold &
went home at near 7 feeling
very uncomfortable — had tea
& during the eve'g coddled
myself over a warm fire
& went to bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 8. 1882.

Very charming day — got to studio
after 10 — Annie walking down with
me — wrote letters & packed etching
sp. etc — & home to lunch at 1 —
Brought drawing materials with
me, so did not return to studio —
very much out of sorts from effects
of a heavy cold — At 3 went up to
Dr. La Roche's — not in, but made
an appointment for to-morrow
m'g. Home at 4 — Before 6 Annie
& I began to dress & at 6.45 we were
at Mr. Chas. Isham's — 5th Ave. where
we had a swell dinner — Mr. & Mrs.
Eastman Johnson — Mr. & Mrs. J. Hob.
Smiths — Judge Horace Russell &
Miss Ward of Boston — a very jolly
company — Ladies did not leave
the table until after 10 — in half
an hour the gentlemen followed.
We soon bid good night, home
at 11 & right to bed.
