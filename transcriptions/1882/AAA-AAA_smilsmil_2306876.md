## Left Page

### December, Sunday 24, 1882

fine day — Annie & I went to
Dr Hastings old ch. in 42d S. & about 9 — then I got to work on
heard Dr. Paxton — a sermon
on the chief element in Xmas
can't say that I was favorabl
impressed — he seemed to me
to lack the fine sympathy de
manded by the subject.
After service we walked down
to studio where I got W. J.
Richard's w.c. drawing that I
had mounted for framing.
Dinner about 2. then a nap
then reading — then to Geo's
for a little while — again to
studio for screw eyes — Home
to tea at 7 — Wert wrote a
Xmas letter home — took it
to G.C. letter box — home & to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 25, 1882

A pleasant Christmas. [illegible].
about 9 — then I got to work on
plans for main hall at Montrose
& worked faithfully all the mf.
in spite of Santa Claus (Geo. Cook
in costume) & the children, who
had possession of the Room from
11.30 to 12.30. Dinner at 1 — at
2.30 went to studio — laid ground
on [illegible] sport plate — etched [illegible].
right inscription & varnished it
ready for acid — Had an attack of
indigestion — very uncomfortable —
at 5 went to Century & wrote a
little while — then home, dressed
& at 7 A & I went to Ed W. Cook's to
a family dinner. Mother C. — Geo. —
& Walter & wife. — E.C. nicely
settled in his new house.
Home at about 11 & right to bed
