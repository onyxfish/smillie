## Left Page

### September, Sunday 3, 1882

Muggy & overcast, with heavy showers between 4 & 5 p.m. — B&p. about 8 — After that, to house to keep the m[illegible] there — Dinner at 1 — a short nap, then to studio — a number of little things fixed up preparatory to going away — Expected callers but the shower prevented — wrote letter to father at Saratoga — walked awhile on piazza with a — To tea at 6.30 — After that, we went to P.O. again to studio & until 10 o'clk — packing sketching traps — then to C's & to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 4, 1882

Delicious day — up at 6 — trunk packed before b&p. — About 9, went over to house & gave last directions, then down town & paid all bills, etc — back to C's at 1 — just in time to labor with driver & trunks — then the team came & at 1.20 we got away — Taking Mrs. C. & baby & two Emmons in back seat — charming drive — got to station at New Milford before 1 — train due at 1.36 — got peaches & ate our lunch — H&f Jessup — G[illegible] Comstock & Turnell on same train — ride to N.Y. not intolerable — ate our 2nd lunch at 6.30 — Reached Hoboken about 8 — then home, Comstock & Jessup riding up from with us — leaving us at our door — found G[illegible] Cosher, Valentine & Mother cook awaiting us — Chatting until near [illegible] — our trunks came after we had gone to bed —
