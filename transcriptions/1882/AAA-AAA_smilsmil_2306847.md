## Left Page

### October, Friday 27, 1882

Rather a dismal day — overcast & raw. Late Bkfs. — after that got out Cotton Duck & spent nearly all the mft. in getting the large piece on Stairway wall from bay window up. Dinner at one — after that made up size-color & put it upon the duck stretched in the mft. About dusk Jo Williams came in — from 5.30 I took a rest — tea at 6 — Rain commenced falling. I did not go down town but spent the evt at home "playing" poker with the girls — after 10 when we got to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 28, 1882

Charming day with dubious close — wind shifting to E. N.E. & clouds banking up grey & cold. Fanny decided not to go home to day, but to wait until Monday & then go down with us. All the mft. spent working over wall covering on Stairway & directing Cottrell & Herrick in putting up & sizing cotton duck in lower N.E. Room — Dinner soon after 1 — the rest of P.M. flipping about in general preparation for our departure. Tea at 6.30 then down town until after 9 o'clk — going about & settling up accts. Picturesque scene — acrobat — tight rope from front of Mr. Webb's end fast in tree — to top of Tarbell's hotel — bespangled tights — torches — lights at each end of balance pole — crowd — court house looming up in background. Home — a game of cards & to bed at 10.30.
