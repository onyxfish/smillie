## Left Page

### December, Monday 18, 1882

Overcast wtf. threatening snow but later cleared charmingly. Wrote several notes after bkfs. & got to studio at 10 — Finished Lake Geo. drawing — To bank & deposited Haden Com. money. Home at 1 — dinner — back at 2.45 — mounted drawings — at 4.30 went to Thos. Moran's — then to Chickering Hall & hired it for the Haden lectures, fel 5t & 8d. Then to Nicoll's studio — Etching Club com. m Haden Rector. Farrer. Chase. Nicoll & Moran there. — At 6 went to Avery about an etching after Jacque that he wants me to make. — Then to [illegible] on Haden business — then to Century & at 6.45 home to tea — After that wrote notes to Yale, Hoe, Betts and Gilder — posted them at 9.10 — then to Am Art gallery. 23d. St. Revival meeting of N.Y. Art Club — there until 10.30 — then home & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 19, 1882

Bright day — After bkfs. wrote a short letter to Koehler — Got to studio at 10 & commenced work on a frame of dog sketches for Oscar Ireland — a X'mas present to his wife — Home to dinner at 1.20 — back, Annie walking down with me. at 2.45 & worked as long as I could see — Home at 6 — then over to Grady's & to the shop. Home to tea at 7 — ever wrote a number of letters — Ogilvie sent me a wedding present to-day — a Serpentine Vase, cut in Florence by one of Hiram Power's workman. To bed at 11.
