## Left Page

### July, Monday 31, 1882

Muggy with a S.E. wind
all day, at times looking very
much like rain. Went down
town about 10.30 & did a number
of errands. Went to Lathrop's
mill about a lot of oak boards
finding that we are short.
To dinner at 1 — The afternoon
was spent in making drawings
for the finishing of lower hall.
Tea soon after 6 — again to house
to C.O. about 8 — then to C's when
after reading the paper we
got to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 1, 1882

Overcast with wind from the east
threatening rain but none came
until night when a little fell.
I was not able to get up until
near noon owing to a headache.
then went to the house & looked
about there until dinner at 1.
Feel better after a light meal —
went back to the house & helped
Hamlin take down his scaffold
on studio chimney — am pleased
with its effect — a large part
of the first flight of stairs put in
place — that too pleases me — to
tea soon after 6 — did not go
down town — Evening spent with
paper — to bed at 10.
