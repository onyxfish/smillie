## Left Page

### January, Monday 30, 1882

Brilliant day — Got to studio at 9.30 & worked on "Evening Shadow" glazing & bringing out sunken in parts with Siccatif — Home to dinner at 1 — Fanny K. at the house but would not stay to dinner — About 2.30 Annie & I went to Union League Club. Ladies Recep. to see the house & the pictures — At 3.40 I walked down to studio — meeting of Etchers in Bellows' room to agree upon plan of sending to Lond. Exhib. Painter-Etchers Soct'y. At near 5 went to Colman's & met a — after looking at Dinner sets then went to Tiffanys — did not quite decide — Home to tea at 6.30 — at 8 we went to Colman's — music — Mr Havemeyer, Miss Elder & Mr. Mr. Roper there — Playing until after 10 — Home at 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 31, 1882

Very heavy snow storm — all day & night — Did not get to studio until 10 — did not feel like work so, spent the m'f putting some of my Etching & water color possessions in order — Dinner at 1.30 — Studio at 3 — Young Platt there with port folio of etchings — he is going to send some to London. The afternoon followed the m'f. — Home at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Well lounging — with headache — To bed soon after 11.
