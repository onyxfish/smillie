## Left Page

### May, Tuesday 30. 1882.

Brilliant day again. After
help — walked over to 5th Ave, all
excitement over the expected
procession — I became interested
too & finding a good place at
cor. of 42d S. & 5th Ave, I reviewed
most of the display — then to Mad.
Sq. — saw Arthur — then to studio
before 11 & worked on Sugar plan-
tation — Home to dinner at 1.30 —
Fanny K. with us — back to studio
at 3 & finished drawing about
5.30 then out & sauntered in
Mad. Sq. for some time — Home to
tea at 7 — Evg., Mr. Knudsen
called — Later Mr. Chas. Collins —
all out soon after 10 — To bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### May, Wednesday 31. 1882.

Very pleasant day — got to studio
at 9.30 & worked on Montrose plans
all day — Nicoll called in the
m'g, & Miss Abbatt — they leave
town for the summer to-morrow.
N. to Shrub Oak, Miss A. to her home.
To dinner at 1.20 after sending
to Will Sugar Plantation drawing.
Back to studio at 3 & worked
until 6 — Walked over to 6th Ave
& paid Cherry for covering my
umbrella & home to tea at 7 —
Evg., Ge 2 came in for awhile
— chatting in parlor until 10 —
To bed soon after 11.
