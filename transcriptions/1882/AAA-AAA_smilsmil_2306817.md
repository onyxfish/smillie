## Left Page

### August, Saturday 26, 1882

Pleasant overcast day, growing more threatening as the day advanced. Spent the day in going over the house, seeing after blinds etc — etc — Dinner as usual — after it, feeling tired took a nap. At 3 o'clk. went to Bank & got new check book — back to studio — morris getting up casing about post in hall & Herrick & Lewis fitting window blinds — Began oiling oak floor in our bed room — To tea at 6.30 — then to P.O — back & had a couple of games of whist — Florence, & [illegible] C. & [illegible] Aunnie & me. To bed at 10.30 or there abouts — Strong east wind & some spitting rain.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 27, 1882

Heavy Rain nearly all day — Ther. 60°. Bkft. about 9 — near 10 when I went to house — Kindled fire in studio — very comfortable & pleasant — Dinner at 2 — back soon after 3 — Had an hours work in digging a trench to carry off overflow from N.E. cistern which was running over into the cellar — At 6.30, a break away & Rainbow then settled down again, but no more rain — At 7 had a cup of tea — At 8 — again to studio — wrote a letter to father care Dr. Strong — Remedial Mt. Saratoga Springs. After 10 I went to P.O. with our letters — then to C's & to bed.
