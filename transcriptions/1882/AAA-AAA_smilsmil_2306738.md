## Left Page

### March, Saturday 25. 1882.

Chilly, blustering & dirty — After
bkfst. Annie went with me to N.A.
Private View Day — The little painting
by Poore that I wanted to buy was
sold to J. Clark, who stole a march
on me. Got to studio at 1 & worked
until 1 on piazza details for Montrose
then home to dinner — then to Bank
& back to studio a few minutes after
3 — worked on drawings until
6 — then home — Tea at 7 — at 8.
A. & I went to Steinway Hall. to
Concert of N.Y. Chorus Socy. Part of
which I enjoyed exceedingly —
met many friends there, among
others, Mr & Mrs. Coleman and Belle
& Mary Tweedy — After the concert
we walked up to N.A. — there
for half an hour — The crowd
had gone, so that it was pleasant
we chatted with some friends —
looked at some pictures, listened
to some of the music — got home
at 11.20 & to bed before midnight.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 26. 1882.

Still quite cold, windy & dirty —
Up late — bkfst. near 10 — then reading
newspapers for awhile — to studio
at 11.30 — there for an hour — then
walked up to the Buckingham &
saw Will & Charl — then home at
2.45 — dinner soon after — then a
nap in my chair before the fire —
about 4.30 I made two or three
of the Golden Wedding cards that I
am preparing for autographs —
At near 6 went out & walked up
to 50th St. & 5th Ave — then home &
had tea about 7.30 — A. & I alone —
Evg. again worked on cards —
at 10.30 went to depot with letter
to Kate from Annie — wrote awhile
after my return & to bed at 11.30
