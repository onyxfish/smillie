## Left Page

### October, Sunday 29, 1882

A novemberish sort of day. wind + occasional dingles
of rain in the m'f. Clouds broke away about 2 + during
P.M. some beautiful effects of sunlight — Up late. Help:
after 9 — much of m'f. Spent packing box in studio with
Rugs + studio belongings —
Dinner after 2 — Hazing around during P.M. doing
all sorts of things. Wrote to Father + Parrish — Tea soon
after 7 — After 8. W. Park and Kramer called — left at 10 —
clear, mild moonlight. I
fussed about my trunk
until 11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 30, 1882

*Home in New York.*

Delicious day — clear + very mild.
B'kf't at 8 — After that much work
in getting the last things done
preparatory to leaving — Finished
packing trunk — Put away many
things, helped Connie — Gave Cotrell
last instructions + at 10.30 Rode
down town with Pettus + got through
with last messages there — Then
back — about 11.30. Pettus got off
for New Milford with Maggie, the
Cook, Young Goodwin, who is to drive
back our team, + five trunks — 15 m.
Later we followed — overtook and
passed P. + stage before we got to
Hart Lake — Glorious ride — Got to
N.M. a few min. after 1 — train on time
at 1.34 — Wm Cooper in same car — Had
our lunch — car overheated — other-
wise the ride was pleasant — View
from Pocono Mt. particularly fine —
Tea lunch at 6.30 — Got to foot of
Chris. S. at 8 — left 7. + E. at 6th ave + [illegible]
S — we got home at 8.40 + to bed about
10.30. The day in N.Y. mild, muggy
+ rather wet.
