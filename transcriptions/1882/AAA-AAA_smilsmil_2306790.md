## Left Page

### July, Monday 3, 1882

Very cool m'f. overcast most of
the day — threatening rain which
came between 7 & 8 o'clk. Bkf
at 7.45 — soon after that went over to
building — most of the m'f. spent
with Peter & Battles boy clearing out
the lumber that is "stuck up" in
the studio & putting holes where
that we may have the studio to
live in. Dinner at 1 — after
that the p.m. was spent at
work getting the glass into the
sashes, much of it was too long,
had to send to Boyd's & Cooley
came up & spent nearly 2 hours
cutting it — Tea at 6.30 — then
again to house & washed glass
as long as I could see — then
downtown to P.O. &c. gentle
rain — back & read paper
until after 9 — then some writing.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 4, 1882

Cold & Rainy — Bkf. before 8 —
after that went over to the house
& worked all the m'f. with Austin
putting glass into the sashes. To
dinner at 1 — & after that had
a long nap. About 5 o'clk
Annie & I went to the house
& for an hour & a half we worked
together washing the glass that
is to be put into the sashes.
Nearly 7 when we had our
tea. Eve'g. still feeling very
tired, I lounged up on the sofa
until near 11 — then to bed
— rain still falling heavily.
