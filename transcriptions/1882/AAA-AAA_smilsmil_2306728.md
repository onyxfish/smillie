## Left Page

### March, Sunday 5, 1882

Pleasant morf, gradually passing to overcast — cloud &, about dusk, to Rain. Wf, Annie &
I went to Dr. Hastings & heard the new pastor, Rev. Dr. Paxton of Washington, preach his first sermon as pastor succeeding Dr. H. Much crude strength but do not think I could learn to like him. After service we walked up to 55th S — thence Annie rode home — I went to LaRoche's to see him about a dental break down — Nearly 2.30 when I got home to dinner — Afternoon Mr. Downs, her Son, Lib Howse, Walter wife & [illegible] called. At 6 I made letter to father — tea at near 7 after tea took letter up to deposit — we had planned to call on the Ehningers this evef, but both feeling out of sorts we gave it up & spent the evef quietly reading — To bed before 11.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 6, 1882

Miserable dirty rainy day — got to studio before 10 — got started on Cat Ben sketch after Borrandt's Academy picture — Farrer came in & we had a long conference about Water Color Matters — Home to dinner soon after 1 — back at 3 & finished the sketch — home at 6.30 & had tea — & at 8 Annie & I walked down to Colman's where we had the usual right-hand piano playing Miss Elder & Mr. Hauemeyer there — Near 11 when we got home — Right to bed.
