## Left Page

### September. Saturday 9. 1882.

A blessed rain fell while we were at sleep — between 8
& 9 o'clk. The m'f, being overcast, was spent indoors, doing
nothing in particular — Dinner
at 1 — After that, Annie & I went
out for a tramp — on our return
found Geo. & family awaiting us
all looking remarkably well.
Before tea time we looked
over some of the summer work
— Tea at 6 — After having had
another tramp, Geo. & Nellie
going with us — After tea
we tramped piazza until
dark — then in parlor chatting
with Mr. Chapin — To bed
soon after 10 —

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 10. 1882.

Pleasant day — Some walking about
in the m'f. At 12.30 took horse and
buggy & with Annie rode down to
Beach Bluff where we had
dinner at Will Cook's by invitation.
After dinner, on piazza until about
4 — then had horse up & we drove
to Marblehead & beyond to Crown-
inshield Point, to Mrs. Dexter's
where Mary Ireland is — but she
was not in — The afternoon quite
breezy & cool — Got back to Atlantic
cottage at 6, having left horse at
stable & walked over — Then tea —
Even'g, as usual, yawning and
wondering why time passed so
slowly — managed to sit up until
about 10 — Then to bed.
