## Left Page

### September, Friday 15, 1882

Very high wind all day with charming cloud effects — Got up with a stupid head ache which bothered me all day. Annie went to Beach Bluff again at 10 & Returned just in time for dinner at 1 — I spent the m'f. making pencil studies or sketches, having to tie everything to keep it from blowing away — After dinner, out again, but between headache & high wind, got but one sketch. Before 5, Annie & I went to P.O. & came back by the Eastern side of Neck. — Superb view off over the ocean — quiet evening — To bed about 10.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 16, 1882

An exasperating day. Brilliantly clear, but the wind blowing a gale. Went out with pen & ink to make some sketches near the house, but found the light was not what I wanted on those particular subjects. So, I took W.C. traps & Annie & David to Castle Rock, where I pitched in & worked until nearly getting a study nearly all in — then to dinner — after that again took pen & ink & went to subjects selected in the m'f. — worked awhile & was then driven off by the wind — Returned to the house in a state of nervous exasperation which was somewhat quieted after lying upon my bed for an hour — between 5 & 6 again went out for a saunter, then to tea — Eve'g reading of papers & just before going to bed at 10, Annie & I had a game of High-Low-Jack. Wind blew nearly all night.
