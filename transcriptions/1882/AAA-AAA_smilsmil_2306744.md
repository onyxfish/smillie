## Left Page

### April, Thursday 6, 1882

overcast day — Got up with a
headache — ate a little before —
then sent a note (Annie wrote it)
down to studio to tell Bridges, a
model) that I could not get
there. I spent the m'f. rolled
in a blanket, in a big chair —
a cup of tea & a pale ale o'clk
— feeling better at 4 — About
5 went to studio — Cyrus Davis
& H.G. Hewes of Portland there.
At 6.45 I got to the Kimball's.
Julie Spring — Emma & Grace
there — At 8 took Annie, Fanny,
Emma & Grace to Singing Club —
then I went on to Century
where I spent the rest of
the evening. At 10.20 went
for A — walked home & to bed
before 12.

## Right Page

### April, Friday 7, 1882

overcast m'f. — after clearing
later in the day — Near 10.30 when
I got to studio, having been about
with Annie — Spent the rest
of the m'f. writing letters, the
one to Cottrell taking most time.
Home to dinner at 1.15. Near
3 when I got back to studio.
The afternoon spent in trying
to make a large drawing of
sash for dining room windows
at Montrose — Had a number
of callers, among them R. H. Stodd
— Home to tea at 6.45 — at
8.30 Annie & I went to Ned's
— there for an hour — then home
read a couple of chapters from
Prescott's Mexico & to bed about
11.
