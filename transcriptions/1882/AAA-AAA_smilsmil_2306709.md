## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 24. 1882.

Brilliant, cold day — Up late —
bed being the most comfortable
place such weather. Got to studio
soon after 10. & to work repainting
Cedar Meadows. Home to dinner
at 1.15 — back, after going to the
Bank — at 3. & finished the re-
painting — think it much im-
proved — At 5, went to Coleman's
to look at some dinner sets —
then up to Pennsi's to quarrel
him about a bill just sent in
— home to tea at 6.30 — Snug
by a big wood fire, Annie
Reading, three a game or two
of cards — about 10, walked to
the cor. of 5th ave & 53d St. then
home & to bed a few minutes
after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 25. 1882.

Bright but soon overcast — snow by
noon — Up late — did not get to studio
until after 10 — then to work repainting
"Evening Shadows" — 33 × 44 — taking
much of the black out of it & trans-
ferring the central dark from the
large cedar on the left to the groups
near the centre — Home to dinner
at 1 — back at 2.30 & worked as
long as I could see — then a
rest on the lounge for half an
hour — then home at 6 — tea at
6.30 — Eve'g. writing letters.
To bed shortly after 11.
