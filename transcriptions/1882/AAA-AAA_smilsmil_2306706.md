## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 18, 1882

A raw, nasty day — trying all day to snow & at night given over to sleet. Got to studio about 10 — all the morning fussing with etching proofs to send to [illegible] at the N.A. book. Two there at 1 o'clk. Home to dinner at 1.30 — after 3 when again I got back to studio — fixed up two more etchings — & at 5.30 took them to N.A. Mr. & Mrs. Morse & Miss Morse made a short call — at 6. went to Chickering Hall, met Annie — dress rehearsal of club — home at 6.30 — tea — a little time before the fire, then to Dr. Yale & Hills. to make wedding calls. Exceedingly nasty night — very pleasant call — Home at 10 — to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 19, 1882

Pleasant overhead, very bad underfoot. Got to studio at 9.45 — most of the mng. mounting some Peto [illegible] Plp. at Dickson's request to send to him. Farner called & I gave my etching paper to him. Annie came at 12.30 & we went to 2nd Nat'l Bank where she opened an acct. I left Dr. Hamilton's check on a London Bank for collection. Home to dinner at 1.30 — back to studio after 3 — had trouble in packing Plp. to Exp. to Boston, but took them to Adams Exp. soon after 5 — then home very tired & headachy & too late to go to the Misses Furness' Recptn. — Dressed. Tea at 6.45 — at 7.30 to Chickering Hall — Club (choral) concert — after much tribulation a very pleasant & successful concert — home at 10.30 & to bed about 11.
