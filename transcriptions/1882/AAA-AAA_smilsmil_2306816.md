## Left Page

### August, Thursday 24, 1882

Overcast mng. clearing into a
charming day — To the house
as usual, after bkft. & after my
look at work in progress, I
settled to some detail drawings
for stair case. Annie set
herself to work clearing up the
studio, intending to have some
company this eveg. Dinner
at 1 — again to studio — finished
drawings & helped Annie —
Tea soon after 6 — about 7 I went
down town — called at Mulford's
& Park's — the P.'s could not come —
met de Schweinitz — then to Boyd's —
to studio before 8 — glorious moon-
light — Studio piazza looked very pretty
with Chinese lanterns, chairs & rugs —
Mr. Mulford, Mr. Gagnebien, Courtright &
wife, Priscilla Powe & Mr. Simmons
came & we played whist & ate
peaches until 10.45 — Annie & I
went home with Priscella —
then to C's & to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 25, 1882

Very pleasant day — got up with
a mean headache which worked
off during the day. To the house
after bkft. Mng. spent unpacking
files & following up carpenters —
Dinner as usual — afternoon
made drawing of vestibule doors
— blinds and for 11 windows —
Tea at 6.45 — Mr. Reed, Misses
Hyde & Gilbert called at studio
— at 7 went to P.O. & to Mulford's
taking Mr. Gagnebien's lace bark
& St. Ml's stick. Then back
for Annie — we went to studio
— Read a letter from Emma J.
just rec'd. & wrote awhile —
back to C's & to bed about 10.30
