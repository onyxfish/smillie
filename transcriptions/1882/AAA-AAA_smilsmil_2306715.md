## Left Page

### February, Sunday 5, 1882

Brilliant & beautiful winter day.
Bkfst. after 9. Read papers & toasted
toes until 11 — then prepared to
accept Haven Putnam's invitation
to dinner — Put on big boots & smoked
glasses & at 11.45 a. & I started —
by Elevated R.R. at 42d S; to 6th ave. we
went to 125 S. & 8th ave. & thence by
cross town cars to 129th S. & Boulevard —
& from there waded through deep snow
to the Cor. of 140th S. where Haven lives.
We enjoyed the walk exceedingly
& were warmly welcomed. About
2 we had dinner — quite a Putnam
gathering during the afternoon. Mr. Dr.
Jacobi & Ruth P. — Irving & his wife &
Bishop & his fiancée, Miss Faulkner.
About 5. Haven's wife walked with us
to the top of the hill, 145 St, they ret'd, we
went on to R.R. Station & home at 6.30
Tea at 7 — Avelfa Reading & writing
my regular home letters. Took
it to depot box soon after 10, then
home & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 6, 1882

Very pleasant day — got to studio
about 10 & spent the mfg. fixing up
etching pls. To leave with Mr. Gale,
salesman at etching club exhibition.
Home to dinner soon after 1 — back
to studio about 3 — after looking over
new china dinner set sent home
from Tiffany's. Then to N.A. with
etching pls. After 4 went to Dr.
LaRoche's — there until near 6 —
then home — tea — & soon after 8
A. & I went to Coleman's — Emma
& Mary J. & Fanny N. there — played
quartettes until after 10 — then
home & to bed before 11.
