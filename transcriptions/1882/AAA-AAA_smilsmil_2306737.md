## Left Page

### March, Thursday 23, 1882

Charming day — got to studio at 9.45 & to work on Elevator drawing for A.B.N. Co. — Stuck to it until near 1 — then home to dinner — back at 2.45 & worked until near 6 — Home to tea at 6.30 — Fanny K. there — at 7.30 took them to Singing class, then I went to Studio & worked on cards for the Golden Wedding next week until 10.15 — there went for Annie — home at 10.45 — to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 24, 1882

Cold & blustering — partially overcast — Got to studio at 9.45 and finished elevator drawing, then before 11 went to N.A. Varnishing Day — There until 1 — then to studio — Home to dinner at 1.30 — back to studio at 3 — the p.m. almost entirely given over to callers, among others, Miss Sartain, — Charl & Bernard of Japan — Home at 6.30 — Will K — Lib Howe, Mr. Downs & her son Jack & Jno. Valentine to tea — after that Will, Lib, Geo., V. Annie & I went to the Circus & there met Collins, Julie Spring and Mary J. Reserved seats and got our money's worth — out at 10 — home & to bed before 11.
