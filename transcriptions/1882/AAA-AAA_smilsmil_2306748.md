## Left Page

### April, Friday 14, 1882

Pleasant day — got to studio at
9.30 + finding it impossible
to finish Portland O. by Saturday
night. I gave myself up to
Cotrell letter + accts. + to writing
out accts. of etchers who sent
work to London last Feby a
year ago. Home to dinner at 1.
At 2, with Annie, at the last
Phil. Rehearsal of the season.
Back to studio at 4.30 + wrote
until 6 — then home — tea + t
soon after 8 got to Nicoll's studio,
51 W. 10th St. Annual meeting of
the Etching Club — interesting +
full — carried a constitutional
amendment requiring members
to etch + exhibit every year —
Walked up town with Chord,
Colman + geo. Home at 11.20
to bed before 12.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 15, 1882

Charming day — got to studio at
9.30 — met Maitland Armstrong + went
with him in to see Tiffany glass for
Montroe Ho. Then to studio + to work
repainting "Old Oaken Bucket" ill.
for Crowell of Boston — At 12.30 went
to Bank — then to D.L+W. R.R. — then
to Newman's, about locks — got home
to dinner at 1.30 — 2.45 when I
got back to studio — worked until
6. then finished O.O.B. Illustration
then home. Tea at 6.30 — then
to last Phil. concert of the season with
a — very much enjoyed — home be-
fore 11 + right to bed.
