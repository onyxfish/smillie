## Left Page

### December, Saturday 2, 1882

Mild, pleasant day. Streets full of dirty water. To studio at 9.30 & after fussing about for some time got settled to work upon Reduction of Rough'd part for large Etching. Home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.45 & worked on Reduction with pencil, changing & correcting from the painting — after I lost light, I rested for awhile — home at 6. Tea at 7 — Grace D. & Elig. Home there — at 8 took Elig. to 106 Mad. Ave & then on with Grace & Annie to 11 E. 14th S. Singing Club meeting & then I went to Century. The Haden Com. Yale, Hor, Betts, Gilder Moran & self. Had a meeting & business was advanced. About 11. I left & walked up home & to bed at midnight — to sleep after 1.

Mr. Bickmore was black balled at the club.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 3, 1882

Very clear & cold — Up late. Help after 9 o'clk — Sat in front of fire until 11.30 reading, then out with Annie & walked up to 5th Ave. & Central Park at 72d St. then across the Park & out at 7th Ave. & 59th St. walked down through 58th St. & looked at some vacant lots — Annie got into a 6th Ave. Car & I walked home arriving at 1.45 — Dinner soon after 2 — a short walk in front of fire, then Charl came with some of his photos taken while at [illegible] — Walter's wife & children also came. Tea at 7 — wrote letters to Father & Ehninger — took these to G.C. depot & at 8.30 went with A. to Aunt Anna's in 17th St. there until 10 — then home & to bed.
