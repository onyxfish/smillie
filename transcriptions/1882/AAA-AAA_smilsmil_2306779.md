## Left Page

### June, Thursday 15, 1882

Another fine day — clear & warm
thunder shower, noisy & wet, bet.
8 & 9 P.M. Got to Tiffany's at 9.30 &
spent half an hour there — then
to studio & worked on Portland, O.
all the m'f. Van Cleef made a
long call. Home to dinner abt. 5 —
back at 3 — just as I was going
into J's, Kate Riley & cousin caught
& detained me for a while, then
in to J's & worried with glass
until 5.30 — then to Studio &
after a rest wrote notes to Soho &
& Miss Buckland — Home to tea
at 7.15 — Walter Cook came
in after 8 — caught by shower —
Very close & warm after. N —
Sat about until 10.30 — then
to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 16, 1882

Steaming hot — got to studio
at 9.30 — wrote my letter to Cotrill
& worked on Portland, O. until
1 o'clk — then home to dinner
Mrs. Brown there — dinner at
1.40 — About 3 got to Tiffany's
there until 6 o'clk — most of
the time a cutter with me.
Got along slowly, but could
not find what I looked for
carefully & with perspiration.
Home at 6.30 — Tea soon after
7 — Every wrote a note to
Mr. Leonard Scott in answer
to his enquiries about Montrose
for a summer boarding place
— and a note to Jno. Courtright.
To bed soon after —
Dr. Adams at the house.
