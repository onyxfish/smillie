## Left Page

### September, Friday 29, 1882

at Mr. Bassett's —
Baltimore S. Lynn.

The eighth day of the storm and about the worst of the lot, howling wind & flying rain — At help. &c? a letter from Mr. Coleman saying that they were expecting Louis Tiffany & wife to spend Sunday, so we cannot go. After help? I sat by fire & finished pen sketch of Castle Rock & Tower. Dinner at 1 — Soon after 3 we got through our good byes & took "Barge" to Devereux & then by 3.32 train to Lynn where Willie Bassett met us at depot & drove us to his home where we found Geo. & family — The rest of P.M. in chatting — dinner at 6 — Every in general chatting — Mr. & Mrs. Ahlborn (Mr. B's sister) called — near 11 when we got to bed —

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 30, 1882

Still dismal weather — a slight shower in the m'g. Geo. & family got off for Cohassett early in the m'g — about 11 Annie & I went into Boston & got out to Will C's in Cambridge about 12.30 — He met us in horse car. Sue is very well — Annie saw her — I saw the boy — dinner at 1.30 — took 2.15 horse car & caught 3.15 train to Lynn — Mr. & Mrs. B. met us & took us on a long drive — back to dinner at 6 — Ev'g. some music and whist — to bed at 10.30
