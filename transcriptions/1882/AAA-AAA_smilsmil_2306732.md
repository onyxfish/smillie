## Left Page

### March, Monday 13, 1882

Cold & windy — late when I
got to studio — worked on M.
drawings for piazza until
near 1 o'clk. Then home to
dinner — Gilbert Paul & J.
G. Brown called. — Back
to studio at 2.30. again at
work on drawings — also
made a letter to Catskill.
Father came in about 4.30 —
Mother & Kate having come
to town with him — We went
home at 6.30 & had tea.
Gee came — about 9 I went
to 33 W 33d. & called upon
Mrs. Towne of Boston who is
suffering from threatened blind-
ness. Saw her daughter &
had a pleasant call until
10 — thins home & to bed
about 11.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 14, 1882

Cold, clear, blustering day — Bkft. at
8.15 — near 10 when I got to studio
to work on Montrose piazza drawings.
F. H. Smith came in & spent some
time discussing Water Color Society
matters & the fight that is to come
off at the Annual meeting to-morrow
night — Home to dinner at 1. After
it, went up to 57 Th. & called on
Federicks. Perkins, Bruce Crane
& Bolton Jones on W. C. matters —
then to Swain Gifford's — not in, then
at 4.30 — back to studio — T. W. Wood.
Farrer, S. Colman. Baldwin, Bellows,
Tom. Moran & Falconer came. &
discussed W.C. matters until
6.30. Home at 7. Matt. had arr'd.
from Po'k. — Will there for a short
time. Tea about 7.30 — Father
Matt & Kate went to George's —
I lounged & did nothing.
