## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 8, 1882

Overcast day — much cooler,
with some very heavy showers.
most of the mf. with Burgess
on Stair platform — also with
the others who were at work
on the bed room floors. Dinner
at 1 — got my printing press
into my etching room. The
heaviest shower fell about 4.
Elisha Mulford called in
the mf. Tea at 6.30 — bid
the Cortis bros. good bye, as
they leave by the early train
Tomorrow mf. Mr. Emmons
of Princeton, joined our family
last night after we had gone
to bed. To studios after tea —
lighted up & wrote to Newman
about Sash Rollers & butts.
With Annie to P.O. at 8.30 — more
letters from Emma — then to
C's & read until 10 o'clk. then
to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 9, 1882

Delightfully cool day with cloud
effects — the Cortis bros. left at 6.30
this mf. The mf. spent in the
usual work — part of the time la-
boring with Burgess & platform
& then making more sizing and
putting it up on the deck in S.W.
Room. Dinner at 1 — after that
had a nap & went to the house
at 3 — finished sizing part of
wall — at 4 Annie came for
me with horse & buggy & then
we rode until 6.45 — met
the stage, Maggie, the serving
girl was in it, from 38 [illegible] this
mf. After tea we went to the
house taking Maggie with us to
show the place to her. About
6 started for P.O. & had to return
on acct. of slight shower — over
in about 15 m., then on again —
to C's before 9 — Sat & read awhile
& to bed soon after 10.
