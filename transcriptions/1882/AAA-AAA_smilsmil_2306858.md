## Left Page

### November, Saturday 18, 1882

Clear & cold - ice & snow, the remains of last night's storm, lying for a short time in shaded places. After bkfst. Annie & I went to Newmarie - then to Mad. Sq. theatre & bought three seats for next Tues. evef. A. went on down town & I went to studio - for some time I worked over drawings mounted yesterday, when A. Rei[illegible] & other [illegible] guest, at 12.30 I went to an auction at Leavitt's (12th St. & B'dway) for a bureau & bedstead - did not get it. - Home to dinner at 1.30 - back at 3.15 and worked on our Printing Press getting it ready for a campaign. - Home before 6. - dressed - at 7.15 we went to Walters' - 6th anniversary of his wedding - 14 there at table - W. & wife, 2d[illegible] & wife - self & wife - Mother Cook, Aunt Anna - Mrs. Sprague - Emma [illegible] Geo C., Jul. Valentine, Ernest Conright & Mr. Willard. We sat down at 7.40 & were three hours at table. - Home soon after 11 - then we sat down & had two games of whist - Geo. & Annie opp. Valentine & self - To bed at 12.45.

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 19, 1882

Clear, cold wintry day. Up at 8.30 feeling none the worse for last night's intemperance. - Bkfst. at 9.30 - Pleasant fire in parlor - sat over it, Reading, until about 1 o'clk. when A. & I went out for a walk. - Up 5th Ave. to Park & back - in at 2.10 & had dinner. After that a lazy time in easy chair. - Walther, wife & children came at dusk. - I took a short walk after 6 - tea before 7 - Evef. wrote letter to father. - Took it to Grand Central letter box before 10 & then walked up to 5th Ave. & 51st street - then home & to bed at 10.30.
