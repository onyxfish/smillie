## Left Page

### May, Thursday 4, 1882.

Overcast & growing rapidly milder. Several spatters of Rain towards night. Got to studio in the m'g. Soon after 9.30 — found several notes to answer — then Nicole came in & Miss Abbatt, & Farrer — all interested in last night's performance. & altho' I started to leave at 2.15 it was about 1.30 when I got home to lunch — After that walked to 24th St. & 5th Ave. with a — then to Bank & soon after 3 back to studio — then revising some writing — at about 5. I went to Dempsey & Carroll's & was there until near 6 — then walked up home — dinner at 6.45. Mr & Mrs. Colman, Edith & Earnest Wright there. Eve'g in parlor — Avery Brown came in — all left before 10 — and we got to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 5, 1882.

Sun & shadow with an unpleasant chill in the air — feeling out of sorts — have caught cold. the m'g spent at studio trying to get up a drawing for A. B. N. Co — from Cuban photos — but made no progress — Home to lunch as usual — back as usual — afternoon, worse than usual — for no work whatever was done, much to my disgust — Before 5 o'clk. went to Putrians with M.S. & enquired about printing — then up to LaRoche's — not at home — then dragged down home, feeling quite fagged out — After tea, wrote a letter to Colville — went out & posted it & then to Urlle's — 163 W. 34th St. met Geo. Hearn & wife there. Stayed until 10 — then home — to bed about 11.
