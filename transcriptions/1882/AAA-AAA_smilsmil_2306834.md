## Left Page

### October, Sunday 1, 1882

Delicious day - very mild and clear. Bkfst before 9 — about 11
Mr. + Mrs. B. Annie + I Rode out to
Reservoir hill + had a glorious
view over all the country, seeing
Lynn, Nahant + the ocean to
perfection. Stopped + drew a
little of the inside of the Mudge
church — then to the Yacht club,
Mr. B. + a Rel? home — Mr. B. + I went
with Willie on his boat "Ruth" and
down to Point of Pines + back — then
to dinner at 2 — at 3. Mr. + Mrs. B.
a + I. drove over Nahant — the
afternoon was superb + full of
delicious effects — climbed over
the Rocks — back at 6.30  Old Mr.
Buffum came in at about 8 —
then we had some tea — After
9 I wrote a letter home.
To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 2, 1882

*at Sam. Colman's —
Redwood Cottage. Newport.*

Lovely day — Bkfst before 8 — Took
10.5 Train in to Boston — with Trunks
drove over to Old Colony R.R. depot,
stopping on the way at Fall River Stu
b? office + buying tickets to N.Y. for W?
eve ?. Took 11.40 Train to Newport —
Mr. Nevins went back way with us.
Got to N. at 2.30 — met Louis Tiffany
\+ wife at depot, on their way to Boston
— they directed us to go at once to
Colman's, which we did, taking
their hack. Very cordially Rec'd.
a cup of tea — we had lunch in the
cars — at 3.30 C's outfit at the door,
he on horseback — I driving the
chariot with Mr. C. + Annie — We
had a long + delightful drive,
getting in after sundown. Tea
about 6.30 — Spent. chatting
until 10 — then to bed.
