## Left Page

### December, Friday 22, 1882

Horrible dark rainy day. Bkfp. late — after it — packed
things & took box with Xmas
presents for Poughkeepsie to the
Am. Exps. Co. at Grand Central
Depot — then across to Brady &
McKeeve's & arranged to have
them send at once for Oscar's
picture & ship it to him. Ner
11 when at last I got to studio.
Mrs. Walworth, of Saratoga called.
I did little & home to dinner at
1 — back at 2.45. Did some
letter writing — some mounting
& ticking up. To Century at
5 — Haden lecture com. Yale
Gilder & Moran came. Session
until 6.30 then walked home &
had tea at 7.15. Evef, Annie,
her mother, Mr. C. & I sat down to
Whist — Jamie Wright soon came
in & stayed until 10.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 23, 1882

Clear mtf. but pleasant with
did it last long — gusty, Raw
overcast weather following — got
to studio about 10 — not in working
humor — got out "Rough Sketch" &
completed pencil outline —
Dinner as usual — Afternoon
got my color boxes in order to
begin W. C. campaign — did
little else — Home to tea at
6.30 — Wef at home, lounging
— reaction after a drive of
work — after 11 when I got to
bed.
