## Left Page

### June, Thursday 1, 1882

Rain during the night and heavy showers during the
mʳˢ. After bkfs. went hunting
for some sort of light Railing or
guard to put in the bay window
went to several places without
finding what I wanted & got
only what I did not want — a
wetting. Got to studio before
11 & worked until 1 on Montrose
plans, then home to dinner.
Back at 3 & worked closely
until 6.40 — then home & had
tea about 7 — Ev. f. Will, Joe,
Minn, Walter & Charl came
& stayed until about 9.45 —
after that I read awhile
& got to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Friday 2, 1882

Bright, beautiful day — After
bkfs. went to depot for Jackson's
Evts. then to studio at 9.45 — wrote
to Cottrell, then worked on Montrose
plans — Home to dinner soon
after 1 — Back at 2.45 — met Miss
Hallowell & had a short call — then
worked on plans until after 5,
then Annie came & by Elevated
RR. we went to 155ᵗʰˢᵗ. & walked
to High Bridge & back to depot —
lovely evʳ., altho' wind still from
S of E — with signs of bad promise.
Home at 7.50 & had tea. Mother
& Kate went home on the 4 o'clk
train. Ev. f. did not feel
well — lounged about until
10 — then prepared for bed &
got into it by about 10.30
