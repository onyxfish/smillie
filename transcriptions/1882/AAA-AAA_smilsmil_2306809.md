## Left Page

### August, Thursday 10, 1882

Pleasant cool day — a driving shower at about 4 P.m. when the mercury fell to 60°. Got up with a headache which made me feel out of sorts all day — To the house, as usual, soon after bkfst — young Quinions of Princeton accompanying us — I finished up the [illegible] [illegible] W. Room & Mr. Lewis commenced putting in the blk. ash finish — Dinner at 1 — a short rest, then again to house, where time was spent directing the construction of platform & making drawings of newels — Tea at 6.15 — again to house — Kindled fire & lighted studio lamp. Then Annie & I walked on the piazza around the house 32 times, making one mile — then in, a wrote & I worked on my drawings. Soon after 10 I lighted lantern, then we trudged over to C's & to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 11, 1882

Pleasant cool day — Still signs of the storm in the sky — very breezy — much time spent looking after the construction of the platform on stairway in main hall — Started Backers sand papering floor in Cherry Room — & Mr. Wilson at work out of doors, clearing the ground near the house — Dinner as usual — Afternoon made a new set of measurements for window blinds — found that Lewis had made a bad blunder in his measurements of finish that he is putting up in the W. Room, but very mulishly, he refuses to confess — I was put very much out of humor. Tea at 6.30 — two Mr. Courtrights & two children arrived — After tea Annie & I went for a short time to studio's, then to P.O. then back to C's — some reading & to bed soon after 10.
