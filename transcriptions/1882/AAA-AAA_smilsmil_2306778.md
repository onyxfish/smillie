## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 13. 1882.

Another very lovely day. At
9.30 went in to Tiffany's — there
until 10.15 — there to studio and
until near 1 worked on Portland
O — then home to dinner. Mary
(Ned) Cook there — Back to studio
at 3 & went at once into T's &
spent the rest of P.M. there with
man cutting glass. At 6 went
to Johsts — 47. 3d S. — not in —
then to Talley's cut 3d S. Found him
& talked of linen & cotton duck
for walls in Montrose — Home to
tea at 7.15 — after 8, Ned, Mary,
Annie & I went to Walter's —
there until near 10 — then
home & to bed soon after.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 14. 1882.

A very pleasant day, growing to
be quite cool in the eveng. To studio
at 9.30 — a few minutes looking after
glass, then to work on Portland O —
Home to dinner abt. 1.15 — to Tiffany's
at about 3 — almost distracted
trying to get harmonious glass &
at 4.30 gave it up for the day, quite
discouraged, without having cut
a piece — To studio for a few min-
utes — then about 5 started for St.
La Roch's — there until after 6 —
We had ready a new lower piece
for me that promises to be satisfactory.
To 3d ave. & rode down to 3d S. & called
on Johst & had a pleasant promise
from him that he would if possible get
my coveted seat (244) Phil — for me next
season — Home to tea at 7.30 — Earnest
Wright called. At I, at 8.30 called on
the Morses, not in — then on Fred J. &
wife — met Miss Brackett there — home
soon after ten & to bed soon after
11.
