## Left Page

### April, Sunday 2, 1882

Very warm April day — heavy showers between 10 & 11 a.m. Cloud & sunshine alternating all day. Up at 8.30 — Mrs: an hour later — About 11.30 sauntered down to N.A. of D. There for an hour — then to studio for a few minutes & home at 1.45 — dinner after 2 — then a writing nap in big chair. At near 6 o'ck. Annie & I went to Walter's where a. made a short call on Marie who is ill — Home after a saunter & had tea at 7 — Evening divided between reading & writing — at 10.30 took a letter to depot for father — home & to bed after 11.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 3, 1882

Not so warm, but springy. Got to studio near 10 o'ck — finished up the old orchard sketch and packed it to S&p. to Miss Sartain — then home to dinner at 1.15 — near 3 when I got back & spent rest of P.m. charcoaling and planning an illustration for the old oaken bucket — went to S&p. Office at 6.30 — then to George's & P.O. station and home to tea at 6.45 — Spent the evening worrying over Paimer–Etcher accounts, trying to straighten them out — Got to bed at 11.30 with my head buzzing & had a wretched night.
