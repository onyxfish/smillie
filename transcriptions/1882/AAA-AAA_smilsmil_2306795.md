## Left Page

### July, Thursday 13, 1882

Charming day, after a heavy rain
during the night — found a leak
under China closet extension; it
led to investigation & the discovery
that that extension had settled and
carried the piazza down with it,
that water would not run off —
Set right to work & had it jacked
up & heavy timbers put under it.
Dinner at 1 — the afternoon spent
with Hamlin on the chimneys &
with seeking for fire brick &c.
Tea at 6 — then to studio & wrote
a letter to Flint & Co. about nursing
wash stand — After 8 when Annie
& I went down town, to P.O. to
Boyd's &c — Back to C's at about
9.20 — Read paper & to bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 14, 1882

Another delightful day with
heavy showers north & south of us
after 4 P.M. — a slight wetting
about 5 — the m[illegible], busily eng-
in directing the laying of floors
in dressing room & bed room hall.
Down town to see Mr. Remyon
about fire brick & Nuelford about
sheeting — Dinner at 1 — a short
rest then again to studio & made
drawing for studio chimney —
Busy until 6.20, then to tea.
Even, after going to P.O, Annie
& I called upon the Chandlers
— then to Park's — not in —
then to de Schwintz's &
found her mother Mrs. Lord
& sister Lucretia — A pleasant
evening until near 10, then
to our quarters & to bed.
