## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 12, 1882

fine clear day — to studio at 9.30 + to work. Painting on view on Lake Geo for Mr. Frd. Condit of Providence — This is the sketch I painted one Rainy day as I sat at Addie Dunning Hawes's bed-room window + that Mr. Condit saw + liked so much, that she ordered it for a Christmas present for her husband — I have not been able to make up my mind to touch it until now. I got along fairly well, but the keynote is cold + low — Home to dinner as usual — After it, to bout with chick from A.B.N.Co. #65 — then to studio + worked as long as I could see — Young Dent called to talk about starting a new art paper — I went to Dewey + Keating's with my new coat then home to tea — after that to Grady + McK's — + fussed over the ordering of frames until 9.30 — then home — R.C. + Annie at work pasting on Hospital motto signs — I made paste — To bed at 11.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 13, 1882

Dismal, dark day with heavy rain — To studio at 9.30 — Too dark to see to paint so I put aside my Condit picture and commenced a W.C. 10×14" view on Lake George. "Tongue Mt. from Harbor Islands" — Home to dinner as usual — back + worked until near 4 — about 5 I went to Wile's in 34th St — not yet home but saw for Minium — Home about 6.30 — Matt + Kate to tea — Charl to come, but detained by death of Winter's eldest boy — he came about 9 — they left about 10.30 — To bed at [illegible].
