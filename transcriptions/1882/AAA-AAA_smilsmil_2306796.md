## Left Page

### July, Saturday 15, 1882

Delightful day — after blf —
went down town to Mr. Rumy's
about fire bricks for fire places.
Very busy between Hamlin
& Studio's chimney, Cotrell
& kitchen stairs & Herrick
& floor in bedroom hallway.
Dinner soon after 1 — The
afternoon as usual spent
about the house — Tea at
6. Jno. Courtright's father
& another man at the house.
At studio's until dusk —
Then to P.O. & back to our
quarters before 9 — Read
paper — To bed after 10.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 16, 1882

Pleasant breezy day, quite warm
at noon, nearly 9 when we had
btfp. Then to studio where Annie
& I spent the aft, Reading &
writing letters — Dinner about
1.30 — again to studio. Annie
read to me Jas. Freeman Clark's
chapter "Mystics of all ages", but
altho' the subject interested
me I must confess that I fell
asleep while she read. Tea
at 6 — again to studio & wrote
to father — then on piazzas until
quite dusk. Then to P.O. with
our letters. Back to Courtright's
about 9 & after a little reading
got to bed at 10.30.
