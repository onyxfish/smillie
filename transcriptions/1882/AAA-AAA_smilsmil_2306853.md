## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 8, 1882

Mf. cloudy & eke rain —
To studio about 10, after having
called at Newman's about
hardware — The rest of the
mf spent writing — Home
to dinner at 1.30 — after 3
when I got to studio —
got out photos & material
for sketch for A.B.N. Co —
but made little progress
owing to callers — among
others Miss Bullard & Mr.
Morse. Again to Newman's
Home to tea after 6. Eve
Annie & I went to 17th St. to
see the Ireland's — Julie
now at home on a visit.
Miss Clinch & her friend
Mr. Paul there — Curious an
of Aunt Anna's wedding —
Home about 10.30 & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 9, 1882

Very mild, pleasant day — Bkfo
late. Rec'd a note from 3d hist.
telling me that he had regained
my rehearsal seat — wrote an
answer, then to Newman's shop
in 29th St. about hardware for
Montrose, ordered Oct 6". To
studio at 11. Made pencil
sketch of Western subject for A.
B.N. Co. Home to dinner at 1.30
— again to studio at 3 — made
reduction of "Rough Sport" for
small etching — at 5 went to
Century — at 5.45 to Schist's —
at 6.15 home to tea — Fanny K. —
there — took her & a. to Singing
Club, 11 E. 14th — thence to Union L.
Club. Exhibit of Etchings by the
N.Y. Etching Club — a good show
— at 10.15 again at 11 E. 14th &
at 11 home & to bed.
