## Left Page

### October, Thursday 5, 1882

at home —

After a tolerable night of rest awoke bet. 6 & 7 — got to landing
at 8 & by E.R.R. & 42d St. Cab — home at 8.30 — found the family at [illegible] —
Mother Cook — Geo., Ned, Valentine &
Miss Fouché. At 11 I went to studio
— saw Geo. who ret'd from Providence
last night — before 12 went to Newman's
& ordered some builder's hardware
then home & had a rest. dinner
at 1.30. At 3 went down to 57
Bdway & spent rest of P.M. with
Walter Cook looking over plans of
houses — Rose up to Houston St. with
him — then to Adolph Johst's — 47 3d
St. & saw him about Phil. seats —
then home & had tea about 7 —
Evg., dawdling about —
To bed soon after 10. [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### October, Friday 6, 1882

Rain in the morning after 6 &c.
Packed trunk. — then down-town to
a B.N. Co. & saw Will — got $35 oo
for Manhattan Storage Warehouse
drawing — called to see Charl. — not
in — home to dinner at 1.20 — after
that, a rest; then to Newman's to
see about window blind fastenings.
Then walked about to see new
buildings at Bdway. 38th & 39th St.
then home to tea at 6.30. Wolf
Walter & wife & bro. Geo. Thellie
called. — To bed at 10.30.
