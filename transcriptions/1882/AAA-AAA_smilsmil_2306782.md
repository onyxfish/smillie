## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 21, 1882

Another charming day, a little warmer than yesterday. About 10 when I went down town — first to Charl's — then to Beaver St. to look after Chinese fans lanterns etc — didn't find them. To Waters; for tea — to A.B.N. Co. — to 53 Bdway — to Pfeiffer's — not in — again to A.B.N. Co. & saw Conkling about frames — to Dey St. about doors — also about Earth Closets — almost 2 o'clk when I got home to dinner. Soon after 3, to Tiffany's — again until 6, with glass — then to Studio. Annie gave Calverly a sitting — Sent telegrams to Ehningen in answer to one from him. Home to tea at 6.45 — Every until 8.30, Resting — then wrote notes to Colman & Boyd — also made tables of glass measurements. To bed as usual, about 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 22, 1882

Up early — bkft. at 7.15 — then to G.C. Dépôt with Annie & her Mother & started them for a day at Sing Sing — then back to house & before 9 went down town — until 1.30 going all over, from Canal St. to Bowling Green & from Hudson to Pearl, Shopping for glass — Tiles, Duck, Burlaps, Doors, Earth Closets etc, etc — At 1.30 had dinner at Cable's — then in to A.B.N. Co. & Rested for a while — then up to Studio & to Tiffany's where I completed my glass selections at about 6 — then went to barber's & had hair cut, & head shampoo'd — Home soon after 7 — At 8 to depot — train in at 8.10 — Home with A & her Mother. Tea at 8.30 — The Rest of the evening Resting & to bed at 10.30.
