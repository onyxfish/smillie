## Left Page

### October, Monday 9, 1882

Delightful weather — cooler than yesterday — Showers about us but the threatenings were not minent — Spent the m'g. giving instructions to carpenters about closets & little things required at once — then downtown on various errands — went for Frank Newton to get him to help move furniture — He came just as I was going to dinner — I hurried my dinner & then helped him until 4 o'clk, when he left — I continued carrying crockery & a thousand & one other things to as many places until 6 — Maggie the cook expected by ev'g stage, but she did not come — Tea at C's — Ev'g being tired we went to bed at 9.30

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 10, 1882

*in our own house —*
*Montrose.*

Charming day, cool & breezy — Hard at work in house preparing to get in — a woman doing scrubbing & cleaning — Carpenters at work upon closets & Payne clearing stone preparatory to ploughing & grading — Dinner at Courtney's — The afternoon like the morning until I lay down upon my bed to rest — then a superb sunset & at 6 o'clk the stage came with Maggie — After that eve went to C's to tea — C's wife went out to a tea party at Dud. Lathrop's — A. & I alone — after tea we [illegible] to our house & after toasting our shins in front of a pleasant wood fire we went to bed at about 10 o'clk.
