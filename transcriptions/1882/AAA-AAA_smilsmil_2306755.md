## Left Page

### April, Friday 28, 1882

A day of sunshine & sprinkling. Got to studio at 9.30 — At work drawing plans for Water pipes for Montrose House — Farrer came in with printed copies of call for Special W.C. meeting, embodying Wood & Smith letters — About 11 went down town to Rumsey Pump Co. & ordered pipe & fittings — then to A.B.N. Co. — 1.30 when I got home to dinner. Having a number of little things to attend to it was near 5 before I got to studio. Mr. Hitchcock of Tribune called. I wrote a long letter to Cottrel — After 7 before I got home to tea. Even some writing. Dr. & Nelie started for home at 8 this mg. Worked over some rough designs for interior finish of the Montrose House & to bed ab 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 29, 1882

Charming day, at times partially overcast. After 4½ pt. went to Dr. Com Thomson's — then to Bates (druggist) then to Reimer & Co. & ordered small frame for Stoddard sketch — then to Mutual Dist. Tel. 5th Ave. 37 S. then to studio, soon after 10 — the rest of the mf was spent in drawing plans for cistern pipes and drains to be sent to Cotrell — Home to dinner at 1.20 — near 3 when I got back to studio — wrote letters and soon after 4 Annie came after me — we went to the Lincrusta Co — to Dampsy & Carrols & to Flint's furniture store & in to Macy's to look at Crockey. Home at 6.30 & soon had tea. Eve — Annie read awhile aloud — I again worked on sketches — got to bed before 11.
