## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 22, 1882

Charming day — after bkfo. went to G.C. Depot & bought tickets for Po't —
then to Studio & to work biting etching for cat. of "Rough Sport" — home
to dinner soon after 1 — back at 2.30 — Miss Walworth came
again & worked upon her drawing — I worked on biting
& cleaned my plates by twilight — then out for a walk — went down
to Wünderleich's & spent some time looking over etchings —
Home about 6.30 & had tea — for some time Reading — then Cha[rles]
Collins came & spent eve[ning] until 10.30 — We got to b[ed]
about 11.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 23, 1882

*Po'keepsie.*

Delicious Indian summer day —
Bkfo. after 8 — at 10.30 Annie & I took train to Po'k. arriving without
incident at the Terrace a little before 1. It was near 2 when we sat down
to dinner, fewer than expected. Will for, Walter & Minn are in Washington —
Al's wife & baby detained by a nurse strike at home. After dinner father
Annie & I went to the new house in Garfield Place & went through it,
then walked to top of Smott's hill in Academy St. — home about sundown
twilight with children, telling stories, then wrote letter to Cottrell & at 7 went to
P.D. with it — Uncle W's family came in from Hill Side & about 8 we had
supper — then a family visit in parlor & about 11. To bed.
