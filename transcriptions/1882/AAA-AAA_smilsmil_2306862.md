## Left Page

### November, Sunday 26, 1882

Early mf. bright, then overcast & at noon, snow, that free all the afternoon, a wet, sloppy mess. Up late — bkfs. a little before 10. About 11 went to Charl's & saw him & some fts. of the [illegible] work he did at Po'keepsie — then out up to Central Park — walking in the snow — home at 1.10 — dinner at 2 — the rest of P.M. lounging in front of fine reading — Walter's family came in — Tea before 7. Evef wrote to Father, Ihringer, S. John Harker & Farren. At 11 oc went out & mailed them — To bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 27, 1882

Dark mf. with a little more snow. Before noon it cleared. To studio at 9.30 — all the mf laying grounds & varnishing plates. To dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 & continued Rebiting on Cat. plate of Rough Spot — cleaned place at dusk. At 5.30 out for a walk — went up to Central Park & 5th Ave — in at 6.30 — Tea at 7 — Evef putting curtains up & writing a note to Seymour Hadsen — To bed before 11.
