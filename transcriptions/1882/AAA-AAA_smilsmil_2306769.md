## Left Page

### May, Friday 26. 1882.

*On Steamboat Bristol — Stateroom 141.*

Lovely mft. Before bkfst. walked to the Sq. to Dawini's Exp. office — then back & had bkfst. at 8 — Exp. man came for raises at 9. After bkfst. Will, Annie & I went to Fresh Pond & tried his boat, then back to the house where Nether Cook joined us & at about 10.30 we went to Ernest Longfellow's. He not in, saw his charming wife, this very pleasant house & studio — from there to Memorial Hall — then to Peabody museum & at 12.30 to the Square & by car home at 1 o'clk. Then dinner — at 2.15 we bid our good byes & Annie, Mother Cook & I took car into Boston. Reaching Revere House on Bowdoin Sq. at 3.05 — then a hack to Old Colony R.R. Station getting there at 3.30 — train for Newport at 3.40 — 67 miles in 2½ hours — getting to N — at 6.10 — Hackman had trouble in finding Coleman's cottage & we were driven to several places before we found it at 6.40 — then Annie Coleman & I hurried around to see his lot & the foundation of his new house — back at 7 — then had dinner — too much of it — up at 8.30 — hackman at the door — rode down to boat which came along from Fall River at 9 o'clk — found W. T. Richards there & spent what remained of the eve'g with him — This night was very brilliant moonlight & cold — Annie & her mother occupied 140 & I turned into 141 before 11 o'clk — & had a tolerably comfortable night.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 27. 1882.

*At home.*

Got up before 7 — cold, misty, mft. with the now unchanging east wind — got to the dock shortly after 7 — W. Richards joined us & we all went up town by 6th Ave Elevated R.R. We got home at 8 & soon after had bkfst. About 10 I went to studio & did little else than write my weekly letter to Cottrell — Home to dinner at 1.30 which I did not eat as I had had too much dinner yesterday — I spent much of the p.m. in the bed & not until after 5 went to studio. Home to tea at 7 — Jas. Wright came in the afternoon — Eve'g Jas. W. & Annie played whist &c. Mother Cook & me until about 10 o'clk — to bed at about 11 & soon after that some heavy rain.
