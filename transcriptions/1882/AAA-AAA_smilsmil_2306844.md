## Left Page

### October, Saturday 21, 1882

Another brilliant day — awoke before 5 — got up & had a splendid view of the comet. Annie, Fanny & Elizabeth joined me. — Up for the day soon after 7 — Bkfst at 8 — the m'f. very cold — the sunshine very warm. Spent the most of the day out of doors, following the men in their grading work about the house. Dinner at 1 — About 3.30 we went out riding — by Hart Lake — through woods to Somerville road & home by Mulford farm at 5.30 — after sunset & cold — Tea shortly after 6. Then I went to P.O., Market, etc — back after 8 — we had some card playing & to bed about 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 22, 1882

Dismal, novemberish sort of day. Bkfst at 9 after some active work with Fines in studio, Itching-Room & furnace. After bkfst we sat in studio & very soon it was 12 o'clk. I then went out & walked to the Lake & back, stopping at 1 o'clk at Courtright's & getting some things left there. Then home. Dinner at about 2 — the weather growing pleasanter — headachy, so I did not accomplish anything in the way of letter writing — or any other way — After 7 when we had tea — Evetf. some moonlight — Wrote letters to Father & Charl & at 10.30 went to P.O. About 11 when I went to bed.
