## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 20, 1882

Pleasant day with threats of a coming storm. To studio
as usual & worked on Oscar's present, pursing over cutting
mounting board etc. also composing the several sketches.
To dinner at 1 — back before
3 & worked until near 5.
Dr. Yale came in & I had
a long talk with him about
Matt's case — At 5.30 I went
down to Fulton St. & bought a
Diary — home at 6.30 &had
tea — Every wrote letters,
among others, one to Matts,
which I went to G. C. Depot
to mail — To bed about
11.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 21, 1882

Dismal day with drizzling
rain part of the time. Got
to studio at 9.30 & to work on
O.B.J's dog souvenir — Home
to dinner soon after 1 & back
at 2.30 — worked as long as
I could see, continuing by gas
light, at work with pen
until I finished it — then
to Fanny F.'s to tea. Annie
& Emma J. there — took
them to their Singing club
& then I went to Century —
wrote for awhile — a fire
in 14th St. near Academy of Music
— I went out in the rain to
see it — there for Annie — At
10.30 took her & Fanny home to
22nd St. & at 11 Annie & I to
our own home — Put Oscar's
picture in its frame and
got to bed at midnight.
