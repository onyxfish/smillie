## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 7, 1882

Very warm — drying wind from
the South — Got to studio about
9.30 & to work on Cuban drawing
Home at 1 — Mr. Jos. Ireland to
dinner — Nellie Ret. Home at 3 —
I back to studio soon after 3 —
finished drawing — At 4.30
went to Dewey's — then to Lin-
crusta Walton Store — & got piece
to test in hall — then to Wunder
lich's — then to Studio — Sent
check to Montrose — another to
Dewey. Home to tea at 7 —
Werf. Chave called for a few
minutes — Then Avery Brown
& his mother — They left at 10 —
after that Annie & I worked
over list of sizes of glass etc
for Montrose House — To bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 8, 1882

Dry, hot South wind blowing,
after 8 &h' — went up to Jarocki's &
waited for some time — he took
another imp. in plaster of lower
jaw — met Dr. [illegible] in cars on my
way to studio where I am. a little
before 11 — wrote to Osgood asking
for acc't of Sales of Ports & Etchers — also
to Orrin Holt, Keene Valley, about
Fayes — Home at 1, Sophie Ireland
to dinner — At 3.30 Annie & I
went to Elevated R.R. & to foot of
Liberty St. Took 4.30 Train to Plain-
field — Belle & Florence I. met us at depot —
with Belle we took a very pleasant
short drive to Netherwood & back. Got
am. at house by earlier Train — Will Kimball
came soon after — we had a very jolly
tea — all seeming to be in good spirits
— then for an hour on piazza — At 8.25
we started for depot; Belle, Mary & Flo.
walking with us — 8.42 Train in — got
home about 10, so much pleased
with our trip — To bed soon after
