## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 4, 1882

Still very cold — part of the day, slightly overcast with a threat of snow. Got to studio at 9.40 & to work on "Old Ice House" all day — Home, as usual, to dinner at 1.15 — & back at 2.30 — About 5 went to Renner's shop & then to the store about frames — then walked up 6th ave. to Central Park & down 5th ave. home — Tea about 6.45 — Mr. Brown & Miss Williams to tea & after went with Mr. Cooke to a church sp[?]rea — Mr. B. ret'd & stayed all night — I spent the evening trying to arrange a preface for catalogue of the coming exhi[bition] of etchings — To bed after 11.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 5, 1882

Not so cold, with threats of snow, which fell very lightly at night. Got to studio a little before 10 and to work on "Old Icehouse" until 1, then home to dinner — back soon after 2 & continued work until I lost light — At 5 I went out for a walk — met Dechaux & walked up to 7th Ave. & 52d St. with him — then on to 5th Ave & 53d St. & home — Had a little nap before tea at 6.30 — Miss Dolphine Ireland of Orange, at the house over night. At 8 went with Annie to her singing club — there to studio — there until 10.15 — then went for a — & home at 10.45 — To bed soon after 11.
