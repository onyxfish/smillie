## Left Page

### September, Friday 1. 1882.

Bkfst. before 7.30 — Heavy fog — everything dripping wet — went to studio by 10 o'clk. Finished A.B.N. Co. drawing — went to Eifp. office & sent it on — Then back — made up more sizing for wall & looked after Morris & balusters. Dinner at 1 — back & spent p.m. in tacking duck on stairway ceiling & sizing it — also in general directions — To tea soon after 6 — the day has been very hot & muggy — about 7.30 Annie & I went to P.O. — then to studio where I drew for awhile & did some writing — wrote a note to ge? — about 10, to C's & to bed —

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 2. 1882.

Very close & muggy — quite dog-dayish — Bkfst. as usual — Spent the m'fg. with Burgess & Morris getting in balusters on main stairway — am quite pleased with effect — so is Annie — Dinner at 1 — Afternoon, general cleaning up — wishing to have an agreeable impression at the last. I worked until 7 — then to tea — Mr Simmons has rel? to P.O. after tea — then up to C's — Florence & there — at 10 escorted her home — — to bed at about 11.
