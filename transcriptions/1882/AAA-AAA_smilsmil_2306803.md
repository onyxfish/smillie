## Left Page

### July, Saturday 29, 1882

Close, muggy day with renewed rain threatenings but
nothing came of it. Spent the m[orning] planning out parts
of stairway details for the lower hall — Everything as
usual — Afternoon finished some details for newel post
& gave them to Burgess —
Tea soon after 6 — a change in the weather — blowing
very cool from N.W. After tea Annie & I went to P.O.
on our way back stopped & made a call at Hy Tyler's
to the C's after 9 — Read paper — then A. Read
aloud awhile from "Cloister & Hearth" — to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 30, 1882

Delightfully clear, cool day —
Bkfst about 9 — at 10.30 went to house & spent the morning
there doing nothing in particular — it was enough to loiter on
the piazzas — Dinner at 1.30
— then again to the house &
wrote letters to Hogarth & Sons
of London about their bill for etching frames & to Geo. Flint & Co —
also to Nattrass & then Mulford came
about 5.30 & we went over the house
with them — tea at 6.30 — then
again to studio — finished a
letter to father — A. & I went
to P.O. after 8 — then to C's — a
beautiful moonlight night —
swung in hammock for some
time — to bed about 11.
