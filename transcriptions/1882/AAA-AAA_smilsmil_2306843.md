## Left Page

### October, Thursday 19. 1882.

Cold, Raw & overcast with some spitting Rains. Bkfs=
at 8.30 — General's up emutienly
as usual — wrote note to Belle
Tuesday about Phil. ticket. Went
down town after 11 — home before
1 — then dinner — Uncle A.
came at 2 & made a short
visit — I kindled fire in
studio's furnace — A. f, & E.
went down town about 4 o'clk.
Ruddy sunset & blustering
night — Tea soon after 6.
Hot as a tinker's ladle in
studio — every after tea I
went to P.O. — soon back —
chatting & reading papers
until a little after 10,
then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 20. 1882.

Brilliant day — cloudless & cool.
Bkfs. at 8 — following the men,
as usual, especially Pettus, as he
ploughed & scraped, grading
the grounds about the house —
went down town before noon and
to Lattrop's mill. — Home to dinner
at 1 — about, as in the mf
until near 4, when with Fanny
& Eliq[illegible], I went over Warner's lot,
around the fairground & home
about 5 — then all took a rest
& at 7 went to Park's to tea, where
we met the Westcotts, Mr. Brewer
— de Schevinin[illegible] & a lady friend —
Tea at 7.30 — in the evef. whist.
It was near midnight when
we got home — brilliant moon-
light — to bed at once.
