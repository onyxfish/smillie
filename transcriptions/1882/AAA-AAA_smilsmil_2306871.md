## Left Page

### December, Thursday 14, 1882

Bright + breezy. To studio at usual time — worked some on drawing but most of the time was taken in mounting + framing etchings for Pie[illegible] [illegible]. To dinner + back at usual time. — Worked on drawing until near 5 — then to Century to meet Hadeu com — as only Yale, Gilder, Moran + self came, no business was transacted — Home at 6.30 — Tea — + at 8, with Annie to her singing club, 11 E. 14 — then to studio — worked there for a while — then to Century + wrote letters until 10 — then for Annie + home at 11 — then to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 15, 1882

Squally day. Got to studio at 9.45 + to work on W. C. Lake [illegible]. — also finished W. T. R. vigt out to square, so that I can frame it — also wrote notes to W. M. S. [illegible] + to Van Antwerp + Bragg + Cin! O. acknowledging receipt + [illegible] $35.00 — to bank with it — then home to dinner at 1 — back at 2.45 + worked as long as I could see — Dr. Yale came in at dusk + I signed invitation to Haden to lecture — also a subscription of $10 for tickets to his lecture — home at 6 — tea at 6.30 — at 8 dressed + at 9. at W. Rob. Gordon's — 7 2. 38 — inauguration of his Music Room — charming entertainment. Phil. club played. Miss Gordon + Miss Little sang. Supper about 11 — home at 12 + right to bed.
