## Left Page

### January, Sunday 22, 1882

A march-like day, but very
cold with flying rain + snow
flurries — Bkft. after 9 — Annie
\+ I went to Dr. Vincent's. Cor.
Park Ave. + 35th S. + heard a
begging sermon — took Annie
home, then by 3d Ave. Elevated
went up to 98th S. + 5th Ave to
look at some lots — Horribly
muddy, windy + cold — Home
at 2.15 — Dinner — The usual
Sunday P.M. calls — At 5.30 I
went to a studio — fixed line +
packed Shninger's Fortiny
Etching to return it to him
by E4p. Home at 7 — then
had tea — Evg. made
home + to Shninger — took
letters to G.C. Debat at 10 —
thru home + to bed about
11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 23, 1882

Bitter cold day with high wind,
nearly 10 o'clk. when I got to studio
set palette + to work repainting
on "Cedar Meadow" near Po'keepsie
Did not get along very happily.
At 12.30 — went to E4p. office with
Shninger's Fortiny Etching — then
home to dinner at 1 — the fire-
side was so pleasant that it
was near 3 before I got back
to studio where I painted as
long as I could see — then I
made notes + at 6 went to
Colman's for a few minutes —
Home to tea at 6.45 — Evening
writing notes + sending out
cards for W.C. Private View —
To bed about 11.30
