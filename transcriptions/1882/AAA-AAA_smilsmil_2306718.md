## Left Page

### February, Saturday 11, 1882

Delightful day — walked to
N.A. with Annie after [illegible] —
got to studio about 10 — Spent
[illegible] with Farrer & fixing up
more plp. of the Bridgman etch-
ing f which I have sold a
number at the etching [illegible]. A
man came from Reiners & I
set him to work mounting the
fab. plp. of "Poets & Triches". Home
to dinner at 1.20 — back at 3.15 —
more fussing with etching plp. —
Home at 6.30 — at 8 A. & I went
to Phil. concert. After it, went
with Edith Wright & Miss Reynolds
to Miss R's in [illegible] — then home,
E. going with us — a supper
of crackers & oranges — then
to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 12, 1882

Charming day — slightly over-
cast in the afternoon with wind
from the S. & E. — [illegible] at 9 — at
11 I went to Arthur Brook's — &
heard him preach a very good
sermon on Christian Habits —
After service walked up to A.
V.V.'s — there for a short time &
then home to dinner at 2.30 —
at near 4 A. E. & I went to
N.A. & D. — there for more than
an hour — A. & E. then went to
Emma J's — I went to studio, &
fixed fire, then joined them —
Started for home after 6 — a
few drops of rain — E. went to
Walter's to dinner — C. & I went
on home & had tea at 7 —
Evg. Reading & writing
letter to Father. Also took
letter to depot — to bed before
11.
