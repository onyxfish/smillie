## Left Page

### February, Thursday 23, 1882

A very March-like day — light snow squall in m'g. Breezy day — Got to studio before 10 — Father came in from P.O. about 10.45 — We went over to N.A. Water Color about 1, F.J. — Annie & her mother came to studio & saw Father there. T. W. Wood also called — Annie came to N.A. while we were there. I left Father with her & Ret'd. to studio & stretched some Jap. p'fs. Home to dinner at 1.15 Father there — We Ret'd. to Studio about 3. Father rested — Ella & May (Divens) called — Father took tea with Geo. & planned to spend the week with Will — Home to tea at 6.30 at 8 went with A. to her singing class then to N.A. & to Kurtz Gallery to see the Rejected W.C.'s — Met Will & friends at N.A. Went for Annie at 10.15 — home near 11 — found that Father had gone to bed — So we followed his example soon after.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 24, 1882

Clean & quite cold — Got to studio at 9.30 — Father breakfasted with Will, my m'g was about writing letters & fussing with etching p'f. Home to dinner soon after 1 — Just after dinner, surprised by a visit from Thininger & wife — just come to town — back to studio at 3 — more work like the morning's work — & at 3.30 Father left for depot to go home. at 4.30 went to N.A. then to [illegible]'s — Home to tea at 6.0 — Mother Cook attending the Mitchelle Garden wedding — about 8 Annie & I went to Walter Cook's & made a short call & from there to Horace Robbin's 1.37 2. 60[illegible] 5. — a very pleasant call — walked home at 10 — waited up for Mother Cook until 11 — to bed at 11.30
