## Left Page

### January, Thursday 26, 1882

~~Nasty day + sleety rain~~
~~Bright beautiful day.~~ Nearly
10 when I got to studio —
Spent the m'f. Painting on
"Evening Shadows" very unsatis-
factorily — Home to dinner
at 1 — back at 2.30 — did not
make much headway in
the afternoon — feeling a
good deal out of sorts, but
after 5 went to 14 S. + saw
Reclining chair + cushions
prepared by order of Annie
thens to the Kimball's — Annie
there — Tea at 6.30 — At 7.30.
took A.T.F. to Singing Club. At
9.30 A. Ret'd. home in carriage
with Emma J. — I went to the
Century + passed the evening
there. — Home soon after
10 — + then to bed before
11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 27, 1882

Dull, overcast day — After b'f't.
went to N.A. of D. Was invited to
go into the Galleries — Spent the
morning looking at the Watercolors
+ the Etchings — the [illegible] is very
interesting + the decorations in the
way of hangings &c is a trac un-
usually rich & harmonious. — I
went to Studio for a few minutes
+ then home to dinner at 1.30 —
Again to Studio about 3 — as
it was almost too dark to work
+ as I felt much under the
weather I did not accomplish
anything but the writing of a
letter or two. Home to tea at
6. Ev'g. about 8.30 — went to
Press + Profession view of Water
Color Soc'ty. There until 11 —
walked up to Union League
Club with Charl. There for
a few minutes — nasty night
— home + to bed about 11.45.
