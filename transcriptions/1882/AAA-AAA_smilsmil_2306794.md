## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 11. 1882.

Very hot day — the [illegible] as usual at the house. Most of my time unpacking furniture — Dinner as usual — After it, again to house — unpacked a lot of Macy goods & got packing boxes out of the studio — Tea at 6 — then to Studio, then until after sunset. Watered some of the trees set out last spring — After 8 went to P.O. with Annie — did some shopping — Ret'd to our quarters about 9 — Read newspaper until 10 and to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 12. 1882.

Very pleasant & cooler. Called up before 7 & went over to house with McWhinney & started him at digging the foundation for a wall. Then back & had [illegible] a little before 8 — the [illegible] was about in general oversight. Cotrell at work on a problem of kitchen stairs. Just before 1. Hamlin came & made arrangements to begin work on studio chimney this p.m. Dinner abt 1. & then again to house — cut out side of studio, for chimney — a number of callers — Aunt H., Mr. Lyons — etc — Tea at 6 — some signs of Rain. [illegible] C. Ret'd home — [illegible] at studio until dusk — down to P.O. then to our quarters about 9 — Read paper & to bed soon after 10.
