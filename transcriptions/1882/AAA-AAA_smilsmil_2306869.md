## Left Page

### December, Sunday 10, 1882

Wretched day of slush — snow
& rain — up late — blkp. after
9 — Spent part of the mf over
some montrose matters — at
10'clk. walked down to studio
& back to dinner at 2. Later
wrote letters — interrupted —
About 6.30 Annie & I went
to Mary Willard's in W. 49th S.
to tea — Miss Davis, her friend was
there, also E. M. C. & wife —
home at 10.30 & soon to bed.
Rain at night.

## Right Page

### December, Monday 11, 1882

Overcast, dark & raw, clearing
cold & windy after 3 p.m. Went
to Grady & McKeever's at 9.30 — to
studio at about 10.30 — finished
view of Mt. Washington — packed
it & wrote letter to V. A. & B. — took
pkg to Adams's Exps — then home to
dinner at 1 — Back at 2.30 &
worked on various things — on
etching pf. for Phil. Exptn — on
large W. C. sketch — on W. C. sketch
for minn — Soon after 5 went
to barber's & had my hair cut —
then to Kimball's — Matt & Kate
down from Po'k — Annie there — tea
at 8, Matt & Kate — Fanny, Will, Mrs. K —
& I went to Standard Theatre
& saw (& heard) Gilbert & Sullivan's
Iolanthe — much amused — out
at 11 — clear & cold — home & to
bed before midnight.
