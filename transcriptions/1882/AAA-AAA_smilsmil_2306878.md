## Left Page

### December, Thursday 28, 1882

Beautiful day — Sent tel. mes: to Dr. Hale — who rec'd answer that he would be ready for us at 10 —
Bhf. about 8.30 — also, went with Matt & had to wait until 10.30 for D.H. — then she underwent an examination & got little comfort from 4 — who counsels excision — he told us that Dr. Stephen Smith approves of the use of caustic & we went to him & had another examination. Resulting in an arrangement to begin with his treatment to-morrow m'tf. There was never 1 when we got home — Dinner — To studio before 3 — Satterlee had been there to see me — I laid grounds for him on 2 plates — worked awhile for myself — at 4. Fok. S.'s plates to him — notin — then to Keppler, Wunderlich's & Knoedler's on Haden business. Home to tea before 7 — with Annie to her club at 8 — then to Courteny — continuing until 10 — then for Annie — home at 11 & soon to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 29, 1882

Pleasant day — after Bkf. wrote a couple of notes — got to studio about 10 — to work on W.C. of Rough Sport & struggled along in rather hopeless mood — Annie came in & we walked up home at 1 o'clk — Dinner — back at 2.30 — Edw. Gay came in with a water color he is painting to get my criticism upon it — Jackson the advert'g ag't. also called about Haden lecture adv't. about 5 I went to Kimmel & Voigt's in counsel with approved m/p. of Cat. plate of Rough Sport — then walked up home, stopping at Biskies and getting new pair of boots — tea before 7. Dr. Adams there — Eve'g. I wrote rough draft of letter to Haden to accompany the invitation to lecture — to bed about 10.30
