## Left Page

### April, Sunday 16. 1882.

Delightful day — Bkfs. about 9 —
then out & had a little walk —
about 11 went to studio & found me
time was engaged there in pre-
paration for leaving town for a
week or so. Home to dinner at
2 — after that a rest, then, near
5 went with A. to see pictures
of Am. Soc't of Artists — there for an hour,
then to see Geo. & Nellie & at near
7, home to tea — At 8.30 went up to
see Will — home at 9.45 — To bed
at 10.30 — wrote to Father —

## Right Page

### April, Monday 17. 1882.

*Montrose, Pa.*

Up at 6 — Charming day — Wkfs. at a
little after 7 — Annie not well — she went
to see Dr. Blackwell — I followed in cab —
all ready to take 8.30 train — Dr. B. not in —
Rec'd. home with A. — & at 9 went to studio.
About 11 Rec'd a note from A. telling me to
make arrangements to leave by 1 o'ck train.
2 lbs? Cornell's old taken bucket bill — to him —
went to Bank & home at 11.45 — a light
lunch & then again started for Montrose
in ample time for 1 o'ck train — a dry,
glaring, grimy ride — no interest. got
to New Milford at 7.45 — Barouche in
waiting for us — plenty of robes, very
easy seat — enjoyed the ride to town
exceedingly — the eve'g air so fresh
without being raw. Got to Courtright's
at 9.30 — Mrs. C. in bed with cold —
no one else in — we went to our
room & to bed soon after 10.
