## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 18, 1882

another very fine day — Up at
6.15 & before bkfst. went to house
& spent some time going over
it. Back to Bkfst. at 8. at 10.30
went down town to P.O. & other
places. Dinner at 1 — a rest
& at 3 again went down town
& saw Stroud about Inst. & to
Bank to have acct. made up.
From 4 to 6 at house with
Coltrelle & making measurements
& taking notes — Tea at 6.30.
Eveff. Florence d. came in &
we had a couple of games of
whist — then Annie read to
me a couple of chapters from
Prescott's Mexico & at 10.30 in
went to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 19, 1882

Clear for awhile in the mfg. but
soon overcast — Rain in the afternoon
& a very heavy thunderstorm & much
rain between 7 & 9 P.m. Bkfst. at 7.30.
Most of the mfg was spent at the
house superintending work and
measuring inside preparatory to
making my detail drawings for
finish — Dinner at 12.30 and
after & had a rest. Then again
to building where I spent
most of the afternoon — some
fine cloud effects between strug[gling]
clouds, opposing winds & efforts to
clear. Tea soon after 6. Eveff.
Florence d. came in & with a.
Courtright we had a couple of
games of whist — after that a. &
I had a chapter or two of Prescott
& to bed soon after 10 — The eveff.
spent in our room, having a
fire in our stove.
