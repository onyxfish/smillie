## Left Page

### May, Sunday 14, 1882

Pouring rain all day & night.
got up feeling quite bright —
but the weather was so cold &
wretched that I did not put
my head out of doors. Spent
the day loafing about the house,
reading and writing. Many
spent the day with us. Every
ned. am? from Phila & stayed over
night. To bed about 11 o'clk

## Right Page

### May, Monday 15, 1882

Dull day — and a dull head. Bkf.
as usual. Then to big chair — Mary
went to Mr. Reynoed's (Wills') in 34th.
St. at about 11 o'clk. Annie out also,
much of the mf. I hanging about
complaining of head & throat. While
out, A. got my drawing materials
from studio & I spent the afternoon
quite closely at work upon drawings
for Cottrell & Etching Room at Montrose
Tea late — the weather having cleared
charmingly I went out about 7.30
& took a walk & altho' I felt weak
& tired, when I came in I was
much better for the air & exercise.
Worked some more during the evef.
Geo. came in & spent the latter part
of the evef. To bed at about 11.
