## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 25, 1882

Brilliant day — clear and cold. Most of evng. out of
doors watching progress of work. — Downtown before
noon — Dinner at 1 — at 2 we went out Riding —
& had a delightful turn over Hartford way & back by
Meshoppeu Road. — Back before 5 — Tea after 6. Then
went to P.O. Back soon after 8. — the rest of the
evng. we indulged in in Poker — which financially
ruined Annie by 10.80 when she insisted up on
going to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 26, 1882

Brilliant day. The evng
until after 10 — was windy &
threatening, after which it
became wonderfully clear
& quite mild. While at [illegible].
Evergreens (24 Spruce) were
brought up — bought last Spring.
very small — under 2 feet — very
much disappointed. Spent
all the evng with Payne & his
son setting out the evergreens —
Dinner at 1. Soon after 2 went
out riding with Annie, Fanny
& [illegible] — down [illegible] to Fairdale & home over Fairview hill —
Aft. A. to make a call on Mr. de S. —
Tea at 6 — Evng. Mr. de S. called
& spent the evng with us —
To bed at 11. Glorious
moonlight.
