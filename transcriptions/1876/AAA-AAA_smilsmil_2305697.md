## Left Page

### November, Friday 3, 1876

Overcast, with a scatter of rain — but cleared charmingly later in the day. As usual, at the carriage [illegible] in waiting — a little later by me we got away — with Geo. I Reviewed much in the main bldg. Then through Machinery Hall & to a number of the outside bldgs. At 2 lunched in Main Bldg. Then to Memorial Hall & worked diligently at the pictures until 4". Then home by P.&R.R.R., — Sea at 6.30 — Eve'g in parlor the Redfield's called — also others — To our room before 10 — packed trunk and to bed before 11.

So ends our Centennial Visit on . .

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 4, 1876

Delicious day — [illegible] at 8 — Good byes said very deliberately & at 10 started with Mr. Lee & Geo. in carriage for W. Phila. to take 11 o'clk. train for N.Y. Had no trouble in getting our seats but it was 11.20 before our train started — ate our lunch at 1.15. Got to Jersey City at 2.45 — ¾ of an hour late. Rode up town & went at once to 123 W. 41 st. Took a nap. Trunk arr'd at 6.30 — Tea at 6.30 — got to Academy of Music — 1st Phil. Concert, at 8 o'clk. Met there the Sweedi[illegible] — After the Concert walked with them to the Colman's — there until 11 o'clk. — Then to 41 st. to bed.
