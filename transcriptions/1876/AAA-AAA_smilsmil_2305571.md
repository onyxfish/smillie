## Left Page

### February, Saturday 19, 1876

Pleasant day — but I have a very unpleasant head — not exactly a headache, but the unpleasant sensation, that has troubled me so much & unfitted me for work two years ago. Did little else than fuss with inside of Siena frame painting it to harmonize with the pictures. To dinner at 1.15 — Mother there — back at 2.30. More painting on frame — Robt. Halpspan made a long call. To tea at 6.30, Mother yet there — At 8.30 at Phil. Concert. After that walked up 4th Ave. to 26th S. with Belle T. & young Edmonds — Then to Studio — to 41 S. at 11.15 & to bed at 11.30 [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 20, 1876

Glorious day but rather windy & dirty. Up at 7.30 — Scrubbed, rubbed & bathed, & breakfasted at 8.30 — Uncle N. & Anita, then at 10 o'clk, went to studio — went late to Mad. Ave. Bap. Ch. hoping to meet Mother there — did not — back to Studio — there until 1.10 then walked up to A.V.V.'s — Dinn at 2.30 — Fischer, Meyer & two Newhalls there — At 5, a little nap. At 6, Uncle A. & I walked over to Willi's — Mother there — At 8.30, took Mother to Mary's — at 9.30 at Studio — had to build a new fire, got to 41 at 8th at about 10.40 & to bed soon after 11.
