## Left Page

### May, Wednesday 17. 1876.

Overcast, with spitting showers during the day. Got to studio at 9.45 — Saw Mr. Wilson — paid my rent two months in advance & arranged about my lease — Spent the rest of the m'f in finishing up catalogue and bossing carpenter work. — To dinner at 1.30 — On my way back, called on Lazernus, Ja. Hart & Miss Woodward — Got to studio about 4 o'clk — Then until 6.30. Then to 41 [illegible] to tea. Father came in for a few minutes. At 8.30 at Fifth Ave Hotel. Parlor 7, meeting called by Parker Godwin for the purpose of starting a summer loan collection of pictures at the M.A. of D. & Met. Museum of Art. I was put on Committee of Printing. Meeting adjourned soon after 10 — to 41 [illegible] & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Thursday 18. 1876.

Heavy Rain in the m'f. At 10 called at Kurtz's gallery — he was not in. Then to studio — there with Carpenter until 1.30 — then to lunch — At 2 went down town to A.B.N.Co — Made out my bill & had an unpleasant passage with Goodall. Saw Father, Will & Charl. Then started up town, went to Devry's and ordered suit of clothes — then to Brooks Bros. Wilson, Clarke to Stewarts' & made purchases — Stopped at studio & finally to [illegible] at 4 H.S. at 7.30 — at 8.30 went up to Harris Pardee's at No. 10 W. 47 S. Found all in. Tried to sell my Schrub Race & at one time thought I had done it; but no conclusion was reached — left at 10.30 and to bed at 11.
