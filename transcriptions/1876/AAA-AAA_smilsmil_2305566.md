## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 9, 1876

Dull mff. then cleared — then mizzled & finally, at
night rained heavily —
Got to studio at 9.30 — got
my lay figure & fussed with
it trying to arrange figure
principal in "Rough Sport"
— no use — Providentially old
Newton came in & I Rigged
him up & painted from him
until 1 o'clk — To dinner at
1.15 — back at 2.15 — So was
N & I painted from him
until 4.30 — getting "Rough
Sport" all in — To tea at 6.45
To studio at 8 & wrote and
washed dishes until near
11 — There to 41° 8?° & to bed
at 11.20

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 10, 1876

Overcast mff. but soon cleared —
Felt out of sorts when I got
up — but bathed & breakfasted
as usual. Got to studio at
10 o'clk. but could not get
to work. Finally, went over
to building going up cor. of
25° S. & 4° Ave. & spent some
time climbing over it — Met
for the first time, W. Wilson,
the owner — Suggested a number
of changes — About 1.30 got to
41° "S" so overpowered with
a headache that I rolled
into bed & did it get up
again until to morrow mff.
This is the first bilious head-
ache I have had this winter
& I was beginning to hope
that I had outgrown them —
