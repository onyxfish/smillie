## Left Page

### August, Friday 25, 1876

Little Refreshment after a hot night of tossing and dreaming — Very hot myself did not dare to go out to my work, so the mf was spent in-doors writing a letter to Bellows and making a start on A.B.N. Co. drawing — got a nap before dinner which was served at 1.30 — Afternoon again worked on drawing. A provoking spatter of Rain came at about 4 o'clk but nothing more — although overcast most of p.m., the heat was very oppressive. Tea at 6.30 — Ever much cooler. Read news to others & got to bed at 10 o'clk. after a short Romp in the hall when boots were found to be as tight-fitting as the skin of a baby's sock & strong enough to bear a strain of 250 lbs.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 26, 1876

Clear, breezy day & pleasantly cool — B.k.b. at 8 — after that took exercise walking on Roadway & about 9.30 got to work ruling A.B.N. Co. drawing — at it until dinner at 1.30 — and again in the afternoon until near 4 o'clk. when I finished it — then into town with Geo. & did messages for the family — back about 5. dry, windy & dirty. Tea soon after 6 — on piazza — then in library — about 9 some ruckus with May, Bea & the gent. To bed soon after 10 — strong wind blowing during the night.
