## Left Page

### June, Monday 26, 1876

A very hot day. After bkfs.
wrote letters — then went in
town, called on W. Williams
& shopped for Merino — Then
spent rest of mf. upholstering a board upon which to
show my little pictures —
Dinner at 12.30 — Afternoon
I got out my water color box
& fixed that up for work,
then worked a little on
three little sketches from
the windows — Tea at 6.
At 7.30 went by street car
out to Vassar College, alone,
& attended the concert of
the season under charge
of Mad. Roche. Home & to
bed at 11.
Matt & Kate went to W.
this mf.

## Right Page

### June, Tuesday 27, 1876

Powerful hot — After bkfs.
Mr. Loomis called to see the
½ doz. little pictures that I
painted since my arrival here.
About 10.30 W. & Mrs. Williams
Mrs. Holley, Miss Wheeler of Prov.
& others came in — had a very
pleasant visit for an hour or
more — Dinner at 12.30 & at 1.30
I went to W. Williams & with
him & Miss Wheeler, Rode out to
Vassar where we enjoyed
"Class Day" Exercises and a
lunch at 5 o'clk. A tremendous
thunderstorm spoiled the out
of door exercises but we enjoyed
indoors very much until 7.30
Then to W. W's where we had
tea & I stayed until 10 o'clk.
Delicious moonlight — Home
& to bed at 10.30. [illegible — ~2 lines]
