## Left Page

### July, Monday 24, 1876

*At Gent. Diven's — Elmira.*

Clear, cloudless mtf and very
cool — Bkfst som after 7 — then
with Mother & Kate, am fan back
walk in garden until 8.30 —
then wrote a letter to Clarence
Cook on the subject of Centennial
Catalogues — Did ufo my chores
Took dinner at 12.30 — bid
mother good bye & off in the
Stage at 1.30 — drove over to
New Milford — Train on time
at 3.33 — got to Binghamton
at 4.15 — called on Emma Smith
at 5. Erie Train, 15 m late, came,
got to Elmira at about 7 —
after having passed through
several showers. Rode to the
Gent's in a shower. Tea about
8 — Every'm genel. Chat —
until about 10, then to bed,

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 25, 1876

Delicious day — Bkfst at
7. Played croquet with
the Gent — after bkfst.
Got my trunk unpacked —
& a little visiting done by
half past ten when we
where we had lunch —
After that, rode up to Horse-
Heads with Gent. Diven and
was caught in a heavy shower.
Back to dinner at 5 — then
croquet with Jack. Feeling
very much out of sorts — side.
Eugene & wife came in — So
cool that fire was made
in back library. My discomfort
increased until I got to bed
after 10. [illegible — ~3 lines]
