## Left Page

### January, Saturday 22, 1876

Overcast & raw, threatening snow, which came in the eve. Got to studio at 9.20 — nearly all the m[or]'f was spent in misting & putting away traps — got out block for Harper's & commenced working up the photo that is on it — from the cartoon of big drawing — To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2 — Mrs. Comstock & lots of other visitors came, but I worked as well as I could — Nicole came at 5 — To tea at 6.30 — at 8 at Thomas's concert — gave Phil ticket to Charl — 9½ Symphony with chorus was very grandly given — after, walked with Lela Martin & father — stopped at studio — then to 4[1st] St — & bed at midnight —

## Right Page

### January, Sunday 23, 1876

Miserable day of Rain & slush. Up early & had bath & bkfp — before 9.30 went to Hastings & heard him preach from V Cor. 6th ch. & part of 20 v, "For ye are bought with a price" — After service, as rain had ceased, Charl. & I walked down to studio — at 1.30 I went to A.V.V. To gentlemen to dinner — friends of Gunther — a very nobby dinner — at 4.30 walked down to Fifth Ave Hotel & at 5.15 called upon Mr. & Mrs. & Irene More & daughter Katy; at 6 — at Emma Ireland's — At 8.30 at studio — wrote until 10 — then to 4[1st] St — & to bed at 10.40
