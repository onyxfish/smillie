## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 14, 1876

Real March day — midyt dusty.
Got to studio at 9.15. Many
calls — Jas. Fraser & friend, Nicale
& others kept me so that I
did not settle down to work
on centennial biz until 11 —
at 12.30 went over to the new
building, 4th ave & 25th S — at 1.15
to dinner — back at 2 and
wrote until near 5 — when,
feeling very tired I gave up,
he big chair until Silva came
at 6 — at 6.45 walked up with
him — To tea at 7. also went
to Rector's at 2d. Moran's 107
2.25 — There until 10.15 — Then
to 4th St. & to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 15, 1876

Charming day. Got to studio
at 9.25 — Traced one of the figures
in "Scomb Race" & transferred it
to Millboard — am going to paint
it for A.B.N. Co. To dinner at
1.30 — back at 2.30. Marie Dwen
surprised me with a call. I
charcoaled on "Head of a Horse"
also for A.B.N. Co — at 4.30 went
to State Centennial Com. meeting
625 B'way — After that walked
up with J. Jaycon Johnston — then
called on Avery. To tea at 6.30
Charl came in at 7.40 — then
at 8 called at Tiffany's — all out.
Then to Studio — wrote letters
until 10 — to 4th St. at 10.45 &
to bed at 11.
