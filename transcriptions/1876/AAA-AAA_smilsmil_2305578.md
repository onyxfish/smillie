## Left Page

### March, Saturday 4, 1876

Mild & pleasant day. Again late, 10 o'clk, nearly, when
I got to studio. Fussed run Scenes without much biting
them. At 12 o'clk, went to barber's & had hair cut — then
to Knoedle's & at 1.15, dinner — back at 2.15. Painted on
little oil sketch of "8 crub Race on the plains" — Had
a number of calls — Another day of little work or progress.
To tea at 6.40 — At 8.30 at studio — washed brushes
& fussed about generally until 10.30, then to 41 [illegible] St.
& to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 5, 1876

Mild & pleasant. [illegible] at
10 — then to Dr. Hastings & heard
him preach from Matt 9th ch,
9th v. "Follow me" — After
service, walked down to studio,
at 1.30 to dinner at Ripley's
13 E. 32° — A very pleasant
time until near 4.30 — then
again to studio — there until
6 — then to tea at the Irelands
Miss Howe there — At 8 again
at studio, wrote to George &
at 10.30 went to 41 [illegible] St. and
at 11 went to bed.
