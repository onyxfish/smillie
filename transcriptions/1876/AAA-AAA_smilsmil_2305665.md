## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 29, 1876

Still clear & cool — that is, cloudless, but very smoky,
so that the near hills can
hardly be seen. Fire in library
after b'kft. did some weeding
on the lawn — got to work som
after 10 — worked very [illegible]
during rest of m'f. Dinner
abt. 30 — then again to work
and stuck to it until 6 —
but did not quite finish
drawing — Tea about 7 —
Som after 8. Mr. Gensstad aft.
W. & Mrs. Jas. Woods called, we
had a game of whist. Ella
& the gent. went to Ithica this
m'f. to return abt. 8 P.M. To-
night, but they did not
get back. To bed at 11,
[illegible — ~2 lines]

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 30, 1876

Still snorting hot and very
hazy — after b'kft. Took a little
exercise, then to work on A.B.
M. Co's drawing & after writing
to father & Will, worked on
it until near dinner time
which to-day was 1.45, o'clk.
After dinner, packed drawing
& the gent. Took it in trim and
24s². I for me. I was entirely
used up — don't know what
the trouble is unless it's this
terrible, baking weather — am
sore all over — weak & feverish
with constant disturbance in
the stomach — Took pencil &
paper & went to meadow &
made a number of little
sketches of cows in action. Tea
at 6.30 — Ev'f in library —
Some Cal. Jacte with gent —
Cap'. May & Maud & Ella —
So bed at 10.
