## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 15, 1876

Heavy rain nearly all day — got to studio at 9.15. Miss Abbott helped me put my books in the cases — Miss Spring came & agreed to take a few lessons of me at $5.00 each — I worked in my closets until now 1 o'clk. Then went to thy Spring's to lunch & had a good time for an hour — all the p.m. until dark fussing in closets — To tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to Mary's — note on M.C, & duty lists until 10 — then to 41 [illegible] & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 16, 1876

Beautiful day — got to studio at 9.15. Woman waiting to clean my room. Set her right to work scrubbing & she kept at it until near 5 o'clk. Miss Spring came & in spite of the mess took her first lesson — me worked until 12.30 — I went to lunch at — back at 2.15 — Miss S — ahead for me — she worked until 3 — I touched up a pf. of "Condor" for A.B.N. Co. At 5.30 went to gas co. and made application for metre — again to studio — To tea at 6.45 — Evng spent writing notes (6) & lists of pictures for distribution from Phila until 10.15 — then to bed
