## Left Page

### April, Thursday 13, 1876

Very mixed & thick weather.
Got to Fred Durand's, after
calling at Studios, at 10 o'clk. —
went through 2 hours of misery
having teeth fixed. — Then
to Studios. — To dinner at 1.30
back at 2.15 & all the p.m.
worked on White horse in "Sand
Race" — M. Murch Lewis &
Anna & Lottie called. — To
tea at 6.30 — at 8 at Mary's
& saw Geo. — At Studios at
9.15 — again Miss G. helped
me, while I wrote — gave
up at 10.30 — To 44 St. at
11 & then to bed — [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### April, Friday 14, 1876

Very warm & muggy — Got
to Studios about 9.30 — Several
heavy showers — Whitridge
came in & ran away with
much of my mf., talking
of Centennial hanging ceremonies.
To dinner at 1.30 — back at
2.30 & spent the afternoon
working on White horse in Sand
Race. To tea at 6.45 — after
tea, worn out & spent the
evng. on sofa — To bed at
10.15 —
