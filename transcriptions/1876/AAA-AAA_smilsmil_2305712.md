## Left Page

### December, Sunday 3, 1876

Clear day — [illegible] at 9 —
mf. went to Hastings &
heard him preach on the
Reading of the voice in
the temple — After service
walked down to Century
Club & saw two shocking
bad paintings by Philip [illegible]
Stammerton — Then up
to Mam's to dinner at 1.30
there, at 3.30 to Studio —
wrote a letter to S.C.T.
At 6 went in to Emma's
to tea — at 7.30 back to
Studio & finished letter
at 9 again to Emma's &
there until 10.20 — Then
to 41, 8. & to bed at 11.
Miss Parker also took [illegible]
at E.'s —

## Right Page

### December, Monday 4, 1876

Very pleasant day. Before
going to Studio went to
Snedecor's & Knoedler's —
Bought paper — got to Studio
at 10.30 — boy waiting, made
a color sketch of him on
my upright study — to
dinner at 1.15 — back at
2 — Another boy awaiting
for me. — Made crayon
sketches of him on study
for "Under the Butternuts" —
about 4 to work mounting
some of my drawings and
did three when I could
not see — then some writing
& to tea at 6.30. At 9.30 went
to Ladies Recep[tion] at Palette. Very
pleasant entertainment. musical
etc — Misses Beebe, Finch —
etc — Bellamy etc — To
41, 8. at 11.30 & Right to bed
