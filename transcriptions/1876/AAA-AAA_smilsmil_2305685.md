## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 10, 1876

Overcast — but soon blew off clear — charming day —
got to studio at 8.30. Pat. Coyle picking up stones
for me. To work on "October" painting over middle dis[tance].
Dinner abt. Worked until about 5.15 — again
overcast — could not see any longer — Had a note
from Jessie Cornstroke & Minna Mulford. Sea
at 6. Went. Smith [illegible]
went to Azure Lathrop's to bid Conntwright good-bye —
then to P.O. (8.30) then to Mr. Cox's to see Mr. & Mrs. Du[?]
Lathrop — not there but met them in the street — Abt. 10
again went to Lathrop's &
played euchre until
10.20 — To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 11, 1876

Bleak, howling windy day. The
coldest of the season so far. Got
to studio at 8.30 & wrote letter
to Geo. Whitney & note to F.H. Smith
then to work on "October" — Painted
in anew the lower R.H. corner.
Dinner at 1 o'clk. Worked
until after 4 — here decided
not to paint any more on the
picture until I go out to W[?].
Feeling very tired, did not
go out to walk, but loafed
in studio. Sea at 6 — went
down to P.O. about 8 — then
back to Layre's & spent
the evening there — To bed
at 10 — [illegible — ~4 words]
