## Left Page

### January, Monday 24, 1876

Brisk & breezy — commenced
the work badly — did not get
to studios until near 10 — Then
much of the remainder of
mf lost in "fussing" — Got
to work on block drawing
of "Scrub Race" — About 1, went
with Rogers's to view studios
bldf, 27. S. & B'way — Got to
dinner at 1.40 — back at 2.30
& worked all P.M. on block
6.30 where I got to tea —
8.30 where I got to 211 m-
14th S- where I found Fau
M, Ben & the D-. Mrs Carnall
& others — there until 10.20
walked up, stopping at
studios — Got to bed at
11.30.

## Right Page

### January. Tuesday 25. 1876.

Overcast, with milder temperature
again late at studio — 9.40 — t
much even of the short mf lost
by going over to N.A. Rev. [illegible]
Smith called & used up what
was left of my morning — to dinner
at 1.30 — back at 2.15 — The
reaction after the strain of big
drawing is upon me — I have a
cold that makes me feel miserable
all over & I cannot work. I
mounted Murch Jeavis's Yosemite
drawing on heavy board preparatory
to finishing it. To tea at 6.30. Then
to Henry's — Then, at 8, to Nellie Jacobs'
— took her to studios — in Miss Woodward's
room, with Misses Gilbert & Blauvelt —
they all worked on a floor mat for me
I went to N.A. finishing hanging — then
until near 10 — Then to Studios — served
the girls with cream — at 10.30 took
Miss B home — Rd? & at 11 took Miss
J home & got to 211. S: & to bed
just at midnight.
