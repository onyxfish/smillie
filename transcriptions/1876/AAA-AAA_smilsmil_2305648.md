## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 26. 1876.

Charming day, cool & bright. Up at 6.30 after a bad night & feeling very slim — Ate a very light. [illegible] & sat about until after 10 — then to bed & had some sleep — Up a little after 12 — Dinner at 1.30 — At 2.30, Jack Cab'd & I Rode over to Race Track to see Base Ball — Ithicas ap- Californias — I's beat 4.53, then carriage came with May & gel. at 4.30 & we Rode home through the Park. I spread out on lounge. Tea about 7. Aleck & family came up & made a call — also Eugene & family. Every. Gent. Cab. May & I had whist until 10, to bed at 10.30 with a miserable headache.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 27. 1876.

Pleasant myf that increased to hotness before noon — B[illegible] at 7. Gent. D. went to N.Y. in the myf. I still felt quite uncomfortable but Rode in town about 11 — Tried to find some card board to make tablets for Centennial pictures. Brought Jack back to dinner at 1.30 — much of the p.m. spent on my back. Reading Gent. Sherman's memoirs — Tea at 6.30 — Aleck & wife came — Croquet — also Cab. Reed & wife — short call — Every. whist. Aleck & Cab'd, & Mrs.' Leck & I — Great luck for us — three whitewashes & then they won one game 7 to our 6. To bed at 10.30
