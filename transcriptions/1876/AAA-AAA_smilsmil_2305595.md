## Left Page

### April, Friday 7, 1876.

Tremendous winds & much dirt. Got to studio at 9.15 feeling very slim after my headache, but I set to work on horse's head for A.B.N. Co & spent most of the [illegible] on it, Repainting & improving generally — To dinner at 1.30 & at 2 Rode down to Phil. After that went into Nicale to 625 B'd way & worked until 6, arranging & measuring W.C.'s — To tea at 6.45. very tired. Nearq when again I got to studio — wrote until 10.45 — then to 41 [illegible] — & to bed at 11.20.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 8. 1876.

Bright clear day, but still very windy & dirty — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work on "Lion's Head" for A.B.N. Co. Painted pretty solidly over all of it — To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.15 & as usual stopped in Pratt's Room & gave a short lesson to Misses Abbott & Sterling — Then to my own work & painted until 4.30 — There to 625 B'd way — Nicale met me there & we finished measuring the W.C.'s — Walked up — To tea at 6.30 — very tired — Back to studio nearly — Tried to write — but after an hour, gave it up — To 41 S. — at 11 & to bed at 11.30 — Had a nervous turn or shock during the night.
