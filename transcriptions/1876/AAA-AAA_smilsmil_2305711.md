## Left Page

### December, Friday 1, 1876

Clear, snapping cold winter day. Got to studio soon after 9 & to work on "Study from Nature — In the woods" — worked until 12.30 — Then several callers so that I did not get to lunch until 1.20 — At 2.15 gave my Phil. Rehearsal ticket to Emma J. or anybody else at home — then to my work. About 4 P.M. made sketch of Nellie J — as milk maid. Went up to Renner's at 5.30. At 6.10 at Fanny K's. Tea at 6.30 — At 8 took her to her Singing club — then to N. A. D — There until near 9 — Then to studio. Fussed & fixed until 11 o'clk. Then to Hl. S. & to bed about 11.30. ## Right Page

### December, Saturday 2, 1876

Somewhat overcast and milder. Got to studio at 9.45 and to work on small drawings until lunch time — two or three callers kept me so that it was 1.30 before I got to Hl. S. Back at 2.15 and during P.M. commenced work on upright W.C. study of Newtown Creek. Dusk got out material for the mounting of drawings & to tea at 6 — Dressed before tea — At 7.30 went to 2° Thos. Symphony con. in a Regular Snow squall. Took Emma J. home in a bright moonlight — Got to bed soon after 11.
