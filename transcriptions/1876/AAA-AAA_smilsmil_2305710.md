## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 29, 1876

Beautiful day — got to studio at 9.15 & after some delay got to work charcoaling on an upright subject for W.C. "Cows coming to the milking" — based upon studies got at Po'Keepsie — worked until 1 — to lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — at 3.30 a model came & I made a slight sketch from her for milk maid. To tea at 6.30 — Back to studio at 8.30 — wrote until about 10. To 41 "8." at 10.45 — to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 30, 1876

Thanksgiving day. Overcast & exceedingly chilly — Up late & did not get to studio until about 10. Spent most of the mng. fussing over drawing of cow in sketch & upright W.C. drawing — went to Mamy's to dinner at 1.15 — left the table at 3.15 and right to studio — worked with water-color on small sketch made in cedar woods near Po'keepsie more than a year ago. About 5, lay down on sofa & rested until 6. Went to 41 28. — had a light tea & at 8 went to N.A. f.D. to see the J.S. Johnston pictures — there until 9.30 — then to studio — there until a few minutes after 10. — thro to 41 "8." & to bed at 10.30
