## Left Page

### March, Friday 10, 1876

Overcast mf. & my head overcast too. Got to studio at
10 & kindled fire, but good for nothing. Beles & Jenny S. —
called. At 1 I gave up and went to 41 S. & to bed where
I had one of my wildest nervous headaches. Very
mad because at Gordon's, last night, I ate & drank
nothing. I could not have had a worse headache if I
had enjoyed everything eatable & drinkable instead of
being such a pattern of abstemious virtue.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 11, 1876

Pleasant day. Got up feeling very slim — got to studio at 9.30
& found my model, Mousees, had been waiting for me for half an hour.
Set Palette & at 10.30 got to work on "Rough Spot". Painted until
12 — To dinner at 1, back at 2 —
at 2.30 M. came again & I painted from him for an hour
or two. W. Burch Lewis came in for a few minutes — on his way
to train for May. To tea at 6.30
at 8.30 at Avery's — Atq. he, his son & I went to R. L. Stuart's
5 Ave. & 20 S. There until 10.45 looking over his pictures. He &
Mrs. S. very pleasant. To studio at 11 — cleaned palette & brushes,
fixed fire & to 41 S. at 11.30 &
to bed at 12.
