## Left Page

### February, Wednesday 23, 1876

Very cold day with tremendous high winds. Got to studio at 9.30 & to work, after two or three callers, on foreground of "Rough Sport" — painting all over it solidly. At 1 it went to Knordleis to see the pictures, then to Kurtz's. Then with Mrs. Jacobs & Nellie until 2.30, Nellie having photo in costume — then to lunch. Met W. & Aunt A. got to P. at 4 o'clk, learned of the death of Mother's sister Apphia — back to studio at 3.15 & painted until 5.30. To tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to Mary's & saw Matt & Kate, who came down to-day. At 9.30 went to ha. Then to studio — as usual it was nearly 11.30 before I got to bed.

## Right Page

### February, Thursday 24, 1876

Still clear, windy and cold, but moderating. Got to studio at 9.20 & to work on "Rough Sport" — Dragged over the foreground & bothered over some middle distance foliage in it. To dinner at 1.15 — dressed & again at studio soon after 3 — worked. Remainder of p.m. on horses. To tea at 6.30 — To studio at 8.30 — to Emma D's at near 9. Musical entertainment — Mosenthal on violin, Bergner on Violoncello & Mrs. Saml. Colman & Annie C. & Emma D. on piano — trios. Hayden, Beethoven & Mendelssohn. Very enjoyable. At 9.30 went to N.a.; back at 10, music over soon after 11 — after which when I left with the K's & Miss Stone, went in to the K's — 12.40 when I got to 4[illegible]. [illegible] when I got to bed.
