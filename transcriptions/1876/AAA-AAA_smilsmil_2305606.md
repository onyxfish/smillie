## Left Page

### April, Saturday 29, 1876

Charming day — got to Studio about 9 o'clk. Renner + men had been there moving things since 5.30 a.m. Horrible confusion + tremendous hard work, to say nothing of dirt. Back + forth between the two buildings, loading + unloading. To dinner at 1 — back at 2.30 + worked on until 4.30 — nearly 8 when I got my tea utterly worn out — got to bed before 10 o'clk —

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 30, 1876

Did it get out of my bed until 6.30 p.m. — then up; dressed + scrubbed + got a good tea about 7.30 — read a little until near 10 — then packed things to wind up at the Dining's + got to bed again at 11.30
