## Left Page

### April, Friday 21, 1876

Charming day — nearly 10 when
I got to studios — all the m[orning]
spent running around, to
new building etc — & after
Greene from whom I got my
check for High Sierras $583.25
finally, to dinner at 2 o'clk
— Dressed — to studio at 3.30
& at 4.30 went down to W-
Mr. S. Johnston's Artists Rep.
Mr. G[illegible] W. & W. S. Richards then
saw lots of folks — Got to tea
at 6.30 — Well, entirely worn
out — went to Mary's for awhile
— then to Burgralf's — then to
41 S. & at 9.30, to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Saturday 22, 1876

Pleasant day — Strong S.E. wind — After
going over to 3d Ave. after Douglas — then
to new building, got to studio at 10.30
Spent the rest of m[orning] arranging
small cards for the hanging of the
Cen. pictures — To lunch at 1.20 and
back to studio at 2.15 — Mr. Sam.
C. Bella & Mary J. & Miss Edwards
called about 11 a.m. Gel. was
then at studio. Most of the p.m.
was spent trying to arrange
the hanging of the W.C. — To tea
at 6.30 — at 8 at Steinway Hall —
6th & last Thomas Concert. Enjoyed
it exceedingly — walked home
with Emma J. & Mrs. Spring — then
to 41 48 — & to bed at 11.30.
