## Left Page

### July, Friday 14, 1876

Still intensely hot — In the mng. got started on Georg's drawing of "Sentinel Rock" for W. Seeves — I made very great changes in distribution of light to mass & simplify the whole. Dinner at 12.30 — Again to work, but about 3 oclk had to give up, so dark owning to a coming storm — heavy wind & Rain from 3.30 until 5.30 — extremely refreshing. Then again at drawing — Tea at 6 oclk — after tea again at work — Every. delightfully cool — Took a short walk & got to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 15, 1876

Delicious day — warm, but with charming breeze from the N.W. Father being out all the mng. I worked with graver on his die of "Gaucho" cutting on the horse's head. Enjoyed the work very much. Dinner at 12.30 — P.M. finished up Georg's "Sentinel Rock" making a very nice thing of it indeed. Then worked on my "Cathedral Spires" adding some new work. & finally at 4.30 I pronounced it done. Then dressed and sauntered awhile in Eastman Park & at 6.10 went with Ge? to W. Williams where, by invitation me met at tea, Miss Ward of Harlem — a very pleasant evng until after 10 — then home & to bed at 11. Mother still improving.
