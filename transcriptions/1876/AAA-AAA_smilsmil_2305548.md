## Left Page

### January, Sunday 2. 1876.

Up about 7.30 — Exercised with
clubs — Took bath + a Rub down
with hair gloves — thru Hkfs
at 9. At 10.30 went to D. Hastings
The motto text for this year is
Mark. 5th Ch. 36th v. "only believe" —
— last year it was "loving kindness"
& the year before "tender mercies"
The sermon was excellent — After
service went to Mary's — there to
dinner. Weather very warm but
treacherous — At 3 went to studio,
note to Geo — Lenheim & Sprout.
Showers of Rain — About 6
to tea in 41st S. Every [illegible] wrote
a note to Miss Alling and
a long letter to Augero —
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 3. 1876.

A very pleasant day, at last,
got to studio at 9.20. Kindled
fire & had other interferences
so that it was 10.30 before I
began work on my big drawing.
To dinner at 1.30 — back at
2.40 and worked on black
horse until about 4.40 —
I commenced also a little
W.C. Sketch to show Miss H
how to work out her W.C.
picture — To tea at 6.30 — At
7.45 called at Judge Howland's
notion (depr[illegible] on Ave. & 37th S. —)
then to the Redfield's — (44th S.
& 7th Ave.) not in — then walked
down to Leila Martens, 21st S. &
7th ave — a very pleasant evg with
her & her friends Mr. & Mrs. Walcott
& Harry Gray & sister — To 41st S. —
at 11 o'clk & Right to bed.
