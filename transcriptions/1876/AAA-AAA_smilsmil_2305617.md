## Left Page

### May, Sunday 21, 1876

Very hot day. Up at 8 & got my exercise — hair brush & rub & my bath & breakfast all before 10 o'clk. Then to Dr. Hastings's & heard him pr. from Matt. 23° ch. 23°. v.— After service walked down to Studio — then again to 41 St. then a car up to Uncle A's. Dinner about 2 — the Eldest Newhall & young Hall there. About 4.30 went to Will's — As a thunderstorm threatened & I had left my studio windows open I hurried down there & about to went in to [illegible] J's. Miss Howe there — Took tea and spent the evef. until after 10. Heavy thunderstorms all around & steady, gentle Rain for 2 or 3 hours. To 41 St. at 10.40 & to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### May, Monday 22, 1876

Very hot day — Got to Studio at 10. Had the last of carpentering & staining done — To lunch at 1 — At 2 at Kurtz's & sat for more photos. It was after 3 when again I got to Studio — There until 6.30 when I ran to get ahead of a thunder storm — Rained furiously while I was in a car, & held up until I got indoors. Tea at 7. All the evef. in my room turning over contents of trunk — closets etc. — Rain until after 10. To bed about 11.
