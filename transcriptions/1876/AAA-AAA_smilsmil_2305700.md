## Left Page

### November, Thursday 9, 1876

Charming day — Got to
studio at 9. Douglas & bro.
there — I spent the m'g
in my closets. Bellens
called at 12.30 — then had
to superintend the mixing
of more wall color — so
that it was near 2 when
I got to dinner, then found
that father had been there.
After dinner, at 2.15 went
to Mary's & saw father, walked
to R.R. Station with him —
He left for Po. in 3.30 train.
I went to studio — there
until 5.30 — Tea at 6.30.
at 8, to Renner's — at 9
to Mary's — there until
10.30 then to 41 [illegible] St. &
to bed [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### November, Friday 10, 1876

Cold Raw m'g — Commenced
to rain about 11 — quite heavy
until middle of P.M. Arg
went to Wilmurt's to see
about the W'f. pictures at
Phila. — There to Curry's, &
there, about 11 to studio —
To lunch at 1, after that
dressed & got to studio
at 3 o'clk. Worked about
studio until after 5 — then
to Hoffman Ho. to see Ella
& Mary Diven — not in — at
6 went to Colmaris to
dinner & met Mr. DeGracia
& Miss Wilde — C. sick in bed
there until 10.15 — then to
41 [illegible] St. & to bed at 11.15.
Started Renner this m'g
off to Phila. at midnight
to get Water Color's from
Centennial Exh'n.
