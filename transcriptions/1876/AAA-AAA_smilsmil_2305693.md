## Left Page

### October, Thursday 26, 1876

Pleasant mt. About 9.30 went to Mr. W's office — Then met Mr. Wm Hamilton of Rausp, & we all went out to 9 m town to spent the mt with Richards looking over his new works. At 12.10 Rid lunch at home at 1 o'clk. After that, Reading & then a nap — At 3 went to Penn. Ac of fine arts — much interested in the museum call of Early Pilgrim & [illegible] interest Back at 5.30 — "High Sea" at 7.30 — quite a number of friends invited. Evn passed in general chattification. To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 27, 1876

Clear, cold mt. At 9 Rode up to 24th — Soon clouded over & was bleak & raw — I fought with a miserable head ache — Spent the mt in the agriculturals, Brewer's & Dairy-man's Halls — at 12.30 lunched. called on Sartain — not in. then, soon after I went to Japan & met Geo & Belle, Mary & Florence Sweedy — with them until 2.30 when I was obliged to give up to my headache & with [illegible] — I Rid to the city in 5 & car. I tumbled at once into bed & lay there until 8 — Richards & wife & Miss F. Bridges to teas. They left at 8.30 — Eight or nine other callers — until after 10.30 — Then to my Room — wrote until 11.30 — then to bed.
