## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 15, 1876

another horrible day — Rain
& winds — Got to studio at 9.30
Set palette & made preparations
to paint on "Swerf among Sierras"
but it was so dark that I gave
it up & set to work on sky &
background of "Rough Sport" —
Did not get along well — To
dinner at 1.45 — back at 2.20 —
then Coleman came in and
gave me a lift. Soon after
Wilson & Markham came and
we talked over new studio &
I finally agreed to take it at
$450.00 Mrs. Jacobs came & I
had callers until dark — To tea
at 6.45 — At 7.30 saw mother
at Mam's & at 8.30, at studio
there until near 11 — after
my usual visit to the M. a
to bed about 11.15.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 16, 1876

fine day, but windy & cold —
Got to studio at 9.30 & soon
to work on "Swerf among the
Sierras" — got into a Regular
mess trying to doctor the sky.
Finally Repainted it & did badly
but the color dried so that
I could not get it even — To
dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.15 &
worked over distant mes —
Repainting them as I did
the sky. At 4.45, at 625
Bdway, State Centennial Com.
meeting — near 6, walked part
way up town with J.F.S. Ward.
called on Danl. Huntington — not
in — Saw Mrs. H. To tea at 6.40 —
went to Mam's & saw mother —
to studio at 8.30 — to N.A. at
10 back, wrote 2 or 3 letters —
to 41 S. at 11 & to bed at
11.20.
