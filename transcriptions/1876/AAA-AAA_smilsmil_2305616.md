## Left Page

### May, Friday 19, 1876

Very warm clear day — Got
up at 9.30 with something of
a headache. Got to Kurtz's
at 11 — & made appointment
with him for to-morrow mng
at 10 — then to studio — Fussed
there until after 1 o'clk. Rev.
W. Loomis of Po'keepsie came in.
So dinner at 2 — back at
3.15 — Youngman staining
woodwork & plasterer at work.
Had calls from Missis Woodword
& Blauvelt. To tea after 7,
At 8.30 called on W. J. S. Williams
18 W.36 & chatted with him until
10 o'clk. Then to 41 [illegible] & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Saturday 20, 1876

Charming day — Got to studio about
9.30 — Swain Gifford came in. At 10
went to Kurtz's & sat for photo. Then
again to studio — There until 12.
30, then to Saml. Coleman's to
lunch — Mrs. C. gone to Brooklyn
S.S. & I had an old batchelor
lunch. At 2.45 stopped at K.
& saw pf. of my photo — then
to studio — there until 7.30
just fussing in the midst of
dirt & confusion trying to assort
things, while the men were
staining the woodwork & finishing
the carpentering. Tea at 8.
Over. Read newspapers
until after — got to bed about
11.
