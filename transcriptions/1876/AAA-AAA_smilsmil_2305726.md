## Left Page

### December, Sunday 31, 1876

Beautiful winter day. Went to Dr. Hastings & heard
a capital sermon on the words
1" Peter, 1"ch. 7" v. After
service, walked down the
Ave. with Mr. & Mrs. E. Anthony
To studios, fixed fire — then
to Mamy's at 30 & had diner
at 3.30 went up to the Park
to walk — charming — at 4.30
to Wille's — at 5.30 walked
down to studios, getting thin
at 6 — went in to Preston
Spring's & had my tea —
Emma & party that went to
Ned Cook's wedding, snowed
in on Erie RR, 350 miles from
NY. — Back to studio at
8.15. wrote to father. Bell
J. & d.c.f. at 10.30 to 41
J. & to bed at 11.15
