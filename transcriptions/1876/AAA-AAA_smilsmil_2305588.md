## Left Page

### March, Friday 24. 1876.

Very nice & pleasant in mf. at noon overcast & threatening chng.
Got to studio at 9.20 — Set palette & to work in color on sketch for
A.B.N. Co. f the Old Liberty Bell.
To dinner at 1.45 — back at 2.50.
D. H.W.P. Taylor & Livingston. f
Newark called — several attons
but by working until 6, I got
sketch finished — To tea at 7.
Summa B — there — at 8 called
on J. T. Williams, 18 W. $6 for
centennial pictures & got them.
Very pleasant call — at 9, to
studio, made a letter or two,
then to 41 4 St & to bed soon
after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Saturday 25. 1876.

Horrible day, pouring rain and
driving wind. Did not get up —
in bed all day with head ache
"missed" Varnishing Day" and
Thomas's Symphony Concert at
night — Up at 8.30 & about 9
had a bowl of gruel. To bed
again at midnight.
