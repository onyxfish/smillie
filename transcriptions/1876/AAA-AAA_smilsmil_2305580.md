## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 8, 1876

Charming day — Father came to the house before I finished
left. He went down town & at 9.20 I was at studio — Got to
work on "High S'ierras". Rubbing
fussing & scumbling over the
sky & distant mountains —
To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30
& the rest of P. M. spent in
painful fussing over sky & mts.
To tea at 6.30, Father walking
up with me & taking tea at
During's. At 8 I dressed & at
8.30 at Miss Burdell's in 42°
St. to meet Mr. Browning —
there until 10.30 — then to 41°
St. & to bed soon after 11.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 9, 1876

Superb day. Got to studio at 8.30
McDonald (model) waiting for me,
Father came in before I got to work.
I painted in "Rough Sport" from Mr. D.
until 10 o'clk — then he left & I worked
on other parts. To dinner at 1.30 —
back at 2.30 — Lots of callers. Amy
K, Anna C, "Sis" H — Miss Marshall
also Josie Gillelan (Toni's wife.) At
4 Mr. D. came again & I painted
from him until after 5. Father
came in & we walked up together.
To 41st to tea at 6.30 — At 8.30
at studio, then to Wm. Robt. Gordon's
T. W. 38th St. a handsome entertainment
to artists & others — there until
11.30 — then to 41st St. & to bed shortly
after 12.
