## Left Page

### September, Saturday 2, 1876

Superb, cold day with strong
wind from the North — I got
to work on 9×16 & painted
until 1, then a little exercise
out of doors until 1.45, then
dinner — Mr. d'Esby there —
P.M. painted until after
5, getting subject all in —
Maud had taken S. to tortoise-
heads & as I went out to walk
had just Ret'd, so I took a short
ride with her. Tea at 6.45
Gen. D. Ret'd from Albany —
Every had Euchre again —
lost 8 games, won 4 — To bed
at 10.30.

## Right Page

### September, Sunday 3, 1876

Beautiful day — B'kf't at 8 —
then walked for some time
for exercise — Read awhile in
"Darwin's Descent of Man" then
wrote to Nicoll concerning H's
Blackburn's offer to the A.S. &c — in
W.C. to send another English
album over to them — Dinner at
2. A little more writing after
dinner & about 5 started on
a climb up the hill side —
went above & beyond the quarries
then back, below them, getting
in at 7 — Tea about 7.30 —
Chatting at table until
8.30 — Then wrote a letter
to Will & got to bed at 10.
