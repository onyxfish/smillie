## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 18. 1876.

Mean, muggy sort of a day beginning with rain — got to studio a little after 9 — gave a little time to Miss Abbatt — then to my own work on figures on "Extreme Rich." of big drawing — worked on them all of the morn. Robbins came in abt — got to lunch at 1.35 — back at 2.20 — Met Ben Mulford on the way & he went up with me — Bellows called all the p.m. on general work finishing — At 5.45 after a call from Swain Gifford went to Avery's about drawings — then to 41 8; at 6.35 to tea — Found Father there — at 8.30 — again at studio — went to Private View Artist's Fund at 9 — Saw lots of fellows & got to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 19. 1876.

Horrible rainy day — father came for me a little before 9 & we went to studio together where he saw for the first time my big drawing — Misses Isham & Olmstead came in & then Fan & Ben W. & Mrs. Wm. Jessup. So many callers that I got nothing done — To dinner abt 30 — back at 2.15 — Tried to work on sky but it grew so dark that I did not dare — Miss Taylor called — at 4.30 went to N.A. at 6 — at Mr. O.D. Munn's — 14 & 21st — got a loan of a picture for $[illegible] — To tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 at N.A. where the B'd of Control met to pass upon pictures — a great no. offend — general disposition to reject even the work of members — I advised caution & much that had been done was reconsidered — almost 11 before I got away & 11.45 when I got to bed.
