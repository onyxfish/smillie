## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 19, 1876

Still cooler & breezy but pleasant — got to studio at 9.10
& finished getting Scrub Race into't frame. Then W. J. J. Wms
came in — Stayed until 11 —
looked over lots of my studies
& finally gave me a general
sort of commission to paint
for him a yosemite picture —
fussed around until 1.15
then to dinner — at 2.30 at
625 B'dway. Met [illegible] then
cored in — see the drawings because
they were packed — Then to [illegible]
Gallery — Saw Blodgett Pictures —
there with Mr. & Mrs. Coleman went
to new Studio & [illegible]. Then to Silledis
& studio & to tea at 6.45 — Spent
some time in at Rennie's — then to
Mary's & to studio at 9.20 — very
tired — visited, besides a little
writing — nearly 11.30 when
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 20, 1876

Pleasant little shower early in
the m'f, then all clear — got to
Studio soon after 9 & to work on
heads of horse & lion for A.B.N. Co.
Many interruptions as things are
now being generally pulled up
in the studios & every body is
unsettled or going. To dinner
at 12.30 — back at 2.30 — Mr. J[illegible].
Whitney called while I was in
Pratt's Room — did not see him —
I should have said that I dined
at Colman's — Emma J — there, we
had music & meat. Finished my
heads & at 6 went to Albemarle &
dined with Mr. W — then to Keutz's
& N.A. Got caught in a shower —
left him at 9.30 — went to studio —
to 44th St at 10.30 — to bed about 11.
