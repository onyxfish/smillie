## Right Page

### December, Monday 18, 1876

A horrible day of Snow +
Sleet — Got to Studios at 10
to work on Charcoal
study of "Under the Butternuts"
Prepared my lunch at 1.30
worked as long as I could
see, finally finishing Charcoal
study — At 4.30 S. Colman,
J.G. Brown, A.F. Bellows, H.
Robbins + J.C. Nicoll came
the entire B.° of Central, ex-
cept F.H. Smith. We discussed
the matter of salesman
until 6 when we adjourned
without decision — To tea at
[illegible] + found father waiting for
me — he + Ge[?] having come
from B[?] this P.M. At 8.15 I
went for Fan + we then went
to the Aquarium — The storm
had ceased but I had a
miserable Sore Throat + Cold.
To bed about 11 o'clk. [illegible]
