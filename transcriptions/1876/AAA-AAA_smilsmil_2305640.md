## Left Page

### July, Monday 10, 1876

Still scorching hot —
Did not go out for any
exercise, but got to work
on Yosemite drawing
upon which I made
very fair progress. Mr.
Williams called and
paid me for little pictu[re].
Dinner at 12.30 — worked
until after 5 — Tea at
6.30 — then got out hose
& played all thru every
back & front. Heavy
thunder storms north
& south of us, but no
Rain reached us —
Emma B. & Carl D. —
spent every with us.
To bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Tuesday 11, 1876

Intensely hot. Thunder storms
north & south of us, their shadow
somewhat mitigating the heat.
I was so much used up with
nervous headache that I could
not work — Did about two
hours work in the morning
then was obliged to give
up for the day — Dinner
at 12.30. Uncle W. & Carl D.
called — On my bed until
near 4 — Minn. arr'd from
N.Y. about 6 o'clk — and
almost immediately after
her a very heavy thunder
storm that gave us a good
ducking — Tea at 6.30 —
much of every spent in
big chair at my window
listening to singing up at
Mr. Williams's — To bed
at 11.30.
