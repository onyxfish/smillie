## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 7, 1876

Dark, rainy day — got to
studio at 9.30. Worked thin
all the m'f. Had part of
wall pulled out to build
a flue that I may put up a
stove. Color-men did not
come to fix wall. To dinnr
at 1 — back at 2.30 —
Beutson & boy worked from
3 to 5 on my stove. At
6 o'clk. to tea — Read
Post until near 8 — then
to Mam's — In Charl's
Room — Miles Palmer &
        came in &
stayed until 10.30 — then
out to get election returns
altho' it was raining hard.
I went to 41 ½ S[t]. & to
my bed, at about 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 8, 1876

Overcast, mild day — Got
to studio at 9.30. Douglas
& bro' came & they worked
until dark coloring the
walls — I went to dinner
at 1 o'clk — back at 2.15.
Beutson & boy spent the
p.m. with my stove which
got a thorough overhauling
& refitting. I need a
lamp for them to see
to polish until 6.30 —
near 7 when I got to tea.
At 8 went to Mam's. Found
that Father had passed
through the city & would
spend the night in Plainfield. I sat in Charl's
Room & Read "Helen's
Babies" until after 10 —
Then to 41 ½ S[t]. & to bed.
During went to Phila this m'f
bought a Butterfly & Ret[urne]d
this even[ing]. [illegible] went to Po[?]
at 3.30 P.m.
