## Left Page

### March, Tuesday 28, 1876

Up late — Did not get to shop until 10 o'clk — Dull, dark day & finally Rain — Worked faithfully over Bell of Juberry for A.B.N. Co — Repainting — To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.15 & worked as long as I could see — So Tea at 6.30 — Pouring Rain — at 8 o'clk. went down to Informal meeting of W.C. Socy — Horrible night — yet in spite of it, had a very full & pleasant meeting. Bayard Taylor there — in very good humor — enjoyed his easy, chatty, lecturing way exceedingly — To 41.S— at 11.45 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 29, 1876

Breezy again & quite cool — got to studio at 9 — Tried to get to work on Horse's Head, but felt so unlike it that I charcoaled Lion's Head — Then, at 11 went over to new studio — then to N.A. of D. — There until 12.30 then again to studio — met there Misses Aitken & Kemp, begging for a fair — at 1.30 at Nicale's who is going to Phila this P.M. & at 1.45 to dinner at 41. S— back to studio at 2.45 — Worked a while on little W.C. sketch & at 4.30 went to 625 B'way. No meeting of Committee — at 6.15 at Kimball's to tea — Matt & Kate there — at 8.30 again at studio — Wrote until after 10 — to 41. S. at 10.45 and to bed at 11. [illegible — entire passage]
