## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 26, 1876

Glorious, clear, crisp day, but
I don't respond — up too late
last night. Got to studio at
9.30 — T.H. Smith came in — went
with him to N.A. Hanging not
yet quite finished — then to the
new bldg going up cor. of 14 Ave &
25th St. & about noon to Studio —
to dinner at 1.15 — back at 2 —
eyes hot & smarting — could not
work — Architect of 4th ave. bldg
came to see me — some time with
him — Then Corinne C. Smith
called — then writing — to tea
at 6.40 — back to studio about
8 & wrote until 9.30 Then
to 41st St, & to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 27, 1876

Rains in the morning — late
again — almost 10 o'clk when
I got to studio. A number of
little things to be done in
Water Color affairs. Got out a
canvas 20×24 & commenced
to charcoal "Rough Spot in
the Yosemite" — about 1.30 made
a cup of tea & lunched in Studio.
Dark & wet afternoon — accomplish
but little — at 4.45 went over
to N.A. The Hanging committee
engaged decorating. Took tickets
to Students League — saw some
of the young folks — at 5.50 at Avery's
to see him on centennial business
— to 41st St. at 6.45 — at 8.15 at
Studio — correcting pps. of W.C.
catalogue until after 11 —
Miss Woodward helping
me — then to 41st St. & to bed
at 11.45 [illegible — ~3 words]
