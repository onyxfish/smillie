## Left Page

### February, Monday 7, 1876

Mild day, mostly overcast —
Got to studio at 10 o'clk — a
badly begun week. Then to
work on "Rough Sport" and
painted in back ground figure
on the Right hand — To dinner
at 1.15 — back at 2.15 and
painted until about 5 —
Siloa came in & at 6.15 we
walked up town together —
Tea at 6.30 at 8.45 went to
N.A. There until near 10 —
Then to studio — To 4[illegible] S.
at 11 & to bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 8, 1876

Very mild & spring-like.
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on "Rough Sport" — Painted in
dark horse in foreground — To
dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.15
& Painted until near 4 — then
down to 625 B'o way & met
the Centennial combination
Committee & presented the
case of the Water Color Society
— all difficulties were soon
cleared away — the Society to
do as much of the work as it
pleases, expenses to be paid
by the state. To tea at 6.40
at 7.30 at 7, H. Smillie's — There
until 9.15 — Then to N.A. at
10 — to studio — at 11. to 4[illegible] S.
& to bed at 11.15
