## Left Page

### July, Friday 28, 1876

Pleasant day with one or two English-like spatters of Rain. The mrf was got rid of very easely with Sherman's Memoirs and chat & play. Dinner at 1.30. The afternoon disappeared as did the mrf without leaving a trace behind. Tea soon after 6. Threats of Rain as there had been during the day. Evef, Cab; May, & — went down to Aleck's where we spent the evef with Whist & a supper. Got home about 11 & — & to bed at 11. [illegible — ~3 lines]

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 29, 1876

Cloudy & threatening Rain, much of the mrf was lost in play. I arranged my plans to make Fabeels for pictures in W.C. Delpi Cen. Egp. & wrote to Reimer for cards. Dinner about 1 o'clk. Much of afternoon spent playing croquet with Maud & Cab. Weather clearing pleasantly. Much talk of work but nothing done. Tea between 6 & 7. Evef, Geo. Maud & the Cab. Rode in town & called upon Mrs. Balcom, Grannis and Jan M. who arrived to day from Montrose. I stayed home & played Cal. Jack with May & Ella. It was after 11, nearly half past when I got to bed.
