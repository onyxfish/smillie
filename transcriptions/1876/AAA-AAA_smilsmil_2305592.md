## Left Page

### April, Saturday 1. 1876.

Horribly windy, dirty day —
9.30 when I got to studio.
To work on little W.C. sketch
"on Conway Meadows" until
12. To lunch at Mary's at
1. She goes to Po' this P.M.
with the children. Matt,
Kate stays, used up with a
sick headache. After lunch
Rode down to 625 B'way to
look after drawings — Then to
studio at 4 — Several callers,
among them, Mrs. Colman,
Sally Kittredge, Miss Sartain
etc — Finished W.C. sketch.
To tea at 6.45 — ordered frames
at Rennie's & boots at Burgraff's
& got to studio at 9.30 — made
three letters — to 41 S— at
10.45 & to bed at 1.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 2. 1876.

Glorious day — perfect weather —
needed — Up at 8.30 — B'kf't at
9.30 & at 11 called on Maud D.
at 10 W 57th St — found her so used
up with neuralgia that she could
not go with me to Staten Island.
I then sauntered through the Park
& at 11.45 took Elevated R.R. at 59th
& 9th ave. got to Battery at 12.10
then to S.I. ferry at 12.30 — met Dr.
Kelsey on the boat — got to Mr.
Jessup's at about 1.15 & had dinner
about 2 — After dinner walked with
Mr. J. back to house at 5 — left
with Dr. K. at 5.30 — got to N.Y. at 6.10
up to 41 S. about 7 — tea & at
8 o'clk. went to Young Syng's. Here
until 9.45 — quite an old fashioned
methodist Revival — then to 41 S
& to bed at 10.30
