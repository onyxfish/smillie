## Left Page

### February, Saturday 5, 1876

Bright cold winter day —
Hard work to get up — Did
not get to studio until 10 —
Then to work painting on
group of horses in "Rough
Sport" — To lunch abt 10 —
back at 2.15 — & painted
until near 5, getting in
three of my horses — At
6 went with Colman to
dinner — Mrs. C — not at home —
At 8 again at studio, about
9 went to N.A. & at 10 down to
Century club — There until
after 11 o'clk. Made arrangements
to have my name put up for
membership — again at studio
— did a little writing — to 41st St. at
12.15 & to bed at 12.30.

## Right Page

### February, Sunday 6, 1876

Overcast & rather mild — Did
not get down to [illegible] until
11.30 — At 12.45 at studio and
at 1.20 at Corinne C. Smith's —
Dinner at 2 — a pleasant
chatty time until 4.30 — Then
to studio, made a couple of
notes & at 6 went to Kimball's
— Rain — Took tea there and
at 8 again at studio — made
to Collins & home & at 9.45
went to 41st St. & at 10.30 to
bed.
