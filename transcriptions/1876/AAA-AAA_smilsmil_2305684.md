## Left Page

### October, Sunday 8, 1876

Cold + bleak. At 9.45 went up to Studio — Then to Presbn Ch. + heard Mr. Miller preach from Jer. 6th ch. 16th v. Again to Studio there until Dinner at 2 at 3 again at Studio. wrote a long letter to father. Tea at 6 — Even with all went to Presbn Ch. + again heard Miller preach from Eccl. 12th ch. v. After service bid Kitty Beutty Schaeffer goodbye — the rest of evng chatting by [illegible] fine side — To bed sometime after 10.

## Right Page

### October, Monday 9, 1876

Brilliant, almost cloudless day — At 9 o'clk. at W. Smith's (dentist) He worked on my mouth for half an hour — Then attended to some messages + got to Studio at 10.30 — To work on "October" + went over all the sky + distance improving it very much — Dinner at 1 o'clk. — worked until 5.30 — Then picked up stones for half an hour. Then tea — At 8 o'clk. I went to Judge Jessup's + spent the evng with Fanny J. + her mother. To bed at 10.30.
