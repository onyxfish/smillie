## Left Page

### October, Thursday 12, 1876

Charming, cloudless Oct.
day. At 8.30 at Dentist
Smith's — there until near
11 — had three fillings, two
of them hard to get at — my
painful. There to Barber's
& had my hair cut — then
at 12, up on the hill. — To
Dinner at 12.45 — Mr. Hand
Ret'd to her home at Honesdale
immediately after. Went to
my "farm" & wandered over
it for nearly an hour, then
in to shop, but before I got to
work Rev. W. Nulker came &
had a long metaphysics — theo
logico-scientific talk — at 4
out to pick up stones — then Tom
I came. At 5 again picking up
stones — to tea at 6 — Studio at 8
Then down town for mail — at
8.45 at A. Nathanple's & played
whist with Flora, Ella & A. &
until 10.20 — then to bed.
Aunt H. Ret'd from N.Y. this eve.

## Right Page

### October, Friday 13, 1876

Another glorious day — At
5.30, on the hill — before 9
man with plough came, two
Samuel Sayre, & under his di-
rection ploughing was commenced
on new land — Most of the
m'g was spent loafing over
the ground & soaking in
the glories of the day. Dinner
at 1 — again to studio & made
sketch on 10×16 canvas of view
out of South window. At 3 went
to the house & at 3.20 Geo & I
went out riding with Mr. Jo.
Comstock — out until 5.40 —
Tea soon after 6 — then called
on aunt H. — went to studio and
fixed fine — then down to P.O.
then to Rev. W. Willis's
& also to Mr. Hurd's & Miss
Dunham — there until
near 10.30 — To bed at 11.
