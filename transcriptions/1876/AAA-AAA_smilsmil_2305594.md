## Left Page

### April, Wednesday 5, 1876.

Windy, dusty, day — Got
to studio at 9.30 Did
some work on &cm& Ran
in the way of Tracing
& Retracing White Horse,
To dinner at 1.15 — back
at 2.30 — Painted over
portions of Gaucho. for
A.B.N.Co At 4.30
went to 625 Bdwy No
Com. meeting, but saw
Jno. T. Johnston & Leslie.
Worked over water colors
awhile — then to lea at
6.30 — Swery, called at
Millbanks & at Ripley's
on W.C. business — To studio
about 9 — wrote until near
11 — then to 41st S. — & to bed
at 1.30.

## Right Page

### April, Thursday 6, 1876.

Delicious day, but when I
got up, found I was "in" for
a headache — Got to studio at
9.30 — wrote two or three letters,
then went over to new studio
then to U.C.A, & finally, at 1,
went to 41 [illegible]. & to bed where
I stayed until to-morrow
mrf. Tom Moran — Mrs.
Sharpless. S.C.T. & others
called.
