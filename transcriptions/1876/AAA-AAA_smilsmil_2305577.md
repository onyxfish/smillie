## Left Page

### March, Thursday 2, 1876

Up before 7 — bkfst at 7.30 & at 8.15 was at Mr. Jas. Fraser's — 26 W. 57th St — We went together to Dr. Hall's, cor. 5th Ave. & 55th St — then met Düning & Mr. Mc Intyre — D. played on the organ until 9.45 — Then I walked down to studio getting there at 10.15 — by this time, bright, windy weather. Got to work on "Rough Sport" & spent Remainder of day in fussing over little bits, trying to correct drawing etc — Dinnr at 1 — back at 2 — Tea at 6.30 At 8, again at Studio with Rennert & together we packed Ferguson's two boxes of stuff. Through at 9.30 — To 41st S— at 10.30 & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Friday 3, 1876

Very fine day — 10 o'clk. when I got to studio. To work on sky of "Sierras" — Cored in, yet it to work right — Poor & blue & cold — everything mussed over & taxed — At 12.45 Marcheau came & with Colman we went to the new building to determine about ceilings etc. To dinner at 1.45 — back at 2.30 — worked down over distant mts — all alike bad. But littles work done during the day. & that of the most discouraging sort. To tea at 6.40 — dressed & at 8.30, called on Mr. Sharpless, 141 E. 37th St — not in — then on Miss Lib Howe, 4th Ave. & 22nd St — there until 10 — Then to Studio, there until 10.50 — Then to 41st S: & to bed at 11.30
