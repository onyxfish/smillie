## Left Page

### August, Saturday 5, 1876

Exceedingly hot day — about 8.40 got to work in out field on oil study outlined some days ago. Painted until after 12, then feeling very much steered, went into the house & rested awhile before dinner soon after 1. Then read news paper for awhile then out to milking yard & worked from the cows who were extremely uneasy owing to heat & flies — in some of the 4 & worked awhile on my drawings. Nearly 6.30 when we had tea. Eveng. spent on piazza until 10.30 — Eugene & wife called — got to bed at 11 feeling indefinably out of sorts.

## Right Page

### August, Sunday 6, 1876

Wakened at 5 o'clk. Had quite a chill that lasted for half an hour — then ½ an hour of fever followed by dripping perspiration. My head hurt & I felt very much discouraged fearing an ague. I took a light [illegible] in bed & finally got up about 11 o'clk. Felt very weak but hope that it is only a bilious attack induced by my broiling in the sun & getting my stomach out of order. Thunderstorms all about us about 1 clk. A light rain fell on us. Splendid thunder. Dined at 2.30 — the p.m. being pleasantly cloudy was spent on piazza. Eveng. deck. Gene & children (also wives) to tea — It was quite 11 o'clk. when I got to bed.
