## Left Page

### September, Wednesday 6, 1876

Charming day. P[illegible]: about
8.30 — a little before 10. The
Genl. started out driving
taking Mr. & Mrs. S. & Mr. Mc. G.
I followed in phaeton with
Miss Mc. G. Drove through
Eldridge Park & down to Mr.
Langdon's. There we had a
very pleasant time & went
all over the magnificent ho.
at 11.30 went to depot with
the Genl. & Mrs. & Miss Mc. G. —
Ret'd to Mr. H's for phaeton
thru to Genl. D. & dinner at
2. Headachy p.m. About
4 Cap't. May, Geo. & Ella went
in carriage, Maud & I in
phaeton to call on Judge Gray.
Stopped at Aleck's on way home.
Tea at 6.30. Evng. Genl. D. came
up with mfr — he, with Jack
& the girls had a romping
time — A little euchre & a
little whist. To bed at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Thursday 7, 1876

Cold, overcast day. About 10
went out with pencil & paper
& spent the m'f in meadow
making outline sketches.
Came in at 12.30 quite
chilled. Dinner at 1.45.
At 3 again went out & up
on the hill & spent 2 hours
getting outline sketch of
Elmira. In at 5.30. Rolled
in blanket on bed until
6.30 — near 7 when we had
tea. Evng. in library.
A little whist, at which
May & I were singularly
fortunate. To bed at 10.30.
Rain during the night.
