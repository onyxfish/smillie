## Left Page

### October, Wednesday 18, 1876

Glorious, cloudless day —
Ake the m't. Running about
attending to messages picking
up loose ends — awhile at
the studios working on closet
plans — W. Sayre & Chiledum
got off for Phila. Dinner at 1.
After that went to Sathmf-
niell & superintended the
sacking & placing of my oak
lumber until near 5 — then
to studio — to tea at 6 —
At 7.30 with Ge. called on
Mrs Rogers & the niece at Mrs.
Seymours — at 8.30 at Fau
sm's & She Rei? from N.Y. till
p.m — There until 10, to
bed at 10.30. [illegible — ~4 words]

## Right Page

### October, Thursday 19, 1876

Another glorious day — Down-
town after 9 for — at studios
soon after 9. Found my lumber
outside my door & stuck [illegible]
properly inside — then to work
& ale the m't. Setting things
to rights to leave & arranging
to pack a the box to Syfs. to
m't.
Dinner at 6 clk.
again at studio & worked
at packing box until after
4 when I got it done —
Then to the house & finished
packing trunks — At 6.30 with
Ge. went to Fau m's to tea
There until 10 o'clk. then
up the hill & to bed.
