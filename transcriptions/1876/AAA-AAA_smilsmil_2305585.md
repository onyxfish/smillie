## Left Page

### March, Saturday 18, 1876

Bitter cold day & windy —
got to studio at 9 o'clk, &c?.
letter from father with notice
from Montrose Bank that my
note had been protested. I made
mistake in date & sent check one
day too late — was so enraged that
I could not work until I wrote
to Lenheims, Surrall & Searce — To
work painting horse under Goucha
To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.15
Many interruptions all the P.m.
& finally was able only to finish
the horse without his feet. To
tea at 6.30 — at 8 o'clk at Fifth
Phil. concert — Clara Dunham
Kirkham & Wm. K. there with Ben
& Mary S — enjoyed concert —
After it, to studio — at 11 to 41+S.
& to bed at 11.20

## Right Page

### March, Sunday 19, 1876

Charming winter day. Mel.
went to D' Hastings's & heard him
preach from 11 Romans — 1 chap.
5° to 6° p.— After service, walked
with Charle to studio — fixed fire
& at 1, walked up to Kirkham's
69 W. 50th St. where the S's are staying.
Dinner at 1.30 — at 4 we all went
to St. Bartholomew's — After service
walked down the ave. with the S's.
Stopped at studio — at 6.15 went to
the Kimball's & got my tea there.
At 8 went over to Prof. Roods. 341
E. 15th St — no one in — then to Mr.
Alfred Mitchell's, cor. Stuyvesant &
41[illegible] St. There until 9 o'clk — then
again to studio — wrote letters to
father & Geo. 7 & 41+S? at 11 &
to bed at 11.15 [illegible — ~5 words]
