## Left Page

### October, Tuesday 24, 1876

Delicious, cloudless day —
Bkfs. at 8 — Carriage did not
come until 9.30 when Gel.
& I rode up to Centennial Sh[ow]
— too late to meet Priscilla —
Spent the mf. in Art Annex,
ate lunch on Singer piazza
at 1.30 — Soon after 2 Gel.
left me & I worked like a
Beaver until 4.30 at carriage,
Japanese & State houses, part
of govt. exhibit etc. etc. — thru
to R. R. & thru to Wm. M's at
5.20 — Lay down for a few
minutes, then had dinner.
Evef. in parlor — two ladies
from next door came in for a
few minutes — all went to
our rooms soon after 9. I
made notes to father, Sm[ith]
& Priscilla B—k— & to bed
at 10 o'clk. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### October, Wednesday 25, 1876

Beautiful mrf. Bkfs. at 8 —
at 9, carriage came & with
Misses Ely & Hotchkiss, Geo.
& I rode up to the Supp—.
m. Looked at some pottery
in the Main Bldg & then to
Mem. Hall — Am. & Eur. Delg.
Then to Annex & with ladies
until 1 o'clk — Geo. went
home early — I left & went
to bldgs. in S. E. Section of
grounds — Vienna Bakery — French
govt., Photo. etc. — until 3 o'clk
ate lunch at 2 back of Police
Station — At 3.15, joined ladies
at Hortic. Hall & R.R. to city
by R.R. at 4 — Rested an hour
& a half on bed — then dinner —
Evef. Reading up Cent.
& playing Euchre — wrote
a note to Dining & got
to bed at 10.30. Much of the
day proved to be overcast,
windy & cold.
