## Left Page

### November, Wednesday 1, 1876

Deliciously mild, cloudless day — but I was not
able to get up until about 11 o'clk. Geo. brought
my bkfst. to my bed —
W. Whitney went to Wl.
by the 8.30 a.m. train.
We had lunch at 1 o'clk
In the afternoon I sunned
myself in the square (dozed)
thru Reading in big chair
& napping until tea time
at 6.30. Mrs. Lee & child
(Mrs. Whitney's sister) arr'd from
Bloomfield, Mrs. Prentice
Took tea with us — Evef
in the parlor — To bed
at 10.30. [illegible — ~6 lines]

## Right Page

### November, Thursday 2, 1876

Another mild cloudless day.
At 9 o'clk. Rode up to Exp. &
after a look through American
portion of Main Bldg & Mineral
Annex. Took a Review of the
Mexican, English, French,
Russian & Swedish Exhibits
then to the Gov't. Bldg and
spent the rest of the mf
there — At 1 o'clk went out
at Machinery Hall gate and
met Miss Wilcox & carriage &
Rode home — Had a short
rest before dinner at 2.15
The P.m. spent in my Room
Reading up". Tea at 6.30
Evef in the parlor —
To bed at 10 —
Mr. W. Ret'd from N.Y.
at 8.30.
