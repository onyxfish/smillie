## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 12, 1876

Mng. opened dismally but soon cleared off — I again fixed library windows & worked all the mng on 9×16 — Mrs Aleck Diven & Mrs. Falk called — Dinner at 30 — worked awhile on painting & finished second painting f't — While the rest went off to ride, I took pencils & paper & went out to sketch but my dinner had got cross-wise & I was unable to do anything. wandered about in discomfort until 6.30, then in to tea, but did it Falce any — Every in library — Euchre with May Cap. & Jack until 10 — to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 13, 1876

Very foggy & cool — fog did not lift so that I dared go out until 10 o'clk — then, altho' a little cool, it was charming. I worked on Study No. 1, near the gardener's until 1/2 past one o'clk — then in just in time for dinner — after that painted over much of the morning's work in-doors, as I had got it too crude in color. Worked until 4 o'clk. Then took pencils & paper & went out to the creek & got outline sketch — after that, walked to get warm as it had clouded up & the wind was cold — in at 6.30 very tired — tea at 6.45 — Mr. & Mrs. Hall to tea. They left at 8 after a couple of games at euchre — we were all tired & most of the evng was spent lolling in easy chairs — to bed at 10.45.
