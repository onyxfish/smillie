## Left Page

### April, Sunday 9, 1876

Glorious day — Very unwilling
to get up, feeling depressed,
up at last, & help — at 10 o'clk.
at 10.45 went to Has tuys?
but his sermon fell on heedless
ears — for I went to sleep —
after service — called & saw
Dr. Wm. Thompson — then to
Mamy's — there to dinner —
P.M. walked up to 57 S.
& called on Maud D — this
in the Park & men to Wile's to
Tea — At 8 o'clk — at Dr. Wm. J —
At 9.15 at studio — did some
writing — to 41 S. at 10.30
& to bed at 11,

## Right Page

### April, Monday 10, 1876

Still another charming day.
Got to studio at 9.45 — feeling
tired, slim & unlike work —
fooled the most away over
some face changes without
doing much. W. J. Williams
called — To dinner at 1.30 —
W. Clark (Rev.) of Patterson then
after going to new studio, back
at 3 o'clk — fussed & puttered
but did little of any account.
To tea at 6.50 — At 8 — went to
Mamy's expecting to see Father
& Joe but they had not arrived —
To studio at 8.45 & wrote
until 10.15 — Then to 44 !?
& to bed about 11.
