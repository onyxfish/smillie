## Left Page

### June, Friday 16. 1876.

Very warm, but charming breeze from the S.W. My head particularly thick & stupid.
Took a short walk & about 11 got started on Repainting
"a Memory of Montmorenci"
Uncle Wille came at noon with Walter's wife & child.
Never had seen her before —
Dinner at 12.45. My head troubled me so much that
I lay on the sofa until 3.30.
Painted for about an hour then went out & walked
until 6 o'clk. W. & Mrs
Kendré came — tea at 6.30
After tea, croquet. Shine in
and politics & fine arts
were discussed until 9.30
Then friends left — I
got to bed about 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 17. 1876.

Very hot & overcast — much of the time a strong wind from S.W. blowing. After
Mclsp — Read paper — went into town — Home again at 10.30 — then sat down & writing
daf. of Horse's Head from my painting for father as he cared not see to do it — got through in time for dinner
at 2.30 — After dinner, painted on Montmorenci — until 6 —
getting over all the water &
reflections — Have not made what I hoped to out of it.
Tea at 6.30 — About 7.30
Music in Eastman Park —
walked about & was caught
by Loomis & did not get
away until 8.30 — Then a
short walk & in. Wrote to
Nicoll & Bille S. — & got to
bed at 10.30
