## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 1. 1876.

Delicious day. Blkfs. at
4.30 About 9 got off with
my oil traps to an oat
field where I spent the
m'f. making sketches
of sheaves etc. Back to
a hearty lunch at 12.30.
After that went out to the
barn yard & studied cows
— got no color, only an out-
line sketch — back at 4
& worked for an hour on
"coupons" — Dinner at 5 —
After that, for nearly two
hours played croquet with
Jack, Cols. & May — In the
library, every, played
too — To bed at 10.15.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 2. 1876.

Charming day, altho' a little
too breezy to work with comfort
out of doors. After blkfs. went
to the bat field back of the house
& spent the m'f. making a
careful outline on canvas
of a group of trees, with a
sketch of meadow beyond.
In at 12, Read West Post
until 1, then lunch — After
that prepared for work in
the milking yard. The cows
did not come along until
after 3. There for an hour & a
half I tried to get something
but the beasts were so uneasy
that I got nothing but unsatis-
factory hints — Dinner at 5 —
About 6, Cols. Riley Read, wife
& Fam M. drove up & took me out
riding for an hour or more —
just trotting on the Race course —
Evening for an hour on piazza —
then "Zoo" with Ella & the [illegible].
To bed at 10 o'clk.
