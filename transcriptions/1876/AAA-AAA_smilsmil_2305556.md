## Left Page

### January, Thursday 20, 1876

Good weather again — cold,
cloudy & breezy — Got to studio
soon after 9 & to work on sky
of big drawing. Spent the mg
on it & improved it greatly —
To dinner at 1.30 — back at —
2.15 & had a job in cleaning
plate glass & getting drawing
into frame — Rosselle came
for it at 4.30 and as it was
carried out it seemed like
the body of some dear one. There
is a painful bareness in my
studio now. Made toaste &
pasted a mount of W.C. study
at Po' — To tea at 6.35 — at 8.30
called to see S.L.F. at Mrs
Zabriskie's — 131 W 21st S— There
until 10.15 — Then to studio
— to 41st S — at 10.45 & to bed
at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 21, 1876

Fine milder day — Got to studio
a little after 9 — Fussed about —
put some little cattle in a
study that I made up at Po —
last June & at noon took it
over to the N.A. There until
after 1 o'clk. Saw my big
picture on the wall and was
distressed to find it low and
muddy in tone and generally
common place — We are to have
a splendid exhibition. To dinner
at 1.30 At 2.30 at Phil. Rehearsal
After that walked down B'way
with Belle & Mary J— Went to Durin's
— then to Avery's — & to tea at 6.30
At 8.30 to N.A. Charl came there
for me — at 9 we went to Nicale's
& at 10 to studio & about 11 I
got to 41st S— & to bed.
