## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 5, 1876

Pleasant day — Up late nearly 10 before I got to
studio — Worked until after 11 mounting some
drawings then commenced work on vigt. of Cows
pasturing to make an oblong drawing of it —
To lunch at 1 o'clk — back at 2.20 and worked
as long as I could see.
To tea at 6.30 — Evely until after 9 at Rennie's
inserting frames — ordered four for W. C.'s — Then to studio
there until 10.40 fixing up some of the mess —
Then to 41 St. & to bed.

[illegible] at 11.20 to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Wednesday 6, 1876

Delightful day — met Miss
Abbatt & Rennie's — ordered frames, then walked to studio
getting there at 9.25 — Then to work on Cattle Pasturing
& worked without interruption until 12.15 — Then fan M. &
the Dr. came in — visited until near 1 — then we went
out together — I left them at Stutt. Ho. got to 41 St. at 1.20
back to studio at 2.30 and worked until 4.15 — no more
light — then hung pictures on screen until after 6 —
To tea at 6.45 — Evelf at 8.30 at Stutt. Ho. & saw fan — we went over to studio
together — back at 10.45 —
I got to 41 St. at 11 &
right to bed.
