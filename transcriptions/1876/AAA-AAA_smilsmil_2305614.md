## Left Page

### May, Monday 15, 1876

Very pleasant mef. Got to
studio about 10 oclk. The day
was spent in trying to get
a little order out of the chaos
& superintending Calverley's
nephew, a carpenter, who is
doing some work for me, also
in writing out water color
cat. for Phila. To dinner in
41 St. at about 12.30 — then
back to studio where I stay
until near 7 — then to tea —
a slight spatter of rain that
became heavy at night — At
8 o'clk at Colman's to meet
a com. from the Mendelssohn
Union to consult together as
to the possibility of putting
up a building for art and
music — Considerable talk
— no cider — nobody knows
where the money is to come
from — walked up with Robertson
— To bed at 11.15.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 16, 1876

Overcast & chilly, clearing
later in the day — Up late
10.30 before I got to studio.
Got Calverley's neph. Started
on carpenter work in my closet.
Dinner in 41 St. at 1.30 —
back at 2.30 — Bellows called,
finished Phil. cat. & commenced
one for Colman to take to
Phila — to have cards & titles
put upon the pictures — To
tea at 7. Father came in
while I was at my tea —
Then Mary & the Dr. They all
left about 9 & I got to bed
some after 10 oclk. [illegible — entire passage]
