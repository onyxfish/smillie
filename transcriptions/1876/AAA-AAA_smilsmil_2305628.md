## Left Page

### June, Wednesday 14, 1876

Overcast again + threatening
but that was all. Did not
go out to walk at all, but
after Reading "Tribune", got
to work about 10.30 in Glimpse
my head troubling me so
much that my work was
fair grinding, but I stuck
to it. Dinner at 12.30 — a
nap after it, then worked
from 2 until 5 — then went
with Father + Geo. + called
on W. Wright — not in — I
chatted with Mrs. Williams + Hall
on croquet ground while Fath.
+ Geo. called on Mr. Loomis.
Tea at 6.30 — At 8, Geo. and
I called on the Misses Wicks
+ had a pleasant, chatty
visit until after 9 — then
home, wrote for an hour
+ got to bed at about
10.45.

## Right Page

### June, Thursday 15, 1876

Very warm day — after Bkfst.
mote for an hour or more,
then went to P.O. with letters
— called on Rev. Mr. Lawrence — not
in — then home + to work painting
an "Adirondack Glimpse" — I in
at 12.30 — very stupid head.
— a short nap — then to work.
+ finally, about 5 o'clk., had
completed second painting.
To my sorrow, I cannot think
it a success. Sponge bath
+ dressed. At 6.30 went to W.
Williams' to tea — W. + Mrs. Jerry
Wright + Misses Wright + sisters
Stone there — Geo. came later
in the eveng. Had a very
pleasant time + sat chatting
on piazza until near 11 o'clk.
Then home + right to bed.
