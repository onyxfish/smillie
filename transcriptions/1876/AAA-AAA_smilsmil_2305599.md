## Left Page

### April, Saturday 15, 1876

Pleasant, fresh & breezy. Got to studio's at 8, 45 & put in a good days work on a Scrubface — At 11 went to — Geo came in during the 6 25 B° way — there until 12 30 — 70 dinner in 41 35 — at 1 — back to studio's at 2 15 & worked until 6 — 70 tea at 6.30 — at 8 went to Mary's — saw Geo — to studio's at 9 — there until 10 — Charle came in — went with him to his new quarters in the Rossmore. Got to 41 28 ab about 11 — to bed — but not to sleep for a long time.

## Right Page

### April, Sunday 16, 1876

Charming mrf. with an April shower about 5 o'clk. — Philp — at Mary's. went to Dr. Hasting's service — Dr. H. preached an excellent discourse, but I was too many to hear it. After service I walked to Mary's with Geo then to studio's & at 2 to Rossmore Ho when, at 2.30, I dined with Charl — about 4.30 we started out and walked up to new beds of Met. Mus. Nat. His. went all over the beds — then across the park at 6 — getting caught in an awful shower — Took tea at A.V.V's — at 8 went to 10 W 57' S. & called on Maud D — at 9 went to studio's — at 10.30 to 41 S. & at 11 to bed.
