### Memoranda

Note for $275.00 to H.f. Tyler —
Dated Sep. 9. 1874 due
March 9. 1875 — $150 paid

Gave H.f. Tyler note for
$250.00 in payment
for new purchase of land.
Date Sep. 29. 1876 — to
be paid within one year
from date.

Gave Thos B. Nelson. note for
$187.50/00 Dec 11. '76 — 3 months
due. March 11. '76. Payment
of Studio Rent for 5 months —
from Nov 1. 1876 to Ap. 1. '77.
