## Left Page

### January, Sunday 16, 1876

Mild day — mrf. went to D. Hastings — heard him preach from Job — 37° ch. 21° v. but must confess that I thought more of my work than I did about the sermon. With Charl walked down to studio — then to F.H.Smith's — there but a moment then to A.N.N.'s at 2 ocltc — found them as far on as fish — finished dinner with them — Charl came in later — at 4.30 we walked down together — I stopped at F.H.S's — there until 6 — then to studio — no tea — not feeling well, some times in lounge — About 10 went to 41st — and did not get to bed until almost 11.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 17, 1876

Dull, muggy day — Up late. Had to go to Renne's — Snedicor's and Goupie's — 10.30 when I got to my studio. All the rest of mrf. at work over flat of big frame, putting on washes of water color until I got in harmonious with picture & frame. A good deal of talk over the new 4th ave. Studios — to lunch at 1.20 — back at 2.10 — a gilder came & repaired some rubbed spots on frame — several callers — demoralized by the weather so that I did not accomplish much work. F.H.Smith came in at 5 — at 6 walked up with him. To tea at 6.30 — at 7.30 called on father at Mary's — at 8.30 on W.W.D. Morgan 26 Washington St. who will lend two drawings to W.C. Exhibn — then on aveny — notin — saw the ladies — at 9.40 at studio — typed five — wrote for awhile — + at       to 41st 5s and to bed at —
