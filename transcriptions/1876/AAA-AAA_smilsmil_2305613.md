## Left Page

### May, Saturday 13. 1876.

Charming day but could
not get up on acct. of a rack-
ing headache — Kept my bed
until about 4 o'clk. — Then
got up, washed & dressed
& went to Mary's — found
that Geo. had gone to Po' —
so I telegraphed home — then
walked down to new studio —
back to tea at about 8 —
Evelf. spent reading up
newspapers & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### May, Sunday 14. 1876.

Did not get up until near 9 o'clk
then was cheated out of my bath
by the young man who rooms under
me — Got my bkfst., then washed &
dressed — At 11.45 went to Hastings'
met Chas. & walked down the Ave.
with him. Then to new Studios. 337
4th ave. met Mr. Spring & then Emma S.
at 1.20 at Saml. Colman's & took dinner
there — talked of plans for new studio
left & at 4.30 with W. & W. C. went up
to the Park. Enjoyed a saunter there
until 6 o'clk. Then down & stopped
at Emma J's, together to go to the C's
with me to tea — she had company &
I went alone — tea at 7 — Evelf. again
discussed plans — left at 10 o'clk
— met up to Grand Central dépôt
& mailed a letter home — Then to
41 [illegible] S. & to bed soon after 11,
