## Left Page

### March, Monday 20. 1876.

Dull m'f. got into feeling effects of cold. Did not get to studio until 9.45 — then set palette & painted in the feet of Goncha's horse — To lunch at 1.15 — Mima B — thim — back at 2.30 — very dark. Gifford, Bellows, Mull & others called, concerning the approaching Water Color election. Charcoaled in Horse's Head for A.B.N. Co. Horrible storm of sleet & snow set in at about 3. To tea at 7.15 & spent the rest of the eveng. poulticing my face (something like a boil seems to be coming on my R. cheek) and drinking hot flax seed tea — To bed at 10.30. Very heavy rain all night.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 21. 1876.

No trace of snow this m'f. got to studio at 9.30 — cleared off very bright at 11 — got to work on painting of Horse's Head for A.B. N. Co. Will came in & gave me an order to paint the old [illegible] bree. for their Centennial Specimen sheet. Got a very mild letter from Lurheim — To dinner at 1.20 back at 2.15 & painted until after 5 when I got head done. To tea at 6.45 — back to studio at 8.50 after a call upon W. Mrs C. Gilman, 31 W. 36 — trying without success to get some of his J.W. Hill drawings for Centennial — wrote a letter to Lurheim — To 41 S: at 11 & to bed at 11.15
