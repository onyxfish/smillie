## Left Page

### January, Friday 14, 1876

Cold, windy & overcast —
After bkfst went to Reumer's
Shop & was there some time
mixing color to build up my
flat for big frame. Got to
Studio at 10. About 11 Dolph
came in & stayed until
near 1 — then I walked up
with him & stopped at his
studio. It was 1.35 before
I got to dinner — back to
Studio at 2.5 & gave Phil
Ticket to Miss Gilbert — and
I worked on foreground and
figures as well as I could in
spite of a number of callers,
to tea at 6.30. About 8 at Studio
at 9 at Y.M.C.A. — Reception
given by Bellows, Gifford,
Robbins & Satterlee — a very
pleasant gathering — there until
near 11 — again to Studio &
finally got to 41 1 St. & bed at
11.30

## Right Page

### January, Saturday 15, 1876

Still windy & disagreeable —
Got to Studio at about 9.30
Tinkered about on foreground &
on distant R.H. figures — Drith
again up to talk about building
going up cor. of 4th Ave. & 25th St.
Studios to be in top floor. To
lunch at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & at
3 went over to 4th Av. & 25th St. and
Saw Architect & plans — many
things to be considered — back
to Studio at about 3.30. Got in
some more work. Miss Hattie
& Louise Alling called. — To
tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 back to
Studio — made paste, mounted
Griswold's two drawings and
got to 41 1 St. & bed about
11.30.
