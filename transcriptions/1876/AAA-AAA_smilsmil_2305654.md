## Left Page

### August, Monday 7, 1876

About 1 a.m. woke with acute chills, followed as the night before, by fever & sweat. Got but little sleep until after daylight. Up in time for B'kf— feeling very slim. After bkf— Rode in town with the Gent to see Dr. Seeley but missed him. Again drove down abt & found him— gave me Remedies. Then back & had dinner about 2.30. Spent a good part of the P.m. on lounge. Did a little work on Tablets for Centennial pictures. Tea abt 5.30. Every spent in lounging & chatting until after 10. Then to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Tuesday 8, 1876

Up at 7 after an undisturbed night of rest. Feeling quite well. Spent most of the morning lettering the Centennial Tablets. Dinner at 1 o'clk. After a call from Miss Gray & niece with invitation to tea to-morrow eveg P.m. Tried again to go to my work & the mtf. but found my head too slim. Findley, in a fit of desperation, started to climb the hills & by easy stages got well up towards the tops, where, for about an hour, I lay under a tree & enjoyed the very delicious cool air — wind from N.N.W. Got down to the house about 5.30. On the lounge until tea abt 6.30 — & after, until 8.30, feeling very much under the weather. Then Cap't. Ds. & May arr'd from Gotham. General chat and visitation until 10.30 then to bed. [illegible — ~4 words]
