## Left Page

### November, Tuesday 21, 1876

Still a disagreeable sort of weather — very moist —
Stopped at Hoffman Hse &
saw Maud & Jack D. at bkfst.
There to Studio at 9.30. Miss
Spring came — I commenced
a little W.C. Sketch from
an Elmira sketch — Went
to lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & worked until
4.30 — At 6 o'clk. at Sam
Colman's to dinner. Emma
J. there — At 7.30 we went
to Water Color Socy meeting —
altho' not Raining, very moist.
A good number out & had
a lovely meeting until 10.
There to C's for awhile &
took Emma home — then
to 41 S. & to bed about
11.30 & [illegible — ~4 words]

## Right Page

### November, Wednesday 22, 1876

Overcast day — Of course
had a stupid head on me
got to Studio at 9.30. Miss
Spring came. My Centennial
pictures also came — then Maud
came & told me of the marriage
of her father to riders [illegible] & a
[illegible] blow to them. — Miss
Durfee called — al[most] the
entire mf. Sent & away by
callers. By 1.15 — went to the
Hoffman Hse & lunched with
Maud & May. Back at 2.30
& worked on W.C. Sketch until
4.30 — Then Nicoll came in. —
At 5.30 went to Vautier's — to
tea at 6.45 — after messages
again to Studio at 9 o'clk.
There until near 11. fighting
the confusion in my closet.
Then to 41 S. & to bed.
Aunt Agnes am° — at the
Dilling's this evng.
