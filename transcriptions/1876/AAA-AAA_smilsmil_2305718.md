## Left Page

### December, Friday 15, 1876

Windy, gusty, cold day. Got to studio at 9.30 + worked on upright, finishing generally until about 1.15, then to lunch. Back at 2.30 + fussed + worked as long as I could see, finally giving the picture up as finished. Fan came in at 5 — went up to Stut. Ho. with her + at 6 to Fanny H's. She did not go to her club, so at 8.30 I went to 57 W. 10th St. Informal meeting of the N.C.S. Sager tobacco until 10.45 — then walked up to H1. 8. + to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Saturday 16, 1876

Very cold + windy with no snow then snow flying — got to studio at 9.30. All the m'f spent mounting + tying up drawings — to dinner at 1 — on my way back, called on Ja. Hart + Lazarus — got to studio at 3 + worked as long as I could see on charcoal sketch "Under the Butternut" — Nicoll came in + stayed until 6. To tea at 6.30. After that, went to Renner's shop + about 8.30 to Stut. Ho. with Fan until 10.20 — then to H1. 8. + to bed at 11.
