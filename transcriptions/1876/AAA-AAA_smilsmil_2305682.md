## Left Page

### October, Monday 2, 1876

Dark & dismal again. Got up to studio at about 8.30 & worked at picking up stones for near an hour. Then in & commenced sketches for a drawing for A.B.N.C. at a venture — to be called "The Pasture" — to be based on Elmira studies — got it planned out before dinner at 1 o'clk. & during the P.M. Traced it, transferred it & outlined almost of it on drawing paper. More picking up of stones — also dressed & at 6.30, to Dan'l. Sayre's — a family "tea-fight" — at 9, went home with Priscilla C. then with Geo. until after 10 — to bed at 11. Glorious moonlight night.

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 3, 1876

Superb, cloudless day. 9 o'clk when I got to studio — Picked stones for awhile — At 9.30 Priscilla & her sister Katie made a short call. I got to work on "Pasture" drawing with water colors — To dinner at 1 — worked on drawing until 4.20. Sprout came in to see about setting out trees. Then went riding with Geo. until 6 o'clk. Tea at 6.25. Every, before 8 o'clk. Geo. & I went to A. Lathrop's. Met Rev. W. Bullard there. At 9, went to studio & fixed up for the night. To bed about 10 o'clk. [illegible — ~4 lines]
