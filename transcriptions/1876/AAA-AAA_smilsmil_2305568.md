## Left Page

### February, Sunday 13, 1876

Very warm and pleasant —
my went to D. Hastings
but heard little of his
sermon as my head was too
full of Centennial + water -
Color matters — After service
walked down 5 ave. with Ch[as]
thru to Mary's — took dinner
there + spent most of the
P.M. with mother — at 4.30
walked down to studio and
fixed fire, ready to light
— to tea at Mary's at 6 — thence
there — the every spent sing-
ing + chatting until 10.30 —
after 11 when I got to bed,

## Right Page

### February, Monday 14, 1876

miserable dark rainy day —
got to studio at 10. Finished
tracings of separate horses +
transferred them to the canvas
\+ then painted in all the
legs, extremities, corrected
lines + a good part of the
ground. At 1 o'clk, prepared
lunch in studio — + painted
until after 5 — To tea at 6.30
after chatting with Johnston
of the "Mail" — Nicoll + J.A. Smith
about 7.45 went to Mary's and
saw Mother — thence to studio
\+ wrote until near 11 — thence
to 41 "S." + at midnight to
bed.
