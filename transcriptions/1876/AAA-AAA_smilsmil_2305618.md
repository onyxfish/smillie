## Left Page

### May, Thursday 25, 1876

Charming day — still bright and
cool — Up early — after [illegible] — went
into town to [illegible] office & arrang[e]
to have my trunk brought from
depot — then to Matt's and
at about 11. back home. Tom[?]
came & I spent some time
before & after dinner in carrying
contents to 3rd story back & placing
them in closets — that Room
being fixed very nicely for me.
After dinner felt very sleepy
& had a long nap on the sofa
in father's Room. Tea about
6.30 — Every spent in Mother's
Room swinging in a Rocking
chair. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### May, Friday 26, 1876

Charming day, cloudless and
cool. [illegible] at 9.30 — About
9 went out & had a half hour
saunter with Geo — then in —
my boy came by [illegible] and
I unpacked it. Then got out
my colors, set palette & started
a little sketch 5×8½ in. Dinner
at 1 — worked awhile after dinner
but feeling very heavy took a nap —
then painted until 5.30 — then
out for a walk with father — went up
to Wilson's — Tea at 6.45 — At 7.45
went with Geo. to W. Loomis's where
a few friends had been invited to hear
Madame Roque (?) of Vassar, Read an
essay on Modern French Art. After the
reading a general discussion took
place as to different national Schools
& their influence upon moral and
intellectual advance. This was
pleasantly kept up until 11 — then
the last of us said "good night" and
I got home & to bed at 11.30
