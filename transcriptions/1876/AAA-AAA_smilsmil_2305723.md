## Left Page

### December, Monday 25, 1876

Overcast day — got to studio at 10 o'clk & to work on
"India mk sketch" — Troyle
Station for Q.B.N. Co — worked
until 1.15 — then to Mamp's
to dinner — at table
until about 3 — got to
studio at 3.30 & worked
until 4.15 — finished the
sketch — to tea at 7 o'clk
after working at studio on
tracing of large drawing
& at 8.30, again at Mamp's —
spent the eveng in the
parlor until 10 o'clk —
then to 41 St. 10.45 bef.
I got to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Tuesday 26, 1876

milder with wet snow —
Got to studio at 10 o'clk &
to work on large drawing
"Under the Butternuts" or
"the Truants" — don't know
what title I will give it.
To dinner at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 — got outline finished
& put on first broad washes —
color went on very badly —
Nicole came in late —
to tea at 6.45 — at 8.30
at Rennie's — walked down
to 26 St. with Magrath &
called on Walter Brown not
in — then we went to studio
m. there until 10 — I matu
awhile & to 41 St. at 11
& right to bed. [illegible — ~3 words]
