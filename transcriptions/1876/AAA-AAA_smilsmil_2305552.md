## Left Page

### January, Wednesday 12. 1876.

Pleasant winter day, barring one or two light snow flurries. Got to studio at 9.15 — After a little time to Misses Abbatt & Jacobs, got to work on my foreground & bothered away all the m'f. Tom Smith came up to talk new studios at 1 o'clk. To dinner at 1.30 back at 2.20 — fussed all the rest of P.M. on foreground. Sam Coleman came in at 4 o'clk. By his advice I then washed off the entire foreground in 30 m. he slabbed in one, broad & bright, for me to finish — At 5.30 went to Knozday & bought paper — To tea at 6.40 after tea to Rennie's & ordered frames for Griswold — then at 8.30 to Chas. Inhams & spent the evening there until 11 — H.H. Smith & wife there to 41½3½ at 11.15 — to bed at 1.30.

## Right Page

### January, Thursday 13. 1876.

Cold, murky day, cloudy & clear by turns — Up late & got to studio at 9.30 — To work on foreground & got along very well on my new basis. Did work also on drawings of Misses Jacobs & Abbatt. To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.20 & continued foreground work — also on small R.H. figures. To tea at 6.35 and after that, went in to Rennie's — At 7.15 called on Mrs. Hegueau & W31½S½, about George's studies that she has — Met her for the first time & had a pleasant little visit. Got one of George's studies & took it to studio — there until after 10 — then to 41½S½ & to bed at 11.15 [illegible — ~5 lines]
