## Left Page

### July, Thursday 20, 1876

Had a wretched night — so
sick that I did not get fairly
to sleep at all — Could not eat
any bkfst. felt very slim —
went over to W. J. J's & sat with
Mother, & at last, with her and
Kate went up to my studio
the breeze was delightfully cool
Rested in doors for an hour —
then back — I went to bed &
had a poor nap — Dinner at
1 o'clk. After that, went up
to Studio & loitered there until
near 4 — then a thunder storm
threatened — Took a short turn
across lots — then to W. J. J.'s, here
until 5.45 — then to Jan's & the
storm broke upon us — it was a
very fine one followed by rain
at intervals — Splendid Saturday —
— were until near 9, with Mother —
then to Jan's — Geo. [illegible] —
Elisha & his wife there. Played
whist until after 10 — To bed at
10.45 — Rec'd letter from W. Church
Lewis enclosing check for $100.
bal. due on Cathedral Spires.

## Right Page

### July, Friday 21, 1876

Glorious day — Perfection
itself — After bkfst. out in the
garden with Mother. Much
of the mng lost in loafing.
Went up to Studio & tried
to "sweep out" — Did a little
at clearing out closet. Dinner
at 1 o'clk. Before that went
down town with Mother & Kate
& Jan; all got drenched — Got
Lewis's checks cashed. After
dinner got our money affairs
straightened — then wrote to
Mr. Lewis. Fixed stretchers
& stretched canvas — then
took something of a walk —
met Jan M. & Priscella P., went
home to Jan's with them & took
tea — there until 10 o'clk —
Took P. home — then to
bed myself before 11.
