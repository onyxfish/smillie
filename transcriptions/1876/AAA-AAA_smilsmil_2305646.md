## Left Page

### July, Saturday 22. 1876.

Charming day. After bkfi-
spent part of mng, walking
in the garden with mother.
Then wrote to father — Josie B.
& Maud D. Then got out my
linnen, flannels, socks, etc &
had a good time marking
them — Dinner at 1 — Continued
marking until about 3 — Then
went down town — got horses
& Barouche from Niurchelis &
about 4.30 took mother, Aunt
H. & Kate out Riding — Had
a delightful drive over the
Searle hill & through the woods.
Back at 6.20 — Tea at 6.30 —
Evng with mother until 9.
Then with Geo. to the P.O. Then
to Fan's — there until 10. Took
Priscilla home — & got to bed
myself about 10.45.

## Right Page

### July, Sunday 23. 1876.

Very breezy, cool & cloudy — a
delightful day after the long
continued heat. Bkfi: at 9.
After that, went over & saw mother
went to Baptist Ch. & heard
Mr. Cheshire preach a flat sermon
from John 19th Ch. 25 – 7th v. There
for awhile on Turrell Piazza —
Then to Studio — slight spatter
of Rain. Dinner at 1.30.
Then again for awhile with mother
— made a piazza call on Flora S.
At 4, Took a little nap — at
5 went down the hill — called
on Fant took a walk with her
& the Dr. at 7.30 went to take Kate
to church. But she was not
ready — With mother until 9.
Then took Katie's letters to the
P.O. Stopped again at Fan's
there until 10 then to bed
at 10.30     Ther. below 66°
