## Left Page

### January, Tuesday 4, 1876

Fine cold day but very windy. Got to studio at 9.30 — worked awhile on Miss A's sketch — then to my black horse — To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.15 & painted until 4.30 — Corey & "the girls" came in & insisted upon my joining a theatre party — I plead my other engagement — Misses Alling & Aittieus called at 4.45 — At 6.30 to [torn] and revision party at Anna Cook's where I met all the Adirondackers of several seasons past. Sat down to table about 7 & got up at 9 — walked up town with Fanny K. Will K. & "Lib" (home at 11 o'clk — glorious night — To 41 & S — at 11.45 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### January, Wednesday 5, 1876

Fine day. Did not get to studio until 9.30 — Then to work on big drawing; black horse & brown horse — Louise Alling called in for a moment — To dinner at 1.30 back at 2.20 — & continued work of myself. Find it very slow, now that I am digging away down in the depths of color & shadow. Mrs. Saml. Thorne came in at a little after 4. After a short visit, went with her into Colman's & Hart's Rooms — to tea at 6.30 — at 9.30 — called on Mrs. Perkins & Mrs. Walsh in 44th St — There until 10.15 — then to 41 & S — & to bed at 10.45 [illegible — ~3 words] [illegible]
