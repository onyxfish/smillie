## Left Page

### November, Thursday 23, 1876

Very pleasant mng, but very soon overcast. Got to studio soon after 9. Miss Spring came & I gave her lessons & examples in" washing". I worked on "Butternuts on Newtown creek" until near 2 — then to 41st S., lunched — dressed & at 3 at Mary's — then with her & Katy went to R.R. Station & took 3.30 train to Po. Aunt A. joined us — got to the Terrace about 6 — Quite a little company to celebrate Father's birthday. Supper about 7.30 — Festivities kept up until after 11 — It was just midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Friday 24, 1876

Up soon after 6 & saw superb sun rise — snow over Catskill mts. Bkfst. at 7.30 — Good byes at 8 — & to R.R. Char. followed — took 8.35 train & got to 42d St. at 11.10 — Mr. Williams & Po. rode with me — Went at once to studio. Miss Spring there. Gave lesson, making little sketch for her. At 1, met Mr. Williams at Rennie's — then to dinner — At 2.30 at Phil. rehearsal, at 5 back to studio — then to Snedecor's — he has sold to Atteuckian a small pic. of mine — then to Kimball's — took tea, & at ½ took Fanny K. & Anna C. to 4th ave — then to Hoffman Ho. to see Divers — near 10 went out with Maud & May & over to Studio's — at 11.30 left them & to 41st St. & to bed at midnight.
