## Left Page

### December, Saturday 9, 1876

Bitter cold day with wicked wind — went to Knordleis and bought more paper as I failed in stretching properly the last. Got to studio at 9.30 & worked for 1½ hours on Cows Pasturing — then got started on upright study on Newtown Creek, worked until 12.30 — on my way to lunch met Belle S — the S's will not go to Phil. con. to night but have given their tickets to Mary — Back to Studio at 2.30 & worked until 4.30, enjoyed big fire in grate until 6 — there to tea — at 7.20 Mary & Carrie Hertzel came to the house for me & we went to Phil — very cold & very few people out — dismal effects — to 44 S — at about 10.45 — warmed myself there until after 11 — then to 41 St & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 10, 1876

Bright & clear — less wind but very cold — Up late — Promised to take fan to Ch — after 11 where I got to first, Ho there until 12 — there to studio — then to Sam. Coleman's — He is very sick confined to bed — I took dinner with Mrs. C — left at 3.15 — went to studio & wrote awhile — at 6 o'clk. at Mary's to tea — Sat in Charl's Room — chatting & looking over books until 11 o'clk — there to 41 S — & to bed.
