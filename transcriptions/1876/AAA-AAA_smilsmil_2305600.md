## Left Page

### April, Monday 17, 1876

Another charming day with several April showers. Got
to studio at 9.30 & to work on
Scrub Race. Had many callers
among them Emma D. & bro g.
To dinner at 1.40 — back at
2.30. Got my drawing so
that it seemed to me about
finished at 5 o'clk. Then
to work cleaning up & to
tea at 6.45 — again at
Studio at 8.30 wrote until
10 — Miss Gilbert again
helping by cutting out my
miniature drawings, to 41°
8: at 10.45 & to bed at 11.15,

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 18, 1876

Pleasant but cooler. Got to
studio at 9.30 after a call on
Charl at the Rossmore. I worked
on "his" Scrub Race" all the mtf &
finally pronounced it done.
To dinner at 1.15. After that
dressed, then to haircutter's
& at 3.30 again at Studio. Found
Harris Pardee & Musgrove, also
Kurtz waiting for me. Had
a long visit from Pardee and
tried to sell him "Scrub Race" —
After they went, until 6, putting
it finally into the frame & pasting
paper back fit. Then to Emma
D's where I dined, & at 7.30 went
with her to Chickering Hall & heard
the Mendelssohn club. Enjoyed it
exceedingly. At 10.30, home
with her — in at Studio for a
few minutes — to 4H & S: at 11
& to bed at 11.30 — [illegible]
