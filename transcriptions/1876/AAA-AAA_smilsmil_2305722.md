### December, Saturday 23, 1876

Very brilliant day with terribly sloppy melting. Got
to studio at 9 + to work on re-
tracing of large drawing +
putin a fair mf? work.
To dinner abt. — had to
wait awhile for [illegible] — back
at 2.30 + worked until
6 as I lighted up my new
gas fixtures — To tea at
6.45 — then in to Renner's
\+ about 9 got to F.H.S's —
there until 10.30 looking
over his studies in color
\+ charcoal. To bed befor
11 —

## Right Page

### December, Sunday 24, 1876

Fine day — Bkfst. about 10 —
Venison steak — my cold
making me feel stupid
\+ generally miserable.
To church — Thos Hastings —
heard him preach from
"The fulness of time" —
That when Christ came
the time was full. + Riper
gel also there — after
service, walked down
to studio where I spent
the p.m. fussing some
drawers into order, writing
lying on the sofa. To
tea at [illegible] at 6 oct.
[illegible]. Chatting with Ger-
mary, etc until 10, then
to H + S + to bed —
