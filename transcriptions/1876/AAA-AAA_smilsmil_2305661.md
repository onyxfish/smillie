## Left Page

### August, Monday 21, 1876

Superb day. Until near 11 writing letters & packing
Tablets which I supp° in the afternoon to Earle & Sons in
Phila. Then set Palette & commenced painting
in sky on 2° oil study near the gardener's house
worked until dinner time — 1 o'clk — There again at
sky — made a batch of it —
about 4 P.m. went out to meadow with large W.
C. & finished the outline about 6 — then in to tea —
Surprised by a call from A.P. Throop & Willie — on their
way to N.Y. from Geneva — a little before 8, walked down the road
with them & got horse car — They go on to N.Y. tonight — By the
same train the Guvl. & Ella started for Saratoga — Every
evening with Maud, Mary & the Cap°. To bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

Charming day — a little milder than yesterday — At
10 o'clk. took traps & went to study No. 1 near Gardener's
Ho. & worked on it until 12.30
Then in — on my back awhile
& had dinner at 1.30 — After dinner washed in sky in
large W.C. study & then took it out & laid in a good part
of it direct from nature — in at 6. Tea at 6.45 — Every
as usual in library with a little Euchre — To bed
