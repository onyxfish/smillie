## Left Page

### August, Thursday 3, 1876

Overcast mf., cool, pleasant air — After bkfs I wrote letters to Santali's Grannies & father. Finally got to work in doors painting sky & distance on study that I outlined yesterday mf. Worked until lunch at 1 oclk. After that with Genl. D. filling gun cartridges until the cows came (about 3) when I sketched in milking yard for about an hour — then in & painted until dinner at 5 — from 6 until after 7 wandering in pastures & by brook hunting up subjects for study — Eve. a little Reading & writing & all being sleepy got to bed shortly after 9 —
Weather still overcast & almost raining. [illegible — ~4 lines]

## Right Page

### August, Friday 4, 1876

Provoking sort of day, so far as my work is concerned. Overcast mf., steaming hot — half clear p.m. As I could not paint on out field study I spent the mf. outlining a new one from a point near the gardener's house on the little brook, setting in the midst of dense sedges and reeds. The study is a very fine one. Dinner at 1 oclk. Rec'd note from Clarence Cook & made answer — From 3 to 4.15 with cows in milking yard — they were so uneasy that I got very little — then came in & painted sky on study that I outlined in the mf. Tea soon after 6. Eugene, wife & two sisters-in-law called — Glorious moonlight night — all out on piazza until 10 oclk. To bed at 10.30
