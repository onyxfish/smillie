## Left Page

### March, Sunday 26. 1876.

Up at 8.30 — feeling slim, but took my regular hair mitten scrub, exercise and bath — About 10.30, walked down to studio — at 1 o'clk. up to A.N.N. & had a regular severe dinner — took fish, flesh & fowl, wines & coffee & everything going, to see if as advised, it would prove a cure for dyspepsia — I am inclined to think favorably of it. At 5, went over to Millis — there until near 8 — then called on Maud D. H.O.W. 57. — also — on F.H. Smith in E. 54. S — there until near 11 — then to 4[illegible] & to bed.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 27. 1876.

Clear, breezy day — Got to studio at 9.30 — Kindred fine and lost much time through callers & centennial biz — to dinner at 1.15 — then to Knoedler's and again to studio about 3 o'clk. Most of rest of p.m. about charcoaling a subject 12×14 Adirondack lake [illegible] — To tea at 6.30 — after reading my Post, dressed & at 8.45 called for Nellie Jacobs & took her to N.A. of D. Private View. Tremendous crush — could see nothing — there until 11 o'clk — then home with N.J. — then to studio — made a sketch home telling them of my good luck in selling "High Sierras" — took it to Grand Central Depot & mailed it, then to 4. S. & to bed at 12.15.
