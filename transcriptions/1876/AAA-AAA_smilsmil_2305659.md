## Left Page

### August, Thursday 17, 1876

Overcast & very cool mng.
Bkfs. abt 8 o'clk. Worked until
after 11 o'clk on Tablets, — a
few drops of Rain — then to
work on study, commenced
some time ago, on edge of little
brook near the Gardener's he.
Dinner abt 1.30 — again
painted on study, getting
in all but the foreground —
then went out & had a pleas-
ant breezing walk on the hill
to tea abt 6.30 — Genl. D-
lets from N.Y. Eve'g. on
awhile on lo appa, then
cards & chat — to bed at
a little before 11.

## Right Page

### August, Friday 18, 1876

Cloudy & clear by turns.
After bkfs. Took canvas & went
to a point near the Gardener's
he & continued another study
Dinner soon after 1 o'clk —
sky clear & sun very hot —
Between 3 & 4, went out to
the meadow & commenced a
large Water Color study upon
the edge of the stream called
Newtown Creek. — Worked upon
outline until after 5 o'clk.
Nearly 7 when we had tea —
Eugene & wife came up — Fire
on the hill S.E. of us — Very Picturesque
effect as every deepened — on
piazza until near 9 — Then in &
had a little Cal. Jack — To bed
before 10.30
