## Left Page

### October, Saturday 28, 1876

overcast [illegible]. At 9.15 started
in carriage with Misses Ely &
Finley & Ge. for 24 6[illegible]. At 10.15
I left the others & all day until
3.15 worked like a good fellow
in the S. W. quarter of the Main
Bldf. giving most attention to
Japan & Russia. At 12.45 ate
lunch in Spain. Was crammed
full & more too with what I
saw. At 3.30 Took Vine St. car
& got to W. W.'s at 4.15. Went
to bed & had a good nap before
dinner at 5.30. Went, Rading
up & also some sushine —
mote a note to During &
to bed soon after 10 —
Heavy Rain from 10.15 a.m. to
about noon — half clear during
P.M. & Rain again at night.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 29, 1876

Overcast & cold [illegible]. Bkfs.
at 8.15 — Soon after 10 o'clk. I
went by Vine St. car out to the
Cen. Sts. where my Comp. ticket
gave me entrance. I went at
once to Mem. Hall & spent a couple
of hours looking over the Castellani
Coll. met W. Snoch Lewis. Great
crowd — probably 3 or 4000 in the
Hall & Annex. Ate lunch at 1.30
out of doors to get warm — clear,
cloudless day. Until 4 o'clk in
the English, Austrian, French & German
depts. then to Race St. cars. Rode to
cor. of 23d St. — walked to W. E. Lewis's
& there met W. W. & Ge. Visited them
until near 5.30 — then walked to W.
W.'s — got there at 5.45 — went right to
bed & got a nap — Sea at 6.30 —
Speedingly tired — later in the
eve[illegible] my head stuffed up with
a cold — Took hot whiskey
& went to bed at 10 o'clk.
