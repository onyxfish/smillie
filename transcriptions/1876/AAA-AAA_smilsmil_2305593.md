## Left Page

### April, Monday 3, 1876.

Pouring rain pretty much all day & night. Got to studio
at 9.45 — Nicole there until 11
telling me of Centennial matters
at Phila where he has been &
of my failure at the "Century"
on Sat. night, which resulted
from no objection to me but spite
at Com. on Admissions. Commenced
the work of redrawing parts
of my Scmb Race. To dinner
at 1 & back at 2 — Scrabbled
out White Horse entries & parts
of others. Made pastei, etc.
To tea at 6.45 — back to shop
at 8.20, wrote until 10 —
then to 41 18? There I had
my feet scalded & finally got
into bed about 11 — The bathing
of my feet was owing to a cold
that is threatening my head.

## Right Page

### April, Tuesday 4, 1876.

Horrible day of Rain & wind.
Got to studios at 9.30 & spent
much of the m'f. mounting
drawings — then finally to
work on Scmb Race — to dinner
at 1, back at 2.15 — Scrabbled
away on Scmb Race — & got out
some more work — Eve'g, the
weather was so bad that I
accepted Miss Gilbert's invitation
& stayed to tea with her —
Chatted until near 9 o'clk —
then in to my own room and
wrote until near 11 — then to
41.8? Rain almost played
out, but lots of wind yet — Got
to bed ab't 11.30 [illegible — ~3 words]
