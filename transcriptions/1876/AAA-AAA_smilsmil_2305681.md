## Left Page

### September, Saturday 30. 1876.

Mixed but cloudy day — Got to Studio at 8.30 & for about half an hour picked up stones, then in & to work on "October." Painted until 11.30 — then with Mr. Tyler — a mistake in "corners" — nothing serious threatened. Then down town & did up a lot of chores. To dinner at 1 o'clk — then to Ado. & painted until 4.30 when Ge. came with buggy & we rode until a little after 6. Went to I. Sprout's. After tea went to Studio & cleaned up. Then at 8 went to Mr. Casper's. Ge. there — At 8.45 to Mr. Jessup's. At 9.30 to Fan's — Mr. Hurd & Miss Dunham there — went with Ge. up to Barety's to get Priscilla to come to Fan's to stay all night, which she did. Took Miss D — home — left Fan's at 11 & right to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 1. 1876.

Cold, cloudy & windy. Rkf. at 8.30 — about 10 went to Studio — at 10.30 to Presbn Ch. & heard Miller preach from Matt. 12 ch. 42 v. After service walked to Studio & wrote until about 1.30 — then walked until 2, where I had dinner. Again to Studio & wrote until about 5. Then walked to the lake & around by the new depot — in to tea at 6.30, but took nothing. Again went to Studio for about half an hour & finished my letters — to Mother, Maud D— & to Schaad — Then down town to P.O. & to Fan's — She goes to Phila. to-morrow my at 6 — Bid her good bye about 9.30 — Got to bed soon after 10.
