## Left Page

### June, Tuesday 20, 1876

Heavy shower early in the
mrt. Then clear with charm-
ing breeze — During the day
another shower. After
4.0p? Read papers & went
in town — Then set Palette
& worked over Montmorenci
Lake Raquette — & Penna.
Hills — getting them all
about finished — Cleaned
up about 5 o'clk. Tea
at 6 — about 7. Geo. Fartun
& I went to Bisby's — He
was not in — Saw Mrs. B.
At 8.30 I went to Opera Ho.
Concert of Night's School —
After the Concert, Reception
at the House — There until
11 o'clk. introduced to
many people — Home &
to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### June, Wednesday 21, 1876

Charmingly cool mrt.
Got back from walk about
10.30 but was so impressibly
lazy that I could not get to
work — Dinner at 12.30 — Had
a nap on the sofa & then
tried to do a little work —
no use — made some little
sketches from the Roof &
upper windows to finish
in Water Color for Mr. Geo.
Whitney — At 4.45 went in
town — got a wagon & Watt
& then the whole family &
we went 3 or 4 miles down
the South Road, on our way
back stopping at Mr. Toby's —
Glorious p.m — Tea about 7 —
Uncle Ch. arrd from N.Y. about
8.20 — Mr. Jornis called in
the evng. Got to bed soon
after 10.
