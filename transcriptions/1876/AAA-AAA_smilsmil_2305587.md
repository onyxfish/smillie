## Left Page

### March, Wednesday 22, 1876

Cold, windy, march like day — got to studio at 9.30
& spent all day trying to make something of one of my
oil sketches for big drawing
— was also much engaged in
preparing a sort of Report
for Annual W.C. Meeting to-
night. To dinner at 1.5 —
back at 2.15 — To tea at
6.30 — At 8 at 51 W. 10th St. —
Annual Meeting of the W.C.
Soety. Was re-elected to my
3d. term by unanimous vote —
free & good tempered meeting.
After stopping at studio, got
to 41 W.S. at 11.15 & to bed at
11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Thursday 23, 1876

Very fine day — Did not get
to studio until 10 o'clk. as
I felt the results of last night's
dissipation — Did little else
than charcoal sketch of old
Liberty Bell for a B.N. Co. &
write some letters — To lunch
at 1.30 — After that, as Mrs. D.
gave me her ticket, I walked
down to Thomas's Matinee — and
enjoyed the "Voica" for a rest.
After that, saw the Webb collection
— then to 625 B'way — tremendous
crush of Centennial pictures coming
in. Transacted a little business
then to Castlemachi's & bought casts
of lions heads — Then to tea at 6.40 —
back to studio at 8.30, made
letter to Geo — To 41st St. at
11 & to bed at 11.20.
