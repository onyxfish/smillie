## Left Page

### September, Monday 18, 1876

Still heavy Rain — a freshet has swollen my little brook until it runs all over the meadow & has ruined its banks so that I cannot hope to finish my studies from nature — I fixed up library with studio light & painted over one ground of Study No. 1, war Gardenie's ho. Dinner at 1.30 — continued painting until near 5 — then cleaned up things & walked on piazza until after 6 — Genl. D. Kei? from Watkins — Sea at 6.30 — Evef. cards as usual. I undertook to play Champion to May and got a clip over the head by a millinery hero for my pains — hit back — no blood — to bed at 11.
