## Left Page

### May, Monday 1. 1876.

*Philadelphia — at W. W's. Whitneys.*

Up at 6 Glorious morning — finished packing — R[R?]cp at 7.30 — attended to some little messages — went to Man, & saw Jno. thru to 9.30 Train foot of Desbroses St. There met Huntington, Hicks, Ward & McEntee. Got to Phila at 2.20 — went right out to Centennial grounds — back to dinner at W. W's at 2.30 & again to grounds at 5 — again in to tea at 7.20 — Every feel very tired but sat & chatted until near 10 — To bed at 10.30. Had a night of kicking & dreaming.

## Right Page

### May, Tuesday 2. 1876.

Charming day — Got out to the Cen. grounds about 9.30 found W. in great distress — could not get any men to help — I soon got 4 men and before 1 o'clk. we had run over at least 400 pictures, placing them in different galleries & picking out those for Mem. Hall. I tried to devote a little time to the W. C. Dep't. but could not get it & at 1.45 started for dinner at W. Whitney's — Got there at 2.30 & had ½ hour for rest, which I very much needed — Dinner at 3 — about 4 again went out to the grounds & worked at sending pictures over to M. Hall until 6 — At 7.30 got to W. W's. & had tea. Every. Spent in an easy chair & then writing a letter home — To bed at 10.30 — a very unRefreshing night of wakefulness & dreaming.
