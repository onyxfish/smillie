## Left Page

### January, Sunday 30. 1876.

Awake between 8 & 9 — but
did not get up until 10.30 —
Quite surprised to find
that I felt very well — I took
my Regular exercise — then
a good Rubbing down with my
hair mittens, from head to heel.
then a warm bath — at 11.45
a cup of tea & a little cracked wheat —
At 12.30 walked down to studio
fixed fire & then walked up
to A.N.N.'s. Charley G's younger
bro., Jacob, there to dinner — Charl
also joined us at 1.30 — At 4.30
C & I walked down, I all the way
to studio's — on lounge for half an
hour, then wrote a letter home —
to 41 S. at 9 & to bed at 10.40.

## Right Page

### January, Monday 31. 1876.

Overcast, dull & Raw — got to studio
at 9.45 & worked on charcoal
of "Rough Spot etc" — Made very
little progress as I cannot get
my horses keep in their places.
To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2 — with
my lay-figure horse & worked
from him during the afternoon
— I have not got onto my working
legs yet — Fani W. came in about
5 o'clk — I walked to W. A. & 7th
ave. with her, then to 41 S. after
hunting through lots of news-
papers for Socy. notices — To
tea at 6.30 — At 8 [illegible] at studio
there until 9 — then to W. A. &
D. There until after 10 — a very
good day — back to studio for
awhile — then at 10.45 to 41 —
