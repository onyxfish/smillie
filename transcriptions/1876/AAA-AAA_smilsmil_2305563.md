## Left Page

### February, Thursday 3, 1876

Pleasant not — thue overcast & at night a driving snow storm.
Got to studio's at 9.30 — worked on "Rough Sport" until 10.30
then to N.A. Frank Leslie came in at 11 by appointment & I am
with him until 12.30. Looking at the pictures & talking ceremonial business. Then to Snedecor & at 1.30 to dinner — back at
2.30 & worked all the p.m. on
Rough Sport, getting it about
ready for color. To tea at 6.30
at 8.30 at studio's — wrote letter
until 9 — then to N.A. at
10.15 again at studio's & at
10.40 to 41st St. & at 11 o bed.
[illegible — ~4 lines]
bed at 12.40

## Right Page

### February, Friday 4, 1876

Overcast day — about 8 in. of snow.
Beautiful moonlight night. Got
to studio's at 9.45 a.m. & about
11 got fairly started painting
on "Rough Sport" — to dinner
at 1.30 — back at 2.30 & painted
until dark. Getting in all of
back & fore ground — a brown
& muddy beginning in spite
of myself — Did not get to
tea until 6.45 — at 8 again
at studio's & wrote letter to the
Times — at 9 went to N.A. Here
until 9.50 — then to studio's &
finished Times letter & at
11.20 to 41 [illegible] St. & to bed at
12 o'clk. [illegible] — [illegible]
