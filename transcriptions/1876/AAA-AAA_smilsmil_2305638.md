## Left Page

### July, Tuesday 4, 1876

A very hot day but with a
delightful breeze — After
fitful sleeping much broken
by all sorts of banging noises
at 9.30 I got up & took my
bath — about 10.30 Charl. & I
had Hclp. & after 11 I went
to studio, then to N.A. [illegible]
to see the Centennial loan
pictures — met F. H. Smith &
Preston Spring — Had only a
running look at the pictures
most of which I knew — At
1.30 at Manjo to dinner —
at 2.45 started for the "Mary
Powell" & at 3.40 was off for
Po'keepsie when I arr'd. at
8 o'clk. — at Newburgh, struck
by thunder squall which tumbled
things about in a lively manner.
Home & had tea at 8.30. Matt &
E² came down — Then I detailed
my adventures & got to bed at
10.30.

## Right Page

### July, Wednesday 5, 1876

Still very hot weather — Up
late after some very sound
sleeping & got up I think at
8.30 — Suffering from severe
nervous exhaustion, so that
I could not get to work —
Finished outlining dag. of
"Gaucho" for Father — Dinner
at 12.30 — tried to work on
"Hillside" drawing but was
obliged to give it up — and at
4 o'clk. went to bed & slept
soundly until 6 — then tea —
all the eveg. felt very slim
& did not go out of house —
About 9 o'clk. got started
up on a little writing.
To bed at 10.30
