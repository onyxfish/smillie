## Left Page

### November, Friday 17, 1876

Pleasant mrf. Later in the day promise of change —
got to studio soon after. Miss Spring came &
most of the mrf I worked on a small W.C. sketch
while she overlooked me. Miss Sherrard of Phila.
Miss Hay & two others called. To lunch at 1.30 — back
at 2.30 & much of the m was again spent on W.C.
Sketch. — To tea at 6.30.
Eve'g I called upon the Ripleys at their
boarding place, 2 W. 59. They are still very tender
upon the subject of the death of their [illegible] Dwight. — Thence
until a little after 10, home to 41, S- & to bed.

## Right Page

### November, Saturday 18, 1876

Overcast with light rain in the mrf. Got to studio
at 9.30 — Miss Spring soon followed me. I had many
callers but tried to work on charcoal design "in a coal
mine", for the A.B.N.Co.
About 12 May Liscum came in & invited me to go over
to the Hoffmans Ho. & lunch with her, which I did —
back at 2.30 — tried to work on W.C. Sketch — Nicoll came
in with a note from the N.A. of D — They want $18.00. Rent
from the W.C. body. Went over to see Richards — not in — then
down & saw Huntington — had a free talk with him — to
tea at 7 — Eve'g running about to Remeis, Burgoal [illegible] — In
at 9.30 & to bed before 10 —
