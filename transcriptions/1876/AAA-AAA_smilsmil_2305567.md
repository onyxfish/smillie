## Left Page

### February, Friday 11, 1876

Miserable raw day with a nuzzling mist at times — Got to studios at 10, feeling very slim of course — T.H. Smith came in for half an hour. Then kindled fire & about 11, went with Colman to the new building cor. of 25th S: & 4th ave. Climbed about there until 12.30 — then to N.A. Met Marcham, Mr Colman, Mary Tweedy & others there — then to Y.M.C.A. & called on Wm Hart & Swain Gifford about Centennial pic. To dinner at 1.30 — To Phil. Rehearsal at 2.30 — Walked up 4th Ave with Mary & Florence I. & stopped cor. of 25th S: to studios at 5.30 — To tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 at W.C. Society Rooms 51 W. 10th S: a little, informal meeting of members & friends — very pleasant & a real success — the beginning I am sure of better things. Left at 10.30 — Got to bed at midnight.

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 12, 1876

Brilliant beautiful day. Got to studios at 9.45 & spent the day drawing separate horses, entire, for my group in "Rough Sport" so that I may get the legs & tails all in their Right places — To dinner at 1.15 — after dinner dressed myself & got to studios about 3. Worked until 4.30 then to building 25th S: & 4th ave, then to N.C. then to Brooklyn — Mr Beach 96. Columbia Heights — just 6 o'clk. H.W. Beecher [illegible] Wilmarth & several Students League ladies there. Half hour chat with B & W. Tea at 7 — at 8.30 Ret. to N.Y. — to N.A. at 9.30 — thru at 10.15 to studios — to 41st S: at 1.30 & to bed at 12 —
