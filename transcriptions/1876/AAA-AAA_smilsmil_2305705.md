## Left Page

### November, Sunday 19, 1876

Dark day with rain and wind. B'cb' at 9.30 & at 10.30 went down to studio to see to my fire, then the storm came down so heavy that I did not care to go out but stayed in looking over old pamphlets and arranging things until after 4 o'clk. without getting any dinner. Then, in a driving, heavy rain to 41 & 3 & about 5.30 had a hearty tea. On sofa, reading until after 8 — then made two or three notes & to bed at 10 o'clk. Rain & wind still having a high old time.

## Right Page

### November, Monday 20, 1876

Horrible day, tearing storm of wind & rain. Got to studio at 9.30 — Miss Spring came. I worked on a W.C. sketch to show her my methods & also on charcoal sketches of coal mining for A.B.N. Co. At 1.30, lunched in my studio — worked as long as I could see — at 4.30 or 5, Nicoll, Bellows, J.G. Brown & H. Robbins came & we had a "Board meeting" — to agree upon the terms to offer the N.A. of D. To tea at 6.30. Eve'g, storm somewhat abated — went to studio & there until near 11 o'clk. fussing in closet, trying to arrange things — then to 4½ S— & to bed.
