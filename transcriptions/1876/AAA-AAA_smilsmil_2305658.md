## Left Page

### August, Tuesday 15. 1876.

Horribly hot. Proposed to start work again out of doors, but my head warned me that it would be unsafe — so I prepared a sketch of out-[illegible] for Wree & worked on Fable[ts] until about 1 o'clk. Dinner soon after. Again worked on Fables — great promise of a Rain Storm about the middle of p.m. — but it only resulted in a tremendous tornado of dust & dirt. Jack started off on a Canadian pleasure trip about 8. p.m. — Every in piazza until near 9 o'clk — then awhile at cards & about 11 o'clk when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### August, Wednesday 16. 1876.

Charming mng. Soon after went out to oat-field and again went to work on oil study commenced some time ago. Painted in the large group of trees & stopped work at 12.40 — Dinner soon after 1. Afternoon, painted in fore-ground of will study, which has just been ploughed up. Then painted in part of study on brook, also commenced some time ago. Cloudy, windy p.m. but no hopes of rain — the day has been pleasantly cool — Some cal. Jack in the evng & to bed soon after 10 o'clk.
