## Left Page

### June, Friday 30, 1876

Hot, but not so oppressive as the preceding days —
After b[reakfas]t, wrote & at 10
went in town — About 11
called at the Williams's &
saw Miss Wheeler & the ladies
chatted of art matters until
about 12.30 — then home to
dinner. After dinner, to
work on "Hill Side" drawing
& kept at it until near
4.30 — then David & carriage
came & with Kate & Geo. I
went out to Hill Side, after
stopping for Mrs. Dudley &
Matt. Had tea at 6.30
then all wandered in
the woods until 8 — on
piazza until 9 — then rode
home — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### July, Saturday 1, 1876

Hot again — or rather, yet.
After b[reakfas]t — wrote to Maud
& to Belle J — went to P.O —
& back to work on Hill Side
drawing about 10.30 — Dinner
at 12.30    worked again on
drawing but made little
progress, there seeming to be
a fundamental error that
I cannot overcome. Washed
out a large part of one of
the trees & at 6 gave up
for the day — Charl. am—
from N.Y.    Jea at 6. Every
played the hose until
9 — To bed about 10.
