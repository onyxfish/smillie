## Left Page

### October, Friday 20. Room 59, 1876.

*Wyoming Valley House, Wilkes-Barre*

Up soon after 6 — made letters to Father, Will & Markham
before bkfp. at 7.30 — About
8.30 went to Studio with Sayrs
men & furnace — made a
final fixing up & left at 9.15
Rushed around bidding some
good-byes & off on 10.15 train —
Tunkhannock at 12 — left at 12.30
got to Wilkes-Barre at 2 — went
first to Wyoming Valley House —
then to Sulphine mine — went
down that, 450 feet & walked
more than 2 miles underground —
Made a number of sketches
in the dark & got back
to Hotel about 6 or 6.30 or
later when I got my tea —
was exceedingly tired —
dozed in parlor until
9 o'clk — then to bed.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 21. 1876.

*at W. Geo. Whitney's, 247 N. 18th St., Philadelphia*

Up at 6.30 — bkfp. at 7.30 &
off for Phila. at 8.30 — the
mts. very nice & somewhat
overcast. Enjoyed the
climb by R.R. over the mt.
Rain came on after we
left Whitehaven but did
not last long — We reached
Phila. at 9th & Greene St. over
the Perkiomen branch of
the P. & R. R.R. at 2.30 —
got to W. W's about 3
& had dinner at once.
From 4.30 until 9.30 we
rested & chatted — then
tea — everg. W. & Mrs.
W., Miss Ely & I indulged
in a little whist —
To bed at 10.
