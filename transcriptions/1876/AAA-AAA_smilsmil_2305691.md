### October, Sunday 22, 1876

Sombre day — at 10.30
Geo & I went to Epis.
Ch. Cor of 19th & Walnut —

I enjoyed the service

Dinner about 2 —
Afternoon, wrote home.

until after 8 o'clk —

## Right Page

### October, Monday 23, 1876

Dull, muggling mf. Bkfst.
at 8, after which Geo &
I got started for Steam R.R.
cars at Collowhill — 8' 8" —
got to Cen. Grounds about 10
went at once to Memorial
Hall — great jam of people.
Saw Sartain. Got through
part of annex by 12 — then
to Main Bldg — there until
1 — then G. & I ate our lunches
out of doors & at 1.20 started
into Machinery Hall — Geo
kept up remarkably well
& at 3 started for home — I
called on Mr Elwath & did
the Shoe & Leather Bldg — Farming
& Machinery Hall — at 4 I started
for cars & got on to wrong train &
was carried in to 9th & Greene —
got to the house at 5.45 — dinner
not yet over — Eveg & put
Reading up for to-morrow.
to bed at 10.30. Cleared up
in the middle of the day — Rains at
night on going to bed.
