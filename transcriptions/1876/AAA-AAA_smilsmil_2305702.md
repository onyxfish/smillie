## Left Page

### November, Monday 13, 1876

Delightful mild day —
At 10, at Sun Mutual Int.
in Wall St. — Soon arranged
Ins. matters — then to Club
& to Will's — Got to dinner
in 41st at P.15 & found
Mother there — just down
from Po. Took her to Mamp
& back to Studio at 2.30
Misses Gilbert & Durfee
& Mrs. Hawley called —
Beutson came & put up
my Stove. To tea at 6.30
— at 8 went to Mamp's
& spent the even't with
Mother & did some writing.
It was near 11 when
I got to bed. [illegible — ~5 lines]

## Right Page

### November, Tuesday 14, 1876

Pleasant m'g — overcast
later in the day — Got to
Studio at 9.30 Kindled
fire in my Stove — All day
at work with my books
& other muss — To dinner
at 1 — at 2 went to Mamp
to see mother — She left
for Po — with Willie at
3.30 — At 3 at Studio —
Miss Abbatt helped
me arrange my books —
at 6 I went to see
Coleman who is still
confined to the house.
Met Louis Tiffany there —
To tea at 6.30 — Rain in
the even't — felt out of
sorts — went to Burgraff's
& to Mamp's — in at 9
o'clk & to bed at 10.
