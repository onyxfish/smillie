## Left Page

### November, Saturday 25, 1876

Charming day. Did not get to studio until 9.30. Miss Spring came and I made a slight sketch for her & then rubbed out & painted in again a part on small sketch "Conway meadows". To lunch at 1 back at 2 & as much of the p.m. as I could get. I worked on "Buttercups on Newton Creek." The Tweedy's, Maud Diven, Miss Spring's sister & Miss Abbatt's sister & others called. At 5.30 went to Wilmarths & Deaints — To tea at 6.30 — Therf, after going to Burggrafs, Renner & others went to Studio & there until 10.30 — then to 4[illegible] 57 & to bed at [illegible] at & went [illegible] at 11 [illegible] in since ho[illegible] at [illegible]

## Right Page

### November, Sunday 26, 1876

A horrible day of snow and rain — Did not get up until 9 — had bkfst. at 10.15 & to Hastings at 11 — Heard him preach on the letters the Spirit from 2° Cor. 3° ch. 6° v. After service walked to Studio — met Avery & he went in with me. At 1.30 to Hoffman Ho & at 3 took dinner with Maud, Mary & Jack Diven. After 4, Jack & I went over to Studio — Jack there for ½ an hour or more. At 6 I went to Mary's & in the evng had a quiet time in Charles Room writing. To 11.8 and to bed at 10.30.
