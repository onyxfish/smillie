## Left Page

### August, Friday 11, 1876

Felt very slim & shaky, but was able to get up & report
at the blsp. table. About 8.30 Rode in to town with Maud
but missed D. Seeley — It was 11 o'clk, before we got
through messages, calling on Jan etc & back to the
house — I lettered a number of Tablets before dinner at
1 — then awhile on lounge. At 5 Maud, May, Capt. Geo
& I Rode in town — calling on Eugene & wife — at the Reed,
doing messages & waiting for the D. until near 7 — he did
not come so we returned home & had a late tea. Geo. Dwen came
in & for a time, with him, the Gen'l,
Capt. & I had games of Cal.
Jack. The Gen'l & I had very bad luck. Got to bed about
10.30.

## Right Page

### August, Saturday 12, 1876

Still intensely hot, dry weather.
Feel better to-day than any time since last Sunday. In
to myself, Right after blsp. I Rode in to town with Maud &
did up the usual messages & a flying call on Jan. Back
at 11 & to work on lettering Tablets. Dinner at 1.30 —
worked again after dinner.
Sky overcast — no Rain, but the shade with southerly breeze
a mitigation of the heat. I went out & did a little
tramping about. Then in and washed & changed — Tea at 6.30
Evelf. Capt. Reed, wife & Jan came
and a genial time followed including some Cal. Jack with Jan,
Capt. Gen'l. & I — great good luck to gen'l & me this time. Soaked cracker & panada generally distributed about 10.30 — Got to bed
at 11.
