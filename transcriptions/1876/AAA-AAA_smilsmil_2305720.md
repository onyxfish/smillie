## Left Page

### December, Tuesday 19. 1876.

Hardly able to get up with headache & sore throat.
Bkf. soon after 9 — went up to Burggrafs — then to Mamy's &
saw Geo. — got to studio about 11 — Very soon after Mr. Geo.
Whitney came in — then A. F. Bellows — then W. Surch
Lewis & at 1 o'clk. we went to the Albemarle & [illegible]
Mr. W. joining us (Mabis, W.L. & me) back at 2.40 to find
that father had left about 10 m. before — R. S. Gifford came
in about 3 o'clk. — to talk over the everlasting sales man
question — my head hurt me so that I did but little —
To tea at 6.30 — Everf at 8 o'clk. at Chickering
Hall — Mr. Taylor Johnston
sale — there until 11.15
then to 41? S. & to bed a
little before 12 —
