## Left Page

### July, Sunday 16, 1876

A Heavenly day — About
10.15 am. W. & Mrs. Williams
called & saw the two drawings.
Then I went to dedication of
new Baptist Chapel in Mill
St. Tremendous beg from Tenchi
— kept us until 12.45 — then had
to walk down home for Minn to
take her to Matt's, where Father,
Kate, Minna B. & I took dinner.
Ret'd home at 3 o'clk. & spent
the P.m. picking up & away my
things to get away to morrow —
Sea ab. about 8 called
on Mrs. Loomis & after 9 went
away over to Group's & paid
his bill. Home & to bed
at 11. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### July, Monday 17, 1876

Very pleasant day, but I got
up sick — which was not
pleasant. arg Mr. Loomis &
3 young ladies called & saw the
drawings — then I set to work
in a feeble way packing to
move to W. Lurch Lewis and
got his drawings off. Dinner
at 12.30 & after much tribulation
at 4 o'clk. I had my trunk
packed. At 4.30 exp. man
came for trunks at 5. I walked
in & delivered W. C.H.S. Williams'
pictures to W. Jerry might & got
W. Lewis's drawings by e/b,
then to tea at 6 — at 6.30 all off in
carriage for Jul. N. Hasbrouck.
Great heat & delay at the different
landings — finally to bed and
passed a miserable, stewing
night. [illegible — ~2 lines]
