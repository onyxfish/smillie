## Left Page

### December, Thursday 21, 1876

Dismal, with snow — after stopping at Remus's shop about frames, got to studio at 9.45 & to work Tracing "Under the Butternut" Will came in with sub. &c for small drawing — at 1.30 prepared lunch & ate it — much troubled with my eyes & headache. Magrath came in — & Miss Durfee — after 6.30 when I got to tea —
About 8 stopped in at Mary's & saw Geo on my way up to Harris Pardee's 16 W. 47th St. found him confined to the house — I tried to get permission to send my "Scrub Race" up to his house — just to try if it is too large, as he thinks — said he would consult with his wife — back & on after 10 & to bed at 10.45.

## Right Page

### December, Friday 22, 1876

Feeling very much under the weather, it was after 10 when I got to studio — found Charl there ahead of me — Bellows, Nicoll & F.H. Smith in Coleman's room, where we talked up a Brown-Durfee compromise — Both to serve at N.C. Society as salesmen — Geo came in. I did a little on Tracing my large drawing. At 12 I went to Sauthiers, then to Magrath's & Silva's — to 41st St. to lunch (in a snowstorm) at 1.30 — back to studio at 2.30. At 4.30 F.H.S. came & met Miss Durfee — Soiss [illegible] came in & made a long call — at 6.10 to Kimball's. Miss Hone there — Tea at 6.45 — Concert of the Arnold Choral Club but I could not go owing to my cold. At 8 went to Knœdler's, there until 9 — there to studio — there until 10.15 — then to 41st St. & to bed at 10.45.
