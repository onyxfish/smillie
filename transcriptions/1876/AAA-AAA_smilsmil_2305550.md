## Left Page

### January, Thursday 6, 1876

Dull mud again with a light rain — Got to shop
at 9.20 and to work on White horse — Found lots
of trouble, as I expected in getting tone, color & modeling.
To dinner at 1.15 — Got a copy of Appleton's Journal
Jany 8. with flattering description of my big drawing — Back
at 2.15 & worked as long as I could see — At 5.30 walked
down to Sautiers — not in —
then to studio — note to Parsons
to tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 called
on the Ripleys, 13 E. 32 & had
a very pleasant evening until
10.15 — then to 41 [illegible] & to
bed at 11.

## Right Page

### January, Friday 7, 1876

Pleasant winter day — Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on White horse. All day at it without being able to get the
drawing or effect that I wish.
I cannot make him come forwards from the group — Rec'd a
letter from W. Geo. Whitney in which he again sends me $250.
on acc't of something, he taking the risk. To dinner at 1 — back
at 2.15 — worked as long as I could see — To tea at 6.45
after writing a letter to Geo.
Back to studio at 8.30 and wrote a letter to W. Whitney.
At 11 to bed — Glorious moonlight night. [illegible — entire passage]
