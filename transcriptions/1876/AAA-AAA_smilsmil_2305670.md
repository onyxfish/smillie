## Left Page

### September, Friday 8. 1876.

Charming mng. Breezy with clouds. Painted on study
No. 2 near Gardener's house &
finished painting in — Geo.
D. went to NY. this mng.
Dinner at 1.45 — Afternoon
Cap. May, Geo. & Ella Took
a drive to the top of Mt. Zoar.
I went out in the meadow
& worked on large water-
color — Quite chilly & very
much bothered by the mud —
Worked until 6 — Tea
at 7 — Maude used up with
headache — Every in Sid.
a little more whist. May
& I paid for our good luck
last night. At 10 washed
up my brushes & at 10.30
to bed.

## Right Page

### September, Saturday 9. 1876.

Raw, overcast mng. with slight
sprinkle of rain — About 10 it
broke away & after that, windy
cloudy day — Went out with
paper & pencils & made an
outline sketch at Gardener's
gate — Spent rest of mng
sketching a group of wheels —
Dinner at 1.30 — Again went
out & made sketch near garden-
er's gate looking up the lane.
In at 4.30 — Rolled up on a
bed for half an hour trying to get
warm — Then up & dressed &
with Ella, May, Jack, Cap. &
Geo. at 6 went to Mrs. Langdon's
to tea. Geo. D. Aleck's wife &
Mrs. Falk there besides the family —
Mr. & Mrs. Chas. L. & Mr. & Mrs. Crane.
At 7.30 an oyster & partridge supper.
Every spent chatting & looking
over photographs — Glorious moon-
light night — Jack & I walked part
way home. All left L.'s at 10 o'clk,
to bed at 11.45.
