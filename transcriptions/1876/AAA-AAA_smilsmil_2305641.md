## Left Page

### July, Wednesday 12, 1876

Very hot but much milder in character than yesterday —
muggy, rather, & moist —
Did not go out for a walk but commenced work on
"Cathedral Spires" & stuck to it until dinner time
at 12.30 — After that made a sketch for Minnie in her
album — then again on drawing until 5.30 —
Tea at 6.30 then a game or two of Croquet — [illegible]
Kate, Minnie & I went to
Dr. Bailey's to consult about Mother's condition
as she is now on her back from her old enemy —
Back near 10 — wrote awhile & at 10.30 took my
letter to P.O — To bed at
11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### July, Thursday 13, 1876

Very hot but tempered by a nice breeze — Did not
go out for a walk in the
mng. but got at Yosemite
drawing — Rubbed out foreground figures & put
them in considerably
larger. Dinner at 12.30 —
worked on drawing until
near 5 o'clk. when at
last I came to conclusion
that I could do nothing
more on it. Tea at 6.
About 8, Kate & I went
next door & called for
the first time on our neighbors
the Millard's & Saylers —
There until near 10 —
Then home & to bed at
10.30 — Mother slowly
improving.
