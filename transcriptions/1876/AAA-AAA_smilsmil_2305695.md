## Left Page

### October, Monday 30, 1876

Another overcast wet, clearing about 10 o'clk. Bkfs. at B. Sttt
so tired & suffering from cold that I determined to keep
Sunday to-day — Atq. 15 Rode
in carriage to Cen. Sup. with
Misses Ely & Hotchkiss & Ret?
then to the house at 10.30. Spent
the rest of the mtf. cramming
from catalogues — lying on the
sofa & doing a little writing.
Lunch at 1 — P.M. Spent
in Resting & Reading —
at 6.30 High tea — W.T. Richards
expected but did not come.
Later in the evtf Mr. Moch
Lewis called. To bed
at 10.30

## Right Page

### October, Tuesday 31, 1876

Dull day but quite mild.
Mr. Mock Lewis called early
& invited us to tea at his house
to-night — 9.30 before we got
started for the Sup. — Drove
around to Penn. R.R. Depot &
secured seats & tickets for N.Y.
on Sat. mtf. There at 10.15 got
to typtn there until 2 o'clk —
then home — Rested ½ an hour
then dinner & another rest
but felt very much out of sorts
when at 6 o'clk. I went alone
up to Mr. L's (gas gave out)
found Miss Justice & Mr. Ames then
after tea Miss Burnham & gentleman
came in — In spite of great bodily
discomfort had a pleasant time
until 9 — then home & right to bed
& had a horrible time with
pain & sickness until mtf. From
excessive tiring then seemed almost
a cessation of functions — no pulse —
no digestion —
