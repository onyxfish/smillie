## Left Page

### February, Thursday 17, 1876

Overcast, with a tendency to
snow & squalls — got to Studio
at 9.15 & to work again on
"Sierras" Painting all over
the distance — To dinner at
1.15 — back at 2.15 & worked
as long as I could see — To
Tea at 6.45 — Uncle W. and
Aunt A. there — At 7.30 went
to Mamy's — Saw Mother —
To Studio at 8.30 — wrote
to N.A. at 9.45 — again at
Studio — made needs near
11 — then to 241 S = & to bed
at 11.20

[illegible — entire passage]

O.S.J.

## Right Page

### February, Friday 18, 1876

Pleasant day — Got to Studio
at 9.20 & spent the morning
painting over foreground and
middle ground trees in Sierras.
To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30
— to Studio for a few minutes,
then walked to Phil. Rehearsal
got there about 3.15 — not
anxious to be early as Lester's
"On the Mountains" led on the
programme. After Rehearsal
walked up 4th Ave. with Belle
J. & Corinne C. Smith, Stopped
at new Studio — got to old one
at 5.45 — To Tea at 6.25 — then
to Mamy's & at 7.15 took Mother
to hear Moody & Sankey. Tremendous
crush but got a good place and
was rewarded by a grand sight.
The Rush of 8000 people into the
great building, filling it in 7 minutes
when the doors were closed, is beyond
description — good effect of singing —
Moody interesting but common. At
8.30 went to N.A. — Dr. took Mother —
& went to Studio — To bed at 11.15
