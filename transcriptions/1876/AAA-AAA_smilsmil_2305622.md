## Left Page

### June, Friday 2, 1876

Still cloudless but hazier & warmer. Bkfst. at 7.30 — at 8 went into town with father & to the new Baptist Ch. to see how the work inside was progressing. On my Return got to work on Adirondack Lake. Dinner at 12.30 — at 2 again at work and about 6 got first foaining done — very crude in form & color. Tea at 6.30 Evest. called on the Hasbrouck's — saw the Dr. & Mrs. H. Jennie (Mrs. Mandaville) and three teachers from W. Wright's across the way. Home at 10 o'clk. & to bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Saturday 3, 1876

Intensely hot. Took no walk in the mrf, but got to work about 10 o'clk, after writing some letters, on 6×10, painting "A Shady Place". Dinner as usual at 12.30 — Again at work at 2 & finished all but the figure about 5 o'clk. Then cleaned up & watched the weather — Heavy thunder storms north & south of us — Tea at 6. About 7 it began to rain very gently, but about 8 a furious storm passed over us with tremendous thunder & lightening & heavy rain. After 9 o'clk — did some more writing & got to bed soon after 10.
