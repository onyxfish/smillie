## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 29, 1876

Some snow in the m'f. Got to studio at 9.45 & to work on "Rough Sport" — Painted all day on the horses, putting in detail & finish — To dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.30 — Big drawing came from N.A. — This is a dismal looking thing to me & I'm obliged to confess another failure — Worked until after 5 — Then trying to clear — went over to 25th St. & 4th Ave. To N.A. — then to 41 [illegible] to tea at 6.30 — At 8, again at Studio & wrote seven letters got through at 10 o'clk & then to 4-1 [illegible] & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### March, Wednesday 1, 1876

Horrible day — dark & gloomy with rain & snow at times — got to studio at 9.30 — Interrupted by several calls — did not begin painting on "Rough Sport" until after 11 — To dinner at 1.30 back at 2.30 & worked until near 5 — then B. of Control met in my Studio — J.G. Brown — Robbins — Wood — Smith & Bricher — Nicoll — Sele — To tea at 6.30 — At 7.45 called on W. Jas. Fraser, 26 W. 51 — not in — then on Charl- at 22 W. 51 — S'ele with cold — there until 8.10 — then to studio wrote 4 letters — To 41' at 10.30 & was kept by D'[illegible] listening to organ music that he played until 11.30 — to bed at 11.45
