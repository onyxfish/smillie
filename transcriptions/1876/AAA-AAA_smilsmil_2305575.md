## Left Page

### February, Sunday 27, 1876

Overcast day. Up at 8. Scrubbed, bathed & gymnasticatied — thin bkfst. at 9.30 — at 10.30 went to Hastings's — a stranger preached after service, walked down to studio — fixed fire — chatted with Colman & back to 41 4th. to dinner at 1.15 — at 3 again at studio & spent the P.M. in getting some of the disorder cleared up. At 6.15 to Mamy's to tea — at 7.30 with W. Hertzel, took Matt & Kate to Ch. at Madison ave. & 54th St. on coming out found it almost impossible to walk, Rain falling & freezing — left the girls at Mamy's. went to 41 st. put on big boots & again went to studio, wrote to father — 41 4th. at 11.15 & to bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### February, Monday 28, 1876

Horrible day — Heavy Rain & sleet. Ice on sidewalks nearly half an inch thick. Got to studio at 10 o'clk. So dark that I did not dare work on either "Sierras" or "Rough Spot" & so set to work on one of the oil sketches that I painted for "Boat Race" & painted it all up except the group of figures & horses — To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.30 & painted as long as I could see — 5.15 — Silva came in & at 6.10 walked up with him — tea at 6.30 — at 8.30 again at studio — a little midnight at 9.15 went to the Kimball's — Matt & Kate there all night. Left at 10.30 — to 41 st. & to bed at 11.
