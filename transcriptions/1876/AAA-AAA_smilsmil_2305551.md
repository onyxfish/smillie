## Left Page

### January, Monday 10, 1876

Cloudy, then clear — very high wind & much dirt. Went up to Burgraf's — Did not get to studio until 10 — Feeling very unlike work. Mrs J — Richards called & most of the mf was spent with him — To dinner at 1.15 — back at 2.20 & the rest of the p.m. was given to working over the foreground of big drawing — I have washed out & in most of it several times — To tea at 6.40 — At 8.30 called on Mrs. Thorne — going South — bid her good bye — At 9.30 at studio — wrote a note to Neuheim — To 44 5th & to bed at 11.10 — Slight snow flurry in early evng & a glorious moonlight after.

## Right Page

### January, Tuesday 11, 1876

Cold & blowing like a March day. Got to studio's at 9.20 & to work on foreground — It is a regular mess — Coleman does not like the foreground color — I scrabbled it out again — To dinner at 1.30 back at 2.30 — worked until near 5 — R. S. Gifford came in — Went to Devir's & paid bill & ordered new coat — To tea at 6.40 — At 8 went in to Rennie's frame store & at 9 called at Mr. Ch?. Delrau's — not in — then to studio's — wrote until after 10 — To 41 1 83 at 10.45 & right to bed. [illegible — ~4 lines]
