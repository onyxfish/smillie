## Left Page

### July, Sunday 30, 1876

Cloudy mild day. Bkfst. about 8 o'clk... Went to Ch.
with Ella & Calp'e & heard
W. Knox preach a sermon
from Acts 26 ch. 25th v. After
service we called for a few
moments at Calp'e Reed's &
& saw fan Mulford & Mrs. Balcom & Grannies. Then rode
home & had dinner at about
1.30 — After dinner walked
over to Eldridge Park with
Jack & Calp'e. Wandered &
loafed there until after 4 —
Then Rain began falling lightly
& we Ret'd home. Nick and
family & Eugene & [illegible]
came & about 7. We had
tea — After & when they left
in the Rain — I walked the
piazza with Jack until near
9 o'clk — then in — Pleasant
time in library — wrote a
while & to bed at about 10,

## Right Page

### July, Monday 31, 1876

Overcast with promise of
Rain — After bkfst — got out
my water color traps and to
work on the little views from
home windows at Pa. for Geo.
Whitney — Dinner at 1.30
After dinner went out to the
oat field & tried to get a
sketch of some oat sheaves.
Did not get much & Returned to
work on "Coulsons" — After tea
at 6, quite a heavy shower —
after that Mr & Mrs. Reed. Mr.
Grannies of Chicago & fan Ne-
came — After general chatty
went into library & played whist
until after 10. Shortly after
11 I got to bed.

Genl. D. Rid. from N.Y.
at 6.35 P.M.
