## Left Page

### June, Sunday 4, 1876

Overcast & extremely muggy & close. Bkfs. at
8 — Mrs, went with geo. to Christ's Ch. (Epis.) Rev.
W. Zeigenfuss. Enjoyed the service there very much.
After that walked awhile then, fearing a shower —
home. Dinner at 1 —
About 4 went out & walked about through the outskirts
of town & finally stopped in again at Christ's Ch. —
To tea at 6. Very very light Rain — made some
more — to-day I have got off six letters — went
up to P.O. about 10.30 — back & to bed about 11.30.

## Right Page

### June, Monday 5, 1876

Overcast day, but very much fresher & cooler — After bkfs,
walked down South Road — then past Spring Side & back home
by Academy St. There to work on new picture 12×14 — "A
Recollection of Montmorenci Falls" — Dinner at 12.30
To work at 2 but not in good trim — fought along
until 6 o'clk, then had to give up before quite finished.
— Tea — rather under the weather. About 8 walked
to Gromp's. On my way back — at 9 o'clk. Stopped
at the Fonda's — there until near 10 — then home
— a little writing & to bed at 10.45.
