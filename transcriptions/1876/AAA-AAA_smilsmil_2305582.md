### March, Sunday 12, 1876

Nasty day — B[illegible] at 9 o'clk
At 10.30 at T. H. Smith's —
there until after 11 — then to
studio — there until 12.45 —
then walked up to Horace
Robbins's, 137 2, 60' S? and
dined — very quiet & pleasant —
At 4.30 walked out to the
park & soon after 5 went
in to Uncle Al's — tea at
7 — At 8, walked down to
studio — wrote a letter to
George — To 41 S. at 11 o'clk
& to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Monday 13, 1876

Overcast m[orning], clearing off very
fresh & windy — Up late — did not
get to studio until 10 — then to
work on Centennial business
& spent the whole day writing
letters — to dinner at 1.15 — back
at 2.15 — At studio until after
5 — then to new studio — Scratch
coat put on to-day — then down
to Silva's — 806 B[roadwa]y — not in — then
to Leavitt's — then to Somerville's &
finally after much bother found out
where Tiffany's Mosque picture is
owned — To tea at 6.30 — at 8 called
on Jerry Milbank to get Tiffany's big
W.C. for Centennial — M. very gracious —
there until 9 — then to studio wrote
awhile — To 41 S. at 10.45 — to bed
at 11.
