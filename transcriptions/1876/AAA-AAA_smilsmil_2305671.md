## Left Page

### September, Sunday 10, 1876

Cold, Raw, m[illegible]. with Rain about 10 that fell slowly &
continuously. Did not go to church but sat in library by cheerful fire & wrote letters.
Cap. Reid & wife came in about 11 & stayed an hour.
Dinner at 1.15. After dinner tried to mist out
could'nt — then took a nap. Eugene, wife & child came at 4 o'clks. Tea at 6.30 — then they left. Everg. finished long letter to father — chatting — to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### September, Monday 11, 1876

Dismal Rain with cold wind — Bright fire in library — Fixed the lights to suit myself & made that my studio for the day. Painted faithfully on 9×16 "Harvest field — North". Dinner at 1.45. Worked until 5 — could no longer — gave it up. Tea at 6 — Everg. as usual, in the library — Whist — as usual, very unfortunate — to bed at 10.30.
