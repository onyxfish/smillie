## Left Page

### November, Sunday 5, 1876

Very pleasant day — R[illegible]
at 9.30 — at 10.30 went
to Hastings's — felt very
sleepy — could say that
I heard his sermon from
St. Luke 6th Ch, 12', 13' v —
After service a short
walk with Charl — Dinn
in 41 [illegible] at 1.30 — Before
I went to Mam's — with
Charl went up to Mill's
& had tea there, Susy
went with Mill & family
& heard Hepworth —
walked home with Dr.
A.P.J, To 41 [illegible] at 10
& Right to bed. [illegible — ~3 words]

## Right Page

### November, Monday 6, 1876

Overcast day — Got to
studio soon after 9 — the
dirt & confusion passed
all description — I set
to work & at 12.30, already
tired & having made little
progress, I went to dinner
back at 2 o'clk, and
continued my distracting
work until about 5, then
called on the Kimballs —
saw them all & at 6
left for tea at 6.30 in 41
[illegible]. Susy at 8 went to
Mam's & stayed in
Charl's Room until 10 &
home to 41 [illegible] & to bed
