## Left Page

### October, Friday 6, 1876

Dismal day — Heavy mist & rain. Got up to
studio about 9 — Finished "Pasture" then worked for an
hour on W.C. sketch "No place like Home" — Dinner
at 1 — worked a while on W.C. study (unfinished) "on
Newtown Creek" — Finished "Home" sketch — packed "Pasture"
& at 4 o'clk. took it down to Sylo. Office. Then went out to
ride with Ge°. The clouds had blown over but it was very hazy,
chilly & windy — back at 6 —
Rec'd very pleasant letter from Mr. Whitney — Tea at 6.15 — at 7
Ge° & I went up to studio. I mate until 9.30 — to meet
Mr. Ellinwood & Dr. Yale —
Then took letters to P.O. &
got to bed at 10.30.

## Right Page

### October, Saturday 7, 1876

Heavy wind from N.W. Spiteful,
spitting & cold — clouds & sunshine —
Picked up stones from 9 until 10 —
Then fussed about studio until
near noon, then down town
& attended to some "chores" — To
dinner at 1 — after that to shop
& worked on "a Coupon" for W.
Ge° Whitney. Picked up stones
then to tea at 6 — then to P.O.
at 7.45 with Ge° — called on Mr.
Jas. DeWitt & Miss Jones —
at 8.30 on Mrs. Schaeffer at
Mr. Ge°. Bentley's — at 9.45
to Hy. Tyler's & gave him a
deed of Right of Way. Sat then
& chatted with him, his wife &
mother in law until 10.20 —
To bed at 10.45 —
a little flumy with nail
at 1 o'clk.
