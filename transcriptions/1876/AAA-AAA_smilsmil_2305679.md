## Left Page

### September, Tuesday 26, 1876

Tremendous, pouring rain —
Got up to studio before
9 & fussed about some
time before beginning work
then Commenced a 6×10
painting from a meadow
sketch near Gent. Diven's
& worked until 1 o'clk. Then
to dinner — back at 2 &
painted until 5 when I
finished sketch — cleaned
up — the storm had blown
off and the wind was high.
So tea at 6. Eve'g about
8 went down town with
Geo. & to Favi's — we had
some High low Jack until
10 o'clk. Me got to bed
about 10.30.
Glorious moonlight night.

## Right Page

### September, Wednesday 27, 1876

More miserable weather.
Bleak wind & rain — Up
with headache — no hope
of working but went to studio
— Set palette & commenced
repainting sketch 4¾×10½
painted long ago "A Recollection
of San Joaquin plains" — Stuck to
it until dinner time — then felt
hungry & ate — went again to
studio feeling better & worked
until after 4 when I finished
painting — About 5 went out
with Geo. & walked — Tea at
6 — At 7 — by appointment, went
to Hy Tyler's office & had a scold
— neither of us showed enough
good judgement or temper to
[illegible] — I am to meet him again
on Friday n'tf. At 8 o'clk. went
to Favi's — Had a little "Cal.
Jack" — left at 10 o'clk — To
bed at once. Again bright
moonlight night.
