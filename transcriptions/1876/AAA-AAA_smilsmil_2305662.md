## Left Page

### August, Wednesday 23. 1876.

Hazy & warm but very pleasant breeze — About
9.30 started on Study No.
2, near gardeners house
& painted in middle
distance & foreground.
Stopped at 12.30 — and
Rested 1½ an hour before
dinner which was served
abt 1.30 — About 3, started
again on W.C. Study &
worked until after 6 —
Then in to tea — Every
in Library — W. & Mrs. G.
preparing to leave to-
morrow m'fg. Cal. Jack
for an hour — to bed
at 10.15.

## Right Page

### August, Thursday 24. 1876.

Still hazy — warm & breezy.
The grand emp's got off
soon after 10 — Then I went
to my work on Study No.
2 near gardener's ho. and
worked until near 1 painting
in oak tree etc. Then up
to dinner — After that a
short nap — at 4 went out
to W.C. Study & worked on
that until 6 — Then in to
tea — Capt. Reid & Mr. Falk
came up on horse back —
had a short Ride — Every
in Library as usual until
10 o'clk — then to bed.
