## Left Page

### April, Sunday 23, 1876

Charming mt. Up at 7.30.
Exercised, scmbbed, bathed
breakfasted & to ch. at 10.30.
Dr. Hastings preached an
excellent discourse, upon
unyielding non conformity —
text. Matt 9t ch. 16th v. Geo.
was there. After service, walked
down to studio — saw Colman for
a few minutes, then to 41 St —
& dinner at 1. At 2.45 again
at studio & made a number
of notes. At 6 o'clk. went to
Emma J's — tea there & atg
back to studio — made a letter
to W. Cary — to 41 St; at 10.30
& to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### April, Monday 24, 1876

Up at 6:30 o'clk — pleasant mt. Bft.
at 7.30 — then to Mary's & at q. 15 at
foot of Desbrosses St. Met Whittredge
& went to Phila on 9.30 Exp. Got to
W. Phila at 12.0 & went right out to the
Centennial Grounds — great confusion —
after much trouble found Bastarie
but were unable to make any progress.
Got to W. Whitney's a little after 3 —
had commenced dinner — Spent
what remained of the afternoon
in the gallery & with Charl who
came in to call — met him this a.m.
to great surprise of both of us, at the
Cen. Grs — went to Earl's — back at
6.30 — tea at 7 — Evef Chatting
with W. W. & trying to arrange
my paper models for W.C. hanging.
To bed at 10.30 — but not to sleep
until about 2 a.m. [illegible — ~2 words]
