## Left Page

### October, Saturday 14, 1876

Overcast & mied until the afternoon when it blew at squally & rainy — Got to studio before 9 & commenced painting on sketch x of "Elk. Mt." Painted until dinner at 6 o'clk. & again from 2 until near 5 when I could see no longer & so called it done. Sea at 6.30 Daul. Sayre & family [illegible] from Phila. & took tea with us. Spent most of the eveg. talking over centennial matters & got to bed at 10 o'clk or [illegible — ~4 lines illegible at bottom of page]

## Right Page

### October, Sunday 15, 1876

Very cold wintry day — Bleak wind & snow. Ther. 27° at noon. Remained in studio at about 9.30. Got interested there & did not go to church. At 12, I started out for a walk & went around the lakes getting in to dinner at 1.45 — Dinner at 2. At 3 again at studio's — freezing cold & very high winds. Wrote to Mrs. Coleman & others — tea at 6 — Eveg. quietly by Sam[?] & [illegible] fireside, the family all at church, writing letters until near 9. Daul S's family took [illegible] dinner & tea with us. At 10 took my letters to the P.O. then to bed.
