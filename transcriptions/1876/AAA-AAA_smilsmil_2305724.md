## Left Page

### December, Wednesday 27, 1876

Charming day — got to studio at 9.15 & commenced work in color on "The Truants"
14×25½. Geo. came soon after 10 & worked on some little sketches — To dinner
at 1 — back at 2 & worked until near 5 getting much of the first washing in.
Several calls — D'Silva came in & walked up with me at 6. Tea at
6.30 — At 7.30 in at Renner's then to Mary's — saw Geo. &
Charl & got $25. from A.B. [illegible] —
then, at 8.30 at W. Walter
Brown's, 28 W. 26". Mr. B —
not in but I had a very
pleasant half hours call with
Mr. & Miss B — At 9.15 — to
Studio, wrote awhile &
to 41; $.; at 10.45 & Right
to bed.

## Right Page

### December, Thursday 28, 1876

Overcast all day & mild.
Got to studio at 9.30 & to work
on "Truants" — Geo. spent the
morning with me — to dinner
at 1 o'clk — back at 2.30 and
worked until 4.30 getting
my "laying in" finished. —
Anna & Lottie Lewis & [illegible]
with Miss Sackett called —
also Prof. Rood & Nicole —
To tea at 6.45 — back to shop
at 8.30 — wrote until 10 —
Got to 41 "S." at 11 & Right
to bed.
