## Left Page

### February, Monday 21, 1876

mild pleasant w[illegible], overcast p.m. & surf. & at 10 o'clk snno, got my blcfs at 9 — then to Mary's & saw mother, who went home to P. at 10.30 A.M. To shops at 10 & to work painting back ground on "Rough Sport" — To dinner at 1.30 back at 2.30 after going to N.A. & new studio painted as long as I cared see — to tea at 6.40 — then to Renner's & to Mary's & to studio's at 9 — To N.A. at 9.45 — met Leila W. & W. & Mrs. Latham — Took Leila home & got to studio at 10.25 — mote a little while — then to 41 St at 11.15 & to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### February, Tuesday 22, 1876

Glorious day in spite of fears & probabilities — Got to studio at 10 o'clk. Mc Donald came som after & I Regarded him and painted from him until near 1 o'clk. on "Rough Sport" — Mr. Gardner then came in & it was 1.30 before I got to dinner — back at 2.30 & painted from Mc D. until 5.30 — then cleaned up & at 6.30 at 41 St. Dressed — took a light tea & at 7.40 went up to Miss Millard's — 106 W. 49t. a few friends to tea — left there at 9.20 & went to N.A. Have had a very fine day there — $393.00 at 10.20 to studio — there until almost 11 — then to 41 45t. & to bed at 11.20
