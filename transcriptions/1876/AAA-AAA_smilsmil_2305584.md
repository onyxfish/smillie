## Left Page

### March, Thursday 16, 1876

A villainous day — Rain, sleet
& wind. Got to studio at 9.30
& to work painting on Gaucha
for A.B.N. Co — To dinner at
1.30 — back at 2.15 & painted
until 5 o'clk — then I could
see no longer — washed up
brushes & did a little miring
& at 6 Rode up to Mary's
44th St. Saw Charl. about
Insurance & Water colors
& at 6.45 to tea in 41st 8 —
To studio at 8.30 — Raining
in torrents — wrote until 10
to 41st S: at 10.35 & to bed at 11,

## Right Page

### March, Friday 17, 1876

miserable, sleety mtf. cleared
cloudless about noon & blew up
cloudy & March-like in the p.m.
Got to studio at 9.15 & to work on
Gauchos. Painted all the mtf
& got in the figure — To dinner
at 1.20 — At 2.30 at Phil. Releam!
After that, walked up to N.A.A.D
with Nicoll — Then to new studio
& at 5.30, back to my own — To tea
at 6.30 — At 8 called on W.W.C,
9 diman. 31 W. 36th S: — notion — then
to studio at 8.40 — wrote until
10 — To 41st S: at 10.30 & to bed
before 11. [illegible — ~3 words]
