## Left Page

### June, Saturday 24, 1876

a very hot day — did not go out for my mrf walk
but set palette & Repainted a twilight sketch — Dinner
at 12.30 — Painted pretty steadily during the P.M.
& got sketch finished &
then worked on "a Shady
Spot" 6×10 & got that finished
Tea at 6. Aunt Jessie &
Emma at tea — At 7
walked in town & saw the
one legged man walk the
tight rope across Market
St. Then home & played
the hose until 9.30 — to
bed at 10.

## Right Page

### June, Sunday 25, 1876

Hottest yet — over 90°. mrf
went to Dutch Reformed &
heard Van Geisen on the
very refreshing subject of
eternal damnation. —
Then to Matt's — then home
to dinner at 1. Spent
a couple of hours in Rocking
chair hanging on to the back
part of my head — Then
made a note to Miss Mary
A. Mitchell. Then, at 6,
to tea at Dr. Hasbrouck's —
there until 8.15 — Then to
Opera Ho. & heard conclusion
of Abbate's discourse on the
Philosophy of Prayer —
Then to Matt's — Home at
10 — to bed at 11 —
