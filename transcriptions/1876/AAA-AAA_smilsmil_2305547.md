## Right Page

### January, Saturday 1, 1876

The same dismal weather that has been with us for some days past "opened" the year. A damp fog with chilly wind continued until about 11, then it lifted & until nearly 5 the day was warm and cloudless, then again the fog. Got up at 8 — the left side of my face still very much swollen, but no pain & very little redness. Enjoyed my sleep. Got to studio a little before 10 — To work on my big drawing & stuck closely to it until 4.30, finishing up figures & horses in principal group. Fixed my lunch at 1.30 — D[illegible] & Cave called. To tea at 6.30 — Evely, dressed & went to Mary's — Dr. out — a heavy fog & mizzle — left my card at Miss Burdell's — back to 41st St. at 9.30 — to bed at 10.30
