## Left Page

### June, Thursday 22, 1876

Delightfully cool day. After
help — went up town with
Uncle A. Back to work
on "Adirondack Glimpses"
at about 11.30 — Dinner
at 12.30 — finished "A. G."
It seems to be strong in color
but too heavy — I cannot
tell where the failure is —
At 4 o'clk. Kate dressed
herself + I painted her
figure into 6×10 "A Shaded
Spot" — Spent some time
on the Roof with father + Kate
Tea soon after 6. Again on
Roof — glorious every. About
7.30 walked up town with
Uncle A. Soon back —
Matt + E? spent the eveny
with us — To bed at 10.30

## Right Page

### June, Friday 23, 1876

Very pleasant day — At 10.35
Uncle A. + I took trains + at
11.5 got to Rhinebeck — Ferry —
\+ at 11.40 horse car from Rondout
to Kingston — got there at 12
15 + called on Mr. Schoonmaker,
then a short walk + back to
the old Pardee House, now the
[illegible] + at 1 o'clk. had a
very good dinner. At 2 o'clk.
we called on Mrs. Hardenberg
(Elizabeth Holmes) + spent an hour
\+ a half there — then cars to Rondout
Uncle A. went to Andersons, I called
on Jervis Mr. Rutee + had a very pleasant
chat with Mrs. M. E. + then walked
over the bluffs until 5.30 then
left Mr. E. + went down to the Thos.
Cornell. There met Uncle A. +
2°. Parlon — got to Po. at 7.20 — walked
up home — + had tea — lounged
about during the eveny + got
to bed at 10.30.
