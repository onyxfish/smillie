## Right Page

### November, Sunday 12, 1876

Charming day — mrƒ. went to
Hastings & there heard him preach
from Mark 12th. ch. 34th v. "Almost
a christian are exceedingly
dangerous positions." After
service walked down the ave.
with Charl & was astonished
to learn that he had insured
the water colors for $50,000. It
made me sick. Walked
down to Colman's — he is sick —
saw him in his bed. Dinner
at Mary's at 1.30 — after dinner
walked down to studio — there
until dusk. Tea at Mary's —
mrƒ. mote to get Whitney
H+ Syler & ge. at Charl's
Fable until near 11 — Then
to H[?] [illegible] & to bed.

Renner Reported this
mrƒ. back from Phila —
pictures all down & on
their way here.
