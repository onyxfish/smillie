## Left Page

### February, Friday 25, 1876

Fine day — clear & cold, but milder than yesterday. Got up with miserable headache — Got to studio at 9.30 went over to new building cor. of 25th S. & 4th Ave. There for some time arranging the details of my new studio. At 1 o'clk went to Kimball's to dinner — Matt & Kate there also, Miss Hawes. Hard time with my head. At 2.30 went down to 10th S. studios — called on DeFham J.G. Brown & Nicoll. La Farge not in — At 4.30 again at studio Matt & Kate came in at 5 — I gave up to headache & Rolled in blanket until 9 o'clk — then up — Mr. Beers gave me cuts of beef tea — went over to N.A. left there at 10 — went to 41st [illegible] & to bed a little before 11 [illegible] 12 [illegible]

## Right Page

### February, Saturday 26, 1876

Charming day, altho' the wind was from the East — mild & milky. Got to studio at 9.30, feeling so stupefied about the head as to be unable to work. Went to 24th S. & 4th Ave, to see how the new studio was getting on — back & drew plans for closets. Calls from Miss McDonald, Fredericks, Baldwin etc., on Centennial biz. At 12.30 again to 4th Ave, then to N.A. & at 1.20 to dinner in 41st S. Dressed & back to studio at 3. Tried to paint a sketch but my head would not let me. At 6 went to Emma J.'s to dinner. Took her & Mary J. to Thomas's Concert at 9.15 at N.A. & D. Last night of W.C. [illegible] — at 10. again to Steinways — took the J's home — then to studio — there until 11.30 — then to 41st S. & to bed at 12 —
