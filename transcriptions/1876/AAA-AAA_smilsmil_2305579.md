## Left Page

### March, Monday 6, 1876

Very mild day — looking like rain in the mst, but clearing hazy & summerish. Got to studio at 9.45 feeling done up & utterly unfit for work. I am much depressed, now as nothing seems to go right. Painted some on "Rough Spar" to dinner at 1.30 — back at 2.15 & painted pretty well during the P.M. finishing about very generally — To tea at 6.40 — back at 8 and wrote a lot of Centennial letters until 10, at 10.30 to 41 S. There found Tone Gillelan & Josie, his wife — chatted with them until 11.15 — to bed at 11.30.

## Right Page

### March, Tuesday 7, 1876

Very mild & enervating — got to studio at 9.15 — waited for model but he did not come — fortunately old Newton came in & I painted from him until 12.30, finishing up hands & bits about the figures in "Rough Spar". After escorting Mrs Clinton Ogilvie & her 2 year old daughter to 6th Ave. cars, I went to dinner at 1.30 — back at 3.30 after going to Satterlee's & Thompson's studios & seeing the Kohn collection of pictures at Kurtz gallery. Painted as long as I could see & had many callers — At 6.30 saw father at Mary's — To tea at 6.50 — at 8.15 at studio, wrote until near 10 — then father came in — at 10.30 we walked up together. I left him at Mary's & to 41 S. & to bed at 11.15
