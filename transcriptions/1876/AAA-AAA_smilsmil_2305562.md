## Left Page

### February, Tuesday 1. 1876.

Dull, overcast & Raw — with a mizzle beginning about 5 &
finally winding up with a pouring Rain. Got to studio
at 9.30 — head, feeling bad &
so generally disinclined to
work that I cneed do nothing.
Drew in about fifty legs to m
of the horses in "Rough Sport"
but could get none of them
to look right — To dinner at
1.15 — back at 2.15 — & did a little
better in the afternoon — The
whole day has been lost & I
feel very much discouraged.
To tea at 6.30 — got to N.A.
about 8.30 — there until 10.15
then to studio — there until
11.15 — then to 41 [illegible] St — & to
bed at 11.45.

## Right Page

### February, Wednesday 2. 1876.

Fierce wind & very cold — Got
to studio at about 9.20 — feeling
better — Worked on Rough Sport
cutting on canvas with 3a [pen]
at 11.45 went with Miss Abbatt
to A. T. Stewart's to see his pictures —
on the whole, was disappointed.
Met then Miss Canning & Mortier.
To lunch at 1.45 — back at 2.30
worked away on "Rough Sport" —
& just about dark made up my
mind that one of the principal
horses was too near the ground
— must be lifted — Johnston of the
Mail came in — Walked to 34[illegible]
with him — To tea at 6.30 — to
studio at 8 — to N.A. at 9 —
back to studio at 10.15 & to
41 [illegible] at 11 — To bed at 11.30
