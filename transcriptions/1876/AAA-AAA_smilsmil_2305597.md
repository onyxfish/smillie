## Left Page

### April, Tuesday 11, 1876

another charming Spring day — very mild — I feel it in my knees — Got to studio at 9.30 — & again most of my day fooled away with callers, centennial business or something else that did not pay — Old Wm. Hill (J.W.) spent much of the m'f with me — I tried to work on Scrub Race — At 12 went to 625 Bdway. then to Lanthier's & at 1 took lunch at Avery's — Back to studio at 2.30 & worked until 6 — To tea at 6.45 — at 8 went to Mamy's & saw Geo. he came down from P. this A.M. — seems very well — Abg. went to Whittridge's house, not in — Then to N.A. there met Huntington & talked over Centennial & Century matters — To 41° S. at 10.30 & to bed at 11.

## Right Page

### April, Wednesday 12, 1876

Still mild & Spring-like — cloudy p.m. & a little spatter of rain about 3 — Father came to 41 S. before I finished — He went down town & I to studio at 9.40 — to work on Scrub Race. Geo. surprised me about 11 — to dinner at 1.20 — back at 2.30 — Worked until 4 — then to 3 yng's — 42° S. & Mad. ave. & heard Joe Guild on Organ — very much pleased — at 6 again at studio — wrote a little at 3 — to tea at 6.45 — Got to studio at 8.30 — Miss Gilbert came in & helped me by cutting papers by scale, to represent centennial N.C. drawings for me to arrange the hanging — wrote several letters — To 41° S. at 10.30 & to bed before 11.
