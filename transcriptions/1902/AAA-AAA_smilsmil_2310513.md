## Left Page

### Sat. Oct. 11, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A hazy, filmy day quite mild \+ quiet till the afternoon when the weather thickened very much with a chilly wind from the Southeast. to-day was a busy one for all of us — the house was dismantled \+ everything was brushed swept or shaked, or all three, — the store rooms were carefully packed \+ all made as nearly ready as possible for our departure on Monday Trumbull \+ Chase worked on the little building getting it partly shingled. Moody worked at all sorts of things taking orders from everybody. The Masons finished laying the wall on the Road but O'his has yet a good deal to do before his job is finished. After dinner I went to P.O. \+ had considerable work to do out of doors. Tea at 6.30. Evef. after reading my newspapers I spent the rest of the evef in studio — sorting papers \+ things \+ preparing to pack my trunk.

T \+ C 1

## Right Page

### Sun. Oct. 12, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dense, formless mist hung low on the mts all day, shutting them entirely out of sight. It was rather close, with but little wind — a dull, quiet, brooding sort of day. We were all up early \+ had bkft. about 8 o'clk. After that, a very busy day — about 10 o'clk. I went to find Reede's to Albert Winch's to give final directions — then I was led by the spirit to go up to Vivanti's house — the old Mc Carthy place — he has improved the place quite wonderfully \+ I was surprised to see the fine view that is opened up by the cutting away of trees. It was about 11 when I got home — busy about the house till dinner at 1 o'clk. It was after 3 o'clk. when I got to the work of packing my trunk — it took me two hours to get everything that I auto pack put along side of the trunk — then I was so tired that I had to rest. Tea at 6.30 — After that, Mary, Bessie \+ I tackled the piano \+ it was 9 o'clk before we got it swathed in comforters \+ covered top \+ bottom with rubber cloths carefully corded on — then I went at my trunk again but I could not see properly with the light I had, so I gave it up \+ went to bed about 10.30.
