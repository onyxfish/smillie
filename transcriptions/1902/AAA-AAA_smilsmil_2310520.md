## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 28, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Kate came from Po'k.

After much rain last night continuing till about 9 a.m. the weather cleared charmingly bright \+ warm, later it clouded up again \+ blew hard \+ rather cool. At studio till 12.30 part of the time getting ready to meet E. C. Stedman with Photo \+ ff. fetched portrait of P. Morgan. I walked to the Sherwood \- No. 6" Ave. \+ 57" St. where he has apartments \- found him with his son Fred \+ Miss Fuller. Stedman \+ son had a long conference about a deluxe publication of Poe's poems \- It must have been near 2 o'clk when Miss Fuller \+ I went down to the restaurant for lunch \- S. followed soon after \- That over we ret? to the apartment where the Morgan p'f was considered \- I am to do more work on it to refine expression of face \- About 3 I went to Fine Arts Gallery where Blashfield's new mural paintings for Baltimore Court House are on view \- Saw B. \+ several other artists \- Walked down to studio \- used up the rest of day right sorting papers, bills \+ old letters \- Home before 6 \- found Sister Kate there \- dinner \- Evening I went to Waller Cook's \+ spent the eve'f till 10 with him \+ Louise.
