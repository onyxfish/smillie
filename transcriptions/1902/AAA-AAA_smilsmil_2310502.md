## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 19, 1902

**Weather:** mild + pleasant in spite of cloudiness  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild + pleasant in spite of cloudiness. It was nearly 10 o'clk when I went to W. G. Neilson's with the Report that he gave me last eve'g to Read. I saw him + had half an hour or more of talk with him about Reserve matters as opposed to hotel affairs. I saw Mr. N. + gave her $10 for S. Dubois's 80 Birthday gift = $2.50 from me — the rest from Mary + gives. Then to hotel, got mail, + to Scott Brown's + with him went up on the Sooy Smith site in the woods next to Adler's. It was after 12 when I got home. — Dinner at 1. — afternoon with Trumbull who came this m'f. Put up doors to my wash closet — I laid art place for small tool house near Kitchen. — Mary, Emma S. + Eliz'h Howe went with Magee on a hard scrabble up a brook by Vivants's — Moody came for a while in the morning — his father has had a very bad turn — About 5 I went for a walk around the square by Putnam camp, Otis's, Weston's, the hotel + home. Tea — Rest + chatting + news papers.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 20, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary + Charl started for Montreal.

Showery day — after a wet night. Misty mizzle + sunshine in the m'f — showers growing heavier till eve'g when change came. I got up with headache which prevented my doing any studio work — Sanders came in the m'f + with Moody split up a rock in the ground N. E. of house — in the afternoon they worked in Reserve + got out more stone for me for steps down to dining room — Trumbull came in the m'f + worked till about 3 p.m. — finished ceiling in large room + other little jobs — left because lumber promised has not yet arr'. I went to hotel + got mail at 10.30. Mary + Charl left the house at 12.30 to catch 1.15 train at Westport going north + expect to stay Sunday + Monday in Montreal — then on to Quebec + back here Sat. p.m. I spent a good part of afternoon in dark room with Ralph developing negatives — Eve'g did some work on Borden outline.
