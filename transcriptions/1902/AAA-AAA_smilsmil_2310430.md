## Left Page

### Sat. April 26, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain last night — cloudy m't clearing windy \+ hot — I got up with severe headache — was able to pull thro' the m't. but gave up \+ spent the afternoon on my bed. Bkfs. at 9 — Edw. Cook came to explain plan of settling his mother's estate \+ to ask me to serve with Jas. Wright as Auditor — I went to studio \+ worked there clearing up the mess from unpacking yesterday \+ putting away paper \+ other stock — at 11.30 I went to cor. Houston \+ Mercer St. Gleason's — \+ took broken glass shade — front hall — \+ ordered new m to be done in a fortnight — walked up home. Stopping at Wanamaker's \+ Am. Art Asso. where I saw Water Color Socy. exhib. A most depressing show — To lunch at 1.30 Then to bed — up before 6 \+ went to studio for a few minutes — dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g — made out a list of various purchases to be made for acc't. of Keene.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 27, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy, windy \+ cool — Bkfs. at 9 — at about 11 I went to N.A. of D. \+ got together some of my things there — then walked over to Riverside Drive and down the Drive to 72d St. \+ thence across the park to Madison Ave. where I took car home, getting in a few minutes late for dinner — from 3.30 to 6.30 I had a nap — then supper \+ at 8 I went to Emma Ireland's in 17th St. \+ spent the eve'g with her — walked home \+ got in at 10.30
