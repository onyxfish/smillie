## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 3, 1902

**Weather:** Very clear with cold \+ high winds  **Temp:**

went to New Haven to Nelie's funeral \-
Katharine \+ Bessie Ret'd. from Saratoga
To Studio about 10. got an excellent transfer of Morgan from gelatine to ground. Home at 11. \+ prepared myself for a cold journey. To G. C. Station \+ met Emma \+ we took 12 o'clk train. Mary J\. joined us later \- To New Haven at 1.40 on getting there found that Ed'w'd Mary \+ daughter Cath'e. \+ Ge'a Coat were on the same train \- We all went together on a Whitney Ave. train to Edwards St. getting there a little after 2. Roger, his wife \+ baby had just arr'd from Buffalo \- the Episcopal service was read by two young clergymen \- one, a Relative of W'm Elkin \- from Boston \- The ceremony was short \+ soon after 3 I went to Jim's room 519 expecting to find him there \- I paced the hall for one hour \+ forty m. \- very uninteresting I found it \- \+ then I had to hurry to catch the 5.10 train met Emma \+ Mary at the Station \- train on time \- got to N.Y. on time \- 7 o'clk \- home dinner served late \- found that Kathanie \+ Bessie had Ret'd from their Saratoga visit. Was very glad to have a quiet, Restful even in front of a warm fire.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 4, 1902

**Weather:** Very cold \+ disagreeable day, snow flying most of the time.  **Temp:**

After bkf'st, at home, I went over my mem'd of work \+ found the no. of hours I had given to Borden order \- Preliminary \- painting \- etching \+ engraving \- also the expenditures of cash \- I have worked nearly 400 hours \+ spent about $145.00. I decided after thinking awhile, to put in a bill for a dollar an hour \- making the total to about $440.00. Then to Studio \+ made pkg. of photos, viz painting \+ working prints \+ went to Scribner's \+ saw Mr. H'y Smith \+ had a Talk with him \- He approved the bill \+ then told me that Mr. Borden has laid out a lot of work for me \+ has asked Mr. Smith to tell me that he would like to see me at his house some day about 2 o'clk \- He says he will have a cat. of his pictures with plates \- also a supplemental cat. of new books. \- Home at 1.30 \- lunch \- then to Studio \- varnished Morgan plate \+ got it ready to commence etching \- Went down to Alexander's Shoe Store \+ ordered Shoes \- Home to dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g Charl came in for a little while \- I fooled about \+ got to bed late \-
