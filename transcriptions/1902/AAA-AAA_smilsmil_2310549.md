## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 28, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day - did not get down to bkfo. till 10 o'clk. After 12 went out with the boys for a little breathing before dinner - Met Edna Cook + we walked together for awhile - then we went to Charls - then home to dinner at 2 - About 4 o'clk gave up to heaviness + slept till near 8 o'clk - Evef. young Whiting, of New Haven - + Sheldon Smillie came in - after they left I had a long talk with Jim about his college work + life - he is quite willing to talk, nevertheless, because of his lack of fluency; it is difficult for me to learn what I wish.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 29, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull wt. - Snow after noon - Rain before night. To studio about 10. On investigation found that I could use again the gelatine tracing as it had not been materially injured by failure on Sat. - I cleaned plate, laid another around + got a good transfer. Then varnished margins of plate - put on leather straps + went home to lunch - back at 2. I made a commencement on etching but the day was so dark that I had to give it up early. I made up plt of O'Malley + Connolly prints to send by Exp to W. Morton. Editor of Brush + Pencil. at Chicago. Home to dinner - Evef. Jim went to Pomfret alumni dinner in the evef. I did some loafing, some reading + some writing.

B. 4 h.
