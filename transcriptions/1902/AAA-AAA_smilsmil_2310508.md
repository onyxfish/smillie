## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 1, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still very mild — heavy clouds \+ showers. Moody came for an hour in the m'f. I wrote to Charlotte Kent \+ at 11 o'clk went to P.O. change in mail hours — mail does not get in after Oct. 1". till afternoon — Telephoned to Tahawus House \+ got Saml. Colman \+ had a short chat with him. Dinner at 1. Colman came about 4 p.m. His trained nurse, Miss [illegible] was with him. He has been to Arizona alone — was taken very ill while there — Telegraphed to his nurse (who has cared for Mr. Colman for the past three years) to meet him in Chicago from there they have journeyed to Niagra Montreal, Plattsburgh, Lake Placid \+ here. He says that he is now recovering strength rapidly. Evening reading newspapers.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 2, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A mild, sulky, cloudy day — no rain fell but there seemed to be lots of it up in the mountains — cooler at night \+ starlight. Trumbull came in the m'f with assistant Chase — his nephew hauling lumber for the little house all day — T. worked part of day in my room getting sashes in south door \+ over wash closet doors. Chase got cedar posts set for corners of new building — Jno. Otis drew six loads of manure from hotel stables — Moony helping him \+ spread it as top dressing over the open ground. I wrote to Emma J. \+ to Jst. S. Case, Cash. of 2d. Nat'l. Bank in the m'f went to Ford. Deede's \+ arranged with him to take Ralph \+ servants to Westport to-morrow — Dinner at 1. Went to P.O. The rest of p.m. with T. — fixing sash \+ glass in So. door \+ painting \+ staining. Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g Mr. \+ Mrs. Maghee with us.

T. \+ C. — 1
