## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 26, 1902

**Weather:** Dull raw day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull raw day - to studio at 9.30. worked all the w\[?] on gelatine tracing - Borden Katharine T. worked there, having Sheldon \+ his new silk tile for model. Home to lunch at 1 - back at 2 \+ worked till near 4, getting tracing almost finished - Then home, dressed \+ went to Edwd Cook's - Edna's last afternoon tea - much chatting till after 6 - all my family contingent there - Then home to dinner — Evening Reading - dietetic works - till 10

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 27, 1902

**Weather:** Fine cold winters day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine cold winters day - After bkfst. wrote a note to Emma J - concerning theatre tickets and inviting her to dine with us Tues. next - to Studio about 10 - laid ground on Borden plate - not a very good one - finished gelatine tracing \+ partially scraped it - to lunch at 1. Emma J. had been to house - left three theatre for boys \+ self - She \+ Mary J. to join us at Theatre then wrote note to Mary asking her to dine with us on Tues - to Studio at 2.30. Finished the preparation of gelatine tracing \+ at 4 o'clk took transfer - failure - [illegible] ink/press not enough - I was too nervous \+ timid - I think I have saved the gelatine so that I may be able to get another impression from it upon a new ground - Went to Clauson's and from there to Saml. Coleman's - Cor. 6" Ave \+ 40' St - Saw him for a few minutes only as he was preparing to go out - He has been painting away furiously for some weeks past. Home before 6 - dinner - Eveg. with the boys \+ Reading -
