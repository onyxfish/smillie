## Left Page

### Wed. April 30, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day but rather hot and windy - I had my bkfs- late + other things kept me from the studio till about 11 - then, from notes that I made last fall I drew a plan of the Keene house system of plumbing showing ranges, boiler, cold, hot + return pipes designated by colors. - Home to lunch at 1.30 - at 2 went to 2d Nat. Bank - and back at 3 - Yale ahead of me a few minutes - P. Alley soon followed + then the consultation began - it lasted about two hours - half of the time being given to irrelevant talk. I gave a full statement of my physical grievances + then Y. + C. consulted for about 20 m - or half an hour - they decide that a masseur is what I need + offer no medical treatment of plan of dietetics - Mr. Mallony's suggestion that I submit to Dr. Van Valezalis treatment does not meet with their immediate approval entirely - advised C. - to make enquiries concerning Vant - of whom they know little - there to Jackson's up. + paid for morning Stand - ing posts to N.A. of D. - then to studio - then a short walk + home - dinner at 6.30 - Even- Read my paper with much trouble my eyes hurting me - Before I went to bed had an enema of salt water.
Wrote to Jim + Ralph reminding them that to-morrow would be the anniversary of their mother's birth day

## Right Page

### Thur. May 1, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Smillie's birth-day -

Glorious day - after bkfs - Douglas came + I spent some time on the roof with him advising as to work to be done there in the attic - then Miller came + some time with him about window to be put in east wall of bedroom on north front - upper floor - then I saw Jane + Mary Hinshelwood - wrote some notes - to studio after 11 + had a consultation with Ryan over the plan of Keene plumbing that I made yesterday - He made two or three propositions that strike me favorably + I shall advise J. T. Broadner to carry them out - Then made plan for ceiling of my bedroom at Keene - lunch at usual hours - felt very heavy after it + as I had a poor night last night I took a nap + felt better for it - Wrote a note to J.N. Maghee in ans to one Rec'd from him this mg concerning Road tax at Keene. Dinner - Eve'g - Mary - Katharin, Bessie + I went to University glee club concert at the Waldorf-Astoria + later were joined by Charl - The concert, as we all thought, hardly came up to the Club average -
