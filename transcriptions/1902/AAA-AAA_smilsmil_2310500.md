## Left Page

### Mon. Sept. 15, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day but I was unfitted for work by a headache that was not sufficient bad to prevent my going about. About 11 I went for the mail + then to Scale Brown's — met Wm. + Katie Elkins + sat on hotel piazza with them till after 12. then home. After dinner, about 2.30 Jim, Ralph + I started out for a walk. we went along the Chafee pond wagon road to the entrance of trail that goes along the ridge between the road on the east + Beaver Brook on the west — then on that trail till it came out on the road where it crosses Chafee pond outlet. The trail in many places is very interesting + I was greatly pleased — after going along the outlet to the foot of the pond we retraced our steps + returned by the trail, leaving it to climb to the top of a ledge of rock from which we had a full view of Chafee pond cliffs + north to White Face — as we sought a way down we came upon the blazes of an old trail + were able to follow it down to the trail we had left. The climbing on the ledge was very difficult over rocks + others. The debris of fallen + partly burned cedars + other smaller growth. We got home at 5.30

### Tues. Sept. 16, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Emma Ireland am.
Sister Kate left for Po'K —

Pleasant day — but hazy — [illegible] A usual time at 8.30 Kate got started on her drive to Westfield to take train for Po'Keepsie. my head continued to trouble me thro' the night + in the aft. I got only partial relief. I did not feel within for studio work + kept about out of doors. After dinner I worked with Moody in transplanting a number of evergreen trees to positions about the house. I should not forget that Lawrence Adler came to the house in the aft. and very obligingly played a number of pieces upon the piano with remarkable excellence. Emma Ireland am. about 6 p.m. alone. Eliz. Howe was belated by a fog. The Hudson failed to meet her train at Albany. She is expected to-morrow. Emma is enthusiastic over the Ark + its surroundings.
