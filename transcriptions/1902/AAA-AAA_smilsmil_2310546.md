## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 22, 1902

**Weather:** fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

fine day. To Studio at 9.30 \+ worked all the mf. on india-ink sketch. Borden N°. 2 \+ finished it at noon. a short walk before lunch at 1 \- after that went with Jm. Ralph to Inauguration ceremonies. Met. Mrs. [illegible] \- there were addresses in the new Entrance Hall \- but few heard them \- we wandered generally till after 4 o'clk \- meeting Marc \+ Katharine in one of the galleries \- they left before we did \- we got home soon after 5 \- dinner \- Wesf. the boys went to the Old Academy of Music with H. S. to see the play of the Ninety \+ Nine. Charles came \+ stayed till near 11.

B. 1/2

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 23, 1902

**Weather:** Clear, cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 9.30 \+ spent the mf. outlining or tracing Borden on gelatine. Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2. Mackellar came with drawing of mon\[u\]m\[en\]t. for Greenwood 1st \- modified as I suggested \+ I approved it \+ he was ordered to go ahead. I worked on gelatine tracing till 8 o'clk. Then I went to Shark's \- 13 Batte St. \+ ordered copper plate for Borden. Then to Fuchs \+ Lang \- 29 Warren S\[t\]. for Zinc plates. To call again to-morrow \- then up town to 6th Ave. Stores \+ tried to do some Xmas shopping in a small way \- but the crowds were too great \- then to Scribner's \- looking at books till 6 o'clk \- got a couple for the boys. Home to dinner \- Wesf. Jim \+ the girls went to a lecture before the Geo. Socty. \- I sat before a good fire till 10 o'clk \- Reading \-

B. 1.
