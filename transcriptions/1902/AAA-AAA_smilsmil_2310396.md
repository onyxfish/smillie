## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 15, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — Put in a long mf. of work on Morgan head — still bothering to get a likeness. It was 1.30 when I got home to my lunch — then to NAD — was stopped on my way by the burning of the old Durland Riding Academy — 8th Ave Broadway — 61st \+ 62d. St. Got to NAD late \+ found portion of class at work from model — no really good work being done — After 4 o'clk, when model left, I laid grounds on several plates — then walked across the Park \+ got home at 5.30 — dinner — evef. at 8.30 to Century — Prof. Putnam gave ill, lecture on the Ruined Pueblos of the Chaco Canon. New Mexico — now being excavated \+ explored by expeditions sent by Hyde bro. The pictures were extremely interesting, many of them good as pictures \+ photos. but the descriptions \+ comments of Putnam were to me very unsatisfactory — He did a great deal of Folklore (having lost all of his notes) but the most desirable information was left out of it — many questions were find at him but all were not answered \+ he gave us very little ethnologic speculation — simply an acct. of digging out what he found — After the lecture — Art Sub Com. of Admissions had a meeting — C.Y. Turner — Daw. French — Arthur \+ self — Sirions, a scene painter was approved \+ the case of Wm Coffin was discussed — it was decided to recommend his Sponsors to withdraw his name.
After midnight when I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 16, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*[written above header: 'gene atwater came to visit the girls]*

Pleasant day — Up late — did not get my sleep till 11 o'clk. At noon went to Studio \+ opened windows — then had a walk on Park Ave. then to Charlie's — home to dinner at 2 — Gene Atwater's came unexpectedly to visit the girls — it had been arranged rather to come to-morrow. After dinner I went to studio, closed windows \+ had another stroll on Park Ave. In before 5 \+ had a nap till 6.30 —
Evef. loafing before the fire.
