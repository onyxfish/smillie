## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 12, 1902

**Weather:** Bright, cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold day — did not get my bkf. till after 10 o'clk. Loafed about feeling somewhat out of sorts — Dinner at 2 — got some writing done in the afternoon — Geo. \+ Charl came in — Geo. took tea with us — again some writing at night \+ to bed early

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 13, 1902

**Weather:** Bright \+ very cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ very cold \+ windy. To studio at 10 — then to Kimmel \+ Voigts. 242 Canal St. Got finished plt (20) of the Borden plate \+ title page \+ Ret. to the studio at 11 — much specking on some of the plt. \+ I was till one o'clk at work arranging them in order of excellence — removing specks and adding my autograph. Home to lunch — back at 2.30 — worked with india ink, pen \+ pencil on photo. reduction of Architects drawing of facade of new Stock Exchange. This is to take the place of the one I so unfortunately lost some days ago — It is to be photo-gravured for history of Stock Exchange. At 4.30 I went to studio of Irving Wiles and had a half hours talk with him about terms under which I wd. mezzotint his portrait of Miss Marlowe — I find him very amiable \+ easy — at 5.15 went to N.A.f D. — 4 you in 57/65. There till 6 o'clk — then home — dinner — Evening about 8.30 Eleanor Cook came under escort of Tom Cook — Soon after her bro Robt. \+ his wife came \+ stayed till 10 —
