## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 11, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

miss Jane left for Lake Placid \-

Brilliant day \- very cool wtf \- growing quite warm during the day + cool again at night. Up at 5.30 + had a cup of coffee with miss Jane + Katharine + Bessie \- Miss J. got away at 6 o° to drive over to Placid to go to Whipple Lake to join friends whose camp is Stag head on Fallinsbee. Then I went back to bed + had a little nap before b'fst. at 8 \- After that I worked awhile on Borden outline \- After 11 I went on a hunt for the trunk that was sent to Ketchees Cook + found it at Lord Beede's \- then Ralph + I went to top of Rock at Iron bridge + got a camera snap shot \- Home at 1 o'clk \- dinner \- After that a little more work on the dismal Borden outline \- William + Katie Elkin came in for a little while \- late I seeded to grass the spot out on the level where for the past two or three years we have had bonfires \- Then supper \- Self scanned "Piro" "The Lady Paramount" of which I think but little.

Borden 3 h.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 12, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hazy, mild + summer-like day \- B'fst. after 9 o'clk. Moody asked for half a day to arrange some outstanding business of his \- granted \- After spending a little time looking about the place I got to work on Borden outline \- After 12, Ralph + I went to Harry Beede's + got quoit hubs that he had made for me \- Then we walked up to the river on his flume \- quite picturesque in some parts \- in trying to cross a part of the River I lost my footing on a slippery stone + got a little wetting \- belated for dinner \- Afternoon I worked on outline till after 4 \- Then I Kalsomined a couple of small panels on ceiling of main room, where I propose to make an alteration \- Then I worked on Reserve Road \- making a three mile run \- Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g in my studio \- worked awhile on Borden outline + read my newspaper.
