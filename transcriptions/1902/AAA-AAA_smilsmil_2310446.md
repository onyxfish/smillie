## Left Page

### Wed. May 28, 1902

**Weather:** Cloudy + very cool — unpleasantly cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy + very cool — unpleasantly cool in-doors without fire — I found the house so cold that I went to studio early but found it quite as cold there (below 65°) as John had not lighted fire. I spent some time with the proofs taken yesterday, dampng those on thin paper + putting them in press — Then I went to Bank + attended to my monthly business there — I paid, with interest, two loans standing there ag'st me — $1750 — this was a great relief to me — on my way home stopped at Clausen's — lunch — did not go to studio in the afternoon — feeling the loss of sleep last night (I waked at 5 a.m.) I made it up in the afternoon. At 7 o'clk went with Mary + the gives to Charlie's + had dinner — left at 8.15 — Sharp shower — had to go home + get umbrella + overshoes — it was 8.45 when I got to Com. on Admissions Century — an interesting session — home at 11.30 — bright starlight —

## Right Page

### Thur. May 29, 1902

**Weather:** Glorious day — brilliant, crisp + cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Glorious day — brilliant, crisp + cool — I went to D. Cilley's + was weighed — 116 lbs — showing a gain of another half a pound — nevertheless I feel strangely weak — this cold snap has affected me unpleasantly — To studio about 10 — for some time changing p.p. between boards + blotters — then worked on drawings for carpenter — arms + seats at Keene — After 12, went to Bank + drew some money — home to lunch — to studio at 2.30 — worked till 4.30 — then to Gleason's — Mercer + Houston — the gaslight globe not yet done — walked up home — feeling tired all the way — dinner — Went with newspapers until 9.30 when Carlson came + gave me some good work bet. 9.30 + 11 o'clk
