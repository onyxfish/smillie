## Left Page

### Mon. July 7, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

D. Laight lanced my finger.

High wind from south & threatening rain which came in the afternoon after which it cleared — wind still high — After bfkb: I went to ferd Reede's — D, I. not expected. I Ret. to house — then Jim & I went down to Beedes — got horse & buckboard & drove to D. Laight's He lanced my finger — very little show of pus. — Then back home — my mouth has been very sore today with neuralgic pains in face & later, headache — altogether I ful very dilapidated. John Otis came, brought gravel for road — got road roller & rolled road. — I slept part of the afternoon — Eve'g with newspapers. [illegible — entire passage]

## Right Page

### Tues. July 8, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph's birth day.

A glorious summer day — bright, breezy \+ warm — but a very uncomfortable one for me. — a very severe headache that gradually passed away — a stiff neck — a very sore mouth, with neuralgic pain — \+ continued discomfort \+ trouble with my sore finger — a sad catalogue of ills. — I got about very little during the m'g but was able to write some notes I wrote to D. Gerster about my finger — Kattie \+ Eliz'th went to Upperkeesable with the Maghees. — Afternoon I was able to get about a little \+ looked after Dennis \+ M. Otis. Eve'g. very warm. — all sit on north piazza with Chinese lanterns — [illegible — entire passage]
