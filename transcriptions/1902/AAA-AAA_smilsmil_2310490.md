## Left Page

### Tues. Aug. 26, 1902

**Weather:** Pleasant, warm day — some rain during last night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Trumbull came + worked on partition in my bed room till after 1 oclk then he left for the day. Moody came in the m'f + did chores + left. I spent some time making diagrams of two doors + writing to Prescott + Son at Keeseville. After 11 when I went to Hotel, got mail + walked into Reserve — found a sunny place by river + for awhile read my paper — met Mr. Mitcheson + daughter + walked out with them — dinner at — Mr + Mrs. Allan Marquand, with Miss Stokes (daughter of A. Phelps Stokes) called very pleasantly about 3 o'clk. After 4 o'clk I went to Mr. W. G. Neilson's reception + met a lot of people — after that walked out to Reserve entrance + then home — tea — eve'g as usual with news paper.

## Right Page

### Wed. Aug. 27, 1902

**Weather:** Rain last night — fine day — cloudless night, cool + quiet.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Before I had quite finished bkf't W. G. Neilson came + I was with him for an hour or two running the lines of two gores of land — one at the N. W. cor. of my lot, that he gives to me in exchange for one at the S. W. cor. of my lot that I give to him. I have used the gore at N.W. Cor. for two seasons under verbal agreement. I gives better grade for entrance of my road. About noon went to Hotel + got mail then home to dinner. Trumbull came to work at 1 o'clk. Moody did chores in m'f + then begged for another day off to finish his haying job. About 3 Ralph + I started on a "Random Scout" — we went up Giant Trail to a cascade where, years ago, I got a study from the flat rocks at top. It was a very pleasant renewal of an old acquaintance. Some portions of the trail pass thro' very fine woods — we got home soon after 6. Jim started on a long walk to Lily Pad Pond in the morning taking his lunch — He did not get quite to the pond owing to intricacy of crossing trails — we met him near Otis's, returning. He got home at 3.30. Tea — eve'g with papers — sketched comfortably at length on alcove seat.
