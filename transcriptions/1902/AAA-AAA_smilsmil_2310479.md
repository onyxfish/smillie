## Left Page

### Mon. Aug. 4, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright cool day - northerly wind - Adams came + did some painting in the afternoon - Otis hauled more rotted rock for road - Trumbull did not come - I accomplished but little - wrote some notes in the m't. - did some carpentering work, changing size of stretching frame + then stretched muslin on it + sized it - + got stretcher with muslin for ceiling of alcove from laundry to north piazza preparatory to restretching that - Short hours of sleep last night + yesterdays walk made me heavy in the afternoon + I had a restful nap - Eve't. with news papers.

## Right Page

### Tues. Aug. 5, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant m't., later, clouded partly - I got up soon after 6 - after bkf't. I transplanted ferns + fixed clematis plants + then tacked a strip of sized muslin on bedroom ceiling - Jim went up Giant alone taking lunch with him - After 11 I went over to Maghee's to arrange to go with him + Scott Brown to the lumbermans camps above Gile brook - B. is bossing the building of a dam + could not go. Home before 1 - Trumbull did not come + Otis did not appear - I worked as carpenter + made stretching frame for larger panels for bed room ceiling - then I stretched muslin on it. All of the feminines of the family went over to Maghees to afternoon tea + did not get home till near 7 - Jim, Ralph + I had our supper alone. Eve't. Mr + Mrs. Maghee came over to bid Mr. Thiss + ane, who leave to-morrow m't., good-bye.
