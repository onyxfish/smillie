## Left Page

### Fri. Jan. 24, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Delightful day - so much exhausted by my nights labors that I did not get up till after 10 - to studio about 11 feeling very weak. Mr. Kane \+ John there cleaning house - it was awfully dirty place - I superintended the work for an hour or more - then home rested, had a light lunch at 2 \+ at about 3 again to studio \+ stayed there till near 5 - then home \+ had a sound sleep till 6.45 then dinner - Well I wrote to the boys \+ got to bed about 11.

## Right Page

### Sat. Jan. 25, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day, mild \+ quiet - Having some notes to write at home it was 10.30 before I got to studio - John \+ Mr. Kane were at work at the cleaning which was finished before 1 - Then I worked at getting contents of closets sorted \+ back in place. Home to lunch at 1 \+ after that - N.A. \+ D. where I met Etching class at work with pen \+ ink after model - They are a sorry lot - but one \+ them, so far, shows any appreciation of the character of work that I am trying to get out of them - I insist upon having careful work \+ they give me sloth. Home at 5 \+ had a rest before dinner - after dinner dressed \+ with many went to the Concert of University Glee Club at the Waldorf - an agreeable affair - met bro. Geo. there - Home after 11.
