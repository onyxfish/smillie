## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 20, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day \- To studio about 10. K. T. there most of the mfg. To finish the sketch she worked on yesterday. I worked all day with india ink on the Platinum reduction \- Bordeu \+ found that I worked very well \- Home to lunch at 1 \- back 2 \+ worked as long as I could see \- a little after 4 \- It was near 5 when I went to the barber's \+ had my hair cut \+ nearly 6 when I got home \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Evelf, Mary, Kate, Ralph \+ I went to Charl's \- There till 10.30 \- then home.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 21, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain \- much of the time falling in torrents. with high wind \- I got started at about 6.30 but it was after 10 before I got down to my wkp. I spent much of the day arranging old letters \+ throwing out a lot of stuff \- I gave a good deal of time to arranging letters from the boys, &c². while they were at school \+ college \+ my replies \- I think such a history will be interesting to them in days to come \- Charl came in during the afternoon \- Evelf Geo. \+ his three boys came \-
