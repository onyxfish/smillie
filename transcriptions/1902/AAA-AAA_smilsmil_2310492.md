## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 30, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A warm, hazy day — southerly wind — prospects of rain with some light splatters. Trumbull did not come to-day — Moody worked at laying wall to terrace east side of a road to get to ice house. I spent part of m'f in dark room with Ralph developing a dozen films — After dinner I gave up for a time to a headache that has bothered me all day. But at 4 o'clk. I rallied dressed \+ went with Mary \+ Kate to Mrs. Mitcheson's tea at her little cottage near the Hotel — Home at 6 — Tea — We't the Maghee family spent the ev'g with us on north piazza.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 31, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charles came.

A hot day — quite like August — hazy with very light breeze. After a night of wakefulness I got up with a headache that was more or less troublesome all day — B'kf't about 10. most of the remainder of m'f was printing from negatives developed yesterday — Charl arr'd at about 1.30 — then dinner. For some time during p.m. I was on my bed. The Maghees \+ others of kin came in the afternoon — After 5 I went to hotel \+ got mail \+ brought home a 6 lb. can of coffee for Charl. Tea at 6.30 — Ev'g on north piazza till 10 —
