## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 13, 1902

**Weather:** left the Ark for N.Y.  **Temp:**

A dull m'f with high wind - about 2 o'clk a heavy shower - very mild temp°. - clearing weather + soft clouds - after 4 more heavy rain continuing for about two hours - then more clearing - at 10 p.m. heavy thunderstorm - storms all about. Up before 6 + to work packing trunk offs°. at 7.30 - Beede came for trunks + got away about 10 - Then I had lots of things to do until 12.30 when we had dinner. This came for last instructions + then Winch came to cut off the water - After dinner I went to Scott, downs + then to P.O. + lost time trying to telephone Patten + "The Westport" for supper - then to Magness + bed them good by a - then home + made final closing leaving Windy + Moody in charge - We got away at the close of a very [illegible] shower that came up suddenly about 2.30 - we started at 3. After we got down into the valley the clouds broke away for a little time it was like a mild, showery Spring day - but after getting over the top of Spruce Mt. the heavy rain came on again + we had to have the curtains buttoned down after which we rode dry + without discomfort - Before we got to Westport, about 7.30, the clouds broke again + we had a little moonlight. we had a good supper at 8 o'clk - fires had been started in the heater + the house or it was insufferably hot - Tem. on piazza, 66°. Went to station - got tickets - checked trunks etc. etc + loafed about unable to stay indoors on acct. of heat - at 10 a violent thunderstorm passed over us - many others over the mts. west of us - train came at 11.10, 6 m. late. No berths reserved for us altho I had written to J. Curtis, agent at Plattsburgh, some days ago - we were able to get one section + two upper berths - I promptly climbed into mine + was soon asleep -

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 14, 1902

**Weather:** got to N.Y. at 7.20 a.m.  **Temp:** Kate came from O'k.

Pleasant day - slept fairly well last night until about 5 this m'f. dozed till after 6 - then got up - Girls already up + dressed - Many left the train at Po'k. to visit there for two or three days. We went directly to 110 + had a very satisfactory breakfast - the work of house cleaning still going on - after bfst. went to Charles + saw him - then home + unpacked trunk - Kate came from Po'k to do some shopping after lunch. 5 feet heavy + had a good two hours nap. Little done before dinner at 6.30 - Emma Dreand came in for awhile - Evef. Geo°. + Nellie + Charles came in -
