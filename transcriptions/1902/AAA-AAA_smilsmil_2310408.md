## Left Page

### Thur. March 13, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Spring day with a smart shower about 1 o'clc. I got up with a beast of a headache that stuck to me all day. I got to studio - ground some special ink + tried to take p.p. from the Morton head but could not get the strength that Mr Voigt got when he pulled p.p. from the plate a few days ago. I was much troubled + did not get home to lunch till near 2 — after that I had to lie down for a while — Soon after 3 o'clk I went to Scribner's + saw Mr. H.f.J. Smith + has had a very satisfactory interview with Mr. Borden, who has? my oil painting of his library + the india p.p. in passe partout — He was so well pleased that he said he would have 15 large paper india p.p. Taken + was altogether in such good humor that he told Mr. Smith to tell me to call on him some day to consult about more work — I told Mr. Smith that I could not afford to do any more such work at the price I had done the library plate for — I could do much better on ordinary book work — Mr. S. said that Borden was the last man to want any one doing satisfactory work to do it for him for less than he could get elsewhere — + that I could correct my bill + he would see that it was paid — this was very kind of him + of course very agreeable to me — + it was arranged that I should add $104. to figures already given — I took B. plate to K. & V. + ordered 15 p.p. To studio at 5 — with new ink — took two p.p. from Morton — still unsatisfactory — Home at 6 — Evely. Charl came in + bid us good bye — he is to start on a Florida play spell of three weeks to-morrow.

## Right Page

### Fri. March 14, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely spring day — cloudless sky — fresh westerly breeze. cool but not cold. After bkfo: wrote a note to Miss Koehler. To studio about 10 + wasted the day taking p.p. from Morton plate — 4 india + 5 Jap — none of them satisfactory — They lack brilliancy — Voigt gets snap + force but it is crude — I try to get the force without the crudity + cannot do it — There is a trick in inking + especially in Retroussage that I have not got — a short walk on Park Ave. before lunch — Afternoon worked till 5 — again, a short walk — then home very tired + had a rest of an hour before dinner — Evely wrote to Jim + Ralph.

Charl started for his Florida Trip This afternoon.
