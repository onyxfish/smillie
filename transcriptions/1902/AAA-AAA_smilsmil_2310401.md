## Left Page

### Tues. Feb. 25, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very disagreeable day — dark, threatening \+ Raw in the m\[orning\] getting worse as the day wore on — heavy rain at night — Got up feeling but little better than yesterday. After bkfst. wrote to Oscar Ireland \+ to Hy \+ L. Smith. It was noon when I went to studio — worked for an hour on Morton plate — then home to lunch — back at 2.30 \+ worked till 5.15 — Men home — dinner — Eve\[nin\]g — Reading \+ looking over mem\[oranda\] made at Keene last fall of things to buy to complete the furnishing of the Ark.

## Right Page

### Wed. Feb. 26, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

wet m\[orning\] — dull day — to studio about 9.30 — laid ground on Morton plate \+ with quadruple point went over most of the background — while varnish was drying I worked on Morgan, scraping \+ rubbing down with stone over bitten parts on the rest — Home to lunch \+ back at 2.30 — bit in background to Morton adding much color — worked with graver till after 5 — Men had a short walk on Park Ave. — Dinner at 6.30 — Eve\[nin\]g Read news paper \+ got to bed early — I still feel very weak \+ discouraged.
