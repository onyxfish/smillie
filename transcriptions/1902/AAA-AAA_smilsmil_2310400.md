## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 23, 1902

*Jim \+ Ralph ret. to School.*

**Weather:** Bright \+ pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ pleasant \- got up at about 9 after a fairly restful night \- enjoyed my rub down \+ bath \+ had bkft. about 11 \- It was about 1 when I went to Millers in 40' S. \+ to Ryans in 39. S. \+ arranged to have men on my roof early to-morrow m'f. to make repairs \- then to studio \- then to Charl's \+ home to dinner at 2 \- a good one in honor of the boys. Late in the afternoon I had a good nap \- Ge'. \+ Charl came in as usual before tea time \- We\+ at 8 went with the boys to G. C. station Jim started for New Haven on 8 o'clk train \+ Ralph for Tarrytown on 8.35 train \- Then I went to Charl's \+ was there till 10.30 \- then home \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 24, 1902

**Weather:** Lovely day overhead  **Temp:** [illegible]

Lovely day overhead. I was up \+ soon fur 8 \+ was on the roof with carpenter \+ roofer till 10 o'clk. superintending a change \- having the old 4" circular hole knocked out \+ a 10" square put in to place for water vent to lead to a big funnel or hopper to be set outside at top of leader. Then down to bkft \- feeling a good deal out of sorts \- at about 11. went to studio for a few minutes \- to Alexander's at noon for photos \- they were ready \- found that they had been made on wrong last \- new pair will have to be made \- disgusting \- Home at 1 \- The girls are to have a lunch party \- I do not know who \- I have to take my lunch in my bed room \- So much out of sorts that I spent the rest of the day on my bed \+ slept considerably \- Up at 6 \- had some clam broth \- We\+ wrote a note to E. A. Abbey \- a little subscription dinner is being given him by N.A's \+ Centurians at the Century \- I was to be there but have had to give it up. Sent note by messenger \-
