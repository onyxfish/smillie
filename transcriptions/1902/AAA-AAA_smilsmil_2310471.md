## Left Page

### Sat. July 19, 1902

**Weather:** Overcast m'f.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast m'f. — increasing cloudiness — rain afternoon \+ ev'f. As agreed, Trumbull did not come to-day. I worked on plans for platforms under east windows in alcove in my room — to make it possible to clean outside of plate glass. I also experimented with typographic outfit on a door panel in my room. It did not work successfully on wood that had been oiled, so, I set palette \+ rubbed a little color on to complete the effect. Ev'f. so stupid that I could not read my newspaper — I have not felt as well as usual for the past two or three days.

## Right Page

### Sun. July 20, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Light rain all last night \+ most of to-day — with mizzle between times — Got up with a headache which bothered me all day until after 4 o'clk. when I gave up to it \+ was on my bed until about 9. During the day I made some progress with drawings for balconies on east windows \+ other odd jobs. Did not go away from the house but had walks on the piazzas. After a light meal at 9 p.m. I read a little \+ then to bed.
