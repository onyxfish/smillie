## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 21, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

In bed all day with influenza. much pain in the head — general misery — sneezing + weeping — the strenuous, sub-way city microbe has found me a ready victims after my four months vacation in pure mountain air. In the evef I got up + went down to the parlor while my room + bed had an airing. Charl came + took Mary + the girls to his house.

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 22, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright pleasant day with rather keen westerly wind — had my blkpt. in bed — up before 11 feeling light in the head + groggy in the legs — went out to my favorite sunning ground on Park Ave. + walked up + down there for awhile. Home to lunch at 1 — most of the afternoon was spent writing letters accompanying checks — payment of bills due. Had another walk on the Avenue — then in + had a nap before dinner. Evef. at 8.30 went to meeting of Com. on Admissions. Century — there until 10.30 — then home directly.
