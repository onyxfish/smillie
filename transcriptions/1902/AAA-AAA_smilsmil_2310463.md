## Left Page

### Thur. July 3, 1902

Jim arrived.

Heavy Rain thro' most of the night \+ till about noon. warm, with heavy clouds — clearing at night. my finger was much in the way — so that I was late at bkfs. Jim surprised us at about 11 o'clk — we had not expected him — he came in cold \+ wet — by E. Town stage. Dinner — \+ soon after I R\. \+ I went to herd Beedes — got horse \+ buckboard \+ drove to Dr. Daights He thinks that my finger will get well with care, but slowly. Got home after 5\. — I did considerable walking on the North piazza — Eve\+, newspapers till 10 —

## Right Page

### Fri. July 4, 1902

Brilliant m\+f — very pleasant day, but warm — clouding over towards eve\+. commenced the day at 7 a.m. by firing a dynamite bomb from my studio balcony. after bkfs\. I went to hotel \+ saw Dr. Magee He looked at my finger — said I had been poulticed too much — cauterized it and advised Balsam of Peru, which I had, mixed with cold cream — This gives me encouragement — I went to Scott B's before I went home — Inflamation of one of my teeth developed suddenly \+ between \+ \+ my finger I was really miserable. Dinner at 1 — after that the boys \+ I walked to the flume on Gill brook \+ back — nearly 4 miles — Then I read newspapers till tea time — Eve\+, all went over to the Hardenburgh-Maghees but Kate, Ralph \+ self — I paced piazza — \+ read \+ to bed at 10.
