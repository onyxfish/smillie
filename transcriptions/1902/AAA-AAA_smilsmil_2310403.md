## Left Page

### Sat. March 1, 1902

**Weather:** Clear  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear \+ mild, spring-like day. After my last night's experience I slept late \+ did not get my bhp. till near 11 o'clk. — feeling very shabby. At noon I went to 2°. Nal Byk \+ at 1.30 I went to N.A.d.D. — met Etching class \+ bit plates for the students. This was their introduction to the use of acid — I left there about 4 \+ went home directly \+ got a nap before dinner — dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g, before 9 went to Century \+ had a very pleasant eve'g until after midnight — chatting with H.D. Smith, Roelliker, Stedman \+ others Hopkinson Smith walked home with me.

This m'g I called at Calvery's studio \+ found that he has modeled an entirely new bust of me — getting much pleasanter expression \+ the same true preserving an equally good likeness.

## Right Page

### Sun. March 2, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright for awhile in the middle \+ late part of the day — but there was a surprise of heavy rain in the morning — it continued cloudy most of the day \+ there was a persistent sprinkling. I got up at about 9 \+ enjoyed my bath \+ rub-down. It was 10.30 when I got my ~~bath~~ breakfast. About 12.30 feeling sleepy I took a nap before dinner at 2 o'clk. Afternoon I went to studio \+ worked for some time to revive the fire — John not having put in an appearance since yesterday eve'g. Clark came to the house \+ in the eve'g Geo. \+ Sheldon came.
