## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 17, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy snow storm — from 6 to 12" — every thing blocked up — I got to studio about 10 o'clk in a full days work on Morgan portrait. Home to lunch as usual — worked till a little after 5 o'clk — Eve'g wrote a letter to Ralph.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 18, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright overhead — Giles of snow under foot. I put in another full day at the studio on Morgan + have got it in shape for a proof (first) — The streets are in such condition with snow that I had no chance to work — I feel out of sorts — needing change + exercise — The condition of my bowels gives me much concern — while they continue as they are I cannot be well. Whether the bowels or the liver is the real sinner I cannot learn — I wish I could find a doctor who could tell me just what organs are out of working gear — Eve'g as usual. On going to bed took a cathartic of Carter's little liver pills + compound licorice powder.
