## Left Page

### Thur. June 5, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A superb day, brilliant, breezy \+ cool. Did not get any sleep after 6 owing to noise in the street. Bkfs. at 8.30 — after that went to 110 \+ was engaged there for some time then to studio — Connelly, an N.A. etching student came \+ stayed for some time. at 11.30 I went to 115 E. 14' St. \+ ordered \+ fo? for Rubber piano covering to Fleese — then to K. \+ V. — got Jak fo? \+ Morton \+ Morgan plates — then to studio — on my way to Caturp to lunch, met Alice Ireland \+ had a walk with her. Collins sat beside me — there was some — there was some entertaining \+ some instructive talk at the table. to the house — engaged at general work meanwhile waiting for the chimney sweeps who did not come — about 4 went to studio — home before 5 — wrote some notes. To Charles' in time for dinner at 7 — Geo. came in later \+ spent the every with us — it was late before I got to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 6, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another fine day — breezy \+ cool. Had my bkfs. with Charles at 9 o'clk. It was about 10 when I went to the house. Jno. came \+ helped me take my trunk down to first floor where I will pack it. Carpenters worked on floor in attic till 11 — I waited until near that time for chimney cleaner — he ~~did~~ not come \+ then I went to Ryan's with John \+ got packing box out of the cellar — then went to 108 Mad. Ave. \+ called to see Eliz. Howe but she was not in — then to 2° Nat'l Bank — then again to studio — then to house — spent some time with chimney cleaner — \+ at 1.30 went to Century \+ had a light lunch — back to house — Douglas came I was at the house till 5 o'clk. Dinner at Charles at 7 — Carlson came at 9.30 \+ we went thro' the regular exercises.
