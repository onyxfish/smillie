## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 30, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very fine day — after bkft. went to Adams Sup. with Phl. [illegible] for W. fat. W. Morton, Ed. Brush \+ Pencil — then to studio — worked till 1 on Borden Etching — Katharine T. was there with female model all the m't. Lunch — back at 2 \+ worked till about 4 — Had a rest at home before dinner. Summa \+ Mary Ireland came to dine with us — They took Jim \+ Ralph to the Theatre (Herald Square) \+ I tagged along, to see \+ hear Julius Caesar — Mansfield as Brutus — Greenaway as Caesar — Arthur Forest as Marcus Antoninus \+ Jos. Haworth as Caius Cassius — The theatre is a poor old affair, abnormally ugly in decoration — the stage setting was good — Alma Tadema much in evidence — The crowds of [illegible] lace were as large as the stage would hold \+ very well drilled.

B. 4 h.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 31, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another fine winter day — to studio before 10 \+ worked till noon on Borden Etching — K. T. at work on her drawing most of the m't — I gave her help for half an hour or more — then to Second nat. But home to lunch at 1.45 — to studio at 2.30 — Worked on Etching till 4 — Then Ralph came \+ I went with him to Rogers, Peet \+ Co. \+ he got a suit of clothes — Then we went thro' 30th 31st 2d \+ 3d Sts. bet 7th \+ 8th Ave. — looking for Deconk, a plaster cast man, without finding him — Home before 6 — dinner — Eve \+ Charl came in —

B. 4 h.
