## Left Page

### Fri. May 2, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- Jine came from New Haven.

Pleasant day \- to studio at usual time \+ for more than an hour worked at wet-ting a number of etching p\[?\] from plates by NA students \+ by self \- preparatory to putting them in blotters in standing press. then Wm. C. Pate came \- He offered me 18 p\[?\] of Goldsmith's Daughter \+ the plates (steel) that I eng'd years ago after my painting "Upper Ausable Lake" \+ Geo's painting "On the Susquehanna" in exchange for my painting "Crowninshield's Point \- Mass. coast" \- I declined but said I w'd make exchange for $25 \- added \- he agreed \- \+ I Regretted \- Home to lunch \- after that I went to K. \+ V's \- Canal S't \+ got imps. from students' plates \+ then went to N. A. of D. \+ put the p\[?\] up in the cases in the etching room \- Had a seance with O'Malley, Connolly \+ several other etchers. Home at 6.30. Found Jim who has come to stay over Sunday. Dinner \- Eve'g my head troubled me so much that I lay down \+ slept till 10 \- then wrote to Radph \+ mailed it at 11 \- a short walk \+ then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. May 3, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy \- some rain between 1 \+ 2 o'clk \- Got up with severe headache \- bothered me all day. At studio spent some time changing blotters for prints in press \- took out feet \+ legs of figure etched by O'Malley on zinc plate \- also \- took out portions of J. P. Morgan's face on the copper plate which has troubled me so much \- Home to lunch at usual time \- Afternoon, I took prints from the O'Malley plate \- After \+ went to Dr. Celley's to learn if he had yet seen a masseur for me \- \+ was weighed. 115 lbs. in my clothes \- then a short walk, \+ home to get a rest before dinner \- at b Carlson \- masseur, called \- arranged with him to give me treatment every other night. Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g. all but Kate went to Charl's \- then Ditoing came \- he went to Charl's \- I went to Century, monthly meeting, \+ had pleasant eve'g \- after 12 when I got home.
