## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 6, 1902

**Weather:** clear  **Temp:** [illegible]

A beautiful winter day — clear \+ bracing — Having some matters to attend to at home it was near 11 before I got to studio — I painted Morgan Pt. all day with only fair success — Home to lunch at 1.30 \+ back at 2.30 — \+ at 4.30 went to Edwd Cook's — 154 E. 37 — a meeting of the Delano Real Estate Trustees — Was there till 6 o'clk — Home to dinner at 6.30 — Evef at 9 o'clk. went to Century — Monthly meeting — a pleasant evef there till 11 o'clk — then home.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 7, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day — some snow in the mf \+ then rain. Temp. above freezing — clear moonlight at night. Bkf. after 9 — About 11 went to studio \+ until after 1 o'clk. was tacking down upon boards the pfo. that I took yesterday that were still damp having been kept under the rubber cloth — home to dinner — After 3 o'clk. I felt so heavy with sleep that I gave up to it \+ did not wake till 6 o'clk. Evef went to studio \+ wet down paper for more Morgan printing to-morrow — After 8 went to Charles \+ was there till about 9 — then home — Read till 11, then to bed.
