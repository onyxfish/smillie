## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 1, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day \- snow in the m'g & rain at night. To studio about 10. \- made tracing of J. P. Morgan portrait in gelatine & got it ready for transfer \- & laid ground on plate \- lunch at 1 & to N.A. f D. School immediately after \- found class at work with interesting model \- a young girl from one of the classes. Two or three are making serious efforts to do careful work \- the others seem to me hopeless \- Home at 5 & had a rest before dinner \- Eve'g Emma Ireland surprised us at 8 o'clk \- had carriage at door & wanted some one to go to Phil. concert with her. I went & had a very pleasant time \- of course she being, as usual, in irrepressible good spirits \-
Monthly meeting of Century.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 2, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain in the m'g continuing till the afternoon \- there high wind & cold. \- BKB' at 11 \- about 1 went to Grand Central Sta'n to get Time table \- then to Charles \- there till 2 \- home to dinner \- along nap after that \- Ge: came late in the afternoon \- tea \- at 6.30 \- Eve'g I went to Emma Ireland's \- there till 10 \- then walked home.
