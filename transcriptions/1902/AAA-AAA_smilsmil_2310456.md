## Left Page

### Tues. June 17, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day - brilliantly clear + cool - Up at 7 after a fair nights rest - wrote part of a letter to Mary before bkfst. After that went to 110 + soon Bormann came + coated Jim's bicycle. I went to Douglas's + then to Miller's - then to 110 + packed barrel with crockery + like breakable stuff - It was after 1 when R. \+ I had our lunch at home - I got big box out + commenced to pack that but about 3 I went to 29 + had a short rest there again to 110 + to Studio - got very tired indeed of the work of gathering so many different things from so many different places + distributing + packing them in so many different receptacles \+ to 29 about 6 o'clk - quite tired out - Dinner at 7 - Wel[illegible] my head troubled me - swimming + aching - so that I was quite unfit for anything.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 18, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another delightful day - feeling better + more fit for the work I have to do - Up at 7 + did some writing before bkfst. At 9 - after that Ralph + I started out - I went to Friedrichs + bought colors - then to house - then to Studio - finished these - then to Freese's - sometime there getting things + things - then to Randall's for neck ties + at last, at noon, got to 110 - worked there at packing big case till after 1 o'clk - then to Century with R. + had lunch - then home + finished big case + began to get things together to pack the two trunks - At 5.30 I was very tired + I lay down upon my bed for an hour + had a rest - To Charl's + had dinner at 7 - Wel[illegible], looking over books + to bed early -
