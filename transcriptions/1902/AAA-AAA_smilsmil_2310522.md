## Left Page

### Sun. Nov. 2, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day — after my late hours last night I had a late breakft. — after 10 — After 12 I went out for a stroll. wandered around to the Bryant Studio bldg. cor. Sixth Ave. \+ 40 S. \+ called on Saml. Coleman — found his room in great confusion — there but a short time — met Kath. \+ Reg. in street \+ went with them to Charl's — Home to dinner at 2. o'clk Had a long nap in the afternoon \+ after tea I went to the Irelands in E. — 17. S. \+ spent a pleasant eveg with Emma \+ Grace.

## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 3, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day — mild \+ quiet — I spent the mfg in renewed attempts to get a print from Morgan plate — working on the face between prints — The result is extremely discouraging — Before 12 o'clk I went to Second Nat. Bank — from there to Scheueling's bindery in W. 23 S. but he was nt. then home to my lunch — the day was so fine \+ my studio so uninviting that I determined to go to Greenewood Cemetery — I started at about 2 \+ got there at 3 — on my way out to our lot I stopped at the David Kerr lot \+ made careful measurements of that monument as a basis for a drawing that I propose to make for Mon. for centre of our lot — I found our lot showing evidence of care — the fine day adding an air of restful serenity — I left soon after 4 \+ was home at 5.30 — dinner — Eveg with newspaper \+ acct.
