## Left Page

### Sat. May 10, 1902

**Weather:** Still bright & cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still bright & cool — To studio at 10 \+ worked all day on Morgan plate — most of the time on the head — then on portions of figure with graver \+ burnisher — At noon went to Pach. \+ got print from the negative that meyer developed on Wed. last. It is, difficulties of light considered, a remarkably successful photograph. After lunch I took it to W. Borden at his house \+ showed it to him — He seemed to be in good humor \+ took me up to his living room to show me some of his treasures. To studio at about 3 \+ worked till 5.30 on Morgan — Then a short walk — dinner — after that read Evg. Post \+ prepared for Carlson who came at 9.30 \+ rubbed me down in the usual style — leaving me at a little before 11.

## Right Page

### Sun. May 11, 1902

**Weather:** Another glorious day — bright & cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another glorious day — bright & cool — Got up earlier than usual — bkfo. at 9 — \+ then to N.A. f.d. getting there soon after 10 — Council \+ School Com. there to judge school work \+ award prizes. It was slow work \+ not till after 12 that the etching class was reached — little time was taken there to reach the verdict — O'Malley first prize — Connally — Second Prize — Then I left \+ walked down \+ through the Park — took Mad. Ave. car \+ got home a little before dinner at 2 — After that I had my usual Sunday P.M. nap — up at 5 \+ again went to N.A. to pick up ph. of O'Malley etching that I forgot in the morning — again walked thro' the Park \+ got home soon after [illegible] o'clk. \+ then had supper — after that I wrote a short note to Jim \+ sent him $2.00 to meet a little bill — altho' the month is but a third of it gone he reports that he is "dead broke" — went to studio \+ wet down some paper for printing to-morrow — Then home — read a little while — Then to bed.
