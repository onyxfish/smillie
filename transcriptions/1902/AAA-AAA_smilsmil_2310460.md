## Left Page

### Fri. June 27, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Disagreeable day — a combination of April \+ Nov. — high cold wind from N.W. with flying showers \+ intervals of sunshine — I did not go out for a walk at all but was busily engaged all day unpacking the freight that came yesterday \+ putting up grille work, shades \+ curtains in alcove off main room. We had our three meals as usual \+ in the eveg Mr. Maghee \+ Katharine Hardenburgh came over \+ for a time played ping-pong with the young folks.

## Right Page

### Sat. June 28, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day — breezy \+ cool — after 6ldp. for some time with Ralph helping him take down his bicycle to clean it — then went to Harry Beede's but he was not at home. — Then I wandered about the place awhile \+ Rachel \+ I went over to the Hotel — then to Jn\. Scott Brown's — saw him \+ his wife \+ Mrs. a short walk in the Reserve \+ home to dinner — After noon fussing with lawn mower \+ the. \+ the stop valve into attic tank — Then R \+ I went to Otis's — but he was not at home — we got home in time for tea — Eveg Otis came \+ stayed about an hour — The girls, after going to Mossy Cascade in the mf., \+ with Maghee riding in the afternoon went in the eveg to his camp-fire.
