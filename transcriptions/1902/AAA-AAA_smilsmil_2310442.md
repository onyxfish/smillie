## Left Page

### Tues. May 20, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Unpleasant day, foggy, misty, muggy \+ cast in the m'f — dryer in the afternoon \+ clearer — Ed'w Cook came in for a moment after bkft. I got to studio at 10 \+ worked on Morgan — laid ground to put in new work — at noon went to the Photo. Sherer — 1162 Bdway \+ ordered dog. \+ Jim's photo — Home to lunch at 1 — to studio at 2.30 — drew in extension \+ background on Morgan — made new Asphaltum varnish — varnished plate for biting \+ after 5 o'clk went to E. M. Hubsch \& Co. — 117 E. 23d. St \+ bought a Pyrograph. outfit for Keene — Home before 6.30 — dinner — Eve'g read Baker — stupid.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 21, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming day. To studio at 10. Bit the added work drawn on Morgan plate \+ then worked with graver — Wrote quite a long letter to Catharnie Elkin with reference to Jim \+ preparation for coming exams. Home to lunch at 1 — after that went down town to Ed'w Cook's office — 150 Nassau St — to act as Auditor, with per. Wright Jun., of S. M. Cook's accts as Trustee of his mother's estate — we were about two hours in getting through — on final division Annie's share am't to $984.64 — In addition to this I rec'd a 2% div. ($1200) on Ireland Real Estate stock. To my great satisfaction. I after, I walked all the way home from Beckman S. to 30th. without being tired — I stopped at Hepke's — Dinner — Eve'g read \+ wrote a little. Charlie came in — At 9.30 Carlson came \+ gave me my first "active" treatment putting me thro' a number of exercises. To bed at 11.
