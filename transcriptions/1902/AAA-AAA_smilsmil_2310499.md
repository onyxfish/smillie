## Left Page

### Sat. Sept. 13, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain \+ strong wind most of the day with few intermissions — clearing late in the afternoon — Remarkable effects at sundown — a great rainbow high over giant sometime after the sun had set to us — During the mf worked on Borden outline, much bothered by headache to which I finally succumbed \+ spent most of the afternoon on my bed — had no supper \+ had an uncomfortable evef.

## Right Page

### Sun. Sept. 14, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day — brilliant \+, except in the sunshine, quite cold — Bkf. at 9 — a good part of the mf spent writing a letter to Mr. Kent at Montrose — After 12 I went out on Windy brow \+ roamed about till dinner time at 1.30 — Katie Elkin \+ her husband took dinner with us \+ after 4 o'clk — they with Jim, Ralph \+ self walked on the Reserve road to near the flume — they returned \+ the boys \+ I continued our walk — We got in soon after 6 after a very brisk turn — Tea at 6.30 — Evening finished letter to Mr. Kent.
