## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 24, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine winter day - clear, cold + windy in the m'f. overcast + raw afternoon. - To studio at 9. 30 + worked on gelatine tracing (Borden) till after 11 - then I went to Century Pub. Co w 17th. - Saw W. Alex. Drake + got from him a lot of old Posters for Jim - then to Scribner's + got a lot more of old Posters + four colored Prints - then to 2d. Nat. Bank. deposited Cht. for $40.00 that May nard paid me this m'f - drew $25.00 then home at 1 - lunch - To studio at 2 - MacKellar came with new drawing having made changes of proportion of tablet monument for Greenwood lot as I desired - I approved the design - worked on Borden Tracing till after 3 - then went down town to Fuchs + Laug - Warren St. + gave order for Zinc plates - on old plates of Zinc 18×28" with 10"×13" opening to take plates of that size that I may do some Registered printing - then to Watermaurs Bdwy + Courtlandt St. then to some Stationers in Nassau St., then to Sharpe's - 13 Bapster + got Copper plate for Borden - then to studio - then Home - Dinner - Eve'g I went to Chas.'s with the boys to give the girls a chance to arrange for the giving out of Xmas Presents to-morrow m'f. - Home at 10.30

B-1-

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 25, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A heavy fall of snow last night + continuing during the m'f. - B'f'st at 9 o'clk. after which came the distribution of Xmas presents. - Chas. came in after 10. - It was about 11 when I went to studio + worked on Borden tracing till near 2 - then home to dinner - Jane + Mary Hinskelwood + Chas. dined with us I did not go to studio again but later in the afternoon had a nap. at 7 went to look after my studio fire. There was no such need. - I read until 11 o'clk.

B. 1/2
