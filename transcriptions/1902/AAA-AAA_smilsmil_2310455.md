## Left Page

### Sun. June 15, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uncomfortably hot \+ muggy day. I got up with a peculiar form of headache for which I took caffeine \+ then acetanelid. The headache passing followed by swimming giddiness that completely used me up in the evef. I accomplished very little during the day. I was at the studio for sometime \- lunched at No 29. at 1.30 \- an afternoon fooled away \- a dinner at 6.30 \+ an evef wasted in great discomfort \- this is the record of a most unsatisfactory day.

## Right Page

### Mon. June 16, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hotter \+ more muggy than yesterday \- with rain beginning soon after 6 \+ continuing through the evef \- at times quite heavy. with thunder \+ lightning \- got up feeling little better than I did yesterday evef. but some relief came in the afternoon \+ I felt better at night. After bkfs. I went to Douglas' \+ with him to Miller's \- then to Stahnuerfeld's \+ studio \- it was about noon when I went to 110 where Raefph was waiting for me \- we got up our own lunch \- Strawberries \- rolls \+ ice cream \- Between discomfort in ning and heat I did not make much progress in backing \- but I have now a good start. After 5 I went to Dr. Cilley's \+ paid his bill \- then to Bormann's to have him crate Jim's bicycle \- then loafed about till it began to rain \- then home \- dinner at 7. August \+ Meyer came in for a call \+ left a little after 9 \-
