## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 18, 1902

**Weather:** Pleasant \- almost cold \- rather windy.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio after 10 \+ found it as cold as yesterday. 58° \- causing me much inconvenience \+ discomfort \- it was near noon before I got Ther. up near 70°. A young man, unknown, came in desiring to buy a Normandy painting of some kind \- unfortunately I had only my Normandy Barn Yard, \+ that was too large. I gave him George's address. \- I painted on Borden library 12×18 \- just as I was going to lunch \- the young man who was one of the Biography Editors for White \+ Co. \- now with the Knickerbocker pub. Co. \- name forgotten \- came \+ kept me 3/4 of an hour talking about the possibility of having etched plates for the new biographic [illegible] now being got out by the K. Pub. Co. \- I told him that etching \+ printing for a 1000 edition would not cost less than $750 \- I do not expect anything to come of it. \- Home to lunch before 2. \- \+ back to studio 2.30 worked on painting till after 4. \- Rested a while then went to 23d S. \- shopped unsuccessfully for envelopes \+ pads \- to studio again \+ home to dinner at 6.45 \- every loafing with newspaper \- at 10 went to studio to see to fire \-

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 19, 1902

**Weather:** Pleasant day \- to studio before 8½°.  **Temp:** Ther.

to look to my fire there \- the man who attends to it is inefficient \- 8½°. \- to studio about 9.30 \+ got to work on Borden painting making painful progress. Katharine Throop came about 10 \- at the same time her model, a young woman who posed in what passed for a riding costume \- K. is trying to work up a simple composition. \- Home to lunch at 1. \- \+ back at 2 \+ painted until 4, when I could see no longer \- was so discouraged by my day's work painting that I went back to the platinum print Reduction over which I started to make a water color sketch the other day \+ I scubbled the color off it \- just at dusk Dr Yale \+ wife came in \+ made a half hour call \- After they left I went down to Wanamaker's taking the water color I ordered some days ago \+ changed it for a smaller size \- then home to dinner \- Ralph came down this afternoon from Tarrytown for the Holidays \- Else \+ Charl came in \+ we had a family reunion \-
