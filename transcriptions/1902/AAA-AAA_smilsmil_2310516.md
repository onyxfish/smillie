## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 17, 1902

**Weather:** Rain in the m'g. \- dry, but cloudy \+ raw thro' the day \- Rain at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I breakfasted alone at 8 o'clk. K.\+B. went to Grand Union Hotel \+ breakfasted with Maghee's Party who reached town at 7.20 this m'g. I spent the day unprofitably running about after little inconsequential things \- Dinner at 1. To studio in the afternoon \+ built fire in furnace there. Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g. wrote letters to Jim \+ Ralph. At 9.30 went to Charl's for a little while after 10 to Gr'd. Cent. Sta'n. \+ mailed letter to Jim \- then to studio to look after fire \- then home \+ to bed at 11.30

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 18, 1902

**Weather:** Dull, disagreeable day \- wet at times.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary arr'd from Po'k.

After 9 o'clk. Mr. Kane commenced the work of cleaning out my closets \+ for some time I was engaged over the contents trying to reduce them to order \- then to studio for a few minutes \- saw Maynard but our interview was interrupted \- then to bank \+ negotiated a loan of $800. then to Scribner's \+ saw Mr. H\+ Smith for a moment \- then to Arnold \+ Constable's \+ bought under clothes \- home to lunch \- Mary had arr'd from Po'k. Afternoon again went to Studio \+ was again interrupted in my attempt to have a talk with Maynard about our relations as landlord \+ tenant. Eve'g. at about 9 o'clk. went to Charl's \- where Mary \+ the girls were \- home at 11.
