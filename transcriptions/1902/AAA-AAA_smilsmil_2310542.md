## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 14, 1902

**Weather:** Brilliant cold m'g. clouded afternoon  **Temp:** [illegible]

did not get down to bkfst. till 10 o'clk - about noon I went to studio for a few minutes - got pf - that I am to show to Mr. Morgan to-morrow - a short work on Park Ave. - went in to Charl's just before 2 - Then home to dinner - After that, a headache that had been coming on developed to such an extent that I had to go to bed. I got up for awhile in the ev'g. while Charl was in, but I was so wretched that I was glad to get back to bed again, where I passed a very uncomfortable night.

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 15, 1902

**Weather:** milder & overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

At 9.30 went to P. P. Morgan's - saw him for a moment & Miss M. - my proof was passed as satisfactory - then to studio where I busied myself about various things until 1 o'clk - then home to lunch - At 2 o'clk. I went down town to 30 Broad St. - Stock Exchange Hist. Co. - taking Morgan Photo - pp. & plate - Saw Mr. Stedman Mr. M. Wilson & others - my pf. was rec'd. with applause & in return I got check for $125. - Then to Charl's office for a few minutes - then I went to Vandewater St. looking for Sanford - manuf'r. of copper & zinc plates, but found that he had moved to Brooklyn - got back to studio soon after 4 - home about an hour later - Ev'g. Charl came in to bid us good bye, as he has to start to-morrow m'g on a trip down-east to be gone several days on business. I spent a good part of the ev'g. culling old letters \+ arranging those that I kept.
