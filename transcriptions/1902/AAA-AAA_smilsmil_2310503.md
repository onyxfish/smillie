## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 21, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day \- bkfst. after 9 \- then some printing + developing of prints from negatives developed yesterday \- I wrote part of a letter to Kate + at 11 Emma. Eliz H. Home. Ralph + I started for a walk \- We went to Mossy Cascade + then to the top of big rock by iron bridge. We got home just before dinner at 1.30 \- after that, some more printing of photo + a little more writing to Kate + about 4 made preparations to go over to Maghee's with the birthday cake for Mr. M \- The wheel barrow was decorated + arranged catafalque fashion + the cake was placed on the top, then we wheeled it over to the VanHorn's where a procession formed + went to the Maghee cottage where we were rec'd. by Mr. M \- + by W. M. in his Algerian costume. The cake was rec'd. with honors. Ice cream was served + we got home before 6 o'clk. leaving Katharine + Elizabeth there \- they did not get home till 10 o'clk \- Ralph + I doing the honors in the mean time. We all passed walking on the north piazza +, later, chatting indoors.

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 22, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dog-dayish weather \- misty, humid \- very warm sunshine \- weather threats of rain that did'nt come. I got up with a headache that harassed me all day \- Sanders came in the m'g + Moody worked all day at laying Stonesteps + walk to dining room + finishing up. After bkf. printing from photo negatives \- writing to Kate + looking after the men \- Before 11 went to P.O. then to Dr. Janeway's cottage to call on the J's \- a.M. Mrs. W. Packer Prentice who are visiting them. Home + busy till dinner at 1. Ralph + Bessie took Emma + Eliz. out to Chalse Pond by trail \- Eliz H \- managed to nimble in + got considerably wet. After dinner I wrote to Chase \- prepared a mail for him + went to P.O. before 3 o'clk \- Then back + spent some time on my bed \- Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g at 8.30 went to Mr. Mitchesnie cottage where most of the cottagers had assembled to celebrate Mr. Janeway's, Mr. Hornig's + others birth days \- Miss Mitcheson \- Miss Janeway + Bessie acted a little skit arranged from Conan Doyle \- as we'd with the Browning Club.
Home at 10.30
