## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 2, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day — bright \+ mild. Some "chores" at home detained me so that it was after 10 when I got to studio. I found more work to do on the Morgan head \+ failed to get a proof to please me before I went home to lunch at 1.30. I telephoned to Mr. Stedman that I w'd call on him at the Sherwood with a proof about 3 o'clk. I hurried home — had lunch \+ back to studio soon after 2 — I was but little more successful in proving — paper too wet \+ face consequently printing heavy — It was 4 o'clk before I left studio — went to "Sherwood" 58 W. 57 S. \+ saw Mr. Stedman \+ Miss Fuller, his secretary — both expressed themselves much pleased with my pf. — Mr. S. wrote a flattering note which I am to present to Mr. Morgan when I submit a pf. for his criticism. Home about 6 o'clk — dinner. Eve'g writing notes \+ writing checks to pay bills.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 3, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain last night — cloudy day clearing very pleasant late. To studio at 10 — worked with graver for a little while — then printing — took four satisfactory pfs. on Jap. paper \+ one on India — then cleaned up. \+ home at 1.30 — lunch — soon after 2 I went to Am. Exp. \+ sent pkg. of Keene photos. to Mr. Kent — then to the Ansonia — Broadway \+ 73d. 4th. Sts. — where I had appointment with H.N. Allen — he kept me till after 4. Put in two fillings — is not very hopeful about saving my few remaining teeth — Walked down through the Park — charming twilight effects — to 59th St. \+ 7th Ave. Took car \+ rode down to studio — daubed \+ sketched one of the Jap. pfs. to take to W. Morgan to-morrow m'g — then home \+ wrote a note to Mr. M. \+ after dinner I went to his house — Mad. Ave. \+ 36th. St. \+ left it with the note written by E. C. Stedman. — Eve'g at home.
