## Left Page

### Sat. May 24, 1902

**Weather:** A muggy, hot day — very uncomfortable  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ to add to my discomfort I had a mean headache all day — Ralph came soon after we had bhf. \+ a little later he \+ I went shopping — to Rogers, Peet \+ Co. — to Alexander — getting his summer outfit — Home to lunch at 1 — After that I gave up to my headache \+ was on my bed till 4 — Then to studio \+ worked till 6.30 on changes on plan that I drew yesterday — then home to dinner — Eve\. Charl came \+ stayed till after 10 —

## Right Page

### Sun. May 25, 1902

**Weather:** A very muggy \+ uncomfortable day —  **Temp:** [illegible]

Thunder showers about — one passed over us between 1 \+ 2 o'clk — another, a lighter one bet. 3 \+ 4 o'clk. Skp. at 9.30 — After that I wrote to J.K. Maghee \+ to Greenleaf Genl. Supt. of Hudson River Telephone Co. at Albany about putting teleph. in Keene house. also to Albert Winch sending him instructions \+ check — At 12.30 went to Charl's \+ got true time — home about 1 — dinner at 2 — At 3.30 to station with Ralph \+ he took 3.35 train to Tarrytown — Then to studio \+ wet down paper for printing to-morrow — home. Dr. George there — After 5 — I was on my bed for an hour or more — another shower between 8 \+ 9 p.m. Andrew Carlson came at 9.30 \+ gave me a good pummeling —
