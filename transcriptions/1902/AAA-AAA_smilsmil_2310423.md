## Left Page

### Sat. April 12, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mable Cooley came to visit.

Pleasant day with many clouds \+ fresh wind that made the street dirt unpleasantly diffusive \- I spent me m'lf at studio working with graves over Pierpont Morgan's clothes trying to give them some texture. Calverly came in for awhile \- Back to studio after lunch \+ worked till 4 \- then to Calverly's \+ gave him a sitting of an hour \+ more \- I am very sorry for C \- he is failing physically \- he is evidently a man discouraged \+ disheartened by failure to attain his ideal \+ get recognition \- He has lost his grip \+ altho' he persists in his efforts his work is nerveless \+ uninteresting \- The pity of it \- all of this comment applies to me. It has modeled an entirely new bust of me altho' I told him a year ago that the old one satisfied me \+ I paid him for it \- I wanted a less formal neckties than the one I then wore \- that was all \- The new one wears a pleasanter expression perhaps \- but the anxiety or care that marked the face of the old one was very characteristic \+ true \- Technically I think the new one is not so good as the old one \- it is plainly marked. The overambitious care of the modeler. It was 6 o'clk. when I left C's \- Dinner at home at 6.30 \- Mable Cooley came from P'k \- this m'f to stay over Sunday with the girls \- Eve'g all went to Charl's \- I followed them at about 9 \- C. played on his Orchestrelle this P'anda \+ seemed in good health \+ spirits \- It was late when I got home \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 13, 1902

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Many clouds, bright sunshine \+ cool to cold wind \- much trouble in getting water in the on m'f so that I did not get my accustomed bath. Bkf. at 9.30 \- About 11 went to studio \+ opened windows \- then to Central Park to the Palm \+ flower houses \- then walked over to N.A. of D. \- school \+ gathered the plates of the etching class \+ home at 2 \- dinner \- afternoon I wrote a number of notes. Geo. \+ Charl came in \- at b I went to studio \+ closed up \- Tea at 6.30 \- Eve'g some newspaper reading \+ a little writing.
