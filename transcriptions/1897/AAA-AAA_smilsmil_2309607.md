## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 17, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very heavy wind from N.W. all last night — it brought down most of the foliage — Brilliant, cloudless day, with strong, cold north wind. I got up at 6.30 — made a fire in my bed room fire place \+ then for an hour or more worked at packing all of the Rugs into the large wood box that I have had moved into my room — Then Rested for a while \+ after that had my usual Sunday m'f scrutiny \+ grooming — Breakfasted alone at 9 o'clk — Then, until 12.30 in studio trying to reduce chaos to some order — Then went to Dr. Q's as my medicine had given out, to learn what he advised — I am somewhat better than yesterday — had not quite so swimmy along visit with the Dr. home to dinner late. After that Dallas Stone came \+ we went over my farm together \+ I explained to him something of what I wished him to do next season — Then to Studio \+ packed until about 6 — After tea I wrote to Spencer Coon — \+ then labored some time over a letter to deGrand Benedict — About 10 I got Thos' with it \+ to P.O. —

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 18, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy frost last night. Superb day — lovely foal color in the m'f. Quiet \+ cloudless — the weather grew rapidly warmer but did not lose its refreshing nip. After bkfp. I kindled fire in the studio furnace — I had a number of out-of-door matters to look after but at about 11 I tackled the packing of big studio box in earnest — went to Bank, P.O. \+ other places — At noon to R.R. Station \+ gave conductor $1.20 to get milage book for me — After dinner, more packing \+ running about — wrote a short letter to Jim — went down town just before 6 — \+ again to Station at 7.30 \+ got milage book. Then to P.O. on my return attended to my fires \+ read newspapers — Katherin went with Wm Warner to hear a lecture by the Rev'd Dixon Jun. —
