## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 1, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine winter day. To studio before 10 with a programme for the day that precluded the possibility of painting. Consequently, when I got to studio \+ saw "Vesuvius" I had an intense desire to "pitch in", \+ I did so \- I threw aside my programme, set my palette, \+ tackled the sky, repainting it solidly \+ entirely. I do not think that it is now "weak \+ fussy". Home to lunch at 1 \- back at 2. Found Farrington waiting for me. He has prepared a Report of Investment fund to date for my use. Painted until 4 \- it was 5.30 before I was ready to take a rest before dinner \- I prepared a brief Report, based upon F's Report, after dinner \+ then went to Council meeting. I made my Report, giving particular attention to the deficit that will appear in the Annual Report to be made at the May meeting \- Adj? at 9 \- then our committee called at Request of "up-town wing, so that Beckwith could present before the Executive Com. the Carriers \+ Hastings' plans for a bldg on 110' 3d lots \- C\+H. came with their plans \- they displayed and explained them \+ undoubtedly made a serious impression on the opposition Blum was evidently capsized. Dielman was the only member absent. Beckwith spoke admirably in favor of plans. Blum inspite of nervousness, was horrible \- fat. Hart evidently did not know just what to make of it. Bolton Jones was absolutely mum \- \+ Perry was somewhat rattled. There was nothing to mar general good feeling except, to me, the officiousness and the complacent freshness of Watrous \- &c, \+ I left shortly before midnight.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 2, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty, raw day \- sleet at 1 o'clk. later in the afternoon a heavy, wet snow. After last night's exercises I did not feel like tackling "Vesuvius" my vital fires did not burn as strong as his \- so, when I got to studio I did some painting \+ later went to Butler's, then to Kurtz's \+ gave him order for Photos. from bust of Annie \- then to 2d. Nat'l. Bk. Then to Y.M.C.A. \+ saw Devooort \- then home to lunch \- at 2.30 Ret? to studio for a little while \- then home, \+ feeling very tired I went to bed \+ had a sound sleep \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Sur'f Read to the boys until near 9 \- then Charl came in. I gave some time to attending to the monthly bills that have come in.
