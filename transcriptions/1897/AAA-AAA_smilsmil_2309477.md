## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 28, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy fall of snow which continued thro' the day. Got to studio at about 10 \+ to work on 20' x 30' oil painting "A meadow stream, Oct." I painted house \+ rock in the middle distance \+ sheep in the foreground — then I went generally over the whole thing spending all day on it. It may be somewhat better for the work but I wish that I had more courage enough to destroy it \+ all the other old canvases that I have. Home to lunch as usual — Dinner at 6.30. As Chave took the three girls to the theatre I was counting up on a quiet eveg after the boys went to bed, but Je— \+ Nellie came in \+ I accomplished none of the work that I had planned to do.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 29, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Miss Waite's visit ended.

Bright \+ cold day. To studio before 10 — for awhile I was engaged getting Utica pictures ready — then I got out my old W.C. "Ravello Cathedral Tower" \+ washed out a portion of the foliage that was most unsatisfactory. At about 12 I walked up to Dr. Villani's in 47th St. \+ had him give a little attention to my teeth. Met there Mr. Flyer, the V. Pres. Nat. School Fine Arts, who talked at some length concerning art in general \+ the Nat. School in particular. On my way home to lunch I stopped at Miss Millens' school \+ Haelph walked home with me. To studio at 2. Worked on Cathedral Tower, using in the washed out part to the betterment of the drawing, I think. At 5. The Carnegie had at come for Utica pictures. I went to Butlers then to Artists Faling Co. - 147, 2, 23, S — Saw young Miss \+ Mr. King of Utica — then to studio and kindled fire in furnace that had burned itself out. Home to dinner at 6.45 — Eveg. went to "Private View" — W.C. Socy. — Stag party. Great crowd — most of them young men whom I do not know. Went around most of the gallery with E. C. Stedman. — My own work is a great disappointment to me. The winter moonlight looks fairly well but the San Remo lights up very badly — too green — \+ is no well plicity of objects \+ too great sharpness of definition — of course. I went home very unhappy.
