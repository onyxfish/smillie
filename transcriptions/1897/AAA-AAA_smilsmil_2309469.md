## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 10, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Charming another day. Bkfp. before 10 — Before 11 I went to studio to look after fine. Fussed about there for some time, Then walked until dinnertime. After dinner I had a few minutes of rest, then with Jim \+ Ralph to mus. of Nat. History — there until closing — 4.30 — walked thro' the Park \+ took Mad. Ave. cars at 60° S. \+ rode home. I had an hours rest before tea at 6.30 — Evt. Oct. 8.30 \+ 8.45 Wood, Brown, Church, Beckwith \+ Ga[illegible] came — a note from Nicole saying that he was too unwell to come — We 6 members of the Na. Site Com. consigned together I definitely gave up my advocacy of the "16° S." Site \+ we made our organization solid for 10° S. Site — Beckwith outlined a plan, very simple and businesslike which we agreed to follow. Meeting broke up at 10.30 Nellie's spent part of evey with Mary \+ Kate,

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 11, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouded m[illegible], mild — colder after noon with light snow flurries. Left studio soon after 10 — went to Calversy's — got bust of Amie — took it to Kurtz's \+ spent an hour with him in having photo. negatives taken from it. I hope great things as the bust looked very lovely under the top light \+ in the camera \+ Kurtz was much interested. Took bust back to Calversy — home to lunch at 1 \+ to studio at 2 — made slight charcoal sketches for Water. Color friseus near [illegible] \+ mounted nice paper for drawings. At 5 went to Girard Hotel. W. 44 S. \+ had a bit met with Nicole concerning site matters. Dinner at 6.30 — Na Council at 8 — at S. Site Com. A long session — much of the discussion being aside from the main question \+ des uction — Pielulau presented his project of a Ways \+ means com. to raise our friends of the Academy additional funds it was near midnight when I got home.
