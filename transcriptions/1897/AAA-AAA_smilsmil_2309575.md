## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 14, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Miss Harris arr?

Pleasant day — almost hot — breeze all day. Immediately after bkfs. I went up on fair grd. to superintend the rolling of the bicycle track — Jim & I took a short turn on our wheels over the cinders. I got to work on Grease \+ put in a fair days work, scraping on face \+ necktie. I had to give a little time to writing a business letter or two — I made a few exposures, Jim's camera, getting the boys, Trixy & Tim on the lane. I worked on Green until about 4.30 — Men went down town \+ paid my taxes — Then walked over to Pettus creek, where the boys had gone after dinner to fish \+ bathe. I found them \+ we came home together — Had a late tea, waiting for Miss Harris, but she did not arrive until after 8, having ridden over from Silver lake with Mr. Rose. At 8 the boys \+ I went to P.O. etc — on our return I read for awhile to them — the rest of the evey I spent making up Developer solution in my dark Room.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 15, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm, muggy mtg. Thunderstorms in the afternoon from 3 to 5 oclk. very cool, clear evef. Bkfs. at usual time — I wandered about the place — picked a large lot of very fine blackberries — Then, the approach of the Thunderstorms was foretold by a headache \+ I rested awhile before dinner — After dinner read awhile — Then wrote some notes — The storms, altho' very threatening in coming up, did not amount to much — After tea I read aloud to the boys — after 9 I went into my dark room to try my freshly made Hydronon developer — I got some rather promising \+ not altogether satisfactory results.
