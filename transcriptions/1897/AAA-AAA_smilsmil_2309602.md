## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 7, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A stormy day - about the middle of the m'f there were signs of clearing for a little while the sun shone out but before noon rain began to fall \+ continued at intervals during the day - moonlight at 9 P.M. After some business letter writing I got to work at Replica pen sketch of Dwelf Hotel of the Capucins. \+ finished it before dinner. After that, for a while mounting photo. prints then finished some N.A. letters - Then to P.O. \+ got another batch from N.A. concerning defraud Benedict \+ the Academy advertising. I had to write again as hard as I could until 5 to catch the ev'f mail - Ev'f altho' my throat is still very troublesome I read aloud for a while to Ralph. To bed early.
Many still continues to improve.

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 8, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy frost last night - A brilliant day - cool breeze - warm sunshine - my cold in the head, with headache interfered with my work all day. I was engaged getting things into groups preparatory to packing for our flitting to N.Y. Rutan \+ mate finished the Barnyard Terrace wall - Pap's \+ Ham worked for them during the afternoon. Many continues to improve.
