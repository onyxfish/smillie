## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 6, 1897

**Weather:** Bright, clear \+ much colder  **Temp:** [illegible]

Before I had finished breakfast, J. C. Nicoll came \+ we talked over Academy Sitt matters until 9.30 — Then I wrote a couple of notes \+ went to studio where I wrote two more. It was 11 o'clk before I got to work on my "View near San Remo" — stuck to it until 1 — Then home to lunch — back at 2 \+ worked until 4. 45 when I could see no longer — Rested in my big chair until near 6 — Then to Davis \+ ordered coal, \+ home. Dinner at 6.30 — a lounging evening.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 7, 1897

**Weather:** Fine day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — I en when I got to studio. I worked for a little while on drawing "Near San Remo" — took it from the drawing board, cut mounting board, cleaned up the frame \+ got it into it — Then I made a camera-lucida reduction of pen sketch to add to my series of European etchings — a plan for the future — I got home from my work rather earlier than usual \+ had a rest to prepare me for the eveg.
A little after 6.30 Charl came \+ together we went to Fine Arts Socy Bldg in W. 57th S. to the first dinner of the Fine Arts federation Socy f NY. A $2 dinner not good enough. Served in the Vanderbilt gallery which was not large enough — I think that as many as 400 may have dined, the surplus being served at tables outside in middle gallery — Time 7 o'clk — we sat down at 7.45 — dinner over at 9. Russell Sturgis began to speak soon after — rattled thro' a long discourse that at times was on the verge of being tedious — Comptroller Fitch made a very good after dinner speech — Park Commissioner Stiles was amusing — well low, painfully studied \+ W. S. Smith, much assurance \+ some poetry. T. W. Wood, meagre \+ feeble — Speaking dragged along until after 11 — many leaving constantly — Then Sturgis dismissed the assembly after many names spoken who were not on the cards — many who were on the cards had no chance to speak — Charl \+ I walked home — to bed at 1. Mabel Cooley took my place at dinner at home.
