## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 1, 1897

**Weather:** Clouded, but not unpleasant.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 10 \+ after a little fussing about I selected a sketch that I made near San Remo — a stream, with bridges, old walls \+ houses, hill sides covered with olive trees, and a glimpse of the Mediterranean — This I composed into shape in a slight charcoal sketch upon a piece of tinted paper. Home to lunch — by the way, at 11.30 I stopped work and walked up to Geo's studio, 57° 5\. to see the work he \+ Nellie has under way — then walked down home to lunch — to studio at 2 \+ got my San Remo sketch in shape to begin work upon it with color. Home at 5 \+ rested until dinner — before we left the table (Clark dined with us) Mr. Jessup, of old-time memories came in \+ spent the evng until near 10 o'clk.
