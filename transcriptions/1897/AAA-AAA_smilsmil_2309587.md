## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 7, 1897

**Weather:** Clear\+f. very warm \+ muggy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy thunder showers passed near us after 12 o'clk. \+ we got a little rain. I felt much better than I did yesterday \+ was able to take two p[illegible] from Gen'l. Green. \+ had a rest before dinner. Fontain \+ Matts worked on barnyard fence until noon \- so did Pope's team \- no work done by them (the teams) after dinner. I worked on Gen'l. Green in the afternoon Will Parkes came in about 5. 30 \+ took tea with us. At 6 I walked down to P.O. with Bessie Park. Ever[illegible] I read until after 10.
