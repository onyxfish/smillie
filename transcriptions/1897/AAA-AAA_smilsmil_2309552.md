## Left Page

### Tues. June 29, 1897

**Weather:** A very half [illegible] \- overcast & cooler  **Temp:** [illegible]

p. m. \- Rain in the eve'g. Spent the m'g at studio & completed my packing up & arranging things \- Dinner at 1.30 \- It was about 4 when I went to studio \- to Thos. Lock's to D. B. Butler's \- to Schmitte Bros. and then to Heka where I found some business to attend to \- then to O'Brien \- & gave orders for him to go ahead with the printing of my self portrait & letter \- then I rode up to 57th S't \- & had half an hour with Beckwith \- He & his wife are to sail for England on Thurs. Then home to tea at 7 \- Eve'g. while reading to Ralph & Edw? Cook came in & made a short call \- Just in from Athol Springs \- reports Jim as well & happy \- Later Charl came in \- Gentle, cool rain as I went to bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 30, 1897

**Weather:** uncomfortably hot day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After [illegible] Ralph & I started out \- went first to Kahler's where I left my shoes to be re[illegible]. Then to Lord & Taylor's where I bought undershirts \- to Cammeyers for R's shoes \- to Hoboken for parlor car seats but learned there that I must go to office Bdway & Leonard S't. Crossed Barclay S't. ferry \+ up Bdway \- no seats \- extra car \- come again Friday \- Home at \- Dinner at 1.30 \- after that, to studio \- to Einer & Almeund & bought chemicals \- then to Schwarz, in W. 23d S't. & bought a tennis set for Montrose for $16.05 \- then to Putnams & got a book on bugs for Jim \- then to studio and recharged battery that runs the door bells and catch \- Home at 6 \- very tired \- Tea at 7 \- eve'g. Charl came & brought me a check for $45.00, for Lenox Creek bonds \- all that is saved out of the $1000.00 that Jimmie put into it more than 11 years ago & losing for the past 8 years the interest of $60.00 per. an.
