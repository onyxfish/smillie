## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 20, 1897

**Weather:** Dull day, snow falling in the afternoon \+ rain at night.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio before 10. \+ to work on "Vinyards etc" — I find several parts that are obstinately perverse \+ I have puzzled over them all day — Just before leaving at night I thought I saw the solution of my troubles \+ am now impatient for to-morrow to try the effect. Home to lunch \+ back at 2 \+ worked until 4.30 — Evg. Reading to Ralph. Charl came at about 9 \+ I went to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 21, 1897

**Weather:** Rather march-like after wind \+ rain last night — clear m't., clouded p.m.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up with a wretched headache that came very near making me entirely useless for the day. But I forced myself to work in "Vinyards etc" having determined what I would do the day before — and I was able to do it — At noon I went to Kurtz's \+ arranged with him for the printing of the photos from bust of Annie. Then to Butlers about frame — Home at 1 — to studio again at 2 \+ worked until 4. when I decided upon other changes in drawing to be made on the morrow — Went home pretty well used up and had a rest before dinner — Evg. Geo. came for me \+ we went down to N. A. — Special Council meeting to meet Jury of Selection \+ Hanging com. for Spring exhibit to discuss the possibility of improving the general character of the show \+ to devise a practical plan for our work — 7 of the 12 jury \+ com. there \+ 9 of the 11 Council. The meeting was not a spirited one — Come. 13 — 2 from Jury — 1 from Council — Appointed \+ would together to seek for desirable new paintings for the Exhib. Home at 10.15
