## Left Page

### Thur. June 3, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A hot, close day — the first real summer day that we have had — I stopped at the studio, saw Maynard \+ got out a new lot of O.W. Paper samples — Then on to the Academy where I found a lot of work to do — which kept me until 1 o'clk. I went over to the 2d Natl before I went home to dinner — After dinner I went to the barbers \+ enjoyed the luxury of a very thorough shampooing — It was quite 6 then I got to Academy — There until 6 then home to tea — a good part of the eveg. I spent writing out checks \+ attending to my own accts
Thunderstorm from 7.30 to about 9 — Charl came in about 9 —

## Right Page

### Fri. June 4, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another close \+ muggy day with threats of thunder showers through the afternoon, fulfilled between 7 \+ 9 p.m. I got up with a very heavy head \+ fought a headache all day \+ was quite used up by it at night — It was about 10.30 when I got to N.A. \+ spent me only there, mainly working on bills payable \+ checks. Beckwith came in at about noon \+ I had a chance to con- fer with him — I went to 2d Natl at 1.30 \+ got home late to dinner — Rel? to N.A. at 3 — a light shower, the edge of a thundershower, hurrying me. I worked awhile on my report \+ home at 5 \+ had an hour on my back before tea — Katey Coleman, our cook who has been with us for some years, announced to me this mng that she had made up her mind, only yesterday, to go to Ireland to see her mother, consequently I would have to seek for one to take her place — This coming at such a time, when in such confusion \+ while we are in the midst of preparation to get off to Montrose, is rather upsetting — But it is hardly possible to guard against such contingencies \+ we must take them as philosophically as we can — No word to-day from Doran, the Central Office detective who now has the warrant for Farrington — Most of the eveg. was spent at my bed in darkened bedroom — Charl came in at 9 o'clk — at 10 we went to Drug Store where I had a drink of Castor oil with me result that I had a lively time during the night — up at 2 — again at 4 \+ again at 7 — I got up to my bkfs at 9 feeling very, very slim
