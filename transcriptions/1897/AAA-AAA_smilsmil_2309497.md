## Left Page

### Tues. March 9, 1897

**Weather:** Dull  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day, growing more disagreeable towards eve'g when it was decidedly wet. It was well on for 11 o'clk. when I went to 5th Ave. Safe Deposit vault \+ got a bundle of securities \+ went down town with them getting to Central Trust, 54 Wall, at 12 o'clk. \- saw V. P. T. Sherman \+ speedily effected a loan of $16000 23% he taking my securities as collateral. Got check on Bk of N. America for amt. Then to 25 Nassau \+ saw Mr W. Lee concerning my tax difficulties \- left a paper with him that I had prepared showing my possessions of all kinds \+ my liabilities. \- Then to R. Nait. Bu \+ took up de my loans there exce'pt about $1200 \- amt'g to about $5700 dollars \- By this arrangement I save 2% per an. or more than $300 \- Got home at about 2 o'clk. \+ had lunch. Yale came in about 3. Ralph resplendant with a measely glow \- but just as good \+ patient as can be. \- Later in the afternoon I darkened the room \+ we both had a nap \- I feel that I needed it very much. Before dark I went to studio \+ attended to matters there. \- Dinner at to. 30. \- Eve'g with Ralph who did not go to sleep until near 10\. \- I wrote a number of notes \- Charle came in \+ spent the eve'g with the girls. \- I got to bed before 11.

## Right Page

### Wed. March 10, 1897

**Weather:** Rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain in the mt's. but cleared into an April-like sort of March day. \- Ralph passed a very restful night \+ is to-day by much improved in appearance. \- I attended to numerous duties until after 10 \- went to Century Club \+ had a quiet study over my Vesuvius \+ decided upon several changes \+ amendments. Home at 12.30 \- lunch at 1 \- at 3 went to Architectural League 64th, 57\+82 galleries \- There for nearly 2 hours \- Then to Sherwood Studios, 57\+ S.\+ 6 Ave, \+ called upon E. A. Abbey \- found him painting upon a large dining room decoration, for White \+ [illegible] Reid \- He had a model posing in a white gown from which he was painting me gorgeously navigated in color. After a half hour with him I went home \+ had a short rest before dinner \- R \- had a two hours sleep there his dinner \- I had to leave him at 8 o'clk. \+ go to a Regular meeting of N.A's at Academy \- a full meeting \- 40 \- but very quiet. \- Election of members at quarterly meeting for first time under amended constitution \- Creedon, Sargent minor elected N.A.s \- I left before the vote for Asso. was complete. \- Home soon after 10.
