## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 30, 1897

**Weather:** Bright, clear \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio soon after 9 \- all the mf spent mounting pf. of etchings \- photo. f Annie \- etc. etc. \- At 12.30 I went up to Yale's \- Taking to him two or three sheets of O. W. paper. I found him able to be up \+ dressed \+ apparently in very good spirits. I did not stay long with him \- Home to lunch \- After it Ralph \+ I walked down to 2°. Nat°. Bk. where I went in \- he went on to make Xmas purchases. I then went to NA \+ waited an hour for Gardner \- he did not come \- Then to studio \- there until 6 \- Trying to coax a little semblance of order out of the general confusion \- Home to dinner at 6. Eve'g quiet reading [illegible — ~8 words]

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 1, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dull day \- Trying to be sunny for a time in the afternoon, but failing \- Got to studio at 9.30 \- cleared up \- Then got out one of the etching pfs. that I mounted yesterday \+ worked on it for some time with the pen. mark Millet called on Maynard \+ I saw him in M's studio \- of course he was in too big a hurry to stop in to see me \- says he'll do that "next time". 2\. Lord Weeks also was in M's studio. At 12.30 I went to NA \- went to 2°. Nat°. Home to lunch at 1.45 \- to studio at 2.30 \- worked awhile on etching pf. \- wrote a couple of notes \- At 4.30 to O'Brien's in 23°. S°. about some of the Record books that I am getting up for NA. Home at 6. Emma J. giving Jim his music lesson. She stayed \+ dined with us. At 8 I took her to 3° Ave. cars \- a little writing \- some newspaper, \+ to bed.
