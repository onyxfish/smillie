## Left Page

### Thur. Jan. 14, 1897

**Weather:** Dull, cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, cold day, with light fall of snow. Got to studio soon after 10, none the worse, apparently, for my dissipation last night. I worked closely all day on my Ravello drawing making progress I think. I returned to Mr. Homer Lee the card for the Banquet of the Sons of Ohio to be at Delmonico's next Sat. evng. with regrets, etc. Home at 5.30 \+ had a short nap before dinner. Evng. after getting Ralph to bed. I worked over my accts \+ had a quiet talk with Mary.

## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 15, 1897

**Weather:** Dull \+ raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull \+ raw. Got to studio before 10 \+ to work on my Ravello drawing — finished it in an hour \+ then to work on another one, a view from same point as other one but looking in apposite direction to a little town on the mountain side not far from Ravello. Priscilla Price \+ her daughter made a short call. Home to lunch and back at 2. Worked with color on said drawing until after 4 — as long as I could see \+ walked up 5th Ave. I stopped in the Temple Emanuel for a few minutes \+ heard some good music. Dinner at 6.30.
