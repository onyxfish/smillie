## Left Page

### Wed. Oct. 13, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delicious day - brilliant + mild - The atmospheric + color effects have been fascinating to-day. I have worked all day - in-doors + out - In the studio, taking down curtains + awnings + packing away things - out of doors, picking apples, pruning trees + directing the men. Dennis + mate worked on Berry terrace until 4 o'clk. when they had to give up for want of stone - no team at work to-day. Whalen worked all day on the terrace - Walker, part of the day - the remainder, digging out beach stumps in front of barn. I wrote a letter to Jas. Hart + took it to P.O. at 6. Suffy with newspapers + to bed early -

After dinner Mary came down to the dining room + spent the afternoon in the big declining chair. Bessie + I carried her up to her room after tea, when she seemed none the worse for her outing.

## Right Page

### Thur. Oct. 14, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another wonderfully beautiful day - we are having an unusual run of delicious autumn weather - The wind has been easterly all day, which may be only a disquieting threat that should be lost in our enjoyment of what we have. I was, in spite of the weather, much out of sorts - indigestion, I suppose - I was able to work at sorting + packing until near noon - Then I went down town - to P.O, to R.R. station, etc - Dinner at 1, but I do not take it. About 2 Mr. Dau. Dayne came to the house + took Kate + Bessie out riding, I rode with them to Drug Store where I took a dose of Castor oil - waited for P.M. mail + home at 3.30 Gill there, + Stone - I looked after them for awhile + then after the men + team at work on Berry terrace. I felt very uncomfortable but kept about - first results about 8 - I read newspaper + got to bed about 10 -

Many continues to improve - was down stairs nearly all day.
