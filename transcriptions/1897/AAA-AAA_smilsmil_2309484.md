## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 11, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine winter day — bright by spells — most of the time, cloudy. Got to studio at 9.30 and tackled Vesuvius — painting most of the day on a.n. distance Then attacked the sky + did not get along swimmingly but I managed to get over the surface with a lot of paint which I spread down flat with a trowel palette knife. After dinner as Maynard's man did not come to the house I took a dinner around to Maynard — then back home + read to the boys — got to bed early.

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 12, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snowing all day — To studio before 10 — worked on Vesuvius, fussing all day over the mt. + when I ceased work at night that as it was with the determination that as soon as the paint was dry enough I wd. scrape it down + paint it in as broadly + simply as I can, using a big brush. Home at 5 + had a rest for an hour — at 7 to Walter's + dined there — Mr. + Mrs. Donaldson + Mr. Sprague were the party — Edw. + Mary + Miss Williams came in after dinner — The D's left at 9.30 — but the members of the family remained until after 10 — then I went to Studio + fixed furnace fire.
