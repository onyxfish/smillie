## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 29, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Altho' threatening in the mf, it did not succeed in being unpleasant - at times it was almost sunshine. I got to studio at about 10 \+ fussed about until noon, unpacking etc. I got a letter from Farrington, very anxious to know what is to be done with him - I thought best not to answer, but sent it to Lee \+ Lee - It was near 1 when I got to N.A., There for but a few minutes \+ home to lunch at 1.30 - to studio until after 3 - Then to the new "Astoria" Hotel, cor. of 5 Ave. \+ 34th St. a sort of Private view to Artists \+ their friends - It was seen under very great disadvantages as there is so much that is yet unfinished \+ in confusion - the Mural decorations by Blashfields, C.Y. Turner \+ Simmons disappointed me very much. I met in the street Post Johnston \+ gave him a little money for which he seemed very grateful - He looked very wretched Home at 6 - dinner at 6.30 - most of the evef was at work unpacking the barrels of canned fruit \+ vegetables that Mary \+ Kate put up at Montrose.

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 30, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Partially overcast, windy cold \+ raw - clear cold night - I got to studio at ordinary time \+ was much put back in my work of getting settled by absence of carpenter \+ gas fitter. About noon I went to N.A. \+ there found so much work for me to do - it being the last working day of the month - that I did not get home to lunch until 2. It was 3 before I got to studio - The carpenter \+ gas fitter came at last - The c— got started at pulling up the black-walnut picture mouldings \+ the gas fitter to work on change of fixture to printing table. I got home about 5 \+ had a rest before dinner - Rec'd. a note from Kingsbury informing me that the Farrington case would be called on Monday next - Jim \+ Ralph spent the day in Bronx Park gathering material for their aquaria - In the evef they went to see Thurston's for Hallowe'en frolic - I feel out of sorts \+ spent the evef in dining room sofa.
