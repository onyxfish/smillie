## Left Page

### Thur. July 1, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Katie \+ Bessie ret\. from Ridgefield

Another hot day but with mitigating breezes \- After 6hfs. Many went with me to Lord \+ Taylor's where I bought curtaining for Jim's bed room at Montrose \- Then I went to 2\. Nat. Bk \+ spent some time there arranging my finances \- Paid off last of loan at 2\. nat. \+ left a check for $4000 to cent. Must go to reduce my $16000 loan there. It was 12 when I rode up to 67\. 5\. to the Students league to see Whitehead \- not in \- back to N. A. I attended to some business there. \- To O'Brien's \- to Charity organization \- home at 1.15\. Dinner \- After that a rest \- then R \- \+ I went to D. B. Bulley \+ then to N. A. There I wrote a letter to McDaughlin, Jamington's lawyer, giving him my position in relation to the prosecution of J\- my reasons for agreeing to his proposal to have a com. in Lunacy \- Copied the letter into the letter book \- and sent a copy to Ali. \+ Daulerbach Dep. Ass. Dis. Atty. Home to tea at 7.20 R. \+ I had tea alone. Evef. read awhile to Ralph, wrote to C. R. Woodin whose three pictures were sent to him this mf. Charl came in \-

## Right Page

### Fri. July 2, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot mf. \- Thunderstorms in the afternoon \+ somewhat cooler evef. Walter Cook came in after 6hfs. for a few minutes \- It was 10.30 \+ for I got to the Students league where I met Mr. D. F. Whitehead \+ he explained to me in detail \+ by exhibition of his books, the system of keeping the rects of the league. I was there until 12 \- then to 42\. St \+ saw Beltz at Spaldings \- Then to barbers \+ had my hair cut \+ head shampood \- Then to Manhattan \+ home at 1.30 \+ had dinner \- At 2.30 went to Ryan's \+ arranged with him to come to the house Tues. mf. to shut off the water \+ gas \+ clean out attic tank. Then home until the thunderstorms were over \- nearly used up with a headache \- At 5 I rode down to Bdway \+ Howard St. \+ arranged for R.K. seats for Monday \- Then to Kahle's \+ got my shoes \- Then to N.A. There until near 7 \- home to a late tea \- Evef. wrote a number of notes \- Charl came in as usual
