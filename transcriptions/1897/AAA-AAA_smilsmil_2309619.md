## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 10, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Neither pleasant or unpleasant, but just betwixt \+ between — The m'f was bright — yes — when I think of it, the whole day was bright, followed by a cloudless moonlight. I got to studio after 10 as I had a number of messages to attend to. Then I puzzled for some time planning forms \+ pages, with rulings \+ headings for Record \+ Acct. books for the N.A. f.d. — I wish to simplify \+ systematize all the Records \+ Accts of the Academy which as now kept are in a very unbusiness like shape. Then, until after 12 I worked on pen sketch, Amalfi, for Mrs. Lawrence. Then to N.A. — transacted my business there \+ to O'Brien's, the printer \+ left copy for one of my new Academy books — Records of [illegible] income. Then home to lunch. — Afternoon again to studio \+ worked for awhile on my pen sketch. Then had considerable walking from 23d St. to 44th St. — from 5th to 8th Ave — doing some shopping. Dinner. Evg. a little reading with Ralph. Then I wrote some N.A. business notes — to Lee \+ Lee \+ to de Grand Benedict. Mailed them at 10.30 — went to studio for a few minutes \+ home soon after. |||

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 11, 1897

**Weather:** Cloudy \+ raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy \+ raw, rain commencing to fall at about 3 p.m. — continuing heavily. Got to studio at about 10 — worked awhile planning pages for N.A.f.d. acct. books \+ then finished the Replica pen sketch for Mrs. Lawrence \+ put it in its frame. It was after 12 when I went to N.A. The architects' drawings are now in place in the library \+ the jury are to met this p.m. I got thro' with my work \+ went to 2d Nat'l. Bk. to make deposit for N.A. It was nearly 2 when I got home to lunch — after it I went to Miller's, the carpenter, \+ to [illegible], the locksmith's — then to studio — so dark that I could not see well to work. The pen sketch replica, being in some respects better than the original, I worked upon the original to bring it up to the new standard. — Then I spent some time sorting the contents of some drawers having sketches \+ such like material in them. Home to dinner at 6.30. Charl came \+ took Bessie to Boston Symphony concert. Ralph \+ I read to each other for awhile, then I did some writing.
