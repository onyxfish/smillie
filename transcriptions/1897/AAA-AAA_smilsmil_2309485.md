## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 13, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day. crisp + clear. To studio at 10. I painted on left hand foreground of "Vesuvius" - painting in the bit of habitation on the cliff - a good idea, I think, bringing the precipitous rocks into sympathy with the other side of the picture. I am discouraged over the picture + feel that I am now considerably beyond my depth in the mire.
Over the hot-water furnace man called in the afternoon + gave me some practical suggestions that I hope will bear fruit in saving something on coal bills - After + when S. got home - Jim went to Rob. Putnams - away up town, to a Valentine party + did not get home until near 11 - I read to Ralph + then to self - after 10 went to studio - looked at my work by gas light - sat + chatted awhile with Maynard + fixed fire - men home + to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 14, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very pleasant day overhead — mild and thawing, making it very unpleasant under foot. Bkft. at 9.30 - when the boys ret. from Sunday School they went with me to the studio - saw Maynards for a little while - he is improving + had gone up to the next block on 36' S. + breakfasted with Farragut. The boys + I went on a stroll up Mad. Ave. to 57th S. + down 5th Ave. They had their cameras + took snap shots at the groups of men, horses + carts at work carting snow from the streets. Maynard dined with us at 2 o'clk - left at 3.30 - my head troubling me I took a nap before tea time. Ev'g, Wood, Brown + Geo. came to consult concerning the site com. meeting to-morrow night - W. + B. left at 10.30 - Nellie came + stayed later with Geo. I went to studio at 11 \- fixed fire for the night
