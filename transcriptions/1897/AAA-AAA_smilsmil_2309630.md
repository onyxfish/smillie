## Left Page

### Thur. Dec. 2, 1897

**Weather:** Bright, pleasant day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, pleasant day. To studio at 9.30. For a time worked on pf. "The Road to the lake Montrose." + finished it — Then I got out my pen sketch of "near San Remo" + made india ink washes on it. At a little after 12 went to J.J. O'Brien's + got The Record of mortgages on the pres. To N.A. Home to lunch at 1.30 — To studio soon after 2. Finished the sketch "near San Remo" — mounted some paper (fine) on trunk board. for Water Col work. Dr. Gerster came in the straight until daylight was gone. Then I fixed up things for the night + went out and walked about the streets until 6.30 — Then in to dinner. Welly. Charl came in for awhile.

## Right Page

### Fri. Dec. 3, 1897

**Weather:** Raw + disagreeable.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Raw + disagreeable. Snow commenced falling about 10 A.M. + continued in a slushy way during the day. I got to studio before 10 + at once got my water colors out + commenced work on one of the beginnings that I mounted yesterday p.m. a subject based upon a photo. taken by Dr. Yale. I shall call it "Evening" adding the name of the place. The drawing is upon a smooth piece of paper + I am going to try the slosh, rewash + sponge manner. Soon after 12 I went to Davies Bro. in 42d. St. Then to Miller in 40th St. Then to N.A. + to Bank + then home to lunch — the p.m. was too dark to see to work. So I made some glue-paste + did more mounting — prepared a piece of D.W. paper to work upon. Home at 5 — dinner at 6.30 — Welly read to Ralph until near 9 — to bed early.
