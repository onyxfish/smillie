## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 16, 1897

**Weather:** mild, hazy  **Temp:** [illegible]

mild, hazy day \- growing overcast late in the afternoon, with rain at night \- After going to studio I went to barber's \+ had my hair cut \+ head shampood \- Then, downtown Called for a few moments on Carrere \+ Hastings at 444 Bdway \+ met Mr [illegible] of the Tilden\-dew Astor Library \+ saw the front elevation for the dem\- ments \+ Lee\+Lee \+ then to Central Trust \+ arranged a loan of $25 00 to pay the competing architects. Then home at 1.30 \- lunch \- To studio at 2.30 \+ worked upon plans for N.A. \+ record books After 4 went to the former O'Brien's with one of the plans of ruling \- Then to N.A. \+ was there engaged for some time \- Then to Brooks Bros. \+ tried on new overcoat \- Home at 6.30 \- Eve'g. Read awhile with Ralph \- Then for some time worrying over some N.A. accts got them straightened out \+ to bed at 11 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 17, 1897

**Weather:** Cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold \+ windy \- a part of the time, cloudy \- To studio at 9.30 \+ soon after that went to N.A. where I spent all of the m'g. Practically attending to business of various kinds \- Geo. Tho. Hastings \+ his wife came \+ requested permission to see the architectural plans of Carrere \+ Hastings \+ I went into the library with them \- I went to O'Brien's, to the bank \+ other places \+ back to studio at 1 \- home to lunch at 1.30 \- At 2.30 back to studio \+ drew some plans for studio's improvement. Home at 5 \+ had a rest before dinner \- Eve'g. went to regular meeting of Water Color Soc'y \+ took an active part. I took a lot of the O.W. Paper \+ distributed it among the members for experimental trial \- I then asked for a committee to take the tests in charge \+ report after the annual Ex'btn. McElhenny \- Frank Jones \+ Geo.
