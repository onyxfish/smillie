## Left Page

### Sun. May 2, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Exceedingly heavy rain during the m'f. — letting up in the afternoon + clearing at night.
Bkfs. at 9.30 + then loafing thro' the rest of the m'f. Walter Cook came after 12 + stayed for half an hour — Then I walked to his door with him + then on to Studio where I stayed until 2. Home to dinner — + more loafing — After 5 I went to S. P. Avery's to ask some questions about some paintings now in Italy in which a friend of Walter's has a pecuniary interest. A — in bed, not well enough to see me. To studio + home to tea. Eve'f. reading to the boys until near 9 — Chave came in —

## Right Page

### Mon. May 3, 1897

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Kate went to Po'k.

Dull + threatening — rain for awhile in the afternoon — After bkfs., went to S. P. Avery's + saw him about the matter referred to yesterday — Then down town to Wall St. — to Central Trust Co — Straightened out N.A. f.D. accts — got my coupons from N.P. + C. + O — Then to Mil. d. S. + W — + got check for interest — Then to Cullen's + got photo-films — Then up B'dway to 18th S. Saw Walter Cook — to Bridgeman's \+ got garden seeds for montrose — to [illegible] Taylor \+ got neck ties — to 2d. Nat'l. Bk's + deposited coupons — to Na f.D. + attended to business. There + home to lunch at 1.15. After that I spent some time studying some f.[illegible] — sent me by Koehler, illustrative of various methods of copper plate printing — Kate left N.f. for Po'k. at 3.30 the beginning of her summer season. I did some letter writing + got a short rest before dinner. Eve'f. Geo. came for me + together we walked to N.A. of D — Council meeting — adjourned before 10 — but after that the subject of the Schools of the new Academy — as they are to be — came up + was carried on with much animation until 11.30 — J. G. Brown opposing the prominence given to West Dept. of the Academy \+ insisting that the A — must, first of all, be for the Academicians — a mean + narrow contention that was opposed strongly, as was right by Beckwith, Blum + Shirlaw — Beckwith, Geo. + I, after it, walked up home together — It was after midnight when I got to bed.
