## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 12, 1897

**Weather:** Strong wind \- some sunshine \- more cloud.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at 9 \- The carpenter went with me \+ spent the m'f. There, putting new hinges on my printing table doors \+ other tinkering \- I fussed about at various little things. At 12 o'clk. I took my replica Amalfi pen sketch to Mr. Lawrence 57 E. 25° S. Then to N.A. \- to 2° Nat'l Buk. \+ to O'Brien, the printers where I got f'f. of a page of one of the new Record Books that I am getting up for the Academy \- then home to lunch at 1.30. To studio at 2.30 \- By the way, I forgot, Spencer Conn called \+ read to me the M.S. of his article to come in the Metropolitan mag. \- it does passably well except where he refers to me as a "master" of etching \- to that I objected. Much of the afternoon was spent in laying out pages for new record books N.a., at 5 went to O'Brien \- then to Siman \+ Anrand's \- Then to Union Sq. Hotel hoping to see S. R. Koehler \- but he had ret'd to Boston to-day. Then home to dinner. Eve. Ralph \+ I read awhile \- \+ then I did a little house cleaning in one of my closets where confusion has reigned for a couple of years or more — then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 13, 1897

**Weather:** Strong, cold wind \- clear m'f. \- cloudy \+ overcast afternoon.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio after bk'f. \- \+ fixed fire \- then home \+ went with Mary to Sloane's where I selected carpet for parlor \- then to Anroed's \+ bought underclothes \- then bought a table egg-boiler \- then to Brook's Bros \+ tried on dress suit \- then to Academy \- home at 1.30 \- After lunch I went to Am. Fine Arts in 57° S \+ saw the Water Color Club Exbn., without being much edified. Then to Am. Art Ass'n in 23° S. \+ saw the decorative panels "Canterbury Pilgrims", by Sewell for the De-Goned house at Lakewood \- then to O'Brien the printers \+ to studio \- Home to dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g the entire family went to Charels \+ had a concert from him on his Italian Orchestrelle \- Home after 11.
