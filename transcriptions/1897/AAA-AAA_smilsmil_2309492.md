## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 27, 1897

**Weather:** Bright, clear cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, clear cold day \- Much under the weather with headache. To Studio at 10 \- in spite of headache pitched into foreground of Vesuvius \+ made the change that I thought of last night. Got telegram from Cornele asking if he sh'd sell N.a. [illegible] U.S. bonds at 122⅞ \- Disturbing to an artistic frame [illegible] Home to lunch \+ back at 2 \- Painted until 4.30 \- cleaned up \- went home \- changed linen \+ then went to a reception given by Mr. \+ Mv? Chamberley, 5th Ave. \+ 15th St \- The Lions, Mr. \+ Mrs. E. A. Abbey \- the amusement, good music \- Had a pleasant chat with Mr. \+ Mrs. A \- met Sly. Home, Bessie Throop \+ Bro. Geo. Walked home with Bessie \- dinner \- Well \- read to the boys until near 9 \- Then a little reading on my own acct. \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 28, 1897

**Weather:** Bright, windy \+ cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, windy \+ cold. Awoke with severe headache \- did not get up until after 9 \- went to Studio at 11, made paste \+ pasted gilded mount on water color "Cathedral Tower, Ravello". Then home \+ with Ralph, who carried his camera, went out for a walk. It was 2.10 when we got home to dinner. I gave up to my headache at 4.30 \- went to bed, slept until 6.30 \+ then got up feeling much better \- tea \- read to boys until 8.30 \- Charl came in \- I went to Studio, and fire, then to N.Y. \+ got from the Safe Bond \+ Mtg of P. F. Brennan for $1700, brought it home \+ wrote a note to Mr. Geo. Sherman, Cent. Trust Co, 54 Wall St. \+ gave it \+ Bt. \+ Mtg to Charl to deliver to Mr. S \- tomorrow \- This is part of a transaction to secure the requisite cash on Mch 6. \- to complete purchase of N. Y. site.
