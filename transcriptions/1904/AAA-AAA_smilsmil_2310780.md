## Left Page

### Sat. March 26, 1904

*Mr. Thurber took possession of studio.*

**Weather:** moist, muggy, dark & disagreeable  **Temp:**

moist, muggy, dark & disagreeable - I awoke early & did some writing in bed - as is not unusual for me. I got to studio a few minutes after 9 - Mills' men waiting at door for me with 7 foot canvas for Mr. Thurber - it could not be got up the stairs, so, was brought in at the big front window. Shortly after that Mr. T- herself came & I was with her for more than an hour arranging her canvas in a satisfactory light - place for her sitter, etc. etc. - & then in consultation about treatment, measures & in particular, after painting - then I gathered such matter as I shall acquire at home in making the drawing for Mr. Borden for one of his library rooms - then I went to Scribners & saw Mr. H[illegible] & Smith - then to Putnams - saw Mr. Jno. Ireland & brought copy of My. C. Taylor's Ancient Ideals - then home to lunch - after that to Etching class, N.A. - little doing - made some arrangements for the commencement of composition work - difficult to get good models. Home at about 5 & had a rest before dinner - Every Red Ionusbury on the Spirit [illegible] & a couple of chapters in Taylor's Ancient Ideals -

## Right Page

### Sun. March 27, 1904

**Weather:** A dull day, unpleasantly chilly clearing at night cold  **Temp:**

A dull day, unpleasantly chilly clearing at night cold - Had my usual scrub & bk[illegible]. Soon after 9 - To studio at 11 - then sauntered about the streets for awhile - then home & did a little writing & some reading. Out again for a few minutes before 2 o'clk - then dinner at 2 - more reading & wrote a letter to W. G. Neilson asking upon what terms he would permit me to cultivate a strip of his land, as kitchen garden, adjoining mine at Keene Heights - Charl came in about 5 P. M. & Roger & Jeanette Adams came at about 6 & made a pleasant call - tea at 6.30 - Self I went to the Irelands - 2, 17. S. - Emma in Boston - & visited with Mary & Grace till 9.15 then walked up to studio & spent some time there looking over all photo. negatives & selecting some to have lantern slides made from them - Home at 11 o'clk.
