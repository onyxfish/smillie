## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 24, 1904

**Weather:** Bright, cold, windy & dirty  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold, windy & dirty. aroused early by headache for which I drugged myself & later in the day it passed off. Up at 7 & had my bath & scrub. & at 9. my bkfst. It was after 11 before I went to studio & opened windows to give it an airing. Then I prowled the streets till about 1.30 — then to Charls. got correct time — home to dinner at 2 — At 4.30 I lay down & slept till 6.30 — then had something to eat. Ev'g. wrote to Bessie Park Ballantine Lewis Skidmore — Frank Millet. Herbert Smith. Jim & Ralph —

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 25, 1904

**Weather:** A fine winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine winter day — I got up with a stupifying headache that increased as the day went on. To studio soon after 10 & tackled the repainting of sky on Vesuvius from near Sorrento — owing to the condition of my head I soon got into trouble through which I floundered all day getting very poor results for my painful labors. Maynard came in & gave me a pleasant surprise in the shape of a check for $200 on acct. of back Rent — This comes most opportunely. — I went to bank with it before lunch. worked at studio till near 5 — on bed at home till dinner at 6.30 — Ev'g. I went to Charl's with letter from Mary for [illegible] — was there for an hour.
