## Left Page

### Fri. Oct. 14, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another bleak \+ dismal day — clouds hung dark \+ low, with now \+ then a left of sunshine \+ the wind Rose as the day advanced. Some cold \+ bleak sunshine in the afternoon — the work of the day was more manifest than on preceding days — I cleared out the attic storeroom of the things put there last spring to be locked up from tenants \+ had the Rugs, cushions, pillows etc carried up to attic to be Ready for packing — Mary H — got the linen closet contents straightened out in a satisfactory manner \+ if we could have a D.H. Train on Sunday we could leave here to-morrow noon for E-town — but we shall have to wait till Monday for a Train — So, I wrote to Kellogg that we could be at the Windsor Sunday evng \+ to the D.H. at Plattsburgh I wrote engaging seats — \+ I wrote letters to Jim \+ Ralph — Well. I had a tough time, with Moody to help me, in swathing the piano in comforters, blankets \+ Rubber cloths —

## Right Page

### Sat. Oct. 15, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Superb day — brilliantly clear \+ growing milder towards night — Kllp. at about 8 — After that, with Moody, I packed the attic storeroom — then Pond came with water \+ filled attic tank — Then Moody \+ I went to the hollow between my Hill \+ "Highland" cottage \+ opened the "union" in the water pipe \+ let out all the water — Then I went to Scott Brown's, but failed to find him — Then to jn. Otis's, foot of hill — not in — then to Harry Reede's \+ saw him — \+ to Jerd. Reede's \+ saw him \+ to Albert Wrench's \+ saw Mr. W—. On my way back I followed the little stream that runs from East to West just north of the old widow Thin Reede house, up to its Spring head. I wish I could get a good pumping engine in there \+ a pipe leading up to the Ark — \+ so, Secure a full supply of most excellent water — Home to lunch at about 1 o'clk. Many H. is maid of all work to-day, miss Sadie Otis not coming — I sat Moody at Raking pine needles — About 3 — I took a nap — After 4 I went to Scott Brown's \+ saw him \+ Mrs Brown — It was after 5 before I got home — Jn. Otis waiting for me — I paid his bill — It was nearly before many had dinner Ready — a very good one — \+ it was 8.30 before we got two cups to main bldg. Mood came in at about 9 — \+ before 10 I turned in.
