## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 23, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some feeble sunshine about the middle of the day - mild - sloppy, dirty streets. To studio about 10 - most of the m't. mounting photos. in albums that I am to give the boys for xmas presents - home to lunch at 12.30 \+ at 1 went to Kimmel \+ Voigt \+ got pl. of Borden photo-gravure, which is now practically finished. To Studio at 2.30 - there till 5 - still engaged on mounting photos. Then home, very tired \+ rested till dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g, worried until midnight over the printing of the roll of films that I developed yesterday m't. - interesting views in Dresden \+ of the Bastei - every one of which is more or less injured by bands of dark across them - I think caused by the machine developer in some way beyond my understanding.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 24, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day - all day expecting some tidings from the Patricia - after lunch went to Chas who was not well enough to go down town, I learned that the P. had been sighted off Nantucket \+ w'd probably get in to-morrow m't. To studio at usual time - most of the m't spent in dark room developing a roll of films exposed on Patricia \+ in Stambourg - I tried developing the whole film in tray \+ was not successful. Home to lunch - afternoon at studio mounting photos. of places visited last summer in two albums for Jim \+ Ralph - Eve'g, more work Velox printing \+ to bed at about midnight.
