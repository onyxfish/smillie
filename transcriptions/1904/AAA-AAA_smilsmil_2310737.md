## Right Page

### Fri. Jan. 1, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day - to studio at 10 \+ painted till 1 on Passing Showers - Re-painting distance \+ portions of foreground Men home to lunch - After that a half hours rest - Then dressed \+ went to Varnishing Day Reception at N.A. of D. Murray Hill Hotel on fire - serious, but as I could do nothing to help the Fire Dept. to put it out I went on my way - Had a very pleasant time at the Reception - met lots of people I knew - the function seemed to be a success - There till near 6 - then home - dinner - Eve'g. went to studio \+ cleaned palette etc - Charl came in for awhile.
