## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 9, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim ret? to N.H. at 2 o'clk.

Rain during the night \+ early mng. dull day\- Bkfst at 8 \- after that went to Charles' for a few minutes \- then to studio at 9.30 \- Set my palette \+ painted a sketch very roughly of what I remember of the eve'g at Naples when I sat upon the steamer deck \+ watched the explosive erruptions of Vesuvius \+ the moonlight. Of course I was not able to get what I wished \- Home to lunch at 1 \- Jim left for his train immediately after \- I ret'd to studio at 2.30 \+ painted till after 4 \- Then washed up \+ went to 23d. St. \+ bought colors \- Home at 6. dinner \- Eve'g, very tired \- Read awhile \+ slept in my chair \- to bed early.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 10, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull day \- rain commencing after 5 p.m. To studio at about 10 o'clk. \+ painted all day upon a picture × commenced more than a year ago \- a mountain suggested by "the Giant" with the last ray of sunlight upon its top, trees \+ water in foreground. A short walk before going home to lunch at 1 \- to studio at 2 \+ painted till after 4 \- then a rest in my chair \+ home at 5 \- wrote part of a letter to Mary before dinner \- dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g Chare came in \+ sat with us till 10 \-
