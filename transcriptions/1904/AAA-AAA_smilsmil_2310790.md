## Left Page

### Fri. April 15, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain during the night — soon cleared. After bkfst. wrote a note of condolence to the sons of Mr. James Gifford of Hudson who died a week ago. I worked all day with pen on Bordeau outline, trying my eyes \+ nerves severely — home to lunch at 1 — back soon after 2 o'clock till 5 — then roamed the streets for about an hour — home at 6 \+ had a very short rest before dinner at 6.30. Evg. I wrote a note to Mr. Thurber at Oxford, O. \+ short letters to Jim \+ Ralph — my eyes \+ head hurting sorely — mailed the letters \+ to bed early —

## Right Page

### Sat. April 16, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

In bed all day with severe nervous headache — Got up at about 9 P.M. \+ had some gruel \+ beef tea \+ to bed again at midnight.
