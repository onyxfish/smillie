## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 23, 1904

Jim \+ Ralph came home.

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant - To studio at usual time. Painted over foreground of "Cumulus" after sunset changing it altogether. Home to lunch \+ back soon after 2 - a little more painting \+ then I spent rest of afternoon writing letter to Kate, planned to get to Crewson after the departure of Mary \+ her girls from Dresden. Home at about 6 - I was surprised by arrival of both Jim \+ Ralph - Jim I had not expected as some days ago he said he had decided to spend Thanksgiving in New-Haven - \+ Ralph I did not expect till to-morrow only. Dinner - Well. The boys went out together - I went to meeting of Cent. Com. on Admissions - The last meeting of my three year term - Wm. Alexander was passed successfully by Com. - After adjournment I wrote him a note of congratulation — home soon after 11.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 24, 1904

Thanksgiving -

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day after a dubious opening - hazy sunlight - Bkf. at 8.30 - I went to studio after 10 \+ until near 2 o'clk was there mounting small photos. prints \+ getting dark-room in shape for work. Then home to Thanksgiving dinner. Ralph's friend Harvey Bundy came down from Hackley \+ was with us at dinner — also brother Charles — It was a very good dinner — Sometime after at Charl \+ I went out for a walk - we inspected the great work of the Central R.R. into excavations in ley. Ave. \+ up Park Ave. to 57th St. Home at 6 - Well. The boys, Jim, Ralph \+ Harvey, went to Casino Theatre. I passed the time stupidly till I went to bed.
