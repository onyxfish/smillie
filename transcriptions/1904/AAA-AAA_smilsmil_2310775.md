## Left Page

### Wed. March 16, 1904

**Weather:** Bright \+ cloudless. windy \+ cold.  **Temp:**

At studio after daubing some pfs. \+ putting them between blotters under press I worked on study of old man's head \- I have overbitten the plate \+ failed to get the spontaneous quality so desirable in Etchings \- I have now put so much work upon the plate that it is not at all what I at first intended to make it \- home to lunch at 1. \+ after that, to Na of D \- These for a short time only got a number of prints in poor condition from Stahr, Schall, Bronson etc to submit to Etching Com \- Friday evett for St Louis \- Then I walked over to the Mt Sinai Hospital \- Fifth Ave. \+ 100 St Private view \- great gain \- Met Yale by good luck soon after entering \- we bored our way about \+ had returned to the front door when we met Mrs Gerster \+ the Dr \- He insisted upon showing me thro' the cellars where the boilers \- engines, dynamos, ventilating, fittings \+ like plants were \- a wonderful system \- also thro' the kitchens \+ quarter-master departments \- Then he put me in charge of others who took me thro' the dispensary \- the pharmacy \- the laboratories etc \- all intensely interesting as being the latest \+ most complete development of a public hospital \- I got home at 6 very tired \- Evelf. I went to the annual meeting Water-Color Socy \+ helped elect J.C. Nicoll [illegible] President \- that done, I left at about 10 \+ went part way with George \+ then walked home.

## Right Page

### Thur. March 17, 1904

**Weather:** Very pleasant \+ much milder.  **Temp:**

When I got to studio at about the usual time I made preparations to print my old man's head but the result was still very unsatisfactory \- I would work a little on the plate, then w\. take a print \- Ernest Roth came bringing some prints of his work taken by Voigt \- I am to show them to the Com. to-morrow evelf. At 2.30 I went to bank \- then home to lunch \+ back to studio \- more unsatisfactory printing \+ in taking the last print \- near 5 o'clk. \- a light broke upon me, too late, I think I know now how to remedy my failure but I cannot do it in time to have a print to submit to the Com \- to-morrow evelf \- Then I washed up \- very tired \+ disgusted \- Charl sent me a ticket for Boston Symphony concert to-night but I am too tired to go \- gave ticket to Geo \- wrote note to Charl \+ left it at his house on my way home \- a short rest before dinner \- Evelf loafed.
