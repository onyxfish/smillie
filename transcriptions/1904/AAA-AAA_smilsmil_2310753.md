## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 1, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow, very light in the m'f, clearing later bright + cold. Mollie went to New Haven on the 9 a.m. trains. I got to studio before 10 — found it cold — so I went to bank — got back in an hour — set palette + tried to work on Vesuvius but could not — feeling miserably + indefinitely out of sorts. I gave it up before 1 o'clk + went home — ate a very light lunch — then on my bed till after 3 — then to studio — pounded some resin into powder for aqua-tint grounds. \+ then cleaned aqua-tint boxes preparatory to doing some work in that line — Home at 5 — ate little or no dinner — my indisposition developing into a headache — between 5 + 11 o'clk took 1 gr. calomel in 1/10 doses — then to bed

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 2, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright cold day — but I was in bed all day after an uncomfortable night. About 1 o'clk I took some gruel + before 6 o'clk. I got up + dressed. Clam broth for dinner. Even, with a very sore head, read upon Parsifal.
