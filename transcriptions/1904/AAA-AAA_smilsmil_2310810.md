## Left Page

### Wed. May 25, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot day — Ther. above 80°. I got to studio about 10 \+ worked for some time on Borden drawing — then I cut a mount for it \+ before going to lunch I pasted it down \+ put it in press —
To lunch \+ back as usual. I then fitted the drawing into a small oak bead frame that I had, \+ made it ready to resubmit to W. Smith to-morrow. Home soon after 5 \+ had a rest before dinner — At dinner rec'd two notes from W. White — in the one of latest date he declines to let me have any of the St. Hubert's Inn plated ware — I at once wrote a note to Miss Mitchell promising to get ware \+ send it on as soon as possible. Then to Century Com-n Admissions — When more than half way there there were threats of a coming thunderstorm — I turned back \+ got my umbrella — consequently was 10 m. late \+ was fined $1.00 — The meeting was interesting — adjo'd at about 11.30 — home \+ to bed soon after midnight.

## Right Page

### Thur. May 26, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very uncomfortably hot day. To studio at about 9.30 \+ at 10 Ernest Roth came \+ I started him at printing some of his etched plates that he brought with him — I got out Vesuvius — 33×144 \+ spent some time scraping the paint off the sky preparatory to another repainting. About 11.30 I went to Scribners \+ delivered to Mr. Smith the drawing that I have just finished of Room in W. Borden's house \+ large photo. of same — Then to Wanamaker's \+ bought a lot of plated table ware for the Ark — Then home to lunch \+ wrote note to Miss Mitchell — After lunch, to studio \+ becoming interested in a couple of Roth's plates I worked upon them \+ printed two or three p'fo— Then I got my old camera \+ prepared to take it to Cullen to have my Dahlmeyer lens put into an up to date camera. Home at 6 after taking lock for St. Hubert's to P.O. Dinner at 6.30 — Evel. Chad. came in \+ stayed till 10.30 talking of my proposed summer trip.
