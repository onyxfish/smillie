## Left Page

### Tues. Dec. 13, 1904

**Weather:** Snow in the am, clearing later — clear cold moonlight night  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow in the am, clearing later — clear cold moonlight night — To studio abt 10 — most of my time given to ans letter to Kate to go out by early mf steamer, to reach her just before Xmas. Home to lunch as usual — Rec? letter from Ralph in which he told me that Rev. T. C. Williams had Resigned as Head Master at Hackley — on my way to studio met Rev. Thos. Slicer \+ had a minuter esting conversation with him on the subject. Finished letter to Kate \+ at 3.30 went home \+ at 4 o'clk to Mr. Sidney Smiths \+ heard Rev. T. C. Williams read selections from poems by Tibullus \+ some bits of Ovid \+ Virgil — The translations were his own \+ seemed to me (illiterate \+ uneducated as I am) to be happy \+ smooth running Set with Mary Cook, Edua \+ Minna Ireland — after the reading walked home with Mary \+ Edna — then to Charles — Ge. there — home at 6.30 — dinner — briefly wrote a couple of notes, read my newspaper \+ then to bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. Dec. 14, 1904

**Weather:** Bright \+ pleasant, but cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ pleasant, but cold — To studio at about 10 — Considerable time spent in putting things to rights \+ in rigging my camera for plate work \+ in taking a couple of photos of The River's Source — Home at 1 to lunch — Rec. a letter from Scott Brown enclosing one he had Rec. from Vivanti making enquiries about The Ark, if it was for sale, etc — I wrote reply to B — \+ got to studio at 1.45 I expected Edna Cook \+ her Murphy to call at 1 — found their initials \+ 1.40 on my door — so I missed them by 5 m. Went down town to Edw. Cook's — 140 Nassau — annual meeting Ireland Real Estate — Edw. Ge. \+ Walter there — I — made Reports — at 3.45 I went to W. C. Cullen's \+ spent some time there — bought two albums for the boys in which to put photo pictorial record of our last summer journeyings — Home at 5 — dinner at 6.30 — Luella two odd to go away up town to N. A. D. meeting so I remained at home \+ made Velox Prints — To bed at 12.15
