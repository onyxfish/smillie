## Left Page

### Thur. Sept. 8, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Hotel du Parc-Beau Séjour-Villa Ceresio + Casa Fattore — full hotel address — Widow A. Pella —

Beautiful m'g. The afternoon was fine but the clouds gathered over the mts. thicker + thicker till at sundown they were pretty well blanketed. I got up soon after 6 + wrote letters to Mary + Grace — Had bkfst with the boys soon after 8 — then I had more writing that delayed us till after 10. Then we went out + prowled about the town — along the Promenade + thro' the old streets — in one of the plazas are found a military band, about 40 strong — on a platform + playing in very good form to a following gathering — we waited about for some time listening to it — I took a number of snap shots — back to our hotel in time for the table d'hote lunch at 12:5 — After that we went out with the intention of going up Mt. Salvatore — but while we waited for a tram car the clouds rolled up so fast, covering the highest peaks, that we decided to take a steamboat trip instead. — We went to the William Tell Pier, or landing, + took 2 o'clk. boat + went to the lower end of the lake, Porto Ceresio — then up the west arm of the lake to Ponte Tresa + then returned, making several landings, getting back to hotel about 6 — after a very pleasant afternoon in close of the clouds — found a letter from Mary — Self — G — at hotel. — I rested at dinner time (7 o'clk) feeling that I wd. be better with a light meal. I had tea + rolls in my room while the boys dined at table d'hote — afternoon in ev'g I wrote while the boys went off on a prowl.

## Right Page

### Fri. Sept. 9, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast but pleasant, mild + quiet. I got up soon after 6 + before bkfst. wrote notes to Dean Wright of Yale + to T. C. Williams of Hackley, stating that Jim + Ralph wd. necessarily be late in getting to College + school for the fall term. + I wrote to the Hinshelwoods concerning my home coming. Breakfasted with the boys at about 8 expecting to make a days trip to Maggiore, thinking that the boat started at 9:40 instead of 8:40 I discovered my mistake when it was just too late to catch the boat — then the boys + I went off on a prowl for picturesque bits + altho' we had no sunshine I tried to take several photos. We found some very interesting places before we got back to the hotel at noon — Table d'hote lunch — then we went to the Paradiso station + on 2:20 Funicular train went to the top of S. Salvatore — we got there in 30 m. — altho' there was no sunshine on any part of the landscape the out look was very fine — the far distant Alpine peaks could not be seen — much to our regret — nevertheless, we were thankful for what we got. we remained on top 1½ hour — came down at 4:20 + reached the bottom at 4:50 — Then we went to a photo. store + bought a no. of photos. + loafed about till dinner — my head has troubled me for two three days past + I thought it would be better for me to fast — so, the boys dined without me + went off + somewhere in the ev'g. — + after a little writing I went to bed early.
