## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 9, 1904

**Weather:** Bright cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at about 9.30 \+ got to work upon "River's Source" to carry out changes that I determined upon when I saw the picture among others at the Century. I nerved myself to the point where I was prepared to paint out anything, no matter how dexterous it may have been done, if I felt that it in any way interfered with the general effect either in tone or color \- \+ so, I painted freely, changing greatly all of the right hand foreground of the picture. I was quite proud of my self for my courage \+ think that the picture is much improved. On my way home to lunch stopped at Chas's \+ saw him \- he is much out of sorts \+ has, at last, called Dr. Chas. Allen \- He seems to be suffering from a cold that affects head \+ throat \+ is very irritable, or excitable \- Home at 4.30 wrote short notes to the boys \- Then had an hour of rest \- Then dressed \- Chas. Nielats came at 6.30 \+ together we went to Mr. Barkley's, 40 [illegible] \+ dined with her \- a dainty dinner and a pleasanter \+ more chatty eve\[ning\] than I had expected \- we left at 10 \- walked home, [illegible] accompanying me.

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 10, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow in the n\[ight\]. about 2 in. fell \- The weather cleared colder in the afternoon. I got to studio earlier than usual \+ put in a good days work on River's source \- it is better but can never be anything but a painfully labored production \- After I could no longer see to paint I made up some photo. developing solution \+ prepared for work in the eve\[ning\]. Home for dinner at 6.30 \- well\[??\]. ret\[urned\] to the studio \+ developed another spool of films \- The machine did fairly well but the result was certainly under-developed \- I observed every direction as to temp\[erature\]. of bath, length of time, etc-etc. It was about 10.30 when I got home \-

This m\[onth\]. I suppose that Mary \+ her girls started for home from Hamburg on the Patricia.
