## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 30, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow during the m'f - attempts at clearing during afternoon - beautiful moonlight at night. To studio at 10 \+ spent the m'f painting over the mts. that I painted yesterday - they were still so wet that I could paint into them - Home to lunch at 1 \+ immediately after it I went to N.A. etching class - found a full class - some of them very enthusiastic. Scholl has done a couple of plates, out of the class, that narrowly escapes being very fine - but he is asurprisingly uneven worker. Sfahr did a lot of printing - I worked very hard - did not stop until 5 - got home at 6.30 - very tired \+ just in time for dinner - After dinner I loafed with news papers. Chat. Collins called to get me to go to Century club with him but I begged off - being very tired - to bed early.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 31, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

mollie Adams spent night with us.

Pleasant day - Some hazy sunshine. Went thro' my m'f \+ scrub \+ got to bkfs. at 9.30 after that, news paper \+ wrote long letter to Fred. W. Morton. Brush \+ Pencil. arent Criticism of M.A. Exh'n written by Hamsor N. Howard. Went to studio - opened windows - then had a walk up \+ down Park Ave. till near 2 - went to Charls - got correct time - then home to dinner - After that spent some time over accounts. Then a rest from 5 to 6. Mollie Adams arr. from Pompton soon after 6 - had supper with us \+ stayed over night - I wrote a short note to Jim, enclosing check for his monthly allowance \+ a second pay m't on his dress-suit acct. Took it to G't. Cent. Station \+ mailed it.
