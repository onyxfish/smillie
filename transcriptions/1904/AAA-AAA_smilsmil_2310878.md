## Left Page

### Mon. Oct. 10, 1904

**Weather:** Mild \+ hazy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mild \+ hazy — I had my bkft. in bed \+ did not get up till after 10 — Found myself very shaky \+ with an uncomfortable feeling in the head — a feeling of bashfulness \+ lightness — Ge? came in to see me on he way to station to go to Po'keepsie to help celebrate 61° Anniversary of wedding of Uncle W. \+ aunt A — I was to go with him but was not able — After I dressed myself I podged about — went to G.C. Station \+ bought tickets \+ seats for West port — About 1.30 I had lunch \+ after that went to Gale's — saw him \+ had a confab with him — he thinks that if I am duly careful not to overtax my little \+ strength \+ to keep my body warm I will not be taking unwise risks in going to-morrow — so reassured I telegraphed to Kellogg at E-town — the rest of the afternoon \+ eve'g was absent writing to the boys \+ in very slow preparation for to-morrow's journey — Dinner at 6.30 — We \+ Ge? came in for a few min. to report his visit to Po'k — where he found all of the invalids in a fairey comfortable state of mind \+ body.

## Right Page

### Tues. Oct. 11, 1904

**Weather:** Roggy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Windsor Hotel. Elizabethtown. N.Y.

Hazy, mild, after a light rain in early am. Up before 6 — bkft. at 7.30 \+ at 8.30 Mary H. \+ I, with Jennings carrying our luggage went to G.C. station where W. \+ I took the 8.45 train running thro' to Albany with but a two min. stop at Po'k — hazy weather till we got was Albany — then a dark fog followed by heavy rain — after which the clouds grew lighter — between Saratoga \+ Whitehall it was delightfully sunny — then it clouded over again — between Albany \+ West port we lost 20 min — getting to W. at 4.30 Kellogg's team waiting — drove smartly to E-town getting to hotel at about 6 — found young Will Kellogg waiting — also his mother \+ two sisters \+ Mr. \+ Mrs. Tracy — the last two having been running the Reserve Club house at St. Hubert's. We had supper (we were hungry as we had had but a poor lunch on a Buffet car) \+ after Tracy had told me about things at St. Hubert's last summer I turned in at about 9.30 pretty well tired but feeling much better than I did in the m[illegible].
