## Left Page

### Sat. May 21, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely m'g \- Up early after a good night's rest \- had a short walk before bkfs'. \+ enjoyed it exceedingly. Bkfs. at 8 \+ at about 10.30 we started for West Port \+ had a delightful ride in strong contrast to the one of yesterday ave'f. We jogged along with ample time to enjoy the beauties about us \- Took 12.15 train \- had a good dinner. Got to Albany soon after 4 \- had 35 m. to wait, so we walked up the hill to the State House \+ took a look at them \- then back to our train \+ got into N.Y. on time at 8 o'clk. Found Charl writing for us at the House \- we had something to eat \+ to bed early \-

## Right Page

### Sun. May 22, 1904

*(see Aug 14)*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

At Vienna \- Hotel Bristol.

A cloudless day \- very warm \- up at 9 \+ busied myself in various ways till about 9 \- Then, the boys being ready, we went down stairs to bkfs'. After that we went to St. Stephans, getting there a few minutes for 10 \- a goodly number of people \- singing what seemed to be a hymn \- a quiet, suppressed sort Congregational singing that to me, the time \+ place considered, was very impressive \- \+ so was the Repetition of the Lord's Prayer, or something like it \- There was no notable music \- such as I had expected \- a priest ascended the pulpit \+ preached energetically for 3/4 of an hour \- we waited thro' it, standing most of the time hoping that each moment w. be the last \+ that then we w. have some High Mass music, but when he dried up \- no music followed \- we left at 11 o'clk. \+ went to the Hof K. \- the Kunstiner \- a poor old deal \- there we heard a lot of loud \+ showy \- music \- but it failed to please us \- Then to Hotel, changed to a lighter suit of clothes \+ out again with our cameras. Went to Stadt Park \- got snap at Mackart monument \&c. Then by car Rose to Art Museum \- walked back of it \- past Royal Stables, Nat. Hist. Mus. Palace of Justice \- the Volks Theatre, back of the Reichsraths Geb \- to the Platz aus \- Taking snap shots \- Then down by Hofburg Theatre \- Thro' Volksgarten to the Restaurant where we had a light lunch \- after that we went over to Art Museum \+ until 4 o'clk. we went thro' the Arms part of the museum \- then being tired I went to Hotel while the boys went to the Prater to finish the day on the Midway \- I went to my room \+ had a good sleep \- The boys came in before 9 \+ we went down \+ dined in good style \- after our dinner we spent some time in Reading along with N.Y. newspapers \- about 9 we went to the Rathaus Keller expecting to find a great crowd \+ get some beer \- we failed in both \- in one of the open courts we were told that wine only was served, so we left \- walked down thro' Volksgarten \- at the Opera Ho. we parted, the boys going for beer \- I went to Hotel.
