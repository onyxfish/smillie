## Left Page

### Sun. Sept. 4, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still the same kind of weather - dull wet mts., clearing about mid day - a few hours of bright, warm sunshine + then, after 4 o' clc - thickening up again the distant mts. at no time visible. I had bkfp. before 9 - the boys slept an hour longer - then we went out after they had breakfasted - bought P.O. cards, pictures + then to the Swiss Nat'l museum + were much interested there till 1 o'clc - then we went out into the Park + at a restaurant there had Rolls + beer - then back to museum + viewed the "Treasury" - then had a random cool to the east side + along the river where there are old bldgs. As the sun shone out clear I ret'd to hotel + got camera - boys went rowing + I ret'd to old houses + got some snap shots. I wandered for some time thro' some of the older streets then to Lake Promenade + on to the Lion statue + took photos - bad light - then back to hotel + rested for an hour - at 7 we went to the Tonhalle, near by, on lake front, a very fine building - Restaurant - garden + concert hall - the concert this eveg. was in the hall, under a large dome. + there we were served a good Table d'hote dinner, while concert was in progress, at a small table - of Tail + soup, salmon, Roast veal - carrots, peas, potatoes - partridge, salad + Ices - also beer, at a total cost, dinner + concert 22f a very interesting experience - great crowd - concert over at 10.30 - then back to hotel.

## Right Page

### Mon. Sept. 5, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Lucerne "Neu Schweizerhaus Pension - Dependence "Hofbourg".

mf. leaden but the clouds were burned away by 11 + the sunshine out brightly - nothing could be seen of the distant mts. + I decided to give up waiting - after 6kf: we packed + gave up our Rooms - Then we strolled out along the east shore of the lake + back + about till after 1 - then in to the Monopole Café + had bread, beer + an ice - then to hotel + settled off by hotel bus at 2.30 - Train for Lucerne at 3.05 - it was after 4.30 when we got to L - + after 5 where we got up to the Pension on the hill, recommended to us by members of the family + friends who have been here - we were given two Rooms in a Dependence near the house - one was good - the other, in the attic, was very unattractive + at first decided to go back + to a hotel but the boys thought they could stand it for one night + I agreed to stay - so we were installed + after that went down the hill to the Promenade + along to the old Bridge - crossed that + recrossed on the bridge nearby, bought some fruit + raised up the hill to our Rooms - washed up + went to our Table d'hote dinner - large dining Room - two long tables + a number of smaller ones - all filled - not less than a hundred diners - largely English - we were given a little Table + had a fair dinner - after that we sat for a while in large sitting Room - seeing nothing that promised to be congenial in the way of company we retired to our Room - The boys tossed up for choice of beds + Jim lost - so he went to the poor attic Room.
