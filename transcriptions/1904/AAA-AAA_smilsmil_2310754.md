## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 3, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright cold day, starting at 15° \+ getting up to about 25° before night. I got up feeling fairly after yesterday's bout, but not yet ready for work. I got to studio late as I stayed at home after bkfst. to clean up electric batteries etc. — at the studio I fussed around fixing things here \+ there till near 1 — then home to lunch \+ at 2 went to Na. fd. Etching class — there till near 5 — home at 5.40 \+ had a little nap before dinner — Eve'g. Charl came in \+ we arranged for the Parsifal service to-morrow.

Mrs. Dexter Thurber called at studio \+ house this afternoon — left word that she wd. write.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 4, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*Mallie Adams came.*

Fine day but very cold. — to studio at usual time \+ found the M'lf working at my Acid sink Repairing a check thro' the bottom, causing a leak — in preparations for some dry aqua-tint experiments for the benefit of Etching class. Home at 1 — found note from Mrs. Thurber asking me to call her on 'phone bet. 1 \+ 2 o'clk at Chelsea W. 23? I — went to Charles' \+ called her on his 'phone — made appointment to meet her in half an hour at my studio — she came with Mr. [illegible] is looking for a studio, for a month, in which to paint a portrait — I promised my aid — then home soon after 3 — had 3/4 of an hour of rest, then to Charles' \+ at 5 we were at the Met.
