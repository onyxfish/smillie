## Left Page

### Thur. Nov. 3, 1904

**Weather:** Dull m'f, clearing off bright later in the day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

At breakfast I rec'd card from K. \+ V. saying that if I came down early I might get a proof from my Photo-grav. plate - I hurried getting there about 9.30 but Pennell was there ahead of me \+ H'd V - was hard at work proving a score or more of plates just done by [illegible]. View of N.Y. city "Sky Scrapers" - a queer lot to me - but very interesting - all etched (or rather, drawn upon the plate,) direct from nature, without reversing. He says "Jimmy" (that is Whistler) did'nt reverse \+ he wants no better authority for not doing it. The result is that the proofs are bewildering to any one. familiar with the streets \+ buildings represented - On my way up town stopped at Scribner's \+ saw Mr. Smith - Then to back - then to Studio at 12 - mounted pen. sketch of yesterday \+ worked over it with india ink washes \+ Chinese white - I am encouraged by the result - Had a short rest before dinner - Sue \+ I went with Charl to Boston Symphony Concert at Carnegie Hall \+ heard two old time friends. Beethoven's 4th Symphony \+ Tannhauser Overture — After Concert we walked up to Columbus Circle \+ by Subway back to Grand Central -

## Right Page

### Fri. Nov. 4, 1904

**Weather:** Dull \+ threatening m'f. but it came off sunny in the middle of the day, thick clouing up again at night - getting ready, I am afraid, to give us bad weather on election day.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rather late again in getting to Studio - Wrote to Mr. Barhydt telling her that I had resigned my place as teacher of Etching - U.A. of D - Went to Davies Coal yard - to grocer \+ butcher - then to Studio. Had a call from Mr. Cole who asks the privilege of putting a toilet placard (Referring to her stable, No. 5 Sniffen Court) on my Studio wall - pleading that I put on her building it cannot be generally seen - I tell her she might put it back of cellar door. Home to lunch \+ back - worked on pen sketch at Dresden - done of Frauen Kirche - Home at 5. had a short rest - while dressing for dinner at Mary took's a p'f - from Cullen came - Six doz - developed negatives \+ a proof from each - I lost time in looking over them - a shabby lot - I cannot understand the inequalities - a few are good - none are first rate - the majority of them are mottled \+ clouded in a discouraging way - I wonder how much of it is owing to poor development - Wrote hurried notes to Jim \+ Ralph - was had to go to Studio to get a clean collar \+ got to E.M.C's at 7.15 - Soup had just been served - Rev'd Mr. Smith \+ wife \+ Henry Murphy were guests - I had a good dinner \+ a good time.
