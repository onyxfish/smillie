## Left Page

### Sat. April 9, 1904

**Weather:** Nasty day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Nasty day — went to studio after bklp — saw Mrs. Thurber — she gave me check for $25 \- \+ said she w'd. give up studio to-day. It was late before I got to work on Borden putting in the new furniture of which I got sketches yesterday — lunch at 1 — I did not get more than two hours on B — in the afternoon — commenced the retracing on to Bristol bd. for the india ink — Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g. reading \+ working on accts trying to distribute my deficit.

## Right Page

### Sun. April 10, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fickle April day — sunshine \+ shadow — a little march in the wind at times. Up at 7 \+ had some exercise \+ a scrub \+ down to bklp: at 9. Ralph had a chafed heel — so we could not go off for a walk — It was after 11 when I went out for a walk — Sauntered down along the East River front, from 38th to 29th S'. going out to the pier ends looking for picturesque stuff — Home before 2 — dinner — at about 4 Jim \+ I went to Walter's then to Edw'. Cook's — nobody at home at either place. Then we went to studio \+ found Mrs. Thurber there — had a half-hour's chat with her — Then home — Took a nap instead of supper — Eve'g. Read Jas.
