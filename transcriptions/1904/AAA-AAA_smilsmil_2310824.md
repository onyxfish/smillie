## Left Page

### Wed. June 22, 1904

*Jim arr? from New-Haven*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very muggy m't - close, heavy \+ hot — a brisk shower at about noon. Quite late in the afternoon a remarkable change in the weather clearing into a sprightly, clear \+ cool condition — Jim arr. before we sat down to bkfs - having come on the night boat - after bkfs: Vondross came to the house - I went to studio with him \+ he got his prints \+ plates - then to grocer \+ butcher \+ home - then with Jim to Second Nat'l Bank \+ got letter \+ Cr. issued by Nat'l City Bank - we were at bank for some time \+ I tried to give Jim an object lesson in finance - home at 1 - lunch - the boys went down town in the afternoon - I worried over balancing of bank acct. I cannot find an error of 10 42¢. St. \+ went to White, Vaughan \+ Co. in that Miller is refitting - then to Millers - Dinner at 6.30 - Evening Charl came in for a little while - Jim, Ralph \+ I went to Edw. Cook's to bed Tom good bye - he is to sail for Europe to-morrow mg - We met there Clarence Bishop, Smith, Murphy - Carleton - \+ members of the family - home soon after 10 -

## Right Page

### Thur. June 23, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day - cool, crisp \+ breezy - W. Kramer called soon after 9 with photo - on glass - Reduction (positive) of Borden drawing preparatory to Photo-gravure. Then I went to Clausen's about 33×44 frame - he sent it to studio before noon - finally I got to studio - set palette \+ commenced a hard days work on "Vesuvius" repainting the water - stuck to it till 1 o'clk - then home to lunch - back at 2.30 \+ worked till 5.30 - painting \+ repainting the sea - but unable to get what I wanted - I got home at about 6 disgusted \+ sick - Dinner at 6.30 - Sue \+ Rc? \+ Emily Cook came in for a few minutes - they brought a couple of boxes for me to keep for them during the summer - I wrote notes \+ got to bed early.
