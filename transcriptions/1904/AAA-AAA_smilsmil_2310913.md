## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 19, 1904

**Weather:** Somewhat dull \+ hazy, but not unpleas  **Temp:** [illegible]

ant — very mild — snow thawing freely \+
very sloppy walking — the rawness of melting
snow in the air — To studio shortly after 10
\+ worked all day (a short one because I
could not see to work after 4.30) on the Borden
photo-gravure plate, getting it almost ready
for, what I hope will be, the last pf. At 12.30
went to bank \+ got a loan of $300 to get me
over Xmas time — then home to lunch — then
to studio — home at 5 \+ worked at Velox printing
till dinner at 6.30 — at 7.30 resumed
printing — after 9 went to Charl's to see how
he got along down town, this being his first
day at the office in nearly three weeks.
Home at 10.30.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 20, 1904

*Jim came home*

**Weather:** Weather badly mixed — a little pale sun-  **Temp:** [illegible]

shine in the m'f — some light, drizzly
snow in the afternoon — clear moonlight
at night — To studio after 10 — worked
for a couple of hours on Borden photo-grav'
\+ did all that I can before I get a proof —
Home at 1 — Jim had arr'd from New-Haven
for his Xmas holidays — At 2.30 I was at
Edw'. Cook's office, 150 Nassau, \+ was engaged
till 4 o'clk. auditing Ireland Real Estate Co's
accts. for '03 \+ '04 — Then I went to W. C. Cullen's
in W'm St. — got some supplies \+ arranged to
return the Velox printing machine, the barrel
of which leaks — went into Charl's office \+ saw
him for a moment — home at about 5.30 — dinner
at 6.30 — Wrote letter to Kate \+ at 10 o'clk.
took it to P.O. Substation H \+ mailed it.
