## Left Page

### Sat. June 6, 1904

Stupid, careless blunder —
Sat I'm getting old & dotty.

**Weather:** Wea.  **Temp:** Ther.

Pleasant day — had my bkfs. in bed —
then up & podged about — not without
unpleasant reminders of the troubles of
the past few days — lunch at 1 & at
2 I was at H.D. Allen's — He spent some
time fitting the new lower set into my
mouth — Receiving some tender shoots
but not all of them — It was after 3
when I got home — had alone rest
on my bed — a light meal at 6.30 —
Evett Charl came in & time passed
with him & Uncle Willie —

This is Saturday —
June 4 th.

## Right Page

### Tues. June 7, 1904

Jim & Ralph Ret. to their schools.

**Weather:** Wea.  **Temp:** Ther.

Thunderstorms & Rain during last night
A very disagreeable day — overcast, muggy
& sticky with a strong breeze blowing making
the sticky part chilly — Up at 6 o'clk — Ralph
got a very light bkfs. & Jim & I went with him
to G.C. station & he Ret. to Hackley on the 7.30
Train — Then Jim & I Ret. to the house & had
bkft. After that I went to Bormann's & got
some developing powders — Then to grocer & butcher
& to studio — Spent rest of on't cleaning up
dark room at studio — at 12 o'clk. went to 2d.
Nat. Bank — home before 1 — Anne, The fine
Waitress, Cook etc. came — After lunch I
bid Jim good bye — he to Return to New Haven
at 4 P.m — I to go down town — Went to Cullen's
with my Camera for a little fixing — Then to Charl's
he gave me card to W. Sanford — Cunard Line —
went There — he went with me to N. German Lloyd
\+ I put my name down for State room No. 266 on
upper deck. Princess Irene — from Genoa — Sept. 22d. —
Then to Clarence Smith's 21 State at 5 — Geo. Cook
came soon after — we waited till near 6 for him —
Then finished up my mortgage to Jim in 110 2.38
for ff 2000 — Rode up town with Geo. & got home at
6.40 & had dinner at 7 — . Evett wrote to Jim &
cut to him Receipts to sign — at 9.30 went to
Charl's — met Mr. Delenoy There — home at 10.30
