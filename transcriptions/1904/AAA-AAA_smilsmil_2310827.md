## Left Page

### Tues. June 28, 1904

**Weather:** Cloudy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cloudy \+ quite cool with two or three light showers. Up early \+ got some work done before bkft. \- after bkft. went to Charl's \+ then up to Yale's \- Then home \+ to butchers \+ grocers \- then to Buskirk's \- Alexander's, for shoes \+ to 2° Nat°. \+ deposited the $300 check that I rec. this m't from Miss Stokes \- I did some preparatory work at home \- selecting \+ assorting \- lunch at 1 \- after that went again to Yales \- then home \+ a little later went to Habakus \+ aboard the Patricia to see First Steward, but failed \- did what I could to reserve seats at table \- back to N.H. \+ left my umbrella at a shop in 6° Ave. to be repaired \- Then home \- Dinner at 6 30 \- Even'g. in desultory work.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 29, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy showers soon after 5 o'clk \- but clearing after 10 to a warm but pleasant day \- Up at 6 \+ soon "got busy" \- bkft. at 8\. After that I went to Scheiff \- The trunk man in 42° St., looked at valises \+ ordered two sent to the house to decide which size w°. be preferable \- \+ to White \+ Van Glahn's \+ got supplies to mend book on St. Frank \- Spent some time on that work when I got home \- went to studio, grocer \+ butcher. Did some tentative packing to find out how much room w°. be required for the belongings we are to take with us \- Ralph \+ I then went to Scheiff's \+ looked for a valise suitable for his requirements \- lunch at 1 \-. At 3 I went to labaratory in 42° St. with specimen of my urine for analyses. Then to Eimer \+ Amend's \- 18° S. \+ 3°. Ave \+ got quinine \- Then to sub-station F. \+ left instructions as to mail \- Then home \+ spent the rest of afternoon in assorting, selecting \+ arranging until dinner at 6.30 \- Even'g. completed copy of inventory of the Ark \+ annex for Miss Stokes \- George came in for awhile in the even'g.
