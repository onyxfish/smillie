## Left Page

### Thur. June 16, 1904

**Weather:** Hazy, muggy \+ warm m't.  **Temp:**

afternoon overcast \- thunderstorms near \- sprinkle of rain only \- I got up with headache which bothered me all day \- Vondrous came to house soon after 9 \- I went to studio with him \+ started him at printing some of his etched plates \- Then I went to 2d. Nat'l \+ deposited Scribner check \- then to Scribners \+ saw W. H\[illegible\]. Smith \- then back to studio \+ gave V \- some helpful instruction. Had a talk with Yogbaum about Maynard telling him that I could not carry him any longer \+ that I wished his friends w'd find some way of relieving me \- Home to lunch at 1 \- after that I made up for loss of sleep since 4 o'clk. This m't. by an hours nap \- Then I went out shopping for some kind of valise \- went to Saks \- \+ to cor. of 23d. St. \+ 6th Ave. \- to Alexanders where I ordered new pair of shoes then walked up to studio \+ set down paper for Vondrous to print on to-morrow \- Home at 6 \- dinner at 6.30 \- eve'g. Reading \-

## Right Page

### Fri. June 17, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:**

[illegible — ~3 words] left \-

Very heavy rain in the early m't. \- The day was pleasant \- I got to studio at about 9.30 \- Vondrous came then \+ spent the m't printing \- I left him \+ went down town to Hamburg. Am. line office \+ paid $252\. for return tickets from Genoa. Sep. 22. Then took receipt to No. German Lloyd office \+ got ticket for Stateroom 266 on SS. Princess Irene \- then went to Hamilton's 50 Broadway \+ ordered a Bellcrostop valise. Then to Charles' \+ to studio at 12 2o. Roth there \- He took away with him some prints that he had taken \+ plate paper \- Home to lunch soon after 1 \- to studio at 3 \+ until 5. I was arranging things to leave during the summer \- Then home \+ wrote a short letter to Mary \- Jane Hinshelwood left after lunch to go to Newark to care for Jas. Abercrombie \- whose wife, Sadie, sails with her children, for England to-morrow \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g. Geo., Charl \+ Sheldon came in \+ spent the eve'g.
