## Left Page

### Sat. Jan. 2, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dark \- Snow storm all day with cold, driving wind \- Got up with headache \- I could not work \- after bkft. I went to Butchers \+ grocers, as usual \- Then to the bank \- \+ then home at about 11 \- wrote a letter to Fredk. Morton of Brush \+ Pencil, Chicago \- Then to Borrmann's with Ralph \+ bought some photo. materials \- then home to lunch \- After that I had a nap in my rooms then Ralph \+ I went to studio \+ worked in dark Room till near 6 o'clk trying to get some good relox prints \- unsuccessful \- then home to dinner. Sue Whiting was to dine with us but the weather prevented \- Evef by a pleasant fire in parlor \- the boys \+ Jane \+ Mary Hinshelwood amusing themselves by playing Halma.

## Right Page

### Sun. Jan. 3, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant \+ very cold day \- up at usual time \- had my usual scout \+ bkfst \- at 9.30 \- went to studio before 11 \+ got photo. paper, toner etc \- then home, \+ with the boys spent rest of mf. printing \+ toning photos. At 1.30 went to Charl's \+ got correct time \- Dinner at home at 2 \- from 3 to 4 I had a nap \- Evef wrote to S'rs. Roger Adams, Katie Elkin \+ Emma D. \- Soon after 10 o'clk went out \+ mailed letters \- then a short walk in the cold, brilliant moonlight.
