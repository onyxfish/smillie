## Left Page

### Wed. April 27, 1904

**Weather:** Wind + Rain all day  **Temp:** [illegible]

after blep- wrote to Miss Mitchell in reply to letter rec'd. yesterday. It was 11 o'clk. when I got to studio - telephoned to 2. Convent Hill where Mr. Chadwick is visiting her cousin + had a few moments conversation with her + her daughter Nathalie. Painted on the Source of the River. Glazing + Painting - Home to lunch. afternoon worked till after 4. the results being satisfactory. then wet down the print that I am to mount for Chas. Collins + home about 5 - Rested - dinner - Welf to Century. Com. on Admissions. Recess at 10.30 when I was excused + went home.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 28, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A nasty day, dark + wet with occasional heavy rains - To studio as usual - most of the m'f. mounting the Muerillo print for Chas. Collins - home to lunch - after that it was so dark + raining so heavily that I gave way to my feelings + indulged in an hours sleep - to studio at 3.30 - I got out big frame for "Source of the River" + got painting into it + then studied some changes that I have in mind but I did no work on it - Home after 6 - dinner - Welf went to call on Mr. + Mrs. Chadwick + Nathalie, their daughter, at Mr. Anderson's - 2 Convent Hill - which is 130th St. somewhere between 8th + Amsterdam Aves. I went up on 6th Ave. Elevated - walked from 125th to 130th St. - walked west, climbed the hill + eventually found the house - had a pleasant call - left at 10 - home by Amsterdam Ave. cars at 11.
