## Left Page

### Sun. Feb. 7, 1904

**Weather:** Stormy - wind & rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

Stormy - wind & rain. In bed all day with headache - from 11 o'clk A.M. to 5 P.M. Took 12 'l0 gr. Tabloids of Calomel. I got up soon after 5 p.m. & dressed - wrote a note to Emma J - & sent it, with her two Parsifal books, by Dist. Mess. to her. Had a bowl of gruel at 6 - Read news paper & to bed at 10.

## Right Page

### Mon. Feb. 8, 1904

**Weather:** Bright, cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold day - after bkft. went to Dr. Yale's & had a conference with him - it was about the usual time when I got to the Studio - 10 o'clk - I spent the m'f in getting press ready to print o'ne laying aqua-tint ground over a plate of Bronson's & biting it - Home to lunch at 1.30 - after that had a rest & got to studio soon after 3 o'clk - Took several proofs from the two plates - the Vandrous plate comes out very well - the other plate will have to have more work on it - but altogether I think the result satisfactory - washed up - had a short walk & home at 6.15 — dinner — Evett. Chase came in — I rec'd. a letter from Mary to-day.
