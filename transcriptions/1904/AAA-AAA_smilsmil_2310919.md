## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 31, 1904

**Weather:** mild, pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mild, pleasant day — Ralph \+ I went to the studio \+ spent the m[orning] there developing a couple of rolls of his films exposed while abroad last summer — Home to lunch — After that my headache had increased so much that I went to bed for about three hours — Then up feeling little better — went down town to Culleci's for the eight rolls of films that he had promised to have developed for me \+ found that he had just sent them to me at the house — so I retraced my steps \+ returned to my bed — got up at midnight \+ while the noises of wild steam-whistles of all calibres \+ all over the city, was raging in celebration of the incoming year I read a letter just rec'd. from Kate with New Year greeting.
