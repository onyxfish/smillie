## Left Page

### Thur. July 28, 1904

Dresden \- Hotel du Nord \-
Rooms 27 & 28.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A murky sort of morning \- I got up early \+ packed my trunk \- Then went down \+ had my bkfst. alone before 8 \- waked the boys at 9 \+ then completed the packing of second trunk. Bought my R.R. tickets for Dresden \- 37 m. for 3. \- bureau in hotel \- Trunks sent for before 11, checked \+ receipt given to me, 2 M. \- Then the boys \+ I went out \- first to Deutsche Bank \+ gave Dresden address \- Then R\- got prints from some of his negatives \- \+ back to Hotel \- growing very dark \- after 12. a heavy downpour of rain that continued till after we got to Anhalt Station at 1.10 clk \- It was nasty but we got thro without wetting \- There being no dining car on train we got at the R.R. Restaurant, coffee, beer, buttered rolls \+ pears. Enough to stay us \- The train started on time 1.30 \- we had a comfortable car \- one other, a Philadelphian, in it \- very flat country \- much again get uncut \- very few dwellings to be seen \- Showers \+ bright intervals \- Philadelphian pleasantly chatty. We got to Dresden on time 4.14 \- fast train \- 112 miles in 3 hours \- much swinging \+ jolting \- small engine \- small cars \- light rails \- will hardly bear comparison with a first class R.R. in the U.S. \- At the station Kate met us \+ took charge of us \- we walked to hotel \- a short distance \- our Gepäck following \- at hotel we got two rooms, front, third floor \- large room with double bed for boys. Smaller room, connected, for self \- everything about the hotel looked dingy compared with Kaiser hof \- After a little we started out \+ were met at the door by Mary \+ Katharine \+ by them were escorted to their quarters \- Racknitzer Str. 14 a couple of blocks from our hotel \- general confab till 6 \- back to hotel \+ had dinner \- we \+ again to Mary's \- there till 10 \- then to hotel \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Fri. July 29, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

I was obliged to give myself a dose of sedelitz powder this m'f. from which I had full result \- my bowels have been giving me trouble ever since I left home \- it seems to be increasing rather than diminishing \- Ralph is in like trouble \+ we cannot get the diet we require \- I had to remain at the hotel during the m'f. while the boys went off with the T\-girls \- They ret for dinner at 1 \- off again in the afternoon \- at about 3.30 I went to Räcknitzer Str. \+ with Mary \+ Kate went to the Grosser Garten where we prowled about walking \+ resting as I was not in fit condition for much exercise \- we found much to enjoy in a quiet way \- I took tea with the girls \+ was introduced to some graham bread \- The boys had their supper elsewhere.
