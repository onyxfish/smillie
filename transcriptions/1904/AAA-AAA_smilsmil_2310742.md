## Left Page

### Sun. Jan. 10, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine winter day — [Okfo?] at 10 — went to Century to take a look at my work there by day light at about 11. Then walked up town to Central Park \+ back \+ around thro' 42d S. \+ looked at the new Theatres there — then back to Century — Saw some bro. artists who said pleasant things — walked home with Jane \+ Mary Hushielwood, stopped at Charls — got correct time — home to dinner at 2. Had an afternoon nap \+ after 5 walked down to Emma's, E. 17 B. Read to her the Williams correspondence — Stayed to tea but Ret. home soon after. Joe \+ wrote to Ralph a letter of advice — Charl came in.

## Right Page

### Mon. Jan. 11, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

I got up with a dismal headache but was able to get to studio after writing a note to E.N.J. At the studio I washed brushes \+ mounted photos — home to lunch \+ then to bed where I remained till 2 a.m, then had some beef tea \+ went to bed prosperously for the rest of the night.
