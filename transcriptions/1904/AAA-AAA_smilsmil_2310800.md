## Left Page

### Thur. May 5, 1904

**Weather:** Very warm + cloudless  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm + cloudless - To studio at usual time + worked on Borden drawing till 12 - Mr. Dexter Thurber came in + got Mr. T's palette + painting apron. At noon went to Mr. Clausen's - saw him he has sold my "Adirondack Lake" - he receipted his bill for frames ($76°°) + promises me Cr. for a new frame - we exchanged checks - I then went home + wrote till lunch at 1 - after that went to Hamburg - Am. line + p°. $50 on acc't. of 3 passages on Patricia - State-Room 45 A - July 2° - $32.5° each - bal. to be p°. June 11 th. Then to studio at 3 + worked on drawing till 5 - left studio a little before 6 + took a short stroll - home soon after 6 - dinner at 6.30 - Eve'g. - bank acc't + other writing.

Wrote to Jim this m'g. asking him to come home on Sunday.

## Right Page

### Fri. May 6, 1904

Jim came from New-Haven

**Weather:** Bright day - chilly east wind later  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright day - chilly east wind later - Up at 6 - wrote nletter to Kate from 7 to 8 - off. To studio at 9.30 - wrote letter to Mary. At 12 went to 2°. Nat'l. bank - Home to lunch at 1. To studio, wrote to Tracy - Moody - Silas Cross - Emma Otis + O. Kellogg - all about "trip to Keene, May 16" — Then to N.A. of D. with p'fs. of Competition plates - put them up in Etching Room - It was 5.45 when I got home - surprised to find Jim there - did not expect to see him till to-morrow. Rested till dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g wrote to Ralph + got to bed early.
