## Left Page

### Wed. Aug. 3, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very hot m'f. up late + bkf'. at 9 - at 10 went to Mary's - Kath'. had not yet had her bkf'! - So, with the boys, we went to the Albertinium + spent the rest of the m'f there - the staircase with wall paintings (+ ceiling) by H. Prell, I thought was impressive + unusually harmonious in design. we did little more than walk thro' the many galleries noting now + then something of special interest. I was pleased to see a cast of the Pilgrim Farther by St. Gaudens - also the Angel at the Tomb + a large photo. of the Shaw memorial. On our way to the Albertinium we stopped at the Dresdner + Ilale Deutschen Bank + got 510 m. for $25. we rode hotel by car + got in at 12.45 dinner - afternoon the boys went out with me + I tried to get a number of snap shots in the streets - clouds interfered at times + most of the exposures were made rather late - so I fear failures - we went into Academy building + saw an exhibit of architectural designs - We got to Mary's late + concluded that we would not go out for the ev'g. so the boys + I went to the hotel + had beer + bread for our ev'g. meal. after attending with aunt Mary + Kath'. - to get day broken sketching table mended - buying fruit and norwegian rough bread - After our meals at hotel went back to Mary's + spent ev'g there -

Changed to bedroom No. 32 - at back of house - same floor as other - to get quiet at night.

## Right Page

### Thur. Aug. 4, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another hopelessly dry day - very hot + cloudless - I passed an almost sleepless night + was horribly uncomfortable with fear of insomnia - . I dozed after 2 o'clk - + fell asleep when daylight began to appear + dreamed till 8 o'clk. Then up - bkf'. after 9 + at about 10 went with the boys to Mary's - after arranging to return about 4. The boys + I went to the Mus. of Paintings + I took them thro' it generally, pointing out to them some of the pictures that I thought most interesting - it was after 1 when we got thro' - then wandered along River Terrace + the Terrace Restaurant where we had a satisfactory dinner while sitting under the trees. it was delightful - we got thro' dinner at 3 + ret. to hotel - Rested for an hour - then at 4 went to Mary's - Bessie went with us as guide + we drove in a hack all over, both sides of the River - outskirts + inskirts - till 6.30 seeing a great deal that was very interesting + much that was sad in the withered trees + other evidences of drought. at Mary's we quickly aroused the Kath'. Bessie + the boys w? go to Terrace Concert + I w? take tea with Mary + Kate - + this I did - + spent the ev'g. in planning the rest of our trip thro' to Genoa, by Sep'. 22 - back to hotel at 10.
