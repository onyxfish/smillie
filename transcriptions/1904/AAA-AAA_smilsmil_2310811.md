## Left Page

### Fri. May 27, 1904

**Weather:** Very close, muggy, hot + depressing  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very close, muggy, hot + depressing — cloudy till about 4 p.m. — when a sudden change came — sky clearing + fresh west wind. A severe headache commenced at about 4 A.m. + I was useless for work till the weather cleared — then head cleared too. I got to studio at 9.30 + busied myself clearing up till about 11 when Roth came to put in another day at printing. Then I walked down to Safe Deposit Co. got deed + other documents relating to 110 E. 37th St., then back home + had lunch — after that went downtown to Charl's office with him + went to Cullen's taking my 4×5 Dallmeyer lens — selected the latest pattern of camera + arranged to have my lens put in it — then I left Charl + went to Clarence Smith — 21 State St. + left with him my title deed etc — with instructions to make a mortgage on the house for $2000 at 5% in favor of Jim to meet the legacy Requirements in his Mother's Will — Then out on the Battery — bright breezy + cool — Spent half an hour in aquarium — men by elevated R.R. went up to the Ansonia, 74th St. + kept an appointment with Allen — the dentist — home at 6 — dinner — Eve. Charl came in for a few minutes — I wrote to Jim + Ralph.

## Right Page

### Sat. May 28, 1904

**Weather:** A delightful day — clear + cool  **Temp:** [illegible]

A delightful day — clear + cool — to studio at about 10.30 — + at about 11 went to Wanamaker's + made some purchases for the Ark as per list sent me by Miss Mitchell — crockery + metal ware — walked up Third Ave. + found a place where an open Franklin stove was for sale — about $30 — complete — I shall not get one for the Misses Stokes if I can help it — then to studio + did a little writing before going home to lunch — after lunch I gave way to my desire for sleep + had an hour + a half solid — to studio at 4 + worked an hour or more on Vesuvius — scraping + sandpapering \+ got it ready to begin painting on Monday. — Then dampened + put in press a number of Roth's p.p. + before 6 went out to 5th Ave. + promenaded for awhile — home soon after 6 — dinner — Eve. at 8 went to Charl's + he laid out for me in detail — a route from Hamburg to Genoa — making dates from July 15 to Sep. 15th + showing illustrative photos. Home at 10.30 —
