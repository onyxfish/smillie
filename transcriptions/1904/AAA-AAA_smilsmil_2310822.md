## Left Page

### Sat. June 18, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph came home from Hackley.

A fine day - warm but breezy - went to Allen's - Ansonia - \+ he fussed for a while with my months - then home - then chores - \+ to studio \+ there till near lunch time selecting material for my summer trip - Had a talk with Ryan about improvements in his quarters. Home to lunch \+ after that took camera \+ went to 57 St. \+ Fifth Ave. \+ took a couple of views there, then into the Park \+ used up my spool - then to studio \+ developed them - I was careless in several small particulars \+ was not particularly successful - We waited dinner till near 7 o'clk - when Ralph came in from Tarrytown - he tells me that he has not been well for a couple of days, headache \+ some fever - the doctor says he has studied too hard - he says he is all right now - Ewell we went to studio for a little while - very warm night - all to bed early.

## Right Page

### Sun. June 19, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very bright \+ pleasant warm day - a sprightly air was moving to temper the heat - Up at 7 \+ had my scrub, \+ shampoo \+ bath \+ breakfast at 9 - after that I did some printing from the negatives developed yesterday p.m. - \+ at 11.30 Ralph \+ I started for down-town to photo- some of the cañons of N.Y. City - got off \+ Hanover Sq. - \+ went to Beaver St. \+ got a shot up Broad - then to Bdeway - then to Exchange Place - then to Nassau St. - \+ there my spool of 6 Exposures was all out - Ralph took two or three shots - then we walked to the Fulton St. Station - took train - then to studio \+ home at 2 o'clk - dinner - after that a nap - at 4 went to studio \+ developed the Exposures of the mg. \+ I think got a half dozen interesting \+ successful photos. Home at 6 - Geo. \+ Val there. I had some tea at 7 - \+ until after 9 o'clk. Ralph \+ I "been used" together.
