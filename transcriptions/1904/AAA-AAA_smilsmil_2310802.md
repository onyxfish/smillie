## Left Page

### Mon. May 9, 1904

**Weather:** Rain during the night \- light rain early m'f, clearing bright \+ pleasant after 10  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- I got to studio at usual time \+ put in am'f of solid work on Borden drawing. At 12:30 went to bank then home to lunch \- to studio at 2:15 \- Zogbaum soon came to say that he had arranged with Maynard to occupy his room for awhile to repaint an old picture which w'd. arrive in a few minutes \- When it came it proved so large that it could not be brought up the stairway. With much labor it was hauled up thro' my big window \+ then carried up to Maynard's room. All of this took quite a slice out of my afternoon. I worked for an hour \+ a half on Borden. Roth came in with prints to send to Brush \+ Pencil \- Home at about 6 \- dinner \- Eve'f, stupid with a headache \- unable to do anything.
Some light showers in the eve'f.

## Right Page

### Tues. May 10, 1904

**Weather:** Bright sunny m'f, shower before noon, other showers later \- making a wet afternoon.  **Temp:** [illegible]

Got a letter from Charl at St Louis enclosing one he had rec'd. from Bessie \- To studio early \- and again, all day, was bothered by headache. I worked for awhile on a design to be burned on panel on bedroom mantelpiece, Keene \- Then on Borden \- to lunch at 1 \- after that, \+ while a heavy shower was passing, wrote a short letter to Bessie \+ mailed it with one sent to her from Po'k. To studio at 3 \+ worked on Borden till 5 \- Home in a shower \- dinner. Eve'f, lolling about, useless.
