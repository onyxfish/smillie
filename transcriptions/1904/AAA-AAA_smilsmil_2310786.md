## Left Page

### Thur. April 7, 1904

**Weather:** Rain last night - wet m'g - clearing early into a warm, misty, sunny spring day  **Temp:** [illegible]

After blkfs'. went about my chores - got a note at Studio from Claussen - a nibble for one of my paintings - went to C's - not in - Then home \+ worked on Borden till near 12 - Then to studio - saw Mr. Thurber - also Ge° Nellie Ge° is yet very unwell altho' he gets about - Home to lunch at 1 - Immediately after it went to Mr. Borden's in 56th S'. met Walter Cook the Told me of Edw'd's great good fortune in getting a very flattering hospital position after exam 70 candidates, 6 places - Edw'd got No 2 - I saw Mr. Borden - he said he thought I was dead - find that he has changed furniture in Room I am making drawing of - for worse I am to go there to-morrow to make some sketches - from there I went to Socy of Am. Artists - 57' S - spent two hours there - more than ever puzzled in trying to get some idea of the standard by which pictures are selected for such an exhn - a lot of the portraits are simply atrocious - some execrable landscapes are hung upon the line - Home at 4.30, worked for an hour on Borden - Then a rest - Dinner at 6.30 - Even'g I gave the boys a cheap treat - tickets to Murray Hill Theatre - I wrote part of letter to sister Mary.

## Right Page

### Fri. April 8, 1904

**Weather:** Bright m'g, with a chill in the air presaging the change that came in the afternoon \+ the rain that fell in the even'g.  **Temp:** [illegible]

After blkfs'. I went to studio - grocer \+ butcher, Then walked up to Mr. Borden's, 56th S'. bet. 5th \+ 6th Aves. I was there till 11.30 making sketches of library table, chairs etc - that have been changed since photo. was taken - from B's I walked down 5th Ave. To bank - 23d S' - from there I rode to Studio \+ saw Ge°. I saw Mr. Thurber in the m'g, after 9 - Then home to lunch. Afternoon I worked for a couple of hours on Borden outline - added a few lines to letter to Mary \+ posted it. Dinner at 6.30 after which the boys hurried off to Barnum \+ Bailey's Circus at Mad. Sq. garden - Charl came in \+ spent the even'g with me.
