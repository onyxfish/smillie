## Left Page

### Sat. Dec. 3, 1904

**Weather:** Dull, overcast & raw cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, overcast & raw cold — To studio at usual time & gave the day entirely to developing photos. snap shots taken last summer — much of the mt f. I was preparing solutions of different kinds & filtering same but developed one roll before I went home to lunch — Afternoon I developed another roll & some time was absent in the washing & drying of the negatives. It was after 6 when I got home to dinner — Well. I went to Century, monthly meeting — my River's Source looks much worse than I expected — it is minced to death & has no color quality — it depressed me very much — I got home about 11.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 4, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another dull day — but the night was clear & star lit. Slept at 9 after a good scrub — About 10.30 I went to studio & worked in my dark-room till dinner time at 2 o'clk — This work has taken hold of me now & I am under a spell. There is a fascination in experimenting with different developers & so far I do not think that I have spoiled any more films than Cullen & Borman did in their developing for me. After dinner I went again to studio & hung up the newly developed films to dry — then to Chad's for a few minutes — then home & rested — at 7.30 I went to Huene J.'s & was there till after 10.
