## Right Page

### Mon. Nov. 28, 1904

**Weather:** clear + cold  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clean + cold - to studio at usual time. went to Kimmel + Voigt's by appointment at 11 o'clc. I found Nickleby there with two on three plates + H.f V - at work for him. We made an amicable arrangement by which I was able at once to get two pf.p. from the two Borden photos - gravure plates. Back to studio at 12.15 - home to lunch at 1 - back at 2 + painted on 12"×16"." Evening in the woods" Till 3.30 getting it in shape to send to Century next Friday - Then I went down town + saw W. C. Cullen + conferred with him concerning photo-work + my film exposures made last summer. he advises a developing machine. Then I went to Charl's office + returned up town with him - dinner at 6.30 - stupid eve'g alongside of a pleasant fire -
