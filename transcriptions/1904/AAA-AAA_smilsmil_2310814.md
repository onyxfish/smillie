## Left Page

### Thur. June 2, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still disagreeable weather - overcast some rain + a good deal of mizzle - I had several disturbances last night - none between 1 AM + 8 this m'f which encouraged me to hope that the end of this trouble had come - but later in the day I slipped back to a condition little if any better than yesterday - I kept to my bed
Jane + Mary had their hands full in running the house without servants.
My new camera came home from Cullen's in the afternoon - Charl came in the ev'f.

## Right Page

### Fri. June 3, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Warm + pleasant - part of the time bright + sunny - I kept my bed all day - last night I was up at 1.30 - 3.15 - + 5.30 - after that I had no further movements but had several seasons of discomfort. Dr. Yale came after 10 o'clk + spent a good part of the remainder of the m'f with me - He gave me some suggestions as to drugs but I do not think that it will be necessary to consider them - The H's have no help yet + I cannot make that they are trying for help with any degree of energy or intelligence - my light meals do not require much time or skill to prepare - I do not know what they provide for Uncle W. + Aunt A - I wrote to Ralph + some other notes - Ev'f Charl came in - from below I heard the voices of Nellie + Sheldon Smillie + Ch. Durang as well as Uncle W. Aunt A. + the H's —
