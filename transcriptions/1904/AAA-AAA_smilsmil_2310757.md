## Left Page

### Tues. Feb. 9, 1904

**Weather:** Bright, cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, cold day — To studio at usual time \+ spent the m'f in further experimental work with aqua-tint on Aronson's plate — To lunch as usual — Afternoon took some proofs — not quite what I hoped — Vondrous came in \+ I gave him his plate \+ au ink. Home at 5 — had a rest — At 7 went to Lotos Club on invitation of Halsey Ives, \+ was surprised to find that it was a meeting of the full Advisory Com. S. Louis Ex'p. some 50 or 60 strong — \+ in full dress as I was in frock coat I was somewhat uneasy — Wolf, the wood engr. was the only other one so attired — we sat down to dinner at about 8 o'clk — good \+ I made some Champagne — after it a long discussion as to present condition of work in hand \+ Mr. Ives mapped out the work that the several sub-coms. were expected to do — the discussion was long \+ not always interesting — it was after 11 when the meeting broke up — The etching contingent, except Nicol, got together, Schilling, Mielatz, Chapman \+ Self — all absent that Com. are prepared to run the business — agreed to a meeting in C's studio next Tues. at 4 P.M. — I got to bed soon after midnight

## Right Page

### Wed. Feb. 10, 1904

**Weather:** Bright cold winter day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright cold winter day — To studio at about 10 \+ spent m'f finishing the aqua-tint experiments on Aronson's plate \+ taking a couple of fine p'fs. from it — Home to lunch at 1 — \+ at 2 went to Etching class — was there but a short time — learned that some one, probably a member of the class, had, in spite of strict orders agt. it, got the printing blankets \+ abused them so that the largest set were cut into strips \+ a smaller set were cut almost as bad. I was so angry that I did not dare trust myself \+ left at once without saying anything — I don't know what the upshot of it may prove to be — Home, before 5 had a good nap before dinner at 6.30 — Read with newspapers till Charl came in bringing with him an excellent enlargement (photo.) from the small negative that Ralph made of me last fall — in mountaineer costume — conical cap —
