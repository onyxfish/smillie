## Left Page

### Tues. Jan. 12, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day - spent most of it recovering from yesterdays debauch. After bkfs. - I wrote again to Ralph + Emma - \+ to Sis - Then I went to G. Cent. Sta". \+ telegraphed to Bessie (Park) Ballantine to come to studio as proposed To-morrow P.M. Then to Hugh Douglas's \+ saw him - Then to Millers \+ to studio - Trouble with heater - Jennings let fire out to have grate bars fixed - Home to lunch - Rested Till 3.30 - then to studio \+ cleared up - Then to Clausens \+ asked him to send back to me "Adirondack Lake" - Then home Dinner - at 8 went to Charl's \+ with him went to Annual Meeting, Zoological Soct after which [illegible] gave a lecture with illustrations on the results to date of Scientific Deep sea soundings. It was an uninteresting presentation of an exceedingly interesting subject. Home soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Wed. Jan. 13, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dark, stormy day - driving wind \+ rain - After bkfs. I went to Charl's with letter just rec'd from Bessie - then to studio - by stripping every thing away from the big windows I got an imperfect light that I managed to work by - I painted freely over Right hand lower corner of "Source" making very considerable changes in tone \+ color - Home to lunch - back soon after 2 \+ painted till 4.15 Then cleaned up \+ rested in big chair Till after 5 - then home - dinner - Eve'g wrote quite a letter to Bessie - to join letter written by Jane \+ —.
