## Left Page

### Sun. May 1, 1904

*Irinie's birth-day -*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A lovely spring mt. weather changed in the afternoon - cloudy + at night somewhat threatening. Up at 7 + in addition to my bath + some I had a shampoo + was ready for my chb: at 9 - I went to studio about 11 + opened up for ventilation then took car down the Bowery + Delaney S'. to the new W m. burg bridge + walked over that - a gigantic structure - but I was surprised at the vibration it parts - the views over River + City are wonderful - Home in time for dinner at 2 - between 3 + 5 I had a good nap - then wrote to Jim - to Roth + to Vondrous - + sent check to Mr. Tilton - Treas'. of Diet Kitchen - no supper + before 8 o'clk. went to Mr. Dodd's (Mattie Parker) 308 W. 73'. S'. + saw her, Katie Parker Riley + her daughter Kitty - I had a pleasant evef - left before 10 - walked to 59th S'. circle + then car home.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 2, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A pleasant day - to studio before 10 - Mrs. Thurber came in - just from train from Chicago - wishing to know if she can have the use of my studio for three or four days - I gave her a note of introduction to Frank Millet telling her that in the old Kurtz galleries now occupied by Millet he might be able to let her have some space - + so it proved. I painted on "The Source" making a radical change in a prominent tree form on the lower l. h. side - urged to do so by Geo. It got me into trouble that I was not able to get out of. Home to lunch at 1 - immediately after, I went to the Hamburg-Am. S. S. Co's office, 35-7 Broadway, to make enquiries about state room on Patricia - a crowd was in the office - but two state rooms of the class I was looking for, were not marked "Taken." They were in the hands of an agent he could not communicate with - he is to send me word later. Back to studio at 3.30 + painted till 5 - washed up - home - dinner - Evef writing notes - paying bills + studying steamer plans + figures.
