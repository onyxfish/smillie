## Left Page

### Wed. April 13, 1904

*Jim ret. to New Haven.*

**Weather:** Clear but cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clear but cold \+ windy \- After bkfs. went to grocers, butchers \+ studio \+ home to my work on Borden soon after 10 \- worked till 1 \- then lunch \- to N.A.D. before 3 \+ met portion of class \+ criticised their competition plates \- then home at about 4.30 \- worked on Borden till 6 \- then Jennings took my work-table back to studio \- I had a short rest before dinner \- after dinner went with Jim to R.R. Station \+ he took 8 o'clk. train for New-Haven \- then home \+ took to studio my drawing board, photo \- etc to studio to resume my work there in the m.f. Met Geo. Val. \+ Gordon there he is changing his boarding place from Park Ave to E. 34th St. then home \- Charl came in \- Rec'd a letter from Miss V. mitchell stating that the misses Stokes wd. Rent the Ark if I wd. give possession from May 20 to Oct. 15th \- \+ Put in a wood burning Frankline Stove in studio instead of the coal burner there now \+ put a Fraucklin in one of the lower bed Rooms. She asked me to Telegraph Reply so I wired from G.C. Station that I would give possession as desired \+ would put Franklin in studio but could not put one in lower bed Room —

## Right Page

### Thur. April 14, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow storm in the m.f. clearing bright, cold \+ windy before noon \- After bkfs. I spent some time writing a letter to Miss mitchell concerning the concessions asked for by the Misses Stokes \- It was 11 o'clk. before I got through with my do-mestic chores \+ to studio where I resumed work \- on Borden outline \- home to lunch at 1 \- back soon after 2 \- worked till 5 \- home at 6 \+ had a short rest before dinner after dinner a half hour with newspaper, then, with Jane \+ Mary H\-, to Charl's where for nearly two hours he showed us a lot of newly prepared lantern slides, some of them remarkably fine \- Home at 10.30 \+ to bed.
