## Left Page

### Sat. Aug. 13, 1904

**Weather:** Charming day — clear & cool  **Temp:**

I was up at 5, but did not get up for all day till 7. I finished a letter to Charl, commenced some days ago at Dresden & Rec'd. a note from Emma J. Before 9 went with the boys to Kursalon in Stadt Park & had our bkf't. then after that we wandered thro' streets to S. Stephans church & spent some time in the interior which to me is very impressive — then we climbed to the top of the tower & spent so much time there that it was noon when we came down — Then to Tho's. Cook & Co — I drew £25 = 507k.50h. then to the Restaurant in the Volks garten — had a good lunch — after that we went to the Reichsraths Ges. & thro' that with an attendant — a fine bldg. but not equal to the Berlin one — Then to the Rathaus & thro' that — the finest Rathaus we have yet seen — after that to the Votive Kirche but found little to admire in that — from there walked down a back way to the K.K. palace, when at 1 o'clk we had listened to the band — went into the Court Ch. (Augustine) & got back to hotel after 5 — I rested for awhile & about 7 went with boys to Volksgarlen Restaurant (where we lunched) & had a light every meal — a fine concert under way — but the every was so chilly, with no protection overhead, that after sitting about an hour I got chilly & uncomfortable — tried sitting under cover, but then could not hear the music properly — Tried moving about but there was no chance for that, everybody, having secured tables, remained seated — the overseer, who had been very attentive & spoke English, noticed my wandering — I told him I was cold & did not dare sit still in the night air — to my surprise, soon after, he sent to me by one of the waiters, his overcoat, because of this kindness, I remained another half hour. at 9.35 we Ret'd. to the hotel.

## Right Page

### Sun. Aug. 11, 1904

*See May 22d*

**Weather:** Very warm & pleasant  **Temp:**

Up early — had scrub, bath & shampoo & bkf't. at 9 — To Hackley School on 10.45 Train getting there at about 12.30 — Ralph just out of bath after a long bicycle ride — was pleasantly rec'd. by Mr. & Mrs. Williams — had my usual seat at table on his right. The relations of Unitarianism Christianity & oriental Religious beliefs were discussed, I taking a weak part. After dinner we had coffee in the parlor — & then to the new Chapel which is now quite finished except the memorial window in the south end — Then, after climbing to the top of the tower, we went to R's Room & chatted till afternoon service — R. went to service while I had a restful nap on his bed — On his Return we walked down the hill — at 6.15 he Ret'd. to school & I went and took 6.47 Train getting home about 8. Charl was there & we had a talk about summer plans.

Owing to incomprehensible carelessness — this diary for Sun. May 22, was written upon this page — the diary for to-day will be found upon the page for May 22d.
