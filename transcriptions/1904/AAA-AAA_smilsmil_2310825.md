## Left Page

### Fri. June 24, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very beautiful day - pleasantly cool except at midday when walking in the sunshine \+ with the wind - Bkfp. at 7.30 Ralph again went to Columbia for his Exams. I got to studio before 10 \+ got to work on Vesuvius. I worked till 11.30 making some considerable changes, both in form \+ color, on foreground trees - Then a peculiar blindness came threatening me with headache I stopped work \+ took a dose of bi-carb. of soda in hot water \+ the blindness passed off in an hour or so. At 12 Jim came \+ I went with him to Savings Bank. Fourth Ave. \+ 15th St. to had his acct. there, which has been in my name as Trustee, transferred to him - then to 2d Nat. \+ drew some money - Then home - lunch - to studio at 3 in an hour decided that Vesuvius is finished - then Joe brought in Mr. \+ Mrs. Simmons Mr. S. met me many years ago at Montrose, when he was visiting the Jessups - Then I cleaned palette - washed brushes - said good bye to Vesuvius - home at 6 \+ wrote part of a letter to Mary before dinner - Chase came in with a photo enlargement for Geo. - Evef I finished letter to Mary \+ posted it.

## Right Page

### Sat. June 25, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An exceedingly \+ uncomfortably hot day after a night of headache I got up with it \+ was discomforted to a greater or less extent all day - Bkfp. at 8 \+ Ralph went up to Columbia for his last Exam. History. I wrote some notes \+ got to studio after 10 - until 12.30 I was taking down curtains \+ shaking, switching \+ folding them. Then to Miller's in 40th St. to arrange with him. To meet me \+ Ryan at Studio Bldg. on Monday with them home - lunch - then gave up to my headache till after 3 - then to studio \+ completed the curtain business - home at 6 - dinner - Evef at about 9 I went to Charl's - Val. there - home about 10 - Jim \+ Ralph had been off somewhere "on a bum".
