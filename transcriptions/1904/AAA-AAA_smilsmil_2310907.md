## Left Page

### Monday. Dec. 7, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow - coming down like an old fashioned storm - small flakes + persistent - Spent the day at studio in a general clearing up - since my paintings have gone to [illegible] + I have engaged in photo work I have left things lying about till there was confusion everywhere. Now I am preparing again to start on the Borden Photo-gravure - To lunch at home, as usual - Home at 5 p.m. - dinner - at 8.30 went to Chas. taking letter from Mary, rec'd. This m'f. + got from him one from Kate - Home at 9.30 + mended printing frames till after 11.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 8, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow in the m'f - dark + dismal - efforts at clearing later in the day - some sunshine. Again I yielded to the temptation of photo + spent the m'f - in fact, practically all day, developing more of the films exposed last summer - using the machine, apparently with success - Home to lunch + back as usual - Afternoon, worked for a little while on Borden Photo-gravure plate, but not long enough to count - At 6. I went to Chas's + saw him - he is at home used up by a cold - I fear he is very near a break-down - The worries + anxieties of his business are too much for him - Home to dinner - Evening fooling with Velox printing till after 11 with no success.
