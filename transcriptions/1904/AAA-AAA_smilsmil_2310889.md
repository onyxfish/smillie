## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 1, 1904

**Weather:** Pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant day — nearly 11 o'clks. before I got to work on Photo-gravure plate, continuing experiments — Home to lunch at 1 oclk. Jane H — came back to stay — her cousin Sadie Abercrombie having ret- from her visit abroad Jane is no longer required in the A — household. — to studio at 2 rgt experimental ph. gr. plate ready for a print. Then prepared to make a few sketch to-morrow of one of my lake Lugano subjects. Home at 6 — dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g worked on bills.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 2, 1904

**Weather:** Fine  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — After 10 when I got to studio \+ started pen study of a picturesque old church or monastery bldg near Lugano for which I got material last summer. Home to lunch at 1 — back at 2.30 \+ at 4.45 I had finished the fore work — now I will add some india ink washes and some Chinese white — At 5 went to Brooks Bros. \+ bought a Tuxedo Coat — Then to house furnishing store in 42d. St. to get spirit lamp — Home at 6 — dinner at 6.30 Eve'g with newspaper \+ writing notes.
