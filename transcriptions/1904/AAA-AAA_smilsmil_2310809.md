## Left Page

### Mon. May 23, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine day — growing warmer. Was
[illegible] at H.D. Allen's — Arizonas. 74
S. \+ Broadway at 9 o'clk — as per engage-
ment the took imps. in wax \+ plaster
of mouth — lower jaw. I got home at 10
then to studio \+ got to work on Borden
drawing at 11 — home to lunch at 1 \+
after that to 2, Nat. Bank — to studio
at 3 \+ worked till 5.30 — then walked
about till dinner time — 6.30 — after
that, feeling very heavy, so, I had a
nap — then after that I wrote on a
letter to Mary till after 11 — nearly 12
when I went to bed.

Roth \+ Vondrous called —

## Right Page

### Tues. May 24, 1904

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very uncomfortably warm \+ nervating —
up at 6 \+ finished letter to Mary, commenced
last night — bkfs — after 10 when I got to
studio — worked on Borden, putting on
what I hope are the finishing touches —
Home to lunch at 1.30 — back soon after 2
cut a mount for the drawing — At 5 o'clk
went to H.D. Allen's \+ he took further imps.
of my mouth — it was 6 when he got
thro' with me — Home to dinner — Eve'g
wrote to W. Wm. A. White asking if I could
hire some of the St. Hubert's nun plated
ware for the use of the Misses Stokes — Then
went to Walker Cook's \+ left for his signature
the Trustee's Cert. declaring a div. of 1½o
on Ireland Real Estate Co. stock. Then
to Ed. Cook's \+ saw Edna \+ Tom — also a
whist party of Carleton \+ wife — Murphy
pere \+ Gomez — home at 9.15
