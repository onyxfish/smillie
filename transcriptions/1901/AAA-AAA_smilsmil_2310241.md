## Left Page

### Mon. April 8, 1901

**Weather:** Clouded but pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Clouded but pleasant. went to murray Hill Theatre \+ bought tickets for friday night for Jim, Ralph \+ self. Then to studio, worked for awhile on Zinc plate — Fish Market, nice. using scraper on sky. Maurice Sterne came in. I took prints from the plate — also from two other experimental \+ soft-ground plates with discouraging results. Why do I persist in this work? James Faiman surprised me by a visit — I have not seen him for years — his card is now — Jas. Faiman M.A. not Col, as of old — He is the same old Faiman only a little more so — valuable \+ abusive. He tells me that he is 76 — is a scholar — able to read his bible in Greek \+ a licensed minister in the Congregational church — a literary man, a lecturer \+ an artist. His second wife has got possession of most of his property — his children have turned against him but he continues on his way, unsubdued \+ undaunted. At 1 o'clk I went to 21st. Home to lunch at 1.45 — again to Studio \+ took large paper, india proofs of etching. Father at pee pen — — but they were not satisfactory — worked as long as I could. see \+ home to dinner at 6.45. Thoroughly tired out \+ disheartened. Every I loafed in my big chair — Charl came in for a little while — to bed early.

## Right Page

### Tues. April 9, 1901

**Weather:** overcast, but pleasant  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim came home for vacation.

overcast, but pleasant — Took the large vol. scrap-book "In memoriam", prepared by father. To studio. Made a tracing of the Beaton portrait — 1825 — transferred it to a ground \+ then drew it up on the plate getting it all in \+ varnished ready for the acid before I went home to lunch at 1.30. Then back \+ bit the plate. I had trouble with it having used an unusually sharp needle. The acid took hold unevenly — some portions of the work biting rapidly while other portions went very slowly — but I cleaned it up \+ then found that it had come out fairly well. There to 6th Ave. \+ 23d. St, \+ bought lotion — there to Bayerdorfer's \+ home at 6.30 — Jim had reached home shortly before having spent last night at New Haven with his tutor of two or three years ago, Curtis Walker. Dinner — We't had some talk with the boys \+ settled accts. with Jim up to May 1st.
