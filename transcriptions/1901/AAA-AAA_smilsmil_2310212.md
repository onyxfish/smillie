## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 9, 1901

**Weather:** Bright \+ sunny day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ sunny day \- I passed a very uncomfortable night with fever, pain \+ restlessness \- During the day the catarrhal symptoms in my head increased \+ the soreness in my throat \- I got a note from Violet Beach \- an invitation to me from her mother to dine with them at the Plaza Hotel this eve'g. Afternoon, soreness of my throat extended to my chest \+ fever increased. Eve'g Mary sent for D. Yale \+ he came about 9 p.m.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 10, 1901

**Weather:** Bright, sunny day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, sunny day \- under the influence of Two 1/8 gr. doses of morphine \- one taken at 10 p.m. the other at 2 A.m, I got thro' the night in comparative comfort \- it was not sleep but a sort of intoxication \- Temp. got up to 101 3/5° \+ then declined \- at 6 This m'g I took Acetanilid \+ caffeine \+ then spent four hours in a dreamy state \- D. Yale came about 11 o'clk \- my pulse normal at that time \- a good deal of discomfort in my head all the time \- bowels moved about 4 p.m. \+ after that I had a solid natural sleep until 6.30 \- I had a little milk \+ biscuit \+ after that a drowsy eve'g, my eyes hurting so that I could not read \+ voice entirely gone.
Ralph brought his male canary to my room \+ it sung very pleasantly.
