## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 25, 1901

**Weather:** Bright cold day  **Temp:** [illegible]

after bkfst. went to murray Hill Theatre \+ bought seats for Ralph \+ self for friday eveg. Then to Miller's. carpenter — Then to Owens \+ to laundry \+ at last, to studio. Painted until 1 o'clk on Rise of River, making important changes in foreground \+ repainting waterfall — Then I went to Clausen's on 5th Ave. \+ enquired about frame he is making for me. Then home to lunch. It was 3 o'clk before I went to studio — painted for about an hour — then washed up \+ walked down to Wanamakers to buy a lamp to write by — but did not find what I wanted — then walked home, stopping at Bayardorf's \+ getting my watches —
Dinner at 6.30 — Eveg reading.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 26, 1901

**Weather:** Pleasant — partially overcast \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio \+ painted all the mts. on "the Rise of the River" — Got the painting in shape to send to the Century Subts next Sat. By the standards that prevailed here twenty years ago I think it is a fairly good picture — by the standards of today I know that it is hopelessly out of fashion. I would like, if I could, to paint something simpler in composition — large in conception \+ form \+ strong \+ subtle in color — That is my ideal — The result is over elaboration in every way — too many forms, too much working out of detail — It is a good piece of work \+ shows the result of much study \+ little inspiration. In the afternoon I went to Lotos Club to see the pictures there — almost everything was in the newest style of sketchplash indefiniteness of form \+ positiveness of color. It amuses me to see the efforts of some of the older men to keep step with the youngsters — I feel the need of new life — more virility — less of conventionality — but I cannot dispense with form the lovliness \+ grace of correct drawing. I love those elements in nature \+ I would not be true to myself if I ignored what my eyes see \+ what I have learned to love \+ gave myself over to the fad of the day which is not lovable to me \+ does not come within the range of my understanding. went to barber's \+ had my hair cut. Got to studio about 5 — just in time to meet Mr. Jobenteen who made a chatty call. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Eveg Read \+ wrote some notes —
