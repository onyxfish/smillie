## Left Page

### Thur. Feb. 7, 1901

**Weather:** Very cold & windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very cold & windy. After an unsatisfactory night I feel somuch out of sorts in themf that I did not get up until noon — I went down to lunch & after 3 I bundled up & went to studio. There I did a little experimenting with soft ground — home before 6 — Dinner at 6.30 — Rest, reading & to bed early feeling as though I was "in" for a siege of influenza —

## Right Page

### Fri. Feb. 8, 1901

**Weather:** Snow  **Temp:** [illegible]

Snow — I am "in for it" — Kept my bed all day — Sent for Dale in the m'f, she came & did what he could to comfort me — but it did nt ant so much.
