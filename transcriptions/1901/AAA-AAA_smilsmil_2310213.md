## Left Page

### Mon. Feb. 11, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A dismal day for me — after a very restless night I had a day of headache \+ disturbed digestives. I took a course of calomel in the m'f. — 1¼ gr. in the afternoon I had a sociality — towards night headache abated \+ then followed the most tiresome night that I remember — failing to get to sleep, at 2 a.m. I lighted candles \+ read for an hour \+ a half — then again I tried to sleep \+ at 4.30 I got up \+ made a bowl of farina gruel — After that I got to sleep \+ was not wakened till about 8 A.M.

## Right Page

### Tues. Feb. 12, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright \+ sunny — cold \+ windy. After my experience last night I felt very much exhausted. I kept my bed during the m'f. but got up in the afternoon \+ in the ev'f. came to the parlor \+ passed the ev'f. with some members of the family — I got to bed at 10 o'clk. — a except for catarrh trouble which disturbed me a number of times I passed a night of normal restful sleep.
