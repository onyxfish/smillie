## Left Page

### Sun. May 12, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day — Bkfst. soon after 9 — Before 11 Raelph \+ I started out to enjoy the m'f — went first to D. Gerster's 34 E, 75, I. — not in — then walked up 5th Ave. To 90th St. — entered Park — walked around the Reservoir \+ out on 5th Ave. at 79th St. \+ got to D. Gerster's at a little before 1 o'clk. I sprang a surprise on Raelph — Evers' niece Yale performed a slight operation on him 8 or 9 years ago — there has been an adhesion of the pressure to the gland puis. Jim has the same trouble — I knew that to separate it would hurt the boy \+ I have dreaded to subject him to pain — at last I have had the courage — R. submitted to the examination with characteristic equanimity \+ p't. q — advised an operation — we made an appointment for next Sat. at 1.30 at Sinai Hosp. Then R \+ I went home to dinner at 2 — from 3 to 4 I had a nap. Then R \+ I went to the studio where, for an hour, I continued the work of dragging out prints — Then home to supper — We'f Mary, R \+ I went to Charls. He is very much out of sorts — fever \+ pain — I went for D't. Putnam, but he was out of town for the night. Charl said he would wait until the m'f — I wrote to Breadner of Port Henry about change in water pipes at Keene Heights \+ some other notes.

## Right Page

### Mon. May 13, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Some heavy rain last night — Cloudy morning clearing to superb day before noon — Before 9 went to Charls to learn how he got thro' the night. D. says he has severe attack of tonsil'tis. Then to Murray Hill Theatre \+ bought two seats for next Friday night — Then to Studio. One of Etching Class called but I was busy with Weitenkampf of Public Library who was with me most of the m'f looking over the proofs that are scattered over my studio — After he left I resumed the work of assorting the prints — arranging them in groups \+ putting all of the duplicates together — Home to lunch soon after 1 — Then back \+ continued the work until after 5 — Then walked down to Madison Sq \+ up Fifth Ave — home — Dinner — Eve'g. I read \+ napped.
