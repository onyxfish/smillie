## Left Page

### Tues. April 16, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Did not get out of my bed all day. Yale came before 10 a.m. Have a little fever \+ much sensitiveness in the Right Iliac Region down to the groin — later in the day got a Porous Plaster \+ applied it much to my comfort. Of course it was a dismal trial to me but I was able to do some reading finding but little satisfaction in it — [illegible — ~30 words]

## Right Page

### Wed. April 17, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very bright pleasant day. I got thro' last night without much discomfort. Still very stiff, lame \+ sore in all my muscles. It was almost as tiresome \+ painful to lie in bed as it was to be up \+ moving about; so, before noon I got up, dressed \+ gave room \+ bed a thorough sunning \+ airing. Altho' very weak I went out of doors for a little while. I wished to stay in the sunshine \+ breath the warm air of out of doors — but I felt so weak that I did not dare trust myself. I had a nap in the afternoon \+ Dr. Yale came about 5 o'clk. I was in dejected mood as I found no improvements in my condition.
