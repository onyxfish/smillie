## Left Page

### Fri. May 10, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy downpour of rain all day - Where does it come from? Spent the day at studio among my prints trying to bring a little order out of a chaotic confusion - I got down dusty portfolios \+ packages that had not been opened for years \+ turned out their contents - I could do little more than roughly assort them - foreign \+ home - further subdividing home into groups of work by father - Hinshelwood - other Am. engravers \+ my own work.
In the Eve\. at home I got out work that had been stored away there - some of the most valuable old autograph pfs. have been kept at home. I wrote a long letter to Jim \+ went to the G. C. Station \+ mailed it after 10 p.m.

## Right Page

### Sat. May 11, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull \+ muggy - shower about 6 p.m. To Wy Miller's immediately after bkft. then to studio - then, after paying several small bills by the way, to Scribner's where I had to wait some time before I could see Mr. Smith. He told me that Mr. Borden had decided to make his Library Cat. much larger, in accordance with my suggestion - the size of Etching Club Cat. \+ also, to have a folio Cat. of his pictures - the size of the Wilson Cat. - with an etching after each painter that he has represented in his collection. This promises to be a mine for me - it was near noon when I got back to studio \+ for the rest of the day I worked persistently at assorting \+ arranging my prints - gathering all of father's work \+ separating it into groups - Very early - Rawdon Wright Hatch \+ Smillie - Smillie \+ Hinshelwood - Smillie - \+ Smillie \+ J.D.S. - The value of the collection grows as I get to be more familiar with it -. I find that I have a large number of Hinshelwood's works \+ can make a good showing for him - also of many other early Am. engravers. I have many fine English proofs \+ prints \+ some very good lithographs - I have so many duplicates of father's work that I shall be able to make a good contribution to the British Museum \+ to the Boston Museum. Dinner - Eve\. loafing with newspapers.
