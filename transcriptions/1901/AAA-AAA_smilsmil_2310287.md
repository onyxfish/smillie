## Left Page

### Thur. July 11, 1901

**Weather:** Overcast m\+f. with light showers about 9  **Temp:** [illegible]

\- Thunderstorms south of us \- a very pleasant day on the whole. I got up feeling very tired \+ demoralized but that passed away as the day advanced. I went to the Keues at 9 but no one was there \- about 10 Radeker, Sagier \+ Dennis came \+ the work then went on \- With Dennis I packed the big box with my Table \+ a great quantity of bed \+ table linen towels etc. At 12 went to Sayre's \+ rested until dinner \- After 2 again went to K's \- gave most of my time to hauling out the contents of my studio's closet. To Sayre's at 5.45 \+ rested till eve\+meal Eve\+. Ralph \+ I went down town and did some shopping which took us until about 9 \- I went to bed at 10 \- [illegible — ~6 words]

## Right Page

### Fri. July 12, 1901

**Weather:** Charming day \- refreshing air \- m\+f. \+ eve\+  **Temp:** [illegible]

\+ got up feeling somewhat rested \- went to Nek's. Radeker came after 9 \+ worked part of the m\+f. I could not keep him going all the time \- I find this part of the packing the most confusing \- most of the stuff being contents of studio's closet \+ a number of very price objects. To Sayre's about 12.30 \+ rested until dinner. To K's after 2 \- Radeker with me for about 2 hours. About 5 o'clk I went down town \+ bought some tea things at True's that Katharine had written to Ralph for \- then to Curny's \+ got shoes \- then to harness shop \+ got straps \- then to foundry \+ saw Herrick \- asked him to see Beach about road roller that I want to sell. To Sayre's after 6 \- most of the family (Ralph with them) took their eve\+. meal at the Country Club house \- Eve\+. I went down town \+ opened negotiations with Cooley for a stove to heat Studio at Keene. Then to S. \+ read until 9.30 \-
