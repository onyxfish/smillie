## Left Page

### Mon. Jan. 28, 1901

**Weather:** Cold \+ windy — clear part of the day — but mostly cloudy.  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio at usual time \+ to work on my Keene Valley painting that I think I shall christen "After Showers" — I painted over parts of the sky drawing it together by quieting the obtrusive cloud drawing, intensifying highlights \+ cooling some of the shadows. I worked also over parts of distance. Home to lunch at 1.30 \+ back at 2.30 — Clausen sent for frame for "Passing Showers" I worked for an hour — then feeling out of humor for more work I went to Clausen's gilding shop \+ made a miniature of bronzes to be put on "Passing Showers" frames — Then to 51 W. 10th S. to see Dielmann — not in — Nicole — gone to California — Then rode up to 57th S. \+ saw Beckwith for a few minutes. Home to dinner at 6.30 — Suet. wrote to Delanoy \+ Delanoy \+ to Herbert Smith about five ms. \+ to Sils \+ Sterue about Etching Class matters — After my talk with Beckwith I shall not attempt to have allied my class — The medal class proves to be a sort of cuckoo bird \+ is already crowding the Etching class out of its nest.

## Right Page

### Tues. Jan. 29, 1901

**Weather:** Clear, cold \+ windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio about 10 and painted all the m'f on Keene Valley (After Showers) going over the middle distance changing the color from very dark green to a purple shade. I have not hit the color I want — Home to lunch at 1.30 — back in an hour \+ after a little painting concluded that I had done all I could at this stage — Then I took "Vesuvius from near Sorrento" out of its frame — a very heavy one \+ by engineering got it on four chairs, face down — \+ there I screwed heavy legs on the back \+ attempted to set it up on its feet after putting "The Rise of the River" on it — It was more than I could do \+ I came near breaking my neck — getting it part way up \+ unable to get it all the way up or let it down — I was in a critical fix for a time but managed to prop it with a small easel while I went to Ryan's office for help. Home at 6 — did not take a walk being lamed by a bruise on something of the kind on my left foot — Suet. loafing — Sheldon Smillie came in for a while.
