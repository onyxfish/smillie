## Left Page

### Sun. March 3, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright day \- somewhat cloudy + windy
Bkfs. at 9.30 \- Read awhile + when R.
ret'd. from Sunday School he + I went
out + had a walk \- stopped at Charl's
+ was there some time \- then to Century
Club + was for some time there \- met a
number of friends + had a talk with Mr.
Jenner about education of young people
Then we walked up 5th Ave. + home to
dinner at 2 \- After dinner I regained
the sleep I lost last night by taking a
little nap \- Ge°. came in the afternoon
+ so did Charl \- Tea at 6.30. Mollie
Adams came in a little before 7 o'clk
having come direct from Buffalo \-
Eve'g. after reading awhile I went
out + posted some notes + then went
to Charl's \- He said he wants painting
"After Showers" + agreed to give me $300
for it without frame \- which was very grati-
fying to me. I took away his painting
by me, "Spring is Coming", to repaint it. I
took it to studio \- then home + to bed.

## Right Page

### Mon. March 4, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dull, mild day, rain coming in the evening
I got to studio at usual time + then work-
ed all day on the upright that I painted
for Charl some years ago \- I found that
the changes w'd. have to be radical so I
painted solidly over the sky changing the
key of color entirely \- I worked through a
headache which made it hard but I
got over the entire canvas pretty gener-
ally \- I changed attitude of calf + introduced
cows behind it. Home to lunch at 1.30
back at 2.30 \- worked until about 4.30
\- had a short walk on Park Ave. + home
before 6 \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g. \- a
little reading + a little writing.
