## Left Page

### Wed. Dec. 4, 1901

**Weather:** Cold + windy  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold + windy - my cold seems to have changed its character - the sore throat of yesterday is less trouble-some - last night the soreness went down to my chest + this out of my head is much stuffed - I am dosing myself with Warburg tinct. + Liver Pills - It was 10 o'clk when I got to the studio but I put in a good days work on Borden plate with graver + dry point. I was much used up + was not able to take any exercise. Ewetf, Charle came in + then Mr. J. H. Maghee. The latter was invited to dine with us to-morrow + he asked the girls to go to Theatre with him Sat.

## Right Page

### Thur. Dec. 5, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine winter day - am feeling much better - Met Mr. Maghee at midoon as I went out after bkfs. I walked to 42 St. with him + then I went to Am. Yp. with skates for Ralph - Then to studio + put in another day on Borden plate. Ewetf. Maghee dined with us - he has added many to his Theatre party.
