## Left Page

### Fri. Nov. 22, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very fine day. I had a very bad night, waking at 1.30 \+ being very restless \+ wakeful after that. mt. temp. 99 1/2 \- Pulse 105 \- at a little before 7 o'clk \- a rush thro' the bowels \- I felt so disheartened that I sent for Dr. Cilley \- he came at 9.30 \- my temp. then had receded to normal \- He seemed to think the trouble was altogether in the bowels that had become clogged \+ he advised a warm enema of salt water \- so I had another clearing out \- Got up at 11.30 had bkft. then to studio \+ finished biting commenced yesterday \- worked till 4\. with dry point \+ graver \- then to Klimmel \+ Voigts \- Canal St. \+ arranged with Henry V\- to get 3 pfts. of Borden Plate to-morrow p.m. Then I walked all the way up home without inconvenience \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Evening headache became quite severe but I wrote short letters to Jim \+ Ralph \- then following Dr. Cilley's direction I took a Castor oil Cock Tail \+ to bed \-

## Right Page

### Sat. Nov. 23, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain \+ overcast \- Rain \+ wind after 5 p.m. It was 9 o'clk when I had bkft. \- as I had three sauces during the past night. It was a little after ten when I got to studio \+ worked on plate with graver \+ dry-point till 12.30 \- then home \- had a slice of bread. then to Klimmel \+ Voigts \+ Hy. V\- drew two working pfts. from Borden plate for me \- three on [illegible] Jap sheets having typo-graphic title page on one half. These I am to dry \+ force \+ touch with india ink to look as near like finished pft. as I can get them to \- Three copies are to be bound immediately. The pfts. come much stronger than I had expected but I think that I can touch them up so that the three will pass muster. to studio before 4 \+ spent an hour tracking down my working pfts. \+ getting the three japs. in blotters between boards \- then home. Dinner at 6.30 \- [illegible] Charl came in \+ stayed till 10.30.
