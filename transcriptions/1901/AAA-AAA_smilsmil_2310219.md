## Left Page

### Sat. Feb. 23, 1901

**Weather:** overcast  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast most of the day — feeling like snow storm. To studio at 10.30 + painted until 1 o'clk on "River's Rise" — then home to lunch + back soon after 2 + painted until 4 — feeling very tired. I am anxious now to get this canvas so far advanced that I can send it to the Century next Sat. — went to see how it looks out of my studio + among other paintings —
Met Maynard in the hall + he inquired after my health. I went in to Titian + opened the subject of a raise in his rent — he met it by asking for a reduction — no progress — Ralph came at about 4.30 — we took a short walk together on Park Ave. Home at 5.30 + rested until dinner. Eve'g. Rather stupid — half headache.

## Right Page

### Sun. Feb. 24, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A light fall of snow last night. Bright day with cold N.W. wind. Up before 8 + had a good rub down before bkf. went to studio + started up fire — then to bkf. — again to studio at about 10.30 + painted on River's Rise until near 1 o'clk. A rare thing for me to work on Sunday — but my sickness has thrown me so seriously behind that I have to do it. I am anxious to get this painting into shape to send to Century next Sat. + have a jute clearly in mind much that I wish to do on it. Cleaned up + for nearly an hour walked up + down Park Ave. on the sunny wind-protected side. Home to dinner at 2 — had my usual Sunday afternoon nap. Chase came in between 5 + 6 o'clk — + Co? a little later. Tea at 6.30 — Eve'g. I wrote to Jim + mounted photo. for Ralph. To G.C. Station with Jim's letter at 10.
