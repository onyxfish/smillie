## Left Page

### Tues. April 30, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mary ret'd from Po'k.

An uncomfortably warm day - growing close \+ muggy - Immediately after bkf. I went to W. Borden's - saw him - he readily agreed to have the etching of his library made horizontal instead of upright - also to have a less no. of jugs on his mantel shelf. I spent the evf. there \+ made a sketch from a point (or rather, a combination of points) that I think is the best composition of lines yet made - I finished the gallery sketch. Home to lunch at 1 - then to N.A. - Etching Cl. full - Have started this afternoon on competition for Baldwin Prizes - old Mondellay (?) the Italian Turk as model - to-day they made studies with crayon \+ pencil preparatory to drawing the same pose upon the plate directly from life. It was after 5 when I got to studio - I spent about an hour there picking up things - home at 6 - Mary had got in from Po'k a little before - dinner at 6.30 - Welf. Charl came in for a little while - I had a little writing to do - wrote a note to Beckwith about jury for Etching class work.

## Right Page

### Wed. May 1, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain in the m'f but clearing in the afternoon with a chilly easterly breeze. After bkf. went to studio \+ worked with pen over the sketch that I made in the Borden library yesterday m'f. I stuck to it until 1.30 but did not finish it - then home to lunch - back to studio at 2.30 \+ in an hour or so had finished pen work \+ at once took it to W. Smith at Scribner's - he was much pleased \+ says - go ahead - then to Pack Bro's - \+ arranged to have one of the operatives meet me at W. Borden's to-morrow at 9 am. to see just what is wanted - then home at a little before 5 - Ralph went with me to Rogers, Peet \+ Co. \+ got some Spring clothes - then to studio - home at 6.15 - Katharine \+ Desire dined at the Holts - a quiet dinner at home - Jane Banks, our "up stairs girl" was obliged to go away sick this afternoon - something like grips - Charl came in for awhile in the ev'f.
