## Left Page

### Thur. Oct. 17, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Smma Bourne came.

Sunny for a little while in early m'f — then clouded — some rain about 10 a.m. Showery until p.m. then again clear — After slept. went to Douglas's about cove in bed room. Then to studio \+ etched on Borden plate till 12 — then to Bradley \[illegible\] in 23d S'. \+ interviewed salesman about tile hearth — Satisfactory — then to bank — then home to dinner — To studio about 2 \+ etched till 5 — interrupted about 3 by darkness from heavy clouds. Then to Alexanders with shoes to be half soled — Home soon after 6. Supper at 6.30 — Emma Bourne arr? from somewhere \+ stayed with us overnight — She, with Mary, went to Charl's for the eve'f. I Read \+ wrate — Rec. from Charl $300 for painting "After showers" — which came conveniently to pay Ralph's half yearly school bill at Hackley Hall.

## Right Page

### Fri. Oct. 18, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright cool \+ breezy — To studio at 9.30 — Etched all day on Borden plate. At 12.30 — to bank with the $300 from Charl — then home to dinner — then to studio \+ worked till near 5 — then to Wanamaker's \+ made ineffectual efforts to get my accts there straightened out — bought an $8.50 wilton rug for my bed room — New home after 6 — supper — Eve'f wrate several notes \+ drew checks — to G.C. Station at 10 go \+ mailed some of them.
