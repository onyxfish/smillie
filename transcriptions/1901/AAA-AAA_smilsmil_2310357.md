## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 30, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very fine day — worked all day with dry point on Borden plate — Had a little walk on the sunny side of Park Ave. before going home to lunch — Afternoon worked till nearly half past four — then to Tonnellé's 12 & 15th St. \+ got Pen sketch of Rialto — am to get the photo. Reproductions on Wed. next. Then walked up home. Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g with news paper — Then I wrote some notes — Then out \+ walked on Park Ave. for half an hour — Then in \+ to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Dec. 1, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A mild day, bringing the frost out of the pavements \+ making it uncomfortably moist underfoot. It was about 10 when I had my bkf't. Sat with news paper till near noon — Then to studio \+ opened the windows to give it a good airing — my head bothered me but I walked up \+ down on the sunny side of Park Ave. till near 2 — Then in to dinner. After dinner I had a long nap \+ after it got up feeling better — had something to eat at 7 — Eve'g. Geo. \+ Charl came in.
