## Left Page

### Sat. May 4, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful spring day - after bkf. I went to N.A., all of the competition plates but one - I had my tools with me & worked on the plates making up defects, etc, until 1.30 - Got home at 2.15 \+ at 2.30, after a light lunch, I went to R. \+ V.'s, taking the competition plates with me - Saw Hy V- he had already printed a number of the plates I took to him yesterday - I am very well pleased with all of the work so far - To Studio's after 4 - then home - wrote awhile - \+ had a good nap before dinner - Eve'g. at 9 o'clk went to Century Club \+ had a very good time until after 12 - then home

## Right Page

### Sun. May 5, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright - breezy - cool m'g \+ rue'f - very warm middle of the day - Bkf. at 9 I went to N.A. p.p. Council met to award prizes in the School competitions - except my etching class. I was there until after 12 - then walked thro' the Park - it was very delicious - Dinner at 2 - From 3.30 to 5 had a nap - then started for Emma Ireland's - met Margaret Parker white in the street \+ walked a short distance with her - found all in at Emma's \+ Katharine Ireland as well, here on a short visit. Took tea \+ had a very pleasant visit - Jno. Valentine came in at about 8 - \+ a little later - just as I was on my way out, Edw'd \+ Mary Cook \+ Tom came in - I remained a few minutes longer \+ then walked up to Charl's where I joined the other members of my family \+ bro. Jo°. Home soon after 10 -
