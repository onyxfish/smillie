## Left Page

### Tues. Aug. 20, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Very warm \+ muggy \- Heavy showers with thunder came about noon \+ continued most of the p.m. \- Heavy mist in the early evef. I had a restless night \- my head troubling me a good deal. \- I did not sleep after 5 o'clk. \+ got up soon after 6. my head still aching. Before bkft. I had made good progress on a poster for an entertainment to be given next Monday evef at the hotel for the benefit of the Keene Heights library \- It is a brown paper about 2×8 feet \- a figure (golfer) swinging his driver \+ shouting "fore". I also fell work on another paper \- same size \- an announcement of the entertainment for the benefit of Andrew Carnegie, who is going to give $5000 to the Keene Heights library \- \+ an invitation "Bring your best girl, or two or more \- if you have 'em \- if married, bring your wife \+ your mother-in-law \- if you have a family, bring 'em all \- nurses with infants in arms, half-price" etc \- etc. I kept most of the mf finishing it \- \+ nearly all the afternoon doing lettering on second poster \- with Ralph. I helped him develop a number of photos. Webb worked all day on extension of my bed room \- got piazza part enclosed \- door \+ window set \+ old partition down \- Otis worked part of my.
Evef. Read a part of Jas. Barnes' "Princetonian".

## Right Page

### Wed. Aug. 21, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A steaming hot mf \- clouds hanging low \- clearing in the afternoon \- some fine moonlight \- later clouded. Webb started shingling the outside of my room extension. Otis came in the afternoon \+ dug holes as I directed along the east back of my road down to main road \+ together we got some arborvitae s from the cut for new road \+ transplanted them. \- I was not able to do much in my studio \+ was disgusted when I found that I had made a mistake in scale in making working drawings for alcove finish in main room, so that I lost a days work. \-
Evef. \- Read "A Princetonian" \-
