## Left Page

### Sun. March 31, 1901

**Weather:** A very fine day, but windy  **Temp:** Bkfo. at 9.30

It was after 10 when Ralph \+ I started out — we went to the end of 6th Ave. Elevated R.R. \+ then walked up the Speedway to Washington Bridge \+ thence home by 3d Ave trolley. Dinner at 2 — After 3. I went to Gelos, card from Mr. Geo. — Geo. is having exhibiting a choice lot of his paintings by Am. artists — He was holding a sort of reception — exchanged a few pleasantry sarcastic words with him. He has a remarkable collection full of autos from early to late showing his evolution in a very interesting manner — Home about 5. Po. \+ Charl There — Tea at 6.30 — Eve'f wrote letters to Mr. Kent \+ to Mrs. Sprague of Buffalo.

## Right Page

### Mon. April 1, 1901

**Weather:** Bright day with a strong \+ rather cold W. wind  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio after bkfo. \+ then went to 2d Nat'l Bank — Saw Buffalo Bill's Wild West parade — To Bayerdorfer's \+ got bill for watch. To studio at 11 — laid soft ground \+ made new drawing of Father at Po'k pig pen — Home to lunch at 1.30 — Afternoon bit the drawing \+ made preparations for another drawing of Road side Chapel at Campino. The biting of drawing made this m't was another failure — the ground seems to be too hard or my touch is not firm enough — The ground is not taken up as it should be in the tissue paper — This is discouraging as an evidence that I am losing such ability as I may have had — Home to dinner at 6.30 — Eve'f Charl spent the eve'f with us.
