## Left Page

### Sun. Aug. 18, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant, but a milky sort of day, as to atmospheric effect. I had laid out much work for the day, but beyond three or four notes \+ short letters I accomplished nothing \- I did not feel well enough, even, to make some calls, as I should, upon my summer neighbors. Ralph \+ I spent a good part of the mf filling the cold-Room icebox \- beyond that I did little but loaf about the place.

## Right Page

### Mon. Aug. 19, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An uninterestingly pleasant day \- very common place effects \- I did not feel at all fit for work of any kind \- Webb came \+ commenced work on extension of my bed room out upon piazza \+ over the bay window in main room. Such strength as I had I gave to completing drawing of finish for bay window \- I must get that order off to the mill \- Otis finished grading terrace north of house \+ he \+ John moved the stone left over \+ the place was generally cleared up \- My headache increased as the day wore on \+ in the very I was quite useless \+ until near 6 o'clk I napped in an easy chair on piazza.
