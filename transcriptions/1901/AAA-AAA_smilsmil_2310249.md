## Left Page

### Wed. April 24, 1901

**Weather:** Rain almost constantly all day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Rain almost constantly all day — at 9 a.m. I was at Mr. Borden's — 25 w. 56th St. \+ had an interview with him — he is not prepossessing in appearance — a very rich dry-goods man — large double house — with every evidence of lavish expenditure — a large collection of elegantly bound books — an expensive collection of pictures by renowned dead — jugs \+ other things in profusion — I did'nt find the picturesque that I had hoped for — I arranged with him to be at his house to-morrow m'g to make some sketches — Then to Charl's \+ at 10.30 we went to his "dark Room" in Park Ave — and selected such things as I wanted — Cullen the photographer is to take the Remainder — I took trays, graduates, \+ alot of other things — it was about 12 when I got through. After lunch I went on a hunt for Jennings with Raefel — \+ found him — He got a truckman \+ we all went to the dark Room \+ had the things carried to my studio — I worked for awhile on my accounts \+ had some comfort by finding that my average of running behind in money matters was not as bad as I had feared — the average for the past six years has been about $560 — for two years out of the six the bal. was on the right side — But the first year after Annie's death was $1870 to the bad; \+ last year it was $1135 — Perhaps by a more rigid economy this year \+ more effort to earn something I can make a better showing — Every [illegible] I went to Charl's house \+ was there until about 10.

## Right Page

### Thur. April 25, 1901

**Weather:** More Rain  **Temp:** [illegible]

More Rain — sometimes exceedingly heavy — all weather prognostications are out — the fall during the day was almost continuous but at 10 p.m. the moon, at the end of its first quarter, shone out — Got to Mr. Borden's at 9.30 and worked very steadily until near 12. getting two pencil sketches done — then walked home.
After lunch my head troubled me so much that I lay on my bed until 4 — then I went to studio \+ worked with pen \+ ink over one of the pencil sketches I made this m'g — It was near 7 before I got home to dinner — Eve'g Charl came in \+ I sat with him for a while — then I worked with pen \+ ink over second sketch of this m'g \+ to bed about 11
