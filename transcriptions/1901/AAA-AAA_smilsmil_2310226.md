## Left Page

### Sat. March 9, 1901

**Weather:** Dark, wet day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Dark, wet day. To studio after bkfp. o spent the mf. working on "Soft g°. etching" "Fish Market, Nice" — after all my troubles it is a failure — I think owing to using water too hot — It closed most of the small openings in ground, the result being a spotted \+ sandy — Home at 1 — lunch, \+ then to N.A. Spent afternoon with class, printing — Took two prints from "Fish Market" — poor thing. At 5, walked in a scotch mist from N.A. across the Park to Lex. Ave. \+ 82d. S. — Saw the "Spite house" \+ then home by car — dinner — evef. after reading awhile I felt "heavy" \+ lay down on my bed \+ had a nap for an hour —

## Right Page

### Sun. March 10, 1901

**Weather:** overcast \+ rather raw  **Temp:** [illegible]

overcast \+ rather raw — Did not get my bkfp. until about 9.30 as I wrote in my bed a short paper that I had promised to a young man attached to the Mail \+ Express concerning my class at Mella. p.s. my effort to develop a taste for Méchand Etching. Then I had my bath \+ comb — Later in the mf. I went out with Ralph — first to Chase's \+ got me time — Then, walking up Madison Ave. we met J. J. Brown, \+ with him we walked above 60th St. Talking of N.A. matters — Home to lunch at 2 — Then some more writing — Geo. came in — Tea at 6.30 — Chare came in. Evef. reading my Sunday paper for awhile until I was so sleepy that I went to bed — Heavy rain during the night.
