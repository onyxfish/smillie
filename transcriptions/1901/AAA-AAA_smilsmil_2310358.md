## Left Page

### Mon. Dec. 2, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another muggy day — It was 10 o'clk when I got to studio \+ I stuck closely all day to the Borden plate working with dry point. At 12 30 I walked up to Cutter's — 18 E. 50" St. \+ left note \+ money to pay small bill — then home to lunch \+ to studio at 2 \+ worked till 4.30 — Then I walked for a time on Park Ave, but the air was damp \+ my throat felt raw \+ I went in a little before 6\. dinner at 6.30 — Most of the eve\+ spent going over my diary \+ making an acc\+ from it of time worked on the Borden order — original sketches — India ink \+ oil painting etc.

## Right Page

### Tues. Dec. 3, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain \+ wind all day — Sleety snow at night. After a most uncomfortable night I awoke with a severe headache — did not get up to bkf\+ till 9. my throat continuing to trouble me — I got to studio soon after 10 \+ worked continuously on Borden plate with dry point \+ graver till nearly 2 — Then home — had lunch \+ at 3.30 lay down on my bed \+ slept soundly till 6 — Dinner at 6.30 — Eve\+ reading
