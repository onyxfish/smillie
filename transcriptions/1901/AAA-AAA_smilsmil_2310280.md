## Left Page

### Tues. June 25, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another very hot day. about 85° about 10 o'clk. went to bank - cut all coupons coming due before Oct \+ left them with the cashier - left pass book to be balanced. Then to Bradley \+ Currier \+ saw Mr. Chester about mantel for my bed room. Selected one that will require modifications of its proportions before I can use it - to studio before noon \+ worked until nearly on a scale drawing of mantel - Then to lunch - wrote a letter to Kate - Then a nap - Then to studio \+ worked until 6 getting together paper \+ printing materials etc. for Keene. Dinner at 6.30 - Charl came in for a few minutes - After dinner Ralph \+ I strolled about for an hour or more finding it cooler in the street than in the house.

## Right Page

### Wed. June 26, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another blazing hot day. After bkf. went to Walter Cook's office in 29th S. \+ showed him the scale drawing I had made of an adaptation of a Bradley \+ Currier design for mantel - he approved - then to 2d Nat. bk. - then to B.\& C. Arranged with Mr. Chester for a mantel to be ordered by Miller for me. - Then to the barber, cor. Park Ave. \+ 42d S. \+ had hair cut \+ a shampoo - Then to Miller's \+ told him about mantel at B.\& C.'s - Then home - wrote to Cambridge Univ. Pub. Co. stating that it w'd be impossible for me to write the chapter on etching for 2d. Ed'n. of Koehler's work as desired. Lunch - Afternoon, wrote a long letter to Ella Diven in reply to one rec'd. from her some days ago. - Then to Gillespie's \+ bought hardware for Keene - did some other small shopping - to studio \+ home. Dinner at 6.30 - Evely went to studio for a short time - Charl came \+ stayed until 10.
