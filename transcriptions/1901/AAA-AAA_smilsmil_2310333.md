## Left Page

### Sun. Oct. 13, 1901

*To New Haven + back.*

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Heavy rain in the early m'f \- cessation between 8 A.m. & 3 p.m., then more heavy rain. I went to New Haven on the 10.? train getting there at 11.55 \- I went direct to D Wm. Elkins \- 477 Prospect St. & then found Jim \- we had dinner at about 1.5 at about 2.30 Jim + I walked down to Reason Hall + spent an hour or more in his Room \- No. 559. Jim is not communicating but I learned that he is having a very good time, would like to have his room more fully furnished + a larger monthly allowance. It is evident that he is having trouble with the sharks who feed on freshmen. I did not learn much about his studies or recitations \- not because he was unwilling to tell \- Rather because of natural reticence + inability to express himself freely \- About 4.30 we walked thro' gentle rain to Nelie Adams' in Edwards St. D. Elkin + Katie then also Mr. Todd, who has the care of Nelie, + her son \- also Mollie, of course. Nellie seemed to be remarkably well \- greeted me as usual \- seemed to be interested in general conversation + to a limited extent took part in it \- she was very quiet in manner \- looked well \- + gave no evidence of mental failing \- We all sat down to tea soon after 6 \- + at 7.30 Jim + I went to R.R. Station where I took 8.10 train + got home at 10 o'clk.

## Right Page

### Mon. Oct. 14, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very wet day \- To studio about 9.30 + at once commenced preparations for making transfer of gelatine tracing to etching ground. I was very nervous about it + took every precaution I could think of + was very successful in getting a clear, sound transfer without injury to the ground. Home to dinner at 1 o'clk \- Afternoon ground points for etching \- varnished plate + cut feathers on margin \- Then, as it was dark + late I thought it unwise to begin the etching + went to W. 36th St. to Saltmann's + saw wood combo coves + took home with me a sample \- Welf wrote a short note to Jim + checks to Prescott + Son \- Geo. F. Beede + others.
