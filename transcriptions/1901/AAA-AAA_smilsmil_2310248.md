### Mon. April 22, 1901

Jim returned to Pomfret
many went to Po'keepsie.

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still dull, unsettled weather - some efforts at clearing - some efforts at Rain - neither quite successful. After bkp. I wrote to Miss Kent giving in a general way a statement of the articles I expected to take from Montrose before giving possession — also to Spensar, sending him a check — Mr. Horace Winans called concerning a Real Estate deal he is trying to make — Mary took 10.30 train to Bk where she expects to stay for a few days. At 10.30 I went to G. C. Station with Jim — He got away for Pomfret on the 11 o'clk train — Then home — wrote until lunch at 1.30 — after lunch I went to studio for a while worked on coat — portrait of father - 1825 - at 4.30 went home \+ with Raelph went to Cantrell's, in 23° St \+ bought for him a fair of shoes — on way home stopped at Wunderlich \+ Clauson's — Dinner at 6.30 — Eve'g. ## Right Page

### Tues. April 23, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Still dull - weak attempts to be good - futile efforts to be bad — I got a note in the m'g from Mr. Marcou, of Scribner's indicating that there was a chance of my getting a job there - so, immediately after bkp. I went there - Saw M. \+ by him was introduced to Mr. H. F. Smith who explained that Mr. W. C. D. Borden of 25 W. 56th St was getting up a very choice catalogue of his books \+ would like to have an etched plate as a frontis piece - do I arranged to see B — to-morrow m'g. Home \+ did some work on posting accts getting somewhat mixed at times — the result of my work so far is depressing as it seems to be made quite plain that living as I am now I cannot live within my income — Lunch at 1.30 again worked on acct — until after 4 — then I had a nap — up at 6 — dressed myself \+ at 7.30 went to Waeter Cooks to dinner — met W. \+ Mr. Smith — parents of Catharine Cook's "intended" — miss. W. \+ Mrs. Edw. Cook — Sallie Cook \+ W. Kikbs — After dinner Sallie's intended W. Vernon Munroe, came in - also Catharine \+ her young man — I got home at 11 \+ found a note from Raelph saying that Charl wanted to see me \+ would be up until 11.30 - so I went to his house \+ saw him — He has to clear out his "Dark Room" on Park Ave. To-morrow \+ he wished to make arrangements with me to meet him there \+ he would turn over a lot of things to me — Home \+ to bed at 12
