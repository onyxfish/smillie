## Left Page

### Fri. Aug. 16, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

*Miss Lane left us.*

Superb day \- bright \+ breezy \- Miss Lane left about 9 o'clk \- Ferd. Beede drove her over to Port Henry \- Geo. spent the day loafing on the piazzas \- Webb \+ Otis worked \- Otis got Wall of terrace north of the house \- I made plan of change in my bed room \+ Webb started on the work of extending it out over the bay window of the floor below \- Made out bill of lumber etc. for Fletcher Beede mill \- also worked on plan of revised Bay window finish \- I was kept so busy that I did nothing on Bordeau work \- After 4 I walked in the Reserve, on the road as far as Gill brook reservoir \- The effects were enchanting \+ I enjoyed the walk immensely, getting some slight pencil notes \- Stopped at Scott Browns on my way out \+ got from him putty \+ white lead also \- Map \+ game laws \- Evef in my bed room with newspapers.

## Right Page

### Sat. Aug. 17, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A warm day \+ rather misty \- Practically all of my day was given to the putting in of plate glass in my studio door \+ the north vestibule doors \- There was much fussing \+ Webb assisted \- Otis did not work to-day \- I worked also on drawing of modified design for finish of bay-window \- \+ getting out a bill for window blinds etc. etc. to be sent to R. Prescott \+ son at Keeseville \- I went over to hotel at noon \- I had a saunter after 5 o'clk in the afternoon \- Evef \- Reading newspaper \+ loafing \- Katharine \+ Elizabeth at Hotel help with Meghee.
