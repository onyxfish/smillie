## Left Page

### Tues. Oct. 15, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day — Etched all day on the Borden plate — home to lunch at 1 o'clk. Miller came \+ I consulted with him concerning different methods of making coves for ceilings — After lunch I went with him to J. Pierpont Morgan's house to see some plaster work there done by Clees \+ Bro. Then to studio \+ worked till near 5 — Then to Clees Bro — near the foot of E. 40th St. Saw one of the bro. — he will call at the house to-morrow — Then to Bayerdorfer's with Ralph's watch. Supper at 6.30 — Eve'g Charl came in \+ I went with him \+ the girls back to his house — there till 10.30.

## Right Page

### Wed. Oct. 16, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Beautiful day. To studio at 10 \+ worked till ) on Borden etching — Then home to lunch. Clees, the plaster moulding man came and measured the cove in my bed room \+ is to send me an estimate of cost — P.M. worked on etching till near 5 o'clk — then I washed my painting "After Showers" \+ "oiled it out" — Then messages — took umbrella to be covered \+ Jim's bag to be mended. Dinner at 6.30. Eve'g. Ge? \+ Nellie came in for awhile. I wrote to Ralph \+ at 9.30 went to studio \+ got "After Showers" \+ took it to Charles.
