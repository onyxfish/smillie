## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 8, 1901

**Weather:** over cast \+ dull \- quiet \+ quite mild  **Temp:** [illegible]

did not get down to bkfst. till about 10 \+ after 1 o'clk. went out on Park Ave. \+ walked up \+ down there till 2 o'clk \- then in to dinner \- The remainder of the afternoon was loafed away in rather uncomfortable manner \- Evef wrote a letter to Fanny Wright Davis \- at Colorado Sprngs to accompany photos of Jim \+ Ralph. These photos have been promised since last spring \-

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 9, 1901

**Weather:** Dull overcast day \- mild \+ humid \-  **Temp:** [illegible]

are the street pavements wet \+ muddy. Had a long day on Borden plate and got it ready for proving \- I hope that it is now very near the end \- for my work I found that the west side of Park Ave. was dry \+ between 5 \+ 6 o'clk I walked there \- dinner \- Evef I did some writing.
