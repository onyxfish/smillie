## Left Page

### Tues. Sept. 17, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A heavy, rainy day - but the rain did not begin to fall until about 9 o'clk. Webb came up but I could not keep him at work all day - Cook has not yet put in an appearance with the Prescott lumber etc. - causing me great annoyance + some expense - He has treated me very badly + failed to keep his promise. Jacqueth + Keeper worked on the culbert - I do not think they could have put in a full day - I worked on Borden drawing + finished it. Afternoon Maghu sat for his Portrait - Katharine painting in my studio while I worked - Late I went to hotel + tried to get Station agent at Ausable Forks, but station was closed - Met Otis + had a talk with him about the line of the new road thro' my property. He drew a discouraging picture of probable wreck along my line with destruction of bank + trees - I have been too easy in signing a right of way without stipulations about mess.
Ev'g after a little reading of newspapers to bed.

## Right Page

### Wed. Sept. 18, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Overcast + raw day - clearing towards ev'g quite cool - After bkft. I was engaged with Ralph until nearly 11 o'clk filling the Cold Room with ice. I found it a heavy piece of work - Then I went to hotel + Telephoned to Station agent at Ausable Forks + learned that Cook had not yet taken the material that has been awaiting him for a fortnight - I told him that I would send Otis for it - Some of the Prescott material is at the American Ho. Ausable Forks - I teleph'd there that I could send Otis for it. Then I went to Scott Brown's + consulted with him about the possibility of having my "dedication" of right of way for a road to town of Keene so amended that I could put on record that I gave right of way on condition that certain trees should be spared. Then I went to Otis's - saw John - He agreed to go to Ausable Forks for the lumber etc. This p.m. Then again to hotel + to dinner at 1:5 - about 3:30 someone - presumably Cook - brought + delivered at the laundry a portion of the material that Otis has gone for this p.m. - a working at cross-purposes that will result in trouble - Katharine painted on Magbee portrait for a while posing - I worked on Borden 12×18 outline preparing to paint to-morrow. After 5 I went to Scott Brown saw him + Mrs. B - + asked if Mr. B. would care for me for a few days after the family leaves - + to this she kindly consented - Then home to tea - Ev'g a good fire in the big stove in studio made it the most comfortable place in the house, and there I sat + read until I went to bed.
