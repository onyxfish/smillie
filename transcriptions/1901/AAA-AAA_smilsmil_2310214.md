## Left Page

### Wed. Feb. 13, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright, sunny, windy & cold — awoke at about 8 o'clk. after a fairly restful night. Bkfs. in bed — got up at 11 — had lunch in Parlor at 1.30. Spent most of the afternoon in Parlor — wrote to Jas. Morris Jr. & to Edw'd. Cook — had a nap from 4 to 6. Dinner in the Parlor — wrote to Charl concerning Smelters telling him that I would like to get out — Katherine & Bessie spent the evng. there. To bed soon after 10.

## Right Page

### Thur. Feb. 14, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Weather still very cold & windy but bright & sunny — Had my bkfs. in bed & got up before 11 o'clk. I then went to the dining room, where there was a good fire, & wrote during the rest of the mng. to Cooley & Son & to Wm. Speuse at Montrose. Lunch at 1.30 — Spent much of the afternoon in my bed room where it is sunny. I had a nap before dinner & got thro' the day without any great discomfort altho' my head ached somewhat at times — I feel stronger than I expected & if the weather will only moderate I hope to get out pretty soon. Charl came in for a little while before dinner \+ Geo. spent a part of the evening with us.
