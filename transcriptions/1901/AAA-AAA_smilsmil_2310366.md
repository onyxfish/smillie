## Left Page

### Fri. Dec. 20, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Ralph came home.

Fine day \- to studio at 9.30 \- spent the m'g cleaning up \+ mounting for Kate a rumpled print that Kate is going to give to Jim \- Home to lunch \+ back \- general work at studio \- changing Borden pf. between plates in Press \- more mounting for Kate \- also some like work for self \- Before 5 went to Scribners \- saw W. Smith \- Borden has seen pf. of plate \- objects to Table because it is not in the middle of the rug \- \+ is too far back in the picture \- otherwise, has no comments to make \- says every print of every description taken from the plate must be given to him \- in answers to my request prone to give to Inst. Dep. Public library says that is his prerogative \- altogether it makes me feel pretty low in my mind to have to deal with a man so uneducated \+ unsympathetic \- Walked until 6 \- Then home \- Ralph there \- came down on 1.46 train \- Quiet evening with family \-

## Right Page

### Sat. Dec. 21, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

clear cold day with strong northerly wind. To studio at 9.30 \+ worked there at general cleaning up till 10.30 \- then to Scribners taking a proof of Borden plate that I took Thursday. Title page sheet. This I left with W. H. Smith to show to Mr. B \- when he comes at 12 to-day \- I then went to Am. Art Ass'n \+ saw E. A. Abbey's "Holy Grail" pictures for the Boston library \- They are interesting but I cannot admire them unreservedly \- To studio after 12 \- home to lunch at \- back to studio at 3 \- Got to work with crayon on a pf. of Borden's plate \+ find that there is much yet to be done on it \- left at 4.30 \- walked on Park Ave. till 6 \- Then home \- Ralph lunched with Bronson Cutting \+ then went to theatre with him \+ his father \- Dinner at 6.30 \- Eve'g before a pleasant fire with newspapers.
