## Left Page

### Sun. Dec. 22, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Fine cold day \- Bkfst at 10 \- then sat by fire + read until noon \- went to studio + opened up windows for ventilation + then took a 2½ mile airing on the sunny side of Park Ave. from 34t to 40t St. Then in to Charl's + got correct time \- Dinner at 2 \- from 4 to 6, slept \- Tea at 6.30 \- Evef wrote to W. Hy Smith \- + made check payments of some bills \- wrote a short note to Dielmann calling his attention to the Chkf defalcation for $30000 in Bureau of Arrears \- under Controller Coler \- as having been effected by methods very like Farrington's in A. of D \-

## Right Page

### Mon. Dec. 23, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

much milder \- overcast, a little bit of snow + wet at night \- After bkfst I went with Ralph to Rogers, Peet + Co. + he bought a suit of clothes \- then down to Scribner's + saw W. Smith \- he told me that Borden consented to let me give pfs of his plate to Print collections here, in Boston + British Museum. Then to Studio Miss Fuller came + made an appointment for me to meet E. C. Stedman at his home to-morrow p.m \- I worked on Borden plate reducing aggressive lines and trying to blend the many objects into a harmonious whole \- Evef at home \- newspaper \- + a little writing.
