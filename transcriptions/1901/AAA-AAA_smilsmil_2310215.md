## Left Page

### Fri. Feb. 15, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very pleasant day. after a fairly good night of rest I had my bkfst. in bed \+ at 10.30 I got up \+ went down to dining room. Read \+ wrote until near 12. Then out \+ had a satisfactory sunning \+ airing on the protected side of Park Ave. for about 3/4 of an hour. Lunch at 1.30. Afternoon Bella Hussey came \+ made a cheerful, old fashioned sort of visit. I wrote a long letter to Jim. I got his report for the month of Jan'y. He has lost one No. in his form being No. 8 in a form of 10. His average is 6 pts. lower than last month — 63 ag't. 69.
Eve'g. Raelph \+ I had the house to ourselves. I wrote a little note Emma Ireland.

## Right Page

### Sat. Feb. 16, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bright day — much milder than yesterday but still pretty cold \+ rather windy. I had my bkfst. in bed but got up at 10.30, and got out for a sunning on Park Ave. before noon. I went to studio \+ loafed about there awhile but did not enjoy it. Lunch at 1.30 and a nap on dining room sofa. Then some writing \+ an exercise walk in the parlor. Lawrence Adler called \+ at last he and Raelph met. Dinner at 6.30.
Some writing at night on accts.
