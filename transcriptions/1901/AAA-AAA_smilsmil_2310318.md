## Left Page

### Fri. Sept. 13, 1901

**Weather:** Rain during the m'g + a prompt clearing in the afternoon but no change in temperature — air heavy + close.  **Temp:** [illegible]

I wrote + worked about indoors during the m'g. After dinner I went to hotel + telephoned to Prescott at Keeseville + then to Station agent at Ausable Forks + at last got the matter of turned columns + lumber + transportation straightened out. Then to barber's + had my hair cut — then to Ferd Beede's to see about getting road roller from Westport here — then home + until tea time reading to grass grounds about the house. Quite a reception at the house — friends calling.

Much alarm caused by unexpected bad news from McKinley — it is thought that he is dying.

## Right Page

### Sat. Sept. 14, 1901

**Weather:** mild day + smoky  **Temp:** [illegible]

McKinley died.

Katchen, Mollie, Bessie, Jim + Ralph were to go up Giant this morning but gave it up because it was so smoky — they walked to Rainbow Fall, instead. Webb came + worked on Attic Store Room — Jaquith + his helper made progress on the culvert where my road joins the public road. I wrote a note or two + then got to work on india ink drawing of W. Borden's Library. Dinner at 1.30 — Afternoon, again I worked on Borden drawing + at about 5 went over to the Maghee camp + made a call there. Then to hotel + to Scott Brown's + home to tea before 7. Eve'g I had a comfortable little fire on my bed room hearth + sat before it reading newspaper until after 9.
