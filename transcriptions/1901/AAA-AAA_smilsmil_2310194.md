## Left Page

### Wed. Jan. 2, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A fine winter day — cold + clear — after bkfst. went to 2° Nat. + then to N.A. ft. + spent the m'f there with my etching class. They made drawings on their plates from Maggie Davis who posed as model. After the class I walked across the Park + found that I had an exceedingly sore stomach — got home to lunch after 2 — my stomach trouble so increased that I lay on my bed until about 4. Then I went to studio + looked after things there — then home — Eve'f, Katie + the boys dined at Charles — Many + the girls entertained at dinner N.W. + Mr. Jessups + Mr. Jessups — I was in bad shape to take the head of the table but did as well as I was able + retired soon after.

## Right Page

### Thur. Jan. 3, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Don't know what sort of a day it was as I was in my bed all day with a beast of a headache + much of the time a pain in my stomach. I did not get up till 8 o'clk — + hung about until midnight when I went to bed + lay awake for an hour or two more.
