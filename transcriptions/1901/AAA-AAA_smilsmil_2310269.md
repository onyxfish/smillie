## Left Page

### Mon. June 3, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

A very lovely day - After bkfs. went to Millers + his man followed me to the studio + completed work in dark room - At 11 I went to Dr. Gerster's + had neck dressed + back to studio about noon + there worked on outline (on hill board) of Borden Library - Home to lunch at 1.30 - to studio at 2.30 + continued work on outline - doing very badly - Ralph stolplsed going to school to-day - He was at the studio making photo-solutions - Before 6 we took a short walk + home to dinner at 6.30 - Suet, W. + Mrs Jaquini called - Ralph + I went to our new dark Room at the studio and christened it by developing a dozen of his films - Home after 10.

## Right Page

### Tues. June 4, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant warm day. Up at 5.30 + wrote a letter to Miss Ellen Major Jones (daughter of Alfred J-) in Reply to one rec'd. from her yesterday with reference to giving pfs. of her father's work to Public Library - wrote other notes till bkfs. at 8 - after bkfs. wrote checks + notes until 10 o'clk. then to studio + worked on Borden Library outline till 1 o'clk - then home to lunch - After 2 Mary, Bessie, Ralph + I went to Greenwood - Mary had not been there for many years - Bessie had never been there - the walk from entrance to our lot was very warm - but trees + grass never looked more beautiful - I tried to see MacKellar, the monument man, but he was not in his office - we got home without difficulty before 6 - Dinner at 6.30 - Suet, it being Charl's birthday, Mary, Katharine, Bessie + I went to call on him - Home soon after 9.
