## Left Page

### Sat. Nov. 16, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Pleasant except when clouded over \+ gusty. To studio betimes \+ worked with graver \+ dry-point all the m'f on Borden plate \- to lunch as usual \+ back at 2 \- then I made preparations to take a print from the plate \- Everything had been so long out of use that I met many difficulties but before dark I succeeded in getting one fairly good india proof that showed about all that I had expected \- Then I had quite a job cleaning \+ washing up \- I had a half-hours walk before dinner \+ after it, feeling sleepy \+ tired I read my Evt'g Post for awhile \+ then to bed.

## Right Page

### Sun. Nov. 17, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Over cast \+ raw \- with a feeling of snow in the air \- my scrubbing \+ rubbing in the m'f took so long that it was 9.30 before I got down to bkf'st. After that I worked in my bed room till 1 o'clk hanging the pictures \+ getting things somewhat to rights. Then I walked the streets until 2 \- \+ so to dinner \- again, in the afternoon between 4 \+ 5 o'clk. I walked three miles \- Ge°. \+ Charl at the house when I got in. I got a short rest before tea time. Entirely I felt disinclined to do any of the things I should have done \+ loafed \+ chatted with many instead of writing one of the many letters that I should have written.
