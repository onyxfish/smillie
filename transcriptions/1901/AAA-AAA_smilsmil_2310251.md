## Left Page

### Sun. April 28, 1901

**Weather:** Beautiful, mild shining day  **Temp:** [illegible]

Bkft. at 9 o by a scramble Ralph \+ I caught the 9.50 train for Fordham \+ then walked over to the Zoo gardens \- R\- had his folding camera \+ managed to get it in undetected \+ got a few snap shots \- we wandered about until near 1 \- then to station \+ get by 1.30 train getting home at 2 \- just in time for dinner \- about 4 \- feeling heavy I lay down \+ had a good sleep until after 6 \- then had supper \+ at 8.30 went to Charl's \- all hands there \- Kate, Katharine, Bessie, Ralph \+ I \- Home at 10.30

## Right Page

### Mon. April 29, 1901

**Weather:** Clear \+ very warm  **Temp:** [illegible]

To studio about 9.30 \+ worked until near 1 o'clk \- getting sketches of Borden library into shape to show to Mr. Smith at Scribner's \- then home \+ took a hasty lunch \- wheluwa Harris lunched with the girls at 1.30 \- I saw her for a moment as I went out \- To 2? nat. Back \- then to Scribner's \- W. Smith not in \- then to Riker's drug store \- back \- Smith yet out \- saw W. Marvin \+ after waiting Mr. Smith came in \- then had a long talk with him \- it was 3.30 or later when I left him \- Then to Richter's in 23? St. \+ ordered some Burlaps for Keene \- then to Barber \- 42d S \+ Park Ave. \+ had hair cut \+ head shampoo'd \- Home at 5.30 \- dinner at 6.30 \- Swell. Charl came in for a little while \-
