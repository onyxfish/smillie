## Left Page

### Thur. Aug. 22, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

An exceedingly muggy dog day — with little sunlight \+ fleecy clouds hanging low upon the mts. \+ driven by a south wind. I had a busy day following up Webb \+ Otis \+ finishing at last (I hope) the pillars \+ attendant problems for finish of alcove, main room. At noon I went to Scott Brown's about shingle stain \+ paint — on my way over saw Mr. Tiffany on her piazza with Mr. Janeway \+ Mr. Sidney Smith. Afternoon I assisted Otis in taking up \+ setting out a number of flat cedars by the side of roadway up to the house — got good specimens in good condition with little trouble. I seeded a piece of ground \+ raked gravel on carriage road \+ got in after 6 very tired \+ clothes wet thro' with perspiration — sponge bath \+ change. Tea — evef. "The Princetonians" — Miss Janeway \+ W. Willie Wisner made a short call — Miss J. — came to see Bessie about some play they are getting up.

## Right Page

### Fri. Aug. 23, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Another day much like yesterday, with a couple of light showers in the early mf \+ a very heavy thundershower between 7 \+ 8 p.m. \+ others about midnight. Webb \+ Otis were at work during the day. W. finished shingling outside \+ put up part of ceiling inside. Otis finished up all the work I have for him \+ helped me set out some flat cedars in the afternoon. I finished up plans \+ measurements \+ made rough draft of order to send to R. Prescott \+ son at Keeseville. Evef. feeling rather unhappy below the belt after looking over newspaper I got to bed early.
