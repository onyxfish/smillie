## Left Page

### Tues. Nov. 26, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Cold, windy day - & consequently, dirty - Worked all day with graver & dry point on Borden plate - At 12.30 went to 2° Nat'l & got Loan of $750. to pay my taxes - Home to lunch at 1.30 - to Studio soon after 2 & worked till 4.30 - Then I tried to get a walk for exercise but the air was so full of dirt that hurt my eyes I had to Retreat - Dinner at 6.30 - Evg. Reading & some writing of Checks etc.

## Right Page

### Wed. Nov. 27, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Jim & Ralph came home. Kate came from Po'k -

Clear & cold - After bkft. I went to Dr. Cilly's & saw him - got an addition to the items in prescription for constipation - Then to Studio & worked on Borden plate till 11 - Then down town - Went to D. L. \+ W. R.R. Co. - & got $3.55 for unused portion of milage book - then to Tax office & left my check with Tax bills - Then to Studio at 12.30 & worked till 1 - Then home to lunch - to Studio again at 2 & worked till 4 - Then to Tonelli's in W. 15th St. with Pee study of Riallo from Flower market & left it to be profd - Then walked up home - Found Jim Maple there - Kate & Jim arr? This mf - Ralph came about 5 P.M. - Dinner at 6.30 - Eveng. in general chatteration - After the others had gone to bed I tried to explain to the boys my book keeping for the estate & showed them my books & accts.
