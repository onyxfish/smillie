## Left Page

### Wed. Nov. 20, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Brilliant day - To studio before 10 \+ continued work on Borden plate - Got the ceiling all laid in very mechanically - then mussed it all up with criss cross lines - there is other work that I am adding on this same ground - I could not see to work much after 4 o'clk - so I walked up to the Audico - 97-98 Central Park West near 70 S. \+ saw Saml. Coleman. Mr. Coleman has rallied again but can not move in her bed - while there Mrs. Hy. Lewis came in - I crossed the Park at 72d. S. - beautiful Twilight \+ moonlight \+ home by Mad. Ave. cars - dinner - Evef wrote letter to Catharine Elkin \+ mailed it.

## Right Page

### Thur. Nov. 21, 1901

**Weather:** [illegible]  **Temp:** [illegible]

Mr \+ Mrs. Wm. G. Parke dined with us.

Beautiful day - To studio at 9.30 - got my plate ready for the acid in the afternoon but was not quite able to finish the biting. I began to feel out of sorts in the afternoon \+ the discomfort increased until at 5 o'clk. when I went home. I felt as tho' on the verge of collapse - no pain - but an anxiety - a sort of going-to-pieces feeling - I lay down before dinner. Mr \+ Mrs. Parke \+ Scranton came in informally \+ took pot-luck dinner with us. It was hard work for me to hold together but I got through it \+ to bed after 10.
